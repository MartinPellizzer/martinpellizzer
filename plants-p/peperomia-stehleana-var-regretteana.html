Peperomia stehleana var. regretteana

Peperomia stehleana var. regretteana, commonly known as the Regretteana Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale green, emerging from the axils of the leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

This variety is prized for its ornamental foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
