Pothos hellwigii

Pothos hellwigii, commonly known as the Variegated Pothos, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a striking variegation of green, white, and yellow, making it highly ornamental. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and is known for its ability to purify indoor air. It has a trailing growth habit and can be trained to climb or cascaded in hanging baskets.

Pothos hellwigii is relatively easy to care for, requiring moderate watering and well-draining soil.
