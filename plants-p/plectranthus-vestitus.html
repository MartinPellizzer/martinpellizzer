Plectranthus vestitus

Plectranthus vestitus, commonly known as the silver plectranthus, is a herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking silver-gray foliage due to a dense covering of fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically lavender to purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Plectranthus vestitus is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and ease of care.
