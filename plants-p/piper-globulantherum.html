Piper globulantherum

Piper globulantherum, commonly known as the globular-flowered pepper vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered white flowers and heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for horticultural and ecological studies.
