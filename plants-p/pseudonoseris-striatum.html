Pseudonoseris striatum

Pseudonoseris striatum, commonly known as the narrowleaf pseudonoseris, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. Native to southern Africa, this plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
