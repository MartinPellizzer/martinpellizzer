Peperomia fruticetorum

Peperomia fruticetorum, commonly known as the Shiny Leaf Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are oval to lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pinkish flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it suitable for use in containers or as part of a shaded garden.
