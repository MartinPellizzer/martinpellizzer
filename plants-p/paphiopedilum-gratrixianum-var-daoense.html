Paphiopedilum gratrixianum var. daoense

Paphiopedilum gratrixianum var. daoense, commonly known as the Dao's slipper orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped pouch that serves as a pollination trap. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments with rich, well-drained soil. Its flowers are usually a blend of green and yellow, with intricate patterns that aid in attracting specific pollinators.

This variety is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts due to its beauty and rarity.
