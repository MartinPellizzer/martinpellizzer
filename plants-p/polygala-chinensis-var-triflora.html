Polygala chinensis var. triflora

Polygala chinensis var. triflora, commonly known as the three-flowered milkvetch, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, and it produces small, elongated fruits that contain several seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
