Potentilla recta var. pejovicii

Potentilla recta var. pejovicii, commonly known as the Pejovicii cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to parts of Central Asia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are usually narrow and lance-shaped, with a rough texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in harsh conditions.
