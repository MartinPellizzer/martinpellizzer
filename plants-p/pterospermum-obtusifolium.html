Pterospermum obtusifolium

Pterospermum obtusifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf pterospermum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features simple, ovate to elliptical leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and are clustered in terminal inflorescences.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release numerous winged seeds, aiding in wind dispersal.
