Peperomia trinervis var. glabricaulis

Peperomia trinervis var. glabricaulis, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its compact growth habit and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container cultivation.
