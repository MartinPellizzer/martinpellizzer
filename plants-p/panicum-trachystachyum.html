Panicum trachystachyum

Panicum trachystachyum, commonly known as little bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and slender, wiry stems. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in prairie ecosystems. It produces small, pale blue to white flowers in late summer, which are arranged in dense, spike-like clusters.

Panicum trachystachyum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soil in native plant landscapes.
