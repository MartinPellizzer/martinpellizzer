Paorchis thailandica

Paorchis thailandica, commonly known as the Thai orchid, is a flowering plant native to Thailand.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate, often fragrant blossoms. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as tropical forests. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, and it produces clusters of small, colorful flowers.

Due to its unique beauty and cultural significance, it is a popular ornamental plant in horticulture.
