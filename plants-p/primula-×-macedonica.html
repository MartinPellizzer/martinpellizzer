Primula × macedonica

Primula × macedonica, commonly known as the Macedonian primrose, is a hybrid plant native to the Balkan region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 15–30 cm. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped, funnel-like flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial to full shade.

Its foliage consists of broad, ovate leaves with a velvety texture.
