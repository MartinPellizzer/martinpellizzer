Pleurothallis navisepala

Pleurothallis navisepala, commonly known as the boat-lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a slender, wiry stem with numerous compact, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually small and white or pale pink, with a distinctive boat-shaped lip.

Pleurothallis navisepala is valued for its unique morphology and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
