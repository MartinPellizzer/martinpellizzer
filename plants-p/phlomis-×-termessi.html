Phlomis × termessi

Phlomis × termessi, commonly known as the hybrid hybrid mullein, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal properties. The plant features tall, upright stems covered in soft, fuzzy hairs, and it produces showy, yellow-orange flowers in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Phlomis × termessi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to well-drained soils in full sun.
