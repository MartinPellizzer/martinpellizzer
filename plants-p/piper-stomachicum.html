Piper stomachicum

Piper stomachicum, commonly known as the stomachic pepper, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants such as black pepper. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. Its small, green flowers are followed by red, fleshy berries that contain the seeds.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional remedies for digestive ailments.
