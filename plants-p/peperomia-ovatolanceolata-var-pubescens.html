Peperomia ovatolanceolata var. pubescens

Peperomia ovatolanceolata var. pubescens, commonly known as the velvety peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features broad, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are densely covered in fine, soft pubescence, giving it a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable addition to both novice and experienced plant collections.
