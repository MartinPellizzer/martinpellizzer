Poa violacea var. nebrodensis

Poa violacea var. nebrodensis, commonly known as the Nebroden violetgrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and produces purple to violet-colored inflorescences. The species is native to the Mediterranean region, particularly found in parts of Italy and surrounding areas. Its foliage is typically blue-green, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

This variety is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive color and compact growth form.
