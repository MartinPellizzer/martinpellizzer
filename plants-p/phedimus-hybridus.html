Phedimus hybridus

Phedimus hybridus, commonly known as the silver carpet, is a low-growing perennial plant native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant features small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Phedimus hybridus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to spread and cover ground effectively.
