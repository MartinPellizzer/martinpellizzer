Peperomia lancetillana

Peperomia lancetillana, commonly known as the lanceleaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to Central and South America.

It features dense, glossy, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia lancetillana is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive foliage.
