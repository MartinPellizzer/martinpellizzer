Phrynium densum

Phrynium densum, commonly known as the dense phrynium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces striking yellow or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or near water sources.

Phrynium densum is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
