Pothuava pectinata

Pothuava pectinata, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow and green foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Pothuava pectinata thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
