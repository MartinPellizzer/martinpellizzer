Petraeovitex pubescens

Petraeovitex pubescens, commonly known as the rock vitex, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in rocky or arid environments. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a hairy texture, and it produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Petraeovitex pubescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
