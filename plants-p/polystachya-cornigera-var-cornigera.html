Polystachya cornigera var. cornigera

Polystachya cornigera var. cornigera, commonly known as the horned polystachya, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and produces clusters of elongated, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant features strap-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
