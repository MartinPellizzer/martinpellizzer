Premna sterculiifolia var. bakhuizenii

Premna sterculiifolia var. bakhuizenii, commonly known as the Bakhuizen's premna, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are usually pubescent on the underside. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by elliptical, woody fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
