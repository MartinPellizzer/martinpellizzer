Plectranthus dichotomus

Plectranthus dichotomus, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Plectranthus dichotomus is popular as a ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and compact size.
