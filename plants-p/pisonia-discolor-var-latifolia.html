Pisonia discolor var. latifolia

Pisonia discolor var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf pisonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are broad and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that are highly fragrant and attract various pollinators. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release numerous hairy seeds.

This species is often found in coastal areas and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.
