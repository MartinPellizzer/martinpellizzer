Panicum trichanthum var. modestum

Panicum trichanthum var. modestum, commonly known as slender panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of North America.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds. The plant has slender, cylindrical stalks with small, spike-like inflorescences that produce minute, wind-dispersed seeds. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slight hairy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This grass is often found in wetland ecosystems and can play a role in stabilizing soil in such environments.
