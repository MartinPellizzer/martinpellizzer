Phaseolus vulgaris var. alboruber

Phaseolus vulgaris var. alboruber, commonly known as the scarlet runner bean, is a variety of the common bean plant.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and white striped pods and edible seeds. The plant is a climbing vine with compound leaves and produces flowers that are typically white or pale pink. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is popular in gardens and for culinary use due to its flavorful beans and ornamental appeal.
