Poterium sanguisorba var. microphyllum

Poterium sanguisorba var. microphyllum, commonly known as lesser dock, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features basal leaves that are typically heart-shaped with a waxy surface, and it produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

It has a fibrous root system and is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.
