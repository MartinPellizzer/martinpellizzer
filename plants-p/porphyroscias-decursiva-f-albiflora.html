Porphyroscias decursiva f. albiflora

Porphyroscias decursiva f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered porphyroscias, is a unique variant of the porphyroscias genus.

It is characterized by its striking white flowers, which contrast sharply with its dark green foliage. This plant is native to specific regions in South America, particularly in the Andean foothills. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid, tropical forests.

The foliage is typically leathery and has a velvety texture, adding to its ornamental appeal.
