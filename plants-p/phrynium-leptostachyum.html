Phrynium leptostachyum

Phrynium leptostachyum, commonly known as the narrow-stalked phrynium, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences that are surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Phrynium leptostachyum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
