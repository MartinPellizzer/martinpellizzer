Pilea sevillensis

Pilea sevillensis, commonly known as the Mexican evergreen or velvet leaf plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Urticaceae.

It is native to Central America and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage.

Pilea sevillensis is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
