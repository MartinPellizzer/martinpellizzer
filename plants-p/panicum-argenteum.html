Panicum argenteum

Panicum argenteum, commonly known as silver panicum, is a flowering plant native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial grass that thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands. The plant is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which results from dense covering of fine hairs, and its delicate, fluffy seed heads that appear in late summer. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.

Panicum argenteum is also known for its drought tolerance and ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.
