Primula xanthobasis

Primula xanthobasis, commonly known as the yellow primrose, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features attractive, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a distinctive yellow throat. The plant typically grows to a height of 10-30 cm, with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial to full shade.

The flowers bloom in early spring, adding a splash of color to woodland gardens.
