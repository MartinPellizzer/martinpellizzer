Pittosporum ramiflorum var. carnosum

Pittosporum ramiflorum var. carnosum, commonly known as the woolly pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are typically dark green and covered in a fine, white pubescence. It produces fragrant yellow flowers in late spring to early summer, which are followed by small, edible berries.

This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.
