Perilla frutescens var. frutescens

Perilla frutescens var. frutescens, commonly known as shiso, is an annual herb native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that range from green to purple, depending on the variety. The plant produces small, pale purple or white flowers arranged in clusters, and its seeds are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Perilla is also valued for its aromatic leaves, which are used in culinary and medicinal applications.
