Peperomia longicaulis var. heteromorpha

Peperomia longicaulis var. heteromorpha, commonly known as the Variegated Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features upright, branching stems with small, ovate to lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white, yellow, or cream markings. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically green or red, which are followed by small, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a versatile and ornamental plant for both indoor and outdoor spaces.
