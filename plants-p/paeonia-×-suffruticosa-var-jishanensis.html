Paeonia × suffruticosa var. jishanensis

Paeonia × suffruticosa var. jishanensis, commonly known as the Jishan peony, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces large, showy flowers in a range of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant has compound, pinnate leaves that are dark green and glossy. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.

This variety is particularly noted for its robust growth and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.
