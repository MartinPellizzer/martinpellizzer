Panicum hermaphroditum

Panicum hermaphroditum, commonly known as the little bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its blue-green foliage and distinctive silver-gray seed heads that appear in the fall. The plant is monoecious, meaning it has both male and female flowers on the same individual. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in prairies and open woodlands.

Panicum hermaphroditum plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
