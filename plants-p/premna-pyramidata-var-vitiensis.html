Premna pyramidata var. vitiensis

Premna pyramidata var. vitiensis, commonly known as the Vitiensis Premna, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific islands, particularly Fiji.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal or lowland areas. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. Its flowers are small, clustered, and typically white or pale green, appearing in axillary or terminal clusters.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic qualities.
