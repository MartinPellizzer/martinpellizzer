Pimelea curviflora var. pedunculata

Pimelea curviflora var. pedunculata, commonly known as the slender leaf candlebush, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, often pendulous stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in late summer to autumn. The leaves are typically narrow and slightly curved, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal heathlands and open forests.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
