Piper treleaseanum

Piper treleaseanum, commonly known as Trelease's pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with opposite, ovate leaves that are often pubescent. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are clustered in spikes, and it produces small, berry-like fruits that are edible.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
