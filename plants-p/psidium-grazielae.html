Psidium grazielae

Psidium grazielae, commonly known as the Graham's guava, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, yellowish berry with a sweet and tangy flavor.
