Pelargonium × boscaweniae

Pelargonium × boscaweniae, commonly known as the Boscawenia geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its attractive, often fragrant flowers that come in a range of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant typically has rounded, lobed leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It is a compact shrub that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated for its visual appeal and ease of care.
