Peperomia aggregata

Peperomia aggregata, commonly known as the clustered peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features rosettes of fleshy, waxy leaves that are typically green with red or purple undersides. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia aggregata is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
