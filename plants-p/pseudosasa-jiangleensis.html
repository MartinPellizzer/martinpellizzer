Pseudosasa jiangleensis

Pseudosasa jiangleensis, commonly known as the Jiangle bamboo, is a species of grass-like plant native to China.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically green and smooth, with a fibrous structure typical of bamboo species. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
