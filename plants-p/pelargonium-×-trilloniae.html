Pelargonium × trilloniae

Pelargonium × trilloniae, commonly known as the trillium geranium, is a hybrid species in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a distinctive yellow or orange center. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, featuring succulent, fleshy leaves that are often lobed or serrated. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated for its vibrant blooms and ease of care.
