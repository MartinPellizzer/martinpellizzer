Pisum sativum var. humillimum

Pisum sativum var. humillimum, commonly known as dwarf garden pea, is a compact variety of the pea plant.

It is characterized by its short stature, typically reaching heights of 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces small, edible pods that contain round, green seeds. It thrives in cool climates and is often cultivated for its culinary and ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it an ideal choice for container gardening and space-limited growing environments.
