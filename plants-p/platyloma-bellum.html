Platyloma bellum

Platyloma bellum, commonly known as the bell-shaped flat-leaf plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in clusters that resemble small bells.

Platyloma bellum is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
