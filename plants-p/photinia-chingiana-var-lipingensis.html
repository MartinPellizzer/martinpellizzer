Photinia chingiana var. lipingensis

Photinia chingiana var. lipingensis, commonly known as the Liping photinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that turn reddish in the fall. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, which are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its vibrant seasonal color changes.
