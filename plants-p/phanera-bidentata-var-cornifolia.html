Phanera bidentata var. cornifolia

Phanera bidentata var. cornifolia, commonly known as the horned phanera, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, sharply lobed leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.
