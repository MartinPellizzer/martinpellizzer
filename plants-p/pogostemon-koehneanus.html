Pogostemon koehneanus

Pogostemon koehneanus, commonly known as the broadleaf pogostemon, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and distinctive square stems, typical of the Lamiaceae family. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually purple or pink in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its leaves are used in traditional medicine and as a culinary herb in some regions.
