Pothos borneensis

Pothos borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean devil's ivy, is a tropical climbing plant native to Borneo.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically bright green with a slightly variegated pattern. The plant produces small, pale greenish-white flowers that are not particularly showy. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening in suitable conditions.
