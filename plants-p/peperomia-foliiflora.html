Peperomia foliiflora

Peperomia foliiflora, commonly known as the silver lace peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with a distinctive silver-gray veining that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia foliiflora is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
