Parinari coccinea

Parinari coccinea, commonly known as the red parinari, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

It is characterized by its smooth, reddish-brown bark and elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy berries, which are an important food source for various birds and mammals.

Parinari coccinea is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional uses.
