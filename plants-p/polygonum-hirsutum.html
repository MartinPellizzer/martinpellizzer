Polygonum hirsutum

Polygonum hirsutum, commonly known as the woolly knotweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy, often reddish stems. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

Polygonum hirsutum is often found in moist, disturbed soils along streambanks and roadsides.
