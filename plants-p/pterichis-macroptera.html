Pterichis macroptera

Pterichis macroptera, commonly known as the large-winged pterichis, is a member of the Malvaceae family.

It is a shrub native to the Caribbean, particularly found in the Greater Antilles. The plant is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves and showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds, aiding in its dispersal.

The plant is often grown as an ornamental due to its striking foliage and floral display.
