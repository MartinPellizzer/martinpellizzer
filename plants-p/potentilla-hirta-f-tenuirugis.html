Potentilla hirta f. tenuirugis

Potentilla hirta f. tenuirugis, commonly known as the slender-hairy cinquefoil, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing, herbaceous habit with finely hairy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically ovate to elliptic in shape, with a soft, hairy texture.

This variety is often found in rocky or gravelly soils in open, sunny habitats.
