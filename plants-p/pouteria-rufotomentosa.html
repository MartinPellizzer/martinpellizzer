Pouteria rufotomentosa

Pouteria rufotomentosa, commonly known as the rough-hairy pouteria, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its rough, hairy bark and elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by edible, orange-red berries. These berries are often used in traditional medicine and as a source of edible oil.
