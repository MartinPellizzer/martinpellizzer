Phanera kockiana var. beccarii

Phanera kockiana var. beccarii, commonly known as the Bornean flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, red-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, making it a striking ornamental plant. The leaves are simple, alternate, and elliptical, with a smooth surface and a glossy appearance. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The plant is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes in tropical gardens.
