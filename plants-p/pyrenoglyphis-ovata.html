Pyrenoglyphis ovata

Pyrenoglyphis ovata, commonly known as the oval-spored grass.

It is a species of grass found in tropical and subtropical regions, characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow leaves. The plant produces small, oval-shaped spores that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its propagation. Its botanical structure includes a fibrous root system and a rhizomatous growth habit, which helps it thrive in diverse soil conditions.

Pyrenoglyphis ovata plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms.
