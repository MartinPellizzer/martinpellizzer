Parosela microphylla var. brevis

Parosela microphylla var. brevis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved parosela, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features small, narrow leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
