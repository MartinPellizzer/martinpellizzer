Pentas quartiniana

Pentas quartiniana, commonly known as the flame flower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

The flowers are known to attract bees and other pollinators, contributing to its ecological significance.
