Podocarpus matudae var. jaliscanus

Podocarpus matudae var. jaliscanus, commonly known as the Jalisco podocarp, is a member of the Podocarpaceae family and is native to Mexico.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows in humid, tropical climates. The plant has scale-like leaves that are dense and provide shade, and its cones are small and woody. It is often found in cloud forests and is adapted to high-altitude environments.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
