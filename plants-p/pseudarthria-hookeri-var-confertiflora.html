Pseudarthria hookeri var. confertiflora

Pseudarthria hookeri var. confertiflora, commonly known as the denseflowered pseudarthria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its typical legume structure. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are often pink or purple in color. It thrives in grassland environments and is adapted to seasonal rainfall patterns.
