Potentilla chinensis var. pseudochinensis

Potentilla chinensis var. pseudochinensis, commonly known as the Chinese cinquefoil, is a perennial shrub native to China.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has woody stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
