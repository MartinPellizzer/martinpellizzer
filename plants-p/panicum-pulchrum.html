Panicum pulchrum

Panicum pulchrum, commonly known as the beautiful panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and feathery panicles. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically green or pale yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for ornamental landscaping.

Panicum pulchrum is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to provide habitat for wildlife.
