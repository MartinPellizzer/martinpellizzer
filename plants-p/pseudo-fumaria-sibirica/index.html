Pseudo-Fumaria Sibirica

Pseudo-fumaria sibirica is a rare and intriguing plant native to the cold regions of Siberia. It belongs to the fumariaceae family, known for its unique and often striking floral structures. Despite its name, it is not closely related to the common fumaria species but shares some morphological similarities. This plant thrives in harsh environments, showcasing remarkable adaptability to extreme climates. Its study offers valuable insights into plant survival strategies in arctic and subarctic ecosystems.
