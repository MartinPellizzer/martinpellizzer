Pleopeltis irioides

Pleopeltis irioides, commonly known as the Irioid fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a soft, velvety texture.

Pleopeltis irioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
