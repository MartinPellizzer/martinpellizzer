Panicum wiliwilinuiense

Panicum wiliwilinuiense, commonly known as the Wiliwilinui grass, is a species native to the tropical regions of the Pacific Islands.

It is a perennial grass characterized by its tall, slender stalks and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and lowland forests. Its leaves are narrow and blade-like, with a slightly hairy surface.

This grass plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for local wildlife.
