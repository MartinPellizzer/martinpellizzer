Polypodium kaniense

Polypodium kaniense, commonly known as the Kaniense water fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, frond-like structures. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. Its rhizome is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The fronds are pinnately divided and can reach lengths of up to 30 centimeters.
