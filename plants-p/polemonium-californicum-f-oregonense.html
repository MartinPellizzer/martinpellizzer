Polemonium californicum f. oregonense

Polemonium californicum f. oregonense, commonly known as the Oregon polemonium, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue to purple, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and arranged alternately along the stem.

This variety is adapted to moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.
