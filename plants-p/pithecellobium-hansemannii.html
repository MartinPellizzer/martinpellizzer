Pithecellobium hansemannii

Pithecellobium hansemannii, commonly known as the yellow clusterpod, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, edible pods that contain several seeds. It is often grown for its ornamental value and has potential use in traditional medicine.

The species is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial in ecological restoration projects.
