Panicum capillare var. stramineum

Panicum capillare var. stramineum, commonly known as straw panicum, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a member of the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit. The plant produces fluffy, light green to golden yellow inflorescences that are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its delicate appearance and seasonal bloom make it a distinctive addition to native plant landscapes.
