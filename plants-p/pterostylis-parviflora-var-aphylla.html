Pterostylis parviflora var. aphylla

Pterostylis parviflora var. aphylla, commonly known as the small-flowered greenhood, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It features a slender, leafless stem that produces a single, erect flower with a distinctive green hood-like labellum. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellow, with a narrow, spurred lip.

This orchid is part of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique floral morphology and specialized pollination mechanisms.
