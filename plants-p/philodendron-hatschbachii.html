Philodendron hatschbachii

Philodendron hatschbachii, commonly known as the Hatschbachii philodendron, is a tropical vine native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are often marked with a distinctive white or silver band along the midrib. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by bright red berries. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This species is popular among plant enthusiasts for its unique foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
