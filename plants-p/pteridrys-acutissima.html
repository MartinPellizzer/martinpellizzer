Pteridrys acutissima

Pteridrys acutissima, commonly known as the sharp-pointed fern, is a species of fern characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping stems that produce new fronds along their length. Pteridrys acutissima thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
