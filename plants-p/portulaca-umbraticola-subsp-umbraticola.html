Portulaca umbraticola subsp. umbraticola

Portulaca umbraticola subsp.

umbraticola, commonly known as the shade portulaca, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States. It is a low-growing annual that produces vibrant orange or red flowers.

The plant has fleshy, succulent leaves and stems, which help it store water in arid conditions. It thrives in partial to full shade and is often found in woodland or shaded garden settings.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to moist, shaded environments.
