Pyrostria variistipula

Pyrostria variistipula, commonly known as the variable-veined pine.

It is a member of the Podocarpaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its slender, evergreen foliage and conical shape. Its leaves are typically narrow and have a distinctive variation in venation patterns.

It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland forests.
