Piper pallescens

Piper pallescens, commonly known as the pale pepper plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. It produces tiny, greenish-white flowers that develop into small, three-lobed berries.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
