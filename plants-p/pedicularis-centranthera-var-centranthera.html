Pedicularis centranthera var. centranthera

Pedicularis centranthera var. centranthera, commonly known as the yellow monkey orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in alpine or subalpine habitats. The plant produces showy, spurred flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are alternate and lance-shaped, often covered in a fine layer of hairs.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its distinctive floral display.
