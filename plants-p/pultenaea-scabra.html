Pultenaea scabra

Pultenaea scabra, commonly known as the rough pultenaea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, often found in heathlands and dry forests. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine, rough hairs, giving it a distinctive texture.

Pultenaea scabra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
