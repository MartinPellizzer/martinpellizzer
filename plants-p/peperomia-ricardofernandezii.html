Peperomia ricardofernandezii

Peperomia ricardofernandezii, commonly known as the Ricardo Fernandez peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a velvety texture and small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has a short, thick rhizome and produces aerial roots, which help it anchor to trees in its natural habitat. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
