Paphiopedilum insigne var. gracile

Paphiopedilum insigne var. gracile, commonly known as the slender lady's slipper orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, hairy rhizome and produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch. The plant has broad, strap-like leaves and thrives in shaded, moist environments. It is prized for its elegant appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

The species is relatively easy to grow, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
