Peperomia arechavaletae

Peperomia arechavaletae, commonly known as the Arechavaleta's peperomia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, specifically Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit, fleshy leaves, and small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.
