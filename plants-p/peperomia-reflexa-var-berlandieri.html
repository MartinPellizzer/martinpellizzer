Peperomia reflexa var. berlandieri

Peperomia reflexa var. berlandieri, commonly known as the Mexican peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Mexico.

It features glossy, elliptical leaves that are often dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and white, which are typically borne on short, erect spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

This variety is prized for its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
