Peperomia ciliata

Peperomia ciliata, commonly known as the velvetleaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive reddish or purple underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia ciliata is relatively low maintenance and resistant to pests, contributing to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
