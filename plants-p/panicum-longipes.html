Panicum longipes

Panicum longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and loose, feathery panicles that give it a delicate appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture and a slight blue-green hue.

Panicum longipes is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
