Pittosporum richii

Pittosporum richii, commonly known as the New Zealand Pittosporum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that turn red or orange in autumn, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in spring, which are followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Pittosporum richii is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often used in horticulture for its seasonal color changes.
