Peperomia surinamensis

Peperomia surinamensis, commonly known as the Surinam peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, often with a distinctive reddish or purple hue. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white, followed by decorative, spathaceous bracts. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia surinamensis is known for its ability to tolerate low light conditions and requires minimal water, making it relatively easy to care for.
