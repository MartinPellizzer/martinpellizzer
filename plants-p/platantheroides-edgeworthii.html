Platantheroides edgeworthii

Platantheroides edgeworthii, commonly known as the Edgeworth's lady's slipper, is a rare and endangered orchid species native to specific regions of the United States.

It is characterized by its distinctive slipper-shaped flower, which is typically pink or white in color. The plant has a short, rhizomatous stem and produces a single, showy flower per stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and requires specific soil conditions for successful growth.

Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it is highly susceptible to population decline.
