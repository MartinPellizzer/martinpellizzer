Pittosporum nubicola

Pittosporum nubicola, commonly known as the African pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, dark blue berries that are edible for some bird species. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Pittosporum nubicola is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand coastal conditions.
