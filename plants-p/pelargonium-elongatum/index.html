Pelargonium Elongatum

Elongated geranium, scientifically known as Pelargonium elongatum, is a strikingly unique member of the geranium family native to southern Africa. Its most distinctive feature is its elongated, trailing stems that can reach impressive lengths, making it a favorite among hanging baskets and cascading displays. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink and red, adding a vibrant touch to any garden or indoor space. Despite its delicate appearance, Pelargonium elongatum is surprisingly hardy and adaptable, thriving in a range of growing conditions. Whether grown for its ornamental beauty or its resilience, this plant is a captivating addition to any collection.
