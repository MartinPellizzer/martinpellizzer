Piper triseriale

Piper triseriale, commonly known as the three-lobed pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three leaflets, and its small, greenish-white flowers. It produces small, round berries that are typically used in traditional medicine.

The plant is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in various herbal remedies.
