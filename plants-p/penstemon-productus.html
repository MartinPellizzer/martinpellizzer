Penstemon productus

Penstemon productus, commonly known as the Sierra penstemon, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and vibrant blue to violet tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or open habitats, preferring full sun and well-drained soils. It is a perennial species that produces showy blooms from late spring to early summer.

Penstemon productus is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and hummingbirds.
