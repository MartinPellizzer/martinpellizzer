Peperomia mapulehuana

Peperomia mapulehuana, commonly known as the Mapulehuana peperomia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a compact, low-growing plant with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green or variegated. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale pink, clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and ease of care have made it a favored plant among succulent enthusiasts.
