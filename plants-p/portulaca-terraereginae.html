Portulaca terraereginae

Portulaca terraereginae, commonly known as the royal purslane, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing annual that produces vibrant red or yellow flowers, which bloom throughout the summer months. The plant has fleshy, succulent leaves and stems, adapted to thrive in arid environments. It is often found in rocky soils and along coastal areas.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
