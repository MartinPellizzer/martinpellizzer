Peucedanum chabraei var. autumnale

Peucedanum chabraei var. autumnale, commonly known as autumn angelica, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbel-shaped flowers.
