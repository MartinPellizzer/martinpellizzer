Philodendron liesneri

Philodendron liesneri, commonly known as the Shiny Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy, dark green leaves that are typically larger than those of other philodendron species. The plant has a distinctive, slender stem with aerial roots that help it climb towards light. Its foliage thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Philodendron liesneri is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
