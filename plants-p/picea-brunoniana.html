Picea brunoniana

Picea brunoniana, commonly known as the Brunon spruce, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, pyramidal shape and dark green, needle-like leaves that are short and blunt. The cones of Picea brunoniana are typically small and woody, containing two to three seeds. This species thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found in high-altitude forests.

Its botanical features make it an important component of its native ecosystems.
