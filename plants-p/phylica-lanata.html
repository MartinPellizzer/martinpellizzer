Phylica lanata

Phylica lanata, commonly known as the silver leaf phylica, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, woolly foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are covered in fine, soft hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Phylica lanata is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
