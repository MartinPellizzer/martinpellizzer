Pelea tietaensis

Pelea tietaensis, commonly known as the Mexican pelea, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a composite head. It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.

Pelea tietaensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in local medicine.
