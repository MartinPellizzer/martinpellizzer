Pleurothallis breviflora

Pleurothallis breviflora, commonly known as the short-flowered pleurothallis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America. The plant typically grows on trees, anchoring itself with its roots and absorbing moisture from the air. Its flowers are usually small, white or pale green, and bloom during the wet season.

It is valued by orchid enthusiasts for its delicate appearance and unique growth habits.
