Persoonia terminalis subsp. terminalis

Persoonia terminalis subsp.

terminalis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf waratah, is a shrub native to eastern Australia. It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and striking, terminal flower heads that range in color from white to pink.

The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
