Pelargonium × megaleion

Pelargonium × megaleion, commonly known as the giant geranium, is a hybrid plant within the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red, with prominent white markings. The plant produces fleshy, lobed leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Pelargonium × megaleion is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and container gardening.
