Pyrethrum tatsienense

Pyrethrum tatsienense, commonly known as Chinese pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant produces fine, hair-like structures called trichomes, which contain pyrethrin, a natural insecticide. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pest control properties.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both horticulture and organic agriculture.
