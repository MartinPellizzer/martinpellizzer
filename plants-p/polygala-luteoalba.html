Polygala luteoalba

Polygala luteoalba, commonly known as the yellow milkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, particularly China.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grassy areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
