Platystele orectoglossa

Platystele orectoglossa, commonly known as the narrow-lipped platystele, is a species of orchid belonging to the genus Platystele in the family Orchidaceae.

It is a terrestrial epiphytic plant characterized by its small, tubular leaves and a single, slender stem. Native to the cloud forests of Central and South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, white to pale green flowers with a unique, narrow lip.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
