Phylica excelsa var. excelsa

Phylica excelsa var. excelsa, commonly known as the silver tree, is a distinctive shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name, and its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, red, urn-shaped flowers that are highly attractive to birds and insects. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and ecological value make it a popular choice for ornamental and conservation purposes.
