Peperomia molleri subsp. molleri

Peperomia molleri subsp.

molleri, commonly known as the velvet plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America. It features thick, waxy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its dense foliage and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
