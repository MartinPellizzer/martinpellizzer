Phaeiris × Violivenosa

Phaeiris × violivenosa is a rare and striking hybrid plant known for its vibrant purple and violet hues. It is a cross between two parent species, combining unique traits from both to create a visually appealing bloom. This plant is often admired for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections. Its delicate petals and subtle fragrance make it a favorite among plant enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure and is rarely found in the wild.
