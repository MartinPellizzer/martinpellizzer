Potentilla effusa var. argyrea

Potentilla effusa var. argyrea, commonly known as silver potentilla, is a low-growing shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which results from dense covering of fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.
