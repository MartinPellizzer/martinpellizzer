Peperomia penicillata var. magnifolia

Peperomia penicillata var. magnifolia, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that emerge from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
