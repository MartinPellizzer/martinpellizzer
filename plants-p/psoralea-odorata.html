Psoralea odorata

Psoralea odorata, commonly known as sweet psoralea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, and it is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Psoralea odorata is also noted for its aromatic fragrance, which contributes to its common name.
