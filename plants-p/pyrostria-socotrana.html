Pyrostria socotrana

Pyrostria socotrana, commonly known as Socotra cypress, is a unique evergreen conifer native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its distinctive, umbrella-shaped crown and twisted, gnarled branches. The plant has scale-like leaves that are dark green and closely arranged along the stems. Its cones are small and woody, containing seeds that are dispersed by wind.

This species is adapted to the arid and rocky environment of its native habitat.
