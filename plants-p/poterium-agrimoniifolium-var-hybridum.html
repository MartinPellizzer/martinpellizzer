Poterium agrimoniifolium var. hybridum

Poterium agrimoniifolium var. hybridum, commonly known as the hybrid agrimony.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its upright growth habit and serrated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in meadows and grasslands.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for both ecological and horticultural purposes.
