Petersia viridiflora

Petersia viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered petersia, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, often found in the Western and Eastern Cape provinces of South Africa. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green in color and have a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and appear in clusters during the late spring to early summer.

Petersia viridiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
