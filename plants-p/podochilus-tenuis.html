Podochilus tenuis

Podochilus tenuis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and linear leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its delicate appearance and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
