Peperomia quirosii

Peperomia quirosii, commonly known as the Quito peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to the Andean regions of Ecuador.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture, and it produces delicate, spadix-like inflorescences. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation. It is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and moderate humidity.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for collectors and indoor gardeners alike.
