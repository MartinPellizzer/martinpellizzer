Polygala bawanglingensis

Polygala bawanglingensis, commonly known as the Bawangling milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant has slender stems and compound leaves, with each leaflet being oval-shaped and slightly hairy. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
