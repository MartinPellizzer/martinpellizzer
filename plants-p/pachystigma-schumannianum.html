Pachystigma schumannianum

Pachystigma schumannianum, commonly known as the Schumann's pachystigma, is a unique succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and small, clustered leaves that help it store water in arid environments. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white, typically during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often found in rocky or sandy regions with full sun exposure.
