Panicum gouinii

Panicum gouinii, commonly known as the Gouin's panicgrass, is a species of grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and savannas.

Panicum gouinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
