Pilasia filifolia

Pilasia filifolia, commonly known as the threadleaf pilasia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, thread-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.
