Prameles

Prameles, commonly known as wild strawberries, are small, herbaceous plants native to temperate regions.

They have trifoliate leaves composed of three heart-shaped leaflets. The flowers are white with five petals and are followed by bright red, fleshy fruits. The fruit contains small seeds and is often used in culinary applications.

Prameles are perennial plants that thrive in well-drained soil and full sun.
