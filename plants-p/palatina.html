Palatina

Palatina, commonly known as the Palatinate or Palatinate rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is native to Europe and has been cultivated for centuries for its ornamental value. Palatina typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, oval-shaped leaves.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice in gardens and landscapes.
