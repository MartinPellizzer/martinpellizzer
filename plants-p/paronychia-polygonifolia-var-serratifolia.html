Paronychia polygonifolia var. serratifolia

Paronychia polygonifolia var. serratifolia, commonly known as the serrated leaf stonecrop, is a low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of fleshy, narrow leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, especially in full sun. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, thriving in well-drained soil and full sunlight.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
