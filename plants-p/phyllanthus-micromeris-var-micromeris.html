Phyllanthus micromeris var. micromeris

Phyllanthus micromeris var. micromeris, commonly known as the small-leaved sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa, the Middle East, and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its small, fleshy leaves and yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a succulent stem and is adapted to survive in dry conditions, storing water in its leaves and stems. It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.
