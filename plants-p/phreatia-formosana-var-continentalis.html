Phreatia formosana var. continentalis

Phreatia formosana var. continentalis, commonly known as the continental form of the Formosan phreatia, is a rare orchid species native to Taiwan.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded forest understories. The plant features slender, dark green leaves and produces delicate, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. It is characterized by its unique inflorescence structure and is adapted to thrive in humid, tropical climates.
