Phragmipedium guianense

Phragmipedium guianense, commonly known as the Guianan phragmipedium, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and green. The plant prefers humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, with a waxy surface that helps retain moisture.

Phragmipedium guianense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
