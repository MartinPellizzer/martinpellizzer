Pholidota rupestris

Pholidota rupestris, commonly known as the rock fig, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting to arid environments. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are often hidden among its foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
