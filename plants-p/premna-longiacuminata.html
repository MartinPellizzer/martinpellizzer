Premna longiacuminata

Premna longiacuminata, commonly known as the long-fruited premna, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Verbenaceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale green, followed by elongated, fleshy fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.
