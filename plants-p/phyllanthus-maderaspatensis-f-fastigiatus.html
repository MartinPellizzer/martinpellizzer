Phyllanthus maderaspatensis f. fastigiatus

Phyllanthus maderaspatensis f. fastigiatus, commonly known as the upright phyllanthus, is a compact shrub characterized by its dense, upright growth habit.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green, arranged in a compact, bushy form. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
