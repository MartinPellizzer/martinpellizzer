Pseudocatalpa caudiculata

Pseudocatalpa caudiculata, commonly known as the slender catalpa, is a deciduous tree native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Bignoniaceae family and is characterized by its elongated, slender trunk and opposite, heart-shaped leaves.
