Perymenium ovalifolium

Perymenium ovalifolium, commonly known as the oval-leaf perymenium, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its oval-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along its stem. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Its botanical significance lies in its role within its native ecosystem and its potential for medicinal use.
