Psychotria rubiginosa var. jacobsii

Psychotria rubiginosa var. jacobsii, commonly known as the red-eyed beauty or Jacob's psychotria, is a species of flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that contrast with its dark green foliage. It typically grows as an epiphytic or terrestrial plant, often found in humid, shaded environments.

The leaves are usually elliptical or ovate, with a glossy surface and prominent veins.
