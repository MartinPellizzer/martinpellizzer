Peperomia pacifica

Peperomia pacifica, commonly known as the Pacific Peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically green with a slight variegation, and it produces small, inconspicuous white or green flowers. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often used as a decorative houseplant due to its compact size and attractive foliage.

Peperomia pacifica is relatively drought-tolerant, making it suitable for beginners in indoor gardening.
