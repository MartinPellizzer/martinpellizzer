Panicum heterocraspedum

Panicum heterocraspedum, commonly known as the narrow-awn panicgrass, is a species of grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and its distinctive, narrow awns on the spikelets. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its leaves are linear and covered in fine hairs, while the inflorescence consists of spikelets arranged in a panicle.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
