Phoebe gamblei

Phoebe gamblei, commonly known as the Gambier phoebe, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocados. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical in shape with a glossy surface. It produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, followed by dark blue to black berries.

Phoebe gamblei is often found in moist, shaded areas such as swamps and woodlands.
