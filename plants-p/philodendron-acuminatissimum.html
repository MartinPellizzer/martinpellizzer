Philodendron acuminatissimum

Philodendron acuminatissimum, commonly known as the sharp-pointed philodendron, is a climbing tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers, which are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate to high humidity.

Its striking foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a houseplant.
