Pentas bussei f. brevituba

Pentas bussei f. brevituba, commonly known as the dwarf pentas, is a compact shrub native to South Africa.

It features showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink, depending on the variety. The plant has a dense, bushy growth habit with small, dark green leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a container plant.
