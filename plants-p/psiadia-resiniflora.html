Psiadia resiniflora

Psiadia resiniflora, commonly known as the resinous psiadia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

Psiadia resiniflora is often found in arid and semi-arid regions, adapting to harsh climates through its drought-resistant features.
