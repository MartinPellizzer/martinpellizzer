Peperomia hahnii

Peperomia hahnii, commonly known as the Watermelon Plant, is a compact, trailing evergreen perennial native to South America.

It features small, fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or pink, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tiny, pale green flowers that are typically hidden beneath its foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia hahnii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental appeal and ease of care.
