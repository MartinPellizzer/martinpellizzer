Pennisetum pachycladum

Pennisetum pachycladum, commonly known as the silver fountain grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender culms that are adorned with feathery, silver-white inflorescences, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are usually gray-green in color, contributing to its distinctive visual appeal. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Pennisetum pachycladum is known for its drought tolerance and ability to add texture and movement to garden designs.
