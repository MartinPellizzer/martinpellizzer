Potentilla chinensis var. divaricata

Potentilla chinensis var. divaricata, commonly known as the Chinese rose or evergreen cinquefoil, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features compound leaves with five oval-shaped leaflets, and its flowers are typically pink or red, arranged in clusters. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This species is also valued for its hardiness and ability to tolerate various environmental conditions.
