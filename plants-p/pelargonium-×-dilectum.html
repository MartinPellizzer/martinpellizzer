Pelargonium × dilectum

Pelargonium × dilectum, commonly known as the Royal Pelargonium, is a hybrid geranium species characterized by its showy, double flowers and vibrant colors.

It is a popular ornamental plant, prized for its lush foliage and long-lasting blooms. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 feet, with serrated, dark green leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Pelargonium × dilectum is often cultivated in gardens and containers, making it a versatile choice for both indoor and outdoor settings.
