Palanostigma abutiloides

Palanostigma abutiloides, commonly known as the African fanflower, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its palmately lobed leaves and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, dark seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.
