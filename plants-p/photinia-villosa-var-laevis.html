Photinia villosa var. laevis

Photinia villosa var. laevis, commonly known as the smooth photinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and clusters of small, white flowers that appear in spring. The plant produces bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in temperate climates.
