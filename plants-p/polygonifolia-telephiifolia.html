Polygonifolia telephiifolia

Polygonifolia telephiifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The leaves are compound, with multiple small, lance-shaped leaflets. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in umbels.

The plant is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and hardiness.
