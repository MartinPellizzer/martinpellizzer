Pilosella plicatula

Pilosella plicatula, commonly known as the wrinkled cat's-paw, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and western Asia, often found in meadows and rocky areas. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are pinnately divided and have a distinctive wrinkled texture.
