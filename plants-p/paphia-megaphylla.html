Paphia megaphylla

Paphia megaphylla, commonly known as the giant paphia, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves and striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often near water sources. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white with prominent red or orange sepals.

Paphia megaphylla is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
