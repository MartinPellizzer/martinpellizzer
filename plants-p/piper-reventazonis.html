Piper reventazonis

Piper reventazonis, commonly known as the Cuyabeno pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the rainforests of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, pendant flowers and heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, peppery fruits that are used in traditional medicine and local cuisines.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
