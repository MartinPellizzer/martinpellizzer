Pteridrys zeylanica

Pteridrys zeylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon fern, is a species of fern native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its rhizome is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The Ceylon fern is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its cultural significance in the region.
