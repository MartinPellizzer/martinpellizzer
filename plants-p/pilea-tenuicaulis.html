Pilea tenuicaulis

Pilea tenuicaulis, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rosettes of thick, waxy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Pilea tenuicaulis is often grown as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
