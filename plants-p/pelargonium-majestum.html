Pelargonium majestum

Pelargonium majestum, commonly known as the Royal Geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant features palmate leaves with lobed margins, often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Pelargonium majestum is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
