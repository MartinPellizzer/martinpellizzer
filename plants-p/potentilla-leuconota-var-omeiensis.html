Potentilla leuconota var. omeiensis

Potentilla leuconota var. omeiensis, commonly known as the Omei potentilla, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a slightly serrated edge.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its compact size and vibrant blooms.
