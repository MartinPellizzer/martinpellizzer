Premna peii

Premna peii, commonly known as the Chinese nettle or Chinese premna, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Verbenaceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in panicles, and it is often found in moist, shaded areas. Its leaves are sometimes used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant's rapid growth and adaptability make it a common species in its native habitat.
