Plectranthus decimus

Plectranthus decimus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf plectranthus, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale purple to white in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Plectranthus decimus is also valued for its aromatic foliage and is often used in traditional medicine.
