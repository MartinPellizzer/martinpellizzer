Parodia piltziorum

Parodia piltziorum, commonly known as the Piltziorum cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of South America.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white, radial spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are typically short-lived but striking in appearance. It has a shallow root system adapted to absorb moisture from infrequent rains.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for succulent collectors.
