Phyllanthus tenuicaulis subsp. haitiensis

Phyllanthus tenuicaulis subsp.

haitiensis, commonly known as Haitian narrowleaf phyllanthus, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family. It is a small shrub characterized by its simple, opposite, and often hairy leaves.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow-green in color. Its fruits are capsules that contain small, smooth seeds.

This subspecies is native to Haiti and is adapted to tropical and subtropical environments.
