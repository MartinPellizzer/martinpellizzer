Pilea hilliana var. corniculata

Pilea hilliana var. corniculata, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and ease of care contribute to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
