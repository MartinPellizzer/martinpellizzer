Potentilla concinna var. bicrenata

Potentilla concinna var. bicrenata, commonly known as the bicrenate potentilla, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, particularly China.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its small, rounded leaves and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade conditions. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant in gardens.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
