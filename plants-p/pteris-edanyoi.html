Pteris edanyoi

Pteris edanyoi, commonly known as the Edanyo water fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along streams. Its leaves are bipinnate, with small, finger-like segments that give it a lacy appearance.

Pteris edanyoi is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
