Polybotrya plumbicaulis

Polybotrya plumbicaulis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, pendulous flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with a prominent lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some horticultural collections.
