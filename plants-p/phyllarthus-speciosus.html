Phyllarthus speciosus

Phyllarthus speciosus, commonly known as the showy phyllarthus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

Phyllarthus speciosus is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value and ecological benefits.
