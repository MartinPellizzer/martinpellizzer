Pandanus guamensis

Pandanus guamensis, commonly known as the Guam pandanus, is a flowering plant native to the island of Guam.

It belongs to the family Pandanaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and broad, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in coastal areas and is often found in wetland or brackish environments.

The Guam pandanus plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
