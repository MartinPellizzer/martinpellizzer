Podocarpus bracteatus var. brevipes

Podocarpus bracteatus var. brevipes, commonly known as the dwarf podocarp or miniature Christmas tree, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a conical shape, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by woody, cone-like structures that contain its seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, exhibiting moderate drought tolerance.

Its slow growth rate and unique appearance make it a popular choice for bonsai and garden decoration.
