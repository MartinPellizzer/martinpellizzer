Potentilla × splendens

Potentilla × splendens, commonly known as the golden potentilla, is a hybrid shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in early summer. The plant typically reaches a height of 1 to 2 meters and is well-suited for rock gardens or as a border plant due to its compact form. Its foliage is usually dark green and remains attractive throughout the growing season.

The species is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
