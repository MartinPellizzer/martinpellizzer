Piper corylistachyum

Piper corylistachyum, commonly known as the African pepper or African long pepper, is a tropical climbing vine native to Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often clustered in spikes. Its fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that turns red when mature.

The plant is valued for its aromatic leaves and is used in traditional medicine and as a spice in some regions.
