Piper subsessilifolium var. palmanum

Piper subsessilifolium var. palmanum, commonly known as the Palmanum pepper plant, is a tropical species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, ovate leaves that are typically sessile or nearly sessile, giving it a compact appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, followed by the development of small, peppery fruits. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its aromatic leaves and medicinal properties.

The variety is noted for its unique foliage and potential use in traditional herbal remedies.
