Piper peltaphyllum var. lasvueltasanum

Piper peltaphyllum var. lasvueltasanum, commonly known as the Las Vueltasanum pepper plant, is a variant of the Piper peltaphyllum species native to specific regions in South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves and clusters of small, aromatic berries. The plant belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species known for their medicinal and culinary uses. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its unique botanical features make it a subject of interest in botanical research and horticulture.
