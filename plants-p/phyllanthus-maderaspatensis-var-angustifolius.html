Phyllanthus maderaspatensis var. angustifolius

Phyllanthus maderaspatensis var. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved phyllanthus, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features small, elliptical leaves with a smooth surface and a dark green color. The plant produces small yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, spherical fruits. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its foliage.
