Piptatherum vicarium

Piptatherum vicarium, commonly known as the slender false brome, is a perennial grass native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and grasslands. The plant has slender, erect stems with narrow leaves that are often hairy. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets that are loosely arranged along the stem.

It plays an important role in maintaining biodiversity in its native ecosystems.
