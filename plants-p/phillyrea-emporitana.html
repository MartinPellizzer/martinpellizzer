Phillyrea emporitana

Phillyrea emporitana, commonly known as the Mediterranean privet, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in clusters during the spring, which are followed by small, spherical berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it adaptable to coastal and rocky environments.

Phillyrea emporitana is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.
