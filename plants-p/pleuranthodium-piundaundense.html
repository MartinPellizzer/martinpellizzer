Pleuranthodium piundaundense

Pleuranthodium piundaundense, commonly known as the Piundaundense starling orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its unique floral structure and limited distribution make it a rare and ecologically significant species.

Conservation efforts are essential to protect this plant from habitat loss and environmental degradation.
