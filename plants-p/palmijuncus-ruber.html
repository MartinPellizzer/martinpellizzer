Palmijuncus ruber

Palmijuncus ruber, commonly known as the red palm fern, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its dense, feathery fronds that display a vibrant red hue.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges, and is native to regions with warm, humid climates. The plant features a short, stout rhizome from which the leaves emerge, creating a lush, compact appearance. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening due to its unique color and texture.

Palmijuncus ruber thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
