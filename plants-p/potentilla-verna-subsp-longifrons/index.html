Potentilla Verna Subsp. Longifrons

Potentilla verna subsp. longifrons is a lesser-known member of the rose family found in northern regions. It is characterized by its delicate yellow flowers and small, hairy leaves. This subspecies thrives in rocky and alpine environments, often growing in shaded areas. Its unique features make it an interesting subject for botanists and plant enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated in the broader horticultural community.
