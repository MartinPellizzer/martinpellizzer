Premna punicea

Premna punicea, commonly known as scarlet rubber vine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy on the underside. Its flowers are small, clustered in umbels, and typically have a pink or red hue.

Premna punicea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its latex, which has industrial applications.
