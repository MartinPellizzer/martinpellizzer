Piper sarcostylum

Piper sarcostylum, commonly known as the bitter pepper plant, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by dark blue to black berries. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a rough texture.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
