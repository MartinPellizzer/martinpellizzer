Pharbitis cathartica f. alba

Pharbitis cathartica f. alba, commonly known as white scarlet runner bean, is a variant of the scarlet runner bean characterized by its white flowers and pods.

It is a climbing vine native to the Americas, belonging to the Fabaceae family. The plant features compound leaves with three oval-shaped leaflets and produces long, cylindrical pods that contain edible seeds. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible beans.

The white flowers add a striking contrast to the green foliage, making it a popular choice for gardens.
