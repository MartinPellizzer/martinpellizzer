Plantago gnaphalioides var. aristata

Plantago gnaphalioides var. aristata, commonly known as the arrowleaf plantain, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves with a prominent central vein, giving it its characteristic arrowhead appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, which are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and roadside areas.

This species is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
