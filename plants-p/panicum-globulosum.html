Panicum globulosum

Panicum globulosum, commonly known as the globular panicgrass, is a grass species native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its compact, globular growth habit, with dense clusters of small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Panicum globulosum is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
