Pilea pumila var. deamii

Pilea pumila var. deamii, commonly known as the Chinese money plant or simply the dollar plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features small, round, fleshy leaves that are typically bright green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its compact growth and air-purifying qualities, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
