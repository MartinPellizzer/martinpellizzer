Pentacalia chaquiroensis

Pentacalia chaquiroensis, commonly known as the Chaquiroensis milkweed, is a flowering plant native to South America, specifically found in Ecuador.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many milkweed species known for their milky sap. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with five petals arranged symmetrically.
