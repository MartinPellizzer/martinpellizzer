Phalaenopsis speciosa var. imperatrix

Phalaenopsis speciosa var. imperatrix, commonly known as the Imperial Phalaenopsis, is a hybrid orchid prized for its large, showy flowers.

It originates from the Philippines and is characterized by its velvety, dark purple petals and a striking white lip. This variety is known for its long blooming period, often lasting several months. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and moderate humidity to thrive.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both collectors and home growers.
