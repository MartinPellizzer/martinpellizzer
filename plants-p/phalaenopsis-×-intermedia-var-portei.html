Phalaenopsis × intermedia var. portei

Phalaenopsis × intermedia var. portei, commonly known as the Portei Phalaenopsis, is a hybrid orchid prized for its elegant, long-lasting blooms.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia, where it thrives in tropical climates. The plant features broad, dark green leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. It is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to bloom repeatedly.

Its botanical characteristics make it a versatile and visually appealing ornamental plant.
