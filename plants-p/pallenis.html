Pallenis

Pallenis, commonly known as the ghost flower, is a rare and enigmatic plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes.

It belongs to the genus Pallenis, which is part of the nightshade family, Solanaceae. The plant is characterized by its delicate, translucent petals that appear to shimmer in the sunlight, giving it its ethereal appearance.
