Potentilla × cultorum

Potentilla × cultorum, commonly known as the shrub cinquefoil, is a hybrid plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, yellow, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and features dark green, oval-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
