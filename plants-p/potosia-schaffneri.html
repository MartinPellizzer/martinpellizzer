Potosia schaffneri

Potosia schaffneri, commonly known as the Schaffner's poinsettia, is a species of flowering plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking red bracts that surround small yellow flowers, which are actually the true flowers of the plant. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, also known as the spurge family, and is closely related to other poinsettia species. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
