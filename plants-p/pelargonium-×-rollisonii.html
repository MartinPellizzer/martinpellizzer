Pelargonium × rollisonii

Pelargonium × rollisonii, commonly known as the rainbow geranium, is a hybrid species within the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a range of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically has rounded, lobed leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It is a compact shrub that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.

Pelargonium × rollisonii is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and as a potted plant.
