Panicum planopteris

Panicum planopteris, commonly known as the flat-leaf panicgrass, is a species of grass native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its flat, blade-like leaves and slender, branching inflorescence. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It is often found in regions with a warm, temperate climate and well-drained soil.

Panicum planopteris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
