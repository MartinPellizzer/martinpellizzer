Peperomia productamenta

Peperomia productamenta, commonly known as the silver mitten plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features thick, fleshy, silver-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and attractive foliage, it is often used as a decorative houseplant.
