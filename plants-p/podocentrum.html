Podocentrum

Podocentrum, commonly known as the flame orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

These plants are native to tropical and subtropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of India, Indonesia, and the Philippines. Podocentrum species are characterized by their vibrant, showy flowers that often resemble flames, with bright red, orange, or yellow hues. They typically grow as epiphytes or terrestrial plants, often found in humid, shaded environments.

The genus includes over 100 species, many of which are cultivated for their ornamental value.
