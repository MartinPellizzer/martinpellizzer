Polygala youngii

Polygala youngii, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, often pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are usually small and opposite, while its stems are slender and branching.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
