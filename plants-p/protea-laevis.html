Protea laevis

Protea laevis, commonly known as the smooth protea, is a species native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family, which is known for its showy, often brightly colored flowers. The plant features a large, rounded flower head composed of numerous white to pinkish bracts that surround a central cluster of small, yellowish flowers. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is typically found in fynbos vegetation.

The smooth protea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
