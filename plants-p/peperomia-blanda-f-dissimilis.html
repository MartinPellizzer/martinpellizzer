Peperomia blanda f. dissimilis

Peperomia blanda f. dissimilis, commonly known as the variegated peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with striking white and yellow variegation, which adds visual interest to any indoor space. The plant has a short, thick stem and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and decorative plant displays.
