Palmijuncus schizospathus

Palmijuncus schizospathus, commonly known as the dwarf fan palm, is a small, ornamental palm native to Madagascar.

It features a slender, unbranched trunk that grows from a basal rosette of fan-shaped leaves. The leaves are typically bright green, with a smooth texture and a slightly wavy margin. It produces small, fragrant flowers that develop into red, fleshy fruits.

This plant is well-suited for container gardening and thrives in warm, humid environments with well-draining soil.
