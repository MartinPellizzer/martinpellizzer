Peperomia woytkowskii

Peperomia woytkowskii, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, fleshy foliage with a distinctive silver or grayish-green coloration due to its thick, waxy leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia woytkowskii is popular as a houseplant for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
