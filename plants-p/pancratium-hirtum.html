Pancratium hirtum

Pancratium hirtum, commonly known as the African daffodil, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features strap-like, grassy leaves and produces bright yellow flowers with a distinctive white center. Its bulbs are adapted for storage and can remain dormant for extended periods.

Pancratium hirtum is often found in coastal and sandy soils, showcasing its resilience to arid conditions.
