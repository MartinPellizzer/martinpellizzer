Periploca Bifida

Periploca bifida is a rare and fascinating plant native to specific regions of Africa. It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, known for its toxic and often ornamental species. The plant is characterized by its unique, bifurcated leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers. Despite its beauty, it is seldom cultivated due to its limited distribution and specific environmental needs. Its ecological role and potential medicinal properties make it a subject of scientific interest.
