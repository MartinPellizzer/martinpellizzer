Pilosella euronota

Pilosella euronota, commonly known as the European piloselle, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit, with hairy stems and leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the Asteraceae family. Pilosella euronota is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas.

It is generally considered a herbaceous perennial, thriving in well-drained soils.
