Polystachya stuhlmannii

Polystachya stuhlmannii, commonly known as the Stuhlmann's spike orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil and leaf litter.

Polystachya stuhlmannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in warm, tropical gardens.
