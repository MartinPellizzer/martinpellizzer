Pleurospermum stellatum var. lindleyanum

Pleurospermum stellatum var. lindleyanum, commonly known as the Lindley's starflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features showy, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.
