Pittosporum spinescens

Pittosporum spinescens, commonly known as the spiny pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 meters. The plant features dark green, elliptical leaves with a rough texture and prominent veins. Its fragrant white flowers appear in clusters and are followed by small, fleshy berries.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive appearance.
