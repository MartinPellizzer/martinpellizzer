Persicaria punctata var. breviciliata

Persicaria punctata var. breviciliata, commonly known as the short-stemmed smartweed, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist to wet soils, often found in marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often reddish at the base. Its flowers are tiny, clustered in dense spikes, and are usually pink to reddish in color.

This variety is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in wetland environments.
