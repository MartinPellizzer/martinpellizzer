Penstemon scouleri

Penstemon scouleri, commonly known as the tall bearded penstemon, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or open habitats, preferring well-drained soil and full sun. It produces showy flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Penstemon scouleri is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
