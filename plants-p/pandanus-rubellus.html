Pandanus rubellus

Pandanus rubellus, commonly known as the red pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, sword-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant red or orange color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically clustered in pendulous inflorescences. Its fibrous leaves are used in traditional crafts and thatching.

Pandanus rubellus thrives in humid, coastal environments and is often found growing near beaches or in wetlands.
