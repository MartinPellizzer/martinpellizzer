Pittosporum mildbraedii

Pittosporum mildbraedii, commonly known as the Kauri Pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy, often with a waxy coating. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, black seeds.

Pittosporum mildbraedii is often found in coastal and forested areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
