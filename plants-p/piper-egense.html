Piper egense

Piper egense, commonly known as the Egense pepper or Egense pepper vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes numerous species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when mature, and they are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are also utilized for their aromatic properties.

Piper egense thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
