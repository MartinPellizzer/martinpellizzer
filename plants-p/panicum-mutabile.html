Panicum mutabile

Panicum mutabile, commonly known as the variable grass or variable panic grass, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, two-lobed flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and blade-like, with a bluish-green hue when mature.

Panicum mutabile is often found in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and prairies.
