Petrophyes × moralis

Petrophyes × moralis, commonly known as the hybrid rock rose, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage, which is adapted to thrive in rocky and well-drained soils. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, providing a drought-resistant feature.

Petrophyes × moralis is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate harsh, arid conditions.
