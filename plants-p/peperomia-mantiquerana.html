Peperomia mantiquerana

Peperomia mantiquerana, commonly known as the Mantiqueira peperomia, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub with thick, fleshy leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

Peperomia mantiquerana is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
