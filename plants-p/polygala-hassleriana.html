Polygala hassleriana

Polygala hassleriana, commonly known as the silver leaf milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, pale green to silver-white leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in sagebrush steppe and desert environments.

Its botanical features make it an important component of arid ecosystem flora.
