Pteris Biaurita Var. Geminata

Geminata brake fern, scientifically known as Pteris biaurita var. geminata, is a strikingly ornamental fern native to Southeast Asia. This variety is prized for its delicate, lace-like fronds that create a soft, feathery appearance in gardens and indoor spaces. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced gardeners. The plant's unique foliage features small, paired pinnae that give it a distinctive look compared to other brake ferns. With its elegant form and adaptability, Geminata brake fern is a versatile addition to any green space.
