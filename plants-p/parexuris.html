Parexuris

Parexuris, commonly known as the golden star fern, is a unique tropical plant characterized by its striking, star-shaped fronds.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia. The plant features a creeping rhizome with clusters of small, bright yellow spore-producing structures. Its foliage is dense and often cascades in a bushy manner, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated for both its aesthetic appeal and its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
