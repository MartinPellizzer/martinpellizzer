Polygonatum kansuense

Polygonatum kansuense, commonly known as the Kansu Solomon's Seal, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of greenish-yellow flowers in early summer. Its berries are red and turn black as they mature, providing food for various wildlife.

Polygonatum kansuense is often found in moist, shaded forest environments.
