Pilocereus royenii var. armatus

Pilocereus royenii var. armatus, commonly known as the Moon Cactus, is a cacti species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful flowers that bloom at night, typically in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its swollen, segmented stems and spiny areoles. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ease of care.

However, it is important to note that the plant is not particularly hardy in colder climates and requires specific growing conditions to thrive.
