Pyrrosia confluens var. confluens

Pyrrosia confluens var. confluens, commonly known as the Chinese fern or rock fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to East Asia.

It typically grows on rocks or tree trunks in moist, shaded environments. The plant has dark green, leathery fronds with a distinctive, serrated margin. Its spores are produced in clusters on the underside of the fronds, and it reproduces through sexual reproduction.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
