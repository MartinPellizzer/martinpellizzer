Panicum arenicoloides

Panicum arenicoloides, commonly known as sand panicgrass, is a grass species native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, wiry leaves and compact, spikelike inflorescences that produce small, pale green to brownish seeds. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures. It plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and is sometimes used in restoration projects.

Its botanical features make it a hardy, low-maintenance plant suitable for xerophytic landscapes.
