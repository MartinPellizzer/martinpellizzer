Phlebosporum cytisoides

Phlebosporum cytisoides, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring, adding a striking visual contrast to the landscape. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces long, slender pods that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for cultivation in warm climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
