Pycreus rubriglumosus

Pycreus rubriglumosus, commonly known as the red-awned pycreus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and distinctive reddish awns on its seed heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. Pycreus rubriglumosus is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and resilience to various environmental conditions.
