Phalaenopsis × casta var. superbissima

Phalaenopsis × casta var. superbissima, commonly known as the Superbissima Phalaenopsis, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant and long-lasting blooms.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, producing multiple shoots from the base. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. Its flowers are showy, with a complex structure of sepals and petals, often displaying a mix of colors such as white, pink, and purple.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of care in indoor environments.
