Pellionia veronicoides

Pellionia veronicoides, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are dark green in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale green. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Pellionia veronicoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
