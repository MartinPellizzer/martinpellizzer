Pleioblastus simonii f. zigzag

Pleioblastus simonii f. zigzag, commonly known as the zigzag bamboo, is a unique variant of the pleioblastus genus.

It is characterized by its distinctive, undulating leaf patterns that create a zigzag appearance along the stems. This ornamental bamboo is native to China and is prized for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and landscapes. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 3 meters, with slender, green culms that are often adorned with a layer of white or silver hair.

It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
