Prinos crenatus

Prinos crenatus, commonly known as the serrated prinos, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, ovate leaves with serrated edges, which are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers during the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Prinos crenatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
