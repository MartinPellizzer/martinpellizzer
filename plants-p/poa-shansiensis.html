Poa shansiensis

Poa shansiensis, commonly known as the Sichuan bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and dark green, narrow leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in meadows and along mountain slopes.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and restoration projects.
