Peperomia punaluuna

Peperomia punaluuna, commonly known as the Hawaiian peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Hawaii.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, often in shades of white or pale pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and limited natural range, it is considered a rare and valuable species in horticulture.
