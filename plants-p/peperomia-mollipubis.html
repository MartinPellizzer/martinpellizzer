Peperomia mollipubis

Peperomia mollipubis, commonly known as the velvety peperomia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a velvety appearance. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color.

Peperomia mollipubis is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.
