Podocarpus neriifolius var. teysmannii

Podocarpus neriifolius var. teysmannii, commonly known as the Teysmann's satinwood, is a species of coniferous evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain seeds, typical of the Podocarpaceae family. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland and mountainous forest regions.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its durable wood.
