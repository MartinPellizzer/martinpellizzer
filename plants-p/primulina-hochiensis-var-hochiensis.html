Primulina hochiensis var. hochiensis

Primulina hochiensis var. hochiensis, commonly known as the Hochiengen primula, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are broad and ovate, with a glossy surface, while its flowers range in color from white to pink.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
