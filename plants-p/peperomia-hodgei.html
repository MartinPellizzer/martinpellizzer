Peperomia hodgei

Peperomia hodgei, commonly known as the Hodge's peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink, giving it an ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

Peperomia hodgei is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
