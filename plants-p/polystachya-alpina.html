Polystachya alpina

Polystachya alpina, commonly known as the alpine polystachya, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae).

It is a herbaceous plant native to high-altitude regions of the Himalayas and Southeast Asia. The plant produces tall, slender stems with dense clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. Its leaves are narrow and linear, adapted to withstand cold and windy conditions.

Polystachya alpina is often found in rocky or alpine habitats, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
