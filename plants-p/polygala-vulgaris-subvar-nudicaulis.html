Polygala vulgaris subvar. nudicaulis

Polygala vulgaris subvar. nudicaulis, commonly known as the slender milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial with slender, upright stems and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. The plant has compound leaves composed of several narrow, linear leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry meadows and rocky habitats.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
