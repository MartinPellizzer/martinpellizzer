Panicum hybridum

Panicum hybridum, commonly known as switchgrass, is a native perennial grass species that thrives in a variety of soil types and environmental conditions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and feather-like leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. This plant is well adapted to temperate climates and is often found in prairies and disturbed areas. Switchgrass is also valued for its ability to withstand drought and its potential as a bioenergy crop.

Its ecological importance includes soil stabilization and providing habitat for wildlife.
