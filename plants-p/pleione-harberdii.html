Pleione harberdii

Pleione harberdii, commonly known as the Harberd's pleione, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, rhizomatous growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically blooms in the spring, with flowers that range from white to pink and often feature a yellow or green lip. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, humus-rich soil.

Pleione harberdii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
