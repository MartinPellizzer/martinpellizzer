Paphiopedilum philippinense var. compactum

Paphiopedilum philippinense var. compactum, commonly known as the Philippine compact paphiopedilum, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It features a short, stocky rhizome and produces compact, showy flowers with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments within forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with intricate patterns.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
