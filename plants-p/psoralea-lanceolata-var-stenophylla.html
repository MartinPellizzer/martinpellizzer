Psoralea lanceolata var. stenophylla

Psoralea lanceolata var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf psoralea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
