Podocarpus oleifolius subsp. equadorensis

Podocarpus oleifolius subsp.

equadorensis, commonly known as the Ecuadorian podocarp, is a member of the Podocarpaceae family. It is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the Andean regions of Ecuador.

The plant has needle-like leaves that are dark green and arranged spirally along the branches. It produces small, spherical cones that contain edible seeds.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in horticulture and reforestation projects.
