Paphiopedilum niveum

Paphiopedilum niveum, commonly known as the white lady's slipper, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, showy flowers that resemble a slipper, with a white or pale pink coloration. The plant has a thick, fleshy rhizome and produces oval, dark green leaves. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Paphiopedilum niveum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty and rarity.
