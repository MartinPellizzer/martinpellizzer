Pelargonium parviflorum

Pelargonium parviflorum, commonly known as the miniature geranium, is a compact, trailing plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has rounded, velvety leaves that are typically a deep green color. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-draining soil.

Pelargonium parviflorum is often used in hanging baskets or as a ground cover due to its dense growth habit.
