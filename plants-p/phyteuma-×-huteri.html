Phyteuma × huteri

Phyteuma × huteri, commonly known as the hybrid sunflower or garden phyteuma, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically blooms in late summer, producing yellow or orange disk flowers surrounded by white to pink ray florets. It prefers full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and rock gardens.

Phyteuma × huteri is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
