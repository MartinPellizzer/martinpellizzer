Pteris mkomaziensis

Pteris mkomaziensis, commonly known as the Mkomazi water fern, is a species of fern native to Tanzania.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as along stream banks or in dense forests. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help anchor it to the substrate while absorbing nutrients.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
