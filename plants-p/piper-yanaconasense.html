Piper yanaconasense

Piper yanaconasense, commonly known as the Yanaconasense pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Piperaceae family.

It is native to the Amazon rainforest of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with long, slender stems that often trail along the forest floor. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into small, round berries, which are used in traditional medicine and local cuisine.
