Physaliastrum kweichouense

Physaliastrum kweichouense, commonly known as the Kweichou Physalia, is a rare and unique member of the Physaliaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, inflated, bladder-like fruits that are typically green or yellowish in color. The plant is native to China, specifically the Kweichou region, and thrives in humid, tropical climates. Its foliage consists of simple, alternate leaves that are often hairy and serrated.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
