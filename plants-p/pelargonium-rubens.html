Pelargonium rubens

Pelargonium rubens, commonly known as the scarlet geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant red petals and dark green, serrated leaves. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and produces clusters of small, showy flowers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Pelargonium rubens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
