Panicum rottleri

Panicum rottleri, commonly known as the Rottler's panicgrass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Panicum rottleri is often found in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and along roadsides.
