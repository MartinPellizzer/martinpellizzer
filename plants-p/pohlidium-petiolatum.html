Pohlidium petiolatum

Pohlidium petiolatum, commonly known as the slender leaf fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated fronds that are typically light green in color. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread along the forest floor. Its leaves are pinnately divided, contributing to its ornamental appeal in shaded environments.

Pohlidium petiolatum thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires indirect light for optimal growth.
