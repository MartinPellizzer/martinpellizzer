Primula bullata var. bullata

Primula bullata var. bullata, commonly known as the Chinese primrose, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple. The leaves are typically oval and slightly hairy, providing a textured appearance. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.

It prefers cool, moist conditions and blooms in early spring.
