Parsonsia diaphanophlebia

Parsonsia diaphanophlebia, commonly known as the glassy-flowered parsonsia, is a tropical shrub belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

It is native to the Pacific Islands, including parts of Australia and New Caledonia. The plant is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that are typically bright yellow or orange, with a translucent appearance. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a waxy texture.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
