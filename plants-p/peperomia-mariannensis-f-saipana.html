Peperomia mariannensis f. saipana

Peperomia mariannensis f. saipana, commonly known as the Saipan peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Northern Mariana Islands.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with a dark green hue and prominent veins, often adorned with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically greenish-white in color, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for collectors and indoor gardeners.
