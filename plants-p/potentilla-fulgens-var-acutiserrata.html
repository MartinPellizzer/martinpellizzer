Potentilla fulgens var. acutiserrata

Potentilla fulgens var. acutiserrata, commonly known as the fiery potentilla, is a flowering shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense foliage, with leaves that are elliptical and sharply serrated. The plant produces showy, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its compact size and ornamental value make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
