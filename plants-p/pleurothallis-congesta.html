Pleurothallis congesta

Pleurothallis congesta, commonly known as the clustered pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. Its inflorescence is often clustered, giving it its common name.

Pleurothallis congesta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
