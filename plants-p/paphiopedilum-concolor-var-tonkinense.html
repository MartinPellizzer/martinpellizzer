Paphiopedilum concolor var. tonkinense

Paphiopedilum concolor var. tonkinense, commonly known as the Tonkin Butterfly Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, butterfly-like flowers with a distinctive purple and white coloration. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are ovate and slightly fleshy, adapted to its humid, tropical habitat.

It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is prized for its ornamental value in horticulture.
