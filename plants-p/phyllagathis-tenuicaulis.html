Phyllagathis tenuicaulis

Phyllagathis tenuicaulis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed phyllagathis, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often found in the understorey of tropical rainforests. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are usually arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. Its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences and are often pink or white in color.

Phyllagathis tenuicaulis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
