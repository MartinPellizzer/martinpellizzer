Pilea hazenii

Pilea hazenii, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its distinctive, round, waxy leaves that resemble coins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically hidden beneath the leaves. It is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in indirect light and well-draining soil.

Pilea hazenii is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
