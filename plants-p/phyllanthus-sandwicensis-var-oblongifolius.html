Phyllanthus sandwicensis var. oblongifolius

Phyllanthus sandwicensis var. oblongifolius, commonly known as the Hawaiian sandwicensis phyllanthus, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often elliptical or oblong in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on short pedicels. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

This variant is part of the Euphorbiaceae family and is notable for its ecological importance in Hawaiian ecosystems.
