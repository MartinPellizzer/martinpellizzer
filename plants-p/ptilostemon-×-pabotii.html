Ptilostemon × pabotii

Ptilostemon × pabotii, commonly known as the hybrid ptilostemon, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, small white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its fragrant blooms and compact growth habit.
