Pelargonium × kolowrathianum

Pelargonium × kolowrathianum, commonly known as the Royal Heritage geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated edge, and it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for gardens and containers due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.

Pelargonium × kolowrathianum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
