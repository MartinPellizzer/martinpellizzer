Peperomia ladronica

Peperomia ladronica, commonly known as the Dragon's Tail Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves that are often adorned with white or silver veins, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically green or red, which are followed by elongated, spiky seed capsules. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant.
