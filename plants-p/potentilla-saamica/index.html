Potentilla Saamica

Potentilla saamica is a hardy shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It is known for its delicate yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for rock gardens and borders. Potentilla saamica is also valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
