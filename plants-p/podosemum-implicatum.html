Podosemum implicatum

Podosemum implicatum, commonly known as the tangled pod plant, is a member of the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its unique, serpentine-like pods that twist around themselves, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a hairy texture.
