Platanthera tipuloides var. ussuriensis

Platanthera tipuloides var. ussuriensis, commonly known as the Ussuri marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to eastern Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant features a slender stem with a single, showy spike of pale yellow or greenish-white flowers. Its roots are associated with mycorrhizal fungi, which aid in nutrient absorption.

The species is characterized by its delicate appearance and ecological significance in its native habitats.
