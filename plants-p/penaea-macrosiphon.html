Penaea macrosiphon

Penaea macrosiphon, commonly known as the giant siphon grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features a robust rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread and colonize new areas effectively. The plant produces tall, cylindrical inflorescences that bear numerous small, inconspicuous flowers. Its leaves are long, narrow, and often have a slightly rough texture.

Penaea macrosiphon is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and resilience.
