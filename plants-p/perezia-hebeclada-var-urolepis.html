Perezia hebeclada var. urolepis

Perezia hebeclada var. urolepis, commonly known as the silver-leaved perezia, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features dense, silver-gray foliage that provides a striking contrast to its dark green stems. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white and pale blue during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a valuable addition to Mediterranean-style gardens.
