Phyllanthus kerstingii

Phyllanthus kerstingii, commonly known as the African date palm, is a flowering plant in the family Phyllanthaceae.

It is native to tropical Africa and is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces edible fruits that are similar in appearance to dates, though they are not related to true dates. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas and riverine forests.

The plant has traditional medicinal uses in some African cultures.
