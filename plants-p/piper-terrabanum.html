Piper terrabanum

Piper terrabanum, commonly known as the Philippine pepper vine, is a climbing shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers. Its fruits are tiny, round berries that turn red when mature.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
