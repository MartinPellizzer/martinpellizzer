Peperomia dendromorphis

Peperomia dendromorphis, commonly known as the tree peperomia, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features a branching stem system that resembles a small tree, with dense foliage of glossy, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia dendromorphis is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
