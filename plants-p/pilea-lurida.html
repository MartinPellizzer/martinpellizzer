Pilea lurida

Pilea lurida, commonly known as the velvet plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense, velvety layer of fine hairs, giving it a soft, fuzzy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
