Phyllostachys heterocycla f. huamozhu

Phyllostachys heterocycla f. huamozhu, commonly known as the bamboo variety from the Huamozhu region, is a clumping bamboo species native to China.

It features slender, green culms that are often used in ornamental landscaping due to their elegant appearance. The leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth texture and a slightly glossy surface. This variety is well-suited for partial shade and requires well-drained soil to thrive.

It is valued for its resilience and ability to add a tropical feel to gardens.
