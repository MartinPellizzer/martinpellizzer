Peperomia multifolia

Peperomia multifolia, commonly known as the Shiny Leaf Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves with a waxy texture and prominent veins, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of green or white, which are followed by tiny, berry-like fruits. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and minimal water requirements.

Its dense foliage makes it an excellent choice for tabletop displays or as part of a living green wall.
