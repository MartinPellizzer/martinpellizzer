Pimelea drupacea var. glabrata

Pimelea drupacea var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth pimelea, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter, featuring dense, compact foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions.
