Pilea apiculata

Pilea apiculata, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. Native to China, this plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its unique, coin-like foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea apiculata is relatively low maintenance, making it an ideal plant for beginners and those with limited space.
