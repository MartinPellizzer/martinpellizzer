Polygonum erythrodes

Polygonum erythrodes, commonly known as redstem smartweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Polygonaceae, which includes many species with distinctive arrow-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist soils along stream banks, wet meadows, and other wetland habitats. Its small, pink to red flowers appear in dense clusters, and it produces small, three-angled berries.

Polygonum erythrodes is an annual herb that is often found in disturbed areas and can spread rapidly in suitable conditions.
