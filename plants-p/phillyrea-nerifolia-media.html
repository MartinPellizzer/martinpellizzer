Phillyrea nerifolia-media

Phillyrea nerifolia-media, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Phillyrea, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Phillyrea nerifolia-media is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
