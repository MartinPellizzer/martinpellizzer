Pourretia sympaganthera

Pourretia sympaganthera, commonly known as the golden crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow ray florets and dark purple disk flowers, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads. It typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils.
