Pobeguinea trispiculata

Pobeguinea trispiculata, commonly known as the African violet, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its rosette of velvety, dark green leaves. Pobeguinea trispiculata is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
