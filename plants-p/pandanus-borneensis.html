Pandanus borneensis

Pandanus borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean pandanus, is a tropical plant native to Borneo.

It is a member of the Pandanaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. Its leaves are often used in traditional crafts and for thatching due to their durability.

The Bornean pandanus thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is commonly found in coastal and lowland areas.
