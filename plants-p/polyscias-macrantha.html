Polyscias macrantha

Polyscias macrantha, commonly known as the large-leaf sycamore, is a member of the Araliaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height, characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are often glossy green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Polyscias macrantha is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate various environmental conditions.
