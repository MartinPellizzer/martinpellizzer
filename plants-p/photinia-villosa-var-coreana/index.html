Photinia Villosa Var. Coreana

Photinia villosa var. coreana is a unique and ornamental plant native to Korea, known for its striking red foliage and compact growth habit. This evergreen shrub thrives in partial shade to full sun and is prized for its vibrant color throughout the growing season. Its soft, velvety leaves add texture and visual interest to any garden or landscape design. While it is relatively low maintenance, it requires well-drained soil and protection from extreme weather conditions. This plant is a popular choice among gardeners seeking both beauty and resilience in their outdoor spaces.
