Pilosella merxmuelleriana

Pilosella merxmuelleriana, commonly known as the Merxmueller's piloselle, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and hairy stems. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in the summer months. Native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia, it thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Pilosella genus, often found in alpine and subalpine regions.
