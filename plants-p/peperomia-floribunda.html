Peperomia floribunda

Peperomia floribunda, commonly known as the coral peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with reddish or purple undersides, giving it a striking visual appeal. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of red, pink, or white, which bloom from spring to summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive foliage, it is widely cultivated as a decorative plant.
