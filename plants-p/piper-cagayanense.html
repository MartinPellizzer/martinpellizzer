Piper cagayanense

Piper cagayanense, commonly known as the Cagayan pepper, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into elongated, slender fruits.

The fruit is often used in traditional medicine and as a spice in local cuisines.
