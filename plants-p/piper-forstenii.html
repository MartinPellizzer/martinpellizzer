Piper forstenii

Piper forstenii, commonly known as the Forsten's pepper vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into elongated, peppery fruits. Its botanical structure is characterized by its vigorous climbing habit and dense foliage.

Piper forstenii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties.
