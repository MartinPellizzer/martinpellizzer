Phagnalon helichrysoides subsp. nitidum

Phagnalon helichrysoides subsp.

nitidum, commonly known as the glossy phagnalon, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and yellow flowers arranged in composite heads.

The subspecies is distinguished by its glossy, waxy foliage, which gives it its common name. It is typically found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.
