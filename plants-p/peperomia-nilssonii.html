Peperomia nilssonii

Peperomia nilssonii, commonly known as the Nilsson's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval to heart-shaped, with a glossy surface and a variety of colors ranging from deep green to reddish hues. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish, often hidden among the foliage. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia nilssonii is also noted for its drought tolerance and air-purifying qualities.
