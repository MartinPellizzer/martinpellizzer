Pileanthus filifolius

Pileanthus filifolius, commonly known as the silky filifolius, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, thread-like leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
