Platyaechmea distichantha var. glaziovii

Platyaechmea distichantha var. glaziovii, commonly known as the Glaziov's spider plant, is a striking member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its rosette of narrow, dark green, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces long, pendant inflorescences that bear showy, red-orange flowers, which are followed by small, white, urn-shaped fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ornamental value and ease of care contribute to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
