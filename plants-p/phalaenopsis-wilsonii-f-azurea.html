Phalaenopsis wilsonii f. azurea

Phalaenopsis wilsonii f. azurea, commonly known as the Azure Phalaenopsis, is a hybrid orchid prized for its striking blue-violet flower spikes.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces elongated, pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, waxy flowers that bloom in succession. Its foliage consists of broad, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.

This cultivar is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
