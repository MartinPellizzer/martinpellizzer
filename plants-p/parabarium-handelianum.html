Parabarium handelianum

Parabarium handelianum, commonly known as the silverleaf spurge, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silver-gray, woolly leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy species for arid environments.

Due to its toxic sap, it is important to handle it with care.
