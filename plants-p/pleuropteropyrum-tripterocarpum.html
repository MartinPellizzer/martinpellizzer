Pleuropteropyrum tripterocarpum

Pleuropteropyrum tripterocarpum, commonly known as the three-winged pod plant, is a member of the Leguminosae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive three-winged pods that enclose the seeds. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded, moist environments. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three leaflets arranged symmetrically.

The flowers are small and clustered, producing a unique structure that aids in seed dispersal.
