Plectranthus oblanceolatus

Plectranthus oblanceolatus, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, oval-shaped leaves and clusters of small, violet or purple flowers. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and blooms.

Plectranthus oblanceolatus is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
