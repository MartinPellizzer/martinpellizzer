Pyrus liljeforsii

Pyrus liljeforsii, commonly known as the Liljefors' pear, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its deciduous nature, with heart-shaped leaves that turn yellow in autumn. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, followed by edible fruits that are similar to pears in appearance. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas with well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value and fruit production, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
