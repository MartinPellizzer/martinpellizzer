Prenanthes ruanensis

Prenanthes ruanensis, commonly known as the Ruan's bladderpod, is a herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds, aiding in its dispersal.
