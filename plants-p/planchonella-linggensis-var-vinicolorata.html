Planchonella linggensis var. vinicolorata

Planchonella linggensis var. vinicolorata, commonly known as the purple-leafed hibiscus, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking purple foliage, which contrasts beautifully with its green stems and pale yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, with a smooth or slightly toothed margin.
