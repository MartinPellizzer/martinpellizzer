Pelargonium × essexiae

Pelargonium × essexiae, commonly known as the English geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, five-petaled flowers that come in a range of colors including pink, red, and white. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with a serrated edge, and the plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil, making it a versatile choice for both container and garden planting.

Pelargonium × essexiae is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
