Ponerorchis taiwanensis

Ponerorchis taiwanensis, commonly known as the Taiwan orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Taiwan.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and linear, lanceolate leaves. The plant produces a single, showy inflorescence with flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with purple or brown markings. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
