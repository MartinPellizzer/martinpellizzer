Pilea elizabethae

Pilea elizabethae, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rounded, fleshy leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Its unique leaf shape and low maintenance requirements contribute to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
