Pinus resinosa f. globosa

Pinus resinosa f. globosa, commonly known as the globe pine, is a rare variant of the pitch pine.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical growth habit, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant exhibits a compact form with closely spaced branches, creating a rounded canopy. Its foliage consists of short, stiff needles that are typically dark green in color.

This form is often observed in specific ecological conditions, such as on rocky outcrops or in areas with limited soil depth.
