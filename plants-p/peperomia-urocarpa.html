Peperomia urocarpa

Peperomia urocarpa, commonly known as the red-veined peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features heart-shaped leaves with distinctive red veins, giving it a striking visual appeal. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by reddish-brown berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia urocarpa is relatively low-maintenance and suitable for beginners due to its adaptability and resilience.
