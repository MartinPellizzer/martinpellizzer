Pilea ekmanii

Pilea ekmanii, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly textured surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale green, followed by round, fleshy fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Pilea ekmanii is valued for its ornamental foliage and ease of care, making it a popular houseplant.
