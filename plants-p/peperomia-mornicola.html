Peperomia mornicola

Peperomia mornicola, commonly known as the Mornicola Peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically oval or elliptical in shape, often with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Peperomia mornicola is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
