Pitcairnia geyskesii

Pitcairnia geyskesii, commonly known as the velvet pitcairnia, is a bromeliad native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive velvety appearance. The plant produces striking, long-lasting inflorescences with vibrant red bracts. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
