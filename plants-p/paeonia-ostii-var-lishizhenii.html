Paeonia ostii var. lishizhenii

Paeonia ostii var. lishizhenii, commonly known as the Lishizhen peony, is a rare and ornamental variant of the peony species native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white, with a distinctive fragrance. The plant is a deciduous herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, and features compound leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic value, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes and botanical study.
