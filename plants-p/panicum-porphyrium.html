Panicum porphyrium

Panicum porphyrium, commonly known as purple panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are often tinged with purple or reddish hues. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy texture.

Panicum porphyrium is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
