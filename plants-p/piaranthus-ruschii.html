Piaranthus ruschii

Piaranthus ruschii, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has small, narrow leaves and produces small, oval-shaped fruits.

Piaranthus ruschii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its vibrant blooms.
