Phalaenopsis amabilis subvar. dayana

Phalaenopsis amabilis subvar. dayana, commonly known as the Dayana orchid, is a hybrid orchid originating from the Philippines.

It is a sympodial orchid with pseudobulbs that support its growth and storage of nutrients. The plant produces showy, long-lasting flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a speckled or streaked pattern. It prefers bright, indirect light and consistent humidity, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its foliage consists of dark green, strap-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern around the pseudobulbs.
