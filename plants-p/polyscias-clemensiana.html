Polyscias clemensiana

Polyscias clemensiana, commonly known as the Hawaiian shield fern, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its dense, glossy, dark green foliage. The plant has compound leaves with trifoliate or pinnate structures, often with a shield-like appearance, giving it its common name. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in coastal forests.

Polyscias clemensiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
