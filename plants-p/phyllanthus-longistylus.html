Phyllanthus longistylus

Phyllanthus longistylus, commonly known as the long-styled phyllanthus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

Phyllanthus longistylus is also noted for its medicinal uses in traditional medicine systems.
