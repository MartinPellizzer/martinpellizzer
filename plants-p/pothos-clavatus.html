Pothos clavatus

Pothos clavatus, commonly known as the silver vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves with a distinctive silver or white variegation pattern. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Pothos clavatus is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
