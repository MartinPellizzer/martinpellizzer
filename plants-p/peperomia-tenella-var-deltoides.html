Peperomia tenella var. deltoides

Peperomia tenella var. deltoides, commonly known as the Shiny Cabbage Plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
