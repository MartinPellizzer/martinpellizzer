Pinanga declinata

Pinanga declinata, commonly known as the drooping bamboo palm, is a flowering plant belonging to the Arecaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant features slender, arching leaves that give it a graceful appearance, often growing in dense clusters. Its inflorescence consists of small yellow flowers that appear at the base of the plant.

Pinanga declinata is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its adaptability and air-purifying qualities.
