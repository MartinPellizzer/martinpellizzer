Pimelea spinescens subsp. spinescens

Pimelea spinescens subsp.

spinescens, commonly known as the Spiny Riceflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia. It is a shrub characterized by its small, needle-like leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It is an important component of the local flora and is often found in heathlands and open woodlands.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance.
