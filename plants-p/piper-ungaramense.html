Piper ungaramense

Piper ungaramense, commonly known as the Ucayali pepper, is a flowering plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruits are small berries that turn red when mature, and they are used in traditional medicine.

The plant's botanical features make it an important species in the study of tropical flora and ethnobotany.
