Piper ledebourii

Piper ledebourii, commonly known as the African pepper or wild pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of tiny, yellow-green flowers. It produces small, red, peppery fruits that are used in traditional medicine and cuisine.

The botanical features of Piper ledeburii make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ethnobotanical studies.
