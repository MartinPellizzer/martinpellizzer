Paederia wallichii

Paederia wallichii, commonly known as the Himalayan false indigo, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and purple. Its fruit is a capsule that disperses seeds through wind.

Paederia wallichii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
