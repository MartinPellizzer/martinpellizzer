Piper bredemeyeri

Piper bredemeyeri, commonly known as the Bredemeyer's pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, clustered white flowers. It produces tiny, berry-like fruits that are often used in traditional medicine.

The plant is valued for its aromatic properties and has been studied for potential medicinal applications.
