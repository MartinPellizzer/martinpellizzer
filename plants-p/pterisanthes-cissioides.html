Pterisanthes cissioides

Pterisanthes cissioides, commonly known as the creeping pterisanthes, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal and sandy habitats. The plant features opposite, ovate to lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the stems.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
