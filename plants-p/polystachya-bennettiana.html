Polystachya bennettiana

Polystachya bennettiana, commonly known as Bennett's spikegrass, is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is a terrestrial herb with a rhizomatous growth habit, often found in shaded, moist environments. The plant produces tall, slender flowering stems that bear racemes of small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, typically arranged in a basal rosette.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance.
