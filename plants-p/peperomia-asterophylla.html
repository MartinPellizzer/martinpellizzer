Peperomia asterophylla

Peperomia asterophylla, commonly known as the star-leaf peperomia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and its distinctive, star-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a metallic sheen. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color.

Peperomia asterophylla is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive foliage.
