Philadelphus zeyheri

Philadelphus zeyheri, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to South Africa.

It features fragrant, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are oval to elliptic in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and ability to attract pollinators make it a valuable addition to native plant landscapes.
