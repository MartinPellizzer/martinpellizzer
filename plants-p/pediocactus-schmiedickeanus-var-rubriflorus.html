Pediocactus schmiedickeanus var. rubriflorus

Pediocactus schmiedickeanus var. rubriflorus, commonly known as the red-flowered hedgehog cactus, is a succulent native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in spiny, tuberculate structures. The plant produces vibrant red flowers during the spring and summer months, adding striking visual contrast to its green foliage. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is often cultivated by cactus enthusiasts.
