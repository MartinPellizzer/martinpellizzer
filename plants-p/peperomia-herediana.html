Peperomia herediana

Peperomia herediana, commonly known as the Herediana Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, often in shades of red or purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia herediana is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and tolerating a range of environmental conditions.
