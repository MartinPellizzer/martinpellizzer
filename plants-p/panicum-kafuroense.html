Panicum kafuroense

Panicum kafuroense, commonly known as the Kafuro grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers.

Panicum kafuroense is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
