Ponerorchis Chusua Var. Nana

Ponerorchis chusua var. nana is a rare and delicate orchid native to the mountainous regions of East Asia. It is known for its small, compact growth habit and pale green to yellowish flowers. This variety thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
