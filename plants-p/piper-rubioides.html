Piper rubioides

Piper rubioides, commonly known as the red raspberry pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into bright red, berry-like fruits. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly serrated edge.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
