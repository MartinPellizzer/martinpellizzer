Portulaca hortensis

Portulaca hortensis, commonly known as garden purslane, is an annual flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy choice for gardens and landscapes. Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in dry conditions.

Portulaca hortensis is also valued for its edible leaves and flowers, which have a slightly tangy flavor.
