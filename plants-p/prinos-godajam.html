Prinos godajam

Prinos godajam, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a vibrant display during the flowering season. The plant has compound leaves with multiple leaflets, and its bark is smooth and reddish in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Prinos godajam is also valued for its ability to provide shade and its ecological benefits to local wildlife.
