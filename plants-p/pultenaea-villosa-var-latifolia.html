Pultenaea villosa var. latifolia

Pultenaea villosa var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved pultenaea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features elliptical to ovate leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange in color, arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and dry sclerophyll forests.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowering display.
