Piptolepis schultziana

Piptolepis schultziana, commonly known as the Schultze's pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers in clusters, which bloom in spring. Its fruit is a capsule that releases seeds when mature.

It is often found in coastal and lowland forest environments.
