Poa gigantea

Poa gigantea, commonly known as the giant grass or giant poa, is a tall, perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Poa gigantea is adapted to a variety of soil types and is often found in open, disturbed habitats.
