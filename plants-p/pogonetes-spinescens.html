Pogonetes spinescens

Pogonetes spinescens, commonly known as the spiny pogon, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive spiny bracts that surround its flowering spikes. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological studies and ornamental gardening.
