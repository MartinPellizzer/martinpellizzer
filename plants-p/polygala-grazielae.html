Polygala grazielae

Polygala grazielae, commonly known as the Graceful Polygala, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, spire-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually pale blue or white, with a distinctive, spurred structure.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
