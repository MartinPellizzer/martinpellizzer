Paphiopedilum markianum

Paphiopedilum markianum, commonly known as the Markian's lady's slipper, is a rare and highly sought-after orchid native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It is characterized by its unique, intricate flower structure, featuring a pouch-like lip that is often brightly colored. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid, thriving in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. Its flowers, which can range in color from white to pink and yellow, bloom during the spring and summer months.

Due to its beauty and rarity, Paphiopedilum markianum is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
