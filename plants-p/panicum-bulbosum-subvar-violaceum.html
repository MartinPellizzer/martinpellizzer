Panicum bulbosum subvar. violaceum

Panicum bulbosum subvar. violaceum, commonly known as purple bulbous grass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, bulbous base and dense, upright growth habit. The plant produces slender, spike-like inflorescences that are often tinged with purple or pink hues. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

This ornamental grass is valued for its attractive appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
