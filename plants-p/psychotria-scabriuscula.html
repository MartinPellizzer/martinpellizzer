Psychotria scabriuscula

Psychotria scabriuscula, commonly known as the rough-leaved psychotria, is a member of the Rubiaceae family and is native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub characterized by its rough, hairy leaves and clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers.
