Polygonum ochroleucum

Polygonum ochroleucum, commonly known as white knotweed.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features slender, erect stems that are often reddish in color. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and covered with a fine, white pubescence.

The flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and typically pink or purple in color.
