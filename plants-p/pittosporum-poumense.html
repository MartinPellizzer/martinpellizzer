Pittosporum poumense

Pittosporum poumense, commonly known as the Japanese Pittosporum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Japan.

It features dark green, glossy leaves that turn a striking red or orange in the autumn. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers in clusters, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its ornamental value comes from its seasonal color changes and pleasant fragrance.
