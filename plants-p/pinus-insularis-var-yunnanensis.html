Pinus insularis var. yunnanensis

Pinus insularis var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan pine, is a variant of the island pine native to China.

It is a coniferous evergreen tree characterized by its needle-like leaves and cone-bearing structure. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with a preference for well-drained soils. Its botanical features include a robust root system and a conical shape that aids in wind resistance.

This species plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
