Petalostemon capitatus

Petalostemon capitatus, commonly known as the clustered petalostemon, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are opposite and lance-shaped. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Petalostemon capitatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
