Polystachya cyperacearum

Polystachya cyperacearum, commonly known as the narrowleaf spikerush, is a member of the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges.

The plant produces small, clustered spikes of flowers that are typically brown or purplish in color. It thrives in wet or moist environments, such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds.

Polystachya cyperacearum plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and food for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
