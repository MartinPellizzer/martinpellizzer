Pentas lanceolata subsp. cymosa

Pentas lanceolata subsp.

cymosa, commonly known as the clustered pentas, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink.

The leaves are lanceolate, meaning they are narrow and pointed, with a slightly hairy texture. This species thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its compact size and showy blooms make it suitable for containers, rock gardens, and borders.
