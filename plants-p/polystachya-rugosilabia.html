Polystachya rugosilabia

Polystachya rugosilabia, commonly known as the rough-leafed spikegrass, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, clump-forming growth habit and produces dense, spike-like inflorescences. The leaves are typically rough to the touch, with a waxy texture, and the flowers are small, often with a greenish or yellowish hue. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
