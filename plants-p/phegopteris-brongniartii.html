Phegopteris brongniartii

Phegopteris brongniartii, commonly known as the broad buckwheat fern, is a species of fern native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its large, bipinnate fronds that can reach lengths of up to two meters. The fronds are typically bright green and have a distinctive, feathery appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.

The plant is known for its ability to tolerate a variety of soil types and is often found in moist, well-drained habitats.
