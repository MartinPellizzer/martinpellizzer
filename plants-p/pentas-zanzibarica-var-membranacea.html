Pentas zanzibarica var. membranacea

Pentas zanzibarica var. membranacea, commonly known as the Zanzibar pentas, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of East Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The leaves are typically ovate and have a smooth, waxy texture. This species thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

It is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
