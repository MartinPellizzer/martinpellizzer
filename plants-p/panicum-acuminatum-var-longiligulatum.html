Panicum acuminatum var. longiligulatum

Panicum acuminatum var. longiligulatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved panicgrass, is a grass species native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, two-lobed flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental potential and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its delicate appearance.
