Ptilotus forrestii

Ptilotus forrestii, commonly known as the desert lily, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, which help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with a distinctive shape resembling a lantern.

Ptilotus forrestii is adapted to survive in nutrient-poor soils and can withstand extreme temperatures.
