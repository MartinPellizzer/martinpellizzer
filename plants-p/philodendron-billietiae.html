Philodendron billietiae

Philodendron billietiae, commonly known as the Billiet's philodendron, is a climbing tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with white or cream patterns, adding to its ornamental appeal. The plant has a robust climbing habit, using aerial roots to attach to tree trunks and other structures. Its foliage is typically dark green, with a velvety texture that enhances its visual presence.

Philodendron billietiae thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil for optimal growth.
