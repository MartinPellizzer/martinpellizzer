Phaedranassa cinerea

Phaedranassa cinerea, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, pale green to silver-colored leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded environments.

Phaedranassa cinerea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique foliage.
