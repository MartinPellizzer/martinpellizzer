Pentameris scandens

Pentameris scandens, commonly known as the climbing pentameris, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its twining stems that climb along other plants or structures. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically purple or pink in color. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.
