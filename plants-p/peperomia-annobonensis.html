Peperomia annobonensis

Peperomia annobonensis, commonly known as the Annobon Island peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to the island of Annobon in Equatorial Guinea.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features glossy, ovate leaves that are often variegated with shades of green and silver. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in spikes.

Peperomia annobonensis is well adapted to humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.
