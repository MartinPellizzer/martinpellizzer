Prunus spinosa proles amelanchierflora

Prunus spinosa proles amelanchierflora, commonly known as the European whitebeam, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its oval, glossy leaves and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces edible berries, which are typically red or black in color, and are often used in jams and jellies.
