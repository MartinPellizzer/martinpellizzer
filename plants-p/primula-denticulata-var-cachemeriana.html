Primula denticulata var. cachemeriana

Primula denticulata var. cachemeriana, commonly known as the Cachemire primrose, is a species of flowering plant in the family Primulaceae.

It is native to the Himalayan regions of India, Nepal, and Bhutan, where it thrives in moist, cool environments. The plant features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, with a distinctive denticulate edge on the petals. Its foliage consists of broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of down.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
