Phyllostachys nigra f. tosaensis

Phyllostachys nigra f. tosaensis, commonly known as the Tosa black bamboo, is a variant of the black bamboo species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black, culms that are often streaked with lighter green or silver. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. It is a clumping bamboo that grows to a moderate height, making it suitable for smaller gardens.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in ornamental landscaping.
