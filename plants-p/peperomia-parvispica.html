Peperomia parvispica

Peperomia parvispica, commonly known as the small-spined peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters. The plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal watering needs. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers moderate to warm temperatures.

Peperomia parvispica is valued for its ornamental foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
