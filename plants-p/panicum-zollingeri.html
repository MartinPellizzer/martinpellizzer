Panicum zollingeri

Panicum zollingeri, commonly known as Zollinger's panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. The plant has slender, wiry stems and produces fluffy, yellowish-green inflorescences that are characteristic of the Panicum genus. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a rough texture.

It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
