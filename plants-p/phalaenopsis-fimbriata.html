Phalaenopsis fimbriata

Phalaenopsis fimbriata, commonly known as the fringed moth orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, linear leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in a raceme. The flowers typically have a white or pale pink coloration with distinctive fringed petals and sepals. It prefers bright, indirect light and moderate humidity, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

This orchid is known for its ability to bloom repeatedly throughout the year under proper care.
