Peperomia quispicanchiana

Peperomia quispicanchiana, commonly known as the Andean peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Andean regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of red, pink, or white, which are often hidden among the foliage. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia quispicanchiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and air-purifying qualities.
