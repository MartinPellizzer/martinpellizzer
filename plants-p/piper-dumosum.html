Piper dumosum

Piper dumosum, commonly known as the creeping pepper plant, is a tropical vine native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant is characterized by its dense, sprawling growth habit and small, heart-shaped leaves. It produces tiny, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into small, round berries.

The berries are often used in traditional medicine and have a mild, peppery flavor.
