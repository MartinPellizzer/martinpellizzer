Polyscias alternifolia

Polyscias alternifolia, commonly known as the variegated goosefoot, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

Polyscias alternifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
