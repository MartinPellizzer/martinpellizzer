Plectranthus burnatii

Plectranthus burnatii, commonly known as the Burnatii coleus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and purple. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Plectranthus burnatii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a container plant.
