Paphiopedilum barbatum var. hennisianum

Paphiopedilum barbatum var. hennisianum, commonly known as the Hennisianum Phalaenopsis, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with fleshy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces showy, butterfly-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the forest floor, and it thrives in cool, humid environments with partial shade.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
