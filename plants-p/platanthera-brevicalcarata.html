Platanthera brevicalcarata

Platanthera brevicalcarata, commonly known as the short-spurred lady's tresses, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial herb with a slender, erect stem that bears a dense spike of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers.
