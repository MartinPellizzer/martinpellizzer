Pennisetum lanatum

Pennisetum lanatum, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and fluffy, feathery plumes that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or pale yellow. Its leaves are long, narrow, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its soft, velvety texture.

Pennisetum lanatum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and graceful blooms.
