Placodiscus boya

Placodiscus boya, commonly known as the African velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its palmate leaves, which are typically large and have a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellow in color. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds.

Placodiscus boya is often found in disturbed habitats and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural areas.
