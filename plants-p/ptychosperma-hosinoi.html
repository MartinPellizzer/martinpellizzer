Ptychosperma hosinoi

Ptychosperma hosinoi, commonly known as the Hosino's areca, is a species of palm native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in clustered inflorescences, followed by elongated, three-angled fruits. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
