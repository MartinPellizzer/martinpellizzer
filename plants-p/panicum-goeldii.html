Panicum goeldii

Panicum goeldii, commonly known as Goeld's panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats, such as marshes and riverbanks. The plant features tall, slender stems with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its inflorescence consists of a panicle of small, spikelet-bearing branches.

It is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for wildlife.
