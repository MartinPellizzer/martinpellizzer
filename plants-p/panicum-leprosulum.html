Panicum leprosulum

Panicum leprosulum, commonly known as the leprosous grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant is characterized by its slender, hairless stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Panicum leprosulum is often found in disturbed soils and is used in some traditional practices for its fibrous materials.
