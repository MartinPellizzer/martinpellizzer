Pfaffia glabratoides var. villosissima

Pfaffia glabratoides var. villosissima, commonly known as the hairy bittercress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, white or pink flowers and its hairy, succulent stems and leaves. Native to regions of South America, this species thrives in moist, temperate climates. The plant produces small, rounded seed pods that split open to release its seeds.

It is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, showcasing its adaptability to various environments.
