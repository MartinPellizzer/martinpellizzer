Pittosporum kweichowense var. buxifolium

Pittosporum kweichowense var. buxifolium, commonly known as the Chinese pittosporum, is a shrub native to China.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, orange-red berries. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

This variety is distinguished by its compact growth habit and unique leaf morphology compared to other Pittosporum species.
