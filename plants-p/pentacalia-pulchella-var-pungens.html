Pentacalia pulchella var. pungens

Pentacalia pulchella var. pungens, commonly known as the spiny pentacalia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, shrub-like growth habit with thick, fleshy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. Its leaves are often covered in fine hairs and may have spines, contributing to its unique appearance.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
