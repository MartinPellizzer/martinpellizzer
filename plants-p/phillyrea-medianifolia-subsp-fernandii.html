Phillyrea medianifolia subsp. fernandii

Phillyrea medianifolia subsp.

fernandii, commonly known as the Canary Island Phillyrea, is a shrub or small tree native to the Canary Islands. It features dense, evergreen foliage with small, oval-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green.

The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. Its fruit is a small, three-lobed capsule that splits open to release seeds.

This species is well adapted to the dry, rocky environments of its native habitat.
