Pandorea oxleyi

Pandorea oxleyi, commonly known as the oxley pandorea, is a climbing shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features heart-shaped leaves and produces fragrant, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant is characterized by its woody stems and is often found in coastal and subtropical regions. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires partial to full sun exposure.

Pandorea oxleyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and parks.
