Perezia lactucoides subsp. palustris

Perezia lactucoides subsp.

palustris, commonly known as the marsh lettuce, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland habitats in Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette growth pattern and small, lobed leaves.
