Prunus × nigrella

Prunus × nigrella, commonly known as the black cherry or blackthorn, is a hybrid species within the Prunus genus.

It is characterized by its small, dark purple to black berries that ripen in late summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are ovate with serrated edges and a glossy green upper surface.

The flowers are white or pale pink, appearing in clusters and blooming in early spring.
