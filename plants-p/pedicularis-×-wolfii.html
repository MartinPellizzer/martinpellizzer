Pedicularis × wolfii

It is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers that resemble a monkey's face, hence its common name. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe, where it thrives in alpine and subalpine habitats. Pedicularis × wolfii is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique and vibrant floral display.
