Patagonica glaziovii

Patagonica glaziovii, commonly known as the Glaziov's cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Argentina.

It is a small, spineless cactus that typically grows to a height of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features a globular stem covered in dense, white or pale green tubercles. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, blooming in late spring or early summer.

Patagonica glaziovii is well adapted to harsh, dry environments and is often found in rocky soils.
