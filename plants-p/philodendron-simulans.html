Philodendron simulans

Philodendron simulans, commonly known as the heartleaf philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy green leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant has aerial roots that allow it to climb and attach to surfaces, making it well-suited for hanging baskets or shelves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Philodendron simulans is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
