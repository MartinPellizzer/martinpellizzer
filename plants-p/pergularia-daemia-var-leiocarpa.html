Pergularia daemia var. leiocarpa

Pergularia daemia var. leiocarpa, commonly known as the white-flowered dwarf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces small, fragrant, white or pale blue flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
