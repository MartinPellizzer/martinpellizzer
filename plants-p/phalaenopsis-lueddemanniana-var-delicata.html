Phalaenopsis lueddemanniana var. delicata

Phalaenopsis lueddemanniana var. delicata, commonly known as the Delicate Phalaenopsis, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, long-lasting flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and lavender. The plant produces pendulous, lip-like petals that resemble moth wings, giving it its common name. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
