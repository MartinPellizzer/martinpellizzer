Phyteuma × hegetschweileri

Phyteuma × hegetschweileri, commonly known as the hybrid sunflower, is a perennial plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with lance-shaped leaves that are hairy and serrated. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its botanical features include a robust root system and a long flowering period from late summer to early autumn.
