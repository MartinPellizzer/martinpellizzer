Phyllodium vestitum

Phyllodium vestitum, commonly known as the velvet phyllodium, is a perennial shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, also known as the legume or pea family. The plant is characterized by its narrow, leathery leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, appearing in shades of pink or purple.
