Pennisetum indicum subvar. parviflorum

Pennisetum indicum subvar. parviflorum, commonly known as dwarf fountain grass, is a compact, ornamental grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, fluffy white or pale pink flowers during the summer months. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. It is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.

Its feathery appearance adds a soft, textural element to landscapes.
