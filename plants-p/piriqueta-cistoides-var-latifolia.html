Piriqueta cistoides var. latifolia

Piriqueta cistoides var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and fleshy, aromatic leaves. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
