Platycoryne ambigua

Platycoryne ambigua, commonly known as the ambiguous flat-lobed orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, flat-lobed petals and sepals. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its flowers are small and often have a delicate, fragrant aroma.

Due to its habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many regions.
