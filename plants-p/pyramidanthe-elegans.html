Pyramidanthe elegans

Pyramidanthe elegans, commonly known as the elegant pyramidanthe, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically blooms during the late summer and early autumn months. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern around the stems.

Pyramidanthe elegans is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
