Piper subtextum

Piper subtextum, commonly known as the slender pepper vine, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many pepper species. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and produces small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruit is a three-lobed, fleshy berry that turns red when mature.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
