Perovskia kudrjaschevii

Perovskia kudrjaschevii, commonly known as Russian blue geranium, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, blue-violet flowers and silver-green foliage. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.
