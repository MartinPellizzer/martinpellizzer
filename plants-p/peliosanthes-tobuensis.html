Peliosanthes tobuensis

Peliosanthes tobuensis, commonly known as the Japanese arrowroot.

It is a flowering plant native to Japan, belonging to the Araceae family. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces striking, arrow-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically small and clustered in an inflorescence called a spadix, surrounded by a colorful spathe.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and unique floral structure.
