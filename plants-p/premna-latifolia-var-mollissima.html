Premna latifolia var. mollissima

Premna latifolia var. mollissima, commonly known as silky rubber vine, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are covered in a dense, silky white pubescence, giving it a soft, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in terminal spikes, followed by small, fleshy berries. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various soil types.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
