Pleurothallis aechme

Pleurothallis aechme, commonly known as the black orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

This epiphytic plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, intricately shaped flowers that often display a dark coloration. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, loose substrates for optimal growth. The plant's pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, enabling it to survive in its natural habitat with minimal rainfall.

Its unique appearance and rarity make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
