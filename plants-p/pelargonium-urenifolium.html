Pelargonium urenifolium

Pelargonium urenifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and showy, five-petaled flowers. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Pelargonium urenifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of garden settings.
