Paracaleana gracilicordata

Paracaleana gracilicordata, commonly known as the slender-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical rainforests of northern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, strap-like leaves and produces striking, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive hooded labellum. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and is often found in association with other orchid species. Its root system is fibrous, and it relies on specific mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient uptake.

Paracaleana gracilicordata is a rare and specialized species, making it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
