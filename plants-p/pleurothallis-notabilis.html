Pleurothallis notabilis

Pleurothallis notabilis, commonly known as the showy pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually small to medium in size, with a wide range of colors including white, yellow, and pink.

Pleurothallis notabilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and unique appearance.
