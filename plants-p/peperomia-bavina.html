Peperomia bavina

Peperomia bavina, commonly known as the Cape Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia bavina is prized for its low maintenance and ornamental appeal in both garden and pot settings.
