Pelargonium razoumovianum

Pelargonium razoumovianum, commonly known as the Razoumovian geranium, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant features serrated, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is a popular choice among gardeners.
