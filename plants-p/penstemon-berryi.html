Penstemon berryi

Penstemon berryi, commonly known as the Sierra penstemon, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in open habitats such as meadows and slopes. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in a fine layer of hair.

Penstemon berryi is adapted to arid conditions and is often found in high elevation environments.
