Philippiamra salsoloides

Philippiamra salsoloides, commonly known as the sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub with gray-green foliage and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions. It has a compact growth habit, often forming dense mats that help prevent soil erosion.

Its ecological importance lies in providing habitat and forage for various desert wildlife species.
