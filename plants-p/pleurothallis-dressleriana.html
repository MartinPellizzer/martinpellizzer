Pleurothallis dressleriana

Pleurothallis dressleriana, commonly known as the Dressler's orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, often with intricate patterns. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and surrounding substrate.

Due to its beauty and rarity, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
