Pteris schlechteri

Pteris schlechteri, commonly known as the Schlechter's water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Pteridaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries such as Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its delicate, pinnately divided fronds that are typically light green in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its rhizomes are creeping and produce numerous spore-bearing sorus clusters on the underside of the fronds.
