Polystachya mafingensis

Polystachya mafingensis, commonly known as the Mafingensis spikegrass, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces dense clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, contributing to its overall appearance.

Polystachya mafingensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
