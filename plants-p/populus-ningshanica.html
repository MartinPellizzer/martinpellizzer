Populus ningshanica

Populus ningshanica, commonly known as Ningshan poplar, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Salicaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and distinctive bark. The plant is typically found in mountainous regions and is adapted to temperate climates. It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.

Its ornamental value also makes it a popular choice for landscaping in certain areas.
