Pleurothallis edwallii

Pleurothallis edwallii, commonly known as the Edwall's orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and green. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually narrow and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
