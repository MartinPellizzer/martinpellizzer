Panicum asiaticum

Panicum asiaticum, commonly known as Asian panicgrass, is a warm-season grass native to Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and small, spike-like inflorescences that produce numerous tiny seeds. This plant is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions. Its fibrous root system helps prevent soil erosion, making it useful in ecological restoration projects.

It is also sometimes cultivated as a forage crop for livestock.
