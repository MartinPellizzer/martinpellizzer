Paederia Olens

Paederia olens is a tropical climbing plant known for its striking heart-shaped leaves and fragrant flowers. Native to regions across Southeast Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. This species is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners and horticulturists. Despite its beauty, it can be invasive in certain ecosystems if not managed properly.
