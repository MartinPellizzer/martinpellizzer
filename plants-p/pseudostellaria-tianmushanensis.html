Pseudostellaria tianmushanensis

Pseudostellaria tianmushanensis, commonly known as Tianmushan starwort, is a rare flowering plant native to the Tianmushan region in China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers and hairy leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or forested areas, preferring well-drained soils. It is noted for its unique morphology and is of interest to botanists due to its limited distribution.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
