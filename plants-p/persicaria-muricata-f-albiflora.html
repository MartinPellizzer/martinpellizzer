Persicaria muricata f. albiflora

Persicaria muricata f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered smartweed, is a cultivar of the common smartweed.

It is characterized by its striking white flowers that contrast with its dark green foliage. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in water gardens or as a landscape plant.
