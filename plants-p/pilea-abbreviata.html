Pilea abbreviata

Pilea abbreviata, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It features glossy, round leaves that are typically a deep green, giving it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters, followed by round, greenish fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate moisture.

Pilea abbreviata is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.
