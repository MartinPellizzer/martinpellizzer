Piper cyrtostachys

Piper cyrtostachys, commonly known as the slender pipevine, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its small, opposite leaves and clusters of tiny, greenish-white flowers. It produces small, dark blue berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.

The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
