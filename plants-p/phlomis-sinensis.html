Phlomis sinensis

Phlomis sinensis, commonly known as Chinese torchflower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its tall, upright stems and clusters of bright yellow or orange flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a fuzzy texture.

It is often used in traditional Chinese medicine and as an ornamental plant in gardens.
