Panicum microthyrsum

Panicum microthyrsum, commonly known as little barley grass, is a low-growing annual grass native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny grains. The plant is characterized by its fine, delicate texture and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides. It has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its botanical features make it well-suited for ornamental use in gardens and landscapes.
