Panicum virescens

Panicum virescens, commonly known as green panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a member of the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, cylindrical inflorescence. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and often have a bluish-green hue, contributing to its aesthetic appeal.

It is also valued for its use in ornamental gardening and as a forage crop.
