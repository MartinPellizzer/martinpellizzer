Protium duckei

Protium duckei, commonly known as the Malayan ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hard, dense wood, which is highly valued for its durability and strength. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, followed by spherical fruits that contain a single seed.

Protium duckei plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
