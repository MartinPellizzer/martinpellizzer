Peperomia rubricaulis var. parvifolia

Peperomia rubricaulis var. parvifolia, commonly known as the red-stemmed peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features small, glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish tinge on the stems. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its attractive foliage and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
