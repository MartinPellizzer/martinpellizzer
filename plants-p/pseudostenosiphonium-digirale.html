Pseudostenosiphonium digirale

Pseudostenosiphonium digirale, commonly known as the tropical orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, often with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Pseudostenosiphonium digirale is valued for its ornamental beauty and is commonly cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
