Pennisetum gossweileri

Pennisetum gossweileri, commonly known as the silver fountain grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to South Africa.

It features tall, slender spikes of silvery-white flowers that stand out against its dark green foliage. The plant has a clumping growth habit and is known for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in full sun. Its feathery plumes add a dramatic visual element to gardens and landscapes.

Pennisetum gossweileri is also valued for its low maintenance requirements and adaptability to various soil types.
