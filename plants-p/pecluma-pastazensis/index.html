Pecluma Pastazensis

Pecluma pastazensis is a rare and unique plant species native to the cloud forests of Ecuador. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is known for its striking foliage. This plant has been largely overlooked by scientific research due to its limited distribution. Its discovery has sparked interest among botanists and conservationists. Protecting Pecluma pastazensis is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its fragile ecosystem.
