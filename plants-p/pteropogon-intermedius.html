Pteropogon intermedius

Pteropogon intermedius, commonly known as the intermediate pteropogon, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Poaceae, which includes grasses.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves that are typically green in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in inflorescences, which are often clustered at the tips of the stems.
