Phaseolus praecox

Phaseolus praecox, commonly known as early bean or dwarf bean, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to the Americas and is characterized by its climbing habit, with trifoliate leaves and small, pod-like fruits. The plant produces yellow flowers and is typically found in temperate regions. It is valued for its edible beans, which are rich in protein and nutrients.

Its botanical features make it an important crop in agricultural systems.
