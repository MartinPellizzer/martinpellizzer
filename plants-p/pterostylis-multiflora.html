Pterostylis multiflora

Pterostylis multiflora, commonly known as the multiflora greenhood, is an orchid species native to eastern Australia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a slender, leafless stem that bears a single spike of flowers. The flowers are greenish-yellow with a hood-like structure, and they are typically pollinated by thynnine wasps. It grows in moist, shaded habitats such as rainforests and understoreys.

The plant is noted for its unique morphology and ecological significance in its native environment.
