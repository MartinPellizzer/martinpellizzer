Polystichum orbiculatum var. boboense

Polystichum orbiculatum var. boboense, commonly known as the Bobo Shield Fern, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a member of the Dryopteridaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, orbicular-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, with a prominent central vein and serrated margins.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
