Peperomia conulifera var. matoubana

Peperomia conulifera var. matoubana, commonly known as the Matouba peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and red, which are followed by decorative, spathate berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

This variety is popular among indoor gardeners for its ornamental foliage and low maintenance requirements.
