Phyllanthus microdendron var. microdendron

Phyllanthus microdendron var. microdendron, commonly known as the small-leaf phyllanthus, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, rounded leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, spherical berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along riverbanks.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological and horticultural contexts.
