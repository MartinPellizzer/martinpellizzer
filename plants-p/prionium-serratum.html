Prionium serratum

Prionium serratum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved flax, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygonaceae, which includes plants such as buckwheat and sorrel. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. Its small, greenish-white flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in late summer.
