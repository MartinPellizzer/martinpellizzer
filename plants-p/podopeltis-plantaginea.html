Podopeltis plantaginea

Podopeltis plantaginea, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture, giving it its common name. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters.

The species is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.
