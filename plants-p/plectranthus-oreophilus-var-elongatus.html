Plectranthus oreophilus var. elongatus

Plectranthus oreophilus var. elongatus, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and elongated leaves that are often variegated with silver and green. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of violet, pink, and white, which bloom throughout the year in warm climates. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

This variety is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and ease of care.
