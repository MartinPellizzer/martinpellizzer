Pristimera polyantha

Pristimera polyantha, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forests and stream sides. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth texture.

Pristimera polyantha is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
