Peperomia epilobioides var. glabrilimba

Peperomia epilobioides var. glabrilimba, commonly known as the smooth-leaved peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features lance-shaped, glossy dark green leaves that are smooth to the touch, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically followed by reddish-brown berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it suitable for both container and garden settings.
