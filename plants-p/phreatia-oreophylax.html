Phreatia oreophylax

Phreatia oreophylax, commonly known as the mountain phreatia, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on tree trunks or in rocky crevices. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and soil, which is essential for its survival in its natural habitat.

Phreatia oreophylax is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated by enthusiasts of tropical flora.
