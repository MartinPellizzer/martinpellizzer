Pachyrhizus orbicularis

Pachyrhizus orbicularis, commonly known as the broadleaf peanut, is a leguminous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and produces edible tubers that are rich in protein and starch. The plant has a deep root system that helps it thrive in poor soil conditions. It is also known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, improving soil fertility.

Its botanical features make it an important crop in tropical and subtropical regions.
