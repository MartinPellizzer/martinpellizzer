Podocarpus chinensis var. aureus

Podocarpus chinensis var. aureus, commonly known as the golden podocarp, is a shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage that exhibits a striking golden-yellow color, especially in full sun. The leaves are leathery and have a distinctive elliptical shape with a pointed tip. It produces small, cone-like structures that contain seeds, typical of the Podocarpaceae family.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adaptable to a variety of soil types.
