Protea vidua

Protea vidua, commonly known as the silver protea, is a species native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, fluffy inflorescence. The plant typically grows in coastal and sandy environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its flowers are white to pale pink, with a unique, brush-like appearance.

Protea vidua is valued for its ornamental qualities and plays a role in local ecosystems.
