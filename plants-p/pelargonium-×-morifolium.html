Pelargonium × morifolium

Pelargonium × morifolium, commonly known as the common geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, lobed leaves and showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a potted plant. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it adaptable to various growing conditions.

Pelargonium × morifolium is also known for its ability to thrive in indoor environments, making it a popular choice for houseplants.
