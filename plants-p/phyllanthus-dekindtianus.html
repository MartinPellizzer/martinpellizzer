Phyllanthus dekindtianus

Phyllanthus dekindtianus, commonly known as the African pear or wild pear, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are often elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by edible, pear-shaped fruits that are typically yellow or orange when mature.
