Pandanus subglobosus

Pandanus subglobosus, commonly known as the globular pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its compact, spherical shape and thick, fleshy leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically clustered at the base of the leaf rosette. Its unique morphology makes it a popular ornamental plant in tropical gardens.

The species is also valued for its ability to thrive in coastal and sandy environments.
