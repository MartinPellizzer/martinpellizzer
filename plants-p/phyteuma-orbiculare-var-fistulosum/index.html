Phyteuma Orbiculare Var. Fistulosum

Phyteuma orbiculare var. fistulosum is a unique and striking plant known for its vibrant purple and white flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in rocky and alpine environments. This variant stands out due to its distinctive tubular flowers and compact growth habit. It is often prized by gardeners for its ornamental value and hardy nature. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively rare and underappreciated in the botanical world.
