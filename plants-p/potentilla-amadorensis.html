Potentilla amadorensis

Potentilla amadorensis, commonly known as Amadore cinquefoil, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the high elevations of the Andes in South America, particularly in regions of Peru and Bolivia. The plant is characterized by its small, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, demonstrating a preference for well-drained soils.
