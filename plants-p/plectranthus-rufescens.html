Plectranthus rufescens

Plectranthus rufescens, commonly known as the red-stemmed plectranthus, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features aromatic, opposite leaves that are often grayish-green with a fuzzy texture. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of purple or pink, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a versatile addition to both ornamental and medicinal plant collections.
