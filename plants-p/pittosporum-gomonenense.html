Pittosporum gomonenense

Pittosporum gomonenense, commonly known as the Kauai pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often followed by small, round berries. The plant has simple, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green on top, with a lighter underside. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is typically found in coastal or lowland habitats.

Pittosporum gomonenense is valued for its ornamental qualities and plays a role in the ecological diversity of its native environment.
