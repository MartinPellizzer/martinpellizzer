Pleurothallis luna-crescens

Pleurothallis luna-crescens, commonly known as the Moonlight Orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that thrives in shaded, humid environments with high air circulation. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, often resembling moonlight. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and surrounding substrate.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
