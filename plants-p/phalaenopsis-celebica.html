Phalaenopsis celebica

Phalaenopsis celebica, commonly known as the Celebes phalaenopsis, is a species of orchid native to Indonesia.

It is characterized by its elegant, long-lasting flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in tropical climates. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often arranged in a fan-like pattern.

This orchid is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
