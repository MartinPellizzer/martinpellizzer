Pistolochia ussuriensis

Pistolochia ussuriensis, commonly known as the Ussuri pistolpod, is a climbing shrub native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, pistol-shaped pods. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

The seeds are enclosed in hard, pod-like structures that split open when mature.
