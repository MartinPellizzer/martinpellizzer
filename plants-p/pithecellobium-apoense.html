Pithecellobium apoense

Pithecellobium apoense, commonly known as the Philippine legume tree, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces edible pods that contain seeds used in traditional medicine and as a food source. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
