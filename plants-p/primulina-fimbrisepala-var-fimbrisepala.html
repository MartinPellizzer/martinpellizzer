Primulina fimbrisepala var. fimbrisepala

Primulina fimbrisepala var. fimbrisepala, commonly known as the velvety primrose.

This species is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves with a distinctive wavy edge. The plant produces small, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining, rich soil.

Its unique foliage and elegant flowers make it a popular choice for indoor cultivation and botanical gardens.
