Parasenecio auriculatus var. matsumurana

Parasenecio auriculatus var. matsumurana, commonly known as the Matsumura's auriculate parasenecio, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped with auriculate (ear-like) bases, and it produces small, yellowish to white flowers.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
