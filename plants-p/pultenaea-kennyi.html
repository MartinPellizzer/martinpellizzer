Pultenaea kennyi

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowers are usually small and yellow or reddish in color, attracting various pollinators.
