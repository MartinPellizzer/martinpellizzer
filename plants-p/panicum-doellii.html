Panicum doellii

Panicum doellii, commonly known as Doell's panicgrass, is a species of grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is a perennial plant that typically grows in wetland areas and along the edges of streams. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in moist to wet conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
