Poa siphonoglossa

Poa siphonoglossa, commonly known as the slender meadowgrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern South America.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and produces slender, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, grassy habitats such as meadows and riverbanks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Poa siphonoglossa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in wetland environments.
