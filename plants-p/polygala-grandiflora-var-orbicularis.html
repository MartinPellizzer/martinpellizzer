Polygala grandiflora var. orbicularis

Polygala grandiflora var. orbicularis, commonly known as the broad-leaf milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its orbicular (rounded) leaves and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. It belongs to the family Polygalaceae, which includes many species with distinctive floral structures.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and bloom.
