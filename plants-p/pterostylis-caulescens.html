Pterostylis caulescens

Pterostylis caulescens, commonly known as the tall helmet orchid, is an epiphytic plant native to eastern Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, helmet-shaped flower. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, relying on its roots for moisture and nutrients. Its flowers are usually green or yellowish, with a single, prominent lip that attracts pollinators.

Pterostylis caulescens is known for its resilience in shaded, humid environments.
