Pennisetum spicatum var. longipedunculatum

Pennisetum spicatum var. longipedunculatum, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender stems with long, drooping inflorescences that produce delicate purple plumes. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its striking appearance and drought tolerance make it a versatile addition to both residential and commercial landscapes.
