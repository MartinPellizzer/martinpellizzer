Polystachya kathriniae

Polystachya kathriniae, commonly known as Kathrinia's spike grass, is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are long and narrow, often with a waxy coating that helps retain moisture.

Polystachya kathriniae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique appearance.
