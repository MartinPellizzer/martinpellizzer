Polygala glaucocarpa

Polygala glaucocarpa, commonly known as the blue-leaf milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, including China and Japan.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually pale blue or white in color. Its leaves are narrow and often have a bluish-green hue, contributing to its common name.

The species is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
