Pyrethrum simulans

Pyrethrum simulans, commonly known as the African pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant produces small, glandular structures called pyrethrum, which contain natural insecticides. Pyrethrum simulans thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for cultivation in temperate climates.

Its ornamental value and pesticidal properties make it both aesthetically pleasing and useful in organic gardening.
