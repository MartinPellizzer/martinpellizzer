Potentilla plattensis

Potentilla plattensis, commonly known as Platt's cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, and its leaves are usually trifoliate, with three leaflets. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in dry, well-drained soils.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
