Pholidota advena

Pholidota advena, commonly known as the African silver bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats. It produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds.

Pholidota advena is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
