Philotheca tomentella

Philotheca tomentella, commonly known as the woolly philotheca, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in loose inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland and rainforest habitats.

Philotheca tomentella is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
