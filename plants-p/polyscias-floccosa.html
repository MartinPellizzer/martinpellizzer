Polyscias floccosa

Polyscias floccosa, commonly known as the fuzzy-leaf polycarpic, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.

Polyscias floccosa is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
