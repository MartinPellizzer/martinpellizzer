Pulicaria aualites

Pulicaria aualites, commonly known as the silverleaf sandmat, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in sandy and rocky soils. The plant features silver-gray foliage due to a dense covering of fine hairs. Its small yellow flowers appear in clusters during the summer months.

It is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
