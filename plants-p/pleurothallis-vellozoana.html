Pleurothallis vellozoana

Pleurothallis vellozoana, commonly known as the Vellozoana orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in warm, moist conditions with good air circulation.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
