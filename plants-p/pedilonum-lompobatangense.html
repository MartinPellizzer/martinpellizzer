Pedilonum lompobatangense

Pedilonum lompobatangense, commonly known as the Lompobatang spider orchid, is a rare and endemic species found exclusively in the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spider-like inflorescence. The plant has small, delicate flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments within the montane forests of its native region.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
