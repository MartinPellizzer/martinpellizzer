Polygala crotalarioides var. glabrescens

Polygala crotalarioides var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smoothleaf milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, often found in grasslands and rocky soils. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are usually smooth and lance-shaped, contributing to its varietal name.

This species is adapted to drought conditions and plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat.
