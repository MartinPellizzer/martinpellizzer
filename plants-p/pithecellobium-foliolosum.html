Pithecellobium foliolosum

Pithecellobium foliolosum, commonly known as the small-leaf pisonia, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, warm climates. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves, which are often divided into small, ovate leaflets. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with a distinctive spurred structure, and it produces small, edible pods that contain seeds.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
