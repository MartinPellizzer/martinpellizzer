Primula × purpurascens

Primula × purpurascens, commonly known as the purple primrose, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Primulaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple to pink flowers, which typically bloom in early spring. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in cool, moist environments.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
