Piper sampaioi

Piper sampaioi, commonly known as Sampaio's pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its vine-like growth habit and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant produces small, peppery fruits that are used in traditional medicine and cuisine. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological and pharmacological research.
