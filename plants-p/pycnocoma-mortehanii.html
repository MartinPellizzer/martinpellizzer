Pycnocoma mortehanii

Pycnocoma mortehanii, commonly known as the Hottentot's beard, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It features a dense, hairy stem that resembles a beard, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its compact growth habit and distinctive appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
