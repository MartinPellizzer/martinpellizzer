Phillyrea obtusata

Phillyrea obtusata, commonly known as the narrowleaf Phillyrea, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, dark green foliage with elliptical leaves that are typically narrow and leathery. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during the spring, which are followed by yellowish-orange berries. Phillyrea obtusata is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and woodland habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in Mediterranean climates.
