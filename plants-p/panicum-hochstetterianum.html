Panicum hochstetterianum

Panicum hochstetterianum, commonly known as the silvergrass, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its slender, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces delicate, fluffy seed heads that resemble small clouds, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored plant in both natural and cultivated settings.
