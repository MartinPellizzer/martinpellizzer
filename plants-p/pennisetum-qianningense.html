Pennisetum qianningense

Pennisetum qianningense, commonly known as the Qianning grass, is a species of grass native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. This plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks. Pennisetum qianningense is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological restoration projects.

It is also used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
