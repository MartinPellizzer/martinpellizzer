Pilea angustata

Pilea angustata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pilea, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Urticaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage.

Pilea angustata is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
