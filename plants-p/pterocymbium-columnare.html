Pterocymbium columnare

Pterocymbium columnare, commonly known as the columnar orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, unbranched stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with a prominent central column. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Pterocymbium columnare is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor settings.
