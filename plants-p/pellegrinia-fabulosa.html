Pellegrinia fabulosa

Pellegrinia fabulosa, commonly known as the fairy lantern, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues, especially in full sun. The plant produces vibrant orange or yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a dramatic contrast to its foliage. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements make it a desirable ornamental plant.
