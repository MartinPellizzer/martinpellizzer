Pyrethrum discoideum

Pyrethrum discoideum, commonly known as the daisy fleabane, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its daisy-like appearance with white petals and a yellow center. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. Its flowers bloom in the summer and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Pyrethrum discoideum is also used in herbal medicine for its mild sedative properties.
