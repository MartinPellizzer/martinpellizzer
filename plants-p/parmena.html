Parmena

Parmena, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a soft, velvety texture. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its capsule fruits contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Parmena is often found in disturbed soils and is considered an invasive species in some regions.
