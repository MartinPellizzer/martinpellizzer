Plesmonium nobile

Plesmonium nobile, commonly known as the noble plesmonium, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of bell-shaped, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or alpine settings.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in horticulture.
