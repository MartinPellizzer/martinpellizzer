Peperomia choroniana var. heterodoxa

Peperomia choroniana var. heterodoxa, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, often adorned with silver or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
