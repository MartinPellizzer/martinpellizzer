Piper mannii var. glabrum

Piper mannii var. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth pepper vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and heart-shaped leaves with a glossy green surface. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by elongated, dark purple berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in some regions for both horticultural and traditional uses.
