Pabstiella hileiaensis

Pabstiella hileiaensis, commonly known as the Hileia rock cress, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes.
