Peperomia lilliputiana

Peperomia lilliputiana, commonly known as the miniature peperomia, is a small, compact plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically green with a subtle sheen, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink. This plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal water needs.

Its unique size and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for container gardening and as a tabletop plant.
