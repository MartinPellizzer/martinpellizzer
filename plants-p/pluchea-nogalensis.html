Pluchea nogalensis

Pluchea nogalensis, commonly known as the Mexican groundcherry, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It produces small, yellow-orange fruits that are often used in traditional medicine.
