Pleioblastus mollissimus

Pleioblastus mollissimus, commonly known as the silky bamboo or velvet bamboo, is a species of grass native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, feathery foliage that gives it a lush, ornamental appearance. The plant produces slender, green shoots that grow in dense clumps, making it suitable for landscaping and erosion control. Its leaves are narrow and have a fine, silky texture, contributing to its aesthetic appeal.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental horticulture for its visual elegance.
