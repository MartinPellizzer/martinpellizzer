Pultenaea muelleri var. reflexifolia

Pultenaea muelleri var. reflexifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pultenaea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, often zigzagging stems and small, elliptical leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The flowers are usually yellow or orange, arranged in dense, cylindrical clusters, and bloom in spring. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open forests or heathlands.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
