Polystichum Aculeatum Var. Scopulinum

Polystichum aculeatum var. scopulinum is a distinctive fern known for its unique appearance and ecological significance. This variety of the common holly fern features dark green, lance-shaped fronds with a serrated edge. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas. Its ability to tolerate poor soil conditions makes it a resilient plant in diverse habitats. The plant's striking foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and conservation efforts.
