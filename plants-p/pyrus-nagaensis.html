Pyrus nagaensis

Pyrus nagaensis, commonly known as the Naga pear, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to the eastern Himalayas, specifically found in regions of India and Myanmar. Its fruit, a small pear-like fruit, is edible but not widely cultivated for commercial purposes.
