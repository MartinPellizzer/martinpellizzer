Piper palawanum

Piper palawanum, commonly known as the Palawan pepper or Palawan long pepper, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with opposite, ovate leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruit is a drupe that matures to a dark red color, containing a single seed.

It is often grown for its aromatic and medicinal properties.
