Potentilla matsumurae f. glabrescens

Potentilla matsumurae f. glabrescens, commonly known as the glabrous potentilla, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically features small, ovate leaves with a smooth, glabrous surface, which distinguishes it from other forms of Potentilla matsumurae. It is native to Japan and thrives in well-drained soils in full sun.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
