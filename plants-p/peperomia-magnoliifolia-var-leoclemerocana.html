Peperomia magnoliifolia var. leoclemerocana

Peperomia magnoliifolia var. leoclemerocana, commonly known as the velvetleaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically followed by small, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with consistent moisture.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
