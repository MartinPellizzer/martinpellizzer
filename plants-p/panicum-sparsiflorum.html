Panicum sparsiflorum

Panicum sparsiflorum, commonly known as the sparseflower panicgrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas, where it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous slender, pale green to golden flowers. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration that adds visual interest to gardens.

Panicum sparsiflorum is often used in ornamental horticulture for its attractive appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
