Pentaphylax euryoides

Pentaphylax euryoides, commonly known as the silverleaf pentaphylax, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the winter months. Its leaves are leathery and covered in a fine silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and seasonal blooms.
