Polemonium schmidtii

Polemonium schmidtii, commonly known as Schmidt's Jacob's ladder, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features upright, branching stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, wooded areas and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a desirable addition to native plant gardens.
