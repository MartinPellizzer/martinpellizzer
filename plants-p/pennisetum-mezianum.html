Pennisetum mezianum

Pennisetum mezianum, commonly known as the blue oat grass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-blue foliage and fluffy, oat-like seed heads that appear in late summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It prefers well-drained soil and is drought-tolerant once established.

Pennisetum mezianum is often used in ornamental landscaping for its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
