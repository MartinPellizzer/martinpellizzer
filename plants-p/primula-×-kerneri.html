Primula × kerneri

Primula × kerneri, commonly known as the Japanese primrose, is a hybrid species within the Primulaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant features broad, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

The flowers are usually borne on short pedicels, adding to the plant's visual appeal.
