Polypodium cavaleriei

Polypodium cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese water fern, is a species of fern native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically bioluminescent in the dark. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions. Its rhizomes are used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant's unique appearance and ecological role make it an important species in its native habitats.
