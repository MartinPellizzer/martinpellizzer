Piper limosum

Piper limosum, commonly known as swamp pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its small, opposite leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.

Piper limosum is often found in wetland or swampy habitats, where it thrives in moist, shady conditions.
