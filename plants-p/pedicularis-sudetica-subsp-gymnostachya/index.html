Pedicularis Sudetica Subsp. Gymnostachya

Pedicularis sudetica subsp. gymnostachya is a rare and striking member of the Orobanchaceae family native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe. This subspecies is distinguished by its vivid red flowers and unique morphological features that set it apart from its relatives. It thrives in nutrient-poor soils and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats. Due to its limited distribution and ecological sensitivity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this rare botanical treasure.
