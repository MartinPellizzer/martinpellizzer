Polystemma cordatum

Polystemma cordatum, commonly known as the heartleaf polystemma, is a small, herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically glossy and arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions.

Polystemma cordatum is often found in the understory of temperate deciduous forests.
