Pteris kilimensis

Pteris kilimensis, commonly known as the Kilimendjik fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Pteridaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines. This fern is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically divided into narrow segments. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often growing in humid tropical forests.

Its spores are produced on the underside of its fronds, contributing to its reproductive strategy in the wild.
