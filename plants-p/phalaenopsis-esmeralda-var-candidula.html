Phalaenopsis esmeralda var. candidula

Phalaenopsis esmeralda var. candidula, commonly known as the White Orchid, is a hybrid orchid prized for its elegant, white blooms.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia, where it thrives in tropical climates. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. It is a sympodial orchid, meaning it grows in a horizontal pattern with multiple stems.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its long-lasting flowers and ease of care.
