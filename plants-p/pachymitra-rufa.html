Pachymitra rufa

Pachymitra rufa, commonly known as the red-leaved pachymitra, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often reddish in color, which help it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
