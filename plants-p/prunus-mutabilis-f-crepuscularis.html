Prunus mutabilis f. crepuscularis

Prunus mutabilis f. crepuscularis, commonly known as the twilight cherry, is a unique cultivar of the Prunus genus.

It is characterized by its striking color change, transitioning from white to pink and then to deep red as the flowers mature. The plant produces small, fragrant blossoms that appear in early spring. It is a deciduous shrub or small tree, typically reaching heights of 3 to 6 meters.

The leaves are simple, alternate, and have a serrated margin, adding to its ornamental value.
