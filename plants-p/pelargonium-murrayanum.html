Pelargonium murrayanum

Pelargonium murrayanum, commonly known as the Murray's geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, clustered flowers. The plant typically produces bright pink to red blooms during the spring and summer seasons. Its leaves are usually rounded and have a slightly hairy texture.

Pelargonium murrayanum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
