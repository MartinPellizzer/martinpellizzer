Pittosporum polyspermum var. leianthum

Pittosporum polyspermum var. leianthum, commonly known as the Kauai Pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to Hawaii.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and have a pleasant, spicy scent when crushed.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland habitats.
