Panicum nitidum var. ensifolium

Panicum nitidum var. ensifolium, commonly known as the silverleaf panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-gray foliage and delicate, feather-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and have a smooth, waxy surface that reflects light, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
