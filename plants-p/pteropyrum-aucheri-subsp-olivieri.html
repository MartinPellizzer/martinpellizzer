Pteropyrum aucheri subsp. olivieri

Pteropyrum aucheri subsp.

olivieri, commonly known as the olive-leaved pteropyrum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color, covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value lies in its attractive foliage and subtle floral display.
