Pteris mohasiensis

Pteris mohasiensis, commonly known as the Mohasi water fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in wet, shaded environments such as marshes and along stream banks. Its rhizomes are often submerged, allowing it to thrive in aquatic or semi-aquatic habitats.

The fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, making it a distinctive and ornamental plant.
