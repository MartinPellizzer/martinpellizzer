Parsonsia comptonii

Parsonsia comptonii, commonly known as the Compton's flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling flames. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate or elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in warm climates.

Parsonsia comptonii is also valued for its ability to attract birds and butterflies due to its nectar-rich flowers.
