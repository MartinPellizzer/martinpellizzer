Piper lanceifolium

Piper lanceifolium, commonly known as the lanceleaf pepper or Indian pepper, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that grow in clusters. Its fruit, a small, three-lobed berry, contains the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and cuisine.

The plant thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland forests.
