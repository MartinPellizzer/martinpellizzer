Peperomia ekakesara

Peperomia ekakesara, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, providing a striking visual appeal. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, which are not particularly showy. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia ekakesara is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.
