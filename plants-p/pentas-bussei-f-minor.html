Pentas bussei f. minor

Pentas bussei f. minor, commonly known as the dwarf pentas, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, depending on the variety. The plant has dense, dark green foliage that provides a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often used in ornamental gardening for its ornate appearance.

Due to its compact size, it is well-suited for container growing and small garden spaces.
