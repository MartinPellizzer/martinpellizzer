Pseudoxalis madrensis

Pseudoxalis madrensis, commonly known as the Mexican snowflake, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers. Pseudoxalis madrensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
