Polygala rupicola

Polygala rupicola, commonly known as the rock polygala, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or dry soils, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments. The plant features small, delicate flowers that are usually pink or purple in color, arranged in clusters. It belongs to the family Polygalaceae, which is characterized by its unique floral structures.

Polygala rupicola is often found in coastal areas and limestone formations, where it thrives in full sun.
