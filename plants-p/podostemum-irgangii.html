Podostemum irgangii

Podostemum irgangii, commonly known as the African water fern, is a unique aquatic plant found in the fast-flowing streams of Central and Eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, floating leaves that are adapted to thrive in turbulent water environments. The plant has specialized roots that anchor it to rocks and provide stability in strong currents. Its flowers are typically small and inconspicuous, with a short flowering period.

Podostemum irgangii plays an important ecological role in its habitat by supporting aquatic insect populations and contributing to the biodiversity of freshwater ecosystems.
