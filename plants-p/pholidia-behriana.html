Pholidia behriana

Pholidia behriana, commonly known as the yellow-leafed hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers with prominent stamens. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
