Panicum munroanum

Panicum munroanum, commonly known as Munro's panicgrass, is a species of grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers.

Panicum munroanum is often found in association with other grassland species and plays a role in maintaining ecological balance in its native environment.
