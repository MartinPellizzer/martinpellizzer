Persoonia angustiflora

Persoonia angustiflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved geebung, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, white or yellowish flowers that appear in clusters. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is found in coastal and inland regions of Western Australia. It produces woody capsules as fruit, which contain numerous small seeds.

Persoonia angustiflora is adapted to dry conditions and is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value.
