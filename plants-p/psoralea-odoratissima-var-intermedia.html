Psoralea odoratissima var. intermedia

Psoralea odoratissima var. intermedia, commonly known as the sweet psoralea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or pale green, attracting pollinators such as bees. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.
