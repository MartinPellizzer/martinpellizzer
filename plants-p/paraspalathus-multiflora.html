Paraspalathus multiflora

Paraspalathus multiflora, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers indirect sunlight. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

The flowers are typically borne on upright stems and bloom throughout the year under suitable conditions.
