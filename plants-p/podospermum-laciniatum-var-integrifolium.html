Podospermum laciniatum var. integrifolium

Podospermum laciniatum var. integrifolium, commonly known as the smooth-leafed podospermum, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth margin, distinguishing it from other varieties. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to cascade from containers or hanging baskets.
