Pelargonium × newshamianum

Pelargonium × newshamianum, commonly known as the Newsham geranium, is a hybrid shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped, serrated leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Pelargonium × newshamianum is also valued for its aromatic foliage and ability to attract pollinators.
