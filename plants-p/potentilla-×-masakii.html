Potentilla × masakii

Potentilla × masakii, commonly known as the Japanese fireweed, is a hybrid shrub native to East Asia.

It features showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. The plant has slender, arching stems and small, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value and hardiness make it a popular choice in horticulture.
