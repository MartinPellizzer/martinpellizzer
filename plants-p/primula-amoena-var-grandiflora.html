Primula amoena var. grandiflora

Primula amoena var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered primrose, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It features rosettes of velvety, dark green leaves and produces clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The flowers bloom in early spring, adding a vibrant touch to alpine gardens. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated for its delicate beauty in horticulture.
