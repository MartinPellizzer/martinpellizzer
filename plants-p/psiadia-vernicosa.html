Psiadia vernicosa

Psiadia vernicosa, commonly known as the glossy leaved daisy, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a basal rosette of glossy, dark green leaves with a waxy texture. The plant produces showy yellow flowers with white ray florets, typically blooming in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Psiadia vernicosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
