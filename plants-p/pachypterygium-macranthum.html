Pachypterygium macranthum

Pachypterygium macranthum, commonly known as the giant winged fern, is a tropical plant characterized by its large, broad leaves and distinctive wing-like structures on its fronds.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is native to regions with high humidity and warm climates. The plant's leaves can grow up to several meters in length, making it a striking feature in its natural habitat. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
