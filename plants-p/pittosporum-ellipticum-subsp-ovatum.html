Pittosporum ellipticum subsp. ovatum

Pittosporum ellipticum subsp.

ovatum, commonly known as the oval-leaf pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand. It features lance-shaped leaves that are usually glossy and dark green, with a leathery texture.

The plant produces fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native ecosystems.
