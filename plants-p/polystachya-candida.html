Polystachya candida

Polystachya candida, commonly known as the white-flowered bamboo orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, bamboo-like stems and clusters of white or pale green flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within forest undergrowth. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Polystachya candida is valued in horticulture for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
