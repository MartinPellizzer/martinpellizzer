Philodendron longistilum

Philodendron longistilum, commonly known as the long-stemmed philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by dark blue to black berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Philodendron longistilum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
