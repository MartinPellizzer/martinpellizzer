Pistorinia hispanica var. flaviflora

Pistorinia hispanica var. flaviflora, commonly known as the Spanish pistorinia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture. This variety is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun exposure to thrive.
