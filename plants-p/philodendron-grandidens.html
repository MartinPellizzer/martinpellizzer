Philodendron grandidens

Philodendron grandidens, commonly known as the giant philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to South America.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length, often with a dark green to almost black coloration. The plant has a robust stem that can climb and trail, making it suitable for hanging baskets or vertical gardens. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Philodendron grandidens is popular as a houseplant due to its dramatic appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
