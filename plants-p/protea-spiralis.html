Protea spiralis

Protea spiralis, commonly known as the spiral protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of petals, which gives it a distinctive and elegant appearance. The plant produces large, showy inflorescences that can reach up to 30 centimeters in height. Its flowers are typically pink to red in color, with a central cluster of stamens that add to its visual appeal.

Protea spiralis thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation, where it plays a role in local ecosystems.
