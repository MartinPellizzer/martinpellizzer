Peperomia lunaria

Peperomia lunaria, commonly known as the moonlight peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically light green with a silvery underside, giving it a distinctive, almost moonlit appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it an ideal choice for indoor cultivation.

Its slow growth and low maintenance requirements make it a popular ornamental plant among foliage enthusiasts.
