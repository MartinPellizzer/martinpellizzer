Pyrethrum Willkommii

Pyrethrum willkommii is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the high altitudes of southern Africa. Known for its vibrant yellow blooms and delicate, fern-like leaves, it captivates botanists and gardeners alike. This species belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and chrysanthemums. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a subject of interest in both conservation and horticultural circles. Despite its beauty, Pyrethrum willkommii remains underappreciated and under-studied in the global plant community.
