Peperomia reflexa f. variipila

Peperomia reflexa f. variipila, commonly known as the Variegated Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves with striking variegation of white and green patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

This ornamental plant is popular for its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
