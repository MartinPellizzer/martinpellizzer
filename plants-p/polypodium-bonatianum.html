Polypodium bonatianum

Polypodium bonatianum, commonly known as the Bonati's sword fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect fronds that are typically divided into lance-shaped segments. The plant produces sporangia on the underside of its fronds, which are clustered in sori covered by a protective indusium. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and conservation efforts.
