Panicum viride var. italicum

Panicum viride var. italicum, commonly known as Italian green panicgrass, is a variant of the green panicgrass species native to southern Europe.

It is a herbaceous perennial grass that typically grows in moist, well-drained soils. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bright green in color. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that release lightweight seeds for wind dispersal.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
