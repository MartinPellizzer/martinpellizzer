Pileostegia viburnoides var. glabrescens

Pileostegia viburnoides var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smoothleaf pileostegia, is a shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the honeysuckle family (Caprifoliaceae) and is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, white to greenish-yellow flowers in umbel-like clusters, followed by red, fleshy berries that are attractive to birds.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges and thickets.
