Plocoglottis angulata

Plocoglottis angulata, commonly known as the angular fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, angular fronds that are often divided into narrow, linear segments. Plocoglottis angulata is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Southeast Asia and the Pacific.
