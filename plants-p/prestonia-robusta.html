Prestonia robusta

Prestonia robusta, commonly known as the robust prestonia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

It is a member of the Apocynaceae family, which includes milkweeds and other ornamental plants. The plant is characterized by its thick, woody stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. Its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers are typically pink or red, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects.

It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
