Potentilla loureironis

Potentilla loureironis, commonly known as the Portuguese firethorn, is a shrub native to the Azores.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has silvery-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It is adapted to grow in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils.

Potentilla loureironis is also noted for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
