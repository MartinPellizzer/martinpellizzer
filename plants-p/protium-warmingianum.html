Protium warmingianum

Protium warmingianum, commonly known as the Warmingia or Warming's protium, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Burseraceae and is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by spherical, woody fruits containing several seeds. It is valued for its aromatic wood and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
