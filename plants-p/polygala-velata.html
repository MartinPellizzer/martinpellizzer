Polygala velata

Polygala velata, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-white flowers that resemble orchids, which contribute to its common name. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, giving it a velvety texture.

Polygala velata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
