Pilea selleana

Pilea selleana, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy, often with a glossy appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is a popular indoor plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.

Pilea selleana thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
