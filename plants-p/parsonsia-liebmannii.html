Parsonsia liebmannii

Parsonsia liebmannii, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange-red flowers that resemble spiders, hence its common name. The plant features strap-like leaves and produces berries that change color as they mature. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal or lowland areas.

Botanically, it belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty.
