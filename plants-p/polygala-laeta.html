Polygala laeta

Polygala laeta, commonly known as the silverleaf milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant has hairy foliage and produces pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and shrublands.

Polygala laeta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
