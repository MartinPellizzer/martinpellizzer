Premna villosa

Premna villosa, commonly known as silky hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a shrub or small tree with opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that are typically followed by small, round, greenish fruits. Its leaves and flowers are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Premna villosa is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
