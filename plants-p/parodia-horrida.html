Parodia horrida

Parodia horrida, commonly known as the "horrid" cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white, radial spines. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10 to 15 centimeters and has a short, thick stem. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange and bloom in the spring.

Parodia horrida is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
