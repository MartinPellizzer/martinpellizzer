Pleurothallis janeirensis var. viridicata

Pleurothallis janeirensis var. viridicata, commonly known as the green-stemmed pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, often pendulous stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically green with a glossy surface. The flowers are usually white or pale green, with a delicate, intricate structure typical of the Pleurothallis genus. This variety is noted for its ability to thrive in shaded, humid environments, often growing as an epiphyte on trees.

It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
