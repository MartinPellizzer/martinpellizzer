Persoonia × angulata

Persoonia × angulata, commonly known as the angular geebung, is a hybrid shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Proteaceae and is characterized by its angular, often spiny stems and clusters of small, white or pink flowers. The plant produces woody, cone-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to coastal and sandy environments.
