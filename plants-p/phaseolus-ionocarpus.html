Phaseolus ionocarpus

Phaseolus ionocarpus, commonly known as the scarlet runner bean, is a climbing legume native to the Americas.

It produces vibrant red or orange flowers and elongated pods that contain edible seeds. The plant thrives in warm climates and requires full sun for optimal growth. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three leaflets that are often hairy in texture.

The species is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its culinary uses.
