Pedicularis muscoides subsp. muscoides

Pedicularis muscoides subsp.

muscoides, commonly known as the musk orchid, is a member of the Orobanchaceae family and is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia. It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow.

The flowers are often visited by bees and other pollinators, contributing to its ecological role in mountain ecosystems.
