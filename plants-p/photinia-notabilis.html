Photinia notabilis

Photinia notabilis, commonly known as the glossy leaf photinia, is a shrub or small tree native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy dark green leaves that provide a striking visual contrast against its red or orange flower clusters. The plant produces small, white flowers that develop into red, fleshy berries, which are an important food source for birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its compact growth habit and seasonal color changes make it a valuable addition to gardens and natural habitats.
