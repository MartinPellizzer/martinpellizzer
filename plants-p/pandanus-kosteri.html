Pandanus kosteri

Pandanus kosteri, commonly known as the Koster's pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are fibrous and have been utilized for making thatch and rope.

Pandanus kosteri thrives in humid, coastal environments and requires well-drained soil.
