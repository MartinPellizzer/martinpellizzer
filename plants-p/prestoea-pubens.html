Prestoea pubens

Prestoea pubens, commonly known as the silverbark or whitebark, is a tropical tree native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its smooth, whitish bark that gives it its common name. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by red, fleshy fruits.

Prestoea pubens is often found in coastal and wetland areas, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
