Polyosma helicioides

Polyosma helicioides, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree with glossy, dark green leaves that have a silvery underside. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
