Pimelea mollis

Pimelea mollis, commonly known as the silky pimelea, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.
