Polycantha

Polycantha, commonly known as the clustered bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its clusters of bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight. Polycantha is native to regions in Europe and parts of Asia, where it thrives in temperate climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking to add visual interest to their landscapes.
