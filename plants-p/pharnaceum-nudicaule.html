Pharnaceum nudicaule

Pharnaceum nudicaule, commonly known as the slender pharnaceum, is a flowering plant native to the southern regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features delicate, upright stems with small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are clustered in dense, terminal spikes and are usually white or pale pink in color.

Pharnaceum nudicaule is characterized by its low-growing habit and is often found in association with other shade-tolerant flora in its native habitat.
