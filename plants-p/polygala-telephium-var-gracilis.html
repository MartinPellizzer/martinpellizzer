Polygala telephium var. gracilis

Polygala telephium var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender milkweed or pink milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, pink to purple flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in dry, rocky habitats. It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.

Its flowers attract various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.
