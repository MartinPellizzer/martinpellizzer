Polypodium wangii

Polypodium wangii, commonly known as the Chinese water fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The fronds are usually bioluminescent, which is a unique feature among ferns.
