Potentilla wallichiana var. robusta

Potentilla wallichiana var. robusta, commonly known as the Himalayan cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often covered in fine hairs.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and vibrant blooms.
