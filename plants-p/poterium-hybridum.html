Poterium hybridum

Poterium hybridum, commonly known as the hybrid marsh mallow, is a herbaceous plant native to wetland habitats in Europe.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, and it produces showy, pink to purple flowers during the summer months. The plant thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils and is often found in marshes, pond edges, and along streams. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in aquatic environments.

Additionally, it has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its soothing properties.
