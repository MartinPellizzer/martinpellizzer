Polystichum mannii

Polystichum mannii, commonly known as the African shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The fronds are bi-pinnate, with a dense arrangement of small, shield-shaped pinnae. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
