Perymenium fayi

Perymenium fayi, commonly known as the fairy's shield, is a rare and unique plant species native to the humid rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that form a protective shield-like structure around its stem. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. Its root system is highly specialized, allowing it to absorb nutrients from the nutrient-poor soil of its habitat.
