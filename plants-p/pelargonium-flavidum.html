Pelargonium flavidum

Pelargonium flavidum, commonly known as the yellow geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and broad, velvety leaves. The plant belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Pelargonium flavidum is known for its long blooming period, adding color to gardens throughout the growing season.
