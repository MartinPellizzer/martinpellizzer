Potentilla chinensis var. latifida

Potentilla chinensis var. latifida, commonly known as broadleaf cinquefoil, is a perennial shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking low-maintenance, attractive plants.
