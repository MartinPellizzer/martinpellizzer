Pleomele Glomerata

Pleomele, scientifically known as Pleomele glomerata, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are thick, fleshy, and often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and green, adding to its ornamental appeal. Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it has become a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens. Its ability to thrive in a variety of conditions makes it a versatile addition to any plant collection.
