Pandanus luzonensis

Pandanus luzonensis, commonly known as the Luzon pandanus, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and long, narrow leaves that form a dense, grass-like foliage. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with fragrant, yellowish flowers. It thrives in coastal areas and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.

Pandanus luzonensis is an important species in local ecosystems and has cultural significance in the region.
