Peperomia epipremnifolia

Peperomia epipremnifolia, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia epipremnifolia is valued for its ornamental foliage and air-purifying qualities.
