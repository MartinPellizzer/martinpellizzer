Peperomia tominana

Peperomia tominana, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features thick, fleshy, waxy leaves that are typically green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, often in shades of white or green. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and drought tolerance.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for decorative purposes in both home and office settings.
