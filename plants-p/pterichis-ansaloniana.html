Pterichis ansaloniana

Pterichis ansaloniana, commonly known as the Florida anise tree, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a slightly waxy texture. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, typically appearing in clusters of three, and are usually white or pale green.

The tree produces fruit that resembles a capsule, containing small, aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine.
