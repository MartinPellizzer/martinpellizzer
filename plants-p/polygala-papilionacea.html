Polygala papilionacea

Polygala papilionacea, commonly known as the butterfly polygala, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, butterfly-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is known for its delicate appearance and unique floral structure. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
