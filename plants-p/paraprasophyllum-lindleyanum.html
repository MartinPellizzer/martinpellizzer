Paraprasophyllum lindleyanum

Paraprasophyllum lindleyanum, commonly known as the Lindley's paraprasophyllum, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant orchid-like flowers. Its botanical features include strap-shaped leaves and a short, erect stem.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in greenhouses and tropical gardens.
