Podochilus densifolius

Podochilus densifolius, commonly known as the dense-leaved orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, dark green foliage. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, often in shades of white or pale yellow.

Podochilus densifolius is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
