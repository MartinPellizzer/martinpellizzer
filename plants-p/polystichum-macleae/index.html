Polystichum Macleae

Polystichum macleae is a rare and striking fern native to the mountainous regions of China. Its lush, dark green fronds form a dense and elegant foliage that makes it a prized addition to any botanical collection. This plant thrives in cool, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture to flourish. Known for its ornamental value, it is often sought after by horticulturists and conservationists alike. Despite its beauty, Polystichum macleae remains under threat due to habitat loss and over-collection.
