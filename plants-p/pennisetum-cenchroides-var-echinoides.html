Pennisetum cenchroides var. echinoides

Pennisetum cenchroides var. echinoides, commonly known as purple lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted clumps and distinctive purple to silver-blue foliage. The plant produces feathery, pinkish to reddish plumes that add ornamental value. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice in landscaping.
