Photinia tushanensis

Photinia tushanensis, commonly known as the Tushan photinia, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its dense, glossy foliage and showy red or pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate to elliptic in shape, and have a smooth texture.

The Tushan photinia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various soil conditions.
