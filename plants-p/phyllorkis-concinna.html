Phyllorkis concinna

Phyllorkis concinna, commonly known as the slender leaf orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It typically grows in heathland and open forests, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a few narrow, linear leaves. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, often with a faintly speckled appearance.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is notable for its delicate and subtle floral display.
