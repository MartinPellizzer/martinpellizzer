Parsonsia Platycentra

Parsonsia platycentra is a rare and striking plant native to the tropical rainforests of Australia. Known for its vibrant, bell-shaped flowers that range from yellow to orange, it captivates botanists and nature enthusiasts alike. This species belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many ornamental and medicinal plants. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of scientific interest and conservation concern. Despite its beauty, Parsonsia platycentra remains under-studied, highlighting the need for further research and protection.
