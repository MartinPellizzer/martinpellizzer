Piper san-joseanum var. tabogense

Piper san-joseanum var. tabogense, commonly known as the Tabogan pepper or Tabogense pepper plant, is a variant of the Piper san-joseanum species native to the Philippines.

It is a flowering plant in the family Piperaceae, characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces small, elongated berries that turn from green to red as they mature. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in botany and horticulture.
