Pittosporum radicans

Pittosporum radicans, commonly known as the creeping pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage that forms a low, spreading mat. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Due to its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil, it is often used in landscaping and erosion control.
