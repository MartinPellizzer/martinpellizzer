Parietaria tibethana

Parietaria tibethana, commonly known as Tibetan wall-ivy, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Tibetan Plateau.

Parietaria tibethana is valued for its resilience and is sometimes used in traditional medicine in the region.
