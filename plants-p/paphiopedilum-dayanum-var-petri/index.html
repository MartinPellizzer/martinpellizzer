Paphiopedilum Dayanum Var. Petri

Dayanum paphiopedilum, scientifically known as Paphiopedilum dayanum var. petri, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. This unique species is prized for its striking, velvety petals and its subtle, long-lasting fragrance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on tree trunks or among mossy rocks. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is now critically endangered, making conservation efforts essential for its survival. Its beauty and rarity have made it a symbol of botanical fascination and ecological importance.
