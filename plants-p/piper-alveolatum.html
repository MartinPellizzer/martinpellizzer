Piper alveolatum

Piper alveolatum, commonly known as the Madagascar pepper or alveolatum pepper, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its small, pale green to white flowers that grow in clusters. Its leaves are typically alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The fruit is a small, three-lobed berry that ripens to a yellowish color.
