Philodendron verapazense

Philodendron verapazense, commonly known as the Verapaz giant philodendron, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light. Its stems are often aerial, allowing it to climb and spread in its natural habitat.

Philodendron verapazense is popular among houseplant enthusiasts due to its striking foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
