Poterium angustifolium

Poterium angustifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved poterium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stems. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers with yellow disc florets surrounded by white to pale purple ray florets. It is native to regions of southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in rocky and sandy soils.

Poterium angustifolium is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along coastal areas.
