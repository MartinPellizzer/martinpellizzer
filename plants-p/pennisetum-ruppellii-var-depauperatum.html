Pennisetum ruppellii var. depauperatum

Pennisetum ruppellii var. depauperatum, commonly known as the silver feather grass, is a compact, ornamental grass native to southern Africa.

It features fine, silvery-white plumes that give it a distinctive, feathery appearance, especially during the flowering season. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often a bluish-green color, adding visual interest throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its low maintenance requirements and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and landscape design.
