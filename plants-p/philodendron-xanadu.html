Philodendron xanadu

Philodendron xanadu, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 12 inches in length, featuring a deep green color with a waxy texture. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ability to purify the air and its striking foliage.

Philodendron xanadu is also known for its rapid growth and adaptability to various indoor environments.
