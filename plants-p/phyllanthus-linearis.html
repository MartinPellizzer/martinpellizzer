Phyllanthus linearis

Phyllanthus linearis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf phyllanthus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its linear, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for cultivation in warm climates.
