Polycarpaea gnaphalodes

Polycarpaea gnaphalodes, commonly known as the yellow star grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, giving it a star-like appearance. It thrives in grassy areas and is often found in open woodlands.
