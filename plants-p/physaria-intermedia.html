Physaria intermedia

Physaria intermedia, commonly known as Blazing Star, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. It features a basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves and can reach heights of up to 60 cm.

Physaria intermedia is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
