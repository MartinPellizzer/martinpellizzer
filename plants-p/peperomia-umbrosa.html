Peperomia umbrosa

Peperomia umbrosa, commonly known as the shadow plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It features glossy, dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in low-light conditions and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia umbrosa is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
