Pilosella fuliginea

Pilosella fuliginea, commonly known as the sooty pilosella, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers with white petals and yellow centers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name.

Pilosella fuliginea is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is often found in disturbed soils.
