Plectranthus palisotii

Plectranthus palisotii, commonly known as the African violet, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features square, hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of violet, pink, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial shade to full sun.

Plectranthus palisotii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
