Pleurothallis echinodes

Pleurothallis echinodes, commonly known as the spiny-lobed pleurothallis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the cloud forests of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, elongated leaves. Its flowers are typically pale green or white, with a distinctive spiny texture on the petals.

It is a terrestrial orchid that often grows in association with mosses and other epiphytic plants.
