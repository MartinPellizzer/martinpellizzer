Pleurothallis minax

Pleurothallis minax, commonly known as the miniature pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, narrow leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It is noted for its compact size and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is relatively challenging to grow outside its natural range.
