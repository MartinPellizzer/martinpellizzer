Peperomia oxyphylla

Peperomia oxyphylla, commonly known as the glossy peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy finish, often adorned with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements, it is widely cultivated as a decorative plant.
