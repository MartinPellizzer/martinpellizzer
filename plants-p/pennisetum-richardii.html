Pennisetum richardii

Pennisetum richardii, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a tall, ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features slender, arching stems that produce fluffy, purple plumes during the flowering season. The plant is characterized by its fine, grass-like leaves and its ability to thrive in full sun to partial shade. It is often used in landscaping for its visual appeal and drought tolerance.

Pennisetum richardii is also valued for its role in attracting birds and insects.
