Pelargonium × exochum

Pelargonium × exochum, commonly known as the hybrid geranium, is a popular ornamental plant characterized by its vibrant flowers and attractive foliage.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is known for its showy, often bi-colored blooms that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant typically has rounded, lobed leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in full sun to partial shade. Pelargonium × exochum is widely cultivated for its ability to flourish in various climates and its low maintenance requirements.

Its botanical features make it a favored choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
