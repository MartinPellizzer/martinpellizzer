Peperomia rhexiifolia

Peperomia rhexiifolia, commonly known as the zigzag peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a distinctive zigzag or wavy margin, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it an attractive addition to shaded or low-light environments.
