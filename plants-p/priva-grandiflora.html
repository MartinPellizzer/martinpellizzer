Priva grandiflora

Priva grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora privet, is a species of flowering plant in the Oleaceae family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of white, fragrant flowers that bloom in the spring. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters.

Priva grandiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
