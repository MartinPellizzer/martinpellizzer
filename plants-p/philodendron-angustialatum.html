Philodendron angustialatum

Philodendron angustialatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are typically narrower than those of other philodendron species. The plant has a distinctive vining growth habit, often reaching several meters in length. Its leaves are usually glossy and have a slightly textured surface.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil for optimal growth.
