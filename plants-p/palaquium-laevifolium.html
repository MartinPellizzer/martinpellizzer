Palaquium laevifolium

Palaquium laevifolium, commonly known as the smooth-leaf palaquium, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are small, round, and have a thick, fleshy pulp that contains several seeds.
