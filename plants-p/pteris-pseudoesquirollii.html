Pteris pseudoesquirollii

Pteris pseudoesquirollii, commonly known as the African water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Pteridaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant features fronds that are typically bipinnate, with a distinctive, delicate appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near water sources.

Its rhizomes are fibrous and help anchor it to rocky or moist soil substrates.
