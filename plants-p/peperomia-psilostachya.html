Peperomia psilostachya

Peperomia psilostachya, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery-white underside, giving it a striking visual appeal. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia psilostachya is prized for its ornamental foliage and air-purifying qualities.
