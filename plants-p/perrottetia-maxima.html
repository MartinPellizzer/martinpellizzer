Perrottetia maxima

Perrottetia maxima, commonly known as the giant perrottetia, is a striking tropical shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in warm climates.

Perrottetia maxima thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
