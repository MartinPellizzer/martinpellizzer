Puhuaea Megaphylla

Puhuaea megaphylla is a rare and striking plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes. Its large, showy leaves make it a unique addition to any botanical collection. This species thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil. Despite its beauty, it remains elusive and poorly understood by most botanists. Its conservation status highlights the need for further study and protection.
