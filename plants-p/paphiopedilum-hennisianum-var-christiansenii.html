Paphiopedilum hennisianum var. christiansenii

Paphiopedilum hennisianum var. christiansenii, commonly known as the Hennisianum Christiansenii Paphiopedilum, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety petals and a unique, intricate floral structure typical of the Paphiopedilum genus. The plant prefers shaded, humid environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually a mix of white, pink, and purple hues, with a distinctive lip that is broader than in many other orchid species.

Due to its rarity and beauty, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
