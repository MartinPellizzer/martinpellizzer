Piper filistilum

Piper filistilum, commonly known as the Filistilum pepper or the Siam pepper, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with opposite, ovate leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruits are berries that turn red when mature, and they contain aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is often cultivated for its unique flavor profile.
