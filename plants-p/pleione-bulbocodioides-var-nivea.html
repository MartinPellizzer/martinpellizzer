Pleione bulbocodioides var. nivea

Pleione bulbocodioides var. nivea, commonly known as the snow pleione, is a rare and elegant orchid native to the high-altitude forests of China.

It is characterized by its striking white to pale pink flowers with a distinctive, almost translucent appearance. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its cool, moist mountain habitat. Its leaves are typically dark green and strap-shaped, emerging from the base of the pseudobulbs.

This variety is highly valued in horticulture for its beauty and rarity, though it is challenging to cultivate outside its natural environment.
