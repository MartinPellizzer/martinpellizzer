Pilosella lividipes

Pilosella lividipes, commonly known as the bluish pilosella, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit and produces small, blue to purple flowers. The plant features soft, hairy leaves that are usually gray-green in color. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Pilosella lividipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
