Pelargonium × byronianum

Pelargonium × byronianum, commonly known as the Byrons' geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red, often with contrasting darker veins. The plant features upright stems and lobed leaves that are usually hairy, giving it a textured appearance. Native to South Africa, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

It is widely cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
