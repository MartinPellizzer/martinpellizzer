Piper glabratum

Piper glabratum, commonly known as the smooth pipevine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Piperaceae, which includes many species of flowering plants known for their aromatic properties. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

The smooth pipevine prefers moist, shaded environments and is relatively easy to care for in a garden setting.
