Polygala densiuscula

Polygala densiuscula, commonly known as the dense-fruited milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant features compound leaves with numerous small leaflets and produces small, bead-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
