Peucedanum altissimum

Peucedanum altissimum, commonly known as the giant parsley or tall cow parsley, is a flowering plant in the carrot family (Apiaceae).

It is native to Central Asia and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and feathery, finely divided leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in umbrella-like clusters, typical of the Apiaceae family. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
