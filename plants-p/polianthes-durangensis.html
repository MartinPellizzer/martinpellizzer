Polianthes durangensis

Polianthes durangensis, commonly known as the Durango lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

It features tall, strap-like leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family, which includes other ornamental and economically important species. It is well-suited for cultivation in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value and resilience make it a notable addition to both horticultural collections and natural landscapes.
