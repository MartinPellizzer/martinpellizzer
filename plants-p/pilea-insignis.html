Pilea insignis

Pilea insignis, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically bright green with a slight variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by round, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it ideal for indoor cultivation.
