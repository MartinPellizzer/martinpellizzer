Philodendron sonderianum

Philodendron sonderianum, commonly known as the Sonder's philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically deep green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, pale greenish-white flowers, though it is more renowned for its ornamental foliage than its blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Philodendron sonderianum is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.
