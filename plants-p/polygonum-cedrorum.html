Polygonum cedrorum

Polygonum cedrorum, commonly known as redroot smartweed, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its upright stems that are typically reddish in color, especially near the base. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in dense clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Polygonum cedrorum is often found in wet or disturbed soils, such as along stream banks and in fields.
