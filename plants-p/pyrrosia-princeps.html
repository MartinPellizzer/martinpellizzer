Pyrrosia princeps

Pyrrosia princeps, commonly known as the rock fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen fronds that are typically less than 30 cm in length. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found on rocks or tree trunks. Its leaves are leathery and have a distinctive, intricate pattern of veins.

Pyrrosia princeps is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to grow in challenging conditions.
