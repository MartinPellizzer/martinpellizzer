Plectranthus porpeodon

Plectranthus porpeodon, commonly known as the purple-leaf plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with a distinctive purple hue. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically a deep violet or purple color, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Plectranthus porpeodon is also valued for its use in herbal medicine and as a source of essential oils.
