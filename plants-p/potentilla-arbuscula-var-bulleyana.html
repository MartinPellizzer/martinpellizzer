Potentilla arbuscula var. bulleyana

Potentilla arbuscula var. bulleyana, commonly known as the Bulleyana cinquefoil, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to New Zealand.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a vibrant touch to gardens. The plant has finely divided, serrated leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
