Polygala lepidota

Polygala lepidota, commonly known as the smooth polygala, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features slender stems and lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered in spicate inflorescences, often with a distinctive, thread-like structure at the base of each flower.

It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open woodlands or along riverbanks.
