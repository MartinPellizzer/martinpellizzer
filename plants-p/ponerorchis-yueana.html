Ponerorchis yueana

Ponerorchis yueana, commonly known as the Yuean orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, intricate flowers with a unique coloration. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in mountainous regions. Its botanical features include a short rhizome and a single, erect stem bearing several flowers.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
