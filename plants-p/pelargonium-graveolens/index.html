Pelargonium Graveolens

Geranium, scientifically known as Pelargonium graveolens, is a popular flowering plant native to South Africa. It is widely cultivated for its vibrant blooms and aromatic leaves, making it a favorite in gardens and homes. This plant is commonly referred to as the geranium or scented geranium, and it comes in a variety of colors including red, pink, and white. Its unique fragrance and ornamental value have made it a staple in horticulture for centuries. Despite its beauty, it requires proper care to thrive, including well-draining soil and adequate sunlight.
