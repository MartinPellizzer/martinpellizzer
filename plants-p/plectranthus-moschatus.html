Plectranthus moschatus

Plectranthus moschatus, commonly known as sweet plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces small, tubular flowers in shades of violet and blue. The plant has aromatic leaves that emit a pleasant, mint-like scent when crushed. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
