Pollia aclisia var. robusta

Pollia aclisia var. robusta, commonly known as the robusta marsh orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wet, shaded environments such as marshes and swampy areas. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to retain moisture in humid conditions.

The flowers are often violet or purple in color, with a complex structure that facilitates pollination by specific insect species.
