Pyrethrum gilliatii

Pyrethrum gilliatii, commonly known as the Gilla's pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a central yellow disc. The plant produces pyrethrin, a natural insecticide, which is used in organic pest control. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value and ecological benefits make it a desirable addition to native plant gardens.
