Pleurothallis longissima

Pleurothallis longissima, commonly known as the long-stemmed pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, often pendant inflorescences and small, delicate flowers that typically range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, contributing to its overall compact and elegant appearance.

Due to its unique beauty and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
