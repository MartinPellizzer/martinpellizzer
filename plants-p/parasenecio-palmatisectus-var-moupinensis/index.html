Parasenecio Palmatisectus Var. Moupinensis

Parasenecio palmatisectus var. moupinensis is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of China. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, known for its showy composite flowers. This variant is distinguished by its palmately lobed leaves and vibrant yellow blooms. Due to its limited distribution and fragile habitat, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique species from further decline.
