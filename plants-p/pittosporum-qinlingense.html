Pittosporum qinlingense

Pittosporum qinlingense, commonly known as the Qinling pittosporum, is a shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with well-drained soil. Its leaves are dark green and glossy, while its flowers are white or pale yellow.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping.
