Potentilla aestiva

Potentilla aestiva, commonly known as the summer cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, arranged in clusters of five. Its leaves are compound, with five oval-shaped leaflets, giving it the common name "cinquefoil.".

Potentilla aestiva thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and woodland edges.
