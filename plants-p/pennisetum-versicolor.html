Pennisetum versicolor

Pennisetum versicolor, commonly known as rainbow grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features tall, arching stems that produce plume-like inflorescences in a range of colors from pink to purple and blue. The plant is known for its vibrant, shifting hues that change with the light and weather conditions. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Pennisetum versicolor is relatively low maintenance and adds a dynamic visual element to any garden setting.
