Platycoryne Latipetala Var. Grandiflora

Platycoryne latipetala var. grandiflora is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its large, showy flowers and unique leaf structure, it captivates botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. This variant of Platycoryne latipetala is distinguished by its exceptionally large blooms that can reach several inches in diameter. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, this plant is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
