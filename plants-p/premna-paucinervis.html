Premna paucinervis

Premna paucinervis, commonly known as the narrow-veined premna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with prominent veins and small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Premna paucinervis is used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.
