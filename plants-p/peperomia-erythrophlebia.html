Peperomia erythrophlebia

Peperomia erythrophlebia, commonly known as the red-veined peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves with striking red veins that contrast beautifully with its dark green foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Peperomia erythrophlebia thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental plant collections.
