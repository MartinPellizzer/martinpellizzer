Pothuava pineliana var. minuta

Pothuava pineliana var. minuta, commonly known as the dwarf pothuava, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions.

It features small, dark green leaves that are glossy and arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
