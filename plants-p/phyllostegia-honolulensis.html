Phyllostegia honolulensis

Phyllostegia honolulensis, commonly known as the Hawaiian stiltgrass, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its small, tubular flowers are typically white or pale purple, and they bloom in clusters during the summer months.

Phyllostegia honolulensis is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forest understories and stream banks.
