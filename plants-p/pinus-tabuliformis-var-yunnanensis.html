Pinus tabuliformis var. yunnanensis

Pinus tabuliformis var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan pine, is a variant of the Chinese pine native to southwestern China.

It is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and needle-like leaves that grow in bundles of five. The tree produces cones that are typically reddish-brown and contain edible seeds. It thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

This species plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing habitat for various wildlife species.
