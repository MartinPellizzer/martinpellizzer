Piper baramense

Piper baramense, commonly known as the Baramense pepper or Baramense long pepper, is a tropical climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, elongated berries that are harvested when they turn from green to red, similar to black pepper. Its leaves are ovate with a slightly serrated edge and emit a mild peppery aroma.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its aromatic fruits and medicinal properties.
