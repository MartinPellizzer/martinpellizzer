Peperomia hispiduliformis var. hispiduliformis

Peperomia hispiduliformis var. hispiduliformis, commonly known as the woolly peperomia, is a compact, low-growing evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, velvety foliage with a distinctive hairy texture, giving it a soft, fuzzy appearance. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, with a glossy surface and dark green coloration. This plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color.

It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and minimal water requirements.
