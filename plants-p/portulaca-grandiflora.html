Portulaca grandiflora

Portulaca grandiflora, commonly known as moss rose, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is an annual species that thrives in hot, arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to drought conditions. The plant features fleshy, succulent leaves and produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, yellow, and orange. It is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its compact growth habit and long blooming period.

Portulaca grandiflora is also known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
