Prunus laurocerasus var. compacta

Prunus laurocerasus var. compacta, commonly known as the dwarf cherry laurel, is a dense, evergreen shrub or small tree native to western Europe.

It features dark green, glossy leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in spring, which are followed by dark red berries. It is valued for its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal in gardens.

Due to its toxicity, it should be planted with caution, especially around children and pets.
