Pyrenacantha staudtii var. staudtii

Pyrenacantha staudtii var. staudtii, commonly known as the Staudt's pyrenacantha, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to dry conditions.
