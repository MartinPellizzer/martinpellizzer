Polyalthia borneensis

Polyalthia borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia, particularly Borneo.

It is characterized by its large, showy, orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, making it a striking ornamental plant. The tree has a straight, cylindrical trunk with smooth, greyish bark, and its leaves are simple, alternate, and ovate in shape. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The plant is also valued for its timber and has cultural significance in some regions.
