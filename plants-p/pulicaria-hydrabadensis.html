Pulicaria hydrabadensis

Pulicaria hydrabadensis, commonly known as the Hyderabad ragwort, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of India.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to withstand high temperatures and low rainfall. It features hairy stems and leaves, which help reduce water loss through transpiration.

Pulicaria hydrabadensis is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.
