Panicum benjamini

Panicum benjamini, commonly known as Benjamin's panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces fluffy, seed-bearing inflorescences. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Panicum benjamini is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its delicate appearance and adaptability.
