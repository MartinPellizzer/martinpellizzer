Planchonella ripicola

Planchonella ripicola, commonly known as the broad-leafed hibiscus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub or tree that typically grows in lowland rainforests. The plant features simple, elliptical leaves with a smooth surface and a glossy appearance. Its flowers are usually yellow or white, with five petals and a prominent central stamen.

The fruit is a small, three-lobed berry that contains several seeds.
