Pimelea angustifolia var. major

Pimelea angustifolia var. major, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bottlebrush, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features striking red to orange flowers that bloom in dense, cylindrical clusters, resembling bottlebrushes. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant once established.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native gardens and landscapes.
