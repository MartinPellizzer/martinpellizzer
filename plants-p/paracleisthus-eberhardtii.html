Paracleisthus eberhardtii

Paracleisthus eberhardtii, commonly known as the Eberhardt's paracichlid, is a freshwater fish species native to South America.

It belongs to the family Cichlidae and is characterized by its elongated body and distinct coloration. The plant associated with this species, though not a true plant, is often cultivated in aquariums to provide habitat and enhance water quality. It thrives in warm, well-lit environments with soft, slightly acidic water.

Careful maintenance of water parameters is essential for its health and longevity.
