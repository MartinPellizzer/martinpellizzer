Patrinia scabiosifolia var. hispida

Patrinia scabiosifolia var. hispida, commonly known as hairy Patrinia, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features slender, erect stems covered in fine, bristly hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale purple flowers arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often hairy as well.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shaded areas.
