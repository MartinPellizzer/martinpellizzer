Pachira pulchra

Pachira pulchra, commonly known as the golden pachira, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its thick, woody stems and large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green. The plant produces yellow flowers followed by round, orange fruits containing several seeds. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its attractive appearance.

Pachira pulchra is also known for its resilience and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
