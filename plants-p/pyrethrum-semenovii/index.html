Pyrethrum Semenovii

Pyrethrum semenovii is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It is known for its striking, daisy-like blooms that appear in late spring and early summer. Despite its beauty, this species is highly sensitive to environmental changes and is considered endangered in its natural habitat. The plant has been studied for its potential medicinal properties due to its natural compounds. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and propagate this unique botanical gem.
