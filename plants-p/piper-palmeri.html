Piper palmeri

Piper palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's pepper, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants. The plant typically grows as a shrub with opposite, ovate leaves that are often glandular-dotted. Its small, greenish-white flowers are borne in clusters and are followed by three-winged berries.

The plant is notable for its aromatic leaves and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
