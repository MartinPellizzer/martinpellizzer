Phyllostachys quilioi f. mazelii

Phyllostachys quilioi f. mazelii, commonly known as the red bamboo, is a variant of the bamboo species native to China.

It is characterized by its striking red culms that transition to green as they mature. The plant belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens and landscapes. It grows in dense clumps and can reach heights of up to 6 meters.

Its foliage is typically a deep green, providing a contrast to its vibrant red stems.
