Peperomia samoensis var. vaupeliana

Peperomia samoensis var. vaupeliana, commonly known as the Samoan Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Pacific Islands.

It features dense, glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are followed by elliptical, red-brown seed capsules. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ornamental foliage and ease of care contribute to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
