Pogostemon philippinensis

Pogostemon philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine betel leaf plant, is a perennial herb native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional practices. The plant belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is known for its aromatic properties. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its leaves are used in various cultural and medicinal applications, reflecting its significance in local communities.
