Polystichum × fujisanense

Polystichum × fujisanense, commonly known as the Japanese forest fern, is a hybrid fern species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant features dark green, leathery leaves with a serrated margin and a distinctive, glossy appearance. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forested areas.

Polystichum × fujisanense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
