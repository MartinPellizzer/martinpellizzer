Parabaena psilophylla

Parabaena psilophylla, commonly known as the slender water fern, is a small aquatic fern found in freshwater habitats.

It has delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in slow-moving or still water, often in shaded environments. Its rhizomes are short and help anchor it to substrates in its aquatic environment.

Parabaena psilophylla plays a role in maintaining water quality by filtering nutrients and providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
