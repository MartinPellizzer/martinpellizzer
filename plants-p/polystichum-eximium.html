Polystichum eximium

Polystichum eximium, commonly known as the exalted wood fern, is a species of fern native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, elegant fronds that can reach up to 1.5 meters in length. The plant has a robust rhizome system that allows it to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are bi- to tri-pinnate, with a distinctive dark green color and a leathery texture.

Polystichum eximium is often found in forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appearance in horticulture.
