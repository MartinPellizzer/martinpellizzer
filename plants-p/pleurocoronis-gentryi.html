Pleurocoronis gentryi

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It is typically found in specific, often moist or wetland habitats, which are crucial for its survival.
