Pittosporum serpentinum

Pittosporum serpentinum, commonly known as the serpentine pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in nutrient-poor, acidic soils, particularly those derived from serpentine rock. Its leaves are typically dark green and glossy, with a leathery texture.

It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate coastal conditions.
