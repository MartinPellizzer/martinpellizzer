Pilosella cymosa var. praealta

Pilosella cymosa var. praealta, commonly known as the tall hairy cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. It has a compact, cushion-like growth form, with leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine, white hairs.

This variety is distinguished by its taller stature compared to other varieties of Pilosella cymosa.
