Prunus carcharias

Prunus carcharias, commonly known as the sea buckthorn, is a shrub native to Europe, western Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its yellow or orange berries, which are rich in nutrients and antioxidants. The plant has small, dark green leaves and produces yellow flowers in the spring. It thrives in coastal and sandy environments, often growing along shores and in dry, exposed areas.

Its hardy nature makes it adaptable to a variety of soil types and climates.
