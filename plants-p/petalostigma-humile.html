Petalostigma humile

Petalostigma humile, commonly known as the small-petaled grass, is a low-growing perennial plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, tufted foliage and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along streambanks.

Its botanical features include narrow leaf blades and a compact growth habit, making it a hardy and resilient species.
