Primulina lutescens

Primulina lutescens, commonly known as the golden primula, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a bold splash of color to its surroundings. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically a deep green, providing a striking contrast to its bright blooms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is popular among plant enthusiasts and gardeners.
