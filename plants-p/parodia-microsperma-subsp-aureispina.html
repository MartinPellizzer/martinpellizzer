Parodia microsperma subsp. aureispina

Parodia microsperma subsp.

aureispina, commonly known as the golden spine cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to southern Africa. It features a rounded stem covered in bright yellow spines, which give it its common name.

The plant produces small, white flowers during the warmer months, typically in summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
