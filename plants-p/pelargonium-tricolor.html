Pelargonium tricolor

Pelargonium tricolor, commonly known as the tricolor geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multicolored flowers that typically display shades of red, white, and yellow. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Pelargonium tricolor is a popular choice for gardens and containers due to its hardy nature and long blooming period.
