Piptanthocereus obtusus

Piptanthocereus obtusus, commonly known as the Mexican night bloomer, is a flowering cactus native to Mexico.

It features a tall, cylindrical stem covered in spines and is adapted to arid environments. The plant produces large, showy white or pale yellow flowers that open at night. It is a member of the Cactaceae family and is known for its nocturnal blooming habit.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
