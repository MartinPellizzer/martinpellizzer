Pleurothallis ottonis

Pleurothallis ottonis, commonly known as the Otto's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, linear leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is part of the Orchidaceae family.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
