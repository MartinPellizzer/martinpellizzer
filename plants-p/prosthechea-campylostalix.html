Prosthechea campylostalix

Prosthechea campylostalix, commonly known as the campylostalix orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically grows on trees in humid, tropical environments. Its inflorescence consists of multiple clustered flowers that bloom during the rainy season.

Prosthechea campylostalix is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
