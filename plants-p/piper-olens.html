Piper olens

Piper olens, commonly known as the slender pepper plant, is a flowering vine native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by elongated, berry-like fruits. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly aromatic scent.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of medicinal compounds.
