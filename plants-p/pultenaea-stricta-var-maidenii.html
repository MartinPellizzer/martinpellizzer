Pultenaea stricta var. maidenii

Pultenaea stricta var. maidenii, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pultenaea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow or orange, appearing in clustered inflorescences during spring.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native gardens.
