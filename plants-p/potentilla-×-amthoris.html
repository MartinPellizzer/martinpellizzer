Potentilla × amthoris

Potentilla × amthoris, commonly known as the alpine cinquefoil, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, thriving in rocky soils and high-altitude environments. Its leaves are usually palmately divided, and it is often found in mountainous areas of Europe and Asia.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
