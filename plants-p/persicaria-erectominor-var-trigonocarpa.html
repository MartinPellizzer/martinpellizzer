Persicaria erectominor var. trigonocarpa

Persicaria erectominor var. trigonocarpa, commonly known as the narrowleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It features upright, slender stems that are often tinged with red, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The flowers are small, clustered in dense spikes, and are typically pink to reddish in color. It produces small, three-angled fruits that are characteristic of the species.

This plant is often found in moist soils along stream banks and in wet meadows.
