Phreatia rivularis

Phreatia rivularis, commonly known as the stream orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces intricate, showy flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with purple markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments near streams or in moist forests.

Phreatia rivularis is an epiphytic species, often growing on trees or rocks in its natural habitat.
