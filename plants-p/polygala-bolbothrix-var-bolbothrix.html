Polygala bolbothrix var. bolbothrix

Polygala bolbothrix var. bolbothrix, commonly known as the pink ghost orchid, is a rare and striking member of the Polygalaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, orchid-like flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees without being parasitic, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered endangered and is found primarily in the southeastern United States.
