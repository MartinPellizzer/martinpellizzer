Parsonsia oligantha

Parsonsia oligantha, commonly known as the white-flowered parsonsia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Australia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its striking white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a waxy texture.
