Persicaria runcinata var. runcinata

Persicaria runcinata var. runcinata, commonly known as narrowleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, wet meadows, and along stream banks. The plant features slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that have a pointed tip. Its small, pinkish-white flowers appear in dense clusters at the tips of the stems during late summer.

It is often found in wetland ecosystems and is valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
