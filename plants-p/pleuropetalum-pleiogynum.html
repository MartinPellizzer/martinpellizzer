Pleuropetalum pleiogynum

Pleuropetalum pleiogynum, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It produces multiple flowers per stem, which bloom in late summer.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
