Polygonatum odoratum var. japonicum

Polygonatum odoratum var. japonicum, commonly known as Japanese yellow flag.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia, characterized by its rhizomatous growth and arching stems. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, yellow-green flowers in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a whorled pattern along the stems.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
