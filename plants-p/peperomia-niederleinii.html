Peperomia niederleinii

Peperomia niederleinii, commonly known as the Niederlein's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, which appear in clusters during the growing season. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for collectors and indoor gardeners seeking a decorative, easy-to-care-for plant.
