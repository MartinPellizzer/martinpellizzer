Phillyrea subangustifolia subsp. virgata

Phillyrea subangustifolia subsp.

virgata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy.

The plant produces clusters of white, fragrant flowers in the spring, which are followed by small, round berries. It is adapted to dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in coastal and arid environments.
