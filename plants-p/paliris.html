Paliris

Paliris, commonly known as the ghost plant, is a unique species of flowering plant native to certain parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pale, almost translucent leaves that give it its ghostly appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. Paliris thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an intriguing subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
