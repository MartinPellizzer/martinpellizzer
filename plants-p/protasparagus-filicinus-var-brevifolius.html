Protasparagus filicinus var. brevifolius

Protasparagus filicinus var. brevifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved fern, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asparagaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically shorter and narrower than those of the typical species. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a fine, lacy texture.

It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by spherical, dry fruits containing tiny seeds.
