Phreatia myriantha

Phreatia myriantha, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 20 centimeters in diameter. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are epiphytic, meaning they attach to trees without parasitizing them.

The orchid's unique structure and vibrant blooms make it a notable species in botanical studies.
