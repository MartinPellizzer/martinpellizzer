Potentilla × peyritschii

Potentilla × peyritschii, commonly known as the peyritschii cinquefoil, is a hybrid shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers in the summer. The plant has finely divided, dark green leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms.

Potentilla × peyritschii prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.
