Philodendron yutajense

Philodendron yutajense, commonly known as the Yutajense philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to the Amazon rainforest of Brazil.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly waxy texture and prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by pale green, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a houseplant.
