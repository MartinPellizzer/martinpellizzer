Philodendron platypodum

Philodendron platypodum, commonly known as the broadleaf philodendron, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant typically has a climbing habit, using its aerial roots to attach to supports. Its leaves are usually a deep green with a slightly glossy texture.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity for optimal growth.
