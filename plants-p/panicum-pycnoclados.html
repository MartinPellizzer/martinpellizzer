Panicum pycnoclados

Panicum pycnoclados, commonly known as the dense panicle grass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compact, dense inflorescence that forms a panicle structure, giving it its scientific name. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture, and it produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.

It is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive, compact growth habit.
