Poa szechuensis var. rossbergiana

Poa szechuensis var. rossbergiana, commonly known as the Rossberg's bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and blue-green foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small, two-lobed flowers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
