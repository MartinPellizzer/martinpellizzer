Polygala javana

Polygala javana, commonly known as the Java milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, clustered leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish, with a distinctive, often zigzag-shaped corolla.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
