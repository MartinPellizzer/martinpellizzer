Peperomia rodriguezii

Peperomia rodriguezii, commonly known as the Rodriguez Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by reddish-brown berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its compact size and ornamental foliage, it is often used as a decorative plant in both home and office settings.
