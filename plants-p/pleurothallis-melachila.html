Pleurothallis melachila

Pleurothallis melachila, commonly known as the dark green orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of green and yellow. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage and subtle floral display make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
