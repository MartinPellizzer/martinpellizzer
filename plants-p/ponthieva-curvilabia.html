Ponthieva curvilabia

Ponthieva curvilabia, commonly known as the curly-lipped ponthieva, is a unique aquatic plant native to the freshwater habitats of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, ribbon-like leaves that curl at the edges, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in slow-moving or still waters, where it can anchor itself to substrates with its rhizomes. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, often clustered in dense inflorescences near the water's surface.

Ponthieva curvilabia is valued in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
