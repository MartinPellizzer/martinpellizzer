Parapolystichum grayi

Parapolystichum grayi, commonly known as the Gray's shield fern, is a species of fern native to the western United States.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its large, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizomes are thick and creeping, allowing it to spread and form dense colonies.
