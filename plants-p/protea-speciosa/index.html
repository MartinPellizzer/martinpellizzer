Protea Speciosa

White protea, scientifically known as Protea speciosa, is a striking flower native to South Africa. This species belongs to the Proteaceae family, which includes many other iconic South African flora. Its elegant white petals and vibrant green bracts make it a popular choice for floral arrangements. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions. Due to its beauty and ecological importance, it is both admired and protected in its natural habitat.
