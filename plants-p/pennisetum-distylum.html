Pennisetum distylum

Pennisetum distylum, commonly known as the narrowleaf fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It features slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that give it a fountain-like appearance. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Pennisetum distylum is also valued for its ability to attract birds and beneficial insects.
