Pycnanthemum loomisii

Pycnanthemum loomisii, commonly known as Loomis' blazing star, is a perennial herb native to the central United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, cone-shaped flower heads that range in color from pale yellow to orange. The plant produces aromatic, glandular leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

Pycnanthemum loomisii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
