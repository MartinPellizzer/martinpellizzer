Pimelea Linifolia Subsp. Collina

Pimelea linifolia subsp. collina is a unique variant of the common white everlasting plant native to southern Australia. This subspecies is distinguished by its slender stems and delicate, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments. Despite its beauty, it is relatively rare and requires specific growing conditions to flourish. Conservation efforts are important to protect this plant from habitat loss and environmental changes.
