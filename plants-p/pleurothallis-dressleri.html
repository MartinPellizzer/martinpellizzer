Pleurothallis dressleri

Pleurothallis dressleri, commonly known as the Dressler's orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale green, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich substrates.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
