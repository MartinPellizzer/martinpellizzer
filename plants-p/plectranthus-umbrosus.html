Plectranthus umbrosus

Plectranthus umbrosus, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers indirect sunlight.

Plectranthus umbrosus is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
