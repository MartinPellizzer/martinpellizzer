Pelargonium × hoareanum

Pelargonium × hoareanum, commonly known as the scented geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its fragrant, serrated leaves that emit various scents depending on the variety. The plant typically produces showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink, red, or white. Native to South Africa, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
