Pyrethrum bornmuelleri

Pyrethrum bornmuelleri, commonly known as the African pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant produces small, cylindrical seed heads that contain numerous fine seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for dry garden environments.

Pyrethrum bornmuelleri is valued for its ornamental appeal and mild toxicity to insects.
