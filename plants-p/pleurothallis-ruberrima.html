Pleurothallis ruberrima

Pleurothallis ruberrima, commonly known as the fiery red orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually bright red or orange, with a distinctive, elongated shape.

Pleurothallis ruberrima is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
