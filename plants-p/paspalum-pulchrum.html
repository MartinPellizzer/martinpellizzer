Paspalum pulchrum

Paspalum pulchrum, commonly known as the beautiful grass or silvergrass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-blue foliage and delicate inflorescence, which gives it a striking appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.

Paspalum pulchrum is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and support local wildlife.
