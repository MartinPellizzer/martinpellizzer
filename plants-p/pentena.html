Pentena

Pentena, commonly known as the bluebell, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in woodland areas and thrives in partial shade. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often forming a basal rosette.

Pentena blooms in early spring, adding a vibrant touch to forest floors and garden landscapes.
