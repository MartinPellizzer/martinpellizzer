Piper ramirezii

Piper ramirezii, commonly known as Ramirez's pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the cloud forests of Mexico and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small green berries that turn red when mature, which are used in traditional medicine by indigenous communities. It is valued for its aromatic properties and is often used in herbal remedies.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
