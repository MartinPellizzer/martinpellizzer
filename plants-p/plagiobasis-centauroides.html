Plagiobasis centauroides

Plagiobasis centauroides, commonly known as the centaur's shield orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, shield-shaped inflorescence. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom during the cooler months. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and requires well-drained conditions.

Plagiobasis centauroides is often found in open woodland and grassland habitats.
