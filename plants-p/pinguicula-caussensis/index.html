Pinguicula Caussensis

Pinguicula caussensis is a rare and fascinating carnivorous plant native to the high altitudes of southern Mexico. It is known for its unique ability to trap and digest insects using its sticky, glandular leaves. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or shaded areas. Its small, delicate flowers add to its ornamental appeal while also playing a role in its reproductive cycle. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Pinguicula caussensis is considered a vulnerable species in the wild.
