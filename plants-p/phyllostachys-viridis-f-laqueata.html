Phyllostachys viridis f. laqueata

Phyllostachys viridis f. laqueata, commonly known as the green bamboo with a lacquered appearance, is a unique variant of the green bamboo species.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and striking, dark green culms that have a glossy, lacquered sheen. The leaves are narrow and elongated, contributing to the plant's elegant, upright form. This cultivar is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in shaded to partially sunny conditions.

It is a popular choice for gardeners seeking a visually appealing and low-maintenance bamboo plant.
