Psychopterys ornata

Psychopterys ornata, commonly known as the golden star cactus, is a member of the Cactaceae family native to Mexico.

It is a shrub-like plant characterized by its thick, ribbed stems and spiny, paddle-shaped leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
