Plectranthus mayporensis

Plectranthus mayporensis, commonly known as Mayporensis mint, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale purple to pink in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its aromatic foliage and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and used in traditional medicine.
