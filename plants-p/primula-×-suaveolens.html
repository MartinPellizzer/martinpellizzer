Primula × suaveolens

Primula × suaveolens, commonly known as the sweet primrose, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Primulaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant features heart-shaped, hairy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in moist, shady environments.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
