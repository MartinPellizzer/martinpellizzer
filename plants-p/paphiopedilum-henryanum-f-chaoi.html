Paphiopedilum henryanum f. chaoi

Paphiopedilum henryanum f. chaoi, commonly known as the Chao's Boat Orchid, is a rare and exquisite orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, boat-shaped lip that is a defining feature of the Paphiopedilum genus. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid, often found in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white with intricate patterns, making it highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in greenhouses to ensure its survival.
