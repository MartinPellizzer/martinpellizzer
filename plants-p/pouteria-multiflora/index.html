Pouteria Multiflora

Pouteria multiflora is a tropical tree known for its unique appearance and ecological importance. Native to Central and South America, it thrives in humid, warm climates with well-drained soil. The tree produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that eventually develop into edible fruits. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits. This versatile plant plays a vital role in supporting local biodiversity and human communities.
