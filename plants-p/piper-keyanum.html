Piper keyanum

Piper keyanum, commonly known as the Keyanum pepper or wild pepper, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy. Its small, greenish-white flowers are followed by elliptical, three-lobed berries that turn red when mature.

The plant is valued for its aromatic leaves and fruits, which are used in traditional medicine and as a spice.
