Pleopeltis rotunda

Pleopeltis rotunda, commonly known as the round pleopeltis, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, orbicular-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually leathery and have a velvety texture.

Pleopeltis rotunda is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
