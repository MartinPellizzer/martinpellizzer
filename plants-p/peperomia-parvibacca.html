Peperomia parvibacca

Peperomia parvibacca, commonly known as the small-stemmed peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are usually arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a dense, compact appearance. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia parvibacca is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and tolerating a range of indoor conditions.
