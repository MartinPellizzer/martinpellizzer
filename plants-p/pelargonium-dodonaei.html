Pelargonium dodonaei

Pelargonium dodonaei, commonly known as the Cape Geranium, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features velvety, dark green leaves and produces clusters of small, white or pink flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Pelargonium dodonaei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in gardens.
