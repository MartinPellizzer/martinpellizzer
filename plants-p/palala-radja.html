Palala radja

Palala radja, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has strap-like leaves that are broad and pointed, often growing in a rosette formation. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Palala radja is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
