Pleurogyne rotata var. bella

Pleurogyne rotata var. bella, commonly known as the pretty water starwort, is a striking aquatic plant native to Australia.

It features delicate, star-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a waxy texture. The plant thrives in shallow, still waters and is often found in ponds and wetlands. Its flowers are small and pale blue, appearing in clusters along the stems.

It is valued in aquariums for its ornamental appeal and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
