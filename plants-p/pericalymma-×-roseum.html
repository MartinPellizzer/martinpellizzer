Pericalymma × roseum

Pericalymma × roseum, commonly known as the pink pericalymma, is a hybrid plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with small, narrow leaves. Its floral display makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening, particularly in coastal and subtropical regions.

The species is also valued for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in full sun.
