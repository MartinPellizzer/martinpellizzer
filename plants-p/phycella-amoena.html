Phycella amoena

Phycella amoena, commonly known as the elegant water lily, is a striking aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface, supported by long, slender petioles. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive, rounded shape. Its roots anchor it in the muddy substrate, while its leaves provide habitat for various aquatic organisms.

Phycella amoena thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often in ponds and marshes.
