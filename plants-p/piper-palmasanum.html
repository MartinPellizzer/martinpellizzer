Piper palmasanum

Piper palmasanum, commonly known as the Palmasanum pepper or the Mexican pepper plant, is a member of the Piperaceae family.

It is a climbing vine native to Central America and parts of South America. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, slender fruits. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

The plant is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
