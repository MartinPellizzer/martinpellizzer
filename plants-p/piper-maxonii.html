Piper maxonii

Piper maxonii, commonly known as the Hawaiian pepper plant, is a flowering vine native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a peppery scent when crushed.

Piper maxonii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
