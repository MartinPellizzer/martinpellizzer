Peperomia manarae

Peperomia manarae, commonly known as the Manarae peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit. The plant features thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a subtle variegation pattern. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color.

Peperomia manarae is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.
