Premna perrieri

Premna perrieri, commonly known as the Perrier's premna, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Vietnam, and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
