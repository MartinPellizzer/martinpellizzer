Pavonia schiedeana

Pavonia schiedeana, commonly known as the scarlet pavonia, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is a shrub that typically grows in open woodlands and disturbed soils. The plant produces showy, pink to red bracts that resemble petals, which are actually modified leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in late spring to early summer.

Pavonia schiedeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade to full sun.
