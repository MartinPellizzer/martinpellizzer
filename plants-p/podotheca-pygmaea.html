Podotheca pygmaea

Podotheca pygmaea, commonly known as the dwarf podotheca, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical regions.

It features delicate, opposite leaves that are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as an indicator species for undisturbed tropical ecosystems.
