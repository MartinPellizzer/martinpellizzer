Prenanthes serpentaria var. barbata

Prenanthes serpentaria var. barbata, commonly known as the hairy bladderwort, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its small, green, bladder-like structures that are used for capturing small aquatic organisms. The plant typically grows in wet, marshy habitats and is often found in shallow water or along the edges of ponds.
