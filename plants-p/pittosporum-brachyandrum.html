Pittosporum brachyandrum

Pittosporum brachyandrum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically narrow and lance-shaped. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

The species is also valued for its ability to tolerate coastal conditions and its use in landscaping.
