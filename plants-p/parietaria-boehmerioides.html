Parietaria boehmerioides

Parietaria boehmerioides, commonly known as the smooth wallrocket, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual or biennial herb that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves with a distinctive waxy coating. Its small yellow flowers are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences called cymes.

Parietaria boehmerioides is often found growing in disturbed soils, such as along roadsides or in rocky areas.
