Pennisetum sagittatum

Pennisetum sagittatum, commonly known as fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It features slender, arching blades that form a fountain-like shape, giving it its common name. The plant produces feathery, plume-like inflorescences that are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Pennisetum sagittatum is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract birds and beneficial insects.
