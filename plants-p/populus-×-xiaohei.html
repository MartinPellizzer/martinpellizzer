Populus × xiaohei

Populus × xiaohei, commonly known as Chinese poplar, is a hybrid species native to China.

It belongs to the Salicaceae family and is characterized by its fast growth rate and adaptability to various soil types. The plant typically features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are smooth on the upper surface and hairy on the underside. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers in the spring, followed by samaras that aid in seed dispersal.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is widely cultivated in urban and rural landscapes.
