Polypodium austrosinicum

Polypodium austrosinicum, commonly known as the Chinese shield fern, is a member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is a perennial evergreen fern characterized by its shield-shaped fronds and is native to Southeast Asia. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizome is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The leaves are usually leathery and have a distinctive, glossy appearance.
