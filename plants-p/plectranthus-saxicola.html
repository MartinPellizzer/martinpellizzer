Plectranthus saxicola

Plectranthus saxicola, commonly known as the rock plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are usually purple or violet in color. It is often found in rocky outcrops and along coastal areas, thriving in full sun.

Plectranthus saxicola is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand dry conditions.
