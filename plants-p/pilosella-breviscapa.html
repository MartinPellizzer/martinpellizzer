Pilosella breviscapa

Pilosella breviscapa, commonly known as the short-stemmed hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, with small, yellow ray flowers surrounding disk flowers. It thrives in rocky and alpine environments, often growing at high elevations.

Pilosella breviscapa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
