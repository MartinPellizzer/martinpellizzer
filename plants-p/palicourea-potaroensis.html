Palicourea potaroensis

Palicourea potaroensis, commonly known as the Potaro piper.

It is a climbing shrub native to the rainforests of northern South America. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and a strong, aromatic odor. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in inflorescences, while its fruits are capsule-like and contain several seeds.

Palicourea potaroensis is noted for its chemical compounds, which have been studied for potential medicinal properties.
