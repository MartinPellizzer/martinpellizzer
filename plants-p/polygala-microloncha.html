Polygala microloncha

Polygala microloncha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish in color, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and natural habitats.
