Peperomia langsdorffii var. resacana

Peperomia langsdorffii var. resacana, commonly known as the Resacana Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are often reddish or purple-tinged, especially in low light conditions. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or brown, which are not showy but attract certain pollinators. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
