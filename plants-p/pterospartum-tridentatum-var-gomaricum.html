Pterospartum tridentatum var. gomaricum

Pterospartum tridentatum var. gomaricum, commonly known as the Gomarico flame tree, is a variant of the flame tree species native to South America.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree with a broad, spreading canopy. Its most striking feature is its vibrant orange to red flowers, which bloom in dense clusters during the summer months. The leaves are simple, alternate, and have a leathery texture.

This plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental tree in tropical and subtropical regions.
