Piper pabstii

Piper pabstii, commonly known as the Pabst pepper plant, is a tropical vine native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piper genus, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, green, elongated berries that turn red when mature. These berries are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Its botanical features include heart-shaped leaves and a climbing habit that requires support for growth.
