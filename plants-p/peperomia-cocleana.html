Peperomia cocleana

Peperomia cocleana, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery-white underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia cocleana is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
