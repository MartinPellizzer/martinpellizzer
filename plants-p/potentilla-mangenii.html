Potentilla mangenii

Potentilla mangenii, commonly known as the Mangei cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are small, oval-shaped, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Potentilla mangenii thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable ornamental plant for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
