Plectranthus wardii

Plectranthus wardii, commonly known as the African violet, is a herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features opposite, ovate to elliptical leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive violet or purple hue. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or blue in color, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

Plectranthus wardii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
