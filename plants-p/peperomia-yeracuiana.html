Peperomia yeracuiana

Peperomia yeracuiana, commonly known as the Yeracuiana Peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit. The plant features thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and silver. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red in color.

Peperomia yeracuiana is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.
