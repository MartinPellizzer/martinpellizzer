Peperomia regelii

Peperomia regelii, commonly known as the Watermelon Plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture, and it produces small, pale green to yellow flowers. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
