Pilea medongensis

Pilea medongensis, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale green, followed by red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Its unique foliage and ease of care contribute to its popularity among indoor gardeners.
