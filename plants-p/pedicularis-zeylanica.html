Pedicularis zeylanica

Pedicularis zeylanica, commonly known as the Sri Lankan lousewort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the Orobanchaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and showy, bilaterally symmetrical flowers. Pedicularis zeylanica thrives in moist, shaded environments and is an important component of its native ecosystems.
