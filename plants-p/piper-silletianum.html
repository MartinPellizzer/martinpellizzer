Piper silletianum

Piper silletianum, commonly known as the Silletian pepper, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing vines and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are greenish-white and are typically clustered in spikes.

The fruit is a small, dark blue to black berry that contains several seeds.
