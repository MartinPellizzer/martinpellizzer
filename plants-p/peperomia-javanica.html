Peperomia javanica

Peperomia javanica, commonly known as the Java Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish or purple hue on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in spadices, typical of the Piperaceae family. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia javanica is valued for its ornamental foliage and air-purifying qualities.
