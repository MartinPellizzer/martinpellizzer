Peperomia lawrancei

Peperomia lawrancei, commonly known as the Lawrence's peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink. The plant is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.
