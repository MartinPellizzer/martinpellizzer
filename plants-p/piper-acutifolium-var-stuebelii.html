Piper acutifolium var. stuebelii

Piper acutifolium var. stuebelii, commonly known as Stuebel's pepper plant, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. It produces small greenish-white flowers that develop into peppery fruits.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and mild peppery flavor.
