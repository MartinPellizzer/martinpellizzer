Pelargonium parvirostre

Pelargonium parvirostre, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed geranium, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, showy flowers. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with a rough texture and a distinctive reddish or purple underside. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
