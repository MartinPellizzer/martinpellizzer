Platanus reuteri

Platanus reuteri, commonly known as the Mexican sycamore, is a species of plane tree native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The tree produces small, pale yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by spherical, woody fruits. Its bark is thick and flaky, often peeling in a distinctive manner.

Platanus reuteri is well adapted to dry and rocky environments, making it a resilient and ornamental plant in its native habitat.
