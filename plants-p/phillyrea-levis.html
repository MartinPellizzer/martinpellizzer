Phillyrea levis

Phillyrea levis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Phillyrea, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features simple, elliptic, and leathery leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are followed by spherical, woody fruits. Phillyrea levis thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
