Piper austini

Piper austini, commonly known as the Austen's pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by small, peppery fruits.

Piper austini is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of mild spices.
