Pleurothallis pergracilis

Pleurothallis pergracilis, commonly known as the slender pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant prefers shaded, humid environments and is often found growing epiphytically on trees. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its overall elegant appearance.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and delicate species in the orchid family.
