Philodendron trilobatum

Philodendron trilobatum, commonly known as the three-lobed philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with distinctive three lobes, giving it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant has a vining growth habit and can reach several meters in length if given proper support. Its foliage is typically a deep green, and it thrives in bright, indirect light with consistently moist soil.

Philodendron trilobatum is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and aesthetic appeal.
