Pavonia somalensis

Pavonia somalensis, commonly known as the Somali hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Horn of Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are usually ovate and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Pavonia somalensis is often found in rocky areas and along coastal regions, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
