Pennisetum massaicum

Pennisetum massaicum, commonly known as giant red grass, is a tall, perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, rust-colored plumes that appear in late summer and persist through the winter. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping and drought-tolerant gardens. Its rapid growth and ability to spread via rhizomes make it an effective erosion control plant.

Pennisetum massaicum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and as a backdrop in garden designs.
