Pecluma camptophyllaria subsp. abbreviata

Pecluma camptophyllaria subsp.

abbreviata, commonly known as the short-leafed pecluma, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family. It is a shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and small, elliptical leaves.

The leaves are typically arranged in opposite pairs and have a smooth, glossy surface. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are often obscured by bracts.

This subspecies is native to parts of East Africa and is adapted to dry, rocky habitats.
