Panicum Cognatum

Panicum cognatum is a unique grass species native to the southeastern united states. it thrives in wetland environments and is often found in marshes and swamps. this plant is known for its tall, slender stalks and delicate, feathery seed heads. it plays an important role in supporting local wildlife and maintaining ecosystem balance. its adaptability makes it a valuable plant for wetland restoration projects.
