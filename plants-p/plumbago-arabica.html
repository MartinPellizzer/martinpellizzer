Plumbago arabica

Plumbago arabica, commonly known as blue plumbago, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
