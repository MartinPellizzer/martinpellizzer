Peperomia trifolia f. obovalifolia

Peperomia trifolia f. obovalifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features glossy, ovate to lance-shaped leaves that are often dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green or red, typically emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for indoor cultivation and container gardening.
