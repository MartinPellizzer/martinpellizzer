Panicum phleiforme

Panicum phleiforme, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and dense clusters of small, reddish-bracted inflorescences. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can thrive in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that becomes more reddish in maturity.

Panicum phleiforme is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to various soil conditions.
