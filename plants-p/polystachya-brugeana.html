Polystachya brugeana

Polystachya brugeana, commonly known as the Brugena torch orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of bright orange to red flowers that resemble torches. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded, moist environments. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, with a waxy surface that helps retain moisture.

Polystachya brugeana is valued in horticulture for its striking floral display and adaptability to various growing conditions.
