Paederia esquirolii

Paederia esquirolii, commonly known as the white-flowered paederia, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the Rubiaceae family.
