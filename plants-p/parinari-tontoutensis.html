Parinari tontoutensis

Parinari tontoutensis, commonly known as the Tontouta parinari, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to western Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grey-brown bark and simple, alternate leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by woody, spherical fruits containing one or two seeds.
