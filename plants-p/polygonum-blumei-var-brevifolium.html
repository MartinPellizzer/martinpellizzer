Polygonum blumei var. brevifolium

Polygonum blumei var. brevifolium, commonly known as the short-leaved smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of East Asia.

It typically grows in moist environments such as wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks. The plant features upright stems that are often reddish in color, and its leaves are narrow with a short, broad base. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink or red, appearing in late summer to early autumn.

The species is notable for its adaptability to wet conditions and its role in stabilizing soil in riparian areas.
