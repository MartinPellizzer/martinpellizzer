Phyllostachys nuda f. lucida

Phyllostachys nuda f. lucida, commonly known as the shining bamboo, is a variant of the silver bamboo species.

It is characterized by its striking, silvery-white sheaths that give it a unique and elegant appearance. This plant is a clump-forming bamboo that grows relatively quickly, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping. The leaves are narrow and slightly pointed, with a smooth texture that enhances its visual appeal.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, thriving in temperate climates.
