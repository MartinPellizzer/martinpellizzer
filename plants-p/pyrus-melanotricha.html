Pyrus melanotricha

Pyrus melanotricha, commonly known as the black-haired pear, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Afghanistan and Pakistan. The plant is characterized by its dark-colored, hairy leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early spring. Its fruit is a small pear-like berry that ripens to a yellow or orange color.
