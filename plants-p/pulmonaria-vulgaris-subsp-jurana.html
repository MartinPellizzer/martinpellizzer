Pulmonaria vulgaris subsp. jurana

Pulmonaria vulgaris subsp.

jurana, commonly known as lungwort, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe. It features distinctive basal rosettes of velvety, spotted leaves that are often tinged with red or purple.

The plant produces upright stems bearing clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, supporting a variety of pollinators.
