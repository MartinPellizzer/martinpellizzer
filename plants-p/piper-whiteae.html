Piper whiteae

Piper whiteae, commonly known as the White's pepper vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by berries that turn red when mature. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly serrated edge.

Piper whiteae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and potential medicinal properties.
