Polygonum venosum

Polygonum venosum, commonly known as Chinese knotweed, is a perennial herb native to Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant has distinctive veins on its leaves, which give it its scientific name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Polygonum venosum is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
