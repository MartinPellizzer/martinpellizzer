Piper balsapuertanum

Piper balsapuertanum, commonly known as the Puerto Rican pepper plant, is a species of flowering vine native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into elongated, berry-like fruits. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly serrated edge.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has potential uses in traditional medicine.
