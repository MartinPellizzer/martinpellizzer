Plinia rupestris

Plinia rupestris, commonly known as the rock plinia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and small, aromatic leaves. The plant produces white or pinkish flowers that are often clustered together, and it is adapted to grow in rocky or well-drained soils.
