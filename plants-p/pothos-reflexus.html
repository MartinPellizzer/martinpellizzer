Pothos reflexus

Pothos reflexus, commonly known as the ivy-leaf pothos, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a slight variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular houseplant.

Its trailing growth habit makes it ideal for hanging baskets or shelves.
