Peperomia rufescentifolia

Peperomia rufescentifolia, commonly known as the red-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or maroon hues, especially along the edges and veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to any plant collection.
