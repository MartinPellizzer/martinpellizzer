Provenzalia heterophyla

Provenzalia heterophyla, commonly known as the variable rock-rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and fleshy, aromatic leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Provenzalia heterophyla is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
