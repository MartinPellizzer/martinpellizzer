Photinia Chingiana Var. Lipingensis

Photinia chingiana var. lipingensis is a unique and striking plant native to southern China. Known for its vibrant red foliage, it stands out among other photinias with its bold coloration. This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a versatile choice for gardeners. Its beauty and adaptability make it a popular choice for those seeking to add visual interest to their outdoor spaces.
