Pterophylla denhamii

Pterophylla denhamii, commonly known as the Denham's pterophylla, is a rare and striking plant native to the eastern region of Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, spirally arranged leaves that are often described as resembling the wings of a bird, giving it its scientific name. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring, adding a subtle ornamental value. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in rainforest understories.
