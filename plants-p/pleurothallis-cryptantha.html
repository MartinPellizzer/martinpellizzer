Pleurothallis cryptantha

Pleurothallis cryptantha, commonly known as the hidden pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with delicate, narrow leaves and clustered, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms in the spring, producing white or pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, intricate pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts in controlled conditions.
