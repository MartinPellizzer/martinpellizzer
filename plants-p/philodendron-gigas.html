Philodendron gigas

Philodendron gigas, commonly known as the giant philodendron, is a large tropical climbing plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its massive, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in length. The plant has a robust, woody stem that supports its rapid growth and climbing habit. Its foliage is typically a deep green, with some varieties exhibiting variegation.

Philodendron gigas thrives in high humidity and bright, indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
