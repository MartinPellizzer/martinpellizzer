Podochilus rhombipetalus

Podochilus rhombipetalus, commonly known as the rhombipetalum orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a rhizomatous root system and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive rhombus-shaped lip.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
