Peltophorum africanum

Peltophorum africanum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are pinnately arranged and often have a reddish or bronze tinge. The tree produces striking yellow or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its fruit is a woody pod that splits open to release seeds.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often planted in gardens and urban landscapes.
