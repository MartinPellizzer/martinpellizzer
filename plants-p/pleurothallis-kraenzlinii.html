Pleurothallis kraenzlinii

Pleurothallis kraenzlinii, commonly known as the Kraenzlin's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, intricate flowers that are typically white or pale green, often with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
