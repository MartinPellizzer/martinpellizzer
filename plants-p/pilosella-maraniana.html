Pilosella maraniana

Pilosella maraniana, commonly known as the Maranian goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its inflorescence consists of yellow disk flowers surrounded by white ray florets.

Pilosella maraniana typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.
