Pilea trichotoma

Pilea trichotoma, commonly known as the velvet pilea, is a low-growing, evergreen perennial native to southern China and Southeast Asia.

It features dense, velvety foliage with dark green, heart-shaped leaves that have a slightly fuzzy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea trichotoma is known for its compact growth habit and is often used as a decorative houseplant.
