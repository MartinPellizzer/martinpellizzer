Persica vulgaris ovalis

Persica vulgaris ovalis, commonly known as the oval peach.

This plant is a variant of the peach tree characterized by its oval-shaped fruits. It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is native to temperate regions of Asia. The leaves are broad and ovate, with a smooth texture and a slightly serrated edge.

The flowers are typically pink or white, and they bloom in the spring before the fruit develops.
