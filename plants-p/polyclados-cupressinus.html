Polyclados cupressinus

Polyclados cupressinus, commonly known as the Mediterranean cypress, is a coniferous evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and a compact, pyramidal shape. The plant produces small, scale-like leaves and has a unique reproductive system involving both male and female cones. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for coastal and dry environments.

Its ornamental value and resilience make it a popular choice in horticulture and landscape design.
