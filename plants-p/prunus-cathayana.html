Prunus cathayana

Prunus cathayana, commonly known as the Chinese bird cherry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring, often in shades of white and pink. The plant produces small, edible berries that turn from green to red as they mature. Its leaves are elliptical with serrated edges and turn yellow in the autumn.

Prunus cathayana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
