Paphiopedilum bullenianum var. bullenianum

Paphiopedilum bullenianum var. bullenianum, commonly known as the Bullen's lady's slipper, is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

This species is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch, which is a defining feature of the Paphiopedilum genus. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid, thriving in shaded, moist environments with well-drained soil. Its foliage consists of broad, leathery leaves that help it conserve moisture in its natural habitat.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts and protected in conservation efforts.
