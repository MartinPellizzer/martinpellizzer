Promenaea stapelioides f. viridiflora

Promenaea stapelioides f. viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered stapelioid, is a rare and striking hybrid within the Apocynaceae family.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated leaves that are typically a deep green, often with a waxy texture.
