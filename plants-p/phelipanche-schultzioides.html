Phelipanche schultzioides

Phelipanche schultzioides, commonly known as the yellow broomweed, is an annual herbaceous plant belonging to the Orobanchaceae family.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and hairy stems, which help it retain moisture in arid environments. Phelipanche schultzioides is native to regions of southern Europe and the Mediterranean, where it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats.
