Polystichum × midoriense

Polystichum × midoriense, commonly known as the Emerald Fern, is a hybrid evergreen fern native to Japan.

It features lush, dark green, leathery fronds that are densely packed and arching, giving it a compact, bushy appearance. The plant produces spore-bearing structures called sori on the undersides of its fronds, which are typically covered by a protective indusium. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage.

The hybrid nature of this fern contributes to its unique characteristics and adaptability.
