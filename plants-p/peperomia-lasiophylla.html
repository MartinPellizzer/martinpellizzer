Peperomia lasiophylla

Peperomia lasiophylla, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance houseplant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features broad, waxy, dark green leaves with a velvety texture, and its foliage often has a bluish or purplish tinge. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia lasiophylla is prized for its ornamental leaves and ability to thrive indoors with minimal care.
