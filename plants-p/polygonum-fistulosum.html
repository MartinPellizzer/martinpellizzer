Polygonum fistulosum

Polygonum fistulosum, commonly known as the slender knotweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to temperate regions of Asia.

It features upright stems that are often hollow and covered with fine hairs. The leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and have a slightly serrated margin. Small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and the plant produces small, three-lobed seeds.

It is often found in moist environments such as stream banks and wet meadows.
