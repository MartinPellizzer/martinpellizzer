Palaquium vexillatum

Palaquium vexillatum, commonly known as the wax apple or white mangosteen, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. The tree is also valued for its potential medicinal properties.
