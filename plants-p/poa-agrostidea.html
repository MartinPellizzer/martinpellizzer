Poa agrostidea

Poa agrostidea, commonly known as slender meadowgrass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along stream banks. The plant has slender, tufted stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences with tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that becomes more prominent in the summer months.

Poa agrostidea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant landscaping.
