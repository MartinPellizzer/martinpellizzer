Pleurothallis compacta

Pleurothallis compacta, commonly known as the compact orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has slender, wiry stems and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white, yellow, or green. Its leaves are usually narrow and arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it conserve moisture.

Pleurothallis compacta is appreciated for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
