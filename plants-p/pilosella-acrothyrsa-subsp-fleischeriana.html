Pilosella acrothyrsa subsp. fleischeriana

Pilosella acrothyrsa subsp.

fleischeriana, commonly known as the Fleischer's pearly everlet, is a flowering plant native to Central and Eastern Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, white to pale yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry grasslands and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are hairy and grayish-green, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is a perennial herb that produces numerous small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.
