Prosthechea tinukiana

Prosthechea tinukiana, commonly known as the Tinukian orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a short, creeping rhizome and produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers. The plant's leaves are ovate and often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow with a distinctive, spurred lip.

Prosthechea tinukiana thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental beauty in horticulture.
