Pterigeron liatroides var. humilis

Pterigeron liatroides var. humilis, commonly known as the dwarf pterigeron, is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight. It is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover due to its compact form and ornamental appeal.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and drought-tolerant choice for xeriscaping.
