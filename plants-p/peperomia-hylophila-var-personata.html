Peperomia hylophila var. personata

Peperomia hylophila var. personata, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features broad, velvety leaves with a deep green color and prominent veins, giving it a striking, textured appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and collection.
