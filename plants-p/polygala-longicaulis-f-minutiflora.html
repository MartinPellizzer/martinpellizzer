Polygala longicaulis f. minutiflora

Polygala longicaulis f. minutiflora, commonly known as the dwarf-flowered milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the Polygalaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. The plant has slender, wiry stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This subspecies is notable for its compact growth habit and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
