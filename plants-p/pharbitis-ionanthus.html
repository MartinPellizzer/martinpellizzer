Pharbitis ionanthus

Pharbitis ionanthus, commonly known as the scarlet runner bean, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and large, showy flowers that range from pink to deep red. The plant produces edible pods that contain seeds used in both culinary and medicinal applications. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three heart-shaped leaflets, and the plant thrives in warm, sunny climates.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its nutritional benefits.
