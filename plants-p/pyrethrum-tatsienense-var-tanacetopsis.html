Pyrethrum tatsienense var. tanacetopsis

Pyrethrum tatsienense var. tanacetopsis, commonly known as Chinese pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant is known for producing pyrethrins, which are natural insecticides. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pesticidal properties.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species in both horticulture and pest control.
