Phaniasia

Phaniasia, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hanging clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with elongated leaflets and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Phaniasia is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
