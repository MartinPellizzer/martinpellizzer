Pelargonium × jenkinsonii

Pelargonium × jenkinsonii, commonly known as the Zonal Geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its colorful, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white, often with a distinctive eye or band in the center. The plant features rounded, velvety leaves with distinct color zones, giving it its common name. It is a popular choice for gardens due to its ornamental value and ability to thrive in various climates.

Pelargonium × jenkinsonii is typically propagated through cuttings and prefers well-drained soil and full sun.
