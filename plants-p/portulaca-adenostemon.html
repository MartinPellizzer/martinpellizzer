Portulaca adenostemon

Portulaca adenostemon, commonly known as the desert purslane, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant is well adapted to hot, dry climates, with a shallow root system that allows it to thrive in sandy soils. It is often found in disturbed areas, such as roadsides and fields, due to its rapid growth and ability to reproduce prolifically.

Portulaca adenostemon is also known for its medicinal properties, traditionally used in herbal remedies.
