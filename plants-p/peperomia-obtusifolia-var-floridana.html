Peperomia obtusifolia var. floridana

Peperomia obtusifolia var. floridana, commonly known as the Florida peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features thick, waxy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a subtle sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements, it is well-suited for both novice and experienced plant growers.
