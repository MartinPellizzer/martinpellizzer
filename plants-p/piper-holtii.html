Piper holtii

Piper holtii, commonly known as the Mexican pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its slender, climbing vines and small, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in spikes. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its ability to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates.

Piper holtii is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
