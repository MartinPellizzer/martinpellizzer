Pomatocalpa tonkinense

Pomatocalpa tonkinense, commonly known as the Tonkin orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant features a slender rhizome and produces leaves that are narrow and grass-like. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellowish with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
