Polystachya seticaulis

Polystachya seticaulis, commonly known as the stiff-stemmed polystachya, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces inflorescences of small, delicate white or pale green flowers that bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat preferences, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
