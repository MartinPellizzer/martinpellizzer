Pleurostelma

Pleurostelma, commonly known as the star orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

These plants are native to tropical and subtropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. They are characterized by their compact growth habit and showy, star-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors. Pleurostelma species typically thrive in well-drained soil and require bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Their unique floral structure and vibrant hues make them popular among orchid enthusiasts.
