Philodendron phlebodes var. kermesinum

Philodendron phlebodes var. kermesinum, commonly known as the Kermesinum Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Its striking foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and hanging baskets.
