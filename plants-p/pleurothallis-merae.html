Pleurothallis merae

Pleurothallis merae, commonly known as the Merae orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and green. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surfaces of trees and other structures without deriving nutrients from them. Pleurothallis merae prefers high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for terrariums and shaded greenhouse environments.

Its unique floral structure and rarity make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
