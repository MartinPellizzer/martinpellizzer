Pearcea sprucei

Pearcea sprucei, commonly known as the Spruce's pearcea, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its evergreen leaves and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or alpine regions. Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.

Pearcea sprucei is of interest to botanists due to its unique adaptations to cold and high-altitude conditions.
