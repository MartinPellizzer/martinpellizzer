Podochilus lucidus

Podochilus lucidus, commonly known as the shining orchid, is a tropical plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its striking, often colorful flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid forest environments. Its leaves are usually broad and glossy, and it produces small, fragrant blossoms that bloom in clusters.

Podochilus lucidus is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
