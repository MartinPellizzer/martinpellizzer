Pleurothallis schudelii

Pleurothallis schudelii, commonly known as the Schudel's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andes of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant has delicate, linear leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its intricate floral structure.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments to ensure its survival.
