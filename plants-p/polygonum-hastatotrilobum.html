Polygonum hastatotrilobum

Polygonum hastatotrilobum, commonly known as the three-lobed smartweed, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Polygonaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive three-lobed leaves that are often hairy and have a reddish or purple tint. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in dense spikes, which are typically borne on slender, erect stems. It thrives in moist environments such as wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks.

Polygonum hastatotrilobum is often found in temperate regions and is known for its ability to tolerate a range of soil conditions.
