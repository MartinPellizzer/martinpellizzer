Peperomia cordulatiformis

Peperomia cordulatiformis, commonly known as the heart-shaped peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique leaf shape and ease of care make it a popular choice for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
