Piper tarapotianum

Piper tarapotianum, commonly known as the Tarapotian pepper, is a climbing vine native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by berries containing aromatic seeds. Its leaves are typically ovate with a rough texture and are often used in traditional medicine.

The plant's botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ethnobotanical study.
