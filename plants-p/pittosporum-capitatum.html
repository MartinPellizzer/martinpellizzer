Pittosporum capitatum

Pittosporum capitatum, commonly known as the yellow pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of fragrant, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, with a glossy appearance.

Pittosporum capitatum is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
