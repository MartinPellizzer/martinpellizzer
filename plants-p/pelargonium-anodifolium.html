Pelargonium anodifolium

Pelargonium anodifolium, commonly known as the silver-leaf geranium, is a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, typically blooming in spring and summer. It is a hardy perennial that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and containers.
