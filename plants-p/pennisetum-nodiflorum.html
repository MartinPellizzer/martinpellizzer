Pennisetum nodiflorum

Pennisetum nodiflorum, commonly known as purple lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and its distinctive purple flower heads that appear in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for a variety of climates.

The species is also valued for its potential in erosion control and as a forage crop.
