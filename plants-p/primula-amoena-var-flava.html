Primula amoena var. flava

Primula amoena var. flava, commonly known as the yellow primrose, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pale yellow to orange, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are slightly hairy. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial to full shade.

Its flowers are often used in garden displays for their ornamental value.
