Philodendron heterophyllum var. elaphoglossoides

Philodendron heterophyllum var. elaphoglossoides, commonly known as the silver spider plant, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive silvery-white variegation pattern, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has a vigorous growth habit and can reach lengths of several meters when provided with adequate support. Its foliage is often described as resembling that of a fern, contributing to its ornamental value.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.
