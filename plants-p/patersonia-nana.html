Patersonia nana

Patersonia nana, commonly known as the dwarf patersonia, is a small flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has a low-growing habit with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grassy areas.

Patersonia nana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
