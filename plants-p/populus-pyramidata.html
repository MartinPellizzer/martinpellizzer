Populus pyramidata

Populus pyramidata, commonly known as the Chinese poplar, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and conical shape, which gives it a striking appearance. The tree has smooth, light gray bark that becomes fissured with age. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a serrated margin, turning yellow in the fall.

Populus pyramidata is known for its rapid growth and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.
