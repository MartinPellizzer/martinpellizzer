Polygonum splendens

Polygonum splendens, commonly known as the flame flower or red polygonum, is a vibrant annual plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, showy red or orange flowers that bloom in dense clusters, often resembling flames. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Polygonum splendens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
