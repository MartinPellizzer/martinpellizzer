Polygonum acre var. brachystachyum

Polygonum acre var. brachystachyum, commonly known as dwarf smartweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist soils along stream banks, wet meadows, and other wetland habitats. The plant features alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often reddish or purple-tinged, especially along the veins. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink or red, and they bloom in late summer to early fall.

The species is characterized by its short, stout stems and is often found in disturbed or wet environments.
