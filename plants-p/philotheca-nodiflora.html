Philotheca nodiflora

Philotheca nodiflora, commonly known as the clustered leafed philotheca, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features aromatic, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white, clustered flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Philotheca nodiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native gardens.
