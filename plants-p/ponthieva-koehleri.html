Ponthieva koehleri

Ponthieva koehleri, commonly known as the Koehler's ponthieva, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, ribbon-like leaves that float on the water's surface. The plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in ponds and wetlands. Its growth is typically submerged, with roots anchoring it to the substrate.

Ponthieva koehleri is valued in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
