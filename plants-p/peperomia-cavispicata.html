Peperomia cavispicata

Peperomia cavispicata, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and attractive foliage, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant.
