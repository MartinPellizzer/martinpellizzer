Phyllanthus rubescens

Phyllanthus rubescens, commonly known as red sanders, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its dense, aromatic foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers. Phyllanthus rubescens is also known for its ability to produce a unique resin that has been used in various cultural and medicinal practices.
