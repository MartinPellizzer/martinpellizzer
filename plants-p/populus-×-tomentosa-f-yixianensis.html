Populus × tomentosa f. yixianensis

Populus × tomentosa f. yixianensis, commonly known as the Yixian poplar, is a unique hybrid species within the Populus genus.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-white tomentose (fuzzy) bark and heart-shaped leaves with a serrated margin. This plant is native to northern China and is often found in mountainous regions with well-drained soils. It exhibits rapid growth and is adapted to temperate climates with cold winters.

The Yixian poplar is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological benefits in reforestation efforts.
