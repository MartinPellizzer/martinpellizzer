Plectranthus gamblei

Plectranthus gamblei, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces clusters of tubular, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The leaves are typically oval-shaped and have a slightly fuzzy texture. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and is often used in gardens for its ornamental value.

This plant is also popular as a houseplant due to its adaptability and ease of care.
