Peperomia reineckei

Peperomia reineckei, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery or grayish waxy coating, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and drought tolerance.

Peperomia reineckei thrives in well-draining soil and prefers moderate to warm temperatures.
