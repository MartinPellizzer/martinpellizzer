Piper talbotii

Piper talbotii, commonly known as Talbot's pepper, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are typically alternate, ovate to elliptic in shape, and have a slightly rough texture.

Piper talbotii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has a mild, peppery aroma.
