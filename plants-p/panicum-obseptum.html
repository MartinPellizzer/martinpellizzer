Panicum obseptum

Panicum obseptum, commonly known as the slender panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It is often found in wetland ecosystems where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife.

Due to its preference for wet conditions, it is sometimes used in wetland restoration projects.
