Peperomia purpureonervosa

Peperomia purpureonervosa, commonly known as the purple-veined peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, waxy leaves with distinct purple veins, giving it a striking visual appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia purpureonervosa is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
