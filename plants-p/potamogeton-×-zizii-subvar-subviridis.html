Potamogeton × zizii subvar. subviridis

Potamogeton × zizii subvar. subviridis, commonly known as the slender pondweed, is a hybrid aquatic plant that exhibits characteristics of both its parent species.

It is typically found in freshwater environments such as ponds, lakes, and slow-moving streams. The plant has long, narrow leaves that are often submerged, with a light green to yellowish-green coloration. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in spike-like inflorescences.

This subvariety is known for its ability to form dense underwater vegetation, which can provide habitat for various aquatic organisms.
