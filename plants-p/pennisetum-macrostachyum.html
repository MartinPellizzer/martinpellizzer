Pennisetum macrostachyum

Pennisetum macrostachyum, commonly known as giant feather grass, is a tall, ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features slender, silver-gray leaves and produces feathery, plume-like flower spikes that add dramatic visual interest. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for landscaping. It has a clumping growth habit and can reach heights of up to 6 feet.

Its botanical characteristics make it both aesthetically pleasing and adaptable to various garden environments.
