Pollichia campestris

Pollichia campestris, commonly known as the field heather, is a flowering plant native to Europe, North Africa, and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or white, blooming in the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing a dense cover in its natural habitat.

Pollichia campestris thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.
