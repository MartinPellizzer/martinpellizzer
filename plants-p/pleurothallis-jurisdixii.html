Pleurothallis jurisdixii

Pleurothallis jurisdixii, commonly known as the jurisdixii orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It requires high humidity and indirect light to thrive, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
