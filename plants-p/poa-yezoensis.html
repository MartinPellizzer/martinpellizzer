Poa yezoensis

Poa yezoensis, commonly known as Yezo grass, is a species of grass native to Japan and parts of eastern Russia.

It is a perennial plant that typically grows in moist, well-drained soils, often found in mountainous regions. The plant has slender, tufted shoots and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Poa yezoensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping or as a ground cover.
