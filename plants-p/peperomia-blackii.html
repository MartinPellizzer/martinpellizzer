Peperomia blackii

Peperomia blackii, commonly known as the Blackii Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
