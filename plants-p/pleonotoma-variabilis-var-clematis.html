Pleonotoma variabilis var. clematis

Pleonotoma variabilis var. clematis, commonly known as the Clematis pleonotoma, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Ranunculaceae.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, usually blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features trifoliate leaves with serrated edges and has a fibrous root system that helps it anchor to supports as it climbs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners looking to add ornamental value to their landscapes.

This variety is valued for its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
