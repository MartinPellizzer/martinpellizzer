Pleurophyllum oresigenesum

Pleurophyllum oresigenesum, commonly known as the Southern Alps bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to New Zealand.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue, typically blooming in spring. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine environments.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
