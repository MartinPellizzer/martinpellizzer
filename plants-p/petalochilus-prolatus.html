Petalochilus prolatus

Petalochilus prolatus, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions such as Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its flowers are small and white, with a delicate, fragrant scent.

Petalochilus prolatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
