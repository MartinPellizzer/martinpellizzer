Plumbago capensis

Plumbago capensis, commonly known as blue plumbago, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple tubular flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has woody stems and is often grown as an ornamental due to its vibrant color. It prefers full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

Its foliage is dark green and evergreen, adding visual interest throughout the year.
