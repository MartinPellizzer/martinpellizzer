Pseudixora truncata

Pseudixora truncata, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green foliage and clusters of showy, tubular flowers. Pseudixora truncata is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
