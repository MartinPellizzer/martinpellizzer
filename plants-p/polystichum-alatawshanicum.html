Polystichum alatawshanicum

Polystichum alatawshanicum, commonly known as the Alatawshan shield fern, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial evergreen fern characterized by its large, leathery, deeply lobed fronds that are typically arranged in a compact, rosette-like structure. The plant thrives in cool, moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories or on rocky slopes. Its fronds are covered in a dense layer of fine, silky hairs, which help to retain moisture and protect against desiccation.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in specialized gardens.
