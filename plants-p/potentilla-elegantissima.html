Potentilla elegantissima

Potentilla elegantissima, commonly known as the elegant cinquefoil, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It features delicate, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, arching stems covered in fine hairs and produces small, rounded leaves with a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in alpine and rocky habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
