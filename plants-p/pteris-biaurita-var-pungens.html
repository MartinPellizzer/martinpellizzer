Pteris biaurita var. pungens

Pteris biaurita var. pungens, commonly known as the spiny water fern, is a variant of the water fern genus Pteris.

It is characterized by its finely divided, bi-pinnate fronds that are often covered with small, spiny projections. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its fronds are usually dark green and can reach lengths of up to 1 meter.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in water gardens or as a houseplant.
