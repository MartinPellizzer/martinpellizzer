Pittosporum illicioides var. stenophyllum

Pittosporum illicioides var. stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or forested areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in native ecosystems.
