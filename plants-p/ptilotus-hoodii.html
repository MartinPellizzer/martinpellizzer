Ptilotus hoodii

Ptilotus hoodii, commonly known as the hooded spinifex, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red, often resembling a hood or cap, hence its common name. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to drought and high temperatures.

Ptilotus hoodii is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
