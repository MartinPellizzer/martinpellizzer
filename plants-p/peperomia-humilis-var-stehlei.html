Peperomia humilis var. stehlei

Peperomia humilis var. stehlei, commonly known as the silver stripe peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or silver markings along the midrib. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its attractive foliage and ease of care contribute to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
