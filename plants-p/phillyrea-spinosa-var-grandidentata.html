Phillyrea spinosa var. grandidentata

Phillyrea spinosa var. grandidentata, commonly known as the grand-leaf Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage with large, elliptical leaves that have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, which are followed by spherical, woody fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
