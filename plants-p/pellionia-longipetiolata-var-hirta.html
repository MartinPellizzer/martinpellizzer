Pellionia longipetiolata var. hirta

Pellionia longipetiolata var. hirta, commonly known as hairy pellionia, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a hairy texture on both surfaces, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its foliage is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticultural settings.
