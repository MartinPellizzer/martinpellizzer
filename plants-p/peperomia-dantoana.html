Peperomia dantoana

Peperomia dantoana, commonly known as the Dantoan Peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to South America, specifically Brazil.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit. The plant features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slight variegation. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color.

Peperomia dantoana is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
