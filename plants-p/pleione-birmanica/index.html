Pleione Birmanica

Burmese orchid, scientifically known as Pleione birmanica, is a rare and exquisite flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Myanmar. This captivating species belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is celebrated for its delicate beauty and unique growth habits. It thrives in cool, humid environments, often growing on tree trunks or in shaded forest floors. With its striking blooms and medicinal properties, it holds cultural and ecological significance in its native habitat. Despite its allure, the Burmese orchid faces threats from habitat loss and overharvesting, making conservation efforts essential for its survival.
