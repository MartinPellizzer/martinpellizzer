Paris polyphylla var. kwantungensis

Paris polyphylla var. kwantungensis, commonly known as the Kwantung snake plant, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically green with a subtle variegation. The plant produces clusters of small, white spadices that are surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It is a hardy perennial that thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is a popular choice for gardens and indoor cultivation.
