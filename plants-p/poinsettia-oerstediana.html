Poinsettia oerstediana

Poinsettia oerstediana, commonly known as the red poinsettia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking red bracts, which are modified leaves that resemble flowers. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is closely related to other poinsettia species. It thrives in warm, sunny environments and requires well-draining soil.
