Pavonia narcissi

Pavonia narcissi, commonly known as the yellow shower vine, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a velvety texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

This plant is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to cascade from containers or trellises.
