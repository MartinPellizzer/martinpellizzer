Pleurothallis dukei

Pleurothallis dukei, commonly known as the Duke's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on tree branches or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, narrow leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its flowers are often clustered in racemes and bloom during the wet season.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts and botanists.
