Potentilla decipiens

Potentilla decipiens, commonly known as the deceptive potentilla, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Potentilla decipiens is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to harsh, high-altitude environments.
