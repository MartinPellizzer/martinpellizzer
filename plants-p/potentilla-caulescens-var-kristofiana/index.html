Potentilla Caulescens Var. Kristofiana

Kristofiana cinquefoil, scientifically known as Potentilla caulescens var. kristofiana, is a rare and striking member of the Rosaceae family found in the alpine regions of the European Alps. This plant is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and rugged, compact growth habit, making it a standout in its native environment. It thrives in rocky soils and high-altitude conditions, showcasing its adaptability to harsh climates. Due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, it is considered a valuable species for botanical research and conservation efforts. Its unique appearance and ecological role make it a fascinating subject for both scientists and nature enthusiasts.
