Pilea chotardiana

Pilea chotardiana, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rounded, fleshy leaves that resemble small coins, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea chotardiana is known for its easy care and ability to purify the air, enhancing both aesthetics and indoor environment quality.
