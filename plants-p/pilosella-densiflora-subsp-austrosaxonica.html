Pilosella densiflora subsp. austrosaxonica

Pilosella densiflora subsp.

austrosaxonica, commonly known as the dense-flowered hawk's-beard, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to central and eastern Europe. It typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils.

The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow composite flowers with ray florets. Its inflorescence is characterized by a dense cluster of small, yellow flowers arranged in a compact head.

It is often found in nutrient-poor soils and is considered a characteristic species of dry, sunny environments.
