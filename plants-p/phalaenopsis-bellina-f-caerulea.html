Phalaenopsis bellina f. caerulea

Phalaenopsis bellina f. caerulea, commonly known as the blue phalaenopsis orchid, is a hybrid orchid prized for its elegant, long-lasting blooms.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, tropical environments. The plant features broad, dark green leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, semi-closed flowers in shades of blue and purple. It is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to bloom year-round under proper care.

This variety is particularly noted for its unique coloration and ornamental value in horticulture.
