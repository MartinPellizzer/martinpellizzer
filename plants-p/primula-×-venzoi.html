Primula × venzoi

Primula × venzoi, commonly known as the Venzoi primrose, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Primula sinensis and Primula veris.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of pink, white, and yellow. The plant features heart-shaped, broad leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

The flowers bloom in early spring, adding a touch of color to the landscape during the transition from winter to summer.
