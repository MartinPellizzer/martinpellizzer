Psychotria nilgiriensis var. nilgiriensis

Psychotria nilgiriensis var. nilgiriensis, commonly known as the Nilgiri Psychotria, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features small, elliptical leaves with a glossy surface and produces clusters of small, white or pale green flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.

The species is of interest in botanical studies due to its unique adaptations to its native habitat.
