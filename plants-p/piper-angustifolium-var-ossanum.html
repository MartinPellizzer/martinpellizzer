Piper angustifolium var. ossanum

Piper angustifolium var. ossanum, commonly known as the Ossanum pepper plant, is a variant of the narrow-leaf pepper species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that transition into tiny, round berries containing a single seed. These berries are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Its botanical features make it a unique subject of study in plant morphology and ethnobotany.
