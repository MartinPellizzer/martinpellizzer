Peperomia pubicaulis

Peperomia pubicaulis, commonly known as the hairy peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, ovate leaves that are covered in fine, short hairs, giving it a distinctive textured appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or white, which are typically borne on short, erect spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia pubicaulis is known for its low maintenance and ability to adapt well to various indoor environments.
