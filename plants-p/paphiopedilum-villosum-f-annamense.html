Paphiopedilum villosum f. annamense

Paphiopedilum villosum f. annamense, commonly known as the Annamese velvet orchid, is a rare and elegant orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and striking, showy flowers with a unique lip structure. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial or epiphytic orchid, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers, which bloom in late spring or early summer, feature a blend of white and pink hues with intricate patterns.

This orchid is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its distinctive appearance.
