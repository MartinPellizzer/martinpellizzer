Prescottia polysphaera

Prescottia polysphaera, commonly known as the Cape mountain lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, slender stems with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant is characterized by its distinctive, spherical bulbs and is adapted to thrive in rocky, well-drained soils. It blooms in late winter to early spring, adding a dramatic presence to its natural habitat.

Due to its beauty and rarity, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
