Plumeria subsessilis

Plumeria subsessilis, commonly known as the Hawaiian plumeria, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant has simple, opposite, and often hairy leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. Its bark is smooth and pale, and it produces a milky latex when the stems are broken.

Plumeria subsessilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Hawaiian cultural practices.
