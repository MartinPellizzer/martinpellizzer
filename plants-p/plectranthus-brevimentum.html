Plectranthus brevimentum

Plectranthus brevimentum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved plectranthus, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features aromatic, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often grayish-green in color. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in hue, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens or as a houseplant.
