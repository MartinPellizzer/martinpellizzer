Peperomia rotundifolia var. nummulariifolia

Peperomia rotundifolia var. nummulariifolia, commonly known as the coinleaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are typically a glossy green, resembling small coins, hence its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, often in shades of white or pale pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its low maintenance and attractive foliage make it suitable for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
