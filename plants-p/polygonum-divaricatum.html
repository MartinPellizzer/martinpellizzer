Polygonum divaricatum

Polygonum divaricatum, commonly known as the redstem filaree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Polygonaceae.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features slender, reddish stems and small, clustered leaves that are alternate along the stem. Its flowers are usually pink or red, appearing in late summer to early fall.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is native to parts of North America.
