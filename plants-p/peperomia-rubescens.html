Peperomia rubescens

Peperomia rubescens, commonly known as the red peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that often have a reddish or purplish tinge on the underside, giving it a striking visual appeal. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring or summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia rubescens is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
