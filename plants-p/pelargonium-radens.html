Pelargonium radens

Pelargonium radens, commonly known as the red geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant red flowers that bloom in late summer. The leaves are typically rounded with a slightly hairy texture, and the plant produces small, dark-colored fruits. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Pelargonium radens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
