Piper betle f. densum

Piper betle f. densum, commonly known as the dense betel leaf plant, is a variant of the betel leaf (Piper betle), native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, broad leaves that are often used in traditional practices and as a spice. The plant belongs to the Piperaceae family and is closely related to other members of the genus Piper. Its leaves are typically harvested for their aromatic properties and are used in various culinary and medicinal applications.

The plant thrives in tropical climates and requires high humidity and well-drained soil for optimal growth.
