Pseuderia takeuchii

Pseuderia takeuchii, commonly known as the Japanese spider orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance with long, slender petals. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. It produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom during the spring season.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
