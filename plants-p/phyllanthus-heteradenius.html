Phyllanthus heteradenius

Phyllanthus heteradenius, commonly known as the African date palm, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub that typically grows up to 2 meters in height. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are elliptic to ovate in shape with a smooth margin. Its flowers are small and clustered in panicles, and it produces small, fleshy fruits that are yellow or orange in color.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
