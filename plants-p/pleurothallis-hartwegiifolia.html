Pleurothallis hartwegiifolia

Pleurothallis hartwegiifolia, commonly known as the Hartweg's pleurothallis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lanceolate leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It thrives in high humidity and requires well-draining, porous media for optimal growth.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in specialized orchid gardens.
