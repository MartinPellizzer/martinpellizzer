Persicaria posumbu var. stenophylla

Persicaria posumbu var. stenophylla, commonly known as narrow-leaf smartweed, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like buckwheat and sorrel. The plant typically grows in moist or wet habitats, such as marshes and stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
