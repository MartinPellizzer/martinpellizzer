Plectranthus mutabilis

Plectranthus mutabilis, commonly known as the purple heart plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its striking purple and pink tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture.

Plectranthus mutabilis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant color and adaptability to various growing conditions.
