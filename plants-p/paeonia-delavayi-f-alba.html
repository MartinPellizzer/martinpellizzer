Paeonia delavayi f. alba

Paeonia delavayi f. alba, commonly known as the white peony, is a cultivar of the peony plant native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant is a herbaceous perennial with thick, fleshy rhizomes that store nutrients. Its foliage consists of deeply lobed, dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to the white blooms.

This variety is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
