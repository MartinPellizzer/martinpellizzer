Pleopeltis moseleyi

Pleopeltis moseleyi, commonly known as the Moseley's rock fern, is a terrestrial fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its creeping rhizomes and dense, compact foliage. The fronds are typically leathery and have a distinctive, often serrated margin. It thrives in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices.
