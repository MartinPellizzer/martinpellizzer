Panicum brevisetum

Panicum brevisetum, commonly known as the short-bracted panicgrass, is a species of grass native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and short, narrow leaves. The plant typically produces small, fluffy panicles that are often pale green to brown in color. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Panicum brevisetum is adapted to arid conditions and is often used in restoration projects due to its resilience.
