Piptochaetium brevicalyx

Piptochaetium brevicalyx, commonly known as the slender needlegrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves with a distinctive blue-green coloration. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and disturbed areas.

Due to its drought tolerance, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
