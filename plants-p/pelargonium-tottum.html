Pelargonium tottum

Pelargonium tottum, commonly known as the Cape rose or the red geranium, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces vibrant pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has rounded, lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Pelargonium tottum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
