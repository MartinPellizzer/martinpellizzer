Prunus × Lannesiana F. Nobilis

Prunus × lannesiana f. nobilis is a unique and elegant ornamental plant known for its striking white flowers and compact growth habit. It is a hybrid of the Japanese cherry (Prunus serrulata) and the weeping cherry (Prunus pendula), resulting in a beautiful blend of characteristics from both parents. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly used in gardens and public landscapes. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a touch of delicate beauty to the landscape. Despite its popularity, it requires careful attention to soil and climate conditions to thrive.
