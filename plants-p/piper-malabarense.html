Piper malabarense

Piper malabarense, commonly known as the Malabar pepper, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing habit and heart-shaped leaves. Its small, greenish-white flowers are followed by the fruit, which is a small, elongated berry.

The plant is valued for its aromatic leaves and is often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.
