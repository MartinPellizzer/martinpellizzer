Peperomia tonduzii

Peperomia tonduzii, commonly known as the Tonduzii Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to Brazil.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually white or pale pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and ornamental foliage, it is often used as a decorative plant in shaded environments.
