Plectranthus congestus

Plectranthus congestus, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The leaves are typically velvety and heart-shaped, with a rich green color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
