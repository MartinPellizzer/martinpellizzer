Polygala nivea

Polygala nivea, commonly known as the snow polygala, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, open habitats such as woodlands and wetlands. The plant features delicate, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

The snow polygala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
