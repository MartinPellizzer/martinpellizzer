Pouzolzia bennettiana var. mysorensis

Pouzolzia bennettiana var. mysorensis, commonly known as the Mysore pouzolzia, is a perennial shrub native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies in tropical rainforests.
