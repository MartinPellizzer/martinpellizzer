Pertya sinensis

Pertya sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elongated and strap-like. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a subtle ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid environments and prefers indirect sunlight, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ability to purify the air and tolerate low light conditions further enhances its appeal as a houseplant.
