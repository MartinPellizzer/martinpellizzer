Pericalia ovatifolia

Pericalia ovatifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf pericalia, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its ovate, glossy leaves that are often arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which bloom in clusters. Pericalia ovatifolia is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and manageable size.

It prefers warm, humid conditions and well-draining soil to thrive.
