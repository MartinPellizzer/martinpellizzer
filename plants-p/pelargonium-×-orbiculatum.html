Pelargonium × orbiculatum

Pelargonium × orbiculatum, commonly known as the African hybrid geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The leaves are typically rounded and have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

This species is also popular as a houseplant due to its adaptability and ease of care.
