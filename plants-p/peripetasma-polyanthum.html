Peripetasma polyanthum

Peripetasma polyanthum, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while the roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption.
