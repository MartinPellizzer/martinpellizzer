Primulina subulata var. yangchunensis

Primulina subulata var. yangchunensis, commonly known as the Yangchun primrose.

It is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to China, specifically the Guangdong province. This plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking foliage, featuring narrow, pointed leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The flowers are typically small, tubular, and come in shades of pink or white, blooming in late spring to early summer.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
