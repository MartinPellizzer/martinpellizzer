Panicum umbonulatum

Panicum umbonulatum, commonly known as the velvet grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and hairy, bluish-green foliage. The plant produces panicles of small, spikelet-based flowers that are often tinged with red or purple. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental and ecological landscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ability to stabilize soil make it a valuable plant in restoration projects.
