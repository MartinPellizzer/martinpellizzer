Palisota akouangoui

Palisota akouangoui, commonly known as the Cameroon arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to West Africa.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and striking spadix inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal in horticultural settings.
