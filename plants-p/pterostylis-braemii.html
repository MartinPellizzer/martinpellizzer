Pterostylis braemii

Pterostylis braemii, commonly known as the Braem's helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as rainforests and understoreys. The plant features a single, erect stem with a single or few flowers, each resembling a helmet-shaped structure. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a prominent purple or brown lip.

The species is noted for its specialized pollination mechanism, relying on specific insect pollinators for reproduction.
