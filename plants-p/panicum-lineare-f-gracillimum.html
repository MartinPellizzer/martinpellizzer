Panicum lineare f. gracillimum

Panicum lineare f. gracillimum, commonly known as slender flatgrass, is a grass species characterized by its narrow, flat leaves and delicate appearance.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in moist to wet environments, such as marshes and wet meadows. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its leaves are usually a bluish-green color and have a smooth texture.

This variety is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal in wetland gardens.
