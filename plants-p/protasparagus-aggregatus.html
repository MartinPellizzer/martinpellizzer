Protasparagus aggregatus

Protasparagus aggregatus, commonly known as the clustered asparagus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, branching stems that often grow in dense clusters, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that are clustered in racemes. Its leaves are needle-like and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems.

It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky soils and along riverbanks.
