Paris kwantungensis

Paris kwantungensis, commonly known as the Kwantung bellflower, is a rare and delicate herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Parisaceae and is characterized by its small, bell-shaped flowers that typically bloom in early spring. The plant has a short, creeping rhizome and produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest undergrowth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
