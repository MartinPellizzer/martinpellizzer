Primula × obristii

Primula × obristii, commonly known as the hybrid primrose, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern China.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has dark green, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine layer of down. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Its hybrid origin results from crosses between various Primula species, contributing to its genetic diversity and adaptability.
