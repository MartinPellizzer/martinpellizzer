Poa sinoglauca

Poa sinoglauca, commonly known as the Chinese bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its blue-green foliage gives it a distinctive appearance, and it is often found in temperate regions.

Poa sinoglauca is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological role in stabilizing soil in natural environments.
