Polystachya aethiopica

Polystachya aethiopica, commonly known as the Ethiopian spikegrass, is a species of orchid native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and dense, spike-like inflorescence that bears numerous small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has fibrous roots and is adapted to dry conditions, making it a hardy species.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both ecological studies and horticultural cultivation.
