Pimpinella major var. bipinnata

Pimpinella major var. bipinnata, commonly known as the bipinnate cowparsley, is a biennial or short-lived perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in umbels, typical of the Apiaceae family. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, along roadsides, and in woodland edges.
