Piper medinillifolium

Piper medinillifolium, commonly known as the Mexican pepper plant, is a tropical shrub native to Central America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants. The plant features dark green, glossy leaves with a slightly serrated edge. Its small, greenish-white flowers are followed by small, round berries that turn red when mature.

The leaves and fruits are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
