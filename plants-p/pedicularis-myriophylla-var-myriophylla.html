Pedicularis myriophylla var. myriophylla

Pedicularis myriophylla var. myriophylla, commonly known as the flame flower, is a striking member of the Orobanchaceae family.

It is characterized by its vivid red or orange tubular flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is native to the alpine regions of Europe and is often found in rocky or open habitats.
