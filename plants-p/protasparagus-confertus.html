Protasparagus confertus

Protasparagus confertus, commonly known as the dense asparagus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered white or pale blue flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Due to its ornamental value and resilience, it is occasionally cultivated in horticultural settings.
