Protea recondita

Protea recondita, commonly known as the silver protea, is a striking South African native plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences composed of numerous white to pinkish flowers arranged in a spherical or conical shape. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, often found in fynbos vegetation. Its unique floral structure attracts a variety of pollinators, including birds and insects.

Protea recondita is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
