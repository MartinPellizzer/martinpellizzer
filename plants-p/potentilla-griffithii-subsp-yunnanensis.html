Potentilla griffithii subsp. yunnanensis

Potentilla griffithii subsp.

yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to China. It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with serrated edges, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and attractive foliage.
