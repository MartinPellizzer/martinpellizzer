Pennisetum gracilescens

Pennisetum gracilescens, commonly known as slender fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and fluffy, feathery plumes that give it a distinctive, ornamental appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas and is adapted to warm, dry climates. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in late summer.

Pennisetum gracilescens is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
