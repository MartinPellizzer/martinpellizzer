Platystachys juncea

Platystachys juncea, commonly known as the Chinese flame bamboo, is a perennial grass native to China.

It features tall, slender stems that can grow up to 3 meters in height, with narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces panicles of small, bright orange to red flowers during the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its fast growth rate and ability to form dense clumps make it both decorative and useful for erosion control.
