Pleurothallis pachyrhyncha

Pleurothallis pachyrhyncha, commonly known as the thick-beaked pleurothallis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This epiphytic plant typically grows on trees and has small, clustered pseudobulbs. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a distinctive, elongated labellum.

The plant is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
