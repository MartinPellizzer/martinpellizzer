Podocarpus humbertii

Podocarpus humbertii, commonly known as the Huertii podocarp, is a member of the Podocarpaceae family and is native to Madagascar.

It is a slow-growing evergreen tree that typically reaches heights of up to 15 meters. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. Its cones are small and woody, containing seeds that are dispersed by wind.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
