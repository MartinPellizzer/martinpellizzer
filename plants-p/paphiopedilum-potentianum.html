Paphiopedilum potentianum

Paphiopedilum potentianum, commonly known as the Chinese lady's slipper, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It features a unique, pouch-like lip that is a distinguishing characteristic of the genus Paphiopedilum. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments with rich, organic soil. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to green with intricate patterns, and it blooms in late spring to early summer.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
