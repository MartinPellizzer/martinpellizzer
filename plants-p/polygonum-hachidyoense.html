Polygonum hachidyoense

Polygonum hachidyoense, commonly known as the Hachidyo Polygonum, is a species of flowering plant in the buckwheat family, Polygonaceae.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its erect growth habit and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along streambanks.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species in its native habitat.
