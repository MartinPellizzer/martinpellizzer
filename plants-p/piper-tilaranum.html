Piper tilaranum

Piper tilaranum, commonly known as the Tilaran pepper, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when ripe, containing the spicy compound piperine. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.

The leaves are also used in traditional herbal remedies.
