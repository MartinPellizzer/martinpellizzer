Piper impube

Piper impube, commonly known as the slender pipevine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Piperaceae, which includes many species of flowering plants. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in spikes. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

The plant is often found in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests.
