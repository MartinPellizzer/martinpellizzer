Pilosella cymosoides

Pilosella cymosoides, commonly known as the clustered pearly everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. Pilosella cymosoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
