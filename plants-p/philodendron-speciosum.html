Philodendron speciosum

Philodendron speciosum, commonly known as the shining heartleaf philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves that often have a striking yellow or white variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Philodendron speciosum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
