Pleurothallis tenuis

Pleurothallis tenuis, commonly known as the slender pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, narrow leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is part of the genus Pleurothallis, which includes over 1,000 species.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often sought after by orchid enthusiasts and botanists.
