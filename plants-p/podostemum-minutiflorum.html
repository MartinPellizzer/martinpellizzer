Podostemum minutiflorum

Podostemum minutiflorum, commonly known as the dwarf flowered water plant, is a rare aquatic plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers and its unique adaptation to growing in fast-flowing freshwater environments. The plant typically attaches itself to rocks or submerged vegetation using its specialized roots. Its leaves are narrow and ribbon-like, and it produces tiny, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom close to the water's surface.
