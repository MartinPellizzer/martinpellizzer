Penstemon sudans

Penstemon sudans, commonly known as the Sudanese beardtongue, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Sudan.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. It is often found in rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in drought-tolerant gardens.
