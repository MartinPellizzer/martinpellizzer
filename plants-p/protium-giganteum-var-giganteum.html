Protium giganteum var. giganteum

Protium giganteum var. giganteum, commonly known as the giant tamanu, is a tropical tree native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, glossy leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The tree's fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The plant has a unique ecological role and is valued for its hardy nature and potential medicinal properties.
