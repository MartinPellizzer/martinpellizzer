Pleurothallis macropoda var. laevis

Pleurothallis macropoda var. laevis, commonly known as the smooth pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and elongated, narrow leaves that are typically covered in a fine, silky texture. The flowers are small and clustered, often displaying a range of colors from white to pale pink, with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
