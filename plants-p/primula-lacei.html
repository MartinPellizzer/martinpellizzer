Primula lacei

Primula lacei, commonly known as the lace primrose, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cool, moist climates. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine, silky hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Primula lacei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
