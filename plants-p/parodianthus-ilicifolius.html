Parodianthus ilicifolius

Parodianthus ilicifolius, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage that gives it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the cooler months. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in dry conditions.

Parodianthus ilicifolius is also valued for its use in traditional medicine.
