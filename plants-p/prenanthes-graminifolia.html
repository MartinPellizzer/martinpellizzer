Prenanthes graminifolia

Prenanthes graminifolia, commonly known as slender burr cucumber, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and distinctive spiny fruits. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, with a spatulate structure typical of the Araceae family.

Prenanthes graminifolia is often found in wetlands and is considered a valuable component of riparian ecosystems.
