Primula tosaensis f. albiflora

Primula tosaensis f. albiflora, commonly known as the Tosa primrose.

This plant is a rare and unique variant of the Tosa primrose, characterized by its pale white flowers. It is native to Japan and is found in specific regions of the Kii Peninsula. The foliage is typically broad and oval, with a smooth texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
