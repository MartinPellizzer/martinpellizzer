Pleurisanthes artocarpi

Pleurisanthes artocarpi, commonly known as the Chinese yellow wood or yellow-flowered pleurisanthes, is a member of the Rutaceae family.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China, particularly in the provinces of Guangdong and Guangxi. The plant is characterized by its yellow flowers and pinnate leaves, which contribute to its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in secondary forests or along stream banks.

The fruit is a small, round drupe that contains a single seed, making it valuable for both ecological and horticultural purposes.
