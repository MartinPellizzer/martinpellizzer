Panicularia nervata var. viridis

Panicularia nervata var. viridis, commonly known as green panicularia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, branching inflorescences that bear small, greenish-white flowers. The leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slightly hairy texture. This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its graceful appearance.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
