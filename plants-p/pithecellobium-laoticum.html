Pithecellobium laoticum

Pithecellobium laoticum, commonly known as the Laotian pisonia, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a distinctive yellow or white coloration.

The seeds are enclosed in hairy, elongated pods, which contribute to its ecological role in seed dispersal.
