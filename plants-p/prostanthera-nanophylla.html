Prostanthera nanophylla

Prostanthera nanophylla, commonly known as narrow-leaf mintbush, is a small shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs.

Prostanthera nanophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native gardens.
