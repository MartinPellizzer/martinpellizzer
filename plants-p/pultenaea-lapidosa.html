Pultenaea lapidosa

Pultenaea lapidosa, commonly known as the coastal pultenaea, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is adapted to sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas. It has a compact growth habit and is sometimes used in coastal landscaping due to its hardiness.

Pultenaea lapidosa is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
