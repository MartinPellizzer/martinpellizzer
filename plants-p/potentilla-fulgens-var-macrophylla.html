Potentilla fulgens var. macrophylla

Potentilla fulgens var. macrophylla, commonly known as the showy cinquefoil, is a flowering plant characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and broad, lance-shaped leaves.

It is native to parts of North America and thrives in well-drained soils in full sun. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant. Its foliage remains attractive throughout the growing season, providing ornamental value beyond its bloom period.

The variety macrophylla is distinguished by its larger leaves compared to the typical form of Potentilla fulgens.
