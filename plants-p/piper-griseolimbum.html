Piper griseolimbum

Piper griseolimbum, commonly known as the gray-leaved pepper, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and small, clustered white flowers. Its fruits are small berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

Piper griseolimbum is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
