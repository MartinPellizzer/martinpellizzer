Protium copal

Protium copal, commonly known as wild copal, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. The tree is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
