Pultenaea laxa

Pultenaea laxa, commonly known as the slender pultenaea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, often spindly growth habit. The plant features small, pea-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, arranged in dense spikes. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Pultenaea laxa is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to coastal and heathland environments.
