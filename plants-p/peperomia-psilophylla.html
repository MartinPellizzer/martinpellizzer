Peperomia psilophylla

Peperomia psilophylla, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white or silver hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia psilophylla prefers well-draining soil and moderate humidity, making it a versatile addition to many plant collections.
