Parodia rudibuenekeri

Parodia rudibuenekeri, commonly known as the Rudibuenekeri cactus, is a species native to Argentina.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and spiny stems. The plant typically features a green to blue-green stem with white or yellowish spines. It produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and collectors.
