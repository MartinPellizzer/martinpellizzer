Piper multistigmum

Piper multistigmum, commonly known as the African pepper or wild pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that develop into elongated, fleshy fruits. Its roots and leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
