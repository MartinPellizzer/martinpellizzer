Persea gongshanensis

Persea gongshanensis, commonly known as the Gongshan avocado, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan province, where it grows in subtropical forests. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers and develops a dark, smooth-skinned fruit resembling a typical avocado. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and have a strong aromatic scent.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its unique genetic makeup and potential for cultivation in new environments.
