Peperomia ponapensis var. trukensis

Peperomia ponapensis var. trukensis, commonly known as the Truk Islands peperomia, is a rare and compact evergreen shrub native to the Caroline Islands.

It features small, ovate leaves with a glossy, dark green surface and prominent veins. The plant produces tiny, pale green flowers arranged in spicate inflorescences, typical of the Peperomia genus. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
