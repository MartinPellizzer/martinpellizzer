Piper storkii

Piper storkii, commonly known as the Stork's bill pepper, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by red, fleshy berries that are edible. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly aromatic scent.

Piper storkii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a spice in local cuisines.
