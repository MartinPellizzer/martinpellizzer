Prunus Insititia Subvar. Dumetorum

Prunus insititia subvar. dumetorum is a lesser-known yet fascinating plant native to the western united states. it is commonly referred to as the wild black cherry or the sand cherry. this shrub thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in arid and semi-arid regions. its small, dark berries are edible but contain toxic compounds that require careful preparation. despite its medicinal and ecological significance, it remains underappreciated in modern horticulture.
