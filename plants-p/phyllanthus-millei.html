Phyllanthus millei

Phyllanthus millei, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features small, green, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a small, round capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Phyllanthus millei is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for various health purposes.
