Pandanus odoratissimus

Pandanus odoratissimus, commonly known as the sweet-scented pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, feather-like leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that develop into edible fruits, which are valued for their unique aroma and flavor. Its roots and leaves are also used in various medicinal and culinary applications.

The species is well adapted to coastal and wetland environments, thriving in humid conditions.
