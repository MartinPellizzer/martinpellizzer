Pteris spinescens

Pteris spinescens, commonly known as the spinelace fern, is a species of fern characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia. The plant features rhizomes that grow horizontally, supporting its upright, bipinnate leaves. Its fronds are typically dark green with a waxy texture and exhibit a distinctive, spiny appearance along the veins.

Pteris spinescens thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
