Piper cernuum f. subglabrirameum

Piper cernuum f. subglabrirameum, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed pepper plant, is a variant of the common pepper vine.

It is characterized by its slender, glabrous stems and heart-shaped leaves with a slightly pubescent underside. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. Its fruits are small, round berries that mature to a dark purple or black color.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and mild flavor in culinary applications.
