Pleione brachyptera

Pleione brachyptera, commonly known as the short-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. The plant typically blooms in late winter or early spring, making it one of the earliest flowering orchids in its habitat. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in shaded, moist environments with well-drained soil.

Pleione brachyptera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
