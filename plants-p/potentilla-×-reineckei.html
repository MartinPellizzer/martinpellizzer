Potentilla × reineckei

Potentilla × reineckei, commonly known as the silver weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that range from pink to red, appearing in late spring to early summer. The plant features finely cut, silver-gray foliage that provides ornamental value throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for a variety of garden settings.

Potentilla × reineckei is often used in landscaping for its ornamental beauty and hardy nature.
