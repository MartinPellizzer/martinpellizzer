Pleiocarpa breviloba

Pleiocarpa breviloba, commonly known as the small-lobed pleiocarpa, is a member of the Malvaceae family.

It is a shrub characterized by its slender, branching stems and hairy leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale yellow to greenish in color. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, round seeds.
