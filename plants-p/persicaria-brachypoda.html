Persicaria brachypoda

Persicaria brachypoda, commonly known as narrowleaf smartweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist or wet habitats such as wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks. The plant features alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, pink to red flowers are clustered in dense, terminal spikes, and it produces small, three-lobed seeds.

Persicaria brachypoda is known for its ability to thrive in wet conditions and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
