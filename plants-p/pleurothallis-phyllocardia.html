Pleurothallis phyllocardia

Pleurothallis phyllocardia, commonly known as the heartleaf pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. Its flowers are often clustered in inflorescences and bloom during the wet season.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike.
