Pothos nitens

Pothos nitens, commonly known as the silver pothos, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, variegated leaves that display a striking combination of white and green. The plant is known for its vigorous growth and ability to climb or trail, making it popular as a houseplant. Its leaves are glossy and emit a mild fragrance when crushed.

Pothos nitens thrives in indirect light and requires moderate watering, making it easy to care for.
