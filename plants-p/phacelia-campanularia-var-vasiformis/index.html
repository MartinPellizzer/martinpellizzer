Phacelia Campanularia Var. Vasiformis

Phacelia campanularia var. vasiformis is a striking member of the Boraginaceae family known for its vibrant blue to purple flowers. This variant is native to the western United States, particularly found in California and Nevada. It thrives in dry, sunny environments such as grasslands and open woodlands. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its unique flower shape and compact growth make it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
