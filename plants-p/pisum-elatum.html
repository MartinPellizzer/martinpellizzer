Pisum elatum

Pisum elatum, commonly known as the tall pea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Its pods contain several seeds, which are edible and similar in taste to common garden peas.

Pisum elatum is often cultivated for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
