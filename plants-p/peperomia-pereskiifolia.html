Peperomia pereskiifolia

Peperomia pereskiifolia, commonly known as the silver lace peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features striking, waxy, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with silver and green, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of green or red, which are followed by tiny, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage makes it a sought-after ornamental plant for both novice and experienced growers.
