Polyactium amatymbicum

Polyactium amatymbicum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. Its ornamental value makes it a popular houseplant worldwide.
