Prunus Pedunculata

Prunus pedunculata is a striking ornamental plant known for its vibrant spring blossoms. Native to eastern Asia, it thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal. This species belongs to the rose family and features delicate, clustered flowers that attract pollinators. Its foliage adds visual interest throughout the growing season. Whether planted in gardens or used in landscape design, Prunus pedunculata is a versatile and beautiful addition to any outdoor space.
