Phyllocalyx cupreus

Phyllocalyx cupreus, commonly known as the copperleaf, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking copper-colored foliage, which gives it its common name. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often overlooked.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
