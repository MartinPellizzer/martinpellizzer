Potentilla × castriferrei

Potentilla × castriferrei, commonly known as the hybrid shrub, is a deciduous plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and features small, serrated leaves that are dark green in color. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

Potentilla × castriferrei thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.
