Polystichum kungianum

Polystichum kungianum, commonly known as the Kung's shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its tall, upright fronds with a distinctive, shield-like shape. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its leaves are bioluminescent, emitting a faint blue glow at night, which aids in attracting nocturnal pollinators.

This unique trait makes it a rare and fascinating example of bioluminescence in the plant kingdom.
