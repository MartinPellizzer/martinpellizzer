Polystachya affinis

Polystachya affinis, commonly known as the slender sword fern, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Polystachyaceae and is characterized by its long, narrow, and leathery fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizome is creeping and produces multiple upright stems with lance-shaped leaves.

Polystachya affinis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
