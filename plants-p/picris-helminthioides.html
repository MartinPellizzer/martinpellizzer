Picris helminthioides

Picris helminthioides, commonly known as goatweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe, North Africa, and parts of Asia, and is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides. The plant features serrated leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clustered inflorescences. It is an annual or biennial herb that can grow up to 60 cm in height.

The species is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
