triloba, commonly known as the Amazonian arrowroot, is a herbaceous plant native to the Amazon rainforest. It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 2 meters in length.

Its botanical features make it an important species for both ecological and culinary purposes in the Amazon.
