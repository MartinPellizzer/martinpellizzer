Pisonia subcordata var. rotundata

Pisonia subcordata var. rotundata, commonly known as the globular pisonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Indo-Pacific.

It is characterized by its dense, round clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has leathery, ovate leaves that are typically arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. It thrives in coastal areas and is often found growing on rocky or sandy shores.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
