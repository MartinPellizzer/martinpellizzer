Phylica variabilis

Phylica variabilis, commonly known as the variable bottlebrush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive cylindrical flower clusters, which are typically red or orange in color. The plant belongs to the Proteaceae family and is known for its adaptability to various soil types. It thrives in full sun and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Phylica variabilis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in garden landscaping.
