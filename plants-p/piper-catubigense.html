Piper catubigense

Piper catubigense, commonly known as the Catubigense pepper vine, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in spikes.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns red when mature, containing several seeds.
