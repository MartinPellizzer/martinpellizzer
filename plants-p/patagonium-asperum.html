Patagonium asperum

Patagonium asperum, commonly known as the rough patagonia, is a flowering plant native to the southern regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Patagonium asperum plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various insects and small animals.
