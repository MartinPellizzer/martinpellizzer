Pyrus Viburnoides

Pyrus viburnoides is a lesser-known member of the rose family known for its ornamental value and ecological benefits. This shrub features showy white flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its foliage turns vibrant shades of red and orange in the fall, adding visual interest to gardens. While it shares some characteristics with viburnums, it is distinct in its fruiting habits and growth form. Due to its hardiness and adaptability, it is a valuable addition to native plant gardens and wildlife habitats.
