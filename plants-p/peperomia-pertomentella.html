Peperomia pertomentella

Peperomia pertomentella, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are often velvety to the touch, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia pertomentella is known for its ease of care and ability to adapt well to a variety of indoor conditions.
