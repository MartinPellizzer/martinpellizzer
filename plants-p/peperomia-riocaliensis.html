Peperomia riocaliensis

Peperomia riocaliensis, commonly known as the Rio Cali peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Colombia.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, arranged in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia riocaliensis is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
