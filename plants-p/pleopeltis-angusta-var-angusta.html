Pleopeltis angusta var. angusta

Pleopeltis angusta var. angusta, commonly known as the narrow pleopeltis, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its slender, elongated fronds with a narrow, linear shape.
