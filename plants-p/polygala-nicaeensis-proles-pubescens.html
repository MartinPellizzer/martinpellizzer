Polygala nicaeensis proles pubescens

Polygala nicaeensis proles pubescens, commonly known as the hairy polygala, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is known for its small, delicate leaves. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and attract various pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species in its native range.
