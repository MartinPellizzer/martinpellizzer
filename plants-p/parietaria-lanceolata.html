Parietaria lanceolata

Parietaria lanceolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved wallweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Urticaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and small greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters. Parietaria lanceolata is often considered a weed but plays a role in supporting local insect populations.
