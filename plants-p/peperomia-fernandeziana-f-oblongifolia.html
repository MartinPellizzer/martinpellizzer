Peperomia fernandeziana f. oblongifolia

Peperomia fernandeziana f. oblongifolia, commonly known as the oblong-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features dense, upright growth with thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval to oblong in shape. The foliage is often a deep green with a slight sheen, and the plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it suitable for both container and garden settings.
