Peperomia stehleana var. praestigiatrix

Peperomia stehleana var. praestigiatrix, commonly known as the Purple Heart Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features oval, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a striking purple or reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by decorative, urn-shaped berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Due to its attractive foliage and compact growth habit, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container cultivation.
