Potentilla jailae

Potentilla jailae, commonly known as the Kashmiri cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features compound leaves with five leaflets, and its flowers are usually yellow or orange, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
