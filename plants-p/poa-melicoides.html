Poa melicoides

Poa melicoides, commonly known as the slender meadowgrass, is a species of grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, blade-like leaves. This plant typically grows in moist, temperate environments such as meadows and grasslands. Its inflorescence consists of a panicle of spikelets, which are small clusters of flowers.

Poa melicoides is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and ability to stabilize soil.
