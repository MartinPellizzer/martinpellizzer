Pleurothallis panduripetala var. minor

Pleurothallis panduripetala var. minor, commonly known as the miniature pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features narrow, strap-like leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It is well adapted to high-altitude conditions and requires specific humidity and light levels to thrive.

Due to its small size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
