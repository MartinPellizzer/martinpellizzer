Persicaria thunbergii f. viridans

Persicaria thunbergii f. viridans, commonly known as the green-leafed polygonum, is a variant of the common smartweed.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are a vibrant green. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in waterlogged conditions.
