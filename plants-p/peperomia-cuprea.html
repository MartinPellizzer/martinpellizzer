Peperomia cuprea

Peperomia cuprea, commonly known as the copperleaf plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that range in color from deep green to reddish-brown, depending on light exposure. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
