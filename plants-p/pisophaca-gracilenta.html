Pisophaca gracilenta

Pisophaca gracilenta, commonly known as the slender leaf fern, is a delicate, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, arching fronds that are typically less than a meter in length, with a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or brown in color, followed by spore-bearing capsules. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its foliage is used in traditional medicine in some cultures for its purported health benefits.
