Polygonum polygamum var. brachystachyum

Polygonum polygamum var. brachystachyum, commonly known as the narrow-stalked smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its distinctive nodes and often reddish stems. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red, arranged in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are lance-shaped with a prominent midrib and may have a slightly hairy texture.

This species is often found in wet or moist soils, such as along stream banks or in marshy areas.
