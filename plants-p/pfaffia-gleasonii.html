Pfaffia gleasonii

Pfaffia gleasonii, commonly known as Paraguay tea, is a flowering plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its leaves, which are used to make a traditional infusion. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.
