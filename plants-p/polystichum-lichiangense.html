Polystichum lichiangense

Polystichum lichiangense, commonly known as the Lichiang fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its upright, leathery fronds with a serrated margin. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds, which are clustered in sori.
