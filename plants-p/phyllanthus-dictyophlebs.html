Phyllanthus dictyophlebs

Phyllanthus dictyophlebs, commonly known as the clustered phyllanthus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its dense clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, with smooth, glossy leaves that are often arranged in whorls. It produces non-showy inflorescences called cyathia, which are typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.

The plant is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
