Phalaenopsis rundumensis

Phalaenopsis rundumensis, commonly known as the Rundumensis orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant produces elegant, long-lasting flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, and it is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of conditions.

This orchid is popular among horticultural enthusiasts for its beauty and relative ease of care.
