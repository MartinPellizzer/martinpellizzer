Peperomia umbrigaudens

Peperomia umbrigaudens, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, dark green leaves that are velvety to the touch, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia umbrigaudens is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
