Pilea pittieri

Pilea pittieri, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its round, waxy leaves that resemble coins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by bright red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Pilea pittieri is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal.
