Peperomia peepuloides

Peperomia peepuloides, commonly known as the "peepul-like peperomia," is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features thick, fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in late spring or early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
