Pleurothallis duplex

Pleurothallis duplex, commonly known as the twin-lobed orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees and rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, linear leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. Its flowers are often clustered in a raceme and are characterized by their intricate, spurred petals.

Pleurothallis duplex is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
