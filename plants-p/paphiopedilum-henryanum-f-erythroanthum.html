Paphiopedilum henryanum f. erythroanthum

Paphiopedilum henryanum f. erythroanthum, commonly known as the Red-flowered Henryan's Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red and green foliage and produces showy, lip-shaped flowers with a distinctive red throat. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are pollinated by specific insects, and it is valued for its ornamental beauty in horticulture.

This orchid is relatively easy to cultivate, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
