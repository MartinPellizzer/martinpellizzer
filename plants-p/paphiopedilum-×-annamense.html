Paphiopedilum × annamense

Paphiopedilum × annamense, commonly known as the Annam hybrid orchid, is a fascinating plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the genus Paphiopedilum, which is known for its unique and often striking orchid flowers. This hybrid species is characterized by its intricate, lip-shaped petals and a variety of color forms, often displaying shades of pink, white, and purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

The plant is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
