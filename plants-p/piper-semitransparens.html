Piper semitransparens

Piper semitransparens, commonly known as the transparent pepper, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its delicate, translucent leaves that give it its common name. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by small, red berries.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in warm climates.
