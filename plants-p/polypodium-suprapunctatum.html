Polypodium suprapunctatum

Polypodium suprapunctatum, commonly known as the Brazilian holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features fronds that are leathery and dark green, with a distinctive pattern of small, rounded sori on the underside. It is often found growing on rocky surfaces or in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and horticultural collections.
