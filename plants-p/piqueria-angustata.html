Piqueria angustata

Piqueria angustata, commonly known as the narrowleaf piqueria, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to drought conditions. Its small, yellow-orange flowers are often clustered in dense, rounded inflorescences.

Piqueria angustata is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
