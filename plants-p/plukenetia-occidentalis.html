Plukenetia occidentalis

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant produces oval, yellow-orange fruits that are rich in oil and used in traditional medicine and as a source of edible oil.
