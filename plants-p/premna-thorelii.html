Premna thorelii

Premna thorelii, commonly known as the creeping premna, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly hairy underside. Its small, greenish-white flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom during the wet season.

Premna thorelii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
