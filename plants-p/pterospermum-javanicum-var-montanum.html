Pterospermum javanicum var. montanum

Pterospermum javanicum var. montanum, commonly known as the mountain pterospermum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in mountainous regions. The plant features elliptical to ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, clustered in panicles, and produce yellowish or pale green petals.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release its seeds, which are often dispersed by wind.
