Phreatia limenophylax

Phreatia limenophylax, commonly known as the narrow-leafed phreatia, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments within forest understories. The plant features elongated, narrow leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in spikes, with a distinctive coloration that ranges from white to pale yellow.

This orchid is known for its ability to thrive in moist, well-drained soil conditions.
