Prostanthera semiteres subsp. semiteres

Prostanthera semiteres subsp.

semiteres, commonly known as the narrow-leaf honeysuckle, is a small evergreen shrub native to southeastern Australia. It features aromatic, narrow elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces small, tubular, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It is typically found in coastal heathlands and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its fragrant foliage and flowering habit.
