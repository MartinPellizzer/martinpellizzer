Pseudophoenix

Pseudophoenix, commonly known as the false date palm, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Arecaceae.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into edible fruits resembling dates.
