Pilea tridentata

Pilea tridentata, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a small, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate light conditions.

Pilea tridentata is popular as a houseplant due to its compact size and ease of care.
