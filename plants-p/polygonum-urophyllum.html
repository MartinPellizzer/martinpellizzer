Polygonum urophyllum

Polygonum urophyllum, commonly known as the redroot smartweed, is a herbaceous annual plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist soils along streambanks, wetlands, and disturbed areas. The plant features upright stems that are reddish in color, especially near the base, and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink to purple and bloom in late summer.

The plant is known for its ability to thrive in wet conditions and is often used in ecological restoration projects.
