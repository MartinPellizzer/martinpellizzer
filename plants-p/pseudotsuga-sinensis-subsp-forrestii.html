Pseudotsuga sinensis subsp. forrestii

Pseudotsuga sinensis subsp.

forrestii, commonly known as the Formosan hemlock, is a subspecies native to Taiwan. It is a coniferous evergreen tree that belongs to the family Pinaceae.

The plant has needle-like leaves that are dark green and arranged spirally along the branches. It produces small, cone-like structures that contain seeds.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in its native habitat.
