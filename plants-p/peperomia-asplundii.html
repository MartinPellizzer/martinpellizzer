Peperomia asplundii

Peperomia asplundii, commonly known as the asplundii peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features broad, glossy, dark green leaves that are often slightly wavy or notched along the edges. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Peperomia asplundii is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and its ability to enhance the aesthetic of indoor spaces.
