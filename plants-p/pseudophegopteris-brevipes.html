Pseudophegopteris brevipes

Pseudophegopteris brevipes, commonly known as the short-footed shield fern, is a member of the Dryopteridaceae family.

It is a small, terrestrial fern that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant has a short, creeping rhizome with densely clustered, pinnately divided fronds. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, with a distinctive, shield-like appearance.

It is native to regions of Southeast Asia and is often found in humid, tropical climates.
