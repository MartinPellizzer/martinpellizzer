Photinia microcarpa subsp. hintonii

Photinia microcarpa subsp.

hintonii, commonly known as the Hinton's photinia, is a shrub or small tree native to southern China. It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth margin.

The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a suitable ornamental plant in gardens.

Its compact growth habit and seasonal color changes make it a popular choice for landscape design.
