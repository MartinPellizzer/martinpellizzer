Polypodium universum

Polypodium universum, commonly known as the common holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically bi- or tri-pinnate, with a distinctive appearance that makes it popular in horticulture. The plant is native to various regions in the Americas and thrives in shaded, moist environments. Its rhizome is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Polypodium universum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation in indoor and outdoor settings.
