Phyteuma elongatum

Phyteuma elongatum, commonly known as the slender phyteuma, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of bell-shaped flowers. The flowers, which range in color from white to purple, bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or grassland habitats.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
