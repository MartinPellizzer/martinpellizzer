Paphiopedilum villosum var. annamense

Paphiopedilum villosum var. annamense, commonly known as the Annamese velvet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a thick, fleshy rhizome and produces striking, velvety greenish-yellow flowers with prominent sepals and petals. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often covered in fine, hairy structures.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
