Polyscias angustifolia

Polyscias angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaf glorybower, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy, with a prominent midrib. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Polyscias angustifolia is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
