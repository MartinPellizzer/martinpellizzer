Persicaria jucunda var. rotunda

Persicaria jucunda var. rotunda, commonly known as roundleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It features upright, hairy stems that bear opposite, ovate to round leaves, which are often reddish or purple-tinged. The plant produces small, clustered flowers with five petals, typically pink or white, that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing food and habitat for various insects and wildlife.
