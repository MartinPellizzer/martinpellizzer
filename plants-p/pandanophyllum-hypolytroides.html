Pandanophyllum hypolytroides

Pandanophyllum hypolytroides, commonly known as the slender pandan, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that are often enclosed in a spadix, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

The leaves are used in traditional cooking and have a fragrant, aromatic quality.
