Primula petitmenginii

Primula petitmenginii, commonly known as the Petitmengin's primrose, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or forested habitats at high altitudes, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are elongated and slightly hairy, while its stems are upright and branched.
