Prionachne pholiuroides

Prionachne pholiuroides, commonly known as the slender grass or slender panic grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and woodlands. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to arid conditions and has a deep root system for water uptake.

Its botanical features make it an important component of native plant communities in its range.
