Pachygenium geralium

Pachygenium geralium, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter, showcasing vibrant colors such as yellow, orange, and red. The plant has a robust root system that helps it thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and waxy, adapted to retain moisture in its humid habitat.

Pachygenium geralium is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological significance.
