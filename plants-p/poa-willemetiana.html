Poa willemetiana

Poa willemetiana, commonly known as the Willemetian grass, is a species of grass native to South America, particularly found in Argentina and Chile.

It is a perennial plant that typically grows in moist, temperate climates, often in areas with well-drained soils. The plant has slender, upright stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that gives it a distinctive appearance.

Poa willemetiana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
