Pleurothallis tsubotae

Pleurothallis tsubotae, commonly known as the Tsubota's orchid, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments within cloud forests. The plant features delicate, linear leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate floral structure.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a valuable species in botanical research and conservation efforts.
