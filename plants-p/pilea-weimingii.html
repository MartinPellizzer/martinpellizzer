Pilea weimingii

Pilea weimingii, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a unique and decorative appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by pale green berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea weimingii is relatively low maintenance and can be propagated easily through stem cuttings.
