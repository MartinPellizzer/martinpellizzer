Primula sinensis

Primula sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese primrose, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant typically blooms in early spring and has heart-shaped, dark green leaves. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as a garden plant or potted specimen.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
