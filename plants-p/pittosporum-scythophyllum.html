Pittosporum scythophyllum

Pittosporum scythophyllum, commonly known as the New Zealand pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are oval in shape and often have a slightly waxy texture. It produces small, red berries that are edible but not particularly palatable.

Pittosporum scythophyllum thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.
