Panicum porranthum

Panicum porranthum, commonly known as the silver panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-green blades and panicle-shaped inflorescences that produce small, fluffy seeds. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental and ecological landscaping. Its drought tolerance and ability to stabilize soil make it valuable in erosion control.

However, it may become invasive in certain environments if not managed properly.
