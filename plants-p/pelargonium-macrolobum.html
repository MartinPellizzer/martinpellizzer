Pelargonium macrolobum

Pelargonium macrolobum, commonly known as the large-leaf geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of showy, pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from spring to autumn.
