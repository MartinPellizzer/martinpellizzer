Prunus serrulata f. nobilis

Prunus serrulata f. nobilis, commonly known as the Japanese cherry blossom or Somei Yoshino, is a cultivar of the Prunus serrulata species.

It is characterized by its delicate, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant is a deciduous tree with a rounded crown and smooth, reddish-bark that peels in thin layers. Its leaves are oval-shaped with finely serrated edges, giving it a distinctive appearance.

This ornamental tree is widely planted in parks and along streets for its seasonal beauty and cultural significance.
