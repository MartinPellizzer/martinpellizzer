Potentilla × breunia

Potentilla × breunia, commonly known as the hybrid shrub, is a deciduous plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a cross between Potentilla fruticosa and Potentilla nanula, resulting in a compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its foliage is often dense and dark green, providing an attractive ornamental appearance.

Potentilla × breunia is well-suited for rock gardens and borders due to its hardiness and low maintenance requirements.
