Poa mustangensis

Poa mustangensis, commonly known as the Mustang bluegrass, is a perennial grass native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in open, dry habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny blue-green flowers.

Poa mustangensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in arid conditions.
