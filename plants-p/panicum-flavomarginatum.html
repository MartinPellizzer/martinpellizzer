Panicum flavomarginatum

Panicum flavomarginatum, commonly known as the golden margin grass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow leaves with distinctive yellow margins. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are often pale green or brown in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental and erosion control purposes.

Its drought tolerance and adaptability contribute to its popularity in landscaping and restoration projects.
