Primula × santii

Primula × santii, commonly known as the Santii primrose, is a hybrid species within the Primulaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, clustered flowers that typically range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped blooms that are often borne on short pedicels. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland or shaded garden settings.

Its foliage consists of broad, heart-shaped leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy texture.
