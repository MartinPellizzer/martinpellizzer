Peperomia subcorymbosa

Peperomia subcorymbosa, commonly known as the clustered pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense clusters of small, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces delicate, spicate inflorescences that bloom in shades of pink or white, adding ornamental value. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia subcorymbosa is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
