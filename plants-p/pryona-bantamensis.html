Pryona bantamensis

Pryona bantamensis, commonly known as the Bantam rose, is a shrub native to Indonesia.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
