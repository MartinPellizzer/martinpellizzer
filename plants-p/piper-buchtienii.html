Piper buchtienii

Piper buchtienii, commonly known as the Buchtienii pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with aerial roots that allow it to ascend trees or other structures. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and glossy, while its small, greenish-white flowers are followed by peppery fruits.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage rather than its edible properties.
