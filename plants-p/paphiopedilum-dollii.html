Paphiopedilum dollii

Paphiopedilum dollii, commonly known as the Dolly's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features a thick, fleshy rhizome and produces a single, showy inflorescence with several pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. The petals and sepals are often marked with deep purple or brown veins, giving it a distinctive appearance. It prefers cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
