Potentilla orientalis

Potentilla orientalis, commonly known as the oriental cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia and parts of Europe.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, usually from June to July. Its leaves are pinnately divided into five to nine leaflets, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Potentilla orientalis thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.
