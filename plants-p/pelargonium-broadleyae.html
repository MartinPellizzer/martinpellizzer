Pelargonium broadleyae

Pelargonium broadleyae, commonly known as the Broadley's geranium, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink and red. The plant typically features broad, rounded leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice among gardeners interested in cultivating rare and exotic plants.
