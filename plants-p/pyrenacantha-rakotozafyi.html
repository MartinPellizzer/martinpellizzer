Pyrenacantha rakotozafyi

Pyrenacantha rakotozafyi, commonly known as the Madagascar starburst plant, is a unique species native to Madagascar.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and striking, star-shaped flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are adapted for dispersal by animals. Its botanical features include hairy stems and leaves with a waxy coating, which help it survive in its arid environment.

This plant is of interest to botanists due to its distinctive morphology and endemic status.
