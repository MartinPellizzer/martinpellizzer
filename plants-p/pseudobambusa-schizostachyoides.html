Pseudobambusa schizostachyoides

Pseudobambusa schizostachyoides, commonly known as the schizostachyoides bamboo, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a clump-forming bamboo with slender, erect culms that are often green or slightly bluish in color. The plant features narrow, lanceolate leaves that are typically bright green and have a smooth texture. It produces small, clustered inflorescences with pale yellow to white flowers.

This bamboo is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and as a decorative plant in gardens.
