Premna Cardiophylla

Premna cardiophylla is a unique and lesser-known plant species native to specific regions in Asia. It is characterized by its striking heart-shaped leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its ornamental qualities make it a desirable addition to botanical gardens and private collections. Despite its beauty, Premna cardiophylla remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticulture.
