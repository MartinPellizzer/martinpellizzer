Paphiopedilum × barbatocharlesworthii

Paphiopedilum × barbatocharlesworthii, commonly known as the Barbato Charlesworth hybrid, is a hybrid orchid that combines traits from its parent species, Paphiopedilum barbatum and Paphiopedilum charlesworthii.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a distinctive, showy flower that typically features a lip with a unique, intricate pattern. The plant has a pseudobulbous structure, which helps it store water and nutrients, making it relatively drought-tolerant. Its foliage consists of broad, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.

This hybrid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and unique morphology.
