Pilea angolensis subsp. angolensis

Pilea angolensis subsp.

angolensis, commonly known as the African pilea, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically bright green with a slightly glossy texture.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact growth habit and ease of care contribute to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
