Peperomia distans

Peperomia distans, commonly known as the spreading pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features upright, branching stems covered in glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a slight waxy sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and red, which are followed by elongated, cylindrical seed capsules. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia distans is valued for its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
