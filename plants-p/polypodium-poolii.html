Polypodium poolii

Polypodium poolii, commonly known as the Chinese shield fern, is a species of fern native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its fronds are usually shield-shaped, with a dense, leathery texture.

Polypodium poolii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
