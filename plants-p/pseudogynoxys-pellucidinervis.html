Pseudogynoxys pellucidinervis

Pseudogynoxys pellucidinervis, commonly known as the transparent-veined pseudogynoxys, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a herbaceous annual plant characterized by its slender, branching stems and alternate leaves. The leaves are typically elliptical or lanceolate, with prominent, translucent veins that give the plant its distinctive appearance. It produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the sunflower family.
