Polygonum mesembricum

Polygonum mesembricum, commonly known as the evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy yellow-orange flowers that open in the late afternoon and close at night. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Polygonum mesembricum is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for various health purposes.
