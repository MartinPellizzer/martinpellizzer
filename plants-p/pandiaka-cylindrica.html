Pandiaka cylindrica

Pandiaka cylindrica, commonly known as the cylinder pandiaka, is a unique plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, tubular stems that can grow several meters in length. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. Its leaves are narrow and arranged spirally along the stem, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Pandiaka cylindrica is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
