Peperomia nudipetiola var. microtricha

Peperomia nudipetiola var. microtricha, commonly known as the silver mitten peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a silvery-white underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant has a short stem and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are rarely seen. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and decorative purposes.
