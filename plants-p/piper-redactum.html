Piper redactum

Piper redactum, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes approximately 2,000 species of flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its striking red flowers and heart-shaped leaves, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in humid environments and is often found growing in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in various parts of the world.
