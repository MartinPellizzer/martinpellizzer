Pleurothallis parvula

Pleurothallis parvula, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has slender, wiry stems and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pink. Its leaves are narrow and often clustered at the base of the plant.

Pleurothallis parvula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
