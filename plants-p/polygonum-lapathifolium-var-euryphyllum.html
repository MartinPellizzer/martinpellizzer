Polygonum lapathifolium var. euryphyllum

Polygonum lapathifolium var. euryphyllum, commonly known as broadleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its erect stems and alternate leaves that are ovate to lanceolate in shape. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red, which bloom in summer. It thrives in moist soils and is often found in wetlands, ditches, and along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in agricultural and disturbed areas.
