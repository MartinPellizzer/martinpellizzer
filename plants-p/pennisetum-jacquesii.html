Pennisetum jacquesii

Pennisetum jacquesii, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to South Africa.

It features tall, slender stalks that produce plume-like inflorescences, typically in shades of purple or pink. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often silver-white underneath, adding visual interest. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Pennisetum jacquesii is drought-tolerant and attracts birds and pollinators, contributing to ecological diversity.
