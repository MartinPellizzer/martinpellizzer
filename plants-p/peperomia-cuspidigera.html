Peperomia cuspidigera

Peperomia cuspidigera, commonly known as the pointed-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with a pointed tip, often tinged with red or purple along the edges. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are typically borne in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia cuspidigera is noted for its low maintenance requirements and ability to tolerate dry conditions.
