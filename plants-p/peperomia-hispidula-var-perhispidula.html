Peperomia hispidula var. perhispidula

Peperomia hispidula var. perhispidula, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy, ovate leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture, giving it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are typically a deep green with a bluish tint and often have a slight waxy coating. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and red, which are usually hidden among the foliage.

This variety is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and minimal water requirements.
