Piper futokadsura

Piper futokadsura, commonly known as the Kadsura vine, is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and partial shade.

The berries it produces are typically small and may be used in traditional medicine.
