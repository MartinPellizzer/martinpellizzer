Panicum oligotrichum

Panicum oligotrichum, commonly known as little bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and tufted seed heads that turn golden in the fall. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in prairie and woodland habitats. It plays a vital role in soil stabilization and provides forage for wildlife.

Panicum oligotrichum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
