Potentilla verna var. astelligera

Potentilla verna var. astelligera, commonly known as the early potentilla, is a low-growing shrub native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically has slender, hairy stems and oval, toothed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous or subalpine habitats.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and its role in stabilizing soil in harsh environments.
