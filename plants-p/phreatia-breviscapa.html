Phreatia breviscapa

Phreatia breviscapa, commonly known as the short-stemmed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the rainforests of Borneo and Sumatra. The plant is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine roots. Its flowers are typically pale yellow or greenish-white, with a short, narrow spur.

Phreatia breviscapa thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or branches in the canopy layer of the forest.
