Prinos verticillatus

Prinos verticillatus, commonly known as the clustered prion.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its dense clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. It produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds.

Prinos verticillatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
