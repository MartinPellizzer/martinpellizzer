Peperomia quadrifolia

Peperomia quadrifolia, commonly known as the four-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, glossy, dark green leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, often with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and ability to thrive in well-draining soil.

Peperomia quadrifolia is valued for its ornamental foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
