Physurus rariflorus

Physurus rariflorus, commonly known as the rare-flowered physurus, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces delicate, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Physurus rariflorus is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
