Piper falculispicum

Piper falculispicum, commonly known as the swordleaf pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves and small, clustered white flowers. Its fruits are typically small berries that ripen to a yellow or orange color.

Piper falculispicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
