Pteroneurum trichocarpum

Pteroneurum trichocarpum, commonly known as the African sarsaparilla, is a climbing plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by elongated, fleshy fruits. Its roots and stems are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
