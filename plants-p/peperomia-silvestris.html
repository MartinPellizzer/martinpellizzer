Peperomia silvestris

Peperomia silvestris, commonly known as the wild peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, often in shades of green or red, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia silvestris is known for its resilience and low maintenance requirements, suitable for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
