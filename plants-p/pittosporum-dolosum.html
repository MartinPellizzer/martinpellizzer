Pittosporum dolosum

Pittosporum dolosum, commonly known as the New Zealand pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are glossy and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by bright red berries that are edible for birds. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in coastal areas and forest margins.

Pittosporum dolosum is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in native ecosystems.
