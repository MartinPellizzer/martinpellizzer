Persicaria rubricaulis

Persicaria rubricaulis, commonly known as red-stemmed knotweed, is a flowering plant in the Polygonaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, reddish stems and heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in late summer. It is often found in moist habitats such as stream banks and wet meadows.

Persicaria rubricaulis is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
