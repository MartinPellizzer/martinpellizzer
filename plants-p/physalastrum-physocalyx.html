Physalastrum physocalyx

Physalastrum physocalyx, commonly known as the golden star, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features striking, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for water gardens and rain gardens.
