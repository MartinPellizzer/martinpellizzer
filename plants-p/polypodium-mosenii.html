Polypodium mosenii

Polypodium mosenii, commonly known as the Mosenii shield fern, is a species of polypodioid fern native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and compact growth habit, often forming thick mats on rocky or shaded terrain. The plant's fronds are typically leathery and have a distinctive, shield-like appearance, contributing to its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Polypodium mosenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
