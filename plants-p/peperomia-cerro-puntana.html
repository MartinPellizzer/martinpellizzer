Peperomia cerro-puntana

Peperomia cerro-puntana, commonly known as the Puntarenas Peperomia, is a species native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub with thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with reddish or purplish undersides. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellowish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant.
