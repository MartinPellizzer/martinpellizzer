Piper minutipetiolum

Piper minutipetiolum, commonly known as the small-leaf pepper or slender pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly found in countries like Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, opposite leaves that are usually less than 2 centimeters in length. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, and it produces small, peppery fruits that are used in local cuisines.

The plant is valued both for its culinary uses and its ornamental qualities in gardens.
