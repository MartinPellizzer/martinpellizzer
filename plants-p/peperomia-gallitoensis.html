Peperomia gallitoensis

Peperomia gallitoensis, commonly known as the Mexican peperomia, is a perennial herb native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically oval or heart-shaped, often with a waxy texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color.

Peperomia gallitoensis is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.
