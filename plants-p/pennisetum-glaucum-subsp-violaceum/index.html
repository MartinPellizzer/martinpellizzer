Pennisetum Glaucum Subsp. Violaceum

Pennisetum glaucum subsp. violaceum is a striking ornamental grass known for its vibrant purple foliage. This subspecies thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. Its feathery plumes add texture and movement to any setting throughout the growing season. The plant is easy to maintain and resistant to most pests and diseases. It is an excellent addition to both residential and commercial green spaces.
