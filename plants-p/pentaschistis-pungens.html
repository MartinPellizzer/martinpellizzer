Pentaschistis pungens

Pentaschistis pungens, commonly known as the spiny pungent bladder, is a member of the family Apiaceae, which includes caraway and parsley.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
