Polygonum sambesicum var. angustifolium

Polygonum sambesicum var. angustifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved smartweed, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist environments such as wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks. The plant features alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are usually less than 2 inches in length. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense spikes, and appear in shades of red or pink.

The species is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and colonize new areas effectively.
