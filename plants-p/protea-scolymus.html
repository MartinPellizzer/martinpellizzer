Protea scolymus

Protea scolymus, commonly known as the silver protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that consist of numerous pale pink to white flowers arranged in a spherical or conical shape. The plant is native to the Western Cape region and thrives in well-drained, acidic soils. It is often found in fynbos vegetation, where it plays a role in the unique biodiversity of the area.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and is also used in floral arrangements.
