Pterichis elliptica var. elliptica

Pterichis elliptica var. elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic pterichis, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its narrow, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
