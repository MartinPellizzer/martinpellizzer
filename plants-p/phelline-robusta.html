Phelline robusta

Phelline robusta, commonly known as the robust phelline, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its upright, herbaceous stems and lance-shaped leaves with a smooth, waxy surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in temperate regions.

Phelline robusta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage.
