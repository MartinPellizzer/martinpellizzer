Plectranthus ecklonii

Plectranthus ecklonii, commonly known as Cape verbena, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive grayish-green color. Its showy blue or purple flowers are borne in clusters and bloom during the cooler months.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
