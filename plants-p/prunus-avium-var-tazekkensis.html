Prunus avium var. tazekkensis

Prunus avium var. tazekkensis, commonly known as the Tazekkent cherry, is a rare variant of the sweet cherry.

It is characterized by its small, dark red fruits with a sweet and tart flavor profile. The plant typically grows in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil. It has a compact growth habit with dense foliage and produces flowers in the spring.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and unique fruit characteristics.
