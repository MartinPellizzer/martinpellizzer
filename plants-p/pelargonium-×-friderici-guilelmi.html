Pelargonium × friderici-guilelmi

Pelargonium × friderici-guilelmi, commonly known as the Royal Pelargonium, is a hybrid geranium species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red and white. The plant typically has broad, lobed leaves with a velvety texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
