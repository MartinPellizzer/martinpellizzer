Peperomia choroniana var. choroniana

Peperomia choroniana var. choroniana, commonly known as the Choroni peperomia, is a compact, low-growing herbaceous plant native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It features dense, fleshy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and ornamental foliage, it is valued for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
