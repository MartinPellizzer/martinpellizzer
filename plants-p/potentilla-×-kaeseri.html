Potentilla × kaeseri

Potentilla × kaeseri, commonly known as the everblooming shrub or everblooming potentilla, is a hybrid plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage, often reaching heights of 30–60 cm. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red, which bloom throughout the growing season. Its adaptability to various soil types and climate conditions makes it a popular choice for garden use.

It is also valued for its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in full sun to partial shade.
