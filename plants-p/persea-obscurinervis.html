Persea obscurinervis

Persea obscurinervis, commonly known as the dark-veined persea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves with prominent, dark-colored veins. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by edible, pear-shaped fruits that are typically green when mature. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.

Its growth habit and leaf structure make it a distinctive species within the Persea genus.
