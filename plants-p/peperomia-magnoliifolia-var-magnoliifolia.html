Peperomia magnoliifolia var. magnoliifolia

Peperomia magnoliifolia var. magnoliifolia, commonly known as the magnolia-leaf peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, waxy, heart-shaped leaves that resemble the foliage of magnolia trees, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique leaf structure and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable ornamental plant.
