Polygonatum × krylovii

Polygonatum × krylovii, commonly known as the Krylov's Solomon's seal, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asparagaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces oval, dark blue berries that are edible but not widely cultivated for consumption. Native to regions of East Asia, it thrives in shaded, moist environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant in horticulture for woodland gardens.
