Paeonia × baokangensis

Paeonia × baokangensis, commonly known as the Baokang peony, is a hybrid species native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant is a deciduous shrub with thick, woody stems and large, palmately compound leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
