Potentilla Rydbergii

Potentilla rydbergii is a striking member of the Rosaceae family known for its vibrant yellow flowers and hardy nature. Native to the arid regions of western North America, this plant thrives in rocky and sandy soils where few others can survive. Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and native plant gardens. The plant's showy blooms appear in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators and adding color to the landscape. Despite its beauty, Potentilla rydbergii remains a relatively obscure species, often overlooked in favor of more common garden plants.
