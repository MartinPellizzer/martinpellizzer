Poa compressa var. langiana

Poa compressa var. langiana, commonly known as the Langian meadow grass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, temperate environments such as meadows and wetlands. The plant has slender, tufted stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and often have a bluish-green hue, and it is characterized by its dense, carpet-like growth habit.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in wetland areas.
