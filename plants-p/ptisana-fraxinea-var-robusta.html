Ptisana fraxinea var. robusta

Ptisana fraxinea var. robusta, commonly known as the robust flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, which are opposite and oval in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The robust flame tree is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as birds and insects.
