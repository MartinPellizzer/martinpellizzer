Phyteuma orbiculare f. stellulatum

Phyteuma orbiculare f. stellulatum, commonly known as the star-like phyteuma, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to Central Asia.

It features showy, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped, hairy leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a valuable addition to garden landscapes.
