Pelargonium occultum

Pelargonium occultum, commonly known as the ghost geranium, is a rare and elusive species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts.

Due to its rarity, it is considered endangered and requires careful conservation efforts.
