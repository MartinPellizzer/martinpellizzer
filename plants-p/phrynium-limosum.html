Phrynium limosum

Phrynium limosum, commonly known as the swamp arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features broad, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are often glossy and have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in spikes, followed by elongated, spiky fruits. It thrives in wet, muddy environments and is often found near water bodies.

Phrynium limosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
