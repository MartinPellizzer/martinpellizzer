Prestoea longepetiolata var. cuatrecasasii

Prestoea longepetiolata var. cuatrecasasii, commonly known as the Cuatrecasas's ceiba, is a tropical tree native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 50 cm in diameter. The tree produces showy, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds.

This species is valued for its ecological role and is often found in lowland rainforests.
