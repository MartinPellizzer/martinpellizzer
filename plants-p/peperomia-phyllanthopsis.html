Peperomia phyllanthopsis

Peperomia phyllanthopsis, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features dark green, velvety leaves that are oval to heart-shaped, often with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia phyllanthopsis is ideal for indoor cultivation due to its adaptability and ornamental appeal.
