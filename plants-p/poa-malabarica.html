Poa malabarica

Poa malabarica, commonly known as the Malabar grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Indian subcontinent.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. The plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Poa malabarica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and ecological restoration projects.
