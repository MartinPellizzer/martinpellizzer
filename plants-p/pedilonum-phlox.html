Pedilonum phlox

Pedilonum phlox, commonly known as the common phlox, is a flowering plant belonging to the Polemoniaceae family.

It is native to North America and is characterized by its showy clusters of pink, red, or white flowers that bloom in early summer. Pedilonum phlox is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
