Peperomia boraborensis

Peperomia boraborensis, commonly known as the Borabora peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Madagascar.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a velvety texture and small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in its native arid environment. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance for low light and minimal water requirements.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for collectors and enthusiasts of rare houseplants.
