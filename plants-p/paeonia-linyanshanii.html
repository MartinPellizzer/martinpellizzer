Paeonia linyanshanii

Paeonia linyanshanii, commonly known as the Linyi peony, is a rare and ornamental species native to China.

It belongs to the芍药科 (Paeoniaceae) family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically features deeply lobed leaves and can grow up to several feet in height. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Due to its limited distribution and beauty, it is often cultivated for its horticultural value.
