Phegopteris Kinabaluensis

Phegopteris kinabaluensis is a rare fern species found exclusively in the high-altitude regions of Mount Kinabalu in Borneo. It thrives in the cool, moist microclimates of the mountain's rainforests, where it adapts to the unique environmental conditions. This plant is notable for its delicate fronds and its limited distribution, making it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Due to habitat destruction and climate change, its survival is increasingly threatened. Protecting Phegopteris kinabaluensis is crucial for preserving the biodiversity of one of the world's most ecologically rich areas.
