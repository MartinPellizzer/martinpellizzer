Prasophyllum viriosum

Prasophyllum viriosum, commonly known as the yellow leek orchid, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is native to Australia.

It is a small, terrestrial herb with a slender, grass-like stem and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces a single spike of yellowish-green flowers, which are typically arranged in a loose cluster. Prasophyllum viriosum thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in swampy or wetland areas.

It is listed as vulnerable due to habitat loss and is protected under various conservation laws.
