Prostanthera stricta

Prostanthera stricta, commonly known as narrow-leaved mintbush, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, tubular flowers in shades of purple or pink. It is typically found in dry, sandy soils and is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Prostanthera stricta is also valued for its fragrant properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
