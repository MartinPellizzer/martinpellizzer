Pilea helxinoides

Pilea helxinoides, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that resemble small coins. The plant produces clusters of white, spadix-like flowers surrounded by a greenish spathe. It is a compact, low-maintenance plant that thrives in indirect light and well-draining soil.

Pilea helxinoides is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
