Polygonum daphnophyllum

Polygonum daphnophyllum, commonly known as the silverleaf knotweed, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies in suitable habitats.
