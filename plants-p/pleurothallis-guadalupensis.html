Pleurothallis guadalupensis

Pleurothallis guadalupensis, commonly known as the Guadalupensis Orchid, is a species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant typically produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
