Phebalium carruthersii

Phebalium carruthersii, commonly known as the Carruthers' hakea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its woody structure and dense foliage. The plant produces small, urn-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to the dry, Mediterranean climate of its native region.
