Ptychopyxis javanica

Ptychopyxis javanica, commonly known as the Javanese starfruit, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, deciduous tree that typically grows to a height of about 5 meters. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a small, star-shaped berry that turns from green to yellow when mature.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
