Poa annua var. sikkimensis

Poa annua var. sikkimensis, commonly known as the Sikkim annual bluegrass, is a variant of the common bluegrass species.

It is a low-growing, perennial grass native to the Himalayan regions, including parts of India and Nepal. This plant typically forms dense mats and has slender, cylindrical leaves with a bluish-green color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows and forest clearings.

The inflorescence consists of spikelets that are loosely arranged, contributing to its ornamental value in certain ecological contexts.
