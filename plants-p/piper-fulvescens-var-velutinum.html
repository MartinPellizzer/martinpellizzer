Piper fulvescens var. velutinum

Piper fulvescens var. velutinum, commonly known as the velvet pepper, is a tropical vine native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many pepper species. The plant is characterized by its hairy, velvety stems and leaves, giving it its common name. Its small, yellowish-green flowers are followed by berry-like fruits that mature to a bright red color.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
