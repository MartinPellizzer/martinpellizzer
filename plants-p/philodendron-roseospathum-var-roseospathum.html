Philodendron roseospathum var. roseospathum

Philodendron roseospathum var. roseospathum, commonly known as the pink-veined philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy, deep green leaves with striking pink to red veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers, though it is primarily cultivated for its ornamental foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique leaf patterns make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
