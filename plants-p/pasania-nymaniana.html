Pasania nymaniana

Pasania nymaniana, commonly known as the African starflower, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Pasania nymaniana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
