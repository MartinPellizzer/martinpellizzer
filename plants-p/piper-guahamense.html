Piper guahamense

Piper guahamense, commonly known as Guahamense pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly in countries like Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with long, slender stems that often twine around other vegetation. Its leaves are typically ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The fruit, a small, spherical berry, contains several seeds and is used in traditional medicine and cuisine in its native regions.
