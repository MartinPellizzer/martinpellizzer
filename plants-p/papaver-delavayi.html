Papaver delavayi

Papaver delavayi, commonly known as Delavay's poppy, is a species of flowering plant in the family Papaveraceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of western China, particularly in Sichuan and Yunnan provinces. The plant is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers, which bloom in early summer. Its leaves are palmately lobed and often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Papaver delavayi is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been cultivated for its vibrant, showy blooms.
