Peltophorum dubium var. berteroanum

Peltophorum dubium var. berteroanum, commonly known as the red flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange-pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with long, slender leaflets. Its bark is smooth and often exhibits a reddish or grayish hue.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often planted in urban landscapes for its vibrant floral display.
