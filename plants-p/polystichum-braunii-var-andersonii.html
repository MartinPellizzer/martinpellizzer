Polystichum braunii var. andersonii

Polystichum braunii var. andersonii, commonly known as the Anderson's wood fern, is a variant of the Braun's wood fern native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It is a perennial fern characterized by its dense, evergreen fronds that form a compact, bushy growth habit. The fronds are typically dark green, with a leathery texture and a distinctive, finely serrated margin. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
