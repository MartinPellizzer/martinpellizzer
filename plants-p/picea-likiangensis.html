Picea likiangensis

Picea likiangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan spruce, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and dense, conical crown, with dark green, needle-like leaves. The plant thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found at high altitudes, typically above 2,500 meters. Its cones are small and woody, containing seeds that are dispersed by wind.

This species plays an important ecological role in forest ecosystems, providing habitat for various wildlife species.
