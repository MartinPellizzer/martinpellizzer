Plenckia integerrima

Plenckia integerrima, commonly known as the rigid plenckia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of Asia.

It typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters, featuring slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually blue or violet in color, arranged in dense clusters at the top of the stems. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Plenckia integerrima is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
