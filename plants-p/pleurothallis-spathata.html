Pleurothallis spathata

Pleurothallis spathata, commonly known as the Spatulate Pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
