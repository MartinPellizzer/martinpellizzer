Pistolochia tarkiensis

Pistolochia tarkiensis, commonly known as the Tarkien ghostflower, is a rare and enigmatic plant native to the remote highlands of Tarkien.

It is characterized by its unique, translucent, waxy leaves that reflect light in a ghostly manner, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom only under specific environmental conditions, making it difficult to study. Its botanical structure includes a short, woody stem and modified leaves that serve as both photosynthetic organs and water storage structures.

Due to its elusive nature and habitat specificity, it remains one of the least understood plants in the region.
