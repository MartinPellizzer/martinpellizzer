Phelipanche Serratocalyx

Phelipanche serratocalyx is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia. It belongs to the Orobanchaceae family, known for its parasitic traits. This species is often found growing on the roots of other plants, drawing nutrients from its host. Its small, pinkish-white flowers bloom in summer, adding a subtle beauty to its habitat. Despite its delicate appearance, it plays a unique role in its ecosystem.
