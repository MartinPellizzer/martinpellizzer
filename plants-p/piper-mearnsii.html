Piper mearnsii

Piper mearnsii, commonly known as the rough leaved hopper, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Piperaceae family, which includes various species of flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.

Its botanical features make it an important component of its native ecosystem.
