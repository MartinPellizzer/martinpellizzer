Paraprenanthes sagittiformis

Paraprenanthes sagittiformis, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

Paraprenanthes sagittiformis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
