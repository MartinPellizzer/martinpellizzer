Phlomis congesta

Phlomis congesta, commonly known as clustered mullein, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or dry soils. The plant features tall, branched stems and produces clusters of pale yellow to greenish-yellow flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in dense, velvety hairs.

Phlomis congesta is often used in herbal medicine for its purported respiratory and anti-inflammatory properties.
