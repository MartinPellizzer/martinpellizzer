Placolobium robustum

Placolobium robustum, commonly known as the hardy groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its erect stems and dense, woolly foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Placolobium robustum is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in arid environments.
