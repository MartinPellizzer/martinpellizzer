Phoebe siltepecana

Phoebe siltepecana, commonly known as the Siltepecan phoebe, is a species of flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are typically glossy and elliptical in shape. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, round berries.

The tree is often found in moist, shaded habitats within tropical and subtropical regions.
