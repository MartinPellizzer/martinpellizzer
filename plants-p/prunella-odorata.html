Prunella odorata

Prunella odorata, commonly known as self-heal, is a perennial herb native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is characterized by its square stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, two-lipped blue or violet flowers that bloom in whorls along the stem. Its leaves are often used in traditional herbal medicine for their purported healing properties.

Prunella odorata thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.
