Pterostylis alata var. robusta

Pterostylis alata var. robusta, commonly known as the broad-leafed greenhood, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its broad, strap-like leaves and a distinctive greenish-brown flower spike. The plant produces a single or few flowers, each with a pouch-like structure that is typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys.

The species is noted for its specialized pollination mechanism, relying on specific insect species for reproduction.
