Panicum ornatum

Panicum ornatum, commonly known as purple lovegrass, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a warm-season grass characterized by its distinctive purple-tinted foliage and fluffy, seed-producing inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open areas, such as roadsides and disturbed soils, and thrives in full sun. Its fibrous root system helps prevent soil erosion, making it a valuable plant in ecological restoration projects.

The ornamental value of Panicum ornatum makes it a popular choice for landscaping and ornamental gardens.
