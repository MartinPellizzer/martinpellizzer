Piper minutantherum

Piper minutantherum, commonly known as the small-flowered pepper vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes numerous species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly glossy surface.

While not widely cultivated for its fruit, it is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and potential medicinal properties.
