Panicum schraderi

Panicum schraderi, commonly known as Schrader's grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its tufted growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Panicum schraderi is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
