Pyrrosia costata

Pyrrosia costata, commonly known as the serrated rock fern, is a species of fern native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage with serrated leaf margins. The plant typically grows on rocky surfaces or in shaded, moist environments. Its fronds are leathery and dark green, often with a waxy coating.

Pyrrosia costata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid, shaded conditions.
