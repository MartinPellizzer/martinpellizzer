Portulaca mauritiensis var. mauritiensis

Portulaca mauritiensis var. mauritiensis, commonly known as the tropical portulaca, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a succulent species characterized by fleshy, erect stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, yellow, and orange, often blooming throughout the day. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in arid conditions.
