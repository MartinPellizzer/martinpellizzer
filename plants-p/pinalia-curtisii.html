Pinalia curtisii

Pinalia curtisii, commonly known as the Curtis's pinyon.

It is a small evergreen shrub native to the high elevations of the western United States. The plant features needle-like leaves and produces small, cone-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, arid environments.

Pinalia curtisii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
