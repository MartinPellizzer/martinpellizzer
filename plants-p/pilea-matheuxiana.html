Pilea matheuxiana

Pilea matheuxiana, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Pilea matheuxiana is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
