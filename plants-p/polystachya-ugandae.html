Polystachya ugandae

Polystachya ugandae, commonly known as the Uganda sword fern, is a member of the Polystachyaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Africa, particularly found in Uganda and surrounding countries. This plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and pinnately compound leaves that resemble a sword. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale green, contributing to its ornamental value.
