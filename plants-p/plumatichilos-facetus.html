Plumatichilos facetus

Plumatichilos facetus, commonly known as the smooth-leafed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glabrous. Its flowers are small and clustered, with a distinctive three-lobed lip that is often brightly colored.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
