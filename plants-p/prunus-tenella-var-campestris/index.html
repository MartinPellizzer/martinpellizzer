Prunus Tenella Var. Campestris

Prunus tenella var. campestris is a unique and lesser-known member of the Prunus genus. This plant is known for its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in challenging environments. It is often found in rocky or arid regions where other plants struggle to survive. Its small, fragrant flowers make it a valuable addition to gardens with limited space. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated and rarely cultivated outside its native range.
