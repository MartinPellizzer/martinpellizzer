Peperomia jamaicana

Peperomia jamaicana, commonly known as Jamaican pepper plant or Jamaican peperomia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the Caribbean, particularly Jamaica, and is characterized by its compact, succulent-like foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia jamaicana is known for its ability to tolerate low light conditions and is relatively easy to care for.
