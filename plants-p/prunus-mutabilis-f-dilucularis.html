Prunus mutabilis f. dilucularis

Prunus mutabilis f. dilucularis, commonly known as the Dawn Cherry, is a unique horticultural form of the Prunus mutabilis species.

It is characterized by its striking change in flower color from white to pink to red as the blooming season progresses. The plant is a deciduous shrub or small tree, typically reaching heights of 3 to 6 meters. Its flowers appear in clusters and are accompanied by small, oval leaves with serrated edges.

The Dawn Cherry is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its seasonal color display.
