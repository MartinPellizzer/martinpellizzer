Pelargonium grevillianum

Pelargonium grevillianum, commonly known as the Grevillea geranium, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant features velvety, dark green leaves with a slightly serrated margin. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Pelargonium grevillianum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
