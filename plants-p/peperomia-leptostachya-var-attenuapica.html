Peperomia leptostachya var. attenuapica

Peperomia leptostachya var. attenuapica, commonly known as the narrow-leaved peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features ovate to elliptical leaves that are often glossy and dark green, with a slightly wavy margin. The plant produces small, spadix-like inflorescences that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate watering.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
