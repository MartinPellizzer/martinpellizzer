Panicum convallium

Panicum convallium, commonly known as white panicgrass, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the grass family, Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces small, fluffy white or pale green inflorescences, which are composed of numerous tiny spikelets. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds.

Panicum convallium is an important plant for wetland ecosystems, providing habitat and food for various insects and birds.
