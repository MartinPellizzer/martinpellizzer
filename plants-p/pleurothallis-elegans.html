Pleurothallis elegans

Pleurothallis elegans, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically features slender, wiry stems and leaves that are usually clustered at the base. Its flowers are usually small, with intricate patterns and a range of colors from white to pale yellow.

Pleurothallis elegans thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found growing epiphytically on trees or rocks in the wild.
