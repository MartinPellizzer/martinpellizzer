Pyrus reflexa

Pyrus reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed pear.

It is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia, particularly China and Korea. The plant is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and alternate, ovate leaves with serrated edges. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in early spring, followed by edible, round fruits that are typically yellow or orange in color.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
