Pterozonium cyclophyllum

Pterozonium cyclophyllum, commonly known as the cyclophyllous pterozoon, is a unique plant species characterized by its intricate leaf morphology.

Its leaves are arranged in a spiral pattern, forming a dense, cyclical structure that enhances light capture. The plant exhibits a specialized vascular system that supports its unusual growth form. It is primarily found in moist, shaded environments, where it thrives in the understory of tropical forests.

Due to its rare occurrence and distinctive features, it is of interest to botanists studying evolutionary adaptations in plant morphology.
