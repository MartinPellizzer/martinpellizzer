Peperomia platyphylla

Peperomia platyphylla, commonly known as the flat-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features oval, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly glossy surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are often overlooked due to their modest size. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia platyphylla is known for its low maintenance and ability to adapt well to a variety of indoor conditions.
