Pediculariopsis verticillata

Pediculariopsis verticillata, commonly known as the clustered monkey orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its compact, clustered growth habit. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are often borne in whorls along the stems. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is typically found in coastal regions.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
