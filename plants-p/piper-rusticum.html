Piper rusticum

Piper rusticum, commonly known as the roughleaf pepper or rustic pepper, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its rough, waxy leaves and small, clustered white flowers. Its fruit is a small, yellowish berry that ripens to red, containing a single seed.

Piper rusticum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
