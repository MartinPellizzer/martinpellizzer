Platyelasma eriostachyum

Platyelasma eriostachyum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved daisies, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to form dense, compact clumps.
