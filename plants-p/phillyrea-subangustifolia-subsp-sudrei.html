Phillyrea subangustifolia subsp. sudrei

Phillyrea subangustifolia subsp.

sudrei, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy.

The plant produces white, fragrant flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, leathery fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rocky or coastal environments.

The subspecies sudrei is distinguished by its narrower leaves and slightly different growth habit compared to other Phillyrea species.
