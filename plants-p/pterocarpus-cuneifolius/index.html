Pterocarpus Cuneifolius

Pterocarpus cuneifolius is a remarkable tree native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. Known for its hard, durable wood, it has been highly valued for centuries in traditional craftsmanship and construction. The tree's unique foliage and striking bark make it a notable presence in its natural habitat. It plays an important role in local ecosystems and is often used in traditional medicine. Despite its significance, it faces threats from deforestation and habitat loss.
