Piper rufum

Piper rufum, commonly known as the red pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves. Its small, greenish-white flowers give way to red, berry-like fruits that are edible.

Piper rufum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of mild spices.
