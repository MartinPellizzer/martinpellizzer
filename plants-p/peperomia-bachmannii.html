Peperomia bachmannii

Peperomia bachmannii, commonly known as the Bachmann's peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, succulent-like foliage. The plant typically features small, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and can produce clusters of tiny white flowers. It is often grown as a houseplant due to its ease of care and ornamental appeal.

Peperomia bachmannii prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
