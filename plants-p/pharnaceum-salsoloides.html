Pharnaceum salsoloides

Pharnaceum salsoloides, commonly known as the saltbush, is a halophytic shrub adapted to saline environments.

It typically grows in coastal areas, salt marshes, and arid regions with high soil salinity. The plant has small, fleshy leaves that help reduce water loss and store moisture. Its flowers are usually greenish or yellowish, arranged in dense clusters.

Pharnaceum salsoloides plays a role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for various wildlife in its native range.
