Primula obconica subsp. fujianensis

Primula obconica subsp.

fujianensis, commonly known as the Fujian primrose, is a flowering plant native to China. It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, bell-shaped flowers.

The plant typically produces clusters of pale pink to white flowers that bloom in early spring. It prefers cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded woodland areas.

Its foliage consists of broad, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface.
