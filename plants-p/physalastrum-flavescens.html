Physalastrum flavescens

Physalastrum flavescens, commonly known as the yellow water starwort, is a flowering plant native to wetland and aquatic environments in North America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and floating leaves that form a dense mat on the water surface. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that helps it spread and colonize new areas. Its leaves are typically rounded with a waxy coating, aiding in water retention.

Physalastrum flavescens plays a role in stabilizing aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
