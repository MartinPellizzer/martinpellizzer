Polyclathra grandiflora

Polyclathra grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered polyclathra, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth, glossy surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
