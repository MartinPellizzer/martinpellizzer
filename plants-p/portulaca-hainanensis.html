Portulaca hainanensis

Portulaca hainanensis, commonly known as Hainan moss rose, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Portulacaceae and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy stems and leaves. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable ornamental plant for arid or semi-arid environments.

Portulaca hainanensis is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
