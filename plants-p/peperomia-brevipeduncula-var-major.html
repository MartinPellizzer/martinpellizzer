Peperomia brevipeduncula var. major

Peperomia brevipeduncula var. major, commonly known as the dwarf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, often with a waxy texture and vibrant green or reddish hues. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually borne on short, erect peduncles. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and drought tolerance.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for collectors and indoor gardeners alike.
