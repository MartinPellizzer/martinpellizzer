Polygonum lapathifolium var. acutifolium

Polygonum lapathifolium var. acutifolium, commonly known as the sharp-leaved smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are often serrated along the edges, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in pink or red hues during the summer months. It thrives in moist soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks.

Its rapid growth and ability to spread through rhizomes make it both a common weed and an important component of wetland ecosystems.
