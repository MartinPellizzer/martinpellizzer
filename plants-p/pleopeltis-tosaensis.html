Pleopeltis tosaensis

Pleopeltis tosaensis, commonly known as the Tosa rock fern, is a species of epiphytic and lithophytic fern native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, often clustered growth habit. The plant features small, lanceolate leaves with a leathery texture and a distinctive, serrated margin. It thrives in shaded, humid environments, typically on rocky surfaces or tree trunks.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
