Pternandra galeata

Pternandra galeata, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its clusters of white, fluffy flowers that resemble small snowballs, blooming in late winter to early spring. The plant has simple, oval-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.

Pternandra galeata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
