Pothos ledermannii

Pothos ledermannii, commonly known as the silver pothos, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically variegated with shades of white and green, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by green, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity to flourish.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
