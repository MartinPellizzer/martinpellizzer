Panicum commonsianum var. euchlamydeum

Panicum commonsianum var. euchlamydeum, commonly known as eastern dropseed, is a perennial grass native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and fine, silver-green foliage. The plant produces fluffy, pale pink to white seed heads that are highly ornamental and attract pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for native gardens and restoration projects.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a valuable addition to ecological landscapes.
