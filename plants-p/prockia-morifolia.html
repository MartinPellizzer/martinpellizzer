Prockia morifolia

Prockia morifolia, commonly known as the common prockia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Prockiaceae.

It is characterized by its small, green leaves and clusters of delicate, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as woodlands and stream banks.
