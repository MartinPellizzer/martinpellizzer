Presla

Presla is a small, hardy plant that thrives in rocky and sunny environments. It is known for its delicate, fern-like leaves and is often found in alpine regions. This plant is easy to care for and can be a great addition to any garden. Presla's unique appearance makes it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts. Its ability to survive in harsh conditions makes it both resilient and fascinating.
