Pontya excelsa

Pontya excelsa, commonly known as the giant sedge, is a tall, herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves and can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spike-like clusters. It thrives in wetland or marshy environments, often forming dense stands.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and colonize new areas effectively.
