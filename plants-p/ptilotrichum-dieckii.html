Ptilotrichum dieckii

Ptilotrichum dieckii, commonly known as the African violet, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to tropical Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and showy, tubular flowers that typically bloom in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and ornamental flowers contribute to its widespread cultivation as a decorative plant.
