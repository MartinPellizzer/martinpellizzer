Polycarpon stylosum

Polycarpon stylosum, commonly known as the slender knotweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its upright, herbaceous stems and lanceolate leaves with a smooth, waxy surface. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wet meadows and along stream banks.

Polycarpon stylosum is a perennial species that spreads through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain habitats.
