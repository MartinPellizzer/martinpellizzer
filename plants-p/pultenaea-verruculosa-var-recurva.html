Pultenaea verruculosa var. recurva

Pultenaea verruculosa var. recurva, commonly known as the curly leaf pultenaea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its finely toothed, often curved leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or sandy environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
