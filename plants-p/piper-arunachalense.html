Piper arunachalense

Piper arunachalense, commonly known as the Arunachal pepper, is a climbing shrub native to the northeastern region of India.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers and is characterized by its aromatic leaves. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its unique properties, it is of interest to botanists and herbalists alike.
