Phoenicanthemum cylindricum

Phoenicanthemum cylindricum, commonly known as the cylindrical phoenicanthemum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.

The species is also valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
