Panicum bechuanense

Panicum bechuanense, commonly known as the Bechuanaland grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open grasslands and savannas, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces fluffy, pale green to golden seed heads. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a bluish-green hue.

It is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
