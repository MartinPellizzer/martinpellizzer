Pteris depauperata

Pteris depauperata, commonly known as the barren fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Pteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically green and have a feathery appearance. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including parts of India, China, and Southeast Asia. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found growing in forest understories.

Pteris depauperata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
