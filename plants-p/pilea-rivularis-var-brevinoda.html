Pilea rivularis var. brevinoda

Pilea rivularis var. brevinoda, commonly known as the Shiny Pilea, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features rounded, glossy dark green leaves with a slightly waxy texture, often marked by a pale midrib. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters, typically during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with consistent moisture.

Its compact growth habit makes it an ideal choice for indoor cultivation and tabletop displays.
