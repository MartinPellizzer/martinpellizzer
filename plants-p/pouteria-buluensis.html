Pouteria buluensis

Pouteria buluensis, commonly known as the Bulu's pouteria, is a tropical tree native to the rainforests of western Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its large, glossy, elliptical leaves. Due to its limited distribution and habitat loss, Pouteria buluensis is considered a rare and endangered species.
