Peperomia reineckei var. glabrior

Peperomia reineckei var. glabrior, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, velvety foliage with deep green, waxy leaves that are slightly rounded and have a textured surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that emerge from the leaf axils, though it is primarily grown for its ornamental foliage rather than its blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor conditions.
