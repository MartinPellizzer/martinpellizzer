Panicum tunicatum

Panicum tunicatum, commonly known as the velvet grass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and distinctive, silky-textured foliage. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically pale green to brown in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
