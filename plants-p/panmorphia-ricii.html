Panmorphia ricii

Panmorphia ricii, commonly known as the variable morph plant, is a rare and enigmatic species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its highly variable morphology, with leaves and flowers that can change shape and color depending on environmental conditions. The plant exhibits a unique photosynthetic adaptation, allowing it to thrive in low-light conditions. Its roots are known to secrete a substance that aids in nutrient absorption from poor soil.

Due to its elusive nature, Panmorphia ricii remains a subject of ongoing botanical research.
