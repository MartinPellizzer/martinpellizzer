Peperomia pubiramea

Peperomia pubiramea, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a velvety texture, often adorned with silver or grayish veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color, appearing in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia pubiramea is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and moderate humidity to flourish.
