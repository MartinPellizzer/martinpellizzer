Parodia salmonea

Parodia salmonea, commonly known as the Salmonea cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular plant with a ribbed structure and typically features a yellow or orange coloration. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
