Phyllanthus comptonii

Phyllanthus comptonii, commonly known as the African milk tree, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant produces a milky sap that is toxic and can cause skin irritation. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.
