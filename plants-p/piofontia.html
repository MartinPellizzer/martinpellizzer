Piofontia

Piofontia, commonly known as the golden star lily, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with a star-like pattern. The plant features strap-shaped leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright light for optimal growth.

Piofontia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a potted plant.
