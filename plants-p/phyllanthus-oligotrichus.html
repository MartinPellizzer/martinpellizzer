Phyllanthus oligotrichus

Phyllanthus oligotrichus, commonly known as the African spurge, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed, and it is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

It has a shallow root system and is adapted to thrive in dry, sunny environments.
