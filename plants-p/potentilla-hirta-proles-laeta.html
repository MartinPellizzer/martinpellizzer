Potentilla hirta proles laeta

Potentilla hirta proles laeta, commonly known as the Happy Potentilla, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Europe.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are hairy and lance-shaped, which gives it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
