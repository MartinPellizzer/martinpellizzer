Pittosporum mannii

Pittosporum mannii, commonly known as the New Zealand Pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

Pittosporum mannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
