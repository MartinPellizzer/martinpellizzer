Peschiera diversifolia

Peschiera diversifolia, commonly known as the variable leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, often variegated leaves that display a range of colors, including green, white, and yellow. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale green or yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by horticulturists.
