Premna aureolepidota

Premna aureolepidota, commonly known as the golden-leaved premna, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, glossy, yellow-green leaves that are often adorned with distinctive white or silver markings. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, spherical fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its attractive foliage.
