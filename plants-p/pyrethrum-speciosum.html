Pyrethrum speciosum

Pyrethrum speciosum, commonly known as the Chinese pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant produces small, spherical flower heads that are typically yellow to orange in color. Pyrethrum speciosum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in the production of natural insecticides.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardens and horticultural collections.
