Pleurothallis ciliifera

Pleurothallis ciliifera, commonly known as the hairy pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and relatively small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
