Pelargonium × cephalanthum

Pelargonium × cephalanthum, commonly known as the shieldleaf geranium, is a hybrid species within the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, shield-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. Native to South Africa, it is a popular ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and container planting.
