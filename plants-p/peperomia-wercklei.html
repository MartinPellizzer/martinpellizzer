Peperomia wercklei

Peperomia wercklei, commonly known as the velvetleaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or purple in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia wercklei is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
