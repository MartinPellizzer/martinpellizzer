Pilosella kalksburgensis subsp. leptobia

Pilosella kalksburgensis subsp.

leptobia, commonly known as the slender pilosella, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers with white ray florets and yellow disk florets.

The subspecies is native to Central Europe and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has hairy leaves and stems, which give it its genus name "Pilosella.".

This plant is typically short-lived and has a low-growing habit, making it a distinctive component of its native flora.
