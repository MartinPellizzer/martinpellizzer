Phyllanthus superbus

Phyllanthus superbus, commonly known as the sacred lotus or Chinese phyllanthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, green, palmate leaves and yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks. Its fruit is a small, three-lobed capsule that contains several seeds.

Phyllanthus superbus is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
