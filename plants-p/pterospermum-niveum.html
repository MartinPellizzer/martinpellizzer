Pterospermum niveum

Pterospermum niveum, commonly known as the white pterospermum, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release seeds when mature.

Pterospermum niveum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
