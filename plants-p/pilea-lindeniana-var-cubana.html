Pilea lindeniana var. cubana

Pilea lindeniana var. cubana, commonly known as the Cuban prayer plant, is a tropical plant native to Cuba and the Bahamas.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves. The plant produces striking white to pale yellow flowers that are surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Pilea lindeniana var. cubana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
