Philodendron fenzlii var. anisotomum

Philodendron fenzlii var. anisotomum, commonly known as the Fenzlii Philodendron, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with light green and white patterns. The plant has a climbing habit and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by spiny, berry-like fruits. Its foliage is prized for its aesthetic appeal in indoor gardening.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and humidity for optimal growth.
