Paphiopedilum exul var. aureum

Paphiopedilum exul var. aureum, commonly known as the golden butterfly orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a unique, butterfly-like flower with a distinctive lip that is often golden or yellow in color. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments with well-drained soil. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its natural habitat.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
