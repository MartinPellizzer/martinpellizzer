Pouzolzia zeylanica var. microphylla

Pouzolzia zeylanica var. microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved pouzolzia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in compact clusters, which are followed by small, round fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in ecological studies of tropical flora.
