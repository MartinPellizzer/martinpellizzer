Protium javanicum

Protium javanicum, commonly known as the Java wild teak, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its evergreen canopy and smooth, greyish-brown bark. The tree is valued for its durable wood and is often found in lowland and hill forests.
