Podocarpus macrophyllus subsp. forrestii

Podocarpus macrophyllus subsp.

forrestii, commonly known as the Chinese podocarp or Chinese mountain pine, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to eastern Asia. It features needle-like leaves that are dark green and arranged in dense clusters, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces small, spherical cones that mature to a reddish-brown color. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping and horticulture.

This subspecies is adapted to a variety of soil types and can thrive in both sunny and partially shaded environments.
