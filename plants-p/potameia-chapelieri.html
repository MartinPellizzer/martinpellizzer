Potameia chapelieri

Potameia chapelieri, commonly known as the Greek bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features delicate blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Potameia chapelieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
