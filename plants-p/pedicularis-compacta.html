Pedicularis compacta

Pedicularis compacta, commonly known as the dwarf monkeyflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and bright orange to red tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
