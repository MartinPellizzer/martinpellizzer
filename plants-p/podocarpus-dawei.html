Podocarpus dawei

Podocarpus dawei, commonly known as the Chinese podocarp, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Podocarpaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and conical shape. The tree produces small, spherical cones that mature to a reddish-brown color. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

Podocarpus dawei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine and as a source of timber.
