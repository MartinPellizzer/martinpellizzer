Piper banaoanum

Piper banaoanum, commonly known as the Banao pepper, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and opposite, ovate leaves. Its small, greenish-white flowers are followed by elongated, woody fruits that contain aromatic seeds.

The plant is often cultivated for its unique flavor and is used in traditional Filipino cuisine and herbal medicine.
