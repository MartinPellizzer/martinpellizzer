Petrobium arboreum

Petrobium arboreum, commonly known as the tree petrobium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are usually simple and opposite, with a somewhat succulent texture.

Petrobium arboreum is adapted to arid environments and is often found in coastal regions.
