Podocarpus aracensis

Podocarpus aracensis, commonly known as the Aracari podocarp, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It belongs to the Podocarpaceae family and is characterized by its dense, needle-like leaves and cone-bearing structure. The plant exhibits a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 20 meters in its natural habitat. It plays a vital role in the ecological balance of its region by providing habitat and contributing to soil stability.

Due to habitat loss, it is also considered a vulnerable species.
