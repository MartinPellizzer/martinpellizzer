Phillyrea medianifolia subsp. allorgeana

Phillyrea medianifolia subsp.

allorgeana, commonly known as the Algerian Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, elliptical leaves that are usually dark green and glossy.

The plant produces clusters of white, fragrant flowers during the spring and early summer. Its fruit is a small, spherical drupe that ripens to a yellowish color.

It is often found in rocky or dry soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
