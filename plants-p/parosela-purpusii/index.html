Parosela Purpusii

Parosela purpusii is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. It is known for its striking purple and white petals that bloom in early spring. This species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories. Due to habitat loss and overcollection, it is now considered endangered. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and propagate this beautiful yet fragile species.
