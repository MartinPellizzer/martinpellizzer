Prunus × kalmykovii

Prunus × kalmykovii, commonly known as the Kalmykovii cherry, is a hybrid species belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its ornamental value, featuring showy pink to red flowers in early spring. The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, with lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens.

The fruit, while edible, is generally smaller and less flavorful compared to other cherry species.
