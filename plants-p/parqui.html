Parqui

Parqui, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other similar plants. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with a dark central disk. Parqui is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
