Primula yuana

Primula yuana, commonly known as the Chinese primrose, is a perennial flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of bell-shaped, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically blooms in early spring, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking early-season color. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in a fine, silvery down, adding visual interest throughout the growing season.

Primula yuana thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated in shaded or partially shaded areas.
