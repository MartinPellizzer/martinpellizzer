Piper caballo-cochanum

Piper caballo-cochanum, commonly known as the Caballo Cochranum pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its vine-like growth habit and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. Its fruits are small, berry-like structures that mature to a dark purple color.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique foliage and compact growth form.
