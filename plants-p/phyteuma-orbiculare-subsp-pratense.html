Phyteuma orbiculare subsp. pratense

Phyteuma orbiculare subsp.

pratense, commonly known as the meadow phyteuma, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple.
