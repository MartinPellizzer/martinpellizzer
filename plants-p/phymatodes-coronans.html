Phymatodes coronans

Phymatodes coronans, commonly known as the crown vetch, is a perennial legume native to Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has a deep root system that helps prevent soil erosion. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping and restoration projects.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it beneficial for soil enrichment.
