Pleurothallis arachnantha

Pleurothallis arachnantha, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, spider-like flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and decaying organic matter.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a popular subject among orchid enthusiasts and botanists.
