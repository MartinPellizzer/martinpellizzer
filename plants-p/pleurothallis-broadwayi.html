Pleurothallis broadwayi

Pleurothallis broadwayi, commonly known as the Broadway orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and green. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, adapted to the humid, shaded environments where it grows.

Pleurothallis broadwayi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
