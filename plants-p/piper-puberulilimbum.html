Piper puberulilimbum

Piper puberulilimbum, commonly known as the glossy pepper vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, clustered white flowers. It produces small, round berries that are often used in traditional medicine.

The plant's botanical features make it an interesting subject for botanical studies and horticultural cultivation.
