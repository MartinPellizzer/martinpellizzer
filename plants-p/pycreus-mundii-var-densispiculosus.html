Pycreus mundii var. densispiculosus

Pycreus mundii var. densispiculosus, commonly known as the dense-pubescent pycreus, is a species native to specific regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and compact growth habit. The plant typically features small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of purple or pink. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
