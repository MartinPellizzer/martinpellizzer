Panicum nuttallianum

Panicum nuttallianum, commonly known as Nuttall's panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of water bodies, thriving in moist to wet soil conditions. The plant has slender, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters, and its leaves are narrow with a slight sheen. It produces fluffy, light green to brown seed heads that are characteristic of the Panicum genus.

This species is often used in wetland restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for wildlife.
