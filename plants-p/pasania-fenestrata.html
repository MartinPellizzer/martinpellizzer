Pasania fenestrata

Pasania fenestrata, commonly known as the window leaf or fenestrated pasania, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that feature distinctive fenestrations, or leaf windows, which give it its common name. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests. Its foliage is not only ornamental but also plays a role in its ecological niche by providing shelter for small insects and microorganisms.

Pasania fenestrata is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and ability to thrive in indoor settings with proper care.
