Pittosporum sinuatum var. sinuatum

Pittosporum sinuatum var. sinuatum, commonly known as the silver leaf pittosporum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-white foliage that provides a striking contrast to its dark green stems. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its adaptability and aesthetic appeal make it suitable for various garden settings.
