Portulaca decorticans

Portulaca decorticans, commonly known as the common purslane, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a low-growing, succulent annual that thrives in hot, dry environments. The plant features fleshy, thick stems and leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. Its small, yellow flowers bloom in summer, and the plant produces small, round, red fruits that contain seeds.

Portulaca decorticans is often found in gardens, fields, and disturbed soils, and it is known for its edible leaves and seeds.
