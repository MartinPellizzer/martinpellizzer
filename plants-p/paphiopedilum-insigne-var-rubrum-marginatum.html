Paphiopedilum insigne var. rubrum-marginatum

Paphiopedilum insigne var. rubrum-marginatum, commonly known as the Crimson-marginated Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb that supports its foliage and flowers. The plant produces showy, lip-shaped flowers in shades of red and orange, often with distinctive crimson margins. Its leaves are elliptical and dark green, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This variety is valued for its vibrant coloration and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
