Piper loxense

Piper loxense, commonly known as the Brazilian pepper vine, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, opposite leaves. Its flowers are typically greenish-white and appear in clusters, followed by small, berry-like fruits.

It is often found in humid, shaded environments such as rainforests.
