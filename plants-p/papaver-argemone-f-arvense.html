Papaver argemone f. arvense

Papaver argemone f. arvense, commonly known as the field poppy, is a variant of the argemone poppy.

It is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Mediterranean region. The plant features showy, bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

The plant is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in dry, sunny environments.
