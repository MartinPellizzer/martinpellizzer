Pouteria torricellensis

Pouteria torricellensis, commonly known as the Torricella sapote, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy yellow flowers. The tree produces a yellow to orange fruit with a thick, sweet pulp and a large, stony seed. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The plant is valued for its edible fruit and has potential uses in traditional medicine.
