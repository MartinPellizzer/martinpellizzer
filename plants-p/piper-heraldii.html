Piper heraldii

Piper heraldii, commonly known as the Herold's pepper or the Philippine pepper, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and opposite, ovate leaves. Its small, greenish-white flowers are borne in clusters and are followed by small, fleshy berries.

Piper heraldii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and growth habit.
