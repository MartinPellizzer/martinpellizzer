Peperomia duidana

Peperomia duidana, commonly known as the Duidana Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically oval or heart-shaped, with a waxy texture and a variety of colors ranging from deep green to reddish hues. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, usually in shades of white or green, which are followed by small, round berries. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia duidana is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to enhance indoor humidity.
