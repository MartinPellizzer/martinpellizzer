Pouzolzia zeylanica var. calcicola

Pouzolzia zeylanica var. calcicola, commonly known as the calcicola pouzolzia, is a variant of the pouzolzia plant native to certain regions of South Asia.

It is a small, evergreen shrub characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and compact growth habit. The leaves are typically narrow and elliptical, with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside. This plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in dry, coastal or mountainous environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
