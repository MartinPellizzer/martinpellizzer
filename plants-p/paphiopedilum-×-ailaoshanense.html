Paphiopedilum × ailaoshanense

Paphiopedilum × ailaoshanense, commonly known as the Ailaoshan orchid, is a hybrid species native to China, specifically the Ailaoshan region in Yunnan Province.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its unique, intricate flower structure with a pouch-like lip that distinguishes it from other orchid species. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often on rocky slopes or in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white with a contrasting purple or red lip, and it blooms during the spring and early summer.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
