Psorospermum stenophyllum var. rufius

Psorospermum stenophyllum var. rufius, commonly known as the red-leaf psorospermum, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows in tropical rainforests. The plant is characterized by its lanceolate leaves, which are often reddish in color, especially when young. Its flowers are small and clustered, producing a distinctive, sweet scent.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.
