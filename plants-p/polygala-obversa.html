Polygala obversa

Polygala obversa, commonly known as the silverleaf milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, branching stems that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and have a distinctive waxy coating. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense spikes, and typically have a pink or purplish hue.

This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
