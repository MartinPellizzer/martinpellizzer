Piriqueta cistoides subsp. cistoides

Piriqueta cistoides subsp.

cistoides, commonly known as the rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and clusters of pink or white flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has a bushy growth habit and is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its leaves are narrow and leathery, and its flowers are borne on short pedicels.
