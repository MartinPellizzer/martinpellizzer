Pelea clusiifolia var. ecuneata

Pelea clusiifolia var. ecuneata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf evergreen, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in tropical and subtropical regions.
