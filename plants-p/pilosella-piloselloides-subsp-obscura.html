Pilosella piloselloides subsp. obscura

Pilosella piloselloides subsp.

obscura, commonly known as the dark hairy cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges. Its leaves are hairy and often have a dark green color, while the flowers are yellow with a central disk.

It is a perennial species that produces small, bristly seeds dispersed by the wind.
