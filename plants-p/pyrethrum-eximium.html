Pyrethrum eximium

Pyrethrum eximium, commonly known as the African pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant is notable for its high concentration of pyrethrins, which are natural insecticides. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for cultivation in temperate climates.

Its ornamental value and pesticidal properties make it both a garden favorite and a useful botanical resource.
