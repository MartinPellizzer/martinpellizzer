Pennisetum flexile

Pennisetum flexile, commonly known as blue oat grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height, with soft, blue-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Pennisetum flexile is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract wildlife.
