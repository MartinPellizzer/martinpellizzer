Pleurothallis sordida

Pleurothallis sordida, commonly known as the dark pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, narrow leaves and produces small, pale green or white flowers with a faint scent. It is noted for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a sought-after plant among orchid enthusiasts.
