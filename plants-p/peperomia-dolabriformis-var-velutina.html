Peperomia dolabriformis var. velutina

Peperomia dolabriformis var. velutina, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, velvety foliage with deep green leaves that have a slightly glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
