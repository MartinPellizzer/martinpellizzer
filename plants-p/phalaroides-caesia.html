Phalaroides caesia

Phalaroides caesia, commonly known as blue panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, blue to purple florets. The plant typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded areas, making it an important component of wetland ecosystems. The seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its widespread distribution.
