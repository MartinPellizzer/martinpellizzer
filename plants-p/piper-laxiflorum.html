Piper laxiflorum

Piper laxiflorum, commonly known as the slender-flowered pepper vine, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn red when mature.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
