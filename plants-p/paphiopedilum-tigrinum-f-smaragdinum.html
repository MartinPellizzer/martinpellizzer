Paphiopedilum tigrinum f. smaragdinum

Paphiopedilum tigrinum f. smaragdinum, commonly known as the Emerald Tiger Orchid, is a rare and striking orchid hybrid with vibrant green and yellow markings.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, tiger-like patterns on its petals and sepals. This plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, shaded environments. It requires well-draining soil and moderate light to flourish.

Its unique appearance makes it a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
