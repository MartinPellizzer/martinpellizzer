Pinalia acutissima

Pinalia acutissima, commonly known as the sharp-leaved pinalia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central America and is characterized by its narrow, pointed leaves and small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to high-altitude environments. It is often found in open woodlands and alpine meadows where it thrives in full sun.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists studying alpine flora.
