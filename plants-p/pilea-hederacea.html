Pilea hederacea

Pilea hederacea, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a flowering plant native to China and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically hidden beneath a leaf-like spathe. It is a popular houseplant due to its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its air-purifying qualities.

Pilea hederacea is also valued for its ease of care and ability to propagate through stem cuttings.
