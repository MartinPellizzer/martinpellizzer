Polygala hypericoides

Polygala hypericoides, commonly known as the flame flower or red-flowered milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, branching stems that bear small, clustered flowers with distinctive red or pink petals. The plant's leaves are alternate, simple, and lance-shaped, often with a hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, commonly found in rocky or dry habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and dryland landscaping.
