Perezia purpurata

Perezia purpurata, commonly known as the purple perezia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems covered in lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of purple and pink during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade conditions.

Perezia purpurata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
