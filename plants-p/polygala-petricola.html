Polygala petricola

Polygala petricola, commonly known as the rock polygala, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or calcareous soils. The plant features small, delicate flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs.

Polygala petricola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
