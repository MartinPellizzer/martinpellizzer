Polygala myrtifolia var. grandiflora

Polygala myrtifolia var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and showy, spiny flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
