Potamogeton gramineus var. fluctuans

Potamogeton gramineus var. fluctuans, commonly known as floating pondweed, is a submerged aquatic plant native to North America.

It has long, ribbon-like leaves that are typically green and float on the water surface. This plant thrives in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds and lakes. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually appearing in clusters.

It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.
