Pellionia cavaleriei

Pellionia cavaleriei, commonly known as the velvetleaf pellionia, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows as a low shrub with slender, creeping stems that root at the nodes. Its leaves are usually ovate to elliptical in shape, with a smooth, waxy surface.

Pellionia cavaleriei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in shaded garden settings.
