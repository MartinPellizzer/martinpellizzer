Peperomia heyneana

Peperomia heyneana, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit. The plant features glossy, ovate leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are clustered in spikes.

Peperomia heyneana is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and minimal water requirements.
