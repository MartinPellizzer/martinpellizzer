Pretrea

Pretrea, commonly known as the golden cup plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, yellow-orange flowers that resemble cups, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has a basal rosette of broad, heart-shaped leaves and produces a long, slender flowering stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

Pretrea is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
