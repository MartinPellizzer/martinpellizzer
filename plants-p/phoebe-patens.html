Phoebe patens

Phoebe patens, commonly known as the trailing phoebe, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the southeastern United States, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are elliptic to ovate in shape, with a glossy upper surface. Its small, fragrant flowers are typically white or pale green and are borne in clusters.

Phoebe patens is often used in ornamental horticulture for its attractive foliage and adaptability to various soil types.
