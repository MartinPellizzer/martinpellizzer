Pisonia nigricans var. oblonga

Pisonia nigricans var. oblonga, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pisonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and clusters of small, showy white or pink flowers that attract birds and insects. The plant produces woody, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, often found in coastal or disturbed habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.
