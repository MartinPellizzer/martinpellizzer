Pelargonium suaveolens

Pelargonium suaveolens, commonly known as the sweet geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant features broad, velvety leaves with a distinctive scent when crushed. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Pelargonium suaveolens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
