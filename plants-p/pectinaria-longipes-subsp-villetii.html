Pectinaria longipes subsp. villetii

Pectinaria longipes subsp.

villetii, commonly known as the slender pectinaria, is a flowering plant native to the Andes region of South America. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant is distinguished by its slender, erect stems and small, tubular flowers that are typically pale yellow to orange in color. It thrives in high-altitude environments, often found in rocky or alpine habitats.
