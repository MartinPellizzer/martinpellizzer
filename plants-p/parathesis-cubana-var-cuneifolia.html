Parathesis cubana var. cuneifolia

Parathesis cubana var. cuneifolia, commonly known as the Cuban flatleaf parathesis, is a perennial herb native to the Caribbean region.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, hairy pubescence. The leaves are lanceolate, with a distinctive cuneate base, giving it its specific epithet. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences, typically displaying shades of white or pale yellow.

This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded, tropical environments.
