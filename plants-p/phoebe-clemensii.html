Phoebe clemensii

Phoebe clemensii, commonly known as the slender phoebe, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the southeastern United States, where it thrives in moist, wooded areas. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and fragrant, small white flowers that bloom in the spring. It produces dark blue, berry-like fruits that are an important food source for local wildlife.

Phoebe clemensii is often found in understory habitats, growing along stream banks and in shaded forest floors.
