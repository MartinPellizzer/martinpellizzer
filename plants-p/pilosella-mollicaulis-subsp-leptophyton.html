Pilosella mollicaulis subsp. leptophyton

Pilosella mollicaulis subsp.

leptophyton, commonly known as the slenderleaf cat's-paw, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and ray florets.

The plant features a low-growing, mat-forming structure with soft, hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are yellow, with a central disk surrounded by pale yellow to white ray florets.

It thrives in dry, sunny habitats and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
