Pandanus loureiroi

Pandanus loureiroi, commonly known as the bottle palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that develop into woody, bottle-shaped fruits. Its leaves are often used in traditional crafts and thatching.

The species is valued for both its ornamental beauty and its practical uses in local communities.
