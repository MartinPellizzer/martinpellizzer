Pircunia

Pircunia, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that are often followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. Pircunia is native to regions in Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands.
