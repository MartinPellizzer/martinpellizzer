Pseudomachaerium

Pseudomachaerium, commonly known as false grass or dwarf bamboo, is a genus of flowering plants in the grass family, Poaceae.

Pseudomachaerium species are often used in landscaping for their ornamental value and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
