Panicum microstachyum

Panicum microstachyum, commonly known as little bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and compact, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically pale green to brown in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in prairie and woodland habitats.

Panicum microstachyum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological restoration projects.
