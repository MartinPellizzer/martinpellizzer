Primula × wettsteinii

Primula × wettsteinii, commonly known as the Wettstein's primrose, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Primulaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hairs. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe, it thrives in cool, moist environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and woodland plantings.
