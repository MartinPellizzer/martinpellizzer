Potentilla rubens

Potentilla rubens, commonly known as scarlet cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in moist, sunny habitats such as meadows and stream banks. The plant features bright red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its leaves are palmately divided into five lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Potentilla rubens is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in landscaping and ecological restoration projects.
