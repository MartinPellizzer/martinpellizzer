Pogostemon nigrescens

Pogostemon nigrescens, commonly known as the black-stemmed pogostemon, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its dark purple to black stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It produces small, two-lipped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color.

Pogostemon nigrescens is often grown for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
