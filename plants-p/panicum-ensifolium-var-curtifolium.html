Panicum ensifolium var. curtifolium

Panicum ensifolium var. curtifolium, commonly known as narrowleaf bluestem, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like clusters of flowers, which are usually pink to purple in hue. It thrives in full sun and is often found in dry, sandy soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in native plant landscapes.
