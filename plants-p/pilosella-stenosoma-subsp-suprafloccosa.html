Pilosella stenosoma subsp. suprafloccosa

Pilosella stenosoma subsp.

suprafloccosa, commonly known as the narrowleaf pilosella, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters.

The leaves are typically narrow and linear, with a hairy surface that gives the plant its common name. This subspecies is native to regions of Europe and is often found in grassy areas and disturbed soils.

It plays a role in supporting local pollinators and contributes to the biodiversity of its habitat.
