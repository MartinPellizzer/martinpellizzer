Paphiopedilum celebesense

Paphiopedilum celebesense, commonly known as the Celebes slipper orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Indonesian islands of Celebes and Sula.

It features a broad, fleshy rhizome and produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive lip that resembles a slipper. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of white or pale green hair. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color with a contrasting red or orange throat.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
