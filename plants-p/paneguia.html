Paneguia

Paneguia, commonly known as the Brazilian nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically purple or white in color. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Paneguia is known for its toxic properties, making it important to handle with care.
