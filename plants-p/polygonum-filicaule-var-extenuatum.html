Polygonum filicaule var. extenuatum

Polygonum filicaule var. extenuatum, commonly known as slender leaf smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist soils along stream banks, wetlands, and other wet habitats. The plant features slender, alternate leaves that are lanceolate in shape and often have a reddish or purple tinge. Its small, greenish-white flowers are arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the stems.

The species is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed or nutrient-rich environments.
