Polystachya duemmeriana

Polystachya duemmeriana, commonly known as the Duemmer's bamboo orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its rhizomes are fibrous and can form dense clumps over time.

Polystachya duemmeriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
