Pilosella pistoriensis

Pilosella pistoriensis, commonly known as the Pistoriella, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features small, yellowish to white ray florets surrounded by numerous disk florets. It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and wastelands.

Pilosella pistoriensis is a low-growing perennial that produces small, fluffy seeds dispersed by the wind.
