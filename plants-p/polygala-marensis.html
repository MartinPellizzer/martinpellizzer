Polygala marensis

Polygala marensis, commonly known as the Marensis milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its flowers are usually pale blue or white, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Polygala marensis is often found in rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
