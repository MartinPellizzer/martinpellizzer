Palala tomentosa

Palala tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly palala, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, white, woolly covering on its stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Palala tomentosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
