Peperomia pubipetiola

Peperomia pubipetiola, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves with a velvety texture, often covered in fine, short hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its low maintenance and attractive foliage make it a versatile and ornamental addition to any plant collection.
