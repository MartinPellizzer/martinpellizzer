Pycnostachys linifolia

Pycnostachys linifolia, commonly known as the slender flame vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its long, slender stems and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain seeds enclosed in a fibrous covering. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Pycnostachys linifolia is also valued for its ability to grow in poor soil conditions and its resilience to drought.
