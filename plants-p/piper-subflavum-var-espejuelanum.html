Piper subflavum var. espejuelanum

Piper subflavum var. espejuelanum, commonly known as the Espejuelanum pepper plant, is a tropical species native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many pepper species. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruits are typically small, round, and turn red when mature.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a spice in regional cuisines.
