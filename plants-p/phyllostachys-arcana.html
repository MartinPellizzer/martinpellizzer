Phyllostachys arcana

Phyllostachys arcana, commonly known as the golden bamboo, is a species of grass in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its golden-yellow culms and slender, arching leaves. This bamboo is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive color and graceful appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Phyllostachys arcana is relatively fast-growing and can be used in landscaping for erosion control and as a decorative plant.
