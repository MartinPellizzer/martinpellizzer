Portenschlagia trifoliata

Portenschlagia trifoliata, commonly known as the three-leaved broom.

It is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight.

Portenschlagia trifoliata is also valued for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
