Primula × morissetii

Primula × morissetii, commonly known as the Morisset's primrose, is a hybrid plant native to China.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

It is a member of the Primulaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants known for their showy blooms.
