Platanthera orbiculata

Platanthera orbiculata, commonly known as the orbicular lady's tresses, is a perennial orchid native to the northeastern United States and parts of Canada.

It features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces a dense, spiraling inflorescence of pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. The plant is characterized by its unique, fragrant, and showy blooms that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wetlands.

Platanthera orbiculata is a rare and endangered species, requiring specific ecological conditions for successful growth and reproduction.
