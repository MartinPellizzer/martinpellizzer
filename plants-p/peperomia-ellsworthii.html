Peperomia ellsworthii

Peperomia ellsworthii, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery-white margin, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that emerge from the leaf axils. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal water needs.

Peperomia ellsworthii thrives in well-draining soil and is relatively resistant to pests and diseases.
