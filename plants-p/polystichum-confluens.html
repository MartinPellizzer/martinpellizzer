Polystichum confluens

Polystichum confluens, commonly known as the clustered sword fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It is characterized by its dense, upright fronds that form a compact, bushy appearance. The fronds are typically dark green, leathery, and have a serrated edge. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This fern is valued for its ornamental qualities and is commonly used in horticulture and landscaping.
