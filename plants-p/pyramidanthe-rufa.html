Pyramidanthe rufa

Pyramidanthe rufa, commonly known as the red pyramid bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive pyramidal inflorescence, which consists of numerous small, tubular flowers arranged in a conical shape. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun conditions. Its flowers are usually pink to reddish in color, and they bloom during the warmer months of the year.

Pyramidanthe rufa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
