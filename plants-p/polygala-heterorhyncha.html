Polygala heterorhyncha

Polygala heterorhyncha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grassland and rocky habitats, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. The plant features slender, erect stems with alternate, narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, clustered in spicate inflorescences, and are usually pale yellow or greenish in color.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and flowering habit.
