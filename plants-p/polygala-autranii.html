Polygala autranii

Polygala autranii, commonly known as the Madagascar milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, often pale flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to the island's unique climate. Its leaves are usually narrow and opposite, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.
