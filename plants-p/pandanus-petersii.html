Pandanus petersii

Pandanus petersii, commonly known as the Hainan pandanus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters at the top of the plant. Its fibrous leaves are used to make mats, baskets, and other woven items.

Pandanus petersii thrives in humid, coastal environments and is often found in mangrove ecosystems.
