Picea abies f. viminalis

Picea abies f. viminalis, commonly known as the weeping spruce, is a unique form of the European spruce.

It is characterized by its pendulous branches that droop gracefully, creating a cascading appearance. This form is a natural variant or a cultivar that exhibits a distinctive weeping habit. The foliage consists of needle-like leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in a dense, bushy manner.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking visual appeal.
