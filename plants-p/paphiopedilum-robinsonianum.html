Paphiopedilum robinsonianum

Paphiopedilum robinsonianum, commonly known as the Robust Paphiopedilum, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short, fleshy rhizome and a single, erect stem that bears several leaves. The plant produces showy, lip-shaped flowers with a distinctive pouch-like structure, typically in shades of yellow and green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

This species is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique floral morphology.
