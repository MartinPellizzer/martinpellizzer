Pteronia callosa

Pteronia callosa, commonly known as the rough pteronia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it adapts well to dry and nutrient-poor conditions.

The flowers bloom in spring and attract various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.
