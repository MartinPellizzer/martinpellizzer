Pleopeltis hammatisora

Pleopeltis hammatisora, commonly known as the rock fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its small, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its fronds are usually dark green and can have a slightly hairy texture.

Pleopeltis hammatisora is well adapted to arid environments and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.
