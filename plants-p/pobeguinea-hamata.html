Pobeguinea hamata

Pobeguinea hamata, commonly known as the African violet, is a tropical perennial plant native to Eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette of velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Pobeguinea hamata is known for its ability to bloom throughout the year under optimal conditions.
