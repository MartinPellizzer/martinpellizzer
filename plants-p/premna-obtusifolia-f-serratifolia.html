Premna obtusifolia f. serratifolia

Premna obtusifolia f. serratifolia, commonly known as the serrated-leaf premna, is a variant of the common premna species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub or vine characterized by its opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves with serrated edges. The leaves are typically glossy and dark green, often with a slightly hairy texture. It produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters, followed by small, spherical fruits.

This plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks.
