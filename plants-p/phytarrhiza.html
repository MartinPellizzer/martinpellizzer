Phytarrhiza

It is characterized by its underground tuberous roots, which are the primary edible part of the plant. The tubers have a starchy, slightly sweet flavor and are often used in traditional cuisines. Botanically, Phytarrhiza belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other plants like taro and芋头.
