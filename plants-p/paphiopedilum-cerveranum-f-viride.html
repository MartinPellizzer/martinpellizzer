Paphiopedilum cerveranum f. viride

Paphiopedilum cerveranum f. viride, commonly known as the Emerald Cabbage Paphiopedilum, is a rare and unique orchid species native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that resemble cabbage leaves, hence its common name. The plant produces striking, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive pouch-like structure typical of the Paphiopedilum genus. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

This orchid is highly sought after by collectors due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal.
