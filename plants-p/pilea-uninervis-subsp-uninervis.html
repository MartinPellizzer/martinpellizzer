Pilea uninervis subsp. uninervis

Pilea uninervis subsp.

uninervis, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to southern China. It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter in color underneath.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by round, red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its attractive foliage and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
