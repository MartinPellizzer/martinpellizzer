Peperomia bilobulata

Peperomia bilobulata, commonly known as the Bilobed Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are often slightly lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia bilobulata is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive foliage.
