Plerandra nesopanax

Plerandra nesopanax, commonly known as the golden wax tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant yellow flowers. The plant is noted for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.

Plerandra nesopanax is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
