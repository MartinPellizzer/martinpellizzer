Plantia flava

Plantia flava, commonly known as the golden leaf plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Overall, Plantia flava is a visually striking plant with both ornamental and medicinal value.
