Piper taboganum

Piper taboganum, commonly known as the Taboga pepper or Tabogana, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing vines and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are greenish-white and clustered in spikes, while the fruit is a small, round, and mildly peppery berry.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in local medicine.
