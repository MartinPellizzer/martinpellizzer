Primulina renifolia

Primulina renifolia, commonly known as the velvet leaf philodendron, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves that are typically deep green with a distinctive reddish or purple tinge on the underside. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes many ornamental and decorative plants. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and can grow quite large, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its lush appearance.
