Pandanus raroiaensis

Pandanus raroiaensis, commonly known as the Raroia pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Pandanus.

It is native to the island of Raroia in the Tuamotu Archipelago of French Polynesia. The plant is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that grow in a rosette formation. It produces small, clustered inflorescences that bear yellowish flowers, which are followed by edible, fibrous fruits.

This species is adapted to coastal and sandy environments, making it an important component of the local ecosystem.
