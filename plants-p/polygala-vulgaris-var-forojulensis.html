Polygala vulgaris var. forojulensis

Polygala vulgaris var. forojulensis, commonly known as the Forojulian milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Europe.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant has slender stems and lance-shaped leaves, often growing in open, sunny habitats. It is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
