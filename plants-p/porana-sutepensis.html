Porana sutepensis

Porana sutepensis, commonly known as the Sutepen rose.

It is a flowering plant native to the Andean region of South America, particularly in Peru. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Porana sutepensis is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and pleasant scent.
