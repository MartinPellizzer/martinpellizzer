Psoralea linearifolia var. robusta

Psoralea linearifolia var. robusta, commonly known as the robust psoralea, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features slender, linear leaves that are often hairy, and it produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or coastal regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its drought tolerance and unique foliage.
