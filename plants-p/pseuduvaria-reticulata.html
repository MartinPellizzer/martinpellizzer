Pseuduvaria reticulata

Pseuduvaria reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a tropical plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a prominent network of veins, giving it its common name. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and collectors.
