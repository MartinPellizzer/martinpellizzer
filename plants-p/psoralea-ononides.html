Psoralea ononides

Psoralea ononides, commonly known as the narrow-leafed psoralea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It produces small, lens-shaped pods that contain several seeds.
