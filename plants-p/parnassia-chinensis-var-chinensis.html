Parnassia chinensis var. chinensis

Parnassia chinensis var. chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese mountain buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features showy, white flowers with yellow centers, typically blooming in late summer. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Ranunculaceae family.
