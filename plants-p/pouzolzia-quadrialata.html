Pouzolzia quadrialata

Pouzolzia quadrialata, commonly known as the four-angled pouzolzia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant has opposite, four-angled leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are usually greenish-white in color and arranged in clusters.
