Panicum nidulans

Panicum nidulans, commonly known as the narrowleaf panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. Its leaves are narrow and often have a bluish-green hue, which helps it conserve water in arid environments.

Panicum nidulans is often found in disturbed soils and can serve as a pioneer species in ecological succession.
