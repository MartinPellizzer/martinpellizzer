Potentilla sororia

Potentilla sororia, commonly known as the alpine potentilla, is a low-growing shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, upright stems and dark green, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in pairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine landscapes.

Potentilla sororia is valued for its ornamental value and its ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
