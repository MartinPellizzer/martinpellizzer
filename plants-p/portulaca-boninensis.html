Portulaca boninensis

Portulaca boninensis, commonly known as the Bonin Portulaca, is a flowering plant native to the Bonin Islands.

It is a low-growing annual herb with fleshy, succulent stems and leaves. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in the morning and close in the afternoon. It thrives in well-drained sandy soils and full sun exposure.

Portulaca boninensis is adapted to arid environments, making it drought-resistant and suitable for xeriscaping.
