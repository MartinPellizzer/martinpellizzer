Protea ovata

Protea ovata, commonly known as the oval protea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its striking, cone-shaped inflorescence. The plant produces large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. Protea ovata thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sunlight.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
