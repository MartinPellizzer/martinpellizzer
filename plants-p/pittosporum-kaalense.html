Pittosporum kaalense

Pittosporum kaalense, commonly known as the Kaalens pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are oval in shape and have a smooth texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers a sunny to partially shaded location.

Pittosporum kaalense is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
