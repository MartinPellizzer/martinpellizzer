Pavonia spiralis

Pavonia spiralis, commonly known as the spiral pavonia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet. The plant features striking, spiral-shaped inflorescences composed of pink or red bracts that resemble petals. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Pavonia spiralis thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.
