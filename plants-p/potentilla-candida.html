Potentilla candida

Potentilla candida, commonly known as white cinquefoil, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It features small, white, five-petaled flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are alternate along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight.

Potentilla candida is often found in moist meadows, stream banks, and open woodlands.
