Posoqueria decora

Posoqueria decora, commonly known as the pink velvet tamarind, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its pinnately compound leaves, which are often covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. Its flowers are small and yellow, followed by distinctive, flat, pink or red fruits that are edible.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
