Phoebe heterotepala

Phoebe heterotepala, commonly known as the slender phoebe, is a species of flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and avocados. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth, glossy surface. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale green, followed by small, dark berries.
