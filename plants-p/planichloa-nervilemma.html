Planichloa nervilemma

Planichloa nervilemma, commonly known as the narrow-leafed moss, is a species of moss belonging to the family Pottiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth form and small, closely packed leaves that are typically narrow and linear. It plays an important role in soil stabilization and nutrient cycling in its habitat.
