Pithecoseris pacourinoides var. capitata

Pithecoseris pacourinoides var. capitata, commonly known as the Cape goldenbush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and yellowish-green foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its compact form and seasonal blooms.
