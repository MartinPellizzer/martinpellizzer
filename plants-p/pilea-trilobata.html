Pilea trilobata

Pilea trilobata, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale green, followed by round, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea trilobata is known for its slow growth and ability to tolerate low light conditions.
