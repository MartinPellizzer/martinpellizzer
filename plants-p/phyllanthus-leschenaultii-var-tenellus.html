Phyllanthus leschenaultii var. tenellus

Phyllanthus leschenaultii var. tenellus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spurge, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
