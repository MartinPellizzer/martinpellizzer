Pyrrocoma foliosa

Pyrrocoma foliosa, commonly known as the leafy pyrrocoma, is a perennial herb native to specific regions of South America.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly serrated edge.

Pyrrocoma foliosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
