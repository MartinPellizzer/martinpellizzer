Phyteuma nigrum var. integrifolium

Phyteuma nigrum var. integrifolium, commonly known as the black nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has simple, entire leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
