Polygala micrantha

Polygala micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces pods that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
