Paeonia × suffruticosa var. spontanea

Paeonia × suffruticosa var. spontanea, commonly known as the tree peony, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its woody stems and large, showy flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and features compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, white, and yellow, and are often fragrant.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and traditional Chinese medicine.
