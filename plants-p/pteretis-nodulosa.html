Pteretis nodulosa

Pteretis nodulosa, commonly known as the nodular fern, is a species of fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its compact, clump-forming growth habit and its distinctive, nodular rhizomes. The fronds are bipinnate, with a delicate, feathery appearance, and they are typically bright green in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological diversity of its native habitat.
