Phyllanthus obdeltophyllus

Phyllanthus obdeltophyllus, commonly known as the broad-leaf phyllanthus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate to elliptical leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its small, yellowish-green flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces small, spherical fruits.

The plant is often found in disturbed habitats and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
