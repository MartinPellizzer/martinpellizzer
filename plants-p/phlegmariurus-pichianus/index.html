Phlegmariurus Pichianus

Phlegmariurus pichianus is a rare and enigmatic liverwort species found in specific microhabitats across the Andes. This plant belongs to the family Porellaceae and is known for its unique morphology and ecological niche. It thrives in humid, shaded environments where it forms dense mats on rocky surfaces. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species facing threats from habitat degradation. Studying Phlegmariurus pichianus offers insights into the adaptation strategies of cryptogamic plants in extreme conditions.
