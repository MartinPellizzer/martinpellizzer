Prunus avium f. fastigiata

Prunus avium f. fastigiata, commonly known as the upright cherry tree, is a compact, columnar form of the sweet cherry.

It features dense, upright growth with small, dark green leaves and produces small, sweet fruits. The plant is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping due to its neat, formal appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun for optimal growth and fruit production.

This variety is relatively disease-resistant and suitable for smaller gardens or urban settings.
