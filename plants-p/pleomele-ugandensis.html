Pleomele ugandensis

Pleomele ugandensis, commonly known as the Uganda pleomele, is a species of cycad native to Uganda.

It is a slow-growing plant with a thick, trunk-like stem and a crown of pinnate leaves. The leaves are typically bright green and can reach lengths of up to 2 meters. It produces cones, with male and female cones on separate plants, which are essential for its reproduction.

This plant is adapted to tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun.
