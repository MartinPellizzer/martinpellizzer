Panicum sprengelii

Panicum sprengelii, commonly known as silver hair grass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, silver-haired leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements further enhance its appeal in landscaping.
