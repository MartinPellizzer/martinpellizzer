Piper nigricaule

Piper nigricaule, commonly known as black-stemmed pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its dark green, often glossy leaves and small, clustered white flowers. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn from green to black as they mature.

The plant is valued for its aromatic leaves and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
