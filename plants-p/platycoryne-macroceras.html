Platycoryne macroceras

Platycoryne macroceras, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a unique member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 20 centimeters in diameter. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded, moist environments. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, and it produces a distinctive, fragrant scent.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a valuable species in botanical research and conservation efforts.
