Pennisetum polycladum

Pennisetum polycladum, commonly known as the purple fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its clumping growth habit and striking purple to pink plumes that appear in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Pennisetum polycladum is also valued for its ability to attract birds and other wildlife.
