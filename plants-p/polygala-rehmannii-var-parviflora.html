Polygala rehmannii var. parviflora

Polygala rehmannii var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features slender, branching stems and small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or limestone habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
