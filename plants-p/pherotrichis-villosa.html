Pherotrichis villosa

Pherotrichis villosa, commonly known as the silky pherotrich, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
