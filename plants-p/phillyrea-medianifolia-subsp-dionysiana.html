Phillyrea medianifolia subsp. dionysiana

Phillyrea medianifolia subsp.

dionysiana, commonly known as the Dionysian Phillyrea, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy.

The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in clusters during the spring. These flowers are followed by small, spherical berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

It is well adapted to rocky and dry soils, making it a hardy and ornamental plant for coastal and Mediterranean gardens.
