Perrottetia sandwicensis

Perrottetia sandwicensis, commonly known as the Hawaiian hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and is characterized by its showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces bright red or yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. It is adapted to the tropical climate of Hawaii and thrives in well-drained soils.
