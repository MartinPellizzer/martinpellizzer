Primula × kraettliana

Primula × kraettliana, commonly known as the hybrid primrose, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically blooms in early spring, often before the leaves fully emerge. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial to full shade.

Its foliage consists of broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture.
