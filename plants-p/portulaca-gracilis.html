Portulaca gracilis

Portulaca gracilis, commonly known as the slender portulaca, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing annual herb that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features fleshy, succulent leaves and produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers. It is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its drought tolerance and aesthetic appeal.

The species is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
