Pilea hygrophila

Pilea hygrophila, commonly known as the water pilea, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green, with a slightly waxy texture. Its small, white flowers bloom in clusters, adding a delicate touch to its appearance.

Pilea hygrophila is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
