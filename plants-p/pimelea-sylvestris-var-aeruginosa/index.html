Pimelea Sylvestris Var. Aeruginosa

Pimelea sylvestris var. aeruginosa is a striking evergreen shrub known for its vibrant blue-green foliage and clusters of yellow flowers. Native to the coastal regions of southeastern Australia, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its unique coloration makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both ornamental value and hardiness. The variety is closely related to the common pimelea, but its distinct hue sets it apart in the botanical world. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to flourish in non-native environments.
