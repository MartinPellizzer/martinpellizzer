Psoralea arborescens

Psoralea arborescens, commonly known as the tree psoralea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant features pinnate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and arranged in spikes, often blooming in spring.

Psoralea arborescens is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
