Polygonum erythranthum

Polygonum erythranthum, commonly known as redstem smartweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist soils along stream banks and wetlands. The plant features upright stems that are reddish in color, especially when mature, and produces small, clustered flowers in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

It is often found in disturbed areas and can serve as a food source for various insects and wildlife.
