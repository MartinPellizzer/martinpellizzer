Pleioblastus altiligulatus var. spongiosus

Pleioblastus altiligulatus var. spongiosus, commonly known as the sponge bamboo, is a variant of the tall bamboo species native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems that often exhibit a slightly zigzagging growth pattern. The leaves are elongated, narrow, and have a soft, silky texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Its unique foliage and graceful form make it a popular choice for both ornamental and ecological landscaping.
