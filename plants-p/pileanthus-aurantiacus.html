Pileanthus aurantiacus

Pileanthus aurantiacus, commonly known as the orange flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red flowers that bloom in dense, showy clusters during the warmer months. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, narrow leaflets that provide a lush green backdrop to its flamboyant blooms. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and seasonal display make it a notable addition to botanical collections and landscaped areas.
