Potentilla × diversifolia var. ranunculus

Potentilla × diversifolia var. ranunculus, commonly known as the variable potentilla, is a hybrid shrub characterized by its diverse floral morphology.

It typically features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. The plant is native to regions of Europe and Asia, where it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. Its botanical variability makes it a subject of interest in horticultural and taxonomic studies.

The species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to different garden conditions.
