Polypodium chunii

Polypodium chunii, commonly known as the Chinese wall fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen fronds that are typically divided into narrow, lance-shaped segments. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in mountainous regions of East Asia. Its rhizome is thick and creeping, supporting the growth of its delicate foliage.

Polypodium chunii is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
