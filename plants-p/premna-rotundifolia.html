Premna rotundifolia

Premna rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a shrub or small tree with opposite, ovate to round leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, showy flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white, pink, or purple. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

Premna rotundifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
