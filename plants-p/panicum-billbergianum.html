Panicum billbergianum

Panicum billbergianum, commonly known as red oat grass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features tall, slender stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height, with narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces panicles of small, crimson-colored flowers, which are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its rapid growth and ability to spread through rhizomes make it both a desirable and sometimes invasive species.
