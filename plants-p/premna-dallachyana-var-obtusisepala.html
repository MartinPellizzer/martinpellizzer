Premna dallachyana var. obtusisepala

Premna dallachyana var. obtusisepala, commonly known as the broad-sepal Premna, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and have distinctive, broad, rounded sepals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
