Primulina longicalyx

Primulina longicalyx, commonly known as the long-leaved primula, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.

Primulina longicalyx prefers well-draining soil and consistent moisture, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
