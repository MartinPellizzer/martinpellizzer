Pyrethrum × sinense

Pyrethrum × sinense, commonly known as Chinese chrysanthemum, is a hybrid plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant is herbaceous, with finely divided leaves and can grow up to 1 meter in height. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Pyrethrum × sinense is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
