Puccinellia retroflexa var. pulvinata

Puccinellia retroflexa var. pulvinata, commonly known as the slender spikegrass, is a perennial grass species native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to harsh environmental conditions. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a compact growth habit.

It is often found in dunes, salt marshes, and other disturbed habitats.
