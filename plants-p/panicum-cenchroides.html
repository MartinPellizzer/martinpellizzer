Panicum cenchroides

Panicum cenchroides, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, linear leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous fine, pale green to brown flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it adaptable to various environmental conditions.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and seasonal color changes.
