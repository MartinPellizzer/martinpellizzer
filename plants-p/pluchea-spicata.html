Pluchea spicata

Pluchea spicata, commonly known as spiked sunflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Pluchea spicata is known for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
