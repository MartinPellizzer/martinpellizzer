Polygala gigantea

Polygala gigantea, commonly known as the giant milkvetch, is a striking plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem that can reach up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, delicate white or pink flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Polygala gigantea is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments.
