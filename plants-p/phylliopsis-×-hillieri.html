Phylliopsis × hillieri

Phylliopsis × hillieri, commonly known as the hybrid evergreen fern, is a unique plant that results from the cross between Phylliopsis falcata and Phylliopsis hirsuta.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy foliage and compact growth habit, making it an attractive ornamental plant. The fronds are typically dark green with a velvety texture and exhibit a feathery, delicate appearance. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.

This hybrid is valued for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in indoor settings with proper care.
