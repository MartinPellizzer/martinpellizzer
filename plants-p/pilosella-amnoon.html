Pilosella amnoon

Pilosella amnoon, commonly known as the alpine mouse-ear, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its small, white to pale yellow daisy-like flowers and hairy, basal leaves. Native to mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in alpine and subalpine environments. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to cold, high-altitude climates.
