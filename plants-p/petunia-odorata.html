Petunia odorata

Petunia odorata, commonly known as the sweet petunia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.
