Pilea hitchcockii

Pilea hitchcockii, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea hitchcockii is known for its ease of care and ability to purify the air, adding both aesthetic and functional value to any space.
