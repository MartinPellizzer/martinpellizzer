Phyllanthus craibii

Phyllanthus craibii, commonly known as the Chinese phyllanthus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including parts of China, India, and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered leaves and yellowish flowers that appear in late spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
