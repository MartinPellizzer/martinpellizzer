Polygala ruderalis

Polygala ruderalis, commonly known as the field milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the Polygalaceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia and is often found in disturbed soils such as roadsides and fields. The plant has slender, erect stems and small, often pale green or yellowish flowers clustered in spikes. Its leaves are typically alternate and pinnately divided, giving it a delicate appearance.

It is a herbaceous perennial that can tolerate a range of environmental conditions, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
