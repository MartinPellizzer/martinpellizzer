Peperomia rupertiana var. pinchonii

Peperomia rupertiana var. pinchonii, commonly known as the Pinchonii Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, fleshy foliage with a variety of leaf shapes, often including ovate or elliptical forms. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for collectors and indoor gardeners.
