Phrynium obscurum

Phrynium obscurum, commonly known as the dark phrynium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and often have a waxy texture with a deep green color.

Phrynium obscurum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in humid gardens for its striking foliage.
