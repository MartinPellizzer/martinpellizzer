Pilosella piloselloides subsp. parciflocca

Pilosella piloselloides subsp.

parciflocca, commonly known as the slender leaf goatweed, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its small, yellowish to greenish flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters.

The leaves are typically narrow and hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance. This subspecies is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in dry, open habitats.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces seeds through a simple, wind-dispersed mechanism.
