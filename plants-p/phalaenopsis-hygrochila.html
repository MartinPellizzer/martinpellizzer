Phalaenopsis hygrochila

Phalaenopsis hygrochila, commonly known as the moist orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically has broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Phalaenopsis hygrochila is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
