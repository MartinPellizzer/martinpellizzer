Pastinaca candolleana

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its hairy, lobed leaves and yellow-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.
