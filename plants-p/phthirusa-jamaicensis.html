Phthirusa jamaicensis

Phthirusa jamaicensis, commonly known as the Jamaican spider orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the Caribbean region.

This terrestrial orchid typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are small and delicate, usually with a greenish-yellow coloration and a distinct, intricate structure. The plant produces a single stem with several flowers arranged in a spiral pattern.

It is characterized by its specialized root system that helps it absorb nutrients from the surrounding soil.
