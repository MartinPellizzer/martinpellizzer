Prostanthera canaliculata var. canosericea

Prostanthera canaliculata var. canosericea, commonly known as the silky lemon myrtle, is a compact shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features small, aromatic leaves with a lemon-scented aroma, and its flowers are typically white or pale blue. The plant is characterized by its dense foliage and is often grown for its ornamental value and medicinal properties. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is noted for its unique fragrance and is sometimes used in herbal remedies.
