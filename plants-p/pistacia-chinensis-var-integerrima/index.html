Pistacia Chinensis Var. Integerrima

Pistacia chinensis var. integerrima is a rare and hardy plant native to the mountainous regions of China. It is known for its distinctive appearance and resilience to harsh environmental conditions. This variety of pistachio tree has unique foliage and is often found in rocky and arid landscapes. Its ecological importance makes it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Despite its hardiness, it remains a relatively obscure species in the broader botanical world.
