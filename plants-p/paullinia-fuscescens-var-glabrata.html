Paullinia fuscescens var. glabrata

Paullinia fuscescens var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth paullinia, is a species of flowering plant in the Sapindaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. It produces small, round fruits that are typically green when mature.
