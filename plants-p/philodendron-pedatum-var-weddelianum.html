Philodendron pedatum var. weddelianum

Philodendron pedatum var. weddelianum, commonly known as the Weddellianum philodendron, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often a deep green with a slightly variegated pattern. The plant has a climbing habit, featuring aerial roots that allow it to attach to supports as it grows. Its foliage is known for its striking appearance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.
