Pteroloma triquetrum subsp. pseudotriquetrum

Pteroloma triquetrum subsp.

pseudotriquetrum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pteroloma, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It typically grows in grassland and rocky habitats, often in regions with a temperate climate.

The plant features linear, narrow leaves that are often hairy, and it produces small, clustered flowers with yellow petals. It is characterized by its distinct leaf morphology and is part of the Asteraceae family.
