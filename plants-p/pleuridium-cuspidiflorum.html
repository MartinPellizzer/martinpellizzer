Pleuridium cuspidiflorum

Pleuridium cuspidiflorum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pleuridium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in grassland habitats. The plant produces delicate, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a hairy surface.

It is often found in open, sunny areas and is adapted to dry conditions.
