Peperomia modicilimba

Peperomia modicilimba, commonly known as the Modicilimba Peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale pink, which appear in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its resilience and aesthetic appeal, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
