Phacelia insularis var. insularis

Phacelia insularis var. insularis, commonly known as the island phacelia, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in coastal areas and dry, rocky habitats. The plant features slender stems and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

It plays an important role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for native pollinators.
