Phillyrea latifolia var. rotundifolia

Phillyrea latifolia var. rotundifolia, commonly known as the rounded-leaved Phillyrea, is a shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with thick, leathery leaves that are rounded or oval in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters, which are followed by bright red, fleshy berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for Mediterranean climates.

The leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their astringent properties.
