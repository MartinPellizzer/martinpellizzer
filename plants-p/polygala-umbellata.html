Polygala umbellata

Polygala umbellata, commonly known as the clustered milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The flowers are typically pale green to yellowish in color and are arranged in umbel-like inflorescences. The plant produces small, bean-like pods that contain several seeds.

It is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
