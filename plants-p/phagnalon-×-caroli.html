Phagnalon × caroli

Phagnalon × caroli, commonly known as the hybrid meadow everlasting, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its showy, daisy-like flowers consist of a yellow disk surrounded by white to pink ray florets.

Phagnalon × caroli is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in garden landscapes for its hardy and drought-resistant nature.
