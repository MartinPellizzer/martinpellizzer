Peperomia irazuana

Peperomia irazuana, commonly known as the Irazu peperomia, is a tropical plant native to Costa Rica.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and glossy, ovate leaves. The plant typically features small, clustered flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia irazuana is relatively low-maintenance and suitable for beginners due to its resilience and aesthetic appeal.
