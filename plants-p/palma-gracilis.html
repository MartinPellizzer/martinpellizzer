Palma gracilis

Palma gracilis, commonly known as the slender palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot. Its growth habit makes it suitable for ornamental use in gardens and as a houseplant.

Palma gracilis is also valued for its ability to purify the air and enhance the aesthetic appeal of indoor spaces.
