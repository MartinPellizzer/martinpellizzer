Paphiopedilum villosum var. densissimum

Paphiopedilum villosum var. densissimum, commonly known as the dense hairy paphiopedilum, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a thick, hairy rhizome and produces showy, lip-shaped flowers with a distinctive yellow and green coloration. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. Its leaves are ovate and often covered in fine, velvety hairs.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
