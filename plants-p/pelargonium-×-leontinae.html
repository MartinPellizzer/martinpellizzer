Pelargonium × leontinae

Pelargonium × leontinae, commonly known as the Lemon Sorbet geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from white to pink and yellow, often with a lemon-yellow hue. The plant produces fragrant, five-petaled blooms that are typically borne on long, slender stalks. Its leaves are usually rounded with a slightly serrated edge and a velvety texture.

Pelargonium × leontinae is a popular ornamental plant, valued for its ornate appearance and ease of cultivation.
