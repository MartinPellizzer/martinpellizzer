Phylica longimontana

Phylica longimontana, commonly known as the long-stemmed phylica, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense clusters of small, needle-like leaves. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers that bloom in winter, making it a notable ornamental species. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Its unique appearance and seasonal flowering make it a popular choice for gardens and horticultural collections.
