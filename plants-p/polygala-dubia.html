Polygala dubia

Polygala dubia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, erect stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences. Its root system is fibrous and adapted to thrive in well-drained, sandy soils.

It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.
