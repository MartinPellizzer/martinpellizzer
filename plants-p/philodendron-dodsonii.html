Philodendron dodsonii

Philodendron dodsonii, commonly known as the golden monstera, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a waxy texture. The plant has a climbing habit and can grow quite large if given adequate space and support. Its leaves often develop a golden or variegated pattern, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Philodendron dodsonii prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
