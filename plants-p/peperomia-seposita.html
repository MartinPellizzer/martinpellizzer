Peperomia seposita

Peperomia seposita, commonly known as the clustered peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to Brazil.

It features dense clusters of dark green, glossy leaves that are often adorned with silver or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia seposita is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
