Pterostylis vitrea

Pterostylis vitrea, commonly known as the glassy greenhood, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and two elliptical, dark green leaves. The plant produces a single, hooded flower with a distinctive white and green coloration. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and heathlands.

The species is notable for its unique floral structure and is part of the Orchidaceae family.
