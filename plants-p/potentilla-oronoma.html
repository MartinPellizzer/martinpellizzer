Potentilla oronoma

Potentilla oronoma, commonly known as the white potentilla, is a species of flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 20 inches. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a finely toothed margin.

Potentilla oronoma is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.
