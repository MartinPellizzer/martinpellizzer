Prunus mutabilis f. blanda

Prunus mutabilis f. blanda, commonly known as the Blanda cherry, is a botanical variant of the Japanese flowering cherry.

It is characterized by its small, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant is native to Japan and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for temperate climates.

The leaves are typically dark green and provide a striking contrast to the delicate blossoms.
