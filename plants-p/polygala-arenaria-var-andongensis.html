Polygala arenaria var. andongensis

Polygala arenaria var. andongensis, commonly known as the Andong polygala, is a perennial herb native to Korea.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered with fine hairs, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

This species is of interest in botanical studies due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
