Picotia verna

Picotia verna, commonly known as the spring broom.

It is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces seeds in small, capsule-like structures. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Picotia verna is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
