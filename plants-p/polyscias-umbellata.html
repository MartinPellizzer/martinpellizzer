Polyscias umbellata

Polyscias umbellata, commonly known as the umbrella plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. Polyscias umbellata prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.
