Phyteuma scorzonerifolium var. eynense

Phyteuma scorzonerifolium var. eynense, commonly known as the Eynense goatsbeard, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite, linear leaves. The plant produces showy yellow to orange inflorescences that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is often cultivated in gardens and natural habitats.
