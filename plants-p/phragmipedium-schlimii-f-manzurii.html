Phragmipedium schlimii f. manzurii

Phragmipedium schlimii f. manzurii, commonly known as the Manzuri phragmipedium, is a hybrid orchid species native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration pattern. The flowers typically feature a yellow lip with a deep red or maroon throat, surrounded by white or pale pink petals. This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high air humidity and good drainage for optimal growth.

It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively manageable care requirements.
