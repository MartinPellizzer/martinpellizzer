Podorungia gesnerioides

Podorungia gesnerioides, commonly known as the silverleaf gesneria, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Podorungia gesnerioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
