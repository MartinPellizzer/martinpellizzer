Pomatocalpa grande

Pomatocalpa grande, commonly known as the large orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink with a yellow lip. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, forming pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a fan-like pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
