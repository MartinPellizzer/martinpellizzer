Paracaryum cariense

Paracaryum cariense, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with waxy, velvety leaves that are often dark green. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Paracaryum cariense is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
