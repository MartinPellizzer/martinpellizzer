Pleurothallis surinamensis

Pleurothallis surinamensis, commonly known as the Surinam orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South America.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments within tropical rainforests. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the forest floor.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is part of the diverse genus Pleurothallis, which includes over 1,500 species.
