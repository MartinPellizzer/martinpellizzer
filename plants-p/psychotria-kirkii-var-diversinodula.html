Psychotria kirkii var. diversinodula

Psychotria kirkii var. diversinodula, commonly known as the African morning glory, is a tropical shrub native to regions of Africa.

It features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated margins and produces striking violet to blue flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is characterized by its unique floral structure, with tubular corolla and prominent stamens. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
