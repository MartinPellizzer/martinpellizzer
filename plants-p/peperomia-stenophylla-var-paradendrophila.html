Peperomia stenophylla var. paradendrophila

Peperomia stenophylla var. paradendrophila, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically silver or variegated with white and green, providing a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or red, which appear in clusters at the base of the leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
