Polystichum × kumamontanum

Polystichum × kumamontanum, commonly known as the Kumamoto holly fern, is a hybrid fern species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen fronds that are dark green and glossy, with a leathery texture. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as woodland areas or rocky crevices. Its fronds are compound, with multiple pinnae that are closely spaced and slightly curled at the tips.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage.
