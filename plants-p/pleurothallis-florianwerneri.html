Pleurothallis florianwerneri

Pleurothallis florianwerneri, commonly known as the Florian Werneri orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, showy blooms in shades of white, pink, and purple, which are clustered in racemes. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments to ensure its conservation.
