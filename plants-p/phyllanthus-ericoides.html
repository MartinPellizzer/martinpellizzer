Phyllanthus ericoides

Phyllanthus ericoides, commonly known as the African balsam, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences, producing a distinctive yellowish-green color.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional African medicine.
