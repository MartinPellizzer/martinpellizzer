Pittosporum nadarivatense

Pittosporum nadarivatense, commonly known as the Kandy pittosporum, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to Sri Lanka.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter on the underside. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often followed by small, orange-red berries. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in forested areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
