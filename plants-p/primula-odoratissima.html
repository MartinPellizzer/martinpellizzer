Primula odoratissima

Primula odoratissima, commonly known as the fragrant primrose, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and prefers cool, moist environments. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, often with a fuzzy texture.

Primula odoratissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and subtle, sweet fragrance.
