Philodendron melinonii

Philodendron melinonii, commonly known as the Melinonii Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and foliage-focused plant collections.
