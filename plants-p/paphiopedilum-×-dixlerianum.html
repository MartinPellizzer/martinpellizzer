Paphiopedilum × dixlerianum

Paphiopedilum × dixlerianum, commonly known as the Dixlerian orchid, is a hybrid of Paphiopedilum sanderianum and Paphiopedilum niveum.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia. The plant features a thick, fleshy rhizome and produces showy, lip-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil.

The orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
