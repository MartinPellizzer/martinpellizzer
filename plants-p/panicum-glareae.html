Panicum glareae

Panicum glareae, commonly known as the silver panicum, is a perennial grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that are often used in ornamental landscaping. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for gardeners in arid regions.
