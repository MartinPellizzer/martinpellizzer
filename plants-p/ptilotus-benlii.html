Ptilotus benlii

Ptilotus benlii, commonly known as the pink ghost flower, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

The plant is a low-growing annual herb with bracts that are often more showy than the actual flowers. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy soils.

Ptilotus benlii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
