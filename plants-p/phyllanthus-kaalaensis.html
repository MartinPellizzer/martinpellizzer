Phyllanthus kaalaensis

Phyllanthus kaalaensis, commonly known as the Kaalaensis spurge, is a shrub native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its small, opposite leaves and distinctive yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces capsules as its fruit, which contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in forested or shaded areas.
