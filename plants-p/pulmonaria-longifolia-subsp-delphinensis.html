Pulmonaria longifolia subsp. delphinensis

Pulmonaria longifolia subsp.

delphinensis, commonly known as the Delphinian lungwort, is a perennial herb native to Central and Eastern Europe. It features basal rosettes of hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with purple or violet.

The plant produces striking clusters of blue to violet flowers in early spring, which are borne on tall, slender stems. It thrives in moist, shady woodlands and is often found in calcareous soils.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting early pollinators.
