Polygonum meisnerianum var. usterianum

Polygonum meisnerianum var. usterianum, commonly known as the Usterian knotweed, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring upright stems that are often reddish in color. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in dense clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It is characterized by its ability to thrive in moist, fertile soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.
