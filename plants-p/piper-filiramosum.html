Piper filiramosum

Piper filiramosum, commonly known as the slender pipevine, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in spikes. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland forests.

The fruits are berries that mature to a dark purple color, containing several seeds.
