Pleurothallis fuegi

Pleurothallis fuegi, commonly known as the Fuegian pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It belongs to the orchid family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the understory of forests. Its leaves are usually elongated and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

The flowers, which can be white or pale pink, are often fragrant and attract various pollinators.
