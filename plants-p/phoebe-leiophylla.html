Phoebe leiophylla

Phoebe leiophylla, commonly known as the smooth-leaf phoebe, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the coastal regions of southern California and Baja California, where it thrives in sandy or rocky soils. The plant is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. It produces small, clustered white flowers that are followed by dark purple berries.

Phoebe leiophylla is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to withstand coastal conditions.
