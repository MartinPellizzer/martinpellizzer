Pentas decora

Pentas decora, commonly known as the five-petaled flower or flame flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth surface and a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

The flowers are typically borne in clusters, adding a dramatic visual impact to the landscape.
