Protium widgrenii

Protium widgrenii, commonly known as the African wild teak, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves and small, fragrant yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed, and its bark is often used in traditional medicine.

Botanically, it is notable for its aromatic properties and its role in local ecosystems.
