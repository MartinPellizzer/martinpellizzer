Persica levis

Persica levis, commonly known as the soft peach.

It is a species of fruit tree native to parts of Asia, particularly China. The plant produces small, delicate fruits with a tender flesh and a sweet, aromatic flavor. Its botanical features include simple leaves with a smooth texture and a small, hairy flower cluster.

Persica levis is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its edible fruit.
