Potamogeton praelongus var. elegans

Potamogeton praelongus var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant pondweed, is a submerged aquatic plant native to freshwater environments in North America.

It features long, narrow leaves that are typically dark green and have a smooth, glossy surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually clustered in spike-like structures. It thrives in shallow to moderately deep waters with soft to medium substrates.

Its dense growth helps stabilize sediments and provides habitat for aquatic organisms.
