Poa foucaudii

Poa foucaudii, commonly known as the Foucaud's bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, dark green foliage and compact growth habit, often forming dense mats. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear blue to purple flowers, which are typical of the Poa genus. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is cultivated in some regions for landscaping purposes.
