Parasystasia somalensis

Parasystasia somalensis, commonly known as the Somali parasystasia, is a flowering plant native to the Horn of Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators and serving as a source of medicinal compounds in traditional practices.
