Pereskia quisqueyana, commonly known as the Cuban cactus or the cactus tree, is a species of flowering plant in the Cactaceae family.

The plant has large, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in its arid environment. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer, adding a striking visual element to its appearance.
