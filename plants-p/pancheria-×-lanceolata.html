Pancheria × lanceolata

Pancheria × lanceolata, commonly known as the hybrid pancheria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces showy, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe, which can range in color from white to pink. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its unique foliage and floral display.
