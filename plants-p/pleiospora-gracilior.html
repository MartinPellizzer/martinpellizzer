Pleiospora gracilior

Pleiospora gracilior, commonly known as the slender pleiospora, is a species of fungus belonging to the family Pleosporaceae.

It is characterized by its filamentous growth and produces spores in compact, spherical structures called pycnidia. The plant typically thrives in moist, temperate environments, often found on decaying wood or soil. Its microscopic structure includes hyphae that branch extensively, and the spores are released through specialized openings.

This fungus plays a role in nutrient cycling in forest ecosystems.
