Paronychia chionaea

Paronychia chionaea, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and open shrublands. The plant features basal leaves that are silver-white on the underside, giving it its common name. Its flowers are small and yellow, appearing in early spring.

Paronychia chionaea is characterized by its hairy stems and is often found in disturbed soils.
