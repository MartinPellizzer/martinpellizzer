Piper ovatifolium

Piper ovatifolium, commonly known as the broad-leaf pepper or ovate-leaf pepper, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its ovate-shaped leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that grow in clusters. Its fruit is a small, dark purple berry that contains several seeds.

The plant is often cultivated for its aromatic leaves and potential medicinal properties.
