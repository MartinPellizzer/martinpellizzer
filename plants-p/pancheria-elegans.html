Pancheria elegans

Pancheria elegans, commonly known as the elegant pancheria, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Pancheria elegans is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
