Peperomia schwackei

Peperomia schwackei, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a distinctive silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of green or white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia schwackei is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
