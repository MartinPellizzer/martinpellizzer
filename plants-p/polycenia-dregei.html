Polycenia dregei

Polycenia dregei, commonly known as the dwarf kalahari orchid, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its flowering period occurs during the warmer months, usually from late spring to early autumn.

Polycenia dregei is well adapted to arid environments and is often found in the Kalahari Desert region.
