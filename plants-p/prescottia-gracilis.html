Prescottia gracilis

Prescottia gracilis, commonly known as the slender Prescottia, is a rare and striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of Mexico, where it thrives in cool, humid environments. The plant features delicate, linear leaves and produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered endangered, highlighting the need for conservation efforts.
