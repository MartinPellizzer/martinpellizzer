Peperomia dextrolaeva

Peperomia dextrolaeva, commonly known as the Dextrolaeva Peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features oval, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Peperomia dextrolaeva is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
