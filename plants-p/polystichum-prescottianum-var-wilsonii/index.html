Polystichum Prescottianum Var. Wilsonii

Polystichum prescottianum var. wilsonii is a rare and striking fern species native to the mountainous regions of western North America. Known for its delicate, lace-like fronds, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments with partial shade. Its unique varietal form distinguishes it from other ferns in the Polystichum genus. The plant's ornamental value has made it a sought-after addition to botanical gardens and specialized collections. Due to habitat loss, conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this beautiful and fragile species.
