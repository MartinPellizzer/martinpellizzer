Periandra densiflora

Periandra densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered periandra, is a tropical shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant in warm climates.
