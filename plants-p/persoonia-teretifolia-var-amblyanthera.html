Persoonia teretifolia var. amblyanthera

Persoonia teretifolia var. amblyanthera, commonly known as the narrow-leaved persoonia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is found in coastal regions. It has a compact growth habit and is often used in gardens for its ornamental value.

The species is known for its adaptability to various environmental conditions.
