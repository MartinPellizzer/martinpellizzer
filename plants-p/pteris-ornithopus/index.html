Pteris Ornithopus

Ornithopus fern, scientifically known as Pteris ornithopus, is a unique species of fern that thrives in moist, shaded environments. This plant is characterized by its delicate fronds that display a distinctive feather-like structure, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Native to parts of Asia, it has gained attention for its ability to adapt to various soil types and climates. Its elegant appearance and low maintenance requirements have made it a favored addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces. With its historical use in traditional medicine, the Ornithopus fern continues to captivate botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
