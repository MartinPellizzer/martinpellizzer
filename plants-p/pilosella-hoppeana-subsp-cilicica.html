Pilosella hoppeana subsp. cilicica

Pilosella hoppeana subsp.

cilicica, commonly known as the Cilician hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves.

The plant produces yellowish to white composite flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky slopes and grasslands.

Its subspecific name, *cilicica*, refers to its native range in the Cilician region of Turkey.
