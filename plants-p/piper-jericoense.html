Piper jericoense

Piper jericoense, commonly known as Jericoense pepper or Brazilian pepper, is a flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of tiny, white to greenish flowers. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible and have a spicy, peppery flavor. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in humid, shaded environments.

Piper jericoense is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its use in traditional medicine.
