Piper orbiculatum

Piper orbiculatum, commonly known as the round-leaf pepper or Indian pepper, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, clustered white flowers. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to red as it matures.

Piper orbiculatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in some cultures.
