Pultenaea stenophylla

Pultenaea stenophylla, commonly known as narrow-leaved pultenaea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, pea-like flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in dry, sandy soils in coastal and inland regions.

It is a shrub that can grow up to 1.5 meters tall and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
