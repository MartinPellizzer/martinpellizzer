Prosartema laotica

Prosartema laotica, commonly known as the Laos starflower, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or purple star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered with a fine hairy texture.

The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
