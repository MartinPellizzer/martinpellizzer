Podochilus minahassae

Podochilus minahassae, commonly known as the Minahassa orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Podochilus minahassae is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its role in local ecosystems.
