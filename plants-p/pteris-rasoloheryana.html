Pteris rasoloheryana

Pteris rasoloheryana, commonly known as the Madagascar water fern, is a rare species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, frond-like leaves that are often submerged or floating in water. The plant exhibits a unique morphology with finely divided, linear pinnae that give it a lacy appearance. It thrives in wet, shady environments, typically near streams or in marshy areas.
