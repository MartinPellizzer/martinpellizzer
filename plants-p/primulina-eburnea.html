Primulina eburnea

Primulina eburnea, commonly known as the silver lace begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, silver-green leaves with intricate, lace-like patterns. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its ornamental appearance.

Due to its unique foliage, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in indoor and greenhouse settings.
