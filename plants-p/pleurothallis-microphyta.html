Pleurothallis microphyta

Pleurothallis microphyta, commonly known as the miniature pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its small, compact growth habit and delicate, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts.
