Piper wibomii

Piper wibomii, commonly known as the Wibomii pepper or Wibomii long pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets. Its fruits are small, dark berries that contain aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and as a spice.

Piper wibomii is valued for its unique flavor and potential medicinal properties.
