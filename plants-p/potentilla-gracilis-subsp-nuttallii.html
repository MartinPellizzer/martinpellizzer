Potentilla gracilis subsp. nuttallii

Potentilla gracilis subsp.

nuttallii, commonly known as Nuttall's cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to North America. It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically alternate, with finely divided lobes, and it thrives in well-drained soils.
