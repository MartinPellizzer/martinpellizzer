Panicum vicarium

Panicum vicarium, commonly known as the common panicgrass, is a species of grass native to the eastern United States.

It is a low-growing, perennial plant that typically forms dense mats on the ground. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

Panicum vicarium plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small invertebrates.
