Pelargonium orobifolium

Pelargonium orobifolium, commonly known as the African geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows as a shrub with upright stems and palmate leaves that are often lobed or deeply divided. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Pelargonium orobifolium is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
