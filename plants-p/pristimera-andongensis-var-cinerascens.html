Pristimera andongensis var. cinerascens

Pristimera andongensis var. cinerascens, commonly known as the Andong ash tree, is a variant of the Pristimera genus native to China.

It is characterized by its grayish-brown bark and slender, upright growth habit. The leaves are typically alternate, simple, and ovate, with a smooth margin. This tree thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and traditional medicine.
