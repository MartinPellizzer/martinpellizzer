Polygonatum silvicola

Polygonatum silvicola, commonly known as the Chinese Solomon's Seal, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features upright, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Polygonatum silvicola thrives in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
