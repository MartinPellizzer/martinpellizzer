Paronychia fastigiata var. nuttallii

Paronychia fastigiata var. nuttallii, commonly known as Nuttall's upright paronychia, is a shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, compact foliage. The plant features small, needle-like leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in a tight, mat-like formation. It produces clusters of tiny, white flowers in the spring, which are followed by small, cylindrical fruits.

This variety is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant plant.
