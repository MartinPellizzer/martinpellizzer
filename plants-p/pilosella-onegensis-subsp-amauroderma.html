Pilosella onegensis subsp. amauroderma

Pilosella onegensis subsp.

amauroderma, commonly known as the dark-leaved pilosella, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.
