Pachyanthus ovatus

Pachyanthus ovatus, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces small, round, orange fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Pachyanthus ovatus is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
