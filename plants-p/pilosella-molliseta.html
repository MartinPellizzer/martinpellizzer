Pilosella molliseta

Pilosella molliseta, commonly known as the soft-hairy groundsel, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial species that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features small, daisy-like flowers with white petals and yellow centers, blooming from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance.

Pilosella molliseta thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
