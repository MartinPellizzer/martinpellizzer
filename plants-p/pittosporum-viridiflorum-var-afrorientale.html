Pittosporum viridiflorum var. afrorientale

Pittosporum viridiflorum var. afrorientale, commonly known as the African Pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in spring. These flowers are followed by dark blue, berry-like fruits that are edible for some bird species.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
