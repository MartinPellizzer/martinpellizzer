Phrynium basiflorum var. nobile

Phrynium basiflorum var. nobile, commonly known as the noble arrowleaf, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings, adding visual interest to its appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, pale green to yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and indoor spaces.
