Peperomia izalcoana

Peperomia izalcoana, commonly known as the Izalco peperomia, is a species native to Costa Rica.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often heart-shaped or ovate. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers indirect sunlight, making it a suitable choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage and manageable size make it a popular ornamental plant among foliage enthusiasts.
