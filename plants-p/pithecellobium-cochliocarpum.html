Pithecellobium cochliocarpum

Pithecellobium cochliocarpum, commonly known as the yellow cluster-fruit.

It is a small, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of the Americas. The plant features compound leaves with elliptical leaflets and produces small, yellow, clustered flowers. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
