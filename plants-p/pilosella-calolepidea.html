Pilosella calolepidea

Pilosella calolepidea, commonly known as the woolly leaf fleabane, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, silver-green foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing on rocky soils.
