Pyrethrum pallidum

Pyrethrum pallidum, commonly known as the pale pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white to pale yellow petals. The plant produces small, spherical seed heads that contain numerous tiny seeds. Pyrethrum pallidum is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.

Its flowers also contain natural compounds that have insecticidal properties.
