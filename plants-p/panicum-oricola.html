Panicum oricola

Panicum oricola, commonly known as purple lovegrass, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, fluffy seed heads. The plant produces small, purple or pink flowers that are arranged in panicles, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and disturbed areas.

Panicum oricola is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone regions.
