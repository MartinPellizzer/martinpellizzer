Ponerorchis kurokamiana

Ponerorchis kurokamiana, commonly known as the Kurokamia orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its complex inflorescence and intricate floral structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to flourish.

Due to its rarity and unique beauty, it is highly valued in botanical collections and conservation efforts.
