Poa leibergii

Poa leibergii, commonly known as Leiberg's bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of North America.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted blades and a dense, mat-like growth habit. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine environments. Its foliage is usually a vibrant blue-green color, which contributes to its ornamental appeal.

Poa leibergii is valued for its drought tolerance and ability to stabilize soil in rocky or steep terrain.
