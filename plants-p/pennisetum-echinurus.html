Pennisetum echinurus

Pennisetum echinurus, commonly known as purple lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, tufted inflorescences that produce fluffy, purple-pink plumes. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color that contrasts with the vibrant flower heads.

Pennisetum echinurus is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.
