Polyspora balansae

Polyspora balansae, commonly known as the Balansae polypody, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically divided into small, lance-shaped segments. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its spore-producing structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Polyspora balansae is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to adapt to various soil types.
