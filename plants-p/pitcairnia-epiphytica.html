Pitcairnia epiphytica

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and vibrant, showy flowers. Pitcairnia epiphytica is well adapted to grow on trees or rocks, absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.
