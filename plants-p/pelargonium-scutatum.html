Pelargonium scutatum

Pelargonium scutatum, commonly known as the scutate geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features upright stems with opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and prolonged blooming period.
