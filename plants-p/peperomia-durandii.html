Peperomia durandii

Peperomia durandii, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features dense, dark green, velvety leaves that are oval to lance-shaped and often have a slightly wavy margin. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that are clustered in spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia durandii is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and moderate humidity to flourish.
