Poa kyongsongensis

Poa kyongsongensis, commonly known as the Kyongsong grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to South Korea and is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and slender blades. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers.

Poa kyongsongensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological significance in its native habitat.
