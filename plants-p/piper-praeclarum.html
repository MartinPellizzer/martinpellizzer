Piper praeclarum

Piper praeclarum, commonly known as the elegant pepper plant, is a climbing vine native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when ripe, and these berries contain the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and cuisine. Its leaves are often used in herbal remedies due to their medicinal properties.

The plant thrives in humid, warm climates and requires support for its climbing growth habit.
