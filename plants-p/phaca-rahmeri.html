Phaca rahmeri

Phaca rahmeri, commonly known as the desert phaca, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers during the rainy season. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to store water in its arid environment.

Phaca rahmeri is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and shrublands.
