Platanthera × andrewsii

Platanthera × andrewsii, commonly known as the Andrews' twayblade, is a hybrid orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is a herbaceous plant with a slender, leafless stem that typically grows to a height of about 15–30 centimeters. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its roots are mycorrhizal, forming symbiotic relationships with fungi to enhance nutrient uptake.

This species is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and wet meadows.
