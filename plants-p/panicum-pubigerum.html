Panicum pubigerum

Panicum pubigerum, commonly known as silver panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and silver-gray foliage due to the presence of fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous narrow, awned florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.

Silver panicgrass is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in erosion control due to its hardy nature.
