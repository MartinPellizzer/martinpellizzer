Polyalthia insignis

Polyalthia insignis, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a fiery appearance. The tree has compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Polyalthia insignis is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
