Plantago media subsp. media

Plantago media subsp.

media, commonly known as broadleaf plantain, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia. It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically ribbed and have a rough texture.

The plant produces small, greenish flowers in a spike-like inflorescence, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and along stream banks.

This species is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally for its healing benefits.
