Psilochilus macrophyllus var. brenesii

Psilochilus macrophyllus var. brenesii, commonly known as the Brenesii Psilochilus, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces striking inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
