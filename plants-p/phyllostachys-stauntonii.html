Phyllostachys stauntonii

Phyllostachys stauntonii, commonly known as the Staunton bamboo, is a species of grass in the bamboo family, Poaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, linear leaves. This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its elegant appearance and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. It is a clump-forming bamboo that grows rapidly, making it suitable for landscaping and erosion control.

The plant is also valued for its use in traditional Chinese medicine and as a source of sustainable materials.
