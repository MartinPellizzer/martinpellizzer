Premna depauperata var. serrulata

Premna depauperata var. serrulata, commonly known as the serrated premna, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, ovate leaves with serrated margins, which give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers, followed by small, berry-like fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
