Phaseolus zebra var. amethystinus

Phaseolus zebra var. amethystinus, commonly known as the amethystine zebra bean, is a unique variety of the zebra bean plant.

It is characterized by its striking, mottled purple and green markings on the pods and leaves. This variety is native to South America and thrives in warm, tropical climates. The plant produces small, edible beans that are often used in traditional cuisines.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both gardens and botanical collections.
