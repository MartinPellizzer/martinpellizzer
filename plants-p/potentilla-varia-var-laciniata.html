Potentilla varia var. laciniata

Potentilla varia var. laciniata, commonly known as the cutleaf potentilla, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features compound leaves that are deeply lobed, giving it a distinctive, feathery appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
