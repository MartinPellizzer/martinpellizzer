Platanthera × kelleyi

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forest floors or wet meadows, where it thrives in nutrient-poor soils. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, Platanthera × kelleyi is considered endangered and is protected in many areas.
