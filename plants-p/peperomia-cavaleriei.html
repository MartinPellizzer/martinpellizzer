Peperomia cavaleriei

Peperomia cavaleriei, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, arranged in clusters at the base of the leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
