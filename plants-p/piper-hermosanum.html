Piper hermosanum

Piper hermosanum, commonly known as the beautiful pepper plant, is a flowering vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes over 3,000 species of flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into elongated, peppery fruits.

The fruits are often used in traditional medicine and as a spice in local cuisines.
