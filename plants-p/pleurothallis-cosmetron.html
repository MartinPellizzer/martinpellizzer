Pleurothallis cosmetron

Pleurothallis cosmetron, commonly known as the beauty orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant typically produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a distinct fragrance. It thrives in high humidity and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements have made it a favored plant among orchid enthusiasts.
