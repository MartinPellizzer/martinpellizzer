Piper politii subsp. politii

Piper politii subsp.

politii, commonly known as the Bornean pepper vine, is a climbing plant native to the rainforests of Borneo. It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants.

The plant is characterized by its slender, woody stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by spherical, three-lobed berries.

The subspecies is notable for its potential medicinal properties and is often found in humid, shaded understory environments.
