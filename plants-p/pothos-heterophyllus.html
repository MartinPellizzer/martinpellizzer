Pothos heterophyllus

Pothos heterophyllus, commonly known as marble queen, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive variegated leaves, which feature a mix of green, white, and cream patterns. The plant is a trailing vine that can be grown in hanging baskets or trained on supports. Its leaves are heart-shaped and can grow up to 10 inches in length.

Pothos heterophyllus is known for its adaptability to low-light conditions and is a popular houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
