Piper scutispicum

Piper scutispicum, commonly known as the African pepper or mountain pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are followed by three-winged berries that are used in traditional medicine.

The plant is valued for its aromatic leaves and fruits, which have various applications in local cultures.
