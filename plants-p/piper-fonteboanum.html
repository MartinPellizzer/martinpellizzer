Piper fonteboanum

Piper fonteboanum, commonly known as the Fonteboanum pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, heart-shaped leaves. It produces tiny, green berries that turn red when mature, which are used in traditional medicine.

The plant is valued for its aromatic leaves and potential medicinal properties.
