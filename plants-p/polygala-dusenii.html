Polygala dusenii

Polygala dusenii, commonly known as the Dusen's milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its leaves are often narrow and slightly hairy, while the flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Polygala dusenii is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
