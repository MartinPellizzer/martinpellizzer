Peperomia basellifolia

Peperomia basellifolia, commonly known as the velvetleaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that are borne on short, erect spikes. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia basellifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and air-purifying qualities.
