Piper rugosum

Piper rugosum, commonly known as the roughleaf pepper or wild pepper, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is often found in moist, shaded environments. Its small, greenish-white flowers are followed by small, berry-like fruits that contain the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine.

Piper rugosum is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in various herbal remedies for its potential health benefits.
