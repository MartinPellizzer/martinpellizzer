Pleurothallis nemorosa

Pleurothallis nemorosa, commonly known as the forest orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is relatively challenging to grow outside its native environment.
