Phacelurus Latifolius F. Angustifolius

Phacelurus latifolius f. angustifolius is a rare and unique variant of the common phacelurus plant. This foliage form is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves that distinguish it from the broader leaves of the standard variety. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories across its native range. The plant's distinctive leaf shape makes it a sought-after specimen among horticultural enthusiasts. Its beauty and rarity have sparked interest in both botanical studies and garden cultivation.
