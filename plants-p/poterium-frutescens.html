Poterium frutescens

Poterium frutescens, commonly known as the African sage, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, pale blue to purple flowers. The plant has hairy stems and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is part of the Lamiaceae family, which includes other aromatic herbs.

Poterium frutescens is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
