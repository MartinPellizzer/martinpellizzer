Pelexia funckiana var. olivacea

Pelexia funckiana var. olivacea, commonly known as the olive-green pelexia, is a rare and distinctive plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage, which features a unique combination of glossy dark green leaves with a subtle olive hue. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a valuable species for botanical study and conservation efforts.
