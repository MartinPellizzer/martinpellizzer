Paederota villosula

Paederota villosula, commonly known as the hairy blister beetle, is a species of beetle rather than a plant, so it does not have botanical aspects in the traditional sense.

However, it is often associated with plants as it feeds on the leaves of various species, including those in the nightshade family. Its larvae are also known to feed on plant material, contributing to its ecological role in certain habitats. The beetle's presence can sometimes indicate the health of its host plants.

Due to its chemical defenses, it is often avoided by predators, which influences its interactions within the ecosystem.
