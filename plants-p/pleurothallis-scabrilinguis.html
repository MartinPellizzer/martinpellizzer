Pleurothallis scabrilinguis

Pleurothallis scabrilinguis, commonly known as the rough-lingued pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, linear leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. Its inflorescence is usually clustered, with flowers that have a unique, intricate structure typical of the orchid family.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and specialized species within its native range.
