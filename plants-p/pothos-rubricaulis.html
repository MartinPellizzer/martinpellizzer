Pothos rubricaulis

Pothos rubricaulis, commonly known as the red-stemmed pothos, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a reddish tint along the veins. The plant's stems are distinctly reddish, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular houseplant.

Pothos rubricaulis is known for its adaptability and ability to purify indoor air.
