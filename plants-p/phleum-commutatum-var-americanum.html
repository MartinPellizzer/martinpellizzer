Phleum commutatum var. americanum

Phleum commutatum var. americanum, commonly known as American oat, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, blue-green leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with florets that are typically green to brown in color. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

This species is often used in restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support wildlife.
