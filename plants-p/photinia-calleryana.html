Photinia calleryana

Photinia calleryana, commonly known as the Chinese photinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its attractive, glossy, dark green leaves that turn bright red in the fall. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is adaptable to a variety of soil types.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
