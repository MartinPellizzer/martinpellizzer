Peperomia alatiscapa

Peperomia alatiscapa, commonly known as the broad-leaved peperomia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub characterized by its glossy, broad, ovate leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color, arranged in spikes along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia alatiscapa is valued for its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
