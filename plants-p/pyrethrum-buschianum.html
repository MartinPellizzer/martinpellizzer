Pyrethrum buschianum

Pyrethrum buschianum, commonly known as the African pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with bright yellow petals. The plant produces small, elongated seed pods that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open habitats.

Pyrethrum buschianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has mild pesticidal properties.
