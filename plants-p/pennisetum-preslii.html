Pennisetum preslii

Pennisetum preslii, commonly known as the purple fountain grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to southern Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that produce feathery, purple-pink plumes during the summer months. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often a deep green color, providing a contrast to the vibrant flower heads. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Pennisetum preslii is known for its drought tolerance and ability to add visual interest to the garden throughout the growing season.
