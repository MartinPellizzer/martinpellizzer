Petesia repens

Petesia repens, commonly known as creeping petesia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a low-growing herbaceous plant that spreads along the ground, forming dense mats. The plant features small, showy flowers that are typically violet or blue in color, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Petesia repens is often used in traditional medicine and is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
