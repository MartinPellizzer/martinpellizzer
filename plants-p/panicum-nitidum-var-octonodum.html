Panicum nitidum var. octonodum

Panicum nitidum var. octonodum, commonly known as the eight-node panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and dense, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a bluish-green coloration.

The variety 'octonodum' is distinguished by its eight nodes along the stem, a feature that aids in its identification.
