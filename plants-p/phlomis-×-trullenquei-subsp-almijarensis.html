Phlomis × trullenquei subsp. almijarensis

Phlomis × trullenquei subsp.

almijarensis, commonly known as the Almijarén phlomis, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and is native to the Iberian Peninsula.

The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a rough, hairy texture and produces bright yellow flowers in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

This subspecies is noted for its ornamental value and is cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
