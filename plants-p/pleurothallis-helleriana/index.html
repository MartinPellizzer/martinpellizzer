Pleurothallis Helleriana

Pleurothallis helleriana is a rare and delicate orchid native to the cloud forests of Central America. This small, epiphytic plant thrives in humid, shaded environments where it clings to tree trunks and branches. Its flowers, often pale green or white, are subtle yet elegant, making it a favorite among orchid enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it is highly sensitive to environmental changes, making conservation efforts crucial for its survival. This plant exemplifies the biodiversity and fragility of tropical ecosystems.
