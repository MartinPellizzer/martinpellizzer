Polygonum newberryi

Polygonum newberryi, commonly known as Newberry's knotweed, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants commonly referred to as buckwheats and smartweeds. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit, with slender stems and small, clustered flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Polygonum newberryi is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and streambanks.
