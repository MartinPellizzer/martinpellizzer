Prosaptia deltoideophylla

Prosaptia deltoideophylla, commonly known as the delta-leaf prosaptia, is a rare and unique plant species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive deltoid-shaped leaves, which are broad and triangular in form, often with a waxy surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, adding to its ornamental appeal. Its growth habit is generally shrub-like, with multiple stems arising from the base.
