Polygonatum ginfoshanicum

Polygonatum ginfoshanicum, commonly known as the Chinese Solomon's seal, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value and traditional medicinal uses make it a notable species in horticulture and herbal medicine.
