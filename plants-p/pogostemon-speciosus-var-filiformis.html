Pogostemon speciosus var. filiformis

Pogostemon speciosus var. filiformis, commonly known as the slender pogostemon, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, thread-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a delicate appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
