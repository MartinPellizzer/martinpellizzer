Premna odorata

Premna odorata, commonly known as the sweet woodruff, is a flowering plant native to Asia and parts of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters tall, featuring opposite, ovate leaves with a fragrant, slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the spring. Its root system is fibrous, and it is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth.

The leaves and flowers are used in traditional medicine and as a flavoring agent in food and beverages.
