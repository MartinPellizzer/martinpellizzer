Patrinia dielsii var. shensiensis

Patrinia dielsii var. shensiensis, commonly known as the Sichuan Patrinia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, arranged in whorled clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shady areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
