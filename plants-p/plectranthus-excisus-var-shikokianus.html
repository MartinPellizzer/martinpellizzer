Plectranthus excisus var. shikokianus

Plectranthus excisus var. shikokianus, commonly known as the Shikokian plectranthus, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often grayish-green in color, with a slightly fuzzy texture. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of violet or purple, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to tolerate light pruning, allowing for shaping into various forms.
