Polygala nitida

Polygala nitida, commonly known as the slender milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Polygala nitida is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
