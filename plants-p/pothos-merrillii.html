Pothos merrillii

Pothos merrillii, commonly known as the silver pothos, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically variegated with silver and green patterns. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its stems are equipped with adventitious roots that help it climb and anchor to surfaces.

Pothos merrillii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
