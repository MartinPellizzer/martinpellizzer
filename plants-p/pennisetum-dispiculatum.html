Pennisetum dispiculatum

Pennisetum dispiculatum, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that produce fluffy, purple-pink plumes during the flowering season. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Its unique appearance and easy maintenance make it a favored choice for landscape design.
