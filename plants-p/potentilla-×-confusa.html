Potentilla × confusa

Potentilla × confusa, commonly known as Shrub Cinquefoil, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features dense, upright growth with multiple stems. The plant has small, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to yellow, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, oval-shaped, and have a rough texture with serrated edges.

Potentilla × confusa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant due to its hardiness and adaptability.
