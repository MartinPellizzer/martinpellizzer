Phyllanthus caribaeus

Phyllanthus caribaeus, commonly known as the Caribbean phyllanthus, is a shrub native to the Caribbean region.

It typically grows in coastal areas and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a smooth surface. The plant produces tiny yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, spherical fruits. It is often found in sandy soils and can tolerate saline conditions.

Phyllanthus caribaeus is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional Caribbean herbal remedies.
