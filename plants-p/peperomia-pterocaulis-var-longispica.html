Peperomia pterocaulis var. longispica

Peperomia pterocaulis var. longispica, commonly known as the long-spined peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features succulent, ovate leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings, providing a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it adaptable to a variety of indoor environments.
