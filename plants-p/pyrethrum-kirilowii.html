Pyrethrum kirilowii

Pyrethrum kirilowii, commonly known as the Korean pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with bright yellow petals. The plant is notable for containing pyrethrins, natural insecticides used in organic pest control. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soils.

Pyrethrum kirilowii is often cultivated for both its ornamental value and its practical use in agriculture.
