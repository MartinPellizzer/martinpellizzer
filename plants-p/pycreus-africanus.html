Pycreus africanus

Pycreus africanus, commonly known as the African pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are clustered in flat-topped inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun exposure.

Pycreus africanus is also noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
