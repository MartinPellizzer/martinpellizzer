Peperomia rata

Peperomia rata, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a silvery or white underside, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which are often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia rata is popular as a houseplant due to its ability to adapt well to indoor environments.
