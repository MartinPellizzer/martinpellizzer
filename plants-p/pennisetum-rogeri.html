Pennisetum rogeri

Pennisetum rogeri, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender stalks with tufts of soft, feathery plumes that range in color from silver to deep purple. The plant is characterized by its clumping growth habit and is often used in ornamental landscaping for its striking appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for drought-tolerant gardens.

Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with the inflorescence being the most visually prominent feature.
