Paeonia peregrina var. compacta

Paeonia peregrina var. compacta, commonly known as the compact peony, is a unique variant of the wild peony species.

It is characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically features dark green, heart-shaped leaves and produces fragrant, pink to red blooms in late spring. It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and thrives in well-drained soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in temperate climates.
