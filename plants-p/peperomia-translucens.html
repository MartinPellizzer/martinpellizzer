Peperomia translucens

Peperomia translucens, commonly known as the glassy peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features fleshy, dark green leaves with a distinctive waxy, translucent appearance on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of red or purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique leaf texture and ease of care make it a popular choice for succulent and foliage enthusiasts.
