Panicum nubigenum

Panicum nubigenum, commonly known as cloud grass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and fluffy, feathery seed heads that resemble clouds, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or pale in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental appearance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique visual appeal.
