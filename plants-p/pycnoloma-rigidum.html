Pycnoloma rigidum

Pycnoloma rigidum, commonly known as the rigid pycnoloma, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is a shrub-like plant characterized by its dense, rigid foliage and small, tubular flowers. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in humid, well-drained soils. The plant has a compact growth habit with thick, leathery leaves that help reduce water loss.

Its flowers, often in shades of red or purple, are adapted for pollination by insects.
