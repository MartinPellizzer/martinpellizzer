Pleurothallis avenacea

Pleurothallis avenacea, commonly known as the golden pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, which bloom during the wet season. Its leaves are usually narrow and elongated, adapted for efficient water absorption in its natural habitat.

Pleurothallis avenacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
