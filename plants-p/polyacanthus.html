Polyacanthus

Polyacanthus, commonly known as the flame violet, is a flowering plant belonging to the Boraginaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often used in ornamental gardening.

Polyacanthus thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
