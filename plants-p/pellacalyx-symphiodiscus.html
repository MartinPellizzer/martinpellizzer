Pellacalyx symphiodiscus

Pellacalyx symphiodiscus, commonly known as the golden star hibiscus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with a distinctive star-shaped pattern. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Pellacalyx symphiodiscus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
