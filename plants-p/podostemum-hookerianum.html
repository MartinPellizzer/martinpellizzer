Podostemum hookerianum

Podostemum hookerianum, commonly known as the water buttercup, is a unique aquatic plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers and its adaptation to growing in fast-flowing water environments. The plant has specialized roots that anchor it to rocks and provide stability in strong currents. Its leaves are typically submerged, while its flowers emerge above the water surface.

Podostemum hookerianum plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
