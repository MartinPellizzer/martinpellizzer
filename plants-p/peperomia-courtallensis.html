Peperomia courtallensis

Peperomia courtallensis, commonly known as the Ceylon peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Sri Lanka.

It features oval, slightly hairy, dark green leaves that are arranged alternately along its stems. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of pink or white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia courtallensis is valued for its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
