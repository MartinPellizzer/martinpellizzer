Polygonum bistorta var. oblongifolium

Polygonum bistorta var. oblongifolium, commonly known as broadleaf bistort, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features upright stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters tall, covered with reddish hairs. The leaves are broad, heart-shaped, and have a wavy margin, often with a reddish or purple tint. It produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers in late summer, which are followed by distinctive, three-lobed seed capsules.

This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
