Peperomia amphitricha

Peperomia amphitricha, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a low-growing perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant typically produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia amphitricha is relatively easy to care for and is appreciated for its compact size and unique foliage.
