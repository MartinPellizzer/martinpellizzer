Philodendron pastazanum

Philodendron pastazanum, commonly known as the Pastaza philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to the Amazon Basin.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly variegated appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by elongated, cylindrical fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
