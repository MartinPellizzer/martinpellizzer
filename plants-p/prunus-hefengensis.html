Prunus hefengensis

Prunus hefengensis, commonly known as the Hefeng cherry, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its small, sweet fruits and ornamental flowers. The plant typically grows as a deciduous shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are elliptical with a smooth margin, and it produces pink to white blossoms in the spring.

Prunus hefengensis is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
