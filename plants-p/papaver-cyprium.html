Papaver cyprium

Papaver cyprium, commonly known as the Cyprus poppy, is a flowering plant native to the island of Cyprus.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange petals that surround a central cluster of stamens. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, dark seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

The Cyprus poppy is valued for its ornamental beauty and has cultural significance in the region.
