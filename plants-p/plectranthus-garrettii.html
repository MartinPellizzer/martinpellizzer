Plectranthus garrettii

Plectranthus garrettii, commonly known as the Cape rosemary, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring aromatic, gray-green leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming throughout the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for Mediterranean climates.

Its ornamental value and fragrant foliage make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
