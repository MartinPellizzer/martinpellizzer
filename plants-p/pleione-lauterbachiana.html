Pleione lauterbachiana

Pleione lauterbachiana, commonly known as the Lauterbach's orchid, is a rare and striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in China, India, and Nepal. The plant is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in cool, shaded environments and requires well-drained, humus-rich soil.

Due to its rarity and beauty, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
