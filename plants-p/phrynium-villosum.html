Phrynium villosum

Phrynium villosum, commonly known as the hairy phrynium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that are often covered in fine, hairy textures. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its striking, arrow-shaped leaves are a distinguishing feature, often with a vibrant green color.

Phrynium villosum is also noted for its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments.
