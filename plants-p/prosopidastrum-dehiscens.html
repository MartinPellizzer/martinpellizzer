Prosopidastrum dehiscens

Prosopidastrum dehiscens, commonly known as the African star grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and along riverbanks.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is cultivated in some regions for landscaping purposes.
