Philodendron hughclarkei

Philodendron hughclarkei, commonly known as the Emerald Queen, is a striking climbing plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant has a unique ability to climb using aerial roots, which it uses to attach itself to trees and other structures. Its foliage is often a deep emerald green, which gives it its common name.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
