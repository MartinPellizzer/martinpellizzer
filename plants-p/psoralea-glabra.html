Psoralea glabra

Psoralea glabra, commonly known as the smooth psoralea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom from late winter to early spring. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often covered with fine hairs.

Psoralea glabra is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in such conditions.
