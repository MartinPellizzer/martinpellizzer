Persea ayui-y

Persea ayui-y, commonly known as the Ayui-y avocado, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central America.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and fragrant flowers. The tree produces small, round fruits with a thick, smooth skin and a creamy, nutty flesh. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and edible fruit, which is used in both culinary and traditional medicine applications.
