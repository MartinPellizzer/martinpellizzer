Polypodium skutchii

Polypodium skutchii, commonly known as the Skutch's shield fern, is a species of fern native to Central America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its fronds are leathery and have a distinctive, shield-like shape, which gives it its common name.

Polypodium skutchii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
