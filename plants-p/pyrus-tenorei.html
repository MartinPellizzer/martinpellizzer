Pyrus tenorei

Pyrus tenorei, commonly known as the Sicilian pear, is a rare species native to the island of Sicily.

It is a deciduous tree belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its small, ornamental leaves and clusters of white flowers. The plant produces small, edible pears that are typically harvested in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers a sunny to partially shaded location.
