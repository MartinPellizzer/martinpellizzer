Psiadia dracaenifolia

Psiadia dracaenifolia, commonly known as the laceleaf plant, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its narrow, twisted leaves that resemble those of a dracaena. The plant features a branching structure with dense foliage, making it an attractive ornamental plant. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Psiadia dracaenifolia is valued for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
