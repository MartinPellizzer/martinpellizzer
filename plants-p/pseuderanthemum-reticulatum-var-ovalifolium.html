Pseuderanthemum reticulatum var. ovalifolium

Pseuderanthemum reticulatum var. ovalifolium, commonly known as the oval-leaved false anthemum, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, ovate leaves that are often dark green in color. The plant produces small, showy flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white and pink. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant.

Its foliage and floral display make it a popular choice for shaded garden areas and indoor cultivation.
