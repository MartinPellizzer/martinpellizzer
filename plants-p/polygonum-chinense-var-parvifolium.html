Polygonum chinense var. parvifolium

Polygonum chinense var. parvifolium, commonly known as the small-leaf smartweed, is a herbaceous annual plant native to Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that are often reddish in color, and its leaves are alternate, ovate to elliptic in shape with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The small, clustered flowers are typically pink or red, appearing in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in wetlands, ditches, and along stream banks.

This species is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
