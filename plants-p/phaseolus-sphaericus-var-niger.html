Phaseolus sphaericus var. niger

Phaseolus sphaericus var. niger, commonly known as black bean gourds, is a variant of the genus Phaseolus native to the Americas.

This plant is characterized by its dark, spherical fruits that are typically harvested when fully mature. The leaves are heart-shaped and compound, with a rough texture. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The plant is often cultivated for its edible fruits, which have a mild, nutty flavor.
