Pyrostria neriifolia

Pyrostria neriifolia, commonly known as the black-leafed fern, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, glossy leaves that are often deeply divided, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by distinctive, elongated fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and botanical collections.
