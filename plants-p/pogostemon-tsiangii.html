Pogostemon tsiangii

Pogostemon tsiangii, commonly known as the Chinese betony, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and clusters of small, pale blue or purple flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas.

Pogostemon tsiangii is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
