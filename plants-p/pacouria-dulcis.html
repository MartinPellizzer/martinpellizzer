Pacouria dulcis

Pacouria dulcis, commonly known as the sweet-scented orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

The orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated for its unique fragrance and visual beauty.
