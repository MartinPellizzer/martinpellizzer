Plexipus latifolius var. glabrescens

Plexipus latifolius var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-leafed broadleaf plexipus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to temperate regions.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically smooth and glabrous, giving it its varietal name. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its foliage and flowers are often used in traditional herbal medicine for their purported health benefits.
