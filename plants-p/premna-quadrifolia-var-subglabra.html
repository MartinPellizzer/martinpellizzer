Premna quadrifolia var. subglabra

Premna quadrifolia var. subglabra, commonly known as the smoothleaf Chinese nettle, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It is a shrub characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are usually smooth or softly hairy. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are borne on slender pedicels. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

The species is noted for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
