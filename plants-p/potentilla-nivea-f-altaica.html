Potentilla nivea f. altaica

Potentilla nivea f. altaica, commonly known as the alpine cinquefoil, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit and is typically found in alpine and subalpine regions. The plant features white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, often in clusters of five. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with small, oval-shaped leaflets.

This variety is adapted to cold climates and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils at high elevations.
