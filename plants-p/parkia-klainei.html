Parkia klainei

Parkia klainei, commonly known as the African sycamore, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The tree produces long, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Its role in local ecosystems includes providing shade and supporting various wildlife species.
