Pronephrium megacuspe

Pronephrium megacuspe, commonly known as the giant kidney fern, is a species of fern characterized by its large, frond-like leaves.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant exhibits a rhizomatous growth habit, with long, creeping underground stems that produce aerial roots. Its leaves are typically bipinnate, with a distinctive lobed structure that enhances its ornamental value.
