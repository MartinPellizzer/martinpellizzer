Persica vulgaris var. tomentosa

Persica vulgaris var. tomentosa, commonly known as the fuzzy peach.

This variant is characterized by its dense, fuzzy pubescence covering the stems, leaves, and fruit. The leaves are ovate with a serrated margin and a velvety texture. The flowers are typically pink or white, with five petals and a prominent central stamen.

The fruit is a fuzzy peach, usually smaller than the common peach, with a sweet, juicy flesh and a thin, hairy skin.
