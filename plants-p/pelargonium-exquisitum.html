Pelargonium exquisitum

Pelargonium exquisitum, commonly known as the exquisite geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows as an upright herb with palmate leaves that are deeply lobed. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its beauty and ability to attract pollinators.
