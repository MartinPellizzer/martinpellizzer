Polyachyrus nesites

Polyachyrus nesites, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically features velvety, dark green leaves and produces flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Polyachyrus nesites is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
