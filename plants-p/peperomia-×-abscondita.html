Peperomia × abscondita

Peperomia × abscondita, commonly known as the hidden peperomia, is a hybrid plant native to South America.

It is a compact, evergreen perennial with dense, fleshy foliage that comes in various shades of green, often with reddish or purple tones. The plant features small, clustered inflorescences that produce tiny, inconspicuous flowers, typical of the Piperaceae family. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ornamental value lies in its unique leaf patterns and low maintenance requirements.
