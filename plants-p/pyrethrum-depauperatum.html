Pyrethrum depauperatum

Pyrethrum depauperatum, commonly known as the slender pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

Pyrethrum depauperatum is known for its potential use in natural pest control due to the presence of pyrethrins in its flowers.
