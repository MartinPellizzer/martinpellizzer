Polystachya excelsa

Polystachya excelsa, commonly known as the giant flame orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to the wet forests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy inflorescence that bears numerous bright orange to red flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil. Its leaves are typically elongated and strap-like, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Polystachya excelsa is valued in horticulture for its vibrant color and unique floral structure.
