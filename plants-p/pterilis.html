Pterilis

Pterilis, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow daisy-like flowers and hairy, lobed leaves. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. The plant is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant appearance.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and attractive addition to gardens and natural landscapes.
