Primulidium sinense

Primulidium sinense, commonly known as the Chinese primrose, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that come in a range of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
