Paeonia parnassica

The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is adapted to high-altitude conditions, often growing in rocky or mountainous terrain. Its leaves are deeply lobed and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stems are sturdy and often bear a fuzzy coating.
