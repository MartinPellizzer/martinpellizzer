Piper tenuirameum

Piper tenuirameum, commonly known as the slender-leaved pepper vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes numerous species of pepper plants. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into elongated, reddish berries. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforest understories.
