Phyllagathis chongzuoensis

Phyllagathis chongzuoensis, commonly known as the Chongzuo phyllagathis, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region. This shrub typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical and subtropical forests. Its leaves are usually elliptical, with a smooth surface and a glossy appearance.
