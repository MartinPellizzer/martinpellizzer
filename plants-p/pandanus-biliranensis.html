Pandanus biliranensis

Pandanus biliranensis, commonly known as the Biliran pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pandanaceae.

It is native to the Philippines, particularly found in the islands of Biliran and Leyte. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. It produces small, clustered inflorescences with fragrant, yellowish flowers.

The plant thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found growing in coastal or lowland areas.
