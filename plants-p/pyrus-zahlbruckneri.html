Pyrus zahlbruckneri

Pyrus zahlbruckneri, commonly known as the Chinese white pear, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its white to pale yellow flowers and smooth, dark grey bark. The tree produces small, edible fruits that are similar to pears but are less commonly cultivated.

It thrives in temperate climates and is often grown for its ornamental value as well as its fruit.
