Pelargonium × mundulum

Pelargonium × mundulum, commonly known as the hybrid geranium, is a flowering plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Pelargonium hortorum and Pelargonium × odoratum. The plant is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are typically rounded with a slightly serrated edge, and the plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Pelargonium × mundulum thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.
