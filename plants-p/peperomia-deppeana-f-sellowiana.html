Peperomia deppeana f. sellowiana

Peperomia deppeana f. sellowiana, commonly known as the Sellow's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and red, which are followed by delicate, fleshy berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ornamental foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a favored plant among collectors and enthusiasts.
