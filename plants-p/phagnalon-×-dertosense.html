Phagnalon × dertosense

Phagnalon × dertosense, commonly known as the hybrid meadow grass.

It is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its fibrous root system and tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the family. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.

Its botanical features make it an important component of diverse grassland ecosystems.
