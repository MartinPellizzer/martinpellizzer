Pelargonium × francisii

Pelargonium × francisii, commonly known as the Cape geranium, is a hybrid shrub native to South Africa.

It features upright stems with opposite, ovate leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, which bloom throughout the growing season. It is well-suited for container gardening and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both garden beds and hanging baskets.
