Piper sphaerocarpum var. tenuifolium

Piper sphaerocarpum var. tenuifolium, commonly known as the slender-leafed pepper vine, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves that are typically less than 5 cm in width. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruits are small, spherical berries that turn from green to dark purple as they mature.

This variant is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and is often found in humid, lowland forests.
