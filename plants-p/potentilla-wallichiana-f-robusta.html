Potentilla wallichiana f. robusta

Potentilla wallichiana f. robusta, commonly known as the robust potentilla, is a shrub native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, showy yellow flowers. The plant features holly-like leaves that are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its bright blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

This cultivar is valued for its hardiness and ornamental appeal in garden settings.
