Prangos abieticola

Prangos abieticola, commonly known as the alpine thistle, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Central Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and robust, hairy stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cool, sunny conditions. Prangos abieticola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and visual interest.
