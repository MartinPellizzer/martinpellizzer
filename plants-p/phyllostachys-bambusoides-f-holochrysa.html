Phyllostachys bambusoides f. holochrysa

Phyllostachys bambusoides f. holochrysa, commonly known as the golden bamboo, is a varietal form of the silver bamboo, characterized by its striking golden-yellow culms.

This ornamental bamboo is native to eastern China and is prized for its aesthetic appeal in landscaping. The plant features slender, upright stems that are often striped with contrasting green and yellow hues. It grows in clumps and prefers well-drained soil with partial to full sunlight.

Its rapid growth rate makes it a popular choice for privacy screens and garden accents.
