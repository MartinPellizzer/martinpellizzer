Polypodium remote-frondigerum

Polypodium remote-frondigerum, commonly known as the remote fronded polypody, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, pinnate fronds that are typically light green and have a leathery texture. This fern thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizomes are wiry and can form dense colonies, contributing to its ability to spread vegetatively.

The plant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
