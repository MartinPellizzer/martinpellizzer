Peperomia delicatula

Peperomia delicatula, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features small, waxy, silver-gray leaves that are often described as resembling the texture of velvet. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia delicatula is prized for its ornamental foliage and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
