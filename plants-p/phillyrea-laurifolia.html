Phillyrea laurifolia

Phillyrea laurifolia, commonly known as the evergreen laurel or Turkish laurel, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It features glossy, dark green, oval-shaped leaves that remain throughout the year, giving it a lush appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the autumn. Phillyrea laurifolia is known for its dense growth habit and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
