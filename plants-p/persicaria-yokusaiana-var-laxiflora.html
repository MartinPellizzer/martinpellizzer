Persicaria yokusaiana var. laxiflora

Persicaria yokusaiana var. laxiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface and produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers are arranged in dense, cylindrical spikes, and it has a fibrous root system.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in wetland habitats.
