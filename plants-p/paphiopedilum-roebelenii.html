Paphiopedilum roebelenii

Paphiopedilum roebelenii, commonly known as the yellow lady's slipper, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a robust, hairy rhizome and produces showy, yellow flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments with well-drained soil. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, often with a waxy coating.

It is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
