Pseudocopaiva hymenaeifolia

Pseudocopaiva hymenaeifolia, commonly known as the Brazilian starfruit tree, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped fruits that resemble miniature starfruits. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, white flowers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
