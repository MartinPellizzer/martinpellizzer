Potentilla oreites

Potentilla oreites, commonly known as the alpine cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers and dark green, serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight. Its botanical features include a woody stem and multiple branches, often forming dense clusters.

Potentilla oreites is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens that mimic its natural habitat.
