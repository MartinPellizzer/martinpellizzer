Pilea yunquensis

Pilea yunquensis, commonly known as the Yunquensis Chinese Evergreen, is a species of flowering plant in the Araceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its glossy, dark green heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are clustered in an inflorescence called a spadix, surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Pilea yunquensis is a popular houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
