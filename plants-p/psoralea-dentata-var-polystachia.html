Psoralea dentata var. polystachia

Psoralea dentata var. polystachia, commonly known as the hairy psoralea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense, racemose inflorescences, which are often clustered at the tips of the branches. Its small, hairy pods contain several seeds, and the plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
