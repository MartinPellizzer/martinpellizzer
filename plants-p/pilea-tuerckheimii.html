Pilea tuerckheimii

Pilea tuerckheimii, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Pilea tuerckheimii is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
