Phalaenopsis psilantha

Phalaenopsis psilantha, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and relatively small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Phalaenopsis psilantha is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
