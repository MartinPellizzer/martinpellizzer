Palma elata

Palma elata, commonly known as the slender palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests. Its trunk is covered in a fibrous, spongy material that helps it retain moisture.

Palma elata is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
