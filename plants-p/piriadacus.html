Piriadacus

Piriadacus, commonly known as the golden star fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features a dense, compact growth habit with bright yellow to gold-colored fronds that create a striking visual display. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant color and textured appearance.

Piriadacus is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor cultivation.
