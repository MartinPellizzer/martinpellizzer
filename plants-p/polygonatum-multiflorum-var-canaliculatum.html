Polygonatum multiflorum var. canaliculatum

Polygonatum multiflorum var. canaliculatum, commonly known as the mountain yellow-lily, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features long, arching stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with yellow. Its showy, bell-shaped flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a prominent yellow or orange corona.

It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
