Pilosella arnoserioides

Pilosella arnoserioides, commonly known as the silverleaf fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. Its leaves are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance.

It produces small, yellow to brown flowers that bloom in the summer months.
