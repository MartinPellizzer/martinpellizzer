Piper fulvescenticaule

Piper fulvescenticaule, commonly known as the red-stemmed pepper plant, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its reddish stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.

The fruit is a small, dark blue berry that contains a single seed.
