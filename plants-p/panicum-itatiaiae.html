Panicum itatiaiae

Panicum itatiaiae, commonly known as the Itatiaia grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are often green or reddish in color. It is adapted to thrive in moist, shaded environments, typically found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
