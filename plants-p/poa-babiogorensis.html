Poa babiogorensis

Poa babiogorensis, commonly known as the Babiogorensis grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of South America, particularly in the Andes. This plant is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, erect stems. Its leaves are narrow and often covered with fine hairs, and it produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, Poa babiogorensis is considered a rare and specialized species.
