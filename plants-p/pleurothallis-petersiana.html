Pleurothallis petersiana

Pleurothallis petersiana, commonly known as the Peters' orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with intricate markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is a sought-after specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
