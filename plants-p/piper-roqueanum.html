Piper roqueanum

Piper roqueanum, commonly known as the Roque pepper or Roque anise, is a climbing shrub native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, green berries that turn yellow when mature, and they are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are aromatic and have a distinct, spicy scent.

The plant's botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and medicinal contexts.
