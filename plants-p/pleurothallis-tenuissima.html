Pleurothallis tenuissima

Pleurothallis tenuissima, commonly known as the slender pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, narrow leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It is part of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its relatively simple structure compared to other orchid species.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments to ensure its survival.
