Peperomia intermixta

Peperomia intermixta, commonly known as the Variegated Peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with striking white and light green variegation, creating a visually appealing contrast. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, which appear in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
