Primula hupehensis

Primula hupehensis, commonly known as the Chinese primrose, is a flowering plant native to eastern China.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant features a basal rosette of leaves and produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers on long stems. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Primulaceae and is valued for its resilience and adaptability in various garden settings.
