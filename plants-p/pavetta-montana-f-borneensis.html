Pavetta montana f. borneensis

Pavetta montana f. borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean pavetta, is a shrub belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Borneo and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are often elliptical in shape. This species is noted for its ecological significance in its native habitat, supporting various pollinators and contributing to forest biodiversity.
