Patis Coreana

Patis coreana is a unique plant native to Korea known for its striking appearance and cultural significance. This plant features vibrant, colorful flowers that bloom in the spring, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements. Its leaves are lush and green, providing a lush backdrop to its showy blooms. Patis coreana has been used in traditional Korean medicine for centuries due to its purported health benefits. Despite its beauty and utility, it remains relatively unknown to many outside of its native region.
