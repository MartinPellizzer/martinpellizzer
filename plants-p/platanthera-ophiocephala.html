Platanthera ophiocephala

Platanthera ophiocephala, commonly known as the yellow twayblade, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, elongated leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. It is typically found in moist, calcareous habitats such as meadows and woodlands.
