Polygonum lapathifolium subsp. nodosum

Polygonum lapathifolium subsp.

nodosum, commonly known as the nodding smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia. It features upright stems that are often reddish in color and bear small, clustered flowers.

The leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture, often with a reddish or purple tinge. It thrives in moist environments such as wetlands and stream banks.

The plant is characterized by its distinctive nodding inflorescence, which gives it its common name.
