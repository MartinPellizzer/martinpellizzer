Platycerium bifurcatum var. quadridichotomum

Platycerium bifurcatum var. quadridichotomum, commonly known as the Queensland fern-ferns, is a unique epiphytic fern native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, lace-like fronds that grow in a spiral pattern along its central stem. The plant's fronds are divided into multiple branches, creating a complex, fern-like structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found on the trunks of trees in rainforests.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
