Piper maclurei

Piper maclurei, commonly known as the Maclure's pepper vine, is a climbing plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by elliptical, dark blue berries. Its leaves are typically ovate with a rough texture and a strong, peppery aroma.

Piper maclurei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
