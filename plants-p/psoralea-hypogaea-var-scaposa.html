Psoralea hypogaea var. scaposa

Psoralea hypogaea var. scaposa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved psoralea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial with erect, slender stems that bear small, yellow flowers clustered in dense spikes. The leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, adapted to thrive in dry, sandy soils. It produces small, lens-shaped pods that contain several seeds.

This plant is often found in grassland and rocky habitats, where it contributes to local biodiversity.
