Pycnostachys descampsii

Pycnostachys descampsii, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a strikingly beautiful orchid native to Madagascar.

It features vibrant, star-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, with intricate patterns resembling stars. The plant has a compact growth habit, with a pseudobulb that supports its foliage and flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, typically arranged in a rosette pattern around the pseudobulb.

This orchid thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
