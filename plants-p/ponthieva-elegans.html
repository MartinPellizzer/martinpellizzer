Ponthieva elegans

Ponthieva elegans, commonly known as the elegant strelitzia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Strelitziaceae family and is characterized by its striking, banana-like leaves and showy, saddle-shaped flowers. The plant is evergreen and thrives in warm, humid climates with bright, indirect light. Its unique appearance makes it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and indoor spaces.

Ponthieva elegans requires well-draining soil and regular watering to maintain its health and vibrancy.
