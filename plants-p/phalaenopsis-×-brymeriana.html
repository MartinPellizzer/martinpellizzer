Phalaenopsis × brymeriana

Phalaenopsis × brymeriana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a popular epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendant inflorescences that bear numerous showy, lip-like flowers in various colors such as white, pink, and purple. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, and its leaves are broad, fleshy, and arranged in a fan shape. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining potting media.

Phalaenopsis × brymeriana is prized for its longevity and ability to bloom repeatedly over several years.
