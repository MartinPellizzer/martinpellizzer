Porcelia nitidifolia

Porcelia nitidifolia, commonly known as the glossy leaf porcelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, waxy leaves that give it a shiny appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
