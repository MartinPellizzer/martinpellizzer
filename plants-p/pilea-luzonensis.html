Pilea luzonensis

Pilea luzonensis, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Philippines.

It features rosettes of heart-shaped, fleshy leaves that are dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Pilea luzonensis is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
