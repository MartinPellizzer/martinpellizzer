Pseudolithos cubiformis

Pseudolithos cubiformis, commonly known as the cube stone plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its ability to form a rounded, stone-like appearance. The plant features small, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Pseudolithos cubiformis is often cultivated for its unique aesthetic and low maintenance requirements.
