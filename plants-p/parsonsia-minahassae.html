Parsonsia minahassae

Parsonsia minahassae, commonly known as the Minahassa flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and its stems are often covered in fine, white or grayish pubescence. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
