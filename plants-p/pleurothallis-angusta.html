Pleurothallis angusta

Pleurothallis angusta, commonly known as the narrow pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, tropical forests. The plant has slender, wiry stems and produces delicate, greenish-white flowers. It is characterized by its narrow leaves and compact growth habit.

Due to its small size and specific habitat requirements, it is often overlooked but holds ecological significance in its native environment.
