Pyrus syriaca var. glabra

Pyrus syriaca var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth pear, is a variety of the Syrian pear.

It is a deciduous tree characterized by its smooth, often glabrous bark and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in the spring, followed by edible, sweet fruits. It is native to the Middle East and thrives in temperate climates.

The smooth pear is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical use in horticulture.
