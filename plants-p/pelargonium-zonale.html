Pelargonium zonale

Pelargonium zonale, commonly known as the zonal geranium, is a popular ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a wide range of colors, including red, pink, and white. The leaves are typically rounded with distinct zonation patterns, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for gardens and containers.

Pelargonium zonale is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
