Philodendron broadwayi

Philodendron broadwayi, commonly known as the Broadway Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns, making it a striking ornamental plant. The plant has aerial roots that allow it to climb and attach to supports, typical of many Philodendron species. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

Its foliage is known for its vibrant coloration and unique leaf patterns, which make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
