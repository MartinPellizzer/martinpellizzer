Paeonia jishanensis

Paeonia jishanensis, commonly known as the Jishan peony, is a rare and ornamental species native to China.

It belongs to the芍药科 (Paeoniaceae) family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant features palmate leaves and has a woody root system typical of many peony species. It is valued for its horticultural beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant colors and fragrance.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a conservation concern in its natural habitat.
