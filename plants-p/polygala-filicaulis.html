Polygala filicaulis

Polygala filicaulis, commonly known as the featherleaf milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually pale blue to white and are visited by various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and naturalized areas.
