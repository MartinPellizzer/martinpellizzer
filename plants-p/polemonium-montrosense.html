Polemonium montrosense

Polemonium montrosense, commonly known as the Montrose bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Appalachian Mountains.

It features upright stems that bear whorled leaves, which are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of blue to purple bell-shaped flowers during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rich woodland soils.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Polemoniaceae family.
