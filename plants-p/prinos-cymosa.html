Prinos cymosa

Prinos cymosa, commonly known as the clustered prinos, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, characterized by its compact growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the cooler months. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, adapting to the dry environment.

Prinos cymosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
