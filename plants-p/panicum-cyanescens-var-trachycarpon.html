Panicum cyanescens var. trachycarpon

Panicum cyanescens var. trachycarpon, commonly known as blue panicgrass, is a grass species characterized by its striking blue-gray foliage.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is native to parts of Africa and the Middle East. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a distinctive bluish hue that intensifies under direct sunlight.

It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and drought tolerance.
