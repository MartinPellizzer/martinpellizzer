Pelargonium × rogersianum

Pelargonium × rogersianum, commonly known as the scented geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves, which emit a pleasant fragrance when touched. The plant typically has trailing stems and produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, or white. Native to South Africa, it thrives in warm climates and well-draining soil.

It is widely cultivated as an ornamental plant and for its use in aromatherapy and herbal remedies.
