Peperomia magnoliifolia var. variifolia

Peperomia magnoliifolia var. variifolia, commonly known as the Variegated Magnolia Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with striking white and yellow variegation, which add visual interest to any indoor or outdoor setting. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color, followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its ornamental foliage, it is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal rather than its flowers.
