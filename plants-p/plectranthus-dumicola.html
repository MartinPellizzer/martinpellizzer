Plectranthus dumicola

Plectranthus dumicola, commonly known as the rock plectranthus, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features aromatic, hairy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to white, often blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping.
