Peperomia biformis

Peperomia biformis, commonly known as the two-lobed peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a low-growing, compact plant with thick, fleshy leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Peperomia biformis is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its minimal water requirements.

Its unique leaf shape and ease of care make it a popular choice for houseplants.
