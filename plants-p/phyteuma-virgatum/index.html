Phyteuma Virgatum

Phyteuma virgatum is a striking member of the Campanulaceae family known for its tall, spire-like flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Europe, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and sunny conditions. Its vibrant purple blooms appear in late summer, adding a splash of color to rocky landscapes. Often found in alpine meadows, it is a hardy species that withstands cold climates. This elegant plant is both a botanical curiosity and a symbol of natural beauty in its native habitat.
