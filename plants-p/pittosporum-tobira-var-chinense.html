Pittosporum tobira var. chinense

Pittosporum tobira var. chinense, commonly known as Chinese pittosporum, is a compact evergreen shrub native to China.

It features dark green, glossy leaves that are oval in shape and emit a pleasant fragrance when crushed. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in clusters during late spring to early summer. These flowers are followed by bright red, berry-like fruits that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
