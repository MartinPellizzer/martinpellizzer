Polygonum arifolium

Polygonum arifolium, commonly known as the Indian knotweed, is a perennial herb native to Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants known for their distinctive arrow-shaped leaves. The plant features upright stems that are often reddish in color and bear small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are typically broad and have a slightly rough texture.

Polygonum arifolium is often found in moist environments such as wetlands and riverbanks.
