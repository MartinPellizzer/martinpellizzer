Petrocosmea kerrii var. kerrii

Petrocosmea kerrii var. kerrii, commonly known as the Kerri's petrocosmea, is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its vibrant violet to purple flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns and a distinctive yellow lip. The plant typically grows in rocky or forested habitats, thriving in cool, humid environments. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.
