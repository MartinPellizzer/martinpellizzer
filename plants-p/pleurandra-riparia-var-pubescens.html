Pleurandra riparia var. pubescens

Pleurandra riparia var. pubescens, commonly known as the pubescent river bamboo, is a variant of the river bamboo species native to Southeast Asia.

This plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, soft hairs. The leaves are typically a deep green color and have a slightly rough texture due to the pubescence. It thrives in moist, shaded environments near rivers and streams, making it a popular choice for water garden landscapes.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appearance and its ability to tolerate wet soil conditions.
