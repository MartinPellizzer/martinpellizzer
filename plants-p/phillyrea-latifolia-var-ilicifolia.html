Phillyrea latifolia var. ilicifolia

Phillyrea latifolia var. ilicifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf Phillyrea, is a shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It features evergreen foliage with glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by dark blue, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for Mediterranean climates.

The leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their astringent properties.
