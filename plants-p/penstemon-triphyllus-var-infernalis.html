Penstemon triphyllus var. infernalis

Penstemon triphyllus var. infernalis, commonly known as the Infernal Penstemon, is a striking perennial native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its vivid red and orange tubular flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique coloration and adaptability make it a notable species in the genus Penstemon.
