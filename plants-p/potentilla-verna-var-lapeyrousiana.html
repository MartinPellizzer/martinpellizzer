Potentilla verna var. lapeyrousiana

Potentilla verna var. lapeyrousiana, commonly known as the early potentilla, is a low-growing perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from April to May. The plant has slender, wiry stems and hairy leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and its ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
