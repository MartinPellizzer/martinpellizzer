Peronocactus tabularis

Peronocactus tabularis, commonly known as the flat cactus or the table cactus, is a species native to the deserts of northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its thick, ribbed stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are typically short-lived but attractive. Its succulent nature allows it to store water efficiently, making it well adapted to arid environments.

Peronocactus tabularis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
