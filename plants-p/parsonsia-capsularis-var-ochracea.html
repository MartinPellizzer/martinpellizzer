Parsonsia capsularis var. ochracea

Parsonsia capsularis var. ochracea, commonly known as the golden trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a member of the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange tubular flowers that bloom in summer. The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
