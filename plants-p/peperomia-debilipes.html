Peperomia debilipes

Peperomia debilipes, commonly known as the weak-stemmed peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia debilipes is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
