Peperomia versteegii

Peperomia versteegii, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery-blue hue on the upper surface, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia versteegii is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive foliage.
