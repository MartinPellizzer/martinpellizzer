Psoralea foliosa var. gazensis

Psoralea foliosa var. gazensis, commonly known as the Gazian leaf psoralea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemes. Its botanical structure reflects adaptations to its native arid and semi-arid environments.
