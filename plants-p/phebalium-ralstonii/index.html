Phebalium Ralstonii

Phebalium ralstonii is a rare and unique plant native to the mountainous regions of southeastern Australia. It belongs to the Rutaceae family, known for its aromatic and often ornamental species. This shrub is characterized by its distinctive, elongated leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Its conservation status highlights the importance of protecting its natural environment from further degradation.
