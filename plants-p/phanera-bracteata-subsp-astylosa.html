Phanera bracteata subsp. astylosa

Phanera bracteata subsp.

astylosa, commonly known as the slender phanera, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink.

The leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a smooth texture. This subspecies is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
