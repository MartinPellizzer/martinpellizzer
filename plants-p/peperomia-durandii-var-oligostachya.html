Peperomia durandii var. oligostachya

Peperomia durandii var. oligostachya, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features dense, glossy, silver-green foliage with a velvety texture, and its leaves are often covered in fine, white pubescence. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically green or reddish, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate watering.

This variety is popular among indoor gardeners for its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
