Parosela attenuata

Parosela attenuata, commonly known as the slender parosela, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and grows in rocky or sandy soils. It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it thrives in well-drained conditions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
