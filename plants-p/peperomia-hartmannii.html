Peperomia hartmannii

Peperomia hartmannii, commonly known as the Hartmann's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features upright, branching stems with small, ovate to elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia hartmannii is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and tolerating a range of environmental conditions.
