Potentilla antwerpiensis

Potentilla antwerpiensis, commonly known as the Antwerp cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to Europe, particularly Belgium and the Netherlands.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30-60 cm. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, usually from June to July. Its leaves are palmately compound, consisting of five to seven leaflets, and are covered in fine hairs.

Potentilla antwerpiensis thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in wet meadows and along stream banks.
