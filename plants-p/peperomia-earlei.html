Peperomia earlei

Peperomia earlei, commonly known as the Emerald Queen Peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green, waxy leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia earlei is prized for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for tabletop displays and indoor gardens.
