Pelargonium × fuerstenbergianum

Pelargonium × fuerstenbergianum, commonly known as the Fuerstenberg geranium, is a hybrid plant that belongs to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a distinctive dark center. The plant typically has rounded, lobed leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft, textured appearance. Native to South Africa, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value makes it a favored plant in both rock gardens and containers.
