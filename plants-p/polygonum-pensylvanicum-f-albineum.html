Polygonum pensylvanicum f. albineum

Polygonum pensylvanicum f. albineum, commonly known as the white-stemmed smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes species known for their distinctive leaf shapes and colorful inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as wetlands, marshes, and stream banks. Its stems are usually white or pale green, distinguishing it from the more common red-stemmed varieties of the same species.

The flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in dense, cylindrical spikes.
