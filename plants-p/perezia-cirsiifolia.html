Perezia cirsiifolia

Perezia cirsiifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf perezia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in gardens across temperate climates.
