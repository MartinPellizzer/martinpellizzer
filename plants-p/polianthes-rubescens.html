Polianthes rubescens

Polianthes rubescens, commonly known as the red-flowered amaryllis, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that bear showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red and pink. The plant produces large, fleshy leaves that are broad and strap-like, often with a waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Polianthes rubescens is often cultivated as a ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and foliage.
