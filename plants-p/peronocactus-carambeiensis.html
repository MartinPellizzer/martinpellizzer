Peronocactus carambeiensis

Peronocactus carambeiensis, commonly known as the Carambey cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in white spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in arid environments.
