Pennisetum uniflorum

Pennisetum uniflorum, commonly known as fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its clumping growth habit and slender, arching blades that create a fountain-like appearance. The plant produces fluffy, white or silver seed heads that are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Pennisetum uniflorum is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract birds and butterflies.
