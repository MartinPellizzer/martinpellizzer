Polyscias cibaria

Polyscias cibaria, commonly known as the white sago palm, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It features a short, stout trunk with a crown of feathery, pinnate leaves that are typically light green to white in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers followed by black, berry-like fruits. It is valued for its ornamental foliage and is often used in landscape design.

Polyscias cibaria is relatively low-maintenance, thriving in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
