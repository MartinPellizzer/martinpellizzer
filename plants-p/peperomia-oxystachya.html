Peperomia oxystachya

Peperomia oxystachya, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to Central and South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery-white underside, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements, it is well-suited for container gardening and spaces with limited room.
