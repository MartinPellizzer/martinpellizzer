Prevostea heterantha

Prevostea heterantha, commonly known as the tropical milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its bright orange to red flowers, which are tubular in shape and attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Prevostea heterantha is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
