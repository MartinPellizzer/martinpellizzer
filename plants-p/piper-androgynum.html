Piper androgynum

Piper androgynum, commonly known as the Androgynum pepper or the African pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant produces small, green, berry-like fruits that turn red when mature, containing the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and cuisine. Its leaves are often used in herbal remedies due to their medicinal properties.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for both its culinary and therapeutic applications.
