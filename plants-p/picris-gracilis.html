Picris gracilis

Picris gracilis, commonly known as slender picris, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, yellowish flowers that appear in clusters. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are linear and often have a rough texture, while its flowers are surrounded by pale green bracts.

Picris gracilis is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is often found in sandy or gravelly environments.
