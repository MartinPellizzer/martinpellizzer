Pteris plumula

Pteris plumula, commonly known as the small shield fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and small, shield-shaped fronds. The fronds are typically dark green and have a smooth, leathery texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Pteris plumula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated as a houseplant.
