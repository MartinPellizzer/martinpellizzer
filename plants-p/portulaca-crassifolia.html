Portulaca crassifolia

Portulaca crassifolia, commonly known as the thickleaf purslane, is a succulent plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the morning and close by midday. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid environments.

Portulaca crassifolia is often used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
