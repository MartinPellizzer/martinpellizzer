Piper merrillii

Piper merrillii, commonly known as the Philippine pepper vine, is a climbing shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, vine-like stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are unisexual, with male and female flowers occurring on separate plants.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns red when mature, often used in local cuisines and traditional medicine.
