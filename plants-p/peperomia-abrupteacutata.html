Peperomia abrupteacutata

Peperomia abrupteacutata, commonly known as the acute-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features ovate to lanceolate leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia abrupteacutata is appreciated for its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
