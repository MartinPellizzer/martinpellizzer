Panicum decoloratum

Panicum decoloratum, commonly known as yellow panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and panicles of small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in warm climates with well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in regions with mild winters.
