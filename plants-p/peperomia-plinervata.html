Peperomia plinervata

Peperomia plinervata, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to Brazil.

It features glossy, dark green, oval-shaped leaves with a silvery underside, giving it a striking visual appeal. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia plinervata is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
