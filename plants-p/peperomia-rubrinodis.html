Peperomia rubrinodis

Peperomia rubrinodis, commonly known as the red-node peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with distinctive red or purple veins, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and drought tolerance.

Peperomia rubrinodis is prized for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
