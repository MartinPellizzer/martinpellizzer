Piper walkeri

Piper walkeri, commonly known as the African pepper plant, is a flowering shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruit is a three-lobed berry that turns red when mature.

Piper walkeri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in some regions.
