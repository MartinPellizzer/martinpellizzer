Peperomia portuguesensis

Peperomia portuguesensis, commonly known as the Portuguese peperomia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and thick, waxy leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia portuguesensis is relatively low maintenance and suitable for beginners due to its adaptability and drought tolerance.
