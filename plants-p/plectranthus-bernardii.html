Plectranthus bernardii

Plectranthus bernardii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant has aromatic leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Plectranthus bernardii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
