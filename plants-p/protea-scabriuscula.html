Protea scabriuscula

Protea scabriuscula, commonly known as the rough-leaved protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences composed of numerous pale pink to white flowers arranged in a spherical shape. The plant typically grows in coastal fynbos vegetation, thriving in well-drained, acidic soils. Its leaves are rough and hairy, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Protea scabriuscula is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays an important role in the biodiversity of its native habitat.
