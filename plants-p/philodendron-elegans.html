Philodendron elegans

Philodendron elegans, commonly known as the elegant philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy green leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. The plant has a vigorous growth habit and can reach lengths of several meters when provided with proper support. Its aerial roots help it climb and attach to surfaces, while its rhizomatous structure allows for easy propagation.

Philodendron elegans thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.
