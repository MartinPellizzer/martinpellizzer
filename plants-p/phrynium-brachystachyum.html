Phrynium brachystachyum

Phrynium brachystachyum, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are often adorned with a waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically borne on leafy shoots. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and tropical gardens.
