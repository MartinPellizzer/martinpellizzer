Peperomia subrenifolia var. microphylla

Peperomia subrenifolia var. microphylla, commonly known as the dwarf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, waxy, ovate leaves that are typically a deep green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
