Pleioblastus angustatus

Pleioblastus angustatus, commonly known as the narrowleaf Japanese grass, is a species of bamboo native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect culms that are often used in ornamental horticulture. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green, providing an attractive visual appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Pleioblastus angustatus is also valued for its ability to tolerate urban environments and its low maintenance requirements.
