Pilea geminata

Pilea geminata, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial shrub native to China.

It features rounded, fleshy leaves that are typically a vibrant green, often with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ability to propagate through stem cuttings makes it easy to grow and maintain.
