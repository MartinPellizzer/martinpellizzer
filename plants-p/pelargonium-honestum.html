Pelargonium honestum

Pelargonium honestum, commonly known as the honesty plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant produces small, white seeds that are often referred to as "honesty" due to their appearance resembling tiny coins. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Pelargonium honestum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its delicate beauty and compact growth habit.
