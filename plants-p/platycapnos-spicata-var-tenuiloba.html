Platycapnos spicata var. tenuiloba

Platycapnos spicata var. tenuiloba, commonly known as slender spikeweed, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear small, greenish-white flowers in spike-like clusters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, alpine environments.
