Pilea modesta

Pilea modesta, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial shrub native to China.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green, with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale green, which appear in the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea modesta is relatively low maintenance and can be propagated easily through stem cuttings.
