Piper fortalezanum

Piper fortalezanum, commonly known as the Fortaleza pepper or Brazilian pepper, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with heart-shaped leaves and small, clustered white flowers. Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns red when mature.

Piper fortalezanum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
