Prunus nana rubra

Prunus nana rubra, commonly known as the dwarf red cherry.

It is a compact shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant is characterized by its small, rounded leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that appear in early spring. Its fruit is a small, red cherry that ripens in late summer.

This species is well-suited for gardens due to its ornamental value and hardy nature.
