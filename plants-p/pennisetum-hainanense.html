Pennisetum hainanense

Pennisetum hainanense, commonly known as Hainan grass, is a perennial grass species native to China, specifically the Hainan Island.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. It is often found in open woodlands and along stream banks, where it contributes to the local ecosystem by providing habitat and stabilizing soil.

Its ornamental value has also led to its cultivation in gardens and landscapes.
