Phalaenopsis fuscata

Phalaenopsis fuscata, commonly known as the dark moth orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, dark green leaves and produces clusters of pale yellow to brownish flowers with a distinctive moth-like appearance. The plant typically blooms once a year, with flowers lasting several weeks. It prefers well-draining soil and moderate humidity, making it suitable for both greenhouse and indoor cultivation.

Its unique coloration and elegant form make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
