Psoralea andongensis

Psoralea andongensis, commonly known as the Andong psoralea, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to temperate climates. Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
