Poa szechuensis var. debilior

Poa szechuensis var. debilior, commonly known as the weak poa, is a variant of the poa grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its delicate, slender leaves and low-growing habit, typically forming dense mats on the ground. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions with cool climates. Its flowering stems are short and bear small, spike-like inflorescences.

The botanical features of Poa szechuensis var. debilior make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
