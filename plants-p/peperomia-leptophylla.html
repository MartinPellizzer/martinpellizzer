Peperomia leptophylla

Peperomia leptophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, elongated, dark green leaves that are often slightly hairy, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tiny, reddish-brown flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Peperomia leptophylla is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to enhance the aesthetic of indoor spaces.
