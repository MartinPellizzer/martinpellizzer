Peperomia petrophila var. brevipetiola

Peperomia petrophila var. brevipetiola, commonly known as the rock peperomia, is a rare and unique plant native to the limestone regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a subtle silver or blue hue. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.
