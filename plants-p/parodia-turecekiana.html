Parodia turecekiana

Parodia turecekiana, commonly known as the Tureck's cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its globular shape and spiny stems. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in late spring or early summer.

Parodia turecekiana is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
