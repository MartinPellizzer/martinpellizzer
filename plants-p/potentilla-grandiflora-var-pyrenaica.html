Potentilla grandiflora var. pyrenaica

Potentilla grandiflora var. pyrenaica, commonly known as the Pyrenean shrub cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the Pyrenees region.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, compound, and covered in fine hairs.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in mountainous habitats.
