Pelargonium × regium

Pelargonium × regium, commonly known as the Royal Pelargonium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often in clusters. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a houseplant and in gardens.
