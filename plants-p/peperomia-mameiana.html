Peperomia mameiana

Peperomia mameiana, commonly known as the Silver Mound Peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, fleshy, silver-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia mameiana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
