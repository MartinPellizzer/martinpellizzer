Phthirusa uniflora

Phthirusa uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower phthirusa, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale yellow. The plant has a low-growing habit with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
