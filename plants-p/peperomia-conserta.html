Peperomia conserta

Peperomia conserta, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically silver-white with dark green veins, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, usually green or red, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia conserta is relatively low maintenance and drought-tolerant, making it suitable for beginners.
