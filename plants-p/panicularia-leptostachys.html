Panicularia leptostachys

Panicularia leptostachys, commonly known as the slender-stalked panicled grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and panicle inflorescences, which consist of multiple branches bearing small, spikelet-like flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slightly rough texture.

Panicularia leptostachys is often found in temperate regions and is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils.
