Potentilla × aurantiaca

Potentilla × aurantiaca, commonly known as the Chinese firethorn, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and vibrant orange to red berries that persist through winter. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers in clusters during early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a hedge.

Its ornamental value comes from its colorful berries and architectural form.
