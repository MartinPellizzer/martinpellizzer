Pennisetum orientale var. altissimum

Pennisetum orientale var. altissimum, commonly known as the giant feather grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to southern Africa.

It features tall, slender, silver-green inflorescences that resemble plumes, adding a dramatic visual effect to gardens. The plant is characterized by its clumping growth habit and ability to thrive in full sun to partial shade. It requires well-drained soil and is drought-tolerant once established.

Pennisetum orientale var. altissimum is often used in landscaping for its architectural form and seasonal color contrast.
