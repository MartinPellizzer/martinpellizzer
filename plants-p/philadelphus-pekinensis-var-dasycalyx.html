Philadelphus pekinensis var. dasycalyx

Philadelphus pekinensis var. dasycalyx, commonly known as the Chinese mock orange, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy on the underside. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for.
