Pentzia tortuosa

Pentzia tortuosa, commonly known as the twisted pentzia, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, commonly referred to as the ice plant family. The plant is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often twisted or spiraled, giving it its common name. Its small, white flowers appear in late summer, adding ornamental value.

It thrives in well-drained sandy soils and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.
