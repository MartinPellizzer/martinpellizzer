Pleuropogon hooverianus

Pleuropogon hooverianus, commonly known as the Hoover's Panicgrass, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are characteristic of the Panicum genus. It is often found in association with other wetland plants, contributing to the ecological diversity of these environments.
