Panicum adenense

Panicum adenense, commonly known as the腺草, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, two-lobed spikelets. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a rough texture.

Panicum adenense is often found in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.
