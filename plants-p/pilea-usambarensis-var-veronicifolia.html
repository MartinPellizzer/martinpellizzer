Pilea usambarensis var. veronicifolia

Pilea usambarensis var. veronicifolia, commonly known as the velvet leaf begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves that range in color from deep green to reddish hues. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its unique foliage and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
