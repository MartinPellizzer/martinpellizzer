Peperomia ciliipetiola

Peperomia ciliipetiola, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green color, often adorned with subtle reddish veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically green or red, which are followed by small, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia ciliipetiola is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental foliage and low maintenance requirements.
