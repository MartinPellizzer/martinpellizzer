Pteropsis scolopendrina

Pteropsis scolopendrina, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped inflorescence. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often arranged in a spiral pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural collections.
