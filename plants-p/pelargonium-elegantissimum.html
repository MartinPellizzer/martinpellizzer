Pelargonium elegantissimum

Pelargonium elegantissimum, commonly known as the elegant geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, producing upright stems with serrated leaves. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from spring to autumn.
