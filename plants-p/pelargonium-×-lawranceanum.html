Pelargonium × lawranceanum

Pelargonium × lawranceanum, commonly known as the Lawrence geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features lobed leaves with a rough texture and a strong, aromatic scent. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated for both decorative and horticultural purposes.
