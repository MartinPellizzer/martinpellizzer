Paphiopedilum × wuliangshanicum

Paphiopedilum × wuliangshanicum, commonly known as the Wuliangshan orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate, slipper-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or terrestrial species, often found in shaded forest environments. Its flowers exhibit a range of colors, including white, pink, and purple, with a distinctive pouch-like structure.

The botanical features of this orchid make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and unique morphology.
