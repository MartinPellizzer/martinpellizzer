Piper anisodorum

Piper anisodorum, commonly known as the star anise plant, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped fruits that are commonly used in traditional medicine and as a spice. The plant belongs to the Piperaceae family and is known for its aromatic leaves and berries. Its essential oil contains compounds like anethole, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

The plant thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its aromatic and therapeutic uses.
