Pterostylis vittata var. viridiflora

Pterostylis vittata var. viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It features a slender, grass-like stem with a single, elongated flower that is typically greenish in color. The plant has a short, fibrous root system and produces small, linear leaves. It blooms during late winter to early spring, often in response to rainfall.

This orchid is adapted to grow in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys.
