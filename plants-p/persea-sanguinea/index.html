Persea Sanguinea

Persea sanguinea is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. It is known for its striking red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a vivid splash of color to its natural habitat. The tree's leaves are glossy and dark green, providing a lush backdrop for its showy blossoms. While not widely cultivated, it is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance. This unique plant offers a glimpse into the rich biodiversity of tropical ecosystems.
