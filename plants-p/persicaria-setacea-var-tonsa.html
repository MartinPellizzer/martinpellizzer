Persicaria setacea var. tonsa

Persicaria setacea var. tonsa, commonly known as slender smartweed, is a herbaceous annual plant native to North America.

It features upright, branching stems that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are lance-shaped, slightly hairy, and often have a reddish tinge, especially along the veins. Small, greenish-white flowers appear in clusters at the tips of the stems, and the plant produces small, three-lobed seeds.

It thrives in moist soils and is often found in wetlands, ditches, and along stream banks.
