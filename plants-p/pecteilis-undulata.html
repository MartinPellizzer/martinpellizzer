Pecteilis undulata

Pecteilis undulata, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, zigzagging stems. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a desirable addition to botanical collections.
