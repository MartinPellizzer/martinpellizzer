Poa perligularis

Poa perligularis, commonly known as the silver hair grass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, where it thrives in cool and moist conditions. Its inflorescence consists of slender, spike-like clusters of small, two-lobed flowers.

Poa perligularis is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
