Portulaca cryptopetala f. phaenopetala

Portulaca cryptopetala f. phaenopetala, commonly known as the showy portulaca, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Portulacaceae.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant is native to southern Africa and thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. Its flowers are typically borne on short, erect stems and are often clustered together.

The species is noted for its ability to store water in its leaves, making it well-adapted to drought conditions.
