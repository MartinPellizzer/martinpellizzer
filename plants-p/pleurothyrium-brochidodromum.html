Pleurothyrium brochidodromum

Pleurothyrium brochidodromum, commonly known as the Brazilian fern, is a member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is a perennial evergreen fern characterized by its feathery, delicate fronds that are typically bi- or tri-pinnate. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit and is native to tropical regions of South America. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
