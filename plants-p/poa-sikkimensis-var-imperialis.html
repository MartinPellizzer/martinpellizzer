Poa sikkimensis var. imperialis

Poa sikkimensis var. imperialis, commonly known as the Imperial Himalayan grass, is a perennial grass species native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, glabrous leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences with florets that are typically pale green to brownish in hue. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
