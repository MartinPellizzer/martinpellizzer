Pimelea nitens subsp. aspera

Pimelea nitens subsp.

aspera, commonly known as the rough pimelea, is a shrub native to southern Australia. It features small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically arranged in dense clusters.

The plant has rough, hairy leaves that are usually narrow and linear in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and rocky areas.

The subspecies is distinguished by its more rugged leaf texture compared to other forms of Pimelea nitens.
