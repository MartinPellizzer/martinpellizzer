Pennisetum orthochaete

Pennisetum orthochaete, commonly known as purple lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and panicles that produce numerous small, fluffy seeds. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green hue and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Pennisetum orthochaete is also valued for its ability to attract birds and other wildlife.
