Pterocarpus stevensonii

Pterocarpus stevensonii, commonly known as the Stevenson's ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark brown wood that is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has compound leaves with elongated leaflets and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a legume pod containing flat, winged seeds.

The tree plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
