Polianthes geminiflora var. clivicola

Polianthes geminiflora var. clivicola, commonly known as the cliff lily, is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems that bear clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and full sun, making it well-suited for rock gardens or alpine settings. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are evergreen, adding visual interest throughout the year.

This variety is noted for its resilience and ability to bloom reliably in cooler climates.
