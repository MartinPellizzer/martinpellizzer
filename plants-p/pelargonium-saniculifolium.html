Pelargonium saniculifolium

Pelargonium saniculifolium, commonly known as the heartleaf geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pink or red flowers. The plant belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Pelargonium saniculifolium is also valued for its ability to thrive in dry conditions.
