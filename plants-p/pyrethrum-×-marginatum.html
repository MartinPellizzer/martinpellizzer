Pyrethrum × marginatum

Pyrethrum × marginatum, commonly known as the marguerite daisy, is a hybrid perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals and yellow centers, typically blooming in summer. The plant has a compact growth habit and is well-suited for rock gardens or borders. Its foliage is dark green and slightly fuzzy, adding texture to the landscape.

Pyrethrum × marginatum is also valued for its natural insect-repelling properties due to its pyrethrin content.
