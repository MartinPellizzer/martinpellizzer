Phebalium tuberculosum subsp. brachyphyllum

Phebalium tuberculosum subsp.

brachyphyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved phebalium, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia. It is characterized by its small, tuberculate leaves and clusters of yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in heathland environments. It is a member of the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants.
