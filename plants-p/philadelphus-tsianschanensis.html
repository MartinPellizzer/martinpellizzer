Philadelphus tsianschanensis

Philadelphus tsianschanensis, commonly known as the Chinese mock orange, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and can grow up to 3 meters in height. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its showy blossoms and pleasant scent.
