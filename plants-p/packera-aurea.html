Packera aurea

Packera aurea, commonly known as golden ragwort, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is part of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features basal leaves that are broadly ovate with a hairy texture and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and streambanks.

Its scientific name reflects its striking yellow color, which is prominent in its showy inflorescence.
