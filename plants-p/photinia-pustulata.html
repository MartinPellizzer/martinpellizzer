Photinia pustulata

Photinia pustulata, commonly known as the glossy abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that provide a striking contrast to its clusters of pink or white flowers. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Photinia pustulata is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions.
