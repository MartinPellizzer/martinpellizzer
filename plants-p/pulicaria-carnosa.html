Pulicaria carnosa

Pulicaria carnosa, commonly known as the woolly groundsel, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its yellow flowers appear in clusters during the summer months, adding a vibrant touch to its habitat.

Pulicaria carnosa is often used in rock gardens due to its hardy nature and ornamental value.
