Pyrus malus apetala

Pyrus malus apetala, commonly known as the apetala apple.

This rare variant of the apple tree is characterized by its lack of functional petals, a trait that distinguishes it from typical apple species. It belongs to the Rosaceae family and exhibits a similar morphology to other Pyrus species, with alternate, simple leaves and clustered flowers.
