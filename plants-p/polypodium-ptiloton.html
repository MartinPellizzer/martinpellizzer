Polypodium ptiloton

Polypodium ptiloton, commonly known as the hairy shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy fronds that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its fronds are usually leathery and have a distinctive, shield-like appearance.

Polypodium ptiloton is known for its ability to thrive in humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
