Polyalthia micrantha

Polyalthia micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered evergreen.

It is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its smooth, gray bark and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in panicles, often blooming during the rainy season. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Polyalthia micrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
