Panicum colliei

Panicum colliei, commonly known as eastern dropseed, is a native grass species of the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and slender, arching growth habit. The plant produces delicate, feathery seed heads that add visual interest during the summer and fall. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sun, making it a popular choice for native plant gardens.

Panicum colliei is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing food and habitat for various wildlife species.
