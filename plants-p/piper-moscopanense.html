Piper moscopanense

Piper moscopanense, commonly known as the KwaZulu-Natal pepper, is a tropical climbing vine native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, berry-like fruits. Its leaves are typically ovate with a slightly hairy surface.

Piper moscopanense is valued for its potential medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
