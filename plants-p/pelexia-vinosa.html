Pelexia vinosa

Pelexia vinosa, commonly known as the purple tamarisk, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal areas. The plant features small, fragrant pink or purple flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in a fine, powdery coating.

Pelexia vinosa is known for its hardiness and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
