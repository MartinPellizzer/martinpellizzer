Pourretia gigantea

Pourretia gigantea, commonly known as the giant pourretia, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that consist of numerous small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical or subtropical climates, thriving in warm and humid conditions. Its leaves are usually large and broad, with a waxy surface that helps reduce water loss.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique floral structure and potential medicinal properties.
