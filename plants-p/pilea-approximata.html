Pilea approximata

Pilea approximata, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically a deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by round, green fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Pilea approximata is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
