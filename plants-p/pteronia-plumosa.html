Pteronia plumosa

Pteronia plumosa, commonly known as the feather grass, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and delicate, fluffy flower heads that resemble small clouds. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color.

Pteronia plumosa is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
