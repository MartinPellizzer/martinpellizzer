Pleurothallis carrisii

Pleurothallis carrisii, commonly known as the Carrisii orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, narrow leaves and produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is part of the genus Pleurothallis, which includes over 1,000 species.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often sought after by orchid enthusiasts and botanists.
