Peperomia niveopunctulata

Peperomia niveopunctulata, commonly known as the snow-dotted peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with distinctive white or silver spots that give it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and purple, which are typically hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate moisture.

Peperomia niveopunctulata is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
