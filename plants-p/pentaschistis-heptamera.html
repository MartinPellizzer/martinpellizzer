Pentaschistis heptamera

Pentaschistis heptamera, commonly known as the seven-armed starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a basal rosette of narrow, linear leaves and produces achenes as its fruits. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in dry, rocky habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
