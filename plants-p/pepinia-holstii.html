Pepinia holstii

Pepinia holstii, commonly known as the Holst's peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Piperaceae and is characterized by its compact, succulent-like foliage. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive leaves and low maintenance requirements.

Pepinia holstii thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor environments.
