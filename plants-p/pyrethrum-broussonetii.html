Pyrethrum broussonetii

Pyrethrum broussonetii, commonly known as the African pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant produces small, spherical seed heads that contain numerous tiny seeds. It is known for its natural insecticidal properties due to the presence of pyrethrins.

Pyrethrum broussonetii is often cultivated for both ornamental purposes and as a source of natural pesticides.
