Pelargonium × carnescens

Pelargonium × carnescens, commonly known as the candy striped geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, bi-colored leaves that display shades of red, pink, and white, creating a striking visual effect. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with multiple stems and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink and red. Native to South Africa, it thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique foliage makes it a standout ornamental plant in both indoor and outdoor settings.
