Panulia panul

Panulia panul, commonly known as the golden cup, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, cup-shaped seed pods that contain numerous seeds.

Panulia panul is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and shrublands.
