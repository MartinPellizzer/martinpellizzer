Perinka reticulata

Perinka reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a species of orchid native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its intricate, net-like vein patterns on its leaves, which give it its scientific name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a variety of colors including white, yellow, and green.

Perinka reticulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
