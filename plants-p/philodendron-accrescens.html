Philodendron accrescens

Philodendron accrescens, commonly known as the giant philodendron, is a climbing plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length. The plant has a robust stem that can climb and spread over other vegetation, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening. Its foliage is typically a deep green with a slightly waxy texture.

Philodendron accrescens thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
