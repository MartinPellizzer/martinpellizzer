Persicaria yokusaiana f. albiflora

Persicaria yokusaiana f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered smartweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Polygonaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. It is native to parts of East Asia and thrives in moist, shady environments.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate foliage and subtle floral display.
