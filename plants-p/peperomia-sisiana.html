Peperomia sisiana

Peperomia sisiana, commonly known as the Sisian peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically heart-shaped or ovate, with a deep green color and prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, usually greenish-white in color, which are followed by small, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia sisiana is known for its low maintenance and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments.
