Pennisetum centrasiaticum var. qinghaiense

Pennisetum centrasiaticum var. qinghaiense, commonly known as the Qinghai grass, is a perennial grass native to the Qinghai region of China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, thriving in cool and arid conditions. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often green or reddish in color.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in local ecosystems.
