Peperomia lancifolia

Peperomia lancifolia, commonly known as the lanceleaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features lance-shaped, glossy dark green leaves that are often slightly wavy along the edges. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that emerge from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia lancifolia is known for its low maintenance and ability to adapt well to a variety of indoor conditions.
