Pentacalia microdon

Pentacalia microdon, commonly known as the dwarf pentacalia, is a member of the Campanulaceae family.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that produces small, bell-shaped flowers. Native to the southeastern United States, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant has hairy stems and leaves with a rough texture.

Its compact size and delicate appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and woodland settings.
