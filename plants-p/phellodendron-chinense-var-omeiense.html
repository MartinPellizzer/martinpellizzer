Phellodendron chinense var. omeiense

Phellodendron chinense var. omeiense, commonly known as the Omei plum tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and alternate, ovate leaves with a serrated margin. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, followed by yellow or orange fruits that are edible. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
