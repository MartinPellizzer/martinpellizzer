Pterostylis arfakensis

Pterostylis arfakensis, commonly known as the Arfankens Nightshade Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique floral structure. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in rainforest or heathland areas. Its flowers are usually small and greenish or brownish, with a distinctive lip that aids in pollination.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its specific habitat requirements and limited distribution.
