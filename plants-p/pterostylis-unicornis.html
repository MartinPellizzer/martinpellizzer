Pterostylis unicornis

Pterostylis unicornis, commonly known as the unicorn orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, often strikingly shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as rainforests or under dense vegetation. Its flowers are usually green or yellowish, with a single, elongated petal that resembles a horn, giving it its common name.

Pterostylis unicornis is known for its specialized pollination mechanisms, often relying on specific insects for reproduction.
