Phreatia oberonioides

Phreatia oberonioides, commonly known as the Oberon's phreatia, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces large, showy inflorescences with numerous fragrant flowers, typically in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts and botanists.
