Pirazzia elliptica

Pirazzia elliptica, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pirazzia, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged alternately along its stem. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
