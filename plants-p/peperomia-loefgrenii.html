Peperomia loefgrenii

Peperomia loefgrenii, commonly known as the Loefgren's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, often with a deep green color and a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or purple in color, which appear in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate watering.

Peperomia loefgrenii is valued for its ornamental foliage and is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
