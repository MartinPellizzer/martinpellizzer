Pleurospermum Yulungense

Pleurospermum yulungense is a rare and striking member of the Asteraceae family native to the mountainous regions of China. This plant is known for its vibrant yellow flowers and distinctive silver-green foliage that catch the sunlight. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas. Due to its limited distribution and unique characteristics, it has become a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Its beauty and ecological significance make it a valuable addition to the natural world.
