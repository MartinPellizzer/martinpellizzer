Peperomia kuntzei

Peperomia kuntzei, commonly known as the Kuntze's peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and red, which are borne on short, erect spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia kuntzei is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.
