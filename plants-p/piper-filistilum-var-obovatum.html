Piper filistilum var. obovatum

Piper filistilum var. obovatum, commonly known as the broad-ovate pepper plant, is a flowering plant in the Piperaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features compound leaves with ovate to broadly ovate leaflets, giving it its varietal name. Its small, greenish-white flowers are typically clustered in spikes, and it produces small, peppery fruits.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
