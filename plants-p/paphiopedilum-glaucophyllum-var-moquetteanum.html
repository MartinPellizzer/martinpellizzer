Paphiopedilum glaucophyllum var. moquetteanum

Paphiopedilum glaucophyllum var. moquetteanum, commonly known as the Moquetteanum Paphiopedilum, is a rare and unique orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, waxy, greenish-blue flowers with a distinctive yellow lip. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid, often found in shaded, humid environments. Its foliage consists of broad, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.

This variant is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and limited natural distribution.
