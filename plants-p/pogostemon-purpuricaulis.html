Pogostemon purpuricaulis

Pogostemon purpuricaulis, commonly known as the purple-stemmed pogostemon, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features upright, hairy stems that are often tinged with purple, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Pogostemon purpuricaulis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks.
