Paederia linearis var. pilosa

Paederia linearis var. pilosa, commonly known as the hairy paederia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, often blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

The hairy texture of its leaves is a key identifying feature of this variant.
