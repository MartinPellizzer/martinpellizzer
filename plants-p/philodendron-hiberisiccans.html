Philodendron hiberisiccans

Philodendron hiberisiccans, commonly known as the Hiberisiccans philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces small, pale green flowers that are followed by dark, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and greenhouse cultivation.
