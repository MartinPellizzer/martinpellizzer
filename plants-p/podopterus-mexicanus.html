Podopterus mexicanus

Podopterus mexicanus, commonly known as the Mexican spider orchid, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded, moist environments.

Podopterus mexicanus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
