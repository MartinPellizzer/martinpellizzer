Pleurothallis apoda

Pleurothallis apoda, commonly known as the slender pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated stems and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant prefers humid, shaded environments and thrives in well-draining soil. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, contributing to its overall low-growing appearance.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
