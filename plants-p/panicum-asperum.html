Panicum asperum

Panicum asperum, commonly known as rough panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its coarse, hairy leaves and upright, branched inflorescences. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can thrive in full sun. It is often found in open habitats such as roadsides, fields, and waste areas.

Panicum asperum is valued for its resilience and is sometimes used in erosion control.
