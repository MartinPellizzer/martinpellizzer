Peucedanum angustisectum

Peucedanum angustisectum, commonly known as narrow-leaved cow parsley, is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small, white or pale yellow flowers.

Its aromatic foliage and flowers attract various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.
