Peperomia tetrica

Peperomia tetrica, commonly known as the Shiny Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, waxy, ovate leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy sheen. The plant produces clusters of tiny, bright red flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal watering needs.

Peperomia tetrica is valued for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
