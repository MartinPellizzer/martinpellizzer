Prevostea

Prevostea, commonly known as the goldenbush, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the southern regions of South America, particularly Argentina and Chile. The plants typically feature erect, branched stems covered in dense, yellow to golden-green foliage. They produce small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in the spring and summer months.
