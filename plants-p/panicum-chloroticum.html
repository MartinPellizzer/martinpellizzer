Panicum chloroticum

Panicum chloroticum, commonly known as yellow panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance and often forms dense mats or clumps. The plant typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and other moist environments, thriving in areas with high humidity. Its leaves are narrow and have a yellowish-green color, which gives it its common name.

Panicum chloroticum is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for various wildlife species.
