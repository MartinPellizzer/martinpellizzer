Pilea rojasiana

Pilea rojasiana, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It features rounded, succulent leaves that are typically a vibrant green, often with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Pilea rojasiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and air-purifying qualities.
