Pelargonium rigidum

Pelargonium rigidum, commonly known as the rigid geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy stems. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are usually narrow and have a serrated edge, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Pelargonium rigidum is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its hardiness and attractive foliage.
