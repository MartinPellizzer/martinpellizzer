Portulaca splendens

Portulaca splendens, commonly known as the dazzling purslane, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a low-growing annual herb that thrives in hot, arid environments. The plant features fleshy, succulent leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to red. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Portulaca splendens is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its resilience and aesthetic appeal.
