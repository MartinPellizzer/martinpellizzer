Peperomia barbata var. puberula

Peperomia barbata var. puberula, commonly known as the velvety peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a velvety texture, and its stems are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Due to its attractive foliage and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
