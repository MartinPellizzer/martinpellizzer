Piper prunifolium var. carboneranum

Piper prunifolium var. carboneranum, commonly known as the Carboneranum pepper plant, is a tropical vine native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that turn red when mature, containing aromatic seeds. It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species.

Due to its ornamental value and unique fragrance, it is often cultivated in warm climates for both aesthetic and culinary purposes.
