Polyscias fruticosa var. plumata

Polyscias fruticosa var. plumata, commonly known as the featherleaf glorybower, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a distinctive feathery appearance, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by yellow-orange berries. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained, moist soil.

This ornamental plant is valued for its attractive foliage and is often used in landscaping and as a houseplant.
