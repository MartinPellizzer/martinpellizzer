Portulaca samoensis

Portulaca samoensis, commonly known as the Samoan purslane, is a flowering plant native to the island of Samoa.

It is a low-growing annual herb with fleshy, succulent leaves and bright yellow flowers. The plant thrives in arid and coastal environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its small, rounded leaves and vibrant blooms make it a distinctive feature of the local flora.

Portulaca samoensis is often found in sandy soils and is used in traditional Samoan medicine.
