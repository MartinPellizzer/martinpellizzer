Pteroceras dalaputtuwa

Pteroceras dalaputtuwa, commonly known as the Sri Lankan fern, is a species of fern native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest undergrowth. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological significance.
