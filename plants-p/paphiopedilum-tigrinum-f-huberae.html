Paphiopedilum tigrinum f. huberae

Paphiopedilum tigrinum f. huberae, commonly known as the tiger orchid, is a rare and highly sought-after orchid species native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, intricate patterns of deep red and yellow on its petals and sepals, resembling a tiger's stripes. The plant is a terrestrial orchid, typically growing in shaded, humid forest environments. Its flowers are usually fragrant and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in greenhouses and is protected in its natural habitat.
