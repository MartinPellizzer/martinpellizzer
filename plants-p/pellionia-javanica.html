Pellionia javanica

Pellionia javanica, commonly known as the Java nettle, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes many stinging plants. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its leaves are sometimes used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
