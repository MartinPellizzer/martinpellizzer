Platanthera ophrydioides var. amabilis

Platanthera ophrydioides var. amabilis, commonly known as the showy pleuranthus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and swamps. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow with a distinctive greenish or purple throat.
