Piper arieianum var. yaracuyense

Piper arieianum var. yaracuyense, commonly known as the Yaracuyense pepper plant, is a member of the Piperaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly Venezuela and Colombia. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with heart-shaped leaves and small, clustered greenish-white flowers. Its fruit, a small, round berry, turns red when mature and is used in traditional medicine and cuisine.

The species is valued for its aromatic properties and potential medicinal uses.
