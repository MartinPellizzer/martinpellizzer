Pentaschistis zeyheri

Pentaschistis zeyheri, commonly known as the Zeyher's pentaschistis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like inflorescence. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
