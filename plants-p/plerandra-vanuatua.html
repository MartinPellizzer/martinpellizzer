Plerandra vanuatua

Plerandra vanuatua, commonly known as the Vanuatu arum, is a striking tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and a distinctive purple spathe that surrounds the spadix, creating an eye-catching floral display. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, known for its unique inflorescence structure. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in coastal or lowland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and private collections.
