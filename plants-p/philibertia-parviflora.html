Philibertia parviflora

Philibertia parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered filbert, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a dense cluster, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
