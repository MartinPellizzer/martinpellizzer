Pentas zanzibarica var. intermedia

Pentas zanzibarica var. intermedia, commonly known as the Zanzibar pentas, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its clusters of vibrant, star-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
