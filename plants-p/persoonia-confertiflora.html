Persoonia confertiflora

Persoonia confertiflora, commonly known as the denseflower geebung, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, often multi-branched stems and dense clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The leaves are usually narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and woodland areas.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
