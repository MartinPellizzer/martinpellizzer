Potentilla pirotensis

Potentilla pirotensis, commonly known as the Pirotensis cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, producing clusters of small, bright yellow petals. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or alpine environments.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its native range.
