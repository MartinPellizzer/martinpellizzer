Piper yoroanum

Piper yoroanum, commonly known as Yoroanum pepper or Yoroanum long pepper, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper and long pepper. The plant produces small, spicate inflorescences that bear tiny, peppercorn-like fruits, which are used in traditional medicine and as a spice. Its leaves are also utilized in herbal remedies due to their aromatic and medicinal properties.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the undergrowth of tropical forests.
