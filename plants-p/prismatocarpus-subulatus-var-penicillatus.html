Prismatocarpus subulatus var. penicillatus

Prismatocarpus subulatus var. penicillatus, commonly known as the slender-leafed prismatocarpus, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by orange-yellow fruits that are edible. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
