Petalochilus minor

Petalochilus minor, commonly known as the small petal orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically produces greenish-yellow or pale brown flowers with a short, narrow spur. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Petalochilus minor is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
