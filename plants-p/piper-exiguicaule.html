Piper exiguicaule

Piper exiguicaule, commonly known as the small-stemmed pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, opposite leaves. It produces tiny, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

The fruit is a small, three-lobed berry that contains the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.
