Phymosia rosea

Phymosia rosea, commonly known as the pink wattle, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, woody growth and clusters of small, pinkish-red flowers. The plant produces oval, leathery leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Phymosia rosea is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in horticulture for its hardy and drought-resistant nature.
