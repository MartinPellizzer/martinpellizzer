Pennisetum latifolium

Pennisetum latifolium, commonly known as broadleaf fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It features upright clumps of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces fluffy, feathery seed heads that add ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Pennisetum latifolium is often used in landscaping for its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
