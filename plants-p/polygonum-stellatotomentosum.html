Polygonum stellatotomentosum

Polygonum stellatotomentosum, commonly known as star-tomentose knotweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants known for their distinctive bract structures. The plant is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are often covered in fine, star-shaped hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
