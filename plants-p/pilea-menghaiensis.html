Pilea menghaiensis

Pilea menghaiensis, commonly known as the Menghai pilea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to China, specifically the Yunnan Province, and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.

Pilea menghaiensis prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
