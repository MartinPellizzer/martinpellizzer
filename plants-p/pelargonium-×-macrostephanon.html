Pelargonium × macrostephanon

Pelargonium × macrostephanon, commonly known as the showy geranium, is a hybrid plant that combines traits from various pelargonium species.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a range of colors, including red, pink, and purple. The plant typically has rounded, lobed leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
