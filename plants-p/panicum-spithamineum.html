Panicum spithamineum

Panicum spithamineum, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and produces panicles of red or reddish-brown flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in disturbed soils or open habitats. It is adapted to warm climates and can thrive in full sun.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant flower color and drought tolerance.
