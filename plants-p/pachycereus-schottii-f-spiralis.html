Pachycereus schottii f. spiralis

Pachycereus schottii f. spiralis, commonly known as the spiral cactus, is a unique variant of the pachycereus cactus family.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of stems, which gives it a visually striking appearance. The plant is native to Mexico and thrives in arid environments with well-draining soil. It produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding to its ornamental value.

Due to its slow growth and specific care requirements, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and desert gardens.
