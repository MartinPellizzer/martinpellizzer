Pennisetum glaucocladum

Pennisetum glaucocladum, commonly known as blue oat grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and striking blue-gray foliage. The plant produces fluffy, silver-tan seed heads that add ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscapes.

Pennisetum glaucocladum is often used in xeriscaping and as a background plant in mixed borders.
