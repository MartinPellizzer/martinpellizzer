Polemonium foliosissimum f. alpinum

Polemonium foliosissimum f. alpinum, commonly known as the alpine leafy polemonium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Polemoniaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in alpine meadows and rocky slopes. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with high elevations.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and delicate species.
