Peperomia macrostachyos

Peperomia macrostachyos, commonly known as the large-stemmed peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green oval leaves that are often slightly wavy along the edges. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers arranged in dense clusters, which are typically borne on upright spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its low maintenance and attractive foliage make it a versatile plant for both indoor and outdoor settings.
