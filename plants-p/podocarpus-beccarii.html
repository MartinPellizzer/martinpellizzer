Podocarpus beccarii

Podocarpus beccarii, commonly known as the Bornean podocarp, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Podocarpaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and cylindrical cones. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, well-drained soils. Its bark is thick and scaly, providing protection against environmental stress.

Podocarpus beccarii plays an important ecological role in forest ecosystems, offering habitat and food for various wildlife species.
