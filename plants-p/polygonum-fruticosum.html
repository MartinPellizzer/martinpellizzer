Polygonum fruticosum

Polygonum fruticosum, commonly known as redroot smartweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and can be found in wetlands, ditches, and along stream banks. The plant features upright stems that are reddish in color, especially near the base, and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its small, clustered flowers are pink to reddish in color and bloom from late spring to early summer.

Polygonum fruticosum is often considered a weed but has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
