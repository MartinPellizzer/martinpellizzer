Phreatia plantaginifolia

Phreatia plantaginifolia, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and linear, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Phreatia plantaginifolia is often found in forest understories and is appreciated for its delicate appearance.
