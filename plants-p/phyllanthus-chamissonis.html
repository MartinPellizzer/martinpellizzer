Phyllanthus chamissonis

Phyllanthus chamissonis, commonly known as the California starflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches heights of 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by spherical, greenish fruits. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly waxy texture.

Phyllanthus chamissonis is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
