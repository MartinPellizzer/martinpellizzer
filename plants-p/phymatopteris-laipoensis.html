Phymatopteris laipoensis

Phymatopteris laipoensis, commonly known as the Laipo fern, is a species of polypodioid fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and rocky crevices. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are clustered on the underside of its leaves.

Phymatopteris laipoensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
