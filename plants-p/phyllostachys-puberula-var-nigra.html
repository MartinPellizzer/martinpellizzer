Phyllostachys puberula var. nigra

Phyllostachys puberula var. nigra, commonly known as black bamboo, is a variant of the common bamboo species Phyllostachys puberula.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black culms that contrast with its lighter, often greenish or yellowish leaves. This plant is a clumping bamboo, forming dense stands that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

The black bamboo is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often used in landscaping and as a decorative plant in gardens.
