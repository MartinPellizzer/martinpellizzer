Polygonum pilushanense

Polygonum pilushanense, commonly known as the Pilushan knotweed, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants known for their distinctive arrow-shaped leaves. The plant features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, clustered flowers are typically greenish-white and appear in late summer.

Polygonum pilushanense is adapted to moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
