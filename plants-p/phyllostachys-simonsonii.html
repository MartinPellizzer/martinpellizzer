Phyllostachys simonsonii

Phyllostachys simonsonii, commonly known as the Simonson bamboo, is a species of grass in the family Poaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically green and smooth in texture. Phyllostachys simonsonii is also valued for its use in traditional Chinese medicine and as a source of sustainable materials.
