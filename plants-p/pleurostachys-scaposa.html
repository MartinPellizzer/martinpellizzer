Pleurostachys scaposa

Pleurostachys scaposa, commonly known as the scapose pleurostachy, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, leafless stems that bear clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.

Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists studying orchid diversity.
