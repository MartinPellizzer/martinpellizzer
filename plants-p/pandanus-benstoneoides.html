Pandanus benstoneoides

Pandanus benstoneoides, commonly known as the Benstone pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is a member of the Pandanus genus, characterized by its pinnate leaves and distinctive, spiky inflorescences. The plant typically grows in coastal or wetland areas, thriving in humid, saline environments. Its leaves are long and narrow, often used in traditional crafts and thatching.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in coastal ecosystems.
