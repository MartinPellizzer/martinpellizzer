Perilla cavaleriei

Perilla cavaleriei, commonly known as Chinese basil or Shiso, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes other aromatic herbs like mint and lavender. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional Asian cuisine. Its small purple or white flowers bloom in clusters, adding visual appeal to gardens.

Perilla cavaleriei is also valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in herbal remedies.
