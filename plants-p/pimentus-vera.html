Pimentus vera

Pimentus vera, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom on long, slender stalks rising from water. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface. Its root system supports the growth of its nutrient-rich, edible seeds.

Pimentus vera is also valued for its symbolic significance in various cultures.
