Phyllanthus filipes

Phyllanthus filipes, commonly known as the slender leaf phyllanthus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including India, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its small, simple leaves that are often clustered at the base of the stems. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow-green in color.

Phyllanthus filipes is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and along stream banks.
