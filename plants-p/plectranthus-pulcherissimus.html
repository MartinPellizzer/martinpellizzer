Plectranthus pulcherissimus

Plectranthus pulcherissimus, commonly known as the purple heart plant, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features striking violet to purple leaves that are velvety to the touch, making it a visually appealing ornamental plant. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale purple, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Plectranthus pulcherissimus is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
