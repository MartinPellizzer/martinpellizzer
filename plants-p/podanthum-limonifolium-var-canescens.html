Podanthum limonifolium var. canescens

Podanthum limonifolium var. canescens, commonly known as the silverleaf podanthum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features succulent, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.

The species is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
