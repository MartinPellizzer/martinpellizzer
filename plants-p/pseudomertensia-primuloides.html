Pseudomertensia primuloides

Pseudomertensia primuloides, commonly known as the fairy moss, is a low-growing, evergreen plant native to the coastal regions of southern California and Baja California.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and small, rounded leaves.
