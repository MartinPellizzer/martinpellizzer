Poa nubensis

Poa nubensis, commonly known as the Ethiopian bluegrass, is a species of grass native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and silver-blue foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes. Poa nubensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.

It plays a role in local ecosystems by stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small organisms.
