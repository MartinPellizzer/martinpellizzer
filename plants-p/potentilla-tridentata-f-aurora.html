Potentilla tridentata f. aurora

Potentilla tridentata f. aurora, commonly known as the aurora potentilla, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a compact, low-growing shrub characterized by its trifoliate leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and is adapted to cold, alpine environments. Its foliage is often covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The aurora potentilla is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardiness in harsh climates.
