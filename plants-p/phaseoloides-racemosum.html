Phaseoloides racemosum

Phaseoloides racemosum, commonly known as the racemose bean plant, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions.

It features pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces elongated, racemose inflorescences bearing small white or pale purple flowers. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several elliptical seeds.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in ecological restoration projects.
