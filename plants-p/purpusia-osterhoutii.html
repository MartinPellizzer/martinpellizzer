Purpusia osterhoutii

Purpusia osterhoutii, commonly known as the Osterhout's lily, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its showy, fragrant white to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.
