Plumeria inaguensis

Plumeria inaguensis, commonly known as the Inagua plumeria, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and tropical gardens.

The Inagua plumeria is also valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in some island cultures.
