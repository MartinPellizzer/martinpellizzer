Peperomia lasiostigma var. tomaniviensis

Peperomia lasiostigma var. tomaniviensis, commonly known as the Tomaniviensis Peperomia, is a compact, low-growing perennial shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture, often adorned with subtle variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container cultivation.
