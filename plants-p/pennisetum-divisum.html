Pennisetum divisum

Pennisetum divisum, commonly known as the divided feathergrass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and fluffy, silver-gray plumes that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping and rock gardens. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a silver sheen that adds visual interest.

Pennisetum divisum is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements in landscaping.
