Potentilla hirta f. cavarnana

Potentilla hirta f. cavarnana, commonly known as the hairy cinquefoil, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves with a serrated edge. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is typically found in rocky or sandy soils in open habitats.

This subspecies is often used in landscaping for its hardy nature and ornamental value.
