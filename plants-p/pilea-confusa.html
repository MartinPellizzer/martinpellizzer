Pilea confusa

Pilea confusa, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a silvery underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, followed by round, yellowish fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Its unique leaf shape and ease of care contribute to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
