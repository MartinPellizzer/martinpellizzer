Parodia tojoensis

Parodia tojoensis, commonly known as the Tjoense Spine Cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and is often covered in spines that vary in color from white to yellow. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth rate, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
