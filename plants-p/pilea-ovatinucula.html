Pilea ovatinucula

Pilea ovatinucula, commonly known as the rounded pilea, is a compact, evergreen houseplant native to Southeast Asia.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a subtle sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Pilea ovatinucula is prized for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
