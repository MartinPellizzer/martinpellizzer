Paphiopedilum callosum var. angustipetalum

Paphiopedilum callosum var. angustipetalum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled velvet orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a distinctive, showy flower that features a pouch-like lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and has lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to green with red or brown markings.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
