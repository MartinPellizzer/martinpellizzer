Pulicaria albida

Pulicaria albida, commonly known as white pulicaria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features silver-gray foliage with small, narrow leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow and occur in clusters, blooming from late spring to early summer.

Pulicaria albida thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.
