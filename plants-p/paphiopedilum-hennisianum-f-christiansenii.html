Paphiopedilum hennisianum f. christiansenii

Paphiopedilum hennisianum f. christiansenii, commonly known as the Hennisianum christiansenii orchid, is a unique hybrid with distinctive botanical features.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate, showy flowers with a pouch-like structure known as a resupinate labellum. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial or epiphytic orchid, depending on its habitat, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually dark green and leathery, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant floral display.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty and rarity in horticultural collections.
