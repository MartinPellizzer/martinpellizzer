Phyllostachys bambusoides var. quilioi

Phyllostachys bambusoides var. quilioi, commonly known as the Kikuyu bamboo, is a clumping bamboo native to eastern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that grow in tight clusters, forming dense stands. The leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and have a bluish-green color with a slight waxy coating. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for ornamental and ecological landscaping.

This variety is valued for its aesthetic appeal and ability to stabilize soil in marginal environments.
