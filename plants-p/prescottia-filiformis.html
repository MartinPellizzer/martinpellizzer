Prescottia filiformis

Prescottia filiformis, commonly known as the slender spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, filamentous stems and its unique, spider-like appearance due to its elongated, thread-like leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in a raceme, often blooming during the summer months. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is typically found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Due to its delicate nature, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or specialized gardens.
