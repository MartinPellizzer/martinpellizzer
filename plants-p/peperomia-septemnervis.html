Peperomia septemnervis

Peperomia septemnervis, commonly known as the seven-veined peperomia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, succulent-like foliage. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, with a waxy texture and a variety of colorations, ranging from green to reddish hues. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish, and it thrives in bright, indirect light with well-draining soil.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
