Paradrymonia lacera

Paradrymonia lacera, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a tropical shrub belonging to the Malvaceae family, characterized by its palmate leaves and showy, yellowish-white flowers. The species is notable for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions and its use in traditional medicine in some parts of its native range.
