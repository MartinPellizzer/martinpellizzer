Pelargonium wonchiense

Pelargonium wonchiense, commonly known as the Wonchi geranium, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, showy flowers. The plant typically features velvety, lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant flower colors, which range from pink to red.
