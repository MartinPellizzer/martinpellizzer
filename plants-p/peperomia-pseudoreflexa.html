Peperomia pseudoreflexa

Peperomia pseudoreflexa, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and a deep red or purple underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that are typically borne in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique leaf coloration and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable ornamental plant.
