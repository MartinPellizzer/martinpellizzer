Pandanus brevistylis

Pandanus brevistylis, commonly known as the short-styled pandanus, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Pandanaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or white in color. It thrives in coastal and wetland environments, often growing in shallow water or on sandy soils.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has cultural significance in some island communities.
