Peperomia chlorostachya

Peperomia chlorostachya, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to South America.

It features glossy, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a silvery or white underside, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white, often hidden among the foliage. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and tolerance for dry conditions.

Peperomia chlorostachya is popular as a houseplant for its ornamental value and ease of care.
