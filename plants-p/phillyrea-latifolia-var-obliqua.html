Phillyrea latifolia var. obliqua

Phillyrea latifolia var. obliqua, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Phillyrea, is a shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, elliptical leaves that are often twisted or curled, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, woody fruits. It is known for its aromatic foliage and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.

Phillyrea latifolia var. obliqua is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils.
