Phyllostegia lanaiensis

Phyllostegia lanaiensis, commonly known as the Lanai ironwood, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian island of Lanai.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue or purple, arranged in whorls along the stems. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

Phyllostegia lanaiensis is well adapted to the dry, rocky environments of its native habitat.
