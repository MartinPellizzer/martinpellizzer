Potamogeton zosteriformis

Potamogeton zosteriformis, commonly known as the ribbon grass or narrow-leaf pondweed, is a submerged aquatic plant native to freshwater environments in North America.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically found in clusters near the water surface.
