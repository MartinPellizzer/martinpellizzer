Peliosanthes pachystachya

Peliosanthes pachystachya, commonly known as the giant sago palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers in branched clusters, followed by small, round fruits. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and as a source of fiber.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in warm climates around the world.
