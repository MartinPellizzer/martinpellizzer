Psorodendron kingii

It is characterized by its unique, spiny stems and small, clustered leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in arid or semi-arid environments, adapting to poor soil conditions through its deep root system.
