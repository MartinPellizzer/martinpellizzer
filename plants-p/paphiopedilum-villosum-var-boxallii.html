Paphiopedilum villosum var. boxallii

Paphiopedilum villosum var. boxallii, commonly known as the Boxall's velvet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and pendulous flowers with intricate, lip-like structures. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish with a distinctive, spurred lip.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique appearance and fragrance.
