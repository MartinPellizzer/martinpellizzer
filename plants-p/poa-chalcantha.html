Poa chalcantha

Poa chalcantha, commonly known as silver hair grass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-blue foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed sites such as roadsides and meadows. Its inflorescence consists of a dense, spike-like cluster of small, spikelets.

Poa chalcantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and restoration projects.
