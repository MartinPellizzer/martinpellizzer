Peperomia woodsonii

Peperomia woodsonii, commonly known as the Woodson's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia woodsonii is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
