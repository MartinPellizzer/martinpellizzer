Plantago adriatica

Plantago adriatica, commonly known as Adriatic plantain, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal areas and dry soils. The plant has broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish flowers in clusters. It is known for its medicinal properties, often used in traditional remedies.

The species is also valued for its ability to thrive in harsh environments.
