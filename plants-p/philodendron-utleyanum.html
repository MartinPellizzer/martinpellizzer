Philodendron utleyanum

Philodendron utleyanum, commonly known as the Utleyanum philodendron, is a tropical vine native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy, dark green leaves that often have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by berries that turn from green to red as they mature. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Philodendron utleyanum is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
