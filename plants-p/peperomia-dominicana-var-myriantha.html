Peperomia dominicana var. myriantha

Peperomia dominicana var. myriantha, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Central America.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and a slightly waxy appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to both novice and experienced plant collectors.
