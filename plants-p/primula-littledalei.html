Primula littledalei

Primula littledalei, commonly known as the Littledale's primrose, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 15 to 30 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. Its leaves are typically basal, broad, and slightly hairy, with a rounded or heart-shaped form.

It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or shaded forest habitats.
