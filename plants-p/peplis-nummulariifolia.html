Peplis nummulariifolia

Peplis nummulariifolia, commonly known as the silver dollar plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features small, round, silver-gray leaves that are densely packed, giving it a compact appearance. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
