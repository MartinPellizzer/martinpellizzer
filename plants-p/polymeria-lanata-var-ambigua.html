Polymeria lanata var. ambigua

Polymeria lanata var. ambigua, commonly known as the woolly candelabra, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in candelabra-like clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a notable species in botanical studies.
