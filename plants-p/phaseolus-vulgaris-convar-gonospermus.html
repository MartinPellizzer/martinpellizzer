Phaseolus vulgaris convar. gonospermus

Phaseolus vulgaris convar. gonospermus, commonly known as the scarlet runner bean, is a variant of the common bean species native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers and elongated, cylindrical pods that contain edible seeds. The plant is a herbaceous annual that thrives in warm climates and well-drained soils. Its leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being heart-shaped and slightly hairy.

The scarlet runner bean is often cultivated for both its ornamental value and its nutritious, flavorful beans.
