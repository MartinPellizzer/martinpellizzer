Pseuderanthemum melanesicum

Pseuderanthemum melanesicum, commonly known as the Melanesian pseuderanthemum, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

It is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its vibrant floral display and adaptability to various growing conditions.
