Perymenium jaliscense

Perymenium jaliscense, commonly known as the Jalisco thistle, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features spiny leaves and produces purple to pink flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in arid regions of southern Mexico.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
