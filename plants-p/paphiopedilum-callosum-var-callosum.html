Paphiopedilum callosum var. callosum

Paphiopedilum callosum var. callosum, commonly known as the Chinese velvet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a thick, fleshy rhizome and hairy pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, lip-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and green, often with a distinctive purple or brown callus. Its leaves are broad, ovate, and covered in a dense layer of fine, velvety hairs.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique appearance and fragrance.
