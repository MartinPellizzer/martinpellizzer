Phyllostachys sulphurea

Phyllostachys sulphurea, commonly known as the yellow bamboo, is a species of grass-like plant native to China.

It is characterized by its striking yellow-green culms and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. This bamboo thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and landscapes.

The plant is also used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
