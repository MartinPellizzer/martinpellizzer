Potentilla recta f. polychaeta

Potentilla recta f. polychaeta, commonly known as the hairy potentilla, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, finely hairy stems. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a serrated margin and are covered in fine hairs.

This variety is often found in rocky or gravelly soils in temperate regions.
