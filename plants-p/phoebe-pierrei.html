Phoebe pierrei

Phoebe pierrei, commonly known as the rock phoebe, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments. It is a shrub that can reach heights of up to 3 feet, with a compact and dense growth habit.

The rock phoebe is well adapted to harsh conditions, making it a hardy and resilient species.
