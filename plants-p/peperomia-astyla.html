Peperomia astyla

Peperomia astyla, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery underside, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia astyla is ideal for indoor cultivation due to its resilience and aesthetic appeal.
