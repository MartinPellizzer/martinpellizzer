Pentzia suffruticosa

Pentzia suffruticosa, commonly known as the Cape rose, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain seeds dispersed by animals. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Pentzia suffruticosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
