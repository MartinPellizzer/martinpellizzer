Pelargonium × rainerianum

Pelargonium × rainerianum, commonly known as the 'Rainer' geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, dark green leaves and clusters of bright pink to red flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant is native to South Africa and is widely cultivated as an ornamental due to its attractive appearance and ease of care. It prefers full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and container planting.

Its compact growth habit and long flowering period make it a popular choice for borders and window boxes.
