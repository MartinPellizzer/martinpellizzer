Phreatia polyantha

Phreatia polyantha, commonly known as the orchid of the rainforest, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with a complex structure typical of the orchid family. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil and air.

Phreatia polyantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
