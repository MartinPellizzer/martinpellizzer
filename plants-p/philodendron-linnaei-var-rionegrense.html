Philodendron linnaei var. rionegrense

Philodendron linnaei var. rionegrense, commonly known as the Rionegrense Philodendron, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins, often adorned with a distinctive reddish or maroon tinge on the underside. This variety is known for its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers, though it is primarily cultivated for its ornamental foliage.

Its unique leaf patterns and ease of care contribute to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
