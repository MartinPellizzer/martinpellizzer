Pilea nipensis

Pilea nipensis, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its rosette of glossy, round leaves that resemble coins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.

Pilea nipensis thrives in indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.
