Piper pseudoblattarum

Piper pseudoblattarum, commonly known as the false blattarum pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its vine-like growth habit and small, opposite leaves. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.
