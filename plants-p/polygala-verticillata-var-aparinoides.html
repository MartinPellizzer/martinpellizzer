Polygala verticillata var. aparinoides

Polygala verticillata var. aparinoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved milkwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
