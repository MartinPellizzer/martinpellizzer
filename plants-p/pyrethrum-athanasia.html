Pyrethrum athanasia

Pyrethrum athanasia, commonly known as the everlasting daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant is known for its long-lasting blooms, which remain intact for extended periods, hence its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Pyrethrum athanasia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in floral arrangements.
