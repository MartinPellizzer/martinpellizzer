Pterolobium indicum var. macropterum

Pterolobium indicum var. macropterum, commonly known as the large-winged pterolobium, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its leguminous structure and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant features compound leaves with elongated, pinnate arrangements and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white or pale yellow. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.

This species is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and shrublands.
