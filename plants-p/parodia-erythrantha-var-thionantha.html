Parodia erythrantha var. thionantha

Parodia erythrantha var. thionantha, commonly known as the "Red-Fleshed Barrel Cactus," is a compact, spherical cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a ribbed, cylindrical stem that is typically green with reddish or pinkish hues, especially when exposed to sunlight. The plant produces small, vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking contrast to its green body. Its thick, fleshy stems are adapted for water storage, making it highly drought-tolerant.

This variety is prized in horticulture for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
