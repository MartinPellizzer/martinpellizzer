Peperomia apoana

Peperomia apoana, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale green, arranged in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia apoana is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive foliage.
