Poa ianthoides

Poa ianthoides, commonly known as the silver hair grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern South America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and tufted growth habit, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of spikelets, which are often tinged with a bluish hue.

Poa ianthoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
