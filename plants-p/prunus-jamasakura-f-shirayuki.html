Prunus jamasakura f. shirayuki

Prunus jamasakura f. shirayuki, commonly known as the Shirayuki cherry, is a cultivar of the Japanese cherry tree.

It is characterized by its pale pink to white flowers, which bloom in early spring. The tree has a compact, rounded shape with dense foliage. It is highly valued for its ornamental beauty in gardens and landscapes.

This variety is particularly noted for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
