Pelargonium × puniceum

Pelargonium × puniceum, commonly known as the scented pelargonium, is a hybrid shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and broad, leathery leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically in spring and summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value and pleasant scent, it is a popular choice for garden use.
