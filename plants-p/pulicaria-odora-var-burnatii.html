Pulicaria odora var. burnatii

Pulicaria odora var. burnatii, commonly known as sweet everlasting, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, silver-green leaves with a woolly texture and produces small, yellowish-white flowers in summer. The plant is characterized by its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun. Its common name reflects the pleasant scent released when the leaves are crushed.

It is often used in herbal medicine and as a decorative plant in gardens.
