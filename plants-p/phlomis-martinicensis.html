Phlomis martinicensis

Phlomis martinicensis, commonly known as the Martinique blazing star, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its foliage is often covered in fine hairs, giving it a slightly fuzzy appearance.
