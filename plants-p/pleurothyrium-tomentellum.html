Pleurothyrium tomentellum

Pleurothyrium tomentellum, commonly known as the woolly pleurothyrium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in humid, shaded environments within the understory of forests. The plant is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage, which gives it a velvety appearance. Its leaves are usually alternate, simple, and have a broad ovate shape.

The flowers are small and clustered, often with white or pale yellow petals, and it produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds.
