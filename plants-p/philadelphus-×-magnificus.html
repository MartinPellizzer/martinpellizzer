Philadelphus × magnificus

Philadelphus × magnificus, commonly known as the magnificent mock orange, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, simple, and serrated leaves that are typically dark green in color. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a versatile addition to gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its showy blooms and pleasant fragrance.
