Philodendron effusilobum

Philodendron effusilobum, commonly known as the velvet-leaf philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a decorative plant.
