Primula Villosa Var. Latifolia

Primula villosa var. latifolia is a unique and charming plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia. It is known for its striking, broad leaves and delicate, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. This variety is a popular choice among gardeners for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant. Its foliage and flowers add both texture and color to any garden setting.
