Prismatomeris albiflora var. javanica

Prismatomeris albiflora var. javanica, commonly known as the Javanese white-flowered prismatomeris, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, white petals that bloom in clusters, giving it a striking appearance. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in moist, well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a smooth texture, while the stems are slender and often covered in fine hairs.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
