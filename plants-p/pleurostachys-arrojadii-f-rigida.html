Pleurostachys arrojadii f. rigida

Pleurostachys arrojadii f. rigida, commonly known as the rigid pleurostachys, is a unique variant of the pleurostachys genus within the orchid family.

This plant is characterized by its stiff, upright growth habit and dense, compact foliage. Its leaves are typically narrow and have a leathery texture, which helps it conserve moisture in arid environments. The inflorescence consists of a single, elongated spike bearing small, white to pale yellow flowers.

It is native to specific regions in Mexico and is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to semi-shade conditions.
