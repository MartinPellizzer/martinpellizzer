Pycreus bathiei

Pycreus bathiei, commonly known as the Madagascar star cactus, is a unique succulent native to Madagascar.

It features a short, branching stem with clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to its arid environment. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native region.
