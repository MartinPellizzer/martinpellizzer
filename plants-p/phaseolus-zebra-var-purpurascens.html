Phaseolus zebra var. purpurascens

Phaseolus zebra var. purpurascens, commonly known as the purple zebra bean, is a unique cultivar of the common bean genus.

It is characterized by its striking bicolor pattern of green and purple on its pod and leaves. The plant is a climbing vine that produces elongated, ridged pods containing edible seeds. Its foliage is typically a deep green with a slightly hairy texture.

This variety is valued for both its ornamental appeal and its potential as a food crop in certain regions.
