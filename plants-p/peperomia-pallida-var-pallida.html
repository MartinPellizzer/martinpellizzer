Peperomia pallida var. pallida

Peperomia pallida var. pallida, commonly known as the pale peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features succulent, oval-shaped leaves that are typically pale green to bluish-gray in color, often with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green or red, which are usually hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia pallida var. pallida is prized for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
