Piper belicense

Piper belicense, commonly known as the Belize pepper, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when ripe, containing the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ornamental and medicinal purposes.
