Passerina tarton-raira f. virescens

Passerina tarton-raira f. virescens, commonly known as the green-flowered passerina, is a unique variant of the passerina genus.

It is characterized by its striking greenish floral hues, which distinguish it from other members of the species. The plant typically features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Its botanical significance lies in its distinct coloration and potential role in ecological interactions within its habitat.
