Potentilla fragarioides var. yamanakae

Potentilla fragarioides var. yamanakae, commonly known as the Yamanakae cinquefoil, is a perennial shrub native to Japan.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with five leaflets, giving it the common name "cinquefoil.". It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
