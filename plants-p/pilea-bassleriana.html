Pilea bassleriana

Pilea bassleriana, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its rosette of rounded, fleshy leaves that resemble small coins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.

Pilea bassleriana thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.
