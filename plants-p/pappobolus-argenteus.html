Pappobolus argenteus

Pappobolus argenteus, commonly known as the silver pappobolus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Pappobolus argenteus is often found in open grasslands and along roadsides, where it contributes to local biodiversity.
