Peperomia angustifolia

Peperomia angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal watering needs.

Peperomia angustifolia is valued for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
