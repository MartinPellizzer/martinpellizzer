Palmijuncus haenkeanus

Palmijuncus haenkeanus, commonly known as the Brazilian palm, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
