Peperomia ciliatifolia var. eciliatifolia

Peperomia ciliatifolia var. eciliatifolia, commonly known as the velvety peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are velvety to the touch and have a distinctive ciliate margin, giving it its scientific name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale pink, emerging from leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant.
