Peperomia transparens

Peperomia transparens, commonly known as the glassy peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to Brazil.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a translucent, waxy texture that gives it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its attractive foliage and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
