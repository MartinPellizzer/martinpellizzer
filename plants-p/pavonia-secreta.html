Pavonia secreta

Pavonia secreta, commonly known as the secret pavonia, is a rare flowering plant native to the humid undergrowth of the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety purple petals and serrated, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that bloom during the early morning hours. Its unique habitat and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to its elusive nature, very little is known about its reproductive habits and ecological role.
