Premna clavata

Premna clavata, commonly known as the clustered premna, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces and small, clustered white flowers. Its fruits are small, fleshy berries that turn red when mature.

Premna clavata is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
