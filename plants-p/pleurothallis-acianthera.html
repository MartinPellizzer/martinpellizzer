Pleurothallis acianthera

Pleurothallis acianthera, commonly known as the violet orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features slender, wiry stems and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of violet and white. Its leaves are narrow and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Pleurothallis acianthera is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
