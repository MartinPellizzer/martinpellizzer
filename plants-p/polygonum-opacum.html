Polygonum opacum

Polygonum opacum, commonly known as smooth knotweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist to wet environments such as marshes, swamps, and stream banks. The plant has opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are smooth and often covered in a waxy coating. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences.

Polygonum opacum is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed or saturated soils.
