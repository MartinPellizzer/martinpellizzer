Primula thearosa

Primula thearosa, commonly known as the Chinese primrose, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10-15 centimeters, with lance-shaped leaves that are slightly hairy. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.

The flowers bloom in early spring, adding a touch of color to the landscape during the cooler months.
