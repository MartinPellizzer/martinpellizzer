Pilea huegelii

Pilea huegelii, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features round, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slight sheen, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in spring, followed by spherical, green fruit that turns red as it matures. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and ease of care contribute to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
