Panicum firmandum

Panicum firmandum, commonly known as the rigid panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental use in gardens.

Panicum firmandum is often used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and ability to form dense, attractive clumps.
