Pilea petiolaris subsp. pseudopetiolaris

Pilea petiolaris subsp.

pseudopetiolaris, commonly known as the Shiny Pilea, is a compact, low-maintenance houseplant native to Southeast Asia. It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green, with a slightly waxy texture.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters, followed by edible, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate watering.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
