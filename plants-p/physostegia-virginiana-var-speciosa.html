Physostegia virginiana var. speciosa

Physostegia virginiana var. speciosa, commonly known as the showy lady's slipper, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple, which bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stem. It thrives in moist, shady woodlands and is often found in rich, well-drained soils.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
