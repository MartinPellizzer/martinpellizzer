Photinia Arguta Var. Salicifolia

Photinia arguta var. salicifolia is a striking ornamental shrub known for its vibrant red foliage and elegant form. Native to China, this plant thrives in temperate climates and is prized for its seasonal color changes. Its lance-shaped leaves turn brilliant shades of red in autumn, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes. The plant is relatively easy to care for, requiring well-drained soil and full to partial sun. With its unique appearance and seasonal beauty, it adds both visual interest and ecological value to any garden.
