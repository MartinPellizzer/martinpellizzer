Peperomia gracilis

Peperomia gracilis, commonly known as the slender peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a neat and compact appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pale green flowers that emerge from the leaf axils, typical of the Piperaceae family. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia gracilis is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and tolerating a range of indoor conditions.
