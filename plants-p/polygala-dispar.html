Polygala dispar

Polygala dispar, commonly known as the slender milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb with slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers arranged in spike-like clusters. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are opposite and linear, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Polygala dispar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
