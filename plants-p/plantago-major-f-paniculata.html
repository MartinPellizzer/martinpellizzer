Plantago major f. paniculata

Plantago major f. paniculata, commonly known as the common plantain with panicle inflorescence, is a variant of the common plantain.

It is a perennial herb characterized by broad, oval leaves with a hairy surface. The plant produces a tall, branched inflorescence of small, greenish flowers. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in lawns, roadsides, and gardens.

This variant is notable for its adaptability and widespread distribution across temperate regions.
