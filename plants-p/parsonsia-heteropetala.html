Parsonsia heteropetala

Parsonsia heteropetala, commonly known as the variable flame tree, is a striking tropical shrub or small tree native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its showy, orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, often in summer. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Parsonsia heteropetala is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
