Parietaria elliptica

Parietaria elliptica, commonly known as the smooth wallwort, is a flowering plant native to Europe, North Africa, and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving them a velvety texture. Its small, greenish-white flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom from spring to early summer.

Parietaria elliptica thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found growing on walls, in gardens, and along stream banks.
