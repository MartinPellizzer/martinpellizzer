Pogonophyllum

Pogonophyllum, commonly known as the beardleaf plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Amaranthaceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, leaf-like structures that resemble a beard, giving it its common name. The flowers are usually small and clustered, with showy bracts that attract pollinators.
