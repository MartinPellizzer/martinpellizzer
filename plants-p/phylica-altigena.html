Phylica altigena

Phylica altigena, commonly known as the silver tree, is a shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, white or yellowish flowers that bloom in winter, followed by red, edible berries. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showing good drought tolerance.

The silver tree is often used in traditional medicine and has ornamental value due to its unique appearance.
