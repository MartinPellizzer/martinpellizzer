Philodendron karstenianum var. callifolium

Philodendron karstenianum var. callifolium, commonly known as the Variegated Philodendron, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves that often feature striking variegation of white and green. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its foliage can reach up to 12 inches in length, making it a visually appealing houseplant.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and humidity to flourish.
