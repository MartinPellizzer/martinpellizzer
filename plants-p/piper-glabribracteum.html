Piper glabribracteum

Piper glabribracteum, commonly known as the smooth-bract pepper, is a flowering plant belonging to the Piperaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features compound leaves with elongated, serrated leaflets and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers. Its fruits are berries that turn red when mature, containing several seeds.

Piper glabribracteum is valued for its aromatic properties and is used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic effects.
