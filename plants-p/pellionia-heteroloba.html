Pellionia heteroloba

Pellionia heteroloba, commonly known as the velvetleaf pellionia, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Pellionia heteroloba thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental leaves and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
