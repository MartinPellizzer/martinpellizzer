Phalaenopsis denisiana

Phalaenopsis denisiana, commonly known as the Denisiana orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. Its flowers are similar to those of other Phalaenopsis species, with a delicate, moth-like appearance.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
