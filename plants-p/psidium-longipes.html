Psidium longipes

Psidium longipes, commonly known as the longipes guava, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are followed by edible berries. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential use in horticulture.
