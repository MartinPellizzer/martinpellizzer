Pelargonium × germanum

Pelargonium × germanum, commonly known as the German geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to red. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with a slightly serrated edge, and the plant is known for its aromatic foliage. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

It is often cultivated as a garden ornamental due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
