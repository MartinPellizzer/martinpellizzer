Populus wobstii

Populus wobstii, commonly known as the Wobst's poplar, is a rare and critically endangered species native to the mountainous regions of central China.

The plant belongs to the Salicaceae family, which includes willows and poplars, and is known for its rapid growth and ability to colonize disturbed sites.
