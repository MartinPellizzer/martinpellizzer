Pilea rufa var. microstipula

Pilea rufa var. microstipula, commonly known as the velvet plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and prominent reddish veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by round, red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements contribute to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
