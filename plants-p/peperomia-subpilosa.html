Peperomia subpilosa

Peperomia subpilosa, commonly known as the woolly peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, velvety foliage with a distinctive grayish-green hue, giving it a unique visual appeal. The plant has small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, which appear during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia subpilosa is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
