Parthenium argentatum f. patens

Parthenium argentatum f. patens, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a variant of the silverleaf plant native to the southern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands. The plant features silver-gray foliage due to dense pubescence, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in leaf axils, blooming in late spring to early summer.

This species is known for its potential medicinal properties and has been studied for its use in traditional herbal remedies.
