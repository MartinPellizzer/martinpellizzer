Pelargonium × jeschekianum

Pelargonium × jeschekianum, commonly known as the German geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated edge, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Pelargonium × jeschekianum is a popular choice for garden borders and containers due to its compact size and long blooming season.
