Prenanthes tatarinowii subsp. macrantha

Prenanthes tatarinowii subsp.

macrantha, commonly known as the tall pearly everlasting, is a flowering plant native to North America. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers.

The plant produces pappus-based seeds, which are adapted for wind dispersal. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.

Its foliage is typically lance-shaped with a rough, hairy texture.
