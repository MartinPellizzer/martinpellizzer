Pittosporum makinoi

Pittosporum makinoi, commonly known as the Japanese pittosporum, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features elliptical, dark green leaves that turn reddish-brown in autumn. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in spring, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for ornamental gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability.
