Populus × xiaozhuanica

Populus × xiaozhuanica, commonly known as the Xiaozhuan poplar, is a hybrid species within the Populus genus.

It is characterized by its fast growth rate and adaptability to various environmental conditions. The tree typically has smooth, gray bark and heart-shaped leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It produces small, catkin-like flowers in the spring, which are wind-pollinated.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ecological benefits in reforestation projects.
