Phyllostachys nigra f. punctata

Phyllostachys nigra f. punctata, commonly known as the black bamboo with spots, is a variant of the black bamboo species.

It is characterized by its dark green to black stems that are adorned with distinctive white or yellow spots. This ornamental bamboo is native to eastern Asia and thrives in temperate climates. The plant has a clumping growth habit and produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design.
