Polygala ciliatifolia var. mundtiana

Polygala ciliatifolia var. mundtiana, commonly known as the narrow-leaved milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, branching stems and small, greenish-white flowers arranged in umbels. The leaves are narrow and often have a hairy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local insect populations.
