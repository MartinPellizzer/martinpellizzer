Peucedanum acuminatum

Peucedanum acuminatum, commonly known as Chinese cowparsley, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like carrots and parsley. The plant features slender, hollow stems and finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often hairy. Its small, yellowish-green flowers are clustered in umbels, typical of the carrot family.
