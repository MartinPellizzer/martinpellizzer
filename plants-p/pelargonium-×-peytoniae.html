Pelargonium × peytoniae

Pelargonium × peytoniae, commonly known as the Peytonia geranium, is a hybrid pelargonium species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, bicolor flowers that range from white to pink and red. The plant features finely cut, lobed leaves that are often velvety to the touch. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Pelargonium × peytoniae is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
