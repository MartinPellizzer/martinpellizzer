Polypodium feuillei f. hastifolium

Polypodium feuillei f. hastifolium, commonly known as the broad-leaved wall fern, is a fern species characterized by its robust, leathery fronds.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia. The plant features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. Its rhizome is thick and often covered in a layer of brown, fibrous material.

This fern thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found growing on rocky surfaces or in dense forest undergrowth.
