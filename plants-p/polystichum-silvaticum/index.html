Polystichum Silvaticum

Polystichum silvaticum is a hardy fern native to temperate regions of the northern hemisphere. It thrives in shady, moist environments such as forests and woodlands. Its feathery fronds are dark green and often have a silvery underside. This plant is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in low-light conditions. It plays an important role in ecosystem stability by contributing to soil health and habitat diversity.
