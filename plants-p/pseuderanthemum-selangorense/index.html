Pseuderanthemum Selangorense

Pseuderanthemum selangorense is a unique flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It is known for its striking appearance and adaptability to various environmental conditions. This species belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is often found in shaded, humid areas. Its foliage and blooms make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to the general public.
