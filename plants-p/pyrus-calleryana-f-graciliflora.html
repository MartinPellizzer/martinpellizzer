Pyrus calleryana f. graciliflora

Pyrus calleryana f. graciliflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered Chinese white pear, is a cultivar of the Chinese white pear tree.

It is characterized by its delicate, slender flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant is a deciduous tree with a rounded, often multi-stemmed growth habit. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the pale flowers.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic beauty.
