Patrinia palmata

Patrinia palmata, commonly known as the broadleaf Patrinia, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its opposite, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, blue-violet flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
