Piper unguiculiferum

Piper unguiculiferum, commonly known as the horned pepper, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, horn-like calyx that encloses its small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, peppercorn-like fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly aromatic scent.

Piper unguiculiferum is valued for both its ornamental appearance and its potential medicinal properties.
