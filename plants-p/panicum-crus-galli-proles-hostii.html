Panicum crus-galli proles hostii

Panicum crus-galli proles hostii, commonly known as the hostii panic grass, is a grass species native to Central America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often reddish or purplish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable ornamental plant in gardens.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in plant taxonomy and ecology.
