Peperomia mercedana

Peperomia mercedana, commonly known as the Mercedana Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Mexico.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its low maintenance and attractive foliage make it a versatile plant for both novice and experienced growers.
