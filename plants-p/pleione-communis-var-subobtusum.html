Pleione communis var. subobtusum

Pleione communis var. subobtusum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pleione, is a species of orchid native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking, showy flowers with a mix of white and yellow petals. The plant typically blooms in late winter or early spring, making it a sought-after addition to alpine gardens. Its leaves are narrow and strap-like, and it prefers cool, humid conditions with well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
