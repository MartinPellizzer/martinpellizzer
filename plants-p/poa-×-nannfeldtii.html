Poa × nannfeldtii

Poa × nannfeldtii, commonly known as the Nannfeldt’s bluegrass, is a hybrid grass species native to southern South America.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and silver-green foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically forms dense, low-growing mats and is often found in moist, temperate environments. Its inflorescence consists of compact, spike-like clusters of small, blue-green flowers.

Poa × nannfeldtii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping for its aesthetic and ecological benefits.
