Pilea michaelensis

Pilea michaelensis, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rosettes of thick, waxy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in early spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea michaelensis is known for its ease of care and ability to propagate through stem cuttings.
