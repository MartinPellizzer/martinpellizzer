Pithecellobium sabanense

Pithecellobium sabanense, commonly known as the Sabanese burr legume, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

Pithecellobium sabanense is often found in disturbed habitats and is valued for its hardy nature and potential ecological benefits.
