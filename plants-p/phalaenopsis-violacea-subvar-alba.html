Phalaenopsis violacea subvar. alba

Phalaenopsis violacea subvar. alba, commonly known as the white phalaenopsis orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender stems and clusters of white, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining potting media.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
