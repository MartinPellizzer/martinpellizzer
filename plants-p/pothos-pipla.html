Pothos pipla

Pothos pipla, commonly known as the silver vine, is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of silver and green. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by red berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Pothos pipla is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
