Phymatopteris rotunda

Phymatopteris rotunda, commonly known as the round spleenwort, is a small, epiphytic fern belonging to the family Pterobryaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and possesses a single, short, erect stem with a cluster of leaves. The leaves are ovate to elliptical in shape, with a smooth margin and a glossy, dark green color. It typically grows on tree trunks or rocks in moist, shaded environments.

This species is native to parts of North America and is often found in forested areas with high humidity.
