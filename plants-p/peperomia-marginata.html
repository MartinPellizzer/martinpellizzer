Peperomia marginata

Peperomia marginata, commonly known as the marginata peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, elongated leaves with striking silver or white margins, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia marginata is valued for its ornamental foliage and air-purifying qualities.
