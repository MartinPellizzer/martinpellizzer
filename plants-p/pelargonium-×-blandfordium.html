Pelargonium × blandfordium

Pelargonium × blandfordium, commonly known as the Blandford's geranium, is a hybrid perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features upright stems and dense clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This plant is known for its long blooming season, adding vibrant color to gardens from spring through early autumn.
