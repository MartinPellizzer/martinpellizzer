Pittosporum lanatum

Pittosporum lanatum, commonly known as the silky pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-white foliage that gives it a distinctive, almost velvety appearance. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that are typically followed by small, red berries. It thrives in coastal areas and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Pittosporum lanatum is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for native wildlife.
