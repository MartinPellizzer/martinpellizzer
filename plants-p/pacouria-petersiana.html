Pacouria petersiana

Pacouria petersiana, commonly known as the Petersian pincushion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, spherical inflorescence composed of numerous small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun conditions. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Pacouria petersiana is also valued for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to arid environments.
