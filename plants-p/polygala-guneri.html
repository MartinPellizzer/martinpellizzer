Polygala guneri

Polygala guneri, commonly known as the silver leaf polygala, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, pale green to silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces delicate, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is cultivated in some botanical gardens.
