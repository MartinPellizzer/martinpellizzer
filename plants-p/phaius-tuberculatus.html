Phaius tuberculatus

Phaius tuberculatus, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its distinctive zigzagging flower stems. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-green flowers with prominent yellow lips. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Phaius tuberculatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
