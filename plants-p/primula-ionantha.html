Primula ionantha

Primula ionantha, commonly known as the Sierra Madre primrose, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, preferring cool and moist conditions. Its leaves are broad and slightly hairy, adapted to retain moisture in its native environment.

Primula ionantha is a perennial species that blooms in spring, adding a vibrant touch to its natural habitat.
