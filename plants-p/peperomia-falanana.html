Peperomia falanana

Peperomia falanana, commonly known as the Falanana Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features rounded, glossy leaves that are typically a deep green, often with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia falanana is valued for its ornamental foliage and air-purifying qualities.
