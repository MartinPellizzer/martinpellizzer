Pulmonaria tuberosa subsp. angustifolia

Pulmonaria tuberosa subsp.

angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf lungwort, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe. It features basal rosettes of lanceolate, hairy leaves that are often tinged with silver or purple.

The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple, typically blooming in early spring. It thrives in moist, partially shaded environments and is often found in woodland or meadow habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking shade-tolerant plants.
