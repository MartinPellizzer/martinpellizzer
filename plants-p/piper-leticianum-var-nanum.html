Piper leticianum var. nanum

Piper leticianum var. nanum, commonly known as dwarf pepper plant, is a compact shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its small, rounded leaves and clusters of tiny, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces small, peppery fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is popular as a ornamental plant due to its dense foliage.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to tropical gardens and greenhouse collections.
