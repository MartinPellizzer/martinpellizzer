Polystichum triangulum var. laxum

Polystichum triangulum var. laxum, commonly known as the loose fern, is a variant of the common wood fern.

It is a perennial evergreen fern that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The fronds are bi- or tri-pinnate, with a distinctive triangular shape and a loose, airy appearance. It has dark green, leathery leaves that are often used in horticultural settings for their ornamental value.

This fern is well-suited for woodland gardens and can thrive in USDA hardiness zones 4 through 8.
