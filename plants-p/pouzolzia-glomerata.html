Pouzolzia glomerata

Pouzolzia glomerata, commonly known as the clustered pouzolzia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its upright growth habit and dense clusters of small, greenish-white flowers.
