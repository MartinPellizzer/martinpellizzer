Parashorea stellata

Parashorea stellata, commonly known as the star-leaf tamanu, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dipterocarpaceae and is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in lowland rainforests and is valued for its hard, durable wood. It also produces small, fragrant flowers that are pollinated by insects.
