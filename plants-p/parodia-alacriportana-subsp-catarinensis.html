Parodia alacriportana subsp. catarinensis

Parodia alacriportana subsp.

catarinensis, commonly known as the Catarinensis Paddle Cactus, is a species native to Brazil. It is a small, compact cactus with a globular shape and thick, ribbed stems.

The plant features white to pale pink flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it has a low water requirement, making it suitable for arid environments.

It is prized for its unique appearance and ease of care in indoor gardening.
