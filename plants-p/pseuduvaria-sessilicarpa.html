Pseuduvaria sessilicarpa

Pseuduvaria sessilicarpa, commonly known as the sessile-fruited pseuduvaria, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia.
