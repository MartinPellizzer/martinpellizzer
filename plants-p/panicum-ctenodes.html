Panicum ctenodes

Panicum ctenodes, commonly known as the silky panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are adorned with silky, hair-like bristles, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a smooth, sometimes hairy surface.

Panicum ctenodes is often found in open, disturbed areas and is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils.
