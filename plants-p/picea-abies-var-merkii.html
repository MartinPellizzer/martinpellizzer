Picea abies var. merkii

Picea abies var. merkii, commonly known as the Merkii fir, is a variant of the Norway spruce.

It is characterized by its slender, conical shape and dark green, needle-like foliage. The tree produces small, spherical cones that mature to a reddish-brown color. It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe and thrives in cooler, high-altitude environments.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in harsh conditions.
