Physosiphon minor

Physosiphon minor, commonly known as the lesser physosiphon, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in desert or semi-arid environments. The plant has a low, sprawling growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually appearing in shades of white or pale green.

Physosiphon minor is known for its ability to survive in harsh, arid conditions due to its drought-resistant adaptations.
