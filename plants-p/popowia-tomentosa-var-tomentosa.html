Popowia tomentosa var. tomentosa

Popowia tomentosa var. tomentosa, commonly known as the wooly popowia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, white to grayish pubescent foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
