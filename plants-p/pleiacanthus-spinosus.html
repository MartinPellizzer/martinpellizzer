Pleiacanthus spinosus

Pleiacanthus spinosus, commonly known as the spiny pleia, is a unique plant species characterized by its intricate, spiky foliage.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments. The plant features elongated, lance-shaped leaves with sharp, needle-like projections along their edges. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically displaying shades of white and pale yellow.

The spiny structure of its leaves serves as a natural defense mechanism against herbivores.
