Polygala kisantuensis var. tenuifolia

Polygala kisantuensis var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the slenderleaf milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Central Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and showy, spiny inflorescences.
