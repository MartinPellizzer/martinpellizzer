Poa araratica subsp. pseudoaltaica

Poa araratica subsp.

pseudoaltaica, commonly known as the Altai meadowgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as alpine meadows and rocky slopes.

The plant has slender, tufted culms with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of spikelets, which are often pale green to brown in color.

This subspecies is adapted to cold climates and is an important component of high-altitude ecosystems.
