Pelargonium × alienatum

Pelargonium × alienatum, commonly known as the hybrid geranium, is a flowering plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Pelargonium hortorum and Pelargonium × inodorum. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. It typically grows as an annual or biennial herb with fleshy, aromatic leaves.

Pelargonium × alienatum is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and as potted plants.
