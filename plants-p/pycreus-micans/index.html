Pycreus Micans

Blue-eyed mary, scientifically known as Pycreus micans, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to South America. This species is prized for its vibrant blue and purple blooms that resemble tiny eyes, giving it its whimsical common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a colorful and resilient addition to their landscapes. With its compact growth habit and long blooming period, it is well-suited for rock gardens, containers, and borders. Despite its beauty, it is also known for its toxicity, requiring caution when handling or allowing pets near the plant.
