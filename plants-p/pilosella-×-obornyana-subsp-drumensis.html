Pilosella × obornyana subsp. drumensis

Pilosella × obornyana subsp.

drumensis, commonly known as Drum's pilosella, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its low-growing habit, with slender stems and dense clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers.

The species is native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe, particularly in the Carpathian Basin. Its leaves are typically narrow and hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This plant is often found in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes.
