Pilea perexigua

Pilea perexigua, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It features rounded, fleshy leaves that are typically a vibrant green, often with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it an ideal indoor plant.

Its unique appearance and ease of care have made it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
