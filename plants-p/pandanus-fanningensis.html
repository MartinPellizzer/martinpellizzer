Pandanus fanningensis

Pandanus fanningensis, commonly known as the Fanning Island pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pandanaceae.

It is native to the Pacific Islands, particularly found on the atoll of Fanning Island in the Line Islands. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. It thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to salty, humid environments.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or green in color.
