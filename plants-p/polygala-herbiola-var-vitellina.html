Polygala herbiola var. vitellina

Polygala herbiola var. vitellina, commonly known as the yellow milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. The plant features slender stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, yellowish flowers are clustered in racemes, and it produces pods that contain several seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in herbal medicine.
