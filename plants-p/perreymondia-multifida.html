Perreymondia multifida

Perreymondia multifida, commonly known as the narrow-leafed pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

Perreymondia multifida is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting native wildlife.
