Plenasium zeylanicum

Plenasium zeylanicum, commonly known as the Ceylon star gooseberry, is a tropical plant native to Sri Lanka.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, featuring glossy, ovate leaves with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by fleshy, star-shaped fruits. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for both its ornamental and medicinal properties.
