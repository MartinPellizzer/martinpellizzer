Polemonium yezoense

Polemonium yezoense, commonly known as the Yezo bellflower, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia, particularly Japan and Korea.

It belongs to the family Polemoniaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to purple floral displays. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
