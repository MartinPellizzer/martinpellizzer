Pandanus kabaenaensis

Pandanus kabaenaensis, commonly known as the Kabaena pandanus, is a rare species native to the island of Kabaena in Indonesia.

It is a member of the Pandanaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant thrives in coastal and wetland environments, where it can tolerate high salinity and periodic flooding.

Its unique morphology and ecological significance make it an important species for conservation efforts in its native habitat.
