Poa kungeica

Poa kungeica, commonly known as the Kungei grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including parts of Kazakhstan and Mongolia. The plant is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Poa kungeica is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
