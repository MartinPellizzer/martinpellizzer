Piper microstigma

Piper microstigma, commonly known as the small-stigma pepper or wild pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and clusters of tiny, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties.
