Plectranthus oertendahlii

Plectranthus oertendahlii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Plectranthus oertendahlii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
