Pilea affinis var. havanensis

Pilea affinis var. havanensis, commonly known as the Mexican Aluminum plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Mexico.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a metallic sheen, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
