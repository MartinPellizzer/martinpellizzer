Panicum fruticosum

Panicum fruticosum, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and tufted inflorescences that produce small, fluffy seeds. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and sandy soils. It has a fibrous root system that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

Panicum fruticosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
