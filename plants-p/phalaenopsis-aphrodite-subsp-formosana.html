Phalaenopsis aphrodite subsp. formosana

Phalaenopsis aphrodite subsp.

formosana, commonly known as the Formosan orchid, is a species native to Taiwan. It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its erect, leafy stems and large, showy flowers.

The plant typically blooms with pale pink to white petals and a deep pink lip, which are adorned with intricate patterns. It prefers shaded, humid environments and is often found in forested areas.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated for horticultural purposes.
