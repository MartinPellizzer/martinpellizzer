Pleurothallis echinantha

Pleurothallis echinantha, commonly known as the spiny pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-white or pale yellow blooms with prominent, spiny structures on the petals and sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
