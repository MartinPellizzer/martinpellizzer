Peperomia haycockii

Peperomia haycockii, commonly known as the Haycock's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia haycockii is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
