Peperomia saligna var. majuscula

Peperomia saligna var. majuscula, commonly known as the large-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically a deep green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale green, arranged in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate moisture.

This variety is prized for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
