Pilea multicaulis

Pilea multicaulis, commonly known as the Shingle Plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to China.

It features a rosette of thick, waxy, dark green leaves that are slightly pointed and have a textured surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers on slender stems, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a favored ornamental plant.
