Palaquium obovatum var. obovatum

Palaquium obovatum var. obovatum, commonly known as the broad-leafed palaquium, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features elliptical to obovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy on both sides. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by edible berries that are yellow when ripe. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for both ornamental and ecological purposes.
