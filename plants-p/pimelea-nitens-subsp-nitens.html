Pimelea nitens subsp. nitens

Pimelea nitens subsp.

nitens, commonly known as the narrow-leaved devil's-bit, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia. It is a shrub that typically grows in dry, sandy soils, often in heathland or open forests.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a distinctive reddish or purplish hue on the underside.
