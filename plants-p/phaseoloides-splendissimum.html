Phaseoloides splendissimum

Phaseoloides splendissimum, commonly known as the splendid phaseoloid, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy blooms that range from deep red to orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant features compound leaves with alternating leaflets and produces small, bean-like pods that contain its seeds. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to its unique appearance and specific growing requirements, it is a sought-after plant among horticultural enthusiasts.
