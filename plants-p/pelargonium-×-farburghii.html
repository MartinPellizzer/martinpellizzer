Pelargonium × farburghii

Pelargonium × farburghii, commonly known as the Zonal Geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a wide range of colors, including red, pink, purple, and white. The leaves are typically round or heart-shaped, often with distinct color zones that give it its common name. It is a popular ornamental plant, widely cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and containers.

This plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.
