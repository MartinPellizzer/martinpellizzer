Pultenaea setulosa

Pultenaea setulosa, commonly known as the silky pultenaea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its finely hairy, often silvery-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or heathland environments.

Pultenaea setulosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
