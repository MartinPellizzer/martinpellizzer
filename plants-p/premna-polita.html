Premna polita

Premna polita, commonly known as silky senna, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Verbenaceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, often with a subtle fragrance. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, wind-dispersed seeds.

Premna polita is often found in moist, shaded areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses.
