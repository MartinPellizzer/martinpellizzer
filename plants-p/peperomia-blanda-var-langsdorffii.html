Peperomia blanda var. langsdorffii

Peperomia blanda var. langsdorffii, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features dense, glossy, dark green leaves with a distinctive silvery or white underside, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental foliage, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
