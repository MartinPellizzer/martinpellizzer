Peperomia variegata

Peperomia variegata, commonly known as the Variegated Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with striking variegation of white, yellow, and green, which adds visual interest to any setting. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia variegata is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
