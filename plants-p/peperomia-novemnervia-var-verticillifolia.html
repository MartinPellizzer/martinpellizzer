Peperomia novemnervia var. verticillifolia

Peperomia novemnervia var. verticillifolia, commonly known as the whorled peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-like arrangement of fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, emerging from the leaf axils. Its leaves are covered in a fine, white pubescence, which helps to reduce water loss in its native habitat.

This variety is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.
