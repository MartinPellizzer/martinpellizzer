Ponapea hosinoi

Ponapea hosinoi, commonly known as the Pohnpei starling tree, is a rare species native to the island of Pohnpei in the Caroline Islands.

It is a small, evergreen tree that typically grows to a height of about 5 to 10 meters. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth, glossy surface. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, producing a mild fragrance.

The fruit is a drupe, containing a single seed enclosed in a fleshy, edible pulp.
