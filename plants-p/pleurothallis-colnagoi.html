Pleurothallis colnagoi

Pleurothallis colnagoi, commonly known as the Colnago's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant typically produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, loose substrates for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
