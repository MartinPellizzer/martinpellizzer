Physurus mayorianus

Physurus mayorianus, commonly known as the Mayrian climbing fern, is a species of fern characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant exhibits a climbing growth habit, using its rhizomes to anchor itself to trees or other structures. Its leaves are typically bi- or tri-pinnate, with a soft, velvety texture.

Physurus mayorianus is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to add a lush, green presence to shaded gardens.
