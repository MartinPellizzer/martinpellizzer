Platyspermum alatum

Platyspermum alatum, commonly known as the winged soursop, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and distinctive wing-like structures on its stems. The plant produces large, green, spiny fruits that are typically harvested when fully mature. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a branching structure that supports its growth in humid, tropical environments.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruit.
