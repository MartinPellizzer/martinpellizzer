Philodendron venosum

Philodendron venosum, commonly known as the veiny philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive veinal pattern that gives it its common name. The plant has aerial roots that help it climb and anchor itself to supports. Its foliage is typically dark green with a glossy texture, and it thrives in bright, indirect light.

Philodendron venosum is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
