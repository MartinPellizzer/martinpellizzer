Phalaenopsis ubonensis

Phalaenopsis ubonensis, commonly known as the Ubon orchid, is a species of orchid native to Thailand.

It is a sympodial orchid characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. The plant has broad, elliptical leaves that are usually dark green with a waxy texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining potting media.

Phalaenopsis ubonensis is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
