Peperomia cueroensis

Peperomia cueroensis, commonly known as the Cueroensis Pepperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Mexico.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Peperomia cueroensis is valued for its ornamental foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
