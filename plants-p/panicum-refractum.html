Panicum refractum

Panicum refractum, commonly known as the refractable grass, is a perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically green or pale yellow in color. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a rough texture and a tendency to roll when dry.

Panicum refractum is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in full sun to partial shade conditions.
