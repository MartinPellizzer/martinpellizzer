Pereilema beyrichianum

Pereilema beyrichianum, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and has a compact, bushy appearance with narrow, linear leaves. It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive blooms and drought tolerance.

Pereilema beyrichianum is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
