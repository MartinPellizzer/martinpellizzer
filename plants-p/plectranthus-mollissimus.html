Plectranthus mollissimus

Plectranthus mollissimus, commonly known as velvetleaf plectranthus, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, velvety, and hairy leaves that give it a unique texture. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale purple to blue in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Plectranthus mollissimus is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and ease of cultivation.
