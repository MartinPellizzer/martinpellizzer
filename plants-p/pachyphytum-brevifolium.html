Pachyphytum brevifolium

Pachyphytum brevifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf stonecrop, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring. It has a compact growth habit and is often used in rock gardens or as a houseplant.

Its slow growth and low maintenance make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
