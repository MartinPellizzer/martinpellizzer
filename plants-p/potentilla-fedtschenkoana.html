Potentilla fedtschenkoana

Potentilla fedtschenkoana, commonly known as the Fedtschenko's cinquefoil, is a perennial flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and hairy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions. It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is known for its hardy nature.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
