Piper venulosum

Piper venulosum, commonly known as the veiny pepper or slender pepper, is a tropical climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, twining stems and heart-shaped leaves with prominent veins. Its small, greenish-white flowers are followed by oval, reddish berries that are edible but not widely consumed.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
