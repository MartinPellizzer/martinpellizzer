Prunus jamasakura f. shibayama

Prunus jamasakura f. shibayama, commonly known as the Shibayama cherry, is a unique flowering plant characterized by its distinct floral morphology.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is known for its striking pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit with a rounded form, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping. Its leaves are ovate with a serrated margin, and the bark is smooth with a light grayish hue.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and parks for its aesthetic appeal.
