Pardanthus sinensis

Pardanthus sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has lance-shaped leaves. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its delicate appearance.

Pardanthus sinensis is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
