Pravinaria leucocarpa

Pravinaria leucocarpa, commonly known as the white-fruited pravinaria, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to South Africa, specifically the Western Cape region. The plant is characterized by its pale yellow to white flowers, which are typically arranged in composite heads. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in fine hairs.

The fruit, which is a small, white achene, is an important food source for certain insect species.
