Pennisetum polygamum

Pennisetum polygamum, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender stalks that produce feathery, purple plumes during the growing season. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green with a slight blue or silver tint. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Pennisetum polygamum is also valued for its ability to attract birds and other wildlife.
