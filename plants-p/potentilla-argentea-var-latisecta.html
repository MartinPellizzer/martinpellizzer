Potentilla argentea var. latisecta

Potentilla argentea var. latisecta, commonly known as silverleaf potentilla, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a valuable addition to horticultural landscapes.
