Pedilonum geluanum

Pedilonum geluanum, commonly known as the Happy Foot plant, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces opposite, ovate leaves with a waxy texture and a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in hanging baskets.
