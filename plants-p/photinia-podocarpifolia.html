Photinia podocarpifolia

Photinia podocarpifolia, commonly known as the glossy leaf photinia, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to eastern China and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage with a glossy, dark green appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive leaves and seasonal color changes.

Photinia podocarpifolia thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
