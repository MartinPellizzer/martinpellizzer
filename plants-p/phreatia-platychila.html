Phreatia platychila

Phreatia platychila, commonly known as the broad-leaved phreatia, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines. The plant features broad, fleshy leaves that help it store water in its natural habitat. Its flowers are typically pale green or yellow, with a distinctive lip that aids in pollination.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.
