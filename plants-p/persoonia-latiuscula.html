Persoonia latiuscula

Persoonia latiuscula, commonly known as the narrow-leaved geebung, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in heathlands and dry forests, characterized by its small, narrow leaves and showy yellow or white flowers. The plant produces small, round, woody fruits that contain seeds enclosed in a fleshy, yellow aril. Persoonia latiuscula is well adapted to nutrient-poor soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions with a Mediterranean climate.

Its unique floral structure and ecological role make it an important species in its native habitat.
