Pachygone adversa

Pachygone adversa, commonly known as the resilient fern, is a perennial plant native to tropical regions.

It features a robust rhizome system that allows it to thrive in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, feather-like fronds that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. Its leaves are typically dark green with a slightly serrated edge.

Pachygone adversa is noted for its ability to withstand adverse conditions, making it a hardy addition to gardens.
