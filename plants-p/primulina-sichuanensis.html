Primulina sichuanensis

Primulina sichuanensis, commonly known as the Sichuan primulina, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, tubular flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant is often found in shaded, moist environments such as mountain forests and rocky crevices. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, soft hair, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
