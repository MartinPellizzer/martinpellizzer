Potentilla opaciformis f. grandiflora

Potentilla opaciformis f. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered potentilla, is a compact shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features dense, upright growth with small, leathery leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces showy, golden-yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in cooler climates.
