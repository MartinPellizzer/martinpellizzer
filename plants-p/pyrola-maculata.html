Pyrola maculata

Pyrola maculata, commonly known as the speckled wintergreen, is a herbaceous perennial native to the northeastern United States and southeastern Canada.

Pyrola maculata is also known for its aromatic leaves and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
