Ptiloria tomentosa

Ptiloria tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly ptiloria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage, which gives it its common name. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in green or reddish bracts.

Ptiloria tomentosa is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in full sun.
