Piper pubisubmarginalum var. ubanum

Piper pubisubmarginalum var. ubanum, commonly known as the Ubanum pepper vine, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with a pubescent underside, giving it the species name "pubisubmarginalum.". The varietal name "ubanum" refers to its geographic origin in the Ubanum region of Indonesia. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into clustered berries.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has potential medicinal uses in traditional medicine.
