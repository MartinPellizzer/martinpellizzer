Poa macroanthera subsp. meilitzyka

Poa macroanthera subsp.

meilitzyka, commonly known as the Meilitzky's tufted grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves.

The inflorescence consists of a single spikelet that is often tightly compacted. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.

The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid conditions, showing resilience to drought and poor soil conditions.
