Prenanthes arborea

Prenanthes arborea, commonly known as the showy tickseed, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as swamps, marshes, and along stream banks. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

The leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and often have a velvety texture.
