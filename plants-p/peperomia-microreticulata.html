Peperomia microreticulata

Peperomia microreticulata, commonly known as the tiny net-veined peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves with intricate, finely veined patterns that give it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal water needs.

Its compact growth habit makes it an attractive choice for tabletop displays and hanging baskets.
