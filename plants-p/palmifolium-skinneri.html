Palmifolium skinneri

Palmifolium skinneri, commonly known as the Skinner's palmleaf, is a unique plant characterized by its large, palm-like leaves.

It belongs to the family Arecaceae, which includes various palm species, though it is not a true palm. The plant features a slender trunk and broad, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length.

Its foliage is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and can thrive in tropical and subtropical climates.
