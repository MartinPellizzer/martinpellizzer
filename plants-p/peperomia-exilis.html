Peperomia exilis

Peperomia exilis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are usually dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces delicate, sparsely branched stems and small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia exilis is valued for its ability to purify the air and add a touch of greenery to any space.
