Pavonia papilionacea

Pavonia papilionacea, commonly known as the flame flower, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, butterfly-shaped pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

Pavonia papilionacea is valued in gardens for its vibrant blooms and ability to attract pollinators.
