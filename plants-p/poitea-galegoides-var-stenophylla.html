Poitea galegoides var. stenophylla

Poitea galegoides var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf poitea, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, aromatic leaves that are often narrow and elongated. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically followed by small, bean-like pods. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to dry conditions.
