Plectronia mitis

Plectronia mitis, commonly known as the soft leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves that are often covered in a fine, soft pubescence. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
