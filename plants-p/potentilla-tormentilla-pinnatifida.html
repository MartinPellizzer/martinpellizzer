Potentilla tormentilla pinnatifida

Potentilla tormentilla pinnatifida, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cinquefoil, is a perennial shrub native to North America.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has finely divided, pinnately arranged leaves that give it a delicate appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or rocky areas.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits, supporting various pollinators.
