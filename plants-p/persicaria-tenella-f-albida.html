Persicaria tenella f. albida

Persicaria tenella f. albida, commonly known as white slender knotweed, is a variant of the species Persicaria tenella.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, white flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving the plant a slightly fuzzy appearance. This form is distinguished by its pale or white flower color, which contrasts with the typical red or pink blooms of the species.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodland areas and stream sides.
