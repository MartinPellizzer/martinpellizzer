Pleione ochracea

Pleione ochracea, commonly known as the yellow pleione, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, lance-shaped leaves and a single, showy flower that typically blooms in spring. The flower has a pale yellow color with a distinctive red or orange lip, which is a key feature for identification. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
