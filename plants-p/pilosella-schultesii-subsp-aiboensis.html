Pilosella schultesii subsp. aiboensis

Pilosella schultesii subsp.

aiboensis, commonly known as the Aiboan goatweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant has a hairy stem and leaves that are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
