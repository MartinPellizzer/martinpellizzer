Pholidota parviflora var. pumila

Pholidota parviflora var. pumila, commonly known as the small-flowered pholidota, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in forest understories.
