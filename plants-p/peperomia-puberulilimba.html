Peperomia puberulilimba

Peperomia puberulilimba, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy, and velvety-textured leaves, which are often covered in a fine, soft pubescence. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and attractive foliage, it is often used as a decorative plant in both home and office settings.
