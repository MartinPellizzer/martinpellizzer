Petrocodon pulchriflorus

Petrocodon pulchriflorus, commonly known as the beautiful flowered petrocodon, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple, often with a distinctive petal arrangement. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it adapts to harsh climatic conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and arranged in a basal rosette, helping to conserve moisture.

Petrocodon pulchriflorus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
