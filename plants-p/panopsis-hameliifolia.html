Panopsis hameliifolia

Panopsis hameliifolia, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Panopsis hameliifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
