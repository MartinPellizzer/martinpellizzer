Peperomia truncicola

Peperomia truncicola, commonly known as the Trunked Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture, and it has a unique, upright growth habit with a short, stout stem. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pale green flowers that are clustered in spikes, which are often hidden among the foliage.

It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.
