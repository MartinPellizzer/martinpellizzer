Pleuroplitis langsdorffii var. chinensis

Pleuroplitis langsdorffii var. chinensis, commonly known as Chinese pleurothallis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia, particularly China and parts of Vietnam. The plant features delicate, narrow leaves and produces fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers with a unique, spurred lip. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some horticultural collections.
