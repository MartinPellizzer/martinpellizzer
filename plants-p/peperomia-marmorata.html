Peperomia marmorata

Peperomia marmorata, commonly known as the marbled peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and silver, giving it a striking, marble-like appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or green flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and attractive foliage, it is often used as a decorative plant in both home and office settings.
