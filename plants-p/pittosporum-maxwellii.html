Pittosporum maxwellii

Pittosporum maxwellii, commonly known as the Maxwell's pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, fragrant foliage and produces showy, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant has smooth, greyish bark and oval-shaped leaves with a leathery texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

Pittosporum maxwellii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native habitats.
