Pouzolzia hirta var. parvifolia

Pouzolzia hirta var. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved pouzolzia, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are small, three-lobed capsules that release seeds when mature.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
