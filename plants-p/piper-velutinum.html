Piper velutinum

Piper velutinum, commonly known as velvet pepper, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its smooth, velvety leaves and small, greenish-yellow flowers. Its fruits are tiny, round berries that turn red when mature.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
