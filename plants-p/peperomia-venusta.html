Peperomia venusta

Peperomia venusta, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the spring or early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia venusta is a low-maintenance plant that is well-suited for indoor cultivation.
