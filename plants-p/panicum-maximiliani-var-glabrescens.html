Panicum maximiliani var. glabrescens

Panicum maximiliani var. glabrescens, commonly known as silverleaf panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which results from dense pubescence on the leaves. The plant typically grows in full sun and thrives in well-drained soils, making it adaptable to various environmental conditions. Its inflorescence consists of loose, open panicles that produce small, spikelet-like flowers.

This variety is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
