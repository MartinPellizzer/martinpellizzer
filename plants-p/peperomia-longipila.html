Peperomia longipila

Peperomia longipila, commonly known as the long-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, upright growth with thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green and have a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pale green flowers that appear in clusters during the spring. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Peperomia longipila is valued for its ornamental foliage and air-purifying qualities.
