Peperomia pellucida var. pellucida

Peperomia pellucida var. pellucida, commonly known as the shining pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating, giving them a shimmering appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
