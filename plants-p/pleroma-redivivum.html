Pleroma redivivum

Pleroma redivivum, commonly known as the resurrection lily, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to survive in dry conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is known for its ability to withstand drought.

Pleroma redivivum is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and resilience.
