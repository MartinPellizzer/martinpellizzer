Plantago zoellneriana

Plantago zoellneriana, commonly known as Zoellner's plantain, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It features a rosette of broad, oval leaves with a waxy surface and prominent veins. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in a dense spike, which are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in dry, sandy or rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Zoellner's plantain is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
