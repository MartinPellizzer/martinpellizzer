Phalaenopsis labukensis

Phalaenopsis labukensis, commonly known as the Labuk orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, long-lasting flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has strap-like leaves that are usually dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in humid environments with indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Phalaenopsis labukensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
