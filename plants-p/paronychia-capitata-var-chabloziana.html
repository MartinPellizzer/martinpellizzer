Paronychia capitata var. chabloziana

Paronychia capitata var. chabloziana, commonly known as the Cape mountain heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, compact growth and small, needle-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white to pink flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is adapted to full sun.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
