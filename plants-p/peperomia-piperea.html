Peperomia piperea

Peperomia piperea, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy, silver-gray leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that emerge from the leaf axils. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Peperomia piperea is also prized for its air-purifying properties and ability to thrive in humid environments.
