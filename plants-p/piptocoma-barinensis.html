Piptocoma barinensis

Piptocoma barinensis, commonly known as the Barine's pittosporum, is a shrub native to the rainforests of northern Queensland, Australia.

It belongs to the family Pittosporaceae and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in understorey habitats. Its leaves are usually elliptical with a glossy surface, and it produces small, fleshy fruits that are dispersed by birds.

Piptocoma barinensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
