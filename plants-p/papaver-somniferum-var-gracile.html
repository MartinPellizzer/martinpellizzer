Papaver somniferum var. gracile

Papaver somniferum var. gracile, commonly known as the slender opium poppy, is a variant of the opium poppy characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, pale pink to white flowers.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. The plant produces small, oval-shaped蒴果 that contain the latex from which opium is derived. Its botanical features include finely lobed leaves and a short, sturdy stem.
