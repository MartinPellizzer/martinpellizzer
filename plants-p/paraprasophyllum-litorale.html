Paraprasophyllum litorale

Paraprasophyllum litorale, commonly known as the coastal paraprasophyllum, is a rare flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that are adapted to store water, allowing it to thrive in arid and saline environments.
