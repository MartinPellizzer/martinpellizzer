Peixotoa megalantha

Peixotoa megalantha, commonly known as the giant peixotoa, is a striking member of the Malvaceae family, native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
