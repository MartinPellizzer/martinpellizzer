Physostemon intermedius

Physostemon intermedius, commonly known as the Chinese skullcap, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features square stems and opposite leaves that are typically lance-shaped. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Botanically, it belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants.
