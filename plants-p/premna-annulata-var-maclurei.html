Premna annulata var. maclurei

Premna annulata var. maclurei, commonly known as Maclure's premna, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy on the underside. It produces small, white or pale purple flowers arranged in clusters.

This variety is often found in tropical lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
