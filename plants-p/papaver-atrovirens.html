Papaver atrovirens

Papaver atrovirens, commonly known as the dark-green poppy, is a species of flowering plant in the family Papaveraceae.

It is native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia, where it thrives in rocky or dry habitats. The plant produces striking, dark green to violet-blue flowers with prominent yellow stamens. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Papaver atrovirens is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated for its unique floral display.
