Ptilagrostis schischkinii

Ptilagrostis schischkinii, commonly known as the Schischkin's feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and fine, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often adorned with silky, feathery bristles. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
