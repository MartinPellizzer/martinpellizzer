Pertya

Pertya, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers and spiny, succulent leaves. Native to coastal areas of the Mediterranean and the eastern Mediterranean, it thrives in sandy or rocky soils. The plant is often found in maritime climates and is adapted to salt spray and strong winds.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
