Peperomia suizana

Peperomia suizana, commonly known as the Suizan peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features broad, waxy leaves that are typically green with a subtle variegation, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pink. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for indoor cultivation, where it can be grown in pots or as part of a living arrangement.
