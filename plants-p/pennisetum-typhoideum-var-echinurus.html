Pennisetum typhoideum var. echinurus

Pennisetum typhoideum var. echinurus, commonly known as fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It features slender, arching stems that produce fluffy, silver-gray plumes, giving it a distinctive, ornamental appearance. The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and is often cultivated for its visual appeal in gardens and landscapes. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a versatile choice for ornamental gardening.

Its ability to attract birds and butterflies also adds ecological value to the environment.
