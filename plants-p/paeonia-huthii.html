Paeonia huthii

Paeonia huthii, commonly known as the Huth peony, is a species native to China.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant features compound, trifoliate leaves that are typically green and hairy. It belongs to the芍药属 (Paeonia) genus within the family Ranunculaceae.

Paeonia huthii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.
