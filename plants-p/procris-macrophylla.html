Procris macrophylla

Procris macrophylla, commonly known as the broad-leafed procris, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Procris macrophylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
