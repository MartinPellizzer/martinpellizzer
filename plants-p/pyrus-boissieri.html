Pyrus boissieri

Pyrus boissieri, commonly known as the Boissier pear, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of the Mediterranean, particularly in parts of southern Europe and western Asia. Pyrus boissieri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its unique fruit, which can be used in culinary applications.
