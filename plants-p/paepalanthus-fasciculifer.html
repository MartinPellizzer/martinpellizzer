Paepalanthus fasciculifer

Paepalanthus fasciculifer, commonly known as the clustered broom.

It is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in Argentina and Chile. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense clusters of small, yellowish flowers. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in coastal areas.

Its botanical features include narrow, grass-like leaves and a compact growth habit.
