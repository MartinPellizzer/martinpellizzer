Plectranthus loxothyrsus

Plectranthus loxothyrsus, commonly known as the velvetleaf plectranthus, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to pink. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for both garden beds and container cultivation. Its aromatic leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Plectranthus loxothyrsus is also valued as an ornamental plant for its attractive appearance and ease of care.
