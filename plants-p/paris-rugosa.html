Paris rugosa

Paris rugosa, commonly known as the Chinese herbaceous plant, is a perennial species native to East Asia.

It features a rhizomatous root system and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in early spring. The plant is characterized by its slender, branching stems and trifoliate leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade.

Paris rugosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional medicinal uses in Chinese medicine.
