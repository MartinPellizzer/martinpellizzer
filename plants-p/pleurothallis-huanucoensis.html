Pleurothallis huanucoensis

Pleurothallis huanucoensis, commonly known as the Huanuco orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Peru.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, intricate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees without deriving nutrients from them. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to flourish.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
