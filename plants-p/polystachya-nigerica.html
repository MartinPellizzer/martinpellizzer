Polystachya nigerica

Polystachya nigerica, commonly known as the black-veined orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking dark green leaves with prominent black veins and its tall, erect inflorescence bearing numerous small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Polystachya nigerica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
