Parodia spegazziniana var. lembckei

Parodia spegazziniana var. lembckei, commonly known as the Lembcke's moon cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Argentina.

It features a green to bluish-green stem with a dense covering of white to pale yellow spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a popular choice for rock gardens and containers due to its compact size and ornamental appearance.

It requires well-draining soil and minimal water to thrive.
