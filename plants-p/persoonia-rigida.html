Persoonia rigida

Persoonia rigida, commonly known as the rigid geebung, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and often have a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Persoonia rigida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and flowers.
