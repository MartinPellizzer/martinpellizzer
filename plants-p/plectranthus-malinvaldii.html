Plectranthus malinvaldii

Plectranthus malinvaldii, commonly known as the Malinvald's plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface and produces small, tubular flowers in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and vibrant flower colors.
