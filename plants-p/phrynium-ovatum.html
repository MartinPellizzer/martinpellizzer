Phrynium ovatum

Phrynium ovatum, commonly known as the arrow leaf plant, is a tropical perennial herb native to Central and South America.

It features broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated with yellow and green patterns. The plant produces striking, showy inflorescences with clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Phrynium ovatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
