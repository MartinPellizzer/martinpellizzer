Pleione formosana f. alba

Pleione formosana f. alba, commonly known as the white form of the Formosan pleione, is a rare and elegant orchid native to Taiwan.

It is characterized by its striking white flowers, which contrast beautifully with its dark green foliage. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in mountainous regions with rich, well-drained soil. Its rhizomes are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water and nutrients.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
