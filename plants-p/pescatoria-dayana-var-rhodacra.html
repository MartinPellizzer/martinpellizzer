Pescatoria dayana var. rhodacra

Pescatoria dayana var. rhodacra, commonly known as the Crimson Flame Orchid, is a strikingly beautiful orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red petals and long, slender sepals that give it a dramatic appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
