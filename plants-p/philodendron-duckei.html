Philodendron duckei

Philodendron duckei, commonly known as the Duckei Philodendron, is a climbing vine native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy, and dark green leaves that are typically veined with a lighter green color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by elongated, cylindrical fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and foliage plants.
