Podocarpium laxum var. laterale

Podocarpium laxum var. laterale, commonly known as the silverleaf podocarp, is a rare and lesser-known plant species belonging to the Podocarpaceae family.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories, where it can thrive in cool and humid conditions. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are followed by cone-like structures that contain its seeds.
