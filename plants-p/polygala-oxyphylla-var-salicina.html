Polygala oxyphylla var. salicina

Polygala oxyphylla var. salicina, commonly known as the Salicin polygala, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal and sandy habitats. The plant features slender, wiry stems and small, clustered leaves. Its flowers are usually pale blue or greenish-white, with a distinctive, spurred shape.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
