Pleurothallis inaequisepala

Pleurothallis inaequisepala, commonly known as the unequal-sepaled pleurothallis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. This epiphytic orchid typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are small, usually white or pale green, and are arranged in a spike.

The plant's unique floral structure and delicate appearance make it a subject of interest for orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike.
