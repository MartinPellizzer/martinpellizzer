Patrinia angustifolia

Patrinia angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf patrinia, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. The plant features slender, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, white or pale blue flowers bloom in clusters, adding to its ornamental appeal.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
