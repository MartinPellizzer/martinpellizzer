Pertya glabrescens f. impressa

Pertya glabrescens f. impressa, commonly known as the impressa form of the glabrescens pertya, is a variant of the Pertya genus.

It is a flowering plant characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology and unique floral features. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit with dense foliage, often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its flowers are typically small and clustered, displaying a range of colors depending on the cultivar. This form is prized in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
