Piper breve var. pseudodumeticola

Piper breve var. pseudodumeticola, commonly known as the short pepper plant, is a variant of the Piper breve species, which belongs to the Piperaceae family.

This plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, aromatic leaves. It typically thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade. The leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticultural and ethnobotanical studies.
