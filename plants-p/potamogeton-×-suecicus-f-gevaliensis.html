Potamogeton × suecicus f. gevaliensis

Potamogeton × suecicus f. gevaliensis, commonly known as the Gevald's pondweed, is a hybrid aquatic plant native to parts of Europe.

It is characterized by its long, ribbon-like leaves that are typically green to reddish in color. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as lakes and ponds. It produces small, greenish flowers that are arranged in spike-like clusters.

Potamogeton × suecicus f. gevaliensis plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various water organisms.
