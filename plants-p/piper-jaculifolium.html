Piper jaculifolium

Piper jaculifolium, commonly known as the arrowleaf pepper or arrowleaf piper, is a tropical climbing shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many pepper species. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers that develop into peppery fruits. Its unique leaf shape and climbing habit make it a distinctive species within its genus.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and traditional use in local cuisines.
