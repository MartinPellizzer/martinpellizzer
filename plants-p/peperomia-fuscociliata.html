Peperomia fuscociliata

Peperomia fuscociliata, commonly known as the dark-veined peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with prominent, dark brown veins that give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are typically borne on short, upright spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia fuscociliata is relatively low maintenance and resistant to pests, making it an ideal plant for beginners.
