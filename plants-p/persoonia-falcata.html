Persoonia falcata

Persoonia falcata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf burr-ace, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, leathery leaves. The plant produces distinctive, woody fruits that are often referred to as "burr-like" due to their prickly appearance. Persoonia falcata typically grows in heathlands and open forests, preferring well-drained soils.

It is known for its ecological importance, providing habitat and food for various native wildlife species.
