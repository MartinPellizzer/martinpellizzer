Pilea macrophylla

Pilea macrophylla, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a popular houseplant native to China.

It is characterized by its round, waxy leaves that resemble small coins, which give it its common name. The plant has a short, stocky stem and prefers indirect light and well-draining soil. It is relatively easy to care for, making it a favored choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea macrophylla is also known for its ability to purify the air and thrive in a variety of indoor environments.
