Pharnaceum confertum var. brachyphyllum

Pharnaceum confertum var. brachyphyllum, commonly known as the short-leaved pharnaceum, is a unique variant of the pharnaceum genus.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and shorter, broader leaves compared to other varieties. This plant typically thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodland areas. Its foliage exhibits a striking coloration, ranging from deep green to reddish tones depending on light exposure.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
