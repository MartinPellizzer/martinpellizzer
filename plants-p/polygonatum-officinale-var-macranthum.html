Polygonatum officinale var. macranthum

Polygonatum officinale var. macranthum, commonly known as the large-flowered yellow flag, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces clusters of showy, yellowish-green to orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are long, narrow, and arranged in a fan-like pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
