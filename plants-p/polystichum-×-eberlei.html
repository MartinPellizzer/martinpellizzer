Polystichum × eberlei

Polystichum × eberlei, commonly known as the Eberlei's shield fern, is a hybrid fern that results from the cross between Polystichum acrostichum and Polystichum setiferum.

It is a hardy, evergreen fern that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features large, leathery, dark green fronds with a distinctive, upright growth habit. Its spores are produced on the undersides of the fronds, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Polystichum × eberlei is valued for its ability to thrive in woodland settings and its elegant, fern-like appearance.
