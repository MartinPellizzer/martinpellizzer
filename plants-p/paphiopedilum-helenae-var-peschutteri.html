Paphiopedilum helenae var. peschutteri

Paphiopedilum helenae var. peschutteri, commonly known as the Helenae Paphiopedilum, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers with a unique lip that resembles a pouch, a feature typical of the genus Paphiopedilum. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial or epiphytic orchid, often found in shaded, humid forest environments. Its foliage consists of dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.
