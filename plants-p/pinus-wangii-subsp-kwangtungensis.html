Pinus wangii subsp. kwangtungensis

Pinus wangii subsp.

kwangtungensis, commonly known as the Guangdong pine, is a rare and endemic species native to southern China. It is a coniferous evergreen tree characterized by its slender, pyramidal shape and needle-like leaves.

The cones are typically small and woody, containing two to three seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers temperate to subtropical climates.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
