Pinguicula vulgaris f. coerulescens

Pinguicula vulgaris f. coerulescens, commonly known as the blue haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are covered in a fine, waxy coating. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of purple or pink during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its vibrant coloration.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
