Polystichum ovatopaleaceum

Polystichum ovatopaleaceum, commonly known as the ovate paleaceum wood fern, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The leaves are typically ovate in shape with a pale, waxy appearance on the underside. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.

The plant has a long rhizome system that helps it spread and establish itself in rocky or moist soil conditions.
