Paeonia veitchii

Paeonia veitchii, commonly known as the Veitch's peony, is a deciduous herbaceous perennial native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep pink, often with a distinctive red eye. The plant features compound, bipinnate leaves that are green and finely divided. It produces woody rhizomes and is known for its robust growth habit.

Paeonia veitchii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to various soil conditions.
