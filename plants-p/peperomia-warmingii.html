Peperomia warmingii

Peperomia warmingii, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green, velvety leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that are typically hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia warmingii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and low maintenance requirements.
