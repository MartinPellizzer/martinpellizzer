Pteris × otomasui

Pteris × otomasui, commonly known as the Japanese shield fern, is a hybrid species within the fern family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, overlapping fronds that form a compact, shield-like shape, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices, where it thrives in humid conditions. Its fronds are usually dark green with a leathery texture and can reach lengths of up to 30 centimeters.

Pteris × otomasui is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
