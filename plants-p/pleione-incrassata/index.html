Pleione Incrassata

Pleione incrassata is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of China. This plant is known for its large, showy flowers that bloom in spring, often displaying vivid pink and white hues. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires specific care to flourish. Due to its beauty and scarcity, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this species from habitat loss and over-collection.
