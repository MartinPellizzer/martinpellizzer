Peperomia venulosa var. avenulosa

Peperomia venulosa var. avenulosa, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to South America.

It features striking, waxy, silver-green leaves with a velvety texture, which help it retain moisture in its natural habitat. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage makes it a standout addition to any plant collection.
