Pennisetum glaucum subsp. sieberianum

Pennisetum glaucum subsp.

sieberianum, commonly known as silverleaf lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its slender, silver-gray foliage and compact growth habit, making it a popular ornamental plant.

The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that add visual interest during the flowering season. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, preferring dry to moderate moisture conditions.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it suitable for xeriscaping and erosion control in suitable climates.
