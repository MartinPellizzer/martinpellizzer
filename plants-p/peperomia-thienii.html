Peperomia thienii

Peperomia thienii, commonly known as the Thai peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface, often adorned with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia thienii is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
