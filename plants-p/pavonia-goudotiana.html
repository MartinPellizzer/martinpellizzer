Pavonia goudotiana

Pavonia goudotiana, commonly known as the redroot pavonia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive red stems and clusters of small, pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, elliptical fruits.

Pavonia goudotiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and hardy nature.
