Petalidium barlerioides

Petalidium barlerioides, commonly known as the Barlerioides petalidium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is known for its small, daisy-like flowers. It has linear to lanceolate leaves and produces yellow to orange blooms.
