Panicum palmifolium

Panicum palmifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, palm-like leaves that are often feathered or lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small, three-lobed flowers.

Panicum palmifolium is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
