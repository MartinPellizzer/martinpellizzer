Populus × jrtyschensis

Populus × jrtyschensis, commonly known as the Weeping Poplar, is a hybrid deciduous tree belonging to the Salicaceae family.

It is characterized by its drooping branches that create a cascading effect, giving it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are simple, alternate, and ovate with a serrated margin, typically turning yellow in the fall. It produces small, fluffy seeds encased in a samarum, which are dispersed by wind.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and parks.
