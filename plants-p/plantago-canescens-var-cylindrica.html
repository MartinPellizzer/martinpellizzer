Plantago canescens var. cylindrica

Plantago canescens var. cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical plantain, is a variant of the common plantain species.

It is a perennial herb native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa, often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. Its flowers are arranged in dense, cylindrical spikes, which are typically greenish-white in color.

This variant is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in herbal remedies.
