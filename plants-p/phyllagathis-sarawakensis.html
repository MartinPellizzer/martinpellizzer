Phyllagathis sarawakensis

Phyllagathis sarawakensis, commonly known as the Sarawak pineapple.

It is a member of the Myrtaceae family and is native to the rainforests of Borneo. This plant is characterized by its small, clustered leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological significance in its native habitat.
