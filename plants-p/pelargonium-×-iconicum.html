Pelargonium × iconicum

Pelargonium × iconicum, commonly known as the zonal geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, five-petaled flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and purple. The leaves are typically serrated and have distinct color zones, which give the plant its common name. It is a popular ornamental plant, often grown in gardens and containers for its vibrant blooms and foliage.

Pelargonium × iconicum thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
