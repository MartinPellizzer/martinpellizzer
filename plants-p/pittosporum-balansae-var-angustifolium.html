Pittosporum balansae var. angustifolium

Pittosporum balansae var. angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features dark green, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in late summer, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in native habitats.
