Phylica eriophoros var. bergiana

Phylica eriophoros var. bergiana, commonly known as the Berg's torch bush, is a flowering shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and striking red or orange-pink flowers. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to coastal and sandy environments.
