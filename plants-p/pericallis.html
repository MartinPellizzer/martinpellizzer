Pericallis

Pericallis, commonly known as the panda plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Cactaceae.

It is native to the Canary Islands and is characterized by its unique, fleshy, and often spiny stems. The plant produces small, colorful flowers that bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red. Pericallis species are typically evergreen and thrive in well-drained soil with bright, indirect sunlight.

They are popular as ornamental plants due to their attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
