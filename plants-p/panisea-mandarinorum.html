Panisea mandarinorum

Panisea mandarinorum, commonly known as the Mandarin Orange Mint, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features fragrant, small white flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a subtle citrus scent. The plant has heart-shaped, aromatic leaves that are often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and indoor plant collections.
