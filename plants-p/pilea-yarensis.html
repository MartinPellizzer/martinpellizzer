Pilea yarensis

Pilea yarensis, commonly known as the Yarensis Begonia, is a species of flowering plant native to South America, specifically Ecuador.

It belongs to the family Begoniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically borne in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage.

Pilea yarensis is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
