Pinalia cochinchinensis

Pinalia cochinchinensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam, and thrives in humid, tropical environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are typically showy, with a complex structure that includes multiple petals and a distinctive lip.

Pinalia cochinchinensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
