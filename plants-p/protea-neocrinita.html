Protea neocrinita

Protea neocrinita, commonly known as the yellow protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence composed of numerous small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant is native to the Western and Eastern Cape provinces of South Africa, where it thrives in well-drained, acidic soils. It is often found in fynbos vegetation, which is a unique type of shrubland ecosystem.

Protea neocrinita is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in local biodiversity and ecological systems.
