Peperomia yananoensis

Peperomia yananoensis, commonly known as the Yananoensis Peperomia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the humid forests of South America, particularly in regions of Brazil and Peru. This plant is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and its glossy, dark green leaves. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color.

Peperomia yananoensis is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
