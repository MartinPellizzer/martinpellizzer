Plectranthus Lasianthus

Plectranthus lasianthus is a unique and striking plant known for its vibrant purple foliage and compact growth habit. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in warm, sunny environments with well-drained soil. This evergreen shrub is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and ease of care. Its leaves are not only visually appealing but also emit a mild minty scent when crushed. Whether grown in pots or in the ground, Plectranthus lasianthus adds a touch of color and fragrance to any space.
