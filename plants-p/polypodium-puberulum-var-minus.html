Polypodium puberulum var. minus

Polypodium puberulum var. minus, commonly known as the lesser wall fern, is a terrestrial fern native to various regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its small, compact fronds that are typically less than 20 cm in length. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as rocky crevices and forest floors. Its fronds are usually covered in fine, short hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance.

This variety is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to grow in shaded, humid conditions.
