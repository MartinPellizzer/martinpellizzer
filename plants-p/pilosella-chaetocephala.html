Pilosella chaetocephala

Pilosella chaetocephala, commonly known as the hairy leaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers with white petals and yellow centers. The plant has hairy leaves and stems, which help it retain moisture in arid environments. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open habitats.
