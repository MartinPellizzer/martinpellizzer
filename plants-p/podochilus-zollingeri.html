Podochilus zollingeri

Podochilus zollingeri, commonly known as the Zollinger's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, upright stem with a few leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a spike. The plant is characterized by its unique inflorescence and is often found in shaded, humid environments. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is appreciated for its ornamental value.

Podochilus zollingeri requires well-drained soil and consistent moisture to thrive.
