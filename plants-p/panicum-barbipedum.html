Panicum barbipedum

Panicum barbipedum, commonly known as the feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, tufted inflorescences that resemble feathers, giving it its common name. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green hue and is often found in open, disturbed habitats. Panicum barbipedum is adapted to well-drained soils and can tolerate drought conditions.

It is sometimes used in ornamental landscaping due to its delicate appearance and low maintenance requirements.
