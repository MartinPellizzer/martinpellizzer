Piper mosenii

Piper mosenii, commonly known as the Mosenii pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with long, slender stems that often twine around other vegetation. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a glossy surface, while the fruit is a small, elongated berry that turns from green to red when mature.

The plant is valued for its aromatic leaves and fruits, which are used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.
