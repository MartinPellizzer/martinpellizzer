Parasamanea

Parasamanea, commonly known as the royal palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that develop into dark purple fruits. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil in humid environments.

Parasamanea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
