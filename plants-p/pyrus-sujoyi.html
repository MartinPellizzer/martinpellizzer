Pyrus sujoyi

Pyrus sujoyi, commonly known as the Sujoy pear, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to the eastern Himalayas, particularly found in regions of India and Nepal. The plant is characterized by its deciduous nature, producing showy white to pink flowers in the spring. Its fruit, which resembles a small pear, is edible and has a sweet, aromatic flavor.

The Sujoy pear is valued for its ornamental beauty and potential agricultural significance in its native habitat.
