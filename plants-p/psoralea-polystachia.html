Psoralea polystachia

Psoralea polystachia, commonly known as the slender psoralea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and pod fruits. The plant features slender, often hairy stems and small, compound leaves with a pinnate structure. Its flowers are typically yellow or yellowish-green, arranged in dense, racemose inflorescences.
