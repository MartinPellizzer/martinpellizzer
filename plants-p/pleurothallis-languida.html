Pleurothallis languida

Pleurothallis languida, commonly known as the languid pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, pendulous inflorescences with small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrates.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
