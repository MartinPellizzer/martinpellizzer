Panicum sulphureum

Panicum sulphureum, commonly known as sulfur panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.

Panicum sulphureum is often used in ornamental gardening due to its vibrant color and drought tolerance.
