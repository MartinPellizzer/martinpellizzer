Panicum distichum var. lancifolium

Panicum distichum var. lancifolium, commonly known as slender panicgrass, is a grass species native to parts of North America.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and tufted inflorescences that produce small, fluffy seeds. This plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. It thrives in full sun and is adapted to a variety of soil types.

Its botanical features make it a common sight in prairie and meadow ecosystems.
