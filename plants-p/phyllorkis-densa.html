Phyllorkis densa

Phyllorkis densa, commonly known as the dense phyllorkis, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft texture. It produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming in late spring to early summer.

Phyllorkis densa is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
