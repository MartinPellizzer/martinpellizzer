Petraeovitex scortechinii

Petraeovitex scortechinii, commonly known as the rock vitex, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub characterized by its slender, often branched stems and small, opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pale blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is drought-tolerant, making it well-suited for arid environments.
