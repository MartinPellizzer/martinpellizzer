Pittosporum zollingerianum

Pittosporum zollingerianum, commonly known as the Zollinger's pittosporum, is a flowering shrub native to New Zealand.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by red, fleshy berries. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

The species is also valued for its role in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and resilience.
