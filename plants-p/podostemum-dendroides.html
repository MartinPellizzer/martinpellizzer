Podostemum dendroides

Podostemum dendroides, commonly known as the tree-like water plant, is a unique aquatic plant found in fast-flowing streams and rivers.

It has a distinctive appearance with its branching, tree-like structure and small, opposite leaves. The plant is adapted to submerged conditions, with specialized structures that help it anchor itself to rocks. Its flowers are typically small and inconspicuous, often floating on the water surface.

Podostemum dendroides plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various organisms.
