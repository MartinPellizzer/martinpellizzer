Pyrethrum karelinii

Pyrethrum karelinii, commonly known as the Karelin's pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Indonesia and the Philippines.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant produces pyrethrins, natural insecticides used in organic pest control. It thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and full sunlight.

Pyrethrum karelinii is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its practical applications in agriculture.
