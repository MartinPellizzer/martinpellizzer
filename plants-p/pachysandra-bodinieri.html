Pachysandra bodinieri

Pachysandra bodinieri, commonly known as Chinese evergreen, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters. The plant has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for ground cover or as a decorative plant in shaded areas. It prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, thriving in cool, humid environments.

Its ornamental value lies in its lush foliage rather than its flowers.
