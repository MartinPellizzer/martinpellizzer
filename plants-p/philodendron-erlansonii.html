Philodendron erlansonii

Philodendron erlansonii, commonly known as the Erlandson's philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly waxy texture and prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Its ornamental value lies in its lush foliage, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
