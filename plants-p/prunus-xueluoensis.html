Prunus xueluoensis

Prunus xueluoensis, commonly known as the Xueluo cherry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its slender branches and clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red or black when mature. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its spring blossoms and autumn fruit.
