Pedicularis lineata var. dissecta

Pedicularis lineata var. dissecta, commonly known as the dissected lousewort, is a perennial herb native to alpine and subalpine regions of Europe and Asia.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and protected areas.
