Pentapetes ovata

Pentapetes ovata, commonly known as the round-leaf pentapetes, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are ovate, with a smooth texture and a slightly hairy underside.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, round seeds.
