Phoenix leonensis

Phoenix leonensis, commonly known as the king's palm, is a species of palm native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery, bright green leaves. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil. It produces small, white flowers that develop into dark purple fruits.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in tropical gardens and landscapes.
