Poa orba

Poa orba, commonly known as the silky bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green blades that are often coated with a waxy bloom, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and meadows. Its inflorescence consists of a loose panicle with small, spikelet-like flowers.

Poa orba is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its attractive foliage and resilience.
