Perilla ocymoides var. purpurascens

Perilla ocymoides var. purpurascens, commonly known as purple perilla, is a variant of the perilla plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its striking purple foliage and pinkish-purple flowers, which appear in clusters during the summer months. The leaves are broad, heart-shaped, and have a slightly aromatic scent when crushed. This plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant coloration and ease of growth.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.
