Phlox speciosa subvar. nitida

Phlox speciosa subvar. nitida, commonly known as the silverleaf phlox, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range from pink to lilac in color. The plant features narrow, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a desirable addition to many landscapes.
