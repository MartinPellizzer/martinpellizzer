Pilea elegantissima

Pilea elegantissima, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its round, glossy leaves that are slightly puckered and resemble small coins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Pilea elegantissima is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
