Poa pyramidalis

Poa pyramidalis, commonly known as the pyramidal grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern South America.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and dense, pyramidal-shaped inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a bluish-green coloration.

Poa pyramidalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and restoration projects.
