Petalochilus porphyreus

Petalochilus porphyreus, commonly known as the purple orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking purple and pink petals, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are relatively small but are highly fragrant, attracting various pollinators.

Petalochilus porphyreus is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique coloration.
