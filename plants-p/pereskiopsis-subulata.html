Pereskiopsis subulata

Pereskiopsis subulata, commonly known as the silver feather plant, is a low-growing, mat-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features narrow, needle-like leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery-white coating, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its compact growth habit makes it an ideal choice for rock gardens or as a ground cover in xeriscaped landscapes.
