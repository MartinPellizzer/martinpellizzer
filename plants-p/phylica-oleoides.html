Phylica oleoides

Phylica oleoides, commonly known as the silver leaf phylica, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, silver-gray leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and coastal environments, showcasing drought tolerance and salt resistance. Its flowers attract pollinators such as bees and birds, contributing to local biodiversity.

The plant's aromatic foliage and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for horticultural use.
