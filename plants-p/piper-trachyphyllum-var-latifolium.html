Piper trachyphyllum var. latifolium

Piper trachyphyllum var. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf pepper plant, is a tropical species native to regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers. Its fruit is a small, three-lobed berry that contains the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine.

This variety is valued for its unique foliage and potential medicinal properties.
