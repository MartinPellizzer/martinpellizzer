Potentilla verna var. gaudinii

Potentilla verna var. gaudinii, commonly known as the early potentilla, is a low-growing shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May. The plant has slender, woody stems and hairy leaves that are oval in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or rocky areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to naturalize in suitable conditions.
