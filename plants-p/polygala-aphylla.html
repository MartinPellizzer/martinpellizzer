Polygala aphylla

Polygala aphylla, commonly known as the smooth polygala, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like clusters of flowers. Its flowers are usually pale blue or white, with five petals and a distinctive central appendage.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
