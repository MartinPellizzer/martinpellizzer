Peperomia podocarpa

Peperomia podocarpa, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, which are followed by round, fleshy fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia podocarpa is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
