Phyteuma orbiculare subsp. austriacum

Phyteuma orbiculare subsp.

austriacum, commonly known as the Austrian Globe Flower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe. It is characterized by its spherical, showy flower heads that resemble small globes, typically ranging in color from yellow to orange.

The plant features basal leaves that are rounded and hairy, and it produces a single flower per stem. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and rocky habitats.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its distinctive floral display.
