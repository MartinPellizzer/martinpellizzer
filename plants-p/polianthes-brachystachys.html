Polianthes brachystachys

Polianthes brachystachys, commonly known as the narrow-stalked amaryllis, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of red, white, and pink.
