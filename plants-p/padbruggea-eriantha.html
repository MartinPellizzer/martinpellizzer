Padbruggea eriantha

Padbruggea eriantha, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray, velvety foliage and produces small, fragrant white flowers. The plant is evergreen and typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, often with a waxy texture.
