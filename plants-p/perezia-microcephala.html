Perezia microcephala

Perezia microcephala, commonly known as the dwarf perezia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features compact, dense foliage with dark green, leathery leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in rock gardens and as a container plant.
