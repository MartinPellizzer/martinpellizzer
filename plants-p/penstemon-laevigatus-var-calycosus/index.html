Penstemon Laevigatus Var. Calycosus

Penstemon laevigatus var. calycosus is a striking member of the plant family Plantaginaceae known for its vibrant blue to purple flower clusters. Native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States, this species thrives in rocky and sandy soils with minimal water requirements. Its showy blooms attract pollinators like bees and butterflies, making it a valuable addition to native gardens. The plant's compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it an excellent choice for xeriscaping and ecological restoration projects. With its unique appearance and low maintenance needs, Penstemon laevigatus var. calycosus is both a beauty and a functional plant in desert landscapes.
