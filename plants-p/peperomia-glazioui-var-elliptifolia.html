Peperomia glazioui var. elliptifolia

Peperomia glazioui var. elliptifolia, commonly known as the elliptifolia peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
