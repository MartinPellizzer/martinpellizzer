Pentaschistis ampla

Pentaschistis ampla, commonly known as the broadleaf sandwort, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens in regions with similar climates.
