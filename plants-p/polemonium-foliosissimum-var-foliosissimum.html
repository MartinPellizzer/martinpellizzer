Polemonium foliosissimum var. foliosissimum

Polemonium foliosissimum var. foliosissimum, commonly known as the broadleafJacob's ladder, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It prefers moist, woodland habitats and is often found in rich, well-drained soils.

Its botanical structure includes a rhizomatous root system that aids in its persistence in natural settings.
