Pyrrosia × nipponica

Pyrrosia × nipponica, commonly known as the Japanese rock fern, is a hybrid species belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is a terrestrial fern that typically grows on rocks or tree trunks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features lance-shaped, leathery fronds with a dark green color and a serrated margin. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds in clusters called sori.

Pyrrosia × nipponica is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
