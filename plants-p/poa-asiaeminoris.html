Poa asiaeminoris

Poa asiaeminoris, commonly known as the Asian fine grass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, temperate climates and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along riverbanks. The plant has slender, tufted stems and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Poa asiaeminoris is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in wetland environments.
