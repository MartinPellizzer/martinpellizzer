Peperomia kotana

Peperomia kotana, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green, velvety leaves that are oval to heart-shaped, with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, typically appearing in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate watering.

Peperomia kotana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and low maintenance requirements.
