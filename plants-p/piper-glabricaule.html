Piper glabricaule

Piper glabricaule, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed pepper plant, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a member of the Piperaceae family, which includes many pepper species. The plant is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and lance-shaped leaves. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in spikes.

The fruit, a small berry, contains the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.
