Poa pichinchensis

Poa pichinchensis, commonly known as the Pichincha grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. This perennial grass typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in moist, temperate climates. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping or as a ground cover.
