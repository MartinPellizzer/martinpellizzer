Pleopeltis appressa

Pleopeltis appressa, commonly known as the narrow-leaf rock fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and dark green, leathery leaves. The fronds are typically narrow and have a distinctive, almost metallic sheen. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or rocky surfaces.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
