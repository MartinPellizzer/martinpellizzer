Podanthum limonifolium var. olympicum

Podanthum limonifolium var. olympicum, commonly known as the Olympic lemonleaf.

This variant is characterized by its distinctive lemon-scented foliage, which emits a fresh, citrus-like aroma. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a waxy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique fragrance and potential ornamental value in horticultural settings.
