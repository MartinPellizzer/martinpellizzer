Pleurothallis verboonenii

Pleurothallis verboonenii, commonly known as the Verboonen's pleurothallis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Ecuador.

It is a small epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments within the cloud forests. The plant features delicate, linear leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It is notable for its intricate floral structure and is often found growing on trees or rocks in its natural habitat.

Due to its rarity and specific ecological requirements, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
