Portulaca Pilosa F. Procumbens

Portulaca pilosa f. procumbens is a unique variant of the common moss rose known for its low-growing, spreading habit. This succulent plant features small, fleshy leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers. Its flowers, which range from pink to red, bloom in late summer and add a vibrant touch to the landscape. Despite its delicate appearance, this plant is remarkably hardy and easy to care for.
