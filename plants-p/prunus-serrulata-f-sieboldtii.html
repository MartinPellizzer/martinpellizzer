Prunus serrulata f. sieboldtii

Prunus serrulata f. sieboldtii, commonly known as the Siebold's cherry, is a compact, ornamental shrub or small tree native to Japan.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and profuse displays of white or pale pink flowers in early spring. The leaves are ovate with finely serrated edges, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
