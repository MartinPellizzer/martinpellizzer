Phoebe cavaleriei

Phoebe cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese phoebe, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like laurel and avocado. The tree is characterized by its simple, opposite, and elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by dark blue berries that are edible for birds.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and seasonal interest.
