Pilea globosa

Pilea globosa, commonly known as the African snowball, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves that are slightly hairy and have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring, creating a fluffy appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Pilea globosa is popular as a decorative indoor plant due to its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements.
