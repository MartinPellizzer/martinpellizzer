Phreatia nana

Phreatia nana, commonly known as the dwarf phreatia, is a small epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees in humid, shaded forest environments. The plant has fleshy, dark green leaves that help it store water in its natural habitat. Its flowers are small and usually white or pale yellow, appearing in clusters.

Phreatia nana is adapted to low light conditions and requires high humidity to thrive.
