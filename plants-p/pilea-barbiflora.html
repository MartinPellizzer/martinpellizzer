Pilea barbiflora

Pilea barbiflora, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a flowering plant native to China.

It features rosettes of glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers indirect sunlight.

Pilea barbiflora is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
