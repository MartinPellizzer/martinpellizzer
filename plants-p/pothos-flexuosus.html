Pothos flexuosus

Pothos flexuosus, commonly known as the devil's ivy, is a trailing evergreen vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, waxy leaves that can vary in color from deep green to variegated patterns. The plant is highly adaptable, thriving in low-light conditions and requiring minimal care. Its stems can grow long and cascading, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets or shelves.

Pothos flexuosus is also known for its ability to purify indoor air, adding to its appeal as a houseplant.
