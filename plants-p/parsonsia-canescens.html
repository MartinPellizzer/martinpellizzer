Parsonsia canescens

Parsonsia canescens, commonly known as the silky nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, featuring smooth, often greyish bark. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange with a distinctive red or purple throat. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often covered in fine, silky hairs.

The fruit is a fleshy, red berry that contains several seeds.
