Pithecellobium brongniartii

Pithecellobium brongniartii, commonly known as the yellow glory pea, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces pods containing edible seeds, which are often used in traditional cuisines. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Botanically, it is notable for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it a valuable plant in agricultural systems.
