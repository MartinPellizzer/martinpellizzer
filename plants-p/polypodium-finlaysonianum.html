Polypodium finlaysonianum

Polypodium finlaysonianum, commonly known as the Finlayson's fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically divided into small, linear segments. The plant is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its rhizome is thick and creeping, allowing it to spread and form dense colonies.

The spores are produced on the underside of the fronds in clusters, contributing to its reproductive strategy.
