Philodendron longipes

Philodendron longipes, commonly known as the longipes philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with light green and white markings. The plant has a vigorous growth habit and can reach lengths of several meters when given support. Its stems are typically reddish-brown and covered in a thick, waxy cuticle.

Philodendron longipes prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
