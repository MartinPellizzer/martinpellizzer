Primulina mollifolia

Primulina mollifolia, commonly known as the velvet leaf.

It is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its thick, velvety leaves that are often covered in a fine, soft pubescence. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color.

Primulina mollifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.
