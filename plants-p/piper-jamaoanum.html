Piper jamaoanum

Piper jamaoanum, commonly known as Jamaican pepper or wild pepper, is a flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the Caribbean, particularly Jamaica, and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into three-lobed berries. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly serrated margin.

The plant is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
