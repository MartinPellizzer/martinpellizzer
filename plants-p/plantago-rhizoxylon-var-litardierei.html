Plantago rhizoxylon var. litardierei

Plantago rhizoxylon var. litardierei, commonly known as the Litardiere's plantain, is a variant of the plantain species native to specific regions in southern Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, oval-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, which are pollinated by wind. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread vegetatively and colonize disturbed soils effectively.

This variant is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
