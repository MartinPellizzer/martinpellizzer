Pilea peploides var. cavaleriei

Pilea peploides var. cavaleriei, commonly known as the velvet pilea, is a compact, low-growing evergreen shrub native to China.

It features dense, rounded foliage composed of velvety, dark green leaves that are slightly hairy to the touch. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and easy care requirements make it a favored ornamental plant in both residential and commercial settings.
