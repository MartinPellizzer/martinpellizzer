Podocarpus ledermannii var. expansus

Podocarpus ledermannii var. expansus, commonly known as the broad-leafed podocarp, is a species of coniferous evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and distinctive, clustered cones. The leaves are typically broad and leathery, providing a dense canopy that offers shade and habitat for various wildlife. This plant thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Its wood is valued for its durability and is used in construction and furniture-making.
