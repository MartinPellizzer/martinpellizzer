Pennisetum setaceum subsp. orientale

Pennisetum setaceum subsp.

orientale, commonly known as the oriental fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to southern and eastern Asia. It features slender, upright clumps with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often tinged with silver or blue-green.

The plant produces delicate, feathery plumes of pale pink or white flowers in late summer to early autumn. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in full sun with well-drained soil.

This species is often used in gardens for its architectural form and seasonal display.
