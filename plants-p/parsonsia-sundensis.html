Parsonsia sundensis

Parsonsia sundensis, commonly known as the Sundanese flame vine, is a flowering plant native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its striking orange-red tubular flowers. The plant features woody stems and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its leaves are simple and opposite, with a glossy surface.
