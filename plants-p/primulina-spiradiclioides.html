Primulina spiradiclioides

Primulina spiradiclioides, commonly known as the spiral-leaved primulina, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, spiral-arranged leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated as a houseplant in many parts of the world.
