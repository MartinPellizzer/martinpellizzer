Picea smithiana subsp. nepalensis

Picea smithiana subsp.

nepalensis, commonly known as the Nepal fir, is a coniferous tree native to the Himalayas. It is characterized by its narrow, conical shape and dark green, needle-like leaves.

The tree produces small, spherical cones that mature to a reddish-brown color. It thrives in cool, moist environments at high altitudes.

Its botanical features make it an important species in alpine and subalpine ecosystems.
