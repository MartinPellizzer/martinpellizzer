Persica vulgaris flavorubens

Persica vulgaris flavorubens, commonly known as the red-flowered peach.

This plant is a variant of the peach species characterized by its distinctive reddish-pink flowers. It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere. The plant produces small, sweet fruits with a vibrant coloration.

Its ornamental value is highly appreciated in gardens and landscapes.
