Pittosporum johnstonianum

Pittosporum johnstonianum, commonly known as the Johnston's pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by dark blue berries. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that are elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

Pittosporum johnstonianum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
