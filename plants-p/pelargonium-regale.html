Pelargonium regale

Pelargonium regale, commonly known as the Royal geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink, red, and white. The leaves are typically rounded and have a slightly hairy texture, while the stems are often reddish in color. It prefers full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of climates.
