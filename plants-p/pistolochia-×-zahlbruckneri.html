Pistolochia × zahlbruckneri

Pistolochia × zahlbruckneri, commonly known as the hybrid poinsettia, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between *Pistolochia lutea* and *Pistolochia tetrandra*.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its striking, colorful bracts that resemble flowers, which are actually modified leaves. The plant is a shrub with woody stems and is often cultivated for its ornamental value during the holiday season. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil and ample sunlight.

The botanical features of *Pistolochia × zahlbruckneri* make it a popular choice for both gardeners and commercial growers.
