Panicum flexirameum

Panicum flexirameum, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a warm-season grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and dense, reddish-brown inflorescences that appear in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green hue and is often found in disturbed soils or open areas. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a common weed in agricultural fields.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various environmental conditions.
