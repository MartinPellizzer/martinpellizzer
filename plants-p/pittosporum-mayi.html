Pittosporum mayi

Pittosporum mayi, commonly known as the New Zealand pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage, which is typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are often followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

Pittosporum mayi is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in native habitats.
