Peperomia australana var. ovalilimba

Peperomia australana var. ovalilimba, commonly known as the oval-leaved peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval in shape, with a deep green color and a slightly textured surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
