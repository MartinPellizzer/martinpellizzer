Philodendron aurantiifolium

Philodendron aurantiifolium, commonly known as the golden leaf philodendron, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant yellow or orange, especially when exposed to bright, indirect light. The plant has a climbing habit and produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers. Its foliage is highly ornamental, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

It thrives in warm, humid environments with well-draining soil and regular watering.
