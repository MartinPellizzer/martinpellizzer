Peperomia spathulata

Peperomia spathulata, commonly known as the broad-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval or spatulate in shape, often with a deep green color and prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, arranged in clusters at the leaf axils. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and drought tolerance.

Peperomia spathulata is valued for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
