Petilium

Petilium, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and small, delicate flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often grown as an ornamental in gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

The leaves are covered in fine hairs, which give the plant its distinctive appearance.
