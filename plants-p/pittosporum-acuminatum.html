Pittosporum acuminatum

Pittosporum acuminatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dark green, narrow, leathery leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers a sunny to partially shaded location.

Pittosporum acuminatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscaping.
