Peperomia bicolor var. peduncularis

Peperomia bicolor var. peduncularis, commonly known as the variegated peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with striking variegation of white, cream, and green patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often hidden beneath its foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
