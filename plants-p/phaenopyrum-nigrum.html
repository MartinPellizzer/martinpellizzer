Phaenopyrum nigrum

Phaenopyrum nigrum, commonly known as the black nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, dark purple to black berries that mature in the fall. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is native to parts of Europe and Asia. Its leaves are ovate with a slightly hairy texture, and it produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers.

The plant contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
