Polygonum amplexicaule var. sinense

Polygonum amplexicaule var. sinense, commonly known as Chinese knotweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features stout, reddish stems that often have a hairy texture. The leaves are opposite, ovate to elliptic in shape, and often have a waxy coating. It produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in spikes, which bloom in late summer.

This plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in moist, disturbed soils.
