Poa lahulensis

Poa lahulensis, commonly known as the Lahul grass, is a species of grass native to the Himalayan regions of India and Nepal.

It is a perennial plant that typically grows in high-altitude meadows and alpine environments. The plant has slender, tufted leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to cold climates and is often found in rocky or mountainous terrain.

Poa lahulensis plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various alpine flora and fauna.
