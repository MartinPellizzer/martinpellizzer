Philodendron laciniatum

Philodendron laciniatum, commonly known as the Shaggy Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are deeply lobed and often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. The plant has a vigorous growth habit and can reach impressive heights when provided with a suitable support structure. Its foliage is typically a deep green, with some varieties displaying variegation.

Philodendron laciniatum thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and humidity to flourish.
