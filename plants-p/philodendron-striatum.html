Philodendron striatum

Philodendron striatum, commonly known as the zebra philodendron, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive variegated leaves, which feature alternating light and dark green stripes. The plant has a climbing habit, often using its aerial roots to scale supports in its natural habitat. Its foliage is evergreen, providing a striking visual appeal in both indoor and outdoor settings.

Philodendron striatum prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
