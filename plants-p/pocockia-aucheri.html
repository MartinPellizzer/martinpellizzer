Pocockia aucheri

Pocockia aucheri, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 10-20 centimeters. The plant features yellow daisy-like flowers with white ray florets, blooming from late winter to early spring. Its leaves are alternate, hairy, and often have a distinctive heart-shaped base.

Pocockia aucheri is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
