Polypodium shensiense var. nigrovenium

Polypodium shensiense var. nigrovenium, commonly known as the black venation polypody, is a fern species native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds with a distinctive black venation pattern. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in forest ecosystems.
