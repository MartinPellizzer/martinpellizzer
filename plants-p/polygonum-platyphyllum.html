Polygonum platyphyllum

Polygonum platyphyllum, commonly known as broadleaf knotweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like buckwheat and sorrel. The plant features rhubarb-like leaves that are broad and slightly notched, with a smooth, waxy surface. Its small, greenish-white flowers appear in clusters during the summer months.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as stream banks and forest floors.
