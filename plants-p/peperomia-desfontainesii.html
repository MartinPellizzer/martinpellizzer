Peperomia desfontainesii

Peperomia desfontainesii, commonly known as the velvetleaf peperomia, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a compact, low-growing plant with thick, fleshy leaves that are usually dark green and often have a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia desfontainesii is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
