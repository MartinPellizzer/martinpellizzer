Peltophorum dubium var. dubium

Peltophorum dubium var. dubium, commonly known as the sacred tree or the flame of the forest, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces long, slender pods that contain several seeds.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
