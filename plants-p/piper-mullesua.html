Piper mullesua

Piper mullesua, commonly known as the Malayan black pepper, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and dark green, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into elongated, cylindrical berries. These berries are harvested when they turn from green to dark purple or black, giving the plant its common name.

Piper mullesua is valued for its aromatic and medicinal properties, often used in traditional herbal remedies.
