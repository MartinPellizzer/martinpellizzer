Prunus serrulata ariake

Prunus serrulata ariake, commonly known as the Ariake cherry blossom, is a species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant is a deciduous tree with a rounded crown and smooth, gray bark. Its leaves are oval-shaped with serrated edges, giving it the species name "serrulata.".

This ornamental tree is highly valued for its aesthetic appeal and cultural significance in Japanese gardens.
