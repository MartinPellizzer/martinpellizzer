Philodendron latilobum

Philodendron latilobum, commonly known as the broad-lobed philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with broad lobes and a dark green, glossy surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by yellowish berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its vigorous growth habit makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and vertical displays.
