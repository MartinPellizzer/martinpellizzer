Paradombeya sinensis

Paradombeya sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese parasol tree, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, umbrella-shaped canopy composed of palmate leaves. The tree produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers in the spring. Its fruit is a woody, spiky capsule that splits open to release seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and shade-providing capabilities.
