Pilosella eusciadia

Pilosella eusciadia, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit, with hairy stems and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in meadows and grasslands. The plant produces small, oval seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

It is often considered a weed in agricultural settings due to its rapid growth and ability to colonize disturbed soils.
