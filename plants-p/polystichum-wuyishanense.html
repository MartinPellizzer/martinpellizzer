Polystichum wuyishanense

Polystichum wuyishanense, commonly known as the Wuyi Mountain shield fern, is a species of shield fern native to the Wuyi Mountains in China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen fronds that form a compact, upright rosette. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its fronds are deeply divided, with a leathery texture and a dark green color.

Polystichum wuyishanense is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
