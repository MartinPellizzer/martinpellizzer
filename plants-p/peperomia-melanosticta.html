Peperomia melanosticta

Peperomia melanosticta, commonly known as the black-stained peperomia, is a compact, low-growing herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are often dark green with a reddish or purplish tinge, especially on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its tolerance for dry air.

Peperomia melanosticta is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care, making it a popular choice for houseplants.
