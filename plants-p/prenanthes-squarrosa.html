Prenanthes squarrosa

Prenanthes squarrosa, commonly known as the hairy leaf arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive hairy texture, and its flowers are small, white, and clustered in umbels. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments such as wetlands and stream banks. It is often found in the eastern and central United States.

Botanically, it belongs to the Araceae family, which is characterized by its unique floral structure.
