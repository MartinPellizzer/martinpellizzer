Pouteria Endlicheri

Pouteria endlicheri is a unique tropical plant known for its distinctive fruit and ecological significance. Native to the rainforests of Central and South America, it thrives in warm, humid climates with rich soil. The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall, featuring broad leaves and a sturdy trunk. Its fruit, often called the "soursop," is prized for its sweet, creamy pulp and medicinal properties. This plant plays an important role in both local ecosystems and traditional medicine practices.
