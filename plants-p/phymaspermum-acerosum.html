Phymaspermum acerosum

Phymaspermum acerosum, commonly known as the hardy phymaspermum, is a member of the family Phymaspermataceae.

It is a small, evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green in color. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, arranged in clusters, and it produces small, fleshy fruits.

Phymaspermum acerosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in harsh environmental conditions.
