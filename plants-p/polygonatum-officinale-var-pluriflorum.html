Polygonatum officinale var. pluriflorum

Polygonatum officinale var. pluriflorum, commonly known as the Chinese Solomon's Seal, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features rhizomatous growth and produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers in late spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a fan-like pattern along the stems. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and traditional herbal medicine.
