Plectranthus bicolor

Plectranthus bicolor, commonly known as the purple heart plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive bi-colored leaves, which feature a striking contrast between deep purple and green. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale purple or white, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Plectranthus bicolor is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and ease of care.
