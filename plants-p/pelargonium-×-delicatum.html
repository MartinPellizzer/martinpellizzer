Pelargonium × delicatum

Pelargonium × delicatum, commonly known as the sweet geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with rounded, lobed leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and pleasant scent.
