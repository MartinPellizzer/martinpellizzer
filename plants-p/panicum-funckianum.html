Panicum funckianum

Panicum funckianum, commonly known as the Funck's panicgrass, is a species of grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by wind. The plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Panicum funckianum plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various insects and small animals.
