Psittacanthus plumieri

Psittacanthus plumieri, commonly known as the Brazilian pineapple or golden star fern, is a member of the Araliaceae family.

It is a climbing plant native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. Psittacanthus plumieri thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
