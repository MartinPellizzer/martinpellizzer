Polystachya transvaalensis

Polystachya transvaalensis, commonly known as the Transvaal grass.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. It typically grows in open woodlands and grasslands, often reaching heights of up to two meters.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
