Potentilla cinerea f. vestita

Potentilla cinerea f. vestita, commonly known as the silver cinquefoil.

This plant is a flowering shrub characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and small, yellow flowers. It is native to the mountainous regions of western North America, including parts of British Columbia and the Pacific Northwest. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.
