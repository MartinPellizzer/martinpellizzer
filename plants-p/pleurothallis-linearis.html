Pleurothallis linearis

Pleurothallis linearis, commonly known as the linear orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale yellow. This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing epiphytically on trees or rocks. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air, making it well-suited for its natural habitat.

Due to its unique appearance and growth habits, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
