Peperomia trinervis var. minor

Peperomia trinervis var. minor, commonly known as the miniature peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, ovate to elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green, with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, emerging from the leaf axils. It is a popular houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.

This variety is well-suited for indoor environments and thrives in bright, indirect light with moderate humidity.
