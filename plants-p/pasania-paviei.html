Pasania paviei

Pasania paviei, commonly known as the Java apple or kembang sari, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in the spring. Its fruit is a small, round, fleshy drupe with a sweet, aromatic flavor.

The leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly serrated edge.
