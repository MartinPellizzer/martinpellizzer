Peperomia fernandopoiana

Peperomia fernandopoiana, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a velvety texture, often covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia fernandopoiana is prized for its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
