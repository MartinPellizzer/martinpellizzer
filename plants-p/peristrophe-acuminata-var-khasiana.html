Peristrophe acuminata var. khasiana

Peristrophe acuminata var. khasiana, commonly known as the Khasi peristrophe, is a climbing plant native to the northeastern regions of India.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
