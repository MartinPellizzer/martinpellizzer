Persicaria mollis

Persicaria mollis, commonly known as soft smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive, velvety foliage that feels soft to the touch. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color, appearing in late summer. Persicaria mollis thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
