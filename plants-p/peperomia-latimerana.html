Peperomia latimerana

Peperomia latimerana, commonly known as the Hawaiian peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It features broad, waxy leaves that are typically light green to bluish-green in color, often with a subtle metallic sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale pink, arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia latimerana is known for its drought tolerance and ability to adapt well to a variety of indoor conditions.
