Phillyrea angustifolia subsp. gabrielis

Phillyrea angustifolia subsp.

gabrielis, commonly known as the Gabriel's Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy.

The plant produces clusters of white, fragrant flowers in the spring, which are followed by small, berry-like fruits. It is adapted to dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in arid climates.
