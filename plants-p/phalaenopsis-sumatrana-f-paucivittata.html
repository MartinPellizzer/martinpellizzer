Phalaenopsis sumatrana f. paucivittata

Phalaenopsis sumatrana f. paucivittata, commonly known as the Sumatran moth orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, moth-like flowers that are typically white or pale pink with intricate patterns. The plant produces showy, long-lasting blooms that can persist for several months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent humidity and well-draining potting media.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
