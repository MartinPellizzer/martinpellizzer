Phillyrea angustifolia var. spinescens

Phillyrea angustifolia var. spinescens, commonly known as the spiny Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and slightly leathery. The plant produces white, fragrant flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, spherical berries. It is characterized by its spiny stems and branches, which give it a distinctive appearance.

This variety is often found in rocky soils and coastal areas, where it thrives in well-drained conditions.
