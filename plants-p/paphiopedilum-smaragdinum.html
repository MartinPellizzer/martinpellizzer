Paphiopedilum smaragdinum

Paphiopedilum smaragdinum, commonly known as the Emerald Lady orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, low-growing plant with a single, fleshy rhizome and dark green, pleated leaves. The flowers are showy, with a greenish-yellow lip and a distinctive, elongated spur. It blooms in spring or early summer, producing up to six fragrant, waxy flowers.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique appearance and hardiness.
