Puccinellia intricata

Puccinellia intricata, commonly known as the intricate needlegrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its fine, wiry leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. This species is typically found in arid and semi-arid regions, often growing in sandy or gravelly soils. Puccinellia intricata is adapted to harsh environments, demonstrating resilience to drought and salinity.
