Polystichum mortizianum

Polystichum mortizianum, commonly known as the Mortizian's sword fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The fronds are deeply divided into narrow, linear segments with a leathery texture and a dark green color. This fern thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

It is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with appropriate conditions.
