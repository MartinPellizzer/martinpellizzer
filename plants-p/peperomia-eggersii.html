Peperomia eggersii

Peperomia eggersii, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically grayish-green with a metallic sheen, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or red, depending on the variety. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia eggersii is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive foliage.
