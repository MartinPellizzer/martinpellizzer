Panterpa mollissima

Panterpa mollissima, commonly known as the silky panther plant, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and somewhat elusive species.
