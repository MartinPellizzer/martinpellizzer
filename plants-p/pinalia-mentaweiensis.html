Pinalia mentaweiensis

Pinalia mentaweiensis, commonly known as the Mentawai pine.

It is a coniferous tree native to the Mentawai Islands in Indonesia. The plant has needle-like leaves that are typically arranged in clusters, and it produces cones that contain its seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in coastal and mountainous regions.

The species is valued for its timber and plays an important ecological role in its native habitat.
