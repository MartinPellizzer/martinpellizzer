Peperomia reflexa var. parvifolia

Peperomia reflexa var. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its low maintenance and attractive foliage make it a versatile addition to both indoor and shaded outdoor spaces.
