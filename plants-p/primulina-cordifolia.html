Primulina cordifolia

Primulina cordifolia, commonly known as the heart-leaf primulina, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. Primulina cordifolia is valued in horticulture for its aesthetic qualities and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in shaded gardens.
