Pelargonium × longipetalum

Pelargonium × longipetalum, commonly known as the long-petaled geranium, is a hybrid perennial plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, long-stemmed flowers that bloom in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a strong, aromatic scent. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Pelargonium × longipetalum is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
