Polystichum libingii

Polystichum libingii, commonly known as the Libing's wood fern, is a species of fern native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its tall, upright fronds with a dark green, leathery texture. The plant produces sporangia on the underside of its fronds, which are clustered in sori, a typical feature of ferns. Polystichum libingii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
