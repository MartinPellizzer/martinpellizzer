Parrycactus schwarzii

Parrycactus schwarzii, commonly known as the Schwerzii cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of northern Mexico.

It is a small, spineless cactus that typically grows to a height of about 10 to 15 centimeters. The plant features a globular stem with a smooth, pale green surface that helps it retain moisture in its harsh environment. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, blooming in late spring or early summer.

This cactus is well adapted to extreme drought conditions, making it a resilient and unique member of the Cactaceae family.
