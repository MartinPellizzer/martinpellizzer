Peperomia guadaloupensis var. pubescens

Peperomia guadaloupensis var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Mexico.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are covered in fine, hairy texture, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of green or red. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its low maintenance and attractive foliage make it a favored ornamental plant.
