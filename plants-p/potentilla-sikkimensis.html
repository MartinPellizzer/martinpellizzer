Potentilla sikkimensis

Potentilla sikkimensis, commonly known as the Sikkim cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in alpine and subalpine environments. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, and its leaves are palmately divided with five lobes. It is characterized by its hardy nature and ability to thrive in rocky, well-drained soils.

Potentilla sikkimensis plays an important role in the ecological balance of its native habitat.
