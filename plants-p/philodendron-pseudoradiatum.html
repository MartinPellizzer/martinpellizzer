Philodendron pseudoradiatum

Philodendron pseudoradiatum, commonly known as the Radiatum Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically bright green with a slightly velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by round, green berries. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique leaf patterns and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
