Pteris barteri

Pteris barteri, commonly known as the Barter's water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Pteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically bright green and have a feathery appearance. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including India, China, and Southeast Asia. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found growing near water bodies or in humid forests.

Pteris barteri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated as a houseplant.
