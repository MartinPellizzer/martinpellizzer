Panicum trichostachyum

Panicum trichostachyum, commonly known as the silky grass or silky panic grass, is a warm-season grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves with a silvery-blue hue on the underside. The plant produces dense, spike-like inflorescences that are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements, it is widely cultivated in regions with mild winters.
