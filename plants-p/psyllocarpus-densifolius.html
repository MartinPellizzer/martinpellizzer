Psyllocarpus densifolius

Psyllocarpus densifolius, commonly known as the dense-leaved psyllocarpus, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and can grow up to 30 meters in height. The plant has compound leaves with numerous small leaflets, and its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow. It produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds.

This species is valued for its timber and is often found in lowland rainforests.
