Pelargonium fusciflorum

Pelargonium fusciflorum, commonly known as the dark-flowered geranium, is a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of small, dark purple to almost black flowers. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub, reaching up to 30 cm in height. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Pelargonium fusciflorum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
