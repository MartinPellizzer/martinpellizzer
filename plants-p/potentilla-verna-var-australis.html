Potentilla verna var. australis

Potentilla verna var. australis, commonly known as the Australian spring cinquefoil, is a low-growing shrub native to Australia.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from September to November. The plant has compound leaves composed of five to seven oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and open woodlands.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
