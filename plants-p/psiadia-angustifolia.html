Psiadia angustifolia

Psiadia angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf psiadia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and it produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer.
