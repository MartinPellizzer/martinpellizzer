Phlox pilosa var. virens

Phlox pilosa var. virens, commonly known as the greenleaf phlox, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Polemoniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, clustered flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.
