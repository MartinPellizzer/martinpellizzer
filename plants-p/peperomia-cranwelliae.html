Peperomia cranwelliae

Peperomia cranwelliae, commonly known as the Cranwell's Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish or bronze hue on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, usually in shades of green or white, which are not particularly showy. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and low maintenance requirements, it is well-suited for container cultivation.
