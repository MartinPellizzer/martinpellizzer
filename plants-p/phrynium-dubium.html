Phrynium dubium

Phrynium dubium, commonly known as the arrowleaf plant, is a tropical perennial belonging to the Araceae family.

It is native to Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features broad, arrow-shaped leaves with a smooth, waxy surface, and it produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.

The rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread easily in suitable conditions.
