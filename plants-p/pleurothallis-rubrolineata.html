Pleurothallis rubrolineata

Pleurothallis rubrolineata, commonly known as the red-lined pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, linear leaves and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and pink, often with red or orange markings. Its flowers are usually arranged in a raceme and bloom during the rainy season.

Due to its striking appearance and specific habitat requirements, it is a sought-after plant among orchid enthusiasts.
