Pseudoliparis kortylewskiana

Pseudoliparis kortylewskiana, commonly known as the Kortylewskiana sea anemone, is a marine organism belonging to the order Actinii.

It is characterized by its delicate, translucent polyps and a central gastrovascular cavity. The plant, or more accurately the animal, exhibits a unique colonial structure with a network of interconnected polyps. It thrives in shallow coastal waters, often found on rocky substrates.

Its botanical description is more accurately classified under marine biology rather than traditional botany.
