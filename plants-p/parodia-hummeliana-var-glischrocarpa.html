Parodia hummeliana var. glischrocarpa

Parodia hummeliana var. glischrocarpa, commonly known as the Hummel's Parodia, is a unique cacti species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and is typically covered in white to pale green spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking contrast to its otherwise muted foliage. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in specialized collections.
