Paphiopedilum niveum f. album

Paphiopedilum niveum f. album, commonly known as the White Orchid, is a rare and striking cultivar of the Paphiopedilum genus.

It is characterized by its predominantly white petals and sepals, which give it a strikingly elegant appearance. This plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, shaded environments. It has a unique sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

The flowers are highly fragrant and bloom during the spring or early summer, making it a prized addition to orchid collections.
