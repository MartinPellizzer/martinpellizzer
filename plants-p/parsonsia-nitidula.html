Parsonsia nitidula

Parsonsia nitidula, commonly known as the silky parsnip, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Australia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its striking, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, smooth seeds. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a waxy, glossy appearance.
