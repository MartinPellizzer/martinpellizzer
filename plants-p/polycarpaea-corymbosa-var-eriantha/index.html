Polycarpaea Corymbosa Var. Eriantha

Polycarpaea corymbosa var. eriantha is a striking flowering plant native to parts of Africa. It is known for its vibrant, showy blooms that appear in clusters during the rainy season. This variant of Polycarpaea corymbosa is distinguished by its soft, hairy leaves and unique seed structures. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or savanna habitats. Due to its ornamental value, it is gaining popularity among horticulturists and plant enthusiasts.
