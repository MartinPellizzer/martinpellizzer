Polianthes americana

Polianthes americana, commonly known as the American spider lily, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, funnel-shaped flowers that range from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 feet and features strap-like, grassy leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a dramatic touch to gardens with their unique appearance.
