Philodendron wittianum

Philodendron wittianum, commonly known as the Wittianum Philodendron, is a climbing tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by dark blue or black berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and greenhouses.
