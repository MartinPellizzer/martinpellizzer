Pygeum gitingense

Pygeum gitingense, commonly known as the Gitinge African cherry, is a small to medium-sized tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and elliptical leaves with a glossy upper surface. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers that are clustered in terminal panicles, followed by small, round fruits containing a single seed.
