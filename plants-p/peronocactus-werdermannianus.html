Peronocactus werdermannianus

Peronocactus werdermannianus, commonly known as the Werdermann's cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stem that is covered in white, woolly spines and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant thrives in arid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is a slow-growing plant that can reach heights of up to 1 meter.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and rockeries.
