Potentilla congdonis

Potentilla congdonis, commonly known as Chinese firecracker, is a flowering plant native to China and Korea.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Potentilla congdonis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
