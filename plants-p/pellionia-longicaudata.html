Pellionia longicaudata

Pellionia longicaudata, commonly known as the velvetleaf pellionia, is a tropical plant native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Pellionia longicaudata thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
