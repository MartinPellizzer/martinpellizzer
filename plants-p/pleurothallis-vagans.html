Pleurothallis vagans

Pleurothallis vagans, commonly known as the wandering pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically growing on trees or rocks. The plant features narrow, strap-like leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its flowers are often clustered in racemes and bloom during the wet season.

Pleurothallis vagans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
