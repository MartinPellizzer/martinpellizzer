Phaleria pilistyla

Phaleria pilistyla, commonly known as the Java Laurel, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its evergreen leaves and fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.
