Plectronia ovata

Plectronia ovata, commonly known as the winged bean, is a tropical legume native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, winged pods that enclose the edible seeds. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its nutritious seeds and edible leaves.

The botanical features of Plectronia ovata make it an important crop in sustainable agriculture.
