Pandanus bilamellatus

Pandanus bilamellatus, commonly known as the double-leaf pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and long, narrow leaves that are often arranged in a spiral pattern. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters at the top of the plant. Its fruit is a spiny, fibrous structure that contains a single seed.

This species is often used in traditional crafts and is valued for its ornamental appearance.
