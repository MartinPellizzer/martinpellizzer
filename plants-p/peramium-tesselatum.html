Peramium tesselatum

Peramium tesselatum, commonly known as the tessellated peramium, is a unique plant species characterized by its intricate leaf patterns.

It is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features small, clustered leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle, aiding in moisture retention. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, producing a subtle fragrance that attracts pollinators.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, Peramium tesselatum is considered a delicately preserved species in its native ecosystems.
