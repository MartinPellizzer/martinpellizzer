Ponista

Ponista, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Artemisia and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and aromatic scent. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Ponista is also valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
