Piper zhorquinense

Piper zhorquinense, commonly known as the Zhorquin pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its vine-like growth habit and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. Its fruits are typically small, berry-like, and contain a mild to spicy flavor.

It is often cultivated for its aromatic leaves and fruit, which are used in local cuisines and traditional medicine.
