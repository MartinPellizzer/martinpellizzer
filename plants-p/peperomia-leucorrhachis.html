Peperomia leucorrhachis

Peperomia leucorrhachis, commonly known as the white-stemmed peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features upright, branching stems that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or white, which are followed by small, round berries. Its foliage consists of thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slight variegation.

Peperomia leucorrhachis is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive appearance.
