Panicum lentigerum

Panicum lentigerum, commonly known as slender panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, often tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers, typical of the grass family. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Panicum lentigerum is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
