Pleurothallis rubens

Pleurothallis rubens, commonly known as the red pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, often red or orange-colored flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded forest environments. Its flowers are usually small, with intricate patterns and a strong scent, which helps attract pollinators like bees and moths.

Due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
