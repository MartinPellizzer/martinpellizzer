Pelargonium asarifolium

Pelargonium asarifolium, commonly known as the asarifolium geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters and features slender, upright stems. Its foliage is often fragrant and can vary in color from dark green to reddish hues.

Pelargonium asarifolium is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and as a houseplant.
