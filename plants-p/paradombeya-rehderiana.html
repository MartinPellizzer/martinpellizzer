Paradombeya rehderiana

Paradombeya rehderiana, commonly known as the Rehder's paradombeya, is a rare and striking member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia, specifically found in parts of Indonesia. The plant features large, showy inflorescences with a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of the Araceae family. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, often with a waxy texture that helps it thrive in humid environments.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by botanists and horticulturists.
