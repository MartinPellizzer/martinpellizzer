Persicaria acuminata

Persicaria acuminata, commonly known as heartleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous annual plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like buckwheat and sorrel. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and covered in fine hairs. It produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming from late spring to early fall.

Persicaria acuminata is often found in wet or disturbed soils, such as along stream banks and in moist meadows.
