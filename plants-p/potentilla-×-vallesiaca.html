Potentilla × vallesiaca

Potentilla × vallesiaca, commonly known as the Vallesia or Vallesian cinquefoil, is a hybrid shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens and alpine landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
