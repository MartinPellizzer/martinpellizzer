Peperomia degeneri

Peperomia degeneri, commonly known as the Shiny Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which appear in clusters along the stems. It is a popular houseplant due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia degeneri prefers well-draining soil and moderate watering, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.
