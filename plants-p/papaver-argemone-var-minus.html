Papaver argemone var. minus

Papaver argemone var. minus, commonly known as the lesser argemone, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features striking yellow flowers with a distinctive red center, typical of the Papaveraceae family. The plant has smooth, lobed leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain oily seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and historical use in traditional medicine, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
