Panicum kraussii

Panicum kraussii, commonly known as Krassmann's grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and savannas, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces fluffy, panicle-like inflorescences that are often pink or red in color. It is drought-tolerant and can withstand poor soil conditions, making it adaptable to various environments.

This grass is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
