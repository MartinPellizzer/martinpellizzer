Poa sierrae

Poa sierrae, commonly known as Sierra grass, is a species of grass native to the mountainous regions of North America.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and fine, blue-green foliage. This plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine environments. Poa sierrae is adapted to cold climates and can withstand harsh weather conditions.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small organisms in its native range.
