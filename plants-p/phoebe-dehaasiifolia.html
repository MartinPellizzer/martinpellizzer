Phoebe dehaasiifolia

Phoebe dehaasiifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf phoebe, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by small, round berries.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
