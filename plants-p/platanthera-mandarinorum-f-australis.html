Platanthera mandarinorum f. australis

Platanthera mandarinorum f. australis, commonly known as the Australian mandarin orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in wet, shaded environments such as rainforests and swampy areas. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a distinctive greenish hue on the lip.
