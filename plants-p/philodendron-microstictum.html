Philodendron microstictum

Philodendron microstictum, commonly known as the laceleaf philodendron, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, finely veined, heart-shaped leaves that give it a delicate, lace-like appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers, which are typically followed by dark red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Philodendron microstictum is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
