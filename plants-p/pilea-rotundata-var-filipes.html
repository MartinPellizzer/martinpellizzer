Pilea rotundata var. filipes

Pilea rotundata var. filipes, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features round, fleshy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slight waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its compact growth habit and air-purifying qualities.
