Prunus aestiva

Prunus aestiva, commonly known as the summer cherry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to parts of Asia.

It produces small, white or pink flowers in the spring, which are followed by edible, sweet red berries in the summer. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and has a moderate growth rate. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Prunus aestiva is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit production.
