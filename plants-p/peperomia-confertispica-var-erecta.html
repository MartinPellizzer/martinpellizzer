Peperomia confertispica var. erecta

Peperomia confertispica var. erecta, commonly known as the upright peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features dense, upright foliage with oval to lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and slightly glossy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters, though it is primarily grown for its foliage rather than its blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its compact size and attractive appearance make it a popular choice for tabletop displays and container gardening.
