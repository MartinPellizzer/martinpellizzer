Pyrus amygdaliformis latifolius

Pyrus amygdaliformis latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf pear, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and showy, white or pink flowers that bloom in spring.
