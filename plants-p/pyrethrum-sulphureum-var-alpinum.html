Pyrethrum sulphureum var. alpinum

Pyrethrum sulphureum var. alpinum, commonly known as the Alpine sulphur pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high elevations. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
