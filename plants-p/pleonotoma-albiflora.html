Pleonotoma albiflora

Pleonotoma albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered pleonotoma, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, white petals that bloom in clusters, giving it a striking appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are simple and lance-shaped, with a smooth texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
