Pollinia microstachya

Pollinia microstachya, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces pollinia, which are specialized structures that contain the pollen grains, a key feature in orchid reproduction. The leaves are linear and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.
