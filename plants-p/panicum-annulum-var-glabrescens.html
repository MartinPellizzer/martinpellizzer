Panicum annulum var. glabrescens

Panicum annulum var. glabrescens, commonly known as the glabrous panic grass, is a variant of the annual panic grass native to warm temperate and subtropical regions.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Due to its adaptability, it is considered an invasive species in some regions.
