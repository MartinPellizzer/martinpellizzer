Phyllobolus nitidus

Phyllobolus nitidus, commonly known as the glossy leaf bug, is a species of plant in the family Phyllobolidae.

It is characterized by its smooth, waxy leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant is native to tropical regions of Africa and is often found in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The plant's botanical features make it an interesting subject for both ecological and medicinal research.
