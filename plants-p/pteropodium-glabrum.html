Pteropodium glabrum

Pteropodium glabrum, commonly known as the smooth pteropodium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant features a short, rhizomatous stem with broad, heart-shaped leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in a spadix, typical of the Araceae family.

The plant is often grown as an ornamental due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
