Philodendron macropodum

Philodendron macropodum, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly variegated appearance. The plant has a robust stem that can grow up to several meters in length, allowing it to climb trees or other structures. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its striking appearance.

Philodendron macropodum thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.
