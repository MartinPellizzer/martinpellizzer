Piper golfitoense

Piper golfitoense, commonly known as the Golfito pepper, is a climbing vine native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various pepper species. The plant produces small, green berries that mature to a dark purple color, containing aromatic seeds. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a rough texture.

Piper golfitoense is valued for its unique flavor and is used in traditional medicine and cuisine.
