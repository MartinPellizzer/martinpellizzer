Phrynium thorelii

Phrynium thorelii, commonly known as the flame leaf plant, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from red to orange. The plant produces striking, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens due to its aesthetic appeal.
