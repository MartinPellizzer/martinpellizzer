Piper kunstleri

Piper kunstleri, commonly known as the Kunstler's pepper vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by small, round berries. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
