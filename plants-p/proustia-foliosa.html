Proustia foliosa

Proustia foliosa, commonly known as the leafy proustia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with a central disk surrounded by ray florets.

Proustia foliosa is often found in rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
