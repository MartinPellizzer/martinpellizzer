Porpax lanii

Porpax lanii, commonly known as the silky porpax, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
