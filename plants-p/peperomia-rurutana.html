Peperomia rurutana

Peperomia rurutana, commonly known as the Rurutana Peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance houseplant native to tropical regions.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia rurutana is valued for its ornamental foliage and ability to purify the air.
