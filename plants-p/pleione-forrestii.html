Pleione forrestii

Pleione forrestii, commonly known as the Yunnan pleione, is a rare and elegant orchid native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan, China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant typically grows at high altitudes, often in shaded, humid environments, and thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its rhizomatous growth habit and foliage consist of broad, strap-like leaves that are evergreen.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, it is highly valued in horticulture and conservation efforts.
