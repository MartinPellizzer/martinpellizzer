Panicum Scoparium Var. Pauciflorum

Little bluestem, scientifically known as Panicum scoparium var. pauciflorum, is a native grass species that thrives in a variety of environmental conditions. Found primarily in the central and eastern United States, it is well adapted to both wet and dry soils. This plant is recognized for its striking blue-green foliage and its ability to form dense, attractive clumps. It plays an important role in ecological restoration due to its resilience and low maintenance requirements. With its unique appearance and ecological benefits, little bluestem is a valuable addition to native plant gardens and natural landscapes.
