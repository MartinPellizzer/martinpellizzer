Phillyrea latifolia var. media

Phillyrea latifolia var. media, commonly known as the dwarf Phillyrea, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, which are followed by small, leathery fruits. It is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and is drought-tolerant once established.

This variety is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
