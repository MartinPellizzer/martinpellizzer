Paeonia officinalis var. glabra

Paeonia officinalis var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth peony, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant produces large, compound leaves with a serrated edge, and its roots are thick and fleshy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

The flowers are often used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties.
