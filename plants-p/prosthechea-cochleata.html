Prosthechea cochleata

Prosthechea cochleata, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its spiral arrangement of leaves, which form a rosette that collects water and nutrients. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Prosthechea cochleata is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
