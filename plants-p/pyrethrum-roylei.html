Pyrethrum roylei

Pyrethrum roylei, commonly known as the Asian pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant produces pyrethrins, which are natural insecticides used in organic pest control. Pyrethrum roylei thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.

Its flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
