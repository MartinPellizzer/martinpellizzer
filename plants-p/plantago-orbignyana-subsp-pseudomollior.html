Plantago orbignyana subsp. pseudomollior

Plantago orbignyana subsp.

pseudomollior, commonly known as the smoothleaf plantain, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks.

The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and slightly waxy, with a prominent midrib. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense spikes, producing seeds that are dispersed by wind.

This subspecies is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
