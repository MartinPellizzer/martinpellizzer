Polygala hondurana

Polygala hondurana, commonly known as the Honduran milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers with a distinctive hooded structure, typical of the genus Polygala. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
