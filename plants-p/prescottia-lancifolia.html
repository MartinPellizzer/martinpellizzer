Prescottia lancifolia

Prescottia lancifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved prescottia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale green, with a distinct lip petal. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
