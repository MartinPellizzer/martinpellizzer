Primula dickieana var. aureostellata

Primula dickieana var. aureostellata, commonly known as the golden star primrose, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features showy, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow with a distinctive central stripe, blooming in early spring. The plant has hairy, upright stems and broad, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and shaded borders.
