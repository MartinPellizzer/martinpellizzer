Pilocereus curtisii

Pilocereus curtisii, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a flowering cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its segmented, branching stems and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in winter. The plant produces bright pink, red, or orange flowers that are typically tubular in shape. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Pilocereus curtisii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
