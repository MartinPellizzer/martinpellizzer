Pittosporum forbesii

Pittosporum forbesii, commonly known as the Forbess Pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a fragrant scent when crushed. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

Pittosporum forbesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
