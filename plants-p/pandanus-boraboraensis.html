Pandanus boraboraensis

Pandanus boraboraensis, commonly known as the Borabora pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Pandanus.

It is native to the island of Borabora in the Society Islands of French Polynesia. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. It produces small, clustered inflorescences with fragrant, yellowish flowers.

The species is adapted to coastal environments and is often found growing in sandy soils near the shoreline.
