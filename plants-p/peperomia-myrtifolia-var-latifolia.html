Peperomia myrtifolia var. latifolia

Peperomia myrtifolia var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are broad and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically green or red, which are followed by small, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it suitable for both container and garden use.
