Pittosporum balansae var. balansae

Pittosporum balansae var. balansae, commonly known as the Balansae Pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves that are often glossy and have a distinctive shape. The plant produces white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, round berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or forested areas.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
