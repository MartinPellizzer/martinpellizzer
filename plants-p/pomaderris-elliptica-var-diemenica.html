Pomaderris elliptica var. diemenica

Pomaderris elliptica var. diemenica, commonly known as the Tasmanian wattle, is a shrub native to Tasmania.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers in cylindrical spikes, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to the cooler, wetter climates of Tasmania.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native habitats.
