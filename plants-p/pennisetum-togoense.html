Pennisetum togoense

Pennisetum togoense, commonly known as Togo grass, is a perennial grass native to West Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces panicles of small, fluffy flowers that are often white or pale green. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for ornamental use in gardens.

Pennisetum togoense is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in marginal lands.
