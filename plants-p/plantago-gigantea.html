Plantago gigantea

Plantago gigantea, commonly known as giant plantain, is a flowering plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in dense spikes, which are typically borne on tall, upright stems. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies for various ailments.
