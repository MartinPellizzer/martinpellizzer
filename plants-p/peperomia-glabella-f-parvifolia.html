Peperomia glabella f. parvifolia

Peperomia glabella f. parvifolia, commonly known as the dwarf peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for tabletop displays and hanging baskets.
