Proustia cuneifolia

Proustia cuneifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf proustia, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
