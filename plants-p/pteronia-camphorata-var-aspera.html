Pteronia camphorata var. aspera

Pteronia camphorata var. aspera, commonly known as the rough pteronia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy, gray-green foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish to orange-red flowers that bloom in summer. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to well-drained, sandy soils.

The variety 'aspera' is distinguished by its rough, textured leaves compared to other varieties of Pteronia camphorata.
