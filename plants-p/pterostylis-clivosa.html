Pterostylis clivosa

Pterostylis clivosa, commonly known as the cliff orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky areas. The plant features a slender, often reddish stem with a single, narrow leaf. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and have a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Pterostylis clivosa is notable for its unique morphology and is often found in specific microhabitats.
