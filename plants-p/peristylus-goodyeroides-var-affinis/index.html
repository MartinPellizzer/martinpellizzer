Peristylus Goodyeroides Var. Affinis

Goodyera affinis, scientifically known as Peristylus goodyeroides var. affinis, is a rare and elegant orchid native to the eastern United States. This species is characterized by its dense, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white to pale green flowers. It thrives in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. Despite its beauty, it is often overlooked due to its subtle appearance and limited distribution. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this delicate plant from habitat loss and overcollection.
