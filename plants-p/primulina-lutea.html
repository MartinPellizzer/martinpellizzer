Primulina lutea

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are heart-shaped and glossy, often with a slight waxy coating.
