Pelargonium Ferulaceum

Pelargonium ferulaceum is a unique species of geranium known for its striking appearance and adaptability. Native to southern Africa, this plant thrives in rocky and arid environments. Its foliage features velvety leaves with a distinctive silver-green hue. The flowers are small but vibrant, often displaying shades of red, pink, and white. This plant is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
