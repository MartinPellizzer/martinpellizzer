Polygala balduini var. carteri

Polygala balduini var. carteri, commonly known as the Carter's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae, which is characterized by its distinctive, often intricate floral structures. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, with a unique morphology that includes a spurred petal, typical of the genus Polygala.
