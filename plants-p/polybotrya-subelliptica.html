Polybotrya subelliptica

Polybotrya subelliptica, commonly known as the narrow-elliptic rock orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly in regions with rocky and arid habitats. The plant features slender, upright stems with small, greenish-white flowers that bloom during the rainy season. Its leaves are narrow and elliptical, adapted to conserve water in its dry environment.

Polybotrya subelliptica is often found growing on rock faces or in crevices, showcasing its resilience to harsh conditions.
