Pavonia imatacensis

Pavonia imatacensis, commonly known as the yellow pavonia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features showy, clustered inflorescences with bright yellow petals and red stamens. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a hairy texture.

Pavonia imatacensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
