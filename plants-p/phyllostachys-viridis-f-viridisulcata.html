Phyllostachys viridis f. viridisulcata

Phyllostachys viridis f. viridisulcata, commonly known as the green bamboo with a green-striped variant, is a variant of the green bamboo species.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems that exhibit a distinctive green coloration. The leaves are narrow and elongated, with a smooth, waxy texture. This bamboo variety is native to East Asia and thrives in humid, temperate climates.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care in garden settings.
