Ptilotus blackii

Ptilotus blackii, commonly known as the black spinifex, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and small, clustered flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments, with hairy stems that help reduce water loss. Its flowers, which range in color from pink to red, bloom during the cooler months.
