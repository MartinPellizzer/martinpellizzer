Polygonum polystachyum

Polygonum polystachyum, commonly known as the Chinese knotweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features upright stems that are often reddish in color and are covered with small, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces dense clusters of tiny, pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread rapidly, making it both a decorative garden plant and a potential weed.

It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.
