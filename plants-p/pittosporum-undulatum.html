Pittosporum undulatum

Pittosporum undulatum, commonly known as the undulating pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features dark green, glossy leaves that are often wavy or undulating in shape, giving it its common name. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that are typically followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

Pittosporum undulatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
