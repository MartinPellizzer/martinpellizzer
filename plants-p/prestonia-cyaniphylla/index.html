Prestonia Cyaniphylla

Prestonia cyaniphylla is a rare and striking plant native to the highlands of Madagascar. Known for its vibrant blue flowers, it captivates botanists and nature enthusiasts alike. This species belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes coffee and gardenias. Its unique coloration makes it a subject of scientific study and conservation efforts. Due to habitat loss, Prestonia cyaniphylla faces significant threats to its survival.
