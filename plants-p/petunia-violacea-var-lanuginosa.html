Petunia violacea var. lanuginosa

Petunia violacea var. lanuginosa, commonly known as the fuzzy petunia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety foliage and clusters of small, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its delicate appearance and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.

Its unique texture and color make it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.
