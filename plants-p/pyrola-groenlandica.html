Pyrola groenlandica

Pyrola groenlandica, commonly known as Greenland mossy orchid, is a rare and delicate plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit, with small, leathery leaves that form a dense mat. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, often with a distinctive dark spot at the base.
