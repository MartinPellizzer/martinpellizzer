Piper pauciramosum

Piper pauciramosum, commonly known as the narrow-ramose pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, ovate leaves with a glossy surface. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant, and it produces small, peppery fruits.

This species is valued for its aromatic properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
