Pelargonium × serpesianum

Pelargonium × serpesianum, commonly known as the hybrid geranium, is a flowering plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Pelargonium hortorum and Pelargonium × inodorum. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, five-petaled flowers that come in a variety of colors. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly serrated edge.

Pelargonium × serpesianum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for gardens and containers due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
