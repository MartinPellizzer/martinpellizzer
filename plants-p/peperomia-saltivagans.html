Peperomia saltivagans

Peperomia saltivagans, commonly known as the bouncing peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a slight pubescent texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique leaf shape and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor gardens.
