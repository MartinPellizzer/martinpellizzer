Peperomia pseudohoffmannii

Peperomia pseudohoffmannii, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery-white underside, giving it a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that emerge from the leaf axils. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Peperomia pseudohoffmannii prefers well-draining soil and moderate humidity, making it adaptable to a variety of indoor environments.
