Paraphlomis hsiwenii

Paraphlomis hsiwenii, commonly known as the Hsiwen's antirheumatic plant, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, clustered flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.
