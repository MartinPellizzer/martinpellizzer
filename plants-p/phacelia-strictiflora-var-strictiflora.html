Phacelia strictiflora var. strictiflora

Phacelia strictiflora var. strictiflora, commonly known as the narrow-flowered phacelia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. The plant features slender stems and small, clustered blue to purple flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a fuzzy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
