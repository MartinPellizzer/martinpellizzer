Phoenix robusta

Phoenix robusta, commonly known as the robust date palm, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is a tall, slender palm with a straight trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves that are deeply divided. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that eventually develop into edible dates. It is well adapted to arid environments and can thrive in poor soil conditions.

Phoenix robusta is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of food.
