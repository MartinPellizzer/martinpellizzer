Plectranthus alboviolaceus

Plectranthus alboviolaceus, commonly known as the silverleaf plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and clusters of small, violet-blue flowers. The plant is a perennial herb with a compact growth habit and aromatic leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique coloration and mild fragrance.
