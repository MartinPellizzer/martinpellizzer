Pelargonium × cosmon

Pelargonium × cosmon, commonly known as the hybrid geranium, is a popular ornamental plant valued for its vibrant flowers and foliage.

It is a cross between different species of geraniums, resulting in a wide variety of cultivars with diverse colors and growth habits. The plant typically has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of five-petaled flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and containers.

Pelargonium × cosmon is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
