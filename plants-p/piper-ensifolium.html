Piper ensifolium

Piper ensifolium, commonly known as the swordleaf pepper or green pepper, is a tropical climbing vine native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its elongated, sword-shaped leaves and small, clustered green berries that mature to red. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used in traditional medicine.

The plant's botanical features make it distinct within its family, with its unique leaf morphology and fruit structure.
