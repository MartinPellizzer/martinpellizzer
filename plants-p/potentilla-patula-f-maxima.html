Potentilla patula f. maxima

Potentilla patula f. maxima, commonly known as the giant potentilla, is a compact shrub characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit.

It features small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are arranged in a compact, upright fashion. It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
