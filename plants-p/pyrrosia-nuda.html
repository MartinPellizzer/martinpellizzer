Pyrrosia nuda

Pyrrosia nuda, commonly known as the naked pyrrosia, is a fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, leathery leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant typically grows on shaded, moist rock surfaces or tree trunks. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a smooth, glossy appearance.

Pyrrosia nuda is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in humid environments with indirect light.
