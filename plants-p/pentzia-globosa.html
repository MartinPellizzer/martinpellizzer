Pentzia globosa

Pentzia globosa, commonly known as the Cape sugarbush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its dense, spherical shape and small, oval leaves. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, red berries. It is adapted to dry, rocky environments and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

Pentzia globosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in traditional medicine.
