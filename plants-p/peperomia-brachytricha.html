Peperomia brachytricha

Peperomia brachytricha, commonly known as the compact peperomia, is a low-growing, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically ovate or elliptic in shape, often with a bluish-green or dark green hue. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually clustered in dense spikes, which are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia brachytricha is popular as a houseplant due to its compact size and ease of care.
