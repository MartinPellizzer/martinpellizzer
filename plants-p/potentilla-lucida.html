Potentilla lucida

Potentilla lucida, commonly known as shining cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. The plant typically grows in open woods, meadows, and rocky areas, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are palmately divided into five pointed lobes, and the stems are often hairy.

Potentilla lucida is a perennial plant that blooms from late spring to early summer.
