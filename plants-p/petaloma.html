Petaloma

Petaloma, commonly known as the ghost flower, is a rare and enigmatic plant belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its translucent, petal-like structures that resemble delicate, floating membranes. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests. Its unique morphology has led to much speculation about its evolutionary origins.
