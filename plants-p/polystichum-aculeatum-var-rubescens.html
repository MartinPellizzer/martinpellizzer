Polystichum aculeatum var. rubescens

Polystichum aculeatum var. rubescens, commonly known as the red-stemmed sword fern, is a variety of the common holly fern.

It is a hardy, evergreen fern that thrives in shady, moist environments. The plant features lance-shaped, dark green fronds with a distinctive reddish-brown coloration on the stems. It produces spore-bearing structures called sori on the underside of its leaves.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in woodland gardens or as a ground cover.
