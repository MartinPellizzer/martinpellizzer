Pleurothallis dariensis

Pleurothallis dariensis, commonly known as the Dariense Orchid, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant typically produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and green, often with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
