Phyllostachys edulis f. ventricosa

Phyllostachys edulis f. ventricosa, commonly known as the broad-leaved bamboo, is a variant of the bamboo species Phyllostachys edulis.

It is characterized by its wide, flat leaves that are typically broader than those of the standard variety. This plant is native to China and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and for the production of bamboo shoots.

The broad leaves make it a distinctive choice for landscaping and garden design.
