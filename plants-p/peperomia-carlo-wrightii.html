Peperomia carlo-wrightii

Peperomia carlo-wrightii, commonly known as the Carlo Wright's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features succulent, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture, and it produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white or pale pink. The plant is well adapted to low-light conditions and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
