Pleurothallis bucaramangae

Pleurothallis bucaramangae, commonly known as the Bucaramanga orchid, is a species of orchid native to Colombia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, tropical rainforest environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is part of the Pleurothallis genus, which includes many epiphytic orchids.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
