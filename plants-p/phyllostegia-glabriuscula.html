Phyllostegia glabriuscula

Phyllostegia glabriuscula, commonly known as the narrow-leaf strobilant, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple, blooming in the late spring to early summer.
