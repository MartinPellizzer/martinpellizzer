Pleomele fontainesiana

Pleomele fontainesiana, commonly known as the Fontaine's ginger.

It is a herbaceous plant native to Madagascar, characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and clusters of narrow, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and white, which are borne on short inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and landscaping in warm climates.
