Piper atlantidanum var. yoroense

Piper atlantidanum var. yoroense, commonly known as Yoroense pepper, is a climbing shrub native to Central America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a serrated margin. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, dark purple berries. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in shaded understory environments.

The berries are used in traditional medicine and as a spice in local cuisines.
