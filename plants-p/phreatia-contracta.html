Phreatia contracta

Phreatia contracta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved phreatia, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and elongated, narrow leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of white or silver hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, Phreatia contracta is considered a rare and delicate species.
