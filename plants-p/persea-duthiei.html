Persea duthiei

Persea duthiei, commonly known as the Mexican persimmon, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, orange-yellow fruits that are edible when fully mature. Persea duthiei is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in moist, well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
