Pelargonium × percyanum

Pelargonium × percyanum, commonly known as the violet-leaf geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking violet or purple foliage, which contrasts beautifully with its smaller, pale pink to white flowers. Native to South Africa, this species thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade conditions. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for containers and rock gardens.
