Pilea lundii

Pilea lundii, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rounded, fleshy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Pilea lundii is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.
