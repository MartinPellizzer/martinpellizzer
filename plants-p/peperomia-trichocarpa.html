Peperomia trichocarpa

Peperomia trichocarpa, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are velvety to the touch, with a distinctive waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia trichocarpa is known for its low maintenance and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor conditions.
