Polyalthia Greveana

Polyalthia greveana is a striking tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Borneo. Known for its lush, dark green foliage and dramatic, fern-like leaves, it adds a unique visual element to any landscape. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the undergrowth of dense jungles. Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice for botanical gardens and private collections. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticulture.
