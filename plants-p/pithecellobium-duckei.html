Pithecellobium duckei

Pithecellobium duckei, commonly known as the yellow velvet tamarind, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are legumes that contain edible seeds, which are sometimes used in traditional medicine.

The plant is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or secondary forests.
