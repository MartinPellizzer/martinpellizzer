Patersonia rudis

Patersonia rudis, commonly known as the rigid patersonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Australia.

It produces striking, violet-blue flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and has a fibrous root system adapted to its arid environment. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and rocky areas.

Patersonia rudis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native plant communities.
