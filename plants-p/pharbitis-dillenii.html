Pharbitis dillenii

Pharbitis dillenii, commonly known as the morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is characterized by its rapid growth and heliotropic leaves that follow the sun's movement. The plant produces trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple, depending on the variety. Its vines can climb and twine around supports, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The seeds are enclosed in a fibrous, spiral-shaped capsule that splits open when mature.
