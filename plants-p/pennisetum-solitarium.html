Pennisetum solitarium

Pennisetum solitarium, commonly known as fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It features slender, arching blades that create a fountain-like appearance, with a height ranging from 3 to 6 feet. The plant produces fluffy, seed-heads that add a soft, ornamental texture to gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for landscaping.

Its drought tolerance and minimal maintenance requirements further enhance its appeal in both residential and commercial settings.
