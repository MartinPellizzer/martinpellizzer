Piper tsarasotrae

Piper tsarasotrae, commonly known as the Madagascar pepper plant, is a species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its fruits are berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

It is primarily valued for its aromatic leaves and potential medicinal properties.
