Potentilla × gelmiana

Potentilla × gelmiana, commonly known as the shrub cinquefoil, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value. The plant typically features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are usually oval-shaped with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Potentilla × gelmiana is relatively low maintenance and suitable for a variety of garden conditions.
