Phlomoides Integior

Phlomoides integior is a lesser-known yet fascinating member of the Caprifoliaceae family. Native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe, this plant thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. Its striking purple flowers bloom in late summer, adding a vibrant touch to its natural habitat. Though not widely cultivated, it is valued by botanists for its unique morphology and ecological role. Understanding this plant offers insight into the diversity of flora in temperate ecosystems.
