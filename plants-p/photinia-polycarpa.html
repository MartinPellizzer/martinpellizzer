Photinia polycarpa

Photinia polycarpa, commonly known as the Chinese photinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that turn reddish in the fall. The plant produces small, white to pink flowers in clusters, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it suitable for garden use and ecological restoration projects.
