Phyllanthus virgatus var. gardnerianus

Phyllanthus virgatus var. gardnerianus, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a species of flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical Africa and is characterized by its small, green, fleshy leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its compact growth habit and attractive foliage. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for warm climates.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
