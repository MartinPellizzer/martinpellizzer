Pyrolirion flammeum

Pyrolirion flammeum, commonly known as the flame iris, is a striking perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems that bear showy, fiery orange to red flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a basal rosette, and it is known for its ability to bloom reliably in well-drained conditions.

Pyrolirion flammeum is a hardy plant that adds vibrant color to gardens and natural landscapes.
