Pappophorum brachystachyum

Pappophorum brachystachyum, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed pappophorum, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant produces lightweight seeds with tufts of hair, which aid in wind dispersal. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.
