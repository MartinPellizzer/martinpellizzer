Pteris hostmanniana

Pteris hostmanniana, commonly known as the hostmann's water fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically bright green and glossy. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce numerous sporangia, which are responsible for its reproductive cycle.

Pteris hostmanniana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in controlled indoor conditions.
