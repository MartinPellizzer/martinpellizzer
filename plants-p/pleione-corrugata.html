Pleione corrugata

Pleione corrugata, commonly known as the wrinkled pleione, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen pseudobulbs and linear, dark green leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with white petals and a distinctive yellow lip. It thrives in cool, shaded environments with high humidity.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
