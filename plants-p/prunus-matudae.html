Prunus matudae

Prunus matudae, commonly known as the Matuda cherry, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Mexico. The plant is characterized by its small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly serrated margin.

The fruit is a small, dark red drupe that ripens in late summer.
