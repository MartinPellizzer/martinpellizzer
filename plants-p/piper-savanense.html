Piper savanense

Piper savanense, commonly known as the savanna pepper or wild pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered white flowers. Its fruits are berries that mature to a dark purple color, containing aromatic seeds.

Piper savanense is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and savannas.
