Prunus × lannesiana f. kriegeri

Prunus × lannesiana f. kriegeri, commonly known as the Kriegeri cherry, is a compact, dwarf form of the Prunus lannesiana species.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in early spring. The leaves are typically dark green and glossy, providing an attractive appearance throughout the growing season. This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in horticultural displays.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.
