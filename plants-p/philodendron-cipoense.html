Philodendron cipoense

Philodendron cipoense, commonly known as the Cipoense Philodendron, is a striking tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy, dark green leaves that often have a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by bright red berries, which are an important food source for local wildlife. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Philodendron cipoense is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
