Polemonium delicatum f. coryphocola

Polemonium delicatum f. coryphocola, commonly known as the purple fringed bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Polemoniaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers, which are often adorned with white or pale pink fringes. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
