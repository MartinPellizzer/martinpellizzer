Pseudopodospermum Hispanicum Subsp. Neapolitanum

Pseudopodospermum hispanicum subsp. neapolitanum is a rare and unique plant native to southern Italy. It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is known for its striking floral characteristics. This subspecies is distinguished by its elongated, tubular flowers and vibrant orange to red colors. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions. Due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical study.
