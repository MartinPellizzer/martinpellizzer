Polygala monspeliaca f. livida

Polygala monspeliaca f. livida, commonly known as the pale spurge or pale polygala, is a rare and distinctive plant species belonging to the Polygalaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, pale green to bluish-gray leaves that give it its common name. The flowers are typically arranged in loose spikes and are often a pale yellow or greenish-white color, which helps it blend into its natural habitat.
