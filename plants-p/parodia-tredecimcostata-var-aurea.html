Parodia tredecimcostata var. aurea

Parodia tredecimcostata var. aurea, commonly known as the Golden Ghost Cactus, is a compact, globular cactus with a distinctive appearance.

It features a pale green to yellowish body with prominent, radial ribs that give it a star-like appearance. The plant is native to the arid regions of Mexico and is well adapted to hot, dry climates. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, blooming in late spring or early summer.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting due to its low maintenance and striking visual appeal.
