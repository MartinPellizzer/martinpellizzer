Pandorea Ricasoliana

Ricasoliana pandorea, scientifically known as Pandorea ricasoliana, is a striking flowering vine native to Australia and Southeast Asia. This plant is celebrated for its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of pink, red, and white. Its climbing habit makes it a popular choice for trellises, arbors, and garden structures. The foliage is evergreen, providing lush green coverage throughout the year. With its ornamental value and adaptability, Pandorea ricasoliana is both a decorative and ecological asset in diverse landscapes.
