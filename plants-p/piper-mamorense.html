Piper mamorense

Piper mamorense, commonly known as the Mamore pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the Amazon rainforest, specifically found in parts of Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are followed by small, round berries that turn red when mature.

Piper mamorense is valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a spice due to its pungent flavor.
