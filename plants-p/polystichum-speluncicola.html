Polystichum speluncicola

Polystichum speluncicola, commonly known as the cave shield fern, is a rare and elusive fern species endemic to subterranean environments.

It thrives in dark, humid cave systems where it relies on limited light and specific microclimatic conditions. The plant's fronds are typically short and broad, adapted to the low-light conditions of its habitat. Its reproductive structures are specialized to ensure successful spore dispersal in confined spaces.

Due to its unique ecological niche, it is considered a valuable subject for studying adaptation to extreme environments.
