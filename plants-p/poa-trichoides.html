Poa trichoides

Poa trichoides, commonly known as silky bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green blades and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats, such as marshes and wet meadows. Its inflorescence consists of a single spikelet, which is usually pale green to brown in color.

Poa trichoides plays an important ecological role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
