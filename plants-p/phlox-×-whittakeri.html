Phlox × whittakeri

Phlox × whittakeri, commonly known as the Whittakeri phlox, is a hybrid perennial flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that typically range in color from pink to red, depending on the specific cultivar. The plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, fertile soil. It is often used in garden landscapes for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

Phlox × whittakeri is relatively low maintenance but may require deadheading to promote continuous blooming.
