Prunus Avium Var. Asplenifolia

Prunus avium var. asplenifolia is a unique and striking variant of the sweet cherry tree known for its distinctive foliage. This plant features large, deeply lobed leaves that resemble the shape of the fern, giving it a striking visual appeal. It is native to the mountainous regions of the western United States, where it thrives in cooler climates. While it shares the fruit-bearing qualities of its parent species, its ornamental value makes it a favorite among horticulturists. Its combination of beauty and utility makes it a noteworthy addition to any landscape.
