Peperomia defracta

Peperomia defracta, commonly known as the broken leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are often crinkled or curled, giving it a unique, textured appearance. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal watering needs.

Peperomia defracta is prized for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments.
