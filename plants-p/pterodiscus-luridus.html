Pterodiscus luridus

Pterodiscus luridus, commonly known as the lurid disc fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, leathery fronds that are often bright green with a waxy texture. Pterodiscus luridus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
