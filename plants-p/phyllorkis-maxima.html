Phyllorkis maxima

Phyllorkis maxima, commonly known as the large-leaved daisy.

It is a flowering plant native to Australia, characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers and robust, upright growth habit. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.

Phyllorkis maxima is known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
