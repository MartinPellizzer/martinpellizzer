Pulmonaria montana subsp. porciusii

Pulmonaria montana subsp.

porciusii, commonly known as the Alpine lungwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It features basal rosettes of velvety, spotted leaves that are often bluish-green in color.

The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky soils.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
