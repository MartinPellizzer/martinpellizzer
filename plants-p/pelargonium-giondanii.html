Pelargonium giondanii

Pelargonium giondanii, commonly known as the Giondanii geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its attractive, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows as a perennial shrub, featuring rounded leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its beauty and ability to attract pollinators.
