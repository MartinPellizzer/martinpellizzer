Potentilla × echizensis

Potentilla × echizensis, commonly known as the Shadbush or Echinacea hybrid, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, cone-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant is native to the northwestern United States and is often found in moist, well-drained soils in woodland areas. It produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Potentilla × echizensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of garden conditions.
