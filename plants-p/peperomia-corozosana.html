Peperomia corozosana

Peperomia corozosana, commonly known as the Colombian peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white or pale pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia corozosana is valued for its ornamental foliage and low maintenance requirements.
