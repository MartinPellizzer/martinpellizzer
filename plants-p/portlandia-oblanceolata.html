Portlandia oblanceolata

Portlandia oblanceolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pincushion.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, belonging to the Asteraceae family. The plant is characterized by its dense, spiky inflorescence and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

The flowers are typically yellow or orange, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat.
