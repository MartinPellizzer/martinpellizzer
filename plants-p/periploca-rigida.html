Periploca rigida

Periploca rigida, commonly known as the rigid periploca, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and attract pollinators such as bees and birds.

Periploca rigida is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
