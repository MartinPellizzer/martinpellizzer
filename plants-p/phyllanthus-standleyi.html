Phyllanthus standleyi

Phyllanthus standleyi, commonly known as African date palm, is a flowering plant in the family Phyllanthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible, date-like fruits. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit production.

The species is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
