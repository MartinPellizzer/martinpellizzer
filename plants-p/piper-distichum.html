Piper distichum

Piper distichum, commonly known as the two-leaved pepper or slender pepper, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, slender berries containing the aromatic seeds. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a rough texture.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
