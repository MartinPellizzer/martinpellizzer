Philadelphus pekinensis

Philadelphus pekinensis, commonly known as the Chinese Mock Orange, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

Philadelphus pekinensis is often used in ornamental gardening for its showy blooms and pleasant fragrance.
