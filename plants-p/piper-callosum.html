Piper callosum

Piper callosum, commonly known as the warty pipevine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of flowering plants known for their aromatic properties. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its unique warty texture on the leaves makes it easily distinguishable from other Piper species.
