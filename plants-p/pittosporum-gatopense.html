Pittosporum gatopense

Pittosporum gatopense, commonly known as the New Zealand Pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green on top and lighter on the underside. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

Pittosporum gatopense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
