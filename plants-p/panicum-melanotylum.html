Panicum melanotylum

Panicum melanotylum, commonly known as black panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dark green, narrow leaves and produces small, fluffy panicles of seeds. The plant thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions. It is drought-tolerant and often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and agricultural contexts.
