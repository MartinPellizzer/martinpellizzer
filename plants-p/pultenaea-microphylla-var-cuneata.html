Pultenaea microphylla var. cuneata

Pultenaea microphylla var. cuneata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pultenaea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
