Panicum luxurians

Panicum luxurians, commonly known as the luxuriant panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and produces fluffy, silver-green seed heads that are visually striking. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, contributing to its textured appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a common sight in open habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
