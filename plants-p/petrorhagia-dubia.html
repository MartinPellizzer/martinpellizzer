Petrorhagia dubia

Petrorhagia dubia, commonly known as the pale sand-verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, pale blue or violet flowers. The plant has slender, branching stems and narrow, hairy leaves adapted to arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
