Pentzia lanata

Pentzia lanata, commonly known as the woolly pentzia, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly grey foliage that covers its stems and leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Pentzia lanata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
