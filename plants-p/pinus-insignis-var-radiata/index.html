Pinus Insignis Var. Radiata

Pinus insignis var. radiata is a striking evergreen tree native to the western coast of the United States. Known for its tall, straight trunk and dense, pyramidal shape, it is a popular choice for both landscaping and commercial forestry. Its needles are long, slender, and often have a distinctive blue-green hue that adds visual interest to any setting. The tree produces cones that hang from the branches, releasing seeds that are dispersed by the wind. This resilient species thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a versatile and valuable plant in many ecosystems.
