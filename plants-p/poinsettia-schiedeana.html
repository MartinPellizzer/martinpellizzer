Poinsettia schiedeana

Poinsettia schiedeana, commonly known as the "False Christmas Star," is a strikingly beautiful shrub native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its vibrant red bracts, which are modified leaves that surround small, inconspicuous yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Poinsettia schiedeana thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements during the holiday season.
