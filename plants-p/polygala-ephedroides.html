Polygala ephedroides

Polygala ephedroides, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to China and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are usually alternate and narrow, while its flowers are often pale green or yellow.

The plant has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
