Peperomia mandioccana f. macrocarpa

Peperomia mandioccana f. macrocarpa, commonly known as the giant peperomia, is a compact shrub native to Brazil.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically broad and ovate, with a deep green color and a textured surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are followed by dark red, fleshy berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and ease of care contribute to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
