Pyrus pseudocalleryana

Pyrus pseudocalleryana, commonly known as the Chinese white pear, is a species of deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and alternate, simple leaves. The tree produces small, white flowers in spring, followed by edible, sweet fruits that are similar to pears.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit production in temperate regions.
