Polyscias crenata

Polyscias crenata, commonly known as the silver nettle or variegated glorybower, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with distinctive white or silver veins, which give it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil.

Polyscias crenata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor environments.
