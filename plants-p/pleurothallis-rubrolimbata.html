Pleurothallis rubrolimbata

Pleurothallis rubrolimbata, commonly known as the red-limbed orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often arranged in a spike. Its leaves are typically lanceolate and covered in a dense layer of fine, white pubescence.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
