Peperomia Rotundata Var. Tequendamana

Peperomia rotundata var. tequendamana is a compact, evergreen shrub known for its vibrant, waxy leaves. Native to the tropical regions of South America, it thrives in warm, humid environments. This variety is prized for its unique foliage, featuring rounded leaves with a glossy texture. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal. Its ability to adapt to various light conditions makes it an ideal plant for both novice and experienced growers.
