Polygonum fissum

Polygonum fissum, commonly known as narrowleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist soils along stream banks, wet meadows, and roadside ditches. The plant features upright stems that are often reddish in color and are covered with small, alternate leaves. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and are usually pink or red in color.

Polygonum fissum is known for its adaptability and can thrive in a variety of wetland environments.
