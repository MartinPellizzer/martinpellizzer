Panicum eitenii

Panicum eitenii, commonly known as the Eiten's grass, is a flowering plant species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers, typical of many grass species. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as savannas and grasslands.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
