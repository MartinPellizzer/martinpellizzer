Panicum spanianthum

Panicum spanianthum, commonly known as the silver panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-gray foliage and tufted inflorescences that resemble panicles. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping. Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements contribute to its adaptability in various environments.

Additionally, it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.
