Podocarpus woltzii

Podocarpus woltzii, commonly known as the Woltz's podocarp, is a species of evergreen conifer native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, cone-like structures that mature into woody cones. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in its natural habitat. It prefers well-drained soils and is often found in humid, shaded environments.

Podocarpus woltzii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens.
