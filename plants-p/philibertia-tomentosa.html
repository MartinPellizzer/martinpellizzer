Philibertia tomentosa

Philibertia tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly filbert, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Philibertia tomentosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
