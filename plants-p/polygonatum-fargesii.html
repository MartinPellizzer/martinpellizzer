Polygonatum fargesii

Polygonatum fargesii, commonly known as the Chinese Solomon's seal, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth and lance-shaped leaves. Polygonatum fargesii is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
