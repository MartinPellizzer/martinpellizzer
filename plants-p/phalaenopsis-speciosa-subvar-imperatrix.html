Phalaenopsis speciosa subvar. imperatrix

Phalaenopsis speciosa subvar. imperatrix, commonly known as the Imperial Phalaenopsis, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink with dark purple or brown markings. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad, flat, and often have a waxy texture.

This orchid thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.
