Poa multiflora

Poa multiflora, commonly known as the Japanese bentgrass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to East Asia.

It features fine, dense foliage and forms a dense mat, often used in ornamental landscaping. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Its ecological value includes soil stabilization and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
