Pteridium latiusculum subsp. latiusculum

Pteridium latiusculum subsp.

latiusculum, commonly known as the giant chain fern, is a large, terrestrial fern native to New Zealand. It is characterized by its robust, upright fronds that can reach heights of up to 3 meters.

The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with a thick, creeping underground stem that allows it to spread and form dense colonies. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with long, slender pinnae that are often covered in fine hairs.

This species is known for its ecological importance, providing habitat and food for various native wildlife.
