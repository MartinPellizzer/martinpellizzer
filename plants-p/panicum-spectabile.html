Panicum spectabile

Panicum spectabile, commonly known as the purpletop millet, is a striking ornamental grass native to North America.

It features tall, upright spikes of lavender-pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Panicum spectabile is often used in gardens for its drought tolerance and visual appeal.
