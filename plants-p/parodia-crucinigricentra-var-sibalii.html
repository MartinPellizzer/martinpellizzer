Parodia crucinigricentra var. sibalii

Parodia crucinigricentra var. sibalii, commonly known as the Sibalii Cactus, is a rare and striking member of the Cactaceae family.

It is native to the arid regions of northern Mexico, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. The plant features a short, cylindrical stem with dense clusters of white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. Its foliage is characterized by spiny, ribbed stems that help reduce water loss in its harsh environment.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is highly sought after by cactus enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
