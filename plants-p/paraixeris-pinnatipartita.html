Paraixeris pinnatipartita

Paraixeris pinnatipartita, commonly known as the featherleaf paraixeris, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its delicate, feathery leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and white, typically blooming in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is more commonly found in the wild.
