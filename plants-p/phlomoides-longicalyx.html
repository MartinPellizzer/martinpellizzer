Phlomoides longicalyx

Phlomoides longicalyx, commonly known as the long-leaved phlomoides, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features slender, hairy stems and opposite leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, clustered in whorls, and typically have a pale blue or purple hue.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassy or rocky habitats.
