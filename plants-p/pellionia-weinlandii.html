Pellionia weinlandii

Pellionia weinlandii, commonly known as the velvetleaf pellionia, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are clustered in an inflorescence called a spadix, surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. Pellionia weinlandii is often grown as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
