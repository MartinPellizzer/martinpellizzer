Pilea chrysosplenioides

Pilea chrysosplenioides, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features round, fleshy leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a silvery underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale green, followed by small, round fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Its unique appearance and ease of care have made it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
