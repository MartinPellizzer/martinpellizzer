Physurus blumei

Physurus blumei, commonly known as the Blume's morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant is a climbing vine with heart-shaped leaves and produces showy blooms that open in the morning. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in moist forest environments.

Physurus blumei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
