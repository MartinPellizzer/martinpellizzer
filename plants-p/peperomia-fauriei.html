Peperomia fauriei

Peperomia fauriei, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to Brazil.

It features glossy, waxy leaves that are typically light green with a silvery underside, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that emerge from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and low maintenance requirements, it is well-suited for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
