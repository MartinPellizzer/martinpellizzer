Piper omega

Piper omega, commonly known as the omega pepper or the African black pepper, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper and long pepper. The plant produces small, dark red berries that contain aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and culinary applications. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly bitter taste, while the plant thrives in humid, shaded environments.

The botanical characteristics of Piper omega make it a unique and valuable species in both ecological and economic contexts.
