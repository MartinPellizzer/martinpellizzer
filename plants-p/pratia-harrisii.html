Pratia harrisii

Pratia harrisii, commonly known as the Harris's pratia, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and damp woodlands. The plant features delicate, grass-like leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in inflorescences that bloom in the spring or early summer.

Pratia harrisii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique foliage.
