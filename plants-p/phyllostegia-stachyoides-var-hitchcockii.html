Phyllostegia stachyoides var. hitchcockii

Phyllostegia stachyoides var. hitchcockii, commonly known as the Hitchcock's stachyoid phyllostegia, is a variant of the stachyoid phyllostegia, a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded to partially shaded environments.
