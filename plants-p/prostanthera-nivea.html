Prostanthera nivea

Prostanthera nivea, commonly known as white mint, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are fragrant and often used in traditional medicine.

Prostanthera nivea thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions.
