Pyrostria syringifolia

Pyrostria syringifolia, commonly known as the Christmas fern, is a perennial evergreen fern native to eastern North America.

It features a distinctive, arching frond with a serrated margin and a dense, spore-producing cluster at the tip. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its leaves remain green throughout the winter, giving it its common name.

Pyrostria syringifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
