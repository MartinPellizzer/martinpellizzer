Petrea pubescens var. klugii

Petrea pubescens var. klugii, commonly known as the Klug's pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage, which is typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its ornamental value comes from its dense, rounded form and its ability to thrive in shaded environments.

It is often cultivated as a decorative shrub in tropical and subtropical gardens.
