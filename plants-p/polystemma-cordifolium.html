Polystemma cordifolium

Polystemma cordifolium, commonly known as the heart-leaf polystemma, is a small evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It features elliptical to heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Polystemma cordifolium thrives in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
