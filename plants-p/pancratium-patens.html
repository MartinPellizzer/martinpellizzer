Pancratium patens

Pancratium patens, commonly known as the sea daffodil, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean and parts of Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils near the shore. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy yellow flowers with a distinctive orange center. It is well adapted to saline and windy environments, making it a hardy coastal species.

Pancratium patens is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and resilience.
