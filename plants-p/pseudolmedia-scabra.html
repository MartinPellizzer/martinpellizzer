Pseudolmedia scabra

Pseudolmedia scabra, commonly known as the rough pseudolmedia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It features slender, upright stems covered in a distinctive rough, scaly texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a textured surface, contributing to its unique appearance.

Pseudolmedia scabra is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth.
