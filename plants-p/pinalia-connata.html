Pinalia connata

Pinalia connata, commonly known as the Mexican orchid, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil efficiently.

Pinalia connata is valued for its ornamental beauty and plays a role in local ecosystems.
