Psidium persoonii

Psidium persoonii, commonly known as the Natal plum, is a small evergreen tree native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces edible berries that are typically yellow or red when ripe, with a tart and juicy flavor. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit.

Psidium persoonii is also valued for its ability to tolerate coastal conditions and poor soils.
