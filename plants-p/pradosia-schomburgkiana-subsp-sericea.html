Pradosia schomburgkiana subsp. sericea

Pradosia schomburgkiana subsp.

sericea, commonly known as the silky pradosia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America. It is characterized by its soft, silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry forest or savanna environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
