Pongelia

Pongelia, commonly known as the water lettuce, is a floating aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It has broad, waxy leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern on the water's surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. Pongelia thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

It is often used in water gardens and can help in water purification by absorbing nutrients.
