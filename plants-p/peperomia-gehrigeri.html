Peperomia gehrigeri

Peperomia gehrigeri, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features dense, glossy, silver-green foliage that is slightly textured, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental leaves, it is popular as a houseplant and is relatively easy to care for.
