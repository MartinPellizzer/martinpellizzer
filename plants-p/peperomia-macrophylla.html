Peperomia macrophylla

Peperomia macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, and often brightly colored leaves that can be green, red, or variegated, depending on the cultivar. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, arranged in clusters at the base of the leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and low maintenance requirements, it is well-suited for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
