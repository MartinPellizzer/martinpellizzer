Pultenaea polifolia var. mucronata

Pultenaea polifolia var. mucronata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pultenaea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features small, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It is typically found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to coastal and inland environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in suitable climates.
