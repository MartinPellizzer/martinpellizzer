Piper ostii

Piper ostii, commonly known as the Madagascar pepper plant, is a tropical climbing vine native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by elongated, peppery fruits. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

Piper ostii is valued for its aromatic foliage and is often used in traditional medicine and as a spice.
