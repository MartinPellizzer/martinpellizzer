Panicum dumetorum

Panicum dumetorum, commonly known as velvetleaf, is a perennial herb native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a common weed in many regions.
