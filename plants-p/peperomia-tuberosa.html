Peperomia tuberosa

Peperomia tuberosa, commonly known as the tuberous peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, often in shades of white or pale green. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia tuberosa is valued for its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
