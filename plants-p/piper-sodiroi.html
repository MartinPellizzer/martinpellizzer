Piper sodiroi

Piper sodiroi, commonly known as the Sodiro's pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with opposite, ovate leaves and small, greenish-yellow flowers. Its fruit is a small, berry-like drupe that contains the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine.

Piper sodiroi is valued for its potential medicinal properties and is often used in local herbal remedies.
