Peucedanum herbidum

Peucedanum herbidum, commonly known as the European cow parsley, is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its finely divided leaves and umbrella-shaped flower clusters.

Its small white or pale yellow flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
