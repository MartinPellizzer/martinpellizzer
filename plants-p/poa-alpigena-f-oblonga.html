Poa alpigena f. oblonga

Poa alpigena f. oblonga, commonly known as the Alpine bentgrass, is a variant of the slender bentgrass species.

It is a low-growing perennial grass that typically thrives in alpine and subalpine environments. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green hue and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and ability to tolerate cold and harsh conditions.

This form is often found in rocky or gravelly soils at high elevations.
