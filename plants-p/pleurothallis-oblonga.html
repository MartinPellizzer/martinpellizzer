Pleurothallis oblonga

Pleurothallis oblonga, commonly known as the long-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features slender, clustered pseudobulbs and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually small and white or pale green, often arranged in a raceme.

Pleurothallis oblonga is valued in horticulture for its delicate appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
