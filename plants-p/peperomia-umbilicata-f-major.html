Peperomia umbilicata f. major

Peperomia umbilicata f. major, commonly known as the Giant Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, waxy, ovate leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purple tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers during the summer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and minimal watering requirements.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for decorative plantings.
