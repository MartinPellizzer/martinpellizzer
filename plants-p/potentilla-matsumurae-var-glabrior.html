Potentilla matsumurae var. glabrior

Potentilla matsumurae var. glabrior, commonly known as the glabrous potentilla, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are typically oval with a smooth surface. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
