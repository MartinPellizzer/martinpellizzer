Prunus desvauxii

Prunus desvauxii, commonly known as the Desvaux cherry, is a species of shrub or small tree native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early spring.
