Prunus jamasakura f. rotundiflora

Prunus jamasakura f. rotundiflora, commonly known as the Japanese cherry plum or roundleaf cherry plum, is a cultivar of the Prunus jamasakura species.

It is characterized by its rounded, often double flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are oval-shaped with a smooth texture and a glossy appearance.

It is valued for its ornamental value, particularly in gardens and landscapes where its spring blossoms add aesthetic appeal.
