Piper kwashoense

Piper kwashoense, commonly known as the Kwasho pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Ghana and Togo. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with long, slender stems that often twine around supporting structures. Its leaves are typically alternate, ovate to elliptic in shape, and have a glossy surface.

The small, greenish-yellow flowers are clustered in spikes, and the fruit is a berry that ripens to a dark purple color.
