Phyllanthodendron dunnianum var. hypoglaucum

Phyllanthodendron dunnianum var. hypoglaucum, commonly known as the smooth-leaved phyllanthodendron, is a species of flowering plant in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers clustered in cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.

The varietal name 'hypoglaucum' refers to its pale underside, distinguishing it from other varieties of the species.
