Pimelea strigosa

Pimelea strigosa, commonly known as the silverleaf pimelea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in heathlands and coastal areas. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance.

Pimelea strigosa is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
