Pulicaria sicula

Pulicaria sicula, commonly known as Sicilian groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Pulicaria sicula is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
