Pleuranthemum

Pleuranthemum, commonly known as the common bluebell, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that typically range from blue to purple in color. These plants are native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. They are popular in horticulture for their ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
