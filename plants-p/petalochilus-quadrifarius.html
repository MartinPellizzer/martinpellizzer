Petalochilus quadrifarius

Petalochilus quadrifarius, commonly known as the four-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are linear and often covered in a fine, white pubescence.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
