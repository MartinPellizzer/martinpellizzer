Peperomia obovalifolia

Peperomia obovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaved peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features broad, oval-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green, with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, often in shades of white or pink, which are not particularly showy but contribute to its ornamental value. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia obovalifolia is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.
