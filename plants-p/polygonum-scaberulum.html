Polygonum scaberulum

Polygonum scaberulum, commonly known as smooth smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like sorrel and buckwheat. The plant features slender, upright stems that are often reddish in color, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a smooth, slightly waxy surface. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or reddish, blooming in late summer to early fall.

Smooth smartweed is often found in moist soils along stream banks, wet meadows, and roadside ditches.
