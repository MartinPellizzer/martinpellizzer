Polypodium varians

Polypodium varians, commonly known as the variable huperzia, is a species of fern belonging to the family Hypnaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and rhizomatous growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and rocky crevices. Its fronds are leathery and often have a distinctive, irregular shape.
