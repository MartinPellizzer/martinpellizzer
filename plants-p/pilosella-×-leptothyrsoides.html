Pilosella × leptothyrsoides

Pilosella × leptothyrsoides, commonly known as the slenderleaf mouseear, is a flowering plant in the family Compositae.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Pilosella lactiflora and Pilosella microphylla. The plant is characterized by its small, hairy leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in early summer. It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides.

Pilosella × leptothyrsoides is native to Europe and has been introduced to other parts of the world, including North America.
