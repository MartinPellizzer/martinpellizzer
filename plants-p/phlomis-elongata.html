Phlomis elongata

Phlomis elongata, commonly known as tall everlasting, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branched stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces showy, yellowish-green flower heads that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped, hairy, and often have a grayish-blue hue.

Phlomis elongata is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens and dry landscapes.
