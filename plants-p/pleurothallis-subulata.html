Pleurothallis subulata

Pleurothallis subulata, commonly known as the slender pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces delicate, white or pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, intricate structure. It is known for its compact size and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a challenging plant to grow outside its natural range.
