Pittosporum littorale

Pittosporum littorale, commonly known as the coastal pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to the coastal regions of New Zealand.

It is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are glossy and dark green with a leathery texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are typically followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal scrub or forest margins.

Pittosporum littorale is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
