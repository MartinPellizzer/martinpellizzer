Pelargonium Reniforme

Reniform geranium, scientifically known as Pelargonium reniforme, is a strikingly unique plant native to South Africa. With its distinctive kidney-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers, it stands out among other geranium species. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers. Its adaptability and ornamental value have made it a favorite among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to watering and light conditions to flourish.
