Psychotria orophila

Psychotria orophila, commonly known as the cloud forest psychotria, is a species native to the humid montane regions of Central and South America.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows in shaded, moist environments within cloud forests. The plant is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release tiny seeds when mature.
