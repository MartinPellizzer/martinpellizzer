Populus nigra f. pseudocaudina

Populus nigra f. pseudocaudina, commonly known as the black poplar weeping form, is a decorative variant of the black poplar tree.

It is characterized by its pendulous branches that create a cascading effect, giving it a striking visual appearance. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter in color underneath. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near water bodies.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental beauty and is commonly used in landscaping and riparian restoration projects.
