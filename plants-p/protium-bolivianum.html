Protium bolivianum

Protium bolivianum, commonly known as the Bolivian copal tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and simple, alternate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by fleshy, orange-red berries. Its sap is used in traditional medicine and for making incense.
