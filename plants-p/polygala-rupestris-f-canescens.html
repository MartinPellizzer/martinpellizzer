Polygala rupestris f. canescens

Polygala rupestris f. canescens, commonly known as the woolly polygala, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, gray-green foliage and small, clustered flowers that range from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its unique floral structure and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

It is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
