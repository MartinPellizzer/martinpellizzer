Psychanthus floribundus

Psychanthus floribundus, commonly known as the clustered psychanthus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, showy flowers that bloom in vibrant shades of yellow and orange. The plant has palmate leaves with lobed edges, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Psychanthus floribundus is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
