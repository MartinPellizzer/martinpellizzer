Prunus × beni-fumotoii

Prunus × beni-fumotoii, commonly known as the Beni-Fumoto cherry, is a hybrid cultivar of the Prunus genus.

It is native to Japan and is prized for its ornamental value, particularly its showy, double flowers. The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree with a rounded canopy. Its leaves are dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the pinkish-white blooms.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and is well-suited to temperate climates.
