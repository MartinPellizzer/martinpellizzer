Pavonia filiformis

Pavonia filiformis, commonly known as the slender pavonia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, showy flowers that are usually pink or red in color. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Pavonia filiformis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
