Paronychia aretioides

Paronychia aretioides, commonly known as the rock rose or coastal rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils, often in coastal dunes or near the ocean. Paronychia aretioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh environments.
