Philodendron williamsii

Philodendron williamsii, commonly known as the "Willis Philodendron," is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or light green markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by round, green fruits that turn red when mature. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity.

Philodendron williamsii is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
