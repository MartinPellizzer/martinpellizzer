Potentilla × smoutii

Potentilla × smoutii, commonly known as the smoutii cinquefoil, is a hybrid shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage, with leaves that are typically oval to elliptic in shape. The plant produces showy, bright yellow flowers in late spring to early summer, which are clustered in inflorescences. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.
