Platanthera acuminata

Platanthera acuminata, commonly known as the acuminata orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that are typically white or pale green with a yellowish-green lip. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and swampy areas. It has a complex root system that helps it absorb nutrients from the soil.

Platanthera acuminata is a perennial herb that blooms in late summer, adding a striking visual element to its native habitat.
