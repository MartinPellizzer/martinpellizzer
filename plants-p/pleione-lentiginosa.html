Pleione lentiginosa

Pleione lentiginosa, commonly known as the spotted pleione, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Asia, including China, India, and Nepal. The plant is characterized by its rhizomatous growth and produces showy, bell-shaped flowers with a distinctive spotted pattern on the petals. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
