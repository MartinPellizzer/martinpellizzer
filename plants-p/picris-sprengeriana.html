Picris sprengeriana

Picris sprengeriana, commonly known as the mountain everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. The plant is known for its resilience, thriving in rocky and arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand harsh conditions.
