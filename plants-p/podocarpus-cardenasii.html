Podocarpus cardenasii

Podocarpus cardenasii, commonly known as the Candelabra Podocarpus, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Podocarpaceae family and is characterized by its dense, conical crown and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, spherical cones that contain seeds, typical of its coniferous relatives. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant in warm climates.
