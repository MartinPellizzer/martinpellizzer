Peperomia magnoliifolia var. microphylla

Peperomia magnoliifolia var. microphylla, commonly known as the dwarf magnolia peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features small, glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slight reddish tint on the underside. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellowish, often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Due to its slow growth and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for terrariums and as a tabletop plant.
