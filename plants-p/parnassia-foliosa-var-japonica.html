Parnassia foliosa var. japonica

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers with five petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its leaves are palmately divided, and it produces a cluster of small, showy flowers in early summer.
