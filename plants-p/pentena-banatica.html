Pentena banatica

Pentena banatica, commonly known as the Banatic banana plant, is a tropical perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Musaceae family, which includes other well-known banana and plantain species. The plant features large, broad leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length, providing a striking visual presence. Its flowering stems produce clusters of small, yellow flowers that eventually develop into edible fruits.

The plant is valued not only for its fruit but also for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in local cultures.
