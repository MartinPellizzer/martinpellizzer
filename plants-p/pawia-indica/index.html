Pawia Indica

Pawiia indica is a lesser-known yet fascinating plant native to tropical regions. It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes over 12,000 species. This plant is known for its ornamental value and unique foliage. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated in horticultural circles.
