Phyllanthus taitensis var. glabrescens

Phyllanthus taitensis var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-leaved phyllanthus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small shrub characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are typically smooth and glabrous. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which are followed by small, round fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional medicinal uses in local cultures.
