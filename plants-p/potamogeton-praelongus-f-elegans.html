Potamogeton praelongus f. elegans

Potamogeton praelongus f. elegans, commonly known as the elegant pondweed, is a submerged aquatic plant native to freshwater environments in Europe and parts of Asia.

It features long, ribbon-like leaves that are typically dark green and slightly ribbed, aiding in its identification. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually found in clusters near the water's surface. It thrives in shallow, slow-moving waters with soft, muddy or sandy substrates.

Due to its dense growth, it can play a role in stabilizing sediments and providing habitat for aquatic invertebrates.
