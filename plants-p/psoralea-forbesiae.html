Psoralea forbesiae

Psoralea forbesiae, commonly known as Forbes' psoralea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small, oval-shaped leaflets.
