Peperomia stehleana var. branquecii

Peperomia stehleana var. branquecii, commonly known as the Branquecii Peperomia, is a compact, compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale green, which are not particularly showy. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
