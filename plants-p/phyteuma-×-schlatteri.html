Phyteuma × schlatteri

Phyteuma × schlatteri, commonly known as the hybrid sunflower or garden sunflower, is a cultivated variety of the genus Phyteuma.

It is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems and large, showy yellow-orange flower heads that resemble sunflowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its vibrant, sun-like blooms.
