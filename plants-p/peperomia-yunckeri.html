Peperomia yunckeri

Peperomia yunckeri, commonly known as the Yuncker's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green, with some varieties displaying variegation. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or red, followed by small, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia yunckeri is relatively low maintenance and suitable for beginners due to its adaptability and resilience.
