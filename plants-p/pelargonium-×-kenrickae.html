Pelargonium × kenrickae

Pelargonium × kenrickae, commonly known as the African Marvel, is a hybrid geranium native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage with deep green to purple hues and its showy, double-petaled flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant is a compact shrub, typically growing to about 12–18 inches in height, with a bushy, rounded shape. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season, adding color to gardens year-round.
