Pandanus magnicavernosus

Pandanus magnicavernosus, commonly known as the giant cave pandanus, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, palm-like leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in coastal or lowland areas. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

However, it requires well-drained soil and protection from strong winds to flourish.
