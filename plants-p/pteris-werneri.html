Pteris werneri

Pteris werneri, commonly known as the Werneri water fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks. Its rhizomes are creeping and help it spread vegetatively.

Pteris werneri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
