Plumeria cayensis

Plumeria cayensis, commonly known as the Cayenne plumeria, is a species native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is a small, ornamental tree or shrub that typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 meters. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to yellow and pink. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a waxy texture.

The bark is smooth and pale, with a distinctive, aromatic scent when crushed.
