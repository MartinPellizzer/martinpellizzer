Prostanthera tysoniana

Prostanthera tysoniana, commonly known as the Tasmanian native mint, is a shrub native to Tasmania, Australia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and medicinal plants. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Prostanthera tysoniana is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
