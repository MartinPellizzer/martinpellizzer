Piper barbirostre

Piper barbirostre, commonly known as the narrow-lipped pepper vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various species of pepper plants. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and produces small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruit is a three-lobed, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.

The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
