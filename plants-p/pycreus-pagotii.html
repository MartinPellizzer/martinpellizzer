Pycreus pagotii

Pycreus pagotii, commonly known as the pagotii cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, adapting to harsh conditions with its succulent structure. Pycreus pagotii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry climates.
