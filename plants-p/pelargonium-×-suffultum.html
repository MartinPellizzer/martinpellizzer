Pelargonium × suffultum

Pelargonium × suffultum, commonly known as the storm petunia, is a hybrid geranium species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a wide range of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are slender and somewhat hairy.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in various garden settings.
