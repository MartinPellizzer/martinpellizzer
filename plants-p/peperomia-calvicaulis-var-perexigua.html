Peperomia calvicaulis var. perexigua

Peperomia calvicaulis var. perexigua, commonly known as the compact peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, fleshy, dark green leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish or reddish in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its ability to thrive in low light conditions and minimal water requirements.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for container gardening and as a decorative plant.
