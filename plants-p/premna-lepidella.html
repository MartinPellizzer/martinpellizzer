Premna lepidella

Premna lepidella, commonly known as the glossy privet, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like India, China, and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale purple, arranged in clusters.

Premna lepidella is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
