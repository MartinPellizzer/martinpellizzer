Panicum cyanescens var. lamarckianum

Panicum cyanescens var. lamarckianum, commonly known as blue panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking blue-green foliage, which becomes more pronounced under direct sunlight. The plant produces fluffy, pale blue to silver-colored inflorescences that resemble a cloud, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This species is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
