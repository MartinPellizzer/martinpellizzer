Ptilophyllum elegans

Ptilophyllum elegans, commonly known as the elegant feather fern, is a delicate, herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery fronds that give it a soft, lace-like appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks. Its rhizomes are slender and creeping, allowing it to spread slowly across the forest floor.

The leaves are usually evergreen, providing a consistent green presence throughout the year.
