Prunus × lannesiana f. masuyama

Prunus × lannesiana f. masuyama, commonly known as the Masuyama cherry, is a unique botanical cultivar prized for its ornamental value.

It is a hybrid of Prunus lannesiana, characterized by its compact growth and vibrant floral display. The plant exhibits distinct foliage and flower characteristics, making it a popular choice in horticulture. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to gardens and landscapes.
