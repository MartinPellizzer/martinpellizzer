Potamogeton × venustus

Potamogeton × venustus, commonly known as the beautiful pondweed, is a hybrid aquatic plant that occurs naturally in freshwater environments.

It is characterized by its long, ribbon-like leaves that are often bright green and can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant typically forms dense underwater stands, which can provide habitat for various aquatic organisms. It blooms with small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in spike-like structures, usually emerging from the water surface.

Potamogeton × venustus is often found in slow-moving or still waters such as lakes, ponds, and wetlands.
