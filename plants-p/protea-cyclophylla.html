Protea cyclophylla

Protea cyclophylla, commonly known as the cyclophylla protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences composed of numerous individual flowers arranged in a circular pattern. The plant produces vibrant, often pink or red, bracts that enclose the true flowers, which are small and clustered. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is adapted to the Mediterranean climate of its native region.

Protea cyclophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
