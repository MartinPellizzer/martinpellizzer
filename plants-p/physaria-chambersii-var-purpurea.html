Physaria chambersii var. purpurea

Physaria chambersii var. purpurea, commonly known as the purple-flowered bladderpod, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, which includes mustard and cabbages, and is characterized by its small, showy purple flowers. Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
