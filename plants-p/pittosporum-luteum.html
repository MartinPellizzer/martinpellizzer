Pittosporum luteum

Pittosporum luteum, commonly known as the golden pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by dark blue berries. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Pittosporum luteum is also valued for its ability to attract birds and provide habitat in native gardens.
