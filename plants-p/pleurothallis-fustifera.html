Pleurothallis fustifera

Pleurothallis fustifera, commonly known as the black orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andes of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features dark green, strap-like leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and purple. It is noted for its unique appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and delicate species.
