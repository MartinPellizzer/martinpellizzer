Peperomia leptostachya var. laxiflora

Peperomia leptostachya var. laxiflora, commonly known as the broad-leaved peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and pink, which appear during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
