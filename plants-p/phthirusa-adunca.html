Phthirusa adunca

Phthirusa adunca, commonly known as the slender sandwort, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with succulent, fleshy stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps reduce water loss.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens.
