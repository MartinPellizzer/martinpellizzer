Peperomia sneidernii

Peperomia sneidernii, commonly known as the silver mitten plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features glossy, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a silvery underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia sneidernii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
