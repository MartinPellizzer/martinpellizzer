Pleurothallis miniatolineolata

Pleurothallis miniatolineolata, commonly known as the miniature line-leaf orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a faintly veined appearance.

This orchid is valued for its unique floral structure and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
