Phlox amoena var. lighthipei

Phlox amoena var. lighthipei, commonly known as the Lighthipei phlox, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in mid to late summer. The plant has upright, branching stems covered with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
