Pennisetum polystachyum

Pennisetum polystachyum, commonly known as fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted, fountain-like growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces showy, plume-like inflorescences that are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Pennisetum polystachyum is also valued for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
