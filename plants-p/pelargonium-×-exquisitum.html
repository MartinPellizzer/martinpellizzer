Pelargonium × exquisitum

Pelargonium × exquisitum, commonly known as the exquisitum geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically has broad, lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is a popular choice for garden use due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.

Pelargonium × exquisitum thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade conditions.
