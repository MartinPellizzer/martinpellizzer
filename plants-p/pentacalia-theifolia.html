Pentacalia theifolia

Pentacalia theifolia, commonly known as the thief's plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped white flowers. The plant has a unique ability to absorb nutrients from the air, which is why it is sometimes called the thief's plant. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
