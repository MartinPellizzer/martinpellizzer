Paspalidium obtusifolium var. acutifolium

Paspalidium obtusifolium var. acutifolium, commonly known as the acuteleaf paspalidium, is a grass species characterized by its narrow, acute leaves.

It typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in disturbed soils or open habitats. The plant has a tufted growth habit with flowering stems that bear small, spikelet-like inflorescences. Its leaves are generally pubescent and have a distinctive acuminate tip.

This variety is noted for its adaptability to various environmental conditions.
