Phylica nigromontana

Phylica nigromontana, commonly known as black mountain phylica, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in spherical inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow, leathery, and often covered in fine hairs.

Phylica nigromontana is adapted to nutrient-poor soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.
