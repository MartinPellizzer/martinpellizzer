Polygala rhinanthoides

Polygala rhinanthoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that often have a distinctive, spurred appearance. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and often hairy, while its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish in color.
