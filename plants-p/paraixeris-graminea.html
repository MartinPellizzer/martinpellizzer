Paraixeris graminea

Paraixeris graminea, commonly known as the slender grass.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family, characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and compact growth habit. Native to South America, particularly Brazil, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or pale in color.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation, it is occasionally grown in gardens and conservation areas.
