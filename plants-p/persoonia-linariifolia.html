Persoonia linariifolia

Persoonia linariifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in spikes, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to full sun conditions.

Persoonia linariifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native habitats.
