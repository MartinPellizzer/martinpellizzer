Peperomia fernandeziana

Peperomia fernandeziana, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to South America.

It features dense, waxy, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia fernandeziana is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
