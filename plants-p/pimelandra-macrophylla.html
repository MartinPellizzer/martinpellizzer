Pimelandra macrophylla

Pimelandra macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf pimelandra, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

Pimelandra macrophylla is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
