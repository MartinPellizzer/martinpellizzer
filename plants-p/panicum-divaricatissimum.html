Panicum divaricatissimum

Panicum divaricatissimum, commonly known as the slender panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically green to brown in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Due to its rapid growth and adaptability, it is considered an invasive species in some regions.
