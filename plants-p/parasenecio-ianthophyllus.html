Parasenecio ianthophyllus

Parasenecio ianthophyllus, commonly known as the blue-leafed groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive blue-green foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in heads, typical of the Compositae family. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
