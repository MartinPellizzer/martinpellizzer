Polygonatum trichosanthum

Polygonatum trichosanthum, commonly known as the Chinese Solomon's seal, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its rhizomes are valued in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
