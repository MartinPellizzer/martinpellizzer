Premna obtusifolia var. madagascariensis

Premna obtusifolia var. madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar glorybower, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate to elliptic in shape, and have a smooth texture.

It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
