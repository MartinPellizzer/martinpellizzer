Pleione Barbata

Pleione barbata is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of China. It is known for its unique appearance, featuring delicate flowers with a distinctive barbed structure on its petals. This plant thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in forested areas at high altitudes. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered and faces threats from deforestation and climate change. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this beautiful and ecologically significant species.
