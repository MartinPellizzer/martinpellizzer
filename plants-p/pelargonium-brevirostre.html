Pelargonium brevirostre

Pelargonium brevirostre, commonly known as the narrow-beaked geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically has hairy stems and leaves that are often lobed or serrated. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant flower colors, which range from pink to red.
