Pouteria natalensis

Pouteria natalensis, commonly known as Natal's pouteria, is a tropical plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are often clustered in panicles. Its fruit is a smooth, fleshy drupe that turns red when mature, containing a single seed.

The leaves are alternate, simple, and have a slightly serrated margin.
