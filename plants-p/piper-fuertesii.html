Piper fuertesii

Piper fuertesii, commonly known as the Fuertes's pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to Central America, particularly found in regions of Mexico and Guatemala. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit and possesses small, heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface. Its fruits are small berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

Piper fuertesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and potential medicinal properties.
