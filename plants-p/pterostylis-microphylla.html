Pterostylis microphylla

Pterostylis microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaf greenhood, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are greenish-yellow with a hood-like structure, and they are often adorned with intricate patterns.

Pterostylis microphylla is a rare and endangered species, requiring specific ecological conditions for successful growth and reproduction.
