Poa subreflexa

Poa subreflexa, commonly known as the reflexed bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems that often curve downward. The leaves are narrow, rough, and slightly hairy, with a bluish-green coloration. Its inflorescence consists of a single spikelet that is usually erect and slightly drooping.

This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.
