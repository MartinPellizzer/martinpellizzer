Picralima macrocarpa

Picralima macrocarpa, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant has a broad, spreading canopy with pinnately compound leaves that provide dense shade. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in warm climates for its vibrant floral display.
