Ptychosperma sanderianum

Ptychosperma sanderianum, commonly known as the Sander's palm, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by oval, dark blue berries. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
