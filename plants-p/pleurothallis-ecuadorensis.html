Pleurothallis ecuadorensis

Pleurothallis ecuadorensis, commonly known as the Ecuadorian pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. Its flowers are often fragrant and bloom during the warmer months of the year.

Pleurothallis ecuadorensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
