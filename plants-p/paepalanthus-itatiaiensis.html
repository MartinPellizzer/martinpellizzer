Paepalanthus itatiaiensis

Paepalanthus itatiaiensis, commonly known as the Itatiaia grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to Brazil, specifically found in the state of Rio de Janeiro. This plant belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often growing in forest understories.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences.
