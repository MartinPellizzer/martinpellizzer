Peperomia flavida

Peperomia flavida, commonly known as the golden peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a subtle yellow or golden hue on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale green, arranged in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia flavida is known for its low maintenance and ability to adapt well to various indoor environments.
