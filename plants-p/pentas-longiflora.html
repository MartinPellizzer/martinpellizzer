Pentas longiflora

Pentas longiflora, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, star-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant produces small, green, oval-shaped leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The flowers are not only visually striking but also attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
