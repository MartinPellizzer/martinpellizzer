Paullinia scarlatina

Paullinia scarlatina, commonly known as the red soursop or Brazilian soursop, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Sapindaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of yellowish-green flowers.
