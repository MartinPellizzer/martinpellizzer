Pleurothallis madsenii

Pleurothallis madsenii, commonly known as the Madsen's pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features slender, hairy pseudobulbs and produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a subtle fragrance. Its leaves are usually narrow and strap-like, adapted to conserve moisture in its natural habitat.

Pleurothallis madsenii is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
