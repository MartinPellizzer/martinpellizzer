Peperomia bequaertii

Peperomia bequaertii, commonly known as the Bequaert's peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale green. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its ability to tolerate low light and minimal water requirements.
