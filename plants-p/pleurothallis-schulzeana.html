Pleurothallis schulzeana

Pleurothallis schulzeana, commonly known as the Schultze's orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small epiphytic orchid, typically growing on tree branches or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate floral structure.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often found in high-altitude regions with consistent moisture and cool temperatures.
