Peperomia martinicensis

Peperomia martinicensis, commonly known as the Martinique peppergrass, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to the Caribbean.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and red, which are borne on short, erect spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia martinicensis is relatively low maintenance and resistant to pests, contributing to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
