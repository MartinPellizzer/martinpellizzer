Paphiopedilum violascens var. violascens

Paphiopedilum violascens var. violascens, commonly known as the purple-flowered lady's slipper orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs and long, leathery leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch, typically in shades of purple and white. It prefers shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
