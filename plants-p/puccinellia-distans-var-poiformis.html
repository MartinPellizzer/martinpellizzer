Puccinellia distans var. poiformis

Puccinellia distans var. poiformis, commonly known as the smoothleaf sea oats, is a perennial grass native to coastal regions of the United States.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant thrives in sandy or saline soils, often found in dunes and salt marshes. Its rhizomatous growth habit helps stabilize coastal ecosystems.

It plays an important role in preventing erosion and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
