Petroselinum petroselinum

Petroselinum petroselinum, commonly known as parsley, is a biennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves and small, yellow flowers that appear in umbels. Parsley is cultivated for its leaves, which are used in cooking, and for its seeds, which are used in spice blends.
