Peperomia ramosii

Peperomia ramosii, commonly known as the Shiny Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to Brazil.

It features dense, glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which appear in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it an ideal choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia ramosii is known for its low maintenance and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor conditions.
