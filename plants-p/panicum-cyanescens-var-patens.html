Panicum cyanescens var. patens

Panicum cyanescens var. patens, commonly known as blue panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking blue-gray foliage and upright growth habit. The plant produces fluffy, pinkish-white inflorescences that are often used in ornamental landscaping. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it adaptable to various environmental conditions.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for erosion control and ornamental purposes.
