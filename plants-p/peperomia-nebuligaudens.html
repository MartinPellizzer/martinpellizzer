Peperomia nebuligaudens

Peperomia nebuligaudens, commonly known as the Cloudy Peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and a unique, slightly wavy margin. The plant produces small, pale green to white flowers that emerge from the leaf axils, adding a subtle ornamental touch. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia nebuligaudens is ideal for indoor cultivation due to its adaptability and decorative appeal.
