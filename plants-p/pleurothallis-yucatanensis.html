Pleurothallis yucatanensis

Pleurothallis yucatanensis, commonly known as the Yucatan orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. It produces fragrant, white or pale green blossoms that bloom during the rainy season.

Its unique morphology and habitat make it a notable species in botanical studies.
