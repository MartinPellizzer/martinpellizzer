Phyllanthus microphyllus var. orbignyanus

Phyllanthus microphyllus var. orbignyanus, commonly known as the small-leaved spurge, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, rounded leaves that are typically less than 1 centimeter in diameter, arranged alternately along its stems. The plant produces tiny, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by small, spherical fruits. It is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, showing adaptability to various environmental conditions.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
