Pothos Rumphii Var. Dolichophyllus

Pothos rumphii var. dolichophyllus is a strikingly unique houseplant known for its long, slender leaves. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in warm and humid environments. This variant stands out for its elongated foliage, which can grow up to several feet in length. Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening. With minimal care, it can flourish and add a touch of greenery to any space.
