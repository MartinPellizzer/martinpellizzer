Peperomia oblanceolata var. glabra

Peperomia oblanceolata var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance houseplant native to tropical regions.

It features broad, oval-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy, with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for tabletop displays or as part of a succulent arrangement.
