Porochna bifolia

Porochna bifolia, commonly known as the two-leaved porochna, is a lesser-known species within the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive bifoliate leaves, which are divided into two lobes, giving it a unique appearance. Porochna bifolia is noted for its aromatic properties, though it is not widely cultivated or utilized in traditional medicine.
