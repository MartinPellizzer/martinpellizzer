Pinanga gracillima

Pinanga gracillima, commonly known as the slender bamboo palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and pinnate leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are long and narrow, contributing to its elegant appearance.

Pinanga gracillima is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
