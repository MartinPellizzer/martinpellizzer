Pilea opaca

Pilea opaca, commonly known as the velvet plant, is a low-growing, evergreen perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate moisture.

Pilea opaca is popular as a houseplant due to its compact size and easy care requirements.
