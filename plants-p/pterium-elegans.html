Pterium elegans

Pterium elegans, commonly known as the elegant fern, is a species of fern characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features compound leaves with a distinctive, fan-shaped structure, and its rhizomes are typically slender and creeping. Pterium elegans thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant collections.
