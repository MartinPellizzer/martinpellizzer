Platylobium spinosum

Platylobium spinosum, commonly known as the spiny flat-lipped orchid, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiny stems and small, showy flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is known for its unique morphology and ecological adaptations. It thrives in sandy soils and is often found in shrublands and open woodlands.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
