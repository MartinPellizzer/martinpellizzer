Pilosella paragoga subsp. biformis

Pilosella paragoga subsp.

biformis, commonly known as the two-form pilosella, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It produces small, yellowish to white flowers arranged in compact clusters.
