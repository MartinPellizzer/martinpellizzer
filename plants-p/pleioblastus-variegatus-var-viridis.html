Pleioblastus variegatus var. viridis

Pleioblastus variegatus var. viridis, commonly known as the green bamboo, is a variety of the variegated bamboo species.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and long, narrow leaves that are typically a vibrant green color. The plant is native to East Asia and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it adaptable to a variety of climates.

Its foliage provides a fresh, elegant appearance, making it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor settings.
