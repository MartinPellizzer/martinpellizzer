Pennisetum adoense

Pennisetum adoense, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that produce feathery, purple-tinted plumes, which add visual interest to gardens. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for perennial borders and containers. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that contrasts beautifully with its vibrant flower heads.

Pennisetum adoense is drought-tolerant and attracts pollinators, making it a valuable addition to sustainable landscapes.
