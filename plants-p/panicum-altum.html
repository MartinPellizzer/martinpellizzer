Panicum altum

Panicum altum, commonly known as tall panicgrass, is a tall, perennial grass species native to the southern United States.

It typically grows in wetland areas and along the edges of water bodies, preferring moist to wet soils. The plant has long, narrow leaves and produces fluffy, seed-bearing spikes that are often visible in late summer. It is a valuable plant for wildlife, providing food and habitat for various birds and small mammals.

Its rapid growth and ability to thrive in wet conditions make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
