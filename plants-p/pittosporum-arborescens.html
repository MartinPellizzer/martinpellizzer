Pittosporum arborescens

Pittosporum arborescens, commonly known as the silver tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features a distinctive silver-gray bark and elliptical, glossy dark green leaves that turn red in autumn. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by bright orange berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Pittosporum arborescens is also valued for its ability to provide habitat for native wildlife.
