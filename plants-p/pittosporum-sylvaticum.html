Pittosporum sylvaticum

Pittosporum sylvaticum, commonly known as the New Zealand Pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features evergreen leaves that are typically elliptical and have a glossy, dark green surface. The plant produces fragrant white or pale yellow flowers, which are often followed by small, rounded berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

Pittosporum sylvaticum is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitat.
