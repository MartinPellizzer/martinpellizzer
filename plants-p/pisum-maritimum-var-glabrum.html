Pisum maritimum var. glabrum

Pisum maritimum var. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth sea pea, is a variety of the sea pea plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is a herbaceous annual plant characterized by its slender, climbing stems and trifoliate leaves. The flowers are small, typically pale blue or purple, and the pods contain several elliptical, smooth seeds. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to maritime climates with salt spray.

It is often found in rocky coastal areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential ecological benefits.
