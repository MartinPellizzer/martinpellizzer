Phreatia maxima

Phreatia maxima, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 15 centimeters in diameter. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and surrounding substrate.

Phreatia maxima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
