Piper trichostigma

Piper trichostigma, commonly known as the wild pepper or Indian pepper, is a flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, Africa, and the Americas. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in spikes. Its fruits are berries that mature to a dark purple color, containing a single seed.

Piper trichostigma is often used in traditional medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
