Peperomia hoffmannii

Peperomia hoffmannii, commonly known as the Hoffman's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture, and it produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its dense foliage and compact growth habit make it an attractive addition to tabletop displays and hanging baskets.
