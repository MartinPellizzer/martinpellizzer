Peperomia salmonicolor var. salmonicolor

Peperomia salmonicolor var. salmonicolor, commonly known as the salmon pink peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant salmon-pink to reddish hue, especially under bright light. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers indirect sunlight, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its compact growth habit and striking foliage make it a popular choice for tabletop displays and indoor gardens.
