Paraphlomis formosana

Paraphlomis formosana, commonly known as Taiwan cowpea, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color.

Paraphlomis formosana is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
