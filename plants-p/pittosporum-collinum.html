Pittosporum collinum

Pittosporum collinum, commonly known as the mountain pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by orange-red berries. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth margin. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Pittosporum collinum is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitat.
