Priva meyeri

Priva meyeri, commonly known as Meyer lemon.

It is a citrus plant native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its small, yellow citrus fruits. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves and produces fragrant, yellow flowers. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil.

Priva meyeri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of edible fruit.
