Pilea petelotii

Pilea petelotii, commonly known as the Mexican aluminum plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Mexico.

It features dense, rounded foliage composed of dark green, leathery leaves with a silvery underside, giving it a metallic appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are followed by round, green berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and ease of care have made it a favored ornamental plant in many households.
