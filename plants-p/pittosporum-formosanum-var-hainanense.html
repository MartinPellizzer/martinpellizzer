Pittosporum formosanum var. hainanense

Pittosporum formosanum var. hainanense, commonly known as the Hainan pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to southern China, particularly Hainan Island.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 meters. The plant features dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a glossy surface. Its fragrant white flowers are borne in clusters and bloom in spring, followed by small, round berries that are typically orange-red in color.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
