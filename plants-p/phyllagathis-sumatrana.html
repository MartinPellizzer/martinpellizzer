Phyllagathis sumatrana

Phyllagathis sumatrana, commonly known as the Sumatran phyllagathis, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Sumatra and Borneo. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a glossy surface with a distinct aromatic scent.

The flowers are small, clustered, and usually white or pale pink, contributing to its ornamental value in horticulture.
