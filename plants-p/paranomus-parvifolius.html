Paranomus parvifolius

Paranomus parvifolius, commonly known as the small-leaved parasol tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually small and ovate in shape. Its flowers are clustered in panicles and are often white or pale yellow in color.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release its seeds.
