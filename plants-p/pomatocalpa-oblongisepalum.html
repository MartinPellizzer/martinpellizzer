Pomatocalpa oblongisepalum

Pomatocalpa oblongisepalum, commonly known as the broad-sepal orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, elliptical sepals and petals, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with a slight fragrance.

It is a terrestrial orchid that produces small, clustered blooms during the wet season.
