Polygonatum cavaleriei

Polygonatum cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese Solomon's seal, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbellate clusters. Its berries are dark purple and contain several seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and traditional medicinal uses.
