Piper polytrichum

Piper polytrichum, commonly known as the creeping pepper vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and emits a strong, spicy scent. Its small greenish-yellow flowers are followed by berries that turn red when mature.

Piper polytrichum is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
