Premna gaudichaudii

Premna gaudichaudii, commonly known as the white-flowered glorybower, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and showy, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. It is often found in coastal and disturbed habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping in tropical climates.
