Pedicularis chroorrhyncha

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including parts of Russia and Kazakhstan. The plant is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers, which are clustered in racemes along the stem. It is a hemiparasitic plant, meaning it partially relies on host plants for nutrients.
