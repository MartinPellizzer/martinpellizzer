Prunus avium var. laciniata

Prunus avium var. laciniata, commonly known as the cherry plum with lobed leaves, is a variant of the common cherry plum.

It is characterized by its distinctive, deeply lobed leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, sweet fruits similar to other Prunus species, though its ornamental value is often its primary appeal. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty and seasonal interest.
