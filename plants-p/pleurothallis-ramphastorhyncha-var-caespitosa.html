Pleurothallis ramphastorhyncha var. caespitosa

Pleurothallis ramphastorhyncha var. caespitosa, commonly known as the clustered pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pale pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is known for its fragrant blooms.

This variety is valued by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
