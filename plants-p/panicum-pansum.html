Panicum pansum

Panicum pansum, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces fluffy, red-tipped seed heads that are visually striking. The plant typically thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping and erosion control. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that adds ornamental value to landscapes.

Panicum pansum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
