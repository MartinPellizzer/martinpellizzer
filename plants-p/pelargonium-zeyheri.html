Pelargonium zeyheri

Pelargonium zeyheri, commonly known as the Zeyher's geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with upright, branched stems and dark green, ovate leaves. The plant produces showy, double flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. It prefers full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Pelargonium zeyheri is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
