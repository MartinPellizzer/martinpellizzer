Pleurospermum densiflorum

Pleurospermum densiflorum, commonly known as the denseflower pleurospermum, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of central and southern China.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and often hairy foliage. The plant features a robust root system and upright, branched stems covered in fine pubescence. Its flowers are small, yellowish, and clustered in dense, flat-topped inflorescences, blooming in late summer.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or forested habitats.
