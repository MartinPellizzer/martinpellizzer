Platyaechmea chantinii

Platyaechmea chantinii, commonly known as the red-tipped pineapple plant, is a striking member of the Bromeliaceae family.

Platyaechmea chantinii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
