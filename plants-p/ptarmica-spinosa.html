Ptarmica spinosa

Ptarmica spinosa, commonly known as the prickly germander, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Geraniaceae.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Ptarmica spinosa is also known for its medicinal properties, historically used in traditional remedies for various ailments.
