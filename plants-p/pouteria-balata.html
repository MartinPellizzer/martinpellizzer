Pouteria balata

Pouteria balata, commonly known as the rubber tree or balata tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and large, elliptical leaves. The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall and is often found in lowland rainforests.
