Peperomia haenkeana

Peperomia haenkeana, commonly known as the Mexican peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, succulent-like foliage. The plant typically features small, ovate leaves with a waxy texture and can produce tiny, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Peperomia haenkeana is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
