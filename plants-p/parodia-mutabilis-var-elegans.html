Parodia mutabilis var. elegans

Parodia mutabilis var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant parodia, is a succulent cactus native to South America.

It features a compact, globular shape with ribbed stems that can develop a reddish or pinkish hue under intense sunlight. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are borne on long, slender stems. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its slow growth and low maintenance requirements.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
