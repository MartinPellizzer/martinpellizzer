Phylloscirpus andesinus

Phylloscirpus andesinus, commonly known as the Andean feather grass, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its delicate, feathery appearance. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, where it forms dense tufts of slender, wiry stems. Phylloscirpus andesinus is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in harsh, high-altitude conditions.
