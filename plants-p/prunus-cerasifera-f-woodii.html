Prunus cerasifera f. woodii

Prunus cerasifera f. woodii, commonly known as the woodii cherry, is a compact, ornamental form of the cherry laurel.

It features dense, dark green foliage that provides year-round visual interest. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in spring, followed by edible, sweet red berries in late summer. It is well-suited for small gardens or containers due to its compact growth habit.

This cultivar is also valued for its resistance to pests and diseases, making it a low-maintenance choice for landscaping.
