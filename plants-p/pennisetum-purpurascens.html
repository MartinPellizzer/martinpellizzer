Pennisetum purpurascens

Pennisetum purpurascens, commonly known as purple lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and feathery, purplish-pink plumes that appear in late summer. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens. Its rapid growth and ability to spread through rhizomes make it both a useful ground cover and a potential weed.

Pennisetum purpurascens is also valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
