Primula dongchuanensis

Primula dongchuanensis, commonly known as the Dongchuan primrose, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in cool, moist environments. Its foliage consists of broad, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical studies.
