Prestonia trifida

Prestonia trifida, commonly known as the Trinidad nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by berries that turn from green to red as they mature. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The plant is adapted to grow in tropical and subtropical climates, often in coastal or disturbed areas.
