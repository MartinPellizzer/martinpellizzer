Panicum macrospermum

Panicum macrospermum, commonly known as large-seeded panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and produces fluffy, wind-dispersed seeds. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as fields and roadsides. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Panicum macrospermum is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its hardiness and ability to stabilize soil.
