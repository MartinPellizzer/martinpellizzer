Polygonum poiretii var. latifolium

Polygonum poiretii var. latifolium, commonly known as broadleaf smartweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It features upright, branched stems that are often reddish in color, and it produces small, clustered flowers in late summer. The leaves are broad, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly serrated margin. It thrives in moist, sunny environments such as wet meadows and stream banks.

The plant is known for its ability to tolerate wet conditions and is sometimes used in wetland restoration projects.
