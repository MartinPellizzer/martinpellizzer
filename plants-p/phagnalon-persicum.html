Phagnalon persicum

Phagnalon persicum, commonly known as Persian phagnalon, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, particularly in the Mediterranean and parts of Asia. The plant features slender stems and small, narrow leaves, adapted to conserve water in harsh environments. Phagnalon persicum is often found in rocky soils and is used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
