Pilea wallichii

Pilea wallichii, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its round, fleshy leaves that resemble small coins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are clustered in an inflorescence called a spadix. It is a popular indoor plant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.

Pilea wallichii prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
