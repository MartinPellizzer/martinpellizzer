Polygonum pedunculare var. assamicum

Polygonum pedunculare var. assamicum, commonly known as the Assam knotweed, is a perennial herb native to the northeastern regions of India.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its succulent stems and rhombic leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the study of medicinal and ornamental plants.
