Piper hermes

Piper hermes, commonly known as Hermes' pepper, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when mature, containing aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are often used in herbal remedies due to their purported medicinal properties.

The plant's botanical features include its climbing habit and the presence of glandular hairs on its stems and leaves.
