Peperomia chanchamayana

Peperomia chanchamayana, commonly known as the Chanchamayana pepperplant, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a small, compact plant with thick, waxy leaves that are typically green or variegated with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or red, followed by small, rounded berries. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal water needs.

Peperomia chanchamayana is valued for its ornamental foliage and air-purifying qualities.
