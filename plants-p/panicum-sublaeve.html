Panicum sublaeve

Panicum sublaeve, commonly known as slender panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its inflorescence consists of a dense spikelet cluster, which is often adorned with short, bristly bracts.

Panicum sublaeve is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its hardy nature.
