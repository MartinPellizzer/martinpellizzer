Peperomia trapezoidalis

Peperomia trapezoidalis, commonly known as the Shiny Leaf Peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green, with some varieties displaying variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or red, often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate watering.

Peperomia trapezoidalis is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
