Phegopteris Sparsisora

Phegopteris fern, scientifically known as Phegopteris sparsisora, is a rare and delicate species of fern found primarily in moist, shaded environments. This plant is characterized by its finely divided, feathery fronds that create a soft, lacy appearance. It thrives in temperate regions with high humidity and well-drained soil, often growing in forest understories. Despite its beauty, Phegopteris sparsisora is endangered due to habitat loss and environmental changes. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
