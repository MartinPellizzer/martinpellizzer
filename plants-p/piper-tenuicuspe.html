Piper tenuicuspe

Piper tenuicuspe, commonly known as the slender pepper vine, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, vine-like stems and small, opposite leaves. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by tiny, peppery fruits.

The botanical features of Piper tenuicuspe make it a unique and interesting species within the Piper genus.
