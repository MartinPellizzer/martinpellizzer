Phaseoloides brandisianum

Phaseoloides brandisianum, commonly known as the Brandis's bean.

It is a climbing leguminous plant native to tropical regions of Africa. The plant features compound leaves with trifoliate or pinnate structures, and its flowers are typically white or pale purple. It produces pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

The species is notable for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, contributing to soil fertility.
