Perlebia × blakeana

Perlebia × blakeana, commonly known as the hybrid perlebia, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Perlebia masonii and Perlebia hirta.

It is native to the tropical regions of Africa, particularly in Kenya and Tanzania. This plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid environment to flourish.
