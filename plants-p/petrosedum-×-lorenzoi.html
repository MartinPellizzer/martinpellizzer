Petrosedum × lorenzoi

Petrosedum × lorenzoi, commonly known as the hybrid stonecrop, is a low-growing succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and opposite, ovate leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, thriving in full sun and well-draining soil.

Petrosedum × lorenzoi is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its hardy nature and ornamental appeal.
