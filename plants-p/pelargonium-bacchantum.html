Pelargonium bacchantum

Pelargonium bacchantum, commonly known as the bacchantum geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a strong, spicy scent. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets and containers.

Pelargonium bacchantum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
