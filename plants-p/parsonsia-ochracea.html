Parsonsia ochracea

Parsonsia ochracea, commonly known as the golden glorybower, is a striking flowering plant native to parts of Australia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, orange-yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring.
