Phlomoides Laevigata

Phlomoides laevigata is a striking member of the Asteraceae family known for its vibrant purple to pink flower heads. Native to the southeastern United States, this plant thrives in open, sunny habitats such as prairies and woodland edges. Its slender stems and showy blooms make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking native plants that support local pollinators. The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits. With its delicate appearance and resilience, Phlomoides laevigata plays an important role in both natural and cultivated landscapes.
