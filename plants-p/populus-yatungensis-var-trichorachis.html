Populus yatungensis var. trichorachis

Populus yatungensis var. trichorachis, commonly known as the Yatungensis poplar, is a variant of the poplar species native to China.

It is characterized by its distinctive trichromatic leaves, which display three different colors on the same leaf surface. The plant has a rapid growth rate and is often found in moist, temperate regions. Its foliage is often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

This variant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in reforestation efforts.
