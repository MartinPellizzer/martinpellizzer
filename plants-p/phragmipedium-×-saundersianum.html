Phragmipedium × saundersianum

Phragmipedium × saundersianum, commonly known as the Saunders' phragmipedium, is a hybrid orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, where it typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces large, showy flowers with intricate patterns on its petals and sepals. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.

The species is often cultivated for its vibrant colors and unique floral structure.
