Polyalthia amoena

Polyalthia amoena, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often giving the appearance of a flame. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland forests.

The tree is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
