Pilosella collina subsp. elegans

Pilosella collina subsp.

elegans, commonly known as the elegant mountain groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It is characterized by its hairy, silver-green leaves and small, yellow flower heads that bloom in early summer.
