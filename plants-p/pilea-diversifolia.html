Pilea diversifolia

Pilea diversifolia, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its rosette-like arrangement of heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.

Pilea diversifolia thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
