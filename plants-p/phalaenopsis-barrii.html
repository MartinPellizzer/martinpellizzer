Phalaenopsis barrii

Phalaenopsis barrii, commonly known as the Barri's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, arching inflorescences that bear numerous small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant has broad, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Phalaenopsis barrii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
