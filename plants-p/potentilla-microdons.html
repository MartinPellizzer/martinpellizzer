Potentilla microdons

Potentilla microdons, commonly known as the small potentilla, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit, with slender stems and small, rounded leaves. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is native to regions of Europe and western Asia, often found in grassy areas and rocky soils.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
