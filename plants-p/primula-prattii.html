Primula prattii

Primula prattii, commonly known as the pink primrose, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving at high altitudes. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of bell-shaped, pink or white flowers. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.

Primula prattii is also noted for its ability to bloom in early spring, adding color to the landscape when few other plants are in flower.
