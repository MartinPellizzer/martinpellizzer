Plectranthus tenuis

Plectranthus tenuis, commonly known as narrowleaf mint, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically lavender to purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Plectranthus tenuis is valued for its aromatic foliage and is often used in ornamental gardening.
