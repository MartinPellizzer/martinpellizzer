Plectranthus glabriflorus

Plectranthus glabriflorus, commonly known as the smoothflower plectranthus, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy on the underside, and it produces small, tubular flowers in shades of purple or pink. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a garden setting.
