Phalaenopsis buyssoniana

Phalaenopsis buyssoniana, commonly known as the velvet phalaenopsis, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, drooping inflorescences and clusters of velvety, dark purple to black flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad, elliptical, and often have a waxy texture.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
