Peperomia ophistachyera

Peperomia ophistachyera, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, and often velvety leaves that are typically dark green with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and attractive foliage, it is often used as a decorative plant in both indoor and outdoor settings.
