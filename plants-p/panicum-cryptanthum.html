Panicum cryptanthum

Panicum cryptanthum, commonly known as the little bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types, including sandy and clay soils. This plant is often found in prairie ecosystems and is adapted to full sun conditions. It produces small, fluffy seed heads that are an important food source for birds and small mammals.

Panicum cryptanthum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological restoration projects.
