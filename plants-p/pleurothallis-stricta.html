Pleurothallis stricta

Pleurothallis stricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has slender, linear leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and unique floral structure.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its beauty and rarity.
