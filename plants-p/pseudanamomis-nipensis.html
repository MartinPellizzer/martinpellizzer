Pseudanamomis nipensis

Pseudanamomis nipensis, commonly known as the Japanese arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are used in traditional cuisine. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers clustered in an inflorescence called a spadix. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
