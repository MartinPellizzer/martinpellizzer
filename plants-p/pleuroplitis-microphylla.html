Pleuroplitis microphylla

Pleuroplitis microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved pleurothallis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid native to tropical regions of South America. The plant features slender, wiry stems and small, ovate leaves that are densely packed along the stem. Its flowers are typically greenish-white with a faintly colored lip, and it blooms during the warmer months.

Due to its delicate structure and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments.
