Peperomia arifolia var. argyreia

Peperomia arifolia var. argyreia, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, waxy leaves that are typically silver or gray-green in color, often with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements, it is widely appreciated as a decorative plant.
