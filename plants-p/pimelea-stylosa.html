Pimelea stylosa

Pimelea stylosa, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mistflower, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, white or yellowish flowers that appear in late winter to early spring. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in heathlands and coastal areas. It has a dense root system that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

Pimelea stylosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
