Pauridia canaliculata

Pauridia canaliculata, commonly known as the narrow-ribbed orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in summer. The plant has slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, with a distinctive lip that is deeply notched.

Pauridia canaliculata is often found in shaded, moist habitats such as forests and mountainous regions.
