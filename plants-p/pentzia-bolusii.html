Pentzia bolusii

Pentzia bolusii, commonly known as the Bolus's daisies, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy yellow daisies with white ray flowers, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. It is often found in rocky or coastal areas, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments.

Pentzia bolusii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
