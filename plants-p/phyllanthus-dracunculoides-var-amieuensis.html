Phyllanthus dracunculoides var. amieuensis

Phyllanthus dracunculoides var. amieuensis, commonly known as the African spurge, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in moist, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by spherical, orange berries. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a waxy texture.

This species is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
