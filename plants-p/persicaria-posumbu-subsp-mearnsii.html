Persicaria posumbu subsp. mearnsii

Persicaria posumbu subsp.

mearnsii, commonly known as Mearns' smartweed, is a flowering plant native to North America. It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes and wet meadows.

The plant features upright, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often reddish in color. Its small, pinkish to reddish flowers appear in dense, spike-like clusters during the summer months.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing food and habitat for various wildlife species.
