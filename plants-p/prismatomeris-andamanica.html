Prismatomeris andamanica

Prismatomeris andamanica, commonly known as the Andaman starling orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to the Andaman Islands.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique star-like floral structure. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often on the forest floor or in rocky crevices. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and display a striking combination of white and purple hues.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its natural range.
