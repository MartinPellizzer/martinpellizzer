Phillyrea medianifolia subsp. flichei

Phillyrea medianifolia subsp.

flichei, commonly known as the Canary Island Phillyrea, is a shrub or small tree native to the Canary Islands. It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter, sometimes whitish underside.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, woody fruits. It thrives in coastal and rocky habitats, often growing in well-drained soils.

This subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and ecological importance in its native environment.
