Pourthiaea pilosicalyx

Pourthiaea pilosicalyx, commonly known as the silky pilosicalyx, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a compact cluster. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
