Pleurothallis affinis

Pleurothallis affinis, commonly known as the pink-veined orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, linear leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers with a distinctive pink and green veining pattern. Its flowers are usually borne in clusters and are known for their subtle beauty and unique coloration.

Pleurothallis affinis is popular among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
