Portulaca oleracea var. gillesii

Portulaca oleracea var. gillesii, commonly known as the Mexican wheatgrass, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Americas.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the summer. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it adaptable to various arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are often consumed as a leafy green vegetable, valued for their mild flavor and nutritional content.

This variety is closely related to other portulaca species and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
