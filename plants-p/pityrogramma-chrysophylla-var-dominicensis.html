Pityrogramma chrysophylla var. dominicensis

Pityrogramma chrysophylla var. dominicensis, commonly known as the Dominican spleenwort, is a fern species native to the Caribbean, particularly the Dominican Republic.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like fronds with a distinctive golden hue. This variant is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in botanical gardens.
