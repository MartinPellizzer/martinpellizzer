Podocarpus costalis var. muroii

Podocarpus costalis var. muroii, commonly known as the Japanese podocarp or Muroii's podocarp, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Podocarpaceae and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, cone-like fruits. The leaves are typically narrow and leathery, with a glossy surface that provides aesthetic appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and ability to tolerate urban environments.
