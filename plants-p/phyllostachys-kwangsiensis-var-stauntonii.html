Phyllostachys kwangsiensis var. stauntonii

Phyllostachys kwangsiensis var. stauntonii, commonly known as the Staunton bamboo, is a variant of the Kwangsi bamboo species native to China.

It is a tall, slender grass that can grow up to 10 meters in height, characterized by its elegant, gracefully arching culms. The leaves are long, narrow, and have a smooth, glossy surface, typically a deep green in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun exposure.

This bamboo is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
