Podanthum otites

Podanthum otites, commonly known as the silverleaf pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features narrow, silvery-green leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically white or pale yellow, which bloom in spring. Its growth habit is compact, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a decorative shrub.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
