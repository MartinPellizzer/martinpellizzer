Philadelphus sericanthus var. rehderianus

Philadelphus sericanthus var. rehderianus, commonly known as the Chinese mock orange, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
