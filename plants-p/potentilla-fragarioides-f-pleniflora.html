Potentilla fragarioides f. pleniflora

Potentilla fragarioides f. pleniflora, commonly known as the Full-flowered Cinquefoil, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its abundant, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features compound leaves with five oval leaflets, giving it the common name "cinquefoil.". It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
