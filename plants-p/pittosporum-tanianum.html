Pittosporum tanianum

Pittosporum tanianum, commonly known as the New Zealand pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. It produces fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by red berries.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in horticulture.
