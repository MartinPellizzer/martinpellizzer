Piper obovatum var. bolivianum

Piper obovatum var. bolivianum, commonly known as the Bolivian pepper plant, is a tropical species native to South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various pepper species. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves and small, clustered white flowers. Its fruits are initially green and turn red when mature, resembling tiny peppers.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
