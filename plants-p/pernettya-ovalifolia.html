Pernettya ovalifolia

Pernettya ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaf cowberry, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the northern regions of Europe and Asia.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy, with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of tiny, red berries that are edible and often used in local cuisines. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in boreal or subarctic climates.

Pernettya ovalifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
