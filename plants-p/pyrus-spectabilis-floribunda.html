Pyrus spectabilis floribunda

Pyrus spectabilis floribunda, commonly known as the showy pear, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its ornamental value, featuring clusters of fragrant white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are typically yellow or red in color. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
