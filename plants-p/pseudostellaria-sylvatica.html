Pseudostellaria sylvatica

Pseudostellaria sylvatica, commonly known as the woodland starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Pseudostellaria sylvatica is a perennial herb that is often found in temperate regions of Asia and Europe.
