Panicum rupestre var. setifolium

Panicum rupestre var. setifolium, commonly known as rock panicum, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, grass-like foliage. The plant produces fluffy, pink to purple flower spikes that bloom in the summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for native plant landscapes.
