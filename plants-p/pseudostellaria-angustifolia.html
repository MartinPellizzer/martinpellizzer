Pseudostellaria angustifolia

Pseudostellaria angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf starwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia and North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early summer, often in shades of white or pale pink. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

The species is characterized by its delicate appearance and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
