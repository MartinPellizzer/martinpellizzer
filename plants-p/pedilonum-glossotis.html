Pedilonum glossotis

Pedilonum glossotis, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
