Pulchranthus adenostachyus

Pulchranthus adenostachyus, commonly known as the red-flowered tickseed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, bright red composite flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has hairy, linear leaves and produces small, dark seeds in its fruiting structure.
