Peperomia reflexa var. piedadeana

Peperomia reflexa var. piedadeana, commonly known as the Piedadean Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, pale green to white flowers that emerge from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental foliage and compact growth habit, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
