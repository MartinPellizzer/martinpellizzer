Piper nematanthera

Piper nematanthera, commonly known as the velvet leaf pepper, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves that give it its common name. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by small, round berries.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in herbal medicine.
