Poterium sanguisorba proles obscurum

Poterium sanguisorba proles obscurum, commonly known as the dark-stemmed sorrel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dark reddish-brown stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, reddish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.
