Peperomia nievecitana

Peperomia nievecitana, commonly known as the Snowy Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Mexico.

It features dense, waxy foliage with a distinctive silvery-blue hue on the underside of its leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are typically hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia nievecitana is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
