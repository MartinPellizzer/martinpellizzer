Psorospermum febrifugum var. ferrugineum

Psorospermum febrifugum var. ferrugineum, commonly known as the rusty psorospermum, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a rusty-colored pubescence. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are woody capsules containing several seeds.

It is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
