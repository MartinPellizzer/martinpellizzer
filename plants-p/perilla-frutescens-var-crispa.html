Perilla frutescens var. crispa

Perilla frutescens var. crispa, commonly known as curled perilla, is a herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive crinkled, wavy leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, pale purple flowers and is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its edible leaves. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.

The leaves are commonly used in Asian cuisine and traditional medicine for their aromatic and medicinal properties.
