Panicum assurgens

Panicum assurgens, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and vibrant red or purple flower spikes that appear in late summer. The plant typically grows in full sun and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for ornamental use in gardens. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a reddish hue that adds visual interest throughout the growing season.

Panicum assurgens is also valued for its ability to withstand drought and poor soil conditions.
