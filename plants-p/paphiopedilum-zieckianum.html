Paphiopedilum zieckianum

Paphiopedilum zieckianum, commonly known as the Zieck's lady's slipper, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the mountainous regions of Borneo.

It is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped pouch, which is a defining feature of the Paphiopedilum genus. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid, with dark green, pleated leaves and showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and white. It prefers shady, humid environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a valuable and protected species in conservation efforts.
