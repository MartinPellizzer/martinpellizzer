Poa tayacajaensis

Poa tayacajaensis, commonly known as the Taya Caja grass, is a species of flowering plant in the Poaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly in Peru and Bolivia. This grass is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems. Its leaves are narrow and often have a bluish-green hue, and it produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

Poa tayacajaensis is adapted to high-altitude environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.
