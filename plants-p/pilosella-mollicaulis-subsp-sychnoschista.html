Pilosella mollicaulis subsp. sychnoschista

Pilosella mollicaulis subsp.

sychnoschista, commonly known as the hairy leaf pearly everlasting, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and showy inflorescences.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are densely hairy, and the flowers are yellow with pale green ray florets.
