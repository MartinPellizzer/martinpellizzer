Pinus Radiata Var. Binata

Pinus radiata var. binata is a unique variant of the radiata pine known for its distinct binate foliage. This plant exhibits a striking two-needle pattern, setting it apart from its standard counterpart. Native to specific regions of New Zealand, it thrives in coastal and mountainous environments. Its ecological and horticultural value has made it a subject of interest for researchers and gardeners alike. The plant's beauty and adaptability highlight its importance in both natural and cultivated landscapes.
