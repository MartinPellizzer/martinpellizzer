Pelargonium glabrifolium

Pelargonium glabrifolium, commonly known as the smooth-leaved geranium, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, smooth leaves and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant is often grown as an ornamental due to its attractive foliage and blooms. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Pelargonium glabrifolium is also valued for its ability to thrive in dry conditions, making it suitable for xeriscaping.
