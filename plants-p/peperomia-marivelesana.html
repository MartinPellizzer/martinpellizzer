Peperomia marivelesana

Peperomia marivelesana, commonly known as the Mariveles peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to the Philippines.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with prominent veins, often displaying a variegated pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of pink or white, which are borne on short spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is appreciated by plant enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
