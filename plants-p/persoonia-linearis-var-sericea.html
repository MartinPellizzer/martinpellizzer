Persoonia linearis var. sericea

Persoonia linearis var. sericea, commonly known as the silky everlasting or silky bottlebrush, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces bright yellow or orange flowers that are clustered in cylindrical inflorescences, resembling bottlebrushes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and rocky areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and long-lasting flowers.
