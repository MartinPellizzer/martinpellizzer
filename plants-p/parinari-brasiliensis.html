Parinari brasiliensis

Parinari brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian cherry or Brazilian apple, is a tropical tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and simple, alternate leaves.
