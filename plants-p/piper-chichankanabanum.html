Piper chichankanabanum

Piper chichankanabanum, commonly known as the Chichankanabanum pepper, is a species native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many pepper species. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing vines and heart-shaped leaves. Its fruit is a small, dark purple berry that contains several seeds.

This plant is valued for its unique flavor and potential medicinal properties.
