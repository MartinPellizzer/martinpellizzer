Piper estrellense var. pilosius

Piper estrellense var. pilosius, commonly known as the pilosius pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Colombia and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and compound leaves with a hairy underside. Its small, greenish-white flowers are followed by elliptical, dark purple berries.

This variety is noted for its unique morphology and potential use in traditional medicine.
