Pilea pusilla

Pilea pusilla, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a small, compact herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features rosettes of glossy, rounded leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in hue. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Pilea pusilla is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
