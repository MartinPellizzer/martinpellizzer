Portulaca dhofarica

Portulaca dhofarica, commonly known as the Dhofar portulaca, is a flowering plant native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It is a low-growing, succulent plant that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant produces small, vibrant flowers that are typically pink or red in color. Its leaves are fleshy and adapted to store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions.

Portulaca dhofarica is often found in rocky soils and coastal areas, making it a resilient species in harsh climates.
