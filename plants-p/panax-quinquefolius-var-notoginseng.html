Panax quinquefolius var. notoginseng

Panax quinquefolius var. notoginseng, commonly known as notoginseng, is a variant of the American ginseng plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its distinctive five-leaf pattern and is often cultivated for its medicinal properties. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers and red berries, which contain the active compounds responsible for its traditional uses. Notoginseng is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported ability to enhance vitality and promote blood circulation.

Its botanical features make it a unique subject of study in both pharmacology and botany.
