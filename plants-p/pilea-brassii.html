Pilea brassii

Pilea brassii, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rounded, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea brassii is known for its easy care and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments.
