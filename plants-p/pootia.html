Pootia

Pootia, commonly known as the ghost flower, is a rare and enigmatic plant native to the remote highlands of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its translucent, waxy leaves that appear to shimmer in the sunlight, giving it its ethereal appearance. The plant produces small, delicate white flowers that bloom only under specific environmental conditions, making it difficult to study. Pootia thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded, forested areas.

Due to its elusive nature and limited distribution, it is considered a highly specialized and endangered species.
