Panicum angustissimum

Panicum angustissimum, commonly known as narrowleaf panicgrass, is a species of grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in open woodlands and disturbed areas.

Its ecological role includes providing habitat and food for various insects and small animals.
