Paraprasophyllum colemaniarum

Paraprasophyllum colemaniarum, commonly known as the Coleman's paraprasophyllum, is a rare and unique plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

The plant produces small, delicate flowers with a distinctive coloration that ranges from pale green to yellowish-white. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.
