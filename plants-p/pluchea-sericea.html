Pluchea sericea

Pluchea sericea, commonly known as silky leaf sunflower, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant features finely pubescent leaves and yellow ray flowers, giving it a delicate appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
