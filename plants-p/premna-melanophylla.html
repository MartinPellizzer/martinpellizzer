Premna melanophylla

Premna melanophylla, commonly known as black-leaf premna, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn from green to black as they mature.

Premna melanophylla is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
