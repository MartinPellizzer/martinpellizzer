Pittosporum pimeleoides var. gilliesianum

Pittosporum pimeleoides var. gilliesianum, commonly known as the coastal pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves and fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in coastal environments, tolerating salt spray and sandy soils. Its fruit is a small, woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing coastal dunes and providing habitat for native wildlife.
