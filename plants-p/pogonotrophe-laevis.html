Pogonotrophe laevis

Pogonotrophe laevis, commonly known as the smooth-tongued orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its delicate structure, it requires careful cultivation to ensure optimal growth and blooming.
