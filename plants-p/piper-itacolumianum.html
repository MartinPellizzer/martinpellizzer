Piper itacolumianum

Piper itacolumianum, commonly known as the Itacolumi pepper, is a flowering plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with opposite, ovate leaves that are often glossy. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are followed by tiny, rounded berries that turn red when mature.

This species is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
