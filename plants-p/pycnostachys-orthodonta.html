Pycnostachys orthodonta

Pycnostachys orthodonta, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and showy, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of violet, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
