Pityrogramma tartarea var. ornithopteris

Pityrogramma tartarea var. ornithopteris, commonly known as the feather fern, is a small, terrestrial fern native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that resemble bird wings, hence its common name. This variety is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
