Piper opacilimbum

Piper opacilimbum, commonly known as the dark pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its dark, glossy leaves and small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It produces small, round berries that contain the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and as a spice.

The botanical features of Piper opacilimbum make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and pharmacological study.
