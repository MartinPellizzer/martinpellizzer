Plectranthus multiflorus

Plectranthus multiflorus, commonly known as the purple heart plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves that range in color from deep purple to green. It produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or blue, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Plectranthus multiflorus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and easy care requirements.
