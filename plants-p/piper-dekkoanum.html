Piper dekkoanum

Piper dekkoanum, commonly known as the Dekkoanum pepper, is a climbing vine native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into elongated, cylindrical fruits. These fruits are typically harvested when they turn red or orange, indicating maturity.

The plant is valued for its aromatic leaves and fruits, which are used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.
