Panicum barbulatum

Panicum barbulatum, commonly known as rough panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a member of the grass family Poaceae and typically grows as a herbaceous perennial. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense, spike-like clusters of small, greenish flowers. Its leaves are narrow and often covered with fine hairs, contributing to its rough texture.

Panicum barbulatum is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
