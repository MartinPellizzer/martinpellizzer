Paphiopedilum × aspersum var. aspersum

Paphiopedilum × aspersum var. aspersum, commonly known as the Chinese velvet slipper orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped pouch that serves as a pollination mechanism. The plant typically produces dark green, velvety leaves and bears fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in horticulture.
