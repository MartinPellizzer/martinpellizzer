Pacouria owariensis

Pacouria owariensis, commonly known as the Owariensis Cabbage, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other cruciferous plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, rounded foliage. Its flowers are small and typically yellow or white, arranged in clusters.
