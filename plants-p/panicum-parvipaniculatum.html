Panicum parvipaniculatum

Panicum parvipaniculatum, commonly known as dwarf panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, feathery inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as lawns, roadsides, and disturbed soils. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

It is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its fine texture and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
