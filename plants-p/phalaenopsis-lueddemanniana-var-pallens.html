Phalaenopsis lueddemanniana var. pallens

Phalaenopsis lueddemanniana var. pallens, commonly known as the pale phalaenopsis orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender stems and clusters of pale yellow to white flowers with a faintly colored lip. The plant typically blooms once a year, producing multiple flower spikes that can last several months. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture and a deep green color.

This variety is prized for its elegant appearance and is popular in horticulture for indoor cultivation.
