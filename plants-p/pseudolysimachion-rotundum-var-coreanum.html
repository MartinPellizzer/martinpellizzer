Pseudolysimachion rotundum var. coreanum

Pseudolysimachion rotundum var. coreanum, commonly known as the Korean roundleaf lythrum, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Lythraceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partially shaded to sunny conditions. Its leaves are ovate to round in shape, with a smooth texture, and the stems are often hairy.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
