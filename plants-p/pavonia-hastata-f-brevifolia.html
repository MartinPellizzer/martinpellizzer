Pavonia hastata f. brevifolia

Pavonia hastata f. brevifolia, commonly known as the narrowleaf wood rose, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, pink to red bract-like flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a bushy growth habit and is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and floral display. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant collections and ecological landscapes.
