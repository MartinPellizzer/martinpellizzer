Polycarena comptonii

Polycarena comptonii, commonly known as the Compton's polycarp, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its small, clustered white flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant thrives in wet, swampy environments and is often found in association with other wetland flora. It plays a role in maintaining the ecological balance of its habitat by providing food and shelter for various insects and small animals.
