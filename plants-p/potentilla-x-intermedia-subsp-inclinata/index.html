Potentilla × Intermedia Subsp. Inclinata

Potentilla × intermedia subsp. inclinata is a lesser-known yet fascinating member of the Rosaceae family. Native to the mountainous regions of central and eastern Europe, this shrub thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. Its compact growth habit and attractive yellow flowers make it a valuable addition to gardens and natural landscapes. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated compared to more popular garden plants. This article explores its characteristics, habitat, and potential uses in horticulture and ecological settings.
