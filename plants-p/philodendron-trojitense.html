Philodendron trojitense

Philodendron trojitense, commonly known as the Tzitzimimey or Tzitzimimey Philodendron, is a climbing plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking white spadix and green spathe flowers, typical of many philodendrons. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical plant collections.
