Pogopetalum orbiculatum

Pogopetalum orbiculatum, commonly known as the globe pogostemon, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are orbicular in shape, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its compact size and attractive foliage.

Pogopetalum orbiculatum prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.
