Pelargonium × coeleste

Pelargonium × coeleste, commonly known as the blue geranium, is a hybrid shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flower clusters, which bloom in summer. The plant features broad, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Pelargonium × coeleste is also valued for its aromatic foliage and compact growth habit.
