Polystichum crinigerum

Polystichum crinigerum, commonly known as the silver fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery fronds that are covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its fronds are pinnately compound, with closely spaced, lance-shaped pinnae.

Polystichum crinigerum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
