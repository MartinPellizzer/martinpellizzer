Piper nigrantherum

Piper nigrantherum, commonly known as black pepper vine, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, green berries that mature to a dark purple or black color, resembling black pepper. The leaves are heart-shaped with a rough texture and emit a peppery aroma.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
