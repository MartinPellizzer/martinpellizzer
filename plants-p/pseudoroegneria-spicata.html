Pseudoroegneria spicata

Pseudoroegneria spicata, commonly known as blue grama, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and compact growth habit. The plant produces slender, spike-like inflorescences that bear small, fluffy seeds. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and prairies.

Blue grama is well adapted to drought conditions and is an important component of many native plant communities.
