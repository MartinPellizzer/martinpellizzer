Phreatia bigibbula

Phreatia bigibbula, commonly known as the giant phreatia, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fleshy leaves and tall, branched inflorescences that bear numerous showy white to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine, and it is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Phreatia bigibbula is a member of the Orchidaceae family and exhibits epiphytic or lithophytic growth habits.
