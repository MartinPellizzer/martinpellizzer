Perlebia macrantha

Perlebia macrantha, commonly known as the giant perlebia, is a striking herbaceous plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features a robust stem with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowering period attracts various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.

Perlebia macrantha is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant display.
