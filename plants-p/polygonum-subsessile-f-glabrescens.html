Polygonum subsessile f. glabrescens

Polygonum subsessile f. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth knotweed, is a variant of the common knotweed species.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia and is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and leaves. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as wetlands, stream banks, and marshy areas. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and are usually pink or red in color.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various insects and small animals.
