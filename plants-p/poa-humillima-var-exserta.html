Poa humillima var. exserta

Poa humillima var. exserta, commonly known as the slender bentgrass, is a low-growing, perennial grass species native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically forms dense, mat-like growths and has slender, wiry stems that often curl upward. The leaves are narrow, flat, and slightly hairy, with a bluish-green coloration. Its flowering stems bear small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny, spikelet-like flowers.

This variety is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodland edges and rocky soils.
