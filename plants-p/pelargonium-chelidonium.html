Pelargonium chelidonium

Pelargonium chelidonium, commonly known as the golden geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its showy, yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features hairy stems and leaves that are typically rounded or heart-shaped. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Pelargonium chelidonium is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
