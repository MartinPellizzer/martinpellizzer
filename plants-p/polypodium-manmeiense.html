Polypodium manmeiense

Polypodium manmeiense, commonly known as the Manmeiense rock fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The leaves are usually dark green and can vary in shape depending on the environmental conditions.
