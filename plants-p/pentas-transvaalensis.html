Pentas transvaalensis

Pentas transvaalensis, commonly known as the Transvaal pentas, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, star-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 60 cm. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
