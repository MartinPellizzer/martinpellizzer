Pleopeltis schultzei

Pleopeltis schultzei, commonly known as the Schultze's rock fern, is a species of epiphytic and lithophytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its creeping rhizomes and dense, overlapping foliage. The fronds are typically narrow and elongated, with a leathery texture and a distinctive, glossy appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments on tree trunks or rocky surfaces.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
