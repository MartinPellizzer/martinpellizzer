Piper sancarlosianum

Piper sancarlosianum, commonly known as the Sancarlos pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its vine-like growth habit and small, clustered flowers. Its fruits are typically small berries that contain a peppery flavor, similar to other members of the Piper genus.

It is often cultivated for its culinary and medicinal properties.
