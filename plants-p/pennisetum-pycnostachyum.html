Pennisetum pycnostachyum

Pennisetum pycnostachyum, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, clumping growth habit and striking purple to silver-colored foliage. The plant produces tall, feathery plumes of flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Pennisetum pycnostachyum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
