Peperomia egleri

Peperomia egleri, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with distinctive silver veins, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal water needs.

Peperomia egleri thrives in well-draining soil and prefers moderate temperatures.
