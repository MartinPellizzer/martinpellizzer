Plantago sphaerostachys

Plantago sphaerostachys, commonly known as the globe plantain, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of broad, waxy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces compact, spherical inflorescences that bear small, greenish flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
