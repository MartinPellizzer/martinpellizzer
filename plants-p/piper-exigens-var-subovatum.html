Piper exigens var. subovatum

Piper exigens var. subovatum, commonly known as the narrow-ovate pepper plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The leaves are typically narrow and ovate, with a glossy surface and a distinct aromatic scent. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into peppery fruits.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and for its potential medicinal properties.
