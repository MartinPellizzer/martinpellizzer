Polygala cantalica

Polygala cantalica, commonly known as the mountain polygala, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or limestone soils. The plant features slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often arranged alternately along the stem.

Polygala cantalica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
