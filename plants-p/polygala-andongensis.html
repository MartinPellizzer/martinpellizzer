Polygala andongensis

Polygala andongensis, commonly known as the Andong milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as mountain forests. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink and are visited by various pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
