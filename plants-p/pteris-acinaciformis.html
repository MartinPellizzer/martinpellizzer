Pteris acinaciformis

Pteris acinaciformis, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Pteridaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, sword-shaped fronds that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, spore-bearing structures called sori on the underside of its fronds. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Pteris acinaciformis is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in indoor settings.
