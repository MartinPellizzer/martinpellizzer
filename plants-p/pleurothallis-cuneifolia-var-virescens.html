Pleurothallis cuneifolia var. virescens

Pleurothallis cuneifolia var. virescens, commonly known as the green-stemded pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the Andes of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green. The flowers are small and clustered, often in shades of white or pale yellow, with a subtle fragrance. This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
