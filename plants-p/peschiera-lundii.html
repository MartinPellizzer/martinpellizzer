Peschiera lundii

Peschiera lundii, commonly known as the Swedish sandwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Montiaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and small, clustered flowers. Its medicinal properties have been explored in traditional herbal remedies.
