Petunia thymifolia f. brevipedunculata

Petunia thymifolia f. brevipedunculata, commonly known as the short-stemmed thyme-leaved petunia, is a compact, ornamental plant with small, fragrant flowers.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its narrow, aromatic leaves that resemble those of thyme. The plant typically produces clusters of violet or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

This cultivar is prized for its compact growth habit and unique foliage.
