Polygonum nitidum

Polygonum nitidum, commonly known as the glossy smartweed, is a flowering plant native to various parts of Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea.

It is a member of the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as wetlands, marshes, and stream banks. Its leaves are smooth and glossy, giving it a unique appearance, while its stems are often reddish in color.

Polygonum nitidum is often used in traditional medicine and horticulture due to its ornamental value and potential health benefits.
