Polyscias bracteata subsp. bracteata

Polyscias bracteata subsp.

bracteata, commonly known as the laceleaf polyscias, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by elliptical, woody fruits containing smooth, black seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage. Polyscias bracteata subsp.

bracteata is also valued for its air-purifying properties and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
