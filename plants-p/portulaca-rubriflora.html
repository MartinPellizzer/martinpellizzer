Portulaca rubriflora

Portulaca rubriflora, commonly known as the red-flowered portulaca, is a species of flowering plant in the family Portulacaceae.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and vibrant red flowers. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to drought conditions. Its flowers typically bloom in summer and are attractively arranged in clusters.

Portulaca rubriflora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
