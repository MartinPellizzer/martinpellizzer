Potentilla × budaiana

Potentilla × budaiana, commonly known as the Budaian cinquefoil, is a hybrid shrub native to Central Europe.

It features showy, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. The plant has compound leaves with five leaflets, which are typically oval and slightly serrated. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value and hardy nature make it a popular choice in horticulture.
