Plantago maritima var. scabrior

Plantago maritima var. scabrior, commonly known as sea plantain or common sea plantain, is a perennial herb native to coastal regions.

It thrives in saline and sandy environments, often found along shorelines and dunes. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid conditions. Its small, greenish flowers are clustered in spikes and bloom in the summer.

It is valued for its hardiness and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
