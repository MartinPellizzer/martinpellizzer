Pavonia blepharicarpa

Pavonia blepharicarpa, commonly known as the showy pavonia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in moist, sunny habitats such as woodlands and wetlands. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Pavonia blepharicarpa is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
