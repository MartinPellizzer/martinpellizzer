Polygonum macrophyllum var. stenophyllum

Polygonum macrophyllum var. stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved smartweed, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, depending on the variety. It thrives in wetland environments and is often found in marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Polygonaceae family, known for its unique leaf morphology and ecological adaptability.
