Peperomia enenyasensis

Peperomia enenyasensis, commonly known as the Eneias Mountains peperomia, is a compact, low-growing perennial plant native to the humid forests of Ecuador.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a velvety texture and small, clustered inflorescences that bloom in shades of red and purple. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and prefers well-draining soil. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.

Peperomia enenyasensis is also valued for its role in preserving biodiversity in its native habitat.
