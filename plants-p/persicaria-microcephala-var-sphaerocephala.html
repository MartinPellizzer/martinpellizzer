Persicaria microcephala var. sphaerocephala

Persicaria microcephala var. sphaerocephala, commonly known as the globe-headed knotweed, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded inflorescences that give it its common name. The plant features small, white to pinkish flowers arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.
