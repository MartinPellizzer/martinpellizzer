Pittosporum oreillyanum

Pittosporum oreillyanum, commonly known as the Orewa pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage, which consists of glossy, dark green leaves with a leathery texture. The plant produces fragrant white or cream-colored flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in spring. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to various soil types.

The fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds.
