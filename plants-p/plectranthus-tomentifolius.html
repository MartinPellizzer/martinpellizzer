Plectranthus tomentifolius

Plectranthus tomentifolius, commonly known as velvetleaf plectranthus, is a herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features broad, velvety leaves covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Plectranthus tomentifolius is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and easy care requirements.
