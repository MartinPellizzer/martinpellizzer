Peperomia reflexa f. brevipes

Peperomia reflexa f. brevipes, commonly known as the compact peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant with thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America. The plant features small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color, clustered in spikes. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its ability to thrive in low light conditions and its minimal water requirements.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for decorative purposes in both homes and offices.
