Planchonella dictyoneura

Planchonella dictyoneura, commonly known as the broad-leaved pittosporum, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are broadly elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by yellowish-orange berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in coastal and lowland rainforests.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture.
