Panicum laetum

Panicum laetum, commonly known as yellow panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces fluffy, yellowish to golden seed heads that are often used in floral arrangements. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a common ornamental and forage species. It is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in disturbed areas.

Its rapid growth and drought tolerance make it adaptable to various environmental conditions.
