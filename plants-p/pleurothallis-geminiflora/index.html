Pleurothallis Geminiflora

Pleurothallis geminiflora is a rare and delicate orchid native to the cloud forests of South America. Known for its strikingly small, clustered flowers, it captivates botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments, often nestled among moss and leaf litter. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of scientific interest. Despite its beauty, it remains a challenging plant to cultivate outside its natural habitat.
