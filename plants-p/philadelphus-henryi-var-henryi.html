Philadelphus henryi var. henryi

Philadelphus henryi var. henryi, commonly known as Chinese mock orange, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
