Phaseolus compressus var. major

Phaseolus compressus var. major, commonly known as the large flat bean, is a tropical legume native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, flat pods that mature to a dark brown color. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with trifoliate leaves and small white or purple flowers. Its seeds are oval-shaped and often used in local cuisines.

The plant is valued for its nutritional content and adaptability to various soil conditions.
