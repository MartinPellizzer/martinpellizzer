Peperomia cyclophylla f. zulianensis

Peperomia cyclophylla f. zulianensis, commonly known as the Zulian peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves with a velvety texture and a deep red underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage and ease of care make it a popular choice for collectors and indoor gardeners.
