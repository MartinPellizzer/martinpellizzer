Panicum rugelii

Panicum rugelii, commonly known as rugel's panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, often reddish-colored stems. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny, pale green to brownish spikelets. Its leaves are narrow, hairy, and typically have a bluish-green hue.

Panicum rugelii is often found in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and prairies.
