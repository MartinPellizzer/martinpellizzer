Peperomia glabella var. brachyphylla

Peperomia glabella var. brachyphylla, commonly known as the compact peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, glossy, dark green foliage with a velvety texture, and its leaves are typically shorter and broader than those of the standard glabella variety. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, which are not particularly showy. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ease of care and attractive appearance.

This variety thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for both beginner and experienced plant enthusiasts.
