Pteronia paniculata

Pteronia paniculata, commonly known as the Cape gumboot, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its upright, grass-like foliage and showy, clustered inflorescences. Its resilience and ornamental value contribute to its widespread cultivation in Mediterranean climates.
