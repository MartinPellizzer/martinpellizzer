Phlox carolina var. ovata

Phlox carolina var. ovata, commonly known as the smooth phlox, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 12 to 24 inches. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and slightly hairy. Its showy clusters of pink to purple flowers bloom in late spring to early summer.

It prefers partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
