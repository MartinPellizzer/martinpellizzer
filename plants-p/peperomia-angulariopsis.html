Peperomia angulariopsis

Peperomia angulariopsis, commonly known as the angular peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval or heart-shaped, with a distinctive angular appearance along the leaf margins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green or red, which are often hidden among the foliage. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Peperomia angulariopsis is valued for its unique foliage and air-purifying qualities, making it a popular choice for ornamental plant collections.
