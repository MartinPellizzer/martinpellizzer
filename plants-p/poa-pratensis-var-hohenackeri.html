Poa pratensis var. hohenackeri

Poa pratensis var. hohenackeri, commonly known as Hohenacker's meadow grass, is a variant of the common bentgrass.

It is a low-growing perennial grass characterized by its fine, tufted foliage and dense, carpet-like growth habit. This plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and grasslands. Its botanical features include narrow, grass-like leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
