Polystachya bicarinata

Polystachya bicarinata, commonly known as the two-ribbed sword fern, is a member of the Polystachyaceae family.

It is a terrestrial fern characterized by its long, strap-like fronds that are often arching or drooping. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizome is thick and fleshy, aiding in water storage and nutrient absorption.

Polystachya bicarinata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant foliage.
