Potentilla philippinensis

Potentilla philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. The plant features small, yellow, five-petaled flowers that bloom in the spring and summer seasons. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a serrated edge, contributing to its ornamental value.

Potentilla philippinensis is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in challenging soil conditions.
