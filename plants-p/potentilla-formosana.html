Potentilla formosana

Potentilla formosana, commonly known as the Formosan cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant features compound leaves with five leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Potentilla formosana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
