Portulaca diversifolia var. saltensis

Portulaca diversifolia var. saltensis, commonly known as saltbush or desert purslane, is a succulent plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It features fleshy, ribbed stems and leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the morning and close by midday. It thrives in saline soils and is well adapted to high temperatures and low rainfall.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy species suitable for xeriscaping and ecological restoration projects.
