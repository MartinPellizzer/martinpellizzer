Pulassarium odoratum

Pulassarium odoratum, commonly known as the sweet sedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Cyperaceae.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces a mild, sweet fragrance, especially when the flowers are in bloom. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and pleasant aroma.
