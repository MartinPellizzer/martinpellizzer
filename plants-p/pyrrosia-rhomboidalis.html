Pyrrosia rhomboidalis

Pyrrosia rhomboidalis, commonly known as the rhomboid fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, lanceolate leaves that are often clustered in a rosette formation. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically growing on trees or rocks. Its fronds are usually dark green with a waxy texture, and they can reach lengths of up to 30 centimeters.

Pyrrosia rhomboidalis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and air-purifying qualities.
