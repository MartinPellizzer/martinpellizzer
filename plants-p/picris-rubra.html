Picris rubra

Picris rubra, commonly known as red hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange-yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges, and it grows up to 60 cm tall. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry meadows and rocky areas.

The plant is known for its ornamental value and has been used traditionally in herbal medicine.
