Plectocephalus dracaenoides

Plectocephalus dracaenoides, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, zigzagging stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often variegated with yellow or white markings. The plant is evergreen and thrives in well-drained soil with bright, indirect light. It is relatively low-maintenance and suitable for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low humidity and infrequent watering.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental purposes.
