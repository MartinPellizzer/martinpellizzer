Perymenium globosum var. rotundisquamum

Perymenium globosum var. rotundisquamum, commonly known as the globular perymenium, is a species of flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, globular growth habit and produces small, clustered flowers. The leaves are typically rounded and covered in a dense layer of fine, scale-like structures. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical forests.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
