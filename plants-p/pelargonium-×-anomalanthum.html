Pelargonium × anomalanthum

Pelargonium × anomalanthum, commonly known as the hybrid geranium, is a flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is characterized by its showy, often bi-colored flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant typically has succulent, lobed leaves that are hairy or glandular, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.

Pelargonium × anomalanthum is often cultivated in gardens and as a potted plant for its aesthetic value.
