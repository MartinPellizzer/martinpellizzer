Parthenium glomeratum

Parthenium glomeratum, commonly known as clustered ragweed, is an annual herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It features slender, branching stems that grow close to the ground, often forming dense mats. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in compact clusters, which are typically inconspicuous and arranged in spikes. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a rough texture.

Parthenium glomeratum is adapted to arid environments and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.
