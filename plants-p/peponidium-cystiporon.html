Peponidium cystiporon

Peponidium cystiporon, commonly known as the golden star fruit, is a tropical plant belonging to the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow, star-shaped fruits that are rich in vitamin C and antioxidants. The fruit develops from the flowering stage and is often harvested when it reaches full maturity.
