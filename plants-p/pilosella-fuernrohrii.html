Pilosella fuernrohrii

Pilosella fuernrohrii, commonly known as the Fuernrohri's pearly everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features dense, white to pale yellow ray flowers surrounded by a ring of yellow disk flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow, linear, and covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance.

Pilosella fuernrohrii is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, thriving in well-drained, sunny conditions.
