Prunus × yedoensis f. nudiflora

Prunus × yedoensis f. nudiflora, commonly known as the Japanese cherry blossom with naked flowers, is a unique cultivar of the Japanese cherry tree.

It is characterized by its early blooming period, with flowers appearing before the leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance. The flowers are typically pale pink to white, and they emit a subtle fragrance. This variety is prized for its ornamental value, particularly in spring when it displays a profusion of blossoms.

It is often cultivated in gardens and parks for its aesthetic appeal and cultural significance.
