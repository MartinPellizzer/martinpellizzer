Plectranthus rotundifolius

Plectranthus rotundifolius, commonly known as African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, rounded leaves and produces showy spikes of tubular flowers in shades of purple, blue, and white. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Plectranthus rotundifolius is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
