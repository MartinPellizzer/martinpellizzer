Piper makruense

Piper makruense, commonly known as the African pepper or Malawi pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when mature, containing the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and culinary applications. Its leaves are often used in local remedies for their purported medicinal properties.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
