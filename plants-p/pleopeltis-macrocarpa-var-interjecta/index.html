Pleopeltis Macrocarpa Var. Interjecta

Cushion fern, scientifically known as Pleopeltis macrocarpa var. interjecta, is a unique and striking plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. Its compact, rounded form resembles a soft, leafy cushion, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens. This variety is distinguished by its large, leathery fronds that display a vibrant green hue with subtle variegation. It thrives in humid environments with indirect light, requiring careful attention to moisture and temperature. Despite its delicate appearance, the cushion fern is remarkably resilient, adapting well to a range of growing conditions when properly cared for.
