Primulina minutimaculata

Primulina minutimaculata, commonly known as the miniature speckled primula, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, velvety leaves that are often speckled with dark green markings, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of delicate, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically blooming in spring and early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is widely appreciated by plant enthusiasts.
