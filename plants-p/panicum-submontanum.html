Panicum submontanum

Panicum submontanum, commonly known as mountain panicgrass, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland and mountainous regions. The plant has slender, upright stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

It is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
