Primula franchetii subsp. forrestii

Primula franchetii subsp.

forrestii, commonly known as the Yunnan primrose, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China. It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are broad and ovate, often covered in a fine layer of hair.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
