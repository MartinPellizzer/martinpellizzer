Pultenaea villosa var. lanata

Pultenaea villosa var. lanata, commonly known as the woolly pultenaea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal and inland regions of southern Australia.

The variety lanata is distinguished by its more extensive pubescence compared to the typical form.
