Philodendron acreanum

Philodendron acreanum, commonly known as the Brazilian Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It features heart-shaped, glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or light green markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers, which are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Philodendron acreanum is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
