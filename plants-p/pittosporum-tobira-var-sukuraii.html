Pittosporum tobira var. sukuraii

Pittosporum tobira var. sukuraii, commonly known as the Japanese Pittosporum, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features glossy, dark green foliage with a slightly fragrant scent, and its flowers are white with a yellow center, appearing in spring. The plant produces small, round, red berries that are edible but not particularly palatable. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

This variety is often used in landscaping for its dense growth habit and seasonal color changes.
