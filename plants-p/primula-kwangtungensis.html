Primula kwangtungensis

Primula kwangtungensis, commonly known as the Guangdong primrose, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. The plant has broad, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hair, giving it a soft appearance. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas or along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is more commonly seen in its natural habitat.
