Pelargonium × mirabile

Pelargonium × mirabile, commonly known as the Marvelous Geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, five-petaled flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture. This plant is native to South Africa and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for both container and in-ground planting.
