Peperomia naranjoana

Peperomia naranjoana, commonly known as the Orange Peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It features dense, glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a reddish or orange tinge on the underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers in clusters, which are followed by bright red berries. It is a popular houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.

Peperomia naranjoana thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.
