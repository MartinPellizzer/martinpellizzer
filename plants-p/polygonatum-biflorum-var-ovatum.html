Polygonatum biflorum var. ovatum

Polygonatum biflorum var. ovatum, commonly known as the yellow-flowered Solomon's seal, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features upright, cylindrical stems that bear clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces oval, dark green leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. Its rhizomatous root system is often used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
