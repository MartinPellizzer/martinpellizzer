Potentilla × savulescui

Potentilla × savulescui, commonly known as the hybrid shrub, is a deciduous plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of yellow and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
