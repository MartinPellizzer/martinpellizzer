Potentilla × szaboi

Potentilla × szaboi, commonly known as the Szabó's cinquefoil, is a hybrid shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse display of yellow flowers in early summer. The plant typically features small, oval leaves with a slightly serrated edge, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy or rocky soils. It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

Due to its limited natural range, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
