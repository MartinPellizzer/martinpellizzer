Pyrus turcomanica f. elliptica

Pyrus turcomanica f. elliptica, commonly known as the Turkestan pear.

It is a compact, shrub-like form of the Turkestan pear, characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are typically yellow or green when ripe. It is native to Central Asia and thrives in well-drained soils with full sun exposure.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various garden conditions.
