Phlomis polioxantha

Phlomis polioxantha, commonly known as the pink hedgehog.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its tall, upright growth and vibrant pink to purple flowers. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Phlomis polioxantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
