Primula argutidens

Primula argutidens, commonly known as the serrated primrose, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, pink to purple flowers. The leaves are ovate with serrated edges, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
