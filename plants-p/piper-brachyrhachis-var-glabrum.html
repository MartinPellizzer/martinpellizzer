Piper brachyrhachis var. glabrum

Piper brachyrhachis var. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed pepper plant, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various pepper species. The plant is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into elongated, cylindrical berries.

The berries are typically harvested when mature, revealing a peppery flavor that is used in traditional cuisines and herbal remedies.
