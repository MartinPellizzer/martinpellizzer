Petunia variabilis

Petunia variabilis, commonly known as the variable petunia, is a species native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The flowers come in a wide range of colors, including white, pink, red, and violet, making it a popular ornamental plant. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Petunia variabilis is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various garden conditions.
