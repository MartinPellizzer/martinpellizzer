Pandanus odoratissimus var. sinensis

Pandanus odoratissimus var. sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese screw pine, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiral arrangement of leaves that form a dense, umbrella-like canopy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or green in color. Its roots are fibrous and often used in traditional medicine.

The plant is well adapted to coastal environments and can thrive in sandy or saline soils.
