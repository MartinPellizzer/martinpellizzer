Physurus metallescens

Physurus metallescens, commonly known as the metallic sunburst vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, glossy leaves that display a metallic sheen under sunlight. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom in clusters. Its rapid growth habit makes it suitable for trellises or as a ground cover in shaded areas.

Physurus metallescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid, warm climates.
