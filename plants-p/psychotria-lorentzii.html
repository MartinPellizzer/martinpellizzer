Psychotria lorentzii

Psychotria lorentzii, commonly known as Lorentz's psychotria, is a species of flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. It produces small, red berries that are often used in traditional medicine.
