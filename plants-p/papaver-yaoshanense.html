Papaver yaoshanense

Papaver yaoshanense, commonly known as the Yaoshan poppy, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Papaveraceae and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous regions, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, and the plant is known for its delicate, papery petals.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
