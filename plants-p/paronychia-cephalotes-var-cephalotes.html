Paronychia cephalotes var. cephalotes

Paronychia cephalotes var. cephalotes, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are showy, with tubular corollas that are typically yellow or orange in color.

It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.
