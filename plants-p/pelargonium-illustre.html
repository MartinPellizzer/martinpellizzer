Pelargonium illustre

Pelargonium illustre, commonly known as the scented geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves, which emit a fragrant scent when touched. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or red flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are often used in aromatherapy and as a natural insect repellent.

Pelargonium illustre thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and indoor cultivation.
