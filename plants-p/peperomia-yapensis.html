Peperomia yapensis

Peperomia yapensis, commonly known as the Yap peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of the western Pacific.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a velvety texture and small, clustered inflorescences that produce showy, red bracts. The plant is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are adapted to store water, making it drought-tolerant. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, requiring minimal watering.

Peperomia yapensis is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
