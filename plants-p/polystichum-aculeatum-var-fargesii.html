Polystichum aculeatum var. fargesii

Polystichum aculeatum var. fargesii, commonly known as the Farges' holly fern, is a hardy evergreen fern native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features tall, upright fronds with a distinctive holly-like appearance, characterized by serrated, spiny leaflets. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens. Its fertile fronds produce spores in late summer, contributing to its reproductive cycle.

This variety is often cultivated for its unique foliage and adaptability to cooler climates.
