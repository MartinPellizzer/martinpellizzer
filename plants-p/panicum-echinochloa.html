Panicum echinochloa

Panicum echinochloa, commonly known as rough bluestem, is a grass species native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Panicum echinochloa is often found in open areas such as prairies, fields, and disturbed sites.
