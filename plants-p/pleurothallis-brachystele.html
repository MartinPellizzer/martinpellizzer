Pleurothallis brachystele

Pleurothallis brachystele, commonly known as the short-stemmed pleurothallis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich substrates.

Due to its compact size and ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant.
