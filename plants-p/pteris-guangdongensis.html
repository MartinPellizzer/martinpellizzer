Pteris guangdongensis

Pteris guangdongensis, commonly known as the Guangdong water fern, is a species of fern native to southern China.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce sporangia on the undersides of the fronds.

Pteris guangdongensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
