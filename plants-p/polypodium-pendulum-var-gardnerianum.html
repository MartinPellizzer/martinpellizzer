Polypodium pendulum var. gardnerianum

Polypodium pendulum var. gardnerianum, commonly known as the hanging fern or garden fern, is a varietal form of the polypody fern.

It is characterized by its delicate, arching fronds that drape gracefully from its rhizomatous growth. The plant typically features glossy, lance-shaped leaves with a serrated margin. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for hanging baskets and shaded garden areas.
