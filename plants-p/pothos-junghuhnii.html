Pothos junghuhnii

Pothos junghuhnii, commonly known as the silver pothos, is a tropical climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves with a distinctive silver or variegated pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its ornamental foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and hanging baskets.
