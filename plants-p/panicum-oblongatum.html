Panicum oblongatum

Panicum oblongatum, commonly known as the narrow panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of a loose panicle made up of small, spikelet-like flowers.

Panicum oblongatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
