Portulaca catharinae

Portulaca catharinae, commonly known as the Cape Town moss rose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing annual herb that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features fleshy, succulent leaves and produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, yellow, and orange. It belongs to the family Portulacaceae and is well adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
