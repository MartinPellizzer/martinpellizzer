Phlomis dincii

Phlomis dincii, commonly known as Dinc's wormwood, is a perennial herb native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its gray-green, hairy leaves and tall, branched flower stalks. The plant produces yellow disk flowers surrounded by pale blue to violet ray florets. It thrives in rocky, arid environments and is adapted to full sun exposure.

Phlomis dincii is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
