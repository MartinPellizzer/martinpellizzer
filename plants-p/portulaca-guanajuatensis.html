Portulaca guanajuatensis

Portulaca guanajuatensis, commonly known as the Guanajuato moss rose, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the family Portulacaceae and is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowers, which range in color from pink to red, bloom in the morning and close by midday.

Portulaca guanajuatensis is adapted to withstand drought conditions due to its thick, water-storing leaves and shallow root system.
