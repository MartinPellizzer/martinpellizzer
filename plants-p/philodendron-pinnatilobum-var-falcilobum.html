Philodendron pinnatilobum var. falcilobum

Philodendron pinnatilobum var. falcilobum, commonly known as the falcilobum philodendron, is a climbing plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. The leaves are typically deeply lobed, giving the plant a distinctive, ornamental appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

This variety is popular among houseplant enthusiasts due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
