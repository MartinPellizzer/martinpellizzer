Pittosporum merrillianum

Pittosporum merrillianum, commonly known as the golden pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically orange or red in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and floral display.

Pittosporum merrillianum is also valued for its ability to provide habitat for native wildlife.
