Pedicularis Elwesii Var. Typica

Pedicularis elwesii var. typica is a striking member of the Orobanchaceae family known for its vivid red and yellow flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Asia, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments. It is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats where it competes with other hardy flora. Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a favorite among botanists and nature enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it remains a rare and elusive species, highlighting the diversity of plant life in high-altitude ecosystems.
