Pepinia alborubra

Pepinia alborubra, commonly known as the white-red peperomia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit. The plant features thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of white and red, giving it its common name. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or reddish in color.

Pepinia alborubra is valued as a houseplant for its ornamental foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
