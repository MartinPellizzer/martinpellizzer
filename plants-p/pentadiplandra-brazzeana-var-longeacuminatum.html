Pentadiplandra brazzeana var. longeacuminatum

Pentadiplandra brazzeana var. longeacuminatum, commonly known as the African star apple, is a tropical tree native to Central and West Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and broad, palmate leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by edible, star-shaped fruits that are typically consumed fresh or processed into beverages.
