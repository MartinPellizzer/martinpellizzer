Pennisetum macrochaetum

Pennisetum macrochaetum, commonly known as giant feather grass, is a tall, ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features slender, arching leaves and long, feathery plumes that give it a distinctive, elegant appearance. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping and drought-tolerant gardens. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to attract birds and pollinators.

Pennisetum macrochaetum is also valued for its low maintenance requirements and adaptability to various climates.
