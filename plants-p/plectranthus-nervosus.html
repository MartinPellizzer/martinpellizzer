Plectranthus nervosus

Plectranthus nervosus, commonly known as the narrow-veined plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves with prominent venation. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight.

Plectranthus nervosus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and medicinal properties.
