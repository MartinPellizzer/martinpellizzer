Polygala tunetana

Polygala tunetana, commonly known as the Tunisian milkweed, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Polygala tunetana is adapted to withstand drought conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
