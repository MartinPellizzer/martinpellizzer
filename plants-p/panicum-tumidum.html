Panicum tumidum

Panicum tumidum, commonly known as purple lovegrass, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and produces fluffy, purple-tinted seed heads that are visually striking. The plant thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Panicum tumidum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
