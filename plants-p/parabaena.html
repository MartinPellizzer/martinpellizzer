Parabaena

Parabaena, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking red flowers with a spider-like appearance. The plant has strap-shaped, evergreen leaves and produces clusters of bell-shaped blooms in the spring. Parabaena is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in well-drained soil with partial shade.

It is also noted for its ability to naturalize and spread through rhizomes.
