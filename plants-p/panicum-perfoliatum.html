Panicum perfoliatum

Panicum perfoliatum, commonly known as curly top grass, is a perennial grass species native to the United States.

It is characterized by its distinctive, curling leaves that form dense tufts, and its flowering spikes that are often reddish in color. The plant typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and is often found in prairie and woodland habitats. It has fibrous roots and produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.

Panicum perfoliatum is also known for its ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
