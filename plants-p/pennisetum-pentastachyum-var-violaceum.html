Pennisetum pentastachyum var. violaceum

Pennisetum pentastachyum var. violaceum, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It features slender, arching stems that bear dense, feathery inflorescences in shades of purple and pink. The plant produces small, wind-pollinated flowers arranged in tight, spike-like clusters. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking floral display and drought tolerance.
