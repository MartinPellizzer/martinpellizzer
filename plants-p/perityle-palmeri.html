Perityle palmeri

Perityle palmeri, commonly known as the desert dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by fluffy, white seed heads that aid in wind dispersal.
