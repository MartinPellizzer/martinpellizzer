Pultenaea hibbertioides var. prostrata

Pultenaea hibbertioides var. prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate hibbertia, is a low-growing shrub native to Australia.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and ornamental value in gardens.
