Polygala melilotoides var. major

Polygala melilotoides var. major, commonly known as the greater milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and the Middle East.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and arid environments. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and often hairy, adapted to reduce water loss.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits and is also used as a forage plant for livestock.
