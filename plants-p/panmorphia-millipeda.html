Panmorphia millipeda

Panmorphia millipeda, commonly known as the phantom millipede, is a unique and enigmatic species of arthropod.

It belongs to the order Millipedia, characterized by its numerous segmented legs and elongated body. This plant-like creature exhibits a remarkable ability to alter its appearance, mimicking various environmental features for camouflage. Its botanical-like structure includes a central stem-like body with appendages resembling leaves and roots.

Despite its name, it is not a true plant but a fascinating example of evolutionary adaptation in the natural world.
