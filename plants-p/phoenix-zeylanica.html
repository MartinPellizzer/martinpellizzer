Phoenix zeylanica

Phoenix zeylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon date palm, is a flowering plant native to Sri Lanka.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that develop into edible dates. It thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil.

The Ceylon date palm is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical significance in the region.
