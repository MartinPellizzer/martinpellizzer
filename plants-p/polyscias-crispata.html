Polyscias crispata

Polyscias crispata, commonly known as the crested or fringed holly.

It is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, including regions like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant features broad, leathery leaves with a distinctive wavy or fringed edge, giving it its common name. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by bright red berries.

Polyscias crispata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and hardy nature.
