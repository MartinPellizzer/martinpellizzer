Phyllostegia glabra

Phyllostegia glabra, commonly known as the smoothleaf honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are smooth and often have a slightly hairy underside. Its flowers are typically white or pale purple, arranged in clusters, and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Phyllostegia glabra is often found in moist, shaded woodlands and thrives in partial to full shade.
