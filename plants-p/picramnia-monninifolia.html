Picramnia monninifolia

Picramnia monninifolia, commonly known as the African tamarind tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by woody, pod-like fruits containing edible seeds.
