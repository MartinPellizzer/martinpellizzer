Premna wui

Premna wui, commonly known as the Chinese nettle or Chinese mulberry, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub or tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters. Its fruits are small, three-lobed berries that turn red when mature.

Premna wui is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
