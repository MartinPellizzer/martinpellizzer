Peperomia storkii

Peperomia storkii, commonly known as the Stork's Bill Peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are often heart-shaped or ovate in form. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its ability to thrive in low light conditions and minimal water requirements.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for tabletop displays and as a decorative houseplant.
