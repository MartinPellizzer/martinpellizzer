Pera eiteniorum

Pera eiteniorum, commonly known as the silverleaf pera, is a rare flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
