Pleurothallis denticulata

Pleurothallis denticulata, commonly known as the toothed pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. Its flowers are often clustered in inflorescences and bloom during the warmer months.

Due to its unique appearance and growth habits, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike.
