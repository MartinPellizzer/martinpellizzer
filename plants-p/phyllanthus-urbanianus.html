Phyllanthus urbanianus

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and small yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested or riverine areas.
