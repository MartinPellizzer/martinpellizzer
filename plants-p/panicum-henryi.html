Panicum henryi

Panicum henryi, commonly known as Henry's panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces fluffy, seed-bearing spikes that are often seen in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for dry, open habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and restoration projects.
