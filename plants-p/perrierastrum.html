Perrierastrum

Perrierastrum, commonly known as the desert candle, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It features a cylindrical stem that resembles a candle, often covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the rainy season. Its leaves are reduced to tubercles, which help minimize surface area for evaporation.

Perrierastrum is well adapted to survive in extreme drought conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
