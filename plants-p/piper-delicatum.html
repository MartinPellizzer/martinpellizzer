Piper delicatum

Piper delicatum, commonly known as the delicate pepper plant, is a tropical climbing vine belonging to the Piperaceae family.

It is native to the rainforests of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by tiny, peppery fruits that are often used in local cuisines. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly aromatic, peppery scent.

Piper delicatum is valued not only for its culinary uses but also for its ornamental appeal in gardens and greenhouses.
