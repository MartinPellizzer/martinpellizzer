Peperomia glabrirhachis

Peperomia glabrirhachis, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its thick, velvety leaves that are typically green with a slight sheen. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or red in color.

Peperomia glabrirhachis is often grown as a houseplant due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.
