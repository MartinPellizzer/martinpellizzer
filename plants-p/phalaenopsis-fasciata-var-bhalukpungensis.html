Phalaenopsis fasciata var. bhalukpungensis

Phalaenopsis fasciata var. bhalukpungensis, commonly known as the Bhalukpungensis Phalaenopsis, is a rare orchid species native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is characterized by its striking, long-lasting blooms that typically feature a combination of white, pink, and purple hues. The plant produces pendulous flowers that are highly prized in horticultural circles for their beauty and fragrance. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its elegance and unique coloration.
