Pennisetum sphacelatum var. tenuifolium

Pennisetum sphacelatum var. tenuifolium, commonly known as narrowleaf fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces feathery, silver-gray plumes during the summer months, which add ornamental value. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and minimal maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.
