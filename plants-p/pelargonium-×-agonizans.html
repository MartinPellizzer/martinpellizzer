Pelargonium × agonizans

Pelargonium × agonizans, commonly known as the hybrid geranium, is a flowering plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Pelargonium hortorum and Pelargonium × inodorum. The plant is characterized by its showy, five-petaled flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. Its foliage is typically dark green and velvety, adding to its ornamental appeal.

It is often cultivated as a bedding plant or in containers due to its adaptability and ease of care.
