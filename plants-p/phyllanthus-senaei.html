Phyllanthus senaei

Phyllanthus senaei, commonly known as the African spurge, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its simple, alternate leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by small, spherical fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and disturbed habitats.

Phyllanthus senaei is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
