Piper adornatispicum

Piper adornatispicum, commonly known as the ornate pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its distinctive, ornate inflorescences and small, aromatic berries. Its leaves are typically alternate and have a rough texture with a strong, spicy scent.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its unique appearance and potential medicinal properties.
