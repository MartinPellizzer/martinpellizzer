Persicaria linicola

Persicaria linicola, commonly known as the slender smartweed, is an annual herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist to wet soils, often found in marshes, swales, and along stream banks. The plant features alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are usually hairless or softly hairy. Its small, pinkish to reddish flowers are arranged in dense spikes, and it produces small, three-lobed seeds.

Persicaria linicola is often considered a weed in agricultural settings due to its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed soils.
