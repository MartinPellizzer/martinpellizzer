Pedilonum sulphureum

Pedilonum sulphureum, commonly known as the golden foot plant, is a unique species within the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a distinctive, hollow stem that resembles a foot, giving it its common name. Its leaves are finely divided and have a fern-like appearance.
