Pleurospermum calcareum

Pleurospermum calcareum, commonly known as the limestone pleurospermum, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, gray-green foliage and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions. Its unique morphology makes it a distinctive species within the Asteraceae family.

It is often found growing on limestone outcrops, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
