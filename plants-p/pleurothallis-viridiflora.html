Pleurothallis viridiflora

Pleurothallis viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, giving it its common name. Its leaves are typically ovate and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
