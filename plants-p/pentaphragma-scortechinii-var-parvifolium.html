Pentaphragma scortechinii var. parvifolium

Pentaphragma scortechinii var. parvifolium, commonly known as the small-leaved pentaphragma, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open areas, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and relatively small, giving it its common name.
