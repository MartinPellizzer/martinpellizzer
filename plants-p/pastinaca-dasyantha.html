Pastinaca dasyantha

Pastinaca dasyantha, commonly known as the woolly bur marigold, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas. It has a taproot system and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

The species is also known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
