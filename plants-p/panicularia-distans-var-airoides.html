Panicularia distans var. airoides

Panicularia distans var. airoides, commonly known as the fragrant panicled bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its showy, panicle-like inflorescences that bear small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white and purple. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its aromatic leaves and flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

It is also valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
