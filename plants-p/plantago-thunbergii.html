Plantago thunbergii

Plantago thunbergii, commonly known as Japanese plantain, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features a rosette of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces erect flowering shoots with small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or grassy areas.

Its adaptability makes it a common sight in both natural and cultivated landscapes.
