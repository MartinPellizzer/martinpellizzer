Peperomia palpebrata var. absalonis

Peperomia palpebrata var. absalonis, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are dark green with a velvety texture, often with a slight reddish tint on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it an excellent choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its slow growth and attractive foliage, it is popular among collectors and indoor gardeners.
