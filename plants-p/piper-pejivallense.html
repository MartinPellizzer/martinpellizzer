Piper pejivallense

Piper pejivallense, commonly known as the Pejivallense pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with long, slender stems that twine around supports. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a glossy surface, while the flowers are small and greenish-white in color.

The fruit is a small, three-lobed berry that contains several seeds.
