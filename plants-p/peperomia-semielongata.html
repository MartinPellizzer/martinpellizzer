Peperomia semielongata

Peperomia semielongata, commonly known as the Variegated Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with a distinctive variegation of white and green, adding visual interest to any indoor space. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, followed by capsule fruits containing seeds. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a low-maintenance choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular ornamental plant for both novice and experienced growers.
