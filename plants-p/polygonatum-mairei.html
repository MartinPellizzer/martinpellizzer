Polygonatum mairei

Polygonatum mairei, commonly known as the Maire's Solomon's seal, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers. The plant produces oval, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.
