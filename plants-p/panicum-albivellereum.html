Panicum albivellereum

Panicum albivellereum, commonly known as silver hairgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its fine, silvery-white hairs that cover the leaves and stems, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its inflorescence consists of a dense, cylindrical spikelet that matures into a fluffy, white to grayish mass.

Panicum albivellereum is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and drought tolerance.
