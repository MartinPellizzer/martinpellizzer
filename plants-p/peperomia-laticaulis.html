Peperomia laticaulis

Peperomia laticaulis, commonly known as the broad-stemmed peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically oval to lance-shaped, with a waxy surface that helps it retain moisture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, usually greenish-white in color, which are followed by round, fleshy berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia laticaulis is relatively low-maintenance, requiring minimal watering and moderate humidity to flourish.
