Pleurothallis intricata

Pleurothallis intricata, commonly known as the intricate pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink and purple.

Pleurothallis intricata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique beauty.
