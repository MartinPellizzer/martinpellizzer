Pilea humbertii

Pilea humbertii, commonly known as the African violet, is a species of flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are often velvety and dark green. The plant produces small, violet-colored flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Pilea humbertii is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
