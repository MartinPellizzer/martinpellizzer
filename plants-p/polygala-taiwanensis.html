Polygala taiwanensis

Polygala taiwanensis, commonly known as the Taiwan milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, often pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves.

Polygala taiwanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
