Polygala pseudohebeclada

Polygala pseudohebeclada, commonly known as the narrow-leaved milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, often greenish or yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and it produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas.
