Premna szemaoensis

Premna szemaoensis, commonly known as the Yunnan premna, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to China, particularly found in the Yunnan province, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly serrated margin. Its small, white flowers are clustered in inflorescences and bloom during the spring and early summer.

Premna szemaoensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
