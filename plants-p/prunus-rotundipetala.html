Prunus rotundipetala

Prunus rotundipetala, commonly known as the round-petaled cherry, is a species of flowering shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its delicate, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has oval, serrated leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Prunus rotundipetala is also valued for its edible fruits, which are small and tart.
