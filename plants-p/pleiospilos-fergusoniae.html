Pleiospilos fergusoniae

Pleiospilos fergusoniae, commonly known as the Fergusonskop bulb, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to survive in arid environments. The plant produces showy, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It has a unique ability to remain dormant for long periods, only emerging to bloom after rainfall.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
