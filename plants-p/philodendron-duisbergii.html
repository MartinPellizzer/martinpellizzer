Philodendron duisbergii

Philodendron duisbergii, commonly known as the Duisbergii Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly variegated pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by bright red berries, which are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

Due to its attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and hanging baskets.
