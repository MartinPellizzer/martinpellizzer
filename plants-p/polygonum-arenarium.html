Polygonum arenarium

Polygonum arenarium, commonly known as sand polygonum, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain water in dry environments. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in sandy or rocky habitats.

Polygonum arenarium is adapted to withstand high temperatures and low moisture availability.
