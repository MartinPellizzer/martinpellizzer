Petastoma broadwayi

Petastoma broadwayi, commonly known as the Broadway lily, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or white in color. The plant features strap-like leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and its role in local ecosystems.
