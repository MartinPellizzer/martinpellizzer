Plectranthus pantadenius

Plectranthus pantadenius, commonly known as the Cape rosemary, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features aromatic, lance-shaped leaves that are often gray-green in color. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue or purple, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its medicinal properties have also been noted in traditional uses.
