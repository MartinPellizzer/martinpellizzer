Pelargonium × lateripes var. walneri

Pelargonium × lateripes var. walneri, commonly known as the Zebra Geranium, is a hybrid ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, mottled leaves with a zebra-like pattern of light and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is often used in gardens for its attractive foliage and is relatively easy to care for.
