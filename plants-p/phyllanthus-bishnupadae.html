Phyllanthus bishnupadae

Phyllanthus bishnupadae, commonly known as Bishnupada Phyllanthus, is a small shrub native to the Indian subcontinent.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers that bloom in yellow or greenish-yellow hues. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found growing in moist, shaded areas such as forests and along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and traditional medicine.
