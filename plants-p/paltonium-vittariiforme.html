Paltonium vittariiforme

Paltonium vittariiforme, commonly known as the narrow-leaf paltonium, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Araceae.

It is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces clusters of small, white spadices surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is a common feature in the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in shaded, moist areas of the rainforest.

Paltonium vittariiforme is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
