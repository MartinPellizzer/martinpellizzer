Pleurothallis producta

Pleurothallis producta, commonly known as the hairy pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and the presence of hairy structures on its leaves and stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
