Polygala callispora

Polygala callispora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the Polygalaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in rocky and sandy soils. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, arranged in clusters. It is a perennial herb that often grows in open habitats such as fynbos vegetation.

Its botanical features include narrow, linear leaves and a distinctive flowering structure that resembles a spurred flower.
