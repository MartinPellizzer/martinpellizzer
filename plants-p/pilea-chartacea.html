Pilea chartacea

Pilea chartacea, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its round, shield-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.

Pilea chartacea thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.
