Pleurothallis mirabilis

Pleurothallis mirabilis, commonly known as the miraculous pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually small, with a variety of colors, and it blooms during the warmer months.

Pleurothallis mirabilis is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
