Pediocactus nigrispinus subsp. beastonii

Pediocactus nigrispinus subsp.

beastonii, commonly known as the black-spined hedgehog cactus, is a succulent native to Mexico. It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in spiny, dark green to black spines.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
