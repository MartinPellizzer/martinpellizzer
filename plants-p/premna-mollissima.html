Premna mollissima

Premna mollissima, commonly known as silky mallow, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, pale green to white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Premna mollissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
