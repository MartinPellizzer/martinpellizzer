Phylica keetii var. mollis

Phylica keetii var. mollis, commonly known as the soft-haired phylica, is a South African shrub belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its soft, woolly foliage and small, delicate white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. It is a hardy, drought-tolerant species that prefers full sun exposure.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in suitable climates.
