Pittosporum dasycaulon

Pittosporum dasycaulon, commonly known as the New Zealand mountain pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a leathery texture and produces fragrant, white flowers in clusters. The plant has a woody stem with a rough, bark-like surface and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in horticulture.

The species is also important in the ecosystem, providing habitat and food for native wildlife.
