Parinari papuana subsp. papuana

Parinari papuana subsp.

papuana, commonly known as the Papuan parinari, is a tropical tree species native to the lowland forests of New Guinea. It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and simple, alternate leaves.

Its ecological role includes providing food for various wildlife species and contributing to forest regeneration.
