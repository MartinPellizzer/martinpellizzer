Philibertia viminalis

Philibertia viminalis, commonly known as the silverleaf tansy, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It is a perennial herb with a bushy growth habit, featuring silver-gray foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open woodlands.

Philibertia viminalis is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
