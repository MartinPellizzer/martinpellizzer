Persicaria mitis var. hirticaulis

Persicaria mitis var. hirticaulis, commonly known as the softleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and alternate leaves that are lance-shaped with a rough texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range from pink to red, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in moist to wet soils, often found in wetlands, stream banks, and marshy areas.

This variant is noted for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
