Potentilla alpestris var. firma

Potentilla alpestris var. firma, commonly known as the alpine potentilla, is a hardy, low-growing shrub native to alpine regions of Europe.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has compact, upright growth habit with hairy stems and leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine displays.

Its botanical resilience and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardeners in cooler climates.
