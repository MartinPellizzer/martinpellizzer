Pleurothallis barbacenensis

Pleurothallis barbacenensis, commonly known as the Barbacenensis orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other plants without deriving nutrients from them. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, organic-rich substrates for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is a sought-after specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
