Peperomia ainana

Peperomia ainana, commonly known as the Shiny Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which bloom during the warmer months. Peperomia ainana thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
