Polystachya goetzeana

Polystachya goetzeana, commonly known as the Goetze's spikegrass, is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Kenya and Tanzania. The plant features a slender rhizome and produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers on elongated spikes. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often growing in dense tufts.

Polystachya goetzeana is adapted to grow in shaded, moist environments, typically in montane forests.
