Psittacanthus acevedoi

Psittacanthus acevedoi, commonly known as the Acevedo's bird's nest fern, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, fan-shaped fronds that resemble a bird's nest. The leaves are covered in a dense layer of fine, scale-like structures that help reduce water loss in its natural habitat.
