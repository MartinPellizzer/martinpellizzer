Primulina carnosifolia

Primulina carnosifolia, commonly known as the meaty-leaved primula, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle, which helps it retain moisture in its natural habitat. The plant produces clusters of bright pink or red flowers that bloom throughout the year, adding vibrant color to its surroundings. Its unique foliage and showy blooms make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.

Primulina carnosifolia thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity to flourish.
