Piper ellipticibaccum

Piper ellipticibaccum, commonly known as the elliptic-leaf pepper, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many pepper species. The plant is characterized by its elliptical-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers. Its fruits are peppery and used in local cuisines.

The botanical features of Piper ellipticibaccum make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ethnobotanical studies.
