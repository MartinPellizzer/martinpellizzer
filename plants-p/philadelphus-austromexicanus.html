Philadelphus austromexicanus

Philadelphus austromexicanus, commonly known as the Mexican Mockorange, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of central Mexico.

It features fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes in suitable climates.
