Pleurothallis rufescens

Pleurothallis rufescens, commonly known as the red-brown pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant typically features small, clustered pseudobulbs with narrow, leathery leaves. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to reddish in color, with a distinctive rufescens hue, and are borne on short, lateral inflorescences.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.
