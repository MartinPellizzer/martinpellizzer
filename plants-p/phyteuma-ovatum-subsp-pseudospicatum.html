Phyteuma ovatum subsp. pseudospicatum

Phyteuma ovatum subsp.

pseudospicatum, commonly known as the narrowleaf phyteuma, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia. It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of bell-shaped flowers.

Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
