Pleopeltis raapii

Pleopeltis raapii, commonly known as the Raapi's rock fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, dense fronds that often form a cushion-like growth. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually leathery and have a distinct, intricate venation pattern.
