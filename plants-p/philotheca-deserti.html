Philotheca deserti

Philotheca deserti, commonly known as the desert philotheca, is a low-growing shrub native to arid regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the cooler months, typically from spring to early summer. It is well adapted to harsh, dry environments and can survive in poor soil conditions.

Philotheca deserti is an important component of the flora in its native habitat, contributing to local biodiversity.
