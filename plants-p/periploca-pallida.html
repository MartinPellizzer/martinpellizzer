Periploca pallida

Periploca pallida, commonly known as the white periploca, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes milkweeds and related plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, white or pale yellow flowers. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.
