Pachira robynsii

Pachira robynsii, commonly known as the golden pachira, is a species of flowering plant in the Araceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green heart-shaped leaves and produces striking yellow flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

The plant's adaptability and aesthetic appeal make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
