Portulaca carrissoana

Portulaca carrissoana, commonly known as the Mexican portulaca, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is an annual or biennial herb that typically grows in dry, sunny environments. The plant features fleshy, succulent leaves and produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers. It belongs to the family Portulacaceae and is characterized by its ability to thrive in arid conditions.

Portulaca carrissoana is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
