Phaius borneensis

Phaius borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean phaius, is a tropical orchid native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It is characterized by its broad, strap-like leaves and produces large, showy inflorescences with multiple fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or terrestrial species, often found in shaded, humid environments. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow with a distinctive purple or brown lip.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
