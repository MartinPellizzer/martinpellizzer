Perilla ocymoides f. viridicrispa

Perilla ocymoides f. viridicrispa, commonly known as curled perilla, is a variant of the perilla plant characterized by its distinctive curled leaves.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family and is native to East Asia, particularly China. The plant is known for its aromatic leaves, which are used in traditional cuisine and herbal medicine. Its foliage exhibits a unique crinkled texture, making it visually striking and suitable for ornamental purposes.

The leaves are typically a vibrant green, with some cultivars displaying variegated patterns.
