Phlomis moluccana

Phlomis moluccana, commonly known as Moluccan giant mullein, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in dense, woolly hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats.
