Pennisetum pedicellatum var. pubirhachis

Pennisetum pedicellatum var. pubirhachis, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender clumps with feathery, purple-tinted plumes that bloom in late summer. The leaves are narrow, gray-green, and often have a fuzzy texture, contributing to its unique appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

This variety is also valued for its ability to attract birds and butterflies, adding ecological benefits to its aesthetic appeal.
