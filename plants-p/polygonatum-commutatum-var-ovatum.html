Polygonatum commutatum var. ovatum

Polygonatum commutatum var. ovatum, commonly known as the Chinese Solomon's seal, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features rhizomatous growth and lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers in late spring. Its berries are red or black and contain several seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and traditional medicinal uses.
