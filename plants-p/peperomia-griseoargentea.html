Peperomia griseoargentea

Peperomia griseoargentea, commonly known as the silver-leaf peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, silver-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia griseoargentea is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal.
