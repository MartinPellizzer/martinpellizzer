Phalaenopsis schilleriana var. grandiflora

Phalaenopsis schilleriana var. grandiflora, commonly known as the Schiller's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, gracefully arching inflorescence that bears numerous large, showy flowers. The blooms are typically white or pale pink with a subtle fragrance, and they can last for several months. The plant has broad, dark green leaves and is well-suited for indoor cultivation.

It requires bright, indirect light and moderate humidity to thrive.
