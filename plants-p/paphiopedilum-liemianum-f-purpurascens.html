Paphiopedilum liemianum f. purpurascens

Paphiopedilum liemianum f. purpurascens, commonly known as the Purple-flowered Liemian Paphiopedilum, is a rare and striking orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spurred lip that is a key feature of the genus. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial or epiphytic orchid, with dark green, leathery leaves and a compact growth habit. Its flowers are notable for their vivid purple and pink hues, often with intricate patterns that enhance its ornamental value.

This cultivar is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts due to its beauty and rarity in cultivation.
