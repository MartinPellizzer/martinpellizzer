Polystachya polychaete

Polystachya polychaete, commonly known as the hairy polystachya, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clustered inflorescences that bear numerous small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and undergrowth. Its leaves are usually elongated and covered with fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Polystachya polychaete is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
