Phalaenopsis sumatrana subvar. paucivittata

Phalaenopsis sumatrana subvar. paucivittata, commonly known as the Sumatran moth orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, long-lasting flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has thick, fleshy roots adapted to absorb moisture from its humid, shaded habitat. Its leaves are broad and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms.

This orchid thrives in well-draining potting media and requires moderate light and consistent humidity for optimal growth.
