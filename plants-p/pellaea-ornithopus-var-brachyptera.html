Pellaea ornithopus var. brachyptera

Pellaea ornithopus var. brachyptera, commonly known as the short-leaf rock fern, is a terrestrial fern species native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Pellaeaceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The fronds are typically short and broad, with a leathery texture and a distinctive dark green color. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded, moist environments.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens.
