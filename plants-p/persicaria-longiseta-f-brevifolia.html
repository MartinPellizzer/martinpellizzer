Persicaria longiseta f. brevifolia

Persicaria longiseta f. brevifolia, commonly known as the short-leaved smartweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its succulent stems and bracteate inflorescences. The plant features small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters, and its leaves are typically shorter and more compact compared to the typical form. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

This fern-like perennial is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
