Pandanus krauelianus

Pandanus krauelianus, commonly known as the Krauel's pandanus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or green in color. Its fruit, known as a pandan fruit, is edible and has a mild, sweet aroma.

Pandanus krauelianus thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found near coastal areas or in dense forests.
