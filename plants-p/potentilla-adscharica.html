Potentilla adscharica

Potentilla adscharica, commonly known as the Caucasian cinquefoil, is a perennial shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with five leaflets, giving it the common name "cinquefoil.". It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.

Potentilla adscharica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in some traditional medicinal practices.
