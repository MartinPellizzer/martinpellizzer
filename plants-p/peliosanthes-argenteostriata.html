Peliosanthes argenteostriata

Peliosanthes argenteostriata, commonly known as the silver-striped cycad, is a unique member of the Cycadaceae family.

It is native to southern China and is characterized by its distinctive, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy, silver-white coating. The plant produces large, cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs. Its slow growth rate and long lifespan make it a rare and valuable specimen in botanical collections.

Due to its limited distribution and conservation status, it is considered an endangered species.
