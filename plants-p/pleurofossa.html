Pleurofossa

Pleurofossa, commonly known as the pleurofossa orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, often colorful flowers that bloom in clusters. Pleurofossa orchids typically grow as epiphytes, attaching themselves to trees in their natural habitat.

They require bright, indirect light and consistent humidity to thrive.
