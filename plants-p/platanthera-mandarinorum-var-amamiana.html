Platanthera mandarinorum var. amamiana

It is a terrestrial herb with a slender, leafless stem that bears a single, showy, white to pale yellow flower. The plant thrives in shaded, moist habitats such as forests and rocky areas, where it relies on mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient uptake.
