Potentilla × danubialis

Potentilla × danubialis, commonly known as the Danube cinquefoil, is a hybrid shrub native to Europe.

It typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and features compound leaves with five to seven leaflets. The plant produces showy yellow flowers in late spring to early summer, arranged in dense, rounded clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for garden cultivation.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
