Pteris omeiensis

Pteris omeiensis, commonly known as the Omei water fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It is characterized by its delicate, pinnate fronds that are typically light green with a feathery appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Pteris omeiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
