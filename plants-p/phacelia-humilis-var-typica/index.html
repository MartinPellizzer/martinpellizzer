Phacelia Humilis Var. Typica

Phacelia humilis var. typica is a low-growing annual plant native to the western united states. it features delicate blue to violet flowers that bloom in early spring. this species is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. its small size and attractive blossoms make it a popular choice for native plant gardens. due to its ecological importance, it plays a key role in supporting local pollinators.
