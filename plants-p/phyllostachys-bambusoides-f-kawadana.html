Phyllostachys bambusoides f. kawadana

Phyllostachys bambusoides f. kawadana, commonly known as the Kawadana bamboo, is a clump-forming ornamental bamboo species native to China.

It features slender, gracefully arching culms that are often tinged with a reddish hue, especially in younger shoots. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, providing a fine texture to the plant's overall appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

This variety is prized for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability in both indoor and outdoor settings.
