Plumbago scandens var. normalis

Plumbago scandens var. normalis, commonly known as blue plumbago, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a vigorous, climbing shrub that produces clusters of bright blue or purple flowers. The plant has opposite, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and trellises.

Its ornamental value comes from its striking flowers and long blooming season.
