Physoceras boryanum

Physoceras boryanum, commonly known as the Boryanum bladderpod, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant is characterized by its low-growing habit, with hairy stems and small, yellow flowers. Its fruit is a spherical, inflated pod that splits open to release seeds.

The species is often found in dry, disturbed soils and is adapted to arid environments.
