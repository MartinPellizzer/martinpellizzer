Peperomia sarcostigma

Peperomia sarcostigma, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a perennial flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It produces small, inconspicuous white or pale green flowers, typically arranged in clusters.

Peperomia sarcostigma is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in indirect light.
