Peperomia chichulana

Peperomia chichulana, commonly known as the Chichulana peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Central America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges, giving it a striking visual appeal. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters, typically during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a suitable choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique foliage and ease of care, it is a popular ornamental plant among succulent enthusiasts.
