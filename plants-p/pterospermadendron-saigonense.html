Pterospermadendron saigonense

Pterospermadendron saigonense, commonly known as the Saigon flame tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its striking orange-red floral displays. The plant produces pods that contain seeds with tufts of hair, aiding in wind dispersal. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
