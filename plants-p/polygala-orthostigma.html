Polygala orthostigma

Polygala orthostigma, commonly known as the upright milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often striking floral structures. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense spikes, often with a distinctive spurred structure.

The leaves are alternate and pinnately compound, contributing to its ornamental appeal in gardens.
