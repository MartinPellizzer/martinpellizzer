Pereskiopsis opuntiiflora

Pereskiopsis opuntiiflora, commonly known as the coral tree or fire tree, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bright red flowers that resemble coral, which bloom in summer. The plant has a shrub-like growth habit with woody stems and small, alternate leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature contribute to its appeal in both natural and cultivated landscapes.
