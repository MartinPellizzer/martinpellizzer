Philodendron urraoense

Philodendron urraoense, commonly known as the Urrao philodendron, is a tropical vine native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by berries that turn from green to red as they mature. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a houseplant.
