Polygala pretzii

Polygala pretzii, commonly known as the pretzii milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, often pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and has small, lance-shaped leaves. Its unique floral structure includes a long, slender spur that extends from the flower's base.

Polygala pretzii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
