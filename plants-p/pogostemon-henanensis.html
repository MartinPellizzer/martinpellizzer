Pogostemon henanensis

Pogostemon henanensis, commonly known as the Henan pegmatite plant, is a unique species native to China.

It is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves, which are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
