Pseudosasa spiculosa f. angustior

Pseudosasa spiculosa f. angustior, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bamboo, is a compact, clump-forming plant native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that are often used in ornamental gardening for their elegant appearance. The leaves are lanceolate, with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside, giving it a striking visual contrast. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

This cultivar is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of garden conditions.
