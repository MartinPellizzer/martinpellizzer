Pleurothallis sagittilabia

Pleurothallis sagittilabia, commonly known as the arrowhead orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its inflorescence is often curved, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Pleurothallis sagittilabia is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
