Pilea triradiata

Pilea triradiata, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are typically light green with a slightly fuzzy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Pilea triradiata is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
