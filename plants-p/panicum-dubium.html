Panicum dubium

Panicum dubium, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a warm-season grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues. The plant produces loose, panicle-like inflorescences that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for use in ornamental landscapes and erosion control.

Panicum dubium is also valued for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
