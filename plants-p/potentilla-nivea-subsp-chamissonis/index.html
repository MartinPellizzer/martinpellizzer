Potentilla Nivea Subsp. Chamissonis

Potentilla nivea subsp. chamissonis is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the coastal regions of western North America. It thrives in sandy soils and is often found in rocky habitats along the Pacific coast. Known for its pale yellow flowers and low-growing habit, this subspecies is a member of the Rosaceae family. Its unique adaptations allow it to survive in harsh, wind-swept environments. Despite its beauty, it remains a poorly studied species with limited conservation status.
