Peperomia pololuana

Peperomia pololuana, commonly known as the Pololuana peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, usually in shades of pink or white, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia pololuana is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
