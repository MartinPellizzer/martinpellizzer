Pilosella nigrolegionensis

Pilosella nigrolegionensis, commonly known as the black legion bellflower, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in South America.

The plant features finely hairy stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive, rugged appearance. Its flowers are typically small and white or pale yellow, blooming in clusters during the spring and summer months.

Pilosella nigrolegionensis is often found in rocky or grassy habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.
