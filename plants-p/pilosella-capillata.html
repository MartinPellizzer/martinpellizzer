Pilosella capillata

Pilosella capillata, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered with fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Pilosella capillata is often found in grassy areas and along the edges of woodlands.
