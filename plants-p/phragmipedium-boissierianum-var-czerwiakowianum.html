Phragmipedium boissierianum var. czerwiakowianum

Phragmipedium boissierianum var. czerwiakowianum, commonly known as the Czerwiakowianum Cattleya, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically feature a yellow lip and a greenish-yellow dorsal sepal. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in its natural habitat. It prefers warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its flowers bloom in late summer or early autumn, adding ornamental value to both greenhouse and outdoor collections.
