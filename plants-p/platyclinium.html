Platyclinium

Platyclinium, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its seeds are encased in a hairy, spiny capsule that aids in seed dispersal.

Platyclinium is often found in wetlands and along riverbanks, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils.
