Persica vulgaris albida

Persica vulgaris albida, commonly known as the white peach.

This plant is a variety of peach tree characterized by its white or pale yellow fruit. It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is native to temperate regions of Asia. The flowers are typically pink or white, and the leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate.

The fruit is usually small to medium in size, with a smooth, thin skin and a sweet, aromatic flesh.
