Pertya glabrescens var. impressa

Pertya glabrescens var. impressa, commonly known as the smooth pertya, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed stems that store water, adapting to its arid environment. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically reduced to small, scale-like structures, which help minimize water loss.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and resilience in dry conditions.
