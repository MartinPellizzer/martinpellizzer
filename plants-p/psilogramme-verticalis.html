Psilogramme verticalis

Psilogramme verticalis, commonly known as the upright psilogramme, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or disturbed areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and sometimes endangered species.
