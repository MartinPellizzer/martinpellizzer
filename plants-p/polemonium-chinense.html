Polemonium chinense

Polemonium chinense, commonly known as Chinese garden bell, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces striking blue to purple bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for woodland gardens and shaded landscapes.
