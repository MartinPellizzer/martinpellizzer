Passiflora itzensis

Passiflora itzensis, commonly known as the Itzens' passionflower, is a climbing vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, intricate flowers that display a mix of blue, purple, and white hues. The plant produces edible, orange-red berries that are rich in nutrients. Its leaves are heart-shaped with a rough texture and a strong, distinctive scent.

Passiflora itzensis is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its potential medicinal properties.
