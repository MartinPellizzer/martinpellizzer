Peperomia esmeraldana

Peperomia esmeraldana, commonly known as the Emerald Necklace Peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a striking silver-blue vein pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, adding subtle interest. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia esmeraldana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
