Pleurothallis brevis

Pleurothallis brevis, commonly known as the short-stemmed orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically produces pale green to white blooms with a slight pinkish hue, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
