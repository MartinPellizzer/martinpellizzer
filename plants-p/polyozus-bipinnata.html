Polyozus bipinnata

Polyozus bipinnata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved polyozus, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that are deeply divided into two pairs of pinnae. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically during the rainy season. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some horticultural settings.
