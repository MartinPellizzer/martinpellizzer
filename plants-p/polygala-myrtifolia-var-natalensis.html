Polygala myrtifolia var. natalensis

Polygala myrtifolia var. natalensis, commonly known as Natal polygala, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with slender, branching stems and small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant produces small, bead-like fruits that contain the seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Natal polygala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
