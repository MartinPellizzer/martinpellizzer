Paphiopedilum × huangrongshuanum

Paphiopedilum × huangrongshuanum, commonly known as the Huangrongshuanum Phalaenopsis, is a hybrid orchid that combines traits from various Paphiopedilum species.

It is characterized by its intricate, slipper-shaped petals and a unique, often dark-colored lip that distinguishes it from other orchid genera. The plant typically produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, with a velvety texture on its petals. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

This hybrid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for both indoor and garden settings.
