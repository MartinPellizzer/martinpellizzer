Plantago loureiroi

Plantago loureiroi, commonly known as the Cape Verde plantain, is a flowering plant native to the Cape Verde Islands.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are thick and fleshy. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in a spike, which are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in arid and rocky environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological significance in its native habitat.
