Philodendron sanmarcoense

Philodendron sanmarcoense, commonly known as the San Marco Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive waxy texture and a deep green coloration. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by bright red berries. It thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded outdoor gardens.
