Polystachya ligulifolia

Polystachya ligulifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf torch orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and dense clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within tropical rainforests. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Polystachya ligulifolia is valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
