Piper barretoi

Piper barretoi, commonly known as the Barreto's pepper vine, is a climbing plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically followed by bright red berries. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a rough texture, while its vines can grow quite long, making it suitable for trellises or natural habitats.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance.
