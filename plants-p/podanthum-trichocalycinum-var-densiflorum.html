Podanthum trichocalycinum var. densiflorum

Podanthum trichocalycinum var. densiflorum, commonly known as the dense-flowered podanthum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, clustered inflorescences. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and its flowers are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
