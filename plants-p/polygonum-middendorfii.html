Polygonum middendorfii

Polygonum middendorfii, commonly known as the middlefork smartweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its distinctive bracteate inflorescences and triangular stems. The plant typically grows in wet or moist habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along streambanks. Its small, pink to red flowers appear in dense clusters, and it produces small, three-angled fruits.

Polygonum middendorfii is often found in the northern United States and southern Canada.
