Panicum speciosum var. martianum

Panicum speciosum var. martianum, commonly known as Martian grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to South Africa.

It features tall, slender stems with feathery, silver-blue foliage that adds a dramatic visual effect to gardens. The plant produces fluffy, pale purple seed heads that persist through the winter, enhancing its ornamental value. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice among gardeners seeking texture and color in their landscapes.
