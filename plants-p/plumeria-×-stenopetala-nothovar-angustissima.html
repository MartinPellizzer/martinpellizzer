Plumeria × stenopetala nothovar. angustissima

Plumeria × stenopetala nothovar. angustissima, commonly known as the narrow-leaved frangipani, is a hybrid plant that exhibits unique botanical features.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is native to tropical regions and thrives in warm, sunny climates with well-draining soil. Its flowering season typically occurs during the dry season, producing clusters of blooms that attract pollinators.

This cultivar is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
