Pygeum brevifolium

Pygeum brevifolium, commonly known as the short-leaved African privet, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and landscapes in regions with a subtropical climate.
