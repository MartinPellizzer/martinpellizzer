Polystichum brachypterum

Polystichum brachypterum, commonly known as the short-winged wood fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery fronds that are densely packed and often form a compact, upright clump. The fronds are typically bisected and have a distinctive, narrow shape with short, stubby pinnae. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky crevices.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural settings for its elegant, evergreen foliage.
