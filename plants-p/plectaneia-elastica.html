Plectaneia elastica

Plectaneia elastica, commonly known as the elastic plectane, is a rare and lesser-known plant species characterized by its unique elastic properties in its stems.

This plant is native to specific regions in South America, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are elongated and have a waxy surface, which helps reduce water loss in its natural habitat. The flowers are small and clustered, typically appearing in shades of white and green.
