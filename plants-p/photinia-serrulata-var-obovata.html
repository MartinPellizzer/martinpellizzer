Photinia serrulata var. obovata

Photinia serrulata var. obovata, commonly known as the glossy leaf photinia, is a shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green, glossy leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white flowers in spring, followed by red, fleshy berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is commonly used in ornamental landscaping.

The varietal form obovata is distinguished by its broader, more rounded leaves compared to the typical variety.
