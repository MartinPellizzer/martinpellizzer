Potentilla × habievii

Potentilla × habievii, commonly known as the Habievii cinquefoil, is a hybrid shrub native to Central Asia.

It features small, yellow, five-petaled flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has a compact growth habit with dark green, lobed leaves that are densely arranged along the stems. It is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, thriving in full sun and well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and landscapes in suitable climates.
