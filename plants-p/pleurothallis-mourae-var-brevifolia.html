Pleurothallis mourae var. brevifolia

Pleurothallis mourae var. brevifolia, commonly known as the short-leaved pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and relatively small, lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically pale green to white, with a subtle fragrance, and are borne on short inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with high air circulation.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
