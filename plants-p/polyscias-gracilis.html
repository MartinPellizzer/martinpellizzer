Polyscias gracilis

Polyscias gracilis, commonly known as the slender sycamore, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy on the upper surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by spherical, fleshy fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Polyscias gracilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
