Peperomia palcana

Peperomia palcana, commonly known as the Shiny Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, glossy, ovate leaves that are typically bright green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia palcana is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
