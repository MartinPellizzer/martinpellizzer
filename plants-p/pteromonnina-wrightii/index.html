Pteromonnina Wrightii

Pteromonnina wrightii is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the southwestern United States. It thrives in arid and rocky environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant is known for its unique, star-shaped blooms that appear in late spring. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is now listed as endangered. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and preserve this beautiful and fragile species.
