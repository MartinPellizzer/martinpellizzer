Phinaea pulchella var. pulchella

Phinaea pulchella var. pulchella, commonly known as the beautiful phinaea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It features showy, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. The plant has slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
