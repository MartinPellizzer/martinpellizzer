Pyrostria louvelii

Pyrostria louvelii, commonly known as the Madagascar bamboo, is a unique grass-like plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow leaves. The plant exhibits a clumping growth habit and is adapted to the tropical climate of its native habitat. Pyrostria louvelii is notable for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed soils.
