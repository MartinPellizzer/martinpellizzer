Paphinia grandiflora

Paphinia grandiflora, commonly known as the giant paphiopedilum, is a striking orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter, with vibrant pink and white petals. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial herb, often found in moist, shaded forest environments. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

Paphinia grandiflora is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and unique floral structure.
