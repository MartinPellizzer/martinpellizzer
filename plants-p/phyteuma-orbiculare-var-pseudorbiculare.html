Phyteuma orbiculare var. pseudorbiculare

Phyteuma orbiculare var. pseudorbiculare, commonly known as the narrow-leaved physostegia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to central and eastern Europe.

It features upright, branching stems that bear clusters of showy, tubular flowers in shades of pink to purple. The leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem, often with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, moist soils.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
