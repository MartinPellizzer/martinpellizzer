Plocama alshehbazii

Plocama alshehbazii, commonly known as the desert broom.

It is a low-growing shrub native to arid regions of the Middle East and North Africa. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss in hot, dry environments. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, appearing in clusters during the spring.

Plocama alshehbazii is valued for its resilience and is often used in xeriscaping due to its ability to thrive with minimal water.
