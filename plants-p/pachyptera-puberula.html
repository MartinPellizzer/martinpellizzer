Pachyptera puberula

Pachyptera puberula, commonly known as the woolly leafed daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-green foliage and daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal regions and is adapted to dry, coastal climates.

Pachyptera puberula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
