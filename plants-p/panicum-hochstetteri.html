Panicum hochstetteri

Panicum hochstetteri, commonly known as the Hochstetter's grass, is a species of grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces delicate, airy plumes of pale green to golden yellow flowers. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping and rock gardens. Its ornamental value comes from its fine texture and the soft, flowing appearance of its foliage and inflorescences.

Panicum hochstetteri is also valued for its low maintenance requirements and ability to attract pollinators.
