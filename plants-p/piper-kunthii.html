Piper kunthii

Piper kunthii, commonly known as the Mexican pepper vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central America and parts of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper-like plants. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by berries that turn red when mature. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly aromatic scent.

Piper kunthii is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties.
