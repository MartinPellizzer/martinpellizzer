Pycnocomon Rutifolium Var. Baeticum

Baetican pygmyweed, scientifically known as Pycnocomon rutifolium var. baeticum, is a rare and diminutive flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing remarkable adaptations to harsh climates. This plant is distinguished by its tiny, clustered flowers and compact growth habit, making it a unique subject of botanical study. Despite its small size, it plays an important ecological role in its native habitat. Its conservation status highlights the need for continued research and protection of such specialized flora.
