Poa cinnoides

Poa cinnoides, commonly known as tufted grass or silver hair grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern and eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-hairy leaves and tufted growth habit, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands, heathlands, and rocky slopes. Its inflorescence consists of a dense, cylindrical spikelet that is often tinged with red or purple.

Poa cinnoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and restoration projects.
