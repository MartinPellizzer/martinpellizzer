Primula rosea var. harrissii

Primula rosea var. harrissii, commonly known as the Harrissii primrose, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features rosettes of velvety, dark green leaves that are slightly hairy and oval in shape. The plant produces clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
