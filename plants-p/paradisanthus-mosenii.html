Paradisanthus mosenii

Paradisanthus mosenii, commonly known as the Mosenii paradise flower, is a rare and striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, bi-colored flowers that range from deep red to bright yellow, often with intricate patterns resembling a paradise scene. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges, and its stems are woody and somewhat succulent, allowing it to store water in arid conditions. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts interested in exotic flora.
