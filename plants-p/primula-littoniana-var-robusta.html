Primula littoniana var. robusta

Primula littoniana var. robusta, commonly known as the robust primrose, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple, with a distinctive throat coloration. The plant has hairy, narrow leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette, and it thrives in cool, moist environments. It is often found in rocky or forested habitats at elevations above 2,000 meters.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
