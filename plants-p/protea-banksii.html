Protea banksii

Protea banksii, commonly known as the silver protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence composed of numerous white to pale pink flowers arranged in a spherical shape. The plant is native to the Western and Eastern Cape provinces, where it thrives in well-drained, acidic soils. Its foliage consists of narrow, evergreen leaves that are often covered in a fine silvery down.

Protea banksii is a popular ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
