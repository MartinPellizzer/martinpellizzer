Pelargonium melananthon

Pelargonium melananthon, commonly known as the black-leaved geranium, is a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to red flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
