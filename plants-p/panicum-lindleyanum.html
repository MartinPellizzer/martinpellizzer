Panicum lindleyanum

Panicum lindleyanum, commonly known as Lindley's panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces panicles of small, delicate flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open fields or along roadsides.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
