Prometheum aizoon

Prometheum aizoon, commonly known as the flameleaf orchid, is a striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, flame-like orange and red petals that resemble the colors of fire. The plant features a slender, upright stem with lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a waxy coating. Its flowers bloom in late spring and early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
