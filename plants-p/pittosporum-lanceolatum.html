Pittosporum lanceolatum

Pittosporum lanceolatum, commonly known as the narrowleaf pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant white or cream flowers that bloom in spring, followed by bright red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and is often used in gardens for its ornamental value.

Pittosporum lanceolatum is also valued for its ability to provide habitat for native wildlife.
