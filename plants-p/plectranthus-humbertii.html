Plectranthus humbertii

Plectranthus humbertii, commonly known as the Lesotho plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture and produces small, tubular flowers in shades of blue or violet. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a garden setting.
