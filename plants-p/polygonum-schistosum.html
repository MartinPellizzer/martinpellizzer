Polygonum schistosum

Polygonum schistosum, commonly known as the stone plant or rock nettle, is a perennial herb native to temperate regions of Asia and North America.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in mountainous or alpine environments. The plant features upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink or purple, blooming in late summer.

Polygonum schistosum is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
