Phyllanthus neurocarpus

Phyllanthus neurocarpus, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It features small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by red, berry-like fruits. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

The species is also known for its medicinal uses in traditional African medicine.
