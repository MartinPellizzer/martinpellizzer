Perymenium blepharolepis

Perymenium blepharolepis, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its silver-gray leaves, which are covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or blue in color. Its fruit is a globose berry that turns from green to yellow or red as it matures.

Perymenium blepharolepis is native to parts of Africa and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.
