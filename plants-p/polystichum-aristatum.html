Polystichum aristatum

Polystichum aristatum, commonly known as the noble fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The fronds are bi-lobed and have a distinctive, elegant appearance with a dark green color and a glossy texture. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest areas.

This fern is valued for its ornamental qualities and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
