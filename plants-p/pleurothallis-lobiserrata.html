Pleurothallis lobiserrata

Pleurothallis lobiserrata, commonly known as the serrated leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often arranged in a raceme. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have serrated edges, giving it its common name.

Pleurothallis lobiserrata thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in its native habitat.
