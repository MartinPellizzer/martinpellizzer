Prunus mahaleb subvar. compacta

Prunus mahaleb subvar. compacta, commonly known as the dwarf cherry or compact cherry, is a small, ornamental shrub native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit, making it ideal for small garden spaces or container planting. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in early spring, followed by red or black berries in the fall. Its leaves are dark green and glossy, providing visual interest throughout the growing season.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types and climates.
