Pilosella argyrolegionensis

Pilosella argyrolegionensis, commonly known as the silverleaf mouseear, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, silver-gray leaves and daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and mountain slopes. It is a perennial herb that produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
