Polystichum microchlamys var. azumiense

Polystichum microchlamys var. azumiense, commonly known as the Azumi fern, is a variant of the Japanese shield fern.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are densely packed and often have a dark green color with a silvery underside. The plant is native to Japan and thrives in shaded, moist environments. It has a compact growth habit and is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its foliage remains evergreen throughout the year, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
