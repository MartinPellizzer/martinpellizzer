Psistus

Psistus, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple ray flowers and bristly, spiny leaves. Native to the Mediterranean region, Psistus thrives in coastal and saline environments. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a notable species in botanical studies and horticulture.
