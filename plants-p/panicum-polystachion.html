Panicum polystachion

Panicum polystachion, commonly known as the purple lovegrass, is a tall, perennial grass native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, fluffy seed heads that turn a striking purple color as they mature. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Panicum polystachion is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological systems.
