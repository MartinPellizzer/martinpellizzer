Premna mekongensis

Premna mekongensis, commonly known as Mekong premna, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Cambodia, Laos, Thailand, and Vietnam. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy on the underside. It produces small, white or pale purple flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the rainy season.

Premna mekongensis is often found in moist forest understories and is used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
