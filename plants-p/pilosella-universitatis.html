Pilosella universitatis

Pilosella universitatis, commonly known as the university grass or alpine groundsel, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the alpine regions of Europe and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in high-altitude environments. Pilosella universitatis has narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

It is often found in mountainous areas and is adapted to cold, harsh climates.
