Pothos hosei

Pothos hosei, commonly known as the marble queen pothos, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are variegated with creamy white and green patterns, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant is known for its adaptability to low light conditions and its ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments. Its aerial roots allow it to climb and attach to surfaces, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets or shelves.

Pothos hosei is also noted for its low maintenance requirements and air-purifying properties.
