Pilea capitellata

Pilea capitellata, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial shrub native to China.

It features rosettes of thick, waxy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers in clusters, which are followed by spherical, fleshy fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and ease of care have made it a favored houseplant worldwide.
