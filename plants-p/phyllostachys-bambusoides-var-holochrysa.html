Phyllostachys bambusoides var. holochrysa

Phyllostachys bambusoides var. holochrysa, commonly known as the golden bamboo, is a variant of the giant bamboo species native to China.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow culms that contrast with its dark green leaves. This bamboo grows in dense clumps and can reach heights of up to 10 meters. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a landscape plant.
