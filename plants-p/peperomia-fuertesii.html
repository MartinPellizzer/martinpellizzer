Peperomia fuertesii

Peperomia fuertesii, commonly known as the Fuertesii Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to Central America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, often with a vibrant green color and subtle variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color, emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate moisture.

Due to its ornamental foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
