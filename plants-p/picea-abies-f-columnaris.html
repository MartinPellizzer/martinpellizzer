Picea abies f. columnaris

Picea abies f. columnaris, commonly known as the columnar fir, is a unique form of the Norway spruce.

It is characterized by its narrow, upright growth habit, making it an ideal choice for ornamental landscaping. The plant retains its needle-like foliage throughout the year, offering year-round visual interest. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its compact form, it is often used in small garden spaces or as a privacy screen.
