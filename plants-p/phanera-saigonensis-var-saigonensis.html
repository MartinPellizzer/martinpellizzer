Phanera saigonensis var. saigonensis

Phanera saigonensis var. saigonensis, commonly known as the Saigon phanera, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its simple, ovate leaves with a smooth, glossy surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, round seeds.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.
