Polianthes × bundrantii

Polianthes × bundrantii, commonly known as the fragrant daffodil, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including yellow, white, and orange. The plant typically grows as a bulbous perennial, producing showy blooms in early spring. Its foliage consists of strap-like, grassy leaves that emerge after the flowers.

Polianthes × bundrantii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a cut flower.
