Podolepis longipedata var. robusta

Podolepis longipedata var. robusta, commonly known as the robust podolepis, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features upright, branched stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale pink flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped, with a rough texture and a strong, aromatic scent.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.
