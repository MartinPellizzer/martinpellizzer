Pseudostelis bradei

Pseudostelis bradei, commonly known as the Brade's pseudostelis, is a rare orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant typically features small, lance-shaped leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to bloom successfully.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a threatened species in its natural range.
