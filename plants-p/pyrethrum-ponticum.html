Pyrethrum ponticum

Pyrethrum ponticum, commonly known as the Crimean pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus and parts of southern Russia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow to orange flowers. The plant produces small, spherical seed heads that contain numerous tiny seeds. Its leaves are typically narrow and grayish-green, with a slightly hairy texture.

Pyrethrum ponticum is known for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential insecticidal properties.
