Platyschkuhria integrifolia

Platyschkuhria integrifolia, commonly known as the smoothleaf goldenbush, is a perennial shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The leaves are alternate, simple, and entire, giving the plant a smooth, refined appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

This plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
