Pterostylis jonesii

Pterostylis jonesii, commonly known as Jones' hooded orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, hood-like labellum. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests and understoreys. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often overlooked due to their inconspicuous appearance.

Pterostylis jonesii is a rare and endangered species, requiring specific conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
