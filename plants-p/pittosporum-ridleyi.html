Pittosporum ridleyi

Pittosporum ridleyi, commonly known as the Malayan pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage and fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Pittosporum ridleyi is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical and subtropical forests.
