Pentace polyantha

Pentace polyantha, commonly known as the white pentace, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces small, white, five-petaled flowers that are clustered in umbels, giving it a delicate appearance. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy surface.

The plant is valued for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
