Picris amalecitana

Picris amalecitana, commonly known as yellow starwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small stars. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny environments. Its yellow flowers bloom in the summer months, and it produces small, hairy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

The leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
