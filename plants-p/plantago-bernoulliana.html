Plantago bernoulliana

Plantago bernoulliana, commonly known as the Bernoulli's plantain, is a species of flowering plant in the family Plantaginaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically forms dense cushions of leaves, often found in alpine and arctic environments. The plant has broad, oval-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, greenish flowers in clusters. It is adapted to cold climates and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its unique ecological adaptations and limited distribution.
