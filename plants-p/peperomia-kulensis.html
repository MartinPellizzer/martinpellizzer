Peperomia kulensis

Peperomia kulensis, commonly known as the Kulens Island peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to the Philippines.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval or elliptical in shape, with a glossy green surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, often in shades of white or pale pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia kulensis is noted for its low maintenance requirements and ability to adapt well to various environmental conditions.
