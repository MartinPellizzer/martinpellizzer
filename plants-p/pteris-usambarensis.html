Pteris usambarensis

Pteris usambarensis, commonly known as the Usambarensis water fern, is a species of fern native to East Africa.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as stream banks and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine, and it plays a role in soil stabilization.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
