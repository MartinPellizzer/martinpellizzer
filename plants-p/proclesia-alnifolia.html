Proclesia alnifolia

Proclesia alnifolia, commonly known as the alderleaf spurge, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, woodland habitats. The plant features alternate, simple leaves that are oval to lance-shaped with a slightly hairy surface. It produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant is known for its ability to thrive in shaded environments and is often found in association with alder trees.
