Pomaderris elliptica var. elliptica

Pomaderris elliptica var. elliptica, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pomaderris, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features narrow, elliptical leaves that are typically grey-green in color, with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal heathlands and open forests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
