Protea compacta

Protea compacta, commonly known as the compact protea, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and its showy, clustered inflorescences. The flowers are typically pink or white, with a distinctive, woolly texture on the bracts. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This plant is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
