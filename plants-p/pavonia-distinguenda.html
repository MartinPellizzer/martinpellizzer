Pavonia distinguenda

Pavonia distinguenda, commonly known as the showy pavonia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or pink bracts that resemble petals, which are actually modified leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers clustered in the axils of the leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Pavonia distinguenda is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
