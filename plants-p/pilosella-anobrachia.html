Pilosella anobrachia

Pilosella anobrachia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cat's-paw, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow flowers with prominent white ray florets. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.

Pilosella anobrachia is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
