Pleurophragma platypodum

Pleurophragma platypodum, commonly known as the flatpod pleurophragma, is a member of the Leguminosae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are typically yellow or green and are clustered in small inflorescences.

The fruit is a legume pod that is flat and oblong, containing several seeds.
