Pilosella Acutisquama

Pilosella acutisquama is a small flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe and western Asia. It is known for its delicate, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant thrives in rocky and dry soils, often found in mountainous areas with high altitudes. Its leaves are hairy and narrow, giving it a unique appearance. Despite its modest size, Pilosella acutisquama plays an important role in its ecosystem.
