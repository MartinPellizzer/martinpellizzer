Pyrus malus var. wojejkowii

Pyrus malus var. wojejkowii, commonly known as the Wojejkowii apple, is a rare and unique variant of the apple tree.

It is characterized by its distinctive foliage, which features a deep green color with a silvery underside. The plant produces small, tart apples that are often used in culinary applications. It is native to specific regions in Central Asia and is highly valued for its ornamental qualities.
