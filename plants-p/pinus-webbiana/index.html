Pinus Webbiana

Pinus webbiana is a unique evergreen conifer native to southern Africa. It is known for its distinctive appearance and adaptability to harsh environments. This tree can grow in rocky soils and withstand periods of drought. Its cones and needles are important for local ecosystems and traditional uses. The plant's resilience makes it a notable species in its native habitat.
