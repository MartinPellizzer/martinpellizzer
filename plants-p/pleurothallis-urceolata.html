Pleurothallis urceolata

Pleurothallis urceolata, commonly known as the urn-shaped orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features narrow, dark green leaves and produces delicate, urn-shaped flowers in shades of white and yellow. Its inflorescence is often clustered, with multiple blooms emerging from a single stem.

This orchid is valued for its unique floral structure and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental appeal.
