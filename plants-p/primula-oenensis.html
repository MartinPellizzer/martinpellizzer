Primula oenensis

Primula oenensis, commonly known as the Japanese primrose or Oenensis primrose, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are usually broad and heart-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Primula oenensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
