Piper gallatlyi

Piper gallatlyi, commonly known as the Galatlyi pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit and has small, heart-shaped leaves. Its fruit is a small, dark purple berry that contains aromatic seeds.

Piper gallatlyi is valued for its unique flavor and is used in traditional medicine and culinary practices.
