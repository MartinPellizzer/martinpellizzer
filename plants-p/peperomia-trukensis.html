Peperomia trukensis

Peperomia trukensis, commonly known as the Truk Island peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the Pacific island of Truk.

It features fleshy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink. The plant is well adapted to humid, shaded environments and thrives in well-draining soil.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
