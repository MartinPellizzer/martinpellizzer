Peperomia breedlovei

Peperomia breedlovei, commonly known as the Oaxaca peperomia, is a rare and striking plant native to the cloud forests of Oaxaca, Mexico.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, growing in clusters along the stems. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by collectors and conservationists alike.
