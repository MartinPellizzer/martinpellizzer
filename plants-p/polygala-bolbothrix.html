Polygala bolbothrix

Polygala bolbothrix, commonly known as the narrowleaf milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are usually pink or violet in color. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

The genus Polygala is known for its unique floral structures, and P. bolbothrix is no exception, featuring a distinctive, spurred corolla.
