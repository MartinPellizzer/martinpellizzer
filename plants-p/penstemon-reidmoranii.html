Penstemon reidmoranii

Penstemon reidmoranii, commonly known as Reid's beardtongue, is a perennial flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. Penstemon reidmoranii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance as a nectar source for pollinators.
