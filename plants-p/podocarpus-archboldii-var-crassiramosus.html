Podocarpus archboldii var. crassiramosus

Podocarpus archboldii var. crassiramosus, commonly known as the thick-branch podocarp, is a member of the Podocarpaceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and compact growth habit. Its leaves are typically dark green and glossy, and it produces small, cone-like structures.

This species is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
