Phalaenopsis grandiflora var. gracillima

Phalaenopsis grandiflora var. gracillima, commonly known as the graceful phalaenopsis, is a hybrid orchid prized for its elegant appearance and long-lasting blooms.

It features pendulous inflorescences with numerous white or pale pink flowers that have a subtle fragrance. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, producing new shoots from the base of the old ones. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and often have a waxy texture.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its adaptability to indoor environments and minimal care requirements.
