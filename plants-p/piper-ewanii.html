Piper ewanii

Piper ewanii, commonly known as the pepper plant or the Mexican pepper plant, is a tropical shrub native to Central America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces tiny berries that are often used in traditional medicine and cuisine. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and practical purposes in horticulture.
