Pilea elliptica

Pilea elliptica, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a popular houseplant native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically light green with a subtle variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, requiring moderate watering.

Pilea elliptica is prized for its compact growth and air-purifying qualities, making it an ideal choice for indoor gardening.
