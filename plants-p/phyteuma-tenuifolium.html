Phyteuma tenuifolium

Phyteuma tenuifolium, commonly known as slenderleaf phyteuma, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

Its delicate appearance and ornamental value make it a popular plant among gardeners interested in cultivating hardy, decorative flora.
