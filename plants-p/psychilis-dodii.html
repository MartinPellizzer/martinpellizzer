Psychilis dodii

Psychilis dodii, commonly known as the Malabar thornapple, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, characterized by its spiny branches and palmate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a fleshy, orange-red berry that contains several seeds.

Psychilis dodii is adapted to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.
