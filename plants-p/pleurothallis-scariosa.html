Pleurothallis scariosa

Pleurothallis scariosa, commonly known as the scarious pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features slender, wiry stems and produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a subtle fragrance. Its leaves are narrow and strap-like, adapted for efficient water absorption in its natural habitat.

Pleurothallis scariosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
