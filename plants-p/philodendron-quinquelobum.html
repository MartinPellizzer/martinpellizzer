Philodendron quinquelobum

Philodendron quinquelobum, commonly known as the five-lobed philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with five distinct lobes, giving it its common name. The plant has a vigorous growth habit and can reach significant heights when provided with support. Its foliage is typically a deep green with a slightly glossy texture.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
