Podocarpus formosensis var. koshuensis

Podocarpus formosensis var. koshuensis, commonly known as the Koshu pine, is a rare and endemic species native to Taiwan.

It is a coniferous evergreen tree characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and distinctive cone structures. The plant belongs to the family Podocarpaceae and is notable for its slow growth and ornamental value.
