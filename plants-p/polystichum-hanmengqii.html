Polystichum hanmengqii

Polystichum hanmengqii, commonly known as the Hanmengqiu cliff fern, is a rare and unique fern species native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves with a distinct, serrated edge. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
