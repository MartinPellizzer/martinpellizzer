Philibertia reflexa

Philibertia reflexa, commonly known as the bentleaf filbert, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that curve inward, giving it its common name. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer.

Philibertia reflexa is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid conditions.
