Plectranthus humulopsis

Plectranthus humulopsis, commonly known as the honey plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale purple to white in color. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Plectranthus humulopsis thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for gardens and landscapes.
