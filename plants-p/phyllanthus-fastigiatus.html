Phyllanthus fastigiatus

Phyllanthus fastigiatus, commonly known as the upright phyllanthus, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by spherical, fleshy fruits. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Phyllanthus fastigiatus is often used in ornamental gardening due to its compact growth habit and attractive foliage.
