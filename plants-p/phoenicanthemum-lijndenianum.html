Phoenicanthemum lijndenianum

Phoenicanthemum lijndenianum, commonly known as the yellow star-of-Bethlem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, fluffy seed heads that are distinctive in appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance.
