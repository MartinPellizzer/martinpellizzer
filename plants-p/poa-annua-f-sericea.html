Poa annua f. sericea

Poa annua f. sericea, commonly known as silky bluegrass, is a variant of the annual bluegrass species.

It is characterized by its fine, silky hairs that cover the leaf blades, giving it a distinctive appearance. This plant typically grows in cool, moist environments and is often found in lawns and disturbed soils. It has a tufted growth habit and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences.

The leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.
