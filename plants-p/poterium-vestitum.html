Poterium vestitum

Poterium vestitum, commonly known as the clothed poterium, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a textured appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, with prominent stamens. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive species within its native range in Southeast Asia.
