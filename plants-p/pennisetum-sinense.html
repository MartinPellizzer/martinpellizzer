Pennisetum sinense

Pennisetum sinense, commonly known as Chinese fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to China.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and long, slender leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces fluffy, feathery seed heads that are often pink or purplish in hue, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Pennisetum sinense is often used in landscaping for its decorative appeal and ability to attract birds and butterflies.
