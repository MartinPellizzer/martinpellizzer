Panicum diffusum

Panicum diffusum, commonly known as wandering grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, spreading growth habit and small, fluffy inflorescences. The plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along water edges. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slight bluish-green hue.

Panicum diffusum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in wetland environments.
