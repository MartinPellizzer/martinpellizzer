Peperomia pirrisana

Peperomia pirrisana, commonly known as the Pari Island Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to the island of Pari in Indonesia.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters. The plant is well adapted to humid, shaded environments and thrives in well-draining soil. It is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.

Peperomia pirrisana is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ability to thrive in indoor conditions.
