Peperomia hypoleuca var. pluvigaudens

Peperomia hypoleuca var. pluvigaudens, commonly known as the Rainforest Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It features glossy, dark green ovate leaves with a slightly waxy texture and a prominent central vein. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that emerge from the axils of the leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with high humidity.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
