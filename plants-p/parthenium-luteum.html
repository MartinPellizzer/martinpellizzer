Parthenium luteum

Parthenium luteum, commonly known as yellow ironweed, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It features a robust root system and upright, hairy stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in panicles, which bloom from late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are lance-shaped, serrated, and covered with fine hairs.

Parthenium luteum is often found in moist to wet soils along stream banks and in wetlands.
