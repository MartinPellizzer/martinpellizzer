Peperomia haematolepis

Peperomia haematolepis, commonly known as the blood-leaf peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with striking red veins that become more pronounced in lower light conditions. The plant has small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers indirect sunlight, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for tabletop displays and decorative plant arrangements.
