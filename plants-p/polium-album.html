Polium album

Polium album, commonly known as silverweed, is a flowering plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which results from dense covering of fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, stream banks, and disturbed areas.

Polium album is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
