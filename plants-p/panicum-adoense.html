Panicum adoense

Panicum adoense, commonly known as the red panic grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense, feather-like inflorescences that produce a reddish hue. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardening and erosion control. Its rapid growth and ability to form dense mats make it a popular choice for landscaping.

Panicum adoense is also valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
