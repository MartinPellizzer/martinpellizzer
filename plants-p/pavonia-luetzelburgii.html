Pavonia luetzelburgii

Pavonia luetzelburgii, commonly known as the scarlet pavonia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, bright red or pink bract-like flowers. The plant features palmately lobed leaves and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for native plant landscaping.
