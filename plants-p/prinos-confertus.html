Prinos confertus

Prinos confertus, commonly known as the dense prion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often covered in fine hairs.

Prinos confertus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
