Pittosporum tenuifolium var. colensoi

Pittosporum tenuifolium var. colensoi, commonly known as Colenso's pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are narrow and elongated, with a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
