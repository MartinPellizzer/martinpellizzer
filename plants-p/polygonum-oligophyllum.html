Polygonum oligophyllum

Polygonum oligophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It typically grows in wet or moist habitats such as marshes, ditches, and along stream banks. The plant features upright stems that are often reddish in color, and its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped. It produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or reddish in color, which bloom in late summer.

Polygonum oligophyllum is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and is often used in wetland restoration projects.
