Piper longovarium

Piper longovarium, commonly known as the longovarium pepper or longovarium vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various pepper species. The plant produces small, elongated fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a rough texture.

The botanical characteristics of Piper longovarium make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and pharmacological studies.
