Pholistoma auritum var. auritum

Pholistoma auritum var. auritum, commonly known as the auriculate pholistoma, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often tinged with red or purple, especially in full sun. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and slightly curved, giving the plant a distinctive, elegant appearance.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
