Puccinellia torejensis

Puccinellia torejensis, commonly known as the Torej's saltbush, is a flowering plant species native to Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in saline and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.

Puccinellia torejensis plays a role in stabilizing soils in its native habitats and is of interest in ecological and botanical studies.
