Ptilotrichum elongatum

Ptilotrichum elongatum, commonly known as the slender leafed daisy, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features linear, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value and drought tolerance make it a popular plant among gardeners in arid regions.
