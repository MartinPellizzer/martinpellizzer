Pelargonium × chandleri

Pelargonium × chandleri, commonly known as the Royal Regal Geranium, is a hybrid plant that belongs to the Geraniaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors such as pink, red, and purple. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with heart-shaped, serrated leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Pelargonium × chandleri is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
