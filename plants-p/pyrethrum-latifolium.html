Pyrethrum latifolium

Pyrethrum latifolium, commonly known as broadleaf pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its broad, serrated leaves and showy yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces small, spherical seed heads that contain numerous tiny seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine.

Pyrethrum latifolium is also valued for its natural insect-repelling properties.
