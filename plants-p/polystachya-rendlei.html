Polystachya rendlei

Polystachya rendlei, commonly known as the red-topped orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in tropical forests. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, with a waxy surface that helps retain moisture.

Polystachya rendlei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
