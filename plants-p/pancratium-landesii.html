Pancratium landesii

Pancratium landesii, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a striking flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. It produces a distinctive, bulbous stem that stores water, allowing it to survive in arid environments.

Pancratium landesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
