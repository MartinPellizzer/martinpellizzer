Potentilla multifida

Potentilla multifida, commonly known as the fernleaf cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves that give it a delicate appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or rocky slopes.

Potentilla multifida is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
