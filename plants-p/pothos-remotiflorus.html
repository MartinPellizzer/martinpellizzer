Pothos remotiflorus

Pothos remotiflorus, commonly known as the remote-flowered pothos, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are not particularly showy. It thrives in humid, warm environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its adaptability.

Pothos remotiflorus is known for its ability to climb and can be trained to grow along trellises or walls.
