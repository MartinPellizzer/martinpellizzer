Pueraria edulis var. likiangensis

Pueraria edulis var. likiangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan cowpea, is a climbing legume native to China.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is valued for its edible tubers, which have a sweet, starchy flavor. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental and nutritional value, it is cultivated in some regions for both culinary and horticultural purposes.
