Pittosporum podocarpum var. angustatum

Pittosporum podocarpum var. angustatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by bright red berries that are an important food source for native birds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is also cultivated in gardens in temperate climates.
