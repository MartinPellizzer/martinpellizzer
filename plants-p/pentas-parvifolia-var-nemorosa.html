Pentas parvifolia var. nemorosa

Pentas parvifolia var. nemorosa, commonly known as the smallflower pentas, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. The plant has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for container gardening or as a ground cover. Its foliage is glossy and dark green, adding visual interest to the landscape.

It prefers full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, thriving in warm, humid climates.
