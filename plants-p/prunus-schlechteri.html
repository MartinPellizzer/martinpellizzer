Prunus schlechteri

Prunus schlechteri, commonly known as the Chinese bird cherry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and showy, white to pink flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are typically red when ripe. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Prunus schlechteri is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its potential health benefits.
