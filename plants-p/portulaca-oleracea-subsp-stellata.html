Portulaca oleracea subsp. stellata

Portulaca oleracea subsp.

stellata, commonly known as the star moss or star spinach, is a low-growing annual plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. It features fleshy, succulent leaves that are typically green or reddish in color, and it produces small, star-shaped flowers that are often pink or yellow.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it adaptable to various arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are edible and have been used in traditional cuisines for their mild, slightly salty flavor.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to prevent soil erosion.
