Perymenium lancifolium

Perymenium lancifolium, commonly known as the sharp-leafed perymenium, is a species of flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest undergrowth.

The botanical features of Perymenium lancifolium make it an interesting subject for study in tropical botany.
