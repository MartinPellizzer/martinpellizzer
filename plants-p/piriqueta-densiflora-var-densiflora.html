Piriqueta densiflora var. densiflora

Piriqueta densiflora var. densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered piriqueta, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of purple and blue. The plant typically grows in shrubland habitats, often in areas with well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its drought-resistant nature.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
