Pringleophytum lanceolatum

Pringleophytum lanceolatum, commonly known as the lanceolate pringlea, is a unique member of the Mesembryanthemaceae family, native to South Africa.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.
