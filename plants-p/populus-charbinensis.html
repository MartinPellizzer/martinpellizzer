Populus charbinensis

Populus charbinensis, commonly known as the Yunnan poplar, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Salicaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and distinctive bark. The plant thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions. It plays an important role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and contributing to soil stabilization.

Due to its ornamental value, it is also cultivated in botanical gardens and parks.
