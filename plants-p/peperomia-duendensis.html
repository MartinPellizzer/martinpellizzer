Peperomia duendensis

Peperomia duendensis, commonly known as the Duende Peperomia, is a compact, low-growing herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, velvety foliage with a variety of shades, including green, silver, and purple, depending on light exposure. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale green, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are often crinkled or wavy, adding a unique textural element to its appearance.

Peperomia duendensis is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and minimal water requirements.
