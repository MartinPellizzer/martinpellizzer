Procris divaricata

Procris divaricata, commonly known as the variable orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Procris divaricata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
