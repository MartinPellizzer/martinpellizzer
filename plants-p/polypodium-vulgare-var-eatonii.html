Polypodium vulgare var. eatonii

Polypodium vulgare var. eatonii, commonly known as the Eaton's shield fern, is a variant of the common shield fern.

It is a perennial evergreen fern that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit with large, leathery, lance-shaped fronds. Its spore-producing structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

This variety is often found in rocky or wooded areas across temperate regions.
