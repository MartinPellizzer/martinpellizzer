Pilosella hypeurya subsp. hanhartii

Pilosella hypeurya subsp.

hanhartii, commonly known as the Alpine Mouse-ear, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, with hairy leaves that give it its common name. It thrives in rocky and gravelly soils at high altitudes. Pilosella hypeurya subsp.

hanhartii is typically found in mountainous areas of the Alps and adjacent regions.
