Polygala rehmannii var. gymnoptera

Polygala rehmannii var. gymnoptera, commonly known as the naked-winged milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, often branched stems and small, raceme-like inflorescences. The flowers are typically pale yellow to greenish in color, with distinctive, winged petals that give the species its common name. It thrives in arid or semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

The plant is known for its medicinal uses in traditional Chinese medicine.
