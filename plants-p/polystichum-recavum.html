Polystichum recavum

Polystichum recavum, commonly known as the Japanese Shield Fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Japan.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant has a dense, compact growth habit and produces spores on the underside of its fronds. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Polystichum recavum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
