Paliurus aculeatus var. inermis

Paliurus aculeatus var. inermis, commonly known as the smooth paliurus, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to parts of Asia.

The variety inermis is distinguished by its lack of thorns, unlike its more spiny relatives.
