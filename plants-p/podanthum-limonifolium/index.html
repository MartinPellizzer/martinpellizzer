Podanthum Limonifolium

Podanthum limonifolium is a rare and unique plant known for its striking yellow-green foliage. Native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are shaped like small lemons, giving it its scientific name. This plant is highly sought after by botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. Due to its limited habitat, it is considered endangered and requires careful conservation efforts.
