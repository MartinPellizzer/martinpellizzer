Pittosporum eugenioides

Pittosporum eugenioides, commonly known as the yellow pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, oval-shaped berries that are typically red when mature. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Pittosporum eugenioides is also valued for its ability to attract native pollinators and birds.
