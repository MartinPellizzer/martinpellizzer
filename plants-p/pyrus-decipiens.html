Pyrus decipiens

Pyrus decipiens, commonly known as the deceptive pear, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to the eastern United States, particularly found in moist woodlands and stream banks. Pyrus decipiens is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
