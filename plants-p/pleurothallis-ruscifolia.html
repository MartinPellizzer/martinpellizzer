Pleurothallis ruscifolia

Pleurothallis ruscifolia, commonly known as the "Ruscifolia Orchid," is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, intricate flowers and foliage. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually dark green and leathery, while its flowers are delicate and often white or pale pink.

Pleurothallis ruscifolia is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
