Pistorinia breviflora var. flaviflora

Pistorinia breviflora var. flaviflora, commonly known as the yellow-flowered pistorinia, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and produces small, clustered flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, while the flowers are notable for their bright yellow color. This variety is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
