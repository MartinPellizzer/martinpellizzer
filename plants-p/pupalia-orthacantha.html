Pupalia orthacantha

Pupalia orthacantha, commonly known as the Indian clove or Pupalia, is a flowering plant native to southern Asia.

It is a small shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pink in color. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly serrated edge.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several small, aromatic seeds.
