Pachyveria pachyphytioides

Pachyveria pachyphytioides, commonly known as the thick-leaved stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its slow growth rate and compact form make it an attractive addition to succulent collections.
