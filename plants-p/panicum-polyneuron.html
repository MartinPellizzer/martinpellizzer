Panicum polyneuron

Panicum polyneuron, commonly known as the narrowleaf panicgrass, is a member of the grass family Poaceae.

It is a perennial plant characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, two-lobed flowers. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions and thrives in well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and potential agricultural applications.
