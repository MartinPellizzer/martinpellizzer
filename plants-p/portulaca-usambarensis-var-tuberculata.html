Portulaca usambarensis var. tuberculata

Portulaca usambarensis var. tuberculata, commonly known as the tuberculate portulaca, is a succulent plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils. Its leaves are typically covered in a waxy cuticle, which helps reduce water loss.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh conditions.
