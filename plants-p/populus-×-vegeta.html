Populus × vegeta

Populus × vegeta, commonly known as the hybrid aspen.

It is a cross between different species of aspen, typically Populus tremula and Populus tremuloides, resulting in a tree with unique genetic characteristics. The plant exhibits a combination of traits from its parent species, including growth habits and leaf morphology. It is often found in temperate regions where its parent species naturally occur.

The hybrid is notable for its adaptability and resilience in various environmental conditions.
