Philodendron lentii

Philodendron lentii, commonly known as the red-veined philodendron, is a climbing tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with striking red veins that contrast beautifully with its deep green foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers, though it is primarily grown for its ornamental leaves rather than its blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Philodendron lentii is popular among indoor gardeners for its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
