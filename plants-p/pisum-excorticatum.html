Pisum excorticatum

Pisum excorticatum, commonly known as the smooth vetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a legume characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow pea-like flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several edible seeds, which are used in various culinary traditions. Pisum excorticatum is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in ecological restoration projects.
