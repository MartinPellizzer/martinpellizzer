Peperomia gloriosifolia

Peperomia gloriosifolia, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, often adorned with subtle variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage, it is often used as a decorative plant in both home and office settings.
