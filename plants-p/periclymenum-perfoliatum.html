Periclymenum perfoliatum

Periclymenum perfoliatum, commonly known as the heartleaf honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its berries are reddish-black and are an important food source for birds and small mammals.

The plant is often found in moist woodlands and along stream banks, where it thrives in partial to full shade.
