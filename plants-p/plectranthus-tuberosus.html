Plectranthus tuberosus

Plectranthus tuberosus, commonly known as African violet, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive aromatic scent. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, typically arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor and garden cultivation.

Its tuberous roots contribute to its resilience and ability to regenerate after pruning.
