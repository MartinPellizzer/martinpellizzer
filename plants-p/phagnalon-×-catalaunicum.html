Phagnalon × catalaunicum

Phagnalon × catalaunicum, commonly known as the hybrid meadow grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, creeping stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellow to brown flowers arranged in compact clusters, typical of the Compositae family. It thrives in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils.

Phagnalon × catalaunicum is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
