Pouzolzia alienata

Pouzolzia alienata, commonly known as the alien gooseberry, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its small, yellow-orange berries that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Pouzolzia alienata is also valued for its ornamental qualities due to its attractive foliage and fruit.
