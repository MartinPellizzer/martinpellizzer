Peddiea arborescens

Peddiea arborescens, commonly known as the tree-like peddiea, is a unique species belonging to the family Peddieaceae.

It is characterized by its woody stem and branching structure, resembling a small tree or shrub. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or pale yellow in color. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Peddiea arborescens is native to specific regions and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
