Psilocaulon acutisepalum

Psilocaulon acutisepalum, commonly known as the sharp-sepal psilocaulon, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and small, greenish-white flowers arranged in umbels. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a distinctive, aromatic scent.
