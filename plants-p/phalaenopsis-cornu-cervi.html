Phalaenopsis cornu-cervi

Phalaenopsis cornu-cervi, commonly known as the horned orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking, long-lasting flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a unique horn-like appendage on the lip. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining potting media.

Phalaenopsis cornu-cervi is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
