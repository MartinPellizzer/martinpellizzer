Praxelis grandifolium

Praxelis grandifolium, commonly known as the large-leaf praxelis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and streambanks. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Praxelis grandifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
