Pyrus × watereri

Pyrus × watereri, commonly known as the Chinese white pear.

It is a hybrid species within the genus Pyrus, resulting from crosses between different pear species. The plant is characterized by its ornamental value, featuring showy white flowers and a moderate growth habit. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal rather than its fruit.

The leaves are typically broad and oval, providing a dense canopy during the growing season.
