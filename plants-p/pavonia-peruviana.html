Pavonia peruviana

Pavonia peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian velvet flower, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, velvety pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for warm climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
