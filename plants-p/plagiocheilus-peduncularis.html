Plagiocheilus peduncularis

Plagiocheilus peduncularis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pendulous flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories or along stream banks. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, and it produces small, fragrant blossoms that attract pollinators like bees and moths.
