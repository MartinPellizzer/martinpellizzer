Pterochrosia neriifolia

Pterochrosia neriifolia, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a strikingly beautiful succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, waxy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a distinctive white or pale blue coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and purple, which bloom during the cooler months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems.

Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
