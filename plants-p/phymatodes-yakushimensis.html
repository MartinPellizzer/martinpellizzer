Phymatodes yakushimensis

Phymatodes yakushimensis, commonly known as the Yakushimen yew, is a rare evergreen conifer native to Japan.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and a distinctive, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, reddish-brown cones that mature into winged seeds, facilitating wind dispersal. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species.
