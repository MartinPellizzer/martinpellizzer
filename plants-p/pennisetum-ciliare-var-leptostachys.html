Pennisetum ciliare var. leptostachys

Pennisetum ciliare var. leptostachys, commonly known as silky bluegrass, is a perennial grass native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and produces delicate, silver-tan inflorescences that resemble plumes. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and other riparian habitats. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.

Its growth habit is clump-forming, making it suitable for both natural and landscaped settings.
