Polystachya holmesiana

Polystachya holmesiana, commonly known as the Holmes' sword fern, is a member of the Polystachyaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to the wet tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit with long, arching leaves that resemble sword blades. Its flowers are typically arranged in dense, branched clusters and are often greenish or brownish in color.

It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
