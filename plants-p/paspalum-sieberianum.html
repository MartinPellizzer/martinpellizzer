Paspalum sieberianum

Paspalum sieberianum, commonly known as silvergrass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain tiny flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny areas.

Paspalum sieberianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to form dense, low-maintenance turf.
