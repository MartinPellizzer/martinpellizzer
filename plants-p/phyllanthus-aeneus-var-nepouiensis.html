Phyllanthus aeneus var. nepouiensis

Phyllanthus aeneus var. nepouiensis, commonly known as the Nepali aeneus, is a shrub native to the Himalayan regions of Nepal and northern India.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has elliptical leaves with a smooth, waxy surface and produces small, round fruits that turn red when mature. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and rocky slopes.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
