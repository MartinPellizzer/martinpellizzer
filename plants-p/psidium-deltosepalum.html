Psidium deltosepalum

Psidium deltosepalum, commonly known as the deltosepal guava, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its fruit, similar to the common guava, is edible and has a mild, sweet flavor.

The plant is valued for its ornamental value and potential use in horticulture and agriculture.
