Polygonum × condensatum

Polygonum × condensatum, commonly known as red polygonum, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Polygonaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive red stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in late summer. It is typically found in moist environments such as wetlands and stream banks.

Polygonum × condensatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in wet conditions.
