Pteris liboensis

Pteris liboensis, commonly known as the Lipo fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense layer of brown, hairy scales.

Pteris liboensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic foliage.
