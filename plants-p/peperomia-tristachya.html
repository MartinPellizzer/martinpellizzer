Peperomia tristachya

Peperomia tristachya, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features succulent, dark green leaves with a silvery underside, giving it a distinctive, almost metallic appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia tristachya is relatively low maintenance and resistant to pests, contributing to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
