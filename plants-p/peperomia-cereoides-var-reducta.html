Peperomia cereoides var. reducta

Peperomia cereoides var. reducta, commonly known as the compact peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale green, arranged in short spikes. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.

The varietal form 'reducta' is characterized by its more compact growth habit compared to the typical species.
