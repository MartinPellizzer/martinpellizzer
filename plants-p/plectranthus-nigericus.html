Plectranthus nigericus

Plectranthus nigericus, commonly known as black plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dark purple to almost black foliage, which gives it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color, arranged in clusters. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Plectranthus nigericus is relatively low maintenance and prefers well-draining soil.
