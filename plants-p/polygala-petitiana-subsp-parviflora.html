Polygala petitiana subsp. parviflora

Polygala petitiana subsp.

parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered milkvetch, is a member of the Polygalaceae family. It is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and often clustered in spikes, with distinctive leaf shapes.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and subtle floral display.
