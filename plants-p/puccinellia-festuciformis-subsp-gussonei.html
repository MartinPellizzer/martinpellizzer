Puccinellia festuciformis subsp. gussonei

Puccinellia festuciformis subsp.

gussonei, commonly known as the coastal needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean. It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and compact, spike-like inflorescences.

The plant thrives in saline and sandy soils, often found in dunes and coastal grasslands. It plays a vital role in stabilizing dune ecosystems and preventing erosion.

Its adaptability to harsh coastal conditions makes it an important component of coastal vegetation.
