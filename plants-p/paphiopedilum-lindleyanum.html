Paphiopedilum lindleyanum

Paphiopedilum lindleyanum, commonly known as the Lindley's lady's slipper, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped pouch that serves as a pollination trap. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or green with intricate patterns.

It is prized for its beauty and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
