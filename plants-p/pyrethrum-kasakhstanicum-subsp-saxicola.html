Pyrethrum kasakhstanicum subsp. saxicola

Pyrethrum kasakhstanicum subsp.

saxicola, commonly known as the saxicolous pyrethrum, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals, typical of the Asteraceae family. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

This subspecies is valued for its potential in ornamental gardening and as a source of natural insecticides.
