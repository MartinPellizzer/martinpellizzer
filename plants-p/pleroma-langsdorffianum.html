Pleroma langsdorffianum

Pleroma langsdorffianum, commonly known as the coastal morning glory, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual with slender, trailing stems that often grow along the ground. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to purple, typically blooming in the morning. Its leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Pleroma langsdorffianum thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to saline and coastal environments.
