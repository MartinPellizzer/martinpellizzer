Pulmonaria × hybrida

Pulmonaria × hybrida, commonly known as lungwort, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the family Violaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive basal rosette of hairy, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. Native to Europe, this plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland settings. The foliage is typically covered in fine, velvety hairs, which contribute to its ornamental appeal.

It is valued in gardens for its early spring blooms and ability to attract pollinators.
