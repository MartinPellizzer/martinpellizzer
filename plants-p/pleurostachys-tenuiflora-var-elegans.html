Pleurostachys tenuiflora var. elegans

Pleurostachys tenuiflora var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant feathergrass, is a perennial grass native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and delicate, feathery inflorescences that give it a graceful appearance. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun, making it well-suited for alpine or rock garden environments. Its foliage is narrow and linear, with a silver-gray hue that adds visual interest in both foliage and flower displays.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated for its unique aesthetic in specialized gardening settings.
