Peperomia argumentos

Peperomia argumentos, commonly known as the Shiny Cabbage Plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to South America.

It features thick, waxy, and often variegated leaves that are typically oval or lance-shaped. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate watering.

Its ornamental foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
