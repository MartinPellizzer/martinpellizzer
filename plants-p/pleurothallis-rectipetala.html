Pleurothallis rectipetala

Pleurothallis rectipetala, commonly known as the straight-leafed pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically white or pale green, with a unique structure that includes a prominent spur. This epiphytic plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
