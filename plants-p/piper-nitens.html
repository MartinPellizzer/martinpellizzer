Piper nitens

Piper nitens, commonly known as the narrowleaf pepper vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Piper nitens is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
