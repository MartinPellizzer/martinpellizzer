Pothos loureiroi

Pothos loureiroi, commonly known as the Variegated Pothos, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive variegation of green, white, and yellow patterns. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are not particularly showy. Its aerial roots allow it to climb and attach to surfaces, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets or shelves.

Pothos loureiroi is known for its hardiness and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
