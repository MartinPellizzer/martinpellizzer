Pyrethrum praecox

Pyrethrum praecox, commonly known as early pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant produces small, spherical seed heads that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Pyrethrum praecox is also valued for its natural insect-repelling properties due to the presence of pyrethrins in its flowers.
