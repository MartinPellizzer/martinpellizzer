Panicum strictum

Panicum strictum, commonly known as rigid panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
