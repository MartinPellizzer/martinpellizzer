Piper nervulosum

Piper nervulosum, commonly known as the slender pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with opposite, ovate leaves that have a slightly hairy texture. Its small, greenish-white flowers are borne in clusters and are followed by elongated, narrow berries.

Piper nervulosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
