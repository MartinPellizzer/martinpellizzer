Pleurothallis renipetala var. intermedia

Pleurothallis renipetala var. intermedia, commonly known as the Intermediate Narrow-leaf Orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small to medium-sized orchid with slender, often pendulous stems and lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically white or pale yellow, with a distinctive three-lobed lip that is often marked with purple or red streaks. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high air humidity and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its delicate appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
