Paepalanthus spadiceus

Paepalanthus spadiceus, commonly known as the yellow torch cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny stems that form a compact, globular shape. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters at the apex of the stems. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, preferring well-drained soils.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
