Pseudostellaria heterantha var. linearifolia

Pseudostellaria heterantha var. linearifolia, commonly known as the linear-leaved starwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors or stream banks. The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
