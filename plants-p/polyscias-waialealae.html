Polyscias waialealae

Polyscias waialealae, commonly known as the Maui fan palm, is a rare and endemic plant species native to the Hawaiian island of Maui.

The plant has compound leaves with long, narrow leaflets that give it a feathery appearance, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by red berries. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered vulnerable and is protected under conservation efforts.
