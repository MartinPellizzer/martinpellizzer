Psorospermum nervosum var. nervosum

Psorospermum nervosum var. nervosum, commonly known as the narrow-veined psorospermum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub characterized by its simple, alternate leaves with prominent venation. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. Its bark is smooth and grayish, often exuding a milky sap when damaged.

It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.
