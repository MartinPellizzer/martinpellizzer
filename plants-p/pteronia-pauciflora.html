Pteronia pauciflora

Pteronia pauciflora, commonly known as the few-flowered pteronia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Pteronia pauciflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
