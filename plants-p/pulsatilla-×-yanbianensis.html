Pulsatilla × yanbianensis

Pulsatilla × yanbianensis, commonly known as the Yanbian pasqueflower, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, cup-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant features a short, hairy stem that supports its basal leaves, which are usually palmately divided. Native to the mountainous regions of northern China, it thrives in cool, moist environments.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as a hybrid species, showcasing genetic diversity within the Pulsatilla genus.
