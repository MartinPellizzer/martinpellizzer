Populus × tomentosa f. deltatifolia

Populus × tomentosa f. deltatifolia, commonly known as the deltoid trembling aspen.

This cultivar is a hybrid poplar species characterized by its unique foliage, featuring broad, deltoid-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense, white pubescence. It is native to China and is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions. The plant typically grows as a medium-sized tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters.

Its rapid growth rate and ability to thrive in temperate climates make it a popular choice for landscaping and reforestation projects.
