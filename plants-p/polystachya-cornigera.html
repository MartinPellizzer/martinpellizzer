Polystachya cornigera

Polystachya cornigera, commonly known as the horned bamboo, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its unique, elongated inflorescence that resembles a horn. The plant produces striking, pale yellow to orange flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Polystachya cornigera is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has a relatively compact growth habit.
