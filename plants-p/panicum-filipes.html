Panicum filipes

Panicum filipes, commonly known as silky grass or silky panicum, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, upright stems with whorled clusters of narrow, leaf-like bracts that give it a delicate, feathery appearance. The plant produces small, fluffy panicles of flowers that are often pinkish or reddish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its unique morphology and ability to tolerate drought conditions contribute to its ecological and horticultural significance.
