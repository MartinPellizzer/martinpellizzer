Pleurothallis instar

Pleurothallis instar, commonly known as the star orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a small epiphytic orchid that typically grows on tree branches or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often arranged in clusters. Its pseudobulbs are short and stout, and its leaves are strap-shaped and arranged in a rosette.

The species is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
