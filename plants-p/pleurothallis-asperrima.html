Pleurothallis asperrima

Pleurothallis asperrima, commonly known as the rough pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically arranged in a raceme. Its leaves are usually lanceolate and covered in a dense layer of short, stiff hairs.

This orchid is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for hobbyist growers.
