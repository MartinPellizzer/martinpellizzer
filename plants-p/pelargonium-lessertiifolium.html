Pelargonium lessertiifolium

Pelargonium lessertiifolium, commonly known as the coastal geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, dark green leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of bright pink to red flowers, which bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in regions with a Mediterranean climate.
