Piper carizalanum

Piper carizalanum, commonly known as the Carizalana pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to South America, specifically found in regions of Peru and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into round, peppery fruits.

This plant is valued for its aromatic leaves and fruits, which are used in traditional culinary and medicinal practices.
