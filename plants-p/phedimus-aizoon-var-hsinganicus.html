Phedimus aizoon var. hsinganicus

Phedimus aizoon var. hsinganicus, commonly known as the Chinese groundcover plant, is a low-growing perennial species native to China.

It features dense, mat-forming growth with small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and as a ground cover.

Its ornamental value and ability to suppress weeds make it a popular choice in horticulture.
