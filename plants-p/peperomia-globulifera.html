Peperomia globulifera

Peperomia globulifera, commonly known as the globe peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, velvety foliage with a rounded, almost globular shape, giving it its common name. The plant has small, ovate leaves that are typically green with a slight sheen, and it produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia globulifera is popular as a decorative indoor plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
