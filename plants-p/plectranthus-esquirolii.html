Plectranthus esquirolii

Plectranthus esquirolii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to white. The plant typically grows as a shrub or perennial herb, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. It prefers well-drained soil and moderate light conditions, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the year under proper care.
