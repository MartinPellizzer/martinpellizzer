Patagonium gilliesii

Patagonium gilliesii, commonly known as the Patagonian lily, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of southern South America.

Patagonium gilliesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in temperate climates.
