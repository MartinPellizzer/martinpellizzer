Piloisia globosa

Piloisia globosa, commonly known as the globular plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and hairy, gray-green foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or blue in color, arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Piloisia globosa is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
