Phebalium rude subsp. amblycarpum

Phebalium rude subsp.

amblycarpum, commonly known as the rough phebalium, is a shrub native to Australia. It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves.
