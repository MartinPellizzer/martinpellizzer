Poincianella pellucida

Poincianella pellucida, commonly known as the glassy leaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, translucent, waxy leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by elongated, woody fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
