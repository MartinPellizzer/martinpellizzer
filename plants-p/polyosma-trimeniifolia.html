Polyosma trimeniifolia

Polyosma trimeniifolia, commonly known as the slender polyosma, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical rainforests of northern Australia.

It typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, with slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, red or orange berries.

It is often found in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
