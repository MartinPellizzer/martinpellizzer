Piper gracillimum var. dactoense

Piper gracillimum var. dactoense, commonly known as the Dactoense pepper plant, is a tropical species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing vines and small, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when mature, containing elliptical seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the undergrowth of tropical forests.

Its botanical features make it a unique subject for ecological and horticultural study.
