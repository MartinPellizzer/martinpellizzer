Podocarpus aspleniifolius

Podocarpus aspleniifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved podocarp, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Podocarpaceae and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage with narrow, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain its seeds, typical of the coniferous family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its aesthetic appeal and hardiness.
