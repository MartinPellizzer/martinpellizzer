Polygala dunniana

Polygala dunniana, commonly known as the mountain polygala, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, branched stems and small, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually pale blue or purple and are visited by various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.

Polygala dunniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
