Pothos nigricans

Pothos nigricans, commonly known as the black pothos, is a trailing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves that often have a slight velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers, followed by green, berry-like fruits that ripen to a dark red. It thrives in low-light conditions and is popular as a houseplant due to its hardiness and ease of care.

Pothos nigricans is also known for its ability to purify indoor air, making it a favored choice for interior landscaping.
