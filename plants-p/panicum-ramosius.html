Panicum ramosius

Panicum ramosius, commonly known as silver panicum, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a member of the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage due to dense pubescence. The plant produces panicles of small, fluffy flowers that are often pink or red in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardens.

Panicum ramosius is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.
