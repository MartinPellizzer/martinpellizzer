Palimbia chabraei var. glabrescens

Palimbia chabraei var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth palimbia, is a rare and lesser-known species within the Palimbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous foliage and compact growth habit, typically reaching heights of 30 to 50 centimeters. Native to specific regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests. Its leaves are usually elliptical in shape with a glossy surface, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often overlooked.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
