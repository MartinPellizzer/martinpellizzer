Paesia rugosula

Paesia rugosula, commonly known as the roughleaf paesia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are rough and hairy, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Paesia rugosula is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to withstand saline and arid conditions.
