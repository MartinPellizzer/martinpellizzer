Peperomia serpyllifolia

Peperomia serpyllifolia, commonly known as the lawn peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to South America.

It features small, grass-like leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia serpyllifolia is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.
