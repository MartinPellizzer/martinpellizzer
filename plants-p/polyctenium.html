Polyctenium

Polyctenium, commonly known as the sea holly, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flower heads that resemble small holly berries. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and saline soils, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and often have a waxy coating to reduce water loss.
