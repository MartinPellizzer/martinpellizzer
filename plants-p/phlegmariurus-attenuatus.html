Phlegmariurus attenuatus

Phlegmariurus attenuatus, commonly known as the slender thornmoss, is a species of liverwort belonging to the family Porellaceae.

It is characterized by its creeping growth habit and the presence of small, elongated leaves that are often slightly curled. This moss is widely distributed across temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.
