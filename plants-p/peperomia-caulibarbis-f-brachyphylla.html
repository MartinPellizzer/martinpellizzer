Peperomia caulibarbis f. brachyphylla

Peperomia caulibarbis f. brachyphylla, commonly known as the compact peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green and slightly glossy.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America. The plant features small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its tolerance for dry air.

Its compact form makes it an ideal choice for tabletop displays or as part of a succulent collection.
