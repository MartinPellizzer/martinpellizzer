Proustia pungens

Proustia pungens, commonly known as the spiked proustia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Proustia pungens is known for its hardy nature and ability to withstand drought conditions.
