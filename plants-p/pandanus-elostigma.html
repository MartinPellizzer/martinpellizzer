Pandanus elostigma

Pandanus elostigma, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pandanus, is a species of palm native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically clustered in branched inflorescences. Its fruit is a fibrous, spiky structure that contains several seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and cultural significance in various regions.
