Psorospermum chionanthifolium var. pilosiusculum

Psorospermum chionanthifolium var. pilosiusculum, commonly known as the silky leaf psorospermum, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage that gives it a soft, velvety appearance. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine pubescence, contributing to its distinctive look. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the rainy season.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
