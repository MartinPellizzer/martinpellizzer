Podochilus rotundipetala

Podochilus rotundipetala, commonly known as the round-leaved orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, rounded leaves that are typically clustered at the base, and its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white with a distinctive, spurred lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.

Podochilus rotundipetala is often cultivated for its ornamental value in indoor gardens and greenhouses.
