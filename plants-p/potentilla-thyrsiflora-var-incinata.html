Potentilla thyrsiflora var. incinata

Potentilla thyrsiflora var. incinata, commonly known as the flame potentilla, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of central Asia.

It features dense, upright growth with bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has small, ovate leaves with a hairy texture and a distinctive reddish-brown stem that becomes more pronounced with age. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant fall color and ability to attract pollinators.
