Primula auricula f. serratifolia

Primula auricula f. serratifolia, commonly known as the serrated auricula, is a cultivar of the auricula primrose.

It is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves that are serrated along the edges. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often grown as a garden ornamental.

This variety is prized for its unique foliage and delicate floral display.
