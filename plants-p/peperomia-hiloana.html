Peperomia hiloana

Peperomia hiloana, commonly known as the Hilo peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a compact, low-growing plant with fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia hiloana is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
