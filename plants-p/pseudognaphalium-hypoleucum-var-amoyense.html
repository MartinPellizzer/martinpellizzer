Pseudognaphalium hypoleucum var. amoyense

Pseudognaphalium hypoleucum var. amoyense, commonly known as the Amoy false dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern China and Southeast Asia.

The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and forest margins.
