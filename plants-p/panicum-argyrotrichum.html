Panicum argyrotrichum

Panicum argyrotrichum, commonly known as silverleaf panicgrass, is a species of grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which results from dense, fine pubescence covering the leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies, roadsides, and disturbed soils. Its inflorescence consists of a cylindrical spikelet with closely arranged, pale green to silver bracts.

Panicum argyrotrichum is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
