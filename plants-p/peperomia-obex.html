Peperomia obex

Peperomia obex, commonly known as the blunt-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval or elliptical in shape, with a smooth, sometimes slightly serrated edge. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, clustered in spikes at the tips of the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia obex is known for its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor conditions.
