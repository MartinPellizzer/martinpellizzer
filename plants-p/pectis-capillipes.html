Pectis capillipes

Pectis capillipes, commonly known as the slender pectis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, branching stems and narrow, linear leaves. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in dense clusters, making it an attractive species for gardens.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and rocky habitats.
