Polystichum asiae-minoris

Polystichum asiae-minoris, commonly known as the Korean shield fern, is a species of fern native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen fronds that form a compact, shield-shaped crown. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its leaves are bi-lobed and have a leathery texture, with a dark green coloration.

Polystichum asiae-minoris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic and ecological benefits.
