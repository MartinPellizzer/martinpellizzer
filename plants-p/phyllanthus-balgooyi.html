Phyllanthus balgooyi

Phyllanthus balgooyi, commonly known as the African spurge, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in savanna and woodland habitats. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, yellow flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces small, hard capsules as fruit.

It is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
