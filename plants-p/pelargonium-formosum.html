Pelargonium formosum

Pelargonium formosum, commonly known as the Chinese geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its showy, brightly colored flowers that typically range from pink to red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated edge, and it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.

Pelargonium formosum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
