Peperomia ligustrina

Peperomia ligustrina, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are often variegated with silvery or white markings, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia ligustrina is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
