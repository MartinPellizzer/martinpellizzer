Potamogeton × heslop-harrisonii

It is a submerged aquatic plant characterized by its long, ribbon-like leaves that are often arranged in whorls around the stem. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are typically clustered in spikelets, and it is adapted to thrive in freshwater environments such as ponds and slow-moving streams. Potamogeton × heslop-harrisonii plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.
