Piper crebrinodum

Piper crebrinodum, commonly known as the slender pepper vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various pepper species. The plant features opposite, elliptical leaves with prominent veins and small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruit is a small, three-lobed berry that turns red when mature.

Piper crebrinodum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in local medicine.
