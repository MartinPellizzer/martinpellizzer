Panicum acutum

Panicum acutum, commonly known as prostrate panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a low-growing perennial grass that typically forms dense mats on the ground. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like clusters of flowers. It thrives in sandy or disturbed soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Panicum acutum is valued for its erosion control capabilities and ornamental use in landscaping.
