Psittacanthus Schiedeanus

Mexican flame tree, scientifically known as Psittacanthus schiedeanus, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America and the Caribbean. With its vibrant red and orange flowers, it stands out as a dramatic addition to any landscape. This tree is not only visually appealing but also holds cultural significance in its native regions. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental planting in warm climates. Despite its beauty, it requires specific care to thrive and flourish.
