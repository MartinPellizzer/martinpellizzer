Prunus × lannesiana f. robusta

Prunus × lannesiana f. robusta, commonly known as the dwarf cherry, is a compact, ornamental shrub prized for its vibrant spring blossoms.

It is a natural hybrid between Prunus lannesiana and Prunus × yedoensis, exhibiting characteristics of both parent species. The plant features small, rounded leaves and clusters of pink or white flowers that bloom in early spring. It is well-suited for container gardening and small spaces due to its compact growth habit.

Its botanical appeal lies in its delicate foliage and showy flowers, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscapes.
