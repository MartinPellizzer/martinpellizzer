Polypodium macropterum var. splendidum

Polypodium macropterum var. splendidum, commonly known as the splendid fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically bright green with a glossy texture. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in forest understories. Its rhizomes are thick and fleshy, allowing it to store moisture and nutrients efficiently.

The varietal name 'splendidum' refers to the striking appearance of its foliage, which makes it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
