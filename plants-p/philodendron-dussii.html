Philodendron dussii

Philodendron dussii, commonly known as the Dussii Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are often variegated with cream or yellow markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by red berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and hanging baskets.
