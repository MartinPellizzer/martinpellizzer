Piper longispicum var. glabratum

Piper longispicum var. glabratum, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed longispicum pepper, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes numerous species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface. Its small, clustered flowers are typically greenish-yellow, and it produces small, peppery fruits that are used in traditional medicine.

This variety is valued for its aromatic foliage and potential medicinal properties.
