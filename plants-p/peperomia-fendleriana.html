Peperomia fendleriana

Peperomia fendleriana, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, waxy leaves that are typically variegated with silver and green hues, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has small, inconspicuous flowers that appear in clusters, usually in late spring or early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia fendleriana is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
