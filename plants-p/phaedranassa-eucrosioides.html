Phaedranassa eucrosioides

Phaedranassa eucrosioides, commonly known as the purple-veined orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking violet and green foliage with distinctive purple veins. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant plant.
