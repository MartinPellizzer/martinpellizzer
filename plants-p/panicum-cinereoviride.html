Panicum cinereoviride

Panicum cinereoviride, commonly known as silver-green panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and its distinctive silver-green foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous spikelets that are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as fields and roadsides.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
