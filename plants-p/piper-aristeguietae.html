Piper aristeguietae

Piper aristeguietae, commonly known as the Aristeguieta's pepper, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.

The fruit is a small, capsule-like structure that contains several seeds.
