Primula hidakana f. kamuiana

Primula hidakana f. kamuiana, commonly known as the Kamuiana primrose, is a rare and ornamental plant native to Japan.

It is a member of the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices.
