Piper cumbasonum

Piper cumbasonum, commonly known as the Cumbasonum pepper vine, is a climbing plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various pepper species. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, slender fruits. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a glossy surface.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and potential medicinal properties.
