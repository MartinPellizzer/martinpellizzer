Protea madiensis var. latifolia

Protea madiensis var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences composed of numerous white to pinkish flowers arranged in a spherical or conical shape. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation. Its leaves are broad and leathery, providing a striking contrast to the soft floral display.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its longevity and aesthetic appeal in floral arrangements.
