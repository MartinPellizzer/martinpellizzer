Piper froesii

Piper froesii, commonly known as the Froesii pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with long, slender stems that can reach several meters in length. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a glossy green surface with a distinct aroma.

The small, greenish-yellow flowers are followed by oval, peppery fruits that are used in local cuisines and traditional medicine.
