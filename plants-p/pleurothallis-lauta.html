Pleurothallis lauta

Pleurothallis lauta, commonly known as the royal pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces strikingly beautiful flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with intricate patterns. Its pseudobulbs are slender and clustered, supporting its overall compact growth form.

Pleurothallis lauta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
