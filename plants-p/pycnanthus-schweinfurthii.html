Pycnanthus schweinfurthii

Pycnanthus schweinfurthii, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of white flowers that bloom in spring, giving it its common name. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green. It prefers well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for cultivation in warm climates.

The tree is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
