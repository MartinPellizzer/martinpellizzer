Panicum violaceum

Panicum violaceum, commonly known as violet panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a warm-season grass that thrives in full sun and well-drained soils. The plant produces dense, tufted clumps with slender, blue-green leaves and delicate, violet-tinted flower spikes. Its botanical features include slender rhizomes and small, three-angled stems.

It is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
