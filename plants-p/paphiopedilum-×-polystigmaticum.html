Paphiopedilum × polystigmaticum

Paphiopedilum × polystigmaticum, commonly known as the hybrid lady's slipper orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique slipper-shaped pouch, which is a distinctive feature of the Paphiopedilum genus. The plant typically produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

This hybrid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
