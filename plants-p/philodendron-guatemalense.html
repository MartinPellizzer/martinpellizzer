Philodendron guatemalense

Philodendron guatemalense, commonly known as the Guatemalan philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically bright green with a slightly textured surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by dark, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
