Pleurothallis membraniflora

Pleurothallis membraniflora, commonly known as the membranous-leaf orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often translucent leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
