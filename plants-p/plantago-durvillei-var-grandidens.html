Plantago durvillei var. grandidens

Plantago durvillei var. grandidens, commonly known as the Grandidens plantain, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a rough, hairy texture and produces small, greenish-white flowers in a dense spike. Its fibrous root system is adapted to retain moisture in dry conditions.

This species is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
