Peperomia sumichrastii

Peperomia sumichrastii, commonly known as the Mexican peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to Mexico.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, often with a waxy texture and vibrant green or reddish hues. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, usually green or red, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It is well adapted to dry conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance requirements.

Peperomia sumichrastii thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
