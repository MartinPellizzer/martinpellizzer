Peperomia kuhliana

Peperomia kuhliana, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features thick, waxy, and velvety leaves that are typically deep green with a slight sheen, giving it a luxurious appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, which are not particularly showy. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia kuhliana is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to adapt to a variety of indoor conditions.
