Pennisetum germanicum

Pennisetum germanicum, commonly known as German fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces fluffy, silver-gray plumes that are highly ornamental and attract birds and insects. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Pennisetum germanicum is drought-tolerant and low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
