Periandra mediterranea var. mucronata

Periandra mediterranea var. mucronata, commonly known as the Mediterranean periandra, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in early summer. Its fruits are small, dry capsules that contain several seeds.

This species is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and semi-arid habitats.
