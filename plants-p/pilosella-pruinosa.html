Pilosella pruinosa

Pilosella pruinosa, commonly known as the woolly cat's-paw, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant has hairy, lobed leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

Pilosella pruinosa is often found in disturbed areas and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
