Pterophylla hooglandii

Pterophylla hooglandii, commonly known as the Cape gold plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features striking golden-yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding vibrant color to its environment. The plant has fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, known for its diverse and often ornamental species.

Pterophylla hooglandii is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and drought tolerance.
