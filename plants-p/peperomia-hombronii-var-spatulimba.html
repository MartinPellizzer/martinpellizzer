Peperomia hombronii var. spatulimba

Peperomia hombronii var. spatulimba, commonly known as the broad-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically ovate to broad-ovate in shape, with a smooth or slightly serrated margin. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are often surrounded by specialized bracts. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a versatile plant for both decorative and low-maintenance settings.
