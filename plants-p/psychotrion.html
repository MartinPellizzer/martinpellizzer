Psychotrion

Psychotrion, commonly known as the moonflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that open at night and close by dawn. The plant belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is known for its fragrant scent. Psychotrion thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological studies.
