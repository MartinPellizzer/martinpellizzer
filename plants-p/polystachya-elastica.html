Polystachya elastica

Polystachya elastica, commonly known as the rubber plant, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are elongated and strap-like. It produces striking orange and yellow flowers in terminal spikes.

Polystachya elastica is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
