Pleurothallis ciliilabia

Pleurothallis ciliilabia, commonly known as the hairy-labied pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering stems. Its leaves are usually elliptical and covered in a fine, hairy texture, giving it its common name.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of rainforests.
