Pelecynthis diffusa

Pelecynthis diffusa, commonly known as the spreading pelecynthis, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Pelecynthis diffusa is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and drought tolerance.
