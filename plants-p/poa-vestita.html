Poa vestita

Poa vestita, commonly known as the dressed grass, is a species of grass native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are narrow and often have a bluish-gray hue, which helps it to retain moisture.

Poa vestita is also known for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its attractive appearance.
