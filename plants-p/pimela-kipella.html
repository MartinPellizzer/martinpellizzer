Pimela kipella

Pimela kipella, commonly known as the African water lily, is a aquatic plant native to parts of Africa.

It typically grows in still or slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and lakes. The plant has broad, floating leaves that provide shade and support for aquatic life. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, and they bloom in the early morning.

Pimela kipella plays an important role in its ecosystem by providing habitat and contributing to water filtration.
