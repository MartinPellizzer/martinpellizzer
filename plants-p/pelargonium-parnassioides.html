Pelargonium parnassioides

Pelargonium parnassioides, commonly known as the Parnassian geranium, is a low-growing perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and small, delicate flowers that range from white to pale pink. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

It is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
