Pleurothallis dura

Pleurothallis dura, commonly known as the hard pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often arranged in a raceme. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil for optimal growth.

Due to its delicate appearance and specific care requirements, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
