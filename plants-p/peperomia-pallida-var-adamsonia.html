Peperomia pallida var. adamsonia

Peperomia pallida var. adamsonia, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features succulent, ovate leaves that are typically pale green with a silvery or white underside, giving it a distinctive, almost metallic appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green or red, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for decorative purposes in both home and office environments.
