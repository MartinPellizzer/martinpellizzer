Prunus jamasakura f. aeruginosa

Prunus jamasakura f. aeruginosa, commonly known as the Azure Cherry, is a rare and striking variant of the Japanese cherry blossom.

This cultivar is characterized by its unique bluish-pink flowers, which result from a genetic mutation affecting pigmentation. The plant is a deciduous tree that typically grows to a moderate height, with slender branches and delicate foliage. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The Azure Cherry is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often planted in gardens and public spaces.
