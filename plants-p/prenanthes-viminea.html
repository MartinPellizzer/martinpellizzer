Prenanthes viminea

Prenanthes viminea, commonly known as slender cowbane, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant has thin, ribbed stems that are often reddish in color, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a slightly hairy texture. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in umbels and bloom in late summer.
