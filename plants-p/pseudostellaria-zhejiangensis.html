Pseudostellaria zhejiangensis

Pseudostellaria zhejiangensis, commonly known as Zhejiang starwort, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in the spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stems.

This species is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky areas.
