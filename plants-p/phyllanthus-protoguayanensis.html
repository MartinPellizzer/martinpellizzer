Phyllanthus protoguayanensis

Phyllanthus protoguayanensis, commonly known as the Guianan phyllanthus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Phyllanthaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly found in countries such as Guyana, Venezuela, and Brazil. The plant typically grows in moist, tropical environments and is often found in lowland rainforests. It features small, green leaves with a rough texture and produces tiny yellow flowers.

The plant is noted for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional remedies for various ailments.
