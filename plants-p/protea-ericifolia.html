Protea ericifolia

Protea ericifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf protea, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces showy, cone-like inflorescences that range in color from white to pink, depending on the variety. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Protea ericifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also valued in traditional medicine.
