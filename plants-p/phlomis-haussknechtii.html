Phlomis haussknechtii

Phlomis haussknechtii, commonly known as the Hausknecht's globe thistle, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems and spherical clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a prized addition to botanical collections.
