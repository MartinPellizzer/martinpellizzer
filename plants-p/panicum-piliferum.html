Panicum piliferum

Panicum piliferum, commonly known as sand dropseed, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and fine, thread-like leaves. The plant produces small, fluffy panicles of seeds that are easily dispersed by the wind. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open, disturbed areas.

Sand dropseed is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
