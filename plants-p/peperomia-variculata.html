Peperomia variculata

Peperomia variculata, commonly known as the variegated peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, glossy, oval-shaped leaves with striking variegation of white, yellow, and green patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, emerging from the leaf axils. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia variculata is valued for its ornamental foliage and air-purifying qualities.
