Persea Shweliensis

Persea shweliensis is a rare and lesser-known species of laurel tree native to the mountainous regions of Myanmar. This plant is characterized by its glossy dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in dense tropical forests. Due to its limited distribution and habitat destruction, it is considered a vulnerable species. Its unique botanical features make it an important subject for ecological and botanical research.
