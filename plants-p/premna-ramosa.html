Premna ramosa

Premna ramosa, commonly known as the hairy premna, is a deciduous shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. Its small, tubular flowers are typically white or pale blue and are arranged in clusters.

Premna ramosa is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
