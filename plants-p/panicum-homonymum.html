Panicum homonymum

Panicum homonymum, commonly known as the red panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, spreading growth habit and its distinctive reddish tinge in the foliage. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.

Its botanical features make it a common ornamental and forage plant in certain regions.
