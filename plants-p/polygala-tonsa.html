Polygala tonsa

Polygala tonsa, commonly known as the slender milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, including China and the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish in color, and it produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds.

Polygala tonsa is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
