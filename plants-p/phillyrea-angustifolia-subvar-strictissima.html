Phillyrea angustifolia subvar. strictissima

Phillyrea angustifolia subvar. strictissima, commonly known as the narrowleaf Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, evergreen leaves that are typically arranged in a dense, compact form. The plant produces small, white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring. Its bark is smooth and often peels to reveal a mottled pattern.

It is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.
