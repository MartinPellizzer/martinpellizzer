Pomaderris argyrophylla subsp. graniticola

Pomaderris argyrophylla subsp.

graniticola, commonly known as the granite pomaderris, is a shrub native to southern Australia. It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often found in dry, sunny environments. Its small, white flowers appear in spring, adding ornamental value.

It is well adapted to arid conditions and requires minimal water once established.
