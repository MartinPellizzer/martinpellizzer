Panicum macranthum

Panicum macranthum, commonly known as little bluestem, is a warm-season grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage that turns coppery in the fall. The plant produces fluffy, silver-gray seed heads that are visually striking and attract wildlife. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for native plant gardens.

Panicum macranthum is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing habitat and food for various insects and birds.
