Phyllostachys quilioi var. castillonii

Phyllostachys quilioi var. castillonii, commonly known as the Castillon's bamboo, is a variant of the slender bamboo species native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, culm-like stems that are typically green and smooth, with a tendency to bend and twist. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellowish-green flowers, which are rarely seen. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often slightly curled at the edges.

This bamboo is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
