Phyllocyclus lucidissimus

Phyllocyclus lucidissimus, commonly known as the shining leaf curler, is a species of plant belonging to the family Phyllocyclaceae.

It is characterized by its small, smooth, and shiny leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellow in color. It is native to certain regions of Australia and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
