Pyrus errata

Pyrus errata, commonly known as the Japanese whitebeam, is a deciduous tree native to Japan.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its small, white flowers and oval-shaped leaves. The tree produces small, edible fruits that are typically yellow or orange in color. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and seasonal interest.

Pyrus errata is relatively hardy and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
