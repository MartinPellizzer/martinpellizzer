Piper mercedense

Piper mercedense, commonly known as the Mercedense pepper plant, is a species of flowering vine in the Piper genus.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when mature, and these berries contain the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with a smooth texture and a mild peppery scent.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in local herbal remedies.
