Phyllanthus nanogynus

Phyllanthus nanogynus, commonly known as the small female plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Phyllanthaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its small, smooth, evergreen leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in shaded, moist environments.

Phyllanthus nanogynus is also known for its potential medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
