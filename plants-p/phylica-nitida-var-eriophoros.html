Phylica nitida var. eriophoros

Phylica nitida var. eriophoros, commonly known as the silver-leafed phylica, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and Mediterranean climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
