Pelargonium lanuginosum

Pelargonium lanuginosum, commonly known as the woolly geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves that give it a fuzzy appearance. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
