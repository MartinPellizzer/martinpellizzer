Peperomia reineckei var. obovatifolia

Peperomia reineckei var. obovatifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
