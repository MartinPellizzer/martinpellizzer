Pterostylis stenochila

Pterostylis stenochila, commonly known as the narrow-lobed duck orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a dense spike. The plant has a fibrous root system and produces a single, erect stem with few leaves. It blooms during the autumn season, often between August and November.

This orchid is adapted to grow in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys.
