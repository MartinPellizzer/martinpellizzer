Pellionia chikushiensis

Pellionia chikushiensis, commonly known as the Chikushi Pellionia, is a herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a ground cover in shaded gardens.
