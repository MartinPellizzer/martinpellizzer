Pavonia kleinii

Pavonia kleinii, commonly known as the red pavonia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or pink bracts that surround its small, white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, and is known for its dense, compact growth habit. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface.

Pavonia kleinii is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
