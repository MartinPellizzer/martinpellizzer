Parinari asperula

Parinari asperula, commonly known as the rough parinari, is a tropical tree belonging to the Sapotaceae family.

It is native to regions of Africa, particularly in countries such as Nigeria and Uganda. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape with a rough texture. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, typically appearing in yellow or greenish-yellow hues.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds, contributing to its role in local ecosystems.
