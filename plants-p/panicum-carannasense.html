Panicum carannasense

Panicum carannasense, commonly known as the Carannasense grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets that are typically clustered in dense, cylindrical formations.

Panicum carannasense is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in full sun conditions.
