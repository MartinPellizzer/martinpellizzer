Polygala chloroptera

Polygala chloroptera, commonly known as the green-winged milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are alternate and pinnate, with small, oval-shaped leaflets.
