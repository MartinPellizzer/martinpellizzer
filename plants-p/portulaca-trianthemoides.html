Portulaca trianthemoides

Portulaca trianthemoides, commonly known as the rock purslane, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing, succulent species that thrives in arid and rocky environments. The plant features fleshy, rounded leaves and produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in the morning. It is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.

Portulaca trianthemoides is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
