Pityrogramma tartarea var. aurata

Pityrogramma tartarea var. aurata, commonly known as the golden shield fern, is a variant of the shield fern genus Pityrogramma.

It is a small, evergreen fern native to tropical and subtropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features delicate, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are typically bright green with a distinctive golden or yellowish hue on the underside. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and has a rhizomatous growth habit.

The fronds are usually pinnately compound, with a unique, shield-like appearance that contributes to its ornamental value.
