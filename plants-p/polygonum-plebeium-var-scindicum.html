Polygonum plebeium var. scindicum

Polygonum plebeium var. scindicum, commonly known as narrowleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, pond edges, and stream banks. The plant has alternate, lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink or reddish, appearing in late summer to early fall.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various insects and wildlife.
