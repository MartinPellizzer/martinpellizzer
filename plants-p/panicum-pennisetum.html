Panicum pennisetum

Panicum pennisetum, commonly known as bloodgrass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red to purple foliage, which becomes more intense as the plant matures. The plant produces fluffy, silver-white seed heads that add visual interest in the fall. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its drought tolerance and ability to attract wildlife further enhance its value in landscaping.
