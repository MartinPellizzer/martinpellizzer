Phaseolus megatylus

Phaseolus megatylus, commonly known as the giant scarlet runner bean, is a species of legume native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, vibrant red flowers and long, bean-like pods that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant is a climbing vine with compound leaves and is often cultivated for its edible seeds. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The botanical features of Phaseolus megatylus make it a notable species within the Phaseolus genus.
