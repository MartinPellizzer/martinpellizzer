Passerina burchellii

Passerina burchellii, commonly known as the Burchell's pasqueflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in spring. Passerina burchellii is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant yellow-orange blooms.
