Parathesis macronema

Parathesis macronema, commonly known as the broad-leafed parathesis, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
