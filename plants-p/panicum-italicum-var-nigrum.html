Panicum italicum var. nigrum

Panicum italicum var. nigrum, commonly known as black panicum, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, erect stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height, often with a reddish or purplish tinge. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny, dark-colored grains. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental and ecological landscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ability to spread via seeds contribute to its adaptability in various environments.
