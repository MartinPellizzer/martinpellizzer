Phalangium favosum

Phalangium favosum, commonly known as the favosum millipede, is a species of millipede belonging to the order Polydesmida.

It is characterized by its elongated, segmented body and numerous legs, typical of millipedes. The exoskeleton is usually dark in color, providing protection against predators and environmental stressors. This species is often found in moist, forested habitats where it plays a role in decomposing organic matter.

Its botanical significance lies in its contribution to soil health and nutrient cycling in its ecosystem.
