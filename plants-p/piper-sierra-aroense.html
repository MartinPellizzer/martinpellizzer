Piper sierra-aroense

Piper sierra-aroense, commonly known as the Sierra Aroense pepper plant, is a species native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with opposite, ovate leaves that have a glossy surface. Its small, greenish-white flowers are followed by elongated, spindle-shaped berries that turn red when mature.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
