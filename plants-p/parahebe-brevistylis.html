Parahebe brevistylis

Parahebe brevistylis, commonly known as the broad-leaved parahebe, is a perennial shrub native to New Zealand.

It features dense, compact growth with glossy, dark green leaves that are broad and oval in shape. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue or purple, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Parahebe brevistylis is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
