Pancratium carolinianum

Pancratium carolinianum, commonly known as the Carolina yellow rain lily, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces striking yellow flowers with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant has strap-like leaves and thrives in well-drained sandy soils. It is often found in coastal dunes and open woodlands.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
