Paracynoglossum imeretinum

Paracynoglossum imeretinum, commonly known as the Imereti bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or mountainous habitats. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
