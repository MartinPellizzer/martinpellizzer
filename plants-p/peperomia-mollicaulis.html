Peperomia mollicaulis

Peperomia mollicaulis, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically deep green with a velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia mollicaulis is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
