Paphiopedilum schoseri

Paphiopedilum schoseri, commonly known as the Schoser's lady's slipper, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its unique and striking flower structure, featuring a pouch-like lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid, thriving in shaded, moist environments with well-drained soil. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a subtle fragrance that attracts specific pollinators.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, Paphiopedilum schoseri is highly sought after in the horticultural trade and is often protected in its natural range.
