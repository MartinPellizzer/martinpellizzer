Pleroma Oleifolium

Pleroma oleifolium is a rare and fascinating plant native to the arid regions of central Australia. It thrives in harsh, dry environments where few other species can survive. The plant is known for its unique, waxy leaves that help reduce water loss in extreme conditions. Its flowers, which bloom in late spring, are small but striking, often appearing in clusters of bright yellow. Despite its resilience, Pleroma oleifolium faces threats from habitat destruction and climate change.
