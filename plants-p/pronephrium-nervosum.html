Pronephrium nervosum

Pronephrium nervosum, commonly known as the slender kidney fern, is a species of fern characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping underground stems that produce new fronds. Its leaves are bipinnate, with a finely divided structure that enhances its ornamental appeal.
