Phreatia perpusilla

Phreatia perpusilla, commonly known as the dwarf phreatia, is a small epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on tree trunks or branches in humid, shaded forest environments. The plant has a compact rosette of dark green, leathery leaves that help retain moisture. Its flowers are small and fragrant, often appearing in clusters during the rainy season.

Phreatia perpusilla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
