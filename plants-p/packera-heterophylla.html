Packera heterophylla

Packera heterophylla, commonly known as the alpine aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. Its showy yellow flowers bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
