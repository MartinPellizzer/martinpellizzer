Prostanthera athertoniana

Prostanthera athertoniana, commonly known as Atherton mintbush, is a small shrub native to Queensland, Australia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically pink to purple in color, arranged in dense spikes. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, giving them a soft, velvety texture.

Prostanthera athertoniana is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to thrive in dry, sunny conditions.
