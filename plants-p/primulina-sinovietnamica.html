Primulina sinovietnamica

Primulina sinovietnamica, commonly known as the Vietnamese primrose, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, tubular flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often glossy and arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some regions as a decorative plant.
