Polygala dependens

Polygala dependens, commonly known as the hanging milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, delicate flowers that often hang downward. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers with prominent, thread-like filaments that give it a unique appearance. It thrives in arid or semi-arid environments and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
