Peperomia ciliifolia

Peperomia ciliifolia, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive reddish or purple hue on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable ornamental plant.
