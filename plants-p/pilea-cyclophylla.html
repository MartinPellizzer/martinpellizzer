Pilea cyclophylla

Pilea cyclophylla, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are round and slightly cup-shaped, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular indoor plant.

Its ability to produce offsets makes it easy to propagate and maintain.
