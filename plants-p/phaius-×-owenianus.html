Phaius × owenianus

Phaius × owenianus, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Phaius flavens and Phaius tankervillii.

It is a herbaceous perennial orchid native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia. The plant features large, showy, dark green leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The botanical characteristics of Phaius × owenianus make it a popular choice for tropical gardens and greenhouse displays.
