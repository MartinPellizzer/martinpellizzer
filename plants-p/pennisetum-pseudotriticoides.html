Pennisetum pseudotriticoides

Pennisetum pseudotriticoides, commonly known as blue beard, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its tufted clumps of stiff, silver-blue foliage. The plant produces panicles of small, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements contribute to its widespread cultivation.
