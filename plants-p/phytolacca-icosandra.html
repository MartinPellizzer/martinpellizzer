Phytolacca icosandra

Phytolacca icosandra, commonly known as the Chinese Pokeweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features tall, erect stems with large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers followed by bright red, fleshy berries. It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is known for its latex-containing sap, which can be toxic.
