Peperomia papulata

Peperomia papulata, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental purposes in both gardens and homes.
