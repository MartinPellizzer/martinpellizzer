Podochilus celebicus

Podochilus celebicus, commonly known as the Celebes orchid, is a tropical plant native to the Indonesian archipelago.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually borne in clusters and can vary in color from white to pink and purple.

Podochilus celebicus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
