Phyllostachys monaii

Phyllostachys monaii, commonly known as the Japanese bamboo or monai bamboo, is a species of grass in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its slender, green culms that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green, providing an ornamental appearance in gardens. It is often cultivated for its aesthetic value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.

Phyllostachys monaii is also valued for its use in traditional crafts and as a decorative plant in landscaping.
