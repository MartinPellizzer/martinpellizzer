Panicum hasskarlii

Panicum hasskarlii, commonly known as the Hasskarli grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, gray-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces panicles of small, fluffy flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often used in xeriscaping and native plant gardens.
