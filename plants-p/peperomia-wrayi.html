Peperomia wrayi

Peperomia wrayi, commonly known as the Wray's peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves that are often adorned with subtle silver or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale green, which are typically hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia wrayi is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
