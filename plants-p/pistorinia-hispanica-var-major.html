Pistorinia hispanica var. major

Pistorinia hispanica var. major, commonly known as the Spanish pistorinia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or white in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
