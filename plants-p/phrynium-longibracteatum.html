Phrynium longibracteatum

Phrynium longibracteatum, commonly known as the long bract phrynium, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by spiny, elongated fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor landscaping.
