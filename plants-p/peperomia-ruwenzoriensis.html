Peperomia ruwenzoriensis

Peperomia ruwenzoriensis, commonly known as the Ruwenzori peperomia, is a species native to the mountainous regions of East Africa.

It is a compact, evergreen perennial shrub with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically oval to lance-shaped. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, often in shades of white or pale green. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
