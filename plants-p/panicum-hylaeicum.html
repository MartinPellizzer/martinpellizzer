Panicum hylaeicum

Panicum hylaeicum, commonly known as the Hylaeic grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, fluffy panicles of seeds that are dispersed by the wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry, open habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated as a ornamental or for erosion control.
