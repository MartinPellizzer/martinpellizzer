Pleione purpurascens

Pleione purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-flowered pleione, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, including China, India, and Nepal. This terrestrial orchid produces striking violet to purple flowers with a distinctive, elongated shape. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments at high altitudes.

Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
