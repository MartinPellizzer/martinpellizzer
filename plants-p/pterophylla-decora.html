Pterophylla decora

Pterophylla decora, commonly known as the decorative fern, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, feathery fronds that grow in a cascading manner, creating an elegant, ornamental appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining, nutrient-rich soil. Its leaves are typically light green with a slightly serrated edge, adding to its visual appeal.

Pterophylla decora is often cultivated as a houseplant or in outdoor gardens for its aesthetic value and air-purifying qualities.
