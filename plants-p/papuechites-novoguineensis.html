Papuechites novoguineensis

Papuechites novoguineensis, commonly known as the New Guinea flame tree, is a tropical tree native to the rainforests of New Guinea.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a striking visual display. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It is a member of the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and has a woody stem with a smooth, pale bark.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
