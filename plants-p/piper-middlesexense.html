Piper middlesexense

Piper middlesexense, commonly known as Middlesex pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its trailing vines and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its fruit, which resembles a tiny pepper, is typically green when immature and turns red when mature.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and unique fruiting habit.
