Parkia ulei var. ulei

Parkia ulei var. ulei, commonly known as the African sycamore or African ironwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and compound leaves with three to five leaflets. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers followed by flat, pod-like fruits containing edible seeds. It is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often used in construction and furniture-making.

The plant also plays an important ecological role in providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
