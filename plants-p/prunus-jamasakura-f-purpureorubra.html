Prunus jamasakura f. purpureorubra

Prunus jamasakura f. purpureorubra, commonly known as the Japanese cherry blossom with a purple-red bloom, is a cultivar of the Prunus jamasakura species.

It is characterized by its striking, deep pink to purplish-red flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant is a deciduous shrub or small tree, typically reaching heights of 5 to 10 meters. Its leaves are oval-shaped with a smooth texture and a glossy green color.

The botanical features of this cultivar make it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and parks, particularly in Japan and other regions with temperate climates.
