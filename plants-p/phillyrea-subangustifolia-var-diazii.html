Phillyrea subangustifolia var. diazii

Phillyrea subangustifolia var. diazii, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and slightly leathery in texture. The plant produces white, fragrant flowers in clusters during the spring, which are followed by small, woody fruits. It is often found in rocky or dry soils and is adapted to coastal environments.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and resilience in harsh conditions.
