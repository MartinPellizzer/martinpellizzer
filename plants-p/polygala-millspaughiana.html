Polygala millspaughiana

Polygala millspaughiana, commonly known as the millspaugh's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae, which is characterized by its unique floral structures and often showy blooms.
