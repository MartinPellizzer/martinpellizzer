Pelargonium urbanum

Pelargonium urbanum, commonly known as the urban geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The leaves are typically rounded or heart-shaped, with a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

This plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
