Pavetta gracillima

Pavetta gracillima, commonly known as the slender pavetta, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. The plant features small, elliptic leaves with smooth margins and a dense, fragrant inflorescence. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with five petals and numerous stamens.

Pavetta gracillima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
