Pelargonium × inscriptum

Pelargonium × inscriptum, commonly known as the variegated geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking variegated leaves, which often feature white, yellow, or cream markings against a dark green background. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, depending on the cultivar. It is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight.

Pelargonium × inscriptum is widely cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
