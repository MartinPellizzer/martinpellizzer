Polygala chinensis var. stenosepala

Polygala chinensis var. stenosepala, commonly known as the narrow-sepaled milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are alternate and linear, while the flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive, spurred structure.

It is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
