Piper trukense

Piper trukense, commonly known as the Truk pepper, is a flowering plant native to the Caroline Islands.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. Its fruit is a small, elongated berry that turns red when mature.

The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental and culinary value.
