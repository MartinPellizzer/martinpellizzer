Pyrethrum gracillimum

Pyrethrum gracillimum, commonly known as slender pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its delicate, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant produces small, spiny seed heads that contain fine, hair-like structures called trichomes, which are the source of its natural insecticidal properties. Pyrethrum gracillimum thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its botanical features make it both aesthetically pleasing and ecologically beneficial.
