Peperomia areolata

Peperomia areolata, commonly known as the Spotted Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features oval, waxy leaves with a distinctive pattern of raised, rounded spots that give it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, followed by small, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia areolata is relatively low maintenance and suitable for beginners due to its drought tolerance and ease of care.
