Prismatocarpus speculum-veneris

Prismatocarpus speculum-veneris, commonly known as the Venus's Looking Glass tree, is a member of the Sapotaceae family.

It is characterized by its unique, translucent, waxy leaves that resemble glass, giving it its common name. The plant is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia, where it thrives in tropical rainforests. Its fruit is a small, spiny capsule that contains edible seeds.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
