Peperomia huahinensis var. tahaensis

Peperomia huahinensis var. tahaensis, commonly known as the Tahaenese Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, ovate to lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, often green or reddish, which are followed by elongated, cylindrical berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Due to its ornamental foliage and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for collectors and indoor gardeners.
