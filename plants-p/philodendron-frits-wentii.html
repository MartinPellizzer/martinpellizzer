Philodendron frits-wentii

Philodendron frits-wentii, commonly known as the Frits Wentii Philodendron, is a climbing plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant has a striking appearance with its dark green foliage and prominent veins. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Philodendron frits-wentii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
