Peperomia parnassiifolia

Peperomia parnassiifolia, commonly known as the Parnassiifolia Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical with a waxy texture and a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and white, which are typically followed by bright red berries. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal watering needs.

Its unique leaf shape and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental purposes.
