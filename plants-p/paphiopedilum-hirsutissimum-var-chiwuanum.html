Paphiopedilum hirsutissimum var. chiwuanum

Paphiopedilum hirsutissimum var. chiwuanum, commonly known as the Chiwanum orchid, is a rare and striking member of the orchid family native to China.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green to purple-hued leaves and its unique, showy flowers that resemble a pouch with a distinctive lip. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish-white blooms with a deep red or maroon throat, making it highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and beauty, it is often cultivated in conservation programs to ensure its survival.
