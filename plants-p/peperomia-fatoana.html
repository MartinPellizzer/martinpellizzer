Peperomia fatoana

Peperomia fatoana, commonly known as the Fatana Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically green with reddish veins, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia fatoana is relatively low-maintenance and appreciated for its ornamental foliage.
