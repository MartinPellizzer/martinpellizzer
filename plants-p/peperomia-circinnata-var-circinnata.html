Peperomia circinnata var. circinnata

Peperomia circinnata var. circinnata, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, dark green leaves with a velvety texture, often adorned with reddish or purple veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, which are not showy but contribute to its botanical interest. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for tabletop displays and as a decorative plant in home environments.
