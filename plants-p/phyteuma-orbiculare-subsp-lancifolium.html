Phyteuma orbiculare subsp. lancifolium

Phyteuma orbiculare subsp.

lancifolium, commonly known as the lanceleaf phyteuma, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late summer.

The plant has lanceolate leaves that are typically hairy and arranged in a basal rosette. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun, often found in grassy habitats and rocky areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
