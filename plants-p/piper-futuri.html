Piper futuri

Piper futuri, commonly known as the future pepper, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when mature, and they are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits. Its leaves are also used in culinary applications, adding a spicy flavor to dishes.

However, due to its rarity and limited research, much about its botanical characteristics remains unknown.
