Polycarpaea corymbosa var. eriantha

Polycarpaea corymbosa var. eriantha, commonly known as the woolly corymbosa, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its unique texture and ornamental value make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
