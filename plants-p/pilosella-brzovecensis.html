Pilosella brzovecensis

Pilosella brzovecensis, commonly known as the Brzovec bellflower, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. Pilosella brzovecensis produces showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
