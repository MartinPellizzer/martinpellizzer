Pedilochilus grandifolius

Pedilochilus grandifolius, commonly known as the large-leaved pedilochilus, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some horticultural collections.
