Polystachya cornigera var. integrilabia

Polystachya cornigera var. integrilabia, commonly known as the smooth-labied rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or shaded forest environments, where it thrives in moist, well-drained soil. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a few elongated, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are clustered in a raceme, with showy, pale yellow to greenish-white petals and sepals.

The variety integrilabia is distinguished by its smooth, uninterrupted labellum, which sets it apart from other varieties within the species.
