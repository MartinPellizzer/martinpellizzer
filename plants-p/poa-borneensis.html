Poa borneensis

Poa borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean meadowgrass, is a species of grass native to the island of Borneo.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, erect culms. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and blade-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Poa borneensis plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity in its native range.
