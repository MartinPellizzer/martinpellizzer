Parodia oxycostata subsp. gracilis

Parodia oxycostata subsp.

gracilis, commonly known as the slender pincushion cactus, is a compact, globular cactus with a distinctive appearance. It features a sparsely branched stem that is typically pale green to blue-gray in color.

The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers during the warmer months. It is native to southern Africa and thrives in well-drained soil with minimal water.

Due to its slow growth and unique form, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
