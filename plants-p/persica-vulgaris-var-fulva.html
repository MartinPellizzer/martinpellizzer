Persica vulgaris var. fulva

Persica vulgaris var. fulva, commonly known as the yellow peach.

This variant is a cultivar of the peach tree characterized by its vibrant yellow fruit. The flowers are typically white with a subtle pinkish tinge, and the leaves are broad and ovate. The fruit is usually smaller than the common peach, with a sweet, juicy flesh.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and unique flavor profile.
