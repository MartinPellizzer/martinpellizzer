Piper munyanum

Piper munyanum, commonly known as the mountain pepper plant, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Western Ghats in India.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant is characterized by its small, green, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of tiny white flowers. It produces small, berry-like fruits that are typically used in traditional medicine.

The botanical features of Piper munyanum make it an important species in the study of tropical flora and medicinal plants.
