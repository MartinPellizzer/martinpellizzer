Psydrax bathieanus

Psydrax bathieanus, commonly known as the African star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters, often releasing a strong, sweet scent at night. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters tall, with a dense, bushy habit.

The botanical features of Psydrax bathieanus make it a popular ornamental plant, especially in warm climates.
