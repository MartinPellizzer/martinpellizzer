Primula tosaensis var. tosaensis

Primula tosaensis var. tosaensis, commonly known as Tosa primrose, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and lavender. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded woodland areas.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and protected species.
