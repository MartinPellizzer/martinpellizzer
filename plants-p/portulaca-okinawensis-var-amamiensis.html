Portulaca okinawensis var. amamiensis

Portulaca okinawensis var. amamiensis, commonly known as the Amami sandmat, is a flowering plant native to the Amami Islands in Japan.

It is a low-growing annual herb with fleshy, succulent leaves and small, vibrant yellow flowers. The plant thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is adapted to coastal and arid environments. It produces numerous seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its propagation.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its unique adaptations and limited distribution.
