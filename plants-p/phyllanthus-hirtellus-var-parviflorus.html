Phyllanthus hirtellus var. parviflorus

Phyllanthus hirtellus var. parviflorus, commonly known as the small-flowered milkweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family, characterized by its milky sap and clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are usually opposite, ovate to elliptic in shape, and covered with fine hairs.
