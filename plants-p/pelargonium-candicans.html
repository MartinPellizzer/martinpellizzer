Pelargonium candicans

Pelargonium candicans, commonly known as the silver geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking silver-white leaves and clusters of pink to red flowers. The plant belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Its foliage and blooms provide both aesthetic appeal and attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
