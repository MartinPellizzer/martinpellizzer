Penstemon Caespitosus Var. Thompsoniae

Penstemon caespitosus var. thompsoniae is a striking evergreen perennial known for its vibrant blue to purple flowers. Native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for rock gardens and xeriscaping. The variety thompsoniae is particularly prized for its showy blooms and drought tolerance. This plant is a valuable addition to any landscape seeking both beauty and low maintenance.
