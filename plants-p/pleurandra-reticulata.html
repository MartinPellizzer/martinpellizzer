Pleurandra reticulata

Pleurandra reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined pleurandra, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinct net-like venation pattern. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family.

Pleurandra reticulata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique floral structure.
