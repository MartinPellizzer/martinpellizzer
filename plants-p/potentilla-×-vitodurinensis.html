Potentilla × vitodurinensis

Potentilla × vitodurinensis, commonly known as the alpine potentilla, is a hybrid shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage, often covered in fine, silver-white hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
