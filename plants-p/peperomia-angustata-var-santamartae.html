Peperomia angustata var. santamartae

Peperomia angustata var. santamartae, commonly known as the Santa Marta peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves with a slightly serrated margin, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink. The plant is characterized by its slow growth and ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

It prefers well-draining soil and moderate humidity, and it is relatively resistant to pests and diseases.
