Pilea portulacina

Pilea portulacina, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers, which are followed by round, fleshy fruits. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant.
