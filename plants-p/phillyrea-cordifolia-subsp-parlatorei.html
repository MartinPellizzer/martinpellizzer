Phillyrea cordifolia subsp. parlatorei

Phillyrea cordifolia subsp.

parlatorei, commonly known as the Parlatore's heartleaf Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are typically heart-shaped and have a leathery texture.

The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by small, fleshy berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural landscapes.
