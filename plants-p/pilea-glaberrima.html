Pilea glaberrima

Pilea glaberrima, commonly known as the silver pilea, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to Mexico.

It is a low-growing, evergreen perennial that features glossy, dark green leaves with a distinctive silvery underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically hidden beneath a leaf-like spathe. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Pilea glaberrima is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
