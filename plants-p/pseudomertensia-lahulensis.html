Pseudomertensia lahulensis

Pseudomertensia lahulensis, commonly known as Lahul Himalayan bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Boraginaceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and showy blue or violet flowers. Pseudomertensia lahulensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often found in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
