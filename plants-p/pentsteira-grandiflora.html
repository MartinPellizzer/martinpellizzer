Pentsteira grandiflora

Pentsteira grandiflora, commonly known as the giant starflower, is a strikingly beautiful plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its unique appearance. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts who appreciate its striking floral display.
