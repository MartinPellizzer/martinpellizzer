Piper longicaule

Piper longicaule, commonly known as the long-stemmed pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into elongated, cylindrical fruits.

The fruits are often used in traditional medicine and are known for their spicy flavor.
