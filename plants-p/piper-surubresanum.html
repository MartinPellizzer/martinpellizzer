Piper surubresanum

Piper surubresanum, commonly known as the Surubresan pepper, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with opposite, ovate leaves that have a glossy surface. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are followed by elongated, berry-like fruits that contain the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in local herbal remedies.
