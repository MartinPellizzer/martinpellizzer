Pycnostachys decussata

Pycnostachys decussata, commonly known as the silverleaf strobilanthes, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and showy clusters of small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are opposite and decussate, meaning they are arranged in pairs at right angles to each other.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique foliage structure.
