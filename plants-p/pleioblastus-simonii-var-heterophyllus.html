Pleioblastus simonii var. heterophyllus

Pleioblastus simonii var. heterophyllus, commonly known as the variegated bamboo, is a variety of the bamboo genus Pleioblastus.

It is characterized by its distinctive variegated foliage, which includes shades of green, yellow, and white. This plant is native to China and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. The leaves are long and narrow, typically growing in clusters along the stems.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
