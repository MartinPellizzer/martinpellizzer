Poecilochroma frondosa

Poecilochroma frondosa, commonly known as the frondose leaf fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that arise from a central rhizome. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture to flourish. Its leaves are typically elongated and have a soft, velvety texture.

Poecilochroma frondosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
