Pyrethrum uliginosum

Pyrethrum uliginosum, commonly known as bog chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to wetland areas of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils and is often found in marshy or swampy habitats. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, clustered flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Pyrethrum uliginosum is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
