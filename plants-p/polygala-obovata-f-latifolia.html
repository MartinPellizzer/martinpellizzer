Polygala obovata f. latifolia

Polygala obovata f. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved milkweed, is a variant of the polygala genus native to regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils. It is a perennial herb that produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local insect populations.
