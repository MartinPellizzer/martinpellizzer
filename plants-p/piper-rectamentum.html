Piper rectamentum

Piper rectamentum, commonly known as the bitter pepper vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers and oval, slightly bitter berries. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a rough texture.

The plant is characterized by its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
