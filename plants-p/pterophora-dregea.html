Pterophora dregea

Pterophora dregea, commonly known as the African grass moth, is a species of moth belonging to the family Pterophoridae.

It is characterized by its delicate, narrow wings that are often adorned with intricate patterns and colors. The plant is native to southern Africa, where it thrives in grassland and shrubland habitats. Its life cycle involves a larvae stage that feeds on grasses and other herbaceous plants.

Due to its ecological role, it contributes to the pollination and biodiversity of its native ecosystems.
