Prescottia orchioides

Prescottia orchioides, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple and white flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its unique floral structure includes a slender stem and a single, showy orchid-like bloom.

Prescottia orchioides is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
