Persicaria setosula

Persicaria setosula, commonly known as the hairy smartweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. It thrives in moist environments such as wetlands and disturbed soils.

Persicaria setosula is often found in wet meadows and along the edges of streams and ponds.
