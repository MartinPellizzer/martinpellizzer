Polystachya elatior

Polystachya elatior, commonly known as the tall spike orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa and the Indian Ocean islands.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of elongated, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous small, greenish-white flowers. Polystachya elatior thrives in moist, shady environments and requires well-drained soil.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
