Prunus mume f. splendens

Prunus mume f. splendens, commonly known as the pink ume or pink Japanese plum, is a cultivar of the Japanese apricot tree.

It is characterized by its striking pink to white flowers that bloom in early spring, often before the leaves appear. The plant is native to East Asia and is valued for its ornamental beauty in gardens and landscapes. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, requiring moderate water and care.

This variety is also appreciated for its edible fruit, which has a sweet and tart flavor.
