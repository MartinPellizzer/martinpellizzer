Philodendron ilsemanii

Philodendron ilsemanii, commonly known as the Ilsemanii philodendron, is a climbing vine native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or light green patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by berries, though it is more prized for its foliage than its blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Philodendron ilsemanii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
