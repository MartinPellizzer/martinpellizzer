Polycarpaea alsinifolia

Polycarpaea alsinifolia, commonly known as the slender everlasting pea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its typical bean-like pods and nitrogen-fixing abilities. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along coastal areas.
