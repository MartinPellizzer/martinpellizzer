Polygonum obtusifolium

Polygonum obtusifolium, commonly known as blunt-leaved smartweed, is a herbaceous annual plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its erect stems and alternate, ovate to elliptic leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in dense clusters at the tips of the branches. It thrives in moist environments such as wetlands, ditches, and along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in agricultural and disturbed areas.
