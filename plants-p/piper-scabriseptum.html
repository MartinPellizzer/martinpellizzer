Piper scabriseptum

Piper scabriseptum, commonly known as the rough-stemmed pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its rough, ridged stems and small, clustered leaves. Its fruits are peppery and used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in dense rainforests.
