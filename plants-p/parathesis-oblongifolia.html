Parathesis oblongifolia

Parathesis oblongifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed parathesis, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, oval-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil.

Parathesis oblongifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
