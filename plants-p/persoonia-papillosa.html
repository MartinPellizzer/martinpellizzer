Persoonia papillosa

Persoonia papillosa, commonly known as the silky pear.

It is an evergreen shrub native to Australia, characterized by its slender, wiry stems and clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Persoonia papillosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to attract native pollinators.
