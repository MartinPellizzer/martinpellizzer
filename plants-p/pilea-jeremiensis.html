Pilea jeremiensis

Pilea jeremiensis, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rosettes of thick, waxy, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in spring, followed by reddish-brown berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Its unique leaf shape and ease of care contribute to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
