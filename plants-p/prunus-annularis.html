Prunus annularis

Prunus annularis, commonly known as the ring cherry, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and produces small, white or pink flowers in spring. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive appearance. Prunus annularis prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

It is relatively drought-tolerant once established, making it suitable for coastal or rocky garden environments.
