Paepalanthus yapacanensis var. yapacanensis

Paepalanthus yapacanensis var. yapacanensis, commonly known as the Yapacanensis Burrobrush, is a flowering plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit.
