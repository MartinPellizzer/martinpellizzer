Protea prolifera

Protea prolifera, commonly known as the common protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences composed of numerous tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant is native to the Western and Eastern Cape provinces, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. It is often found in fynbos vegetation, contributing to the region's biodiversity.

Protea prolifera is also cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
