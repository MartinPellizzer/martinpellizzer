Paepalanthus aequalis

Paepalanthus aequalis, commonly known as the equal grass.

It is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in regions of Argentina and Chile. This species belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats and is adapted to withstand arid conditions.

Its small, inconspicuous flowers are usually clustered at the base of the plant.
