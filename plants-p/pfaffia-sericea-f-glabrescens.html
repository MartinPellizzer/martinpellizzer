Pfaffia sericea f. glabrescens

Pfaffia sericea f. glabrescens, commonly known as the silky partridge pea, is a variant of the partridge pea species native to tropical regions of the Americas.

This plant is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Fabaceae family.
