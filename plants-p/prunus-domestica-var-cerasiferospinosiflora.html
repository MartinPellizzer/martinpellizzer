Prunus domestica var. cerasiferospinosiflora

Prunus domestica var. cerasiferospinosiflora, commonly known as the spiny-flowered European plum, is a rare and ornamental variant of the European plum.

It is characterized by its thorny stems, showy pink to red flowers, and small, edible fruits. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens with adequate space.

Due to its rarity, it is often cultivated for its unique botanical features and ornamental value.
