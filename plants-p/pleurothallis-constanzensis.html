Pleurothallis constanzensis

Pleurothallis constanzensis, commonly known as the Constantz orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and green. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees without deriving nutrients from them.

Its intricate floral structure and specific habitat requirements make it a rare and specialized species within the Pleurothallis genus.
