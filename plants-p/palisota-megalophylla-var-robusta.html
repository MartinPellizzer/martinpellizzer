Palisota megalophylla var. robusta

Palisota megalophylla var. robusta, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a striking aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, arrowhead-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length, providing dense coverage in water. The plant thrives in slow-moving or still freshwater environments, often found in marshes and ponds. Its leaves are typically a vibrant green, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are rarely seen.

It is valued in aquascaping for its aesthetic appeal and ability to oxygenate water.
