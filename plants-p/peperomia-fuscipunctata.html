Peperomia fuscipunctata

Peperomia fuscipunctata, commonly known as the speckled peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features rounded, waxy leaves with distinctive dark spots, giving it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia fuscipunctata is ideal for indoor cultivation due to its adaptability and ease of care.
