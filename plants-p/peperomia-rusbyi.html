Peperomia rusbyi

Peperomia rusbyi, commonly known as the Rusby's peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically a vibrant green, often with a waxy texture and subtle variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color, arranged in tight clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia rusbyi is prized for its ornamental foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
