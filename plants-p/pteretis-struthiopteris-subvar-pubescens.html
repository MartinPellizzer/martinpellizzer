Pteretis struthiopteris subvar. pubescens

Pteretis struthiopteris subvar. pubescens, commonly known as the pubescent bracken, is a variant of the bracken fern that is characterized by its hairy or pubescent fronds.

It is a fern belonging to the family Pteridaceae and is native to certain regions of Asia, including Japan and China. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous areas.
