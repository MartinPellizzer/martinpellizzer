Potameia nitens

Potameia nitens, commonly known as the slender water fern, is a species of aquatic plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, thread-like fronds that float on the surface of still or slow-moving water. The plant typically grows in shallow, freshwater habitats such as ponds and marshes.
