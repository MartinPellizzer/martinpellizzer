Palanostigma martiana

Palanostigma martiana, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and rough, fissured bark that becomes more pronounced with age. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody, elongated pods containing flat, winged seeds. It is known for its hard, heavy wood, which is highly valued for construction and tool-making.

The plant also plays an important role in local ecosystems, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
