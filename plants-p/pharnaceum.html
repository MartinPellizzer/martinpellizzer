Pharnaceum

Pharnaceum, commonly known as the marsh hibiscus, is a genus of flowering plants in the mallow family, Malvaceae.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, red, or white. The genus includes several species, some of which are used in ornamental gardening due to their vibrant flowers and adaptability to wet conditions.
