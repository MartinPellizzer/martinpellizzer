Peperomia kauaiensis

Peperomia kauaiensis, commonly known as Kauai peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to the island of Kauai in Hawaii.

It is a small, compact shrub with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is adapted to the humid, tropical climate of its native habitat.

Peperomia kauaiensis is considered a rare and endemic species, making it a unique part of Hawaii's botanical diversity.
