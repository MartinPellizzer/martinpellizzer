Polygala linearis

Polygala linearis, commonly known as the slender milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny environments. The plant features slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers arranged in spikes. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Polygala linearis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
