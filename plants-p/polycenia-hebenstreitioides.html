Polycenia hebenstreitioides

Polycenia hebenstreitioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved polycenia, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance, it is popular among gardeners in regions with a Mediterranean climate.
