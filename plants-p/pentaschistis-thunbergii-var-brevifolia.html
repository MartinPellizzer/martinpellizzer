Pentaschistis thunbergii var. brevifolia

Pentaschistis thunbergii var. brevifolia, commonly known as the Japanese white wood, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It is a member of the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its small, white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically oval-shaped with a smooth margin. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its delicate flowers and compact growth habit.
