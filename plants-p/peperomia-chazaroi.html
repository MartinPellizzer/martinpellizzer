Peperomia chazaroi

Peperomia chazaroi, commonly known as the Chazaroi Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a distinctive silver or white underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green or red, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia chazaroi is a low-maintenance plant suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
