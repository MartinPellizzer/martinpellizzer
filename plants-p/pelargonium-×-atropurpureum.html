Pelargonium × atropurpureum

Pelargonium × atropurpureum, commonly known as the purple geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple to reddish foliage and showy flowers that range from pink to deep red. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with a compact habit, making it suitable for containers and gardens. Its leaves are often hairy and have a distinctive velvety texture.

Pelargonium × atropurpureum is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
