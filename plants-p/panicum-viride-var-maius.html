Panicum viride var. maius

Panicum viride var. maius, commonly known as green panicgrass, is a variant of the panic grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a perennial grass characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces panicles of small, greenish-white flowers that are often clustered at the tips of the stems. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it adaptable to various environmental conditions.

Its rapid growth and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for erosion control and ornamental landscaping.
