Poa mollis

Poa mollis, commonly known as soft brome, is a species of grass native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and slender, bluish-green leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Poa mollis is often used in turfgrass mixes and is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability.
