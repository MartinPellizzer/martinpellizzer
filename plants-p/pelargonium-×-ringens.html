Pelargonium × ringens

Pelargonium × ringens, commonly known as the angel's trumpets, is a hybrid species within the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant is native to South Africa and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It typically grows as an upright shrub with velvety, dark green leaves.

Pelargonium × ringens prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
