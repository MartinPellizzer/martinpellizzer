Pittosporum gracile

Pittosporum gracile, commonly known as the slender pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features dense, leathery foliage with a distinctive dark green color on the upper surface and a lighter, often silvery underside. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by spherical, orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

Pittosporum gracile is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping and conservation efforts.
