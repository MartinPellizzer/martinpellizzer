Phoberos gaertneri var. cordifolius

Phoberos gaertneri var. cordifolius, commonly known as the heart-leaf phoberos, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves, which are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and delicate floral display.
