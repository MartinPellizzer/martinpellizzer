Polychilos maculata

Polychilos maculata, commonly known as the spotted hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Polychilos maculata thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
