Pelargonium hibridifolium

Pelargonium hibridifolium, commonly known as the hybrid geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors such as red, pink, and white. The plant typically has palmate or lobed leaves, which are often hairy and provide a textured appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden use.

Pelargonium hibridifolium is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated for its attractive foliage and blooms.
