Polylobium debile

Polylobium debile, commonly known as the weakly lobed plant, is a rare and delicate species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves that are intricately lobed, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and fragile growth habits, Polylobium debile is considered a vulnerable species.
