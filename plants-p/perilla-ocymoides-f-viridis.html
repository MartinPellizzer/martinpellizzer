Perilla ocymoides f. viridis

Perilla ocymoides f. viridis, commonly known as green perilla, is a variant of the perilla plant characterized by its distinctive green foliage.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and ornamental plants. The leaves are typically broad and lance-shaped, with a slightly serrated edge. This cultivar is often cultivated for its ornamental value and mild, slightly peppery flavor.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for both garden and container planting.
