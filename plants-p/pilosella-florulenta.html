Pilosella florulenta

Pilosella florulenta, commonly known as the showy pearly everlasting, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features silver-gray, woolly leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open fields and meadows.

Pilosella florulenta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in dried flower arrangements.
