Persicaria lapathifolia f. alba

Persicaria lapathifolia f. alba, commonly known as white heartleaf dock, is a variant of the heartleaf dock species characterized by its pale white to cream-colored flowers.

This perennial plant belongs to the Polygonaceae family and is native to parts of Europe and Asia. It features heart-shaped leaves that are usually smooth and slightly succulent, with a bluish-green hue. The flowers are small and clustered in dense, upright spikes, which bloom in late summer.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodland edges and stream banks.
