Picea excelsa f. herjedalica

Picea excelsa f. herjedalica, commonly known as the Herjedal white spruce, is a rare and unique variant of the white spruce.

It is characterized by its dense, white-barked trunk and elongated, slender needles. This subspecies is native to specific regions in Scandinavia, particularly in Sweden. Its botanical features include a conical shape and a slow growth rate, making it a distinctive ornamental tree.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
