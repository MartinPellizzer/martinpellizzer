Peperomia apodophylla

Peperomia apodophylla, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Brazil.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically silver or gray-green in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are arranged in a rosette pattern and often have a velvety texture. This plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color.

It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its ability to thrive in low light and minimal water conditions.
