Pelargonium × ichorosum

Pelargonium × ichorosum, commonly known as the hybrid geranium, is a species that results from the crossbreeding of different geranium species.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that come in a variety of colors such as red, pink, and white. The plant typically has succulent, hairy leaves that are often aromatic. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Pelargonium × ichorosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
