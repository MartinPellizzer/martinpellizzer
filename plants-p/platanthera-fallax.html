Platanthera fallax

Platanthera fallax, commonly known as the slender lady's tresses, is a species of orchid native to the northeastern United States and southeastern Canada.

It is a perennial herb with a distinctive flowering stem that bears a dense, linear arrangement of white to pale green flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and wetlands. Its roots are mycorrhizal, forming symbiotic relationships with fungi to aid in nutrient uptake.

Platanthera fallax is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other orchid species in its native range.
