Pseuderanthemum loyaltyense

Pseuderanthemum loyaltyense, commonly known as the Loyalty Island pseuderanthemum, is a flowering plant native to the Loyalty Islands in French Polynesia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. Its flowers are small, clustered in whorls, and typically display shades of white and pink.
