Papaver pseudohaussknechtii

Papaver pseudohaussknechtii, commonly known as the false haussknecht's poppy, is a rare and striking member of the Papaveraceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant red to orange flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of white hairs.
