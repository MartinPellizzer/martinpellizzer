Pyrularia edulis

Pyrularia edulis, commonly known as the edible pyrularia, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into round, fleshy fruits containing edible seeds. Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and agricultural contexts.
