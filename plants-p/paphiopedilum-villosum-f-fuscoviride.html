Paphiopedilum villosum f. fuscoviride

Paphiopedilum villosum f. fuscoviride, commonly known as the dark green velvet orchid, is a rare and striking variant of the velvet orchid.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety foliage and unique, intricate flower structure. The plant is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by enthusiasts due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal.
