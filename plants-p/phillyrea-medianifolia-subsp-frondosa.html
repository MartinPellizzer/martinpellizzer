Phillyrea medianifolia subsp. frondosa

Phillyrea medianifolia subsp.

frondosa, commonly known as the broad-leaved Phillyrea, is a shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage with lance-shaped, leathery leaves that are typically broader than those of the typical subspecies.

The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in clusters, followed by woody, spherical fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas. Phillyrea medianifolia subsp.

frondosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
