Prunus nigra f. roseiflora

Prunus nigra f. roseiflora, commonly known as the dark cherry rose-flowered form, is a cultivar of the Prunus nigra species, which belongs to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black bark and its distinctive pink to rose-colored flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, typically reaching heights of 5 to 10 meters. Its leaves are ovate with a pointed tip and a smooth, dark green upper surface.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal, particularly for its striking floral display and unique bark texture.
