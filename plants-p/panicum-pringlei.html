Panicum pringlei

Panicum pringlei, commonly known as Pringle's grass, is a species of grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is a perennial plant characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, two-lobed spikelets. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue and a hairy texture.

Panicum pringlei is often found in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and along stream banks.
