Pimelea verschaffeltii

Pimelea verschaffeltii, commonly known as the yellow geebung, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in heathland and coastal regions, characterized by its compact, dense form. Pimelea verschaffeltii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and attractive blooms.
