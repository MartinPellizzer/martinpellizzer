Pedilonum verruculosum

Pedilonum verruculosum, commonly known as the warty pedilonum, is a unique flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive warty appearance on its stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its rare occurrence and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicate and less commonly studied species.
