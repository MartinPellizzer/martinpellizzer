Pleurothallis tantilla

Pleurothallis tantilla, commonly known as the Tantilla orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its small, intricate flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow with a distinctive, narrow spur.

Pleurothallis tantilla is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its delicate beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
