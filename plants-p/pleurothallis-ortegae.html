Pleurothallis ortegae

Pleurothallis ortegae, commonly known as the Ortegae's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the cloud forests of Central America.

This epiphytic plant typically grows on tree branches and thrives in humid, shaded environments. It produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and green, often with a subtle fragrance. The plant has narrow, elongated leaves that help it conserve moisture in its natural habitat.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
