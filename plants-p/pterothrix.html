Pterothrix

Pterothrix, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch, giving it its common name. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that are typically clustered in racemes. Its roots are fibrous and can become invasive in certain environments.
