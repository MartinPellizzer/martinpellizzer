Panicum ichunense

Panicum ichunense, commonly known as the Ichun grass, is a species of grass native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and along stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy texture.

Panicum ichunense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticultural settings.
