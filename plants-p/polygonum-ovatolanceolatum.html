Polygonum ovatolanceolatum

Polygonum ovatolanceolatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which is characterized by its distinctive nodes and often reddish stems. The plant features alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are typically smooth or slightly hairy. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink or red, and it produces small, three-lobed seeds.

It thrives in moist environments such as wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks.
