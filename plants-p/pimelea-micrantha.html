Pimelea micrantha

Pimelea micrantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pimelea, is a low-growing shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring, adding a vibrant touch to its natural habitat. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in heathlands and open forests.

Pimelea micrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and seasonal blooms.
