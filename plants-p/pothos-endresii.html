Pothos endresii

Pothos endresii, commonly known as the Endres' pothos, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers, though it is more admired for its foliage than its blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers indirect light.

Pothos endresii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
