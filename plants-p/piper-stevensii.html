Piper stevensii

Piper stevensii, commonly known as the Stevens' pepper vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by bright red berries, which are edible but have a peppery taste. Its leaves are typically ovate with a glossy surface and are often used in traditional medicine.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its potential medicinal properties.
