Paeonia × yananensis

Paeonia × yananensis, commonly known as the Yanan peony, is a hybrid species native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces large, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant is characterized by its thick, hairy stems and compound leaves with multiple leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

The Yanan peony is valued for its fragrance and is often cultivated for both its aesthetic appeal and medicinal properties.
