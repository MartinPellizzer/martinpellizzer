Pelargonium × wachendorfiae

Pelargonium × wachendorfiae, commonly known as the Wachendorfia geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink. The leaves are typically rounded and have a slightly serrated edge, often covered in fine hairs. Native to South Africa, this plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
