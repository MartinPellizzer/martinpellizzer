Philodendron tatei subsp. tatei

Philodendron tatei subsp.

tatei, commonly known as the Philodendron tatei, is a climbing perennial plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America. It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy green leaves that often have a slight waxy coating.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by orange-red berries, which are a feature of its reproductive biology. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical plant collections.
