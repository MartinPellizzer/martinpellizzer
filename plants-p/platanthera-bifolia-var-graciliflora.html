Platanthera bifolia var. graciliflora

Platanthera bifolia var. graciliflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered twayblade, is a rare orchid species native to certain regions of North America.

The plant is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forest floors and wetlands.
