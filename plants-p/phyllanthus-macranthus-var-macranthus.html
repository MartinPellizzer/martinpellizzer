Phyllanthus macranthus var. macranthus

Phyllanthus macranthus var. macranthus, commonly known as the large-flowered phyllanthus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and clusters of small yellowish-green flowers. It produces small, fleshy fruits that are often used in traditional medicine.
