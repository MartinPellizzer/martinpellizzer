Peperomia martiana var. nervosa

Peperomia martiana var. nervosa, commonly known as the veined peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with prominent, raised veins that give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for tabletop displays and as a decorative houseplant.
