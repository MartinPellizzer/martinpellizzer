Potentilla butandae

Potentilla butandae, commonly known as the Butan Himalayan cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is a low-growing shrub with woody stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has palmate leaves divided into five to seven lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in rocky and alpine environments, often found at high elevations.

Potentilla butandae is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
