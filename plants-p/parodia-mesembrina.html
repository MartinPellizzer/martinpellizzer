Parodia mesembrina

Parodia mesembrina, commonly known as the Evening Primrose, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that open in the evening, hence its common name. The plant produces small, round, green fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Parodia mesembrina is also valued for its medicinal properties, particularly for its oil-rich seeds.
