Pentacme siamensis var. laevis

Pentacme siamensis var. laevis, commonly known as the smooth pentacme, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a glossy surface that reflects light.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
