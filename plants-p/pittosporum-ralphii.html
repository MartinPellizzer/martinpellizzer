Pittosporum ralphii

Pittosporum ralphii, commonly known as the New Zealand pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are elliptical, leathery, and have a smooth texture with a glossy appearance.

Pittosporum ralphii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.
