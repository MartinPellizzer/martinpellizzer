Peperomia spiritus-sancti

Peperomia spiritus-sancti, commonly known as the Holy Spirit Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
