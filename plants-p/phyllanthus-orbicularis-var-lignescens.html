Phyllanthus orbicularis var. lignescens

Phyllanthus orbicularis var. lignescens, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, woody stems and dark green, orbicular-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in traditional medicine and as a ornamental plant.
