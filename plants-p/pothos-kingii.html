Pothos kingii

Pothos kingii, commonly known as the marble queen pothos, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are variegated with light green and white patterns. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers warm, humid conditions. Its aerial roots allow it to climb and attach to surfaces, making it an excellent choice for hanging baskets or shelves.

Pothos kingii is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular houseplant for indoor gardens.
