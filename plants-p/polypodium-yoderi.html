Polypodium yoderi

Polypodium yoderi, commonly known as Yoder's huperzia, is a species of fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen rhizome and simple, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of hair. Polypodium yoderi is often found growing on rocky substrates or in rich, organic soil in woodland areas.
