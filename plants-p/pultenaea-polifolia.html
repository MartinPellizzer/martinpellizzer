Pultenaea polifolia

Pultenaea polifolia, commonly known as the silky pultenaea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, often prostrate stems and small, finely hairy leaves that give it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and open forests.

Pultenaea polifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
