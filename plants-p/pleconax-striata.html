Pleconax striata

Pleconax striata, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, zigzag-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests. Its leaves are often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Pleconax striata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique foliage structure.
