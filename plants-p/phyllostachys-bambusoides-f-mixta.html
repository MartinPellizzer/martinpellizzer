Phyllostachys bambusoides f. mixta

Phyllostachys bambusoides f. mixta, commonly known as the hybrid bamboo, is a varietal form of the giant bamboo species.

It is characterized by its diverse foliage, with leaves varying in color from green to yellow and even red, depending on the season and environmental conditions. This plant typically grows in clusters, forming dense clumps that can reach significant heights. Its stems are sturdy and hollow, with a smooth, often grayish surface.

The plant is well-suited to temperate climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes.
