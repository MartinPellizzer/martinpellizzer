Polygonum persicaria f. albiflorum

Polygonum persicaria f. albiflorum, commonly known as whiteflower smartweed, is a variant of the common smartweed.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its upright growth habit and simple, alternate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist environments such as wetlands and along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological studies and wetland restoration projects.
