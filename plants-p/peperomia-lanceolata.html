Peperomia lanceolata

Peperomia lanceolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features upright stems with oval, dark green leaves that have a slightly serrated margin. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of green or red, which are followed by small, round berries. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal water needs.

Peperomia lanceolata is valued for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
