Panicum aubertii

Panicum aubertii, commonly known as purpletop panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a warm-season grass that thrives in full sun and well-drained soils. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and striking purple-tinted inflorescences. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Panicum aubertii is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
