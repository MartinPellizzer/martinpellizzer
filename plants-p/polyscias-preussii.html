Polyscias preussii

Polyscias preussii, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowroot, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in diameter. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by dark blue berries containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of starch.

Polyscias preussii is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
