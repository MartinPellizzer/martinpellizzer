Peperomia formonensis

Peperomia formonensis, commonly known as the Formonensis Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a velvety texture and possesses a unique, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia formonensis is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
