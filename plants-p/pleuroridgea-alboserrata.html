Pleuroridgea alboserrata

Pleuroridgea alboserrata, commonly known as the white-serrated pleuroridgea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are typically lance-shaped with serrated edges, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance.
