Premna decaryi

Premna decaryi, commonly known as the African nettle or Decary's premna, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters. Its fruit is a small, three-lobed capsule that releases tiny seeds when mature.

Premna decaryi is often found in forest understories and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
