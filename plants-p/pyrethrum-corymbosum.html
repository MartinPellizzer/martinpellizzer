Pyrethrum corymbosum

Pyrethrum corymbosum, commonly known as the pyrethrum flower, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its clustered, daisy-like flowers with bright yellow petals and a central brown cone. The plant produces a variety of pyrethrins, which are natural insecticides used in organic pest control. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Pyrethrum corymbosum is also valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in traditional medicine.
