Peperomia elmeri

Peperomia elmeri, commonly known as the Elmer's Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features upright stems adorned with small, glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a subtle reddish or purple tinge on the underside. The plant produces clusters of tiny, inconspicuous white or pale green flowers, which are followed by small, round berries. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Peperomia elmeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and air-purifying qualities.
