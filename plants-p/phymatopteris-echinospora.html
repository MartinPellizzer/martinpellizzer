Phymatopteris echinospora

Phymatopteris echinospora, commonly known as the spiny-spleenwort, is a species of fern belonging to the family Pterobryaceae.

It is a small, epiphytic fern that typically grows on tree trunks or rocks in moist, shaded environments. The plant features a creeping rhizome with clusters of small, lanceolate leaves. Its sporangia are borne on the underside of the leaves, and the fronds are often covered with a fine, velvety coating.

This species is native to parts of North America and is characterized by its distinctive spiny appearance on the leaf margins.
