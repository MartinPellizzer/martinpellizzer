Provencheria cerastoides

Provencheria cerastoides, commonly known as the desert dandelion, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, fluffy seeds dispersed by the wind.
