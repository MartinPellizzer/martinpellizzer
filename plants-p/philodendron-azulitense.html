Philodendron azulitense

Philodendron azulitense, commonly known as the Blue Lithuanian Philodendron, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its glossy, deep green heart-shaped leaves that often feature a distinctive blue or bluish-green hue, giving it its common name. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its unique leaf patterns and vibrant coloration. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among indoor gardeners and collectors.
