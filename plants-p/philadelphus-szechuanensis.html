Philadelphus szechuanensis

Philadelphus szechuanensis, commonly known as the Sichuan mock orange, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its showy blooms and pleasant scent.
