Panicum cupreum

Panicum cupreum, commonly known as copper panicum, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a member of the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and delicate, feathery plumes. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and often have a bluish or silver-gray hue, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Copper panicum is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
