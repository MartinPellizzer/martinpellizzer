Paralbizzia

Paralbizzia, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has compound leaves with multiple leaflets and produces pods that contain seeds. Native to regions of South America, it is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
