Peperomia zipaquirana

Peperomia zipaquirana, commonly known as the Zipaquirana peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features oval-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and produces small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is appreciated by plant enthusiasts.
