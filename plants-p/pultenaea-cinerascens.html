Pultenaea cinerascens

Pultenaea cinerascens, commonly known as the ash-leaf pultenaea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant features narrow, grey-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, followed by elongated, woody pods.

Pultenaea cinerascens is typically found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
