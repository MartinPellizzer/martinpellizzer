Parasenecio ambiguus

Parasenecio ambiguus, commonly known as the ambiguous parasenecio, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

Parasenecio ambiguus is often found in alpine or subalpine environments, where it plays a role in local ecosystems.
