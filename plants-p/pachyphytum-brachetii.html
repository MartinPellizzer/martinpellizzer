Pachyphytum brachetii

Pachyphytum brachetii, commonly known as the silver star cactus, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded shape and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink or purple. The plant has thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. Its leaves are typically short and cylindrical, contributing to its unique appearance.

Pachyphytum brachetii is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its low maintenance requirements.
