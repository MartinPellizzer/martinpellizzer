Piptatherum tianschanicum

Piptatherum tianschanicum, commonly known as the Tian Shan needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, thriving in cold, high-altitude environments. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing forage for grazing animals in its native range.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to the harsh climatic conditions of its habitat.
