Pentas fililoba

Pentas fililoba, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from red to orange, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has opposite, simple, and elliptic-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Pentas fililoba is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
