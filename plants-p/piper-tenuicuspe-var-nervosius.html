Piper tenuicuspe var. nervosius

Piper tenuicuspe var. nervosius, commonly known as the slender-leafed pepper vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with prominently veined surfaces, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into elongated, cylindrical fruits. Its botanical characteristics include a fibrous root system and a vigorous growth habit.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and traditional medicinal uses.
