Pulicaria aucheri

Pulicaria aucheri, commonly known as silverleaf groundsel, is a perennial plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Pulicaria aucheri is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
