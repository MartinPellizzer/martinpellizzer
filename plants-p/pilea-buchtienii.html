Pilea buchtienii

Pilea buchtienii, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a tropical perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its round, shield-shaped leaves that resemble coins, giving it its common name. The plant has a compact growth habit and produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and ease of care have made it a favored ornamental plant among plant enthusiasts.
