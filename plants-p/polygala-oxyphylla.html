Polygala oxyphylla

Polygala oxyphylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and small, clustered flowers. Polygala oxyphylla is often found in open habitats such as shrublands and grasslands.
