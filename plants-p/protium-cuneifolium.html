Protium cuneifolium

Protium cuneifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf sandarac tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Sapindaceae and is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by woody, spiny fruits containing edible seeds. The plant is valued for its ecological role and has some commercial applications in traditional medicine.
