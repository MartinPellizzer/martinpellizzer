Pterostylis tunstallii

Pterostylis tunstallii, commonly known as the Tunstall's helmet-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, helmet-like flower structure that resembles a small, elongated helmet. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as rainforests and understoreys. Its flowers are usually green or brownish in color, with a distinctive lip that is often darker and more textured.

Pterostylis tunstallii is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its specialized pollination mechanisms.
