Polygonum binsarii

Polygonum binsarii, commonly known as the redroot smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its upright stems that are often reddish in color, especially near the base. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Polygonum binsarii is often found in moist soils along stream banks, wetlands, and disturbed areas.
