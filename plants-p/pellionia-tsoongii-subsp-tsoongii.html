Pellionia tsoongii subsp. tsoongii

Pellionia tsoongii subsp.

tsoongii, commonly known as the silver leaf pellionia, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia. It features heart-shaped leaves with a silvery-gray underside, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically clustered in the axils of the leaves. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its foliage is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
