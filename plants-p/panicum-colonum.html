Panicum colonum

Panicum colonum, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the central and southern United States.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and slender, tufted inflorescences that bloom in late summer. This plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a common sight in prairies and disturbed areas. Its drought tolerance and ability to form dense stands make it an important component of native grassland ecosystems.

Silver bluestem also serves as a valuable forage plant for livestock.
