Peperomia hydrocotyloides var. major

Peperomia hydrocotyloides var. major, commonly known as the broad-leaf peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features glossy, dark green, heart-shaped leaves that are broad and thick, giving it a lush appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental foliage, it is often used as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
