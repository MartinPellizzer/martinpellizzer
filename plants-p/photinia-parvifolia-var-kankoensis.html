Photinia parvifolia var. kankoensis

Photinia parvifolia var. kankoensis, commonly known as the Kankoensis Photinia, is a shrub or small tree native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of white to pale pink flowers in spring, followed by red berries in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a suitable ornamental plant for gardens.

Its compact growth habit and seasonal color changes make it a desirable addition to landscape designs.
