Prunus × tschonoskii var. leiogyna

Prunus × tschonoskii var. leiogyna, commonly known as the smooth-leaved cherry, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its smooth, glossy leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its fruits are small, dark red drupes that ripen in late summer.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
