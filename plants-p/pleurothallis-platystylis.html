Pleurothallis platystylis

Pleurothallis platystylis, commonly known as the broad-styled pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, linear leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. Its inflorescence is usually a raceme of several blooms, and it is known for its relatively short flowering period.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and challenging species to cultivate.
