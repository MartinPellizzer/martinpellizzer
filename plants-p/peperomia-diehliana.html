Peperomia diehliana

Peperomia diehliana, commonly known as the Mexican Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Mexico.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish or purple hue on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia diehliana is relatively low maintenance and suitable for beginners due to its tolerance for moderate watering and humidity.
