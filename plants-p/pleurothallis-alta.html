Pleurothallis alta

Pleurothallis alta, commonly known as the high orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are elongated and often covered in a fine, waxy coating, which helps retain moisture.

Pleurothallis alta is valued for its ornamental beauty and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
