Potentilla paucijuga

Potentilla paucijuga, commonly known as the narrowleaf cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and serrated, with a distinctive silver-white underside.

Potentilla paucijuga is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.
