Paeonia decora var. elatior

Paeonia decora var. elatior, commonly known as the tall peony, is a cultivar of the peony plant native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep pink, often with a distinctive red eye. The plant is a deciduous herbaceous perennial, producing thick, fleshy roots and rhizomes. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its botanical features make it a striking addition to both ornamental gardens and botanical collections.
