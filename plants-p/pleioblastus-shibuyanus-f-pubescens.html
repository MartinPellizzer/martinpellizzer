Pleioblastus shibuyanus f. pubescens

Pleioblastus shibuyanus f. pubescens, commonly known as the Shibuya bamboo, is a unique form of the Japanese bamboo species.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and the presence of fine, silky hairs on its culms, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green, adding to its ornamental value. It is a clump-forming bamboo that thrives in shaded to partially sunny conditions, making it suitable for rock gardens and small landscapes.

Its slow growth rate and evergreen nature make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
