Pelargonium × sphondylifolium

Pelargonium × sphondylifolium, commonly known as the Sphondylifolium geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors such as red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive wavy edge, which adds to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Due to its hardiness and adaptability, it is often cultivated for both decorative and medicinal purposes.
