Pisum sativum var. nanum

Pisum sativum var. nanum, commonly known as dwarf garden pea, is a compact variety of the common pea plant.

It is characterized by its short stature, typically reaching only a few inches in height, making it ideal for small garden spaces. The plant produces small, edible pods that contain round, yellow or green seeds. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its compact growth habit and attractive foliage.

This variety is well-suited for container gardening and can thrive in a variety of soil types with adequate sunlight.
