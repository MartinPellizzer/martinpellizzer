Polygonum rufescens

Polygonum rufescens, commonly known as redstem smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes other plants such as buckwheat and sorrel. The plant is characterized by its reddish stems and small, pinkish to reddish flowers that appear in clusters. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and it often grows in moist soils along stream banks or in wetlands.

Polygonum rufescens is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
