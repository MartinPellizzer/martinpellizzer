Plectranthus wollastonii

Plectranthus wollastonii, commonly known as the Woolly Plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, fuzzy foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from violet to purple. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers. It is also valued for its aromatic leaves, which can be used in herbal teas.

This species is known for its adaptability and resilience in various environmental conditions.
