Polemonium nyctelea

Polemonium nyctelea, commonly known as nightshade polemonium, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Polemonium genus within the family Polemoniaceae, which includes several species of flowering plants. The plant features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.
