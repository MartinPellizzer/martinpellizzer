Panicum sieberianum

Panicum sieberianum, commonly known as silver panicum, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and delicate, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces fluffy, pale pink to white inflorescences that resemble a cloud, making it visually striking. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
