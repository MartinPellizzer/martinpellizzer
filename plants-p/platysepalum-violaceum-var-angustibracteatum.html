Platysepalum violaceum var. angustibracteatum

Platysepalum violaceum var. angustibracteatum, commonly known as the narrow-bracted violet trumpet.

It is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its striking violet-colored flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces pendulous, trumpet-shaped blooms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and potential ecological role in its native habitat.
