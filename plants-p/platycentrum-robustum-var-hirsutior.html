Platycentrum robustum var. hirsutior

Platycentrum robustum var. hirsutior, commonly known as the hairy flat centipede plant, is a unique species within the Bromeliaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and hairy, strap-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces striking inflorescences with bright red flowers, which are borne on a tall, branching stem. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is native to parts of South America.

Due to its ornamental value and specific care requirements, it is a sought-after plant among bromeliad enthusiasts.
