Polygala monninoides

Polygala monninoides, commonly known as the narrowleaf milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, and it produces small, hairy fruits that contain several seeds.

It is often found in shrubland or along coastal areas where it thrives in well-drained conditions.
