Pleurothallis lineolata

Pleurothallis lineolata, commonly known as the lineolated pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in the understory of humid forests. The plant features delicate, linear leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. Its blooming period occurs during the wet season, and it is prized for its ornamental value in horticulture.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments to ensure its survival.
