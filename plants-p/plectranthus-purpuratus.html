Plectranthus purpuratus

Plectranthus purpuratus, commonly known as purple devil or purple heart, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple to reddish-pink flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture, giving it a unique appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Plectranthus purpuratus is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in herbal remedies.
