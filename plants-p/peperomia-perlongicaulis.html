Peperomia perlongicaulis

Peperomia perlongicaulis, commonly known as the long-stemmed peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy, dark green leaves that are often glossy and have a slightly wavy margin. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters, typically during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to both novice and experienced plant collectors.
