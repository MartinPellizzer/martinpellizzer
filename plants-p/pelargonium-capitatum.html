Pelargonium capitatum

Pelargonium capitatum, commonly known as the Cape geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit and dense clusters of small, showy flowers. The plant typically produces pink, red, or purple blooms that appear in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are velvety and dark green, often with a slightly serrated edge.

Pelargonium capitatum is well-suited for rock gardens and containers due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.
