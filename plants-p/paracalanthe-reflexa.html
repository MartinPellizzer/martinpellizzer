Paracalanthe reflexa

Paracalanthe reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, silky hairs. Its flowers are typically arranged in a raceme and bloom during the warmer months.

Paracalanthe reflexa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
