Prunus apetala f. multipetala

Prunus apetala f. multipetala, commonly known as the multi-petaled apricot.

This botanical variant is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its distinctive floral structure. It is a cultivar of Prunus apetala, which is a species native to China. The plant is notable for its showy, multi-petaled flowers that resemble those of the peach or apricot.
