Paphiopedilum hiepii

Paphiopedilum hiepii, commonly known as the Hiepii Paphiopedilum, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features a thick, fleshy rhizome and produces showy, lip-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. Its flowers are characterized by a distinctive, spurred lip that aids in pollination by insects.

Paphiopedilum hiepii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses due to its specific growing requirements.
