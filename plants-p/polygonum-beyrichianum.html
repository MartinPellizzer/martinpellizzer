Polygonum beyrichianum

Polygonum beyrichianum, commonly known as the red-rooted smartweed, is a herbaceous annual plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pinkish to reddish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist soils along stream banks, wet meadows, and disturbed sites. Its roots are reddish in color, which gives it its common name, and it is often found in the eastern and central United States.

The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
