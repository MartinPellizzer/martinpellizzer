Pomax hirta

Pomax hirta, commonly known as the hairy pomax, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features upright stems covered in fine hairs, giving it its common name. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences, often displaying shades of yellow or green.

Pomax hirta is known for its adaptability to arid conditions and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
