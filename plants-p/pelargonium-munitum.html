Pelargonium munitum

Pelargonium munitum, commonly known as the Cape geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, pink or red flowers. The leaves are typically rounded and have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
