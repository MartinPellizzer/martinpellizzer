Piper hieronymi var. glabrum

Piper hieronymi var. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth piper or glabrous piper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and leaves, which are typically alternate and ovate in shape. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, and its fruits are berries that contain several seeds.

This plant is often found in humid, shaded environments such as rainforests and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
