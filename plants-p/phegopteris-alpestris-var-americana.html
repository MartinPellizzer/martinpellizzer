Phegopteris alpestris var. americana

Phegopteris alpestris var. americana, commonly known as the American mountain fern, is a variant of the alpine fern native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky slopes. The plant features fronds that are pinnately divided, with a distinctive, feather-like appearance. Its spore-producing structures are located on the underside of the fronds, typical of ferns.

This variety is often found in temperate regions with cool, humid climates.
