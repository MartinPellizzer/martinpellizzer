Peperomia pullispica

Peperomia pullispica, commonly known as the coral peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with red or purple veins, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia pullispica is relatively low maintenance and drought-tolerant, suitable for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
