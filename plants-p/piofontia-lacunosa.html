Piofontia lacunosa

Piofontia lacunosa, commonly known as the laceleaf fern, is a species of flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lacy fronds that give it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests or along stream banks. Its leaves are compound, with fine, thread-like segments that create a feathery effect.

Piofontia lacunosa is valued in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
