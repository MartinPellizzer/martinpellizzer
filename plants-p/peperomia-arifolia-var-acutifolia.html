Peperomia arifolia var. acutifolia

Peperomia arifolia var. acutifolia, commonly known as the sharp-leaf peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features dense, glossy, dark green leaves with a distinctive sharp, pointed tip, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers, though it is primarily grown for its attractive foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its compact size and ornamental leaves make it a popular choice for tabletop arrangements and shaded garden spaces.
