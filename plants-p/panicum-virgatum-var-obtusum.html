Panicum virgatum var. obtusum

Panicum virgatum var. obtusum, commonly known as switchgrass, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, bluish-green leaves. The plant produces fluffy, seed-bearing panicles that are often used in ornamental landscaping. It is well adapted to a variety of soil types and is drought-tolerant.

This variety is particularly valued for its ecological benefits, including erosion control and wildlife habitat support.
