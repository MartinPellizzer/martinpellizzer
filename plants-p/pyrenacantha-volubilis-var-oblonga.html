Pyrenacantha volubilis var. oblonga

Pyrenacantha volubilis var. oblonga, commonly known as the oblong-leafed pyrenacantha, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped, leathery leaves that are typically dark green with a smooth, waxy surface. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the rainy season. Its fruit is a spherical, fleshy berry that turns red when mature, containing several seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden settings for its vigorous growth and adaptability.
