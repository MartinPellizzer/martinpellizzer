Penstemon gairdneri var. gairdneri

Penstemon gairdneri var. gairdneri, commonly known as Gairdner's penstemon, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
