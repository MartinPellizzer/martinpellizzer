Physurus chinensis

Physurus chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant is a member of the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and caladiums. It thrives in humid, warm environments and prefers indirect sunlight.

Physurus chinensis is prized for its ease of care and ability to purify indoor air.
