Prunella × fontqueri

Prunella × fontqueri, commonly known as the hybrid selfheal, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the British Isles.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
