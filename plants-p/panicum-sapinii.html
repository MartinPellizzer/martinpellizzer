Panicum sapinii

Panicum sapinii, commonly known as the slender panicgrass, is a species of grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, fluffy spikelets that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in open woodlands and disturbed areas.

Panicum sapinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local wildlife.
