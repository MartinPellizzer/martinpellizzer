Poikilacanthus oncodes

Poikilacanthus oncodes, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its segmented, leaf-like stems that are often used for propagation. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically during the holiday season. Its ability to thrive in low-light conditions makes it a popular houseplant.

The Christmas cactus is also noted for its unique blooming pattern, which can be influenced by temperature and light cycles.
