Piper tenuinerve

Piper tenuinerve, commonly known as the slender nerve pepper, is a tropical climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its slender, finely veined leaves and small, clustered green berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

The berries, when ripe, turn red and are used in traditional medicine and as a spice in some cuisines.
