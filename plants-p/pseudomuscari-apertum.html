Pseudomuscari apertum

Pseudomuscari apertum, commonly known as the open muscari, is a species of bulbous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, clustered blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring.
