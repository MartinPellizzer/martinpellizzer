Podochilus reflexus var. cycloglossus

Podochilus reflexus var. cycloglossus, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, arching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often adorned with a waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of small, pale green flowers with a distinctive zigzag pattern on the petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouse settings.
