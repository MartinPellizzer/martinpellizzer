Phalaenopsis tsii

Phalaenopsis tsii, commonly known as the Orchid of the Southern Hemisphere, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flower spikes. The plant produces showy, long-lasting flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

Phalaenopsis tsii is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
