Pilea cangyuanensis

Pilea cangyuanensis, commonly known as the Cangyuan prayer plant, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it has gained popularity among plant enthusiasts.
