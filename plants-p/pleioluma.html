Pleioluma

Pleioluma, commonly known as the star orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to various regions of South America, particularly in the Andes, where it thrives in high-altitude environments. The plants are characterized by their striking, star-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and purple. Pleioluma species typically grow as epiphytes or terrestrial plants, often found in moist, shaded areas.

Their unique floral structure and vibrant colors make them popular among orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike.
