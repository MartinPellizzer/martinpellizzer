Phyllostachys nevinii var. hupehensis

Phyllostachys nevinii var. hupehensis, commonly known as the Hubei bamboo, is a variety of bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in landscaping and traditional Chinese gardens.
