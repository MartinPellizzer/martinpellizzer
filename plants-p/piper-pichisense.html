Piper pichisense

Piper pichisense, commonly known as the Pichis pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly in Peru and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its fruits are peppery and used in traditional medicine and local cuisine.

It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in cloud forests.
