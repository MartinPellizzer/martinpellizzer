Polycarena lilacina

Polycarena lilacina, commonly known as the lilac-stemmed polycarena, is a unique aquatic plant characterized by its delicate, lilac-colored stems.

It typically grows in slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and lakes, where it thrives in moderate to low light conditions. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem. Its flowers are inconspicuous, consisting of tiny, greenish-white blooms that appear in clusters.

Polycarena lilacina is valued in aquascaping for its aesthetic appeal and ability to contribute to a balanced aquatic ecosystem.
