Pterichis acuminata

Pterichis acuminata, commonly known as the slender pterichis, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its flowers are usually pale purple to white, and it produces small, three-lobed fruits that contain seeds.

Pterichis acuminata is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
