Pluchea mollis

Pluchea mollis, commonly known as white crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its fluffy, white inflorescence that resembles a crown. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as wetlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often reddish in color.

Pluchea mollis is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
