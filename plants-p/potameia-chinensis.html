Potameia chinensis

Potameia chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features upright, cylindrical stems covered in dense, waxy leaves that are typically green or variegated. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by round, fleshy fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its foliage is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
