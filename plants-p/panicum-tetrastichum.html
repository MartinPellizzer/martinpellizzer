Panicum tetrastichum

Panicum tetrastichum, commonly known as purpletop grass, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a warm-season grass that thrives in full sun and well-drained soils. The plant produces tall, slender stalks with narrow, blue-green leaves and dense, spikelike clusters of purple flowers. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil.

Panicum tetrastichum is also valued for its role in supporting pollinators and wildlife.
