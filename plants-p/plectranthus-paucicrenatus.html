Plectranthus paucicrenatus

Plectranthus paucicrenatus, commonly known as the silver plectranthus, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features aromatic, fuzzy leaves that are often silver or gray in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or blue in hue, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a versatile addition to both indoor and outdoor landscapes.
