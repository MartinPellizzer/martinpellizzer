Pomatocalpa kunstleri

Pomatocalpa kunstleri, commonly known as the Kunstler's pterostylis, is an orchid species native to Australia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understoreys. Its flowers have a unique structure with a single, erect column and a lip that is distinctly shaped.

Pomatocalpa kunstleri is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
