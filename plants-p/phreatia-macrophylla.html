Phreatia macrophylla

Phreatia macrophylla, commonly known as the giant phreatia, is a striking orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 15 centimeters in length. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil and air.

Phreatia macrophylla is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
