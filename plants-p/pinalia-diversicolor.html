Pinalia diversicolor

Pinalia diversicolor, commonly known as the rainbow fern, is a strikingly colorful plant native to tropical regions.

It features intricate, frilly leaves that display a wide range of hues, including green, red, and purple, depending on environmental conditions. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture to maintain its vibrant appearance. Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant collections.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and sporangia that produce spores for reproduction.
