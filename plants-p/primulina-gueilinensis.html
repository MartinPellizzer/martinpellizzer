Primulina gueilinensis

Primulina gueilinensis, commonly known as the Guangzhou arrowhead, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves and vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range from white to pink. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers indirect sunlight, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for terrariums and shaded garden areas.
