Philodendron luxurians

Philodendron luxurians, commonly known as the velvetleaf philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by yellowish-green berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its rapid growth and striking foliage make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
