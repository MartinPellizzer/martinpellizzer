Pittosporum hejiangense

Pittosporum hejiangense, commonly known as the Hejiang pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage and clusters of fragrant white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are leathery and glossy, providing a striking visual contrast to its delicate blooms.

Pittosporum hejiangense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in diverse soil conditions.
