Pandorea australis subsp. pandorea

Pandorea australis subsp.

pandorea, commonly known as the Australian trumpet vine, is a climbing shrub native to eastern Australia. It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves and produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red.

The plant is characterized by its woody stems and is often found in coastal and subtropical regions. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and as a horticultural specimen.
