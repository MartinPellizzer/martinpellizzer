Panicum wallichianum

Panicum wallichianum, commonly known as silver panicum, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, including China, India, and Nepal.

It is a perennial grass that typically grows in moist or wetland habitats. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces fluffy, silver-gray inflorescences that are highly visible and attractive. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a rough texture.

Silver panicum is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in wet conditions.
