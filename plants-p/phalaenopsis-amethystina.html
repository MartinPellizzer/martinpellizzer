Phalaenopsis amethystina

Phalaenopsis amethystina, commonly known as the amethyst orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its long, broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, long-lasting flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining potting media.

Phalaenopsis amethystina is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
