Phyllanthus mahengeaensis

Phyllanthus mahengeaensis, commonly known as the Mahenge phyllanthus, is a shrub native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its small, clustered leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
