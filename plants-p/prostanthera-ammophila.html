Prostanthera ammophila

Prostanthera ammophila, commonly known as beach mint, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. This plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pale blue flowers and narrow, linear leaves. It typically grows in sandy coastal environments, thriving in well-drained soils.
