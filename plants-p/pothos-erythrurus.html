Pothos erythrurus

Pothos erythrurus, commonly known as the crimson pothos, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that often display a vibrant red or orange variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in low-light conditions and is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and ornamental value.

Pothos erythrurus is also known for its ability to purify indoor air, making it a favored choice for improving air quality in homes.
