Plantago monosperma

Plantago monosperma, commonly known as the single-seeded plantain, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves arranged in a rosette pattern. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense spikes, which are borne on long, slender stems.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
