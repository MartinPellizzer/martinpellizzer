Polystachya porphyrochila

Polystachya porphyrochila, commonly known as the purple-veined bamboo orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of dark purple to black flowers. Its leaves are long and narrow, often with a distinct purple veining.

Polystachya porphyrochila is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and unique coloration.
