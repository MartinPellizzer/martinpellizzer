Pancovia macrophylla

Pancovia macrophylla, commonly known as the giant leaf fern, is a striking plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach several feet in length. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often near streams or in dense forests. Its fronds are deeply divided, giving it a feathery appearance.

Pancovia macrophylla is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
