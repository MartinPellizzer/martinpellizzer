Piper aghaense

Piper aghaense, commonly known as the Aghaense pepper, is a flowering plant species native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes numerous species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in spikes. Its fruit is a small, elongated berry that turns red when mature.

The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and is often grown for its ornamental value as well as its potential medicinal properties.
