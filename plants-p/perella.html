Perella

Perella, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a species of flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant orange to red, lantern-shaped fruits. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are ovate with a serrated margin, and it produces small, white flowers that bloom in the summer.

The fruits are often used in culinary and ornamental contexts due to their striking appearance.
