Phreatia klabatensis

Phreatia klabatensis, commonly known as the Klabat orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Indonesia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on the forest floor or in rocky crevices.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
