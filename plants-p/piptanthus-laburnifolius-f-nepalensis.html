Piptanthus laburnifolius f. nepalensis

Piptanthus laburnifolius f. nepalensis, commonly known as the Nepalese goldenpea, is a shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features compound leaves with a bright yellow floral display, resembling those of the laburnum. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant in horticulture.
