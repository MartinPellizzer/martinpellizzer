Pittosporum crassifolium var. strictum

Pittosporum crassifolium var. strictum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with narrow, leathery leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, woody fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

This variety is notable for its compact growth habit and ability to withstand coastal conditions.
