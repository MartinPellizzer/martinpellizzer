Pavia glabra

Pavia glabra, commonly known as the smooth pavia, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features woody stems that are smooth and often reddish in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Pavia glabra is often found in dry, rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
