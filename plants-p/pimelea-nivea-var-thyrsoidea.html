Pimelea nivea var. thyrsoidea

Pimelea nivea var. thyrsoidea, commonly known as the white-leafed candlebush, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, giving the plant a distinctive, silvery appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
