Pleurothallis inornata

Pleurothallis inornata, commonly known as the unadorned pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It is known for its relatively simple floral structure compared to other orchid species.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in the wild.
