Peperomia allorgeana f. genuina

Peperomia allorgeana f. genuina, commonly known as the Allorge's peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a smooth texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of red or orange, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
