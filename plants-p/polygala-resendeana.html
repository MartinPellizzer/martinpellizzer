Polygala resendeana

Polygala resendeana, commonly known as Resende's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, often pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. It has small, compound leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
