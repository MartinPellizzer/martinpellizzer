Psychotria paloensis var. bukidnonensis, commonly known as the Bicol Psychotria, is a species of flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to the Philippines, specifically found in the Bicol Region. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a glossy surface and clusters of small, white flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically in lowland to montane forests.
