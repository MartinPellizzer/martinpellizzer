Phylica thunbergii

Phylica thunbergii, commonly known as the silver bush, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, pink or red flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Phylica thunbergii is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and is also valued for its ecological benefits.
