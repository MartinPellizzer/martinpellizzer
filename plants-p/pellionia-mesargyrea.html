Pellionia mesargyrea

Pellionia mesargyrea, commonly known as the silver nettle, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features heart-shaped, glabrous leaves with a distinctive silvery-white underside, giving it a striking visual appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on short pedicels. Pellionia mesargyrea thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its rapid growth and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
