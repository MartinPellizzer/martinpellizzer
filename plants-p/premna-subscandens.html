Premna subscandens

Premna subscandens, commonly known as the climbing premna, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Lamiaceae.

It is characterized by its woody stems and opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale purple in color. It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in moist, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a common species in forest understories and along stream banks.
