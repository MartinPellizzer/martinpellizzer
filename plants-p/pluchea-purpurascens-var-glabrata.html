Pluchea purpurascens var. glabrata

Pluchea purpurascens var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth false ragweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaves. The plant has smooth, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often found in disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
