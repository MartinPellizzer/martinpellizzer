Pellionia hoffmanniana

Pellionia hoffmanniana, commonly known as Hoffmann's pellionia, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically light green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are clustered in umbels, often blooming in the spring. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in rainforests.

Pellionia hoffmanniana is valued for its ornamental foliage and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
