Polystichum nephrolepioides

Polystichum nephrolepioides, commonly known as the Japanese shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to eastern Asia.

It features compound leaves that are deeply lobed, giving it a distinctive, intricate appearance. The fronds are typically upright and can grow up to 60 cm in length, with a leathery texture. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
