Ponerorchis wolongensis

Ponerorchis wolongensis, commonly known as the Wolong orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique morphological features, including its striking purple and white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and forested areas, thriving in cool and humid environments. Its roots are specialized for absorbing nutrients from the substrate, and it plays a role in the local ecosystem by supporting pollinators.
