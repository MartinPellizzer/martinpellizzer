Physematium vestitum

Physematium vestitum, commonly known as the velvet fern, is a unique plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant is a succulent, adapted to arid environments with its ability to store water in its leaves. Its leaves are typically thick and fleshy, with a textured surface that aids in reducing water loss.

Physematium vestitum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry conditions.
