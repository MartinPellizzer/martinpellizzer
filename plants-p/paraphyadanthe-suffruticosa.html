Paraphyadanthe suffruticosa

Paraphyadanthe suffruticosa, commonly known as the red-flowered rock rose, is a perennial shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to deep red, blooming in spring and summer. The plant has woody stems and is adapted to grow in rocky, well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.

Paraphyadanthe suffruticosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh environments.
