Pluchea doniana

Pluchea doniana, commonly known as the African blueweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its blue to purple flowers and hairy foliage. The plant typically grows in wetland areas and is often found in marshy or swampy habitats. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a distinct aromatic scent, while its flowers are arranged in clusters.

Pluchea doniana is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
