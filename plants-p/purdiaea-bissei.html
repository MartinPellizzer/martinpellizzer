Purdiaea bissei

Purdiaea bissei, commonly known as the pink fairy fan, is a unique flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, fan-shaped inflorescence that consists of numerous small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Purdiaea bissei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its subtle beauty.
