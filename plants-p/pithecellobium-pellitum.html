Pithecellobium pellitum

Pithecellobium pellitum, commonly known as the dwarf pisonia, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, round, yellowish fruits that are edible but often have a bitter taste.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
