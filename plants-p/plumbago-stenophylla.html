Plumbago stenophylla

Plumbago stenophylla, commonly known as blue plumbago, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of striking blue or purple flowers, which bloom throughout the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
