Plumbago lanceolata

Plumbago lanceolata, commonly known as blue plumbago, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
