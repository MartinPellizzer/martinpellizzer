Pennisetum pungens

Pennisetum pungens, commonly known as fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its clumping growth habit and slender, fountain-like arrangement of leaves. The plant produces fluffy, silver-gray seed heads that add visual interest during the fall and winter months. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ability to spread through rhizomes contribute to its adaptability in various climates.
