Pithecellobium filipes

Pithecellobium filipes, commonly known as the slender pod bean, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are often clustered together. Its seeds are enclosed in long, slender pods, which are a distinctive feature of the species.

Pithecellobium filipes is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
