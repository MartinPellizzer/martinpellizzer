Poinciana playfairii

Poinciana playfairii, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, feathery, flame-like inflorescences that bloom in vibrant red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, lance-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a street tree.

The flowers are not only visually appealing but also attract pollinators such as birds and insects.
