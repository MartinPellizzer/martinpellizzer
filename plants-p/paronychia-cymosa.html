Paronychia cymosa

Paronychia cymosa, commonly known as the clustered leaf speedwell, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from spring to summer. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white or grayish hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
