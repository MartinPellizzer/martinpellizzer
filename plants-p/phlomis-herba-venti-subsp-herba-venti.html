Phlomis herba-venti subsp. herba-venti

Phlomis herba-venti subsp.

herba-venti, commonly known as the windgrass, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia. It typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky slopes and sandy soils.

The plant features upright, branching stems that can reach up to 1 meter in height. Its leaves are lance-shaped, hairy, and often have a grayish-green hue.

The flowers are clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences, typically in shades of pink to purple, blooming from summer to autumn.
