Pilea thymoidea

Pilea thymoidea, commonly known as the Thyme Pilea, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Urticaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and simple, opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, typically blooming in spring. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a suitable houseplant for indoor cultivation.

Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
