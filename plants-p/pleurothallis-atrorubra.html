Pleurothallis atrorubra

Pleurothallis atrorubra, commonly known as the black-red pleurothallis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a small epiphytic orchid, typically growing on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, pendulous flowers in shades of red and purple. Its inflorescence usually consists of several small, tubular flowers that bloom during the rainy season.

Due to its striking coloration and unique morphology, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
