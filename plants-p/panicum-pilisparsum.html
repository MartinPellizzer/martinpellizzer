Panicum pilisparsum

Panicum pilisparsum, commonly known as silky panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, hair-like leaves and produces delicate, feathery inflorescences that give it a soft, silky appearance. The plant is adapted to well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for ornamental and erosion control purposes. Its rapid growth and drought tolerance contribute to its popularity in landscaping.

However, it can become invasive in some environments if not managed properly.
