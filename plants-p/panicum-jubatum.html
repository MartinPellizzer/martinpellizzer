Panicum jubatum

Panicum jubatum, commonly known as the yellow panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features tall, slender stems that can reach up to 3 meters in height, with narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces fluffy, golden-brown inflorescences that are often used as ornamental elements in gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a hardy and low-maintenance plant.

Its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions makes it a versatile choice for landscaping and erosion control.
