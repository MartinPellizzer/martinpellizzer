Pelargonium roseopetalum

Pelargonium roseopetalum, commonly known as the pink petal geranium, is a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, pink to rose-colored flowers. The plant features rounded, lobed leaves that are often hairy, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Pelargonium roseopetalum is popular in gardens for its vibrant blooms and ease of cultivation.
