Phlomis herba-venti subsp. lenkoranica

Phlomis herba-venti subsp.

lenkoranica, commonly known as the Lenkoran thyme. It is a perennial herb native to the Caucasus region, particularly found in Azerbaijan and Georgia.

The plant has aromatic, hairy leaves and produces showy pink to purple flowers in summer. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous areas.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the mint family, Lamiaceae.
