Phyllostegia glabra var. lanaiensis

Phyllostegia glabra var. lanaiensis, commonly known as the Lanai stiltleaf, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its smooth, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that typically bloom in shades of purple or pink. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its native range.
