Plecospermum cuneifolium

Plecospermum cuneifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved plecospermum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and small, clustered white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The species is valued for both its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
