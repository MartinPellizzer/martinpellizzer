Pleurothallis systremmata

Pleurothallis systremmata, commonly known as the golden rosette orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a compact rosette of narrow, fleshy leaves that store water, aiding in its survival in arid conditions. Its flowers are usually yellow or golden, with a delicate, fragrant scent that attracts pollinators.

Due to its unique appearance and growth habits, it is a popular choice for collectors and botanical gardens.
