Peperomia yapasana

Peperomia yapasana, commonly known as the Yapasana Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which appear in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia yapasana is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
