Polystichum aculeatum var. mucronifolium

Polystichum aculeatum var. mucronifolium, commonly known as the blunt-leaved wood fern, is a perennial fern native to North America.

It features a tall, slender rhizome with dark brown, fibrous roots. The fronds are bi- to tri-pinnate, with lanceolate, serrated, and slightly curled leaflets. The plant produces spore-bearing sorus clusters on the underside of the fronds.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
