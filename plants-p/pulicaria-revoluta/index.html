Pulicaria Revoluta

Pulicaria revoluta is a hardy perennial plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa. It is known for its distinctive silver-gray foliage and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its low maintenance requirements and drought tolerance make it an excellent addition to xeriscaped landscapes. Pulicaria revoluta is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
