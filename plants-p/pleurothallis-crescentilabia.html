Pleurothallis crescentilabia

Pleurothallis crescentilabia, commonly known as the crescent-labellum orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a delicate, wiry stem with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its most distinctive characteristic is its intricate, fragrant flower with a uniquely shaped labellum.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to cultivated conditions.
