Primula × kuntzeana

Primula × kuntzeana, commonly known as the Kuntze's primrose, is a hybrid plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and thrives in cool climates. Its foliage consists of broad, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

Primula × kuntzeana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
