Panicum coursii

Panicum coursii, commonly known as the red panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States and parts of Mexico.

It is a perennial grass that typically grows in wetland areas and along the edges of water bodies. The plant produces dense, reddish-brown inflorescences that are often used in ornamental gardening. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color that adds visual interest.

Panicum coursii is valued for its ability to thrive in moist conditions and its aesthetic appeal in landscapes.
