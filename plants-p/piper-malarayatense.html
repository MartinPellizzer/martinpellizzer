Piper malarayatense

Piper malarayatense, commonly known as the Malarayatense pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the Western Ghats of India, where it thrives in moist, tropical environments. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers that are typically green or white in color. Its fruits are berries that contain several seeds, and the plant is often found in dense undergrowth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
