Poincianella laxiflora

Poincianella laxiflora, commonly known as the slender flame tree, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, pendulous flowers that range in color from white to pink, often blooming in clusters. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a landscape plant.
