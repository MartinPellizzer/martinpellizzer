Polygonum macranthum var. scabridum

Polygonum macranthum var. scabridum, commonly known as the rough-leaf smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, bristly hairs, giving them a rough texture. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to reddish-purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist to wet soil conditions, often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a common species in various wetland ecosystems.
