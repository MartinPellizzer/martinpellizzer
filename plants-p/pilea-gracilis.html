Pilea gracilis

Pilea gracilis, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to China.

It features heart-shaped, glossy, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Pilea gracilis is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
