Persica fromentina

Persica fromentina, commonly known as the Fromentina peach.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region. The plant produces showy, single or double pink or white flowers in early spring. Its fruit, similar to a peach, is typically small and has a delicate flavor.

Persica fromentina is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its floral display.
