Pleione × confusa

Pleione × confusa, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink. The plant is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and long, strap-like leaves. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
