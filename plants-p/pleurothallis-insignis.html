Pleurothallis insignis

Pleurothallis insignis, commonly known as the pink lady's tresses, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. This epiphytic orchid typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and often pink or white in color, arranged in a dense, truss-like inflorescence.

The plant is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
