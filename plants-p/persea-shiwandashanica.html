Persea shiwandashanica

Persea shiwandashanica, commonly known as the Shiwandashan persimmon, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to China, specifically the Shiwandashan region in Yunnan Province. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are typically elliptical and have a glossy surface. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by edible berries that are often consumed raw or cooked.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential medicinal uses.
