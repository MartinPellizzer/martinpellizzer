Perrierangraecum sambiranoense

Perrierangraecum sambiranoense, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to the eastern region of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that exhibit a vivid combination of purple and white hues. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is distinguished by its intricate floral structure and specialized pollination mechanisms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments within the dense rainforests of its native habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat loss, it is considered an endangered species requiring conservation efforts.
