Pulsatilla patens var. kioviensis

Pulsatilla patens var. kioviensis, commonly known as the Kioviensis pasqueflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with specific horticultural requirements.
