Petroselinum sativum var. latifolium

Petroselinum sativum var. latifolium, commonly known as Italian parsley, is a biennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its flat, broad, dark green leaves and a mild, fresh aroma. The plant belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes other aromatic herbs like carrots and dill. Its leaves are often used in cooking for their subtle flavor and nutritional value.
