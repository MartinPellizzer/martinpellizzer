Piper yzabalanum var. pubinerve

Piper yzabalanum var. pubinerve, commonly known as the pubinerve pepper vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves with a distinctive pubescent (hairy) underside, giving it its varietal name. Its small, yellowish-green flowers are borne in clusters and are followed by elongated, oval-shaped berries.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
