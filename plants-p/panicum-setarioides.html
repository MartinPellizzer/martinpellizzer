Panicum setarioides

Panicum setarioides, commonly known as the slender panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically green to purplish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open areas such as fields and roadsides.

Panicum setarioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and use in erosion control.
