Pentacalia verticillata

Pentacalia verticillata, commonly known as the clustered milkweed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces a milky sap that is typical of the Asclepiadaceae family. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.
