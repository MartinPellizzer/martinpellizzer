Phyllanthus nhatrangensis

Phyllanthus nhatrangensis, commonly known as Nhatrang gooseberry, is a flowering plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam, and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. It produces small, round fruits that are typically green when unripe and turn red when mature.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for potential medicinal properties.
