Pennisetum breve

Pennisetum breve, commonly known as dwarf fountain grass, is a compact, ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that produce fluffy, silver-gray seed heads, which add visual interest throughout the growing season. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color, providing a striking contrast to its fluffy inflorescences. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Pennisetum breve is drought-tolerant and low-maintenance, suitable for both container planting and in-ground use.
