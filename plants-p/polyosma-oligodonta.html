Polyosma oligodonta

Polyosma oligodonta, commonly known as the narrow-leafed greenbark.

It is a small evergreen shrub native to the eastern regions of Australia. The plant features elliptical, glossy, dark green leaves that are typically narrow and pointed. Its flowers are small, white or pale green, and are borne in clusters along the stems.

The fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds.
