Polystachya erythrocephala

Polystachya erythrocephala, commonly known as the red-headed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red and orange inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as forest understories. Its flowers are clustered in dense spikes and are pollinated by various insects.

Polystachya erythrocephala is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
