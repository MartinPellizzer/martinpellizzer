Polygonum segetum var. stamineum

Polygonum segetum var. stamineum, commonly known as red root, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Polygonaceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems that are often reddish in color, especially when mature. The leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a prominent midrib and a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in clusters, and it is often found in wet or disturbed soils.

It is used in traditional medicine for its purported diuretic and anti-inflammatory properties.
