Pachylarnax praecalva

Pachylarnax praecalva, commonly known as the white-flowered pachylarnax, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in a dense cluster. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
