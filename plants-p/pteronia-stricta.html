Pteronia stricta

Pteronia stricta, commonly known as the narrowleaf pteronia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, depending on the variety. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.

Pteronia stricta thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal and Mediterranean climates.
