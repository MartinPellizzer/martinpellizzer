Paphiopedilum villosum var. fuscoviride

Paphiopedilum villosum var. fuscoviride, commonly known as the dark green velvet slipper orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a fleshy, hairy rhizome and produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive, pouch-like lip. The leaves are broad and ovate, often covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. The flower's coloration ranges from deep green to brownish-green, with a contrasting yellow or white throat.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
