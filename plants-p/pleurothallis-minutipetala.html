Pleurothallis minutipetala

Pleurothallis minutipetala, commonly known as the miniature petal orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

This orchid is characterized by its small, delicate flowers and compact growth habit, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts. It typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces fragrant, white to pale yellow blossoms.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments to ensure its survival.
