Priva armata

Priva armata, commonly known as the armed privet, is a shrub native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its opposite, elliptical leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in clusters during the spring. Its fruit is a small, black drupe that contains a single seed.

Priva armata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to various soil types.
