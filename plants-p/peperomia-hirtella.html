Peperomia hirtella

Peperomia hirtella, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of green or red. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia hirtella is valued for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
