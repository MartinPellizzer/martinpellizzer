Payena dasyphylla

Payena dasyphylla, commonly known as the African sandthorn, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant has compound leaves with opposite arrangement and produces yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, round drupe that turns red when mature.

The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it resilient to drought conditions.
