Piper contraverrugosum

Piper contraverrugosum, commonly known as the zigzag pepper, is a climbing vine native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many pepper species. The plant is characterized by its unique, zigzagging stems and heart-shaped leaves. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
