Peperomia tlapacoyoensis

Peperomia tlapacoyoensis, commonly known as the Mexican peperomia, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub characterized by its glossy, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate light conditions.

Peperomia tlapacoyoensis is appreciated for its ornamental value and adaptability to indoor environments.
