Platydesma grandifolium

Platydesma grandifolium, commonly known as the large-leaf flatweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, which bloom during the rainy season. Its root system is fibrous and can spread extensively, allowing it to thrive in moist, nutrient-rich soils.

Platydesma grandifolium is often found in wetlands and along riverbanks, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
