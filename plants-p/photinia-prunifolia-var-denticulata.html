Photinia prunifolia var. denticulata

Photinia prunifolia var. denticulata, commonly known as the serrated photinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves with finely serrated edges, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of white, five-petaled flowers in the spring, followed by red, fleshy berries in the fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
