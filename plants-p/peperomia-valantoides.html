Peperomia valantoides

Peperomia valantoides, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a distinctive silvery-white underside, giving it a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive foliage, it is widely appreciated as a decorative plant.
