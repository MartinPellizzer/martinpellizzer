Pelargonium compositum

Pelargonium compositum, commonly known as the composite geranium, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, clustered flowers. The plant features palmate leaves with lobed segments, often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically pink or red, with a distinctive composite appearance due to the arrangement of petals and sepals.

Pelargonium compositum thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
