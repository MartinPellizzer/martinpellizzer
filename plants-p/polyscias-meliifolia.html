Polyscias meliifolia

Polyscias meliifolia, commonly known as the sweet-scented glorybower, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and showy inflorescences.

Polyscias meliifolia is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in humid, tropical climates.
