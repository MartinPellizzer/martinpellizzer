Peperomia knudsenii

Peperomia knudsenii, commonly known as the Knudsen's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia knudsenii is a low-maintenance plant that is well-suited for indoor cultivation.
