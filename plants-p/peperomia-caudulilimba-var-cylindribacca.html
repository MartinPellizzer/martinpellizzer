Peperomia caudulilimba var. cylindribacca

Peperomia caudulilimba var. cylindribacca, commonly known as the cylindrical-stemmed peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, providing a dense, foliage-rich appearance. The plant's stems are notably cylindrical and often display a reddish or purple hue, adding visual interest. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique morphology and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
