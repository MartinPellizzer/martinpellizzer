Phyllostachys bambusoides f. lacrima-deae

Phyllostachys bambusoides f. lacrima-deae, commonly known as the Weeping Bamboo, is a unique variant of the Bambusoides bamboo species.

It is characterized by its gracefully drooping culms that create a weeping appearance, making it visually striking in gardens. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green, providing a lush, feathery texture. It is native to China and thrives in moist, well-drained soils, preferring partial to full sun.

This ornamental bamboo is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
