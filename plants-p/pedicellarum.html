Pedicellarum

Pedicellarum, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its distinctive blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a compact growth habit with silver-gray foliage and is often found in sandy or rocky soils. Pedicellarum is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
