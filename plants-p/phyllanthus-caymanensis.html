Phyllanthus caymanensis

Phyllanthus caymanensis, commonly known as the Cayman Islands spurge, is a flowering plant native to the Cayman Islands.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in coastal and limestone habitats. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a distinctive waxy texture.

It is adapted to the tropical climate and is often found in disturbed or open areas.
