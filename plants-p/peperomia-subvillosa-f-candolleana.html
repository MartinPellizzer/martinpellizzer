Peperomia subvillosa f. candolleana

Peperomia subvillosa f. candolleana, commonly known as the Candolleana peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are often slightly hairy, giving it a textured appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to avoid root rot.

Due to its attractive foliage and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
