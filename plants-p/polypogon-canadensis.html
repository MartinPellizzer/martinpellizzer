Polypogon canadensis

Polypogon canadensis, commonly known as Canada bluegrass, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green blades and tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in prairies, meadows, and along stream banks. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers.

Canada bluegrass is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological restoration projects.
