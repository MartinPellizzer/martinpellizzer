Pozoa roughii

Pozoa roughii, commonly known as the rough pozoa, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features wiry, branched stems that are often covered in fine, rough hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early summer. Its leaves are narrow, linear, and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Pozoa roughii is typically found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to thrive in arid environments.
