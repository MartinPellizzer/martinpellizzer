Potentilla fastigiata var. jucunda

Potentilla fastigiata var. jucunda, commonly known as the compact potentilla, is a low-growing shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features dense, upright growth with small, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in early summer, which are borne on short pedicels. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its compact form and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
