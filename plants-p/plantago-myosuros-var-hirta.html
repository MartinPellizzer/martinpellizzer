Plantago myosuros var. hirta

Plantago myosuros var. hirta, commonly known as hairy plantain, is a variant of the common plantain species.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a hairy appearance. It produces small, greenish-white flowers in dense clusters during the summer months.

This variety is often found in disturbed soils and is known for its medicinal properties.
