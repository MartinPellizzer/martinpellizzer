Potentilla × diversifolia var. proxima

Potentilla × diversifolia var. proxima, commonly known as the alpine cinquefoil, is a low-growing shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, typically arranged in clusters of five. The plant has slender, wiry stems and hairy leaves that are often lobed or divided. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to harsh alpine environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and ornamental plant suitable for rock gardens and alpine displays.
