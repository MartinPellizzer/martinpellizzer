Pityrogramma intermedia

Pityrogramma intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate water fern, is a member of the family Pteridaceae.

It is a small, terrestrial fern that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant has delicate, pinnately divided fronds with a distinctive, glossy appearance. Its sporangia are clustered in sori on the undersides of the fronds.

It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in humid conditions.
