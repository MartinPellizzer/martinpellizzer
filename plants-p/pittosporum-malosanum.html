Pittosporum malosanum

Pittosporum malosanum, commonly known as the golden pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces small, reddish-brown berries that are edible but not particularly palatable. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance.

Pittosporum malosanum is also valued for its role in ecological restoration projects.
