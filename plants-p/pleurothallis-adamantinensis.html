Pleurothallis adamantinensis

Pleurothallis adamantinensis, commonly known as the "Adamantinense Orchid," is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, narrow leaves and produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its flowers are typically arranged in a loose inflorescence and bloom during the rainy season.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
