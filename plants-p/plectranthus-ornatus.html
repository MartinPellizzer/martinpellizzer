Plectranthus ornatus

Plectranthus ornatus, commonly known as the silver bush or velvetleaf, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features striking silver-gray foliage with a soft, velvety texture, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of purple or pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Plectranthus ornatus is drought-tolerant and suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.
