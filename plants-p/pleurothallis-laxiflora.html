Pleurothallis laxiflora

Pleurothallis laxiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers that are often clustered in small inflorescences. It has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern around its stem.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
