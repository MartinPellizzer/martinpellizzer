Philonotion williamsii

Philonotion williamsii, commonly known as the Williams' ghost orchid, is a rare and elusive flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees without deriving nutrients from them. Due to habitat destruction and its limited distribution, Philonotion williamsii is considered endangered and is of great interest to botanists and conservationists.
