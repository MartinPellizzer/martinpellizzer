Pinus cembra f. columnaris

Pinus cembra f. columnaris, commonly known as the columnar pine, is a unique form of the Swiss stone pine.

It is characterized by its narrow, upright growth habit, forming a tall, slender tree with a distinct columnar shape. The foliage consists of dense, needle-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. This variant is often found in high-altitude regions, where it thrives in cold and harsh climates.

Its botanical features make it a striking ornamental plant in both natural and cultivated settings.
