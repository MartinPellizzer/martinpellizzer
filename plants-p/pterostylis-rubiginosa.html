Pterostylis rubiginosa

Pterostylis rubiginosa, commonly known as the red-stained helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. The plant features a slender, hairy stem with a single, erect, reddish-brown flower that resembles a helmet. Its floral structure includes a labellum that is often darker in color, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Pterostylis rubiginosa is a small plant, usually reaching heights of less than 20 centimeters.
