Perymenium jaliscense var. latifolium

Perymenium jaliscense var. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf perymenium, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellowish-orange composite flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
