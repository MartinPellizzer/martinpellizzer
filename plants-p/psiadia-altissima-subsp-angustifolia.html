Psiadia altissima subsp. angustifolia

Psiadia altissima subsp.

angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement.
