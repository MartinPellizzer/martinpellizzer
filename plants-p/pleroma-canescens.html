Pleroma canescens

Pleroma canescens, commonly known as the silverleaf pleroma, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale blue or violet in color. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing on rocky soils.

Pleroma canescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
