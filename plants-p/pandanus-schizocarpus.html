Pandanus schizocarpus

Pandanus schizocarpus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pandanus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, white flowers that develop into fibrous, spiky fruits. Its roots are fibrous and can be used in traditional crafts.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or swampy areas.
