Peperomia hassleri

Peperomia hassleri, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a distinctive silvery or white underside, giving it a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate moisture.

Peperomia hassleri is known for its ease of care and ability to adapt well to indoor environments.
