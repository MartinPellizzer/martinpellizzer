Pelargonium clarum

Pelargonium clarum, commonly known as the clear geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its showy, bright pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has rounded, velvety leaves with a distinctive dark green color and a slightly serrated edge. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.

Pelargonium clarum prefers full sun to partial shade and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
