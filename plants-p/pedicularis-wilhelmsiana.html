Pedicularis wilhelmsiana

Pedicularis wilhelmsiana, commonly known as the yellow monkey orchid, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Orobanchaceae and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that resemble tiny monkeys, hence its common name. Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its ornamental value.
