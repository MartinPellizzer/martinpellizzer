Platanthera blephariglottis f. holopetala

Platanthera blephariglottis f. holopetala, commonly known as the white helleborine, is a rare orchid species characterized by its unique floral structure.

The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surfaces of trees without deriving nutrients from them. It is native to parts of Europe and is often found in moist, shaded forest environments.
