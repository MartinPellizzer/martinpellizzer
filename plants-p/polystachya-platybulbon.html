Polystachya platybulbon

Polystachya platybulbon, commonly known as the flatbulb orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, rhizomatous growth habit and produces clusters of small, white to pale green flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories or along stream banks. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

The orchid's unique floral structure and ecological adaptations make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
