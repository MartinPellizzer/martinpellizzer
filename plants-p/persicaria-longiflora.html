Persicaria longiflora

Persicaria longiflora, commonly known as slender smartweed, is an herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist environments such as wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks. The plant features upright stems that are often reddish in color, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a smooth surface. It produces small, white to pinkish flowers arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

The species is known for its ability to thrive in wet conditions and is often found in disturbed soils.
