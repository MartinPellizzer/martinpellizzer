Piaranthus mennellii

Piaranthus mennellii, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a small, perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features delicate, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, typically blooming in spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and a compact growth habit, making it well-suited for rock gardens or containers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring minimal water once established.

Piaranthus mennellii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
