Pouteria micrantha

Pouteria micrantha, commonly known as the small-fruited soursop, is a tropical plant belonging to the Sapotaceae family.

It is native to Central and South America, particularly found in regions with warm, humid climates. The plant produces small, oval fruits that are typically yellow or green when mature, with a rough, spiny exterior. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and elliptical, with a glossy surface.

The tree can grow up to 15 meters tall and is valued for its ornamental qualities and edible fruit.
