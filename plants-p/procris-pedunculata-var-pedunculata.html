Procris pedunculata var. pedunculata

Procris pedunculata var. pedunculata, commonly known as the white-flowered procris, is a species of flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, white to pale yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or open areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture.

It is a perennial herb that blooms from late spring to early summer.
