Pilosella piloselloides

Pilosella piloselloides, commonly known as the silky weed or silky groundsel, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky soils. Its leaves are hairy and silvery-green, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.
