Persicaria bussei

Persicaria bussei, commonly known as the narrowleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants known for their distinctive leaf shapes and clusters of small flowers. Persicaria bussei produces pinkish to reddish flowers in dense, upright clusters, which attract various pollinators.
