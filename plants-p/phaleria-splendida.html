Phaleria splendida

Phaleria splendida, commonly known as the Splendid Cucumber Tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces yellow-orange fruits that resemble cucumbers, which are edible but often used in traditional medicine. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.

The tree thrives in humid conditions and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
