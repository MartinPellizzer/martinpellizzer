Pteronia camphorata var. stricta

Pteronia camphorata var. stricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved camphor grass, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often aromatic and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers arranged in dense spikes, typically blooming during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
