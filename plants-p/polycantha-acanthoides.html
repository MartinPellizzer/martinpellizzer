Polycantha acanthoides

Polycantha acanthoides, commonly known as the spiny pincushion, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of spiny, fleshy leaves that form a spherical or cylindrical shape, resembling a pincushion. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the cooler months, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
