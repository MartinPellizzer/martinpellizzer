Phyllanthus decipiens var. boivinianus

Phyllanthus decipiens var. boivinianus, commonly known as the Boivin's false crossandra, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous shrub, often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

Its botanical features include yellowish-green flowers clustered in cyathia, which are typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.
