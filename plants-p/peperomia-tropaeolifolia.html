Peperomia tropaeolifolia

Peperomia tropaeolifolia, commonly known as the pepperleaf or pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions.

It features small, ovate to elliptical leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia tropaeolifolia is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
