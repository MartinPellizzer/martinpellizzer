Phebalium bilobum

Phebalium bilobum, commonly known as the two-lobed phebalium, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and visual appeal.
