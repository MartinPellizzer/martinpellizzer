Pilocarpus goudotianus var. mollis

Pilocarpus goudotianus var. mollis, commonly known as the yellow pill plant, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and aromatic leaves, which contain various bioactive compounds. The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in open woodlands and savannas. Its leaves are used in traditional medicine for their stimulant and diuretic properties.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
