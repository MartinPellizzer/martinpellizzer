Piper dennisii

Piper dennisii, commonly known as the Dennis's pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by round, peppery fruits.

Piper dennisii is valued for its unique flavor and is used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.
