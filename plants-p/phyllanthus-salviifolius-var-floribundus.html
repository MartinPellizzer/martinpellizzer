Phyllanthus salviifolius var. floribundus

Phyllanthus salviifolius var. floribundus, commonly known as the flowering phyllanthus, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves with a glossy, dark green surface. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in summer, followed by elliptical, red or orange berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
