Pagesia leucantha

Pagesia leucantha, commonly known as the white-veined orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, pale green to white leaves with prominent white veins. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
