Polygonatum kingianum var. cavaleriei

Polygonatum kingianum var. cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese Solomon's Seal, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.
