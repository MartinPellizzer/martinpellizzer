Philodendron narinoense

Philodendron narinoense, commonly known as the Narino Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy green leaves. The plant produces striking spadices with bright yellow spadix and white bracts, which are typical of the genus Philodendron. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a sought-after houseplant for collectors and enthusiasts.
