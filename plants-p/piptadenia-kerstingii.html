Piptadenia kerstingii

Piptadenia kerstingii, commonly known as the kersting's tree or the yellow glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 20 meters tall, characterized by its smooth, grey bark and compound leaves. The plant produces clusters of yellow, fragrant flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Piptadenia kerstingii is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional uses in local medicine.
