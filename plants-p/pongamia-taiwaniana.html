Pongamia taiwaniana

Pongamia taiwaniana, commonly known as the Taiwan pongamia, is a small to medium-sized leguminous tree native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated pods containing seeds that are often used for ornamental purposes. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
