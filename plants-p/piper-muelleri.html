Piper muelleri

Piper muelleri, commonly known as the Mueller's pepper plant, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Queensland, Australia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that develop into berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understorey of tropical rainforests.

The plant has ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
