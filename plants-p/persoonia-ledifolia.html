Persoonia ledifolia

Persoonia ledifolia, commonly known as the silky persoonia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, leathery leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions and sandy areas.

Persoonia ledifolia is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
