Phyllanthus nobilis var. panamensis

Phyllanthus nobilis var. panamensis, commonly known as the Panamanian sand mat, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It is a low-growing herbaceous plant characterized by its dense clusters of small, white flowers. The leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and have a hairy texture. It thrives in sandy or well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing sandy soils.
