Panicum bambusiflorum

Panicum bambusiflorum, commonly known as bambooflower grass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, erect stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height, often with a bamboo-like appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers, which are typically pale green to brown in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its rapid growth and ability to form dense clumps make it both a useful ground cover and a potential weed in certain environments.
