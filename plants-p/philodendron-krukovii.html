Philodendron krukovii

Philodendron krukovii, commonly known as the Krukovii philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a glossy texture and prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by green, spherical berries. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and growth habits, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
