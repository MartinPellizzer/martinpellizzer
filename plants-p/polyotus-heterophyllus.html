Polyotus heterophyllus

Polyotus heterophyllus, commonly known as the variable leaf water plantain, is a species of aquatic plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its ability to produce leaves of varying shapes and sizes depending on its growth conditions. The plant typically grows in shallow, freshwater habitats such as ponds and wetlands. Its leaves are often long and ribbon-like, with a smooth or slightly serrated edge.

It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
