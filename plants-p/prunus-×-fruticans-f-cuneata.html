Prunus × fruticans f. cuneata

Prunus × fruticans f. cuneata, commonly known as the dwarf cherry or compact cherry, is a compact, shrub-like form of the Prunus genus.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a glossy green on top and slightly hairy underneath. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or white, which bloom in early spring. Its fruit is a small, sweet cherry that ripens in late summer.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden conditions.
