Puschkinia Scilloides Var. Libanotica

Puschkinia scilloides var. libanotica is a striking member of the Amaryllidaceae family known for its vibrant, bell-shaped flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of the eastern Mediterranean, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. Its delicate lilac or purple blooms appear in early spring, adding a touch of color to gardens and natural landscapes. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation, making it a favorite among gardeners. With its elegant appearance and resilience, it stands out as a unique addition to any botanical collection.
