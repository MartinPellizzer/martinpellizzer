Polygala verticillata var. isocycla

Polygala verticillata var. isocycla, commonly known as the spiral milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its botanical name. The plant produces small, usually greenish or yellowish flowers arranged in racemes. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open grasslands or rocky areas.

Due to its unique morphology, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
