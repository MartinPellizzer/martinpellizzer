Peperomia myrtifolia var. distans

Peperomia myrtifolia var. distans, commonly known as the Distal Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink, adding visual interest to any collection. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters, typically during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its slow growth rate and ability to tolerate low light conditions make it an ideal plant for beginners and experienced growers alike.
