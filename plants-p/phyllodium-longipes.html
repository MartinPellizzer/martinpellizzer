Phyllodium longipes

Phyllodium longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed phyllodium, is a species of flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, narrow, and often hairy leaves. The plant produces small, yellow or greenish flowers that are clustered in racemes, and it is typically found in arid or semi-arid regions.
