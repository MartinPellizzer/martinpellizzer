Peperomia trineura var. robustior

Peperomia trineura var. robustior, commonly known as the broad-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, ovate leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purplish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers, which are often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its low maintenance and attractive foliage make it a versatile addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
