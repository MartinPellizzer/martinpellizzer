Pentopetia cotoneaster subsp. pentopetiopsis

Pentopetia cotoneaster subsp.

pentopetiopsis, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a shrub native to China. It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green.

The plant produces clusters of white flowers in the spring, followed by red berries in the fall. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.

The subspecies is characterized by its compact growth habit and adaptability to various soil types.
