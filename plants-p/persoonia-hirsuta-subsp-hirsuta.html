Persoonia hirsuta subsp. hirsuta

Persoonia hirsuta subsp.

hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy persoonia, is a shrub native to Australia. It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant typically grows in heathlands and dry forests, thriving in well-drained soils. Its unique morphology and adaptation to arid conditions make it an important component of its native ecosystem.

The subspecies is distinguished by its dense, hairy foliage and compact growth habit.
