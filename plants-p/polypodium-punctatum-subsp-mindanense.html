Polypodium punctatum subsp. mindanense

Polypodium punctatum subsp.

mindanense, commonly known as the Mindanao polypody, is a fern native to the Philippines. It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The subspecies is distinguished by its unique leaf morphology and geographical distribution.
