Polystichum venosum

Polystichum venosum, commonly known as the veined shield fern, is a species of fern native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The fronds are dark green with a distinctive veined pattern, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland areas and stream banks.

The plant reproduces through spores and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.
