Paeonia × smoutii

Paeonia × smoutii, commonly known as the Smoutii peony, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the芍药科 (Paeoniaceae) family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically display a range of colors from white to deep red, often with intricate patterns and ruffled petals. The plant is native to China and has been cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, requiring moderate water and good air circulation.

Paeonia × smoutii is valued for its long blooming period and is often used in both traditional and modern horticultural settings.
