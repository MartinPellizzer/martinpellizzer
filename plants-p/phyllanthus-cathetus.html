Phyllanthus cathetus

Phyllanthus cathetus, commonly known as the small-leaf phyllanthus, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 3 meters. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences, often with yellow or white petals.

The fruit is a small, capsule-like structure that contains several seeds.
