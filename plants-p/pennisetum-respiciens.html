Pennisetum respiciens

Pennisetum respiciens, commonly known as fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that produce dense, fountain-like clusters of small, fluffy flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Pennisetum respiciens is known for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators such as birds and insects.
