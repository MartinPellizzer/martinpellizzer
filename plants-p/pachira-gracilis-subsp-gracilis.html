Pachira gracilis subsp. gracilis

Pachira gracilis subsp.

gracilis, commonly known as the slender pachira, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America. It features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically arranged in whorls along the stems.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by woody, elongated fruits containing several seeds. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.

This subspecies is well-suited for indoor environments and thrives in bright, indirect light.
