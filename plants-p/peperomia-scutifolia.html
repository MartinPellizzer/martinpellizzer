Peperomia scutifolia

Peperomia scutifolia, commonly known as the shield-leaf peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves that are often marked with silver or white veins, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia scutifolia is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in humid environments.
