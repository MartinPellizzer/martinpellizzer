Pitcairnia trianae var. trianae

Pitcairnia trianae var. trianae, commonly known as the Triana's pitcairnia, is a species of bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped foliage that forms a central cup to collect water. The plant produces striking, inflorescence with vibrant pink or red flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its unique structure and ornamental value make it a popular choice for tropical gardens and collections.
