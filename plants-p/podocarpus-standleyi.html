Podocarpus standleyi

Podocarpus standleyi, commonly known as the Mexican podocarp, is a evergreen coniferous tree native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Podocarpaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and conical shape. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain seeds, typical of conifers. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
