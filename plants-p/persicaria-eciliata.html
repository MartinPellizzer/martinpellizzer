Persicaria eciliata

Persicaria eciliata, commonly known as the slender knotweed, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Asia.

It features upright stems that are often hairy and can grow up to 1 meter in height. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

Persicaria eciliata is often found in moist environments such as wet meadows and stream banks.
