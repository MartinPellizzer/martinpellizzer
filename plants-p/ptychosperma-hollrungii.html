Ptychosperma hollrungii

Ptychosperma hollrungii, commonly known as the African date palm, is a tropical plant native to eastern Africa.

It is a large, ornamental tree that can grow up to 20 meters tall, featuring a slender trunk and broad, fan-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by edible, date-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it both an ecological and horticultural interest in suitable environments.
