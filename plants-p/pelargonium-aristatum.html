Pelargonium aristatum

Pelargonium aristatum, commonly known as the spiked geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and features showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are typically lobed or serrated. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Pelargonium aristatum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
