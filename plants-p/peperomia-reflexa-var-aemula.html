Peperomia reflexa var. aemula

Peperomia reflexa var. aemula, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, velvety foliage with a silvery-blue hue, which is characteristic of its varietal form. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape and often have a waxy texture. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that emerge from the leaf axils.

This plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and minimal water requirements.
