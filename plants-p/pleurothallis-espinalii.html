Pleurothallis espinalii

Pleurothallis espinalii, commonly known as the Espinal's pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It is part of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate floral structure.

Due to its habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
