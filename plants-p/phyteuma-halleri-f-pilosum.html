Phyteuma halleri f. pilosum

Phyteuma halleri f. pilosum, commonly known as the hairy phyteuma, is a flowering plant in the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of bell-shaped, usually blue or purple flowers. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.
