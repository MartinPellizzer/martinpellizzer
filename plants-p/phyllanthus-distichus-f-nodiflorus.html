Phyllanthus distichus f. nodiflorus

Phyllanthus distichus f. nodiflorus, commonly known as the nodiflorous phyllanthus, is a unique variant of the phyllanthus genus.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive foliage, with leaves arranged in a dense, upright manner. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, adding a subtle ornamental value. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions and thrives in well-drained soil with partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental appeal and adaptability, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
