Panicum ingens

Panicum ingens, commonly known as giant panicgrass, is a tall grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It can grow up to 6 meters in height, making it one of the tallest grasses in the world. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces large, fluffy seed heads that are wind-dispersed. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and savannas.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the ecosystems it inhabits.
