Phreatia bicallosa

Phreatia bicallosa, commonly known as the two-spined orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, often pendulous stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with a distinctive two-lobed lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates.

Phreatia bicallosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
