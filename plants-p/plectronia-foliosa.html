Plectronia foliosa

Plectronia foliosa, commonly known as the featherleaf plectronia, is a unique plant species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like leaves that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in greenish-white hues, and are not particularly showy.

Plectronia foliosa is of interest to botanists due to its specialized adaptations to its ecological niche.
