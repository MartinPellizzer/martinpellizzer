Panicum lasiosoma

Panicum lasiosoma, commonly known as silky panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and produces slender, spike-like inflorescences that are often tinged with red or purple. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves with a silky texture, which gives it its common name. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Panicum lasiosoma is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.
