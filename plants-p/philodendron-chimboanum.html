Philodendron chimboanum

Philodendron chimboanum, commonly known as the Chimboanum philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It features heart-shaped, glossy green leaves that are often adorned with intricate vein patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by red or orange berries. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ability to climb and its ornamental foliage make it a valued addition to green spaces.
