Picea excelsa f. gracillima

Picea excelsa f. gracillima, commonly known as the slender silver fir, is a rare and elegant variant of the silver fir species.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and relatively smaller size compared to the typical silver fir. The foliage is dense and dark green, with a silvery-blue hue on the underside of the needles. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in high-altitude regions.

This form is prized for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in specialized gardens.
