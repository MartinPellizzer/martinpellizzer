Podocarpus letocartii

Podocarpus letocartii, commonly known as the Letocartii podocarp, is a species of coniferous tree native to the island of Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Podocarpaceae and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and cone-bearing structure. The plant exhibits a slow growth rate and is often found in humid, tropical forest environments. Its botanical features include needle-like leaves and small, woody cones that mature over several years.

This species plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, contributing to forest structure and biodiversity.
