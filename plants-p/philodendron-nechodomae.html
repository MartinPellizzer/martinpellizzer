Philodendron nechodomae

Philodendron nechodomae, commonly known as the Nechodomae Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
