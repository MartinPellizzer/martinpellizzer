Piper schizonephros

Piper schizonephros, commonly known as the red pepper vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, opposite leaves, and small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruits are fleshy, red berries that contain several seeds.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
