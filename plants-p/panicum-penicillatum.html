Panicum penicillatum

Panicum penicillatum, commonly known as silky grass or panicum grass, is a warm-season grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its fine, silky texture and tufted growth habit, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant produces small, delicate spikelets that are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring minimal water once established.

Panicum penicillatum is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in natural landscapes.
