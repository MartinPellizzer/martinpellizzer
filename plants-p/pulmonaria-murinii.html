Pulmonaria murinii

Pulmonaria murinii, commonly known as mouse ear pulmonaria, is a low-growing perennial plant native to central and eastern Europe.

It belongs to the family Boraginaceae and is characterized by its basal rosettes of hairy, kidney-shaped leaves. Pulmonaria murinii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
