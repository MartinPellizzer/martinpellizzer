Pandanus humbertii

Pandanus humbertii, commonly known as the Humbert's pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pandanaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of the Pacific, particularly found in parts of French Polynesia. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has cultural significance in the regions where it is found.
