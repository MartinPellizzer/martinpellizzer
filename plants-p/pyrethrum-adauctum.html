Pyrethrum adauctum

Pyrethrum adauctum, commonly known as the African pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant is notable for its production of pyrethrin, a natural insecticide used in organic pest control. Pyrethrum adauctum typically grows in dry, sunny environments and thrives in well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value and ecological benefits make it a popular choice for gardens and sustainable landscaping.
