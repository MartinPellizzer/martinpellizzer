Polygonum multiflorum var. angulatum

Polygonum multiflorum var. angulatum, commonly known as the angular polygonum, is a variant of the Chinese knotweed species.

It is characterized by its distinctive angular stems and heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along streambanks.

This variant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
