Phylica vulgaris

Phylica vulgaris, commonly known as the silver bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage and produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
