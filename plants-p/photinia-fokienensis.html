Photinia fokienensis

Photinia fokienensis, commonly known as the Chinese firethorn, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, red berries that persist through winter. The plant produces white to pink flowers in spring, which are followed by the ornamental fruits. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Photinia fokienensis prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
