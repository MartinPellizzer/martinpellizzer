Potentilla verna var. montana

Potentilla verna var. montana, commonly known as mountain cinquefoil, is a low-growing shrub native to mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from April to June. The plant has alternate, palmately lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or rocky habitats.

This variant is notable for its hardy nature and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
