Panicum repens var. leiogonum

Panicum repens var. leiogonum, commonly known as silky foxtail, is a grass species native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silky-textured leaves and inflorescences that resemble a foxtail. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist to well-drained soils.

Due to its rapid growth and ability to spread, it can become a weed in agricultural settings.
