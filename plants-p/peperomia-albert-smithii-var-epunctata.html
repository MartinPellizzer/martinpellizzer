Peperomia albert-smithii var. epunctata

Peperomia albert-smithii var. epunctata, commonly known as the spotted peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features oval, dark green leaves with distinctive white or silver spots, giving it a striking visual appeal. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its unique foliage, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in both home and office settings.
