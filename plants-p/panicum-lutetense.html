Panicum lutetense

Panicum lutetense, commonly known as the French panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces dense, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in wet meadows and marshy areas. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Panicum lutetense is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
