Peperomia obtusifolia var. alveolata

Peperomia obtusifolia var. alveolata, commonly known as the Alveolata Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves with a unique, textured surface that has a slightly raised, honeycomb-like pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of greenish-white, which are typically hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
