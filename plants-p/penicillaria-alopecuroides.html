Penicillaria alopecuroides

Penicillaria alopecuroides, commonly known as the desert milkweed, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of North America.

Penicillaria alopecuroides plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is sometimes used in restoration projects in degraded landscapes.
