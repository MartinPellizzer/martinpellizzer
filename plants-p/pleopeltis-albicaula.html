Pleopeltis albicaula

Pleopeltis albicaula, commonly known as the white-veined rock fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lobed fronds with a distinctive white vein pattern. Pleopeltis albicaula is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
