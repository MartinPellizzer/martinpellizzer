Phoenix hanceana var. formosana

Phoenix hanceana var. formosana, commonly known as the Formosan date palm, is a rare and ornamental palm native to Taiwan.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by edible date-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its limited natural range and habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in the wild.
