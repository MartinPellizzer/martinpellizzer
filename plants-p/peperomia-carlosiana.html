Peperomia carlosiana

Peperomia carlosiana, commonly known as the Carlosiana Peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture, often adorned with subtle variegation. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or red, emerging from the axils of the leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Due to its slow growth and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for tabletop displays and as a houseplant.
