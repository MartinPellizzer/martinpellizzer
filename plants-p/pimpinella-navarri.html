Pimpinella navarri

Pimpinella navarri, commonly known as Navarre cow-wheat, is a perennial herb native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its umbellate inflorescences and aromatic foliage. The plant typically grows in calcareous soils and is often found in open woodlands and rocky areas. Its leaves are pinnately divided, and the flowers are small, white to pale yellow.
