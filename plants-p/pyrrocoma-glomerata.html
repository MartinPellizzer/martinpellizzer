Pyrrocoma glomerata

Pyrrocoma glomerata, commonly known as the clustered pyrrocoma, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small shrub characterized by its dense clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces oval, woody fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Pyrrocoma glomerata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
