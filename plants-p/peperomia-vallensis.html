Peperomia vallensis

Peperomia vallensis, commonly known as the valley peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features broad, fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or green, which are often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia vallensis is relatively easy to care for and is known for its attractive, compact form.
