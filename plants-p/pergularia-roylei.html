Pergularia roylei

Pergularia roylei, commonly known as the Himalayan spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky and alpine environments. The plant features slender, erect stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, clustered in spikes, and come in shades of blue or purple.

Pergularia roylei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its resilience and beauty.
