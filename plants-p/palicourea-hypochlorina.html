Palicourea hypochlorina

Palicourea hypochlorina, commonly known as the blue-eyed whiteleaf.

It is a species of flowering plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically ovate and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Palicourea hypochlorina is often found growing in shaded, humid environments, thriving in the understory of tropical forests.
