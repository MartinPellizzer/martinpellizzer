Piper klugii

Piper klugii, commonly known as the Java pepper or Klug's pepper, is a tropical climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes numerous species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by bright red, peppercorn-like berries. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly serrated margin.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid climates.
