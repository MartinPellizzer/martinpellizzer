Persicaria runcinata

Persicaria runcinata, commonly known as narrowleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous annual plant native to North America.

It features slender, ascending stems that are often reddish in color, and its leaves are lance-shaped with serrated edges. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in dense clusters at the tips of the stems, typically blooming from late spring to early fall. It thrives in moist to wet soils, often found in marshy areas, stream banks, and wet meadows.

Persicaria runcinata is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
