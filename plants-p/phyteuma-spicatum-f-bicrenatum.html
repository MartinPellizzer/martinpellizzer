Phyteuma spicatum f. bicrenatum

Phyteuma spicatum f. bicrenatum, commonly known as the bicrenate phyteuma, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its striking, spires of violet-blue flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and its stems are upright and branched. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

The bicrenate form is distinguished by its unique leaf arrangement and floral structure.
