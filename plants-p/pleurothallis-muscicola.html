Pleurothallis muscicola

Pleurothallis muscicola, commonly known as the mossy orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, greenish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. It is often found growing in association with mosses and other epiphytic plants.

Due to its specialized habitat, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
