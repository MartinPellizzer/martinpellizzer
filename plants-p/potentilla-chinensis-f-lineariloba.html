Potentilla chinensis f. lineariloba

Potentilla chinensis f. lineariloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaf cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and is adapted to temperate climates. It is often found in rocky soils and has a compact growth habit.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
