Pilea hookeriana

Pilea hookeriana, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are round and thick, resembling small coins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It is a member of the Araceae family, which includes other plants like philodendrons and monstera.

Pilea hookeriana is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
