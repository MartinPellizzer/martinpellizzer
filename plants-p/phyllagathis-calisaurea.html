Phyllagathis calisaurea

Phyllagathis calisaurea, commonly known as the slender-leafed piper, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

This species is often found in shaded, moist habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
