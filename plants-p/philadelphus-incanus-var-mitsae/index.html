Philadelphus Incanus Var. Mitsae

Philadelphus incanus var. mitsae is a unique and lesser-known cultivar of the mock orange plant. Native to specific regions in China, it stands out for its delicate, fragrant flowers and compact growth habit. This variety is prized by gardeners for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil conditions. Its foliage offers a soft, glossy appearance that complements its showy blooms. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the horticultural world.
