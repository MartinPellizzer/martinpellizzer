Piper sphaeroides

Piper sphaeroides, commonly known as the globe pepper or spherical pepper, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its small, round, yellowish fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly serrated margin and emits a mild peppery aroma. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in terminal panicles.

The botanical features of Piper sphaeroides make it a distinctive member of the Piperaceae family.
