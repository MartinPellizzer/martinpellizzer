Peperomia apodophylla var. hirtella

Peperomia apodophylla var. hirtella, commonly known as the hairy peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy, hairy leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pale green flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements contribute to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
