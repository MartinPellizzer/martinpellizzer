Prestonia vaupesana

Prestonia vaupesana, commonly known as the Vaupesana prestonia, is a climbing plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae, which includes various species with milky sap and showy flowers. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy surface.
