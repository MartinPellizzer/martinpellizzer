Phoebe sericea

Phoebe sericea, commonly known as silky phoebe, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and avocados. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, small white flowers that appear in clusters. Its leaves are typically glossy and elliptical, with a silvery underside.
