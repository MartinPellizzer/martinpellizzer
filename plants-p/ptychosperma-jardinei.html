Ptychosperma jardinei

Ptychosperma jardinei, commonly known as the African palm, is a tropical evergreen tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers followed by edible, coconut-like fruits. Ptychosperma jardinei is adapted to grow in sandy soils and can thrive in coastal areas.
