Petunga venulosa

Petunga venulosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are often clustered in spikes. Its fruits are capsules that release tiny seeds when mature.

Petunga venulosa is often found in disturbed soils and is considered a weed in agricultural areas.
