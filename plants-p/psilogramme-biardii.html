Psilogramme biardii

Psilogramme biardii, commonly known as the African dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual that grows up to 30 centimeters tall, with lance-shaped leaves and bright yellow flowers. The plant produces small, wind-dispersed seeds that are characteristic of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and traditional medicine.
