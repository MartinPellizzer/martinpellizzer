Polygala mathusiana

Polygala mathusiana, commonly known as the Cape sugarbush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that resemble those of the genus *Bignonia*. The plant typically grows in shrubland and rocky habitats, preferring well-drained soils. It produces yellow or orange flowers that bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
