Peddiea africana var. africana

Peddiea africana var. africana, commonly known as the African peddie, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grassy areas and along riverbanks, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink or purple, blooming in spring.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
