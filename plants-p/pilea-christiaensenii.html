Pilea christiaensenii

Pilea christiaensenii, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to southern China and is characterized by its round, coin-like leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is a popular indoor plant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.

Pilea christiaensenii thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
