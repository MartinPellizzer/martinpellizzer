Plethiandra acuminata

Plethiandra acuminata, commonly known as the slender bamboo, is a member of the grass family (Poaceae).

It is characterized by its slender, erect culms that are often branched and covered with fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, greenish flowers. Its leaves are narrow, linear, and have a smooth or slightly rough texture.

Plethiandra acuminata is native to tropical and subtropical regions and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
