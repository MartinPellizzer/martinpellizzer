Persicaria macrantha subsp. conspicua

Persicaria macrantha subsp.

conspicua, commonly known as the showy smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America. It features upright, branched stems that bear clusters of bright red to purple flowers, which are showy and attract pollinators.

The leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface, and they are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist to wet soils, often found in wetlands, ditches, and along stream banks.

The plant is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
