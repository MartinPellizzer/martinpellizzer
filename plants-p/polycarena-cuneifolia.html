Polycarena cuneifolia

Polycarena cuneifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf polycarena, is a species of flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas, where it typically grows in moist, well-drained soils. The plant features slender, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or red. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.

Polycarena cuneifolia is also used in traditional medicine in some regions for its purported health benefits.
