Pilea crateriforma

Pilea crateriforma, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rosettes of thick, waxy, oval-shaped leaves that are dark green with a slightly textured surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique leaf shape and low maintenance requirements contribute to its popularity as a decorative plant.
