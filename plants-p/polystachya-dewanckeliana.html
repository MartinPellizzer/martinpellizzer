Polystachya dewanckeliana

Polystachya dewanckeliana, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a tropical orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and arranged in a rosette pattern, and it has a rhizomatous root system.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouse settings.
