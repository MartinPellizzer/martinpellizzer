Psychotria mendozii

Psychotria mendozii, commonly known as the Mendoza Psychotria, is a species of flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. It produces small, dark blue to black berries that are often used in traditional medicine.
