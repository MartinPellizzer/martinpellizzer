Pleopeltis tenuicauda

Pleopeltis tenuicauda, commonly known as the narrow-tailed wall-rug, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, strap-like leaves that form a dense, mat-like growth. The plant typically grows on rocky surfaces or tree trunks in humid, shaded environments. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a slender, tapering shape.

Pleopeltis tenuicauda is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
