Pleurothallis ximenae

Pleurothallis ximenae, commonly known as the Ximena's orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and green. The plant grows as an epiphyte, often found on tree branches in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and dark green, adapted to capture limited light.

Pleurothallis ximenae is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique floral structure and relatively easy care requirements.
