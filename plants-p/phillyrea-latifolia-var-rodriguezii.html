Phillyrea latifolia var. rodriguezii

Phillyrea latifolia var. rodriguezii, commonly known as the Rodriguez's broadleaf Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage, with lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its fruit is a small, spherical berry that turns red when mature.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to coastal and rocky environments.
