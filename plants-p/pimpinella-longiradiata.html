Pimpinella longiradiata

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in umbels. Native to regions of Asia, including China and Japan, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant has a taproot system and produces small, dry fruits known as schizocarps.
