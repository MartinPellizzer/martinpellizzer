Pyrus nivalis f. austriaca

Pyrus nivalis f. austriaca, commonly known as the Austrian white pear, is a cultivar of the white pear species.

It is characterized by its small, white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant is typically deciduous, shedding its leaves in the autumn. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and floral display.
