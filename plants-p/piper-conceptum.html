Piper conceptum

Piper conceptum, commonly known as the Vietnamese pepper or wild pepper, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and opposite, ovate leaves. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are followed by small, berry-like fruits that are used in traditional medicine.

The plant is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in various herbal remedies.
