Potentilla × gardneriana

Potentilla × gardneriana, commonly known as the evergreen potentilla, is a hybrid shrub native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and leathery, dark green foliage that remains throughout the year. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers in early summer, which are clustered in rounded inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its hardiness and adaptability make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
