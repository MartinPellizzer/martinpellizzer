Peperomia cookiana var. ovatilimba

Peperomia cookiana var. ovatilimba, commonly known as the ovate-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It features glossy, ovate-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or white, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for container gardening and as a houseplant.
