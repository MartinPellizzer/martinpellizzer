Prunus × fruticans var. microstigma

Prunus × fruticans var. microstigma, commonly known as the dwarf cherry, is a small ornamental shrub native to Japan.

It features compact, upright growth with glossy dark green foliage and produces small, showy pink to white flowers in early spring. The plant is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. Its berries are edible but typically not consumed by humans.

It is often used in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and seasonal interest.
