Polystachya fallax

Polystachya fallax, commonly known as the slender spike orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and compact, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Polystachya fallax is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements in cultivation.
