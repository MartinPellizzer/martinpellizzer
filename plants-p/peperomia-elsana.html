Peperomia elsana

Peperomia elsana, commonly known as the silver mounding peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, waxy foliage with a distinctive silver-gray hue, giving it a striking appearance. The leaves are typically oval to heart-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, enhancing its ornamental appeal. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that emerge from the leaf axils.

This plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation, thriving in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
