Pelargonium × knappianum

Pelargonium × knappianum, commonly known as the knappian geranium, is a hybrid shrub native to South Africa.

It features upright stems with ovate, serrated leaves that are typically green with a slight hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, or purple, which bloom from spring to summer. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a container plant.

Pelargonium × knappianum prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it adaptable to various garden conditions.
