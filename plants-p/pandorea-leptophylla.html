Pandorea leptophylla

Pandorea leptophylla, commonly known as the slender-leaf pandorea, is a climbing shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its woody stems and heart-shaped leaves that are typically small and lanceolate. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are usually pale blue to purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is often cultivated in gardens and natural landscapes.
