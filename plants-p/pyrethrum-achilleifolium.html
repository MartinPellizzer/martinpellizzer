Pyrethrum achilleifolium

Pyrethrum achilleifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, daisy-like yellow flowers and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for producing pyrethrin, a natural insecticide. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and medicinal cultivation.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and valuable in pest control applications.
