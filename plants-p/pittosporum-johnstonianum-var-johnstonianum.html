Pittosporum johnstonianum var. johnstonianum

Pittosporum johnstonianum var. johnstonianum, commonly known as the Johnston's pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery bloom. The plant produces fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. These flowers are followed by small, spherical berries that turn red as they mature.

It is well adapted to coastal environments and thrives in well-drained soils.
