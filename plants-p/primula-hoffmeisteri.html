Primula hoffmeisteri

Primula hoffmeisteri, commonly known as the Hoffmeister's primrose, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its vibrant, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Primula hoffmeisteri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
