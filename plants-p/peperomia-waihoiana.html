Peperomia waihoiana

Peperomia waihoiana, commonly known as the Waihoan peperomia, is a species native to New Zealand.

It is a compact, succulent-like plant with thick, fleshy leaves that are usually green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate light, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for collectors and gardeners.
