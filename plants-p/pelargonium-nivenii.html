Pelargonium nivenii

Pelargonium nivenii, commonly known as the Cape geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and succulent, hairy leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid environments. It produces vibrant pink or red flowers that bloom in spring and summer.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and used in horticultural displays.
