Philodendron marahuacae

Philodendron marahuacae, commonly known as the Mexican heartleaf philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central America.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically bright green with a slightly variegated appearance. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread and climb effectively. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect sunlight.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value.
