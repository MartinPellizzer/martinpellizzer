Phyllocactus phyllanthoides

Phyllocactus phyllanthoides, commonly known as the silver crown cactus, is a unique hybrid between a cactus and a euphorbia.

It features a thick, fleshy stem covered in a dense layer of white, woolly hair that gives it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its silvery foliage. It is native to southern Africa and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Due to its unusual appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
