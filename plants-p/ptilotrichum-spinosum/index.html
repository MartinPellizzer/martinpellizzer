Ptilotrichum Spinosum

Ptilotrichum spinosum is a rare and striking member of the Apiaceae family known for its unique spiny foliage. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are covered in fine, bristle-like hairs that give it a distinctive appearance. Despite its rugged look, it produces delicate white flowers that bloom in late spring. This plant is both a botanical curiosity and an important indicator of its specialized habitat.
