Phacelia Hastata Var. Dasyphylla

Phacelia hastata var. dasyphylla is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the western United States. It is known for its vibrant blue and purple blooms that appear in late spring and early summer. This variety is particularly valued for its ability to thrive in dry, rocky soils and its role in supporting pollinators. Its unique appearance and ecological benefits make it a favorite among gardeners and conservationists alike. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively underappreciated in mainstream horticulture.
