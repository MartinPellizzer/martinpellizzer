Panicum mixtum

Panicum mixtum, commonly known as mixed panic grass, is a low-growing, clump-forming grass that thrives in a variety of soil conditions.

It features fine, silver-green foliage with a compact growth habit, making it ideal for ornamental use in gardens and landscapes. The plant produces delicate, fluffy seed heads that add visual interest during the late summer and autumn months. It is drought-tolerant and prefers full sun, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.

Panicum mixtum is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in disturbed areas.
