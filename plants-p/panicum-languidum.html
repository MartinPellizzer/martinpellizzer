Panicum languidum

Panicum languidum, commonly known as the drooping panic grass, is a flowering plant in the grass family, Poaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its inflorescence consists of a panicle made up of numerous spikelets, which are arranged in a loose, open structure.

The leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color and hairy surfaces.
