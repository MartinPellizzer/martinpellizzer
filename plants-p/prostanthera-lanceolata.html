Prostanthera lanceolata

Prostanthera lanceolata, commonly known as narrow-leaved mintbush, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It features aromatic, narrow lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Prostanthera lanceolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional Indigenous medicine.
