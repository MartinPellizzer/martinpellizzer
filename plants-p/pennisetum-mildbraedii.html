Pennisetum mildbraedii

Pennisetum mildbraedii, commonly known as the narrowleaf fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and feathery plumes that give it a distinctive, ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Pennisetum mildbraedii is also valued for its ability to attract birds and other wildlife.
