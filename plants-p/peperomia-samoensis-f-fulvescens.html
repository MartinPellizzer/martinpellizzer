Peperomia samoensis f. fulvescens

Peperomia samoensis f. fulvescens, commonly known as the Samoan peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a reddish or bronze tinge on the underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its unique foliage and compact growth habit, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
