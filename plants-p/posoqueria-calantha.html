Posoqueria calantha

Posoqueria calantha, commonly known as the velvetleaf or purplestem velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple, depending on the species. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Posoqueria calantha is also known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
