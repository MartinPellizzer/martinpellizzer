Pleurothallis dayana

Pleurothallis dayana, commonly known as the silver orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces tiny, white or pale green flowers that bloom in clusters. It is noted for its unique appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered threatened in the wild.
