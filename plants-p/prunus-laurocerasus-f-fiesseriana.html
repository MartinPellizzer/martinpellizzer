Prunus laurocerasus f. fiesseriana

Prunus laurocerasus f. fiesseriana, commonly known as the Fiesseriana form of the cherry laurel, is a cultivar characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and showy white flowers.

It is a compact shrub that typically grows to a height of about 2 to 3 meters, with a rounded, bushy form. The leaves are leathery and evergreen, providing year-round interest in gardens. Its flowers appear in early spring, adding ornamental value before the leaves fully develop.

This form is prized for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
