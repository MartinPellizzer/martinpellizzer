Pleurothallis pulcherrima

Pleurothallis pulcherrima, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and the production of striking, golden-yellow flowers. The plant typically features long, slender stems with clustered blooms that appear during the rainy season. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a dense, rosette-like pattern.

This orchid is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
