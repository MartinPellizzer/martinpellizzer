Perilla ocymoides var. crispa

Perilla ocymoides var. crispa, commonly known as curled perilla, is a herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive crinkled, wavy leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, pale purple flowers arranged in spikes, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional culinary and medicinal practices.
