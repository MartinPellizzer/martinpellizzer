Polycodium kunthianum

Polycodium kunthianum, commonly known as the Kunth's polycodium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are narrow and linear, while its flowers are small and clustered in a composite head.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
