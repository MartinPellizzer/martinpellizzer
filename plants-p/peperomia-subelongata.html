Peperomia subelongata

Peperomia subelongata, commonly known as the long-stemmed peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features upright, branching stems covered in small, ovate leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually green or red, depending on the variety. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal water needs.

Peperomia subelongata is prized for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for houseplants.
