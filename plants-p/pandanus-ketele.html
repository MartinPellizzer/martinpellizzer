Pandanus ketele

Pandanus ketele, commonly known as the Ketele pandanus, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Pandanaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or green in color. It thrives in coastal and lowland areas, often growing near freshwater sources.

The leaves are sometimes used in traditional crafts and for thatching.
