Pancovia holtzii subsp. holtzii

Pancovia holtzii subsp.

holtzii, commonly known as the desert holly, is a perennial shrub native to arid regions of North America. It features succulent, spiny stems and lance-shaped leaves that are adapted to conserve water.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for drought-tolerant gardens.
