Podocoma spegazzinii

Podocoma spegazzinii, commonly known as the Spegazzini's podocarp, is a unique evergreen shrub native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Podocarpaceae family, which includes various coniferous and shrub-like plants. The plant is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around the stems. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers and is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
