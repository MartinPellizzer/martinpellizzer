Phragmipedium hartwegii f. baderi

Phragmipedium hartwegii f. baderi, commonly known as the Bader's phragmipedium, is a rare and unique orchid species native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature a blend of green, yellow, and red hues. The plant grows as a terrestrial orchid, often found in shaded, moist environments with rich, organic soil. Its inflorescence is usually a single, elongated spike bearing several blooms, and it is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and rarity.
