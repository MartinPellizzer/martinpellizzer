Paphiopedilum concolor subsp. reynieri

Paphiopedilum concolor subsp.

reynieri, commonly known as the Yunnan butterfly orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to China, particularly Yunnan Province. It features a unique, butterfly-like lip that is often brightly colored, typically in shades of yellow and red.

The plant produces a single, upright stem with several lance-shaped leaves and blooms with a single, showy flower. It prefers shady, moist environments and is known for its ornamental value in horticulture.

This subspecies is valued for its striking appearance and is often cultivated for its beauty.
