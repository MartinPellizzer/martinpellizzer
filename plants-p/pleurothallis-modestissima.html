Pleurothallis modestissima

Pleurothallis modestissima, commonly known as the modest orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, often with intricate patterns on the petals. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit.

Due to its beauty and rarity, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
