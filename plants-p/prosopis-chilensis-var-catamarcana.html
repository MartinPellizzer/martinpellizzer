Prosopis chilensis var. catamarcana

Prosopis chilensis var. catamarcana, commonly known as the Catamarca Prosopis, is a shrub or small tree native to Argentina and Chile.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow flowers. The plant is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in dry regions.

It produces small, woody pods that contain edible seeds, which are used by local communities for food and traditional purposes.
