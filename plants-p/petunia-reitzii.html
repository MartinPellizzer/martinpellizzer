Petunia reitzii

Petunia reitzii, commonly known as the Reitzii petunia, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial, depending on the climate, and features trailing stems that make it suitable for hanging baskets or containers.

Its foliage is heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are tubular at the base and expand into a five-lobed shape.
