Penstemon rattanii var. rattanii

Penstemon rattanii var. rattanii, commonly known as the rattanii penstemon, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This species is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.
