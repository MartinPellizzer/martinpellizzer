Peperomia quadrifolia f. angusta

Peperomia quadrifolia f. angusta, commonly known as the narrow-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
