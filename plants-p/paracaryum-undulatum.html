Paracaryum undulatum

Paracaryum undulatum, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, undulating leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, and purple, which bloom during the summer months. Its leaves are typically arranged in a zigzag pattern along the stems, creating an ornamental appearance.

Paracaryum undulatum is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its unique foliage and vibrant floral display.
