Paspalum humboldtianum var. elegantissimum

Paspalum humboldtianum var. elegantissimum, commonly known as the elegant paspalum, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It features slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with spikelets that are often tinged with red or purple hues. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along waterways.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in regions with a subtropical or temperate climate.
