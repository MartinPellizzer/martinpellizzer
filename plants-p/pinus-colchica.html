Pinus colchica

Pinus colchica, commonly known as the Colchis pine, is a species of coniferous tree native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and dense, pyramidal crown. The tree has needle-like leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in clusters of five. Its cones are woody and can reach lengths of up to 15 centimeters.

This species is adapted to a variety of soil types and is often found in mountainous areas with moderate rainfall.
