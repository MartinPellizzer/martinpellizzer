Pavonia cryptocalyx

Pavonia cryptocalyx, commonly known as the narrowleaf pavonia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 feet. The plant features small, showy pink or red flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Pavonia cryptocalyx is often found in moist, open habitats such as woodlands and wetlands.
