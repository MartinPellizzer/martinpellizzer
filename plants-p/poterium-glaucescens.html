Poterium glaucescens

Poterium glaucescens, commonly known as the blue-green grass.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family, characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish in color. It is native to South Africa and thrives in sandy or rocky soils.

Poterium glaucescens is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive, feathery appearance.
