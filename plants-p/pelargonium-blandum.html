Pelargonium blandum

Pelargonium blandum, commonly known as the sandstone geranium, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a velvety texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

This plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
