Pterocassia polyantha

Pterocassia polyantha, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year, making it a striking ornamental plant. The plant has compound leaves with elongated leaflets and produces small, winged fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Pterocassia polyantha is often cultivated for its aesthetic value and can also provide habitat for various insects and birds.
