Peperomia bueana

Peperomia bueana, commonly known as the Bueana Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink, giving it an ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, which bloom in the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia bueana is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
