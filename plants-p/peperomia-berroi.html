Peperomia berroi

Peperomia berroi, commonly known as the Emerald Gem, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia berroi is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
