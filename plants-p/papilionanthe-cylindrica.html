Papilionanthe cylindrica

Papilionanthe cylindrica, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

Papilionanthe cylindrica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
