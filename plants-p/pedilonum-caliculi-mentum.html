Pedilonum caliculi-mentum

Pedilonum caliculi-mentum, commonly known as the moonlit cup-ment, is a rare flowering plant characterized by its unique morphology.

It features a short, sturdy stem with broad, waxy leaves that resemble small cups. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers that open at night, emitting a subtle fragrance. Its roots are fibrous and capable of absorbing moisture from the soil efficiently.

Native to humid, shaded environments, it thrives in cool, moist climates.
