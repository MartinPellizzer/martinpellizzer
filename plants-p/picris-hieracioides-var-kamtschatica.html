Picris hieracioides var. kamtschatica

Picris hieracioides var. kamtschatica, commonly known as Kamtschatka hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the cold climates of eastern Siberia and the Russian Far East.

It features upright, branched stems covered in dense, yellowish-green foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subarctic environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in ecological studies of high-altitude flora.
