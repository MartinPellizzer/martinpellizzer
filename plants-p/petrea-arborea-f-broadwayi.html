Petrea arborea f. broadwayi

Petrea arborea f. broadwayi, commonly known as the Broadway's flame tree, is a compact, evergreen shrub or small tree native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its showy, bright orange-red bracts that resemble flames, which appear in clusters at the ends of its branches. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by elongated, woody fruits.

This ornamental plant is well-suited for tropical and subtropical climates, thriving in full sun and well-drained soil.
