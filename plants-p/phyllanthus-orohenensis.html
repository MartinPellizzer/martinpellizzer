Phyllanthus orohenensis

Phyllanthus orohenensis, commonly known as the African spurge, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant produces yellowish-green capsules that contain numerous seeds. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Phyllanthus orohenensis is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
