Pothos rumphii var. giganteus

Pothos rumphii var. giganteus, commonly known as the giant pothos, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length, often with a waxy texture and a deep green color. This variety thrives in bright, indirect light and is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its ability to climb or trail makes it suitable for hanging baskets or shelves.

The plant is also known for its air-purifying qualities, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
