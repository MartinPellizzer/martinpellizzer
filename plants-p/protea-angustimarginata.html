Protea angustimarginata

Protea angustimarginata, commonly known as the narrow-margin protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences composed of numerous papilionaceous flowers, often in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant typically grows in fynbos vegetation, thriving in well-drained, acidic soils. Its leaves are leathery and narrow, with a distinctive white margin.

Protea angustimarginata is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
