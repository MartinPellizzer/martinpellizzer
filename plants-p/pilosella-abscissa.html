Pilosella abscissa

Pilosella abscissa, commonly known as the slender pearly everlasting, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features small, white or pale yellow flowers that are surrounded by bracts resembling leaves. Pilosella abscissa typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands.

It is known for its delicate appearance and is often used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms.
