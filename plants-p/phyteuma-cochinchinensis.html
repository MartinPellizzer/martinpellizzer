Phyteuma cochinchinensis

Phyteuma cochinchinensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are lanceolate and often have a hairy texture.
