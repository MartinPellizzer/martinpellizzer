Plectranthus ramosior

Plectranthus ramosior, commonly known as the woolly plectranthus, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves that give it a velvety appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Plectranthus ramosior is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and ease of cultivation.
