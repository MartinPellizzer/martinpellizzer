Parnassia longipetala var. brevipetala

Parnassia longipetala var. brevipetala, commonly known as the short-petalled parnassia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

Its leaves are broadly ovate with a smooth margin, and the plant produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in late summer.
