Panicum pulipes

Panicum pulipes, commonly known as the slender panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, fluffy panicles that are often wind-pollinated. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open fields or along roadsides.

Panicum pulipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in disturbed areas.
