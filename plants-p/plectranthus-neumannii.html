Plectranthus neumannii

Plectranthus neumannii, commonly known as the Cape rosemary, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and mild aromatic scent.

Plectranthus neumannii thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.
