Peperomia schultzei

Peperomia schultzei, commonly known as the Schultze's pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It features dense, waxy, ovate leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish or bronze tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are clustered in spikes, often emerging from the leaf axils. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Peperomia schultzei is also valued for its ability to purify the air and its ornamental appeal in both foliage and flowering.
