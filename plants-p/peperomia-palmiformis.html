Peperomia palmiformis

Peperomia palmiformis, commonly known as the palmleaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features broad, waxy, and often lobed leaves that resemble palm fronds, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its slow growth rate and low maintenance requirements make it an ideal plant for both novice and experienced gardeners.
