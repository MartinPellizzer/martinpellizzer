Peridium bicolor var. tomentosum

Peridium bicolor var. tomentosum, commonly known as the hairy two-colored peridium, is a rare variant of the peridium species characterized by its distinctive two-colored appearance.

Its leaves are typically a mix of green and white, with a fuzzy, tomentose texture that gives it a soft, woolly appearance. The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellow in color.

The peridium bicolor var. tomentosum is noted for its unique morphology and is of interest to botanists studying plant adaptation and variation.
