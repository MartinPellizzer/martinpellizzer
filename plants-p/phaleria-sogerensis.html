Phaleria sogerensis

Phaleria sogerensis, commonly known as the Soger's evergreen tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and can grow up to 20 meters tall. Its leaves are typically elliptical with a smooth surface and a strong aromatic scent.

Phaleria sogerensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
