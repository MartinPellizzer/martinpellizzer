Proustia cuneifolia subsp. cuneifolia

Proustia cuneifolia subsp.

cuneifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf proustia, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically linear and pointed, growing in dense clusters.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-yellow in color, arranged in spikes. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species for ecological studies and horticultural interest.
