Pentapeltis peltigera

Pentapeltis peltigera, commonly known as the golden snake plant, is a tropical evergreen with a distinctive, upright growth habit.

It features long, strap-like leaves that are typically a bright yellow-green in color, often with a subtle variegation. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in warm, humid environments. It is known for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
