Peperomia endlicheri var. fijiana

Peperomia endlicheri var. fijiana, commonly known as the Fijian peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It features small, waxy, ovate leaves that are usually dark green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces delicate, spathaceous inflorescences with tiny, inconspicuous white flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
