Pachyphytum coeruleum

Pachyphytum coeruleum, commonly known as the Azure Pachyphytum, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The leaves are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating that reduces water loss. It produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

This plant is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
