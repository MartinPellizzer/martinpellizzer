Podopterus guatemalensis

Podopterus guatemalensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan orchid, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as tropical rainforests. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the rich, organic soil of its habitat.

Podopterus guatemalensis is valued not only for its aesthetic appeal but also for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
