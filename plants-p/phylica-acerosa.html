Phylica acerosa

Phylica acerosa, commonly known as the silver tree, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and dense, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

The silver tree is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in gardens and landscapes.
