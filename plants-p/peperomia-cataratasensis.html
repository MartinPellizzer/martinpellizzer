Peperomia cataratasensis

Peperomia cataratasensis, commonly known as the Cataratas peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive foliage. The plant features broad, glossy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green and silver. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color.

Peperomia cataratasensis is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.
