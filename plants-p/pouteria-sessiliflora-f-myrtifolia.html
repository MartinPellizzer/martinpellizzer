Pouteria sessiliflora f. myrtifolia

Pouteria sessiliflora f. myrtifolia, commonly known as the myrtle-leaved pouteria, is a tropical plant belonging to the Sapotaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in small inflorescences. Its fruit is a small, fleshy drupe with a smooth, yellowish skin.
