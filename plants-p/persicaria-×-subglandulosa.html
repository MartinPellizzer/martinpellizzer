Persicaria × subglandulosa

Persicaria × subglandulosa, commonly known as the hybrid smartweed, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in moist soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks.

Persicaria × subglandulosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing habitat and food for various insects and wildlife.
