Portulaca kermesina var. kermesina

Portulaca kermesina var. kermesina, commonly known as the crimson portulaca, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its fleshy, succulent stems and leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces vibrant pink to red flowers that bloom in summer, adding a striking visual element to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

This variety is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
