Podanthum amplexicaule var. aucheri

Podanthum amplexicaule var. aucheri, commonly known as the silver velvet plant, is a low-growing perennial succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, silver-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
