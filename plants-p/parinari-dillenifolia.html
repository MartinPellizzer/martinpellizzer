Parinari dillenifolia

Parinari dillenifolia, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits containing several seeds.
