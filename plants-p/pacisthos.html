Pacisthos

Pacisthos, commonly known as the peace plant, is a rare and unique species native to the high altitudes of the Andes.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale blue. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported calming properties.
