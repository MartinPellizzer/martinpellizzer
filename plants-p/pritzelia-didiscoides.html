Pritzelia didiscoides

Pritzelia didiscoides, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale blue in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

Pritzelia didiscoides is often found in rocky soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.
