Polygala exilis

Polygala exilis, commonly known as the slender milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features small, delicate flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, bean-like seed pods.

Polygala exilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
