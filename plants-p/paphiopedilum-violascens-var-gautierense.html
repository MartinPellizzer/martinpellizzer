Paphiopedilum violascens var. gautierense

Paphiopedilum violascens var. gautierense, commonly known as the purple-flowered slipper orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Vietnam and Laos.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its sympodial growth habit and elongated pseudobulbs. The plant produces striking violet to purple flowers that are often adorned with intricate patterns, resembling a slipper or a pouch. Its leaves are typically elliptical and arranged in a rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This variety is valued in horticulture for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
