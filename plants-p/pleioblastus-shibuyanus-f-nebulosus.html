Pleioblastus shibuyanus f. nebulosus

Pleioblastus shibuyanus f. nebulosus, commonly known as the Cloudy Shibuya bamboo, is a cultivar of the Shibuya bamboo species.

It is characterized by its distinctive, pale green to white variegated foliage that creates a striking visual contrast. The plant features slender, upright culms that are often used in ornamental landscaping. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This bamboo is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for gardeners.
