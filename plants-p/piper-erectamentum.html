Piper erectamentum

Piper erectamentum, commonly known as the upright pepper plant, is a species of flowering vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants such as black pepper. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into elongated, fleshy berries. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped with a glossy surface and a strong, peppery aroma.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its unique, spicy scent.
