Peperomia phrymatopsis var. brevipedunculata

Peperomia phrymatopsis var. brevipedunculata, commonly known as the compact peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and red, which are borne on short, branched inflorescences. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

This variety is prized for its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
