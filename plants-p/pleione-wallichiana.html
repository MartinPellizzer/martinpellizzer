Pleione wallichiana

Pleione wallichiana, commonly known as the Himalayan orchid, is a herbaceous plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its erect stem and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms in spring, producing white or pale yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas at elevations between 2,000 and 4,000 meters.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
