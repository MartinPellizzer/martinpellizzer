Peperomia cubilquitziana

Peperomia cubilquitziana, commonly known as the Mexican peperomia, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a slight pubescence. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

Peperomia cubilquitziana is relatively drought-tolerant and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.
