Polygala hainanensis var. hainanensis

Polygala hainanensis var. hainanensis, commonly known as Hainan polygala, is a flowering plant native to the Hainan Island in China.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth or along stream banks. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.

Due to its restricted habitat, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
