Pennisetum cenchroides

Pennisetum cenchroides, commonly known as purple moondrop, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, plume-like inflorescences that range in color from purple to pink and white. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Pennisetum cenchroides is often used in ornamental gardening for its visual appeal and drought tolerance.
