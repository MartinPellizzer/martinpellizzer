Piper leiophyllum

Piper leiophyllum, commonly known as the smooth-leaved pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its glossy, smooth leaves and small, clustered white flowers. Its fruits are small, three-lobed berries that contain the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.

Piper leiophyllum is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in herbal remedies.
