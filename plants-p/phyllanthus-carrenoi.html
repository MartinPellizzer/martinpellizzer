Phyllanthus carrenoi

Phyllanthus carrenoi, commonly known as the Malabar phyllanthus, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its small, greenish-yellow flowers clustered in dense inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, tropical environments and has a short, shrub-like stature. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a waxy texture.
