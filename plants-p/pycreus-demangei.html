Pycreus demangei

Pycreus demangei, commonly known as the Demange's pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant produces small, spherical seed heads that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by the wind. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Pycreus demangei is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in horticulture.
