Platycrater involucrata f. plenissima

Platycrater involucrata f. plenissima, commonly known as the full-flowered Japanese spurge, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features dense clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that provide year-round visual interest. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its ornamental value lies in its abundant flowering and compact growth habit.
