Parodia Mammulosa Subsp. Submammulosa

Parodia mammulosa subsp. submammulosa is a captivating cacti species native to southern Africa. It is known for its unique, spherical shape and vibrant green color with distinct white bumps. This plant thrives in arid environments and requires minimal water to survive. Its compact size makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens. With proper care, it can produce stunning flowers that add a touch of beauty to any collection.
