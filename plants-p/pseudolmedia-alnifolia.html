Pseudolmedia alnifolia

Pseudolmedia alnifolia, commonly known as the narrowleaf alder, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the western United States.

It belongs to the family Betulaceae, which includes birches and alders, and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically oval to lance-shaped. Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
