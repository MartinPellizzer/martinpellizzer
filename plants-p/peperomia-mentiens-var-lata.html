Peperomia mentiens var. lata

Peperomia mentiens var. lata, commonly known as the broad-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions.

It features glossy, ovate to lance-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, often in shades of pink or white, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

This variety is prized for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
