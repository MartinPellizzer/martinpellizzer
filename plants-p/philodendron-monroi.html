Philodendron monroi

Philodendron monroi, commonly known as the Mexican evergreen philodendron, is a climbing plant native to Central America.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, pale greenish-white flowers, though it is primarily grown for its foliage rather than its blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Philodendron monroi is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
