Pilea commersoniana

Pilea commersoniana, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China and Taiwan.

It features round, shield-shaped leaves that are glossy and slightly waxy in texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are typically hidden beneath its foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea commersoniana is known for its ease of care and ability to purify the air, contributing to its widespread popularity as a houseplant.
