Petaloxis

Petaloxis, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Petaloxis is sometimes considered a weed due to its rapid growth and ability to spread easily.
