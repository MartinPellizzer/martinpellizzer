Paraixeris denticulata var. dilatata

Paraixeris denticulata var. dilatata, commonly known as the broad-leaved paraixeris, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves with serrated edges. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often found in moist, shaded environments. Its flowers are small and clustered in cyathia, a feature typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.

The varietal form dilatata is distinguished by its broader leaves compared to the typical variety.
