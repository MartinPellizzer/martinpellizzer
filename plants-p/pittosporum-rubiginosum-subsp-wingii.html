Pittosporum rubiginosum subsp. wingii

Pittosporum rubiginosum subsp.

wingii, commonly known as the New Zealand winged pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand. It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for native birds. It thrives in coastal and lowland forest environments, often growing in well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
