Phillyrea rubioana

Phillyrea rubioana, commonly known as the Spanish broom.

It is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its dense, thorny growth and yellow flowers. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it adaptable to various coastal and rocky habitats.

Phillyrea rubioana is also valued for its ornamental qualities and use in traditional medicine.
