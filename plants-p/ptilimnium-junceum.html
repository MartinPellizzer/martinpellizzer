Ptilimnium junceum

Ptilimnium junceum, commonly known as slender bladderwort, is a small aquatic plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater habitats such as ponds and ditches. The plant has delicate, thread-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers. It is characterized by its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor water conditions.

Ptilimnium junceum is often found in wetland environments and plays a role in maintaining aquatic ecosystems.
