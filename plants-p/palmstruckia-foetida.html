Palmstruckia foetida

Palmstruckia foetida, commonly known as the stinking palm, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its palm-like leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant is known for its strong, unpleasant odor, which is most noticeable when the flowers are in bloom. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, shaded environments.

Its unique appearance and scent make it a notable species in botanical studies.
