Pennisetum exiguum

Pennisetum exiguum, commonly known as dwarf fountain grass, is a compact, ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a distinctive fountain-like growth habit. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that add visual interest, especially in the fall. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Pennisetum exiguum is valued for its low maintenance requirements and ability to provide texture and movement in the garden.
