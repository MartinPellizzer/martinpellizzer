Poaephyllum fuscum

Poaephyllum fuscum, commonly known as the darkleaf poaephyllum, is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate flowers.

Its small, yellowish-white flowers bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
