Potentilla adscendens f. robusta

Potentilla adscendens f. robusta, commonly known as the robust potentilla, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has elliptical leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and its stems are woody and often branched. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy and resilient species.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
