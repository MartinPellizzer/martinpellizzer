Pyrrhocactus krausii

Pyrrhocactus krausii, commonly known as the red-spined cactus, is a species of cactus native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are typically green with red or purple spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are clustered at the ends of the stems. It thrives in well-drained sandy soils and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
