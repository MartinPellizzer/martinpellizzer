Phalaenopsis decumbens var. lobbii

Phalaenopsis decumbens var. lobbii, commonly known as the Lobb's phalaenopsis, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendant inflorescences that bear numerous fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, exhibiting an epiphytic growth habit. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and often have a waxy texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in a suitable environment.
