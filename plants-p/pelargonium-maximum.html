Pelargonium maximum

Pelargonium maximum, commonly known as the large geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its various health benefits.
