Pleuroplitis centrasiatica

Pleuroplitis centrasiatica, commonly known as the Central Asian pleurothallis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually small and delicate, with a variety of colors including white, yellow, and pink.

It has a complex root system adapted to absorb nutrients from the poor soils of its habitat.
