Pleurospermum rubellum

Pleurospermum rubellum, commonly known as redstem pleurospermum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its bright yellow flowers bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators.

The leaves are usually basal, linear, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
