Peperomia ibiramana

Peperomia ibiramana, commonly known as the Ibirama peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Brazil.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink, giving it an ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia ibiramana is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.
