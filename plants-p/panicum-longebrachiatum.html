Panicum longebrachiatum

Panicum longebrachiatum, commonly known as the long-bracted panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and long, bracted inflorescences that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture and a bluish-green color.

Panicum longebrachiatum is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
