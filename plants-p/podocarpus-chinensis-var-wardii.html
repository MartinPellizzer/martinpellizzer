Podocarpus chinensis var. wardii

Podocarpus chinensis var. wardii, commonly known as the Chinese podocarp or Ward's podocarp, is a flowering plant in the family Podocarpaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including parts of China, India, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage, with lanceolate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It produces small, spherical cones that contain seeds, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

This species is adapted to a variety of soil types and can thrive in both tropical and subtropical climates.
