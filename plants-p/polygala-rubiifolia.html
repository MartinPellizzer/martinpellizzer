Polygala rubiifolia

Polygala rubiifolia, commonly known as the red-leaf polygala, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces delicate, pinkish-white flowers arranged in spicate inflorescences. Its leaves are often red or reddish-purple, which gives it its common name.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained conditions.
