Peltogyne campestris var. rigida

Peltogyne campestris var. rigida, commonly known as the rigid acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant has rigid, often thorned stems and bipinnate leaves with small, ovate leaflets. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in dense, cylindrical clusters.

The species is adapted to dry environments and is often found in savannas and open woodlands.
