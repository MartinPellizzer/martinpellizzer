Pimpinella magna var. dissecta

Pimpinella magna var. dissecta, commonly known as the dissected cow parsley, is a biennial or short-lived perennial plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.
