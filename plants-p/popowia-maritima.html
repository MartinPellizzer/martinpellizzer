Popowia maritima

Popowia maritima, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean and the eastern Mediterranean.

It is a perennial shrub that thrives in sandy or rocky soils, often found in coastal dunes and saline environments. The plant features spiny leaves and showy blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. Its adaptations to salty and windy conditions make it a hardy species suited to harsh maritime climates.

Popowia maritima is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing coastal ecosystems.
