Phenax rugosus f. uberrima

Phenax rugosus f. uberrima, commonly known as the velvetleaf begonia, is a striking ornamental plant characterized by its velvety, dark green foliage.

The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, with a prominent central vein and a slightly wavy margin. This variant exhibits a more robust and lush growth habit compared to other forms of Phenax rugosus. It thrives in partial to full shade and requires consistent moisture to maintain its vibrant appearance.

The plant is prized for its aesthetic value in both indoor and outdoor garden settings.
