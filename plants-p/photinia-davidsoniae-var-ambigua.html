Photinia davidsoniae var. ambigua

Photinia davidsoniae var. ambigua, commonly known as the Chinese photinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It features attractive, glossy, dark green leaves that turn vibrant red in the fall. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in early spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping.

Its growth habit is compact, making it suitable for hedging and container gardening.
