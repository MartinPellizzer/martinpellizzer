Philodendron millerianum

Philodendron millerianum, commonly known as the Mexican evergreen philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with yellow or white patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by round, red berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Philodendron millerianum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
