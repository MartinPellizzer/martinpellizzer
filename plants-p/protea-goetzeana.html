Protea goetzeana

Protea goetzeana, commonly known as the Goetze's protea, is a species native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shrubland and coastal vegetation, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowers are characterized by their vibrant pink to red colors and are arranged in a cylindrical inflorescence.

Protea goetzeana plays an important ecological role, providing nectar for various pollinators.
