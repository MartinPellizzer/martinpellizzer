Pharbitis nil var. abbreviata

Pharbitis nil var. abbreviata, commonly known as the dwarf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Asia.

It is a herbaceous annual that grows up to 30 centimeters in height, featuring heart-shaped leaves and showy blue to purple flowers. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and flowers.

However, it can become invasive in certain environments if not managed properly.
