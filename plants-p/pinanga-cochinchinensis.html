Pinanga cochinchinensis

Pinanga cochinchinensis, commonly known as the Philippine bamboo palm, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is native to the Philippines and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers followed by edible, round fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
