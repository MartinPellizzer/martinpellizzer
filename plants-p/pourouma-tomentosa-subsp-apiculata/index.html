Pourouma Tomentosa Subsp. Apiculata

Pourouma tomentosa subsp. apiculata is a unique species of palm native to the Amazon rainforest. Known for its striking appearance and ecological importance, this plant thrives in the humid, nutrient-rich soils of the region. Its leaves are large, feather-like, and often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant plays a vital role in supporting local biodiversity and maintaining the delicate balance of its ecosystem. Due to habitat loss, it faces increasing threats to its survival.
