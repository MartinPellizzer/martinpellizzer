Parapolystichum barterianum

Parapolystichum barterianum, commonly known as the Barter's shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polystichaceae.

It is characterized by its robust, evergreen fronds that are typically leathery and dark green in color. Native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping underground stems that help in spreading and forming dense colonies.

Its spore-producing structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the fronds, contributing to its reproductive strategy.
