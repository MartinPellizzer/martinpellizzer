Pleurothallis tikalensis

Pleurothallis tikalensis, commonly known as the Tikal orchid, is a species native to the rainforests of Central America.

It is a small epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant has slender, wiry stems and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted for efficient water absorption in its natural habitat.

Pleurothallis tikalensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts of rare orchids.
