Peperomia cuneata

Peperomia cuneata, commonly known as the Shiny Cane Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually green or red in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Peperomia cuneata prefers well-draining soil and moderate watering to prevent root rot.
