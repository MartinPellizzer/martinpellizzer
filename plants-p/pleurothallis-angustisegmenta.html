Pleurothallis angustisegmenta

Pleurothallis angustisegmenta, commonly known as the narrow-segmented pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features elongated, narrow leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. Its blooms are usually clustered in inflorescences and are adapted to attract specific pollinators.

Due to its ornamental value and habitat specificity, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
