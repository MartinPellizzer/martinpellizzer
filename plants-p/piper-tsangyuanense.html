Piper tsangyuanense

Piper tsangyuanense, commonly known as the Guangxi pepper vine, is a tropical climbing shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by elongated, peppery fruits. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped with a rough texture and a distinct aromatic scent.

It is often cultivated for its culinary and medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
