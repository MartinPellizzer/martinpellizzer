Potentilla recta genuina

Potentilla recta genuina, commonly known as the true upright potentilla, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wet meadows or along stream banks. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a slightly serrated edge.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in garden landscaping.
