Polygala shinnersii

Polygala shinnersii, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, often pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has small, lance-shaped leaves. It produces small, bean-like pods that contain the seeds.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
