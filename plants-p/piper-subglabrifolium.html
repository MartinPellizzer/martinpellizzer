Piper subglabrifolium

Piper subglabrifolium, commonly known as the slenderleaf pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and small, clustered flowers. Its fruits are berries that contain the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.

Piper subglabrifolium is often cultivated for its medicinal properties and as a spice.
