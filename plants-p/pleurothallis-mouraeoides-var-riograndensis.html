Pleurothallis mouraeoides var. riograndensis

Pleurothallis mouraeoides var. riograndensis, commonly known as the Rio Grande pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, tropical forest environments. The plant features delicate, narrow leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

This variety is part of the larger Pleurothallis genus, which includes over 1,000 species of terrestrial and epiphytic orchids.
