Poa sichotensis

Poa sichotensis, commonly known as the Sichotian meadow grass, is a species of grass native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate environments, often found in meadows and along riverbanks. Its flowering stems bear small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny flowers.

Poa sichotensis plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity in its native habitat.
