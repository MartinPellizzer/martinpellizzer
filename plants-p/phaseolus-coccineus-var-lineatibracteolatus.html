Phaseolus coccineus var. lineatibracteolatus

Phaseolus coccineus var. lineatibracteolatus, commonly known as the scarlet runner bean, is a climbing legume native to the Americas.

It features vibrant red or orange flowers and long, edible pods that contain numerous seeds. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for both ornamental and culinary purposes.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice in gardens and for bean production.
