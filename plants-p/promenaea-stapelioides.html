Promenaea stapelioides

Promenaea stapelioides, commonly known as the Ghost Plant, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, pale green leaves that resemble ghostly shapes, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to survive.

Its distinctive appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
