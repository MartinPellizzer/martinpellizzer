Piqueria sodiroi

Piqueria sodiroi, commonly known as the Mexican pricklypoppy, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered with fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Piqueria sodiroi is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
