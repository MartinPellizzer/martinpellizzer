Papaver pseudocanescens subsp. udocanicum

Papaver pseudocanescens subsp.

udocanicum, commonly known as the Udocanica poppy, is a rare and endemic species found in specific regions of southern Europe. It is characterized by its striking pink to red flowers and papery, often hairy leaves.
