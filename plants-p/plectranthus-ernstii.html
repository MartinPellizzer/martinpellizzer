Plectranthus ernstii

Plectranthus ernstii, commonly known as the silver bush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and small, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to white. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers. Its aromatic leaves release a pleasant scent when crushed, and it is often used in traditional medicine.

Plectranthus ernstii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
