Parodia taratensis

Parodia taratensis, commonly known as the Taratensis Cactus, is a species of cactus native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are often covered in white to yellowish spines. The plant produces small, white flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for succulent collectors.
