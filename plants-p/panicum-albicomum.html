Panicum albicomum

Panicum albicomum, commonly known as silver panicum, is a flowering grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and its distinctive silver-white to gray-green foliage. The plant produces fluffy, light green to brownish inflorescences that resemble a bottlebrush, which are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Panicum albicomum is also valued for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
