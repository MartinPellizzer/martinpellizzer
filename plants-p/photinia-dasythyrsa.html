Photinia dasythyrsa

Photinia dasythyrsa, commonly known as the glossy leaf photinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green, glossy leaves that provide striking visual contrast against its red or orange bark. The plant produces small, white flowers in spring, followed by red or orange berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for hedges or as a specimen plant in gardens.
