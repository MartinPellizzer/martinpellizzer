Prasophyllum densiflorum

Prasophyllum densiflorum, commonly known as the dense-flowered leek orchid, is an endemic Australian plant belonging to the orchid family.

It is a small, terrestrial herb with a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces dense clusters of pale green to yellowish flowers. The plant is adapted to grow in heathland and woodland environments, typically in areas with well-drained soils. It is often found in regions of New South Wales and Victoria.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered threatened and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
