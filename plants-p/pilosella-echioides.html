Pilosella echioides

Pilosella echioides, commonly known as the hairy bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a low-growing habit with hairy stems and narrow, linear leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow or white, arranged in a single head with ray florets.
