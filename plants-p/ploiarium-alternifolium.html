Ploiarium alternifolium

Ploiarium alternifolium, commonly known as the alternating leaf moss, is a unique species of moss characterized by its distinctive leaf arrangement.

Its leaves are alternately positioned along the stem, giving it a visually striking appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest undergrowth or along rocky surfaces. It has a simple yet efficient structure, with a protonema stage that aids in its dispersal.

Due to its delicate nature, it requires careful cultivation to maintain its health and growth.
