Peperomia pyramidata

Peperomia pyramidata, commonly known as the Shiny Leaf Peperomia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It features glossy, dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a compact and attractive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia pyramidata is a low-maintenance plant that is well-suited for indoor cultivation.
