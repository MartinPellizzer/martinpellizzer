Populus cilicica

Populus cilicica, commonly known as the Eastern Poplar, is a deciduous tree native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that turn yellow in the autumn. The tree produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous areas.

Populus cilicica is valued for its rapid growth and has been used in reforestation efforts.
