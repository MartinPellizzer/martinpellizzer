Panicum linkianum

Panicum linkianum, commonly known as linkian grass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, blade-like leaves. The plant produces small, fluffy panicles that are often used in ornamental landscaping. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for dry climates.

Its adaptability and drought resistance contribute to its popularity in xeriscaping and erosion control.
