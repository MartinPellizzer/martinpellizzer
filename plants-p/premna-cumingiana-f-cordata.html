Premna cumingiana f. cordata

Premna cumingiana f. cordata, commonly known as the heart-shaped premna, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are usually glossy and have a slightly serrated margin.
