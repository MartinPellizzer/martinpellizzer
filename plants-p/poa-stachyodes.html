Poa stachyodes

Poa stachyodes, commonly known as silver hair grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern South America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces slender, spike-like inflorescences that contain small, spikelet-based flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and wetlands.

Poa stachyodes is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in wetland environments.
