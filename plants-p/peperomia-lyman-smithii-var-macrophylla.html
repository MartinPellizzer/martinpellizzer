Peperomia lyman-smithii var. macrophylla

Peperomia lyman-smithii var. macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or green. The plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and drought tolerance.

Its foliage is often used in decorative gardening for its ornamental appeal and ease of care.
