Pityrogramma triangularis var. maxonii

Pityrogramma triangularis var. maxonii, commonly known as the narrow-leaf water clover, is a fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, trifoliate leaves that are often submerged or floating on water surfaces. The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in aquatic gardens.
