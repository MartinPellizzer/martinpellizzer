Pityrogramma schaffneri

Pityrogramma schaffneri, commonly known as the Schaffner's shield fern, is a member of the water fern family, Marsileaceae.

It is characterized by its small, floating leaves that are typically less than 1 inch in diameter. The plant has a unique structure with a central stalk and four leaflets that form a shield-like shape. It thrives in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds and ditches.

Its reproductive structures include spore-producing sporocarps, which are essential for its propagation.
