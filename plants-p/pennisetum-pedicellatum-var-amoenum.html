Pennisetum pedicellatum var. amoenum

Pennisetum pedicellatum var. amoenum, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that produce fluffy, purple-tinted plumes during the late summer and autumn months. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are generally green with a slight blue or silver hue. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant, feathery appearance and ability to add texture and color to the landscape.
