Poa acicularis

Poa acicularis, commonly known as the needlegrass, is a species of grass native to the Southern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its slender, sharp leaves that give it its common name. This plant is typically found in alpine and subalpine regions, thriving in cool, moist environments. Poa acicularis has a clumping growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil in high-altitude ecosystems.
