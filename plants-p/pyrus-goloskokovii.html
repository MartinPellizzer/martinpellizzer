Pyrus goloskokovii

Pyrus goloskokovii, commonly known as the Goloskokov pear, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to the Caucasus region and is characterized by its small, ornamental leaves and clusters of white flowers. The plant produces small, edible pears that are typically used in local cuisines. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens.
