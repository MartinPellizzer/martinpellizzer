Pyrethrum woronowii

Pyrethrum woronowii, commonly known as the Siberian pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow-orange composite flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, spherical flower heads composed of numerous tiny florets. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Pyrethrum woronowii is valued for its natural pyrethrin content, which makes it a source of botanical insecticides.
