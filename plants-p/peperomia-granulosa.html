Peperomia granulosa

Peperomia granulosa, commonly known as the granular peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are often covered in a fine, granular texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Peperomia granulosa is valued for its unique foliage and ability to add a touch of greenery to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
