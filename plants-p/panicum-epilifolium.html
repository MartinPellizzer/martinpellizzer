Panicum epilifolium

Panicum epilifolium, commonly known as the silky grass or silky panicum, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, fluffy panicles of flowers that are often pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, giving it a silvery appearance.

Panicum epilifolium is often found in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.
