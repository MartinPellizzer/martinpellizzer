Pelargonium × burnettianum

Pelargonium × burnettianum, commonly known as the Burnettian geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, five-petaled flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant typically has a compact growth habit with rounded leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Native to South Africa, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

It is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
