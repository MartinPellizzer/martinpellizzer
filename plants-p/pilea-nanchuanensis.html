Pilea nanchuanensis

Pilea nanchuanensis, commonly known as the Nanchuan begonia, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Begoniaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with dense foliage, often reaching a height of up to 30 centimeters. Its flowers are usually red or pink, with distinctive bracts that enhance its ornamental appeal.

Pilea nanchuanensis is valued in horticulture for its aesthetic qualities and relatively easy care requirements.
