Phalaenopsis bellina f. fangtasticana

Phalaenopsis bellina f. fangtasticana, commonly known as the Fangtasticana Phalaenopsis, is a hybrid orchid species prized for its ornamental value.

It originates from the hybridization of Phalaenopsis bellina with other Phalaenopsis species, resulting in unique floral characteristics. The plant features dense, velvety leaves and produces clusters of long-lasting, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It is a sympodial orchid, meaning it grows in a horizontal growth pattern with multiple stems.

This variety is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
