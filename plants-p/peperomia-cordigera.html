Peperomia cordigera

Peperomia cordigera, commonly known as the heart-leaf peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly textured surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or red, adding subtle interest to its appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia cordigera is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
