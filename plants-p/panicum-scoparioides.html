Panicum scoparioides

Panicum scoparioides, commonly known as silver panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance, and its inflorescence consists of open, loose spikelets. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping. It is often used as a ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.

Panicum scoparioides is also valued in ecological restoration projects for its ability to stabilize soil and support local wildlife.
