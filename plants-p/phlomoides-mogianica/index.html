Phlomoides Mogianica

Phlomoides mogianica is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the wetlands of Brazil. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its striking purple and white blossoms. This species thrives in humid, marshy environments and is often found near water bodies. Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts. Its unique beauty and ecological importance make it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts.
