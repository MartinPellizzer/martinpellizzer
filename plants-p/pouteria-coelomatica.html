Pouteria coelomatica

Pouteria coelomatica, commonly known as the Brazilian starfruit or golden starfruit, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy yellow flowers. Pouteria coelomatica is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
