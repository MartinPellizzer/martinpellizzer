Paeonia cambessedesii

Paeonia cambessedesii, commonly known as the Cambessedes peony, is a rare and endemic species native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink, with a distinctive fragrance. The plant belongs to the芍药科 (Paeoniaceae) family and is known for its ornamental value in horticulture. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a cool climate with adequate sunlight.
