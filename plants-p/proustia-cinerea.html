Proustia cinerea

Proustia cinerea, commonly known as the silverleaf proustia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, pale blue or violet flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a hardy perennial in suitable climates.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
