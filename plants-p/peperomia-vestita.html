Peperomia vestita

Peperomia vestita, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It features thick, velvety, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of green or red, which are followed by reddish-brown fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate moisture.

Peperomia vestita is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
