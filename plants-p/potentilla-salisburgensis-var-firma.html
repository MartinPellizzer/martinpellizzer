Potentilla salisburgensis var. firma

Potentilla salisburgensis var. firma, commonly known as the Salzburg rose.

This variant is a compact, low-growing shrub native to the alpine regions of Central Europe. It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has finely divided, dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms.

It is well-suited for rock gardens and requires well-drained soil and full sun.
