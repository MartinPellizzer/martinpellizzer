Preslia cervina subvar. villiflora

Preslia cervina subvar. villiflora, commonly known as the slender cow-wheat, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and grasslands. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and arranged in racemes, while its roots form symbiotic relationships with nitrogen-fixing bacteria.

This subspecies is distinguished by its slender stems and finely divided leaves.
