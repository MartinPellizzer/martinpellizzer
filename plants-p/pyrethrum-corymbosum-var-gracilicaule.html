Pyrethrum corymbosum var. gracilicaule

Pyrethrum corymbosum var. gracilicaule, commonly known as slender-stemmed pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its aromatic foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
