Phrynium unilaterale

Phrynium unilaterale, commonly known as the one-sided arrowhead plant, is a tropical perennial belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are often asymmetrical, giving it its common name. The plant produces vibrant, spatulate leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length, with a glossy, dark green surface. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is native to parts of Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands.

Phrynium unilaterale is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
