Plantago major f. yezomaritima

Plantago major f. yezomaritima, commonly known as the Yezomaritima plantain, is a botanical variant of the common plantain.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves with a prominent midrib and a dense covering of fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, typical of the Plantaginaceae family. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.

This form is noted for its adaptability and resilience in various environmental conditions.
