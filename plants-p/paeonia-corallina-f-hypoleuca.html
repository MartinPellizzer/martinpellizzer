Paeonia corallina f. hypoleuca

Paeonia corallina f. hypoleuca, commonly known as the white coral peony, is a rare and striking cultivar of the peony plant.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that contrast beautifully with its lush green foliage. The plant is native to China and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens. It typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter.

Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a delicate and elegant touch to the landscape.
