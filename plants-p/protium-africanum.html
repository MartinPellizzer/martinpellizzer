Protium africanum

Protium africanum, commonly known as the African sycamore, is a medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and simple, alternate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by oval, woody fruits containing a single seed. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The tree is also valued for its durable wood, which is used in construction and furniture-making.
