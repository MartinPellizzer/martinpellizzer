Pittosporum pancheri

Pittosporum pancheri, commonly known as the Pancher's pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its aromatic, leathery leaves that are often reddish or bronze in color when young. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are clustered together, followed by orange to red berries. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas.

Pittosporum pancheri is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitat.
