Primula reinii var. kitadakensis

Primula reinii var. kitadakensis, commonly known as the Japanese primrose, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It prefers cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and shady borders.
