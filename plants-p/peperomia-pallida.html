Peperomia pallida

Peperomia pallida, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, waxy leaves that are typically silver or gray-green in color, with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green or white, which are often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia pallida is known for its low maintenance and ability to adapt well to a variety of indoor conditions.
