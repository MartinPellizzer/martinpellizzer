Polygonum grandifolium

Polygonum grandifolium, commonly known as the large-leaf smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern North America.

It typically grows in moist environments such as wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks. The plant features alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often reddish or purple-tinged on the underside. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink or red, appearing in late summer to early fall.

Polygonum grandifolium is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed soils.
