Pleurothallis acanthodes

Pleurothallis acanthodes, commonly known as the spiny leech orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often fragrant flowers that range in color from white to yellow. The plant typically produces a single, elongated inflorescence bearing several small, lance-shaped flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike.
