Plectranthus aulihanensis

Plectranthus aulihanensis, commonly known as the Cape umbrella plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive umbrella-like clusters of small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of purple and blue. The plant has a trailing growth habit and produces aromatic leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright light for optimal growth.

Plectranthus aulihanensis is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
