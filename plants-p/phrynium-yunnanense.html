Phrynium yunnanense

Phrynium yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to China, particularly Yunnan Province.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are often adorned with distinctive markings. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a spadix, which is enclosed by a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its foliage is characterized by a waxy texture and deep green color, making it a visually striking plant in its native habitat.
