Perymenium rotundisquamum

Perymenium rotundisquamum, commonly known as the round-scaled perymenium, is a unique plant characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology.

It features broad, rounded leaves that are covered in small, scale-like structures, giving it a textured appearance. The plant is native to specific regions in South America, where it thrives in humid, tropical environments. Its growth habit is typically shrub-like, with multiple stems branching out from the base.

Botanically, it is noted for its unusual floral structure, which includes modified leaves that serve as reproductive organs.
