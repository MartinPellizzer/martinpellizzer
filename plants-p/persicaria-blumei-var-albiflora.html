Persicaria blumei var. albiflora

Persicaria blumei var. albiflora, commonly known as whiteflower smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Asia.

It features erect, branched stems that are often reddish in color, and it produces small, white to pale pink flowers in clusters. The leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in moist soils and is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and marshy areas.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in wetland habitats.
