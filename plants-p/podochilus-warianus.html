Podochilus warianus

Podochilus warianus, commonly known as the Malayan orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a rhizomatous root system and produces showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within tropical forests. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow with a distinctive lip that aids in pollination.

Podochilus warianus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
