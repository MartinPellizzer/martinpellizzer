Panicum sanguinolentum

Panicum sanguinolentum, commonly known as crimson panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and vibrant red or orange panicles. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it a common sight in open landscapes. Its striking floral display makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The seeds are small and wind-dispersed, contributing to its natural spread in suitable environments.
