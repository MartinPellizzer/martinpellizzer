Pometia lactescens

Pometia lactescens, commonly known as the milkwood tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that can grow up to 15 meters in height. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, and it produces woody capsules as fruit.

The bark and leaves of Pometia lactescens contain compounds that have been used in traditional medicine.
