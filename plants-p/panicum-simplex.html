Panicum simplex

Panicum simplex, commonly known as little barley, is a grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and is often found in prairie and woodland habitats. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture.

Panicum simplex is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological restoration projects.
