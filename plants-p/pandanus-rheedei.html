Pandanus rheedei

Pandanus rheedei, commonly known as the giant pandanus, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and long, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or green in color. Its roots are fibrous and help stabilize the soil in coastal areas.

Pandanus rheedei is often used in traditional crafts and has cultural significance in many island communities.
