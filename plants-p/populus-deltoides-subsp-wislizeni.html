Populus deltoides subsp. wislizeni

Populus deltoides subsp.

wislizeni, commonly known as the Wislizen's cottonwood, is a deciduous tree native to the western United States. It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, fluffy seed heads that release seeds in the spring.

The plant thrives in riparian zones and is adapted to moist, well-drained soils. It has a rapid growth rate and is often found along rivers and streams.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological restoration and wildlife habitat.
