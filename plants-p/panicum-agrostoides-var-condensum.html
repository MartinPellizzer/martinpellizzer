Panicum agrostoides var. condensum

Panicum agrostoides var. condensum, commonly known as black panicum, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, dark green leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. The plant produces dense, spike-like inflorescences that are typically brown or black in color, giving it its common name. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it suitable for use in xeriscaped gardens.
