Panicum nitidum var. densiflorum

Panicum nitidum var. densiflorum, commonly known as denseflower panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, fluffy flowers, which are often white or pale pink in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for use in ornamental landscaping.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for erosion control and habitat support in native ecosystems.
