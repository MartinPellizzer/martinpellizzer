Paphiopedilum lindenii

Paphiopedilum lindenii, commonly known as the Lindenberg's slipper orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, pouch-like lip that resembles a slipper, a feature typical of the Paphiopedilum genus. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments with well-drained soil. Its flowers are usually yellow or green with reddish or purple markings, and it produces a single bloom per stem.

Paphiopedilum lindenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
