Peperomia tetraphylla var. piedadeana

Peperomia tetraphylla var. piedadeana, commonly known as the Piedadeana Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features dense, waxy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental foliage and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
