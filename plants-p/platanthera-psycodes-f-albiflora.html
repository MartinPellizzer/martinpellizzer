Platanthera psycodes f. albiflora

Platanthera psycodes f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered lady's tresses, is a rare orchid species characterized by its distinctive, fragrant, white flowers arranged in a loose, spiraling spike.

The plant has long, strap-like leaves and relies on mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient uptake, a common trait among orchids.
