Philbornea

Philbornea, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or pale yellow. Philbornea is often found in moist, shady environments such as forests or along riverbanks.

It is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value or used in traditional medicine.
