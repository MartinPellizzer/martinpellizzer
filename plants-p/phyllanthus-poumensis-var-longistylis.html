Phyllanthus poumensis var. longistylis

Phyllanthus poumensis var. longistylis, commonly known as the long-styled phyllanthus, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features small, clustered leaves with a glossy surface and produces tiny, yellowish-green flowers arranged in inflorescences. The plant is characterized by its elongated stamens, which give it its varietal name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is noted for its potential medicinal uses in traditional herbal practices.
