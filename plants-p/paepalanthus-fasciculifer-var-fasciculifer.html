Paepalanthus fasciculifer var. fasciculifer

Paepalanthus fasciculifer var. fasciculifer, commonly known as the clustered arrowroot.

This plant is a herbaceous perennial native to the Andean regions of South America. It features a rhizomatous root system and produces clusters of small, white, tubular flowers. The plant's leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

It is adapted to grow in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous habitats.
