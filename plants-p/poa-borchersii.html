Poa borchersii

Poa borchersii, commonly known as the Borchers' bluegrass, is a species of grass native to the western United States.

It is a perennial plant that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats, including slopes and open woodlands. The plant has fine, blue-green leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is well adapted to arid conditions and is often found in areas with low soil moisture.

Poa borchersii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in native plant communities.
