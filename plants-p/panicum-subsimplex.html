Panicum subsimplex

Panicum subsimplex, commonly known as little bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that are often used in ornamental gardening. It is well adapted to prairie ecosystems and is drought-tolerant.

Panicum subsimplex plays an important role in soil stabilization and wildlife habitat.
