Paeonia delavayi var. lutea

Paeonia delavayi var. lutea, commonly known as the Yellow Peony, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features large, palmately compound leaves with a serrated edge. It typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

The Yellow Peony is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
