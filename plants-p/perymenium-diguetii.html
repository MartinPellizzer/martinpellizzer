Perymenium diguetii

Perymenium diguetii, commonly known as the yellow ghost orchid, is a rare and enigmatic orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated inflorescence that resembles a ghostly figure, giving it its common name. The plant produces striking yellow flowers with intricate patterns that mimic the appearance of a ghostly face. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in the understory of dense rainforests.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, Perymenium diguetii is highly sought after by botanists and conservationists alike.
