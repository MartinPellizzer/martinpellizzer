Pandanus koidzumii

Pandanus koidzumii, commonly known as the Koidzumi pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the pandan family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. This plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are typically yellow or orange in color.

It thrives in humid, coastal environments and is often found growing near mangroves or in sandy soils.
