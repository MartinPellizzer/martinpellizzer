Pancratium americanum

Pancratium americanum, commonly known as the American yellow bulbous plant, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and bulbous base, which store nutrients for the plant. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and swampy areas, where it thrives in moist, sandy soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it blooms from late summer to early fall.

Pancratium americanum is also known for its use in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant floral display.
