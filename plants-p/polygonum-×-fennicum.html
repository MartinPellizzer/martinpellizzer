Polygonum × fennicum

Polygonum × fennicum, commonly known as the hybrid knotweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Polygonaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Polygonum orientale and Polygonum sachalinense. The plant typically features upright, reddish stems with alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves that have a distinctive reddish or purplish hue. Its small, clustered flowers are usually greenish-white and appear in late summer.

Polygonum × fennicum is often found in moist, disturbed soils along streambanks and roadsides.
