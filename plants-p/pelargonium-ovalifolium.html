Pelargonium ovalifolium

Pelargonium ovalifolium, commonly known as the oval-leaf geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its oval-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. Its compact growth habit and long blooming period make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Pelargonium ovalifolium is also valued for its aromatic foliage, which can be used in herbal teas or as a natural remedy.
