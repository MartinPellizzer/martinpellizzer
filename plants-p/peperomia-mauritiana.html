Peperomia mauritiana

Peperomia mauritiana, commonly known as the Mauritius pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface, often adorned with distinctive veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, which are followed by unique, club-shaped fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia mauritiana is known for its low maintenance and ability to adapt well to various indoor environments.
