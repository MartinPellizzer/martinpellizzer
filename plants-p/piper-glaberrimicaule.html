Piper glaberrimicaule

Piper glaberrimicaule, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed pepper vine, is a tropical plant belonging to the Piperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, glabrous stems and heart-shaped, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into three-lobed, berry-like fruits. It is native to the humid lowland forests of Southeast Asia, particularly in regions like Indonesia and Malaysia.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in warm climates.
