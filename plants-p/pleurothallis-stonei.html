Pleurothallis stonei

Pleurothallis stonei, commonly known as the stone's pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It is part of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in horticulture.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments to ensure its survival.
