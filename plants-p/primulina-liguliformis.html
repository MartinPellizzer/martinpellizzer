Primulina liguliformis

Primulina liguliformis, commonly known as the narrow-leafed primula, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rock crevices. Its leaves are usually narrow and lance-shaped, while the flowers come in shades of white, pink, and red.

Primulina liguliformis is popular among collectors for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
