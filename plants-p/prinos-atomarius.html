Prinos atomarius

Prinos atomarius, commonly known as the narrow-leafed prion, is a species of flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal areas. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. Prinos atomarius is characterized by its compact growth habit and drought tolerance.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
