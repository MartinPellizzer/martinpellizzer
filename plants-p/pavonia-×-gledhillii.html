Pavonia × gledhillii

Pavonia × gledhillii, commonly known as the Gledhill's wood rose, is a hybrid plant in the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, rose-like flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a white or yellow center. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with hairy, ovate leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is native to the southeastern United States.

Pavonia × gledhillii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various soil types.
