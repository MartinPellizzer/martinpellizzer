Polystachya elegans

Polystachya elegans, commonly known as the elegant spike orchid, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and marshy areas. Its inflorescence consists of multiple spikes of white or pale yellow flowers, which bloom during the rainy season.

Polystachya elegans is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
