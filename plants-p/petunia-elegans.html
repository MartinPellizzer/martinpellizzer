Petunia elegans

Petunia elegans, commonly known as the elegant petunia, is a species native to South America.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and tobacco. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in a range of colors, including white, pink, and purple. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Petunia elegans is also noted for its relatively compact growth habit and ability to bloom continuously throughout the growing season.
