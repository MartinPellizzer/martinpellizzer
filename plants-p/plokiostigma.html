Plokiostigma

Plokiostigma, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in a wide range of colors. The leaves are typically velvety and heart-shaped, often with a waxy coating. These plants are popular as houseplants due to their ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

They thrive in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
