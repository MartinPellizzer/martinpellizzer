Parasenecio hastatus var. orientalis

Parasenecio hastatus var. orientalis, commonly known as the oriental hawksbeard, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been introduced to other parts of the world for horticultural purposes.
