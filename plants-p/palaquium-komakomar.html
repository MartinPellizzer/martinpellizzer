Palaquium komakomar

Palaquium komakomar, commonly known as the Komakomar apple.

It is a small evergreen tree native to Madagascar, belonging to the Sapotaceae family. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically red or yellow in color, with a sweet and tart flavor. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and elliptic in shape, often with a glossy surface.
