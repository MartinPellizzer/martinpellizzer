Physaria scrotiformis

Physaria scrotiformis, commonly known as the bladderpod, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, which includes mustard and cabbage plants, and is characterized by its unique, inflated seed pods that resemble small bladders.
