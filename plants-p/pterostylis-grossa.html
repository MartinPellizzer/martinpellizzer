Pterostylis grossa

Pterostylis grossa, commonly known as the large helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, helmet-like floral structure. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a prominent, elongated labellum.

Pterostylis grossa is a perennial herb that produces a single, showy flower per stem, often blooming in spring or early summer.
