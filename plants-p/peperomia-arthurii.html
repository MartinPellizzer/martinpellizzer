Peperomia arthurii

Peperomia arthurii, commonly known as the Arthurii Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale green, arranged in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia arthurii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
