Polycarena selaginoides

Polycarena selaginoides, commonly known as the silver thread plant, is a perennial evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, needle-like leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery bloom, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers during the spring and summer months. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and aesthetic appeal.

Polycarena selaginoides prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and container planting.
