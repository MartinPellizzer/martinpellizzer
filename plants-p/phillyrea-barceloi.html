Phillyrea barceloi

Phillyrea barceloi, commonly known as the Barcelona Phillyrea, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features narrow, dark green leaves that are leathery and arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in clusters during the spring, which are followed by small, round berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Phillyrea barceloi is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in Mediterranean climates.
