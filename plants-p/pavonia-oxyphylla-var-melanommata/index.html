Pavonia Oxyphylla Var. Melanommata

Pavonia oxyphylla var. melanommata is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the southeastern United States. It is known for its vibrant red flowers and distinctive dark purple stems that stand out in any garden. This variant of Pavonia oxyphylla is particularly prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil conditions. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often used in landscaping for its hardy nature. Whether in gardens or as a container plant, this species adds a bold and colorful presence to the landscape.
