Pleioneura griffithiana

Pleioneura griffithiana, commonly known as the Himalayan pleione, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact, rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow or greenish-white with intricate patterns. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountain forests and alpine meadows.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
