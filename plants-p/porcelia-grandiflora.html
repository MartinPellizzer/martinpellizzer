Porcelia grandiflora

Porcelia grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora porcelain plant, is a striking ornamental species native to tropical regions.

It features large, waxy, porcelain-like leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from deep green to vibrant red or purple. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or red, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor cultivation.
