Peperomia mathieui

Peperomia mathieui, commonly known as the Mathieu's Peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, often with a slight reddish or bronze tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color, which are borne on short spikes. It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.

Peperomia mathieui prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
