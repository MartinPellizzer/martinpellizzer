Polygala sinensis

Polygala sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are alternate and often have a distinctive heart-shaped base.

The flowers, which are usually pink or white, attract various pollinators including bees and butterflies.
