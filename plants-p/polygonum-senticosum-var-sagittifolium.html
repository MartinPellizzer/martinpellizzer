Polygonum senticosum var. sagittifolium

Polygonum senticosum var. sagittifolium, commonly known as arrowleaf knotweed, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive arrowhead shape, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in late summer. Its stems are often reddish and covered in fine hairs, while the leaves are usually smooth and slightly hairy underneath.

It is often found in moist environments such as stream banks and wet meadows.
