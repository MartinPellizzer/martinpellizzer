Polypodium fortunei

Polypodium fortunei, commonly known as the Chinese shield fern, is a species of fern native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery fronds that form a compact, shield-like structure. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine, and it is valued for its ornamental appearance.

Polypodium fortunei is a hardy plant that can thrive in a variety of soil types as long as it remains consistently moist.
