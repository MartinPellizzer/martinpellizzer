Peperomia subanomala

Peperomia subanomala, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia subanomala is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal.
