Philadelphus grandiflorus var. floribundus

Philadelphus grandiflorus var. floribundus, commonly known as the floribundus mock orange, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

Its rapid growth habit makes it suitable for hedges or screens in landscaping.
