Pultenaea biloba var. scabra

Pultenaea biloba var. scabra, commonly known as the rough pultenaea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant has a woody stem and produces pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

The rough texture of its stems and leaves gives it its common name.
