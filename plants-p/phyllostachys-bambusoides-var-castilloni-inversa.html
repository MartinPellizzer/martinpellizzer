Phyllostachys bambusoides var. castilloni-inversa

Phyllostachys bambusoides var. castilloni-inversa, commonly known as the reverse castillon bamboo, is a variegated cultivar of the giant bamboo species.

It is characterized by its unique coloration, featuring a mix of white, green, and gold stripes along the culms. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in horticultural displays. The plant grows in a clumping form and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

It is relatively low maintenance but requires protection from strong winds and extreme weather conditions.
