Poa portalii

Poa portalii, commonly known as the portal sedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. Poa portalii is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical features make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
