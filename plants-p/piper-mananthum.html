Piper mananthum

Piper mananthum, commonly known as the Mananthu pepper, is a climbing vine native to the rainforests of India.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when mature, containing aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are often used in Ayurvedic practices for their purported health benefits.

The plant's botanical features include opposite, ovate leaves and a distinctive, pungent aroma.
