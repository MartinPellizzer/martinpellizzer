Pyrus andersonii

Pyrus andersonii, commonly known as Anderson's pear, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to the western United States, particularly found in California and Nevada. This shrub or small tree typically grows in rocky soils and arid environments. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a serrated margin.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by edible, sweet fruits that are similar to pears.
