Potentilla × argolitana

Potentilla × argolitana, commonly known as the Argolic potentilla, is a hybrid shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage, often featuring bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region, particularly Greece, and thrives in well-drained soils. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in mild climates.

Due to its hybrid origin, it exhibits a range of morphological variations depending on the parent species.
