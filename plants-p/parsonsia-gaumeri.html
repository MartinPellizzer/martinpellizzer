Parsonsia gaumeri

Parsonsia gaumeri, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. Its flowers are tubular and have a unique structure that attracts nectar-feeding birds and insects.
