Physalis kansuensis

Physalis kansuensis, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive inflated calyx that encloses the fruit, resembling a small lantern. The plant is native to China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its fruit, which turns red when mature, is edible and has a tangy, slightly sweet flavor.

Physalis kansuensis thrives in temperate climates and is relatively easy to grow in gardens.
