Plantago kurtzii

Plantago kurtzii, commonly known as the Kirtz's plantain, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and riverbanks. The plant features a rosette of broad, heart-shaped leaves covered in fine hairs. It produces small, greenish flowers in dense clusters during the warmer months.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological studies and potential medicinal applications.
