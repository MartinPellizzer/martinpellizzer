Protasparagus filicinus var. lycopodineus

Protasparagus filicinus var. lycopodineus, commonly known as the lycopodineus variant of the fern-like protasparagus, is a rare and unique plant species characterized by its intricate frond structure.

It exhibits a mix of fern-like and lycopod features, with elongated, segmented leaves that resemble those of clubmosses. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate forest understories. Its reproductive structures are distinctive, featuring sporangia that produce spores for propagation.
