Panicum heterostachyum

Panicum heterostachyum, commonly known as the tall bluestem grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often blue-green in color. The plant produces panicles of small, spikelet-like flowers that are typically pale green to brown in hue. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is cultivated in gardens and used in ecological restoration projects.
