Pseudostellaria rupestris

Pseudostellaria rupestris, commonly known as the rock starwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early summer, typically arranged in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in a fine layer of hair.
