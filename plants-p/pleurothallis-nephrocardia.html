Pleurothallis nephrocardia

Pleurothallis nephrocardia, commonly known as the kidney-heart orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in the understory of humid forests. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers with white petals and a yellow lip. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires high humidity for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
