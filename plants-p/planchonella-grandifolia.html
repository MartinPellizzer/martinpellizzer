Planchonella grandifolia

Planchonella grandifolia, commonly known as the large-leafed native hibiscus, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia and the Pacific.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. Its leaves are large, glossy, and ovate, with a smooth texture and a dark green color. The plant produces clusters of small, white, five-petaled flowers that bloom throughout the year.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and coastal areas.
