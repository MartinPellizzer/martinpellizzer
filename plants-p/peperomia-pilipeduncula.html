Peperomia pilipeduncula

Peperomia pilipeduncula, commonly known as the laceleaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with a distinctive lace-like pattern on the underside, giving it a unique visual appeal. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, which appear in late summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Peperomia pilipeduncula is relatively low-maintenance and resistant to pests, making it a popular choice for gardeners and plant enthusiasts.
