Penstemon payettensis

Penstemon payettensis, commonly known as Payette penstemon, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a perennial species that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils in mountainous regions. The plant produces tall, slender stems with showy, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. It is adapted to cold climates and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Penstemon payettensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
