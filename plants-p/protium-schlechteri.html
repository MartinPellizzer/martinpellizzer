Protium schlechteri

Protium schlechteri, commonly known as the Malabar ironwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Western Ghats of India.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its dense, hard wood and thick, leathery leaves. Due to its slow growth and ecological importance, it is considered a valuable but endangered species in its native habitat.
