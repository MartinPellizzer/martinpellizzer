Polygala suanica

Polygala suanica, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands. The plant features small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemes, and its leaves are opposite and pinnately compound. It belongs to the family Polygalaceae, which includes over 700 species of flowering plants.
