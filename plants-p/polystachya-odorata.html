Polystachya odorata

Polystachya odorata, commonly known as the sweet flag, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae).

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including India, China, and the Philippines. The plant features long, grass-like leaves and produces fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near water bodies.

Its aromatic properties have made it a traditional ingredient in herbal medicine and perfumery.
