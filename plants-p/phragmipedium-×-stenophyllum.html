Phragmipedium × stenophyllum

Phragmipedium × stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf phragmipedium, is a hybrid orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy coating. The plant produces pendulous flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange, often with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high air humidity and good drainage for optimal growth.

Phragmipedium × stenophyllum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
