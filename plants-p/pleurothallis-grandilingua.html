Pleurothallis grandilingua

Pleurothallis grandilingua, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a distinct lip petal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its beauty and rarity, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
