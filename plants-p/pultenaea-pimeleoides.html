Pultenaea pimeleoides

Pultenaea pimeleoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pultenaea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in racemes. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Pultenaea pimeleoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
