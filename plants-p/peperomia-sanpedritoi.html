Peperomia sanpedritoi

Peperomia sanpedritoi, commonly known as the Sanpedrito peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features oval, glossy leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia sanpedritoi is a low-maintenance plant suitable for indoor cultivation.
