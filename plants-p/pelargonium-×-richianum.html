Pelargonium × richianum

Pelargonium × richianum, commonly known as the richianum geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive reddish or purplish underside. It is a popular ornamental plant cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and containers.

Pelargonium × richianum thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.
