Pleiochiton parvifolius

Pleiochiton parvifolius, commonly known as the small-leaved pleiochiton, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, ovate leaves that are usually small and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
