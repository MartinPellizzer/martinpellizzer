Protea chionantha var. divaricata

Protea chionantha var. divaricata, commonly known as the silver protea, is a striking South African plant characterized by its distinctive, fluffy white inflorescence.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is native to the Western Cape region of South Africa. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, spherical flower heads that resemble clouds. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often used in garden landscapes.
