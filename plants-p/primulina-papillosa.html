Primulina papillosa

Primulina papillosa, commonly known as the velvet leaf.

It is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family, native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its thick, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale yellow or orange in color.

Primulina papillosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique floral display.
