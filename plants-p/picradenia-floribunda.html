Picradenia floribunda

Picradenia floribunda, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, white inflorescences that resemble snowballs, which appear in clusters during the flowering season. The plant has compound leaves with several elliptical leaflets, and its bark is often rough and fissured. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or along riverbanks.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional medicinal uses in some African communities.
