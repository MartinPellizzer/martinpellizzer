Patrinia glabrifolia

Patrinia glabrifolia, commonly known as broadleaf patrinia, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky slopes.

Patrinia glabrifolia is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
