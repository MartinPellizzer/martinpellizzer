Piletocarpus vitiensis

Piletocarpus vitiensis, commonly known as the Vitiensis ironwood, is a tropical tree native to the islands of Fiji and Tonga.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has compound leaves with long, narrow leaflets, and its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences. It produces small, woody fruits that contain a single seed.

This species plays an important ecological role in its native habitats, contributing to biodiversity and soil stability.
