Piper obversum

Piper obversum, commonly known as the reverse pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit, a small, three-lobed berry, contains several seeds and is used in traditional medicine.

Piper obversum is also cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique appearance.
