Pauletia dodecandra

Pauletia dodecandra, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white to pink petals. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and is adapted to dry conditions. It has a bushy growth habit and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Pauletia dodecandra is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
