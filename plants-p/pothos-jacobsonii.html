Pothos jacobsonii

Pothos jacobsonii, commonly known as the Snow Queen Pothos, is a trailing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that feature a white and green pattern, giving it a snow-like appearance. The plant thrives in low-light conditions and is highly tolerant of neglect, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its rapid growth rate allows it to quickly fill spaces, often cascading from hanging baskets or shelves.

The leaves are glossy and can grow up to several inches in length, adding a decorative touch to any room.
