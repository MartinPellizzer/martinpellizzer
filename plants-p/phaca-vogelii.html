Phaca vogelii

Phaca vogelii, commonly known as the yellow star grass, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flower heads that resemble small stars. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and has a fibrous root system that helps it anchor in sandy soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, herbaceous stems.

Phaca vogelii is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
