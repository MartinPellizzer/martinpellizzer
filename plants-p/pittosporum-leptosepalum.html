Pittosporum leptosepalum

Pittosporum leptosepalum, commonly known as the narrow-sepal pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features aromatic, glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
