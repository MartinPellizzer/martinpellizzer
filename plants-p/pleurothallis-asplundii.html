Pleurothallis asplundii

Pleurothallis asplundii, commonly known as the asplundii orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale green, with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, loose soil for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
