Paraphlomis jinggangshanensis

Paraphlomis jinggangshanensis, commonly known as the Jinggangshan parthenium, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its slender stems and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or forested areas, preferring well-drained soil. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while its flowers bloom in clusters.
