Phreatia kusaiensis

Phreatia kusaiensis, commonly known as the Kusai's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its inflorescence consists of a single spike bearing several showy, fragrant flowers.

The species is notable for its unique floral structure and is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
