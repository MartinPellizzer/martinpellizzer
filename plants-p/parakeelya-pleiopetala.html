Parakeelya pleiopetala

Parakeelya pleiopetala, commonly known as the velvetleaf orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, velvety green leaves and clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer. The plant belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is known for its ornamental value in horticulture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage and showy inflorescences make it a popular choice for collectors and gardeners.
