Panicum molinioides var. lasiocoleum

Panicum molinioides var. lasiocoleum, commonly known as silky panic grass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, hair-like leaves that give it a distinctive, feathery appearance. The plant produces small, pale green to brown spikelets that are clustered along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as fields and roadsides.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
