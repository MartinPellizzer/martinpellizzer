Panicum obtectum

Panicum obtectum, commonly known as little bluestem, is a native grass species found throughout much of North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and tufted inflorescence that turns rusty brown in the fall. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in prairies, meadows, and open woodlands. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.

Panicum obtectum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in native plant landscaping.
