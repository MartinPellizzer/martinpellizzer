Peperomia quaesita

Peperomia quaesita, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance houseplant native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It features small, waxy, silver-green leaves that give it a unique, almost metallic appearance. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia quaesita is popular for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care, making it a favored choice for indoor gardens.
