Persoonia muelleri var. densifolia

Persoonia muelleri var. densifolia, commonly known as the dense-leaved geebung, is a shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and small, urn-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in heathlands and coastal dunes, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for native wildlife.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
