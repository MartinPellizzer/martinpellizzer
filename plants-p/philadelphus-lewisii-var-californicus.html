Philadelphus lewisii var. californicus

Philadelphus lewisii var. californicus, commonly known as the California mock orange, is a deciduous shrub native to the western United States.

It features showy, fragrant white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and a woody, branched structure. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it adaptable to various garden conditions.

Its ornamental value and ecological benefits make it a popular choice for native plant landscaping.
