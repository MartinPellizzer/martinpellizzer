Polygonum amphibium f. hartwrightii

Polygonum amphibium f. hartwrightii, commonly known as the Hartwright's knotweed, is a variant of the common knotweed species.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in wet or marshy environments, often found near water bodies.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and taxonomic studies.
