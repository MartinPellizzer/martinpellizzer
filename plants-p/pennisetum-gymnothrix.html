Pennisetum gymnothrix

Pennisetum gymnothrix, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a warm-season perennial grass native to Africa.

It features slender, fountain-like clumps of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces showy, plume-like inflorescences that range from white to purple, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for landscapes in USDA hardiness zones 7 through 10.

Pennisetum gymnothrix is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract birds and beneficial insects.
