Polygala calcarea subsp. somedana

Polygala calcarea subsp.

somedana, commonly known as the limestone milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa. It typically grows in rocky or limestone soils, showcasing its adaptability to nutrient-poor environments.

The plant features slender, branching stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from pale yellow to pink. Its leaves are often narrow and covered in fine hairs, aiding in water conservation.

This subspecies is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soils and providing nectar for pollinators.
