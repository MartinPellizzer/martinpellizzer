Polygala nummularia

Polygala nummularia, commonly known as the silver dollar plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that features small, round, silver-green leaves arranged in a rosette. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique foliage and delicate blooms make it an attractive addition to drought-tolerant landscapes.
