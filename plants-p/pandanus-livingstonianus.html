Pandanus livingstonianus

Pandanus livingstonianus, commonly known as the giant pandanus, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and large, feather-like leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. These flowers are clustered in branched inflorescences and are often used in traditional crafts and as ornamental plants.

The species is adapted to coastal and wetland environments, thriving in humid, saline conditions.
