Pseudolycopodiella Goyazensis

Pseudolycopodiella goyazensis is a rare and ancient fern-like plant found in the dense forests of Brazil. It belongs to the family Lycopodiaceae and is closely related to modern mosses and clubmosses. This plant is notable for its unique morphology and its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species by conservationists. Studying Pseudolycopodiella goyazensis offers valuable insights into the evolution of early land plants.
