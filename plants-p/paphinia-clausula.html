Paphinia clausula

Paphinia clausula, commonly known as the narrow-leaved paphiopedilum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green, narrow leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with a unique lip that resembles a pouch, a feature common in slipper orchids. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Paphinia clausula is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
