Phagnalon phagnaloides

Phagnalon phagnaloides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved phagnalon, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in summer. This perennial herb is typically found in grassy areas and rocky soils across Europe and parts of Asia. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil.

It is also valued in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
