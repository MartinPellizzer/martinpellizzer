Premna alstonii var. subcrenata

Premna alstonii var. subcrenata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed premna, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically has opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves with serrated edges. It produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink, followed by small, fleshy fruits.

This species is often found in moist, shaded forest understories and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
