Peperomia japonica var. okinawensis

Peperomia japonica var. okinawensis, commonly known as the Okinawa peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Japan and the Ryukyu Islands.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of greenish-white, which are followed by rounded, red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate moisture.

This variety is prized for its ornamental foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
