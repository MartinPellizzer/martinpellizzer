Paeonia splendens

Paeonia splendens, commonly known as the Chinese peony, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red and white. The plant produces compound, trifoliate leaves that are dark green and serrated. It typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and blooms in late spring to early summer.

Paeonia splendens is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
