Pothos brevispathus

Pothos brevispathus, commonly known as the short-path pothos, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant produces small, white flowers that are typically followed by green berries. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a glossy texture, making it an attractive ornamental plant.

It thrives in humid environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its low maintenance requirements.
