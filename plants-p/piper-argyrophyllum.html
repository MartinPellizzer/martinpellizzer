Piper argyrophyllum

Piper argyrophyllum, commonly known as silverleaf pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, silver-gray leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

Piper argyrophyllum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in herbal medicine.
