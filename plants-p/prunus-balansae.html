Prunus balansae

Prunus balansae, commonly known as the Balansae cherry, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of China, particularly in the provinces of Sichuan and Yunnan. The plant is characterized by its small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are ovate with a smooth margin and a slightly serrated edge.

Prunus balansae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
