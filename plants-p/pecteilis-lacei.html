Pecteilis lacei

Pecteilis lacei, commonly known as the lacey spurge, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wetland or marshy environments. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are distinctive, with a crinkled, lace-like appearance that gives it its common name.

Pecteilis lacei is often found in moist soils and can thrive in partial to full sunlight.
