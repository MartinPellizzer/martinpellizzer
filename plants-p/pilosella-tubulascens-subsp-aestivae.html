Pilosella tubulascens subsp. aestivae

Pilosella tubulascens subsp.

aestivae, commonly known as the summer pilosella, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces small, yellowish flower heads that are typically arranged in clusters. It is native to parts of Europe and is often found in meadows and grassy areas. Pilosella tubulascens subsp.
