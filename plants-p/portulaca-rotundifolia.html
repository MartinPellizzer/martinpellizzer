Portulaca rotundifolia

Portulaca rotundifolia, commonly known as roundleaf portulaca, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and vibrant, bright flowers that typically range from yellow to red. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to drought conditions. Its short stem and clustered growth habit allow it to spread efficiently in sandy or rocky soils.

Portulaca rotundifolia is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
