Pleopeltis mildbraedii

Pleopeltis mildbraedii, commonly known as the mildbraed's rock fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, dense foliage and rhizome system that allows it to attach to trees and rocks. The fronds are typically leathery and have a dark green color, with a distinctive, narrow shape. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological niche, it is a sought-after plant among fern enthusiasts and conservationists.
