Primula calliantha subsp. calliantha

Primula calliantha subsp.

calliantha, commonly known as the Chinese primrose, is a flowering plant native to China. It is characterized by its dense, low-growing habit and produces clusters of bright pink or purple flowers.

The leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with a velvety texture. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

This subspecies is valued in horticulture for its resilience and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
