Polyosma glaucescens

Polyosma glaucescens, commonly known as the blue-leaved myrtle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its aromatic, glossy blue-green leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring. It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and fragrant properties.

Polyosma glaucescens prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
