Plantago afra subsp. zwierleinii

Plantago afra subsp.

zwierleinii, commonly known as the narrow-leaved plantain, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It features a basal rosette of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, dense hairs.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, which are borne on a slender flowering stem. It is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

This subspecies is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
