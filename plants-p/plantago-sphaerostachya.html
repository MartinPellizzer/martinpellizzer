Plantago sphaerostachya

Plantago sphaerostachya, commonly known as the globular plantain, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces a dense, spherical inflorescence composed of small, greenish flowers that are surrounded by white bracts. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

The species is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
