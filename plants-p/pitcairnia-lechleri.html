Pitcairnia lechleri

Pitcairnia lechleri, commonly known as the sangre de drago, is a flowering plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that store water. The plant produces striking red flowers that bloom in the early morning. Its sap contains compounds used in traditional medicine for treating various ailments.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
