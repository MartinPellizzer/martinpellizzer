Phalaenopsis sumatrana var. gersenii

Phalaenopsis sumatrana var. gersenii, commonly known as the Gersen's phalaenopsis, is a rare and elegant orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender pseudobulbs and clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom repeatedly throughout the year. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and thrives in humid environments with well-draining, acidic soil. It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its rarity and unique beauty, it is often sought after by orchid enthusiasts and collectors.
