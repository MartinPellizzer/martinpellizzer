Portulaca linearis

Portulaca linearis, commonly known as the slender portulaca, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing annual herb that produces small, bright yellow flowers. The plant has fleshy, linear leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a hardy species suitable for xeriscaping.

Its ability to survive in dry conditions makes it a popular choice for drought-tolerant gardens.
