Polystachya fusiformis var. purpurascens

Polystachya fusiformis var. purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-stemmed flame orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its tall, erect stems that are distinctly purple in color, contrasting with its green foliage. Its flowers are clustered in spikes and typically display vibrant orange or red hues, adding to its ornamental value.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
