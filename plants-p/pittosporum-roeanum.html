Pittosporum roeanum

Pittosporum roeanum, commonly known as the silver pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in temperate climates and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.

Pittosporum roeanum is also valued for its ability to tolerate a range of soil conditions.
