Peperomia jarisiana

Peperomia jarisiana, commonly known as the Jarisiana Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
