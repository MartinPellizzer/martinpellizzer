Pennisetum pedicellatum var. pallidum

Pennisetum pedicellatum var. pallidum, commonly known as the silver grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-gray blades that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats and is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, fluffy seed heads that are adapted for wind dispersal.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping and as a decorative plant in gardens.
