Polypodium Evenium

Even's polypody, scientifically known as Polypodium evenium, is a unique fern species that thrives in shaded, moist environments. It is native to parts of Asia, particularly in regions like China and Japan, where it grows on rocky outcrops and forest floors. This plant is notable for its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that display a striking contrast of green and gold hues. Despite its modest appearance, Even's polypody holds cultural and medicinal significance in traditional practices. Its resilience and adaptability make it an interesting subject for both botanists and nature enthusiasts.
