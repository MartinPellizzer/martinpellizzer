Paeonia intermedia

Paeonia intermedia, commonly known as the Chinese peony, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can range in color from white to deep red. The plant has compound, pinnately arranged leaves that are typically green and slightly hairy. Its roots are thick and fleshy, often used in traditional medicine.

Paeonia intermedia thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun.
