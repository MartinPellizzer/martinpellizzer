Platysepalum scaberulum

Platysepalum scaberulum, commonly known as the rough-sepal orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features a slender rhizome and produces small, clustered flowers with distinctive, rough-textured sepals. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in a fine, hairy layer.

This species is valued for its unique floral structure and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
