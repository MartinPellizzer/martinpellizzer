Panicum bobartii

Panicum bobartii, commonly known as Bobart's bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in wet to mesic habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds. The plant has slender, blue-green blades and produces fluffy, pinkish-white inflorescences that are distinctive in appearance. Its leaves are narrow and have a slightly hairy texture, contributing to its unique botanical characteristics.

It is often found in wetland ecosystems where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
