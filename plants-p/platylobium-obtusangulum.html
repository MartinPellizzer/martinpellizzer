Platylobium obtusangulum

Platylobium obtusangulum, commonly known as the broad-angled flat-lipped orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its compact, herbaceous growth habit. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its leaves are fleshy and arranged in a rosette, which helps it conserve water in its arid habitat.

Platylobium obtusangulum is often found in sandy or rocky soils, thriving in well-drained environments.
