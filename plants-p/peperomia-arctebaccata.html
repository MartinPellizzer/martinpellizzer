Peperomia arctebaccata

Peperomia arctebaccata, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are usually a deep green with a silvery or white underside, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive foliage.

Peperomia arctebaccata thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor environments.
