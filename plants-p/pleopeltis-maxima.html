Pleopeltis maxima

Pleopeltis maxima, commonly known as the giant tree fern, is a large, upright fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It has a thick, trunk-like stem covered in a fibrous, brownish layer called the rhyzome. Its fronds are pinnate, with long, narrow leaflets that are often glossy and dark green. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and can grow up to several meters in height.

It is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often used in tropical landscaping.
