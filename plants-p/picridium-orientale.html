Picridium orientale

Picridium orientale, commonly known as the oriental picrid, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Picridaceae and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with a distinctive red or orange center. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sunlight. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while the stems are upright and branching.

Picridium orientale is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
