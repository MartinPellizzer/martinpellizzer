Peperomia gentryi

Peperomia gentryi, commonly known as the Gentry's Peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval or heart-shaped, with a glossy surface that helps it retain moisture in its humid native environment. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish or reddish in color, often hidden among the foliage. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its preference for indirect light and moderate watering.

Peperomia gentryi is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care, making it a popular choice for collectors and houseplant enthusiasts.
