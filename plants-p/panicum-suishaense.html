Panicum suishaense

Panicum suishaense, commonly known as the Suisha grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, particularly China and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Panicum suishaense is often found in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and forest edges.
