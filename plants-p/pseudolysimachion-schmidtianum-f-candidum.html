Pseudolysimachion schmidtianum f. candidum

Pseudolysimachion schmidtianum f. candidum, commonly known as the white-flowered form of the Schmidt's lysimachion, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Plantaginaceae.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring.
