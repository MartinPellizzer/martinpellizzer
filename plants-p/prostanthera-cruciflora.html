Prostanthera cruciflora

Prostanthera cruciflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mintbush, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its narrow, aromatic leaves and clusters of small, white or pale blue flowers. It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and tableland regions.
