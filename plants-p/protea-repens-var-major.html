Protea repens var. major

Protea repens var. major, commonly known as the silver protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences composed of numerous white to pale pink flowers arranged in a spherical or conical shape. The plant is native to the coastal regions of South Africa, where it thrives in sandy, well-drained soils. It is adapted to Mediterranean climates with mild, wet winters and hot, dry summers.

The silver protea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is an important species in local biodiversity.
