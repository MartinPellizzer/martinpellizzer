Poa kunthii

Poa kunthii, commonly known as Kunth's bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and fine, bluish-green leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Poa kunthii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in high-altitude ecosystems.
