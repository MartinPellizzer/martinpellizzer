Pennisetum spicatum var. speciosum

Pennisetum spicatum var. speciosum, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a tall, ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features slender, arching stems that bear dense, spike-like inflorescences in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for landscaping. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, adding a soft texture to garden designs.

It is also known for its ability to attract birds and butterflies, enhancing biodiversity in natural and cultivated environments.
