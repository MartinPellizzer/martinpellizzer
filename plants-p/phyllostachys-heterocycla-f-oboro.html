Phyllostachys heterocycla f. oboro

Phyllostachys heterocycla f. oboro, commonly known as the silver bamboo, is a variant of the common bamboo species characterized by its striking silvery-white to light green culms.

This ornamental bamboo is native to China and is prized for its elegant, slender appearance and slow growth rate. The leaves are typically narrow and a lighter green than the culms, adding to its visual appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Its unique coloration makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design.
