Parodia buenekeri

Parodia buenekeri, commonly known as the "Buenekeri Cactus," is a compact, globular cactus native to southern Africa.

It features a ribbed stem with prominent areoles, from which it produces pale yellow flowers during the summer months. The plant has a dense covering of white to grayish spines, which help protect it from herbivores and intense sunlight. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and slow growth make it a popular choice for rock gardens and cactus collections.
