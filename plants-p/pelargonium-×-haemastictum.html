Pelargonium × haemastictum

Pelargonium × haemastictum, commonly known as the regal geranium, is a hybrid species within the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, double flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically has rounded, lobed leaves with a hairy texture, and it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively easy to grow in temperate climates.

Pelargonium × haemastictum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
