Pleroma petrophylax

Pleroma petrophylax, commonly known as the rock pleuroma, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form and features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to withstand dry, sunny conditions. Its foliage consists of narrow, leathery leaves that help reduce water loss.

Pleroma petrophylax is often found growing on rocky outcrops and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
