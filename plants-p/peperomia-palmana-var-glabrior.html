Peperomia palmana var. glabrior

Peperomia palmana var. glabrior, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish or purple tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Its slow growth rate makes it ideal for indoor cultivation and container gardening.
