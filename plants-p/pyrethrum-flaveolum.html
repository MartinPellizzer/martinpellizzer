Pyrethrum flaveolum

Pyrethrum flaveolum, commonly known as the golden pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its fine, feathery leaves. Its flowers contain pyrethrin, a natural insecticide used in organic pest control.

Pyrethrum flaveolum is often cultivated for both ornamental purposes and for its medicinal and agricultural applications.
