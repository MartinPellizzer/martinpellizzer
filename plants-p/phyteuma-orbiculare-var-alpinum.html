Phyteuma orbiculare var. alpinum

Phyteuma orbiculare var. alpinum, commonly known as the alpine phyteuma, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, often with a distinctive purple or brownish throat. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes, where it thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and somewhat hairy, adapted to withstand cold and harsh weather conditions.

This variety is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom in challenging environments.
