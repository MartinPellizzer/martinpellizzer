Pterisanthes glabra

Pterisanthes glabra, commonly known as the smooth pterisanthes, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features lanceolate, smooth, and often hairy leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, forming a single head with tubular florets.

Pterisanthes glabra is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
