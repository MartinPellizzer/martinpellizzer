Pedilochilus parvulus

Pedilochilus parvulus, commonly known as the small-lobed helmet orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a slender stem with a few leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. Its flowers are characterized by a distinctive helmet-shaped lip, which is a key identifying feature.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
