Prestonia peregrina

Prestonia peregrina, commonly known as the wandering prestonia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes milkweeds and related species. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth texture.

Prestonia peregrina is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in humid, shaded environments.
