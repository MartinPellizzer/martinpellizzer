Plantago argentina var. glabra

Plantago argentina var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth plantain, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and often covered in a fine, waxy cuticle. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense spikes, which are typically borne on slender stalks. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks.

This variety is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
