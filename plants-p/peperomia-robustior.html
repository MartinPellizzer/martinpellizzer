Peperomia robustior

Peperomia robustior, commonly known as the Shiny Caper or Velvetleaf Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, glossy, dark green leaves that are often adorned with a waxy coating, giving them a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers, typically emerging from the leaf axils. Peperomia robustior thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it suitable for both container growing and as part of a succulent collection.
