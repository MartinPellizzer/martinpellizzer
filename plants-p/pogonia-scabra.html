Pogonia scabra

Pogonia scabra, commonly known as the rough pogonia, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern North America.

It typically grows in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial shade. The plant features a slender rhizome and produces a single, erect stem with several oval, lance-shaped leaves. Its most distinctive characteristic is its showy, pinkish-brown flowers with intricate patterns and a unique, fragrant scent.

Pogonia scabra is known for its delicate beauty and is often considered a rare and endangered species in many regions.
