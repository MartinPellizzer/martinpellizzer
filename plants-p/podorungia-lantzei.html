Podorungia lantzei

Podorungia lantzei, commonly known as the Lantze's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to thrive in moist, well-drained soils.

Podorungia lantzei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and private collections.
