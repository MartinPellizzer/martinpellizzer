Platysepalum violaceum var. ebracteolatum

Platysepalum violaceum var. ebracteolatum, commonly known as the purple-flowered flat-sepal plant, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, violet-colored flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate to lanceolate in shape. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a popular subject in botanical gardens and horticultural studies.
