Panicum pectinatum

Panicum pectinatum, commonly known as the silky grass or silky panicum, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, grass-like leaves with a soft, silky texture due to the fine hairs covering its surface. The plant produces small, clustered spikelets that are often pale green to brown in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a common sight in open habitats.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
