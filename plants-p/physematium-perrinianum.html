Physematium perrinianum

Physematium perrinianum, commonly known as the broad-leafed fern, is a member of the Dryopteridaceae family.

It is a terrestrial fern characterized by its rhizomatous growth and fronds that are typically bipinnate. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including India and Southeast Asia. Its leaves are often broad and have a distinctive, feathery appearance.

Physematium perrinianum thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
