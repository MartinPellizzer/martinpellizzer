Pleurothallis tigridens

Pleurothallis tigridens, commonly known as the tiger orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with intricate patterns resembling a tiger's stripes. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings.
