Peperomia rufispica

Peperomia rufispica, commonly known as the red-haired peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish or purplish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that are usually hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia rufispica is popular as a decorative indoor plant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
