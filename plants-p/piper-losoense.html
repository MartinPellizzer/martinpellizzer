Piper losoense

Piper losoense, commonly known as the Losoense pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its vine-like growth habit and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its fruits are berries that turn from green to yellow when ripe, and they are used in traditional medicine.

The plant's botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and pharmacological research.
