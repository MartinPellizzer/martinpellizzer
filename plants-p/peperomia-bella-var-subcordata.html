Peperomia bella var. subcordata

Peperomia bella var. subcordata, commonly known as the Heartleaf Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple on the underside, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it an ideal plant for container gardening or as a tabletop specimen.
