Pleurothallis modestiflora

Pleurothallis modestiflora, commonly known as the modest flower orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often with a unique lip structure that aids in pollination. Its leaves are usually narrow and arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it conserve moisture.

Due to its striking appearance and specific habitat requirements, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
