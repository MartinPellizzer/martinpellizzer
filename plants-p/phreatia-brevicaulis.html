Phreatia brevicaulis

Phreatia brevicaulis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and possesses pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant features strap-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Its flowers are typically white or pale green, with a distinctive, elongated lip.

Phreatia brevicaulis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
