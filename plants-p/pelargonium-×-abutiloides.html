Pelargonium × abutiloides

Pelargonium × abutiloides, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or the African violet.

This plant is a hybrid species within the Geraniaceae family, characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers. Its leaves are typically velvety and variegated, often displaying shades of green, silver, and red. The flowers are usually borne in clusters and come in a range of colors, including pink, red, and white.

It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its preference for bright, indirect light and moderate watering.
