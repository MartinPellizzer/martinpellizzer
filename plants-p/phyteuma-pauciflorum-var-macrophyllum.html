Phyteuma pauciflorum var. macrophyllum

Phyteuma pauciflorum var. macrophyllum, commonly known as the large-leaved phyteuma, is a herbaceous perennial native to Central Asia.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to purple, typically blooming in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
