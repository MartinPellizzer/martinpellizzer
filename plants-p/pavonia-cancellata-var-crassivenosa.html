Pavonia cancellata var. crassivenosa

Pavonia cancellata var. crassivenosa, commonly known as the broadleaf pavonia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to purple bracts that surround its small, inconspicuous flowers. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in warm climates.
