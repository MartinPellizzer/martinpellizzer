Pittosporum tobira var. lanatum

Pittosporum tobira var. lanatum, commonly known as the Silver Pittosporum, is a deciduous shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, velvety foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers in spring, which are followed by small, dark blue berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its unique leaf texture and seasonal blooms contribute to its aesthetic value in gardens.
