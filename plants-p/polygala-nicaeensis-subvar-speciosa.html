Polygala nicaeensis subvar. speciosa

Polygala nicaeensis subvar. speciosa, commonly known as the showy milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces distinctive, spirally arranged leaves and has a fibrous root system. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
