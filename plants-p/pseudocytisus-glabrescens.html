Pseudocytisus glabrescens

Pseudocytisus glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth broom, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, often thornless stems and pairs of opposite leaves that are typically narrow and lance-shaped. Pseudocytisus glabrescens is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden conditions.
