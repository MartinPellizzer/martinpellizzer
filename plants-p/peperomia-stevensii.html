Peperomia stevensii

Peperomia stevensii, commonly known as the Stevens' peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal water needs.

Peperomia stevensii is valued for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
