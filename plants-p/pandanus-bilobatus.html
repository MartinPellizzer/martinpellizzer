Pandanus bilobatus

Pandanus bilobatus, commonly known as the two-lobed pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is a member of the Pandanaceae family and is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its fruit is a spiny, fibrous structure that contains seeds.

Pandanus bilobatus thrives in coastal and wetland environments, often growing near water sources.
