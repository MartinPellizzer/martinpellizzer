Peperomia lagunaensis

Peperomia lagunaensis, commonly known as the Laguna peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and silver. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or brown, clustered in inflorescences.

Peperomia lagunaensis is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive foliage.
