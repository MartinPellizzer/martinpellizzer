Panicum floribundum

Panicum floribundum, commonly known as the flowering panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and produces showy, panicle-like inflorescences that are often pink or purple in color. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping. Its flowering period typically occurs during the warm months, adding visual interest to gardens.

Panicum floribundum is also valued for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
