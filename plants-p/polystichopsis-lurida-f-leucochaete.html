Polystichopsis lurida f. leucochaete

Polystichopsis lurida f. leucochaete, commonly known as the white-hairy fern, is a fern species characterized by its distinctive silvery-white hairy foliage.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is native to certain regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features fronds that are typically lanceolate in shape with a leathery texture. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The varietal form 'leucochaete' refers to its characteristic white or pale hairy covering on the leaves.
