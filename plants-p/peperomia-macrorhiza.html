Peperomia macrorhiza

Peperomia macrorhiza, commonly known as the large-rooted peperomia, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy rhizomes and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique root structure and compact growth habit contribute to its ornamental appeal.
