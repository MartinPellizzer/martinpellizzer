Phalangium capillare

Phalangium capillare, commonly known as the slender finger fern, is a delicate and elegant fern species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features a rhizomatous growth habit with slender, arching fronds that are finely divided into soft, hair-like pinnae. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as along stream banks or in dense undergrowth. Its leaves are evergreen, providing a lush green appearance throughout the year.

Phalangium capillare is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid, low-light conditions.
