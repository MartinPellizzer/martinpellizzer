Philodendron meraense

Philodendron meraense, commonly known as the Meraense philodendron, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology and inflorescence structure. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a spadix, typical of the Araceae family.

Philodendron meraense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in indoor environments.
