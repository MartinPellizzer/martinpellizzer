Plerandra veilloniorum

Plerandra veilloniorum, commonly known as the veillonii arum, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves and colorful inflorescence. The plant features a spadix surrounded by a spathe, which is typical of arum species. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Plerandra veilloniorum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
