Phanera fabrilis

Phanera fabrilis, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests. Its leaves are thick and waxy, which helps it retain moisture in humid conditions.

Phanera fabrilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is popular in horticultural settings.
