Peperomia arifolia var. epeltata

Peperomia arifolia var. epeltata, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features broad, velvety leaves that are typically dark green with a rich, almost metallic sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color, emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage makes it a standout addition to any plant collection.
