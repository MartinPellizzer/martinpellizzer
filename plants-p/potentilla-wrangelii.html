Potentilla wrangelii

Potentilla wrangelii, commonly known as Wrangel's cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches heights of up to 20 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Potentilla wrangelii is adapted to cold climates and is often found in tundra and rocky habitats.
