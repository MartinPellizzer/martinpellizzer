Paris marmorata

Paris marmorata, commonly known as the marble Paris, is a striking member of the Paris family, known for its ornamental value.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 10-20 centimeters. The plant features delicate, fern-like leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. Its flowers are small and white, arranged in a dense, spherical cluster at the top of the stems.

Paris marmorata thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.
