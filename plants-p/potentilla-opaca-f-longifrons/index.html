Potentilla Opaca F. Longifrons

Potentilla opaca f. longifrons is a rare and intriguing plant species known for its distinctive floral features and ecological significance. This variety of the common potentilla exhibits elongated flower stems that set it apart from its relatives. It thrives in specific alpine and subalpine habitats, often found in rocky soils and high elevation areas. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts. Its unique morphology and ecological role make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
