Prunus × donarium f. gioiko

Prunus × donarium f. gioiko, commonly known as the Gioiko cherry, is a hybrid ornamental shrub or small tree native to Japan.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, dark red fruits that are edible but not typically consumed by humans. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate foliage and striking floral display.
