Pilea dauciodora var. pilosula

Pilea dauciodora var. pilosula, commonly known as the velvety pilea, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture, giving it a soft appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a subtle ornamental touch. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its health.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
