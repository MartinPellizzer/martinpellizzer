Prunus webbii

Prunus webbii, commonly known as Webb's cherry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its simple, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. The plant produces small, white flowers in early spring, followed by red or black berries. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found at high elevations.

Prunus webbii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
