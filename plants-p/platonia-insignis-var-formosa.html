Platonia insignis var. formosa

Platonia insignis var. formosa, commonly known as the red-veined or variegated platonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with distinctive red veins and a variegated pattern of green and white. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers partial to full shade. Its foliage is often used in landscape design for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in shaded garden settings.

It is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for tropical and subtropical gardens.
