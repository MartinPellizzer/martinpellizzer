Phyllostachys pubescens f. purpurescens

Phyllostachys pubescens f. purpurescens, commonly known as the purple bamboo, is a variant of the silver bamboo species native to China.

It is characterized by its striking purple foliage, which transitions to a deep green as the plant matures. This ornamental bamboo is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and landscapes. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, requiring moderate water and care.

Its unique coloration makes it a popular choice for adding visual interest to bamboo collections.
