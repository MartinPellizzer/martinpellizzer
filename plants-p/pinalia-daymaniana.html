Pinalia daymaniana

Pinalia daymaniana, commonly known as the Turkish sand thistle, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and robust, hairy stems. The plant typically grows in arid and rocky environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Pinalia daymaniana produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer, attracting various pollinators.
