Phragmipedium lindleyanum var. lindleyanum

Phragmipedium lindleyanum var. lindleyanum, commonly known as the Lindley's phragmipedium, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature a vibrant yellow lip and a contrasting green or brown column. The plant grows as an epiphyte, often found on trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, with a waxy surface that helps it retain moisture.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
