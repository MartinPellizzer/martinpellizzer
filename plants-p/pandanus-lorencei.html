Pandanus lorencei

Pandanus lorencei, commonly known as the Hawaiian mountain pandanus, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a member of the Pandanaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stem and large, compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences at the tips of the leaves. Its leaves are often used in traditional Hawaiian crafts and have a distinctive, aromatic scent.

Pandanus lorencei thrives in coastal and upland habitats, often growing in sandy soils.
