Polypodium gracile

Polypodium gracile, commonly known as the slender holly fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are often covered in a layer of fine, hairy structures.

Polypodium gracile is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
