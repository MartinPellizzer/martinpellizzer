Plectranthus ovatus

Plectranthus ovatus, commonly known as the woolly mint, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fuzzy, ovate leaves that give it a distinctive, velvety appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale purple to blue in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Plectranthus ovatus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic foliage.
