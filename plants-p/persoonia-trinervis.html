Persoonia trinervis

Persoonia trinervis, commonly known as the three-veined persoonia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves with three distinct veins. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. These flowers are typically pollinated by insects and birds.

Persoonia trinervis is adapted to dry environments and is often found in heathlands and sandplain areas.
