Piper chanchamayanum

Piper chanchamayanum, commonly known as the Chanchamayanum pepper, is a climbing vine native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various pepper species. The plant produces small, green berries that mature to a bright red color, containing aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are often used in local remedies due to their medicinal properties.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
