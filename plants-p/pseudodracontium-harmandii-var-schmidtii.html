Pseudodracontium harmandii var. schmidtii

Pseudodracontium harmandii var. schmidtii, commonly known as the Schmidt's arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves and striking, spatulate inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
