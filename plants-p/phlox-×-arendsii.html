Phlox × arendsii

Phlox × arendsii, commonly known as the Arendsii phlox, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the United States.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink, purple, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for garden landscapes due to its ornamental value and hardiness.
