Pothos grandifolius

Pothos grandifolius, commonly known as the giant pothos, is a trailing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 15 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil. It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its rapid growth rate and ability to climb or trail make it versatile for various indoor spaces.
