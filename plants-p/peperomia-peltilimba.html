Peperomia peltilimba

Peperomia peltilimba, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, waxy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish or purple tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, usually white or pale green, which are followed by small, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia peltilimba is known for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
