Pelargonium reliquifolium

Pelargonium reliquifolium, commonly known as the rare leaf pelargonium, is a unique species within the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. Native to South Africa, this plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are typically small and pink or red, appearing in clusters during the summer months.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by collectors and horticulturists.
