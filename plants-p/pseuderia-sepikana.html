Pseuderia sepikana

Pseuderia sepikana, commonly known as the Sepik orbweaver, is a species of spider found in the tropical regions of Papua New Guinea.

It belongs to the family Nephilidae, which includes some of the largest and strongest spiders in the world. The plant-like appearance of its web, which resembles a flower, is a key feature used for camouflage. Its silk is exceptionally strong and has unique structural properties.
