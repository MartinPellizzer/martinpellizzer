Paphiopedilum tonsum

Paphiopedilum tonsum, commonly known as the Tonsum lady's slipper, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped flower that is typically pink or white with a yellowish throat. The plant has fleshy, dark green leaves and grows in shaded, humid environments. It produces a single, showy flower per stem, which blooms during the wet season.

Paphiopedilum tonsum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
