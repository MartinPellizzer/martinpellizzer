Pleurothallis molleturoi

Pleurothallis molleturoi, commonly known as the Molleturoi orchid, is a species native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that often display a range of colors from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees and deriving moisture and nutrients from the air and rain. Its foliage consists of narrow, strap-like leaves that help it thrive in the humid, shaded environments of its natural habitat.

Pleurothallis molleturoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
