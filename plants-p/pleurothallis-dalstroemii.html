Pleurothallis dalstroemii

Pleurothallis dalstroemii, commonly known as the Dalstrom's orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

Due to its beauty and rarity, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
