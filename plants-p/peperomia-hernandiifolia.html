Peperomia hernandiifolia

Peperomia hernandiifolia, commonly known as the Hawaiian peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of the Pacific.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are oval to lance-shaped, often with a slightly wavy margin. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia hernandiifolia is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
