Pleurothallis schweinfurthii

Pleurothallis schweinfurthii, commonly known as the Schweinfurth's orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically greenish-white with yellow markings, often blooming in late spring or early summer. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, loose soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a challenging but rewarding plant for experienced orchid growers.
