Psoralea argophylla var. robustior

Psoralea argophylla var. robustior, commonly known as the robust psoralea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically features compound leaves with numerous leaflets and produces yellow or pale green pea-like flowers. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

This variant is noted for its robust growth compared to other forms of Psoralea argophylla.
