Pterocymbium tubulatum

Pterocymbium tubulatum, commonly known as the tubular orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, tubular-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests, thriving in humid and shaded environments. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with a distinctive structure that aids in pollination by specific insect species.

Pterocymbium tubulatum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
