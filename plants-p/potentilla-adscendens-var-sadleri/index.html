Potentilla Adscendens Var. Sadleri

Potentilla adscendens var. sadleri is a striking evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern China. Known for its vibrant yellow flowers and dense, leathery foliage, it adds both color and texture to any garden. This variety is prized for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for rock gardens or as a border plant. Whether in full sun or partial shade, it brings a touch of elegance to the landscape.
