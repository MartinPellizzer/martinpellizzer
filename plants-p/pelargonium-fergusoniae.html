Pelargonium fergusoniae

Pelargonium fergusoniae, commonly known as the Cape Flats geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant flowers. The plant typically produces showy, five-petaled blooms in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a sought-after species among horticultural enthusiasts.
