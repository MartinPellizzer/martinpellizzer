Pleurothallis titan

Pleurothallis titan, commonly known as the giant pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually broad and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral displays.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
