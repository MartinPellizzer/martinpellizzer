Potentilla canescens stenomalla

Potentilla canescens stenomalla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cinquefoil, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has narrow, serrated leaves and is typically found in dry, rocky habitats. It is native to regions of Europe and Asia, where it thrives in well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
