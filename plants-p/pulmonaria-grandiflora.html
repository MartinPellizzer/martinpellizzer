Pulmonaria grandiflora

Pulmonaria grandiflora, commonly known as lungwort, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It features distinctive spotted leaves with a mottled appearance, often with silver and green hues. The plant produces showy clusters of pink or purple flowers in early spring. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Pulmonaria grandiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
