Polybotrya kunzei

Polybotrya kunzei, commonly known as the mountain hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cool, temperate climates. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often covered in a fine pubescence.

Polybotrya kunzei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
