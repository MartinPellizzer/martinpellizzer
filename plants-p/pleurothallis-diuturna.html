Pleurothallis diuturna

Pleurothallis diuturna, commonly known as the long-lasting lady's tresses, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Colombia and Ecuador. This epiphytic orchid typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are small and fragrant, often arranged in a dense, mat-like inflorescence.

The plant is appreciated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
