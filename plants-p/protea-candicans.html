Protea candicans

Protea candicans, commonly known as the silver protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence composed of numerous white to pale pink flowers arranged in a spherical shape. The plant is native to the Western and Eastern Cape provinces of South Africa, where it thrives in sandy, well-drained soils. It is often found in fynbos vegetation, which is a unique biome known for its high biodiversity.

The protea is not only ornamental but also plays a role in supporting local pollinators and ecosystems.
