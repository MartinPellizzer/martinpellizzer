Paeonia tenuifolia var. hybrida

Paeonia tenuifolia var. hybrida, commonly known as the slender leaf peony, is a hybrid cultivar of the Chinese peony.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant is native to China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, preferring cooler climates.

This variety is prized for its delicate foliage and vibrant blooms, making it a popular choice among horticultural enthusiasts.
