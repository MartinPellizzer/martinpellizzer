Peperomia curtipes var. curtipes

Peperomia curtipes var. curtipes, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy, silver-green leaves with a waxy texture that help it retain moisture in dry conditions. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for succulent and air plant collections.
