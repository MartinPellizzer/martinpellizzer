Pelargonium × enigmaticum

Pelargonium × enigmaticum, commonly known as the enigmatic geranium, is a hybrid species within the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to purple. The plant produces five-petaled flowers with prominent stamens, and its leaves are usually rounded with a slightly serrated edge. Native to South Africa, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated for decorative purposes in gardens and landscapes.
