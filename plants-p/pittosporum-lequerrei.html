Pittosporum lequerrei

Pittosporum lequerrei, commonly known as the silver pittosporum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a valued addition to gardens and landscapes.
