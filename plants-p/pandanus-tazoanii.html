Pandanus tazoanii

Pandanus tazoanii, commonly known as the Tazoanii pandanus, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Pandanaceae family and is characterized by its robust, sword-like leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with fragrant flowers, which are typically yellow or green in color. Its roots are fibrous and can be used for making ropes and mats in local communities.

The plant thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to salt spray and sandy soils.
