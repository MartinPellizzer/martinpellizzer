Piper boerlagei

Piper boerlagei, commonly known as the Boerlage's pepper, is a tropical climbing vine native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Piper genus, which includes many species of flowering plants in the family Piperaceae. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to yellow as it matures.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
