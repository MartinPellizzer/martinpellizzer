Portulaca hilaireana

Portulaca hilaireana, commonly known as Hilaire's moss rose, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing, succulent plant with fleshy, thick stems and leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, typically blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its drought tolerance, it is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
