Pilosella suecica

Pilosella suecica, commonly known as Swedish goatweed, is a flowering plant native to northern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and sandy soils. It has a low-growing habit with basal leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Pilosella suecica is an annual or biennial herb that produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.
