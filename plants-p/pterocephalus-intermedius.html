Pterocephalus intermedius

Pterocephalus intermedius, commonly known as the intermediate winged bean, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its distinctive winged pods. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid regions and is adapted to withstand drought conditions. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a yellowish or greenish hue.
