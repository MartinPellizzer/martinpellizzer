Premna suavis

Premna suavis, commonly known as sweetleaf or sweet thorn, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3-5 meters in height. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually aromatic and have a mild, sweet scent. Its small, greenish-white flowers are borne in clusters and are followed by small, round berries.

Premna suavis is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
