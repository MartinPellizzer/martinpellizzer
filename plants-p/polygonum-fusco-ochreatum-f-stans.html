Polygonum fusco-ochreatum f. stans

Polygonum fusco-ochreatum f. stans, commonly known as the dark ochre polygonum, is a variant of the species Polygonum fusco-ochreatum, characterized by its distinctive dark ochre-colored stems and leaves.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants known for their rhomboidal leaves and often edible seeds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors or stream banks. Its foliage exhibits a unique combination of coloration and texture, making it a notable species in botanical studies.

The f. stans form is distinguished by its upright growth habit and compact inflorescences.
