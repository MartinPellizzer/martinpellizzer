Peristrophe tianmuensis

Peristrophe tianmuensis, commonly known as the Tianmu bamboo leaf grass, is a species of flowering plant in the family Poaceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the mountainous regions of Zhejiang Province. The plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers that appear in spikelets. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
