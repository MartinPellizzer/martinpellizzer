Peperomia calimana

Peperomia calimana, commonly known as the Calimana Peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish or purple hue on the undersides. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia calimana is an ideal choice for indoor gardening due to its ease of care and ornamental foliage.
