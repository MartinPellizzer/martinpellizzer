Pilea microphylla var. microphylla

Pilea microphylla var. microphylla, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rounded, fleshy leaves that are dark green with a silvery underside, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that bloom in spring. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and drought tolerance.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
