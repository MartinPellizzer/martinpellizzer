Pittosporum ovatifolium

Pittosporum ovatifolium, commonly known as the oval-leaved pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage, with lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Pittosporum ovatifolium is also valued for its ability to attract native pollinators and its use in ecological restoration projects.
