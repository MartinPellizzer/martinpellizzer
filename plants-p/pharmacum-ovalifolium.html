Pharmacum ovalifolium

Pharmacum ovalifolium, commonly known as the oval-leaf medicinal plant, is a herbaceous species native to temperate regions.

It features lanceolate leaves that are typically arranged alternately along its stem. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its roots are the primary source of its medicinal compounds, which have been traditionally used in herbal medicine.

The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas.
