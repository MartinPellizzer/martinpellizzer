Pronacron ramosissimum

Pronacron ramosissimum, commonly known as the Brazilian rosewood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its dense, dark reddish-brown wood, which is highly valued for its rich tone and resonance. The plant has compound leaves with long, narrow leaflets and produces small, fragrant white flowers. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and timber, it is also cultivated in botanical gardens and as a decorative plant.
