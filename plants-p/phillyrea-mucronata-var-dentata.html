Phillyrea mucronata var. dentata

Phillyrea mucronata var. dentata, commonly known as the serrated Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, elliptical leaves with serrated edges, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces white, clustered flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by small, fleshy berries. It is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Phillyrea mucronata var. dentata is valued for its ornamental qualities and resilience in harsh environments.
