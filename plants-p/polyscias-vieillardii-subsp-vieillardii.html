Polyscias vieillardii subsp. vieillardii

Polyscias vieillardii subsp.

vieillardii, commonly known as the broadleaf sycamore, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves that are typically dark green and glossy on the upper surface.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in panicles, which are followed by round, orange-red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in tropical gardens.
