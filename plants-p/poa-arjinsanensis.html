Poa arjinsanensis

Poa arjinsanensis, commonly known as the Arjinsan grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of the Tibetan Plateau and adjacent areas in China. This perennial grass is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, erect stems. Its leaves are typically narrow and hairless, with a bluish-green coloration.

Poa arjinsanensis plays a minor ecological role in its native habitat, contributing to soil stabilization and providing forage for local wildlife.
