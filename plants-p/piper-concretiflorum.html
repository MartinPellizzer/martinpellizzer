Piper concretiflorum

Piper concretiflorum, commonly known as the concrete-flowered pepper, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered white or greenish flowers that appear on short pedicels. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slight pubescence.

The fruit is a small, three-lobed berry that matures to a dark purple color.
