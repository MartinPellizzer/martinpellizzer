Polypodium bolivari

Polypodium bolivari, commonly known as the Bolivian holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly Bolivia and Peru. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit with arching fronds that can reach up to 1 meter in length. Its fronds are bi-lobed and have a leathery texture, with a distinctive deep green color and serrated margins.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests at elevations between 1,500 and 3,000 meters.
