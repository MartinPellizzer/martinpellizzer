Paris verticillata var. obovata

Paris verticillata var. obovata, commonly known as the white wood sorrel, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are heart-shaped and often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and relatively rare species within the Ranunculaceae family.
