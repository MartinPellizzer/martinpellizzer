Pittosporum acacioides

Pittosporum acacioides, commonly known as the New Zealand pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters tall. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It produces fragrant, white or cream-colored flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, orange berries.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
