Panicum agrostideum

Panicum agrostideum, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and panicles of small, red or brown flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as fields and disturbed soils. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in warm climates with well-drained soil.

Its fibrous roots help prevent soil erosion, making it a useful plant in ecological restoration projects.
