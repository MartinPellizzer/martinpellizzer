Peperomia volkensii

Peperomia volkensii, commonly known as the Congo peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pale green flowers arranged in dense clusters. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia volkensii is valued for its ornamental appeal and air-purifying qualities.
