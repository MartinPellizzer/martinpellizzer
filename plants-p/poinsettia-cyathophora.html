Poinsettia cyathophora

Poinsettia cyathophora, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its vibrant red bracts that surround small, inconspicuous yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its unique floral structures. Poinsettia cyathophora thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its foliage is typically green, with a waxy texture that adds to its aesthetic appeal.
