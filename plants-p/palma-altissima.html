Palma altissima

Palma altissima, commonly known as the giant palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its slow growth rate makes it a long-lived species, often reaching heights of over 20 meters.

The palm is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and tropical landscapes.
