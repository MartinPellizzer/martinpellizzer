Podocarpus rubens subsp. pabinamaensis

Podocarpus rubens subsp.

pabinamaensis, commonly known as the Pabinamaensis Podocarp, is a member of the Podocarpaceae family and is native to parts of Southeast Asia. It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant has lanceolate leaves that are usually leathery and arranged spirally on the stems. Its cones are small and woody, containing seeds that are dispersed by wind.

The species is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often found in lowland rainforests.
