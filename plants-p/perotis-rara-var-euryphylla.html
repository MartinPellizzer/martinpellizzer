Perotis rara var. euryphylla

Perotis rara var. euryphylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf perotis, is a rare and striking plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a subtle yet elegant touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy choice for arid landscapes.

Due to its limited distribution and unique morphology, it is considered a valuable species for botanical study and conservation efforts.
