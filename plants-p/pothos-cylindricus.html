Pothos cylindricus

Pothos cylindricus, commonly known as the cylindrical pothos, is a trailing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically a glossy green, with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by yellowish-orange berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular houseplant.

Its rapid growth and ability to climb or trail make it versatile for indoor gardening.
