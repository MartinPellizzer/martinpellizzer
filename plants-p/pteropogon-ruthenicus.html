Pteropogon ruthenicus

Pteropogon ruthenicus, commonly known as the Ruthenian grasshopper orchid, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, spurred flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as tropical forests. Its morphology includes a short stem with a single, showy flower that has a distinctive, elongated spur.

Pteropogon ruthenicus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts of rare and exotic plants.
