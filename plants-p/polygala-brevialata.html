Polygala brevialata

Polygala brevialata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant features slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open grasslands and along coastal areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native habitat.
