Platyclinis minima

Platyclinis minima, commonly known as the small-leaved platyclinis, is a rare flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged alternately along its slender stems. The plant produces delicate white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.
