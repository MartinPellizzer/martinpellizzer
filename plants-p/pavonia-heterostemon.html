Pavonia heterostemon

Pavonia heterostemon, commonly known as the scarlet pavonia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces clusters of showy, scarlet-red flowers. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and covered in fine pubescence. Its stems are erect and can grow up to 2 to 3 feet tall, making it a striking addition to gardens.

Pavonia heterostemon is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in full sun to partial shade.
