Persicaria sieboldii var. aestiva

Persicaria sieboldii var. aestiva, commonly known as the summer smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features upright stems that are often reddish in color, and it produces small, pinkish-white flowers in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds.

Its botanical characteristics make it an attractive addition to wetland gardens and a valuable plant for ecological restoration projects.
