Pseuderia smithiana var. amplifolia

Pseuderia smithiana var. amplifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved pseuderia, is a striking member of the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale green flowers surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical plant collections.
