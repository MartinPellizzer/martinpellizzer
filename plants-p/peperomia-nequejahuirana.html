Peperomia nequejahuirana

Peperomia nequejahuirana, commonly known as the Nequejahuiran Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It features rounded, glossy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink, adding visual interest to its foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in spadices, a characteristic feature of the Piperaceae family. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
