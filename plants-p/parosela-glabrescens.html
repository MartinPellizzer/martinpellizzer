Parosela glabrescens

Parosela glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth parosela, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in a fine, powdery coating, giving it a distinctive appearance.
