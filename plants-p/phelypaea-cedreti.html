Phelypaea cedreti

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories, where it thrives in humid conditions. Its spore-producing structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the fronds, contributing to its reproductive strategy.

Phelypaea cedreti is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to add a lush, green element to shaded gardens.
