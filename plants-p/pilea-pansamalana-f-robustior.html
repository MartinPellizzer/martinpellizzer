Pilea pansamalana f. robustior

Pilea pansamalana f. robustior, commonly known as the thick-leaf pilea, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features broad, oval-shaped leaves that are often thick and waxy, with a deep green coloration. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
