Poa hedbergii

Poa hedbergii, commonly known as the Hedberg's blue grass, is a perennial grass species native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and tufted growth habit, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically green to purplish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Poa hedbergii is valued for its drought tolerance and ability to form dense, attractive lawns or ground covers.
