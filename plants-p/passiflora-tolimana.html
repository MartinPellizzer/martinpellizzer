Passiflora tolimana

Passiflora tolimana, commonly known as the Tolima passionflower, is a species native to Colombia.

It is a climbing vine that produces striking, colorful flowers with intricate structures. The plant features five sepals, five petals, and a crown of filaments surrounding the reproductive organs. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that contains numerous seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
