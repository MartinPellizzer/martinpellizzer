Primula sinohumilis

Primula sinohumilis, commonly known as the Chinese primrose, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its clustered, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are broad, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Primula sinohumilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
