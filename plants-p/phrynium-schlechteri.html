Phrynium schlechteri

Phrynium schlechteri, commonly known as the Schlechter's arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Araceae family, characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves and striking inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often near water bodies. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
