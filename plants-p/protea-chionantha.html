Protea chionantha

Protea chionantha, commonly known as the silver protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence that resembles a fluffy cloud, hence its common name. The plant features dense, white to pale pink bracts that enclose numerous small, tubular flowers. Native to the Western Cape region, it thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and requires full sun.

Protea chionantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also valued in traditional medicine.
