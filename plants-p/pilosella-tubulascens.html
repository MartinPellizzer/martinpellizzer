Pilosella tubulascens

Pilosella tubulascens, commonly known as the slender pearly everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are hairy and linear, while its flowers are small and white or pale yellow.

Pilosella tubulascens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
