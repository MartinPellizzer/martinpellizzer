Pentzia microphylla

Pentzia microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved pentzia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it thrives in well-drained environments.
