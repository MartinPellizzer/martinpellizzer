Podocarpus rubens

Podocarpus rubens, commonly known as the red podocarp, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Podocarpaceae and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and reddish-brown bark. The tree produces small, spherical cones that contain edible seeds, which are often consumed by various bird species. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in mountainous regions with high humidity.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
