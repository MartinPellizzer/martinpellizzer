Pilosella neotremedalis

Pilosella neotremedalis, commonly known as the Neotropical goatweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit, with small, daisy-like flowers that are typically yellow or white in color. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which are often covered in fine, soft pubescence. It is native to South America and thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and grasslands.

Pilosella neotremedalis is often found in disturbed soils and can spread vegetatively through its rhizomes.
