Portulaca australis

Portulaca australis, commonly known as the Australian moss rose, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing annual herb with fleshy, succulent leaves that help it store water in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom throughout the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ability to withstand drought conditions makes it a resilient addition to xeriscaped landscapes.
