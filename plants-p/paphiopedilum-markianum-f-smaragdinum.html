Paphiopedilum markianum f. smaragdinum

Paphiopedilum markianum f. smaragdinum, commonly known as the Emerald Orchid, is a rare and striking hybrid orchid native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its vibrant green and yellow flowers, which are unique in their coloration and structure. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in cool, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
