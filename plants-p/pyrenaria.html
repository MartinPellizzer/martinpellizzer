Pyrenaria

Pyrenaria, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers and rosette-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil. Pyrenaria species are popular as ornamental plants due to their compact size and long-lasting blooms.

They are also known for their ability to thrive indoors with proper care.
