Peperomia quindioensis

Peperomia quindioensis, commonly known as the Quindio peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Andes mountains of Colombia.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture, adapted to conserve moisture in its native habitat. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which bloom during the warmer months. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.

Peperomia quindioensis thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for both novice and experienced plant growers.
