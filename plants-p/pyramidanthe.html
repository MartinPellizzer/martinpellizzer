Pyramidanthe

Pyramidanthe, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

These plants are characterized by their compact growth habit and showy, velvety flowers that come in a variety of colors. The leaves are typically rounded and arranged in a rosette pattern, often with a waxy texture. Pyramidanthe species are popular houseplants due to their ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

They thrive in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
