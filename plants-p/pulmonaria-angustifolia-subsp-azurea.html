Pulmonaria angustifolia subsp. azurea

Pulmonaria angustifolia subsp.

azurea, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lungwort, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe. It features basal rosettes of dark green, velvety leaves with distinctive silver veins.

The plant produces clusters of blue or violet flowers in early spring, which attract pollinators. It thrives in moist, shaded woodland areas and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for shady garden landscapes.
