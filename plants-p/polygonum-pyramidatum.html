Polygonum pyramidatum

Polygonum pyramidatum, commonly known as the Chinese knotweed, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its stout, bamboo-like stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Polygonum pyramidatum is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
