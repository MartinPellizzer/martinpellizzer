Phyllanthus bokorensis

Phyllanthus bokorensis, commonly known as the Bokorensis spurge, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Cambodia. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth.

The plant contains various bioactive compounds, making it of interest in pharmacological research.
