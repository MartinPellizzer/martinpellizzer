Pelexia decora

Pelexia decora, commonly known as the showy pelexia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features striking, palmately lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are showy, with five to seven petals that range in color from white to pink.

Pelexia decora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance, providing nectar for pollinators.
