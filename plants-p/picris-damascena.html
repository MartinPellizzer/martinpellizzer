Picris damascena

Picris damascena, commonly known as everlasting flower or scabious, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers and lance-shaped leaves. The plant is often found in dry, sunny grasslands and along roadsides. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

Picris damascena is known for its long-lasting flowers, which remain intact even when dried, making it popular in floral arrangements.
