Peperomia subdita

Peperomia subdita, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically silver or gray-green in color, with prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, usually in shades of white or pale pink. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and drought tolerance.

Peperomia subdita is often prized for its attractive foliage and ease of care.
