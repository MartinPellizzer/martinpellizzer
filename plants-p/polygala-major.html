Polygala major

Polygala major, commonly known as the greater milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in open, dry habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, and it produces pods that contain small, round seeds.

Polygala major is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and bloom.
