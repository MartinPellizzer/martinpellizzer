Phyllanthus urinaria var. laevis

Phyllanthus urinaria var. laevis, commonly known as the smooth bladder nut, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, green, bladder-like fruits that contain a single seed. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has alternate, ovate leaves with a smooth texture. It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is often found in wetlands and along riverbanks.
