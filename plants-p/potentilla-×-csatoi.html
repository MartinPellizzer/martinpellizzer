Potentilla × csatoi

Potentilla × csatoi, commonly known as the Chinese firethorn, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny growth habit and small, bright red berries that persist through the winter. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and produces clusters of yellow flowers in early spring. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

Botanically, it is a hybrid between Potentilla fruticosa and Potentilla indica.
