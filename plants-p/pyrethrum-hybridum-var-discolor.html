Pyrethrum hybridum var. discolor

Pyrethrum hybridum var. discolor, commonly known as the painted lady.

This plant is a member of the Asteraceae family and is native to South Africa. It is characterized by its showy, semi-double to double flowers that range in color from white to pink, yellow, and red. The flowers are typically arranged in dense, spherical clusters, and the plant produces small, green, oval-shaped fruits called cypsela.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
