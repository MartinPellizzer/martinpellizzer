Pilea microphylla var. trianthemoides

Pilea microphylla var. trianthemoides, commonly known as the Chinese money plant or pancake plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rounded, fleshy leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its unique leaf shape and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
