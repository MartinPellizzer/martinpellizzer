Primula iljinskii

Primula iljinskii, commonly known as the Iljinskii primrose, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows at high elevations. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, with a velvety texture, and it produces small, clustered flowers during the spring season.
