Peperomia roqueana

Peperomia roqueana, commonly known as the Rock Peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It features fleshy, ovate leaves that are usually green with a waxy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink. The plant is well adapted to rocky soils and can thrive in bright, indirect light.

It is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.
