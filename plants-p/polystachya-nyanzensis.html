Polystachya nyanzensis

Polystachya nyanzensis, commonly known as the Nyanza bamboo, is a tropical plant native to East Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its clustered growth habit and slender, grass-like leaves. The plant produces striking inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
