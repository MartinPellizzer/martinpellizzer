Ptychosperma rosselense

Ptychosperma rosselense, commonly known as the African fan palm, is a species of cycad native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid climate to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is often cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
