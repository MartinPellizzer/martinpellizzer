Panicum distichum var. luxurians

Panicum distichum var. luxurians, commonly known as purple lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and distinctive purple-tinted foliage. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often tinged with red or purple hues. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for various ecological and ornamental applications.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and ability to form dense, attractive clumps.
