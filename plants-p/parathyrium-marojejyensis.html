Parathyrium marojejyensis

Parathyrium marojejyensis, commonly known as the Marojejy pteridium, is a rare fern species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Pteridaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like fronds. The plant is notable for its unique morphology, featuring intricate leaf structures and a preference for shaded, moist environments. It is endemic to the Marojejy National Park, where it thrives in the humid, tropical rainforest.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
