Phlomis turkestana

Phlomis turkestana, commonly known as Turkistan thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and gray-green foliage with a fuzzy texture. The plant produces showy purple to pink flower heads in late summer, which are arranged in dense, spherical clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
