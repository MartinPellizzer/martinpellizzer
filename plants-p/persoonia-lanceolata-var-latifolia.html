Persoonia lanceolata var. latifolia

Persoonia lanceolata var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved mistflower, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are broader than the typical variety, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white, five-petaled flowers that bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal and heathland environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in suitable climates.
