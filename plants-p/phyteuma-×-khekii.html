Phyteuma × khekii

Phyteuma × khekii, commonly known as the hybrid sunflower.

This plant is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Phyteuma spicatum and Phyteuma campanulatum. It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, where it thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. The plant produces tall, spiky flower heads that range in color from yellow to orange, making it a striking addition to gardens.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions.
