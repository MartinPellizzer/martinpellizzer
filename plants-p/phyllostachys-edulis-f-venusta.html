Phyllostachys edulis f. venusta

Phyllostachys edulis f. venusta, commonly known as the ornamental bamboo, is a variant of the bamboo species Phyllostachys edulis.

It is characterized by its striking, often variegated foliage that displays shades of yellow, white, and green. This plant is typically cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and landscapes. It grows as a clump-forming bamboo, reaching heights of up to 10 feet.

The leaves are long, narrow, and have a smooth texture, making it a popular choice for ornamental planting.
