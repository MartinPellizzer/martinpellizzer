Peraltea oxyphylla

Peraltea oxyphylla, commonly known as the slender flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas or near freshwater sources.

Peraltea oxyphylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual impact.
