Phyllanthus claussenii var. oblongifolius

Phyllanthus claussenii var. oblongifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf phyllanthus, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged alternately along its stems. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule containing several small, hard seeds.

This species is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth.
