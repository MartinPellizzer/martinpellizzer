Potentilla Verticillaris F. Acutipetala

Potentilla verticillaris f. acutipetala is a unique variant of the common potentilla plant known for its distinctive foliage and delicate flowers. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are arranged in a whorled pattern, giving it a compact and tidy appearance. The flowers, typically yellow or cream, bloom in spring and add a subtle yet charming touch to gardens. Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is a favored choice for rock gardens and borders.
