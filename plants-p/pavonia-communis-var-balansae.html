Pavonia communis var. balansae

Pavonia communis var. balansae, commonly known as the Balansae Pavonia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red to pink bract-like flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub with lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
