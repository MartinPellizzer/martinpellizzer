Polygala mariamae

Polygala mariamae, commonly known as the Mariamae's milkweed, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often pale yellow or greenish flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are usually narrow and opposite, while its stems are slender and branching.
