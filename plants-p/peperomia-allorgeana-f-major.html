Peperomia allorgeana f. major

Peperomia allorgeana f. major, commonly known as the Giant Peperomia, is a compact shrub native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or brown, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate light conditions.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
