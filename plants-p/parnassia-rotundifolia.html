Parnassia rotundifolia

Parnassia rotundifolia, commonly known as the roundleaf parnassia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Ranunculaceae.

It is characterized by its smooth, waxy leaves that are typically round or kidney-shaped. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that are often borne on long, slender stems. It is commonly found in moist, shaded habitats such as bogs and wet meadows.

Parnassia rotundifolia is known for its striking appearance and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.
