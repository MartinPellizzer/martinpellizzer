Panicum tephrosanthum

Panicum tephrosanthum, commonly known as the red panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and feathery inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

It produces small, spike-like clusters of flowers that are often pink or red in color.
