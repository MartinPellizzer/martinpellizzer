Piper breve var. bellae-vistae

Piper breve var. bellae-vistae, commonly known as the dwarf pepper plant, is a compact shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant features small, glossy leaves and produces tiny, berry-like fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
