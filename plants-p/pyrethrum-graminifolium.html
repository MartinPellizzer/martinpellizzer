Pyrethrum graminifolium

Pyrethrum graminifolium, commonly known as the grass-leaved pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its fine, grass-like leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant is known for producing pyrethrins, which are natural insecticides used in organic pest control. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Pyrethrum graminifolium is often cultivated for both ornamental purposes and its medicinal properties.
