Peperomia houelmonte

Peperomia houelmonte, commonly known as the Houelmonte peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and silver, adding visual interest to any setting. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia houelmonte is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
