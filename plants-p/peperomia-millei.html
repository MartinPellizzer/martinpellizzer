Peperomia millei

Peperomia millei, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically silver or grayish-white in color, with a textured, almost metallic appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal water needs.

Peperomia millei is valued for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments.
