Pediocactus pulcherrimus

Pediocactus pulcherrimus, commonly known as the beautiful cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of southern Arizona and northern Sonora, Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a smooth, greenish-gray stem that often has a bluish tinge. The plant produces white to pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its spines are typically short and woolly, providing protection from herbivores and reducing water loss.

Pediocactus pulcherrimus is well adapted to harsh desert conditions, storing water in its thick, fleshy stem.
