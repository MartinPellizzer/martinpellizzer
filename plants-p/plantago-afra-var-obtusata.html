Plantago afra var. obtusata

Plantago afra var. obtusata, commonly known as African plantain or broadleaf plantain, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of broad, oval, and somewhat waxy leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along stream banks.

This variety is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
