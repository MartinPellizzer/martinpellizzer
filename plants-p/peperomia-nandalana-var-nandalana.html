Peperomia nandalana var. nandalana

Peperomia nandalana var. nandalana, commonly known as the Nandalana peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, with a glossy, dark green surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, arranged in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and conservation gardens.
