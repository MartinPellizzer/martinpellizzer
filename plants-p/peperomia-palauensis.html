Peperomia palauensis

Peperomia palauensis, commonly known as the Palau peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to the island of Palau in the Western Pacific.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Peperomia palauensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
