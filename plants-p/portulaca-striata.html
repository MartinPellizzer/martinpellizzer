Portulaca striata

Portulaca striata, commonly known as the striped portulaca, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing, succulent species that produces vibrant pink or red flowers. The plant features fleshy, ribbed leaves and is adapted to thrive in arid and sandy environments. It blooms throughout the growing season, often from spring to autumn.

Portulaca striata is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to withstand drought conditions.
