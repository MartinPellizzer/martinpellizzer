Pleopeltis schoutenii

Pleopeltis schoutenii, commonly known as the Schouten's rock fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, dense growth habit. The plant has leathery, lance-shaped fronds with a distinctive, pale underside that contrasts with its darker upper surface. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on tree trunks or rocks in tropical forests.

Pleopeltis schoutenii is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in terrariums or as a houseplant.
