Psidium prostratum

Psidium prostratum, commonly known as the prostrate guava, is a flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is a low-growing shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are followed by edible, berry-like fruits. Its leaves are oval, glossy, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Psidium prostratum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of edible fruits in tropical regions.
