Pothos argyraeus

Pothos argyraeus, commonly known as the silver pothos, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are predominantly dark green with a silvery or white variegation. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers warm, humid environments. Its trailing growth habit makes it an excellent choice for hanging baskets or shelves.

Pothos argyraeus is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular houseplant for beginners.
