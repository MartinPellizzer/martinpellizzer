Poa perligulata

Poa perligulata, commonly known as the silver hair grass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-tipped leaves and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along stream banks. Its inflorescence consists of a dense spike of small, spikelet-like flowers.

Poa perligulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
