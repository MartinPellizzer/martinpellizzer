Piper cyphophyllopse var. brevipes

Piper cyphophyllopse var. brevipes, commonly known as the short-stemmed pepper plant, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piper genus, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and produces small, clustered flowers. Its fruits are berries that mature to a dark purple color.

It is valued for its aromatic leaves and potential medicinal properties.
