Polystichum mulleri

Polystichum mulleri, commonly known as the Chinese shield fern, is a species of fern native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The fronds are deeply divided into lance-shaped segments, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

This fern is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
