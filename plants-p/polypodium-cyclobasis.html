Polypodium cyclobasis

Polypodium cyclobasis, commonly known as the cyclobasis holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its evergreen, leathery fronds that are typically divided into narrow, lance-shaped segments. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including countries such as India and China. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found growing on rocks or tree trunks.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a distinctive addition to both natural and cultivated landscapes.
