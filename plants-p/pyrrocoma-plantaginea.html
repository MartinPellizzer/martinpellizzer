Pyrrocoma plantaginea

Pyrrocoma plantaginea, commonly known as the silverleaf pyrrocoma, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ericaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in Argentina and Chile. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.
