Pultenaea angustifolia

Pultenaea angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaf pultenaea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is found in regions such as New South Wales and Victoria. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is often used in native gardens due to its ornamental value and adaptability.

The species is also valued for its role in supporting local wildlife and maintaining ecological balance.
