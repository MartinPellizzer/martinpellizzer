Persicaria longiseta f. divaricatiramosa

Persicaria longiseta f. divaricatiramosa, commonly known as the branched-stemmed smartweed, is a variant of the smartweed species characterized by its distinctive branching pattern in the stems.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a smooth upper surface and a hairy underside, typical of the Polygonaceae family. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense, terminal spikes, producing a pinkish to reddish coloration.

This cultivar is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in moist, shady environments.
