Pilea petiolaris

Pilea petiolaris, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features round, fleshy leaves that are typically bright green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by spherical, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant.
