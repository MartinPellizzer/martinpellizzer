Piper gentryi

Piper gentryi, commonly known as Gentry's pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the humid lowland forests of Central America, particularly in countries like Guatemala and Belize. The plant is characterized by its vine-like growth habit, with dark green, ovate leaves that have a glossy surface. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into elongated, fleshy fruits.

The plant is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and potential medicinal properties.
