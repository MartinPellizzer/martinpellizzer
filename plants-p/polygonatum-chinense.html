Polygonatum chinense

Polygonatum chinense, commonly known as Chinese yellow flag, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Asparagaceae and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces yellowish-green flowers in early summer. Its berries are red and edible, often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Polygonatum chinense is valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.
