Panicum albomarginatum

Panicum albomarginatum, commonly known as silverleaf panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, grass-like appearance with narrow, silver-gray leaves that have distinct white margins. The plant produces fluffy, pale pink or white inflorescences that are highly visible during the growing season. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in open fields and along roadsides.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping and native plant gardens.
