Puccinellia Preslii

Puccinellia preslii is a rare and delicate grass species native to specific regions of Central Asia. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in saline or alkaline soils. This plant is known for its small, narrow leaves and compact growth habit. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered vulnerable to environmental changes. Studying Puccinellia preslii offers insights into plant adaptation and conservation in harsh ecosystems.
