Plectranthus harrisii

Plectranthus harrisii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and produces small, tubular flowers in shades of purple and blue. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.

Plectranthus harrisii thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
