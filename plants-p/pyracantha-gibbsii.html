Pyracantha gibbsii

Pyracantha gibbsii, commonly known as the yellow-fruited firethorn, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant produces bright orange to yellow berries that remain on the shrub throughout the winter. Its leaves are elliptical, dark green, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Pyracantha gibbsii is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
