Ponthieva schaffneri

Ponthieva schaffneri, commonly known as the Schaffner's ponthieva, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, ribbon-like leaves that float on the water surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically submerged, which is a distinguishing feature of the genus. It thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in swampy areas.

Ponthieva schaffneri is valued in aquascaping for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
