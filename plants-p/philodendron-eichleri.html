Philodendron eichleri

Philodendron eichleri, commonly known as the Eichleri philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green with a prominent midrib. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and climb efficiently through its environment.

Philodendron eichleri is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its adaptability and relatively low maintenance requirements.
