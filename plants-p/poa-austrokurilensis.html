Poa austrokurilensis

Poa austrokurilensis, commonly known as the Austrokuril grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the southern islands of the Kuril archipelago and is adapted to cool, temperate climates. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas or mountainous regions. Its slender, grass-like leaves are usually a vibrant green, and it produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its unique ecological adaptations and limited distribution.
