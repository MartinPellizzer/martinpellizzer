Panicum stenodoides

Panicum stenodoides, commonly known as narrow-leaf panicgrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. The plant is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of water bodies.

Its botanical features make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
