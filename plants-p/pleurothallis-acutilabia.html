Pleurothallis acutilabia

Pleurothallis acutilabia, commonly known as the sharp-labiate pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features slender, wiry stems and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. Its leaves are usually narrow and elongated, adapted for efficient water absorption in its natural habitat.

Pleurothallis acutilabia is valued by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
