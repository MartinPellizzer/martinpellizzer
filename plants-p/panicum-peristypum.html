Panicum peristypum

Panicum peristypum, commonly known as the slender panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, pale green to golden spikelets that are often clustered at the ends of the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Panicum peristypum is typically drought-tolerant and can withstand moderate grazing.
