Phaleria urens

Phaleria urens, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Anacardiaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that contain seeds, which are often dispersed by animals. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.
