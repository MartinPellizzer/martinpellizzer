Perieteris tristis

Perieteris tristis, commonly known as the sad fern, is a delicate tropical fern native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It features finely divided, arching fronds that create a graceful, flowing appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments with consistently moist, well-draining soil. Its leaves are typically a deep green, with a soft, velvety texture.

Perieteris tristis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to add a touch of elegance to shaded garden spaces.
