Phyllanthus lathyroides

Phyllanthus lathyroides, commonly known as the African spurge, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its branched stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellowish flowers and is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides. It contains various chemical compounds, some of which have been studied for their potential medicinal properties.
