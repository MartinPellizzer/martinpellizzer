Pleurothallis spiculifera

Pleurothallis spiculifera, commonly known as the spiculate pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, often appearing in late summer or early autumn. Its leaves are usually narrow and arranged in a rosette, helping to retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Due to its unique appearance and specific growing conditions, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
