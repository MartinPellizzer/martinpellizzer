Poa sinattenuata

Poa sinattenuata, commonly known as the slender meadow grass, is a species of grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, narrow leaves and compact growth habit, often forming dense mats in its natural habitat. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along stream banks. Its inflorescence consists of a single spikelet, which is usually terminal and slightly curved.

Poa sinattenuata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
