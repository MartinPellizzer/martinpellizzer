Pueraria yunnanensis

Pueraria yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan kudzu, is a climbing legume native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its pinnately compound leaves and showy purple or white flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several oval, flat seeds. It is often found in forested areas and is adapted to thrive in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and potential ecological benefits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation projects.
