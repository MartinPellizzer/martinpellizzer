Parodia gaucha

Parodia gaucha, commonly known as the Gauchas Cactus, is a species of cactus native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in dense white spines. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, blooming in late spring or early summer.

Parodia gaucha is a slow-growing plant that thrives in full sun and requires minimal water once established.
