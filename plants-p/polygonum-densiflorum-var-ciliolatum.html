Polygonum densiflorum var. ciliolatum

Polygonum densiflorum var. ciliolatum, commonly known as the velvetleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving them a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, reddish-purple flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks.

Its rapid growth and ability to spread vegetatively make it both a common weed and an important plant in wetland ecosystems.
