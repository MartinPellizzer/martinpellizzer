Pyrethrum deserticola

Pyrethrum deserticola, commonly known as desert pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and pale yellow to white flowers. The plant produces fine, hairy leaves that help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. Its flowers contain pyrethrins, which are natural insecticides, making it valuable in organic pest control.

Pyrethrum deserticola is well adapted to drought and poor soil conditions, thriving in semi-arid to desert climates.
