Pursegloveia minima

Pursegloveia minima, commonly known as the dwarf purselove, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant features small, narrow leaves and produces clusters of tiny, pale yellow flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it well-suited to harsh environments.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
