Peperomia cogniauxii

Peperomia cogniauxii, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to Madagascar.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, giving it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color, which are not particularly showy. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia cogniauxii is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and occasional fertilization during the growing season.
