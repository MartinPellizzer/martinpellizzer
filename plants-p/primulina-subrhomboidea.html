Primulina subrhomboidea

Primulina subrhomboidea, commonly known as the rhomboid primula, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Vietnam, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.

Primulina subrhomboidea requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to flourish.
