Prioria buchholzii

Prioria buchholzii, commonly known as the yellow elder, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal areas and is well adapted to saline and sandy soils. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting a variety of pollinators. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
