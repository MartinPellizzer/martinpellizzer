Phyllostachys bambusoides var. sulphurea

Phyllostachys bambusoides var. sulphurea, commonly known as the golden bamboo, is a variety of bamboo characterized by its vibrant yellow-green foliage.

It is a clump-forming plant that produces long, slender culms with a smooth, pale surface. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often displaying a striking yellow hue that intensifies in sunlight. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

This bamboo is also valued for its fast growth and ability to create dense screens or hedges.
