Pilea longzhouensis

Pilea longzhouensis, commonly known as the Longzhou pilea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to China, specifically the Guangxi region, and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically green with a subtle variegation. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in a spadix, typical of the Araceae family.

Pilea longzhouensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
