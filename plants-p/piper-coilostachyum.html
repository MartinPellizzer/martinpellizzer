Piper coilostachyum

Piper coilostachyum, commonly known as the spiral-stalked pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves along its stem, which gives it its common name. It produces small, green berries that mature to a dark purple color.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has potential uses in traditional medicine.
