Pterostylis × ingens

Pterostylis × ingens, commonly known as the giant spider orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like appearance with long, slender petals and a prominent, elongated labellum. The plant typically grows in dense, moist forests and requires a cool, shady environment to thrive. Its flowers are often pollinated by specific species of wasps, showcasing a unique mutualistic relationship.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts due to its rarity and beauty.
