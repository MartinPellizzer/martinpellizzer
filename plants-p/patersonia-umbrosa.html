Patersonia umbrosa

Patersonia umbrosa, commonly known as the shadowy patersonia, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Australia.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant thrives in shady, moist environments, often found in forest understoreys. It is characterized by its ability to form dense mats, which help stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

Patersonia umbrosa is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
