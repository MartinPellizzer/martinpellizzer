Phrynium laoticum

Phrynium laoticum, commonly known as the Laotian arrowleaf, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to deep red. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Phrynium laoticum is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its traditional uses in local cultures.
