Pulicaria Arabica

Pulicaria arabica is a hardy shrub native to arid regions of the Middle East and North Africa. It thrives in sandy and rocky soils with minimal water requirements. Known for its small, yellow flowers and silver-gray foliage, it adds a unique aesthetic to drought-tolerant gardens. This plant is valued for its resilience and ability to stabilize soil in harsh environments. Its medicinal properties have also been studied for potential health benefits.
