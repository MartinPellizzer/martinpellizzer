Peltariopsis grossheimii

Peltariopsis grossheimii, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in coastal regions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, clustered blooms in shades of white and pink.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and protected species in its native range.
