Patrinia scabiosifolia f. hispida

Patrinia scabiosifolia f. hispida, commonly known as the hairy leaf patrinia, is a flowering plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its hairy, lance-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shady areas.
