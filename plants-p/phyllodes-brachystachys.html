Phyllodes brachystachys

Phyllodes brachystachys, commonly known as the narrow-leaf wattle, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features narrow, strap-like phyllodes that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small yellow flowers in late spring, which are followed by elongated, cylindrical seed pods.

This species is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant plant.
