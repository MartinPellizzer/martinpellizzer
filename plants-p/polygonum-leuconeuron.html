Polygonum leuconeuron

Polygonum leuconeuron, commonly known as the redstem filaree, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants known for their distinctive bracts and often edible parts. The plant features slender, reddish stems and small, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Polygonum leuconeuron is typically found in moist environments such as meadows and stream banks.
