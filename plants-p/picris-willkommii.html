Picris willkommii

Picris willkommii, commonly known as the yellow rocket, is a flowering plant native to Europe, North Africa, and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and serrated leaves, which are often mistaken for other members of the Asteraceae family. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open areas, thriving in temperate climates. Its flowers bloom in the summer months, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The plant has been used historically in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
