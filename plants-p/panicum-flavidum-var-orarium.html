Panicum flavidum var. orarium

Panicum flavidum var. orarium, commonly known as the golden panicum, is a variant of the panic grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow inflorescences that appear in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.

This ornamental grass is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often used in landscaping and erosion control.
