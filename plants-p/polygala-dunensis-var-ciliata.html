Polygala dunensis var. ciliata

Polygala dunensis var. ciliata, commonly known as the Ciliate milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink. The leaves are alternate, narrow, and often have ciliate margins, giving the plant its specific epithet. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

This species is of interest to botanists and horticulturists due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
