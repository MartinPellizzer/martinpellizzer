Pouteria chocoensis

Pouteria chocoensis, commonly known as the Chocoan tamarind, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and fragrant, yellowish-green flowers.
