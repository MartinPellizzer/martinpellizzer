Pennisetum distachyum

Pennisetum distachyum, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its clumping growth habit and striking purple to pink plumes that appear in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, feathery inflorescences that add ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for landscaping.

Pennisetum distachyum is also valued for its ability to attract birds and butterflies.
