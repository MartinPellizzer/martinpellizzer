Peltandra Virginica F. Heterophylla

Peltandra virginica f. heterophylla is a rare variant of the yellow flag iris known for its unique leaf morphology. This plant is native to specific wetland habitats in the southeastern united states. it exhibits a striking difference in leaf shape compared to the typical form of the species. the f. heterophylla form is often found in shallow water or along the edges of ponds. its conservation status highlights the importance of protecting wetland ecosystems.
