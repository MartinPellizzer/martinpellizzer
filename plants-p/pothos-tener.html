Pothos tener

Pothos tener, commonly known as the variegated pothos, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a striking pattern of white and yellow variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers, though it is primarily grown for its ornamental foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Pothos tener is a hardy and low-maintenance plant, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
