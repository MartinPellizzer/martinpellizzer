Paepalanthus planifolius var. minor

Paepalanthus planifolius var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf palm tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is a small, slow-growing palm that typically reaches a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features a slender, unbranched trunk and pinnately compound leaves with long, narrow leaflets. This variety is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates.
