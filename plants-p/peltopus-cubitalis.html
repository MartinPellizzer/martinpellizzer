Peltopus cubitalis

Peltopus cubitalis, commonly known as the knee plant, is a unique species characterized by its distinctive growth pattern.

It features a short, thick stem that resembles a knee, giving it its common name. The plant typically has waxy, broad leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often overlooked due to their subtle appearance.

Peltopus cubitalis is native to tropical regions and thrives in moist, shaded environments.
