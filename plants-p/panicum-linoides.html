Panicum linoides

Panicum linoides, commonly known as the slender panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. It is well adapted to dry conditions and can tolerate poor soil quality.

Its botanical characteristics make it a useful plant for erosion control and ornamental purposes.
