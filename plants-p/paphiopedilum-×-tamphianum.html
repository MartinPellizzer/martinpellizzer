Paphiopedilum × tamphianum

Paphiopedilum × tamphianum, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a captivating member of the orchid family native to Southeast Asia.

This plant is characterized by its unique, showy flowers that often feature intricate patterns and vibrant colors. It thrives in tropical climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. The hybrid is prized for its long-lasting blooms and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Its botanical structure includes fleshy roots and broad, strap-like leaves that contribute to its overall aesthetic appeal.
