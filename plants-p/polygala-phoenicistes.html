Polygala phoenicistes

Polygala phoenicistes, commonly known as the flame dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. The plant produces striking yellow-orange flowers that resemble dandelions, with a distinctive crown of bracts. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
