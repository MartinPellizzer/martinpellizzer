Peperomia agusanensis

Peperomia agusanensis, commonly known as the Agusan peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit. The plant typically features thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and silver. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable ornamental plant.
