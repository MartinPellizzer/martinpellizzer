Pinanga tenella var. tenuissima

Pinanga tenella var. tenuissima, commonly known as the slender-leafed bamboo palm, is a compact, evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features narrow, arching leaves that give it a delicate, fern-like appearance. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, making it suitable for indoor cultivation. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread slowly, forming dense clumps over time.

The leaves are usually dark green and glossy, with a slight sheen that enhances its ornamental value.
