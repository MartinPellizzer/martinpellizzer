Polystachya bradei

Polystachya bradei, commonly known as the Brade's spider orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like appearance, with long, slender inflorescences that bear numerous small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it produces a distinct, fragrant scent to attract pollinators.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat preferences, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
