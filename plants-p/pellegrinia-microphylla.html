Pellegrinia microphylla

Pellegrinia microphylla, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, silver-gray leaves that are densely packed, giving it a compact, bushy appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for succulent collections.
