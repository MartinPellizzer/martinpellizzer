Piper wichmannii

Piper wichmannii, commonly known as the Hainan pepper or Hainan long pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the island of Hainan in China and is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, clustered green berries that turn red when ripe, which are used in traditional Chinese medicine. It is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used for its purported medicinal benefits.

The plant's botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and pharmacological study.
