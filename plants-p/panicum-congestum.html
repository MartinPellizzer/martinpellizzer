Panicum congestum

Panicum congestum, commonly known as purple lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and produces fluffy, purple-tinted seed heads that are visually striking. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it adaptable to various environmental conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that adds to its ornamental appeal.

Panicum congestum is often used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and ability to provide habitat for small wildlife.
