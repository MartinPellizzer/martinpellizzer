Phyllostachys violascens f. viridisulcata

Phyllostachys violascens f. viridisulcata, commonly known as the green-striped bamboo, is a variant of the green bamboo species characterized by its distinctive green stripes on the culms.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is native to eastern Asia, particularly China. The plant is a clump-forming perennial, reaching heights of up to 6 meters, with slender, hollow stems that are often used in ornamental landscaping.
