Polyscias racemosa

Polyscias racemosa, commonly known as the clustered glorybower, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and glossy, dark green leaves. Polyscias racemosa is valued for its aesthetic appeal and ability to tolerate partial shade in garden settings.
