Pelazoneuron puberulum

Pelazoneuron puberulum, commonly known as the silver-leafed fern, is a rare and striking plant characterized by its soft, velvety foliage.

Its leaves are typically broad and lobed, with a silvery sheen that reflects light, giving it a unique appearance. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in temperate rainforests. It produces spores in clusters rather than flowers, making it a true fern.

Due to its delicate nature, it requires careful cultivation to ensure its survival.
