Pleurothallis semiscabra

Pleurothallis semiscabra, commonly known as the rough-stemmed pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features narrow, leathery leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is noted for its intricate floral structure.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often found in cloud forests and is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique beauty.
