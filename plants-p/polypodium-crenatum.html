Polypodium crenatum

Polypodium crenatum, commonly known as the serrated huperzia, is a species of fern native to the tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

The plant grows as a terrestrial or epiphytic fern, often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Polypodium crenatum is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
