Pimelea rugulosa

Pimelea rugulosa, commonly known as the rough pimelea, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and open forests.

Pimelea rugulosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
