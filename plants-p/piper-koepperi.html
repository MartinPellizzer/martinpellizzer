Piper koepperi

Piper koepperi, commonly known as the Java pepper or koepperi pepper, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when mature, containing the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and cuisine. Its leaves are also used in herbal remedies due to their medicinal properties.

Piper koepperi is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its culinary and medicinal uses.
