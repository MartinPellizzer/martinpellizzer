Penstemon pononii

Penstemon pononii, commonly known as the Sierra penstemon, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils in mountainous regions. The plant produces tall, upright stems with clusters of tubular, two-lipped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Penstemon pononii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in dry, well-drained conditions.
