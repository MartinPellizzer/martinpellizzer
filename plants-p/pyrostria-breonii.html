Pyrostria breonii

Pyrostria breonii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical tree native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, red-orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has a straight, slender trunk with smooth, grey bark and large, pinnate leaves. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Pyrostria breonii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
