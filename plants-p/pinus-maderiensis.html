Pinus maderiensis

Pinus maderiensis, commonly known as the Maderas Pine, is a coniferous tree native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its needle-like leaves and cone-bearing structure typical of the pine family. The tree produces long, slender cones that contain seeds enclosed in woody scales. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical features make it an important species for both ecological and commercial purposes.
