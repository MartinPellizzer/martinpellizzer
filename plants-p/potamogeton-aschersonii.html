Potamogeton aschersonii

Potamogeton aschersonii, commonly known as the ascherson's pondweed, is a submerged aquatic plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It has long, slender stems that are often branched and bear small, oval leaves arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments such as lakes and ponds.
