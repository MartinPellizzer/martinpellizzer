Pimelea linifolia var. smithiana

Pimelea linifolia var. smithiana, commonly known as Smith's feathery geebung, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub with slender, wiry stems and small, needle-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in suitable climates.
