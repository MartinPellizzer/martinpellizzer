Phyllanthus angustatus

Phyllanthus angustatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf sandwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a low-growing herbaceous plant characterized by its small, narrow leaves and clusters of tiny yellow flowers. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
