Polygala oedipus

Polygala oedipus, commonly known as the "Maiden's blush" or "Pearly everlasting," is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, spindly stems and small, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as fields and woodland edges. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Polygala oedipus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
