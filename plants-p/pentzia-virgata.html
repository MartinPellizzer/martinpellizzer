Pentzia virgata

Pentzia virgata, commonly known as the silver cotula, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features silver-gray foliage with a woolly texture, and its small yellow flowers bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Pentzia virgata is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
