Pterostylis erecta

Pterostylis erecta, commonly known as the erect helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest floors and is characterized by its distinctive, helmet-shaped flower. The plant has a short, stocky stem with a single, erect flower that is usually greenish-yellow in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it blooms during the autumn season.

Pterostylis erecta is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique morphology and ecological significance in its habitat.
