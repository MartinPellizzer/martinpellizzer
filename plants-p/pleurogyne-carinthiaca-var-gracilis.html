Pleurogyne carinthiaca var. gracilis

Pleurogyne carinthiaca var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender pleurogyne, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal or inland regions.

It is a small shrub that produces small, clustered inflorescences, making it a notable species in its native habitat.
