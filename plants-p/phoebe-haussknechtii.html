Phoebe haussknechtii

Phoebe haussknechtii, commonly known as the Texas phoebe, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant has simple, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, white or pale green flowers. It is often found in open woodlands and along streambanks.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
