Peperomia sidifolia

Peperomia sidifolia, commonly known as the swordleaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a slightly textured surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia sidifolia is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and pruning to maintain its compact form.
