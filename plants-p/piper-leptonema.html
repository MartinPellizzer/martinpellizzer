Piper leptonema

Piper leptonema, commonly known as the slender pipevine, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with heart-shaped leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. It produces small, round berries that are typically yellow or orange when mature.

Piper leptonema is valued in traditional medicine for its potential pharmacological properties.
