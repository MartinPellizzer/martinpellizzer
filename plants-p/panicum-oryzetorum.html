Panicum oryzetorum

Panicum oryzetorum, commonly known as ricegrass, is a flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia. The plant is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a bluish-green hue.

Panicum oryzetorum is often used as a forage crop and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
