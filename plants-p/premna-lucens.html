Premna lucens

Premna lucens, commonly known as the shining premna, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, including India and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine white hairs, giving them a silvery appearance. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue or violet, arranged in clusters.

Premna lucens is often found in moist, shady areas and is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
