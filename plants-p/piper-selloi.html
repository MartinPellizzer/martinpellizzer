Piper selloi

Piper selloi, commonly known as the Sello's pepper or Sello's black pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, dark purple berries that are used as a spice, similar to black pepper. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly spicy aroma.

Piper selloi thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the undergrowth of rainforests.
