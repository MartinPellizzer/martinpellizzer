Potentilla speciosa subsp. illyrica

Potentilla speciosa subsp.

illyrica, commonly known as the Illyrian potentilla, is a perennial shrub native to the Balkan Peninsula. It features slender, wiry stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink.

The plant has hirsute (hairy) leaves that are typically elliptical or ovate in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
