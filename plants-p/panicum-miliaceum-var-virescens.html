Panicum miliaceum var. virescens

Panicum miliaceum var. virescens, commonly known as green millet, is a variant of millet that is characterized by its vibrant green foliage.

It is a grass species native to warm temperate and tropical regions, often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that mature into pale to dark brown grains. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for both ornamental and agricultural purposes.
