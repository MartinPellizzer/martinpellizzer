Panicum oligosanthes

Panicum oligosanthes, commonly known as the narrowleaf panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland areas and along stream banks. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in late summer. Its botanical characteristics include a fibrous root system and a preference for partial to full sunlight.

It is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
