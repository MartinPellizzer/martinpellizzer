Punotia

Punotia, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves that have a distinctive silvery underside. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruit is a small, round berry that changes color as it ripens.

Punotia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in some cultures.
