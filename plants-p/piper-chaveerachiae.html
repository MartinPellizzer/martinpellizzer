Piper chaveerachiae

Piper chaveerachiae, commonly known as the Chaveera pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the rainforests of South America, specifically Brazil. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its fruit, which is a small, dark purple berry, contains the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine.

Piper chaveerachiae is valued for its unique flavor and potential medicinal properties.
