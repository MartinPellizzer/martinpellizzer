Pedilonum Aphanochilum

Coral bean, scientifically known as Pedilonum aphanochilum, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America. This species is prized for its vibrant, flame-like blooms that appear in clusters along its slender, branching stems. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardens and botanical collections. While it thrives in warm climates, it requires careful attention to soil and water conditions to flourish. Despite its beauty, it is important to note that some parts of the plant can be toxic to humans and animals.
