Physurus mystacinus

Physurus mystacinus, commonly known as the African violet.

This plant is native to Africa and is a member of the Gesneriaceae family. It is a herbaceous perennial with thick, fleshy leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The leaves are typically velvety and come in various shades of green.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.
