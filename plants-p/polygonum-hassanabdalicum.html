Polygonum hassanabdalicum

Polygonum hassanabdalicum, commonly known as Hassanabad polygonum, is a flowering plant species native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the family Polygonaceae, which includes plants commonly referred to as buckwheats. The plant is characterized by its erect, herbaceous stems and lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color.

Polygonum hassanabdalicum is often found in moist environments such as riverbanks and wetlands.
