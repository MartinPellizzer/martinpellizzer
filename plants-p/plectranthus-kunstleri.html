Plectranthus kunstleri

Plectranthus kunstleri, commonly known as the Purple Heart plant, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically purple or reddish in color, with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in clusters, usually in shades of purple or pink, which bloom in summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its compact growth habit and striking foliage make it an attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
