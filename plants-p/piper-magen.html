Piper magen

Piper magen, commonly known as the Madagascar pepper or Malagasy pepper, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant produces small, green berries that mature to a bright red color, containing a spicy, peppery flavor. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly glossy texture.

Piper magen is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of natural spices.
