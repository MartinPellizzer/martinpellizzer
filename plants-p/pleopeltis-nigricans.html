Pleopeltis nigricans

Pleopeltis nigricans, commonly known as the black-stemmed rock fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its dark, leathery fronds and robust rhizomes. The plant typically grows on rocks or trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are pinnately divided with a distinctive, glossy appearance.

Pleopeltis nigricans is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
