Pelazoneuron patens

Pelazoneuron patens, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a species of flowering plant native to arid regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, reflective hairs that help reduce water loss. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the cooler months. It is often found in rocky soils and can thrive in low-nutrient environments.

Its adaptability to harsh conditions makes it a resilient and interesting subject for botanical study.
