Pomaderris halmaturina subsp. continentis

Pomaderris halmaturina subsp.

continentis, commonly known as the Grey Box, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to southern Australia. It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its distinctive greyish bark and narrow, leathery leaves.
