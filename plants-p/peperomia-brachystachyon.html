Peperomia brachystachyon

Peperomia brachystachyon, commonly known as the short-stalked peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features upright, branching stems covered in dense, velvety foliage that ranges from dark green to reddish-purple. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of green or red, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container cultivation.
