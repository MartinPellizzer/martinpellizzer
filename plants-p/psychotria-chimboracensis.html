Psychotria chimboracensis

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant produces small, dark blue berries that contain the seeds. Due to its restricted habitat, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
