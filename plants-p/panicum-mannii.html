Panicum mannii

Panicum mannii, commonly known as dwarf panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and slender, tufted foliage. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, spikelet-based flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Panicum mannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
