Parashorea densiflora

Parashorea densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered parashorea, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Dipterocarpaceae family and is characterized by its large, buttressed roots and broad, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Parashorea densiflora plays an important ecological role in its native habitats, contributing to forest biodiversity and soil stabilization.
