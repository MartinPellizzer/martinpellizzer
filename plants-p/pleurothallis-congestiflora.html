Pleurothallis congestiflora

Pleurothallis congestiflora, commonly known as the clustered pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pale yellow to greenish-white blooms, often with a slight reddish or purplish tinge. It thrives in high humidity and partial shade, making it a popular choice for terrariums and greenhouse cultivation.

Due to its delicate appearance and specific care requirements, it is considered a challenging but rewarding plant for experienced growers.
