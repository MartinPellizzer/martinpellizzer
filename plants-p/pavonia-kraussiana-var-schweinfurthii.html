Pavonia kraussiana var. schweinfurthii

Pavonia kraussiana var. schweinfurthii, commonly known as the Schweinfurth's wood rose, is a compact shrub native to South Africa.

It features showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in dense clusters during the summer months. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
