Patrinia dielsii var. palustris

Patrinia dielsii var. palustris, commonly known as swamp patrinia, is a flowering plant native to wetland habitats in East Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features slender, hairy stems and small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence.
