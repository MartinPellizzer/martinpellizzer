Panicum chondrachne

Panicum chondrachne, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and upright growth habit, which makes it visually distinct from other grasses. The plant produces fluffy, tan seed heads that mature in the fall, contributing to its ornamental value. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping and erosion control.

Silver bluestem is also valued for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
