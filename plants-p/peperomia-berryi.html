Peperomia berryi

Peperomia berryi, commonly known as the velvetleaf peperomia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Peperomia berryi is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and low maintenance requirements.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
