Perenideboles ciliatum

Perenideboles ciliatum, commonly known as the silky leaf plant, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
