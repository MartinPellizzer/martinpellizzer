Paphiopedilum micranthum var. glanzeanum

Paphiopedilum micranthum var. glanzeanum, commonly known as the Glanzeanum Paphiopedilum, is a rare and exotic orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety petals and a unique, intricate lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid, thriving in shaded, humid environments with well-draining soil. Its flowers, which bloom in late spring or early summer, are prized for their beauty and fragrance.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in specialized horticultural settings.
