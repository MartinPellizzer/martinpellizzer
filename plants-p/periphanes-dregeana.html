Periphanes dregeana

Periphanes dregeana, commonly known as the African orchid or the Zulu orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, often in full sun. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange with a distinctive purple or red lip.

Periphanes dregeana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
