Pelargonium × tibbitsianum

Pelargonium × tibbitsianum, commonly known as the Tibbits' geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The leaves are typically rounded and often have a slightly hairy texture. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant flower colors, which can range from red to pink and white.

This species is well-suited for container gardening and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.
