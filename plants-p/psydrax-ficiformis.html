Psydrax ficiformis

Psydrax ficiformis, commonly known as the giant spider plant, is a tropical climbing plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its long, slender stems and large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces striking yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by dark purple berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its rapid growth and ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
