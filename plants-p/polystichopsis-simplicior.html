Polystichopsis simplicior

Polystichopsis simplicior, commonly known as the simple fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its simple, bipinnate fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in temperate forests. Its reproductive structures include sori that are clustered on the underside of the fronds.

Polystichopsis simplicior is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in forest ecosystems.
