Panicum ignoratum

Panicum ignoratum, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the southeastern United States and thrives in wetland environments. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces dense clusters of small, red or purplish flowers. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Panicum ignoratum is often used in wetland restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for wildlife.
