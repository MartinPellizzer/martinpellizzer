Phillyrea barceloi subsp. moraudii

Phillyrea barceloi subsp.

moraudii, commonly known as the Moraudii Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula. It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, pale green leaves.

The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees. It thrives in rocky soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and horticultural use.
