Psoralea plicifolia

Psoralea plicifolia, commonly known as the pleated psoralea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive pleated leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in racemes, and it thrives in dry, rocky habitats.
