Psychotria minima

Psychotria minima, commonly known as the dwarf psychotria, is a species of flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is a low-growing shrub with small, elliptic leaves and produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. It is often found in humid, shaded environments such as rainforests and undergrowth.
