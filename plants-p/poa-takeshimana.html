Poa takeshimana

Poa takeshimana, commonly known as the Taketsuna grass, is a perennial grass species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, bluish-green leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky slopes. Its inflorescence consists of a single, spike-like cluster of small, spikelet-bearing stems.

Poa takeshimana is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soil in ecologically sensitive areas.
