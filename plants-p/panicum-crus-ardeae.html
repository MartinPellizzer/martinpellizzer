Panicum crus-ardeae

Panicum crus-ardeae, commonly known as the red panicgrass, is a species of grass native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. It thrives in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to a variety of environmental conditions.
