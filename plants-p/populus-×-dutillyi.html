Populus × dutillyi

Populus × dutillyi, commonly known as the Dutilly's poplar, is a hybrid deciduous tree native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the genus Populus, which includes aspen, balsam poplar, and white poplar species. This plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are typically 5–9 inches long and have a serrated edge. It produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers in early spring, followed by winged seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

The tree is often found in moist, well-drained soils along riverbanks and in floodplain forests.
