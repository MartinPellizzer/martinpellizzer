Plectranthus Decurrens

Plectranthus decurrens is a versatile and attractive plant known for its vibrant foliage and ease of care. Often mistaken for a succulent due to its fleshy leaves, it thrives in well-draining soil and bright indirect light. This plant is popular among indoor gardeners for its ability to adapt to various environments. Its leaves come in a range of colors, from deep green to variegated patterns, making it a striking addition to any space. With proper care, Plectranthus decurrens can flourish and bring a touch of nature indoors.
