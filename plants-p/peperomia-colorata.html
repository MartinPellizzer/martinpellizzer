Peperomia colorata

Peperomia colorata, commonly known as the Rosy Glow Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, glossy, ovate leaves that are typically a deep green with a reddish or pinkish hue on the underside, giving it a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, usually white or greenish, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it an excellent addition to shaded or low-light environments.
