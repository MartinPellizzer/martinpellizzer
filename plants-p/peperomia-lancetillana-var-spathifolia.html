Peperomia lancetillana var. spathifolia

Peperomia lancetillana var. spathifolia, commonly known as the Spatulate Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate to spatulate-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of green or red, which are not particularly showy but contribute to its ornamental value. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia lancetillana var. spathifolia is a low-maintenance plant suitable for indoor cultivation.
