Pfaffia lanata var. discolor

Pfaffia lanata var. discolor, commonly known as the yellow partridge pea, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in dry or sandy soils. It produces small, legume-like pods that contain several seeds.
