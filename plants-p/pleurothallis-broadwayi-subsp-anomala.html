Pleurothallis broadwayi subsp. anomala

Pleurothallis broadwayi subsp.

anomala, commonly known as the Broadway's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central America. It is a small epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees in humid, shaded environments.

The plant features slender, wiry stems and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. Its leaves are usually narrow and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
