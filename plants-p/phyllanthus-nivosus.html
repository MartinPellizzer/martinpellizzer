Phyllanthus nivosus

Phyllanthus nivosus, commonly known as the snowflake plant, is a small, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded foliage and small, white or pale green flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its attractive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight.

Phyllanthus nivosus is also known for its ability to tolerate drought conditions once established.
