Peperomia glabella var. eustatiana

Peperomia glabella var. eustatiana, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance houseplant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, velvety, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish or purple tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are rarely seen, making it more appreciated for its foliage than its blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique leaf texture and adaptability to indoor conditions make it a popular choice for plant enthusiasts.
