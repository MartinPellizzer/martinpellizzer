Patrinia scabiosifolia var. crassa

Patrinia scabiosifolia var. crassa, commonly known as roughleaf patrinia, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features upright stems covered in coarse, hairy foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and traditional medicinal uses.
