Paleya albida

Paleya albida, commonly known as the white paleya, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.
