Pittosporum elegans

Pittosporum elegans, commonly known as the silver tree, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are silver-gray on the upper surface and dark green underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Pittosporum elegans is also valued for its ability to tolerate coastal conditions and its role in ecological restoration projects.
