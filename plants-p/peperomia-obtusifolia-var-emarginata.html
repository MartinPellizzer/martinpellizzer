Peperomia obtusifolia var. emarginata

Peperomia obtusifolia var. emarginata, commonly known as the margined peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with prominent white or silver veins, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its slow growth and ease of care make it an ideal plant for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
