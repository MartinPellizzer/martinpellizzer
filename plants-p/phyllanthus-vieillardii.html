Phyllanthus vieillardii

Phyllanthus vieillardii, commonly known as the African date palm, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are often consumed by both humans and animals. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or secondary vegetation.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological and agricultural contexts.
