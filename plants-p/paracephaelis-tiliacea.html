Paracephaelis tiliacea

Paracephaelis tiliacea, commonly known as the Tiliaceae plant, is a species belonging to the Tiliaceae family, which includes flowering plants.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate or elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, often with five petals. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions and thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

The plant is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and floral display.
