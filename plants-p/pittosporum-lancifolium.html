Pittosporum lancifolium

Pittosporum lancifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features elliptical, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, with a distinctively narrow and pointed appearance. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers in clusters, which are followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

Pittosporum lancifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and floral display.
