Primula × jiraseckiana

Primula × jiraseckiana, commonly known as the hybrid primrose, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, white, and purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 15–30 cm and features lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture.

It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and horticultural collections.
