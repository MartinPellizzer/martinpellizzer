Piper nigrescens

Piper nigrescens, commonly known as black pepper vine, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, green berries that turn black when ripe, giving it its common name. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly peppery scent.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
