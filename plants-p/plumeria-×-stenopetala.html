Plumeria × stenopetala

Plumeria × stenopetala, commonly known as the frangipani hybrid, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant has simple, lance-shaped leaves and produces fruit that contains fibrous, inedible seeds. It thrives in warm, sunny climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The hybrid is a result of crossbreeding between Plumeria rubra and Plumeria alba.
