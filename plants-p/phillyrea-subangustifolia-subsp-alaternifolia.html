Phillyrea subangustifolia subsp. alaternifolia

Phillyrea subangustifolia subsp.

alaternifolia, commonly known as the alaternifolia Phillyrea, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and slightly glossy.

The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, berry-like fruits. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor soils.

This subspecies is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and drought tolerance.
