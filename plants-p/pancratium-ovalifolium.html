Pancratium ovalifolium

Pancratium ovalifolium, commonly known as the yellow false lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and low maintenance requirements.
