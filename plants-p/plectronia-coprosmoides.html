Plectronia coprosmoides

Plectronia coprosmoides, commonly known as the New Zealand coral tree, is a small evergreen shrub native to New Zealand.

It features striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. The plant has compound leaves with lance-shaped leaflets, and its bark is smooth and reddish-brown. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Plectronia coprosmoides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in temperate climates.
