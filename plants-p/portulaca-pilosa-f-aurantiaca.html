Portulaca pilosa f. aurantiaca

Portulaca pilosa f. aurantiaca, commonly known as the golden moss rose, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent perennial that features fleshy, hairy stems and leaves. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its compact growth habit and colorful blooms make it an attractive addition to dry garden environments.
