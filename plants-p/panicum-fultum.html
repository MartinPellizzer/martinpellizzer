Panicum fultum

Panicum fultum, commonly known as purple lovegrass, is a flowering plant native to the United States.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and produces showy, purple to violet plumes that attract pollinators. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping. Its foliage is typically narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Panicum fultum is often used in ornamental landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
