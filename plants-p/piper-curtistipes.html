Piper curtistipes

Piper curtistipes, commonly known as the slender pepper vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by berries that turn red when mature. Its leaves are typically ovate with a glossy surface and are often used in traditional medicine.

The plant is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
