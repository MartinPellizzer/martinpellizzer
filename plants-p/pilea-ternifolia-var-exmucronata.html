Pilea ternifolia var. exmucronata

Pilea ternifolia var. exmucronata, commonly known as the Shingle Plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features dense clusters of small, oval-shaped leaves that are dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
