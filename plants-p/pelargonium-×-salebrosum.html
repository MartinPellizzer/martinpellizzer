Pelargonium × salebrosum

Pelargonium × salebrosum, commonly known as the scented geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its fragrant, aromatic leaves that emit various scents depending on the variety. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with rounded, lobed leaves and clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, or white. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant fragrance.

Pelargonium × salebrosum is also used in aromatherapy and as a natural pest repellent due to its strong scent.
