Protea poggei subsp. mwinilungensis

Protea poggei subsp.

mwinilungensis, commonly known as the Mwinilungensis protea, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa. It belongs to the Proteaceae family, characterized by its showy, inflorescence-like flower heads.

The plant features dense, needle-like leaves that are typically covered in a fine, white pubescence. Its floral display consists of numerous small, white to cream-colored flowers arranged in a rounded head.

This subspecies is particularly valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
