Plectranthus japonicus var. glaucocalyx

Plectranthus japonicus var. glaucocalyx, commonly known as the silverleaf plectranthus, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It features lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive silver-gray underside, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale purple or blue, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and easy care requirements.
