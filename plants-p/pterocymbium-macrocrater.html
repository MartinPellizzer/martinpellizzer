Pterocymbium macrocrater

Pterocymbium macrocrater, commonly known as the giant air plant, is a unique epiphytic species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fleshy, and often colorful leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant thrives in humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth. Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental purposes in both indoor and outdoor settings.

Due to its specific care requirements, it is not commonly found in the wild outside of its native habitat.
