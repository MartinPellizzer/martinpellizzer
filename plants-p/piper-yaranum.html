Piper yaranum

Piper yaranum, commonly known as the Yaranum pepper or Yaranum vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many pepper species. The plant produces small, elongated fruits that are often used in traditional medicine and cuisine. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a glossy surface and a distinct aromatic scent.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
