Peperomia ottoniana var. boliviensis

Peperomia ottoniana var. boliviensis, commonly known as the Bolivian peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Andean regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white and green, which are borne on short, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a desirable ornamental plant for both collectors and home gardeners.
