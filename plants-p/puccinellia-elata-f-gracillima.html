Puccinellia elata f. gracillima

Puccinellia elata f. gracillima, commonly known as slender spikegrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The inflorescence consists of spikelets that are loosely arranged, with small, two-ranked flowers. This plant is typically found in sandy or saline soils, often in coastal or inland dune environments.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms.
