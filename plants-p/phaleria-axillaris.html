Phaleria axillaris

Phaleria axillaris, commonly known as the tropical evergreen tree, is a member of the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by oval, fleshy fruits containing aromatic seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The leaves are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
