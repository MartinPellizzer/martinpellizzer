Paeonia × suffruticosa var. qiui

Paeonia × suffruticosa var. qiui, commonly known as the Chinese peony or the tree peony, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its thick, woody stems and large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, white, and yellow. The plant produces compound, pinnately arranged leaves that are dark green and velvety in texture. It blooms in late spring to early summer, and its flowers often have a subtle fragrance.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated in gardens and parks.
