Pteris × Nayariana

Pteris × nayariana is a unique hybrid fern known for its striking, feathery fronds. This plant is a cross between Pteris cretica and Pteris multifida, resulting in a blend of characteristics from both parents. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is popular among indoor gardeners. Its elegant appearance makes it a favored choice for both decorative and ornamental purposes. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to watering and light conditions to flourish.
