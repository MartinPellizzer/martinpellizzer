Peperomia emarginella var. ovalis

Peperomia emarginella var. ovalis, commonly known as the oval-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features oval-shaped, waxy, and slightly hairy leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish or purple underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is often used as a decorative plant in both indoor and outdoor settings.
