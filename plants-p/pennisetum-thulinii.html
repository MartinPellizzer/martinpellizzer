Pennisetum thulinii

Pennisetum thulinii, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender stalks that produce dense, fluffy plumes of purple or pink flowers, which are highly ornamental. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adding visual interest to landscapes. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Pennisetum thulinii is often used in containers or as a border plant due to its striking appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
