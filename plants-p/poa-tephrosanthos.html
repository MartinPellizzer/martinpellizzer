Poa tephrosanthos

Poa tephrosanthos, commonly known as the silver hair grass, is a species of grass native to southern South America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in dense spikelets.

Poa tephrosanthos is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
