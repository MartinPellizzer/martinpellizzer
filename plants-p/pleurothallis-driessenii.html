Pleurothallis driessenii

Pleurothallis driessenii, commonly known as the Driessen's pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil. Its foliage consists of narrow, leathery leaves that help it conserve moisture.

This orchid is often cultivated by enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
