Ptyas disticha

Ptyas disticha, commonly known as the double-flowered ptyas, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Ptyas disticha is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
