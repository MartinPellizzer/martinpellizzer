Pulicaria vieraeoides

Pulicaria vieraeoides, commonly known as the Cape mountain sandwort, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that thrives in rocky and sandy soils. The plant features small, green, succulent leaves and produces yellow flowers in spring. It is well adapted to arid environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Pulicaria vieraeoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
