Panicum chusqueoides

Panicum chusqueoides, commonly known as the slender panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in wetland habitats, including marshes and along the edges of ponds. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It is characterized by its ability to thrive in moist conditions and its adaptability to various soil types.

The species is often used in wetland restoration projects due to its ecological benefits.
