Proteroceras holttumii

Proteroceras holttumii, commonly known as the Holttum's orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, showy flowers with intricate patterns. It typically thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
