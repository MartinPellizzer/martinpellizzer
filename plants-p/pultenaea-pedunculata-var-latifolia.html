Pultenaea pedunculata var. latifolia

Pultenaea pedunculata var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved pultenaea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically broader than the standard variety, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in late winter to early spring, which are arranged in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
