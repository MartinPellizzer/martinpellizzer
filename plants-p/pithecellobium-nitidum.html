Pithecellobium nitidum

Pithecellobium nitidum, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers, which are followed by elongated, pod-like fruits containing edible seeds. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a velvety texture, contributing to its common name.
