Pilea selbyanorum

Pilea selbyanorum, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to southern China and is characterized by its round, glossy leaves that resemble small coins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by greenish berries. It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.

Pilea selbyanorum prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
