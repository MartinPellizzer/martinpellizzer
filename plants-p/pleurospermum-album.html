Pleurospermum album

Pleurospermum album, commonly known as white pleurospermum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features silver-gray foliage and produces showy white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Pleurospermum album is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy perennial in suitable climates.
