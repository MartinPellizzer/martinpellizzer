Polyscias richardsiae

Polyscias richardsiae, commonly known as the richardsia or the evergreen hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, followed by bright red berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Polyscias richardsiae is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor environments.
