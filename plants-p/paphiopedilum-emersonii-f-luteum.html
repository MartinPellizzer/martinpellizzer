Paphiopedilum emersonii f. luteum

Paphiopedilum emersonii f. luteum, commonly known as the yellow lady's slipper orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the high elevations of southern China.

It is characterized by its unique, pouch-like flower structure, which is a defining feature of the Paphiopedilum genus. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers with intricate patterns, making it highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts. It thrives in cool, humid environments with well-draining soil and partial shade.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a conservation concern in its native range.
