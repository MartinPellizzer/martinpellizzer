Pennisetum natalense

Pennisetum natalense, commonly known as Natal grass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically pale green or yellowish in hue. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping and erosion control.

Pennisetum natalense is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract wildlife.
