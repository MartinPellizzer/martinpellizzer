Pilea swartzii

Pilea swartzii, commonly known as the African velvet plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features dense, dark green, velvety leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and ease of care contribute to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
