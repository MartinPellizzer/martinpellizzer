Pachyphytum rogeliocardenasii

Pachyphytum rogeliocardenasii, commonly known as the Cardenasii Barrel Cactus, is a compact, barrel-shaped cactus native to Mexico.

It features a thick, ribbed stem that stores water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant has sparse, spiny foliage and produces small, yellow flowers during the spring. It is well adapted to full sun and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
