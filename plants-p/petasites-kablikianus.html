Petasites kablikianus

Petasites kablikianus, commonly known as the giant cow parsley, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

Petasites kablikianus is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
