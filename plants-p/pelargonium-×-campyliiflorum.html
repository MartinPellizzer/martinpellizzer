Pelargonium × campyliiflorum

Pelargonium × campyliiflorum, commonly known as the Campyliiflorum geranium, is a hybrid perennial plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a contrasting central stripe. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–30 cm and features heart-shaped, serrated leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Its ornamental value is further enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
