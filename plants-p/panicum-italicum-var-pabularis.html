Panicum italicum var. pabularis

Panicum italicum var. pabularis, commonly known as Italian grass or red panicum, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a perennial grass characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, tufted foliage. The plant produces fluffy, pinkish to reddish inflorescences that are often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and as a border plant.

Its botanical features include narrow, grass-like leaves and a compact growth form that adds visual interest to landscapes.
