Peperomia erythropremna

Peperomia erythropremna, commonly known as the red-veined peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features broad, waxy leaves with striking red veins that contrast beautifully with its dark green foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom during the warmer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia erythropremna is prized for its ornamental value and air-purifying qualities.
