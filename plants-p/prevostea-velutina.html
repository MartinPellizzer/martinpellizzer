Prevostea velutina

Prevostea velutina, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of the Americas. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a velvety texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color.

Prevostea velutina is often found in moist, shaded environments and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
