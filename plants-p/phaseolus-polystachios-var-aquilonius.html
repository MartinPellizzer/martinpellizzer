Phaseolus polystachios var. aquilonius

Phaseolus polystachios var. aquilonius, commonly known as the Mexican runner bean, is a climbing legume native to Central America.

It produces vibrant green, pod-like fruits that mature to a striking red or orange color. The plant features compound leaves with three leaflets and is often cultivated for its edible beans. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for both its ornamental appeal and culinary use.
