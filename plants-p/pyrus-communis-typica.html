Pyrus communis typica

Pyrus communis typica, commonly known as the common pear.

It is a deciduous tree native to Europe and parts of western Asia. The plant features broad, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a serrated margin. Its flowers are white and clustered in panicles, blooming in early spring.

The fruit is a pear-shaped berry that ripens in late summer, typically turning yellow or brown depending on the variety.
