Panicum oxyanthum

Panicum oxyanthum, commonly known as the silverleaf panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which results from dense pubescence covering the leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or disturbed areas.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
