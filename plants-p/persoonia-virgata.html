Persoonia virgata

Persoonia virgata, commonly known as the silverleaf pear.

It is a flowering plant native to Australia, characterized by its small, leathery leaves and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically yellow or red when mature. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Persoonia virgata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
