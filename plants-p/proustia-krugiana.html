Proustia krugiana

Proustia krugiana, commonly known as the Krug's proustia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
