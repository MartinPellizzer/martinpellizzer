Phlomis × commixta

Phlomis × commixta, commonly known as the hybrid blanketflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, branched stems and showy, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Phlomis × commixta is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
