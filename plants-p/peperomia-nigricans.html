Peperomia nigricans

Peperomia nigricans, commonly known as the Black Nightshade Peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are often tinged with purple or reddish hues, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, followed by small, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia nigricans is valued for its ornamental foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
