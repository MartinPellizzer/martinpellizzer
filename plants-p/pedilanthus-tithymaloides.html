Pedilanthus tithymaloides

Pedilanthus tithymaloides, commonly known as the sacred lotus of the Amazon, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a shrub that produces striking, showy flowers with bright yellow petals and a red calyx. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and is often found in humid, shaded environments. It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is known for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
