Paphiopedilum malipoense var. malipoense

Paphiopedilum malipoense var. malipoense, commonly known as the Malipo butterfly orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It is characterized by its unique, butterfly-shaped flowers that are typically yellow with deep red veins. The plant is a terrestrial orchid with thick, fleshy roots adapted to its mountainous habitat. It thrives in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat threats, it is considered vulnerable and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
