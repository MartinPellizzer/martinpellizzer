Pimelea argentea

Pimelea argentea, commonly known as silverleaf waratah, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces striking, red or orange flowers in late summer to autumn. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and coastal areas.

Pimelea argentea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
