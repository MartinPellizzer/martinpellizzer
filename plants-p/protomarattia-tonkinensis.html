Protomarattia tonkinensis

Protomarattia tonkinensis, commonly known as the Tonkin fern, is a member of the Marattiales order and is characterized by its large, compound fronds.

It is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam and southern China. The plant features a short, unbranched rhizome with a thick, fibrous covering. Its fronds are pinnate, with numerous pinnae that are often curved and have a leathery texture.

Protomarattia tonkinensis is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
