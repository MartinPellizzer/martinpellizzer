Physocarpus opulifolius f. atropurpureus

Physocarpus opulifolius f. atropurpureus, commonly known as smokebush, is a deciduous shrub native to North America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy panicles of small, pinkish-white flowers that appear in late summer. The foliage is typically bronze to purple in color, providing striking visual interest throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is adaptable to a variety of soil types.

This plant is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and easy maintenance.
