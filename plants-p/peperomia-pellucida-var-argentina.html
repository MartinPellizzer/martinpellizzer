Peperomia pellucida var. argentina

Peperomia pellucida var. argentina, commonly known as the Argentine pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features rounded, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating, giving them a waxy appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically borne in clusters, adding a subtle ornamental touch. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a versatile and attractive addition to any collection.
