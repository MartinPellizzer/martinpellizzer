Potentilla × stiriaca

Potentilla × stiriaca, commonly known as the shrub cinquefoil, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically reaches heights of 1 to 2 feet and features dark green, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
