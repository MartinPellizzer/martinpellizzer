Piper oerstedii

Piper oerstedii, commonly known as the Oersted's pepper, is a tropical climbing vine native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by bright red berries that are edible. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly peppery scent when crushed.

Piper oerstedii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm climates for its vibrant foliage and fruit.
