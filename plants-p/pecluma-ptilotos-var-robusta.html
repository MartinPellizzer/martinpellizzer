Pecluma ptilotos var. robusta

Pecluma ptilotos var. robusta, commonly known as the robust pecluma, is a variant of the pecluma plant native to parts of Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, sprawling growth habit and hairy stems. The leaves are typically broad and oval, with a rough texture and a distinct serrated edge. It produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by oval, woody fruits.

This plant is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
