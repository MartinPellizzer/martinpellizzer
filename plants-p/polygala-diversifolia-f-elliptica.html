Polygala diversifolia f. elliptica

Polygala diversifolia f. elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic milkvetch, is a rare and distinctive plant species belonging to the Polygalaceae family.

It is characterized by its elliptical, lanceolate leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in arid or semi-arid environments. It is native to parts of South Africa, specifically the Western Cape region.
