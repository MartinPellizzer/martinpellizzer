Polypodiastrum molle

Polypodiastrum molle, commonly known as the velvet fern, is a unique plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, mat-forming fern that thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant is characterized by its soft, velvety fronds that are covered in fine, silky hairs. It prefers full sun and is drought-tolerant once established.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
