Potentilla verna f. stenopetala

Potentilla verna f. stenopetala, commonly known as the narrow-petaled shrub cinquefoil, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a compact, low-growing perennial shrub characterized by its small, upright growth habit and dense foliage. The leaves are typically trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets, and the flowers are yellow, five-petaled, and appear in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in alpine and rocky habitats.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native gardens.
