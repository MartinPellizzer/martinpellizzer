Phalaenopsis fasciata f. flava

Phalaenopsis fasciata f. flava, commonly known as the yellow-streaked orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking white petals with prominent yellow streaks and a yellow lip, making it visually distinct. The plant is an epiphyte, typically growing on trees in humid tropical environments. It produces long, pendulous inflorescences with numerous flowers that bloom in clusters.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
