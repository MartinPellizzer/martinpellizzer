Pycnocoma rigidifolia

Pycnocoma rigidifolia, commonly known as the rigid-leaved pycnocoma, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The flowers are usually small and clustered, contributing to its ornamental value in some horticultural settings.
