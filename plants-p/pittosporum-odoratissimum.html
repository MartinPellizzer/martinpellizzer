Pittosporum odoratissimum

Pittosporum odoratissimum, commonly known as the sweet pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage, which is dark green and glossy. The plant produces fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Its small, elliptical leaves are arranged alternately along the stems.

Pittosporum odoratissimum is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and pleasant scent.
