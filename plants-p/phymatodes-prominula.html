Phymatodes prominula

Phymatodes prominula, commonly known as the desert dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by small, oval-shaped fruits that contain seeds with silky hairs for wind dispersal.
