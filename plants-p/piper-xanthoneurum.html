Piper xanthoneurum

Piper xanthoneurum, commonly known as the yellow-veined pepper, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves with yellow veins, giving it its common name. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in spikes.

The fruit is a small, smooth, and yellowish berry that ripens to a bright orange color.
