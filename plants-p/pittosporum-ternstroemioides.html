Pittosporum ternstroemioides

Pittosporum ternstroemioides, commonly known as the New Zealand pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically yellow or red when mature. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

Pittosporum ternstroemioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native ecosystems.
