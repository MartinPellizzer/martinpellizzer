Pelargonium × oblatum

Pelargonium × oblatum, commonly known as the African marble plant, is a species of flowering plant in the geraniaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green markings. It produces showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically blooming in spring and summer.

Pelargonium × oblatum is well-suited for container gardening and is valued for its ornamental appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
