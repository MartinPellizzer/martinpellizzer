Periploca hastata

Periploca hastata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved periploca, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features small, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a distinctive narrowing at the tip. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, often in the spring. Its fruit is a small, round capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Periploca hastata is adapted to grow in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.
