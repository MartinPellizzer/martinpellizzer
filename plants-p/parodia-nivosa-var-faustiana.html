Parodia nivosa var. faustiana

Parodia nivosa var. faustiana, commonly known as the snowflake cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to southern Africa.

It features a pale blue to white body with a fine, powdery coating that gives it a frost-like appearance. The plant has short, cylindrical spines and produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for succulent collections.
