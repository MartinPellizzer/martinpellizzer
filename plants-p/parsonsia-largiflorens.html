Parsonsia largiflorens

Parsonsia largiflorens, commonly known as the large-flowered parsonsia, is a tropical shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a red or purple throat. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are edible for some bird species. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant blooms and unique foliage structure.
