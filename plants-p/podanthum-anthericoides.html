Podanthum anthericoides

Podanthum anthericoides, commonly known as the silver leaf plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that bloom in late summer. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant foliage and ability to attract pollinators.
