Pediocactus spinosior var. schlesseri

Pediocactus spinosior var. schlesseri, commonly known as the Schlesser's spiny cactus, is a rare and striking member of the Cactaceae family native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are covered in dense, spiny areoles, giving it a rugged appearance. The plant produces clusters of vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and early summer, adding a striking contrast to its spiny form. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for xerophytic environments.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
