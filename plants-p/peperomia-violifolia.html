Peperomia violifolia

Peperomia violifolia, commonly known as violet leaf peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a bluish or purplish tint, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, violet-colored flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia violifolia is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
