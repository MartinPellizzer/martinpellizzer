Portulaca grandiflora var. aurantiaca

Portulaca grandiflora var. aurantiaca, commonly known as the golden moss rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and vibrant, bright orange flowers that bloom throughout the summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers. Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it easy to care for, while its showy blooms add a splash of color to gardens.

The variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of climates.
