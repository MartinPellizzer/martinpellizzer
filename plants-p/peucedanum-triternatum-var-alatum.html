Peucedanum triternatum var. alatum

Peucedanum triternatum var. alatum, commonly known as the winged cow parsley, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, fern-like leaves that are finely divided and have a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in umbels, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges and meadows.
