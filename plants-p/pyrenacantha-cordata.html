Pyrenacantha cordata

Pyrenacantha cordata, commonly known as the African hibiscus, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, often with a yellow throat. The plant has dark green, ovate leaves that are often slightly hairy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to produce flowers throughout the growing season.
