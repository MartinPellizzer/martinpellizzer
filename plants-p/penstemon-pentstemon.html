Penstemon pentstemon

Penstemon pentstemon, commonly known as the tall beardtongue, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple, typically blooming in summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its drought tolerance.

Penstemon pentstemon is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
