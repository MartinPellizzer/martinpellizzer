Peperomia nizaitoensis

Peperomia nizaitoensis, commonly known as the Nizaito peperomia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Japan, specifically the island of Shikoku. This plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tint. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellowish in color.

Peperomia nizaitoensis is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive foliage.
