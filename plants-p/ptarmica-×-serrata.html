Ptarmica × serrata

Ptarmica × serrata, commonly known as the hybrid between ptarmica and serrata, is a hybrid plant that exhibits characteristics of both parent species.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming plant with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often serrated along the edges. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish or white in color. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Due to its hybrid nature, it can exhibit a wide range of morphological variation.
