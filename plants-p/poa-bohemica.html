Poa bohemica

Poa bohemica, commonly known as Bohemian grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial grass that typically forms dense, mat-like growths. Native to Europe, it thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

Its botanical features make it an important component of ecologically rich habitats.
