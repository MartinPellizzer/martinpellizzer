Philodendron micans

Philodendron micans, commonly known as the velvet leaf philodendron, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves that often have a metallic sheen under light. The plant produces small, pale green flowers that are typically hidden among its foliage. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Philodendron micans is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
