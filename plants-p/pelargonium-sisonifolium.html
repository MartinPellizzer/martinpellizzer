Pelargonium sisonifolium

Pelargonium sisonifolium, commonly known as the Sison's geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage and showy, pink to red flowers. The leaves are typically lobed or serrated, and the plant often exhibits a compact, bushy growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated for both its foliage and floral display.
