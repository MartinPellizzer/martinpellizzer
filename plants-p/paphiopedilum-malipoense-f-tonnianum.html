Paphiopedilum malipoense f. tonnianum

Paphiopedilum malipoense f. tonnianum, commonly known as the Tonnian Paphiopedilum, is a rare and highly sought-after orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure. The plant features a thick, fleshy rhizome and produces showy, long-lasting flowers with a distinctive lip. Its leaves are typically dark green and leathery, adapted to thrive in shaded, humid environments.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in greenhouses and is considered a conservation concern in its natural habitat.
