Pyrrosia linearis

Pyrrosia linearis, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Asia.

It is characterized by its long, linear, and sword-shaped fronds that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a leathery texture.

Pyrrosia linearis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ease of care.
