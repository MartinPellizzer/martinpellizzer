Paphiopedilum glanduliferum var. kimballianum

Paphiopedilum glanduliferum var. kimballianum, commonly known as the Kimball's Boat Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome and is characterized by its fleshy, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, lip-shaped flowers that are typically pale yellow to orange with purple or red markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
