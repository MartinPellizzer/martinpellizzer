Phasellus lathyroides

Phasellus lathyroides, commonly known as the creeping bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky soils and dry habitats, exhibiting a low, sprawling growth habit. The plant features small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to violet, often with a white throat. Its leaves are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its drought tolerance.

Phasellus lathyroides is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging environments.
