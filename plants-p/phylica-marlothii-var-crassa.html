Phylica marlothii var. crassa

Phylica marlothii var. crassa, commonly known as the thick-leafed flame bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves that are often a deep green color. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid conditions and requires minimal water once established.

This species is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
