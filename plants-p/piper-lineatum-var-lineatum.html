Piper lineatum var. lineatum

Piper lineatum var. lineatum, commonly known as the lineated pepper plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves with prominent veins that run parallel to the leaf edges. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically green or white in color. Its fruit, a small, round berry, turns from green to red when mature.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid climates.
