Pleurothallis onagriceps

Pleurothallis onagriceps, commonly known as the silver-veined orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces intricate, fragrant flowers with white and pink petals. Its inflorescence typically consists of several small blooms arranged along a curved stem.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
