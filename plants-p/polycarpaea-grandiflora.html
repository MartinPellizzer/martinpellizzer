Polycarpaea grandiflora

Polycarpaea grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered polycarpaea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant produces multiple flowers along its stems, which are often clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or grassland habitats.

Polycarpaea grandiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
