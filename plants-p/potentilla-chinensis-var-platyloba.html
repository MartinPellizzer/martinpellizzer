Potentilla chinensis var. platyloba

Potentilla chinensis var. platyloba, commonly known as the flatleaf cinquefoil, is a perennial shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching stems that bear small, yellow flowers clustered in groups of five. The leaves are typically oval-shaped with a smooth margin and a slightly hairy surface. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value comes from its delicate flowers and compact growth habit.
