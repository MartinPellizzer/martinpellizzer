Prunus sativa proles elegans

Prunus sativa proles elegans, commonly known as the elegant cherry, is a cultivar of the Prunus genus that exhibits striking ornamental features.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and clusters of white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, sweet-tart berries that are edible and often used in culinary applications. Its leaves are dark green and provide attractive foliage throughout the growing season.

This variety is well-suited for ornamental gardens and can thrive in temperate climates with well-drained soil.
