Pyrethrum pallidum subsp. virescens

Pyrethrum pallidum subsp.

virescens, commonly known as the greenleaf pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers.

The plant is known for its high concentration of pyrethrins, which are natural insecticides. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a distinctive silvery-green appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental and pest-control gardening.
