Perityle deltoidea

Perityle deltoidea, commonly known as the delta leaf perityle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its deltoid-shaped leaves, which are typically broad and heart-shaped at the base. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Perityle deltoidea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
