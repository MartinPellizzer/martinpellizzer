Phillyrea olleri

Phillyrea olleri, commonly known as the Canary Island Phillyrea, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features dense, leathery leaves that are dark green and glossy, often with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that ripens to a red or orange color.

Phillyrea olleri is well adapted to dry, rocky environments and is often found in coastal areas.
