Polygonum × intercedens

Polygonum × intercedens, commonly known as the hybrid smartweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its erect stems and alternate leaves that are often reddish or purplish in color. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist to wet environments, such as marshes and along stream banks.

Polygonum × intercedens is often used in wetland restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for various wildlife species.
