Philodendron mesae

Philodendron mesae, commonly known as the Mesae Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy green leaves that are typically a deep emerald color. The plant has a vining growth habit and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by reddish-brown berries. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a houseplant.
