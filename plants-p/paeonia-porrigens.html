Paeonia porrigens

Paeonia porrigens, commonly known as the Chinese peony, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Paeoniaceae and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant features compound leaves with a serrated margin and produces red or pink flowers with numerous petals. It is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been cultivated for centuries in traditional Chinese gardens.

Paeonia porrigens is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
