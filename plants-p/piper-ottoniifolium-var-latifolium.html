Piper ottoniifolium var. latifolium

Piper ottoniifolium var. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf pepper plant, is a tropical vine native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, ovate leaves that can reach up to 20 cm in length. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by elongated, cylindrical berries that turn red when mature. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruits.

The leaves are also used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
