Pennisetum benthamii

Pennisetum benthamii, commonly known as the bentham lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, pale green to brownish flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping and erosion control.

Pennisetum benthamii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to provide habitat for native wildlife.
