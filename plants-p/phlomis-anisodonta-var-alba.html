Phlomis anisodonta var. alba

Phlomis anisodonta var. alba, commonly known as white betony, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems covered in soft, gray-green foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, showy white or pale pink flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its aromatic leaves and ornamental flowers make it a valued addition to both garden and ecological settings.
