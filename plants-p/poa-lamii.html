Poa lamii

Poa lamii, commonly known as the silver hair grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It produces small, pale green to blue-gray spikelets that are clustered at the ends of its stems.

Poa lamii is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
