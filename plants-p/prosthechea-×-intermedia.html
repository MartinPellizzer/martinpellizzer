Prosthechea × intermedia

Prosthechea × intermedia, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a fascinating plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Prosthechea luehmannii and Prosthechea venosa, showcasing a blend of characteristics from both parent species. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, typically in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with intricate patterns, and it produces small, fleshy pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Prosthechea × intermedia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
