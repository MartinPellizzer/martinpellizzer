Pteris stridens

Pteris stridens, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Pteridaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, bi- or tri-pinnate fronds that are often bright green and covered in a layer of fine, silky hairs. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, Africa, and the Pacific Islands. It prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found growing on rocks or in forest understories.

Pteris stridens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
