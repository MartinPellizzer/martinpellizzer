Proustia pungens var. cuneifolia

Proustia pungens var. cuneifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf proustia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and features striking, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from violet to red, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
