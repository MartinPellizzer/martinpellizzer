Petrosedum × hegnaueri

Petrosedum × hegnaueri, commonly known as the hybrid stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and fleshy, often blue-green leaves. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, typically in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Petrosedum × hegnaueri is also valued for its low maintenance requirements and ability to withstand drought conditions.
