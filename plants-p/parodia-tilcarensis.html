Parodia tilcarensis

Parodia tilcarensis, commonly known as the "Cactus of the Desert," is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are often covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sunlight.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
