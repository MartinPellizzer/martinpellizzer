Pausinystalia zenkeri

Pausinystalia zenkeri, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant has a robust, often multi-trunked structure with thick, woody bark. Its leaves are pinnately compound, consisting of numerous small, oval-shaped leaflets.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often planted in gardens and parks for its striking floral display.
