Peperomia heterophylla var. grandis

Peperomia heterophylla var. grandis, commonly known as the Giant Peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that can vary in shape and size, often with a glossy texture and deep green coloration. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale green, arranged in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for decorative purposes in homes and offices.
