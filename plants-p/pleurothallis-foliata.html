Pleurothallis foliata

Pleurothallis foliata, commonly known as the leafy orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, often greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often epiphytic on trees or rocks. Its foliage is dense and broad, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It requires careful watering and well-draining soil to thrive.
