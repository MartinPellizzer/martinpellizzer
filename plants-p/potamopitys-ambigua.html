Potamopitys ambigua

It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves and conical shape, typical of many pine species. The plant has a unique ecological niche, thriving in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions. Its cones are small and often remain closed for extended periods, a trait that may be an adaptation to its harsh habitat.
