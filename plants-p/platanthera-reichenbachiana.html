Platanthera reichenbachiana

Platanthera reichenbachiana, commonly known as the Reichenbach's lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to the temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It features a slender, grass-like stem with numerous small, pale green to white flowers arranged in a dense, loose spiral. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows, often in association with mycorrhizal fungi. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and it produces a single, elongated inflorescence.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is often found in regions with high humidity and well-drained soil.
