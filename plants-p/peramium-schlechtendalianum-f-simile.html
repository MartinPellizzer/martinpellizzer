Peramium schlechtendalianum f. simile

Peramium schlechtendalianum f. simile, commonly known as the silverleaf peramium, is a rare and striking plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage, which is covered in fine, waxy hairs that give it a unique, almost metallic appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, adding to its ornamental value.
