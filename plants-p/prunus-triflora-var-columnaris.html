Prunus triflora var. columnaris

Prunus triflora var. columnaris, commonly known as the columnar cherry, is a unique variant of the triflora cherry species.

It is characterized by its upright, narrow growth habit, making it ideal for space-efficient landscaping. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in spring, followed by edible berries that are often used in culinary applications. Its foliage turns a vibrant red in autumn, adding ornamental value to gardens.

This variety is relatively low-maintenance and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.
