Piper fenixii

Piper fenixii, commonly known as the Phoenix pepper or Fenixii pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by elliptical, dark purple berries.

The berries are edible and have a mild, peppery flavor, making them a popular ingredient in local cuisines.
