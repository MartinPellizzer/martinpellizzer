Peperomia maleuvreana

Peperomia maleuvreana, commonly known as the maleuvrean peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia maleuvreana is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
