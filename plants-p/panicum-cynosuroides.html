Panicum cynosuroides

Panicum cynosuroides, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense clusters of small, spikelet-like flowers. The plant produces reddish or brownish inflorescences, which are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a common ornamental and forage crop.

Panicum cynosuroides is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in disturbed areas.
