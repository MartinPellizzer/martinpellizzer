Platyaechmea wittmackiana

Platyaechmea wittmackiana, commonly known as the Wittmack's star cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and is covered in dense, white spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
