Piper obumbratum var. parvifolium

Piper obumbratum var. parvifolium, commonly known as the dwarf pepper plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and a dark green coloration. The plant produces tiny, pale yellow flowers that develop into small, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique addition to tropical gardens and indoor plant collections.
