Paronychia Argentea Var. Mauritanica

Paronychia argentea var. mauritanica is a hardy succulent native to arid regions of North Africa. It features dense clusters of silver-blue leaves that reflect sunlight and conserve moisture. This plant is known for its ability to thrive in poor soil and extreme temperatures. Its compact growth habit makes it an ideal choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping. With its striking appearance and low maintenance needs, it's a popular plant among succulent enthusiasts.
