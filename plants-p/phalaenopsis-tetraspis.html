Phalaenopsis tetraspis

Phalaenopsis tetraspis, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender pseudobulbs and linear, dark green leaves. The plant produces striking, long-lasting flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a speckled appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Phalaenopsis tetraspis is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
