Prunus miqueliana

Prunus miqueliana, commonly known as the Miquel cherry, is a species of flowering shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its pink to white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are similar in appearance to cherries but are less commonly consumed by humans. Prunus miqueliana typically grows in woodland areas and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical significance in traditional Chinese horticulture.
