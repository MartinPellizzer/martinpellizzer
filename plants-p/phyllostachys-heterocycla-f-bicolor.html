Phyllostachys heterocycla f. bicolor

Phyllostachys heterocycla f. bicolor, commonly known as the rainbow bamboo, is a striking ornamental grass native to China.

It is characterized by its variegated foliage, featuring a mix of green, yellow, and white stripes along the leaf blades. This bamboo thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil, making it adaptable to a variety of garden conditions. Its slender, upright stems grow in clusters, reaching heights of up to 10 feet, and it is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal.

The plant is also valued for its resilience and ability to withstand mild frosts, making it a popular choice for both ornamental and environmental purposes.
