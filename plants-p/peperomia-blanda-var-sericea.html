Peperomia blanda var. sericea

Peperomia blanda var. sericea, commonly known as silver peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery sheen, giving it a striking appearance. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape and have a velvety texture. This variety is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are rarely seen, making it a popular choice for ornamental purposes.
