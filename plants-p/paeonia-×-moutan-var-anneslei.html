Paeonia × moutan var. anneslei

Paeonia × moutan var. anneslei, commonly known as the Anneslei peony, is a deciduous perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the芍药属 (Paeonia) family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant produces compound, trifoliate leaves that are typically green and heart-shaped. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

The Anneslei peony is also valued for its fragrance and is often cultivated for both its aesthetic and medicinal properties.
