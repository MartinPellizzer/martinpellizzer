Ptarmica lereschii

Ptarmica lereschii, commonly known as the Leresch's wintergreen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming in the spring. Ptarmica lereschii thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.
