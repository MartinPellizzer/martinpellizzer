Placus purpurascens var. glabratum

Placus purpurascens var. glabratum, commonly known as the smooth purple orchid, is a flowering plant characterized by its velvety, purple-pink petals and smooth, glabrous leaves.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often near streams or in dense undergrowth. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are known for their striking coloration and subtle fragrance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
