Potentilla quinquefolia

Potentilla quinquefolia, commonly known as fiveleaf cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, typically with five leaflets, and produces small, yellowish flowers in the summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in open woodlands and rocky areas. It is a perennial shrub that grows to a height of about 1 to 3 feet.

Potentilla quinquefolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
