Prunus jamasakura f. orbicularis

Prunus jamasakura f. orbicularis, commonly known as the Japanese cherry blossom with orbicular leaves, is a cultivar of the Prunus jamasakura species.

It is characterized by its distinctive broad, rounded leaves that give it a unique appearance compared to typical cherry blossoms. The plant produces clusters of pale pink to white flowers in early spring, which are highly ornamental. It is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value and is adaptable to a variety of soil types.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is a popular choice in horticulture.
