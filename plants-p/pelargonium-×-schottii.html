Pelargonium × schottii

Pelargonium × schottii, commonly known as the Zonal Geranium, is a hybrid shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rounded, velvety leaves with distinct color zones, typically in shades of green, silver, and red. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Pelargonium × schottii is widely cultivated as an ornamental plant for gardens and container gardening due to its attractive appearance and adaptability.
