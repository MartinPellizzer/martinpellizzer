Perotis flavinodula

Perotis flavinodula, commonly known as the yellow-veined perotis, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant has slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves with prominent yellow veins. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters, contributing to its ornamental value.
