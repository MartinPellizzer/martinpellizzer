Peperomia abyssinica var. subrotundata

Peperomia abyssinica var. subrotundata, commonly known as the Abyssinian pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Ethiopia.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically rounded or subrotund, with a deep green color and prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it suitable for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
