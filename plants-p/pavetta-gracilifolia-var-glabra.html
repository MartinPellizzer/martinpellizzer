Pavetta gracilifolia var. glabra

Pavetta gracilifolia var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth pavetta, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, glabrous stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in whorls. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its fruits are capsules that release tiny seeds when mature.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and thickets.
