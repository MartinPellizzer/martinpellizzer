Plectranthus pulneyensis

Plectranthus pulneyensis, commonly known as the pulney's plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or blue in color. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.

Plectranthus pulneyensis prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
