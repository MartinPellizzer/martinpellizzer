Pyrrosia fuohaiensis

Pyrrosia fuohaiensis, commonly known as the Fuohai spleenwort, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on tree trunks or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are usually bright green and covered in a fine layer of trichomes. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds in clusters called sori.

Pyrrosia fuohaiensis is native to China and is often found in subtropical and tropical regions with high humidity.
