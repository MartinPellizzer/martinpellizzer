Poterium fruticosum

Poterium fruticosum, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal regions, thriving in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Its leaves are narrow and opposite, with a slightly hairy texture.

Poterium fruticosum is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate coastal conditions.
