Pilea stipulata

Pilea stipulata, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it ideal for indoor cultivation.
