Platonia nodiflora

Platonia nodiflora, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a tropical plant native to Central and South America, characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a soft, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along riverbanks.

Platonia nodiflora is also known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its use in traditional medicine.
