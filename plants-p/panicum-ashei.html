Panicum ashei

Panicum ashei, commonly known as sand dropseed, is a native grass species found primarily in the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-green foliage and delicate, fluffy seed heads that appear in late summer. This plant thrives in well-drained sandy soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for native plant gardens. It is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.

Panicum ashei also plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.
