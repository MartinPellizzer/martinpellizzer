Pelargonium × giganteum

Pelargonium × giganteum, commonly known as the giant geranium, is a hybrid plant that exhibits large, showy flowers and robust growth.

It is native to South Africa and belongs to the Geraniaceae family. The plant is characterized by its broad, lobed leaves and clusters of bright red or pink flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.
