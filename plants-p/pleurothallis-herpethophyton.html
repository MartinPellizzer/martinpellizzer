Pleurothallis herpethophyton

Pleurothallis herpethophyton, commonly known as the Herpeth's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in the understory of dense vegetation. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow in color. It has a pseudobulb structure and fibrous roots, typical of many orchid species.

Due to its habitat specificity and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
