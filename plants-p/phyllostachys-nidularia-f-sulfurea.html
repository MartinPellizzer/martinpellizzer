Phyllostachys nidularia f. sulfurea

Phyllostachys nidularia f. sulfurea, commonly known as the golden bamboo, is a variant of the silver bamboo species.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow shoots and culms, which contrast beautifully with its darker green leaves. This ornamental bamboo is native to China and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and landscapes. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a versatile choice for temperate climates.

Its unique coloration makes it a popular selection for bonsai and decorative planting.
