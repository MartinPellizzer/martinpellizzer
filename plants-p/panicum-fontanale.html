Panicum fontanale

Panicum fontanale, commonly known as fountain grass, is a species of grass native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its tufted, fountain-like growth habit, with slender, wiry stems that form dense clumps. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often adorned with delicate, feathery florets. Its foliage is typically narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that adds a striking visual contrast.

Panicum fontanale is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
