Pleione chinensis

Pleione chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese pleione, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and rhizomatous root system. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with three petals and a distinctive lip, typically in shades of white, pink, or yellow. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
