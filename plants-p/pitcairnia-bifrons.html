Pitcairnia bifrons

Pitcairnia bifrons, commonly known as the twin-leaf pitcairnia, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, Colombia, and Ecuador. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in shaded, humid environments.
