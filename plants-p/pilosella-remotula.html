Pilosella remotula

Pilosella remotula, commonly known as the slender goat's beard, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, which contribute to its common name. Its flowers are yellowish-green, with ray florets that are often obscured by the dense central disc.

Pilosella remotula is typically found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
