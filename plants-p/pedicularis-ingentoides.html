Pedicularis ingentoides

Pedicularis ingentoides, commonly known as the giant monkey orchid, is a striking member of the Orobanchaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including parts of Russia and China. This herbaceous perennial produces tall, showy flowers with bright orange petals and a distinctive spurred labellum. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Its unique morphology and vibrant coloration make it a notable species in botanical studies.
