Penelopeia suburceolata

Penelopeia suburceolata, commonly known as the velvetleaf penelopeia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental foliage, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its unique leaf appearance.
