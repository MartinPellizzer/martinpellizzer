Perezia multicapitata

Perezia multicapitata, commonly known as the clustered perezia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense clusters of small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence.

Perezia multicapitata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
