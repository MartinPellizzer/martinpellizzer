Peperomia armondii

Peperomia armondii, commonly known as the Shiny Leaf Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, glossy green leaves that are oval-shaped with a waxy texture, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia armondii is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
