Polystachya biteaui

Polystachya biteaui, commonly known as the African bamboo orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to tropical Africa.

This plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems that resemble bamboo, often reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, providing a lush green backdrop to its showy inflorescences. The flowers are clustered in dense, raceme-like spikes, featuring vibrant colors such as yellow, orange, and red.

Polystachya biteaui thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
