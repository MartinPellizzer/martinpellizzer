Potentilla × fussii

Potentilla × fussii, commonly known as the Shrub cinquefoil, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow or orange. The plant features small, serrated leaves that are arranged alternately along its stems. It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Potentilla × fussii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy and adaptable choice for landscaping.
