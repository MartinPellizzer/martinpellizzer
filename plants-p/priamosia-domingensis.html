Priamosia domingensis

Priamosia domingensis, commonly known as the Dominican orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the Caribbean island of Hispaniola.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the moist soil and decaying organic matter.

Priamosia domingensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
