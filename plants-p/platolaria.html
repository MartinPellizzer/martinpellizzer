Platolaria

Platolaria, commonly known as the golden star lily, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Platolaria is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a cut flower.
