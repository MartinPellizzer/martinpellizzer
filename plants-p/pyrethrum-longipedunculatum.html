Pyrethrum longipedunculatum

Pyrethrum longipedunculatum, commonly known as the long peduncle pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant produces small, spherical flower heads that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem.

Pyrethrum longipedunculatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
