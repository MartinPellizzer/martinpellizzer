Peperomia subrenifolia

Peperomia subrenifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a subtle reddish or purple hue on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it an ideal choice for indoor cultivation.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular ornamental plant for shaded or low-light environments.
