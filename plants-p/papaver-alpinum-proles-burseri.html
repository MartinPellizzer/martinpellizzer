Papaver alpinum proles burseri

Papaver alpinum proles burseri, commonly known as the Burseri poppy, is a rare and striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red petals and a distinctive, elongated seed pod that persists through winter. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, harsh climates. Its flowers bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
