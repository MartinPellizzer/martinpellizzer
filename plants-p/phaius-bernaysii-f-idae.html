Phaius bernaysii f. idae

Phaius bernaysii f. idae, commonly known as the Idaean phaius, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in late summer or early autumn. The plant features long, arching stems with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often a deep green color. Its flowers are usually pale yellow with a distinctive dark brown or purple lip, adding to its ornamental appeal.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful cultivation to maintain its health and vigor.
