Pycnostachys ballotoides

Pycnostachys ballotoides, commonly known as the ball-shaped pycnostachys, is a unique flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of flowers, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, white to pale pink blooms that are arranged in tight, rounded inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
