Primula × varians

Primula × varians, commonly known as the hybrid primrose, is a popular ornamental plant characterized by its showy, clustered flowers.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Primula vulgaris and Primula obconica, resulting in a wide range of color variations. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves and blooms in shades of white, yellow, pink, and purple. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a versatile choice for both garden beds and container planting.
