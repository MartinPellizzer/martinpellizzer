Plettkea macrophylla

Plettkea macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf plettkea, is a striking member of the Gesneriaceae family native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to orange, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant features broad, glossy leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along its stems. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil to flourish.

Plettkea macrophylla is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
