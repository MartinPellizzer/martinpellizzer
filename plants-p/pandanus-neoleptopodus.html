Pandanus neoleptopodus

Pandanus neoleptopodus, commonly known as the slender-leaf pandanus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into fibrous, spiky fruits. Its roots are sometimes used in local medicine for their purported healing properties.

Overall, it is an important species in both ecological and cultural contexts within its native regions.
