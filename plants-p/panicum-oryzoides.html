Panicum oryzoides

Panicum oryzoides, commonly known as ricegrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and panicles that produce small, spikelet-like flowers. The plant is often found in wetland habitats, including marshes and along waterways. It has a fibrous root system that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

Its seeds are a food source for various birds and small mammals.
