Polygonum acetosum

Polygonum acetosum, commonly known as sourgrass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants known for their distinctive arrow-shaped leaves. The plant is characterized by its slender stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from green to red. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Polygonum acetosum thrives in moist, shady environments and is sometimes cultivated as a ornamental plant.
