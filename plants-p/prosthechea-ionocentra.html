Prosthechea ionocentra

Prosthechea ionocentra, commonly known as the ionocentra orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its erect, leafy stems and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant's leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a waxy coating, which helps in water retention. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The flowers, which are usually pink or white, bloom in late summer and early autumn.
