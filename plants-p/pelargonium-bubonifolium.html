Pelargonium bubonifolium

Pelargonium bubonifolium, commonly known as the sickle-leaf geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its distinctive, sickle-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or red flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant foliage and compact growth habit.
