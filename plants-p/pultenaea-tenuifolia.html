Pultenaea tenuifolia

Pultenaea tenuifolia, commonly known as slender leaf pultenaea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its pod fruits and often nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant typically grows in shrubland and woodland habitats, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and it produces small, yellowish flowers in spring.

Pultenaea tenuifolia is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
