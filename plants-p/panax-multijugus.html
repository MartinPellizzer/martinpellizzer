Panax multijugus

Panax multijugus, commonly known as the Chinese ginseng, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its fleshy, yellowish roots and its distinctive compound leaves with opposite arrangement. The plant belongs to the Araliaceae family and is known for its adaptogenic properties. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often cultivated for its medicinal value.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both traditional and modern pharmacology.
