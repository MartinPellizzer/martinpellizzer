Phyllostegia catenulata

Phyllostegia catenulata, commonly known as the catenulate phyllostegia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, tubular, and typically clustered in whorls along the stems.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas.
