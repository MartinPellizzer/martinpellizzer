Pterostyrax psilophyllus var. discolor

Pterostyrax psilophyllus var. discolor, commonly known as the variegated Chinese pterostyrax, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that exhibit a striking variegation of white and green, adding visual interest to the landscape. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by bright red, capsule-like fruits. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for ornamental gardens.

Its unique foliage and seasonal blooms make it a valuable addition to botanical collections.
