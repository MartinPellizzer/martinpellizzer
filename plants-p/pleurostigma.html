Pleurostigma

Pleurostigma, commonly known as the pleurostigma plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its composite flower heads, which consist of both ray and disk flowers. The leaves are typically alternate and lanceolate, with hairy surfaces. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are often clustered in compact heads.

It is native to various regions in Africa and is often found in dry, open habitats.
