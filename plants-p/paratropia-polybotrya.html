Paratropia polybotrya

Paratropia polybotrya, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, star-shaped flowers that are often brightly colored, with a distinctive central column. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in horticulture. It typically grows as an epiphyte or lithophyte, attaching itself to trees or rocks in its natural habitat.

Its botanical features make it a popular subject for both scientific study and botanical gardens.
