Pelargonium × cavrianum

Pelargonium × cavrianum, commonly known as the hybrid geranium, is a popular ornamental plant characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of climates, making it a versatile choice for gardeners. The plant typically features palmate leaves with lobed edges and produces clusters of flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. Pelargonium × cavrianum is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is used in both container gardening and landscape design.

Its botanical features make it a favorite among those looking to add color and texture to their gardens.
