Pelargonium × rienzii

Pelargonium × rienzii, commonly known as the hybrid geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, brightly colored flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant produces rounded, velvety leaves with a distinctive serrated edge, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and containers.

Native to South Africa, Pelargonium × rienzii thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
