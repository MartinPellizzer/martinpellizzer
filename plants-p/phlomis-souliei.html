Phlomis souliei

Phlomis souliei, commonly known as the Chinese torchflower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its tall, upright stems and showy, pink to purple flower clusters that resemble torches. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a soft texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.
