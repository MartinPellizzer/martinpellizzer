Peperomia poasana var. herediana

Peperomia poasana var. herediana, commonly known as the Herediana Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental foliage, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
