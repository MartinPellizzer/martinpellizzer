Palaquium sericeum

Palaquium sericeum, commonly known as the silky palaquium, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and elliptical leaves with a leathery texture. Palaquium sericeum is often cultivated as a decorative plant in tropical gardens.
