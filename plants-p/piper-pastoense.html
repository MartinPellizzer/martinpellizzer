Piper pastoense

Piper pastoense, commonly known as the Pasto pepper or Pasto pepper plant, is a climbing vine native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by tiny, edible berries. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

Piper pastoense is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
