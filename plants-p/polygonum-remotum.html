Polygonum remotum

Polygonum remotum, commonly known as tall polygonum, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, appearing in late summer. Its leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Polygonum remotum thrives in moist environments such as wetlands and stream banks.
