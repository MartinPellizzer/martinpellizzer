Panicum vannum

Panicum vannum, commonly known as yellow panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and panicles of golden-yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it adaptable to various environmental conditions. Its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes contribute to its success as a weed in agricultural and natural settings.

Panicum vannum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
