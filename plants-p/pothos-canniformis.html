Pothos canniformis

Pothos canniformis, commonly known as the heartleaf pothos, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically bright green and slightly variegated. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments and prefers indirect sunlight. Its trailing growth habit makes it popular as a houseplant or for hanging baskets.

Pothos canniformis is relatively low maintenance, making it an ideal choice for beginners in plant care.
