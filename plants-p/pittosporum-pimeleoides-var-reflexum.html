Pittosporum pimeleoides var. reflexum

Pittosporum pimeleoides var. reflexum, commonly known as the coastal pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are typically narrow, elliptical, and have a leathery texture with a distinct midrib. It thrives in coastal environments, tolerating salt spray and sandy soils.

This variety is often used in coastal landscaping due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
