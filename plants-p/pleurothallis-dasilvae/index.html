Pleurothallis Dasilvae

The pleurothallis dasilvae is a rare orchid species native to the cloud forests of Ecuador. It is known for its delicate, elongated flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to flourish. Due to its limited habitat and threats from deforestation, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and beautiful orchid.
