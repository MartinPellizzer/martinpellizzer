Psoralea tenax

Psoralea tenax, commonly known as the rigid psoralea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its upright, branching habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in racemes, and its leaves are pinnately compound with small, oval leaflets. It is also noted for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
