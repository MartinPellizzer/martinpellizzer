Pleurothallis altoserrana

Pleurothallis altoserrana, commonly known as the high serrated orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant typically produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and green, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, loose substrate for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
