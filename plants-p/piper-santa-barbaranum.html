Piper santa-barbaranum

Piper santa-barbaranum, commonly known as Santa Barbara pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the coastal regions of southern California, particularly around Santa Barbara. The plant is characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its fruit is a small, elongated berry that turns from green to red as it matures.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in warm, coastal climates for its unique appearance.
