Podochilus polytrichoides

Podochilus polytrichoides, commonly known as the slender leaved orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid environments, relying on its aerial roots for absorption of moisture and nutrients. Its flowers, which are usually white or pale yellow, bloom in clusters and attract various pollinators.

Podochilus polytrichoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
