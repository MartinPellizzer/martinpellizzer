Potentilla musartana

Potentilla musartana, commonly known as the alpine potentilla, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are usually narrow and serrated, adapted to thrive in harsh alpine conditions.

Potentilla musartana is often found in rocky soils and is well-suited to high-altitude environments.
