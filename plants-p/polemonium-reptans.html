Polemonium reptans

Polemonium reptans, commonly known as running polemonium, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features upright, branching stems that are often reddish in color and bear clusters of bell-shaped, showy flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, woodland environments and is often found in shaded to partially shaded areas.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
