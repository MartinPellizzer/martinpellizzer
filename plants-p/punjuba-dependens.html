Punjuba dependens

Punjuba dependens, commonly known as the hanging bell plant, is a unique species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its pendulous, bell-shaped flowers that dangle from long, wiry stems. The plant features small, narrow leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a contrasting red or purple base, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Punjuba dependens is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.
