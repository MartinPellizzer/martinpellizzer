Pterostylis diversiflora

Pterostylis diversiflora, commonly known as the variable helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rainforests. The plant has a slender, upright stem with a single or few erect, pale green to yellowish flowers. Its unique floral structure includes a labellum that is often adorned with intricate markings.

Pterostylis diversiflora is known for its specialized pollination mechanism, relying on specific insect species for reproduction.
