Prunella aequinoctialis

Prunella aequinoctialis, commonly known as the equinox plant, is a flowering plant belonging to the mint family, Lamiaceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in moist meadows and woodland edges. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Prunella aequinoctialis is known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
