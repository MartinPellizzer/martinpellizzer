Pulicaria rajputanae

Pulicaria rajputanae, commonly known as the Rajputana groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of northern India.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. Pulicaria rajputanae is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is well-suited to xerophytic environments.
