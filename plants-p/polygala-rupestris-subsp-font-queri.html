Polygala rupestris subsp. font-queri

Polygala rupestris subsp.

font-queri, commonly known as the Font-Queri's milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows in rocky or limestone soils, thriving in dry and sunny environments.

The plant features slender stems and small, usually pink or white flowers arranged in spicate inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to poor soils.
