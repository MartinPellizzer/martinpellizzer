Portulaca poliosperma var. cubensis

Portulaca poliosperma var. cubensis, commonly known as Cuban portulaca, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and vibrant, bright orange or red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for arid or coastal environments. It has a compact growth habit and is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal.

The species is also known for its ability to tolerate drought conditions, which contributes to its resilience in various climates.
