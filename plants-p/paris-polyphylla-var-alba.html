Paris polyphylla var. alba

Paris polyphylla var. alba, commonly known as the white-flowered Indian arrowroot, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Melanthiaceae and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. It is often found in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in some regions for both horticultural and traditional uses.
