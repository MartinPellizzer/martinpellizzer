Peperomia bracteispica

Peperomia bracteispica, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, velvety foliage with deep green leaves that have a slightly textured surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, arranged in clusters at the ends of stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its slow growth rate and attractive appearance make it suitable for container cultivation and as a decorative plant.
