Prostanthera Serpyllifolia Subsp. Microphylla

Prostanthera serpyllifolia subsp. microphylla is a rare and delicate plant native to the coastal regions of southern Australia. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, known for its aromatic and often medicinal properties. This subspecies is distinguished by its small, narrow leaves and fragrant, pale blue to violet flowers. It thrives in sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and coastal dunes. Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable, making conservation efforts essential for its survival.
