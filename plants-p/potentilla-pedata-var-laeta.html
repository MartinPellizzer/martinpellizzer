Potentilla pedata var. laeta

Potentilla pedata var. laeta, commonly known as the cheerful potentilla, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub with dense, compact foliage that ranges from dark green to reddish hues. The plant produces clusters of small, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a favored plant among gardeners.
