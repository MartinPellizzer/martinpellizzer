Pyrus mesogea var. laxa

Pyrus mesogea var. laxa, commonly known as the slender pear, is a variant of the Mesogea pear native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated fruit shape and thin, edible skin. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a glossy green upper surface.

The flowers are small, white, and fragrant, appearing in clusters during the spring.
