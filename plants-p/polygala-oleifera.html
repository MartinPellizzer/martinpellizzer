Polygala oleifera

Polygala oleifera, commonly known as the olive polygala, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces distinctive, elongated seed pods that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
