Panicularia elata

Panicularia elata, commonly known as the tall panicled bellflower, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of bell-shaped flowers. Panicularia elata prefers well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for gardens and naturalized areas.
