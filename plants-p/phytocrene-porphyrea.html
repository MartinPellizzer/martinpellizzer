Phytocrene porphyrea

Phytocrene porphyrea, commonly known as the purple flame orchid, is a strikingly colorful flowering plant native to the temperate forests of Southeast Asia.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and has a unique root system that helps it absorb nutrients efficiently. Its flowers bloom in late spring and early summer, attracting a variety of pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
