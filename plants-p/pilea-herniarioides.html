Pilea herniarioides

Pilea herniarioides, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a unique and attractive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ability to propagate through leaf cuttings adds to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
