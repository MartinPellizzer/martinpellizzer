Pavonia glabra

Pavonia glabra, commonly known as the smooth pavonia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet. The plant features showy, pink to red bract-like flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and covered with fine hairs, giving it a slightly rough texture.

Pavonia glabra is often found in moist to wet soils in woodland areas and along stream banks.
