Phragmanthera redingii

Phragmanthera redingii, commonly known as the redingii paphiopedilum, is a rare and exotic orchid species native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, dark green to reddish-brown foliage and its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid, often found in humid, shaded forest environments. Its flowers are highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts due to their unique appearance and rarity.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered endangered and is protected in many regions.
