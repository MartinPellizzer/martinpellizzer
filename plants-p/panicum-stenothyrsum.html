Panicum stenothyrsum

Panicum stenothyrsum, commonly known as narrowleaf panicgrass, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with spikelets that are often tinged with red or purple. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a common sight in open fields and disturbed areas.

Its botanical features make it an important component of native grassland ecosystems.
