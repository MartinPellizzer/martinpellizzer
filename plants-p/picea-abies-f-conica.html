Picea abies f. conica

Picea abies f. conica, commonly known as the dwarf spruce, is a compact form of the Norway spruce.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and short, stiff needles. This variety is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its attractive, conical shape. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

The plant is relatively slow-growing and is often used in rock gardens or as a specimen plant in gardens.
