Priva socotrana

Priva socotrana, commonly known as Socotra cypress, is a unique conifer native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its distinctive, umbrella-shaped crown and spirally arranged leaves. The plant has scale-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, and it produces cones that are often covered in a waxy coating. Priva socotrana is adapted to the arid and rocky environment of Socotra, with deep roots that help it survive in dry conditions.

It is a protected species due to its limited distribution and ecological significance.
