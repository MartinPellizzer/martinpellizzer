Puccinellia chinampoensis

Puccinellia chinampoensis, commonly known as the Chinese puccinellia, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in saline or coastal environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Puccinellia chinampoensis plays a role in stabilizing coastal soils and is often found in wetland ecosystems.
