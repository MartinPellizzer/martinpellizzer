Plocama Iljinii

Plocama iljinii is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Russia. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories. This species is known for its small, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. Due to its limited distribution and fragile habitat, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical wonder.
