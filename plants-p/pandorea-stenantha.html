Pandorea stenantha

Pandorea stenantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pandorea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, fleshy fruits that are edible for certain animals. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Pandorea stenantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
