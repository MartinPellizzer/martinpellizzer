Primulina gongchengensis

Primulina gongchengensis, commonly known as the Gongcheng primula, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. It has elliptical leaves with a smooth texture and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
