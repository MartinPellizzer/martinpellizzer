Peperomia bellatula

Peperomia bellatula, commonly known as the Shiny Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and red, which are borne on short, upright spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia bellatula is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
