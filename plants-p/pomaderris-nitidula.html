Pomaderris nitidula

Pomaderris nitidula, commonly known as the glossy leaf pomaderris, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and dry forest habitats.

Pomaderris nitidula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native garden landscapes.
