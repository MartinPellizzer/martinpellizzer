Potentilla detommasii f. aprica

Potentilla detommasii f. aprica, commonly known as the alpine potentilla, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is a low-growing shrub with small, rounded leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to harsh mountain environments. It is often found in meadows and on slopes at high altitudes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.
