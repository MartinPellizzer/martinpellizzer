Phragmipedium × Conchiferum

Phragmipedium × conchiferum is a fascinating hybrid orchid known for its striking, showy flowers. It is a result of crossbreeding between Phragmipedium kovachii and Phragmipedium sandovalii, creating a unique blend of characteristics from both parents. This plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in tropical rainforests. Its flowers, which resemble seashells, are a major attraction for orchid enthusiasts. Due to its beauty and rarity, it is highly sought after in the horticultural trade.
