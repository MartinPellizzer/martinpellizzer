Peperomia caffra

Peperomia caffra, commonly known as the African pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia caffra is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
