Piper broadwayi

Piper broadwayi, commonly known as the Broadway pepper vine, is a climbing plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically followed by bright red berries. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a rough texture, while the stems are hairy and vine-like.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has a mild, peppery scent when the leaves are crushed.
