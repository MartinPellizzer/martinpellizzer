Pelargonium tenuiflorum

Pelargonium tenuiflorum, commonly known as the narrow-flowered geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture. Native to South Africa, this plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and blooms.
