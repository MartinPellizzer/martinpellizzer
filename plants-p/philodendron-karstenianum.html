Philodendron karstenianum

Philodendron karstenianum, commonly known as the Karsten's philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by round, berry-like fruits that are yellow when mature. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
