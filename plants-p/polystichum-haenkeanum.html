Polystichum haenkeanum

Polystichum haenkeanum, commonly known as the Hainan fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its upright, leathery fronds with a dark green color. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as mountain forests. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

The fronds can reach up to 1 meter in length, making it a striking addition to botanical gardens.
