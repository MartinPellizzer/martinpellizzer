Palmifolium medium

Palmifolium medium, commonly known as the medium fan palm, is a tropical plant characterized by its broad, fan-shaped leaves.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is native to regions with warm, humid climates. The plant typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 meters, with a slender trunk covered in persistent leaf bases. Its leaves are usually a vibrant green, providing a striking visual contrast to its woody stem.

This plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its elegant appearance and adaptability to various soil types.
