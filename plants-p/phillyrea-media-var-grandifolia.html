Phillyrea media var. grandifolia

Phillyrea media var. grandifolia, commonly known as the large-leaved Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, white, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in spring. The leaves are thick, leathery, and have a glossy appearance, often with a bluish-green hue. The plant is evergreen, with a dense, compact growth habit that makes it suitable for hedges or ornamental planting.

It is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to coastal gardens.
