Peperomia rizzinii

Peperomia rizzinii, commonly known as the Rizzini peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, often green or reddish in color, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia rizzinii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
