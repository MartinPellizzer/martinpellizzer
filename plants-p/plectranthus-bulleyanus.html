Plectranthus bulleyanus

Plectranthus bulleyanus, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of violet, pink, and white. The leaves are typically velvety and heart-shaped, with a deep green color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
