Pouteria minima

Pouteria minima, commonly known as the dwarf soursop, is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has limited use in traditional medicine and as a food source.
