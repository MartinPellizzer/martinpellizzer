Phaulopsis lankesterioides var. longituba

Phaulopsis lankesterioides var. longituba, commonly known as the long-tube phaulopsis, is a species of flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from violet to deep purple. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas or near freshwater sources.

The unique floral structure of this plant makes it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists alike.
