Podoluma gardneri

Podoluma gardneri, commonly known as the Gardner's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, such as grasslands and sandy soils. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters.
