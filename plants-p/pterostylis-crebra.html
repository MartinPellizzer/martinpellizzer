Pterostylis crebra

Pterostylis crebra, commonly known as the dense-leafed sun-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. The plant features a slender, hairy stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Pterostylis crebra is a perennial herb that produces small, fragrant flowers during the autumn season.
