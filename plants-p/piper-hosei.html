Piper hosei

Piper hosei, commonly known as the Hosen's pepper vine, is a climbing plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by berries containing aromatic seeds. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a rough texture, while its stems are often covered in fine hairs.

Piper hosei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and potential medicinal properties.
