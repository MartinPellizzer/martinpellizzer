Podocarpus ustus

Podocarpus ustus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf podocarp, is a member of the Podocarpaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a coniferous evergreen tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has needle-like leaves that are dark green and arranged in spirals along the branches. Its cones are small and woody, containing seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Podocarpus ustus is valued for its durable wood and is often used in horticulture and landscaping.
