Peperomia macraeana

Peperomia macraeana, commonly known as the Macrae's peperomia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features compact, oval-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green color with a waxy texture. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, which bloom during the warmer months.

Peperomia macraeana is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.
