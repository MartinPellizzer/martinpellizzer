Perilla ocymoides var. rosea

Perilla ocymoides var. rosea, commonly known as purple perilla, is a herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive purple-pink leaves and small, clustered flowers that range from white to pale pink. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic foliage.

Perilla ocymoides var. rosea is also used in traditional medicine and as a culinary herb in some cultures.
