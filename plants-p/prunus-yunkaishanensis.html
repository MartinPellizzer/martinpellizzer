Prunus yunkaishanensis

Prunus yunkaishanensis, commonly known as the Yunkai Shan cherry, is a rare species native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in mountainous regions. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers in early spring, followed by edible berries. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a serrated margin.

The botanical characteristics of P. yunkaishanensis make it an interesting subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
