Psoralidium stenostachys

It is characterized by its slender, aromatic stems and small, clustered umbels of white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate regions and is often found in woodland or shaded areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a distinctive, slightly hairy texture.
