Phyllostachys heterocycla f. obliquinoda

Phyllostachys heterocycla f. obliquinoda, commonly known as the twisted bamboo, is a variant of the bamboo genus Phyllostachys.

It is characterized by its unique, twisted culms that give it a distinctive appearance. This plant is native to China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a smooth, dark green surface.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
