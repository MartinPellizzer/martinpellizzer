Pandanus eumekes

Pandanus eumekes, commonly known as the New Caledonia pandanus, is a flowering plant native to the island of New Caledonia.

It belongs to the Pandanaceae family and is characterized by its tall, palm-like structure with long, narrow leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically clustered in branched inflorescences. Its leaves are often used in traditional crafts and for making thatch.

The species is adapted to coastal and lowland environments, thriving in humid, tropical climates.
