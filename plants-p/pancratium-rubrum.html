Pancratium rubrum

Pancratium rubrum, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces a tall, slender stem. Its bulbs are toxic and contain alkaloids, making it important to handle with care.

Pancratium rubrum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
