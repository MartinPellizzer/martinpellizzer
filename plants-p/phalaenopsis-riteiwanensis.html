Phalaenopsis riteiwanensis

Phalaenopsis riteiwanensis, commonly known as the Ritewan orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant features a pseudobulb with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green in color. Its flowers are small and white, often arranged in a spike with a faint fragrance.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
