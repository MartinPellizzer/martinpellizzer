Pyrus curiosissima

Pyrus curiosissima, commonly known as the Chinese white pear.

It is a deciduous tree native to China, characterized by its smooth, gray bark and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers in early spring, followed by round, yellowish-green fruits that ripen in late summer. The leaves are often used in traditional Chinese medicine, and the tree is valued for its ornamental appeal.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
