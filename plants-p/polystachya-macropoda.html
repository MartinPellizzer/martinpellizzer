Polystachya macropoda

Polystachya macropoda, commonly known as the giant spider orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, arching inflorescences that bear numerous fragrant, spider-like flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest understories, thriving in humid environments. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often forming a dense rosette at the base.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat requirements, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts and conservationists.
