Persica domestica var. albida

Persica domestica var. albida, commonly known as the white peach.

This variant of the domestic peach is characterized by its pale yellow to white flesh and a delicate, sweet flavor. It typically has a smaller fruit size compared to standard peaches, with a thinner skin that is often more tender. The flowers are usually white or pale pink, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.

It is often cultivated for both its fruit and aesthetic appeal.
