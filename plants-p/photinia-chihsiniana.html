Photinia chihsiniana

Photinia chihsiniana, commonly known as the Chinese firethorn, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features glossy, elliptical leaves that turn vibrant red in the autumn. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden landscapes.

Photinia chihsiniana prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
