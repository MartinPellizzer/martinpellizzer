Primula × bilekii

Primula × bilekii, commonly known as the Bilek's primrose, is a hybrid species within the Primulaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of pink, purple, and white. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. Its foliage consists of broad, ovate leaves that are usually covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Primula × bilekii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom reliably in garden settings.
