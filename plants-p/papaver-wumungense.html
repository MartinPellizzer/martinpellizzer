Papaver wumungense

Papaver wumungense, commonly known as the Wumungense poppy, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically range in color from deep red to orange. The plant has a taproot system and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. Its leaves are usually alternate, with a broad, heart-shaped form.

Papaver wumungense is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
