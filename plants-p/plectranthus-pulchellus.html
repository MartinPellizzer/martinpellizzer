Plectranthus pulchellus

Plectranthus pulchellus, commonly known as the purple heart plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its attractive, tubular purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive reddish-brown color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
