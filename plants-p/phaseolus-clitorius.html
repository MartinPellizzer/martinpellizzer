Phaseolus clitorius

Phaseolus clitorius, commonly known as the winged bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions.

It produces distinctive kidney-shaped seeds enclosed in pods, and its leaves are trifoliate with prominent veins. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in diverse soil conditions. It is also valued for its nutritional content, including high levels of protein and iron.

Additionally, it plays a role in nitrogen fixation, improving soil fertility.
