Peucedanum graminifolium

Peucedanum graminifolium, commonly known as Chinese cowparsley, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often finely toothed. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in umbels, which bloom in early summer. Its fruits are elongated, ribbed schizocarps that split into two parts when mature.

Peucedanum graminifolium is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
