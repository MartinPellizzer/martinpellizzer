Peperomia circinnata

Peperomia circinnata, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, though they are not particularly showy. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia circinnata is relatively low maintenance and resistant to pests, making it an ideal plant for both novice and experienced growers.
