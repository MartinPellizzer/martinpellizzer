Polygala axillaris

Polygala axillaris, commonly known as the narrow-leaved milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces delicate, pinkish-white flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slight pubescence.

Polygala axillaris is adapted to arid environments and is often found in fynbos vegetation.
