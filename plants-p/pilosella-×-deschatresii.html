Pilosella × deschatresii

Pilosella × deschatresii, commonly known as the common mouse-ear hawkweed, is a hybrid plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its dense, grayish-white foliage that resembles mouse ears, giving it its common name. It is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, often growing in disturbed soils and open habitats.
