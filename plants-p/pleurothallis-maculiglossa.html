Pleurothallis maculiglossa

Pleurothallis maculiglossa, commonly known as the spotted lip orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small to medium-sized epiphytic orchid, typically found growing on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a slender, wiry stem with clustered, small greenish-white flowers that have a distinctive spotted lip. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a popular specimen among orchid enthusiasts and botanists.
