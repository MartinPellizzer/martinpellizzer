Pachygone zeylanica

Pachygone zeylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant produces a starchy tuberous root that is used as a food source. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in swampy areas.

Due to its unique morphology and nutritional value, it holds cultural and economic significance in its native region.
