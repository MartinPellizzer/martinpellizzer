Phyllanthus aeneus var. longistylis

Phyllanthus aeneus var. longistylis, commonly known as the long-styled aeneus phyllanthus, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are typically arranged in inflorescences at the ends of branches. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and often have a waxy texture.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
