Porophyllum linaria

Porophyllum linaria, commonly known as the linaria ragweed, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellow-green flowers that bloom in clusters. It is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragweeds.

Due to its pollen, it can contribute to seasonal allergies in sensitive individuals.
