Pennisetum chudeaui subsp. monodianum

Pennisetum chudeaui subsp.

monodianum, commonly known as the purple fountain grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to southern Africa. It features slender, upright stems that produce fluffy, pinkish-purple plumes during the late summer and early autumn.

The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green with a slight blue-gray hue. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its unique appearance and seasonal bloom make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological plantings.
