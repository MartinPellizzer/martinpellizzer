Polygonum lichiangense

Polygonum lichiangense, commonly known as the Yunnan knotweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Polygonaceae.

It is native to China, particularly found in the Yunnan Province, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its upright stems, heart-shaped leaves, and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. It is often found in mountainous regions and is adapted to cool, temperate climates.

Polygonum lichiangense is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
