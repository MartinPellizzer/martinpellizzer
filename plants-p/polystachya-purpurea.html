Polystachya purpurea

Polystachya purpurea, commonly known as the purple sword fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to tropical regions of Africa and Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, erect rhizome and pinnately compound leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy sheen. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Polystachya purpurea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with high humidity.
