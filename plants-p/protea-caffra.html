Protea caffra

Protea caffra, commonly known as the Natal candle.

It is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa, characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that resemble candles. The plant features a woody stem with dense, leathery leaves that are typically gray-green in color. Its flowers are composed of a central cone surrounded by petal-like bracts, which come in shades of white, pink, and red.

Protea caffra is well adapted to coastal and sandy soils, and it thrives in full sun.
