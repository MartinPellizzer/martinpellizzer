Piper sonsonatense

Piper sonsonatense, commonly known as the Sonsonatense pepper, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when ripe, and these berries are used in traditional medicine and as a spice. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

The leaves are often used in herbal remedies due to their medicinal properties.
