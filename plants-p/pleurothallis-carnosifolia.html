Pleurothallis carnosifolia

Pleurothallis carnosifolia, commonly known as the fleshy-leafed orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically growing on trees or rocks. The plant features compact, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to its often dry or seasonal habitats. Its flowers are usually small and delicate, with a variety of colors including white, yellow, and pink.

Pleurothallis carnosifolia is valued in horticulture for its unique foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
