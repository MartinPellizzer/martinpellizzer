Pearsonia multiflora

Pearsonia multiflora, commonly known as the New Zealand broom, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, characterized by its yellow flowers and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Pearsonia multiflora plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing food and shelter for native wildlife.
