Physokentia

Physokentia, commonly known as the giant kentia palm, is a species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a slow growth rate and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its elegant appearance and air-purifying qualities. Physokentia thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor landscaping.
