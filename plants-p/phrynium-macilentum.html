Phrynium macilentum

Phrynium macilentum, commonly known as the slender flame plant, is a tropical perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive waxy texture and a deep green color. The plant produces striking yellow-orange flowers arranged in a spike-like inflorescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor plant collections.
