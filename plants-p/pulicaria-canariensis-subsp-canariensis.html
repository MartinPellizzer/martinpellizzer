Pulicaria canariensis subsp. canariensis

Pulicaria canariensis subsp.

canariensis, commonly known as Canaryweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its silver-green, woolly foliage and small yellow flowers.

The plant typically thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is native to the Canary Islands. It is often found in coastal and inland habitats, where it forms dense mats.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
