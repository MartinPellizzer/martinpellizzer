Pleurothallis caymanensis

Pleurothallis caymanensis, commonly known as the Cayman Islands orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Cayman Islands.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often growing in coastal areas or near freshwater sources.

Its unique floral structure and limited distribution make it a notable species within the Pleurothallis genus.
