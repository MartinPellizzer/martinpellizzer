Pachyrhizus vernalis var. integrifolius

Pachyrhizus vernalis var. integrifolius, commonly known as the smooth-leafed vernal vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with smooth margins and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces edible tubers that are rich in nutrients and have been traditionally used in local cuisines. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its rapid growth habit makes it suitable for ornamental use in gardens and as a cover crop.
