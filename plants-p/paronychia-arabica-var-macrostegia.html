Paronychia arabica var. macrostegia

Paronychia arabica var. macrostegia, commonly known as the silverleaf iceplant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white, waxy powder, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, pink to white flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a valuable addition to arid landscapes.
