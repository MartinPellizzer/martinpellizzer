Podocarpus matudae subsp. matudae

Podocarpus matudae subsp.

matudae, commonly known as the Matudae Podocarp, is a species of coniferous tree native to the highlands of Ecuador. It belongs to the family Podocarpaceae and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and conical shape.

The plant exhibits a slow growth rate and is often found in humid, subtropical climates. Its leaves are typically narrow and leathery, with a dark green coloration.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
