Pedicularis pectinata

Pedicularis pectinata, commonly known as the velvetleaf lousewort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and showy, spurred flowers that range in color from pink to red.
