Peperomia circulifolia var. eciliata

Peperomia circulifolia var. eciliata, commonly known as the curled leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions.

It features glossy, waxy leaves that are curled and spiraled, giving it a unique, decorative appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of pink or white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
