Pternandra appendiculata

Pternandra appendiculata, commonly known as the African orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial herb with linear, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color and are clustered in racemes.

Pternandra appendiculata is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and grasslands.
