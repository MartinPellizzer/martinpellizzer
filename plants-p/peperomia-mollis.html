Peperomia mollis

Peperomia mollis, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy, waxy leaves that are typically deep green with a velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, which appear in clusters near the leaf axils. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and ability to thrive in dry conditions.

Peperomia mollis is prized for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
