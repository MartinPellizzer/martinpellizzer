Pterostylis coccina

Pterostylis coccina, commonly known as the crimson helmet orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows on tree trunks or branches in subtropical and temperate rainforests. The plant features a slender, wiry stem with a single, showy, red or orange-flushed flower that resembles a helmet. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

The orchid blooms during spring and early summer, often in shaded, humid environments.
