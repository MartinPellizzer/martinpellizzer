Panicum bullockii

Panicum bullockii, commonly known as Bullock's grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, erect stems that often form dense clumps. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically pale green to golden in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a prominent midrib and a rough texture.

Panicum bullockii is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy plant suitable for coastal and sandy soils.
