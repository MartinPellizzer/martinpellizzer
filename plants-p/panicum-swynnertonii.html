Panicum swynnertonii

Panicum swynnertonii, commonly known as Swynnerton's panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. The plant features slender, erect stems and produces fluffy, panicle-like inflorescences that are often pink or purplish in color. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
