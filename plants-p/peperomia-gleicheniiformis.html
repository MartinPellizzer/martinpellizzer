Peperomia gleicheniiformis

Peperomia gleicheniiformis, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to South America.

It features waxy, silver-gray leaves that are often variegated with white and green, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in late spring or early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate watering.

Peperomia gleicheniiformis is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ornamental value.
