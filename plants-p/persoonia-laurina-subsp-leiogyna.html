Persoonia laurina subsp. leiogyna

Persoonia laurina subsp.

leiogyna, commonly known as the smooth-leaved geebung, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia. It is characterized by its smooth, elliptical leaves that are often glaucous in appearance.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in coastal heathlands and dry forests. Persoonia laurina subsp.

leiogyna is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
