Perama humilis

Perama humilis, commonly known as the small perama, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to southern Australia.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or sandy habitats.

Perama humilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in native landscaping.
