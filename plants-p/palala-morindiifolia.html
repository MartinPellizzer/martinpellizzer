Palala morindiifolia

Palala morindiifolia, commonly known as the Madagascar star vine, is a climbing plant native to the island of Madagascar.

It features delicate, star-shaped white flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting various pollinators. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a light green hue. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Palala morindiifolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
