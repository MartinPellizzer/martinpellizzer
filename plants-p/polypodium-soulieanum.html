Polypodium soulieanum

Polypodium soulieanum, commonly known as the Souchong fern, is a tropical fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features pinnately compound leaves with a distinctive, often serrated margin. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, typically in forest understories.

Its fronds are usually dark green and can reach lengths of up to 1 meter.
