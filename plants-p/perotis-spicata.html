Perotis spicata

Perotis spicata, commonly known as silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and daisy-like yellow flowers with a white center. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.

Perotis spicata is a perennial herb that can reach heights of up to 30 centimeters.
