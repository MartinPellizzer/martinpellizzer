Polypodium plumosum

Polypodium plumosum, commonly known as the feathery polypody, is a fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically light green and finely divided. The plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found growing on rocks or tree trunks. Its reproductive structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Polypodium plumosum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ease of cultivation.
