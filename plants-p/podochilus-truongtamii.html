Podochilus truongtamii

Podochilus truongtamii, commonly known as the Truong Tam orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to Vietnam.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, white or pale yellow blooms with intricate patterns on the petals and lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
