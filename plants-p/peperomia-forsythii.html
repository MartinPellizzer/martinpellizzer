Peperomia forsythii

Peperomia forsythii, commonly known as the Forsyth's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to Brazil.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of pink or red, which appear during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia forsythii is relatively low maintenance and resistant to pests, contributing to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
