Philodendron davidsonii subsp. bocatoranum

Philodendron davidsonii subsp.

bocatoranum, commonly known as the Bocatoranum philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to South America. It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers, though it is primarily grown for its ornamental foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity.

Its unique leaf patterns make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical plant collections.
