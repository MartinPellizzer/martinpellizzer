Pilea nigrescens

Pilea nigrescens, commonly known as the black nightshade plant, is a tropical species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, heart-shaped leaves that often turn a deep blackish-purple color when mature. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its striking foliage, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in indoor settings.
