Paphia rubrocalyx var. rubrocalyx

Paphia rubrocalyx var. rubrocalyx, commonly known as the red calyx paphiopedilum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish tint along the midrib. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive lip that is often red or orange in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
