Pultenaea stricta var. largiflorens

Pultenaea stricta var. largiflorens, commonly known as the broad-flowered pultenaea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features slender, often woody stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and opposite, with a slightly hairy texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
