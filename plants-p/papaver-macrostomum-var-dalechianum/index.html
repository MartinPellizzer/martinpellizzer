Papaver Macrostomum Var. Dalechianum

Papaver macrostomum var. dalechianum is a striking member of the poppy family known for its vibrant red petals and distinctive, large seed pods. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, this plant thrives in rocky and arid environments. Its unique appearance has made it a popular subject in botanical studies and horticultural collections. The variety is often mistaken for other poppy species due to its similar characteristics. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively rare and less commonly cultivated plant.
