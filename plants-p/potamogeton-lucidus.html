Potamogeton lucidus

Potamogeton lucidus, commonly known as the smooth pondweed, is a submerged aquatic plant found in freshwater environments.

It has long, ribbon-like leaves that are typically smooth and glossy, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually borne in clusters near the water surface. It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.

Potamogeton lucidus is often used in wetland restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize sediments and improve water quality.
