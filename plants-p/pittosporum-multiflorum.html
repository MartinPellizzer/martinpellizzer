Pittosporum multiflorum

Pittosporum multiflorum, commonly known as the Chinese pittosporum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a glossy appearance, and its leaves are elliptical with a pointed tip. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers in the spring, which are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Its adaptability and aesthetic appeal contribute to its widespread cultivation in temperate regions.
