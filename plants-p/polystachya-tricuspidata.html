Polystachya tricuspidata

Polystachya tricuspidata, commonly known as the three-lobed bamboo orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Polystachya tricuspidata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
