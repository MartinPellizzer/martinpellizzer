Pulmonaria oblongata

Pulmonaria oblongata, commonly known as the broad-leaf lungwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features basal rosettes of lance-shaped, spotted leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces upright flowering stems bearing clusters of bell-shaped flowers, typically in shades of pink and white. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Pulmonaria oblongata is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
