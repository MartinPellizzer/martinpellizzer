Pongamia rosea

Pongamia rosea, commonly known as the pink pongam, is a tropical leguminous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive pinkish-white flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces long, cylindrical pods containing flat, dark brown seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas.

Pongamia rosea is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional uses in various cultures.
