Piper australe

Piper australe, commonly known as the Australian pepper vine, is a climbing shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by red, peppery fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in coastal regions.

Its ornamental value and unique fruit make it a notable species in horticulture.
