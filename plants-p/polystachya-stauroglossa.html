Polystachya stauroglossa

Polystachya stauroglossa, commonly known as the star-lip orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique floral structure. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with numerous small, greenish-white flowers that have a distinct star-shaped lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Polystachya stauroglossa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
