Psychotria burgeri

Psychotria burgeri, commonly known as the Burger's Psychotria, is a species of flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a glossy green surface.

The flowers are small, clustered in umbels, and are usually white or pale pink in color.
