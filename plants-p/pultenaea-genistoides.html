Pultenaea genistoides

Pultenaea genistoides, commonly known as the golden wattle, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery foliage and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is known for its nitrogen-fixing abilities. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland and heathland environments.
