Pomelina

Pomelina, commonly known as the Chinese tangerine, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is closely related to citrus plants. The plant produces fragrant, orange-colored fruits that are edible and often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are glossy and oval-shaped, with a distinctive aromatic scent.

Pomelina thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its fruit.
