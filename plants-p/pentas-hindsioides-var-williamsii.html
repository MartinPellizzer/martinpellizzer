Pentas hindsioides var. williamsii

Pentas hindsioides var. williamsii, commonly known as the Williams' pentas, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a glossy appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
