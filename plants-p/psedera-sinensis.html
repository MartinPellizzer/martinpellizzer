Psedera sinensis

Psedera sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese hibiscus, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with a prominent yellow or orange center. The plant has palmate leaves that are usually lobed and have a velvety texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

The flowers bloom in the summer and are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.
