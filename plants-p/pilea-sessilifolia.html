Pilea sessilifolia

Pilea sessilifolia, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are typically green with a subtle variegation, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Pilea sessilifolia is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and aesthetic appeal.
