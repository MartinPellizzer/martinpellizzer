Peperomia lignescens

Peperomia lignescens, commonly known as the velvet plant, is a tropical species native to Central and South America.

It is a perennial evergreen shrub characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that give it a velvety texture. The plant features upright, branching stems and small, inconspicuous flowers that appear in clusters. Its foliage is typically dark green with a glossy surface, and it has a compact growth habit that makes it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia lignescens prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for low-maintenance houseplants.
