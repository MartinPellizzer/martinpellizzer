Pyrethrum punctatum

Pyrethrum punctatum, commonly known as the dotted pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with distinctive black spots, which give it its common name. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. Its foliage is typically gray-green with a fuzzy texture, adding visual interest.

Pyrethrum punctatum is also valued for its potential use in natural pest control due to the presence of pyrethrins in its flowers.
