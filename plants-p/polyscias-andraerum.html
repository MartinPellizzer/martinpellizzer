Polyscias andraerum

Polyscias andraerum, commonly known as the New Zealand nettle, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant typically grows to a height of 1–2 meters and features small, white or greenish flowers. Its foliage is often used in traditional Māori medicine for its purported healing properties.

Polyscias andraerum is also valued as an ornamental plant due to its attractive, feathery appearance.
