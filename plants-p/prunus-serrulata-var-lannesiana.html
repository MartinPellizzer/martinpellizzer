Prunus serrulata var. lannesiana

Prunus serrulata var. lannesiana, commonly known as the Japanese cherry blossom, is a deciduous tree native to Japan.

It is characterized by its delicate, pink to white flowers that bloom in early spring, creating a stunning visual display. The tree has small, oval leaves that turn yellow in autumn, adding to its seasonal beauty. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and cultural significance.
