Panicum acuminatum var. columbianum

Panicum acuminatum var. columbianum, commonly known as Colombian hairgrass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and produces fluffy, pinkish-white inflorescences. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for water gardens and bog landscapes.

The plant is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and support local wildlife.
