Peronocactus horstii

Peronocactus horstii, commonly known as the golden barrel cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its spherical shape and a dense covering of golden-yellow spines. The plant has a thick, water-storing stem that helps it survive in harsh desert conditions. Its flowers, which appear in spring, are typically yellow or orange and bloom at the top of the plant.

Peronocactus horstii is popular as a houseplant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
