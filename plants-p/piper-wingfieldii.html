Piper wingfieldii

Piper wingfieldii, commonly known as the wingfield's pepper or wingfieldii pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It produces small, peppery fruits that are used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.

The plant's botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
