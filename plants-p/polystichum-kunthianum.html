Polystichum kunthianum

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds in clusters called sori, which are protected by a papery indusium.

Polystichum kunthianum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
