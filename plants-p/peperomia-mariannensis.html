Peperomia mariannensis

Peperomia mariannensis, commonly known as the Mariana peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces delicate, spadix-like inflorescences with tiny, white to greenish flowers, which are characteristic of the Piperaceae family. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements, it is often appreciated by plant enthusiasts.
