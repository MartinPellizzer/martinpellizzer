Prunus persica f. glabra

Prunus persica f. glabra, commonly known as the smooth peach.

This variant of the peach tree is characterized by its smooth, hairless bark and fruit. The leaves are ovate with a pointed tip and a smooth margin. The flowers are pink, typically appearing before the leaves, and the fruit is usually smaller and less fuzzy compared to the common peach.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and sometimes for its unique fruit characteristics.
