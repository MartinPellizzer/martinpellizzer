Pisum elatius var. leiospermum

Pisum elatius var. leiospermum, commonly known as the smooth-seeded grass vetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in racemes. The plant has smooth, glabrous pods that contain several elliptical seeds. It is adapted to dry, rocky habitats and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it valuable for soil enrichment in agricultural settings.
