Plectranthus linearifolius

Plectranthus linearifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaf plectranthus, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features lance-shaped, aromatic leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or pink in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its versatility and ornamental value make it a favored plant among horticulturists.
