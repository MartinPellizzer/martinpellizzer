Pilosella piloselliflora

Pilosella piloselliflora, commonly known as the woolly cat's-paw, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features hairy, lobed leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Pilosella piloselliflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
