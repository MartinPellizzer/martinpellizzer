Potentilla bifurca subsp. orientalis

Potentilla bifurca subsp.

orientalis, commonly known as the oriental cinquefoil, is a perennial shrub native to eastern Asia. It features small, yellow, five-petaled flowers that bloom in early summer.

The plant has slender, branched stems and dark green, trifoliate leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
