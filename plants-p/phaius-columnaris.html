Phaius columnaris

Phaius columnaris, commonly known as the tall marsh orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features tall, upright stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters, adorned with broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of yellow and green, often with a distinctive white or greenish throat. It thrives in wet, shady environments, typically near water bodies such as swamps and marshes.

Its unique morphology and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and orchid enthusiasts.
