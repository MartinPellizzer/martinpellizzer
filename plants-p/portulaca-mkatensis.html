Portulaca mkatensis

Portulaca mkatensis, commonly known as the Makati moss rose, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping. Its short blooming period typically occurs in the summer months.

Portulaca mkatensis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand drought conditions.
