Persica domestica var. rotundata

Persica domestica var. rotundata, commonly known as the round-leaf peach.

This variety is characterized by its rounded, thick leaves and is typically found in warmer climates. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Its fruit is usually smaller than standard peaches and has a more intense flavor.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its edible fruit.
