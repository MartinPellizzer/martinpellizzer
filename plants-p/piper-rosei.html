Piper rosei

Piper rosei, commonly known as the rose pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with aerial roots that allow it to attach to supports. Its small, greenish-white flowers are followed by berries that turn red when mature.

Piper rosei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and potential medicinal properties.
