Pterocarpus dekindtianus

Pterocarpus dekindtianus, commonly known as the African桃花心木, is a tropical tree native to Central and West Africa.

It is characterized by its dark, durable wood, which is highly valued for furniture and decorative purposes. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by flat, winged fruits that aid in seed dispersal. Its leaves are bipinnate, consisting of numerous small leaflets, and it thrives in humid, tropical climates.

The plant is also noted for its slow growth and long lifespan, making it an important species in local ecosystems.
