Pilea muscosa var. alba

Pilea muscosa var. alba, commonly known as the white pilea, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features rounded, fleshy leaves that are typically a pale green to white in color, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in hue. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate watering.

This variety is prized for its unique foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
