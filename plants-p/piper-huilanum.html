Piper huilanum

Piper huilanum, commonly known as the Huilan pepper, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant produces small, green fruits that mature to a bright red color, containing aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are often used in local herbal remedies due to their medicinal properties.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
