Phalaenopsis amabilis var. erubescens

Phalaenopsis amabilis var. erubescens, commonly known as the Red Phalaenopsis, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, long-lasting flowers.

It features dense, velvety petals that range from deep red to pink, often with a contrasting white or cream lip. This variety is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ease of care and ability to bloom repeatedly.

The plant typically grows on tree trunks or in shaded forest floors, showcasing its epiphytic nature.
