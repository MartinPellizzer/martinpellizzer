Piper malamiris

Piper malamiris, commonly known as the Malamir mountain pepper, is a climbing vine native to the highlands of Madagascar.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various pepper species. The plant produces small, clustered green berries that turn red when mature, containing numerous seeds. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped with a rough texture and a distinct aromatic scent.

Piper malamiris is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional remedies by local communities.
