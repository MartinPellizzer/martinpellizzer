Phaleria cumingii

Phaleria cumingii, commonly known as the Cuming's evergreen tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its flowers are small and clustered, often greenish-yellow in color, and it produces drupes that are used in traditional medicine.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and medicinal properties.
