Polygonum fastigiatoramosum var. angustissimum

Polygonum fastigiatoramosum var. angustissimum, commonly known as narrowleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It typically grows in wet or moist soils, often found in marshes, ditches, and along stream banks. The plant has alternate, lanceolate leaves that are usually less than 1 inch wide, giving it its characteristic narrow appearance. It produces small, greenish-white flowers in dense clusters during the summer months.

The species is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed sites and is often used in wetland restoration projects.
