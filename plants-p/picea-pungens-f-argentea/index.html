Picea Pungens F. Argentea

Picea pungens f. argentea is a striking variant of the blue spruce known for its silvery-blue foliage. This ornamental tree is prized for its unique coloration and hardy nature. It thrives in cooler climates and is often used in landscaping for its visual appeal. The variegated needles create a shimmering effect under sunlight. Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for both residential and commercial gardens.
