Phyllanthus huntii

Phyllanthus huntii, commonly known as the African spurge, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, characterized by its dense, spiny foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, round fruits. Its leaves are simple and opposite, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Phyllanthus huntii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
