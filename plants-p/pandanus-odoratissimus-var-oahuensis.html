Pandanus odoratissimus var. oahuensis

Pandanus odoratissimus var. oahuensis, commonly known as the Oahu pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional Hawaiian crafts. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically clustered in branched inflorescences. Its fibrous leaves are valued for their durability and versatility in making thatch and baskets.

This variety is adapted to coastal environments and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.
