Pilea pedunculata

Pilea pedunculata, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by round, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea pedunculata is known for its easy care and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments.
