Polygonum pedunculare var. angustissimum

Polygonum pedunculare var. angustissimum, commonly known as narrowleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia and North America.

It typically grows in moist environments such as wet meadows, marshes, and stream banks. The plant has slender, upright stems that are often reddish in color, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Small, clustered flowers appear in late summer, producing a pinkish to reddish hue.

It is characterized by its ability to thrive in wet soils and its use in traditional herbal medicine.
