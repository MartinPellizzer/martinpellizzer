Polygala nicaeensis f. speciosa

Polygala nicaeensis f. speciosa, commonly known as the showy milkwort, is a striking flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to purple. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are clustered in spikelike inflorescences, and it blooms during the spring and early summer.

This ornamental plant is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual impact.
