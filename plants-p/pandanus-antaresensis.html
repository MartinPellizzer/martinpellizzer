Pandanus antaresensis

Pandanus antaresensis, commonly known as the Antares pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Pandanus.

It is native to the Pacific Islands, particularly found in areas such as Fiji and Tonga. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, sword-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or green in color.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and cultural significance in the regions where it grows.
