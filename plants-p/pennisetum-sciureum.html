Pennisetum sciureum

Pennisetum sciureum, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender culms that produce dense clusters of lavender-pink to purple inflorescences, giving it a dramatic visual appeal. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adding a cool contrast to its vibrant flowers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Pennisetum sciureum is known for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
