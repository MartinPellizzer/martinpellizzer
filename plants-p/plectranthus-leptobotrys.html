Plectranthus leptobotrys

Plectranthus leptobotrys, commonly known as the slender-leaved plectranthus, is a herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often slightly hairy. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or blue in color, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and ability to attract pollinators.
