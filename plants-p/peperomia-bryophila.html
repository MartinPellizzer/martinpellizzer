Peperomia bryophila

Peperomia bryophila, commonly known as the moss peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green or variegated with silver or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or red, followed by tiny, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and bonsai enthusiasts.
