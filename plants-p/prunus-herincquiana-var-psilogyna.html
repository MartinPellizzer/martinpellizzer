Prunus herincquiana var. psilogyna

Prunus herincquiana var. psilogyna, commonly known as the Chinese date or longan, is a deciduous tree native to southern China, Vietnam, and the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white or pink flowers that bloom in spring. The tree produces small, round fruits with a sweet, juicy pulp surrounding a single seed. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate with a glossy surface.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its edible fruit, which is commonly consumed fresh or used in culinary applications.
