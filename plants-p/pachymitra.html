Pachymitra

Pachymitra, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

These plants are characterized by their thick, fleshy leaves and showy, clustered flowers that come in a variety of colors. They typically grow in moist, shady environments and are often found in forest understories. Pachymitra species are popular as ornamental plants due to their attractive foliage and blooms.
