Pretrea zanguebarica

Pretrea zanguebarica, commonly known as the East African orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of East Africa.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces strikingly large, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or near water sources.

Pretrea zanguebarica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
