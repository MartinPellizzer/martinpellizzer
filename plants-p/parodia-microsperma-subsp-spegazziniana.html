Parodia microsperma subsp. spegazziniana

Parodia microsperma subsp.

spegazziniana, commonly known as the Spegazzini's pincushion cactus, is a compact, spiny cactus native to Argentina. It features a short, cylindrical stem that is often covered in white, woolly bristles.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance and slow growth make it a popular choice for succulent collectors.
