Poecilochroma punctata

Poecilochroma punctata, commonly known as the spotted leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and strikingly patterned leaves with distinct spots. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
