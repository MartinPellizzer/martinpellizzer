Pyranthus tullearensis subsp. tullearensis

Pyranthus tullearensis subsp.

tullearensis, commonly known as the Tullea sandwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils.

The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It has a compact growth habit with linear, grass-like leaves.

This species is often found in coastal or inland dune environments, where it thrives in well-drained conditions.
