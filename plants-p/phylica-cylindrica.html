Phylica cylindrica

Phylica cylindrica, commonly known as the slender flame bush, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and clusters of small, bright red or orange flowers. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds covered in a fibrous coating. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Phylica cylindrica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
