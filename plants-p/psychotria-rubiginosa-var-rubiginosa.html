Psychotria rubiginosa var. rubiginosa

Psychotria rubiginosa var. rubiginosa, commonly known as the red-eyed wonder, is a species of flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly in countries like Costa Rica and Panama. The plant is characterized by its striking red bracts that surround its small, white flowers, making it visually distinctive. It typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters.

The leaves are opposite, elliptical, and glossy, with a deep green color that contrasts well with the vibrant red bracts.
