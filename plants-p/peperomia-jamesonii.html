Peperomia jamesonii

Peperomia jamesonii, commonly known as the Jameson's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple on the underside, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, arranged in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia jamesonii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
