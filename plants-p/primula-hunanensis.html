Primula hunanensis

Primula hunanensis, commonly known as the Hunan primrose, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are oval-shaped with a slightly hairy texture, and the flowers come in shades of white, pink, and purple.

Primula hunanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
