Parakmeria

Parakmeria, commonly known as the sweet myrtle, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas, particularly in Central and South America. The plants are shrubs or small trees with aromatic, often fragrant leaves and showy, white or pink flowers.
