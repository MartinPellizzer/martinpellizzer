Panicum fasciculatum var. dissitiflorum

Panicum fasciculatum var. dissitiflorum, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and produces fluffy, pale blue to white seed heads. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it adaptable to a variety of environmental conditions. It is often used in restoration projects due to its drought tolerance and ability to stabilize soil.

Its ornamental value comes from its striking appearance and seasonal color changes.
