Peperomia remyi

Peperomia remyi, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically silver-gray in color, with a waxy texture that helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white, often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia remyi is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and moderate humidity to flourish.
