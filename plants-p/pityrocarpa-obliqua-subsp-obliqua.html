Pityrocarpa obliqua subsp. obliqua

Pityrocarpa obliqua subsp.

obliqua, commonly known as the oblique pittosporum, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to southern South America. It belongs to the family Pittosporaceae and is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and showy yellow flowers.
