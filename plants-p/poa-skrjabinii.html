Poa skrjabinii

Poa skrjabinii, commonly known as the Skrjabin's grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in Kazakhstan and southern Russia. This perennial grass is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and fine, blue-green leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine environments.

Poa skrjabinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
