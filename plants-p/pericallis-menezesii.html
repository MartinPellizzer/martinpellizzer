Pericallis menezesii

Pericallis menezesii, commonly known as the Menezes' pericallis, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Pericallis menezesii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
