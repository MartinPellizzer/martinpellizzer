Penstemon Glaber Var. Speciosus

Penstemon glaber var. speciosus is a striking member of the Plantain family known for its vibrant flowers and adaptability. This variety is prized for its ability to thrive in a range of environmental conditions, making it a popular choice for gardeners. Its showy blooms, often in shades of blue and purple, add a dramatic flair to any landscape. Native to the western United States, it has gained recognition for its ornamental value and ecological benefits. Whether in a wildflower meadow or a cultivated garden, this plant is a standout performer.
