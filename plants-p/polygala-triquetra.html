Polygala triquetra

Polygala triquetra, commonly known as the three-lobed milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish in color, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds.
