Pentas geophila

Pentas geophila, commonly known as the purple pentas, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that produces clusters of showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often used in gardens for its ornamental value. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and slightly hairy, while its stems are woody and branching.

Pentas geophila thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for container gardening and landscape design.
