Pleurothallis leptopetala

Pleurothallis leptopetala, commonly known as the slender petal orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, linear leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. Its flowers are often arranged in a raceme and bloom during the rainy season.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
