Parodia aureispina var. mutabilis

Parodia aureispina var. mutabilis, commonly known as the variable sun rose, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with prominent, spiny areoles and produces yellow to orange flowers during the summer months. The plant is known for its variable coloration, with stems that can range from green to red depending on light exposure and moisture levels. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its striking appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for rock gardens and cactus collections.
