Paepalanthus damazioi

Paepalanthus damazioi, commonly known as the Damaziano's torch cactus, is a rare and striking plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its tall, spiny stems that can reach up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the dry season, which are often followed by small, red fruits.
