Phyllagathis rufa

Phyllagathis rufa, commonly known as the red-stemmed piper, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Piperaceae, which includes other species of piper plants. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and its distinctive reddish stems. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
