Peperomia galioides

Peperomia galioides, commonly known as the silver mohair plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia galioides is relatively low-maintenance and resistant to pests, making it an ideal plant for both novice and experienced growers.
