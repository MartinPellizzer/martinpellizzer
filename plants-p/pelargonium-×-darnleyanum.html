Pelargonium × darnleyanum

Pelargonium × darnleyanum, commonly known as the Darnley's geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows to a height of 15–30 cm and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.

Pelargonium × darnleyanum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
