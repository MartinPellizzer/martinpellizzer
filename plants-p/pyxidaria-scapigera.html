Pyxidaria scapigera

Pyxidaria scapigera, commonly known as the scarlet globemallow, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its showy, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in desert or rocky habitats.
