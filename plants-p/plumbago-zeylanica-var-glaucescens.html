Plumbago zeylanica var. glaucescens

Plumbago zeylanica var. glaucescens, commonly known as blue plumbago, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has woody stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its medicinal properties have also been studied for their potential health benefits.
