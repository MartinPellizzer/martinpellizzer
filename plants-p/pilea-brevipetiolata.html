Pilea brevipetiolata

Pilea brevipetiolata, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features heart-shaped, glossy, dark green leaves that are short-petioled, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers in spring, followed by red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular indoor plant.

Its ability to propagate through leaf cuttings adds to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
