Panicum unilineatum

Panicum unilineatum, commonly known as slender panicgrass, is a grass species native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves that are typically green in color. The plant produces small, fluffy inflorescences that are often wind-dispersed, aiding in its propagation. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in landscaping or as a forage plant.
