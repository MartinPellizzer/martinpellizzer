Pelargonium × mixtum

Pelargonium × mixtum, commonly known as the zonal geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The leaves are typically marked with distinct color zones, giving the plant its common name. It is a popular ornamental plant, often grown in gardens and containers for its aesthetic appeal.

Pelargonium × mixtum thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
