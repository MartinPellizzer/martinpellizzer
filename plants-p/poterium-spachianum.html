Poterium spachianum

Poterium spachianum, commonly known as the Spach's sedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Cyperaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, well-drained soils, often found in wetlands or along stream banks. Poterium spachianum is native to parts of Europe and Asia and is valued for its ornamental and ecological benefits.
