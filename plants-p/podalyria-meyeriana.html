Podalyria meyeriana

Podalyria meyeriana, commonly known as Meyer's podalyria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
