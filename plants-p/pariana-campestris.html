Pariana campestris

Pariana campestris, commonly known as the field pariana, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are usually narrow and linear, with a rough texture.

Pariana campestris is often found in temperate regions and is adapted to thrive in low-nutrient environments.
