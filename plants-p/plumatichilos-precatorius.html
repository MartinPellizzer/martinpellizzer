Plumatichilos precatorius

Plumatichilos precatorius, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow petals and sepals, which form a star-like shape. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom during the summer months.

The orchid is noted for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
