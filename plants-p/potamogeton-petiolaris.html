Potamogeton petiolaris

Potamogeton petiolaris, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pondweed, is a submerged aquatic plant native to North America.

The plant produces small, green flowers that are clustered in spikes, and it reproduces both sexually and asexually through rhizomes.
