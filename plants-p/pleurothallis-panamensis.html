Pleurothallis panamensis

Pleurothallis panamensis, commonly known as the Panama orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Central America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, tropical environments. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a subtle fragrance, often blooming during the rainy season. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Pleurothallis panamensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
