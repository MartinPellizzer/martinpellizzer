Peperomia bernouillii

Peperomia bernouillii, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically silver or gray-green in color, with a textured surface that helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia bernouillii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
