Panicum oaxacense

Panicum oaxacense, commonly known as the Oaxaca grass, is a perennial grass species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
