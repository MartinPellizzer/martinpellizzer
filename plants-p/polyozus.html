Polyozus

Polyozus, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. Due to its rapid growth and invasive tendencies, it can become a weed in agricultural areas.
