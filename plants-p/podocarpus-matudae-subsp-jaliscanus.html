Podocarpus matudae subsp. jaliscanus

Podocarpus matudae subsp.

jaliscanus, commonly known as the Jaliscan Podocarp, is a small evergreen tree native to the Mexican states of Jalisco and Nayarit. It belongs to the Podocarpaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, cone-like structures. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in montane forests.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in horticulture due to its compact size and attractive appearance.
