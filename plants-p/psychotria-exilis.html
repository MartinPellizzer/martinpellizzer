Psychotria exilis

Psychotria exilis, commonly known as the "Cocaine Plant," is a species of flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly the Amazon basin. The plant is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white or pale green flowers. It contains alkaloids, including cocaine, which are found in its leaves.
