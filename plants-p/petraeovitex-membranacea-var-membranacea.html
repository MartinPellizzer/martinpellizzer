Petraeovitex membranacea var. membranacea

Petraeovitex membranacea var. membranacea, commonly known as the membranaceae variant of petraeovitex, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, woody stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by small, round fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and horticultural use.
