Phillyrea medianifolia subsp. luciae

Phillyrea medianifolia subsp.

luciae, commonly known as the Lucie's Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green.

The plant produces clusters of white, tubular flowers that bloom in spring. Its fruit is a small, round drupe that turns red when mature. Phillyrea medianifolia subsp.

luciae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
