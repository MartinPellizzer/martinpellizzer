Peperomia acrostigma

Peperomia acrostigma, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to South America.

It features dense, waxy, velvety foliage that ranges in color from deep green to reddish-brown, depending on light exposure. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique leaf texture and low maintenance requirements contribute to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
