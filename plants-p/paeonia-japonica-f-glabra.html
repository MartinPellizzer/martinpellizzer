Paeonia japonica f. glabra

Paeonia japonica f. glabra, commonly known as the Japanese peony with smooth leaves, is a cultivar of the peony plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous foliage, which distinguishes it from other peony varieties. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of pink, white, and red, typically blooming in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in traditional Japanese horticulture.
