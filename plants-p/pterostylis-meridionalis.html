Pterostylis meridionalis

Pterostylis meridionalis, commonly known as the southern spider-orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It features a slender, leafless stem with a single, narrow, lance-shaped leaf. The plant produces a single, intricate flower with a dark, hairy labellum and a yellowish-green column. It blooms during late winter to early spring, typically between June and August.

This orchid thrives in shaded, humid environments, often growing on the trunks of rainforest trees.
