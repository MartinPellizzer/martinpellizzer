Pilea schimpffii

Pilea schimpffii, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are round and slightly wavy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by spathes, which are leaf-like structures enclosing the true flowers. It is a popular indoor plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.

Pilea schimpffii prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for a variety of indoor environments.
