Peucedanum lanceolatum

Peucedanum lanceolatum, commonly known as narrowleaf cowparsley, is a flowering plant in the carrot family (Apiaceae).

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in grasslands and along roadsides. The plant features slender, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers in umbels. Its seeds are small and have a distinctive, aromatic scent.

It is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
