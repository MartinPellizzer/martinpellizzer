Pleione × kingdonwardii

Pleione × kingdonwardii, commonly known as the Kingdonward's pleione, is a hybrid orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces striking, showy flowers with a wide range of colors, including white, pink, and yellow. The plant typically blooms in late winter or early spring, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its leaves are usually dark green and strap-like, while its roots are fibrous and adapted to its high-altitude habitat.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and ease of care, making it a favorite among orchid enthusiasts.
