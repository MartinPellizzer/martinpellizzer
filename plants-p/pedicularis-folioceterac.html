Pedicularis folioceterac

Pedicularis folioceterac, commonly known as the leafy spurred orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and often clustered at the base, contributing to its common name.

It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.
