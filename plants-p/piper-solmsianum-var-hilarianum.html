Piper solmsianum var. hilarianum

Piper solmsianum var. hilarianum, commonly known as the Hilarian pepper plant, is a tropical vine native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes numerous species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. It produces small, peppery fruits that are used in traditional medicine and cuisine.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is cultivated in some tropical regions.
