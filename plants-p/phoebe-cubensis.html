Phoebe cubensis

Phoebe cubensis, commonly known as Cuban phoebe, is a small evergreen tree native to the Caribbean, particularly Cuba and the Bahamas.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant has simple, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green, often with a slight sheen. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by berries that are edible for some bird species.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often planted in gardens for its dense foliage and adaptability to coastal environments.
