Philodendron rubrijuvenile

Philodendron rubrijuvenile, commonly known as the red-veined philodendron, is a tropical vine native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves that often display striking red and green veins. The plant typically grows in a climbing habit, using its aerial roots to attach to supports. Its foliage is highly ornamental, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and humidity to maintain its vibrant appearance.
