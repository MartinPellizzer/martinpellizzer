Pentacrophys wrightii

It is characterized by its distinctive five leaves that emerge from a single point, forming a rosette-like structure. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in swampy areas or along the edges of wetlands.
