Peltochlaena nephrodioides

Peltochlaena nephrodioides, commonly known as the kidney-leaf fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, kidney-shaped fronds that are often deeply lobed and have a glossy appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is commonly found in rainforests. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help anchor it to rocky or moist soil substrates.

The foliage is not only ornamental but also plays a role in the ecological dynamics of its habitat.
