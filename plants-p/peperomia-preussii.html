Peperomia preussii

Peperomia preussii, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal water needs.

Peperomia preussii is prized for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice among houseplants.
