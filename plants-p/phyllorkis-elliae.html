Phyllorkis elliae

Phyllorkis elliae, commonly known as the Cape York rock fig, is a small, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of northern Australia.

It belongs to the Moraceae family, which includes figs and mulberries, and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences and is adapted to thrive in rocky, well-drained soils.
