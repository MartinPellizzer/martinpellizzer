Pycreus globosus var. erectus

Pycreus globosus var. erectus, commonly known as the upright globosus bellflower, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems that support its clusters of bell-shaped, violet-blue flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are slightly hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact growth habit and showy blooms make it a valuable addition to both garden and natural landscapes.
