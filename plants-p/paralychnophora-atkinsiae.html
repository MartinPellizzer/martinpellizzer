Paralychnophora atkinsiae

Paralychnophora atkinsiae, commonly known as the Atkinsia orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, intricate flowers with a unique coloration pattern. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions with partial shade. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that help it conserve water in its arid habitat.
