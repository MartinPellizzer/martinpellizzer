Philodendron goeldii

Philodendron goeldii, commonly known as the Goeldii philodendron, is a climbing plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
