Phyllanthus aubletianus

Phyllanthus aubletianus, commonly known as the African spurge, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, featuring simple, alternate leaves with a leathery texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruits are capsules that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

The plant is adapted to dry environments and is often found in savannas and rocky areas.
