Pasania parvula

Pasania parvula, commonly known as the small pasania, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters and features dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer, followed by bright red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Pasania parvula is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
