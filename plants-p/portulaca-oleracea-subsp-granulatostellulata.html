Portulaca oleracea subsp. granulatostellulata

Portulaca oleracea subsp.

granulatostellulata, commonly known as the starry purslane, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features fleshy, rounded leaves that are covered in fine, star-like protrusions, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the morning and close by midday. It thrives in hot, arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant.
