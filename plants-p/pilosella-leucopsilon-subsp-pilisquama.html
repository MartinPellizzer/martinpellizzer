Pilosella leucopsilon subsp. pilisquama

Pilosella leucopsilon subsp.

pilisquama, commonly known as the whitehead mouse-ear, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and small, daisy-like yellow flowers.

Native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa, this species thrives in rocky and sandy soils. The subspecies is distinguished by its pale, almost white, leaf surfaces and compact growth habit.

It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.
