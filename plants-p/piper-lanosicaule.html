Piper lanosicaule

Piper lanosicaule, commonly known as the hairy-stemmed pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by peppery, berry-like fruits.

Piper lanosicaule is often grown for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
