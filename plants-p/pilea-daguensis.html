Pilea daguensis

Pilea daguensis, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features oval, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea daguensis is known for its ease of care and ability to propagate through stem cuttings.
