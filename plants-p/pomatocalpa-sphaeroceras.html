Pomatocalpa sphaeroceras

Pomatocalpa sphaeroceras, commonly known as the globe orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, showy flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant produces a single, rounded pseudobulb that supports its foliage and flowering structures. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption in its natural habitat.

The species is noted for its unique floral structure and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
