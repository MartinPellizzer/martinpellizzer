Patrinia kozushimensis

Patrinia kozushimensis, commonly known as Japanese Patrinia, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, two-lipped flowers in clusters, typically in shades of purple or blue.

Patrinia kozushimensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
