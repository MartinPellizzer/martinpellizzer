Pilea nicholasii

Pilea nicholasii, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rosettes of glossy, round, dark green leaves that resemble coins, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Pilea nicholasii is known for its easy care and ability to purify indoor air.
