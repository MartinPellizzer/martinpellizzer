Peperomia larecajana var. angustifolia

Peperomia larecajana var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features small, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale green to white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
