Pultenaea daphnoides var. obcordata

Pultenaea daphnoides var. obcordata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pultenaea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features slender, aromatic leaves that are typically obcordate in shape, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pale yellow to reddish in color, blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and open forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to local wildlife.
