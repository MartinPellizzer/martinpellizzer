Peperomia naranjoana var. brevipetiola

Peperomia naranjoana var. brevipetiola, commonly known as the compact peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Central and South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish or purple tint on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
