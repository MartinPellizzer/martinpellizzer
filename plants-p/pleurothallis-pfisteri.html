Pleurothallis pfisteri

Pleurothallis pfisteri, commonly known as the Pfister's pleurothallis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America. The plant typically grows on trees, using them for support rather than deriving nutrients from them. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, often in shades of white, yellow, or green.

It is appreciated for its delicate appearance and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
