Peperomia portulacoides var. perrottetiana

Peperomia portulacoides var. perrottetiana, commonly known as the Perrottet's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, often in shades of white or pale pink. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and drought tolerance.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for collectors and houseplants enthusiasts.
