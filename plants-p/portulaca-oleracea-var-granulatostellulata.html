Portulaca oleracea var. granulatostellulata

Portulaca oleracea var. granulatostellulata, commonly known as the starry purslane, is a variant of the common purslane, characterized by its distinctive star-shaped flowers.

It is an annual herb that thrives in warm, sunny environments and is often found in disturbed soils. The plant produces small, fleshy leaves and vibrant yellow or orange flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It is valued for its edible leaves and seeds, which are used in various culinary traditions.

Its botanical features make it a hardy and adaptable species within the Portulacaceae family.
