Pinus tabuliformis var. densata

Pinus tabuliformis var. densata, commonly known as the dense-flowered pine, is a variant of the Chinese pine characterized by its dense, compact foliage.

It is native to China and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soils. The tree has a conical shape with short, stout needles that are typically dark green in color. Its cones are small and woody, containing seeds that are dispersed by wind.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often planted in gardens and landscapes for its aesthetic qualities.
