Parafaujasia mauritiana

Parafaujasia mauritiana, commonly known as the Mauritius star orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to the island of Mauritius.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers with a vibrant purple and white coloration. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are strap-shaped and evergreen, while its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the rich, moist soil.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is protected in its native region.
