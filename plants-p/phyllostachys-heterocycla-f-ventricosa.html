Phyllostachys heterocycla f. ventricosa

Phyllostachys heterocycla f. ventricosa, commonly known as the thick-stemmed bamboo, is a clumping bamboo species native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its robust, thick-walled stems that are often used in construction and as a decorative plant. The plant produces long, slender leaves that are usually a deep green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it adaptable to various garden environments.

Its rapid growth and ornamental value make it a popular choice for landscaping and bamboo cultivation.
