Phalaenopsis lowii f. alba

Phalaenopsis lowii f. alba, commonly known as the white phalaenopsis orchid, is a hybrid orchid species characterized by its elegant, white flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia, including regions like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is an epiphytic orchid, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, typically in humid tropical environments. Its foliage consists of broad, dark green leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern.

The flowers are long-lasting, often blooming multiple times a year, making it a popular choice for ornamental purposes.
