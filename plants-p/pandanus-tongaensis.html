Pandanus tongaensis

Pandanus tongaensis, commonly known as the Tongan pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pandanaceae.

It is native to the South Pacific, particularly found in Tonga and other nearby islands. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically clustered at the base of the leaves.

The plant thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to saline and windy conditions.
