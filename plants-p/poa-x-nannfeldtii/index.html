Poa × Nannfeldtii

Poa × nannfeldtii is a naturally occurring hybrid grass species that results from the cross between Poa annua and Poa pratensis. It is commonly found in temperate regions where these parent species overlap in habitat. This plant is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in both sunny and partially shaded conditions. Its fine texture and dense growth make it a popular choice for lawn and turfgrass applications. Despite its practical uses, Poa × nannfeldtii remains a subject of interest for botanists studying plant hybridization and genetic diversity.
