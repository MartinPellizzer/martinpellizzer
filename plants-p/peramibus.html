Peramibus

Peramibus, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plants typically grow in humid, shaded environments and require well-draining soil to thrive. Peramibus species are popular houseplants due to their ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
