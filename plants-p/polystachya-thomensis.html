Polystachya thomensis

Polystachya thomensis, commonly known as the Tomonga orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically features long, slender leaves and is adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Polystachya thomensis is part of the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Its unique floral structure and habitat preferences make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
