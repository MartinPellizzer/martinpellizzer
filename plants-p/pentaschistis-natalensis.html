Pentaschistis natalensis

Pentaschistis natalensis, commonly known as Natal star-thistle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, star-shaped yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has a compact, bushy appearance. Its leaves are usually narrow and hairy, adapted to reduce water loss in arid environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
