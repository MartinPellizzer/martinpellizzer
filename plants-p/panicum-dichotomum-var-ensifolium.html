Panicum dichotomum var. ensifolium

Panicum dichotomum var. ensifolium, commonly known as the swordleaf panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are often glaucous in appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, two-lobed florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a common ornamental and forage grass.

Its rapid growth and drought tolerance contribute to its adaptability in various ecological settings.
