Phreatia sphaerocarpa

Phreatia sphaerocarpa, commonly known as the globular orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, rounded pseudobulbs and long, arching inflorescences that bear clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, organic-rich substrates. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and surrounding environment.

Due to its striking appearance and specific cultivation needs, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
