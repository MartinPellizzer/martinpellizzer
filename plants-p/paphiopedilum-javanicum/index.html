Paphiopedilum Javanicum

Paphiopedilum javanicum is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the tropical regions of Indonesia. Known for its striking, velvety petals and intricate floral structure, it stands out among other orchid species. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific care to flourish. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it faces threats from habitat loss and over-collection.
