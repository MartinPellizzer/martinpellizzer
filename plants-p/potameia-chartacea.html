Potameia chartacea

Potameia chartacea, commonly known as the paperleaf pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its distinctive, chartaceously thin, papery leaves that are often blue-green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded conditions.

Potameia chartacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscapes.
