Pourthiaea laevis var. crassiuscula

Pourthiaea laevis var. crassiuscula, commonly known as the thick-leaved pourthiaea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically a deep green color, often with a waxy texture to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
