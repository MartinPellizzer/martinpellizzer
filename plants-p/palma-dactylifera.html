Palma dactylifera

Palma dactylifera, commonly known as the date palm, is a tall, perennial tree native to the arid regions of the Middle East and North Africa.

It produces clusters of date fruits, which are rich in natural sugars and nutrients. The plant has a robust trunk with a fibrous, spongy core called the "palm heart.". Its leaves are long, fan-shaped, and can reach up to 4 meters in length.

The date palm is well adapted to hot, dry climates and has been cultivated for thousands of years.
