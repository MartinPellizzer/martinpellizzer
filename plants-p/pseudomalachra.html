Pseudomalachra

Pseudomalachra, commonly known as the ghost orchid, is a rare and enigmatic plant native to the wetlands of northern Australia.

The plant lacks leaves and instead relies on its roots to absorb nutrients from the surrounding water. Pseudomalachra thrives in moist, swampy environments and is often found growing on the roots of other plants.
