Plantago Maritima Subsp. Crassifolia

Plantago maritima subsp. crassifolia is a hardy plant commonly found in coastal areas and saline environments. It thrives in sandy or muddy soils where other plants struggle to survive. This subspecies is known for its broad, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in harsh conditions. It plays an important role in stabilizing shorelines and supporting local ecosystems. Despite its tough environment, it remains a resilient and adaptable species.
