Peperomia playapampana

Peperomia playapampana, commonly known as the Playa Pampanga Peperomia, is a species native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often glossy and green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light, making it a suitable houseplant for indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
