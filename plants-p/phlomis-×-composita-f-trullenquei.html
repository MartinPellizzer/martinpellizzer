Phlomis × composita f. trullenquei

Phlomis × composita f. trullenquei, commonly known as the Trullenquei phlomis, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and produces showy, yellow-orange composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves with a woolly texture, and it is often found in rocky or gravelly soils. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in full sun.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in garden settings.
