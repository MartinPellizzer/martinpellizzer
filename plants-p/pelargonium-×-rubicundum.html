Pelargonium × rubicundum

Pelargonium × rubicundum, commonly known as the Zebra plant, is a hybrid succulent native to South Africa.

It features striking, fleshy, ribbed leaves with a distinctive zebra-like pattern of light and dark green. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink or white. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
