Prunus pseudocerasus f. hortensis

Prunus pseudocerasus f. hortensis, commonly known as the Chinese date plum or sour plum, is a cultivar of the Prunus genus native to China.

It is a small to medium-sized deciduous tree that produces small, yellowish-green fruits with a tart flavor. The plant is characterized by its simple, ovate leaves and clusters of small white flowers that bloom in early spring. The fruit is typically harvested in late summer and is used in traditional Chinese medicine and culinary applications.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its edible, nutrient-rich fruit.
