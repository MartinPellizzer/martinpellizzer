Polyscias corticata

Polyscias corticata, commonly known as the satin leaf or velvetleaf, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Polyscias corticata is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
