Pisum sativum convar. humile

Pisum sativum convar. humile, commonly known as the dwarf pea, is a botanical variant of the common pea.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and shorter stems compared to the standard pea variety. The plant produces small, pea-shaped pods that contain one to three seeds. It is often cultivated as a garden plant due to its ornamental value and ease of growth.

This variety is also used in some traditional cuisines for its edible seeds.
