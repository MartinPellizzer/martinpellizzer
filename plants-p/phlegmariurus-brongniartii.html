Phlegmariurus brongniartii

It is characterized by its distinctive, branching fronds that are covered with a dense layer of rhizomes, which help anchor it to tree trunks or rocky surfaces. This fern is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited for hanging baskets or as a decorative epiphyte.
