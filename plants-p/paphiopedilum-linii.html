Paphiopedilum linii

Paphiopedilum linii, commonly known as the Chinese mantis orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive lip that resembles a mantis, hence its common name. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a deep red or purple lip.

Paphiopedilum linii thrives in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
