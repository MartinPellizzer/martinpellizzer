Panicum basisetum

Panicum basisetum, commonly known as the slender panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its fine, delicate foliage and slender, upright growth habit. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its inflorescence consists of a narrow, cylindrical spikelet that matures into a fluffy, wind-dispersed seed head.

Panicum basisetum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and restoration projects.
