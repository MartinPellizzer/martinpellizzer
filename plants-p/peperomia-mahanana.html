Peperomia mahanana

Peperomia mahanana, commonly known as the Mahanana peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia mahanana is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
