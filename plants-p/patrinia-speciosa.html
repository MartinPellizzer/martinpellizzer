Patrinia speciosa

Patrinia speciosa, commonly known as the beautiful patrinia, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and clusters of small, pale blue or purple flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Patrinia speciosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
