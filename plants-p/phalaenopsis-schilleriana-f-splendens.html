Phalaenopsis schilleriana f. splendens

Phalaenopsis schilleriana f. splendens, commonly known as the Splendens Phalaenopsis, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.

It features dense, velvety petals in shades of pink, white, and purple, often with intricate patterns. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, typically in warm, humid environments. Its flowers can last for several months, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

The foliage consists of broad, dark green leaves that contribute to its ornamental appeal.
