Persea sphaerocarpa

Persea sphaerocarpa, commonly known as the globe avocado, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Mexico and Central America.

It produces large, round fruits that are similar in appearance to avocados but have a distinct, waxy skin. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and produces small, fragrant white flowers. Its fruit contains a single large seed surrounded by a creamy, edible pulp.

The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and has limited commercial cultivation due to its specific climate requirements.
