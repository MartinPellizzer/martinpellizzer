Psilogramme lechleri

Psilogramme lechleri, commonly known as the rubber tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves and produces a milky latex sap that is used to make natural rubber. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and can grow up to 30 meters tall. Its bark and latex have been traditionally used for medicinal and industrial purposes.

The tree thrives in humid, warm climates with rich, well-drained soil.
