Potentilla × burnatii

Potentilla × burnatii, commonly known as the Burnatii cinquefoil, is a hybrid shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. The plant features small, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated in horticulture.
