Phyllanthus obliquus var. mollis

Phyllanthus obliquus var. mollis, commonly known as the smooth-leafed phyllanthus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is native to parts of Asia, including India and Southeast Asia, and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant is characterized by its smooth, elliptical leaves and small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. The plant is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
