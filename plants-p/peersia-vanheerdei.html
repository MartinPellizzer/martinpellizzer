Peersia vanheerdei

Peersia vanheerdei, commonly known as the Vanheerde's pebbleplant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, stone-like leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.

Peersia vanheerdei prefers well-draining soil and full sun exposure.
