Polycline proteiformis

Polycline proteiformis, commonly known as the variable proteiform, is a unique plant species characterized by its morphological plasticity.

It exhibits a wide range of growth forms, adapting to different environmental conditions, which makes it challenging to classify. The plant's leaves can vary in shape, size, and texture depending on its habitat. It is often found in arid and semi-arid regions, where it thrives in poor soil conditions.

Its botanical significance lies in its ability to demonstrate adaptive evolution in response to environmental stressors.
