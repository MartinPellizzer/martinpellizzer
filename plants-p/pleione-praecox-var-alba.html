Pleione praecox var. alba

Pleione praecox var. alba, commonly known as the white early pleione, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, slender stems and large, showy white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, providing a lush green backdrop to its delicate flowers.

This variety is prized for its beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its ornamental value.
