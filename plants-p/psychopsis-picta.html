Psychopsis picta

Psychopsis picta, commonly known as the picta orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that display a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and red, often with intricate patterns. The plant has a short, stocky stem and produces a single spike of flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to the cool, moist environments it inhabits.

Psychopsis picta is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
