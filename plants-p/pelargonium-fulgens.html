Pelargonium fulgens

Pelargonium fulgens, commonly known as the flame geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from red to orange, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant has succulent, hairy leaves that are typically lobed or toothed, and it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking appearance and ability to attract pollinators.

Pelargonium fulgens is also valued for its resilience and ease of care in garden settings.
