Peperomia esperanzana

Peperomia esperanzana, commonly known as the Esperanza Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia esperanzana is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
