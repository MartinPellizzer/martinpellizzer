Piper capillipes

Piper capillipes, commonly known as the slender pipevine, is a tropical climbing plant belonging to the Piperaceae family.

It is native to regions of Southeast Asia, including countries like India, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its slender, vine-like stems and heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in spikes, while the fruit is a three-lobed capsule containing tiny seeds.

Piper capillipes is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
