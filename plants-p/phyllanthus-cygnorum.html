Phyllanthus cygnorum

Phyllanthus cygnorum, commonly known as the swan plant, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a shrub characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are often glossy and elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that are typically surrounded by modified leaves called bracts. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous tiny seeds.

Phyllanthus cygnorum is native to tropical regions and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
