Pratia atacamensis

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae and is characterized by its small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to survive in extremely dry conditions, with a compact growth form and reduced leaf surfaces. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.
