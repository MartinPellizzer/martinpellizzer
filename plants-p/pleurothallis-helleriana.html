Pleurothallis helleriana

Pleurothallis helleriana, commonly known as the Heller's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the Andean regions of South America.

This epiphytic plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. It features small, delicate flowers that are often white or pale yellow, with intricate patterns on the petals. The plant has linear, narrow leaves that help it conserve moisture in its natural habitat.

It is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
