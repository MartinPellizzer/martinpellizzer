Potamogeton turionifer

Potamogeton turionifer, commonly known as the turion-bearing pondweed, is a submerged aquatic plant found in freshwater environments.

It belongs to the family Potamogetonaceae and is characterized by its long, narrow leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant produces turions, which are overwintering buds that help it survive in colder conditions. These turions can detach and float to new areas, aiding in the plant's spread.

Potamogeton turionifer plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.
