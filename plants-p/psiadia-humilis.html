Psiadia humilis

Psiadia humilis, commonly known as the dwarf heliotrope, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing in clusters. The plant has hairy, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Psiadia humilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
