Panicum spadiferum

Panicum spadiferum, commonly known as the purple panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and clusters of purple or pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets that are often adorned with long, slender bristles.

Panicum spadiferum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its vibrant color and graceful appearance.
