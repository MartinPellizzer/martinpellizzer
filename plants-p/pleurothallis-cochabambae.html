Pleurothallis cochabambae

Pleurothallis cochabambae, commonly known as the Cochabamba orchid, is a species of orchid native to Bolivia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on trees or rocks in the Andean region. Its flowers are small and clustered, with a unique structure that includes a prominent lip.

This orchid is of interest to botanists due to its specific ecological requirements and limited distribution.
