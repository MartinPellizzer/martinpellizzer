Periploca gracilis

Periploca gracilis, commonly known as the slender periploca, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several small, dark seeds.

Periploca gracilis is often found in rocky or coastal areas, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
