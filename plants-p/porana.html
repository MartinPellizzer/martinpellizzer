Porana

Porana, commonly known as the "Blue Bottle" or "Bluebell," is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a velvety texture.

Porana is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its traditional medicinal uses in some cultures.
