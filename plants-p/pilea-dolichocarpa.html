Pilea dolichocarpa

Pilea dolichocarpa, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions of China, Japan, and the Philippines. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are borne on a spadix, typical of the Araceae family.

Pilea dolichocarpa is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
