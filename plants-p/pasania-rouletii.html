Pasania rouletii

Pasania rouletii, commonly known as the African violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are often velvety in texture and come in various shades of green. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, which bloom in clusters at the ends of its stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light, making it a popular houseplant.

Its botanical characteristics make it both ornamental and relatively easy to care for in indoor environments.
