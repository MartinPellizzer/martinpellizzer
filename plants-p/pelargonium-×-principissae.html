Pelargonium × principissae

Pelargonium × principissae, commonly known as the Princess geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The leaves are usually lobed and have a slightly hairy texture, adding to its ornamental appeal. This plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is often cultivated as a bedding plant or in containers due to its adaptability and aesthetic value.
