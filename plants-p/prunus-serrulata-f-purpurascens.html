Prunus serrulata f. purpurascens

Prunus serrulata f. purpurascens, commonly known as the pink-flowered cherry blossom, is a cultivar of the Japanese cherry tree.

It is characterized by its striking pink to pale red flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a dramatic visual effect to gardens and landscapes. The plant is a deciduous shrub or small tree, typically reaching heights of 4 to 8 meters. Its leaves are oval-shaped with serrated edges, and they turn a vibrant red in the fall.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its seasonal floral display.
