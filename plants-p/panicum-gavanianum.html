Panicum gavanianum

Panicum gavanianum, commonly known as Gavan's bluestem, is a grass species native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and produces dense, cylindrical inflorescences that are often blue-green in color. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has narrow, linear leaves that are often hairy along the edges.

Panicum gavanianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping or as a stabilizing plant in erosion-prone areas.
