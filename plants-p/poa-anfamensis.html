Poa anfamensis

Poa anfamensis, commonly known as the Anfamensian grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of southern Africa, particularly in Namibia and South Africa. The plant is characterized by its tufted growth habit, with slender, wiry stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, spikelet-bearing inflorescences that are typically green or reddish in color.

Poa anfamensis is adapted to harsh, arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
