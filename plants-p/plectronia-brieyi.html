Plectronia brieyi

Plectronia brieyi, commonly known as the golden mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wetlands and along stream banks, preferring moist, sandy soils. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.
