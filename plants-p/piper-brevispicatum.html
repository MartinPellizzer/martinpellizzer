Piper brevispicatum

Piper brevispicatum, commonly known as the short-spined pepper vine, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its small, ovate leaves and clusters of tiny, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns red when mature.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
