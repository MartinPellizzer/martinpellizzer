Pleopeltis werneri

Pleopeltis werneri, commonly known as the Werneri's rock fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The fronds are typically leathery and have a dense, overlapping arrangement, which helps the plant retain moisture. The plant's unique morphology and ecological adaptations make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
