Pandanus delessertii

Pandanus delessertii, commonly known as the Madagascar pandanus, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically clustered in spadices. Its roots are used in local medicine for their purported healing properties.

It thrives in wet, coastal environments and is adapted to withstand salt spray.
