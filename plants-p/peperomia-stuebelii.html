Peperomia stuebelii

Peperomia stuebelii, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to Brazil.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery-white underside, giving it a striking, almost metallic appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia stuebelii is a low-maintenance houseplant that is well-suited for indoor environments.
