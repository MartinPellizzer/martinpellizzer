Phaeneilema oliganthum

Phaeneilema oliganthum, commonly known as the small-flowered phaeneilema, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
