Peperomia syringifolia

Peperomia syringifolia, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery underside, giving it a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white or pink, during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
