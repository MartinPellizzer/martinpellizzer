Pyrus ovoidea

Pyrus ovoidea, commonly known as the oval-shaped pear.

It is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae, native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. The plant is characterized by its small, oval-shaped fruits that are typically yellow or green when mature. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a serrated margin.
