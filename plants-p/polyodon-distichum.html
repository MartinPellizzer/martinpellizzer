Polyodon distichum

Polyodon distichum, commonly known as the two-ranked water plant, is a perennial aquatic plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its opposite, linear leaves that are typically submerged or floating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in shallow, still or slow-moving freshwater habitats.

Polyodon distichum plays a role in stabilizing aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
