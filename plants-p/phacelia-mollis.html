Phacelia mollis

Phacelia mollis, commonly known as soft phacelia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It is often found in regions with mild, Mediterranean climates.

Phacelia mollis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
