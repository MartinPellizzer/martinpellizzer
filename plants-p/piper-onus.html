Piper onus

Piper onus, commonly known as the velvet leaf pepper, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. Its small, red berries are edible and have a peppery flavor.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
