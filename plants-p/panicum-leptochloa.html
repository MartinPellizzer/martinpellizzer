Panicum leptochloa

Panicum leptochloa, commonly known as slender grass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It is often found in areas with well-drained soil and can tolerate moderate drought conditions.

Its botanical features make it a valuable component of ecological studies and potential for use in restoration projects.
