Pleione Nervosa

Pleione nervosa is a rare and exquisite orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Asia. It is known for its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. This plant thrives in cool, shaded environments and requires specific humidity levels to flourish. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Due to habitat loss, Pleione nervosa is considered endangered, highlighting the need for protective measures.
