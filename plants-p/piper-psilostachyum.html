Piper psilostachyum

Piper psilostachyum, commonly known as the black pepper vine, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, green berries that turn dark when mature, containing the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and cuisine. Its leaves are often used in herbal remedies due to their medicinal properties.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for both its ornamental and practical uses.
