Polystichum × ongataense var. utsumii

Polystichum × ongataense var. utsumii, commonly known as the Utsu-mi Japanese shield fern, is a hybrid fern species native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its large, leathery, and deeply lobed fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its fronds are often arching and can reach lengths of up to 1 meter.

The varietal form utsumii is distinguished by its unique frond morphology and coloration, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
