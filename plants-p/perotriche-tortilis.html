Perotriche tortilis

Perotriche tortilis, commonly known as the curled perotis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous annual that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, pale blue or white flowers. Its seeds are enclosed in a distinctive, spiral-shaped capsule that opens to release the seeds.
