Peperomia subpetiolata

Peperomia subpetiolata, commonly known as the Shiny Leaf Peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, followed by reddish-brown berries. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and drought tolerance.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for tabletop displays and hanging baskets.
