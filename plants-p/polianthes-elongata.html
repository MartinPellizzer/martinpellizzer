Polianthes elongata

Polianthes elongata, commonly known as the spider lily, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and the southern United States.

It is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, strap-like leaves and striking, white or pale pink flowers that resemble spiders. The bulbs are toxic, so caution is advised when handling the plant.
