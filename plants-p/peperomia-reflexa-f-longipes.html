Peperomia reflexa f. longipes

Peperomia reflexa f. longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed peperomia, is a compact, low-growing perennial plant native to tropical regions.

It features succulent, ovate leaves with a waxy texture and a distinctive reddish or bronze hue on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique leaf coloration and compact growth make it a popular choice for container gardening and as a decorative houseplant.
