Phragmipedium schlimii var. schlimii

Phragmipedium schlimii var. schlimii, commonly known as the Schlim's phragmipedium, is a species of orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and green. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with multiple flowers, each featuring a distinctive lip that is often marked with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
