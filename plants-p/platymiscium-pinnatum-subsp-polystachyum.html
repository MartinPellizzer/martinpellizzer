Platymiscium pinnatum subsp. polystachyum

Platymiscium pinnatum subsp.

polystachyum, commonly known as the broad-leafed evergreen, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its large, pinnately compound leaves that are often glossy and dark green.

The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release seeds when mature.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
