Potentilla taurica f. callieri

Potentilla taurica f. callieri, commonly known as the Caucasian potentilla, is a shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It is often found in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
