Phellodendron chinense var. yunnanense

Phellodendron chinense var. yunnanense, commonly known as Yunnan phellodendron, is a deciduous tree native to southwestern China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark that exudes a yellowish resin. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by elliptical, dark green leaves. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds, and it thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil.

The species is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional Chinese medicine.
