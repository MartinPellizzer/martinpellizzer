Petamenes splendens

Petamenes splendens, commonly known as the shining petamene, is a flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from bright yellow to deep orange. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to the trunks of trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and glossy, often with a waxy texture that helps it retain moisture.

Petamenes splendens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden environments.
