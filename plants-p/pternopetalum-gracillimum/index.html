Pternopetalum Gracillimum

Pternopetalum gracillimum is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. It is known for its striking, pale yellow blooms that appear in late spring. This species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories. Due to its limited habitat and slow growth, it is considered endangered by conservationists. Its unique beauty and ecological importance make it a subject of both scientific study and botanical fascination.
