Pelargonium spectabile

Pelargonium spectabile, commonly known as the showy geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red and white. The plant features upright, branching stems and heart-shaped, velvety leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Pelargonium spectabile is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
