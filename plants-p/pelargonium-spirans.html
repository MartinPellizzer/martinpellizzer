Pelargonium spirans

Pelargonium spirans, commonly known as the spiral geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiraling arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Pelargonium spirans is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
