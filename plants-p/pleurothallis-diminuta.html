Pleurothallis diminuta

Pleurothallis diminuta, commonly known as the dwarf pleurothallis, is a small orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the understory of rainforests. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences.

Due to its size and beauty, it is a popular choice for terrariums and miniature orchid collections.
