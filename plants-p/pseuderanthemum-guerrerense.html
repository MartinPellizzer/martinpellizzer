Pseuderanthemum guerrerense

Pseuderanthemum guerrerense, commonly known as the Guerrerense false jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. Pseuderanthemum guerrerense is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
