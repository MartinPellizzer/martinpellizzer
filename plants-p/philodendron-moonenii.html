Philodendron moonenii

Philodendron moonenii, commonly known as the Moonlight Philodendron, is a climbing plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical plant collections.
