Polygonum omerostromum

Polygonum omerostromum, commonly known as redroot smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its reddish stems and lance-shaped leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, ditches, and along stream banks.

Polygonum omerostromum is a member of the Polygonaceae family, known for its distinctive bead-like fruits.
