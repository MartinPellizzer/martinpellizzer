Pteris tokioi

Pteris tokioi, commonly known as the Tokyo water fern, is a species of fern native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, frilly fronds that are typically light green with a slightly glossy texture. The plant thrives in humid environments and prefers indirect sunlight, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its rhizomes spread horizontally, allowing it to form dense colonies in moist, shaded areas.

Pteris tokioi is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to purify air in indoor spaces.
