Pachystela argyrophylla

Pachystela argyrophylla, commonly known as silverleaf pachystela, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
