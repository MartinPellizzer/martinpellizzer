Pinanga subintegra var. multifida

Pinanga subintegra var. multifida, commonly known as the multifid palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves that give it a distinctive, feathery appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The multifid palm is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
