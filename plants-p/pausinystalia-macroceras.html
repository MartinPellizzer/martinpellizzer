Pausinystalia macroceras

Pausinystalia macroceras, commonly known as the giant arrowroot, is a striking member of the Araceae family native to the tropical regions of South America.

This plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to several feet in diameter, providing a dramatic visual presence. The inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The plant's unique morphology and vibrant foliage make it a popular choice for tropical gardens and greenhouses.
