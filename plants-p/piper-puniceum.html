Piper puniceum

Piper puniceum, commonly known as the scarlet pepper plant, is a member of the Piperaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its vibrant red berries, which are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a spicy aroma.

Piper puniceum thrives in humid, warm climates and is often grown as an ornamental plant.
