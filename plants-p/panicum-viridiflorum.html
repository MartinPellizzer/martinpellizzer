Panicum viridiflorum

Panicum viridiflorum, commonly known as greenflower panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a fibrous texture.

Panicum viridiflorum is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to various soil conditions.
