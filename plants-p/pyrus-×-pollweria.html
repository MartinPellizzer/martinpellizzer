Pyrus × pollweria

Pyrus × pollweria, commonly known as the hybrid pear.

It is a cross between Pyrus communis (European pear) and Pyrus ussuriensis (Manchurian pear). This hybrid is valued for its cold hardiness and disease resistance. The plant typically produces medium-sized, sweet fruits with a firm texture.

It is often cultivated in temperate climates for both ornamental and edible purposes.
