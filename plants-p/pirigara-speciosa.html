Pirigara speciosa

Pirigara speciosa, commonly known as the showy pirigara, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, colorful flowers that range from red to orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to two meters. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

Pirigara speciosa thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
