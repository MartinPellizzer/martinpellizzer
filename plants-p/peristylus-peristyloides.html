Peristylus peristyloides

Peristylus peristyloides, commonly known as the clustered peristylus, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a low-growing, mat-forming habit with small, narrow leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in dense clusters.

It thrives in dry, sunny conditions and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.
