Premna oblongifolia var. angustata

Premna oblongifolia var. angustata, commonly known as narrow-leaf Premna, is a flowering plant species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves that are often slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale green flowers that are typically borne on short pedicels. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The leaves are used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
