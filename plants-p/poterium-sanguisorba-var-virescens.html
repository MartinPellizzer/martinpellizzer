Poterium sanguisorba var. virescens

Poterium sanguisorba var. virescens, commonly known as redroot sandwort, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It features upright stems that are typically reddish in color, especially at the base, and bear clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and other aquatic habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species for ecological studies and wetland restoration projects.
