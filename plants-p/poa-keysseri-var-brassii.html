Poa keysseri var. brassii

Poa keysseri var. brassii, commonly known as the silver hair grass, is a perennial grass species native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-tipped leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments where it forms dense, low-growing mats. Its inflorescence consists of a single, narrow spikelet that is often covered in fine, hairy structures.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in harsh environments.
