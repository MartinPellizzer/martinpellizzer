Photinia zhejiangensis

Photinia zhejiangensis, commonly known as the Zhejiang photinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red or pink flowers that appear in late spring. The plant has elliptical leaves with serrated edges, and its bark is smooth and reddish-brown in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Due to its attractive foliage and flowering display, it is often used in landscaping and horticultural settings.
