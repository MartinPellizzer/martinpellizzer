Piptatherum blancheanum

Piptatherum blancheanum, commonly known as the Blancheanum grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant typically features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
