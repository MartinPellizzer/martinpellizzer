Picea jezoensis subsp. ajanensis

Picea jezoensis subsp.

ajanensis, commonly known as the Ajanensis fir, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to northeastern Asia. It is characterized by its slender, conical shape and dark green needle-like leaves.

The tree produces small, cylindrical cones that mature to a reddish-brown color. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in horticultural settings.
