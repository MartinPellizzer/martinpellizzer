Pleione ganchuenensis

Pleione ganchuenensis, commonly known as the Ganchuen orchid, is a rare and exquisite orchid species native to the high-altitude regions of Yunnan, China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows on rock faces or in mossy areas, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its rhizomes are fleshy and often covered in a thick layer of mycorrhizal fungi, which aid in nutrient absorption.

Due to its rarity and unique beauty, it is highly valued in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
