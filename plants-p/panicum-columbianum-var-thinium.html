Panicum columbianum var. thinium

Panicum columbianum var. thinium, commonly known as slender panicgrass, is a grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers.

This variety is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and resilience.
