Piper fragile

Piper fragile, commonly known as the brittle pepper, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by tiny, berry-like fruits. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

Piper fragile is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
