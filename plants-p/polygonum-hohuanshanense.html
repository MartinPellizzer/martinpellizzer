Polygonum hohuanshanense

Polygonum hohuanshanense, commonly known as the Hohuanshan knotweed, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its distinctive nodes and often zigzagging stems. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are lance-shaped with a smooth or slightly hairy surface, and it produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
