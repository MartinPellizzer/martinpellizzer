Polygonum × martinianum

Polygonum × martinianum, commonly known as redroot smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems that often turn reddish-purple as the plant matures. The leaves are lance-shaped, alternate, and have a slightly hairy texture. It produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, which bloom in late summer.

This plant is often found in moist soils along stream banks, wet meadows, and other wetland habitats.
