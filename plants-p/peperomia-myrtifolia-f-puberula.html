Peperomia myrtifolia f. puberula

Peperomia myrtifolia f. puberula, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with a velvety texture, often covered in fine, short hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
