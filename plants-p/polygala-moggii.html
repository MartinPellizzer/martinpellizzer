Polygala moggii

Polygala moggii, commonly known as the Malayan milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Polygala moggii is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
