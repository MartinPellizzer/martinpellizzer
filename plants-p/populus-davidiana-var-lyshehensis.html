Populus davidiana var. lyshehensis

Populus davidiana var. lyshehensis, commonly known as the Lyshehensis poplar, is a rare variant of the David's poplar native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind, aiding in its propagation. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
