Peperomia reflexa var. filiformis

Peperomia reflexa var. filiformis, commonly known as the threadleaf peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, elongated, pale green leaves that are often described as thread-like in appearance. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and ease of care make it an attractive addition to any plant collection.
