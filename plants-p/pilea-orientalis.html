Pilea orientalis

Pilea orientalis, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a popular houseplant native to China.

It features round, shield-shaped leaves that are glossy and succulent, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Pilea orientalis is easy to care for and often propagated through stem cuttings, making it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
