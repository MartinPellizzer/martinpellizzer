Physurus

Physurus, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to tropical Africa.

These plants are characterized by their rosette-shaped leaves and showy, often violet-colored flowers. They are popular houseplants due to their attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements. Physurus species typically thrive in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Their ability to bloom year-round makes them a favorite among indoor gardeners.
