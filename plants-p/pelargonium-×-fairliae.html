Pelargonium × fairliae

Pelargonium × fairliae, commonly known as the Fairlie's geranium, is a hybrid species within the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant produces small, rounded leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and its stems are often reddish in color. Native to South Africa, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom prolifically throughout the growing season.
