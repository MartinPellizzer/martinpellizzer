Premna tiliifolia

Premna tiliifolia, commonly known as Indian nettle or velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture on the upper surface. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in umbels and are followed by small, round berries.

Premna tiliifolia is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and along stream banks.
