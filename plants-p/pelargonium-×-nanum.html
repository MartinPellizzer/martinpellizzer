Pelargonium × nanum

Pelargonium × nanum, commonly known as the miniature geranium, is a hybrid species within the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors such as pink, red, and white. The plant typically features rounded, velvety leaves that are often marked with distinctive patterns. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for container gardening.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, Pelargonium × nanum is widely cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
