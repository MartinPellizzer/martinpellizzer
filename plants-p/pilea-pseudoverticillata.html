Pilea pseudoverticillata

Pilea pseudoverticillata, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and glossy, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea pseudoverticillata is known for its ease of care and ability to purify the air, enhancing its appeal as a decorative and functional plant.
