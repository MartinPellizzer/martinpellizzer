Pityrogramma Triangularis

Pityrogramma triangularis is a unique fern species known for its distinctive triangular fronds. Found primarily in tropical regions, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its delicate structure makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening. The plant's intricate leaf patterns add aesthetic value to any collection. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to maintain its health and vibrancy.
