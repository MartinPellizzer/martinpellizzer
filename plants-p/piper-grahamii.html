Piper grahamii

Piper grahamii, commonly known as Graham's pepper, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Queensland, Australia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into three-lobed, yellowish fruits.

Piper grahamii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
