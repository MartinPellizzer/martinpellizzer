Peperomia linaresii

Peperomia linaresii, commonly known as the Linares' peperomia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, waxy, ovate leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color.

Peperomia linaresii is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.
