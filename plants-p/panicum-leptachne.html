Panicum leptachne

Panicum leptachne, commonly known as slender panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as fields, roadsides, and disturbed areas. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny grains.

Panicum leptachne is often used in ecological studies due to its adaptability and role in soil stabilization.
