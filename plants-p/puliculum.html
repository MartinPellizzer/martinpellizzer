Puliculum

Puliculum, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can become invasive in certain regions.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological and agricultural studies.
