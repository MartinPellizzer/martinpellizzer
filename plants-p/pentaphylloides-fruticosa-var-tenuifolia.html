Pentaphylloides fruticosa var. tenuifolia

Pentaphylloides fruticosa var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pentaphylloides, is a shrub native to certain regions of southern Africa.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically narrow and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
