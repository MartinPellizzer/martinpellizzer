Pyrus schizomeles

Pyrus schizomeles, commonly known as the European whitebeam, is a deciduous tree native to Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its small, white flowers and edible, yellowish fruits. The leaves are alternate, simple, and have a serrated margin. It typically grows in woodland areas and thrives in well-drained soils.

The plant is notable for its ornamental value and historical significance in traditional medicine.
