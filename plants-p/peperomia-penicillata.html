Peperomia penicillata

Peperomia penicillata, commonly known as the shield fern peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green, shield-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in spikes, though they are not showy. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia penicillata is relatively low maintenance and well-suited for beginners due to its tolerance for moderate watering and humidity.
