Populus wuana

Populus wuana, commonly known as the Yunnan poplar, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Salicaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and smooth, gray bark. The plant produces catkin-like flowers in the spring, which are wind-pollinated. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its wood is lightweight and has been used traditionally for various purposes.
