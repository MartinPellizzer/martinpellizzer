Prestonia hirsuta

Prestonia hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy prestonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae, which includes milkweeds and other ornamental plants. The plant is characterized by its hairy, woody stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses in its native regions.
