Phillyrea spinosa

Phillyrea spinosa, commonly known as the Mediterranean privet, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in the spring, which are followed by small, round berries. Phillyrea spinosa is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in harsh environments.
