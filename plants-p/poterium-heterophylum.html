Poterium heterophylum

Poterium heterophylum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rock heath, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its small, needle-like leaves and clusters of pinkish-white flowers. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous areas. It has a compact growth habit and is well adapted to dry, sunny conditions.

Poterium heterophylum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and hardiness.
