Panicum heynei

Panicum heynei, commonly known as silver panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny grains of pollen. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

Panicum heynei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
