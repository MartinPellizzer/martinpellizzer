Perezia nitidifolia

Perezia nitidifolia, commonly known as the smoothleaf perezia, is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic compounds and umbellate inflorescences.

It is native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico, where it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features smooth, lance-shaped leaves and small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its root system is fibrous and adapted to retain moisture in dry soils.

Perezia nitidifolia is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties.
