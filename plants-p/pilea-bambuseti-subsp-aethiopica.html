Pilea bambuseti subsp. aethiopica

Pilea bambuseti subsp.

aethiopica, commonly known as the African pilea, is a compact, low-growing plant native to eastern Africa. It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slight sheen, arranged in a rosette formation.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its slow growth and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
