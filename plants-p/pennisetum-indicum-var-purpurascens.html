Pennisetum indicum var. purpurascens

Pennisetum indicum var. purpurascens, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features slender, arching blades that turn a vibrant purple-pink color in the fall, adding visual interest to gardens. The plant produces fluffy, silver-white seed heads that persist through winter, enhancing its seasonal appeal. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping and low-maintenance landscapes.

Its feathery appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for modern garden designs.
