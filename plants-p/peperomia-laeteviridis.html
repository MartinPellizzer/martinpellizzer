Peperomia laeteviridis

Peperomia laeteviridis, commonly known as the Emerald Gem, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, which appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia laeteviridis is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
