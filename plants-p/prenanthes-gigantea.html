Prenanthes gigantea

Prenanthes gigantea, commonly known as giant bladderwort, is a member of the Hydrocharitaceae family.

It is an aquatic plant characterized by its inflated, bladder-like structures that trap small aquatic organisms. The plant typically grows in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds and lakes. Its leaves are long and ribbon-like, often floating on the water surface.
