Pennisetum robustum

Pennisetum robustum, commonly known as fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that grow in dense clumps, often reaching heights of up to 6 feet. The plant produces feathery, plume-like inflorescences that give it a distinctive, ornamental appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its drought tolerance and ability to spread through rhizomes make it both hardy and versatile in landscaping.
