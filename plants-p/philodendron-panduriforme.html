Philodendron panduriforme

Philodendron panduriforme, commonly known as the heartleaf philodendron, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a prominent midrib. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Philodendron panduriforme is a popular houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
