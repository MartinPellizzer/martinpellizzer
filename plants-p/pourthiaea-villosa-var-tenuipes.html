Pourthiaea villosa var. tenuipes

Pourthiaea villosa var. tenuipes, commonly known as the slender-stemmed thistle, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a compact, upright growth habit with grey-green, somewhat hairy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. Its fleshy leaves are adapted to store water, making it well-suited for arid environments.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
