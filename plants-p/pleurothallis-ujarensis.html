Pleurothallis ujarensis

Pleurothallis ujarensis, commonly known as the Ujarensis orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is a small epiphytic orchid with elongated, narrow leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
