Pteropsis piloselloides

Pteropsis piloselloides, commonly known as the hairy groundsel, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has hairy leaves and produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer.
