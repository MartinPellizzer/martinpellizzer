Pennisetum chinense

Pennisetum chinense, commonly known as Chinese fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to China and Southeast Asia.

It features tall, slender stems that produce fluffy, feathery plumes, which are often used in floral arrangements. The plant typically grows between 1 to 3 meters in height and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture and a slight blue-green hue.

Pennisetum chinense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its seasonal display.
