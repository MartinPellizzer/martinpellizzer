Pittosporum helleri

Pittosporum helleri, commonly known as the Heller's pittosporum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that turn a striking red or orange in the autumn. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, orange berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Pittosporum helleri is also valued for its ability to provide habitat for native wildlife.
