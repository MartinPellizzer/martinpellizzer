Piper kadsura f. macrophyllum

Piper kadsura f. macrophyllum, commonly known as the giant arrowroot or large-leaf arrowroot, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a unique species within the Piper genus, valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal uses.
