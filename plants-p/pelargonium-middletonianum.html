Pelargonium middletonianum

Pelargonium middletonianum, commonly known as the Middletons' geranium, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its upright growth habit and showy flowers. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically rounded and have a slightly hairy texture.

Pelargonium middletonianum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
