Pleurothallis trinitensis

Pleurothallis trinitensis, commonly known as the Trinidad orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Caribbean island of Trinidad.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other plants without deriving nutrients from them. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical rainforests.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
