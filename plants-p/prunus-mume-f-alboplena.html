Prunus mume f. alboplena

Prunus mume f. alboplena, commonly known as the white-flowered Chinese plum, is a cultivar of the Japanese apricot.

It is characterized by its large, fully double white flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant is a deciduous shrub or small tree, typically growing to a height of 3 to 6 meters. Its flowers are highly fragrant and are often used in traditional Chinese medicine and as ornamental plants.

The fruit, when it matures, is small and yellow, with a sweet and tart flavor.
