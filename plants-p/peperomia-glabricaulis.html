Peperomia glabricaulis

Peperomia glabricaulis, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically oval to lance-shaped, with a smooth texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in terminal clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia glabricaulis is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
