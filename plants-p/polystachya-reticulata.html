Polystachya reticulata

Polystachya reticulata, commonly known as the reticulate basket palm, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its clustered inflorescence and elongated leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in dense, racemose clusters. Polystachya reticulata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adaptable to a variety of growing conditions.
