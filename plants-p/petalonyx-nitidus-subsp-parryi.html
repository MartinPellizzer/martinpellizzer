Petalonyx nitidus subsp. parryi

Petalonyx nitidus subsp.

The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its otherwise muted appearance. It is adapted to thrive in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
