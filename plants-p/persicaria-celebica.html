Persicaria celebica

Persicaria celebica, commonly known as Java knotweed, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist or wet environments. The plant features upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink or white, appearing in late summer.

Persicaria celebica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
