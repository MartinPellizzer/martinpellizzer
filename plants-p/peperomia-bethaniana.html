Peperomia bethaniana

Peperomia bethaniana, commonly known as the Bethania Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Central America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval or elliptical in shape, often with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia bethaniana is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and moderate temperatures.
