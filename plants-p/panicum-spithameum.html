Panicum spithameum

Panicum spithameum, commonly known as the silky panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its delicate, silver-tomentose foliage. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are often pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its attractive appearance and ease of care.
