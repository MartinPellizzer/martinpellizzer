Panmorphia rabei

Panmorphia rabei, commonly known as the variable morph plant, is a rare and enigmatic species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its highly variable morphology, with leaves and flowers that can change shape and color depending on environmental conditions. The plant exhibits a unique photosynthetic adaptation that allows it to thrive in low-light environments. Its roots are capable of absorbing nutrients from decaying organic matter, contributing to its survival in nutrient-poor soils.

Despite its ecological significance, Panmorphia rabei remains understudied, with limited knowledge about its reproductive biology and genetic diversity.
