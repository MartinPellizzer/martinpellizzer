Pseuderanthemum bolivianum

Pseuderanthemum bolivianum, commonly known as Bolivian false geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Bolivia, and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Pseuderanthemum bolivianum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
