Pittosporum lignilobum

Pittosporum lignilobum, commonly known as the silver pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves that are glossy and often have a silvery underside. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in spring, which are followed by spherical, red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

Pittosporum lignilobum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in horticulture for its hardiness and adaptability.
