Philadelphus kunmingensis var. parvifolius

Philadelphus kunmingensis var. parvifolius, commonly known as the small-leaved mock orange, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, ovate to elliptical leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value lies in its showy blossoms and aromatic foliage.
