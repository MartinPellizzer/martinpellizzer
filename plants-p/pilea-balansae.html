Pilea balansae

Pilea balansae, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its round, glossy leaves that resemble silver coins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by green berries. It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.

Pilea balansae prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
