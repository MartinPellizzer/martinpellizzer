Philippia esterhuyseniae

Philippia esterhuyseniae, commonly known as the Esterhuysenia cactus, is a species native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its spiny, columnar stems. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, adapting to harsh, dry conditions. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Philippia esterhuyseniae is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in low-water landscapes.
