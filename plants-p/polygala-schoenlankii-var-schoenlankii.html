Polygala schoenlankii var. schoenlankii

Polygala schoenlankii var. schoenlankii, commonly known as the Schoenlank's milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family, which is known for its distinctive floral structures. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink or purple, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

This species is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
