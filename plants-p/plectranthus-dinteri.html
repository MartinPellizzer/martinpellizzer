Plectranthus dinteri

Plectranthus dinteri, commonly known as Dinter's coleus, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a striking purple underside. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually violet or blue in color. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and prefers well-drained soil.

Plectranthus dinteri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.
