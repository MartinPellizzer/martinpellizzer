Phalaris cylindrica

Phalaris cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical wild oat, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to southern Europe and parts of Asia, often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides. The plant has slender, cylindrical spikelets that are typically green or reddish in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture.

Phalaris cylindrica is an annual plant that produces small, wind-pollinated flowers, contributing to its role in local ecosystems.
