Pterostylis hadra

Pterostylis hadra, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions. The plant has a slender, leafless stem that bears a single, showy greenish-yellow flower. Its morphology is adapted to its environment, with the flower often opening in spring or early summer.

The species is part of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique pollination mechanisms.
