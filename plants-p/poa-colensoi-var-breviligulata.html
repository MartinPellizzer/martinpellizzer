Poa colensoi var. breviligulata

Poa colensoi var. breviligulata, commonly known as the Colenso's bentgrass, is a low-growing perennial grass species native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted foliage and slender, often reddish stems. The plant typically forms dense mats, which help stabilize soil in its natural habitat. Its leaves are narrow with a distinct midrib and are covered in short, stiff hairs.

This variety is notable for its ecological importance in supporting local biodiversity and its use in conservation landscaping.
