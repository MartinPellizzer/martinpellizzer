Pleurothallis Neibana

Pleurothallis neibana is a rare and captivating orchid native to the cloud forests of Costa Rica. This delicate plant is part of the vast Pleurothallis genus, which includes over 1,000 species. Known for its striking greenish-white flowers, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its unique beauty makes it a favorite among orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike. Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered, highlighting the need for conservation efforts.
