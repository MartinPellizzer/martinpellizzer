Portulaca minuta

Portulaca minuta, commonly known as the dwarf portulaca, is a small, annual flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features fleshy, succulent leaves and produces vibrant, bright orange or yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. Its compact size and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Portulaca minuta is also known for its ability to spread rapidly through seed dispersal.
