Polystachya fractiflexa

Polystachya fractiflexa, commonly known as the flexile spike orchid, is a species of orchid native to the wet tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that bloom during the rainy season. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests and along stream banks. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a spiral pattern, contributing to its unique appearance.

Polystachya fractiflexa is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological diversity of its habitat.
