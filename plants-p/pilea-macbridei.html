Pilea macbridei

Pilea macbridei, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features oval, waxy, dark green leaves that are slightly curled under at the edges, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers in early spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea macbridei is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and moderate temperatures.
