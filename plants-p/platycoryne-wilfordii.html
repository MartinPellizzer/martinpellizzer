Platycoryne wilfordii

Platycoryne wilfordii, commonly known as the Wilford's flat-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinct lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as forest understories. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique floral structure.

Botanically, it is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native environment.
