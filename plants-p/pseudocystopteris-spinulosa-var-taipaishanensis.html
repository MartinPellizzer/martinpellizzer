Pseudocystopteris spinulosa var. taipaishanensis

Pseudocystopteris spinulosa var. taipaishanensis, commonly known as the Taipai Shan spleenwort, is a rare fern species native to China.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its small, delicate fronds with a distinctive veining pattern.
