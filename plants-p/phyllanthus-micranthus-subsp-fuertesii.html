Phyllanthus micranthus subsp. fuertesii

Phyllanthus micranthus subsp.

fuertesii, commonly known as the Mexican spurge, is a shrub native to Central America and parts of Mexico. It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered leaves and yellowish flowers.

The plant produces small, greenish fruits that contain seeds surrounded by a fleshy coating. It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.
