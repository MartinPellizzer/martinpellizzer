Pennisetum polystachion

Pennisetum polystachion, commonly known as purple lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and its striking purple to pink plumes that appear in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Pennisetum polystachion is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
