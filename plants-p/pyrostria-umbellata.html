Pyrostria umbellata

Pyrostria umbellata, commonly known as the umbrella fern, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, feathery fronds that grow in a compact, umbrella-like formation. The plant has a short, stout rhizome from which the fronds emerge. Its leaves are typically dark green and glossy, with a slightly waxy texture.

Pyrostria umbellata thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
