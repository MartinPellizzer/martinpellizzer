Piper ulei

Piper ulei, commonly known as African black pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its small, dark purple berries. The plant produces a pungent aroma due to the presence of volatile oils, primarily containing compounds like piperine. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a glossy surface.

The fruit is typically harvested when fully mature, and it is used in traditional medicine and as a spice.
