Peperomia ovatolanceolata

Peperomia ovatolanceolata, commonly known as the broad-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia ovatolanceolata is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
