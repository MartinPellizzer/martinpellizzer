Prunus odorata

Prunus odorata, commonly known as the sweet cherry or cherry laurel, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces fragrant white or pink flowers in spring, which are followed by edible red drupes in summer. The plant has elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath, with a smooth texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

The fruit is often used in culinary applications, while the plant also has historical uses in herbal medicine.
