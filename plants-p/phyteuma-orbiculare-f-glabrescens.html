Phyteuma orbiculare f. glabrescens

Phyteuma orbiculare f. glabrescens, commonly known as the glabrous phyteuma, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded leaves and clusters of bell-shaped, pale blue to lilac flowers. This variety is notable for its reduced pubescence, giving it a smooth, glabrous appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

The plant is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
