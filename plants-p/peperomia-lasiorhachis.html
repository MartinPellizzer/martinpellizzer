Peperomia lasiorhachis

Peperomia lasiorhachis, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are clustered in spikes. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and minimal water requirements.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental purposes in gardens and as a houseplant.
