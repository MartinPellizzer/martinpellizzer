Peperomia galioides var. crassispica

Peperomia galioides var. crassispica, commonly known as the thick-stemmed peperomia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and small, ovate leaves. The plant typically grows as a shrub or a trailing plant, often with a compact and bushy appearance. Its foliage is usually dark green with a waxy texture, and it produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers.

It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.
