Polygala sennenii

Polygala sennenii, commonly known as the Japanese milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces yellow or greenish flowers that are often surrounded by leaf-like bracts. Its leaves are typically opposite and have a slightly hairy texture.

Polygala sennenii is often found in woodland areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
