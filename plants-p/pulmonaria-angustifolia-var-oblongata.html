Pulmonaria angustifolia var. oblongata

Pulmonaria angustifolia var. oblongata, commonly known as narrow-leaved lungwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features basal rosettes of elliptical, hairy leaves that are typically dark green with silver-white markings. The plant produces upright spikes of bell-shaped, pinkish-purple flowers in early spring. It thrives in moist, shady woodland environments and is valued for its ornamental foliage and early bloom.

Pulmonaria angustifolia var. oblongata is a member of the Geraniaceae family and is often used in garden landscapes for its visual appeal.
