Pedilonum loesenerianum

Pedilonum loesenerianum, commonly known as the Loesener's slipper orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped flowers that are typically pale green or yellowish in color. The plant produces small, clustered roots and has a slender, upright stem with few leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful cultivation due to its specific ecological needs.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as an indicator of undisturbed tropical ecosystems.
