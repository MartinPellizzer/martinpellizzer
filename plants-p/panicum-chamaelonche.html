Panicum chamaelonche

Panicum chamaelonche, commonly known as the silvergrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, silver-gray blades that give it a distinctive appearance, often growing in dense clumps. The plant produces fluffy, pale-green to brownish spikelets that are typical of the grass family. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking hardy, decorative plants.
