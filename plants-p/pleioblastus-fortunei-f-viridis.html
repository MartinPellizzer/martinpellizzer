Pleioblastus fortunei f. viridis

Pleioblastus fortunei f. viridis, commonly known as the Green Bamboo, is a variant of the Fortune bamboo characterized by its vibrant, emerald-green foliage.

It is a clumping bamboo that grows in dense, upright clusters, making it an excellent choice for ornamental landscaping. The plant features slender, segmented culms that are covered in a fine, powdery bloom, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance, it is widely cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
