Posoqueria multiflora

Posoqueria multiflora, commonly known as the golden star ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that resemble stars, which bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often arranged in a whorled pattern around the stem. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Posoqueria multiflora is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
