Poa szechuensis var. szechuensis

Poa szechuensis var. szechuensis, commonly known as the Sichuan bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and slender, blue-green blades. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its inflorescence consists of a single spikelet, which is usually short and compact.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and ecological restoration projects.
