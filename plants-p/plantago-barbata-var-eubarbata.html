Plantago barbata var. eubarbata

Plantago barbata var. eubarbata, commonly known as the smooth plantain, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features upright, slender stems that bear lance-shaped leaves with a smooth, waxy surface. The plant produces small, clustered greenish-white flowers that are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and disturbed areas.

Its fibrous roots make it a hardy and adaptable species within its native range.
