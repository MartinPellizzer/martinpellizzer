Prinos verticillatus var. tenuifolius

Prinos verticillatus var. tenuifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved prinos, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, aromatic leaves that are typically narrow and arranged in whorls around the stem. The plant produces clusters of tiny, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the spring. Its foliage is often used in traditional herbal remedies due to its aromatic properties.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
