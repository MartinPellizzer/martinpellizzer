Pothos triphyllus

Pothos triphyllus, commonly known as the Marble Queen Pothos, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are variegated with shades of white and green. The plant thrives in low-light conditions and is known for its adaptability to a variety of indoor environments. Its aerial roots allow it to climb and attach to surfaces, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets or shelves.

The plant is also noted for its ability to purify air, adding to its appeal as a decorative and beneficial houseplant.
