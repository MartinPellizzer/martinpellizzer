Premna straminicaulis

Premna straminicaulis, commonly known as the golden thread plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides.
