Pyrrosia linearifolia var. heterolepis

Pyrrosia linearifolia var. heterolepis, commonly known as the crested fern, is a variant of the linear-leafed pyrrosia.

It is a perennial epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features elongated, linear leaves that are often slightly curled or twisted, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its fronds are typically dark green and can grow up to 30 centimeters in length.

The species thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often seen growing on tree trunks or rocks in its natural habitat.
