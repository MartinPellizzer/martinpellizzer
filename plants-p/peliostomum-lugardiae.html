Peliostomum lugardiae

Peliostomum lugardiae, commonly known as the African water lily, is a aquatic plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features broad, floating leaves that provide shade and habitat for aquatic life. The plant produces showy, white flowers that open in the morning and close at night. Its roots anchor it in shallow water, while its rhizomes spread horizontally.

Peliostomum lugardiae is often cultivated in ponds and water gardens for its ornamental value.
