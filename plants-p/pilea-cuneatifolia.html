Pilea cuneatifolia

Pilea cuneatifolia, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact evergreen shrub native to China.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are dark green with a subtle variegation of lighter green and silver. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Pilea cuneatifolia is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
