Potentilla wimanniana f. koernickei

Potentilla wimanniana f. koernickei, commonly known as the Koernicke's cinquefoil, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments.
