Pleurothallis pernambucensis

Pleurothallis pernambucensis, commonly known as the Pernambuco pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in tropical rainforests. The plant has delicate, linear leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a conservation concern.
