Polygonatum intermedium

Polygonatum intermedium, commonly known as the Chinese Solomon's seal, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features rhizomatous growth and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of white or greenish-white flowers. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are typically red or orange in color. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Polygonatum intermedium is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
