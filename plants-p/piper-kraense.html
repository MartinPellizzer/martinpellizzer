Piper kraense

Piper kraense, commonly known as Kraense pepper, is a climbing shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when mature, and these berries are used in traditional medicine. The leaves are often used for their aromatic properties and are sometimes brewed into a tea.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its medicinal and culinary uses.
