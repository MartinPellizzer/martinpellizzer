Peperomia gracilicaulis

Peperomia gracilicaulis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features upright, branching stems covered in small, glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color, which are followed by small, round berries. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal watering needs.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for succulent and foliage enthusiasts.
