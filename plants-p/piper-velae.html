Piper velae

Piper velae, commonly known as the velvet pipevine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Piperaceae, which includes many species of flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release seeds when mature.

The plant is often found in humid, shaded environments such as rainforests.
