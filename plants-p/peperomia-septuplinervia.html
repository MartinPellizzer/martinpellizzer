Peperomia septuplinervia

Peperomia septuplinervia, commonly known as the seven-veined peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale green flowers that are borne on short, erect spikes. Its foliage is often variegated, with shades of green, white, and sometimes pink or red.

Peperomia septuplinervia is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.
