Pelargonium × insignitum

Pelargonium × insignitum, commonly known as the African marigold, is a hybrid species within the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces fleshy, lobed leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
