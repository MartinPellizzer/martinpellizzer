Potentilla khasiana

Potentilla khasiana, commonly known as the Khasi potentilla, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Himalayas.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant features small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are typically narrow and elliptical, with a slightly hairy texture.

Potentilla khasiana is often found in moist, well-drained soils in mountainous regions.
