Philotheca obovatifolia

Philotheca obovatifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed mountain pepper, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a shrub characterized by its aromatic, lance-shaped leaves and small, white or pale yellow flowers that appear in clusters. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are typically red or black when mature. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and dry forests.

Philotheca obovatifolia is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Australian herbal medicine.
