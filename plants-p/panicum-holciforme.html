Panicum holciforme

Panicum holciforme, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and upright growth habit, making it visually striking in gardens and natural landscapes. The plant produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in prairies and open woodlands.

Silver bluestem is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.
