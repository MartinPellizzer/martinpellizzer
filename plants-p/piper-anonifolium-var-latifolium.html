Piper anonifolium var. latifolium

Piper anonifolium var. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf pepper plant, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are typically green when mature. It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species.

Its botanical features make it a unique subject for study in tropical botany and horticulture.
