Peperomia psiloclada var. magnifolia

Peperomia psiloclada var. magnifolia, commonly known as the magnolia peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features glossy, dark green, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or silver markings, adding visual interest to any indoor space. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, which are followed by spore-bearing structures called sporangia. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
