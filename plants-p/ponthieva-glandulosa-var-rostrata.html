Ponthieva glandulosa var. rostrata

Ponthieva glandulosa var. rostrata, commonly known as the rostrated ponthieva, is a species of flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

Its flowers are usually violet or purple, adding ornamental value to its natural habitat.
