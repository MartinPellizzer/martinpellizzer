Petrosedum dianium

Petrosedum dianium, commonly known as the silver rosemary.

It is a succulent perennial native to the Canary Islands, characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to violet, typically blooming in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its aromatic leaves and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and ornamental purposes.
