Piper guedesii

Piper guedesii, commonly known as the Guedes pepper, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many pepper species. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, peppery fruits. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly serrated margin.

The plant is valued for its aromatic foliage and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
