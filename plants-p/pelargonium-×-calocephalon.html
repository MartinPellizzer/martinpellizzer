Pelargonium × calocephalon

Pelargonium × calocephalon, commonly known as the angel's trumpet geranium, is a hybrid plant within the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant typically features lobed, serrated leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Pelargonium × calocephalon is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and is often used in containers or as part of floral arrangements.
