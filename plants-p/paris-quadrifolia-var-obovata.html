Paris quadrifolia var. obovata

Paris quadrifolia var. obovata, commonly known as the white wood sorrel, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive white or pale green coloration. The plant produces delicate, white flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in woodland environments and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique addition to shaded gardens and natural habitats.
