Peperomia mandioccana

Peperomia mandioccana, commonly known as the Mandioca Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to Brazil.

It features glossy, dark green elliptical leaves with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and red, which are typically hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance, it is often used as a decorative houseplant.
