Piper reflexum

Piper reflexum, commonly known as the creeping pepper vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that develop into reddish berries. Its vines can grow up to several meters long, often trailing along the ground or climbing supports.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
