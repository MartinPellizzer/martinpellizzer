Peperomia subrotunda

Peperomia subrotunda, commonly known as the Round-leaf Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are typically bright green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color, which are followed by tiny, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia subrotunda is relatively low-maintenance and resistant to pests, making it an ideal plant for beginners.
