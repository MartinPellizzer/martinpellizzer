Potentilla aperta var. aperta

Potentilla aperta var. aperta, commonly known as the open potentilla, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, rounded form. The plant features small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are typically oval or elliptic, with a slightly hairy surface, and the plant thrives in well-drained soils in full sun.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
