Paronychia hieronymi var. hieronymi

Paronychia hieronymi var. hieronymi, commonly known as the silverleaf sandthrift, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant features small, silvery-green leaves with a waxy coating, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. Its flowers are typically pink or purple, appearing in clusters during the spring and summer months.

It is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
