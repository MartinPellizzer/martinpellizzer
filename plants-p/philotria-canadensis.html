Philotria canadensis

Philotria canadensis, commonly known as Canada anemone, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features showy, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are palmately divided, with a distinctive, delicate appearance.

Philotria canadensis plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is often found in moist, temperate regions.
