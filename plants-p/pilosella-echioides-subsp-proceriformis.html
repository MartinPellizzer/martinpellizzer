Pilosella echioides subsp. proceriformis

Pilosella echioides subsp.

proceriformis, commonly known as the slender pilosella, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and small, daisy-like yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This subspecies is native to parts of Europe and is often found in disturbed or marginal environments.
