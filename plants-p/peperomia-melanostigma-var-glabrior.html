Peperomia melanostigma var. glabrior

Peperomia melanostigma var. glabrior, commonly known as the smooth-leaved pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a smooth texture, distinguishing it from other peperomia varieties. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers arranged in spicate inflorescences, which are often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a versatile and low-maintenance houseplant.
