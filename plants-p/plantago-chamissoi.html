Plantago chamissoi

Plantago chamissoi, commonly known as the Chamisso's plantain, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It features a basal rosette of broad, lance-shaped leaves covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, which are wind-pollinated. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine environments.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
