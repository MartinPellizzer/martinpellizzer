Panicum megiston f. pauciflora

Panicum megiston f. pauciflora, commonly known as the sparse-flowered panicgrass, is a flowering plant species belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its reduced floral display, with fewer inflorescences compared to other varieties of Panicum. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils, showing a preference for temperate climates. Its slender, wiry stems and small, spike-like clusters of flowers are typical of the genus Panicum.

It is often found in regions with well-drained soils and moderate rainfall.
