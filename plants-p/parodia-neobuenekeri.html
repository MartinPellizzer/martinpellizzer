Parodia neobuenekeri

Parodia neobuenekeri, commonly known as the "Cactus of the Desert," is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spiny stems that form a globular shape. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact size and striking appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
