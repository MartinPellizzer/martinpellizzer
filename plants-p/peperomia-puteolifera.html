Peperomia puteolifera

Peperomia puteolifera, commonly known as the swamp peperomia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, evergreen shrub characterized by its dense, waxy leaves and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish or reddish in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near water bodies.

Peperomia puteolifera is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
