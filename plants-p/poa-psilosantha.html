Poa psilosantha

Poa psilosantha, commonly known as the slender meadowgrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open grasslands and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. The plant has slender, upright culms with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of a loose cluster of spikelets, each bearing small, two-lobed flowers.

Poa psilosantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
