Paronychia arabica var. elongata

Paronychia arabica var. elongata, commonly known as the elongated sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that produces small, yellowish flowers clustered in dense inflorescences. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in dry environments. It thrives in well-drained sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in rocky habitats.

Its botanical features make it well-adapted to harsh, semi-desert conditions.
