Paepalanthus macrocaulon var. contasensis

Paepalanthus macrocaulon var. contasensis, commonly known as the Contasensis grass tree, is a unique species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family, characterized by its tall, grass-like appearance with dense clusters of inflorescences. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, forming a rosette structure at the base.
