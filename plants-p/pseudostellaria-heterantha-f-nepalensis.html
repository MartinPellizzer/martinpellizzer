Pseudostellaria heterantha f. nepalensis

Pseudostellaria heterantha f. nepalensis, commonly known as the Nepalese starflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its branched stems and small, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in early summer. The plant features alternate, ovate leaves with a hairy texture, and its flowers are usually pink to white in color. Native to the Himalayan regions, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
