Pilea rufescens

Pilea rufescens, commonly known as the Shiny Pilea, is a low-growing, evergreen perennial native to Mexico.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and deep green, with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea rufescens is relatively easy to care for and is often propagated through stem cuttings.
