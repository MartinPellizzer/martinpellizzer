Polygonum styligerum

Polygonum styligerum, commonly known as the slender knotweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like buckwheat and sorrel. The plant features slender, upright stems with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often reddish at the base. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces small, three-lobed fruits.

Polygonum styligerum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
