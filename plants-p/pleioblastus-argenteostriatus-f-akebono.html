Pleioblastus argenteostriatus f. akebono

Pleioblastus argenteostriatus f. akebono, commonly known as the Akebono bamboo, is a variegated form of the silver-stripped bamboo.

It features striking white and green streaks along its culms, which are slender and often arching in growth habit. The plant is native to Japan and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, requiring moderate watering.

Its foliage remains lush throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for ornamental bamboo cultivation.
