Peperomia trollii

Peperomia trollii, commonly known as the Congo peperomia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly the Democratic Republic of the Congo. The plant is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with green, white, and silver tones. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or purple in color.

Peperomia trollii is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive foliage.
