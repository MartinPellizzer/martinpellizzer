Phalaenopsis zebrina

Phalaenopsis zebrina, commonly known as the zebra orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, long-lasting blooms that come in a variety of colors, often with intricate patterns resembling zebra stripes. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, forming pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Phalaenopsis zebrina is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and aesthetic appeal.
