Podocarpium podocarpum var. japonicum

Podocarpium podocarpum var. japonicum, commonly known as the Japanese podocarp, is a variant of the podocarp genus native to East Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its evergreen leaves and coniferous-like cones. The plant exhibits a distinctive morphology with spirally arranged foliage and small, rounded leaves. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forest understories.

Botanically, it is noted for its unique reproductive structures and ecological adaptations.
