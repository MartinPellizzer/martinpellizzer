Phalangium latifolium

Phalangium latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf phalangium, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Apocynaceae.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or white in color. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions and thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
