Podochilus steinii

Podochilus steinii, commonly known as the purple-flowered orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, branching stem and produces clusters of fragrant, violet-colored flowers. The plant has strap-like, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Podochilus steinii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
