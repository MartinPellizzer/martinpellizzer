Peritoma arborea var. globosa

Peritoma arborea var. globosa, commonly known as the globe peritoma, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit and small, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink and purple. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
