Philodendron laciniatum var. palmatisectum

Philodendron laciniatum var. palmatisectum, commonly known as the Variegated Shield Leaf Philodendron, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive shield-like appearance and deep green to variegated coloration. The leaves are typically deeply lobed, giving the plant a unique and dramatic visual appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

This plant is popular among indoor gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
