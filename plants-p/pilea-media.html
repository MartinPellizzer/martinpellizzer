Pilea media

Pilea media, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a slightly variegated appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers, which are followed by round, coin-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea media is known for its ease of care and ability to propagate through stem cuttings.
