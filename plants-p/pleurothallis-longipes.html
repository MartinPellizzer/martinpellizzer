Pleurothallis longipes

Pleurothallis longipes, commonly known as the longiped orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich substrates.

Due to its delicate appearance and specific care requirements, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
