Pleurothallis rostellata

Pleurothallis rostellata, commonly known as the rostellate orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, narrow leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its intricate floral structure.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments to ensure its survival.
