Pilosella saeva

Pilosella saeva, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Pilosella saeva is often found in open habitats and can spread aggressively in some regions.
