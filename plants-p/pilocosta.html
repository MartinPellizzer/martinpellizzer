Pilocosta

Pilocosta, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stem is hairy and erect.

Pilocosta is valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
