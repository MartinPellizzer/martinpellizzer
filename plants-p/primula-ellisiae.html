Primula ellisiae

Primula ellisiae, commonly known as the Ellisia's primrose, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant features hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves, which are often covered in fine, soft pubescence. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.
