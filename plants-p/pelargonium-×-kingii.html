Pelargonium × kingii

Pelargonium × kingii, commonly known as the zonal geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a wide range of colors, including red, pink, and purple. The leaves are typically rounded with distinct zonation patterns, giving the plant its common name. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in various growing conditions.

Pelargonium × kingii is often used in gardens and as a potted plant because of its resilience and aesthetic appeal.
