Polystachya tessellata var. tricruris

Polystachya tessellata var. tricruris, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped inflorescence and vibrant, colorful flowers that range from red to orange and yellow. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in its natural habitat. Its leaves are typically linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This species is often cultivated for its aesthetic value and is relatively easy to care for in the right growing conditions.
