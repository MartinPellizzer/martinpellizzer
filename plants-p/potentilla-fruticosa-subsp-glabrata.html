Potentilla fruticosa subsp. glabrata

Potentilla fruticosa subsp.

glabrata, commonly known as the smooth cinquefoil, is a low-growing shrub native to North America. It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant has smooth, glabrous stems and leaves that are typically oval or elliptic in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or rocky areas.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
