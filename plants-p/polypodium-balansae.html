Polypodium balansae

Polypodium balansae, commonly known as the Balansae fern, is a species of polypodioid fern native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear fronds that are often arching or drooping, giving it a graceful appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its sporangia are clustered in sori on the underside of the fronds, a typical feature of ferns.

Polypodium balansae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
