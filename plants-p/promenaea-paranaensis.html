Promenaea paranaensis

Promenaea paranaensis, commonly known as the Parana river orchid, is a tropical plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its elegant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments near rivers and streams. Its leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, while its flowers are often pink or purple in color.

Promenaea paranaensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
