Pilea swinglei

Pilea swinglei, commonly known as the Shingle Plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to China.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring. It thrives in indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ease of care and attractive foliage make it a favored ornamental plant.
