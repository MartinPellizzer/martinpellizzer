Populus × Petrowskiana

Populus × petrowskiana is a hybrid poplar known for its unique combination of traits from its parent species. This tree is commonly found in regions with temperate climates and is often used in reforestation and phytoremediation projects. It exhibits rapid growth and adaptability to various soil conditions, making it a valuable species for ecological restoration. The plant's ability to tolerate pollutants makes it an important tool in cleaning contaminated environments. Its striking foliage and hardiness have also made it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
