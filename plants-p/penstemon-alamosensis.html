Penstemon alamosensis

Penstemon alamosensis, commonly known as the Alamos penstemon, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from violet to blue. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It is often found in desert scrub and chaparral habitats.

Penstemon alamosensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance as a pollinator plant.
