Planchonella longipetiolata

Planchonella longipetiolata, commonly known as the long-petioled hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or white, followed by fleshy, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
