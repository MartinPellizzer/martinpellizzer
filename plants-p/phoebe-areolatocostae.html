Phoebe areolatocostae

Phoebe areolatocostae, commonly known as the spotted costate phoebe, is a species of flowering plant native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive foliage, which features a unique pattern of spots and streaks on its leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
