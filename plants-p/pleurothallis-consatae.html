Pleurothallis consatae

Pleurothallis consatae, commonly known as the silver moon orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that often have a silvery or pale greenish hue. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually narrow and dark green, and it produces fragrant blooms during the rainy season.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
