Priva roxburghii

Priva roxburghii, commonly known as the Kashmir pine, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and needle-like leaves that are arranged in clusters. The tree produces cones that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

The Kashmir pine is valued for its timber and has ecological importance in its native habitat.
