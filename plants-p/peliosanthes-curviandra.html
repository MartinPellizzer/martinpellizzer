Peliosanthes curviandra

Peliosanthes curviandra, commonly known as the curved-leaf cycad, is a unique member of the Cycadaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant features a stout, unbranched trunk that is covered with a fibrous, spongy layer. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with a distinctive curved shape that gives it its common name.

Peliosanthes curviandra is known for its slow growth and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
