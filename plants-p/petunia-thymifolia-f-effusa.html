Petunia thymifolia f. effusa

Petunia thymifolia f. effusa, commonly known as the effuse thymifolia petunia, is a compact, trailing plant with dense clusters of small, showy flowers.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other flowering plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant features hairy stems and leaves that are typically oval to lance-shaped. Its flowers are usually blue or violet, with a distinctive tubular shape that attracts pollinators.

It is often grown as a decorative plant in hanging baskets or containers due to its cascading growth habit.
