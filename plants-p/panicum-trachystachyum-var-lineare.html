Panicum trachystachyum var. lineare

Panicum trachystachyum var. lineare, commonly known as narrow-stemmed millet, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, pale green to yellowish flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its potential use in sustainable agriculture.
