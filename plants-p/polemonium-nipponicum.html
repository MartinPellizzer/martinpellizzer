Polemonium nipponicum

Polemonium nipponicum, commonly known as Japanese garden bell, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features upright, branching stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue to purple. The plant produces showy, tubular blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive blue-green hue.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shaded garden settings.
