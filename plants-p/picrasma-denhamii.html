Picrasma denhamii

Picrasma denhamii, commonly known as the Denham's picrasma, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the family Picrasmales and is characterized by its woody structure and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded forest environments.
