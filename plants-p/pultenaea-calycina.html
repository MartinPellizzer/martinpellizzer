Pultenaea calycina

Pultenaea calycina, commonly known as the silky pultenaea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils, often in coastal regions. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in well-drained conditions.
