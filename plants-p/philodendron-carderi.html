Philodendron carderi

Philodendron carderi, commonly known as the Carderi Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy green leaves that are often adorned with prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by red or orange berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its rapid growth and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
