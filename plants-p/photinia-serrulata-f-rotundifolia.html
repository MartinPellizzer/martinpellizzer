Photinia serrulata f. rotundifolia

Photinia serrulata f. rotundifolia, commonly known as the rounded-leaf firethorn, is a compact shrub native to China.

It features small, glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are rounded at the tips, giving it its distinguishing appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in spring, followed by bright red berries in autumn. It is valued for its ornamental foliage and seasonal color changes.

This cultivar is often used in gardens for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
