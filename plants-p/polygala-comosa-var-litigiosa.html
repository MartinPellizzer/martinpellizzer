Polygala comosa var. litigiosa

Polygala comosa var. litigiosa, commonly known as the litigious polygala, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant produces delicate, raceme-like inflorescences with small, often pink or white flowers. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and it is known for its ornamental value in gardens.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
