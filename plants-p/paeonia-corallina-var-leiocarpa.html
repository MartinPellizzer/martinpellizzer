Paeonia corallina var. leiocarpa

Paeonia corallina var. leiocarpa, commonly known as the coral peony, is a unique variant of the coral peony species.

It is native to the mountainous regions of China, where it thrives in temperate climates. The plant is characterized by its striking, coral-like flowers that bloom in shades of pink and red. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This variety is valued for both its aesthetic beauty and its role in traditional Chinese horticulture.
