Piper rotundibaccum var. fraijanesanum

Piper rotundibaccum var. fraijanesanum, commonly known as the Fraijanes pepper, is a variant of the Piper rotundibaccum species native to Central America.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, round fruits. The plant belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. Its fruits are typically harvested when they turn from green to red, indicating maturity.

The botanical features of this plant make it well-suited for tropical and subtropical climates.
