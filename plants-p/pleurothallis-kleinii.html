Pleurothallis kleinii

Pleurothallis kleinii, commonly known as the Klein's orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, clustered inflorescences with small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrate.

Pleurothallis kleinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
