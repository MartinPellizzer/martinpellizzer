Peperomia demissa

Peperomia demissa, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically silver or gray-green in color, with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia demissa is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
