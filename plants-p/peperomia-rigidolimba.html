Peperomia rigidolimba

Peperomia rigidolimba, commonly known as the rigid leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish or purple underside, giving it a striking visual appeal. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, usually green or reddish, which are followed by small, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia rigidolimba is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and tolerating a range of indoor conditions.
