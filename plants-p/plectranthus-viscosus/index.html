Plectranthus Viscosus

Plectranthus viscosus is a unique and intriguing plant known for its sticky leaves and vibrant foliage. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in warm, sunny environments with well-draining soil. This plant is often grown as an ornamental due to its attractive appearance and ease of care. Its ability to produce small, fragrant flowers makes it a favorite among gardeners. Despite its modest size, Plectranthus viscosus holds cultural and medicinal significance in many regions.
