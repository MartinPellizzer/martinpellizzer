Peucedanum gracile

Peucedanum gracile, commonly known as slender cowparsley, is a flowering plant in the carrot family (Apiaceae).

It is native to Europe and western Asia, often found in grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant features slender, erect stems with finely divided, fern-like leaves. Its small, yellowish-green flowers appear in umbels, typical of the Apiaceae family.

Peucedanum gracile is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in herbal medicine.
