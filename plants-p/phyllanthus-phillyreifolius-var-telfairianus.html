Phyllanthus phillyreifolius var. telfairianus

Phyllanthus phillyreifolius var. telfairianus, commonly known as the Telfair's spurge, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features opposite, ovate leaves with smooth margins and a distinctive yellowish-green underside. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters, followed by spherical, fleshy fruits that are orange to red in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
