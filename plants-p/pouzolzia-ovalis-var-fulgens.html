Pouzolzia ovalis var. fulgens

Pouzolzia ovalis var. fulgens, commonly known as the showy pouzolzia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, showy yellow flowers that appear in late summer. The leaves are typically elliptical, with a glossy surface and a slightly waxy texture. This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun exposure.
