Piper carniconnectivum

Piper carniconnectivum, commonly known as the red-horned pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its distinctive red, tubular fruits that contain a single seed. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The leaves are typically heart-shaped with a glossy surface and emit a mild peppery aroma.
