Pavonia semperflorens

Pavonia semperflorens, commonly known as the flame flower or scarlet runner, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant red or orange tubular flowers. The plant produces showy, clustered blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Pavonia semperflorens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
