Phrynium blandum

Phrynium blandum, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and a spadix inflorescence. The plant produces a starchy root that is used as a food source in some regions. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Phrynium blandum thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
