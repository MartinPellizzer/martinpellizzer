Phegopteris wagneri

Phegopteris wagneri, commonly known as the Wagner's sword fern, is a species of fern native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its large, lance-shaped fronds that can grow up to 3 feet in length. The plant has a creeping rhizome that allows it to spread and form dense colonies. Its leaves are typically divided into multiple segments, giving it a feathery appearance.

Phegopteris wagneri thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and along stream banks.
