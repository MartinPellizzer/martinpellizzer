Phalaenopsis pallens var. alba

Phalaenopsis pallens var. alba, commonly known as the white phalaenopsis orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, long-lasting white flowers that bloom along the stem. The plant has broad, dark green leaves that are arranged in a compact rosette. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and ease of care.
