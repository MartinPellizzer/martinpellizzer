Polypodium pycnophyllum

Polypodium pycnophyllum, commonly known as the dense-leaf huperzia, is a species of fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, dark green foliage. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its fronds are usually leathery and have a distinctive, closely packed arrangement of pinnae.

Polypodium pycnophyllum is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in humid, temperate climates.
