Peperomia luxii

Peperomia luxii, commonly known as the Shiny Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically green with a subtle variegation, adding visual interest to any indoor space. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which bloom from late spring to early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia luxii is relatively low maintenance, making it an ideal choice for indoor gardening enthusiasts.
