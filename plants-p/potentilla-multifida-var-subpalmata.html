Potentilla multifida var. subpalmata

Potentilla multifida var. subpalmata, commonly known as the palmate potentilla, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features compound leaves with deeply lobed, palmately arranged leaflets that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in early summer, which are clustered in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate foliage and long blooming period.
