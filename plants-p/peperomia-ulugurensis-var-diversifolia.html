Peperomia ulugurensis var. diversifolia

Peperomia ulugurensis var. diversifolia, commonly known as the Variegated Peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with striking white and yellow variegation, adding visual interest to any setting. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique leaf patterns and ease of care have made it a popular choice for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
