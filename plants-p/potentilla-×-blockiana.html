Potentilla × blockiana

Potentilla × blockiana, commonly known as the evergreen potentilla, is a hybrid shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and dark green, leathery leaves that remain throughout the year. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers in early summer, which are highly attractive to pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice in horticulture.
