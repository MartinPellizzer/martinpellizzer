Polygonum rumicifolium var. oblongum

Polygonum rumicifolium var. oblongum, commonly known as redroot smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist soils along stream banks, wetlands, and disturbed areas. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a reddish midrib and produces small, clustered flowers in late summer. Its stems are reddish and often have a distinct, slightly hairy texture.

It is valued in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for wildlife.
