Pteris wallichiana

Pteris wallichiana, commonly known as the Himalayan water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Pteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds that are typically light green and have a feathery appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions of Asia. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce roots that help anchor it to rocky substrates.

Pteris wallichiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
