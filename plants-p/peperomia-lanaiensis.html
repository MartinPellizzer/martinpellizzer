Peperomia lanaiensis

Peperomia lanaiensis, commonly known as the Lanai peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Hawaiian island of Lanai.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are usually dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia lanaiensis is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
