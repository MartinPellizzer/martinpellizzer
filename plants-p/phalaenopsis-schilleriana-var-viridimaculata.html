Phalaenopsis schilleriana var. viridimaculata

Phalaenopsis schilleriana var. viridimaculata, commonly known as the green-spotted phalaenopsis, is a hybrid orchid prized for its elegant, long-lasting blooms.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a subtle, speckled pattern of lighter green. The flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive, velvety texture and a fragrant scent. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent humidity and well-draining potting media.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
