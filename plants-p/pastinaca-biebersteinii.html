Pastinaca biebersteinii

Pastinaca biebersteinii, commonly known as the Biberstein's burdock, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of purple to pink flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its root system is fibrous and often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Pastinaca biebersteinii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.
