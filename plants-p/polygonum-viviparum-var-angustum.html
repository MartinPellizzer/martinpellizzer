Polygonum viviparum var. angustum

Polygonum viviparum var. angustum, commonly known as narrowleaf smartweed, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, stream banks, and wet meadows. The plant has slender, upright stems that are often reddish in color, and its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped. It produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters, which are followed by three-lobed, reddish-brown seed capsules.

The plant is known for its ability to thrive in nutrient-rich soils and is often found in disturbed or wetland habitats.
