Pitcairnia patentiflora var. patentiflora

Pitcairnia patentiflora var. patentiflora, commonly known as the showy pitcairnia, is a striking bromeliad native to the cloud forests of Brazil.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to reddish hues. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing numerous showy, pink or red flowers. Its leaves are typically thick and waxy, adapted to collect and store water in its native humid environment.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in shaded, humid gardens.
