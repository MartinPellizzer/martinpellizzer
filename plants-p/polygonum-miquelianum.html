Polygonum miquelianum

Polygonum miquelianum, commonly known as the Chinese knotweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its upright stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from greenish-white to reddish-purple, depending on the variety. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, streambanks, and marshy areas.

Polygonum miquelianum is known for its rapid growth and can become invasive in certain environments.
