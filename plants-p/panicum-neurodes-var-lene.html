Panicum neurodes var. lene

Panicum neurodes var. lene, commonly known as slender panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along riverbanks. The plant features slender, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are narrow and often have a bluish-green color, with a distinct midrib.

The inflorescence consists of loose, open panicles, which produce small, spikelet-based flowers.
