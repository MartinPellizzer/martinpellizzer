Parodia miguillensis

Parodia miguillensis, commonly known as the Miguillense Cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white, radial spines. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are often followed by fruit.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and collectors.
