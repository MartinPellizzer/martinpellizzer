Peponia lagenarioides

Peponia lagenarioides, commonly known as the bottle gourd or calabash, is a flowering plant in the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas and is characterized by its large, bottle-shaped fruits. It is often cultivated for its edible young fruits and has been used historically for making containers and utensils.
