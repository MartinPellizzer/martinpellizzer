Potentilla × diskleii

Potentilla × diskleii, commonly known as the shrub cinquefoil, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and flowers. The plant produces small, yellowish to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its leaves are typically lobed and have a slightly rough texture, contributing to its ornamental value.
