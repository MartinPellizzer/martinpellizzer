Polygala wurdackiana

Polygala wurdackiana, commonly known as the Wurdack's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, and it produces small, oval-shaped fruits.
