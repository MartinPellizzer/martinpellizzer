Pittosporum tenuifolium subsp. colensoi

Pittosporum tenuifolium subsp.

colensoi, commonly known as Colenso's pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand. It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are narrow and lance-shaped.

The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by small, red berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
