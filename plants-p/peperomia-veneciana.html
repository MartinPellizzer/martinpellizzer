Peperomia veneciana

Peperomia veneciana, commonly known as the Venetian Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its compact size and ornamental foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container plantings.
