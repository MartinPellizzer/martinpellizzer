Poa boelckei

Poa boelckei, commonly known as the Boelcke's bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, low-growing habit and fine, bluish-green leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

Poa boelckei is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in wetland habitats.
