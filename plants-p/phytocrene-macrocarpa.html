Phytocrene macrocarpa

Phytocrene macrocarpa, commonly known as the giant cup plant, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has a robust root system and can grow up to several feet tall. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a hairy texture.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.
