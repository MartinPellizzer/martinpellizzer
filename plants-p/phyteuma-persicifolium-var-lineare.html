Phyteuma persicifolium var. lineare

Phyteuma persicifolium var. lineare, commonly known as the linear-leaved phyteuma, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It features upright, branching stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, pink to purple flowers. The leaves are elongated and linear, giving the plant its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in alpine or rocky habitats.

This variety is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
