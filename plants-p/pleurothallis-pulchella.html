Pleurothallis pulchella

Pleurothallis pulchella, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Colombia and Ecuador.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil to flourish.

Its leaves are usually elongated and grass-like, contributing to its overall ornamental appeal.
