Paphiopedilum boissierianum

Paphiopedilum boissierianum, commonly known as the Boissier's lady's slipper, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a unique, pouch-like lip that is a defining characteristic of the Paphiopedilum genus. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. Its flowers are usually pale pink to white with intricate patterns, and it blooms during the late spring to early summer.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty and rarity.
