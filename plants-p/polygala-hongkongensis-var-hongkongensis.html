Polygala hongkongensis var. hongkongensis

Polygala hongkongensis var. hongkongensis, commonly known as Hong Kong milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, herbaceous stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its leaves are alternate and pinnately compound, with small, ovate leaflets.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
