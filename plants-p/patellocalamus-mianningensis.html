Patellocalamus mianningensis

Patellocalamus mianningensis, commonly known as the Mianning grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan province. This plant belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

The plant is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and potential ecological significance in its native habitat.
