Peperomia punctatifolia var. munyecoana

Peperomia punctatifolia var. munyecoana, commonly known as the Munyecoan Peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with distinctive white or silver markings, giving it a striking visual appearance. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape and often have a velvety texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color.

This variety is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its resilience and minimal care requirements.
