Piper yunckeri var. latum

Piper yunckeri var. latum, commonly known as the broadleaf pepper plant, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various pepper species. The plant is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and small, clustered flowers. Its fruits are typically small berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

This variant is noted for its potential use in traditional medicine and as a spice in local cuisines.
