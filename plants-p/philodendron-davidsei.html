Philodendron davidsei

Philodendron davidsei, commonly known as the Davidsei Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with light green or white patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by berries containing seeds. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Philodendron davidsei is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
