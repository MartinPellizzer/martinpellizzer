Physaria pulvinata

Physaria pulvinata, commonly known as the bladderpod, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant features showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a bluish-green hue.

The plant produces small, round seed pods that split open to release its seeds.
