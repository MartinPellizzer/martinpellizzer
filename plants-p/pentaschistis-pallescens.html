Pentaschistis pallescens

Pentaschistis pallescens, commonly known as the pale sandwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It features small, white to pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the cooler months. The plant has a low-growing habit with succulent, fleshy leaves that help it conserve water in its harsh environment. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to anchor firmly in sandy or rocky soils.

Pentaschistis pallescens is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
