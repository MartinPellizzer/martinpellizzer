Panicum turnerianum

Panicum turnerianum, commonly known as Turner's panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, erect stems. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its leaves are narrow and often have a bluish-green hue, and it is typically found in disturbed soils or open habitats.

Panicum turnerianum is sometimes used in ornamental gardening due to its delicate appearance and adaptability.
