Pyrostegia ornata

Pyrostegia ornata, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, pointed leaflets. Its rapid growth rate makes it a popular choice for ornamental planting in warm climates.

However, it can become invasive in some regions if not managed properly.
