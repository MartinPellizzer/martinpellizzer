Porphyrostemma grantii var. chevalieri

Porphyrostemma grantii var. chevalieri, commonly known as the Malagasy orchid, is a rare and striking plant native to Madagascar.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant purple and pink flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are usually broad and glossy, and it produces intricate, showy inflorescences.
