Polygala verticillata var. sphenostachya

Polygala verticillata var. sphenostachya, commonly known as the narrow-leaved milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture. This species thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal or semi-arid regions.

It plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
