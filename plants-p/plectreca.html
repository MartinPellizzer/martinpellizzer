Plectreca

Plectreca, commonly known as the false hellebore, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Polygonaceae.

It consists of several species native to Europe and western Asia, typically found in moist, shaded environments. The plants have simple, alternate leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. They produce small, clustered flowers that are usually greenish or reddish in color.
