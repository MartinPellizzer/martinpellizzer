Pleurothallis volans

Pleurothallis volans, commonly known as the flying pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features slender, branching stems and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. Its flowers are typically arranged in racemes and bloom during the rainy season.

Pleurothallis volans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
