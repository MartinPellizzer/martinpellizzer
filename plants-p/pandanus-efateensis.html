Pandanus efateensis

Pandanus efateensis, commonly known as the Efate pandanus, is a species of palm native to Vanuatu.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are often used in traditional crafts and for thatching in the local communities.

The Efate pandanus plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
