Potentilla fragarioides var. japonica

Potentilla fragarioides var. japonica, commonly known as Japanese cinquefoil, is a perennial shrub native to East Asia.

It features small, yellow, five-petaled flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has a compact growth habit with upright stems and trifoliate leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.
