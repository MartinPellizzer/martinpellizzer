Philodendron jenmanii

Philodendron jenmanii, commonly known as the Jenman Philodendron, is a climbing plant native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green coloration, often with a slight reddish tinge on the veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by red berries, which are an important food source for local wildlife. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Philodendron jenmanii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
