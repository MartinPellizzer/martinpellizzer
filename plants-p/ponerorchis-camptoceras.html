Ponerorchis camptoceras

Ponerorchis camptoceras, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a rare and specialized orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and green, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is typically found in dense forests.

Due to its specific habitat requirements and limited distribution, it is considered an endangered species.
