Peperomia pseudomajor

Peperomia pseudomajor, commonly known as the broadleaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, usually in shades of green or red, which are followed by small, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its low maintenance and attractive foliage make it a versatile plant for both outdoor gardens and indoor spaces.
