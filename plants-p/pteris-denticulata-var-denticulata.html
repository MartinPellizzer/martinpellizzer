Pteris denticulata var. denticulata

Pteris denticulata var. denticulata, commonly known as the serrated water fern, is a fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It features fronds that are deeply divided and have a distinctive serrated edge, giving it its common name. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found near water bodies. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and indoor spaces.
