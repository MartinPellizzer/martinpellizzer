Philodendron rimachii

Philodendron rimachii, commonly known as the golden heart philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, waxy leaves that often exhibit a striking yellow-green to golden hue. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. Its aerial roots allow it to climb and anchor itself to various surfaces.

Philodendron rimachii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
