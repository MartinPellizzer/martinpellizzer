Ptilimnium nuttallii

Ptilimnium nuttallii, commonly known as Nuttall's ptilimnium, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in early summer. Its leaves are narrowly elliptic and often have a distinctive wavy margin.

The species is characterized by its ability to colonize disturbed soils and is often found in areas with high moisture content.
