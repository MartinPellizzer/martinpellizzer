Phyllostegia olokuiensis

Phyllostegia olokuiensis, commonly known as the Olokui ferntree, is a species native to the tropical regions of the Pacific.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in coastal or lowland areas. Its botanical features include opposite leaf arrangement and a distinctive, fern-like appearance.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
