Polyscias acuminata

Polyscias acuminata, commonly known as the sharp-pointed sycamore, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in umbels, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
