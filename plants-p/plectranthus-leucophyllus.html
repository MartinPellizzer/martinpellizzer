Plectranthus leucophyllus

Plectranthus leucophyllus, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its aromatic leaves and ornamental value make it both a decorative and potentially useful plant in horticulture.
