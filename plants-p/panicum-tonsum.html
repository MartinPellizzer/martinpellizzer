Panicum tonsum

Panicum tonsum, commonly known as African millet, is a grass species native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and small, spikelet-like inflorescences. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in poor soil conditions, making it a hardy crop. Its seeds are often used as a staple food in many African countries.

The plant also has cultural and traditional significance in various communities.
