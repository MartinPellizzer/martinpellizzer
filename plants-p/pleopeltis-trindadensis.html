Pleopeltis trindadensis

Pleopeltis trindadensis, commonly known as the Trinidad rock fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, dense growth habit. The plant typically grows on rock surfaces or tree trunks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its fronds are usually short and broad, with a leathery texture that helps it retain moisture.

Pleopeltis trindadensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
