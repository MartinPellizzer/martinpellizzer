Polygala hongkongensis var. stenophylla

Polygala hongkongensis var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Hong Kong.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and small, clustered flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and linear, giving the plant its specific epithet "stenophylla.". It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

The plant is noted for its delicate appearance and is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
