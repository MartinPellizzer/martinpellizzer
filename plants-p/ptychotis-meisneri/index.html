Ptychotis Meisneri

Blue-eyed mary, scientifically known as Ptychotis meisneri, is a delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America. With its striking blue petals and white centers, it resembles a tiny portrait of a human eye, earning it its charming common name. This perennial plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded woodland areas. Its unique appearance and subtle beauty make it a popular choice among gardeners seeking a touch of elegance. Despite its modest size, Blue-eyed mary holds cultural and ecological significance in its natural habitat.
