Peristethium polystachyum

Peristethium polystachyum, commonly known as the mountain bamboo or giant bamboo, is a member of the grass family (Poaceae).

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish flowers at the ends of its stalks. Its leaves are long, narrow, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Peristethium polystachyum is typically found in high-altitude regions with cool, moist climates.
