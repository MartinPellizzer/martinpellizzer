Phlomoides fulgens

Phlomoides fulgens, commonly known as the blazing phlomis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces showy, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its rugged appearance.

Phlomoides fulgens thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens and borders.
