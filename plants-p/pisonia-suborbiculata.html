Pisonia suborbiculata

Pisonia suborbiculata, commonly known as the red pisonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, bright red flowers that attract birds and insects. The plant produces small, round, woody fruits that contain numerous seeds. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, often found in coastal and forested areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.
