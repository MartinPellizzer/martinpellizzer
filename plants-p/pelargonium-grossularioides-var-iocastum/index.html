Pelargonium Grossularioides Var. Iocastum

Pelargonium grossularioides var. iocastum is a rare and striking plant known for its unique foliage and delicate flowers. Native to South Africa, it thrives in specific conditions that mimic its natural habitat. Its leaves are often described as resembling miniature citrus fruits, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant is highly sought after by collectors due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal. Proper care is essential to ensure its survival and growth in cultivation.
