Primula cawdoriana

Primula cawdoriana, commonly known as the Cawdor primrose, is a species of flowering plant native to the Scottish Highlands.

It is characterized by its vibrant, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments, often in woodlands or along stream banks. It has dark green, heart-shaped leaves and is known for its early spring blooming habit.

Primula cawdoriana is a valued ornamental plant and is also of interest to botanists due to its unique ecological adaptations.
