Peristylus Timorensis

Peristylus timorensis is a rare and unique plant native to the island of Timor. It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is known for its striking, tubular flowers. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions. Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts. Its ornamental value has made it a sought-after plant among horticulturists.
