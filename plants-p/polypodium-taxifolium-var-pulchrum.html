Polypodium taxifolium var. pulchrum

Polypodium taxifolium var. pulchrum, commonly known as the beautiful holly fern, is a variant of the common holly fern.

It is a fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense, moss-like growth, which helps in moisture retention.

The fronds are bi-lobed and have a distinctive, almost holly-like appearance, giving it its common name.
