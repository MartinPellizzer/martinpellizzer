Pentace excelsa

Pentace excelsa, commonly known as the African star, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive yellowish-green color. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters, and it produces small, woody fruits.

Pentace excelsa is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, arid environments.
