Penicillaria solitaria

Penicillaria solitaria, commonly known as the solitary penicillaria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, sandy soils. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in arid gardens.
