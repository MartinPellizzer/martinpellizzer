Phlomis bovei

Phlomis bovei, commonly known as the Bove's blazingstar, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features tall, branching stems covered in soft, grayish-green foliage. Its showy, yellow flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
