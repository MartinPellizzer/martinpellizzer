Portulacaria afra f. variegata

Portulacaria afra f. variegata, commonly known as the elephant's food or African ice plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by orange-red berries. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
