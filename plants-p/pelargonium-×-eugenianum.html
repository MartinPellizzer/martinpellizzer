Pelargonium × eugenianum

Pelargonium × eugenianum, commonly known as the Regal Geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, single blooms that range in color from white to deep red, often with a distinctive dark eye in the center. The plant typically grows as an upright herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 30 cm. Its leaves are palmately lobed and have a textured, slightly hairy surface.

Pelargonium × eugenianum thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.
