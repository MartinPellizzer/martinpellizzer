Pachycentria hanseniana

Pachycentria hanseniana, commonly known as the Hansen's thick-shelled turtle.

It is a species of freshwater turtle native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the rivers of Thailand and Malaysia. The shell of this turtle is thick and robust, providing significant protection against predators. It has a distinctive appearance with a dark, almost black shell and a streamlined body adapted for aquatic life.

This species plays an important role in its ecosystem as both a predator and a prey animal.
