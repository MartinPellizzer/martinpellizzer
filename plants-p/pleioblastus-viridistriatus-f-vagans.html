Pleioblastus viridistriatus f. vagans

Pleioblastus viridistriatus f. vagans, commonly known as the silver stripe bamboo, is a cultivar of the green stripe bamboo species.

It is characterized by its striking variegated foliage, featuring a combination of light green and white or silver stripes along the leaf blades. This bamboo is native to East Asia and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. It grows as a clumping bamboo, forming dense stands with slender, upright stems.

The plant is relatively low-maintenance, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sun exposure.
