Polygala senegambica

Polygala senegambica, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the savannas of Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and delicate, often pink or white flowers. The plant produces small, bead-like fruits that contain the seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, seasonal climates.

Polygala senegambica is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
