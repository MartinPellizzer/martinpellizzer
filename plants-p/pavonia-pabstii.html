Pavonia pabstii

Pavonia pabstii, commonly known as the Pabst's pride-of-Egypt, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features palmately lobed leaves with a rough texture.
