Piper barbatum

Piper barbatum, commonly known as the bat's wing or bat's wing pepper, is a climbing vine native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. Its leaves contain various bioactive compounds, making it valuable in both medicinal and culinary applications.

Piper barbatum is also noted for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage.
