Polystachya hypocrita

Polystachya hypocrita, commonly known as the red-tailed bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces inflorescences that are often reddish at the base, giving it its common name. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in forest understories.

Polystachya hypocrita is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
