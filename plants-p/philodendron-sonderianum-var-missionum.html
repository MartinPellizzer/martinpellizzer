Philodendron sonderianum var. missionum

Philodendron sonderianum var. missionum, commonly known as the Mission Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly variegated pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers, though it is more valued for its foliage than its blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and humidity.

Its rapid growth and attractive leaves make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
