Persoonia gunnii var. alpina

Persoonia gunnii var. alpina, commonly known as the alpine persoonia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its small, rounded leaves and clusters of yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to harsh, alpine environments. It is often found in the southern regions of Western Australia.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
