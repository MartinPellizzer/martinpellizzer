Pelargonium × glaciale

Pelargonium × glaciale, commonly known as the frost geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The leaves are typically rounded and often have a slightly hairy texture. Native to South Africa, it thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it well-suited for rock gardens and shaded borders.
