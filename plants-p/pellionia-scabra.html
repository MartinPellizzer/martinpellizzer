Pellionia scabra

Pellionia scabra, commonly known as rough pellionia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves with a distinctive rough texture on the upper surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Pellionia scabra is valued for its ornamental foliage and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
