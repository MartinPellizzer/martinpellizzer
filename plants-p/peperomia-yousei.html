Peperomia yousei

Peperomia yousei, commonly known as the Silver Bush Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense clusters of small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically light green with a silvery underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color, which appear in the axils of the leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia yousei is relatively low maintenance and resistant to pests, making it an ideal plant for both novice and experienced growers.
