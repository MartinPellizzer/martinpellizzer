Peddiea bambuseti

Peddiea bambuseti, commonly known as the bamboo orchid, is a rare and unique plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, bamboo-like stems and delicate, grass-like leaves. Native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers, which appear in clusters, are typically small and have a subtle fragrance.
