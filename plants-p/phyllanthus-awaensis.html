Phyllanthus awaensis

It belongs to the family Phyllanthaceae and is characterized by its small, clustered leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
