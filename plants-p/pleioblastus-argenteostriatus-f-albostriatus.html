Pleioblastus argenteostriatus f. albostriatus

Pleioblastus argenteostriatus f. albostriatus, commonly known as the silver-striped bamboo, is a variant of the silver stripe bamboo characterized by its striking white streaks on the green stems.

It is a clump-forming grass that grows to a moderate height, typically reaching around 2 to 3 meters. The leaves are long and narrow, with a slightly bluish-green hue and a waxy texture. This plant is well-suited for ornamental use in gardens and is relatively low maintenance.

It thrives in partial to full sunlight and prefers well-drained soil.
