Pedilonum eitapense

Pedilonum eitapense, commonly known as the Eitapen Flameleaf, is a rare flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Eitap.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored leaves that range from deep red to orange, often with intricate vein patterns. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of yellow and pink, which bloom during the wet season. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the rich, moist soil of its natural habitat.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, Pedilonum eitapense is considered a botanical curiosity and is often studied for its potential medicinal properties.
