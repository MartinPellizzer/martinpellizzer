Pimelea phylicoides var. husseyana

Pimelea phylicoides var. husseyana, commonly known as the Grey Mulla Mulla, is a low-growing shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, needle-like leaves that are typically grey-green in color, providing a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and open woodlands.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
