Peucedanum japonicum var. latifolium

Peucedanum japonicum var. latifolium, commonly known as Japanese cowparsley, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It features compound leaves with broad, ovate leaflets and produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in umbels. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its fruit is a dry, two-lobed schizocarp, and it is part of the Apiaceae family, known for its aromatic compounds.

It has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
