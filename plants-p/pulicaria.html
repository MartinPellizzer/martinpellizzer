Pulicaria

Pulicaria, commonly known as silverweed, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

These plants are characterized by their silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. They are typically found in arid or rocky environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Pulicaria species produce small, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to yellow.

The genus includes several species, some of which are used in traditional medicine or as ornamental plants.
