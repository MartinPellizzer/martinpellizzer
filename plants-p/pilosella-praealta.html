Pilosella praealta

Pilosella praealta, commonly known as the tall pearly everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features wiry, branched stems covered in dense, white or grayish hairs. Its flowers are typically yellow to orange in color, with ray florets that are shorter than the disk florets.

Pilosella praealta is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
