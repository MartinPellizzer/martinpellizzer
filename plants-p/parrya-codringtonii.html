Parrya codringtonii

Parrya codringtonii, commonly known as the Codrington's parrya, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically produces yellow-orange composite flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.
