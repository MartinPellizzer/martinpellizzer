Piper biolleyi

Piper biolleyi, commonly known as the Bollyei pepper, is a climbing vine native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when mature, and they are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a distinctive, peppery aroma.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
