Pitcairnia Nigra Var. Pulchella

Pitcairnia nigra var. pulchella is a striking bromeliad native to the rainforests of South America. Known for its dark, velvety leaves and vibrant orange flowers, this plant is a popular choice among tropical plant enthusiasts. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its unique appearance makes it a standout addition to both indoor and outdoor gardens. Careful attention to its specific growing conditions ensures its health and longevity.
