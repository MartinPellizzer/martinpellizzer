Pentas decora f. opposita

Pentas decora f. opposita, commonly known as the opposite pentas, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its opposite leaf arrangement, which distinguishes it from other varieties of Pentas decora. The plant produces clusters of showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, making it a popular ornamental species. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This cultivar is often used in gardens and landscapes for its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
