Phlomis jeholensis

Phlomis jeholensis, commonly known as Jehol phlomis, is a perennial herb native to northern China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features tall, erect stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. Its flowers are typically pink to purple, arranged in dense spikes at the top of the plant.

It is often found in rocky or gravelly soils in mountainous regions.
