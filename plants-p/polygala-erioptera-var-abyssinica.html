Polygala erioptera var. abyssinica

Polygala erioptera var. abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces delicate, pale yellow to greenish flowers that are often covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a hairy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This species is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient plant in challenging climates.
