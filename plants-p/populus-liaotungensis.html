Populus liaotungensis

Populus liaotungensis, commonly known as the Liaotung poplar, is a deciduous tree native to northeastern China.

It belongs to the Salicaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and distinctive bark. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in moist, well-drained soils. It plays an important role in ecological restoration due to its tolerance to environmental stress.

Its flowers are catkins, and it produces small, winged seeds that are dispersed by wind.
