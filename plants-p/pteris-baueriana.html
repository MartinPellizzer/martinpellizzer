Pteris baueriana

Pteris baueriana, commonly known as the Bauer's water fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are bipinnate, with numerous small, pointed pinnae that give it a lacy appearance.

Pteris baueriana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
