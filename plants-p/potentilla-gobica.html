Potentilla gobica

Potentilla gobica, commonly known as the mountain potentilla, is a low-growing shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has holly-like leaves that are elliptical in shape and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or rocky habitats.

Potentilla gobica is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh environmental conditions.
