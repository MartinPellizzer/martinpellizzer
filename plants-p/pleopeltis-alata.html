Pleopeltis alata

Pleopeltis alata, commonly known as the broad-leafed rock fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its large, leathery, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its fronds are often divided into segments with a distinctive, wavy margin.
