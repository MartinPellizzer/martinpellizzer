Piper balaoense

Piper balaoense, commonly known as the Balaoense pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with long, slender stems that often twine around supports. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a glossy surface, while the flowers are small and clustered in spikes.

The fruit is a small, elongated berry that contains several seeds, typical of the Piper genus.
