Picea nordmanniana

Picea nordmanniana, commonly known as the Nordmann fir, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its dense, pyramidal shape and needle-like leaves that are blue-green in color. The tree produces small, cylindrical cones that mature to a reddish-brown hue. Its bark is thick and scaly, providing protection against harsh environmental conditions.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
