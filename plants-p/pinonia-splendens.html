Pinonia splendens

Pinonia splendens, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fiery orange to red flowers that bloom in dense clusters during the summer months. The plant has palmate leaves that are deeply lobed, adding to its visual appeal. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for warm climates.

Its rapid growth and showy display make it a popular choice for gardens and public landscapes.
