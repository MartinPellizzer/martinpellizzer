Parodia macrancistra

Parodia macrancistra, commonly known as the giant parodia, is a species of cactus native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stems that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces large, white flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking visual contrast to its green foliage. It is a slow-growing species that requires well-draining soil and minimal water to thrive.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
