Peperomia mosenii

Peperomia mosenii, commonly known as the Mosenii Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features oval to elliptical, glossy dark green leaves that are often slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with tiny white to pale pink flowers, which are not particularly showy but contribute to its ornamental value. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia mosenii is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and tolerating a range of indoor conditions.
