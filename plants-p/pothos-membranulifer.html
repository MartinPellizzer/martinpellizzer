Pothos membranulifer

Pothos membranulifer, commonly known as the velvet leaf pothos, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its waxy, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
