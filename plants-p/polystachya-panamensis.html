Polystachya panamensis

Polystachya panamensis, commonly known as the Panamanian sword fern, is a member of the Polystachyaceae family.

It is native to Central and South America, particularly found in humid tropical regions. This plant is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, pinnate leaves that resemble a sword. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant's ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
