Peperomia multiplinervia

Peperomia multiplinervia, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are often velvety to the touch, giving it its common name. The plant has a compact growth habit with multiple stems, making it suitable for indoor cultivation. Its foliage is characterized by intricate, multi-nerved veins that add to its ornamental appeal.

Peperomia multiplinervia thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
