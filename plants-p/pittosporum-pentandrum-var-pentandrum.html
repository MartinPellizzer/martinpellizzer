Pittosporum pentandrum var. pentandrum

Pittosporum pentandrum var. pentandrum, commonly known as the golden pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are glossy green and often have a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by orange-red berries. Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and showy floral display.

It thrives in well-drained soil and is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
