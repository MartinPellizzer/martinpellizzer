Pleopeltis × pinnatisecta

Pleopeltis × pinnatisecta, commonly known as the hybrid tree fern, is a natural hybrid between Pleopeltis polypodioides and Pleopeltis pinnatisecta.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds that can reach several meters in length. The fronds are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, with a distinct midrib and lance-shaped pinnae. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its unique morphology makes it a striking addition to botanical gardens and a subject of interest in plant hybridization studies.
