Phymatopteris quasidivaricata

Phymatopteris quasidivaricata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf wallriven fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is native to the southeastern United States, particularly found in Georgia and Florida. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds in clusters, which is a common feature among ferns.
