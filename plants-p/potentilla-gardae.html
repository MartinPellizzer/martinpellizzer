Potentilla gardae

Potentilla gardae, commonly known as the Garden Potentilla, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun conditions. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slight hairy texture.
