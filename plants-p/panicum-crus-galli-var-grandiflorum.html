Panicum crus-galli var. grandiflorum

Panicum crus-galli var. grandiflorum, commonly known as purple lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and fluffy, purple-tinted panicles that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, wind-pollinated flowers arranged in loose, branching clusters. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it adaptable to various environments.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its striking, feathery appearance and ability to attract wildlife.
