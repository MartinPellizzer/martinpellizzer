Peperomia umbricola var. glabrior

Peperomia umbricola var. glabrior, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features broad, waxy leaves with a velvety texture, often displaying shades of green, silver, and purple. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, which appear in clusters. It thrives in indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for tabletop displays and as a decorative houseplant.
