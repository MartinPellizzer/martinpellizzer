Petunia regnellii

Petunia regnellii, commonly known as the violet petunia, is a species native to South America.

It is a herbaceous plant that typically grows to a height of about 10 to 15 centimeters. The plant features showy, funnel-shaped flowers that range in color from violet to blue and purple. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine, soft hairs.

Petunia regnellii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and containers.
