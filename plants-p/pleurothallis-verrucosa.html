Pleurothallis verrucosa

Pleurothallis verrucosa, commonly known as the疣状万代兰, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a slight purple tint. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Pleurothallis verrucosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
