Pogonarthria falcata var. condensata

Pogonarthria falcata var. condensata, commonly known as the denseleaf grass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and erosion control.
