Peperomia tecticola var. tilirina

Peperomia tecticola var. tilirina, commonly known as the silver velvet peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features dark green, velvety leaves with a striking silver or white midrib, giving it a unique and elegant appearance. The plant has small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically borne in clusters near the base of the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its attractive foliage and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
