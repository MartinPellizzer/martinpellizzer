Phagnalon vacciniifolium

Phagnalon vacciniifolium, commonly known as red sandrift, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Its leaves are usually narrow and slightly hairy, and it produces small, wind-pollinated flowers that attract various pollinators.

Phagnalon vacciniifolium is known for its resilience to harsh environmental conditions and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
