Panicum herzogii

Panicum herzogii, commonly known as Herzog's panicgrass, is a species of grass native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial plant characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems. The leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, two-lobed spikelets.

This plant is often found in open, sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
