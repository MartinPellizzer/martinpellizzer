Protasparagus bayeri

Protasparagus bayeri, commonly known as the bayern asparagus.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asparagaceae, characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. Native to southern Africa, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive, compact growth habit.

The plant is also noted for its ability to tolerate drought conditions once established.
