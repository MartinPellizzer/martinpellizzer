Phlomoides transoxana

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. It typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in steppe and desert environments.
