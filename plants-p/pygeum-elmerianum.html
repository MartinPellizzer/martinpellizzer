Pygeum elmerianum

Pygeum elmerianum, commonly known as the African sycamore, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and large, heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions and is often found in woodland and savanna habitats.

Pygeum elmerianum is valued for its hard, durable wood and has been used traditionally for various purposes.
