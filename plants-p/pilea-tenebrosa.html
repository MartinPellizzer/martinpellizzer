Pilea tenebrosa

Pilea tenebrosa, commonly known as the dark pilea, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves that are slightly hairy and have a unique, almost velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. It prefers indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact growth habit and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to many plant collections.
