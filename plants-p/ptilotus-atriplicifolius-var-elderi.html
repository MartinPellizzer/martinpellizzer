Ptilotus atriplicifolius var. elderi

Ptilotus atriplicifolius var. elderi, commonly known as the Elder's ptilotus, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, spiky inflorescences. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid regions, showcasing adaptations to harsh environmental conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.
