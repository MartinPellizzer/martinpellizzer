Poinciana Punctata

The poinciana punctata, also known as the flame tree, is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant red and orange flowers. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in warm climates and is often planted in gardens for its ornamental value. Its feathery leaves and showy blooms make it a popular choice for landscaping and public spaces. The plant is easy to care for, requiring well-drained soil and plenty of sunlight. With its eye-catching appearance, the poinciana punctata adds a burst of color to any environment.
