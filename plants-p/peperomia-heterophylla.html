Peperomia heterophylla

Peperomia heterophylla, commonly known as the variegated peperomia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features oval to lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are typically green or red and appear in clusters.

Peperomia heterophylla is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and low maintenance requirements.
