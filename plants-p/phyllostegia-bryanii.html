Phyllostegia bryanii

Phyllostegia bryanii, commonly known as the Bryan's stiltgrass, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, creeping stems that often root at the nodes, forming dense mats. Its small, lance-shaped leaves are opposite and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are pale blue to purple, arranged in whorls along the stems.

This species is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
