Platystele oxyglossa

Platystele oxyglossa, commonly known as the narrow-leafed platystele, is a rare and endangered epiphytic orchid native to the cloud forests of Colombia and Ecuador.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, strap-shaped leaves and delicate, white flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high air humidity and indirect light for optimal growth. Its unique morphology and habitat make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat loss, it faces significant threats to its survival in the wild.
