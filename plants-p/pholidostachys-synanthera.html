Pholidostachys synanthera

Pholidostachys synanthera, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of yellow flowers that resemble small showers, hence its common name. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are usually glossy green. It produces small, fleshy fruits that are edible and contain seeds.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
