Pachycarpus natalensis

Pachycarpus natalensis, commonly known as Natal thornveld, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by fleshy, red berries. It thrives in dry, rocky environments and is adapted to withstand periods of drought.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the fynbos vegetation in its native habitat.
