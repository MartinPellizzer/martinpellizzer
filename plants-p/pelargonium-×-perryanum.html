Pelargonium × perryanum

Pelargonium × perryanum, commonly known as the perryanum geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically has rounded, lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Pelargonium × perryanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in floral arrangements.
