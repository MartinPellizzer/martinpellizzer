Panicum vexillare

Panicum vexillare, commonly known as the purpletop grass, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and distinctive purple-tinted inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Panicum vexillare plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing forage for wildlife.
