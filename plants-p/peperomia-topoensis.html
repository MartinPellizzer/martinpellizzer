Peperomia topoensis

Peperomia topoensis, commonly known as the Topoensis Pepperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are often heart-shaped or oval in form. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale pink flowers that bloom during the warmer months. Peperomia topoensis thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its low maintenance and attractive foliage make it a desirable addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
