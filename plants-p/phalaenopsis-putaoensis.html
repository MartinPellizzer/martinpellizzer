Phalaenopsis putaoensis

Phalaenopsis putaoensis, commonly known as the Putao orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, long-lasting flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
