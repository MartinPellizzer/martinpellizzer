Paeonia willmottiae

Paeonia willmottiae, commonly known as the Willmott's peony, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, single or semi-double flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green and velvety in texture. It blooms in late spring to early summer and is valued for its ornamental beauty in gardens.

Paeonia willmottiae is relatively low maintenance and thrives in well-drained soil with partial to full sun.
