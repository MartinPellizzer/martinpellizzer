Piper hohenackeri

Piper hohenackeri, commonly known as the Hohenacker's pepper, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are berries that turn from green to yellow as they mature.

Piper hohenackeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
