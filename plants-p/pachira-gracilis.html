Pachira gracilis

Pachira gracilis, commonly known as the slender pachira, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and smooth, dark green leaves. The plant produces striking yellow flowers that are often followed by orange or red fruits containing seeds. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.

Pachira gracilis prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
