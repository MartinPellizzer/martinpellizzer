Phalaenopsis amabilis subvar. erubescens

Phalaenopsis amabilis subvar. erubescens, commonly known as the Red Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendant inflorescences that bear clusters of showy, velvety flowers in shades of red and pink. The plant has broad, dark green leaves and produces fragrant blooms that can last for several months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent humidity and well-draining potting media.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
