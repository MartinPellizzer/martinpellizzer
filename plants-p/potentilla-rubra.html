Potentilla rubra

Potentilla rubra, commonly known as scarlet cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its bright red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are palmately divided into five lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Potentilla rubra is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
