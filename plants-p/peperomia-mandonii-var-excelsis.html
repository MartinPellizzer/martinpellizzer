Peperomia mandonii var. excelsis

Peperomia mandonii var. excelsis, commonly known as the Emerald Gem Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, ovate leaves that are typically bright green with a slight sheen, often adorned with subtle variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
