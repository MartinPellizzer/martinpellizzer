Phyllostachys edulis f. gimmei

Phyllostachys edulis f. gimmei, commonly known as the golden bamboo, is a variant of the bamboo species Phyllostachys edulis.

It is characterized by its striking golden-green culms that contrast with its dark green leaves. This ornamental bamboo is native to China and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. It grows in clumps and can reach heights of up to 10 meters, making it suitable for privacy screens or landscape accents.

Its unique coloration makes it a popular choice for both garden design and horticultural collections.
