Panicum pilipes

Panicum pilipes, commonly known as the silky grass or silky panicum, is a species of grass native to parts of Africa and the Americas.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces fluffy, pale green to brownish spikelets that are typical of the grass family Poaceae. It thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as fields and roadsides.

Panicum pilipes is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil.
