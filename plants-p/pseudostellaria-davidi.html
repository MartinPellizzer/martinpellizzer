Pseudostellaria davidi

Pseudostellaria davidi, commonly known as David's starwort, is a small, herbaceous perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are slender and branching.

Pseudostellaria davidi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its subtle beauty.
