Pteris leptophylla var. latisecta

Pteris leptophylla var. latisecta, commonly known as the broad-leafed water fern, is a variant of the water fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, deeply divided fronds that give it a feathery appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found near streams or in humid forests. Its fronds are typically bright green and can reach lengths of up to 1 meter.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in aquatic gardens or as a houseplant.
