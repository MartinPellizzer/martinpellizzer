Puccinellia distans var. halophila

Puccinellia distans var. halophila, commonly known as saltgrass, is a halophytic plant adapted to saline environments.

It is a perennial grass that thrives in coastal areas, salt marshes, and other areas with high soil salinity. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and preventing erosion in salt-affected regions.

Its ability to tolerate high salt concentrations makes it valuable for ecological restoration projects.
