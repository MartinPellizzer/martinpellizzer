Persoonia muelleri subsp. angustifolia

Persoonia muelleri subsp.

angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved geebung, is a shrub native to eastern Australia. It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine, white hairs.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in spikes, which bloom in spring. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in heathlands and dry forests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
