Peperomia perciliata

Peperomia perciliata, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, waxy leaves that are usually dark green with silver or white markings, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has upright stems and small, inconspicuous flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia perciliata is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in a variety of potting soils.
