Philadelphus schrenkii var. jackii

Philadelphus schrenkii var. jackii, commonly known as the Chinese mock orange, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and can grow up to 3 meters in height. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its pleasant scent and showy blossoms.
