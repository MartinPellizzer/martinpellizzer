Phoebe lichuanensis

Phoebe lichuanensis, commonly known as the Lichuan phoebe, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to China, specifically the provinces of Guizhou, Sichuan, and Yunnan. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are typically elliptical with a smooth or slightly toothed margin.

Phoebe lichuanensis is often found in moist, shaded forest environments, where it thrives in the understory of tropical and subtropical woodlands.
