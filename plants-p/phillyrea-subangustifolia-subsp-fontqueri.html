Phillyrea subangustifolia subsp. fontqueri

Phillyrea subangustifolia subsp.

fontqueri, commonly known as the Fontqueri Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula. It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy on the underside.

The plant produces white, fragrant flowers in clusters during the spring, which are followed by small, woody fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

The subspecies is noted for its ornamental value and hardiness in Mediterranean climates.
