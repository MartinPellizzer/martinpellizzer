Panicum myuros

Panicum myuros, commonly known as prostrate panicgrass, is a flowering plant species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a low-growing perennial grass that typically forms dense mats on the ground. The plant has slender, branching stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered with fine hairs.

Panicum myuros is adapted to poor soils and is often found in disturbed or arid environments.
