Piper glabrilimbum

Piper glabrilimbum, commonly known as the smooth-leaved pepper vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. Its small, greenish-white flowers are followed by small, round berries that turn red when mature.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
