Paeonia corallina var. triternata

Paeonia corallina var. triternata, commonly known as the coral peony, is a variety of the coral peony species native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers with a distinctive coral-like appearance. The plant is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. Its leaves are compound, consisting of three to five leaflets, and it produces showy blooms in late spring to early summer.

This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
