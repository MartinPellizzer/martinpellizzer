Phillyrea cordifolia subsp. vinyalsii

Phillyrea cordifolia subsp.

vinyalsii, commonly known as the Mediterranean Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, ovate, dark green leaves with a leathery texture.

The plant produces yellowish-white flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, spherical fruits. It is typically found in rocky or limestone soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

This subspecies is notable for its slow growth and long lifespan, often living for several decades.
