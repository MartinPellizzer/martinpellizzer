Philodendron krebsii

Philodendron krebsii, commonly known as the silver stripe philodendron, is a climbing tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with distinctive silver or white stripes that run vertically along the leaf surface. The plant has a vining growth habit and produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are typically glossy and can grow quite large, making it a striking addition to indoor spaces.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.
