Primulina tiandengensis

Primulina tiandengensis, commonly known as the Tiandeng primula, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, spurred flowers. The plant typically features broad, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and produces showy blooms in shades of pink and white. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
