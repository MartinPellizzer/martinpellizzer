Pandanus kennedyensis

Pandanus kennedyensis, commonly known as the Kennedy's pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pandanaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of northern Australia, particularly the Northern Territory and Queensland. The plant is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or green, arranged in dense clusters.

The species is often found in coastal or sandy environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
