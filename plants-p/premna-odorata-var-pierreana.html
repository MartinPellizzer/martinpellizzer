Premna odorata var. pierreana

Premna odorata var. pierreana, commonly known as the fragrant premna, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

The leaves are opposite, ovate to elliptic in shape, and often have a pleasant, spicy scent when crushed.
