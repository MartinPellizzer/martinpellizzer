Piper obovatum

Piper obovatum, commonly known as the broad-leafed pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly the Amazon basin. The plant is characterized by its glossy, ovate leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. It produces small, peppery fruits that are used in traditional medicine and cuisine.

Piper obovatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.
