Peperomia vanheurckii

Peperomia vanheurckii, commonly known as the Vanheurck's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, often with a slight pubescence. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish or reddish in color, arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia vanheurckii is known for its ability to tolerate low light conditions and is relatively easy to care for.
