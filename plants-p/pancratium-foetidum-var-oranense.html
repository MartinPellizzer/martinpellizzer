Pancratium foetidum var. oranense

Pancratium foetidum var. oranense, commonly known as the Orange Crowned Flower, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange and yellow flowers that resemble a crown, giving it its common name. The plant produces narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.
