Pilea peplidifolia

Pilea peplidifolia, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically round or heart-shaped, often with a slightly notched or lobed appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Pilea peplidifolia is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
