Platycoryne mediocris

Platycoryne mediocris, commonly known as the small-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in a raceme. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories.

Due to its delicate appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
