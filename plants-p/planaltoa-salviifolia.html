Planaltoa salviifolia

Planaltoa salviifolia, commonly known as the Mexican sage.

It is a flowering plant native to Mexico, belonging to the Lamiaceae family. The plant features aromatic, lance-shaped leaves and produces spikes of purple or blue flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or limestone habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in gardens and used in traditional herbal remedies.
