Parita

Parita, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its blue or purple ray flowers and yellow disk flowers. It is also known for its ability to tolerate salt spray, making it well adapted to coastal conditions.
