Phyllostachys puberula f. nigropunctata

Phyllostachys puberula f. nigropunctata, commonly known as the black dot bamboo, is a variant of the silver bamboo species.

It is characterized by its dark green, slender culms that are adorned with small, black specks along the nodes. The plant is a clumping bamboo that grows in dense stands, often reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

This ornamental bamboo is valued for its unique appearance and is often used in landscaping and garden design.
