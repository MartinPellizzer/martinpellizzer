Polygala bringelii

Polygala bringelii, commonly known as the Cape horsehead, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, horsehoe-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has adapted to sandy, well-drained soils. It produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom throughout the summer months.

Polygala bringelii is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
