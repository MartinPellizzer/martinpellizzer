Pilea wollastonii

Pilea wollastonii, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rosettes of rounded, fleshy, dark green leaves that resemble small coins, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It is a popular indoor plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.

Pilea wollastonii thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
