Platanthera undulata

Platanthera undulata, commonly known as the wavy-leaf twayblade, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, greenish-yellow flowers. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and wet meadows. It plays a role in supporting pollinators and is often found in nutrient-poor soils.
