Podocarpus madagascariensis var. rotundus

Podocarpus madagascariensis var. rotundus, commonly known as the rounded podocarp, is a slow-growing evergreen tree native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and compact, rounded shape. The leaves are leathery and have a glossy appearance, contributing to its ornamental value. It produces small, cone-like structures that contain its seeds, typical of the Podocarpaceae family.

This variety is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
