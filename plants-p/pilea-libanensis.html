Pilea libanensis

Pilea libanensis, commonly known as the Lebanese prayer plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Middle East.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Pilea libanensis is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
