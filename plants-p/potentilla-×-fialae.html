Potentilla × fialae

Potentilla × fialae, commonly known as the Shrub Cinquefoil, is a hybrid plant that combines traits from various Potentilla species.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and vibrant yellow flowers. The plant typically reaches a height of 1 to 3 feet and features small, lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, sandy or loamy soils.

The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
