Psoralea monosperma

Psoralea monosperma, commonly known as the single-seeded psoralea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces a single seed per pod, which is a distinguishing feature of its species name. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Due to its unique botanical traits, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
