Pittosporum glabratum var. glabratum

Pittosporum glabratum var. glabratum, commonly known as the smooth pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically smooth and lance-shaped. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Due to its hardiness, it is adaptable to a range of environmental conditions.
