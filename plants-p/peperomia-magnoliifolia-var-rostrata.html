Peperomia magnoliifolia var. rostrata

Peperomia magnoliifolia var. rostrata, commonly known as the rostrata peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves with a slightly wavy margin and a distinctive reddish-brown midrib. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are clustered in spikes, typically appearing in late spring or early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements, it is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
