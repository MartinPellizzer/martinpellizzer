Peperomia reflexa var. valantoides

Peperomia reflexa var. valantoides, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery sheen, which are often marked with white or silver veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
