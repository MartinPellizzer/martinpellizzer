Peperomia drusophila

Peperomia drusophila, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, providing a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish-white in hue. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia drusophila is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal.
