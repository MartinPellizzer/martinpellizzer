Podochilus tenuispicus

Podochilus tenuispicus, commonly known as the slender-spined orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit. The plant features linear, leathery leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Podochilus tenuispicus is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique floral structure.
