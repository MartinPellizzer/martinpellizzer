Pilosella Fennica Subsp. Graciliglandula

Pilosella fennica subsp. graciliglandula is a rare and delicate plant native to northern Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its small, white to pale yellow flowers. This subspecies is distinguished by its finely hairy leaves and glandular structures. It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in open habitats. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
