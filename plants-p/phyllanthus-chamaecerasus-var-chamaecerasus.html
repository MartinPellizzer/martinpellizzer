Phyllanthus chamaecerasus var. chamaecerasus

Phyllanthus chamaecerasus var. chamaecerasus, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It features small, green, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces tiny, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, black seeds.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
