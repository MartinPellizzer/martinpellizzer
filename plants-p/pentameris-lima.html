Pentameris lima

Pentameris lima, commonly known as the lima bean vine, is a climbing plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its legume fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into pods containing edible seeds. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three leaflets that are ovate to lanceolate in shape.

Pentameris lima is valued for its nutritional seeds and is cultivated for both food and ornamental purposes.
