Peperomia magnoliifolia var. subrotunda

Peperomia magnoliifolia var. subrotunda, commonly known as the broad-leaf peperomia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, ovate leaves that are often a deep green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among indoor gardeners due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
