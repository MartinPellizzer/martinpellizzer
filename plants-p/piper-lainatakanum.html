Piper lainatakanum

Piper lainatakanum, commonly known as the Kankanum pepper, is a tropical climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are used in traditional medicine and local cuisines. Its leaves are often used as a spice or herb in various regional dishes.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
