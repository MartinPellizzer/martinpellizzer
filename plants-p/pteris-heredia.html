Pteris heredia

Pteris heredia, commonly known as the Heredia's water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Pteridaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, bipinnate fronds with a distinctive, finely divided structure. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense, spongy layer that aids in water retention.

Pteris heredia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
