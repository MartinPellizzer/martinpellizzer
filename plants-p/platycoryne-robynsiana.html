Platycoryne robynsiana

Platycoryne robynsiana, commonly known as the Robyn's orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
