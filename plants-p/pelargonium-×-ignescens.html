Pelargonium × ignescens

Pelargonium × ignescens, commonly known as the Flame Zonal Geranium, is a hybrid flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from red to orange and yellow, often with contrasting colored leaves. The plant produces clusters of flowers on long, slender stems, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring regular watering but avoiding waterlogging.

Its ornamental value makes it a favored plant in both ornamental horticulture and landscape design.
