Pelargonium setulosum

Pelargonium setulosum, commonly known as the serrated geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive serrated leaves. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Pelargonium setulosum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
