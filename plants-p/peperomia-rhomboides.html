Peperomia rhomboides

Peperomia rhomboides, commonly known as the rhomboid peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features oval, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly textured surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia rhomboides is valued for its attractive foliage and air-purifying qualities.
