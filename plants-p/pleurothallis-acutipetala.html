Pleurothallis acutipetala

Pleurothallis acutipetala, commonly known as the sharp-petaled pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments within cloud forests. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with prominent, acuminate petals. Its growth habit is typically compact, with short, branched stems and clustered leaves.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and specialized orchid species.
