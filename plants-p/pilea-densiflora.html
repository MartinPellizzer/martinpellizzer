Pilea densiflora

Pilea densiflora, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a flowering plant native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and ability to purify the air.

Pilea densiflora is also known for its ability to propagate through leaf cuttings, making it a favorite among plant enthusiasts.
