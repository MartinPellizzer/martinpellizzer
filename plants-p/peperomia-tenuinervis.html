Peperomia tenuinervis

Peperomia tenuinervis, commonly known as the narrow-veined peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and prominent, narrow veins that give it its name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, followed by bright red berries. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and drought tolerance.

Peperomia tenuinervis is valued for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments.
