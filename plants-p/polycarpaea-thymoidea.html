Polycarpaea thymoidea

Polycarpaea thymoidea, commonly known as the African thyme, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features aromatic leaves and small, tubular flowers that are often pink or purple in color. It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs.

Polycarpaea thymoidea is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for various health purposes.
