Phaius Crinita

Crimson orchid, scientifically known as Phaius crinita, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia. With its vibrant red petals and intricate floral structure, it captivates botanists and gardeners alike. This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful cultivation to flourish. Its unique appearance and rarity make it a prized specimen in specialty collections. Despite its beauty, it remains a challenging plant to grow outside its natural habitat.
