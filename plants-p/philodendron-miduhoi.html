Philodendron miduhoi

Philodendron miduhoi, commonly known as the Miduhoi Philodendron, is a climbing tropical plant native to Central America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and a prominent midrib. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Philodendron miduhoi is valued for its ornamental foliage and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
