Pasania finetii

Pasania finetii, commonly known as the African moonflower, is a striking plant native to southern Africa.

It is a climbing shrub that produces large, fragrant, white flowers that open at night. The flowers have a unique, moon-like appearance, which gives the plant its common name. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for its nocturnal blooming habit.
