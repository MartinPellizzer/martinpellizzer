Peperomia aphylla

Peperomia aphylla, commonly known as the leafless peperomia, is a unique species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its lack of leaves, with only small, scale-like structures covering its stems. The plant produces compact, spadix-like inflorescences that bear tiny, inconspicuous flowers. Peperomia aphylla thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unusual appearance makes it a popular choice for collectors and indoor gardeners seeking distinctive plants.
