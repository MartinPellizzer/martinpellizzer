Polygala polycephala

Polygala polycephala, commonly known as the "dwarf milkweed," is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
