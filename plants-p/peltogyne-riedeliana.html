Peltogyne riedeliana

Peltogyne riedeliana, commonly known as the Riedel's ironwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its thick, dark bark and dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its durability. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptic to ovate in shape. It produces small, pale yellow flowers followed by woody pods containing flat, elliptical seeds.

Peltogyne riedeliana is often found in lowland tropical forests and is adapted to thrive in humid, warm climates.
