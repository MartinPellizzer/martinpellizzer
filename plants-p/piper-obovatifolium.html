Piper obovatifolium

Piper obovatifolium, commonly known as the broad-leafed pepper plant, is a climbing vine native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruit, a small, three-lobed berry, contains aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine.

The botanical features of Piper obovatifolium make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and pharmacological study.
