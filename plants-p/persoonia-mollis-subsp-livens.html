Persoonia mollis subsp. livens

Persoonia mollis subsp.

livens, commonly known as the silky everlasting or silky rattlebox, is a flowering plant native to Australia. It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage and produces showy, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant is a shrub that typically grows in heathlands and open forests, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowers are clustered in cylindrical inflorescences and are pollinated by insects.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
