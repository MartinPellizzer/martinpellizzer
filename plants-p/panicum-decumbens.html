Panicum decumbens

Panicum decumbens, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It features slender, creeping stems that often lie flat on the ground, forming dense mats. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous delicate, pale green to reddish bracts. Its foliage is typically narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Panicum decumbens is often found in wetlands, marshes, and other moist habitats, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.
