Pilea aenea

Pilea aenea, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial shrub native to China.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a metallic sheen, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by pale green berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea aenea is relatively low maintenance and can be propagated easily through stem cuttings.
