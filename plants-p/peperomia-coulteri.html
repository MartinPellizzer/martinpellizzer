Peperomia coulteri

Peperomia coulteri, commonly known as Coulter's peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia coulteri is valued for its ornamental foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
