Peperomia recurvata var. philippinensis

Peperomia recurvata var. philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a subtle ornamental touch. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is valued for its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor growing conditions.
