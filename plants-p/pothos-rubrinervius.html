Pothos rubrinervius

Pothos rubrinervius, commonly known as the red-veined pothos, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves with striking red veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers, though it is primarily grown for its ornamental foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Pothos rubrinervius is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and aesthetic appeal.
