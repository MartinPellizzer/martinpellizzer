Plantago orbignyana subsp. orbignyana

Plantago orbignyana subsp.

orbignyana, commonly known as the Andean plantain, is a perennial herb native to the Andes region of South America. It is characterized by its broad, oval leaves that are covered in fine, dense hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, which are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous areas with well-drained soil.

This subspecies is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
