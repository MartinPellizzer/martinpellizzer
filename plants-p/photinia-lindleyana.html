Photinia lindleyana

Photinia lindleyana, commonly known as the glossy leaf photinia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to eastern Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea, and is characterized by its attractive, glossy, dark green leaves. Photinia lindleyana prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for a variety of garden settings.
