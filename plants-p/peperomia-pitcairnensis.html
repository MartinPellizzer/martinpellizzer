Peperomia pitcairnensis

Peperomia pitcairnensis, commonly known as the Pitcairn peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the remote Pitcairn Islands in the South Pacific.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture, and it produces delicate, pinkish-white flowers during the warmer months. The plant is well adapted to the humid, coastal climate of its native habitat, thriving in shaded to semi-shaded conditions.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
