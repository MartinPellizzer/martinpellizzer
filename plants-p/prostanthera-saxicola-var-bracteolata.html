Prostanthera saxicola var. bracteolata

Prostanthera saxicola var. bracteolata, commonly known as the rock prostanthera, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pale blue flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, and it typically grows in rocky or sandy soils.
