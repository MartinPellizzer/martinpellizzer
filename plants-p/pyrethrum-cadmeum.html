Pyrethrum cadmeum

Pyrethrum cadmeum, commonly known as the golden yarrow, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its finely divided, silver-green leaves and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and chrysanthemums. Its flowers are rich in pyrethrins, natural insecticides used in organic pest control.

Pyrethrum cadmeum thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
