Polanisia foliosa

Polanisia foliosa, commonly known as the leafy polanisia, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Polygonaceae, which includes plants commonly known as buckwheats. The plant is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks.

Polanisia foliosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
