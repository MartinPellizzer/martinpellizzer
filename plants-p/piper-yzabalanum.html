Piper yzabalanum

Piper yzabalanum, commonly known as the Yzabalanum pepper, is a flowering plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns red when ripe.

The plant is valued for its aromatic leaves and has been used in traditional medicine by indigenous communities.
