Phalaenopsis chibae

Phalaenopsis chibae, commonly known as the Chiba phalaenopsis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of long, pendulous flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically has broad, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Phalaenopsis chibae is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
