Pilea marginata

Pilea marginata, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves with a distinctive silver margin, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea marginata is known for its ease of care and ability to propagate through leaf cuttings.
