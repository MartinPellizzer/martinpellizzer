Pelargonium × pyrethrifolium

Pelargonium × pyrethrifolium, commonly known as the pyrethrum geranium, is a hybrid plant that combines characteristics of various geranium species.

It is native to South Africa and is known for its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows as a shrub with upright stems and lobed, serrated leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Its flowers are not only ornamental but also contain natural pyrethrin compounds, which have insect-repelling properties.
