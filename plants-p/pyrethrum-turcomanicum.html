Pyrethrum turcomanicum

Pyrethrum turcomanicum, commonly known as the Turkestan pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white to pink petals. The plant produces small, spherical seed heads that contain numerous tiny seeds. Its botanical significance lies in its ability to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments.

Pyrethrum turcomanicum is also noted for its potential in natural pest control due to the presence of pyrethrins in its flowers.
