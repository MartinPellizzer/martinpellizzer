Piper burenii

Piper burenii, commonly known as the Burenii pepper or a type of wild pepper, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant typically grows as a shrub with opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy. Its small, greenish-white flowers are followed by small, round berries that contain the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and cuisine.

The plant's botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological and medicinal purposes.
