Prunus lamponga

Prunus lamponga, commonly known as the Chinese plum or Chinese date, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and showy pink to white flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically sweet and juicy, often used in culinary and medicinal applications. Prunus lamponga thrives in temperate climates and is valued for its ornamental and edible qualities.

Its botanical features make it a significant species in both traditional medicine and horticulture.
