Podospermum buxbaumii

Podospermum buxbaumii, commonly known as the Buxbaum's plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
