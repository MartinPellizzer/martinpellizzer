Poa lettermanii

Poa lettermanii, commonly known as Letterman's bluegrass, is a perennial grass native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green blades and tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has narrow, cylindrical leaves and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences.

Poa lettermanii is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and low maintenance requirements.
