Pilea minutiflora

Pilea minutiflora, commonly known as the tiny flower pilea, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of Mexico.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces tiny, pale green flowers that bloom in spring, adding a subtle floral touch to its appearance. It thrives in indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and attractive foliage, it is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
