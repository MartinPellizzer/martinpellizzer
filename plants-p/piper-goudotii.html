Piper goudotii

Piper goudotii, commonly known as the Goudot's pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with dark green leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruits are tiny, berry-like structures that contain the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in local herbal remedies.
