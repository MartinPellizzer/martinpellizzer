Peperomia pongoana

Peperomia pongoana, commonly known as the Red-veined Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with striking red veins that create a dramatic contrast. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia pongoana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and low maintenance requirements.
