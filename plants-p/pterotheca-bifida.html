Pterotheca bifida

Pterotheca bifida, commonly known as the narrowleaf pterotheca, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in desert environments.
