Peperomia baronii

Peperomia baronii, commonly known as the Baron's Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which are followed by dark red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia baronii is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and moderate humidity to flourish.
