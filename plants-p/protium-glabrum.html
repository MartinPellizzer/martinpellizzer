Protium glabrum

Protium glabrum, commonly known as the smooth-flowered sycamore, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Burseraceae family and is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and large, palmate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its fruit is a woody drupe that contains a single seed.
