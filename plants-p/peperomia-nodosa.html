Peperomia nodosa

Peperomia nodosa, commonly known as the Rosy Nodding Peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purplish tinge, especially on the undersides. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale green, arranged in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and ease of care contribute to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
