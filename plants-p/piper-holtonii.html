Piper holtonii

Piper holtonii, commonly known as the Hawaiian pepper plant, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its fruits are small, berry-like structures that contain several seeds.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Piper holtonii is considered a rare and endangered species.
