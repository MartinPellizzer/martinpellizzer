Pleioblastus simonii f. variegatus

Pleioblastus simonii f. variegatus, commonly known as the Variegated Japanese Forest Grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to East Asia.

It features slender, arching blades with a distinctive variegated pattern of light green and white, creating a visually appealing contrast. The plant thrives in partial shade to full sun and is well-suited for USDA hardiness zones 6 to 8. Its foliage remains vibrant throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for garden borders and rock gardens.

It produces small, inconspicuous flowers in late spring, followed by fluffy seed heads that add interest in the fall.
