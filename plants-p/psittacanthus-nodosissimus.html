Psittacanthus nodosissimus

Psittacanthus nodosissimus, commonly known as the nodding fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of large, feather-like fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant has a robust root system that helps it anchor in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically evergreen and can grow quite long, contributing to its ornamental value.

Psittacanthus nodosissimus plays an important ecological role in its native habitats by providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
