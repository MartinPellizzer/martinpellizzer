Polystichum diffundens

Polystichum diffundens, commonly known as the spreading wood fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to temperate regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, arching fronds that can reach lengths of up to 1.5 meters. The plant features bipinnate leaves with a delicate, feathery appearance, giving it a lush, ornamental look. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Polystichum diffundens is valued in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and ability to add texture to shaded garden spaces.
