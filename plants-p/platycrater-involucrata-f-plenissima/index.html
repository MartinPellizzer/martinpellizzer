Platycrater Involucrata F. Plenissima

Platycrater involucrata f. plenissima is a rare and striking variant of the platycrater plant known for its abundant, showy flowers. This plant belongs to the Rutaceae family and is native to specific regions in Asia. Its most distinctive feature is its densely packed, golden-yellow blooms that create a striking visual display. The f. plenissima form is particularly prized by horticulturists for its enhanced floral abundance. Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it has become a sought-after specimen in botanical gardens and private collections.
