Pulsatilla albana var. bungeana

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its showy, early spring blooms of pale yellow to white petals. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky soils, thriving in cold, dry climates. It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with specific horticultural requirements.
