Phanerophlebiopsis bipinnata

Phanerophlebiopsis bipinnata, commonly known as the two-pinnate fern, is a species of fern characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping rhizomes that produce aerial roots. Its fronds are typically divided into two sets of pinnae, giving it a finely divided, lacy appearance.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
