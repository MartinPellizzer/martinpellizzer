Pleioblastus simonii f. heterophyllus

Pleioblastus simonii f. heterophyllus, commonly known as the variegated bamboo, is a unique cultivar of the bamboo species Pleioblastus simonii.

It is characterized by its striking variegated foliage, featuring white and green stripes or spots on the leaves. This plant is a clumping bamboo, forming dense, compact growth that is ideal for small garden spaces. The leaves are typically narrower and more elongated compared to the parent species, adding a distinctive visual appeal.

It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
