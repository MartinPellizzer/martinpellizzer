Pleione hookeriana

Pleione hookeriana, commonly known as the Hooker's pleione, is a species of orchid native to the Himalayas and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, leafy stems and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows at high altitudes, preferring cool and moist environments. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Pleione hookeriana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
