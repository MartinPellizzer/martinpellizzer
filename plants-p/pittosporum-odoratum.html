Pittosporum odoratum

Pittosporum odoratum, commonly known as the sweet Pittosporum, is a flowering shrub native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath, with a fragrant, white to pale yellow flower cluster. The plant produces small, edible berries that turn from green to red as they mature. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, often found in coastal and forested areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
