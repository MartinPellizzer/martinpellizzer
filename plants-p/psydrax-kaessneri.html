Psydrax kaessneri

Psydrax kaessneri, commonly known as the African soursop or Kaffir lime, is a tropical shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round fruits with a thick, waxy rind and a segmented interior. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
