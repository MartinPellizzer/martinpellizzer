Piper sabaletasanum

Piper sabaletasanum, commonly known as the Sabaletasanum pepper, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing vines and small, heart-shaped leaves. It produces tiny, greenish-white flowers that develop into small, round berries.

The berries are typically harvested when they turn dark purple, and the plant is valued for its aromatic properties and potential medicinal uses.
