Pleurothallis stevensonii

Pleurothallis stevensonii, commonly known as Stevenson's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features slender, erect stems and produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with intricate patterns. Its leaves are usually narrow and arranged in a rosette-like formation.

Pleurothallis stevensonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
