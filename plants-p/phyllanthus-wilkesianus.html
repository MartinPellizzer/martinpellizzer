Phyllanthus wilkesianus

Phyllanthus wilkesianus, commonly known as the African date palm, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by fleshy, orange-colored fruits. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a smooth, glossy surface.

The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy species found in regions with limited rainfall.
