Primulina wentsaii

Primulina wentsaii, commonly known as the Wentsaii globose, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color, with a distinctive spurred shape. Its leaves are glossy and oval-shaped, adding to its aesthetic appeal in both indoor and outdoor settings.
