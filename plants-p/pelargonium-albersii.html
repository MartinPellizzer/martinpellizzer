Pelargonium albersii

Pelargonium albersii, commonly known as the Albers' geranium, is a herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features upright stems and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink to red. The plant has serrated, lobed leaves that are often hairy to the touch. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and compact growth habit.
