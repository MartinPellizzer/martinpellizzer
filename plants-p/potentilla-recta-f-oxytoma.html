Potentilla recta f. oxytoma

Potentilla recta f. oxytoma, commonly known as the oxytoma form of the upright cinquefoil, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The leaves are typically trifoliate, with three leaflets that are serrated along the edges. This plant is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open fields.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
