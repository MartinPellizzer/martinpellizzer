Polycarena lilacina var. lilacina

Polycarena lilacina var. lilacina, commonly known as the lilac-flowered polycarena, is a species of flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands. The plant is characterized by its delicate, lilac-colored flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The botanical interest of this plant lies in its unique floral structure and its role in local ecosystems.
