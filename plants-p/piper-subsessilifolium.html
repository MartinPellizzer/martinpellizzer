Piper subsessilifolium

Piper subsessilifolium, commonly known as the sessile-leaf pepper, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its small, ovate leaves that are sessile, meaning they lack stalks. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into dark blue berries.

This species is often grown for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
