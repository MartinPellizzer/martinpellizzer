Pennisetum reversum var. gymnochaetum

Pennisetum reversum var. gymnochaetum, commonly known as silver grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, silvery-gray foliage and tufted inflorescences that resemble plumes. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping and ornamental gardens. Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for landscaping in arid regions.

It is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
