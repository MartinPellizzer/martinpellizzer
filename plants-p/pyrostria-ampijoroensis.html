Pyrostria ampijoroensis

Pyrostria ampijoroensis, commonly known as the Ampijoroa palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in forested areas.
