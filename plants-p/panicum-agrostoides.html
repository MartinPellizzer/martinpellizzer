Panicum agrostoides

Panicum agrostoides, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in open areas, such as roadsides and disturbed soils, and thrives in full sun. The plant has slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. Its inflorescence consists of a panicle of small, spikelet-like flowers that are wind-pollinated.

Panicum agrostoides is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its distinctive coloration and resilience.
