Piper ottonis

Piper ottonis, commonly known as the Mexican pepper plant, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. It produces small, peppery fruits that are often used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it a unique and valuable species in both ecological and medicinal contexts.
