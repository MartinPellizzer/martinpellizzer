Platytaenia tragioides

Platytaenia tragioides, commonly known as the slender burrweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its small, inconspicuous flowers and bract-like leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments. Its seed capsules contain tiny, wind-dispersed seeds, which contribute to its ability to spread in open habitats.
