Penaea acuta

Penaea acuta, commonly known as the sharp-tipped fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically bright green and slightly serrated at the edges. The plant thrives in humid environments and requires well-drained, fertile soil to flourish. Penaea acuta is often found growing in shaded areas under trees or in dense undergrowth.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant collections.
