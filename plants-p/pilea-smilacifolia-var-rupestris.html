Pilea smilacifolia var. rupestris

Pilea smilacifolia var. rupestris, commonly known as the rock pilea, is a compact, evergreen perennial shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves that are slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers in clusters, which are followed by spherical, red berries. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
