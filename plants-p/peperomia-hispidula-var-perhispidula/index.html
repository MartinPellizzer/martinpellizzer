Peperomia Hispidula Var. Perhispidula

Velvet peperomia, scientifically known as Peperomia hispidula var. perhispidula, is a strikingly unique houseplant prized for its velvety, dark green leaves. Native to the tropical regions of South America, it thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
