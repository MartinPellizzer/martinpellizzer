Peperomia allorgeana f. lata

Peperomia allorgeana f. lata, commonly known as the broad-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically broad and ovate, often with a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its foliage is prized for its vibrant coloration and ability to add a lush, tropical feel to any space.
