Plectranthus orbicularis

Plectranthus orbicularis, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Plectranthus orbicularis is also valued for its ability to produce fragrant foliage and its resilience in various growing conditions.
