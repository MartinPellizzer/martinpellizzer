Polygala Nicaeensis Subsp. Gerundensis

Polygala nicaeensis subsp. gerundensis is a rare and lesser-known member of the Polygalaceae family. Native to specific regions in southern Europe, it thrives in rocky and arid environments. This subspecies is distinguished by its unique floral structure and delicate foliage. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated and poorly studied by botanists. Understanding its ecology and conservation status is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its native habitat.
