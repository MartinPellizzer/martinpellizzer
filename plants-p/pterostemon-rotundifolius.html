Pterostemon rotundifolius

Pterostemon rotundifolius, commonly known as the round-leaf pterostemon, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features opposite, rounded leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
