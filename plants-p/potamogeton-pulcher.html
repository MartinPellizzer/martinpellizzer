Potamogeton pulcher

Potamogeton pulcher, commonly known as the beautiful pondweed, is a submerged aquatic plant found in freshwater environments.

It has long, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around the stem. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often submerged, with the pollen released into the water. It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various organisms.

Its presence is often an indicator of good water quality.
