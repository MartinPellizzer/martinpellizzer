Phillyrea latifolia f. ilicifolia

Phillyrea latifolia f. ilicifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf Phillyrea, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, dark green, leathery leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers in the spring, followed by small, dark blue berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
