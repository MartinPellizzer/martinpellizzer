Pterostylis spatalium

Pterostylis spatalium, commonly known as the Spatalium starry greenhood, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist forest floors, often in sandy or clay soils. The plant features a single, erect stem with a single leaf and a distinctive green hood-shaped flower. Its flowers are usually pale green with a white or yellowish throat, and they bloom in late spring to early summer.

The species is part of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique morphology and ecological significance in its habitat.
