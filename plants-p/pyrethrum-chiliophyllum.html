Pyrethrum chiliophyllum

Pyrethrum chiliophyllum, commonly known as the sweet pepper pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that contain numerous seeds. Its botanical features include hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has potential uses in natural pest control due to its pyrethrin content.
