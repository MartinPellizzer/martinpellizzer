Packera indecora

Packera indecora, commonly known as the showy germander.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America, typically found in moist meadows and open woodlands. The plant features a rosette of basal leaves that are deeply lobed and often covered in fine hairs. It produces yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months, which are attractive to pollinators.

Its botanical name reflects its ornamental value, though it is not widely cultivated.
