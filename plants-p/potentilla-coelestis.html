Potentilla coelestis

Potentilla coelestis, commonly known as skyblue potentilla, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Potentilla coelestis thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.
