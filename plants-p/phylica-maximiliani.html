Phylica maximiliani

Phylica maximiliani, commonly known as the silverleaf phylica, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its dense, silvery-white foliage. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
