Portulaca giuliettiae

Portulaca giuliettiae, commonly known as Juliet's moss or the Italian moss, is a low-growing, succulent plant native to southern Europe.

It features fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purplish tinge. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding a vibrant touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
