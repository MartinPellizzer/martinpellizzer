Pavetta abyssinica var. glabra

Pavetta abyssinica var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth pavetta, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It is a shrub characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and potential ecological importance in its native habitat.
