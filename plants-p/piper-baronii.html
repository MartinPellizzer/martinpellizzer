Piper baronii

Piper baronii, commonly known as the Baron's pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit and heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by small, round berries.

Piper baronii is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
