Pedilonum keytsianum

Pedilonum keytsianum, commonly known as the Kiritimati palm, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. Its fruit is a fibrous, spiky capsule containing seeds.

Pedilonum keytsianum plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
