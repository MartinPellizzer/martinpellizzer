Pteropsis nummularia

Pteropsis nummularia, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the Asteraceae family. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.

Pteropsis nummularia prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it adaptable to various garden environments.
