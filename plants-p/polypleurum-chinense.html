Polypleurum chinense

Polypleurum chinense, commonly known as the Chinese pleurisy vine, is a climbing plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Araliaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces berries that are typically red or yellow when mature. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forests and along stream banks.
