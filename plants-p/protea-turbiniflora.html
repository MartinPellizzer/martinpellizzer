Protea turbiniflora

Protea turbiniflora, commonly known as the Turban protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy, turban-shaped inflorescences that can reach up to 30 cm in height. The flowers are typically pink or red, with a distinctive central style that protrudes from the flower head. The plant thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

It plays an important role in attracting pollinators such as birds and insects.
