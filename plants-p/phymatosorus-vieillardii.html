Phymatosorus vieillardii

Phymatosorus vieillardii, commonly known as the Vietnamese tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a robust rhizome system that supports its growth in moist, shaded environments. Its spores are produced on the undersides of the fronds, and it reproduces through both sexual and asexual means.

This fern is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in humid and nutrient-rich soils.
