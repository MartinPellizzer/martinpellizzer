Peperomia cotoneasterifolia

Peperomia cotoneasterifolia, commonly known as the snowflake peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, ovate to elliptical leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that resemble snowflakes, giving it its common name. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia cotoneasterifolia is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
