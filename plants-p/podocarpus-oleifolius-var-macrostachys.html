Podocarpus oleifolius var. macrostachys

Podocarpus oleifolius var. macrostachys, commonly known as the large-stamined podocarp, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and cylindrical, often pendulous cones. The leaves are leathery and glossy, with a distinctive aromatic scent. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its wood and essential oils.
