Pyrethrum demetrii

Pyrethrum demetrii, commonly known as the African pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pale yellow to white flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. Pyrethrum demetrii is valued for its natural insecticidal properties due to the presence of pyrethrins in its flowers.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant and for its pest control benefits in organic gardening.
