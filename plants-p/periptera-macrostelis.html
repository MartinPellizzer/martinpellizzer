Periptera macrostelis

Periptera macrostelis, commonly known as the large-stemmed periptera, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its long, slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.

It thrives in humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
