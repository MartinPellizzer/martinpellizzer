Pittosporum viridiflorum subsp. dalzielii

Pittosporum viridiflorum subsp.

dalzielii, commonly known as the Dalziel's green-flowered pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand. It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, blooming in spring. These flowers are followed by woody, cone-like fruits that contain several seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden conditions.
