Psilogramme cinnamomea

Psilogramme cinnamomea, commonly known as the cinnamon fern, is a species of fern native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender fronds that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant has a distinctive central fiddlehead that unfurls into a compound structure with multiple leaflets. Its rhizomes are covered in a cinnamon-colored sheath, giving it its common name.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and wetlands.
