Poa szechuensis

Poa szechuensis, commonly known as the Sichuan bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a bluish-green hue that gives it its common name.

Poa szechuensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in horticultural settings.
