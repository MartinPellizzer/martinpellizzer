Pulmonaria rubra subsp. rubra

Pulmonaria rubra subsp.

rubra, commonly known as red lungwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It features striking pink to red flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May.

The plant has basal leaves that are broadly ovate with a distinctive white or silver vein pattern. It thrives in moist, shady woodland habitats and prefers well-drained, fertile soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking early-flowering plants.
