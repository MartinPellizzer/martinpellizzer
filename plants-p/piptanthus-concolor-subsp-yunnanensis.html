Piptanthus concolor subsp. yunnanensis

Piptanthus concolor subsp.

yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan peashrub, is a shrub native to western China. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers.

The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky or sandy soils. It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for local wildlife.

Its ornamental value has also led to its cultivation in some regions.
