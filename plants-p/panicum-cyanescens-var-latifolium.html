Panicum cyanescens var. latifolium

Panicum cyanescens var. latifolium, commonly known as broadleaf blue panicum, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its wide, flat leaves that are often bluish-green in color, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces fluffy, pale blue to silver-white inflorescences, which are typical of the Panicum genus. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental use in gardens.

This variety is also valued for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
