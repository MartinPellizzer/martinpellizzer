Petunia excellens

Petunia excellens, commonly known as the grandiflora petunia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a wide range of colors. The plant is an annual herb that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers. Its foliage is dark green and slightly hairy, while the stems are sturdy and branching.

Petunia excellens is valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to bloom continuously throughout the growing season.
