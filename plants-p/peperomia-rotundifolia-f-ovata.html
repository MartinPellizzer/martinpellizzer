Peperomia rotundifolia f. ovata

Peperomia rotundifolia f. ovata, commonly known as the rounded-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions.

It features glossy, ovate-shaped leaves that are often slightly wavy along the edges. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and red, which are typically followed by reddish berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
