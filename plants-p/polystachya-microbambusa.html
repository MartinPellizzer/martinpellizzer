Polystachya microbambusa

Polystachya microbambusa, commonly known as the miniature bamboo orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines. This species is characterized by its slender, bamboo-like stems and dense clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories.

Its ornamental value lies in its elegant appearance and ease of cultivation in tropical and subtropical climates.
