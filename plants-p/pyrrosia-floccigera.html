Pyrrosia floccigera

Pyrrosia floccigera, commonly known as the cloud fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, soft fronds that resemble clouds, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are pinnate, with a pale, fuzzy underside that contributes to its unique appearance.

Pyrrosia floccigera is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and can thrive in both indoor and outdoor settings with proper care.
