Palaquium Globosum

Palaquium globosum is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its attractive, glossy leaves. The plant thrives in humid climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Its fruit, which resembles a small ball, is edible and has a sweet, tangy flavor. This versatile plant is popular among gardeners and botanists alike for its beauty and resilience.
