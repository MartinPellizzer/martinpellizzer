Phaseolus schottii f. grandiflorus

Phaseolus schottii f. grandiflorus, commonly known as the large-flowered scarlet bean, is a botanical variant of the scarlet bean species.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers and elongated, bean-like pods. This plant belongs to the Fabaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America. Its foliage consists of trifoliate leaves, typical of many legumes.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its edible seeds.
