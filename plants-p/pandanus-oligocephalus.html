Pandanus oligocephalus

Pandanus oligocephalus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pandanus, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that bear both male and female flowers. Its fibrous leaves are valued for their durability and versatility in weaving.

Pandanus oligocephalus plays an important ecological role in coastal and wetland habitats.
