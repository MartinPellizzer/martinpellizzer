Pellionia velutina

Pellionia velutina, commonly known as velvet pellionia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Pellionia velutina thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
