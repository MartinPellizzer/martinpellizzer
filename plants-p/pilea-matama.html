Pilea matama

Pilea matama, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are typically light green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are followed by round, green berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular houseplant.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
