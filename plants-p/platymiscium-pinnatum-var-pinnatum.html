Platymiscium pinnatum var. pinnatum

Platymiscium pinnatum var. pinnatum, commonly known as the broadleaf ironwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and compound leaves that are pinnately arranged. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by small, woody fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood and is used in traditional medicine and construction.
