Polystichum perelegans

Polystichum perelegans, commonly known as the golden shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It is characterized by its large, leathery, dark green fronds with a distinctive golden-yellow margin. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are thick and fleshy, allowing it to store water and nutrients.

Polystichum perelegans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking foliage.
