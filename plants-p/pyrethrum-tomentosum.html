Pyrethrum tomentosum

Pyrethrum tomentosum, commonly known as the woolly pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy perennial in suitable climates.

The plant's botanical features make it both visually appealing and ecologically beneficial.
