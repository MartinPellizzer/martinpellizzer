Peperomia uphofii

Peperomia uphofii, commonly known as the Upland Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside, providing a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia uphofii is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
