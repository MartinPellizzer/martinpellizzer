Pinelia alticola

Pinelia alticola, commonly known as the alpine pinelia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It typically grows in high-altitude environments, where it thrives in cool, moist, and well-drained soils. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale blue in color. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in a fine layer of hair.

Pinelia alticola is valued in traditional medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
