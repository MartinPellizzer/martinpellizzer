Premna cumingiana

Premna cumingiana, commonly known as Cuming's premna, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Vietnam, and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often pubescent on the underside. It produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale purple, which bloom during the rainy season.

The plant is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
