Peperomia ciliifera

Peperomia ciliifera, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green color, often adorned with reddish veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia ciliifera is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and moderate humidity to flourish.
