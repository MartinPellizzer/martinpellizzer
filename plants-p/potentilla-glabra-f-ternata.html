Potentilla glabra f. ternata

Potentilla glabra f. ternata, commonly known as the smooth cinquefoil, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has slender, wiry stems and heart-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and vibrant floral display.
