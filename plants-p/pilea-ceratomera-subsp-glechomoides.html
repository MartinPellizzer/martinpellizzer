Pilea ceratomera subsp. glechomoides

Pilea ceratomera subsp.

glechomoides, commonly known as the glechomoides pilea, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia. It features heart-shaped, slightly lobed leaves that are typically light to dark green with a waxy texture.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.

Its ornamental value lies primarily in its foliage rather than its flowers.
