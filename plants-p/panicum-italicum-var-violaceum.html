Panicum italicum var. violaceum

Panicum italicum var. violaceum, commonly known as purple lovegrass, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and produces fluffy, purple-tinted seed heads that resemble small clouds. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas and is often found in disturbed soils. It is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Its vibrant purple color makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and erosion control.
