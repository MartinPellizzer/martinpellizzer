Peperomia mannii var. fernandopoana

Peperomia mannii var. fernandopoana, commonly known as the Fernandopoana peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features rounded, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental purposes in both gardens and indoor spaces.
