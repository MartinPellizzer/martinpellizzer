Phaseolus prostratus f. flavoviridis

Phaseolus prostratus f. flavoviridis, commonly known as the yellow-leafed prostrate bean, is a varietal form of the prostrate bean species.

It is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Americas, characterized by its sprawling growth habit. The leaves are typically ovate with a distinct yellowish-green coloration, and the plant produces small, white or pale green flowers. It is often cultivated for its edible beans, which are used in various traditional cuisines.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to different growing conditions.
