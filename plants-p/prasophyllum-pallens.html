Prasophyllum pallens

Prasophyllum pallens, commonly known as the pale leek orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in open habitats such as heathlands and woodland edges. The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces a single flowering stem with a spike of pale green to white flowers. It is a small to medium-sized orchid, usually reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.

Prasophyllum pallens is part of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is known for its delicate appearance and specific ecological requirements.
