Polypodium africanum

Polypodium africanum, commonly known as the African wall fern, is a species of fern native to Africa.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features lance-shaped, serrated fronds that are typically evergreen. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often growing on rocks or tree trunks.

Its reproductive structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the fronds and contain spores for propagation.
