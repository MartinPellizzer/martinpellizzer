Plumatichilos sigmoideus

Plumatichilos sigmoideus, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a unique orchid species native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzag-shaped inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pink, often with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in cool, high-altitude environments and requires specific humidity and light conditions to flourish.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
