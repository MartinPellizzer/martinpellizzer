Pentzia quinquefida

Pentzia quinquefida, commonly known as the five-lobed milkweed, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves that split into five lobes, giving it its scientific name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and rocky habitats.

Pentzia quinquefida is valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife and its ornamental appeal in gardens.
