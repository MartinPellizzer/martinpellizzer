Peperomia lanceolatopeltata

Peperomia lanceolatopeltata, commonly known as the lanceolate peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and red, which are often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
