Pittosporum ferrugineum

Pittosporum ferrugineum, commonly known as the rusty pittosporum, is a deciduous shrub native to New Zealand.

It features bright yellow flowers that appear in late spring, followed by dark blue, edible berries. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that turn reddish in autumn. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

Pittosporum ferrugineum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting native wildlife.
