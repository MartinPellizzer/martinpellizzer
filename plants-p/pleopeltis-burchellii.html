Pleopeltis burchellii

Pleopeltis burchellii, commonly known as the Burchell's rock fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, clustered growth habit. The plant features intricate, lanceolate leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, silky hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically growing on tree trunks or rocky surfaces.
