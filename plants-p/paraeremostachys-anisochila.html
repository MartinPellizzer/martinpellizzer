Paraeremostachys anisochila

Paraeremostachys anisochila, commonly known as the variable spikegrass, is a perennial grass species native to certain regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
