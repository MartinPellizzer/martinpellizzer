Pittosporum kauaiense

Pittosporum kauaiense, commonly known as Kauai Pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian island of Kauai.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows in moist, shaded habitats. The plant produces fragrant white or pale yellow flowers, which are often clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are elliptic to ovate, with a glossy, dark green upper surface and a lighter, sometimes hairy underside.

Pittosporum kauaiense is considered a rare and endangered species, threatened by habitat loss and invasive species.
