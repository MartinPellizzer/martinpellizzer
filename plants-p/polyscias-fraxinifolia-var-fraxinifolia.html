Polyscias fraxinifolia var. fraxinifolia

Polyscias fraxinifolia var. fraxinifolia, commonly known as the Florida hibiscus, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy, with a waxy texture. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red, often followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
