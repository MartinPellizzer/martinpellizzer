Plectranthus hadiensis

Plectranthus hadiensis, commonly known as the Cape rose or African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb with square stems and opposite leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Plectranthus hadiensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for.
