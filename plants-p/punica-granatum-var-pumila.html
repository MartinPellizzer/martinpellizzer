Punica granatum var. pumila

Punica granatum var. pumila, commonly known as dwarf pomegranate, is a compact variety of the pomegranate plant.

It is characterized by its small stature, typically reaching heights of 1 to 2 meters, making it ideal for container gardening or small spaces. The plant produces small, ornamental flowers that range in color from white to pink, followed by bright red berries. Its leaves are broad and leathery, typical of the Punica genus.

This variety is well-suited to Mediterranean climates and prefers well-drained soil and full sun.
