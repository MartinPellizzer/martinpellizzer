Philodendron dispar

Philodendron dispar, commonly known as the Variegated Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a striking variegation of light green and white patterns. The plant has a short, stocky stem and produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and humidity.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appeal.
