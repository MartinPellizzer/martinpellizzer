Primula wangii

Primula wangii, commonly known as the Wang's primrose, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and delicate species.
