Pandanus welwitschii

Pandanus welwitschii, commonly known as the giant pandanus, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves that can reach lengths of up to 3 meters. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or green in color. Its roots are fibrous and often used in traditional crafts.

The plant thrives in humid, coastal environments and is adapted to withstand salt spray and strong winds.
