Panicum vilvoides var. campestre

Panicum vilvoides var. campestre, commonly known as yellow panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and bright yellow to golden-green foliage. The plant produces fluffy, seed-bearing inflorescences that are often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it adaptable to a variety of environmental conditions.

Its drought tolerance and ability to spread through rhizomes make it a resilient choice for landscaping and erosion control.
