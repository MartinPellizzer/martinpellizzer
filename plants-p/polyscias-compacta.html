Polyscias compacta

Polyscias compacta, commonly known as the compact umbrella plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features a dense, rounded growth habit with small, dark green, leathery leaves that are often glossy in appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by black, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Polyscias compacta is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its compact form and attractive foliage.
