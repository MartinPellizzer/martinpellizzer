Peperomia cordifolia

Peperomia cordifolia, commonly known as the heart-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage, it is widely appreciated as a decorative plant.
