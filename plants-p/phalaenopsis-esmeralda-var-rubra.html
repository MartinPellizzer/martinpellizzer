Phalaenopsis esmeralda var. rubra

Phalaenopsis esmeralda var. rubra, commonly known as the Red Emerald Orchid, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant red and green coloration.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves and strikingly colored flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of long-lasting, showy blooms in shades of red, pink, and green. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
