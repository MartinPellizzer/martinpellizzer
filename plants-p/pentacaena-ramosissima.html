Pentacaena ramosissima

Pentacaena ramosissima, commonly known as the hairy pentachaena, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, branched growth habit and small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in grassland and savanna environments. It has small, ovate leaves covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
