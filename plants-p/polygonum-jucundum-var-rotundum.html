Polygonum jucundum var. rotundum

Polygonum jucundum var. rotundum, commonly known as roundleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its rounded, succulent leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, sunny habitats such as wet meadows and streambanks. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread vegetatively, forming dense mats.

It is often found in wetland environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
