Piper fagopyricarpum

Piper fagopyricarpum, commonly known as the fagopyrum pepper, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit and heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface. Its fruits are small, berry-like structures that contain the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and cuisine.

The botanical features of Piper fagopyricarpum make it distinct within its family, with unique chemical compounds contributing to its medicinal properties.
