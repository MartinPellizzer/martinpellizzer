Pultenaea spinosa

Pultenaea spinosa, commonly known as the spiny pultenaea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, often spiny stems and small, elliptical leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in dry, open habitats.

Pultenaea spinosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
