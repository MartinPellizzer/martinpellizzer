Potamogeton vaginatus var. canadensis

Potamogeton vaginatus var. canadensis, commonly known as Canadian pondweed, is a submerged aquatic plant native to North America.

It has long, ribbon-like leaves that are typically green to reddish in color, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often submerged. The plant thrives in freshwater environments such as lakes, ponds, and slow-moving streams. It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.

Its presence is often an indicator of good water quality and stable ecological conditions.
