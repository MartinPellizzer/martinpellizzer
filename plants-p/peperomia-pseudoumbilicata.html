Peperomia pseudoumbilicata

Peperomia pseudoumbilicata, commonly known as the umbilicate peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces delicate, spadix-like inflorescences that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive foliage, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant.
