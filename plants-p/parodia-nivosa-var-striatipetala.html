Parodia nivosa var. striatipetala

Parodia nivosa var. striatipetala, commonly known as the Snowy Parodia, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It features a short, thick stem with a dense cluster of cylindrical, ribbed leaves that are typically pale green to white in color. The leaves are covered in a fine, powdery coating that helps reduce water loss in arid environments. It produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a subtle touch of color to its otherwise muted appearance.

This variety is prized for its compact growth and unique, frost-like appearance.
