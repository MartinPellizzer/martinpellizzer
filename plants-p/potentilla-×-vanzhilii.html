Potentilla × vanzhilii

Potentilla × vanzhilii, commonly known as the Siberian firethorn, is a hybrid shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, glossy leaves and produces clusters of bright orange or red berries that are highly ornamental. The plant is evergreen, with a compact growth habit that makes it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, exhibiting good drought tolerance.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant autumn foliage and winter interest.
