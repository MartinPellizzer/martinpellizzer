Peschiera Salzmannii

Peschiera salzmannii is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky crevices or shaded forest floors. Known for its small, star-shaped blossoms, this species is a favorite among botanists and plant enthusiasts. Its unique appearance and limited habitat make it a subject of both scientific study and conservation concern. Despite its beauty, Peschiera salzmannii remains a challenging plant to cultivate outside its natural range.
