Piper emollitum

Piper emollitum, commonly known as the velvet pepper, is a climbing vine native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its smooth, waxy leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruits are berries that mature to a dark purple color, containing several seeds.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
