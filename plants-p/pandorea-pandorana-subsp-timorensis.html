Pandorea pandorana subsp. timorensis

Pandorea pandorana subsp.

timorensis, commonly known as the Timor pandorea, is a flowering plant native to the Timor-Leste region of Southeast Asia. It is a climbing shrub characterized by its woody stems and heart-shaped leaves.

The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
