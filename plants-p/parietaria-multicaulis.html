Parietaria multicaulis

Parietaria multicaulis, commonly known as the common wall-rocket, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, green, oval-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. Its flowers are tiny, yellowish-white, and clustered in dense, branched inflorescences.

Parietaria multicaulis is often found growing in moist, rocky soils along walls and in disturbed areas.
