Piper zonulatispicum

Piper zonulatispicum, commonly known as the Zonula Spicata pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its fruits are peppery, with a distinct flavor, and are used in local cuisines.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
