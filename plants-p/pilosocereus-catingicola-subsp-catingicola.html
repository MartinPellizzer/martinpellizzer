Pilosocereus catingicola subsp. catingicola

Pilosocereus catingicola subsp.

catingicola, commonly known as the Catinga cactus, is a species native to Brazil. It is a columnar cactus with a distinctive appearance, featuring a smooth, pale green stem and spiny areoles.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom during the dry season. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky soils.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the Cactaceae family.
