Peperomia haworthiana

Peperomia haworthiana, commonly known as the Haworth's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features broad, glossy, dark green leaves that are often slightly wavy or notched along the edges. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia haworthiana prefers well-draining soil and moderate humidity, making it a popular choice for indoor gardeners.
