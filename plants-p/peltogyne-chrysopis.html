Peltogyne chrysopis

Peltogyne chrysopis, commonly known as the yellow ironwood, is a tropical tree native to regions of Southeast Asia and northern Australia.

It belongs to the family Leguminosae (Fabaceae), and is characterized by its dense, hard wood and distinctive yellowish bark. The plant has bipinnate leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.
