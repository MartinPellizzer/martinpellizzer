Peperomia pachyphylla var. molokaina

Peperomia pachyphylla var. molokaina, commonly known as the Molokai peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Hawaii.

It features thick, waxy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The leaves are arranged in a rosette pattern, giving the plant a compact and dense appearance. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color.

This variety is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its slow growth and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.
