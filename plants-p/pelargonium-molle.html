Pelargonium molle

Pelargonium molle, commonly known as the velvetleaf geranium, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety foliage and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate in shape and covered in fine, pubescent hairs.

Pelargonium molle is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
