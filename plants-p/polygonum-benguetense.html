Polygonum benguetense

Polygonum benguetense, commonly known as the Benguet knotweed, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes species known for their distinctive leaf shapes and small flowers. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit, with slender stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences and are typically greenish-white in color.

Polygonum benguetense is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forest understories and along stream banks.
