Piper salzmannii

Piper salzmannii, commonly known as the Salzmann's pepper or Salzmann's piper, is a climbing shrub native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers and has compound leaves with opposite arrangement. Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns red when mature.

The plant is notable for its aromatic leaves and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
