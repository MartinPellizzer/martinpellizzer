Peperomia papantlacensis

Peperomia papantlacensis, commonly known as the Mexican peperomia, is a tropical plant native to Mexico.

It is a member of the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit. The plant features small, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color.

Peperomia papantlacensis is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.
