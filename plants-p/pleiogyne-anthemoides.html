Pleiogyne anthemoides

Pleiogyne anthemoides, commonly known as the golden daisy or African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features showy yellow flowers with a central disk surrounded by ray florets, which bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are opposite, narrow, and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
