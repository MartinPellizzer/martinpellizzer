Pharbitis heterophylla

Pharbitis heterophylla, commonly known as morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its rapid growth and distinctive spiral-shaped leaves. The plant produces vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in the early morning. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and blooms.

Pharbitis heterophylla is also known for its climbing habit and can be found in a variety of habitats, including gardens and wild areas.
