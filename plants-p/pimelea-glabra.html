Pimelea glabra

Pimelea glabra, commonly known as the smooth pimelea, is a low-growing shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Rutaceae and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in cylindrical inflorescences. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in a fine, silky coating, giving it a distinctive appearance.
