Panicum viride var. nanum

Panicum viride var. nanum, commonly known as dwarf green panicgrass, is a low-growing, perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bright green and have a fine texture. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with delicate, greenish or yellowish flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental use in gardens.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is often used in landscaping for erosion control and as a ground cover.
