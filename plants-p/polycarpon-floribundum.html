Polycarpon floribundum

Polycarpon floribundum, commonly known as the clustered filaree, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender stems that often form dense clusters. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Polycarpon floribundum is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.
