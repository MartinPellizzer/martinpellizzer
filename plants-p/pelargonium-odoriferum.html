Pelargonium odoriferum

Pelargonium odoriferum, commonly known as the sweet geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, often fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as a shrub with hairy, glandular leaves that emit a pleasant scent. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

The flowers are produced in clusters and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
