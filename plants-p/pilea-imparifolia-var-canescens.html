Pilea imparifolia var. canescens

Pilea imparifolia var. canescens, commonly known as the silver pilea, is a variant of the pilea plant characterized by its distinctive silvery-gray foliage.

The leaves are heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, with a lighter coloration compared to the typical green of the species. It is a member of the Araceae family and is native to southern China. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
