Piptatherum paradoxum

Piptatherum paradoxum, commonly known as the paradox grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and fine, blue-green foliage. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Piptatherum paradoxum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
