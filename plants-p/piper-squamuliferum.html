Piper squamuliferum

Piper squamuliferum, commonly known as the velvet leaf pepper, is a tropical climbing shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves that are covered in fine, scale-like hairs. Its small, yellowish-green flowers are followed by small, peppercorn-like fruits.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
