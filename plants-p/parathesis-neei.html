Parathesis neei

Parathesis neei, commonly known as the white-flowered parathesis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the wetlands of Southeast Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters at the tips of the stems. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Parathesis neei thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in marshy or swampy habitats.
