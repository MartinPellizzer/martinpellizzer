Periandra mediterranea

Periandra mediterranea, commonly known as the Mediterranean periandra, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is well adapted to Mediterranean climates. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring, and its leaves are narrow and leathery. Periandra mediterranea is often found in coastal areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various insects and small animals.
