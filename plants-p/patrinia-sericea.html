Patrinia sericea

Patrinia sericea, commonly known as silverleaf patrinia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shaded areas.

Patrinia sericea is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
