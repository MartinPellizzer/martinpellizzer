Pelargonium × notabile

Pelargonium × notabile, commonly known as the showy geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red, with five petals and a distinctive five-lobed calyx. The plant features upright, branching stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Native to South Africa, it is cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a potted plant.

Pelargonium × notabile thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
