Petrea arborescens

Petrea arborescens, commonly known as the Brazilian flame tree, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall. The plant produces showy, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well adapted to tropical and subtropical climates.
