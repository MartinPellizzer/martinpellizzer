Piper nubigenum var. nubigenum

Piper nubigenum var. nubigenum, commonly known as the cloud forest pepper, is a climbing shrub native to the humid highlands of Central and South America.

It features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy green surface and a slightly pubescent underside. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, followed by elongated, cylindrical berries that turn red when mature. Its aromatic leaves are often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.

The species is adapted to shade-tolerant environments and thrives in moist, well-drained soils.
