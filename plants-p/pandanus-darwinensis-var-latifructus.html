Pandanus darwinensis var. latifructus

Pandanus darwinensis var. latifructus, commonly known as the broad-fruited pandanus, is a species native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and long, narrow leaves that form a rosette at the top. The plant produces large, branched inflorescences bearing numerous small, fragrant flowers. Its fruits are typically fibrous and edible, used in traditional cuisine.

This variant is notable for its adaptability to coastal and wetland environments.
