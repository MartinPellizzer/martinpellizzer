Pittosporum ledifolium

Pittosporum ledifolium, commonly known as the silver leaf pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features dense, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a silvery underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers a sunny to partially shaded environment.

Pittosporum ledifolium is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
