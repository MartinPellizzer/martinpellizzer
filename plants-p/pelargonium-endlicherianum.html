Pelargonium endlicherianum

Pelargonium endlicherianum, commonly known as the Cape marigold, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive orange to yellow flowers and hairy, lobed leaves. The plant typically grows in shrub form, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun exposure.

Pelargonium endlicherianum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
