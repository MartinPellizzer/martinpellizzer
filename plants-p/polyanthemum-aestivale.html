Polyanthemum aestivale

Polyanthemum aestivale, commonly known as the summer polyanthemum, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces clusters of small, fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often grown as a decorative ground cover. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its flowering period adds a delicate floral display to gardens during the warmer months.
