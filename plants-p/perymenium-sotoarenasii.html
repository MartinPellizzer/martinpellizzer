Perymenium sotoarenasii

Perymenium sotoarenasii, commonly known as the Soto Arenasii fern, is a rare and specialized plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that emerge from a creeping rhizome system. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in cloud forests or humid woodlands. Its reproductive structures are small and inconspicuous, relying on wind pollination for propagation.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
