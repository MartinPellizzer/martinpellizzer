Peperomia pseudobcordata

Peperomia pseudobcordata, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or purple in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia pseudobcordata is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
