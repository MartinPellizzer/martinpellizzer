Philodendron hederaceum var. kirkbridei

Philodendron hederaceum var. kirkbridei, commonly known as the Mexican evergreen philodendron, is a climbing vine native to Mexico.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a glossy texture and a slightly waxy surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red berries, though it is primarily grown for its ornamental foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers warm, humid environments.

This variety is known for its ability to climb and trail, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
