Poa phoenix

Poa phoenix, commonly known as the Phoenix grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, bluish-green leaves. It produces small, spikelet-based flowers that are typically green or reddish in color.

Poa phoenix is adapted to drought conditions and thrives in well-drained soils.
