Potentilla porciusii

Potentilla porciusii, commonly known as the Chinese cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are typically simple and alternate, with a slightly serrated margin.
