Pinus × rhaetica nothosubsp.

rhaetica, commonly known as the Rhaetian pine, is a hybrid conifer native to the Alps. It is a cross between Pinus cembra and Pinus mugo, resulting in a tree with distinctive characteristics.

The plant typically grows at high altitudes, where it thrives in cold and rocky environments. Its needles are short and dark green, often retaining their color year-round.

The cones are small and woody, containing seeds that are dispersed by wind.
