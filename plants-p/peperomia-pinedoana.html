Peperomia pinedoana

Peperomia pinedoana, commonly known as the Pinedoana pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Piperaceae family.

It is native to Central and South America, particularly found in regions of Brazil, Colombia, and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit, glossy dark green leaves, and small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Peperomia pinedoana is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
