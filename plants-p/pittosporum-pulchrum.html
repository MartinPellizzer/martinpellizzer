Pittosporum pulchrum

Pittosporum pulchrum, commonly known as the elegant pittosporum, is a flowering shrub native to New Zealand.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and emit a sweet fragrance when crushed. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in coastal and temperate regions.
