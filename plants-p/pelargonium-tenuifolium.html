Pelargonium tenuifolium

Pelargonium tenuifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features upright stems and small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, typically blooming in spring and summer. It is a hardy perennial that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Pelargonium tenuifolium is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
