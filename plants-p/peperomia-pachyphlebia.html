Peperomia pachyphlebia

Peperomia pachyphlebia, commonly known as the thick-stemmed peperomia, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and broad, waxy leaves. The leaves are typically dark green with a glossy texture and can be heart-shaped or ovate in form. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color.

This plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in indirect light.
