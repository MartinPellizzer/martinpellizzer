Phyteuma orbiculare var. fistulosum

Phyteuma orbiculare var. fistulosum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved phyteuma, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It features upright stems that bear showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to purple. The leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slight curl, and are arranged alternately along the stem. This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it adaptable to a range of garden conditions.
