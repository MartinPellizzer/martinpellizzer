Piper perobumbratum

Piper perobumbratum, commonly known as the dark pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers that develop into elongated, dark purple berries. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of unique spices.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
