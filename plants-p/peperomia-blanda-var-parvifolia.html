Peperomia blanda var. parvifolia

Peperomia blanda var. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaf peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are smaller and more rounded compared to other peperomia varieties. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters, typically during the spring or early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
