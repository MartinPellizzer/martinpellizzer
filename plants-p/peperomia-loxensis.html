Peperomia loxensis

Peperomia loxensis, commonly known as the Loxense Peperomia, is a compact evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are often dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white or pale pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and ornamental foliage, it is valued for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
