Polyalthia nervosa var. nieuwenhuisii

Polyalthia nervosa var. nieuwenhuisii, commonly known as the Nieuwenhuis's evergreen tree, is a variant of the species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a tall, evergreen tree that can reach heights of up to 30 meters, with a straight trunk and a dense canopy. The leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a smooth margin and a glossy green upper surface. It produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, followed by elongated, woody pods containing flat, winged seeds.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.
