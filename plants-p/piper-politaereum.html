Piper politaereum

Piper politaereum, commonly known as the velvet leaf pepper, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves and small, clustered flowers. Its fruit, a small, three-lobed berry, contains the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine.

The plant's botanical features make it valuable for both ornamental and medicinal purposes.
