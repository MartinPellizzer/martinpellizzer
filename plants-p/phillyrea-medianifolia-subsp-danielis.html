Phillyrea medianifolia subsp. danielis

Phillyrea medianifolia subsp.

danielis, commonly known as the Mediterranean Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy.

The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers in clusters during the spring. Its fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that ripens to a red or orange color.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
