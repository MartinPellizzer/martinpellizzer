Pelonastes tillaeacea

Pelonastes tillaeacea, commonly known as the desert thornbush, is a shrub native to arid regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its small, spiny leaves and yellowish flowers that bloom during the rainy season. The plant has a deep root system that allows it to survive in drought conditions. It plays a vital role in stabilizing sandy soils and providing habitat for local wildlife.

Its resilience makes it an important species in ecological restoration projects.
