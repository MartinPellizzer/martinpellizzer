Pennisetum ciliare var. robustior

Pennisetum ciliare var. robustior, commonly known as purple lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant purple to reddish plumes that appear in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, fluffy seed heads that attract birds and other wildlife. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for drought-tolerant landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to provide seasonal color and texture in gardens.
