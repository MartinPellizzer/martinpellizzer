Pilosella straminea

Pilosella straminea, commonly known as the common goat's beard, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, leafy stems and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine, soft hairs.

Pilosella straminea is often found in temperate regions of Europe and parts of Asia.
