Pleurothallis rodriguesii

Pleurothallis rodriguesii, commonly known as Rodrigues's pleurothallis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Madagascar.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features slender, wiry stems and produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinct, fragrant scent. Its leaves are usually narrow and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
