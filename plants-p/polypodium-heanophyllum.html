Polypodium heanophyllum

Polypodium heanophyllum, commonly known as the Heanophyllum fern, is a species of evergreen fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that typically grow in a compact rosette form. The plant produces spores in small, round sporangia located on the underside of its leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its unique morphology and habitat preferences make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
