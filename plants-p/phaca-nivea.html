Phaca nivea

Phaca nivea, commonly known as the snowflake plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with small, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
