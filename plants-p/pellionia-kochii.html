Pellionia kochii

Pellionia kochii, commonly known as the silver mitten, is a low-growing evergreen perennial native to the wet tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a distinctive silvery underside that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. Pellionia kochii thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental foliage.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and shaded garden settings.
