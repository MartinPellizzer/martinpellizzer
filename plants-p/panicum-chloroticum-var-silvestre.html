Panicum chloroticum var. silvestre

Panicum chloroticum var. silvestre, commonly known as wild millet, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and produces small, pale green or yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. It is an annual species that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various environmental conditions.
