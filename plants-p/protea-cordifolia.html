Protea cordifolia

Protea cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences composed of numerous pink to red floral bracts. The plant typically grows in coastal and mountainous regions, thriving in well-drained, acidic soils. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, giving it its common name.

Protea cordifolia is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological importance in supporting local biodiversity.
