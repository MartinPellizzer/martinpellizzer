Pennisetum setaceum var. orientale

Pennisetum setaceum var. orientale, commonly known as the oriental fountain grass, is a decorative grass native to Asia.

It features slender, upright stems with fine, silver-blue foliage that creates a soft, feathery appearance. The plant produces delicate, plume-like flower spikes that add a graceful touch to gardens. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
