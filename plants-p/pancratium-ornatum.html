Pancratium ornatum

Pancratium ornatum, commonly known as the yellow rain lily, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that are typically green and grow from a bulbous base. Its flowers are tubular and often have a distinctive orange or red throat, making it visually appealing.

Pancratium ornatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
