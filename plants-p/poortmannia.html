Poortmannia

Poortmannia, commonly known as the purple tansy, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in dry, sandy soils. Poortmannia is native to regions of Europe and parts of Asia.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
