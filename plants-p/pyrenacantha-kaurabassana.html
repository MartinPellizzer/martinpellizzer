Pyrenacantha kaurabassana

Pyrenacantha kaurabassana, commonly known as the Kaurabassan pyrenacantha, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its woody stems and dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
