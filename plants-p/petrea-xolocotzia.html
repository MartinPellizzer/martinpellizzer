Petrea xolocotzia

Petrea xolocotzia, commonly known as the Mexican trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its striking orange-red tubular flowers. The plant features woody stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Petrea xolocotzia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract hummingbirds.
