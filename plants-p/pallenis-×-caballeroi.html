Pallenis × caballeroi

Pallenis × caballeroi, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between two species of orchids, typically Pallenis and another related species.

It is a herbaceous plant with striking, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant has a pseudobulb structure typical of many orchids, which helps it store water and nutrients. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This hybrid is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and collections.
