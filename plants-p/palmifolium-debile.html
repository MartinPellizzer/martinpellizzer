Palmifolium debile

Palmifolium debile, commonly known as the delicate palm fern, is a small, tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its feathery, pinnately divided leaves that give it a delicate, fern-like appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are usually green and may have a slight sheen, while its stems are slender and often creeping along the ground.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid, low-light conditions.
