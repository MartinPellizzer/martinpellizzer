Perezia virens

Perezia virens, commonly known as the greenleaf perezia, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a sunny to partially shaded environment.

Perezia virens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
