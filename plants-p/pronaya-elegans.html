Pronaya elegans

Pronaya elegans, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are long and strap-like, often with a waxy texture, while its flowers are usually white or pale pink with intricate patterns.

Pronaya elegans is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
