Palura chinensis var. pilosa

Palura chinensis var. pilosa, commonly known as the hairy Chinese palura, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its woody stems and dense, hairy foliage that gives it its varietal name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, well-drained soils.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
