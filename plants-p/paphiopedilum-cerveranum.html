Paphiopedilum cerveranum

Paphiopedilum cerveranum, commonly known as the Cervera's lady's slipper, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped flower with a vibrant pink and white coloration. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. It produces a single, showy flower per stem, which blooms during the summer months.

Paphiopedilum cerveranum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
