Piper klugianum

Piper klugianum, commonly known as the Klug's pepper, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various pepper species. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by dark blue berries that are edible. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly peppery aroma.

Piper klugianum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of medicinal compounds.
