Protium inconforme

Protium inconforme, commonly known as the Indian beech, is a deciduous tree native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and simple, alternate leaves. The tree is valued for its hard wood and is also used in traditional medicine.
