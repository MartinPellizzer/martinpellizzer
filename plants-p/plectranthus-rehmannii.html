Plectranthus rehmannii

Plectranthus rehmannii, commonly known as the silver velvet plant, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features hairy, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate sunlight.

Plectranthus rehmannii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and ease of care.
