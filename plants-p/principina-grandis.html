Principina grandis

Principina grandis, commonly known as the celestial magnolia, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter, featuring a unique combination of white and lavender hues. The plant has broad, dark green leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil rich in organic matter.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and tropical landscapes.
