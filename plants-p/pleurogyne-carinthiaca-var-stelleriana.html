Pleurogyne carinthiaca var. stelleriana

Pleurogyne carinthiaca var. stelleriana, commonly known as the Steller's pleurogyne, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
