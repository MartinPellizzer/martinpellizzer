Pandanus kanehirae

Pandanus kanehirae, commonly known as the Kona pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pandanaceae.

It is native to the Hawaiian Islands and is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional Hawaiian crafts. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or green in color. It thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to salty air and well-drained soil.

Due to its unique appearance and cultural significance, it is considered an important part of Hawaii's natural heritage.
