Petrosedum × affomarcoi

Petrosedum × affomarcoi, commonly known as the hybrid stonecrop, is a low-growing succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It features fleshy, upright stems covered in small, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, yellowish flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in full sun make it a hardy and resilient plant for gardeners in arid climates.
