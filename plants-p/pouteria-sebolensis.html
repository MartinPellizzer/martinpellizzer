Pouteria sebolensis

Pouteria sebolensis, commonly known as the golden spondias, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and simple, alternate leaves. The tree is also valued for its ornamental qualities and potential medicinal properties.
