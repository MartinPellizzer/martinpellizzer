Pennisetum baojiense

Pennisetum baojiense, commonly known as the Baoji grass, is a species of grass native to China.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. This plant is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and riverbanks. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential in ecological restoration projects.
