Pleconax conica var. subconica

Pleconax conica var. subconica, commonly known as the narrow-conical pleconax, is a unique variant of the pleconax genus.

It is characterized by its conical or subconical shape, which distinguishes it from other varieties within the species. The plant typically exhibits a compact growth habit with a single, upright stem. Its foliage is usually dense and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in controlled environments for its aesthetic appeal.
