Peperomia saxigaudens

Peperomia saxigaudens, commonly known as the rock peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to South America.

It features thick, waxy, and often variegated leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
