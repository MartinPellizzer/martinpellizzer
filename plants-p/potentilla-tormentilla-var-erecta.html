Potentilla tormentilla var. erecta

Potentilla tormentilla var. erecta, commonly known as erect cinquefoil, is a perennial shrub native to North America.

It features small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with five leaflets, giving it the common name "cinquefoil.". It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or rocky areas.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
