Pennisetum panormitanum

Pennisetum panormitanum, commonly known as the Panicum grass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green to bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, fluffy seeds that are often dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread across open habitats. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it adaptable to various environmental conditions.

Pennisetum panormitanum is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
