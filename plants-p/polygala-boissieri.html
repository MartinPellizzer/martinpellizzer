Polygala boissieri

Polygala boissieri, commonly known as the Boissier's milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, often pale pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are usually small and opposite, while its stems are often erect and branched.

The flowers are arranged in clusters and attract various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.
