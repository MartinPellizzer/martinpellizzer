Pelargonium × rubefactum

Pelargonium × rubefactum, commonly known as the scarlet geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers and rounded, velvety leaves. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, depending on the climate. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from spring to fall.
