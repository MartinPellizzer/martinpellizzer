Pittosporum nativitatis

Pittosporum nativitatis, commonly known as the New Zealand pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features dense, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring, followed by yellowish-orange berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

Pittosporum nativitatis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native ecosystems.
