Polygonum toktogulicum

Polygonum toktogulicum, commonly known as the Toktogul knotweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants commonly referred to as buckwheats and smartweeds. The plant is characterized by its erect stems and lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color.

Polygonum toktogulicum is often found in moist or wet habitats such as riverbanks and marshy areas.
