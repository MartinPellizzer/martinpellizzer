Premna tapintzeana

Premna tapintzeana, commonly known as the Taiti or Taiti tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale purple, blooming during the rainy season. It is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and along stream banks.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in local medicine.
