Phyllanthus sublanatus subsp. eliae

Phyllanthus sublanatus subsp.

eliae, commonly known as the African moonseed, is a shrub native to tropical Africa. It is characterized by its small, elliptic leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain several seeds.

It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and open woodlands.
