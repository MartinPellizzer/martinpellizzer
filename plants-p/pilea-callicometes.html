Pilea callicometes

Pilea callicometes, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features round, fleshy leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by round, orange-red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and ease of care have made it a favored ornamental plant worldwide.
