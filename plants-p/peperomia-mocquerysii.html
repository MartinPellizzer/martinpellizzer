Peperomia mocquerysii

Peperomia mocquerysii, commonly known as the Mocquery's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, upright growth with thick, waxy leaves that are usually dark green and sometimes variegated. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia mocquerysii is prized for its attractive foliage and low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
