Pilea hollickii

Pilea hollickii, commonly known as the Shingle Plant, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub characterized by its dense, overlapping foliage that resembles shingles, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by bright red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea hollickii is relatively low maintenance and suitable for beginners due to its adaptability and resilience.
