Pelargonium sericeum

Pelargonium sericeum, commonly known as the silky geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray, fuzzy foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or red flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Pelargonium sericeum is also valued for its aromatic leaves and is often used in traditional medicine.
