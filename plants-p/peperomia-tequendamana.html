Peperomia tequendamana

Peperomia tequendamana, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to South America.

It features thick, waxy, silver-green leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia tequendamana is relatively low maintenance and suitable for indoor cultivation.
