Piper subcaniramum

Piper subcaniramum, commonly known as the Sipitang pepper, is a climbing shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by oval, dark blue berries. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped with a rough texture and a mild peppery scent.

The fruit is often used in local cuisine and traditional medicine for its aromatic and medicinal properties.
