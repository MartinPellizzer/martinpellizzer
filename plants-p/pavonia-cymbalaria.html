Pavonia cymbalaria

Pavonia cymbalaria, commonly known as the scarlet fanflower, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 feet. The plant features showy, pink to red, fan-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Pavonia cymbalaria thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
