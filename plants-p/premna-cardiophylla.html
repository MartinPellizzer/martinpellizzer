Premna cardiophylla

Premna cardiophylla, commonly known as the heart-leaf premna, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking heart-shaped leaves, which are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Premna cardiophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
