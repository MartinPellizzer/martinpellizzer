Prosthechea × chixoyensis

Prosthechea × chixoyensis, commonly known as the Chixoy hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species within the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its epiphytic growth habit, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other structures without being parasitic. The plant produces intricate, showy inflorescences with pendulous flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its leaves are elongated and fleshy, adapted for water storage in its natural habitat.

Prosthechea × chixoyensis is native to the tropical regions of Central America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments.
