Prunus rubicunda

Prunus rubicunda, commonly known as the red cherry plum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its attractive, reddish-pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant produces small, sweet, edible fruits that are often used in jams and preserves. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth texture with a bluish-green hue.

Prunus rubicunda thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.
