Polygala kerneri

Polygala kerneri, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery inflorescences. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. Its small, white or pink flowers bloom in late spring to early summer.

Polygala kerneri is often used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
