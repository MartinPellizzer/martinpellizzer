Peperomia sachatzinzumba

Peperomia sachatzinzumba, commonly known as the Zambian peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires indirect sunlight to prevent leaf scorch.

Peperomia sachatzinzumba is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
