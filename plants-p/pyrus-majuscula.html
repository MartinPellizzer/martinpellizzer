Pyrus majuscula

Pyrus majuscula, commonly known as the large pear.

It is a deciduous tree native to the mountainous regions of central and southern China. The plant produces large, fragrant flowers in the spring, followed by edible pears that ripen in the autumn. Its leaves are broad and serrated, providing a distinctive appearance.

The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and its fruit, which is used in both culinary and medicinal applications.
