Peperomia brevipeduncula

Peperomia brevipeduncula, commonly known as the short-stemmed pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features rounded, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences of pale greenish-white flowers. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
