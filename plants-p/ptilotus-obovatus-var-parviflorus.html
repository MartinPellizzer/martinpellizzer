Ptilotus obovatus var. parviflorus

Ptilotus obovatus var. parviflorus, commonly known as the small-flowered spinifex, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, pink or red flowers that bloom in summer. It is well adapted to arid and coastal environments, thriving in sandy soils.

This species is often used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
