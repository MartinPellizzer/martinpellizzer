Plectranthus sphaerophyllus

Plectranthus sphaerophyllus, commonly known as the globe-leaf plectranthus, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rounded, fleshy leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and ease of care.
