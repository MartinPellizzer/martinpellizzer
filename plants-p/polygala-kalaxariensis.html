Polygala kalaxariensis

Polygala kalaxariensis, commonly known as the Kalaxari polygala, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae, which includes a variety of herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and purple. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
