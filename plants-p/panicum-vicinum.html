Panicum vicinum

Panicum vicinum, commonly known as slender panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slightly rough texture.

It is often found in meadows, along roadsides, and in areas with poor soil conditions.
