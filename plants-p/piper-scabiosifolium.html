Piper scabiosifolium

Piper scabiosifolium, commonly known as the scabious-leaved pepper, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its elongated, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are clustered in spikes and are followed by small, fleshy fruits.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
