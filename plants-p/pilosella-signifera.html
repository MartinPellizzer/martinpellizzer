Pilosella signifera

Pilosella signifera, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance.

Pilosella signifera produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.
