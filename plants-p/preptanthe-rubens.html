Preptanthe rubens

Preptanthe rubens, commonly known as the red-flowered preptanthe, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking red petals and small, clustered flowers that bloom during the warm seasons. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and undergrowth. Its leaves are broad and glossy, with a slightly serrated edge.

Preptanthe rubens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
