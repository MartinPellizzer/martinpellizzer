Pilea villicaulis

Pilea villicaulis, commonly known as the silky pilea, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it a soft, luxurious appearance. The plant features small, inconspicuous flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in the spring. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea villicaulis is relatively low maintenance, making it an ideal plant for beginners.
