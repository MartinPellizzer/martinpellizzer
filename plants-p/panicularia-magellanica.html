Panicularia magellanica

It belongs to the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, panicle-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It produces small, wind-pollinated flowers that are often green or pale in color.
