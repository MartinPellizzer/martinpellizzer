Packera neomexicana var. toumeyi

Packera neomexicana var. toumeyi, commonly known as the New Mexico ragwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of lanceolate, hairy leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the family. It thrives in open, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive component of the local flora in its native range.
