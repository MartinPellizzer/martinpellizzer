Phyllorkis leptosepala

Phyllorkis leptosepala, commonly known as the slender-sepaled grassveld orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to southern Africa.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a few narrow leaves and produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with elongated sepals. Its unique morphology and habitat make it a notable species in the region's flora.

Due to its specific ecological requirements, it is considered vulnerable to habitat loss and environmental changes.
