Pourthiaea laevis var. albescens

Pourthiaea laevis var. albescens, commonly known as the white-flowered pourthiaea, is a variant of the pourthiaea laevis species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its pale to white flowers, which contrast with its green foliage. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has a compact growth habit with small, narrow leaves.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal in rock gardens and as a drought-tolerant plant.
