Podochilus ovalis

Podochilus ovalis, commonly known as the oval orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Podochilus ovalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
