Polystichum × fominii

Polystichum × fominii, commonly known as the Fomin's wood fern, is a hybrid fern species belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its elegant, lance-shaped fronds that are typically dark green with a silvery underside. The plant produces sporangia on the underside of its fertile fronds, which are clustered in elongated sori. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in temperate regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
