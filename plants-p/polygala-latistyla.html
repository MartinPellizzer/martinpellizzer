Polygala latistyla

Polygala latistyla, commonly known as the broad-leaved milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, often pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sandy or rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. Its leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, and it produces small, bead-like fruits.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
