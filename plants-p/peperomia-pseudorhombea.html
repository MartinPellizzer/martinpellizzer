Peperomia pseudorhombea

Peperomia pseudorhombea, commonly known as the oval peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia pseudorhombea is valued for its ornamental foliage and air-purifying qualities.
