Phebalium Elatius

Phebalium elatius is a unique and striking plant native to the south-western region of Australia. Known for its vibrant yellow flowers and distinctive foliage, it stands out among native flora. This species thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners. Its ecological importance supports local pollinators and wildlife. With its beauty and adaptability, Phebalium elatius is a valuable addition to both natural and cultivated landscapes.
