Pleurothallis regalis

Pleurothallis regalis, commonly known as the royal pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is appreciated for its ornamental value.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
