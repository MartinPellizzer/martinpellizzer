Pilea wrightiana var. intermedia

Pilea wrightiana var. intermedia, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by round, spiny fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and ease of care contribute to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
