Peperomia cilibractea

Peperomia cilibractea, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia cilibractea is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
