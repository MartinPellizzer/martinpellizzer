Poterium Guestphalicum

Common nasturtium, scientifically known as Poterium guestphalicum, is a vibrant and hardy annual plant native to the Mediterranean region. With its bold, colorful flowers and edible leaves, it has become a popular choice for gardens and home landscapes. This versatile plant is not only ornamental but also has culinary and medicinal uses, making it a valuable addition to both the garden and kitchen. Its ability to thrive in poor soil and attract beneficial insects makes it a favored companion plant. Whether grown for beauty, food, or ecological benefits, the common nasturtium remains a beloved and adaptable plant in many regions.
