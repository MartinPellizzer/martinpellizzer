Plantago lagopus var. minor

Plantago lagopus var. minor, commonly known as lesser plantain, is a variant of the common plantain.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia. The plant features broad, oval leaves with a waxy surface and produces small, greenish flowers in spikes. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.

This variety is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
