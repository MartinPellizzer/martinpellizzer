Plocoglottis gigantea

Plocoglottis gigantea, commonly known as the giant fern, is a striking member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is native to New Zealand and is characterized by its large, lush fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant features a robust rhizome system that supports its extensive growth. Its fronds are typically bipinnate, with a feathery appearance that adds to its ornamental value.

Plocoglottis gigantea thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
