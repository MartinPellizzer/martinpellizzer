Phebalium distans

Phebalium distans, commonly known as the narrow-leaf wattle, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow flowers in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Phebalium distans is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native habitats.
