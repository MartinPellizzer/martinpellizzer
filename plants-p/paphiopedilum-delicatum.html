Paphiopedilum delicatum

Paphiopedilum delicatum, commonly known as the elegant lady's slipper, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped pouch that serves as a pollination trap, a feature typical of the orchid family. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers with a velvety texture. It prefers cool, shaded environments and well-drained, humus-rich soil.

Paphiopedilum delicatum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its delicate appearance.
