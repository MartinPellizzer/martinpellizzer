Parodia bellavistana

Parodia bellavistana, commonly known as the Bellavistana cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed structure and a gray-green color. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are typically short-lived. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
