Pteris lorigera

Pteris lorigera, commonly known as the narrow-leaf water fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically dark green and finely divided. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near streams or in humid forests. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Pteris lorigera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in aquatic or terrarium settings.
