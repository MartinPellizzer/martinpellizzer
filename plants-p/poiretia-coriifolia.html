Poiretia coriifolia

Poiretia coriifolia, commonly known as the smooth-leafed poiretia, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
