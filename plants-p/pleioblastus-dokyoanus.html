Pleioblastus dokyoanus

Pleioblastus dokyoanus, commonly known as the Japanese silver bamboo, is a species of grass-like plant native to Japan.

Pleioblastus dokyoanus is also valued for its use in traditional Japanese gardens and as a low-maintenance landscaping plant.
