Pilea flexuosa

Pilea flexuosa, commonly known as the Shingle Plant, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its ability to form a rosette of fleshy, oval-shaped leaves. The leaves are typically a deep green with a waxy texture and often have a slightly hairy surface. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers on slender stems, which appear in spring.

This plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and ease of care.
