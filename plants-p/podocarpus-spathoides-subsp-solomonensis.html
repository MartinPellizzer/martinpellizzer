Podocarpus spathoides subsp. solomonensis

Podocarpus spathoides subsp.

solomonensis, commonly known as the Solomon Islands podocarp, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of the Solomon Islands. It belongs to the Podocarpaceae family and is characterized by its needle-like leaves and conical shape.

The plant produces small, spherical cones that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland and montane forests.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
