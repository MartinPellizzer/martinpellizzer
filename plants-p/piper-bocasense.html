Piper bocasense

Piper bocasense, commonly known as the Bocasense pepper or Bocas Island pepper, is a flowering plant native to the Bocas del Toro region of Panama.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant is characterized by its trailing growth habit, with small, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of tiny white flowers. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

Piper bocasense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and potential medicinal properties.
