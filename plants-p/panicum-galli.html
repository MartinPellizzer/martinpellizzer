Panicum galli

Panicum galli, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a species of grass native to the warm temperate and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense, fluffy panicles that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, spikelet-based flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. Panicum galli thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands, grasslands, and along roadsides.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its upright growth habit and seasonal color changes.
