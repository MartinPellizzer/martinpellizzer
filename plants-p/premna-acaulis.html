Premna acaulis

Premna acaulis, commonly known as the rock premna, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and is often found growing on rocky slopes or in shaded forest areas. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green in color. Its flowers are small, white or pale green, and it produces small, round berries that are edible but not particularly flavorful.

Premna acaulis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture for its ability to thrive in challenging environments.
