Pergularia odora

Pergularia odora, commonly known as the sweet-scented plantain, is a flowering plant belonging to the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, arranged in spikes. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Pergularia odora is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
