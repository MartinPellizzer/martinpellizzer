Panicum viride

Panicum viride, commonly known as green panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, erect stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height, with narrow, elongated leaves that are usually a vibrant green. The plant produces fluffy, three-branched spikelets that are characteristic of the grass family. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it adaptable to various environmental conditions.

Panicum viride is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
