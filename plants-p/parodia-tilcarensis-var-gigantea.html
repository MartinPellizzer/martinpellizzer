Parodia tilcarensis var. gigantea

Parodia tilcarensis var. gigantea, commonly known as the giant Parodia, is a cacti species native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its large, ribbed stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its size and striking appearance, it is a popular choice for outdoor gardens and rockeries.
