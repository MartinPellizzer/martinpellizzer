Palicourea orosiana

Palicourea orosiana, commonly known as the mountain beauty or silver leaf, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in humid, shaded environments at high elevations. The plant is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, black seeds.

Palicourea orosiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
