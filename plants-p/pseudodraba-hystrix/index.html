Pseudodraba Hystrix

Pseudodraba hystrix is a rare and resilient plant native to the arid regions of central Asia. It thrives in harsh environments where few other species can survive. Its unique adaptations allow it to store water and withstand extreme temperatures. The plant's striking appearance and ecological significance make it a subject of scientific interest. Despite its hardiness, it remains endangered due to habitat loss and climate change.
