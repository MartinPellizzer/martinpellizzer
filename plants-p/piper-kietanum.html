Piper kietanum

Piper kietanum, commonly known as the Kietanum pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered green berries that turn red when mature. It is often found in humid, shaded environments within the understory of tropical forests.

The plant has been studied for its potential medicinal properties due to the presence of various bioactive compounds.
