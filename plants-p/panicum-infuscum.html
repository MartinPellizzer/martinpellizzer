Panicum infuscum

Panicum infuscum, commonly known as black panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dark green to almost black foliage and produces dense, fluffy seed heads. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural areas. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and is known for its rapid growth and adaptability.

Panicum infuscum is sometimes considered a weed but can also be used for erosion control in certain environments.
