Pavonia amplifolia

Pavonia amplifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf pavonia, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy, clustered flowers are usually pink or red, blooming in summer.

Pavonia amplifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
