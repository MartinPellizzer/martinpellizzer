Pelargonium ecklonii

Pelargonium ecklonii, commonly known as the Cape geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces clusters of small, pink to red flowers. The leaves are typically rounded with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and containers.
