Pultenaea rotundifolia

Pultenaea rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf bush pea, is a small shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. The plant features rounded, often hairy leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. Its seeds are enclosed in slender, curved pods and are dispersed by wind.

Pultenaea rotundifolia is typically found in sandy soils and is adapted to coastal and dry environments.
