Peperomia fraseri var. resediflora

Peperomia fraseri var. resediflora, commonly known as the Reseda-flowered Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or red, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental appeal and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
