Pagamea plicata var. glabrescens

Pagamea plicata var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth pagamea, is a variant of the pagamea plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves and compact growth habit. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade conditions. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Botanically, it belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and vegetables.
