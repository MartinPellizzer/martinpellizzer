Pouteria loretensis

Pouteria loretensis, commonly known as the Loretense soursop, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and fragrant, white flowers. The plant thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
