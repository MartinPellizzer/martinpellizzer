Polyscias gomphophylla

Polyscias gomphophylla, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmately lobed leaves that are often a vibrant green with a yellowish or golden hue on the underside. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are followed by orange-red berries containing several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Polyscias gomphophylla is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
