Peperomia sucumbiosensis

Peperomia sucumbiosensis, commonly known as the Ecuadorian peperomia, is a species native to the Andean regions of Ecuador.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia sucumbiosensis is valued for its ornamental foliage and low maintenance requirements.
