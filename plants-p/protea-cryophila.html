Protea cryophila

Protea cryophila, commonly known as the snow protea, is a unique flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, spherical inflorescence composed of numerous bracts that resemble petals. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and is typically found at high altitudes where temperatures are lower. Its flowers are usually white or pale blue, with a central cluster of small, tubular blooms.

Protea cryophila is well adapted to nutrient-poor soils and requires specific conditions to flourish.
