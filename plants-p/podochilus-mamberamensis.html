Podochilus mamberamensis

Podochilus mamberamensis, commonly known as the Mamberamense Podochilus, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant has strap-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the undergrowth of tropical forests.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a lesser-known but ecologically significant species.
