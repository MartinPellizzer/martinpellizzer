Phillyrea medianifolia subsp. verdagueri

Phillyrea medianifolia subsp.

verdagueri, commonly known as the Canary Island Phillyrea, is a shrub or small tree native to the Canary Islands. It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruits are small, woody drupes that contain a single seed.

This subspecies is adapted to the dry, rocky environments of its native habitat.
