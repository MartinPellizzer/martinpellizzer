Peperomia ciliatifolia

Peperomia ciliatifolia, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, ovate to lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers during the summer months, which are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a desirable ornamental plant.
