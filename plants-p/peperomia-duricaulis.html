Peperomia duricaulis

Peperomia duricaulis, commonly known as the upright peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, upright growth with thick, waxy leaves that are typically green to reddish in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for tabletop displays and hanging baskets.
