Pilosella kalksburgensis subsp. cymosella

Pilosella kalksburgensis subsp.

cymosella, commonly known as the Kalksburg dwarf hawkweed, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Central Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth habit.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense, cymose inflorescences, which are typical of the genus Pilosella. Its leaves are usually covered in fine, white hairs, giving the plant a distinctive, fuzzy appearance.

It thrives in rocky or limestone soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.
