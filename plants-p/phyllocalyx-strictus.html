Phyllocalyx strictus

Phyllocalyx strictus, commonly known as the narrow-leafed acanthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and showy, spiky inflorescences. Phyllocalyx strictus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
