Premna corymbosa var. minor

Premna corymbosa var. minor, commonly known as the small-leaf false mallow, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, ovate leaves that are usually hairy on the underside. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in certain regions.
