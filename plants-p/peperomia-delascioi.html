Peperomia delascioi

Peperomia delascioi, commonly known as the Delascio peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slight variegation, often adorned with red or purple veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or red, emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia delascioi is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
