Polystichum bicolor

Polystichum bicolor, commonly known as the silver fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the eastern United States.

It features dark green, leathery fronds with a distinctive silvery-white margin that gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its fronds are bi-lobed, with a narrow, linear shape and a sturdy, upright growth habit.

Polystichum bicolor is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural settings for its elegant appearance.
