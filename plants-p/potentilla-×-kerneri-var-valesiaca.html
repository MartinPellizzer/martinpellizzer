Potentilla × kerneri var. valesiaca

Potentilla × kerneri var. valesiaca, commonly known as the alpine cinquefoil, is a hybrid shrub native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun, making it well-suited for alpine gardens. Its leaves are palmately divided, with five to seven leaflets that are often covered in fine hairs.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
