Peperomia quotifolia

Peperomia quotifolia, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, often adorned with reddish or purple veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish, emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia quotifolia is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and tolerating a range of indoor conditions.
