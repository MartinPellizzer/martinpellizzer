Polemonium pulcherrimum subvar. shastense

Polemonium pulcherrimum subvar. shastense, commonly known as ShastanJacob's Ladder, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It features upright stems with whorled, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces clusters of blue to purple flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
