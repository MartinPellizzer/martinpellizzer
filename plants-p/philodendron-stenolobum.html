Philodendron stenolobum

Philodendron stenolobum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves that are typically narrower than those of other philodendron species. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by elongated, cylindrical fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
