Plumeria oligoneura

Plumeria oligoneura, commonly known as the sweet scented plumeria, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, waxy flowers that bloom in shades of white, yellow, and pink. The plant has soft, paddle-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is well-suited for tropical gardens.

The flowers are also used in traditional ceremonies and have cultural significance in some regions.
