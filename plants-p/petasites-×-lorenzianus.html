Petasites × lorenzianus

Petasites × lorenzianus, commonly known as the giant cow parsley, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flower heads that bloom in the spring. Native to the mountainous regions of Europe, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its size and striking appearance, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its ornamental value.
