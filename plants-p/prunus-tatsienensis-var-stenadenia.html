Prunus tatsienensis var. stenadenia

Prunus tatsienensis var. stenadenia, commonly known as the Chinese bird's eye cherry, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its small, bright red fruits that resemble bird's eyes. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are ovate with serrated edges, and it produces fragrant white flowers in early spring.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
