Puccinellia retroflexa var. suecica

Puccinellia retroflexa var. suecica, commonly known as the Swedish glasswheat, is a perennial grass species native to northern Europe.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy or gravelly soils, often in coastal or inland dunes. Its leaves are narrow and glabrous, with a bluish-green coloration.

It plays a role in stabilizing dune ecosystems and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
