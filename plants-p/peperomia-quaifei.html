Peperomia quaifei

Peperomia quaifei, commonly known as the Queensland Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Australia.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which appear in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia quaifei is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
