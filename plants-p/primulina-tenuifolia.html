Primulina tenuifolia

Primulina tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf primula, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or white in color. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating, giving it a unique appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Due to its delicate nature, it requires careful attention to watering and light conditions to flourish.
