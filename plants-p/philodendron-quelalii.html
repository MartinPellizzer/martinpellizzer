Philodendron quelalii

Philodendron quelalii, commonly known as the Mexican Shield-leaf Philodendron, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth. Its leaves often feature a distinctive shield-like shape, giving it its common name.

Philodendron quelalii is a popular houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
