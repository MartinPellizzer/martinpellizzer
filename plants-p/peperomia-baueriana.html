Peperomia baueriana

Peperomia baueriana, commonly known as the Bauer's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves that are often slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically borne in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and attractive foliage, it is often used as a decorative plant in both home and office environments.
