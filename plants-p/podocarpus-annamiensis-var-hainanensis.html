Podocarpus annamiensis var. hainanensis

Podocarpus annamiensis var. hainanensis, commonly known as the Hainan podocarp, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the Podocarpaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, scale-like leaves. The tree produces cone-like structures that contain seeds, typical of conifers. It thrives in humid, subtropical climates and is often found in mountainous regions with well-drained soil.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
