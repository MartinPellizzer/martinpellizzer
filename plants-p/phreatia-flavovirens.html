Phreatia flavovirens

Phreatia flavovirens, commonly known as the yellow-green orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow and green floral coloration, which is unique among its genus. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are clustered in inflorescences and emit a faint fragrance, attracting pollinators such as bees and moths.

The plant's botanical structure includes long, narrow leaves and a complex root system adapted for nutrient absorption from its host.
