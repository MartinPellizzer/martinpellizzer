Pleurothallis ionantha

Pleurothallis ionantha, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a small epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, narrow leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its flowers are usually clustered in racemes and bloom during the wet season.

Due to its compact size and ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings.
