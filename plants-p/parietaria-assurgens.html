Parietaria assurgens

Parietaria assurgens, commonly known as the wall rocket or stinging nettle weed, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and Africa.

It is a low-growing annual or biennial herb that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features alternate, heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges and produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters. Its leaves contain histamine and other irritants, which can cause skin reactions in some individuals.

It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found along walls, roadsides, and in gardens.
