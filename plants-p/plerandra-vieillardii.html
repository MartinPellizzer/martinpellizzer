Plerandra vieillardii

Plerandra vieillardii, commonly known as the Giant Arrowroot, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrowhead-shaped leaves that can grow up to 2 meters in length. The plant produces strikingly colorful inflorescences with bright yellow spathe and spadix, often surrounded by a hood-like structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Plerandra vieillardii is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its potential medicinal properties.
