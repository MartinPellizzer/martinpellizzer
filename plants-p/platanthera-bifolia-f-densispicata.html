Platanthera bifolia f. densispicata

Platanthera bifolia f. densispicata, commonly known as the dense-spiked lady's tresses, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, spike-like inflorescence composed of numerous small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and marshes. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

This orchid is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in temperate regions of Europe and North America.
