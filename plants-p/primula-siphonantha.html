Primula siphonantha

Primula siphonantha, commonly known as the Siphonanthus primrose, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained soil. Its foliage consists of broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down.

Primula siphonantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
