Pelargonium × versicolor

Pelargonium × versicolor, commonly known as the multicolor geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant typically has rounded, lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in a range of climates.

Pelargonium × versicolor is often cultivated in gardens and as a potted plant for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
