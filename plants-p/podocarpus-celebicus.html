Podocarpus celebicus

Podocarpus celebicus, commonly known as the Celebes podocarp, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Podocarpaceae family and is characterized by its dense, needle-like leaves and cone-bearing structure. The plant is typically found in humid, tropical rainforest environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils. It has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 30 meters in its natural habitat.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
