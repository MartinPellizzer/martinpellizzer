Protea odoratissima

Protea odoratissima, commonly known as the sweet-scented protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences composed of numerous petal-like bracts. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pink in color. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

The sweet, fragrant scent of its flowers attracts pollinators such as bees and birds.
