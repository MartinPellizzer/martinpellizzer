Primula × salisii

Primula × salisii, commonly known as the hybrid primrose, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features showy, funnel-shaped flowers that come in a range of colors including white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–30 cm, with hairy, grass-like leaves. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

The hybrid is valued for its hardiness and ability to bloom reliably in temperate climates.
