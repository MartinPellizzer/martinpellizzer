Peperomia fragrans

Peperomia fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant peperomia, is a perennial evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features compact, glossy, dark green leaves that are often heart-shaped or ovate. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Due to its attractive foliage and pleasant scent, it is a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
