Phalaenopsis × leucorrhoda var. grandiflora

Phalaenopsis × leucorrhoda var. grandiflora, commonly known as the hybrid phalaenopsis orchid, is a popular epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically light green to bluish-green in color. The plant produces long, pendant inflorescences with numerous white or pale yellow flowers that have a velvety texture. It is known for its ability to bloom multiple times throughout the year under proper care.

The variety is favored for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation in both indoor and greenhouse settings.
