Peperomia tenella var. tyleri

Peperomia tenella var. tyleri, commonly known as the Silver Bracts Peperomia, is a compact, low-growing herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features striking, glossy, silver-green ovate leaves with prominent veins, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically hidden beneath the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate watering.

Due to its ornamental foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and bonsai enthusiasts.
