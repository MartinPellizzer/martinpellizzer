Pycreus atronervatus

Pycreus atronervatus, commonly known as the black-veined pycreus, is a striking member of the Aroid family, characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves with prominent black veins.

Native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of many Aroid species. The plant's unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value.

However, it requires careful attention to watering and light conditions to flourish.
