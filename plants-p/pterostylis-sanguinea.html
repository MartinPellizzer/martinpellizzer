Pterostylis sanguinea

Pterostylis sanguinea, commonly known as the red-stemmed helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. The plant features a slender, often reddish stem and produces small, greenish flowers with a distinctive helmet-shaped labellum. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

Pterostylis sanguinea is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique morphology and ecological niche.
