Palura chinensis var. pubescens

Palura chinensis var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy Chinese palura, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by elliptical, woody fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its foliage and unique growth habit.
