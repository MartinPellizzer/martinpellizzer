Paepalanthus Fastigiatus

Paepalanthus fastigiatus is a unique and striking plant known for its dense, spiny foliage and vibrant red flowers. Native to the arid regions of South America, it thrives in harsh, rocky environments where few plants can survive. This plant is often mistaken for a cactus due to its appearance, but it belongs to the bromeliad family. Its compact size and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for collectors and gardeners. Despite its beauty, it requires specific care to flourish in non-native settings.
