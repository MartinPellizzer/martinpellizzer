Peperomia huahinensis

Peperomia huahinensis, commonly known as the Huahin peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to Thailand.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive, often variegated foliage. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and drought tolerance.

Peperomia huahinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care in a home environment.
