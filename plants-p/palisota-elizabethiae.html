Palisota elizabethiae

Palisota elizabethiae, commonly known as Elizabeth's spider plant, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features long, arching leaves that are dark green with a silvery-blue underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in summer, adding visual interest. It is relatively easy to care for, thriving in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Palisota elizabethiae is often used in gardens and as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability.
