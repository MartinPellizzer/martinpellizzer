Potentilla erecta var. sciaphila

Potentilla erecta var. sciaphila, commonly known as the shade-loving potentilla, is a low-growing shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens. The plant features small, rounded leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, appearing in clusters and blooming in early summer.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging, shaded conditions.
