Phaeocordylis

Phaeocordylis, commonly known as the ghost mushroom, is a genus of fungi belonging to the family Tricholomataceae.

These fungi are characterized by their pale, often ghostly appearance, with a cap that can range from white to pale yellow or even reddish in some species. Their delicate structure and unique coloration make them both ecologically significant and visually intriguing.
