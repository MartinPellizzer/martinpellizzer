Pisonia graciliflora var. subferruginosa

Pisonia graciliflora var. subferruginosa, commonly known as the red-veined pisonia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its delicate, pendulous flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a soft, velvety texture. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that contain numerous seeds.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal or limestone areas.
