Polystachya kindtiana

Polystachya kindtiana, commonly known as the Kindt's spikegrass, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance. The plant produces dense clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
