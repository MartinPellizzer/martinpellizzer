Pilea mexicana var. vegetior

Pilea mexicana var. vegetior, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Mexico.

It features rounded, fleshy leaves that are typically a vibrant green, often with a slight reddish tinge on the undersides. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ease of care and attractive foliage make it a favored ornamental plant.
