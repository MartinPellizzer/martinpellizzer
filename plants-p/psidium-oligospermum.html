Psidium oligospermum

Psidium oligospermum, commonly known as the small passionfruit, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, waxy flowers and edible, citrus-scented fruits. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also cultivated for its unique flavor in some regions.
