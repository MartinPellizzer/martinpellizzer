Peperomia granata

Peperomia granata, commonly known as the red peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with prominent veins and reddish stems, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white or pale pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia granata is relatively low-maintenance, requiring minimal watering and pruning to maintain its compact form.
