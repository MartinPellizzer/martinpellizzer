Panicum elegantulum

Panicum elegantulum, commonly known as the elegant panicgrass, is a grass species native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and produces small, delicate flowers that are typically pale green to brown in color. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas. It is relatively drought-tolerant and can withstand moderate grazing.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in regions with warm climates.
