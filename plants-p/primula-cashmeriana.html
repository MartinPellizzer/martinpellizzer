Primula cashmeriana

Primula cashmeriana, commonly known as the Kashmir primrose, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are broad and ovate, with a hairy texture that helps retain moisture.

Primula cashmeriana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
