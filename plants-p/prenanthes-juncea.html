Prenanthes juncea

Prenanthes juncea, commonly known as slender burr cup, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist habitats such as wet meadows and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture.

The flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters, contributing to its ornamental value.
