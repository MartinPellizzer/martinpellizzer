Psychanthus speciosus

Psychanthus speciosus, commonly known as the showy psychanthus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that display a mix of vibrant colors, often including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are large, lance-shaped, and have a glossy surface, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Psychanthus speciosus is valued in horticulture for its unique floral display and adaptability to controlled indoor environments.
