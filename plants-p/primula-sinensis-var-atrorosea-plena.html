Primula sinensis var. atrorosea-plena

Primula sinensis var. atrorosea-plena, commonly known as the Japanese primrose or Christmas rose, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its dense, double flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant features broad, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

This variety is particularly noted for its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
