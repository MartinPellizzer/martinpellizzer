Phyllanthus boninsimae

Phyllanthus boninsimae, commonly known as the Bonin sandmat, is a flowering plant native to the Bonin Islands.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are borne on short pedicels. Its leaves are elliptical or ovate, with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy appearance.

Phyllanthus boninsimae is adapted to rocky and coastal environments, showcasing its resilience to harsh conditions.
