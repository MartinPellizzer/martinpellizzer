Pinalia lyonii

Pinalia lyonii, commonly known as the Lyon's pinyon, is a rare and endemic shrub native to specific regions of the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Pinaceae family, which includes pines and other conifers, though it is not a true pine. The plant is characterized by its small, needle-like leaves and produces cone-like structures that contain its seeds.

Due to its limited geographic range and habitat specificity, Pinalia lyonii is considered a botanically significant species for ecological and conservation studies.
