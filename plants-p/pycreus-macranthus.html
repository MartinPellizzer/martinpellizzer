Pycreus macranthus

Pycreus macranthus, commonly known as the large-flowered pycreus, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. Native to tropical regions of Africa, this plant thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. Its foliage is broad and lance-shaped, adding a lush texture to its overall appearance.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
