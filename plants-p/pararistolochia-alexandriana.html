Pararistolochia alexandriana

Pararistolochia alexandriana, commonly known as Alexandrian birthwort, is a climbing vine native to southern Africa.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves with a distinctive veining pattern. The plant produces intricate, horseshoe-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
