Polygala leptosperma

Polygala leptosperma, commonly known as the narrow-leaved milkwort, is a small, herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. It is adapted to arid environments and has a deep root system to access moisture.

Polygala leptosperma is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
