Phoebe sheareri var. longepaniculata

Phoebe sheareri var. longepaniculata, commonly known as the longpanicle phoebe, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and distinctive long, drooping panicles of small, fragrant white flowers. The leaves are typically elliptical with a smooth texture and a glossy surface. It produces small, round, yellowish fruits that are borne in clusters.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid, tropical climates.
