Pelargonium × laxiflorum

Pelargonium × laxiflorum, commonly known as the sweet geranium, is a hybrid species within the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to red. The leaves are typically rounded and covered in fine hairs, giving them a velvety texture. Native to South Africa, this plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a container plant.
