Pyrrosia nudicaulis

Pyrrosia nudicaulis, commonly known as the naked-leaf fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, leathery fronds that are typically light green to yellowish in color. The plant grows on trees and rocks, often in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are simple, with a smooth margin and a short, blunt apex.

Pyrrosia nudicaulis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
