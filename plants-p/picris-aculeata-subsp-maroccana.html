Picris aculeata subsp. maroccana

Picris aculeata subsp.

maroccana, commonly known as the Moroccan prickly lettuce, is a flowering plant native to North Africa. It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. It has a compact growth habit with hairy stems and is often found in open landscapes.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural studies.
