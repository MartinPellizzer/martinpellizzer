Pittosporum podocarpum

Pittosporum podocarpum, commonly known as the New Zealand pittosporum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath, with a fragrant, waxy texture. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring, followed by dark purple berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

Pittosporum podocarpum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in New Zealand's native ecosystems.
