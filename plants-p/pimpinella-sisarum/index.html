Pimpinella Sisarum

Pimpinella sisarum is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central Asia. It is known for its small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in late spring. This species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique plant from extinction.
