Plumeria xanthostoma

Plumeria xanthostoma, commonly known as the Yellow Plumeria, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and pink. The plant has smooth, pale green bark and produces waxy, palmate leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. Its flowers are typically borne on long, slender peduncles and bloom during the dry season.

The plant is popular in ornamental horticulture due to its striking appearance and cultural significance in various regions.
