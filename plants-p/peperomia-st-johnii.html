Peperomia st-johnii

Peperomia st-johnii, commonly known as the St. John's Peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and glossy, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia st-johnii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance requirements and ornamental appeal.
