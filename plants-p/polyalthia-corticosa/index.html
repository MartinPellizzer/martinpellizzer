Polyalthia Corticosa

Polyalthia corticosa is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia, known for its striking foliage and ecological significance. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in primary rainforests. Its leaves are large, glossy, and have a unique veining pattern that sets it apart from other plants in the family. The tree plays a vital role in supporting local biodiversity by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, Polyalthia corticosa is increasingly being studied for conservation and sustainable use.
