Philodendron orionis

Philodendron orionis, commonly known as the Orion Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves that are often marked with silver or white veins, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has a vigorous growth habit and can reach lengths of several meters when provided with adequate support. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its foliage makes it an attractive addition to hanging baskets or shelves.
