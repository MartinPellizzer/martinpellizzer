Polyadenia insignis

Polyadenia insignis, commonly known as the yellow evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that open in the evening. The plant has hairy, ribbed stems and lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in open fields or along roadsides.
