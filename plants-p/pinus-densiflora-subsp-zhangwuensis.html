Pinus densiflora subsp. zhangwuensis

Pinus densiflora subsp.

zhangwuensis, commonly known as the Zhangwu pine, is a rare and relict species native to southern China. It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and distinctive cone structure.

The tree typically grows in mountainous regions with acidic soils and a humid climate. It has small, needle-like leaves that are often arranged in clusters.

This subspecies is of significant botanical interest due to its limited distribution and unique adaptations to its environment.
