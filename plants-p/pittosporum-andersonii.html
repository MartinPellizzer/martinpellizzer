Pittosporum andersonii

Pittosporum andersonii, commonly known as the Anderson's pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a reddish or purplish hue on the underside. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by orange to red berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland forest areas.

Pittosporum andersonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native habitats.
