Pandanus kusaicola

Pandanus kusaicola, commonly known as the Kusaicola pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the palm family.

It is native to the Pacific islands, particularly found in parts of Micronesia. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. It produces small, fragrant flowers that develop into edible fruits.

This species is adapted to coastal and sandy environments, making it an important component of tropical coastal ecosystems.
