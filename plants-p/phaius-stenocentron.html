Phaius stenocentron

Phaius stenocentron, commonly known as the narrow-lobed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in summer. The plant has strap-shaped leaves and produces a dense cluster of pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Phaius stenocentron is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
