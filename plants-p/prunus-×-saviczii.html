Prunus × saviczii

Prunus × saviczii, commonly known as the Chinese bird cherry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. Its fruits are small, yellowish drupes that ripen in late summer.

Prunus × saviczii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its spring blossoms.
