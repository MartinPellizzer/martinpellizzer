Piper azuaiense

Piper azuaiense, commonly known as the Azua pepper or Azua pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the Amazon rainforest, specifically found in parts of Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with dark green, elongated leaves and small, clustered white flowers. Its fruit is a small, green berry that turns red when ripe, and it is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The plant's botanical features make it an important component of the biodiversity in its native habitat.
