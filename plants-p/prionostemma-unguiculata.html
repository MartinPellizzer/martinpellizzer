Prionostemma unguiculata

Prionostemma unguiculata, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a waxy coating.

The plant is known for its milky sap, which is toxic and can cause skin irritation.
