Pisonia nitida

Pisonia nitida, commonly known as the silky pisonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its delicate, white, pendulous flowers that are often surrounded by soft, silky bracts. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a leathery texture.

Pisonia nitida is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm climates.
