Pittosporopsis kerrii

Pittosporopsis kerrii, commonly known as the Kew Pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 meters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and a reddish-brown underside. Its flowers are small, white, and fragrant, appearing in clusters during the spring.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.
