Protea angolensis var. angolensis

Protea angolensis var. angolensis, commonly known as the Angola protea, is a striking evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in a compact, bushy form. The plant produces striking inflorescences composed of large, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, which bloom in spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
