Paphiopedilum delenatii f. vinicolor

Paphiopedilum delenatii f. vinicolor, commonly known as the violet-flowered slipper orchid, is a rare and exquisite orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple petals and sepals, which contrast beautifully with its green and yellow floral structures. The plant has a thick, fleshy rhizome and produces large, showy flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil for optimal growth.

This orchid is highly valued in horticulture for its unique and vibrant floral display.
