Philotheca myoporoides subsp. myoporoides

Philotheca myoporoides subsp.

myoporoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved myopor, is a small shrub native to southeastern Australia. It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence.

The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers arranged in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in coastal or sandy habitats.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
