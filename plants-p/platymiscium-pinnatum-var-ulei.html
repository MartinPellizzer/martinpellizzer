Platymiscium pinnatum var. ulei

Platymiscium pinnatum var. ulei, commonly known as the Ulei plum tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are pinnately arranged and often have a glossy appearance. The tree produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by edible, round fruits resembling plums. Its botanical features make it suitable for ornamental purposes and agroforestry systems.

The plant is also valued for its timber, which is lightweight and durable.
