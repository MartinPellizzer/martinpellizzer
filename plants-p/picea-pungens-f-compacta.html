Picea pungens f. compacta

Picea pungens f. compacta, commonly known as the compact form of the blue spruce, is a cultivar of the blue spruce tree.

It is characterized by its dense, pyramidal shape and striking blue-gray needles. The plant is evergreen, with a slow growth rate, making it suitable for smaller garden spaces. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, exhibiting good resistance to pests and diseases.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping and ornamental gardening.
