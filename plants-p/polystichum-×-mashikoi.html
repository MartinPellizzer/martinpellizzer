Polystichum × mashikoi

Polystichum × mashikoi, commonly known as the hybrid evergreen fern, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Polystichum setiferum and Polystichum hirtum.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green, leathery fronds that are typically upright and form a compact, bushy plant. The plant is native to the temperate regions of Asia and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden areas.

The fronds are evergreen, providing year-round interest in the garden.
