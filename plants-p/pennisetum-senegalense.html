Pennisetum senegalense

Pennisetum senegalense, commonly known as red oat grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and striking red or purple plumes that appear in late summer. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a silver-gray appearance that adds visual interest to gardens.

Pennisetum senegalense is often used as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
