Peperomia villicaulis

Peperomia villicaulis, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance houseplant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, and velvety leaves that are typically deep green with a glossy texture. The plant has a short, upright stem and produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that are not showy. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for those seeking ornamental foliage without the need for extensive care.
