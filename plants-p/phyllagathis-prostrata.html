Phyllagathis prostrata

Phyllagathis prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate phyllagathis, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features small, oval-shaped leaves that are usually arranged in opposite pairs along its stems. Its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences and are often pale pink or white in color.

Phyllagathis prostrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture for ground cover in tropical gardens.
