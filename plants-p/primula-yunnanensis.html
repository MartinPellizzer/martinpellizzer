Primula yunnanensis

Primula yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan primrose, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that come in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas at high elevations. Its foliage consists of broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hair.

Primula yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is a popular species among gardeners and botanists alike.
