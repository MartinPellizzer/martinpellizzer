Pultenaea parviflora var. angustifolia

Pultenaea parviflora var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pultenaea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features small, narrow leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces clusters of tiny, bright yellow flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal heathlands and open woodlands.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and restoration projects.
