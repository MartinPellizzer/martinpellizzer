Piper pitalitoense

Piper pitalitoense, commonly known as the Pitalito pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Colombia. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with heart-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers. Its fruits are small berries that turn from green to red when mature.

Piper pitalitoense is valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a spice in local cuisines.
