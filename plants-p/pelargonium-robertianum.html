Pelargonium robertianum

Pelargonium robertianum, commonly known as the Robert's geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, while the flowers come in shades of pink, red, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

This plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
