Primula gagnepainii

Primula gagnepainii, commonly known as the Gagnepain's primrose, is a species of flowering plant in the Primulaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of China, particularly in Sichuan and Yunnan provinces. This perennial herb features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and is adapted to cool, moist environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
