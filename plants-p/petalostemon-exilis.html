Petalostemon exilis

Petalostemon exilis, commonly known as the slender petalweed, is a small flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves and small, clustered white or pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to withstand salt spray and drought conditions.

Petalostemon exilis is a perennial herb that blooms from spring to early summer.
