Populus × canadensis var. erecta

Populus × canadensis var. erecta, commonly known as the European aspen.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Populus tremula and Populus tremuloides. This tree is characterized by its trembling leaves, which are small and ovate with a serrated margin. It typically grows in temperate regions and prefers moist, well-drained soils.

The plant has a shallow root system and is often found in riparian zones and forest edges.
