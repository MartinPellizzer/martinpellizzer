Pyrus sichuanensis

Pyrus sichuanensis, commonly known as the Sichuan pear, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and alternate, ovate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, followed by edible, sweet fruit in the autumn.

It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit production.
