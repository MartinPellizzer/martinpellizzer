Psedera Henryana

Psedera henryana is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China. It is known for its small, pale pink blossoms that bloom in early spring. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and requires careful cultivation to survive. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is now listed as endangered. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and propagate this unique species.
