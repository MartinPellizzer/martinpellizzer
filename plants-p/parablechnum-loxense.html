Parablechnum loxense

Parablechnum loxense, commonly known as the slender lichen, is a foliose lichen that thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It consists of a symbiotic association between a green alga and a fungus, which allows it to absorb nutrients from the air and rain. The plant has a thin, leaf-like structure with a pale green to yellowish coloration. It is often found on tree trunks and rocks in temperate forest regions.

Due to its delicate nature, it is sensitive to environmental changes and air pollution.
