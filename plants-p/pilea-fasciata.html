Pilea fasciata

Pilea fasciata, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are usually light green with distinct white or silver markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Pilea fasciata is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
