Pectis stenophylla var. stenophylla

Pectis stenophylla var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pectis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable growing conditions.
