Pluchea pinnatifida

Pluchea pinnatifida, commonly known as the slenderleaf mistletoe, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is an evergreen shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are pinnately divided, giving it a delicate, fern-like appearance.

It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by fluffy, white to pale pink seeds that are dispersed by wind.
