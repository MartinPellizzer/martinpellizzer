Polygonum speciosum

Polygonum speciosum, commonly known as the flame flower or red polygonum, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and orange, often appearing in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along streambanks.

Polygonum speciosum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
