Pycreus Megapotamicus Var. Jaeggii

Pycreus megapotamicus var. jaeggii is a rare and unique plant species native to the high Andean regions of South America. It belongs to the Apiaceae family, known for its aromatic and often edible members. This particular variety is distinguished by its striking white and green foliage and small, delicate flowers.
