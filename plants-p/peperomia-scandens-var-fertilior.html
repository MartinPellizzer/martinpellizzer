Peperomia scandens var. fertilior

Peperomia scandens var. fertilior, commonly known as the climbing peperomia, is a compact, trailing plant native to tropical regions.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for hanging baskets and shelves.
