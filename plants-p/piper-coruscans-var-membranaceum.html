Piper coruscans var. membranaceum

Piper coruscans var. membranaceum, commonly known as the membranous pepper plant, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, ovate leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by elliptical, red or purple berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for shade gardens and indoor plant collections.
