Pothos platyneuron

Pothos platyneuron, commonly known as the marble queen pothos, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are usually variegated with white and green patterns. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil. It is known for its ability to climb using aerial roots, often twisting around supports.

Its adaptability and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
