Phyteuma Scorzonerifolium Var. Angustissimum

Phyteuma scorzonerifolium var. angustissimum is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe. It is known for its tall, slender stems and clusters of delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep red. This variety is distinguished by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and its ability to thrive in rocky, well-drained soils. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique and beautiful plant from further decline.
