Parodia atroviridis

Parodia atroviridis, commonly known as the Black Ghost Cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its dark green, cylindrical stems that often appear almost black in color. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are typically borne at the tips of the stems. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and full sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for succulent collectors.
