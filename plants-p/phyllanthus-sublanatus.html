Phyllanthus sublanatus

Phyllanthus sublanatus, commonly known as the smooth phyllanthus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. It produces small, clustered flowers that are often greenish-yellow in color.

The species is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value or used in traditional medicine.
