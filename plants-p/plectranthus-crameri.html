Plectranthus crameri

Plectranthus crameri, commonly known as the Cape verbena, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant purple or violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets and containers.

Plectranthus crameri is also valued for its aromatic foliage and ability to attract pollinators.
