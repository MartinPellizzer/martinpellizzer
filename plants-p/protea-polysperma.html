Protea polysperma

Protea polysperma, commonly known as the many-seeded protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence composed of numerous tubular flowers surrounded by colorful bracts. The plant is native to the Western and Eastern Cape provinces of South Africa, where it thrives in nutrient-poor soils. Its unique floral structure attracts a variety of pollinators, including birds and insects.

Protea polysperma is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
