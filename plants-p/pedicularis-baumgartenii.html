Pedicularis baumgartenii

Pedicularis baumgartenii, commonly known as the yellow monkey orchid, is a member of the Orobanchaceae family and is native to the alpine regions of Central Asia.

It is a hemiparasitic plant, meaning it partially relies on host plants for water and nutrients while still performing photosynthesis. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils at high elevations, often in mountainous habitats.
