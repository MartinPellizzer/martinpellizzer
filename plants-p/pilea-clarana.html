Pilea clarana

Pilea clarana, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its round, glossy leaves that resemble coins. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are usually a deep green with a slightly waxy texture.

Pilea clarana is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
