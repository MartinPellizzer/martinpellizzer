Phalaenopsis amabilis var. rimestadiana-alba

Phalaenopsis amabilis var. rimestadiana-alba, commonly known as the White Rimestadiana Phalaenopsis, is a hybrid orchid prized for its elegant, white flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia, where it thrives in tropical climates. The plant features broad, dark green leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, long-lasting blooms. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.

This variety is particularly popular in horticultural trade for its ornamental value and adaptability to indoor growing conditions.
