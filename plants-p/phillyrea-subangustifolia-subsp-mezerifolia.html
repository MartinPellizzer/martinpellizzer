Phillyrea subangustifolia subsp. mezerifolia

Phillyrea subangustifolia subsp.

mezerifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside.

The plant produces white, fragrant flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its fruit is a small, woody capsule that splits open to release seeds. Phillyrea subangustifolia subsp.

mezerifolia is often found in rocky or dry soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
