Primula × tottenhamensis

Primula × tottenhamensis, commonly known as the Tottenham hybrid primrose, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Primula vulgaris and Primula veris.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to white. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value. It prefers partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

The species is also valued for its genetic diversity, contributing to the broader Primula genus.
