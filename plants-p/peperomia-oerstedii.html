Peperomia oerstedii

Peperomia oerstedii, commonly known as the velvetleaf peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features glossy, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia oerstedii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
