Peperomia macrandra

Peperomia macrandra, commonly known as the broad-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale green, arranged in clusters. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia macrandra is valued for its ornamental foliage and air-purifying qualities.
