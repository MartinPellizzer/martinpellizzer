Paramagnolia fraseri

It belongs to the Magnoliaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall, with a slow growth rate and a long lifespan.
