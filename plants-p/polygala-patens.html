Polygala patens

Polygala patens, commonly known as the narrow-leaved milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, and it produces small, bean-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Polygala patens is often found in open vegetation types such as grasslands and shrublands.
