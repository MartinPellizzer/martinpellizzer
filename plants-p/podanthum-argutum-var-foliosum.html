Podanthum argutum var. foliosum

Podanthum argutum var. foliosum, commonly known as the velvetleaf plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its foliage is popular in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
