Piper claseanum

Piper claseanum, commonly known as the Claseanum pepper, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to red as it matures.

The plant is often grown for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
