Plectranthus holstii

Plectranthus holstii, commonly known as the silver bush or African violet, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features aromatic, hairy leaves that are often silver or grayish in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in hue. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate sunlight, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Plectranthus holstii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens or as a houseplant.
