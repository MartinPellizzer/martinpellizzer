Peperomia limana

Peperomia limana, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are usually silver or variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of green or red. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia limana is prized for its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
