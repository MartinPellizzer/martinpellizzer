Piper amplilimbum

Piper amplilimbum, commonly known as African pepper or wild pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with opposite, ovate leaves and small, greenish-yellow flowers. Its fruit is a small, three-lobed berry that turns dark when mature.

The plant is valued for its aromatic properties and is used in traditional medicine and as a spice.
