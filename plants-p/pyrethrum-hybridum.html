Pyrethrum hybridum

Pyrethrum hybridum, commonly known as chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Asia and is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and insecticidal properties. The plant produces daisy-like flowers in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and red. Its flowering stems are rich in pyrethrins, which are natural compounds used in organic pesticides.

Pyrethrum hybridum thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and commercial cultivation.
