Peperomia scutellifolia

Peperomia scutellifolia, commonly known as the shield-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green, shield-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that emerge from the leaf axils. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and drought tolerance.

Peperomia scutellifolia is prized for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
