Pedicularis rhizomatosa

Pedicularis rhizomatosa, commonly known as the yellow monkey orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Orobanchaceae and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit, which allows it to spread and form dense colonies. Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
