Philadelphus coronarius f. deutziiflorus

Philadelphus coronarius f. deutziiflorus, commonly known as the Deutzii-flowered Mock Orange, is a cultivar of the Mock Orange plant.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant is a deciduous shrub with arching branches and dark green, elliptical leaves. It is native to the Caucasus region and is valued for its ornamental appeal and aromatic qualities.

This cultivar is often used in gardens for its pleasant scent and showy blooms.
