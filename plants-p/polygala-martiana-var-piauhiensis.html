Polygala martiana var. piauhiensis

Polygala martiana var. piauhiensis, commonly known as the Piauhi polygala, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, often delicate floral structures. The species typically grows in humid, shaded environments within the Atlantic Forest biome. Its leaves are usually opposite and oval-shaped, while the flowers are clustered in spicate inflorescences.

This variant is of interest to botanists due to its limited distribution and unique morphological features.
