Picrella trifoliata var. trifoliata

Picrella trifoliata var. trifoliata, commonly known as the three-leaved picrella, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three leaflets arranged symmetrically. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in clusters during the spring. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
