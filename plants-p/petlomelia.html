Petlomelia

Petlomelia, commonly known as the African violet, is a tropical plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and thrives in well-draining soil. Petlomelia is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, white, and blue.
