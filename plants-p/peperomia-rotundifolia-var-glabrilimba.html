Peperomia rotundifolia var. glabrilimba

Peperomia rotundifolia var. glabrilimba, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, dark green leaves that are round or ovate in shape, often with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers, which are typically followed by small, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
