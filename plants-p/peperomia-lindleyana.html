Peperomia lindleyana

Peperomia lindleyana, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, waxy leaves that are typically variegated with shades of silver and green, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, usually in shades of white or pale pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia lindleyana is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
