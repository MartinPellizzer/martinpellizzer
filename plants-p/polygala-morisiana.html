Polygala morisiana

Polygala morisiana, commonly known as the Morisiana milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often yellow or greenish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are usually small and linear, while its stems are slender and branching.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
