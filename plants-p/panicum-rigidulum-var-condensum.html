Panicum rigidulum var. condensum

Panicum rigidulum var. condensum, commonly known as dense panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in full sun and is often found in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and open fields.

Its hardy nature makes it a common weed in agricultural and natural settings.
