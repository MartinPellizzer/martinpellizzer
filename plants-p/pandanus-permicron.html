Pandanus permicron

Pandanus permicron, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically grouped in compact inflorescences. Its fibrous leaves are valued for their durability and versatility in weaving.

Pandanus permicron thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to saline and humid environments.
