Peperomia magnoliifolia var. alata

Peperomia magnoliifolia var. alata, commonly known as the winged peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features broad, waxy, lance-shaped leaves that are often adorned with distinctive, wavy margins and a pale green to white underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically green or reddish, which are followed by rounded, fleshy fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique leaf morphology and low maintenance requirements contribute to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
