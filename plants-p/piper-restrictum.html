Piper restrictum

Piper restrictum, commonly known as the restricted pepper or narrow-leaved pepper, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when ripe, and these berries contain the aromatic compound responsible for its distinctive flavor. It is often cultivated for its culinary and medicinal uses.

The leaves are also used in traditional herbal remedies due to their purported health benefits.
