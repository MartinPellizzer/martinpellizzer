Protium glabrescens

Protium glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-leafed tamanu, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Burseraceae family and is characterized by its smooth, glossy leaves and fragrant resinous bark. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in panicles. Its fruits are drupes, containing a single seed enclosed in a fleshy, orange-colored pulp.

The tree is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
