Piper asperiusculum var. glabricaule

Piper asperiusculum var. glabricaule, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed pepper plant, is a variant of the Piper asperiusculum species native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, clustered flowers followed by berries that are often used in traditional medicine. Its botanical features make it suitable for cultivation in warm, humid climates.

It is valued for both its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
