Pleione huettneriana

Pleione huettneriana, commonly known as the Huettner's pleione, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and long, narrow leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge. The plant produces a single or few showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple, often with a yellow or green lip. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its beauty and rarity, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
