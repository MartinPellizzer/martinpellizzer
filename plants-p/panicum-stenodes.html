Panicum stenodes

Panicum stenodes, commonly known as narrow-leaved panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences, which are typically green to brown in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Due to its adaptability, Panicum stenodes is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
