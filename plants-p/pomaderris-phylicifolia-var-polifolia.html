Pomaderris phylicifolia var. polifolia

Pomaderris phylicifolia var. polifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pomaderris, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically narrow and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or heathlands.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to native wildlife.
