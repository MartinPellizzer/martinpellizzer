Piresia macrophylla

Piresia macrophylla, commonly known as the broad-leaved piresia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, ovate leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and horticultural interest.
