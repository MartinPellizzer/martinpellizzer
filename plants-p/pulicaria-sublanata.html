Pulicaria sublanata

Pulicaria sublanata, commonly known as the woolly groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Pulicaria sublanata is a low-growing perennial that is well adapted to Mediterranean climates.
