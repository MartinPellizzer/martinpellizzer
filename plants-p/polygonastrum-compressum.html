Polygonastrum compressum

Polygonastrum compressum, commonly known as the slender aster.

This plant is a flowering species native to arid regions of North America. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of purple or blue. The plant is adapted to survive in dry conditions with deep root systems.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
