Prenanthes hastata var. sagittata

Prenanthes hastata var. sagittata, commonly known as arrowhead watercress, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in moist, aquatic or semi-aquatic environments, often found in wetlands and along the edges of ponds. It has a compact growth habit and is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage.

The species is also valued for its edible leaves, which have a peppery flavor similar to watercress.
