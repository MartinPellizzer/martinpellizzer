Pilosella lyccensis

Pilosella lyccensis, commonly known as the Lyccensis hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months.
