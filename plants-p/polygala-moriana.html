Polygala moriana

Polygala moriana, commonly known as the slender milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, often pale pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its small, clustered flowers are surrounded by leaf-like bracts, giving it a unique appearance.

Polygala moriana is often found in coastal areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
