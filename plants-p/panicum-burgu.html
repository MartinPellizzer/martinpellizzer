Panicum burgu

Panicum burgu, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces small, fluffy panicles of seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its propagation. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a common ornamental and forage plant.

Its drought tolerance and adaptability make it suitable for various ecological and agricultural applications.
