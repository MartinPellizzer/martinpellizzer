Phreatia goliathensis

Phreatia goliathensis, commonly known as the giant phreatia, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on tree trunks or in the undergrowth of dense forests. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding environment, which is crucial for its survival in its natural habitat.

The orchid's unique structure and blooming patterns make it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
