Panicum schiedeanum

Panicum schiedeanum, commonly known as Schiedea panicum, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the Caribbean, particularly the Greater Antilles, and is often found in wetland or marshy habitats. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stem and dense, spike-like inflorescence composed of small, two-lipped flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a distinct veining pattern.

Panicum schiedeanum is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive appearance.
