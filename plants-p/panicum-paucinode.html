Panicum paucinode

Panicum paucinode, commonly known as little bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and compact growth habit, making it a popular choice for ornamental and native plant gardens. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that add visual interest in the fall. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in prairie and open woodland habitats.

Panicum paucinode is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing food and habitat for various wildlife species.
