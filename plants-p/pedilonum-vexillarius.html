Pedilonum vexillarius

Pedilonum vexillarius, commonly known as the banner-footed orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, spurred petals that resemble a banner, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, moist environments. Its flowers are usually a striking combination of white and purple, with a strong, sweet fragrance.

Due to its limited habitat and slow growth rate, Pedilonum vexillarius is considered a vulnerable species.
