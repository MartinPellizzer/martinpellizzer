Peperomia cumulicola

Peperomia cumulicola, commonly known as the cloud peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, waxy foliage with a broad, rosette-like arrangement, often displaying vibrant shades of green, silver, or variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light with moderate humidity.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
