Pilea pleuroneura

Pilea pleuroneura, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is a low-growing, evergreen perennial with heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. Native to China and Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its unique, coin-like leaves make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by spathes, typical of the Araceae family.
