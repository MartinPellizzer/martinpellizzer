Pelargonium × imperiale

Pelargonium × imperiale, commonly known as the imperial geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with contrasting darker veins. The plant typically has rounded, lobed leaves that are hairy and dark green. It is a popular ornamental plant, often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and ease of care.

Pelargonium × imperiale thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and containers.
