Pouteria richardii

Pouteria richardii, commonly known as the yellow mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and dense, rounded canopy. It plays an important ecological role in its native habitats and is valued for both its ornamental and culinary uses.
