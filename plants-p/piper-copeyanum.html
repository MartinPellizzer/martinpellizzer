Piper copeyanum

Piper copeyanum, commonly known as the Copeyanum pepper or Cope's pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with opposite, ovate leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruits are berries that turn red when mature, containing a single seed.

The plant is valued for its aromatic leaves and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
