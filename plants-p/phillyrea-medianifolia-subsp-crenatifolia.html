Phillyrea medianifolia subsp. crenatifolia

Phillyrea medianifolia subsp.

crenatifolia, commonly known as the Crenate Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, elliptical leaves with a distinctive wavy or toothed margin, which gives it its subspecies name.

The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers followed by dark blue berries. It is adapted to dry, rocky environments and is often found in coastal areas.

Its foliage provides habitat for various insects and birds, contributing to local biodiversity.
