Pepinia heliophila

Pepinia heliophila, commonly known as the sun-loving peperomia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows as a low shrub with thick, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in its natural habitat. It thrives in bright, indirect light and is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental appeal and ease of care.

Pepinia heliophila is also valued for its ability to purify indoor air and enhance the aesthetic of any indoor space.
