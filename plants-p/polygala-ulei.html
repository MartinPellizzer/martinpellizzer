Polygala ulei

Polygala ulei, commonly known as the velvet tangle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, hairy fruits that contain several seeds.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
