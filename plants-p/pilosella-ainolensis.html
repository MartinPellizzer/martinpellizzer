Pilosella ainolensis

Pilosella ainolensis, commonly known as the Alboran Islands fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the Balearic Islands.

It is a perennial herb with a low-growing habit, characterized by its hairy stems and basal leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats and is often found on rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
