Prunus pseudocerasus var. serrulata

Prunus pseudocerasus var. serrulata, commonly known as the Chinese date plum or the sour plum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its elliptical, serrated leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, round fruits that turn from green to reddish when ripe, typically in late summer. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
