Ptychomeria breviflora

Ptychomeria breviflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ptychomeria, is a small shrub native to South Africa.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers clustered in umbels, which bloom in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
