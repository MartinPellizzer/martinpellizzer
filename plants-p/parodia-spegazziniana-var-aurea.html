Parodia spegazziniana var. aurea

Parodia spegazziniana var. aurea, commonly known as the golden parodia, is a compact, globular cactus native to Argentina.

It features a dense covering of yellowish to golden spines, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its slow growth rate and striking color make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
