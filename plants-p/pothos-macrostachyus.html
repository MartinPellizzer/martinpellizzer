Pothos macrostachyus

Pothos macrostachyus, commonly known as the giant pothos, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are often variegated with yellow or white markings. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers followed by orange berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light.

Its vigorous growth makes it an excellent choice for hanging baskets or trellises.
