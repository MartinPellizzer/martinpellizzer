Periploca ephedriformis

Periploca ephedriformis, commonly known as the African angel's trumpet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy coating.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its striking floral display.
