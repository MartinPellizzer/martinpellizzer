Pithecellobium scalare f. parvifolium

Pithecellobium scalare f. parvifolium, commonly known as the small-leaf velvet bean, is a shrub native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features trifoliate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces yellow to orange flowers. Its fruit is a legume that contains edible seeds, which are often used in traditional cuisines.

The plant is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in agroforestry systems.
