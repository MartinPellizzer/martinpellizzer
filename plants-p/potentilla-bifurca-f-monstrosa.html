Potentilla bifurca f. monstrosa

Potentilla bifurca f. monstrosa, commonly known as the giant potentilla, is a striking variation of the common potentilla.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 5 cm in diameter, typically in shades of yellow or orange. The plant features a bushy growth habit with woody stems and dark green, lobed leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and rockeries.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
