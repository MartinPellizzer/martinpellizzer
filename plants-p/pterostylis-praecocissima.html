Pterostylis praecocissima

Pterostylis praecocissima, commonly known as the early helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It is a small, ground-dwelling plant with a single, slender stem and a single, narrow leaf. The plant produces a single, pale green or brownish flower with a distinctive helmet-like lip. It typically blooms in late winter to early spring, making it one of the earliest flowering orchids in its habitat.

The species is characterized by its unique morphology and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
