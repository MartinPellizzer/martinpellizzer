Primula Farinosa F. Alba

Primula farinosa f. alba is a rare and delicate flowering plant known for its striking white blooms. It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is native to the mountainous regions of Europe. This alpine species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky soils. Its pale flowers contrast beautifully with its hairy, dark green leaves. Due to its limited habitat, it is considered a vulnerable species in the wild.
