Psilogramme imbricata

Psilogramme imbricata, commonly known as the African star grass, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or along riverbanks.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated as a ornamental or for its ecological benefits.
