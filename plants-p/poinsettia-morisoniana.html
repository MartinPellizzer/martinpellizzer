Poinsettia morisoniana

Poinsettia morisoniana, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its vibrant red bracts, which are modified leaves that surround small, inconspicuous yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is closely related to other poinsettia species. It thrives in warm, sunny environments with well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for holiday decorations and ornamental gardening.
