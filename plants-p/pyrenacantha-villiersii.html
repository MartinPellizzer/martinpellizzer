Pyrenacantha villiersii

Pyrenacantha villiersii, commonly known as the Natal holly.

It is a shrub native to South Africa, characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, glossy leaves. The plant produces clusters of white flowers followed by bright red berries, which are a striking feature. It is adapted to dry, rocky environments and is often found in coastal regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.
