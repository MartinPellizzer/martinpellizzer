Pavonia mogotensis

Pavonia mogotensis, commonly known as the Brazilian velvetleaf, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and a dark green color. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to red flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Pavonia mogotensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticultural settings.
