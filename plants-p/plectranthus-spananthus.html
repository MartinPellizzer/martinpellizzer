Plectranthus spananthus

Plectranthus spananthus, commonly known as the purple-leaf plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive purple foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, and prefers well-drained soil and full sun. Its leaves are often aromatic and have been used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Plectranthus spananthus is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for gardeners and indoor plant enthusiasts.
