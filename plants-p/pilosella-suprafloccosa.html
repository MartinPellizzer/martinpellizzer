Pilosella suprafloccosa

Pilosella suprafloccosa, commonly known as the slender pappus flower, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in meadows and grasslands. The plant is characterized by its small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in a compact cluster. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered with fine hairs, giving the plant a fuzzy appearance.
