Plantago media var. prolifera

Plantago media var. prolifera, commonly known as the common plantain, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features a rosette of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, spadix-like inflorescences with small, greenish flowers that are wind-pollinated. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

This species is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies for centuries.
