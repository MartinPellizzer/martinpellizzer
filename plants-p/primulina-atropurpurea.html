Primulina atropurpurea

Primulina atropurpurea, commonly known as the purple primula, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in the undergrowth of tropical forests. Its leaves are ovate and glossy, with a deep green color that contrasts beautifully with its vibrant blooms.

Primulina atropurpurea is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in controlled indoor settings.
