Piper anisum

Piper anisum, commonly known as star anise, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped fruit that resembles a tiny star, hence its common name. The plant belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other spice plants like black pepper. Its fruit contains essential oils that contribute to its distinctive licorice-like flavor.

Star anise is widely used in both culinary and medicinal applications due to its aromatic properties and potential health benefits.
