Peperomia albert-smithii var. albert-smithii

Peperomia albert-smithii var. albert-smithii, commonly known as the Albert-Smith peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Brazil.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of green or red. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and minimal water requirements.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for tabletop displays and hanging baskets.
