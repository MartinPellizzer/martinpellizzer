Photinia prionophylla var. nudifolia

Photinia prionophylla var. nudifolia, commonly known as the glossy leaf photinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy bloom. The plant produces small, white flowers in spring, which are followed by red, fleshy berries in the fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its vibrant foliage and seasonal interest.
