Pultenaea patellifolia

Pultenaea patellifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bush pea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features small, pea-like flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, blooming in spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.

Pultenaea patellifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
