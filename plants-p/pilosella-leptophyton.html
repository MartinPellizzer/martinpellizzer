Pilosella leptophyton

Pilosella leptophyton, commonly known as the slenderleaf gothic dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. It has hairy, narrow leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer.

Pilosella leptophyton is often found in alpine environments and is adapted to cold, high-altitude conditions.
