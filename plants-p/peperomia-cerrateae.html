Peperomia cerrateae

Peperomia cerrateae, commonly known as the Cerrado peperomia, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a compact, low-growing plant with thick, fleshy leaves that are often dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Peperomia cerrateae is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
