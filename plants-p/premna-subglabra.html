Premna subglabra

Premna subglabra, commonly known as the smooth premna, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by spherical, woody fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Premna subglabra is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
