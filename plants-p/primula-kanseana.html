Primula kanseana

Primula kanseana, commonly known as the Kansai primrose, is a species of flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Primula kanseana is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
