Polyscias scutellarioides

Polyscias scutellarioides, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or broadleaf arrowhead, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by berries containing black seeds. It is widely cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

Polyscias scutellarioides is also valued for its air-purifying properties and is commonly used in indoor gardening.
