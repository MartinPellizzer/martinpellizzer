Philodendron barbourii

Philodendron barbourii, commonly known as the Mexican evergreen philodendron, is a climbing perennial plant native to Mexico.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a prominent central vein. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth. Its aerial roots allow it to climb and attach to surfaces, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets or shelves.

Philodendron barbourii is valued for its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
