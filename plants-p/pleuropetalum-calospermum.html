Pleuropetalum calospermum

Pleuropetalum calospermum, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture. It produces showy, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
