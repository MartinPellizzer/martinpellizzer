Pellionia viridis var. basiinaequalis

Pellionia viridis var. basiinaequalis, commonly known as the glossy green moss-like plant, is a variant of Pellionia viridis, a member of the Urticaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it a mossy appearance, with leaves that are typically dark green and slightly glossy. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are usually opposite and have a slightly heart-shaped base, contributing to its unique aesthetic.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant for its lush, carpet-like growth habit.
