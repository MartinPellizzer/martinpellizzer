Polybotrya puberulenta

Polybotrya puberulenta, commonly known as the velvet fern, is a unique succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage that gives it a velvety appearance. The plant has a rosette growth habit, with leaves that are typically blue-gray in color. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer.

Due to its striking appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
