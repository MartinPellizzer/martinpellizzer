Physalis cavaleriei

Physalis cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a species native to China.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces distinctive orange-red capsules resembling small lanterns. The plant features hairy, lobed leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. Its fruits are edible when fully mature, though they are often used ornamentally due to their vibrant appearance.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.
