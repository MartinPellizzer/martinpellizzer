Potentilla Fragarioides Var. Togasii

Potentilla fragarioides var. togasii is a striking shrub native to the mountainous regions of East Asia. Known for its vibrant yellow flowers and dense, bushy growth habit, it is a popular choice for ornamental gardening. This variety thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it adaptable to various climates. Its resilience and beauty make it a favorite among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts. With its historical use in traditional medicine, it holds both aesthetic and practical value.
