Pabstiella Spathuliglossa

Pabstiella spathuliglossa is a rare and intriguing member of the lichen family. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found on tree trunks and rocks. Its unique appearance features a bright yellow to orange color with a distinctive, tongue-shaped thallus. This species is particularly sensitive to environmental changes, making it an indicator of ecological health. Studying Pabstiella spathuliglossa offers valuable insights into lichen ecology and conservation efforts.
