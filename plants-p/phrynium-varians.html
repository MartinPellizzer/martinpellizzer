Phrynium varians

Phrynium varians, commonly known as the arrow leaf plant, is a tropical perennial herb native to Central and South America.

It features large, arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers followed by spathes that enclose the fruit. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread vegetatively, making it an adaptable species in moist, shady environments.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
