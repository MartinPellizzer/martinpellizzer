Physostegia formosior f. alba

Physostegia formosior f. alba, commonly known as the white form of the Chinese lantern plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that resemble small lanterns, typically ranging in color from pale pink to white. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are hairy and slightly serrated. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden landscapes for its unique floral display.
