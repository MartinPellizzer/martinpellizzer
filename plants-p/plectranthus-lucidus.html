Plectranthus lucidus

Plectranthus lucidus, commonly known as the glossy plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is popular in gardens for its ability to thrive in a variety of conditions. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile choice for gardeners.

Plectranthus lucidus is also valued for its aromatic foliage, which can be used in herbal teas or as a fragrant garnish.
