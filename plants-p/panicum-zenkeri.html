Panicum zenkeri

Panicum zenkeri, commonly known as Zenker's grass, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and panicles of small, spikelet-like flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
