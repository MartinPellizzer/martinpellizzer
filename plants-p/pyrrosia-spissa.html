Pyrrosia spissa

Pyrrosia spissa, commonly known as the rock fern, is a terrestrial fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and compact growth habit, often forming dense mats on rocky substrates. The fronds are typically leathery and have a deep green color, with a distinctive, narrow, and elongated shape. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in shady gardens.
