Pilea delicatula

Pilea delicatula, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a small evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rosettes of glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers that are followed by round, white berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Pilea delicatula is noted for its compact growth and ease of care, making it ideal for indoor gardening.
