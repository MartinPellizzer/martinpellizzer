Plantago myosuros subsp. humilior

Plantago myosuros subsp.

humilior, commonly known as the lesser plantain, is a low-growing perennial herb native to temperate regions of Europe and parts of Asia. It typically features a basal rosette of broad, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy surface.

The plant produces small, greenish flowers in dense spikes during the summer months. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and woodland edges.

This subspecies is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
