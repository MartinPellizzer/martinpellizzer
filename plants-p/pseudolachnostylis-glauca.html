Pseudolachnostylis glauca

Pseudolachnostylis glauca, commonly known as the blue glaucous fern, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of the Araceae family.

Pseudolachnostylis glauca is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens.
