Panicum neurodes var. amplissimum

Panicum neurodes var. amplissimum, commonly known as giant panicgrass, is a tall, perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves with a prominent midrib and produces fluffy, panicle-shaped inflorescences. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth rate and ability to thrive in disturbed soils. It is often used in erosion control due to its extensive root system.

Its botanical significance lies in its adaptability and ecological role in various habitats.
