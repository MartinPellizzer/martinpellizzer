Pilea minutepilosa

Pilea minutepilosa, commonly known as the baby rubber plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features dense, rounded foliage with small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in the axils of the leaves. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardeners.
