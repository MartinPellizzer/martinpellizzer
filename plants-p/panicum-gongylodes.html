Panicum gongylodes

Panicum gongylodes, commonly known as hairgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and long, slender leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces panicles of small, spikelet-like flowers that are typically golden to brown in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along waterways.

Panicum gongylodes is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in aquatic environments.
