Peperomia non-alata

Peperomia non-alata, commonly known as the Shiny Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which appear in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and ease of care, it is well-suited for beginners and can be propagated through stem cuttings.
