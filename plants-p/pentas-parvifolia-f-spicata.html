Pentas parvifolia f. spicata

Pentas parvifolia f. spicata, commonly known as the spicata pentas, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, brightly colored flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The leaves are typically ovate to lance-shaped and have a smooth texture. This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a container plant.

It prefers full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it adaptable to a variety of garden settings.
