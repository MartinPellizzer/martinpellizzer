Panicum longeracemosum

Panicum longeracemosum, commonly known as silky grass or silky panicum, is a species of grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and long, silky, hair-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, clustered spikelets that are often wind-pollinated.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
