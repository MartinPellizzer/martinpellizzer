Panicum nitidum var. villosum

Panicum nitidum var. villosum, commonly known as silky panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves with a distinctive silvery or whitish bloom on the underside, giving it a soft, velvety appearance. The plant produces small, loose panicles of flowers that are typically greenish or reddish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or disturbed areas.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate a range of environmental conditions.
