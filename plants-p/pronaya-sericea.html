Pronaya sericea

Pronaya sericea, commonly known as the silky pronaya, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features slender, hairy stems that give it a distinctive silken appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often with a slight fragrance. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its overall texture.

Pronaya sericea is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth.
