Piper pallidirubrum

Piper pallidirubrum, commonly known as the pale red pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its climbing habit and dark green, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into elongated, pale red berries. Its botanical features include a slender stem with adventitious roots and a distinctive aromatic compound in its leaves and fruits.

It is valued both for its ornamental appeal and for its potential medicinal properties.
