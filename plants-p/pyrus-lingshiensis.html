Pyrus lingshiensis

Pyrus lingshiensis, commonly known as the Lingshi pear, is a rare species native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its deciduous nature, producing small, oval-shaped fruits. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with a preference for well-drained soils. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a serrated margin.

The flowers are white and fragrant, appearing in clusters before the fruit develops.
