Polystichum stenophyllum var. abbreviatum

Polystichum stenophyllum var. abbreviatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf wood fern, is a variant of the stenophyllum wood fern.

It is a perennial fern that typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories. The plant features lance-shaped, serrated leaves that are relatively narrow compared to other members of the genus. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, with sporangia clustered on the undersides of the leaves.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shady gardens.
