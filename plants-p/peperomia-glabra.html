Peperomia glabra

Peperomia glabra, commonly known as the smooth peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia glabra is appreciated for its ornamental foliage and ability to adapt well to a variety of indoor conditions.
