Pleione Praecox Var. Candida

Candida orchid, scientifically known as Pleione praecox var. candida, is a rare and captivating member of the orchid family found primarily in the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas. This delicate plant is prized for its striking white blooms and unique ability to thrive in cold, mountainous environments. Its name reflects the pale, almost luminous appearance of its flowers, which stand out against the rugged terrain. Despite its beauty, the Candida orchid remains elusive and is often difficult to locate in the wild. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this rare species from habitat loss and overharvesting.
