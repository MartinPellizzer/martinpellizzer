Pteris scabristipes

Pteris scabristipes, commonly known as the scaly cliff fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found growing on rocky substrates or in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are typically dark green with a scaly texture on the underside, giving it its common name.

Pteris scabristipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
