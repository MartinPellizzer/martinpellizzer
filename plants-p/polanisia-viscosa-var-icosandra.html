Polanisia viscosa var. icosandra

Polanisia viscosa var. icosandra, commonly known as the woolly antler plant, is a variant of the species Polanisia viscosa.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia, particularly China and Korea. The plant is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and distinctive, branched inflorescences. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or rocky slopes.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a notable species in its native habitats.
