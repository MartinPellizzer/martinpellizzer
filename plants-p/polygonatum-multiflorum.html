Polygonatum multiflorum

Polygonatum multiflorum, commonly known as the Chinese Solomon's Seal, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers in early summer. Its berries are red and edible, often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Polygonatum multiflorum is valued for its ornamental appearance and medicinal properties.
