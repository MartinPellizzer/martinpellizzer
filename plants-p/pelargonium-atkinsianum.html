Pelargonium atkinsianum

Pelargonium atkinsianum, commonly known as the Atkins' geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its attractive, rounded foliage and showy flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and produces flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Pelargonium atkinsianum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
