Protea lenta

Protea lenta, commonly known as the silver leaf protea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its striking, cone-shaped inflorescence. The plant features broad, silvery-green leaves that give it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Protea lenta is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
