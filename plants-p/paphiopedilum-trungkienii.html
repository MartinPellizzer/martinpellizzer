Paphiopedilum trungkienii

Paphiopedilum trungkienii, commonly known as the Trung Kien orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Vietnam.

It features a pseudobulb with a single, lance-shaped leaf and produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive lip. The plant has a thick, fleshy root system adapted to its moist, shaded habitat. It blooms in summer, with flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses due to its specific care requirements.
