Pterolobium brucei

It is native to parts of Africa, particularly the savannas of Nigeria and Cameroon. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. It produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.

Pterolobium brucei is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in semi-arid environments.
