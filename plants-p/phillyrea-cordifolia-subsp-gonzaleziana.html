Phillyrea cordifolia subsp. gonzaleziana

Phillyrea cordifolia subsp.

gonzaleziana, commonly known as the Gonzalezian Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula. It is characterized by its evergreen foliage, with dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape.

The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. These flowers are followed by small, spherical berries that are typically yellow or orange when mature.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in Mediterranean climate regions.
