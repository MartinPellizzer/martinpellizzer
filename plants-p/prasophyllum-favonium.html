Prasophyllum favonium

Prasophyllum favonium, commonly known as the yellow leek orchid, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and heathlands. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish in color, with a distinctive three-lobed structure.

Prasophyllum favonium is a perennial species that often forms dense colonies in its native environment.
