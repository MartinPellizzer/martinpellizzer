Pilea chevalieri

Pilea chevalieri, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a popular houseplant native to China and Taiwan.

It features round, shield-shaped leaves that are glossy and often have a slight waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Pilea chevalieri is known for its ease of care and ability to purify indoor air.
