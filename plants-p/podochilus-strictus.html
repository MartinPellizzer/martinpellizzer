Podochilus strictus

Podochilus strictus, commonly known as the rigid orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and its ability to produce fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically features linear leaves and produces small, greenish-white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Podochilus strictus is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
