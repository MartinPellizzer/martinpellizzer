Puccinellia Phryganodes

Puccinellia phryganodes is a small, annual grass species commonly found in arid and semi-arid regions. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often seen along roadsides and in dry fields. This plant is known for its tufted growth habit and its ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions. Its seeds are dispersed by wind, allowing it to colonize new areas quickly. Despite its common presence, it remains an important component of local ecosystems.
