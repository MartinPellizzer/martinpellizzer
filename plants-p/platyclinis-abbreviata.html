Platyclinis abbreviata

Platyclinis abbreviata, commonly known as the dwarf pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features dense, dark green foliage with a slightly glossy texture. Its small, fragrant white flowers appear in clusters during the spring and summer months.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature in temperate climates.
