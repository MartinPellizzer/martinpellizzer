Prunus persica f. compressa

Prunus persica f. compressa, commonly known as the compact peach, is a compact form of the common peach.

It exhibits a dense, rounded growth habit with closely spaced branches and leaves. The plant produces small, often dwarfed fruit compared to standard peaches. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and suitability for smaller garden spaces.

Its botanical features make it an interesting choice for both landscape design and experimental horticulture.
