Prionitis falcaria

Prionitis falcaria, commonly known as the slender broomrape, is a parasitic plant that lacks chlorophyll and relies on host plants for nutrients.

It belongs to the Orobanchaceae family and is characterized by its yellowish-green stems and small, scale-like leaves. Due to its parasitic nature, it can have negative effects on the growth and health of its host plants.
