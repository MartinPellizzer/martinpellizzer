Polypodium triseriale var. gladiatum

Polypodium triseriale var. gladiatum, commonly known as the sword fern, is a variant of the polypody fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped, serrated fronds that grow in a compact, upright form. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and has a rhizomatous growth habit. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
