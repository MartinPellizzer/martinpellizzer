Panicum minimum

Panicum minimum, commonly known as little bluestem, is a low-growing grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and delicate, feather-like flowers that appear in late summer. This plant is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in prairie ecosystems. It plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.

Panicum minimum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in native plant landscaping.
