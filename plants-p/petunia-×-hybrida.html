Petunia × hybrida

Petunia × hybrida, commonly known as the garden petunia, is a hybrid species within the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a wide range of colors, including blue, purple, red, and white. The plant is herbaceous, with opposite leaves that are usually simple and ovate in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Petunia × hybrida is often used in bedding plants and containers due to its rapid growth and long blooming period.
