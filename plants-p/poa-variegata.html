Poa variegata

Poa variegata, commonly known as the variegated bluegrass, is a cool-season grass native to the northern regions of North America.

It is characterized by its fine texture and distinctive variegated leaves that display a mix of green and silver-white hues. This plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight. Poa variegata is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.

It is also valued for its ability to form dense, attractive turf when properly cared for.
