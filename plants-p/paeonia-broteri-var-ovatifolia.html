Paeonia broteri var. ovatifolia

Paeonia broteri var. ovatifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf peony, is a rare and ornamental plant native to the mountainous regions of northern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often with a distinctive greenish tinge. The leaves are broad and ovate, contributing to its striking visual appeal. This variety is prized for its unique foliage and delicate blooms, making it a sought-after plant in botanical gardens.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, requiring careful cultivation to maintain its health and floral display.
