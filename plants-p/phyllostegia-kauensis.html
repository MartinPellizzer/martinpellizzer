Phyllostegia kauensis

Phyllostegia kauensis, commonly known as Kauai ironwood, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian island of Kauai.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen, lance-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of pink and purple. The plant is typically found in moist, shaded forest habitats and is adapted to the humid tropical climate of Hawaii. It plays an important ecological role in its native environment, providing habitat and food for various native insects and birds.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
