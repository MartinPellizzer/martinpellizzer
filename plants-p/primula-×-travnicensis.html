Primula × travnicensis

Primula × travnicensis, commonly known as the Travnicensis primrose, is a hybrid plant native to the Balkan region.

It is characterized by its vibrant, funnel-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens. Its foliage consists of broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its delicate and colorful blooms.
