Prangos longistylis

Prangos longistylis, commonly known as the long-styled fennel, is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbel-shaped flowers.

Prangos longistylis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has a mild, anise-like scent.
