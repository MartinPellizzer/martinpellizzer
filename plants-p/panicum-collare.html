Panicum collare

Panicum collare, commonly known as the collar panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are often clustered at the base of the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, disturbed areas.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and ornamental gardening.
