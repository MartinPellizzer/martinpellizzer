Phyllanthus platycalyx

Phyllanthus platycalyx, commonly known as the broad-calyx phyllanthus, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features small, simple leaves that are alternate and often have a slightly waxy texture. Its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences and are usually greenish-white in color.

The fruit is a small, smooth capsule that contains several seeds.
