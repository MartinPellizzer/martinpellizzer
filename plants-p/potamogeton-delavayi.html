Potamogeton delavayi

Potamogeton delavayi, commonly known as Delavay's pondweed, is a submerged aquatic plant native to Asia.

It typically grows in freshwater environments such as lakes, rivers, and ponds. The plant has long, slender stems that are often branched and bear small, arrowhead-shaped leaves. Its flowers are unisexual, with male and female flowers occurring on separate plants.

This species plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.
