Piper transpontinum

Piper transpontinum, commonly known as the Transpontine pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various pepper species. The plant produces small, green, oval fruits that turn red when mature, containing aromatic seeds. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a slightly serrated edge.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and mild peppery flavor.
