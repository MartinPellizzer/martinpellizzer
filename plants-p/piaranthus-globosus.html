Piaranthus globosus

Piaranthus globosus, commonly known as the globular lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and striking, showy flowers. The plant typically produces white or pale pink bell-shaped flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated for decorative purposes.
