Paeonia oreogeton

Paeonia oreogeton, commonly known as the Chinese peony, is a species native to China and belongs to the芍药属 (Paeonia) genus.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, white, and yellow. The plant is a herbaceous perennial with deeply lobed leaves and a short, thick rhizome. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

The flowers typically bloom in late spring, adding a striking visual element to the landscape.
