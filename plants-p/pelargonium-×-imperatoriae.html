Pelargonium × imperatoriae

Pelargonium × imperatoriae, commonly known as the Imperial geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, double flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically has rounded, lobed leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Pelargonium × imperatoriae is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for gardens and containers due to its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
