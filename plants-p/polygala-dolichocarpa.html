Polygala dolichocarpa

Polygala dolichocarpa, commonly known as the long-stemmed milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, delicate flowers. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are typically arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
