Phillyrea spinosa var. angustifolia

Phillyrea spinosa var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf Phillyrea, is a deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, elongated leaves that are typically dark green and glossy on top. The plant produces white, fragrant flowers in clusters during the spring, which are followed by small, capsule-like fruits. It is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Phillyrea spinosa var. angustifolia is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
