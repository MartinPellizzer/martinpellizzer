Pteris transparens

Pteris transparens, commonly known as the transparent water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Pteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that exhibit a translucent appearance, especially when viewed from the underside. Native to Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found near streams or in humid forests. Its rhizomes are creeping and can produce new plants through vegetative propagation.

The plant's unique leaf structure makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and terrariums.
