Persepolium

Persepolium, commonly known as the Persian shield, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Marantaceae and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and vibrant hues. Persepolium thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

It is popular as a ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
