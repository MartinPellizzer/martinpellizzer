Pellionia trichosantha

Pellionia trichosantha, commonly known as the silver fern or velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves that give it a delicate, almost feathery appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Pellionia trichosantha is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is relatively easy to care for in a garden setting.
