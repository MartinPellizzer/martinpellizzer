Peperomia latifolia

Peperomia latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, oval-shaped leaves that are usually dark green with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically green or red, which are followed by small, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is often used as a decorative plant in both homes and offices.
