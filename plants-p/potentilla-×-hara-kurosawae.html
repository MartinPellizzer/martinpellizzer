Potentilla × hara-kurosawae

Potentilla × hara-kurosawae, commonly known as the Japanese fireweed, is a hybrid shrub native to Japan.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage is often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to attract pollinators.
