Phaseolus panduratus f. ovatifolius

Phaseolus panduratus f. ovatifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf scarlet runner bean, is a variant of the scarlet runner bean characterized by its ovate leaves.

It is a climbing legume native to South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, tropical climates. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers that give way to elongated, edible pods. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This variety is valued for both its ornamental appeal and its use in culinary applications.
