Persicaria tenuiflora var. albiflora

Persicaria tenuiflora var. albiflora, commonly known as white flower smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It features slender, upright stems that are often hairless or only slightly hairy. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from mid-summer to early fall. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth texture, often with a reddish or purple tint on the underside.

This variety is often found in moist soils along streambanks, wet meadows, and other damp habitats.
