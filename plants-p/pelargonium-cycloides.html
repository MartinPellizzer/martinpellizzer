Pelargonium cycloides

Pelargonium cycloides, commonly known as the cycloid geranium, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces distinctive seed pods that are cylindrical and spirally twisted, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets and containers.

Pelargonium cycloides is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
