Potentilla multifida f. glabrata

Potentilla multifida f. glabrata, commonly known as the smoothleaf potentilla, is a variant of the multifida potentilla, characterized by its smooth, hairless foliage.

It is a deciduous shrub native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia, typically found in moist, well-drained soils. The plant features small, clustered white flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for garden use.

Botanically, it belongs to the Rosaceae family and is valued for its hardiness and adaptability to various garden conditions.
