Peperomia smithii

Peperomia smithii, commonly known as the Smith's pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to Central and South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features compact, fleshy leaves that are often variegated with white or silver markings. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, and it is prized for its ornamental value in indoor gardening.

Peperomia smithii is relatively easy to care for, requiring well-draining soil and moderate watering.
