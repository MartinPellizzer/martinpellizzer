Pleurothallis williamsii

Pleurothallis williamsii, commonly known as the Williams's orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and green. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil for optimal growth. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in conditions with irregular rainfall.

This orchid is popular among enthusiasts for its unique appearance and ease of cultivation under the right conditions.
