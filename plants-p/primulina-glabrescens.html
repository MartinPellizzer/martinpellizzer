Primulina glabrescens

Primulina glabrescens, commonly known as the velvet leaf.

It is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family, native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its thick, velvety leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value.

The plant's unique foliage and delicate blooms make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
