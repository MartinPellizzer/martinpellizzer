Phalaenopsis denevei

Phalaenopsis denevei, commonly known as the Denevei orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, long-lasting flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are strap-like, broad, and often glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This orchid prefers bright, indirect light and moderate humidity, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
