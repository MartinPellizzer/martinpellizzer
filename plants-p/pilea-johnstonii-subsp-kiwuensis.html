Pilea johnstonii subsp. kiwuensis

Pilea johnstonii subsp.

kiwuensis, commonly known as the African velvet plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to eastern Africa. It features dense, dark green, velvety leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a slightly waxy texture.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by spherical, red berries that attract birds. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate moisture.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
