Posoqueria versicolor

Posoqueria versicolor, commonly known as the variable posoqueria, is a species of flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescences that display a range of hues, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to saline and sandy soils. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, fragrant flowers.

Posoqueria versicolor is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and coastal landscapes.
