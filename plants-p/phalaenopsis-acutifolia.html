Phalaenopsis acutifolia

Phalaenopsis acutifolia, commonly known as the sharp-leafed orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, narrow, and sharply pointed leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces elegant, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often arranged in a spike with multiple blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining potting media.

Phalaenopsis acutifolia is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
