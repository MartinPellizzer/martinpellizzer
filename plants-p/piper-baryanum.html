Piper baryanum

Piper baryanum, commonly known as the black pepper plant, is a flowering vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piper genus within the family Piperaceae. The plant produces small, dark-colored berries that are the source of the spice black pepper. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a glossy appearance.

The plant thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.
