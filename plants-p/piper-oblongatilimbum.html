Piper oblongatilimbum

Piper oblongatilimbum, commonly known as the broad-leafed pepper vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, green, oval-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. Its fruit is a small, yellowish berry that contains aromatic seeds.

The plant is valued for its potential medicinal properties and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental foliage.
