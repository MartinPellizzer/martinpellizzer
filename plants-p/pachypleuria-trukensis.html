Pachypleuria trukensis

Pachypleuria trukensis, commonly known as the Truk Island tree, is a rare tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its thick, woody trunk and dense, evergreen foliage. The plant typically grows in humid, coastal environments with well-drained soil. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, often with a glossy surface.
