Polystachya ensifolia

Polystachya ensifolia, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of flowering plant in the family Polystachyaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, sword-shaped leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in length. It produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

Polystachya ensifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
