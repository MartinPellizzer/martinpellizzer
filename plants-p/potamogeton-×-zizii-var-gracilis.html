Potamogeton × zizii var. gracilis

Potamogeton × zizii var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender pondweed, is a hybrid aquatic plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, ribbon-like leaves that are typically less than 1 mm in width. The plant thrives in slow-moving to still freshwater environments, such as ponds and lakes. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually appearing in clusters along the stems.

It plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms and contributing to water clarity through its dense growth.
