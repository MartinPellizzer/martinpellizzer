Peperomia calcicola

Peperomia calcicola, commonly known as the limestone peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, succulent-like foliage and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light. It is well adapted to arid and rocky environments, often found growing on limestone substrates.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
