Pilosella cieselskii

Pilosella cieselskii, commonly known as the alpine mouse-ear, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-green leaves and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. Native to mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in alpine and subalpine environments. The plant is typically found in rocky soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude climates.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for alpine wildlife.
