Pelargonium × stamfordianeae

Pelargonium × stamfordianeae, commonly known as the hybrid geranium, is a flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Pelargonium hortorum and Pelargonium × inodorum. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its foliage is typically dark green and often has a slightly hairy texture.
