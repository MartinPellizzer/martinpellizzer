Pastinaca hookeriana

Pastinaca hookeriana, commonly known as Hooker's burr.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America. The plant features a robust root system and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. Its leaves are broad and lobed, often with a rough texture.

The species is noted for its ability to thrive in rocky and well-drained soils.
