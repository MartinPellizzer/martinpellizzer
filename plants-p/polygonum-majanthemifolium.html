Polygonum majanthemifolium

Polygonum majanthemifolium, commonly known as red root, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It is characterized by its reddish-orange roots and heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the summer months.

Polygonum majanthemifolium is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
