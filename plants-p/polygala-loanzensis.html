Polygala loanzensis

Polygala loanzensis, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the Polygalaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and opposite, and it produces pods that contain small, hard seeds.
