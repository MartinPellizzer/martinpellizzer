Panicum telmatodes

Panicum telmatodes, commonly known as swamp panicum, is a grass species native to wetland environments.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically thrives in saturated soils and is often found in marshy areas or along the edges of ponds. Its flowering spikes are usually slender and bear small, spikelet-like flowers.

Panicum telmatodes plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wetland organisms.
