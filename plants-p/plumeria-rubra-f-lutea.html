Plumeria rubra f. lutea

Plumeria rubra f. lutea, commonly known as the yellow frangipani, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that range from yellow to orange in color. The plant produces large, waxy leaves that are typically light green and have a smooth texture. It thrives in warm, sunny climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The flowers are commonly used in floral arrangements and traditional ceremonies due to their vibrant appearance.
