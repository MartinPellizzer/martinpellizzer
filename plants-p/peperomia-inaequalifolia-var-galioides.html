Peperomia inaequalifolia var. galioides

Peperomia inaequalifolia var. galioides, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features small, elliptical leaves with a distinctive silvery or white underside, giving it a unique appearance. The plant has short, upright stems and produces tiny, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its attractive foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
