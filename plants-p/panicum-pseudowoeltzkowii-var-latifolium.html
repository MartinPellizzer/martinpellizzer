Panicum pseudowoeltzkowii var. latifolium

Panicum pseudowoeltzkowii var. latifolium, commonly known as broadleaf panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to southeastern Asia.

It features slender, cylindrical stems and produces fluffy, spike-like inflorescences that are often pink or purplish in color. The leaves are broad and flat, with a smooth texture and a bluish-green hue. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for ornamental gardens and erosion control.

This variety is valued for its attractive appearance and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
