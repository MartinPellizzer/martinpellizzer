Phyllostegia mollis var. lydgatei

Phyllostegia mollis var. lydgatei, commonly known as Lydgate's stiltgrass, is a variant of the stiltgrass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features slender, ascending stems with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, greenish-white flowers are borne in clusters and are pollinated by insects.

This variety is characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology and is often found in moist, disturbed soils in woodland edges and stream banks.
