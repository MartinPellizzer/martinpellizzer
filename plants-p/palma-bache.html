Palma bache

Palma bache, commonly known as the Bache palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and mangrove ecosystems.

The Bache palm is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
