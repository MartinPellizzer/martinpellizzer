Polygonatum zanlanscianense

Polygonatum zanlanscianense, commonly known as the Zhejiang yellow bamboo, is a herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces clusters of yellow-green flowers in the spring. It is often found in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions.
