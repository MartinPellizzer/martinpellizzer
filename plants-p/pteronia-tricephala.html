Pteronia tricephala

Pteronia tricephala, commonly known as the three-headed pteronia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant features small, daisy-like flowers that are typically pale yellow to cream in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Pteronia tricephala is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
