Phalaenopsis stuartiana var. punctulata

Phalaenopsis stuartiana var. punctulata, commonly known as the speckled phalaenopsis, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendant inflorescences that bear clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The leaves are broad, glossy, and dark green, often with a waxy texture. The flowers typically range in color from white to pale pink, with distinctive speckles or spots on the petals.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
