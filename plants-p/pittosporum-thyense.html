Pittosporum thyense

Pittosporum thyense, commonly known as the silver tree, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

The silver tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens around the world.
