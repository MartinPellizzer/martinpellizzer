Piper funckii

Piper funckii, commonly known as the Funck's pepper vine, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by small, round berries. Its leaves are typically ovate with a slightly serrated edge and have a strong, peppery aroma.

Piper funckii is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
