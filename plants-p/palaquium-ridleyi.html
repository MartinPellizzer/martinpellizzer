Palaquium ridleyi

Palaquium ridleyi, commonly known as the ridley's palaquium, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its elliptical, leathery leaves that are glossy and dark green.
