Peperomia rioalbae

Peperomia rioalbae, commonly known as the Rio Alba Peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to South America.

It features dense, waxy foliage with a striking silver-green coloration, making it a visually appealing houseplant. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape and often have a slightly textured surface. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white, which are not particularly showy.

This species prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it relatively easy to care for in a home environment.
