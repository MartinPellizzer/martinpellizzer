Potentilla alterniflora

Potentilla alterniflora, commonly known as the alternateleaf cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in moist, sunny habitats such as stream banks and meadows. The plant features alternate, palmately compound leaves with five leaflets, giving it its common name. Its showy yellow flowers appear in clusters and bloom in the summer months.

Potentilla alterniflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in wetland environments.
