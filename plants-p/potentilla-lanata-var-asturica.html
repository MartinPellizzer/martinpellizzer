Potentilla lanata var. asturica

Potentilla lanata var. asturica, commonly known as the Asturian cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to harsh, sunny environments.
