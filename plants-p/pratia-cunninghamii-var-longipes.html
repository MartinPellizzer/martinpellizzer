Pratia cunninghamii var. longipes

Pratia cunninghamii var. longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed pratia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or heathlands.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
