Pilea glaberrima var. lanceolaria

Pilea glaberrima var. lanceolaria, commonly known as the Shingle Plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features dense clusters of small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces inconspicuous yellowish-green flowers that are followed by small, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

This variety is prized for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
