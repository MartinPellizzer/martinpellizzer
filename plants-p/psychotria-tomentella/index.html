Psychotria Tomentella

Psychotria tomentella is a strikingly beautiful shrub native to the tropical regions of South America. Known for its velvety leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers, it captivates both botanists and gardeners alike. This plant belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes over 13,000 species of flowering plants. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a subject of scientific interest and admiration. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticulture.
