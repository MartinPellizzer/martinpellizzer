Peliosanthes separata

Peliosanthes separata, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often a deep green color. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on short pedicels. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Peliosanthes separata is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
