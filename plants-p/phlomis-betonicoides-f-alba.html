Phlomis betonicoides f. alba

Phlomis betonicoides f. alba, commonly known as white betony, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its tall, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale blue, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
