Podocarpus macrophyllus f. spontaneus

Podocarpus macrophyllus f. spontaneus, commonly known as the Chinese podocarp or the evergreen tree, is a variant of the podocarpus species native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and a slow growth rate, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping. The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters in its natural habitat. Its leaves are leathery and have a glossy appearance, contributing to its aesthetic appeal.

This form is often found in naturalized settings and is valued for its durability and adaptability to various soil conditions.
