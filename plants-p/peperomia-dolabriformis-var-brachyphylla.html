Peperomia dolabriformis var. brachyphylla

Peperomia dolabriformis var. brachyphylla, commonly known as the short-stemmed pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, broad, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often hidden among the foliage, followed by elliptical, reddish-brown berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements contribute to its appeal in both ornamental and horticultural settings.
