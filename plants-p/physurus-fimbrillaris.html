Physurus fimbrillaris

Physurus fimbrillaris, commonly known as the slender thread-leaf fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are finely divided and resemble threads. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as rainforests or along stream banks. Its rhizomes are wiry and creeping, allowing it to spread along the forest floor.

The leaves are evergreen, providing a lush green appearance throughout the year.
