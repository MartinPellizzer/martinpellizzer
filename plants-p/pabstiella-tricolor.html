Pabstiella tricolor

Pabstiella tricolor, commonly known as the three-color orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andes mountains in South America.

It is characterized by its strikingly colorful flowers, which display three distinct hues on the same bloom. The plant typically grows in high-altitude cloud forests, where it thrives in cool, humid conditions. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the rocky substrates in its native habitat.

Pabstiella tricolor is a rare and delicate species, making it a sought-after plant among orchid enthusiasts.
