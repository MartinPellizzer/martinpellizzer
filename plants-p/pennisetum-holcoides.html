Pennisetum holcoides

Pennisetum holcoides, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and fluffy, purple-pink plumes that appear in the fall. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Pennisetum holcoides is also valued for its ability to attract birds and other wildlife.
