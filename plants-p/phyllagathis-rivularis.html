Phyllagathis rivularis

Phyllagathis rivularis, commonly known as the river phyllagathis, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as stream sides and mountainous forests. The plant features opposite, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. Its small, white flowers are clustered in inflorescences and are often borne in the axils of the leaves.

Phyllagathis rivularis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings for its aesthetic qualities.
