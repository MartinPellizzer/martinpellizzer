Pterostylis × furcillata

Pterostylis × furcillata, commonly known as the forked greenhood, is a hybrid orchid native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, hood-like labellum that is typically green with purple markings. The plant produces a single spike of flowers, each with a distinctive, narrow, and often curved lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and contributes to the biodiversity of its native habitat.
