Periploca africana

Periploca africana, commonly known as African moonseed, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and showy, yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that contain seeds covered in a sticky, resinous substance.

It is often found in moist, shaded areas and is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
