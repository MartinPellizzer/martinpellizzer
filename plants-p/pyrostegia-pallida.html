Pyrostegia pallida

Pyrostegia pallida, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking white to pale pink flowers that appear in clusters, often resembling flames. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth, glossy surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Pyrostegia pallida is also known for its rapid growth and ability to produce abundant blooms throughout the growing season.
