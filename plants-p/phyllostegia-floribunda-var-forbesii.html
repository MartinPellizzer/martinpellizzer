Phyllostegia floribunda var. forbesii

Phyllostegia floribunda var. forbesii, commonly known as the Forbes' strobilanthes, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
