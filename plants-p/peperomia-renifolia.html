Peperomia renifolia

Peperomia renifolia, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features broad, waxy, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
