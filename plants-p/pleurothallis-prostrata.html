Pleurothallis prostrata

Pleurothallis prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features delicate, narrow leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale yellow. It is part of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is known for its unique growth habit.

Due to its habitat preferences, it is often found in forest understories where it can thrive in the filtered light.
