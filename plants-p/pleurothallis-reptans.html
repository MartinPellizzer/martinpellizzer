Pleurothallis reptans

Pleurothallis reptans, commonly known as the snake orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on tree branches or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, slender stems and produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers with a subtle fragrance. Its leaves are narrow and arranged in a spiral pattern, contributing to its unique appearance.

Pleurothallis reptans is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
