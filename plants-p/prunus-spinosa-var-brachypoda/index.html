Prunus Spinosa Var. Brachypoda

Blackthorn, scientifically known as Prunus spinosa var. brachypoda, is a hardy shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is characterized by its thorny stems and small, dark berries that ripen in late summer. Despite its sharp appearance, the plant plays an important role in local ecosystems, providing food for birds and other wildlife. Its dense growth also offers shelter to various animals. However, it is often considered a weed due to its aggressive spreading habit.
