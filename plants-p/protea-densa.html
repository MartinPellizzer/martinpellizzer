Protea densa

Protea densa, commonly known as the dense protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical inflorescence composed of numerous small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows in coastal fynbos vegetation, thriving in well-drained, acidic soils. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, often covered in a fine, white pubescence.

Protea densa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
