Panicum laevinode

Panicum laevinode, commonly known as the slender panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to thrive in warm climates with well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in agricultural settings.
