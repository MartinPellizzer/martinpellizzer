Potentilla × ligurica

Potentilla × ligurica, commonly known as the shrub cinquefoil, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–30 cm and features trifoliate leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Potentilla × ligurica is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in temperate climates.
