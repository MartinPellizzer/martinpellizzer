Pimenta pimento

Pimenta pimento, commonly known as the pimento pepper, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It produces small, round fruits that range in color from red to yellow, depending on the variety and ripeness. The plant belongs to the Solanaceae family, which also includes tomatoes and potatoes. Its leaves are dark green and oval-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

The fruit is often used in cooking, particularly in stuffed peppers and as a spice.
