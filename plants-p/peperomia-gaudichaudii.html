Peperomia gaudichaudii

Peperomia gaudichaudii, commonly known as the Gaudichaud's pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, with a deep green coloration. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white, arranged in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia gaudichaudii is known for its ability to tolerate low light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it an easy plant to care for.
