Premna tahitensis var. rimatarensis

Premna tahitensis var. rimatarensis, commonly known as the Tahitian nettle, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and humid environments. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruits are small, spherical capsules that contain numerous tiny seeds.

It is often used in traditional medicine and has ecological significance in its native habitat.
