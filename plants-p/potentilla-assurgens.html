Potentilla assurgens

Potentilla assurgens, commonly known as the alpine potentilla, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Potentilla assurgens is well adapted to cold, alpine environments and is often found in rocky soils at high elevations.
