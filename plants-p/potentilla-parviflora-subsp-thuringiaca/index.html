Potentilla Parviflora Subsp. Thuringiaca

Potentilla parviflora subsp. thuringiaca is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to central Europe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Known for its small, bright yellow blooms, it adds a subtle yet striking touch to its natural habitat. This subspecies is often overlooked due to its modest appearance but holds significant ecological value. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this fragile species from habitat loss and climate change.
