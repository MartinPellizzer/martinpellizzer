Phyllanthus essequiboensis

Phyllanthus essequiboensis, commonly known as the Essequibo starleaf, is a shrub native to the Guyana Shield region.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, star-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
