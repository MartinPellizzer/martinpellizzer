Pleurothallis multirostris

Pleurothallis multirostris, commonly known as the multirostris orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
