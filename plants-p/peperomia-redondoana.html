Peperomia redondoana

Peperomia redondoana, commonly known as the Redondoana Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge on the underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tiny, clustered flowers in shades of white and green, which are borne on short, reddish peduncles. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and ornamental foliage, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in both home and office settings.
