Potentilla multifida var. glabrata

Potentilla multifida var. glabrata, commonly known as the smoothleaf potentilla, is a shrub native to parts of North America.

It features small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The leaves are finely divided and have a smooth texture, distinguishing it from other varieties. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

This plant is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
