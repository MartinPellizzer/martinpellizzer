Pennisetum leonis

Pennisetum leonis, commonly known as the lion's tail, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, arching inflorescences that resemble a lion's tail, hence its common name. The plant produces fluffy, silver-gray plumes that are highly ornamental and attract birds and insects. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for landscaping.

Pennisetum leonis is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to provide habitat for wildlife.
