Phoebe neurantha var. cavaleriei

Phoebe neurantha var. cavaleriei, commonly known as the Cavalerie's phoebe, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
