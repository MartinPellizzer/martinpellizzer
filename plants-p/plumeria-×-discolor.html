Plumeria × discolor

Plumeria × discolor, commonly known as the frangipani, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. Its flowers are typically borne in clusters at the ends of branches.

The plant is well adapted to warm climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
