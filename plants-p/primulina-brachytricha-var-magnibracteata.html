Primulina brachytricha var. magnibracteata

Primulina brachytricha var. magnibracteata, commonly known as the giant bract primulina, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, large bracts that are often more showy than the actual flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, thriving in humid, shaded environments within tropical rainforests. Its leaves are broad and glossy, and it produces small, tubular flowers in shades of white and pink.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
