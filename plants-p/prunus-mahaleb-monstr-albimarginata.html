Prunus mahaleb monstr. albimarginata

Prunus mahaleb monstr.

albimarginata, commonly known as the white-margin cherry, is a cultivar of the Prunus mahaleb species. It is characterized by its distinctive white-edged leaves, which provide a striking visual contrast against its dark green foliage.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in the spring, followed by edible cherries that are typically smaller than those of other cherry varieties. It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often used in gardens for its seasonal beauty.
