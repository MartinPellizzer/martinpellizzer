Peperomia molleri

Peperomia molleri, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a distinctive silvery or white underside, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has short, sturdy stems and produces small, inconspicuous white or pale green flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia molleri is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
