Pristimera luteoviridis var. kribiana

Pristimera luteoviridis var. kribiana, commonly known as the Krib Island African violet, is a compact, bushy plant native to Tanzania.

It features velvety, dark green leaves with a distinctive yellowish-green underside, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom throughout the year. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value lies in its foliage and continuous flowering habit.
