Philodendron sellowii

Philodendron sellowii, commonly known as the Sellow's philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and humidity.

Its ornamental foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a houseplant.
