Plectranthus saphinensis

Plectranthus saphinensis, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its showy, tubular flowers are typically pink or purple and attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in various garden conditions.
