Pterostylis rufa var. despectans

Pterostylis rufa var. despectans, commonly known as the red-stained helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It features a slender, often reddish stem with a single, elongated, helmet-shaped flower that is typically pale green to reddish in color. The plant has a short, fibrous root system and produces a single, narrow leaf. It blooms during late winter to early spring, and its flowers are pollinated by specific insect species.

This orchid is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as rainforests and understorey environments.
