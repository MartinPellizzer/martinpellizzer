Podocarpus filicifolius

Podocarpus filicifolius, commonly known as the fern-leaf podocarp, is a species of evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Podocarpaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage. The plant has scale-like leaves that are arranged in a compact, feathery pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. It is often found in humid, shaded environments such as tropical forests.

The tree produces small, cone-like structures that contain its seeds, typical of many coniferous species.
