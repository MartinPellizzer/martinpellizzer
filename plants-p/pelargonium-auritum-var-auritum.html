Pelargonium auritum var. auritum

Pelargonium auritum var. auritum, commonly known as the ear-shaped geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features vibrant pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with distinctive ear-like protrusions on the leaf edges. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
