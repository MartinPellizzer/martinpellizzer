Parnassia fimbriata var. hoodiana

Parnassia fimbriata var. hoodiana, commonly known as the Hood's fairy cup, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, cup-shaped white flowers with a distinctive yellowish-green center. The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
