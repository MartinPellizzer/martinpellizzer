Phlomis mesopotamica

Phlomis mesopotamica, commonly known as Mesopotamian blazingstar, is a perennial herb native to the Middle East and Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with hairy surfaces, and its root system is deep and fibrous. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in steppe and arid environments.

Phlomis mesopotamica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
