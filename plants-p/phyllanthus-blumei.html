Phyllanthus blumei

Phyllanthus blumei, commonly known as the Blume's phyllanthus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, including India, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered leaves and inconspicuous flowers, which are often surrounded by bracts. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
