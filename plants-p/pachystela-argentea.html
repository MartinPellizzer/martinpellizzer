Pachystela argentea

Pachystela argentea, commonly known as the silverleaf pachystela, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping.
