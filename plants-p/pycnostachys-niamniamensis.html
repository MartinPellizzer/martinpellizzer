Pycnostachys niamniamensis

Pycnostachys niamniamensis, commonly known as the African orchid, is a flowering plant native to Central and West Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its showy, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as under forest canopies. Its leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, and it produces clusters of small, colorful blooms.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
