Phyllanthus muscosus

Phyllanthus muscosus, commonly known as the rubber plant or Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with yellow or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often milky sap.

Phyllanthus muscosus is popular as a houseplant due to its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its air-purifying qualities.
