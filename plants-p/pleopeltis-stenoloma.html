Pleopeltis stenoloma

Pleopeltis stenoloma, commonly known as the narrow-leaf rock fern, is a species of epiphytic fern found in tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, dense growth habit. The plant has leathery, lance-shaped leaves that are usually arranged in a rosette pattern. Its rhizomes are short and often covered with a thick, protective layer of moss-like tissue.

It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often seen growing on tree trunks or rocky surfaces.
