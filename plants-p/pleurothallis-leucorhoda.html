Pleurothallis leucorhoda

Pleurothallis leucorhoda, commonly known as the white-lipped pleurothallis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small epiphytic orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America. The plant features slender, wiry stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive white lip.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.
