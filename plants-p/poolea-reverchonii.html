Poolea reverchonii

Poolea reverchonii, commonly known as the silver leaf poolea, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Aizoaceae and is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
