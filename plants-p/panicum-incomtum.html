Panicum incomtum

Panicum incomtum, commonly known as the slender panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Due to its adaptability, it is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
