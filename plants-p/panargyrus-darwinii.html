Panargyrus darwinii

Panargyrus darwinii, commonly known as the Darwin's panther.

It is a flowering plant native to the Andean region of South America. The plant is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves and showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun exposure.
