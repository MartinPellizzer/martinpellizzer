Pittosporum comptum

Pittosporum comptum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that appear in clusters, followed by yellowish-orange berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

Pittosporum comptum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in horticulture for its attractive foliage and flowering.
