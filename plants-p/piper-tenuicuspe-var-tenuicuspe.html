Piper tenuicuspe var. tenuicuspe

Piper tenuicuspe var. tenuicuspe, commonly known as the slender pepper plant, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into elongated, slender fruits. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit and is often found in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The fruit, though not typically consumed by humans, plays a role in local ecosystems and may be used in ornamental horticulture.
