Phillyrea medianifolia subsp. jorroi

Phillyrea medianifolia subsp.

jorroi, commonly known as the Canary Island Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands. It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically evergreen and have a glossy surface.

The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and rocky habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its native range.
