Potamogeton × nitens var. heterophyllus

Potamogeton × nitens var. heterophyllus, commonly known as the variable pondweed, is a hybrid aquatic plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its morphological variation, with different leaf shapes depending on environmental conditions. The plant typically grows in freshwater habitats such as ponds, lakes, and slow-moving streams. Its leaves are usually narrow and ribbon-like, with a smooth or serrated margin.

It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various water organisms.
