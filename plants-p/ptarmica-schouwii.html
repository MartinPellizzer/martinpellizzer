Ptarmica schouwii

Ptarmica schouwii, commonly known as the Schouw's wintergreen, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth habit. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, which bloom in early summer. Its leaves are evergreen, dark green, and leathery, adapted to withstand cold temperatures and harsh environments.

Ptarmica schouwii is often found in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in nutrient-poor conditions typical of its alpine habitat.
