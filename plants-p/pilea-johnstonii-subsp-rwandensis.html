Pilea johnstonii subsp. rwandensis

rwandensis, commonly known as the African velvetleaf, is a species of flowering plant in the family Urticaceae. It is native to eastern Africa, particularly Rwanda, and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture.
