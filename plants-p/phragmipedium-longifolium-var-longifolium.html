Phragmipedium longifolium var. longifolium

Phragmipedium longifolium var. longifolium, commonly known as the long-leaved phragmipedium, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that can reach several inches in length. The plant produces pendulous flowers with showy, spurred petals in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This orchid is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
