Pennisetum rupestre

Pennisetum rupestre, commonly known as red hot poker, is a striking ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features tall, upright clumps of sword-shaped leaves that range in color from blue-gray to silver, depending on the variety. The plant produces showy, spiky flower heads that are typically red, orange, or yellow, adding vibrant color to gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Pennisetum rupestre is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
