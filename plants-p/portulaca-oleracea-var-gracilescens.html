Portulaca oleracea var. gracilescens

Portulaca oleracea var. gracilescens, commonly known as the dwarf portulaca, is a herbaceous annual plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features prostrate or ascending stems that are typically covered in fine, glandular hairs. The plant produces small, fleshy, clustered leaves and vibrant yellow or orange flowers that bloom in the morning. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy species suitable for xeriscaping.

Its succulent nature allows it to store water, enabling it to survive in arid conditions.
