Piper confusionis

Piper confusionis, commonly known as the confusion pepper or the false pepper, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when mature, and these berries contain tiny seeds. Piper confusionis is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and unique appearance.

However, it is important to note that the plant is toxic and should not be ingested.
