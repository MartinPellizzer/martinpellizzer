Plectranthus strobilifer

Plectranthus strobilifer, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate light exposure.

Plectranthus strobilifer is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and blooms.
