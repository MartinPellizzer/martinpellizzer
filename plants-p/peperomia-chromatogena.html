Peperomia chromatogena

Peperomia chromatogena, commonly known as the variegated peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with striking white or cream variegation patterns, which add visual interest to any indoor space. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green or red, though it is primarily grown for its foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia chromatogena is relatively pest-resistant and requires minimal watering, making it a popular choice for plant enthusiasts.
