Pentaschistis tortuosa

Pentaschistis tortuosa, commonly known as the twisted leaf fern, is a unique plant belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its intricate, curling fronds that give it a distinctive, almost spiral appearance. The leaves are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, which helps the plant retain moisture in its natural habitat.
