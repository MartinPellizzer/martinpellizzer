Pelargonium condensatum

Pelargonium condensatum, commonly known as the condensed geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically produces vibrant pink or red blooms, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Pelargonium condensatum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
