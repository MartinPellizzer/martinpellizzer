Passiflora ornithoura var. ornithoura

Passiflora ornithoura var. ornithoura, commonly known as the purple passionflower, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, complex flowers that feature a blend of purple, blue, and white hues. The plant produces edible berries that are rich in antioxidants and have a tangy flavor. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to flourish.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
