Polygonum punctatum var. robustius

Polygonum punctatum var. robustius, commonly known as the robust polygonum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that are often branched and covered in fine hairs. The leaves are alternate, ovate to elliptic in shape, and have a slightly serrated margin. It produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters, typically blooming in late summer.

This plant is often found in moist soils along stream banks and in wet meadows.
