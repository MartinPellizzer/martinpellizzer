Punica granatum var. microcarpa

Punica granatum var. microcarpa, commonly known as the dwarf pomegranate, is a compact variety of the pomegranate plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, rounded leaves and produces clusters of vibrant red flowers that give way to small, sweet fruits. The plant is characterized by its short stature, making it suitable for container gardening and small spaces. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and edible, nutrient-rich fruits.
