Panicum brachythyrsum

Panicum brachythyrsum, commonly known as little bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and distinctive silver-gray inflorescence. This plant is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in prairie and woodland habitats. It plays an important role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various wildlife species.

Panicum brachythyrsum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in native plant gardens.
