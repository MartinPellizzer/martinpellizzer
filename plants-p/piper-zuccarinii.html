Piper zuccarinii

Piper zuccarinii, commonly known as the yellow horned pepper, is a tropical shrub native to the Andes region of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes numerous species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and heart-shaped leaves, which are often used in traditional medicine. Its fruit, a small, elongated berry, contains the aromatic seeds used as a spice.

The botanical features of Piper zuccarinii make it a unique and valuable species in both horticultural and medicinal contexts.
