Plectranthus tenuiflorus

Plectranthus tenuiflorus, commonly known as narrowflower plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and opposite, ovate to lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for gardens and containers.

Plectranthus tenuiflorus is often used as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and modest blooms.
