Panicum mauryi

Panicum mauryi, commonly known as Maury's panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often hairy. Its inflorescence consists of a panicle made up of small, spikelet-bearing branches.

Panicum mauryi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping or for erosion control.
