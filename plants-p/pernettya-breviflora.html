Pernettya breviflora

Pernettya breviflora, commonly known as the dwarf cowberry, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the temperate regions of the northern hemisphere.

It features small, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and have a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces tiny, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or white, which bloom in early summer. Its berries, which are edible, are small and red, appearing in late summer to autumn.

Pernettya breviflora is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky slopes.
