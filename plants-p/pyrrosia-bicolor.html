Pyrrosia bicolor

Pyrrosia bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, leathery fronds. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are bi-colored, with a dark green upper surface and a lighter green underside.
