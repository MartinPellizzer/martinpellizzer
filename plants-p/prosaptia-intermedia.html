Prosaptia intermedia

Prosaptia intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes and wet meadows. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in the summer. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

Prosaptia intermedia plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms.
