Polemonium grande

Polemonium grande, commonly known as the tall bluebell, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along its stems. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Its showy flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
