Prangos fedtschenkoi

Prangos fedtschenkoi, commonly known as the Kyzylkum sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss in its harsh environment. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in sandy or gravelly habitats.

Prangos fedtschenkoi is a hardy species that demonstrates resilience to extreme temperatures and low precipitation.
