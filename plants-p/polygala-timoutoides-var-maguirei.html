Polygala timoutoides var. maguirei

Polygala timoutoides var. maguirei, commonly known as the Maguire's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often pink or white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, helping to reduce water loss, while its stems are often sprawling or ascending.
