Parodia aureicentra var. rauschii

Parodia aureicentra var. rauschii, commonly known as the Rauschii Pincushion Cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a dense, spiny crown with a yellowish-green body that often develops a reddish hue when exposed to full sun. The plant produces pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking contrast to its foliage. It is well-suited for rock gardens or containers due to its slow growth and drought tolerance.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
