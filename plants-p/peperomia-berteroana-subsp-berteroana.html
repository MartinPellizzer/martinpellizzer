Peperomia berteroana subsp. berteroana

Peperomia berteroana subsp.

berteroana, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, low-growing perennial plant native to South America. It features thick, waxy, and often velvety leaves that are typically green with a subtle metallic sheen.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color, which are not the primary ornamental feature. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique leaf texture and ease of care contribute to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
