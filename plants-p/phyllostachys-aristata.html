Phyllostachys aristata

Phyllostachys aristata, commonly known as the Fargesia rattan, is a species of bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright culms that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. The plant produces small, clustered panicles of greenish-white flowers in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth, waxy texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its graceful appearance.
