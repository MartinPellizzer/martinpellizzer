Patrinia longifolia

Patrinia longifolia, commonly known as Chinese violet or blue violet, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface and produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its flowers are typically clustered in dense inflorescences, and the plant is known for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.

Patrinia longifolia is also cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and blooms.
