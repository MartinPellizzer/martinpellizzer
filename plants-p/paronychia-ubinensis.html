Paronychia ubinensis

Paronychia ubinensis, commonly known as the Ubinensis leafcress, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cress species. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often hairy, which helps in reducing water loss in its arid habitat.

Paronychia ubinensis is often found growing in rocky soils and is adapted to thrive in high-altitude environments.
