Polygala vulgaris f. multicaulis

Polygala vulgaris f. multicaulis, commonly known as the many-stemmed milkvetch, is a variant of the common milkvetch.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polygalaceae, characterized by its slender, often branched stems. The plant typically produces small, yellowish or greenish flowers arranged in clusters. It is native to Europe and is often found in grassy areas and along roadsides.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
