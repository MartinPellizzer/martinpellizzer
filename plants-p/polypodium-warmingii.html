Polypodium warmingii

Polypodium warmingii, commonly known as the Chinese shield fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its rhizome is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, making them distinctive in appearance.
