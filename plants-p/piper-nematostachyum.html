Piper nematostachyum

Piper nematostachyum, commonly known as the slender pipevine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes numerous species of flowering plants. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in spikes. Its fruits are fleshy berries that contain seeds, and the plant is often used in traditional medicine.

The botanical characteristics of Piper nematostachyum make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and pharmacological study.
