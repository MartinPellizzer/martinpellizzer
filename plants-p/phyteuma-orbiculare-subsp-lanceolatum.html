Phyteuma orbiculare subsp. lanceolatum

Phyteuma orbiculare subsp.

lanceolatum, commonly known as the lanceolate phyteuma, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

This subspecies is often used in garden designs for its early summer blooming period and compact growth habit.
