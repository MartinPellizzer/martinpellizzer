Phreatia parvula

Phreatia parvula, commonly known as the small phreatia orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces pale green to yellowish flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Phreatia parvula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
