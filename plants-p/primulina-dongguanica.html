Primulina dongguanica

Primulina dongguanica, commonly known as the Guangdong primula, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest undergrowth, and prefers well-drained soil. Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
