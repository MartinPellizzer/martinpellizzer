Pleiogyne australis

Pleiogyne australis, commonly known as the Australian flame tree, is a tropical tree native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, flame-like inflorescences that bloom in bright red or orange hues. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It produces small, fleshy fruits that contain seeds enclosed in a gelatinous pulp.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to coastal and subtropical environments.
