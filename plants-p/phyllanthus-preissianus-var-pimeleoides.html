Phyllanthus preissianus var. pimeleoides

Phyllanthus preissianus var. pimeleoides, commonly known as the African spurge, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, pale green leaves with a distinctive waxy coating, and it produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in arid and semi-arid conditions, making it a hardy species. It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The varietal form, pimeleoides, is characterized by its unique leaf morphology and growth habit.
