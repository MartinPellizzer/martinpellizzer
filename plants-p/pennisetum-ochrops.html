Pennisetum ochrops

Pennisetum ochrops, commonly known as purpletop grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and striking purple to pink inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats, such as marshes and swamps. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color.

Pennisetum ochrops is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in water gardens and wetland restoration projects.
