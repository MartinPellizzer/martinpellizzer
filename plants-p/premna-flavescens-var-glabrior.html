Premna flavescens var. glabrior

Premna flavescens var. glabrior, commonly known as the smooth-flowered premna, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are often borne on short pedicels. Its leaves are usually opposite and have a slightly waxy texture.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
