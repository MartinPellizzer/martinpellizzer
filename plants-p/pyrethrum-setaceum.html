Pyrethrum setaceum

Pyrethrum setaceum, commonly known as the slender pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its delicate, daisy-like flowers. The plant produces small, yellowish to white flowers that are typically clustered in a flat-topped inflorescence. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Pyrethrum setaceum is cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for its mild insecticidal properties.
