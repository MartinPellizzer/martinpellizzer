Peperomia williamsii

Peperomia williamsii, commonly known as the Shiny Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves that are often adorned with silver or white veins, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia williamsii is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and moderate humidity to flourish.
