Pavonia procumbens

Pavonia procumbens, commonly known as the prostrate pavonia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of less than 30 centimeters. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often covered in fine hairs.

Pavonia procumbens is often found in moist, sandy soils along stream banks and in open woodlands.
