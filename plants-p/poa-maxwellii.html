Poa maxwellii

Poa maxwellii, commonly known as Maxwell's bentgrass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to southern Australia.

It forms dense, mat-like growth with slender, dark green blades that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences containing numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as coastal dunes and rocky outcrops.

Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
