Pittosporum perryanum

Pittosporum perryanum, commonly known as the New Zealand pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a glossy appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

Pittosporum perryanum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
