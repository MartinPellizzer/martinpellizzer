Prospero autumnale

Prospero autumnale, commonly known as the autumn beauty plant, is a deciduous shrub native to the temperate regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow and orange foliage that transforms in the fall, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and features small, clustered flowers in the spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

Prospero autumnale thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden landscapes in cooler climates.
