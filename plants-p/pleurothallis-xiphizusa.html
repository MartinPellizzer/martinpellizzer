Pleurothallis xiphizusa

Pleurothallis xiphizusa, commonly known as the sword orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, lance-shaped flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of greenish-white or pale yellow blooms, which are often adorned with subtle markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, loose soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique and elegant appearance.
