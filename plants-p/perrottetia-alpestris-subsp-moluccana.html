Perrottetia alpestris subsp. moluccana

Perrottetia alpestris subsp.

moluccana, commonly known as the Moluccan perrottetia, is a flowering plant native to the Moluccas in Indonesia. It is a shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually pink or white, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
