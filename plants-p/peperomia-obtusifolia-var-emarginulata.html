Peperomia obtusifolia var. emarginulata

Peperomia obtusifolia var. emarginulata, commonly known as the notched-leaf peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, oval-shaped leaves with a distinctive notched or serrated edge, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its slow growth and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for tabletop displays and decorative plantings.
