Peperomia sciaphila

Peperomia sciaphila, commonly known as the shade peperomia, is a compact, low-growing perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically oval or elliptical in shape, often with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pale green flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in low-light conditions, making it an ideal choice for indoor environments.

Peperomia sciaphila is valued for its attractive foliage and ease of care, requiring minimal watering and well-draining soil.
