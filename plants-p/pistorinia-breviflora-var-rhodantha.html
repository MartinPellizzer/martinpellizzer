Pistorinia breviflora var. rhodantha

Pistorinia breviflora var. rhodantha, commonly known as the pink-flowered pistorinia, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking pink to lavender-colored flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are densely arranged along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its prolonged blooming period and drought tolerance.
