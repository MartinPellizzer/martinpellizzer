Pomaderris lanigera

Pomaderris lanigera, commonly known as the silky pomaderris, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a soft, silky appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically flowering from autumn to spring. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive look.

Pomaderris lanigera is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
