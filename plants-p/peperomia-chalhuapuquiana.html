Peperomia chalhuapuquiana

Peperomia chalhuapuquiana, commonly known as the Huastec peperomia, is a rare and endemic species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features small, ovate leaves that are usually dark green with a glossy texture. It produces tiny, spadix-like inflorescences, typical of the genus Peperomia.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
