Piper elliptico-oblongifolium

Piper elliptico-oblongifolium, commonly known as the elliptic-ovate pepper plant, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a climbing shrub belonging to the Piperaceae family, characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces tiny, peppery fruits that are often used in traditional medicine and local cuisines. Its botanical features include smooth, dark green foliage and a distinct aromatic scent.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
