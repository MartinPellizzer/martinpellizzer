Pennisetum scaettae

Pennisetum scaettae, commonly known as the Scetta grass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green to bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like flower clusters that are usually brown or purplish in hue. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Pennisetum scaettae is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to add texture to garden designs.
