Plectranthus globosus

Plectranthus globosus, commonly known as the globe thyme, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb with aromatic, gray-green leaves that are often used in culinary and medicinal applications. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or violet in color, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a versatile plant for both decorative and functional use in gardens.
