Polygala restiacea

Polygala restiacea, commonly known as the mountain polygala, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in mountainous regions. The plant features slender stems and small, intricate flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it blooms during the cooler months of the year.

Polygala restiacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
