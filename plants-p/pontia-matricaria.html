Pontia matricaria

Pontia matricaria, commonly known as the common tansy, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in sunny, well-drained soils. The plant features dark green, lobed leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It is often found in meadows, wastelands, and along roadsides.

Despite its ornamental appearance, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
