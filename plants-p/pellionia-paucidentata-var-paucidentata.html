Pellionia paucidentata var. paucidentata

Pellionia paucidentata var. paucidentata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed pellionia, is a low-growing, herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features slender, trailing stems that produce small, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly serrated margin. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale green flowers, which are typically borne on short pedicels. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its foliage is characterized by a glossy surface and a distinctive, narrow leaf shape.
