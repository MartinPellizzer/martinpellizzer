Philodendron tenue

Philodendron tenue, commonly known as the slender philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are typically smaller and more elongated compared to other philodendron species. The plant has a slender stem that allows it to climb and trail, making it suitable for hanging baskets or shelves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and humidity.

Its foliage is often variegated, adding visual interest to indoor spaces.
