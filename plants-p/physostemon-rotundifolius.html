Physostemon rotundifolius

Physostemon rotundifolius, commonly known as the round-leaf physostemon, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are smooth and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and purple, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
