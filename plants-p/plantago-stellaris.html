Plantago stellaris

Plantago stellaris, commonly known as the common plantain, is a perennial herb native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It features rosettes of broad, oval, waxy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, pale green to yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. It is a hardy plant that thrives in a variety of soil types and is often found in disturbed areas.

Botanically, it is characterized by its basal leaf rosette and its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
