Panicum menyharthii

Panicum menyharthii, commonly known as the red panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and dense, spike-like inflorescences that often display a striking red or purple hue. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a coarse texture that adds visual interest to gardens.

Panicum menyharthii is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.
