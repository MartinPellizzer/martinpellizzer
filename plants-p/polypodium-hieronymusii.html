Polypodium hieronymusii

Polypodium hieronymusii, commonly known as the Hieronymus' shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen fronds that form a compact, mat-like growth habit. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are wiry and fibrous, allowing it to spread and colonize new areas effectively.

The fronds are bioluminescent, a unique trait that aids in attracting pollinators and deterring herbivores.
