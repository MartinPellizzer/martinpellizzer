Pleioblastus yamakitensis

Pleioblastus yamakitensis, commonly known as the Yamakita bamboo, is a species native to Japan.

It is a clumping bamboo that forms dense, compact colonies, often reaching heights of up to 3 meters. The plant has slender, gracefully arching culms that are typically green, with a smooth, glabrous surface. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy.

This bamboo is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a landscape plant.
