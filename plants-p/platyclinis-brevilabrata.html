Platyclinis brevilabrata

Platyclinis brevilabrata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved platyclinis, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for gardens in warm climates.

Due to its compact size and aesthetic appeal, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in horticultural settings.
