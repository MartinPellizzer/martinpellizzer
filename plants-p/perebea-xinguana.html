Perebea xinguana

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant is notable for its latex-producing properties, which have been traditionally used by indigenous communities. Its ecological role includes supporting a wide range of flora and fauna within its native habitat.
