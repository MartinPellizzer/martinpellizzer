Prunus laurocerasus f. benardii

Prunus laurocerasus f. benardii, commonly known as the Benardii laurel, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a slightly glossy texture, and its small, fragrant white flowers appear in spring. The plant produces small, black berries that are edible but not particularly palatable. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

This cultivar is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and aesthetic qualities.
