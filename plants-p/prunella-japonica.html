Prunella japonica

Prunella japonica, commonly known as Japanese selfheal, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Prunella japonica is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
