Peperomia resediflora

Peperomia resediflora, commonly known as the Reseda-leaved Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape, with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pink, typically arranged in clusters at the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia resediflora is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and occasional pruning to maintain its shape.
