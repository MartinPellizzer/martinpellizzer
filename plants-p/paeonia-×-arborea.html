Paeonia × arborea

Paeonia × arborea, commonly known as the tree peony, is a hybrid species native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and can reach heights of up to 6 meters, making it a striking ornamental tree. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, white, and yellow, often with a fragrant aroma. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three heart-shaped leaflets, and it has a deep taproot system.

Paeonia × arborea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is commonly cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
