Philodendron fibrosum

Philodendron fibrosum, commonly known as the fibrous philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often variegated with light green and white patterns. The plant has a fibrous stem that allows it to climb and trail along supports. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Its lush foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and hanging baskets.
