Phillyrea laevis

Phillyrea laevis, commonly known as the smooth Phillyrea, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, leathery leaves that are typically gray-green in color, with a smooth texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. Phillyrea laevis is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and woodland habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting local wildlife.
