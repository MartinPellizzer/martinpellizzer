Polymnia parviceps

Polymnia parviceps, commonly known as the small-headed polymnia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Polygonaceae, which includes plants commonly known as buckwheats. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, triangular leaves with a distinctive reddish hue. Its flowers are tiny, clustered in dense inflorescences, and typically bloom in late spring to early summer.

Polymnia parviceps is often found in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and woodland edges.
