Pothos leptospadix

Pothos leptospadix, commonly known as the golden pothos, is a vigorous climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a slightly variegated pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers, though it is more valued for its ornamental foliage than its blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and is highly tolerant of low light conditions.

Pothos leptospadix is also known for its ability to purify indoor air, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
