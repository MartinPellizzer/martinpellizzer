Plectranthus shikokianus var. intermedius

Plectranthus shikokianus var. intermedius, commonly known as the Shikoku plectranthus, is a species native to Japan.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its aromatic, fuzzy leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or blue in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.
