Pultenaea rubiifolia

Pultenaea rubiifolia, commonly known as the red-flowered pultenaea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright red, pea-like flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland and heathland habitats.

Pultenaea rubiifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
