Peperomia brevispica

Peperomia brevispica, commonly known as the short-haired peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Central and South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink. The plant is characterized by its short, sturdy stems and dense foliage, making it an ideal choice for indoor cultivation. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia brevispica is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for beginners in the world of houseplants.
