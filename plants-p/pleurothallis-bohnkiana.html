Pleurothallis bohnkiana

Pleurothallis bohnkiana, commonly known as the Bohnkiana orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments within the rainforest canopy. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers that are typically arranged in a raceme, with a subtle fragrance. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding organic matter.

Pleurothallis bohnkiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts of rare orchids.
