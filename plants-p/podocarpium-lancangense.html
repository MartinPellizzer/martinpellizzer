Podocarpium lancangense

Podocarpium lancangense, commonly known as the Lancang Podocarp, is a species of flowering plant in the family Podocarpaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in China, Myanmar, and Vietnam. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage and conical shape, with leaves that are typically narrow and leathery. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.

Due to its ecological significance and limited distribution, it is considered a valuable species for conservation efforts.
