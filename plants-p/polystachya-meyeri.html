Polystachya meyeri

Polystachya meyeri, commonly known as the Meyer's bamboo orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

Polystachya meyeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
