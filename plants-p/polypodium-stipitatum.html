Polypodium stipitatum

Polypodium stipitatum, commonly known as the slender wall fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, often pale green fronds. The plant typically grows on rocks or tree trunks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its rhizomes are short and often covered in a layer of moss-like tissue.

The fronds are pinnately compound, with small, ovate pinnae that are often clustered at the base.
