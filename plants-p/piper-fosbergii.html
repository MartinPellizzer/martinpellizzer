Piper fosbergii

Piper fosbergii, commonly known as the Fosberg's pepper vine, is a climbing shrub native to the rainforests of New Guinea.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by berries that turn red when mature. Its leaves are typically ovate with a smooth or slightly serrated margin.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understorey of dense tropical forests.
