Peperomia obtusifolia var. oblongifolia

Peperomia obtusifolia var. oblongifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance houseplant native to tropical regions.

It features glossy, ovate to elliptical leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually white or pale green. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique leaf shape and ease of care make it a popular choice for beginners and experienced plant enthusiasts alike.
