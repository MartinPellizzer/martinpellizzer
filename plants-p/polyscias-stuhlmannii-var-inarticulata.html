Polyscias stuhlmannii var. inarticulata

Polyscias stuhlmannii var. inarticulata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf holly, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellowish-green flowers followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its foliage is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in horticulture for its dense, evergreen growth.
