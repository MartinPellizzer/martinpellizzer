Primulina yandongensis

Primulina yandongensis, commonly known as the Yandong primula, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant floral display. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves and produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions with high humidity.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
