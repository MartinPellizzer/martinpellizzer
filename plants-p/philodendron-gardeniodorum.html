Philodendron gardeniodorum

Philodendron gardeniodorum, commonly known as the Garden Island Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to the Caribbean.

It features heart-shaped, glossy green leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by round, blue-black berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
