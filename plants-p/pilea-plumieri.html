Pilea plumieri

Pilea plumieri, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to China.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically light green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by round, edible fruit resembling small lanterns. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with consistent moisture.

Pilea plumieri is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
