Persicaria quarrei

Persicaria quarrei, commonly known as the narrowleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist or wet habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along stream banks. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink or red, blooming in summer.

Persicaria quarrei is characterized by its upright growth habit and is often found in wetland ecosystems.
