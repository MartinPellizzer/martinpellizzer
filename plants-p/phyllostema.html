Phyllostema

Phyllostema, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver or gray-green foliage, which is covered in a fine layer of wax. The leaves are typically opposite and ovate, with a slightly serrated edge. Phyllostema species are native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

They are often cultivated as ornamental plants due to their attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
