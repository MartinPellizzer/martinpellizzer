Plumbago pendula

Plumbago pendula, commonly known as blue plumbago, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces clusters of bright blue or violet flowers throughout the summer months. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes primarily from its striking floral display.
