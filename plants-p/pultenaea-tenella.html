Pultenaea tenella

Pultenaea tenella, commonly known as the slender pultenaea, is a low-growing shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in dense spikes during the spring and summer months. It is typically found in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils.

Pultenaea tenella is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native gardens.
