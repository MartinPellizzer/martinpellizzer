Panicum compositoproximum

Panicum compositoproximum, commonly known as the composite proximal panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, blade-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based flowers that are typically arranged in panicles, contributing to its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
