Polygala recognita

Polygala recognita, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to regions of China and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, often pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are usually alternate and pinnately compound, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Polygala recognita is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
