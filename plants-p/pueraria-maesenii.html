Pueraria maesenii

Pueraria maesenii, commonly known as the Japanese kudzufuji, is a vigorous climbing vine native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to form dense foliage, often used in ornamental gardening. Its roots contain compounds that have been studied for potential medicinal properties.

However, it can become invasive if not properly managed.
