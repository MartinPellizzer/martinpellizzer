Pagamea capitata f. breviloba

Pagamea capitata f. breviloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pagamea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered yellow flowers. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.
