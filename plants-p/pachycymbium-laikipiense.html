Pachycymbium laikipiense

Pachycymbium laikipiense, commonly known as the Laikipia cymbium, is a rare and unique plant species native to Kenya.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, an adaptation to its arid and semi-arid habitat. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It is often found growing in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in regions with low rainfall.
