Polystachya piersii

Polystachya piersii, commonly known as the Piers' sword fern, is a member of the Polystachyaceae family.

It is a terrestrial fern characterized by its erect, wiry stems and pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces spore-bearing clusters called sori, which are typically located on the underside of the fronds. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
