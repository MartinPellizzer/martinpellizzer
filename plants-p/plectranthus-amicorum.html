Plectranthus amicorum

Plectranthus amicorum, commonly known as the friendship plant or African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes other aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a fuzzy texture. It produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from violet to white, typically blooming in spring and summer.

Plectranthus amicorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens.
