Portulaca aurea

Portulaca aurea, commonly known as moss rose, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a low-growing annual herb with fleshy, succulent stems and leaves. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom throughout the summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ability to tolerate drought conditions makes it a hardy and easy-to-care-for ornamental plant.
