Prunus Cerasus Monstr. Semiplena

Prunus cerasus monstr. semiplena is a rare and captivating variant of the cherry tree known for its unique floral display. This cultivar features a semi-double bloom with petals that cascade in a dramatic, layered fashion. Its striking appearance makes it a prized addition to gardens and botanical collections. The plant thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure, adding to its allure among horticultural enthusiasts.
