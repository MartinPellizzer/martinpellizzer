Pilosella antennarioidiformis

Pilosella antennarioidiformis, commonly known as the narrowleaf pearly everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are narrow and covered with fine, silky hairs. Pilosella antennarioidiformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
