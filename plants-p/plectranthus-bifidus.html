Plectranthus bifidus

Plectranthus bifidus, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of purple and violet. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Plectranthus bifidus is also valued for its ability to produce fragrant essential oils.
