Parasenecio Hastatus Subsp. Tanakae

Parasenecio hastatus subsp. tanakae is a rare and fascinating plant native to the mountainous regions of Japan. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, known for its diverse and ecologically important members. This subspecies is characterized by its unique leaf shape and striking floral structure. Due to its limited habitat and conservation status, it is considered a vulnerable species. Studying this plant offers insights into adaptive strategies and biodiversity in high-altitude ecosystems.
