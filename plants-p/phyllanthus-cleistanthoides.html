Phyllanthus cleistanthoides

Phyllanthus cleistanthoides, commonly known as the small-leaved phyllanthus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 3 meters. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its small, white to yellowish flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces small, spherical fruits.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
