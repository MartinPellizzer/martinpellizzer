Primula macropoda

Primula macropoda, commonly known as the large-flowered primrose, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, typically blooming in spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
