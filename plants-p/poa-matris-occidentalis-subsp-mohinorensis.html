Poa matris-occidentalis subsp. mohinorensis

Poa matris-occidentalis subsp.

mohinorensis, commonly known as the Mohinorensis grass, is a perennial grass species native to specific regions of South America. It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and fine, narrow leaves.

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in grasslands and open areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
