Pelargonium praemorsum subsp. speciosum

Pelargonium praemorsum subsp.

speciosum, commonly known as the Cape marigold, is a species native to South Africa. It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange.

The plant features hairy, ovate leaves with a distinctive serrated margin. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, typically from spring to autumn.
