Panicum radicosum

Panicum radicosum, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and bright red or purple inflorescences. The plant has fibrous roots that help stabilize soil, making it useful for erosion control. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color.

Panicum radicosum is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in full sun conditions.
