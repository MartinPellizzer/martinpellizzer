Pentas hindsioides var. parensis

Pentas hindsioides var. parensis, commonly known as the Purple Pentas, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptical with a smooth margin.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
