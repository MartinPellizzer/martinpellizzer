Petrocoptis Hispanica

Petrocoptis hispanica is a rare and striking plant native to southern Europe. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Known for its unique appearance, it features delicate, spurred flowers that bloom in spring. This species is often found in limestone areas, making it a symbol of mountainous landscapes. Due to its limited habitat, it is considered vulnerable and requires careful conservation efforts.
