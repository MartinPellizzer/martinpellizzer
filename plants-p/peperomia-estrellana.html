Peperomia estrellana

Peperomia estrellana, commonly known as the Starry Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It features small, thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and silver, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, which bloom in spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a desirable addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
