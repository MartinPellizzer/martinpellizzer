Pterostylis nutans

Pterostylis nutans, commonly known as the nodding greenhood, is an orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a slender, leafless stem that produces a single, hooded flower. The flower has a greenish hue with a distinctive hood-like structure that shelters the reproductive organs. It typically blooms in spring and is found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and heathlands.

The plant is characterized by its unique morphology and is part of the Orchidaceae family.
