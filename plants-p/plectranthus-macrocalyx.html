Plectranthus macrocalyx

Plectranthus macrocalyx, commonly known as the purple heart plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and clusters of violet or purple tubular flowers. The plant belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and indoor cultivation.
