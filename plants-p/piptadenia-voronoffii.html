Piptadenia voronoffii

Piptadenia voronoffii, commonly known as the African balsa or wild balsa, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is notable for its lightweight wood, which is used in various traditional applications. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.
