Pinalia ebulbis

Pinalia ebulbis, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its vibrant orange and yellow blooms.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is native to the humid regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its colorful flowers. Pinalia ebulbis thrives in warm, moist environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and botanical collections.
