Pancratium macquaria

Pancratium macquaria, commonly known as the Macquarie lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces striking white or pale yellow flowers with a strong fragrance. The plant features strap-like leaves and a tall, slender stem that supports its showy blooms. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or sandy environments.

Pancratium macquaria is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
