Pteroceras simondianum

Pteroceras simondianum, commonly known as the African orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its showy, colorful flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny environments. Its unique floral structure includes three sepals and three petals, with one of the petals modified into a lip.

Pteroceras simondianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
