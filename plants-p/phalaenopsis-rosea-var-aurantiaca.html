Phalaenopsis rosea var. aurantiaca

Phalaenopsis rosea var. aurantiaca, commonly known as the orange phalaenopsis orchid, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green with a waxy texture, typical of the Phalaenopsis genus. The flowers are showy, with petals and sepals that range from pale yellow to deep orange, often with a contrasting lip. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent humidity and well-draining potting media.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of care.
