Pittosporum insigne f. pertinax

Pittosporum insigne f. pertinax, commonly known as the golden pittosporum, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to New Zealand.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a slight waxy texture. The plant produces fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by bright orange berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage, floral display, and seasonal fruiting.
