Pinus Densiflora F. Umbraculifera

Pinus densiflora f. umbraculifera is a unique variant of the Japanese black pine known for its distinctive dense foliage. This form exhibits a compact, umbrella-like shape that sets it apart from its typical upright growth. It thrives in shaded environments, making it an ideal choice for woodland gardens. The plant's ornamental value lies in its lush green canopy and slow growth rate. Its adaptability and visual appeal make it a sought-after specimen in horticulture.
