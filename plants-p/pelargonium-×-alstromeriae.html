Pelargonium × alstromeriae

Pelargonium × alstromeriae, commonly known as the hybrid geranium, is a flowering plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Pelargonium hortorum and Pelargonium × inodorum, resulting in a variety with diverse foliage and flower colors. The plant typically features rounded leaves with serrated edges and produces showy blooms in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often used in gardens for its ornamental value.

Pelargonium × alstromeriae is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced gardeners.
