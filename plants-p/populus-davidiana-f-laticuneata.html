Populus davidiana f. laticuneata

Populus davidiana f. laticuneata, commonly known as the broad-leaf poplar, is a unique variant of the Populus davidiana species.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are often slightly serrated along the edges. This form exhibits a more open growth habit compared to the typical narrow-leaf form, making it visually distinct. The plant is native to regions of China and is known for its rapid growth and adaptability to various soil conditions.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological restoration and ornamental gardening.
