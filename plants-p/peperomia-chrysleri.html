Peperomia chrysleri

Peperomia chrysleri, commonly known as the golden peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features vibrant, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of yellow and green, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for tabletop displays and hanging baskets.
