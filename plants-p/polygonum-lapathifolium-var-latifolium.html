Polygonum lapathifolium var. latifolium

Polygonum lapathifolium var. latifolium, commonly known as broadleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It features upright, slender stems that often branch near the top, bearing clusters of small, pinkish to reddish flowers. The leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and have a slightly rough texture, with a waxy coating that helps reduce water loss. It thrives in moist, sunny environments such as wet meadows and streambanks.

The plant is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a common weed in agricultural and disturbed areas.
