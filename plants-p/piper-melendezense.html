Piper melendezense

Piper melendezense, commonly known as Melendez's pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing vines and small, heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, slender fruits.

Piper melendezense is valued for its potential medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
