Pithecellobium nipense

Pithecellobium nipense, commonly known as the velvet bean tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces elongated, bean-like pods that contain edible seeds, which are rich in protein and used in various cuisines.

Its roots have also been traditionally used in herbal medicine for their purported health benefits.
