Peperomia martagonifolia var. contempta

Peperomia martagonifolia var. contempta, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features velvety, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a subtle metallic sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
