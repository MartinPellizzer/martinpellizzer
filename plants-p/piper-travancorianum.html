Piper travancorianum

Piper travancorianum, commonly known as Travancore pepper, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing vines and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its fruits are berries that mature to a dark purple color, containing aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
