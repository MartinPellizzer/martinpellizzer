Pleione testacea

Pleione testacea, commonly known as the golden pleione, is a species of orchid native to the Himalayas and Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments. The plant features a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients, supporting its growth in less hospitable conditions. Its flowers are showy, with yellow petals and a distinctive orange or red lip.

Pleione testacea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
