Paeonia handel-mazzettii

Paeonia handel-mazzettii, commonly known as the Chinese tree peony, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and its ability to develop a tree-like form over time. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white during the spring. Its leaves are compound, with serrated edges, and it has a deep root system that helps it thrive in well-drained soils.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
