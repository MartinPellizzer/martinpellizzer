Planchonella petitiana

Planchonella petitiana, commonly known as the Tanna plum, is a small evergreen tree native to the island of Tanna in Vanuatu.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and elliptical, dark green leaves. The species is of interest for its potential medicinal properties and ecological role in its native habitat.
