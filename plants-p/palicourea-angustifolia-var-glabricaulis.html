Palicourea angustifolia var. glabricaulis

Palicourea angustifolia var. glabricaulis, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed palicourea, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a climbing shrub characterized by its slender, glabrous stems and small, elliptical leaves. The plant produces clusters of white to pale yellow flowers, which are typically followed by small, berry-like fruits. It is often found in humid lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental qualities.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
