Plectronia hispida var. glabrescens

Plectronia hispida var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth plectronia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of South America.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is not widely known.
