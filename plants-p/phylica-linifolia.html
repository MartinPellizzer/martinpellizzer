Phylica linifolia

Phylica linifolia, commonly known as the silver bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Phylica linifolia is also valued for its hardiness and ability to tolerate drought conditions.
