Perilla ocymoides f. purpurea

Perilla ocymoides f. purpurea, commonly known as purple perilla, is a variant of the perilla plant characterized by its striking purple foliage.

This plant belongs to the Lamiaceae family and is native to East Asia, particularly China. Its leaves are often used in traditional cuisines for their aromatic and slightly spicy flavor. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter.

It thrives in temperate climates and is valued both for its ornamental appeal and culinary applications.
