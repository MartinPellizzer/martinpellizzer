Pleurothallis tubulosa

Pleurothallis tubulosa, commonly known as the tubular orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, often epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color, with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, loose soil for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
