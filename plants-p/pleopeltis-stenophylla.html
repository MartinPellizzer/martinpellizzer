Pleopeltis stenophylla

Pleopeltis stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf rock fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or rocky surfaces, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a leathery texture that helps it retain moisture.
