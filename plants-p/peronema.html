Peronema

Peronema, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Solanaceae.

It consists of several species, most notably Peronema canescens, which is native to eastern Asia. These plants are characterized by their showy, bell-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. The leaves are usually opposite and ovate, with a hairy texture.

Peronema species are often cultivated for their ornamental value in gardens and are also used in traditional medicine.
