Piper holstii

Piper holstii, commonly known as the Holst's pepper or the white pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly in countries like Mexico, Guatemala, and Panama. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit, a small, three-lobed berry, contains the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.

Piper holstii is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its medicinal properties.
