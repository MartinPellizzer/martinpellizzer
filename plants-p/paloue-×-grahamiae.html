Paloue × grahamiae

Paloue × grahamiae, commonly known as the hybrid paloue, is a rare and intriguing plant resulting from the cross between two species of the Paloue genus.

It is characterized by its unique combination of traits from its parent plants, including striking foliage and floral structures. The plant typically exhibits a compact growth habit with dense, leathery leaves that display a range of colors from green to reddish hues. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright light conditions.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts in controlled environments.
