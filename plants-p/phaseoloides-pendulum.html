Phaseoloides pendulum

Phaseoloides pendulum, commonly known as the weeping bean plant, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions.

It features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant bears pods that contain several oval seeds, which are typically used in traditional medicine. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value comes from its cascading foliage and delicate floral display.
