Panicum walteri var. molle

Panicum walteri var. molle, commonly known as velvet grass, is a flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, soft, and hairy foliage that gives it a velvety texture. Native to the southeastern United States, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often reddish or purplish in color.

It is often used in ornamental landscaping for its attractive appearance and ability to tolerate a variety of growing conditions.
