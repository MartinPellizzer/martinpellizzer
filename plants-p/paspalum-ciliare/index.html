Paspalum Ciliare

Paspalum ciliare is a versatile grass species known for its adaptability to various environmental conditions. It thrives in coastal areas and is often found in sandy soils along the shores of Florida and the Gulf Coast. This plant is valued for its ability to stabilize dunes and prevent erosion. Its delicate, hair-like leaves give it a unique appearance that distinguishes it from other grasses. Despite its modest size, Paspalum ciliare plays an important ecological role in its native habitats.
