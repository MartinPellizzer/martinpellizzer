Panicum densepilosum

Panicum densepilosum, commonly known as densehairy panicgrass, is a species of grass native to certain regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine, short hairs, giving it a distinctive texture.

Panicum densepilosum is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and withstand drought conditions.
