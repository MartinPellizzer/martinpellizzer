Philodendron simsii

Philodendron simsii, commonly known as the heart-leaf philodendron, is a tropical houseplant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slight variegation. The plant has a climbing habit and can be trained to grow vertically or allowed to trail from hanging baskets. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers warm, humid environments.

Philodendron simsii is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
