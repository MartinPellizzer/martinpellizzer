Piper crenulatum

Piper crenulatum, commonly known as the zigzag pepper or zigzag sylph, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many pepper species. The plant is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems and heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by bright red berries.

Piper crenulatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
