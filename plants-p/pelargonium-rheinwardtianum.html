Pelargonium rheinwardtianum

Pelargonium rheinwardtianum, commonly known as the Rhine Valley geranium, is a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces oval, glossy leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
