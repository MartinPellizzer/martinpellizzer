Piper fischerianum

Piper fischerianum, commonly known as Fischer's pepper.

It is a tropical climbing vine belonging to the Piperaceae family, native to Southeast Asia. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by elongated, berry-like fruits that turn red when mature. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a glossy surface.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
