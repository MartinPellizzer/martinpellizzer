Pultenaea lanata

Pultenaea lanata, commonly known as the woolly bush pea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy regions.

Pultenaea lanata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
