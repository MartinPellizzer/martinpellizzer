Pelargonium heterophyllum

Pelargonium heterophyllum, commonly known as the Cape marigold, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a trailing growth habit and produces small, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets and containers.

Pelargonium heterophyllum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
