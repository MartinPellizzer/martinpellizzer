Phragmipedium monstruosum

Phragmipedium monstruosum, commonly known as the giant phragmipedium, is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Colombia and Ecuador.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 inches in diameter, featuring a vibrant mix of yellow, orange, and red hues. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and leathery, adapted to capture moisture from the humid air.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
