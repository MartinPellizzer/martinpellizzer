Pilea robusta

Pilea robusta, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial shrub native to China.

It features rosettes of thick, waxy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, followed by round, fleshy fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea robusta is known for its ease of care and ability to propagate through stem cuttings.
