Polygala arvensis var. obovata

Polygala arvensis var. obovata, commonly known as the broad-leaf milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polygalaceae.

It is characterized by its obovate leaves and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. This perennial herb is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and disturbed soils. The plant has a fibrous root system and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds.

It is valued in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
