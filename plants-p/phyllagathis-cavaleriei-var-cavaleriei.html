Phyllagathis cavaleriei var. cavaleriei

Phyllagathis cavaleriei var. cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese teatree, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale pink flowers, which are followed by woody capsules containing numerous small seeds. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in mountainous regions with high humidity.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in horticultural settings.
