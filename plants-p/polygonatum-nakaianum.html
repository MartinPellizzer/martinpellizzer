Polygonatum nakaianum

Polygonatum nakaianum, commonly known as the New Zealand Solomon's Seal, is a perennial herb native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its clumping growth habit and arching stems. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that mature in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and remain evergreen throughout the year.
