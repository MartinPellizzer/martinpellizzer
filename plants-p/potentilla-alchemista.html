Potentilla alchemista

Potentilla alchemista, commonly known as the alpine cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, consisting of five oval-shaped leaflets, and are covered in a fine, silvery-white coating.

Potentilla alchemista thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine environments.
