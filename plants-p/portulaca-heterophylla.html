Portulaca heterophylla

Portulaca heterophylla, commonly known as the variable portulaca, is a species of flowering plant in the family Portulacaceae.

It is native to regions of Africa, southern Europe, and parts of Asia, and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to red. It typically grows as a low-growing annual or perennial herb, often forming dense mats on the ground.

Portulaca heterophylla is well adapted to hot, dry conditions, making it a hardy plant in regions with limited water availability.
