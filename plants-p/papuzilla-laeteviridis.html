Papuzilla laeteviridis

Papuzilla laeteviridis, commonly known as the cheerful green papuzilla, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and clusters of small, bright green flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders. Its foliage remains lush throughout the growing season, adding a vibrant touch to garden landscapes.

Papuzilla laeteviridis is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
