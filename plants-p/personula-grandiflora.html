Personula grandiflora

Personula grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora personula, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in South America.

It is characterized by its striking, large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, or yellow. The plant features woody stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological importance, it is also valued for its role in supporting local pollinators.
