Pleurothallis nutans

Pleurothallis nutans, commonly known as the nodding pleurothallis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid native to the cloud forests of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. The plant features delicate, pendulous flowers that are typically white or pale green, with a distinctive nodding habit. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the humid, shaded understory where it grows.

Pleurothallis nutans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
