Pimelea avonensis

Pimelea avonensis, commonly known as Avon Banksia, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, yellow to orange flowers that are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in open habitats.

Pimelea avonensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
