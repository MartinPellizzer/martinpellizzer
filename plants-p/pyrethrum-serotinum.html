Pyrethrum serotinum

Pyrethrum serotinum, commonly known as late pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Pyrethrum serotinum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
