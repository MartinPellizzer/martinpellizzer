Peperomia tovariana

Peperomia tovariana, commonly known as the Tovar's pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to South America.

It features thick, fleshy, ovate leaves that are typically green with reddish or purple undersides. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia tovariana is known for its low maintenance and ability to tolerate dry conditions.
