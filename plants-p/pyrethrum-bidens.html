Pyrethrum bidens

Pyrethrum bidens, commonly known as common pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. Pyrethrum bidens produces small, spherical flower heads that contain numerous fine, hair-like structures called trichomes.

These trichomes are the source of natural insecticides known as pyrethrins.
