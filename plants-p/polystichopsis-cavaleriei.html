Polystichopsis cavaleriei

Polystichopsis cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese shield fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are divided into segments, forming a dense, shield-like appearance.

The spores are produced on the underside of the fronds, contributing to its reproductive strategy.
