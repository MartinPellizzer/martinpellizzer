Piper loefgrenii

Piper loefgrenii, commonly known as the Loefgren's pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing vines and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically greenish-white and are clustered in spikes, and it produces small, berry-like fruits.

Piper loefgrenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in warm climates for its unique foliage.
