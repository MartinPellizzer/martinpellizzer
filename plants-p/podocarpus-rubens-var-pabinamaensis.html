Podocarpus rubens var. pabinamaensis

Podocarpus rubens var. pabinamaensis, commonly known as the Pabinamaensis Podocarp, is a species of coniferous tree native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Podocarpaceae and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and conical shape. The plant exhibits a slow growth rate and is often found in mountainous areas with high humidity and well-drained soil. Its leaves are typically narrow and dark green, and it produces small, woody cones that contain seeds.

This variant is of particular interest to botanists due to its unique ecological adaptations and limited distribution.
