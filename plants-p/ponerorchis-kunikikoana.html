Ponerorchis kunikikoana

Ponerorchis kunikikoana, commonly known as the Kunikikoan orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate and delicate floral structure. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying a mix of white and green hues with subtle veining.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
