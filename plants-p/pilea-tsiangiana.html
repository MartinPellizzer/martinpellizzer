Pilea tsiangiana

Pilea tsiangiana, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that resemble small coins, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is a popular houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.

Pilea tsiangiana thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
