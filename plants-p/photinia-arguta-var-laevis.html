Photinia arguta var. laevis

Photinia arguta var. laevis, commonly known as the smooth-leaved photinia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are smooth and velvety to the touch, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, white flowers in spring, followed by red, fleshy berries that are attractive to birds. It is valued in horticulture for its dense foliage and ornamental appeal.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in landscaping for borders and foundation plantings.
