Potentilla rubens var. gadensis

Potentilla rubens var. gadensis, commonly known as the Gadenskaya potentilla, is a variant of the common cinquefoil.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub characterized by its hairy stems and small, rounded leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in alpine and rocky habitats.

This variety is notable for its hardiness and adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
