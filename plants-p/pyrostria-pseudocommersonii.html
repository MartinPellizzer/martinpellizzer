Pyrostria pseudocommersonii

Pyrostria pseudocommersonii, commonly known as the Commerson's pyrostria, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains in South America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, thriving in cool and moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small and yellow, forming dense clusters.
