Pleurostachys polygonatum

Pleurostachys polygonatum, commonly known as the Chinese yam, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features a rhizomatous root system that is often harvested for its starchy tubers. The plant has alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are typically hairy on the underside. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, arranged in clusters, and it produces berries that turn red when mature.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is commonly used in traditional Chinese medicine.
