Polystachya asper

Polystachya asper, commonly known as the rough polystachya, is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. The plant features erect, hairy stems and produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in a rough, pubescent texture.

Polystachya asper is often found in shaded, humid environments such as forests and rocky outcrops.
