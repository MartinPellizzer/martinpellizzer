Peridium ferrugineum

Peridium ferrugineum, commonly known as the rusted peridium, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive rust-colored exoskeleton.

It belongs to the family Apiaceae and is native to specific regions in southern Europe. The plant's structure includes a spiky, protective outer layer that encases its reproductive structures. Its botanical features are adapted to thrive in dry, rocky environments.
