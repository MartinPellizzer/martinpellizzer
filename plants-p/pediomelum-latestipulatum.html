Pediomelum latestipulatum

Pediomelum latestipulatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pediomelum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping.
