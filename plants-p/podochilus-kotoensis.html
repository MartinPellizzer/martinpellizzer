Podochilus kotoensis

Podochilus kotoensis, commonly known as Koto's orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink.

Podochilus kotoensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
