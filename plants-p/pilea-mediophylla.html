Pilea mediophylla

Pilea mediophylla, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are typically a vibrant green, often with a slightly glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Pilea mediophylla is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
