Pentaloba zeylanica

Pentaloba zeylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon starfruit or the Indian starfruit, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters tall, characterized by its simple, opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces yellow or orange fruits that are typically five-lobed, resembling a star, hence its common name. The fruit is edible, with a sweet and tangy flavor, and is often used in culinary applications.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae, which includes other plants with latex-producing properties.
