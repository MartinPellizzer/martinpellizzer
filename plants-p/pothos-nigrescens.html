Pothos nigrescens

Pothos nigrescens, commonly known as the black pothos, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, almost black, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and leathery in texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers, followed by red berries that contain the seeds. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers warm, humid environments.

Pothos nigrescens is a popular houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
