Podocarpus deflexus

Podocarpus deflexus, commonly known as the flexible podocarp, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Podocarpaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces small, spherical cones that mature to a reddish-brown color. Its leaves are typically narrow and leathery, providing a dense canopy.

Podocarpus deflexus is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to various soil conditions.
