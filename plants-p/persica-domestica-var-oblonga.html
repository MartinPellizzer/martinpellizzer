Persica domestica var. oblonga

Persica domestica var. oblonga, commonly known as the oval peach.

This variant is characterized by its elongated, oblong-shaped fruits, which are typically smaller than standard peaches. The plant produces sweet, juicy flesh with a distinct aroma, similar to other peach varieties. It thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

This cultivar is often cultivated for both its ornamental value and edible fruit.
