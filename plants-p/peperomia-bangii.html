Peperomia bangii

Peperomia bangii, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically silver or gray-green in color, often with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale green, arranged in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate watering.

Peperomia bangii is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive foliage.
