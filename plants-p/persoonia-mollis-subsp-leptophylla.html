Persoonia mollis subsp. leptophylla

Persoonia mollis subsp.

leptophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leafed geebung, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia. It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

The plant typically grows in heathlands and sclerophyllous forests, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowering period occurs during the autumn season, and it is often found in coastal and inland regions of New South Wales and Victoria.

The species is notable for its ecological importance, providing nectar for native pollinators.
