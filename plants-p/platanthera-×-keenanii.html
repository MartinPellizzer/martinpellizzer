Platanthera × keenanii

Platanthera × keenanii, commonly known as the Keenan's twayblade, is a hybrid orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is a terrestrial herb with a slender, leafless stem that bears two opposite leaves near the base. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, which are typically pollinated by flies and beetles. It thrives in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and wetlands.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
