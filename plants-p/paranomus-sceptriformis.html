Paranomus sceptriformis

Paranomus sceptriformis, commonly known as the swordleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, sword-shaped leaves and showy yellow flower heads. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its blooming period occurs during the spring and summer months, adding vibrant color to the landscape.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
