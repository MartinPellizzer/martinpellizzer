Panicum furvum

Panicum furvum, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its coarse, reddish-colored foliage and erect, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. It has fibrous roots and produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.

Panicum furvum is often used for erosion control due to its rapid growth and hardy nature.
