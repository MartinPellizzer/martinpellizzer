Palicourea ruelliifolia

Palicourea ruelliifolia, commonly known as the velvetleaf palicourea, is a species of flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family.

Palicourea ruelliifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
