Potentilla Patula Var. Sadleri

Potentilla patula var. sadleri is a compact, flowering shrub known for its vibrant yellow blooms and hardy nature. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. This variety is prized for its low maintenance and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its foliage remains attractive throughout the growing season, adding visual interest to any landscape. Despite its small size, it offers a bold and colorful presence in garden settings.
