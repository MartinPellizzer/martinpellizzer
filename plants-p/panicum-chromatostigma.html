Panicum chromatostigma

Panicum chromatostigma, commonly known as the colorful panic grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender spikes that display a range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow, depending on the season and environmental conditions. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green, contrasting beautifully with its vibrant flower spikes. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its unique coloration and architectural form make it a standout in both wild and cultivated settings.
