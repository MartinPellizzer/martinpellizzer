Pleioblastus variegatus f. major

Pleioblastus variegatus f. major, commonly known as the Variegated Bambu or Variegated Bamboo, is a cultivar of the bamboo genus Pleioblastus.

It is characterized by its striking variegated foliage, featuring a mix of green, white, and yellow hues on its narrow, lance-shaped leaves. This plant is a clumping bamboo, forming dense, upright growth with slender culms that are often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden spaces.

The plant's foliage provides year-round visual interest, making it a popular choice for landscape design.
