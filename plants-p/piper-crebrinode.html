Piper crebrinode

Piper crebrinode, commonly known as the dense-leaf pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, clustered flowers that develop into peppery fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

The botanical features of Piper crebrinode make it an interesting subject for both ecological studies and horticultural cultivation.
