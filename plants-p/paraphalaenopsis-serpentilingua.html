Paraphalaenopsis serpentilingua

Paraphalaenopsis serpentilingua, commonly known as the serpent's tongue orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, strap-like leaves that are dark green and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces striking, orchid-like flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, with a unique, snake-like appearance on the petals. It thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

This species is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
