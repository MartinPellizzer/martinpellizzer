Persicaria plantaginea

Persicaria plantaginea, commonly known as narrowleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, swales, and along stream banks. The plant features upright stems that are often reddish in color and bear small, white to pinkish flowers arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth texture, often with a slight waxy coating.

Persicaria plantaginea is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in wetland environments.
