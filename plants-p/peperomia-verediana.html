Peperomia verediana

Peperomia verediana, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters. The plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and ability to thrive in moderate humidity. It prefers well-draining soil and is relatively drought-tolerant, making it an easy care plant for beginners.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for decorative purposes in both homes and offices.
