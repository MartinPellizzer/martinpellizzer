Pleurothallis minutiflora

Pleurothallis minutiflora, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, narrow leaves and produces tiny, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its compact size and unique floral structure.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments to ensure its survival.
