Physokentia tete

Physokentia tete, commonly known as the Tete palm, is a species of cycad native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that give it a distinctive, tropical appearance. The plant has a slow growth rate and can live for several decades, making it a long-lived species. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and conservation status, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
