Phanera cardinalis

Phanera cardinalis, commonly known as the cardinal flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species.

Its rapid growth and showy blossoms make it a notable addition to botanical gardens and landscaped areas.
