Phyllanthus jaubertii

Phyllanthus jaubertii, commonly known as the African spurge, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture and is characterized by its small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Phyllanthus jaubertii is often found in disturbed areas and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
