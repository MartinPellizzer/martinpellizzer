Primula hymenophylla

Primula hymenophylla, commonly known as the silver leaf primrose, is a delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often appearing in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest areas.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is a sought-after species among horticulturists.
