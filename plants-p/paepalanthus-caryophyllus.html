Paepalanthus caryophyllus

Paepalanthus caryophyllus, commonly known as the pink fairy fan, is a striking member of the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is known for its vibrant pink or purple flowers. The plant features a compact, bushy growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are arranged in dense, fan-like clusters, giving it a unique and ornamental appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
