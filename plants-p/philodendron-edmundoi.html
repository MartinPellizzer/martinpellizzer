Philodendron edmundoi

Philodendron edmundoi, commonly known as the Emerald Queen, is a tropical climbing plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy, deep green leaves that are often variegated with lighter green or white veins. The plant produces small, pale greenish-white flowers, though it is more valued for its ornamental foliage than its blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Philodendron edmundoi is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
