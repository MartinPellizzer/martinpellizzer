Polygala ukirensis

Polygala ukirensis, commonly known as the Natal milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often spirally arranged leaves. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal regions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
