Poecilostachys leandrii

Poecilostachys leandrii, commonly known as the Leander grass, is a perennial grass native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or along riverbanks.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
