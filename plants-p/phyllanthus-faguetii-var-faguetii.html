Phyllanthus faguetii var. faguetii

Phyllanthus faguetii var. faguetii, commonly known as the African spurge, is a perennial shrub native to tropical Africa.

It features small, elliptic leaves with a smooth surface and a distinctive yellowish-green color. The plant produces tiny, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, followed by small, spherical fruits. It is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in full sun.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
