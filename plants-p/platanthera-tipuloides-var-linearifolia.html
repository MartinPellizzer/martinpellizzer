Platanthera tipuloides var. linearifolia

Platanthera tipuloides var. linearifolia, commonly known as the linear-leaf lady's-tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single, dense spike of pale green to white flowers. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a spiral along the stem.

This variety is distinguished by its narrow, elongated leaves and is often found in association with specific fungal symbionts.
