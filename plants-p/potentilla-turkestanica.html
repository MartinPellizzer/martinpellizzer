Potentilla turkestanica

Potentilla turkestanica, commonly known as the Turkestan cinquefoil, is a perennial shrub native to Central Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are usually compound with serrated edges. It is often found in mountainous regions and can thrive in poor soil conditions.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional medicinal uses.
