Pleurothallis melicoides

Pleurothallis melicoides, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features delicate, narrow leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green. It is part of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is known for its unique floral structure.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often found in protected areas.
