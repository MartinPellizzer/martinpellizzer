Pseudocamelina bakhtiarica

Pseudocamelina bakhtiarica, commonly known as the Bakhtiaric false flax, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and linear, grass-like leaves. It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.
