Premna resinosa subsp. resinosa

Premna resinosa subsp.

resinosa, commonly known as the sticky fig or resinous premna, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It is a small to medium-sized shrub or tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height.

The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, sticky resin. Its small, greenish-white flowers are arranged in clusters and are followed by small, round fruits that are also sticky.

The plant is often found in moist, shaded areas in tropical and subtropical regions.
