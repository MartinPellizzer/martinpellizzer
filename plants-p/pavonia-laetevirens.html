Pavonia laetevirens

Pavonia laetevirens, commonly known as the brightleaf pavonia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in moist, well-drained soils. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are usually a vibrant green, and it produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink to red. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Pavonia laetevirens is also valued for its ability to thrive in partial shade and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
