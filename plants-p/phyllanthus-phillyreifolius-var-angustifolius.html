Phyllanthus phillyreifolius var. angustifolius

Phyllanthus phillyreifolius var. angustifolius, commonly known as narrow-leaved spurge, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in clusters, followed by small, fleshy fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas.

Its foliage and flowers are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
