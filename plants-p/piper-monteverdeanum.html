Piper monteverdeanum

Piper monteverdeanum, commonly known as the Monteverde pepper vine, is a climbing plant native to the cloud forests of Costa Rica.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many pepper species. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that develop into peppery fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical rainforests.

Due to its unique habitat and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
