Patrinia intermedia

Patrinia intermedia, commonly known as Chinese yarrow, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in dense clusters, typically blooming in summer. It is commonly found in moist meadows and woodland edges.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Caprifoliaceae and is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
