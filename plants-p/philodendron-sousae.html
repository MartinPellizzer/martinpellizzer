Philodendron sousae

Philodendron sousae, commonly known as the golden phantom plant, is a tropical climbing plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves that often display a striking golden or variegated pattern. The plant has a vining growth habit and can reach lengths of several meters when given support. Its leaves are typically a deep green with prominent yellow veins, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Philodendron sousae prefers bright, indirect light and consistent moisture, though it is relatively low maintenance compared to other philodendrons.
