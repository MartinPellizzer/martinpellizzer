Piper gibbilimbum

Piper gibbilimbum, commonly known as the broad-leaved pepper vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruits are berry-like and typically contain several seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of traditional medicinal compounds.
