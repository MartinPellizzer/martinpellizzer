Phlomis forrestii var. taronensis

Phlomis forrestii var. taronensis, commonly known as the Taronian phlomis, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces striking, showy spikes of purple to pink flowers in the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its rugged appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.
