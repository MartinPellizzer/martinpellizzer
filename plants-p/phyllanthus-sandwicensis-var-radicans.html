Phyllanthus sandwicensis var. radicans

Phyllanthus sandwicensis var. radicans, commonly known as the Hawaiian sandwicensis var. radicans, is a unique plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal areas, characterized by its fleshy, opposite leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are capsules that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This variety is notable for its adaptability to the arid and rocky conditions of its native habitat.
