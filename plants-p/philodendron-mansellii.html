Philodendron mansellii

Philodendron mansellii, commonly known as the silver mottled philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, dark green leaves with distinctive silver or white mottling. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by red or orange berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Philodendron mansellii is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
