Plectranthus cymosus

Plectranthus cymosus, commonly known as the purple leaf plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking purple leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant is often grown as an ornamental due to its attractive foliage and compact growth habit. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Plectranthus cymosus is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in herbal remedies.
