Pyrrhocactus robustus var. vegasanus

Pyrrhocactus robustus var. vegasanus, commonly known as the Vegas Pyrrhocactus, is a succulent native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States.

It features thick, ribbed stems that store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are typically small and spiny, providing protection from herbivores.

It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for xeriscaping.
