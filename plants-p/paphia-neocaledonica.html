Paphia neocaledonica

Paphia neocaledonica, commonly known as the New Caledonia paphiopedilum, is a terrestrial orchid native to the island of New Caledonia.

It features a short, hairy stem with several fleshy, elliptical leaves that are dark green in color. The plant produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive lip that is often violet or purple in hue. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
