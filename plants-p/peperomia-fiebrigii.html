Peperomia fiebrigii

Peperomia fiebrigii, commonly known as the Fiebrig's pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to Central and South America.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves with a slightly waxy texture and prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pale green flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its low maintenance and attractive foliage make it a favored ornamental plant for both novice and experienced gardeners.
