Peperomia erythrocorma

Peperomia erythrocorma, commonly known as the red-cormed peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are often adorned with red or purple veins, giving it a striking visual appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

Peperomia erythrocorma is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
