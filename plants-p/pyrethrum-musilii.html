Pyrethrum musilii

Pyrethrum musilii, commonly known as the Italian pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces fine, hair-like structures called trichomes on its leaves and stems, which are the source of its natural insecticidal properties. Pyrethrum musilii thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and agricultural settings.

Its botanical features make it valuable both ornationally and for pest control purposes.
