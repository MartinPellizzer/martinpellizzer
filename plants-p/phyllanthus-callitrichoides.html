Phyllanthus callitrichoides

Phyllanthus callitrichoides, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its dense, dark green leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the summer. Its fruit is a capsule containing small, hard seeds.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for various health purposes.
