Primula × kellereri

Primula × kellereri, commonly known as the Japanese primrose, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Primulaceae family.

It is characterized by its clustered, bell-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and red. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–30 cm and produces lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Native to Japan, it thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as a garden ornamental.

Its flowers bloom in early spring, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and shady borders.
