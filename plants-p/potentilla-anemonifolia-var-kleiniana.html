Potentilla anemonifolia var. kleiniana

Potentilla anemonifolia var. kleiniana, commonly known as the Klein's cinquefoil, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features delicate, five-petaled white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has a mat-forming growth habit with numerous slender stems and small, rounded leaves. It thrives in well-drained, rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.
