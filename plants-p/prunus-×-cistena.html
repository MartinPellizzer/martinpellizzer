Prunus × cistena

Prunus × cistena, commonly known as the Cistena cherry, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Prunus lusitanica and Prunus sargentii.

It is characterized by its ornamental value, featuring vibrant flowers and attractive foliage. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

The fruits of Prunus × cistena are edible but are generally less prominent than its ornamental features.
