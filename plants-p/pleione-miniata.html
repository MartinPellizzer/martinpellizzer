Pleione miniata

Pleione miniata, commonly known as the miniature pleione orchid, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact size and striking, showy flowers. The plant typically produces small, bell-shaped blooms in shades of pink and white, often with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and unique appearance.
