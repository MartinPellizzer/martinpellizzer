Peperomia rupigaudens

Peperomia rupigaudens, commonly known as the rock peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Brazil.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Peperomia rupigaudens is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its drought tolerance and attractive foliage.
