Pleione microphylla

Pleione microphylla, commonly known as the dwarf pleione, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant has a short, stout rhizome and produces few, narrow leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow with a contrasting lip.

It is a prized plant among orchid enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
