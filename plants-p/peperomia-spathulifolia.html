Peperomia spathulifolia

Peperomia spathulifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically ovate to spatulate in shape, with a deep green color and prominent veins. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, which appear during the warmer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and tolerance for dry conditions.

Peperomia spathulifolia is prized for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
