Panicum caroniense

Panicum caroniense, commonly known as the Caroni grass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces fluffy, panicle-like inflorescences that are often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it adaptable to various environmental conditions.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for local wildlife.
