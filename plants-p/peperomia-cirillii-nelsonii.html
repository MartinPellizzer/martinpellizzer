Peperomia cirillii-nelsonii

Peperomia cirillii-nelsonii, commonly known as the Nelson's Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or brown, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its unique foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements, it is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
