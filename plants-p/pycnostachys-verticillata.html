Pycnostachys verticillata

Pycnostachys verticillata, commonly known as the spiral-leaved pycnostachys, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, spirally arranged leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique morphology makes it a subject of interest in botanical studies and horticulture.
