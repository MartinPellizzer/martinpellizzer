Pinanga nannospadix

Pinanga nannospadix, commonly known as the narrow-spadix fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender fronds that can reach up to 2 meters in length. The plant features a creeping rhizome with clusters of small, spore-bearing structures called sporangia. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical rainforests.

Its unique morphology and ecological role make it an important component of its native ecosystem.
