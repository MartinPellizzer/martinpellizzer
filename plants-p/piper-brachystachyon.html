Piper brachystachyon

Piper brachystachyon, commonly known as the short-stalked pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, peppery fruits that are used in traditional medicine and cuisine.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in plant morphology and ecology.
