Plantaginorchis

Plantaginorchis, commonly known as the orchid orchis, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

These plants are characterized by their showy, often fragrant flowers that typically feature a complex structure with three sepals and three petals, one of which is modified into a lip. They are native to various regions in the Northern Hemisphere, including Europe, Asia, and North America. Plantaginorchis species often grow in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows.
