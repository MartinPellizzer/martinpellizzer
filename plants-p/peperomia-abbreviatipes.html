Peperomia abbreviatipes

Peperomia abbreviatipes, commonly known as the compact peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, waxy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slight sheen. The plant produces tiny, clustered flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
