Polygala michaletii

Polygala michaletii, commonly known as the Michaleti's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to withstand arid conditions. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, and it produces small, bean-like fruits that contain several seeds.
