Poa pratensis var. retroflexa

Poa pratensis var. retroflexa, commonly known as the bentgrass, is a variant of the common bentgrass species.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and dense, fine-textured foliage. The plant typically forms low, mat-like growth and is often found in moist, well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

This variety is often used in turfgrass management due to its durability and aesthetic appeal.
