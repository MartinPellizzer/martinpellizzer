Protea tenax

Protea tenax, commonly known as the rigid protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, upright inflorescence composed of numerous small, tubular flowers clustered around a central axis. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the Mediterranean climate of the region. Its flowers range in color from white to pale pink, and it blooms during the spring and summer months.

Protea tenax is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
