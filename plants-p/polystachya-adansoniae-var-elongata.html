Polystachya adansoniae var. elongata

Polystachya adansoniae var. elongata, commonly known as the elongated flame orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and striking, elongated flowers that often display vibrant red or orange hues. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and savannas. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of flowers arranged in a raceme, which can reach several feet in length.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in warm, humid environments.
