Pleurothallis nana

Pleurothallis nana, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact size and delicate appearance. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually small and come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink.

Pleurothallis nana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
