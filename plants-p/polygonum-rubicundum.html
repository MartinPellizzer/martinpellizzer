Polygonum rubicundum

Polygonum rubicundum, commonly known as red polygonum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants commonly referred to as buckwheats. The plant is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of small, reddish-bracted flowers. Its leaves are typically ovate with a distinctive reddish tint, especially on the underside.

Polygonum rubicundum is often found in moist habitats such as wetlands and stream banks.
