Pelargonium tuberosum

Pelargonium tuberosum, commonly known as the tuberous geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture and possesses tuberous roots that store nutrients. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Pelargonium tuberosum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
