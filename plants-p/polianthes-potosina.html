Polianthes potosina

Polianthes potosina, commonly known as the Potosi amaryllis, is a striking tropical plant native to Mexico.

It features tall, strap-like leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in length, and it produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in warm, humid climates. It requires well-draining soil and partial to full sunlight to flourish.

Polianthes potosina is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for with proper conditions.
