Pultenaea stricta var. gunnii

Pultenaea stricta var. gunnii, commonly known as the narrow-leafed pultenaea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features narrow, elliptical leaves that are typically covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often appearing in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or rocky habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowering display.
