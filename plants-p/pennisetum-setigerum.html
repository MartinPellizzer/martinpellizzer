Pennisetum setigerum

Pennisetum setigerum, commonly known as purple lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and striking purple plumes that appear in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces fluffy, seed-bearing inflorescences. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Pennisetum setigerum is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
