Piper antioquiense var. pitaense

Piper antioquiense var. pitaense, commonly known as the Pitaense Pepper, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of Colombia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. It produces small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and fruit.
