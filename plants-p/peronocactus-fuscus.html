Peronocactus fuscus

Peronocactus fuscus, commonly known as the dark peronocactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its spiny, cylindrical stems that are typically a dark green to brownish color. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking contrast to its dark foliage. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
