Philadelphus texensis

Philadelphus texensis, commonly known as Texas Mock Orange, is a deciduous shrub native to the southern United States.

It features fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy on the underside. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

The shrub is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
