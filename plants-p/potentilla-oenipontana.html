Potentilla oenipontana

Potentilla oenipontana, commonly known as the alpine potentilla, is a low-growing shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August. The plant has wiry, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine layer of hairs. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine meadows and scree slopes.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens with rocky or gravelly conditions.
