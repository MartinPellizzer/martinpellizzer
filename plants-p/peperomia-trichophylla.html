Peperomia trichophylla

Peperomia trichophylla, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, velvety leaves that are typically dark green with a textured surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers, which are borne on short, upright spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage makes it a standout addition to any plant collection.
