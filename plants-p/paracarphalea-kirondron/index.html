Paracarphalea Kirondron

The paracarphalea kirondron is a rare and enigmatic plant native to the remote highlands of central africa. it belongs to the asteraceae family, known for its diverse and often ornamental members. this plant is distinguished by its unique leaf structure and delicate, daisy-like flowers. despite its beauty, it remains largely understudied due to its limited distribution and elusive habitat. conservation efforts are crucial to protect this fragile species from threats like habitat loss and climate change.
