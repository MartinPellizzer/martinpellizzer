Primula taliensis subsp. procera

Primula taliensis subsp.

procera, commonly known as the tall primrose, is a perennial herb native to China. It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, bell-shaped flowers.

The plant typically produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. It prefers cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
