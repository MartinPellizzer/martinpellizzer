Pierardia macrophylla

Pierardia macrophylla, commonly known as the giant panda bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its large, broad leaves and tall, slender stalks. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It is notable for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological importance, it is often studied for its role in local ecosystems.
