Panopia

Panopia, commonly known as the golden cup, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that resemble small cups, hence its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance.
