Polyscias papuana

Polyscias papuana, commonly known as the Papuan glorybower, is a shrub or small tree native to the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets. Polyscias papuana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant.
