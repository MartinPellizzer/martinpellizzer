Phryne pinnatifida

Phryne pinnatifida, commonly known as the narrow-leafed daisy.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Phryne pinnatifida is adapted to arid conditions and is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance.
