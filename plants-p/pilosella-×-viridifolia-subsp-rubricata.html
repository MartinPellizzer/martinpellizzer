Pilosella × viridifolia subsp. rubricata

Pilosella × viridifolia subsp.

rubricata, commonly known as the red-stemmed blueweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its slender, herbaceous stems and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer.

The subspecies rubricata is distinguished by its reddish-brown stems and leaves that are often covered with fine, white hairs. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

This plant is native to Europe and parts of western Asia, where it thrives in temperate climates.
