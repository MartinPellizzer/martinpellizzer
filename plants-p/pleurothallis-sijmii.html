Pleurothallis sijmii

Pleurothallis sijmii, commonly known as the Sijm's orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Costa Rica.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a unique fragrance. Its leaves are elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern, facilitating water retention in its humid habitat.

Pleurothallis sijmii is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
