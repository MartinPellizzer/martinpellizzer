Philodendron fenzlii

Philodendron fenzlii, commonly known as the Fenzlii philodendron, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length, often with a glossy, dark green surface. The plant features a climbing habit, with aerial roots that allow it to scale supports as it grows. Its foliage is typically variegated, displaying shades of green, white, and cream, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.
