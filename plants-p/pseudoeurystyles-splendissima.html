Pseudoeurystyles splendissima

Pseudoeurystyles splendissima, commonly known as the splendid star orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, star-shaped flowers that range in color from deep purple to bright yellow. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree trunks and branches in its natural habitat. Its leaves are long and narrow, adapted to capture moisture from the humid air.

Due to its limited distribution and unique beauty, it is highly sought after by botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
