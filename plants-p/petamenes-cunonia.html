Petamenes cunonia

Petamenes cunonia, commonly known as the red-flowered rock heath.

It is a flowering plant native to the eastern regions of Australia, typically found in rocky and sandy soils. Petamenes cunonia is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
