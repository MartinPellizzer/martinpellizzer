Parodia variicolor var. robustispina

Parodia variicolor var. robustispina, commonly known as the robust spiny cactus, is a member of the Cactaceae family native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, thick stems that are typically ribbed and covered with white, fluffy spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and collectors.
