Pittosporum xanthanthum var. parvifolium

Pittosporum xanthanthum var. parvifolium, commonly known as the small-leaf pittosporum, is a deciduous shrub native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, with a fine, silvery underside. The plant produces fragrant, yellow flowers in spring, which are followed by small, red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
