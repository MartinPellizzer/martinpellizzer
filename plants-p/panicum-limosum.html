Panicum limosum

Panicum limosum, commonly known as yellow foxtail, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It thrives in moist to wet soils, often found in marshes, swales, and along streambanks. The plant produces dense, spike-like inflorescences that are typically golden-yellow in color. Its leaves are narrow and blade-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Panicum limosum plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
