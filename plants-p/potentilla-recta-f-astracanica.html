Potentilla recta f. astracanica

Potentilla recta f. astracanica, commonly known as the Astracanica cinquefoil, is a compact, low-growing shrub native to Central Asia.

It features small, rounded leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and its flowers are typically yellow or orange, appearing in clusters. The plant is adapted to dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and hardiness.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive addition to alpine or rock garden settings.
