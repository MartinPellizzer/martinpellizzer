Potamogeton chongyangensis

Potamogeton chongyangensis, commonly known as the Yangtze water milfoil, is a submerged aquatic plant native to China.

It typically grows in freshwater environments such as lakes, rivers, and ponds. The plant has slender, ribbon-like leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stem. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, often clustered in spikes at the top of the plant.

It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.
