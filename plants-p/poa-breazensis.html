Poa breazensis

Poa breazensis, commonly known as the Breazens grass, is a species of grass native to the mountainous regions of Romania.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and dark green foliage. The plant typically thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky soils. Its flowering stems bear small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny flowers.

Poa breazensis plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat and is of interest to botanists due to its unique ecological adaptations.
