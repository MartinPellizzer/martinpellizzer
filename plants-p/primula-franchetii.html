Primula franchetii

Primula franchetii, commonly known as the Chinese primrose, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features attractive, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters, with broad, oval leaves that are often hairy. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial to full shade.

Botanically, it belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its clustered flower arrangement and distinctive foliage.
