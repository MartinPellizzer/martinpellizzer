Peperomia fortipes

Peperomia fortipes, commonly known as the Shiny Peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features glossy, dark green, heart-shaped leaves that are densely packed along its upright stems. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia fortipes is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and occasional pruning to maintain its neat appearance.
