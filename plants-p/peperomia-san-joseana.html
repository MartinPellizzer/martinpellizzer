Peperomia san-joseana

Peperomia san-joseana, commonly known as the San Jose peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous white or pale green flowers. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive foliage.
