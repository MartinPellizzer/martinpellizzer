Physalis szechuanica

Physalis szechuanica, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange, inflated calyces that enclose the fruit, resembling small lanterns. The plant is native to China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It produces small, yellow, berry-like fruits that are edible but not commonly consumed.

The leaves are ovate and have a slightly hairy texture, while the stems are hairy and somewhat woody.
