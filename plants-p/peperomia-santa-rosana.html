Peperomia santa-rosana

Peperomia santa-rosana, commonly known as the Santa Rosa peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features oval, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia santa-rosana is relatively low maintenance and drought-tolerant once established.
