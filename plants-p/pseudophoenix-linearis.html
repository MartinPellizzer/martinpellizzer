Pseudophoenix linearis

Pseudophoenix linearis, commonly known as the slender fan palm, is a striking tropical plant native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its slender, unbranched trunk and long, narrow leaves that form a feathery crown. The plant's leaves are typically blue-green in color and have a distinctive linear shape, giving it a unique appearance. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

Pseudophoenix linearis is also notable for its slow growth rate and its role in the unique ecosystem of Socotra.
