Phyllanthus petelotii

Phyllanthus petelotii, commonly known as the African spurge, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows up to 1 meter in height, characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
