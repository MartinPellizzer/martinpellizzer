Prunus plurinervis

Prunus plurinervis, commonly known as the Chinese plum or Chinese date, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape with a smooth margin. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically white or pink, which bloom in early spring. Its fruit is a drupe, usually yellow or red when ripe, and is edible.

Prunus plurinervis is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been cultivated for centuries in traditional Chinese medicine.
