Portulaca platensis

Portulaca platensis, commonly known as the broadleaf portulaca, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a low-growing annual herb with fleshy, succulent stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the morning and close by midday. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Portulaca platensis is also known for its ability to tolerate drought conditions due to its succulent nature.
