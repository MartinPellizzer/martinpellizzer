Parodia nivosa

Parodia nivosa, commonly known as the snowball cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its dense, globular shape and white, woolly spines that give it a fluffy appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for succulent collectors and xeriscaping.
