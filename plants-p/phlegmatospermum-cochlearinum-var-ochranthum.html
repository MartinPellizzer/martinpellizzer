Phlegmatospermum cochlearinum var. ochranthum

Phlegmatospermum cochlearinum var. ochranthum, commonly known as the ochre-flowered phlegmatospermum, is a rare and ancient plant species belonging to the family Phlegmatospermaceae.

It is characterized by its unique, fleshy, and often succulent stems that store water, adapting it to arid environments.
