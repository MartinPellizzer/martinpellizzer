Pseudosopubia hildebrandtii

Pseudosopubia hildebrandtii, commonly known as the desert sandmat, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that thrives in sandy and rocky soils, often found in desert environments.
