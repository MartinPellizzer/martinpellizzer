Pandanus chevalieri

Pandanus chevalieri, commonly known as the Madagascar pandanus, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and long, narrow leaves that form a dense canopy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or green in color. These flowers are clustered in branched inflorescences at the top of the plant.

Pandanus chevalieri is often used in traditional crafts and has cultural significance in its native region.
