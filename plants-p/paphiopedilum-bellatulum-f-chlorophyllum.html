Paphiopedilum bellatulum f. chlorophyllum

Paphiopedilum bellatulum f. chlorophyllum, commonly known as the Emerald Paphiopedilum, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant green foliage and showy, pale yellow flowers.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines, thriving in humid, shaded environments. The plant features thick, leathery leaves that are a striking emerald green, contributing to its ornamental value. Its flowers are borne on tall, erect stems and have a unique, spurred shape typical of the Paphiopedilum genus.

This cultivar is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
