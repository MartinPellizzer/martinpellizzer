Polygonum coccineum

Polygonum coccineum, commonly known as red polygonum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It features upright stems that are often reddish in color, especially when mature. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to rhombic in shape, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Polygonum coccineum is often found in moist environments such as wet meadows and stream banks.
