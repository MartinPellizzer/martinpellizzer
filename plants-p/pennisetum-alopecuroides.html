Pennisetum alopecuroides

Pennisetum alopecuroides, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that produce feathery, purple-pink plumes during the summer months. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often gray-green in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant foliage and showy inflorescences, which attract birds and other wildlife.
