Platanthera × colwelliae

Platanthera × colwelliae, commonly known as the hybrid lady's tresses, is a perennial orchid native to parts of North America.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and its striking, fragrant inflorescence composed of numerous white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wetlands. It is a hybrid between Platanthera filicifolia and Platanthera chlorophaea, exhibiting traits from both parent species.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is often cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
