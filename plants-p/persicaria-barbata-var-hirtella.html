Persicaria barbata var. hirtella

Persicaria barbata var. hirtella, commonly known as the hairy smartweed, is a flowering plant in the Polygonaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright, herbaceous growth habit and hairy stems that are often reddish in color. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters, which are typically borne on slender, branching inflorescences. Its leaves are lance-shaped with a rough texture and may have a reddish or purple tinge along the edges.

This variant is often found in moist soils in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds.
