Piper brevistylum

Piper brevistylum, commonly known as the short-stemmed pepper vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various species of pepper plants. The plant features slender, woody stems and heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface. Its small, greenish-white flowers are followed by tiny, berry-like fruits that turn red when mature.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of aromatic foliage.
