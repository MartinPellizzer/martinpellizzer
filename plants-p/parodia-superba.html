Parodia superba

Parodia superba, commonly known as the "Moon Cactus," is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stem and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has a unique appearance due to its distinct coloration, with a green stem and a brightly colored crown. It is a hardy plant that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Parodia superba is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its striking visual appeal.
