Parsonsia stenocarpa

Parsonsia stenocarpa, commonly known as the narrow-fruited parsonsia, is a species of flowering plant in the Apocynaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Australia and is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in coastal and sandy soils, often found in open woodlands and heathlands.

Parsonsia stenocarpa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
