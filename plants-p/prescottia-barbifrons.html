Prescottia barbifrons

Prescottia barbifrons, commonly known as the velvetleaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety green leaves and clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers have a unique structure with prominent, hairy sepals and petals.

Prescottia barbifrons is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its aesthetic qualities.
