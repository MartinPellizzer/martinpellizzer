Pittosporum wingii

Pittosporum wingii, commonly known as the New Zealand pittosporum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features compound leaves with a distinctive yellowish-green color and a serrated margin. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Following flowering, it develops bright red berries that are a food source for native birds.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.
