Peperomia trinervis var. brachyphylla

Peperomia trinervis var. brachyphylla, commonly known as the compact peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with a dark green color and prominent veins, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of pink or white. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its ability to thrive in low light and minimal water conditions.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for container gardening and as a decorative plant.
