Pouteria gnaphaloclados

Pouteria gnaphaloclados, commonly known as the Brazilian nut tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, elliptical leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers.
