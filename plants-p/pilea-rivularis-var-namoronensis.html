Pilea rivularis var. namoronensis

Pilea rivularis var. namoronensis, commonly known as the Namoronensis Pilea, is a rare and unique variant of the pilea genus.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features small, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green, with a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

This variety is prized by collectors for its distinctive foliage and ease of propagation through stem cuttings.
