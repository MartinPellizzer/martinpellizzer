Pseuderanthemum adenocalix

Pseuderanthemum adenocalix, commonly known as the silverleaf pseuderanthemum, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
