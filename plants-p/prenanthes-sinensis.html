Prenanthes sinensis

Prenanthes sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese water drop plant, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its unique inflorescence, which consists of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, with a smooth texture.

Prenanthes sinensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
