Prunus serrulata okiku

Prunus serrulata okiku, commonly known as the Okiku cherry.

It is a species of flowering shrub or small tree native to Japan. The plant is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are oval-shaped with finely serrated edges, giving the plant its scientific name.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and parks.
