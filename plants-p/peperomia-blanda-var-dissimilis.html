Peperomia blanda var. dissimilis

Peperomia blanda var. dissimilis, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Central and South America.

It features dense, glossy foliage with a distinctive silvery-blue hue on the upper surface and a darker green underside. The plant has upright, branching stems and small, ovate leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia blanda var. dissimilis is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
