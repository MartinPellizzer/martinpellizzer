Pelargonium strigosum

Pelargonium strigosum, commonly known as the woolly geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-green leaves and clusters of pink or red flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice among gardeners.

It is also known for its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
