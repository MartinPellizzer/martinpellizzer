Pennisetum compressum

Pennisetum compressum, commonly known as purple lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and striking purple to silver-colored foliage. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and produces small, fluffy seed heads that are often used in floral arrangements. Pennisetum compressum thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in various climates contribute to its widespread cultivation.
