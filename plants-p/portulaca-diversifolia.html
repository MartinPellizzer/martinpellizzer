Portulaca diversifolia

Portulaca diversifolia, commonly known as the variegated purslane, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a hardy species suitable for arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are often marked with distinctive white or silver markings, adding to its ornamental value.

Portulaca diversifolia is also known for its ability to tolerate drought conditions, which contributes to its widespread cultivation in gardens and landscapes.
