Proboscidea louisiana

Proboscidea louisiana, commonly known as the Louisiana marsh hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the wetlands of Louisiana and Texas.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in moist, swampy environments. It is a perennial herb that produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

Proboscidea louisiana plays an important role in the ecological balance of its habitat by providing nectar for pollinators.
