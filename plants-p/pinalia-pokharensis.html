Pinalia pokharensis

Pinalia pokharensis, commonly known as the Pokhara pinyon, is a rare and endemic species native to the mountainous regions of Nepal.

It belongs to the Pinaceae family, which includes various coniferous trees and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its needle-like leaves and cone-bearing structure, typical of many pine species. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and well-drained soils.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Pinalia pokharensis is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
