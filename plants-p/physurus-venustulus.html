Physurus venustulus

Physurus venustulus, commonly known as the velvet leaf or velvet fern, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are palmately lobed, and it thrives in moist, shady environments.

Physurus venustulus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique texture and appearance.
