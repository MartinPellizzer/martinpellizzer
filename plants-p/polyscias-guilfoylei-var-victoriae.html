Polyscias guilfoylei var. victoriae

Polyscias guilfoylei var. victoriae, commonly known as the Victoria's Polyscias, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Australia.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, often with a slight waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red berries, which are edible for birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its dense foliage and ability to tolerate coastal conditions.
