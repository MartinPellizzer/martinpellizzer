Panicum wiehei

Panicum wiehei, commonly known as Chinese silver grass, is a perennial grass native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and delicate, feathery inflorescences that resemble plumes. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders. Its ornamental value comes from its unique appearance and ability to add texture to landscapes.

It is also valued for its low maintenance requirements and drought tolerance.
