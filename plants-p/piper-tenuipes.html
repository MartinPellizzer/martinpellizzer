Piper tenuipes

Piper tenuipes, commonly known as the slender pepper vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into elongated, peppery fruits. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has a mild peppery flavor.

The botanical characteristics of Piper tenuipes make it a unique and interesting species within the Piper genus.
