Pleione yunnanensis

Pleione yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan pleione, is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It is characterized by its elegant, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often with a distinctive yellow lip. The plant typically grows at high altitudes, thriving in cool, humid environments with well-drained soil. Its rhizome is thick and fleshy, allowing it to store moisture and nutrients in its native habitat.

Due to its beauty and rarity, it is highly valued in horticulture and conservation efforts.
