Peperomia herthae

Peperomia herthae, commonly known as the Herth's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features succulent, ovate leaves that are usually dark green with a waxy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale green. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
