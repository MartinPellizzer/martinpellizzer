Polygala stenoclada var. stenosepala

Polygala stenoclada var. stenosepala, commonly known as the narrow-sepaled milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, and it produces small, indehiscent fruits.
