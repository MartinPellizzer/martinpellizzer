Paneion pratensiforme

Paneion pratensiforme, commonly known as the broadleaf grass.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia. The plant features slender, erect stems that can grow up to 60 cm in height. Its leaves are lanceolate, with a smooth surface and a bluish-green hue.

The small, pale yellow flowers are arranged in dense spikes and bloom in early summer.
