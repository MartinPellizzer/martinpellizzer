Phylica capitata var. brachycephala

Phylica capitata var. brachycephala, commonly known as the narrow-headed phylica, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, needle-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers, which are highly attractive to pollinators such as bees and birds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
