Pleurothallis fumioi

Pleurothallis fumioi, commonly known as the smoky pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and its ability to thrive in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are usually dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it conserve moisture.

Pleurothallis fumioi is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
