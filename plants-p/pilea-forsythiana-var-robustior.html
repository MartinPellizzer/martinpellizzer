Pilea forsythiana var. robustior

Pilea forsythiana var. robustior, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or pilea peperomioides, is a compact, evergreen houseplant native to China.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with subtle variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are followed by rounded, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ease of care and aesthetic appeal in indoor gardening.
