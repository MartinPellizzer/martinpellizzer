Pleione barbata

Pleione barbata, commonly known as the barbed pleione, is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows at high altitudes, thriving in cool, moist environments with partial shade. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern around the pseudobulbs.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
