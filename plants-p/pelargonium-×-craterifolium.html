Pelargonium × craterifolium

Pelargonium × craterifolium, commonly known as the rock geranium, is a hybrid plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features fuzzy, rounded leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for decorative purposes in gardens and landscapes.
