Primulina brachystigma

Primulina brachystigma, commonly known as the dwarf primula, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value.

Its leaves are broad and glossy, adding to its aesthetic appeal in both indoor and outdoor settings.
