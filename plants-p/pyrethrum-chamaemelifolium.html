Pyrethrum chamaemelifolium

Pyrethrum chamaemelifolium, commonly known as the alpine pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces a fine, powdery substance on its flowers, which contains natural insecticides.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its use in organic pest control.
