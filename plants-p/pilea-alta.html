Pilea alta

Pilea alta, commonly known as the Shiny Pilea, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Pilea alta is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
