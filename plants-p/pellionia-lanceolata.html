Pellionia lanceolata

Pellionia lanceolata, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. Pellionia lanceolata thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in partial shade.
