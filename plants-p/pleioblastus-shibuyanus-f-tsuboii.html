Pleioblastus shibuyanus f. tsuboii

Pleioblastus shibuyanus f. tsuboii, commonly known as the Shibuya bamboo, is a compact, clumping bamboo species native to Japan.

It features slender, dark green culms with a smooth, almost metallic sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers in late summer, followed by fluffy, pale brown seeds. It is well-suited for container gardening and small spaces due to its slow growth and elegant appearance.

Its foliage remains lush throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for ornamental purposes.
