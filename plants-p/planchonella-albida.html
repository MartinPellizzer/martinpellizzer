Planchonella albida

Planchonella albida, commonly known as the white planchonella, is a tropical tree native to parts of Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and elongated, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by round, fleshy fruits containing a single seed. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
