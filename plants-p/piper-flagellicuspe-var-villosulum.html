Piper flagellicuspe var. villosulum

Piper flagellicuspe var. villosulum, commonly known as the hairy sarsaparilla, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of flowering plants known for their aromatic properties.
