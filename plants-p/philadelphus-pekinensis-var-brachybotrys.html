Philadelphus pekinensis var. brachybotrys

Philadelphus pekinensis var. brachybotrys, commonly known as the compact-fruit mock orange, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its fruit is a small, round drupe that matures in late summer.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant fragrance.
