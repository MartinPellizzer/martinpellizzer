Pellaea Falcata

Pellaea falcata is a striking evergreen fern native to southern africa known for its distinctive silvery-gray fronds. its leaves are narrow and sharply pointed, giving it a unique appearance that stands out in shaded environments. this plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade. it is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative plant in shaded areas. pellaea falcata is also valued for its ability to add texture and interest to garden landscapes.
