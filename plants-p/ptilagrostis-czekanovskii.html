Ptilagrostis czekanovskii

Ptilagrostis czekanovskii, commonly known as the Czekanovsky's feathergrass, is a species of grass native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and fine, feather-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as steppes and rocky slopes. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers.

Ptilagrostis czekanovskii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate, grass-like foliage.
