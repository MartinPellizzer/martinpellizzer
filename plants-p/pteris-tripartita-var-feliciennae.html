Pteris tripartita var. feliciennae

Pteris tripartita var. feliciennae, commonly known as the Felicienne's water fern, is a variant of the common water fern, characterized by its distinctive fronds with three distinct segments.

It is a fern belonging to the family Pteridaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant exhibits a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping rhizomes that allow it to spread along the soil surface. Its fronds are typically leathery and dark green, with a serrated or lobed margin.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in aquatic and bog gardens.
