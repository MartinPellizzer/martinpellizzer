Pinalia clavata

Pinalia clavata, commonly known as the clustered clubmoss, is a small evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Lycopodiaceae and is characterized by its dense, low-growing clusters of needle-like leaves. Its unique morphology and ecological role make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
