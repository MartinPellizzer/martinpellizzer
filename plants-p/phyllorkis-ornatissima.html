Phyllorkis ornatissima

Phyllorkis ornatissima, commonly known as the ornate phyllorkis, is a rare and distinctive member of the orchid family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in countries like Bolivia and Peru. The plant is characterized by its striking, intricately patterned leaves and its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in the early spring. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a conservation concern in its native range.
