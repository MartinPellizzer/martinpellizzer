Polypodium mengtzeense

Polypodium mengtzeense, commonly known as Mengtze's water fern, is a species of polypodiaceae native to China.

It is a terrestrial fern that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit with fronds that are pinnate and often have a dark green coloration. Its reproductive structures include sori that are clustered on the undersides of the fronds.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
