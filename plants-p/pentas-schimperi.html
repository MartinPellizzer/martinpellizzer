Pentas schimperi

Pentas schimperi, commonly known as the African starburst, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of bright, star-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
