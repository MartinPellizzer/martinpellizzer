Peperomia cylindribacca

Peperomia cylindribacca, commonly known as the cylindrical peperomia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright, cylindrical stems that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its leaves are usually ovate or elliptical, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Peperomia cylindribacca is a compact, low-maintenance plant well-suited for indoor cultivation.
