Polystachya johnstonii var. johnstonii

Polystachya johnstonii var. johnstonii, commonly known as the Johnston's polystachya, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in forest undergrowth or near streams. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture and a bluish-green hue.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique floral display.
