Pilosella lenifolia

Pilosella lenifolia, commonly known as the slenderleaf goat's beard, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant features slender, hairy stems and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Pilosella lenifolia is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.
