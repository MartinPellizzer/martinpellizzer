Periploca viridiflora

Periploca viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered periploca, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a leathery texture, adapted to conserve water.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
