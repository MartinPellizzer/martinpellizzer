Polygonatum leveilleanum

Polygonatum leveilleanum, commonly known as the Chinese Solomon's seal, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to regions of East Asia, including China, Korea, and Japan, where it thrives in shaded, moist forest environments. The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in woodland ecosystems.
