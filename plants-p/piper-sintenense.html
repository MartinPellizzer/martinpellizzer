Piper sintenense

Piper sintenense, commonly known as the Sintenese pepper, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various species of pepper plants. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a distinctive aromatic scent.

The fruit, which is a small, dark purple berry, is used in traditional medicine and as a spice in some local cuisines.
