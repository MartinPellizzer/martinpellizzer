Plectranthus stocksii

Plectranthus stocksii, commonly known as the Cape rosemary, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub that produces clusters of small, fragrant, pale blue to violet flowers. The plant has gray-green, velvety leaves that are often aromatic when crushed. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and drought tolerance.
