Polygonum geniculatum

Polygonum geniculatum, commonly known as the zigzag knotweed, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender, zigzagging stems that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, greenish-white flowers appear in clusters and bloom in late summer.

Polygonum geniculatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique growth habit.
