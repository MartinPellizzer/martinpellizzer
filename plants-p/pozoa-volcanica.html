Pozoa volcanica

Pozoa volcanica, commonly known as the volcanic pozoa, is a rare and unique plant species native to specific volcanic regions.

It thrives in nutrient-rich, volcanic soils and is adapted to high-altitude environments. The plant features a compact, cushion-like growth form with dense, overlapping leaves that help retain moisture. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, often displaying shades of violet or purple.

Pozoa volcanica plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity in its specialized habitat.
