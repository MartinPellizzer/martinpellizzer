Phyllanthus tiebaghiensis

Phyllanthus tiebaghiensis, commonly known as the African date palm, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are thick and waxy, adapted to reduce water loss in arid environments.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
