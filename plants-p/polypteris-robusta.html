Polypteris robusta

Polypteris robusta, commonly known as the broad-leaf water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Pteridaceae.

It is characterized by its large, leathery fronds that are typically divided into numerous pinnae. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Southeast Asia, Africa, and the Caribbean. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near water bodies.
