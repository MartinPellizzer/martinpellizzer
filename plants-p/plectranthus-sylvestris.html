Plectranthus sylvestris

Plectranthus sylvestris, commonly known as wild plectranthus, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features opposite, ovate to lance-shaped leaves that are usually gray-green with a fuzzy texture. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of purple or lilac, which bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Plectranthus sylvestris is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and ability to attract pollinators.
