Phippsia deschampsioides

Phippsia deschampsioides, commonly known as the rock phippsia, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It typically grows in rocky, arid environments, showcasing adaptations to harsh, dry conditions. The plant features small, narrow leaves and produces clusters of tiny, pale yellow flowers. It is often found in open, sunny areas such as gravelly slopes and desert scrublands.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for xerophytic habitats and low-maintenance landscaping.
