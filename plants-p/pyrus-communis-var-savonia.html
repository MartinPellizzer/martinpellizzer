Pyrus communis var. savonia

Pyrus communis var. savonia, commonly known as the Savonia pear, is a variety of the common pear tree native to Europe.

It is characterized by its medium-sized, oval-shaped fruits that are typically yellow to golden in color when ripe. The plant has a moderate growth habit with a spreading canopy, making it suitable for both ornamental and fruit production purposes. Its leaves are dark green and glossy, providing aesthetic value in gardens.

The Savonia pear is valued for its sweet, aromatic fruit and its adaptability to various soil types.
