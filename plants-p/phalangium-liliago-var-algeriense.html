Phalangium liliago var. algeriense

Phalangium liliago var. algeriense, commonly known as Algerian lily, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
