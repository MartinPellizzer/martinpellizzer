Phaleria montana

Phaleria montana, commonly known as the mountain phaleria, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its evergreen leaves and fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as mountain forests. Its fruit is a dark-colored drupe that contains a single seed.

Phaleria montana is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
