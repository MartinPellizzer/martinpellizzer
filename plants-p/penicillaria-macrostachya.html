Penicillaria macrostachya

Penicillaria macrostachya, commonly known as the large-stemmed penicillaria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, linear leaves and has a distinctive, branched inflorescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
