Phrynium micholitzii

Phrynium micholitzii, commonly known as the Micholitz's arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking inflorescences with a spathe and spadix, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its foliage is used in traditional medicine and as a decorative plant in ornamental gardens.
