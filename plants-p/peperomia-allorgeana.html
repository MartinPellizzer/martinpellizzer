Peperomia allorgeana

Peperomia allorgeana, commonly known as the Allorge's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are usually dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale green to reddish flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia allorgeana is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
