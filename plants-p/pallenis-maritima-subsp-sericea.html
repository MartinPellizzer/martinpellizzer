Pallenis maritima subsp. sericea

Pallenis maritima subsp.

sericea, commonly known as sea holly, is a coastal plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant has a robust root system that helps stabilize sandy or rocky soils along the shore. Its leaves are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving them a distinctive, almost metallic appearance.

It thrives in saline and well-drained environments, making it a resilient species adapted to harsh maritime conditions.
