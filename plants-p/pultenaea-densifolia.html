Pultenaea densifolia

Pultenaea densifolia, commonly known as the denseleaf pultenaea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, compact foliage and small, pea-like flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft, velvety texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Pultenaea densifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
