Peperomia fimbriata var. gracilior

Peperomia fimbriata var. gracilior, commonly known as the narrow-leaf peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges, giving it a striking visual appeal. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that are typically borne in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate moisture.

Due to its compact size and ornamental foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
