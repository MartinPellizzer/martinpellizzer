Pilea versteegii

Pilea versteegii, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial shrub native to China.

It features round, fleshy leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Pilea versteegii is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
