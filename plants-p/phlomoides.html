Phlomoides

Phlomoides, commonly known as blueweed, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It consists of several species, most of which are native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere. Phlomoides species are valued for their ornamental appeal and have been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
