Podosemum foliosum

Podosemum foliosum, commonly known as the leafy podosemum, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers followed by oval, woody pods containing seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.
