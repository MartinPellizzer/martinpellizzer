Piper itayanum var. pichisense

Piper itayanum var. pichisense, commonly known as the Pichisense pepper plant, is a flowering vine native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into elongated, spiny fruits containing aromatic seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of dense tropical forests.

Due to its unique appearance and potential medicinal properties, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
