Pyrrosia nummulariifolia var. rufa

Pyrrosia nummulariifolia var. rufa, commonly known as the red-stemmed rock fern, is a variant of the rock fern genus Pyrrosia.

It is characterized by its leathery, dark green leaves with a reddish hue on the underside. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often on rock surfaces or in forest understories. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help anchor it to rocky substrates.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in shaded gardens.
