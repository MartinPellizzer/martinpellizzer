Ptychopyxis costata var. costata

Ptychopyxis costata var. costata, commonly known as the zigzag fern, is a species of fern characterized by its distinctive, undulating fronds.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. The plant features slender, wiry stems that produce compound leaves with a zigzag or undulating pattern along their margins. Its fronds are typically bright green and can grow up to several feet in length, making it a striking ornamental plant.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.
