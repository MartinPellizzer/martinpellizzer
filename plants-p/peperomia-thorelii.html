Peperomia thorelii

Peperomia thorelii, commonly known as the velvet plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically a deep green with a velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia thorelii is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
