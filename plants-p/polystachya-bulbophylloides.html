Polystachya bulbophylloides

Polystachya bulbophylloides, commonly known as the bulbophyll orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulb-like structure and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are usually fleshy and succulent, adapted to store water.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
