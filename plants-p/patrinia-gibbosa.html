Patrinia gibbosa

Patrinia gibbosa, commonly known as the hairy skullcap, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces, giving it its common name. Its flowers are small, clustered in whorls, and are usually blue or purple in color.

Patrinia gibbosa is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
