Peperomia obscurifolia

Peperomia obscurifolia, commonly known as the dark-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, glossy, dark green foliage with a velvety texture, and its leaves are often adorned with intricate, dark veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that emerge from the axils of the leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia obscurifolia is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
