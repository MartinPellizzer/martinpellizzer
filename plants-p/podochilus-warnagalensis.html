Podochilus warnagalensis

Podochilus warnagalensis, commonly known as the Warnagalensis orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant typically produces slender, upright stems with a few leaves and bears small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in rainforests or under dense vegetation.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
