Phreatia pulchella

Phreatia pulchella, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that often feature vibrant colors. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and surrounding substrates.

Phreatia pulchella is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
