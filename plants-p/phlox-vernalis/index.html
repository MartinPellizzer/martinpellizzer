Phlox Vernalis

Phlox vernalis, commonly known as spring phlox, is a charming member of the Polemonium family that blooms early in the spring. This hardy perennial thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate regions. Its delicate, clustered flowers range from white to pink and are often adorned with a subtle purple hue. The plant is noted for its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies, enhancing biodiversity in natural and cultivated landscapes. With its graceful appearance and easy care requirements, Phlox vernalis is a delightful addition to any garden.
