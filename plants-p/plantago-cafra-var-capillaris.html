Plantago cafra var. capillaris

Plantago cafra var. capillaris, commonly known as the slender plantain, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, hairless leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense spikes, which are borne on slender, erect stalks. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks.

The species is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
