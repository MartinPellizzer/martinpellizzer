Pelargonium × gardeniae

Pelargonium × gardeniae, commonly known as the gardenia geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant, white or pink flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in moderate light conditions. It prefers well-draining soil and moderate watering to prevent root rot.

Pelargonium × gardeniae is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability.
