Pyrethrum pulchrum

Pyrethrum pulchrum, commonly known as the beautiful pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant produces small, yellowish to orange-colored flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its foliage is typically gray-green with a slightly fuzzy texture, and it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Pyrethrum pulchrum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can also be used in natural pest control due to its pyrethrin content.
