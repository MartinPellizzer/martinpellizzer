Pancratium foetidum var. tunetanum

Pancratium foetidum var. tunetanum, commonly known as the Tunisian yellow nutmeg, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces striking yellow flowers with a strong, sometimes unpleasant odor. The plant features strap-shaped, grass-like leaves and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Its bulbs are used in traditional medicine and as a source of essential oils.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun for optimal growth.
