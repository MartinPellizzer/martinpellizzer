Pleurothallis spectrilinguis

Pleurothallis spectrilinguis, commonly known as the Spectral Lipstick Orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its flowers are often clustered in inflorescences and are noted for their intricate, lip-like structures.

Due to its striking appearance and rarity, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
