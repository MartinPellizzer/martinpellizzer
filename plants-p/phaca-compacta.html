Phaca compacta

Phaca compacta, commonly known as the compact phaca, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is characterized by its dense, low-growing habit. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in shades of white or pale pink.

Phaca compacta is adapted to arid environments and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.
