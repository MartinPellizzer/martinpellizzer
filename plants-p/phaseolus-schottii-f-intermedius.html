Phaseolus schottii f. intermedius

Phaseolus schottii f. intermedius, commonly known as the intermediate scarlet runner bean, is a botanical variant of the scarlet runner bean.

It is characterized by its intermediate traits between the typical scarlet runner bean and other Phaseolus species. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers and edible pods that are often used in culinary applications. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its genetic diversity makes it an interesting subject for botanical and agricultural research.
