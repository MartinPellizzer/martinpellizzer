Pilosella subcymiflora

Pilosella subcymiflora, commonly known as the slenderleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and roadsides. It features slender, hairy stems and small, yellowish flowers arranged in cymes.

Pilosella subcymiflora is known for its adaptability and is often found in temperate climates.
