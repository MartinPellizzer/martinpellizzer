Peperomia tancitaroana

Peperomia tancitaroana, commonly known as the Tancitaro peperomia, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a subtle variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate light, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for collectors and indoor gardeners.
