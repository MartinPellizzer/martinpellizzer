Piper zarceroense

Piper zarceroense, commonly known as the Zarcero pepper or Zarcero pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the cloud forests of Central America, specifically found in countries like Costa Rica and Panama. The plant is characterized by its trailing growth habit, with opposite, ovate leaves that have a rough texture. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into small, peppery fruits.

Due to its unique flavor and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant or for its potential culinary uses.
