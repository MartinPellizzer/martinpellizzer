Pleurothallis moschata

Pleurothallis moschata, commonly known as the orchid bee orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and its ability to thrive in high humidity and partial shade. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are often visited by bees and other pollinators. Its inflorescence typically consists of multiple showy, white or pale yellow blooms.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is occasionally cultivated by enthusiasts of rare orchids.
