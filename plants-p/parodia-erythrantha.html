Parodia erythrantha

Parodia erythrantha, commonly known as the red-flowered cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rounded shape and spiny stems. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in rock gardens.
