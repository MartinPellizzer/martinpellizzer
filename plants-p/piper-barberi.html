Piper barberi

Piper barberi, commonly known as the Madagascar pepper or barber's pepper, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruit is a small, three-lobed berry that turns red when mature.

Piper barberi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often grown in tropical gardens.
