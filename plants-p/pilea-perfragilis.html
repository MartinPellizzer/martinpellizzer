Pilea perfragilis

Pilea perfragilis, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features rosettes of waxy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slight variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by spherical, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Its unique foliage and ease of care contribute to its popularity among indoor gardeners.
