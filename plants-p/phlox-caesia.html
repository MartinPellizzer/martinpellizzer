Phlox caesia

Phlox caesia, commonly known as blue phlox, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, clustered flowers that range in color from blue to purple, depending on soil pH. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Phlox caesia is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
