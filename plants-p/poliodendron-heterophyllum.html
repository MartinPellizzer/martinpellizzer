Poliodendron heterophyllum

Poliodendron heterophyllum, commonly known as the African star grass, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and long, narrow leaves that are often blue-green in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pale yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and savanna habitats.

Poliodendron heterophyllum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticultural settings.
