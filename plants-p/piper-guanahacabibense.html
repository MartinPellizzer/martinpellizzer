Piper guanahacabibense

Piper guanahacabibense, commonly known as the Guanahacabibense pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly found in countries like Colombia and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with dark green, heart-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn from green to yellow or red as they mature.

This species is valued for its aromatic leaves and is sometimes used in traditional medicine and as a spice.
