Polystachya boliviensis

Polystachya boliviensis, commonly known as the Bolivian spike orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in montane forests. Its rhizomes are fibrous, and it has linear, grass-like leaves.

Polystachya boliviensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
