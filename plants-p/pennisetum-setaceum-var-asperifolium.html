Pennisetum setaceum var. asperifolium

Pennisetum setaceum var. asperifolium, commonly known as stiff leaf fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It features slender, upright stems with narrow, gray-green leaves that have a rough, textured surface. The plant produces fluffy, silver-white plumes during the summer months, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it suitable for a variety of landscaping applications.
