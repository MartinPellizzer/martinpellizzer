Paxistima schaefferi

Paxistima schaefferi, commonly known as the rock pine or stone pine, is a rare and unique succulent native to the southern United States.

The plant features fleshy, evergreen leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Paxistima schaefferi is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.
