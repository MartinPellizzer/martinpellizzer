Planchonella tomentosa

Planchonella tomentosa, commonly known as the wooly planchonella, is a tropical evergreen tree native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are typically ovate or elliptic in shape. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by edible, fleshy fruits.

The tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
