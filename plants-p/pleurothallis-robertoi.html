Pleurothallis robertoi

Pleurothallis robertoi, commonly known as the Robert's orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, organic-rich substrates.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
