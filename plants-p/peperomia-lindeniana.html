Peperomia lindeniana

Peperomia lindeniana, commonly known as the Lindenberg peperomia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit, with thick, waxy leaves that are typically green or variegated. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or red, arranged in clusters.

Peperomia lindeniana is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
