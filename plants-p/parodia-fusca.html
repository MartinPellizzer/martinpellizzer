Parodia fusca

Parodia fusca, commonly known as the desert rose or sun rose, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense covering of white to pale green spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its green body. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

Parodia fusca is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
