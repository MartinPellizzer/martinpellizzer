Polygala sinisica

Polygala sinisica, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, while its flowers are usually pink or purple in color.

Polygala sinisica is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
