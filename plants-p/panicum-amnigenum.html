Panicum amnigenum

Panicum amnigenum, commonly known as the yellow bluestem, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and bright yellow inflorescence that appears in late summer. The plant typically grows in wetland areas, such as marshes and swamps, and thrives in moist, acidic soils. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is sometimes cultivated in wetland restoration projects.
