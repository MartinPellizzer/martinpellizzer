Palatina sinuata

Palatina sinuata, commonly known as the sinuous palatina, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive sinuous leaf shape and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
