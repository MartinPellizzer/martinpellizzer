Phaseolus lasiocarpus var. balansae

Phaseolus lasiocarpus var. balansae, commonly known as the Balansae scarlet bean, is a climbing legume native to Central America.

It produces vibrant red to orange flowers and is characterized by its hairy, pod-like fruits that mature to a dark brown color. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets and is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional agriculture.

This variety is relatively rare and requires specific conditions to thrive.
