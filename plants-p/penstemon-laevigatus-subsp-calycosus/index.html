Penstemon Laevigatus Subsp. Calycosus

Penstemon laevigatus subsp. calycosus is a striking native plant found in the western united states. it belongs to the gentian family and is known for its vibrant purple and pink flowers. this subspecies thrives in rocky soils and dry environments. it is a popular choice for xeriscaping due to its low water needs. its unique appearance makes it a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
