Phaleria laurifolia var. javanica

Phaleria laurifolia var. javanica, commonly known as the Java privet, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are oval-shaped with a glossy surface and a strong aromatic scent.
