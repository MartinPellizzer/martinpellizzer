Phlomis angrenica

Phlomis angrenica, commonly known as the Caucasian phlomis, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant that typically grows in rocky and dry habitats. The plant features upright, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its flowers are clustered in dense spikes and are usually pale purple to pink in color.

Phlomis angrenica is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in challenging environments.
