Pittosporum dognyense

Pittosporum dognyense, commonly known as the Dungy's pittosporum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pittosporaceae.

It is native to New Zealand and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are glossy and dark green, while its white or cream-colored flowers bloom in late spring to early summer.

Pittosporum dognyense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to a variety of soil types.
