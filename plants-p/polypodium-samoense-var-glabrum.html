Polypodium samoense var. glabrum

Polypodium samoense var. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth shield fern, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage. The fronds are typically leathery and have a smooth texture, which distinguishes it from other varieties of the same species. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often growing on rock surfaces or in forest understories.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
