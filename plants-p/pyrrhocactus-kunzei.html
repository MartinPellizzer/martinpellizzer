Pyrrhocactus kunzei

Pyrrhocactus kunzei, commonly known as the red barrel cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stem that is typically red or pink in color. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Pyrrhocactus kunzei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and hardy nature.
