Persicaria canadensis

Persicaria canadensis, commonly known as Canadian knotweed, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are heart-shaped with serrated edges and are covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in moist environments such as wetlands and stream banks, and is often found in shaded to partially sunny areas.
