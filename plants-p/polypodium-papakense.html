Polypodium papakense

Polypodium papakense, commonly known as the Papakene huperzia, is a fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its leathery, dark green fronds with a distinct midrib and serrated margins. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices.

Polypodium papakense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
