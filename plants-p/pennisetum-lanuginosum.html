Pennisetum lanuginosum

Pennisetum lanuginosum, commonly known as the silky panic grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It features slender, grayish-green leaves with a fine, silky texture that gives it its common name. The plant produces fluffy, silver-gray inflorescences that are visually striking and often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it adaptable to various climates.

Pennisetum lanuginosum is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators like birds and insects.
