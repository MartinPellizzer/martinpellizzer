Phillyrea angustifolia f. rosmarinifolia

Phillyrea angustifolia f. rosmarinifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a glossy green, with a slightly aromatic scent. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by dark blue berries. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its foliage and fragrance contribute to its use in traditional herbal medicine and horticulture.
