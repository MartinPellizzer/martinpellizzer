Polypodium tonkinense

Polypodium tonkinense, commonly known as the Tonkin shield fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its rhizome is often covered with a dense layer of brown, woolly hairs.

Polypodium tonkinense is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
