Phyllanthus tenuicaulis var. tenuicaulis

Phyllanthus tenuicaulis var. tenuicaulis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed phyllanthus, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small shrub characterized by its narrow, upright stems and small, opposite leaves. The leaves are typically elliptical or lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, followed by small, round fruits.

This plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks.
