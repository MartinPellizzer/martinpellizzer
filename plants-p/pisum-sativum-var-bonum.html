Pisum sativum var. bonum

Pisum sativum var. bonum, commonly known as the bonum pea, is a cultivated variety of the common pea.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and high yield of edible pods. The plant typically produces small, round pods that contain several seeds, which are rich in protein and nutrients. It thrives in temperate climates and is often grown for both culinary and ornamental purposes.

The variety is valued for its adaptability and resilience to various environmental conditions.
