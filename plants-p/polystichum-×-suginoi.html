Polystichum × suginoi

Polystichum × suginoi, commonly known as the Japanese forest fern, is a hybrid fern species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The fronds are deeply divided into narrow, lance-shaped segments with a dark green color and a glossy texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
