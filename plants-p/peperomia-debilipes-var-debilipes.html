Peperomia debilipes var. debilipes

Peperomia debilipes var. debilipes, commonly known as the slender peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically a glossy green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container cultivation.
