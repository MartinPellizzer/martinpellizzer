Polystachya zambesiaca var. malilaensis

Polystachya zambesiaca var. malilaensis, commonly known as the Malila mountain orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the Eastern African region.

This terrestrial orchid is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth or rocky areas. The plant has fleshy, strap-like leaves that help it retain moisture in its native habitat.

Its unique floral structure and ecological adaptations make it a distinctive species within its genus.
