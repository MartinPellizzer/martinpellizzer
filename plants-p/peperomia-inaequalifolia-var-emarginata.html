Peperomia inaequalifolia var. emarginata

Peperomia inaequalifolia var. emarginata, commonly known as the zigzag peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features distinctive, wavy-edged leaves that are typically dark green with a silvery underside, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which are followed by tiny, rounded berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage makes it a visually appealing addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
