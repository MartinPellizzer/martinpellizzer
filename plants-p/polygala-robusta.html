Polygala robusta

Polygala robusta, commonly known as the robust milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, sandy or rocky soils. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, pale green or yellowish flowers arranged in racemes. Its leaves are usually narrow and opposite, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Polygala robusta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
