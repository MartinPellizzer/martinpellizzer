Phillyrea hybrida

Phillyrea hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid Phillyrea, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, dark green foliage with small, leathery leaves that are glossy and aromatic. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by small, spherical berries that are typically yellow or orange. Phillyrea hybrida is known for its adaptability to poor soils and drought conditions, making it a hardy plant for landscaping.

It is often used in hedges or as a specimen plant due to its compact growth habit and ornamental value.
