Pentacalia carinata

Pentacalia carinata, commonly known as the zigzag centipede plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features striking, ribbed stems that curve in a zigzag pattern, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming in spring. Its leaves are fleshy and arranged in a spiral, contributing to its unique appearance.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
