Plumbago rhomboidea

Plumbago rhomboidea, commonly known as the rhomboid plumbago, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has slender, woody stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and compact growth habit.
