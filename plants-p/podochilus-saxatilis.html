Podochilus saxatilis

Podochilus saxatilis, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found growing on rocky substrates. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with a faint fragrance. It has a fibrous root system adapted to nutrient-poor soils.

Podochilus saxatilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens.
