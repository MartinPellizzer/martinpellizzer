Peperomia aemula

Peperomia aemula, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery or white-veined pattern that gives it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia aemula is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
