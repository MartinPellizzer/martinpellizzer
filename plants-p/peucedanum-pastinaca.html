Peucedanum pastinaca

Peucedanum pastinaca, commonly known as cow parsley, is a flowering plant in the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are aromatic and often used in herbal remedies. Its seeds are used in traditional medicine for their purported digestive and anti-inflammatory properties.
