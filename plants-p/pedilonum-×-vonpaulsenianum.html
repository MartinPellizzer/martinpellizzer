Pedilonum × vonpaulsenianum

Pedilonum × vonpaulsenianum, commonly known as the hybrid hibiscus, is a botanical hybrid resulting from the cross between *Pedilonum* and *Vonpaulsenia* species.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically display vibrant colors such as red, pink, or yellow. The plant is a shrub or small tree, often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.
