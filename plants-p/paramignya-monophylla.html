Paramignya monophylla

Paramignya monophylla, commonly known as the single-leaf arrowhead, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive single, arrow-shaped leaf that floats on the water surface. The plant has a short, stout stem and produces small, white flowers that bloom above the water. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated in water gardens and ponds.
