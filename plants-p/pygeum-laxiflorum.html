Pygeum laxiflorum

Pygeum laxiflorum, commonly known as the slender-flowered African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Anacardiaceae, which includes plants known for their aromatic properties. The tree is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and small, clustered leaves. Its flowers are white or pale yellow and appear in spring, followed by small, round fruits.
