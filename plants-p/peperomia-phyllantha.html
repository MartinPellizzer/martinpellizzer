Peperomia phyllantha

Peperomia phyllantha, commonly known as the Shiny Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are often dark green with a waxy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters. The plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal water needs. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers moderate temperatures, making it a popular choice for houseplants.

Its ornamental value lies primarily in its attractive foliage rather than its blooms.
