Phrynium riedelianum

Phrynium riedelianum, commonly known as the Riedel's arrowleaf, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking spadices, which are typically green with a yellow or orange spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid lowland forests.

Phrynium riedelianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
