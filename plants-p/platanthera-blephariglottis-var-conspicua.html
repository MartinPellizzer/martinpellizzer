Platanthera blephariglottis var. conspicua

Platanthera blephariglottis var. conspicua, commonly known as the showy lady's tresses, is a perennial orchid native to the eastern United States.

It features a dense, dark green foliage and produces a long, spiraling inflorescence of white to pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wetlands. It is characterized by its unique, fragrant blossoms that bloom in late summer.

The variety *conspicua* is distinguished by its more showy and larger flowers compared to the typical form.
