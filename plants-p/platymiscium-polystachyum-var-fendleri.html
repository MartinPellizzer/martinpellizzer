Platymiscium polystachyum var. fendleri

Platymiscium polystachyum var. fendleri, commonly known as the Fendler's evergreen mahogany, is a tropical tree native to Central America.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and elongated, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by woody, clustered fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland tropical rainforests.

This species is valued for its durable wood and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
