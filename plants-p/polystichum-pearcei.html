Polystichum pearcei

Polystichum pearcei, commonly known as the Townsend's wood fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The fronds are bipinnate, with a distinctive grayish-green color and a silvery underside. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural settings.
