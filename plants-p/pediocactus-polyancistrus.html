Pediocactus polyancistrus

Pediocactus polyancistrus, commonly known as the Mexican apple cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are typically green to blue-green in color. The plant produces large, showy flowers that range from yellow to orange in hue. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its striking appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
