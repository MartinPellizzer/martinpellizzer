Polypodium cyatheifolium

Polypodium cyatheifolium, commonly known as the giant fern or Cyatheafolium fern, is a tropical evergreen fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its large, leathery, compound fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The fronds are also valued for their ornamental appeal in horticulture.
