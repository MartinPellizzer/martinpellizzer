Phaseolus vexillatus

Phaseolus vexillatus, commonly known as the scarlet runner bean, is a climbing legume native to the Americas.

It produces vibrant red or orange flowers and elongated, edible pods that contain kidney-shaped seeds. The plant thrives in warm climates and requires full sun for optimal growth. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three heart-shaped leaflets.

This species is valued for both its ornamental appeal and culinary uses.
