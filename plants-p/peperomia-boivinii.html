Peperomia boivinii

Peperomia boivinii, commonly known as the Boivin's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pink, which are clustered in spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia boivinii is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and occasional fertilization during the growing season.
