Pyrrothrix ruficaulis

Pyrrothrix ruficaulis, commonly known as the red-stemmed pyrrothrix, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red, often attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Pyrrothrix ruficaulis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
