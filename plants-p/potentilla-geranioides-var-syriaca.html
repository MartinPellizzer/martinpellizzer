Potentilla geranioides var. syriaca

Potentilla geranioides var. syriaca, commonly known as Syrian cinquefoil, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has finely divided, serrated leaves that are often covered in a fuzzy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
