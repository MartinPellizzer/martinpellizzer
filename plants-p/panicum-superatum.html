Panicum superatum

Panicum superatum, commonly known as the tall panic grass, is a perennial grass species native to the warmer regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and feathery plumes that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open areas such as fields, roadsides, and disturbed soils, thriving in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a bluish-green color that becomes more prominent as the plant matures.

Panicum superatum is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and drought tolerance.
