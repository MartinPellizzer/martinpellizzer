Phyllanthus flagelliformis var. genuinus

Phyllanthus flagelliformis var. genuinus, commonly known as the slender leaf phyllanthus, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves that are often arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This species is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
