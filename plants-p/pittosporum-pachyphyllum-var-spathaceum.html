Pittosporum pachyphyllum var. spathaceum

Pittosporum pachyphyllum var. spathaceum, commonly known as the Spathaceum Pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are typically elliptical and have a leathery texture, while the bark is rough and fissured. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
