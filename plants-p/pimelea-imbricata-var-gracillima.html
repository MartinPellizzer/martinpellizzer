Pimelea imbricata var. gracillima

Pimelea imbricata var. gracillima, commonly known as the slender yellow pimelea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and open forests.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively low maintenance in cultivation.
