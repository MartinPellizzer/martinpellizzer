Peperomia subflaccida var. subgeminispica

Peperomia subflaccida var. subgeminispica, commonly known as the twin-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, ovate to elliptical leaves that are often slightly curled and have a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale green to white flowers, which are typically borne on short, erect spikes. Its foliage is characterized by a distinctive pattern of light and dark green veins.

This variety is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and drought tolerance.
