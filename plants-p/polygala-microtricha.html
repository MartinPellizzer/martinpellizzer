Polygala microtricha

Polygala microtricha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved milk-vetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the genus Polygala, which is part of the family Polygalaceae. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its small, delicate flowers are usually white or pale yellow, and it produces small, bean-like fruits.
