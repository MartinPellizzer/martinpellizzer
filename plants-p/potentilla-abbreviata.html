Potentilla abbreviata

Potentilla abbreviata, commonly known as the short-stemmed cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches heights of less than 30 centimeters. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, consisting of five oval-shaped leaflets, and are covered in fine hairs.

Potentilla abbreviata thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.
