Ptyssiglottis staminodifera

Ptyssiglottis staminodifera, commonly known as the staminodifera orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, delicate flowers with intricate patterns. Its leaves are typically ovate and arranged in a basal rosette, while the inflorescence consists of a single spike bearing several flowers.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
