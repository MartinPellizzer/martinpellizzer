Pisonia olfersiana var. nitida

Pisonia olfersiana var. nitida, commonly known as the silver flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are typically glossy and dark green, with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, round, orange-red fruits that contain numerous seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to warm, humid climates.
