Pilea miconiifolia

Pilea miconiifolia, commonly known as the "Chinese money plant," is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its round, fleshy leaves that resemble small coins, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in clusters. It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

Pilea miconiifolia is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and its ability to adapt well to indoor environments.
