Poa neesii

Poa neesii, commonly known as the Nees' bentgrass, is a species of grass native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in alpine environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Poa neesii is often found in rocky or gravelly soils, and it plays a role in stabilizing soil in these harsh habitats.

Its botanical features make it an important component of alpine ecosystems.
