Pilosella Officinarum Subsp. Saussureoides

Pilosella officinarum subsp. saussureoides is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It is characterized by its small, white flowers and hairy leaves that help it survive in harsh mountain environments. This subspecies is often found in rocky soils and open meadows at high altitudes. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique plant from habitat loss and climate change.
