Procris montana

Procris montana, commonly known as the mountain helleborine, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces showy, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive spurred lip, typical of orchids. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and alpine meadows.

Procris montana is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
