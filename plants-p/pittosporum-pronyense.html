Pittosporum pronyense

Pittosporum pronyense, commonly known as the Prony's pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 meters. The plant produces fragrant white or pale yellow flowers, which are clustered in umbels. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a leathery texture with a glossy surface.

The fruit is a small, dark berry that contains several seeds.
