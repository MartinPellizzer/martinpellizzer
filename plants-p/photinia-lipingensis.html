Photinia lipingensis

Photinia lipingensis, commonly known as the Liping photinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its ornamental red or pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its attractive foliage and flowers, it is often cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.
