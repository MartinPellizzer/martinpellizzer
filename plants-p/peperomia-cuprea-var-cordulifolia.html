Peperomia cuprea var. cordulifolia

Peperomia cuprea var. cordulifolia, commonly known as the red peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with prominent veins and a reddish tint on the undersides, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia cuprea var. cordulifolia is relatively low-maintenance and makes an excellent indoor plant due to its adaptability and ornamental appeal.
