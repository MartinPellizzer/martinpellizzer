Piper cabaianum var. obliquifolium

Piper cabaianum var. obliquifolium, commonly known as the zigzag pepper or obliquifoliate pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its unique, zigzagging leaves that give it its common name. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by small, peppery fruits.

This variety is often cultivated for its aromatic leaves and potential medicinal properties.
