Pelargonium × nubilum

Pelargonium × nubilum, commonly known as the storm cloud geranium, is a hybrid plant that exhibits unique botanical characteristics.

It is a herbaceous perennial with velvety, dark green leaves and showy, bi-colored flowers that range from pink to white. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers. Its flowers are typically borne in clusters and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Pelargonium × nubilum is noted for its adaptability and ornamental value in horticultural settings.
