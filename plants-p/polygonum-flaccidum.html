Polygonum flaccidum

Polygonum flaccidum, commonly known as the flaccid knotweed, is a perennial herb native to temperate regions of Asia and North America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants commonly referred to as buckwheats and knotweeds. The plant features erect, branched stems covered with soft, hairy foliage. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences.

Polygonum flaccidum is often found in moist, disturbed soils along stream banks and in wetlands.
