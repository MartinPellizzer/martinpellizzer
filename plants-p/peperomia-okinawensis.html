Peperomia okinawensis

Peperomia okinawensis, commonly known as the Okinawa peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the Ryukyu Islands of Japan.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its ability to tolerate low light conditions and minimal water requirements.
