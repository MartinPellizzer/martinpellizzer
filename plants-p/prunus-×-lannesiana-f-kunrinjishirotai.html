Prunus × lannesiana f. kunrinjishirotai

Prunus × lannesiana f. kunrinjishirotai, commonly known as the Japanese cherry blossom or sakura, is a cultivar of the Prunus genus.

It is characterized by its delicate, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant is a deciduous tree with a rounded shape and smooth, grey bark. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This ornamental tree is highly valued for its aesthetic beauty and cultural significance in Japan.
