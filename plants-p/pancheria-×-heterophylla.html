Pancheria × heterophylla

Pancheria × heterophylla, commonly known as the variable pancheria, is a hybrid plant species within the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its variable leaf morphology, which can range from broad and heart-shaped to narrow and lanceolate. The plant typically produces striking, colorful inflorescences that consist of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal.

Pancheria × heterophylla is native to tropical regions of the Americas and is well-suited for greenhouse or indoor cultivation.
