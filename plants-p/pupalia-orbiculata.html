Pupalia orbiculata

Pupalia orbiculata, commonly known as the globe thistle, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region and parts of central and southern Europe. The plant is characterized by its spherical, spiky blue or purple flower heads, which are surrounded by stiff, bract-like leaves. Its foliage consists of narrow, gray-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Pupalia orbiculata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
