Primula × floerkeana

Primula × floerkeana, commonly known as the hybrid primrose, is a perennial plant belonging to the Primulaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Primula vulgaris and Primula veris, resulting in a plant with attractive, showy flowers. The foliage is typically heart-shaped and has a waxy texture, while the flowers come in a range of colors, including white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.

The plant is also valued for its ability to naturalize and form dense clusters in suitable conditions.
