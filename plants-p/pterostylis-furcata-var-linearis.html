Pterostylis furcata var. linearis

Pterostylis furcata var. linearis, commonly known as the linear spider-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It features a slender, leafless stem with a single, narrow, greenish-white flower that resembles a spider in appearance. The plant produces a single, elongated, linear flower spike, which is characteristic of the spider-orchid group. It thrives in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests and understoreys.

The species is known for its delicate structure and is often found growing on the forest floor or among leaf litter.
