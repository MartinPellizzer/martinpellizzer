Populus simonii f. liaotungensis

Populus simonii f. liaotungensis, commonly known as the Liaotung poplar, is a rare and distinctive variant of the poplar genus native to northern China.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and distinctive bark that becomes fissured with age. This cultivar is often found in mountainous regions and is adapted to cooler, temperate climates. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in regions with suitable growing conditions.

The plant plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and contributing to soil stabilization.
