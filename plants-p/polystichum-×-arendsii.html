Polystichum × arendsii

Polystichum × arendsii, commonly known as the Arends' shield fern, is a hybrid fern species resulting from the cross between Polystichum setiferum and Polystichum acutiusculum.

It is characterized by its dense, upright fronds that are typically dark green with a silvery-white underside. The plant is native to the Netherlands and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and prefers well-drained, humus-rich soil.

Polystichum × arendsii is a hardy perennial that can grow up to 60 cm tall, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.
