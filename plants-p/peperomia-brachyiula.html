Peperomia brachyiula

Peperomia brachyiula, commonly known as the dwarf peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which are often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
