Pentacalia gonocaulos

Pentacalia gonocaulos, commonly known as the showy pentacalia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
