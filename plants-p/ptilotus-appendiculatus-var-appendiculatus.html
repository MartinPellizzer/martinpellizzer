Ptilotus appendiculatus var. appendiculatus

Ptilotus appendiculatus var. appendiculatus, commonly known as the desert lily, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Australia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has hairy stems and leaves adapted to conserve water in dry environments. Its flowers are typically borne in clusters at the ends of the stems.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal dunes and sandy plains.
