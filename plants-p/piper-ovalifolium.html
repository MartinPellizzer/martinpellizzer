Piper ovalifolium

Piper ovalifolium, commonly known as the oval-leaf pepper or *Piper ovalifolium*, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, Peru, and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, clustered green berries that turn red when mature. It is often found in humid, lowland forests and thrives in shaded, moist environments.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
