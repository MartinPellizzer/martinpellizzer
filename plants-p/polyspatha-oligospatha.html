Polyspatha oligospatha

Polyspatha oligospatha, commonly known as the narrow-leaf arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial native to the wetlands of North America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a whorled pattern along its stems. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its roots are fibrous and often used in traditional medicine by indigenous communities.

Polyspatha oligospatha thrives in moist, swampy environments and is often found in marshes and along the edges of ponds.
