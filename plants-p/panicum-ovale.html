Panicum ovale

Panicum ovale, commonly known as smooth panicgrass, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the southern United States.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, oval-shaped spikelets that are often arranged in dense, panicle-like clusters. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a bluish-green coloration.

Panicum ovale is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
