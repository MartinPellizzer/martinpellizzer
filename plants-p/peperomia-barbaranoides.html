Peperomia barbaranoides

Peperomia barbaranoides, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to Brazil.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are typically silver or gray-green in color, with a textured surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, usually in shades of green or red, which are followed by round, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia barbaranoides is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
