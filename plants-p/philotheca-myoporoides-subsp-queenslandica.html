Philotheca myoporoides subsp. queenslandica

Philotheca myoporoides subsp.

queenslandica, commonly known as the Queensland myopor, is a small shrub native to eastern Australia. It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance.

The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and dry open forests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
