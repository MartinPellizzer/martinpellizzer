Phalacrodiscus arvensis

Phalacrodiscus arvensis, commonly known as the field starwort, is a small flowering plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, fleshy leaves and produces small, white or pinkish flowers with five petals. The species is also notable for its medicinal properties, having been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
