Phyllanthus dictyospermus

Phyllanthus dictyospermus, commonly known as the African spurge, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, characterized by its simple, opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
