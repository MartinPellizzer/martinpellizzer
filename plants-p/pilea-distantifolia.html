Pilea distantifolia

Pilea distantifolia, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or pilea plant, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including China, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically light green with a slight variegation. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by dark, berry-like fruits.

Pilea distantifolia is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
