Potentilla crantzii

Potentilla crantzii, commonly known as the yellow cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or open areas. It has compound leaves with five leaflets and produces small, rounded fruits.

Potentilla crantzii is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in suitable conditions.
