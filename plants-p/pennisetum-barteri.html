Pennisetum barteri

Pennisetum barteri, commonly known as blue oat grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It features slender, silver-blue foliage that adds a striking visual contrast to gardens. The plant produces fluffy, oat-like seed heads that are both ornamental and attractive to birds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a versatile choice for landscapes.

Pennisetum barteri is drought-tolerant and low-maintenance, suitable for xeriscaping and erosion control.
