Phyllostachys flavescens-inversa

Phyllostachys flavescens-inversa, commonly known as the reverse yellow bamboo, is a unique variant of the yellow bamboo species.

It is characterized by its striking reverse coloration, where the culms display a yellow hue on the outer surface and a greenish tint on the inner part. This plant is native to China and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. It is a clump-forming bamboo that can grow up to several meters in height, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

Its distinctive appearance makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and specialty plant collections.
