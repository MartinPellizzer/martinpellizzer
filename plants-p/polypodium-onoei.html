Polypodium onoei

Polypodium onoei, commonly known as the Japanese wall fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is native to East Asia, particularly Japan, Korea, and China, where it thrives in shaded, moist environments. The plant has long, slender fronds that are often recurved, and it produces sporangia on the underside of its leaves. It is typically found growing on rocks or tree trunks, showcasing its epiphytic nature.

The fronds are evergreen and can reach lengths of up to 30 centimeters, making it a distinctive and ornamental plant in its native habitat.
