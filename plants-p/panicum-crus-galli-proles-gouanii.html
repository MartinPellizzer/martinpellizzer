Panicum crus-galli proles gouanii

Panicum crus-galli proles gouanii, commonly known as the Gouanii panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in open woodlands or grassy areas. Its leaves are narrow and have a slight rough texture, while the flowers are wind-pollinated.

This species is often used in ecological studies due to its adaptability and role in soil stabilization.
