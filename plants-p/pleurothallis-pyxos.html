Pleurothallis pyxos

Pleurothallis pyxos, commonly known as the pyxos orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich substrates.

Due to its delicate nature, it is often cultivated in specialized greenhouses or terrariums.
