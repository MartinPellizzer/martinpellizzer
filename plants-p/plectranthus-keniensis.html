Plectranthus keniensis

Plectranthus keniensis, commonly known as Kenyan mint, is a flowering plant native to Kenya.

It is characterized by its aromatic, fragrant leaves that emit a mint-like scent. The plant typically grows as a shrub with opposite, ovate to lance-shaped leaves. It produces small, tubular flowers that are usually violet or purple in color.

Plectranthus keniensis is often used in traditional medicine and as a culinary herb due to its aromatic properties.
