Pteroneurum javanicum

Pteroneurum javanicum, commonly known as the Java pteroneurum, is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in shaded forest environments.
