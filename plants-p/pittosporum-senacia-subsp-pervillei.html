Pittosporum senacia subsp. pervillei

Pittosporum senacia subsp.

pervillei, commonly known as the New Zealand pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand. It features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath.

The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by bright orange berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
