Poa kingii

Poa kingii, commonly known as King's grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to New Zealand and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, two-lobed flowers. Poa kingii is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forest margins and open areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
