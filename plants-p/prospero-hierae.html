Prospero hierae

Prospero hierae, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom on long, slender stalks rising from water. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green and glossy. Its flowers can be pink, white, or yellow, depending on the species.

The sacred lotus is known for its symbolic significance in various cultures and its ability to thrive in aquatic environments.
