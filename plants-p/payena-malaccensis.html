Payena malaccensis

Payena malaccensis, commonly known as the Malacca payena, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and lance-shaped leaves. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
