Pinus densiflora f. liaotungensis

Pinus densiflora f. liaotungensis, commonly known as the Liaotung pine, is a variant of the dense-flowered pine native to eastern China.

It is characterized by its slender, often twisted trunk and needle-like leaves that are shorter and more densely packed compared to the typical species. The plant produces cone-like structures that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions with a temperate climate.

This form is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
