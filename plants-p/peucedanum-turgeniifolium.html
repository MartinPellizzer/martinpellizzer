Peucedanum turgeniifolium

Peucedanum turgeniifolium, commonly known as the Turkestan cowparsley, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in umbrella-shaped clusters.
