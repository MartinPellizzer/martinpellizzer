Pleurothallis cremasta

Pleurothallis cremasta, commonly known as the golden tassel orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often resembling tassels. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and surrounding substrate.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
