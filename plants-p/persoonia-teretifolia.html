Persoonia teretifolia

Persoonia teretifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved geebung, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne in the axils of the leaves. Persoonia teretifolia is often found in heathlands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
