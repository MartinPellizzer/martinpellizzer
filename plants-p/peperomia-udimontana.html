Peperomia udimontana

Peperomia udimontana, commonly known as the Udimontan peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features small, ovate to lance-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green, often with a slight pubescence. The plant produces clusters of small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia udimontana is relatively low-maintenance, requiring minimal watering and moderate humidity levels.
