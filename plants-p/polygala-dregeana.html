Polygala dregeana

Polygala dregeana, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, often zigzag-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, with five petals. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Polygala dregeana is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
