Petaloma album

Petaloma album, commonly known as the white petaloma, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, white petals that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as along forest floors or in wetlands. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a smooth, waxy surface.

Petaloma album is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
