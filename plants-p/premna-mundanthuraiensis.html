Premna mundanthuraiensis

Premna mundanthuraiensis, commonly known as the Nilavu plant, is a flowering shrub native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often aromatic. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue or violet in color. It is often found growing in moist, shaded areas of tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
