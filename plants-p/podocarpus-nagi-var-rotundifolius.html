Podocarpus nagi var. rotundifolius

Podocarpus nagi var. rotundifolius, commonly known as the Japanese podocarp or round-leaf podocarp, is a species of coniferous evergreen tree native to Japan.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, rounded leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain its seeds, typical of the Podocarpaceae family. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
