Protea formosa

Protea formosa, commonly known as the silver protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence composed of many small, white to pinkish flowers arranged in a spherical or conical shape. The plant thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation. It produces a unique, urn-shaped seed pod that splits open to release its seeds.

Protea formosa is a popular ornamental plant due to its beauty and longevity in floral arrangements.
