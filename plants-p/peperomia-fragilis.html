Peperomia fragilis

Peperomia fragilis, commonly known as the brittle peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a slight waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Due to its delicate leaves, it requires careful handling to avoid damage.
