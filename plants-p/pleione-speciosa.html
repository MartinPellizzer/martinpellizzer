Pleione speciosa

Pleione speciosa, commonly known as the showy pleione, is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant features a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients, supporting its growth in cool, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually broad and strap-like, and it thrives in high-altitude forests with high humidity.

Due to its beauty and rarity, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
