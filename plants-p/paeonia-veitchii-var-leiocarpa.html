Paeonia veitchii var. leiocarpa

Paeonia veitchii var. leiocarpa, commonly known as the Chinese peony or leiocarpa peony, is a variant of the peony species native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and sometimes red. The plant is a herbaceous perennial with thick, fleshy roots and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. Its leaves are palmately compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a striking visual element to the landscape.
