Peperomia sclerophylla

Peperomia sclerophylla, commonly known as the glossy peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture, adapted to retain moisture in dry environments. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, followed by reddish-brown berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant.
