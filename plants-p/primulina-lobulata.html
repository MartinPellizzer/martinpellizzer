Primulina lobulata

Primulina lobulata, commonly known as the velvet leaf.

This species is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a prominent midrib. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of pink or white, often clustered in inflorescences.

It is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
