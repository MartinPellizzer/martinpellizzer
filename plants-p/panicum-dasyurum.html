Panicum dasyurum

Panicum dasyurum, commonly known as the rough bluestem, is a perennial grass native to Australia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and woodlands. The plant features wiry, erect stems and narrow, blue-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of a dense, cylindrical spikelet that produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.

Panicum dasyurum plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small animals and insects.
