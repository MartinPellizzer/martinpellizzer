Pyrus aucuparia proles glabrata

Pyrus aucuparia proles glabrata, commonly known as the smooth-leafed European pear, is a cultivar of the European pear species.

It is characterized by its smooth, waxy leaves and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in early spring. The fruit is typically small, round, and has a sweet, juicy flesh with a slight tartness. It is often grown for its ornamental value as well as for its edible fruit.

This plant prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for temperate climates.
