Piper riodocense

Piper riodocense, commonly known as the Riodocense pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit and has small, heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its fruit is a small, round berry that contains a single seed, and it is often used in traditional medicine.

The plant's botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and pharmacological studies.
