Pyrethrum willkommii

Pyrethrum willkommii, commonly known as the African pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant produces a natural insecticide called pyrethrin, which is extracted from its flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for cultivation in arid regions.

Pyrethrum willkommii is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological benefits in pest control.
