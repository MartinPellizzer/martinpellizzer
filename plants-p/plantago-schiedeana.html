Plantago schiedeana

Plantago schiedeana, commonly known as the Schiede's plantain, is a species of flowering plant in the family Plantaginaceae.

It is native to regions of South America, particularly in countries like Argentina and Chile. The plant is characterized by its broad, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. It produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, typically found in the axils of the leaves.

This species is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky areas.
