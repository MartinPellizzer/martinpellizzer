Petalanisia

Petalanisia, commonly known as the flame violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to orange, resembling the petals of a violet. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 3 feet. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a glossy green surface.

Petalanisia thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires full to partial sunlight for optimal growth.
