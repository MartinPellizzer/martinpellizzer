Pittosporum nervosum

Pittosporum nervosum, commonly known as the slender pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped leaves with a prominent midrib, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by small, woody fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

Pittosporum nervosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
