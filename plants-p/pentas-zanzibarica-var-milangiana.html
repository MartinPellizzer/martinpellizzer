Pentas zanzibarica var. milangiana

Pentas zanzibarica var. milangiana, commonly known as the Zanzibar pentas, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, star-shaped flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant produces showy inflorescences that bloom throughout the year, making it a popular ornamental species. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often used in gardens and as a container plant.

Its botanical features include opposite, ovate leaves and a compact growth habit.
