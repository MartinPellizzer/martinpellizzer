Philodendron dolosum

Philodendron dolosum, commonly known as the false arrowhead plant, is a tropical climbing vine native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are usually dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers enclosed in a spathe, which is typical of the arum family. Philodendron dolosum thrives in humid, warm environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

It is relatively easy to care for, requiring bright indirect light and regular watering.
