Pleurothallis chloroleuca

Pleurothallis chloroleuca, commonly known as the green-leaved orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate appearance. The plant typically features strap-like leaves that are a vibrant green, giving it its common name. Its flowers are small and often white or pale yellow, clustered in a raceme.

Pleurothallis chloroleuca thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in the understory of rainforests.
