Palhinhaea suffruticosa

Palhinhaea suffruticosa, commonly known as the Brazilian broom.

It is a shrub native to Brazil, characterized by its small, narrow leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. It has a compact growth habit and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.

Palhinhaea suffruticosa is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
