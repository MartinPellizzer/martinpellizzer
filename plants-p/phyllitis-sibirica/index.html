Phyllitis Sibirica

Phyllitis sibirica is a small, delicate fern native to the cold regions of Siberia and northern Asia. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found along streams or in rocky crevices. Its unique, fan-shaped fronds make it easily recognizable among other fern species. Despite its modest size, it plays an important role in its ecosystem by providing habitat for small insects and microorganisms. This plant is a symbol of resilience, surviving in harsh climates where few others can grow.
