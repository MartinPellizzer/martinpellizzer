Phyllostachys quilioi var. castilloniholochrysa

Phyllostachys quilioi var. castilloniholochrysa, commonly known as the golden bamboo, is a variant of the giant bamboo species native to China.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow culms that contrast beautifully with its green leaves. The plant is a fast-growing perennial, often reaching heights of up to 10 meters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

Its unique coloration and structural form make it a popular choice for gardeners and horticulturists seeking visual interest in their plantings.
