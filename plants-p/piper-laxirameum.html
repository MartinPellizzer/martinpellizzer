Piper laxirameum

Piper laxirameum, commonly known as the slender pipevine, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Piperaceae, which includes many species of flowering plants known for their aromatic properties. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by elongated, dark blue berries. Its leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape, with a glossy surface and a distinct aromatic scent.

Piper laxirameum is often found in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests.
