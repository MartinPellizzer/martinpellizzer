Peperomia lasiostigma var. carnosa

Peperomia lasiostigma var. carnosa, commonly known as the velvety peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, velvety leaves that are typically dark green with a slight reddish or purple tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are not particularly showy but are characteristic of the peperomia genus. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among indoor gardeners for its attractive foliage and ease of care.
