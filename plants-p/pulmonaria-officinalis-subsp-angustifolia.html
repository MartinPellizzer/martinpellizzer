Pulmonaria officinalis subsp. angustifolia

Pulmonaria officinalis subsp.

angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaved lungwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It features basal leaves that are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a distinctive pattern of white and green spots.

The plant produces upright flowering stems bearing clusters of bell-shaped pink or violet flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and meadows.

Its ornamental value and historical use in traditional medicine make it a notable species in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
