Phyllanthus nigrescens

Phyllanthus nigrescens, commonly known as black nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and dark green, waxy leaves. It produces small, black berries that are often used in traditional medicine.

The species is known for its adaptability to various soil types and can thrive in both moist and dry environments.
