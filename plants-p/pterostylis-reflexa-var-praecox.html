Pterostylis reflexa var. praecox

Pterostylis reflexa var. praecox, commonly known as the early reflexed helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its small, greenish-yellow flowers that resemble a helmet, with a distinctive reflexed lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests and understoreys. Its roots are fibrous, and it produces a single, erect stem with a few leaves.

The species is notable for its early flowering period, often blooming in late winter or early spring.
