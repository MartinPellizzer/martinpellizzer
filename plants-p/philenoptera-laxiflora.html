Philenoptera laxiflora

Philenoptera laxiflora, commonly known as the slender-leaved plectranthus, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Philenoptera laxiflora is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and ease of cultivation.
