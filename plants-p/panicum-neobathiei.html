Panicum neobathiei

Panicum neobathiei, commonly known as the Neobathie Panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to the grasslands of South America.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, two-lobed spikelets. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, adapted to withstand arid conditions.

Panicum neobathiei is often found in disturbed soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in landscaping.
