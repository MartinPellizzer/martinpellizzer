Piper belterraense

Piper belterraense, commonly known as the Belterra pepper vine, is a climbing plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into elongated, fleshy fruits. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a glossy surface.

The plant is characterized by its vigorous growth and is often found in humid, shaded environments.
