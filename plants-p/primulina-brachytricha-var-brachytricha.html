Primulina brachytricha var. brachytricha

Primulina brachytricha var. brachytricha, commonly known as the compact primulina, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit, with thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle. It produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming during the warmer months.

This species is popular among succulent enthusiasts due to its ease of cultivation and attractive appearance.
