Pittosporum rehderianum

Pittosporum rehderianum, commonly known as the Rehder's pittosporum, is a species native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 meters. The plant features elliptical, dark green leaves with a glossy surface and a distinctive aromatic scent. Its flowers are white and fragrant, appearing in clusters, and it produces small, fleshy berries that are edible for some bird species.

This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.
