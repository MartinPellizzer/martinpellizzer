Pennisetum henryanum var. pluristylum

Pennisetum henryanum var. pluristylum, commonly known as purple moondrop grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to southern Africa.

It features tall, arching stems with silvery-blue foliage and striking purple-tinted flower plumes that appear in late summer. The plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established. Its feathery appearance adds visual interest to gardens, and it is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

It prefers well-drained soil and is suitable for zones 6-9.
