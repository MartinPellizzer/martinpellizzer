Pleurothallis tarantula

Pleurothallis tarantula, commonly known as the tarantula orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees or other plants in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow, with a distinctive, intricate structure.

Pleurothallis tarantula is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
