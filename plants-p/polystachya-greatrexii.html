Polystachya greatrexii

Polystachya greatrexii, commonly known as the Greatrex's spikegrass, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that bloom during the wet season. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or rocky outcrops. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its overall appearance as a grass-like orchid.

Polystachya greatrexii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique floral structure.
