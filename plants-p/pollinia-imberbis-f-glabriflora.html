Pollinia imberbis f. glabriflora

Pollinia imberbis f. glabriflora, commonly known as the smooth-flowered form of the genus Pollinia, is a unique variant characterized by its reduced floral structures.

This plant belongs to the orchid family and exhibits specialized pollination mechanisms typical of orchids. The inflorescence is compact, with flowers that are smaller and less hairy compared to the typical form. Its pollinia are adapted for attachment to specific pollinators, enhancing reproductive success.

The botanical features of this form make it an interesting subject for studies on plant adaptation and evolution.
