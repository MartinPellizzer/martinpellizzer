Platyaechmea tillandsioides

Platyaechmea tillandsioides, commonly known as the silver star bromeliad, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to collect water. The plant's foliage is covered in a dense layer of fine, silvery-white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with high humidity.

Platyaechmea tillandsioides is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking visual appeal and ease of care.
