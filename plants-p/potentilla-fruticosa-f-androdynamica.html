Potentilla fruticosa f. androdynamica

Potentilla fruticosa f. androdynamica, commonly known as the Androdynamic cinquefoil, is a variant of the shrub Potentilla fruticosa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, rounded form. The plant typically produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are usually oval to elliptic in shape, with a slightly hairy surface.

This form is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a ground cover due to its hardy nature.
