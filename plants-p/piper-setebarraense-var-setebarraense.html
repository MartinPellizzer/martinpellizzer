Piper setebarraense var. setebarraense

Piper setebarraense var. setebarraense, commonly known as the Setebarra pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are borne in clusters, and it produces small, peppery fruits.

This variety is valued for its unique flavor and is used in traditional cuisines and herbal remedies.
