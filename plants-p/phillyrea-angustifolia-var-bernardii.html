Phillyrea angustifolia var. bernardii

Phillyrea angustifolia var. bernardii, commonly known as the Bernard's narrowleaf Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces white, fragrant flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, woody fruits. It is evergreen and thrives in well-drained soils, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and ornamental plant suitable for xeriscaping and coastal gardens.
