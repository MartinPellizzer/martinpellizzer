Pulicaria steinbergii

Pulicaria steinbergii, commonly known as the desert groundsel, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It features silver-gray foliage with small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a compact, mat-forming habit, making it well-suited for rocky or sandy soils. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
