Parsonsia variabilis

Parsonsia variabilis, commonly known as the variable flame tree, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, orange-red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly serrated edge.
