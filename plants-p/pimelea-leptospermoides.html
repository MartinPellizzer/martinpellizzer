Pimelea leptospermoides

Pimelea leptospermoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pimelea, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a leathery texture, and it is often found in dry, sandy soils.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its compact growth and seasonal blooms.
