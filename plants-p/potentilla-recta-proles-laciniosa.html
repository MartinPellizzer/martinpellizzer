Potentilla recta proles laciniosa

Potentilla recta proles laciniosa, commonly known as the lacecap potentilla, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, lacy appearance, with small white or pink flowers clustered in spherical inflorescences. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is native to regions of Europe and western Asia. Its foliage consists of narrow, serrated leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its compact growth and seasonal blooms.
