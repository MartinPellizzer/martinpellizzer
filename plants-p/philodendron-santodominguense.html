Philodendron santodominguense

Philodendron santodominguense, commonly known as the Santa Domingo philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to the Caribbean.

It features heart-shaped, glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and green spaces.
