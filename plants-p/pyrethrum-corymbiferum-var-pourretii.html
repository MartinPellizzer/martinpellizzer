Pyrethrum corymbiferum var. pourretii

Pyrethrum corymbiferum var. pourretii, commonly known as the African pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces dense clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers. The plant is notable for its high concentration of pyrethrin, a natural insecticide used in organic pest control. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and container planting.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant blooms and long-lasting flowers.
