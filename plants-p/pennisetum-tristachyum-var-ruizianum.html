Pennisetum tristachyum var. ruizianum

Pennisetum tristachyum var. ruizianum, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to southern Africa.

It features tall, arching clumps of slender, grass-like leaves that turn a vibrant purple hue in the fall. The plant produces fluffy, silver-white plumes that add a dramatic visual element to gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for landscape design.

Its drought tolerance and ability to attract birds and butterflies further enhance its ecological value.
