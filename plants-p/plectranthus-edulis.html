Plectranthus edulis

Plectranthus edulis, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant produces small, velvety leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate sunlight to flourish.

Plectranthus edulis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is widely cultivated as a houseplant.
