Prosthechea regentii

Prosthechea regentii, commonly known as the royal orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, large, and showy flowers that often display vibrant colors such as red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its natural habitat.

Prosthechea regentii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or warm climates.
