Peperomia balsapuertana

Peperomia balsapuertana, commonly known as the Balsapuerto peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Central America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purple hue on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Peperomia balsapuertana is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
