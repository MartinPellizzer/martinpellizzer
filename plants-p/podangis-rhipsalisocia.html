Podangis rhipsalisocia

Podangis rhipsalisocia, commonly known as the silver star cactus, is a unique epiphytic cactus native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features a compact, branching structure with cylindrical stems that are covered in a dense layer of white, woolly hairs. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and white, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are reduced to small, scale-like structures, and it thrives in bright, indirect light with well-draining soil.

Podangis rhipsalisocia is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
