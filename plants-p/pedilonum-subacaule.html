Pedilonum subacaule

Pedilonum subacaule, commonly known as the slender pedilonum, is a unique member of the orchid family.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and distinctive, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, pendulous blooms that are usually white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its delicate nature, it requires careful cultivation to maintain its health and floral display.
