Pothos acuminatissimus

Pothos acuminatissimus, commonly known as the marble queen pothos, is a trailing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a variegated pattern of light and dark green, giving it a striking appearance. The plant thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot. Its aerial roots allow it to climb and attach to surfaces, making it suitable for hanging baskets or shelves.

Overall, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
