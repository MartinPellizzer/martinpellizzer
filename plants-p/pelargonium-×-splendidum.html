Pelargonium × splendidum

Pelargonium × splendidum, commonly known as the splendid geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, five-petaled flowers that come in a range of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically has a trailing or upright growth habit, with velvety, lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Native to South Africa, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Its ornamental value is further enhanced by its ability to produce flowers throughout the growing season.
