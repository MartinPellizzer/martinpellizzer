Perrierbambus tsarasaotrensis

Perrierbambus tsarasaotrensis, commonly known as the Madagascar bamboo, is a unique grass-like plant native to the island of Madagascar.

It belongs to the Bambusoideae subfamily and is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and dense, grass-like foliage. The plant is adapted to the tropical and subtropical climates of its native habitat, thriving in well-drained soils.
