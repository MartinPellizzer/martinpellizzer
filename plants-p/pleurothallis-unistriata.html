Pleurothallis unistriata

Pleurothallis unistriata, commonly known as the single-striated pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant prefers shaded, humid environments and is often found growing epiphytically on trees in the understory of rainforests. Its leaves are usually green and arranged in a rosette pattern, and it has a short, stout rhizome.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
