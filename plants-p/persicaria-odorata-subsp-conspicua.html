Persicaria odorata subsp. conspicua

Persicaria odorata subsp.

conspicua, commonly known as the Vietnamese enoki or sweet flag, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers.

The plant produces distinctive, elongated, fleshy fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near water bodies.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ornamental and medicinal purposes.
