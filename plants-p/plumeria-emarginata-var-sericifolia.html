Plumeria emarginata var. sericifolia

Plumeria emarginata var. sericifolia, commonly known as the silky plumeria, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The leaves are ovate and have a soft, velvety texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal. This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
