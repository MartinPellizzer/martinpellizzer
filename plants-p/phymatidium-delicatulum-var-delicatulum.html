Phymatidium delicatulum var. delicatulum

Phymatidium delicatulum var. delicatulum, commonly known as the delicate phymatidium, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features delicate, grass-like leaves that are often tinged with a pale blue or silver hue. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually appearing in clusters along the stems.

The species is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in nutrient-rich soils.
