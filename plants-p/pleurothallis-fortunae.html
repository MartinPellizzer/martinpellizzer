Pleurothallis fortunae

Pleurothallis fortunae, commonly known as the Fortune's pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with intricate patterns. Its leaves are typically narrow and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
