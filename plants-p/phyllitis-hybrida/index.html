Phyllitis Hybrida

Phyllitis hybrida, also known as the water fern, is a unique aquatic plant that thrives in freshwater environments. It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that resemble the structure of true ferns. This plant is commonly found in slow-moving streams, ponds, and wetlands across various regions. Its ability to absorb nutrients from the water makes it an important indicator of water quality. Despite its small size, Phyllitis hybrida plays a vital role in maintaining the ecological balance of its habitat.
