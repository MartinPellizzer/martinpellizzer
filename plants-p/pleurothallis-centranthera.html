Pleurothallis centranthera

Pleurothallis centranthera, commonly known as the yellow pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, yellow or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the rainy season. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This species is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
