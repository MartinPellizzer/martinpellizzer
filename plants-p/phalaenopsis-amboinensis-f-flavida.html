Phalaenopsis amboinensis f. flavida

Phalaenopsis amboinensis f. flavida, commonly known as the yellow-flowered moth orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow petals and sepals, which distinguish it from other Phalaenopsis varieties. The plant typically produces a single, elongated inflorescence with numerous fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

This cultivar is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
