Physurus preslii

Physurus preslii, commonly known as the Preslia's spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, blue or purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and ability to bloom reliably in suitable conditions.
