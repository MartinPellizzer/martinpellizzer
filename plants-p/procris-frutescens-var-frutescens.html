Procris frutescens var. frutescens

Procris frutescens var. frutescens, commonly known as the red-flowered rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features woody stems and small, aromatic leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces clusters of showy, pink to red flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in Mediterranean climates.
