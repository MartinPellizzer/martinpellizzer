Polystichum moorei

Polystichum moorei, commonly known as the Moore's hart's-tongue fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its narrow, leathery, evergreen leaves that form a dense, compact rosette. The fronds are typically dark green and have a distinctive, recurved shape, giving the plant a unique appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

This fern is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
