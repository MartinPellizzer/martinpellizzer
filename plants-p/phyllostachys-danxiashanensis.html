Phyllostachys danxiashanensis

Phyllostachys danxiashanensis, commonly known as Danxiashan bamboo, is a species of grass in the family Poaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a smooth, glossy surface and a pale green color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is a subject of interest in botanical research and conservation efforts.
