Prameles prattii

Prameles prattii, commonly known as the coastal morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its trumpet-shaped, violet-blue flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in sandy, well-drained soils along coastal areas. It is a climbing vine that can reach lengths of up to 10 feet.

Prameles prattii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize dune ecosystems.
