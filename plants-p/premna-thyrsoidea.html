Premna thyrsoidea

Premna thyrsoidea, commonly known as the silky premna, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with a soft, silky texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, spherical berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some cultures.
