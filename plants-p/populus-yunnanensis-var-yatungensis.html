Populus yunnanensis var. yatungensis

Populus yunnanensis var. yatungensis, commonly known as Yatung Poplar, is a rare and ornamental tree native to the Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Salicaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and showy, yellowish-green catkins. The plant thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils. It has been cultivated for its aesthetic value and is sometimes used in horticulture for its unique foliage.

Due to its limited natural range, it is considered a valuable species for botanical study and conservation efforts.
