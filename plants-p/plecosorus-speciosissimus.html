Plecosorus speciosissimus

Plecosorus speciosissimus, commonly known as the jewel orchid, is a rare and striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

Native to the high-altitude regions of tropical South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its most notable botanical feature is its intricate, colorful inflorescence, which displays a complex arrangement of vibrant, showy flowers. The plant has a compact growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
