Plumeria caracasana

Plumeria caracasana, commonly known as the Caracas flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant produces large, palmate leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

The flowers are typically followed by woody, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.
