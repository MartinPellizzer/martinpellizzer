Peperomia carnicaulis

Peperomia carnicaulis, commonly known as the woolly peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, woolly texture, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its compact size and ornamental foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
