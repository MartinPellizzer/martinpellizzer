Phyllopodium minimum

Phyllopodium minimum, commonly known as the miniature fern, is a small, delicate plant native to tropical regions.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its finely divided, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows close to the ground, forming a dense carpet of foliage. Its leaves are usually less than 10 centimeters in length, with a soft, leathery texture.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental gardening for its elegant appearance.
