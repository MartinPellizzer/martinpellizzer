Parathelypteris japonica f. viridescens

Parathelypteris japonica f. viridescens, commonly known as the green-veined water fern, is a fern species characterized by its distinctive fronds with a bright green coloration.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is native to eastern Asia, particularly Japan. The plant features bipinnate fronds that are often arching and have a delicate, feathery appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically near water bodies such as streams and ponds.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and conservation efforts.
