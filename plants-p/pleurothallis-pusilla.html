Pleurothallis pusilla

Pleurothallis pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf pleurothallis, is a small orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-white or yellowish blooms that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in shaded, humid environments with well-draining soil.

Due to its size and beauty, it is a popular choice for terrariums and miniature orchid collections.
