Pasania areca

Pasania areca, commonly known as the areca palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Its fruit, known as the areca nut, is widely used in traditional medicine and as a stimulant.

Pasania areca is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
