Prosthechea fuertesii

Prosthechea fuertesii, commonly known as the Fuertes's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its aerial roots and ability to grow on trees or other structures. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and good air circulation.

Prosthechea fuertesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in tropical ecosystems.
