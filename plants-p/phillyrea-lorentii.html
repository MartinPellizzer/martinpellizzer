Phillyrea lorentii

Phillyrea lorentii, commonly known as the Mediterranean laurel, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, dark green, leathery leaves that are oval in shape and arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, followed by spherical, bright red berries that are attractive to birds. Phillyrea lorentii is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant in coastal and rocky environments.

Its aromatic foliage and ornamental berries make it a valuable addition to gardens and landscapes.
