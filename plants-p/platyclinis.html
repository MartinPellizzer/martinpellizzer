Platyclinis

Platyclinis, commonly known as the flat-leaf shrub, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plants are characterized by their simple, alternate leaves that are often elliptical or ovate in shape. They produce small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, which attract various pollinators.

The genus includes several species, some of which are cultivated for their ornamental value in gardens.
