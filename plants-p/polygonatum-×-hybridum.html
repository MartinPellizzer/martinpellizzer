Polygonatum × hybridum

Polygonatum × hybridum, commonly known as the Chinese Solomon's Seal, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asparagaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright, herbaceous stems and clusters of small, fragrant, white or greenish-white flowers. The plant produces dark purple berries that are edible but often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Polygonatum × hybridum is native to eastern Asia and thrives in shaded, moist environments.
