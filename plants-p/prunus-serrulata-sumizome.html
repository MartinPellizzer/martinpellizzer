Prunus serrulata sumizome

Prunus serrulata sumizome, commonly known as the Sumizome cherry, is a variety of Japanese cherry tree renowned for its ornamental value.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded canopy and clusters of small, pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant is native to Japan and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, thriving in temperate climates.

The Sumizome cherry is also valued for its hardiness and ability to withstand cold winters.
