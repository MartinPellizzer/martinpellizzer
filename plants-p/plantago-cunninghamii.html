Plantago cunninghamii

Plantago cunninghamii, commonly known as the Cunningham's plantain, is a perennial herb native to Australia.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in grasslands and open woodlands. The plant features rosettes of broad, oval-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It produces small, greenish-white flowers in a dense spike during the spring and summer months.

This species is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
