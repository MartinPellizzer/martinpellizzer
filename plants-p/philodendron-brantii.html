Philodendron brantii

Philodendron brantii, commonly known as the Brantii Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves that are often marked with silver or white veins, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Philodendron brantii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
