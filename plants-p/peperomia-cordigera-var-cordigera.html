Peperomia cordigera var. cordigera

Peperomia cordigera var. cordigera, commonly known as the heart-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are heart-shaped and often have a deep green color with a slight reddish tint on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically green or reddish, which are followed by small, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
