Parathesis tomentosa

Parathesis tomentosa, commonly known as the silky parathesis, is a species of flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
