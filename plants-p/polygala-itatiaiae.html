Polygala itatiaiae

Polygala itatiaiae, commonly known as the Itatiaia milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically appear in clusters. The plant has slender, often hairy stems and compound leaves with small, lance-shaped leaflets. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
