Polygala oreotrephes

Polygala oreotrephes, commonly known as the mountain polygala, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, branched inflorescences and showy, often violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.

Due to habitat loss, it is also considered a species of conservation concern in some areas.
