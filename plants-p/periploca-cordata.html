Periploca cordata

Periploca cordata, commonly known as the heartleaf periploca, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant features simple, heart-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds.

Periploca cordata is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
