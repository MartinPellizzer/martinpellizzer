Portulaca oligosperma

Portulaca oligosperma, commonly known as the common purslane, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant thrives in hot, dry environments and is often found in disturbed soils. Its seeds are small and numerous, allowing it to spread easily through wind and water.

Portulaca oligosperma is also known for its edible leaves and stems, which are used in various culinary traditions.
