Ptilostemon strictus

Ptilostemon strictus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ptilostemon, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with slender, upright stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Ptilostemon strictus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
