Piper anonifolium var. rigidulum

Piper anonifolium var. rigidulum, commonly known as the rigid-leaved pepper plant, is a tropical species native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves that are stiff and rigid, giving it its varietal name. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are typically greenish-white in color.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of aromatic foliage.
