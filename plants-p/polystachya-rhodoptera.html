Polystachya rhodoptera

Polystachya rhodoptera, commonly known as the red-winged bamboo orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Kenya and Tanzania. This herbaceous plant features slender, bamboo-like stems with lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its most striking characteristic is its inflorescence, which consists of clusters of small, delicate orchid flowers in shades of pink and white.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded, moist environments.
