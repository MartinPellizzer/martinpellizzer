Podocarpus Madagascariensis Subsp. Procerus

Podocarpus madagascariensis subsp. procerus is a unique evergreen conifer native to Madagascar. It is known for its tall, slender growth habit and needle-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. This subspecies thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in mountainous regions. Its wood is valued for its durability and is used in traditional crafts and construction. Despite its beauty and utility, it remains a relatively rare and endangered plant in its natural habitat.
