Pilosocereus brasiliensis subsp. brasiliensis

Pilosocereus brasiliensis subsp.

brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian night bloomer, is a cactus native to Brazil. It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines.

The plant produces large, fragrant, white flowers that open at night. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique nocturnal blooming habit makes it a striking addition to desert gardens.
