Pelargonium × haemanthon

Pelargonium × haemanthon, commonly known as the scented geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its aromatic foliage, which emits a pleasant scent when touched or bruised. The plant typically features rounded, velvety leaves with a variety of colors, including red, purple, and green. Its flowers are small and usually pink or white, arranged in clusters.

Pelargonium × haemanthon is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its fragrance and visual appeal.
