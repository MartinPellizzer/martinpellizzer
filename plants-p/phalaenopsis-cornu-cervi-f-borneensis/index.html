Phalaenopsis Cornu-Cervi F. Borneensis

The phalaenopsis cornu-cervi f. borneensis is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the tropical regions of Borneo. Known for its striking white petals and velvety green leaves, this plant captivates botanists and plant lovers alike. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing as an epiphyte on trees in the rainforest canopy. Due to its limited natural habitat and threats from deforestation, it is considered a vulnerable species. Its unique beauty and ecological significance make it a subject of both admiration and conservation efforts.
