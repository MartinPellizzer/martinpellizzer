Pleurothallis pluriflora

Pleurothallis pluriflora, commonly known as the many-flowered pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces multiple small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often in clusters. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and surrounding substrate.

Due to its delicate appearance and specific habitat requirements, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
