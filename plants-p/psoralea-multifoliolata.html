Psoralea multifoliolata

Psoralea multifoliolata, commonly known as the many-leaved psoralea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves with multiple leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemes, which are typically borne on slender stems. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grassland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
