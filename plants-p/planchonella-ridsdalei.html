Planchonella ridsdalei

Planchonella ridsdalei, commonly known as the Ridsdale's hibiscus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and lowland areas. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a smooth surface and a glossy appearance. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with five petals and a prominent central stamen.

The fruit is a small, woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.
