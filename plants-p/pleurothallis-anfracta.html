Pleurothallis anfracta

Pleurothallis anfracta, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, narrow leaves and produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It is prized for its unique floral structure and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in the wild.
