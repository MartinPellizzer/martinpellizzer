Potentilla alexeenkoi

Potentilla alexeenkoi, commonly known as the Siberian cinquefoil, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, yellow, five-petaled flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and a compact, upright growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its bright flowers and hardy nature.
