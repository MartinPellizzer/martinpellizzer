Paulownia shensiensis

Paulownia shensiensis, commonly known as the Chinese empress tree, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Paulowniaceae and is characterized by its rapid growth and heart-shaped leaves. The tree produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in early spring before the leaves emerge. Its wood is lightweight and has been historically used for musical instruments and furniture.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological benefits in reforestation projects.
