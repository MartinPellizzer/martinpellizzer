Polygonum cynanchoides var. glabriusculum

Polygonum cynanchoides var. glabriusculum, commonly known as smooth knotweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along stream banks. The plant has opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are smooth and glabrous, giving it its common name. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense spikes, and are usually pink to reddish in color.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
