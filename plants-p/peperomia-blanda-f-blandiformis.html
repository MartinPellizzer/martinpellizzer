Peperomia blanda f. blandiformis

Peperomia blanda f. blandiformis, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with a distinctive silvery-blue hue on the upper surface and a darker green underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale green, emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Due to its attractive foliage and compact size, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
