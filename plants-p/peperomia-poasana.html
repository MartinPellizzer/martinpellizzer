Peperomia poasana

Peperomia poasana, commonly known as the Poasana peperomia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is typically found in shaded, humid environments. The plant features compact, upright growth with glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are often dark green in color. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are usually green or reddish and appear in clusters.

Peperomia poasana is valued as a decorative plant for its attractive foliage and ease of care in indoor settings.
