Peperomia polystachya var. godetiana

Peperomia polystachya var. godetiana, commonly known as the Godetiana Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive red or purple underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that are borne in clusters, though it is primarily grown for its ornamental foliage rather than its blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

This variety is popular among indoor gardeners for its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
