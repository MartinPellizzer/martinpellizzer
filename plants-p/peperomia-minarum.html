Peperomia minarum

Peperomia minarum, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a distinctive silvery-blue hue on the upper surface, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate moisture.

Peperomia minarum is a popular houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
