Pinus Dalatensis Var. Procera

Pinus dalatensis var. procera is a striking evergreen conifer native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. This variant of the Dalat pine is known for its tall, slender form and dark green foliage. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found in forested highlands. The tree's unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental planting. Its resilience and adaptability contribute to its significance in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
