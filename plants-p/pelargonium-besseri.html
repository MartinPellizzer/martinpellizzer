Pelargonium besseri

Pelargonium besseri, commonly known as the Bessera geranium, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically produces bright pink to red blooms during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are usually rounded and have a slightly hairy texture, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Pelargonium besseri is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant flowers and adaptability to various growing conditions.
