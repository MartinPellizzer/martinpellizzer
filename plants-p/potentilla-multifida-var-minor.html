Potentilla multifida var. minor

Potentilla multifida var. minor, commonly known as the lesser multiflora rose, is a low-growing shrub native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It features small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that give it a delicate, lacey appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its compact size and ornamental value make it a desirable plant for both ornamental and ecological gardens.
