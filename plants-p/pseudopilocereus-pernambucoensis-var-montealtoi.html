Pseudopilocereus pernambucoensis var. montealtoi

Pseudopilocereus pernambucoensis var. montealtoi, commonly known as the Monte Alto cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its spiny stems and ability to store water efficiently. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, adapting to harsh climatic conditions. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, appearing during the warmer months.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in dry climates.
