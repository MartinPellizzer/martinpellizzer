Pimelea lanata

Pimelea lanata, commonly known as the silky pimelea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a soft, velvety appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. It is typically found in heathlands and dry sclerophyll forests, thriving in well-drained soils.

Pimelea lanata is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
