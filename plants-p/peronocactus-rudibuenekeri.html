Peronocactus rudibuenekeri

Peronocactus rudibuenekeri, commonly known as the Rudibuenekeri cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of northern Chile.

It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed stem that typically grows to a height of about 15 centimeters. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are clustered at the top of the stem. It is well adapted to dry conditions, with a thick, waxy cuticle that reduces water loss.

This cactus is often found in rocky soils and is a characteristic species of the Atacama Desert ecosystem.
