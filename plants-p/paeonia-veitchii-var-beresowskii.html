Paeonia veitchii var. beresowskii

Paeonia veitchii var. beresowskii, commonly known as the Beresowskii peony, is a variant of the Veitchii peony, native to China.

This plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. It is a herbaceous perennial that produces thick, fleshy roots and has compound, bipinnate leaves. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
