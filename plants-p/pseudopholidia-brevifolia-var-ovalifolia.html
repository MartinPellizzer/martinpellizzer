Pseudopholidia brevifolia var. ovalifolia

Pseudopholidia brevifolia var. ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaved pseudopholidia, is a variant of the species native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its broad, oval-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually clustered in inflorescences. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
