Polygonum senticosum

Polygonum senticosum, commonly known as the Chinese knotweed, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, somewhat woody stems that often have a reddish hue. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered with fine hairs.

Polygonum senticosum is often found in moist, shady environments such as stream banks and forest floors.
