Peperomia caliginigaudens

Peperomia caliginigaudens, commonly known as the misty peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to the shaded understories of tropical forests.

It features dense, waxy foliage with a velvety texture, often displaying shades of green, silver, and bluish-gray. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires consistent humidity and well-draining soil.

Due to its attractive appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
