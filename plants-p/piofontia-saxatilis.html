Piofontia saxatilis

Piofontia saxatilis, commonly known as the rock piofontia, is a hardy perennial plant native to rocky and arid environments.

It features small, waxy leaves that help reduce water loss in its harsh habitat. The plant produces clusters of pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and full sun exposure.

Its resilience makes it a valuable species for xeriscaping and ecological restoration projects.
