Philadelphus calvescens var. compositus

Philadelphus calvescens var. compositus, commonly known as the hybrid weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy beneath. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a versatile ornamental plant.

Its compact growth habit and prolonged flowering period make it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
