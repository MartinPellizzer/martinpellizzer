Pycreus malabaricus f. atroglumosa

Pycreus malabaricus f. atroglumosa, commonly known as the Black-stemmed Pycreus, is a rare and unique cultivar of the Pycreus genus.

It is characterized by its deep, dark green foliage and strikingly dark, almost black stems. This plant is native to the tropical regions of India and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

The plant's foliage and stems are often used in traditional herbal medicine for their purported health benefits.
