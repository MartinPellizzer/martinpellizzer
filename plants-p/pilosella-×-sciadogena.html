Pilosella × sciadogena

Pilosella × sciadogena, commonly known as the silverweed.

It is a hybrid plant in the Asteraceae family, characterized by its hairy, silver-green foliage. The species is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, including North America. It typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and meadows.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.
