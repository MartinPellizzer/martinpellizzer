Polygala elegans

Polygala elegans, commonly known as the elegant milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, branched stems and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grassland habitats. Its flowers have a unique structure with a long, slender spur that extends from the base of the petals.

Polygala elegans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
