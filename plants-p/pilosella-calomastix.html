Pilosella calomastix

Pilosella calomastix, commonly known as the woolly pincushion, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, fluffy appearance. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Pilosella calomastix produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.
