Palicourea Tepuiensis

Palicourea tepuiensis is a rare and enigmatic plant species native to the tepuis of South America. Found primarily in the high-altitude regions of Venezuela and Guyana, it thrives in the unique microclimates of these isolated mountain tabletops. This evergreen shrub is known for its striking, elongated leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. Despite its beauty, it remains poorly studied due to its remote habitat and limited distribution. Its discovery and conservation are critical to understanding the biodiversity of the region's fragile ecosystems.
