Philodendron wilburii

Philodendron wilburii, commonly known as the Wilburii philodendron, is a climbing plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically deep green with a glossy texture. The plant is characterized by its unique, elongated, and often twisted stems that allow it to climb toward light. Its foliage is prized in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.

Philodendron wilburii thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.
