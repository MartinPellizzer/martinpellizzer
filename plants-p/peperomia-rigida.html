Peperomia rigida

Peperomia rigida, commonly known as the rigid peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval or elliptic in shape, with a deep green color and prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, usually greenish-white in color, which appear in the axils of the leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia rigida is relatively low maintenance and resistant to pests, making it an ideal plant for beginners.
