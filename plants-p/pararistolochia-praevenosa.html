Pararistolochia praevenosa

Pararistolochia praevenosa, commonly known as the Indian birthwort, is a climbing vine native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and distinctive, pipe-like flowers. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often hidden within a complex, hollow structure. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous tiny seeds.

However, it is important to note that this plant is toxic and should not be ingested.
