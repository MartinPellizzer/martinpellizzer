Piper villiramulum var. gamboanum

Piper villiramulum var. gamboanum, commonly known as the Gamboanum pepper plant, is a variant of the Piper villiramulum species native to the Philippines.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces tiny, peppery fruits that are used in local cuisines for their pungent flavor. Botanically, it belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species.

Its morphology and growth habit make it a unique subject of study in tropical botany.
