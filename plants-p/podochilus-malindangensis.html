Podochilus malindangensis

Podochilus malindangensis, commonly known as the Malindang orchid, is a rare and endemic species found in the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on tree trunks or branches in the montane forests of Mindanao. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, while its roots are specialized for absorbing nutrients from the air.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
