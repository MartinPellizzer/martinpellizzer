Peperomia trinervis var. trinervis

Peperomia trinervis var. trinervis, commonly known as the Three-veined Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with a distinctive three-veined pattern, giving it its scientific name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or brown, which are followed by elongated, spathaceous bracts. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its low maintenance and attractive foliage make it a favored ornamental plant.
