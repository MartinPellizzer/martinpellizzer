Psychotria kribiensis

Psychotria kribiensis, commonly known as the Kribi psychotria, is a species of flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of Central Africa, particularly Cameroon and the Democratic Republic of the Congo. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers. It produces small, berry-like fruits that are often consumed by various bird species.

Psychotria kribiensis is also noted for its potential ethnobotanical uses in traditional medicine.
