Platycoryne affinis var. affinis

Platycoryne affinis var. affinis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved orchid, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a raceme, which are typically borne on the upper part of the stem. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including parts of India and Southeast Asia.

This species thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
