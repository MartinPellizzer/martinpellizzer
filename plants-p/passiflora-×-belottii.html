Passiflora × belottii

Passiflora × belottii, commonly known as the purple passionflower, is a hybrid species within the Passifloraceae family.

It is native to South America and is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers with intricate patterns. The plant produces edible berries that are rich in antioxidants and have a tart, sweet flavor. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
