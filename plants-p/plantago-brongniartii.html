Plantago brongniartii

Plantago brongniartii, commonly known as the African plantain, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features rosettes of broad, oval-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, which are borne on long, slender stalks. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks.

The species is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
