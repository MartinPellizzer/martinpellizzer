Piper rubrospadix

Piper rubrospadix, commonly known as the red-stemmed pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its reddish stems and heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, green berries that turn red when ripe, containing the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it a unique subject of study in both horticulture and pharmacology.
