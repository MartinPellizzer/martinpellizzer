Paeonia lactiflora var. hirta

Paeonia lactiflora var. hirta, commonly known as the Hirta peony, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and large, showy flowers that range from white to deep pink. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and a woody root system. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its blooming period typically occurs in late spring to early summer.
