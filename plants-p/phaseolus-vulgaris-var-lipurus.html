Phaseolus vulgaris var. lipurus

Phaseolus vulgaris var. lipurus, commonly known as the scarlet runner bean, is a climbing legume native to the Americas.

It produces vibrant red or orange flowers and edible pods that contain round, kidney-shaped seeds. The plant thrives in warm climates and requires full sun for optimal growth. Its botanical features include compound leaves with three leaflets and a vining growth habit.

This variety is valued for both its ornamental appeal and culinary use in various cuisines.
