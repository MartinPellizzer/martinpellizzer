Philadelphus schrenkii

Philadelphus schrenkii, commonly known as the Chinese Mock Orange, is a deciduous shrub native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges and is typically found in rocky or dry habitats. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.

Philadelphus schrenkii is also noted for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
