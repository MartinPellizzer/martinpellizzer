Polygala tamamschaniae

Polygala tamamschaniae, commonly known as the Persian milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish, and it produces small, dry fruits that contain several seeds.
