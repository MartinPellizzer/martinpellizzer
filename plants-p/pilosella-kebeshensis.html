Pilosella kebeshensis

Pilosella kebeshensis, commonly known as the Kebesh pearlyweed, is a flowering plant native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It has small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

Pilosella kebeshensis is known for its hairy stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance.
