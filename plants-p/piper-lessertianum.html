Piper lessertianum

Piper lessertianum, commonly known as the lesser pepper vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by berries containing aromatic seeds. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a rough texture.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
