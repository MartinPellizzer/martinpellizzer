Polygonatum virens

Polygonatum virens, commonly known as green thread, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its long, slender stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces bright yellow berries that are edible and often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies in suitable habitats.
