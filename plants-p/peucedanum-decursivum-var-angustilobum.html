Peucedanum decursivum var. angustilobum

Peucedanum decursivum var. angustilobum, commonly known as narrow-lobed angelica, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that are often branched and covered with fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in complex umbels, typical of the Apiaceae family. Its leaves are finely divided, with narrow, elongated lobes that give it its common name.

The plant is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
