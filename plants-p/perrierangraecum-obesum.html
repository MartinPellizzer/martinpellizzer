Perrierangraecum obesum

Perrierangraecum obesum, commonly known as the fat-leaf plant, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, pale green flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are often covered in a fine, waxy coating that helps reduce water loss.

Perrierangraecum obesum is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to dry conditions.
