Pandanus corneri

Pandanus corneri, commonly known as the New Caledonia pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the island of New Caledonia.

It is a member of the Pandanaceae family and is characterized by its long, narrow leaves that grow in dense clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are often used in traditional crafts and for their aromatic properties.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique adaptations to its island environment.
