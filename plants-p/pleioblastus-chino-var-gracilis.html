Pleioblastus chino var. gracilis

Pleioblastus chino var. gracilis, commonly known as the Chinese bamboo or slender bamboo, is a variety of the Pleioblastus chino species.

It is a clump-forming evergreen bamboo that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are usually a vibrant green, with a slightly rough texture. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers in the spring, followed by cylindrical, woody seed pods.

This bamboo is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its graceful appearance and hardy nature.
