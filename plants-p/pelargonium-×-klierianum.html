Pelargonium × klierianum

Pelargonium × klierianum, commonly known as the African hybrid geranium, is a hybrid plant resulting from crosses between various species of geraniums.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a range of colors including red, pink, and white. The plant typically features upright stems and lobed leaves, which are often hairy or glandular. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is well-suited for container gardening due to its compact growth habit.

Pelargonium × klierianum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
