Prunus praedura

Prunus praedura, commonly known as the Japanese cherry plum.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Japan, characterized by its delicate pink to white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape, with serrated edges. Its fruit is a small, yellow or orange drupe that ripens in late summer.

Prunus praedura is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic flowers and compact growth habit.
