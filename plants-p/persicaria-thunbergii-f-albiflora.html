Persicaria thunbergii f. albiflora

Persicaria thunbergii f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered Japanese knotweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features upright, slender stems that produce clusters of small, white flowers in late summer. The leaves are heart-shaped with a slightly serrated edge, and they are often covered in fine hairs. This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in moist, shady conditions.

It is often used in gardens for its attractive foliage and floral display.
