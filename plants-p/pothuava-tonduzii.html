Pothuava tonduzii

Pothuava tonduzii, commonly known as the Tonduzii plant, is a rare and unique species native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, waxy leaves that display a range of colors from deep green to vibrant red. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, adding to its ornamental value. Its growth habit is typically compact, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a conservation concern.
