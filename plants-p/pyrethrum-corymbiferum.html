Pyrethrum corymbiferum

Pyrethrum corymbiferum, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces small, cylindrical flower heads that are composed of numerous tiny florets. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it thrives in full sun with well-drained soil.

Pyrethrum corymbiferum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in some traditional medicinal practices.
