Pertya desmocephala

Pertya desmocephala, commonly known as the golden star moss, is a small, creeping liverwort found in moist, shaded environments.

It belongs to the family Pterygopariaceae and is characterized by its delicate, branched stems and small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces distinctive yellowish-green gametophores that bear the reproductive structures. It thrives in humid conditions, often growing on rocks or decaying wood in temperate regions.
