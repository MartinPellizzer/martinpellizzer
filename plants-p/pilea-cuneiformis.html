Pilea cuneiformis

Pilea cuneiformis, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically light green with a slightly variegated pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by round, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Its unique foliage and easy care requirements have made it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
