Peperomia nudicaulis

Peperomia nudicaulis, commonly known as the naked-stem peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Central and South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish or purple hue on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia nudicaulis is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
