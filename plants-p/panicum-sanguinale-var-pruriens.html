Panicum sanguinale var. pruriens

Panicum sanguinale var. pruriens, commonly known as goatweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in open fields or along roadsides.

Goatweed is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various health purposes.
