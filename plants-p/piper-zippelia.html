Piper zippelia

Piper zippelia, commonly known as the Zippelia pepper or Zippelia vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piper genus, which includes many species of flowering plants in the family Piperaceae. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, berry-like fruits. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can also be used in traditional medicine.
