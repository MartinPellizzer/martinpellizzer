Pilea variegata

Pilea variegata, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically green with white or silver markings, giving it a striking, variegated appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in the spring, followed by spherical, greenish fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea variegata is known for its easy care and ability to purify the air, enhancing both the aesthetic and health benefits of indoor spaces.
