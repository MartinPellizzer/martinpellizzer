Primulina roseoalba

Primulina roseoalba, commonly known as the rose-white primula, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, pale pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and have a velvety texture.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
