Piper mutisii

Piper mutisii, commonly known as the Malayan pepper or wild long pepper, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with slender stems that twine around supports. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a glossy surface.

The fruit, which is a small, elongated berry, contains the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and cuisine.
