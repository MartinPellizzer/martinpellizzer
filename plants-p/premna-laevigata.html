Premna laevigata

Premna laevigata, commonly known as silky balsa, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub or tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky white pubescence. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in racemes and are followed by small, spherical fruits.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
