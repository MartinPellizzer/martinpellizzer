Petunia macrodactylon

Petunia macrodactylon, commonly known as the large-leaved petunia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, purple, and white. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Petunia macrodactylon is known for its long blooming period, typically from spring through early autumn.
