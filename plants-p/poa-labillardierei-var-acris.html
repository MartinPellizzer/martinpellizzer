Poa labillardierei var. acris

Poa labillardierei var. acris, commonly known as the slender meadowgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often hairy. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny flowers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soils.
