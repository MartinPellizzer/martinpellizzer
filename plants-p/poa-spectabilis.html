Poa spectabilis

Poa spectabilis, commonly known as the showy bluegrass, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its dark green, slender leaves and produces striking blue-gray flowers in the spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense mats.

Poa spectabilis is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing forage for wildlife and supporting pollinators.
