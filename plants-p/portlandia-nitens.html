Portlandia nitens

Portlandia nitens, commonly known as the slender rock heath, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. This low-growing shrub typically thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade. Its small, bell-shaped flowers are usually pink or white, and it produces small, leathery berries.

The plant is adapted to arid environments and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
