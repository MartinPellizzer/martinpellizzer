Pandanus akeassii var. akeassii

Pandanus akeassii var. akeassii, commonly known as the Akeassii pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pandanaceae.

It is native to the Pacific Islands, particularly found in regions such as Hawaii and the Marquesas. The plant is characterized by its long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts and as thatch. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically grouped in inflorescences.

The plant thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to salt spray and sandy soils.
