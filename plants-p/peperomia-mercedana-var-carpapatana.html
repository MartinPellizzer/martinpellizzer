Peperomia mercedana var. carpapatana

Peperomia mercedana var. carpapatana, commonly known as the Carpapatana Peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It features dense, glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of red or pink, which appear during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for collectors and indoor gardeners alike.
