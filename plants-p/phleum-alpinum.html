Phleum alpinum

Phleum alpinum, commonly known as alpine fescue, is a perennial grass native to mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like clusters of flowers. It is well adapted to harsh climates and is resilient to cold temperatures.

Phleum alpinum plays an important role in stabilizing soil in high-altitude ecosystems.
