Potentilla thuringiaca var. jurassica

Potentilla thuringiaca var. jurassica, commonly known as the Jurassic potentilla, is a perennial shrub native to Central Europe.

It is characterized by its dense, low-growing habit and produces small, yellow flowers in clusters. The leaves are typically elliptical and have a slightly hairy texture. This variety is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
