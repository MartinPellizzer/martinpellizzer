Polyochnella brevipes

Polyochnella brevipes, commonly known as the short-footed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, often in well-drained soils. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single, showy flower that is usually pink or purple in color. Its flowers are characterized by three narrow petals and a distinctive lip that is often marked with dark veins.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
