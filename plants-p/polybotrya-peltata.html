Polybotrya peltata

Polybotrya peltata, commonly known as the broad-leafed fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, pinnately compound leaves that are often broad and leathery in texture. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a distinctive, feathery appearance.

Polybotrya peltata plays an important role in its ecosystem by providing habitat and contributing to soil stability.
