Polygala maxima

Polygala maxima, commonly known as the giant milkweed, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces a milky sap, which is a distinctive botanical feature. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland or rocky habitats.

Its unique morphology and ecological role make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
