Palisota stevartii

Palisota stevartii, commonly known as Stewart's water fern, is a aquatic plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds and wetlands. The plant has long, narrow leaves that float on the water surface, with roots submerged in the water. It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that help it absorb nutrients from the water.

Palisota stevartii plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
