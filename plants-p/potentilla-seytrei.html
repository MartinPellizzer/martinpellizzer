Potentilla seytrei

Potentilla seytrei, commonly known as Seytre's cinquefoil, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, sprawling growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. Its leaves are compound, consisting of five oval-shaped leaflets, giving it the common name "cinquefoil.".

Seytre's cinquefoil is often found in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun.
