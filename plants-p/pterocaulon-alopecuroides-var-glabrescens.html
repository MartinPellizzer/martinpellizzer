Pterocaulon alopecuroides var. glabrescens

Pterocaulon alopecuroides var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smoothleaf pterocaulon, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun, requiring minimal water once established.
