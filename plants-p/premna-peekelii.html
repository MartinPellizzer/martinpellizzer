Premna peekelii

Premna peekelii, commonly known as the Malabar premna, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and thickets. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture and serrated edges. Its small, fragrant flowers are usually white or pale yellow and are clustered in umbels.

Premna peekelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses in local communities.
