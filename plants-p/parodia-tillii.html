Parodia tillii

Parodia tillii, commonly known as the "Pineapple Cactus," is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in dense, white to grayish spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.

Parodia tillii is relatively easy to care for, requiring minimal water and well-draining soil.
