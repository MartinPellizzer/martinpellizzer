Pulmonaria longistyla

Pulmonaria longistyla, commonly known as the long-styled lungwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of oval, velvety leaves. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to white, depending on the variety. Its blooming period typically occurs in late spring to early summer.

Pulmonaria longistyla is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in woodland or shaded garden settings.
