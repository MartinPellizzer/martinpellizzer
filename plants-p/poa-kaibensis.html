Poa kaibensis

Poa kaibensis, commonly known as Kaibab bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and silver-blue foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. This plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and open slopes. Poa kaibensis is adapted to withstand drought conditions and has a deep root system for water retention.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small wildlife in its native range.
