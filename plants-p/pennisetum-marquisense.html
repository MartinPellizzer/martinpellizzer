Pennisetum marquisense

Pennisetum marquisense, commonly known as the Marquis grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bright green in color. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that are often used in ornamental landscaping due to their aesthetic appeal. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for drought-tolerant gardens.

Pennisetum marquisense is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in marginal environments.
