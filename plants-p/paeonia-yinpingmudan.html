Paeonia yinpingmudan

Paeonia yinpingmudan, commonly known as the Yinpingmudan peony, is a rare and ornamental species native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from deep red to pink and white. The plant belongs to the芍药科 (Paeoniaceae) family and is known for its robust root system and deciduous foliage. Its botanical features include thick, fleshy rhizomes and broad, heart-shaped leaves.

This species is highly valued in traditional Chinese horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and cultural significance.
