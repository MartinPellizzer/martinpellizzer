Pilosella vernicosa

Pilosella vernicosa, commonly known as the vernicle cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and aromatic foliage. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are pinnately divided with a silky, woolly texture, and its yellow flowers bloom in early spring.

Pilosella vernicosa is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
