Panicum hensii

Panicum hensii, commonly known as silver hairgrass, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its delicate, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that give it a distinctive, airy appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Panicum hensii is valued for its ornamental qualities and its ability to stabilize soil in wet environments.
