Panicum atrosanguineum

Panicum atrosanguineum, commonly known as black blood grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to southern Africa.

It features tall, upright clumps of dark purple to black foliage that contrast beautifully with its silver-green inflorescences. The plant produces delicate, feathery panicles that add movement and texture to gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its unique coloration and architectural form make it a popular choice for modern landscaping.
