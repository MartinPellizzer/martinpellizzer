Phrynium zebrinum

Phrynium zebrinum, commonly known as the zebra plant, is a tropical perennial herb native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that often feature a zebra-like pattern of green and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, with broad, lance-shaped foliage. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

The leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
