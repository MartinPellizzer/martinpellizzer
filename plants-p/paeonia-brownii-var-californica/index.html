Paeonia Brownii Var. Californica

Paeonia brownii var. californica is a rare and unique species of peony native to the western united states. it is known for its striking pink and white flowers that bloom in early spring. this variety is often found in rocky soils and dry habitats, making it well adapted to arid environments. due to its limited range and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. its beauty and ecological importance make it a valuable subject for conservation efforts.
