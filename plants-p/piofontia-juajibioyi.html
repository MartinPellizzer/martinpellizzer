Piofontia juajibioyi

Piofontia juajibioyi, commonly known as the Huayna Picchu orchid, is a rare and enigmatic flowering plant native to the cloud forests of Peru.

The plant thrives in high-altitude environments, typically between 2,000 and 3,500 meters above sea level, where it requires cool, moist conditions.
