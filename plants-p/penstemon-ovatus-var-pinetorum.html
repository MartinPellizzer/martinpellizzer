Penstemon ovatus var. pinetorum

Penstemon ovatus var. pinetorum, commonly known as the white beard scale penstemon, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and showy, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of pink to white. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping. It is a perennial species that produces numerous small, seed-like fruits following flowering.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
