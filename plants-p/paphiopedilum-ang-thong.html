Paphiopedilum ang-thong

Paphiopedilum ang-thong, commonly known as the Thai lady's slipper, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a distinctive pouch-shaped lip that is a hallmark of the Paphiopedilum genus. The plant typically has a single, erect stem with broad, elliptic leaves. Its flowers are usually pale yellow with a deep red or brown lip, and it blooms during the rainy season.

It prefers shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.
