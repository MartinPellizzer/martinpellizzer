Polymniastrum variabile

Polymniastrum variabile, commonly known as the variable polymniastrum, is a rare and lesser-known plant species belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, branched stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.
