Pulmonaria officinalis subsp. officinalis

Pulmonaria officinalis subsp.

officinalis, commonly known as lungwort, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe. It features basal leaves that are heart-shaped with distinctive purple and green markings, and its flowers are clustered in upright spikes of pink to violet hues.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas. It has a long blooming period from late spring to early summer. Pulmonaria officinalis subsp.

officinalis is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in traditional medicine.
