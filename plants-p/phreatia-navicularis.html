Phreatia navicularis

Phreatia navicularis, commonly known as the narrow-leafed phreatia, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, tropical environments. The plant features long, slender pseudobulbs and linear leaves that are adapted to retain moisture. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a distinctive greenish-yellow coloration.

It thrives in shaded, well-drained conditions and requires consistent humidity for optimal growth.
