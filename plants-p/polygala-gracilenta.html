Polygala gracilenta

Polygala gracilenta, commonly known as slender milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to parts of Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, delicate flowers that are often pink or purple in color. Its leaves are alternate and ovate, with a soft, hairy texture.

Polygala gracilenta is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits and is also cultivated for ornamental purposes.
