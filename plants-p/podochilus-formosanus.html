Podochilus formosanus

Podochilus formosanus, commonly known as the Taiwan orchid, is a species of orchid native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pale yellow to orange flowers with prominent spurred sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Podochilus formosanus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated for its unique floral display.
