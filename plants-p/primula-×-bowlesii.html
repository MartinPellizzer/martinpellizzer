Primula × bowlesii

Primula × bowlesii, commonly known as the hybrid primrose, is a perennial flowering plant native to the British Isles.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically blooms in late winter to early spring, making it one of the earliest flowering plants in the Primula genus. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

Primula × bowlesii is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in cool, moist conditions.
