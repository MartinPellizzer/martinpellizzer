Phalaenopsis inscriptiosinensis

Phalaenopsis inscriptiosinensis, commonly known as the inscribed moth orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, elongated flower spikes that bear numerous small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically has broad, dark green leaves that are arranged in a compact rosette. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Phalaenopsis inscriptiosinensis is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
