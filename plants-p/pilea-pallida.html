Pilea pallida

Pilea pallida, commonly known as the white prayer plant, is a tropical herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically pale green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture without being overwatered.

Pilea pallida is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
