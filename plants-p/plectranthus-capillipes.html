Plectranthus capillipes

Plectranthus capillipes, commonly known as the purple heart plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking purple to violet leaves, which are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale purple or white, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Plectranthus capillipes is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and ease of care.
