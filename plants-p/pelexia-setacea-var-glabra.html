Pelexia setacea var. glabra

Pelexia setacea var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth pelexia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and natural habitats.
