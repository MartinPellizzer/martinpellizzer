Phyllanthus bernierianus var. glaber

Phyllanthus bernierianus var. glaber, commonly known as the smooth phyllanthus, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in cup-like structures called cyathia. Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.
