Pedilochilus bantaengensis

Pedilochilus bantaengensis, commonly known as the Bantaeng orchid, is a rare and endemic species found in specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and vibrant coloration. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or in rocky crevices.
