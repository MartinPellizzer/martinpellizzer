Pittosporum polyspermum

Pittosporum polyspermum, commonly known as the New Zealand pittosporum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Pittosporum polyspermum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in ecological restoration projects.
