Psychotria inaequifolia

Psychotria inaequifolia, commonly known as the unequal-leaved psychotria, is a species of flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Colombia and Ecuador. The plant is also notable for containing psychoactive alkaloids, similar to other members of the Psychotria genus.
