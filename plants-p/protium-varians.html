Protium varians

Protium varians, commonly known as the variable sycamore, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and alternate, simple leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by fleshy, orange-red berries. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
