Parietaria welwitschii

Parietaria welwitschii, commonly known as the Welwitsch's groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open, sandy or rocky soils. The plant features basal leaves that are broadly ovate with a rough texture and a distinctive white underside. Its small yellow flowers are clustered in dense, spherical heads, and it blooms during the warmer months.
