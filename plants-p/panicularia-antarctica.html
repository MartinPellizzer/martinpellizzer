Panicularia antarctica

Panicularia antarctica, commonly known as the Antarctic panicled grass, is a flowering plant native to the extreme environments of Antarctica.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that thrives in the harsh conditions of the continent's cold, dry climate. The plant produces small, panicled inflorescences composed of numerous tiny florets, which are adapted for wind pollination. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, helping to reduce water loss in the arid environment.

Panicularia antarctica plays a minor but ecologically significant role in the sparse flora of Antarctica.
