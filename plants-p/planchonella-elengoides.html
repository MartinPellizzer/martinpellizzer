Planchonella elengoides

Planchonella elengoides, commonly known as the red hibiscus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are showy, with five petals that range in color from red to pink, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
