Polylepis hieronymi var. saltensis

Polylepis hieronymi var. saltensis, commonly known as the Saltanensis Polylepis, is a rare and endemic species found in the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It is characterized by its distinctive, small, and often frost-covered leaves, which help it survive in harsh, cold environments. The tree produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in the spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
