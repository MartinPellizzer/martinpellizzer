Pennisetum hamiltonii

Pennisetum hamiltonii, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender stalks that produce plumes of soft, purple to pinkish flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Pennisetum hamiltonii is known for its drought tolerance and ability to attract birds and pollinators.
