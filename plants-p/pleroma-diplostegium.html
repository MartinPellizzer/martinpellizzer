Pleroma diplostegium

Pleroma diplostegium, commonly known as the red-flowered plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and clusters of tubular, red or purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its medicinal properties have also been studied for their potential health benefits.
