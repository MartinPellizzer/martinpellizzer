Pterolobium kantuffa

Pterolobium kantuffa, commonly known as the Kantuffa legume, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features trifoliate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow, arranged in racemose inflorescences.

Pterolobium kantuffa is often found in open woodlands and savannas, thriving in well-drained soils.
