Polygala ericifolia var. eckloniana

Polygala ericifolia var. eckloniana, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features slender, woody stems and small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to coastal and sandy environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
