Pelargonium barklyi

Pelargonium barklyi, commonly known as the Barkly geranium, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, pink or red flowers. The leaves are typically rounded and have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in warmer climates.
