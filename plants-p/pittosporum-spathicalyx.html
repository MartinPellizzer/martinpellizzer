Pittosporum spathicalyx

Pittosporum spathicalyx, commonly known as the golden pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy on top with a lighter underside. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white flowers that are often tinged with yellow, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued for its ability to attract native birds and insects.
