Polystachya fusiformis var. virescens

Polystachya fusiformis var. virescens, commonly known as the green-stemmed fir.

It is a member of the orchid family, characterized by its slender, erect stems and elongated leaves. The plant produces striking inflorescences with numerous small, greenish-yellow flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
