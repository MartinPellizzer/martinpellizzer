Psyllocarpus asparagoides

Psyllocarpus asparagoides, commonly known as the slender bamboo fern, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and is often found in shaded, moist environments. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, which allows it to spread and form dense mats. Its fronds are typically lance-shaped and can reach lengths of up to 1 meter.

Psyllocarpus asparagoides is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
