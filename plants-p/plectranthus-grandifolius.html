Plectranthus grandifolius

Plectranthus grandifolius, commonly known as the large-leaved plectranthus, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features broad, velvety, dark green leaves that are often heart-shaped or ovate in form. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of purple or violet, typically arranged in clusters at the leaf axils. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and aromatic foliage, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
