Peperomia reflexa var. geraensis

Peperomia reflexa var. geraensis, commonly known as the Geraens Glandular Peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a slightly waxy texture and a unique, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate watering.

Due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
