Pleurothallis humboldtiana

Pleurothallis humboldtiana, commonly known as the Humboldt orchid, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features intricate, narrow leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments to ensure its survival.
