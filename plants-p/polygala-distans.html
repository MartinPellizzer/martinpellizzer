Polygala distans

Polygala distans, commonly known as the narrow-leaf milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in grassland habitats and thrives in well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, and it produces small, round pods that contain seeds.

Polygala distans is often found in open areas and is adapted to seasonal rainfall patterns.
