Pelargonium aloysianum

Pelargonium aloysianum, commonly known as the Aloysia geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant produces clusters of five-petaled, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are typically lobed and covered in fine hairs, giving them a fuzzy texture.

Pelargonium aloysianum is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and ability to thrive in various garden settings.
