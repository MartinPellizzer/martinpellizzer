Pteris elegans

Pteris elegans, commonly known as the elegant water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Pteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds that are typically light green with a waxy texture. Native to Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found near streams or in humid forests. The fronds are usually arched and can reach lengths of up to 60 centimeters.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in indoor conditions with proper humidity and light.
