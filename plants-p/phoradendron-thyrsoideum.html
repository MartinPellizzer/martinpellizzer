Phoradendron thyrsoideum

Phoradendron thyrsoideum, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a climbing plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and distinctive star-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.

Its growth habit makes it suitable for training on trellises or as a ground cover.
