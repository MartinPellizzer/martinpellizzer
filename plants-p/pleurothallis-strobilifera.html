Pleurothallis strobilifera

Pleurothallis strobilifera, commonly known as the pineapple orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and clustered inflorescences. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often arranged in a pineapple-like cluster. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and delicate blooms, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
