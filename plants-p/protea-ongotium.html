Protea ongotium

Protea ongotium, commonly known as the silver protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence composed of numerous white to pale pink flowers arranged in a spherical or conical shape. The plant is native to the Western Cape region and thrives in well-drained, acidic soils. Its unique floral structure attracts a variety of pollinators, including birds and insects.

Protea ongotium is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
