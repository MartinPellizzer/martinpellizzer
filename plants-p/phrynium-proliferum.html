Phrynium proliferum

Phrynium proliferum, commonly known as the arrowleaf plant, is a tropical perennial belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking, yellowish-green spadices with red-brown bracts, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded, tropical environments.

Phrynium proliferum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
