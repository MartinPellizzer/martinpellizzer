Podocarpium densum

Podocarpium densum, commonly known as the dense podocarp, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces distinctive, three-lobed leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is typically found in temperate regions.

Podocarpium densum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
