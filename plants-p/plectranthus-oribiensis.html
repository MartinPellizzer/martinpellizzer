Plectranthus oribiensis

Plectranthus oribiensis, commonly known as the Oribi grass or Oribi sedge, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along waterways. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in spikes. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Plectranthus oribiensis is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
