Pilea miguelii

Pilea miguelii, commonly known as the Mexican evergreen or velvet plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Mexico.

It features dense, velvety foliage with dark green, oval-shaped leaves that have a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea miguelii is relatively low maintenance, requiring moderate watering and occasional pruning to maintain its compact form.
