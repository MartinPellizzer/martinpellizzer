Parodia obtusa var. atochana

Parodia obtusa var. atochana, commonly known as the "Paddle Cactus," is a compact, rounded cactus native to Mexico.

It features thick, ribbed stems that are typically pale green to bluish-gray in color. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its unique shape and slow growth make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container cultivation.
