Phaius reflexipetalus

Phaius reflexipetalus, commonly known as the reflexipetal orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant with tall, slender stems that bear broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, showy inflorescences with large, fragrant flowers that have reflexed petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, loamy soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and conservatories.
