Piper tenuiculispicum

Piper tenuiculispicum, commonly known as the slender-leafed pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, elongated leaves and clusters of tiny, greenish-white flowers. It produces small, round berries that are typically green when unripe and turn red when mature.

This plant is often cultivated for its aromatic leaves and potential medicinal properties.
