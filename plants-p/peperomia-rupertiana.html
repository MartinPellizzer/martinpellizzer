Peperomia rupertiana

Peperomia rupertiana, commonly known as the Rupert's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a velvety texture and small, clustered white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store moisture, making it well adapted to dry environments. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia rupertiana is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
