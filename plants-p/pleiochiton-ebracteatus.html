Pleiochiton ebracteatus

Pleiochiton ebracteatus, commonly known as the golden bamboo or yellow bamboo, is a member of the grass family (Poaceae).

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and bright yellow-green foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in panicles. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation, it is often used in landscaping and as a decorative plant in gardens.
