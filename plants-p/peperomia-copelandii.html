Peperomia copelandii

Peperomia copelandii, commonly known as the Emerald Gem, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features glossy, dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in late spring or early summer. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Peperomia copelandii is prized for its attractive foliage and air-purifying qualities.
