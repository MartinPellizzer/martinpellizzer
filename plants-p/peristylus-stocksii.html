Peristylus stocksii

Peristylus stocksii, commonly known as the golden star dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Peristylus stocksii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
