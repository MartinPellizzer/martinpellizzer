Pseudocarapa

It consists of several species, most of which are native to South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The fruits are legumes, containing one or more seeds, and the trees can grow quite tall, reaching up to 30 meters in height.

Pseudocarapa species are valued for their hard, heavy wood and are sometimes used in traditional medicine.
