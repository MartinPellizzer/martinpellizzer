Pseuderanthemum cuatrecasasii

Pseuderanthemum cuatrecasasii, commonly known as the Cuatrecasas's false anthemum, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and produces small, tubular flowers in shades of white and pink. Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
