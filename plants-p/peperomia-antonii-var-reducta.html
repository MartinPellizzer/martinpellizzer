Peperomia antonii var. reducta

Peperomia antonii var. reducta, commonly known as the "Lemon Lime Peperomia," is a compact, trailing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically light green with a subtle yellow or cream variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in clusters, though it is primarily grown for its ornamental foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant leaf color make it an attractive addition to hanging baskets or shelves.
