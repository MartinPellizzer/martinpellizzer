Pothos junghuhnianus

Pothos junghuhnianus, commonly known as the silver pothos, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically variegated with shades of silver and green. The plant thrives in humid environments and prefers indirect sunlight, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its aerial roots allow it to climb and trail, adding a decorative touch to hanging baskets or shelves.

Pothos junghuhnianus is known for its low maintenance and air-purifying properties.
