Peperomia pallida var. tuamotensis

Peperomia pallida var. tuamotensis, commonly known as the Tuamotu peperomia, is a compact, low-growing perennial plant native to the Tuamotu Archipelago in French Polynesia.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically pale green with a waxy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and white. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is valued for its ornamental foliage and is often grown as a houseplant due to its adaptability and ease of care.
