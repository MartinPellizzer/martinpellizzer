Pandiaka rubrolutea

Pandiaka rubrolutea, commonly known as the red-and-yellow pandiaka, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its strikingly colorful flowers, which range from deep red to bright yellow, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy surface, while its flowers are borne in clusters along the stems.

Pandiaka rubrolutea is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its potential medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
