Pycreus macrostachyos var. tenuis

Pycreus macrostachyos var. tenuis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed pycreus, is a variant of the pycreus genus native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with pale yellow flowers, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
