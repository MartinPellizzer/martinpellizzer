Peperomia mourae

Peperomia mourae, commonly known as the Shiny Cabbage Plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to Brazil.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pale green flowers, which are followed by reddish-brown seed capsules. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its low maintenance and attractive foliage make it a favored ornamental plant.
