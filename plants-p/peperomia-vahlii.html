Peperomia vahlii

Peperomia vahlii, commonly known as the Vahli's Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, arranged in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia vahlii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
