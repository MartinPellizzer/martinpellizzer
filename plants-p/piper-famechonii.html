Piper famechonii

Piper famechonii, commonly known as the Mauve Pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its distinctive mauve-colored flowers and small, peppery fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the undergrowth of rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
