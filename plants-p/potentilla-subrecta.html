Potentilla subrecta

Potentilla subrecta, commonly known as the upright potentilla, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, upright stems with small, dark green leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow and appear in clusters, blooming in late spring to early summer.

Potentilla subrecta is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardiness in temperate climates.
