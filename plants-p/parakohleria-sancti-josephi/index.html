Parakohleria Sancti-Josephi

The parakohleria sancti-josephi is a rare and unique flowering plant native to the montane forests of western Africa. It is known for its striking, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. This species was first discovered in the early 20th century and has since been the subject of botanical interest. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered critically endangered. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and study this rare botanical treasure.
