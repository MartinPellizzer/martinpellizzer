Piper fanshawei

Piper fanshawei, commonly known as the Fan Shawei pepper plant, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, fragrant berries that mature to a dark purple color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The berries are often used in traditional medicine and as a spice due to their aromatic properties.
