Panicum hydrophilum

Panicum hydrophilum, commonly known as water panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to wetland environments.

It thrives in moist soils and is often found in marshes, along stream banks, and in flooded areas. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces small, spike-like clusters of green to golden-colored flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture.

Panicum hydrophilum plays an important ecological role by stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wetland organisms.
