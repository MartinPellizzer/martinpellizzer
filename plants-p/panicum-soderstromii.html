Panicum soderstromii

Panicum soderstromii, commonly known as Soderstrom's Panicum, is a grass species native to parts of Asia, particularly China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves that are often tinged with silver or blue. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear delicate, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and restoration projects.
