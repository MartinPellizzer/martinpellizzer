Peperomia cubana f. platana

Peperomia cubana f. platana, commonly known as the Cuban peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Cuba.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale green, emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it an ideal plant for tabletop displays or as part of a succulent collection.
