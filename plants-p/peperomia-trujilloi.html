Peperomia trujilloi

Peperomia trujilloi, commonly known as the Trujillo peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Andean regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are often dark green with a reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its slow growth and unique foliage, it is valued as a decorative plant in both home and garden settings.
