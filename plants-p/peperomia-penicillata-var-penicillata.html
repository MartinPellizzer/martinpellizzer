Peperomia penicillata var. penicillata

Peperomia penicillata var. penicillata, commonly known as the velvet plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, velvety foliage with deep green, ovate leaves that have a distinctive waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers during the growing season, which are followed by spore-bearing structures. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it an ideal houseplant for both novice and experienced growers.
