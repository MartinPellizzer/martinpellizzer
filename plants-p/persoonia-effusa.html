Persoonia effusa

Persoonia effusa, commonly known as the silky persoonia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, silky-hairy foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters. The plant belongs to the Proteaceae family and is adapted to the dry, sandy soils of its native habitat. Its flowers are typically pollinated by insects, and the plant produces woody, capsule-like fruits.

Persoonia effusa is often found in coastal and sandy areas, where it thrives in full sun.
