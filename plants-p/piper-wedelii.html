Piper wedelii

Piper wedelii, commonly known as the Wedel's pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruit, which resembles a small berry, contains the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.

Piper wedelii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
