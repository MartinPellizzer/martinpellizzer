Phrynium rubrum

Phrynium rubrum, commonly known as the red swamp lily, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant produces showy, orange-red bracts that resemble flowers, though they are actually modified leaves. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near waterways.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor plant collections.
