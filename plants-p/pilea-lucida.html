Pilea lucida

Pilea lucida, commonly known as the shining pilea, is a compact, evergreen houseplant native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a waxy sheen, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in late spring or early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate watering.

Pilea lucida is prized for its easy care and aesthetic appeal, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
