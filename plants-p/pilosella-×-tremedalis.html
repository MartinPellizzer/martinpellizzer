Pilosella × tremedalis

Pilosella × tremedalis, commonly known as the common yarrow or slender yarrow, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is a hybrid species that occurs naturally in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, gray-green leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in flat-topped clusters. It is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, including North America.

Pilosella × tremedalis is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its hardy nature and ability to stabilize soil.
