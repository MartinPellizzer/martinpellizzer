Panicum transvenulosum

Panicum transvenulosum, commonly known as the slender panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands, disturbed soils, and along roadsides. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it adaptable to various environmental conditions.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in agricultural and urban settings.
