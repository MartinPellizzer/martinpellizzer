Pilosella Banaticola

Pilosella from banat, scientifically known as Pilosella banaticola, is a rare and endemic flowering plant found primarily in the Banat region of Central Europe. This species belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its delicate white or pale yellow flowers and hairy stems. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Pilosella banaticola is considered a vulnerable species facing threats from habitat loss and climate change. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving its natural environment and ensuring its survival for future generations. Its unique ecological role and aesthetic appeal make it a valuable subject for botanical study and environmental protection.
