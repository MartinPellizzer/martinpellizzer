Panicum alatum var. longiflorum

Panicum alatum var. longiflorum, commonly known as the feather grass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, hair-like leaves that give it a feathery appearance. The plant produces panicles of small, often pink or red flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its delicate structure and ornamental value make it a favorite in landscaping and floral arrangements.
