Psoralea multijuga

Psoralea multijuga, commonly known as the broad-leaved psoralea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its seeds are enclosed in pods and are adapted for dispersal by wind or animals.

Psoralea multijuga is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
