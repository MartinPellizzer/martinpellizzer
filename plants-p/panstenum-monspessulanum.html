Panstenum monspessulanum

Panstenum monspessulanum, commonly known as the Spanish broom, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub characterized by its slender, branched stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has small, leathery leaves and produces pods that contain seeds. It is often found in dry, sunny environments and is adapted to poor soils.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically significant in its native habitat.
