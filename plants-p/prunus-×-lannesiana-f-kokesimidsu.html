Prunus × lannesiana f. kokesimidsu

Prunus × lannesiana f. kokesimidsu, commonly known as the Kokesimidsu cherry, is a unique hybrid species within the Prunus genus.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically features dark green, oval-shaped leaves and produces small, edible fruits that are often used in culinary applications. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and valuable addition to botanical collections.
