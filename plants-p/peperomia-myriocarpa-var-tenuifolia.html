Peperomia myriocarpa var. tenuifolia

Peperomia myriocarpa var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, elongated leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often greenish-white in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for decorative purposes in homes and offices.
