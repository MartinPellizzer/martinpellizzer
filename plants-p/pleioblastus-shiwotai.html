Pleioblastus shiwotai

Pleioblastus shiwotai, commonly known as the Shiwotai bamboo, is a species native to China.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and fine, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in mountainous regions. Its foliage exhibits a delicate silver-blue hue, which becomes more pronounced under direct sunlight.

Pleioblastus shiwotai is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
