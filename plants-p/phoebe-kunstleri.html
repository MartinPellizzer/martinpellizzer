Phoebe kunstleri

Phoebe kunstleri, commonly known as the Kunstler's phoebe, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sun. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, often with a waxy texture. Its small, fragrant flowers are usually white or pale yellow, and it produces small, dark berries that are edible for some bird species.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden settings.
