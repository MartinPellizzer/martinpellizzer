Phacelia endiplus

Phacelia endiplus, commonly known as the California phacelia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet. The plant features showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Phacelia endiplus is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
