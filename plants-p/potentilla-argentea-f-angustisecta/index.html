Potentilla Argentea F. Angustisecta

Silverleaf cinquefoil, scientifically known as Potentilla argentea f. angustisecta, is a strikingly elegant flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America. Its silvery-white foliage and delicate yellow flowers make it a standout in both wild and cultivated landscapes. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes. With its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a favorite among gardeners seeking low-maintenance, ornamental plants. Its beauty and adaptability have earned it a place in both ecological and horticultural discussions.
