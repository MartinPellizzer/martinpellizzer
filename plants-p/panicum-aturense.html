Panicum aturense

Panicum aturense, commonly known as slender panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and other moist environments, showcasing its adaptability to waterlogged soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces panicles of small, fluffy flowers that are often white or light brown in color. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soil in wetland ecosystems.

Its rapid growth and ability to thrive in nutrient-rich environments make it a useful plant for ecological restoration projects.
