Polymnia africana

Polymnia africana, commonly known as the African polymnia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and savanna regions. The plant features erect, branching stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. Its small, showy flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom in clusters.

Polymnia africana is noted for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
