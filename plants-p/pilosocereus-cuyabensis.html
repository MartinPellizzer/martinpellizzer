Pilosocereus cuyabensis

Pilosocereus cuyabensis, commonly known as the Cuyabeno cactus, is a species of cactus native to the Amazon rainforest of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant features spines and is adapted to thrive in the humid, tropical environment. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, appearing at the tips of the stems.

Pilosocereus cuyabensis plays an important ecological role in its habitat, providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
