Palala bicuhyba

Palala bicuhyba, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow and orange flowers that resemble flames, which bloom during the warmer months. The plant has strap-like leaves that are broad and glossy, providing a lush green backdrop to its showy blooms. It thrives in high humidity and requires indirect sunlight to flourish.

Palala bicuhyba is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both gardens and indoor settings.
