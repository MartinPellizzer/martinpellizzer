Pycreus altus

Pycreus altus, commonly known as the tall pycreus, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in open habitats such as grasslands.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
