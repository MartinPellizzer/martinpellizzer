Paronychia ellenbergii

Paronychia ellenbergii, commonly known as the rock rose, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and produces showy, pink or red flowers in spring. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Paronychia ellenbergii is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
