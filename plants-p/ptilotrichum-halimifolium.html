Ptilotrichum halimifolium

Ptilotrichum halimifolium, commonly known as the silverleaf ptilotrichum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
