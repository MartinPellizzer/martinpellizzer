Polypodium enterosoroides

Polypodium enterosoroides, commonly known as the slender wall fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows on rocks or tree trunks, thriving in moist, shaded environments. Its reproductive structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Polypodium enterosoroides is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
