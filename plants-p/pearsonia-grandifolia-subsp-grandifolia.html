Pearsonia grandifolia subsp. grandifolia

Pearsonia grandifolia subsp.

grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered pearsonia, is a species of flowering plant native to New Zealand. It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs.
