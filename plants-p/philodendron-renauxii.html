Philodendron renauxii

Philodendron renauxii, commonly known as the Réné Lévesque philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant has a vigorous growth habit and produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Philodendron renauxii is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
