Phyllanthus dekindtii

Phyllanthus dekindtii, commonly known as the African snowball, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of white flowers that bloom in winter. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or sandy habitats.

Phyllanthus dekindtii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
