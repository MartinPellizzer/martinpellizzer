Potameia lotungensis

Potameia lotungensis, commonly known as the lotungensis water lily, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, floating leaves that provide shade and habitat for aquatic life. The plant produces showy white or pink flowers that bloom above the water surface. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Its roots help stabilize the sediment, contributing to water clarity and ecosystem health.
