Pentzia dichotoma

Pentzia dichotoma, commonly known as the silver tree, is a unique succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its branching structure and silvery-gray foliage, which helps it conserve water in arid environments. The plant has a thick, woody trunk that stores water, allowing it to survive prolonged dry periods. Its small, pale flowers appear in clusters and are typically pollinated by insects.

Due to its slow growth and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and protected species.
