Polygala nimborum

Polygala nimborum, commonly known as the cloud fern, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, and they bloom in late spring to early summer.

Polygala nimborum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
