Polycarpon polycarpoides subsp. catalaunicum

Polycarpon polycarpoides subsp.

catalaunicum, commonly known as the Catalaunic polycarp, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, clustered flowers.
