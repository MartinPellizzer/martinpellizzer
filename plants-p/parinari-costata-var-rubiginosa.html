Parinari costata var. rubiginosa

Parinari costata var. rubiginosa, commonly known as the red-stemmed parinari, is a tropical tree native to regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive reddish-brown bark and elongated, elliptical leaves with a glossy upper surface. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into fleshy, orange-red berries. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses in local communities.
