Pleurothallis juninensis var. cuzcoensis

Pleurothallis juninensis var. cuzcoensis, commonly known as the Cuzco orchid, is a species native to the highlands of Peru.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically greenish-white with yellow and purple markings. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining substrates.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicately preserved species.
