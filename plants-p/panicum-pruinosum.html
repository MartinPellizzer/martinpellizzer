Panicum pruinosum

Panicum pruinosum, commonly known as silver panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray sheaths and inflorescences that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as swamps and marshes. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a prominent midrib.

Panicum pruinosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in wetland environments.
