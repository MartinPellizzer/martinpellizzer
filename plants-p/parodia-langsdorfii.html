Parodia langsdorfii

Parodia langsdorfii, commonly known as the langsdorfii cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of South America.

It is a small, globular cactus with a compact, rounded shape and a smooth, pale green or bluish-green stem. The plant features white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It has a shallow root system and is adapted to thrive in well-drained, sandy soils.

Parodia langsdorfii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
