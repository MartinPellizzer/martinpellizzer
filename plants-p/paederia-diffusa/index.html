Paederia Diffusa

Paederia diffusa is a tropical climbing plant known for its medicinal properties and unique appearance. It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is native to regions in Asia, Africa, and the Pacific. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and small white or greenish flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its roots and leaves are used in traditional medicine to treat various ailments. Due to its potential health benefits, it has gained attention in modern herbal research.
