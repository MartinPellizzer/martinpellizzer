Phillyrea subangustifolia subsp. rigoi

Phillyrea subangustifolia subsp.

rigoi, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside.

The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, woody fruits. It is often found in rocky or dry habitats, showing a preference for well-drained soils.

This subspecies is noted for its hardiness and ability to thrive in coastal and arid environments.
