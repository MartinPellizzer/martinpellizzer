Pseudocentrum sphaerocorys

Pseudocentrum sphaerocorys, commonly known as the spherical starfruit tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded clusters of small, glossy green leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by orange-red, star-shaped fruits resembling starfruit. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for both its ornamental and medicinal properties.
