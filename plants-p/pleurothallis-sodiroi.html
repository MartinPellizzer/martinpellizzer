Pleurothallis sodiroi

Pleurothallis sodiroi, commonly known as the "Carnival Orchid," is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with delicate, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late winter. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often on forest floors or among leaf litter. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the surrounding soil.

Pleurothallis sodiroi is noted for its intricate floral structure and is a popular species among orchid enthusiasts.
