Pittosporum pauciflorum var. brevistamineum

Pittosporum pauciflorum var. brevistamineum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, which appear in clusters. These flowers are followed by small, round berries that are edible but not particularly flavorful.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.
