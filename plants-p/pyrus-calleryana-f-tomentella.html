Pyrus calleryana f. tomentella

Pyrus calleryana f. tomentella, commonly known as the silver-leaf pear, is a cultivar of the Callery pear species.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-white pubescence that covers the leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant is a deciduous tree with a rounded, often spreading form. Its foliage is thick and provides excellent fall color, transitioning from yellow to red.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil conditions.
