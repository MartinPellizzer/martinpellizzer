Peperomia obtusifolia var. macropoda

Peperomia obtusifolia var. macropoda, commonly known as the large-leaf peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a prominent central vein, often adorned with a slight bloom. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and decorative purposes.
