Polypodium oosorum var. micropecten

Polypodium oosorum var. micropecten, commonly known as the narrow-caledula polypody, is a fern species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense, moss-like layer of root hairs.

The leaves are usually bi- or tri-pinnate, with small, scale-like structures along the veins.
