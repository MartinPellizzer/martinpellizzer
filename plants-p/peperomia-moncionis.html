Peperomia moncionis

Peperomia moncionis, commonly known as the Monteverde peperomia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the cloud forests of Costa Rica and is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit. The plant features waxy, ovate leaves that are typically green with a subtle variegation pattern. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often hidden among the foliage.

Peperomia moncionis is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and drought tolerance.
