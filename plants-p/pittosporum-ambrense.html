Pittosporum ambrense

Pittosporum ambrense, commonly known as the New Zealand pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features evergreen leaves that are glossy and dark green, often with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that are typically clustered in umbels, followed by small, red berries. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.

Pittosporum ambrense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
