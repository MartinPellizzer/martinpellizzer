Phreatia saccolabioides

Phreatia saccolabioides, commonly known as the saccolabium orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking inflorescences with numerous flowers that have a distinctive, often fragrant scent. Its flowers typically feature a yellow lip with a contrasting purple or brown pattern.

Phreatia saccolabioides thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.
