Pyrrhocactus vollianus var. breviaristatus

Pyrrhocactus vollianus var. breviaristatus, commonly known as the short-spined pyrrhocactus, is a member of the Cactaceae family native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, spherical shape and short, spiny stems that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a vibrant touch to its structure. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it thrives in well-draining soil with minimal water.

This variety is popular among cactus enthusiasts for its unique morphology and low maintenance requirements.
