Photinia myriantha

Photinia myriantha, commonly known as the glossy leaf photinia, is a shrub or small tree native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage that is often glossy in appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during the spring, which are followed by red, fleshy fruits. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a smooth margin and a pointed tip.

Photinia myriantha is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
