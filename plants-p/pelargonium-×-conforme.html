Pelargonium × conforme

Pelargonium × conforme, commonly known as the zonal geranium, is a hybrid plant that belongs to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a wide range of colors, including red, pink, purple, and white. The leaves are typically serrated and often have distinct color zones, which give the plant its common name. It is a popular ornamental plant cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in various growing conditions.

Pelargonium × conforme is often used in gardens, containers, and as bedding plants due to its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
