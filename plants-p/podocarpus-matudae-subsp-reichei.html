Podocarpus matudae subsp. reichei

Podocarpus matudae subsp.

reichei, commonly known as the Matudae Podocarp, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Podocarpaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, cone-like fruits.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions. It has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 20 meters.

Its wood is valued for its durability and is used in construction and furniture-making.
