Philodendron sodiroi

Philodendron sodiroi, commonly known as the Sodiro's philodendron, is a climbing vine native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by bright red berries, which are an important food source for local wildlife. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
