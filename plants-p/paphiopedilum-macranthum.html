Paphiopedilum macranthum

Paphiopedilum macranthum, commonly known as the large-flowered slipper orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique slipper-shaped lip, which is a key feature of the Paphiopedilum genus. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and has fleshy, dark green leaves. Its flowers are large, showy, and come in shades of white, yellow, and green.

Paphiopedilum macranthum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
