Palala magnifica

Palala magnifica, commonly known as the royal orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can grow up to 12 inches in diameter, often displaying vibrant hues of purple, pink, and white. The plant has a thick, fleshy stem and broad, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in its humid environment. It thrives in partial shade and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Palala magnifica is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
