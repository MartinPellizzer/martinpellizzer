Peperomia tuerckheimii

Peperomia tuerckheimii, commonly known as the Tuerckheim's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface, and it produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers. The plant is well adapted to low-light conditions and prefers well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

It is also known for its ability to thrive in humid environments and is often used as a decorative foliage plant.
