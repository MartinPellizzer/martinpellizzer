Poikilacanthus oncodes var. fulvopubescens

Poikilacanthus oncodes var. fulvopubescens, commonly known as the golden pubescent sandverbena, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, often with a reddish or purple tinge. It thrives in well-drained sandy soils and is adapted to hot, dry climates.
