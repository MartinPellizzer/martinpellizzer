Polygonum cavaleriei

Polygonum cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese knotweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes various species known for their distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, with a smooth or slightly serrated edge.

It produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or red, adding a vibrant touch to its natural habitat.
