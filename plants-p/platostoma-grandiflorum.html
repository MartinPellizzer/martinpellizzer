Platostoma grandiflorum

Platostoma grandiflorum, commonly known as the large-flowered rockcress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves and produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. Native to rocky and arid environments, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight. The plant is often found in mountainous regions of the Mediterranean and parts of southern Europe.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after species for rock gardens and alpine collections.
