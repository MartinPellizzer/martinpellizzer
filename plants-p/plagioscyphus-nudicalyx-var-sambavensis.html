Plagioscyphus nudicalyx var. sambavensis

Plagioscyphus nudicalyx var. sambavensis, commonly known as the Sambava star orchid, is a unique orchid species native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. The plant has a slender, erect stem with few leaves, and its flowers are arranged in a dense, compact inflorescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
