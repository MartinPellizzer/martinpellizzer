Panicum porphyrrhizos

Panicum porphyrrhizos, commonly known as purpleroot grass, is a flowering plant species belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and distinctive purple-tinged roots, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically green or reddish in color. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and is often found in moist, disturbed soils.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
