Pleurothallis dubbeldamii

Pleurothallis dubbeldamii, commonly known as the Dubbeldam's orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-yellow to white blossoms that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
