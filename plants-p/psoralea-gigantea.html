Psoralea gigantea

Psoralea gigantea, commonly known as the giant psoralea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland and savanna habitats. The plant features erect stems with opposite, pinnately compound leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its small, yellowish flowers are clustered in spikes and bloom during the warmer months.

Psoralea gigantea is notable for its ability to thrive in arid conditions, making it an important component of its native ecosystems.
