Phalaenopsis chuxiongensis

Phalaenopsis chuxiongensis, commonly known as the Chuxiong Phalaenopsis, is a species of orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its elegant, long-lasting flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has broad, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green with a waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
