Polystichum tsingkanshanense

Polystichum tsingkanshanense, commonly known as the Tsingkanshan fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to China.

It belongs to the Dryopteridaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green, leathery fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help anchor it in soil, while its spore-producing structures are located on the underside of its leaves.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
