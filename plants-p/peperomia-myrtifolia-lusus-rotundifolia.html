Peperomia myrtifolia lusus rotundifolia

Peperomia myrtifolia lusus rotundifolia, commonly known as the rounded-leaf peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically round or oval in shape, with a vibrant green coloration. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually clustered in spikes, though they are not showy. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and ease of care make it an attractive addition to shaded or semi-shaded environments.
