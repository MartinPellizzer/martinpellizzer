Podolobium aciculiferum

Podolobium aciculiferum, commonly known as the slender-leaved podolobium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. Its seeds are enclosed in pods, which are typical of the Fabaceae family.

Podolobium aciculiferum is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
