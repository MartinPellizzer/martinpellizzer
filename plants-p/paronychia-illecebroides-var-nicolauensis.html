Paronychia illecebroides var. nicolauensis

Paronychia illecebroides var. nicolauensis, commonly known as the Nicoll's ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink and purple during the summer months. Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in arid environments.
