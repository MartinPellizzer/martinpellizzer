Plectranthus hjalmarii

Plectranthus hjalmarii, commonly known as the mountain mint, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually purple or violet in color. It has a square stem and hairy leaves, which are characteristic of the Lamiaceae family.

Plectranthus hjalmarii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
