Phalaenopsis subparishii

Phalaenopsis subparishii, commonly known as the Purple Wandering Dragon, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, long-lasting flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home environment.
