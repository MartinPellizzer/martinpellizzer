Piper ichangense

Piper ichangense, commonly known as the Ichang pepper or Chinese pepper, is a flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including China, Indonesia, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers that are typically green or yellowish. Its fruits are small berries that mature to a dark purple color, often used in traditional medicine.

The plant is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in herbal remedies for various ailments.
