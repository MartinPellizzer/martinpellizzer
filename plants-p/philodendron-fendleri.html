Philodendron fendleri

Philodendron fendleri, commonly known as the Mexican Shield Leaf Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central America.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread and climb along supports. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its striking foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
