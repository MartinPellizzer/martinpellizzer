Pennisetum cauda-ratti

Pennisetum cauda-ratti, commonly known as the rat-tail grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and distinctive, drooping inflorescences that resemble a rat's tail. The plant produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by wind, contributing to its natural spread. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Pennisetum cauda-ratti is often used in ornamental gardening for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
