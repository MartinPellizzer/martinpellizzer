Parkia dongnaiensis

Parkia dongnaiensis, commonly known as the Dong Nai acacia, is a leguminous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its bipinnate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, which enriches the soil. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in forested areas.

Its seeds are used in traditional medicine and as a source of protein.
