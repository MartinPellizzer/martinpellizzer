Piper ellipticolanceolatum

Piper ellipticolanceolatum, commonly known as the narrow-lanceleaf pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its elliptical, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into elongated, cylindrical berries. It is often found in humid, shaded environments within tropical rainforests.

The plant is valued for its aromatic leaves and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
