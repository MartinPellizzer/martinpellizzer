Plectranthus peulhorum

Plectranthus peulhorum, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate to lance-shaped leaves and clusters of tubular, fragrant flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

Plectranthus peulhorum is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
