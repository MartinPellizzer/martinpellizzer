Peperomia acuminata

Peperomia acuminata, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces delicate, tubular flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia acuminata is relatively low maintenance and suitable for beginners due to its tolerance for dry conditions.
