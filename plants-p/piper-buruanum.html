Piper buruanum

Piper buruanum, commonly known as Buruan pepper, is a flowering plant native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its fruit is a peppery berry that is used in traditional medicine and local cuisines.

The plant's botanical features make it an important species in the study of tropical flora.
