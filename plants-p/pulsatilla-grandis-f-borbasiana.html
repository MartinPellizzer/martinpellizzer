Pulsatilla grandis f. borbasiana

Pulsatilla grandis f. borbasiana, commonly known as the Borbasian pasqueflower, is a rare and distinctive variant of the pasqueflower.

The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, preferring well-drained, rocky soils.
