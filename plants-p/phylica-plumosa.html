Phylica plumosa

Phylica plumosa, commonly known as the silver tree, is a distinctive shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, feathery foliage that gives it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by red, velvety berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid climates.

Its striking appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
