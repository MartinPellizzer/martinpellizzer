Plagius ageratifolius

Plagius ageratifolius, commonly known as the leafy groundsel, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are usually alternate, simple, and covered in fine hairs.
