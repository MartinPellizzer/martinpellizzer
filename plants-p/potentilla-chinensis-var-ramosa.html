Potentilla chinensis var. ramosa

Potentilla chinensis var. ramosa, commonly known as Chinese firecracker or Chinese rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has a compact growth habit with multiple stems, and its leaves are simple, alternate, and ovate with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

The plant is valued for its ornamental value and is often used in traditional Chinese horticulture.
