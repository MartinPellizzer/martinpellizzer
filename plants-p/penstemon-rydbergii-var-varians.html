Penstemon rydbergii var. varians

Penstemon rydbergii var. varians, commonly known as the Sierra penstemon, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky habitats and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, often covered in a fine layer of hairs.

Penstemon rydbergii var. varians is valued in gardens for its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
