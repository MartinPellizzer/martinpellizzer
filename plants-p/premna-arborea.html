Premna arborea

Premna arborea, commonly known as the Indian nettle or tree nettle, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Lamiaceae.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height, characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. Premna arborea is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
