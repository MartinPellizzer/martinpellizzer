Podranea Ricasoliana

Podranea ricasoliana is a striking climbing plant known for its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in a dazzling array of colors. Native to the Canary Islands, this species thrives in warm, sunny climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Its unique ability to produce flowers on new growth makes it a favorite among gardeners and botanists alike. Often mistaken for a cactus due to its succulent stems, it is actually a member of the Apocynaceae family. This plant not only adds beauty to gardens but also serves as a valuable food source for pollinators.
