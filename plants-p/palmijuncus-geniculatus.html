Palmijuncus geniculatus

Palmijuncus geniculatus, commonly known as the zigzag palm, is a unique species of palm native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzag-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant has a slender trunk that is often covered with a fibrous sheath, and its leaves are typically arranged in a spiral pattern. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is adapted to grow in rocky or well-drained soils.

Due to its striking appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in botanical gardens and private collections.
