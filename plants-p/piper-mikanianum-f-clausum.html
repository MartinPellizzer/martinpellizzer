Piper mikanianum f. clausum

Piper mikanianum f. clausum, commonly known as the closed-flower betel vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its dark green, ovate leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are typically green when mature. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its foliage.
