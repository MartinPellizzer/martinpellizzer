Proteopsis argentea

Proteopsis argentea, commonly known as the silver proteopsis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Proteaceae, which is known for its diverse and often showy flowers. The plant is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. It produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

Proteopsis argentea is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to the Mediterranean climate of its native region.
