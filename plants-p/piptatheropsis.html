Piptatheropsis

Piptatheropsis, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of silver-gray leaves and yellow composite flowers. The genus includes several species, most of which are native to South America. These plants typically grow in dry, open habitats and are adapted to arid conditions.
