Phreatia caudata var. tenuissima

Phreatia caudata var. tenuissima, commonly known as the slender-stalked phreatia, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender pseudobulbs and long, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky pubescence. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences bearing small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically in tropical rainforests.

Due to its delicate appearance and specific habitat requirements, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
