Psychotria nicobarica

Psychotria nicobarica, commonly known as the Nicobar Psychotria, is a tropical shrub native to the Andaman and Nicobar Islands.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes coffee and gardenias, and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with smooth margins. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that are often fragrant, attracting various pollinators. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.

Psychotria nicobarica is also noted for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional remedies.
