Pteris novae-caledoniae

Pteris novae-caledoniae, commonly known as the New Caledonia water fern, is a species of fern native to the island of New Caledonia.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically green with a bluish tinge. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests. Its rhizomes are creeping and help in the spread of the plant across the forest floor.

Pteris novae-caledoniae is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological balance of its native habitat.
