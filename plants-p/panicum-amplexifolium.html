Panicum amplexifolium

Panicum amplexifolium, commonly known as blue panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its distinctive blue-gray foliage and tufted inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are often curled around the stem, giving it a unique appearance.

Panicum amplexifolium is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
