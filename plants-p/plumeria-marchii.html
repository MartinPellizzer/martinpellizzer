Plumeria marchii

Plumeria marchii, commonly known as the Madagascar plumeria, is a tropical flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate or lance-shaped. It produces woody, branched stems and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Plumeria marchii thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
