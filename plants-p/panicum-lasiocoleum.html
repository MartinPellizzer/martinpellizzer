Panicum lasiocoleum

Panicum lasiocoleum, commonly known as the silky panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a grass species characterized by its delicate, silky-haired leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and disturbed soils. Its flowering period occurs in the summer months, and it produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.

Botanically, it is notable for its fine pubescence and adaptability to wetland environments.
