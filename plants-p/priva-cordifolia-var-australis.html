Priva cordifolia var. australis

Priva cordifolia var. australis, commonly known as the Australian heartleaf privet, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the spring. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various soil types.

However, it can become invasive in certain regions if not managed properly.
