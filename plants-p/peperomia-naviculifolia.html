Peperomia naviculifolia

Peperomia naviculifolia, commonly known as the canoe-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a distinctive, compact appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are typically borne on short spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate moisture.

Due to its ornamental foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
