Paraspalathus arenarioides

Paraspalathus arenarioides, commonly known as the sand dune spurge, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. Paraspalathus arenarioides plays a role in stabilizing sand dunes and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
