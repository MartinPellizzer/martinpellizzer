Peperomia peninsularis

Peperomia peninsularis, commonly known as the Peninsular Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, rounded, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia peninsularis is a low-maintenance plant that is ideal for indoor cultivation due to its ability to tolerate dry conditions.
