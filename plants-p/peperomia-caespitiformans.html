Peperomia caespitiformans

Peperomia caespitiformans, commonly known as the compact peperomia, is a low-growing perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, rosette-like clusters of dark green, velvety leaves that are typically broad and ovate in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that emerge from the leaf axils. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its compact size and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

Peperomia caespitiformans is often grown as a decorative foliage plant for its attractive appearance and ease of care.
