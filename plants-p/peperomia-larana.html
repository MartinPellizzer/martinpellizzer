Peperomia larana

Peperomia larana, commonly known as the Shiny Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture, giving it a striking visual appeal. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which are borne on short, erect spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia larana is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
