Primulina shouchengensis

Primulina shouchengensis, commonly known as the Shoucheng primula, is a rare and endangered species native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically features dark green, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to habitat loss, conservation efforts are crucial for its survival.
