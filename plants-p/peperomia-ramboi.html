Peperomia ramboi

Peperomia ramboi, commonly known as the Ramboi Peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, fleshy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, often in shades of green or red, which are followed by small, round berries. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and minimal water requirements.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for tabletop displays and as a decorative houseplant.
