Platystemon commixtus

Platystemon commixtus, commonly known as the mixed-stemmed daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and features small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. The plant has hairy, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Platystemon commixtus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
