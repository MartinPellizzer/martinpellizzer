Pellionia longipetiolata

Pellionia longipetiolata, commonly known as the long-petioled pellionia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Pellionia longipetiolata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ease of cultivation.
