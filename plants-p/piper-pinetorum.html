Piper pinetorum

Piper pinetorum, commonly known as the pineapple pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, green fruits that mature to a yellow or orange color, resembling a miniature pineapple. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a serrated margin.

Piper pinetorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique fruit.
