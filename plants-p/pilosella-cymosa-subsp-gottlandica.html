Pilosella cymosa subsp. gottlandica

Pilosella cymosa subsp.

gottlandica, commonly known as the Gottland mouse-ear, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit.

The plant features dense, soft, gray-green foliage covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. Its small yellow flowers are typically arranged in cymes and bloom in late summer.

It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous habitats.
