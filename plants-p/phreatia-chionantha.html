Phreatia chionantha

Phreatia chionantha, commonly known as the snow-lace orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

The orchid's flowering period usually occurs during the cooler months, making it a unique addition to botanical collections.
