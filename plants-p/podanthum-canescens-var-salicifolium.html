Podanthum canescens var. salicifolium

Podanthum canescens var. salicifolium, commonly known as the silverleaf podanthum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features lanceolate, silver-gray leaves with a fuzzy texture, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
