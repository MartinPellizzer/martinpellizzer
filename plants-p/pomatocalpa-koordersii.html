Pomatocalpa koordersii

Pomatocalpa koordersii, commonly known as the Koord's spider orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance with long, slender sepals and petals. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in arid or semi-arid regions. It produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often blooming during the cooler months.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
