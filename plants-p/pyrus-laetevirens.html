Pyrus laetevirens

Pyrus laetevirens, commonly known as the sweet cherry, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Pyrus.

It is native to Europe and western Asia, and it thrives in temperate climates. The plant produces small, green leaves that turn yellow in the autumn, and it bears clusters of yellowish-green flowers in the spring. Its fruit, the sweet cherry, is widely cultivated for its juicy, flavorful berries.

The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities as well as its edible fruit.
