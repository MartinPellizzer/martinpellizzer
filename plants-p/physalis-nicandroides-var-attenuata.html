Physalis nicandroides var. attenuata

Physalis nicandroides var. attenuata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Chinese lantern, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its inflated, orange-red calyces that enclose the fruit, resembling small lanterns. The plant produces yellow flowers that develop into the distinctive, hairy, berry-like fruits. Its leaves are narrow and alternate, often covered in fine hairs.

This species is valued in traditional medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
