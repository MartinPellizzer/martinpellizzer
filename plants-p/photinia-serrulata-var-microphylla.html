Photinia serrulata var. microphylla

Photinia serrulata var. microphylla, commonly known as the glossy leaf photinia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to southeastern China.

It features small, leathery, elliptical leaves that are glossy dark green on top and lighter green underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers in spring, followed by red berries in autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its dense growth habit and attractive foliage make it suitable for hedges or as a specimen plant.
