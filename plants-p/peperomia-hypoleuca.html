Peperomia hypoleuca

Peperomia hypoleuca, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically silver or grayish-white on the upper surface, with a darker green underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that appear in clusters, often during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia hypoleuca is known for its low maintenance and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments.
