Pedicularis streptorhyncha

Its flowers are specialized for pollination by bees and other insects, with a long, tubular corolla that facilitates nectar access.

Pedicularis streptorhyncha thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.
