Pleurogyne rotata var. floribunda

Pleurogyne rotata var. floribunda, commonly known as the blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, blue-violet flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is popular in gardens for its attractive appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
