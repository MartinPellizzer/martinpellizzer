Pilosella piloselliflora subsp. paxii

Pilosella piloselliflora subsp.

paxii, commonly known as the slender pearly everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe. It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters. Its leaves are usually gray-green and covered with fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.
