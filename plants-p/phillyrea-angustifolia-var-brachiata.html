Phillyrea angustifolia var. brachiata

Phillyrea angustifolia var. brachiata, commonly known as the brachiata Phillyrea, is a variant of the narrow-leaf Phillyrea.

It is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It produces small, white flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by small, red berries.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to coastal and well-drained soils.
