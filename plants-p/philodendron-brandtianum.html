Philodendron brandtianum

Philodendron brandtianum, commonly known as the Brandt's philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy green leaves that are often variegated with white or cream patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and foliage-focused collections.
