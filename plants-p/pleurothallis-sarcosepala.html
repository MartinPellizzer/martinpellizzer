Pleurothallis sarcosepala

Pleurothallis sarcosepala, commonly known as the pink-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, pendulous flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
