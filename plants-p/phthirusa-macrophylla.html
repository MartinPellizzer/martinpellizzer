Phthirusa macrophylla

Phthirusa macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf phthirusa, is a species of flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, adding ornamental value to its appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The leaves are sometimes used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
