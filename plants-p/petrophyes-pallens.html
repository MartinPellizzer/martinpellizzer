Petrophyes pallens

Petrophyes pallens, commonly known as the rock lichen, is a unique organism that thrives in harsh, rocky environments.

It is a composite organism, consisting of a fungal partner and algal or cyanobacterial symbionts. This lichen is typically found on exposed rock surfaces in arid or semi-arid regions. Its structure is characterized by a crust-like thallus that adheres tightly to the substrate.

Petrophyes pallens plays an important role in soil formation and serves as an indicator of environmental conditions.
