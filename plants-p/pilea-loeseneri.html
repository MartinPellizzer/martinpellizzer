Pilea loeseneri

Pilea loeseneri, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Araceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its glossy, rounded leaves that resemble small coins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It is a popular indoor plant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.

Pilea loeseneri prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
