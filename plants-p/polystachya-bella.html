Polystachya bella

Polystachya bella, commonly known as the elegant sword fern, is a member of the Polystachyaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant features slender, arching fronds that are often used in ornamental horticulture. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a leathery texture.

Polystachya bella thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
