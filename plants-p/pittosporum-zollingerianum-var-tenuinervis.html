Pittosporum zollingerianum var. tenuinervis

Pittosporum zollingerianum var. tenuinervis, commonly known as the narrow-veined pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive, narrow vein pattern, and it produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and is often found in coastal and lowland forest habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and floral display.
