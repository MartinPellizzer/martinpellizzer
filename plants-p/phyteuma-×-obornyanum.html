Phyteuma × obornyanum

Phyteuma × obornyanum, commonly known as the hybrid sunflower, is a perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like yellow flowers with a central disk. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in dry meadows and rocky areas.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
