Pseudotenanthera stocksii

Pseudotenanthera stocksii, commonly known as the African orchidgrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, orchid-like flowers. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and along riverbanks, preferring well-drained soils. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Pseudotenanthera stocksii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
