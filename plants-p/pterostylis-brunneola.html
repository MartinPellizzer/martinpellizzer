Pterostylis brunneola

Pterostylis brunneola, commonly known as the brown spider-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in heathlands and open forests, often in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant features a slender, hairy stem with a single, narrow leaf and a single, pale yellow to brownish flower. Its flowers are characterized by their spider-like appearance, with a long, slender labellum.

The species is known for its low-growing habit and is often found in shaded, moist environments.
