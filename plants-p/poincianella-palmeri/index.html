Poincianella Palmeri

Poincianella palmeri is a striking desert shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. Known for its vibrant red flowers and feathery foliage, it adds a bold splash of color to arid landscapes. This plant thrives in hot, dry environments and requires minimal water once established. It is often found in rocky soils and is well adapted to extreme desert conditions. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively rare and less commonly cultivated species.
