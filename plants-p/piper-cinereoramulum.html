Piper cinereoramulum

Piper cinereoramulum, commonly known as the ash-colored pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually greenish-white and are borne in clusters, while the fruit is a small, three-lobed berry.

It is primarily cultivated for its ornamental value and has limited use in traditional medicine.
