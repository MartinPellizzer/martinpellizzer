Pistacia massiliensis

Pistacia massiliensis, commonly known as the Massiliensis pistachio tree, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to the Mediterranean region.

Due to its ornamental value and unique fruit, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and for minor commercial use.
