Portulaca samhaensis

Portulaca samhaensis, commonly known as the Korean moss rose, is a flowering plant native to Korea.

It is a low-growing annual species that thrives in dry, sandy soils. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers that are typically pink or red, resembling those of other portulaca species. Its leaves are small, fleshy, and succulent, adapted to store water.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its hardiness and attractive blooms.
