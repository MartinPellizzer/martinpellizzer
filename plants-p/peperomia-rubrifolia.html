Peperomia rubrifolia

Peperomia rubrifolia, commonly known as the red-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with striking red veins, which become more pronounced in lower light conditions. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically followed by reddish-brown berries. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers indirect sunlight, making it an ideal houseplant.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
