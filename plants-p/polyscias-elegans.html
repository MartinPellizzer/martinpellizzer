Polyscias elegans

Polyscias elegans, commonly known as the velvetleaf sycamore, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellowish-green flowers followed by dark blue berries that are edible for some bird species. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical forests.

Polyscias elegans is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to tolerate partial shade.
