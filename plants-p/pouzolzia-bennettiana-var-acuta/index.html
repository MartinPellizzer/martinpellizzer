Pouzolzia Bennettiana Var. Acuta

Pouzolzia bennettiana var. acuta is a unique fern species native to parts of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that display a striking contrast of light and dark green. This variety is often found in humid, shaded environments along forest floors or rocky outcrops. Its distinctive appearance makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and conservation efforts. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticulture.
