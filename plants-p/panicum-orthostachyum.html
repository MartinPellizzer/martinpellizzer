Panicum orthostachyum

Panicum orthostachyum, commonly known as stiff-stalk panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its upright, stiff stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers.

Panicum orthostachyum is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
