Persoonia angustifolia

Persoonia angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved geebung, is a small shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers clustered in spikes, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in coastal and sandy areas.

Persoonia angustifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
