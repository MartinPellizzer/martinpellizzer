Pandorea montana

Pandorea montana, commonly known as the mountain pandorea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a climbing shrub that produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are borne in clusters and bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds.

Pandorea montana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
