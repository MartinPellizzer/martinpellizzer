Peperomia helleri var. knudsenii

Peperomia helleri var. knudsenii, commonly known as the Knudsen's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features thick, waxy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental foliage and is relatively easy to care for in a home environment.
