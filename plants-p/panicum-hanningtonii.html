Panicum hanningtonii

Panicum hanningtonii, commonly known as the Hannington's grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces fluffy, panicle-like inflorescences that are often pink to reddish in color. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid conditions, showing drought tolerance and resilience.

Its botanical characteristics make it an attractive ornamental plant in gardens and landscaping.
