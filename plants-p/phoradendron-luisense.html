Phoradendron luisense

Phoradendron luisense, commonly known as the Luisana mistletoe, is a hemiparasitic plant that grows on the branches of host trees, primarily in the tropical regions of Central America.

Due to its parasitic nature, it can significantly affect the health of its host tree if left unchecked.
