Paramachaerium ormosioides

Paramachaerium ormosioides, commonly known as the sea hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its palmate leaves and showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant thrives in saline and sandy soils, making it well adapted to coastal environments. It produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
