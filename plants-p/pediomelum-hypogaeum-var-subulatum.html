Pediomelum hypogaeum var. subulatum

Pediomelum hypogaeum var. subulatum, commonly known as the slender pediomelum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its subulate (needle-like) leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and delicate blooms.
