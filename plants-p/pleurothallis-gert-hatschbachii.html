Pleurothallis gert-hatschbachii

Pleurothallis gert-hatschbachii, commonly known as the Gert-Hatschbach pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to Ecuador.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that grows on trees in humid, shaded forest environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, often with intricate patterns on the petals. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and specialized root system.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a challenging species to cultivate outside its natural range.
