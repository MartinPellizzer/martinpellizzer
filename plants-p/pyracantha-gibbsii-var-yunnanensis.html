Pyracantha gibbsii var. yunnanensis

Pyracantha gibbsii var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan firethorn, is a shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its bright orange to red berries that persist through winter, providing food for wildlife. The plant has thorny stems and elliptical leaves with a glossy green surface. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Its showy fruits make it a popular choice for hedging and landscape design.
