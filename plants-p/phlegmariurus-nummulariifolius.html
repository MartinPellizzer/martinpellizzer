Phlegmariurus nummulariifolius

Phlegmariurus nummulariifolius, commonly known as the silver shield moss, is a species of liverwort belonging to the family Porellaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth and small, shield-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a powdery coating. This moss is an important indicator species for assessing the health of moist, undisturbed habitats.
