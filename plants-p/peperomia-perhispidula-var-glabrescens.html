Peperomia perhispidula var. glabrescens

Peperomia perhispidula var. glabrescens, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to South America.

It features thick, waxy, and slightly hairy leaves that give it a velvety appearance. The plant has small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
