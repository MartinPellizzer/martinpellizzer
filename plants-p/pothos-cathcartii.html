Pothos cathcartii

Pothos cathcartii, commonly known as the Variegated Pothos, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves that often display a striking combination of green, white, and yellow variegation. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and is known for its ease of care, making it a popular houseplant. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers, though these are rarely seen in cultivation.

Pothos cathcartii is also valued for its ability to purify indoor air and its adaptability to various growing conditions.
