Pavonia urbaniana var. macrantha

Pavonia urbaniana var. macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered pavonia, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features showy, pink to red bracts that resemble flowers, though they are actually modified leaves. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant foliage make it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
