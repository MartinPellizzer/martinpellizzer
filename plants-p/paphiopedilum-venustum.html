Paphiopedilum venustum

Paphiopedilum venustum, commonly known as the Venustum orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a distinctive, showy flower with a pouch-like lip that is often brightly colored. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments with rich, well-drained soil. Its leaves are usually broad and strap-shaped, contributing to its lush appearance.

Paphiopedilum venustum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
