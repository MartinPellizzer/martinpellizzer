Piper mungpooanum

Piper mungpooanum, commonly known as the Malayan pepper vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by red, peppercorn-like fruits. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of aromatic foliage.
