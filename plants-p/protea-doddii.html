Protea doddii

Protea doddii, commonly known as the Dodd's protea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family, which is known for its showy, often brightly colored inflorescences. The plant features a distinctive, rounded flower head composed of numerous small, petal-like bracts. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is typically found in fynbos vegetation.

Protea doddii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
