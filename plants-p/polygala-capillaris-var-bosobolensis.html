Polygala capillaris var. bosobolensis

Polygala capillaris var. bosobolensis, commonly known as the Bosobol polygala, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are alternate and lanceolate, often with a slightly hairy texture.

The flowers are usually pale green to white, with a distinctive spiny structure at the base of the petals.
