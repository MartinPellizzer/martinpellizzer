Panicum certificandum

Panicum certificandum, commonly known as the certified panicgrass, is a species of grass native to the southern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny seeds.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in ecological restoration projects.
