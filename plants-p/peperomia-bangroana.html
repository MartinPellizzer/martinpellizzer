Peperomia bangroana

Peperomia bangroana, commonly known as the Bangroan peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with prominent veins, often displaying a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia bangroana is valued for its ornamental foliage and is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
