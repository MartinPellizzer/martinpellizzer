Pittosporum novoguineense

Pittosporum novoguineense, commonly known as the New Guinea pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Guinea.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms.

The fruit is a small, round capsule that contains several seeds.
