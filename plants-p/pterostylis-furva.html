Pterostylis furva

Pterostylis furva, commonly known as the black orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black flowers, which are often mistaken for non-plant objects due to their coloration. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests and understoreys. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces a single spike of flowers.

The species is part of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique and striking appearance.
