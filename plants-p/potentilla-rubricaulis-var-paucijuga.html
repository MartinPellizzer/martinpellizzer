Potentilla rubricaulis var. paucijuga

Potentilla rubricaulis var. paucijuga, commonly known as the slender fireweed, is a flowering plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It is characterized by its erect, slender stems and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of red and pink. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, adapted to cold climates. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in tundra and rocky habitats.

This variety is noted for its hardiness and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
