Pavonia spectabilis

Pavonia spectabilis, commonly known as the showy pavonia, is a perennial shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of vibrant pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is well-suited for moist, well-drained soils.

Pavonia spectabilis is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
