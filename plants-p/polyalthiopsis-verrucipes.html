Polyalthiopsis verrucipes

Polyalthiopsis verrucipes, commonly known as the warty-stemmed evergreen.

It is a member of the Rutaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its smooth, warty bark and lance-shaped leaves with a leathery texture. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by elliptical fruits.
