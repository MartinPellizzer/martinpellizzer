Peperomia steinbachii

Peperomia steinbachii, commonly known as the Steinbach's pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, with a glossy texture and varying shades of green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale green, arranged in clusters along the stems. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light with moderate humidity.

Peperomia steinbachii is valued for its ornamental foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
