Pongelion grande

Pongelion grande, commonly known as the giant pongelion, is a striking member of the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence that consists of a spadix surrounded by a brightly colored spathe. Native to tropical regions, this plant thrives in humid, warm environments with high light exposure. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, often reaching several feet in length.

The plant is noted for its rapid growth and ability to produce multiple blooms throughout the growing season.
