Pandanus smitinandii

Pandanus smitinandii, commonly known as the Smitinandii pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pandanaceae.

It is native to the island of Madagascar and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or green in color, which develop into fibrous fruits.

Its unique morphology and ecological significance make it an important species in the study of tropical flora.
