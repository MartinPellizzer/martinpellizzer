Portulaca longiusculotuberculata

Portulaca longiusculotuberculata, commonly known as the tuberculate portulaca, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a low-growing, succulent perennial that thrives in hot, dry environments. The plant features fleshy, ribbed stems and small, clustered leaves that help it conserve water. Its flowers are typically bright red or yellow, blooming during the day and closing at night.

It is well adapted to sandy or rocky soils and requires minimal water, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.
