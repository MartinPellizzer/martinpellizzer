Paphiopedilum malipoense var. angustatum

Paphiopedilum malipoense var. angustatum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed lady's slipper, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its unique, pouch-like lip that is often a striking pink or purple hue. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, where it relies on specific symbiotic relationships with fungi for nutrient uptake. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and rarity in cultivation.
