Polygala inexpectata

Polygala inexpectata, commonly known as the unexpected milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of western North America.

It is a member of the Polygalaceae family, which includes a variety of herbs and shrubs. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, arranged in clusters along the stems. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained soils.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
