Plectranthus purpuratus subsp. montanus

Plectranthus purpuratus subsp.

montanus, commonly known as the purple-leaf plectranthus, is a herbaceous perennial native to South Africa. It features oval, dark green leaves with a distinctive purple underside, giving it a striking visual contrast.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a versatile addition to both indoor and outdoor landscapes.
