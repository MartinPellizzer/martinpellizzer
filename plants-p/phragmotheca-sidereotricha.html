Phragmotheca sidereotricha

Phragmotheca sidereotricha, commonly known as the star-tailed spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and purple. The plant produces tiny, wind-dispersed seeds, which contribute to its spread in suitable habitats. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often growing in dense tufts.

Phragmotheca sidereotricha is typically found in grasslands and along riverbanks, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
