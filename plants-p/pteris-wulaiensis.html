Pteris wulaiensis

Pteris wulaiensis, commonly known as the Wulai fern, is a species of fern native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as mountain forests and stream sides. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce clusters of spore-bearing sorus.

The Wulai fern is valued for its ornamental appearance and ecological significance in its native habitat.
