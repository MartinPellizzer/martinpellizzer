Pleiochiton longipetiolatus

Pleiochiton longipetiolatus, commonly known as the long-petioled arrowleaf, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves with long petioles, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces bright yellow spadices surrounded by a green spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in horticultural settings.
