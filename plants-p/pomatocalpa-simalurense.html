Pomatocalpa simalurense

Pomatocalpa simalurense, commonly known as the Malagasy spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance. The plant features a slender, wiry stem with a single, narrow leaf and a spike of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is adapted to the specific ecological conditions of its native habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
