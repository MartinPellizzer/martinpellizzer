Pleurothallis ariana-dayanae

Pleurothallis ariana-dayanae, commonly known as the Dayanae orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

This epiphytic plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. It features small, intricate flowers that are usually pale green or white, with a subtle fragrance. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of tiny, delicate blooms.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments to ensure its survival.
