Phyllanthus pervilleanus

Phyllanthus pervilleanus, commonly known as the African date palm, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in some regions.

The species is also noted for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in semi-arid conditions.
