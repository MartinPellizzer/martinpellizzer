Polyochnella barteri

Polyochnella barteri, commonly known as the Barter's spider orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its intricate, spider-like appearance, with long, slender petals and a distinctive, elongated lip. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in areas with high sunlight exposure. It blooms during the late winter to early spring, producing delicate, fragrant flowers that attract specific pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
