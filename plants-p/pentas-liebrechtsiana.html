Pentas liebrechtsiana

Pentas liebrechtsiana, commonly known as the flame violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, five-petaled flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
