Pyrethrum Vahlii Var. Subflosculosum

Pyrethrum vahlii var. subflosculosum is a lesser-known member of the Asteraceae family known for its striking floral display. Native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in cool, humid climates with well-drained soil. Its unique blooms feature a combination of white and yellow petals that resemble small flowers, giving it a delicate and elegant appearance. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated in horticultural circles due to its limited availability and specific growing requirements. This plant offers both aesthetic value and potential ecological benefits, making it a fascinating subject for plant enthusiasts and researchers alike.
