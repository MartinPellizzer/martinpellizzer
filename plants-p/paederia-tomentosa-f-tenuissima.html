Paederia tomentosa f. tenuissima

Paederia tomentosa f. tenuissima, commonly known as the slender hairy paederia, is a variant of the hairy paederia species.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its fine, soft hairs covering its stems and leaves. The leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape and have a slightly pubescent surface. It produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are often borne on slender pedicels.

This plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions and thrives in moist, shaded environments.
