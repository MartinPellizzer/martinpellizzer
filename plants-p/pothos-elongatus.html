Pothos elongatus

Pothos elongatus, commonly known as the marble queen pothos, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a striking variegation of light green and white patterns. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil. It is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ability to climb and trail makes it suitable for hanging baskets or shelves.
