Physinga

Physinga, commonly known as the common tamarisk, is a shrub or small tree native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It has small, scale-like leaves that reduce water loss, making it well adapted to drought conditions. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically pink or white. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to access deep groundwater.

Physinga is often found in coastal areas and can tolerate saline soils.
