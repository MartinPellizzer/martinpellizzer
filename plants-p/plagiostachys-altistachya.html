Plagiostachys altistachya

Plagiostachys altistachya, commonly known as the tall-stemmed bamboo, is a species of grass native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 6 meters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves with a smooth surface and a fibrous texture. It is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forested or mountainous regions.

Plagiostachys altistachya is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
