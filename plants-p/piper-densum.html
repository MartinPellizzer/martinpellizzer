Piper densum

Piper densum, commonly known as the dense pepper or hairy pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruits are small berries that turn red when mature, and they are sometimes used in traditional medicine.

The plant's botanical features make it an interesting subject for both ecological studies and horticultural cultivation.
