Paraphlomis foliata subsp. foliata

Paraphlomis foliata subsp.

foliata, commonly known as the broad-leaved paraphlomis, is a perennial herb native to parts of Asia. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks.

The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface, and its flowers are small, clustered in spikes. It has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread vegetatively.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
