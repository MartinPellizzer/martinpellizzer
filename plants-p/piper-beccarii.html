Piper beccarii

Piper beccarii, commonly known as the Bornean pepper or Beccari's pepper, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many pepper species. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by elongated, fleshy fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a distinct, peppery aroma.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes cultivated for its fruit.
