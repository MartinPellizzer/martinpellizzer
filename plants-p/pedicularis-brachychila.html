Pedicularis brachychila

Pedicularis brachychila, commonly known as the short-stemmed monkey orchid, is a member of the Orobanchaceae family and is native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe.

It is a hemiparasitic plant, meaning it partially relies on other plants for water and nutrients through its specialized root system. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or rocky slopes.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many parts of its range.
