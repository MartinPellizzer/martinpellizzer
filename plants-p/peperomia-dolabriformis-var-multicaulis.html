Peperomia dolabriformis var. multicaulis

Peperomia dolabriformis var. multicaulis, commonly known as the clustered peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense clusters of small, waxy, ovate leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly glossy texture. The plant produces inconspicuous, spadix-like inflorescences, which are typical of the Piperaceae family. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
