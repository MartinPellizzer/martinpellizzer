Pritchardia lowreyana

Pritchardia lowreyana, commonly known as the Lowrey's palm, is a species of cycad from the family Cycadaceae.

It is native to the Hawaiian Islands, specifically found on the island of Oahu. This plant is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and a crown of feathery, pinnately compound leaves. It thrives in well-drained, volcanic soil and requires a tropical climate with high humidity.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat threats, Pritchardia lowreyana is considered a rare and endangered species.
