Poincianella pinnata

Poincianella pinnata, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange inflorescences that resemble flames, making it a popular ornamental species. The plant has compound leaves with long, narrow leaflets that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display.

Poincianella pinnata is also valued for its fast growth and ability to provide shade in tropical landscapes.
