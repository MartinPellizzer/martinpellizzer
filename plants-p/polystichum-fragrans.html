Polystichum fragrans

Polystichum fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant wood fern, is a species of fern native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant features dark green, glossy leaves with a distinctive, fragrant scent when crushed. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique aroma and elegant appearance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
