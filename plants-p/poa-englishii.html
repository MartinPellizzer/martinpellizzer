Poa englishii

Poa englishii, commonly known as English bentgrass, is a cool-season grass native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, low-growing habit and fine, bluish-green foliage. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Poa englishii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and turfgrass applications.
