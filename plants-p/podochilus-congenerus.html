Podochilus congenerus

Podochilus congenerus, commonly known as the small-flowered orchid, is a tropical plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant features slender, erect stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and typically have three petals and three sepals, with a distinctive lip that is often brightly colored.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in the undergrowth of tropical forests.
