Protea madiensis

Protea madiensis, commonly known as the Madiensis protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence composed of numerous pink to white floral bracts. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils in the Cape Floristic Region. It is a shrub that can reach heights of up to 2 meters, with leathery, evergreen leaves.

Protea madiensis is also noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
