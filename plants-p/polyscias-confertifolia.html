Polyscias confertifolia

Polyscias confertifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf sycamore, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, palmately lobed leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter in color underneath. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellowish-green flowers followed by orange-red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.
