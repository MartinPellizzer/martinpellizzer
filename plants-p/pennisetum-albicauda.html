Pennisetum albicauda

Pennisetum albicauda, commonly known as silver beard grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and fluffy, silver-white plumes that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens. Its feathery foliage adds visual interest throughout the growing season.

Pennisetum albicauda is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
