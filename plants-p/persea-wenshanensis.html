Persea wenshanensis

Persea wenshanensis, commonly known as the Wenshan persimmon, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to China, specifically the Yunnan Province, and is known for its ornamental value and edible fruit. The plant is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptic in shape, with a glossy green upper surface and a lighter underside.

The fruit is a globose berry that ripens to a deep orange color, often used in local cuisine and traditional medicine.
