Peperomia argyreia

Peperomia argyreia, commonly known as the silver mohair plant, is a compact, low-maintenance houseplant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, velvety foliage with a striking silver-gray appearance, giving it a unique and elegant look. The plant has short, upright stems and produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate watering.

Its compact size and ornamental leaves make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
