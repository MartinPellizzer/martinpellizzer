Potentilla polyphylla var. kinabaluensis

Potentilla polyphylla var. kinabaluensis, commonly known as the Kinabalu potentilla, is a shrub native to the high-altitude regions of Borneo.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has dark green, ovate leaves with serrated edges, and its stems are covered in fine, glandular hairs. It thrives in cool, moist environments, typically found in cloud forests and montane areas.

This variant is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique appearance.
