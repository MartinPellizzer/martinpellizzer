Peperomia nicoyana

Peperomia nicoyana, commonly known as the Nicoyan Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Central America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia nicoyana is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
