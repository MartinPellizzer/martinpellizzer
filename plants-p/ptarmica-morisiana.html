Ptarmica morisiana

Ptarmica morisiana, commonly known as the Morisian penny-cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions. Ptarmica morisiana has pinnately divided leaves and produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds.

It is a relatively rare species, with limited distribution in its native habitat.
