Pellionia elatostemoides

Pellionia elatostemoides, commonly known as the silverleaf pellionia, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in the undergrowth of tropical forests.

Its foliage is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture for its aesthetic qualities.
