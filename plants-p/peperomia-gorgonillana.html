Peperomia gorgonillana

Peperomia gorgonillana, commonly known as the Gorgonilla peperomia, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a compact, low-growing plant with thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

Peperomia gorgonillana is relatively easy to care for and is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage.
