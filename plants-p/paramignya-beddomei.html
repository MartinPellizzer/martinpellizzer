Paramignya beddomei

Paramignya beddomei, commonly known as the Bornean orchid.

It is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the family Orchidaceae, native to the rainforests of Borneo. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with a short rhizome and a single, narrow leaf. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive hooded labellum.

Paramignya beddomei is often found in shaded, humid environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
