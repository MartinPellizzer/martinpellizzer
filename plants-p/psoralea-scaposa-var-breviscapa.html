Psoralea scaposa var. breviscapa

Psoralea scaposa var. breviscapa, commonly known as the short-stemmed psoralea, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. The plant features small, pinnately compound leaves and produces yellowish-green flowers arranged in spikes. Its seeds are small and often dispersed by wind, contributing to its spread in arid environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to harsh conditions.
