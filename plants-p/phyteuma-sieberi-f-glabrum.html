Phyteuma sieberi f. glabrum

Phyteuma sieberi f. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth phyteuma, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. It is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
