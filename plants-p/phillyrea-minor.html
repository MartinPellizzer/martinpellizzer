Phillyrea minor

Phillyrea minor, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Phillyrea, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers during the spring, which are followed by small, spherical berries. Phillyrea minor is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and woodland areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
