Physurus foliosus

Physurus foliosus, commonly known as the leafy physurus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a compact head. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. The plant is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its compact form and delicate blooms.

It is also used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory properties.
