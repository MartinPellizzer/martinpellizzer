Pittosporum kusaiense

Pittosporum kusaiense, commonly known as the Kusaiense Pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, white flowers that are fragrant and typically bloom in spring. These flowers are followed by spherical, red berries that are edible for some bird species.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
