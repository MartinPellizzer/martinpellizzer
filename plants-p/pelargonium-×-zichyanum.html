Pelargonium × zichyanum

Pelargonium × zichyanum, commonly known as the Zanzibar geranium, is a hybrid species native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to orange and yellow. The plant typically has broad, lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a garden plant.
