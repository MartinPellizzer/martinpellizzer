Pilea affinis var. affinis

Pilea affinis var. affinis, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rosettes of rounded, fleshy leaves that are typically light green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by pale green berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it ideal for indoor cultivation and decorative purposes.
