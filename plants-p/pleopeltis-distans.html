Pleopeltis distans

Pleopeltis distans, commonly known as the broad-leafed rock fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its large, leathery, ovate to elliptical leaves. The plant typically grows on rocky substrates, often in humid, shaded environments. Its fronds are usually bi- or tri-pinnate, with a distinctive, sometimes hairy underside.

Pleopeltis distans is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various soil conditions.
