Panicum terminale

Panicum terminale, commonly known as little bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and tufted seed heads that turn reddish-brown in the fall. This plant is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in prairie ecosystems. It plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.

Panicum terminale is also a popular choice for native plant gardens due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
