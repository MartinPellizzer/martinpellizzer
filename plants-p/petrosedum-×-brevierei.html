Petrosedum × brevierei

Petrosedum × brevierei, commonly known as the hybrid stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, compact leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, white, or purple, typically in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its hybrid nature, it may exhibit a variety of leaf and flower colors, adding visual interest to garden landscapes.
