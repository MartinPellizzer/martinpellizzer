Pleonotoma Glaberrima

Coral tree, scientifically known as Pleonotoma glaberrima, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa. Its vibrant coral-like flowers bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual display that captivates gardeners and nature lovers alike. This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value, adding a splash of color to landscapes and gardens. Despite its beauty, it requires specific care to thrive, including well-drained soil and adequate sunlight. With its unique appearance and ecological significance, the coral tree remains a popular choice for both horticultural and environmental purposes.
