Podochilopsis dalatensis

Podochilopsis dalatensis, commonly known as the Dalat orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam.

It is a herbaceous plant with slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white or yellowish in color. The plant produces aerial roots and has a distinctive inflorescence with numerous tiny, fragrant blossoms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions with rich, moist soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
