Polygala chuiti

Polygala chuiti, commonly known as the Chuiti milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often pink or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its small, compound leaves and unique floral structure contribute to its ecological significance in its native habitat.

Polygala chuiti is also valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in herbal medicine.
