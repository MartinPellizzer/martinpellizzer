Pilea truncata

Pilea truncata, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rounded, fleshy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique leaf shape and low maintenance requirements contribute to its widespread cultivation.
