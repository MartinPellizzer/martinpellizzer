Panicum eccentricos

Panicum eccentricos, commonly known as the eccentric grass, is a perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow leaves. The plant produces fluffy, seed-bearing panicles that are often brightly colored, enhancing its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Due to its unique growth pattern and visual appeal, it is a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
