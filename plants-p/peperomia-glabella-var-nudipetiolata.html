Peperomia glabella var. nudipetiolata

Peperomia glabella var. nudipetiolata, commonly known as the naked petiole peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance houseplant native to South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture, and it has a unique characteristic of having short or absent petioles, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or red, which are not particularly showy.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it an ideal choice for indoor gardening.
