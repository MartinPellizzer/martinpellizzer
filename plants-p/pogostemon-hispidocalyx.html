Pogostemon hispidocalyx

Pogostemon hispidocalyx, commonly known as the hairy calyx pogostemon, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, clustered flowers. Its hairy calyx is a distinguishing feature, giving it its common name.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks.
