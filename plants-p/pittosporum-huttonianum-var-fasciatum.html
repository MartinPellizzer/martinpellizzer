Pittosporum huttonianum var. fasciatum

Pittosporum huttonianum var. fasciatum, commonly known as the Hutton's Pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with distinct white or yellow variegation. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in spring, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its unique foliage and adaptability make it a valuable addition to gardens and conservation efforts.
