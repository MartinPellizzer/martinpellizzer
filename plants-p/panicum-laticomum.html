Panicum laticomum

Panicum laticomum, commonly known as broadleaf panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its wide, flat leaves and tall, branched inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has a fibrous root system and is often used in restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil.

Its flowering period usually occurs in the summer months, producing small, spikelet-like flowers.
