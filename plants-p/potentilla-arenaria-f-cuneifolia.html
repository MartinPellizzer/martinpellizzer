Potentilla arenaria f. cuneifolia

Potentilla arenaria f. cuneifolia, commonly known as the sandwort cinquefoil, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It features small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, which is characteristic of the 'cuneifolia' form. It thrives in well-drained, sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open habitats.

This plant is valued for its hardiness and ornamental appeal in garden settings.
