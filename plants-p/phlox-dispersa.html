Phlox dispersa

Phlox dispersa, commonly known as spreading phlox, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features showy clusters of pink to violet flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Phlox dispersa is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
