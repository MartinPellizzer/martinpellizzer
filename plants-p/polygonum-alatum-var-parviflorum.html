Polygonum alatum var. parviflorum

Polygonum alatum var. parviflorum, commonly known as narrow-leaved smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It features slender, erect stems that are often reddish in color, and it produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purplish in hue. The leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a distinctive winged appearance along the edges. It thrives in moist, sunny environments such as wet meadows and stream banks.

This plant is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
