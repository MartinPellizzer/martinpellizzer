Phragmipedium longifolium var. dariense

Phragmipedium longifolium var. dariense, commonly known as the Darien phragmipedium, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange, with intricate patterns on the petals. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

This orchid is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by enthusiasts due to its unique and striking floral display.
