Peperomia attenuata var. roseispica

Peperomia attenuata var. roseispica, commonly known as the Rosy Spicy Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a slightly wavy margin and a striking reddish-pink hue on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique leaf coloration and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to shaded or semi-shaded spaces.
