Pennilabium hewittii

Pennilabium hewittii, commonly known as the Hewitt's pincushion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, spherical inflorescence that resembles a pincushion. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.
