Pithecellobium larensis

Pithecellobium larensis, commonly known as the Larensis burr legume, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its typical compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. Its seed pods are woody and contain several seeds, which are sometimes used in traditional medicine.

The species is noted for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and is often found in dry forest ecosystems.
