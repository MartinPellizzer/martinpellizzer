Peperomia humifusa

Peperomia humifusa, commonly known as the ground peperomia, is a low-growing perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, often with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or red, emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

Due to its compact size and ornamental foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
