Peperomia obruenda

Peperomia obruenda, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, waxy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia obruenda is relatively low maintenance and resistant to pests, making it an ideal plant for beginners.
