Panicum panis

Panicum panis, commonly known as yellow panicgrass, is a warm-season grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces fluffy, yellowish to reddish panicles during the flowering season. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a hardy choice for ornamental and erosion control purposes. Its rapid growth and drought tolerance contribute to its adaptability in various environmental conditions.

The seeds of Panicum panis are small and wind-dispersed, aiding in its natural propagation.
