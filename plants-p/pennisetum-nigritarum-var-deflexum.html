Pennisetum nigritarum var. deflexum

Pennisetum nigritarum var. deflexum, commonly known as black fountain grass, is a clump-forming perennial grass native to Africa.

It features slender, arching stems that produce dense clusters of dark purple to black bracts, giving it its striking appearance. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its unique coloration and architectural form make it a standout in landscape design.
