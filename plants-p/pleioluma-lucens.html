Pleioluma lucens

Pleioluma lucens, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, bright yellow petals with a white center, creating a star-like appearance. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers bloom in clusters, often during the rainy season, and it prefers warm, humid conditions with indirect light.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty in horticulture.
