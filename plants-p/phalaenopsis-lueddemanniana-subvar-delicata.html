Phalaenopsis lueddemanniana subvar. delicata

Phalaenopsis lueddemanniana subvar. delicata, commonly known as the delicate phalaenopsis, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically blooms once a year, producing long-lasting flowers that can last several months. It prefers bright, indirect light and moderate humidity, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its elegant appearance and ease of care contribute to its popularity among orchid enthusiasts.
