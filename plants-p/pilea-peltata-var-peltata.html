Pilea peltata var. peltata

Pilea peltata var. peltata, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rounded, shield-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green, often with a subtle variegation of silver or white. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant.
