Platanthera formosana

Platanthera formosana, commonly known as the Formosan twayblade, is an orchid native to Taiwan.

It is a terrestrial herb with a slender, unbranched stem that grows up to 30 centimeters in height. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and grasslands.

Its root system is adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil, and it relies on mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient uptake.
