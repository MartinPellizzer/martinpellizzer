Polystachya cultriformis var. alba

Polystachya cultriformis var. alba, commonly known as the white-tipped sword fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polystachyaceae and is characterized by its sword-shaped, glossy leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, fertile soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
