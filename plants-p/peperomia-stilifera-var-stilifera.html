Peperomia stilifera var. stilifera

Peperomia stilifera var. stilifera, commonly known as the upright peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which bloom during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a versatile addition to both indoor and outdoor gardens.
