Phylica plumigera

Phylica plumigera, commonly known as the silver crown or white flame, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical crown of white, fluffy flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Phylica plumigera is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
