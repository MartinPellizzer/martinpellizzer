Pinus yunnanensis var. yunnanensis

Pinus yunnanensis var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan pine, is a coniferous tree native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and dense, pyramidal crown, which provides habitat for various wildlife species. The tree produces cones that contain edible seeds, which are an important food source for birds and small mammals. Its needles are typically dark green and arranged in bundles of five, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This species is well adapted to high-altitude environments and plays a significant role in the ecological balance of its native habitat.
