Parasenecio adenostyloides

Parasenecio adenostyloides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved greenweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its flowering stems bear small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in the spring.

The species is characterized by its robust root system and adaptability to arid conditions.
