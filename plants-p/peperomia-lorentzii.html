Peperomia lorentzii

Peperomia lorentzii, commonly known as the Lorentz's pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features oval, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or brown, followed by spore-bearing structures called sporangia. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia lorentzii is relatively low maintenance and suitable for indoor cultivation.
