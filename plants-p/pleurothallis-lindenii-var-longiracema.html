Pleurothallis lindenii var. longiracema

Pleurothallis lindenii var. longiracema, commonly known as the long-stemmed spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pendulous inflorescences that bear numerous small, intricate flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded forest environments. Its flowers are often white or pale green, with intricate patterns that mimic the appearance of spider-like structures.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique morphology and relatively easy care requirements.
