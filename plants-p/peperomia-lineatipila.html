Peperomia lineatipila

Peperomia lineatipila, commonly known as the zigzag peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves with a distinctive zigzag pattern along the midrib, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is a popular houseplant due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia lineatipila prefers well-draining soil and moderate humidity, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.
