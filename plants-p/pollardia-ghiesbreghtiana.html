Pollardia ghiesbreghtiana

Pollardia ghiesbreghtiana, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to survive in arid conditions.

The orchid has a short-lived bloom period, making it a rare and sought-after specimen in botanical gardens.
