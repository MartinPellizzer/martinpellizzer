Pilosella pseudopiloselliflora

Pilosella pseudopiloselliflora, commonly known as the false pilosella, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky areas, thriving in cool and moist conditions. Its small, yellowish flowers are arranged in dense clusters, and the leaves are usually hairy and narrow.
