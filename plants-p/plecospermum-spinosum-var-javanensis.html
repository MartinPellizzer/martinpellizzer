Plecospermum spinosum var. javanensis

Plecospermum spinosum var. javanensis, commonly known as the Javanese spiny plecospermum, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and alternate, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by woody capsules containing numerous seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in disturbed soils and open areas.

Due to its hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated as an ornamental plant.
