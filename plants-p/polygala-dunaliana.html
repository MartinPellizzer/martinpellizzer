Polygala dunaliana

Polygala dunaliana, commonly known as the yellow milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the Polygalaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in spikes, and it is often found in coastal and inland fynbos vegetation. It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
