Piper roemeri

Piper roemeri, commonly known as the Mexican pepper plant, is a tropical vine native to Central America and parts of Mexico.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants such as the common black pepper. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into elongated, peppery berries. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental and medicinal value.
