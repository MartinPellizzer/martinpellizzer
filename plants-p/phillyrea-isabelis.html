Phillyrea isabelis

Phillyrea isabelis, commonly known as Isabel's Phillyrea, is a species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in clusters during the spring, which are followed by small, spherical berries. Phillyrea isabelis thrives in well-drained soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded locations.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate coastal conditions.
