Polygonum lapathifolium subsp. mesomorphum

Polygonum lapathifolium subsp.

mesomorphum, commonly known as the broad-leaved smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia. It features upright stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height, often branched towards the top.

The leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a rough, hairy texture on the upper surface. Its small, clustered flowers are typically pink to red, appearing in late summer to early autumn.

This species is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of water bodies.
