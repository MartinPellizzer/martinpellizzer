Piper deamii var. yojoanum

Piper deamii var. yojoanum, commonly known as the Yojoanum pepper plant, is a variant of the Piper deamii species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for horticultural study.
