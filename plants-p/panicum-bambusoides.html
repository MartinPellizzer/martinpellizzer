Panicum bambusoides

Panicum bambusoides, commonly known as the bamboo grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and feathery, panicle-shaped inflorescences. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are usually green and slightly hairy. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental and ecological landscaping.

Its growth habit resembles bamboo, hence its common name, and it is often used in erosion control and as a decorative plant in gardens.
