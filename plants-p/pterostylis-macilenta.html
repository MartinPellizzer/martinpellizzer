Pterostylis macilenta

Pterostylis macilenta, commonly known as the slender helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. The plant has a slender, hairy stem and produces a single, greenish-brown flower with a distinctive helmet-shaped labellum. Its small size and inconspicuous appearance make it challenging to spot in its natural habitat.

This orchid is part of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique pollination mechanisms.
