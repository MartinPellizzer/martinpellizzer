Polystichum franchetii

Polystichum franchetii, commonly known as the Franchet's wood fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The fronds are deeply divided into narrow, lance-shaped segments, giving the plant a feathery appearance. It prefers shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

This fern is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
