Panicum obtusiglume

Panicum obtusiglume, commonly known as broad-leaved panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and dense, cylindrical inflorescences. The plant produces small, two-ranked spikelets with short, blunt glumes, giving it its specific epithet. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

Panicum obtusiglume is valued for its ornamental appeal and use in erosion control.
