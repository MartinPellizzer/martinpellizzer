Phegopteris canescens

Phegopteris canescens, commonly known as the silver fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Pteridaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, silver-colored fronds that are covered in fine, silky hairs. Native to regions of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
