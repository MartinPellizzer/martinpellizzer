Pericopsis Elata

Pericopsis elata is a rare and striking tree native to the rainforests of Central Africa. Known for its massive, heavy wood and distinctive bark, it plays a vital role in its ecosystem. The plant's unique appearance and ecological significance make it a subject of scientific interest. Unfortunately, it faces threats from deforestation and habitat loss. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival for future generations.
