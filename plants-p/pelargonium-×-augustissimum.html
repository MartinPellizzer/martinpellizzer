Pelargonium × augustissimum

Pelargonium × augustissimum, commonly known as the Swedish geranium, is a hybrid species within the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, rounded leaves that are often adorned with distinctive white or silver markings. The plant produces vibrant clusters of five-petaled flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Native to southern Africa, it is a popular ornamental plant due to its showy appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.

Pelargonium × augustissimum prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for both garden beds and container planting.
