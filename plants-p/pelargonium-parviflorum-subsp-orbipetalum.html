Pelargonium parviflorum subsp. orbipetalum

Pelargonium parviflorum subsp.

orbipetalum, commonly known as the miniature geranium, is a compact, flowering plant native to southern Africa. It features small, rounded leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, giving them a velvety texture.

The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is widely cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
