Pertya scandens f. maximowicziana

Pertya scandens f. maximowicziana, commonly known as the Maximowicziana form of the climbing pertya, is a variant of the species native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing herb characterized by its slender stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
