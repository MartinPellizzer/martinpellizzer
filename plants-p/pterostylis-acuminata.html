Pterostylis acuminata

Pterostylis acuminata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf greenhood, is an orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a slender, often hairy stem and a single, narrow lance-shaped leaf. The plant produces a single, greenish-yellow flower with a distinctive hood-like structure, which is typical of the greenhood orchid genus. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understoreys.

The species is known for its intricate floral morphology and is part of the Orchidaceae family.
