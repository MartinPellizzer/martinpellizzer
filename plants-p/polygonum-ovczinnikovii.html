Polygonum ovczinnikovii

Polygonum ovczinnikovii, commonly known as the Ovczinnikov's knotweed, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants commonly referred to as smartweeds or knotweeds. The plant is characterized by its erect stems, alternate leaves, and small, clustered flowers. It typically grows in moist or wet habitats such as marshes and along riverbanks.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and taxonomic studies.
