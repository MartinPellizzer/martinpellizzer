Parrasia camporum

Parrasia camporum, commonly known as the Florida panther lily, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers with prominent, reflexed petals. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along riverbanks.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is protected under various conservation programs.
