Polygonum strigosum var. sanguineum

Polygonum strigosum var. sanguineum, commonly known as redstem smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It features upright, slender stems that are typically reddish in color, especially when mature. The leaves are lance-shaped, covered in fine hairs, and have a slightly hairy texture. It produces small, clustered flowers that range from pink to red, which bloom in late summer.

This plant is often found in moist soils along stream banks, wetlands, and disturbed areas.
