Pleopeltis ballivianii

Pleopeltis ballivianii, commonly known as the Bolivian rock fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, leathery fronds. The plant typically grows on rocky surfaces or tree trunks in humid, tropical environments. Pleopeltis ballivianii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
