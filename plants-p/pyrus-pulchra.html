Pyrus pulchra

Pyrus pulchra, commonly known as the beautiful pear, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to parts of East Asia, including China and Japan, where it thrives in temperate climates. The plant is characterized by its ornamental value, featuring showy white or pink flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are typically broad and lance-shaped, providing a lush green canopy throughout the growing season.

The fruit, a small pear-like berry, is edible and has a sweet, aromatic flavor.
