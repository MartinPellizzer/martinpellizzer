Petalochilus Minor

Petalochilus minor is a rare and delicate orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. This small flowering plant is known for its unique, fragrant blooms that appear in late spring. Despite its beauty, it remains largely unknown to the general public due to its limited distribution. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this species from habitat loss and illegal collection. Its existence highlights the importance of preserving biodiversity in fragile ecosystems.
