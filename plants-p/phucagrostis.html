Phucagrostis

Phucagrostis, commonly known as the narrowleaf grass, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plants typically produce small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. Phucagrostis species are often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

They play a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various insects and small animals.
