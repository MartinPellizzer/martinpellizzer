Pandanus lucidus

Pandanus lucidus, commonly known as the shining pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or green in color. Its fruits are covered in a fibrous husk and contain edible seeds.

Pandanus lucidus thrives in coastal areas and is often used in local cultures for both practical and ceremonial purposes.
