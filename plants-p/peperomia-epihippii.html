Peperomia epihippii

Peperomia epihippii, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to South America.

It features oval, waxy leaves that are typically a silvery-gray color, with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and white, which are usually hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia epihippii is ideal for indoor cultivation due to its ability to tolerate dry conditions and minimal care requirements.
