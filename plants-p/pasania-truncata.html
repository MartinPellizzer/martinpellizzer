Pasania truncata

Pasania truncata, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white to pale pink petals. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Pasania truncata thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
