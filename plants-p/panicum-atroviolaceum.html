Panicum atroviolaceum

Panicum atroviolaceum, commonly known as black violet panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and dense, violet-colored inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a bluish-green hue.

It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its striking appearance and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
