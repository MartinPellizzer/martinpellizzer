Panicum racemosum

Panicum racemosum, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and dense clusters of small, reddish-brown spikelets. The plant typically grows in open areas such as fields, roadsides, and disturbed soils. Its flowering period occurs during the warm season, and it produces wind-pollinated flowers.

Panicum racemosum is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
