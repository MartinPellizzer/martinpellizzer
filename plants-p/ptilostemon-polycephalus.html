Ptilostemon polycephalus

Ptilostemon polycephalus, commonly known as the clustered ptilostemon, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or inland regions. The plant features a short stem with numerous small, clustered leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are usually small and white or pale blue, arranged in dense clusters at the tips of the stems.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
