Pennisetum nigritarum

Pennisetum nigritarum, commonly known as blackfoot grass, is a species of grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

Pennisetum nigritarum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
