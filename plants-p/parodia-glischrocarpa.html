Parodia glischrocarpa

Parodia glischrocarpa, commonly known as the Spiny Pincushion Cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that are covered in spines and are often adorned with clusters of small, white flowers. The plant thrives in arid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.

Parodia glischrocarpa is relatively easy to care for, making it suitable for both novice and experienced growers.
