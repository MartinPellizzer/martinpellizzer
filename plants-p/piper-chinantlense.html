Piper chinantlense

Piper chinantlense, commonly known as Chinantlan pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the highlands of southern Mexico, particularly in the states of Oaxaca and Chiapas. The plant is characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, heart-shaped leaves. It produces clusters of tiny, greenish-white flowers that develop into small, peppery fruits.

This species is valued for its aromatic leaves and fruits, which are used in traditional culinary and medicinal practices.
