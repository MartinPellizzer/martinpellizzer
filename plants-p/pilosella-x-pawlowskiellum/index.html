Pilosella × Pawlowskiellum

Pilosella × pawlowskiellum is a hybrid plant known for its distinctive appearance and ecological significance. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is often found in meadows and open habitats. This species exhibits a combination of traits from its parent plants, making it a subject of interest for botanists. Its delicate white and yellow flowers add beauty to the landscape while supporting local pollinators. Despite its popularity, it remains relatively rare and requires specific conditions to thrive.
