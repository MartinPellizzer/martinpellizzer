Plectranthus tysonii

Plectranthus tysonii, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces small, oval leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Plectranthus tysonii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
