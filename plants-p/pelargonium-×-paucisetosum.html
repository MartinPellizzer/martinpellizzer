Pelargonium × paucisetosum

Pelargonium × paucisetosum, commonly known as the silver-leaf geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are typically rounded or heart-shaped and often have a velvety texture.

It produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, depending on the cultivar. This plant is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
