Pyrostria comorensis

Pyrostria comorensis, commonly known as the Comorian star grass, is a perennial herb native to the Comoros Islands.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is typically found in forest understories.

Pyrostria comorensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
