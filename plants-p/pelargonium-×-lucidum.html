Pelargonium × lucidum

Pelargonium × lucidum, commonly known as the angel's trumpets, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that can be white, pink, or purple in color. The plant is native to South Africa and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

The leaves are typically lobed and have a slightly hairy texture, adding to its distinctive appearance.
