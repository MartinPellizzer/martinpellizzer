Physosiphon inaequisepalus

Physosiphon inaequisepalus, commonly known as the unequal-sepaled physosiphon, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Physosiphonaceae.

It is characterized by its unique floral structure, featuring sepals of unequal size and shape. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are usually alternate and have a smooth, waxy surface.

The flowers are small and inconspicuous, often clustered in dense inflorescences.
