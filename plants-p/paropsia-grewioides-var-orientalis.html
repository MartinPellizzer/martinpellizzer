Paropsia grewioides var. orientalis

Paropsia grewioides var. orientalis, commonly known as the oriental paropsia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated margin.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland forest understories.
