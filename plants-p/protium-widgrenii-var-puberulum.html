Protium widgrenii var. puberulum

Protium widgrenii var. puberulum, commonly known as the smooth-leafed tamanu, is a tropical plant belonging to the Burseraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. Its small, yellowish flowers are followed by spherical, woody fruits containing several seeds.

The species is valued for its timber and has traditional uses in local medicine.
