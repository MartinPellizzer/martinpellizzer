Pariti tiliaceum

Pariti tiliaceum, commonly known as the silky tiliaceum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the family Tiliaceae, which includes species known for their showy flowers and often heart-shaped leaves. The plant is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom during the wet season. Its leaves are typically smooth, glossy, and have a soft, velvety texture.

Pariti tiliaceum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along riverbanks.
