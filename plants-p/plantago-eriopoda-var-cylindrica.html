Plantago eriopoda var. cylindrica

Plantago eriopoda var. cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical plantain, is a perennial herb native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It features rosettes of broad, oval leaves covered in dense, soft hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. The plant produces tall, cylindrical flower spikes that bear small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and disturbed areas.

Its fibrous roots and succulent leaves make it adaptable to various environmental conditions.
