Plantago fengdouensis

Plantago fengdouensis, commonly known as the Fengdou plantain, is a species of flowering plant in the Plantaginaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically the Guangdong province, and thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a smooth surface and produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters. It is known for its medicinal properties, often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species for both ecological and pharmaceutical studies.
