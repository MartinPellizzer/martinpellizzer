Peperomia grisebachii

Peperomia grisebachii, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a species of flowering plant in the Piperaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically borne in clusters. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive foliage.

Peperomia grisebachii thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.
