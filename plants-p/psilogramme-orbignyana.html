Psilogramme orbignyana

Psilogramme orbignyana, commonly known as the orchid orchilla, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers and is often found in humid, shaded environments. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Psilogramme orbignyana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
