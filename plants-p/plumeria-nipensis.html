Plumeria nipensis

Plumeria nipensis, commonly known as the Hawaiian plumeria, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant has smooth, pale green bark and opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in coastal areas and dry forests.

The flowers are used in traditional Hawaiian ceremonies and are also popular in ornamental gardening.
