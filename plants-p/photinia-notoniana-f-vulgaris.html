Photinia notoniana f. vulgaris

Photinia notoniana f. vulgaris, commonly known as the glossy leaf photinia, is a compact shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves that provide a striking visual contrast against its showy, pinkish-red flower clusters. The plant produces small, elliptical fruits that are edible for some bird species. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is often used in gardens for its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
