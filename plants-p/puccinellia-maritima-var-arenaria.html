Puccinellia maritima var. arenaria

Puccinellia maritima var. arenaria, commonly known as beach sand grass, is a perennial grass species adapted to coastal environments.

It thrives in sandy soils and is often found in dunes and salt marshes along shorelines. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is well-suited to withstand salt spray and strong winds.

Its ecological role includes stabilizing dunes and providing habitat for coastal wildlife.
