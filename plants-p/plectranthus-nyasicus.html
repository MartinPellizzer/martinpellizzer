Plectranthus nyasicus

Plectranthus nyasicus, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of tubular flowers that range in color from purple to white. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.

Plectranthus nyasicus is popular as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and blooms.
