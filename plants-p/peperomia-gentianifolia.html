Peperomia gentianifolia

Peperomia gentianifolia, commonly known as the gentian leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with a vibrant green color and a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white or pale blue, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia gentianifolia is relatively low maintenance and drought tolerant once established.
