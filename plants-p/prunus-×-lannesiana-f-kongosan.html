Prunus × lannesiana f. kongosan

Prunus × lannesiana f. kongosan, commonly known as the Kongoasan cherry, is a compact, ornamental shrub native to Japan.

It is a natural hybrid between Prunus lannesiana and Prunus × yedoensis, characterized by its dense growth habit and showy, double pink flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1–2 meters and is prized for its vibrant spring blossoms.

Its foliage is dark green, providing an attractive backdrop to the floral display, and it is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value.
