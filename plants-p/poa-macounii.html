Poa macounii

Poa macounii, commonly known as Macoun's bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and tufted growth habit, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and disturbed soils. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of spikelets, which are often tinged with a bluish hue.

Poa macounii is valued for its ornamental qualities and its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
