Pleurandra riparia var. glabriuscula

Pleurandra riparia var. glabriuscula, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed pleurandra, is a variant of the pleurandra plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, often in shades of white or pale yellow. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near streams or in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
