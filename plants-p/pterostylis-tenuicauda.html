Pterostylis tenuicauda

Pterostylis tenuicauda, commonly known as the slender-tailed sun-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single, narrow, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and have a distinctive, elongated spur.

It is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is known for its unique morphology and ecological significance.
