Plantago orbignyana subsp. niederleinii

Plantago orbignyana subsp.

niederleinii, commonly known as the Niederlein's plantain, is a perennial herb native to South America. It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and have a rough texture.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers with yellow petals, which are borne on slender stalks. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands or near water sources.

This subspecies is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
