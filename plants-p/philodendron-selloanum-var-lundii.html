Philodendron selloanum var. lundii

Philodendron selloanum var. lundii, commonly known as the Selloanum lundii, is a climbing plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant has a vining growth habit, making it suitable for hanging baskets or trained on trellises. Its foliage is often marked with subtle variegation, adding visual interest.

It prefers bright, indirect light and consistent moisture, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
