Poa tovarii

Poa tovarii, commonly known as Tovar's bluegrass, is a species of grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and bluish-green foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and grasslands. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of spikelets, which are small, compact flower clusters.

Poa tovarii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its attractive appearance and hardiness.
