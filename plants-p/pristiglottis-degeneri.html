Pristiglottis degeneri

Pristiglottis degeneri, commonly known as the dwarf Christmas star, is a small, evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with small, waxy, dark green leaves that have a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped white flowers in late winter to early spring. It is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and can tolerate light frost.

Pristiglottis degeneri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
