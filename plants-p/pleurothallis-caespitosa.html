Pleurothallis caespitosa

Pleurothallis caespitosa, commonly known as the clustered pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, clumping growth habit and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the understory of forests. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it has a short rhizome system.

This orchid is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
