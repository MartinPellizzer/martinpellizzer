Polygala sciophila

Polygala sciophila, commonly known as the shade polygala, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and thickets. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, delicate flowers that are usually pink or white in color. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, contributing to its overall delicate appearance.

Polygala sciophila is valued in native plant gardens for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits.
