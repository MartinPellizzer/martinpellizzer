Prestonia macrocarpa

Prestonia macrocarpa, commonly known as the giant prestonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant produces fleshy, berry-like fruits that contain seeds enclosed in a thick, fleshy pulp. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local cultures.
