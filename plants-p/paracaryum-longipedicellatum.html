Paracaryum longipedicellatum

Paracaryum longipedicellatum, commonly known as the long-pedicellate fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant produces spores in small, clustered structures called sporangia, which are borne on the undersides of its leaves. The plant's unique morphology and habitat make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
