Polystichum falcatum f. polypterum

Polystichum falcatum f. polypterum, commonly known as the broad-leafed shield fern, is a fern species characterized by its large, leathery, dark green fronds.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is native to regions in Asia, particularly China and Japan. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.
