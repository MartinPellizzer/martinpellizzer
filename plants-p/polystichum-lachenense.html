Polystichum lachenense

Polystichum lachenense, commonly known as the Lachen's wood fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green fronds that are typically divided into narrow, lance-shaped segments. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories. Its rhizome is thick and creeping, allowing it to spread and form dense colonies.

Polystichum lachenense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
