Poa kanaii

Poa kanaii, commonly known as Kanai's bentgrass, is a native grass species found primarily in the Pacific Northwest of the United States.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and fine, silver-green blades that often form dense mats. This plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. Poa kanaii has small, spike-like inflorescences and produces tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
