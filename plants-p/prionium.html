Prionium

Prionium, commonly known as the sea holly, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers and silver-gray foliage. Prionium species are typically found in coastal areas and rocky soils, thriving in saline environments. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often used in ornamental gardening.
