Prunus sphaerocarpa

Prunus sphaerocarpa, commonly known as the globe cherry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its rounded, globe-like fruit, which is typically red or yellow when mature. The plant produces small, white flowers in early spring, followed by the development of its distinctive berries. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a serrated margin.

Prunus sphaerocarpa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
