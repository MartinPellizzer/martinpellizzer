Peperomia nahikuensis

Peperomia nahikuensis, commonly known as the Nahiku peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to Hawaii.

It is a succulent plant with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green and have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
