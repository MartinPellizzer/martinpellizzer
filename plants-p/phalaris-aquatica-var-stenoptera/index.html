Phalaris Aquatica Var. Stenoptera

Phalaris aquatica var. stenoptera is a unique grass-like plant known for its striking silver-blue foliage. It thrives in wet environments such as marshes and along riverbanks. This variety is often mistaken for a flowering plant due to its dense, grassy appearance. It is native to parts of Europe and Asia but has been introduced to other regions. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for water gardens and bog landscapes.
