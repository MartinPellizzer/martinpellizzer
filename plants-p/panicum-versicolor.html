Panicum versicolor

Panicum versicolor, commonly known as the variable panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and delicate, feather-like leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. Panicum versicolor is often found in open woodlands and along roadsides, thriving in full sun.

Its ability to adapt to various soil conditions makes it a resilient and widespread species.
