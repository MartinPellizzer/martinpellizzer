Pleudia

Pleudia, commonly known as the fairy lantern, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Araceae.

It is characterized by its unique, showy inflorescence that resembles a lantern, with a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. Native to tropical regions, Pleudia thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
