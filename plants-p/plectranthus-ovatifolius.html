Plectranthus ovatifolius

Plectranthus ovatifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features ovate, serrated leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a textured appearance. It produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of violet or purple, which attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Plectranthus ovatifolius is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
