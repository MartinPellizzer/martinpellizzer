Panicum jainii

Panicum jainii, commonly known as Jain's panicgrass, is a species of grass native to the Indian subcontinent.

It is characterized by its erect, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, fluffy spikelets that are often wind-dispersed, aiding in its propagation. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
