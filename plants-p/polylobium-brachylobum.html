Polylobium brachylobum

Polylobium brachylobum, commonly known as the short-lobed polylobium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its basal leaves that are broad and have a distinctive lobed shape. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is native to certain regions of Southeast Asia and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
