Platantheroides linifolia

Platantheroides linifolia, commonly known as the slender lady's tresses, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, leafy stems and dense, spike-like inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and have a delicate fragrance.

It is known for its specialized mycorrhizal associations, which are essential for its nutrient uptake.
