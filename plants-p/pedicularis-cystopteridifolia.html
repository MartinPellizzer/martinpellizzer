Pedicularis cystopteridifolia

Pedicularis cystopteridifolia, commonly known as the fern-leaved lousewort, is a member of the Orobanchaceae family and is native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe.

It is a hemiparasitic plant, meaning it derives some of its nutrients from the roots of neighboring plants.
