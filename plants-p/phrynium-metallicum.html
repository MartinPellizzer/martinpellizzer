Phrynium metallicum

Phrynium metallicum, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a silvery or metallic sheen, giving it its common name. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its striking foliage rather than its flowers. It thrives in humid, warm environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its leaves are also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
