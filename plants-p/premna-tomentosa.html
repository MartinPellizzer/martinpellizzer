Premna tomentosa

Premna tomentosa, commonly known as velvetleaf or white-leaf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree with opposite, ovate leaves that are covered in a dense, white, woolly pubescence. The plant produces small, pale green to white flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release small, wind-dispersed seeds.

Premna tomentosa is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
