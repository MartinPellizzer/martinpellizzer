Phyllanthus reticulatus var. glaber

Phyllanthus reticulatus var. glaber, commonly known as the smooth nettle tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, and its fruits are capsules containing minute seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
