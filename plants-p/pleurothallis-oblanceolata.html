Pleurothallis oblanceolata

Pleurothallis oblanceolata, commonly known as the broad-leafed pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features slender, wiry stems and elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often dark green in color. Its flowers are small and delicate, usually white or pale yellow, with a fragrant aroma.

It is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
