Populus bonatii

Populus bonatii, commonly known as the Bonati poplar, is a species of poplar native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained environments and is often found near water bodies. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, appearing in catkins, which are typical of the Salicaceae family.

Populus bonatii is valued for its ornamental qualities and its role in ecological restoration projects.
