Polystachya striata

Polystachya striata, commonly known as the streaked spike orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and stream sides. Its leaves are elongated and often have a distinctive striated pattern, giving it its scientific name.

Polystachya striata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
