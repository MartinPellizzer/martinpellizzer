Phylica chionophila

Phylica chionophila, commonly known as the silver-leafed phylica, is a small shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
