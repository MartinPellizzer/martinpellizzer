Proboscella

Proboscella, commonly known as the orchid mantis, is a unique plant that exhibits striking mimicry of orchid flowers.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant's inflorescence resembles an orchid, with intricate floral structures that attract pollinators. Its leaves are broad and waxy, adapted to retain moisture in arid environments.

Proboscella thrives in humid, shaded habitats and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
