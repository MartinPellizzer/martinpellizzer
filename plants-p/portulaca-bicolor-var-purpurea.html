Portulaca bicolor var. purpurea

Portulaca bicolor var. purpurea, commonly known as the purple portulaca, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing, succulent annual that thrives in hot, dry climates. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to deep purple, depending on the variety. Its leaves are fleshy and succulent, adapted to store water for survival in arid conditions.

It is often cultivated as a ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and drought tolerance.
