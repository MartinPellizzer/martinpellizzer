Peperomia tenebraegaudens

Peperomia tenebraegaudens, commonly known as the Shadow Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a velvety texture and striking, deep red or maroon undersides on its leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia tenebraegaudens is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
