Pterostylis elegantissima

Pterostylis elegantissima, commonly known as the elegant greenhood, is an orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and a single, hooded flower that is typically green with a purple or brown lip. The plant produces a single, narrow leaf and blooms during the spring and early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is often found growing on the forest floor or on rocks.
