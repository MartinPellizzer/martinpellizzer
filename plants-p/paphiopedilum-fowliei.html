Paphiopedilum fowliei

Paphiopedilum fowliei, commonly known as the Fowlie's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, leathery leaves and produces striking, showy flowers with a unique lip structure. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. Its flowers are often pink or purple with yellow markings, and it blooms during the summer months.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
