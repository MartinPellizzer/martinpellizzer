Psoralea subacaulis

Psoralea subacaulis, commonly known as the slender psoralea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous annual that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, pinnately compound leaves. Its flowers are yellow and occur in racemes, adding to its ornamental appeal.

It is often found in coastal and inland regions, thriving in well-drained environments.
