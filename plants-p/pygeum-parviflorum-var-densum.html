Pygeum parviflorum var. densum

Pygeum parviflorum var. densum, commonly known as the dense-flowered African cherry, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces hard, woody fruits containing a single seed, which is used in traditional medicine. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and medicinal contexts.
