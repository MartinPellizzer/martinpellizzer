Peperomia truncigaudens

Peperomia truncigaudens, commonly known as the Truncigaudens Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, cream, and silver, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its attractive foliage and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
