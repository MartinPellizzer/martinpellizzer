Polypodium preslii

Polypodium preslii, commonly known as the Preslia's wall fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen fronds. The plant typically grows on rocky surfaces or in shaded, moist environments. Its fronds are leathery and have a distinctive, narrow shape.

Polypodium preslii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
