Pennisetum nitens

Pennisetum nitens, commonly known as silver grass, is a member of the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and produces panicle-like flower heads that are often pink or red in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Pennisetum nitens is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
