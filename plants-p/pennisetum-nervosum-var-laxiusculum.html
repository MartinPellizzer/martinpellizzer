Pennisetum nervosum var. laxiusculum

Pennisetum nervosum var. laxiusculum, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It features slender, arching stems that produce fluffy, purple-pink plumes during the growing season. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its striking appearance and drought tolerance contribute to its widespread use in landscaping.
