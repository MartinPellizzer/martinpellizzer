Psychotria cumanensis

Psychotria cumanensis, commonly known as the Cumanensis Psychotria, is a species of flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. It produces small, red berries that are often used in traditional medicine.
