Panicum ouonbiense

Panicum ouonbiense, commonly known as the Ouonbiense grass, is a species native to tropical Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, disturbed soils along riverbanks and swampy areas. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its ecological role includes stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms.
