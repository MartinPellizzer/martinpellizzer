Pleurothallis simplex

Pleurothallis simplex, commonly known as the simple pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in the understory of humid forests. The plant features delicate, narrow leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
