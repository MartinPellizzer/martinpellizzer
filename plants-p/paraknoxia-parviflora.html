Paraknoxia parviflora

Paraknoxia parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered paraknoxia, is a rare flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.
