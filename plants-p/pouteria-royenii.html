Pouteria royenii

Pouteria royenii, commonly known as the royal pouteria, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that can grow up to 15 meters tall, characterized by its smooth, gray bark and elongated, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its fruit is a small, round drupe with a thick, leathery exterior and a sweet, aromatic pulp.

Pouteria royenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
