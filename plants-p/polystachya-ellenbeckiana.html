Polystachya ellenbeckiana

Polystachya ellenbeckiana, commonly known as the Ellenbeck's bamboo orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and clustered inflorescences. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
