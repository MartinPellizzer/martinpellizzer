Palmijuncus aruensis

Palmijuncus aruensis, commonly known as the Aru palm, is a flowering plant species native to the island of Aru in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family, which includes various palm species. The plant is characterized by its slender trunk and pinnate leaves, which are typically green and feather-like in appearance. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in coastal or lowland areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
