Poa fax

Poa fax, commonly known as the slender meadow grass, is a species of grass native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial plant that typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. The plant has slender, upright stems and small, spike-like clusters of flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Poa fax plays an important ecological role in maintaining soil stability and supporting local wildlife.
