Peperomia luisana

Peperomia luisana, commonly known as the Luisana Peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically a deep green with a subtle sheen. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences of white to pale pink flowers, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia luisana is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
