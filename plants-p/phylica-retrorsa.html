Phylica retrorsa

Phylica retrorsa, commonly known as the silver tree, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in winter, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, adapted to conserve water in arid environments.

Phylica retrorsa is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
