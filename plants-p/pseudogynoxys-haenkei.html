Pseudogynoxys haenkei

Pseudogynoxys haenkei, commonly known as the Mexican sunflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

Pseudogynoxys haenkei is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
