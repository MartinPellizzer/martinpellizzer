Peperomia novae-helvetiae

Peperomia novae-helvetiae, commonly known as the New Zealand pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to New Zealand.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, which appear in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant.
