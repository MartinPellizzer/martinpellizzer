Ponerorchis renzii

Ponerorchis renzii, commonly known as the Chinese ghost orchid, is a rare and enigmatic orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, ghost-like appearance with pale greenish-white flowers. The plant is epiphytic, growing on tree bark without deriving nutrients from its host. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is known for its delicate, fragrant blooms.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a conservation concern.
