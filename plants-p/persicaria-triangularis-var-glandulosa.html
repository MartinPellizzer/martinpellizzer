Persicaria triangularis var. glandulosa

Persicaria triangularis var. glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular smartweed, is a herbaceous plant characterized by its triangular, succulent leaves that are covered in small glands.

It typically grows in wet or moist soils, often found in marshy areas or along stream banks. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its stems are erect and can reach heights of up to 60 cm, often branching near the base.

The species is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed or nutrient-rich environments.
