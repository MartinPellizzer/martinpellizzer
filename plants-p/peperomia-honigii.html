Peperomia honigii

Peperomia honigii, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery-white underside, giving it a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white and green, typically blooming in late spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia honigii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
