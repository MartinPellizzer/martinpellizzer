Phyllanthus atabapoensis

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its small, yellowish-green leaves and clustered flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in swampy or wetland areas.
