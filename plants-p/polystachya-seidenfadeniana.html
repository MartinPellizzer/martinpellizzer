Polystachya seidenfadeniana

Polystachya seidenfadeniana, commonly known as the Seidenfaden's spikegrass, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing numerous small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.

Polystachya seidenfadeniana is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is known for its resilience in tropical conditions.
