Pouteria queenslandica

Pouteria queenslandica, commonly known as the Queensland pouteria, is a tropical tree native to northern Australia.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and elliptical leaves. Pouteria queenslandica thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in rainforest or coastal habitats.
