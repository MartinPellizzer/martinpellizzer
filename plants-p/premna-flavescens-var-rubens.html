Premna flavescens var. rubens

Premna flavescens var. rubens, commonly known as the red-flowered premna, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including parts of India, China, and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often reddish or purple in color, and its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
