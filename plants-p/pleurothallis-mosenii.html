Pleurothallis mosenii

Pleurothallis mosenii, commonly known as the Mosenii orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Colombia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often arranged in clusters. Its leaves are usually narrow and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for both botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
