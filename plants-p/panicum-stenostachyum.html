Panicum stenostachyum

Panicum stenostachyum, commonly known as the narrow-stalked panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands, wetlands, and disturbed soils. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, linear leaves that are often hairy. Its inflorescence consists of a loose panicle with small, spikelet-like flowers.

Panicum stenostachyum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological restoration projects.
