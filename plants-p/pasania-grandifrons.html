Pasania grandifrons

Pasania grandifrons, commonly known as the large-flowered pasania, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are typically elliptical with a smooth texture and a glossy appearance. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are edible and often consumed by birds and small mammals.

It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in forest understories.
