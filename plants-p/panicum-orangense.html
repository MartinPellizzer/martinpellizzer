Panicum orangense

Panicum orangense, commonly known as Orange Panicum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and vibrant orange to red bracts. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters that attract various pollinators.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
