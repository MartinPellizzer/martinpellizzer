Piper pseudodelectans

Piper pseudodelectans, commonly known as the false pepper plant, is a member of the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species.

It is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, berry-like fruits. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly aromatic scent.

The plant is often grown for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
