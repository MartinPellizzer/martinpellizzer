Pilea loefgrenii var. bradeana

Pilea loefgrenii var. bradeana, commonly known as the Shingle Plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features dense, rounded rosettes of glossy, dark green, ovate leaves with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers on short pedicels, which are followed by pale green, shield-shaped bracts. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant.
