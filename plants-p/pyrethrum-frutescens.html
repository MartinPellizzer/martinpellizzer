Pyrethrum frutescens

Pyrethrum frutescens, commonly known as chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to Asia and belongs to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its daisy-like flowers, which consist of a central disc surrounded by ray florets. The plant is known for producing pyrethrin, a natural insecticide used in organic pest control. Pyrethrum frutescens thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun.

It is widely cultivated for ornamental purposes and for its medicinal and pesticidal properties.
