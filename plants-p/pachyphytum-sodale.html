Pachyphytum sodale

Pachyphytum sodale, commonly known as the "Sunburst Pachyphytum," is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a yellow or orange edge, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its compact growth habit and low maintenance requirements make it an ideal plant for both novice and experienced gardeners.
