Papaver burseri var. elegans

Papaver burseri var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant poppy, is a striking perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from deep red to orange, with a distinctive yellow center. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky hair. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom reliably in cooler climates.
