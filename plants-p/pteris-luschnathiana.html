Pteris luschnathiana

Pteris luschnathiana, commonly known as the Luschnath's water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Pteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds that are typically light green with a slightly curled appearance. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. The fronds are often used in horticulture for their ornamental value.

It reproduces through spores, which are released from the sori located on the underside of the fronds.
