Piper merrittii

Piper merrittii, commonly known as the Merritt's pepper vine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit and heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into elongated, fleshy berries.

The berries are often used in traditional medicine and are known for their spicy flavor.
