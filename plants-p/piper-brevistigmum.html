Piper brevistigmum

Piper brevistigmum, commonly known as the dwarf black pepper plant, is a flowering vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its small, dark purple berries that mature on the vine. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a slightly serrated margin.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of aromatic foliage.
