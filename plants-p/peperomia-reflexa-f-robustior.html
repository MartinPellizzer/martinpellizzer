Peperomia reflexa f. robustior

Peperomia reflexa f. robustior, commonly known as the robust peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance for low light and minimal watering requirements.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for container gardening and as a decorative plant.
