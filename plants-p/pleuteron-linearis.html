Pleuteron linearis

Pleuteron linearis, commonly known as the slender pleuteron, is a unique flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along its stem. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, which bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of dense rainforests.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
