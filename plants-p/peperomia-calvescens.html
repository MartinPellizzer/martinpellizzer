Peperomia calvescens

Peperomia calvescens, commonly known as the Emerald Ripple Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features thick, waxy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually white or pale green. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia calvescens is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
