Potentilla townsendii

Potentilla townsendii, commonly known as Townsend's cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant features showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are palmately compound, with five to seven leaflets that are often covered in fine hairs.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
