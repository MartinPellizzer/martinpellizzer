Pancovia turbinata

Pancovia turbinata, commonly known as the turban lily, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are often a vibrant green. Pancovia turbinata typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories.

It is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
