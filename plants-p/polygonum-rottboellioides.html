Polygonum rottboellioides

Polygonum rottboellioides, commonly known as the narrowleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist soils along stream banks, wet meadows, and other wetland habitats. Its stems are usually erect and can reach heights of up to 1 meter.

The species is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of wetland environments.
