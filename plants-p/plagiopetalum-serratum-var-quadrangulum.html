Plagiopetalum serratum var. quadrangulum

Plagiopetalum serratum var. quadrangulum, commonly known as the quadrangular plagiopetalum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive quadrangular stems, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Its foliage is broad and lance-shaped, contributing to its ornamental value in horticulture.
