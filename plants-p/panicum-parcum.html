Panicum parcum

Panicum parcum, commonly known as little bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green blades that turn reddish in the fall. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and sandy soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often pink or purplish in color.

Panicum parcum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological restoration projects.
