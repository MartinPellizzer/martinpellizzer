Pentaschistis effusa

Pentaschistis effusa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rock cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to parts of Europe and Asia, typically found in rocky or mountainous habitats. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, rounded pods that contain several seeds.

Pentaschistis effusa is generally a short-lived perennial, adapted to grow in poor, well-drained soils.
