Primula aitchisonii

Primula aitchisonii, commonly known as the Aitchison's primrose, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Pakistan.

It is characterized by its vibrant violet to purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in cool, moist environments. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with specific care requirements.
