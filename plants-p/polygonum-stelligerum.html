Polygonum stelligerum

Polygonum stelligerum, commonly known as the zigzag knotweed, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes species known for their distinctive bract structures. The plant is characterized by its upright, branched stems that often exhibit a zigzag pattern, hence its common name. Its small, greenish-white flowers appear in clusters and are typically found in late summer to early fall.

Polygonum stelligerum is often found in moist, disturbed soils along streambanks and roadsides.
