Peperomia dolabriformis var. lombardii

Peperomia dolabriformis var. lombardii, commonly known as the Lombardii Peperomia, is a compact, low-growing perennial plant native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves with a slightly wavy margin and a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it an excellent addition to terrariums and hanging baskets.
