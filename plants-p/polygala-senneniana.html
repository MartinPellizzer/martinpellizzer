Polygala senneniana

Polygala senneniana, commonly known as the mountain polygala, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, often pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in well-drained environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and lance-shaped, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
