Polystichum Scopulinum

Polystichum scopulinum is a lesser-known fern species that thrives in shaded, moist environments. It is native to parts of Europe and Asia, often found in rocky or woodland areas. This plant is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds and unique spore structures. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in its ecosystem. Its resilience and adaptability make it a fascinating subject for botanical study.
