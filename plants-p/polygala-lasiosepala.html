Polygala lasiosepala

Polygala lasiosepala, commonly known as the silky sepala polygala, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and features delicate, feathery inflorescences. The plant has small, usually green or white flowers that are clustered in spiky arrangements. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is valued in native plant gardens for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits.
