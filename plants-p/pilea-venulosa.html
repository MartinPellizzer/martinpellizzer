Pilea venulosa

Pilea venulosa, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to southern China and is characterized by its round, fleshy leaves that resemble small coins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are often hidden beneath the leaves. It is a popular indoor plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.

Pilea venulosa thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
