Parsonsia graciliflora

Parsonsia graciliflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered parsonsia, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, pendant-like flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fleshy fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Parsonsia graciliflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
