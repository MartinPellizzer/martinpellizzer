Pleurothallis bicolor

Pleurothallis bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, white or pale yellow flowers with a distinct two-toned appearance. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of cultivation, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
