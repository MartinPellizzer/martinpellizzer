Protasparagus plumosus

Protasparagus plumosus, commonly known as the silky asparagus or silky fern, is a delicate, fern-like plant native to the eastern United States.

It features feathery, plume-like fronds that give it its common name, and it typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread slowly and form dense colonies. Its leaves are finely divided, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by spore-bearing structures.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its graceful appearance and ability to thrive in woodland settings.
