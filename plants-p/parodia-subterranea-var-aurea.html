Parodia subterranea var. aurea

Parodia subterranea var. aurea, commonly known as the golden star cactus, is a compact, slow-growing cactus native to South America.

It features a cylindrical stem with a pale green to yellowish coloration, often adorned with white or yellow spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
