Perezia pungens var. cernua

Perezia pungens var. cernua, commonly known as the hanging perezia, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its long, pendulous inflorescences.

It features vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a striking yellow throat. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically a deep green, providing a lush backdrop to its colorful blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
