Piper prismaticum var. tilaranum

Piper prismaticum var. tilaranum, commonly known as the Tilaran pepper, is a tropical vine native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its strikingly colorful, prism-like fruits that range from yellow to red and orange. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface and produces small, white flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The fruit is edible and has a mild, peppery flavor, making it a unique addition to local cuisines.
