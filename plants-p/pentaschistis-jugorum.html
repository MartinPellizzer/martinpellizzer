Pentaschistis jugorum

Pentaschistis jugorum, commonly known as the mountain holly, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows at high altitudes. The plant features dark green, leathery leaves and produces small, white or pinkish flowers. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens.

The botanical characteristics of Pentaschistis jugorum make it well adapted to cool, moist environments.
