Prosthechea vita

Prosthechea vita, commonly known as the violet orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. The plant produces striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a pleasant fragrance. Its leaves are typically elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Prosthechea vita thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
