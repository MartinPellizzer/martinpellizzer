Planera × verschaffeltii

Planera × verschaffeltii, commonly known as the hybrid plane tree, is a deciduous tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a natural hybrid between Planera orientalis and Platanus hispanica, characterized by its broad, palmate leaves and showy yellow flowers. The tree typically grows to a height of 20–30 meters and is known for its rapid growth and adaptability to various soil types. It is often planted in urban landscapes for its ornamental value and ability to provide shade.

However, it can become invasive in certain regions if not managed properly.
