Pleurothallis doucetteana

Pleurothallis doucetteana, commonly known as the Doucette's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features intricate, delicate flowers with a wide range of colors, often including shades of white, pink, and purple. Its inflorescence is usually a raceme, bearing several blooms that are highly fragrant.

The genus Pleurothallis is known for its diversity, and Pleurothallis doucetteana is a notable example with its unique floral structure and ornamental value.
