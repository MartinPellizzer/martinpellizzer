Polystichum plicatum

Polystichum plicatum, commonly known as the pleated wood fern, is a species of fern native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its finely divided, pinnately compound fronds that are often pleated or folded. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its fronds are evergreen and can reach lengths of up to 1 meter.

Polystichum plicatum is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
