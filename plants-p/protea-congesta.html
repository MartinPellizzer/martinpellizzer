Protea congesta

Protea congesta, commonly known as the dense protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence composed of numerous small, tubular flowers that are often brightly colored. The plant typically grows in coastal fynbos vegetation, thriving in well-drained, acidic soils. Its unique floral structure attracts a variety of pollinators, including birds and insects.

Protea congesta is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
