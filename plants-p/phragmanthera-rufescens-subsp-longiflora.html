Phragmanthera rufescens subsp. longiflora

Phragmanthera rufescens subsp.

longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered phragmanthera, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland and rocky habitats.

The plant features slender stems and small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its overall appearance.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
