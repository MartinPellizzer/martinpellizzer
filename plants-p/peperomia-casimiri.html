Peperomia casimiri

Peperomia casimiri, commonly known as the Casimiri peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which are clustered in spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia casimiri is appreciated for its ornamental foliage and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
