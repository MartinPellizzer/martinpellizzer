Polystachya caespitosa

Polystachya caespitosa, commonly known as the clustered spike orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, clump-forming growth habit and produces dense, spike-like inflorescences. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and is typically found in shaded, moist environments. Its flowers are usually small and come in various colors, including white, yellow, and pink.

Polystachya caespitosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
