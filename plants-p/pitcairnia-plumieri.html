Pitcairnia plumieri

Pitcairnia plumieri, commonly known as the velvet leaf pitcairnia, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, waxy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety coating. The plant produces striking pink to purple flowers that bloom in a spiral pattern along the central stem. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
