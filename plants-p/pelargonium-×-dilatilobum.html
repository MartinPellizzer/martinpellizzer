Pelargonium × dilatilobum

Pelargonium × dilatilobum, commonly known as the African violet, is a hybrid species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and showy, clustered flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant is typically grown as a houseplant due to its preference for warm, humid conditions and indirect light. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for containers and tabletop displays.

Botanically, it belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value.
