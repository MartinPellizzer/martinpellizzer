Plectranthus asymmetricus

Plectranthus asymmetricus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with silver on the underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, tubular flowers in shades of purple or pink, which bloom in clusters during the summer months.

Plectranthus asymmetricus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
