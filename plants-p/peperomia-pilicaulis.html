Peperomia pilicaulis

Peperomia pilicaulis, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia pilicaulis is relatively low maintenance and suitable for both beginners and experienced plant enthusiasts.
