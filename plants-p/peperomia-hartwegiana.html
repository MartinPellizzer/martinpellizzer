Peperomia hartwegiana

Peperomia hartwegiana, commonly known as the Hartweg's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval or elliptic in shape, with a glossy surface and a variety of colors ranging from dark green to reddish hues. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually greenish-white or reddish, which bloom during the warmer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia hartwegiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and air-purifying qualities.
