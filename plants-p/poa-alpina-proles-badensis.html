Poa alpina proles badensis

Poa alpina proles badensis, commonly known as the Baden Alpine Bluegrass, is a low-growing grass species native to alpine regions of Europe.

It thrives in cold, rocky environments and is adapted to harsh climatic conditions. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. It plays a vital role in stabilizing soil in mountainous areas.

Its botanical features make it an important component of alpine ecosystems.
