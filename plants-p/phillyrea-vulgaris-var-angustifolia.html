Phillyrea vulgaris var. angustifolia

Phillyrea vulgaris var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf Phillyrea, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in the spring, which are followed by spherical, woody fruits. It is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and woodland areas.

Phillyrea vulgaris var. angustifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
