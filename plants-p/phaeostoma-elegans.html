Phaeostoma elegans

Phaeostoma elegans, commonly known as the elegant phaeostoma, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stem and small, daisy-like flowers that typically bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or disturbed habitats.

Phaeostoma elegans is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local pollinator populations.
