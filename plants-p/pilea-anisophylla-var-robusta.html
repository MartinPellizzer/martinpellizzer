Pilea anisophylla var. robusta

Pilea anisophylla var. robusta, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slight variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by spherical, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage and ease of care contribute to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
