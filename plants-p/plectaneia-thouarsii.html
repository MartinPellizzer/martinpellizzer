Plectaneia thouarsii

Plectaneia thouarsii, commonly known as the Madagascar flame tree, is a member of the Fabaceae family.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its striking red and orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces elongated pods that contain seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.

The species is also noted for its ability to thrive in tropical climates with well-drained soil.
