Polianthes × blissii

Polianthes × blissii, commonly known as the Mexican spider lily, is a hybrid ornamental plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, upward-facing flowers that resemble spiders, typically in shades of white, pink, or red. The plant produces strap-shaped, grass-like leaves that form a dense rosette at the base. It is native to Mexico and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a potted plant.

Polianthes × blissii prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile choice for various garden settings.
