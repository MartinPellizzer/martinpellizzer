Pittosporum brevicalyx var. brevistamineum

Pittosporum brevicalyx var. brevistamineum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by bright red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers a sunny to partially shaded environment.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its hardy nature and seasonal color changes.
