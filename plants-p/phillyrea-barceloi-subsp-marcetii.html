Phillyrea barceloi subsp. marcetii

Phillyrea barceloi subsp.

marcetii, commonly known as the Balearic Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Balearic Islands. It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a leathery texture.

The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, woody fruits. It thrives in rocky and coastal habitats, showing adaptability to saline and well-drained soils.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within the region.
