Pleurothallis tenuifolia

Pleurothallis tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender-leaved orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, narrow leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It thrives in warm, moist conditions with indirect light and is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance.

Due to its specific care requirements, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or specialized orchid setups.
