Phoebe holosericea

Phoebe holosericea, commonly known as the narrow-leaved phoebe, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and fragrant, white flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain smooth, black seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an attractive plant for horticultural purposes in suitable climates.
