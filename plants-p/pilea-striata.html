Pilea striata

Pilea striata, commonly known as the Shiny Pilea, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The leaves have a distinctive pale green color with a waxy texture, and they often feature subtle markings or striations. This plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color.

It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive appearance.
