Panicum atrovirens

Panicum atrovirens, commonly known as black panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, dark green leaves and produces dense, panicle-like inflorescences that turn reddish-brown when mature. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it adaptable to various environmental conditions. It is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive foliage and resilience.

However, it can become invasive in some areas if not managed properly.
