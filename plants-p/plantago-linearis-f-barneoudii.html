Plantago linearis f. barneoudii

Plantago linearis f. barneoudii, commonly known as the Barneoud's narrowleaf plantain, is a variant of the common plantain.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its long, narrow leaves that are often ribbed and have a rough texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in dense clusters, typically in the summer months. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

This plant is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides, fields, and gardens.
