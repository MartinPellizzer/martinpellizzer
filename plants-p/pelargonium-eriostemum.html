Pelargonium eriostemum

Pelargonium eriostemum, commonly known as the silverleaf geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-white undersides on its leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red, depending on the variety. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Pelargonium eriostemum is also valued for its aromatic foliage and is often used in traditional medicine.
