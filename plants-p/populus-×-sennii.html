Populus × sennii

Populus × sennii, commonly known as the Chinese poplar, is a hybrid deciduous tree belonging to the Salicaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color and have a rough texture. The tree produces small, inconspicuous flowers in catkins, which are pollinated by wind. Its bark is smooth and light gray, becoming fissured with age.

Populus × sennii is native to China and is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
