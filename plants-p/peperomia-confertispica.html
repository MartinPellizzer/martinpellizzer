Peperomia confertispica

Peperomia confertispica, commonly known as the dense-flowered peperomia, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant white or pink flowers. The plant has ovate, glossy leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia confertispica is also valued for its ability to tolerate low light conditions and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
