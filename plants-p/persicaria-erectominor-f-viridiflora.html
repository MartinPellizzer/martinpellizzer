Persicaria erectominor f. viridiflora

Persicaria erectominor f. viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered smartweed, is a herbaceous plant characterized by its upright growth habit and slender stems.

It features simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to lanceolate in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The flowers are small and clustered in dense spikes, typically greenish in color, which gives the plant its distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist environments such as wet meadows and marshy areas.

This subspecies is notable for its unique foliage and floral coloration, making it a subject of interest in botanical studies.
