Peperomia leonardii var. acuminata

Peperomia leonardii var. acuminata, commonly known as the dwarf peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to South America.

It features broad, ovate leaves that are typically green with a slight waxy coating, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and red. This variety is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and minimal water requirements.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for tabletop displays and hanging baskets.
