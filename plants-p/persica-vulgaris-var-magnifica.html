Persica vulgaris var. magnifica

Persica vulgaris var. magnifica, commonly known as the magnificent peach.

This variant is characterized by its large, showy flowers and vibrant, often red or pink petals. The plant produces sweet, juicy fruits with a distinctive aroma. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, requiring regular watering during the growing season.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
