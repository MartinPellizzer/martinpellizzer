Paphiopedilum barbatum var. nigritum

Paphiopedilum barbatum var. nigritum, commonly known as the Black Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, velvety petals and sepals, which give it its striking appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. Its flowers are usually a deep black or dark purple, with a distinctive lip that is often lighter in color.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
