Peperomia leptostachya f. cambodiana

Peperomia leptostachya f. cambodiana, commonly known as the Cambodian peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically oval or elliptical in shape, often with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences of tiny, reddish or purple flowers, which are characteristic of the peperomia genus. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers indirect light, making it a suitable houseplant.

Its ornamental foliage and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
