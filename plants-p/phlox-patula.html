Phlox patula

Phlox patula, commonly known as smooth phlox, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms make it a versatile addition to gardens and borders.
