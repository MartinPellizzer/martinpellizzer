Protea aitonii

Protea aitonii, commonly known as the Aiton's king protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence composed of numerous papilionaceous flowers arranged in a spherical or conical shape. The plant is native to the Western and Eastern Cape provinces of South Africa, where it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. Its flowers are typically pink or red, with a distinctive central style that extends beyond the petals.

Protea aitonii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
