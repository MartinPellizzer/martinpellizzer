Piper brevistrigillosum

Piper brevistrigillosum, commonly known as the short-haired pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and clusters of tiny, fragrant flowers. Its fruit, a small, elongated berry, contains several seeds and is used in traditional medicine.

This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the undergrowth of rainforests.
