Prangos iranica

Prangos iranica, commonly known as Persian fennel, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Iran.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes other aromatic herbs like parsley and dill. The plant features slender, hollow stems and finely divided, feathery leaves that give it a delicate appearance. Its small yellow flowers are arranged in umbels, typical of the Apiaceae family.

Prangos iranica is valued for its aromatic seeds, which are used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.
