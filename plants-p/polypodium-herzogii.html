Polypodium herzogii

Polypodium herzogii, commonly known as the Herzog's shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds that are typically leathery and dark green in color. This fern is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, Africa, and the Americas. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often growing on rocks or tree trunks.

The plant's rhizome is perennial, allowing it to spread and form dense colonies over time.
