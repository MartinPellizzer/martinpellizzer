Perlebia Pottsii Var. Decipiens

Potts' perlebia, scientifically known as Perlebia pottsii var. decipiens, is a rare and striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of Ethiopia. This unique species belongs to the Gesneriaceae family, which includes many ornamental and horticulturally significant plants. Its most notable feature is its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red and orange, resembling delicate lanterns. Despite its beauty, Potts' perlebia is highly endangered due to habitat loss and over-collection. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this botanical gem for future generations.
