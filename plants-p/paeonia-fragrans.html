Paeonia fragrans

Paeonia fragrans, commonly known as the Chinese peony, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant produces fragrant, single or double blooms in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, with multiple leaflets, and it has a robust root system.

Paeonia fragrans is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been cultivated for centuries in traditional Chinese gardens.
