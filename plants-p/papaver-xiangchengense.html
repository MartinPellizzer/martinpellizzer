Papaver xiangchengense

Papaver xiangchengense, commonly known as the Xiangcheng poppy, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It belongs to the Papaveraceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with well-drained soils, preferring temperate climates. Its botanical features include lobed leaves and a tall, slender stem that supports its vibrant blooms.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
