Psoralea jacquiniana

Psoralea jacquiniana, commonly known as the Jacquin's psoralea, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in regions of Argentina and Bolivia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats and is often found in sandy or gravelly soils.
