Panicum oreades

Panicum oreades, commonly known as mountain bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the central and eastern United States.

It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open woodlands and mountainous regions. The plant has slender, blue-green blades and produces panicles of small, fluffy seeds in the summer. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soils in disturbed areas.

Panicum oreades is also an important food source for birds and small mammals.
