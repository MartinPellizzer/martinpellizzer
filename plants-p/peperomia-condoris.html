Peperomia condoris

Peperomia condoris, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the rainforests of South America.

It features thick, waxy, and textured leaves that range in color from deep green to reddish-brown, often with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of green or red, which are followed by small, round berries. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and drought tolerance.

Peperomia condoris is prized for its unique foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
