Planchonella spathulata var. densiflora

Planchonella spathulata var. densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces woody capsules that contain numerous small, hard seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and ornamental horticulture.
