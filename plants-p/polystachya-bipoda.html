Polystachya bipoda

Polystachya bipoda, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pteridium, is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is a terrestrial herb characterized by its slender, erect stem and a rosette of narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces a single, branched inflorescence bearing numerous small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Polystachya bipoda is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions.
