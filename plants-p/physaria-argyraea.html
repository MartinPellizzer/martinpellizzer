Physaria argyraea

Physaria argyraea, commonly known as silverleaf sandmat, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant features a low-growing, mat-forming structure with pale green to silver-gray foliage. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in dense, branched inflorescences.

Physaria argyraea thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or rocky soils.
