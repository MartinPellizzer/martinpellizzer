Pleurothallis campestris

Pleurothallis campestris, commonly known as the field pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It typically grows in humid, shaded environments such as cloud forests. The plant features small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its roots are epiphytic, meaning it often grows on the surface of trees or rocks.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is popular among orchid enthusiasts.
