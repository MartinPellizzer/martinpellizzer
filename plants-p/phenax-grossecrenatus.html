Phenax grossecrenatus

Phenax grossecrenatus, commonly known as the giant crested fern, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its large, ornate fronds.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to the humid rainforests of Southeast Asia. The plant features a robust rhizome system that supports its extensive foliage, which can grow up to several meters in length. Its fronds are typically bipinnate, with a distinctive crested appearance that gives it its common name.

This plant thrives in high humidity and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
