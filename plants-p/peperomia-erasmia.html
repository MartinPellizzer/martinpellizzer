Peperomia erasmia

Peperomia erasmia, commonly known as the Erasmus peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval or heart-shaped, with a deep green color and prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green or red, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in moderate light conditions.

Peperomia erasmia is often appreciated for its unique foliage and air-purifying qualities.
