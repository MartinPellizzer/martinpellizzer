Perilepta plumulosa

Perilepta plumulosa, commonly known as the silky feather plant, is a unique species native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like leaves that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its foliage is covered in fine, silky hairs, which contribute to its distinctive appearance.

Perilepta plumulosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in humid, low-light conditions.
