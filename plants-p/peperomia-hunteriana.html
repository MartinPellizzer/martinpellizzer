Peperomia hunteriana

Peperomia hunteriana, commonly known as the Hunter's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, glossy, ovate leaves that are often variegated with white or silver markings, giving it an ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and white, typically emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia hunteriana is prized for its compact size and ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
