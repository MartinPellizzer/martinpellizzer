Phillyrea angustifolia subvar. parvifolia

Phillyrea angustifolia subvar. parvifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Phillyrea, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in clusters during the spring. Its fruit is a small, round drupe that matures to a dark blue color.

Phillyrea angustifolia subvar. parvifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor, rocky soils.
