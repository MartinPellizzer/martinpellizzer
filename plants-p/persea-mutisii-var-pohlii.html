Persea mutisii var. pohlii

Persea mutisii var. pohlii, commonly known as the Pohli's persimmon, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central America.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, yellowish-green fruits that mature to a reddish-brown color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has limited commercial use.
