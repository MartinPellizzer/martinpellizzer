Pimelea prostrata

Pimelea prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate geebung, is a low-growing shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a vibrant touch to its foliage. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is commonly found in coastal and heathland environments.

Pimelea prostrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native gardens.
