Pultenaea reticulata

Pultenaea reticulata, commonly known as the reticulated pultenaea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of yellow to orange flowers, which bloom in spring and summer. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Pultenaea reticulata is often found in coastal areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
