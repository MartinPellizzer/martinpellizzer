Pilea nummulariifolia var. klugii

Pilea nummulariifolia var. klugii, commonly known as the silver dollar plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Mexico.

It features rounded, glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery-white underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its slow growth and low maintenance requirements make it an ideal plant for both novice and experienced growers.
