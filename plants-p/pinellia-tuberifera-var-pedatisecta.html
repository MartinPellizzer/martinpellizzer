Pinellia tuberifera var. pedatisecta

Pinellia tuberifera var. pedatisecta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pinellia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features a rhizomatous root system that produces tubers, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant has lanceolate to narrowly ovate leaves that are finely divided, giving it a delicate, fern-like appearance. Its flowers are typically small and greenish-white, clustered in inflorescences.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
