Petroselinum sativum var. giganteum

Petroselinum sativum var. giganteum, commonly known as giant parsley, is a cultivar of parsley characterized by its large, robust leaves and stems.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes other aromatic herbs like dill and carrots. The plant is valued for its mild, fresh flavor and is often used in culinary applications. It thrives in cool climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its tall stature makes it a striking addition to gardens and a versatile ingredient in various dishes.
