Polycarpaea stellata

Polycarpaea stellata, commonly known as the starflower or starwort, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. The plant has slender, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Polycarpaea stellata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
