Paracaryum cristatum

Paracaryum cristatum, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has a compact, shrub-like growth habit with woody stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and unique appearance.
