Peperomia schizostachya

Peperomia schizostachya, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features upright, branching stems with dense clusters of small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically silver-white to gray-green in color. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white, arranged in compact clusters near the base of the stems. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Peperomia schizostachya is often grown as a decorative foliage plant for its attractive, textured leaves.
