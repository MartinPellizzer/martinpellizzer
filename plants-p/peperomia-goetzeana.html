Peperomia goetzeana

Peperomia goetzeana, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features broad, waxy, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia goetzeana is known for its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments.
