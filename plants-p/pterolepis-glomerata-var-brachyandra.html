Pterolepis glomerata var. brachyandra

Pterolepis glomerata var. brachyandra, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pterolepis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish to orange-colored composite flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is not widely commercially available.
