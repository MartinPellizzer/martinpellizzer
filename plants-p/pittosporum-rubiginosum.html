Pittosporum rubiginosum

Pittosporum rubiginosum, commonly known as the red-flowered pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and clusters of fragrant, reddish-pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, oval berries that are edible but not particularly palatable. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
