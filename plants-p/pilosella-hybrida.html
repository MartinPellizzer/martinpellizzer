Pilosella hybrida

Pilosella hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid goatweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. It is native to Europe and western Asia but has been introduced to other regions.
