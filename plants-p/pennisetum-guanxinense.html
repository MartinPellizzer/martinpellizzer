Pennisetum guanxinense

Pennisetum guanxinense, commonly known as the Chinese silver grass, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and fluffy, silver-gray plumes that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its elegant and feathery foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Pennisetum guanxinense is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
