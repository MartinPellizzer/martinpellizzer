Peperomia rugatifolia var. rugatifolia

Peperomia rugatifolia var. rugatifolia, commonly known as the rugate peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy, waxy leaves that are typically green with a textured, rugose surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique leaf texture and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive and easy-to-care-for houseplant.
