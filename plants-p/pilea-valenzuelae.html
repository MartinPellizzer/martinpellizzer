Pilea valenzuelae

Pilea valenzuelae, commonly known as the Mexican begonia, is a species of flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the family Urticaceae and is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Pilea valenzuelae is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
