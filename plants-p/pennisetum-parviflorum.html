Pennisetum parviflorum

Pennisetum parviflorum, commonly known as fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It features slender, arching stems that produce dense clusters of small, fluffy white or silver flowers. The plant is characterized by its fine, grass-like leaves and is often used in ornamental landscaping. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for drought-tolerant gardens.

Pennisetum parviflorum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
