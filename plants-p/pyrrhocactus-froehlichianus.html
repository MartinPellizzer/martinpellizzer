Pyrrhocactus froehlichianus

Pyrrhocactus froehlichianus, commonly known as the froehlichianus cactus, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its green body. It is adapted to thrive in hot, dry environments with minimal water requirements.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in collections of succulents.
