Plectranthus oresbius

Plectranthus oresbius, commonly known as the Cape rosemary, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features aromatic, hairy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pale purple or white, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for Mediterranean climates.

Its ornamental value and fragrance make it a desirable plant for gardens and herbal use.
