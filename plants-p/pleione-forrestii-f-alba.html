Pleione forrestii f. alba

Pleione forrestii f. alba, commonly known as the white-flowered pleione, is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan, China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, white petals and a showy, spurred lip. This hybrid form exhibits unique floral characteristics compared to its parent species, Pleione forrestii. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments at high altitudes.

Its botanical significance lies in its ornamental value and its role in the study of orchid hybridization and evolution.
