Papaver Alpinum Subvar. Elegans

Papaver alpinum subvar. elegans is a striking alpine poppy known for its vibrant, cup-shaped flowers. This rare variant thrives in high-altitude environments, often found in mountainous regions across Europe. Its petals display a range of colors from deep red to soft pink, making it a popular subject among botanists and gardeners. The plant is characterized by its delicate stems and foliage, adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions. Despite its beauty, it remains a challenging species to cultivate outside its natural habitat.
