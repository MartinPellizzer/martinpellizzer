Piper demeusei

Piper demeusei, commonly known as the Demeuse's pepper, is a climbing vine native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into elongated, spiky fruits. Its leaves are typically alternate, heart-shaped, and have a rough texture.

The plant is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
