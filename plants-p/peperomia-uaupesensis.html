Peperomia uaupesensis

Peperomia uaupesensis, commonly known as the Brazilian peperomia, is a species native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It is a compact, evergreen perennial with glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and have a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale green, arranged in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia uaupesensis is known for its low maintenance and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor conditions.
