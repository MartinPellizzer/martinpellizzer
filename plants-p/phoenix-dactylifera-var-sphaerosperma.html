Phoenix dactylifera var. sphaerosperma

Phoenix dactylifera var. sphaerosperma, commonly known as the spherical date palm, is a cultivated variety of the date palm native to the Middle East and North Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces small, round dates that are typically harvested in late summer. The plant has a short trunk and produces multiple shoots from the base, making it suitable for small spaces. Its fruit is less commonly consumed than the standard date palm but is valued for its unique flavor and texture.

This variety is often grown for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and resilience in arid climates.
