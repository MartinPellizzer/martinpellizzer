Peperomia trifolia f. genuina

Peperomia trifolia f. genuina, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, waxy, silver-gray leaves that are oval to lance-shaped, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its slow growth and low maintenance requirements make it an ideal plant for beginners and experienced growers alike.
