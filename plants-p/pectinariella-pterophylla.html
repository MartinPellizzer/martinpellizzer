Pectinariella pterophylla

Pectinariella pterophylla, commonly known as the coastal sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern California and Baja California.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial with small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in the spring.
