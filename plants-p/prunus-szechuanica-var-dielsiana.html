Prunus szechuanica var. dielsiana

Prunus szechuanica var. dielsiana, commonly known as the Sichuan cherry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its delicate pink to white flowers that bloom in early spring, often before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are oval to ovate in shape with serrated edges. Its fruits are small, round drupes that turn red when mature.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its spring blooms.
