Pyrenaria hirta var. hirta

Pyrenaria hirta var. hirta, commonly known as the hairy pyrenaria, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptability to harsh conditions. Its hairy leaves and showy yellow flowers make it visually distinctive.

It is often found in rocky soils and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
