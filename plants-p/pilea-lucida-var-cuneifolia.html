Pilea lucida var. cuneifolia

Pilea lucida var. cuneifolia, commonly known as the Shiny Pilea, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are elongated and tapering, giving it a distinctive cuneiform shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a favored ornamental plant.
