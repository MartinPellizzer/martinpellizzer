Panicum rarisetum

Panicum rarisetum, commonly known as the raresetum grass, is a species of grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, delicate panicles of flowers that are typically greenish or brownish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or disturbed areas.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered plant species.
