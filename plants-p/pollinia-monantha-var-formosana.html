Pollinia monantha var. formosana

Pollinia monantha var. formosana, commonly known as the Formosan orchid, is a rare orchid species native to Taiwan.

It is characterized by its unique pollinia, which are clustered in a single mass, a feature typical of the Orchidaceae family. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, with a complex structure that aids in specialized pollination.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered endangered and is protected in its native range.
