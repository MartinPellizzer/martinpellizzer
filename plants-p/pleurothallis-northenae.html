Pleurothallis northenae

Pleurothallis northenae, commonly known as the Northen's orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Central America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, often intricate floral structure.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
