Peperomia exigua

Peperomia exigua, commonly known as the miniature pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces tiny, red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Peperomia exigua is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for collectors and indoor gardeners alike.
