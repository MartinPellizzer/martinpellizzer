Peperomia blanda var. porriginifera

Peperomia blanda var. porriginifera, commonly known as the silver mottled peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves with a distinctive silvery-white mottling that gives it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that emerge from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
