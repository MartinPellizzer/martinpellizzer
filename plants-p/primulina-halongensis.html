Primulina halongensis

Primulina halongensis, commonly known as the Halong primula, is a species of flowering plant native to Vietnam.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically features dark green, waxy leaves and produces clusters of brightly colored blossoms in shades of pink, white, or purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
