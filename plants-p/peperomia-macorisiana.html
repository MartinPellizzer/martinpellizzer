Peperomia macorisiana

Peperomia macorisiana, commonly known as the Madagascar peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia macorisiana is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
