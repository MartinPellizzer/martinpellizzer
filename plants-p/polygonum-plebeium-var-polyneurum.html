Polygonum plebeium var. polyneurum

Polygonum plebeium var. polyneurum, commonly known as the slender knotweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist environments such as stream banks and wet meadows, where it thrives in partial to full shade. The plant features slender, often reddish stems that are covered with small, alternate leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered in spikes, producing tiny, greenish-white blooms.

The species is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit, which allows it to spread vegetatively and form dense colonies.
