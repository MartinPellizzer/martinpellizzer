Paraprenanthes yangtoushanensis

Paraprenanthes yangtoushanensis, commonly known as the Yangtoushan aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous areas, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and arranged in a basal rosette, while its flowers bloom in clusters.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
