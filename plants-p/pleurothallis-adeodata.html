Pleurothallis adeodata

Pleurothallis adeodata, commonly known as the silver leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small to medium-sized epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, often with a subtle metallic sheen. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in a fine, silvery coating, which helps reflect excess light.

Pleurothallis adeodata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
