Peperomia tilarana

Peperomia tilarana, commonly known as the Tilarana pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly Brazil. This plant is characterized by its compact growth habit, glossy dark green leaves, and small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia tilarana is relatively low maintenance and resistant to pests, contributing to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
