Panicum cinereum

Panicum cinereum, commonly known as gray bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its gray-green foliage and slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread across open habitats. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in prairies and disturbed areas.

Panicum cinereum is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing food for wildlife.
