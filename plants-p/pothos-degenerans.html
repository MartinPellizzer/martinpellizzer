Pothos degenerans

Pothos degenerans, commonly known as the marble queen pothos, is a popular houseplant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing vines and heart-shaped leaves that are variegated with white and green patterns. The plant thrives in low-light conditions and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is known for its ability to purify the air and is often used in interior landscaping.

Its easy care and aesthetic appeal make it a favorite among plant enthusiasts.
