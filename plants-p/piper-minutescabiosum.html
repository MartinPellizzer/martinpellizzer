Piper minutescabiosum

Piper minutescabiosum, commonly known as the small-leaved pepper vine, is a tropical climbing plant belonging to the Piperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender stems and small, opposite leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into elongated, three-lobed berries. Native to regions of South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and traditional herbal practices.
