Piper plurinervosum

Piper plurinervosum, commonly known as the multivenous pepper plant, is a tropical species native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many pepper species. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves with multiple veins, giving it its scientific name. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by berries that turn red when mature.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has potential uses in traditional medicine.
