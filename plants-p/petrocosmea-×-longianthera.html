Petrocosmea × longianthera

Petrocosmea × longianthera, commonly known as the long-armed petrocosmea, is a hybrid species native to China.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically features elongated, tubular corollas with vibrant colors, often in shades of red or orange. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.
