Pterandra hatschbachii

It is characterized by its showy, yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a whorled pattern around the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded woodland areas.
