Pleomele Steudneri

Pleomele steudneri is a striking tropical plant native to the island of Moorea in French Polynesia. Known for its dramatic, sword-like leaves and unique growth habit, it is a popular choice among plant enthusiasts and collectors. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its elegant appearance makes it a standout in both indoor and outdoor gardens. Despite its beauty, it is important to note that Pleomele steudneri is toxic to humans and animals, so caution should be exercised when handling it.
