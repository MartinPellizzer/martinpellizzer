Podanthum canescens var. nudiflorum

Podanthum canescens var. nudiflorum, commonly known as the naked flowered podanthum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are often covered in a fine, white pubescence, giving it a silvery appearance.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
