Pelargonium × bellum

Pelargonium × bellum, commonly known as the ivy-leaved geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, depending on the cultivar. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full to partial sunlight for optimal growth.

Pelargonium × bellum is widely cultivated as a decorative plant, both outdoors in temperate climates and indoors as a houseplant.
