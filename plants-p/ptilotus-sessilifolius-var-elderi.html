Ptilotus sessilifolius var. elderi

Ptilotus sessilifolius var. elderi, commonly known as the elderi spinifex, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Australia.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, needle-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. Its flowers are typically pink or purple, appearing in clusters and blooming during the warmer months.

This species is well adapted to sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland dunes.
