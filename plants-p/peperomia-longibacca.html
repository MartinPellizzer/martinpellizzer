Peperomia longibacca

Peperomia longibacca, commonly known as the long-stemmed peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, with a glossy green surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, often followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its slow growth and low maintenance requirements make it ideal for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
