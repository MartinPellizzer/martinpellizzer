Paphiopedilum superbiens var. sanderae

Paphiopedilum superbiens var. sanderae, commonly known as the Sanderae lady's slipper, is a rare and captivating orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its distinctive, showy flowers with a pouch-like lip that is often a vibrant pink or purple. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid, with thick, fleshy roots adapted to its humid, tropical environment. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, providing a lush green backdrop to its striking blooms.

This variety is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and unique floral structure.
