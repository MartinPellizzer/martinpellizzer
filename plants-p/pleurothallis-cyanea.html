Pleurothallis cyanea

Pleurothallis cyanea, commonly known as the blue orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually small to medium in size, with a delicate structure that enhances its ornamental appeal.

Pleurothallis cyanea is valued in horticulture for its vibrant color and unique floral morphology.
