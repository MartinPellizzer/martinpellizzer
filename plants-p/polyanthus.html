Polyanthus

Polyanthus, commonly known as the chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that come in a wide range of colors, including yellow, orange, red, and white. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, with upright stems and lobed leaves. Polyanthus varieties are often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and as houseplants.

Its ability to thrive in various climates makes it a popular choice for both decorative and commercial use.
