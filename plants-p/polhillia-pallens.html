Polhillia pallens

Polhillia pallens, commonly known as the white-flowered rock heath.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its succulent, shrub-like appearance. The plant features dense, evergreen foliage and produces clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Polhillia pallens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a houseplant.
