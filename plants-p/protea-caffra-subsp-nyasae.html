Protea caffra subsp. nyasae

Protea caffra subsp.

nyasae, commonly known as the Natal protea, is a striking evergreen shrub native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences composed of numerous white to pinkish flower heads.

The plant typically grows in coastal and mountainous regions, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its botanical features include serrated, leathery leaves and a distinctive, woody stem structure.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
