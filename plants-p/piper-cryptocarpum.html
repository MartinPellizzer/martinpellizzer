Piper cryptocarpum

Piper cryptocarpum, commonly known as the hidden fruit pepper, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, inconspicuous fruits that are rarely seen, hence its common name. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a rough texture.

The plant is often grown for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
