Polygala africana

Polygala africana, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in open woodlands and grasslands, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers are often pale green or yellowish, with a distinctive spurred structure at the base of each bloom.

Polygala africana is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
