Pithecellobium Pedicellare

Pithecellobium tree, scientifically known as Pithecellobium pedicellare, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America. It is prized for its ornamental value, featuring clusters of fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The tree is also valued for its edible fruits, which are often consumed fresh or used in traditional dishes. Due to its hardiness and adaptability, it has been introduced to various regions around the world. Despite its benefits, the plant remains relatively unknown outside of horticultural circles.
