Peperomia angularis var. noveninervia

Peperomia angularis var. noveninervia, commonly known as the nine-veined peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features thick, waxy leaves with a distinctive angular shape and a vibrant green coloration. The plant has a short, stout stem and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale green. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique leaf structure and ease of care make it a popular choice for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
