Peperomia immolata

Peperomia immolata, commonly known as the flame peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves with striking red or orange veins, creating a vibrant, eye-catching appearance. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color, which are followed by small, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia immolata is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and occasional fertilization during the growing season.
