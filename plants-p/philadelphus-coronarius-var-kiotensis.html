Philadelphus coronarius var. kiotensis

Philadelphus coronarius var. kiotensis, commonly known as the Kiotensian Mockorange, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its pleasant fragrance.
