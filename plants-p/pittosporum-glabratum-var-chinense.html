Pittosporum glabratum var. chinense

Pittosporum glabratum var. chinense, commonly known as Chinese pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to China.

It features elliptical, dark green leaves that are glossy and often emit a fragrant scent. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring, followed by orange to red berries. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

This species is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and landscaping.
