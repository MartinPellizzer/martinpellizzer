Petastoma sceptrum

Petastoma sceptrum, commonly known as the sacred rod fern, is a striking evergreen fern native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features tall, slender fronds that can grow up to several meters in length, with a distinctive, upright growth habit. The plant has a unique, almost architectural appearance, with its stiff, sword-like leaves arranged in a rosette pattern. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening due to its elegance and visual impact.

Petastoma sceptrum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.
