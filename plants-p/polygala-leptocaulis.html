Polygala leptocaulis

Polygala leptocaulis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polygalaceae.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in rocky and sandy soils. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, delicate flowers that are typically pale yellow or white.
