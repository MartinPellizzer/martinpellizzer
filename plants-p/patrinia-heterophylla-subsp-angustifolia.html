Patrinia heterophylla subsp. angustifolia

Patrinia heterophylla subsp.

angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaf patrinia, is a perennial herb native to East Asia. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, white to pale blue flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces small, oval-shaped fruits.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
