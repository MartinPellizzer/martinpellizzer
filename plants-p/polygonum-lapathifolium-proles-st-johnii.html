Polygonum lapathifolium proles st-johnii

Polygonum lapathifolium proles st-johnii, commonly known as St. John's Wort.

This plant is a perennial herb characterized by its upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant is often found in moist, sunny habitats such as meadows and stream banks.

It has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
