Peperomia carnevalii

Peperomia carnevalii, commonly known as the Crimson Beauty, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a striking reddish or purplish hue on the underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Peperomia carnevalii is prized for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to a variety of growing conditions.
