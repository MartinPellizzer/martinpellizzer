Polygala apparicioi

Polygala apparicioi, commonly known as the Appalachian milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in woodland habitats and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, and it produces small, bean-like pods that contain seeds.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
