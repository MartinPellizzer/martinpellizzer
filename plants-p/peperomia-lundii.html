Peperomia lundii

Peperomia lundii, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to Brazil.

It features thick, waxy, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and a slightly reddish underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that are typically hidden beneath the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia lundii is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
