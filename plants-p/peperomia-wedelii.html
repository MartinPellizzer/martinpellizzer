Peperomia wedelii

Peperomia wedelii, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and a slightly waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and attractive foliage, it is often used as a decorative plant in shaded or low-light environments.
