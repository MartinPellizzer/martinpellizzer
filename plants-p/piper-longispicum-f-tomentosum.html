Piper longispicum f. tomentosum

Piper longispicum f. tomentosum, commonly known as the tomentose pepper plant, is a variant of the longispicum species within the Piper genus.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. It produces small, green berries that mature to a dark purple color.

This ornamental plant is often cultivated for its unique texture and potential medicinal properties.
