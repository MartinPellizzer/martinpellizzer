Portulaca cyclophylla

Portulaca cyclophylla, commonly known as the cyclophyll sunflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing annual herb with fleshy, succulent leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Its botanical features make it an attractive addition to rock gardens and xeriscapes.
