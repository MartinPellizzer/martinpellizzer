Phylica spicata var. piquetbergensis

Phylica spicata var. piquetbergensis, commonly known as Piquetberg candlebush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing drought resistance. Its flowers attract pollinators such as bees and birds, contributing to local biodiversity.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in herbal medicine.
