Pilea mollis

Pilea mollis, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a tropical perennial shrub native to China.

It features heart-shaped, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by round, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular houseplant.

Its unique foliage and ease of care contribute to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
