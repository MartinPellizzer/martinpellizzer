Pleurothallis cuneifolia var. atrorubra

Pleurothallis cuneifolia var. atrorubra, commonly known as the dark red pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit and produces small, intricately shaped flowers. The leaves are usually elliptical and dark green, while the flowers are typically red or reddish with contrasting white or yellow markings. This variety is noted for its striking coloration and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

It prefers cool, humid environments and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
