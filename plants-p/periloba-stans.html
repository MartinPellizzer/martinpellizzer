Periloba stans

Periloba stans, commonly known as the African moonflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, white, night-blooming flowers that emit a sweet fragrance. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, round fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in woodland and savanna habitats.

Periloba stans is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional uses in some African cultures.
