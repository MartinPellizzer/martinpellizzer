Pleurothallis praegrandis

Pleurothallis praegrandis, commonly known as the grand beauty orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-white or pale yellow blooms with intricate patterns, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments. It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Pleurothallis praegrandis is also noted for its ability to grow epiphytically on trees, making it well adapted to its natural habitat.
