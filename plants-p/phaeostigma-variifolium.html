Phaeostigma variifolium

Phaeostigma variifolium, commonly known as the variable leaf phaeostigma, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that range from red to orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that vary in size and color depending on environmental conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a notable species in botanical collections.
