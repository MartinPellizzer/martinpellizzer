Pothuava nudicaulis var. glabriuscula

Pothuava nudicaulis var. glabriuscula, commonly known as the smooth-leaved pothuava, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, rounded leaves that are typically hairless or nearly so, giving it a smooth texture. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale yellow flowers that bloom during the warm season. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
