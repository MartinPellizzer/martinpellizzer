Phyllostachys lithophila

Phyllostachys lithophila, commonly known as the rock bamboo, is a species of bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright culms that often grow in dense clumps. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically a bright green color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Phyllostachys lithophila is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in horticulture for landscaping purposes.
