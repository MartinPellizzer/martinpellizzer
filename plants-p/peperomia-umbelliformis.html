Peperomia umbelliformis

Peperomia umbelliformis, commonly known as the umbellate peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, glossy, ovate leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges, adding visual interest. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that resemble umbrellas, giving it its common name. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia umbelliformis is relatively low maintenance and resistant to pests, making it an ideal plant for both novice and experienced growers.
