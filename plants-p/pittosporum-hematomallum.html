Pittosporum hematomallum

Pittosporum hematomallum, commonly known as the New Zealand Pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth margin. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native habitats.
