Piper allenii

Piper allenii, commonly known as Allen's pepper, is a tropical climbing vine native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when mature, containing the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are often used in herbal remedies due to their medicinal properties.

Piper allenii thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for both its ornamental and pharmacological uses.
