Pontederia Cordata Var. Ovalis

Pontederia cordata var. ovalis is a striking aquatic plant native to the wetlands of the southeastern united states. it is known for its broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface. this variety differs from the common type by its slightly oval leaf shape and vibrant blue flowers. it thrives in shallow waters and is often found in marshes and ponds. it plays an important role in supporting local wildlife and improving water quality.
