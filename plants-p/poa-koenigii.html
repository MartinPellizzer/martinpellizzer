Poa koenigii

Poa koenigii, commonly known as Indian mountain grass, is a species of grass native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that contain both florets and bracts. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine and subalpine regions.

Poa koenigii plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitats and is of interest to botanists and ecologists.
