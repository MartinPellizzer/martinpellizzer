Phalangium flexifolium

Phalangium flexifolium, commonly known as the flexible leaf phalangium, is a unique plant species characterized by its ability to alter the shape of its leaves in response to environmental stimuli.

It belongs to the family Phalangiaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features long, thin stems with elongated, flexible leaves that can curl or flatten depending on light and moisture conditions. Its botanical structure supports photosynthesis while adapting to varying ecological pressures.

This adaptability makes it an interesting subject for botanical research.
