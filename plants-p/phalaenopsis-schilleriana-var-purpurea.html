Phalaenopsis schilleriana var. purpurea

Phalaenopsis schilleriana var. purpurea, commonly known as the purple phalaenopsis, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.

It features large, velvety petals that range from deep purple to lavender, often with intricate patterns of darker hues. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in less-than-ideal conditions.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
