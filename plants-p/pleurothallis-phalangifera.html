Pleurothallis phalangifera

Pleurothallis phalangifera, commonly known as the feather-leaf orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features slender, wiry stems and narrow, elongated leaves that give it its common name. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with white or pale pink petals.

It is valued in horticulture for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
