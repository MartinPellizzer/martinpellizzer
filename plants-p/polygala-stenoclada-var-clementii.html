Polygala stenoclada var. clementii

Polygala stenoclada var. clementii, commonly known as the narrow-leaf milkvetch, is a perennial shrub native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, often branched stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

This species is of interest to botanists and horticulturists due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
