Piper mapirense var. magnifolium

Piper mapirense var. magnifolium, commonly known as the giant arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other species of pepper plants. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
