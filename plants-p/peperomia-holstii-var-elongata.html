Peperomia holstii var. elongata

Peperomia holstii var. elongata, commonly known as the elongated peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions.

It features upright, branching stems with ovate, dark green leaves that have a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of green or red, which are not particularly showy. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
