Phalaenopsis appendiculata

Phalaenopsis appendiculata, commonly known as the appendiculate phalaenopsis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, long-lasting flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering spikes. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native humid environments.

This orchid is popular among growers for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
