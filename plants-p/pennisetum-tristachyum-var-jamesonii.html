Pennisetum tristachyum var. jamesonii

Pennisetum tristachyum var. jamesonii, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender culms that produce fluffy, purple-tinted plumes, which add visual interest throughout the growing season. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. It is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.

Its botanical characteristics include narrow, grass-like leaves and a clumping growth habit.
