Populus × tomentosa f. lerigata

Populus × tomentosa f. lerigata, commonly known as the silky poplar or Chinese poplar, is a unique horticultural form of the poplar tree.

It is characterized by its soft, silvery-gray bark and heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy layer. This variety is prized for its ornamental value, particularly in gardens and urban landscapes. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is relatively fast-growing, making it a popular choice for quick shade.

However, it is susceptible to certain pests and diseases, requiring regular maintenance to ensure its health and longevity.
