Piper mollissimum

Piper mollissimum, commonly known as velvetleaf pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, dark purple berry that contains several seeds.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
