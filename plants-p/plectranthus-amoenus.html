Plectranthus amoenus

Plectranthus amoenus, commonly known as the purple heart plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its striking violet to purple tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture.

Plectranthus amoenus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil and full sun.
