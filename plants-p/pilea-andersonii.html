Pilea andersonii

Pilea andersonii, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, trailing plant native to China.

It features glossy, round, shield-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Pilea andersonii is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
