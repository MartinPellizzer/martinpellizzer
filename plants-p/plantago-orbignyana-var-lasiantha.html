Plantago orbignyana var. lasiantha

Plantago orbignyana var. lasiantha, commonly known as the Andean plantain, is a perennial herb native to the Andean regions of South America.

It features a basal rosette of broad, ovate leaves with a hairy surface and a distinctive white to pale green coloration. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous or high-altitude environments.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties.
