Patrisa affinis

Patrisa affinis, commonly known as the narrow-leafed spurge, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has a low, spreading growth habit. Its flowers are small and clustered in cyathia, which are specialized inflorescences typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.
