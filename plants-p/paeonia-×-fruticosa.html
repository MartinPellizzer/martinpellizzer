Paeonia × fruticosa

Paeonia × fruticosa, commonly known as the tree peony, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its woody stems and showy, often double flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and has dark green, glossy leaves. Its botanical features include a thick, fibrous root system and a structure that resembles a small tree or shrub.

The flowers come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, white, and yellow, making it a popular ornamental plant.
