Penianthus camerounensis

Penianthus camerounensis, commonly known as the Cameroon flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to West Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its habitat. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest margins or along riverbanks.

Penianthus camerounensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its showy floral display.
