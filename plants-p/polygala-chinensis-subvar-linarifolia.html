Polygala chinensis subvar. linarifolia

Polygala chinensis subvar. linarifolia, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features slender, erect stems that are often branched and covered with fine hairs. The leaves are alternate, linear to oblanceolate, and have a slightly hairy surface. The flowers are small and clustered in spikelike inflorescences, typically in shades of white or pale yellow.

This plant is adapted to dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions.
