Plectranthus stuhlmannii

Plectranthus stuhlmannii, commonly known as the African violet, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive purple underside. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, typically arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial shade to full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
