Phillyrea medianifolia subsp. heribaudii

Phillyrea medianifolia subsp.

heribaudii, commonly known as the Heribaud's Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy.

The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring. Its fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that ripens to a reddish color.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained, rocky soils.
