Polystachya kubalae

Polystachya kubalae, commonly known as the Cuban flame orchid, is a striking orchid native to Cuba.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and vibrant orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Polystachya kubalae is a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
