Poecilanthe parviflora

Poecilanthe parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered poecilanthe, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers, which are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly serrated margin.

The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth.
