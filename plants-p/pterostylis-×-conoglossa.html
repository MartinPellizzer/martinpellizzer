Pterostylis × conoglossa

Pterostylis × conoglossa, commonly known as the broad-leafed greenhood, is a hybrid orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, hood-like floral structure. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rainforests. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and the flowers are usually green or yellowish in color.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
