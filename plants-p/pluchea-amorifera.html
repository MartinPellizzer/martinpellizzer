Pluchea amorifera

Pluchea amorifera, commonly known as the love-leaf or sweetleaf plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
