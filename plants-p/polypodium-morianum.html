Polypodium morianum

Polypodium morianum, commonly known as the Morian's water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically bioluminescent under certain conditions. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. The fronds are usually pinnately divided, with small, rounded lobes that give it a distinctive appearance.

It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and is also studied for its potential medicinal properties.
