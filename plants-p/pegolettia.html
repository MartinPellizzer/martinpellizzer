Pegolettia

Pegolettia, commonly known as the small-flowered rockcress, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically thrives in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Pegolettia is native to various regions in Europe and Asia, and it is often found in alpine or arctic environments.
