Phthirusa gonioclada

Phthirusa gonioclada, commonly known as the zigzag rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and distinctive zigzag arrangement of its stems. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils along riverbanks and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a slightly hairy surface.

Phthirusa gonioclada is often used in ornamental gardening for its unique appearance and ability to thrive in wet conditions.
