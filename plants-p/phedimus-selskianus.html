Phedimus selskianus

Phedimus selskianus, commonly known as the Selskian sedum, is a low-growing succulent native to Central Asia.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid and rocky environments, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it an attractive addition to xeriscaped landscapes.
