Panicum penicilligerum

Panicum penicilligerum, commonly known as the slender panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Panicum penicilligerum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in certain environments.
