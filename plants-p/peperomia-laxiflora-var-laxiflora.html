Peperomia laxiflora var. laxiflora

Peperomia laxiflora var. laxiflora, commonly known as the broad-leaved peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval or elliptic in shape, often with a bluish-green or dark green hue. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, usually white or pale green, which are borne on short, erect spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a versatile addition to both indoor and outdoor gardens.
