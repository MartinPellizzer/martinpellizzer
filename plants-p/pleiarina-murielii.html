Pleiarina murielii

Pleiarina murielii, commonly known as the Muriel's pleiarina, is a rare and unique plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form with small, dark green leaves that are arranged in a dense, clustered manner. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the cooler months, which are followed by distinctive, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it well adapted to its harsh environment.
