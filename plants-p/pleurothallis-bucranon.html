Pleurothallis bucranon

Pleurothallis bucranon, commonly known as the golden pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically growing on tree trunks or branches. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, which bloom during the warmer months. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrate.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts and botanists.
