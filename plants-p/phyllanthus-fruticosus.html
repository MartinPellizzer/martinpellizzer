Phyllanthus fruticosus

Phyllanthus fruticosus, commonly known as the African spurge, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, sprawling growth habit and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by small, round fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or arid environments.

Phyllanthus fruticosus is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
