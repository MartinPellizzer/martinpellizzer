Phalaenopsis bellina f. bowringiana

Phalaenopsis bellina f. bowringiana, commonly known as the Bowring's phalaenopsis, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, long-lasting flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink. The plant features broad, dark green leaves and produces multiple inflorescences with clustered blossoms. It is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.

This orchid thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining potting mix.
