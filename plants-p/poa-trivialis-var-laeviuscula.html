Poa trivialis var. laeviuscula

Poa trivialis var. laeviuscula, commonly known as the smooth meadow grass, is a variant of the common meadow grass.

It is a perennial grass species characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and slender leaves. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as lawns, meadows, and roadside verges. Its small, spike-like inflorescence contains numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

This variety is notable for its adaptability and is often found in temperate regions across Europe and parts of Asia.
