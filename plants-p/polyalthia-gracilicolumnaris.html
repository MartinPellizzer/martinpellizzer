Polyalthia gracilicolumnaris

Polyalthia gracilicolumnaris, commonly known as the slender columnar polyalthia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, columnar growth habit and long, drooping inflorescences. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and lanceolate, with a smooth surface.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
