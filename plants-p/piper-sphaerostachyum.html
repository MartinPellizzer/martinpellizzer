Piper sphaerostachyum

Piper sphaerostachyum, commonly known as the spherical pepper or round pepper plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of small, dark green leaves that give it its common name. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by red, peppercorn-like berries.

The plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its unique appearance and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
