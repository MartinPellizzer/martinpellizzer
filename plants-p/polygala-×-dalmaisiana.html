Polygala × dalmaisiana

Polygala × dalmaisiana, commonly known as the hybrid milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polygalaceae.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, often clustered flowers. The plant produces distinctive, spiny fruits that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is typically found in arid or semi-arid regions.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
