Polygonum cespitosum subsp. yokusaianum

Polygonum cespitosum subsp.

yokusaianum, commonly known as the Japanese knotweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia. It belongs to the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit, which allows it to spread aggressively.

The plant features alternate, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive reddish sheath at the base. Its small, greenish-white flowers appear in dense clusters during the summer months.

It is often found in moist, disturbed soils along riverbanks and in wetlands.
