Peperomia subbracteiflora

Peperomia subbracteiflora, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery underside, giving it a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and air-purifying properties.

Peperomia subbracteiflora thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
