Poa trivialis var. hohenackeri

Poa trivialis var. hohenackeri, commonly known as the Hohenacker's meadow grass, is a variant of the common bentgrass.

It is a low-growing, perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant typically forms dense, mat-like growths and thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a blue-green hue when fresh.

It is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along the edges of wetlands.
