Persicaria meisneriana var. beyrichiana

Persicaria meisneriana var. beyrichiana, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves that are silver-white on the underside and dark green on top, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range from pink to red, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for wetland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its foliage, which remains attractive throughout the growing season.
