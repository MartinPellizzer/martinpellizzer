Prestoea trinitensis

Prestoea trinitensis, commonly known as the Trinidad cedar, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Caribbean, particularly Trinidad and Tobago.

It belongs to the family Podocarpaceae and is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and dense, dark green foliage. The tree produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by winged seeds that are dispersed by wind.

It is valued for its durable wood and plays an important ecological role in its native habitat.
