Panicum nitidum var. ciliatum

Panicum nitidum var. ciliatum, commonly known as the silky grass or silky panicum, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a silky texture. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardens and erosion control.

Its delicate appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice in landscape design.
