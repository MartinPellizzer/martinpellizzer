Premna claessensii

Premna claessensii, commonly known as the silverleaf premna, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters.

Premna claessensii is often used in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.
