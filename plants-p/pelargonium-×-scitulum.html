Pelargonium × scitulum

Pelargonium × scitulum, commonly known as the shield geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant features broad, rounded leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It is native to South Africa and is well-suited for rock gardens and containers due to its drought tolerance.

Pelargonium × scitulum is also valued for its ability to thrive in full sun and well-drained soil conditions.
