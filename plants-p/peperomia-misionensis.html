Peperomia misionensis

Peperomia misionensis, commonly known as the Misiones peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, often with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, arranged in spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia misionensis is valued for its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
