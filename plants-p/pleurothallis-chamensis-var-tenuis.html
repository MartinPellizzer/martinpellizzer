Pleurothallis chamensis var. tenuis

Pleurothallis chamensis var. tenuis, commonly known as the slender pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to South America, particularly in regions of Ecuador and Peru.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments within the cloud forests. The plant features delicate, linear leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in association with other epiphytic orchids.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicate and less commonly cultivated species.
