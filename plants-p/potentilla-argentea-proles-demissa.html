Potentilla argentea proles demissa

Potentilla argentea proles demissa, commonly known as silver cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its silvery-white undersides on its leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky soils. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
