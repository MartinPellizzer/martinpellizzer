Platanthera stricta var. gracilis

Platanthera stricta var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender lady's tresses, is a flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces spikes of small, white to pale pink flowers arranged in a loose, loose raceme. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows.

This variety is notable for its delicate appearance and its role in supporting local pollinator populations.
