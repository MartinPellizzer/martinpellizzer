Polygonum poiretii

Polygonum poiretii, commonly known as the redstem smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes many species with distinctive arrow-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense, reddish inflorescences, often appearing in late summer.

Polygonum poiretii is characterized by its reddish stems and leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
