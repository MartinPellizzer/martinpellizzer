Pterotum procumbens

Pterotum procumbens, commonly known as the prostrate pterotum, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It is a low-growing herb with slender, spreading stems that often lie close to the ground. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers arranged in clusters along the stems. Its leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Pterotum procumbens thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical regions.
