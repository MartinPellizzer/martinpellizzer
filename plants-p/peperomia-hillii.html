Peperomia hillii

Peperomia hillii, commonly known as the Hill's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, often with a striking coloration of green, red, or purple. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually clustered in spike-like inflorescences. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Peperomia hillii is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and decorative appeal in both indoor and outdoor settings.
