Phyllanthus cedrelifolius

Phyllanthus cedrelifolius, commonly known as African snowball, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves with numerous small leaflets. The plant produces clusters of white flowers that resemble snowballs, giving it its common name. Its fruit is a capsule that contains small, yellowish seeds.

It is often found in moist, wooded areas and is used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
