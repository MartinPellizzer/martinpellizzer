Persea jariensis

Persea jariensis, commonly known as the Jari avocado, is a species native to the Amazon rainforest of South America.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant produces elliptical, dark green leaves that are arranged alternately along the branches. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and typically appear in clusters.

The fruit is a large, pear-shaped berry with a rough, bumpy skin and a creamy, oily pulp that is rich in nutrients.
