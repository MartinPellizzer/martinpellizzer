Pulmonaria helvetica

Pulmonaria helvetica, commonly known as Swiss lungwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features basal leaves that are broadly ovate with distinctive purple and green markings. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped, pinkish-purple flowers in early spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Pulmonaria helvetica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
