Parsonsia platycentra

Parsonsia platycentra, commonly known as the flat-centre nightshade, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange with a distinctive flat center. The plant grows as a shrub or small tree, often reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and usually ovate or elliptic in shape.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds, aiding in its dispersal.
