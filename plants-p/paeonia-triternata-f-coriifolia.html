Paeonia triternata f. coriifolia

Paeonia triternata f. coriifolia, commonly known as the Coriifolia peony, is a cultivar of the peony plant characterized by its unique foliage and floral features.

It is native to China and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens. The leaves are deeply lobed, giving the plant a distinctive, almost fern-like appearance. The flowers are typically pink or white, with a soft, velvety texture.

This variety is relatively hardy and thrives in well-drained soil with partial shade to full sun.
