Pavonia communis

Pavonia communis, commonly known as the common pavonia, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, pink to red bract-like flowers. The plant typically grows in full sun to partial shade and thrives in well-drained soils. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a rough texture and serrated edges.

Pavonia communis is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant floral display and adaptability to various growing conditions.
