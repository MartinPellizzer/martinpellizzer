Prangos multicostata

Prangos multicostata, commonly known as the multi-costate bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or arid environments. The plant features slender, branching stems and produces striking blue to violet bell-shaped flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with multiple longitudinal veins.

Prangos multicostata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
