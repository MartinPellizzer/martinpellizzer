Peperomia tarapotana

Peperomia tarapotana, commonly known as the Tarapotana pepperomia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the Andes mountains in South America, specifically Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit, glossy dark green leaves, and clusters of small, white to pale green flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia tarapotana is relatively low maintenance and suitable for beginners due to its tolerance for moderate conditions.
