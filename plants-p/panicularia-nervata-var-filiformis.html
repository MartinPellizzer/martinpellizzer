Panicularia nervata var. filiformis

Panicularia nervata var. filiformis, commonly known as the slender panicled bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, panicled inflorescence composed of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. The leaves are narrow and linear, often appearing in pairs along the stems, contributing to its elegant appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its refined floral display.
