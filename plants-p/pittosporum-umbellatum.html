Pittosporum umbellatum

Pittosporum umbellatum, commonly known as the white pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white, umbellate flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Pittosporum umbellatum is also valued for its ability to provide habitat for native birds and insects.
