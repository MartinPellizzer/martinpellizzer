Parnassia vanensis

Parnassia vanensis, commonly known as the Van's fairy bell, is a striking member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of China, particularly in the Yunnan province. The plant is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped white flowers that are often tinged with green at the base. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Parnassia vanensis thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.
