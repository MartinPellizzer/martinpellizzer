Polygonum umbrosum

Polygonum umbrosum, commonly known as the shadowplant, is a perennial herb native to the northeastern United States.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, clustered flowers are typically greenish-white and appear in late summer.

Polygonum umbrosum is valued for its ability to stabilize soil and support local wildlife.
