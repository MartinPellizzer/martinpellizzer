Primula suffrutescens

Primula suffrutescens, commonly known as the Chinese primrose, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features a low-growing rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodlands and rocky areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
