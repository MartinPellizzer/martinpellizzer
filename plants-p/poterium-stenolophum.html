Poterium stenolophum

Poterium stenolophum, commonly known as the narrow-ridge papyrus, is a flowering plant native to the wetlands of eastern Africa.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and related grass-like plants. Due to its ecological importance, it plays a role in stabilizing wetland soils and supporting local biodiversity.
