Pimelea nana var. glabrifolia

Pimelea nana var. glabrifolia, commonly known as the smoothleaf pimelea, is a low-growing shrub native to Australia.

It features small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, blooming in spring. The plant has smooth, often glabrous stems and leaves that are narrow and lance-shaped. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathland and open woodland environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
