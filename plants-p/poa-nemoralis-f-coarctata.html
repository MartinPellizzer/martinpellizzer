Poa nemoralis f. coarctata

Poa nemoralis f. coarctata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved meadow grass, is a horticultural form of the common meadow grass.

It is characterized by its slender, narrow leaves and compact growth habit, making it suitable for ornamental use in gardens. This plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its fine-textured foliage.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ground cover and in landscape design.
