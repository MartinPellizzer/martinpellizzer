Pedicularis erubescens

Pedicularis erubescens, commonly known as the scarlet monkey orchid, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a member of the Orobanchaceae family and is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that resemble monkey faces. The plant is hemiparasitic, meaning it partially relies on other plants for nutrients through its root system. It thrives in alpine and subalpine environments, often growing in rocky or open habitats.
