Peperomia subrubrispica f. glabrifolia

Peperomia subrubrispica f. glabrifolia, commonly known as the smooth-leaved peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, with a smooth, hairless surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of red or pink, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
