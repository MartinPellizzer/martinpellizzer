Ptilotus obovatus var. grandiflorum

Ptilotus obovatus var. grandiflorum, commonly known as the "Desert Flax," is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils, adapting well to harsh, dry environments.
