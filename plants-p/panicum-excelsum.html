Panicum excelsum

Panicum excelsum, commonly known as tall panicgrass, is a tall, perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and can reach heights of up to 6 feet. The plant produces dense, fluffy panicles that are often pink to red in color, adding ornamental value to landscapes. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for native plant gardens.

Panicum excelsum is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and support local wildlife.
