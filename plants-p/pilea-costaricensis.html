Pilea costaricensis

Pilea costaricensis, commonly known as the Mexican shield plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to Central America.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a silvery underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea costaricensis is known for its ease of care and ability to form dense, compact foliage.
