Pyrethrum debeauxii

Pyrethrum debeauxii, commonly known as the golden pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant produces small, spherical seed heads that are covered in fine, bristly hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Pyrethrum debeauxii is also valued for its ornamental beauty and potential use in natural pest control.
