Pagamea thyrsiflora

Pagamea thyrsiflora, commonly known as the Christmas tree fern, is a tropical fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, tree-like trunk covered in a fibrous, dark brown rachis. The plant produces dense, feathery fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture.

Pagamea thyrsiflora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ability to add a lush, tropical feel to gardens.
