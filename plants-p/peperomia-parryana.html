Peperomia parryana

Peperomia parryana, commonly known as the Parry's peperomia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub characterized by its glossy, ovate leaves that are often dark green with a reddish or purple underside. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, which appear during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia parryana is also known for its ability to tolerate low light conditions and is relatively easy to care for.
