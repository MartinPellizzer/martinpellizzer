Plappertia

Plappertia, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a genus of flowering plants native to southern Africa.

These plants typically have showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. They are often found in rocky or sandy soils and are adapted to arid environments. Plappertia species are generally shrubs or herbs with hairy leaves and are valued for their ornamental appeal.
