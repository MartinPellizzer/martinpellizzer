Pteris nanlingensis

Pteris nanlingensis, commonly known as the Nanling water fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds, which are clustered in sori.

Pteris nanlingensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological significance in its native habitat.
