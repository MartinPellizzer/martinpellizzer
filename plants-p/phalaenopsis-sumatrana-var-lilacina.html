Phalaenopsis sumatrana var. lilacina

Phalaenopsis sumatrana var. lilacina, commonly known as the lilac orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender stems and large, showy flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep lilac. The plant produces a single spike of flowers, which can last for several weeks. It prefers bright, indirect light and moderate humidity, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

The variety is prized for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
