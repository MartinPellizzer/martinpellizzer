Panzerina lanata var. alaschanica

Panzerina lanata var. alaschanica, commonly known as the Alashan hairy panzerina, is a rare and unique plant species native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a subtle floral presence to its habitat.
