Polychilos fuscata

Polychilos fuscata, commonly known as the dark polychilos, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and resilience.
