Pavonia andrade-limae

Pavonia andrade-limae, commonly known as the Andrade-Lima pavonia, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its showy, pink to red bract-like flowers that resemble those of the genus *Pavonia*. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters, with pinnately compound leaves and hairy stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
