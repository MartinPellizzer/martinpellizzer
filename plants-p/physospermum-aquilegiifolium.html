Physospermum aquilegiifolium

Physospermum aquilegiifolium, commonly known as the heartleaf physospermum, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically bright green and have a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
