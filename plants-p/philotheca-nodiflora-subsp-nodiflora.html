Philotheca nodiflora subsp. nodiflora

Philotheca nodiflora subsp.

nodiflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved philotheca, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as heathlands and woodland edges.

The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds, aiding in its propagation.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting native wildlife.
