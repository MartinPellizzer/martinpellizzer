Pleioblastus hupehensis

Pleioblastus hupehensis, commonly known as the Chinese bamboo or Hubei bamboo, is a species of grass native to eastern China.

It is a clump-forming plant with slender, arching stems that produce long, narrow leaves with a bluish-green hue. The plant is characterized by its ability to form dense, upright clumps and is often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Pleioblastus hupehensis is valued for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
