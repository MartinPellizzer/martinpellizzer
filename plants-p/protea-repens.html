Protea repens

Protea repens, commonly known as the silver leaf protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that consist of numerous white to pale pink flower heads. The plant typically grows in coastal and mountainous regions, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun. Its leaves are narrow and silvery-white, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Protea repens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also an important species in the region's biodiversity.
