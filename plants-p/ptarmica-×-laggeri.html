Ptarmica × laggeri

Ptarmica × laggeri, commonly known as the hybrid stonecrop, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Sedum acre and Sedum leibfritzii.

It is a low-growing succulent that typically forms dense mats of fleshy, blue-green leaves. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is native to the mountainous regions of central and eastern Europe.

Ptarmica × laggeri is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
