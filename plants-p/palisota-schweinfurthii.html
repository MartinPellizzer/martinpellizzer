Palisota schweinfurthii

Palisota schweinfurthii, commonly known as the Schweinfurth's spider plant, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically surrounded by a spadix, a common feature in the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.

Palisota schweinfurthii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care.
