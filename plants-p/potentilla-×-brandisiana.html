Potentilla × brandisiana

Potentilla × brandisiana, commonly known as the silverleaf potentilla, is a hybrid shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive and ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in early summer, adding a delicate touch to its overall structure. It is typically evergreen, with compact growth habits that make it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

The species is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
