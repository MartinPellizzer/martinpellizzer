Paphiopedilum × spicerovenustum

Paphiopedilum × spicerovenustum, commonly known as the spicerovenustum orchid, is a hybrid orchid belonging to the genus Paphiopedilum, which is part of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its unique and often strikingly colored flowers, which typically feature a lip that is modified into a specialized structure for pollination. The plant is an epiphytic orchid, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other plants without deriving nutrients from them. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich substrates for optimal growth.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as a hybrid that combines traits from its parent species, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
