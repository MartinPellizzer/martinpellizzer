Panicum megastachyum

Panicum megastachyum, commonly known as the giant panic grass, is a tall, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its coarse, grass-like leaves and large, showy inflorescences that can reach several feet in height. The plant produces dense clusters of small, spike-like flowers that are often pink or red in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a common ornamental and forage species.

Its rapid growth and hardiness make it adaptable to various environmental conditions.
