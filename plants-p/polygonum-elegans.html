Polygonum elegans

Polygonum elegans, commonly known as the elegant knotweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants commonly referred to as buckwheats and smartweeds. The plant features slender, erect stems that are often tinged with red, and its leaves are ovate with a smooth or slightly toothed margin. It produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters, which bloom in late summer.

Polygonum elegans is often found in moist habitats such as stream banks and wet meadows.
