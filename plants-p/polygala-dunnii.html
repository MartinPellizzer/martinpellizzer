Polygala dunnii

Polygala dunnii, commonly known as the dwarf milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple in color, with distinctive spurred petals. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in well-drained soils in full sun.

Polygala dunnii is often found in rocky or sandy habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
