Pyrethrum hirtum

Pyrethrum hirtum, commonly known as the hairy pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, spherical flower heads that are covered in fine, hairy structures. Pyrethrum hirtum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.

Its flowers contain natural insecticides called pyrethrins, which are used in organic pest control.
