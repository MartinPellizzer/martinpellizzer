Pittosporum heterophyllum var. ledoides

Pittosporum heterophyllum var. ledoides, commonly known as the New Zealand Pittosporum, is a flowering shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and fragrant white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in coastal and lowland areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are elliptical, leathery, and have a distinctive aromatic scent when crushed.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and visual interest.
