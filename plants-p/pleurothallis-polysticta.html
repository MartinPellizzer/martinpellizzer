Pleurothallis polysticta

Pleurothallis polysticta, commonly known as the spotted pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant typically has elliptical or ovate leaves that are arranged in two rows along the stem. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
