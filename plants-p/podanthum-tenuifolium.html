Podanthum tenuifolium

Podanthum tenuifolium, commonly known as the slender-leaf podanthum, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, narrow leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its foliage is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported calming properties.

Podanthum tenuifolium is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid climates.
