Piper fragrans

Piper fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant pepper plant, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into dark purple berries. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant's essential oils are also valued for their fragrance and potential therapeutic properties.
