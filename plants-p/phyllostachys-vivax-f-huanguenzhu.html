Phyllostachys vivax f. huanguenzhu

Phyllostachys vivax f. huanguenzhu, commonly known as the Huanguenzhu bamboo, is a variant of the common bamboo species native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, erect culms that can reach heights of up to 6 meters. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth, glossy surface. This bamboo thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for ornamental planting in gardens.

It is valued for its elegant appearance and resilience in various climates.
