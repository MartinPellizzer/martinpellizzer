Pterothrix flaccida

Pterothrix flaccida, commonly known as the flaccid pterothrix, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Pterothrix flaccida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
