Phyllanthus ciccoides var. ciccoides

Phyllanthus ciccoides var. ciccoides, commonly known as the African spurge, is a perennial shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along its stems. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, greenish fruits containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it adaptable to various environmental conditions.
