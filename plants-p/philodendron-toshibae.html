Philodendron toshibae

Philodendron toshibae, commonly known as the Toshibae Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by distinctive, spiky berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
