Primulina tribracteata var. tribracteata

Primulina tribracteata var. tribracteata, commonly known as the velvetleaf streptocarpus, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Thailand and Malaysia.
