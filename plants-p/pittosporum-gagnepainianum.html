Pittosporum gagnepainianum

Pittosporum gagnepainianum, commonly known as the Gagnepain's pittosporum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pittosporaceae.

It is native to New Zealand and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, with a glossy appearance, and it produces yellowish-white flowers in clusters.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
