Phyteuma hemisphaericum f. tatianae

Phyteuma hemisphaericum f. tatianae, commonly known as the Tatiana's Globe Thistle, is a cultivated form of the globe thistle.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces spherical, blue to purple flower heads. The plant features silver-gray foliage with a fuzzy texture, and its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens or containers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, and it is valued for its ornamental appeal.

The inflorescence is unique, resembling a globe, and it blooms in late spring to early summer.
