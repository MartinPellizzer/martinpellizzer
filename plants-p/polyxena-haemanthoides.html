Polyxena haemanthoides

Polyxena haemanthoides, commonly known as the red-flowered bellvista, is a striking perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features showy, tubular red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and botanical study.
