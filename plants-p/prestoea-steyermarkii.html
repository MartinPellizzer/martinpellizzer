Prestoea steyermarkii

Prestoea steyermarkii, commonly known as the Steyermark's poinciana, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its distinctive, fern-like leaves. The plant produces showy, pink to red bracts that resemble petals, which are actually modified leaves. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, well-drained soils.

Prestoea steyermarkii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
