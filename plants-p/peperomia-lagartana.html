Peperomia lagartana

Peperomia lagartana, commonly known as the Mexican Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Central America.

It features thick, waxy, oval-shaped leaves that are often dark green with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance for low light and minimal water requirements.

Peperomia lagartana is prized for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
