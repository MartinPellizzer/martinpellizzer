Piper herzogii

Piper herzogii, commonly known as the Herzog's pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with opposite, ovate leaves that are often pubescent. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
