Polygala stenopetala

Polygala stenopetala, commonly known as the narrow-petalled milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant has compound leaves with a few pairs of leaflets and produces small, bead-like fruits. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
