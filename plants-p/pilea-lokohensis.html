Pilea lokohensis

Pilea lokohensis, commonly known as the Lokohensis prayer plant, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to Cameroon and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that often display variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, followed by berries that mature to a deep red. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Pilea lokohensis is popular among plant enthusiasts for its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
