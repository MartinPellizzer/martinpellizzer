Phyllanthus guianensis var. acuminatus

Phyllanthus guianensis var. acuminatus, commonly known as the acuminated phyllanthus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Guyana. The plant is characterized by its smooth, succulent stems and opposite, elliptical leaves with acute tips. This plant is often found in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
