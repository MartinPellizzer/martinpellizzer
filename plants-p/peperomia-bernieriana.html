Peperomia bernieriana

Peperomia bernieriana, commonly known as the Bernier's pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and thick, waxy leaves that are typically green or variegated. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or red, followed by small, round berries.

Peperomia bernieriana is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
