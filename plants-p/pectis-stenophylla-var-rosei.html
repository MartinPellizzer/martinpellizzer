Pectis stenophylla var. rosei

Pectis stenophylla var. rosei, commonly known as the roseleaf pectis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces showy, pinkish-red flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
