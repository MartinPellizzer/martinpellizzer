Polygonum renii

Polygonum renii, commonly known as the kidney leaf plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants commonly referred to as buckwheats. The plant is characterized by its alternate, ovate leaves that are often kidney-shaped, giving it its common name. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color.

Polygonum renii is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks.
