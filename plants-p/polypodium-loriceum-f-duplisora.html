Polypodium loriceum f. duplisora

Polypodium loriceum f. duplisora, commonly known as the double-leaf water fern, is a rare fern species characterized by its unique frond morphology.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia. The plant exhibits a distinctive feature where its fronds produce two separate leaves, a trait that sets it apart from other members of the species. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found near streams or in dense forest undergrowth.
