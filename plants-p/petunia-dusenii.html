Petunia dusenii

Petunia dusenii, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada petunia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of California and Nevada.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 10 to 15 centimeters. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of purple, violet, and white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Petunia dusenii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.
