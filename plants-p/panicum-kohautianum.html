Panicum kohautianum

Panicum kohautianum, commonly known as the Kohaut's panicgrass, is a species of grass native to Central America.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or disturbed areas.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
