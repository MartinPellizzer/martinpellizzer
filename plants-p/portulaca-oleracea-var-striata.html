Portulaca oleracea var. striata

Portulaca oleracea var. striata, commonly known as the striped portulaca, is a flowering plant native to warm climates and often found in disturbed soils.

It is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a common weed in gardens and agricultural fields. Its rapid growth and ability to propagate through seeds and vegetative means contribute to its widespread presence.

The striped pattern on its leaves is a distinguishing feature of this variant.
