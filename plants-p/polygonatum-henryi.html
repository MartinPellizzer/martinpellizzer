Polygonatum henryi

Polygonatum henryi, commonly known as the Chinese Solomon's Seal, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are lanceolate and arranged in a basal rosette, and it thrives in shaded, moist environments.
