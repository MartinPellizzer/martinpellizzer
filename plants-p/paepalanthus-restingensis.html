Paepalanthus restingensis

Paepalanthus restingensis, commonly known as the Restinga pincushion.

It is a flowering plant native to Brazil, specifically found in the coastal restinga ecosystems. The plant is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and clusters of small, white flowers. It thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is adapted to the harsh, salty conditions of the coastal environment.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species.
