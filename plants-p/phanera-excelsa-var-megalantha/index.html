Phanera Excelsa Var. Megalantha

Phanera excelsa var. megalantha is a striking tropical plant known for its large, showy flowers. Native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. This variant is particularly prized for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms that come in shades of red and orange. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among horticulturists and plant enthusiasts. Cultivation requires careful attention to moisture and light conditions to ensure its health and beauty.
