Porpax macrantha subsp. macrantha

Porpax macrantha subsp.

macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered porpax, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It features erect, branched stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height.

The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink to purple in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, white hairs, giving them a silvery appearance.

This species is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
