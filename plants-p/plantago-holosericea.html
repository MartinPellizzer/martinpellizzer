Plantago holosericea

Plantago holosericea, commonly known as the silky plantain, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of broad, lance-shaped leaves covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces tall, slender flowering stems that bear dense clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

The species is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
