Pleione × kohlsii

Pleione × kohlsii, commonly known as the hybrid pleione orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Pleione formosana and Pleione kohlsii.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to China, particularly in the Yunnan Province. This plant is characterized by its elegant, lance-shaped leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in spring. The flowers are usually pale yellow to white with red or orange markings, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.

It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil for optimal growth.
