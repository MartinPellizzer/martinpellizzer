Polygonum reichenbachii

Polygonum reichenbachii, commonly known as the Chinese knotweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants known for their distinctive leaf shapes and colorful flowers. The plant features triangular, glossy leaves and produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and streambanks.

Polygonum reichenbachii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
