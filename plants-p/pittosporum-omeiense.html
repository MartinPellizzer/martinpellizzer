Pittosporum omeiense

Pittosporum omeiense, commonly known as the Chinese pittosporum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, dark purple berries that are edible but not particularly palatable. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Pittosporum omeiense is also valued for its ability to tolerate various soil types and its use in traditional Chinese medicine.
