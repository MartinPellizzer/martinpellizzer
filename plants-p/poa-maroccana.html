Poa maroccana

Poa maroccana, commonly known as Moroccan bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to North Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and dark green, narrow leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny blue to purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Poa maroccana is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
