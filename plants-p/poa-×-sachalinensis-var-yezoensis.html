Poa × sachalinensis var. yezoensis

Poa × sachalinensis var. yezoensis, commonly known as Yezo grass, is a hybrid perennial grass native to the cooler regions of East Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted foliage and slender, often blue-green colored blades. This variety thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is typically found in wet meadows and along the edges of ponds. Its growth habit makes it suitable for erosion control and ornamental use in gardens.

The plant plays an important ecological role in its native habitats by providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
