Phyllanthus odontadenius var. braunii

Phyllanthus odontadenius var. braunii, commonly known as the African date palm, is a tropical plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small tree or shrub that typically grows up to 5 meters in height, characterized by its pinnate leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces edible fruits that are similar in appearance to dates, hence its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and riverine areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in local communities.
