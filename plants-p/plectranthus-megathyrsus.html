Plectranthus megathyrsus

Plectranthus megathyrsus, commonly known as African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of purple or violet flowers. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Plectranthus megathyrsus is also valued for its aromatic leaves, which are used in traditional medicine and as a source of essential oils.
