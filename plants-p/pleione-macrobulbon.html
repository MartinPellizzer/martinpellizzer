Pleione macrobulbon

Pleione macrobulbon, commonly known as the giant pleione orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to the Himalayas.

This plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in spring, featuring a vivid combination of pink and white petals. It thrives in cool, humid environments at high altitudes, often growing on rocky slopes or in forest understories. The plant has a pseudobulb structure that stores water and nutrients, supporting its growth in challenging mountainous conditions.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a prized species among orchid enthusiasts.
