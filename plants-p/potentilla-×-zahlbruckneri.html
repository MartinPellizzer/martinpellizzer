Potentilla × zahlbruckneri

Potentilla × zahlbruckneri, commonly known as the Zahlsbruckner rose.

It is a hybrid shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces showy, double flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom in early summer. It is well-suited for rock gardens and borders due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice among gardeners seeking ornamental and hardy plants.
