Paphiopedilum × nitens

Paphiopedilum × nitens, commonly known as the hybrid velvet orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Paphiopedilum sanderianum and Paphiopedilum suzannae.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia. The plant features thick, velvety leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a unique lip structure that resembles a pouch. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

This hybrid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in both greenhouse and garden settings.
