Panicum notochthonum

Panicum notochthonum, commonly known as the subterranean panic grass, is a unique plant species characterized by its specialized adaptation to underground environments.

It possesses elongated rhizomes that allow it to thrive in soil layers where light is limited. The plant's leaves are typically narrow and adapted for minimal water loss, reflecting its arid habitat preferences. Its reproductive structures are enclosed within the soil, ensuring protection from environmental stressors.

Due to its elusive nature, little is known about its full botanical characteristics and ecological role.
