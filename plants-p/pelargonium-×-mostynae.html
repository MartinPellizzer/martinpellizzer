Pelargonium × mostynae

Pelargonium × mostynae, commonly known as the mostynae geranium, is a hybrid plant that belongs to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white, often with a distinctive bicolor pattern. The plant is native to South Africa and is cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it adaptable to various growing conditions.

Its foliage is usually dark green and velvety, adding to its aesthetic appeal.
