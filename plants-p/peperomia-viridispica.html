Peperomia viridispica

Peperomia viridispica, commonly known as the green-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia viridispica is prized for its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
