Pavonia mutisii var. mollis

Pavonia mutisii var. mollis, commonly known as the silky pavonia, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it a distinctive tactile quality. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil, making it a suitable choice for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value and adaptability have made it a popular plant among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts.
