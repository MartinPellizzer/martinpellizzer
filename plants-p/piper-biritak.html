Piper biritak

Piper biritak, commonly known as the Biritak pepper, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with slender stems that often require support. Its leaves are typically alternate, ovate, and have a glossy surface.

The small, greenish-yellow flowers are followed by the fruit, which is a small, elongated berry containing the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and cuisine.
