Pilea rivularis var. macrodonta

Pilea rivularis var. macrodonta, commonly known as the large-leaf pilea, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically larger than those of the standard Pilea rivularis. The plant produces small, white flowers that appear in clusters, often followed by red berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
