Peperomia wernerrauhii

Peperomia wernerrauhii, commonly known as the Wernerrauhii peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a subtle sheen, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
