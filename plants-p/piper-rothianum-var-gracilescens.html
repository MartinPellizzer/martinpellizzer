Piper rothianum var. gracilescens

Piper rothianum var. gracilescens, commonly known as the slender pepper plant, is a tropical climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, often with a slight pubescence. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that develop into bright red, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, warm environments and is often grown as an ornamental or for its edible berries.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for both horticultural and medicinal purposes.
