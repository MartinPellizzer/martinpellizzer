Peperomia sanramonensis

Peperomia sanramonensis, commonly known as the San Ramon peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture, adapted to conserve moisture in its natural habitat. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or red, emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its slow growth and ornamental foliage, it is often favored by collectors and enthusiasts of compact houseplants.
