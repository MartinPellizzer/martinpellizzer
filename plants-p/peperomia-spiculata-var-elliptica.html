Peperomia spiculata var. elliptica

Peperomia spiculata var. elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, elliptical-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its low maintenance and attractive foliage make it a versatile addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
