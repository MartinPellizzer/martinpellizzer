Pseudolysimachion × mauksii

Pseudolysimachion × mauksii, commonly known as the hybrid lily, is a perennial plant belonging to the Liliaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often grown for its beauty and fragrance.
