Polycampium hastatum

Polycampium hastatum, commonly known as the broad-leaved polycampium, is a herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of Europe.

It features upright stems that can grow up to 60 cm in height, covered with fine hairs. The leaves are lance-shaped, with a distinctive serrated margin and a bluish-green coloration. It produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer.

This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens and borders.
