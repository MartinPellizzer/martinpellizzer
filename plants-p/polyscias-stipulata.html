Polyscias stipulata

Polyscias stipulata, commonly known as the laceleaf polycarpic, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its finely serrated, ovate leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by spherical, red or orange berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.
