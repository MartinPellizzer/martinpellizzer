Piper cyphophyllopse var. cyphophyllopse

Piper cyphophyllopse var. cyphophyllopse, commonly known as the Cyphophyllopse pepper, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piper genus in the family Piperaceae, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, dark berries that are used in traditional medicine and as a spice. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical features include heart-shaped leaves and a distinctive, aromatic fruit.
