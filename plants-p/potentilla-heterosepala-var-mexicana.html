Potentilla heterosepala var. mexicana

Potentilla heterosepala var. mexicana, commonly known as Mexican potentilla, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with serrated edges, and it prefers well-drained soils in full sun.
