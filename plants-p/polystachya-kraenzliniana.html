Polystachya kraenzliniana

Polystachya kraenzliniana, commonly known as the Zebra Orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, horizontally arranged leaves that give it a unique, zebra-like appearance. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing numerous small, white or pale green flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, loose soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
