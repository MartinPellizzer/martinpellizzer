Plocama Dezfulensis

Plocama dezfulensis is a rare and unique plant species native to the Dezful region of Iran. It thrives in specific environmental conditions that are increasingly threatened by human activity and climate change. This plant is notable for its distinct morphological features and ecological significance within its habitat. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered endangered and requires urgent conservation efforts. Studying Plocama dezfulensis offers valuable insights into biodiversity and the importance of preserving fragile ecosystems.
