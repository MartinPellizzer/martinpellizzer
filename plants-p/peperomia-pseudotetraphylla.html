Peperomia pseudotetraphylla

Peperomia pseudotetraphylla, commonly known as the four-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically heart-shaped or ovate, with a vibrant green coloration. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are followed by tiny, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a suitable plant for tabletop displays or as part of a curated plant collection.
