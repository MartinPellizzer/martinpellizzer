Platyloma adiantoides

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in tropical or subtropical regions. Its rhizome is creeping and produces both roots and new shoots.
