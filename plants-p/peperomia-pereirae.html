Peperomia pereirae

Peperomia pereirae, commonly known as the Brazilian peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia pereirae is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
