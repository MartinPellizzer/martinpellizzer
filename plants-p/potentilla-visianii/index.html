Potentilla Visianii

Potentilla visianii is a rare and beautiful flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It is known for its delicate yellow flowers and compact growth habit, making it a sought-after species among plant enthusiasts. This shrub thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring careful cultivation to flourish. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered in many areas. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a valuable subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
