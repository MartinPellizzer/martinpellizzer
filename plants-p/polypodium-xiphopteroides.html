Polypodium xiphopteroides

Polypodium xiphopteroides, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features a distinctive, elongated frond with a narrow, linear shape, giving it its common name. The plant has a rhizome that is often covered in a layer of brown, membranous tissue. Its fronds are typically evergreen and can grow up to several feet in length.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in tropical forests.
