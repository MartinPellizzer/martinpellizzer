Polystachya praealta

Polystachya praealta, commonly known as the tall sword fern, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its tall, arching fronds that can reach heights of up to 3 meters, making it a dramatic addition to any landscape. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit, with long, slender stems that produce dense clusters of lance-shaped leaves. Its foliage is typically a deep green, with a metallic sheen that reflects light beautifully.

Polystachya praealta thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical forests.
