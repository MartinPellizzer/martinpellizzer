Phillyrea cordifolia var. kebdanensis

Phillyrea cordifolia var. kebdanensis, commonly known as the Kebdanensis Phillyrea, is a rare variant of the Phillyrea cordifolia species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by woody, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.
