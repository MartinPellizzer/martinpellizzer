Podocarpus Macrophyllus F. Grandifolius

Podocarpus macrophyllus f. grandifolius is a unique variation of the sacred bamboo known for its strikingly large leaves. This evergreen plant is native to southern China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its foliage provides a lush green canopy that enhances any garden setting. The plant is also valued for its resilience and ability to thrive in various soil conditions. It remains a popular choice for both landscape design and indoor gardening.
