Peschiera blanda

Peschiera blanda, commonly known as the soft marsh gentian, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to wetland and marsh environments in Europe.

It features showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has slender, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils and is often found in shallow water or along the edges of ponds.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species for wetland ecosystems and a popular choice for aquatic gardens.
