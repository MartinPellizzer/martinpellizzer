Pyrus yunnanensis var. veitchii

Pyrus yunnanensis var. veitchii, commonly known as the Yunnan pear, is a variant of the Yunnan pear tree native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree characterized by its smooth, gray bark and alternate, simple leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, followed by edible, sweet fruits that are similar to common pears.

It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit production.
