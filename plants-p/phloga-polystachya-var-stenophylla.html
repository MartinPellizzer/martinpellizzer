Phloga polystachya var. stenophylla

Phloga polystachya var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf phloga, is a species of flowering plant in the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually greenish or reddish in color. It is native to certain regions of Europe and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

This variant is distinguished by its more compact growth habit compared to other varieties of Phloga polystachya.
