Pilea balfourii

Pilea balfourii, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to Yunnan Province in China and is characterized by its rosette of heart-shaped, fleshy leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are clustered in a spadix, typical of the Araceae family. It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.

Pilea balfourii thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.
