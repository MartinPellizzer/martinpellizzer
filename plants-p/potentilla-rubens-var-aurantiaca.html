Potentilla rubens var. aurantiaca

Potentilla rubens var. aurantiaca, commonly known as the golden potentilla, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features finely divided, feathery leaves that are typically covered in a soft, silvery down. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant color and compact growth habit.
