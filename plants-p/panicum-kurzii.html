Panicum kurzii

Panicum kurzii, commonly known as Kurz's panicgrass, is a species of grass native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial plant that typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to dry conditions and can tolerate poor soil, making it a hardy species.

Its botanical features make it an important component of local ecosystems and a subject of study in plant ecology.
