Peperomia heydei

Peperomia heydei, commonly known as the Shiny Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of greenish-white, which appear in clusters along the stems. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Peperomia heydei is prized for its attractive foliage and air-purifying qualities.
