Polygonum coccineum var. terrestre

Polygonum coccineum var. terrestre, commonly known as the red root or redstem filaree, is a low-growing annual plant native to North America.

It features slender, reddish stems that are often tinged with purple, and it produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and wetlands.

This plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through seeds and rhizomes.
