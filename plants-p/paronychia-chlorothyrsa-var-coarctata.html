Paronychia chlorothyrsa var. coarctata

Paronychia chlorothyrsa var. coarctata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf sandmat, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a subtle ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
