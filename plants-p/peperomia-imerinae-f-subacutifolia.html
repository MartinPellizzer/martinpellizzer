Peperomia imerinae f. subacutifolia

Peperomia imerinae f. subacutifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions.

It features small, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it suitable for both container and garden settings.
