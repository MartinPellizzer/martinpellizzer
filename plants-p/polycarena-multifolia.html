Polycarena multifolia

Polycarena multifolia, commonly known as the many-leaved polycarena, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, rosette-like growth habit and broad, strap-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces striking inflorescences with vivid, colorful bracts that can range from red to orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated as a decorative plant in greenhouses and botanical gardens.
