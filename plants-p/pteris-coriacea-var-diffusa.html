Pteris coriacea var. diffusa

Pteris coriacea var. diffusa, commonly known as the rough holly fern, is a variant of the common holly fern.

It is a perennial evergreen fern native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features leathery, serrated fronds that are typically dark green with a rough texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant collections.
