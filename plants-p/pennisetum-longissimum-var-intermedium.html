Pennisetum longissimum var. intermedium

Pennisetum longissimum var. intermedium, commonly known as the giant feather grass, is a tall, ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features long, slender, arching leaves that can grow up to 6 feet in height, giving it a dramatic, flowing appearance. The plant produces feathery plumes that add visual interest, especially in the fall. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for perennial gardens and landscapes.

Its drought tolerance and minimal maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
