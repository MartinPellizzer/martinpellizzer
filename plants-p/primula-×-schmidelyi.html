Primula × schmidelyi

Primula × schmidelyi, commonly known as the Schmidelyi primrose, is a hybrid species within the Primulaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, bell-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. Its foliage consists of broad, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of down.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to garden conditions.
