Polygala dura

Polygala dura, commonly known as the hard polygala, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowers are usually pale blue or white, with five petals and a distinctive spiny appendage.

Polygala dura is often found in open woodlands and along rocky outcrops.
