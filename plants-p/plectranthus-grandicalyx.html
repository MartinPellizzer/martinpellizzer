Plectranthus grandicalyx

Plectranthus grandicalyx, commonly known as the purple heart plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive velvety purple leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of purple and pink. The plant belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it suitable for container gardening and rock gardens.
