Philodendron alternans

Philodendron alternans, commonly known as the silver vine, is a climbing plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with silver and green patterns, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has a vining growth habit, making it suitable for hanging baskets or trained on supports. Its leaves are typically glossy and can grow quite large, adding visual interest to any indoor space.

Philodendron alternans thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its health.
