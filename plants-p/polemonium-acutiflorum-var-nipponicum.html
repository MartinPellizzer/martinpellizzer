Polemonium acutiflorum var. nipponicum

Polemonium acutiflorum var. nipponicum, commonly known as the Japanese garden bell, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped flowers, typically in shades of blue or purple. The leaves are lance-shaped and have a velvety texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

This variety is valued for its early spring blooms and ability to naturalize in suitable conditions.
