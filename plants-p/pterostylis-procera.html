Pterostylis procera

Pterostylis procera, commonly known as the tall greenhood orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. The plant features a single, upright stem with a single leaf and a single, elongated greenhood-shaped flower. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a distinctive hood-like structure that protects the reproductive organs.

Pterostylis procera is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its specialized pollination mechanisms.
