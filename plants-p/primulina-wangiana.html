Primulina wangiana

Primulina wangiana, commonly known as the Wangian violet, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of purple and pink. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and thrives in humid, shaded environments. It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Primulina wangiana is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
