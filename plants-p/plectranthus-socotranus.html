Plectranthus socotranus

Plectranthus socotranus, commonly known as Socotra starleaf, is a unique plant native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue or purple in color. It thrives in arid environments and is adapted to the harsh conditions of its native habitat.

Due to its distinctive appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical studies.
