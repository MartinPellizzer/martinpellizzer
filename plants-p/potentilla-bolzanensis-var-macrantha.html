Potentilla bolzanensis var. macrantha

Potentilla bolzanensis var. macrantha, commonly known as the Bolzanensis rose.

This plant is a flowering shrub native to the high-altitude regions of the Alps, particularly in Italy and Austria. It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, showy white or pale yellow flowers. The leaves are typically oval-shaped with a slightly hairy surface, and the plant is often found in rocky or alpine meadows.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in harsh, cold environments.
