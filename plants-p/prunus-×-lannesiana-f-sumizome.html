Prunus × lannesiana f. sumizome

Prunus × lannesiana f. sumizome, commonly known as the Sumizome cherry, is a unique ornamental cultivar prized for its exquisite floral display.

It is a hybrid of Prunus lannesiana, characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, double flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features glossy, ovate leaves that provide a striking contrast to its showy blossoms. It is well-suited for small gardens or container cultivation due to its manageable size.

The Sumizome cherry is also noted for its excellent cold hardiness, making it adaptable to a range of temperate climates.
