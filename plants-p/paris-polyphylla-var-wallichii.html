Paris polyphylla var. wallichii

Paris polyphylla var. wallichii, commonly known as the Himalayan arrowhead, is a member of the Melanthiaceae family and is native to the Himalayas.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white or greenish flowers. The plant is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes.

Its rhizomes are often harvested for their bioactive compounds.
