Piper batuanum

Piper batuanum, commonly known as the Batuanum pepper, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with heart-shaped leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruits are berries that turn red when mature, and they are used in traditional medicine and as a spice.

The botanical features of Piper batuanum make it a valuable species in both ecological and economic contexts.
