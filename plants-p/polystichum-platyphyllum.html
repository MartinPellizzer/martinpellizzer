Polystichum platyphyllum

Polystichum platyphyllum, commonly known as the broad-leaf fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, leathery, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The fronds are typically dark green and have a distinctive, broad, flat shape. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
