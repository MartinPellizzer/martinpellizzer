Petunia violacea f. intermedia

Petunia violacea f. intermedia, commonly known as the violet petunia, is a hybrid form of the violet petunia, characterized by its striking violet-blue flower colors.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshade plants. The plant is known for its compact growth habit and profuse blooming, making it a popular choice for gardeners. Its flowers are typically five-petaled and have a delicate, velvety texture.

This variety thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
