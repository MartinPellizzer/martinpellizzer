Platyloma andromedifolium

Platyloma andromedifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf buckthorn, is a shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features compound leaves with three to five elliptical leaflets, often with a silvery underside. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by dark blue berries in the fall. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in woodland edges and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Rhamnaceae family.
