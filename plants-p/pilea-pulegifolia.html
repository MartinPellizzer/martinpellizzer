Pilea pulegifolia

Pilea pulegifolia, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and slightly wavy, often with a pale green or white margin. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ornamental foliage and ease of care contribute to its widespread cultivation.
