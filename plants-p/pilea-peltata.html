Pilea peltata

Pilea peltata, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its round, shield-shaped leaves that give it a unique and decorative appearance. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color.

Pilea peltata is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and aesthetic appeal.
