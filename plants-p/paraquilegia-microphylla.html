Paraquilegia microphylla

Paraquilegia microphylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually blue or violet in color, blooming in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a rough texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.
