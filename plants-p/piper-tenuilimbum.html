Piper tenuilimbum

Piper tenuilimbum, commonly known as the slenderleaf pepper, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into elongated, narrow berries. Its leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The plant is valued for its aromatic leaves and fruits, which are used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.
