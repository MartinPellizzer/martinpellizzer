Pietrosia agardhii

Pietrosia agardhii, commonly known as the rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant produces showy, pink or red flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Pietrosia agardhii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in gardens and landscapes.
