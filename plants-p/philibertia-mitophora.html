Philibertia mitophora

Philibertia mitophora, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy yellow to orange ray flowers surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers. It typically grows in dry, rocky areas and is well adapted to Mediterranean climates.

Philibertia mitophora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
