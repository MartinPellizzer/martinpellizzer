Panicum monostachyum var. robustius

Panicum monostachyum var. robustius, commonly known as the robust panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and produces dense, cylindrical inflorescences that are often used in ornamental landscaping. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping and erosion control. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a coarse texture that adds visual interest to gardens.

It is also valued for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
