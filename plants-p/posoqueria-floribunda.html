Posoqueria floribunda

Posoqueria floribunda, commonly known as the Mexican flamebush, is a flowering plant native to Central America and the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its showy, orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly rough texture.

Posoqueria floribunda is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability to different soil types.
