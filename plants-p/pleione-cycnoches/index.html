Pleione Cycnoches

Pleione cycnoches is a rare and elegant orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. It is known for its striking white and purple flowers that bloom during the spring months. This plant thrives in cool, humid environments and requires specific care to flourish. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, Pleione cycnoches remains a challenging plant to cultivate outside its natural habitat.
