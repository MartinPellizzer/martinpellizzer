Peperomia barbarana

Peperomia barbarana, commonly known as the Congo peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features dense, waxy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a versatile addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
