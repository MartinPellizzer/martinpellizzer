Palmijuncus collinus

Palmijuncus collinus, commonly known as the slender palm, is a unique plant native to the island of Socotra.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its slender, unbranched trunk and clustered, fan-shaped leaves. The plant exhibits a distinctive growth pattern, with its leaves emerging in a spiral arrangement around the stem.

Its botanical features make it an important subject of study in the field of botany due to its adaptation to the arid environment of Socotra.
