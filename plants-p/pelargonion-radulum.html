Pelargonion radulum

Pelargonion radulum, commonly known as the silverleaf geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant has a trailing growth habit, making it suitable for hanging baskets or containers. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
