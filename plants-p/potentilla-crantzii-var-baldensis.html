Potentilla crantzii var. baldensis

Potentilla crantzii var. baldensis, commonly known as the Bald Mountain potentilla, is a low-growing shrub native to the Appalachian Mountains.

It features dense clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has wiry, ascending stems covered with fine, grayish hairs. Its leaves are narrow, elliptic, and have a slightly serrated margin.

It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
