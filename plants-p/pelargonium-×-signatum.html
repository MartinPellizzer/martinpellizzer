Pelargonium × signatum

Pelargonium × signatum, commonly known as the signatum geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant produces trifoliate leaves with serrated edges, and its stems are often covered in fine hairs. Native to South Africa, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

It is widely cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
