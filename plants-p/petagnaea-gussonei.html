Petagnaea gussonei

Petagnaea gussonei, commonly known as the Gussone's grass, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. Petagnaea gussonei is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to thrive in arid or semi-arid environments.
