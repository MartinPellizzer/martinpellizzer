Plenasium claytonianum

Plenasium claytonianum, commonly known as the giant swamp lily, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can grow up to 2 meters in length, often with a waxy, dark green surface. The plant produces impressive, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange, which bloom in the evening. It thrives in wet, swampy environments and requires high humidity and consistent moisture.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental cultivation in warm, humid climates.
