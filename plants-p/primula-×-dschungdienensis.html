Primula × dschungdienensis

Primula × dschungdienensis, commonly known as the Dschungdien primrose, is a hybrid species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has oval, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It prefers cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded woodland areas.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
