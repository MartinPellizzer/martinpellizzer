Premna grandipaniculata

Premna grandipaniculata, commonly known as the large panicle premna, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces showy, panicle-like inflorescences that are typically white or pale purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
