Polychilos × gersenii

Polychilos × gersenii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring cross between two species of the genus Polychilos.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically come in vibrant shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by horticulturists.
