Pellionia procridioides

Pellionia procridioides, commonly known as the velvetleaf pellionia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often a deep green with a soft texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Pellionia procridioides is valued for its ornamental leaves and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
