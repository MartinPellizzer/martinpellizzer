Potentilla verna var. rubens

Potentilla verna var. rubens, commonly known as red sandalwood or red potentilla, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, depending on the variety. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges, meadows, and rocky areas.
