Panicum chaetophoron

Panicum chaetophoron, commonly known as silky panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces delicate, silky-haired inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist to wet soils, often found in swampy areas or along the edges of ponds. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Panicum chaetophoron is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in wetland restoration projects.
