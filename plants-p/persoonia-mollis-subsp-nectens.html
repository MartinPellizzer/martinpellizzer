Persoonia mollis subsp. nectens

Persoonia mollis subsp.

nectens, commonly known as the silky persoonia, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia. It is characterized by its soft, silvery foliage and small, urn-shaped flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in well-drained conditions. Its leaves are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
