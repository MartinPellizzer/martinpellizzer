Pleomele surculosa

Pleomele surculosa, commonly known as the silver pleomele, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It features large, leathery, silver-green leaves that are often marked with distinctive white or pale veins. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by red berries. It is well adapted to humid, tropical climates and thrives in partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor spaces.
