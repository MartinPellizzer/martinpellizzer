Physematium × maxonii

Physematium × maxonii, commonly known as the hybrid spider orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its intricate, spider-like flowers that are typically white or pale green with a distinctive central column. This plant is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. It has linear, grass-like leaves and produces showy, fragrant blooms during the warmer months.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
