Polygala fishiae

Polygala fishiae, commonly known as the Fish Island milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern China.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils along the shoreline. The plant produces small, spiny pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellowish, with a distinctive structure that resembles a spurred horn.
