Portulaca olitoria

Portulaca olitoria, commonly known as purslane, is an annual flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in the sun. The plant thrives in hot, dry environments and is often found growing in gardens, fields, and along roadsides. Its leaves are edible and rich in omega-3 fatty acids, making it a nutritious addition to salads and cooked dishes.

Portulaca olitoria is also valued for its ability to withstand drought and poor soil conditions.
