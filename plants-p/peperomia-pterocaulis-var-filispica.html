Peperomia pterocaulis var. filispica

Peperomia pterocaulis var. filispica, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, giving it a unique, ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters, though it is primarily cultivated for its foliage rather than its blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ability to thrive indoors with minimal care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
