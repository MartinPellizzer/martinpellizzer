Pentzia spinescens

Pentzia spinescens, commonly known as the spiny pentzia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting to its arid environment. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the cooler months. Its spiny foliage serves as a defense mechanism against herbivores.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
