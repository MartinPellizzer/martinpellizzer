Peperomia pedicellata

Peperomia pedicellata, commonly known as the Rosy Glow Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are often tinged with pink or red hues, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia pedicellata is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and occasional pruning to maintain its shape.
