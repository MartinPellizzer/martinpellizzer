Pinguicula bicolor f. gorcensis

Pinguicula bicolor f. gorcensis, commonly known as the Gorcenskaya butterwort, is a rare carnivorous plant native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It is characterized by its small, sticky leaves that trap and digest small insects, a feature typical of the genus Pinguicula. The plant produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in bogs or rocky crevices.
