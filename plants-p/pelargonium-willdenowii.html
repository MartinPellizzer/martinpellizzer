Pelargonium willdenowii

Pelargonium willdenowii, commonly known as the willdenow's geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with hairy, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Pelargonium willdenowii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
