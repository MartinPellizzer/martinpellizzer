Premna merinoi

Premna merinoi, commonly known as the Wooly Premna, is a small shrub native to the tropical regions of eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are typically elliptical or ovate, with a pale green upper surface and a white underside. It produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink, followed by small, fleshy berries.

This plant is often found in coastal and wet tropical rainforest areas, where it thrives in moist, shaded conditions.
