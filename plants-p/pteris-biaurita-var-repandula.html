Pteris biaurita var. repandula

Pteris biaurita var. repandula, commonly known as the broad-leafed water fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, bipinnate fronds that are often arched or pendant. The fronds are typically bright green and have a distinctive, delicate appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near streams or in humid forests.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and indoor spaces.
