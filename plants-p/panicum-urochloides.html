Panicum urochloides

Panicum urochloides, commonly known as buffelgrass, is a perennial grass native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh climates.

However, it is also considered an invasive species in some regions, where it can outcompete native vegetation.
