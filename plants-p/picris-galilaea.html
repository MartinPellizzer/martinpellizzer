Picris galilaea

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its yellow flowers and feathery leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Picris galilaea is also known for its medicinal properties, having been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
