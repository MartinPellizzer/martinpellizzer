Pennisetum spectabile

Pennisetum spectabile, commonly known as purple top panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, arching spikes of purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is drought-tolerant and prefers well-drained soil.

Pennisetum spectabile is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
