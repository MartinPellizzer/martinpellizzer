Peperomia cubensis var. haitensis

Peperomia cubensis var. haitensis, commonly known as the Haitian Peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the Caribbean.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture, often adorned with subtle variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its resilience and ornamental appeal.
