Polystichum venustum

Polystichum venustum, commonly known as the silver feather fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It features fronds that are bipinnate, with a distinctive silvery-blue hue due to the presence of calcified sori on the undersides of the leaves. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its rhizomes are thick and wiry, allowing it to spread vegetatively and form dense colonies.

The fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with high humidity and acidic soil.
