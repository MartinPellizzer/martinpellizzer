Pleione kaatiae

Pleione kaatiae, commonly known as the Kaatia orchid, is a rare and exquisite member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in China and Myanmar. This plant is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in the wild.
