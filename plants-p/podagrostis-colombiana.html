Podagrostis colombiana

Podagrostis colombiana, commonly known as the Colombian grass or Colombian panic grass, is a species of grass native to South America, particularly Colombia.

It is a perennial plant that typically grows in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and wetlands. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It is characterized by its robust growth and ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.

Podagrostis colombiana plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms.
