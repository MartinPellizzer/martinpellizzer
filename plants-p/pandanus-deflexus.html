Pandanus deflexus

Pandanus deflexus, commonly known as the flexible pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Pandanus.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves that are often drooping or flexible, giving it its common name. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of many pandanus species.

Pandanus deflexus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional crafts in its native regions.
