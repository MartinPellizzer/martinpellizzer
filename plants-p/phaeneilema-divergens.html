Phaeneilema divergens

Phaeneilema divergens, commonly known as the divergent leaf orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stem and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically greenish-white in color.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
