Ptychosperma hartmannii

Ptychosperma hartmannii, commonly known as the African fan palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that form a distinctive fan shape. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas or near riverbanks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses around the world.
