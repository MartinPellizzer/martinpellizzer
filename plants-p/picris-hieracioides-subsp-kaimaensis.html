Picris hieracioides subsp. kaimaensis

Picris hieracioides subsp.

kaimaensis, commonly known as Kaimanawa goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its native range.
