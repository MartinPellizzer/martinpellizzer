Pfaffia luzuliflora f. gracilis

Pfaffia luzuliflora f. gracilis, commonly known as the slender leaf feverfew, is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and aromatic foliage. It is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly for its potential anti-inflammatory and analgesic effects.
