Pelargonium × odontophyllum

Pelargonium × odontophyllum, commonly known as the zonal geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, colorful, and often variegated leaves with distinct zones of different colors. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically blooming in summer. It is a popular ornamental plant, often used in gardens and as a potted plant due to its vibrant foliage and flowers.

Pelargonium × odontophyllum thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
