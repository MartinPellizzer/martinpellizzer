Paphiopedilum barbigerum var. lockianum

Paphiopedilum barbigerum var. lockianum, commonly known as the Lockian Paphiopedilum, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange with distinctive purple or brown markings. The plant produces a single, showy bloom on a stem that can reach up to 30 cm in length. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store moisture in its natural habitat.

This orchid thrives in shaded, humid environments and is prized for its ornamental value in horticulture.
