Polygala densiracemosa

Polygala densiracemosa, commonly known as the dense raceme milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, delicate flowers that are often pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and opposite, contributing to its drought-resistant adaptation.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
