Pleurothallis duplooyi

Pleurothallis duplooyi, commonly known as the Crimson Orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid with delicate, lance-shaped leaves and striking, pale pink to red flowers. The plant typically blooms during the dry season, producing fragrant, spurred flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
