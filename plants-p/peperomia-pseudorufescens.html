Peperomia pseudorufescens

Peperomia pseudorufescens, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically variegated with silver and green hues, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia pseudorufescens is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
