Peperomia wheeleri

Peperomia wheeleri, commonly known as the Wheeler's Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are usually dark green with a slight waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which are typically borne on short, erect spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia wheeleri is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and moderate humidity to flourish.
