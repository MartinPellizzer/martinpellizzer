Potentilla hirta var. afra

Potentilla hirta var. afra, commonly known as African sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has wiry, upright stems covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, often covered in a dense layer of fine pubescence.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
