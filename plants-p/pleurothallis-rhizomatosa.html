Pleurothallis rhizomatosa

Pleurothallis rhizomatosa, commonly known as the rhizomatous orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and green, often arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
