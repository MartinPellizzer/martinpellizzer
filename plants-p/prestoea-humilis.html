Prestoea humilis

Prestoea humilis, commonly known as the white cedar, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the Caribbean islands, including Cuba, Hispaniola, and the Bahamas. The plant is characterized by its small, white flowers and aromatic, evergreen leaves. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in tropical and subtropical regions.
