Phyteuma orbiculare f. glabratum

Phyteuma orbiculare f. glabratum, commonly known as the smooth-leaved physostegia, is a compact perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink to purple in color, blooming in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glabrous, giving it its specific epithet "glabratum.". It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
