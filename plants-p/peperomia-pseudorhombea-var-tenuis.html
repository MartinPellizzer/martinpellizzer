Peperomia pseudorhombea var. tenuis

Peperomia pseudorhombea var. tenuis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features thick, waxy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers in clusters, which are often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its slow growth and attractive foliage make it an ideal plant for container cultivation.
