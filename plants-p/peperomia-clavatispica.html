Peperomia clavatispica

Peperomia clavatispica, commonly known as the clustered spine peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are often dark green with a slight sheen, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale green. The plant is characterized by its dense, clustered growth habit and unique, spiky inflorescence, which gives it its common name. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia clavatispica is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
