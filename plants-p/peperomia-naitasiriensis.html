Peperomia naitasiriensis

Peperomia naitasiriensis, commonly known as the Naitasiri peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit. The plant features ovate to elliptical leaves with a glossy surface and a variety of colors, ranging from green to reddish hues. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, and it thrives in well-drained soil with indirect light.

Peperomia naitasiriensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
