Pleurothallis sphaeroglossa

Pleurothallis sphaeroglossa, commonly known as the globe-lipped orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive globular lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, airy substrates for optimal growth.

Due to its unique morphology and ornamental value, it is a sought-after plant among orchid enthusiasts.
