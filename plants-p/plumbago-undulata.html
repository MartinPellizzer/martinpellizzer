Plumbago undulata

Plumbago undulata, commonly known as blue plumbago, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, narrow, and slightly hairy leaves that are usually a vibrant green. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Plumbago undulata is also known for its ornamental value and is often used in ornamental hedges or as a border plant.
