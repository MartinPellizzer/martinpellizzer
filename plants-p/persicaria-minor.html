Persicaria minor

Persicaria minor, commonly known as narrowleaf smartweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in moist soils along stream banks and wetlands. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are clustered in dense, pink to red inflorescences, which bloom from late summer to early fall.

Persicaria minor is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed environments.
