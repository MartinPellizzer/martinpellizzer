Polystachya oligantha

Polystachya oligantha, commonly known as the slender-panicle orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant produces inflorescences of small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It prefers shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread slowly and form dense colonies.
