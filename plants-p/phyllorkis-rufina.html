Phyllorkis rufina

Phyllorkis rufina, commonly known as the red-flowered phyllorkis, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland and rocky habitats. The plant features slender stems and lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive reddish hue. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying shades of red and purple.

Phyllorkis rufina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
