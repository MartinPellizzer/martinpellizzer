Philippia latifolia

Philippia latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf philippia, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
