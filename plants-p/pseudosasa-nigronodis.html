Pseudosasa nigronodis

Pseudosasa nigronodis, commonly known as black-nodded bamboo, is a clumping bamboo species native to eastern China.

It is characterized by its dark green, narrow leaves and distinctive black nodules along the stems. The plant has a robust root system that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Pseudosasa nigronodis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ecological benefits in landscaping and restoration projects.
