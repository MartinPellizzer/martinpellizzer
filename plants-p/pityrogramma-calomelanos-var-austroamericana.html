Pityrogramma calomelanos var. austroamericana

Pityrogramma calomelanos var. austroamericana, commonly known as the South American water fern, is a variant of the water fern species native to the Americas.

It is a small, terrestrial fern that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. The plant features delicate, bipinnate fronds with a distinctive, almost lace-like appearance. Its leaves are usually bright green and have a waxy coating that helps retain moisture.

This variant is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in high humidity and partial shade.
