Piper jauaense

Piper jauaense, commonly known as the Jauaense pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with tendrils that allow it to ascend trees or other structures. Its leaves are typically alternate, ovate, and have a rough texture.

The fruit is a small, elongated berry that contains several seeds, and it is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
