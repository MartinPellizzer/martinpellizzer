Pittosporum anomalum

Pittosporum anomalum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped, glossy dark green leaves that are often slightly hairy beneath. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, round berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers a sunny to partially shaded location.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its attractive foliage and floral display.
