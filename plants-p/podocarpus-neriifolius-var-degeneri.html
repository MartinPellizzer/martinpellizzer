Podocarpus neriifolius var. degeneri

Podocarpus neriifolius var. degeneri, commonly known as the Degener's Neriifolius Podocarpus, is a variant of the Neriifolius Podocarpus, native to Southeast Asia.

It is a slow-growing evergreen tree characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, leathery leaves. The plant has a compact, rounded canopy and is often cultivated as a ornamental tree due to its aesthetic appeal. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden environments.

This variety is noted for its durability and resistance to pests, contributing to its popularity in horticultural settings.
