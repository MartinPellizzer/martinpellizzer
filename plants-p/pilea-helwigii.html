Pilea helwigii

Pilea helwigii, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features round, glossy, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and ability to thrive in moderate humidity.

Pilea helwigii is valued for its ornamental foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for houseplants.
