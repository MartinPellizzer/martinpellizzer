Polygala dewevrei var. schmitzii

Polygala dewevrei var. schmitzii, commonly known as the Schmitz's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, clustered flowers.
