Platycrater arguta var. hortensis

Platycrater arguta var. hortensis, commonly known as the Japanese spurge or hortensia, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features attractive, showy inflorescences composed of numerous small, white to pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with dense foliage that turns red in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to produce clusters of berries in the autumn.
