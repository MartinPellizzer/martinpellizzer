Panicum protensum

Panicum protensum, commonly known as tall panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks that can reach heights of up to six feet. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically green to reddish in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in open woodlands and prairies.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
