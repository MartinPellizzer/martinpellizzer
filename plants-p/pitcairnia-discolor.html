Pitcairnia discolor

Pitcairnia discolor, commonly known as the silver leaf pitcairnia, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a water-holding cup. The plant produces striking violet or purple flowers that bloom from the center of the rosette. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
