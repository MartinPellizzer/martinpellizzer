Philotheca salsolifolia

Philotheca salsolifolia, commonly known as the coastal daisy or sand daisy, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Australia.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub with gray-green foliage and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to coastal conditions, including salt spray and strong winds. It plays a role in stabilizing dunes and is often found in coastal heathlands.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for coastal gardens.
