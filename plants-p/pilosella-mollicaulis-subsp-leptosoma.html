Pilosella mollicaulis subsp. leptosoma

Pilosella mollicaulis subsp.

leptosoma, commonly known as the slender pilosella, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant is typically found in alpine and subalpine habitats, thriving in well-drained, rocky soils. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting various pollinators.

Its slender stems and hairy leaves distinguish it from other members of the Pilosella genus.
