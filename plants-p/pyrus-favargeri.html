Pyrus favargeri

Pyrus favargeri, commonly known as the favargeri pear.

This species is a small, deciduous tree native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It produces small, yellowish-green fruits that are typically harvested in late summer. The plant has a compact growth habit with dark green, oval-shaped leaves.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and fruit.
