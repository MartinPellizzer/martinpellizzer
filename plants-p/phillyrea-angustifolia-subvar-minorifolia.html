Phillyrea angustifolia subvar. minorifolia

Phillyrea angustifolia subvar. minorifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically narrower than those of the typical variety, giving it a distinct appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in axillary inflorescences, followed by small, spherical fruits. It is adapted to rocky and dry soils, making it a hardy species suitable for coastal and arid environments.

Its foliage and structure make it a valuable plant for ornamental and ecological landscaping.
