Pleurothallis siphoglossa

Pleurothallis siphoglossa, commonly known as the yellow-lipped pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features narrow, dark green leaves and produces delicate, yellow-green flowers with a distinctive lip. It is noted for its unique floral structure and is often found in understory habitats.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
