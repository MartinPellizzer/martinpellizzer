Poterium annuum

Poterium annuum, commonly known as the annual poterium, is an herbaceous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, oval-shaped seeds that are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for dry climates.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and traditional medicine.
