Premna serratifolia var. minor

Premna serratifolia var. minor, commonly known as the small-toothed premna, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, followed by small, spherical berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Its leaves are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
