Paranotis mitrasacmoides

Paranotis mitrasacmoides, commonly known as the Brazilian starfruit or golden starfruit, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces small, yellow, star-shaped flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical with a smooth margin.

The fruit is a small, yellow, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.
