Platyopuntia atroglobosa

Platyopuntia atroglobosa, commonly known as the dark globular cactus, is a species of flowering plant in the cactus family, Cactaceae.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its globular shape and dark green to grayish-blue stems. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its textured foliage. It is well adapted to arid environments, with spines that provide protection and reduce water loss.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry climates.
