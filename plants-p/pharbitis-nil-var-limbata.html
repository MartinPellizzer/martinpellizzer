Pharbitis nil var. limbata

Pharbitis nil var. limbata, commonly known as the scarlet runner bean, is a climbing vine native to tropical America.

It produces vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by elongated pods containing edible seeds. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vigorous growth habit, often reaching several meters in length. It thrives in warm climates and requires full sun for optimal growth.

The variety 'limbata' is distinguished by its distinctive leaf shape and striking flower color.
