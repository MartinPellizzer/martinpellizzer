Polygonum liaotungense

Polygonum liaotungense, commonly known as the Liaotung knotweed, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes species known for their distinctive bracts and flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

It produces small, clustered flowers that are usually green or reddish in color, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
