Pellinia chilensis

Pellinia chilensis, commonly known as the Chilean Pellinia, is a perennial herb native to South America, particularly Chile.

Its flowers are small and clustered in umbels, a common feature among members of the Apiaceae family.

Pellinia chilensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
