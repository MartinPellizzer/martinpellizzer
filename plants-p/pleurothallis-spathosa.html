Pleurothallis spathosa

Pleurothallis spathosa, commonly known as the spathose pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments within the rainforest canopy. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. Its growth habit is typically compact, with multiple flowering shoots emerging from the base.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike.
