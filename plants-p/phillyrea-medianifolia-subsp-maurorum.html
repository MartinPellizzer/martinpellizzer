Phillyrea medianifolia subsp. maurorum

Phillyrea medianifolia subsp.

maurorum, commonly known as the Algerian Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green.

The plant produces white, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, often in spring. Its fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds.

This species is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous areas.
