Peperomia attenuata var. taveuniana

Peperomia attenuata var. taveuniana, commonly known as the Taveuniana Peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Madagascar.

It features dense, fleshy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
