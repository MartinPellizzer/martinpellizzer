Pleurothallis gyas

Pleurothallis gyas, commonly known as the golden pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and white, often with intricate patterns. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrates.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
