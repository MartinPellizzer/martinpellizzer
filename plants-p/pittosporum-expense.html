Pittosporum expense

Pittosporum expense, commonly known as the coastal pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a slightly serrated margin. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, orange-red berries. It thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy or rocky soils.

Pittosporum expense is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
