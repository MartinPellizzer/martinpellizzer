Polygala arillata var. angustifolia

Polygala arillata var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

It is often found in coastal and inland regions where it thrives in well-drained conditions.
