Porana triserialis var. lasia

Porana triserialis var. lasia, commonly known as the Lasian porana, is a rare and ornamental plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, lance-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly valued by botanists and horticulturists alike.
