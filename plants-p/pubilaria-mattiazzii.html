Pubilaria mattiazzii

Pubilaria mattiazzii, commonly known as the Alpine bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of bell-shaped blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Pubilaria mattiazzii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in harsh mountain environments.
