Phoebe baishyae

Phoebe baishyae, commonly known as the Bishan phoebe, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern China, particularly in Yunnan Province. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are typically elliptical and have a glossy surface, while its bark is smooth and dark in color.

Phoebe baishyae is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
