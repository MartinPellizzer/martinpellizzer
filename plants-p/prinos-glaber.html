Prinos glaber

Prinos glaber, commonly known as the smooth prinos, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems and small, elliptical leaves that are smooth and often glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer. Its fruit is a capsule containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Prinos glaber is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid conditions.
