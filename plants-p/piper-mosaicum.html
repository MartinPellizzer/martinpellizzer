Piper mosaicum

Piper mosaicum, commonly known as the mosaic pepper or black pepper plant, is a flowering vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and aromatic leaves. The plant is cultivated primarily for its fruit, which is used to produce the spice black pepper. Its leaves are also used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
