Panicum scoparium var. minus

Panicum scoparium var. minus, commonly known as little bluestem, is a low-growing grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and tufted inflorescences that turn golden in the fall. This variety is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for native plant gardens. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.

Its ornamental value and ecological benefits make it a valuable addition to sustainable landscaping.
