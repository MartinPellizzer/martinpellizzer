Phyllanthus nyassae

Phyllanthus nyassae, commonly known as African date palm, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a small tree or shrub that can grow up to 5 meters tall, characterized by its pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in spikes, and it is known for its edible fruits, which are similar in appearance to dates.

Phyllanthus nyassae is also valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
