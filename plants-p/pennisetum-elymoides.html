Pennisetum elymoides

Pennisetum elymoides, commonly known as fountain grass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted clumps of slender, arching leaves that give it a fountain-like appearance. The plant produces fluffy, pinkish-white seed heads that are highly ornamental and attract birds and insects. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Pennisetum elymoides is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to provide erosion control in landscaping.
