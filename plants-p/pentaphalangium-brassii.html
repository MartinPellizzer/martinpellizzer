Pentaphalangium brassii

Pentaphalangium brassii, commonly known as the silver spider plant, is a strikingly beautiful plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching stems that bear clusters of small, white, spider-like flowers. The plant has a unique growth habit, often forming dense, cascading foliage that makes it ideal for hanging baskets or as a ground cover. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a silvery-blue hue that adds a striking visual contrast to its surroundings.

It is relatively easy to care for, thriving in well-drained soil and bright, indirect light.
