Platanthera shensiana

Platanthera shensiana, commonly known as the Shensian twayblade, is a rare orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wet meadows. It has a symbiotic relationship with specific mycorrhizal fungi, which are essential for its nutrient uptake.
