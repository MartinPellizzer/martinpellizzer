Pavonia praemorsa

Pavonia praemorsa, commonly known as the scarlet pavonia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces vibrant red to purple flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Pavonia praemorsa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
