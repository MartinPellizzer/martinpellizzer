Phaleria neumannii

Phaleria neumannii, commonly known as the Neumann's starfruit tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and small, fragrant flowers. The tree produces yellow, star-shaped fruits that are edible and resemble the common starfruit. Its leaves are typically elliptical with a smooth surface and a slightly glossy appearance.

The plant is often cultivated for both ornamental purposes and its fruit, which is used in culinary applications.
