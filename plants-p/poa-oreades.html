Poa oreades

Poa oreades, commonly known as mountain grass or alpine bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences with blue-green florets. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.

Poa oreades plays an important role in stabilizing soil in rocky and high-altitude areas.
