Piper vitaceum var. airaoense

Piper vitaceum var. airaoense, commonly known as the Airao pepper, is a climbing shrub native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its dark green, heart-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, yellowish fruits that are edible and have a spicy, peppery flavor. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in dense understory vegetation.

Due to its unique flavor and potential medicinal properties, it is of interest to botanists and food scientists alike.
