Poikilacanthus macranthus

Poikilacanthus macranthus, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and a woody stem that can grow quite tall. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas or near rivers.

Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
