Parsonsia capsularis var. rosea

Parsonsia capsularis var. rosea, commonly known as the pink lantern plant, is a flowering species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, typical of the萝藦科 (Asclepiadaceae) family. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded or semi-shaded environments.
