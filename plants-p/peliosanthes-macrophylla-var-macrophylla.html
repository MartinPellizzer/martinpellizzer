Peliosanthes macrophylla var. macrophylla

Peliosanthes macrophylla var. macrophylla, commonly known as the broad-leaved peliosanthes, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
