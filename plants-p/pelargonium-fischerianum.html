Pelargonium fischerianum

Pelargonium fischerianum, commonly known as the Fischer's geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clustered, showy flowers. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces vibrant pink or red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value and adaptability contribute to its popularity among gardeners.
