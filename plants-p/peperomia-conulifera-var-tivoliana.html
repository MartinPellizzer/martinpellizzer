Peperomia conulifera var. tivoliana

Peperomia conulifera var. tivoliana, commonly known as the Tivolian Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container cultivation.
