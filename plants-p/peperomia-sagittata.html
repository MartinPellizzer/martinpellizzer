Peperomia sagittata

Peperomia sagittata, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often white or greenish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia sagittata is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
