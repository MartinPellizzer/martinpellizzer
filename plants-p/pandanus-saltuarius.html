Pandanus saltuarius

Pandanus saltuarius, commonly known as the saltbush pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Pandanus.

It is native to the Pacific Islands and is characterized by its tall, slender stem and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are typically yellow or green in color. It thrives in coastal and saline environments, making it well adapted to coastal ecosystems.

Its unique morphology and ecological niche make it an important component of its native habitat.
