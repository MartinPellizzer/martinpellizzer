Premna oblongata var. subglabra

Premna oblongata var. subglabra, commonly known as the smooth-leafed glorybower, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elliptical, smooth-textured leaves that are often glabrous or nearly so. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale pink flowers during the summer months. Its adaptability to various soil types makes it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

It is also valued for its use in traditional medicine in some regions.
