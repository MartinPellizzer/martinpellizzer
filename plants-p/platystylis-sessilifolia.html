Platystylis sessilifolia

Platystylis sessilifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed star-flower, is a tropical plant native to parts of Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant features narrow, strap-like leaves and produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
