Phillyrea angustifolia var. stenophylla

Phillyrea angustifolia var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, winged seeds.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in rocky or well-drained soils.
