Phreatia kanehirae

Phreatia kanehirae, commonly known as the Kanehira's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pale green to yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Phreatia kanehirae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
