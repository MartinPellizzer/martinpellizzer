Pelargonium × porphyreon

Pelargonium × porphyreon, commonly known as the Porphyreon geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to purple, often with contrasting white or pink markings. The plant typically features upright stems and lobed, serrated leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Pelargonium × porphyreon is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
