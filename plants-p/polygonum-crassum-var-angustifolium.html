Polygonum crassum var. angustifolium

Polygonum crassum var. angustifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaf smartweed, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It typically grows in moist soils along stream banks, wet meadows, and other wetland habitats. The plant features upright stems that are usually reddish in color, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a smooth margin. The small, clustered flowers are greenish-white and appear in late summer to early fall.

It is often found in disturbed areas and can form dense colonies, contributing to soil stabilization.
