Peperomia parcicilia

Peperomia parcicilia, commonly known as the compact peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to Brazil.

It is a succulent plant characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Peperomia parcicilia is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
