Pegaeophyton sinense var. stenophyllum

Pegaeophyton sinense var. stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of China.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in maritime environments. It has a compact growth habit and is adapted to withstand salt spray and strong winds.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
