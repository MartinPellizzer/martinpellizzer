Pilosella piloselloides subsp. praealta

Pilosella piloselloides subsp.

praealta, commonly known as the tall hairgrass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands, rocky slopes, and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow, hairy, and often bluish-green in appearance.

The subspecies praealta is distinguished by its taller stature and more robust flowering structure compared to other subspecies of Pilosella.
