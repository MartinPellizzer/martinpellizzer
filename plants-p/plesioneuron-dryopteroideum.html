Plesioneuron dryopteroideum

Plesioneuron dryopteroideum, commonly known as the silver hair fern, is a species of fern characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and Africa. The plant features a creeping rhizome with clusters of small, spore-producing sorus on the underside of its leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and horticultural collections.
