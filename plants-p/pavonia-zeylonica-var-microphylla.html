Pavonia zeylonica var. microphylla

Pavonia zeylonica var. microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved flamebush, is a flowering plant native to Sri Lanka.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant red to orange flowers that bloom in terminal clusters. The leaves are small, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, giving the plant a soft, textured appearance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
