Phyllanthus koumacensis var. brevitepalus

Phyllanthus koumacensis var. brevitepalus, commonly known as the short-palm phyllanthus, is a shrub native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests. Its leaves are usually elliptical with a smooth margin, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.
