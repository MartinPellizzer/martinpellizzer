Poa hansenii

Poa hansenii, commonly known as the Hansen's bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and forms dense, low-growing sod. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. Its flowering stems bear small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny flowers.

Poa hansenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its hardy nature and aesthetic qualities.
