Persicaria yokusaiana var. stenophylla

Persicaria yokusaiana var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its distinctive nodes and often hairy stems. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

This variant is notable for its compact growth habit and ornamental value in garden settings.
