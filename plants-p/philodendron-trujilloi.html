Philodendron trujilloi

Philodendron trujilloi, commonly known as the Trujillo's philodendron, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that often exhibit a vibrant green color with subtle variegation. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, known for its unique inflorescence structure consisting of a spadix and spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appeal.
