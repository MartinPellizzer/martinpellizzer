Pittosporum viridiflorum subsp. feddeanum

Pittosporum viridiflorum subsp.

feddeanum, commonly known as the Feddeanum Pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand. It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture.

The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by edible, orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
