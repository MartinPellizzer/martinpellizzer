Peperomia metapalcoensis

Peperomia metapalcoensis, commonly known as the Mexican peperomia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and glossy, dark green foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. Peperomia metapalcoensis is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and air-purifying qualities.
