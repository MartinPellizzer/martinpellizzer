Pelargonium × luculentum

Pelargonium × luculentum, commonly known as the regal geranium, is a hybrid species within the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red, with prominent, often frilly petals. The plant features velvety, dark green leaves that are usually heart-shaped or ovate in form. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Pelargonium × luculentum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.
