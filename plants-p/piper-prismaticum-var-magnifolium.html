Piper prismaticum var. magnifolium

Piper prismaticum var. magnifolium, commonly known as the rainbow pepper plant, is a striking tropical species native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which can display shades of red, orange, and yellow, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale green, followed by bright red berries. It thrives in warm, humid conditions with indirect sunlight and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in both indoor and outdoor settings.
