Potentilla Assurgens Subsp. Thuringiaca

Potentilla assurgens subsp. thuringiaca is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to central Europe. It belongs to the rose family and is known for its small, yellow blooms that appear in early summer. This subspecies thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its unique characteristics make it a sought-after plant for botanists and gardeners alike. Due to habitat loss, it is now considered endangered and requires careful conservation efforts.
