Psilochilus minutifolius

Psilochilus minutifolius, commonly known as the small-leaved psilochilus, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its delicate, linear leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. Native to the cloud forests of Central and South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant has a short rhizome and produces numerous tiny pseudobulbs that support its growth.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
