Pterostylis celans

Pterostylis celans, commonly known as the golden helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, helmet-shaped flower that is typically golden in color. The plant has a slender, leafless stem that supports the single or clustered inflorescence. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil in its natural habitat.

Pterostylis celans thrives in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys.
