Parnassia europaea

Parnassia europaea, commonly known as the European white water-plantain, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Ranunculaceae and is characterized by its delicate, white, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils, often near streams or in wet meadows. Its leaves are palmately divided, with a waxy coating that helps reduce water loss.

Parnassia europaea is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
