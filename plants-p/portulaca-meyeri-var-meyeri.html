Portulaca meyeri var. meyeri

Portulaca meyeri var. meyeri, commonly known as Meyer's moss rose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming species with fleshy, succulent leaves that help it retain moisture in arid conditions. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, yellow, and orange, which bloom throughout the summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its compact growth habit and long blooming period make it a valuable addition to drought-tolerant landscapes.
