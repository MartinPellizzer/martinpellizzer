Pomaderris andromedifolia subsp. confusa

Pomaderris andromedifolia subsp.

confusa, commonly known as the Grey Pomaderris, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia. It is characterized by its small, oval, grey-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. It is typically found in heathlands and open forests, often growing on sandy or rocky soils.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and attractive foliage.
