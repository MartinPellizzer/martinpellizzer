Panicum leptostachyum

Panicum leptostachyum, commonly known as slender-stalked panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in open areas, wetlands, and disturbed soils, exhibiting a clump-forming habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces fluffy, pale green to brownish inflorescences. It is adapted to high light conditions and has a shallow root system.

Its botanical characteristics make it suitable for erosion control and ornamental use in certain landscapes.
