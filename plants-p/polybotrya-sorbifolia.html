Polybotrya sorbifolia

Polybotrya sorbifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves and daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with composite flower heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
