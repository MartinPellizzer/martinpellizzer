Pyracantha koidzumii

Pyracantha koidzumii, commonly known as the Japanese firethorn, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers that appear in spring. The plant produces red, berry-like fruits in the fall, which are attractive to birds. Its leaves are dark green and oval-shaped, providing dense foliage throughout the growing season.

Pyracantha koidzumii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant seasonal display and hardy nature.
