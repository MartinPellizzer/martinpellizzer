Ptychostigma saxifragum

Ptychostigma saxifragum, commonly known as the saxifrage, is a flowering plant belonging to the Crassulaceae family.

It is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and small, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. Native to rocky and alpine environments, this plant is well adapted to harsh, arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, often growing in crevices or on rock surfaces.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
