Phaseolus caracalla var. brachystachys

Phaseolus caracalla var. brachystachys, commonly known as the dwarf scarlet runner bean, is a variety of the scarlet runner bean plant.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant red flowers. The plant produces edible pods that are similar to those of other Phaseolus species but are typically smaller in size. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for both its ornamental value and edible yield.

This variety is well-suited for container gardening due to its manageable size.
