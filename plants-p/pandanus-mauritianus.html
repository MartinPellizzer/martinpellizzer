Pandanus mauritianus

Pandanus mauritianus, commonly known as the Mauritius pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the island of Mauritius.

It is a member of the Pandanaceae family and is characterized by its stiff, sword-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in spike-like inflorescences. Its roots and leaves are sometimes utilized in local cuisines and medicinal practices.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in coastal environments.
