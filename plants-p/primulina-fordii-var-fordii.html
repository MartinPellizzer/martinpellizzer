Primulina fordii var. fordii

Primulina fordii var. fordii, commonly known as the Ford's primulina, is a species native to China.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in mountainous regions.
