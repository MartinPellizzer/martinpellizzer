Panicum myurum

Panicum myurum, commonly known as the red panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a perennial grass that typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands. The plant produces tall, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves, and it blooms with inflorescences that are usually pink to red in color. It is adapted to arid conditions and has mechanisms to conserve water, making it resilient in harsh environments.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
