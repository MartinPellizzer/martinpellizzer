Phlomis likiangensis

Phlomis likiangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan mullein, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features tall, erect stems covered in soft, woolly hairs, and it produces showy, yellowish-green flower clusters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a fuzzy texture.

It thrives in rocky or dry habitats and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
