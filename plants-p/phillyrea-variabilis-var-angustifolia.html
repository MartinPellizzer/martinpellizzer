Phillyrea variabilis var. angustifolia

Phillyrea variabilis var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf Phillyrea, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, dark green leaves that are typically arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by spherical, woody fruits. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions.

Phillyrea variabilis var. angustifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in coastal and arid environments.
