Peperomia flavescens var. markhamii

Peperomia flavescens var. markhamii, commonly known as the Markham's peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green oval leaves with a slightly waxy texture, and its stems are often reddish or purple in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in hue. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care, it is widely cultivated as a decorative plant.
