Phyllostachys heterocycla f. tubiformis

Phyllostachys heterocycla f. tubiformis, commonly known as the bamboo species with tubular leaves, is a variant of the giant bamboo genus Phyllostachys.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated, and often tubular-shaped leaves that distinguish it from other bamboo varieties. This plant is native to East Asia and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. Its growth habit is typically clumping, forming dense stands that can reach significant heights.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its aesthetic appeal.
