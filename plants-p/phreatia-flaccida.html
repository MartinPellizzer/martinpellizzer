Phreatia flaccida

Phreatia flaccida, commonly known as the flaccid orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its pseudobulbs and linear leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Phreatia flaccida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts worldwide.
