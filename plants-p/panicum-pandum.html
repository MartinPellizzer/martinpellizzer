Panicum pandum

Panicum pandum, commonly known as the red panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and small, reddish-brown spikelets that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.

Panicum pandum is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its visual appeal and low maintenance requirements.
