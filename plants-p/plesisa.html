Plesisa

Plesisa, commonly known as the bluebell or fairy lantern, is a flowering plant belonging to the nightshade family.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in woodland areas and thrives in partial shade. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Plesisa is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
