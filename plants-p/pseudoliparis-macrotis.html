It belongs to the order Pennatulacea and is characterized by its long, flexible polyp-like structures. This species thrives in cold, dark waters at depths exceeding 200 meters, where it relies on filter feeding. Its body is composed of a central axial skeleton surrounded by a colony of polyps.
