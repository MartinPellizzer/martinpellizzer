Peperomia obtusifolia f. grandifolia

Peperomia obtusifolia f. grandifolia, commonly known as the Grandifolia Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
