Physurus micranthus

Physurus micranthus, commonly known as the small-flowered burrweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and bristly seed pods. Physurus micranthus is often found in arid or semi-arid regions and is adapted to survive in harsh, dry climates.
