Poa mnioides

Poa mnioides, commonly known as the slender bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green blades and tufted growth habit. This plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wet meadows and along stream banks. Poa mnioides has small, spike-like inflorescences that produce tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and forage for various wildlife species.
