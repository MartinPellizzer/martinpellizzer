Poa smithii

Poa smithii, commonly known as Smith's bluegrass, is a perennial grass native to western North America.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Poa smithii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
