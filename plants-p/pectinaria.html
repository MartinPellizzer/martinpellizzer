Pectinaria

Pectinaria, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple ray florets and is native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean. The plant thrives in saline and sandy soils, often found in maritime environments. Pectinaria species are typically herbaceous perennials with a rosette growth habit.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for coastal gardens and ecological restoration projects.
