Phymatodes thouarsii

Phymatodes thouarsii, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Madagascar.

It features a pseudobulb with a single, leathery leaf and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its unique morphology and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for tropical gardens and greenhouses.

It plays a vital role in the biodiversity of its native habitat.
