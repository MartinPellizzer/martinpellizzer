Panicum insulare var. tenerrimum

Panicum insulare var. tenerrimum, commonly known as the slender panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The inflorescence consists of a panicle made up of small, spikelet-like flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
