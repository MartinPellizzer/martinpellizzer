Peperomia plurispica

Peperomia plurispica, commonly known as the clustered pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense clusters of small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color, which appear in the axils of the leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate moisture.

Peperomia plurispica is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
