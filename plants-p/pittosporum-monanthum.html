Pittosporum monanthum

Pittosporum monanthum, commonly known as the single-flower pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 meters. The plant features aromatic leaves that are dark green and glossy, with a leathery texture. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in dense inflorescences, blooming in spring.

Pittosporum monanthum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and fragrant blooms.
