Pyrrhocactus truncatipetalus

Pyrrhocactus truncatipetalus, commonly known as the truncated petal cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, spineless cactus with a compact, globular shape. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a vibrant touch to its appearance. Its leaves are typically reduced to small, scale-like structures, a common adaptation in arid environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in succulent gardens.
