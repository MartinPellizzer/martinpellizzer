Platysace tenuissima

Platysace tenuissima, commonly known as the slender stellaria, is a small, herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. The plant features delicate, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, white or pinkish flowers clustered in umbels. Its flowering period occurs during the warmer months, and it is often found in lowland tropical regions.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
