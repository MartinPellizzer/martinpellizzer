Plantago rigens

Plantago rigens, commonly known as rigid plantain, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features erect, slender stems that bear lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive waxy coating. The plant produces small, greenish flowers in dense spikes, which are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and wetlands.

Its leaves are used in traditional medicine for their soothing and anti-inflammatory properties.
