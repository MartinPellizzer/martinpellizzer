Pilosella megargyrocoma

Pilosella megargyrocoma, commonly known as the narrowleaf pilosella, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers with yellow disc florets surrounded by white to pale blue ray florets. Pilosella megargyrocoma is native to regions of Europe and western Asia, where it thrives in temperate climates.
