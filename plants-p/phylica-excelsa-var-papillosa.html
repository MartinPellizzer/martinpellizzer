Phylica excelsa var. papillosa

Phylica excelsa var. papillosa, commonly known as the silky bristled phylica, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces distinctive, feathery bracts that give it a fluffy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique visual characteristics.
