Polypodium martensii

Polypodium martensii, commonly known as the Martens' fern, is a species of polypodiaceae found in temperate regions.

It is a small, evergreen fern with a rosette-like arrangement of fronds. The plant typically grows on rocky substrates or in shaded, moist environments. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture.

Polypodium martensii is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.
