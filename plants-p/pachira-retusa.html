It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its woody, cylindrical stems that are often reddish in color, and its large, heart-shaped leaves. Pachira retusa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
