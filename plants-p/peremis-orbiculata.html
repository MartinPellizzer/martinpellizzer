Peremis orbiculata

Peremis orbiculata, commonly known as the African snowball, is a shrub native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid regions.

Peremis orbiculata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
