Pterocymbium laoticum

Pterocymbium laoticum, commonly known as the Laotian orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions with rich, well-drained soil. Its botanical features include slender stems, lance-shaped leaves, and a single, elongated flower spike.
