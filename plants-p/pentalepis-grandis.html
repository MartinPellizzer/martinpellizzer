Pentalepis grandis

Pentalepis grandis, commonly known as the giant feather grass, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, feathery plumes that give it a dramatic appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid climates.

Its unique foliage and architectural form make it a popular choice for landscaping and garden design.
