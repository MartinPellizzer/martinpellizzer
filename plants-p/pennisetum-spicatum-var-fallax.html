Pennisetum spicatum var. fallax

Pennisetum spicatum var. fallax, commonly known as fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted, fountain-like growth habit and slender, silver-green leaves. The plant produces fluffy, white plumes that give it a striking appearance, especially in late summer and autumn. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements further enhance its appeal in garden settings.
