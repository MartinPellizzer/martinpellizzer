Persicaria ruderalis f. angustifolia

Persicaria ruderalis f. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture. This plant thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in roadsides, fields, and waste areas.

It is often considered a weed but has ornamental value due to its delicate appearance.
