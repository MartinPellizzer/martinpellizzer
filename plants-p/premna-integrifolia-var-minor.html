Premna integrifolia var. minor

Premna integrifolia var. minor, commonly known as the small-leaf glorybower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.
