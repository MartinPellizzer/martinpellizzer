Potentilla Anserina Var. Lanata

Potentilla anserina var. lanata is a unique variant of the silverweed plant known for its fuzzy, woolly leaves. Native to alpine regions, it thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. This plant is often found in mountainous areas where it adapts to harsh environmental conditions. Its distinctive appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to flourish.
