Pleioblastus cucphuongensis

Pleioblastus cucphuongensis, commonly known as the Cuc Phuong bamboo, is a species native to Vietnam.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect culms that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a smooth, grassy texture. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
