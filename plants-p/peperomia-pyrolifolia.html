Peperomia pyrolifolia

Peperomia pyrolifolia, commonly known as the flame peperomia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub characterized by its glossy, ovate leaves that are often reddish or bronze in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, which are followed by distinctive, lantern-like seed pods. Peperomia pyrolifolia thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive foliage.
