Pothuava nudicaulis var. cuspidata

Pothuava nudicaulis var. cuspidata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pothuava, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and clusters of tiny, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The species is valued for both its ornamental appeal and its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
