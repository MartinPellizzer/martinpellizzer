Patrinia monandra var. sinensis

Patrinia monandra var. sinensis, commonly known as Chinese Patrinia, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions.

The species is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional Chinese medicine.
