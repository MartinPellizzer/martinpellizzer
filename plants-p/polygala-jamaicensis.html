Polygala jamaicensis

Polygala jamaicensis, commonly known as the Jamaican milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly Jamaica.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, often pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks. Its leaves are simple and opposite, with a smooth texture, while its stems are often hairy.

Polygala jamaicensis plays a role in local ecosystems, providing nectar for pollinators and supporting biodiversity.
