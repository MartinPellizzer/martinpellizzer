Peperomia montium

Peperomia montium, commonly known as the Montium Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, often with a bluish or silver hue. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green or red, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia montium is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
