Plantago Media Subsp. Stepposa

Plantago media subsp. stepposa is a lesser-known member of the Plantaginaceae family that thrives in dry, open habitats. This subspecies is often found in steppes and grasslands across central and eastern Europe. It is characterized by its compact growth and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in supporting local ecosystems. Its resilience to harsh conditions makes it a valuable plant for ecological studies and conservation efforts.
