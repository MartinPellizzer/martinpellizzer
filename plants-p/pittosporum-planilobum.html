Pittosporum planilobum

Pittosporum planilobum, commonly known as the New Zealand pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces fragrant white flowers in clusters, which bloom in spring and attract pollinators. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Pittosporum planilobum is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
