Portulaca grandiflora var. boliviensis

Portulaca grandiflora var. boliviensis, commonly known as the Bolivian moss rose, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a low-growing, succulent plant with fleshy, thick stems and leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom throughout the summer. It prefers full sun and well-drained soil, making it an excellent choice for rock gardens or containers.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and drought-tolerant, suitable for xeriscaping.
