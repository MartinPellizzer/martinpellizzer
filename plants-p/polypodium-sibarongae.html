Polypodium sibarongae

Polypodium sibarongae, commonly known as the Sibarong fern, is a species of polypodioid fern native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically bipinnate, with a light green coloration. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests. Its rhizomes are relatively short and produce a cluster of small, lanceolate leaves.

Polypodium sibarongae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
