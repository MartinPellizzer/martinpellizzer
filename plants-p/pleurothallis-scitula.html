Pleurothallis scitula

Pleurothallis scitula, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is a small epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant has delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces tiny, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It is part of the Orchidaceae family and is appreciated for its ornamental value.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments to ensure its survival.
