Peperomia canalensis

Peperomia canalensis, commonly known as the Canalensis Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia canalensis is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
