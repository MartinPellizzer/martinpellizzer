It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including India, China, and Southeast Asia. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a waxy texture, while the flowers are small and clustered in cyathia, a characteristic feature of the Euphorbiaceae family.
