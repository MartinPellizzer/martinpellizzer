Portulaca macrosperma

Portulaca macrosperma, commonly known as the large-seeded purslane, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a succulent species characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves and vibrant yellow flowers. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to drought conditions. Its succulent leaves store water, enabling it to survive in hot, dry climates.

The flowers typically bloom in the morning and close by midday, a trait known as nyctinasty.
