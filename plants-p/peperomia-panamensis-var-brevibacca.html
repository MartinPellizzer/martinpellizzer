Peperomia panamensis var. brevibacca

Peperomia panamensis var. brevibacca, commonly known as the Panama peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Central America.

It features broad, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly textured surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and white, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and ease of care make it a desirable addition to both novice and experienced plant collectors.
