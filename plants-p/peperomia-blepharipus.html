Peperomia blepharipus

Peperomia blepharipus, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal water needs.

Peperomia blepharipus is valued for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for plant enthusiasts.
