Piper macrostachyum

Piper macrostachyum, commonly known as the giant sarsaparilla, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruits are berries that mature to a bright red color, containing several seeds.

Piper macrostachyum is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
