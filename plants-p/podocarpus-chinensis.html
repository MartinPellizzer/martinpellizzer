Podocarpus chinensis

Podocarpus chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese podocarp, is a species of coniferous evergreen tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the family Podocarpaceae and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces both male and female cones, with the female cones developing into woody, edible seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in mountainous regions with well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is cultivated in various horticultural settings.
