Panicum guatemalense

Panicum guatemalense, commonly known as Guatemalan panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a bluish-green hue.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
