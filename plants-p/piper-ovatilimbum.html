Piper ovatilimbum

Piper ovatilimbum, commonly known as the broad-leaf pepper or African pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers that develop into berries. Its fruit is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
