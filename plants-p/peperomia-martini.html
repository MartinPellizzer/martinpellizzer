Peperomia martini

Peperomia martini, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery or grayish pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia martini is relatively low-maintenance and resistant to pests, making it an ideal plant for beginners.
