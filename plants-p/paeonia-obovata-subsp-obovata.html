Paeonia obovata subsp. obovata

Paeonia obovata subsp.

obovata, commonly known as the Chinese peony, is a herbaceous perennial native to China. It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to white and red.

The plant has palmately divided leaves and produces woody underground rhizomes. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, preferring cool climates.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental beauty and is widely cultivated in gardens.
