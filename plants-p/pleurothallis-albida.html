Pleurothallis albida

Pleurothallis albida, commonly known as the white pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. The plant grows as an epiphyte, often found on trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

Pleurothallis albida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
