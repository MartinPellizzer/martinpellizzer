Parinari excelsa

Parinari excelsa, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a large evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and dense, glossy green leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by spherical, red fruits containing several seeds. It is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often found in moist lowland forests.

Parinari excelsa plays an important ecological role in supporting local wildlife and maintaining forest biodiversity.
