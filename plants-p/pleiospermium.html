Pleiospermium

Pleiospermium, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Euphorbiaceae.

The flowers are typically small and clustered in cup-like structures called cyathia, which is a defining feature of the Euphorbiaceae family. Pleiospermium species are native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.
