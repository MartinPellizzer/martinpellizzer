Poa compressa var. expansa

Poa compressa var. expansa, commonly known as the broadleaf bluegrass, is a variant of the common bluegrass species.

It is characterized by its broader, more robust leaves compared to the typical form of Poa compressa. This plant is native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and turfgrass applications.
