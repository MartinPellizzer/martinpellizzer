Potentilla rouyana

Potentilla rouyana, commonly known as the Chinese fireweed, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a hairy texture.

Potentilla rouyana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
