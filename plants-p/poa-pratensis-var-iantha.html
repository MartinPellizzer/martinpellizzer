Poa pratensis var. iantha

Poa pratensis var. iantha, commonly known as the blue meadowgrass, is a variant of the common meadowgrass species.

It is characterized by its distinctive blue-green foliage, which gives it a unique visual appearance compared to other grasses. This plant is typically found in moist, temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often used in landscaping and turfgrass management due to its hardy nature and aesthetic appeal.

The varietal form is notable for its dense growth habit and ability to form a fine, uniform turf.
