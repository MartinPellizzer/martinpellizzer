Potentilla chenteica

Potentilla chenteica, commonly known as the Chinese cinquefoil, is a perennial shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its small, showy yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has compound leaves with five leaflets, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Potentilla chenteica is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine.
