Pelargonium × flammeum

Pelargonium × flammeum, commonly known as the flame geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from red to orange, often with contrasting yellow centers. The plant typically has rounded, velvety leaves that are dark green in color. Native to South Africa, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

It is widely cultivated as an ornamental plant for its colorful blooms and adaptability to various climates.
