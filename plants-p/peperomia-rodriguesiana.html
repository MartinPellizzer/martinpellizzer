Peperomia rodriguesiana

Peperomia rodriguesiana, commonly known as Rodrigues peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It features dense, glossy, dark green foliage with a velvety texture, and its leaves are typically oval to elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, which are followed by small, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance make it a desirable ornamental plant.
