Polygala britteniana

Polygala britteniana, commonly known as the Britten's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae, which is characterized by its distinctive floral structures. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing adaptations to harsh climates.
