Prunus cerasifera f. subglobosa-putaminata

Prunus cerasifera f. subglobosa-putaminata, commonly known as the European cherry plum or subglobosa-putaminata form, is a compact, rounded shrub or small tree with dense, rounded growth habit.

It features small, oval leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers in spring, followed by small, yellow or red fruits. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its compact size and seasonal interest.

This cultivar is relatively hardy and adaptable to a variety of soil types.
