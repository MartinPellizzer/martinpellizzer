Pyracantha pyracantha f. lalandei

Pyracantha pyracantha f. lalandei, commonly known as the Laland's firethorn, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to southern Europe.

It features showy, orange-red berries that persist through winter, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has dense, thorny branches and dark green, glossy foliage that provides year-round visual interest. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for hedging or container gardening.

This cultivar is valued for its hardiness and ability to attract birds with its fruit.
