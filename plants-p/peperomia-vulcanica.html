Peperomia vulcanica

Peperomia vulcanica, commonly known as the Fire Peperomia, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and vibrant, often red or orange-bracted inflorescences. The plant features thick, waxy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, providing a striking visual appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia vulcanica is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and occasional fertilization to maintain its health and bloom.
