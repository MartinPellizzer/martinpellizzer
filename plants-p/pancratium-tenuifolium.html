Pancratium tenuifolium

Pancratium tenuifolium, commonly known as the slender-leaf yellow daffodil, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and a single, showy yellow flower with a distinctive orange corona. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in coastal areas or sandy habitats. Its flowers bloom in late winter to early spring, adding a splash of color to the landscape.

Pancratium tenuifolium is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
