Pandanus brevicornutus

Pandanus brevicornutus, commonly known as the short-horned pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pandanaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its robust, fibrous leaves and a short, stout stem. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a large, leaf-like bract.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional crafts due to its durable leaves.
