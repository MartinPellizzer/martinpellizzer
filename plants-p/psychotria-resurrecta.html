Psychotria resurrecta

Psychotria resurrecta, commonly known as the resurrection plant, is a member of the Rubiaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves and small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers. This unique adaptation has earned it the common name "resurrection plant.".
