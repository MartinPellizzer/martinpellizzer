Paphiopedilum malipoense var. hiepii

Paphiopedilum malipoense var. hiepii, commonly known as the Malipo hiepii orchid, is a rare and unique species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, intricate floral structure. The plant features a tall, slender stem with a single, large, showy flower that has a distinctive lip and a vibrant color palette. It thrives in cool, humid environments with partial shade, making it a challenging but rewarding plant for cultivation.

Its botanical beauty and rarity make it a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
