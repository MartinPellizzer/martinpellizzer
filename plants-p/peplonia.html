Peplonia

Peplonia, commonly known as the peplonia plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is native to regions of southern Africa, particularly in the Western Cape province. The plant typically features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. Peplonia species are often found in rocky or sandy soils and are adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

They are valued for their ornamental appeal and are sometimes cultivated in gardens for their vibrant blooms.
