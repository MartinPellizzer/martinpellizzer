Panicum perrottetii

Panicum perrottetii, commonly known as the silver panicum, is a species of grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and upright growth habit, making it a striking ornamental plant. The plant produces fluffy, seed-bearing panicles that add visual interest throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in open woodlands and prairies.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
