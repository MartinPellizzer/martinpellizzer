Pilosella heteromelana subsp. neofidalgoana

Pilosella heteromelana subsp.

neofidalgoana, commonly known as the Neofidalgoan hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit with densely packed, hairy stems.

The species produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clustered inflorescences, typical of the genus Pilosella. It is native to specific regions in North America, particularly in the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

This subspecies is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
