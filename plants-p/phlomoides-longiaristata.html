Phlomoides longiaristata

Phlomoides longiaristata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved phlomoides, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open, sandy or rocky soils. The plant features slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense spikes, often displaying shades of pink or purple.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
