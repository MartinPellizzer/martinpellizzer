Pterogyne nitens f. parvifolia

Pterogyne nitens f. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved sand-verbena, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and sandy environments. The plant features small, elliptical leaves with a smooth surface and a pale green color. Its flowers are small, clustered, and usually pink or purple in color, blooming throughout the warmer months.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
