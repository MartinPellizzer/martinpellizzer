Peperomia stuebelii var. glabrata

Peperomia stuebelii var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth-leaved peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, often green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and bonsai cultivation.
