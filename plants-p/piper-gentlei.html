Piper gentlei

Piper gentlei, commonly known as the gentle pepper or the slender pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly found in the understory of humid forests. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant, and it produces small, peppery fruits.

Piper gentlei is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
