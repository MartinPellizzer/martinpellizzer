Potentilla thuringiaca var. nestleriana

Potentilla thuringiaca var. nestleriana, commonly known as the Thuringian cinquefoil, is a low-growing perennial shrub native to Central Europe.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. The plant has trifoliate leaves with serrated edges, and its stems are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
