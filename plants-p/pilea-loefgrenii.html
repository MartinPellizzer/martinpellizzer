Pilea loefgrenii

Pilea loefgrenii, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically light to dark green, often with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea loefgrenii is relatively low maintenance, requiring moderate watering and occasional pruning to maintain its compact form.
