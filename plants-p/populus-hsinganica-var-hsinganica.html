Populus hsinganica var. hsinganica

Populus hsinganica var. hsinganica, commonly known as the Hsianing poplar, is a deciduous tree native to northern China.

It belongs to the Salicaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and smooth, gray bark. The plant is adapted to temperate climates and thrives in well-drained soils. It produces small, yellowish flowers in early spring, followed by samaras for seed dispersal.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in its native habitat.
