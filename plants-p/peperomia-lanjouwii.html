Peperomia lanjouwii

Peperomia lanjouwii, commonly known as the Lanjouw peperomia, is a rare species native to Indonesia.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and thick, waxy leaves that vary in color from green to reddish hues. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is a sought-after plant among collectors.
