Picea pindrow

Picea pindrow, commonly known as the Pindrow spruce, is a coniferous tree native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its narrow, pyramidal shape and dark green, needle-like leaves. The tree produces small, cone-like structures that contain its seeds, typical of conifers. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

The bark of Picea pindrow is thick and fissured, providing protection against harsh weather conditions.
