Pittosporum aneityense

Pittosporum aneityense, commonly known as the New Zealand pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

Pittosporum aneityense is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitat.
