Persoonia muelleri subsp. muelleri

Persoonia muelleri subsp.

muelleri, commonly known as the Mueller's Persoonia, is a shrub native to Western Australia. It is characterized by its small, green, egg-shaped leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It is often found in coastal and inland regions, thriving in full sun.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
