Peperomia rotundifolia f. pubescens

Peperomia rotundifolia f. pubescens, commonly known as the velvety peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features rounded, waxy leaves with a distinctive velvety texture due to its dense pubescence. The leaves are typically a deep green, sometimes with a reddish tinge, and the plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive indoors with minimal care.
