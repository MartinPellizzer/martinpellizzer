Piper nigribaccum

Piper nigribaccum, commonly known as black pepper vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its dark, peppery fruits that are used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, oval berries that turn from green to black as they mature. Its leaves are typically broad and glossy, with a strong aromatic scent.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in herbal remedies.
