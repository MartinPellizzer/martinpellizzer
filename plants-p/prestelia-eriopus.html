Prestelia eriopus

Prestelia eriopus, commonly known as the silverleaf mistplant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Prestelia eriopus is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
