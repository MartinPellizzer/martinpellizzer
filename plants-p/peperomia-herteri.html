Peperomia herteri

Peperomia herteri, commonly known as the Herter's pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features dense, glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pale green flowers that appear in clusters during the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia herteri is known for its low maintenance requirements and ability to adapt well to various indoor environments.
