Pouteria Lucumifolia

Pouteria lucumifolia is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America. It is known for its unique, waxy leaves and distinctive fruit that resembles a small, green lantern. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of dense forests. Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice for horticultural enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the broader plant world.
