Peperomia rotundata f. glabrilimba

Peperomia rotundata f. glabrilimba, commonly known as the glossy peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a smooth, glossy surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which appear in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
