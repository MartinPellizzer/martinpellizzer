Polychilos cochlearis

Polychilos cochlearis, commonly known as the spiral lily, is a striking member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves and its showy, bell-shaped flowers. Polychilos cochlearis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil with adequate sunlight.
