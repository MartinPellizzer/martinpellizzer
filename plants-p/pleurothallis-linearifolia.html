Pleurothallis linearifolia

Pleurothallis linearifolia, commonly known as the slender-leaf orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small epiphytic orchid that typically grows on tree branches or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pink. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate floral structure.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often found in protected natural reserves.
