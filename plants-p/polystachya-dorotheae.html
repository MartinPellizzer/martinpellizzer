Polystachya dorotheae

Polystachya dorotheae, commonly known as the Dorothea's bamboo orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are typically long and narrow, with a smooth, waxy texture.

Polystachya dorotheae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated in horticultural collections for its unique appearance.
