Polygonum lapathifolium var. acuminatum

Polygonum lapathifolium var. acuminatum, commonly known as narrow-leaved smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia and North America.

It features erect stems that are often reddish in color and bear small, clustered flowers. The leaves are lanceolate, with a pointed tip, and are typically arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist soils, often found in wetlands, marshes, and along streambanks.

This plant is known for its adaptability and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
