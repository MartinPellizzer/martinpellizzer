Piqueria pilosa

Piqueria pilosa, commonly known as the hairy piqueria, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in arid regions. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Piqueria pilosa is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
