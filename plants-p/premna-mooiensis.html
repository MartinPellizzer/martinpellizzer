Premna mooiensis

Premna mooiensis, commonly known as the Natal premna, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants, and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color, arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal forests and riverine habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
