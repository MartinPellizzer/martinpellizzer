Polygala coursiereana

Polygala coursiereana, commonly known as the Madagascar milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to the arid conditions of its native environment. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.
