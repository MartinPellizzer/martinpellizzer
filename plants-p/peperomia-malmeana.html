Peperomia malmeana

Peperomia malmeana, commonly known as the Malme's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval to ovate in shape, with a glossy surface and a variety of colors ranging from deep green to variegated patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia malmeana is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
