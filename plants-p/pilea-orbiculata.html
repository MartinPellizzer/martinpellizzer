Pilea orbiculata

Pilea orbiculata, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its round, coin-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters. It is a popular indoor plant due to its ease of care and aesthetic appeal.

Pilea orbiculata thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
