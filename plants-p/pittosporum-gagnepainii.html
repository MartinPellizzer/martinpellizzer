Pittosporum gagnepainii

Pittosporum gagnepainii, commonly known as the Gagnepain's pittosporum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pittosporaceae.

It is native to New Zealand and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Pittosporum gagnepainii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
