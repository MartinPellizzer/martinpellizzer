Piper rodatzii

Piper rodatzii, commonly known as the Rottler's pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit and the presence of small, elliptical leaves with a slightly serrated margin. Its fruit is a small, dark purple drupe that contains a single seed.

Piper rodatzii is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic effects.
