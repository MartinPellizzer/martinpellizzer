Peperomia quimiriana

Peperomia quimiriana, commonly known as the Quimirian pepper plant, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub with thick, fleshy leaves that are often variegated in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in hue. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
