Peperomia oblongifolia

Peperomia oblongifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a compact, evergreen perennial with thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval or oblong in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish or reddish in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and drought tolerance.

Peperomia oblongifolia is often grown for its attractive foliage rather than its flowers.
