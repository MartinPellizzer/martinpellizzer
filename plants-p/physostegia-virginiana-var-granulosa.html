Physostegia virginiana var. granulosa

Physostegia virginiana var. granulosa, commonly known as the showy orchid.

This perennial herb is native to North America and features distinctive, showy flowers with a spurred shape. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture, while the flowers range in color from white to pink.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
