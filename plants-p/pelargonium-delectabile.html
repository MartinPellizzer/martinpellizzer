Pelargonium delectabile

Pelargonium delectabile, commonly known as the sweet geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant features rounded, velvety leaves and often grows in a bushy form. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Pelargonium delectabile is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
