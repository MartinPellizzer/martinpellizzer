Panicum aristidoides

Panicum aristidoides, commonly known as slender panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and fine, thread-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Panicum aristidoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soil in ecological restoration projects.
