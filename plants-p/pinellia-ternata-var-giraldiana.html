Pinellia ternata var. giraldiana

Pinellia ternata var. giraldiana, commonly known as the Yunnan Pinellia, is a variant of the Pinellia genus native to China.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its rhizomatous growth and distinctive, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale green flowers, followed by blue or violet berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Botanically, it is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
