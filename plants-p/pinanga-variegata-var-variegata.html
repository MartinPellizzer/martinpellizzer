Pinanga variegata var. variegata

Pinanga variegata var. variegata, commonly known as the variegated bamboo palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking variegated leaves with white and green patterns, enhancing its ornamental value. The plant has a slender, upright growth habit with clustered, bamboo-like stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its health.

Its foliage provides a lush, tropical aesthetic, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
