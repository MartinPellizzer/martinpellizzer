Pouteria zenkeri

Pouteria zenkeri, commonly known as the African shea tree, is a tropical tree native to Central and West Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves and yellowish flowers. The tree produces a fruit with a thick, inedible outer layer and a fleshy, aromatic interior that contains seeds. Its latex is used in traditional medicine and as a natural adhesive.
