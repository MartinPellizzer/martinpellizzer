Pilea sohayakiensis

Pilea sohayakiensis, commonly known as the Shizuoka pilea, is a species native to Japan.

It is a small, herbaceous plant with heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a unique and interesting subject for plant enthusiasts and researchers.
