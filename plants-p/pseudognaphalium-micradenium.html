Pseudognaphalium micradenium

Pseudognaphalium micradenium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets.
