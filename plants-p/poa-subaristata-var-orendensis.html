Poa subaristata var. orendensis

Poa subaristata var. orendensis, commonly known as the Orendon grass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and fine, soft blades that often form dense mats. The plant typically thrives in cool, moist environments and is adapted to high-altitude conditions. Its inflorescence consists of a panicle of spikelets, which are usually pale green to brown in color.

This variety is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
