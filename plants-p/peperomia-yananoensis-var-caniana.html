Peperomia yananoensis var. caniana

Peperomia yananoensis var. caniana, commonly known as the Yananoensis Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale green. This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.
