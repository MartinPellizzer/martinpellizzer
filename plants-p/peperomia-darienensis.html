Peperomia darienensis

Peperomia darienensis, commonly known as the Darien peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and thick, fleshy leaves. The plant typically features small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a houseplant.
