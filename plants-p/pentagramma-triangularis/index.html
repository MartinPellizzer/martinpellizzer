Pentagramma Triangularis

Pentagramma triangularis is a small evergreen shrub native to the moist forests of southern and eastern Asia. It is known for its distinctive foliage, with leaves that form intricate patterns resembling a five-pointed star. This plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found growing in mountainous regions. Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking to add visual interest to shaded gardens. Despite its delicate look, Pentagramma triangularis is surprisingly resilient and easy to care for.
