Pleurothallis ocellata

Pleurothallis ocellata, commonly known as the spotted pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on tree branches or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. Its flowers are often adorned with intricate patterns and spots, giving it its common name.

Pleurothallis ocellata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
