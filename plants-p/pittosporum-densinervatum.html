Pittosporum densinervatum

Pittosporum densinervatum, commonly known as the dense-veined pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and lowland forest environments. The plant features dense, dark green leaves with prominent veins, and it produces fragrant, white or cream-colored flowers. The fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that attracts birds, aiding in seed dispersal.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitat.
