Pyrrosia assimilis

Pyrrosia assimilis, commonly known as the rock fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth pattern. The plant features lanceolate, leathery leaves that are typically green to bluish-green in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on rocks or tree trunks.

Pyrrosia assimilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is popular in horticulture for indoor and outdoor cultivation.
