Polyscias biformis

Polyscias biformis, commonly known as the two-leaf goosefoot, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are typically divided into two pairs of opposite leaflets. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by dark blue, spherical berries. Polyscias biformis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.

It thrives in humid, shady environments and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
