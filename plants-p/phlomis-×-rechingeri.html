Phlomis × rechingeri

Phlomis × rechingeri, commonly known as the hybrid Jerusalem sage, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems covered in grayish-green, fuzzy foliage. The plant produces showy, purple or pink flower clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
