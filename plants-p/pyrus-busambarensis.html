Pyrus busambarensis

Pyrus busambarensis, commonly known as the Busambarensis pear, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to the highland regions of eastern Africa, particularly in countries like Uganda and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its deciduous leaves and clusters of white flowers that bloom in spring. Its fruit, a small pear-like fruit, is edible and has a sweet, aromatic flavor.
