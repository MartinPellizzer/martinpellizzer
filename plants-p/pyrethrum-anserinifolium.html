Pyrethrum anserinifolium

Pyrethrum anserinifolium, commonly known as the grayleaf pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its grayish-green foliage and daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant produces small, spherical seed heads that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are covered in a fine layer of white wax, giving them a distinctive silvery appearance.

Pyrethrum anserinifolium is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its mild insecticidal properties.
