Priva curtisiae

Priva curtisiae, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers. Priva curtisiae is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
