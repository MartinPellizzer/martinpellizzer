Pentzia stenocephala

Pentzia stenocephala, commonly known as the narrow-headed pentzia, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the daisy family. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
