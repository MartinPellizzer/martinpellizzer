Piper peltobryon

Piper peltobryon, commonly known as the creeping pepper vine, is a member of the Piperaceae family.

It is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of South America. The plant features small, heart-shaped leaves that are often pubescent. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.

The fruit is a small, three-lobed berry that contains several seeds.
