Philodendron inaequilaterum subsp. zulianum

Philodendron inaequilaterum subsp.

zulianum, commonly known as the Zulianum philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to South America. It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a glossy green surface and prominent veins.

The plant is characterized by its aerial roots, which it uses to climb and support itself. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

This species is popular among houseplants due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
