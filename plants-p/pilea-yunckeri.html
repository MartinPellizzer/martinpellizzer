Pilea yunckeri

Pilea yunckeri, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species native to China.

It is a small, evergreen shrub characterized by its round, fleshy leaves that resemble coins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by round, green berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea yunckeri is valued for its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
