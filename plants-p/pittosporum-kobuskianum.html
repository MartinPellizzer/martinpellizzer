Pittosporum kobuskianum

Pittosporum kobuskianum, commonly known as the New Zealand pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath, with a distinctive, fragrant, white to pale yellow flower cluster. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red when mature. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

Pittosporum kobuskianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native New Zealand forests.
