Pilosella sulphurea

Pilosella sulphurea, commonly known as the sulphur cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has a low-growing habit with hairy stems and leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.

The species is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
