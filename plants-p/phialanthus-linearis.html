Phialanthus linearis

Phialanthus linearis, commonly known as the linear leaf euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, thick stem with narrow, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
