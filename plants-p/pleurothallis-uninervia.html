Pleurothallis uninervia

Pleurothallis uninervia, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has a compact growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces a single, slender inflorescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts of rare orchids.
