Peiranisia gaumeri

Peiranisia gaumeri, commonly known as the Mexican moonseed, is a member of the Fabaceae family and is native to Mexico.

It is a climbing vine that features small, heart-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers. The plant is characterized by its unique seed pods that resemble a moon, hence its common name. Peiranisia gaumeri is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in shaded, moist environments.
