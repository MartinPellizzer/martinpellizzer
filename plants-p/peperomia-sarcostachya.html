Peperomia sarcostachya

Peperomia sarcostachya, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a distinctive silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in late spring or early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia sarcostachya is a low-maintenance plant that is well-suited for indoor cultivation.
