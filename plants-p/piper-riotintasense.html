Piper riotintasense

Piper riotintasense, commonly known as the red spiny holly, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes numerous species of shrubs and vines. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with prominent veins and spiny margins. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in spikes.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns red when mature, attracting various bird species.
