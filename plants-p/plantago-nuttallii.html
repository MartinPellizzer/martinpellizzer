Plantago nuttallii

Plantago nuttallii, commonly known as Nuttall's plantain, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It typically grows in moist to wet soils, often found in prairies, meadows, and along stream banks. The plant features rosettes of broad, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences.

Nuttall's plantain is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally for its healing benefits.
