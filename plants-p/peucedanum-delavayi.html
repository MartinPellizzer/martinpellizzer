Peucedanum delavayi

Peucedanum delavayi, commonly known as Chinese cowparsley, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with finely divided, fern-like leaves. Its flowers are small and white or pale green, forming dense clusters at the top of the stems.
