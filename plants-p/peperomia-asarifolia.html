Peperomia asarifolia

Peperomia asarifolia, commonly known as the featherleaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are dark green and deeply lobed, giving it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia asarifolia is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive foliage.
