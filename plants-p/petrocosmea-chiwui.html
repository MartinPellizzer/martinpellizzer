Petrocosmea chiwui

It is characterized by its unique floral structure, featuring showy, violet-blue petals with a distinctive yellow lip. The plant typically grows on rocky outcrops, adapting to nutrient-poor and well-drained substrates. Its flowering period occurs in late spring to early summer, and it is pollinated by specific insect species.
