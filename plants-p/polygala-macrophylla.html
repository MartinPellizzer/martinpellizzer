Polygala macrophylla

Polygala macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to parts of Australia.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its showy, butterfly-like flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal or inland areas. It has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces pods that contain several seeds.

Polygala macrophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
