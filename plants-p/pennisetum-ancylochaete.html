Pennisetum ancylochaete

Pennisetum ancylochaete, commonly known as the fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its fountain-like growth habit, with multiple stems arising from the base. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces fluffy, bottlebrush-shaped seed heads that are often pink or red in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Pennisetum ancylochaete is also valued for its ability to attract birds and other wildlife.
