Phebalium glandulosum subsp. nitidum

Phebalium glandulosum subsp.

nitidum, commonly known as the glossy wattle, is a flowering plant native to Australia. It is characterized by its bright yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant has smooth, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in open woodlands and heathlands.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
