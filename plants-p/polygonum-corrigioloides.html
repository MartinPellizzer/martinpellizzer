Polygonum corrigioloides

Polygonum corrigioloides, commonly known as the slender knotweed, is a herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender, upright stems with alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, greenish-white flowers are arranged in dense clusters and bloom in late summer.

Polygonum corrigioloides is known for its ability to thrive in wet soils and is often found in riparian habitats.
