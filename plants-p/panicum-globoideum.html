Panicum globoideum

Panicum globoideum, commonly known as little barley or globular panicum, is a flowering plant species native to Africa.

It is a member of the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its compact, globular growth habit. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences with pale green to brown bracts. Its leaves are narrow, linear, and often covered with fine hairs.

Panicum globoideum is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient plant in dry climates.
