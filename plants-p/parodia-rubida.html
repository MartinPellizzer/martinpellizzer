Parodia rubida

Parodia rubida, commonly known as the red ghost cactus, is a species of cactus native to the Mexican state of Coahuila.

It is characterized by its compact, spherical shape and vibrant red to pinkish spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its green body. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Parodia rubida is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its hardy nature and aesthetic appeal.
