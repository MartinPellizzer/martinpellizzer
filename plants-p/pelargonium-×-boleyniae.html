Pelargonium × boleyniae

Pelargonium × boleyniae, commonly known as the Boleyn hybrid geranium, is a hybrid species within the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant typically features serrated, velvety leaves that are often variegated with contrasting colors. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden beds and containers.

Pelargonium × boleyniae is known for its resilience and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
