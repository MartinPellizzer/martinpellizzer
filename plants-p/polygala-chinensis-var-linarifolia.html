Polygala chinensis var. linarifolia

Polygala chinensis var. linarifolia, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features slender, erect stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers arranged in dense, cylindrical clusters. Its leaves are alternate, linear, and often covered with fine hairs.

It is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
