Philodendron popenoei

Philodendron popenoei, commonly known as the Mexican evergreen philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Mexico.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are often glossy and have a slightly wavy margin. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate to high humidity.

Philodendron popenoei is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
