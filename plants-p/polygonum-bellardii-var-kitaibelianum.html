Polygonum bellardii var. kitaibelianum

Polygonum bellardii var. kitaibelianum, commonly known as the Kitaibel's smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as wetlands, marshes, and stream banks. It has a fibrous root system and produces capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and wetland restoration projects.
