Podocarpus aristulatus var. buchii

Podocarpus aristulatus var. buchii, commonly known as the dwarf podocarp or Burmese podocarp, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features dense, dark green foliage with leathery leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain its seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its compact size and attractive appearance.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Podocarpaceae and is characterized by its unique growth habit and adaptability to various soil conditions.
