Peperomia negrosensis

Peperomia negrosensis, commonly known as the Negros peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage. The plant typically features small, ovate leaves that are glossy and arranged in a rosette pattern. It produces delicate, spadix-like inflorescences with tiny, inconspicuous flowers.

Peperomia negrosensis is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ornamental appeal.
