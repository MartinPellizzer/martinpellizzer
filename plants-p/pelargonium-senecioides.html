Pelargonium senecioides

Pelargonium senecioides, commonly known as the African geranium, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems and produces clusters of small, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink, red, or white. The leaves are typically rounded or heart-shaped with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
