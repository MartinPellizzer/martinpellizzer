Polystachya geniculata

Polystachya geniculata, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky areas. Its leaves are linear and often covered in a fine, hairy texture.

Polystachya geniculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
