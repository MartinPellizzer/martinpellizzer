Piper laxivenum

Piper laxivenum, commonly known as the slender pipevine, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit and heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in axillary inflorescences.

The fruit is a three-lobed capsule that releases tiny seeds when mature.
