Pilea plicatidentata var. microphylla

Pilea plicatidentata var. microphylla, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that appear in clusters during the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
