Polygala vulgaris proles dunensis

Polygala vulgaris proles dunensis, commonly known as the Duneland milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas. The plant features small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early summer, and its leaves are pinnately divided with a hairy texture. It has a fibrous root system that helps it survive in arid conditions.

The species is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soils and providing nectar for pollinators.
