Pertya multiflora

Pertya multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered pertya, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a leathery texture, while its flowers emit a subtle fragrance.

Pertya multiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
