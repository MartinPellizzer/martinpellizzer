Polyalthia gracilipes

Polyalthia gracilipes, commonly known as the slender flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, drooping branches and clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom in the summer. The leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth, glossy surface. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking floral display.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is adaptable to various soil types.
