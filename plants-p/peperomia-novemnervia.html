Peperomia novemnervia

Peperomia novemnervia, commonly known as the nine-veined peperomia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its compact, succulent-like leaves that are often glossy and arranged in a rosette pattern. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are typically borne in clusters.

Peperomia novemnervia is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.
