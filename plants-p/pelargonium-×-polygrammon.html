Pelargonium × polygrammon

Pelargonium × polygrammon, commonly known as the variegated geranium, is a hybrid species within the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white, often with contrasting markings. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow patterns, adding visual interest. It is a popular choice for gardens and containers due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.

Native to South Africa, it thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.
