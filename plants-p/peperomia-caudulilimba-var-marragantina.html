Peperomia caudulilimba var. marragantina

Peperomia caudulilimba var. marragantina, commonly known as the Cape Cod peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to South America.

It features thick, waxy, ovate leaves that are typically green with reddish or maroon undersides, giving it a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color, typical of the Peperomia genus. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and ease of care make it a desirable ornamental plant for both novice and experienced growers.
