Peperomia pringlei

Peperomia pringlei, commonly known as the Pringle's pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit, glossy dark green leaves, and small, inconspicuous flowers. It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.

Peperomia pringlei thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor environments.
