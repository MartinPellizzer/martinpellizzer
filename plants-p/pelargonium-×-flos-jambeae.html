Pelargonium × flos-jambeae

Pelargonium × flos-jambeae, commonly known as the Madagascar violet.

It is a hybrid geranium species native to Madagascar, characterized by its striking violet and purple flowers. The plant typically has a compact, bushy growth habit with velvety, dark green leaves. Its flowers are borne in dense clusters, often with a sweet, spicy fragrance.

It is well-suited for container gardening and thrives in well-drained soil with ample sunlight.
