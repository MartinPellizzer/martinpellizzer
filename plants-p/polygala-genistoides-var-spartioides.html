Polygala genistoides var. spartioides

Polygala genistoides var. spartioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaf milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems with opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in spike-like clusters, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or gravelly habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
