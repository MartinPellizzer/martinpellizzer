Podolepis rosmarinifolia

Podolepis rosmarinifolia, commonly known as the mountain sage.

It is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa. The plant features aromatic, needle-like leaves that are often gray-green in color. It produces small, blue to purple flowers during the summer months.

Podolepis rosmarinifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping.
