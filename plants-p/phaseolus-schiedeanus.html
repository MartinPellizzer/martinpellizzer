Phaseolus schiedeanus

Phaseolus schiedeanus, commonly known as the scarlet runner bean, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its climbing habit, with long, trailing stems that can reach up to 6 meters in length. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers, which are followed by elongated, cylindrical pods containing edible seeds. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

This species is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its edible beans, which are rich in nutrients.
