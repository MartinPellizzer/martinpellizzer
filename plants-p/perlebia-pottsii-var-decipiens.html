Perlebia pottsii var. decipiens

Perlebia pottsii var. decipiens, commonly known as the false sand orchid, is a small, delicate flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, fragrant flowers. The plant produces showy, greenish-yellow blooms that are often mistaken for true sand orchids due to their similar appearance. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and requires well-drained conditions to prevent root rot.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens with specific horticultural care.
