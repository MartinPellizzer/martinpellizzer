Philibertia pavonii

Philibertia pavonii, commonly known as the golden crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow ray florets and dark purple disk florets. Philibertia pavonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
