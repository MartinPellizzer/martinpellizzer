Pisonia subcordata f. albida

Pisonia subcordata f. albida, commonly known as the white-flowered pisonia, is a rare and striking variant of the pisonia plant.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that contrast sharply with its dark green foliage. The plant is native to the tropical regions of the Pacific Islands and thrives in warm, humid climates. Its flowers are typically clustered in panicles, and the leaves are simple and ovate in shape.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
