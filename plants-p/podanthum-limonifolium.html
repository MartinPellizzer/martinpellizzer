Podanthum limonifolium

Podanthum limonifolium, commonly known as the lemon-leaf podanthum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a waxy, glossy surface, giving it a distinct appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and greenhouses.
