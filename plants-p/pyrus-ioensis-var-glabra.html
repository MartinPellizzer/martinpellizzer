Pyrus ioensis var. glabra

Pyrus ioensis var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth pear.

It is a variety of the Io pear, native to the eastern United States. The plant is characterized by its smooth, glabrous bark and broad, oval-shaped leaves. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers in the spring, followed by edible, sweet fruits.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical use in horticulture.
