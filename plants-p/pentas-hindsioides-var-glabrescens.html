Pentas hindsioides var. glabrescens

Pentas hindsioides var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth pentas, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, brightly colored flowers, typically in shades of red, pink, or white. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often hairy but become smoother as the plant matures. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its compact growth habit and showy blooms make it a versatile plant for both container and garden use.
