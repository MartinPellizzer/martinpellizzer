Pereskia vargasii var. longispina

Pereskia vargasii var. longispina, commonly known as the long-spined cactus, is a unique species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems that are covered in short, stiff spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its distinctive appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
