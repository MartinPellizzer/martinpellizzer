Poinsettia pinetorum

Poinsettia pinetorum, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a striking plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its vibrant red bracts that surround small, inconspicuous yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in warm, sunny environments. Its foliage consists of dark green, broad leaves that provide a lush contrast to the colorful bracts.

Poinsettia pinetorum is widely cultivated as a holiday ornamental due to its festive appearance and seasonal blooming.
