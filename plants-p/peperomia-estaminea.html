Peperomia estaminea

Peperomia estaminea, commonly known as the Orange Jewel or Orange Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a striking orange or reddish hue on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia estaminea is noted for its low maintenance and ability to add a vibrant touch to any plant collection.
