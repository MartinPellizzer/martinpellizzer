Peliosanthes weberi

Peliosanthes weberi, commonly known as the Chinese sand orchid, is a rare and endangered species native to southern China.

It is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, sand-like appearance of its flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in humid, shaded environments. It has small, elongated leaves and produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is critically endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
