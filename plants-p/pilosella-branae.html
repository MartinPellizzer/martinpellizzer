Pilosella branae

Pilosella branae, commonly known as the Brana's goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance.

The flowers are yellow and appear in clusters, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
