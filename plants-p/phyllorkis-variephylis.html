Phyllorkis variephylis

Phyllorkis variephylis, commonly known as the variable leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, often variegated leaves that display a range of colors, including green, yellow, and white. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in shades of white or pale pink, and bloom during the rainy season.

Phyllorkis variephylis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
