Pluchea dodonaeifolia

Pluchea dodonaeifolia, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with slender stems and small, hairy leaves. Its flowers are yellow and clustered in spherical heads, often appearing in late summer to early autumn.

It is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional African medicine for various ailments.
