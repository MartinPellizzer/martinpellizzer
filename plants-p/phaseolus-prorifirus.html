Phaseolus prorifirus

Phaseolus prorifirus, commonly known as the scarlet runner bean, is a climbing legume native to the Americas.

It produces vibrant red or orange flowers and elongated pods that contain edible seeds. The plant has compound leaves with small, pointed leaflets and thrives in warm, sunny climates. It is valued for its nutritional content and is often cultivated for both culinary and ornamental purposes.

The species is closely related to other Phaseolus species, including the common bean.
