Pappophorum hassleri

Pappophorum hassleri, commonly known as the African feather grass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It features slender, wiry stems and produces fluffy, feathery inflorescences that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant is characterized by its fine, needle-like leaves and is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Pappophorum hassleri is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.
