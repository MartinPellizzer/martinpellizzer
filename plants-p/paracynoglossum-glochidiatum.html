Paracynoglossum glochidiatum

Paracynoglossum glochidiatum, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family, characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, velvety hairs. Due to its invasive potential, it is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
