Podochilus obovatipetalus

Podochilus obovatipetalus, commonly known as the broad-petalled orchid, is a tropical orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, broad, and often colorful petals that give it its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid forest environments. Its flowers are usually fragrant and attract various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The genus Podochilus is known for its diverse floral morphology, and P. obovatipetalus is a notable member due to its ornamental value.
