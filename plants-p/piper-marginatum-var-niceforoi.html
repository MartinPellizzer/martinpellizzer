Piper marginatum var. niceforoi

Piper marginatum var. niceforoi, commonly known as the Niceforo's pepper plant, is a variant of the pepper plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, small, and often spiny fruits, which are used in traditional medicine and culinary applications. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to several meters. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped with a glossy surface and emit a strong, peppery scent when crushed.

This variety is valued for its unique flavor profile and medicinal properties.
