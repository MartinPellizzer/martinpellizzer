Piper coruscans

Piper coruscans, commonly known as the scarlet pepper plant, is a member of the Piperaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its vibrant red berries, which are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are aromatic and can be used to make teas.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
