Pterocarya caucasica

Pterocarya caucasica, commonly known as the Caucasian wingnut, is a deciduous tree native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Juglandaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive winged fruits. The tree has compound leaves with serrated margins and produces small, yellowish flowers in early spring. Its bark is smooth and light gray, and it can grow up to 20 meters tall.
