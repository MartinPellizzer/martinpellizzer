Peperomia capitis-bovis

Peperomia capitis-bovis, commonly known as the cow's head peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers arranged in spikes, which are often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its unique leaf shape and easy care requirements, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant.
