Panicum petrense

Panicum petrense, commonly known as the coastal bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the coastal regions of the southeastern United States.

It thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is often found in dunes and maritime habitats. The plant has narrow, bluish-green leaves and produces fluffy, pale blue to silver-gray seed heads that are highly visible in the landscape. It is drought-tolerant and plays an important role in stabilizing coastal soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for erosion control and landscape design in coastal areas.
