Pleurothallis bovilabia

Pleurothallis bovilabia, commonly known as the Bovilabia orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments within the rainforest canopy. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a subtle purple or greenish tinge. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrate.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
