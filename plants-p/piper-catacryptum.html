Piper catacryptum

Piper catacryptum, commonly known as the cryptic pepper, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with opposite, ovate leaves that are often pubescent. Its small, greenish-white flowers are borne in clusters and are followed by small, fleshy berries.

The plant's unique morphology and habitat make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
