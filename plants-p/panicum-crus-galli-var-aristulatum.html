Panicum crus-galli var. aristulatum

Panicum crus-galli var. aristulatum, commonly known as purple lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to the Americas.

It features slender, erect stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height, often with a reddish or purplish tint. The plant produces dense, fluffy panicles of small, pale green to brown seeds, which are wind-dispersed. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slight hairy texture.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a common ornamental and forage plant in temperate regions.
