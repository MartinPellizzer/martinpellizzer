Penstemon multicaulis

Penstemon multicaulis, commonly known as the tall beardtongue, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and open habitats, such as meadows and slopes. It produces numerous flowering stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters.

Penstemon multicaulis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.
