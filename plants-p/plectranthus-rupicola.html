Plectranthus rupicola

Plectranthus rupicola, commonly known as the rock plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky or limestone habitats. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of tubular, violet to purple flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value comes from its compact growth habit and attractive floral display.
