Peperomia carpinterana var. sparsipila

Peperomia carpinterana var. sparsipila, commonly known as the sparse-leaved peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It features small, waxy, ovate leaves that are typically pale green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale green to reddish flowers that bloom in spring. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal water needs.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for foliage-focused plant collections.
