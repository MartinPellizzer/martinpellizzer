Phacelia pinnatifida var. robusta

Phacelia pinnatifida var. robusta, commonly known as the robust phacelia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, pinnately divided leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for native plant gardens.

Its rapid growth and ability to attract pollinators make it an ecologically valuable species.
