Pilea nitida

Pilea nitida, commonly known as the velvet plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Pilea nitida is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
