Peliostomum calycinum

Peliostomum calycinum, commonly known as the golden water star, is a aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that resemble stars, which bloom throughout the growing season. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often found in ponds and lakes. Its leaves are typically floating or submerged, depending on the water conditions.

Peliostomum calycinum is valued in ornamental water gardens for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
