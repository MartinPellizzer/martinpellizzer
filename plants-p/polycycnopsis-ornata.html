Polycycnopsis ornata

Polycycnopsis ornata, commonly known as the ornate polycycnopsis, is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of hair, which helps reduce water loss.

Polycycnopsis ornata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
