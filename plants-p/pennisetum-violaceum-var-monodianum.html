Pennisetum violaceum var. monodianum

Pennisetum violaceum var. monodianum, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that produce fluffy, purple-tinted plumes during the flowering season. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for landscaping.

Its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored plant in gardens and public spaces.
