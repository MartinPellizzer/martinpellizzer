Pilea velutinipes

Pilea velutinipes, commonly known as the velvet pilea, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features dense, velvety foliage with dark green, oval-shaped leaves that have a soft, fuzzy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by spherical, red berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements contribute to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
