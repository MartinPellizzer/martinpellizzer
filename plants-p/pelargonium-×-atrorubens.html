Pelargonium × atrorubens

Pelargonium × atrorubens, commonly known as the dark red geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking deep red to almost black flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture, often displaying a dark green color with a glossy sheen. This species is native to South Africa and is cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

It prefers full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a versatile choice for various growing conditions.
