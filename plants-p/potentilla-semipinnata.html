Potentilla semipinnata

Potentilla semipinnata, commonly known as the silverleaf potentilla, is a deciduous shrub native to Europe and western Asia.

It features small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has finely divided, silver-gray leaves that are semi-pinnate, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Potentilla semipinnata is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.
