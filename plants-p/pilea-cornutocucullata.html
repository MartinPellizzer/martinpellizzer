Pilea cornutocucullata

Pilea cornutocucullata, commonly known as the curlleaf pilea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Urticaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Vietnam, and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are deeply lobed and often curl at the edges. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it has become a popular ornamental plant in indoor gardening.
