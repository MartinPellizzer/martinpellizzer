Pleioblastus naibunensis

Pleioblastus naibunensis, commonly known as the Naibun bamboo, is a species of grass in the family Poaceae.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its slender, upright culms and fine, silvery-green leaves. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. It is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often cultivated in gardens.
