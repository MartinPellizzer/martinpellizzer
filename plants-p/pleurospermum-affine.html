Pleurospermum affine

Pleurospermum affine, commonly known as narrowleaf pleurospermum, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy, and its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in umbels. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

The species is part of the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
