Pimelea holroydii

Pimelea holroydii, commonly known as the Narrow-leaved Riceflower, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils, often in open habitats. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers during the spring season. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a distinctive grey-green color.

Pimelea holroydii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also of ecological significance in its native environment.
