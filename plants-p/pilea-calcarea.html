Pilea calcarea

Pilea calcarea, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a popular houseplant native to Southeast Asia.

It features rosettes of round, fleshy leaves that resemble small coins, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale green. It thrives in indirect light and well-draining soil, making it easy to care for.

Pilea calcarea is known for its ability to purify indoor air and is often used in interior landscaping.
