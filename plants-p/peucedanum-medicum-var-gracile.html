Peucedanum medicum var. gracile

Peucedanum medicum var. gracile, commonly known as slender angelica, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its umbellate inflorescences and aromatic foliage. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with finely divided, fern-like leaves. Its flowers are small and white to greenish in color, arranged in compound umbels.
