Pomatocalpa spicatum

Pomatocalpa spicatum, commonly known as the spicate pterostylid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with purple markings. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Pomatocalpa spicatum thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
