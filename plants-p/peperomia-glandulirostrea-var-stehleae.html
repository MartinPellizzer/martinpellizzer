Peperomia glandulirostrea var. stehleae

Peperomia glandulirostrea var. stehleae, commonly known as the Stehle's glandular peperomia, is a compact, low-growing herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are often adorned with tiny, glandular structures, giving it a unique textured appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically borne on short, upright spikes. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and minimal water requirements.

Its ornamental value lies primarily in its attractive foliage rather than its flowers.
