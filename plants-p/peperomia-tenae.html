Peperomia tenae

Peperomia tenae, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically silver or gray-green in color, with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, usually in shades of white or pale pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia tenae is relatively low maintenance and resistant to pests, contributing to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
