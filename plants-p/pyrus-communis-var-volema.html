Pyrus communis var. volema

Pyrus communis var. volema, commonly known as the Volema pear, is a cultivated variety of the common pear.

It is characterized by its medium-sized, yellowish-green fruit with a slight russeting. The plant typically grows as a deciduous tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are broad and ovate, with a smooth texture and a glossy upper surface.

The Volema pear is valued for its sweet, aromatic fruit, which is often used in both fresh consumption and culinary applications.
