Perama hirsuta var. stricta

Perama hirsuta var. stricta, commonly known as the stiff-haired perama, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant typically features small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
