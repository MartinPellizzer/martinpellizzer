Pentas bussei

Pentas bussei, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, five-petaled flowers that come in vibrant shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a smooth texture.

Pentas bussei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
