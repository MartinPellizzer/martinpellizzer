Pterotropis virens

Pterotropis virens, commonly known as the green fern, is a species of fern characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features a creeping rhizome with upright, lanceolate leaves that are usually bright green. Its spore-producing structures, called sori, are clustered on the underside of the fronds.

Pterotropis virens thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
