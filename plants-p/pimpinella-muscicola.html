Pimpinella muscicola

Pimpinella muscicola, commonly known as the mossy parsley, is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbel-like inflorescences.

Its small white flowers appear in spring, followed by small, dry fruits that contain a single seed.
