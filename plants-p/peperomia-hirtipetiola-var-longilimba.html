Peperomia hirtipetiola var. longilimba

Peperomia hirtipetiola var. longilimba, commonly known as the Long-leaf Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, clustered in spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
