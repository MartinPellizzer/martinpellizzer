Panicum acuminatum var. leucothrix

Panicum acuminatum var. leucothrix, commonly known as silver panicum, is a variant of the panic grass species characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage.

It is a warm-season grass that thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in dry, open habitats. The plant produces fluffy, light-colored seed heads that contribute to its ornamental value. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a dense, upright growth habit.

This variety is valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements in landscaping.
