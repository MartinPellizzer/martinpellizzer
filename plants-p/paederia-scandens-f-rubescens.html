Paederia scandens f. rubescens

Paederia scandens f. rubescens, commonly known as the red-stemmed paederia, is a flowering plant species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its hairy, reddish stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its botanical features make it a notable species in tropical flora studies.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth.
