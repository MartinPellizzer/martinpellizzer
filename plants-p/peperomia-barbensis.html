Peperomia barbensis

Peperomia barbensis, commonly known as the Barben's peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to Central America.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves that are often heart-shaped or ovate in form. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color.

Peperomia barbensis is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.
