Pouteria mongaguensis

Pouteria mongaguensis, commonly known as the mangaba tree, is a tropical plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant is valued for its fruit, which is used in local cuisines and traditional medicine.
