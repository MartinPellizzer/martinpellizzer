Pterophylla mammea

Pterophylla mammea, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, conical shape and its distinctive, star-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in late winter, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a valuable addition to both landscape design and horticultural collections.
