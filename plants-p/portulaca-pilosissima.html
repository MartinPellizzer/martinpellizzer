Portulaca pilosissima

Portulaca pilosissima, commonly known as the silky purslane, is a low-growing annual plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It features fleshy, succulent leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a soft, textured appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the morning and close by midday. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it well-suited for xeriscaping.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a notable species in botanical studies and horticulture.
