Portulaca guineensis var. nitidissima

Portulaca guineensis var. nitidissima, commonly known as the shining portulaca, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and bright, vibrant flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments. Its flowers are typically short-lived, opening in the morning and closing by midday.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to withstand drought conditions.
