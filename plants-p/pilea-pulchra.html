Pilea pulchra

Pilea pulchra, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial shrub native to China.

It features rosettes of round, glossy, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slight variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea pulchra is relatively low maintenance and can be propagated through stem cuttings or division.
