Piper jamesonii

Piper jamesonii, commonly known as the Jameson's pepper, is a tropical flowering plant native to the rainforests of Queensland, Australia.

It belongs to the Piper genus, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, green, egg-shaped fruits that turn red when mature. It has opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and a fragrant aroma.

Piper jamesonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in humid, shaded environments.
