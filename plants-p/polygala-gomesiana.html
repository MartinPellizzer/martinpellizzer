Polygala gomesiana

Polygala gomesiana, commonly known as the Gomes' milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, often yellow or greenish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, showing a preference for well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually small and linear, and it produces small, bead-like fruits that contain the seeds.
