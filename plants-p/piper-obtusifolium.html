Piper obtusifolium

Piper obtusifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf pepper or Australian pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to Australia and is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant produces small, peppery fruits that are often used in traditional Australian cuisine. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions.

Its botanical features make it an important component of its native ecosystem.
