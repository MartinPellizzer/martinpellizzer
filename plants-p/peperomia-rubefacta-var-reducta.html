Peperomia rubefacta var. reducta

Peperomia rubefacta var. reducta, commonly known as the Red-veined Peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features succulent, oval-shaped leaves with striking red veins and a deep green base, giving it a vibrant, ornamental appearance. The plant has small, inconspicuous flowers that appear in clusters, typically in late spring or early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
