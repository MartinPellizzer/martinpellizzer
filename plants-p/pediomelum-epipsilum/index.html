Pediomelum Epipsilum

Pediomelum epipsilum is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the western United States. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and open slopes. Known for its small, pale yellow flowers, it is a member of the Asteraceae family. This plant is particularly sensitive to environmental changes, making it a valuable indicator of ecosystem health. Its unique characteristics and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
