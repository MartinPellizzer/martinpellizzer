Plocama aucheri

Plocama aucheri, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a perennial herb native to the Pacific Northwest of the United States.

It features silver-gray leaves that are densely covered with fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale green to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It prefers moist, shady environments and is often found in forested areas.

Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening due to its striking color and texture.
