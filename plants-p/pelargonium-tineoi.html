Pelargonium tineoi

Pelargonium tineoi, commonly known as the Tineo's geranium, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of bright red or pink blooms during the summer months. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Pelargonium tineoi is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
