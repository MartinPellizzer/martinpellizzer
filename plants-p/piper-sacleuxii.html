Piper sacleuxii

Piper sacleuxii, commonly known as the Sacleux's pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its vine-like growth habit and small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are typically ovate to elliptic in shape, with a rough texture.

Piper sacleuxii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
