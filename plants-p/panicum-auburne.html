Panicum auburne

Panicum auburne, commonly known as the crimson grass or red panicum, is a species of grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and vibrant red or purple inflorescences that appear in late summer. The plant typically grows in full sun and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping and native gardens. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a reddish tinge that adds visual interest throughout the growing season.

Panicum auburne is often used in ornamental landscaping for its striking color and drought tolerance.
