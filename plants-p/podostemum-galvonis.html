Podostemum galvonis

Podostemum galvonis, commonly known as the aquatic buttercup, is a unique aquatic plant found in fast-flowing freshwater habitats.

It is characterized by its small, floating leaves that are typically less than 1 cm in diameter. The plant has specialized root systems that anchor it to rocks in turbulent waters. Its flowers are usually yellow or white, and they bloom in clusters near the water's surface.

Podostemum galvonis plays an important ecological role in maintaining water quality and providing habitat for aquatic organisms.
