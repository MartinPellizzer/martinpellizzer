Piper barkleyi

Piper barkleyi, commonly known as the Mexican pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into three-lobed berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Piper barkleyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
