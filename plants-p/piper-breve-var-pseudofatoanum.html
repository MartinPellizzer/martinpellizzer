Piper breve var. pseudofatoanum

Piper breve var. pseudofatoanum, commonly known as the dwarf pepper plant, is a compact shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its small, ovate leaves and clusters of tiny, peppery fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species in botanical collections.
