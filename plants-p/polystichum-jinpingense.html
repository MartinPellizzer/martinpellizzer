Polystichum jinpingense

Polystichum jinpingense, commonly known as the Jinping holly fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest undergrowth. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds, which are arranged in a distinctive pattern.
