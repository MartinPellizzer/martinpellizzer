Pilosella Suchonensis

Pilosella suchonensis is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads. This species is characterized by its small, white to pale yellow flowers and hairy stems. Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it faces significant conservation challenges. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it an important subject for botanical study.
