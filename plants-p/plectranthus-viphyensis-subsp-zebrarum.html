Plectranthus viphyensis subsp. zebrarum

Plectranthus viphyensis subsp.

zebrarum, commonly known as the Zebra plant, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa. It features distinctive, lance-shaped leaves with a striking zebra-like pattern of light and dark green.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale purple or white. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.

This species is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
