Pennisetum validum

Pennisetum validum, commonly known as blue oat grass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and feathery, blue-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often used in ornamental landscaping. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it adaptable to various climates.

Pennisetum validum is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract wildlife.
