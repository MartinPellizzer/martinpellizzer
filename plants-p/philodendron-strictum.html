Philodendron strictum

Philodendron strictum, commonly known as the curly-leaf philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a waxy texture and a distinctive curling pattern along the edges. The plant has a robust stem that can climb or trail, making it suitable for hanging baskets or shelves. Its foliage thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain healthy growth.

Philodendron strictum is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
