Pinalia dayana

Pinalia dayana, commonly known as the yellow bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Pinalia dayana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
