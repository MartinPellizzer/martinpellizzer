Poa ustulata

Poa ustulata, commonly known as the scarlet grass or red grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the southern regions of South America, particularly Chile and Argentina. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often tinged with a reddish hue. It produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically green or reddish in color.

Poa ustulata is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
