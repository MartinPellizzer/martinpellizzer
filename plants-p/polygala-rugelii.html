Polygala rugelii

Polygala rugelii, commonly known as the rugel's milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the genus Polygala, which is part of the family Polygalaceae. The plant is characterized by its delicate, feathery inflorescences and small, often pink or white flowers. It typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, adapting well to arid conditions.

Polygala rugelii is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
