Pelargonium × jacquinianum

Pelargonium × jacquinianum, commonly known as the Jacquinian geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, five-petaled flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically has rounded, lobed leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.

Native to South Africa, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun conditions.
