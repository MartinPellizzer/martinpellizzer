Perrierangraecum palmicola

Perrierangraecum palmicola, commonly known as the Socotra cypress, is a unique and rare plant species native to the island of Socotra.

It is a coniferous tree with distinctive, scale-like leaves and a twisted, gnarled trunk. The plant is adapted to the arid and harsh conditions of its native habitat, featuring a deep root system and thick, waxy leaves to reduce water loss. It is considered a living fossil, with its evolutionary lineage dating back millions of years.
