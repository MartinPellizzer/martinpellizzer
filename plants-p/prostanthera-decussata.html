Prostanthera decussata

Prostanthera decussata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mintbush, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic foliage. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue to purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

Prostanthera decussata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
