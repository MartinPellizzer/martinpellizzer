Phalaenopsis × leda

Phalaenopsis × leda, commonly known as the Orchid Hybrid, is a hybrid orchid species resulting from the cross between Phalaenopsis equestris and Phalaenopsis aphrodite.

It is characterized by its elegant, long-lasting blooms that typically range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green with a waxy texture. It is epiphytic, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, and thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light.

Phalaenopsis × leda is popular in horticulture for its beauty and ease of care.
