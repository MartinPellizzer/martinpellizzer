Pelargonium × kolowrathiae

Pelargonium × kolowrathiae, commonly known as the Royal Sunset geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from pink to red, often with a contrasting white or yellow center. The plant typically produces trailing stems with opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is well-suited for container gardening or as a bedding plant.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice in gardens and landscapes for its colorful blooms and compact growth habit.
