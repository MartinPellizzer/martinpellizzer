Prunus dolichadenia

Prunus dolichadenia, commonly known as the Chinese white cherry, is a small deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its smooth, grey-barked trunk and oval-shaped leaves that turn yellow in autumn. The tree produces small, white flowers in early spring, which are followed by edible, sweet fruits. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Prunus dolichadenia prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
