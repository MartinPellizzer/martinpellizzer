Palicourea micrantha

Palicourea micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered palicourea, is a member of the Rubiaceae family.

It is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features opposite, elliptic leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its small, white to pale yellow flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences.

The plant is noted for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
