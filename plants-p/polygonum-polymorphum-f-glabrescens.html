Polygonum polymorphum f. glabrescens

Polygonum polymorphum f. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth polygonum, is a variant of the polygonum species characterized by its reduced pubescence.

It is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia, particularly China, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features upright stems with opposite, ovate leaves that are typically smooth to the touch. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds.

This form is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ecological benefits in wetland restoration projects.
