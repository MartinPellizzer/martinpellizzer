Panicum candicans

Panicum candicans, commonly known as bloodgrass, is a striking ornamental grass native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It features long, slender leaves that turn a vibrant red or pink hue as they age, creating a dramatic visual effect in gardens. The plant produces delicate, feathery spikes that add movement and texture to landscapes. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for both residential and commercial landscaping.
