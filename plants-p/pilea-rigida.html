Pilea rigida

Pilea rigida, commonly known as the Shaved Plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are often ribbed or serrated, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to any plant collection.
