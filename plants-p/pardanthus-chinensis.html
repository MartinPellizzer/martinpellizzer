Pardanthus chinensis

Pardanthus chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves that are slightly hairy. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Botanically, it belongs to the Campanulaceae family, known for its bell-shaped flowers.
