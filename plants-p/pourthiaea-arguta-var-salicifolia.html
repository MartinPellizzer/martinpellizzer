Pourthiaea arguta var. salicifolia

Pourthiaea arguta var. salicifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf plectranthus, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue or violet in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
