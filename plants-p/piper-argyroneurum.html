Piper argyroneurum

Piper argyroneurum, commonly known as silver-veined pepper or argyroneurum pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with prominent silver-white veins, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by bright red, peppercorn-like fruits.

This ornamental plant is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a houseplant.
