Piper veneralense

Piper veneralense, commonly known as the Venus's tears plant, is a member of the Piperaceae family.

It is a climbing shrub native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters. Its most distinctive feature is the production of translucent, tear-like droplets on its leaves, which are actually secretions containing essential oils.

These droplets give the plant its common name and contribute to its unique appearance and fragrance.
