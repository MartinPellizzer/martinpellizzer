Piper sagittifolium

Piper sagittifolium, commonly known as arrowleaf pepper or arrowleaf pipper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, Colombia, and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruits are small berries that develop from the flowers and are often used in traditional medicine.

Piper sagittifolium is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in various herbal remedies.
