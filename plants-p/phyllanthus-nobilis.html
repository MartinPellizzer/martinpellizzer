Phyllanthus nobilis

Phyllanthus nobilis, commonly known as the sacred lotus of India, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in a striking pink or red color. The plant has palmate leaves that are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits. Its root system is fibrous, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soil.

Phyllanthus nobilis is also valued for its ornamental beauty and cultural significance in various regions.
