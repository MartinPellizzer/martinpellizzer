Pleurothallis steyermarkii

Pleurothallis steyermarkii, commonly known as the Steyermark's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It is characterized by its unique floral structure, which includes a narrow, spurred lip.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often found in cloud forests and is considered a rare and specialized species.
