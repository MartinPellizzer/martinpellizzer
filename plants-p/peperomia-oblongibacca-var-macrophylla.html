Peperomia oblongibacca var. macrophylla

Peperomia oblongibacca var. macrophylla, commonly known as the giant peperomia, is a tropical plant characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 12 inches in length.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is native to the rainforests of Central and South America. The plant features a compact, bushy growth habit with thick, waxy foliage that exhibits a vibrant green color. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color, and it thrives in bright, indirect light with moderate humidity.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care in indoor environments.
