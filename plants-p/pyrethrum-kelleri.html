Pyrethrum kelleri

Pyrethrum kelleri, commonly known as the Keller's pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with bright yellow petals. The plant produces small, pale green leaves and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
