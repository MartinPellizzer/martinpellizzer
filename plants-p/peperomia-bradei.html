Peperomia bradei

Peperomia bradei, commonly known as the Brade's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features rounded, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Peperomia bradei is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
