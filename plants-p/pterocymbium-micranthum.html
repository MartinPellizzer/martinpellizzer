Pterocymbium micranthum

Pterocymbium micranthum, commonly known as the small-flowered pterocymbium, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, particularly found in countries like India, Indonesia, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, delicate orchid-like flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.
