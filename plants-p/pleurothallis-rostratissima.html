Pleurothallis rostratissima

Pleurothallis rostratissima, commonly known as the rostrated pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and intricate petal arrangement.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a challenging plant to cultivate outside its natural range.
