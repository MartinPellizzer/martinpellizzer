Plectronia polyneura

Plectronia polyneura, commonly known as the silverleaf plectronia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves, which are covered in fine, fuzzy hairs. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Plectronia polyneura is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
