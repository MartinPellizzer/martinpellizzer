Pithecellobium maestrense

Pithecellobium maestrense, commonly known as the Maestrense crowderia, is a tropical shrub native to Central America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in green or yellowish inflorescences.

The fruit is a legume, containing several seeds that are dispersed by animals or wind.
