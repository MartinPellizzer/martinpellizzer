Panicum paradoxum

Panicum paradoxum, commonly known as the paradox grass, is a perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and fluffy, panicle-shaped inflorescences that resemble a cloud. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens. Its unique appearance and drought tolerance contribute to its appeal in landscaping.

However, it can become invasive in some areas if not properly managed.
