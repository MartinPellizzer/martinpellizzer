Piper oblongatum

Piper oblongatum, commonly known as the broadleaf pepper or oblongatum pepper, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, green, oblong-shaped fruits that turn red when mature, containing aromatic seeds. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
