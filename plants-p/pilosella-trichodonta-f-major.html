Pilosella trichodonta f. major

Pilosella trichodonta f. major, commonly known as the hairy leafed pilosella, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky areas. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered with fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is often found in temperate regions of Europe and parts of Asia.
