Pedicularis Cranolopha Var. Garnieri

Pedicularis cranolopha var. garnieri is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central Asia. Known for its vibrant red and orange petals, this species captivates botanists and nature enthusiasts alike with its unique appearance. It belongs to the Orobanchaceae family, a group of plants commonly known as the broomrape family. This variety is particularly notable for its specialized habitat requirements and limited distribution. Due to its ecological significance and aesthetic appeal, it is often considered a flagship species for conservation efforts in its native range.
