Phegopteris punctata var. mauiensis

Phegopteris punctata var. mauiensis, commonly known as Maui's fern, is a rare and endemic species found primarily on the island of Maui in Hawaii.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories, often growing on rocky or humus-rich soil. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of its fronds, a common feature among ferns.
