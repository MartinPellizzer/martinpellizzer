Pandorea australis var. oxleyi

Pandorea australis var. oxleyi, commonly known as Oxley's pandorea, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a climbing shrub that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink to purple. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, narrow leaflets and is often found in coastal and subtropical regions. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Oxley's pandorea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscaping.
