Premna macroscyphon

Premna macroscyphon, commonly known as the giant leaf premna, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on long, slender peduncles. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental foliage and is occasionally cultivated for horticultural purposes.
