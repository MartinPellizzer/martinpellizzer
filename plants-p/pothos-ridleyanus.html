Pothos ridleyanus

Pothos ridleyanus, commonly known as the golden pothos, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a trailing vine that produces heart-shaped leaves with a waxy texture and a vibrant green color. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and is often used as an indoor ornamental plant. Its flowers, though not showy, are small and white, with a spathe and spadix typical of the Araceae family.

Pothos ridleyanus is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ease of care.
