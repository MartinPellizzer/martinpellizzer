Philodendron pinnatifidum var. virescens

Philodendron pinnatifidum var. virescens, commonly known as the Emerald Queen, is a tropical climbing plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant has a vining growth habit and can reach significant lengths if provided with adequate support. Its leaves are often adorned with subtle veins that add to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.
