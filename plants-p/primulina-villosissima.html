Primulina villosissima

Primulina villosissima, commonly known as the velvet primula, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its velvety, hairy leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often grown as a houseplant.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
