Pedrosia berthelotii

Pedrosia berthelotii, commonly known as the desert lily, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments through its deep root system and thick, waxy leaves that reduce water loss. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Pedrosia berthelotii is often found in rocky soils and plays a role in stabilizing desert ecosystems.
