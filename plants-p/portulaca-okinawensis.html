Portulaca okinawensis

Portulaca okinawensis, commonly known as the Okinawa moss rose, is a flowering plant native to the Ryukyu Islands of Japan.

It is a low-growing annual herb that thrives in arid and sandy soils, often found in coastal areas. The plant produces vibrant pink to red flowers that bloom throughout the summer months. Its leaves are succulent, fleshy, and covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Portulaca okinawensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
