Ptilosia

Ptilosia, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a velvety texture. Ptilosia is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain regions due to its rapid growth and ability to outcompete native vegetation.
