Piper scabridulilimbum

Piper scabridulilimbum, commonly known as the rough-skinned pepper vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with opposite, ovate leaves that have a rough texture. Its small, greenish-white flowers are typically clustered in axillary inflorescences.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that ripens to a yellow or orange color.
