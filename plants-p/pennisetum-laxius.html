Pennisetum laxius

Pennisetum laxius, commonly known as the soft feather grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, arching stems and soft, fluffy plumes that give it a delicate appearance. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats, such as marshes and stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color.

Pennisetum laxius is often used in ornamental landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
