Polygala pohliana

Polygala pohliana, commonly known as the mountain polygala, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, often blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is known for its small, clustered inflorescences and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and habitat specificity, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
