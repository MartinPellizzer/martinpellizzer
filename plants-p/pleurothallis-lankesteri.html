Pleurothallis lankesteri

Pleurothallis lankesteri, commonly known as the Lankester's orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant prefers humid, shaded environments and is often found growing in the understory of tropical rainforests. Its roots are epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or rocks rather than in soil.

Pleurothallis lankesteri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
