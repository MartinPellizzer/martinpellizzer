Pelargonium buysii

Pelargonium buysii, commonly known as the Cape geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The leaves are typically rounded and have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

This species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
