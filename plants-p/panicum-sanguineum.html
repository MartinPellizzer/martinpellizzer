Panicum sanguineum

Panicum sanguineum, commonly known as crimson foxtail, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spike-like inflorescences that range in color from red to purple, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open areas, thriving in full sun and well-drained soil. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Panicum sanguineum is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant, eye-catching display.
