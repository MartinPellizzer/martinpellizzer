Parodia microsperma var. juanensis

Parodia microsperma var. juanensis, commonly known as the Juanensis Cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to southern South America.

It features a dense covering of white to pale blue spines, which help protect it from intense sunlight and herbivores. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a desirable addition to rock gardens and succulent collections.
