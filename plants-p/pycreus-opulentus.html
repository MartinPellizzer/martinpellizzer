Pycreus opulentus

Pycreus opulentus, commonly known as the opulent pycreus, is a unique and rare flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety purple petals that bloom in late summer. The plant has a compact growth habit with lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in specialized botanical gardens.
