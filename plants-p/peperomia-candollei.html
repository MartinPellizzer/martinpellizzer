Peperomia candollei

Peperomia candollei, commonly known as the candollei peperomia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and thick, fleshy leaves that often display shades of green, red, or purple. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia candollei is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal water and moderate temperatures to flourish.
