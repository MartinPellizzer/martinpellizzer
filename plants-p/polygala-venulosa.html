Polygala venulosa

Polygala venulosa, commonly known as the slender milk-vetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. Polygala venulosa is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and coastal dunes.
