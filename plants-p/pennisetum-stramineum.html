Pennisetum stramineum

Pennisetum stramineum, commonly known as giant feather reed, is a tall, grass-like plant native to Africa.

It features slender, arching stems that are often covered in silky, silver-green foliage. The plant produces fluffy, feathery plumes that give it a distinctive and ornamental appearance. It thrives in moist environments and is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic value.

Pennisetum stramineum is also valued for its ability to attract birds and other wildlife.
