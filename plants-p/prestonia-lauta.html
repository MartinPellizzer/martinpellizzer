Prestonia lauta

Prestonia lauta, commonly known as the giant prestonia, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its fruit is a follicle that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Prestonia lauta is often found in humid, lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
