Philodendron standleyi

Philodendron standleyi, commonly known as the silver star philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, waxy leaves that often have a striking silver or white margin, giving it its common name. The plant has a vigorous climbing habit and can reach significant heights if provided with adequate support. Its foliage is evergreen, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are not typically visible above the leaves.

Philodendron standleyi is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
