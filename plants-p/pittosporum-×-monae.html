Pittosporum × monae

Pittosporum × monae, commonly known as the hybrid pittosporum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a slight fragrance. The plant produces white or pale yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its adaptability and attractive foliage make it a versatile plant in both garden and landscape settings.
