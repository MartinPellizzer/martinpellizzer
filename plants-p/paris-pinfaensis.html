Paris pinfaensis

Paris pinfaensis, commonly known as the Chinese water fern, is a rare aquatic fern native to China.

It thrives in freshwater environments, often found in slow-moving streams and ponds. The plant has delicate, floating fronds that are typically divided into small, finger-like segments. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Paris pinfaensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
