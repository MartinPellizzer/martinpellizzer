Plantago ovata f. linearifolia

Plantago ovata f. linearifolia, commonly known as the slender psyllium, is a variant of the common plantain.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. This plant is native to regions of Europe and Asia and thrives in temperate climates. The seeds of Plantago ovata f. linearifolia are often used in traditional medicine and as a source of soluble fiber.

Its botanical features make it distinct from the standard Plantago ovata variety.
