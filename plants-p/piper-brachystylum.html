Piper brachystylum

Piper brachystylum, commonly known as the short-stemmed pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and clusters of tiny, greenish-white flowers. It produces small, round berries that are typically green when unripe and turn red when mature.

This plant is often grown for its ornamental value and for its potential medicinal properties.
