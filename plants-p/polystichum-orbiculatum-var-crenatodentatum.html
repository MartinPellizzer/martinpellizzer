Polystichum orbiculatum var. crenatodentatum

Polystichum orbiculatum var. crenatodentatum, commonly known as the serrated margined wood fern, is a variant of the broad-leaf wood fern.

It is characterized by its large, leathery, orbicular-shaped fronds with a distinctive crenate-dentate margin. The fronds are typically dark green and have a rugged, textured appearance. It is a perennial plant that thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

This fern is valued for its ornamental foliage and is commonly cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
