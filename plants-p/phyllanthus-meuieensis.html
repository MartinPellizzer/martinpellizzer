Phyllanthus meuieensis

Phyllanthus meuieensis, commonly known as the Meuie's spurge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly found in countries like Nigeria and Cameroon. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are typically clustered in cyathia, a unique structure typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
