Polygonum humifusum f. yamatutae

Polygonum humifusum f. yamatutae, commonly known as the Yamatutae knotweed, is a variant of the common knotweed species.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its prostrate growth habit and reddish stems. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptic, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or red, blooming in late summer.

This form is native to Japan and is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks.
