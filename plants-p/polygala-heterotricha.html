Polygala heterotricha

Polygala heterotricha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved milk-vetch, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces yellow or greenish-yellow flowers that are often clustered in spicate inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and linear, typically arranged alternately along the stem.

Polygala heterotricha is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in sandy or rocky soils.
