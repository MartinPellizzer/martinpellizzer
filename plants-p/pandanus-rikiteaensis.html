Pandanus rikiteaensis

Pandanus rikiteaensis, commonly known as the Rikitea pandanus, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to saline and humid environments.

The Rikitea pandanus plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
