Pancratium formosum

Pancratium formosum, commonly known as the yellow rain lily, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant features strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

Pancratium formosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
