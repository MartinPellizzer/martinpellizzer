Pilea moragana

Pilea moragana, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy, resembling small coins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It is a popular indoor plant due to its easy care requirements and attractive foliage.

Pilea moragana thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.
