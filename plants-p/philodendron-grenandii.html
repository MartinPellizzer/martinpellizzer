Philodendron grenandii

Philodendron grenandii, commonly known as the Grenadilla Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to the Caribbean.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by bright red berries that attract birds. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
