Peperomia porriginifera

Peperomia porriginifera, commonly known as the Porrigina Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, glossy, dark green leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale green flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and attractive foliage, it is often used as a decorative plant in shaded or low-light environments.
