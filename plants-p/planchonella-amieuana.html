Planchonella amieuana

Planchonella amieuana, commonly known as the Amieu's hibiscus, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows in coastal and humid lowland areas. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually oval or elliptical in shape. Its flowers are showy, with five petals and often display vibrant colors such as yellow or orange.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, hard seeds.
