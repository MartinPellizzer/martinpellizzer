Philibertia peduncularis

Philibertia peduncularis, commonly known as the slender filbert, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. It typically grows in open grasslands and is adapted to dry, well-drained soils.

Philibertia peduncularis is often found in rocky or sandy habitats and is relatively drought-tolerant.
