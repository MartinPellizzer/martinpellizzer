Phyllostachys funhuaensis

Phyllostachys funhuaensis, commonly known as the Funhua bamboo, is a species native to China.

It is a tall, slender bamboo that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant has long, narrow leaves that are dark green on top and lighter on the underside. Its culms are typically smooth and have a pale yellowish hue.

This bamboo is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
