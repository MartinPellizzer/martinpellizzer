Plectranthus excisus

Plectranthus excisus, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from violet to white. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and long blooming period.

Plectranthus excisus is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
