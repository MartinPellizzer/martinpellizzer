Panicum errabundum

Panicum errabundum, commonly known as wandering grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and feathery, light green foliage. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often wind-pollinated. It thrives in open, sunny habitats such as fields and disturbed soils.

Due to its adaptability, it is often considered a weed in agricultural settings.
