Phreatia retusa

Phreatia retusa, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. The plant has a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients, supporting its growth in humid conditions. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a distinctive lip that aids in pollination.

Phreatia retusa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
