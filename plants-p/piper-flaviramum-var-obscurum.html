Piper flaviramum var. obscurum

Piper flaviramum var. obscurum, commonly known as the dark-flowered pepper plant, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and small, clustered flowers that range from green to reddish in color. It produces small, round fruits that are typically harvested when they turn from yellow to red.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in traditional medicine.
