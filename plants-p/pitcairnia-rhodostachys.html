Pitcairnia rhodostachys

Pitcairnia rhodostachys, commonly known as the red-stemmed pitcairnia, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to the Andes mountains of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru. Pitcairnia rhodostachys is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
