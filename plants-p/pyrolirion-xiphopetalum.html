Pyrolirion xiphopetalum

Pyrolirion xiphopetalum, commonly known as the sword-leaved lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, and it produces striking, showy flowers with reflexed petals. The flowers are usually pink to purple in hue and bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to grow in suitable climates.
