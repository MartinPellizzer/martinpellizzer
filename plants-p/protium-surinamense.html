Protium surinamense

Protium surinamense, commonly known as Surinam chestnut, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood and has been used in traditional medicine.
