Peperomia guayabillosana

Peperomia guayabillosana, commonly known as the Guayabillosana pepper plant, is a species native to Central America.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white in color.

Peperomia guayabillosana is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive foliage.
