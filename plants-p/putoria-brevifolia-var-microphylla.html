Putoria brevifolia var. microphylla

Putoria brevifolia var. microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved putoria, is a variant of the putoria plant characterized by its diminutive foliage.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The leaves are typically broad and ovate, with a smooth texture and a glossy surface. This plant is often found in lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Its botanical features make it a unique subject for horticultural study and conservation efforts.
