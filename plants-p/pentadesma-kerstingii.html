Pentadesma kerstingii

Pentadesma kerstingii, commonly known as the Kersting's pentadesma, is a tropical plant native to West Africa.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 20 meters tall, with a dense canopy and smooth, greyish bark. The leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a pointed tip and a rough texture. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by orange or red berries containing several seeds.

This species is valued for its timber and is often found in moist, lowland forests.
