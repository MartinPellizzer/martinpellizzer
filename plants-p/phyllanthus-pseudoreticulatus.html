Phyllanthus pseudoreticulatus

Phyllanthus pseudoreticulatus, commonly known as the African spurge, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant has small, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences and are often yellow or greenish in color.

The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds.
