Parianella

Parianella, commonly known as the dwarf pomegranate, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, bright red flowers that bloom in spring. Its fruit, though not as well-known as the pomegranate, is edible and has a tart flavor.
