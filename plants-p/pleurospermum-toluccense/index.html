Pleurospermum Toluccense

Pleurospermum toluccense is a lesser-known member of the Asteraceae family that thrives in arid and rocky environments. Native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico, this plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry conditions. Its unique appearance features narrow, blue-gray leaves and small yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. Despite its resilience, it remains underappreciated in both horticulture and ecological studies. Understanding its characteristics and habitat can enhance conservation efforts and botanical knowledge.
