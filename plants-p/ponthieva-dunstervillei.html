Ponthieva dunstervillei

Ponthieva dunstervillei, commonly known as the Bornean arrowroot, is a herbaceous plant native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its broad, arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant produces a spadix inflorescence surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of many aroid species. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Ponthieva dunstervillei is valued for its ornamental appeal and has potential uses in traditional medicine.
