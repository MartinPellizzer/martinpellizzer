Peperomia quaternata var. puberula

Peperomia quaternata var. puberula, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features broad, velvety leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Due to its attractive foliage and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
