Papilionanthe sillemiana

Papilionanthe sillemiana, commonly known as the Sillem's phalaenopsis, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant features long, slender petals and a distinctive, narrow lip that sets it apart from other phalaenopsis species. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

This orchid is popular among growers for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
