Paepalanthus anamariae

Paepalanthus anamariae, commonly known as the Anamaria's grass of paraguay.

It is a species of flowering plant in the family Poaceae, native to Paraguay. The plant is characterized by its tufted growth habit and produces small, spikelet-like inflorescences. It thrives in moist, open habitats and is often found in grasslands and wetlands.
