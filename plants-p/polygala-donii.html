Polygala donii

Polygala donii, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically produces yellow or greenish-yellow blooms that are clustered in racemes. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is not widely grown outside its native habitat.
