Palimbia chrysantha

Palimbia chrysantha, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow and orange flowers, which bloom in clusters during the warm season. The plant has strap-like leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Palimbia chrysantha is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
