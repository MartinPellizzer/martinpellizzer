Piper monteverdense

Piper monteverdense, commonly known as the Monteverde pepper plant, is a flowering vine native to the cloud forests of Costa Rica.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that develop into elongated, berry-like fruits. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly serrated edge, often used in traditional medicine.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
