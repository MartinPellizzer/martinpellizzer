Plantago lorentzii

Plantago lorentzii, commonly known as the Lorentz's plantain, is a species of flowering plant in the family Plantaginaceae.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, temperate environments. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves with a rough, hairy texture. Its small, greenish flowers are arranged in dense, spike-like clusters.

It is often found in meadows, along stream banks, and in woodland edges.
