Prunus cerasus var. humilis

Prunus cerasus var. humilis, commonly known as the dwarf cherry, is a compact variety of the sweet cherry tree.

It is characterized by its small stature, making it ideal for small gardens or container cultivation. The plant produces small, sweet fruits that are typically harvested in early summer. It has a shallow root system and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is also valued for its ornamental appeal, with attractive pinkish-white flowers in spring.
