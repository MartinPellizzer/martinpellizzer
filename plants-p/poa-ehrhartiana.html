Poa ehrhartiana

Poa ehrhartiana, commonly known as smooth meadowgrass, is a cool-season grass native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, pastures, and along stream banks.

Poa ehrhartiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological restoration projects.
