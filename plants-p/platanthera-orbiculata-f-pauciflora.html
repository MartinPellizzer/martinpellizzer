Platanthera orbiculata f. pauciflora

Platanthera orbiculata f. pauciflora, commonly known as the rare orchid with few flowers, is a herbaceous perennial native to specific regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces a small number of pale yellow to greenish flowers, which are often clustered at the tips of the stems. It thrives in moist, calcareous habitats such as meadows and woodlands.

Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
