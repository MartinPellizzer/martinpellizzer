Panicum nitidum var. gracile

Panicum nitidum var. gracile, commonly known as slender hairgrass, is a compact, low-growing grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features fine, hair-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color, giving it a soft, ornamental appearance. The plant produces delicate, fluffy panicles that are typically pink to reddish in hue, adding visual interest. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Due to its small size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and as a ground cover.
