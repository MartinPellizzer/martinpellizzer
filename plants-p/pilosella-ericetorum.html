Pilosella ericetorum

Pilosella ericetorum, commonly known as the alpine hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant native to mountainous regions of Europe.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
