Pavonia burchellii var. schweinfurthii

Pavonia burchellii var. schweinfurthii, commonly known as the redroot false mallow, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. The plant has rough, hairy stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive reddish hue on the underside. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice for gardens in arid climates.
