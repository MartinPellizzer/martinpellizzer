Physostemon melanospermus

Physostemon melanospermus, commonly known as blackseed physostemon, is a perennial herb native to parts of East Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal properties. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and clusters of small, tubular flowers. It produces distinctive black seeds that are often used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and pharmacological studies.
