Persicaria hassegawae f. albiflora

Persicaria hassegawae f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered smartweed, is a herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its distinctive leaf shapes and clusters of small, often brightly colored flowers. The plant features alternate, lanceolate leaves with a reddish sheen and produces white or pale pink flowers in dense, terminal clusters. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
