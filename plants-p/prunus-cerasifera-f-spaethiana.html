Prunus cerasifera f. spaethiana

Prunus cerasifera f. spaethiana, commonly known as the Spaethiana cherry, is a compact, ornamental shrub or small tree native to Europe.

It is a natural hybrid between Prunus cerasifera and Prunus padus, characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and showy, white to pale pink flowers. The plant produces small, dark red fruits that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and landscapes.
