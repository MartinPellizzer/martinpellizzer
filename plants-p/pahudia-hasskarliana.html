Pahudia hasskarliana

Pahudia hasskarliana, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage that gives it its common name. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its unique appearance and hardiness.
