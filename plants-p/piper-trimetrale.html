Piper trimetrale

Piper trimetrale, commonly known as the purple pepper vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. Its small, fragrant flowers are typically greenish-white in color, and it produces small, peppery berries.

The botanical features of Piper trimetrale make it a unique and interesting subject for horticultural study.
