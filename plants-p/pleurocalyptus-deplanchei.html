Pleurocalyptus deplanchei

Pleurocalyptus deplanchei, commonly known as the Mexican fan palm, is a tall, slender palm native to Mexico.

It features a slender trunk with a fibrous, smooth, and often slightly flaky bark. Its leaves are large, fan-shaped, and composed of numerous radiating leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which develop into dark blue, fleshy fruits.

It is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical landscapes.
