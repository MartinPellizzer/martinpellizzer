Populus × yuana

Populus × yuana, commonly known as the Canadian poplar, is a hybrid species within the Populus genus.

It is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types, making it adaptable to different environments. The plant typically features large, heart-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green during the growing season. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, appearing in catkins, which are a common feature in many poplar species.

Populus × yuana is also notable for its role in ecological restoration due to its hardiness and potential for phytoremediation.
