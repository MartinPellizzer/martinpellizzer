Petrocarya excelsa

Petrocarya excelsa, commonly known as the Chinese ironwood, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Hippocastanaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and robust, often twisted trunk. The tree produces showy, yellowish-white flowers in spring, followed by spiny, capsule-like fruits. It is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Petrocarya excelsa is also cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and resilience.
