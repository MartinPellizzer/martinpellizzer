Pericallis steetzii

Pericallis steetzii, commonly known as the Steetz's orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family, characterized by its delicate, fragrant blossoms. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid conditions.

Pericallis steetzii is prized by horticulturists for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
