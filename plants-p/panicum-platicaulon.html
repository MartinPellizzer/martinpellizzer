Panicum platicaulon

Panicum platicaulon, commonly known as the flat-stemmed panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its inflorescence consists of a panicle, which is a branched cluster of spikelets.

Panicum platicaulon is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and ability to stabilize soil.
