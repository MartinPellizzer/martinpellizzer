Peperomia killipii

Peperomia killipii, commonly known as the Killipii peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.
