Peperomia reflexa var. americana

Peperomia reflexa var. americana, commonly known as the American peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Peperomia reflexa var. americana is a popular houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
