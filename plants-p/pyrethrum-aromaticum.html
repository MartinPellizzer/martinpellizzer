Pyrethrum aromaticum

Pyrethrum aromaticum, commonly known as sweet everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like yellow flowers and aromatic, feathery foliage. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils. Its flowers are typically visited by bees and other pollinators, contributing to its ecological value.

Pyrethrum aromaticum is also valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in herbal medicine.
