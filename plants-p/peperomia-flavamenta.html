Peperomia flavamenta

Peperomia flavamenta, commonly known as the golden peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a subtle golden or yellowish tint on the underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for container gardening and as a decorative houseplant.
