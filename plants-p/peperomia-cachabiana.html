Peperomia cachabiana

Peperomia cachabiana, commonly known as the Cachabian peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink, giving it an ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate light, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia cachabiana is valued for its low maintenance and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
