Pelargonium × fragrans

Pelargonium × fragrans, commonly known as the sweet geranium, is a hybrid flowering plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that come in a range of colors including pink, red, and white. The plant typically has rounded, lobed leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Pelargonium × fragrans is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant scent.
