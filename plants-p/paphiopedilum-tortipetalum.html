Paphiopedilum tortipetalum

Paphiopedilum tortipetalum, commonly known as the twisted petal orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, spurred lip that is often curved or twisted, giving it its common name. The plant typically produces a single or few showy flowers with a distinctive coloration, often in shades of pink or white. Its leaves are broad and ovate, arranged in a rosette pattern, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
