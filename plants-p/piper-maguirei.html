Piper maguirei

Piper maguirei, commonly known as the Maguire's pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with slender stems and compound leaves that are often hairy. Its small, greenish-white flowers are borne in clusters and are followed by small, fleshy berries.

Piper maguirei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
