Pilea obscura var. obscura

Pilea obscura var. obscura, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its round, fleshy leaves that resemble small coins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are typically hidden beneath its foliage. It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive appearance.

Pilea obscura var. obscura prefers indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.
