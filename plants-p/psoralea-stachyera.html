Psoralea stachyera

Psoralea stachyera, commonly known as the narrowleaf psoralea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, pea-like flowers that are typically yellow or pale green. The plant has compound leaves with slender, opposite leaflets, and it produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
