Panicum scalarum

Panicum scalarum, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and slender, grass-like leaves that are typically a vibrant green or reddish hue. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that are often used in floral arrangements due to their ornamental appeal. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental and erosion control purposes.

Panicum scalarum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
