Pleurothallis bangii

Pleurothallis bangii, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in the understory of rainforests. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, contributing to its overall ornamental appeal.

Pleurothallis bangii is valued in horticulture for its striking color and relatively easy care requirements.
