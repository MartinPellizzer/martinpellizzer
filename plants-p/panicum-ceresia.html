Panicum ceresia

Panicum ceresia, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and distinctive reddish-purple inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a bluish-green hue.

Panicum ceresia is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
