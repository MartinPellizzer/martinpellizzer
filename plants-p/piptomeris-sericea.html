Piptomeris sericea

Piptomeris sericea, commonly known as silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which gives it its common name, and small, star-shaped white or purple flowers. The plant produces smooth, oval berries that are typically green when immature and turn red when ripe. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils.

Piptomeris sericea is a member of the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and potatoes.
