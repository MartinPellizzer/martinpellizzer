Peperomia fissispica

Peperomia fissispica, commonly known as the Shiny Leaf Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are usually dark green with a glossy texture, often adorned with subtle veining. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Peperomia fissispica is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
