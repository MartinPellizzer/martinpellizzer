Polygala chinensis var. normalis

Polygala chinensis var. normalis, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, fertile soils and is often found in woodland or shrubland habitats. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, and it produces small, bean-like pods that contain seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
