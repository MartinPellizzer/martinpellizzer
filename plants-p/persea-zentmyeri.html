Persea zentmyeri

Persea zentmyeri, commonly known as the Zentmyer's persimmon, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central America, particularly Costa Rica and Panama. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are elliptical and have a glossy surface. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale green, followed by fruits that are similar in appearance to persimmons.
