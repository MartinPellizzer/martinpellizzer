Phyllanthus bacciformis

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are typically yellow or red in color. It is often grown for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
