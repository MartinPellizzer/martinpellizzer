Piper prianamense

Piper prianamense, commonly known as the Prianamense pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in spikes, and it produces small, peppery fruits.

This species is valued for its potential medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
