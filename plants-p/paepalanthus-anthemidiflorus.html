Paepalanthus anthemidiflorus

Paepalanthus anthemidiflorus, commonly known as the yellow star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow flowers that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and wetlands. It has a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers in the spring.
