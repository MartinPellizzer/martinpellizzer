Peperomia acutifolia

Peperomia acutifolia, commonly known as the acute-leaved peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, elongated leaves that are typically dark green with a slight sheen, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot. Its rhizomatous growth habit makes it suitable for both indoor cultivation and as a ground cover in shaded garden settings.

Peperomia acutifolia is appreciated for its ornamental foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor plant enthusiasts.
