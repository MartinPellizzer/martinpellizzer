Psila retamoides

Psila retamoides, commonly known as the silky retama, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub characterized by its slender, often branched stems and small, aromatic leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in the spring and summer seasons. Its foliage is covered in fine hairs, giving it a silky texture.

Psila retamoides is adapted to dry and rocky soils, making it a hardy species suitable for drought-prone environments.
