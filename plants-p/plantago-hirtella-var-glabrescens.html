Plantago hirtella var. glabrescens

Plantago hirtella var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth plantain, is a variant of the common plantain species.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America, typically found in moist to wet habitats such as meadows and wetlands. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, hair-like structures, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters, which are borne on slender stalks.

This variant is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
