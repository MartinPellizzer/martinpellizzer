Paeonia × moutan var. rawesii

Paeonia × moutan var. rawesii, commonly known as the Rawesii peony, is a cultivar of the peony plant native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features compound, bipinnate leaves that are green and slightly hairy. It is a deciduous shrub that can grow up to 1.5 meters tall.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
