Peperomia gruendleri

Peperomia gruendleri, commonly known as the Gruendler's Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or white, which appear during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia gruendleri is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
