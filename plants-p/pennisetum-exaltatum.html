Pennisetum exaltatum

Pennisetum exaltatum, commonly known as fountain grass, is a tall, ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features slender, arching stems that produce fluffy, white or silver plumes during the summer months. The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and is often used in landscaping for its visual appeal. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it adaptable to various climates.

Pennisetum exaltatum is also valued for its ability to attract birds and other wildlife.
