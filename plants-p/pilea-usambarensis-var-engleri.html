Pilea usambarensis var. engleri

Pilea usambarensis var. engleri, commonly known as the Engler's pilea, is a rare and compact houseplant native to East Africa.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a slight variegation. The plant produces small, pale green flowers on slender stems, which are followed by spherical, red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, requiring moderate watering.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a sought-after specimen among plant enthusiasts.
