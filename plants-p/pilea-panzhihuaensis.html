Pilea panzhihuaensis

Pilea panzhihuaensis, commonly known as the Panzhihua stonecrop, is a rare and unique species native to China.

It is a low-growing perennial plant characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its limited distribution and ornamental appeal, it is often cultivated by plant enthusiasts.
