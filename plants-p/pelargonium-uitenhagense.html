Pelargonium uitenhagense

Pelargonium uitenhagense, commonly known as the Uitenhag geranium, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive, lobed leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to red flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is a popular choice among gardeners in mild climates.
