Phillyrea coriacea

Phillyrea coriacea, commonly known as the evergreen privet, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It has leathery, oval-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, followed by smooth, dark blue berries. Phillyrea coriacea is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is drought-tolerant.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in coastal areas.
