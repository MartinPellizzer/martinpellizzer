Pilea ceratocalyx

Pilea ceratocalyx, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a small, compact houseplant native to China.

It features rosettes of rounded, waxy, light green leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it an easy-to-care-for indoor plant.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for plant enthusiasts.
