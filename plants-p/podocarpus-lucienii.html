Podocarpus lucienii

Podocarpus lucienii, commonly known as the Lucien's podocarp, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Podocarpaceae and is characterized by its needle-like leaves and cone-bearing structure. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 20 meters in its natural habitat. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

The wood of Podocarpus lucienii is valued for its durability and is used in construction and furniture making.
