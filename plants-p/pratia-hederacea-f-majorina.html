Pratia hederacea f. majorina

Pratia hederacea f. majorina, commonly known as the giant ivy.

This variant is a large, vigorous climbing plant with heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in diameter. It produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by orange-red berries. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions and thrives in humid, shaded environments.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to cover large surfaces.
