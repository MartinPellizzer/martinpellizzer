Pentzia intermedia

Pentzia intermedia, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant is often found in coastal areas and has adapted to grow in sandy or well-drained soils. It produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind.

Pentzia intermedia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in coastal environments.
