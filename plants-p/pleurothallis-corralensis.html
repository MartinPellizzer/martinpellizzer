Pleurothallis corralensis

Pleurothallis corralensis, commonly known as the Corralensis orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in the cloud forests. Its pseudobulbs are short and thick, supporting the plant's growth in its natural habitat.

Pleurothallis corralensis is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
