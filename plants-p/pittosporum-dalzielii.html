Pittosporum dalzielii

Pittosporum dalzielii, commonly known as the Dalziel's pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features dark green, glossy leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers in clusters, which are followed by bright red berries that are edible for birds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Pittosporum dalzielii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is used in horticulture for its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
