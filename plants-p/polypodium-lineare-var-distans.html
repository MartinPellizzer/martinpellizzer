Polypodium lineare var. distans

Polypodium lineare var. distans, commonly known as the slender cliff fern, is a variant of the common polypody.

It is a fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae, characterized by its delicate, linear fronds. The plant typically grows on rocky surfaces or in shaded, moist environments. Its fronds are usually pale green and have a distinct, narrow shape.

This variety is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
