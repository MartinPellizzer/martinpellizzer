Persoonia abietina

Persoonia abietina, commonly known as the silverleaf persoonia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and coastal areas.

Persoonia abietina is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
