Piper atrorameum

Piper atrorameum, commonly known as blackstem pepper, is a tropical climbing shrub native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn red when mature, and they are used in traditional medicine.

The plant's botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and potential medicinal research.
