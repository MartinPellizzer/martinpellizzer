Phragmipedium czerwiakowianum

Phragmipedium czerwiakowianum, commonly known as the Czerwiakowianum Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit with broad, fleshy leaves that help it thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are often borne in clusters and are adapted for pollination by specific insect species.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated by enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
