Phyteuma orbiculare f. nanum

Phyteuma orbiculare f. nanum, commonly known as the dwarf globe thistle, is a compact perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, globe-shaped blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has silver-gray foliage with a fuzzy texture, and its stems are typically upright and branching. It is adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, often growing in alpine or subalpine habitats.

This form is a naturally occurring dwarf variant of the larger Phyteuma orbiculare species.
