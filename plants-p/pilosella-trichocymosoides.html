Pilosella trichocymosoides

Pilosella trichocymosoides, commonly known as the slenderleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant features hairy stems and narrow, linear leaves, which help it thrive in open, disturbed habitats. Its flowers are typically yellow and appear in clusters, attracting various pollinators.

Pilosella trichocymosoides is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides.
