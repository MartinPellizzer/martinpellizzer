Pittosporum abyssinicum var. angolense

Pittosporum abyssinicum var. angolense, commonly known as the Angolan pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are oval in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by bright red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and is often found in coastal areas or near rocky outcrops.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture.
