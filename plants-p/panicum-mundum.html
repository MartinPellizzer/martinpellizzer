Panicum mundum

Panicum mundum, commonly known as the common panic grass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, hair-like leaves and produces fluffy, seed-bearing spikes that are often wind-dispersed. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is often found in open areas such as fields and roadsides. It is a hardy perennial that thrives in full sun and requires minimal water.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in many agricultural settings.
