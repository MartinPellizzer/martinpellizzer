Platantheroides liguliglossa

Platantheroides liguliglossa, commonly known as the narrow-lipped twayblade, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with a slender, leafless stem that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces a single, narrow, greenish-white flower with a distinctive lip that is shorter than the rest of the flower. It is found in temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere, often in woodlands and along stream banks.
