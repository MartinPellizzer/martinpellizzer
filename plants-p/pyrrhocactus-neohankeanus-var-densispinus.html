Pyrrhocactus neohankeanus var. densispinus

Pyrrhocactus neohankeanus var. densispinus, commonly known as the dense-spined pyrrhocactus, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in dense, sharp spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
