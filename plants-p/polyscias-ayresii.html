Polyscias ayresii

Polyscias ayresii, commonly known as the Australian holly, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Australia.

It features lance-shaped, glossy dark green leaves with prominent veins, and it produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by red berries. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight.

Polyscias ayresii is relatively low maintenance and can tolerate a range of environmental conditions.
