Pennisetum rueppelianum

Pennisetum rueppelianum, commonly known as purple top grass, is a tall, perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its striking purple-tinted leaves and dense, fountain-like growth habit. The plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant color and upright form. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Pennisetum rueppelianum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
