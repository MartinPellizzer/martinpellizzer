Phragmipedium pearcei

Phragmipedium pearcei, commonly known as the Pearl Orchid, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink with a distinctive yellow lip. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil for optimal growth. Its flowers bloom during the late spring to early summer, adding a striking visual element to its habitat.

Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
