Paxiuscula rhizantha

Paxiuscula rhizantha, commonly known as the slender root orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the temperate regions of Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its small, intricate flowers and rhizomatous growth habit. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its roots are specialized for absorbing nutrients from the soil, and it relies on specific mycorrhizal fungi for successful germination.
