Panicum semialatum var. ecklonianum

Panicum semialatum var. ecklonianum, commonly known as Ecklon's panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that are typically pinkish or reddish, adding visual interest to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for xeriscaping.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
