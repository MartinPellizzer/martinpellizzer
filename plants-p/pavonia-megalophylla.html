Pavonia megalophylla

Pavonia megalophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf poinsettia, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful bracts that resemble petals, which are actually modified leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters at the base of the bracts. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
