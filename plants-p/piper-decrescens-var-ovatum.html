Piper decrescens var. ovatum

Piper decrescens var. ovatum, commonly known as the ovate pepper plant, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its ovate-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. Its fruits are peppery and can be used in culinary applications, though it is less commonly cultivated than other pepper varieties.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforest understories.
