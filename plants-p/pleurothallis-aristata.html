Pleurothallis aristata

Pleurothallis aristata, commonly known as the aristate pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, linear leaves and produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. Its flowers are typically arranged in a raceme and bloom during the wet season.

Pleurothallis aristata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
