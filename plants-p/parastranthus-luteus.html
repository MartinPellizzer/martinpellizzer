Parastranthus luteus

Parastranthus luteus, commonly known as the yellow starling plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to southern Africa, where it thrives in rocky and sandy soils. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. It typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter.
