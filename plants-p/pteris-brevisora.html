Pteris brevisora

Pteris brevisora, commonly known as the broad-leafed water fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, frond-like structures. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or near water bodies. Its fronds are usually broad and have a feathery appearance, with a light green color.

Pteris brevisora is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in horticulture.
