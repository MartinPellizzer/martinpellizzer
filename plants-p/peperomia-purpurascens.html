Peperomia purpurascens

Peperomia purpurascens, commonly known as the Purple-leaf Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a striking purple or reddish tint on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or red, followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage makes it a visually appealing addition to any plant collection.
