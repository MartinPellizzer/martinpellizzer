Panicum ramisetum

Panicum ramisetum, commonly known as the slender panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Panicum ramisetum is adapted to full sun and is relatively drought-tolerant.
