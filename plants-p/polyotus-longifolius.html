Polyotus longifolius

Polyotus longifolius, commonly known as the long-leaved polyotus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
