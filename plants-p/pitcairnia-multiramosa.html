Pitcairnia multiramosa

Pitcairnia multiramosa, commonly known as the branched pitcairnia, is a species of bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central tank to collect water. The plant produces striking inflorescences with brightly colored flowers, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and horticultural purposes.
