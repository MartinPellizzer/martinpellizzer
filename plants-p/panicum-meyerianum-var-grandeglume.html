Panicum meyerianum var. grandeglume

Panicum meyerianum var. grandeglume, commonly known as giant panicgrass, is a tall, perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features long, narrow leaves and produces large, spike-like inflorescences with dense clusters of bracts. The plant is characterized by its robust growth habit and ability to thrive in well-drained soils. It is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its striking appearance and resilience.

Botanically, it belongs to the Poaceae family and is valued for its ecological and aesthetic benefits.
