Polygala tenuifolia var. linearis

Polygala tenuifolia var. linearis, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands or along riverbanks. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellowish, and it is known for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.

The species is valued for its potential health benefits and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
