Phlox gooddingii

Phlox gooddingii, commonly known as Goodding's phlox, is a perennial herb native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its showy clusters of pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are evergreen in some regions.

It is valued in native plant gardens for its ornamental beauty and ecological benefits.
