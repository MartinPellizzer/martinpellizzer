Peperomia ellipticorhombea

Peperomia ellipticorhombea, commonly known as the oval-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, elliptical or rhomboid-shaped leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically followed by reddish-brown berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it an excellent ornamental plant for both indoor and outdoor settings.
