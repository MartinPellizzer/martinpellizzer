Platanthera × lassenii

Platanthera × lassenii, commonly known as the Lassen's lady's tresses, is a hybrid orchid native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its dense, white to pale green floral spikes that resemble a tresses of hair. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. It has long, strap-like leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late summer.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
