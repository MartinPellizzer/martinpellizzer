Psidium huanucoense

Psidium huanucoense, commonly known as the Huanuco guava, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the Andean region of South America, specifically Peru. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its fruit, similar to a guava, is edible and has a sweet, aromatic flavor.

Psidium huanucoense is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential medicinal uses.
