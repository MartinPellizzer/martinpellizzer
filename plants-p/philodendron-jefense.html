Philodendron jefense

Philodendron jefense, commonly known as the Jefense philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy green leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant has aerial roots that allow it to climb and attach to surfaces, making it suitable for hanging baskets or shelves. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Its ornamental foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
