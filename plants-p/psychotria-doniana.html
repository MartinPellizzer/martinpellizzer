Psychotria doniana

Psychotria doniana, commonly known as the African morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, with opposite, ovate leaves that are often pubescent. The plant produces striking purple or violet flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the rainy season. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain several seeds.

Psychotria doniana is notable for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
