Phlebochilus brevisurus

Phlebochilus brevisurus, commonly known as the short-tailed orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and green. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often clustered at the base of the plant.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species, making conservation efforts crucial for its survival.
