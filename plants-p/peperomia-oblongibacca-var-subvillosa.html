Peperomia oblongibacca var. subvillosa

Peperomia oblongibacca var. subvillosa, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, giving it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are oblong in shape and often have a slightly undulating margin. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color.

This variety is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance for low light and minimal water requirements.
