Philadelphus subcanus var. wilsonii

Philadelphus subcanus var. wilsonii, commonly known as Wilson's mock orange, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy underside. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value comes from its showy blooms and pleasant scent.
