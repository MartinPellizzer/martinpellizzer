Peperomia amantlanensis

Peperomia amantlanensis, commonly known as the Mexican Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Mexico.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is a popular choice for collectors and home gardeners alike.
