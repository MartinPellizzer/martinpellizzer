Pneumonanthe media

Pneumonanthe media, commonly known as the common lungwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features a rosette of broad, lance-shaped leaves with distinctive white and green markings. The plant produces tall, erect stems bearing clusters of small, pink or purple flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Pneumonanthe media is known for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
