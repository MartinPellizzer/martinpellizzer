Pomaderris queenslandica

Pomaderris queenslandica, commonly known as the Queensland pomaderris, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub native to eastern Australia.

The plant features narrow, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. Pomaderris queenslandica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its hardy, drought-resistant nature.
