Pennisetum prieurii

Pennisetum prieurii, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender culms that are adorned with feathery, purple-tinted inflorescences, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes in temperate climates. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that adds visual interest throughout the growing season.

Pennisetum prieurii is often used in borders, containers, or as a specimen plant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
