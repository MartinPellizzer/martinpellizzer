Parodia zecheri

Parodia zecheri, commonly known as the Zecher's moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to southern Africa.

It is a small, globular plant that typically grows to a height of about 10 to 15 centimeters. The plant features a smooth, pale green to bluish-green stem covered with short, white spines. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in late spring or early summer.

Parodia zecheri is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.
