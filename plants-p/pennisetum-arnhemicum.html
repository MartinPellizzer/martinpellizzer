Pennisetum arnhemicum

Pennisetum arnhemicum, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender culms that are often tinged with purple, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces plume-like inflorescences that add a dramatic visual element to gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a silver-blue hue that enhances its ornamental value.
