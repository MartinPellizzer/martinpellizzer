Pimpinella serra

Pimpinella serra, commonly known as the mountain parsley, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is native to regions of Europe and western Asia, often found in mountainous areas. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves and small white or greenish-yellow flowers arranged in umbels. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
