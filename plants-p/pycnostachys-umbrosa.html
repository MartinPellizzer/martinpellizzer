Pycnostachys umbrosa

Pycnostachys umbrosa, commonly known as the shadowy pycnostachys, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil. Its leaves are broad and leathery, adapting to low-light conditions.

Pycnostachys umbrosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to add a lush, tropical feel to shaded gardens.
