Premna rubroglandulosa

Premna rubroglandulosa, commonly known as red glandular premna, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, including India, Sri Lanka, and parts of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with red glandular dots on the underside, which give it its common name. Its small, tubular flowers are typically pink or purple and are arranged in clusters.

Premna rubroglandulosa is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and thickets.
