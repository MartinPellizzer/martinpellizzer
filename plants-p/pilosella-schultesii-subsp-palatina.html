Pilosella schultesii subsp. palatina

Pilosella schultesii subsp.

palatina, commonly known as the narrowleaf pearly everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets.
