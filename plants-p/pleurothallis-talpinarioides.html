Pleurothallis talpinarioides

Pleurothallis talpinarioides, commonly known as the Talpanarioides orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with intricate patterns, often blooming during the rainy season. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and decaying organic matter in its natural habitat.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and contributes to the biodiversity of its ecosystem.
