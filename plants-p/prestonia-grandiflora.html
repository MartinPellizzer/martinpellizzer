Prestonia grandiflora

Prestonia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered prestonia, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Apocynaceae family, which includes milkweeds and other ornamental plants. The plant is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It produces fleshy, berry-like fruits that contain seeds enclosed in a gelatinous coating.

Prestonia grandiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to grow in warm, humid climates.
