Pleurospermum hookeri var. haidongense

Pleurospermum hookeri var. haidongense, commonly known as the Haidong bellflower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous areas, preferring well-drained soils. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in the summer.
