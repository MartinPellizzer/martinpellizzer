Parsonsia baudouinii, commonly known as the Baudouin's glory flower, is a striking member of the Apocynaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Malaysia and Indonesia. Parsonsia baudouinii is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and unique appearance.
