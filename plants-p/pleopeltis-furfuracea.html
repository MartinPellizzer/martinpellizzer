Pleopeltis furfuracea

Pleopeltis furfuracea, commonly known as the hairy shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, hairy fronds that give it a fuzzy appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce a dense mat of foliage.

The fronds are pinnately compound, with lanceolate or ovate leaflets that are often covered in fine, hairy structures.
