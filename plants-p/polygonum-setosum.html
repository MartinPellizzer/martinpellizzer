Polygonum setosum

Polygonum setosum, commonly known as the hairy knotweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes other species like buckwheat and sorrel. The plant is characterized by its upright, branched stems covered in stiff, hairy structures. Its small, greenish-white flowers appear in clusters and bloom in late summer.

Polygonum setosum is often found in moist, disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
