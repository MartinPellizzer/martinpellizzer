Pedicularis heterophylla

Pedicularis heterophylla, commonly known as the yellow monkey orchid, is a member of the Orobanchaceae family and is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Its flowers are pollinated primarily by bees and other insects, contributing to its ecological significance in its native habitat.
