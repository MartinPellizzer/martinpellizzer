Piper rivialbi

Piper rivialbi, commonly known as the Rivalbi pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with elongated stems that often require support. Its leaves are typically alternate, ovate, and have a glossy green surface.

The small, green berries it produces are edible and have a peppery, spicy flavor.
