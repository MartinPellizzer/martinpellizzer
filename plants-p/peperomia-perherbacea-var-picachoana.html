Peperomia perherbacea var. picachoana

Peperomia perherbacea var. picachoana, commonly known as the Picachoan Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Mexico.

It features dense, rounded foliage with broad, ovate leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value lies primarily in its attractive foliage rather than its flowers.
