Podocarpus neriifolius var. membranaceus

Podocarpus neriifolius var. membranaceus, commonly known as the Chinese podocarp or membranaceous podocarp, is a species of evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often leathery and dark green in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by cone-like structures that contain its seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its timber.
