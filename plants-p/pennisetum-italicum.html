Pennisetum italicum

Pennisetum italicum, commonly known as fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its clumping growth habit and tufted, fountain-like appearance. The plant produces feathery, silver-green plumes that are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Pennisetum italicum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
