Pseudolysimachion schmidtianum f. tomentosum

Pseudolysimachion schmidtianum f. tomentosum, commonly known as the velvety pseudolysimachion, is a unique horticultural form of this rare plant species.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a soft, velvety appearance. The leaves are typically a deep green with a silvery-white underside, creating a striking visual contrast. This form is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and collections.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a versatile addition to shady garden spaces.
