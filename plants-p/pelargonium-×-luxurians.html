Pelargonium × luxurians

Pelargonium × luxurians, commonly known as the royal pelargonium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, velvety, dark green leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that range from pink to red. The plant is native to South Africa and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight to flourish.

Pelargonium × luxurians is also known for its ability to produce numerous blooms throughout the growing season.
