Pleurothallis alopex

Pleurothallis alopex, commonly known as the silver hair orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrate.

Pleurothallis alopex is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
