Paphiopedilum lowii

Paphiopedilum lowii, commonly known as the Sichuan Butterfly Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short, fleshy rhizome and a single, lanceolate leaf. The plant produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive butterfly-shaped lip, which is a key feature of the Paphiopedilum genus. Its flowers are typically pale green to yellow with purple markings, and it blooms in late spring to early summer.

This orchid prefers shady, moist environments and is valued for its unique and elegant appearance.
