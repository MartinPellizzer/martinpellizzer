Peperomia jaliscana

Peperomia jaliscana, commonly known as the Jalisco peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Mexico.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, often with a bluish or grayish hue. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

Peperomia jaliscana is relatively low maintenance and suitable for indoor cultivation.
