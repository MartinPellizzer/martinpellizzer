Piper strigosum

Piper strigosum, commonly known as the hairy pepper vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. Its small, greenish-white flowers are followed by berries that mature to a bright red color.

Piper strigosum is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
