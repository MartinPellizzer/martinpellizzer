Polygonum yokusaianum var. stenophyllum

Polygonum yokusaianum var. stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf smartweed, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks, preferring moist to aquatic environments. The plant features slender, upright stems with alternate, lanceolate leaves that are often tinged with red or purple. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink or red, appearing in late summer to early autumn.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
