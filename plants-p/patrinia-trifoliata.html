Patrinia trifoliata

Patrinia trifoliata, commonly known as Chinese violet or three-leaf violet, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features trifoliate leaves, with each leaflet being ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color, arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Patrinia trifoliata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
