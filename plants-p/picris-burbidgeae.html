Picris burbidgeae

Picris burbidgeae, commonly known as the Burbridge's wormwood, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features upright, branched stems covered in fine, silky hairs. Its leaves are linear to lance-shaped, with a distinctive grayish-white color due to dense pubescence.

The small yellow flowers appear in clusters, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
