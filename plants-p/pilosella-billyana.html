Pilosella billyana

Pilosella billyana, commonly known as the Billyana's goatweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit, with hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the summer months. It is native to parts of Europe and is often found in grassy areas and disturbed soils.

Pilosella billyana is generally considered a weed but has some ornamental value due to its delicate appearance.
