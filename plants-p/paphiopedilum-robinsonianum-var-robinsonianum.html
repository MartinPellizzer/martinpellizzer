Paphiopedilum robinsonianum var. robinsonianum

Paphiopedilum robinsonianum var. robinsonianum, commonly known as the Robust Paphiopedilum, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, spurred flowers in shades of yellow and green, often with a distinctive lip that is broader than the other petals. Its leaves are typically elliptical and arranged in two rows along the stem.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral structure and resilience in various growing conditions.
