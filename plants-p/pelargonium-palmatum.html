Pelargonium palmatum

Pelargonium palmatum, commonly known as the Sowbread plant, is a species of geranium native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its palmate, lobed leaves that resemble a hand, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers during the summer months. It is a perennial shrub that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
