Potentilla wallichiana var. minor

Potentilla wallichiana var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf potentilla, is a low-growing shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has slender, wiry stems and small, elliptical leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in alpine and subalpine environments.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders in horticulture.
