Pennisetum sichuanense var. equidistans

Pennisetum sichuanense var. equidistans, commonly known as the Sichuan bottlebrush grass, is a perennial grass native to China.

It is characterized by its distinctive cylindrical inflorescences that resemble bottles, which are typically bright red or orange in color. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained soils, often found in moist or wetland environments. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping and restoration projects.

The species is also noted for its ability to tolerate partial shade and its potential ecological benefits in stabilizing soil.
