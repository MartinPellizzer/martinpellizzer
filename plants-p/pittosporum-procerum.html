Pittosporum procerum

Pittosporum procerum, commonly known as the slender pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage, which consists of dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, rounded berries. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Pittosporum procerum is also valued for its role in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and resilience.
