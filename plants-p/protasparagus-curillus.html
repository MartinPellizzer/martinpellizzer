Protasparagus curillus

Protasparagus curillus, commonly known as the dwarf asparagus fern, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is a low-growing, evergreen perennial shrub characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage. The plant features small, scale-like leaves that form a compact rosette, and it produces white or pale green flowers in clusters. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
