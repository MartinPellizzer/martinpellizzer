Polystichum talamancanum

Polystichum talamancanum, commonly known as the Talamancan Shield Fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Central America.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery fronds that are typically dark green and have a shield-like shape. The plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. It has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense colonies.

The spores are produced on the undersides of the fronds, contributing to its reproductive strategy in humid climates.
