Pourthiaea villosa var. chingshuiensis

Pourthiaea villosa var. chingshuiensis, commonly known as the Chingshui hairy pincushion, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous regions, preferring well-drained soil and full sun. Its unique appearance and habitat make it a notable species in botanical studies.
