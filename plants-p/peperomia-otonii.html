Peperomia otonii

Peperomia otonii, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to Brazil.

It features glossy, velvety foliage with deep green to purple tones, and its leaves are typically heart-shaped or ovate. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered in spikes, though they are not showy. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate watering.

Peperomia otonii is prized for its ornamental foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
