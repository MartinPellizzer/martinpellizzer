Panicum zizanioides var. angustatum

Panicum zizanioides var. angustatum, commonly known as narrowleaf barnyardgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and panicles of small, greenish-brown flowers. The plant thrives in wet or disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and wetlands. It has a robust root system that allows it to spread rapidly, making it a common weed in many regions.

Its botanical features make it both ecologically significant and a challenge in crop management.
