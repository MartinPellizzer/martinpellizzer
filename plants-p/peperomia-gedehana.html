Peperomia gedehana

Peperomia gedehana, commonly known as the Java Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to Indonesia.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and sometimes pink. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of red or purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and attractive foliage, it is often used as a decorative plant in both home and office settings.
