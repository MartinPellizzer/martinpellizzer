Poa hachadoensis

Poa hachadoensis, commonly known as the Hachado bentgrass, is a species of grass native to the Andes Mountains in South America.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and is often found in high-altitude grasslands. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is adapted to cool, temperate climates. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Poa hachadoensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique ecological adaptations and limited distribution.
