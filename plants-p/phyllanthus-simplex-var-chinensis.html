Phyllanthus simplex var. chinensis

Phyllanthus simplex var. chinensis, commonly known as Chinese phyllanthus, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy surface. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are small, spherical berries that turn red when mature.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
