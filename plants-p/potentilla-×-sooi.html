Potentilla × sooi

Potentilla × sooi, commonly known as the hybrid cinquefoil, is a perennial shrub native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, typically blooming in early summer. Its leaves are typically divided into five to seven lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Potentilla × sooi is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
