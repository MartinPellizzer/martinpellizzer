Perilla

Perilla, commonly known as shiso, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small purple or white flowers. The plant is cultivated for its leaves, which are used in various culinary and medicinal applications. Perilla thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a popular choice for both home gardens and commercial cultivation.
