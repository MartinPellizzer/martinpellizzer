Panicum schlechteri

Panicum schlechteri, commonly known as the Schlechter's panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, two-lobed flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slightly rough texture.
