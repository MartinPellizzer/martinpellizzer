Panicum spretum var. leucothrix

Panicum spretum var. leucothrix, commonly known as silverhair panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed sites such as roadsides and fields. It produces small, fluffy seed heads that are wind-dispersed, aiding in its propagation.

Silverhair panicgrass is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
