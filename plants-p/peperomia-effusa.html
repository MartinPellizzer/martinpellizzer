Peperomia effusa

Peperomia effusa, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its thick, fleshy, and often velvety leaves that are typically green or reddish in color. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white, which are clustered in spikes.

Peperomia effusa is a popular houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
