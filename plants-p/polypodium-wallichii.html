Polypodium wallichii

Polypodium wallichii, commonly known as the Himalayan shield fern, is a member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is a terrestrial fern characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and evergreen fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its fronds are bi-lobed and have a distinctive, shield-like appearance.

Polypodium wallichii is native to the Himalayas and is often found at high altitudes.
