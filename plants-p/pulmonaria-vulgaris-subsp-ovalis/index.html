Pulmonaria Vulgaris Subsp. Ovalis

Pulmonaria vulgaris subsp. ovalis is a striking perennial plant known for its vibrant, bell-shaped flowers. It thrives in woodland and shaded garden environments, preferring moist, well-drained soils. The plant features attractive, heart-shaped leaves that add visual interest throughout the growing season. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among gardeners seeking both beauty and ecological value. This subspecies is a valuable addition to native plant collections and supports local pollinators.
