Pultenaea tenuifolia var. recurvifolia

Pultenaea tenuifolia var. recurvifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pultenaea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features slender, often recurved leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or heathland environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting native wildlife.
