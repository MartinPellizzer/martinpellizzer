Prunus harae

Prunus harae, commonly known as the Hara cherry, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its small, sweet fruits and ornamental flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are elliptical with a smooth texture and a dark green color, while its flowers are white or pale pink.

Prunus harae is valued for both its edible fruit and its aesthetic appeal in gardens and landscapes.
