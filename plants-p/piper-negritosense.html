Piper negritosense

Piper negritosense, commonly known as the Negrito pepper, is a climbing vine native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when mature, and the fruits are used in traditional medicine. The leaves are often used in local remedies for their purported health benefits.

Its botanical structure includes opposite, ovate leaves and small, clustered flowers.
