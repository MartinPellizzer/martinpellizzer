Piper blanchetii

Piper blanchetii, commonly known as the Andean pepper or wild pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, including Peru, Ecuador, and Bolivia. The plant is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of tiny, greenish-white flowers. It produces small, peppery fruits that are used in traditional medicine and cuisine.

Piper blanchetii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its potential medicinal properties.
