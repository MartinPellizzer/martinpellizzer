Peucedanum polyanthum

Peucedanum polyanthum, commonly known as the Chinese cowparley or many-flowered cowparley, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is native to regions of China, Korea, and Japan, where it thrives in temperate climates. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. Its seeds are often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant's aromatic properties are attributed to the presence of volatile oils in its leaves and stems.
