Photinia latouchei

Photinia latouchei, commonly known as the redleaf photinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its striking red foliage, which becomes more vibrant in the autumn season. The plant produces small, white flowers in the spring, followed by red or orange berries that attract birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique leaf color and compact growth habit make it a valuable addition to landscapes.
