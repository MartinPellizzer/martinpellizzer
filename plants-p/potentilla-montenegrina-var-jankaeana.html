Potentilla montenegrina var. jankaeana

Potentilla montenegrina var. jankaeana, commonly known as the Montenegro potentilla, is a shrub native to the Balkan region.

It typically grows in rocky or mountainous areas, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features small, rounded leaves and produces clusters of yellow flowers in the summer. It is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.

Botanically, it belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and ornamental value.
