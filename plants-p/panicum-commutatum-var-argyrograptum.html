Panicum commutatum var. argyrograptum

Panicum commutatum var. argyrograptum, commonly known as silverleaf bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It features narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces fluffy, pale blue to silver-green seed heads that are highly visible during the fall and winter months. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in open woodlands and prairies.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens and erosion control.
