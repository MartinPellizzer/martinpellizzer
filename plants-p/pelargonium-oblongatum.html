Pelargonium oblongatum

Pelargonium oblongatum, commonly known as the oblong leaf geranium, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family, characterized by its upright growth habit and aromatic foliage. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are typically oblong and have a slightly serrated edge, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Pelargonium oblongatum is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.
