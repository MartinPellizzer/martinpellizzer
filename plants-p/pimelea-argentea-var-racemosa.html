Pimelea argentea var. racemosa

Pimelea argentea var. racemosa, commonly known as silverleaf candlebush, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It features narrow, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to native wildlife.
