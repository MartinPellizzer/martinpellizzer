Prostanthera schultzii

Prostanthera schultzii, commonly known as Schultze's mintbush, is a small shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue to purple in color. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils in woodland and heathland environments.

Prostanthera schultzii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
