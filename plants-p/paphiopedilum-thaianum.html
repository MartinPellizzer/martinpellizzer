Paphiopedilum thaianum

Paphiopedilum thaianum, commonly known as the Thai lady's slipper, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a distinctive pouch-shaped lip that is typically yellow or orange with red markings. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It prefers shady, moist environments and has a sympodial growth habit.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
