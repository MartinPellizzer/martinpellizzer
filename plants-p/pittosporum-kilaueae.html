Pittosporum kilaueae

Pittosporum kilaueae, commonly known as Kilauea pittosporum, is a native Hawaiian plant found primarily on the Big Island.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in wet forest habitats. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, woody capsule containing several seeds.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
