Pelargonium × gurneyanum

Pelargonium × gurneyanum, commonly known as the Gurney geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically has rounded, lobed leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Pelargonium × gurneyanum is prized for its compact growth habit and ability to bloom profusely throughout the growing season.
