Phyteuma orbiculare f. minus

Phyteuma orbiculare f. minus, commonly known as the dwarf phyteuma, is a compact variant of the common phyteuma.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically grows to a height of about 20-30 centimeters, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders. Its foliage consists of broad, ovate leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation in well-drained soils.
