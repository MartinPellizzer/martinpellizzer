Poa hengshanica

Poa hengshanica, commonly known as the Hengshan bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and bluish-green foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its inflorescence consists of compact, spike-like clusters of small, two-lobed spikelets.

Poa hengshanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
