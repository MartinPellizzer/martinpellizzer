Phlomis angustissima

Phlomis angustissima, commonly known as narrowleaf plumeless tarragon, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, gray-green leaves and showy, yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It is known for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in herbal teas.

Phlomis angustissima is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
