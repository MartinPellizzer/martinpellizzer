Pectis stella

Pectis stella, commonly known as the star daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy yellow ray flowers surrounding a central disk, giving it a star-like appearance. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Pectis stella is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
