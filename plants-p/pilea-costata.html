Pilea costata

Pilea costata, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Pilea costata is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.
