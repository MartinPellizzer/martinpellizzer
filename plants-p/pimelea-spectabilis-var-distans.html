Pimelea spectabilis var. distans

Pimelea spectabilis var. distans, commonly known as the showy mistflower, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub characterized by its slender, wiry stems and dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and linear, with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and dry inland areas.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
