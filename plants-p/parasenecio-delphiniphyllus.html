Parasenecio delphiniphyllus

Parasenecio delphiniphyllus, commonly known as the delphinium-leaved senecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, upright stems with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters, and bloom during the spring and summer seasons.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
