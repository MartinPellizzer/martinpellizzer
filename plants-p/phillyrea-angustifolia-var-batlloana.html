Phillyrea angustifolia var. batlloana

Phillyrea angustifolia var. batlloana, commonly known as the Batlloana Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features small, elliptic leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, spherical berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for Mediterranean climates.

The variety is noted for its ornamental value and resistance to pests and diseases.
