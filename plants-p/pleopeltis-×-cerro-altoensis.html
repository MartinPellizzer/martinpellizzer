Pleopeltis × cerro-altoensis

Pleopeltis × cerro-altoensis, commonly known as the Cerro Alto fern, is a hybrid species within the Polypodiaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like fronds that are typically divided into narrow, linear segments. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizome is creeping, allowing it to spread and form dense mats.

The leaves are usually evergreen, contributing to its ornamental value in horticultural settings.
