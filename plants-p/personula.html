Personula

Personula, commonly known as the small bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to blue or purple. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight. Personula is native to various regions in Europe and Asia, often found in woodland edges and meadows.

Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem.
