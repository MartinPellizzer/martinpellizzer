Pelargonium × consimile

Pelargonium × consimile, commonly known as the common geranium, is a hybrid plant within the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features palmate leaves with lobed margins, often adorned with hairy surfaces. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Pelargonium × consimile is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
