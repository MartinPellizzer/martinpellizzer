Pennisetum obovatum

Pennisetum obovatum, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and dense, fluffy plumes that turn a striking purple-pink color in the fall. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy on the underside. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Pennisetum obovatum is also valued for its ability to attract birds and other wildlife.
