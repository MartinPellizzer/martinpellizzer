Polemonium molle

Polemonium molle, commonly known as soft bluebell, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in late spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy, and it thrives in moist, woodland habitats. It is part of the Polemoniaceae family, which includes other flowering plants with showy blooms.

Polemonium molle is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
