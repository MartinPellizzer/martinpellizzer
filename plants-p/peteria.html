Peteria

Peteria, commonly known as the spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to Africa, Asia, and Europe.

It is a flowering plant that produces small, white or greenish flowers followed by red berries. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of conditions, making it a popular houseplant.

Peteria is also noted for its air-purifying qualities and ease of care.
