Protasparagus divaricatus

Protasparagus divaricatus, commonly known as the zigzag asparagus, is a climbing shrub native to southern Africa.

It features slender, wiry stems that often twist and turn, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The plant is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
