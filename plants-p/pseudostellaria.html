Pseudostellaria

Pseudostellaria, commonly known as starwort, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and linear, grass-like leaves. The plants typically grow in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Pseudostellaria species are often found in temperate regions of Asia, particularly in China and Japan.

They are valued for their ornamental appeal and are sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
