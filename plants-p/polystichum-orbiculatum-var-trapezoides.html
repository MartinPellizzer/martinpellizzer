Polystichum orbiculatum var. trapezoides

Polystichum orbiculatum var. trapezoides, commonly known as the trapezoid fern, is a variant of the orbiculate wood fern.

It is a perennial evergreen fern that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The fronds are lanceolate to trapezoidal in shape, with a distinctive, glossy dark green color. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit and produces spore-bearing structures on the undersides of its fronds.

It is often found in forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appearance in horticulture.
