Prunus communis lusus compacta

Prunus communis lusus compacta, commonly known as the dwarf cherry tree, is a compact variety of the common cherry species.

It is characterized by its small stature, making it ideal for small gardens or container planting. The plant produces small, sweet fruits that are typically harvested in early summer. Its foliage is dense and provides attractive spring blossoms.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and ease of maintenance.
