Pueraria mirifica

Pueraria mirifica, commonly known as Kwao Krua, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant is notable for its root system, which contains various phytoestrogens and other bioactive compounds. These compounds have been studied for their potential health benefits, including phytoestrogenic properties.

Its botanical features make it an important species in traditional medicine and modern herbal research.
