Paracaryum emiri

Paracaryum emiri, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, serrated leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Paracaryum emiri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
