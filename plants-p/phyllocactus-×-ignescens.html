Phyllocactus × ignescens

Phyllocactus × ignescens, commonly known as the fire cactus, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, spiky stems that can range in color from green to red or orange, depending on light exposure. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight to maintain its coloration.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rockeries.
