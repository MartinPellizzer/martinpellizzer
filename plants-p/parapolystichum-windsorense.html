Parapolystichum windsorense

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that exhibit a striking contrast between the dark green upper surface and the lighter, sometimes silvery underside. Its reproductive structures, including sporangia, are clustered in compact clusters on the undersides of the fronds.
