Peperomia nigrescens

Peperomia nigrescens, commonly known as the Black Nightshade Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dark green, velvety leaves that often turn reddish or almost black when exposed to low light conditions. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and attractive foliage, it is often used as a decorative plant in shaded areas.
