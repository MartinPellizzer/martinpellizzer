Polygonum bistorta var. nitens

Polygonum bistorta var. nitens, commonly known as the slender bistort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks. Its stems are often reddish and covered with fine hairs, while the leaves are lanceolate and have a slightly rough texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
