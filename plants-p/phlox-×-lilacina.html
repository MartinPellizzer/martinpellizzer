Phlox × lilacina

Phlox × lilacina, commonly known as the common phlox, is a hybrid shrub that combines traits from various phlox species.

It typically features showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has upright, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Phlox × lilacina is widely cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
