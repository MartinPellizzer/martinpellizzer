Potentilla verna var. billottii

Potentilla verna var. billottii, commonly known as the spring cinquefoil, is a low-growing perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from April to May. The plant has hairy, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.

This variety is closely related to other members of the Potentilla genus, which are known for their ornamental value and ecological importance in native habitats.
