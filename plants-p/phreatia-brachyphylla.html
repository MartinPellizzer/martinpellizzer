Phreatia brachyphylla

Phreatia brachyphylla, commonly known as the short-leafed phreatia, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. The plant features a pseudobulb and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil, which is characteristic of its habitat.

Phreatia brachyphylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
