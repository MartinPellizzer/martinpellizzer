Panicularia leptostachya

Panicularia leptostachya, commonly known as the slender panicle grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, feathery inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are arranged in elongated panicles, which are often wind-pollinated.
