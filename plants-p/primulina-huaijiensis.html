Primulina huaijiensis

Primulina huaijiensis, commonly known as the Huaijiensis African violet, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple and white, typically blooming in spring. Its leaves are velvety and dark green, often with a waxy texture.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to indoor growing conditions.
