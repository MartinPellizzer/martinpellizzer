Pilea anthotricha

Pilea anthotricha, commonly known as the velvet pilea, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and compact growth habit. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with a smooth texture and a deep green color. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color.

This plant is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental appeal and ease of care.
