Pogoniopsis nidus-avis

Pogoniopsis nidus-avis, commonly known as the bird's nest orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique inflorescence, which resembles a bird's nest, hence its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its flowers are usually small and greenish-white, with a distinct lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns.

Pogoniopsis nidus-avis is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens.
