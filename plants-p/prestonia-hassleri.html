Prestonia hassleri

Prestonia hassleri, commonly known as the Trinidad nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, tropical environments. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually purple or violet in color. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture.

Prestonia hassleri is notable for its nocturnal blooming habit, attracting night-active pollinators.
