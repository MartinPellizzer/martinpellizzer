Punduana volkameriifolia

Punduana volkameriifolia, commonly known as the Volkameria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are often pinnately arranged and have a distinctive, feathery appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, adding ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens.

The species is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
