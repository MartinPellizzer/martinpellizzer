Panicum gladiatum

Panicum gladiatum, commonly known as the swordgrass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit. The plant produces dense clusters of small, spike-like inflorescences that are often green or reddish in color. Its leaves are long, narrow, and typically have a bluish-green hue.

Panicum gladiatum is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to various soil conditions.
