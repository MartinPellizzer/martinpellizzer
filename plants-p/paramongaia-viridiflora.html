Paramongaia viridiflora

Paramongaia viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered orchid, is a rare tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking emerald-green petals and vibrant yellow stamens, which contribute to its distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree trunks and branches in its natural habitat.
