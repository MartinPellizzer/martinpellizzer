Pleurothallis roraimensis

Pleurothallis roraimensis, commonly known as the Roraima pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tepuis of South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its roots are often epiphytic, growing on the trunks of trees in the rainforest canopy.

Due to its limited distribution and unique habitat, it is considered a rare and specialized orchid species.
