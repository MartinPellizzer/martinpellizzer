Parinari pohlii

Parinari pohlii, commonly known as the Pohlii flame tree, is a tropical tree native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to the landscape. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It produces small, round fruits that contain one or two seeds.

Parinari pohlii is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its showy floral display.
