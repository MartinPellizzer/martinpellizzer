Polystachya stodolnyi

Polystachya stodolnyi, commonly known as the Stodolny's spikegrass, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are long and narrow, often arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

Polystachya stodolnyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
