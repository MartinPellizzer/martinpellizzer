Peperomia auberyana

Peperomia auberyana, commonly known as the Shiny Leaf Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia auberyana is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
