Prunus × slavinii

Prunus × slavinii, commonly known as the Slavinii cherry, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Prunus lusitanica and Prunus padus.

It is characterized by its ornamental value, featuring showy pink to white flowers in early spring. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are oval-shaped with a glossy green upper surface and a lighter underside.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
