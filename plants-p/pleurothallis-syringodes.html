Pleurothallis syringodes

Pleurothallis syringodes, commonly known as the orchid bell, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This epiphytic plant typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and often white or pale pink, arranged in a racemose inflorescence.

The plant has a compact growth habit and is appreciated for its ornamental value in horticulture.
