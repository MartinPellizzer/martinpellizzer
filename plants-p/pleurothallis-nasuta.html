Pleurothallis nasuta

Pleurothallis nasuta, commonly known as the nasute orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, epiphytic growth habit. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers that are often white or pale yellow in color. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Pleurothallis nasuta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
