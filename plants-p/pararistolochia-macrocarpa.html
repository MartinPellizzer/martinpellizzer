Pararistolochia macrocarpa

Pararistolochia macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-fruited birthwort, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, aromatic leaves. The plant produces showy, irregularly shaped flowers that are often greenish or brownish in color. Its fruit is a fleshy, berry-like structure that contains several seeds.

However, it is important to note that all parts of this plant contain toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
