Prangos ferulacea var. carinata

Prangos ferulacea var. carinata, commonly known as the Carinated Ferulace, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, branching stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and have a slightly hairy surface.

It is typically found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to thrive in sunny, arid environments.
