Panicum sordidum

Panicum sordidum, commonly known as the dirty panicgrass, is a species of grass native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its coarse, dark green foliage and tufted inflorescences that produce small, spikelet-like flowers. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is often found in open areas such as roadsides and fields. It is drought-tolerant and can thrive in a variety of soil types.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in agricultural and urban environments.
