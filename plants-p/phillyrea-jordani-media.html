Phillyrea jordani-media

Phillyrea jordani-media, commonly known as Jordan's Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. Its flowers are white and clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces small, fleshy fruits that are edible for some bird species.

Phillyrea jordani-media is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in harsh, arid conditions.
