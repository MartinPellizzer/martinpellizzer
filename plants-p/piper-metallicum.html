Piper metallicum

Piper metallicum, commonly known as the metallic pepper or silver vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a member of the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, white or greenish flowers that appear in clusters. Its fruits are small, three-lobed berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

Piper metallicum is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique appearance.
