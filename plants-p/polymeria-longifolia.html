Polymeria longifolia

Polymeria longifolia, commonly known as the longleaf polymeria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The showy yellow flowers appear in clusters and attract various pollinators, contributing to its ecological significance in its native habitat.
