Palisota satabiei

Palisota satabiei, commonly known as the Indian arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and striking inflorescence. The plant typically grows in wet, marshy areas and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizomes are used in traditional medicine and as a starchy food source.
