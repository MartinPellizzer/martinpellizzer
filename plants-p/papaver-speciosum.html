Papaver speciosum

Papaver speciosum, commonly known as the showy poppy, is a perennial flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its striking, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and branching.

Papaver speciosum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
