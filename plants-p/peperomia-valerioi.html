Peperomia valerioi

Peperomia valerioi, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a velvety texture, often adorned with intricate patterns of silver or white. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green or red, which are followed by red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia valerioi is relatively low maintenance and drought-tolerant, ideal for beginners and experienced plant enthusiasts alike.
