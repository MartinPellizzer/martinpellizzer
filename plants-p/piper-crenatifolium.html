Piper crenatifolium

Piper crenatifolium, commonly known as the waxy leaf pepper, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant is characterized by its broad, waxy leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that develop into berries. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the undergrowth of tropical forests.
