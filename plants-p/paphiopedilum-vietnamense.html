Paphiopedilum vietnamense

Paphiopedilum vietnamense, commonly known as the Vietnamese lady's slipper, is a rare and exotic orchid native to Vietnam.

It is characterized by its striking, slipper-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in mountainous regions with rich, well-drained soil. Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat threats, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
