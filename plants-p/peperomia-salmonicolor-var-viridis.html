Peperomia salmonicolor var. viridis

Peperomia salmonicolor var. viridis, commonly known as the green peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions.

It features glossy, dark green, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its low maintenance and attractive foliage make it ideal for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
