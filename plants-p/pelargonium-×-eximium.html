Pelargonium × eximium

Pelargonium × eximium, commonly known as the showy geranium, is a hybrid species within the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red and white, often with distinctive markings. The plant typically has a trailing or bushy growth habit, making it suitable for hanging baskets or containers. Its leaves are usually rounded or heart-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Pelargonium × eximium thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners and indoor plant enthusiasts.
