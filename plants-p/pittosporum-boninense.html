Pittosporum boninense

Pittosporum boninense, commonly known as the Bonin pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to the Bonin Islands.

It belongs to the family Pittosporaceae and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils. Its leaves are elliptical and glossy, while its flowers are white and clustered, often emitting a sweet scent.

Due to its limited natural range, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
