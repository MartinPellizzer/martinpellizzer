Phyteuma orbiculare subsp. ellipticum

Phyteuma orbiculare subsp.

ellipticum, commonly known as the narrowleaf phyteuma, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Europe. It features upright, branching stems that bear lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface.

The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, loamy soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
