Pelargonium renifolium

Pelargonium renifolium, commonly known as the African geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its succulent, rounded leaves that are often tinged with red or purple. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage and ability to thrive in a variety of conditions.

Pelargonium renifolium is often grown in gardens and as a houseplant, requiring well-drained soil and moderate sunlight.
