Primula zhui

Primula zhui, commonly known as the Zhui primrose, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as woodland areas or along stream banks. Its leaves are broad and oval, with a slightly hairy texture, and its flowers come in shades of white, pink, and purple.

Primula zhui is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
