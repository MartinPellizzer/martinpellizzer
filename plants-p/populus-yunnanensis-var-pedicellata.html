Populus yunnanensis var. pedicellata

Populus yunnanensis var. pedicellata, commonly known as the Yunnan poplar with pedicellate flowers, is a variant of the Yunnan poplar native to China.

It is characterized by its distinctive floral structure, with flowers that have elongated pedicels, distinguishing it from other poplar species. The plant is a deciduous tree that typically grows in mountainous regions, preferring moist and well-drained soils. Its leaves are alternate, broadly ovate, and often have a serrated margin.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitat.
