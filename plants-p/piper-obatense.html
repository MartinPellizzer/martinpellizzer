Piper obatense

Piper obatense, commonly known as the Obatense pepper or wild pepper, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, green, and often spiny fruits that mature to a dark red or black color. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly serrated edge.

The plant is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
