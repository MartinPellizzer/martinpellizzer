Psychotria thyrsiflora

Its leaves and roots are often processed to extract these compounds, making it an important species in both ethnobotanical and pharmacological studies.

Psychotria thyrsiflora thrives in humid, shaded environments and plays a role in the complex ecosystems of the Amazon basin.
