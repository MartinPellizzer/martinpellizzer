Phlomis × composita f. almijarensis

Phlomis × composita f. almijarensis, commonly known as the Almijarensis phlomis, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange composite flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It is native to the Iberian Peninsula and thrives in sunny, well-drained soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in Mediterranean climates.
