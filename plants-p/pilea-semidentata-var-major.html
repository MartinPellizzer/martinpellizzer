Pilea semidentata var. major

Pilea semidentata var. major, commonly known as the Shiny Cabbage Plant, is a compact, low-maintenance houseplant native to Southeast Asia.

It features rounded, waxy leaves with a smooth, glossy texture and a vibrant green color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically followed by round, green fruit. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for plant enthusiasts.
