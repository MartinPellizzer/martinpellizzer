Phyllostachys altiligulata

Phyllostachys altiligulata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bamboo, is a species native to China.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright culms and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. This bamboo thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in landscaping and traditional Chinese gardens.

The plant is also known for its rapid growth and ability to form dense clumps.
