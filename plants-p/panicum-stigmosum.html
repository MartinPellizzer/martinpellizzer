Panicum stigmosum

Panicum stigmosum, commonly known as the slender panic grass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands, disturbed soils, and along roadsides. The plant has slender, erect stems with narrow, linear leaves that are often hairy. Its inflorescence consists of a panicle made up of spikelets, which are small, clustered flower clusters.

Panicum stigmosum is known for its adaptability and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects due to its resilience.
