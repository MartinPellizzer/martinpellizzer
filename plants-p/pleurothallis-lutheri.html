Pleurothallis lutheri

Pleurothallis lutheri, commonly known as the Luther's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, intricate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in the understory of forests. Its foliage consists of narrow, strap-like leaves that help it conserve moisture in its natural habitat.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
