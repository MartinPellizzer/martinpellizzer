Pleurothallis carrenoi

Pleurothallis carrenoi, commonly known as the Guatemalan pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, tropical environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique floral structure and relatively low maintenance requirements.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat needs, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
