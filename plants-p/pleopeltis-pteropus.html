Pleopeltis pteropus

Pleopeltis pteropus, commonly known as the broad-leafed tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of large, pinnately compound leaves. The plant belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is often found in humid, shaded environments. Its rhizome system supports the growth of its fronds, which can reach several meters in length.
