Peperomia dependens

Peperomia dependens, commonly known as the hanging peperomia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a compact, trailing plant with thick, fleshy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. The leaves are usually oval to heart-shaped and can grow in a rosette formation. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, and it thrives in bright, indirect light with well-draining soil.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.
