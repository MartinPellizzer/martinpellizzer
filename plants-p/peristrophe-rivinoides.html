Peristrophe rivinoides

Peristrophe rivinoides, commonly known as the river senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.
