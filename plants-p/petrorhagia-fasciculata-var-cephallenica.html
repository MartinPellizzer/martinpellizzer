Petrorhagia fasciculata var. cephallenica

Petrorhagia fasciculata var. cephallenica, commonly known as the Cephallenic sandthrift, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub characterized by its erect stems and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its hardy nature and ornamental flowers make it a valuable addition to xeriscaped environments.
