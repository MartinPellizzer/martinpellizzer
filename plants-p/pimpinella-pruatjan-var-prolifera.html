Pimpinella pruatjan var. prolifera

Pimpinella pruatjan var. prolifera, commonly known as the mountain parsley, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate inflorescences.
