Phlomoides dentosa

Phlomoides dentosa, commonly known as the hairy beardtongue, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and purple to pink flowers that bloom in summer. It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in open woodlands or meadows.

Phlomoides dentosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
