Pimelea esulifolia

Pimelea esulifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved geebung, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in heathlands and open forests, preferring well-drained soils. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

Pimelea esulifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its compact growth and seasonal flowering.
