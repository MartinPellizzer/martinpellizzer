Philodendron rotundatum

Philodendron rotundatum, commonly known as the rounded-leaf philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly glossy surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by round, greenish-yellow berries. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appeal and ease of care.
