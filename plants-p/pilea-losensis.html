Pilea losensis

Pilea losensis, commonly known as the Mexican shield plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Urticaceae.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically shield-shaped, hence its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are borne in clusters. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea losensis is known for its ease of care and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments.
