Pellionia curtisii

Pellionia curtisii, commonly known as the velvetleaf pellionia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. Pellionia curtisii thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ease of cultivation.
