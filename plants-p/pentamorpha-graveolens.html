Pentamorpha graveolens

Pentamorpha graveolens, commonly known as the stinking nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its dark green, ovate leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces violet-black berries that are toxic to humans and animals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
