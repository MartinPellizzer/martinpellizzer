Panicum ambongense

Panicum ambongense, commonly known as the Ambongense grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly found in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets, typical of many grass species.

This plant is adapted to grow in open, sunny habitats and is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks.
