Peperomia alata

Peperomia alata, commonly known as the Shiny Leaf Peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and silver, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for tabletop displays and hanging baskets.
