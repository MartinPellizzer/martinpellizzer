Piper rovirosae

Piper rovirosae, commonly known as the Rovirosa pepper or the Guatemalan pepper, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Piper genus in the family Piperaceae, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, elliptical leaves. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by small, peppery fruits.

This species is often cultivated for its aromatic leaves and fruits, which are used in traditional medicine and cuisine.
