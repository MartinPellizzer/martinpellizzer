Pelargonium acugnaticum

Pelargonium acugnaticum, commonly known as the Cape geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant flowers. The plant features serrated leaves and produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink and red. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Pelargonium acugnaticum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
