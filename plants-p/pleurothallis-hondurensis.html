Pleurothallis hondurensis

Pleurothallis hondurensis, commonly known as the Honduran pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-white or pale yellow blooms that are clustered in racemes. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Pleurothallis hondurensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
