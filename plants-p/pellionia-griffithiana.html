Pellionia griffithiana

Pellionia griffithiana, commonly known as the silver leaf pellionia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive silvery underside, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant.

Pellionia griffithiana is valued for its attractive foliage and ease of care in indoor settings.
