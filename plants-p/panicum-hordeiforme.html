Panicum hordeiforme

Panicum hordeiforme, commonly known as wild barleygrass, is a member of the grass family Poaceae.

It is a perennial plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and along streambanks.

Its botanical characteristics make it adaptable to various environmental conditions.
