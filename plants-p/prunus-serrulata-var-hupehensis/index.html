Prunus Serrulata Var. Hupehensis

Prunus serrulata var. hupehensis is a stunning ornamental tree known for its vibrant pink and white blossoms. Native to China, it is a popular choice for gardens and public spaces due to its beauty and hardiness. This variety of cherry blossom is often mistaken for the more famous Prunus serrulata, but it has distinct characteristics. Its flowers appear in early spring, creating a breathtaking display that attracts pollinators and delights observers. The tree is also valued for its graceful form and seasonal changes, making it a cherished addition to landscapes.
