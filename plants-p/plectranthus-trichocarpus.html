Plectranthus trichocarpus

Plectranthus trichocarpus, commonly known as the purple-leaf plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with a slight purple tinge on the underside. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or pink in color.

Plectranthus trichocarpus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.
