Papaver tenue

Papaver tenue, commonly known as the slender poppy, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, slender stems and small, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant produces capsule-like fruits that contain numerous small, smooth seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in alpine and rocky habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
