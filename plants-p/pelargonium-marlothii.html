Pelargonium marlothii

Pelargonium marlothii, commonly known as the Marloth's geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant produces vibrant red or pink blooms during the spring and summer seasons. Its leaves are typically rounded and covered in fine hairs, giving them a velvety texture.

Pelargonium marlothii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
