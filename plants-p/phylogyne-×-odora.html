Phylogyne × odora

Phylogyne × odora, commonly known as the fragrant hybrid orchid, is a botanical hybrid resulting from the cross between two orchid species.

It is characterized by its showy, colorful flowers that often emit a sweet, aromatic scent. The plant typically features long, slender stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright but indirect light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and floral arrangements.
