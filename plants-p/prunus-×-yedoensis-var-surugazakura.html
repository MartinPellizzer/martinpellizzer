Prunus × yedoensis var. surugazakura

Prunus × yedoensis var. surugazakura, commonly known as the Surugazakura cherry blossom, is a cultivar of the Japanese cherry tree.

It is characterized by its delicate, pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a striking visual effect to gardens and parks. The tree has a compact, rounded form with short, sturdy branches, making it suitable for smaller spaces. Its leaves are dark green and glossy, providing an attractive backdrop during the growing season.

This variety is highly valued for its ornamental beauty and is often planted in urban landscapes for its seasonal floral display.
