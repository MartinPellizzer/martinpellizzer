Plectranthus preussii

Plectranthus preussii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of purple, blue, and white. The leaves are typically velvety and heart-shaped, often with a striking color contrast. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

This plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor conditions.
