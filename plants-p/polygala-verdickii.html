Polygala verdickii

Polygala verdickii, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal and inland grassland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
