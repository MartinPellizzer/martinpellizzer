Pourthiaea Villosa Var. Coreana

Pourthiaea villosa var. coreana is a unique and lesser-known plant species native to southern Korea. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, known for its diverse and often showy flowers. This variant is distinguished by its hairy foliage and compact growth habit, making it a striking addition to gardens. Its flowers, which bloom in shades of yellow and white, attract a variety of pollinators. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated and rarely cultivated outside its native habitat.
