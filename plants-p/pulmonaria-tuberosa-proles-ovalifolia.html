Pulmonaria tuberosa proles ovalifolia

Pulmonaria tuberosa proles ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaf lungwort, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe.

It features basal rosettes of oval, velvety, dark green leaves with distinctive purple markings. The plant produces showy spikes of pink to purple flowers in early spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
