Pseudarthria gyroides

Pseudarthria gyroides, commonly known as the slender legume, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters. Its seed pods are slender and cylindrical, containing several small, kidney-shaped seeds.

Pseudarthria gyroides is often found in grasslands and is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils.
