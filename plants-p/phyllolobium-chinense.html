Phyllolobium chinense

Phyllolobium chinense, commonly known as the Chinese phyllolobium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in woodland and shrubland habitats, preferring well-drained soils. It is often used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and medicinal contexts.
