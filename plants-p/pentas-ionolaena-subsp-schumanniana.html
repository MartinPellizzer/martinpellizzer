Pentas ionolaena subsp. schumanniana

Pentas ionolaena subsp.

schumanniana, commonly known as the Schumann's pentas, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa. It is characterized by its showy, star-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and purple.

The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slightly different flower morphology compared to other pentas species.
