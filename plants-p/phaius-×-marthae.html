Phaius × marthae

Phaius × marthae, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Phaius tankervillii and Phaius flavopurpurea.

It is a herbaceous perennial with large, showy flowers that typically bloom in late summer. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves that are often dark green with a waxy texture. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is native to Southeast Asia.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
