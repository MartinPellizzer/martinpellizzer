Pithecellobium lucidum

Pithecellobium lucidum, commonly known as the glossy pithecellobium, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, showy yellow flowers. The plant produces edible pods that contain small, round seeds, which are often used in local cuisines.

It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
