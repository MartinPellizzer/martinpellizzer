Philagonia sambucina

Philagonia sambucina, commonly known as the purple beauty, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its aromatic foliage and long-lasting blooms.
