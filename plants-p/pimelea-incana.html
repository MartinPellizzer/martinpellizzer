Pimelea incana

Pimelea incana, commonly known as the silverleaf riceflower, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, silver-grey leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its small, yellowish-white flowers appear in late winter to early spring, adding ornamental value.

Pimelea incana is often found in sandy or gravelly soils in heathlands and woodlands.
