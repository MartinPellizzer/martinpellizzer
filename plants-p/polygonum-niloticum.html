Polygonum niloticum

Polygonum niloticum, commonly known as water hemp, is a herbaceous annual plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in dense clusters, which are typically arranged in spikes. It thrives in moist environments and is often found near water bodies or in wetlands.

Polygonum niloticum is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread aggressively, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
