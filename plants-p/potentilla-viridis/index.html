Potentilla Viridis

Potentilla viridis is a small, evergreen shrub known for its vibrant yellow flowers and hardy nature. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. This plant is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in harsh conditions. Its compact size makes it ideal for rock gardens or as a border plant. With its resilience and beauty, Potentilla viridis is a favorite among gardeners seeking low-maintenance, attractive flora.
