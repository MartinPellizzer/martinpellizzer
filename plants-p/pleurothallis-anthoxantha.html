Pleurothallis anthoxantha

Pleurothallis anthoxantha, commonly known as the golden pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. Its flowers are often adorned with intricate patterns and are highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a challenging plant to cultivate outside its natural range.
