Polygala magna

Polygala magna, commonly known as the giant milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features pinnately compound leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemes. Its root system is extensive and fibrous, allowing it to thrive in well-drained soils.

The plant is notable for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
