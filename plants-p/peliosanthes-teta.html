Peliosanthes teta

Peliosanthes teta, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow and orange floral displays, which resemble flames, making it a visually captivating plant. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are borne on slender stems and are known for their strong, sweet fragrance.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly valued in horticultural collections.
