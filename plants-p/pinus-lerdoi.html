Pinus lerdoi

Pinus lerdoi, commonly known as the Lerdo pine, is a species of evergreen conifer native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and dense, needle-like foliage. The tree produces cones that are typically reddish-brown in color and contain edible seeds. It thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil.

The Lerdo pine is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental and ecological significance.
