Prunus Serrulata Luteoides

Prunus serrulata luteoides is a rare and striking deciduous shrub native to Japan. Known for its vibrant yellow flowers, it stands out among the more common cherry blossom varieties. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a unique addition to gardens. Its blooming period typically occurs in early spring, adding a splash of color to the landscape. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure compared to its more famous relatives.
