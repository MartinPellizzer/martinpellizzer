Polyspora concentricicatrix

Polyspora concentricicatrix, commonly known as the concentric spore plant, is a unique species characterized by its intricate spore structure.

Its spores are arranged in concentric layers, forming a visually striking pattern that aids in identification. The plant belongs to the fungal kingdom and exhibits a specialized reproductive system. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest floors or damp soil.

Botanically, it is notable for its complex spore morphology and potential ecological roles in nutrient cycling.
