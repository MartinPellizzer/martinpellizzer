Pinus palmieri

Pinus palmieri, commonly known as the palmieri pine, is a species of conifer native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its slender, often twisted trunk and its dense, feather-like foliage. The plant produces small, pale yellow cones that mature over several years. Its leaves are needle-like, typically arranged in bundles of five, and they remain on the tree for several years before falling.

Pinus palmieri is adapted to coastal and limestone soils, making it a hardy species in tropical environments.
