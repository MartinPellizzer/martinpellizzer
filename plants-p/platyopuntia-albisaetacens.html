Platyopuntia albisaetacens

Platyopuntia albisaetacens, commonly known as the white-haired flat cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its flattened, ribbed stems that are covered in dense, white to pale green hair. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are typically clustered at the ends of the stems. It is a succulent plant, adapted to store water in its fleshy stems.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated in dry gardens and rockeries.
