Polycarpaea rheophytica

Polycarpaea rheophytica, commonly known as the stream orchid, is a rare and specialized plant adapted to grow in fast-flowing mountain streams.

The plant has slender, strap-shaped leaves that help reduce water resistance in its aquatic environment. It thrives in cool, shaded, and nutrient-rich stream beds, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
