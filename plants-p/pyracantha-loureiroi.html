Pyracantha loureiroi

Pyracantha loureiroi, commonly known as the yellow-fruited firethorn, is a shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces bright orange to yellow berries that remain on the plant throughout the winter. Its leaves are dark green and glossy, providing ornamental value in gardens.

Pyracantha loureiroi is often cultivated for its ornamental fruits and winter interest.
