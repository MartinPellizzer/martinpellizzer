Pleurothallis bradeorum

Pleurothallis bradeorum, commonly known as the Brade's orchid, is a species of orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. This epiphytic plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. It produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and green, often with intricate patterns.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
