Platanthera bifolia subsp. bifolia

Platanthera bifolia subsp.

bifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved twayblade, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, greenish-white flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and meadows. It has a symbiotic relationship with mycorrhizal fungi, which aid in nutrient uptake.
