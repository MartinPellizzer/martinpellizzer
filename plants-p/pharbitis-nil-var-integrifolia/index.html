Pharbitis Nil Var. Integrifolia

Pharbitis nil var. integrifolia is a striking climbing plant known for its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers. Native to China, it thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces deep purple or red blooms that attract pollinators. It is a popular choice for vertical gardening and can be grown in containers or trellises. Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a favorite among gardeners and plant enthusiasts.
