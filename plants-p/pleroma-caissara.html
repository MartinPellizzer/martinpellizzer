Pleroma caissara

Pleroma caissara, commonly known as the pink orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking pink and white flowers, which bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Pleroma caissara is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
