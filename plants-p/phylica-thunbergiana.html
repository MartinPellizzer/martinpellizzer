Phylica thunbergiana

Phylica thunbergiana, commonly known as the silver tree, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and produces small, red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy leaves. It is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental appearance.

Phylica thunbergiana is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in dry regions.
