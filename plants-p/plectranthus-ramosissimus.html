Plectranthus ramosissimus

Plectranthus ramosissimus, commonly known as the purple heart plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and its distinctive purple-pink tubular flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are typically oval-shaped and have a slightly fuzzy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

This plant is also valued for its aromatic foliage and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
