Psittacanthus stenanthus

Psittacanthus stenanthus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spiny tree fern, is a member of the Cyatheaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a tall, unbranched trunk covered in a fibrous sheath, with fronds that are often spiny at the base. Its leaves are typically narrow and elongated, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This fern is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
