Persicaria posumbu subsp. posumbu

Persicaria posumbu subsp.

posumbu, commonly known as swamp smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to wetland environments in Asia. It typically grows in marshy areas and along the edges of ponds, thriving in moist to saturated soils.

The plant features upright stems that are often reddish in color, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or red, appearing in late summer to early autumn.

The species is valued for its ability to stabilize soil in wetland habitats and for its ornamental appeal in water garden settings.
