Psydrax acutiflorus

Psydrax acutiflorus, commonly known as the narrow-flowered psidium, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of white, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red when ripe. It is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and ornamental gardening.
