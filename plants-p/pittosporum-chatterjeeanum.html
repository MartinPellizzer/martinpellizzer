Pittosporum chatterjeeanum

Pittosporum chatterjeeanum, commonly known as the Chatterjee's pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage and fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are leathery, elliptical, and have a distinctive aromatic scent.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in moist, shaded environments within its native habitat.
