Polypodium celebicum

Polypodium celebicum, commonly known as the Celebes polypody, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is a small, terrestrial fern that typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. The plant features a rhizomatous stem with clustered fronds that are usually lanceolate in shape. Its sporangia are borne on the undersides of the fronds, and it reproduces via spores rather than seeds.

This species is native to Southeast Asia, including regions of Indonesia and the Philippines.
