Phyllostachys bambusoides f. zitchiku

Phyllostachys bambusoides f. zitchiku, commonly known as the Zitaku bamboo, is a compact, clump-forming grass native to China.

It features narrow, dark green leaves that are slightly curled and covered in a fine, silvery-white pubescence. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in panicles during the summer months. It is highly ornamental and thrives in well-drained soil with partial to full sun.

This bamboo is prized for its unique appearance and is often used in ornamental gardening and landscape design.
