Philodendron ruizii

Philodendron ruizii, commonly known as the Emerald Queen, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are deep green and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Philodendron ruizii is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
