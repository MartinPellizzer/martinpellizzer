Phyllanthus orbiculatus

Phyllanthus orbiculatus, commonly known as the globe spurge, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its rounded, fleshy leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant produces distinctive, bead-like fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and wastelands.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural studies.
