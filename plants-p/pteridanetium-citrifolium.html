Pteridanetium citrifolium

Pteridanetium citrifolium, commonly known as the citronleaf fern, is a tropical plant characterized by its large, frond-like leaves that resemble those of a fern.

It thrives in humid environments and requires indirect sunlight to flourish. The plant's leaves are typically a vibrant green, with a slightly serrated edge. Its unique structure allows it to absorb moisture efficiently, making it well-suited for moist, shaded areas.

Pteridanetium citrifolium is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
