Piper vellosoi

Piper vellosoi, commonly known as the Vellosoi pepper, is a tropical climbing plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when ripe, containing aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are often used in local remedies for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
