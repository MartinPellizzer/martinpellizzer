Persicaria thunbergii var. hastatotriloba

Persicaria thunbergii var. hastatotriloba, commonly known as the broad-leafed knotweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves with a distinctive three-lobed base, giving it a unique foliage appearance. The plant produces small, clustered pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
