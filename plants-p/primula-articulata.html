Primula articulata

Primula articulata, commonly known as the Chinese primrose, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its clustered, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are usually broad and oval, with a slightly hairy texture.

Primula articulata is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its role in traditional herbal medicine.
