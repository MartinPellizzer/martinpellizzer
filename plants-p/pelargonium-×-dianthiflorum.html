Pelargonium × dianthiflorum

Pelargonium × dianthiflorum, commonly known as the Christmas rose, is a hybrid plant that belongs to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, white or pink flowers that resemble roses. The plant is native to South Africa and is typically cultivated as a decorative shrub. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Pelargonium × dianthiflorum is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for.
