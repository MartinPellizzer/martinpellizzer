Prenanthes purpurea

Prenanthes purpurea, commonly known as purple ray flover, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking purple spathe with a yellow spadix. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wetlands. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a rough texture, while its flowers are short-lived but attract various pollinators.

Prenanthes purpurea is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
