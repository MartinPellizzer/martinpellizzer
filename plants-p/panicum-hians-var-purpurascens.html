Panicum hians var. purpurascens

Panicum hians var. purpurascens, commonly known as purple lovegrass, is a variant of the panicum genus native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a perennial grass characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, tufted foliage. The plant produces fluffy, purple-tinted inflorescences that are visually striking and often used in ornamental landscaping. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that adds a refined appearance to gardens.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for low-maintenance, drought-tolerant gardens.
