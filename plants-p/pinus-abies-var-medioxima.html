Pinus abies var. medioxima

Pinus abies var. medioxima, commonly known as the Swiss stone pine, is a variant of the Austrian pine.

It is native to the Alps and is characterized by its slender, conical shape and dark green needles. The tree produces cones that are typically reddish-brown and contain edible seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to temperate climates.

This variety is known for its slow growth and long lifespan, making it a valuable species in horticulture and forestry.
