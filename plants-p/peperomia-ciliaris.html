Peperomia ciliaris

Peperomia ciliaris, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and dark green coloration. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, followed by spherical, spiny fruits. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Peperomia ciliaris is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
