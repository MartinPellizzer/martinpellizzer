Phillyrea latifolia subsp. daninii

Phillyrea latifolia subsp.

daninii, commonly known as the Mediterranean buckthorn, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage with small, glossy leaves.

The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, dark berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its botanical features make it an important species in Mediterranean ecosystems.
