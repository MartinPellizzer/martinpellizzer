Piper cambessedesii

Piper cambessedesii, commonly known as the Cambessedes pepper, is a flowering plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes over 3,000 species of flowering plants. The plant produces small, yellow flowers and has dark green, heart-shaped leaves. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

The plant is valued for its aromatic leaves and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
