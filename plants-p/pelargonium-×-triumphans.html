Pelargonium × triumphans

Pelargonium × triumphans, commonly known as the zonal geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a wide range of colors, including red, pink, and purple. The leaves are typically rounded with distinct color zones, giving the plant its common name. Native to South Africa, it thrives in full sun and well-draining soil.

This plant is widely cultivated as a decorative ornamental due to its hardiness and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
