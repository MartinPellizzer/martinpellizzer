Pseuderanthemum tuberculatum

Pseuderanthemum tuberculatum, commonly known as the tuberculate pseuderanthemum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and sometimes hairy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Pseuderanthemum tuberculatum is valued in horticulture for its vibrant blooms and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
