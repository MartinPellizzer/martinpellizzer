Phalaenopsis stobartiana

Phalaenopsis stobartiana, commonly known as the Stobart's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically blooms once a year, producing multiple flower spikes that can last for several months. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining potting media, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
