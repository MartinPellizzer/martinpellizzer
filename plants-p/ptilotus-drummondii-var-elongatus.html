Ptilotus drummondii var. elongatus

Ptilotus drummondii var. elongatus, commonly known as the long-stemmed spinifex, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, spiny stems and small, clustered flowers.
