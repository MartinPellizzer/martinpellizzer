Pleurothallis oscitans

Pleurothallis oscitans, commonly known as the silent pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, narrow leaves and produces small, white or pale green flowers with a subtle fragrance. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its relatively simple floral structure.

Due to its habitat requirements, it is often difficult to cultivate outside of its natural environment.
