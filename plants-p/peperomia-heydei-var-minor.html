Peperomia heydei var. minor

Peperomia heydei var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features small, waxy, ovate leaves that are typically a vibrant green, sometimes with a reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or red, adding subtle interest to its appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for tabletop displays and decorative plant arrangements.
