Panicum avenaceum

Panicum avenaceum, commonly known as millet grass, is a flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. The plant is characterized by its tufted growth habit and spikelet-based inflorescence. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Panicum avenaceum is often used in ornamental gardening and has some traditional uses in local cultures.
