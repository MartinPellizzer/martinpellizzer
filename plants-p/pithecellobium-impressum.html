Pithecellobium impressum

Pithecellobium impressum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf burr legume, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive seed pods. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are often clustered in dense inflorescences. Its seeds are encased in thick, woody pods that can remain on the plant for extended periods.

Pithecellobium impressum is adapted to grow in a variety of soil types but thrives in well-drained, sunny environments.
