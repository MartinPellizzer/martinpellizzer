Philibertia albiflora

Philibertia albiflora, commonly known as the whiteflower filbert, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white flowers that bloom in early spring. Its botanical classification places it within the family Asteraceae, also known as the daisy family.

Philibertia albiflora is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to thrive in temperate climates.
