Piper yaracuyense

Piper yaracuyense, commonly known as Yaracuy pepper, is a flowering plant native to South America, specifically Venezuela.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into elongated, thin fruits.

The fruits are often used in traditional medicine and for their aromatic properties.
