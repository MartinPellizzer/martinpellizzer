Panicum meneri

Panicum meneri, commonly known as the red panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to Africa, particularly South Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and reddish-purple inflorescence. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, while the seed heads are often clustered at the tips of the stems.

Panicum meneri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive appearance.
