Phlomis kawaguchii

Phlomis kawaguchii, commonly known as Kawaguchii phlomis, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features tall, upright stems covered in soft, hairy leaves that are often gray-green in color. Its flowers are typically pink or purple, arranged in dense spikes at the top of the stems.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
