Pilosocereus albisummus

Pilosocereus albisummus, commonly known as the white summery cactus, is a species of cactus native to southern Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stems that are covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces large, showy white flowers that bloom at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

Pilosocereus albisummus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
