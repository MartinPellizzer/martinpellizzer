Philodendron antonioanum

Philodendron antonioanum, commonly known as the Antonio's Philodendron, is a climbing vine native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity.

Due to its attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
