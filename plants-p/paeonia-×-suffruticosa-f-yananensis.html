Paeonia × suffruticosa f. yananensis

Paeonia × suffruticosa f. yananensis, commonly known as the Yenan peony, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and produces large, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has thick, woody stems and heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It blooms in late spring to early summer, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Its botanical features make it well-suited for cultivation in temperate climates.
