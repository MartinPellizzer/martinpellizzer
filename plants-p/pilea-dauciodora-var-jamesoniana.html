Pilea dauciodora var. jamesoniana

Pilea dauciodora var. jamesoniana, commonly known as the Jameson's Pilea, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are followed by spherical, red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and manageable size make it an attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
