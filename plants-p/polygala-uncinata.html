Polygala uncinata

Polygala uncinata, commonly known as the hooked polygala, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its unique, spiraling inflorescence. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

Polygala uncinata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
