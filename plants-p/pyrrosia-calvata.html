Pyrrosia calvata

Pyrrosia calvata, commonly known as the smooth shield fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees and rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has leathery, evergreen fronds that are usually dark green and glossy. Its spore-producing structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the leaves.

Pyrrosia calvata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in terrariums and shaded gardens.
