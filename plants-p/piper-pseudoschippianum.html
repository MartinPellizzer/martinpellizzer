Piper pseudoschippianum

Piper pseudoschippianum, commonly known as the false schippianum pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves with opposite arrangement. Its small, greenish-white flowers are followed by small, berry-like fruits.

It is often found in humid, shaded environments within the understory of tropical forests.
