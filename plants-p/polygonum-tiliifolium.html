Polygonum tiliifolium

Polygonum tiliifolium, commonly known as soft-stemmed smartweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist or wet environments such as wetlands, ditches, and stream banks. The plant features upright stems that are often covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a smooth or slightly toothed margin. It produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or red, which bloom in late summer.

The plant is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and is often found in areas with high water content.
