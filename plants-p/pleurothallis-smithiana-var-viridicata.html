Pleurothallis smithiana var. viridicata

Pleurothallis smithiana var. viridicata, commonly known as the Emerald Lady Orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers with a subtle fragrance. Its unique coloration and compact size make it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often grown in controlled conditions to ensure its survival.
