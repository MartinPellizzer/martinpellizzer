Phyllanthus reticulatus var. orae-solis

Phyllanthus reticulatus var. orae-solis, commonly known as the sun's edge phyllanthus, is a flowering plant native to regions of South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical forests. The plant features small, clustered leaves with a distinctive reticulate venation pattern, which is a key identifying characteristic. Its flowers are usually greenish-white and clustered in small inflorescences, contributing to its ornamental value.

The species is noted for its resilience and adaptability to varying soil conditions, making it a popular choice for horticultural use.
