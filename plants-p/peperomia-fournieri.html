Peperomia fournieri

Peperomia fournieri, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically silver or grayish-white with a waxy texture, which helps it retain moisture in its natural habitat. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color, often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia fournieri is relatively low-maintenance, requiring minimal watering and tolerating a range of indoor conditions.
