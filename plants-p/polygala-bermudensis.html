Polygala bermudensis

Polygala bermudensis, commonly known as Bermuda mistflower, is a flowering plant native to Bermuda.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, often found in dunes or open habitats. It has small, narrow leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Due to its limited range and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
