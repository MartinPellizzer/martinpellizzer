Pyrethrum atkinsonii

Pyrethrum atkinsonii, commonly known as the Atkinson's pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant produces pyrethrins, natural insecticides used in organic pest control. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value and ecological benefits make it a desirable addition to sustainable landscapes.
