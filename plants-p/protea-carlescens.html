Protea carlescens

Protea carlescens, commonly known as the Silver Curly Protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence composed of numerous white to pinkish flowers arranged in a spherical shape. The plant is native to the Western and Eastern Cape provinces of South Africa, where it thrives in coastal fynbos vegetation. It prefers well-drained, sandy soils and full sun exposure, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often used in floral arrangements and as a landscape plant.
