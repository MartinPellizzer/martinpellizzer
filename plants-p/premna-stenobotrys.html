Premna stenobotrys

Premna stenobotrys, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Premna, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, including India, China, and Southeast Asia. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
