Pennisetum alopecuroides f. purpurascens

Pennisetum alopecuroides f. purpurascens, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a ornamental grass species native to Africa.

It features slender, arching stems that produce fluffy, purple-tinted plumes during the flowering season. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green with a slight blue-gray hue. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping and garden borders.

Its striking appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
