Peperomia terraegaudens

Peperomia terraegaudens, commonly known as the Terraegaudens peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
