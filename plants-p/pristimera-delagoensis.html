Pristimera delagoensis

Pristimera delagoensis, commonly known as the Delagoa orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

Pristimera delagoensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and contributes to the biodiversity of its native habitat.
