Piper amalago var. medium

Piper amalago var. medium, commonly known as the medium pepper plant, is a variant of the Piper amalago species, which belongs to the Piperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing vines and small, heart-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces tiny, clustered fruits that are often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires support for its vine growth.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant in both horticultural and medicinal contexts.
