Plectranthus mannii

Plectranthus mannii, commonly known as the silver velvet plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fuzzy, silver-green leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale purple or violet in color. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.

Plectranthus mannii prefers well-draining soil and partial to full sunlight.
