Pinalia glabra

Pinalia glabra, commonly known as the smooth pinalia, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, slender stems that are smooth and often slightly hairy. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped, with a slightly serrated edge, and are arranged alternately along the stem.

Pinalia glabra is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
