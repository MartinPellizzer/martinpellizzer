Psydrax mutimushii subsp. mutimushii

Psydrax mutimushii subsp.

mutimushii, commonly known as the African star gooseberry, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its small, yellowish-green berries that are often clustered together.

The plant has simple, alternate leaves with a rough texture and a slightly aromatic scent. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance.
