Polygala violacea var. robusta

Polygala violacea var. robusta, commonly known as the robust polygala, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and woodlands. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, violet to purple flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits and is also cultivated for ornamental purposes.
