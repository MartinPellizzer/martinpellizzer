Phyteuma orbiculare var. angustifolium

Phyteuma orbiculare var. angustifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved phyteuma, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features upright, branching stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, yellowish-orange flowers. The leaves are narrow and lanceolate, with a hairy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
