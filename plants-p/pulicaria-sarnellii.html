Pulicaria sarnellii

Pulicaria sarnellii, commonly known as Sarnian groundsel, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, gray-green foliage and small yellow flowers. The plant thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates. It produces tiny, wind-dispersed seeds, contributing to its ability to colonize disturbed areas.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
