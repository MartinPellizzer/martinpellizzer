Pleurothallis chiquindensis

Pleurothallis chiquindensis, commonly known as the Chiquinze orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-white or pale yellow blooms with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
