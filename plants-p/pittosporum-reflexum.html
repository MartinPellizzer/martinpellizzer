Pittosporum reflexum

Pittosporum reflexum, commonly known as the coastal pittosporum, is a deciduous shrub native to New Zealand.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are ovate and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters, which are followed by bright orange berries. It thrives in coastal environments and is adapted to tolerate salt spray and strong winds.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for coastal gardens and landscaping.
