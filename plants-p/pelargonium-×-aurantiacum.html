Pelargonium × aurantiacum

Pelargonium × aurantiacum, commonly known as the scarlet geranium, is a hybrid species within the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers and serrated, dark green leaves. Native to South Africa, this plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a potted plant.

The flowers bloom in summer, producing a showy display that attracts pollinators like bees and butterflies.
