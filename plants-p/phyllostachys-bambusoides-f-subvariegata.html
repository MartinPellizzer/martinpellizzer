Phyllostachys bambusoides f. subvariegata

Phyllostachys bambusoides f. subvariegata, commonly known as the variegated bamboo, is a striking ornamental bamboo species characterized by its distinctive variegated foliage.

The leaves exhibit a combination of white, yellow, and green hues, creating a visually appealing contrast against the dark green stems. This plant is a compact, clump-forming bamboo that grows slowly, making it ideal for small garden spaces or container planting. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, requiring minimal maintenance once established.

Its unique coloration makes it a popular choice for adding texture and interest to landscape designs.
