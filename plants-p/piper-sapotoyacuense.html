Piper sapotoyacuense

Piper sapotoyacuense, commonly known as the Sapotoyacuense pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the Amazon rainforest, specifically found in regions of Peru and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its slender, vine-like structure and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its fruits are berries that turn from green to dark purple as they mature.

The plant is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and potential medicinal properties.
