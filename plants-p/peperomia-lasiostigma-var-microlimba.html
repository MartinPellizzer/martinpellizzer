Peperomia lasiostigma var. microlimba

Peperomia lasiostigma var. microlimba, commonly known as the narrow-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale green to reddish flowers, which are often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate moisture.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
