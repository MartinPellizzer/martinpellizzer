Peperomia lenticularis

Peperomia lenticularis, commonly known as the lens-shaped peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval or heart-shaped, often with a glossy appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to avoid root rot.

Peperomia lenticularis is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
