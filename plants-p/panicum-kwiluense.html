Panicum kwiluense

Panicum kwiluense, commonly known as the Kwiluense grass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Africa.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats. The plant features slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green hue. Its flowering spikes are usually slender and produce small, spikelet-based flowers.

Panicum kwiluense is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential ecological benefits in supporting local wildlife.
