Pilosella cymosa var. gottlandica

Pilosella cymosa var. gottlandica, commonly known as the Gotland hawk's-beard, is a perennial herb native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, grayish-green leaves and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has a compact growth habit with a central stem and several flowering stems emerging from the base.

The variety gottlandica is distinguished by its specific geographic distribution and subtle morphological differences from the typical form.
