Pterosenecio grandis

Pterosenecio grandis, commonly known as the giant pterosenecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features tall, sturdy stems with lance-shaped, hairy leaves that are arranged alternately. Its showy yellow flowers are typically grouped in clusters, attracting various pollinators.

Pterosenecio grandis is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in full sun conditions.
