Pallenis spinosa subsp. asteroidea

Pallenis spinosa subsp.

asteroidea, commonly known as the star-shaped spurge, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its spiny, fleshy stems and leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in star-like formations, giving it its common name. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, preferring well-drained soils.

Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
