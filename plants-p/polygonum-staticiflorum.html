Polygonum staticiflorum

Polygonum staticiflorum, commonly known as the water plantain, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland environments.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its upright stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist soils and is often found in marshes, ponds, and along stream banks.

Polygonum staticiflorum is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
