Pilosella × maschukensis

Pilosella × maschukensis, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. The plant features small, yellow to orange flowers arranged in composite inflorescences, which are typical of the daisy family. It thrives in rocky, alpine environments and is often found in open, sunny areas at high elevations.

Due to its hybrid origin, it exhibits a range of morphological variations across its natural distribution.
