Phaseolus sphaericus var. nanus

Phaseolus sphaericus var. nanus, commonly known as the dwarf scarlet runner bean, is a compact variety of the scarlet runner bean species.

It is characterized by its small, rounded pods that mature to a vibrant red color. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, making it suitable for container gardening or smaller spaces. Its flowers are usually pale yellow, and the plant is known for its early maturation and good yield.

This variety is often cultivated for both ornamental and culinary purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and edible pods.
