Peperomia melanostigma var. angustifolia

Peperomia melanostigma var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features broad, glossy, dark green leaves that are often slightly wavy along the edges. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and white, which are typically borne on short, upright spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate moisture.

Its attractive foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tabletop displays.
