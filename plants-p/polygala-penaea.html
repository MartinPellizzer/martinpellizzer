Polygala penaea

Polygala penaea, commonly known as the slender milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, often pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its leaves are usually small and opposite, while its stems are slender and branching.

Polygala penaea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
