Papilionanthe subulata

Papilionanthe subulata, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features slender, lance-shaped leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.

Due to its specific care requirements, it is considered a challenging but rewarding plant for experienced growers.
