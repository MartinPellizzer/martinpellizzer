Peperomia nivalis var. sanmarcensis

Peperomia nivalis var. sanmarcensis, commonly known as the Snowy Peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically light green to silver in color, giving it a distinctive, almost frosty appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
