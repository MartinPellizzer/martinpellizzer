Potentilla Argentea Var. Decora

Potentilla argentea var. decora is a strikingly beautiful shrub known for its silvery-white leaves and vibrant yellow flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of western North America, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. Its delicate, daisy-like blooms make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a low-maintenance yet visually appealing plant. The variety 'decora' stands out for its enhanced foliage color and more compact growth habit. This plant not only adds ornamental value to landscapes but also supports local pollinators with its nectar-rich flowers.
