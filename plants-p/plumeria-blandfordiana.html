Plumeria blandfordiana

Plumeria blandfordiana, commonly known as the Madagascar plumeria, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that come in various colors, including white, pink, and yellow. The plant has smooth, pale green bark and lance-shaped leaves with a waxy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for warm climates.

The flowers are often used in traditional ceremonies and for ornamental purposes due to their beauty and scent.
