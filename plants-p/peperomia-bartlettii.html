Peperomia bartlettii

Peperomia bartlettii, commonly known as the Bartlett's peperomia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly found in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and its glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, which bloom in clusters.

Peperomia bartlettii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive foliage.
