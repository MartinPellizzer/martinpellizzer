Peperomia reflexa f. americana

Peperomia reflexa f. americana, commonly known as the American peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features glossy, dark green ovate leaves with a slightly wavy margin, and small, inconspicuous flowers that appear in spring. The plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and ability to thrive in well-draining soil. It is often grown as a decorative plant for its attractive foliage and ease of care.

Peperomia reflexa f. americana is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in humid environments.
