Protium pecuniosum

Protium pecuniosum, commonly known as the "money tree," is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grayish-brown bark and elliptical leaves with a glossy upper surface.
