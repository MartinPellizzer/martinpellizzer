Persicaria × condensata

Persicaria × condensata, commonly known as Chinese sunset.

It is a hybrid perennial plant that belongs to the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces striking red to purple flowers that bloom in late summer, adding vibrant color to gardens. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of hair, giving them a distinctive texture.

Persicaria × condensata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in moist, shady conditions.
