Pyrostria longiflora

Pyrostria longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered pyrostria, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and large, showy white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant's leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
