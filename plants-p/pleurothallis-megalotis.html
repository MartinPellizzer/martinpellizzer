Pleurothallis megalotis

Pleurothallis megalotis, commonly known as the giant pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and the presence of long, slender pseudobulbs. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
