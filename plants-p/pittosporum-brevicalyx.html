Pittosporum brevicalyx

Pittosporum brevicalyx, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pittosporum, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to eastern Australia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are narrow and elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are typically clustered in umbels, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subtropical regions.

Pittosporum brevicalyx is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in landscaping.
