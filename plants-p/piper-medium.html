Piper medium

Piper medium, commonly known as the giant pepper plant, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers that develop into peppery fruits. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
