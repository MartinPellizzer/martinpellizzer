Plectranthus graniticola

Plectranthus graniticola, commonly known as the granite plectranthus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple, often blooming in summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for low-maintenance gardening.
