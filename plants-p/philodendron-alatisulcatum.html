Philodendron alatisulcatum

Philodendron alatisulcatum, commonly known as the curly vine philodendron, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that have a wavy, undulating edge, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers that are typically not showy. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its rapid growth and trailing habit make it a popular choice for hanging baskets or as a climbing plant in indoor spaces.
