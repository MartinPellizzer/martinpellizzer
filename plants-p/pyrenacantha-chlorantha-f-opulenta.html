Pyrenacantha chlorantha f. opulenta

Pyrenacantha chlorantha f. opulenta, commonly known as the opulent greenleaf pyrenacantha, is a rare and striking variant of the base species.

It is characterized by its lush, dense foliage, which is a deep, vibrant green, and its elongated, gracefully curved leaves. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, adding a delicate contrast to its foliage. It is native to specific regions in southern Africa, where it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils.
