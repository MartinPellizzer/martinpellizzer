Panicum aureum

Panicum aureum, commonly known as golden panicum, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its vibrant golden-yellow inflorescences that appear in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green with a slight sheen. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies adds to its ecological value.
