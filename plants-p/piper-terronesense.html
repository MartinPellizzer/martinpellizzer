Piper terronesense

Piper terronesense, commonly known as the Terronesense pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Colombia and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with long, slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are followed by berry-like fruits that contain the aromatic seeds used for culinary purposes.

Piper terronesense is valued for its unique flavor profile and is often used in traditional cooking and herbal remedies.
