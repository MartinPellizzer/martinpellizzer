Pelargonium × hortorum

Pelargonium × hortorum, commonly known as the geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, five-petaled flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically has hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated edge. It is widely cultivated as an ornamental plant for gardens and indoor spaces.

Pelargonium × hortorum thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
