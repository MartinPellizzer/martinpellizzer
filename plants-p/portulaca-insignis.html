Portulaca insignis

Portulaca insignis, commonly known as the Mexican portulaca, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of the southwestern United States.

It is an annual herb with fleshy, succulent leaves and bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it adaptable to arid environments. Its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Portulaca insignis is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
