Piper decumanum var. paluense

Piper decumanum var. paluense, commonly known as the broadleaf pepper plant, is a tropical species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces small, peppery fruits that are used in traditional cuisines. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and culinary contexts.
