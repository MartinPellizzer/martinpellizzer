Peperomia filici-decorans

Peperomia filici-decorans, commonly known as the laceleaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, waxy, heart-shaped leaves that are deeply lobed and resemble the foliage of ferns, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage makes it a striking addition to any plant collection.
