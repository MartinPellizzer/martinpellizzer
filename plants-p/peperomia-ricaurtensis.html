Peperomia ricaurtensis

Peperomia ricaurtensis, commonly known as the Ricaurt's peperomia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive, waxy leaves that often display variegation. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia ricaurtensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
