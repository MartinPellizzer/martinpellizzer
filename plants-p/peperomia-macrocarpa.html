Peperomia macrocarpa

Peperomia macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-leaf peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves that are often adorned with a waxy coating, giving them a unique sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, which appear in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
