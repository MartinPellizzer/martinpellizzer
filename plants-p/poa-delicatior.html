Poa delicatior

Poa delicatior, commonly known as the slender meadow grass, is a species of grass native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fine leaves and compact growth habit, often forming dense, low-growing clumps. The plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along stream banks. Its flowering stems bear small, spike-like inflorescences, which produce numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Poa delicatior is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in wetland environments.
