Pleurothallis subserrata

Pleurothallis subserrata, commonly known as the serrated pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with delicate, linear leaves and a compact inflorescence. The flowers are typically white or pale yellow, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or botanical gardens.
