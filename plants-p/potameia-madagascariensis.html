Potameia madagascariensis

Potameia madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar spider orchid, is a unique flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, spider-like appearance, which gives it its common name. The plant features delicate, pale green or yellowish flowers with a distinct, elongated spur. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
