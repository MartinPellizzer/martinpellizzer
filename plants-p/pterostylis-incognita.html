Pterostylis incognita

Pterostylis incognita, commonly known as the unknown hooded orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understorey areas. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single, hooded flower that is often pale green or yellowish in color. Its flowers are characterized by a labellum that is distinctly divided and has a unique shape.

Pterostylis incognita is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its specialized pollination mechanisms.
