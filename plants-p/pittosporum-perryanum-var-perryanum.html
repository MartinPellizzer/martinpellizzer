Pittosporum perryanum var. perryanum

Pittosporum perryanum var. perryanum, commonly known as the Stewart's pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a glossy appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters, which are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is used in horticulture for its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
