Pinus murrayana var. sargentii

Pinus murrayana var. sargentii, commonly known as the Sargent's pine, is a variant of the Murray pine native to eastern Australia.

It is a medium-sized evergreen conifer with needle-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces cones that are initially yellow but mature to a reddish-brown hue. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical significance in botanical studies.
