Potentilla sylvestris proles dyscritos

Potentilla sylvestris proles dyscritos, commonly known as the shrub cinquefoil, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its woody stems and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant typically thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in alpine or arctic regions. Its leaves are palmately lobed, and it produces small, oval fruits known as achenes.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
