Phacelia humilis var. calycosa

Phacelia humilis var. calycosa, commonly known as the California phacelia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing annual herb with hairy stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces delicate blue to purple flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and disturbed areas.

Its nectar-rich flowers attract a variety of pollinators, including bees and butterflies.
