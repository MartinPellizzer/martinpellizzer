Paronychia arabica subsp. lenticulata

Paronychia arabica subsp.

lenticulata, commonly known as the lenticular paronychia, is a member of the Rubiaceae family. It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial shrub characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
