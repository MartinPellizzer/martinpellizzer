Primula maguirei

Primula maguirei, commonly known as the Maguire's primrose, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are oval and covered in a fine layer of white hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Primula maguirei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with specific humidity and light conditions.
