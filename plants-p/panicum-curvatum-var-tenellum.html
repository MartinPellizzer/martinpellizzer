Panicum curvatum var. tenellum

Panicum curvatum var. tenellum, commonly known as slender panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open fields or disturbed areas.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
