Piper nitidum

Piper nitidum, commonly known as the glossy pepper plant, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, aromatic berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The berries, while not typically eaten, are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
