Piper riitosense

Piper riitosense, commonly known as the Java pepper or wild pepper, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes over 2,000 species of flowering plants. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into three-lobed berries, which are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly aromatic scent.

The plant is valued for its potential medicinal properties and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental appeal.
