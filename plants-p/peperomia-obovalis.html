Peperomia obovalis

Peperomia obovalis, commonly known as the oval peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features oval-shaped, glossy dark green leaves that are often slightly wavy along the edges. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pale green flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal watering needs.

Peperomia obovalis is prized for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
