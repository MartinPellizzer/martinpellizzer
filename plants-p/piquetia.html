Piquetia

Piquetia, commonly known as the "spider plant," is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette-like arrangement of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often followed by bright blue or purple berries. Piquetia is native to southern Africa and thrives in well-drained soil with moderate sunlight.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and air-purifying qualities.
