Pennisetum pilcomayense

Pennisetum pilcomayense, commonly known as the purple fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking purple plumes that appear in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 feet and has narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Pennisetum pilcomayense is also valued for its ability to attract birds and insects, contributing to ecological diversity in landscapes.
