Prunus × fruticans var. euligerina

Prunus × fruticans var. euligerina, commonly known as the European bird cherry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Europe.

It features small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by edible, dark red to black berries. The plant has alternate, simple leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in the cultivation of cherry species.
