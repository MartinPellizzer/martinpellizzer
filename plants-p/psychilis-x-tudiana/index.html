Psychilis × Tudiana

Psychilis tudiana, scientifically known as Psychilis × tudiana, is a rare and intriguing hybrid plant native to the humid rainforests of Southeast Asia. This unique species is the result of natural crossbreeding between two closely related Psychilis species, leading to its distinctive morphological and genetic traits. Despite its limited distribution, it has captured the attention of botanists and plant enthusiasts due to its striking appearance and ecological significance. The plant is often found growing in shaded, moist environments, thriving in the understory of dense tropical forests. Its cultivation and conservation remain important topics in botanical research and horticultural practice.
