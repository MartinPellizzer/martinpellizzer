Polypogon × adscendens

Polypogon × adscendens, commonly known as the ascending grass, is a hybrid plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its erect, slender growth habit. The plant typically produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brownish flowers. Its leaves are narrow and often have a bluish-gray hue, which helps in distinguishing it from other grass species.

Polypogon × adscendens is often found in moist, well-drained soils in open woodlands and along stream banks.
