Prasophyllum pallidum

Prasophyllum pallidum, commonly known as the pale leek orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in heathlands and open forests, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike. It is a perennial species that blooms during the autumn season.

Prasophyllum pallidum is listed as vulnerable due to habitat loss and degradation.
