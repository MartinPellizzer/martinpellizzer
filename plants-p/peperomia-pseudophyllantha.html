Peperomia pseudophyllantha

Peperomia pseudophyllantha, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features small, waxy, silver-gray leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia pseudophyllantha is prized for its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
