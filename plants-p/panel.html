Panel

Panel, commonly known as the panel plant, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it an important component of riparian ecosystems.
