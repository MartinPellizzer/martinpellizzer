Papaver lijiangense

Papaver lijiangense, commonly known as the Liangjiang poppy, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Papaveraceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and has lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture. Its capsule fruit contains numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological significance in its native habitat.
