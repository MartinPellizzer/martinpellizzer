Polygala adoensis

Polygala adoensis, commonly known as the Ado's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to parts of East Asia, particularly China.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, herbaceous stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or mountainous regions. Polygala adoensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
