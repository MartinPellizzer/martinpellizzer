Pararistolochia dictyophlebia

Pararistolochia dictyophlebia, commonly known as the Chinese broadleaf arrowhead, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and distinctive, intricate floral structures. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often covered in fine hairs. Its fruit is a smooth, elongated capsule that contains numerous seeds.

Due to its complex morphology and medicinal uses, it is of interest in botanical and pharmacological studies.
