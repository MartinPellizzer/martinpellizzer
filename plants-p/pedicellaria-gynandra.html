Pedicellaria gynandra

Pedicellaria gynandra, commonly known as African nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and peppers. The plant has alternate, ovate leaves with a rough texture and produces small, clustered white or purple flowers. Its fruit is a capsule containing numerous seeds, and it is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
