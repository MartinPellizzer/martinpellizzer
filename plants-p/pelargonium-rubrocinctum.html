Pelargonium rubrocinctum

Pelargonium rubrocinctum, commonly known as the red-banded geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The leaves are typically rounded with a velvety texture and often have distinctive red or purple markings. This species thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which can extend from spring through early autumn.
