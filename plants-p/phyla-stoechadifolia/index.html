Phyla Stoechadifolia

Phyla stoechadifolia is a unique flowering plant known for its striking violet-blue blooms. It thrives in coastal and saline environments, making it a hardy species adapted to harsh conditions. This plant is native to regions such as the Mediterranean and parts of Europe. Its distinctive appearance and ecological resilience have made it a subject of interest in botany. Understanding its biology helps in preserving biodiversity in sensitive habitats.
