Pinalia perpusilla

Pinalia perpusilla, commonly known as the dwarf pinalia, is a small, herbaceous plant native to specific regions of South America.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. The plant features delicate, narrow leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are often white or pale pink in color. It is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in arid conditions.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological niche, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
