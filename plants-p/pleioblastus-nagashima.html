Pleioblastus nagashima

Pleioblastus nagashima, commonly known as the Nagashima bamboo, is a species native to Japan.

It is a clumping bamboo that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant features slender, green culms with a smooth, slightly glossy surface. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly waxy appearance.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in garden landscapes.
