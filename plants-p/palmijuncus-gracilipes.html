Palmijuncus gracilipes

Palmijuncus gracilipes, commonly known as the slender palm fern, is a tropical plant characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
