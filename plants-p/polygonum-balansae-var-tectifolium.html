Polygonum balansae var. tectifolium

Polygonum balansae var. tectifolium, commonly known as the shield-leaf polygonum, is a variant of the species Polygonum balansae, native to specific regions in China.

It is characterized by its distinctive foliage, with leaves that are broad and shield-shaped, providing a unique visual appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments, such as forest floors or stream sides. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in late summer to early autumn.

The botanical features of this variant make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.
