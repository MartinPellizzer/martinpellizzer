Pursegloveia orientalis

Pursegloveia orientalis, commonly known as the oriental purselove, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly rough texture.

The oriental purselove is adapted to arid conditions, making it a resilient plant in dry environments.
