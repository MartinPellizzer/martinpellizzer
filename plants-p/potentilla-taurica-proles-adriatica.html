Potentilla taurica proles adriatica

Potentilla taurica proles adriatica, commonly known as the Adriatic potentilla, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of the Adriatic Sea.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in a fine layer of silvery hair.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy coastal habitats.
