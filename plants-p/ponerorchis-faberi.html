Ponerorchis faberi

Ponerorchis faberi, commonly known as the Chinese orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Ponerorchis faberi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
