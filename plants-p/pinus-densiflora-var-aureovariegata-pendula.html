Pinus densiflora var. aureovariegata-pendula

Pinus densiflora var. aureovariegata-pendula, commonly known as the golden-variegated weeping pine, is a unique variant of the Japanese black pine.

It exhibits striking golden-yellow variegation on its foliage, which contrasts beautifully with its dark green needles. The plant has a cascading growth habit, with branches drooping gracefully to form a weeping appearance. It is native to southern Japan and thrives in well-drained soils with full sun exposure.

This ornamental conifer is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its visual interest.
