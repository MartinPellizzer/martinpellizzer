Pelargonium × hoariflorum

Pelargonium × hoariflorum, commonly known as the woolly flowering geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, typically blooming in summer. Native to South Africa, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its decorative leaves and vibrant blooms.
