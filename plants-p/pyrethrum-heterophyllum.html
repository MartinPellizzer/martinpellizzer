Pyrethrum heterophyllum

Pyrethrum heterophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces pyrethrins, natural insecticides used in organic pest control. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and commercial cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is widely appreciated in horticulture and agriculture.
