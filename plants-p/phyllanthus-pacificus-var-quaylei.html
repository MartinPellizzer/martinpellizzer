Phyllanthus pacificus var. quaylei

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. It has a short stem with opposite leaves that are oval to lance-shaped.
