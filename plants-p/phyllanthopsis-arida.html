Phyllanthopsis arida

Phyllanthopsis arida, commonly known as the desert phyllanthus, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa and Asia.

Phyllanthopsis arida is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
