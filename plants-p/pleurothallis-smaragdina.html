Pleurothallis smaragdina

Pleurothallis smaragdina, commonly known as the Emerald Pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in the understory of humid forests. The plant features delicate, narrow leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts worldwide.
