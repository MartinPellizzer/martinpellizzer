Polyarrhena reflexa subsp. reflexa

Polyarrhena reflexa subsp.

reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed polyarrhena, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polygonaceae. It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and alternate, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, often with reddish bracts. It is native to parts of Africa and thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

This subspecies is often found in grasslands and along stream banks, where it contributes to local biodiversity.
