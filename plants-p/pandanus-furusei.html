Pandanus furusei

Pandanus furusei, commonly known as the Japanese pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pandanaceae.

It is native to the Ryukyu Islands of Japan and is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically clustered in branched inflorescences. Its roots and leaves have been utilized in local cultures for their aromatic properties and practical applications.

Pandanus furusei thrives in humid, coastal environments and is often found growing near the shoreline.
