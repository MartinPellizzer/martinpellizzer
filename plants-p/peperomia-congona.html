Peperomia congona

Peperomia congona, commonly known as the Congo peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, often adorned with subtle markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white, which appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia congona is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and tolerating a range of indoor conditions.
