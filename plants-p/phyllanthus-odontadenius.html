Phyllanthus odontadenius

Phyllanthus odontadenius, commonly known as the African date palm, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a small tree or shrub that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. Its small yellow flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces small, fleshy fruits that are edible.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine.
