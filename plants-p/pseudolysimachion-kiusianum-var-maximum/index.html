Pseudolysimachion Kiusianum Var. Maximum

Pseudolysimachion kiusianum var. maximum is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Korea. Known for its large, showy inflorescences, it captivates botanists and gardeners alike with its unique appearance. This variety stands out due to its extended bloom period and vibrant coloration. Its cultivation is challenging, requiring specific environmental conditions to thrive. Despite its rarity, it holds significant value in both horticultural and ecological contexts.
