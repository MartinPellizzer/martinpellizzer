Pleurothallis wawraeana

Pleurothallis wawraeana, commonly known as the Wawra's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, intricate flowers. The plant typically produces pale yellow or greenish-white blooms, which are often adorned with subtle markings. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its delicate nature, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a potted plant.
