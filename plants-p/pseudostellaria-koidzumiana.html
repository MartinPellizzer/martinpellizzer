Pseudostellaria koidzumiana

Pseudostellaria koidzumiana, commonly known as the Japanese starwort, is a small herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. The plant features delicate, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, arranged in a basal rosette.

Pseudostellaria koidzumiana is often found in rocky or clay soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
