Polygala grossheimii

Polygala grossheimii, commonly known as the Cape Kruger polygala, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, spire-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually pale blue or white, with a distinctive, spurred structure that attracts pollinators.

It is a relatively small shrub, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.
