Pelargonium × brightianum

Pelargonium × brightianum, commonly known as the Brightianum geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a range of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically has rounded, lobed leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Its compact growth habit and long blooming period make it a versatile ornamental plant.
