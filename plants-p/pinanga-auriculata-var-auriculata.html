Pinanga auriculata var. auriculata

Pinanga auriculata var. auriculata, commonly known as the golden shield fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant features broad, leathery fronds with a distinctive golden hue on the underside, which gives it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
