Patagonium adenophorum

Patagonium adenophorum, commonly known as the Patagonian thistle, is a flowering plant native to the southern regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Patagonium adenophorum plays a role in local ecosystems by providing food for various insects and animals.
