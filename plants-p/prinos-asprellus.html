Prinos asprellus

Prinos asprellus, commonly known as the rough prinos, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. Its leaves are characterized by their rough, hairy texture and dark green color.

Prinos asprellus is often found in rocky or dry soils, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments.
