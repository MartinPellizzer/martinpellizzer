Peperomia conjungens

Peperomia conjungens, commonly known as the clustered peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features broad, glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and red, which are typically followed by round, fleshy berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate watering.

Peperomia conjungens is an excellent choice for indoor gardening due to its ease of care and ornamental foliage.
