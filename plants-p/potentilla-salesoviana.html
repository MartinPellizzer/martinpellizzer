Potentilla salesoviana

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy yellow flowers. Its leaves are compound, consisting of five ovate leaflets, and it produces small, aggregate fruits known as achenes.

Potentilla salesoviana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardiness and visual interest.
