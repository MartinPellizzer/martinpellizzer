Poa malmundariensis

Poa malmundariensis, commonly known as the Malmundariensis grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe, particularly in the Pyrenees and the Alps. This plant is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and fine, needle-like leaves. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.

Poa malmundariensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soil in high-altitude ecosystems.
