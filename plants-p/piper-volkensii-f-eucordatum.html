Piper volkensii f. eucordatum

Piper volkensii f. eucordatum, commonly known as the velvetleaf pepper or Ecuadorian pepper, is a tropical shrub native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, green berries that mature to a bright red color, containing aromatic seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its ornamental value and unique fragrance, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
