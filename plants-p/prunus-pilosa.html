Prunus pilosa

Prunus pilosa, commonly known as the Chinese bird cherry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its pale pink to white flowers that bloom in early spring, often before the leaves appear. The plant has simple, oval-shaped leaves that turn yellow in autumn. Its fruit is a small, yellowish drupe that ripens in summer.

Prunus pilosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its spring blossoms.
