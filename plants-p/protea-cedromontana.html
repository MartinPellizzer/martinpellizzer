Protea cedromontana

Protea cedromontana, commonly known as the Cedromontana protea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family, which is characterized by its diverse and often showy floral structures. The plant produces large, cone-like inflorescences that are typically composed of numerous small, petal-like bracts. These bracts are usually pink to red in color, giving the plant a striking appearance.

Protea cedromontana is adapted to nutrient-poor soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous habitats.
