Pedilonum amethystoglossum

Pedilonum amethystoglossum, commonly known as the amethyst lip orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet-lavender petals and a distinctive, lip-like structure that is often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its flowers bloom in late summer, adding a rare and beautiful touch to its natural habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and sensitivity to environmental changes, it is considered a vulnerable species.
