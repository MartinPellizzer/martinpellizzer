Polycenia

Polycenia, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers that resemble small raindrops. The plant belongs to the family Leguminosae, also known as the legume or bean family. Polycenia is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice in tropical and subtropical gardens.
