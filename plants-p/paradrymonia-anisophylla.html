Paradrymonia anisophylla

Paradrymonia anisophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leafed paradrymonia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of northern Australia, particularly in Queensland. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. It produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, typically blooming during the wet season.
