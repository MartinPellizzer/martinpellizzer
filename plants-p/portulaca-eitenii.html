Portulaca eitenii

Portulaca eitenii, commonly known as the Eiten's moss rose, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is a low-growing, succulent plant with fleshy, rounded leaves and small, vibrant flowers that bloom in shades of red, yellow, and orange. The plant thrives in hot, dry climates and is adapted to store water in its thick, fleshy stems and leaves. It produces seeds in capsules that are dispersed by wind, allowing it to colonize disturbed soils and rocky areas.

Portulaca eitenii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its resilience and colorful blooms.
