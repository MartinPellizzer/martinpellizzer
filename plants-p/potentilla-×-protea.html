Potentilla × protea

Potentilla × protea, commonly known as the hybrid shrub, is a botanical hybrid resulting from the cross between Potentilla and Protea species.

It typically exhibits a compact growth habit with dense, often hairy foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white, pink, or red, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display and hardy nature.
