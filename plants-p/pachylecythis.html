Pachylecythis

Pachylecythis, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets. Its bark is smooth and greyish, often shedding in strips as the tree matures.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often planted in gardens and parks for its vibrant floral display.
