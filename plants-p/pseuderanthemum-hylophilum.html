Pseuderanthemum hylophilum

Pseuderanthemum hylophilum, commonly known as the silverleaf pseuderanthemum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and opposite, ovate leaves. The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate foliage and flowering display.
