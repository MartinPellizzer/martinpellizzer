Peperomia teresitensis

Peperomia teresitensis, commonly known as the compact peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Brazil.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a slight sheen, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale green. The plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its minimal water requirements.

Its compact form and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for tabletop displays and container gardening.
