Pothos neoroxburghii

Pothos neoroxburghii, commonly known as the silver vine, is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically variegated with silver and green patterns. The plant thrives in humid environments and prefers indirect sunlight. It is known for its ability to climb using aerial roots, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets or shelves.

Its ornamental foliage makes it a sought-after houseplant for its aesthetic appeal.
