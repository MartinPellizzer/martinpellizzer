Psoralea sericea

Psoralea sericea, commonly known as silky psoralea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, silky-hairy foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
