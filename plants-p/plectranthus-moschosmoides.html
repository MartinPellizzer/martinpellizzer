Plectranthus moschosmoides

Plectranthus moschosmoides, commonly known as the musk plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color.
