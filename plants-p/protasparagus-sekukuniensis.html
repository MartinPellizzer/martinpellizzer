Protasparagus sekukuniensis

Protasparagus sekukuniensis, commonly known as the Sekukuni's asparagus, is a rare and endemic plant species found primarily in the mountainous regions of South Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, grass-like foliage and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, with thick leaves that store water to help it survive in dry conditions. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
