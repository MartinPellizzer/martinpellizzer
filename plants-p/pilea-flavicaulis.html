Pilea flavicaulis

Pilea flavicaulis, commonly known as the golden stem pilea, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features upright, slender stems that are often tinged with a yellowish hue, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a smooth, waxy texture.

Pilea flavicaulis is often grown as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
