Plectranthus kameba var. latifolius

Plectranthus kameba var. latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, velvety leaves that are typically a deep green in color. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually purple or violet, arranged in spikes. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for both container and garden cultivation.

This variety is often used as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and easy care requirements.
