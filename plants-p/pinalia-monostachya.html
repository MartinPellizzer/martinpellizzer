Pinalia monostachya

Pinalia monostachya, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed pinyon, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a member of the Pinaceae family, which includes various coniferous trees and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, needle-like leaves. It produces cone-like structures that contain its seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Pinalia monostachya is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky soils.
