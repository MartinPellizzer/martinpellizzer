Plantago major var. jehohlensis

Plantago major var. jehohlensis, commonly known as the Jeholensis plantain, is a variant of the common plantain.

It is characterized by its broad, oval-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, which are borne on slender stalks. It is native to specific regions in China and is adapted to grow in moist, well-drained soils.

This variant is often found in open areas such as meadows and along roadsides.
