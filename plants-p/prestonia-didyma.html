Prestonia didyma

Prestonia didyma, commonly known as the showy prestonia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

It is a member of the Apocynaceae family, which includes many plants with showy flowers and milky sap. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a waxy texture.

Prestonia didyma is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and unique appearance.
