Polygonum sagittifolium

Polygonum sagittifolium, commonly known as arrowleaf plantain, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically arrowhead-shaped, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, depending on the variety. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Polygonum sagittifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in wetland habitats.
