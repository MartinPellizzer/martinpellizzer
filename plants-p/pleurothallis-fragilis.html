Pleurothallis fragilis

Pleurothallis fragilis, commonly known as the delicate orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its delicate appearance and ephemeral blooming period make it a prized addition to specialized orchid collections.

Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is often cultivated by experienced growers.
