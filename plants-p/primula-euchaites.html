Primula euchaites

Primula euchaites, commonly known as the Sierra Madre primrose, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or well-drained soils at high altitudes. Its leaves are ovate and hairy, often with a bluish-green hue.

Primula euchaites is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
