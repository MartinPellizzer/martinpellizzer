Proustia cuneifolia subsp. mollis

Proustia cuneifolia subsp.

mollis, commonly known as the softleaf proustia, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features slender, grass-like leaves that are soft to the touch, giving it its subspecies name.

The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological studies and horticultural use.
