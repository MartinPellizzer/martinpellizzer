Polygala dhofarica

Polygala dhofarica, commonly known as the Dhofar polygala, is a flowering plant native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky and arid environments. The plant features small, delicate flowers that are usually pale green or yellowish in color. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces capsules as its fruit.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
