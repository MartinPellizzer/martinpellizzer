Psoralea rigida

Psoralea rigida, commonly known as rigid psoralea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its upright, woody structure. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Psoralea rigida is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
