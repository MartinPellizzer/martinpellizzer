Podocarpus reichei

Podocarpus reichei, commonly known as the Chinese podocarp, is a member of the Podocarpaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has a distinctive conical shape with dense, dark green foliage. Its leaves are leathery and arranged spirally along the branches.

The tree produces small, cone-like structures that contain its seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
