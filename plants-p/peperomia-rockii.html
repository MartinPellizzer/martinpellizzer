Peperomia rockii

Peperomia rockii, commonly known as the Rock's Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features thick, fleshy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by reddish-brown berries. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light with moderate humidity.

Peperomia rockii is prized for its ornamental foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
