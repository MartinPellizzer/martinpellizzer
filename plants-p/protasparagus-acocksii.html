Protasparagus acocksii

Protasparagus acocksii, commonly known as the Acocks' asparagus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Asparagaceae and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
