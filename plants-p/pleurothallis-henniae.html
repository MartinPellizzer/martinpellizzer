Pleurothallis henniae

Pleurothallis henniae, commonly known as the Hennia's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically growing on tree branches or rocks. The plant features delicate, narrow leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its floral structure is typical of the genus, with a slender column and spurred lip.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicate species with limited cultivation success outside its natural range.
