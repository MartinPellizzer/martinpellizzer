Piper schlechtendalianum

Piper schlechtendalianum, commonly known as the Schlechtendalianum pepper plant, is a flowering vine native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piper genus, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, green berries that mature to a bright red color, often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly aromatic scent.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
