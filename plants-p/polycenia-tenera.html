Polycenia tenera

Polycenia tenera, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves that are covered in fine white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and ability to thrive in a variety of conditions.

Polycenia tenera prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it adaptable to different garden environments.
