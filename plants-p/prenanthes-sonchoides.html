Prenanthes sonchoides

Prenanthes sonchoides, commonly known as the common winged sunflower, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist habitats such as wet meadows and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped with a distinctive winged appearance along the edges.

The yellow flowers bloom in the summer and attract various pollinators like bees and butterflies.
