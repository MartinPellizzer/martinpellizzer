Plantago major var. paniculata

Plantago major var. paniculata, commonly known as the panicled plantain, is a variant of the common plantain.

It is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia, characterized by its rosette of broad, ribbed leaves. The plant produces a tall, branching inflorescence that bears small, greenish flowers. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and gardens.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various environmental conditions.
