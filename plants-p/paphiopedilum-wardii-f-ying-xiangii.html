Paphiopedilum wardii f. ying-xiangii

Paphiopedilum wardii f. ying-xiangii, commonly known as the Yingxiang Orchid, is a rare and exquisite species of orchid native to China.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure. The plant typically features dark green, leathery leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive pouch-like lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by collectors and horticulturists.
