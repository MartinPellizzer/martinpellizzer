Peucedanum ferulaceum var. stadense

Peucedanum ferulaceum var. stadense, commonly known as Swedish cow parsley, is a biennial or perennial herb native to Europe.

It features finely divided, fern-like leaves and produces small, yellowish-white flowers in umbels.
