Peperomia mocoana

Peperomia mocoana, commonly known as the Mocoana peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink, giving it an ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences, which are often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia mocoana is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
