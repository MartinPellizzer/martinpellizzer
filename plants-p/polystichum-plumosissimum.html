Polystichum plumosissimum

Polystichum plumosissimum, commonly known as the silky fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, silky-textured fronds that give it a delicate and ornamental appearance. Native to the forests of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in shaded, moist environments. Its fronds are typically arching and can reach lengths of up to 1 meter.

Polystichum plumosissimum is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
