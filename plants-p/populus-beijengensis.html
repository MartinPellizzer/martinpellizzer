Populus beijengensis

Populus beijengensis, commonly known as the Beijing poplar, is a deciduous tree native to northern China.

It belongs to the Salicaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and distinctive white flowers. The plant is adapted to cold climates and can thrive in a variety of soil types. It plays an important role in ecological restoration projects due to its resilience and fast growth rate.

Its bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for their potential health benefits.
