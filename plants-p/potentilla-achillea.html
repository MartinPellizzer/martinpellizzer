Potentilla achillea

Potentilla achillea, commonly known as mountain sorrow, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, white or yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, with several leaflets arranged along the stem.

The plant is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to harsh, alpine environments.
