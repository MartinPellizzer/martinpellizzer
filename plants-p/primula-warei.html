Primula warei

Primula warei, commonly known as the Chinese primrose, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in a fine, velvety texture, while its stems are often prostrate or upright depending on the variety.

Primula warei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its early spring blooms.
