Paullinia clavigera var. bullata

Paullinia clavigera var. bullata, commonly known as the Bullate Surinam cherrie, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is a climbing shrub that produces striking red berries with a thick, waxy coating. The leaves are glossy and dark green, often with a slightly serrated edge. The plant is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional South American healing practices.

Its unique appearance and ecological role make it an important species in its native habitat.
