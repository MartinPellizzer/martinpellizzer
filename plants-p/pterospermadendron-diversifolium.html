Pterospermadendron diversifolium

Pterospermadendron diversifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf pterospermum, is a tropical tree species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, compound leaves that vary in shape and size, often with a serrated or lobed edge. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody pods containing flat, winged seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the forest canopy in its native habitat.
