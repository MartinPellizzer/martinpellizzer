Plectranthus lanuginosus

Plectranthus lanuginosus, commonly known as the woolly plectranthus, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves that give it a velvety appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or blue in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Plectranthus lanuginosus is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in a variety of conditions.
