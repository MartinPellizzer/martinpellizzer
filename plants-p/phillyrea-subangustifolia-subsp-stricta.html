Phillyrea subangustifolia subsp. stricta

Phillyrea subangustifolia subsp.

stricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and slightly glossy.

The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, woody fruits. It is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
