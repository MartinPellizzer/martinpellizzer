Peperomia acuminata f. rubra

Peperomia acuminata f. rubra, commonly known as the red-veined peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves with a striking red or pinkish veining against a deep green background. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and decorative purposes.
