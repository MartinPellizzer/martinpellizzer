Panicum itieri

Panicum itieri, commonly known as the slender panicgrass, is a herbaceous plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets.

Due to its adaptability, Panicum itieri is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
