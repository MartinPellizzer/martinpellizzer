Phenax madagascariensis

Phenax madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure. The plant typically features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs and linear leaves. Its flowers are often brightly colored, with a complex arrangement of sepals and petals.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental beauty.
