Peperomia consoquitlana

Peperomia consoquitlana, commonly known as the Mexican peperomia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color.

Peperomia consoquitlana is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.
