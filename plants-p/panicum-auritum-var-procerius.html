Panicum auritum var. procerius

Panicum auritum var. procerius, commonly known as the tall panic grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, erect stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height, with long, narrow leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces fluffy, panicle-like inflorescences that are characteristic of the Panicum genus. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental and erosion control purposes.

Its adaptability and drought tolerance contribute to its popularity in landscaping and ecological restoration projects.
