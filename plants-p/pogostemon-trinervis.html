Pogostemon trinervis

Pogostemon trinervis, commonly known as the three-veined pogostemon, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions of India, Thailand, and Malaysia. Pogostemon trinervis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
