Pleione yunnanensis var. chiwuana

Pleione yunnanensis var. chiwuana, commonly known as the Yunnan pleione, is a rare and elegant orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration pattern. The plant typically blooms in late winter or early spring, adding a touch of beauty to its natural habitat. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in shaded forest areas.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by botanical enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
