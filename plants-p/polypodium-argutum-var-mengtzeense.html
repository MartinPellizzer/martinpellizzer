Polypodium argutum var. mengtzeense

Polypodium argutum var. mengtzeense, commonly known as the Mengtze fern, is a variant of the common holly fern.

It is a terrestrial fern characterized by its lanceolate, serrated leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces sporangia on the underside of its fronds, which are clustered in strobila. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
