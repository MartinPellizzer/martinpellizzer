Persea croatii

Persea croatii, commonly known as the Croatian persimmon, is a small evergreen tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and fragrant flowers. The tree produces small, orange-red berries that are edible when fully mature. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its dense foliage and winter coloration.

The plant is adapted to coastal and limestone soils, making it a hardy choice for Mediterranean gardens.
