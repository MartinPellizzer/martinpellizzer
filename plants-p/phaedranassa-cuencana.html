Phaedranassa cuencana

Phaedranassa cuencana, commonly known as the Cuencan orchid, is a species of orchid native to Ecuador.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and green. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in cloud forests. Its leaves are broad and dark green, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
