Peperomia myrtifolia var. oblongifolia

Peperomia myrtifolia var. oblongifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions.

It features glossy, ovate to elliptical leaves with a waxy texture and a vibrant green to reddish hue. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value lies in its attractive foliage rather than its flowers.
