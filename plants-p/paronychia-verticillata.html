Paronychia verticillata

Paronychia verticillata, commonly known as the spiral lily or spiral grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of leaves around the stem, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy perennial suitable for rock gardens.

Its unique foliage and modest blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
