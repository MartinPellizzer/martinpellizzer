Pharnaceum confertum var. confertum

Pharnaceum confertum var. confertum, commonly known as the dense pharnaceum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of South America.

It features a basal rosette of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and green, typically blooming in late spring. Its growth habit is characterized by a compact form with dense foliage, making it suitable for rock gardens or alpine settings.

The species is noted for its resilience in well-drained soils and full sun conditions.
