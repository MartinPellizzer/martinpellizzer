Piliostigma pyrrhocarpum

Piliostigma pyrrhocarpum, commonly known as the red-bark tree, is a deciduous tree native to the savannas of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive red-barked trunk. The tree produces yellow flowers followed by pod-like fruits containing edible seeds. It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
