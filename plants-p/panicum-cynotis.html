Panicum cynotis

Panicum cynotis, commonly known as blue panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant produces fluffy, seed-filled spikes that are often used in ornamental settings. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscapes.

Its botanical features include narrow leaves and a compact growth habit, contributing to its aesthetic appeal.
