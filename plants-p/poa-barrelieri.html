Poa barrelieri

Poa barrelieri, commonly known as the Barrelier's bluegrass, is a species of grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and blue-green foliage. The plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grassy areas or along stream banks. Its inflorescence consists of a narrow spike of small, spikelet-bearing branches.

Poa barrelieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual texture and resilience.
