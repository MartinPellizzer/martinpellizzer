Pittosporum × intermedium

Pittosporum × intermedium, commonly known as the hybrid pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, with a distinctive aromatic scent when crushed. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in spring, followed by bright orange berries that are edible for birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Its adaptability and aesthetic appeal contribute to its widespread cultivation in temperate climates.
