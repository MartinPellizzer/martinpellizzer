Pleurothallis tuberculosa

Pleurothallis tuberculosa, commonly known as the tuberculate orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers and epiphytic growth habit. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, relying on atmospheric moisture for nourishment. Its leaves are usually dark green and velvety, while its flowers are often white or pale yellow with intricate patterns.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
