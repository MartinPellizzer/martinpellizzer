Piper hollrungii

Piper hollrungii, commonly known as the Malayan pepper vine, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in spikes. Its fruits are berries that turn from green to red when mature, and they contain a single seed.

Piper hollrungii is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
