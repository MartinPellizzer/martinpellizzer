Pittosporum silamense

Pittosporum silamense, commonly known as the Silamense Pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
