Peperomia knoblecheriana

Peperomia knoblecheriana, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial shrub native to South America.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery or grayish bloom, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, which appear in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia knoblecheriana is relatively low maintenance and suitable for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
