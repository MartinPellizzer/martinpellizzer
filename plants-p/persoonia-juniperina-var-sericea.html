Persoonia juniperina var. sericea

Persoonia juniperina var. sericea, commonly known as the silky persoonia, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its small, rounded leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving the plant its common name. The species produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal regions.

This plant is adapted to arid conditions and is well-suited for cultivation in dry, well-drained gardens.
