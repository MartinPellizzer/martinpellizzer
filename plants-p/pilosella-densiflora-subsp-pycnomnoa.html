Pilosella densiflora subsp. pycnomnoa

Pilosella densiflora subsp.

pycnomnoa, commonly known as the denseflower pilosella, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.
