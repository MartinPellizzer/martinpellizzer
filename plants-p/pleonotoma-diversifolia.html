Pleonotoma diversifolia

Pleonotoma diversifolia, commonly known as the diverse-leaf pleonotoma, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that vary in color from dark green to deep purple. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, adding visual interest to its foliage. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
