Phyllostachys nidularia f. vexillaris

Phyllostachys nidularia f. vexillaris, commonly known as the Purple Bamboo, is a variant of the Chinese bamboo species.

It is characterized by its striking purple to pinkish stems that contrast with its green leaves. This ornamental bamboo is native to China and thrives in temperate climates. It grows as a clump-forming plant, reaching heights of up to 6 meters.

The plant is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often used in landscaping and garden design.
