Polystachya cultriformis var. erubescens

Polystachya cultriformis var. erubescens, commonly known as the red-flowered sword fern, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its elegant, sword-like fronds.

It belongs to the Polystachyaceae family and is native to regions in Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant features bright red bracts that contrast with its dark green foliage, making it a visually appealing ornamental species. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
