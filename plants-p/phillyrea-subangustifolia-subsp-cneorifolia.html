Phillyrea subangustifolia subsp. cneorifolia

Phillyrea subangustifolia subsp.

cneorifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside.

The plant produces white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, woody fruits. It is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and woodland habitats.

This subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and resilience in harsh environmental conditions.
