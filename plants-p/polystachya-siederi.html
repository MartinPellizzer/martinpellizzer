Polystachya siederi

Polystachya siederi, commonly known as the Sieder's spikegrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia, where it grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant produces slender, erect stems that bear clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Polystachya siederi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
