Peperomia pichisensis

Peperomia pichisensis, commonly known as the Pichisensis Peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andes mountains in South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit. The plant features oval-shaped, glossy dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color.

Peperomia pichisensis is often grown as a decorative indoor plant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
