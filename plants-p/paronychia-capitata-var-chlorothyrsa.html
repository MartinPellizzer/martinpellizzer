Paronychia capitata var. chlorothyrsa

Paronychia capitata var. chlorothyrsa, commonly known as the green-leafed sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features dense clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in rocky or coastal environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
