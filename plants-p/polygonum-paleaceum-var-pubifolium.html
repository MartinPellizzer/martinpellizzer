Polygonum paleaceum var. pubifolium

Polygonum paleaceum var. pubifolium, commonly known as the hairy leaf smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink to red in color, blooming in late summer. It thrives in moist environments such as wetlands and along stream banks.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense stands in suitable habitats.
