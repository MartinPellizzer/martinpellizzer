Premna latifolia var. gamblei

Premna latifolia var. gamblei, commonly known as the Gambler's premna, is a shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate to elliptical leaves that are usually dark green on the upper surface and lighter on the underside. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
