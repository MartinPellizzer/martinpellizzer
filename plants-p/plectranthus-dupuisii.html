Plectranthus dupuisii

Plectranthus dupuisii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and produces small, tubular flowers in shades of purple and white. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.

Plectranthus dupuisii is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
