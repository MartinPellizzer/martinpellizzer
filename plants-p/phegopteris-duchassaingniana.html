Phegopteris duchassaingniana

Phegopteris duchassaingniana, commonly known as the giant shield fern, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, shield-shaped fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a complex rhizome system that allows it to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are typically bipinnate, with a distinctive arrangement of small, lance-shaped pinnae.

This fern is often found in rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appearance and ecological significance.
