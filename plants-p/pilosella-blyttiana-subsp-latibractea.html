Pilosella blyttiana subsp. latibractea

Pilosella blyttiana subsp.

latibractea, commonly known as the broad-bract pilosella, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
