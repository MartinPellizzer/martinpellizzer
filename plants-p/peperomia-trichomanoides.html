Peperomia trichomanoides

Peperomia trichomanoides, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green, velvety leaves that are typically heart-shaped or ovate in form. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia trichomanoides is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and low maintenance requirements.
