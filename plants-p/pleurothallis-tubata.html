Pleurothallis tubata

Pleurothallis tubata, commonly known as the tuberous pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees and rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. Its flowers are usually small and delicate, with a variety of colors including white, yellow, and pink, and are often fragrant.

Pleurothallis tubata is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
