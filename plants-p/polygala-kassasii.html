Polygala kassasii

Polygala kassasii, commonly known as the Kassasi polygala, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, often spindly stems and clusters of small, intricate flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to survive in drought conditions.
