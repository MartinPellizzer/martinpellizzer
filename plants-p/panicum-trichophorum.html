Panicum trichophorum

Panicum trichophorum, commonly known as silky grass or silky panicum, is a species of grass native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, silky hairs that cover its leaves and inflorescence, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically significant in its native range.
