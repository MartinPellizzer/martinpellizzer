Pyrethrum arvense

Pyrethrum arvense, commonly known as field pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant produces small, spherical seed heads that contain numerous tiny seeds. Its leaves are typically alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Pyrethrum arvense is known for its natural insecticidal properties, making it valuable in organic pest control.
