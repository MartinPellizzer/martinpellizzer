Pogonotrophe foveolata

Pogonotrophe foveolata, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth where it can receive indirect sunlight.
