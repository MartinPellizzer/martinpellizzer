Pancratium limense

Pancratium limense, commonly known as the Cape dune lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal dunes and sandy soils. The plant features strap-shaped, grass-like leaves and produces striking yellow or orange flowers. Its bulbs are adapted to store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in arid conditions.

Pancratium limense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
