Primula × muretii

Primula × muretii, commonly known as the hybrid primrose, is a flowering plant belonging to the Primulaceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Primula vulgaris and Primula veris, resulting in a plant with distinctive floral characteristics. The species typically produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with a contrasting throat. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland or meadow habitats.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
