Phyllanthus phillyreifolius var. commersonii

Phyllanthus phillyreifolius var. commersonii, commonly known as the Commerson's false crossberry, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a small shrub characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers. The leaves are typically elliptical or ovate, with a smooth texture and a glossy appearance. The plant produces non-showy yellow flowers followed by small, berry-like fruits.

It is native to South America and is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and scrublands.
