Passiflora jianfengensis

Passiflora jianfengensis, commonly known as the Jianfeng Mountain passionflower, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It belongs to the Passifloraceae family and is characterized by its striking, complex flowers with vibrant colors and unique structures. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture, and it produces edible fruits that are similar to other passionfruit varieties.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
