Psittacanthus linearis

Psittacanthus linearis, commonly known as the linear arrowleaf, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features elongated, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, a characteristic feature of the Araceae family.

Psittacanthus linearis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique floral structure.
