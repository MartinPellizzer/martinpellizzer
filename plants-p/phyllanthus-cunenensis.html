Phyllanthus cunenensis

Phyllanthus cunenensis, commonly known as the African spurge, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in open woodland and savanna habitats. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are often glandular and have a distinctive appearance. It produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, followed by small, capsule-like fruits.

The species is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
