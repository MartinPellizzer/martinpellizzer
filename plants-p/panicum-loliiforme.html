Panicum loliiforme

Panicum loliiforme, commonly known as the slender panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, fluffy panicles that are often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for landscaping.

Its botanical features make it both aesthetically pleasing and ecologically beneficial in native habitats.
