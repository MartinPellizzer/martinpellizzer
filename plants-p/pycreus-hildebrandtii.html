Pycreus hildebrandtii

Pycreus hildebrandtii, commonly known as the Hildebrandt's pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant produces small, spiny capsules that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
