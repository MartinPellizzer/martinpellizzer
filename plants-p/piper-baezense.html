Piper baezense

Piper baezense, commonly known as the Baez's pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central America, particularly Panama and Costa Rica. The plant is characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by bright red berries.

Piper baezense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded, humid environments.
