Pandanus elegans

Pandanus elegans, commonly known as the elegant pandanus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters at the top of the stems. Its roots and leaves are utilized in various cultural and medicinal practices.

Pandanus elegans thrives in humid, coastal environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
