Petrocosmea longipedicellata

Petrocosmea longipedicellata, commonly known as the long-stemmed rock orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
