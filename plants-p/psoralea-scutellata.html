Psoralea scutellata

Psoralea scutellata, commonly known as the narrowleaf psoralea, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its slender, erect stem and small, trifoliate leaves. Psoralea scutellata is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to thrive in arid conditions.
