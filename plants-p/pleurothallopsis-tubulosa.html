Pleurothallopsis tubulosa

Pleurothallopsis tubulosa, commonly known as the tubular pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, elongated flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant has a slender, creeping stem with narrow, strap-like leaves. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its delicate appearance and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or conservatories.
