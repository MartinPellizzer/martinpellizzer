Peperomia fernandopoiana var. acutifolia

Peperomia fernandopoiana var. acutifolia, commonly known as the pointed-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, glossy, ovate leaves with a slightly pointed tip, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its ornamental foliage, it is often used as a decorative plant in gardens and homes.
