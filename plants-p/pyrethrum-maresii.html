Pyrethrum maresii

Pyrethrum maresii, commonly known as the Maresii chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with bright yellow petals and a central disc. The plant produces pyrethrins, natural insecticides used in organic pest control. It thrives in cool, moist climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Pyrethrum maresii is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its practical applications in agriculture.
