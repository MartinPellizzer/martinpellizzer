Peperomia collocata

Peperomia collocata, commonly known as the compact peperomia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and glossy, dark green leaves. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color.

Peperomia collocata is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive foliage.
