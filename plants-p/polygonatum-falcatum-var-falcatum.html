Polygonatum falcatum var. falcatum

Polygonatum falcatum var. falcatum, commonly known as the Chinese Solomon's Seal, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features long, arching stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbels, which are followed by red or orange berries. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental foliage.

Its rhizomes have been used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
