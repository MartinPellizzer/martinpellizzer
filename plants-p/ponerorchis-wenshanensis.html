Ponerorchis wenshanensis

Ponerorchis wenshanensis, commonly known as the Wenshan orchid, is a rare and unique species of orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking floral morphology. The plant typically produces small, intricate flowers with a mix of colors, often featuring a distinctive yellow lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions with rich, well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
