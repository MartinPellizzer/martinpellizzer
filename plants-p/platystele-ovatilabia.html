Platystele ovatilabia

The plant belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is known for its epiphytic growth habit, meaning it grows on the surface of trees without deriving nutrients from them. Its leaves are narrow and strap-like, and it produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom during the wet season.
