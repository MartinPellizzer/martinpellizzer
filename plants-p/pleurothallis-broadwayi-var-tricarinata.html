Pleurothallis broadwayi var. tricarinata

Pleurothallis broadwayi var. tricarinata, commonly known as the Broadway's pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. The plant typically features a short, creeping rhizome with multiple pseudobulbs, which store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, arranged in a rosette pattern.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
