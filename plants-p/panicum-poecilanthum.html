Panicum poecilanthum

Panicum poecilanthum, commonly known as the red panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it adaptable to various environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Panicum poecilanthum is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
