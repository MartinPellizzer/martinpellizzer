Pyrenacantha

Pyrenacantha, commonly known as the African snowball, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of white flowers that bloom in winter. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are typically covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Pyrenacantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
