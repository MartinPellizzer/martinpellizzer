Peperomia herrerae

Peperomia herrerae, commonly known as the Herrera's peperomia, is a species of flowering plant in the Piperaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Colombia and Ecuador. This compact shrub typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters and features thick, fleshy leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or purple in color.

It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.
