Piper arieianum f. nubicola

Piper arieianum f. nubicola, commonly known as the Nubian pepper or African pepper, is a tropical climbing shrub native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, green, berry-like fruits that turn red when mature, containing aromatic seeds. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a rough, textured surface.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional remedies.
