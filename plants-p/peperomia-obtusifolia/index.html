Peperomia Obtusifolia

Peperomia obtusifolia is a compact, evergreen houseplant known for its thick, waxy leaves and unique appearance. Native to tropical regions of South America, it thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil. Its rounded foliage and subtle variegation make it a popular choice for indoor gardens. Easy to care for, it requires minimal watering and is ideal for beginners. This plant adds a touch of elegance to any space with its lush, foliage-rich look.
