Peucedanum cynorhiza

Peucedanum cynorhiza, commonly known as wild carrot or bishop's weed, is a biennial or perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Its leaves are finely divided and resemble those of parsley, contributing to its ornamental value.
