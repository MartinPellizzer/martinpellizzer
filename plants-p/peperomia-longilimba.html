Peperomia longilimba

Peperomia longilimba, commonly known as the long-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves that are often adorned with subtle veins and a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically green or reddish, which are followed by tiny, spherical berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia longilimba is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
