Panicum leptocaulon

Panicum leptocaulon, commonly known as slender-stemmed panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as fields and roadsides. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brownish flowers.

Panicum leptocaulon is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and tolerate various environmental conditions.
