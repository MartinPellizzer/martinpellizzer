Polygala ukambica f. condensata

Polygala ukambica f. condensata, commonly known as the condensed milkweed, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces yellowish or greenish flowers that are often arranged in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The leaves are typically alternate and have a lanceolate shape with a hairy surface.
