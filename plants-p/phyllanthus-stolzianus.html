Phyllanthus stolzianus

Phyllanthus stolzianus, commonly known as the African star apple, is a tropical shrub native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by edible, star-shaped fruits. Its root system is fibrous, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its fruit in home gardens.
