Podocarpus dulcamara

Podocarpus dulcamara, commonly known as the climbing podocarp, is a evergreen shrub or vine native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features opposite, leathery, elliptical leaves that are often dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers followed by red or purple berries. It is adapted to grow in a variety of soils but prefers moist, well-drained conditions.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping in warm climates.
