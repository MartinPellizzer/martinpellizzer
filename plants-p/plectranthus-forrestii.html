Plectranthus forrestii

Plectranthus forrestii, commonly known as the Forest's plectranthus, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features opposite, ovate to lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually violet or blue in color, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Plectranthus forrestii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in a variety of garden settings.
