Pternix cynarioides

Pternix cynarioides, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of silver-gray, hairy leaves that help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in spring, followed by fluffy white seeds dispersed by the wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Pternix cynarioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in drought conditions.
