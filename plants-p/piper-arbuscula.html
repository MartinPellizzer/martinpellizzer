Piper arbuscula

Piper arbuscula, commonly known as the dwarf pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered leaves and pale yellow flowers. It produces small, peppery fruits that are used in traditional medicine and cuisine.

Piper arbuscula is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and attractive foliage.
