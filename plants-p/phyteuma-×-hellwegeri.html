Phyteuma × hellwegeri

Phyteuma × hellwegeri, commonly known as the hybrid sunflower or evening glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a dark center. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, with lance-shaped leaves that are hairy on the underside. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its blooming period occurs in late summer to early autumn, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
