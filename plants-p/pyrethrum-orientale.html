Pyrethrum orientale

Pyrethrum orientale, commonly known as Oriental pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers. The plant produces small, spherical flower heads that contain numerous fine, hair-like structures called trichomes. These trichomes contain pyrethrins, which are natural insecticides.

Pyrethrum orientale is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and for the extraction of its insecticidal compounds.
