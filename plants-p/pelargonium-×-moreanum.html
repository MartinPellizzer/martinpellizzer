Pelargonium × moreanum

Pelargonium × moreanum, commonly known as the moreanum geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant features broad, lobed leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.

Pelargonium × moreanum is also valued for its adaptability to various garden conditions and its use in both landscape design and container gardening.
