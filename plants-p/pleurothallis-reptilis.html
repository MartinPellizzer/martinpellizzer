Pleurothallis reptilis

Pleurothallis reptilis, commonly known as the snake orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that grows on trees in tropical rainforests. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a subtle fragrance. Its leaves are narrow and strap-like, adapted for efficient water absorption in its humid environment.

Pleurothallis reptilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
