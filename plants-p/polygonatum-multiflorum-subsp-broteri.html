Polygonatum multiflorum subsp. broteri

Polygonatum multiflorum subsp.

broteri, commonly known as the African yellow flag, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit.

The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are lanceolate and arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its ornamental value.
