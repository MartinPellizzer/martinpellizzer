Protea squarrosa

Protea squarrosa, commonly known as the squarrosa protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences composed of numerous pale pink to white flowers arranged in a spherical shape. The plant is native to the Western and Eastern Cape provinces, where it thrives in well-drained, acidic soils. Its foliage consists of narrow, leathery leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating.

Protea squarrosa is a popular ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
