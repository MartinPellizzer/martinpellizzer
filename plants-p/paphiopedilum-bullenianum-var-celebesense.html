Paphiopedilum bullenianum var. celebesense

Paphiopedilum bullenianum var. celebesense, commonly known as the Celebes Slipper Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Indonesian island of Sulawesi.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short, fleshy rhizome and produces showy, slipper-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and green. The plant has broad, ovate leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. It thrives in shaded, humid environments with well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for greenhouse cultivation.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a prized ornamental plant among orchid enthusiasts.
