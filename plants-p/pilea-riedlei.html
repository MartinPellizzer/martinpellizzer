Pilea riedlei

Pilea riedlei, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a slightly variegated appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are rarely seen above the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Pilea riedlei is prized for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
