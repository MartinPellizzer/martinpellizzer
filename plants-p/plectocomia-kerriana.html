Plectocomia kerriana

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces striking, feather-like inflorescences that bear small, yellowish flowers, which are followed by edible, round fruits. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
