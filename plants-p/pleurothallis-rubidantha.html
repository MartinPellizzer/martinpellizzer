Pleurothallis rubidantha

Pleurothallis rubidantha, commonly known as the red-veined orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant produces small, delicate flowers with a striking red and yellow coloration, often with a velvety texture. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by enthusiasts but is not widely available in commercial markets.
