Pittosporum galai

Pittosporum galai, commonly known as the Galai pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers a sunny to partially shaded environment.

Pittosporum galai is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to maintain in a garden setting.
