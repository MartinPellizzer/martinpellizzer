Philodendron panamense

Philodendron panamense, commonly known as the Panama philodendron, is a climbing plant native to Central America.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by berries containing seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a houseplant.
