Pleione cycnoches

Pleione cycnoches, commonly known as the white pleione, is a rare and elegant orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, strap-like leaves and a single, showy white flower with a distinctive greenish-tinged lip. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in shaded forest understories. Its flowers bloom in late winter or early spring, making it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its habitat specificity and rarity, it is considered a conservation concern in many areas.
