Prenanthes glomerata

Prenanthes glomerata, commonly known as clustered water hemlock, is a herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes carrots and parsley, and is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves. Due to its toxic compounds, it should be handled with care.
