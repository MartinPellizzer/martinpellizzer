Pavonia harleyi

Pavonia harleyi, commonly known as the scarlet pavonia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sun. Its leaves are alternate and pinnately compound, with small, ovate leaflets.

Pavonia harleyi is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
