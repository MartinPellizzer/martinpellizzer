Pycreus unioloides var. angulatus

Pycreus unioloides var. angulatus, commonly known as the angular pycreus, is a rare aquatic plant native to specific freshwater habitats in eastern North America.

Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually appearing in clusters near the water's surface.
