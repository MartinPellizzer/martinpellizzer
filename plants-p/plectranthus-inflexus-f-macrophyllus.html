Plectranthus inflexus f. macrophyllus

Plectranthus inflexus f. macrophyllus, commonly known as the large-leaf plectranthus, is a cultivar of the Plectranthus genus native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a slightly velvety texture. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or violet in color, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and is often grown as a houseplant or in shaded outdoor gardens.

This variety is prized for its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
