Pittosporum ramiflorum var. ramiflorum

Pittosporum ramiflorum var. ramiflorum, commonly known as the ramiflorum pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a slight serration along the edges. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that are clustered in racemes, typically blooming in spring. These flowers are followed by small, berry-like fruits that are edible for some bird species.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.
