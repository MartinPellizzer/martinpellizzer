Perymenium rosei

Perymenium rosei, commonly known as the rose spurge, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant has smooth, succulent leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry environments.

Perymenium rosei is known for its ability to tolerate drought conditions and is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
