Phaius Villosus

Phaius villosus is a unique orchid species known for its striking, velvety blooms. Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers, which range in color from white to pale yellow, are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant's hairy stems and broad leaves make it easily identifiable. Despite its beauty, Phaius villosus remains a relatively rare and underappreciated orchid in the botanical world.
