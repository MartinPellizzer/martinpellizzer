Pennisetum centrasiaticum

Pennisetum centrasiaticum, commonly known as the Chinese silver grass, is a perennial grass native to Asia.

It is characterized by its clumping growth habit and striking silver-gray foliage that adds visual interest to gardens. The plant produces tall, slender inflorescences that bloom in late summer, adding a delicate touch of color. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a low-maintenance ornamental plant.

Its fine texture and architectural form make it a popular choice for borders and rock gardens.
