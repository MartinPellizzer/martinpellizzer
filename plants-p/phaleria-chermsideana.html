Phaleria chermsideana

Phaleria chermsideana, commonly known as the Chermsidean starleaf, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, waxy leaves that are typically arranged in a star-like pattern. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental plant collections.
