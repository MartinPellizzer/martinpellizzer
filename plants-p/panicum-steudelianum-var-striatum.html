Panicum steudelianum var. striatum

Panicum steudelianum var. striatum, commonly known as the stripe panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves with distinct striped patterns along the blade. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as fields and disturbed soils. It produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes and erosion control.
