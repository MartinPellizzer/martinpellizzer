Phyllanthus sananei

Phyllanthus sananei, commonly known as the African date palm, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a small tree or shrub that typically grows to a height of about 3 to 5 meters. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, and its fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that contains a single seed. It is often found in moist, well-drained soils near streams or in forest margins.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local communities.
