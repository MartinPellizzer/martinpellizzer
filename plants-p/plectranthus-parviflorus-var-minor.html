Plectranthus parviflorus var. minor

Plectranthus parviflorus var. minor, commonly known as the small-flowered plectranthus, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically gray-green in color, covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of purple or lilac, arranged in clusters at the leaf axils. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its aromatic foliage and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
