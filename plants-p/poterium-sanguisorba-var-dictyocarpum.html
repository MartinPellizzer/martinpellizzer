Poterium sanguisorba var. dictyocarpum

Poterium sanguisorba var. dictyocarpum, commonly known as red sanders, is a variant of the genus Poterium within the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive red, fleshy roots that are often harvested for medicinal purposes. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in late spring to early summer.
