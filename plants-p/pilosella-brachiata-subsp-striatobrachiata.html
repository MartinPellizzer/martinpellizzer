Pilosella brachiata subsp. striatobrachiata

Pilosella brachiata subsp.

striatobrachiata, commonly known as the striated pilosella, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in grassy areas and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped with hairy surfaces, and the flowers are yellow with ray florets.

This subspecies is distinguished by its finely striated leaf surfaces and compact growth habit.
