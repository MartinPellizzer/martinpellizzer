Piper mohomoho

Piper mohomoho, commonly known as the Mohomoho pepper, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically harvested when they turn from green to yellow or red. Its leaves are aromatic and have been traditionally used in local cuisines.

The plant thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
