Platyestes nervosa

Platyestes nervosa, commonly known as the narrow-veined orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and lance-shaped leaves with distinct, parallel veins. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale green to yellowish in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its delicate structure and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many areas.
