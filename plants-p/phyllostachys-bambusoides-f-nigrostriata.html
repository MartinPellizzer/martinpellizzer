Phyllostachys bambusoides f. nigrostriata

Phyllostachys bambusoides f. nigrostriata, commonly known as the black-striated bamboo, is a variant of the bamboo species characterized by its distinctive black stripes on the culms.

This ornamental plant is native to China and is prized for its aesthetic appeal in horticultural settings. The leaves are typically narrow and green, providing a striking contrast to the darkened stems. It is a clump-forming perennial that grows in USDA hardiness zones 6 to 9.

The plant's unique coloration makes it a popular choice for landscape design and bamboo collections.
