Plantago rugelii

Plantago rugelii, commonly known as the rugel's plantain, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a rosette of broad, oval-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, which bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their soothing properties.

The plant is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in open woodland areas.
