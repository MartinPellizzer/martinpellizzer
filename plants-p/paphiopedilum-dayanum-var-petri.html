Paphiopedilum dayanum var. petri

Paphiopedilum dayanum var. petri, commonly known as the Dayanum Paphiopedilum, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green to reddish-brown leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers with intricate, lip-like structures. The plant typically blooms in late spring or early summer, showcasing a range of colors from pale yellow to deep orange. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil for optimal growth.

Due to its rarity and unique beauty, it is highly valued in horticulture and conservation efforts.
