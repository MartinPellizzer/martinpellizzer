Priva mitchellii

Priva mitchellii, commonly known as Mitchell's privet, is a species of flowering plant in the Oleaceae family.

It is native to the southeastern United States, particularly Florida and the Gulf Coast. The plant is characterized by its small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and have a glossy appearance.

Priva mitchellii is often used in landscaping due to its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
