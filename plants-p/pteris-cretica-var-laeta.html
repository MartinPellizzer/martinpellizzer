Pteris cretica var. laeta

Pteris cretica var. laeta, commonly known as the golden shield fern, is a variegated variety of the common water fern.

It features striking, fan-shaped fronds with a mix of bright yellow and green hues, making it a popular ornamental plant. The fronds are typically semi-circular, with a leathery texture and a glossy appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often used in indoor gardening due to its low maintenance requirements.

This fern is native to Southeast Asia and is valued for its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
