Peperomia belangeri

Peperomia belangeri, commonly known as the Belanger's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features broad, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers indirect light, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia belangeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
