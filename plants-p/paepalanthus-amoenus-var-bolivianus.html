Paepalanthus amoenus var. bolivianus

Paepalanthus amoenus var. bolivianus, commonly known as the Bolivian star cactus, is a species native to the Andean regions of Bolivia.

It is a small, compact cactus with a distinctive star-shaped appearance, featuring numerous short, spiny stems. The plant produces bright yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking visual element to its habitat. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure to flourish.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
