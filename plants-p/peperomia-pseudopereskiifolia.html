Peperomia pseudopereskiifolia

Peperomia pseudopereskiifolia, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch, giving it its common name. The plant has a short, sturdy stem and produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia pseudopereskiifolia is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
