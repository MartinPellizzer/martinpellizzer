Panicum chrysanthum

Panicum chrysanthum, commonly known as golden panicum, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its vibrant yellow inflorescences. The plant typically grows as a perennial herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
