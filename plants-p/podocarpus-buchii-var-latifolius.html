Podocarpus buchii var. latifolius

Podocarpus buchii var. latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf podocarp, is a species of coniferous evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and narrow, cylindrical cones. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are leathery and have a distinctive aromatic scent.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture for its hardiness and aesthetic qualities.
