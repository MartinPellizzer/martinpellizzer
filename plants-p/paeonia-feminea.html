Paeonia feminea

Paeonia feminea, commonly known as the female peony, is a species of herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of central and eastern Europe.

Its foliage consists of deeply lobed, dark green leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem.

Paeonia feminea is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
