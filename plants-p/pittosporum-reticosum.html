Pittosporum reticosum

Pittosporum reticosum, commonly known as the reticulate pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage with a glossy, dark green appearance. The plant produces fragrant white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Its bark is rough and fissured, often with a reddish hue.

Pittosporum reticosum is commonly cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
