Puccinellia kanashiroi

It is characterized by its succulent, often fleshy leaves and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or purple. Its leaves are often used in traditional herbal medicine, and it plays a role in stabilizing soil in wetland ecosystems.
