Potentilla fallens

Potentilla fallens, commonly known as the falling potentilla, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It features small, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to yellow, typically blooming in early summer. The plant has alternate, simple leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a slightly fuzzy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile choice for garden settings.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular plant in horticulture.
