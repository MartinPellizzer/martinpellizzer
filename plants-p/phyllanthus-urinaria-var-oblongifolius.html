Phyllanthus urinaria var. oblongifolius

Phyllanthus urinaria var. oblongifolius, commonly known as the kidney bean plant, is a species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a small shrub characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or oblong in shape. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, round fruits containing a single seed. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate sunlight, making it suitable for cultivation in warm climates.

The plant is also noted for its potential medicinal uses in traditional herbal medicine.
