Pavonia eurychlamys

Pavonia eurychlamys, commonly known as the showy pavonia, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink, often with a distinctive yellow center. The plant typically grows as a shrub or subshrub, reaching heights of up to 3 feet, and is known for its lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is adapted to well-drained soils.

Pavonia eurychlamys is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
