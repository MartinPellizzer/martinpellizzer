Petrea arborea var. broadwayi

Petrea arborea var. broadwayi, commonly known as the Broadway petrea, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central America.

It is characterized by its glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom in the spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for warm, humid climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its aesthetic appeal.
