Payanelia

Payanelia, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, oval-shaped leaves with a distinctive silvery or grayish underside. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow and appear in clusters.

Payanelia is known for its ability to thrive in rocky and dry soils, making it a hardy plant in arid environments.
