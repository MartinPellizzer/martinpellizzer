Piper finlaysonianum

Piper finlaysonianum, commonly known as the Finlayson's pepper, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that grow in clusters. It produces small, round berries that turn red when mature, which are edible and have a peppery flavor.

This species is valued both for its ornamental qualities and its potential medicinal uses.
