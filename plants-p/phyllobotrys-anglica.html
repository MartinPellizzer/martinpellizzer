Phyllobotrys anglica

Phyllobotrys anglica, commonly known as the English leaf plant, is a perennial herb native to Europe.

It features slender, upright stems that bear alternate, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are often used in traditional herbal remedies for their mild medicinal properties.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges and meadows.
