Poincianella californica

Poincianella californica, commonly known as the California flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the southwestern United States.

It features striking, bright red or orange flower clusters that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves that are opposite and often have a velvety texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its showy flowers and attractive foliage.
