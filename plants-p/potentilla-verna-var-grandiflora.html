Potentilla verna var. grandiflora

Potentilla verna var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered cinquefoil, is a low-growing perennial shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features compound leaves with five to seven oval leaflets, and it produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in early spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders. Its ornamental value comes from its showy blooms and hardy nature.

It is also valued in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
