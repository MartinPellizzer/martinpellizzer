Pennantia odorata

Pennantia odorata, commonly known as the sweet pepper plant, is a flowering shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshade plants. The plant is characterized by its small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Its fruit, which is edible, is typically harvested when it turns a vibrant red or yellow.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and culinary uses.
