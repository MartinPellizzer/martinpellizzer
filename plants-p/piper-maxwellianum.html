Piper maxwellianum

Piper maxwellianum, commonly known as Maxwell's pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into elongated, cylindrical fruits.

The plant is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
