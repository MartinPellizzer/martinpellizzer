Pentzia argentea

Pentzia argentea, commonly known as silverleaf iceplant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, silver-gray leaves that are covered in a fine, waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, Pentzia argentea is often cultivated in regions with Mediterranean climates.
