Philodendron fontanesii

Philodendron fontanesii, commonly known as the Fontanesii Philodendron, is a climbing vine native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by bright red berries that attract birds. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its striking foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical plant collections.
