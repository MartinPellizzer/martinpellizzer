Polygonum brunonis

Polygonum brunonis, commonly known as the redstem smartweed, is a herbaceous annual plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist soils along stream banks, wet meadows, and roadside ditches. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a reddish tint and produces small, clustered flowers that are pink to purple in color. It has a sprawling growth habit and can reach heights of up to 1 meter.

Polygonum brunonis is often found in disturbed habitats and is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
