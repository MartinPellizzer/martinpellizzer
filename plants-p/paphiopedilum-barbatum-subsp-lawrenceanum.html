Paphiopedilum barbatum subsp. lawrenceanum

Paphiopedilum barbatum subsp.

lawrenceanum, commonly known as the Lawrenceanum Paphiopedilum, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia. It features a slender, hairy stem and produces large, showy flowers with a distinctive lip that is often curled or twisted.

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white with red or purple markings, adding to its ornamental appeal.

This subspecies is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
