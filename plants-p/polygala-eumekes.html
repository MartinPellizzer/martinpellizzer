Polygala eumekes

Polygala eumekes, commonly known as the blue milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the Polygalaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and often hairy, adapting to arid conditions.

Polygala eumekes is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
