Pelargonium × niveum

Pelargonium × niveum, commonly known as the snow geranium, is a hybrid shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant has fuzzy, velvety leaves that are often tinged with silver or gray, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a decorative plant.
