Plectranthus mairei

Plectranthus mairei, commonly known as the Maire's coleus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and ornamental plants. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of purple, red, and white. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and often have a velvety texture.

Plectranthus mairei is cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil and sunny conditions.
