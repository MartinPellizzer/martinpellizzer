Panicularia pauciflora

Panicularia pauciflora, commonly known as the few-flowered panicled grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces small, greenish or pale yellow inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and sandy soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a rough texture.

Panicularia pauciflora is often found in temperate regions and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
