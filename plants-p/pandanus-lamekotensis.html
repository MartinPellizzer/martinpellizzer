Pandanus lamekotensis

Pandanus lamekotensis, commonly known as the Lamekotensis pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pandanaceae.

It is native to the Pacific islands, particularly found in parts of Polynesia and Micronesia. The plant is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts and basket weaving. It produces small, clustered inflorescences with fragrant, yellowish flowers.

The plant thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to saline and humid environments.
