Pelea clusiifolia var. sapotifolia

Pelea clusiifolia var. sapotifolia, commonly known as the sapotilla pelea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and valuable species in both horticulture and ecological studies.
