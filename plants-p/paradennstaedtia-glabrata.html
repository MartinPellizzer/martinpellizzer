Paradennstaedtia glabrata

Paradennstaedtia glabrata, commonly known as the smoothleaf paradennstaedtia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically smooth and glossy, with a dark green coloration. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental appeal and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
