Polypodium werneri

Polypodium werneri, commonly known as the Werneri's shield fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizome is covered with a thick layer of sori, which are clusters of sporangia used for spore production.

The fronds are usually leathery and have a distinctive, rugged appearance.
