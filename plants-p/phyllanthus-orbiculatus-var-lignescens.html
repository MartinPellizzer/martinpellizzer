Phyllanthus orbiculatus var. lignescens

Phyllanthus orbiculatus var. lignescens, commonly known as the hardy spurges, is a perennial shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features woody stems and small, orbicular leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically inconspicuous. It is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and can tolerate drought conditions.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and resilient plant suitable for various landscaping applications.
