Premna discolor var. dianiensis

Premna discolor var. dianiensis, commonly known as the African nettle, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often pubescent. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its leaves are used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
