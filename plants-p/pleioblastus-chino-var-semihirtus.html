Pleioblastus chino var. semihirtus

Pleioblastus chino var. semihirtus, commonly known as the Chinese bamboo or silver bamboo, is a variant of the Pleioblastus chino species.

It is a type of grass-like plant native to East Asia, particularly China. This plant is characterized by its slender, arching shoots and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The foliage tends to have a silvery or grayish hue due to the presence of these hairs.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability.
