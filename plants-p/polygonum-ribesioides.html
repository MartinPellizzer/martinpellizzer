Polygonum ribesioides

Polygonum ribesioides, commonly known as the redstem smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its swollen nodes and distinctive leaf shapes. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks. Its leaves are alternate, lanceolate, and often have a reddish tint, while its small, greenish flowers appear in clusters.

Polygonum ribesioides is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed soils.
