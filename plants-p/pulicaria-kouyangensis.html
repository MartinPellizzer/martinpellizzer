Pulicaria kouyangensis

Pulicaria kouyangensis, commonly known as the Kouyang pulicaria, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are typically gray-green with a woolly texture. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
