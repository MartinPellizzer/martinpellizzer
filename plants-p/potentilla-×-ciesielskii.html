Potentilla × ciesielskii

Potentilla × ciesielskii, commonly known as the Ciesielskii cinquefoil, is a hybrid shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and its stems are often reddish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is a popular choice among gardeners in temperate climates.
