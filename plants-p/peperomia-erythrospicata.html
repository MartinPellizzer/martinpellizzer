Peperomia erythrospicata

Peperomia erythrospicata, commonly known as the red-spotted peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves with a distinctive red or purple undersurface, giving it a striking visual appearance. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia erythrospicata is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
