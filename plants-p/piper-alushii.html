Piper alushii

Piper alushii, commonly known as the Alushii pepper, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when ripe, and these berries are used in traditional medicine and cuisine. Its leaves are often used in herbal remedies due to their aromatic properties.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
