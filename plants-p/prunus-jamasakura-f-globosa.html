Prunus jamasakura f. globosa

Prunus jamasakura f. globosa, commonly known as the globe cherry, is a cultivar of the Japanese cherry tree.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and compact form, making it ideal for small spaces. The tree produces small, pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring, adding ornamental value. Its foliage is dark green and provides a striking contrast to the blossoms.

This variety is often used in urban landscaping due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
