Peperomia alsinoides

Peperomia alsinoides, commonly known as the silver mitten plant, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It features dense, waxy foliage with a distinctive silver-gray underside, giving it a striking visual appeal. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia alsinoides is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
