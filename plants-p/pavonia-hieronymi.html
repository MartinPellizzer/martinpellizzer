Pavonia hieronymi

Pavonia hieronymi, commonly known as the Brazilian pavonia, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to red bract-like flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with hairy stems and ovate leaves. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in open woodlands or along stream banks.
