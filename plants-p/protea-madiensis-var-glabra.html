Protea madiensis var. glabra

Protea madiensis var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences composed of numerous white to pinkish flowers arranged in a spherical or conical shape. The plant is native to the fynbos vegetation of the Western and Eastern Cape provinces. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun exposure, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
