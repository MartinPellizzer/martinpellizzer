Pavonia alnifolia

Pavonia alnifolia, commonly known as the silky pavonia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, sunny to partially shaded environments. The plant features slender, upright stems and heart-shaped, hairy leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. Its showy, pink to red flowers bloom in clusters during the summer months.

Pavonia alnifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.
