Porpax nummularia

Porpax nummularia, commonly known as the silver dollar plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are oval-shaped and covered in a fine, silvery-white coating. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by spherical, red berries. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
