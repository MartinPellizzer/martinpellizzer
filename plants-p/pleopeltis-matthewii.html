Pleopeltis matthewii

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its intricate, lace-like fronds that often grow on tree trunks or rocks in humid environments. Pleopeltis matthewii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in shaded, moist conditions.
