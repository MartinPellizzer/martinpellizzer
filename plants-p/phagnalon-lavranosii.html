Phagnalon lavranosii

Phagnalon lavranosii, commonly known as the Greek starwort, is a low-growing perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region, particularly found in Greece and other parts of southern Europe. The plant features slender stems and small, star-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or white, appearing in clusters during the summer months.

Phagnalon lavranosii is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
