Pleurothallis diffusiflora

Pleurothallis diffusiflora, commonly known as the spreading-flower orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale green in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
