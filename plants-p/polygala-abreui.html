Polygala abreui

Polygala abreui, commonly known as the slender milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae, which is characterized by its distinctive floral structures. The plant typically grows as a low-growing shrub with wiry stems and small, clustered leaves. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, with a unique structure that resembles a spurred petal.

Polygala abreui is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.
