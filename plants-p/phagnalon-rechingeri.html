Phagnalon rechingeri

Phagnalon rechingeri, commonly known as the mountain everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth form, which helps it survive in harsh, arid environments.
