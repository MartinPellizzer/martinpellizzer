Pyrus calleryana var. lanceata

Pyrus calleryana var. lanceata, commonly known as the Chinese white ash, is a variant of the Callerya pear.

It is a deciduous tree characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, followed by fruit that matures to a yellow or brown color. It is native to China and has been widely cultivated in ornamental horticulture due to its attractive foliage and adaptability.

The plant is also known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and climates.
