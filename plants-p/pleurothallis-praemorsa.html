Pleurothallis praemorsa

Pleurothallis praemorsa, commonly known as the death's-head orchid, is a species native to the Andes of South America.

It is a small epiphytic orchid with delicate, pale green to yellow-green flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are often marked with dark spots or patterns, giving it its ominous common name.

Pleurothallis praemorsa is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
