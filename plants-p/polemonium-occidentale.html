Polemonium occidentale

Polemonium occidentale, commonly known as Western Jacob's ladder, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features upright, branching stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue, purple, or white. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
