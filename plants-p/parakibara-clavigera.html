Parakibara clavigera

Parakibara clavigera, commonly known as the Japanese horned poppy, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers with prominent yellow stamens that resemble horns. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks. Its leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Parakibara clavigera is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
