Potentilla laevissima

Potentilla laevissima, commonly known as silver potentilla, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are elliptical, with a silvery-gray underside, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and rocky areas.
