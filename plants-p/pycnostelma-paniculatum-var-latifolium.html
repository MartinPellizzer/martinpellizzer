Pycnostelma paniculatum var. latifolium

Pycnostelma paniculatum var. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf pycnostelma, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, branching structure and small, opposite leaves. The leaves are typically broad and elliptical, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

This plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
