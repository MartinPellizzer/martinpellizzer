Poa argunensis var. tshuensis

Poa argunensis var. tshuensis, commonly known as the Tshuensky bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and bluish-green foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a dense root system that helps stabilize soil in its native habitat.

This variety is often found in high-altitude meadows and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in mountain ecosystems.
