Peperomia glabrilimba

Peperomia glabrilimba, commonly known as the smooth-leaved peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically ovate or elliptical in shape, often with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate moisture.

Peperomia glabrilimba is appreciated for its ornamental foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
