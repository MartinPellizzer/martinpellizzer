Phalaenopsis sumatrana subvar. sanguinea

Phalaenopsis sumatrana subvar. sanguinea, commonly known as the Sumatran red phalaenopsis, is a tropical orchid native to the Indonesian island of Sumatra.

It is characterized by its long, slender stems and clusters of showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from pink to deep red. The plant typically produces multiple blooms along its inflorescence, which can last for several months. It prefers bright, indirect light and moderate humidity, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique coloration and prolonged blooming period contribute to its ornamental value in horticulture.
