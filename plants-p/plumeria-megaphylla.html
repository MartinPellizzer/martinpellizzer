Plumeria megaphylla

Plumeria megaphylla, commonly known as the giant plumeria, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, waxy flowers that can reach up to 15 centimeters in diameter. The plant has broad, paddle-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Its flowers are typically white, yellow, or pink, and they bloom in the summer months.
