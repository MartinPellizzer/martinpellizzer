Peperomia riedeliana

Peperomia riedeliana, commonly known as the Riedel's peppergrass, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically oval or lance-shaped. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
