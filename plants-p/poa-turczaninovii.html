Poa turczaninovii

Poa turczaninovii, commonly known as Turczaninov's bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and bluish-green foliage. The plant typically forms dense mats and is often found in rocky or alpine environments. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets that are tightly clustered, and it produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.

Poa turczaninovii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
