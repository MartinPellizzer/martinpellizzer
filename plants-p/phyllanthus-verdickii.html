Phyllanthus verdickii

Phyllanthus verdickii, commonly known as the Indian cheeseweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often found in dry, sandy soils. Phyllanthus verdickii is also noted for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for various health purposes.
