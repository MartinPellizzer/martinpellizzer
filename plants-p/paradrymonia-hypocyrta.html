Paradrymonia hypocyrta

Paradrymonia hypocyrta, commonly known as the narrow-leafed lily.

It is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like Vietnam and Thailand. The plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, pale yellow to white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
