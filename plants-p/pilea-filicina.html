Pilea filicina

Pilea filicina, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, round, shield-shaped leaves that are often variegated with silver and green hues. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Pilea filicina is known for its easy care and ability to purify indoor air.
