Paeonia szechuanica

Paeonia szechuanica, commonly known as the Sichuan peony, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep pink, with a distinctive double petal structure. The plant produces large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

The Sichuan peony is also valued for its fragrant blooms and is often cultivated for both its aesthetic and medicinal properties.
