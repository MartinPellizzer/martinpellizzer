Paracalanthe gracilis

Paracalanthe gracilis, commonly known as the slender calanthe, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial herb with a rhizomatous root system and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces showy, spurred orchid flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is native to regions in Asia.

Due to its delicate appearance and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
