Pothos parvispadix

Pothos parvispadix, commonly known as the compact pothos, is a trailing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically a few centimeters in size, giving it a compact and dense appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers, though it is more appreciated for its foliage than its blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular houseplant.

Its adaptability and aesthetic appeal contribute to its popularity among indoor gardeners.
