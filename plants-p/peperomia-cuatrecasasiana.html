Peperomia cuatrecasasiana

Peperomia cuatrecasasiana, commonly known as the Cuatrecasas peperomia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the humid forests of Costa Rica and Panama, where it thrives in shaded, moist environments. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are typically green or brown, and it produces berries that are edible but not widely consumed.

Peperomia cuatrecasasiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its low maintenance requirements.
