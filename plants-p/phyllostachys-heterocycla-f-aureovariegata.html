Phyllostachys heterocycla f. aureovariegata

Phyllostachys heterocycla f. aureovariegata, commonly known as the golden bamboo or variegated bamboo, is a cultivar of the bamboo genus Phyllostachys.

It is characterized by its striking yellow and green variegated leaves, which provide a vibrant visual contrast. This plant is a clump-forming bamboo that grows rapidly, reaching heights of up to 10 meters in suitable conditions. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained, fertile soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
