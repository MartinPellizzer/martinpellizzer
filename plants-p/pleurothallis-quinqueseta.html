Pleurothallis quinqueseta

Pleurothallis quinqueseta, commonly known as the five-lobed pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are usually ovate to lanceolate, with a smooth surface and a waxy coating.

Pleurothallis quinqueseta is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
