Palaquium membranaceum

Palaquium membranaceum, commonly known as the membrane palaquium, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by oval, smooth fruits containing a single seed.

It is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
