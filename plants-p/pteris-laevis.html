Pteris laevis

Pteris laevis, commonly known as the smooth water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Pteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically light green in color. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizomes are creeping and help in the spread of the plant across the soil surface.

Pteris laevis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
