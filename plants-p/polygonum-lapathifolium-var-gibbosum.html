Polygonum lapathifolium var. gibbosum

Polygonum lapathifolium var. gibbosum, commonly known as the rounded-leaved smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its distinctive nodes and hollow stems. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly wavy margin, and it produces small, greenish-white flowers in dense clusters. It thrives in moist soils along stream banks and wetlands, often forming dense stands.

This species is known for its ability to tolerate a range of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in wetland ecosystems.
