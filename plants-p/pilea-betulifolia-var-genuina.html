Pilea betulifolia var. genuina

Pilea betulifolia var. genuina, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green, with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Its ability to propagate through stem cuttings adds to its appeal among indoor gardeners.
