Primula Farinosa Var. Hannasanensis

Primula farinosa var. hannasanensis is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Korea. Known for its small, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink, it thrives in cool, moist environments. This variant of the common primrose is particularly prized for its unique appearance and limited distribution. Due to habitat loss and climate change, it faces increasing threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this beautiful and fragile species for future generations.
