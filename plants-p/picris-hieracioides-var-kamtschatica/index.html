Picris Hieracioides Var. Kamtschatica

Kamtschatka st. john's wort, scientifically known as Picris hieracioides var. kamtschatica, is a rare and resilient plant native to the cold climates of the Russian Far East. It thrives in rocky and alpine environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. This variant of st. john's wort is distinguished by its unique floral structure and medicinal properties. Its presence in its natural habitat highlights the biodiversity of the Kamtschatka region. Despite its ecological significance, it remains relatively unknown to the wider botanical community.
