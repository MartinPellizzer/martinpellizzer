Polystichum tagawanum f. atrosquamatum

Polystichum tagawanum f. atrosquamatum, commonly known as the dark-scaled Japanese shield fern, is a fern species native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its dark, leathery fronds with a distinctive, glossy appearance. The fronds are typically bipinnate, with closely spaced, rounded pinnae that give the plant a dense, shield-like appearance. The plant's unique morphology and ornamental qualities make it a popular choice for horticultural use in shaded garden settings.
