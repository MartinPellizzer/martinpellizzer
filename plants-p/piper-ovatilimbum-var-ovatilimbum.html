Piper ovatilimbum var. ovatilimbum

Piper ovatilimbum var. ovatilimbum, commonly known as the broad-leafed pepper vine, is a tropical climbing shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into three-lobed berries. Its botanical structure supports its role as a climbing plant, with adventitious roots aiding in attachment to supports.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
