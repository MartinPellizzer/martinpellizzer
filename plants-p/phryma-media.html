Phryma media

Phryma media, commonly known as the broadleaf plantain, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture. The plant produces small, clustered greenish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. Its roots are fibrous and can spread extensively, making it a resilient species.

It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.
