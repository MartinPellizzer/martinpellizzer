Paronychia arabica var. macrathera

Paronychia arabica var. macrathera, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sandthrift, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, upright stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in garden settings.
