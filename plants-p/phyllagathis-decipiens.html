Phyllagathis decipiens

Phyllagathis decipiens, commonly known as the deceptive phyllagathis, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is a small shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical in shape, and have a smooth texture.
