Panicum ruprechtii

Panicum ruprechtii, commonly known as Ruprecht's panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a grass species that typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. The plant produces tall, slender stems with narrow, grass-like leaves and panicles of small, spikelet-based flowers. Its flowering period usually occurs during the summer months, and it is known for its adaptability to dry conditions.

Panicum ruprechtii is often used in ornamental gardening for its graceful appearance and drought tolerance.
