Pachyphyllum peperomioides

Pachyphyllum peperomioides, commonly known as the silver star cactus, is a succulent native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically reduced to spines, which help minimize water loss.

The species is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
