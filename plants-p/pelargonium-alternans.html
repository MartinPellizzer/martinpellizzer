Pelargonium alternans

Pelargonium alternans, commonly known as the zigzag geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems and opposite leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Pelargonium alternans is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
