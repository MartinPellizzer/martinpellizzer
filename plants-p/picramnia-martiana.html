Picramnia martiana

Picramnia martiana, commonly known as the purple flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in clusters, often in late spring or early summer. The plant has compound leaves with several oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species in warm climates.

Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it a notable addition to gardens and landscapes.
