Pseudomyrcianthes kochiana

Pseudomyrcianthes kochiana, commonly known as the Brazilian pepper tree, is a flowering plant native to South America.

The plant produces small, red, fleshy berries that are an important food source for various wildlife species. It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in moist, well-drained soils.
