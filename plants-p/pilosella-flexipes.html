Pilosella flexipes

Pilosella flexipes, commonly known as the hairy fleabane, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish to white flowers that are typically arranged in flat-topped clusters. Pilosella flexipes is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and open woodland areas.

It is an herbaceous perennial that blooms from late spring to early summer.
