Prioria micrantha

Prioria micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered prioria, is a deciduous shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, and it is often found in moist, tropical habitats.

Its botanical features make it an important component of local ecosystems, supporting various pollinators and wildlife.
