Panicum cienkowskii

Panicum cienkowskii, commonly known as the Cienkowsky panicgrass, is a species of grass native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has fibrous roots and is adapted to arid or semi-arid conditions.

Panicum cienkowskii is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its resilience and ability to stabilize soil.
