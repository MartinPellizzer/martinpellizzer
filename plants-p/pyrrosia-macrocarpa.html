Pyrrosia macrocarpa

Pyrrosia macrocarpa, commonly known as the giant shield fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, leathery, shield-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its fronds are often divided into segments with a distinctive, wavy margin.

Pyrrosia macrocarpa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded gardens or as a houseplant.
