Pennisetum sieberianum

Pennisetum sieberianum, commonly known as silver grass, is a perennial grass native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and silvery-blue foliage that gives it a striking visual appearance. The plant produces panicles of small, fluffy flowers that are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Pennisetum sieberianum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
