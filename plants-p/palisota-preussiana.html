Palisota preussiana

Palisota preussiana, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowhead, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically green and glossy, with a distinctive arrowhead shape. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Palisota preussiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in aquatic environments.
