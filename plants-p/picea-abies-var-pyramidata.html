Picea abies var. pyramidata

Picea abies var. pyramidata, commonly known as the Norwegian spruce pyramidal form, is a variant of the Norway spruce characterized by its narrowly conical shape.

It features dense, dark green foliage that is typically arranged in sharp, vertical clusters. The cones are slender and pointed, maturing to a reddish-brown color. It is a slow-growing evergreen tree that thrives in cool, moist climates with well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes due to its distinctive appearance.
