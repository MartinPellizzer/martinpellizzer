Piperi nematantherum

Piperi nematantherum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, strap-like leaves and small, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil to flourish. Its unique morphology and restricted habitat make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat destruction, it is considered endangered and is often found in protected areas.
