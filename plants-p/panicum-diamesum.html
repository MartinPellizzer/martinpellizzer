Panicum diamesum

Panicum diamesum, commonly known as slender panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. Its leaves are narrow and often have a bluish-green hue, and it is often found in disturbed soils or open habitats.

Panicum diamesum is adapted to thrive in environments with full sun and well-drained soil.
