Plantago benisnassenii

Plantago benisnassenii, commonly known as the Benisnassen plant, is a species of plantain native to parts of North Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, rocky, or sandy soils. The plant features a rosette of broad, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy surface. It produces small, greenish flowers arranged in a spike, which are wind-pollinated.

This species is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
