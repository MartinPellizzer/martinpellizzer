Premna puerensis

Premna puerensis, commonly known as the Chinese nettle or small-leaved nettle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to regions of East Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy and have a serrated edge. Its small, pale blue or purple flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces small, oval fruits.

Premna puerensis is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
