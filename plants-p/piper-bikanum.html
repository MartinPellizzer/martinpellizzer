Piper bikanum

Piper bikanum, commonly known as Bikan kew or Bikan, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. It produces small, round berries that are often used in traditional medicine.

The botanical features of Piper bikanum make it a valuable species in both horticulture and pharmacological research.
