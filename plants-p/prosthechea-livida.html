Prosthechea livida

Prosthechea livida, commonly known as the green rosette orchid, is a tropical orchid species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit with fleshy, dark green leaves. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences bearing small, white or pale green flowers with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Prosthechea livida is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
