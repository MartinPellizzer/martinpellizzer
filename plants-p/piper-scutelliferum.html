Piper scutelliferum

Piper scutelliferum, commonly known as the scarlet pepper plant, is a tropical vine native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its bright red, peppery fruits that resemble small peppers. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves and thrives in humid, warm climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of spices.

The fruits are typically harvested when fully mature for their unique flavor and medicinal properties.
