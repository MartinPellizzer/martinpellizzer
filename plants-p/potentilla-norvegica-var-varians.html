Potentilla norvegica var. varians

Potentilla norvegica var. varians, commonly known as the Norwegian cinquefoil, is a perennial shrub native to northern Europe.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and a compact growth habit, often forming dense thickets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is commonly found in rocky or alpine environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and naturalized areas.
