Phyllostachys pubescens f. bicolor

Phyllostachys pubescens f. bicolor, commonly known as the bamboo with variegated leaves, is a striking ornamental grass native to China.

It features slender, upright culms that are often tinged with red or pink, adding visual interest to gardens. The leaves are lance-shaped and display a unique bicolor pattern, typically with green on the upper surface and a lighter, often white or cream, underside. This variety is prized for its ability to thrive in a range of conditions, making it a popular choice for landscaping.

Its growth habit is clump-forming, and it can reach heights of up to 6 feet, providing both texture and color in the garden.
