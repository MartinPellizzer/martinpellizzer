Pteris tripartita var. milneana

Pteris tripartita var. milneana, commonly known as the Milne's water fern, is a variant of the common water fern, characterized by its distinctive fronds.

It is a fern belonging to the family Pteridaceae, and it is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and the Philippines. The fronds are pinnately divided, with a triangular shape that gives the plant its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near streams or in humid forests.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative fern in gardens and indoor spaces.
