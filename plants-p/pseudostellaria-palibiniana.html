Pseudostellaria palibiniana

Pseudostellaria palibiniana, commonly known as the Chinese starwort, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and small, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while its flowers are pale pink to white in color.

Pseudostellaria palibiniana is often used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
