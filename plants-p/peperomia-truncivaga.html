Peperomia truncivaga

Peperomia truncivaga, commonly known as the rounded peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval or rounded in shape, with a textured surface that helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color, which are followed by capsule fruits containing the seeds. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its slow growth rate and low maintenance needs make it an ideal plant for both novice and experienced plant lovers.
