Pelargonium × broadleyae

Pelargonium × broadleyae, commonly known as the broadleya geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically produces clusters of five to seven petals, with a distinctive, elongated shape. Native to South Africa, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners and horticulturists.
