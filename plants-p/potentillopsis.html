Potentillopsis

Potentillopsis, commonly known as the rosemary family, is a group of flowering plants that includes several genera such as Potentilla and Rosa.

These plants are typically shrubs or herbs with simple, alternate leaves and showy, often fragrant flowers. They are found in a variety of habitats, from arctic tundras to temperate woodlands. Many species are valued for their ornamental appeal and are used in horticulture.
