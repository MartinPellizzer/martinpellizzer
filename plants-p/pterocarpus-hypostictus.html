Pterocarpus hypostictus

Pterocarpus hypostictus, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The tree produces flat, woody pods that contain several seeds, and its leaves are pinnately compound with small, oval leaflets.

It is valued for its durable wood, which is used in furniture and construction, and for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
