Peperomia lasierrana

Peperomia lasierrana, commonly known as the serrated peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features small, waxy, ovate leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia lasierrana is a low-maintenance plant that is well-suited for indoor cultivation.
