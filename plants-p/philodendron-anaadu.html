Philodendron anaadu

Philodendron anaadu, commonly known as the Congo heartleaf, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and veins. The plant produces small, pale green flowers that are not typically showy, and it is primarily cultivated for its ornamental foliage rather than its blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and humidity to flourish.

Philodendron anaadu is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its lush appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
