Pilea nudicaulis var. latifolia

Pilea nudicaulis var. latifolia, commonly known as the Shiny Pilea, is a compact, low-growing houseplant native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a silvery sheen, giving it a unique and eye-catching appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its attractive foliage and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
