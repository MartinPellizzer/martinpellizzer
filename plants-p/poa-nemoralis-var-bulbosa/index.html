Poa Nemoralis Var. Bulbosa

Poa nemoralis var. bulbosa is a lesser-known grass species that thrives in shaded, moist environments. This variant of the common wood sedge is often found in forested areas and along stream banks. Its unique bulbous base gives it a distinct appearance compared to other grasses. While not widely cultivated, it plays an important role in local ecosystems. Understanding this plant helps highlight the diversity of grasses in natural habitats.
