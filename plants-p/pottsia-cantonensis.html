Pottsia cantonensis

Pottsia cantonensis, commonly known as the Guangdong pottsia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cupressaceae.

It is a small, evergreen shrub characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and scale-like leaves. Native to southern China, particularly Guangdong province, it thrives in humid, subtropical climates. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by cone-like structures.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
