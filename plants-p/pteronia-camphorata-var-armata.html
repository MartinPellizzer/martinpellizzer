Pteronia camphorata var. armata

Pteronia camphorata var. armata, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, attracting pollinators like birds and insects. The plant has aromatic leaves that emit a strong, camphor-like scent, contributing to its medicinal uses. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and Mediterranean climates.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
