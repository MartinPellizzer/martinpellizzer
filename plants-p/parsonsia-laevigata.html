Parsonsia laevigata

Parsonsia laevigata, commonly known as the silky nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a red or purple throat. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a smooth, waxy texture.

The fruit is a fleshy capsule that splits open to release numerous small, smooth seeds.
