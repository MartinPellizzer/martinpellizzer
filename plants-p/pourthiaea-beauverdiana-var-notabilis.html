Pourthiaea beauverdiana var. notabilis

Pourthiaea beauverdiana var. notabilis, commonly known as the Cape goldenbush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, golden-yellow inflorescences. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
