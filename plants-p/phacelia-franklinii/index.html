Phacelia Franklinii

Phacelia franklinii is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the western United States. It is known for its striking blue-violet blooms that appear in late spring. This species thrives in rocky and arid environments, often found in mountainous regions. Its limited distribution makes it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Protecting Phacelia franklinii is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its fragile habitat.
