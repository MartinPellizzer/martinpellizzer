Petalolepis Coronata

Coral tree, scientifically known as Petalolepis coronata, is a striking flowering plant native to southern Africa. Its vibrant, flame-like blossoms display a unique combination of red, orange, and yellow hues, creating a dazzling visual display. The tree is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated in gardens and public spaces. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. With its distinctive appearance and seasonal blooms, the coral tree is a beloved addition to landscapes around the world.
