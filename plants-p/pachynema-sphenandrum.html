Pachynema sphenandrum

Pachynema sphenandrum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to parts of Asia, particularly China. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream sides.

Its botanical features make it an important component of its native ecosystem.
