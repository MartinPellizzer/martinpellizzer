Piper mestonii

Piper mestonii, commonly known as Meston's pepper, is a tropical vine native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when mature, containing aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are often used in local herbal remedies due to their medicinal properties.

Piper mestonii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
