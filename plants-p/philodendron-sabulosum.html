Philodendron sabulosum

Philodendron sabulosum, commonly known as the sand-veined philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves with prominent veins that give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by spiny, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical plant collections.
