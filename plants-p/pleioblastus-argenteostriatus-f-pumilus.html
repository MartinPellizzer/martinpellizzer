Pleioblastus argenteostriatus f. pumilus

Pleioblastus argenteostriatus f. pumilus, commonly known as the silver-striped bamboo, is a compact variety of bamboo characterized by its fine, slender culms.

It features a striking silver-green coloration with distinctive white stripes running along the stems. This variety is native to eastern Asia and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant in indoor settings.

The plant grows slowly, making it suitable for small spaces and easy to maintain.
