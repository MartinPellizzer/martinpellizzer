Pandanus ater

Pandanus ater, commonly known as the black pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and long, narrow leaves that form a rosette at the top. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its roots are fibrous and can be used in traditional crafts and construction.

The plant thrives in humid, coastal environments and is often found near mangroves or in sandy soils.
