Peperomia glabripes

Peperomia glabripes, commonly known as the smoothleaf peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance houseplant native to Central and South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slightly glossy texture, often adorned with subtle variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pale green flowers that emerge from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia glabripes is prized for its compact growth habit and air-purifying qualities, making it an excellent choice for indoor gardening.
