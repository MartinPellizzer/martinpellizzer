Phyllanthus hongkongensis

Phyllanthus hongkongensis, commonly known as Hong Kong spurge, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by spherical, three-lobed berries.
