Promicrantha Alyssifolia

Promicrantha alyssifolia is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the arid regions of South Africa. It belongs to the family Aizoaceae, commonly known as the ice plant family, and is characterized by its small, fleshy leaves and vibrant, star-shaped flowers. Its unique appearance and ecological importance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
