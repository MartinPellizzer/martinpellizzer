Pentas warburgiana

Pentas warburgiana, commonly known as the flame flower or red pentas, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from red to pink and yellow. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and slightly hairy, with a rounded to ovate shape.

The flowers bloom in clusters, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.
