Pilea weddellii

Pilea weddellii, commonly known as the Weddell's pilea, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly textured surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by spherical, red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements contribute to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
