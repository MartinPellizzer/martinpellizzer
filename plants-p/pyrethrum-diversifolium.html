Pyrethrum diversifolium

Pyrethrum diversifolium, commonly known as the alpine pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces pyrethrins, natural insecticides that are effective against a variety of pests. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in gardens and used in natural pest control.
