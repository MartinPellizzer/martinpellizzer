Ptilotus obovatus

Ptilotus obovatus, commonly known as the "Bottlebrush Bush," is a flowering plant native to Australia.

The plant has small, obovate leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, contributing to its overall textured appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

Ptilotus obovatus is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
