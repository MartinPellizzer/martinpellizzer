Phenax rugosus var. mandonii

Phenax rugosus var. mandonii, commonly known as the Mandon's rough phenax, is a rare and distinctive plant native to specific regions of southern Africa.

The plant produces unique, showy flowers that are typically blue or violet in color, adding to its ornamental appeal.
