Planetanthemum

Planetanthemum, commonly known as the starflower, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its showy, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red. These plants are native to various regions in the Americas and are often found in open, sunny habitats. Planetanthemum species are perennial herbs with opposite, lance-shaped leaves.

They are valued for their ornamental appeal and are sometimes cultivated in gardens for their vibrant floral displays.
