Protium guacayanum

Protium guacayanum, commonly known as the Guanabana tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Sapindaceae and is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves. The tree produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into large, inedible fruits with a thick, spiky rind. The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitats.
