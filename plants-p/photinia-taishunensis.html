Photinia taishunensis

Photinia taishunensis, commonly known as the Taishun photinia, is a shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, glossy foliage. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by red berries in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its hardy nature and attractive appearance make it suitable for various landscape applications.
