Persea sichuanensis

Persea sichuanensis, commonly known as the Sichuan avocado, is a small evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits with a thick, leathery skin and a creamy, oily pulp. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in subtropical climates.

Due to its ornamental value and potential economic importance, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
