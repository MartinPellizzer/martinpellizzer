Paraderris montana

Paraderris montana, commonly known as the mountain paraderris, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is a popular choice among gardeners in Mediterranean climates.
