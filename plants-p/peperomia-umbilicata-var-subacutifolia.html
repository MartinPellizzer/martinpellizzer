Peperomia umbilicata var. subacutifolia

Peperomia umbilicata var. subacutifolia, commonly known as the umbilical peperomia, is a compact, trailing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, ovate leaves with a slightly pointed tip and a velvety texture, often displaying a deep green to reddish hue. The plant produces delicate, spadix-like inflorescences that are typically hidden within the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant.
