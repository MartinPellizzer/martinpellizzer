Pelargonium chamaedrifolium

Pelargonium chamaedrifolium, commonly known as the rock geranium, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, clustered flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
