Panicum magnum

Panicum magnum, commonly known as giant panicgrass, is a tall, grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features long, narrow leaves and produces fluffy, panicle-shaped seed heads that are often wind-pollinated. The plant is characterized by its robust growth and ability to thrive in full sun and well-drained soils. It is often found in disturbed areas, such as roadsides and fields, due to its adaptability.

Its botanical structure makes it an important species in ecological studies and ornamental horticulture.
