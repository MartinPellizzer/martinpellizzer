Phlox

Phlox, commonly known as the blazing star, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Polemoniaceae.

These plants are native to North America and are characterized by their showy, clustered flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, purple, and white. They are popular garden plants due to their attractive appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.
