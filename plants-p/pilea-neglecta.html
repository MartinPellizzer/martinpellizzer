Pilea neglecta

Pilea neglecta, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features round, shield-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea neglecta is known for its ease of care and ability to propagate through stem cuttings.
