Panicum omega

Panicum omega, commonly known as silverleaf panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, silver-gray blades with a distinctive waxy coating that helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, pale green to brown spikelets that are typically arranged in panicles. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or disturbed areas.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal, it is sometimes used in landscaping and erosion control.
