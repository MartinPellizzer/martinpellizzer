Piper minutescabiosum var. arborescens

Piper minutescabiosum var. arborescens, commonly known as the tree pepper, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature. It is often cultivated for its aromatic leaves and medicinal properties.

The varietal form, arborescens, distinguishes it by its more tree-like growth habit compared to other varieties.
