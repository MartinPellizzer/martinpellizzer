Piper san-lorenzanum

Piper san-lorenzanum, commonly known as San Lorenzo pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with opposite, ovate leaves that are often glossy in appearance. Its small, clustered flowers are typically greenish-white in color, and it produces small, peppery fruits that are used in traditional medicine.

The plant is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in folk remedies for various ailments.
