Polyscias thailandica

Polyscias thailandica, commonly known as Thai gloryberry, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
