Phoenix dactylifera var. adunca

Phoenix dactylifera var. adunca, commonly known as the slender date palm, is a variety of the date palm native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its narrow, upright trunk and smaller, more compact leaves compared to the standard date palm. The plant produces small, clustered date fruits that are typically harvested before they fully ripen. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid climates, making it a resilient choice for cultivation in hot, dry regions.

This variety is often used for ornamental purposes due to its elegant appearance and drought tolerance.
