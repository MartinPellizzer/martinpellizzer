Paulseniella pamirensis

Paulseniella pamirensis, commonly known as the Pamir stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Pamir Mountains.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit and fleshy, gray-green leaves that store water. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and white, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, arid environments.

Paulseniella pamirensis is a hardy species often found in alpine meadows and mountainous areas with limited vegetation.
