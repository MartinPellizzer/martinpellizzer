Phoenix loureiroi

Phoenix loureiroi, commonly known as the loureiro palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the island of Madeira, Portugal, and is characterized by its slender, unbranched trunk and pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that develop into red, fleshy fruits containing seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to grow in well-drained soils with full sun exposure.

The loureiro palm is also notable for its ability to thrive in coastal environments.
