Piper orthostachyum

Piper orthostachyum, commonly known as the upright-stemmed pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and compound leaves with serrated edges. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into small, round berries.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
