Peperomia puberulispica

Peperomia puberulispica, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia puberulispica is an excellent choice for indoor gardening due to its resilience and ornamental appeal.
