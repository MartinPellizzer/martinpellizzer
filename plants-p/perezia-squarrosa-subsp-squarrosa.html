Perezia squarrosa subsp. squarrosa

Perezia squarrosa subsp.

squarrosa, commonly known as the squarrosa perezia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing adaptations to arid environments.

The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine, white waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss. Its flowers are small, clustered in umbels, and usually display shades of pink or white.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging soil conditions.
