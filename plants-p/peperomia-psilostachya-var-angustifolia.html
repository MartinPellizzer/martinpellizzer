Peperomia psilostachya var. angustifolia

Peperomia psilostachya var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are often slightly curled at the edges. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of green or red, which are not as showy as the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate watering.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
