Panicum sanguinale var. brevifolium

Panicum sanguinale var. brevifolium, commonly known as red panic grass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like blades and produces panicles of red or purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in open fields and disturbed areas. Its foliage is typically narrow and short, giving it a compact appearance.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in landscaping.
