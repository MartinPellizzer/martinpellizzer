Polystichopsis sericea

Polystichopsis sericea, commonly known as the silky fern, is a species of fern that belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, silky-hair-covered fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. Native to regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of its fertile fronds.

The plant's ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation in shaded gardens.
