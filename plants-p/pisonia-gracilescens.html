Pisonia gracilescens

Pisonia gracilescens, commonly known as the slender pisonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant produces bright red, fleshy fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by birds. It thrives in humid, sunny environments and is often found in coastal areas or near rivers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain ecosystems.
