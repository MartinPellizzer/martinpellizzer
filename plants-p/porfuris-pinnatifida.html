Porfuris pinnatifida

Porfuris pinnatifida, commonly known as the broadleaf porfuris, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, pinnately compound leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in dense forest undergrowth.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and traditional medicine.
