Paphiopedilum micranthum f. singchianum

Paphiopedilum micranthum f. singchianum, commonly known as the Singchian Orchid, is a rare hybrid orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration pattern. The plant typically features dark green, velvety leaves and blooms in shades of white and pink, often with a contrasting lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its rarity and horticultural appeal, it is highly valued among orchid enthusiasts.
