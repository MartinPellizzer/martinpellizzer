Pelargonium × ardens

Pelargonium × ardens, commonly known as the Zonal Geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors such as red, pink, and purple. The plant typically has rounded, velvety leaves with distinct color zones, which give it its common name. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Pelargonium × ardens is widely cultivated as an ornamental plant for gardens and container planting due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
