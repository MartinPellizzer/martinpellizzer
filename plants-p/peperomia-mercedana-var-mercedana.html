Peperomia mercedana var. mercedana

Peperomia mercedana var. mercedana, commonly known as the Mercedana Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, often in shades of white or green. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in moderate light conditions.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for collectors and indoor gardeners alike.
