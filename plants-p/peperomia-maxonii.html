Peperomia maxonii

Peperomia maxonii, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, and often velvety leaves that are typically green with prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal watering needs.

Peperomia maxonii is valued for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments.
