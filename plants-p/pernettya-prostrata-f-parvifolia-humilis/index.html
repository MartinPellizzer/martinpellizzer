Pernettya Prostrata F. Parvifolia-Humilis

Pernettya prostrata f. parvifolia-humilis is a rare and delicate plant belonging to the Ericaceae family. It is known for its small, clustered leaves and pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring. This plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest undergrowth. Its unique appearance makes it a sought-after specimen among plant enthusiasts. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered vulnerable and requires careful conservation efforts.
