Pfaffia reticulata

Pfaffia reticulata, commonly known as Brazilian ginseng, is a climbing plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces red, berry-like fruits that contain seeds. It is often found growing on trees or in shaded undergrowth.
