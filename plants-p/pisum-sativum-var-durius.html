Pisum sativum var. durius

Pisum sativum var. durius, commonly known as the hardy garden pea, is a variety of the common pea plant.

It is characterized by its robust growth habit and resistance to environmental stress. The plant produces pods that contain edible seeds, which are rich in protein and nutrients. Its botanical features include compound leaves and small, fragrant flowers.

This variety is often cultivated for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
