Phyllanthus gardneri

Phyllanthus gardneri, commonly known as the African buttonweed, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features simple, opposite leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several small, smooth seeds.

Phyllanthus gardneri is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
