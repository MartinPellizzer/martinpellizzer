Pandiaka oblanceolata

Pandiaka oblanceolata, commonly known as the broadleaf pandiaka, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun exposure.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
