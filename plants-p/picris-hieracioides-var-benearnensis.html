Picris hieracioides var. benearnensis

Picris hieracioides var. benearnensis, commonly known as the Benearn's St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small clusters of flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are linear and slightly hairy, while the flowers are yellow with prominent central disk flowers.

It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along coastal areas.
