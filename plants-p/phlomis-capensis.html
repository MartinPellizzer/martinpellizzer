Phlomis capensis

Phlomis capensis, commonly known as Cape thyme, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal areas. The plant features gray-green, aromatic leaves and produces spikes of pale purple or pink flowers. It is well adapted to dry climates and requires minimal water once established.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and ornamental plant suitable for xeriscaping.
