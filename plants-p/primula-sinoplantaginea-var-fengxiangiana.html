Primula sinoplantaginea var. fengxiangiana

Primula sinoplantaginea var. fengxiangiana, commonly known as the Fengxiang primrose, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing habit and clusters of bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically features broad, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers, which range in color from white to pale pink, bloom in early spring.

It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.
