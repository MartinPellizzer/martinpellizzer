Pelargonium patulum var. latilobum

Pelargonium patulum var. latilobum, commonly known as the broad-lobed geranium, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems and clusters of showy, pink to red flowers with five petals. The leaves are palmately lobed, often with a waxy texture and a distinctive dark green color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
