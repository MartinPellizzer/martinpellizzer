Phyllostachys purpurata f. decurtata

Phyllostachys purpurata f. decurtata, commonly known as the purple bamboo, is a variant of the purple bamboo species native to China.

It is characterized by its striking reddish-brown culms that transition to a purplish hue, especially under sunlight. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often a mix of green and purple tones. It is a clump-forming bamboo that grows in dense stands, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

This variety is valued for its unique coloration and adaptability to various soil conditions.
