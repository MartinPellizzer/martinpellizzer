Peperomia stelechophila

Peperomia stelechophila, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically deep green with a velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal water needs.

Peperomia stelechophila is valued for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for houseplants.
