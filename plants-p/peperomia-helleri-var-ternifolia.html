Peperomia helleri var. ternifolia

Peperomia helleri var. ternifolia, commonly known as the Three-leaf Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique leaf arrangement and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to shaded or semi-shaded spaces.
