Pilea topensis

Pilea topensis, commonly known as the Mexican evergreen, is a compact, bushy plant native to Mexico.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a slight variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by round, fleshy fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Pilea topensis is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
