Poa chapelieri

Poa chapelieri, commonly known as the chapelier grass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It typically forms dense, mat-like growths and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and compact inflorescences. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.

Poa chapelieri is also used in horticulture for its hardiness and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
