Pleurothallis deuterodriessenii

Pleurothallis deuterodriessenii, commonly known as the Deuterodriessen's pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, intricate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant grows as an epiphyte, often found clinging to tree branches in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it produces elongated, pendant inflorescences.

Due to its delicate appearance and specific habitat requirements, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
