Prunus pseudocerasus var. elegans

Prunus pseudocerasus var. elegans, commonly known as the Chinese date plum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black, edible fruits that are rich in nutrients and have a sweet, tart flavor. The plant produces small, white flowers in early spring, which are followed by the fruiting season in late summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy green appearance.

This variety is valued for both ornamental purposes and its culinary uses in traditional Chinese cuisine.
