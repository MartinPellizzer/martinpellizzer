Psathyrostachys dagestanica subsp. dagestanica

Psathyrostachys dagestanica subsp.

dagestanica, commonly known as Dagestan grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Caucasus region. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is well adapted to temperate climates and can tolerate a range of soil conditions.

This subspecies is of interest in botanical studies due to its genetic diversity and potential agricultural value.
