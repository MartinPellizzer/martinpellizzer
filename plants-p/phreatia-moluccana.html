Phreatia moluccana

Phreatia moluccana, commonly known as the Moluccan orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to the Indo-Pacific region.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its pendulous, showy flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant has fleshy, leathery leaves that help it store water in its natural habitat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Phreatia moluccana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
