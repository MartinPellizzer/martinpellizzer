Prunus henryi

Prunus henryi, commonly known as the Chinese cherry or Henry cherry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It features small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by dark red or black berries. The plant has simple, ovate leaves with serrated edges and a smooth, greyish-bark trunk. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.

Prunus henryi is also used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
