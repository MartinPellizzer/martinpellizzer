Psammochloa Villosa

Psammochloa villosa is a hardy grass species native to arid and semi-arid regions. It thrives in sandy soils and is commonly found in desert environments. This plant is known for its ability to withstand extreme temperatures and limited water availability. Its fluffy, silver-green leaves help reduce water loss through evaporation. Psammochloa villosa plays a vital role in stabilizing dunes and preventing soil erosion.
