Piper dagatpanum

Piper dagatpanum, commonly known as the sea hibiscus or coastal pepper, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its slender, woody stems and opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by fleshy, red berries.

This species is adapted to coastal environments and is often found growing near the shoreline.
