Peperomia vulcanicola var. punctatissima

Peperomia vulcanicola var. punctatissima, commonly known as the volcanic peperomia, is a unique plant native to the volcanic regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and striking, dark green leaves with a distinctive, speckled pattern. The leaves are often covered in a fine, powdery coating, which helps the plant retain moisture in its arid habitat. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color.

This variety is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.
