Parsonsia mollis

Parsonsia mollis, commonly known as the silky parson's hat, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, pendant-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a distinctive red or purple throat. The plant features smooth, glabrous stems and leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or lowland areas.

Parsonsia mollis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
