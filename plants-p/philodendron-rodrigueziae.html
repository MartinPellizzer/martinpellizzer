Philodendron rodrigueziae

Philodendron rodrigueziae, commonly known as the Rodriguez's philodendron, is a climbing plant native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by green, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical plant collections.
