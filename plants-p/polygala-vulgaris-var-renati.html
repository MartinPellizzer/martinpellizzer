Polygala vulgaris var. renati

Polygala vulgaris var. renati, commonly known as the Renati's milkwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features slender stems with small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
