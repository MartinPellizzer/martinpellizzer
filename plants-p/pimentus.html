Pimentus

Pimentus, commonly known as the pimento plant, is a species of shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, peppers, and potatoes. The plant produces small, yellow or orange berries that are often used in cooking and as a spice. Its leaves are ovate and have a slightly hairy texture.

The pimento plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to grow optimally.
