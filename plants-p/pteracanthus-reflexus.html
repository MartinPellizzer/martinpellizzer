Pteracanthus reflexus

Pteracanthus reflexus, commonly known as the reflexed pteracanthus, is a lesser-known member of the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves that give it a delicate appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in umbels, typical of its family. It thrives in moist, shady environments, often found in woodland areas.
