Potentilla kleiniana var. robusta

Potentilla kleiniana var. robusta, commonly known as the robust potentilla, is a shrub native to the Pacific Northwest of the United States.

It features dense, upright growth with bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy choice for garden landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
