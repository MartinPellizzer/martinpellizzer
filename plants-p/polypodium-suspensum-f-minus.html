Polypodium suspensum f. minus

Polypodium suspensum f. minus, commonly known as the lesser climbing fern, is a rare fern species belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that typically grow in a trailing or climbing habit. The plant prefers shaded, moist environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its small, spore-bearing sporangia are clustered on the undersides of the fronds.

This fern is notable for its unique morphological features and is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
