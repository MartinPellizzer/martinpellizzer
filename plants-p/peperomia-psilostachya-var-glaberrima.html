Peperomia psilostachya var. glaberrima

Peperomia psilostachya var. glaberrima, commonly known as the glabrous peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dark green, velvety leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are often overlooked due to their modest appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
