Piper tenellum

Piper tenellum, commonly known as the slender pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into three-lobed berries.

Piper tenellum is valued for its aromatic leaves, which are used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.
