Procris pedunculata var. ornata

Procris pedunculata var. ornata, commonly known as the ornate procris, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, pendulous inflorescences and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth or along stream banks. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a velvety texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
