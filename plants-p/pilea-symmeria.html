Pilea symmeria

Pilea symmeria, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features round, waxy leaves that are often referred to as "Chinese coins," giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are typically hidden beneath its foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea symmeria is known for its ease of care and ability to propagate through stem cuttings.
