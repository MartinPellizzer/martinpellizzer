Pleurothallis megalops

Pleurothallis megalops, commonly known as the giant-leaf pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, strap-like leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and growth habits, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
