Piper formosum

Piper formosum, commonly known as the Chinese pepper vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. It produces small, peppery fruits that are used in traditional medicine and cuisine.

Its rapid growth and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for gardeners in warm climates.
