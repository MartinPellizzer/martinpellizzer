Peperomia barthelemyana var. genuina

Peperomia barthelemyana var. genuina, commonly known as the Barthelemy's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to Central America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green to reddish-brown coloration. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable addition to collections of tropical plants.
