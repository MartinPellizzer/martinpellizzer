Philodendron martianum

Philodendron martianum, commonly known as the Martian Philodendron, is a striking tropical climbing plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to climb using its aerial roots. Its foliage often exhibits a variegated pattern with shades of green, white, and yellow.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.
