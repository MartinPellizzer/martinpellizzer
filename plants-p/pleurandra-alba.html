Pleurandra alba

Pleurandra alba, commonly known as the white pleurandra, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, strap-like leaves that are typically bright green and slightly glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Pleurandra alba is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
