Philotheca hassellii

Philotheca hassellii, commonly known as the mountain tea tree, is a small to medium-sized shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features aromatic, grey-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers in clusters, which bloom in spring and attract various pollinators. Philotheca hassellii is well adapted to rocky and sandy soils, often growing in dry, sunny environments.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging conditions.
