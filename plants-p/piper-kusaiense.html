Piper kusaiense

Piper kusaiense, commonly known as the Kusaiense pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into elongated, peppery fruits.

The plant is valued for its aromatic leaves and fruits, which are used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.
