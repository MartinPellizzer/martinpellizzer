Peperomia tsakiana var. victoriana

Peperomia tsakiana var. victoriana, commonly known as the Victorian Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and red, which are usually hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental foliage and compact growth habit, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
