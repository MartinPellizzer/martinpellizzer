Petasioides laurifolium

Petasioides laurifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf petasioides, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features elliptical, glossy leaves that are typically dark green and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. Its botanical characteristics include a woody stem and opposite leaf arrangement.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
