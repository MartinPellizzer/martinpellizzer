Prostanthera tozerana

Prostanthera tozerana, commonly known as the Tozer's mintbush, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue or violet in color. Its leaves are narrow and aromatic, often used in traditional herbal practices.

Prostanthera tozerana thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and dry woodland habitats.
