Procris fagifolia

Procris fagifolia, commonly known as the maple-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, maple-like foliage and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are broad and have a distinct lobed shape, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Procris fagifolia is valued in horticulture for its aesthetic beauty and unique floral scent.
