Piper maypurense

Piper maypurense, commonly known as the Maypuren pepper or Maypuren long pepper, is a climbing vine native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, elongated berries that are used in traditional medicine and as a spice. Its leaves are often used in local herbal remedies for their purported medicinal properties.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
