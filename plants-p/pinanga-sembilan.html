Pinanga sembilan

Pinanga sembilan, commonly known as the Sembilan bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves that are typically green in color. It often grows in dense clusters and can reach heights of up to several meters.

Pinanga sembilan is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional landscaping.
