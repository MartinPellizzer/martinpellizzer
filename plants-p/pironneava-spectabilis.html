Pironneava spectabilis

Pironneava spectabilis, commonly known as the showy pironneava, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, large, and colorful inflorescences that display a range of hues, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and elliptical in shape, with a smooth margin.

Pironneava spectabilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and tropical landscapes.
