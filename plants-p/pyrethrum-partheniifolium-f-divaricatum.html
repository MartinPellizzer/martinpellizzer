Pyrethrum partheniifolium f. divaricatum

Pyrethrum partheniifolium f. divaricatum, commonly known as the slenderleaf pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Argentina.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its natural insecticidal properties due to the presence of pyrethrins.

This cultivar is often grown in gardens for its delicate foliage and vibrant blooms.
