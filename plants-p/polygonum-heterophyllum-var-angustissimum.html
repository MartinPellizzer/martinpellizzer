Polygonum heterophyllum var. angustissimum

Polygonum heterophyllum var. angustissimum, commonly known as narrowleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, swales, and along stream banks. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often reddish or purplish at the base. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink to reddish in color and bloom in late summer.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is often found in disturbed or wetland environments.
