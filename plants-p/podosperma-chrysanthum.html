Podosperma chrysanthum

Podosperma chrysanthum, commonly known as the yellow-flowered rock rose, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of the eastern Mediterranean.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a vibrant touch to rocky landscapes. The plant has woody stems and small, leathery leaves that are adapted to arid conditions. It is often found growing in limestone soils and is well-suited to rocky or gravelly habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
