Psathyrostachys hyalantha

Psathyrostachys hyalantha, commonly known as the silvergrass, is a species of grass native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-colored leaves and compact growth habit. This plant is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has fibrous roots and produces small, spike-like inflorescences.
