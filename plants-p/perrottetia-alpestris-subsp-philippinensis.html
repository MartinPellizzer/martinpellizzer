Perrottetia alpestris subsp. philippinensis

Perrottetia alpestris subsp.

philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine alpine perrottetia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Philippines. It is a shrub characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of pink or white.

The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in high-altitude forests and rocky slopes. Its botanical features include lance-shaped leaves and a compact growth habit, making it well adapted to its alpine habitat.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native ecosystems.
