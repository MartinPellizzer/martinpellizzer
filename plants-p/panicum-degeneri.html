Panicum degeneri

Panicum degeneri, commonly known as the silver hairgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and delicate, feathery panicles that give it a soft, ethereal appearance. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often used in ornamental landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.

Panicum degeneri is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in coastal and sandy environments.
