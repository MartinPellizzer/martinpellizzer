Ptiloria cichoriacea

Ptiloria cichoriacea, commonly known as the African dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers. The plant has basal leaves that are deeply lobed and covered in fine hairs. It produces small, wind-dispersed seeds similar to those of the common dandelion.

Ptiloria cichoriacea is often found in grasslands and along roadsides, thriving in full sun.
