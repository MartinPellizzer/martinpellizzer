Peperomia penninervia

Peperomia penninervia, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that are typically hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its low maintenance and attractive foliage make it a versatile plant for both indoor and outdoor settings.
