Piper opizianum

Piper opizianum, commonly known as the Java pepper or Opizianum pepper, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant is characterized by its trailing vines and small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruits are initially green and turn red when mature, resembling tiny peppers.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
