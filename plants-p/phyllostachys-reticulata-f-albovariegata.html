Phyllostachys reticulata f. albovariegata

Phyllostachys reticulata f. albovariegata, commonly known as the white-veined bamboo, is a variegated cultivar of the giant bamboo species.

It features striking white and green striped stems that add visual interest to gardens and landscapes. The plant is a clumping bamboo, growing in dense clusters with a moderate growth rate. Its foliage is typically a mix of light green and creamy white, creating a unique aesthetic.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is well-suited for temperate climates.
