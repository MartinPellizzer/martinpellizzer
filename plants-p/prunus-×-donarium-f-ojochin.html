Prunus × donarium f. ojochin

Prunus × donarium f. ojochin, commonly known as the Ojochin cherry, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, sweet-tart fruits that are typically harvested in late summer. The plant is native to Japan and is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive pink flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

The fruit is used in both culinary and medicinal applications in traditional practices.
