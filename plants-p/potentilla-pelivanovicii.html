Potentilla pelivanovicii

Potentilla pelivanovicii, commonly known as the Pelivanovicii cinquefoil, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in Kazakhstan and parts of Russia. The plant is characterized by its small, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.
