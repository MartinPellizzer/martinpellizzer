Peperomia discistila

Peperomia discistila, commonly known as the Rosy Peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are often adorned with a subtle pink or red hue on the underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color, which appear in the axils of the leaves. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia discistila prefers well-draining soil and moderate humidity, making it suitable for a variety of indoor environments.
