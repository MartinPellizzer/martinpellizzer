Psoralea tenuissima

Psoralea tenuissima, commonly known as the narrowleaf psoralea, is a small, herbaceous annual plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant has slender, branched stems and small, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and yellowish, arranged in clusters, and it produces small, dry fruits that contain several seeds.

It is noted for its adaptability to harsh conditions and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
