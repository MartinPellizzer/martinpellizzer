Pleurothallis Grobleri

Pleurothallis grobleri is a rare and delicate orchid native to the cloud forests of South America. It thrives in humid, shaded environments where it clings to tree bark and rocky surfaces. Known for its small, fragrant flowers, this plant is a favorite among orchid enthusiasts. Its unique appearance and challenging cultivation requirements make it a prized specimen. Despite its beauty, it remains elusive and underappreciated in the broader horticultural community.
