Pelargonium × roseum

Pelargonium × roseum, commonly known as the hybrid geranium, is a popular ornamental plant characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of growing conditions. The plant typically produces clusters of five-petaled blooms in shades of red, pink, and white. Its foliage is often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture.

Pelargonium × roseum is widely cultivated for garden use and as a potted plant due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.
