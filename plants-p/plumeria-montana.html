Plumeria montana

Plumeria montana, commonly known as the mountain plumeria, is a species of flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to yellow and pink. Its leaves are broad and ovate, often with a waxy texture, and its bark is smooth and pale.

Plumeria montana is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and aromatic blooms.
