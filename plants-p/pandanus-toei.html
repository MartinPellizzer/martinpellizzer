Pandanus toei

Pandanus toei, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pandanus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender leaves that are often used in traditional crafts and thatching. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into fibrous, spiky fruits. Its root system is fibrous and helps stabilize the soil in its natural habitat.

The leaves are also used in local cuisines and for making baskets and mats.
