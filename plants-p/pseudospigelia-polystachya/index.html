Pseudospigelia Polystachya

Pseudospigelia polystachya is a striking member of the Onagraceae family known for its vibrant pink flowers. Native to the southeastern United States, this plant thrives in moist, sunny habitats like swamps and wetlands. Often mistaken for related species, it has distinct leaf shapes and floral structures that set it apart. This plant not only adds beauty to its environment but also plays a vital role in local ecosystems.
