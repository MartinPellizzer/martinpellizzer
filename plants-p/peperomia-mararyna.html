Peperomia mararyna

Peperomia mararyna, commonly known as the Mararyna Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slight sheen, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and white. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.
