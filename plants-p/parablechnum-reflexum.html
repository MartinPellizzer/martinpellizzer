Parablechnum reflexum

Parablechnum reflexum, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its silvery-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, round fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
