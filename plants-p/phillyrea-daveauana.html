Phillyrea daveauana

Phillyrea daveauana, commonly known as the Daveau's Phillyrea, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in spring, which are clustered in umbels. Phillyrea daveauana is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and full sun exposure.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
