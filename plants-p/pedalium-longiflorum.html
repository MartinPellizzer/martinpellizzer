Pedalium longiflorum

Pedalium longiflorum, commonly known as the long-flowered pedalium, is a member of the Leguminosae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white or yellow flowers. Its fruits are pod-like structures containing several seeds.

Pedalium longiflorum is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
