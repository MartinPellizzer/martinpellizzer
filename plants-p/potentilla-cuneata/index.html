Potentilla Cuneata

Potentilla cuneata is a small, hardy shrub native to the mountainous regions of central and eastern Europe. It is known for its delicate yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, adding a touch of brightness to rocky and woodland habitats. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its compact size and drought tolerance make it an excellent addition to low-maintenance landscapes. Despite its modest appearance, Potentilla cuneata plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and wildlife.
