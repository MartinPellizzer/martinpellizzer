Pleurothallis manicosa

Pleurothallis manicosa, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, often with intricate patterns. Its leaves are typically narrow and strap-like, adapted for efficient water absorption.

Pleurothallis manicosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
