Pleroma tomentulosum

Pleroma tomentulosum, commonly known as the woolly pleuraea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
