Peperomia osana

Peperomia osana, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery or whitish powdery coating, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia osana is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and tolerating a range of indoor conditions.
