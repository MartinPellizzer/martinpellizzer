Pancratium stellare

Pancratium stellare, commonly known as the star lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a tall, slender stem adorned with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bright yellow, star-shaped flowers. The flowers are often fragrant and bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant is adapted to arid and coastal environments, showcasing its resilience to drought and salt spray.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often used in gardens and landscapes.
