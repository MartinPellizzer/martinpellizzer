Parodia uhligiana

Parodia uhligiana, commonly known as the "Sunburst Cactus," is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its spherical shape and bright yellow to greenish-yellow spines that give it a striking appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding to its ornamental value. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and desert landscapes.
