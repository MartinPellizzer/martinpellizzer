Pleurothallis cuneifolia var. alboviridis

Pleurothallis cuneifolia var. alboviridis, commonly known as the white-green pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The flowers are typically pale green to white in color, with a distinctive, narrow lip that is usually a contrasting shade. This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in shaded, humid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
