Pyrus aria var. rosea

Pyrus aria var. rosea, commonly known as the pink-flowered pear.

This variant of the pear tree is characterized by its distinctive pinkish-red flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are typically broad and have a slightly serrated edge. It is a deciduous plant that produces small, edible fruits in late summer.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and floral display.
