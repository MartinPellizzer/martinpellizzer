Pilosella piloselloides subsp. rubrobauhinii

Pilosella piloselloides subsp.

rubrobauhinii, commonly known as the red-brown pilosella, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and small, yellowish-white composite flowers.
