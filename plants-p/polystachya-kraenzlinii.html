Polystachya kraenzlinii

Polystachya kraenzlinii, commonly known as the Kraenzlin's spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces clusters of delicate, spider-like flowers with long, slender petals. The plant typically blooms during the dry season, showcasing vibrant colors such as yellow and orange. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and greenhouse cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is often sought after by orchid enthusiasts.
