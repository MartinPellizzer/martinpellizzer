Piper niceforoi

Piper niceforoi, commonly known as the Niceforo pepper or the Bornean pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the Piperaceae family.

It is native to the rainforests of Borneo and is characterized by its small, aromatic berries. The plant typically grows as a climbing vine, with opposite, ovate leaves that have a rough texture. Its flowers are small and green, clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces fruit that ripens to a bright red or yellow color.

The plant is valued for its unique flavor and is used in traditional medicine and local cuisines.
