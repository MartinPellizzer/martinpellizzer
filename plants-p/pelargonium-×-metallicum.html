Pelargonium × metallicum

Pelargonium × metallicum, commonly known as the silver geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces showy flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, typically in clusters at the ends of its stems. Native to South Africa, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a houseplant and in gardens.
