Potentilla × boetzkesii

Potentilla × boetzkesii, commonly known as the Boetzkesii cinquefoil, is a hybrid shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in early summer. The plant has a compact growth habit with dark green, serrated leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardiness in temperate climates.

The species is also valued for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions and its resistance to pests and diseases.
