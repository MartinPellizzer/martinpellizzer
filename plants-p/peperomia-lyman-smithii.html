Peperomia lyman-smithii

Peperomia lyman-smithii, commonly known as the Lyman-Smith peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features small, glossy, ovate leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a compact and decorative appearance. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, which are followed by small, red berries. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers indirect light, making it a suitable houseplant for indoor environments.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
