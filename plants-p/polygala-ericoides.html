Polygala ericoides

Polygala ericoides, commonly known as the heath polygala, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils, often in coastal or inland heathlands. The plant produces delicate, spathaceous flowers that are usually pink or purple in color, with a distinctive, thread-like structure. Polygala ericoides plays an important ecological role in its habitat, providing nectar for pollinators and shelter for small animals.
