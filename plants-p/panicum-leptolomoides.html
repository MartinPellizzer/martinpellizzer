Panicum leptolomoides

Panicum leptolomoides, commonly known as slender false panicgrass, is a grass species native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various environmental conditions.
