Picea wilsonii var. shanxiensis

Picea wilsonii var. shanxiensis, commonly known as the Shanxi fir, is a rare variant of the Wilson fir native to China.

It is a coniferous evergreen tree characterized by its needle-like leaves and cone-bearing structure. The plant exhibits typical conifer morphology, with a conical shape and dense foliage. It thrives in temperate climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a valuable species for botanical study and conservation efforts.
