Pastinaca sativa var. soleirolii

Pastinaca sativa var. soleirolii, commonly known as the Soleirolii scorzonera, is a variant of the common salsify.

It is a biennial or perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. The plant features thick, fleshy roots that are typically harvested for culinary use. Its leaves are lobed and can grow up to 30 cm in length, while its flowers are yellow and borne in clusters.

This variety is valued for its unique flavor and nutritional content.
