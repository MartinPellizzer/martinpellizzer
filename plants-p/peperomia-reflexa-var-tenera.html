Peperomia reflexa var. tenera

Peperomia reflexa var. tenera, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, velvety foliage with deep green, ovate leaves that have a distinctive waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that are typically hidden among the leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant.
