Phyteuma sieberi f. glabrescens

Phyteuma sieberi f. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-leaved phyteuma, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pale blue to white. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in a fine, smooth texture, giving the plant its "glabrescens" designation. Native to Central Asia, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
