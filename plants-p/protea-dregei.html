Protea dregei

Protea dregei, commonly known as the Cape protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence composed of numerous tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant is native to the Western and Eastern Cape provinces, where it thrives in sandy, well-drained soils. Its unique floral structure attracts a variety of pollinators, including birds and insects.

Protea dregei is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
