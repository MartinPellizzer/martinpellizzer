Peperomia dendroides

Peperomia dendroides, commonly known as the tree peperomia, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves that are slightly wavy along the edges. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers during the warmer months, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Peperomia dendroides is often prized for its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.
