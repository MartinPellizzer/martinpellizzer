Phacelia crenulata var. orbicularis

Phacelia crenulata var. orbicularis, commonly known as the broadleaf phacelia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its orbicular, or rounded, leaves and clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. It has a short growing season and is often found in regions with a Mediterranean climate.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for pollinators and ecological restoration projects.
