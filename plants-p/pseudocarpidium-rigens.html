Pseudocarpidium rigens

Pseudocarpidium rigens, commonly known as the rigid pseudocarpidium, is a member of the family Pterostomataceae.

It is a small, evergreen shrub characterized by its dense, leathery foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish in color. Its fruits are fleshy, berry-like structures that contain multiple seeds.

Pseudocarpidium rigens is often found in shaded, moist environments within tropical and subtropical regions.
