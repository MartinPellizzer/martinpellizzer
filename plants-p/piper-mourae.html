Piper mourae

Piper mourae, commonly known as the Moura pepper, is a climbing shrub native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when mature, containing a spicy and aromatic compound. Its leaves are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The botanical features of Piper mourae make it an important species in both ecological and cultural contexts.
