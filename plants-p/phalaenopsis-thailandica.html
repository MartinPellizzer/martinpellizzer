Phalaenopsis thailandica

Phalaenopsis thailandica, commonly known as the Thai orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Thailand.

It features a compact, pseudobulbous stem with broad, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers with a velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
