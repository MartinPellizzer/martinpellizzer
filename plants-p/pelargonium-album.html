Pelargonium album

Pelargonium album, commonly known as the white geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, velvety leaves and clusters of white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and thrives in well-drained soil. It prefers full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Pelargonium album is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
