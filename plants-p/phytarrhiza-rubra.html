Phytarrhiza rubra

Phytarrhiza rubra, commonly known as the crimson root plant, is a unique species characterized by its vivid red, tuberous roots.

Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves with a glossy green surface. Its flowering stems produce clusters of small, pale yellow flowers.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
