Pellaea nivea

Pellaea nivea, commonly known as the silver fern, is a perennial fern native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, silvery-white fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and rocky crevices. Its leaves are pinnately divided, with a soft, velvety texture.

Pellaea nivea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
