Pleurothallis trifida

Pleurothallis trifida, commonly known as the three-lobed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This small epiphytic orchid typically grows on tree branches or rocks in humid environments. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with three distinct lobes on the petals.

The plant is noted for its compact size and delicate appearance, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
