Pilea entradana

Pilea entradana, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically bright green with a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale green, followed by round, fleshy fruits. It thrives in indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
