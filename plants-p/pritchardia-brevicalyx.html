Pritchardia brevicalyx

Pritchardia brevicalyx, commonly known as the short-leaved cycad, is a species of cycad native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves that are often a deep green in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers and is pollinated by specific insects, which is a unique feature of cycads. Pritchardia brevicalyx is adapted to the tropical climate of Hawaii and is often found in coastal or lowland habitats.
