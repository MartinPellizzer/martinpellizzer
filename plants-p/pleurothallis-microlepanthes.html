Pleurothallis microlepanthes

Pleurothallis microlepanthes, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, narrow leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its compact size and unique floral structure.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments to ensure its survival.
