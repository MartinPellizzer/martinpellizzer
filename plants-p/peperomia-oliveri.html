Peperomia oliveri

Peperomia oliveri, commonly known as the Oliver's peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are usually heart-shaped or ovate in form. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia oliveri is known for its ability to tolerate low light conditions and requires minimal water, making it an easy plant to care for.
