Pancratium undulatum

Pancratium undulatum, commonly known as the yellow rain lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant produces a tall, slender stem with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its bulbs are used in traditional medicine and as ornamental plants in gardens.

Pancratium undulatum thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal and Mediterranean climates.
