Polypodium kaieturum

Polypodium kaieturum, commonly known as the Kaieteur fern, is a species of polypodioid fern native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically green and covered in a thin layer of trichomes. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in rainforests and along stream banks. Its rhizome is creeping and produces numerous sporangia on the underside of its fronds.

Polypodium kaieturum plays a role in the ecological balance of its habitat by contributing to soil stability and providing microhabitats for various organisms.
