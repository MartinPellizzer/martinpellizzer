Peperomia jamesoniana var. longifolia

Peperomia jamesoniana var. longifolia, commonly known as the long-leaved peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It features dense, upright growth with lance-shaped, dark green leaves that have a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white or pale pink, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

This variety is prized for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
