Peperomia pleiomorpha

Peperomia pleiomorpha, commonly known as the variegated peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink, adding visual interest to any indoor space. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color, followed by dark blue or black berries. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and attractive foliage, it is well-suited for container cultivation and decorative purposes.
