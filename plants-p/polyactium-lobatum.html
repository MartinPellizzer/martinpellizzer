Polyactium lobatum

Polyactium lobatum, commonly known as the lobed pteridium, is a fern native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping underground stems that allow it to spread across the forest floor. Its leaves are bi-lobed and often have a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.
