Polystichum omeiense

Polystichum omeiense, commonly known as the Omeiense shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its tall, upright fronds with a distinctive, shield-like shape. The plant thrives in cool, moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories at high altitudes. Its fronds are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, with serrated margins that contribute to its ornamental appeal.

Polystichum omeiense is valued for its aesthetic qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a decorative plant.
