Pelargonium burgerianum

Pelargonium burgerianum, commonly known as the Cape geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with rounded, succulent leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Pelargonium burgerianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
