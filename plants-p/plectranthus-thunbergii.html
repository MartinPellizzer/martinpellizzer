Plectranthus thunbergii

Plectranthus thunbergii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from violet to white. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Plectranthus thunbergii is also valued for its ability to produce aromatic essential oils.
