Piper megalophyllum

Piper megalophyllum, commonly known as the giant arrowleaf, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of flowering plants. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically greenish-white in color.

The plant is often found in humid, shaded environments such as rainforests.
