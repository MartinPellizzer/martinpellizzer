Plectranthus floribundus

Plectranthus floribundus, commonly known as the purple heart plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its attractive, tubular purple flowers and thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and ornamental plants. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms make it both ornamental and easy to maintain.
