Pipalia

Pipalia, commonly known as the Indian lantern plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its striking, lantern-shaped flowers that are often red or orange in color. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Pipalia thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
