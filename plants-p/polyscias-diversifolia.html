Polyscias diversifolia

Polyscias diversifolia, commonly known as the variegated glory bush, is a member of the Araliaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, including parts of India and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display shades of green, white, and yellow. It typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters.

Polyscias diversifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
