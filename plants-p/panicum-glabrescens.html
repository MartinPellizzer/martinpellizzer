Panicum glabrescens

Panicum glabrescens, commonly known as little bluestem, is a warm-season grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green blades and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types, including sandy and clay soils. This plant is often found in prairie ecosystems and is well adapted to drought conditions. It produces small, fluffy seed heads that are visually distinctive and contribute to its ornamental value.

Panicum glabrescens plays an important role in soil stabilization and is a key component of native grasslands.
