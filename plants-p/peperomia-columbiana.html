Peperomia columbiana

Peperomia columbiana, commonly known as the Columbian peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to South America.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, often with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and ornamental foliage, it is well-suited for container cultivation.
