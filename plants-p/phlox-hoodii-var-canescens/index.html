Phlox Hoodii Var. Canescens

Phlox hoodii var. canescens is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high elevations of the southwestern United States. Known for its soft, fuzzy leaves and clusters of pale pink to white blooms, this species thrives in cool, moist environments. It is often found in rocky crevices and open meadows where it can receive ample sunlight. Due to its limited range and sensitivity to environmental changes, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and beautiful plant for future generations.
