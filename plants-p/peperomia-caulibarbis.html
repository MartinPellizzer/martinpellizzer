Peperomia caulibarbis

Peperomia caulibarbis, commonly known as the hairy-stemmed peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in fine, hairy textures, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia caulibarbis is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive foliage.
