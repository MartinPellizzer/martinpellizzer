Poa canbyi

Poa canbyi, commonly known as Canby's bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in open woodlands and meadows. Its inflorescence consists of a spike-like cluster of small, two-rowed spikelets.

Poa canbyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant landscaping.
