Pastoraea saviana

Pastoraea saviana, commonly known as the slender milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many milkweed species. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, clustered in umbels, and typically have a yellowish or greenish hue.

It is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks, thriving in well-drained soils.
