Piper fulvescens

Piper fulvescens, commonly known as the yellow pepper plant, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as the common black pepper. The plant produces small, yellowish-green berries that are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits. Its leaves are also used in herbal remedies due to their aromatic properties.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
