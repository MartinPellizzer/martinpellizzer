Prunus Prostrata Var. Humilis

Prunus prostrata var. humilis is a low-growing, flowering shrub native to eastern Asia. It is known for its delicate pink or white flowers that bloom in early spring. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade. Its compact form makes it ideal for rock gardens or as a ground cover. Despite its modest size, it adds a subtle beauty to any garden setting.
