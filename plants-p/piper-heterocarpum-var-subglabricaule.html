Piper heterocarpum var. subglabricaule

Piper heterocarpum var. subglabricaule, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed pepper plant, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, glabrous stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly pubescent underside. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in spikes, and the fruit is a one-seeded berry that turns red when mature.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
