Phalaenopsis × vesta

Phalaenopsis × vesta, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a popular epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, long-lasting flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its flowers bloom sequentially from the base of the spike, giving the appearance of continuous blooming.

This orchid is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and ability to thrive in humid environments.
