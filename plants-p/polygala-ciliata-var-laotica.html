Polygala ciliata var. laotica

Polygala ciliata var. laotica, commonly known as the Laos featherplant, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Laos and Vietnam.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped with ciliate margins, and the flowers are usually pale green to white, arranged in clusters.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
