Pogonotrophe ribesioides

Pogonotrophe ribesioides, commonly known as the red-flowered honeysuckle, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces clusters of small, fragrant, red flowers. The plant is characterized by its distinctive yellow stamens and is often found in moist, shaded environments. Its berries are orange-red and contain small, hard seeds.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in partial shade.
