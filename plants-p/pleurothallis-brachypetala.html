Pleurothallis brachypetala

Pleurothallis brachypetala, commonly known as the short-petaled pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, intricately patterned flowers. The plant typically produces a single or few flowers per inflorescence, which are often pale green or yellowish in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicate and sought-after specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
