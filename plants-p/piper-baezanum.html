Piper baezanum

Piper baezanum, commonly known as the Baezan pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the humid lowland forests of South America, particularly in countries like Peru and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, dark green leaves. Its fruit, which is a small, spherical berry, contains the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and cuisine.

Piper baezanum is valued for its unique flavor and potential medicinal properties.
