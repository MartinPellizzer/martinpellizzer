Plectranthus fraternus

Plectranthus fraternus, commonly known as the blue plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are usually a striking blue-gray color. It produces small, tubular flowers that are often violet or purple in color, arranged in clusters.

Plectranthus fraternus is well-suited for rock gardens and containers due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.
