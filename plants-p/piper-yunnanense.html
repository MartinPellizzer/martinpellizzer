Piper yunnanense

Piper yunnanense, commonly known as Yunnan pepper, is a flowering plant native to the Yunnan province in China.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. It produces peppery fruits that are used in traditional Chinese medicine and local cuisines.

The botanical features of Piper yunnanense make it an important species in both ecological and ethnobotanical contexts.
