Psidium cinereum

Psidium cinereum, commonly known as the silver star or white guava, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-green leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are often clustered together. Its fruits are small, round, and typically have a smooth, whitish or yellowish skin.

Psidium cinereum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
