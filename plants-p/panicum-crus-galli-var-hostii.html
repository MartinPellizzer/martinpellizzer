Panicum crus-galli var. hostii

Panicum crus-galli var. hostii, commonly known as purple lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and produces showy, plume-like inflorescences that range in color from purple to pink. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping and erosion control. Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant fall color and long-lasting floral display.

It is often used in gardens and as a wildlife habitat due to its ability to support pollinators.
