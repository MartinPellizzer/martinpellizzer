Penstemon oreophilus

Penstemon oreophilus, commonly known as the Sierra penstemon, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils in mountainous regions. The plant features upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy flowers, which range from blue to violet, bloom in late spring to early summer.

Penstemon oreophilus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging environmental conditions.
