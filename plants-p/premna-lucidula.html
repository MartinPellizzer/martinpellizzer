Premna lucidula

Premna lucidula, commonly known as the glossy abutilon, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy green leaves and showy, lantern-shaped yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Premna lucidula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
