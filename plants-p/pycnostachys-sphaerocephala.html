Pycnostachys sphaerocephala

Pycnostachys sphaerocephala, commonly known as the globe-headed pycnostachy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

The species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience to drought conditions.
