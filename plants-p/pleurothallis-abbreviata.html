Pleurothallis abbreviata

Pleurothallis abbreviata, commonly known as the short-stemmed orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, linear leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often found in protected areas and is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
