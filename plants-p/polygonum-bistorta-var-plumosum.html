Polygonum bistorta var. plumosum

Polygonum bistorta var. plumosum, commonly known as the silky bistort, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture, and its flowers are small, clustered in dense spikes. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
