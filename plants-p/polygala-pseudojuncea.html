Polygala pseudojuncea

Polygala pseudojuncea, commonly known as the narrow-leaved milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, including China and Japan.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in woodland or shrubland habitats and prefers well-drained soil. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces seed pods that split open to release its seeds.

Polygala pseudojuncea is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive floral display.
