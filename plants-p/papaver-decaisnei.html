Papaver decaisnei

Papaver decaisnei, commonly known as the Decaisne poppy, is a species of flowering plant in the family Papaveraceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Kazakhstan and Kyrgyzstan. The plant is characterized by its striking, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from deep red to orange, often with a contrasting black or purple center. It typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun.

Papaver decaisnei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
