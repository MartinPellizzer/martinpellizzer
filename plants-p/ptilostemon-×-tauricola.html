Ptilostemon × tauricola

Ptilostemon × tauricola, commonly known as the Tauric hybrid ptilostemon, is a hybrid plant native to the Tauric Chersonese region.

It belongs to the family Plantaginaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage. The plant typically features lance-shaped leaves with a bluish-gray hue, often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
