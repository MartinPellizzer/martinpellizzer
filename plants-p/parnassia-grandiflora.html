Parnassia grandiflora

Parnassia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered parnassia, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the family Ranunculaceae.

It is characterized by its striking white, cup-shaped flowers with yellow stamens, which are often adorned with a distinctive greenish glandular rim. Parnassia grandiflora is found in temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere, including parts of North America and Asia.
