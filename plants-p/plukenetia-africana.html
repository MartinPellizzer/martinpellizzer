Plukenetia africana

Plukenetia africana, commonly known as African black oil palm, is a tropical shrub native to western Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers clustered in inflorescences. The plant is notable for its fruit, which contains a high percentage of oil-rich seeds. These seeds are used to extract palm oil, which has various industrial and culinary applications.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and economic contexts within its native habitat.
