Phrynium pusillum

Phrynium pusillum, commonly known as the dwarf arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves and striking yellow-orange flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters, making it suitable for smaller garden spaces. Its foliage is often variegated, featuring a mix of green and white or yellow tones.

Phrynium pusillum prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, thriving in warm, humid climates.
