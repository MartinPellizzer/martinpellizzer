Polystichum morii

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its dense, upright fronds that are typically dark green and leathery. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Polystichum morii is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to create a lush, textured ground cover.
