Piptomeris stricta

Piptomeris stricta, commonly known as the narrowleaf ironweed, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink to purple in color, which bloom in the summer months. Piptomeris stricta thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks.

It plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining wetland ecosystems.
