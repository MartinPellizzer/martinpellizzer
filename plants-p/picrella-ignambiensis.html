Picrella ignambiensis

Picrella ignambiensis, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Picrella ignambiensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and public spaces.
