Pentaschistis aristifolia

Pentaschistis aristifolia, commonly known as the featherleaf pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage with finely serrated leaf margins. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. These flowers are followed by small, round berries that turn red as they mature.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
