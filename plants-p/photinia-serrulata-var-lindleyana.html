Photinia serrulata var. lindleyana

Photinia serrulata var. lindleyana, commonly known as the Lindley's photinia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to China.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have finely serrated edges. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in spring, followed by bright red berries that persist into winter. It is valued for its dense foliage and ornamental appeal in gardens.

This variety is relatively low maintenance and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
