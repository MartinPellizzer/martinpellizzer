Pyrrosia mollis var. mollissima

Pyrrosia mollis var. mollissima, commonly known as the velvet fern, is a terrestrial fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, leathery fronds that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, spore-bearing structures called sori on the underside of its leaves. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest floors or rocky crevices.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
