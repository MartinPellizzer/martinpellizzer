Potentilla montenegrina proles jankaeana

Potentilla montenegrina proles jankaeana, commonly known as the Montenegro potentilla, is a shrub native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a slightly hairy texture.

It is well adapted to rocky and mountainous environments, thriving in well-drained soils.
