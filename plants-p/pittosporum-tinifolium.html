Pittosporum tinifolium

Pittosporum tinifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features dark green, narrow, leathery leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters, which are followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

Pittosporum tinifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting native wildlife.
