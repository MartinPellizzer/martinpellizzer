Pulmonaria officinalis var. ovalis

Pulmonaria officinalis var. ovalis, commonly known as the oval-leaved lungwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Plantaginaceae and is characterized by its distinctive basal rosette of oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of purple and white, typically blooming in early spring. Its leaves are covered in fine, hairy textures, and the plant is often found in moist, woodland habitats.

It is valued both for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
