Pimpinella apiodora var. nudicaulis

Pimpinella apiodora var. nudicaulis, commonly known as the slender cowparsley, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its finely divided leaves and umbel-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. It has a slender, erect stem and produces small white to pale yellow flowers in early spring.
