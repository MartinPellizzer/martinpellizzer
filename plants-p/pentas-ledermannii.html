Pentas ledermannii

Pentas ledermannii, commonly known as the flame flower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

The flowers bloom throughout the growing season, adding continuous color to the landscape.
