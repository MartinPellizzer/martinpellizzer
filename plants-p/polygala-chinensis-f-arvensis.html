Polygala chinensis f. arvensis

Polygala chinensis f. arvensis, commonly known as the field polygala, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, herbaceous stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as fields and roadsides. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with five petals arranged in a distinctive spurred shape.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
