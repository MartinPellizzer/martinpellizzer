Pleurothallis compressicaulis

Pleurothallis compressicaulis, commonly known as the compressed stem pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, often densely packed inflorescence and small, intricate flowers that typically display a range of colors from white to pale yellow. The plant grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, adapted to conserve moisture in its natural habitat.

Pleurothallis compressicaulis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
