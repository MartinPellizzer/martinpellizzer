Psanacetum

Psanacetum, commonly known as purple coneflower, is a genus of flowering plants in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is native to North America and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant typically grows in prairies and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soil and full sun. Psanacetum species are known for their tall, slender stems and are often used in gardens for their ornamental value.
