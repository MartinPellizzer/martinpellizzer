Phyteuma halleri f. umbrosum

Phyteuma halleri f. umbrosum, commonly known as the shadow phyteuma, is a botanical variant of the Phyteuma genus.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe. This plant is characterized by its striking, tall spikes of blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color.

It thrives in well-drained, sunny to partially shaded habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
