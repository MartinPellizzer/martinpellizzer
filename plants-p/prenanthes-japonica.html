Prenanthes japonica

Prenanthes japonica, commonly known as Japanese bur reed, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, showcasing its adaptability to moist soil conditions. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its unique seed dispersal mechanism.

Prenanthes japonica is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
