Pilea glaucophylla

Pilea glaucophylla, commonly known as the silver pilea, is a compact evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are typically a mix of green and silver, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its slow growth and attractive foliage make it a low-maintenance addition to any plant collection.
