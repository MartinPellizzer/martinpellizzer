Petroselinum sativum subsp. crispum

Petroselinum sativum subsp.

crispum, commonly known as curly parsley, is a biennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves that are often curled or crinkled, giving it a distinctive appearance.
