Pilea auricularis

Pilea auricularis, commonly known as the ear-shaped pilea, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is a compact, evergreen perennial shrub native to Southeast Asia. The plant features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are often described as resembling small ears, giving it its common name. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color.

Pilea auricularis is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
