Pentacalia herzogii

Pentacalia herzogii, commonly known as the Herzog's pentacalia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.
