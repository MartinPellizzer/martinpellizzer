Physiglochis paniculata

Physiglochis paniculata, commonly known as the clustered grass.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. The plant is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and small, inconspicuous flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks.
