Plantago jehohlensis

Plantago jehohlensis, commonly known as the Jehol plantain, is a flowering plant native to northern China.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy surface. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters, which are typically borne on slender stalks. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas or along streambanks.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
