Peschiera latiflora

Peschiera latiflora, commonly known as the broad-flowered peschiera, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, showy white or pale blue flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Its flowering period generally occurs in late spring to early summer.
