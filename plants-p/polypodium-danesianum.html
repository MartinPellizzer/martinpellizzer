Polypodium danesianum

Polypodium danesianum, commonly known as the Dansen's water fern, is a tropical fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically green and glossy. The plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forests. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help it anchor to rocky surfaces or soil.

Polypodium danesianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
