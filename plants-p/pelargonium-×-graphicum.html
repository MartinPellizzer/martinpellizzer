Pelargonium × graphicum

Pelargonium × graphicum, commonly known as the graphic geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that often feature intricate patterns and contrasting colors. The plant typically produces clusters of small, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink, red, or white. Native to South Africa, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight.

It is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
