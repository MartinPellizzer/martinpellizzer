Peperomia hirta var. glaberrima

Peperomia hirta var. glaberrima, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, ovate leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a velvety texture. The leaves are typically a deep green with a slightly waxy appearance, and they tend to be arranged in a rosette pattern. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color, which appear on short stalks above the foliage.

This plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its slow growth and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
