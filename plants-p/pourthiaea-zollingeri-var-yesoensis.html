Pourthiaea zollingeri var. yesoensis

Pourthiaea zollingeri var. yesoensis, commonly known as the Yeso endemic gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Korean Peninsula.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as mountain forests. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
