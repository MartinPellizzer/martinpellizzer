Pouteria petenensis

Pouteria petenensis, commonly known as the Maya plum or wild plum, is a tropical tree native to Central America and parts of South America.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and large, glossy leaves. The plant produces yellowish flowers that are followed by edible, orange-colored fruits resembling plums. The fruit contains a single large seed surrounded by a sweet, juicy pulp.
