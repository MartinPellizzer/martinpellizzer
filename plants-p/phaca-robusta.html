Phaca robusta

Phaca robusta, commonly known as the robust phaca, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually broad and hairy, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.
