Primulina pseudomollifolia

Primulina pseudomollifolia, commonly known as the velvet leaf.

This plant is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color.

It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
