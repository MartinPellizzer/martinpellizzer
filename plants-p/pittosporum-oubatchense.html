Pittosporum oubatchense

Pittosporum oubatchense, commonly known as the Oubatche pittosporum, is a species of flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and have a leathery texture, while its bark is smooth and greyish in color.

Pittosporum oubatchense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
