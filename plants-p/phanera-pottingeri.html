Phanera pottingeri

Phanera pottingeri, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern and southern Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant is typically evergreen and prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil. It is popular as a houseplant due to its compact size and long blooming period.

Phanera pottingeri is also known for its ability to thrive in humid environments with regular watering.
