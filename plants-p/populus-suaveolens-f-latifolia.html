Populus suaveolens f. latifolia

Populus suaveolens f. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved poplar, is a cultivar of the poplar tree characterized by its wide, flat leaves.

It belongs to the Salicaceae family and is native to parts of Asia, particularly China. The plant is known for its fast growth and ability to thrive in moist, well-drained soils. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped with a smooth margin and a pale underside.

This cultivar is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive foliage and rapid growth rate.
