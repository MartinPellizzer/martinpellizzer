Polystichum kodamae

Polystichum kodamae, commonly known as the Kodama's sword fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Japan.

It is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The leaves are bi-pinnate, with a dark green color and a glossy texture. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
