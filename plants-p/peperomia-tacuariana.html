Peperomia tacuariana

Peperomia tacuariana, commonly known as the Tacuari peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features dense, glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and attractive foliage, it is often used as a decorative plant in both home and office environments.
