Phillyrea ellipticifolia

Phillyrea ellipticifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Phillyrea, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features simple, elliptic to lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy on top with a lighter underside. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by small, fleshy, yellowish fruits. It is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and inland areas.

Phillyrea ellipticifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh environmental conditions.
