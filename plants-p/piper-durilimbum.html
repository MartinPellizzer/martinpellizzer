Piper durilimbum

Piper durilimbum, commonly known as the broadleaf pepper, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn red when mature.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
