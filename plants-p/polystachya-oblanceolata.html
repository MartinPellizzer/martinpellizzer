Polystachya oblanceolata

Polystachya oblanceolata, commonly known as the broadleaf hawksbeard, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and long, narrow leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces inflorescences of numerous yellowish-green flowers arranged in a panicle-like structure. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
