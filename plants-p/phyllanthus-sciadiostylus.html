Phyllanthus sciadiostylus

Phyllanthus sciadiostylus, commonly known as the broadleaf phyllanthus, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Asia. The plant features alternate, simple, and often glossy leaves with a broad, ovate shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.

The plant produces small, smooth, and often green or yellowish fruits that are characteristic of the genus.
