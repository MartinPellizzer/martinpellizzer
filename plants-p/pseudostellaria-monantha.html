Pseudostellaria monantha

Pseudostellaria monantha, commonly known as the single-flowered starwort, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stems.
