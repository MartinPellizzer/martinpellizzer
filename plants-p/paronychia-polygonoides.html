Paronychia polygonoides

Paronychia polygonoides, commonly known as the cushion plant, is a low-growing succulent native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It features dense, mat-forming foliage with fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Due to its compact growth habit and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and ornamental gardening.
