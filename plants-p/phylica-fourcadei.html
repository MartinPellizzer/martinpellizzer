Phylica fourcadei

Phylica fourcadei, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, white, star-shaped flowers that resemble snowflakes. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in coastal areas. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing a contrast to its showy blooms.

Phylica fourcadei is well adapted to arid conditions and is often found in fynbos vegetation.
