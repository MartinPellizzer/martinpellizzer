Panicum concinnius

Panicum concinnius, commonly known as the slender panicgrass, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, pale green to brown spikelets that are typically clustered at the tips of the stems. It thrives in full sun and is often found in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and open fields.

Panicum concinnius is a hardy perennial that plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small insects.
