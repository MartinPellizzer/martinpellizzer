Plectrelminthus caudatus var. caudatus

Plectrelminthus caudatus var. caudatus, commonly known as the red-stemmed filaree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender, branching stems that often turn reddish in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and slightly hairy, and it thrives in well-drained soils in full sun.
