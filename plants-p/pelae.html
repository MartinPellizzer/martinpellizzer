Pelae

Pelae, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant has silvery-gray leaves covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in its arid environment.
