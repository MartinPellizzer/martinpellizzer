Pennisetum gibbosum

Pennisetum gibbosum, commonly known as fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its clumping growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green with a silvery underside. The plant produces fluffy, feathery seed heads that give it a distinctive, ornamental appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Pennisetum gibbosum is also valued for its ability to attract birds and other wildlife.
