Phyllanthus pulcher

Phyllanthus pulcher, commonly known as the beauty leaf, is a species of flowering plant in the family Phyllanthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its attractive, glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Phyllanthus pulcher is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
