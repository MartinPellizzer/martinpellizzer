Petrosedum amplexicaule var. ciliatum

Petrosedum amplexicaule var. ciliatum, commonly known as the curled leaf stonecrop, is a low-growing succulent native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features dense, compact rosettes of fleshy, often curled leaves that are typically green to bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
