Prosthechea brachychila

Prosthechea brachychila, commonly known as the short-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant has a sympodial growth pattern, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually ovate and arranged in a rosette-like formation.

Prosthechea brachychila is often found growing on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments.
