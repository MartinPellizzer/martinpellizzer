Pentas lanceolata var. usambarica

Pentas lanceolata var. usambarica, commonly known as the Usambara pentas, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded or semi-shaded environments. Its lance-shaped leaves are narrow and pointed, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It is valued in horticulture for its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various soil types.
