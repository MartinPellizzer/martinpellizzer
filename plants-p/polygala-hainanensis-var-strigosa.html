Polygala hainanensis var. strigosa

Polygala hainanensis var. strigosa, commonly known as the Hainan polygala, is a rare and endemic species native to southern China.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, branched stems and small, often clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered with fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
