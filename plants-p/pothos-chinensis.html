Pothos chinensis

Pothos chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese ivy, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically bright green, sometimes with variegated patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by white, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its adaptability and low maintenance requirements.

Pothos chinensis is valued for its ornamental foliage and ability to purify indoor air.
