Pueraria stricta

Pueraria stricta, commonly known as the broad-leafed cowpea, is a vigorous climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are often trifoliate, with a deep green color and a hairy texture. The plant produces purple or pink flowers followed by pods containing edible seeds. It is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in various soil types.

Pueraria stricta is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing properties, making it useful in agricultural systems.
