Primula amoena var. minuta

Primula amoena var. minuta, commonly known as the dwarf primrose, is a small flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe.

It features delicate, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, often with a slight pinkish tinge. The plant typically grows to a height of only a few inches, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant. Its leaves are dark green, heart-shaped, and covered in a fine, hairy texture.

It blooms in early spring, adding a touch of elegance to gardens with its subtle beauty.
