Plathymenia foliolosa

Plathymenia foliolosa, commonly known as the African daisy or African sunflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces vibrant yellow flowers with a distinctive yellow center and white petals. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
