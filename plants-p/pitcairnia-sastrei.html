Pitcairnia sastrei

Pitcairnia sastrei, commonly known as the Sastre's pitcairnia, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. This evergreen plant is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant produces striking inflorescences with colorful, showy flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to indoor conditions.
