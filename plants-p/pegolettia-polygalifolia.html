Pegolettia polygalifolia

Pegolettia polygalifolia, commonly known as the blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Pegolettia polygalifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance.
