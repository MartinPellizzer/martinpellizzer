Polypodiodes someyae

Polypodiodes someyae, commonly known as the Japanese polypody, is a fern species native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The spores are released from the sori, which are clustered on the underside of the fronds.
