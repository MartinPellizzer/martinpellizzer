Pavetta Indica F. Subvelutina

Pavetta indica f. subvelutina is a rare and intriguing variant of the Pavetta indica plant. It is characterized by its soft, velvety foliage that sets it apart from the typical form. This plant is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its unique appearance has made it a subject of interest among botanists and plant enthusiasts. Due to its limited distribution, it remains a relatively obscure but fascinating species in the botanical world.
