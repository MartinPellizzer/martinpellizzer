Peperomia blanda

Peperomia blanda, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features small, waxy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green or red, typically emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia blanda is prized for its ornamental foliage and ability to purify the air, adding both aesthetic and functional value to any space.
