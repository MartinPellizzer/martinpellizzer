Pilea nerteroides

Pilea nerteroides, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features rounded, fleshy leaves that are typically a vibrant green, often with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by round, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements contribute to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
