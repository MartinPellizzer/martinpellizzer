Protea heterophylla

Protea heterophylla, commonly known as the silver protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that consist of a central cone surrounded by a cluster of bracts. The plant typically grows in coastal fynbos vegetation, where it thrives in well-drained, acidic soils. Its leaves are usually narrow and silver-white, providing a striking contrast to the pinkish flowers.

Protea heterophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays an important ecological role in its native habitat.
