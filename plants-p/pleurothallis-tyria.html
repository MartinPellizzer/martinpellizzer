Pleurothallis tyria

Pleurothallis tyria, commonly known as the Tyrian orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid forest environments. Its flowers are usually small and come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and green.

Pleurothallis tyria is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
