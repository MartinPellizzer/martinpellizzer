Piper ottoniifolium

Piper ottoniifolium, commonly known as the Mexican pepper plant, is a tropical shrub native to Central America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many pepper species. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and small, clustered white flowers. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
