Podanthum lycium

Podanthum lycium, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, violet flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruits are small, red berries that contain several seeds.

Podanthum lycium is often found in rocky or dry soils and is adapted to withstand periods of drought.
