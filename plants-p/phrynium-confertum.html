Phrynium confertum

Phrynium confertum, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length. The plant produces striking orange or red flowers that bloom in clusters at the base of the leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Phrynium confertum is often used in ornamental gardening for its dramatic foliage and vibrant floral display.
