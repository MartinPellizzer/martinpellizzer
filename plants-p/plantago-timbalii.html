Plantago timbalii

Plantago timbalii, commonly known as the Timbalii plant, is a species of flowering plant in the plantain family.

It is native to certain regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Tanzania and Kenya. The plant is characterized by its broad, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. It produces small, greenish flowers arranged in a dense cluster, which are typically pollinated by insects.

This species is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
