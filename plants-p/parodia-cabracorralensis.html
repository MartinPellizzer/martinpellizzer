Parodia cabracorralensis

Parodia cabracorralensis, commonly known as the Cabracorralensis Cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and pale green to blue-gray coloration. The plant features areoles that produce spines and flowers that range from yellow to orange in color. It is adapted to arid environments, with a slow growth rate and the ability to store water in its thick, fleshy stems.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry climates.
