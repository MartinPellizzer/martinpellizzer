Pterostylis angusta

Pterostylis angusta, commonly known as the narrow-leaf greenhood, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understorey habitats. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single or few leaves, and it produces a distinctive greenish-yellow flower spike. Its flowers are often hooded, a characteristic feature of the Pterostylis genus.

The species is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other orchid species in its native range.
