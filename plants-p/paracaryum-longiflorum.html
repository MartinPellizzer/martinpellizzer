Paracaryum longiflorum

Paracaryum longiflorum, commonly known as the long-flowered paracaryum, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy flowers that can reach up to 15 cm in diameter. The flowers are typically yellow with prominent red or orange markings, making it a popular ornamental plant. It thrives in humid, warm environments with high light exposure and requires well-draining soil.

The plant is also valued for its air-purifying properties and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
