Pleurospermum stellatum

Pleurospermum stellatum, commonly known as the starflower or star daisy, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, gray-green leaves and produces showy, star-shaped yellow flowers in the summer. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It has a taproot system that allows it to survive in arid conditions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
