Pennisetum typhoideum

Pennisetum typhoideum, commonly known as purple lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted clumps and vibrant purple plumes that appear in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, fluffy seed heads that are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Pennisetum typhoideum is also valued for its ability to attract birds and other pollinators.
