Psychotria diffusa var. diffusa

Psychotria diffusa var. diffusa, commonly known as Ayahuasca.

It is a flowering plant native to the Amazon basin, characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of white flowers. The plant contains alkaloids, including DMT, which contribute to its psychoactive properties. It is often cultivated in humid, shaded environments with well-drained soil.

Its botanical significance lies in its role in traditional shamanic practices and its complex chemical composition.
