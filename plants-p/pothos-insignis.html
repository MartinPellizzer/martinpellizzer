Pothos insignis

Pothos insignis, commonly known as the golden pothos, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically variegated with shades of yellow and green. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and is known for its ability to purify indoor air. Its rapid growth rate makes it a popular choice for hanging baskets or as a trailing plant.

Pothos insignis is also notable for its adaptability to a wide range of environmental conditions.
