Poa kengii

Poa kengii, commonly known as the Keng's bentgrass, is a species of grass native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is a low-growing perennial plant that typically forms dense, mat-like turf. The leaves are narrow, grass-like, and often have a bluish-green hue. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Poa kengii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
