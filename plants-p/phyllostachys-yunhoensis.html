Phyllostachys yunhoensis

Phyllostachys yunhoensis, commonly known as Yunho bamboo, is a species of bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its rapid growth rate makes it a popular choice for landscaping and erosion control.
