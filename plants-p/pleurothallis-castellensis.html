Pleurothallis castellensis

Pleurothallis castellensis, commonly known as the Castell's orchid, is a species of orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. This epiphytic plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. It features small, lance-shaped leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow.

The plant is relatively rare and is often found in cloud forests, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions.
