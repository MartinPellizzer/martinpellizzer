Pristimera klaineana

Pristimera klaineana, commonly known as the Klamath lily, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its striking yellow petals and is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors. The plant belongs to the Liliaceae family and produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. Its bulbs are an important food source for various wildlife species.

Pristimera klaineana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
