Phillyrea quercifolia subsp. floribunda

Phillyrea quercifolia subsp.

floribunda, commonly known as the flowering Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features broad, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and oval in shape.

The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters, which bloom in spring. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils. Phillyrea quercifolia subsp.

floribunda is relatively drought-tolerant and suitable for rock gardens or as a hedge.
