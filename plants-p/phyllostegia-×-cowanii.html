Phyllostegia × cowanii

Phyllostegia × cowanii, commonly known as the hybrid flame flower, is a shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often blooming in summer. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant floral display.

The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly fuzzy texture.
