Pseudostellaria × segeolsanensis

Pseudostellaria × segeolsanensis, commonly known as the hybrid starwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit, with slender stems that often lie close to the ground. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This hybrid is often found in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or grassland habitats.
