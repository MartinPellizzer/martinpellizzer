Pellionia hallieri

Pellionia hallieri, commonly known as the silver fern, is a low-growing evergreen plant native to New Zealand.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are typically bright green with a soft, velvety texture.

Pellionia hallieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
