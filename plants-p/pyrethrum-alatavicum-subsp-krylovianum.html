Pyrethrum alatavicum subsp. krylovianum

Pyrethrum alatavicum subsp.

krylovianum, commonly known as the Krylovian pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals.

The plant produces pyrethrin, a natural insecticide, making it valuable in organic pest control. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun, often growing in mountainous regions.

This subspecies is noted for its ornamental value and ecological significance in its native habitat.
