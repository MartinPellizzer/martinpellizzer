Prunus × uyekii var. macrantha

Prunus × uyekii var. macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered cherry, is a hybrid species within the Prunus genus.

It is characterized by its showy, large pink to reddish flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic, with a smooth texture and a serrated margin.

The fruit is a small, dark purple drupe that matures in late summer, adding ornamental value to the plant.
