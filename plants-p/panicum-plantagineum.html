Panicum plantagineum

Panicum plantagineum, commonly known as silver grass, is a perennial grass native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as meadows and grasslands.

Panicum plantagineum is valued for its ornamental qualities and its ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.
