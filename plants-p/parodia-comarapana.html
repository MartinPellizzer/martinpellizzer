Parodia comarapana

Parodia comarapana, commonly known as the Comarapana cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed structure and typically produces yellow flowers. The plant has a dense covering of white to grayish spines, which provide protection from herbivores. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appearance, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant.
