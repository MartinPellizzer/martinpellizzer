Polygala eckloniana

Polygala eckloniana, commonly known as the Cape plume or sea plume, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery inflorescences. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in saline environments. It produces small, white or pale blue flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is often cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
