Phyteuma spicatum f. grossidentatum

Phyteuma spicatum f. grossidentatum, commonly known as the giant flowered phyteuma, is a horticultural form of the phyteuma species.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe, thriving in full sun to partial shade. It prefers well-drained soil and is often used in garden landscapes for its ornamental value.

Its foliage consists of lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy.
