Peperomia reflexa f. argentina

Peperomia reflexa f. argentina, commonly known as the Argentine peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically lance-shaped and have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, often in shades of green or red, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This ornamental plant is popular for its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
