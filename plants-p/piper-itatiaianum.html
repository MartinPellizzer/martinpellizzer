Piper itatiaianum

Piper itatiaianum, commonly known as the Itatiaia pepper or Brazilian pepper, is a flowering plant native to the Atlantic Forest region of Brazil.

It is a member of the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in spikes, and it is cultivated for its spicy fruits, which are used in traditional cuisine. Its leaves are also used in herbal remedies due to their medicinal properties.

The plant thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
