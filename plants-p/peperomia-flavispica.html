Peperomia flavispica

Peperomia flavispica, commonly known as the golden peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically bright green with a subtle golden hue on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is often used as a decorative plant in both residential and commercial settings.
