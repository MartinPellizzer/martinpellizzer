Peperomia congestifolia

Peperomia congestifolia, commonly known as the clustered peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense clusters of small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, which appear in late spring or early summer. Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for indoor cultivation, requiring minimal light and water.

Peperomia congestifolia is well-suited for bonsai-style pruning and can thrive in a variety of potting mediums.
