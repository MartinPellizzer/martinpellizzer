Prunus lannesiana

Prunus lannesiana, commonly known as the Lannesian cherry, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central America, particularly in countries like Guatemala and Mexico. The plant is characterized by its small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate with a serrated margin.

The fruit is a small, yellowish drupe that ripens in the summer months.
