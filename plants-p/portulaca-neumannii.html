Portulaca neumannii

Portulaca neumannii, commonly known as the dwarf portulaca, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches a height of only a few inches. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, yellow, and orange, which bloom throughout the summer months. Its leaves are succulent and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments.

Portulaca neumannii thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
