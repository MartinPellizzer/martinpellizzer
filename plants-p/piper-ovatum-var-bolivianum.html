Piper ovatum var. bolivianum

Piper ovatum var. bolivianum, commonly known as the Bolivian pepper plant, is a tropical vine native to South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces peppery berries that are used in traditional medicine and culinary applications. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
