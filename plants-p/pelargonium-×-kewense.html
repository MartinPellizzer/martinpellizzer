Pelargonium × kewense

Pelargonium × kewense, commonly known as the African marigold, is a hybrid geranium species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to yellow flowers and fuzzy, aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 feet. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Pelargonium × kewense is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
