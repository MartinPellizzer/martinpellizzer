Pseuderanthemum haughtii

Pseuderanthemum haughtii, commonly known as the Haught's false anthemum, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, clustered flowers. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
