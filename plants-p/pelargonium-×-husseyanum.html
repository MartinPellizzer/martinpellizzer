Pelargonium × husseyanum

Pelargonium × husseyanum, commonly known as the Persian geranium, is a hybrid species within the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically produces fragrant, rounded leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for container gardening and rock gardens.

Pelargonium × husseyanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.
