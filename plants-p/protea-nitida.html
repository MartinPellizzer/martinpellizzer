Protea nitida

Protea nitida, commonly known as the silver protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its intricate, feathery inflorescence that resembles a crown, composed of numerous small, tubular flowers. The plant is native to the Western Cape region and thrives in well-drained, acidic soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique appearance and long-lasting blooms.

Protea nitida is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
