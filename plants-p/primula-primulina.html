Primula primulina

Primula primulina, commonly known as the fairy lantern, is a small flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a yellow or orange throat. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are slightly hairy.

Primula primulina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
