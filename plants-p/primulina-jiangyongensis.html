Primulina jiangyongensis

Primulina jiangyongensis, commonly known as the Jiangyong primula, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often vibrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as mountain forests. Its leaves are usually ovate to lanceolate, with a glossy surface.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
