Pittosporum pickeringii

Pittosporum pickeringii, commonly known as the Pickering's pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features dark green, leathery leaves that are oval in shape and have a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that bloom in late summer, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native habitats.
