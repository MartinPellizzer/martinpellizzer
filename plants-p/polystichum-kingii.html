Polystichum kingii

Polystichum kingii, commonly known as the King's shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The fronds are deeply divided into narrow, lance-shaped segments with a dark green color and a leathery texture. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
