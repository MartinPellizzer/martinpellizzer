Peristylus australis

Peristylus australis, commonly known as the Australian rock rose, is a perennial shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. The plant has woody stems and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

Peristylus australis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
