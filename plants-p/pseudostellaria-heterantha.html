Pseudostellaria heterantha

Pseudostellaria heterantha, commonly known as the starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Asia.

It features small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, linear leaves that are often hairy, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors or stream banks.

Its botanical structure is adapted for efficient reproduction in its natural habitat.
