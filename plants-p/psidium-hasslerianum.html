Psidium hasslerianum

Psidium hasslerianum, commonly known as the Natal plum, is a small evergreen tree native to South Africa.

It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, followed by edible, yellow or orange fruits resembling small plums. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Its leaves are oval-shaped with a slightly waxy texture, and the tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its fruit.

The Natal plum is also valued for its ability to attract wildlife, particularly birds, due to its nutritious fruit.
