Portulaca dodomaensis

Portulaca dodomaensis, commonly known as the Dodoma moss rose, is a flowering plant native to Tanzania.

It is a low-growing annual herb that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features fleshy, succulent stems and leaves that store water, aiding in its survival in dry conditions. Its flowers are typically bright red or yellow, adding vibrant color to its habitat.

Portulaca dodomaensis is well adapted to hot, dry climates and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
