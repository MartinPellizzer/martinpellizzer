Peperomia delecta

Peperomia delecta, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery or grayish bloom, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia delecta is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and moderate humidity to flourish.
