Ptarmica

Ptarmica, commonly known as mountain sorrel, is a flowering plant belonging to the genus Potentilla.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Ptarmica has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges, and it produces edible leaves that have a tangy, sour flavor.

It is often used in herbal remedies and as a ornamental plant in gardens.
