Portulaca grandiflora var. grandiflora

Portulaca grandiflora var. grandiflora, commonly known as the moss rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a succulent annual with fleshy, cylindrical stems and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and yellow. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in arid conditions.
