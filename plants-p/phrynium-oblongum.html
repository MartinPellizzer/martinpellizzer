Phrynium oblongum

Phrynium oblongum, commonly known as the arrow leaf plant, is a tropical perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated with yellow and green patterns. The plant produces tall, spike-like inflorescences with small, inconspicuous flowers. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread vegetatively, making it a common ornamental in tropical gardens.

The leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported therapeutic properties.
