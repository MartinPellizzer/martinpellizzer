Polytoca gigantea

Polytoca gigantea, commonly known as the giant polytoca, is a striking plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, waxy leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Due to its size and visual impact, it is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical climates.
