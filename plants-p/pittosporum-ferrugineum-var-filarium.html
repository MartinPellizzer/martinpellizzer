Pittosporum ferrugineum var. filarium

Pittosporum ferrugineum var. filarium, commonly known as the golden pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features attractive, glossy, dark green leaves that turn a striking golden yellow in autumn. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscapes.
