Pouteria gallifructa

Pouteria gallifructa, commonly known as the Brazilian cherry or araçá, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. Pouteria gallifructa plays an important ecological role in its native habitats, supporting various wildlife species.
