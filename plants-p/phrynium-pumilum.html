Phrynium pumilum

Phrynium pumilum, commonly known as the dwarf arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

Phrynium pumilum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage.
