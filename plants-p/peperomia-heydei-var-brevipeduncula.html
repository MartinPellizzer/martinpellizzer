Peperomia heydei var. brevipeduncula

Peperomia heydei var. brevipeduncula, commonly known as the compact peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to South America.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
