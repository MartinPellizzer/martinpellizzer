Pteris deltodon var. deltodon

Pteris deltodon var. deltodon, commonly known as the shield fern, is a species of fern characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including China, Japan, and India. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and rocky crevices. Its fronds are bi- or tri-pinnate, with a dark green color and a leathery texture.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a humid, indoor environment.
