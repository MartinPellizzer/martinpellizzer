Pennisetum perspeciosum

Pennisetum perspeciosum, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender stalks that produce fluffy, purple-pink plumes during the growing season. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are usually green with a slight blue or silver tint. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Pennisetum perspeciosum is easy to grow and adds a dramatic, feathery texture to the garden.
