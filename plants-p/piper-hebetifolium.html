Piper hebetifolium

Piper hebetifolium, commonly known as the bitterleaf pepper or wild pepper, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant features small, elliptical leaves with a rough texture and a distinct, pungent aroma. Its flowers are clustered in spikes and are typically greenish-white in color.

The fruit is a small, three-lobed berry that contains the aromatic compounds responsible for its characteristic flavor.
