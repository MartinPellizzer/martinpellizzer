Persica oblonga

Persica oblonga, commonly known as the elongated peach.

It is a species of shrub or small tree native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. Its flowers are typically pink or white, and it produces small, round fruits with a fuzzy skin.

Persica oblonga is valued for its ornamental qualities and has limited use in fruit cultivation compared to other peach species.
