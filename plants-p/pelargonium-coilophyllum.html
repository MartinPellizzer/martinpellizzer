Pelargonium coilophyllum

Pelargonium coilophyllum, commonly known as the spiral geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique foliage and compact growth habit.
