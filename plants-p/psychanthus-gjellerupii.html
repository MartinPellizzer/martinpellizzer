Psychanthus gjellerupii

Psychanthus gjellerupii, commonly known as the Gjellerup's psychanthus, is a rare and specialized plant native to the high altitudes of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, tubular flowers that are adapted for specific pollinators, primarily certain species of bees. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil, often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are typically narrow and succulent, helping to conserve water in its harsh habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species.
