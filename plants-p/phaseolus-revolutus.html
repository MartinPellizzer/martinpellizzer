Phaseolus revolutus

Phaseolus revolutus, commonly known as the scarlet runner bean, is a climbing legume native to the Americas.

It produces vibrant red or orange flowers and edible pods that contain kidney-shaped seeds. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and can grow up to several meters in height. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This species is valued for both its ornamental appeal and culinary uses.
