Piper boquetense

Piper boquetense, commonly known as the Bouquet's pepper, is a climbing shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in spikes. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly rough texture.

The fruit is a small, three-lobed berry that turns from green to yellow or red as it matures.
