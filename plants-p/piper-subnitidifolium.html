Piper subnitidifolium

Piper subnitidifolium, commonly known as the glossy leaf pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and small, clustered white flowers. Its fruits are small berries that contain aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
