Phalaenopsis × delicata

Phalaenopsis × delicata, commonly known as the orchid with the "delicate" appearance, is a hybrid orchid species originating from crosses between Phalaenopsis and other orchid species.

It is characterized by its elegant, long-lasting flowers that typically come in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in a variety of conditions. Its leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, providing a lush, green backdrop to its showy blooms.

This orchid is popular in horticulture for its beauty and ability to bloom repeatedly under proper care.
