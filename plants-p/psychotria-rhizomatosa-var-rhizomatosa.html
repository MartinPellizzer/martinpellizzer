Psychotria rhizomatosa var. rhizomatosa

Psychotria rhizomatosa var. rhizomatosa, commonly known as the red-rooted psychotria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its slender, rhizomatous stems that allow it to spread vegetatively and form dense mats. The plant typically has opposite, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and often have a striking red or orange hue.

The plant is known for its chemical composition, which includes various alkaloids, making it of interest in ethnobotanical and pharmacological studies.
