Pandanus delestangii

Pandanus delestangii, commonly known as the Delestang's pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is a member of the Pandanaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically clustered in branched inflorescences. Its leaves are often used in traditional crafts and for thatching due to their durability.

Pandanus delestangii thrives in humid, coastal environments and is often found growing near the shoreline.
