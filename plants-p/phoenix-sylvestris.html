Phoenix sylvestris

Phoenix sylvestris, commonly known as the Indian date palm, is a tall, slender tree native to southern Asia.

It produces clustered, sweet-smelling flowers that develop into edible dates. The plant has a robust root system and can thrive in arid and semi-arid climates. Its leaves are long, feather-like, and can reach lengths of up to 4 meters.

The Indian date palm is also valued for its ornamental beauty and historical significance in traditional medicine.
