Pleopeltis zosteriformis

Pleopeltis zosteriformis, commonly known as the Zosteriformis fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its dense, creeping growth habit. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green fronds that are often covered in fine, silvery hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or rocky surfaces.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and conservation efforts.
