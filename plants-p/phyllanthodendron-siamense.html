Phyllanthodendron siamense

Phyllanthodendron siamense, commonly known as the Siam leaf tree, is a flowering plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand, and is characterized by its simple, alternate, and often elliptical leaves. Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in horticulture.
