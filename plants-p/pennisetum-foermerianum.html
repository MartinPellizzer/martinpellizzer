Pennisetum foermerianum

Pennisetum foermerianum, commonly known as fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, clumping growth habit and its feathery, silver-green foliage. The plant produces fluffy, pinkish or pale green seed heads that give it a soft, ornamental appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Pennisetum foermerianum is also valued for its ability to attract birds and other wildlife.
