Persoonia hirsuta subsp. evoluta

Persoonia hirsuta subsp.

evoluta, commonly known as the silky everlasting, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia. It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its unique morphology, including the reflexed leaf tips, aids in reducing water loss.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry conditions.
