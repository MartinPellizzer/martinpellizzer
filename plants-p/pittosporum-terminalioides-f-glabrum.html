Pittosporum terminalioides f. glabrum

Pittosporum terminalioides f. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth-leaved pittosporum, is a variant of the pittosporum species native to New Zealand.

This plant is characterized by its smooth, glabrous foliage, which distinguishes it from other forms of the species. It typically features dense, evergreen foliage with a glossy appearance. The leaves are elliptical in shape and have a dark green coloration.

It produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by orange or red berries.
