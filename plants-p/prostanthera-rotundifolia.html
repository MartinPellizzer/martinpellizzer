Prostanthera rotundifolia

Prostanthera rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features oval, rounded leaves that are often covered in a fine, soft hair. Its fragrant, tubular flowers are usually pale blue to violet and appear in clusters during the spring.

Prostanthera rotundifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional Indigenous medicine.
