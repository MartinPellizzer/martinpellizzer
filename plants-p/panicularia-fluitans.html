Panicularia fluitans

Panicularia fluitans, commonly known as the floating panicled grass, is a flowering plant that thrives in aquatic or semi-aquatic environments.

It is characterized by its delicate, floating stems and small, panicled inflorescences that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in shallow water, such as ponds, marshes, and slow-moving streams. Its leaves are narrow and linear, adapted for buoyancy and efficient light absorption.

Panicularia fluitans plays a role in stabilizing aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
