Pentagonia velutina

Pentagonia velutina, commonly known as the velvet pentagonia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, pentagonal-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has hairy, velvety leaves that give it its common name and contribute to its unique appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Pentagonia velutina is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to various soil types.
