Piperia elongata

Piperia elongata, commonly known as the slender piperia, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes plants like carrots and parsley, and is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves. Piperia elongata thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
