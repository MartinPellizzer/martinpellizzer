Pleurothallis crinita

Pleurothallis crinita, commonly known as the hair orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

This epiphytic plant is characterized by its delicate, hairy appearance, with long, soft bracts that give it its common name. It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The flowers are small and often pale green or white, with a subtle fragrance.

Due to its unique appearance and growth habits, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
