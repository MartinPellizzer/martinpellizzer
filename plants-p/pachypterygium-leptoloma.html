Pachypterygium leptoloma

Pachypterygium leptoloma, commonly known as the slender winged fern, is a unique species belonging to the family Pteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically green and finely divided. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizome is short and stout, supporting the growth of its intricate foliage.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in botanical gardens.
