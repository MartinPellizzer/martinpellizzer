Phyllostachys makinoi

Phyllostachys makinoi, commonly known as the Makino bamboo, is a species of grass native to eastern China.

It is characterized by its slender, green culms that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant has long, narrow leaves that are typically a vibrant green color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This bamboo is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
