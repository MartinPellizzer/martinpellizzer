Pleurothallis obovata

Pleurothallis obovata, commonly known as the broad-ovate pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features narrow, dark green leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments to ensure its survival.
