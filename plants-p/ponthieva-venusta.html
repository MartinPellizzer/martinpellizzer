Ponthieva venusta

Ponthieva venusta, commonly known as the velvet leaf.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa. The plant is characterized by its striking, velvety green leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns. It produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters.

Ponthieva venusta is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
