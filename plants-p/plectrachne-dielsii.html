Plectrachne dielsii

Plectrachne dielsii, commonly known as the narrowleaf plectrachne, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges. The plant has slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of a dense panicle with small, spikelet-like flowers.

Plectrachne dielsii is adapted to dry conditions and plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native environment.
