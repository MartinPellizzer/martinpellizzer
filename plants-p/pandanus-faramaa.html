Pandanus faramaa

Pandanus faramaa, commonly known as the giant pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific islands.

It is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces striking inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant flowers. It thrives in coastal areas and is often found near mangroves or in sandy soils.

Its robust structure and adaptability make it a notable species in its native ecosystems.
