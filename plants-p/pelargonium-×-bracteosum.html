Pelargonium × bracteosum

Pelargonium × bracteosum, commonly known as the angel's wings geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety, broad leaves that often display a mix of green, silver, and red hues. The plant produces showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, typically in late spring to early summer. Native to South Africa, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and ability to tolerate mild frost.
