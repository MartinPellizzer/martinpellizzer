Plectranthus carnosus

Plectranthus carnosus, commonly known as Cape rosemary, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features aromatic, gray-green leaves with a fuzzy texture and a strong, mint-like scent. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to violet, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for both ornamental and medicinal use.
