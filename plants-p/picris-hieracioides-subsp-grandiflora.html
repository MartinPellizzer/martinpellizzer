Picris hieracioides subsp. grandiflora

Picris hieracioides subsp.

grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered groundsel, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.
