Paphiopedilum callosum var. viniferum

Paphiopedilum callosum var. viniferum, commonly known as the grape-leaved slipper orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a single, leathery leaf that is broad and often has a reddish or purple hue. The plant produces a single, pendulous flower with a distinctive slipper-shaped lip that is typically yellow or orange with purple markings. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
