Pelargonium × ornatum

Pelargonium × ornatum, commonly known as the angel wing begonia, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its elongated, wing-like leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
