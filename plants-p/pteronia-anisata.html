Pteronia anisata

Pteronia anisata, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant typically grows in shrubland and coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Pteronia anisata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
