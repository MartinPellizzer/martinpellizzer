Phyllorkis exigua

Phyllorkis exigua, commonly known as the narrow-leaved daisy.

It is a flowering plant native to Australia, belonging to the Asteraceae family. The plant features small, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.
