Platymiscium trinitatis var. trinitatis

Platymiscium trinitatis var. trinitatis, commonly known as the Trinidad ironwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Caribbean, particularly Trinidad and Tobago.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and smooth, dark bark that becomes fissured with age. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a glossy surface. It produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by woody capsules containing numerous seeds.

This species is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often found in moist lowland forests.
