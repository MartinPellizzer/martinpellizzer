Portulaca elongata var. pedicellata

Portulaca elongata var. pedicellata, commonly known as the clustered portulaca, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing annual herb with fleshy, succulent stems and leaves that are typically green to reddish in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, and it is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its vibrant appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Its botanical features include a prostrate growth habit and a distinctive clustering of flowers along the stems.
