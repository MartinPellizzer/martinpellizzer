Pavonia schimperiana var. glabrescens

Pavonia schimperiana var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth pavonia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, pink to red bract-like flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and may have a slightly hairy texture, though the variety glabrescens is noted for its smoother foliage. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
