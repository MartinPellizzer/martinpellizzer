Polystichum lobatum var. chinense

Polystichum lobatum var. chinense, commonly known as the Chinese shield fern, is a perennial fern native to eastern Asia.

It features fronds that are typically bipinnate, with a deep green color and a leathery texture. The plant thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in forest understories. Its spores are produced on the undersides of the fronds, which are clustered in sori.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
