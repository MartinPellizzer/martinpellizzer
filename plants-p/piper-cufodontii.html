Piper cufodontii

Piper cufodontii, commonly known as the African black pepper, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants. The plant produces small, dark red berries that are used in traditional medicine and as a spice. Its leaves are often used in herbal remedies due to their aromatic properties.

The botanical features of Piper cufodontii include its opposite leaves and small, clustered flowers.
