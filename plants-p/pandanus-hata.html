Pandanus hata

Pandanus hata, commonly known as the Hata pandanus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that develop into fibrous, spiky fruits. Its leaves are fibrous and can be woven into mats and baskets.

The Hata pandanus thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.
