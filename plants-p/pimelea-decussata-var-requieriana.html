Pimelea decussata var. requieriana

Pimelea decussata var. requieriana, commonly known as the Narrowleaf Riceflower, is a low-growing shrub native to southern Australia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, which are clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and coastal areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
