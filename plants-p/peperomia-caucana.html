Peperomia caucana

Peperomia caucana, commonly known as the Caucana Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features broad, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia caucana is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
