Portea tillandsioides

Portea tillandsioides, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage, which is covered in dense, hair-like trichomes that help reduce water loss. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the spring.

Portea tillandsioides is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and air-purifying properties.
