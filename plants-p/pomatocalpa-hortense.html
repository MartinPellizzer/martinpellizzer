Pomatocalpa hortense

Pomatocalpa hortense, commonly known as the garden orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

The orchid thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial shade for optimal growth.
