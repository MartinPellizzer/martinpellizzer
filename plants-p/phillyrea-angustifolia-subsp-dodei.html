Phillyrea angustifolia subsp. dodei

Phillyrea angustifolia subsp.

dodei, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green.

The plant produces clusters of white, tubular flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators like bees. Its fruit is a small, woody capsule that splits open to release seeds. Phillyrea angustifolia subsp.

dodei is adapted to rocky and dry soils, making it a hardy, drought-tolerant species.
