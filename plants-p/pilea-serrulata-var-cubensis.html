Pilea serrulata var. cubensis

Pilea serrulata var. cubensis, commonly known as the Cuban prayer plant, is a species of flowering plant native to Cuba.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves with serrated edges, which give it its scientific name. The plant produces small, pale green flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This plant is popular among indoor gardeners for its attractive foliage and easy care requirements.
