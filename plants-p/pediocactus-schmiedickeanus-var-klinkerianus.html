Pediocactus schmiedickeanus var. klinkerianus

Pediocactus schmiedickeanus var. klinkerianus, commonly known as the Klinkerianus cactus, is a member of the Cactaceae family native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a short, thick stem and is typically covered in dense, white to pale blue spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking contrast to its blue-green body. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy stems.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is often cultivated by cactus enthusiasts.
