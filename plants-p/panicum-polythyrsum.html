Panicum polythyrsum

Panicum polythyrsum, commonly known as silverleaf panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a grass species characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which results from dense pubescence covering its leaves. The plant produces fluffy, pale brown seed heads that are typical of the Panicum genus. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in disturbed areas and along roadsides.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for native gardens and erosion control.
