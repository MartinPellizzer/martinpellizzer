Peperomia candelaber

Peperomia candelaber, commonly known as the candlestick peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features upright, branching stems that resemble a candlestick, with small, waxy, oval-shaped leaves arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate watering.

Peperomia candelaber is popular as a houseplant due to its unique appearance and ease of care.
