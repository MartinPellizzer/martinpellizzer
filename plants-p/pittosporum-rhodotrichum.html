Pittosporum rhodotrichum

Pittosporum rhodotrichum, commonly known as the silver leaf pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and silvery white underneath, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which bloom in spring. These flowers are followed by dark purple berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.
