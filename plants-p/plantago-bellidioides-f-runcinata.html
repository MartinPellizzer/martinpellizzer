Plantago bellidioides f. runcinata

Plantago bellidioides f. runcinata, commonly known as the broadleaf plantain, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a basal rosette of broad, lance-shaped leaves with a rough, hairy texture. The plant produces erect flowering stems that bear dense clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

Its leaves are used in traditional medicine for their anti-inflammatory and healing properties.
