Pelargonium × rochlederianum

Pelargonium × rochlederianum, commonly known as the Regal Geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, single or double flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically has upright stems and heart-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Pelargonium × rochlederianum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a potted plant.
