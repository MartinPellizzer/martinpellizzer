Premna sunyiensis

Premna sunyiensis, commonly known as the Sichuan premna, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale purple, arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
