Paphinia levyae var. angustisegmenta

Paphinia levyae var. angustisegmenta, commonly known as the narrow-segmented paphinia, is a rare orchid species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often fragrant flowers with a unique coloration pattern. The plant typically features lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-drained soil, making it a challenging but rewarding species for experienced growers.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered and requires careful conservation efforts.
