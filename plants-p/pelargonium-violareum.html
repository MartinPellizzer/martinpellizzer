Pelargonium violareum

Pelargonium violareum, commonly known as the violet geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Pelargonium violareum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and pleasant scent.
