Premna mortehanii

Premna mortehanii, commonly known as the Malabar premna, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to the Indian subcontinent, particularly found in the Western Ghats and other regions of southern India. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understoreys.

Premna mortehanii is often used in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
