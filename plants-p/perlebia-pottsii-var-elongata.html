Perlebia pottsii var. elongata, commonly known as the elongated perlebia, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to specific regions in Africa.

This plant is characterized by its slender, trailing stems and elongated, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
