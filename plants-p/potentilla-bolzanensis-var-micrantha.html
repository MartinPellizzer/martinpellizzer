Potentilla bolzanensis var. micrantha

Potentilla bolzanensis var. micrantha, commonly known as the Bolzanensis rose.

This variant is a shrub native to the alpine regions of Europe, particularly in the Dolomites. It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in well-drained, rocky soils.

It is often found in high-altitude meadows and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in alpine ecosystems.
