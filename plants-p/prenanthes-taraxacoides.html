Prenanthes taraxacoides

Prenanthes taraxacoides, commonly known as the yellow pearly everlasting, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has slender, hairy stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and rocky soils.

Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its ability to colonize new areas.
