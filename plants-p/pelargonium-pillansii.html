Pelargonium pillansii

Pelargonium pillansii, commonly known as the Pillans' geranium, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, rounded leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tinge. The plant produces small, pale pink to white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
