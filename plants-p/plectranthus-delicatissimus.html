Plectranthus delicatissimus

Plectranthus delicatissimus, commonly known as the velvetleaf plectranthus, is a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Plectranthus delicatissimus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a garden setting.
