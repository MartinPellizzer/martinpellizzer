Peperomia × subglabra

Peperomia × subglabra, commonly known as the glossy peperomia, is a hybrid plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often dark green with a glossy sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia × subglabra is relatively low maintenance and resistant to pests, making it an ideal plant for both novice and experienced growers.
