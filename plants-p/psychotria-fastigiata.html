Psychotria fastigiata

Psychotria fastigiata, commonly known as the red-eyed wonder, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers with contrasting white veins, which are highly attractive to pollinators. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a waxy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical rainforests.

Psychotria fastigiata is also notable for its role in traditional medicine and its use in certain cultural practices.
