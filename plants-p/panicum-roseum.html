Panicum roseum

Panicum roseum, commonly known as the rose panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its soft, pinkish to purple-colored inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Panicum roseum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
