Phyllorkis javanica

Phyllorkis javanica, commonly known as the Javanese orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the surrounding soil.

Phyllorkis javanica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
