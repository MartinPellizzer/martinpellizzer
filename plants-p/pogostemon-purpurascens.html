Pogostemon purpurascens

Pogostemon purpurascens, commonly known as the purple pogostemon, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square, hairy stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically purple or violet, arranged in whorls along the stem.

It is often grown for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
