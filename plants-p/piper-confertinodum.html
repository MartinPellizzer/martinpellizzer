Piper confertinodum

Piper confertinodum, commonly known as the dense-veined pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves with opposite arrangement and small, greenish-white flowers. It produces small, berry-like fruits that are typically used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it an important species for both ecological and medicinal research.
