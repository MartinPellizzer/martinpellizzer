Philodendron borgesii

Philodendron borgesii, commonly known as the Brazil Philodendron, is a climbing plant native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that can grow quite large. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Philodendron borgesii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
