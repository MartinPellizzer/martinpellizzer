Primula roylei var. alba

Primula roylei var. alba, commonly known as the white primrose, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky habitats at high elevations. Its leaves are basal, ovate, and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
