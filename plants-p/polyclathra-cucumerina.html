Polyclathra cucumerina

Polyclathra cucumerina, commonly known as the cucumber tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, grayish-brown bark and broad, ovate leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by edible, cucumber-like fruits. Its leaves and fruits are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The tree is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid, tropical climates.
