Peperomia ornata

Peperomia ornata, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub with upright stems and small, clustered flowers that range from white to pinkish in color. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to both novice and experienced plant collectors.
