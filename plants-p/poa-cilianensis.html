Poa cilianensis

Poa cilianensis, commonly known as the Cilician grass, is a perennial species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Poa cilianensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in low-maintenance landscaping.
