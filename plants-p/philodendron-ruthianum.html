Philodendron ruthianum

Philodendron ruthianum, commonly known as the Ruthianum philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded gardens.
