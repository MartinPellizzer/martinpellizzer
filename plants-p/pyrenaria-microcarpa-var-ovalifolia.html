Pyrenaria microcarpa var. ovalifolia

Pyrenaria microcarpa var. ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaf pyrenaria, is a species of flowering plant in the family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves and produces yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is native to parts of Southeast Asia and is often found in forest understories. It has a compact growth habit and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.

The fruit is a legume, typically containing several seeds.
