Pleurothallis albiflora

Pleurothallis albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically have white petals and a yellow lip. The plant grows as an epiphyte, often found on trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and arranged in a rosette pattern, helping it to conserve moisture.

This species is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
