Piper angustiae

Piper angustiae, commonly known as the narrowleaf pepper or Malabar pepper, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, opposite leaves. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.

The fruit, a small, spherical berry, contains the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.
