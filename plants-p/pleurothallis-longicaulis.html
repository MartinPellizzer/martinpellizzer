Pleurothallis longicaulis

Pleurothallis longicaulis, commonly known as the long-stemmed pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated stems and compact, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically arranged in a dense inflorescence. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
