Peperomia serpyllifolia f. nana

Peperomia serpyllifolia f. nana, commonly known as the dwarf groundsel peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions.

It features small, succulent-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate light conditions.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
