Pimelea petraea

Pimelea petraea, commonly known as the rock pimelea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky or sandy soils, often in heathlands and coastal areas. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers that appear in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing a dense, compact growth habit.

Pimelea petraea is well adapted to harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
