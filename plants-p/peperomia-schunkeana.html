Peperomia schunkeana

Peperomia schunkeana, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, often adorned with silver or gray veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white, emerging from the leaf axils. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it an ideal choice for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia schunkeana is relatively easy to care for, requiring minimal water and thriving in humid environments.
