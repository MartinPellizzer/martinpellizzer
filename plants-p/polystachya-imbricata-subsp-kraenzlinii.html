Polystachya imbricata subsp. kraenzlinii

Polystachya imbricata subsp.

kraenzlinii, commonly known as the Kraenzlin's feather-plant, is a member of the Orchidaceae family. It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense, feathery inflorescences composed of numerous small, white to pale green flowers.

The plant is native to tropical regions of Africa and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in a fine layer of white pubescence.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
