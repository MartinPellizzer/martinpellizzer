Psoralea bituminosa var. palaestina

Psoralea bituminosa var. palaestina, commonly known as the Palestinian psoralea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region, including parts of Israel, Palestine, and Jordan. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and semi-arid habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in traditional medicine and ecological restoration projects.
