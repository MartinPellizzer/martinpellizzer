Polygonum kawagoeanum

Polygonum kawagoeanum, commonly known as Kawagoe knotweed, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes species known for their distinctive leaf shapes and vibrant flowers. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a hairy texture and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or red. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscape design.
