Phreatia spathulata

Phreatia spathulata, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees in tropical rainforests. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and decaying organic matter.

Phreatia spathulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in greenhouses.
