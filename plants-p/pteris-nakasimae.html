Pteris nakasimae

Pteris nakasimae, commonly known as the Nakasima water fern, is a species of fern native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce clusters of small, spore-bearing leaves.

The botanical features of Pteris nakasimae make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and conservation efforts.
