Physosiphon carinatus

Physosiphon carinatus, commonly known as the zigzag swamp grass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Poaceae, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The inflorescence consists of spikelets that produce small, wind-pollinated flowers.
