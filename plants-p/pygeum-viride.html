Pygeum viride

Pygeum viride, commonly known as the green African tulip tree, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces clusters of showy, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit is a three-lobed capsule that contains three winged seeds.

Pygeum viride is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
