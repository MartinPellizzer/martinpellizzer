Pennisetum tenuifolium

Pennisetum tenuifolium, commonly known as blue oat grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, blue-gray foliage and delicate, feathery plumes that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 4 feet and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It prefers well-drained soil and is drought-tolerant once established.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
