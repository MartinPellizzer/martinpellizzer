Pterogonum hieraciifolium

Pterogonum hieraciifolium, commonly known as the common pterogonium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a low-growing shrub with small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in dry, open habitats.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in arid ecosystems.
