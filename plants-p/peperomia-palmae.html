Peperomia palmae

Peperomia palmae, commonly known as the palm peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features glossy, dark green, palm-like leaves that are elongated and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or green, which are followed by reddish berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage makes it a striking addition to any plant collection.
