Peperomia rotundifolia

Peperomia rotundifolia, commonly known as the Roundleaf Peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, rounded leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its low maintenance and attractive foliage make it a favored ornamental plant.
