Poa kitaibelii

Poa kitaibelii, commonly known as the Kitaibel's bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. The plant typically thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments. Its leaves are narrow and often have a bluish-green hue, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Poa kitaibelii is valued in horticulture for its resilience and aesthetic qualities in alpine gardens.
