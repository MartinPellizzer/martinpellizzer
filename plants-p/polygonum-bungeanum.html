Polygonum bungeanum

Polygonum bungeanum, commonly known as Chinese knotweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features upright stems with opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often reddish in color. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range from white to pink, typically blooming in late summer. It is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Its botanical characteristics make it both a ornamental plant and a potential invasive species in certain regions.
