Pilea lineolata

Pilea lineolata, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a subtle variegation of white or silver markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Pilea lineolata is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
