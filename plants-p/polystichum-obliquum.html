Polystichum obliquum

Polystichum obliquum, commonly known as the zigzag fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the temperate regions of the northern hemisphere.

It is characterized by its distinctive, oblique fronds that curve in a zigzag pattern, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its fronds are leathery and dark green, with a serrated edge.

Polystichum obliquum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its unique foliage.
