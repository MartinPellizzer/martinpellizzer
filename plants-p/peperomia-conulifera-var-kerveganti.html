Peperomia conulifera var. kerveganti

Peperomia conulifera var. kerveganti, commonly known as the Kervegant peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Madagascar.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a velvety texture and prominent, upright inflorescences. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers that appear in clusters, adding a delicate ornamental touch. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
