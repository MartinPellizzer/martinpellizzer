Plectranthus sieberi

Plectranthus sieberi, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces vibrant violet flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its foliage is thick and velvety, often with a striking color contrast to the flowers.

Plectranthus sieberi is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
