Polystichum decorum

Polystichum decorum, commonly known as the golden shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to eastern Asia.

It features dark green, leathery fronds with a distinctive golden hue on the underside, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are thick and creeping, allowing it to spread and form dense colonies.

Polystichum decorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
