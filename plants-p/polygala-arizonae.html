Polygala arizonae

Polygala arizonae, commonly known as Arizona milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features compound leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are often pink or purple in color. It plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and provides nectar for pollinators.
