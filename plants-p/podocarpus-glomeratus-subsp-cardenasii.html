Podocarpus glomeratus subsp. cardenasii

Podocarpus glomeratus subsp.

cardenasii, commonly known as the Candelabra Podocarp, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its dense, pyramidal crown and clustered cones, which give it its common name.

The plant has leathery, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. It is typically found in lowland rainforests and is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its attractive appearance.

This subspecies is valued for its ability to thrive in humid climates and its potential for timber and medicinal uses.
