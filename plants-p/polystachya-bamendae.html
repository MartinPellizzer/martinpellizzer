Polystachya bamendae

Polystachya bamendae, commonly known as the Bamenda sword fern, is a species of fern native to Cameroon.

It belongs to the family Polystachyaceae and is characterized by its elongated, sword-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce clusters of small, delicate leaves.

Polystachya bamendae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
