Podocarpus chinensis var. appressus

Podocarpus chinensis var. appressus, commonly known as the Chinese podocarp or narrow-leaf podocarp, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the family Podocarpaceae and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, cone-like fruits. The leaves are typically narrow and leathery, providing a dense canopy that offers shade and habitat for various organisms. This species is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

It plays a role in local ecosystems by contributing to soil stability and providing food for wildlife.
