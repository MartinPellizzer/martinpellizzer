Persicaria thunbergii f. viridialba

Persicaria thunbergii f. viridialba, commonly known as the silverleaf polygonum, is a variant of the common knotgrass.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-white variegation on its broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers in late summer, which are followed by dark purple, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

This ornamental plant is valued for its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
