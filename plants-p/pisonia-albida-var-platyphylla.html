Pisonia albida var. platyphylla

Pisonia albida var. platyphylla, commonly known as the broadleaf pisonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Pacific.

It is characterized by its large, showy white bracts that resemble petals, which are actually modified leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in coastal areas and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and landscapes.
