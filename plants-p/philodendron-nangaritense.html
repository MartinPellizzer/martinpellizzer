Philodendron nangaritense

Philodendron nangaritense, commonly known as the Nangaritense Philodendron, is a tropical plant native to the humid forests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by bright red berries, which are an important food source for local wildlife. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its lush foliage.

Due to its striking appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
