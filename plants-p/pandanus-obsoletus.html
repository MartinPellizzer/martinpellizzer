Pandanus obsoletus

Pandanus obsoletus, commonly known as the broad-leafed pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Pandanus.

It is native to the Pacific Islands, including parts of Hawaii and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences.

The plant is often used in traditional crafts and has ecological importance in its native habitats.
