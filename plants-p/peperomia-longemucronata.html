Peperomia longemucronata

Peperomia longemucronata, commonly known as the long-tailed peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a waxy texture and distinctive, elongated leaf tips that give it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white or pale pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia longemucronata is ideal for indoor gardening due to its resilience and ornamental appeal.
