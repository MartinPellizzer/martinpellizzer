Peperomia barthelemyana var. reducta

Peperomia barthelemyana var. reducta, commonly known as the compact peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Central America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly variegated appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.
