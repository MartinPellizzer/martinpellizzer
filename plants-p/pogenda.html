Pogenda

Pogenda, commonly known as the false dandelion, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragweed. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

Pogenda is often found in meadows and along roadsides, where it thrives in disturbed soils.
