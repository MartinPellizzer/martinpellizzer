Peperomia austin-smithii

Peperomia austin-smithii, commonly known as the Austin-Smith peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features oval, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or white, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia austin-smithii is a low-maintenance plant suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
