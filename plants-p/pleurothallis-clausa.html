Pleurothallis clausa

Pleurothallis clausa, commonly known as the closed pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a compact growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. Its flowers are typically clustered in small inflorescences and bloom during the wet season.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
