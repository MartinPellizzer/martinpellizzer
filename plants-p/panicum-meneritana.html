Panicum meneritana

Panicum meneritana, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a flowering plant species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and dense, reddish-brown inflorescence. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its slender, wiry stems and small, spike-like clusters of flowers are notable botanical features.

Panicum meneritana is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
