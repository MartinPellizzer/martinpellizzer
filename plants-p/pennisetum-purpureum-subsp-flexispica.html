Pennisetum purpureum subsp. flexispica

Pennisetum purpureum subsp.

flexispica, commonly known as giant blazing grass, is a tall, perennial grass native to Africa. It is characterized by its coarse, bluish-green leaves and striking, purple-tinted inflorescences that resemble plumes.

This subspecies is well adapted to tropical and subtropical climates and thrives in full sun. It is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its dramatic appearance and resilience.

The plant is also valued for its potential in erosion control and as a forage crop.
