Piper flaviramum

Piper flaviramum, commonly known as the yellow-flowered pepper plant, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, yellowish-green flowers. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a smooth texture and a mild, peppery scent.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of aromatic compounds.
