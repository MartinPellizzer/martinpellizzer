Peperomia macrothyrsa

Peperomia macrothyrsa, commonly known as the large-leaved peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, and often variegated leaves that are typically oval or lance-shaped. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia macrothyrsa is valued for its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
