Piper politii

Piper politii, commonly known as the velvetleaf pepper, is a member of the Piperaceae family.

It is a tropical climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Vietnam. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, often with a waxy texture. Its small, greenish-white flowers are followed by tiny, edible berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

Piper politii is valued for its aromatic leaves used in traditional medicine and as a spice in local cuisines.
