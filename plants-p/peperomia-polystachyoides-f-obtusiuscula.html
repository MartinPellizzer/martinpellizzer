Peperomia polystachyoides f. obtusiuscula

Peperomia polystachyoides f. obtusiuscula, commonly known as the broad-leaved peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically broad and rounded, often with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, spadix-like inflorescences that are usually greenish or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among indoor gardeners for its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
