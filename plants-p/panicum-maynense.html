Panicum maynense

Panicum maynense, commonly known as the Maynense grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas, particularly in Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, blade-like leaves. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets.

Panicum maynense is often found in open habitats such as grasslands, savannas, and along riverbanks.
