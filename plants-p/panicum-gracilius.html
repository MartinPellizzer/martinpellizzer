Panicum gracilius

Panicum gracilius, commonly known as slender panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its fine, narrow leaves and delicate, fluffy seed heads that give it a soft, feathery appearance. This plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Its growth habit is often clump-forming, with tall, slender stems that can reach up to 1 meter in height.

Panicum gracilius is also valued for its ability to withstand drought and poor soil conditions, contributing to its adaptability in various landscapes.
