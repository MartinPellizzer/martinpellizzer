Peperomia marcapatana

Peperomia marcapatana, commonly known as the Marcapata peperomia, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a compact, low-growing plant with thick, fleshy leaves that are often variegated in color. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or reddish in hue. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements make it a favored ornamental plant among plant enthusiasts.
