Piper nobile

Piper nobile, commonly known as the noble pepper plant, is a flowering vine native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by elongated, fleshy fruits containing aromatic seeds. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly spicy scent when crushed.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and its use in traditional medicine.
