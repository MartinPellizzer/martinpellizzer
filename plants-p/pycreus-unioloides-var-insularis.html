Pycreus unioloides var. insularis

Pycreus unioloides var. insularis, commonly known as the island marsh orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to specific island habitats.

It typically grows in wet, marshy environments, often in coastal or brackish areas. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, pale green or white flowers with a distinctive, intricate structure. It is characterized by its specialized adaptations to saline and humid conditions.
