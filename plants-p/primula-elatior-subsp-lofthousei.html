Primula elatior subsp. lofthousei

Primula elatior subsp.

lofthousei, commonly known as the Lofthouse's primrose, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of China. It typically grows in cool, moist environments such as forest understories and rocky slopes.

The plant features basal leaves that are broadly ovate to elliptic in shape, with a hairy surface. Its flowers are usually pale to dark purple, with five petals that are slightly reflexed at the tips.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
