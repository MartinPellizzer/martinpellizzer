Phoradendron wattii var. productum

Phoradendron wattii var. productum, commonly known as the Hawaiian mistletoe, is a hemiparasitic plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the family Viscaraceae and is characterized by its small, evergreen leaves and white, fleshy flowers. This species plays a unique ecological role in Hawaiian forests, contributing to the biodiversity of the region.
