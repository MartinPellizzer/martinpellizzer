Phillyrea prunellifolia-media

Phillyrea prunellifolia-media, commonly known as the Mediterranean laurustin, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a glossy appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in early spring, which are followed by blue-black berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a hardy and low-maintenance plant.

Phillyrea prunellifolia-media is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate coastal conditions.
