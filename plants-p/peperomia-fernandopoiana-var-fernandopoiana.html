Peperomia fernandopoiana var. fernandopoiana

Peperomia fernandopoiana var. fernandopoiana, commonly known as the Fernandopoiana Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It features dense, glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually green or red, depending on the variety. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This species is prized for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to indoor growing conditions.
