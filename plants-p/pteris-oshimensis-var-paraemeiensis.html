Pteris oshimensis var. paraemeiensis

Pteris oshimensis var. paraemeiensis, commonly known as the Oshimensis fern, is a variant of the Japanese spleenwort.

It is a fern species characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically bright green with a silvery underside. This plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its rhizomes spread horizontally, allowing it to form dense mats in its natural habitat.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
