Peperomia obscurifolia var. minor

Peperomia obscurifolia var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, dark green, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly waxy texture and a glossy appearance. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its compact size and ornamental foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
