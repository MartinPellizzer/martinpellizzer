Piper venturii var. yesoanum

Piper venturii var. yesoanum, commonly known as the Yesoanum pepper plant, is a variant of the Piper venturii species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a slightly pubescent surface. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into peppercorns, which are used in traditional medicine and culinary applications. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires support for its vine growth.

The botanical features of this plant make it an important species in both horticulture and ethnobotanical studies.
