Pycnandra sclerophylla

Pycnandra sclerophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pycnandra, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small shrub characterized by its evergreen, narrow leaves and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in horticultural and ecological studies.
