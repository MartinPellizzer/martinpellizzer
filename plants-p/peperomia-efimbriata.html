Peperomia efimbriata

Peperomia efimbriata, commonly known as the fringed peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with distinctive fringed edges, giving it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale green, often emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate moisture.

Peperomia efimbriata is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor conditions.
