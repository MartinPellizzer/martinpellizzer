Polypodium pellucidifolium

Polypodium pellucidifolium, commonly known as the translucent-leaf polypody, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, translucent fronds that give it a unique appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The species is relatively rare and is found in specific regions of Southeast Asia.
