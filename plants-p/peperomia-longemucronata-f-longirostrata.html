Peperomia longemucronata f. longirostrata

Peperomia longemucronata f. longirostrata, commonly known as the long-beaked peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a distinctive elongated, pointed tip that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and white, typical of the peperomia genus. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for collectors and those seeking ornamental foliage plants.
