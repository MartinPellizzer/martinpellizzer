Pilea mimema

Pilea mimema, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy, often with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate light conditions.

Pilea mimema is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
