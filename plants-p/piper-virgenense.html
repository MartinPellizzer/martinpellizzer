Piper virgenense

Piper virgenense, commonly known as the Virgin Islands pepper, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a slightly rough texture.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
