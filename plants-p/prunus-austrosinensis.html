Prunus austrosinensis

Prunus austrosinensis, commonly known as the Chinese plum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are typically yellow or red when ripe. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit production.

Prunus austrosinensis is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its potential health benefits.
