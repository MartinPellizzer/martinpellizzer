Pelargonium Staticifolium

Staticleaf geranium, scientifically known as Pelargonium staticifolium, is a unique plant native to South Africa. It is named for its ability to produce a static charge, which can be felt when touching its leaves. This characteristic makes it a fascinating subject for both botanists and curious gardeners. The plant is prized for its ornamental value and its ability to thrive in a variety of conditions. Despite its unusual properties, it remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticulture.
