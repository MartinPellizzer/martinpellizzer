Poa formosissima

Poa formosissima, commonly known as the beautiful bluegrass, is a perennial grass native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its fine, dark green foliage and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spikelet-like flowers that are typically blue to purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and meadows.

Poa formosissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
