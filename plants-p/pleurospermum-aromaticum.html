Pleurospermum aromaticum

Pleurospermum aromaticum, commonly known as sweet everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its long-lasting blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
