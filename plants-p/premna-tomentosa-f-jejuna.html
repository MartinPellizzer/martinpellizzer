Premna tomentosa f. jejuna

Premna tomentosa f. jejuna, commonly known as silky balm or velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a soft, velvety appearance. The leaves are opposite, ovate to elliptic, and covered in fine, white hairs, contributing to its distinctive look. It produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, round berries.

This plant is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.
