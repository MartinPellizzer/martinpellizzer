Phoebe hekouensis

Phoebe hekouensis, commonly known as the Hekou phoebe, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to China, specifically the Hekou region in Yunnan Province. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in mountainous areas.
