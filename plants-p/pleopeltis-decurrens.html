Pleopeltis decurrens

Pleopeltis decurrens, commonly known as the running pleopeltis, is a tropical fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its long, slender fronds that often trail or hang from trees. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping stems that root at the nodes. Its leaves are usually pinnately divided, with a soft, leathery texture and a dark green color.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
