Panicum willdenowii

Panicum willdenowii, commonly known as yellow panicgrass, is a warm-season grass native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and produces panicles of golden-yellow flowers. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Panicum willdenowii is often used in ornamental landscaping for its attractive foliage and floral display.
