Pilea longicaulis

Pilea longicaulis, commonly known as the Shiny Pilea, is a striking houseplant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and vibrant green, with a unique, elongated stem that gives it its scientific name. The plant thrives in indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is often admired for its ornamental value, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea longicaulis is relatively easy to care for, making it an ideal plant for beginners.
