Psiadia dodonaeifolia

Psiadia dodonaeifolia, commonly known as the coastal daisy, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is often found in dune environments. It has small, narrow leaves that are covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Psiadia dodonaeifolia is adapted to harsh coastal conditions, making it a resilient and hardy species.
