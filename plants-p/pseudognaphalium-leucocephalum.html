Pseudognaphalium leucocephalum

Pseudognaphalium leucocephalum, commonly known as whitehead cudweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and is often found in open habitats such as roadsides and fields.

The leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its drought-resistant nature.
