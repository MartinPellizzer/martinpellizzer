Polygala harleyi

Polygala harleyi, commonly known as the harley's milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, often zigzag-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a unique structure that resembles a milkweed flower. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endemic species in its native range.
