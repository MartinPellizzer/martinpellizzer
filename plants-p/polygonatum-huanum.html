Polygonatum huanum

Polygonatum huanum, commonly known as the Huan mountain yellow bamboo, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. Its leaves are lanceolate and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Polygonatum huanum is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional Chinese medicine.
