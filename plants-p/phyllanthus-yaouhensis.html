Phyllanthus yaouhensis

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, tropical environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
