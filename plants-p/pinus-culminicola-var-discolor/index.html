Pinus Culminicola Var. Discolor

Pinus culminicola var. discolor is a unique variant of the mountain pine known for its striking coloration. This rare plant thrives in high-altitude environments where few species can survive. Its foliage displays a distinct discoloration that sets it apart from other pine species. Found primarily in specific regions of the Andes, it is a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Due to its limited habitat, it faces increasing threats from climate change and human activity.
