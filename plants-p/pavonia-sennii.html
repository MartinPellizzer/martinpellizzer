Pavonia sennii

Pavonia sennii, commonly known as the pink false mallow, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, pink to red flowers with five petals. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and have a rough texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Pavonia sennii is often grown for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
