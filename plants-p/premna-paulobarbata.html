Premna paulobarbata

Premna paulobarbata, commonly known as the silky fig or Indian nettle, is a tropical shrub belonging to the Verbenaceae family.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, round fruits. It is native to regions of Southeast Asia, including India, Malaysia, and Indonesia.

Premna paulobarbata is often found in moist, shaded areas and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
