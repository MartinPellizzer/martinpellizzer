Polyscias gymnocarpa

Polyscias gymnocarpa, commonly known as the smooth-seeded glory bush, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate, ovate to elliptic leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers followed by spherical, smooth-seeded berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of timber in some regions.
