Phyllanthus fotii

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It is often found in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments.

Phyllanthus fotii is valued for its traditional medicinal uses in local communities.
