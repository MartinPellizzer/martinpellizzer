Polystachya fariae

Polystachya fariae, commonly known as the fairy's polystachya, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces clusters of small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elliptical and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Polystachya fariae is valued in horticulture for its unique floral display and adaptability to cultivated conditions.
