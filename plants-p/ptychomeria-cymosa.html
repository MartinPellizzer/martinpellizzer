Ptychomeria cymosa

Ptychomeria cymosa, commonly known as the mountain daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a basal rosette.

Ptychomeria cymosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
