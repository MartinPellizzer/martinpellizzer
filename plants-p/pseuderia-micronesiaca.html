Pseuderia micronesiaca

Pseuderia micronesiaca, commonly known as the Micronesian pseuderia, is a rare and endemic species of orchid found exclusively in the Micronesian islands.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, delicate flowers with a unique coloration pattern. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments within tropical rainforests, often growing on tree trunks or branches. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species facing threats from deforestation and climate change.
