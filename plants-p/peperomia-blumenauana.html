Peperomia blumenauana

Peperomia blumenauana, commonly known as the Blumenau peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant.
