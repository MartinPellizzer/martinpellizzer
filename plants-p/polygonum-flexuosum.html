Polygonum flexuosum

Polygonum flexuosum, commonly known as redroot smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems that are often reddish in color, especially near the base. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Polygonum flexuosum is often found in moist soils along stream banks, wet meadows, and other wetland habitats.
