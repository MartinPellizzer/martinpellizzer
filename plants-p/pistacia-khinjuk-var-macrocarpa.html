Pistacia khinjuk var. macrocarpa

Pistacia khinjuk var. macrocarpa, commonly known as the Indian chilgoza, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Himalayan regions of India and Pakistan.

It belongs to the Anacardiaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, spiny fruits. The tree is also valued for its ornamental features and traditional uses in local communities.
