Peperomia obtusifolia var. obtusifolia

Peperomia obtusifolia var. obtusifolia, commonly known as the glossy peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features thick, waxy, dark green leaves that are broad and rounded, often with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically greenish-white in color, which appear during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its slow growth and low maintenance requirements make it an ideal plant for both novice and experienced growers.
