Pilea lucens subsp. triplinervis

Pilea lucens subsp.

triplinervis, commonly known as the three-veined pilea, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia. It features heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves with a distinctive tripartite venation pattern.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Its unique foliage and ease of care contribute to its appeal among indoor gardeners.
