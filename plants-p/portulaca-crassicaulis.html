Portulaca crassicaulis

Portulaca crassicaulis, commonly known as the thick-stemmed purslane, is a succulent plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems and thick, fleshy leaves that help it store water, making it highly drought-tolerant. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the morning and close during the heat of the day. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in gardens and disturbed soils.

Its ability to survive in harsh conditions makes it a resilient and adaptable species.
