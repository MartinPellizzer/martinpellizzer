Polygala arillata var. purpurascens

Polygala arillata var. purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-flowered milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, pinkish-purple flowers. The plant produces distinctive, spiny pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
