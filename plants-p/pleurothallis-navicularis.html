Pleurothallis navicularis

Pleurothallis navicularis, commonly known as the boat orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, tropical environments. The plant features narrow, strap-like leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
