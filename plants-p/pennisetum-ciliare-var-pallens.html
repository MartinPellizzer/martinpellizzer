Pennisetum ciliare var. pallens

Pennisetum ciliare var. pallens, commonly known as silver fountain grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features slender, arching blades that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive, shimmering appearance. The plant produces fluffy, pale pink to white plumes in late summer, which add visual interest to gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping and drought-tolerant landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and elegant foliage make it a popular choice for borders and containers.
