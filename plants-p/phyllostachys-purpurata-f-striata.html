Phyllostachys purpurata f. striata

Phyllostachys purpurata f. striata, commonly known as the Zebra Bamboo, is a variant of the purple bamboo species.

It is characterized by its distinctive striped foliage, which features alternating light and dark green bands along the leaves. This ornamental bamboo is native to China and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. It is a clump-forming plant that can grow up to several meters in height, making it suitable for both garden and landscape use.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and bamboo collections.
