Polygonum ciliosum

Polygonum ciliosum, commonly known as the silky knotweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants commonly referred to as buckwheats and knotweeds. The plant features upright stems covered in soft, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its leaves are lance-shaped with a smooth or slightly serrated margin, and it produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or red.

Polygonum ciliosum is often found in moist, disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
