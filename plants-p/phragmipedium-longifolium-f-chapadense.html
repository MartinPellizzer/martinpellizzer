Phragmipedium longifolium f. chapadense

Phragmipedium longifolium f. chapadense, commonly known as the Chapadense phragmipedium, is a rare and striking orchid species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white pubescence. The plant produces strikingly large, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern, typically featuring a pale yellow lip and a deep orange or red dorsal sepal. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining, rich soil to flourish.

This orchid is highly sought after by enthusiasts due to its rarity and ornamental value.
