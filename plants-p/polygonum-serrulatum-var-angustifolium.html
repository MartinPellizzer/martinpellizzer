Polygonum serrulatum var. angustifolium

Polygonum serrulatum var. angustifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaf smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It features slender, upright stems that are often reddish in color, and its leaves are lance-shaped with serrated edges. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist soils and is often found in wetlands, ditches, and along stream banks.

Its botanical structure makes it adaptable to a variety of wetland environments.
