Phaseolus linearis var. latifolius

Phaseolus linearis var. latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf runner bean, is a variant of the common runner bean.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and elongated, bean-like pods. This plant is native to Central and South America and thrives in warm, temperate climates. It produces edible beans that are often used in culinary applications.

The plant is typically grown as an annual and requires well-drained soil and full sun for optimal growth.
