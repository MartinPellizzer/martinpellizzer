Plantago aucklandica

Plantago aucklandica, commonly known as the Auckland plantain, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its rosette of broad, fleshy leaves. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in damp, shaded environments such as forest floors and wetlands.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
