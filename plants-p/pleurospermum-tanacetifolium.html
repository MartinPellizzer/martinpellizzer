Pleurospermum tanacetifolium

Pleurospermum tanacetifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tansy, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, with a strong, aromatic scent. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, typically blooming from June to August. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in dry grasslands and rocky areas.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been historically used in traditional herbal remedies.
