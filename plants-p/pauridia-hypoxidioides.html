Pauridia hypoxidioides

Pauridia hypoxidioides, commonly known as the narrowleaf pauridia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in dry, sunny environments. The plant has slender, upright stems with small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and yellow, appearing in clusters during the cooler months.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh conditions.
