Plectranthus megafolius

Plectranthus megafolius, commonly known as the giant-leaf plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Plectranthus megafolius is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage and easy care requirements.
