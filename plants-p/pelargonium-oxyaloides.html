Pelargonium oxyaloides

Pelargonium oxyaloides, commonly known as the waxleaf geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.

Pelargonium oxyaloides prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for gardens and containers.
