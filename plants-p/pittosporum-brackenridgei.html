Pittosporum brackenridgei

Pittosporum brackenridgei, commonly known as the Brackenridge's pittosporum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pittosporaceae.

It is native to New Zealand and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy white or cream flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a leathery texture, while its flowers are clustered in umbels.

Pittosporum brackenridgei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
