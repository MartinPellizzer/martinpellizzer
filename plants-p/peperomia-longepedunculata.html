Peperomia longepedunculata

Peperomia longepedunculata, commonly known as the long pedunculate peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often dark green with a glossy texture, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, borne on elongated peduncles. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its low maintenance and attractive foliage make it a favored ornamental plant.
