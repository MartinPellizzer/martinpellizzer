Piper tolimae

Piper tolimae, commonly known as the Tolima pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the Andean region of South America, specifically Colombia. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with opposite, ovate leaves that have a rough texture. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are followed by elongated, cylindrical fruits that turn red when mature.

Piper tolimae is valued for its peppery flavor and is used in traditional cooking and herbal remedies.
