Pleurothallis Neoharlingii

Pleurothallis neoharlingii is a rare and delicate orchid native to the cloud forests of Colombia. This small epiphytic plant is known for its unique and striking floral display. It thrives in humid, shaded environments where it clings to tree trunks and branches. Due to its limited habitat and vulnerability to deforestation, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
