Pennisetum setosum var. laxiusculum

Pennisetum setosum var. laxiusculum, commonly known as the silky blue grass, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often a bluish-green in color. The plant produces fluffy, silver-gray flower spikes that bloom in late summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental grasses.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it suitable for a variety of landscaping applications.
