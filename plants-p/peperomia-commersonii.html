Peperomia commersonii

Peperomia commersonii, commonly known as the Commerson's pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purple hue on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or green flowers that are usually hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia commersonii is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
