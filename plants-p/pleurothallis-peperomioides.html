Pleurothallis peperomioides

Pleurothallis peperomioides, commonly known as the peperomoides orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant typically produces small, clustered flowers that are often white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, organic-rich substrates.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
