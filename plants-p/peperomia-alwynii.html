Peperomia alwynii

Peperomia alwynii, commonly known as the Alwyn's pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia alwynii is a low-maintenance plant that is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
