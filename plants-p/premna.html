Premna

Premna, commonly known as false mallow, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Lamiaceae.

These plants are native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific. They are typically shrubs or small trees with opposite, simple leaves that are often hairy. The flowers are usually small and clustered in dense inflorescences, often with two lips.

Premna species are valued for their ornamental qualities and have been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
