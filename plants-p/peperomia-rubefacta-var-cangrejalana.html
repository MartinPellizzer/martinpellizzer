Peperomia rubefacta var. cangrejalana

Peperomia rubefacta var. cangrejalana, commonly known as the Cangrejalana Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a distinctive reddish or maroon hue on the underside, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that are often hidden beneath its foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

This variety is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
