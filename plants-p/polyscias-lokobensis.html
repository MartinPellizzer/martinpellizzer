Polyscias lokobensis

Polyscias lokobensis, commonly known as the Madagascar gloryberry, is a shrub or small tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy berries. Its leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, with a smooth or slightly serrated margin.
