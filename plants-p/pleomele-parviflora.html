Pleomele parviflora

Pleomele parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered pleomele, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It features broad, leathery leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
