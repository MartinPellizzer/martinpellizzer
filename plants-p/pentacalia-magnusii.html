Pentacalia magnusii

Pentacalia magnusii, commonly known as the Magnusii pentacalia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
