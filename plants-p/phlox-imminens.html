Phlox imminens

Phlox imminens, commonly known as the showy phlox, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet. The plant features upright stems covered in opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its most striking feature is its clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of pink, red, and white.

Phlox imminens thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.
