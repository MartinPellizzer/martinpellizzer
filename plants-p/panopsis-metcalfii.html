Panopsis metcalfii

Panopsis metcalfii, commonly known as the Metcalf's panicle.

It is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant is characterized by its branched inflorescence, which consists of small, clustered flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and linear, adapted to arid environments.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in desert scrub and rocky habitats.
