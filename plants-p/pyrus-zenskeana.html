Pyrus zenskeana

Pyrus zenskeana, commonly known as the Zenske apple, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. The plant is characterized by its deciduous nature, producing small, yellowish flowers in the spring. Its fruit, a type of apple, is typically small and has a tart flavor.

The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential in horticultural breeding programs.
