Protasparagus compactus

Protasparagus compactus, commonly known as the dwarf asparagus, is a low-growing perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact foliage with small, needle-like leaves that are typically bright green. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white, fragrant flowers during the spring season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
