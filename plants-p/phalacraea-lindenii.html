Phalacraea lindenii

Phalacraea lindenii, commonly known as the Lindeni's ghost orchid, is a rare and enigmatic member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, pale greenish-white flowers that resemble ghostly apparitions, hence its common name. Native to the Democratic Republic of the Congo, this plant thrives in shaded, humid environments within montane forests. Its unique morphology includes elongated, strap-like leaves and a slender stem that supports its striking floral display.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Phalacraea lindenii is considered critically endangered and is of great interest to botanists and conservationists.
