Pothos

Pothos, commonly known as devil's ivy, is a popular houseplant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its trailing vines and heart-shaped leaves. The plant is known for its adaptability, thriving in low light conditions and requiring minimal care. Its waxy, dark green leaves often have a variegated pattern, adding visual interest to any space.

Pothos is also valued for its ability to purify indoor air, making it a favorite among plant enthusiasts.
