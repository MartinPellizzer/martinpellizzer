Paeonia forrestii

Paeonia forrestii, commonly known as the Chinese peony, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Paeoniaceae and is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant features palmately divided, dark green leaves and produces thick, fleshy rhizomes. It is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been cultivated for centuries in traditional Chinese horticulture.

Paeonia forrestii is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
