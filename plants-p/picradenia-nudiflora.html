Picradenia nudiflora

Picradenia nudiflora, commonly known as the naked-flowered picradenia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and natural habitats.
