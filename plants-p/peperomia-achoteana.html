Peperomia achoteana

Peperomia achoteana, commonly known as the red peperomia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its compact, succulent-like foliage and vibrant, red-tinged leaves. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color.

Peperomia achoteana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
