Phyllostegia tongaensis

Phyllostegia tongaensis, commonly known as the Tongan ironwood, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the South Pacific.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or lowland habitats.

Due to its hardy nature and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
