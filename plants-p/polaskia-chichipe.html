Polaskia chichipe

Polaskia chichipe, commonly known as the Chichipe barrel cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stem and is often covered in spines. The plant produces large, showy yellow flowers during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for desert gardens.
