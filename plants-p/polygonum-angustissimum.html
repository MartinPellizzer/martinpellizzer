Polygonum angustissimum

Polygonum angustissimum, commonly known as narrowleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist soils along streambanks and wetlands.

Polygonum angustissimum is often found in disturbed areas and is considered an invasive species in some regions.
