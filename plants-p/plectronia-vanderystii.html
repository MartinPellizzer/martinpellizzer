Plectronia vanderystii

Plectronia vanderystii, commonly known as the Vanderyst's plectron, is a rare flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Central Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically violet or purple in color, with intricate patterns resembling a plectron, a type of feather. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and thrives in humid, shaded environments. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds dispersed by wind.

Due to habitat loss, Plectronia vanderystii is considered endangered and is of great interest to botanists and conservationists.
