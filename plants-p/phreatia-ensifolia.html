Phreatia ensifolia

Phreatia ensifolia, commonly known as the sword-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves that resemble a sword, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a distinctive coloration that ranges from white to pale yellow.

Phreatia ensifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
