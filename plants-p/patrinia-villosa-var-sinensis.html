Patrinia villosa var. sinensis

Patrinia villosa var. sinensis, commonly known as Chinese Patrinia, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features opposite, hairy leaves that are typically lance-shaped and have a slightly serrated edge. Its flowers are small, tubular, and typically violet or blue in color, arranged in whorled clusters.

It is often used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
