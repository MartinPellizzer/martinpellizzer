Psychotria orosioides

Psychotria orosioides, commonly known as the Orosi poison vine, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Due to its toxic properties, it is often avoided in traditional medicine and landscaping.
