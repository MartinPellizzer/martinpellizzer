Peperomia martagonifolia var. wercklei

Peperomia martagonifolia var. wercklei, commonly known as the Wercklei peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves that are often adorned with silver or white veins, creating a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the warmer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and moderate humidity.

Its unique leaf patterns and manageable size make it a popular choice for tabletop displays and shaded garden areas.
