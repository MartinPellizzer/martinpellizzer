Peperomia limaensis

Peperomia limaensis, commonly known as Lima peperomia, is a compact, low-growing herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in its natural habitat. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, arranged in clusters at the tips of the stems. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its ability to thrive in low light and minimal water conditions.

Its ornamental value comes primarily from its unique leaf shapes and textures.
