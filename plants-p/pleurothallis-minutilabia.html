Pleurothallis minutilabia

Pleurothallis minutilabia, commonly known as the tiny-lobed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

This small epiphytic orchid typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. It features delicate, narrow leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant is noted for its intricate floral structure and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a challenging species for cultivation.
