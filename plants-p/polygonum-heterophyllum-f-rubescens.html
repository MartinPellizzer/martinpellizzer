Polygonum heterophyllum f. rubescens

Polygonum heterophyllum f. rubescens, commonly known as the red-stemmed smartweed, is a flowering plant in the Polygonaceae family.

It is characterized by its reddish stems and alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers with pale green to pinkish sepals, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in moist soils and is often found in wetlands, ditches, and along stream banks.

This variant is distinguished by its vibrant red stem coloration, which sets it apart from other forms of Polygonum heterophyllum.
