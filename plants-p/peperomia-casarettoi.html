Peperomia casarettoi

Peperomia casarettoi, commonly known as the Casaretto's peperomia, is a compact, low-growing perennial plant native to South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale green, which are usually borne on short spikes. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its compact size and ornamental foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
