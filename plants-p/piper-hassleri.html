Piper hassleri

Piper hassleri, commonly known as the Mexican pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its climbing habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by berries containing the seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.

The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.
