Prangos alata

Prangos alata, commonly known as the winged bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, often with a white or yellow throat. The plant has distinctive winged stems and leaves that are lance-shaped with a rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or alpine environments.

Prangos alata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
