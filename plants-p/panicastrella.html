Panicastrella

Panicastrella, commonly known as the bottlebrush plant, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, dense clusters of red or orange flowers that resemble a bottlebrush. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 feet. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.
