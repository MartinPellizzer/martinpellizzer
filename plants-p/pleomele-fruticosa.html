Pleomele fruticosa

Pleomele fruticosa, commonly known as the silver pleomele, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, leathery leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color, which bloom during the warmer months. Its foliage is evergreen, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant is also valued for its ability to thrive in shaded environments, contributing to its versatility in landscaping.
