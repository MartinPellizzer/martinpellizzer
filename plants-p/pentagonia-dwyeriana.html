Pentagonia dwyeriana

Pentagonia dwyeriana, commonly known as the dwarf pentagonia, is a member of the Malvaceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub characterized by its simple, ovate leaves and showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 cm and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
