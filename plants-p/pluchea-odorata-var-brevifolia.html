Pluchea odorata var. brevifolia

Pluchea odorata var. brevifolia, commonly known as sweet everlasting, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant yellow flowers and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its aromatic properties. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature and pleasant scent.
