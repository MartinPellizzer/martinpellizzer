Pennisetum pauperum

Pennisetum pauperum, commonly known as poor grass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. This plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping and drought-tolerant gardens. Pennisetum pauperum is often used as a ornamental plant due to its attractive, feathery appearance.

It is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in arid environments.
