Paeonia ostii

Paeonia ostii, commonly known as the Chinese peony, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, white, and yellow. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and produces woody stems that can grow up to several feet tall. Paeonia ostii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its spring blooms.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
