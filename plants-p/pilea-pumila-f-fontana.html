Pilea pumila f. fontana

Pilea pumila f. fontana, commonly known as the Fontana pilea, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, rounded leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a waxy texture. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
