Pseuderia trachychila

Pseuderia trachychila, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a distinct, intricate pattern on the petals.

Pseuderia trachychila is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts of tropical flora.
