Paeonia lutea f. superba

Paeonia lutea f. superba, commonly known as the noble peony, is a distinctive cultivar of the peony plant.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant is a herbaceous perennial, known for its robust growth and ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.

Its botanical features include compound leaves and a tall, sturdy stem that supports its vibrant blooms.
