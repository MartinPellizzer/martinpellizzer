Paeonia arietina var. orientalis

Paeonia arietina var. orientalis, commonly known as the oriental peony, is a deciduous perennial plant native to Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with intricate patterns. The plant features compound, bipinnate leaves that are green and velvety in texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

The oriental peony is also valued for its fragrant blooms and is often cultivated for its aesthetic and cultural significance.
