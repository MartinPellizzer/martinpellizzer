Peperomia polycephala

Peperomia polycephala, commonly known as the "Crown of Thorns," is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense clusters of small, waxy, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a unique and compact appearance. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color, which are followed by small, berry-like fruits. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Peperomia polycephala is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to add a tropical touch to any indoor space.
