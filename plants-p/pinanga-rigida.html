Pinanga rigida

Pinanga rigida, commonly known as the rigid pinang, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant features a short, stout rhizome with dark green, leathery leaves that are often clustered at the base. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe, typical of the Araceae family.

Pinanga rigida is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique floral structure.
