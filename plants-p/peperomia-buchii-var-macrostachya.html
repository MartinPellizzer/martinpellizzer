Peperomia buchii var. macrostachya

Peperomia buchii var. macrostachya, commonly known as the large-stalked peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green elliptical leaves with a slightly waxy texture and a prominent midrib. The plant produces small, pale green to white spathes that enclose clusters of tiny, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and ease of care contribute to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
