Psoralea algoensis

Psoralea algoensis, commonly known as the desert psoralea, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and compound leaves. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments, with deep roots that help it access water from underground sources.
