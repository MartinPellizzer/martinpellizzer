Phylica dodii

Phylica dodii, commonly known as the Natal flame bush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces bright orange-red flowers that are clustered in spherical inflorescences, which are highly attractive to birds and insects. Its leaves are narrow, leathery, and arranged in whorls around the stems.

Phylica dodii is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its striking appearance.
