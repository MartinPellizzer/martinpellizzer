Psychilis truncata

Psychilis truncata, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped foliage. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of violet, pink, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Psychilis truncata is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
