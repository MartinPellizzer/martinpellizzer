Plectocomia elongata var. elongata

Plectocomia elongata var. elongata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf plectocomia, is a perennial herb native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, elongated leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Its botanical features make it a unique species within the Apiaceae family.
