Polygonatum odoratum var. thunbergii

Polygonatum odoratum var. thunbergii, commonly known as the Chinese Solomon's seal, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features rhizomatous stems that produce lanceolate, dark green leaves arranged in a rosette. The plant produces clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers in early summer, followed by red berries. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value and medicinal properties make it a popular choice in horticulture and traditional medicine.
