Pleurothallis arbusculoides

Pleurothallis arbusculoides, commonly known as the tree orchid, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. The plant features dense clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the humid, shaded forest floor.

Pleurothallis arbusculoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
