Phyllanthus balansae

Phyllanthus balansae, commonly known as the African spurge, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, characterized by its small, clustered leaves and yellow flowers. The plant has a woody stem and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides. Its botanical features include opposite leaf arrangement and small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds.

It is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
