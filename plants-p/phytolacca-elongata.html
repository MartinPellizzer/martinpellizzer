Phytolacca elongata

Phytolacca elongata, commonly known as the Chinese Pokeweed, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that can grow up to 3 meters tall, featuring alternate, ovate leaves with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers followed by dark purple berries. Its roots and leaves contain toxic compounds, making it potentially harmful to humans and animals.

Despite its toxicity, it has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
