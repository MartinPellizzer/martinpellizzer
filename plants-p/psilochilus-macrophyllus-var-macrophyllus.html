Psilochilus macrophyllus var. macrophyllus

Psilochilus macrophyllus var. macrophyllus, commonly known as the broad-leafed psilochilus, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with a distinctive yellow and purple coloration. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in greenhouses and botanical gardens.
