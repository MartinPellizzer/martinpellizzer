Polystachya golungensis

Polystachya golungensis, commonly known as the Golung's spikegrass, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of Africa.

This herbaceous plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks. It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has fibrous roots and produces rhizomes, which help it spread and establish in suitable habitats.
