Phillyrea angustifolia subvar. brevifolia

Phillyrea angustifolia subvar. brevifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically narrower than those of the typical variety. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. Its fruit is a capsule containing several seeds.

This subvariety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
