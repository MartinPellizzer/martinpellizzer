Pilea boiviniana

Pilea boiviniana, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, often with a slight waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and ease of care have made it a favored ornamental plant worldwide.
