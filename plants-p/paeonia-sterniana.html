Paeonia sterniana

Paeonia sterniana, commonly known as the Chinese peony, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant has deeply lobed leaves and produces distinctive, spiny seed pods that mature in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Paeonia sterniana is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its historical significance in Chinese culture.
