Poa boecheri

Poa boecheri, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada bluegrass, is a species of grass native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in mountainous regions. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale blue to greenish flowers.

Poa boecheri is often found in meadows and along streambanks, where it contributes to the ecological diversity of its habitat.
