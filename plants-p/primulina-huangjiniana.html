Primulina huangjiniana

Primulina huangjiniana, commonly known as the Huangjiniana gloxinia, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, velvety leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows as a low-growing herb with a compact rosette structure. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements have made it a favored plant among enthusiasts of tropical foliage.
