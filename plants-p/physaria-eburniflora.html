Physaria eburniflora

Physaria eburniflora, commonly known as the silverleaf bladderpod, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its glaucous, silver-green leaves and distinctive inflated seed pods. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer.

Physaria eburniflora is an important species for pollinators and is often found in disturbed or arid environments.
