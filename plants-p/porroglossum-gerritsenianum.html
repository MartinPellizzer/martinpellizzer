Porroglossum gerritsenianum

Porroglossum gerritsenianum, commonly known as the Florida porroglossum, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in wetland environments and is often found in marshes and along the edges of ponds.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native habitat.
