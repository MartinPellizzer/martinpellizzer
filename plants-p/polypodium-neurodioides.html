Polypodium neurodioides

Polypodium neurodioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaf huperzia, is a species of fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its simple, linear leaves that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant is evergreen and thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its fronds are usually unbranched and have a distinctive, narrow shape.

Polypodium neurodioides is notable for its ability to survive in low-light conditions and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.
