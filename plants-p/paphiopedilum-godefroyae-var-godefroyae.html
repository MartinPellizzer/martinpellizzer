Paphiopedilum godefroyae var. godefroyae

Paphiopedilum godefroyae var. godefroyae, commonly known as the Godefroy's slipper orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive pouch-like lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and has a compact growth habit. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to green with reddish or brownish markings.

This orchid is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
