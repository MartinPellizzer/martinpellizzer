Polygala friesii

Polygala friesii, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often attracting pollinators like bees and birds. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Polygala friesii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
