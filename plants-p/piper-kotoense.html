Piper kotoense

Piper kotoense, commonly known as the Kotoense pepper or Kotoense pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with long, slender stems that often require support. Its leaves are typically alternate, ovate to elliptic in shape, and have a rough, slightly hairy texture.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by small, peppery fruits that are used in traditional culinary and medicinal practices.
