Phaca glareosa

Phaca glareosa, commonly known as the shining phaca, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its stems are erect and branching.

Phaca glareosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.
