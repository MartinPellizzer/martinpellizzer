Protasparagus aethiopicus

Protasparagus aethiopicus, commonly known as the Ethiopian asparagus, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its erect, spiny stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant in dry climates.
