Prosartes

Prosartes, commonly known as the bottlebrush fern, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its unique, bottle-shaped inflorescences that resemble a bottle brush, hence its common name. They are valued in horticulture for their ornamental foliage and distinctive floral structures.
