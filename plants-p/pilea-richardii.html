Pilea richardii

Pilea richardii, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its round, fleshy leaves that resemble small coins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are typically hidden beneath its leaves. It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.

Pilea richardii thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
