Primula Glabra Subsp. Genestieriana

Primula glabra subsp. genestieriana is a rare and delicate plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. It is known for its small, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. This subspecies thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded woodland areas. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a threatened species. Its beauty and rarity make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
