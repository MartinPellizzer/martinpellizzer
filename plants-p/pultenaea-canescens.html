Pultenaea canescens

Pultenaea canescens, commonly known as the grey pultenaea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features narrow, greyish-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and dry woodland habitats.

Pultenaea canescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and visual interest.
