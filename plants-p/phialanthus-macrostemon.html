Phialanthus macrostemon

Phialanthus macrostemon, commonly known as the giant cupplant, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a stout, unbranched stem that can grow up to 6 feet tall. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

The fruit is a three-lobed, cup-shaped structure that contains three seeds.
