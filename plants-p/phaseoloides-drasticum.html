Phaseoloides drasticum

Phaseoloides drasticum, commonly known as the deadly nightshade.

This plant is a member of the Solanaceae family and is characterized by its toxic alkaloids, which make it highly poisonous. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or white in color. The berries are bright red when mature and are often mistaken for edible fruits.

Due to its toxicity, it is considered a dangerous plant and should be handled with care.
