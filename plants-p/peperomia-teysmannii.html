Peperomia teysmannii

Peperomia teysmannii, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery-white bloom, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia teysmannii is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
