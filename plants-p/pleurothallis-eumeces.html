Pleurothallis eumeces

Pleurothallis eumeces, commonly known as the golden-lipped pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers that are often yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
