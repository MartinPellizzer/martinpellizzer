Psittacanthus intermedius

Psittacanthus intermedius, commonly known as the intermediate arrowroot or arrowleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Araceae.

It is native to Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features arrow-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy, with a prominent midrib. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, a characteristic feature of the Araceae family.

Psittacanthus intermedius is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ability to purify indoor air.
