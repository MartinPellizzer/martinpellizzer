Pelargonium althaeifolium

Pelargonium althaeifolium, commonly known as the Althaeifolium geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, typically blooming in summer. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and flowers.

Pelargonium althaeifolium prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
