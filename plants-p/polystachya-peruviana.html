Polystachya peruviana

Polystachya peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian featherplant, is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly Peru and Ecuador. This plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in high-altitude cloud forests.

The plant's unique appearance and adaptability make it a subject of interest for both botanists and horticulturists.
