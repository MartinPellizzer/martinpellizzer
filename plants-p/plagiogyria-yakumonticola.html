Plagiogyria yakumonticola

It thrives in extremely cold, nutrient-poor environments, often growing on rocky substrates or in moist, shaded areas. The plant exhibits a compact, cushion-like growth form, which helps it retain moisture and withstand harsh climatic conditions. Its leaves are typically narrow and linear, with a distinct midrib, and it reproduces via spores released from tiny, clustered capsules.
