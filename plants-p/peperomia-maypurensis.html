Peperomia maypurensis

Peperomia maypurensis, commonly known as the Maypurensis Peperomia, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a compact, evergreen perennial shrub characterized by its glossy, oval-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters and features small, clustered flowers in shades of white or pale pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia maypurensis is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
