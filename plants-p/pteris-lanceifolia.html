Pteris lanceifolia

Pteris lanceifolia, commonly known as the lanceleaf water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Pteridaceae.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped, serrated fronds that are typically green and glossy. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce roots that help anchor it to substrates like soil or rocks.

Pteris lanceifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
