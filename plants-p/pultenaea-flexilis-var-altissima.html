Pultenaea flexilis var. altissima

Pultenaea flexilis var. altissima, commonly known as the tall pultenaea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and small, often pink or red flowers that appear in late winter to early spring. The leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a leathery texture. This variety is distinguished by its height, often reaching up to 2 meters, making it one of the tallest forms within the Pultenaea genus.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.
