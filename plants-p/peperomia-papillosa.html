Peperomia papillosa

Peperomia papillosa, commonly known as the velvety peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with a distinctive velvety texture and a deep green color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia papillosa is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
