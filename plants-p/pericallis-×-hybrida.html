Pericallis × hybrida

Pericallis × hybrida, commonly known as the African violet, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically produces violet-colored blooms in shades of purple, blue, and white, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Pericallis × hybrida is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
