Prangos pachypoda

Prangos pachypoda, commonly known as the broad-leaved centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its sturdy, upright stems and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Prangos pachypoda thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable plant for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
