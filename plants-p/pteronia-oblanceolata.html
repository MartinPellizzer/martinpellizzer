Pteronia oblanceolata

Pteronia oblanceolata, commonly known as the silver leaf geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red, typically blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Pteronia oblanceolata is also valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
