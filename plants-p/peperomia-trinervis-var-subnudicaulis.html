Peperomia trinervis var. subnudicaulis

Peperomia trinervis var. subnudicaulis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and have a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces delicate, spathiform bracts that enclose small, inconspicuous flowers, which are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its compact size and attractive foliage, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
