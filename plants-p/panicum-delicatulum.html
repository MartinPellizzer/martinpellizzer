Panicum delicatulum

Panicum delicatulum, commonly known as slender panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its delicate, slender stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and prairies. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Panicum delicatulum is often found in wet or seasonally flooded areas, where it thrives in the presence of standing water.
