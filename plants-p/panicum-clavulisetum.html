Panicum clavulisetum

Panicum clavulisetum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or disturbed areas.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
