Pleurothallis arachnion

Pleurothallis arachnion, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small epiphytic orchid, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features intricate, spider-like flowers that are often pale green or yellowish in color, with a distinctive, elongated shape. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrate.

Pleurothallis arachnion is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relative ease of cultivation.
