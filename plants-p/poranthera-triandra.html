Poranthera triandra

Poranthera triandra, commonly known as the pink sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of California.

It is characterized by its delicate, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and thrives in well-drained sandy soils. It is often found in dunes and coastal scrub habitats.
