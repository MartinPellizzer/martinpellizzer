Poa ajanensis

Poa ajanensis, commonly known as the Ajanensis grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly found in Argentina and Chile. This plant typically grows in moist, temperate environments such as meadows and grasslands. Poa ajanensis is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, erect stems.

Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration that helps it thrive in its natural habitat.
