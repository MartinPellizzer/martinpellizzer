Potentilla freyniana var. sinica

Potentilla freyniana var. sinica, commonly known as Chinese potentilla, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and is often used in ornamental horticulture. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically rounded and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.
