Pachychilus pantanus

Pachychilus pantanus, commonly known as the Brazilian velvet tree, is a member of the Malvaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are palmately lobed and have a soft, hairy texture.
