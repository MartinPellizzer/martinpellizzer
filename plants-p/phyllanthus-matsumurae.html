Phyllanthus matsumurae

Phyllanthus matsumurae, commonly known as the Japanese Phyllanthus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including parts of Japan, China, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered yellow flowers and smooth, dark green leaves. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas.

Phyllanthus matsumurae is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
