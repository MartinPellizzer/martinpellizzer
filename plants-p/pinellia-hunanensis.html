Pinellia hunanensis

Pinellia hunanensis, commonly known as Hunan pinellia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its underground rhizomes and erect, leafy stems. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in a spadix, enclosed by a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its medicinal properties, it is valued in traditional Chinese medicine.
