Potentilla verna var. asterotricha

Potentilla verna var. asterotricha, commonly known as the early potentilla, is a low-growing shrub native to Europe and western Asia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, often before the leaves fully emerge. The plant has slender, arching stems covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and typically have a hairy underside.

This variety is often found in moist, shady habitats such as woodlands and streambanks.
