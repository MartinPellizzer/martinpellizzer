Piper holtonii var. cundinamarcanum

Piper holtonii var. cundinamarcanum, commonly known as the Cundinamarca pepper, is a flowering plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing vines and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically greenish-white and are followed by small, round berries that turn red when ripe.

This variety is valued for its unique flavor and is used in traditional cuisines and herbal remedies.
