Peperomia calderoniae

Peperomia calderoniae, commonly known as the Caldeonia peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves that are often slightly curled at the edges. The plant produces small, white, clustered flowers during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Peperomia calderoniae is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
