Pterolepis herincquiana

Pterolepis herincquiana, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of white, spherical flower heads that resemble small snowballs, blooming in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a leathery texture with a rough surface.

The species is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
