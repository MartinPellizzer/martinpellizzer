Phillyrea longifolia subsp. andreuana

Phillyrea longifolia subsp.

andreuana, commonly known as the Andalusian Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula. It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a leathery texture.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in axillary inflorescences. It is adapted to dry, rocky habitats and is often found in Mediterranean climates.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slightly different leaf morphology compared to the typical form of Phillyrea longifolia.
