Phaius hainanensis

Phaius hainanensis, commonly known as the Hainan orchid, is a tropical plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its large, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in wet, shaded environments, such as mountainous forests. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it produces clusters of pale yellow to greenish-white blooms.

Phaius hainanensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
