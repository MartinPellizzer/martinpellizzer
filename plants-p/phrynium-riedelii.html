Phrynium riedelii

Phrynium riedelii, commonly known as the Riedel's arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, usually appearing in shades of green or red.

Phrynium riedelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking foliage.
