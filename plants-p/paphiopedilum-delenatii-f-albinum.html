Paphiopedilum delenatii f. albinum

Paphiopedilum delenatii f. albinum, commonly known as the white-flowered slipper orchid, is a rare and highly prized hybrid within the Paphiopedilum genus.

It is characterized by its striking white petals and sepals, which contrast sharply with its deep green foliage. This orchid is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, shaded environments with high air circulation. It produces fragrant, slipper-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
