Pelargonium Papilionaceum

Pelargonium papilionaceum is a unique species of geranium native to southern Africa. It is known for its striking, butterfly-like flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and hardiness. Despite its charm, it remains relatively rare in cultivation, making it a sought-after addition to botanical collections.
