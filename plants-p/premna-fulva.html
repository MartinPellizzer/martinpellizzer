Premna fulva

Premna fulva, commonly known as the Chinese nettle or Indian nettle, is a perennial shrub native to Asia.

It is characterized by its opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by spherical, fleshy fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Premna fulva is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
