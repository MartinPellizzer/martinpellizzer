Piper eocenicum

Piper eocenicum, commonly known as the Eocene pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of tiny, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces small, peppery fruits that are similar in appearance to those of other pepper species. It is native to tropical regions and thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its historical significance and unique botanical features, it is of interest to botanists and plant collectors.
