Phymatodes stewartii

Phymatodes stewartii, commonly known as the stewartii phymatode, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its deep, extensive root system that can extend several meters underground, allowing it to survive in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, and it is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It plays a role in stabilizing soil and preventing erosion in its native habitat.
