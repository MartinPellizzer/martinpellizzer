Philodendron melloi

Philodendron melloi, commonly known as the Melloi Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are often variegated with light green and white patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by bright red berries, which are an important food source for wildlife. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Due to its striking foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and vertical landscapes.
