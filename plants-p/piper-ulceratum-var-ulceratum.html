Piper ulceratum var. ulceratum

Piper ulceratum var. ulceratum, commonly known as the ulcerated pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit and dark green, glossy leaves. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are followed by elongated, fleshy fruits that contain the aromatic seeds.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has a mild, peppery flavor.
