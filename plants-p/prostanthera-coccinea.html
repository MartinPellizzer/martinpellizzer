Prostanthera coccinea

Prostanthera coccinea, commonly known as the scarlet mint bush, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its vibrant red flowers, which are tubular and clustered in dense spikes. Its leaves are small, aromatic, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Prostanthera coccinea thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.
