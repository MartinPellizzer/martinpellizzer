Pulicaria odora

Pulicaria odora, commonly known as sweet everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, yellowish-green flowers clustered in dense, spherical heads. The plant has aromatic leaves that release a sweet, honey-like scent when crushed. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its resilience and ornamental value make it a valued addition to both garden landscapes and ecological habitats.
