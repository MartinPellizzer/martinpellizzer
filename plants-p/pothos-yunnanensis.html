Pothos yunnanensis

Pothos yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan pothos, is a climbing vine native to China.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically light green with a slightly variegated appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
