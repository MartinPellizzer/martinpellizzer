Polygala forojulensis subsp. carniolica

Polygala forojulensis subsp.

carniolica, commonly known as the Carniolan milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Europe. It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, and it produces small, bead-like fruits.
