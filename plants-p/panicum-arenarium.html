Panicum arenarium

Panicum arenarium, commonly known as sand panicgrass, is a low-growing grass species native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It thrives in sandy soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is often used for erosion control due to its extensive root system.

Its botanical features make it an important component of desert ecosystems.
