Pennisetum angolense var. laxispicatum

Pennisetum angolense var. laxispicatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It features slender, arching stems that produce dense clusters of feathery, silver-green inflorescences. The plant is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its foliage and flowering display add visual interest throughout the growing season.
