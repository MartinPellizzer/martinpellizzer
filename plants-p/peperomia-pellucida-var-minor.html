Peperomia pellucida var. minor

Peperomia pellucida var. minor, commonly known as the translucent peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically a glossy green, often with a waxy texture and a slightly translucent appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that are usually borne on short, erect spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate moisture.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
