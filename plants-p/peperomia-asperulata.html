Peperomia asperulata

Peperomia asperulata, commonly known as the roughleaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating, giving them a slightly textured appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, emerging from the leaf axils. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and ability to thrive in dry conditions.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for collectors and indoor gardeners seeking a striking yet easy-to-care-for plant.
