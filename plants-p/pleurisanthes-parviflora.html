Pleurisanthes parviflora

Pleurisanthes parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered pleurisanthes, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features compound leaves with serrated margins and produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the spring. The plant is characterized by its woody stems and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is not commonly found outside its native region.
