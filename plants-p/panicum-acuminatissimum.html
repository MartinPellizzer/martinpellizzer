Panicum acuminatissimum

Panicum acuminatissimum, commonly known as the narrowleaf panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, spikelet-based flowers. Its leaves are narrow and often have a bluish-green hue, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Panicum acuminatissimum is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
