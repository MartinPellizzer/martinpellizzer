Peperomia obovatilimba

Peperomia obovatilimba, commonly known as the broad-leaf peperomia, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a compact, evergreen perennial with heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or white, which bloom during the warmer months. It prefers indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ornamental foliage and low maintenance requirements contribute to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
