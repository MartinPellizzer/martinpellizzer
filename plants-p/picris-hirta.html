Picris hirta

Picris hirta, commonly known as goat's beard, is an annual flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and wastelands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

Picris hirta is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
