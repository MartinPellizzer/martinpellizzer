Panicum fiebrigii

Panicum fiebrigii, commonly known as fevergrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces fluffy, spike-like inflorescences that are often wind-pollinated. It is well adapted to dry conditions and can thrive in poor soil environments.

Its ornamental value is sometimes appreciated in landscaping due to its distinctive appearance.
