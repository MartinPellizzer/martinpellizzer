Piper jianfenglingense

Piper jianfenglingense, commonly known as the Hainan pepper vine, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to China, specifically the Hainan Island, where it grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with heart-shaped leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruits are berries that mature to a dark purple color, and it is often found in humid, shaded environments.

The plant has potential medicinal uses and is of interest to botanists due to its unique ecological niche.
