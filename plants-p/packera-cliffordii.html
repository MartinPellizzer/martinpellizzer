Packera cliffordii

Packera cliffordii, commonly known as clifford's fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and hairy foliage. This perennial herb typically grows in rocky or open habitats, such as meadows and slopes. Its yellow to orange flowers bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
