Plantago lanceolata var. sphaerostachya

Plantago lanceolata var. sphaerostachya, commonly known as the spherical-stalked plantain, is a variant of the common plantain.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and a distinctive, spherical inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is found in various parts of Europe and Asia. Its flowers are small and clustered, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds.

This variant is often used in traditional medicine and as a ground cover in ecological restoration projects.
