Poincianella pyramidalis var. diversifolia

Poincianella pyramidalis var. diversifolia, commonly known as the variegated flame tree, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves that often display vibrant variegation of green, white, and yellow. The plant produces showy, clustered inflorescences of bright orange or red flowers, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for warm climates.

Its rapid growth and ornamental value make it a favored plant in gardens and landscapes.
