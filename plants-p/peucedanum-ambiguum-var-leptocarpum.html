Peucedanum ambiguum var. leptocarpum

Peucedanum ambiguum var. leptocarpum, commonly known as the slender-fruited angelica, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic leaves and umbellate flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate climates and can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters.
