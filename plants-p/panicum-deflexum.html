Panicum deflexum

Panicum deflexum, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a member of the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit. The plant features slender, reddish stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets.

Panicum deflexum is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
