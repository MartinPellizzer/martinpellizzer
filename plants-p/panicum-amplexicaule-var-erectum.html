Panicum amplexicaule var. erectum

Panicum amplexicaule var. erectum, commonly known as the upright flatweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that can grow up to 60 cm in height, often branching near the base. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences composed of numerous pale green to golden bracts. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slightly hairy surface.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along the edges of water bodies.
