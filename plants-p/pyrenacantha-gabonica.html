Pyrenacantha gabonica

Pyrenacantha gabonica, commonly known as the African star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that can grow up to 3 meters tall, featuring aromatic, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, elliptical leaves and produces small, white to pale yellow flowers with a strong, sweet scent. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its persistent foliage and attractive floral display.
