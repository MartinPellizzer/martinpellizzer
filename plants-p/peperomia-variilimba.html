Peperomia variilimba

Peperomia variilimba, commonly known as the Variegated Peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance houseplant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink, adding visual interest to any indoor space. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, and it thrives in bright, indirect light with moderate humidity. Peperomia variilimba is well-suited for beginners due to its resilience and ability to tolerate a range of indoor conditions.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for those seeking an attractive, easy-to-care-for plant.
