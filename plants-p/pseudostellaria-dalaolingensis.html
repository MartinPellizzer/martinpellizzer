Pseudostellaria dalaolingensis

Pseudostellaria dalaolingensis, commonly known as the Dalaoling starwort, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and stream banks. Its leaves are lanceolate and arranged alternately along the stem.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
