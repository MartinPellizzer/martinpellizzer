Phyllanthus grandifolius var. genuinus

Phyllanthus grandifolius var. genuinus, commonly known as the large-leaf phyllanthus, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and southern Asia.

It features small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks.

The species is noted for its rapid growth and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
