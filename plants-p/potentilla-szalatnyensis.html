Potentilla szalatnyensis

Potentilla szalatnyensis, commonly known as the Szalatnyi cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are compound, with five leaflets, giving it the common name "cinquefoil.".

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and colorful blooms.
