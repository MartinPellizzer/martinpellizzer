Pennisetum thunbergii

Pennisetum thunbergii, commonly known as fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It features slender, arching stems that produce fluffy, feathery plumes, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 4 feet and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a blue-green hue that adds visual interest to gardens.

Pennisetum thunbergii is valued for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
