Phalaenopsis taenialis

Phalaenopsis taenialis, commonly known as the slender moth orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves and clusters of small, pale green or white flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees or other structures in its natural habitat. Its flowers often have a subtle fragrance and bloom throughout the year under proper care.

This orchid is popular among growers for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
