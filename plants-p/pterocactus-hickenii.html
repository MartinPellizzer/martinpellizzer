Pterocactus hickenii

Pterocactus hickenii, commonly known as the Hicken's pterocactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that are often covered in spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sunlight.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
