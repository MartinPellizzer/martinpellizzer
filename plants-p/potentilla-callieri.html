Potentilla callieri

Potentilla callieri, commonly known as the Chinese cinquefoil, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves with five to seven leaflets. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer, attracting pollinators such as bees. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
