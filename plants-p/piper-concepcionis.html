Piper concepcionis

Piper concepcionis, commonly known as the Concepcionis pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its slender, vine-like growth habit and small, dark green leaves. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into small, round berries.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and potential medicinal properties.
