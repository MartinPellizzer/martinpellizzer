Pterocelastrus dregeanus

Pterocelastrus dregeanus, commonly known as the African moonseed, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Celastraceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by spherical, woody fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland forested areas.

The plant is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
