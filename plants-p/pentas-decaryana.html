Pentas decaryana

Pentas decaryana, commonly known as the flame flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its vibrant, five-petaled flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and lance-shaped with a smooth texture.

Pentas decaryana thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
