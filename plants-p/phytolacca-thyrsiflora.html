Phytolacca thyrsiflora

Phytolacca thyrsiflora, commonly known as the Chinese phytolacca, is a herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, branched stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces red, fleshy berries that are toxic to humans and animals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.
