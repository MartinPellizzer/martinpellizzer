Premna Obtusifolia Var. Gaudichaudii

Premna obtusifolia var. gaudichaudii is a lesser-known yet fascinating plant species belonging to the Lamiaceae family. Native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. This variant is distinguished by its unique leaf shape and dense, fragrant foliage. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated in horticultural circles. Understanding its ecology and cultivation can enhance its recognition among plant enthusiasts.
