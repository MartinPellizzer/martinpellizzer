Polygala font-queri

Polygala font-queri, commonly known as the Font-Queri milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its slender, often branched stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowers are usually pale blue or purple, and it produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Polygala font-queri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
