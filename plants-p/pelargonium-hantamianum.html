Pelargonium hantamianum

Pelargonium hantamianum, commonly known as the Hantam geranium, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous plant characterized by its vibrant pink to red flowers and serrated leaves. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. Its foliage is often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Pelargonium hantamianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for garden decoration.
