Pedicularis × gillotana

It belongs to the family Orobanchaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant is hemiparasitic, meaning it partially relies on host plants for nutrients while also performing photosynthesis. Its unique floral structure and vibrant color make it a popular subject in both horticulture and botanical studies.
