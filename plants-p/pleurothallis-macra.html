Pleurothallis macra

Pleurothallis macra, commonly known as the Macra orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often pendulous inflorescences. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees and drawing moisture and nutrients from the air. Its flowers are usually small, with intricate patterns and a range of colors including white, pink, and purple.

Pleurothallis macra is valued by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
