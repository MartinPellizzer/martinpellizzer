Pennisetum simeonis var. purpureum

Pennisetum simeonis var. purpureum, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender stalks that produce plume-like inflorescences in shades of purple and silver. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for perennial gardens.

Its striking appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for landscaping.
