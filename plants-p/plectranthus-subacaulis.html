Plectranthus subacaulis

Plectranthus subacaulis, commonly known as the silver plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray, hairy leaves and compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Plectranthus subacaulis is also valued for its aromatic foliage and is often used in herbal remedies.
