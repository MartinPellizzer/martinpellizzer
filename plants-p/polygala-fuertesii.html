Polygala fuertesii

Polygala fuertesii, commonly known as the Fuertes' milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant features slender stems and lance-shaped leaves, which are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to the arid conditions of its native habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, Polygala fuertesii is considered a rare and endangered species.
