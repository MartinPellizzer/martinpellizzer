Pilea howelliana var. longipedunculata

Pilea howelliana var. longipedunculata, commonly known as the long pedunculate pilea, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves with prominent veins, and its flowering stems bear clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers. The plant produces tiny, round, greenish-brown fruits that are edible but not commonly consumed.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
