Phormium tenax var. colensoi

Phormium tenax var. colensoi, commonly known as Colenso's flax, is a striking perennial plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 2 meters in length, often with a reddish or purple hue at the base. The plant produces striking spiky inflorescences of purple or red flowers in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value and resilience make it a favored plant in horticulture.
