Pentzia pinnatisecta

Pentzia pinnatisecta, commonly known as the narrow-leafed restharrow, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its finely dissected, pinnatisected leaves that give it a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense, terminal clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental plant in both natural and cultivated settings.
