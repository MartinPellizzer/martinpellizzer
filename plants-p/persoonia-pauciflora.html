Persoonia pauciflora

Persoonia pauciflora, commonly known as the sparse-flowered pear.

It is a shrub native to Western Australia, characterized by its small, often glandular leaves and showy, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowers are borne in clusters and are visited by various pollinators, including bees and birds.

Persoonia pauciflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
