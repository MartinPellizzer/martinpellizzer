Pleurothallis dunstervillei

Pleurothallis dunstervillei, commonly known as the Dunsterville's orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually small, with intricate patterns and colors ranging from white to pale yellow.

Pleurothallis dunstervillei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts of rare orchids.
