Peperomia insueta

Peperomia insueta, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, often adorned with silver or white veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color, which are not particularly showy. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia insueta is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and ornamental foliage.
