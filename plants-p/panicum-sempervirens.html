Panicum sempervirens

Panicum sempervirens, commonly known as purple lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and striking purple-tinted foliage. The plant produces fluffy, silver-gray seed heads that add visual interest throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its drought tolerance and ability to form dense mats make it useful for erosion control and as a low-maintenance landscape plant.
