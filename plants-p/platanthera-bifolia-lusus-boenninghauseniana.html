Platanthera bifolia lusus boenninghauseniana

Platanthera bifolia lusus boenninghauseniana, commonly known as the Boenninghausen's fairy orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to specific regions of Europe.

The plant is notable for its ephemeral nature, often appearing only briefly before vanishing, which contributes to its mystique.
