Pittosporum malaxanii

Pittosporum malaxanii, commonly known as the New Zealand pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are oval in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, orange-red berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland forests.

Pittosporum malaxanii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in New Zealand's native ecosystems.
