Polygala welwitschii var. ovatifolia

Polygala welwitschii var. ovatifolia, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, bead-like fruits that contain seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and full sun.
