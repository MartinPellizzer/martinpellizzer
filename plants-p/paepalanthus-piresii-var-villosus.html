Paepalanthus piresii var. villosus

Paepalanthus piresii var. villosus, commonly known as the hairy papyrus plant, is a unique species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges, and is characterized by its grass-like appearance. The plant features dense, fibrous rhizomes and produces small, clustered inflorescences. Its hairy leaves and compact growth habit make it distinct within its genus.
