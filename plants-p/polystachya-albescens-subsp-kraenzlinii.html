Polystachya albescens subsp. kraenzlinii

Polystachya albescens subsp.

kraenzlinii, commonly known as the white-flowered bamboo orchid, is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae. It is native to the high-altitude regions of Central and South America, particularly in the Andes.

This species is characterized by its slender, bamboo-like stems and clusters of white or pale green flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or forested habitats and thrives in cool, humid conditions.

It is a rare and seldom cultivated orchid, valued for its unique appearance and ecological significance.
