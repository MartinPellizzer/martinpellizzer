Peperomia muelleri

Peperomia muelleri, commonly known as the Mueller's pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color.

Peperomia muelleri is a popular houseplant due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive foliage.
