Persicaria donii

Persicaria donii, commonly known as the Himalayan knotweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

The plant features upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences, typically appearing in shades of pink or red.

Persicaria donii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
