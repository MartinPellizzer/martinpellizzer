Phalaenopsis lobbii f. flavilabia

Phalaenopsis lobbii f. flavilabia, commonly known as the Yellow-lipped Phalaenopsis, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, yellowish-white flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia, including regions like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. It is a sympodial orchid, meaning it grows in a branching pattern, and produces clusters of fragrant, long-lasting blooms.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
