Pittosporum pauciflorum

Pittosporum pauciflorum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dark green, narrow leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.

Pittosporum pauciflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
