Perilla ocymoides f. citriodora

Perilla ocymoides f. citriodora, commonly known as lemon basil, is a fragrant herb native to Southeast Asia.

It is a variant of the perilla plant, characterized by its distinct lemon-like aroma and flavor. The leaves are oval-shaped with a slightly serrated edge and a vibrant green color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often used in both culinary and medicinal applications.

The plant produces small white or pale purple flowers and is valued for its aromatic properties.
