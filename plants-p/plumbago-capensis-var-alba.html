Plumbago capensis var. alba

Plumbago capensis var. alba, commonly known as silver leaf plumbago, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking white, tubular flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has finely divided, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from both its foliage and its long-lasting, showy blooms.
