Paphiopedilum venustum f. pardinum

Paphiopedilum venustum f. pardinum, commonly known as the dark-leaved vanda orchid, is a hybrid orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its dark green to almost black foliage, which gives it a striking appearance. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with a unique lip structure typical of the Paphiopedilum genus. It thrives in humid, well-drained environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in both indoor and greenhouse settings.
