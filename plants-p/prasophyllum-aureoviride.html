Prasophyllum aureoviride

Prasophyllum aureoviride, commonly known as the yellow-green leek orchid, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is native to Australia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces a tall flowering stem bearing greenish-yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to grow in sandy or gravelly soils, often in open habitats such as heathlands or woodlands.

It is a rare and threatened species, requiring specific environmental conditions for successful growth and reproduction.
