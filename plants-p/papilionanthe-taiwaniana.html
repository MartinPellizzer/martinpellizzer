Papilionanthe taiwaniana

Papilionanthe taiwaniana, commonly known as the Taiwan orchid, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, pendulous flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip that is often a different color.

Papilionanthe taiwaniana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions worldwide.
