Phoebe longepetiolata

Phoebe longepetiolata, commonly known as the long-petioled phoebe, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, where it typically grows in humid, shaded environments. Phoebe longepetiolata produces small, fragrant flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, dark berries that are an important food source for local wildlife.
