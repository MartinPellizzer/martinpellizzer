Poa kurilensis

Poa kurilensis, commonly known as the Kuril grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the islands of the Kuril archipelago and surrounding regions in the Pacific Ocean. The plant is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, spikelet-based flowers that are typically green to brown in color.

Poa kurilensis is adapted to cool, moist environments and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.
