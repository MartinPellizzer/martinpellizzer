Panicum haenkeanum

Panicum haenkeanum, commonly known as the scarlet panicgrass, is a species of grass native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and bright red or orange plumes that appear in late summer. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental use in gardens. Its flowering period typically occurs from August to October, adding vibrant color to landscapes during the fall season.

Panicum haenkeanum is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators such as birds and insects.
