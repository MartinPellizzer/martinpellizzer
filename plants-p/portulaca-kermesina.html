Portulaca kermesina

Portulaca kermesina, commonly known as Crimson Flower or Bladder Campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb with fleshy, succulent leaves and vibrant red or pink flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its flowers bloom throughout the summer, adding a splash of color to gardens.

Portulaca kermesina is also known for its ability to withstand drought conditions due to its succulent nature.
