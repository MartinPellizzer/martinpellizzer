Piper posusanum

Piper posusanum, commonly known as the Posusanum pepper or mountain pepper, is a flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the rainforests of Indonesia, particularly in the Maluku Islands. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and has dark green, glossy leaves. It produces small, peppery fruits that are used in local traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it an important species in the study of Piperaceae diversity in tropical regions.
