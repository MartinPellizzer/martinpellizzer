Petunia linearis

Petunia linearis, commonly known as the slender petunia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and clusters of trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant is typically herbaceous, with stems that are often hairy and upright. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Petunia linearis is also known for its fragrance and is often used in ornamental gardening.
