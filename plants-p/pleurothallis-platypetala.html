Pleurothallis platypetala

Pleurothallis platypetala, commonly known as the flat-petaled pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, delicate flowers with a unique coloration pattern. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the understory of rainforests. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a distinctive flat petal structure that sets it apart from other orchid species.

Pleurothallis platypetala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts due to its striking floral display.
