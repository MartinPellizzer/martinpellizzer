Plectronia dewevrei

Plectronia dewevrei, commonly known as the African moonflower, is a striking member of the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its large, white, fragrant flowers that open at night. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain numerous seeds. Its leaves are broad and ovate, with a slightly hairy texture.

Plectronia dewevrei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and nocturnal beauty.
