Pilosella hirtovasconica

Pilosella hirtovasconica, commonly known as the hairy pearly everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

Pilosella hirtovasconica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
