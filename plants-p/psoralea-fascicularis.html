Psoralea fascicularis

Psoralea fascicularis, commonly known as the clustered psoralea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are trifoliate, and the flowers are usually pale yellow or white.
