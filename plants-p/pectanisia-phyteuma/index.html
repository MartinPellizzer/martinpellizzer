Pectanisia Phyteuma

Pectanisia phyteuma is a rare and striking flowering plant native to specific regions of southern Africa. It is known for its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. This species thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and often grows in coastal or mountainous areas. Its unique appearance makes it a popular subject among botanists and plant enthusiasts. Due to habitat loss, Pectanisia phyteuma is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
