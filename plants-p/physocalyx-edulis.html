Physocalyx edulis

Physocalyx edulis, commonly known as the edible nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant features showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are alternate and ovate, with a slightly hairy texture.
