Peliosanthes oksanae

Peliosanthes oksanae, commonly known as the Oksana's cycad, is a rare and unique member of the Cycadaceae family.

It is native to the island of Socotra, where it thrives in arid and rocky habitats. The plant features a stout, unbranched stem covered in a fibrous, spongy layer. Its leaves are pinnate, with long, narrow leaflets that give it a distinctive appearance.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
