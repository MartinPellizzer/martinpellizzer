Pandaca ciliata var. lanceolata

Pandaca ciliata var. lanceolata, commonly known as the slender-fruited pandaca, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lanceolate leaves, which are elongated and tapering at both ends. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for horticultural study and conservation efforts.
