Polygonum tenuifolium

Polygonum tenuifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaf smartweed, is a herbaceous annual plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist soils along stream banks, wetlands, and disturbed areas. The plant features alternate, lance-shaped leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its small, greenish-white flowers are arranged in dense clusters at the tips of the stems.

It is often used in traditional medicine and as a forage plant for livestock.
