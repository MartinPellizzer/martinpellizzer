Pilea symmeria var. salwinensis

Pilea symmeria var. salwinensis, commonly known as the Salwin pilea, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features rounded, fleshy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
