Pilea serratifolia

Pilea serratifolia, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a striking evergreen shrub native to China.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves with serrated edges that give it its scientific name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and moderate watering.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental purposes.
