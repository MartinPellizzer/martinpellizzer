Polystichum mollissimum var. laciniatum

Polystichum mollissimum var. laciniatum, commonly known as the velvet fern, is a species of fern characterized by its soft, velvety fronds.

It is native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in countries like Argentina and Chile. The plant features compound leaves that are finely divided, giving it a lacy appearance. Its fronds are typically dark green and densely covered with fine hairs, contributing to its unique texture.

This fern thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded, forested areas.
