Pinelia pumilio

Pinelia pumilio, commonly known as the dwarf pinel.

It is a small, herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of South America. The plant features delicate, hairy leaves and produces small, white flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.

Due to its limited range and habitat specificity, Pinelia pumilio is considered a rare and endangered species.
