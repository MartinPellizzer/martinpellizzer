Panicum flavidum var. jubiflorum

Panicum flavidum var. jubiflorum, commonly known as yellow panicgrass, is a flowering grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and clusters of small, yellowish-green to golden spikelets. The plant produces fluffy, wind-dispersed seeds, which contribute to its ability to spread in open, disturbed habitats. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in grasslands and along roadsides.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its striking autumn coloration, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
