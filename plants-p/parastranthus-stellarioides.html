Parastranthus stellarioides

Parastranthus stellarioides, commonly known as the starry weed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

The flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters that give the plant its "starry" appearance.
