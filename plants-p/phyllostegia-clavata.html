Phyllostegia clavata

Phyllostegia clavata, commonly known as the clustered strobilanthes, is a species of flowering plant in the mint family, Lamiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color.
