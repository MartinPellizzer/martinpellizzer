Phalaenopsis schilleriana f. purpurea

Phalaenopsis schilleriana f. purpurea, commonly known as the Purple Phalaenopsis, is a hybrid orchid prized for its striking violet and white floral display.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia, where it thrives in tropical climates. The plant features broad, dark green lance-shaped leaves and produces long, pendulous flower spikes. Its flowers bloom in a range of colors, including shades of purple, pink, and white, making it a popular choice for ornamental purposes.

This orchid requires bright, indirect light and consistent moisture to flourish.
