Pseudosolisia

Pseudosolisia, commonly known as the ghost orchid, is a rare and enigmatic flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

Pseudosolisia thrives in tropical and subtropical regions, often in areas with high humidity and specific soil conditions.
