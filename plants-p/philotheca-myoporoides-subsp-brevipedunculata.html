Philotheca myoporoides subsp. brevipedunculata

Philotheca myoporoides subsp.

brevipedunculata, commonly known as the narrow-peduncled myopor, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia. It features small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring, typically arranged in dense clusters.

The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal heathlands.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ornamental species within the Rutaceae family.
