Phreatia repens

Phreatia repens, commonly known as the climbing orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fibrous root system and its ability to climb using its aerial roots. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow or greenish-white. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in lowland rainforests.

Phreatia repens is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
