Pelargonium × myrtillum

Pelargonium × myrtillum, commonly known as the myrtle geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its fragrant, small, pink to white flowers and dark green, glossy leaves. The plant is native to South Africa and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden beds and containers.

Its compact growth habit and long blooming period make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
