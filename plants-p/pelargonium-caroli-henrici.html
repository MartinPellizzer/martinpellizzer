Pelargonium caroli-henrici

Pelargonium caroli-henrici, commonly known as the Caroli-Henrici geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The leaves are typically rounded or heart-shaped, with a serrated edge, and the plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
