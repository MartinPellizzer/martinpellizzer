Prunus domestica var. culinaria

Prunus domestica var. culinaria, commonly known as the dwarf European plum, is a compact variety of the European plum tree.

It is characterized by its small, rounded growth habit and produces small, sweet fruits that are typically harvested in late summer. The plant has dark green, lobed leaves and produces fragrant white flowers in early spring. It is well-suited for small gardens due to its size and ease of cultivation.

This variety is often used in both ornamental and culinary applications.
