Paullinia eriantha f. mollis

Paullinia eriantha f. mollis, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a variant of the tamarind tree native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its smooth, velvety leaves and distinctive yellow flowers. The plant produces edible, sour fruits that are used in traditional cuisine and herbal remedies. It thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and agricultural contexts.
