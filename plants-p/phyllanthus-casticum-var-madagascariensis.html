Phyllanthus casticum var. madagascariensis

Phyllanthus casticum var. madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar spurge, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is a shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant has small, opposite leaves and produces yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release tiny seeds when mature.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
