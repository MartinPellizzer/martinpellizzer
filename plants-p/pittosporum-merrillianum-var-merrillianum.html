Pittosporum merrillianum var. merrillianum

Pittosporum merrillianum var. merrillianum, commonly known as the Merril's pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage, which consists of glossy, dark green leaves with a leathery texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are typically clustered in terminal umbels. These flowers are followed by small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
