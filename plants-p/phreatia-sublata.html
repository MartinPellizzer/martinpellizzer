Phreatia sublata

Phreatia sublata, commonly known as the slender phreatia, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and elongated, narrow leaves. The plant produces large, showy inflorescences with pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. It typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often near water sources.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
