Persicaria krugii

Persicaria krugii, commonly known as Krug's smartweed, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It typically grows in moist or wet habitats such as marshes, swamps, and stream banks. The plant features erect, hairy stems that can reach up to 1 meter in height. Its leaves are lance-shaped, with a rough texture and a prominent central vein.

The small, pink to reddish-purple flowers appear in dense clusters at the top of the plant, and they bloom from late summer into early autumn.
