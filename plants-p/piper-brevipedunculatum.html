Piper brevipedunculatum

Piper brevipedunculatum, commonly known as the short-stalked pepper plant, is a tropical vine native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. Its leaves are usually ovate with a rough texture and emit a mild peppery scent.

The fruit is a small, elongated berry that turns from green to red when mature.
