Pelargonium × atrosanguineum

Pelargonium × atrosanguineum, commonly known as the dark red geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, dark red to almost black flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with rounded, velvety leaves that have a distinctive gray-green hue. Native to South Africa, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and compact growth habit.
