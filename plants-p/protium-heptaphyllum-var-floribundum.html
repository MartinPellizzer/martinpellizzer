Protium heptaphyllum var. floribundum

Protium heptaphyllum var. floribundum, commonly known as the Florida holly or sweet bay.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to the southeastern United States, particularly Florida and the Caribbean. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement and often have a waxy texture. It produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters, which are followed by edible, fleshy berries.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
