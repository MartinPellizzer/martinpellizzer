Pariti hastatum

Pariti hastatum, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and slightly glossy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale yellow. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Its rapid growth and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for water gardens and bog environments.
