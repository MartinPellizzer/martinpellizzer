Polygala volkensii

Polygala volkensii, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and opposite, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

The plant is notable for its ecological importance, providing nectar for various pollinators.
