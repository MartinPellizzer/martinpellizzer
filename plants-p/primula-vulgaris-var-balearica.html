Primula vulgaris var. balearica

Primula vulgaris var. balearica, commonly known as the Balearic primrose, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Balearic Islands.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has a rosette of broad, hairy leaves that help it retain moisture in its native habitat. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodlands and rocky areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
