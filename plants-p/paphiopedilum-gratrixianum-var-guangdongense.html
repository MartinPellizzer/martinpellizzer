Paphiopedilum gratrixianum var. guangdongense

Paphiopedilum gratrixianum var. guangdongense, commonly known as the Guangdong slipper orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique slipper-shaped pouch, a feature typical of the Paphiopedilum genus. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-draining soil, often found in mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white with intricate patterns, and it blooms once or twice a year.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a valuable and protected species in its native range.
