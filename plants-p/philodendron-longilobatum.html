Philodendron longilobatum

Philodendron longilobatum, commonly known as the long-lobed philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves with distinctive elongated lobes along the edges. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by green, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity.

Philodendron longilobatum is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
