Palaua

Palaua, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a flowering plant native to various regions in Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, dark purple berries that are often used in traditional medicine. Its flowers are typically white or pale blue, and it thrives in warm, tropical climates.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
