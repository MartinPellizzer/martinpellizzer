Peucedanum latifolium

Peucedanum latifolium, commonly known as broadleaf angelica, is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate flowers.

It is often cultivated for its medicinal properties, with the roots and leaves used in traditional remedies.
