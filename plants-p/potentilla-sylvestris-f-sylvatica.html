Potentilla sylvestris f. sylvatica

Potentilla sylvestris f. sylvatica, commonly known as the woodland cinquefoil, is a compact, flowering shrub native to Europe.

It features small, dark green leaves and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodland habitats, preferring well-drained soil. Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant blooms and dense foliage, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

It is also valued for its role in supporting local wildlife and enhancing biodiversity in natural landscapes.
