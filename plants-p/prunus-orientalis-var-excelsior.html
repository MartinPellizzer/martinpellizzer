Prunus orientalis var. excelsior

Prunus orientalis var. excelsior, commonly known as the Persian lilac, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pale lilac-colored flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has smooth, greyish bark and dark green, oval-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its showy flowers and attractive form.
