Peperomia nivalis f. diminuta

Peperomia nivalis f. diminuta, commonly known as the Snowy Peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a velvety texture and small, clustered white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often cultivated as a decorative foliage plant. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
