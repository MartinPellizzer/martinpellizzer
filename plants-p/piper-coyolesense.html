Piper coyolesense

Piper coyolesense, commonly known as Coyol tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to Central America and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, greenish flowers. The plant produces small, spherical berries that are typically green when immature and turn red when mature. It is often found in humid, lowland tropical regions and thrives in shaded environments.

The botanical features of Piper coyolesense make it an important component of the understory vegetation in its native habitat.
