Paphiopedilum × iago

Paphiopedilum × iago, commonly known as the Iago phalaenopsis.

This hybrid orchid is a cross between Paphiopedilum micranthum and Paphiopedilum delenatii, resulting in a plant with unique floral characteristics. It features large, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink with intricate patterns resembling butterflies. The plant has thick, fleshy roots adapted for epiphytic growth, and its leaves are broad and leathery.

It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
