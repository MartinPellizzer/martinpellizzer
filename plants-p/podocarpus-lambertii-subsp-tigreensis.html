Podocarpus lambertii subsp. tigreensis

Podocarpus lambertii subsp.

tigreensis, commonly known as the Tigrean Podocarpus, is a member of the Podocarpaceae family and is native to Ethiopia. It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and aromatic leaves.

The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain its seeds, typical of the coniferous family. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in montane forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
