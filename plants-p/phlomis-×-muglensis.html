Phlomis × muglensis

Phlomis × muglensis, commonly known as the hybrid giant mullein, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant features large, gray-green, velvety leaves with a distinctive fuzzy texture. Its flowers are typically pink to purple, forming dense spikes at the top of the stems.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy and ornamental plant in dry, rocky environments.
