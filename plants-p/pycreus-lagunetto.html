Pycreus lagunetto

Pycreus lagunetto, commonly known as the Lagunetto fern, is a rare and delicate species native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its intricate fronds, which are typically divided into fine, lacy segments. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical or subtropical forests. Its reproductive structures are small and clustered, contributing to its limited distribution.

Due to habitat loss, Pycreus lagunetto is considered vulnerable and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
