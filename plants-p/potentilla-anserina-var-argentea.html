Potentilla anserina var. argentea

Potentilla anserina var. argentea, commonly known as silver cinquefoil, is a low-growing perennial shrub native to temperate regions of Europe.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has hairy, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance and helps reflect sunlight. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or alpine environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
