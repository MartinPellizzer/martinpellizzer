Polygonum compactum

Polygonum compactum, commonly known as the compact knotweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in small, ovate leaves that are often reddish in color. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and are usually greenish-white in hue.

Polygonum compactum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
