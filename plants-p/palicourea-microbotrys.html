Palicourea microbotrys

Palicourea microbotrys, commonly known as the pink velvet leaf.

It is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of South America. The plant is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, with a distinctive pinkish hue.

Palicourea microbotrys is valued in traditional medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
