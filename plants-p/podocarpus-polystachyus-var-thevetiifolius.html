Podocarpus polystachyus var. thevetiifolius

Podocarpus polystachyus var. thevetiifolius, commonly known as the Indian teak or thevetia.

This evergreen tree is native to the tropical regions of India and Sri Lanka. It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
