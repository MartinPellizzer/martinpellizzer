Pleomele cylindrica

Pleomele cylindrica, commonly known as the slender ginger plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features upright, cylindrical stems that are often covered in a waxy, yellowish to reddish bark. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers, followed by bright orange or red berries. Its leaves are broad, elliptical, and have a smooth, waxy texture.

Pleomele cylindrica is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded, humid environments.
