Piper calvarii

Piper calvarii, commonly known as the Calvarii pepper or Calvarii's pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly in countries like Colombia and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. Its small, greenish-white flowers are followed by the production of small, peppercorn-like fruits.

Piper calvarii is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
