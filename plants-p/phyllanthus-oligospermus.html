Phyllanthus oligospermus

Phyllanthus oligospermus, commonly known as the African date palm, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by fleshy, orange berries. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in local medicine.
