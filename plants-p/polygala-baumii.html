Polygala baumii

Polygala baumii, commonly known as the Baumann's milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features slender, branching stems with small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Polygala baumii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
