Pilosella densiflora subsp. pseudomagyariciformis

Pilosella densiflora subsp.

pseudomagyariciformis, commonly known as the denseflower pilosella, is a flowering plant native to Central and Eastern Europe. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and hairy foliage.

The subspecies is distinguished by its dense, compact inflorescences and finely pubescent leaves. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

The plant produces small white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.
