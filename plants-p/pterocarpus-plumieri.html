Pterocarpus plumieri

Pterocarpus plumieri, commonly known as the Java ebony, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, dense wood that is highly valued for its strength and fine grain. The plant has compound leaves with trifoliate or pinnate arrangements, and its flowers are small and yellowish-green. It produces long, slender pods containing flat, winged seeds.

The tree thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland forests.
