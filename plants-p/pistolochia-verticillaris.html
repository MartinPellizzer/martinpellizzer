Pistolochia verticillaris

Pistolochia verticillaris, commonly known as the spiralleaf spurge, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is characterized by its helical arrangement of leaves, which gives it a unique and striking appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats, including roadsides and open woodlands.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
