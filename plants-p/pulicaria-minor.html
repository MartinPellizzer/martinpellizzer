Pulicaria minor

Pulicaria minor, commonly known as lesser groundsel, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically features small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has hairy, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine, white pubescence. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open fields.

Pulicaria minor is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
