Polypodium triste

Polypodium triste, commonly known as the weeping fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features a distinctive, cascading frond structure that gives it its common name. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with slender, dark green leaves that are finely divided. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Polypodium triste is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
