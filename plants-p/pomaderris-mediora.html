Pomaderris mediora

Pomaderris mediora, commonly known as the Narrow-leaf Pomaderris, is a small to medium-sized shrub native to eastern Australia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry to moist sclerophyllous forests and heathlands, preferring well-drained soils. The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting native wildlife.
