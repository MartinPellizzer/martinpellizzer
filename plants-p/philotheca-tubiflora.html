Philotheca tubiflora

Philotheca tubiflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved mountain pepper, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments within temperate rainforests. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are borne on short pedicels. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly serrated margin.

Philotheca tubiflora is valued for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
