Pachiloma verticillata

Pachiloma verticillata, commonly known as the spiral leaf plant, is a striking member of the Araceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its foliage is typically dark green with a glossy appearance, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers clustered in an inflorescence.

This ornamental plant is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care in indoor settings.
