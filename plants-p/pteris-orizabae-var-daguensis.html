Pteris orizabae var. daguensis

Pteris orizabae var. daguensis, commonly known as the Daguensian water fern, is a variant of the Orizaba water fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial fern characterized by its delicate, frond-like structures that resemble small, feathered leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or near water bodies. Its fronds are usually bipinnate, with a finely divided structure that enhances its ornamental appeal.

This variety is valued in horticulture for its aesthetic qualities and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
