Peperomia blanda var. pseudodindygulensis

Peperomia blanda var. pseudodindygulensis, commonly known as the Bornean peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It features dense, glossy, ovate leaves that are typically a deep green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually white or pale green. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and sought-after specimen among collectors.
