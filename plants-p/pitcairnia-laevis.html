Pitcairnia laevis

Pitcairnia laevis, commonly known as the smooth pitcairnia, is a species of bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. The plant produces striking inflorescences with bright red flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Pitcairnia laevis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
