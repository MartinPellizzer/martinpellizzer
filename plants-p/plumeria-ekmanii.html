Plumeria ekmanii

Plumeria ekmanii, commonly known as the White Plumeria, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that produces fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are often hairy on the underside. Its bark is smooth and light-colored, and it is commonly cultivated for ornamental purposes.

The flowers are typically borne in clusters and bloom during the rainy season.
