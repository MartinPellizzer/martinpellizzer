Pyrethrum pallidum f. xanthoglossum

Pyrethrum pallidum f. xanthoglossum, commonly known as the yellow-lipped pyrethrum, is a botanical variant of the pyrethrum genus.

It is characterized by its pale yellow flowers with a distinct yellow lip, which sets it apart from other pyrethrum species. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils with full sunlight. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and has mild insect-repelling properties.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to gardens and botanical collections.
