Plectranthus parviflorus var. major

Plectranthus parviflorus var. major, commonly known as the Cape rosemary, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features aromatic, gray-green leaves that are often used in traditional medicine and as a culinary herb. The plant produces small, pale purple flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a subtle ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its versatility and hardy nature make it a popular choice for both ornamental and functional gardening.
