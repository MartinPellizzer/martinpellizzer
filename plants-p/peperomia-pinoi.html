Peperomia pinoi

Peperomia pinoi, commonly known as the Pinoi Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features thick, waxy, and often variegated leaves that are lance-shaped and have a distinctive reddish or bronze edge. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, clustered in spikes near the base of the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and minimal care requirements, it is well-suited for beginners and space-limited environments.
