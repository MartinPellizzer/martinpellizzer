Potentilla tugitakensis

Potentilla tugitakensis, commonly known as the Tugitankens rose.

This shrub is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, where it thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense foliage, making it suitable for ornamental use in gardens.

Its hardy nature allows it to withstand harsh environmental conditions, including cold temperatures and drought.
