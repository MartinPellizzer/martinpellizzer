Physospermum angelicifolium

Physospermum angelicifolium, commonly known as angel's leaf, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that resemble those of angelica, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and medicinal properties, it is of interest to botanists and herbalists alike.
