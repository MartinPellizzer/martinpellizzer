Protium neglectum var. sessiliflorum

Protium neglectum var. sessiliflorum, commonly known as the sessile-flower protium, is a member of the Burseraceae family.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. Its flowers are small, clustered, and typically sessile, giving it its specific epithet.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds, contributing to its role in local ecosystems.
