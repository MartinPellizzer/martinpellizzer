Phillyrea longifolia subsp. grandifolia

Phillyrea longifolia subsp.

grandifolia, commonly known as the largeflower Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features narrow, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy.

The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring. Its fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to coastal and rocky environments.
