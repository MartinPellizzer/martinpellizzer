Psychotria viburnoides

Psychotria viburnoides, commonly known as the velvetleaf psychotria, is a tropical shrub belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of pubescence. Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
