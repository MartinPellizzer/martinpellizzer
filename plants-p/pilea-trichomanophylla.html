Pilea trichomanophylla

Pilea trichomanophylla, commonly known as the silver pilea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery underside, which give it its common name. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are often hidden beneath the leaves.

Pilea trichomanophylla is a popular houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
