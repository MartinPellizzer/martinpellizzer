Peperomia ephemera

Peperomia ephemera, commonly known as the transient peperomia, is a unique species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and glossy, ovate leaves that are typically a vibrant green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, which bloom during the warmer months. Peperomia ephemera is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Its short flowering period and delicate appearance make it a rare and sought-after addition to botanical collections.
