Prunus mume splendens-plena

Prunus mume splendens-plena, commonly known as the double-flowered Chinese plum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is prized for its showy, double blooms that appear in early spring before the leaves emerge. The flowers are typically white or pink, with numerous petals that give it a lush, ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, edible fruits known as mume or Chinese plums, which are used in culinary and medicinal applications.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.
