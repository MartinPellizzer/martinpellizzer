Picris angustifolia subsp. merxmuelleri

Picris angustifolia subsp.

merxmuelleri, commonly known as narrowleaf picris, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and aromatic foliage.
