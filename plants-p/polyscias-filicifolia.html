Polyscias filicifolia

Polyscias filicifolia, commonly known as the fern-leaf polycarpic, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features compound leaves that resemble ferns, with long, slender leaflets arranged in a feathery pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its foliage is valued for its aesthetic appeal and its ability to thrive in a variety of indoor and outdoor settings.
