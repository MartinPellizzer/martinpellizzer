Phyllanthus galeottianus

Phyllanthus galeottianus, commonly known as the African spurge, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant features simple, opposite, and often hairy leaves that are elliptical in shape. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, and its fruits are capsule-like structures containing several seeds.

This species is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
