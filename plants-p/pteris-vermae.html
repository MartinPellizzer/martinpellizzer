Pteris vermae

Pteris vermae, commonly known as the Verma's water fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Pteridaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The fronds are bioluminescent under certain conditions, making it a unique and visually striking plant.
