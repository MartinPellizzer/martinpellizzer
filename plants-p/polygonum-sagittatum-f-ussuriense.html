Polygonum sagittatum f. ussuriense

Polygonum sagittatum f. ussuriense, commonly known as the Ussuri arrowhead, is a variant of the arrowhead plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant red or purple flowers that bloom in summer. This plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as stream banks and wetlands. It has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread vegetatively.

The foliage and flowers are often used in traditional herbal medicine for their purported health benefits.
