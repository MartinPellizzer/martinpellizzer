Poeciloneuron indicum

Poeciloneuron indicum, commonly known as the Indian orchid.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia. This plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees without parasitizing them. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors including white, yellow, and pink.

The plant thrives in humid environments with partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
