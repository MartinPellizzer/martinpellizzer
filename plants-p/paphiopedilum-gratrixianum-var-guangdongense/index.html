Paphiopedilum Gratrixianum Var. Guangdongense

Paphiopedilum gratrixianum var. guangdongense is a rare and striking orchid native to southern China. Known for its vibrant flowers and unique morphology, this variety stands out among its relatives in the Paphiopedilum genus. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions with rich soil. Its beauty has made it a sought-after plant among orchid enthusiasts and botanists. Despite its allure, it remains a challenging species to cultivate due to its specific care requirements.
