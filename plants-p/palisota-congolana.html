Palisota congolana

Palisota congolana, commonly known as African arrowroot, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves and striking white spadix inflorescence. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in rainforests. Its rhizomes are edible and have been traditionally used as a starch source.

Palisota congolana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
