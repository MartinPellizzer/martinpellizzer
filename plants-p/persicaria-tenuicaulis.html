Persicaria tenuicaulis

Persicaria tenuicaulis, commonly known as slender stemmed knotweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Asia.

It features upright stems that are often tinged with red, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along streambanks.

Due to its rapid growth, it can become invasive in certain regions if not managed properly.
