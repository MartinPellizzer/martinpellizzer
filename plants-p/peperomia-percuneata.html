Peperomia percuneata

Peperomia percuneata, commonly known as the silver mitten plant, is a compact evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive silvery-white underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia percuneata is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
