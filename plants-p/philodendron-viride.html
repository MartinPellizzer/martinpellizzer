Philodendron viride

Philodendron viride, commonly known as the Emerald Heart Philodendron, is a popular houseplant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green, though some varieties may have variegation. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers warm, humid environments with well-draining soil. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers, though it is primarily grown for its foliage rather than its blooms.

Philodendron viride is relatively easy to care for, making it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
