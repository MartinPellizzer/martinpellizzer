Pilea adamsiana

Pilea adamsiana, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by round, red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Its unique foliage and ease of care contribute to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
