Pyrethrum bipinnatum

Pyrethrum bipinnatum, commonly known as the doubleleaf pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its finely divided, bipinnate leaves that give it a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers in compact inflorescences. It is widely cultivated for its pyrethrin content, which is used in natural insecticides.

Pyrethrum bipinnatum thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental and agricultural purposes.
