Podostemum scaturiginum

Podostemum scaturiginum, commonly known as the black water plant, is a unique aquatic plant found in the fast-flowing streams of tropical regions.

It has small, ribbon-like leaves that are adapted to the strong currents of its habitat. The plant is often submerged, with its stems and leaves extending into the water. Podostemum scaturiginum plays a vital role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.

Its presence is an indicator of clean, oxygen-rich water in its native environment.
