Polygonatum odoratum var. odoratum

Polygonatum odoratum var. odoratum, commonly known as the Chinese Solomon's Seal, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features rhizomatous growth and lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped, white or pale yellow flowers in early summer. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in late summer.

It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often used in traditional herbal medicine.
