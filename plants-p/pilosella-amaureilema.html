Pilosella amaureilema

Pilosella amaureilema, commonly known as the silverleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a low-growing habit with silver-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. Its flowers are typically small and yellow, arranged in clustered inflorescences.

Pilosella amaureilema is often found in rocky or sandy soils in arid or semi-arid regions.
