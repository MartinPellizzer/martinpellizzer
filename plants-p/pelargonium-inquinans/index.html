Pelargonium Inquinans

Purple geranium, scientifically known as Pelargonium inquinans, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to southern Africa. Its vibrant purple petals and lush green foliage make it a popular choice for gardens and indoor spaces. This species is known for its adaptability to various growing conditions, making it relatively easy to care for. The plant's ability to thrive in both sun and partial shade adds to its versatility. With its ornamental value and hardy nature, the purple geranium remains a favorite among horticulturists and plant lovers alike.
