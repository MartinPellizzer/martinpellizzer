Panicum egleri

Panicum egleri, commonly known as the silver panic grass, is a perennial grass native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and delicate, fluffy seed heads that appear in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist to wet soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its ornamental value comes from its striking color and airy appearance, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

It is also sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
