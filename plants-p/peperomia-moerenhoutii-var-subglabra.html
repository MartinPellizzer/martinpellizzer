Peperomia moerenhoutii var. subglabra

Peperomia moerenhoutii var. subglabra, commonly known as the smooth-leaved peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features upright stems with thick, waxy leaves that are typically lance-shaped and have a smooth, glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually green or reddish in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for collectors and indoor gardeners.
