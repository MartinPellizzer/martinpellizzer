Prosthechea jauana

Prosthechea jauana, commonly known as the Jauana orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the Amazon rainforest and is characterized by its unique, fleshy, and often hairy pseudobulbs. The plant produces pendulous, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive, spurred lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Prosthechea jauana is noted for its ornamental value and is occasionally cultivated for horticultural purposes.
