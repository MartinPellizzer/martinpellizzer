Petunia felipponei

Petunia felipponei, commonly known as the Felippone's petunia, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers.
