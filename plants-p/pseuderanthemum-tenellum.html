Pseuderanthemum tenellum

Pseuderanthemum tenellum, commonly known as the slender false spider flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color, often arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy surface.
