Pelargonium auritum var. carneum

Pelargonium auritum var. carneum, commonly known as the velvetleaf geranium, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves that are often heart-shaped or ovate. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
