Pseudosasa membraniligulata

Pseudosasa membraniligulata, commonly known as the membrane-leaf bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are covered in a fine, membranous sheath, giving it its common name.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
