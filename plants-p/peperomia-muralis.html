Peperomia muralis

Peperomia muralis, commonly known as the wall peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a dense, compact appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white or pale pink. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia muralis is valued for its attractive foliage and air-purifying qualities, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
