Plectranthus nigrescens

Plectranthus nigrescens, commonly known as Black Nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green and often have a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are small, tubular, and typically purple or violet in color, blooming in clusters.

The plant is known for its aromatic leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.
