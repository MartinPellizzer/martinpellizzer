Peperomia linatifolia

Peperomia linatifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features thick, waxy, ovate to lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its low maintenance and attractive foliage make it a versatile addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
