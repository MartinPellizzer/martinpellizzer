Parianella lanceolata

Parianella lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceolate parianella, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly found in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
