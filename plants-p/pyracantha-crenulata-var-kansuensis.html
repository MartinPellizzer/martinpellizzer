Pyracantha crenulata var. kansuensis

Pyracantha crenulata var. kansuensis, commonly known as the Kansu firethorn, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces orange-red berries in the fall, which are edible but often used as ornamental features. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for hedges and garden borders.

Its hardy nature allows it to withstand cold climates, contributing to its adaptability in various landscapes.
