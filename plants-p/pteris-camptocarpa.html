Pteris camptocarpa

Pteris camptocarpa, commonly known as the zigzag water fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzagging fronds that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are pinnately divided, with a soft, matte green appearance.

Pteris camptocarpa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in water gardens or as a houseplant.
