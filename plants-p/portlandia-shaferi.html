Portlandia shaferi

Portlandia shaferi, commonly known as the Shafer's broom.

It is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern California. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences.

It is often found in sandy or gravelly soils along the shoreline.
