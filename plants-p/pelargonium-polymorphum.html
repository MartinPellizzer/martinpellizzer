Pelargonium polymorphum

Pelargonium polymorphum, commonly known as the variable geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its variable leaf shape and coloration. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or red flowers that bloom in summer. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and flowers.

Pelargonium polymorphum prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
