Pfaffia miraflorensis

Pfaffia miraflorensis, commonly known as sacred lotus, is a flowering plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the genus Pfaffia within the family Amaranthaceae. The plant is characterized by its large, showy pink or purple flowers and heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils and requires a warm, humid climate.
