Piper roseiflorum

Piper roseiflorum, commonly known as the rose-flowered pepper plant, is a tropical vine belonging to the Piperaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and Malaysia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its striking rose-pink bracts that surround small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a peppery scent, typical of many Piper species.

The plant is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its potential medicinal properties.
