Platea riedeliana

Platea riedeliana, commonly known as the Riedel's platea, is a member of the Rubiaceae family, known for its ornamental value.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, adding to its visual appeal. Native to Central and South America, it thrives in shaded, moist environments.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
