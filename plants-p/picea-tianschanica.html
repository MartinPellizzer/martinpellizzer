Picea tianschanica

Picea tianschanica, commonly known as the Tianshan spruce, is a coniferous tree native to the Tianshan mountain range in Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, pyramidal shape and dark green, needle-like leaves. The tree produces small, cone-like structures that contain seeds, typical of conifers. It thrives in cold, high-altitude environments with well-drained soils.

Picea tianschanica plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing habitat for various wildlife species.
