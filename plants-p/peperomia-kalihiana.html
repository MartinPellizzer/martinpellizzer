Peperomia kalihiana

Peperomia kalihiana, commonly known as the Java Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves that are often slightly curled at the edges. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white or pale pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its slow growth and attractive foliage, it is well-suited for container gardening and bonsai-style displays.
