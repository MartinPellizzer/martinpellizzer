Photinia Villosa Var. Zollingeri

Photinia villosa var. zollingeri is a striking evergreen shrub known for its vibrant red foliage and dense, glossy leaves. Native to southern China, this plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is popular in ornamental gardening. Its unique appearance makes it a favorite for hedges and privacy screens in landscaped settings. The variety is prized for its compact growth and ability to withstand mild frost. With proper care, it offers year-round visual interest and a lush, textured look to any garden.
