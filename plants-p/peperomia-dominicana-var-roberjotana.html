Peperomia dominicana var. roberjotana

Peperomia dominicana var. roberjotana, commonly known as the Robe's Peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves with a slightly waxy texture and a prominent midrib. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among indoor gardeners due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
