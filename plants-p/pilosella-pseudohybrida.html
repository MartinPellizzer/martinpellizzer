Pilosella pseudohybrida

Pilosella pseudohybrida, commonly known as the slender cat's-paw, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, giving it a distinctive, fluffy appearance. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.

Pilosella pseudohybrida is often found in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.
