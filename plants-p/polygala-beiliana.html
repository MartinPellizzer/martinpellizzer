Polygala beiliana

Polygala beiliana, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forests and rocky slopes. The plant features slender stems and small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or white in color. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, and it produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Polygala beiliana is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
