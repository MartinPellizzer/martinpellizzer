Pothos brassii

Pothos brassii, commonly known as the golden pothos, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a yellow or variegated pattern. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal care. Its stems can grow long and trailing, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets or shelves.

Pothos brassii is also noted for its air-purifying properties, contributing to indoor air quality.
