Premna elskensii

Premna elskensii, commonly known as the silverleaf premna, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Verbenaceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in a silvery-gray pubescence. Premna elskensii is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
