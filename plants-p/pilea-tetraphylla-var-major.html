Pilea tetraphylla var. major

Pilea tetraphylla var. major, commonly known as the Shrub Pilea, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are typically light green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red berries, which are edible but not particularly flavorful. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its dense foliage and ease of care make it a versatile and attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
