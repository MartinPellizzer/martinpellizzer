Parnassia subscaposa

Parnassia subscaposa, commonly known as the scapose crowfoot, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

Parnassia subscaposa is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats, where it thrives in cool, humid conditions.
