Peperomia guadaloupensis var. liebmanniana

Peperomia guadaloupensis var. liebmanniana, commonly known as the Liebmann's peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Mexico.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and white, which are borne on short, upright spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
