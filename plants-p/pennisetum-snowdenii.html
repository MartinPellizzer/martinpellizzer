Pennisetum snowdenii

Pennisetum snowdenii, commonly known as Snowden's beardgrass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and feathery plumes that give it a delicate, ornamental appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that adds visual interest.

Pennisetum snowdenii is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a landscape plant.
