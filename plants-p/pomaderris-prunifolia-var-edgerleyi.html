Pomaderris prunifolia var. edgerleyi

Pomaderris prunifolia var. edgerleyi, commonly known as the Edgerley's Pomaderris, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically grey-green in color, covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. It is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and woodland habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
