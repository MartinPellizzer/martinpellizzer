Piper schmidtii

Piper schmidtii, commonly known as the Schmidt's pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit and the presence of small, heart-shaped leaves. Its fruit is a small, dark blue berry that contains several seeds.

Piper schmidtii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of medicinal compounds.
