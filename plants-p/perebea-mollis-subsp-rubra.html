Perebea mollis subsp. rubra

Perebea mollis subsp.

rubra, commonly known as the red-leaf perebea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rubiaceae. It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia.

The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that often have a reddish tinge on the underside. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, and its fruit is a small, spherical berry.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden environments.
