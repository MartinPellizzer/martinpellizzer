Pittosporum colensoi var. fasciculatum

Pittosporum colensoi var. fasciculatum, commonly known as the Colenso's pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often arranged in clusters, giving the plant its varietal name "fasciculatum.". It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland forest areas.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in native New Zealand ecosystems.
