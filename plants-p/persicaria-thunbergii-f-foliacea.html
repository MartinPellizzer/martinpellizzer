Persicaria thunbergii f. foliacea

Persicaria thunbergii f. foliacea, commonly known as the velvetleaf knotweed, is a variant of the common knotgrass.

It is characterized by its broad, velvety leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters.

Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening for its unique texture and color.
