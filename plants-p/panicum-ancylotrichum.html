Panicum ancylotrichum

Panicum ancylotrichum, commonly known as the silky grass or silky panicum, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silky hairs that cover the leaves and stems, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and used in ecological restoration projects.
