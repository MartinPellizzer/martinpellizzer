Pittosporum brevispinum

Pittosporum brevispinum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features dark green, narrow, leathery leaves that are often slightly hairy beneath. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers a sunny to partially shaded location.

Pittosporum brevispinum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping.
