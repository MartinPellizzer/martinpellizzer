Pilosella hybrida subsp. hybrida

Pilosella hybrida subsp.

hybrida, commonly known as the common hawk's beard, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies.
