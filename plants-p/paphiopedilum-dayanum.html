Paphiopedilum dayanum

Paphiopedilum dayanum, commonly known as the Dayanum orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features a pseudobulb with a single, fleshy leaf and produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive lip. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained soil. Its flowers are typically pink or white with a yellowish throat, and it blooms in late spring or early summer.

Paphiopedilum dayanum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
