Pulmonaria × mollis, commonly known as the common lungwort, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Boraginaceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves that are often spotted with purple or silver markings. Pulmonaria × mollis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to naturalize in suitable conditions.
