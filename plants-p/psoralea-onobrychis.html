Psoralea onobrychis

Psoralea onobrychis, commonly known as sweet pea bush or yellow sweet pea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow to orange flowers. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.
