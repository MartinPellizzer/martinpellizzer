Pandanus platyphyllus

Pandanus platyphyllus, commonly known as the broadleaf pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically clustered in branched inflorescences. Its roots are fibrous and often used in traditional crafts and construction.

The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near coastal areas or in wetlands.
