Peperomia mollisoides

Peperomia mollisoides, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, low-growing perennial plant native to South America.

It features thick, fleshy, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate moisture.

Peperomia mollisoides is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
