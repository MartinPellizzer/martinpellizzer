Polyozus lanceolata

Polyozus lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf polyozus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of East Asia.

It features slender, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along its stem. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, often in shades of white or pale green. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological studies and potential medicinal research.
