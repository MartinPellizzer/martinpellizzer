Panicum chrysoblephare

Panicum chrysoblephare, commonly known as golden panic grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with erect, branching stems. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear golden-yellow to reddish-brown florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Panicum chrysoblephare is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate, colorful appearance.
