Pandanus rhizophorensis

Pandanus rhizophorensis, commonly known as the red pandanus, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific islands.

It is characterized by its robust, fibrous leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with yellowish flowers that attract pollinators. Its root system is particularly adapted to thrive in wet, coastal environments.

This species plays an important ecological role in its native habitat by stabilizing soil and providing shelter for various organisms.
