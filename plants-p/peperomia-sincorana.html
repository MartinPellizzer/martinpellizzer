Peperomia sincorana

Peperomia sincorana, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, waxy foliage with a distinctive silvery-blue hue on the upper surface and a deep green underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia sincorana is known for its low maintenance and ability to adapt well to various indoor environments.
