Peperomia gazauntana

Peperomia gazauntana, commonly known as the Gaza Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, rounded leaves that are typically a deep green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces delicate, upright spikes of tiny, pale pink to white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Peperomia gazauntana is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
