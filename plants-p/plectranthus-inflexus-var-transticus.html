Plectranthus inflexus var. transticus

Plectranthus inflexus var. transticus, commonly known as the African violet, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and a range of colors from deep green to purple. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in clusters, typically in shades of violet or blue. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate light conditions.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental appeal and ease of care.
