Philadelphus tenuifolius f. seoulensis

Philadelphus tenuifolius f. seoulensis, commonly known as the Seoul honeysuckle, is a compact shrub native to Korea.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, dark green leaves that provide a nice contrast to its showy blooms. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a border plant due to its compact size and ornamental value.

The Seoul honeysuckle is also valued for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
