Piper seitzii

Piper seitzii, commonly known as the Seitz's pepper, is a climbing shrub native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when mature, and they are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are often used to make tea, and the plant is valued for its aromatic properties.

The botanical characteristics of Piper seitzii make it an important species in both ecological and medicinal contexts.
