Pimpinella magna var. elata

Pimpinella magna var. elata, commonly known as the tall cow parsley, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, compound umbels of white or pale yellow flowers. The leaves are finely divided and have a distinctive, fern-like appearance. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

This variety is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and along stream banks.
