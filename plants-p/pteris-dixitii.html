Pteris dixitii

Pteris dixitii, commonly known as the golden fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, frond-like structure with a distinctive golden hue. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are pinnately divided, with a feathery appearance that contributes to its ornamental value.

Pteris dixitii is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
