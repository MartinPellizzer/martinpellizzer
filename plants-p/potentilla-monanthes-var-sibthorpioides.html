Potentilla monanthes var. sibthorpioides

Potentilla monanthes var. sibthorpioides, commonly known as the Sibthorp's cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

The plant typically has compound leaves with five leaflets, and its flowers are often clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or rocky habitats.
