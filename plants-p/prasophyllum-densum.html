Prasophyllum densum

Prasophyllum densum, commonly known as the dense leek orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in heathlands and open forests, preferring well-drained sandy or loamy soils. The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces tall, slender flowering stems. Its flowers are usually pale green to brownish in color, with a distinctive, elongated labellum.

Prasophyllum densum is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its specialized pollination mechanisms.
