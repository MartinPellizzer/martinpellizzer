Pelargonium × conchiifolium

Pelargonium × conchiifolium, commonly known as the giant geranium, is a hybrid species within the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant features broad, lobed leaves that are often velvety to the touch, contributing to its ornamental appeal. Native to South Africa, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its unique foliage and vibrant blooms make it a standout in both container and landscape settings.
