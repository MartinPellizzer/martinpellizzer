Pleonotoma imperatoris-maximilianii

Pleonotoma imperatoris-maximilianii, commonly known as the Maximilian's emperor beetle, is a species of leaf beetle native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Chrysomelidae and is characterized by its elongated, metallic green body with intricate patterns. The plant association of this beetle is primarily with the leaves of the genus *Pterosperma*, particularly *Pterosperma macrocarpa*. Its larvae feed on the foliage, contributing to the ecological dynamics of its habitat.

The beetle's presence is often an indicator of the health and diversity of the plant communities it inhabits.
