Picramnia antidesma var. nervosa

Picramnia antidesma var. nervosa, commonly known as the veined picramnia, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves with prominent venation. The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
