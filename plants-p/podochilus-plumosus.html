Podochilus plumosus

Podochilus plumosus, commonly known as the feathered orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, feathery inflorescences. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or lithophyte, often found on tree trunks or rocks in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are small and fragrant, displaying a range of colors from white to pink and purple.

Podochilus plumosus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated for its unique, airy appearance.
