Pinalia acuminata

Pinalia acuminata, commonly known as the slender pinescale.

It is a small, evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region. The plant features narrow, needle-like leaves that are typically clustered at the base of the stems. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by berry-like fruits.

Pinalia acuminata is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas.
