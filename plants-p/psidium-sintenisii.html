Psidium sintenisii

Psidium sintenisii, commonly known as the Sintenis guava, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and produces edible, aromatic fruits similar to common guavas. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a rough texture.
