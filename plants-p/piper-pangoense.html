Piper pangoense

Piper pangoense, commonly known as the Pangoense pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its fruits are tiny, berry-like structures that turn from green to red when mature.

Piper pangoense is valued for its aromatic leaves, which are used in traditional medicine and as a spice in local cuisines.
