Pleurogyne carinthiaca

Pleurogyne carinthiaca, commonly known as the Carinthian pleurogyne, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, clustered white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal or inland regions.
