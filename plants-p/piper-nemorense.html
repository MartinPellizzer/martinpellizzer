Piper nemorense

Piper nemorense, commonly known as the swamp pepper or water pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is a member of the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant typically grows in moist, swampy environments and has small, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.

The fruit is a small, three-lobed berry that contains the seeds.
