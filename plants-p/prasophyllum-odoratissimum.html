Prasophyllum odoratissimum

Prasophyllum odoratissimum, commonly known as the fragrant leek orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its grass-like leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils in open habitats. It produces pale green to white flowers with a distinctive, sweet scent.

Prasophyllum odoratissimum is often found in heathlands and is adapted to nutrient-poor environments.
