Pilea crassifolia

Pilea crassifolia, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to China.

It features rounded, fleshy leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique leaf shape and ease of care contribute to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
