Patagonium dentatum

Patagonium dentatum, commonly known as the Patagonian toothed plant, is a rare and unique species native to the high-altitude regions of southern South America.

The plant typically grows in rocky, arid environments, showcasing a high degree of adaptation to harsh conditions.
