Peperomia conjugata

Peperomia conjugata, commonly known as the Shiny Peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are often overlooked due to their modest appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate moisture.

Peperomia conjugata is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
