Peperomia ciliatifolia var. santarosana

Peperomia ciliatifolia var. santarosana, commonly known as the Santa Rosa peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features upright, branching stems covered in small, ovate leaves with finely serrated edges. The foliage is typically a deep green, often with a slight reddish or purple tinge on the underside. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, followed by distinctive, elongated bracts.

This variety is prized for its ornamental leaves and adaptability to indoor growing conditions.
