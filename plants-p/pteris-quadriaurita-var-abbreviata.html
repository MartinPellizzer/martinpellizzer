Pteris quadriaurita var. abbreviata

Pteris quadriaurita var. abbreviata, commonly known as the abbreviated water fern, is a variant of the broad-leafed water fern.

It is a fern belonging to the family Pteridaceae, characterized by its delicate, frond-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its fronds are usually shorter and more compact compared to the standard variety, with a distinctive, lobed shape.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
