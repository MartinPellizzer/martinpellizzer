Parodia arnostiana

Parodia arnostiana, commonly known as the "Cactus of the Moon," is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stems that often branch into multiple stems, forming a bushy appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It has a dense covering of white to gray spines, which provide protection from herbivores.

Parodia arnostiana is well adapted to arid environments, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant plant.
