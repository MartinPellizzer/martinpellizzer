Phylica vulgaris var. major

Phylica vulgaris var. major, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid conditions.

The silver leaf tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
