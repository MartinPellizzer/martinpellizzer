Polygonum littorale

Polygonum littorale, commonly known as seaside knotweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to coastal regions.

It thrives in saline and brackish environments, often found in marshes, estuaries, and tidal flats. The plant has slender, erect stems that are often reddish in color and bear small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Polygonum littorale plays an important role in stabilizing coastal soils and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
