Pleurothallis Bicolor

Bicolor orchid, scientifically known as Pleurothallis bicolor, is a captivating member of the orchid family native to the lush cloud forests of South America. With its striking dichromatic flowers, it displays a unique blend of white and green hues that make it a standout among its relatives. This delicate plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on trees or rocks in its natural habitat. Its rarity and beauty have made it a prized specimen for orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike. Despite its modest size, the Bicolor orchid holds a special place in the world of horticulture and conservation.
