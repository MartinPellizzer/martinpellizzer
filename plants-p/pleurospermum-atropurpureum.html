Pleurospermum atropurpureum

Pleurospermum atropurpureum, commonly known as purplestem pleurospermum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and robust, hairy stems. The plant features dark purple to reddish stems and leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer.

Pleurospermum atropurpureum is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
