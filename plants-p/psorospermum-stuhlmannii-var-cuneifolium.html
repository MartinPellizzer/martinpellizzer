Psorospermum stuhlmannii var. cuneifolium

Psorospermum stuhlmannii var. cuneifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf psorospermum, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, white flowers clustered in inflorescences, which are followed by distinctive, elongated fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in ecological studies of tropical flora.
