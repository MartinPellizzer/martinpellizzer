Pittosporum ovoideum

Pittosporum ovoideum, commonly known as the ovalleaf pittosporum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native habitats.
