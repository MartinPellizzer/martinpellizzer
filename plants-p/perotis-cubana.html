Perotis cubana

Perotis cubana, commonly known as Cuban perotis, is a flowering plant native to Cuba.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow-orange blooms. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny areas.

Perotis cubana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
