Pleurothallis uvifera

Pleurothallis uvifera, commonly known as the grapefruit orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This epiphytic plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant's pseudobulbs are short and thick, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and relatively easy care requirements.
