Phormium tenax var. veitchii

Phormium tenax var. veitchii, commonly known as the silver fern, is a striking evergreen plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of white to silver hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tall, spiky inflorescences with vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Its unique foliage and hardy nature make it a valued addition to both coastal and temperate landscapes.
