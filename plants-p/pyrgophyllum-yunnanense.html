Pyrgophyllum yunnanense

Pyrgophyllum yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan bellflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky habitats at high elevations. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine pubescence.
