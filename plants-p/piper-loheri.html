Piper loheri

Piper loheri, commonly known as the Loher's pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with long, slender stems that often twine around supports. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to elliptic in shape, and have a glossy surface.

The small, greenish flowers are followed by elongated, three-lobed berries that are typically yellow or orange when mature.
