Ptilotus nobilis subsp. angustifolius

Ptilotus nobilis subsp.

angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spinifex, is a flowering plant native to Australia. It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit.

The plant produces small, pinkish to reddish flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is adapted to arid and saline environments.

It is often used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
