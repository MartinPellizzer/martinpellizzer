Pilea laevicaulis

Pilea laevicaulis, commonly known as the African violet, is a perennial herb native to Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with a smooth texture. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Pilea laevicaulis is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
