Primula cavaleriei

Primula cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese primrose, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountainous regions. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture.

Primula cavaleriei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
