Pandanus Polyacris Var. Pseudolinnaei

Cabbage palm, scientifically known as Pandanus polyacris var. pseudolinnaei, is a striking tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands. It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and long, narrow leaves that form a distinctive crown. This plant thrives in coastal areas and is often found near sandy beaches or in moist, well-drained soils. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to ensure its survival in non-native environments.
