Plectranthus baumii

Plectranthus baumii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to white. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its compact size and ornamental value.

Plectranthus baumii is also valued for its ability to produce fragrant flowers, making it a popular choice in gardens and indoor spaces.
