Pellionia pellioniana

Pellionia pellioniana, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety, silvery underside that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels. Pellionia pellioniana thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
