Persoonia × lucida, commonly known as the silver-leafed waratah, is a hybrid shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Proteaceae and is characterized by its striking, bottlebrush-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in coastal regions and is adapted to sandy soils and maritime climates. Its leaves are narrow and silvery-gray, providing a distinctive appearance.
