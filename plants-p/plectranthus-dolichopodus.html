Plectranthus dolichopodus

Plectranthus dolichopodus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, tubular flowers in shades of violet or purple, typically arranged in clusters.

Plectranthus dolichopodus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.
