Parietaria lusitanica var. micrantha

Parietaria lusitanica var. micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered wall-rocket, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in rocky or wall crevices. The plant features alternate, ovate leaves with a rough, hairy texture, and small, greenish-white flowers that appear in dense clusters. It is part of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

This variety is distinguished by its smaller flowers compared to other Parietaria species.
