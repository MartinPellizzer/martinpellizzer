Phaseolus romanus var. minor

Phaseolus romanus var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf kidney bean, is a variety of the common bean plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, kidney-shaped seeds. The plant typically produces clusters of white flowers followed by pods containing several seeds. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its edible beans.

Due to its smaller size, it is well-suited for container gardening and small spaces.
