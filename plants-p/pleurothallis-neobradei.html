Pleurothallis neobradei

Pleurothallis neobradei, commonly known as the Neobrade's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is part of the Orchidaceae family, which includes over 25,000 species. This epiphytic plant typically grows on trees and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are small and often white or pale green, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination.

Pleurothallis neobradei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
