Peperomia subretusa

Peperomia subretusa, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features broad, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia subretusa is valued for its attractive foliage and air-purifying qualities.
