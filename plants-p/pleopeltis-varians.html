Pleopeltis varians

Pleopeltis varians, commonly known as the variable pleopeltis, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped with a finely toothed margin.

Pleopeltis varians is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in humid, shaded environments.
