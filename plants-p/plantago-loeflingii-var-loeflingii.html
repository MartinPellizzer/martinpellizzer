Plantago loeflingii var. loeflingii

Plantago loeflingii var. loeflingii, commonly known as the loefling's plantain, is a perennial herb native to the southwestern United States.

It features a basal rosette of broad, lance-shaped leaves with a waxy coating. The plant produces erect flowering stems topped with a dense cluster of small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in desert or semi-desert environments.

Botanically, it is characterized by its robust root system and adaptability to arid conditions.
