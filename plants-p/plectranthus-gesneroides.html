Plectranthus gesneroides

Plectranthus gesneroides, commonly known as the purple heart plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple to reddish-brown tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Plectranthus gesneroides is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking appearance and ease of care.
