Phrynium ornatum

Phrynium ornatum, commonly known as the golden arrow plant, is a striking tropical plant native to the Americas.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with yellow and green patterns. The plant produces showy, spadix-like flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, which adds to its ornamental value. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for tropical landscapes and indoor settings.
