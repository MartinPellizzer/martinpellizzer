Pereskiopsis autumnalis

Pereskiopsis autumnalis, commonly known as the autumn pereskiopsis, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the autumn season, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slight waxy coating.

Pereskiopsis autumnalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and seasonal blooms.
