Peperomia dolabriformis

Peperomia dolabriformis, commonly known as the broad-leaf peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape, with a glossy surface and a deep green coloration. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, arranged in spikes along the stems. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Peperomia dolabriformis is valued for its attractive foliage and air-purifying qualities, making it a popular choice for ornamental purposes.
