Polygonum pulchrum

Polygonum pulchrum, commonly known as the beautiful knotweed, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a member of the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants commonly referred to as buckwheats and smartweeds. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit, with slender stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a distinctive reddish or purple hue on the underside.

Polygonum pulchrum thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, streambanks, and disturbed areas.
