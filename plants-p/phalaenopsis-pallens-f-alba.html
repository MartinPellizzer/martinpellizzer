Phalaenopsis pallens f. alba

Phalaenopsis pallens f. alba, commonly known as the white phalaenopsis orchid, is a hybrid cultivar prized for its elegant, white blooms.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia, where it thrives in tropical climates. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. Its flowers are pendulous and resemble moth wings, hence its common name.

This variety requires bright, indirect light and consistent moisture to flourish.
