Peperomia lankesteri

Peperomia lankesteri, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are dark green with a waxy texture, giving it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance for low light and minimal water requirements.

Peperomia lankesteri is popular among plant enthusiasts for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
