Piper romboutsii

Piper romboutsii, commonly known as the Rombouts's pepper, is a tropical flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into peppery berries.

Due to its unique appearance and potential medicinal properties, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
