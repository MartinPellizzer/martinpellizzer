Prinos montanus

Prinos montanus, commonly known as the mountain prion, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in rocky or well-drained soils, often in areas with high altitude and cool climates. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing a dense canopy that helps retain moisture in its arid habitat.

Prinos montanus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and aesthetic features.
