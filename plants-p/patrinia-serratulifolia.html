Patrinia serratulifolia

Patrinia serratulifolia, commonly known as the serrated leaf patrinia, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its serrated, lance-shaped leaves and small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions.

Patrinia serratulifolia is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties.
