Paullinia morii

Paullinia morii, commonly known as the Brazilian grapefruit or araçá-do-brejo, is a member of the Sapotaceae family.

It is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. It is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental and culinary properties.
