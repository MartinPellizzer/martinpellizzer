Phyteuma reniforme

Phyteuma reniforme, commonly known as the kidney-shaped phyteuma, is a flowering plant native to central and eastern Europe.

Phyteuma reniforme is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
