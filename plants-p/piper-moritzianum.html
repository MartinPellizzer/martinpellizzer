Piper moritzianum

Piper moritzianum, commonly known as the Moritz's pepper plant, is a flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. It produces small, berry-like fruits that are often used in traditional medicine.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some pharmacological properties.
