Pelargonium × fastuosum

Pelargonium × fastuosum, commonly known as the showy geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors such as red, pink, and purple. The plant typically has rounded, velvety leaves with a distinctive serrated edge. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Pelargonium × fastuosum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
