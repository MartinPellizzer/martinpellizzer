Poa brandegeei

Poa brandegeei, commonly known as Brandegge's bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and forms dense, low-growing clumps. The plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks. Its inflorescence consists of a single spikelet, and it blooms in late spring to early summer.

Poa brandegeei is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in wetland environments.
