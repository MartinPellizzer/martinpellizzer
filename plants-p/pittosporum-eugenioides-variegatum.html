Pittosporum eugenioides variegatum

Pittosporum eugenioides variegatum, commonly known as the variegated pittosporum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped, dark green leaves with distinctive white or yellow variegation along the midrib. The plant produces fragrant white flowers in clusters, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its unique foliage and seasonal color changes make it a valuable addition to gardens and landscapes.
