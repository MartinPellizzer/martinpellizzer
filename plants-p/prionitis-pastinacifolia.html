Prionitis pastinacifolia

Prionitis pastinacifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf hawksbeard, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

Prionitis pastinacifolia is often found in dry, open habitats like grasslands and rocky slopes.
