Parosela frutescens

Parosela frutescens, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that are usually a deep red or orange color, blooming in winter. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for.
