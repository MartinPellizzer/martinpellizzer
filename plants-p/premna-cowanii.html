Premna cowanii

Premna cowanii, commonly known as the silverleaf or white-leaf premna, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant is characterized by its silvery-white undersides on its leaves, which give it its common name. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels.

The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a leathery texture.
