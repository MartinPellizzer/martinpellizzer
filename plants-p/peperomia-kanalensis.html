Peperomia kanalensis

Peperomia kanalensis, commonly known as the Kanal's peperomia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green to reddish in color. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white, and it thrives in well-drained soil with moderate light conditions.

Peperomia kanalensis is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
