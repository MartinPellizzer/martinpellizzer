Podospermum decumbens var. resedifolium

Podospermum decumbens var. resedifolium, commonly known as the redflower palaquium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its prostrate growth habit and small, fragrant white or pink flowers. Its leaves are typically ovate to elliptic in shape and have a leathery texture.
