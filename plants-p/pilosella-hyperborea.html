Pilosella hyperborea

Pilosella hyperborea, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to high-altitude regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and bract-like leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance, and its flowers are usually yellow or white.

Pilosella hyperborea is often found in mountainous areas of Europe, Asia, and North America.
