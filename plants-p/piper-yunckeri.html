Piper yunckeri

Piper yunckeri, commonly known as the Yunkers' pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing vines and small, opposite leaves. Its flowers are typically greenish-white and arranged in spikes, and it produces small, peppery fruits.

Piper yunckeri is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
