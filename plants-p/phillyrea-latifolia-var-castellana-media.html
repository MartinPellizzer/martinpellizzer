Phillyrea latifolia var. castellana-media

Phillyrea latifolia var. castellana-media, commonly known as the Castellana-medium Phillyrea, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features small, leathery, dark green leaves that are densely packed along its branches. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in Mediterranean climates.
