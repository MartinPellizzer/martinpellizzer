Picridium intermedium var. janii

It is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and trifoliate leaves, typical of many legumes. The plant is native to specific regions in the Mediterranean, where it thrives in rocky and arid environments. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
