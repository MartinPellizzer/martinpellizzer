Peperomia incognita

Peperomia incognita, commonly known as the unknown pepper plant, is a member of the Piperaceae family.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America. The plant features small, ovate leaves that are usually dark green with a slightly waxy texture. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically clustered in spikes, and it produces berries that are red when ripe.

Peperomia incognita is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
