Potentilla Neglecta Var. Decora

Potentilla neglecta var. decora is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant known for its vibrant yellow blooms and delicate foliage. Native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe, this variety thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. Its compact growth habit makes it an ideal choice for rock gardens and borders. The plant is also valued for its hardiness and ability to flourish in challenging environments. With its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements, it has gained popularity among gardeners and botanists alike.
