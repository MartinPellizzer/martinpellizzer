Phebalium Albiflorum

Phebalium albiflorum is a unique and striking plant native to the arid regions of Western Australia. Known for its delicate white flowers and compact growth habit, it stands out among the local flora. This species is often found in rocky outcrops and dry sclerophyll forests, thriving in harsh environmental conditions. Its beauty makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and drought-tolerant landscaping. Despite its charm, it remains a relatively obscure plant in the broader horticultural community.
