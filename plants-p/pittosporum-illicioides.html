Pittosporum illicioides

Pittosporum illicioides, commonly known as the Chinese pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to China and Taiwan.

It features dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a glossy appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant yellowish-white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Pittosporum illicioides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in landscaping and horticulture.
