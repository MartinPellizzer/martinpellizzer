Pilocereus sterkmanii

Pilocereus sterkmanii, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its segmented stems that resemble leaves. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white during the holiday season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Pilocereus sterkmanii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
