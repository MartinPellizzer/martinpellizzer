Petunia violacea var. atkinsiana

Petunia violacea var. atkinsiana, commonly known as the Atkinsian petunia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshade plants. The species is characterized by its vibrant violet to purple flowers and trailing growth habit. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and containers.
