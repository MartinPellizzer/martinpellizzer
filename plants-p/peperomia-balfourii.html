Peperomia balfourii

Peperomia balfourii, commonly known as the Balfour's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are often dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale green, which are typically borne on short spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia balfourii is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
