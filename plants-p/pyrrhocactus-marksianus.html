Pyrrhocactus marksianus

Pyrrhocactus marksianus, commonly known as the Mark's pyrrhocactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, cylindrical stems that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
