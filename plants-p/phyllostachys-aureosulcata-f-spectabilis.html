Phyllostachys aureosulcata f. spectabilis

Phyllostachys aureosulcata f. spectabilis, commonly known as the golden bamboo or yellow bamboo, is a striking ornamental grass native to China.

It features slender, golden-yellow culms with a distinctive yellow stripe running along the interior, giving it a unique visual appeal. The plant grows in clumps, producing feathery, greenish-yellow panicles of flowers in the summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Due to its rapid growth and aesthetic value, it is often used in ornamental horticulture.
