Pelea myrtoidea

Pelea myrtoidea, commonly known as the rock rose or Spanish rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal areas. The plant produces showy, pink or purple flowers that bloom in the spring and summer. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing a lush green contrast to the vibrant flowers.

Pelea myrtoidea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and garden design.
