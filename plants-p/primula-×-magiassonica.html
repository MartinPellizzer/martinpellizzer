Primula × magiassonica

Primula × magiassonica, commonly known as the Magiassonic primrose, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Primulaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that come in a range of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 15–30 cm and prefers cool, moist environments. Its foliage consists of broad, ovate leaves with a velvety texture.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
