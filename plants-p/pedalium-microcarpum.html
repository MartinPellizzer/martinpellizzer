Pedalium microcarpum

Pedalium microcarpum, commonly known as the small-fruited pedaliaceae, is a member of the Pedaliaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia, including India and China. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are often ovate or elliptic in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in racemes, typically with five petals.

The fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, dry seeds.
