Phyllanthus moorei var. moorei

Phyllanthus moorei var. moorei, commonly known as the African moonseed, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a small, deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and clustered in racemes, while the fruit is a capsule containing several seeds.

The plant is characterized by its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in semi-arid conditions.
