Pittosporum ferrugineum var. javanicum

Pittosporum ferrugineum var. javanicum, commonly known as the Javanese Pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
