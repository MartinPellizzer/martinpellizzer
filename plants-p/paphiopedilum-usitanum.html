Paphiopedilum usitanum

Paphiopedilum usitanum, commonly known as the Usitanum orchid, is a rare and terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It features a rhizomatous growth habit with a single, fleshy, and often hairy pseudobulb. The plant produces a single, showy, lip-shaped flower with a distinctive yellow and green coloration. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat destruction, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts.
