Potentilla villosa var. unifoliolosa

Potentilla villosa var. unifoliolosa, commonly known as the silky cinquefoil, is a low-growing shrub native to the western United States.

It features small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has soft, hairy leaves that are simple and oval-shaped, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
