Philadelphus × monstruosus

Philadelphus × monstruosus, commonly known as the giant mock orange, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and can grow up to 6 meters in height. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value comes from its showy blooms and aromatic foliage.
