Piper arrectispicum

Piper arrectispicum, commonly known as the erect-spined pepper, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its hairy, spiny stems and small, opposite leaves. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.

The fruit is a small, three-lobed berry that contains the seeds.
