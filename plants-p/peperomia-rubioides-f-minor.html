Peperomia rubioides f. minor

Peperomia rubioides f. minor, commonly known as the miniature red peperomia, is a compact, trailing plant native to tropical regions.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically a deep red or bronze color, with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for container gardening and indoor spaces.
