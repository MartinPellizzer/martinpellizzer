Peperomia bayajana

Peperomia bayajana, commonly known as the Bayajana peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture, often adorned with subtle veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, which bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia bayajana is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
