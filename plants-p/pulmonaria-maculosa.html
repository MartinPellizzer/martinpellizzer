Pulmonaria maculosa

Pulmonaria maculosa, commonly known as spotted lungwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features rosettes of dark green, velvety leaves with distinctive red or pink spots. The plant produces tall spikes of bell-shaped, pink or purple flowers in early spring. It thrives in moist, shady woodland environments and is often found in moist meadows.

Pulmonaria maculosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
