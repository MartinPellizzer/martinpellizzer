Pittosporum pangalanense

Pittosporum pangalanense, commonly known as the Luzon pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, orange-red berries that contain seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
