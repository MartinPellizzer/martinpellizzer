Pleurothallis exotica

Pleurothallis exotica, commonly known as the exotic orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features elongated, strap-like leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrate.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly valued in horticultural collections.
