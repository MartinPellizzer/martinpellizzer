Potentilla Anserina Var. Yukonensis

Yukon cinquefoil, scientifically known as Potentilla anserina var. yukonensis, is a hardy perennial plant native to the northern regions of North America. It thrives in rocky and alpine environments, showcasing its resilience to cold and harsh conditions. With its bright yellow flowers and rugged appearance, it adds a striking touch to mountainous landscapes. This variety is particularly valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to stabilize soil in difficult terrain. Its unique characteristics make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking low-maintenance, tough plants.
