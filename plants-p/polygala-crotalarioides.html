Polygala crotalarioides

Polygala crotalarioides, commonly known as the Cape mountain catchfly, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, often branched stems and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowers are usually pale blue or white, and it produces small, dry fruits that contain seeds.
