Panicum duchaissingii

Panicum duchaissingii, commonly known as the Duchaissing's panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically greenish or reddish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
