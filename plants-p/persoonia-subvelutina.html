Persoonia subvelutina

Persoonia subvelutina, commonly known as the silky persoonia, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves that give it a velvety appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. These flowers are typically visited by a variety of pollinators, including bees and birds.

Persoonia subvelutina is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and coastal areas.
