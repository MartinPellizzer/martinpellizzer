Paronychia davei var. minorifoliata

Paronychia davei var. minorifoliata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf paronychia, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, needle-like leaves that are typically dark green and covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white, star-shaped flowers in spring, which are followed by small, round berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or containers.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
