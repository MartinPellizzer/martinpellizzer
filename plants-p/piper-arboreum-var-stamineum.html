Piper arboreum var. stamineum

Piper arboreum var. stamineum, commonly known as the stamen pepper or stamen plant, is a tropical climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that turn from yellow to red as they mature. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often grown as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.

The varietal name "stamineum" refers to the presence of stamens in its flowers, which is a distinguishing feature.
