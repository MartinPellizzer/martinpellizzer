Plumeria rubra var. acutifolia

Plumeria rubra var. acutifolia, commonly known as the flame tree or sacred tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and orange. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and produces a milky sap when cut. It thrives in warm, sunny climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

The flowers are typically followed by woody, spiny capsules that contain numerous seeds.
