Phillyrea angustifolia var. lancifolia

Phillyrea angustifolia var. lancifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Phillyrea, is a shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by woody, berry-like fruits. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in arid and semi-arid regions.
