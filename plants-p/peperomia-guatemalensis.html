Peperomia guatemalensis

Peperomia guatemalensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan peperomia, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia guatemalensis is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive foliage.
