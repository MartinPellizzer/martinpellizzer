Pleioblastus yamadorianus

Pleioblastus yamadorianus, commonly known as the Yamadori bamboo, is a clumping bamboo native to Japan.

It features slender, gracefully arching culms that are typically light green to bluish-green in color. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are a striking silver-white on the underside, creating a visually appealing contrast. It is well-suited for rock gardens and shaded areas due to its preference for cool, moist environments.

This ornamental bamboo is prized for its elegant appearance and ease of maintenance in garden settings.
