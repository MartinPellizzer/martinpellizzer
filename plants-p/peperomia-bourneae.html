Peperomia bourneae

Peperomia bourneae, commonly known as the Bornean peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It features thick, fleshy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining, loose soil. It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.

Peperomia bourneae is also valued for its ability to purify the air and enhance indoor humidity levels.
