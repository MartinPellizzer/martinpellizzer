Paphiopedilum × maudiae

Paphiopedilum × maudiae, commonly known as the Maudie's orchid, is a hybrid orchid that exhibits a wide range of floral colors and forms.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its broad, strap-like leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pendulous blooms with a distinctive lip that is often brightly colored. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Paphiopedilum × maudiae is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
