Peperomia brachytrichoides

Peperomia brachytrichoides, commonly known as the compact trichoid peperomia, is a low-growing perennial plant native to South America.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, often in shades of green or red. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for container gardening and terrariums.
