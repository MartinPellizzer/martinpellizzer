Polygala sinaica

Polygala sinaica, commonly known as the Sinai polygala, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the Middle East and North Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that often display a range of colors from white to pink. Polygala sinaica is often found in open habitats such as coastal dunes and limestone formations.
