Puccinellia koeieana

Puccinellia koeieana, commonly known as Koeie's grass, is a perennial grass species native to arid and semi-arid regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny flowers. It is adapted to withstand drought conditions, making it a hardy species in harsh environments.

Puccinellia koeieana is often found in sandy soils and is used in ecological restoration projects due to its resilience.
