Polygala kerrii

Polygala kerrii, commonly known as the Kerri's milk-vetch, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its unique, often zigzagged stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions.
