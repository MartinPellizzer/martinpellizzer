Poterium alveolosum

Poterium alveolosum, commonly known as the sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, annual herb that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often arranged in opposite pairs along the stem.

Poterium alveolosum is known for its ability to thrive in arid environments and is often used in xeriscaping.
