Pterocactus australis

Pterocactus australis, commonly known as the Australian feather cactus, is a succulent plant native to Australia.

It features a cylindrical stem covered in soft, spiny tubercles that give it a unique appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding vibrant color to its habitat. It is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

Pterocactus australis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking form and resilience.
