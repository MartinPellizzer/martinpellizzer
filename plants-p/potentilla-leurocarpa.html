Potentilla leurocarpa

Potentilla leurocarpa, commonly known as silver potentilla, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its silver-gray leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings. Its foliage is covered in fine, white hairs that give it a silvery appearance.

Potentilla leurocarpa is valued for its ornamental beauty and hardy nature in colder climates.
