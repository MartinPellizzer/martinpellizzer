Pennisetum giganteum

Pennisetum giganteum, commonly known as giant feather grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and soft, feathery plumes that give it a delicate, flowing appearance. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping. It is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to create a soft, ethereal texture in garden settings.
