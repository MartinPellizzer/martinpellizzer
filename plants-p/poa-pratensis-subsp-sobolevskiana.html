Poa pratensis subsp. sobolevskiana

Poa pratensis subsp.

sobolevskiana, commonly known as Sobolevskian meadowgrass, is a subspecies native to the temperate regions of Europe and parts of Asia. It is a perennial grass characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and fine, blue-green foliage.

The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, pastures, and along riverbanks. Its inflorescence consists of a single spikelet that is usually erect and slightly compressed.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and ecological restoration projects.
