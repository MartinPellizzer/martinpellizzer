Polianthes runyonii

Polianthes runyonii, commonly known as the Runyon's spider lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to the highlands of Mexico.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces elegant, spider-like flowers with white petals and a distinctive red or purple spathe. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. It is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique appearance.

However, it is important to note that it can be toxic if ingested, so care should be taken when handling it.
