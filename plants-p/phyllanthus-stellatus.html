Phyllanthus stellatus

Phyllanthus stellatus, commonly known as the starleaf spurge, is a perennial shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, star-shaped leaves that give it its common name, and its flowers are typically yellow or green, arranged in clusters. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are often green or red when mature. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.

Phyllanthus stellatus is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
