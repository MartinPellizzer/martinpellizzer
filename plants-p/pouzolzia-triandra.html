Pouzolzia triandra

Pouzolzia triandra, commonly known as the African snowball, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of small white flowers that bloom in winter. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
