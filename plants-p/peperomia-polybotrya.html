Peperomia polybotrya

Peperomia polybotrya, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, pink, and white, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia polybotrya is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and ornamental foliage.
