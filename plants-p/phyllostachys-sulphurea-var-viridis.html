Phyllostachys sulphurea var. viridis

Phyllostachys sulphurea var. viridis, commonly known as the Green Bamboo, is a variant of the yellow bamboo species.

It is characterized by its lush, emerald-green culms that contrast sharply with its yellow-green leaves. This plant is native to East Asia and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. The leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth texture and a subtle sheen.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a versatile addition to various garden settings.
