Pelargonium Gracile

Pelargonium gracile, commonly known as the slender geranium, is a compact and elegant plant that thrives in well-drained soil and sunny conditions. Native to South Africa, this species is prized for its delicate foliage and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink and red. Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for container gardening, window sills, and small garden spaces. Despite its modest size, Pelargonium gracile is a hardy plant that can withstand mild frost and requires minimal maintenance. With its charming appearance and ease of care, it has become a popular choice among both novice and experienced gardeners alike.
