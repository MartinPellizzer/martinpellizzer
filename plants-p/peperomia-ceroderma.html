Peperomia ceroderma

Peperomia ceroderma, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color.

Peperomia ceroderma is a popular houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
