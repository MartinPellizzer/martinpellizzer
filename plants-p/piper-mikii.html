Piper mikii

Piper mikii, commonly known as Mikii pepper or Mikii pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing vines and small, heart-shaped leaves. It produces tiny, peppercorn-like fruits that are used in traditional medicine and as a spice.

The botanical features of Piper mikii make it an important species in both ecological and medicinal contexts.
