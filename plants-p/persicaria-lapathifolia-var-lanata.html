Persicaria lapathifolia var. lanata

Persicaria lapathifolia var. lanata, commonly known as woolly smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It features upright stems that are covered in soft, white, woolly hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in moist soils and is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and other wet habitats.
