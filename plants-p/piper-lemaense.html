Piper lemaense

Piper lemaense, commonly known as the Lemaense pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the rainforests of South America, particularly in regions of Peru and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with dark green, glossy leaves and small, clustered flowers. Its fruits are tiny, red berries that contain the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and as a spice.

The plant's botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and ethnobotanical contexts.
