Phreatia emarginata

Phreatia emarginata, commonly known as the Margined Orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on trees in the rainforest canopy. The plant produces strikingly large, showy flowers with a distinctive white and green coloration. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and decaying organic matter.

Phreatia emarginata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
