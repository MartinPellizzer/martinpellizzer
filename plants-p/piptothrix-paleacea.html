Piptothrix paleacea

Piptothrix paleacea, commonly known as the paleaceae or the white thistle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant is characterized by its spiny, cylindrical stems and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. It has adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability.

The leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.
