Pouteria torta subsp. glabra

Pouteria torta subsp.

glabra, commonly known as the smooth pouteria, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America. It is a small to medium-sized tree characterized by its smooth, glabrous bark and elliptical leaves with a leathery texture.

The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a spherical, fleshy berry that turns from green to yellow or orange when mature.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
