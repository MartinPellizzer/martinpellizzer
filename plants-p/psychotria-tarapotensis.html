Psychotria tarapotensis

Psychotria tarapotensis, commonly known as the Tarapotense Psychotria, is a species of flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, white to pale green flowers. It produces small, dark blue to black berries that are often used in traditional medicine.

Psychotria tarapotensis is also noted for its potential psychoactive properties due to the presence of certain alkaloids.
