Peronocactus stockingeri

Peronocactus stockingeri, commonly known as the Stockinger's cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of northern Mexico.

It is a solitary, columnar cactus with a smooth, green stem that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by small, red fruits. It has a deep root system that allows it to thrive in poor, sandy soils with minimal water.

Its structure and adaptations make it well-suited for desert environments.
