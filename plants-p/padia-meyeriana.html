Padia meyeriana

Padia meyeriana, commonly known as Meyer's padia, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky outcrops, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Padias are often used in traditional medicine and are valued for their ornamental qualities.
