Prunus Serrulata F. Albida

White cherry blossom, scientifically known as Prunus serrulata f. albida, is a striking variant of the traditional cherry blossom tree. This ornamental plant is celebrated for its delicate, pale pink to white petals that bloom in early spring. Native to Japan, it has become a symbol of beauty and transience in many cultures. The tree's ethereal appearance makes it a popular choice for gardens and public spaces. Its short blooming period adds to its allure, capturing the hearts of nature lovers worldwide.
