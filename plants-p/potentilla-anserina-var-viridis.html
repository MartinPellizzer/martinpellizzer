Potentilla anserina var. viridis

Potentilla anserina var. viridis, commonly known as green alpine cinquefoil, is a low-growing perennial shrub native to alpine regions of Europe.

It features small, bright green leaves that are typically elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in harsh climates.
