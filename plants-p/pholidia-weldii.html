Pholidia weldii

Pholidia weldii, commonly known as the African star gooseberry, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red or orange when ripe. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Pholidia weldii is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
