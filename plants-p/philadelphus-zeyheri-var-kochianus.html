Philadelphus zeyheri var. kochianus

Philadelphus zeyheri var. kochianus, commonly known as the Koechian mock orange, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and hardy nature.
