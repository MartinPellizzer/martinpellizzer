Phalaenopsis × portei

Phalaenopsis × portei, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a popular epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces long, pendulous flower spikes with numerous showy, lip-like blooms in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining potting media.

Due to its resilience and aesthetic appeal, it is widely cultivated as a houseplant and for commercial orchid blooms.
