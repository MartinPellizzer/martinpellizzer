Phyteuma rigidifolium

Phyteuma rigidifolium, commonly known as the rigid-leaved phyteuma, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its upright stems and showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to purple. Phyteuma rigidifolium is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
