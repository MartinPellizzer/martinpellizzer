Pyrola intermedia

Pyrola intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate wintergreen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the northern regions of North America.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its evergreen leaves and showy white or pinkish flowers. Pyrola intermedia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
