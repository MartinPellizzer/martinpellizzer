Pterocactus kuntzei

Pterocactus kuntzei, commonly known as the Kuntze's pterocactus, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that are often covered in spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are typically clustered at the top of the stems. It is a member of the Cactaceae family and is well adapted to drought conditions.

Its unique morphology and ecological role make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
