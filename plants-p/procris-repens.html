Procris repens

Procris repens, commonly known as the wandering jew, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Commelinaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, trailing stems that can spread across the ground, often forming dense mats. Procris repens is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions and its low maintenance requirements.
