Phylica horizontalis

Phylica horizontalis, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows up to 1 meter in height, featuring narrow, evergreen leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually yellow or orange, which attract nectar-feeding birds and insects. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

Phylica horizontalis is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.
