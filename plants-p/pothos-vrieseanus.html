Pothos vrieseanus

Pothos vrieseanus, commonly known as the Variegated Pothos, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are typically variegated with shades of green, white, and yellow. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers warm, humid environments. Its aerial roots allow it to climb and attach to surfaces, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets or shelves.

It is relatively low-maintenance, making it an ideal plant for both indoor and outdoor settings.
