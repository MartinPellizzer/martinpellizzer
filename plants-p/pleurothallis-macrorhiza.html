Pleurothallis macrorhiza

Pleurothallis macrorhiza, commonly known as the large-lobed pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small to medium-sized epiphyte, typically found growing on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features linear to lanceolate leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. Its inflorescence is usually a raceme, with flowers that bloom sequentially.

Pleurothallis macrorhiza is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
