Pittosporum lynesii

Pittosporum lynesii, commonly known as the Lyne's pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are typically glossy and elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and parks around the world.
