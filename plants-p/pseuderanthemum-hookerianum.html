Pseuderanthemum hookerianum

Pseuderanthemum hookerianum, commonly known as Hooker's false rose, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.
