Peperomia leuconeura

Peperomia leuconeura, commonly known as the silver lace peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with a distinctive silvery-white margin and deep green center, creating a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia leuconeura is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and moderate humidity to flourish.
