Polystichum macrophyllum

Polystichum macrophyllum, commonly known as the giant fern or evergreen wood fern, is a species of fern native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It is characterized by its large, dark green, leathery fronds that can grow up to three meters in height. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forests and along stream banks. Its rhizomes are thick and fleshy, allowing it to store water and nutrients efficiently.

Polystichum macrophyllum plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
