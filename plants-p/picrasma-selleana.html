Picrasma selleana

Picrasma selleana, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often giving the tree a fiery appearance. The plant has compound leaves with elongated leaflets and produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for their potential therapeutic properties.
