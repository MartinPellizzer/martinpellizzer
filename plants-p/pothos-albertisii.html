Pothos albertisii

Pothos albertisii, commonly known as the Albertis' ivy, is a climbing vine native to Madagascar.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are not particularly showy. It thrives in bright, indirect light and is relatively low maintenance.

Pothos albertisii is popular as a houseplant due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.
