Phalaris arundinacea var. thyrsoidea

Phalaris arundinacea var. thyrsoidea, commonly known as silver hair grass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-haired inflorescences and tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as wet meadows, marshes, and along riverbanks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

It is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support biodiversity.
