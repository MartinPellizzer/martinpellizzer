Paris quadrifolia f. obovata

Paris quadrifolia f. obovata, commonly known as the white wood sorrel, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are often covered in fine hairs, and the stems are slender and somewhat succulent.

It is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
