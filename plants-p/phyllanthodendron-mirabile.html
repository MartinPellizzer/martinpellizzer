Phyllanthodendron mirabile

Phyllanthodendron mirabile, commonly known as the golden star tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded crown and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain a single seed. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a smooth texture.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
