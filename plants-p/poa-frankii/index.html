Poa Frankii

Poa frankii is a species of grass native to the mountainous regions of western North America. It is commonly found in alpine and subalpine habitats where it thrives in cold, dry conditions. This plant is known for its tufted growth habit and fine, needle-like leaves that help it withstand harsh climates. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife. Due to its unique adaptations, Poa frankii is a key component of high-altitude ecosystems.
