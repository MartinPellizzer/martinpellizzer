Pleurospermum albimarginatum

Pleurospermum albimarginatum, commonly known as the white-margin tansy, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features upright, branched stems covered in fine hairs and lance-shaped leaves with a silvery-white margin. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in flat-topped clusters, attracting various pollinators.
