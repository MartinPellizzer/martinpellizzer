Polygala kunzei

Polygala kunzei, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by the wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Polygala kunzei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
