Phyllostachys heteroclada f. decurtata

Phyllostachys heteroclada f. decurtata, commonly known as the shortened bamboo, is a variant of the Phyllostachys heteroclada species.

It is a clump-forming bamboo that typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 meters. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often slightly curved, and its culms are generally smooth and have a pale green to yellowish hue. It is native to China and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens and landscapes.

This bamboo is relatively cold-hardy and can thrive in a variety of soil types, making it a versatile choice for horticultural use.
