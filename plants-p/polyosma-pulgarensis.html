Polyosma pulgarensis

Polyosma pulgarensis, commonly known as the purple-flowered polyosma, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
