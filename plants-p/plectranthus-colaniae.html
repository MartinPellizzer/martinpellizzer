Plectranthus colaniae

Plectranthus colaniae, commonly known as the Cape mint, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its aromatic foliage and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for both ornamental and medicinal purposes.
