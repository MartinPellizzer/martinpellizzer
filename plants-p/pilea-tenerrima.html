Pilea tenerrima

Pilea tenerrima, commonly known as the aluminum plant, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the family Urticaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with a metallic sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically hidden beneath its foliage. It is a compact, low-maintenance plant that thrives in indirect light and well-draining soil.

Pilea tenerrima is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
