Picris oligocephala

Picris oligocephala, commonly known as narrow-headed groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has finely divided, grass-like leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as rocky slopes and gravelly areas.

Picris oligocephala is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
