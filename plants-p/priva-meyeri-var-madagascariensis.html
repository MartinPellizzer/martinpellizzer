Priva meyeri var. madagascariensis

Priva meyeri var. madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar privet, is a species of flowering plant in the Oleaceae family.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a smooth texture with a glossy appearance.

The fruit is a small, capsule-like structure that contains several seeds.
