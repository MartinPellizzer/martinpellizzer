Pseudobaccharis Macrophylla

Pseudobaccharis macrophylla is a striking evergreen shrub native to southern Brazil. Known for its large, waxy leaves and showy yellow flowers, it adds dramatic visual interest to gardens. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a versatile choice for coastal and tropical landscapes. Its resilience and ornamental value have made it a popular selection among horticulturists and landscape designers. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to avoid overwatering and disease.
