Peperomia phyllostachya

Peperomia phyllostachya, commonly known as the laceleaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with a textured, waxy surface that gives it a distinctive, almost metallic appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are clustered in spikes, typically blooming in the spring. Its foliage is often variegated with shades of green, silver, and white, making it a visually appealing houseplant.

Peperomia phyllostachya prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, thriving in humid environments with moderate temperatures.
