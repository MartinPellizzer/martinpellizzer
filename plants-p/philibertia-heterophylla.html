Philibertia heterophylla

Philibertia heterophylla, commonly known as the variable filbert, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland and rocky habitats. The plant features a basal rosette of leaves that are often deeply lobed and vary in shape depending on the environment. Its flowers are small and clustered in a spike, with yellow petals that attract pollinators.

Philibertia heterophylla is notable for its adaptability and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
