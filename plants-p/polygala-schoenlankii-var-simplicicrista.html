Polygala schoenlankii var. simplicicrista

Polygala schoenlankii var. simplicicrista, commonly known as the simple-crested polygala, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, often branched inflorescences. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its small, delicate flowers are usually pink or purple, and it produces small, bead-like fruits.
