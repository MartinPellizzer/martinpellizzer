Primula yuparensis var. sorachiana

Primula yuparensis var. sorachiana, commonly known as the Sorachi primrose, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Japan.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically blooms in early spring, producing clusters of pale yellow to white petals with a distinctive reflexed shape. Its leaves are ovate and covered in a fine layer of silky hairs, contributing to its unique appearance.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native environment.
