Populus gamblei var. qiongdaoensis

Populus gamblei var. qiongdaoensis, commonly known as the Qiongdao poplar, is a rare and unique variant of the Populus gamblei species.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Qiongdao region of Hainan Island. This plant is characterized by its distinctive foliage, which features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, it is considered a valuable species for botanical research and conservation efforts.
