Podosemum elegans

Podosemum elegans, commonly known as the elegant podosemum, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and showy, spadix-like inflorescences. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically enclosed within a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
