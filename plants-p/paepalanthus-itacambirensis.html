Paepalanthus itacambirensis

Paepalanthus itacambirensis, commonly known as the Itacambire cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant is typically found in rocky or sandy soils in tropical and subtropical regions. It has adapted to arid conditions with thick, fleshy stems that store water.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
