Persicaria senticosa var. sagittifolia

Persicaria senticosa var. sagittifolia, commonly known as arrowleaf smartweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of North America.

It is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves and bright red or pink flowers that appear in summer. The plant typically grows in moist soils along stream banks and wetlands. It has a sprawling growth habit and can reach heights of up to 2 meters.

Its flowers are clustered in dense spikes and are attractive to pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
