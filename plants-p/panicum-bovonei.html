Panicum bovonei

Panicum bovonei, commonly known as the Bovone bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and swamps, and is adapted to moist to saturated soils. The plant has narrow, blue-green leaves and produces fluffy, pale blue to purple seed heads in the summer. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for wildlife.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a species of conservation concern in some regions.
