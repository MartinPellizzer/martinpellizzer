Phyllorkis tenella

Phyllorkis tenella, commonly known as the slender leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive, fragrant scent. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its flowers bloom from late summer to early autumn, and it relies on specific pollinators for reproduction.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native environment.
