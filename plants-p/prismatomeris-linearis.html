Prismatomeris linearis

Prismatomeris linearis, commonly known as the slender glassworts, is a halophytic plant that thrives in saline environments.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy stems and leaves that store water, aiding in its survival in arid or coastal conditions. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It is often found in salt marshes, estuaries, and other brackish habitats along the Atlantic and Pacific coasts.

Its unique structure and ability to tolerate high salinity make it an important component of coastal ecosystems.
