Pennisetum setosum var. breve

Pennisetum setosum var. breve, commonly known as dwarf fountain grass, is a compact, ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a fine, silky texture. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that add a soft, feathery appearance to the foliage. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its compact size and ornamental appeal make it suitable for borders, containers, and rock gardens.
