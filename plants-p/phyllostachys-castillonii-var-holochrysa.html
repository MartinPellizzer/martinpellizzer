Phyllostachys castillonii var. holochrysa

Phyllostachys castillonii var. holochrysa, commonly known as the golden bamboo, is a variant of the Phyllostachys castillonii species.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow culms that contrast with its dark green leaves. This bamboo is native to China and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. It grows as a clump-forming perennial, reaching heights of up to 6 meters.

The plant is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping and bamboo gardening.
