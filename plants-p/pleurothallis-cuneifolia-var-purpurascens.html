Pleurothallis cuneifolia var. purpurascens

Pleurothallis cuneifolia var. purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-flowered slipper orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small epiphytic orchid with dark green, strap-like leaves that are often clustered together. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of purple and white, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in high humidity and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
