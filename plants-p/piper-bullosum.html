Piper bullosum

Piper bullosum, commonly known as the bubble vine, is a climbing perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, twining stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that ripens to a yellow or orange color, often containing several seeds.
