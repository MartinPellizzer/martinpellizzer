Peperomia vacciniifolia

Peperomia vacciniifolia, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, and velvety leaves that are typically dark green with a prominent midrib. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia vacciniifolia is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
