Pleurothallis halbingeriana

Pleurothallis halbingeriana, commonly known as the Halbinger's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in the canopy layer of the forest. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often with intricate patterns on the petals. It prefers high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often found in specialized horticultural collections.
