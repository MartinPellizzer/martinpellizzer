Pandanus hollrungii f. caroliniana

Pandanus hollrungii f. caroliniana, commonly known as the Caroline pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves that grow in a fan-like arrangement, often reaching several meters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or green, followed by distinctive, sword-shaped fruits. It thrives in coastal and wetland environments, often growing near mangroves or in brackish water.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological importance, it is a notable species in its native habitat.
