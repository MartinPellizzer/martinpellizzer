Pelargonium × sophiae

Pelargonium × sophiae, commonly known as the Sophie's geranium, is a hybrid geranium species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from pink to purple, often with a white or pale center. The plant has serrated, dark green leaves that are somewhat hairy to the touch. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Pelargonium × sophiae is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and pleasant scent.
