Pelargonium × rustii

Pelargonium × rustii, commonly known as the rust-red geranium, is a hybrid species of the geranium genus native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, rust-colored flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of red and orange. The plant features rounded, velvety leaves with a slightly fuzzy texture, often displaying a reddish or purple tint. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Pelargonium × rustii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
