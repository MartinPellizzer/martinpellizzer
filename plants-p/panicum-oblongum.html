Panicum oblongum

Panicum oblongum, commonly known as the slender panicgrass, is a species of grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and spikelet-based inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and prairies. It has a fibrous root system and produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.

Its botanical features make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
