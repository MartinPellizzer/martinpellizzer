Phyllostachys tianmuensis

Phyllostachys tianmuensis, commonly known as Tianmu bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the bamboo family, Poaceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the mountainous regions of Zhejiang and Anhui provinces. This bamboo is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 20 meters. The leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth texture and a bluish-green color.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its aesthetic appeal.
