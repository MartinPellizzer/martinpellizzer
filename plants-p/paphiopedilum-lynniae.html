Paphiopedilum lynniae

Paphiopedilum lynniae, commonly known as the Lynne's lady's slipper, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the mountainous regions of Borneo.

It is characterized by its unique, pouch-like lip that resembles a slipper, a feature typical of the Paphiopedilum genus. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its rarity and habitat destruction, it is considered endangered and is protected in many regions.
