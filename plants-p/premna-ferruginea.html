Premna ferruginea

Premna ferruginea, commonly known as the rusty leaf premna, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are typically covered in a rusty or reddish-brown pubescence. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers arranged in clusters, followed by small, spherical berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Premna ferruginea is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some cultures.
