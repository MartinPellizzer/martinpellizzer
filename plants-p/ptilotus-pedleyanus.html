Ptilotus pedleyanus

Ptilotus pedleyanus, commonly known as the ghost flower, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

Ptilotus pedleyanus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking visual appearance.
