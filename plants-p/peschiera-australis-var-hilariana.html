Peschiera australis var. hilariana

Peschiera australis var. hilariana, commonly known as the Hilarian southern peashrub, is a shrub native to the south of Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, tubular flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.

Its botanical features make it a suitable choice for coastal and sandy garden environments.
