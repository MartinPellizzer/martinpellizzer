Podocarpus urbanii

Podocarpus urbanii, commonly known as the Philippine podocarp, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Podocarpaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and conical shape. The plant produces small, spherical cones that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in forested areas with high humidity.

Podocarpus urbanii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in reforestation efforts due to its ecological importance.
