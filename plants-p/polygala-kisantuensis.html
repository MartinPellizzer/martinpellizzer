Polygala kisantuensis

Polygala kisantuensis, commonly known as the Kisantu milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are simple and alternate, often with a hairy texture.

The flowers, which are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, are adapted for insect pollination.
