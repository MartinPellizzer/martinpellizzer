Polyodon

Polyodon, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant that belongs to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive blue or purple ray flowers and spiky, bristly leaves. Native to coastal regions of Europe and western Asia, it thrives in salty or brackish environments. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often found in wetlands or along the edges of salt marshes.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in coastal areas.
