Pseudosasa japonica f. akebonosuzi

Pseudosasa japonica f. akebonosuzi, commonly known as the Akebono Suzi bamboo, is a compact, ornamental grass native to Japan.

It features narrow, bright green leaves with a slight bluish tint, forming dense, upright clumps. The plant is characterized by its slow growth and ability to thrive in partial shade to full sun. It is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for horticultural collections.
