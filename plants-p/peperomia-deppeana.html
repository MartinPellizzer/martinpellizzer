Peperomia deppeana

Peperomia deppeana, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to Central and South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically silver or gray-green in color, providing a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia deppeana is known for its low maintenance and ability to tolerate dry conditions.
