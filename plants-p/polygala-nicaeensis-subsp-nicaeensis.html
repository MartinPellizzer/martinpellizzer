Polygala nicaeensis subsp. nicaeensis

Polygala nicaeensis subsp.

nicaeensis, commonly known as the Narrow-leaved milk-vetch, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

The plant features slender stems and small, intricate flowers that are often pale blue or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
