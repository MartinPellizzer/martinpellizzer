Peperomia fraijanesana var. san-isidroana

Peperomia fraijanesana var. san-isidroana, commonly known as the San Isidro peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Mexico.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale green to white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care in indoor settings.
