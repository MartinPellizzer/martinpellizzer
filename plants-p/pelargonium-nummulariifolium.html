Pelargonium nummulariifolium

Pelargonium nummulariifolium, commonly known as the dollar plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features small, round, fleshy leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or red flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
