Pilosella hadromera

Pilosella hadromera, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and is native to Europe and parts of western Asia. The plant produces small, yellowish-white composite flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are typically narrow and hairy. Pilosella hadromera thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and roadsides.

It is often found in temperate climates and is relatively easy to cultivate in gardens.
