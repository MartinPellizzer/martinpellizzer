Pelargonium × clintoniae

Pelargonium × clintoniae, commonly known as the Clinton geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features upright stems with opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Native to South Africa, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

It is widely cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
