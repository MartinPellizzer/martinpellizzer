Peperomia incisa

Peperomia incisa, commonly known as the cut-leaf peperomia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are deeply lobed and often have a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia incisa is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its compact size and attractive foliage.
