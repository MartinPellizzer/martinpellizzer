Pouteria sipapoensis

Pouteria sipapoensis, commonly known as the Brazilian star apple, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and large, glossy green leaves. It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and potential economic importance in local markets.
