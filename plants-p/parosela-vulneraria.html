Parosela vulneraria

Parosela vulneraria, commonly known as the wounded plant or the bleeding heart plant, is a member of the Violaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant's botanical characteristics make it both ecologically significant and of interest in horticultural studies.
