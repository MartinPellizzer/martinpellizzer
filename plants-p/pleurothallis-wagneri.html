Pleurothallis wagneri

Pleurothallis wagneri, commonly known as the Wagner's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually small and come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink.

Pleurothallis wagneri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
