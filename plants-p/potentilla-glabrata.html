Potentilla glabrata

Potentilla glabrata, commonly known as smooth cinquefoil, is a low-growing perennial plant native to North America.

It features small, yellow, five-petaled flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has slender, upright stems covered with fine, soft hairs. Its leaves are alternate, usually divided into five narrow lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Potentilla glabrata thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or rocky areas.
