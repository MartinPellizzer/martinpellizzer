Pasania spicata var. brevipetiolata

Pasania spicata var. brevipetiolata, commonly known as the short-stemmed pasania, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, lance-shaped leaves with a short petiole. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in spicate inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in suitable climates.
