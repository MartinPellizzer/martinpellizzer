Pimelea Imbricata Var. Piligera

Pimelea imbricata var. piligera is a strikingly beautiful shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Australia. Known for its vibrant yellow flowers and dense, compact growth habit, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking both ornamental value and hardiness. This variety stands out for its fine, silvery foliage that adds a unique texture to any landscape. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it adaptable to a range of gardening conditions. With its ability to attract pollinators and its resilience to coastal climates, it is a valuable addition to native plant collections.
