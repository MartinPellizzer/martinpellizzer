Pleuroplitis major

Pleuroplitis major, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is known for its complex and intricate floral structure. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture that helps retain moisture in its native habitat.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
