Papaver tunetanum

Papaver tunetanum, commonly known as the Tunisian poppy, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Papaveraceae and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments. Its capsules contain numerous small, dark seeds that are dispersed by wind.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
