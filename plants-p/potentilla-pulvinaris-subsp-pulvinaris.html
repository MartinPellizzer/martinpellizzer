Potentilla pulvinaris subsp. pulvinaris

Potentilla pulvinaris subsp.

pulvinaris, commonly known as the Alpine Cinquefoil, is a low-growing shrub native to alpine regions of Europe. It features small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant has dark green, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to harsh mountain environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.
