Peperomia fagerlindii

Peperomia fagerlindii, commonly known as the Fagerlind's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences of white or pale pink flowers, which are not particularly showy but add subtle charm. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia fagerlindii is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and moderate humidity to flourish.
