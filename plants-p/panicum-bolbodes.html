Panicum bolbodes

Panicum bolbodes, commonly known as the globular panicgrass, is a grass species characterized by its distinctive seed heads.

It produces dense, spherical clusters of seeds that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as fields and roadsides. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Panicum bolbodes is native to parts of Africa and is often found in semi-arid environments.
