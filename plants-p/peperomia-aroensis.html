Peperomia aroensis

Peperomia aroensis, commonly known as the Aroen's pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This compact, evergreen shrub typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters, featuring thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are clustered in spikes.

Peperomia aroensis is valued for its ornamental foliage and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its low maintenance requirements.
