Pelargonium roseum

Pelargonium roseum, commonly known as the rose geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pinkish-red flowers and velvety, dark green leaves. The plant belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Pelargonium roseum is also valued for its use in aromatherapy and as a natural air purifier.
