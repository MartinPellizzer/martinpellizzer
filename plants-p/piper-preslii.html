Piper preslii

Piper preslii, commonly known as the Preslia pepper or Preslia's pepper, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants such as black pepper. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. It produces small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

Piper preslii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
