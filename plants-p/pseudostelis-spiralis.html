Pseudostelis spiralis

Pseudostelis spiralis, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and green, typically blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in greenhouses and as a houseplant.
