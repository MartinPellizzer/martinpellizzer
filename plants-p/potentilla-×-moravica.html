Potentilla × moravica

Potentilla × moravica, commonly known as the Moravian cinquefoil, is a hybrid shrub native to Central Europe.

It typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and features compound leaves with five to seven elliptical leaflets. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant foliage and long-lasting blooms.
