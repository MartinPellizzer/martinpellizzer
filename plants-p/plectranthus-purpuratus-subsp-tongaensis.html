Plectranthus purpuratus subsp. tongaensis

Plectranthus purpuratus subsp.

tongaensis, commonly known as Tonga purpleleaf, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its aromatic, hairy leaves that range in color from light green to deep purple.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale purple or white, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.
