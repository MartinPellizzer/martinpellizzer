Pelargonium rogersii

Pelargonium rogersii, commonly known as the Rogers geranium, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing herbaceous plant with rounded, velvety leaves and showy, five-petaled flowers. The flowers typically bloom in shades of pink, red, or white, depending on the cultivar. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

This plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
