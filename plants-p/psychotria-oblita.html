Psychotria oblita

Psychotria oblita, commonly known as the "Cocaine Plant" or "Coca Plant," is a species of flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly the Andean regions of Peru, Bolivia, and Ecuador. Due to its psychoactive properties, it is also of interest in ethnobotanical and pharmacological research.
