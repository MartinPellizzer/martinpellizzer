Pyrethrum partheniifolium

Pyrethrum partheniifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant produces fine, hair-like structures called trichomes on its leaves and stems, which contain the natural insecticides pyrethrins. These compounds make it valuable in organic pest control.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
