Pelargonium felteri

Pelargonium felteri, commonly known as the Felter's geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features rounded, lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets and containers.

Pelargonium felteri is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care in garden settings.
