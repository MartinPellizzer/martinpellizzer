Panicum milleflorum

Panicum milleflorum, commonly known as crimson lovegrass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, fluffy plumes that appear in late summer and early autumn. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, pink to red inflorescences. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Panicum milleflorum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
