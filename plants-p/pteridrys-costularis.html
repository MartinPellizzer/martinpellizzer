Pteridrys costularis

Pteridrys costularis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf fern, is a species of fern characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is native to regions with moist, temperate climates. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments such as forests or along stream banks. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a finely serrated margin.

Pteridrys costularis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its graceful appearance.
