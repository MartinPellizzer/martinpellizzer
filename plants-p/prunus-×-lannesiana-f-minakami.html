Prunus × lannesiana f. minakami

Prunus × lannesiana f. minakami, commonly known as the Minakami cherry, is a botanical hybrid belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is typically cultivated as a decorative ornamental plant. The species is native to Japan and is prized for its vibrant, early-blooming flowers that display a range of colors from white to pink. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often used in gardens and public landscapes for its aesthetic appeal.
