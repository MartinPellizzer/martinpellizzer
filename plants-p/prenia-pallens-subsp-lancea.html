Prenia pallens subsp. lancea

Prenia pallens subsp.

lancea, commonly known as the lanceolate prenia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in grassland habitats and is often found in open, sunny areas. It has a creeping or spreading growth habit, with stems that root at the nodes.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
