Phebalium wilsonii

Phebalium wilsonii, commonly known as Wilson's hakea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and striking, cone-like inflorescences. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are often clustered in cylindrical spikes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to coastal and sandy environments.
