Peperomia calyculata

Peperomia calyculata, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, waxy foliage with a velvety texture, often displaying shades of green, silver, and variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, followed by spherical, reddish berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia calyculata is valued for its ornamental leaves and low maintenance requirements.
