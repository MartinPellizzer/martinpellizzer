Piper griseum

Piper griseum, commonly known as the gray pipevine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Piperaceae, which includes many species of flowering plants known for their aromatic properties. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests.

Piper griseum is also noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
