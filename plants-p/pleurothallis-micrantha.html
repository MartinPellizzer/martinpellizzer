Pleurothallis micrantha

Pleurothallis micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments within the rainforest canopy. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, typically blooming during the warmer months. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrate.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological niche, it is a subject of interest for both botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
