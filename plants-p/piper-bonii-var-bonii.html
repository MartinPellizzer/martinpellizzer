Piper bonii var. bonii

Piper bonii var. bonii, commonly known as the African pepper plant, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive aromatic scent. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by elongated, spindle-shaped berries. The berries are typically harvested when they turn from green to red, indicating maturity.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
