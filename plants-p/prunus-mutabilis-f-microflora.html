Prunus mutabilis f. microflora

Prunus mutabilis f. microflora, commonly known as the miniature cherry or dwarf cherry, is a compact cultivar of the Prunus genus.

It is characterized by its small stature, typically reaching only a few feet in height, making it suitable for small gardens or containers. The plant produces delicate pink to white flowers in early spring, which can change color as the season progresses. Its foliage is dark green and provides ornamental value throughout the growing season.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to various soil conditions.
