Potentilla arenaria f. stenopetala

Potentilla arenaria f. stenopetala, commonly known as the narrow-petaled sand cinquefoil, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arid and sandy environments.

It features small, five-petaled flowers that are typically yellow or pale yellow in color, blooming in early summer. The plant has a compact, mat-forming growth habit with silver-gray foliage that reflects sunlight, aiding in water conservation. Its leaves are narrow and linear, adapted to reduce water loss in dry conditions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in challenging, xerophilic habitats.
