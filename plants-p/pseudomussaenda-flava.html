Pseudomussaenda flava

Pseudomussaenda flava, commonly known as yellow trumpetbush, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate to lanceolate in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Pseudomussaenda flava is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
