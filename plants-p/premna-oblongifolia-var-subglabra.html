Premna oblongifolia var. subglabra

Premna oblongifolia var. subglabra, commonly known as the smooth-leafed premna, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features ovate to elliptical leaves that are typically smooth on both surfaces, giving it its varietal name "subglabra.". The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, followed by small, spherical fruits. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.
