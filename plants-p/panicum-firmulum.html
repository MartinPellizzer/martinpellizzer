Panicum firmulum

Panicum firmulum, commonly known as little bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and compact growth habit, typically reaching heights of 1 to 2 feet. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often tinged with red or purple in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it a hardy choice for native plant gardens.

Its ecological value includes providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
