Peperomia curtispica

Peperomia curtispica, commonly known as the curly peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, waxy leaves that are often curled or twisted, giving it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia curtispica is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its resilience and attractive foliage.
