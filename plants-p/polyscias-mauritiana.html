Polyscias mauritiana

Polyscias mauritiana, commonly known as the African nettle, is a tropical shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by small, round berries that turn red when mature. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in moist forest understories.

Polyscias mauritiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
