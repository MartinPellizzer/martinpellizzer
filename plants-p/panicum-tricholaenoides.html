Panicum tricholaenoides

Panicum tricholaenoides, commonly known as slender panicgrass, is a grass species native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically green or pale in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along water bodies.

Due to its adaptability, it can be both a desirable ornamental plant and a potential weed in certain environments.
