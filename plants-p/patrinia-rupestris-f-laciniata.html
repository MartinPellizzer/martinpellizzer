Patrinia rupestris f. laciniata

Patrinia rupestris f. laciniata, commonly known as the shredded leaf patrinia, is a flowering plant belonging to the mint family, Lamiaceae.

It is characterized by its finely dissected, lobed leaves that give it a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. Native to China, it thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

It is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
