Polycycliska

Polycycliska, commonly known as the cyclical flower, is a unique plant species characterized by its intricate, multi-layered petals that resemble concentric circles.

Its botanical structure features a central stem with multiple whorls of leaves and blossoms, creating a visually complex appearance. The plant is native to tropical regions and thrives in humid, shaded environments. It produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom sequentially throughout the growing season.

Due to its rare and delicate nature, Polycycliska is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its ornamental value.
