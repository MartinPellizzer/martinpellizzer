Phyllanthodendron moi

Phyllanthodendron moi, commonly known as the golden star tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are often arranged in a whorled pattern along the stems. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by bright red, fleshy berries. Its leaves and fruits are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal due to its attractive foliage and vibrant fruit.
