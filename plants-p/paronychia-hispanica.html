Paronychia hispanica

Paronychia hispanica, commonly known as Spanish nail grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia.

Paronychia hispanica is notable for its ability to thrive in harsh environments and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
