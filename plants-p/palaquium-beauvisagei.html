Palaquium beauvisagei

Palaquium beauvisagei, commonly known as the Beaufisage oil palm, is a species native to Madagascar.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree characterized by its dense, dark green leaves and clustered inflorescences. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into drupes containing a single seed. Its fruit is valued for its oil, which is used in traditional applications.

The species is also noted for its ecological importance in its native habitat.
