Pagameopsis maguirei var. angustifolius

Pagameopsis maguirei var. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pagameopsis, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves that are typically narrow and lance-shaped. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
