Panicum bambusiculme

Panicum bambusiculme, commonly known as bamboo grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and feathery, panicle-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are long and narrow, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Panicum bambusiculme is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping or for erosion control.
