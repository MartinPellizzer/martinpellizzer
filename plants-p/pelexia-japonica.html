Pelexia japonica

Pelexia japonica, commonly known as Japanese pelexia, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often used in traditional landscaping.
