Paracaleana parvula

Paracaleana parvula, commonly known as the small-flowered paracaleana, is a species of orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in arid conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Paracaleana parvula is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
