Pelargonium × cuneiflorum

Pelargonium × cuneiflorum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The leaves are typically narrow and serrated, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. Native to South Africa, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

This plant is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its attractive blooms and relatively low maintenance requirements.
