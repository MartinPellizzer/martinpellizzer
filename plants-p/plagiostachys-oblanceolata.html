Plagiostachys oblanceolata

Plagiostachys oblanceolata, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often glossy and have a distinct arrowhead shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along streambanks.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and wetland restoration projects.
