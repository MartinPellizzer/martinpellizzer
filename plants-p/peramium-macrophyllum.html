Peramium macrophyllum

Peramium macrophyllum, commonly known as the large-leaf peramium, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
