Pavonia velvetiana

Pavonia velvetiana, commonly known as the velvetleaf pavonia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet. The plant features palmately lobed leaves with a velvety texture, giving it its common name. Its showy, pink to red flowers bloom in clusters during the summer months.

Pavonia velvetiana is often found in moist, sunny habitats such as wetlands and woodland edges.
