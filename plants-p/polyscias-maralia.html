Polyscias maralia

Polyscias maralia, commonly known as the Maralai sedge, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by spherical berries. Polyscias maralia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
