Pelargonium magenteum

Pelargonium magenteum, commonly known as the magenta geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant magenta-pink flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Its compact growth habit and long blooming period make it a versatile addition to both indoor and outdoor landscapes.
