Peperomia hondoana

Peperomia hondoana, commonly known as the Hondoan Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the island of Hondo in the Philippines.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a velvety texture and produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its attractive appearance. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it relatively easy to care for.

Peperomia hondoana is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
