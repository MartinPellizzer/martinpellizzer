Phaenopyrum Ellipticum

Blue mist shrub, scientifically known as Phaenopyrum ellipticum, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern China. It is prized for its delicate, bluish-gray foliage that resembles a soft mist, giving it its common name. This evergreen shrub thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a border plant. Its compact growth habit and year-round color make it a popular choice among gardeners seeking low-maintenance beauty. With its unique appearance and adaptability, the Blue mist shrub adds a touch of elegance to any landscape.
