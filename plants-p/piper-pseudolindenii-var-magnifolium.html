Piper pseudolindenii var. magnifolium

Piper pseudolindenii var. magnifolium, commonly known as the giant arrowleaf, is a tropical vine native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its fruit is a small, yellowish berry that ripens to a red color.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in shaded gardens.
