Polycodium stamineum var. austromontanum

Polycodium stamineum var. austromontanum, commonly known as the Australian polycodium, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of southern Australia.

It is a herbaceous perennial with slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its flowers are usually pink or red, attracting pollinators like bees and birds.

The species is characterized by its finely divided leaves and is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value.
