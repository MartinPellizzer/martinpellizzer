Peperomia pilinervia

Peperomia pilinervia, commonly known as the feather-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are lance-shaped and have a distinctive, feathery appearance along the veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia pilinervia is known for its ability to tolerate low light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it easy to care for.
