Pyrus Horizontalis

Horizontal crabapple, scientifically known as Pyrus horizontalis, is a unique and ornamental tree native to China. It is characterized by its spreading habit and small, showy flowers that bloom in early spring. The tree produces edible fruits that are often used in traditional medicine and cooking. Its adaptability to various soil types makes it a popular choice for landscaping. With its graceful form and seasonal beauty, it adds both aesthetic and ecological value to gardens and parks.
