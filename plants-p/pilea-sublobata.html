Pilea sublobata

Pilea sublobata, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rounded, fleshy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a desirable addition to indoor gardens.
