Piper lanceifolium var. latifolium

Piper lanceifolium var. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf pepper plant, is a variant of the piper species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are followed by berries containing the aromatic seeds used in culinary and medicinal applications. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its unique flavor profile.

This variant is valued for its adaptability and potential health benefits.
