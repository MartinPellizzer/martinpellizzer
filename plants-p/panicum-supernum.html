Panicum supernum

Panicum supernum, commonly known as the sky grass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and delicate, feathery plumes. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and disturbed soils. Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its wide distribution.

The plant is often used in ornamental gardening for its elegant appearance and light, airy foliage.
