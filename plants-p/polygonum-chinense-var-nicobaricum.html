Polygonum chinense var. nicobaricum

Polygonum chinense var. nicobaricum, commonly known as the Nicobar morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from violet to blue. The plant is typically found in moist, shady environments such as forests and stream banks. It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes other species like buckwheat and sorrel.

The flowers bloom in the early morning, giving the plant its common name.
