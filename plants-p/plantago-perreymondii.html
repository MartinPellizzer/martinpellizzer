Plantago perreymondii

Plantago perreymondii, commonly known as the Perreymond's plantain, is a species of flowering plant in the plantain family.

It is native to the western United States, particularly found in California and Nevada. The plant has broad, oval-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. It produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, which are wind-pollinated.

This species is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.
