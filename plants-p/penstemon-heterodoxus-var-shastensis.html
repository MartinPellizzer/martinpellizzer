Penstemon heterodoxus var. shastensis

Penstemon heterodoxus var. shastensis, commonly known as Shasta penstemon, is a perennial flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white down.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to arid environments.
