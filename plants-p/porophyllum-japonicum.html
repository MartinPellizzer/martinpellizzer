Porophyllum japonicum

Porophyllum japonicum, commonly known as Japanese fleabane, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, yellow flowers that are surrounded by white ray florets.
