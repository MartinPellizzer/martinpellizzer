Pelargonium conspicuum

Pelargonium conspicuum, commonly known as the showy geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features upright stems and opposite, heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Pelargonium conspicuum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
