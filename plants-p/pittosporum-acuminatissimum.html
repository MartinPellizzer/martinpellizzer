Pittosporum acuminatissimum

Pittosporum acuminatissimum, commonly known as the slender pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features glossy, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and have a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by bright orange berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers a cool, moist climate.

Pittosporum acuminatissimum is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate partial shade.
