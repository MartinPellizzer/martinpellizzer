Phlomis × pabotii

Phlomis × pabotii, commonly known as the hybrid hybrid mullein, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features tall, upright stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, covered with soft, velvety leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flower clusters in late summer, which are composed of numerous small flowers arranged in a spherical shape. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a fuzzy texture, while its roots are fibrous and often used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
