Peucedanum linearilobum

Peucedanum linearilobum, commonly known as narrow-lobed parsley.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often aromatic and slightly hairy. Its small, yellowish-green flowers are arranged in umbels, typical of the Apiaceae family.

The seeds are small, ovoid, and have a distinctive ridged surface, often used in traditional medicine.
