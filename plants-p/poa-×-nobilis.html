Poa × nobilis

Poa × nobilis, commonly known as the silver hair grass, is a hybrid grass species belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and tufted growth habit, which gives it a distinctive appearance. This plant is native to parts of Europe and is often found in moist, well-drained soils in meadows and along stream banks. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping for its visual texture and color.

Poa × nobilis is also known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a versatile choice for garden settings.
