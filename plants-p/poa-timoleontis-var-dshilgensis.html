Poa timoleontis var. dshilgensis

Poa timoleontis var. dshilgensis, commonly known as the Dshilgen sedge, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and is adapted to temperate to cold climates. It is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.

Its botanical features make it an important component of high-altitude ecosystems.
