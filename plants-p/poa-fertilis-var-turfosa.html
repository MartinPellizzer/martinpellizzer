Poa fertilis var. turfosa

Poa fertilis var. turfosa, commonly known as the turf grass poa, is a variant of the species Poa fertilis.

It is characterized by its dense, low-growing habit and is often found in temperate regions. The plant typically forms a sod-like growth, making it suitable for turf applications. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue when mature.

This variety is valued for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and its resilience to moderate grazing.
