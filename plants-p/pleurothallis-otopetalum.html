Pleurothallis otopetalum

Pleurothallis otopetalum, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers with yellow petals and a distinctive white lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its beauty and rarity, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
