Pentaschistis minor

Pentaschistis minor, commonly known as the lesser pentaschistis, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant is native to regions of southern Africa, where it thrives in rocky or sandy soils. It has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is more commonly encountered in its natural wild state.
