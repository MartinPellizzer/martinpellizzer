Pyrethrum macrophyllum

Pyrethrum macrophyllum, commonly known as the large-leaved pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its high concentration of pyrethrins, which are natural insecticides. Pyrethrum macrophyllum thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its botanical features make it both aesthetically pleasing and ecologically valuable.
