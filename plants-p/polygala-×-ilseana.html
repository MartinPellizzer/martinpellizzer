Polygala × ilseana

Polygala × ilseana, commonly known as the hybrid milkvetch, is a flowering plant in the family Polygalaceae.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its slender, often branched stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces delicate, often yellow or greenish flowers that are arranged in spikes, typical of the genus Polygala. It is native to regions of southern Africa and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
