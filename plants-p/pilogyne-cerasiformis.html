Pilogyne cerasiformis

Pilogyne cerasiformis, commonly known as the cherry-shaped leaf plant, is a unique species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, rounded leaves that resemble small cherries, hence its common name. Pilogyne cerasiformis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in shaded, humid conditions.
