Pleurothallis hastata

Pleurothallis hastata, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically growing on tree branches or rocks. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and white, often resembling miniature orchids. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrate.

Pleurothallis hastata is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
