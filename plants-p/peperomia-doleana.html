Peperomia doleana

Peperomia doleana, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and compact growth habit. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, with a waxy texture and deep green coloration. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale green, emerging from the leaf axils.

This plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in moderate light conditions.
