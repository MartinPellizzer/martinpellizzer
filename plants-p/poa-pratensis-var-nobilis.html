Poa pratensis var. nobilis

Poa pratensis var. nobilis, commonly known as noble bluegrass, is a cool-season grass native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and dense, tufted growth habit. This variety is often used in turfgrass applications due to its durability and aesthetic appeal. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires moderate to high levels of sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping and golf course maintenance.
