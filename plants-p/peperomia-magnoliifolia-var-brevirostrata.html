Peperomia magnoliifolia var. brevirostrata

Peperomia magnoliifolia var. brevirostrata, commonly known as the broad-leaf peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with a waxy texture and a deep green color, often marked with subtle veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
