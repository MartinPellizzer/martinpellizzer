Potentilla elegans

Potentilla elegans, commonly known as the elegant cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the northern regions of North America.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, yellow, five-petaled flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or rocky slopes.

Potentilla elegans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
