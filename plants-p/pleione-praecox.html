Pleione praecox

Pleione praecox, commonly known as the early pleione, is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, deciduous growth habit and produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers with a distinctive white to pale pink coloration. The plant typically blooms in early spring, making it one of the earliest flowering orchids in its habitat. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its arid, high-altitude environment.

Pleione praecox is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts due to its beauty and rarity in cultivation.
