Peperomia langsdorffii var. increscens

Peperomia langsdorffii var. increscens, commonly known as the Silver Spire Peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to South America.

It features waxy, silver-green leaves that are slightly undulated and have a distinctive spire-like growth habit. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or brown, which are not showy but are important for its reproductive cycle. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and decorative purposes.
