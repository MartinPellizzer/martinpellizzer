Pentaphragma integrifolium

Pentaphragma integrifolium, commonly known as the smoothleaf bladderwort, is a small aquatic plant belonging to the family Lentibulariaceae.

It is characterized by its simple, entire leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers that typically bloom in early summer. Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a common sight in wetland environments.
