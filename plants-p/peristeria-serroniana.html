Peristeria serroniana

Peristeria serroniana, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that help it store water, adapting to its often arid habitat. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peristeria serroniana is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
