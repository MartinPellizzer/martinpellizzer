Phyteuma orbiculare var. ellipticum

Phyteuma orbiculare var. ellipticum, commonly known as the narrowleaf phyteuma, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces striking, showy flower heads composed of numerous small, tubular flowers arranged in a dense cluster. These flowers are usually pale yellow to orange in color and bloom in late summer.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
