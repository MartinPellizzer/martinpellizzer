Peperomia aldrinii

Peperomia aldrinii, commonly known as the Aldrin's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval or lance-shaped, with a vibrant green coloration. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia aldrinii is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
