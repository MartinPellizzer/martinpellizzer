Pleurothallis orthostachys

Pleurothallis orthostachys, commonly known as the erect-stem pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, slender stems and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. Its leaves are usually narrow and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in moisture retention.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
