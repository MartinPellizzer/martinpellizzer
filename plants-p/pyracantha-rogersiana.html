Pyracantha rogersiana

Pyracantha rogersiana, commonly known as the Chinese firethorn, is a shrub native to China and Korea.

It is characterized by its bright orange to red berries that persist through winter, providing food for birds and other wildlife. The plant has thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for hedges and ornamental gardens.

Its showy flowers in spring and vibrant berries in fall contribute to its ornamental value.
