Pittosporum chinense

Pittosporum chinense, commonly known as Chinese pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters tall, featuring dark green, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its adaptability and aesthetic appeal contribute to its use in landscaping and gardens.
