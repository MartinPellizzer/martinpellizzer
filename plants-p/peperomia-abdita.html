Peperomia abdita

Peperomia abdita, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically silver or gray-green in color, providing a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in hue. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia abdita is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
