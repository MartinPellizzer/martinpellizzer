Pilea elliptifolia

Pilea elliptifolia, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically bright green with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Pilea elliptifolia is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
