Peperomia pilosula

Peperomia pilosula, commonly known as the silky peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery or white fuzzy coating, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia pilosula is valued for its ornamental foliage and air-purifying qualities.
