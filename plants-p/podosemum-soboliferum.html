Podosemum soboliferum

Podosemum soboliferum, commonly known as the flameleaf hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, lobed leaves that range in color from bright red to deep orange, often changing with the seasons. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red, blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Podosemum soboliferum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
