Piper hieronymi

Piper hieronymi, commonly known as the Hieronymi pepper or the Madagascar pepper, is a tropical climbing vine native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by elongated, thin fruits that turn red when mature. The fruits are typically harvested when they are still tender and have a mild, peppery flavor.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of edible fruits in its native habitat.
