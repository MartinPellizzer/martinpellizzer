Picconia azorica

Picconia azorica, commonly known as the Azorean broom.

It is a shrub native to the Azores, characterized by its dense, thorny growth and bright yellow flowers. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to the humid, subtropical climate of its native region.

Picconia azorica is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
