Pelargonium balsameum

Pelargonium balsameum, commonly known as the balsam geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, velvety leaves and clusters of fragrant, pink or red flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners. It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and ease of cultivation.

Pelargonium balsameum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
