Paphiopedilum philippinense var. roebelenii

Paphiopedilum philippinense var. roebelenii, commonly known as the Philippine lady's slipper, is a rare and striking orchid native to the Philippines.

The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or rocks in the undergrowth of tropical forests. It has small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
