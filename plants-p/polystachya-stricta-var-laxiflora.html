Polystachya stricta var. laxiflora

Polystachya stricta var. laxiflora, commonly known as the loose-flowered bamboo orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and clustered inflorescences. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often arranged in loose, drooping clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated for its unique floral display.
