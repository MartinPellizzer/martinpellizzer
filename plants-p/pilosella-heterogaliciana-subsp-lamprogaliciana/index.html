Pilosella Heterogaliciana Subsp. Lamprogaliciana

Pilosella heterogaliciana subsp. lamprogaliciana is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of southern Europe. It is distinguished by its pale yellow to white blooms and finely hairy stems, which help it thrive in its unique habitat. This subspecies is often found in rocky or gravelly soils, where it adapts to harsh environmental conditions. Its limited distribution makes it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Protecting this plant is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its native ecosystems.
