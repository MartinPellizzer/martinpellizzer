Priva glaberrima

Priva glaberrima, commonly known as the smooth privet, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers, which are followed by small, black berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Priva glaberrima is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions.
