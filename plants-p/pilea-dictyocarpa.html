Pilea dictyocarpa

Pilea dictyocarpa, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its round, fleshy leaves that resemble small coins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.

Pilea dictyocarpa thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.
