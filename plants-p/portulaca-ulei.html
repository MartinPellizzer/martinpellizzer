Portulaca ulei

Portulaca ulei, commonly known as moss rose, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a succulent plant with fleshy, thick leaves that help it store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and yellow, which bloom throughout the growing season. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Portulaca ulei is also known for its ability to self-seed, ensuring its propagation in suitable environments.
