Piper multinervium var. hirsuticaule

Piper multinervium var. hirsuticaule, commonly known as the hairy-stemmed pepper plant, is a tropical species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, slender stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into elongated, berry-like fruits. It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for horticultural and ethnobotanical studies.
