Psorospermum febrifugum var. glabrum

Psorospermum febrifugum var. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth psorospermum, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous bark and lanceolate leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by yellowish, fleshy fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used traditionally for its fever-reducing effects.
