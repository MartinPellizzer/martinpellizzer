Plectranthus axillariflorus

Plectranthus axillariflorus, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and velvety foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Plectranthus axillariflorus is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
