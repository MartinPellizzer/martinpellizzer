Potentilla jiaozishanensis

Potentilla jiaozishanensis, commonly known as the Jiaozishan cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its small, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.
