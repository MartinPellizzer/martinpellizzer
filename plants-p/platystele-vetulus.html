Platystele vetulus

Platystele vetulus, commonly known as the slender ghost orchid, is a rare and delicate member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its small, elongated pseudobulbs and narrow, grass-like leaves. Native to the cloud forests of Central and South America, it thrives in shaded, humid environments with high rainfall.
