Piper spicilongum

Piper spicilongum, commonly known as longleaf pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves with elongated leaflets. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are followed by elliptical, dark blue berries.

The plant is valued for its aromatic foliage and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
