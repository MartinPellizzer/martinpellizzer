Panicum leptolomoides var. ambrense

Panicum leptolomoides var. ambrense, commonly known as the Ambrense grass, is a variant of the slender panic grass species.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to specific regions of South America, particularly in Argentina and Uruguay. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, fluffy inflorescences that are characteristic of the Panicum genus. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Its botanical structure makes it an interesting subject for ecological and taxonomic studies.
