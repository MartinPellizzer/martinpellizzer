Pithecellobium glaziovii

Pithecellobium glaziovii, commonly known as the Glaziov's pita, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. Its seeds are enclosed in hard, woody pods, which can remain on the plant for extended periods.

The species is valued for its ornamental potential and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
