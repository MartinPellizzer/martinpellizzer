Pyrus ulleungensis

Pyrus ulleungensis, commonly known as the Ulleung Island pear, is a rare species native to Ulleungdo, an island in the Sea of Japan.

It is a deciduous tree belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its broad leaves and small, edible fruits. The plant thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil and moderate rainfall. Its flowers are typically white or pink, appearing in early spring before the leaves emerge.
