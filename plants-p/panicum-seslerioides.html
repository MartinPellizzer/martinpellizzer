Panicum seslerioides

Panicum seslerioides, commonly known as the silver panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and silvery-gray foliage due to dense pubescence. The plant produces panicles of small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value comes from its striking foliage and drought tolerance.
