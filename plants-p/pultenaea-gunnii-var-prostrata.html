Pultenaea gunnii var. prostrata

Pultenaea gunnii var. prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate golden bush, is a low-growing shrub native to Australia.

It features small, narrow leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, giving it a textured appearance. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate coastal conditions.
