Papaya dodecaphylla

Papaya dodecaphylla, commonly known as the Christmas star or star fruit, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped fruit, which turns yellow when ripe. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers. Its fruit is valued for its sweet, tangy flavor and is often consumed fresh or used in culinary applications.

Botanically, it belongs to the Caricaceae family and is closely related to the papaya.
