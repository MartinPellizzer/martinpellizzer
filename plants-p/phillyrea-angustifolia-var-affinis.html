Phillyrea angustifolia var. affinis

Phillyrea angustifolia var. affinis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Phillyrea, is a shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, which are followed by elliptical, woody fruits. It is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas and scrublands.

Phillyrea angustifolia var. affinis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
