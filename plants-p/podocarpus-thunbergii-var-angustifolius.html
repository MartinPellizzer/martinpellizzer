Podocarpus thunbergii var. angustifolius

Podocarpus thunbergii var. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Christmas tree, is a coniferous evergreen shrub or small tree native to East Asia.

It features dense, pyramidal growth with dark green, glossy, lance-shaped leaves that are narrow and pointed. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by woody cones that release seeds. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping due to its hardiness and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.

The species is also appreciated for its aromatic foliage and resistance to pests and diseases.
