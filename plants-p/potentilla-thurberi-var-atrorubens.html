Potentilla thurberi var. atrorubens

Potentilla thurberi var. atrorubens, commonly known as the black-red potentilla, is a shrub native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its distinctive dark red to black foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value comes from its striking foliage and hardy nature.
