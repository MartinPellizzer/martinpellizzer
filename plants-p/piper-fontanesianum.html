Piper fontanesianum

Piper fontanesianum, commonly known as the Fontanesian pepper, is a tropical climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when ripe, and they are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are also utilized in herbal remedies due to their aromatic properties.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the undergrowth of tropical forests.
