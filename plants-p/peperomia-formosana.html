Peperomia formosana

Peperomia formosana, commonly known as the Formosa Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Taiwan.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a subtle reddish or purple hue on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually pink or red, adding a touch of color to its foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia formosana is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and tolerating a range of environmental conditions.
