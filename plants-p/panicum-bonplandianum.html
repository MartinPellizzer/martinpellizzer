Panicum bonplandianum

Panicum bonplandianum, commonly known as the Bonpland grass, is a species of grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, erect stems. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, two-lobed florets. It thrives in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the flora in its native range.
