Pteris patula

Pteris patula, commonly known as the shield fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The fronds are typically dark green and have a smooth, glossy surface. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Pteris patula is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
