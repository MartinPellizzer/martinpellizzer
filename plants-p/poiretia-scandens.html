Poiretia scandens

Poiretia scandens, commonly known as the climbing poiretia, is a perennial vine native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, which bloom in clusters. Its climbing habit allows it to grow along trellises or other supports.

Poiretia scandens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to provide dense foliage.
