Potentilla recta var. pragensis

Potentilla recta var. pragensis, commonly known as the Pragmatic potentilla, is a perennial shrub native to Central Europe.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine regions. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh environments.
