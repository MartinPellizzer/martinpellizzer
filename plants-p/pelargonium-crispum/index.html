Pelargonium Crispum

Crisp geranium, scientifically known as Pelargonium crispum, is a strikingly beautiful plant native to South Africa. With its unique, wavy leaves and vibrant, showy flowers, it stands out in any garden or indoor space. This species is prized for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of conditions. Its foliage is often described as crisp and textured, adding a distinctive visual element. Whether grown in pots or in the ground, Pelargonium crispum is a versatile and appealing choice for plant lovers.
