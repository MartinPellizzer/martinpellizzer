Photinia glabra var. chinensis

Photinia glabra var. chinensis, commonly known as Chinese photinia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that provide a striking visual contrast against its showy, pinkish-red inflorescences. The plant produces small, white flowers that develop into red, fleshy berries, which are a food source for birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its dense growth habit also makes it suitable for hedges or as a specimen plant in landscaped areas.
