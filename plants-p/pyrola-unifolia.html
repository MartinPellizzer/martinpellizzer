Pyrola unifolia

Pyrola unifolia, commonly known as the white wintergreen, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the northern hemisphere.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its evergreen leaves and showy, bell-shaped flowers. Pyrola unifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
