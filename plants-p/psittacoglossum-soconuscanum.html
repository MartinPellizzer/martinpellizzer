Psittacoglossum soconuscanum

Psittacoglossum soconuscanum, commonly known as the red-tipped nettle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Urticaceae.

It is characterized by its upright, hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves with reddish veins. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically wind-pollinated.
