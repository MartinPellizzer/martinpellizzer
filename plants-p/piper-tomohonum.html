Piper tomohonum

Piper tomohonum, commonly known as Tomohon pepper, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Indonesia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are followed by elongated, cylindrical fruits that mature to a reddish color.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in local traditional medicine.
