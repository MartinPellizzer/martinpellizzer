Potentilla × angarensis

Potentilla × angarensis, commonly known as the Siberian cinquefoil, is a hybrid shrub native to the cold regions of Siberia and northern Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and features dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or alpine gardens.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for cold-climate landscapes.
