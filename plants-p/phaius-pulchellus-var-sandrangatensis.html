Phaius pulchellus var. sandrangatensis

Phaius pulchellus var. sandrangatensis, commonly known as the Sandrangatensis Spathe Orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, showy, pale yellow to cream-colored spathe that encloses a greenish-yellow spadix. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded forest environments, where it thrives in well-drained, rich soil. Its botanical features include a rhizomatous growth habit and broad, elliptical leaves.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
