Polygala ligularis

Polygala ligularis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers clustered in racemes. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and opposite, often with a slightly hairy texture.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
