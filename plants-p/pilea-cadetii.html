Pilea cadetii

Pilea cadetii, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped foliage, with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically a vibrant green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular indoor plant.

Pilea cadetii is also noted for its ability to purify the air and is relatively easy to care for.
