Peperomia tsakiana

Peperomia tsakiana, commonly known as the Shiny Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces tiny, reddish-brown flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia tsakiana is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
