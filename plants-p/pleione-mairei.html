Pleione mairei

Pleione mairei, commonly known as the Maire's orchid, is a rare and elegant orchid native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on rocky slopes or in forest understories. Its rhizomes are tuberous, and it produces large, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green in color.

Due to its beauty and rarity, Pleione mairei is highly valued in horticulture and conservation efforts.
