Polyrhabda

Polyrhabda, commonly known as the narrow-leafed thistle, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and composite flower heads that typically consist of yellow disk flowers surrounded by ray florets. The plant's structure includes a stout, woody stem with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
