Primula shweli-calliantha

Primula shweli-calliantha, commonly known as the Shweli Calliantha primrose, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a compact growth habit. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas at higher elevations.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
