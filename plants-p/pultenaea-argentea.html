Pultenaea argentea

Pultenaea argentea, commonly known as the silver pultenaea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal regions, characterized by its silvery-gray foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often appearing in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and coastal dunes.

Pultenaea argentea is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
