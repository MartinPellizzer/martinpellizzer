Pilea pauciflora

Pilea pauciflora, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features glossy, round leaves that are often described as resembling small coins, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea pauciflora is relatively low maintenance and can be propagated easily through stem cuttings.
