Phyllolobium sanbilingense

Phyllolobium sanbilingense, commonly known as the Sanbiling grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, particularly China. The plant is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets that are clustered at the base of the plant.

Phyllolobium sanbilingense is often found in moist, well-drained soils in open habitats such as grasslands and forest edges.
