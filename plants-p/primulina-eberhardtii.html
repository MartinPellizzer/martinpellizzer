Primulina eberhardtii

Primulina eberhardtii, commonly known as the Eberhardt's primula, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers.
