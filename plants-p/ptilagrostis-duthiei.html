Ptilagrostis duthiei

Ptilagrostis duthiei, commonly known as the Duthie's hairgrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, hair-like leaves and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along stream banks. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of spikelets, which are small and often hidden among the foliage.

Ptilagrostis duthiei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
