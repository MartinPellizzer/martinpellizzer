Pilea matsudae

Pilea matsudae, commonly known as the Shingle Plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to China.

It features dense clusters of small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Pilea matsudae is popular as a low-maintenance indoor plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
