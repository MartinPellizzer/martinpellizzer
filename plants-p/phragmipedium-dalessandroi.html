Phragmipedium dalessandroi

Phragmipedium dalessandroi, commonly known as the Dalessandro's boat orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its pendulous, boat-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with dark red or purple markings. The plant grows as an epiphyte, often found on trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, and it produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

This orchid is prized for its unique floral structure and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
