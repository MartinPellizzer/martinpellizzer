Peperomia choroniana var. puberulenta

Peperomia choroniana var. puberulenta, commonly known as the velvety peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture, giving it a unique appearance. The leaves are typically dark green and emit a mild, aromatic scent when touched. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

This variety is noted for its resilience and ease of care, making it suitable for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
