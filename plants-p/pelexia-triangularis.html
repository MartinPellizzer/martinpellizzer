Pelexia triangularis

Pelexia triangularis, commonly known as the triangular pelexia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive triangular-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Pelexia triangularis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and easy maintenance in garden settings.
