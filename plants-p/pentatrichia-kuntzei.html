Pentatrichia kuntzei

Pentatrichia kuntzei, commonly known as the Kuntze's grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa, particularly in Namibia and Angola. The plant is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Pentatrichia kuntzei is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with low nutrient soils.
