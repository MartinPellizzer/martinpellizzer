Protea welwitschii

Protea welwitschii, commonly known as the Welwitsch protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence composed of numerous white to pinkish flowers arranged in a spherical or conical shape. The plant is native to the Eastern Cape and is often found in rocky, well-drained soils. Its unique appearance makes it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and botanical collections.

Protea welwitschii is also valued for its ecological role, providing nectar for pollinators and contributing to the biodiversity of its native habitat.
