Panicum mlahiense

Panicum mlahiense, commonly known as the Mlahi grass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open areas, such as grasslands and disturbed soils, and thrives in full sun. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny seeds.

Botanically, it is part of the Poaceae family, which includes many other grass species.
