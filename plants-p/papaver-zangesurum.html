Papaver zangesurum

Papaver zangesurum, commonly known as the Zanghese poppy, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red to orange petals and a prominent, rounded seed pod. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its botanical features include a basal rosette of leaves and a tall, slender stem that bears the showy flowers.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
