Premna rufidula

Premna rufidula, commonly known as the red-stemmed premna, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, including India, Sri Lanka, and parts of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its reddish-brown stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in clusters.

Premna rufidula is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
