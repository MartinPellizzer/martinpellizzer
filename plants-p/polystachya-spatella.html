Polystachya spatella

Polystachya spatella, commonly known as the slender flame orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa and southern Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and clusters of showy, red or orange flowers that resemble flames. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and forest edges. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette at the base of the stem.

Polystachya spatella is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
