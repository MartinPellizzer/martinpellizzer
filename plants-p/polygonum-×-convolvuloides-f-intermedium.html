Polygonum × convolvuloides f. intermedium

Polygonum × convolvuloides f. intermedium, commonly known as the intermediate knotweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Polygonaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive reddish sheen. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or reddish in color. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found along riverbanks and in wetlands.

Due to its aggressive growth, it can become invasive in certain environments.
