Phalaris californica

Phalaris californica, commonly known as California grassvetch, is a perennial herb native to coastal areas of California and Baja California.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces spikes of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils along the coastline. It is adapted to coastal conditions, including salt spray and strong winds.

Phalaris californica plays a role in stabilizing dune ecosystems and supporting local wildlife.
