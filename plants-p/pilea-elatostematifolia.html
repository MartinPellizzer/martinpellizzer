Pilea elatostematifolia

Pilea elatostematifolia, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features round, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Pilea elatostematifolia is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
