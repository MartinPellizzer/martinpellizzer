Pennisetum sagittifolium

Pennisetum sagittifolium, commonly known as swordleaf grass, is a tall, ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features slender, arching blades with a distinctive sword-like shape, often reaching heights of 3 to 6 feet. The plant produces panicles of small, fluffy flowers that bloom in late summer, adding a soft, feathery texture to the landscape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its foliage is evergreen, providing year-round visual interest.
