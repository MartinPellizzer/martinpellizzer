Panicum agrostidiforme

Panicum agrostidiforme, commonly known as the slender panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or open fields.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
