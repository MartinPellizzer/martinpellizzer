Polygala comosa var. hybrida

Polygala comosa var. hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid spurred orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Polygalaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that typically range in color from white to pink, with some varieties exhibiting subtle purple or red hues. The plant is native to regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of China and India, and thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture, while the stems are slender and branching.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
