Phacelia ramosissima var. latifolia

Phacelia ramosissima var. latifolia, commonly known as broadleaf phacelia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its bushy growth habit and profuse display of blue to purple flowers. The leaves are typically broad and opposite, with a soft, fuzzy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for native gardens.

This plant plays an important role in supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
