Pinus mughus var. laeta

Pinus mughus var. laeta, commonly known as the Kashmir pine, is a variant of the Himalayan blue pine.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of the western Himalayas, including parts of Jammu and Kashmir. The tree is characterized by its slender, often twisted trunk and its distinctive blue-green needles. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is adapted to rocky and mountainous terrain.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in local ecosystems.
