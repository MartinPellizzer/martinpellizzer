Polyosma robusta

Polyosma robusta, commonly known as the robust mountain tea, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern China and northern Vietnam.

It belongs to the family Theaceae and is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are elliptical and have a glossy surface. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in spring. Its bark is thick and rugged, often exuding a mild aromatic scent.
