Parodia backebergiana

Parodia backebergiana, commonly known as the Backberg Cactus, is a species of cactus native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its globular shape and smooth, pale green to blue-gray skin. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding a striking contrast to its foliage. It is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice for succulent collectors and arid garden landscapes.
