Piper fuscescentispicum

Piper fuscescentispicum, commonly known as the dark-stemmed pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and small, clustered white flowers. It produces small, round berries that are typically green when unripe and turn red when mature.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated for its unique foliage.
