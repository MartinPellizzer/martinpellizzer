Phlox costata

Phlox costata, commonly known as the smooth phlox, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of pink to purple. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both ornamental and ecological value.
