Pleurothallis costata

Pleurothallis costata, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant features slender, often twisted stems and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Pleurothallis costata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded, humid environments.
