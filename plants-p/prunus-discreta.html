Prunus discreta

Prunus discreta, commonly known as the Japanese white cherry, is a small deciduous tree native to Japan.

It is characterized by its delicate white flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves appear. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Prunus discreta is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and parks.
