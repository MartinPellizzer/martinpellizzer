Pilea tabularis

Pilea tabularis, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are round and slightly wavy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is a popular indoor plant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.

Pilea tabularis thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
