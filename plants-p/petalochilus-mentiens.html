Petalochilus mentiens

Petalochilus mentiens, commonly known as the Mentien's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, often arranged in a fan shape.

The orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
