Pimpinella apiodora

Pimpinella apiodora, commonly known as the narrowleaf cow-weed, is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic, often fern-like foliage.

Pimpinella apiodora is native to North America and is often found in the eastern United States.
