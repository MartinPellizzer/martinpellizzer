Pulicaria jaubertii

Pulicaria jaubertii, commonly known as the Algerian groundsel, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub with silver-gray foliage and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has small, narrow leaves and produces numerous small, dandelion-like flowers that attract pollinators. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates.

Pulicaria jaubertii is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
