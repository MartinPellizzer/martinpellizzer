Pycnobregma

Pycnobregma, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to tropical Africa.

The leaves are typically rounded and often have a fuzzy texture, contributing to the plant's ornamental appeal. Pycnobregma species are popular as houseplants due to their ability to thrive in low-light conditions and their ease of care.
