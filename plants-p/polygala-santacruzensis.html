Polygala santacruzensis

Polygala santacruzensis, commonly known as the Santa Cruz milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of California.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils along the shoreline. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. It has compound leaves with slender, thread-like leaflets.

Polygala santacruzensis is adapted to coastal conditions, including salt spray and strong winds.
