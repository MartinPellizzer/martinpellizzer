Paphiopedilum Leucochilum

Paphiopedilum leucochilum is a rare and captivating orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its striking white petals and vivid green sepals, this plant exhibits a unique beauty that sets it apart from other orchid species. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires careful cultivation to flourish. Due to its limited distribution and habitat destruction, it is considered a threatened species. This orchid not only fascinates botanists but also holds cultural significance in its native regions.
