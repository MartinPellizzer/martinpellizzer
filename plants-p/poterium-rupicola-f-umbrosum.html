Poterium rupicola f. umbrosum

Poterium rupicola f. umbrosum, commonly known as the shadowy rock cress, is a rare and intriguing plant species characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green foliage.

It thrives in rocky, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions with well-drained soil. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a delicate touch to its otherwise subdued appearance. Its leaves are typically broad and leathery, adapted to conserve moisture in its arid habitat.
