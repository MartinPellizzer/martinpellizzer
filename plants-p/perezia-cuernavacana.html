Perezia cuernavacana

Perezia cuernavacana, commonly known as the Cuernavaca orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in montane forests. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, and the flowers are usually pink or white with a distinctive yellow lip.

Perezia cuernavacana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
