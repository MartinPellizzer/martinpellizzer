Peperomia ramosa

Peperomia ramosa, commonly known as the Shiny Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features upright stems with dense clusters of small, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces delicate, spike-like inflorescences with tiny, white to pale green flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and attractive foliage, it is often used as a decorative plant in both home and office settings.
