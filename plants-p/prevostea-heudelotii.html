Prevostea heudelotii

Prevostea heudelotii, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow and green floral display. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, utilizing its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the environment. Its flowers are clustered in loose inflorescences, with each bloom featuring a distinctive star-shaped pattern.

Prevostea heudelotii thrives in humid, shaded conditions and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
