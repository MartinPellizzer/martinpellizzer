Poa conceptionis

Poa conceptionis, commonly known as the Concepcion grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Chile and Argentina, and thrives in temperate climates. The plant is characterized by its tufted growth habit, with slender, wiry stems and small, narrow leaves. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of spikelets, which are typically green to reddish in color.

Poa conceptionis is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil in marginal environments.
