Phyllobolus splendens

Phyllobolus splendens, commonly known as the splendid leaf beetle, is a species of leaf beetle belonging to the family Chrysomelidae.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often metallic green or blue coloration, which serves as both a warning signal and a means of camouflage. The plant it feeds on, typically species of the genus *Phyllanthus*, is a key aspect of its ecological niche. Its larvae primarily consume the leaves of these plants, contributing to their population dynamics.

The beetle's life cycle and feeding habits make it an important component of the ecosystems in which it resides.
