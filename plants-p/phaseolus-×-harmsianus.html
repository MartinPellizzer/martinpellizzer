Phaseolus × harmsianus

Phaseolus × harmsianus, commonly known as the scarlet runner bean, is a flowering plant in the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its vigorous climbing habit and large, showy flowers that range in color from white to red. The plant produces elongated, edible pods that contain kidney-shaped beans, which are rich in protein and nutrients. Native to the Americas, it thrives in warm climates and is widely cultivated for both culinary and ornamental purposes.

Its botanical features make it an important species in agricultural and horticultural contexts.
