Phreatia jayaweerae

Phreatia jayaweerae, commonly known as the Jayaweera's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the genus Phreatia, which is part of the orchid family Orchidaceae. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest understories, where it thrives in humid and cool conditions. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a distinctive coloration that ranges from white to pale yellow.

The species is named in honor of Dr. R. Jayaweera, a botanist who contributed significantly to the study of Sri Lankan flora.
