Poa rangkulensis

Poa rangkulensis, commonly known as the Rangkul grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to New Zealand and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, erect stems. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences with pale green to brownish flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along streambanks.

Poa rangkulensis plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for invertebrates.
