Pilea rapensis

Pilea rapensis, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen herbaceous plant native to China.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically bright green, often with a slight variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by round, papery seed capsules. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea rapensis is known for its easy care and ability to adapt well to a variety of indoor environments.
