Pteronia lucilioides

Pteronia lucilioides, commonly known as the Cape bell, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and pink to red petals. The plant typically grows in coastal regions and thrives in well-drained sandy soils. It produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind, aiding in its propagation.

Pteronia lucilioides is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and coastal landscapes.
