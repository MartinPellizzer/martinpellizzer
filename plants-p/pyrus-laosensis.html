Pyrus laosensis

Pyrus laosensis, commonly known as the Laos pear, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Laos, Cambodia, and Vietnam. The plant is characterized by its deciduous nature, producing small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring. Its fruit, which resembles a pear, is typically small and has a sweet, aromatic flavor.

The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine in its native regions.
