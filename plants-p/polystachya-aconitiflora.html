Polystachya aconitiflora

Polystachya aconitiflora, commonly known as the blue orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, deep blue to violet flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are typically elongated and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Polystachya aconitiflora thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
