Podochilus taiwanianus

Podochilus taiwanianus, commonly known as the Taiwan orchid, is a species of orchid native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant is an epiphyte, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, often found in tropical and subtropical forests. Its leaves are long and strap-like, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Podochilus taiwanianus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
