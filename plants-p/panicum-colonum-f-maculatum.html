Panicum colonum f. maculatum

Panicum colonum f. maculatum, commonly known as the red fountain grass, is a ornamental grass characterized by its striking red and bronze colored foliage.

It features slender, upright stems that produce fluffy, silver-white plumes during the flowering season. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions and thrives in full sun with well-draining soil. It is often used in landscaping for its visual appeal and ability to add texture to gardens.

Its foliage turns reddish in cooler temperatures, making it a popular choice for seasonal displays.
