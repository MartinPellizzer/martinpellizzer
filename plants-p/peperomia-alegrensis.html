Peperomia alegrensis

Peperomia alegrensis, commonly known as the Brazilian peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to Brazil.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically green with reddish veins, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, often in shades of white or pale pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia alegrensis is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
