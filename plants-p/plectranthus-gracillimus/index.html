Plectranthus Gracillimus

Purple heart plant, scientifically known as Plectranthus gracillimus, is a strikingly beautiful and hardy member of the mint family. Native to South Africa, this plant is celebrated for its vibrant purple and pink foliage that adds a unique pop of color to any indoor or outdoor space. Its compact size and trailing growth habit make it an ideal choice for hanging baskets, window sills, or as a decorative accent in gardens. Easy to care for, it thrives in bright indirect light and requires minimal watering, making it a favorite among plant enthusiasts. With its eye-catching appearance and low maintenance requirements, the Purple heart plant is a versatile and rewarding addition to any plant collection.
