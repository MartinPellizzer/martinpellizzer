Peperomia monteverdensis

Peperomia monteverdensis, commonly known as the Monteverde peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the cloud forests of Costa Rica.

It features small, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and a dark green coloration. The plant produces tiny, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Peperomia monteverdensis is appreciated for its ornamental value and ease of care in indoor settings.
