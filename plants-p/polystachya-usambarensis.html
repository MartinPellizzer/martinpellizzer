Polystachya usambarensis

Polystachya usambarensis, commonly known as the Usambarensis Sword Fern, is a terrestrial orchid native to the high-altitude regions of East Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and long, linear leaves that resemble sword blades, giving it its common name. The plant produces striking, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in montane forests.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
