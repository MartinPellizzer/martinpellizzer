Pistorinia breviflora var. rubella

Pistorinia breviflora var. rubella, commonly known as the red-flowered pistorinia, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, showy red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax, giving it a unique appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by gardeners interested in indigenous flora.
