Paphiopedilum moquetteanum f. flavoviride

Paphiopedilum moquetteanum f. flavoviride, commonly known as the green-flowered moth orchid, is a rare and distinctive orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated flower spikes and striking greenish-yellow flowers with a velvety texture. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial or epiphytic orchid, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually broad and dark green, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

This hybrid variety is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and rarity.
