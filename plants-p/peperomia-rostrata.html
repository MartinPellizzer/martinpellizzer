Peperomia rostrata

Peperomia rostrata, commonly known as the Rosy Periwinkle, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are dark green with a slight waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, often resembling a rosette. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia rostrata is known for its low maintenance and ability to adapt well to various indoor environments.
