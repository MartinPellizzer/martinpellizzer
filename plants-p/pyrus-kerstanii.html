Pyrus kerstanii

Pyrus kerstanii, commonly known as the Kerstan's pear, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Vietnam, Cambodia, and Thailand. The plant is characterized by its deciduous nature, producing small, yellowish flowers in early spring. Its fruit, a small pear-like berry, is edible but not widely cultivated for commercial use.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological role in its native habitats.
