Piper guineense var. velutinum

Piper guineense var. velutinum, commonly known as African pepper or velvet pepper, is a tropical vine native to West Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by black, peppery berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both traditional medicine and horticulture.
