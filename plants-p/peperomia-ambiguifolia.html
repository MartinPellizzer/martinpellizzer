Peperomia ambiguifolia

Peperomia ambiguifolia, commonly known as the Variegated Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with striking white or silver variegation, which add visual interest to any indoor space. The plant has small, inconspicuous flowers that appear in clusters, typically in shades of green or red. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
