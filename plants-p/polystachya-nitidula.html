Polystachya nitidula

Polystachya nitidula, commonly known as the glossy-leafed spiraling orchid, is a species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and along stream banks. Its leaves are usually smooth and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Polystachya nitidula is often cultivated for its unique appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
