Potentilla cardotiana var. nepalensis

Potentilla cardotiana var. nepalensis, commonly known as the Nepal cinquefoil, is a perennial shrub native to the Himalayan regions of Nepal and northern India.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has trifoliate leaves with serrated edges and thrives in well-drained, rocky soils. It is often found in alpine meadows and forest edges at high elevations.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
