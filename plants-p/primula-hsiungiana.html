Primula hsiungiana

Primula hsiungiana, commonly known as the Hsiung's primrose, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Primula hsiungiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
