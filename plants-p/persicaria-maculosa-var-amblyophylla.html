Persicaria maculosa var. amblyophylla

Persicaria maculosa var. amblyophylla, commonly known as spotted knotweed, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features upright stems with heart-shaped leaves that have a distinctive purplish tinge and reddish veins. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and disturbed areas.

Its rapid growth and spreading habit make it an invasive species in many regions.
