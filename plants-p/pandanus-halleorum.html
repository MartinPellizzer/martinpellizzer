Pandanus halleorum

Pandanus halleorum, commonly known as the Halle's pandanus, is a flowering plant native to the Indo-Pacific region.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with fragrant, yellowish flowers. Its roots are sometimes used in local medicine for their purported healing properties.

Overall, Pandanus halleorum is an important species in both ecological and cultural contexts.
