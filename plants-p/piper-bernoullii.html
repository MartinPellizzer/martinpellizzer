Piper bernoullii

Piper bernoullii, commonly known as the Bornean black pepper, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its vine-like growth habit and compound leaves with serrated edges. Its small, green flowers are followed by berries that mature to a dark purple or black color.

Piper bernoullii is valued for its aromatic fruits, which are used in traditional medicine and as a spice.
