Pleuropogon Californicus

Pleuropogon californicus is a striking grass species native to the western United States. Known for its vibrant blue-green foliage and tall, slender stalks, it thrives in sunny, well-drained soils. This plant is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is a popular choice for xeriscaping due to its low water needs. Its unique appearance makes it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively underappreciated compared to more common ornamental grasses.
