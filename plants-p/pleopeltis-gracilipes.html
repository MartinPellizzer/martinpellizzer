Pleopeltis gracilipes

Pleopeltis gracilipes, commonly known as the slender-leaf wall fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on tree trunks and rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has long, slender fronds with a delicate, feathery appearance. Its leaves are usually bright green and can reach lengths of up to 1 meter.

Pleopeltis gracilipes is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
