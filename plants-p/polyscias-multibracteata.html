Polyscias multibracteata

Polyscias multibracteata, commonly known as the broad-leaved glory bush, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
