Peperomia violacea

Peperomia violacea, commonly known as the violet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a subtle violet or purple tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal water needs.

Peperomia violacea is valued for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for ornamental purposes.
