Phellodendron chinense var. chinense

Phellodendron chinense var. chinense, commonly known as Chinese yellow tree, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark that often exudes a yellowish sap. The leaves are alternate, simple, and have a serrated margin, while the flowers are small and yellowish-green, appearing in clusters. The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

This species is valued for its medicinal properties, with the bark containing compounds used in traditional Chinese medicine.
