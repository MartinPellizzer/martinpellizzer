Polygonum flavescens

Polygonum flavescens, commonly known as the yellow polygonum, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants commonly referred to as buckwheats. The plant is characterized by its smooth, succulent stems and heart-shaped leaves with a bluish-green hue. It produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to red, depending on the variety.

Polygonum flavescens is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.
