Ptychosperma lineare

Ptychosperma lineare, commonly known as the slender cycad, is a species of cycad native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched stem and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a dense, globular inflorescence. Its seeds are enclosed in a fibrous, woody capsule.

Ptychosperma lineare is a slow-growing plant that thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.
