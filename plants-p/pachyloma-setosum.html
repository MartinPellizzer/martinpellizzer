Pachyloma setosum

Pachyloma setosum, commonly known as the hairy pachyloma, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. The plant features small, fleshy leaves that are covered in fine, hair-like structures, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a vibrant touch to its appearance.

Due to its unique morphology and adaptability to arid conditions, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
