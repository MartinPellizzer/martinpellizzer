Poa plurinodis

Poa plurinodis, commonly known as the nodding meadow grass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, often nodding inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and flat, with a bluish-green coloration.

Poa plurinodis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting biodiversity.
