Pelargonium × victorianum

Pelargonium × victorianum, commonly known as the Victorian geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers that come in a range of colors, including red, pink, and white. The leaves are typically rounded with a slightly hairy surface, and the plant produces distinctive seed pods that split open to release tiny seeds.

Native to South Africa, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
