Pomaderris helianthemifolia subsp. minor

Pomaderris helianthemifolia subsp.

minor, commonly known as the small-flowered pomaderris, is a shrub native to southern Australia. It features small, yellowish-green leaves that are opposite and oval in shape.

The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is typically found in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native gardens.
