Polystachya guatemalensis

Polystachya guatemalensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan spike orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments such as cloud forests. Its leaves are elongated and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Polystachya guatemalensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
