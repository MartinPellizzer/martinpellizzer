Peperomia aceroana

Peperomia aceroana, commonly known as the maple-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, maple-like leaves that are typically deep green with reddish veins, giving it a striking visual appeal. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that are rarely seen above the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia aceroana is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
