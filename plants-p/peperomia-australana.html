Peperomia australana

Peperomia australana, commonly known as the Australian peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Australia.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval to lance-shaped, often with a bluish-green or grayish hue. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, usually greenish-white in color, which are followed by distinctive, three-lobed seed capsules. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia australana is valued for its ornamental foliage and low maintenance requirements.
