Panicum malacodes

Panicum malacodes, commonly known as the red panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and produces dense clusters of small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Panicum malacodes is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and resilience to poor soil conditions.
