Pleurothallis orygmoglossa

Pleurothallis orygmoglossa, commonly known as the golden lip orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small epiphytic orchid with elongated, narrow leaves and delicate, yellowish-green flowers that resemble lips. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are often fragrant and bloom during the warmer months.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
