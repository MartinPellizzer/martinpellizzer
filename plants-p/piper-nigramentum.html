Piper nigramentum

Piper nigramentum, commonly known as black pepper plant, is a flowering vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its small, dark purple berries that mature into the familiar black peppercorns. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small white flowers. Its fruits are harvested and dried to develop the characteristic pungent flavor.

Piper nigramentum is widely cultivated for its culinary and medicinal uses.
