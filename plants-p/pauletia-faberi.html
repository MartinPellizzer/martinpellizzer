Pauletia faberi

Pauletia faberi, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a prominent midrib. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white in color. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Pauletia faberi is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its attractive foliage and air-purifying properties.
