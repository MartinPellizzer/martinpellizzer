Pilosella caespitosa subsp. stenocephala

Pilosella caespitosa subsp.

stenocephala, commonly known as the narrow-headed cat's-paw, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its compact, tufted growth habit and small, daisy-like yellow flowers.

The plant has linear to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.

The subspecies is distinguished by its narrower flower heads compared to other members of the species.
