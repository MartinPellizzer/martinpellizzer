Photinia wuyishanensis

Photinia wuyishanensis, commonly known as the Wuyishan photinia, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically the Wuyi Mountains region, and is characterized by its dense, glossy foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, which are followed by red, fleshy fruits. It is typically found in moist, shaded forest environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
