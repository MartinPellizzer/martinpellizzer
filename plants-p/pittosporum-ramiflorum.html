Pittosporum ramiflorum

Pittosporum ramiflorum, commonly known as the ramiflorum pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that appear in clusters. The plant has smooth, dark green leaves that are lance-shaped and often have a waxy texture. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Pittosporum ramiflorum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
