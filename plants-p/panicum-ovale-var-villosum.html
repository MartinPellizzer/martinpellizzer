Panicum ovale var. villosum

Panicum ovale var. villosum, commonly known as silky panicgrass, is a variant of the panic grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its fine, silky hairs covering the leaves and stems, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its inflorescence consists of a dense, cylindrical cluster of spikelets, which are often green or reddish in color.

This grass is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping or as a component in native plant gardens.
