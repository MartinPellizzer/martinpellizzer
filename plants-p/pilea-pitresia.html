Pilea pitresia

Pilea pitresia, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features rosettes of thick, waxy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellowish-green flowers that are followed by round, fleshy fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea pitresia is known for its compact growth habit and air-purifying qualities.
