Parthenice mollis var. mollis

Parthenice mollis var. mollis, commonly known as silky blueweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to North America.

It features fine, silver-gray foliage covered in dense, soft hairs that give it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and wetlands.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
