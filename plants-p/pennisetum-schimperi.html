Pennisetum schimperi

Pennisetum schimperi, commonly known as the silver fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to eastern Africa.

It features tall, slender stalks that produce fluffy, white plumes, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery down, contributing to its ornamental value. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Pennisetum schimperi is often used in ornamental horticulture for its visual appeal and drought tolerance.
