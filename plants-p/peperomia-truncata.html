Peperomia truncata

Peperomia truncata, commonly known as the rounded peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically rounded or heart-shaped, with a waxy texture and a variety of colors ranging from deep green to variegated patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, usually green or reddish, which are followed by bright red berries. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and drought tolerance.

Peperomia truncata is valued for its ornamental foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for tabletop gardens and shaded indoor spaces.
