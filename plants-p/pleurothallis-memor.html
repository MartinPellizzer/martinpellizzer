Pleurothallis memor

Pleurothallis memor, commonly known as the memory orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom during the wet season. Its leaves are usually narrow and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

Pleurothallis memor is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
