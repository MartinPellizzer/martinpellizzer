Pterocarpus brenanii

Pterocarpus brenanii, commonly known as the African rosewood, is a tropical tree native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its dark, aromatic wood, which is highly valued for furniture and musical instruments. The plant has compound leaves with long, slender leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers. Its fruit is a woody pod containing several seeds.

The tree is also known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
