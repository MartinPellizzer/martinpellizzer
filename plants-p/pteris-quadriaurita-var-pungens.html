Pteris quadriaurita var. pungens

Pteris quadriaurita var. pungens, commonly known as the spiny water fern, is a variant of the broad-leafed water fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is a fern that typically grows in wet, shaded environments such as marshes and along stream banks. The plant features fronds with a distinctive, serrated edge that gives it its common name. Its leaves are usually dark green with a glossy appearance and can reach lengths of up to 1 meter.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in aquatic gardens or as a houseplant.
