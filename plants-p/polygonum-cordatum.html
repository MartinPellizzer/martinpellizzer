Polygonum cordatum

Polygonum cordatum, commonly known as heartleaf polygonum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, depending on the variety. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Polygonum cordatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
