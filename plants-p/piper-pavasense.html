Piper pavasense

Piper pavasense, commonly known as the Pava pepper or Pava vine, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are followed by elongated, spindle-shaped fruits that turn red when mature.

Piper pavasense is valued for its aromatic properties and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
