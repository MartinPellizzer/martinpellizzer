Pennisetum centrasiaticum var. lanpingense

Pennisetum centrasiaticum var. lanpingense, commonly known as the Lanping grass, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often adorned with purple or reddish hues.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
