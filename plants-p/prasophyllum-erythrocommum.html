Prasophyllum erythrocommum

Prasophyllum erythrocommum, commonly known as the red-stemmed leek orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in moist, sandy or clay soils in forested areas. The plant has a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces tall, slender flowering stems. Its flowers are usually pale green to reddish in color, with a single, showy labellum.

It is a perennial herb that blooms from late summer to early autumn.
