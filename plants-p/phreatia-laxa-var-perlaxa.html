Phreatia laxa var. perlaxa

Phreatia laxa var. perlaxa, commonly known as the Philippine ghost orchid, is a rare and captivating orchid species native to the Philippines.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and elongated, pale green to white leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often with a slight pinkish tinge. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically in mountainous regions with rich, well-drained soil.
