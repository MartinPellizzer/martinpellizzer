Panicum chnoodes

Panicum chnoodes, commonly known as dwarf panicgrass, is a low-growing grass species native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, wiry leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences that produce light green to golden seeds. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields. It is drought-tolerant and can survive in poor soil conditions, making it a hardy, resilient species.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a useful plant for erosion control and landscaping in arid regions.
