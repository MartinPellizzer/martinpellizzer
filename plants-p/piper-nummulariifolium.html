Piper nummulariifolium

Piper nummulariifolium, commonly known as the broad-leaf pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple. It produces small, peppery fruits that are used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and research settings.
