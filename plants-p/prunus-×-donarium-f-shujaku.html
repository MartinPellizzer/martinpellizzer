Prunus × donarium f. shujaku

Prunus × donarium f. shujaku, commonly known as the Shujaku cherry, is a compact, ornamental shrub prized for its delicate, early spring blossoms.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is a hybrid of Prunus × donarium, characterized by its short stature and dense branching. The plant produces small, pale pink flowers that appear before the leaves, creating a striking visual display. It is well-suited for small gardens or container planting due to its manageable size.

The leaves are dark green and oval-shaped, providing a lush backdrop to the floral display.
