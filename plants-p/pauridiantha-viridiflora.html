Pauridiantha viridiflora

Pauridiantha viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered pauridiantha, is a flowering plant native to the humid forests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green. The plant produces small, clustered flowers with yellow petals and green sepals, giving it its common name. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in the understory of tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
