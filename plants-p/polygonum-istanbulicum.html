Polygonum istanbulicum

Polygonum istanbulicum, commonly known as Istanbul knotweed, is a perennial herb native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants known for their distinctive arrow-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas such as along stream banks and in woodland edges. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces distinctive bead-like fruits.

Polygonum istanbulicum is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
