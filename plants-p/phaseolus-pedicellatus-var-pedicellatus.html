Phaseolus pedicellatus var. pedicellatus

Phaseolus pedicellatus var. pedicellatus, commonly known as the scarlet runner bean, is a climbing legume native to the Americas.

It produces vibrant red, orange, or yellow flowers and elongated, edible pods that contain numerous seeds. The plant thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Its leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet having a serrated edge.

The scarlet runner bean is valued for its nutritional content and is widely cultivated for both culinary and ornamental purposes.
