Petalochilus tonellii

Petalochilus tonellii, commonly known as the Malayan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption from the soil.

Petalochilus tonellii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
