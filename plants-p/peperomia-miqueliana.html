Peperomia miqueliana

Peperomia miqueliana, commonly known as the Miquel's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in its natural habitat. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, arranged in clusters along the stems. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and drought tolerance.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for collectors and indoor gardeners.
