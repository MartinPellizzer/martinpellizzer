Panicum schweinfurthii

Panicum schweinfurthii, commonly known as the red panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces fluffy, red-tawny seed heads that resemble small clouds. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping and erosion control. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that becomes more reddish in the fall.

Panicum schweinfurthii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand drought conditions.
