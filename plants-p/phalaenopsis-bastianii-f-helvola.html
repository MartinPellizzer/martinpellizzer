Phalaenopsis bastianii f. helvola

Phalaenopsis bastianii f. helvola, commonly known as the white phalaenopsis.

This orchid is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves and clusters of white flowers with a faint yellowish tint. It is a hybrid form of Phalaenopsis bastianii, known for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements. The plant typically blooms during the winter months, producing long-lasting inflorescences.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining potting media.
