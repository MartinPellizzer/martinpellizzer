Plectranthus zernyi

Plectranthus zernyi, commonly known as the Cape rosemary, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, grey-green leaves and clusters of small, pale blue to purple flowers. The plant is often grown as an ornamental due to its attractive foliage and fragrant properties. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for Mediterranean climates.

Plectranthus zernyi is also valued for its use in herbal medicine and as a source of essential oils.
