Phragmipedium lindleyanum var. kaieteurum

Phragmipedium lindleyanum var. kaieteurum, commonly known as the Kaieteur orchid, is a species native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with pseudobulbs and is characterized by its long, pendant-like floral spikes. The flowers are typically yellow with purple or red markings, and they bloom during the wet season. This variety thrives in high humidity and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

It is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
