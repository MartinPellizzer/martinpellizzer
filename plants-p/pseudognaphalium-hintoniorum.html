Pseudognaphalium hintoniorum

Pseudognaphalium hintoniorum, commonly known as Hinton's everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically yellow or white, with a central disc of tubular flowers.
