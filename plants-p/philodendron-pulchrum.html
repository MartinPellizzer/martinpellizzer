Philodendron pulchrum

Philodendron pulchrum, commonly known as the beautiful philodendron, is a tropical vine native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with white or cream patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Philodendron pulchrum is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
