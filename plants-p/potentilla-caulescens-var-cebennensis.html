Potentilla caulescens var. cebennensis

Potentilla caulescens var. cebennensis, commonly known as the Cebennan shrub, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, often yellow or white flowers. The plant typically features woody stems and trifoliate leaves, which are lance-shaped and have a hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
