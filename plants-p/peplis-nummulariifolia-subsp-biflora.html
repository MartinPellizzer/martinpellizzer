Peplis nummulariifolia subsp. biflora

Peplis nummulariifolia subsp.

biflora, commonly known as the dwarf snowflake, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features small, rounded, silver-green leaves that are densely packed, giving it a compact, cushion-like appearance.

The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
