Phalaenopsis natmataungensis

Phalaenopsis natmataungensis, commonly known as the Natmataung orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to Myanmar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, long-lasting floral display. The plant typically produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom sequentially along the inflorescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich substrates.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
