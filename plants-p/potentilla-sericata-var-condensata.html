Potentilla sericata var. condensata

Potentilla sericata var. condensata, commonly known as the dense-flowered cinquefoil, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, five-petaled white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, wiry stems covered with fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive, somewhat fuzzy appearance. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.
