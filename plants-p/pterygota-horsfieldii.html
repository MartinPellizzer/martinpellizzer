Pterygota horsfieldii

Pterygota horsfieldii, commonly known as the Horsfield's pteridium, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
