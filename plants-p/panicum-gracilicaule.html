Panicum gracilicaule

Panicum gracilicaule, commonly known as slender panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open areas such as fields and disturbed sites.

Panicum gracilicaule is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
