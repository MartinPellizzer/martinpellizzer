Ptilotus Holosericeus

Ptilotus holosericeus is a unique and striking plant native to Australia. Known for its vibrant, spiky flowers, it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. This species belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is often referred to as the "pincushion flower" due to its distinctive appearance. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife. Its ability to survive in harsh conditions makes it a fascinating subject for both botanists and gardeners alike.
