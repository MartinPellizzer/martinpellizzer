Pimelea mesoa subsp. mesoa

Pimelea mesoa subsp.

mesoa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pimelea, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia. It is a shrub characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, narrow leaves.

The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers, which are typically borne in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and open woodlands.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
