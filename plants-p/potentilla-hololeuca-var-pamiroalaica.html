Potentilla hololeuca var. pamiroalaica

Potentilla hololeuca var. pamiroalaica, commonly known as the Pamir potentilla, is a flowering plant native to the high mountain regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its compact growth and dense foliage. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in alpine and subalpine environments, often growing at elevations above 3,000 meters.

Its hardy nature and ability to withstand harsh climatic conditions make it a notable species in its ecological niche.
