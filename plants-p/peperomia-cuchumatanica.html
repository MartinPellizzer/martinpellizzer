Peperomia cuchumatanica

Peperomia cuchumatanica, commonly known as the Cuchumatanica Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Central America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are often dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia cuchumatanica is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
