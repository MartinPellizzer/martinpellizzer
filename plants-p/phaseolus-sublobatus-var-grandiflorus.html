Phaseolus sublobatus var. grandiflorus

Phaseolus sublobatus var. grandiflorus, commonly known as the large-flowered runner bean, is a cultivated variety of the common runner bean.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant is a vigorous climber with trifoliate leaves and produces edible pods that are similar to other Phaseolus species. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often grown for both its ornamental value and its edible beans.
