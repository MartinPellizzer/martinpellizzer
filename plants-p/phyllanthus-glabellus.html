Phyllanthus glabellus

Phyllanthus glabellus, commonly known as the smooth-leafed phyllanthus, is a small, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, often clustered in dense inflorescences.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several tiny, hard seeds.
