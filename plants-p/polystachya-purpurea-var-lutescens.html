Polystachya purpurea var. lutescens

Polystachya purpurea var. lutescens, commonly known as the golden-stemmed orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to the Caribbean.

It features vibrant, lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with a reddish hue. The plant produces clusters of delicate, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
