Polyscias jacobsii

Polyscias jacobsii, commonly known as the Jacobi's glorybower, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, leathery leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Polyscias jacobsii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
