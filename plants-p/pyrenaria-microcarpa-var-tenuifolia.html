Pyrenaria microcarpa var. tenuifolia

Pyrenaria microcarpa var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pyrenaria, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in compact clusters, which bloom in the summer months. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
