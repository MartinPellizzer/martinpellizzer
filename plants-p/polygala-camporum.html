Polygala camporum

Polygala camporum, commonly known as the camp orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and showy, orchid-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom from late spring to early autumn.

Polygala camporum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
