Pilea wullschlaegelii

Pilea wullschlaegelii, commonly known as the silver mottled pilea, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to China.

It features rounded, dark green leaves with distinctive silver or white markings that create a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in spring, which are followed by red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
