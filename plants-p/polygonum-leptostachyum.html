Polygonum leptostachyum

Polygonum leptostachyum, commonly known as the slender-stamen knotweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Polygonaceae.

It is native to regions of Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea, and thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features upright, herbaceous stems that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, clustered flowers are typically greenish-white in color, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

Polygonum leptostachyum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
