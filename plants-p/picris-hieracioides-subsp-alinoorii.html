Picris hieracioides subsp. alinoorii

Picris hieracioides subsp.

alinoorii, commonly known as Alinoor's hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to parts of Central Asia. It typically grows in dry, rocky or sandy soils, often in mountainous regions.

The plant has bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, elongated seeds.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
