Pennisetum parviflorum var. majus

Pennisetum parviflorum var. majus, commonly known as fountain grass, is a ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features slender, arching stems that produce fluffy, silver-white plumes during the summer months. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its delicate appearance and seasonal blooms add visual interest to the garden throughout the growing season.
