Pultenaea empetrifolia

Pultenaea empetrifolia, commonly known as the hardy pultenaea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its dense, woody growth habit. The plant features narrow, leathery leaves and produces small, yellowish-pink flowers in spring. It is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Pultenaea empetrifolia is valued for its resilience and ornamental appeal in horticultural settings.
