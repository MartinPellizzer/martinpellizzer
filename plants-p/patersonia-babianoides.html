Patersonia babianoides

Patersonia babianoides, commonly known as the Babianan bell, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Patersonia babianoides is well-suited for rock gardens and dry soils, making it a popular choice in native gardens.
