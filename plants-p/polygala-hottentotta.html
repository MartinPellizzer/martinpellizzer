Polygala hottentotta

Polygala hottentotta, commonly known as the hottentot's spurge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. The plant features delicate, spirally arranged leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. It is often found in coastal and inland areas, thriving in well-drained environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
