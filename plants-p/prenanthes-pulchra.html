Prenanthes pulchra

Prenanthes pulchra, commonly known as the showy bladderwort, is a herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, spadix-like inflorescence surrounded by a broad, showy spathe. The plant typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds, thriving in moist to aquatic environments. Prenanthes pulchra plays an important ecological role in wetland ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.
