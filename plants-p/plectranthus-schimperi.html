Plectranthus schimperi

Plectranthus schimperi, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to white. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and long blooming period.

Plectranthus schimperi is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
