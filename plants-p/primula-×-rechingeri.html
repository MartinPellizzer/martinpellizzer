Primula × rechingeri

Primula × rechingeri, commonly known as the hybrid primrose, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and violet. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–30 cm, with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down.

It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
