Pilosella unamunoi

Pilosella unamunoi, commonly known as the "Unamuno's pilosella," is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant has hairy leaves and stems, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

Pilosella unamunoi is also valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological significance in its native habitat.
