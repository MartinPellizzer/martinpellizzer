Pleurothallis flexibilis

Pleurothallis flexibilis, commonly known as the flexible pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, often pendulous stems and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its leaves are usually elongated and grass-like, contributing to its overall airy appearance.

This orchid is popular among enthusiasts for its unique beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
