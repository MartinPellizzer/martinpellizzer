Psilochilus sanderianus

Psilochilus sanderianus, commonly known as the Sander's psilochilus, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a slender, wiry stem with several leaves that are elliptic in shape. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

It is valued in horticulture for its delicate appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
