Phalangium pendulum

Phalangium pendulum, commonly known as the hanging phalangium, is a species of plant characterized by its unique growth habit.

It is a climbing plant that produces delicate, pendulous inflorescences, which are often adorned with small, showy flowers. The leaves are typically elongated and alternate along the stems, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.
