Pternandra multiflora

Pternandra multiflora, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of white flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has soft, gray-green leaves that are opposite and oval in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Pternandra multiflora is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
