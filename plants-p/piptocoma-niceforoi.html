Piptocoma niceforoi

Piptocoma niceforoi, commonly known as the New Caledonian pittosporum, is a shrub native to the island of New Caledonia.

It belongs to the family Pittosporaceae and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats within tropical rainforests. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth, waxy texture, while its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences.

Piptocoma niceforoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
