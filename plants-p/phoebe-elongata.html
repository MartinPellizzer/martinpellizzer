Phoebe elongata

Phoebe elongata, commonly known as the elongated phoebe, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the southeastern United States, where it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a glossy, dark green surface. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale green, followed by small, spherical berries.

Phoebe elongata is often found in woodland areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
