Prestoea pubens var. semispicata

Prestoea pubens var. semispicata, commonly known as the white hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, showy white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.
