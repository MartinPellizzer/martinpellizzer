Psammotropha androsacea

Psammotropha androsacea, commonly known as the Androsace sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the alpine and subalpine regions of Europe and Asia.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is often cultivated in rock gardens and alpine plant collections.
