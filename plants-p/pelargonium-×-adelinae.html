Pelargonium × adelinae

Pelargonium × adelinae, commonly known as the Adelina geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red. The leaves are typically rounded and have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a potted plant.
