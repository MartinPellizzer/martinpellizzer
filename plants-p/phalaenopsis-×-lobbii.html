Phalaenopsis × lobbii

Phalaenopsis × lobbii, commonly known as the Orchid of the Month, is a hybrid orchid resulting from the cross between Phalaenopsis amabilis and Phalaenopsis lobbii.

It is an epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia, typically found growing on trees in tropical rainforests. The plant features dense, dark green leaves and produces long, pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It is prized for its longevity and ability to bloom repeatedly, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
