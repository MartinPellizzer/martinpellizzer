Pleurothallis valenzuelana

Pleurothallis valenzuelana, commonly known as the Valenzuela orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru.

It is a small epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments within the rainforest canopy. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its inflorescence typically consists of several small, clustered blooms that bloom during the rainy season.

Due to its ornamental value and habitat specificity, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
