Piper brachypodon var. hirtellum

Piper brachypodon var. hirtellum, commonly known as the hairy-stemmed pepper plant, is a tropical species native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes numerous pepper species. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit, hairy stems, and small, oval-shaped leaves. It produces tiny, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into small, round berries.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and potential medicinal properties.
