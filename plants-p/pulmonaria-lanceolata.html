Pulmonaria lanceolata

Pulmonaria lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf lungwort, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe.

It features distinctive lance-shaped leaves with a bluish-green hue and a dense covering of fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped, pink or purple flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Pulmonaria lanceolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
