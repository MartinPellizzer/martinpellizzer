Polystachya huyghei

Polystachya huyghei, commonly known as the Huyghe's spikegrass, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Polystachya huyghei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
