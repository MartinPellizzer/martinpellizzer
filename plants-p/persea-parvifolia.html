Persea parvifolia

Persea parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved persimmon, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Mexico and Central America, where it typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has a shallow root system and prefers well-drained soil, making it adaptable to various habitats.
