Polium ramosum

Polium ramosum, commonly known as the slender blueweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a slight pubescence.

Polium ramosum is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is often found in disturbed or wet soils.
