Pleurothallis sandemanii

Pleurothallis sandemanii, commonly known as the Sandeman's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually small, with intricate patterns and a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink.

Pleurothallis sandemanii is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
