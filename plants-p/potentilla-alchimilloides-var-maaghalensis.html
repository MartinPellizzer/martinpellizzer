Potentilla alchimilloides var. maaghalensis

Potentilla alchimilloides var. maaghalensis, commonly known as the Maag's cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub with small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often covered in a soft, silvery pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in Mediterranean climates.
