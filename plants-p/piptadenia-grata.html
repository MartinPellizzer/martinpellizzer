Piptadenia grata

Piptadenia grata, commonly known as yellow trumpet tree, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The tree has a straight trunk and can grow up to 20 meters tall, with compound leaves and pod fruits that contain seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in folk medicine.
