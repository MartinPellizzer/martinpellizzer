Phyllanthus cauticola

Phyllanthus cauticola, commonly known as the African spurge, is a flowering plant native to sub-Saharan Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has small, elliptical leaves with a waxy surface and produces small yellowish-green flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds.

Phyllanthus cauticola is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
