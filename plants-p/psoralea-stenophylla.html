Psoralea stenophylla

Psoralea stenophylla, commonly known as narrow-leaf psoralea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its narrow, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers clustered in racemes, which are typically borne on the upper part of the stem. It is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight.

Psoralea stenophylla is often found in grassland and rocky habitats, where it plays a role in soil stabilization and supports local biodiversity.
