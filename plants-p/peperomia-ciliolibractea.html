Peperomia ciliolibractea

Peperomia ciliolibractea, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are typically borne in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and ease of care make it a favored ornamental plant among plant enthusiasts.
