Pyrostria hystrix

Pyrostria hystrix, commonly known as the arrowroot plant, is a tropical shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves and striking, spadix-like inflorescence. The plant produces edible tubers that are rich in starch and are used in various culinary applications. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Pyrostria hystrix is also valued for its ornamental qualities due to its lush foliage and unique floral structures.
