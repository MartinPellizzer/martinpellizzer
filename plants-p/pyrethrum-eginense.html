Pyrethrum eginense

Pyrethrum eginense, commonly known as the Eginian pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant is known for its production of pyrethrins, natural insecticides used in organic pest control. Pyrethrum eginense typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun.

Its ornamental value and ecological benefits make it a valuable addition to gardens and natural habitats.
