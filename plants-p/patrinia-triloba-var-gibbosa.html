Patrinia triloba var. gibbosa

Patrinia triloba var. gibbosa, commonly known as the hairy patrinia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale blue, arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its medicinal properties have been recognized in traditional Chinese medicine.
