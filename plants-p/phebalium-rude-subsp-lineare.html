Phebalium rude subsp. lineare

Phebalium rude subsp.

lineare, commonly known as the narrow-leaved phebalium, is a shrub native to southern Australia. It typically grows in dry, sandy or rocky soils and is often found in heathland and woodland habitats.

The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters. It is a hardy species that is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
