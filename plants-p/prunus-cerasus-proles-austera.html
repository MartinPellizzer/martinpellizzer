Prunus cerasus proles austera

Prunus cerasus proles austera, commonly known as the dwarf cherry, is a compact variety of the sweet cherry tree.

It is characterized by its small stature, making it ideal for small gardens or container planting. The plant produces small, dark red fruits that are typically harvested in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun for optimal growth and fruit production.

This variety is also valued for its ornamental appeal, with attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
