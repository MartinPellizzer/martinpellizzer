Phyteuma orbiculare var. ciliata

Phyteuma orbiculare var. ciliata, commonly known as the hairy or curled phyteuma, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its rounded, hairy leaves and clusters of bright yellow or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments. It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
