Paphiopedilum concolor f. dahuaense

Paphiopedilum concolor f. dahuaense, commonly known as the Dahua Orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to China.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves and its striking, showy flowers with a distinctive lip. The flowers typically bloom in shades of pink and white, with a prominent, velvety lip that resembles a pouch. This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike.
