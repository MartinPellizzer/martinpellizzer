Plerandra actinostigma

Plerandra actinostigma, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a species of orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and is often found in rainforest environments. The plant has long, narrow leaves and produces clusters of fragrant blooms. It thrives in humid conditions and requires well-drained soil.

Plerandra actinostigma is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
