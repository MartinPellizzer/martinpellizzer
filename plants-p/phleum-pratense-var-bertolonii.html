Phleum pratense var. bertolonii

Phleum pratense var. bertolonii, commonly known as the Bertoloni's Timothy grass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along riverbanks.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, supporting various pollinators and wildlife.
