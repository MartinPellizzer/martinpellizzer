Philodendron cretosum

Philodendron cretosum, commonly known as the Cretaceous philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy green leaves that are often variegated with cream or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by red, fleshy fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Philodendron cretosum is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
