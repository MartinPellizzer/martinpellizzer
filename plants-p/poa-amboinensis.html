Poa amboinensis

Poa amboinensis, commonly known as the Amboina grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to various parts of Asia, including Indonesia, India, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry leaves that are often hairy. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to reddish-brown flowers.

Poa amboinensis is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its fine texture and adaptability to different soil conditions.
