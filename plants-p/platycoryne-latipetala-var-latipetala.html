Platycoryne latipetala var. latipetala

Platycoryne latipetala var. latipetala, commonly known as the broad-petaled flat-lobed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, flat petals and a distinctive floral structure typical of the Orchidaceae family. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often on rocky substrates or in undergrowth. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish in color, with a subtle fragrance.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
