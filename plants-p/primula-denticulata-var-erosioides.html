Primula denticulata var. erosioides

Primula denticulata var. erosioides, commonly known as the erose-leaved primrose, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features hairy, broadly ovate leaves with a distinctive erose margin, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forest understories or rocky habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and shady borders.
