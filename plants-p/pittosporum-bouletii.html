Pittosporum bouletii

Pittosporum bouletii, commonly known as the Boulet's pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth margin. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland forest environments.

Pittosporum bouletii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native ecosystems.
