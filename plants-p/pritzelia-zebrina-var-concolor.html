Pritzelia zebrina var. concolor

Pritzelia zebrina var. concolor, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a unique member of the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display a combination of green, white, and cream patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically hidden beneath its foliage. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental appeal, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
