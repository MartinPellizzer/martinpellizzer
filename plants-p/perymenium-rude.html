Perymenium rude

Perymenium rude, commonly known as the rough perymenium, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, rough-textured leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and fields. It has a taproot system and can spread through seeds dispersed by wind.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the aster family in its native range.
