Peperomia puberulirhachis

Peperomia puberulirhachis, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its compact growth habit makes it an ideal choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
