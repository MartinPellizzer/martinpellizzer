Poa ovalis

Poa ovalis, commonly known as oval bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, lawns, and along stream banks. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves that are slightly curved and exhibit a bluish-green color. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small, spikelet-bearing flowers.

Poa ovalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and turfgrass management.
