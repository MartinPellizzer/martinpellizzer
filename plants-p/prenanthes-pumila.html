Prenanthes pumila

Prenanthes pumila, commonly known as the smooth bladderleaf, is a small, herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy, with a distinctive bluish-green color. Its small, yellow flowers bloom in clusters, often appearing in late summer.

Prenanthes pumila is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local insect populations.
