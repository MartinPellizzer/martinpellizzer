Peperomia parvulifolia

Peperomia parvulifolia, commonly known as the small-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically small, ovate, and glossy, with a deep green hue. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale pink, clustered in spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its slow growth and low maintenance requirements make it ideal for beginners and space-limited environments.
