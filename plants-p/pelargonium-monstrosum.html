Pelargonium monstrosum

Pelargonium monstrosum, commonly known as the Madagascar dragon tree, is a striking succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from deep red to purple and green. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that appear in clusters during the warmer months. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.

It requires well-draining soil and minimal water to thrive.
