Photinia longifolia

Photinia longifolia, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange flowers that appear in clusters during the spring. The leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a glossy appearance. The plant is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

It is also known for its rapid growth and adaptability to different climates.
