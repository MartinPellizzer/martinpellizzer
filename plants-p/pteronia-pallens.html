Pteronia pallens

Pteronia pallens, commonly known as the silver leaf pteronia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Pteronia pallens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
