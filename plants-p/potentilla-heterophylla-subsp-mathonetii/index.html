Potentilla Heterophylla Subsp. Mathonetii

Potentilla heterophylla subsp. mathonetii is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China. It is known for its unique leaf shapes and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. This subspecies is often found in rocky soils and humid environments, thriving in cool, shaded areas. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered endangered and is protected in several conservation programs. Its beauty and rarity make it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
