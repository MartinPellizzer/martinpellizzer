Philadelphus purpurascens var. szechuanensis

Philadelphus purpurascens var. szechuanensis, commonly known as the Sichuan Mockorange, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its showy blossoms.
