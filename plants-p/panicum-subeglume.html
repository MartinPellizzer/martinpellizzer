Panicum subeglume

Panicum subeglume, commonly known as slender panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and compact inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and small, spike-like clusters of flowers.

It is often used in ecological restoration due to its resilience and ability to stabilize soil.
