Pimelea nieppergiana

Pimelea nieppergiana, commonly known as the Narrow-leaf Grevillea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features narrow, needle-like leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers clustered in dense, cylindrical spikes, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and dry forests.

Pimelea nieppergiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting native wildlife.
