Patagonula americana var. glabra

Patagonula americana var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth Patagonula, is a rare and unique plant species native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves and compact, low-growing habit. The plant typically thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically greenish or white in color.
