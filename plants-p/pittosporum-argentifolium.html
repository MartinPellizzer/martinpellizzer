Pittosporum argentifolium

Pittosporum argentifolium, commonly known as silverleaf pittosporum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silvery-white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in spring, followed by dark purple berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its adaptability and aesthetic appeal make it a valuable addition to gardens and landscapes.
