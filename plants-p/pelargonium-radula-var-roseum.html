Pelargonium radula var. roseum

Pelargonium radula var. roseum, commonly known as the roseum geranium, is a compact, trailing plant native to South Africa.

It features small, rounded leaves with a distinctive velvety texture and a deep red or maroon coloration. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pink to purple flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It is well-suited for hanging baskets or as a trailing plant in containers due to its cascading growth habit.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor and outdoor conditions.
