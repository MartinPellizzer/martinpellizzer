Pellaea Dura Var. Holstii

Pellaea dura var. holstii is a striking fern species native to the rocky slopes of southern Africa. It is known for its dark, leathery fronds that display a unique pattern of silvery veins. This variety is particularly hardy, thriving in harsh, arid environments where few plants can survive. Its resilience and distinctive appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and drought-tolerant landscapes. Despite its rugged habitat, it remains a fascinating subject for botanists and gardeners alike.
