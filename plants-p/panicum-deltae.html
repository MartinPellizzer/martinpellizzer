Panicum deltae

Panicum deltae, commonly known as Delta grass, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds and lakes. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces fluffy, seed-bearing spikes that are often visible in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Panicum deltae plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
