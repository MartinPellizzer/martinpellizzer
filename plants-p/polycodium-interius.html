Polycodium interius

Polycodium interius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved polycodium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, erect stems that are often branched and covered with fine hairs. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly serrated margin. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters at the tips of the stems.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.
