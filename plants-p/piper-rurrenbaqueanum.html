Piper rurrenbaqueanum

Piper rurrenbaqueanum, commonly known as the Rurrenbaque pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the Amazon rainforest, particularly in regions of Peru and Bolivia. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with long, slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. Its small, greenish-white flowers are followed by berries that turn red when mature.

This plant is valued for its potential medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
