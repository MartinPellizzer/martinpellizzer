Pilea auriculata

Pilea auriculata, commonly known as the ear-shaped pilea, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to China.

It features rosettes of heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are dark green with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear on slender stems in the spring. It thrives in indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ease of care and attractive foliage make it a favored houseplant.
