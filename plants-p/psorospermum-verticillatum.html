Psorospermum verticillatum

Psorospermum verticillatum, commonly known as the mountain ebony, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, clustered flowers that bloom in white or pale yellow. The plant has a straight trunk with a smooth, dark bark and produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical features make it valuable for both ecological and ornamental purposes.
