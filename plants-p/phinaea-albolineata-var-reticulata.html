Phinaea albolineata var. reticulata

Phinaea albolineata var. reticulata, commonly known as the reticulated phinaea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It features striking, waxy leaves with a distinctive reticulated pattern, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sun exposure.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor cultivation.
