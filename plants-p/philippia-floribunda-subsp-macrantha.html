Philippia floribunda subsp. macrantha

Philippia floribunda subsp.

macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered philippia, is a species native to the Philippines. It is a shrub characterized by its dense foliage and showy, clustered flowers.

The flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
