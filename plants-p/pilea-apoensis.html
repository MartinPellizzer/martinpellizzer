Pilea apoensis

Pilea apoensis, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically bright green with a slightly glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Pilea apoensis is popular for its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.
