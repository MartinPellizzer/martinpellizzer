Panicum procurrens villosum

Panicum procurrens villosum, commonly known as silky grass or silky panicum, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, rhizomatous stems that spread along the ground, forming dense mats. The leaves are narrow, hairy, and often have a bluish-green hue, contributing to its ornamental appeal. Its inflorescence consists of paniculate clusters of small, pale green to golden flowers.

This plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping and erosion control.
