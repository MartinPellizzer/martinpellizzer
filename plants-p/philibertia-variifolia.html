Philibertia variifolia

Philibertia variifolia, commonly known as the variable filbert, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often variegated, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Philibertia variifolia is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
