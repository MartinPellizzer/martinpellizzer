Peperomia ellipticibacca

Peperomia ellipticibacca, commonly known as the silver velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are often variegated with silver and green hues, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia ellipticibacca is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
