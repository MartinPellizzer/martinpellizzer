Parsonsia warenensis

Parsonsia warenensis, commonly known as the Waren's flame vine, is a striking member of the Apocynaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its vibrant orange to red tubular flowers, which bloom in clusters along the stems. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Parsonsia warenensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its dramatic floral display.
