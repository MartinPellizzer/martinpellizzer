Pteronia onobromoides

Pteronia onobromoides, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats and is well adapted to dry conditions. It features a low-growing, spreading habit with narrow, grass-like leaves.

Pteronia onobromoides is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
