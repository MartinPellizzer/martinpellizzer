Panicum pseudoracemosum

Panicum pseudoracemosum, commonly known as the slender panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along riverbanks. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its ability to colonize new areas.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms.
