Polygonum zigzag

Polygonum zigzag, commonly known as zigzag hemp or redstem filaree, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its zigzagging stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as wetlands, streambanks, and disturbed soils. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and often have a reddish hue.

Polygonum zigzag is an annual or biennial plant that plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing food for various insects.
