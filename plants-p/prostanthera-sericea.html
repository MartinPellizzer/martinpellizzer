Prostanthera sericea

Prostanthera sericea, commonly known as silver leaf honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its narrow, silver-gray leaves and fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in winter. It is often found in coastal and sandy habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.

Prostanthera sericea is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
