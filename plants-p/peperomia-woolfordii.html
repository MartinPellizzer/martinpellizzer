Peperomia woolfordii

Peperomia woolfordii, commonly known as Woolford's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a reddish tint on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish-white in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal water needs.

Its compact growth habit makes it an ideal choice for tabletop displays or as part of a succulent collection.
