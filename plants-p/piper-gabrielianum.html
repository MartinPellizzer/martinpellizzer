Piper gabrielianum

Piper gabrielianum, commonly known as the Gabrielian pepper or wild pepper, is a tropical climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into small, red berries. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a serrated margin.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
