Polypodium lineare var. wightianum

Polypodium lineare var. wightianum, commonly known as the Wight's shield fern, is a variant of the common shield fern.

It is a perennial evergreen fern that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features broad, lance-shaped fronds with a distinctive, shield-like appearance. It has a rhizomatous growth habit, which allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

This variety is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in humid, shaded habitats.
