Prunella paninii

Prunella paninii, commonly known as the Panini's deadnettle, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Europe and North Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, two-lipped flowers that are typically purple or blue in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Prunella paninii is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
