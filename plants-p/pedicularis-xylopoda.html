Pedicularis xylopoda

Pedicularis xylopoda, commonly known as the showy lady's slipper, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

The plant is hemiparasitic, meaning it derives some of its nutrients from the roots of neighboring plants. Its unique floral structure and ecological role make it a notable species in botanical studies.
