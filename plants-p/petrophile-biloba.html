Petrophile biloba

Petrophile biloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bottlebrush, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive bottlebrush-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and it produces small, tubular flowers that are usually red or pink in color.

Petrophile biloba is well adapted to arid conditions and is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value.
