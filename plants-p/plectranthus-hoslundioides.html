Plectranthus hoslundioides

Plectranthus hoslundioides, commonly known as the purple heart plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple heart-shaped leaves, which are velvety to the touch. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale purple or white, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Plectranthus hoslundioides is often used as a ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and easy care requirements.
