Poa taldyksuensis

Poa taldyksuensis, commonly known as the Taldyksu grass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Asia.

It is typically found in arid and semi-arid regions, often growing in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant has slender, cylindrical stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color.

Poa taldyksuensis is adapted to withstand drought and temperature extremes, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
