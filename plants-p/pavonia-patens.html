Pavonia patens

Pavonia patens, commonly known as the showy pavonia, is a species of flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, featuring upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of showy, pink to red bract-like flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Pavonia patens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in gardens and landscapes.
