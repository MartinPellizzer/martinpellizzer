Phyllanthus panayensis

Phyllanthus panayensis, commonly known as the Panay sandmat, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a waxy coating.
