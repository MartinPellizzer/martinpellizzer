Piper dumiformans

Piper dumiformans, commonly known as the "Clove Pepper," is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by tiny, red berries. Its leaves are ovate with a slightly hairy texture and a strong, aromatic scent.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of medicinal compounds.
