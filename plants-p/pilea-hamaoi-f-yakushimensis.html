Pilea hamaoi f. yakushimensis

Pilea hamaoi f. yakushimensis, commonly known as the Yakushimen pilea, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are typically a glossy dark green with subtle variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and ease of care contribute to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
