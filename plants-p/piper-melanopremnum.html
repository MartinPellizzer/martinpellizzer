Piper melanopremnum

Piper melanopremnum, commonly known as blackstem pepper vine, is a tropical evergreen vine native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants like the common black pepper. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into elongated, dark purple berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The berries are edible but not commonly used in culinary applications.
