Peperomia cerea

Peperomia cerea, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a striking silvery or white variegation that adds visual interest to any indoor space. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, which are not particularly showy but contribute to its ornamental value. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia cerea is ideal for beginners due to its resilience and ability to adapt to a variety of indoor growing conditions.
