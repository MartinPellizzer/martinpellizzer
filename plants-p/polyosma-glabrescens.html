Polyosma glabrescens

Polyosma glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth polyosma, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as heathlands and rocky slopes. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. Its small, white or pale yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in spring.

The smooth polyosma is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
