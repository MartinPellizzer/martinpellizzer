Paphiopedilum × fanaticum

Paphiopedilum × fanaticum, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Paphiopedilum niveum and Paphiopedilum sukhetskyanum.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a sympodial growth habit, characterized by its thick, fleshy rhizomes and pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, lip-shaped flowers that are typically yellow with red or orange markings, often resembling a lady's slipper. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is popular among orchid enthusiasts.
