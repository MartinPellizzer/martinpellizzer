Pilea pyrrhotricha

Pilea pyrrhotricha, commonly known as the firetail begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive reddish or maroon edge, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea pyrrhotricha is relatively low maintenance and suitable for beginners due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.
