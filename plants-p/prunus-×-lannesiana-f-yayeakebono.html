Prunus × lannesiana f. yayeakebono

Prunus × lannesiana f. yayeakebono, commonly known as the Yayeakebono cherry, is a unique cultivar of the Prunus genus.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, double pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant is a hybrid between Prunus lannesiana and Prunus × yedoensis, resulting in a blend of ornamental traits. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for ornamental gardens.

Its foliage provides attractive color throughout the growing season, adding year-round interest to the landscape.
