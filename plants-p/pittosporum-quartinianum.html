Pittosporum quartinianum

Pittosporum quartinianum, commonly known as the New Zealand pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 meters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters, and it produces small, red berries that are edible for birds.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
