Poikilospermum amoenum

Poikilospermum amoenum, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvetleaf sida, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, characterized by its alternate, ovate leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by capsule fruits that contain numerous small, rounded seeds.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
