Paramomum petaloideum

Paramomum petaloideum, commonly known as the featherleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers bloom in shades of white and yellow, adding a subtle beauty to gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in coastal and Mediterranean climates.
