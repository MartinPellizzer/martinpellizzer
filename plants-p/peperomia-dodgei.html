Peperomia dodgei

Peperomia dodgei, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to Brazil.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery or grayish pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring or summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia dodgei is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
