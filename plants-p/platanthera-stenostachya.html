Platanthera stenostachya

It is a terrestrial herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. Native to eastern North America, it thrives in moist, shaded woodlands and is often found in association with other orchids. The plant has a short underground stem and produces a single spike of flowers, typically in late summer.
