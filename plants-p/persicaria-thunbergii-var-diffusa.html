Persicaria thunbergii var. diffusa

Persicaria thunbergii var. diffusa, commonly known as velvetleaf or broadleaf plantain, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically a deep green with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range from white to pale pink, often appearing in late summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its foliage is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
