Peperomia martiana

Peperomia martiana, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Brazil.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a distinctive silvery or grayish underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia martiana is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
