Pleurostachys millegrana var. grandifolia

Pleurostachys millegrana var. grandifolia, commonly known as the grandiflora orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. The plant has strap-like leaves that are long and narrow, arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
