Pelargonium × melissimum

Pelargonium × melissimum, commonly known as the lemon-scented geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its fragrant, lemon-scented leaves and showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to red. Native to South Africa, this plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.

The plant is also popular as a potted plant due to its compact growth habit and ability to thrive indoors.
