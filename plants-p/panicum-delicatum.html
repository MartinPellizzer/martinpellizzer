Panicum delicatum

Panicum delicatum, commonly known as the delicate panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and fine, thread-like leaves. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that are often wind-dispersed, aiding in its propagation. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a common sight in open grasslands and disturbed areas.

Due to its ornamental appeal, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
