Pelargonium amoenum

Pelargonium amoenum, commonly known as the rose geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its fragrant, rose-scented flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with upright stems and broad, serrated leaves. It produces clusters of small, pink to reddish-pink flowers that bloom throughout the summer.

Pelargonium amoenum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
