Photinia cucphuongensis

Photinia cucphuongensis, commonly known as the Cuc Phuong photinia, is a species of shrub or small tree native to Vietnam.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and showy red or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments within forest ecosystems. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and have a smooth upper surface with a hairy underside.

The fruit is a small, reddish drupe that contains a single seed.
