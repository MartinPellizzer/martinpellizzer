Phalaenopsis sumatrana var. alba

Phalaenopsis sumatrana var. alba, commonly known as the white phalaenopsis, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendant inflorescences that bear multiple white flowers with a subtle, fragrant aroma. The plant has broad, ovate leaves that are typically a deep green color with a waxy texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent humidity and well-draining potting media.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
