Pappophorum foxii

Pappophorum foxii, commonly known as the Fox's pappophore, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant produces lightweight, feathery seeds that are dispersed by the wind, a feature typical of the genus Pappophorum.
