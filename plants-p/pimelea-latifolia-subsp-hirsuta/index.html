Pimelea Latifolia Subsp. Hirsuta

Pimelea latifolia subsp. hirsuta is a unique and resilient plant native to the coastal regions of southern Australia. Known for its hairy foliage and striking yellow flowers, this subspecies thrives in sandy and well-drained soils. It is often found in dune environments, where it plays a vital role in stabilizing the ecosystem. The plant's adaptability to harsh coastal conditions makes it an important subject of ecological study. Its beauty and ecological significance highlight the importance of preserving native flora in vulnerable habitats.
