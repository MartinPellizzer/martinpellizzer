Phalaenopsis esmeralda var. punctulata

Phalaenopsis esmeralda var. punctulata, commonly known as the Emerald Dragon Orchid, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant green and white coloration.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia, where it typically grows as an epiphyte on trees. The plant features long, arching inflorescences with numerous small, showy flowers that bloom sequentially over several months. Its foliage consists of broad, dark green leaves that are often marked with subtle speckles or spots, giving it a unique appearance.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
