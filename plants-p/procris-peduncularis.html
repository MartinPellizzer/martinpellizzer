Procris peduncularis

Procris peduncularis, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. Procris peduncularis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
