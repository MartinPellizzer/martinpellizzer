Peperomia Brachyphylla

Peperomia brachyphylla is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America. It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. This plant is popular among indoor gardeners due to its attractive appearance and ease of care. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil. Its unique foliage makes it a great addition to any plant collection.
