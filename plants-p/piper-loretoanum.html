Piper loretoanum

Piper loretoanum, commonly known as Loreto pepper, is a flowering plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes over 3,000 species of flowering plants. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when ripe, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in humid, tropical climates with well-drained soil.

The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly aromatic scent.
