Prepusa campanulata

Prepusa campanulata, commonly known as the bell-shaped prepusa, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family, which includes various bell-shaped flowers. The plant is characterized by its delicate, bell-like flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Prepusa campanulata thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
