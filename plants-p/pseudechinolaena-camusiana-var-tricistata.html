Pseudechinolaena camusiana var. tricistata

Pseudechinolaena camusiana var. tricistata, commonly known as the three-lobed spikerush, is a monocotyledonous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds.
