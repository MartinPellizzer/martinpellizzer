Pomaderris subretusa

Pomaderris subretusa, commonly known as the silky pomaderris, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves. This plant is often found in dry sclerophyll forests and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
