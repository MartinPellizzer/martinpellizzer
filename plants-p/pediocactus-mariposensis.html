Pediocactus mariposensis

Pediocactus mariposensis, commonly known as the Mariposa prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are typically green to blue-green in color. The plant produces bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Pediocactus mariposensis is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
