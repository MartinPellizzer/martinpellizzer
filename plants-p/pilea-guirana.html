Pilea guirana

Pilea guirana, commonly known as the Mexican evergreen or pilea plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Urticaceae.

It is native to Central America, particularly Mexico, and is characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy green leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels.

Pilea guirana is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
