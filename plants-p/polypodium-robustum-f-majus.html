Polypodium robustum f. majus

Polypodium robustum f. majus, commonly known as the giant shield fern, is a striking fern species characterized by its large, leathery fronds.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to tropical regions of the Americas. The plant features a robust rhizome system that supports its tall, upright growth habit. Its fronds are typically divided into segments with a distinctive, shield-like appearance.

This fern thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
