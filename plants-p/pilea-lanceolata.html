Pilea lanceolata

Pilea lanceolata, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are dark green with a subtle sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by spherical, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it ideal for indoor cultivation.
