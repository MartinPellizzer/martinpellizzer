Phaca pycnostachya

Phaca pycnostachya, commonly known as the dense-stalked phaca, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Phaca pycnostachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
