Philadelphus japonicus

Philadelphus japonicus, commonly known as Japanese mock orange, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are dark green on top and pale underneath. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
