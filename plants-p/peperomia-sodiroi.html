Peperomia sodiroi

Peperomia sodiroi, commonly known as the Sodiro's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape, often with a waxy texture and a variety of colors ranging from deep green to reddish hues. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, usually emerging from the axils of the leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rockeries.
