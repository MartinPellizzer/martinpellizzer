Prunus ligustrina

Prunus ligustrina, commonly known as Chinese white cherry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It features small, white flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by edible berries that turn red in the fall. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are oval to ovate in shape. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Prunus ligustrina prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
