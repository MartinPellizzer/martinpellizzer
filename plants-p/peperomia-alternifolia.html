Peperomia alternifolia

Peperomia alternifolia, commonly known as the zigzag peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features striking, alternating leaves that give it its common name, with a glossy, dark green foliage that adds visual interest. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white or green, which are followed by reddish-brown seed pods. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.

Peperomia alternifolia is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and moderate humidity to flourish.
