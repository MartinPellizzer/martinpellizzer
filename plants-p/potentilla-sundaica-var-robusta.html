Potentilla sundaica var. robusta

Potentilla sundaica var. robusta, commonly known as the Sunda potentilla, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit with woody stems.
