Phoebe neurantha var. lobophylla

Phoebe neurantha var. lobophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved phoebe, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are often slightly waxy in texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by dark blue to black berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and conservation efforts.
