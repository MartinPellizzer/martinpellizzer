Pleurothallis punctatiflora

Pleurothallis punctatiflora, commonly known as the spotted-flowered pleurothallis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small epiphytic orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and green. Its flowers are typically clustered in inflorescences and are often adorned with distinctive markings.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
