Phreatia crinonioides

Phreatia crinonioides, commonly known as the orchid of the genus Phreatia, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and the presence of pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces large, showy inflorescences with flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its leaves are long and strap-like, adapted to capture moisture from the air.

Phreatia crinonioides thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high air humidity for optimal growth.
