Paphiopedilum micranthum f. kienii

Paphiopedilum micranthum f. kienii, commonly known as the Kienii's Paphiopedilum, is a rare and striking orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often purple or pinkish flowers with intricate patterns and a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree trunks or branches in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually dark green and leathery, adapted to retain moisture in its natural habitat.

This orchid is highly sought after by collectors due to its unique appearance and limited distribution.
