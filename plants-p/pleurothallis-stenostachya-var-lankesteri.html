Pleurothallis stenostachya var. lankesteri

Pleurothallis stenostachya var. lankesteri, commonly known as the Lankester's pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves and produces clustered inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
