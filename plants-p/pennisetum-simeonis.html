Pennisetum simeonis

Pennisetum simeonis, commonly known as the purple fountain grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features tall, arching inflorescences that bloom with vibrant purple plumes, adding a dramatic visual element to gardens. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, providing a striking contrast to its floral displays. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for landscapes in warm climates.

Pennisetum simeonis is also valued for its ability to attract birds and butterflies, enhancing biodiversity in the garden.
