Pedilonum angustiflorum

Pedilonum angustiflorum, commonly known as the narrow-flower pedilonum, is a rare and specialized plant species found in specific alpine habitats.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color.
