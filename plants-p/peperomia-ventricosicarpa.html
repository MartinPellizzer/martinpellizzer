Peperomia ventricosicarpa

Peperomia ventricosicarpa, commonly known as the swollen-stem peperomia, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a compact shrub characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and small, ovate leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
