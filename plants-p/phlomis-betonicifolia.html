Phlomis betonicifolia

Phlomis betonicifolia, commonly known as the betony fleabane, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features upright, branched stems covered in grayish-blue, hairy foliage. The plant produces showy, yellow-centered composite flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to xeriscapes and native plant gardens.
