Primula × kolbeana

Primula × kolbeana, commonly known as the hybrid primrose, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Primula vulgaris and Primula obconica.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and thrives in cool, moist environments. It has a compact growth habit with broad, dark green leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

The flowers bloom in early spring, adding a splash of color to alpine and woodland settings.
