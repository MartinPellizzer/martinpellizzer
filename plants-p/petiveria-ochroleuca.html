Petiveria ochroleuca

Petiveria ochroleuca, commonly known as whiteleaf petiveria, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

Petiveria ochroleuca is also known for its ability to tolerate a range of soil conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
