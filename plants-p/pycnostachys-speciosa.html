Pycnostachys speciosa

Pycnostachys speciosa, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, pendulous clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

The tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
