Persicaria natans

Persicaria natans, commonly known as water smartweed, is a floating herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that remain just above the water surface. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Persicaria natans is often found in ponds, lakes, and along the edges of wetlands.
