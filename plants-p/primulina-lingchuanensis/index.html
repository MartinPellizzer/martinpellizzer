Primulina Lingchuanensis

Lingchuan primrose, scientifically known as Primulina lingchuanensis, is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of China. This species belongs to the Gesneriaceae family, which includes many ornamental and ecologically significant plants. Its vibrant flowers, often in shades of red, orange, and yellow, make it a standout among botanical collections. Discovered relatively recently, it has sparked interest among botanists and conservationists alike. Due to its limited habitat and threats from environmental changes, the Lingchuan primrose is considered endangered and requires urgent protection.
