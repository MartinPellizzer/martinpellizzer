Peperomia nummulariifolia var. obcordata

Peperomia nummulariifolia var. obcordata, commonly known as the heart-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features thick, waxy, heart-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
