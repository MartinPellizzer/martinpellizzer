Piper bopianum

Piper bopianum, commonly known as the Borneo pepper or Bornean long pepper, is a flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Borneo, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces small, elongated fruits that are used as a spice and traditional medicine. Its leaves are also used in local cuisines and have aromatic properties.

Piper bopianum is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves with serrated edges.
