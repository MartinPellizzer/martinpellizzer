Pilea plataniflora var. taitoensis

Pilea plataniflora var. taitoensis, commonly known as the Taitoensis Chinese Evergreen, is a compact, low-growing houseplant native to China.

It features glossy, dark green, heart-shaped leaves with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by yellowish-green berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.

Its air-purifying qualities and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
