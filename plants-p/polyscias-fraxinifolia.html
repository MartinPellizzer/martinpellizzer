Polyscias fraxinifolia

Polyscias fraxinifolia, commonly known as the golden nettle, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features compound leaves with a distinctive, finely toothed margin and a yellowish-green underside. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by spherical, fleshy fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Polyscias fraxinifolia is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
