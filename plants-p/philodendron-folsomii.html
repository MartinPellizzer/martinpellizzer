Philodendron folsomii

Philodendron folsomii, commonly known as the Folsomii philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by bright red berries, which are edible for birds but toxic to humans. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

Philodendron folsomii is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
