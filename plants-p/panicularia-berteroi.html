Panicularia berteroi

Panicularia berteroi, commonly known as the desert broom.

It is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It has narrow, gray-green leaves and is adapted to survive in hot, dry conditions.

Panicularia berteroi is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
