Paepalanthus manicatus

Paepalanthus manicatus, commonly known as the maniacal palm.

This plant is a flowering plant species native to South America, particularly Brazil. It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its clustered growth habit and distinctive palm-like appearance. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with yellowish-white flowers.
