Pimenta cainitoides

Pimenta cainitoides, commonly known as the Brazilian pepper tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the Caribbean and parts of South America, including Brazil, Colombia, and Venezuela. The plant is characterized by its small, bright red berries that are often used in traditional medicine. It has glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, white flowers.
