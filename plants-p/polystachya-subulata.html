Polystachya subulata

Polystachya subulata, commonly known as the slender spike orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and delicate, spike-like inflorescence. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often arranged in a compact cluster. Polystachya subulata thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
