Peperomia crotalophora

Peperomia crotalophora, commonly known as the crotalophora peperomia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a compact shrub with upright stems and dense foliage, featuring oval to heart-shaped leaves that are usually dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, arranged in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a suitable choice for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia crotalophora is valued for its ornamental foliage and low maintenance requirements.
