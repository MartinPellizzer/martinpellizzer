Pleurothallis jostii

Pleurothallis jostii, commonly known as the Jost's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant typically produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and green, often with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
