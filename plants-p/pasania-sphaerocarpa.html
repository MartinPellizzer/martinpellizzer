Pasania sphaerocarpa

Pasania sphaerocarpa, commonly known as the globe-berry.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the family Myrtaceae, native to Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its spherical, red berries that resemble small globes, which are a key feature in its common name. The leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a glossy appearance.
