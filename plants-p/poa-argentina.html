Poa argentina

Poa argentina, commonly known as Argentine bluegrass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and dark green, needle-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically blue-green in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils.

Poa argentina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
