Polygonum mezianum

Polygonum mezianum, commonly known as the Mezianum knotweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants commonly referred to as buckwheats and smartweeds. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit, with slender stems and small, clustered flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically alternate, with a distinctive reddish or purple coloration on the underside.

Polygonum mezianum is often found in moist habitats such as riverbanks and wet meadows.
