Pavetta macrophylla

Pavetta macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf pavetta, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It features broad, oval leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the rainy season. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, brown seeds.

Pavetta macrophylla is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and along stream banks.
