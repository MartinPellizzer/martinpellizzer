Panicum zonale

Panicum zonale, commonly known as the zonal panicgrass, is a ornamental grass native to the southeastern United States.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green with a distinct blue-gray hue, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces delicate, fluffy seed heads that add visual interest throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Panicum zonale is often used in gardens for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
