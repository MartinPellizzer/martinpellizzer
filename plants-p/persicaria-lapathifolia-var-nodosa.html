Persicaria lapathifolia var. nodosa

Persicaria lapathifolia var. nodosa, commonly known as the nodular smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features upright, branching stems that are typically hairy and can grow up to 1 meter in height. The leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and have a slightly rough texture with prominent veins. Its small, clustered flowers are pink to purple in color and bloom in late summer.

This plant thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in wet meadows, ditches, and along stream banks.
