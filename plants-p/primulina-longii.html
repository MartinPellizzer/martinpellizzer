Primulina longii

Primulina longii, commonly known as the longii African violet, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, velvety leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a compact, evergreen perennial with a rosette-shaped foliage structure. Its flowers, which are usually violet or purple in color, bloom in spring and are accompanied by a pleasant, subtle fragrance.

Primulina longii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
