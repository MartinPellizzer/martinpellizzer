Panicum rhabdinum

Panicum rhabdinum, commonly known as the slender panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open areas such as fields and disturbed sites.

Panicum rhabdinum is typically drought-tolerant and can withstand a range of environmental conditions.
