Polemonium mellitum var. speciosum

Polemonium mellitum var. speciosum, commonly known as the showy bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, showy flowers in shades of blue to purple. The leaves are lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
