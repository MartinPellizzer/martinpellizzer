Peperomia tubericordata

Peperomia tubericordata, commonly known as the tuberous heart peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often heart-shaped, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or purple in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia tubericordata is valued for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
