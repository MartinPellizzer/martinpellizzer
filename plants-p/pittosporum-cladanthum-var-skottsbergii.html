Pittosporum cladanthum var. skottsbergii

Pittosporum cladanthum var. skottsbergii, commonly known as the Skottsberg's pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by spherical, orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping due to its hardy nature.
