Phalaenopsis pulchra

Phalaenopsis pulchra, commonly known as the beautiful orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments. It prefers indirect light and consistent moisture, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value has made it a sought-after species in horticulture and botanical gardens.
