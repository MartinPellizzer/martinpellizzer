Pectis caymanensis var. caymanensis

Pectis caymanensis var. caymanensis, commonly known as the Cayman Islands pectis, is a flowering plant native to the Cayman Islands.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to saline and sandy soils. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences.

The species is notable for its resilience in tropical and subtropical environments.
