Psoralea Melilotoides

Psoralea melilotoides is a small, annual legume native to the Mediterranean region and parts of southern Europe. It is known for its delicate yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats. Despite its modest size, it plays an important role in local ecosystems by supporting pollinators and improving soil fertility. Its unique characteristics make it a fascinating subject for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
