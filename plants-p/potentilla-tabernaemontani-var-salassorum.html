Potentilla tabernaemontani var. salassorum

Potentilla tabernaemontani var. salassorum, commonly known as the Salasson potentilla, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. The plant features small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in a fine layer of soft, white hairs.

This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
