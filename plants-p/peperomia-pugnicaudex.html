Peperomia pugnicaudex

Peperomia pugnicaudex, commonly known as the battle-tail peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the rainforests of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are often dark green with a distinctive, almost metallic sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that emerge from leaf axils, followed by bright red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia pugnicaudex is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
