Pyrethrum nivale

Pyrethrum nivale, commonly known as snow pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe and Asia.

It produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves and compact growth habit. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine meadows.

Pyrethrum nivale is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential insecticidal properties.
