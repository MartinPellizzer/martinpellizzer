Pedicularis imperialis

It is a hemiparasitic plant that grows in alpine and subalpine regions, primarily in the European Alps and Scandinavia. Its roots form symbiotic relationships with mycorrhizal fungi, aiding in nutrient absorption.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered and is protected in many regions.
