Podoluma benai

Podoluma benai, commonly known as the Benai thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny, erect stems and dark green, lobed leaves. The plant produces pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators. Podoluma benai is often found in dry, rocky soils and can thrive in full sun.

Its botanical features make it an important component of its native ecosystem.
