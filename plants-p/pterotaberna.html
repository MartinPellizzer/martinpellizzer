Pterotaberna

Pterotaberna, commonly known as the feather-leaf plant, is a unique species characterized by its intricate, fern-like foliage.

It belongs to the family Pterotabernaceae and is native to specific regions in South America. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit with finely divided, lance-shaped leaves that give it a delicate appearance. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically producing a subtle fragrance.

Pterotaberna is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in shaded garden environments.
