Persicaria sagittata var. sieboldii

Persicaria sagittata var. sieboldii, commonly known as Siebold's arrowhead, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and glossy. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink to purple, which bloom in summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies, making it a useful plant for erosion control.
