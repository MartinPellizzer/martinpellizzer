Perezia macrocephala

Perezia macrocephala, commonly known as the large-headed perezia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its showy, fragrant blooms. The plant typically produces large, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, spurred lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Perezia macrocephala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
