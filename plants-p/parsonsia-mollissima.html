Parsonsia mollissima

Parsonsia mollissima, commonly known as the silky parsonsia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are typically alternate and have a soft, velvety texture.

Parsonsia mollissima is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils and saline conditions.
