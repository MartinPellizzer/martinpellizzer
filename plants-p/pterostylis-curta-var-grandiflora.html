Pterostylis curta var. grandiflora

Pterostylis curta var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered duck orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It features a slender, leafless stem that produces a single, showy, greenish-yellow flower with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. Its flowers are adapted for pollination by specific insects, particularly bees.

The species is notable for its unique morphology and ecological significance in its native habitat.
