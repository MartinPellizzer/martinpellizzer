Piper condotoense

Piper condotoense, commonly known as the Condoto pepper or Condoto pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the Andean region of South America, specifically Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with dark green leaves and small, clustered flowers. Its fruits are typically small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

Piper condotoense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance and potential medicinal properties.
