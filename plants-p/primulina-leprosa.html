Primulina leprosa

Primulina leprosa, commonly known as the Leprosa Calathea, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage. The leaves are typically broad and have a waxy texture, with colors ranging from deep green to purple and white. It thrives in low light conditions and requires consistent moisture and high humidity.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
