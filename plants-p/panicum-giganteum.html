Panicum giganteum

Panicum giganteum, commonly known as giant panicgrass, is a tall, perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features long, narrow leaves and produces fluffy, seed-bearing spikes that are often used in floral arrangements. The plant is characterized by its robust growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. It is often found in open areas, such as fields and disturbed sites, where it can spread rapidly.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is cultivated in gardens and landscapes worldwide.
