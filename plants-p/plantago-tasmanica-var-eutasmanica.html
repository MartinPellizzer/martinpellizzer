Plantago tasmanica var. eutasmanica

Plantago tasmanica var. eutasmanica, commonly known as the Tasmanian plantain, is a perennial herb native to Tasmania, Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and heathlands. The plant features a rosette of broad, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy surface. Its flowering stems bear dense clusters of small, greenish-white flowers.

This variant is distinguished by its slightly larger leaves and more robust growth compared to the typical form of Plantago tasmanica.
