Protea aspera

Protea aspera, commonly known as the rough protea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, spiky inflorescence. The plant typically grows in coastal regions and is adapted to nutrient-poor soils. Its flowers are usually pink or red, with a rough, hairy texture on the outer bracts.

Protea aspera is valued for its ornamental qualities and plays a role in local ecosystems.
