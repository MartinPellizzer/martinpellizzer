Peperomia mansericheana

Peperomia mansericheana, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to South America.

It features glossy, elliptical leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of green or red, which are not particularly showy. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a versatile addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
