Polypodium drepanopteron

Polypodium drepanopteron, commonly known as the narrow-leaf huperzia, is a species of fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, erect, and often branched stems covered with lance-shaped leaves. The leaves are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, with a distinct midrib and serrated margins. This plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, Africa, and the Americas.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
