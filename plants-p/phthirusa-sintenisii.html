Phthirusa sintenisii

Phthirusa sintenisii, commonly known as the Sintenisi's spider.

It is primarily found in specific microhabitats, such as moist, shaded areas in tropical or subtropical regions. The spider's web-spinning behavior is an essential part of its ecological role, contributing to local biodiversity.
