Platytaenia schischkinii

Platytaenia schischkinii, commonly known as the Schischkin's shield fern, is a rare and unique fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and leathery in texture. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds, a common feature among ferns.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, Platytaenia schischkinii is considered a conservation concern.
