Pittosporum oligodontum

Pittosporum oligodontum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 meters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath, with a distinctive narrow, elongated shape. Its flowers are small, white, and fragrant, appearing in clusters during the spring.

The fruit is a dark blue berry that contains several seeds, contributing to its role in local ecosystems.
