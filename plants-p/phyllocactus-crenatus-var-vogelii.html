Phyllocactus crenatus var. vogelii

Phyllocactus crenatus var. vogelii, commonly known as the Vogel's cactus, is a unique hybrid plant that combines characteristics of both cacti and euphorbias.

It features a thick, segmented stem covered in small, spiny tubercles, which provide structural support and aid in water retention. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding ornamental value. It is native to South America and thrives in well-draining soil with ample sunlight.

Due to its hybrid nature, it is relatively rare and often cultivated for its distinctive appearance.
