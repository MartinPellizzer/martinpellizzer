Polystichum mohrioides var. elegans

Polystichum mohrioides var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant water fern, is a species of fern native to certain regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically adorned with a waxy coating. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often near streams or in humid forests. Its rhizomes are creeping and help it spread along the forest floor.

The leaves are usually dark green and have a fine, feathery appearance, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
