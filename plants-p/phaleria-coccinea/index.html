Phaleria Coccinea

Phaleria coccinea is a strikingly colorful plant known for its vibrant red and orange hues. Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in warm, humid climates. This species belongs to the family Lauraceae and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts and gardeners. With proper care, Phaleria coccinea can flourish and add a splash of vivid color to any indoor or outdoor space.
