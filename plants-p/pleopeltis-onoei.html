Pleopeltis onoei

Pleopeltis onoei, commonly known as the Onoe's rock fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, leathery fronds that often grow on tree trunks or rocks. The plant has a compact rosette structure with closely spaced, lance-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slightly glossy surface. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Pleopeltis onoei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
