Polygonum blumei var. contractum

Polygonum blumei var. contractum, commonly known as the narrowleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes other species known for their distinctive leaf shapes and flowering structures. The plant typically features slender, upright stems with alternate, narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It produces small, clustered flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, often appearing in late summer.

This species is often found in moist environments such as wetlands and stream banks.
