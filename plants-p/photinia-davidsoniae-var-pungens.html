Photinia davidsoniae var. pungens

Photinia davidsoniae var. pungens, commonly known as the Davidson's photinia, is a shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and striking red or pink inflorescences that appear in spring. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The species is known for its hardiness and ability to tolerate urban environments.
