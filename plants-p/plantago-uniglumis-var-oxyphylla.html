Plantago uniglumis var. oxyphylla

Plantago uniglumis var. oxyphylla, commonly known as the oxyphylla plantain, is a variant of the common plantain species.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive waxy coating, and it produces small, greenish flowers in spikes. Its botanical characteristics include a fibrous root system and a robust growth habit.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is adaptable to various environmental conditions.
