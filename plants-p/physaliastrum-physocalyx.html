Physaliastrum physocalyx

Physaliastrum physocalyx, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a member of the Malvaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow, with five petals and numerous stamens.

The plant is often found in disturbed soils and can become invasive in certain areas.
