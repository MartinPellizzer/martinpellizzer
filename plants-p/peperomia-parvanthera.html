Peperomia parvanthera

Peperomia parvanthera, commonly known as the compact peperomia, is a small, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval or heart-shaped, with a deep green color and a slightly pubescent surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia parvanthera is popular as a houseplant due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.
