Pandanus subinermis

Pandanus subinermis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pandanus, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or green in color. It thrives in coastal and wetland environments, often growing in shallow water or on sandy soils.

Pandanus subinermis is an important species for both ecological and cultural reasons in its native regions.
