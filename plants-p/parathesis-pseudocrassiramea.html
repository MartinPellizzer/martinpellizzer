Parathesis pseudocrassiramea

Parathesis pseudocrassiramea, commonly known as the narrow-leaf parathesis, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in the summer.
