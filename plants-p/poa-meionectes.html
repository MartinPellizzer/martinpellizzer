Poa meionectes

Poa meionectes, commonly known as the slender meadow grass, is a species of grass native to southern South America.

It is a low-growing perennial plant that typically forms dense mats on the ground. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in moist, temperate climates and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Botanically, it is characterized by its fine texture and ability to form extensive vegetative cover.
