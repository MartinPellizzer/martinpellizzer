Prunus avium f. asplenifolia

Prunus avium f. asplenifolia, commonly known as the leafy cherry, is a unique cultivar of the sweet cherry tree.

It is characterized by its distinctive, finely serrated, fern-like leaves that give it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, sweet, red fruits similar to those of the common sweet cherry. It is typically grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.

The botanical features of this variety make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
