Phyllanthus salicifolius var. dracunculoides

Phyllanthus salicifolius var. dracunculoides, commonly known as the dragon's blood tree, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features lanceolate to ovate leaves that are often arranged oppositely on the stems. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that contains a single seed.

The species is notable for its ability to thrive in poor soils and its historical use in traditional medicine.
