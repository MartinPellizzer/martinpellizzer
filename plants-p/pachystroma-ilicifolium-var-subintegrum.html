Pachystroma ilicifolium var. subintegrum

Pachystroma ilicifolium var. subintegrum, commonly known as the smooth-leaved pachystroma, is a perennial shrub native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features small, elliptical leaves with a leathery texture and a distinctive silvery-white underside. Its flowers are small and clustered, producing a subtle fragrance that attracts pollinators.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and resilience in challenging climates.
