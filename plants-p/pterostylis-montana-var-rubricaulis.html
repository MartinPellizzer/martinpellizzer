Pterostylis montana var. rubricaulis

Pterostylis montana var. rubricaulis, commonly known as the red-stemmed helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It features a single, erect stem that is distinctly red or reddish in color, contrasting with its green leaves. The plant produces a single, helmet-shaped flower with a greenish-yellow dorsal sepal and a white or pale pink labellum. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests or under dense vegetation.

This orchid is noted for its unique morphology and is often found in association with other orchid species in its habitat.
