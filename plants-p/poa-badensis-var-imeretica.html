Poa badensis var. imeretica

Poa badensis var. imeretica, commonly known as the Imereti grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and narrow, bluish-green leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous areas. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
