Pinus caucasica

Pinus caucasica, commonly known as the Caucasian pine, is a coniferous tree native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and dense, pyramidal crown. The tree produces scale-like leaves that are dark green and arranged in dense clusters. Its cones are woody and typically measure between 5 to 10 centimeters in length.

The species is adapted to cold climates and is often found in mountainous areas with well-drained soils.
