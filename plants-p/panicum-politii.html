Panicum politii

Panicum politii, commonly known as the smooth panicgrass, is a species of grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are usually smooth to the touch. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically green to reddish in color. Panicum politii thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along the edges of ponds.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms.
