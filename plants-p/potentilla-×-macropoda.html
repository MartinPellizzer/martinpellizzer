Potentilla × macropoda

Potentilla × macropoda, commonly known as Shrub cinquefoil, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically features compound leaves with five leaflets, giving it the common name "cinquefoil.". It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or rocky areas.

Potentilla × macropoda is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.
