Peperomia granulatifolia

Peperomia granulatifolia, commonly known as the granulate peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a distinctive granular texture and a waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia granulatifolia is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
