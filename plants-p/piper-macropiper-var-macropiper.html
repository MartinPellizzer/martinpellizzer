Piper macropiper var. macropiper

Piper macropiper var. macropiper, commonly known as the large-leaf pepper plant, is a member of the Piperaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruits are tiny, three-lobed berries that turn red when mature.

This variant is valued for its aromatic leaves used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.
