Prostanthera ovalifolia

Prostanthera ovalifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color, arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a fragrant, minty scent.

Prostanthera ovalifolia is often found in dry, open habitats such as woodlands and heathlands.
