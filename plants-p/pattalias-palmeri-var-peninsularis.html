Pattalias palmeri var. peninsularis

It is a small, slow-growing palm with a short trunk and a dense crown of feathery leaves. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and coastal areas. Its flowers are small and clustered, producing yellowish-white inflorescences that attract various pollinators.
