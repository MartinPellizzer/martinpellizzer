Potentilla laciniosa var. samothracica

Potentilla laciniosa var. samothracica, commonly known as the Samothrace potentilla, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has finely toothed, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a soft, silvery pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in temperate climates.
