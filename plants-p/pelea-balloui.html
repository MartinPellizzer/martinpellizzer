Pelea balloui

Pelea balloui, commonly known as the Ballou's pelea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is a perennial species that thrives in full sun and well-drained environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
