Pleioblastus yingdeensis

Pleioblastus yingdeensis, commonly known as the Yingde bamboo, is a species of grass native to China.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect culms and fine, linear leaves. This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its elegant appearance and ability to thrive in shaded environments. The plant's botanical features make it a popular choice for landscaping and ecological restoration projects.
