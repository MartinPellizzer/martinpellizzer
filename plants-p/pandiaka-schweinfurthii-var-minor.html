Pandiaka schweinfurthii var. minor

Pandiaka schweinfurthii var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf pandiaka, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the island of Madagascar.

It features small, leathery leaves that are dark green and glossy, often with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a sought-after addition to botanical collections.
