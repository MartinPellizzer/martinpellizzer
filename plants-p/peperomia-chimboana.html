Peperomia chimboana

Peperomia chimboana, commonly known as the Chimboan peperomia, is a species native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It is a low-growing perennial plant characterized by its compact rosette of fleshy, oval-shaped leaves. The leaves are typically dark green with a waxy texture and often display subtle variegation. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color.

This plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its drought tolerance and minimal care requirements.
