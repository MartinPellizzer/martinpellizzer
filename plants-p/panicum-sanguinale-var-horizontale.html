Panicum sanguinale var. horizontale

Panicum sanguinale var. horizontale, commonly known as the horizontal bloodgrass, is a striking ornamental grass native to South America.

It features slender, arching blades that grow in a horizontal manner, creating a cascading effect. The plant produces delicate, pinkish-red inflorescences that add a vibrant touch to gardens. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for modern landscape designs.
