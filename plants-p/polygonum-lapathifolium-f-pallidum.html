Polygonum lapathifolium f. pallidum

Polygonum lapathifolium f. pallidum, commonly known as the paleleaf smartweed, is a variant of the species Polygonum lapathifolium, characterized by its pale green foliage.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to temperate regions of Asia, including China and Korea. The plant features erect, branching stems that are often reddish in color, and it produces small, clustered flowers in late summer. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth or slightly toothed margin.

This plant is often found in moist soils along stream banks and in wet meadows, where it thrives in partial to full sunlight.
