Plectranthus wui

Plectranthus wui, commonly known as the African Violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and produces small, tubular flowers in shades of purple and blue. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.

Plectranthus wui thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.
