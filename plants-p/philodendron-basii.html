Philodendron basii

Philodendron basii, commonly known as the silver vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves that often have a silvery sheen, giving it its common name. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth. Its stems are capable of trailing or climbing, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets or trained trellises.

Philodendron basii is also noted for its ability to purify indoor air, making it a favored houseplant.
