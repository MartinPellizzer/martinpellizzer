Poa trichodes

Poa trichodes, commonly known as the silky bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the temperate regions of North America.

It is characterized by its fine, bluish-green foliage and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and have a prominent midrib, while the inflorescence consists of a panicle of spikelets.

Poa trichodes is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soil in natural habitats.
