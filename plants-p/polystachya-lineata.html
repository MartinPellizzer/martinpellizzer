Polystachya lineata

Polystachya lineata, commonly known as the lineated flame bamboo, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of vibrant red and orange flowers. The plant features long, narrow leaves that are often lined with a distinctive white or yellow stripe, giving it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Polystachya lineata is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
