Pleione reichenbachiana

Pleione reichenbachiana, commonly known as the Reichenbach's pleione, is a rare and elegant orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually pink or white, with a distinctive, spurred lip that aids in pollination.

This orchid is appreciated for its beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
