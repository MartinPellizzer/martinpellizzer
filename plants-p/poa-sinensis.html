Poa sinensis

Poa sinensis, commonly known as Chinese bluegrass, is a cool-season grass native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted blades and a dense, carpet-like growth habit. The plant typically forms low-growing, mat-forming vegetation that thrives in moist, well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually bluish-green in color, and it produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

Poa sinensis is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to form a lush, uniform turf.
