Pleurothallis juvenilis

Pleurothallis juvenilis, commonly known as the baby pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features slender stems and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pink. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the humid forest floor.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists.
