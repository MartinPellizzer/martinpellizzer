Peperomia montefrionis

Peperomia montefrionis, commonly known as the Mexican peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Mexico.

It features oval, waxy, dark green leaves that are often slightly hairy and have a distinctive reddish underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of pink or white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia montefrionis is relatively low maintenance and resistant to pests, contributing to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
