Pycnocomon tataricum

Pycnocomon tataricum, commonly known as the Tatarian thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It features a robust root system and produces large, spiny, purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grayish-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in dry steppes and rocky soils.

The Tatarian thistle is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
