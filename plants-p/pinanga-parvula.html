Pinanga parvula

Pinanga parvula, commonly known as the small-leaf bamboo palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Indonesia and Malaysia. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Pinanga parvula produces small, white flowers followed by dark blue berries, contributing to its ecological and horticultural significance.
