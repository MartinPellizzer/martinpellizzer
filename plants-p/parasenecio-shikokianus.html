Parasenecio shikokianus

Parasenecio shikokianus, commonly known as the Shikoku ragwort, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its yellow-orange flowers bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
