Pachygenium itatiayae

Pachygenium itatiayae, commonly known as the Itatiaya giant lily, is a rare and striking plant native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It is characterized by its massive, white, bell-shaped flowers that can reach up to two meters in height. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its unique morphology makes it a subject of interest for botanists studying tropical flora.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is protected in several conservation areas.
