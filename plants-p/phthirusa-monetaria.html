Phthirusa monetaria

Phthirusa monetaria, commonly known as the money plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that bloom in clusters. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.

Phthirusa monetaria is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires moderate watering.
