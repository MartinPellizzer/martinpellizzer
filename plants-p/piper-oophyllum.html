Piper oophyllum

Piper oophyllum, commonly known as the eggplant pepper, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, round, purple berries that resemble tiny eggs, hence its common name. The plant belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a glossy appearance.

Piper oophyllum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
