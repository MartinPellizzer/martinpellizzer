Pelargonium glabrellum

Pelargonium glabrellum, commonly known as the Cape geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces vibrant pink or red flowers throughout the growing season. The plant features rounded leaves with a slightly hairy texture and a compact, bushy growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Pelargonium glabrellum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
