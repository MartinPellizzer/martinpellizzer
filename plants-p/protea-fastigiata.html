Protea fastigiata

Protea fastigiata, commonly known as the silver protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, narrow, evergreen leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces large, showy inflorescences composed of numerous small, white to pale pink flowers. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Protea fastigiata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
