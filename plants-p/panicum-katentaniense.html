Panicum katentaniense

Panicum katentaniense, commonly known as the Katentani grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands, savannas, and disturbed soils. The plant features erect, slender stems with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of a panicle made up of spikelets, which are small clusters of flowers.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
