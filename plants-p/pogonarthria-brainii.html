Pogonarthria brainii

Pogonarthria brainii, commonly known as the African grass or red grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, often reddish-colored stems. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically green or brown in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to semi-arid climates.

Pogonarthria brainii is often used in landscaping due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
