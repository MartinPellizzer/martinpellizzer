Parodia aureispina var. rubriantha

Parodia aureispina var. rubriantha, commonly known as the red-spined pincushion cactus, is a succulent native to southern Africa.

It features a compact, rounded body with dense clusters of bright yellow to orange spines, which are often tinged with red, giving it its common name. The plant produces pale yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its spiny structure. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
