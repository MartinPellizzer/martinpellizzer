Poa turfosa

Poa turfosa, commonly known as the tufted bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of North America.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and bluish-green foliage. The plant typically forms dense, low-growing mats, making it well-suited for lawns and erosion control. It prefers well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight.

Poa turfosa is valued for its durability and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
