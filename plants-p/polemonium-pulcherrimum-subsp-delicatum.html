Polemonium pulcherrimum subsp. delicatum

Polemonium pulcherrimum subsp.

delicatum, commonly known as the showy lady's mantle, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America. It features lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a distinctive waxy coating that gives them a shiny appearance.

The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, woodland habitats and is often found in shaded, well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and natural landscapes.
