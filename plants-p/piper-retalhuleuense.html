Piper retalhuleuense

Piper retalhuleuense, commonly known as the Retalhuleu pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central America, particularly in the country of Guatemala. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing vines and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its fruit is a tiny, elongated berry that contains a single seed.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and potential medicinal properties.
