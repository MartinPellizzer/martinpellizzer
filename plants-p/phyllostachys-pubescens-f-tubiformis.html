Phyllostachys pubescens f. tubiformis

Phyllostachys pubescens f. tubiformis, commonly known as the bamboo with tubular leaves, is a unique variant of the silver bamboo species.

It is characterized by its narrow, tubular-shaped leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant is a member of the grass family and is native to Asia, particularly China. It grows as a clump-forming bamboo, reaching heights of up to 10 meters.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes and bonsai cultivation.
