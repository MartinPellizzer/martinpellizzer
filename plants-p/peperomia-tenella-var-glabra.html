Peperomia tenella var. glabra

Peperomia tenella var. glabra, commonly known as the Smooth Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, waxy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a smooth, glossy surface. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale green, arranged in clusters. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for tabletop displays and as a decorative houseplant.
