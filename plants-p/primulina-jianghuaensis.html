Primulina jianghuaensis

Primulina jianghuaensis, commonly known as Jianghua's primula, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically features broad, velvety leaves and produces clusters of brightly colored blooms in shades of pink and white. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions with cool climates.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
