Poa aucklandica

Poa aucklandica, commonly known as Auckland grass, is a perennial grass native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems that often form dense clumps. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing tiny, two-lobed flowers. Its leaves are narrow, grass-like, and often have a bluish-green hue.

Poa aucklandica is adapted to a variety of soil types and is commonly found in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and forest margins.
