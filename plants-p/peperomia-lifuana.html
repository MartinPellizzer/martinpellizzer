Peperomia lifuana

Peperomia lifuana, commonly known as the Lifuana Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are often variegated with shades of white and green, giving it an attractive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal water needs.

Peperomia lifuana is valued for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments.
