Piper cararense

Piper cararense, commonly known as the Carare pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central America, particularly Belize and Guatemala. The plant is characterized by its vine-like growth habit and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its fruit, a bright red berry, is used in traditional medicine and as a spice.

Piper cararense is valued for its unique flavor and potential medicinal properties.
