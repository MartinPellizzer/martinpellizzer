Pavonia gossweileri

Pavonia gossweileri, commonly known as the yellow pavonia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its showy, clustered yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub with pinnately compound leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Pavonia gossweileri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
