Pyrrosia birii

Pyrrosia birii, commonly known as the Guangdong huperzia, is a species of fern native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its leathery, evergreen fronds that are typically dark green in color. The plant grows in shaded, moist environments, often on rocky or forested slopes. Its reproductive structures, including spore-producing sorangia, are located on the undersides of the fronds.

Pyrrosia birii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
