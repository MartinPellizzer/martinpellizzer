Pittosporum inopinatum

Pittosporum inopinatum, commonly known as the New Zealand pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

Pittosporum inopinatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
