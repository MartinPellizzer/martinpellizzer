Phreatia leptophylla

Phreatia leptophylla, commonly known as the slender-leafed phreatia, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and is typically found growing in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom during the rainy season. It is a terrestrial orchid that thrives in moist, well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it a unique and sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
