Peperomia quinquenervia

Peperomia quinquenervia, commonly known as the Five-Nerved Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves with a waxy texture and a striking, variegated coloration that often includes shades of green, white, and pink. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color, arranged in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia quinquenervia is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
