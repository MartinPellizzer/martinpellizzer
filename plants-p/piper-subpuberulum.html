Piper subpuberulum

Piper subpuberulum, commonly known as the pubescent pepper vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, woody stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture.
