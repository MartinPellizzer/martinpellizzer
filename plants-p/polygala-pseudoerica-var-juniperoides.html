Polygala pseudoerica var. juniperoides

Polygala pseudoerica var. juniperoides, commonly known as the Juniper-like Polygala, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in racemes. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and a branching structure that resembles juniper, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
