Polygala hebeclada var. latifolia

Polygala hebeclada var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved milkwort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, particularly China.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The leaves are typically broad and lance-shaped, with a smooth texture, and the flowers are usually pale pink or white. This species thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
