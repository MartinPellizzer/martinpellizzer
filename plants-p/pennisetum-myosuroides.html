Pennisetum myosuroides

Pennisetum myosuroides, commonly known as purple moondrop grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that produce fluffy, purple-pink seed heads, which give it its common name. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a soft, feathery texture that adds visual interest.

Pennisetum myosuroides is often used in floral arrangements due to its delicate and ephemeral beauty.
