Plectranthus suaveolens

Plectranthus suaveolens, commonly known as the sweet plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, fuzzy leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant is often grown as an ornamental due to its attractive foliage and pleasant fragrance. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Plectranthus suaveolens is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
