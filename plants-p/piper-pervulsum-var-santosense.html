Piper pervulsum var. santosense

Piper pervulsum var. santosense, commonly known as the Santos pepper or Brazilian pepper tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces berries that are typically yellow or orange when mature, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
