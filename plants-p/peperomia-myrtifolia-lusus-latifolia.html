Peperomia myrtifolia lusus latifolia

Peperomia myrtifolia lusus latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are broad and slightly wavy, often with a deep green color and a subtle sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
