Papaver zangnanense

Papaver zangnanense, commonly known as the Zangnan poppy, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Papaveraceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that range from red to orange. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and is adapted to temperate climates. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
