Pilosella stemmatina

Pilosella stemmatina, commonly known as the slender hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow composite flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Pilosella stemmatina is a perennial species that is native to Europe and parts of Asia.
