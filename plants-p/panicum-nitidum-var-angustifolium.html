Panicum nitidum var. angustifolium

Panicum nitidum var. angustifolium, commonly known as narrowleaf panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, grass-like blades with a narrow, linear shape and a smooth, glossy surface. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its inflorescence consists of a panicle with small, spikelet-like flowers.

It is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
