Panicum decaryanum

Panicum decaryanum, commonly known as Decary's panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in open areas such as savannas and disturbed soils, showcasing a robust growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous florets. Its fibrous roots contribute to soil stabilization, making it ecologically significant in certain habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental potential and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
