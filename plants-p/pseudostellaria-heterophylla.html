Pseudostellaria heterophylla

Pseudostellaria heterophylla, commonly known as the common starwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters, and its leaves are usually alternate and lance-shaped. It has a fibrous root system and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

The species is known for its adaptability and is often found in disturbed or semi-natural habitats.
