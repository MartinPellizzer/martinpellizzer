Potentilla argentea var. thyrsiflora

Potentilla argentea var. thyrsiflora, commonly known as silver potentilla or silver thistle, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and frost-resistant nature.
