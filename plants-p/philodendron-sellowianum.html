Philodendron sellowianum

Philodendron sellowianum, commonly known as the Sellow's philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slight variegation. The plant has a vigorous growth habit and can reach lengths of several meters when provided with support. Its stems are usually green and can develop a reddish hue under certain conditions.

It is well-suited for indoor cultivation and thrives in bright, indirect light.
