Pennisetum violaceum var. chudeaui

Pennisetum violaceum var. chudeaui, commonly known as the purple fountain grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to South Africa.

It features slender, arching stems that produce feathery plumes of purple and pink flowers in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are bluish-green in color and contribute to its elegant appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping and drought-tolerant gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract birds and butterflies, adding ecological benefits to the landscape.
