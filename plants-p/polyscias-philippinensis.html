Polyscias philippinensis

Polyscias philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine glorybower, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in umbels, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated as a houseplant and in gardens.
