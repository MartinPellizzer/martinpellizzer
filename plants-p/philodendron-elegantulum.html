Philodendron elegantulum

Philodendron elegantulum, commonly known as the silver velvet philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Philodendron elegantulum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
