Ptychosperma commune

Ptychosperma commune, commonly known as the African umbrella tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and Southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, umbrella-like leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in diameter. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by black, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ptychosperma commune is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.
