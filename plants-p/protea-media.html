Protea media

Protea media, commonly known as the giant protea, is a striking member of the Proteaceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence composed of numerous white to pink bracts that surround a central cluster of small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its foliage consists of long, narrow, leathery leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems.

Protea media thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in suitable climates.
