Peperomia vogelii

Peperomia vogelii, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, usually white or pale green, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia vogelii is known for its low maintenance and ability to adapt to a variety of indoor environments.
