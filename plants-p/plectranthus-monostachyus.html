Plectranthus monostachyus

Plectranthus monostachyus, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or blue in color. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and blooms.

Plectranthus monostachyus prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
