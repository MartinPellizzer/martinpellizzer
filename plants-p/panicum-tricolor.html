Panicum tricolor

Panicum tricolor, commonly known as rainbow grass, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking tri-colored inflorescence, which typically displays red, purple, and green hues. The plant belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its vibrant appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its growth habit is clump-forming, with slender, grass-like leaves that contribute to its aesthetic appeal.
