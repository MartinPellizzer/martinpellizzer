Panicum kotschyanum

Panicum kotschyanum, commonly known as Kotschyan's panicgrass, is a flowering plant species native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets, typical of many grass species. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects in arid regions.
