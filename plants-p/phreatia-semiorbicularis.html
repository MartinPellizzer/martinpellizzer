Phreatia semiorbicularis

Phreatia semiorbicularis, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its robust, rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces large, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration, often featuring a yellow lip and purple or brown markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is typically found in forest understories.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and ecological role in tropical ecosystems.
