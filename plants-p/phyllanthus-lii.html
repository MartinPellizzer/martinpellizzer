Phyllanthus lii

Phyllanthus lii, commonly known as the Chinese phyllanthus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is native to southern China and is characterized by its small, green, fleshy leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

Phyllanthus lii is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
