Pseudosedum kuramense

Pseudosedum kuramense, commonly known as the Kurame sedum, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Japan.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in arid environments. Pseudosedum kuramense is valued for its low maintenance requirements and ability to adapt to various growing conditions.
