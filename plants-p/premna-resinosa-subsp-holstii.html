Premna resinosa subsp. holstii

Premna resinosa subsp.

holstii, commonly known as the silky balsa or silky premna, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia. It is a small to medium-sized shrub or tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, spherical berries.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
