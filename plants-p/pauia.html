Pauia

Pauia, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to red, attracting various pollinators. Its seeds are encased in a hairy, spiny capsule that aids in seed dispersal.
