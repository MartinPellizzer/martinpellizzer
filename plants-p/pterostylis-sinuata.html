Pterostylis sinuata

Pterostylis sinuata, commonly known as the sinuous spider-orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows on tree trunks or branches in moist, shaded environments. The plant features a slender, wiry stem with a single, narrow leaf. Its flowers are small, greenish-brown, and have a distinctive, elongated spur.

It is a rare and protected species, often found in subtropical rainforests.
