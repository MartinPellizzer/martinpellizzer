Phyllanthus formosus

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and smooth, glossy leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
