Phyllanthus borneensis

Phyllanthus borneensis, commonly known as Bornean phyllanthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Borneo.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical rainforests, often in shaded, moist environments. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate or elliptical in shape. Its small, yellowish-green flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed.

The plant is valued for its potential medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
