Palaquium richardsii

Palaquium richardsii, commonly known as the Richards' palaquium, is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences, followed by spherical fruits containing a single seed.
