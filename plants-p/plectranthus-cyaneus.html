Plectranthus cyaneus

Plectranthus cyaneus, commonly known as blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a mist-like appearance. The plant has opposite, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Plectranthus cyaneus is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
