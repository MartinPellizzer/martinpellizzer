Potentilla × mischkinii

Potentilla × mischkinii, commonly known as the Mischkin cinquefoil, is a hybrid shrub native to the western United States.

It typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features dense, compact growth with bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has finely divided, dark green foliage that provides a striking contrast to its vibrant blossoms. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is well-suited for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Potentilla × mischkinii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
