Peperomia reflexa var. capensis

Peperomia reflexa var. capensis, commonly known as the Cape peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with a distinctive waxy coating and prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically hidden beneath the leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a versatile and low-maintenance houseplant.
