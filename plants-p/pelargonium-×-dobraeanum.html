Pelargonium × dobraeanum

Pelargonium × dobraeanum, commonly known as the Dobraean geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and purple. The plant typically has upright stems and broad, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a bedding plant and in rock gardens.
