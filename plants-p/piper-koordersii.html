Piper koordersii

Piper koordersii, commonly known as the Koorders' pepper vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various pepper species. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that develop into clustered berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its ornamental value and unique foliage make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and greenhouses.
