Peperomia sellowiana

Peperomia sellowiana, commonly known as the Sellow's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a compact and decorative appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, which appear in clusters during the growing season. It is a popular houseplant due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia sellowiana is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments.
