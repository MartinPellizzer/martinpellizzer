Phyllanthus subcarnosus

Phyllanthus subcarnosus, commonly known as the African spurge, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant has small, elliptical leaves with a waxy texture, and its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow. It produces small, fleshy fruits that contain seeds.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
