Polycodium neglectum

Polycodium neglectum, commonly known as the neglected polycodium, is a small, herbaceous plant native to certain regions of North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features delicate, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are usually greenish-white in color and appear in clusters.

Polycodium neglectum is relatively rare and has limited distribution, making it of interest to botanists and conservationists.
