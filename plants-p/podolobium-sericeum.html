Podolobium sericeum

Podolobium sericeum, commonly known as the silky podolobium, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant features slender, branching stems covered in a fine, silky hair that gives it its common name. Its small, pea-like flowers are typically white or pale yellow, and it produces elongated, hairy pods that contain seeds.

This species is often found in open woodland and savanna habitats, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
