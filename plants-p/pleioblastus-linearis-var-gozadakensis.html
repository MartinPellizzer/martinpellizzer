Pleioblastus linearis var. gozadakensis

Pleioblastus linearis var. gozadakensis, commonly known as the Gozadakensis bamboo, is a slender, clump-forming bamboo native to southern China.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically a fresh green color, giving it a delicate and elegant appearance. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to form dense stands, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, thriving in temperate climates.

This variety is often used in garden design for its aesthetic appeal and ease of maintenance.
