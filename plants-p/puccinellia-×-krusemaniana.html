Puccinellia × krusemaniana

Puccinellia × krusemaniana, commonly known as the hybrid glassworts, is a halophytic plant that thrives in saline and coastal environments.

It is a sterile hybrid between Puccinellia maritima and Puccinellia tenuiflora, exhibiting characteristics of both parent species. The plant has fleshy, succulent leaves that store water, aiding in its survival in arid and salty conditions. It produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.

This species plays a role in stabilizing coastal soils and is often found in salt marshes and estuarine areas.
