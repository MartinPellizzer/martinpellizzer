Patrinia hispida

Patrinia hispida, commonly known as Chinese burnet, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, upright stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate in shape and covered with fine, stiff hairs.

Patrinia hispida is often used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
