Pilea microphylla var. tenerrima

Pilea microphylla var. tenerrima, commonly known as the Chinese money plant or pancake plant, is a compact, low-growing species native to China.

It features rounded, fleshy leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy finish. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
