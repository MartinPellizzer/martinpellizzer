Polyscias grandifolia

Polyscias grandifolia, commonly known as the large-leaf soursop, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a spiny, green berry that contains a white, pulpy flesh with black seeds.

Polyscias grandifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
