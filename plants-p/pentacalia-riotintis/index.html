Pentacalia Riotintis

Pentacalia riotintis is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Mexico. It is known for its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red and orange, making it a standout in any garden. This species belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Due to its limited habitat and threats from habitat loss, Pentacalia riotintis is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and beautiful plant for future generations.
