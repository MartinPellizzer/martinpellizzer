Peperomia foliosa

Peperomia foliosa, commonly known as the Shiny Leaf Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture, often adorned with silver or white veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which are followed by dark red to black berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia foliosa is valued for its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
