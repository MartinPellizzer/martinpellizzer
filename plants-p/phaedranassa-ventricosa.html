Phaedranassa ventricosa

Phaedranassa ventricosa, commonly known as the red-flowered plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or purple flowers and hairy, glandular leaves. The plant belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky areas.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
