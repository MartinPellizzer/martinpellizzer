Protium costaricense

Protium costaricense, commonly known as the Costa Rican wild coffee.

It is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central America, particularly Costa Rica and Panama. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves with a glossy dark green upper surface and a lighter underside. Its small, white flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces small, spherical fruits that turn from green to red when mature.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine.
