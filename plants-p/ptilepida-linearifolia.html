Ptilepida linearifolia

Ptilepida linearifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ptilepida, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice among gardeners seeking low-maintenance plants.
