Phaius blumei var. pulchra

Phaius blumei var. pulchra, commonly known as the Pink Orchid, is a striking orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to white. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, lip-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and ease of cultivation.
