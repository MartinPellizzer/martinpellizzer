Pedilonum calicopis

Pedilonum calicopis, commonly known as the calyx-leafed pedilonum, is a rare and unique plant species characterized by its distinctive floral structure.

It features a calyx that resembles a leaf, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It is native to a specific region in South America and thrives in humid, shaded environments.
