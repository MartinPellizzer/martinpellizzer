Primula simsii

Primula simsii, commonly known as the Chinese primrose, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters and features dark green, velvety leaves.

It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
