Persicaria angustifolia

Persicaria angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf smartweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and small, pinkish-white flowers that appear in clusters. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, stream banks, and ditches. It has a fibrous root system and produces seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Persicaria angustifolia is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in partial to full sunlight.
