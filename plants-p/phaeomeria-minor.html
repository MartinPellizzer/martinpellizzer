Phaeomeria minor

Phaeomeria minor, commonly known as the lesser phaeomeria, is a small, annual herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces tiny, greenish flowers that are typically inconspicuous and not showy. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.
