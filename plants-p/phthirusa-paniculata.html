Phthirusa paniculata

Phthirusa paniculata, commonly known as the clustered panicled orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, clustered inflorescences. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the nutrient-poor soils in which it thrives.

Phthirusa paniculata is often found in humid, shaded environments such as cloud forests and mountainous regions.
