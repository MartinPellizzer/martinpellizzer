Pfaffia spicata var. pretensis

Pfaffia spicata var. pretensis, commonly known as the Pretoria arrowroot, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Marantaceae and is characterized by its erect, slender stems and lanceolate leaves. The species is noted for its ornamental value and has been used traditionally in some African cultures for its purported medicinal properties.
