Plantago Subulata Var. Radicata

Plantago subulata var. radicata is a hardy perennial plant known for its ability to thrive in harsh environments. It is commonly found in rocky soils and open areas where few other plants can survive. This variant of Plantago subulata has a compact growth habit and produces small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are clustered in dense spikes and bloom in the early spring. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.
