Pavetta tomentosa var. glabrescens

Pavetta tomentosa var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth pavetta, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is a shrub characterized by its hairy leaves that become smoother as the plant matures. The flowers are typically small and clustered, often in inflorescences that are pink or white in color. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
