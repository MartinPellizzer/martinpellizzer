Persea splendens var. chrysophylla

Persea splendens var. chrysophylla, commonly known as the golden leaf mahogany, is a tropical tree native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow leaves, which contrast with its dark green foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by edible fruits that are rich in nutrients. Its bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in its native habitat.
