Phrygilanthus sternbergianus

Phrygilanthus sternbergianus, commonly known as the desert broom.

It is a shrub native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant has small, narrow leaves and produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies in desert ecosystems.
