Parahebe vandewateri

Parahebe vandewateri, commonly known as the Vandewater parahebe, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to New Zealand.

It features dense, glossy green foliage with a compact, mat-like growth habit. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white and pale blue, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This ornamental plant is valued for its attractive appearance and ability to form dense ground cover in woodland settings.
