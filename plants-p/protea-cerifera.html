Protea cerifera

Protea cerifera, commonly known as the candle protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence that resembles a candle, composed of numerous small, tubular flowers. The plant is native to the Western and Eastern Cape provinces of South Africa, where it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. Protea cerifera is a shrub that can grow up to 2 meters tall, featuring leathery, evergreen leaves.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also an important species in local ecosystems.
