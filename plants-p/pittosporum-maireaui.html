Pittosporum maireaui

Pittosporum maireaui, commonly known as the Maireaui pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage with dark green, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are followed by yellowish berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers a sunny to partially shaded location.

Pittosporum maireaui is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
