Pelargonium × translucens

Pelargonium × translucens, commonly known as the translucent geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its unique foliage, which features a waxy, translucent appearance on the underside of its leaves. This plant typically grows as an upright shrub with stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The flowers are usually pink or red, with five petals and a prominent central stamen.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
