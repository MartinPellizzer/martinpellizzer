Philibertia pannosa

Philibertia pannosa, commonly known as the woolly filbert, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Philibertia pannosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
