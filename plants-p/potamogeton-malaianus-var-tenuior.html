Potamogeton malaianus var. tenuior

Potamogeton malaianus var. tenuior, commonly known as the slender water milfoil, is a submerged aquatic plant native to parts of Asia.

It typically grows in freshwater environments such as lakes, ponds, and slow-moving rivers. The plant has slender, branching stems with small, opposite leaves that are often narrowly elliptical in shape. Its flowers are unisexual, with male and female flowers occurring on separate plants.

It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.
