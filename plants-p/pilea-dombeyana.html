Pilea dombeyana

Pilea dombeyana, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features distinctive, round, fleshy leaves that resemble small coins, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea dombeyana is known for its ability to propagate easily through leaf cuttings, enhancing its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
