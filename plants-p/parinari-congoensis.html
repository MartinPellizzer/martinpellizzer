Parinari congoensis

Parinari congoensis, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a tropical tree native to Central and West Africa.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and dense, evergreen foliage. However, due to overharvesting and habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable in some regions.
