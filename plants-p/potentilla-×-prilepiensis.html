Potentilla × prilepiensis

Potentilla × prilepiensis, commonly known as the Prilep potentilla, is a hybrid shrub native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces showy, yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and features small, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy and low-maintenance plant for gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
