Piptadenia novoguineensis

Piptadenia novoguineensis, commonly known as the Guianese locust tree, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America and parts of Central America. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and produces yellow flowers that are often clustered in racemes. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds.

It is often used in traditional medicine and has ecological importance in its native habitats.
