Peperomia kunthiana var. puberula

Peperomia kunthiana var. puberula, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to South America.

It features thick, waxy, and velvety leaves that are typically dark green with a subtle sheen. The leaves are usually ovate to elliptical in shape and often have a slightly pointed tip. This variety is known for its compact growth habit and is well-suited for indoor cultivation.

It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color.
