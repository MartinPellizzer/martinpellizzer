Peperomia leucostachya

Peperomia leucostachya, commonly known as the white-stemmed peperomia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves with a slightly waxy texture and produces small, white or pale green flowers. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Peperomia leucostachya is also valued for its ornamental appeal and air-purifying qualities.
