Palisota bicolor

Palisota bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored nettle, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its distinctive bi-colored leaves, often with a lighter underside. The plant belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes other stinging nettles. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs that can cause skin irritation.

Palisota bicolor is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.
