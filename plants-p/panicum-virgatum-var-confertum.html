Panicum virgatum var. confertum

Panicum virgatum var. confertum, commonly known as switchgrass, is a warm-season grass native to the United States.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces fluffy, seed-bearing panicles that are often used in ornamental landscaping. It is highly adaptable, thriving in a variety of soil types and conditions.

Switchgrass is also valued for its potential in biofuel production and erosion control.
