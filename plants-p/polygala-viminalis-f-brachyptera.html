Polygala viminalis f. brachyptera

Polygala viminalis f. brachyptera, commonly known as the short-winged milkwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Polygalaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its small, clustered flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. The plant features slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. Native to arid and semi-arid regions, it thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

This variety is noted for its unique floral structure and is of interest in botanical studies due to its ecological and evolutionary significance.
