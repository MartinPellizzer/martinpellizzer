Pothuava nudicaulis

Pothuava nudicaulis, commonly known as the slender pothos, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically light green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Pothuava nudicaulis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
