Pilea astrogramma

Pilea astrogramma, commonly known as the star mottled pilea, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to China.

It features rounded, slightly hairy leaves that are often mottled with silver or white markings, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers during the spring and summer months. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its slow growth and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to any indoor garden.
