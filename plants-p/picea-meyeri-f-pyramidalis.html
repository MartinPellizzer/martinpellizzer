Picea meyeri f. pyramidalis

Picea meyeri f. pyramidalis, commonly known as the pyramidal spruce, is a cultivar of the white spruce.

It is characterized by its narrow, conical shape and dark green, needle-like foliage. This evergreen shrub or small tree is native to the mountainous regions of central Asia. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a specimen plant.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in colder climates.
