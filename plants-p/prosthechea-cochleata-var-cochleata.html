Prosthechea cochleata var. cochleata

Prosthechea cochleata var. cochleata, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in a spiral pattern along the stem. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
