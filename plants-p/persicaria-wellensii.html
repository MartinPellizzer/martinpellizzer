Persicaria wellensii

Persicaria wellensii, commonly known as the red stemmed filbert.

It is a herbaceous perennial native to Asia, characterized by its upright growth habit and striking red stems. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers in clusters, which are typically borne on slender pedicels. Its foliage is lance-shaped and has a slightly hairy texture, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Persicaria wellensii thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its attractive foliage and seasonal color changes.
