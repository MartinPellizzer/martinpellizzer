Protea scabra

Protea scabra, commonly known as the hard-leaved protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and showy, cone-like inflorescences that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is typically found in fynbos vegetation. It is adapted to Mediterranean climates with wet winters and dry summers.

Protea scabra is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in local ecosystems.
