Panicum vollesenii

Panicum vollesenii, commonly known as the Vollesen's panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces fluffy, spike-like inflorescences that are often pink or purple in color. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its distinctive appearance.

Due to its hardy nature, it is relatively low maintenance and adaptable to various environmental conditions.
