Protasparagus suaveolens

Protasparagus suaveolens, commonly known as the sweet-scented asparagus, is a herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, fragrant leaves and stems. The plant produces small, white to greenish-white flowers that are clustered in panicles. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a soft, velvety texture.

Protasparagus suaveolens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant fragrance.
