Philodendron melanum

Philodendron melanum, commonly known as the black satin plant, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features dark green, waxy leaves that are often glossy and have a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. Philodendron melanum thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent humidity and well-draining soil.

It is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
