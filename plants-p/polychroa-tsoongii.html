Polychroa tsoongii

Polychroa tsoongii, commonly known as the Chinese orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in cool, humid climates. Its flowers exhibit a range of colors, including white, pink, and purple, making it a visually striking ornamental plant.

Polychroa tsoongii is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
