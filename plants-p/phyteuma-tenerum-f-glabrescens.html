Phyteuma tenerum f. glabrescens

Phyteuma tenerum f. glabrescens, commonly known as the soft-haired phyteuma, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to purple. The plant has finely textured, hairy leaves that are often covered in a soft, downy layer, giving it its specific epithet "glabrescens.". It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its blooming period typically occurs in late spring to early summer.
