Polygonum roxburghii var. longifolium

Polygonum roxburghii var. longifolium, commonly known as the long-leaved smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia.

It typically grows in wet or marshy environments, showcasing its adaptability to moist soils. The plant features slender, upright stems that bear alternate, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink or red, blooming in late summer to early autumn.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in wetland habitats.
