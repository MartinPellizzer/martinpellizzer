Pelargonium × magnifolium

Pelargonium × magnifolium, commonly known as the African marigold, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant typically has a compact, bushy growth habit with dark green, velvety leaves. Native to South Africa, it thrives in warm climates and well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
