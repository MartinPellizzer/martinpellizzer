Persicaria iseana

Persicaria iseana, commonly known as the slender knotweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern North America.

It features upright, slender stems that are often reddish in color and bear small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and other riparian habitats.

Persicaria iseana is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent and sometimes invasive species in certain environments.
