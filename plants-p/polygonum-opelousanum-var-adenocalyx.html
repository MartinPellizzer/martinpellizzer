Polygonum opelousanum var. adenocalyx

Polygonum opelousanum var. adenocalyx, commonly known as the腺萼虎耳草, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of China.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its succulent leaves and distinctive inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are often arranged in a rosette pattern, and the flowers are small, clustered, and usually pink or red in color.

The variety adenocalyx is distinguished by its unique calyx structure, which is glandular and contributes to its scientific name.
