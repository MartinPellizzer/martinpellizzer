It is characterized by its thallus that is typically green to yellowish green in color, with a distinct, arrow-shaped lobes. The lichen grows on bark, stones, and soil in temperate and tropical regions. It is a symbiotic organism composed of a fungal partner and a green algal partner.

Paratinospora sagittata is often found in humid environments and is used in ecological studies to monitor air quality.
