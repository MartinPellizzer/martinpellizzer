Panicum mandshuricum var. pekinense

Panicum mandshuricum var. pekinense, commonly known as the Peking Panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to eastern Asia.

It features tall, slender stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are usually gray-green in color. The plant produces fluffy, light brown seed heads that are characteristic of the Panicum genus. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in open habitats such as meadows and grasslands.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting local wildlife.
