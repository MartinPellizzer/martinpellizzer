Pterocarya macroptera var. macroptera

Pterocarya macroptera var. macroptera, commonly known as the Chinese wingnut, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Juglandaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive winged samaras, which are its seeds. The tree has compound leaves with serrated edges and can grow up to 20 meters in height. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions.
