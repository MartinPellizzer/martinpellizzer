Pilosella longisquama subsp. naretensis

Pilosella longisquama subsp.

naretensis, commonly known as the Naretan hairy eyebright, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves, which are typically covered in fine, white pubescence.

The plant produces small, yellowish to white composite flowers arranged in flat-topped clusters. It is native to the alpine regions of Europe, particularly in the Carpathian Mountains.

This subspecies is adapted to rocky and dry habitats, showcasing a compact growth form and drought tolerance.
