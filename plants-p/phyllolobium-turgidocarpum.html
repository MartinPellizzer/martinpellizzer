Phyllolobium turgidocarpum

Phyllolobium turgidocarpum, commonly known as the swollen-fruit phyllolobium, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive botanical features.

The plant is notable for its thick, fleshy fruits that remain on the plant for an extended period, a trait that aids in seed dispersal.
