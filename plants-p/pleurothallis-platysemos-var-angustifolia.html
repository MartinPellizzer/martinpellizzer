Pleurothallis platysemos var. angustifolia

Pleurothallis platysemos var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique inflorescence structure.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists.
