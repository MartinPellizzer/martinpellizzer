Psittacanthus salicifolius

Psittacanthus salicifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf hoya, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and hanging baskets.
