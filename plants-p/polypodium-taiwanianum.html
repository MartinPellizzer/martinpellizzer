Polypodium taiwanianum

Polypodium taiwanianum, commonly known as the Taiwan huperzia, is a species of fern native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the family Huperziaceae and is characterized by its evergreen, leathery fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
