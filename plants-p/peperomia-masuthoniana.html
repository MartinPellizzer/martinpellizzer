Peperomia masuthoniana

Peperomia masuthoniana, commonly known as the African violet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features dense, waxy foliage with elliptical to ovate leaves that are often dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white or pale pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia masuthoniana is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
