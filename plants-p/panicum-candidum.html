Panicum candidum

Panicum candidum, commonly known as white panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a grass species that typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as roadsides and disturbed soils. The plant produces fluffy, white plumes that are highly visible and often used in floral arrangements. Its slender, upright stems and small, narrow leaves are characteristic of the Panicum genus.

It is generally drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils.
