Peperomia san-buenaventurana

Peperomia san-buenaventurana, commonly known as the San Buenaventura peperomia, is a compact, low-growing shrub native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval or elliptic in shape, with a vibrant green coloration. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually borne in clusters, adding subtle ornamental value. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia san-buenaventurana is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.
