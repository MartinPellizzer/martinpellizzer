Pilosella stupposipila

Pilosella stupposipila, commonly known as the slender goat's beard, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in a dense, spherical inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has hairy stems and leaves, which give it its scientific name "Pilosella," meaning "hairy.".

Pilosella stupposipila is native to Europe and parts of Asia, where it thrives in temperate climates.
