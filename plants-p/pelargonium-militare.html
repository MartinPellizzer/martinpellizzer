Pelargonium militare

Pelargonium militare, commonly known as the military geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces distinctive, palmate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a bedding plant.

Pelargonium militare is relatively drought-tolerant and prefers well-drained soil.
