Polygonum × lenticulare

Polygonum × lenticulare, commonly known as the red polygonum, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Polygonaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking red stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often tinged with purple. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and used in ecological restoration projects.
