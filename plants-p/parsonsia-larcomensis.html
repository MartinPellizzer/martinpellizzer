Parsonsia larcomensis

Parsonsia larcomensis, commonly known as the larcomensis flameflower, is a species of flowering plant in the Apocynaceae family.

It is native to the coastal regions of eastern Australia, particularly Queensland and New South Wales. The plant is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged opposite along the stems.
