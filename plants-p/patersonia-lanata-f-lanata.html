Patersonia lanata f. lanata

Patersonia lanata f. lanata, commonly known as the woolly patersonia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its hairy, woolly stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Patersonia lanata f. lanata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
