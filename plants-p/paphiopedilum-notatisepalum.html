Paphiopedilum notatisepalum

Paphiopedilum notatisepalum, commonly known as the spotted lip orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a thick, fleshy rhizome and a single, fleshy pseudobulb. The plant produces showy, greenish-yellow flowers with distinctive red or purple markings on the lip. Its leaves are broad, elliptical, and often have a waxy texture.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil for optimal growth.
