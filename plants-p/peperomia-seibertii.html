Peperomia seibertii

Peperomia seibertii, commonly known as the Shiny Peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which are followed by reddish-brown berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia seibertii is a low-maintenance plant suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation in suitable climates.
