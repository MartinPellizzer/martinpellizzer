Peperomia albopilosa

Peperomia albopilosa, commonly known as the white-haired peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable addition to both indoor and outdoor gardens.
