Pilea deltoidea

Pilea deltoidea, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Urticaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its round, succulent leaves that resemble small coins. The plant typically grows as a low shrub with dense foliage and produces small, inconspicuous flowers. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

Pilea deltoidea is popular as a decorative plant for its attractive appearance and ease of care.
