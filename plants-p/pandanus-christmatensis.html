Pandanus christmatensis

Pandanus christmatensis, commonly known as the Christmas pandanus, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and long, narrow leaves that form a dense crown. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or green in color. Its fruit is a large, spiky structure that contains numerous seeds.

This species is often used in traditional crafts and is also valued for its ornamental appearance.
