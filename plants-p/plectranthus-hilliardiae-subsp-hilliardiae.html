Plectranthus hilliardiae subsp. hilliardiae

Plectranthus hilliardiae subsp.

hilliardiae, commonly known as the African violet, is a species native to South Africa. It is a flowering plant characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers.

The plant typically grows in a compact, bushy form, with leaves that are broad and heart-shaped. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

This subspecies is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
