Pimelea brevituba

Pimelea brevituba, commonly known as the narrow tube wattle, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its small, yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, tubular leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and coastal areas.

Pimelea brevituba is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
