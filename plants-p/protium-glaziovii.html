Protium glaziovii

Protium glaziovii, commonly known as the Brazilian sandarac tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Amazon Basin.

It belongs to the Burseraceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and elongated, elliptical leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its fruit is a globose, woody capsule that splits open to release three winged seeds.
