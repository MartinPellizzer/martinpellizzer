Polygonum heterophyllum var. rubescens

Polygonum heterophyllum var. rubescens, commonly known as red-stem smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its distinct nodes and swollen stems. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as wetlands, marshes, and stream banks. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a reddish tint, while the flowers are small and clustered in spikes.

The species is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed soils.
