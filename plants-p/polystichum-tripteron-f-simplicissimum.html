Polystichum tripteron f. simplicissimum

Polystichum tripteron f. simplicissimum, commonly known as the simple-leaf barren fern, is a fern species characterized by its distinctive, lance-shaped fronds.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is native to certain regions of Asia. The plant features a compact, upright growth habit with dark green, leathery leaves that have a smooth, non-lobed margin. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.
