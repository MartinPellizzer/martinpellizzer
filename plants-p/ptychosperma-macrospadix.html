Ptychosperma macrospadix

Ptychosperma macrospadix, commonly known as the giant papyrus, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces strikingly large, cylindrical inflorescences that are often mistaken for stems, which contain numerous small white flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental cultivation in warm climates.
