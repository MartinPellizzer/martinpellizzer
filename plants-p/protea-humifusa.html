Protea humifusa

Protea humifusa, commonly known as the coastal protea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, inflorescence composed of numerous tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils. It is part of the Proteaceae family, which includes many other ornamental and ecologically significant plants.

Protea humifusa is often cultivated for its aesthetic value and plays a role in local ecosystems.
