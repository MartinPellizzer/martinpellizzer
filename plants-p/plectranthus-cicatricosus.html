Plectranthus cicatricosus

Plectranthus cicatricosus, commonly known as the wounded leaf plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often marked with scars, giving it its scientific name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of purple or blue, typically during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
