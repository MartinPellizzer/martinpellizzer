Platantheroides clavigera

Platantheroides clavigera, commonly known as the slender lady's tresses, is a perennial orchid native to wetlands and marshy areas in North America.

It features a slender, erect stem with a single, loose spike of pale green to yellowish flowers. The plant has a fibrous root system and produces small, ovate leaves that are arranged in a rosette at the base. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in bogs and along the edges of ponds.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and ecological importance in wetland ecosystems.
