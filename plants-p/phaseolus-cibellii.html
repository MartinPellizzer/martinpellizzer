Phaseolus cibellii

Phaseolus cibellii, commonly known as the scarlet runner bean, is a climbing legume native to the Americas.

It produces striking red or orange flowers and edible pods that contain kidney-shaped beans. The plant thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Its leaves are compound, consisting of three to five oval-shaped leaflets.

Phaseolus cibellii is valued for its ornamental appeal and culinary use in various cuisines.
