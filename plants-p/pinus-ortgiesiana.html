Pinus ortgiesiana

Pinus ortgiesiana, commonly known as the Ortgies' pine, is a species of evergreen conifer native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, needle-like leaves that are typically arranged in bundles of five. The tree produces cones that are often reddish-brown in color and contain edible seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
