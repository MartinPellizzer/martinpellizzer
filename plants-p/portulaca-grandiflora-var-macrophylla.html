Portulaca grandiflora var. macrophylla

Portulaca grandiflora var. macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf moss rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers. Its ability to tolerate drought conditions makes it a hardy and low-maintenance plant.

The flowers typically bloom in summer and last for several weeks, adding a splash of color to gardens throughout the season.
