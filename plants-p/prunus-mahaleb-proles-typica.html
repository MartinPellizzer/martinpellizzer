Prunus mahaleb proles typica

Prunus mahaleb proles typica, commonly known as the cherry laurel, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, dark red berries that are edible but often used in culinary applications.
