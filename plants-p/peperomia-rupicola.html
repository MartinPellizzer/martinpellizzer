Peperomia rupicola

Peperomia rupicola, commonly known as the rock peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often bluish-green with a waxy texture, adapted to store water. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Peperomia rupicola is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to tolerate dry conditions.
