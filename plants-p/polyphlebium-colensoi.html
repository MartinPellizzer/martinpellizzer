Polyphlebium colensoi

Polyphlebium colensoi, commonly known as the Colenso's grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
