Peperomia semipuberula

Peperomia semipuberula, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia semipuberula is known for its low maintenance and ability to adapt well to various growing conditions.
