Phaca lanosissima

Phaca lanosissima, commonly known as the woolly phaca, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are small and yellow, often clustered in dense inflorescences.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
