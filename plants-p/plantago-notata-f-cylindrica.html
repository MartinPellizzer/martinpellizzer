Plantago notata f. cylindrica

Plantago notata f. cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical plantain, is a variant of the common plantain.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its upright, cylindrical seed stalks that distinguish it from other plantain species. The leaves are broad, ribbed, and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in roadsides, fields, and waste areas.

This plant is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
