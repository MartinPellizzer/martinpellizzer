Pleurothallis chama

Pleurothallis chama, commonly known as the ground pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments within the undergrowth of forests. The plant features a compact, rhizomatous growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with colors ranging from white to pale yellow.

Pleurothallis chama is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
