Pittosporum yunckeri

Pittosporum yunckeri, commonly known as the New Zealand pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves and showy, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, red, berry-like fruits that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

Pittosporum yunckeri is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in native ecosystems.
