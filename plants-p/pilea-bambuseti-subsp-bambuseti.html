Pilea bambuseti subsp. bambuseti

Pilea bambuseti subsp.

bambuseti, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to southern China. It features round, fleshy leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by tiny, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Its ability to propagate through stem cuttings adds to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
