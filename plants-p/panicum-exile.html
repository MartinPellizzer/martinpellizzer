Panicum exile

Panicum exile, commonly known as slender panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its fine, slender blades and often grows in disturbed soils or open areas. The plant typically reaches a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and has a tufted growth habit. Its inflorescence consists of a narrow spikelet that is often slightly drooping.

Panicum exile is adapted to dry conditions and is sometimes used in landscaping for its ornamental value.
