Peperomia trineuroides

Peperomia trineuroides, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to South America.

It features waxy, silver-green leaves that are often variegated with shades of white and cream, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of green or red, which appear during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and low maintenance requirements, it is well-suited for beginners and space-limited environments.
