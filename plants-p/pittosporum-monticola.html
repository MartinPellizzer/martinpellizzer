Pittosporum monticola

Pittosporum monticola, commonly known as the mountain pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It typically grows in coastal and mountainous regions, preferring well-drained soils and mild climates. The plant features dark green, glossy leaves that are oval in shape and have a leathery texture. It produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by orange-red berries that are attractive to birds.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitat.
