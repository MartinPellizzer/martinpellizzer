Peperomia rubrivenosa

Peperomia rubrivenosa, commonly known as the red-veined peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and striking foliage with deep green leaves featuring prominent red veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia rubrivenosa is relatively low maintenance and resistant to pests, contributing to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
