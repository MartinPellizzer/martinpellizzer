Pteris guichenotiana

Pteris guichenotiana, commonly known as the Guichenot's water fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds that are often used in ornamental horticulture. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-drained soil. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine in some regions.

The plant's ability to thrive in humid conditions makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
