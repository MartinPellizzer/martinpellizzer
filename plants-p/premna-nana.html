Premna nana

Premna nana, commonly known as smallleaf morning glory, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae and is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves and delicate, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. It produces small, berry-like fruits that contain seeds dispersed by animals.

Premna nana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
