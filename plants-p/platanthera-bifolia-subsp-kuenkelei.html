Platanthera bifolia subsp. kuenkelei

Platanthera bifolia subsp.

kuenkelei, commonly known as the Kuenkele's lady's tresses, is a rare orchid species native to the temperate regions of Europe. It is characterized by its dense, dark green leaves and a distinctive, fragrant floral spike that resembles a tresses of hair.

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and meadows. It produces small, white to pale green flowers that bloom in late summer.

Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, this subspecies is considered endangered in many parts of its range.
