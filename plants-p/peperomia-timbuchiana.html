Peperomia timbuchiana

Peperomia timbuchiana, commonly known as the Timbuktu peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia timbuchiana is known for its low maintenance requirements and ability to adapt well to a variety of indoor conditions.
