Pellionia pellucida

Pellionia pellucida, commonly known as the silverleaf pellionia, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, greenish-white flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Pellionia pellucida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture for its aesthetic qualities.
