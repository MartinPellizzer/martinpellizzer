Photinia melanostigma

Photinia melanostigma, commonly known as the glossy leaf photinia, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage that exhibits a deep, glossy green hue. The plant produces small, white flowers clustered in spherical inflorescences, which are typically borne in spring. Its leaves are elliptical with a smooth margin and a prominent midrib.

Photinia melanostigma is often cultivated as an ornamental shrub for its attractive appearance and adaptability to various soil types.
