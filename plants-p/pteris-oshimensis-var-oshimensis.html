Pteris oshimensis var. oshimensis

Pteris oshimensis var. oshimensis, commonly known as the Japanese cliff brake, is a fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It features fronds that are typically bi- or tri-pinnate, with a distinctive, upright growth habit. The plant has a creeping rhizome that allows it to spread and form dense colonies. Its leaves are often glossy and have a leathery texture, with a dark green coloration.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found growing on rocky surfaces or in forest understories.
