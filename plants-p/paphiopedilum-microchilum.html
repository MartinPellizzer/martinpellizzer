Paphiopedilum microchilum

Paphiopedilum microchilum, commonly known as the miniature-lipped orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact growth habit with a single, often reddish-brown rhizome and a few broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive, lip that is smaller than the other petals. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

This species is prized for its unique appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
