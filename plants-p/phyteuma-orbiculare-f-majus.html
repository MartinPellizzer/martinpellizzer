Phyteuma orbiculare f. majus

Phyteuma orbiculare f. majus, commonly known as the Giant Globe Thistle, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features large, showy spherical flower heads that are typically blue to purple in color, surrounded by spiny, silver-green leaves. The plant is characterized by its robust growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, and it is known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The foliage and flowers are covered in fine, silvery hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
