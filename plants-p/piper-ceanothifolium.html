Piper ceanothifolium

Piper ceanothifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. It produces small, peppery fruits that are used in traditional medicine and cuisine.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in some tropical gardens.
