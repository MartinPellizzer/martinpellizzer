Pellegrinia spectabilis

Pellegrinia spectabilis, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
