Potentilla tianschanica

Potentilla tianschanica, commonly known as the Tian Shan cinquefoil, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, sprawling growth habit and produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers during the summer months. The plant has dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.

Potentilla tianschanica is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
