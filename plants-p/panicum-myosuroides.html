Panicum myosuroides

Panicum myosuroides, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green to reddish in color. The plant produces fluffy, light brown inflorescences that are highly visible and often mistaken for tumbleweeds. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in roadsides, fields, and open areas.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it an invasive species in many regions.
