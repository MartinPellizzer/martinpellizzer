Plantago bungei

Plantago bungei, commonly known as the Bunge's plantain, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features a rosette of broad, oval-shaped leaves with a hairy surface and a waxy coating. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, which are wind-pollinated. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in alpine environments.

The species is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
