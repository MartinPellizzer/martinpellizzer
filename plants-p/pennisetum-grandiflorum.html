Pennisetum grandiflorum

Pennisetum grandiflorum, commonly known as purple lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and clusters of purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Pennisetum grandiflorum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
