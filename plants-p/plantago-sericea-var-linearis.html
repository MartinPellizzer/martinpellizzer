Plantago sericea var. linearis

Plantago sericea var. linearis, commonly known as slender plantain, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It features lance-shaped, slightly hairy leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. The plant produces small, greenish flowers in dense spikes during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, along roadsides, and in disturbed areas.

This species is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in herbal remedies.
