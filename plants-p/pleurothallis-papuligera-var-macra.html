Pleurothallis papuligera var. macra

Pleurothallis papuligera var. macra, commonly known as the Macra pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on tree branches or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features slender, dark green leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It is noted for its compact size and unique floral structure, which includes a spurred lip.

This variety is relatively rare and requires specific growing conditions to thrive, making it a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
