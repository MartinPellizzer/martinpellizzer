Prunus mahaleb f. albomarginata

Prunus mahaleb f. albomarginata, commonly known as the white-margin cherry, is a cultivar of the Prunus mahaleb species.

It is characterized by its distinctive white-edged leaves that contrast with its dark green foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in the spring, which are typically pink to white in color. It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that thrives in temperate climates.

The white-margin variety is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
