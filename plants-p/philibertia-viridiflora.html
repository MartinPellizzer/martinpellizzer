Philibertia viridiflora

Philibertia viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered filbert, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in composite inflorescences. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
