Pleurothallis guttulata

Pleurothallis guttulata, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, greenish-white flowers with distinctive spots on the petals and sepals. It has narrow, strap-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.

Pleurothallis guttulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
