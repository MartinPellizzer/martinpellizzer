Prunus × fruticans var. fastigiata

Prunus × fruticans var. fastigiata, commonly known as the upright cherry tree, is a compact, columnar shrub or small tree native to Japan.

It features dense, upright growth with dark green, glossy leaves that provide year-round visual interest. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in early spring, followed by bright red or orange berries in late summer. It is well-suited for small spaces and is often used as a hedge or ornamental plant.

Its hardy nature makes it adaptable to a variety of soil types and climates.
