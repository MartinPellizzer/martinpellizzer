Peperomia lauterbachii

Peperomia lauterbachii, commonly known as the Lauterbachii peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often adorned with distinctive patterns and colors, such as silver or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, followed by round, fleshy berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia lauterbachii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
