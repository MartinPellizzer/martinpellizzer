Phyteuma corniculatum var. serratum

Phyteuma corniculatum var. serratum, commonly known as the serrated phyteuma, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and Eastern Europe.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange. The leaves are lanceolate and serrated, contributing to its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in meadows and woodland edges.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
