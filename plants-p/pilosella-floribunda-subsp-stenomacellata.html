Pilosella floribunda subsp. stenomacellata

Pilosella floribunda subsp.

stenomacellata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pilosella, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its dense, fluffy white to pale yellow inflorescences, which consist of both ray and disk florets.

The subspecies is distinguished by its narrow, linear leaves and compact growth habit. It typically thrives in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes.

This plant is often found in temperate regions of Europe and parts of western Asia.
