Pilosella densiflora subsp. melananthela

Pilosella densiflora subsp.

melananthela, commonly known as the dark-flowered pearly everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its dense, fluffy inflorescences composed of small, white to pale yellow flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its darker flower coloration compared to other varieties within the species. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is valued for its ability to retain its appearance after drying.
