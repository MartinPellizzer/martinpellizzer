Peristrophe krebsii

Peristrophe krebsii, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its dense, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers. The plant is known for its hard, heavy wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
