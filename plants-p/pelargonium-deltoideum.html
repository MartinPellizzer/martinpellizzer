Pelargonium deltoideum

Pelargonium deltoideum, commonly known as the deltoid geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its rounded, deltoid-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom in summer. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and flowers.

Pelargonium deltoideum prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
