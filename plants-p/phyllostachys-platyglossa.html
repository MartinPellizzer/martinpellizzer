Phyllostachys platyglossa

Phyllostachys platyglossa, commonly known as the broad-leaf bamboo, is a species of grass in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its broad, flat leaves that give it its common name. This bamboo is known for its rapid growth and is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal. The plant produces slender, green culms that can reach heights of up to 10 meters.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
