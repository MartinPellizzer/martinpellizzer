Potentilla lomakinii

Potentilla lomakinii, commonly known as the Lomakine cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Potentilla lomakinii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or rocky habitats.
