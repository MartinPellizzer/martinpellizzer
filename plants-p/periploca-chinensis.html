Periploca chinensis

Periploca chinensis, commonly known as Chinese periploca, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits containing numerous small, flat seeds. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides.

Its latex contains toxic compounds, making it a plant with both medicinal and cautionary significance.
