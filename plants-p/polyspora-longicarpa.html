Polyspora longicarpa

Polyspora longicarpa, commonly known as the long-fruited polyspora, is a member of the family Polysporaceae.

It is a small, perennial herb characterized by its slender, erect stems and simple, alternate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a distinctive, elongated capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Polyspora longicarpa is typically found in moist, shaded habitats in tropical and subtropical regions.
