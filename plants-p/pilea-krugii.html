Pilea krugii

Pilea krugii, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are typically a vibrant green, sometimes with a reddish tinge. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Pilea krugii is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its unique appearance and ease of care.
