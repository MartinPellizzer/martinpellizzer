Polystachya brassii

Polystachya brassii, commonly known as the golden spider orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like inflorescence that bears numerous golden-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within the understory of tropical forests. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the humid air.

Polystachya brassii is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
