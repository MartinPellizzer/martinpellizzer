Platantheroides odontopetala

Platantheroides odontopetala, commonly known as the narrow-leaved twayblade, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, often leafless stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and wet meadows. It has a rhizomatous root system and produces pseudobulbs that store nutrients.
