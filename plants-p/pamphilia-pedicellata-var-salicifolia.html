Pamphilia pedicellata var. salicifolia

Pamphilia pedicellata var. salicifolia, commonly known as the willow-leafed puffball, is a variant of the puffball mushroom characterized by its distinctive, elongated, willow-like leaves.

It belongs to the family Lepiota and is typically found in woodland areas, often growing in association with certain tree species.
