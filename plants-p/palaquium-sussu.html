Palaquium sussu

Palaquium sussu, commonly known as the Sussu wax apple, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by round, waxy fruits with a sweet, tangy flavor. The tree is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in tropical climates.
