Pleurothallis plumosa

Pleurothallis plumosa, commonly known as the feather orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its elegant, feathery inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually small and come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink.

Pleurothallis plumosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
