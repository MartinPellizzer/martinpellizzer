Poa debilior

Poa debilior, commonly known as the slender meadowgrass, is a species of grass native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is a low-growing perennial plant characterized by its fine, delicate leaves and tufted inflorescence. The plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along stream banks. Its small, spike-like flowers are usually greenish or brownish in color, and it produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.

Poa debilior plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates in its native ecosystems.
