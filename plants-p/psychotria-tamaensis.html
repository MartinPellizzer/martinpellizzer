Psychotria tamaensis

Psychotria tamaensis, commonly known as the Tamaenese psychotria, is a species of flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Colombia and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, white to pale green flowers. It produces small, red berries that are often used in traditional medicine.

Psychotria tamaensis is also noted for its potential psychoactive properties due to the presence of alkaloids.
