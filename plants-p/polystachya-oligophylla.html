Polystachya oligophylla

Polystachya oligophylla, commonly known as the slender bamboo orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that are often fragrant. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Polystachya oligophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
