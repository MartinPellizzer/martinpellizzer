Platanthera × beckneri

Platanthera × beckneri, commonly known as the Beckner's twayblade, is a hybrid orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial with a slender, grass-like stem and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and wetlands. It has a rhizomatous root system and produces tiny, linear leaves.

Due to its hybrid origin, it exhibits a range of morphological variations in its natural populations.
