Pulmonaria officinalis proles obscura

Pulmonaria officinalis proles obscura, commonly known as the shadow lungwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features distinctive basal leaves with a bluish-gray coloration and silver-white markings, which give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and purple, typically blooming in early spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
