Pittosporum glabratum var. ciliicalyx

Pittosporum glabratum var. ciliicalyx, commonly known as the coastal pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are typically oval-shaped with a smooth, glossy surface. This variety is well adapted to coastal environments, tolerating salt spray and strong winds.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
