Pelargonium schizopetalum

Pelargonium schizopetalum, commonly known as the African geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, pink or red flowers. The leaves are typically lobed and have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
