Podocarpus pinetorum

Podocarpus pinetorum, commonly known as the Chinese podocarp or the evergreen tree, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to southern China and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Podocarpaceae and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and conical shape. The plant produces small, spherical cones that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in humid, subtropical climates and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Podocarpus pinetorum is also valued for its durable wood, which has been used in traditional construction and furniture-making.
