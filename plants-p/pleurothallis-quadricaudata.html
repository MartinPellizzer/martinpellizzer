Pleurothallis quadricaudata

Pleurothallis quadricaudata, commonly known as the four-tailed pleurothallis, is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. This epiphytic plant typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are small and usually white or pale yellow, with a delicate, fragrant aroma.

The plant is noted for its unique, branched inflorescence, which gives it its common name.
