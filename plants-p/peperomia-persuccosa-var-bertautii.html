Peperomia persuccosa var. bertautii

Peperomia persuccosa var. bertautii, commonly known as the silver lace peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to Brazil.

It features waxy, silver-green leaves with a textured, lace-like appearance that gives it a unique aesthetic. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10-15 centimeters and has a trailing growth habit, making it suitable for hanging baskets or shelf displays. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
