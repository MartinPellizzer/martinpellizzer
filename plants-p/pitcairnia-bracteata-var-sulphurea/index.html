Pitcairnia Bracteata Var. Sulphurea

Yellow pitcairnia, scientifically known as Pitcairnia bracteata var. sulphurea, is a striking bromeliad native to the cloud forests of South America. Its vibrant yellow flowers stand out against its lush green foliage, making it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts. This unique variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments. The plant's compact size and long-lasting blooms make it ideal for both indoor and outdoor cultivation. With proper care, it can add a tropical touch to any garden or living space.
