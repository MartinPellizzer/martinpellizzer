Pternandra latifolia

Pternandra latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf pternandra, is a tropical plant native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in tropical and subtropical regions.
