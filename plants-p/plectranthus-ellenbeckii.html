Plectranthus ellenbeckii

Plectranthus ellenbeckii, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its opposite, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of tubular, brightly colored flowers that range from red to orange. The plant produces small, oval fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
