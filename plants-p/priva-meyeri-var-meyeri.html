Priva meyeri var. meyeri

Priva meyeri var. meyeri, commonly known as the Meyer lemon.

It is a compact citrus tree with smooth, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The fruit is small, round, and typically has a thin, yellow-orange peel. It is prized for its sweet, aromatic fruit and is often grown in subtropical climates.

The plant is relatively cold-hardy and suitable for container gardening.
