Pentas concinna

Pentas concinna, commonly known as the flame flower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Pentas concinna is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
