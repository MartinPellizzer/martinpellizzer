Prostanthera pulchella

Prostanthera pulchella, commonly known as the pretty honeysuckle, is a small shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its fragrant, tubular flowers are typically pale blue to violet, and they bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months.

Prostanthera pulchella is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its pleasant fragrance and attractive appearance.
