Piper nubigenum var. brevifolium

Piper nubigenum var. brevifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pepper plant, is a tropical species native to the humid regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and a distinct, aromatic fragrance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into clusters of dark blue to black berries. Its leaves and fruits contain various bioactive compounds, making it of interest in traditional medicine and research.

The variety brevifolium is distinguished by its shorter leaves compared to the typical form of Piper nubigenum.
