Panzeria lanata

Panzeria lanata, commonly known as the woolly panzeria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that covers its stems and leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in the spring. Panzeria lanata thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Its unique morphology and habitat preferences make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
