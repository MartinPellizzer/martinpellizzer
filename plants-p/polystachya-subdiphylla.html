Polystachya subdiphylla

Polystachya subdiphylla, commonly known as the narrow-leafed torch orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short, creeping rhizome and produces multiple flowering stems. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. Its inflorescence consists of several clustered, spike-like racemes bearing small, yellowish-green flowers.

Polystachya subdiphylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to grow in humid, shaded environments.
