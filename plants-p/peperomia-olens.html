Peperomia olens

Peperomia olens, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features broad, glossy, dark green leaves with a velvety texture, often with a reddish or purplish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and drought tolerance.

Peperomia olens is prized for its ornamental foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
