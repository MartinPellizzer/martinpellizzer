Peperomia pilostigma

Peperomia pilostigma, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, dark green, velvety leaves that are often slightly hairy, giving it a unique texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia pilostigma is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
