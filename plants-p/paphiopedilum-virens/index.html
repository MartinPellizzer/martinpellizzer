Paphiopedilum Virens

Paphiopedilum virens is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its vibrant green and yellow foliage, this plant is often referred to as the "green orchid" due to its unique coloration. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful cultivation to flourish. Despite its beauty, it is listed as endangered due to habitat loss and over-collection. This article explores the characteristics, care, and conservation efforts surrounding this remarkable species.
