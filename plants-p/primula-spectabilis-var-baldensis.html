Primula spectabilis var. baldensis

Primula spectabilis var. baldensis, commonly known as the Bald Mountain primrose, is a perennial herb native to the high alpine regions of North America.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, harsh environments. Its leaves are basal, broad, and often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in alpine ecosystems.
