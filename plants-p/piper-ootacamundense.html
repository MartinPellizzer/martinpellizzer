Piper ootacamundense

Piper ootacamundense, commonly known as the Ootacamund pepper, is a flowering plant in the Piperaceae family.

It is native to the Western Ghats of India and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically harvested when they turn red. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.

Its growth habit includes climbing or trailing vines, making it suitable for trellises or hanging baskets.
