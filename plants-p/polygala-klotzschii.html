Polygala klotzschii

Polygala klotzschii, commonly known as the blue milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slight hairy texture.

Polygala klotzschii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
