Potentilla recta var. herbichii

Potentilla recta var. herbichii, commonly known as the herbichii potentilla, is a variant of the upright potentilla.

It is a low-growing, deciduous shrub that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, rounded leaves that are typically gray-green in color, covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are usually yellow, appearing in clusters during the summer months.

It is native to regions of Europe and is often found in dry, rocky soils.
