Peperomia tenerrima f. robustior

Peperomia tenerrima f. robustior, commonly known as the Shiny Leaf Peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, waxy, ovate leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale green, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
