Piper regale

Piper regale, commonly known as the royal pepper or sacred pepper, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, heart-shaped leaves and small, clustered white flowers. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

Piper regale is often cultivated for its ornamental value and traditional medicinal uses.
