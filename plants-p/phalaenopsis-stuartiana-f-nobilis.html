Phalaenopsis stuartiana f. nobilis

Phalaenopsis stuartiana f. nobilis, commonly known as the Stuart's orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, forming pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

This orchid thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
