Pyrrosia mannii var. birii

Pyrrosia mannii var. birii, commonly known as the Shikumen fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, leathery fronds with a distinctive, compact growth habit. The plant's rhizomes are often covered in a dense layer of moss-like structures, aiding in moisture retention. Its leaves are typically divided into small, lanceolate segments, giving it a lush, textured appearance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in shaded, humid environments.
