Piper dussii

Piper dussii, commonly known as the Mexican pepper plant, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. It produces small, peppery fruits that are often used in traditional medicine and cuisine.

Piper dussii thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
