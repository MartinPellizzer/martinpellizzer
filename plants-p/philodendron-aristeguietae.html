Philodendron aristeguietae

Philodendron aristeguietae, commonly known as the Aristeguietae Philodendron, is a climbing plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly glossy surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by distinctive, elongated fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its health.

Due to its unique appearance and growth habits, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
