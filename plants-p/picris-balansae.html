Picris balansae

Picris balansae, commonly known as the Balansae goat's beard, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and foliage composed of linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters, which are typically visited by various pollinators. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and naturalized areas.
