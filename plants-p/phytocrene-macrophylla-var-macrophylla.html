Phytocrene macrophylla var. macrophylla

Phytocrene macrophylla var. macrophylla, commonly known as the broadleaf phytocrene, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and traditional herbal practices.
