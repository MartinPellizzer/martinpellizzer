Plocoglottis Bokorensis

Plocoglottis bokorensis is a newly discovered fern species found in the remote rainforests of Cambodia. This plant belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its unique leaf structure and growth patterns. Its discovery highlights the ongoing exploration of biodiversity in understudied regions of the world. The species was named in honor of the Bokor region where it was first identified. Researchers are now studying its ecological role and potential conservation status.
