Polybotrya intermedia

Polybotrya intermedia, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers. Polybotrya intermedia is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
