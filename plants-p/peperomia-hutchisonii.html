Peperomia hutchisonii

Peperomia hutchisonii, commonly known as the Hutchison's peperomia, is a compact, low-growing perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia hutchisonii is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.
