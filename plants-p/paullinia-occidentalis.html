Paullinia occidentalis

Paullinia occidentalis, commonly known as the Brazilian pepper tree, is a member of the Sapotaceae family.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of South America. The plant is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and opposite, elliptical leaves with prominent veins.
