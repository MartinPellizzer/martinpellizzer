Peliosanthes densiflora

Peliosanthes densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered peliosanthes, is a member of the Arecaceae family, native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized palm characterized by its robust, clustered trunk and pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers arranged in dense inflorescences, which are typically yellowish-green in color. Its ornamental value lies in its compact size and attractive foliage, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

However, it is relatively rare in the wild and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
