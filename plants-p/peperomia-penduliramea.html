Peperomia penduliramea

Peperomia penduliramea, commonly known as the hanging peperomia, is a compact, trailing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a subtle ornamental appeal. Its trailing growth habit makes it ideal for hanging baskets or as a cascading plant in containers.

Peperomia penduliramea prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
