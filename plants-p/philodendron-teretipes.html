Philodendron teretipes

Philodendron teretipes, commonly known as the narrow-leaf philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are typically narrower than those of other philodendron species. The plant has a vigorous growth habit and can reach significant heights when provided with support. Its stems are often reddish or purple in color, adding visual interest to its appearance.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its health and vigor.
