Phthirusa delicatula

Phthirusa delicatula, commonly known as the slender leaf beetle, is a small, herbivorous insect belonging to the family Chrysomelidae.

It is primarily found in temperate regions and feeds on various species of flowering plants, particularly those in the Brassicaceae family. The beetle's body is elongated and covered in a smooth, often metallic sheen, which helps it blend with its host plants. Its life cycle includes egg, larval, pupal, and adult stages, with the adults being the most commonly observed.
