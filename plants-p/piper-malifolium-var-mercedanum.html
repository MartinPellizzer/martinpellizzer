Piper malifolium var. mercedanum

Piper malifolium var. mercedanum, commonly known as the Mercedanum pepper plant, is a variant of the false pepper species native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, round fruits that turn from green to red as they mature. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous vine, often climbing or trailing along supports. Its botanical features include opposite, ovate leaves and a distinct, aromatic scent.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and mild, peppery flavor in culinary applications.
