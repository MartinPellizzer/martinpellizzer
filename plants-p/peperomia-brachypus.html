Peperomia brachypus

Peperomia brachypus, commonly known as the short-stemmed peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale green, arranged in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia brachypus is prized for its low maintenance and ability to thrive indoors, making it a popular choice for indoor gardeners.
