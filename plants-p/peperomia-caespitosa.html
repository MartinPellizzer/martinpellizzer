Peperomia caespitosa

Peperomia caespitosa, commonly known as the compact peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with a waxy texture and a subtle reddish or purple hue on the undersides. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in the axils of the leaves. It is well adapted to low-light conditions and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its dense foliage makes it an excellent choice for indoor gardening and container cultivation.
