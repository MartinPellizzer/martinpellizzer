Potentilla beanii

Potentilla beanii, commonly known as the yellow cinquefoil, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are compound, consisting of five oval-shaped leaflets, and it has a low-growing, spreading habit.

Potentilla beanii is often used in native plant gardens due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
