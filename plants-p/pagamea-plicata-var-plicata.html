Pagamea plicata var. plicata

Pagamea plicata var. plicata, commonly known as the crested pagamea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, wavy, and often ridged leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
