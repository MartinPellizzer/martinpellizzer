Phylica odorata

Phylica odorata, commonly known as the sweet thorn, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers that bloom in summer, followed by red, fuzzy fruits. The plant has small, leathery leaves and is often found in dry, rocky habitats. It is well adapted to arid conditions and can thrive in poor soils.

Phylica odorata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in local communities.
