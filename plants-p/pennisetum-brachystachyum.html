Pennisetum brachystachyum

Pennisetum brachystachyum, commonly known as blue oat grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense, blue-green foliage that gives it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that are often used in ornamental arrangements. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Pennisetum brachystachyum is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract birds and pollinators.
