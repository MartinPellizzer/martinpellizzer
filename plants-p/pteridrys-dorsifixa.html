Pteridrys dorsifixa

Pteridrys dorsifixa, commonly known as the narrow-leaf fern, is a species of fern characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit, with underground stems that produce new fronds and roots. Its leaves are typically bi- or tri-pinnate, with a distinctive upward-curving shape.

Pteridrys dorsifixa thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
