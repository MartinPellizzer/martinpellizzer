Pyrethrum oligocephalum

Pyrethrum oligocephalum, commonly known as the narrow-headed pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow ray florets and a central disk. The plant produces small, narrow-headed flower heads that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassy areas or open woodlands.

Pyrethrum oligocephalum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
