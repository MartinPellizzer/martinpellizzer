Philodendron laciniatum var. weddellianum

Philodendron laciniatum var. weddellianum, commonly known as the Weddellianum philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to South America.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. The plant has a unique, segmented stem that allows it to climb and trail gracefully. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical plant collections.
