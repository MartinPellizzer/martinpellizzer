Piper petiolare

Piper petiolare, commonly known as the petiole pepper or Indian pepper, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its slender stems, heart-shaped leaves, and small, clustered flowers. Its fruit, a small berry, contains the aromatic compounds responsible for its distinctive flavor.

Piper petiolare is often used in traditional medicine and as a spice in various cuisines.
