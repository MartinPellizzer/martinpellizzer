Philodendron narvaezii

Philodendron narvaezii, commonly known as the Narvaezii philodendron, is a climbing tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by spiny, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
