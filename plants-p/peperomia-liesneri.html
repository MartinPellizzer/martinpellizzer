Peperomia liesneri

Peperomia liesneri, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to South America.

It features glossy, waxy leaves that are typically silver or gray-green in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant has short, thick stems and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or red. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia liesneri is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
