Pleioblastus virens var. tenuifolius

Pleioblastus virens var. tenuifolius, commonly known as the bamboo grass or slender-leaf bamboo, is a variant of the evergreen bamboo species native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves that are typically a vibrant green, providing a delicate and elegant appearance. The plant grows in clumps and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are rarely seen. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

This variety is valued for its ability to form dense foliage, making it suitable for erosion control and ornamental gardens.
