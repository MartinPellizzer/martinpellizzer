Phyllorkis gracilis

Phyllorkis gracilis, commonly known as the slender lily, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, strap-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Phyllorkis gracilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in shaded garden settings.
