Philippiamra densiflora

Philippiamra densiflora, commonly known as the denseflower shrub, is a species native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, woody structure and dense clusters of small, white flowers that bloom during the winter months. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dune environments. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing year-round foliage.

Philippiamra densiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize coastal soils.
