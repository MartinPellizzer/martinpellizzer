Portulaca usambarensis var. obtusa

Portulaca usambarensis var. obtusa, commonly known as the broad-leaf portulaca, is a succulent plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, broad leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments. Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in dry conditions.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
