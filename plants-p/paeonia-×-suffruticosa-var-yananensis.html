Paeonia × suffruticosa var. yananensis

Paeonia × suffruticosa var. yananensis, commonly known as the Yanan peony, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and has woody stems that persist through the winter. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces follicles as its fruit.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
