Pyrethrum tenuisectum

Pyrethrum tenuisectum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves and small yellow to orange flowers. The plant is known for producing pyrethrins, which are natural insecticides used in organic pest control. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun.

Pyrethrum tenuisectum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
