Populus pseudosimonii var. pseudosimonii

Populus pseudosimonii var. pseudosimonii, commonly known as the Yunnan poplar, is a deciduous tree native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Salicaceae family and is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant exhibits a distinct growth habit, often forming dense stands in its natural habitat. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

This species is notable for its ecological importance in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
