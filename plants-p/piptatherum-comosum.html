Piptatherum comosum

Piptatherum comosum, commonly known as blue grama, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. Its seed heads are often tufted, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Blue grama is an important component of native grassland ecosystems and is adapted to withstand drought and grazing.
