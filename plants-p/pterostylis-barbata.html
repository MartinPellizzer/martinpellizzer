Pterostylis barbata

Pterostylis barbata, commonly known as the barbed helmet-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. The plant features a single, erect stem with a single, showy greenish-white flower. Its unique structure includes a helmet-like labellum and a distinctive, barbed spur that is adapted for pollination by specific insects.

The species is part of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is known for its specialized reproductive strategies.
