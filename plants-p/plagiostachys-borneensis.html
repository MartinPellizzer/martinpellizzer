Plagiostachys borneensis

Plagiostachys borneensis, commonly known as Bornean grass.

It is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of Borneo. The plant features slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets.

Plagiostachys borneensis is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical rainforests.
