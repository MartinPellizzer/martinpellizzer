Podocarpus macrophyllus var. angustifolius

Podocarpus macrophyllus var. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Chinese pine, is a species of evergreen conifer native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and slender, conical shape. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain seeds, typical of the Podocarpaceae family. Its leaves are typically narrow and leathery, providing a dense canopy.

This species is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
