Polygala rogeri

Polygala rogeri, commonly known as the Cape Flora or Cape Flax.

It is a flowering plant species native to South Africa, specifically the Western Cape region. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, or purple. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Polygala rogeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
