Polygala piauhiensis

Polygala piauhiensis, commonly known as the Piauhi's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its unique floral structure, which includes specialized nectar guides. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. It produces small, delicate flowers that are often pink or white in color.
