Pittosporum oblongilimbum

Pittosporum oblongilimbum, commonly known as the New Zealand Pittosporum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped, dark green leaves that turn reddish-brown in autumn. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by orange-red berries that are edible for some bird species. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in suitable climates.
