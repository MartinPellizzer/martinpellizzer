Panicum ischaemum

Panicum ischaemum, commonly known as yellow bluestem, is a grass species native to North America.

It is a perennial plant that typically grows in open woodlands and prairie habitats. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear golden-yellow flowers. It is characterized by its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions and its role in stabilizing soil in disturbed areas.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is often used in landscaping and restoration projects.
