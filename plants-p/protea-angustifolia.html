Protea angustifolia

Protea angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved protea, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and its distinctive, showy inflorescences composed of numerous small flowers clustered around a central stem. The leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in a fine, silky pubescence. This species thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its long-lasting blooms and ability to add dramatic visual impact to gardens.
