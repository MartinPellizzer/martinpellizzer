Pilea viridissima

Pilea viridissima, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features glossy, round leaves that are typically a deep green, often with a slight waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Pilea viridissima is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
