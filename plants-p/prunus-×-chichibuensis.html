Prunus × chichibuensis

Prunus × chichibuensis, commonly known as the Chichibu cherry, is a hybrid deciduous shrub or small tree native to Japan.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and is prized for its ornamental value, particularly its showy spring blooms. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that appear before or with the foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in bonsai and as a ornamental plant in temperate climates.
