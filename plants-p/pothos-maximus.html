Pothos maximus

Pothos maximus, commonly known as the golden pothos, is a popular houseplant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically bright green with a yellowish or golden variegation. The plant is a member of the Araceae family, which includes other familiar plants like peace lilies and philodendrons. It thrives in low light conditions and is known for its ability to purify indoor air.

Pothos maximus is easy to care for, making it an ideal choice for beginners in plant cultivation.
