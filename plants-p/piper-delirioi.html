Piper delirioi

Piper delirioi, commonly known as the delirious pepper vine, is a climbing plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into berries, which are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a peppery aroma when crushed.

The plant is known for its psychoactive properties due to the presence of various alkaloids.
