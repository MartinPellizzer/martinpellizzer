Paphiopedilum inamorii

Paphiopedilum inamorii, commonly known as the Chinese velvet slipper orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It features a dense, dark green foliage with broad, leathery leaves. The plant produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive pouch-shaped lip, typical of the slipper orchid genus. It blooms in late spring to early summer, producing fragrant, pale yellow to orange flowers.

This species is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique and elegant appearance.
