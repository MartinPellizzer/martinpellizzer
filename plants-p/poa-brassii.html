Poa brassii

Poa brassii, commonly known as the silver hair grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-colored foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It produces small, spiky seed heads that are often clustered at the base of the plant.

Poa brassii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in landscaping for its visual appeal.
