Pieris japonica var. koidzumiana

Pieris japonica var. koidzumiana, commonly known as the Japanese pieris, is a species of evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features glossy, dark green, elliptical leaves that provide an attractive evergreen presence in gardens. The plant produces clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers in early spring, adding ornamental value. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained, acidic soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its foliage and blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
