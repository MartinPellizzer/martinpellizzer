Phaius × gravesii

Phaius × gravesii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Phaius tankervillii and Phaius inflata.

It is a tall, herbaceous orchid that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features large, broad leaves that are usually dark green and strap-like in appearance. Its flowers are showy, with three sepals and three petals, and are often a combination of yellow and orange hues.

It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, shaded environments.
