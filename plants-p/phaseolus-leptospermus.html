Phaseolus leptospermus

Phaseolus leptospermus, commonly known as the slender bean, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that give way to elongated, pod-like fruits containing several seeds.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both agricultural and ecological studies.
