Polylychnis ovata

Polylychnis ovata, commonly known as the broad-leafed grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa. The plant features broad, flat leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. Its inflorescence consists of a spikelet-bearing panicle, which is often adorned with small, delicate flowers.

Polylychnis ovata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and garden design.
