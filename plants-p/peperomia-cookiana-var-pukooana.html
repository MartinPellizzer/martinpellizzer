Peperomia cookiana var. pukooana

Peperomia cookiana var. pukooana, commonly known as the Pukooana Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Hawaii.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

This variety is popular among collectors for its unique foliage and ease of care.
