Pleopeltis sculpturata

Pleopeltis sculpturata, commonly known as the sculptured wall fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its intricate, sculptured leaf surfaces that give it its common name. The fronds are typically lanceolate and have a distinctive, rugged texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or rocky surfaces.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in tropical gardens.
