Peperomia pereneana

Peperomia pereneana, commonly known as the glossy peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy sheen, often adorned with irregular white or silver markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia pereneana is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
