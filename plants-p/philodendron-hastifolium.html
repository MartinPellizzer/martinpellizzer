Philodendron hastifolium

Philodendron hastifolium, commonly known as the arrowhead vine, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant has long, climbing stems that can reach several meters in length, making it suitable for hanging baskets or trained growth on trellises. Its foliage is evergreen, providing year-round visual interest in indoor settings.

Philodendron hastifolium thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.
