Phyllostachys viridis f. houzeauana

Phyllostachys viridis f. houzeauana, commonly known as the green bamboo, is a variant of the green bamboo species native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, green stalks and long, narrow leaves that give it a delicate appearance. This plant is a member of the grass family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. It prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

The foliage remains lush throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for vertical gardening and ornamental purposes.
