Panicum dichotomiflorum var. geniculatum

Panicum dichotomiflorum var. geniculatum, commonly known as the zigzag grass, is a perennial grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzag-shaped inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in wetlands and along stream banks, thriving in moist to wet soils. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that adds ornamental value.

The species is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support local wildlife.
