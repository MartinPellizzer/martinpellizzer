Philodendron grahamii

Philodendron grahamii, commonly known as the Graham's philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly glossy surface and prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers, though it is primarily grown for its ornamental foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and humidity.

Philodendron grahamii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
