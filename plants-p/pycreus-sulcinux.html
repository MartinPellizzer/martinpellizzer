Pycreus sulcinux

Pycreus sulcinux, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and delicate, fragrant blooms that typically appear in the spring. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and botanical collections.

Pycreus sulcinux is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
