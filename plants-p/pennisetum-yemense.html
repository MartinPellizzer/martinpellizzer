Pennisetum yemense

Pennisetum yemense, commonly known as the Yemen grass, is a perennial grass native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that are often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid climates.

Pennisetum yemense is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.
