Polypodium asiaticum

Polypodium asiaticum, commonly known as the Asian cliff fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, frond-like leaves that are often clustered together. The plant typically grows on rocky surfaces or in shaded, moist environments, thriving in humid tropical climates. Its rhizome is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The fronds are usually bi-lobed and have a distinctive, lace-like appearance.
