Pandanus magnifibrosus

Pandanus magnifibrosus, commonly known as the giant pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters at the top of the plant. Its roots are fibrous and often used in traditional crafts and construction.

The species is well adapted to coastal and wetland environments, thriving in humid, saline conditions.
