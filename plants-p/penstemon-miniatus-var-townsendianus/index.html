Penstemon Miniatus Var. Townsendianus

Penstemon miniatus var. townsendianus is a striking perennial plant native to the western united states. it is known for its vibrant blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring and early summer. this variety is particularly prized for its ability to thrive in rocky and well-drained soils. it is a popular choice for xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements. its unique appearance and ecological benefits make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
