Panicum leptachyrium

Panicum leptachyrium, commonly known as slender grass or slender panicgrass, is a grass species native to the United States.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by wind, a common strategy among grasses. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in open fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

Its botanical features make it a hardy and adaptable species within the Poaceae family.
