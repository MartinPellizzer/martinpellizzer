Piper debilicaule

Piper debilicaule, commonly known as the slender pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and clusters of tiny, greenish-white flowers. It produces small, peppery fruits that are often used in traditional medicine.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
