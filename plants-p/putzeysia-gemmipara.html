Putzeysia gemmipara

Putzeysia gemmipara, commonly known as the gemmipara tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red or orange when mature. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Putzeysia gemmipara is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its foliage and fruit.
