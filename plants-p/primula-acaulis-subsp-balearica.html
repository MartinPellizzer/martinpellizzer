Primula acaulis subsp. balearica

Primula acaulis subsp.

balearica, commonly known as the Balearic primrose, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to the Balearic Islands. It features clustered, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in early spring.

The plant has a dense, cushion-like growth habit with hairy, dark green leaves. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
