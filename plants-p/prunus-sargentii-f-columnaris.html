Prunus sargentii f. columnaris

Prunus sargentii f. columnaris, commonly known as the columnar cherry, is a unique cultivar of the Prunus sargentii species.

It is characterized by its narrow, upright growth habit, making it an ideal choice for space-constrained landscapes. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers in early spring, followed by edible, dark red berries. It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Its compact form and ornamental value make it popular in urban gardening and ornamental horticulture.
