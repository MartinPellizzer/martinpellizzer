Pleurothallis sanguinolenta

Pleurothallis sanguinolenta, commonly known as the blood-red pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often reddish or pinkish flowers. The plant typically produces small, showy blooms that are clustered in inflorescences, which are often fragrant. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
