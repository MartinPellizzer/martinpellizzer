Phylica secunda

Phylica secunda, commonly known as the silver tree, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, spherical flower heads that are typically white or pink in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Phylica secunda is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
