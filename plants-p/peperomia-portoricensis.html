Peperomia portoricensis

Peperomia portoricensis, commonly known as the Puerto Rican peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean island of Puerto Rico.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, with a glossy green surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, arranged in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia portoricensis is valued for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
