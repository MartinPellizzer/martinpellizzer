Pleurothallis hirtziana

Pleurothallis hirtziana, commonly known as the hairy pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and hairy stems, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
