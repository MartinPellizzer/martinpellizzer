Pelargonium × aemulum

Pelargonium × aemulum, commonly known as the African marigold, is a hybrid geranium species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for both garden beds and container planting.

Pelargonium × aemulum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
