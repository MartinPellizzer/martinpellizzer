Phyllopentas schimperiana

Phyllopentas schimperiana, commonly known as the starfish plant, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It features striking, waxy, star-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from red to yellow. The plant has a compact, rosette-like growth habit, with leaves that curl inward when dry. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
