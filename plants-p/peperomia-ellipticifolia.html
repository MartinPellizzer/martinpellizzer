Peperomia ellipticifolia

Peperomia ellipticifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaved peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features oval-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically bright green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia ellipticifolia is prized for its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
