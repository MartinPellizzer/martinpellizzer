Polygonum bistortoides var. oblongifolium

Polygonum bistortoides var. oblongifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved bistort, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often reddish or purple-tinged, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its small, greenish-white flowers appear in spikes during the summer months.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
