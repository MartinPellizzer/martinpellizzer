Pilea propinqua

Pilea propinqua, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a small, herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features round, fleshy leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It is a popular indoor plant due to its ease of care and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

Pilea propinqua is often grown for its ornamental foliage rather than its flowers.
