Pomatocalpa laoticum

Pomatocalpa laoticum, commonly known as the Laotian spider orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, spider-like floral structure. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest habitats, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and feature a unique coloration with pale yellow and green tones.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
