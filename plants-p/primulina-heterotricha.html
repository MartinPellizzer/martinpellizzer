Primulina heterotricha

Primulina heterotricha, commonly known as the velvet leaf.

This evergreen perennial is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia. It features striking, velvety leaves that are dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom during the warmer months.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and compact growth habit.
