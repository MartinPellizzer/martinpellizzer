Pleurothallis cryptoceras

Pleurothallis cryptoceras, commonly known as the hidden hooded orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features intricate, narrow leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers with a unique hood-like structure. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive coloration pattern that helps in attracting specific pollinators.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicate species with limited distribution.
