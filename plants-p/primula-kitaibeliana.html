Primula kitaibeliana

Primula kitaibeliana, commonly known as the Kitaibel's primrose, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are oval and slightly hairy, forming a basal rosette.

Primula kitaibeliana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its early spring blooms.
