Philadelphus billiardii

Philadelphus billiardii, commonly known as the Chinese mock orange, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are dark green on top and hairy underneath. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Philadelphus billiardii is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and pleasant scent.
