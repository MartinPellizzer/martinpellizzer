Pteris × delchampsii

Pteris × delchampsii, commonly known as the crested fern, is a hybrid species within the water fern family, Pteridaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, curled fronds that give it a unique, ornamental appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as woodland floors or rocky crevices. Its fronds are usually dark green with a leathery texture and can reach lengths of up to 1 meter.

Pteris × delchampsii is valued in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant or garden specimen.
