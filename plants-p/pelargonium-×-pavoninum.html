Pelargonium × pavoninum

Pelargonium × pavoninum, commonly known as the Persian geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, double flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with dark green, velvety leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Pelargonium × pavoninum is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and as a potted plant.
