Prenanthes abietina

Prenanthes abietina, commonly known as the white wood aster, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, woodsy areas and has hairy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its flowering stems are often branched and can reach heights of up to 1 meter.

Prenanthes abietina is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
