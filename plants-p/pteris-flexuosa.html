Pteris flexuosa

Pteris flexuosa, commonly known as the zigzag water fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that exhibit a distinctive zigzag pattern along their length. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks. Its leaves are bi-lobed and can reach lengths of up to 30 centimeters.

Pteris flexuosa is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
