Petrea kohautiana var. anomala

Petrea kohautiana var. anomala, commonly known as the pink shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking pink and white bract flowers that resemble small, delicate petals. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The variety anomala is distinguished by its more vibrant floral display compared to other varieties of Petrea kohautiana.
