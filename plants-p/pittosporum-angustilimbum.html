Pittosporum angustilimbum

Pittosporum angustilimbum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, whitish bloom. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by spherical, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

Pittosporum angustilimbum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
