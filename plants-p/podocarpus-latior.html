Podocarpus latior

Podocarpus latior, commonly known as the broadleaf podocarp or Chinese podocarp, is a species of coniferous evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Podocarpaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and conical shape. The plant has scale-like leaves that are arranged in dense clusters, and it produces small, spherical cones.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
