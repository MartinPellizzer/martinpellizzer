Plectranthus spectabilis

Plectranthus spectabilis, commonly known as the purple heart plant, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features square, hairy stems and produces clusters of tubular, violet to purple flowers. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Plectranthus spectabilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in gardens and as a houseplant.
