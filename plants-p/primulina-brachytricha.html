Primulina brachytricha

Primulina brachytricha, commonly known as the short-stemmed primula, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit, with short, hairy stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically violet or purple, with five petals and a distinctive spurred shape.

Primulina brachytricha is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and blooms.
