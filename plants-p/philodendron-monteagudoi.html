Philodendron monteagudoi

Philodendron monteagudoi, commonly known as the Monteagudo philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to the rainforests of Central America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by round, orange-red berries that are edible for birds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance and growth habit make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
