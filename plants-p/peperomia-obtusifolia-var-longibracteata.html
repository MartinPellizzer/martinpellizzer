Peperomia obtusifolia var. longibracteata

Peperomia obtusifolia var. longibracteata, commonly known as the broadleaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a reddish or purple hue on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are often surrounded by long, bract-like structures characteristic of its variety. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
