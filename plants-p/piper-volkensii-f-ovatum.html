Piper volkensii f. ovatum

Piper volkensii f. ovatum, commonly known as the velvetleaf pepper or the waxy-leaf pepper, is a rare and unique variant of the Piper volkensii species.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant is native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes in South America, where it thrives in cool, humid climates. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant's botanical features make it a subject of interest for both horticulturists and botanists.
