Podocarpus fleuryi var. parvifolia

Podocarpus fleuryi var. parvifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved podocarp, is a species of evergreen tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features dense, dark green foliage with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically less than 5 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, woody cones that contain edible seeds, which are often consumed by various wildlife species. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic and ecological benefits.
