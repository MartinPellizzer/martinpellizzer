Pouteria brousmichei

Pouteria brousmichei, commonly known as the Brousmiche's sapodilla, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and large, simple leaves. The tree produces yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in panicles, followed by edible, sweet, and sometimes sticky fruits.
