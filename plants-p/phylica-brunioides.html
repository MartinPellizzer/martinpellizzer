Phylica brunioides

Phylica brunioides, commonly known as the silver bruni, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.

Phylica brunioides also plays a role in supporting local wildlife, particularly birds and insects.
