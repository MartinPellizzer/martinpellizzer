Pyrethrum allionii

Pyrethrum allionii, commonly known as the alpine pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that are typically clustered in flat-topped inflorescences. Pyrethrum allionii is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
