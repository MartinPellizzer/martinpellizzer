Poterium magnolii f. minor

Poterium magnolii f. minor, commonly known as the miniature magnolia.

This plant is a compact form of the magnolia, characterized by its smaller size and reduced floral display. It retains the typical characteristics of the parent species, including its showy, fragrant flowers. The leaves are broad and glossy, providing a lush green backdrop to the blooms.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and containers due to its elegant appearance and manageable size.
