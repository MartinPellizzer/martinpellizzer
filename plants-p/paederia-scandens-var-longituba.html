Paederia scandens var. longituba

Paederia scandens var. longituba, commonly known as the long-tubed paederia, is a flowering vine native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale green. The plant is characterized by its long, slender floral tubes, which are adapted for pollination by specific insect species. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
