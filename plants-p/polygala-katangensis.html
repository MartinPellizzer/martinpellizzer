Polygala katangensis

Polygala katangensis, commonly known as the Katanga milk-vetch, is a flowering plant native to central Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and is often found in regions with a tropical climate. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, and it produces small, spherical fruits that contain several seeds.
