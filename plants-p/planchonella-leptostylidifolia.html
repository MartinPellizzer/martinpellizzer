Planchonella leptostylidifolia

Planchonella leptostylidifolia, commonly known as the slender-styled hibiscus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The flowers are typically yellow or orange with a distinctive red throat, blooming during the wet season. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or lowland forest areas.
