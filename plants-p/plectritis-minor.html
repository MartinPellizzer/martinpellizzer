Plectritis minor

Plectritis minor, commonly known as lesser plectritis, is a small annual herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, pale green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are tiny, clustered in dense heads, and are usually yellowish-green in color.

Plectritis minor is known for its ability to thrive in poor soils and is often found in early successional plant communities.
