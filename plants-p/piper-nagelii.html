Piper nagelii

Piper nagelii, commonly known as the Nagel's pepper vine, is a climbing shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a waxy texture with a distinct aroma.

The fruit is a small, three-lobed berry that turns from green to yellow or orange when mature.
