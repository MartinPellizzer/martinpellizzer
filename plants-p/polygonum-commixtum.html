Polygonum commixtum

Polygonum commixtum, commonly known as Chinese knotweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near streams or in wetlands.

Polygonum commixtum is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread aggressively, making it a common weed in disturbed soils.
