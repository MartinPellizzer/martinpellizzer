Polystichum munitum f. incisoserratum

Polystichum munitum f. incisoserratum, commonly known as the incisoserrate western sword fern, is a fern species native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It is a variant of the western sword fern, characterized by its finely toothed, serrated leaf margins. The fronds are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, with a distinctive sword-like shape. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural settings.
