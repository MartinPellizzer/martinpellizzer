Peperomia herzogii

Peperomia herzogii, commonly known as the Herzog's peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and silver, adding visual interest to any setting. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia herzogii is a low-maintenance plant suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation in suitable climates.
