Plectranthus nankinensis

Plectranthus nankinensis, commonly known as Chinese mint or fragrant mint, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and clusters of small, pale purple to white flowers. It is often cultivated for its fragrant foliage and ornamental value.

Plectranthus nankinensis thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
