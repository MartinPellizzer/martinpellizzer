Polybotrya appendiculata var. major

Polybotrya appendiculata var. major, commonly known as the greater appendiculate polybotrya, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polybotryaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, clustered leaves. Its flowers are typically greenish-white and lack showy petals, instead featuring prominent stamens.

This species is often found in humid, shaded environments within lowland forests.
