Palaquium theoideum

Palaquium theoideum, commonly known as the Theoideum palm.

It is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features a slender trunk and produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. Its leaves are long, pinnately compound, and often have a waxy texture.

The fruit is a small, round berry that turns red when mature, attracting various bird species.
