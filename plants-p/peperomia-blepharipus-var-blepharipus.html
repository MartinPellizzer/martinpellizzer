Peperomia blepharipus var. blepharipus

Peperomia blepharipus var. blepharipus, commonly known as the silver mottled peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance houseplant native to tropical regions.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with distinctive silver or white mottling, giving it a striking visual appeal. The plant has a short, upright stem and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and decorative arrangements.
