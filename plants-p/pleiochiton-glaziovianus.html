Pleiochiton glaziovianus

Pleiochiton glaziovianus, commonly known as the Glaziov's pleiochiton, is a tropical climbing plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescence known as a spathe. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers enclosed within a large, showy leaf-like structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
