Paepalanthus diffissus

Paepalanthus diffissus, commonly known as the Brazilian rosewood or the purple-leafed plant.

It is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking purple foliage and compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.
