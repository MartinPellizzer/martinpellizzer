Pelargonium × seymouriae

Pelargonium × seymouriae, commonly known as the regal geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant typically has velvety, lobed leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy. It is a popular ornamental plant grown for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and as a houseplant.

Pelargonium × seymouriae thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate sunlight to flourish.
