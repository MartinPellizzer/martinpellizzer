Pteris comans var. berteroana

Pteris comans var. berteroana, commonly known as the Bertero's water fern, is a variant of the common water fern, characterized by its delicate, frond-like structures.

It is a fern species native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant features bipinnate leaves that are typically light green and have a feathery appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near water bodies.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
