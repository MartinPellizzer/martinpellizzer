Paphiopedilum insigne var. sanderianum

Paphiopedilum insigne var. sanderianum, commonly known as the Sander's lady's slipper, is a rare and highly sought-after orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety purple flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch, which is a unique feature among orchids. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid, preferring shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. It has a sympodial growth habit, producing new shoots from the base of the previous ones.

Due to its rarity and beauty, it is often cultivated in greenhouses and is considered a valuable addition to botanical collections.
