Phoebe mexicana

Phoebe mexicana, commonly known as Mexican beech, is a small evergreen tree native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes other aromatic trees and shrubs. The plant features dense, dark green foliage with glossy leaves that provide shade and habitat for various wildlife species. Its bark is smooth and reddish-brown, often exuding a mild aromatic scent.

Phoebe mexicana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
