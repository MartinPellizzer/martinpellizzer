Phoebe neurantha var. neurantha

Phoebe neurantha var. neurantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaf phoebe, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as tropical rainforests.

The wood of this species is valued for its fine grain and is used in furniture and cabinetry.
