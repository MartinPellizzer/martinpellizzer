Polystichum mehrae

Polystichum mehrae, commonly known as the Mehra's wood fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant has a complex, branched rhizome system that allows it to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically bipinnate, with a dark green color and a leathery texture.

Polystichum mehrae is often found in rocky areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
