Platantheroides strictissima

Platantheroides strictissima, commonly known as the narrow-leaved twayblade, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a spike. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows.

Its root system is specialized for mycorrhizal associations, which aid in nutrient uptake.
