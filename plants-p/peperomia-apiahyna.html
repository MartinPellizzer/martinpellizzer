Peperomia apiahyna

Peperomia apiahyna, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are often tinged with silver or white markings, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color, which appear in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia apiahyna is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and moderate temperatures for optimal growth.
