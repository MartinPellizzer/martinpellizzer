Pleurothallis huebneri

Pleurothallis huebneri, commonly known as the "Huebner's Pleurothallis," is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It is known for its unique floral structure and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and challenging species to grow in cultivation.
