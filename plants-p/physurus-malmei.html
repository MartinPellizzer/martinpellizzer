Physurus malmei

Physurus malmei, commonly known as the Malme's spurge, is a herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, milky sap. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually green with a waxy texture. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color.

Physurus malmei thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
