Phylica lucida

Phylica lucida, commonly known as the silver tree, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the winter months. It is a member of the Proteaceae family, which includes many other ornamental and ecologically important plants.

Phylica lucida is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in dry, well-drained soils.
