Phyllitis brasiliensis var. decurrens

Phyllitis brasiliensis var. decurrens, commonly known as the Brazilian water fern, is a variant of the water fern native to South America.

It is a aquatic plant that typically grows in slow-moving freshwater environments. The plant has finely divided, feather-like leaves that are adapted for water survival. It is often found in marshes, ponds, and along the edges of streams.

Its botanical structure supports its role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small organisms.
