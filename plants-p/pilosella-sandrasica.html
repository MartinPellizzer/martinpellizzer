Pilosella sandrasica

Pilosella sandrasica, commonly known as the sandrasic mouse-ear, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, grayish-blue foliage and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. Native to certain regions of Europe, it thrives in dry, open habitats such as rocky soils and gravelly areas. Its botanical features make it an important component of local flora in its native range.
