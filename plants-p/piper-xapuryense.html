Piper xapuryense

Piper xapuryense, commonly known as the Xapury pepper, is a flowering plant native to the Amazon rainforest in Brazil.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into small, round berries.

The fruit is used in traditional medicine and is also valued for its aromatic properties.
