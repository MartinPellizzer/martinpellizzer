Protea congensis

Protea congensis, commonly known as the Cape protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence composed of numerous pink to red flower heads. The plant is native to the Cape Floristic Region, a biodiversity hotspot known for its unique flora. Protea congensis thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun exposure.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for cultivation in gardens and botanical collections.
