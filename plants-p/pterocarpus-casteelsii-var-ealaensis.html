Pterocarpus casteelsii var. ealaensis

Pterocarpus casteelsii var. ealaensis, commonly known as the Ealaensis Pterocarpus, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often fragrant wood. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. Its leaves are bipinnate, with numerous small leaflets arranged in a feathery pattern.

The tree is valued for its hard, heavy wood used in traditional crafts and furniture.
