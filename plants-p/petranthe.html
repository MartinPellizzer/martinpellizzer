Petranthe

Petranthe, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its daisy-like flowers.

It features silvery-white, fuzzy leaves that help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Native to regions with dry climates, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
