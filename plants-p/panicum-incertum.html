Panicum incertum

Panicum incertum, commonly known as the uncertain grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a rough texture, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Panicum incertum is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and open woodlands.
