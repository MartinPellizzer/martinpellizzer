Piper colombianum

Piper colombianum, commonly known as the Colombian pepper or Colombian long pepper, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by elongated, curved fruits containing aromatic seeds. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly glossy texture.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
