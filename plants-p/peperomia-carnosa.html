Peperomia carnosa

Peperomia carnosa, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, and velvety leaves that are typically dark green with a slight reddish or purple tint on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
