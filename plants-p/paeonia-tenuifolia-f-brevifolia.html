Paeonia tenuifolia f. brevifolia

Paeonia tenuifolia f. brevifolia, commonly known as the dwarf peony or short-leaved peony, is a compact variety of the peony plant.

It is characterized by its small, upright growth habit and delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. The leaves are narrow and closely spaced, giving the plant a dense appearance. This cultivar is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a versatile addition to many garden settings.
