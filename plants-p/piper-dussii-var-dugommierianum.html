Piper dussii var. dugommierianum

Piper dussii var. dugommierianum, commonly known as the Mexican pepper plant, is a climbing shrub native to southern Mexico.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its slender, vine-like stems and small, opposite leaves. The plant produces tiny greenish-white flowers that develop into small, round berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to both ornamental and medicinal plant collections.
