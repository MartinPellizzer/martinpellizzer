Pyrus astateria

Pyrus astateria, commonly known as the alpine pear, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia. This shrub or small tree typically grows at high elevations, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a silvery underside.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by edible, sweet-tart fruits.
