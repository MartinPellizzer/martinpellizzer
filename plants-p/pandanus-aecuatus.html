Pandanus aecuatus

Pandanus aecuatus, commonly known as the bottlebrush pandanus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and long, narrow leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces striking, bottlebrush-like inflorescences that are often used in traditional crafts. Its leaves are fibrous and can be woven into mats and thatch.

Pandanus aecuatus thrives in coastal areas and is often found in mangrove ecosystems.
