Philodendron graveolens

Philodendron graveolens, commonly known as the Sweetheart Vine, is a climbing tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are often overlooked due to its ornamental foliage. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Philodendron graveolens is popular as a houseplant for its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
