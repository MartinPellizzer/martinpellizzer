Pappophorum lindleyanum var. convolutum

Pappophorum lindleyanum var. convolutum, commonly known as the spiral grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of leaves, which give it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for dry garden environments.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
