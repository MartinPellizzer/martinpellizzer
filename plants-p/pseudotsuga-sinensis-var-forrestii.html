Pseudotsuga sinensis var. forrestii

Pseudotsuga sinensis var. forrestii, commonly known as the Chinese fir var. Forrestii, is a variant of the Chinese fir native to China.

It is a coniferous evergreen tree characterized by its needle-like leaves and cone-bearing structure. The foliage is typically dark green and dense, providing a lush canopy. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions with well-drained soil.

This variant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for timber and landscape purposes.
