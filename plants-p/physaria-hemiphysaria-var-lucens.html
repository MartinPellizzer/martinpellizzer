Physaria hemiphysaria var. lucens

Physaria hemiphysaria var. lucens, commonly known as the shining bladderpod, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, rounded leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and thrives in full sun conditions. Its seed pods are inflated and contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

The species is known for its distinctive bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
