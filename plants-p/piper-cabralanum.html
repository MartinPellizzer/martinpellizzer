Piper cabralanum

Piper cabralanum, commonly known as Cabral's pepper, is a tropical vine native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in spikes. Its fruits are berries that turn red when mature, containing a single seed.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
