Phillyrea latifolia var. buxifolia

Phillyrea latifolia var. buxifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf Phillyrea, is a shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with dark green, leathery leaves that are oval to elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, fleshy berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
