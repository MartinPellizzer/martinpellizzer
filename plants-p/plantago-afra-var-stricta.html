Plantago afra var. stricta

Plantago afra var. stricta, commonly known as African plantain or narrowleaf plantain, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features upright, slender stems with lance-shaped leaves that have a waxy surface and prominent veins. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in dense spikes, which are typically borne on the upper part of the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along waterways.

This species is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
