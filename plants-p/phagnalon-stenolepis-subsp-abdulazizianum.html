Phagnalon stenolepis subsp. abdulazizianum

Phagnalon stenolepis subsp.

abdulazizianum, commonly known as the Persian phagnalon, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the Middle East. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads.
