Phalangium holosericeum

Phalangium holosericeum, commonly known as the slender velvet ant.

This species is a type of ant-like arachnid belonging to the family Phalangidae, characterized by its elongated body and hairy appearance. It is primarily found in tropical and subtropical regions, often inhabiting leaf litter or under bark. The plant-like structure of its body can sometimes lead to confusion with certain plants, but it is distinctly an arachnid.

Its botanical relevance is more about its ecological role in soil aeration and nutrient cycling rather than being a plant itself.
