Pleurothallis citrophila

Pleurothallis citrophila, commonly known as the citron orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, yellowish-green blooms that are often clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and fragrance, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
