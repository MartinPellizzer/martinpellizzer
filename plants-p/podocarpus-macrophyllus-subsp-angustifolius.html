Podocarpus macrophyllus subsp. angustifolius

Podocarpus macrophyllus subsp.

angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved podocarp, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia. It features needle-like leaves that are dark green and arranged in whorls around the stem.

The plant produces small, spherical cones that contain seeds, typical of the Podocarpaceae family. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

This species is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.
