Polypodium lineare var. ussuriense

Polypodium lineare var. ussuriense, commonly known as the Ussuri cliff fern, is a variant of the common polypody.

It is a perennial fern that typically grows in rocky or shaded forest environments. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit with fronds that are once-pinnate and have a distinctive, serrated margin. Its leaves are usually dark green and can reach lengths of up to 30 centimeters.

This variety is native to eastern Asia, including regions of Russia, China, and Korea.
