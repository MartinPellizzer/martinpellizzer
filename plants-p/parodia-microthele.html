Parodia microthele

Parodia microthele, commonly known as the miniature star cactus, is a species of cactus native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, globular shape and pale green to yellowish-green coloration. The plant features a dense covering of white spines and produces yellow flowers during the warmer months. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
