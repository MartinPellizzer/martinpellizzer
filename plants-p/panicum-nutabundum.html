Panicum nutabundum

Panicum nutabundum, commonly known as the silver panicum, is a flowering grass species native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and fluffy, silver-gray inflorescences that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its seeds are small and wind-dispersed, aiding in its spread across various environments.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and drought tolerance.
