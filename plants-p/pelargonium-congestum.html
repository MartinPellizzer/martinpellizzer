Pelargonium congestum

Pelargonium congestum, commonly known as the clustered geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its dense clusters of small, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as a succulent perennial, with fleshy leaves and upright stems. Its flowers are usually pink or red, and it blooms throughout the growing season.

Pelargonium congestum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
