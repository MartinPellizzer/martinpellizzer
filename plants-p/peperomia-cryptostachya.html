Peperomia cryptostachya

Peperomia cryptostachya, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a silvery or whitish underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish-white in color. It is a popular houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.

Peperomia cryptostachya thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.
