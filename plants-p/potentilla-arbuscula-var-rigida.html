Potentilla arbuscula var. rigida

Potentilla arbuscula var. rigida, commonly known as stiff potentilla, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August. The plant has upright, branched stems that are covered in fine, stiff hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its leaves are narrow and elongated, with a slightly hairy surface, and it prefers well-drained, rocky soils.

It is often found in alpine environments and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.
