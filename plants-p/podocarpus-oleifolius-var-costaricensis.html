Podocarpus oleifolius var. costaricensis

Podocarpus oleifolius var. costaricensis, commonly known as the Costa Rican podocarp, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central America.

It belongs to the Podocarpaceae family and is characterized by its dense, glossy green foliage. The plant has small, cone-like structures that produce seeds, typical of the coniferous family. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in tropical regions.
