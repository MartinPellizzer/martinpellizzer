Peperomia reflexifolia

Peperomia reflexifolia, commonly known as the curled leaf peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are often curled or reflexed at the edges, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia reflexifolia is valued for its ornamental foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for tabletop displays and shaded gardens.
