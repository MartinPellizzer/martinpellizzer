Philotheca myoporoides subsp. obovatifolia

Philotheca myoporoides subsp.

obovatifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed myopor, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia. It is a shrub characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal and heathland environments. Its foliage is aromatic, and it is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

It plays a role in supporting local wildlife, particularly insects and birds.
