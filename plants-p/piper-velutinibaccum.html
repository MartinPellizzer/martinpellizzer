Piper velutinibaccum

Piper velutinibaccum, commonly known as the velvetleaf pepper, is a tropical vine native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. Its small, greenish-white flowers are followed by bright red berries that contain the edible seeds.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
