Pittosporum obovatum

Pittosporum obovatum, commonly known as the broadleaf pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate to elliptical leaves that are often fragrant. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that appear in clusters, followed by orange to red berries. It thrives in temperate climates and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued for its ability to tolerate a range of soil conditions.
