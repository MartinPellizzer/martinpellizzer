Plectranthus smithianus

Plectranthus smithianus, commonly known as the Cape hybrid verbena, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of purple and blue. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.

Plectranthus smithianus is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
