Polycarpaea corymbosa var. torrensis

Polycarpaea corymbosa var. torrensis, commonly known as the Torrens polycarpaea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal or inland areas. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers, which are arranged in corymbs. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
