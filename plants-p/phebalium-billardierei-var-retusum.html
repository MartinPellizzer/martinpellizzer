Phebalium billardierei var. retusum

Phebalium billardierei var. retusum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed hakea, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, typically blooming in spring. Its growth habit is compact, making it suitable for small garden spaces.

The species is well adapted to dry conditions and requires minimal water once established.
