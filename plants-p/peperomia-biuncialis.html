Peperomia biuncialis

Peperomia biuncialis, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a distinctive silvery-white underside, giving it its common name. The plant has small, rounded leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color.

Peperomia biuncialis is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.
