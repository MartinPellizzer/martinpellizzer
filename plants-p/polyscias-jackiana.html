Polyscias jackiana

Polyscias jackiana, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red berries. It thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Polyscias jackiana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
