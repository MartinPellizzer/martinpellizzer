Pelargonium × clarum

Pelargonium × clarum, commonly known as the hybrid geranium, is a popular ornamental plant characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is a hybrid between Pelargonium hortorum and Pelargonium × inodorum. The plant typically features lobed leaves and flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often with a distinctive double petal structure. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is commonly cultivated in gardens and containers.

Pelargonium × clarum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care, making it a favorite among gardeners.
