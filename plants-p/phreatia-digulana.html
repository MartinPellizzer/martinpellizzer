Phreatia digulana

Phreatia digulana, commonly known as the Mindanao orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant typically produces a single, upright stem with a few leaves and bears small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a unique subject for both horticultural and ecological study.
