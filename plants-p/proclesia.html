Proclesia

Proclesia, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds.

Proclesia is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
