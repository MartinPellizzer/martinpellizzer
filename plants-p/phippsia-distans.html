Phippsia distans

Phippsia distans, commonly known as the slender phippsia, is a rare flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated stems and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale yellow. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in humid tropical forests. Its leaves are usually narrow and lance-shaped, contributing to its overall elegant appearance.
