Potentilla bodinieri

Potentilla bodinieri, commonly known as the Chinese silverweed, is a perennial shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Potentilla bodinieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping for its hardy nature and attractive appearance.
