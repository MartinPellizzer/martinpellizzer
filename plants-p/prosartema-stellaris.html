Prosartema stellaris

Prosartema stellaris, commonly known as the star-like beauty, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

The botanical features of Prosartema stellaris make it an interesting subject for horticultural study and conservation efforts.
