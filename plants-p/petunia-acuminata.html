Petunia acuminata

Petunia acuminata, commonly known as the sharp-leaved petunia, is a species native to South America.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the nightshade family, Solanaceae. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. Its leaves are ovate with a pointed tip, giving it its common name.

Petunia acuminata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
