Potentilla balansae

Potentilla balansae, commonly known as the Balansae cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky and alpine environments. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are usually simple, alternate, and have a serrated edge.

Potentilla balansae is valued for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and aesthetic appeal.
