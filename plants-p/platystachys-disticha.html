Platystachys disticha

Platystachys disticha, commonly known as the silverleaf honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching branches that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a silvery underside. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white or pale yellow. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and floral display.
