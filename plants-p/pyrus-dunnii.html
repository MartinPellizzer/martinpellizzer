Pyrus dunnii

Pyrus dunnii, commonly known as the Dunns pear, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern South America, particularly in Chile and Argentina. The plant is characterized by its deciduous leaves, which are alternate and ovate in shape. The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been cultivated for its fruit in certain regions.
