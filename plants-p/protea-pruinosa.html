Protea pruinosa

Protea pruinosa, commonly known as the silver protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that consist of numerous white to pinkish flowers surrounded by a dense, woolly bract. The plant is native to the Western and Eastern Cape provinces, where it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. Its unique appearance makes it a popular ornamental plant, though it requires specific conditions to flourish.

The species also plays a role in supporting local pollinators and ecosystems.
