Peperomia variabilis

Peperomia variabilis, commonly known as the variable pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that vary in shape and color, often displaying shades of green, silver, and red. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal, Peperomia variabilis is valued for both its ornamental qualities and ease of care.
