Phalaenopsis kapuasensis f. flava

Phalaenopsis kapuasensis f. flava, commonly known as the Kapuas yellow phalaenopsis, is a rare and ornamental orchid species native to the rainforests of Indonesia.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green color. The plant produces clusters of showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and white, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and botanical collectors.
