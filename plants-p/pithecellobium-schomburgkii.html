Pithecellobium schomburgkii

Pithecellobium schomburgkii, commonly known as the yellow pithecellobium, is a species of legume native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 meters. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves, which are usually pinnate with 5 to 9 pairs of leaflets. Its flowers are yellow and clustered in racemes, and it produces edible, fleshy pods containing seeds.
