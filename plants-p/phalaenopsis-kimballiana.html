Phalaenopsis kimballiana

Phalaenopsis kimballiana, commonly known as the Kimball's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, long-lasting flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
