Pilosella pubescens

Pilosella pubescens, commonly known as the hairy pearly everlasting, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are covered in fine, hairy texture. Pilosella pubescens is often used in dried flower arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms and delicate appearance.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
