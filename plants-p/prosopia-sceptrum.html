Prosopia sceptrum

Prosopia sceptrum, commonly known as the blue-flowered rock heath, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its compact form and evergreen foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, sky-blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, adapted to withstand dry and rocky environments.

Prosopia sceptrum is often found in coastal areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
