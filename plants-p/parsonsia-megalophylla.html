Parsonsia megalophylla

Parsonsia megalophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf parsonsia, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy yellow flowers that are often adorned with red or orange markings. The plant produces bright orange or red berries that contain seeds, which are dispersed by animals. Its leaves are large, glossy, and elliptical in shape, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Parsonsia megalophylla thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and rainforest areas.
