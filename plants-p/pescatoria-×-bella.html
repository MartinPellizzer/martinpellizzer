Pescatoria × bella

Pescatoria × bella, commonly known as the hybrid beauty bellflower, is a perennial plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, often with white or pink markings. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, featuring lance-shaped leaves that are slightly hairy. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
