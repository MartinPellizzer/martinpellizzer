Pleurothallis poasensis

Pleurothallis poasensis, commonly known as the Poas orchid, is a species of orchid native to Costa Rica.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insects.

Pleurothallis poasensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and contributes to the biodiversity of its native habitat.
