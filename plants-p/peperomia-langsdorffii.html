Peperomia langsdorffii

Peperomia langsdorffii, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery or grayish pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are typically borne in clusters. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and tolerance for dry conditions.

Its slow growth rate makes it an ideal choice for container gardening and bonsai-style styling.
