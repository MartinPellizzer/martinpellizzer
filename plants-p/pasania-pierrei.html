Pasania pierrei

Pasania pierrei, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and showy, tubular flowers. Pasania pierrei is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
