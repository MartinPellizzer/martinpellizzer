Potentilla hirta f. albanica

Potentilla hirta f. albanica, commonly known as the Albanian cinquefoil, is a perennial shrub native to the Balkans.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has serrated, hairy leaves that are often covered in fine, soft pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
