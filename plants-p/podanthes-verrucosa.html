Podanthes verrucosa

Podanthes verrucosa, commonly known as the rough podanthes, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its woody stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the dry season. It thrives in sunny, well-drained coastal habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
