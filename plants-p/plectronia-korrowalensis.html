Plectronia korrowalensis

It is a small shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color, which bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires a specific microclimate to flourish.
