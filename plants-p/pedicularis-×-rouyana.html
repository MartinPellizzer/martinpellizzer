Pedicularis × rouyana

Pedicularis × rouyana, commonly known as the hybrid monkey orchid, is a member of the Orobanchaceae family and is native to the mountainous regions of central and southern China.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, spurred flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, resembling the face of a monkey. The plant is characterized by its fleshy, hairy stems and leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections worldwide.
