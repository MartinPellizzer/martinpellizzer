Persoonia acuminata

Persoonia acuminata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved geebung, is a shrub native to the eastern regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in heathlands and coastal areas. It produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds dispersed by animals.

Persoonia acuminata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
