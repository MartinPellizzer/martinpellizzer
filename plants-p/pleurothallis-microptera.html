Pleurothallis microptera

Pleurothallis microptera, commonly known as the miniature pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, narrow leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its flowers are often clustered in a spike and bloom during the rainy season.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
