Pouzolzia trialata

Pouzolzia trialata, commonly known as the silverleaf pouzolzia, is a shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a leathery texture.

Pouzolzia trialata is often found in moist, shaded areas such as woodland edges and stream banks.
