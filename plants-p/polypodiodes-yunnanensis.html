Polypodiodes yunnanensis

Polypodiodes yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan polypody, is a fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
