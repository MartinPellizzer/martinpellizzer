Persoonia revoluta

Persoonia revoluta, commonly known as the silver-leafed pear.

It is an evergreen shrub native to Australia, characterized by its silvery, hairy leaves and small, white flowers that appear in spring. The plant produces woody, edible fruits that are often consumed by native wildlife. Persoonia revoluta thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to coastal and sandy environments.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and resilience to drought.
