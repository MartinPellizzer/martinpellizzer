Pittosporum japonicum

Pittosporum japonicum, commonly known as the Japanese pittosporum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Japan.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that turn reddish-brown in the fall. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers in clusters, followed by orange-red berries that are edible for birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its adaptability and aesthetic appeal make it a valued addition to gardens and landscapes.
