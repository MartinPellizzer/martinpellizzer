Paracaryum hedgei

Paracaryum hedgei, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its compact size and ornamental value.

Paracaryum hedgei is known for its ability to bloom year-round under proper care.
