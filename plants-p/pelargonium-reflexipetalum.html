Pelargonium reflexipetalum

Pelargonium reflexipetalum, commonly known as the reflexipetalum geranium, is a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The leaves are typically rounded with a slightly hairy texture, and the plant produces small, clustered flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets and containers.

This plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
