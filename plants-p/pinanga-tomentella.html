Pinanga tomentella

Pinanga tomentella, commonly known as the hairy bamboo palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. Pinanga tomentella is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
