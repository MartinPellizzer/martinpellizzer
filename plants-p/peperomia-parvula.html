Peperomia parvula

Peperomia parvula, commonly known as the small-leaved peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval to ovate in shape, with a glossy surface and a deep green color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, arranged in clusters along the stems. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and ability to thrive in well-draining soil.

Peperomia parvula is prized for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
