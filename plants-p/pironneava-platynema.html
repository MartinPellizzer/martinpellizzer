Pironneava platynema

Pironneava platynema, commonly known as the golden leaf fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically a vibrant yellow-green in color. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal.

Pironneava platynema is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
