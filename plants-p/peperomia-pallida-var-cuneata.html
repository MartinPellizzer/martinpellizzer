Peperomia pallida var. cuneata

Peperomia pallida var. cuneata, commonly known as the pink peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features striking, velvety pink to purple foliage with a waxy texture, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. The plant typically grows in a rosette form, with small, lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or silver markings. It produces delicate, spathaceous flowers in shades of green and white, though these are less prominent than its foliage.

Peperomia pallida var. cuneata prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, thriving in humid environments.
