Peperomia stolonifera

Peperomia stolonifera, commonly known as the creeping peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval or heart-shaped, often with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish or reddish in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ability to spread via stolons allows it to form dense mats, enhancing its ornamental appeal.
