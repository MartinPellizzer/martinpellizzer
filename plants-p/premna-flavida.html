Premna flavida

Premna flavida, commonly known as the yellow premna, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including India, China, and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy and have a yellowish-green hue. Its small, clustered flowers are typically white or pale pink, and they bloom during the warmer months.

Premna flavida is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forests and along stream banks.
