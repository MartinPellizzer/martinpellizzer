Piper mindorense

Piper mindorense, commonly known as Mindoro pepper, is a climbing vine native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many pepper species. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, peppery fruits. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly serrated margin.

The plant is often cultivated for its aromatic and spicy fruits, which are used in traditional Filipino cuisine.
