Persoonia baeckeoides

Persoonia baeckeoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaf burr-pear, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and distinctive, woody, spherical fruits that resemble small burrs. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It produces small white or yellowish flowers during the spring and summer months.

Persoonia baeckeoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native habitats.
