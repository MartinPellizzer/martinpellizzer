Piper san-joseanum var. kobense

Piper san-joseanum var. kobense, commonly known as the San Jose pepper or Koben's pepper, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by elongated, peppery fruits that are typically harvested when mature. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly rough texture.

The plant is valued for its aromatic leaves and fruits, which are used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.
