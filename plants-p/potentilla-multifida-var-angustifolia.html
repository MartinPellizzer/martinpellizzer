Potentilla multifida var. angustifolia

Potentilla multifida var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaf potentilla, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves that give it a delicate appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is typically found in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open woodlands.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for gardens and landscape design.
