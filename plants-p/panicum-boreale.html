Panicum boreale

Panicum boreale, commonly known as northern dropseed, is a perennial grass native to the northern regions of North America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and produces delicate, fluffy seed heads that resemble small clouds. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for xeriscaping and native plant gardens. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that adds visual interest throughout the growing season.

Panicum boreale is valued for its low maintenance requirements and ability to provide habitat for pollinators and small wildlife.
