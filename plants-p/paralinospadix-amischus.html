Paralinospadix amischus

Paralinospadix amischus, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers that resemble spiders, hence its common name. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are glossy and elongated. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, fertile soil.

Paralinospadix amischus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice in tropical gardens.
