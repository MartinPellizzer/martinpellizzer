Pleurothallis hystrix

Pleurothallis hystrix, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its unique, spiraling inflorescence that gives it its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually small and come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink.

Pleurothallis hystrix is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
