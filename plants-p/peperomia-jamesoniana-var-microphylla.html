Peperomia jamesoniana var. microphylla

Peperomia jamesoniana var. microphylla, commonly known as the miniature peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green, with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate moisture.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container cultivation.
