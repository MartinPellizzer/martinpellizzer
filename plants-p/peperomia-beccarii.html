Peperomia beccarii

Peperomia beccarii, commonly known as the Beccari's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, often green or reddish, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia beccarii is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
