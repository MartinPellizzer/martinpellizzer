Prasophyllum plumosum

Prasophyllum plumosum, commonly known as the feather-leaved leek orchid, is an Australian native plant belonging to the orchid family (Orchidaceae).

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in spring. This plant is typically found in heathlands and open forests in southeastern Australia. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and sometimes endangered species.
