Pyrus Tushanensis

Pyrus tushanensis is a rare and lesser-known species of apple tree native to China. It thrives in the mountainous regions of Tushan, where its unique adaptations allow it to survive in harsh climates. This plant is notable for its small, ornamental fruits and its potential medicinal properties. Due to habitat loss and limited research, it remains a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Understanding Pyrus tushanensis is key to preserving biodiversity in its native ecosystem.
