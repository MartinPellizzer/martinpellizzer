Psychotria rubra

Psychotria rubra, commonly known as the red psychotria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its striking red flowers, which are often clustered in inflorescences. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often glossy, with a dark green color.

Psychotria rubra is also notable for its role in the ecological interactions of its native habitats, particularly as a food source for certain pollinators.
