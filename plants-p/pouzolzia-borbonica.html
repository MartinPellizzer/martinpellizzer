Pouzolzia borbonica

Pouzolzia borbonica, commonly known as the Madagascar pouzolzia, is a member of the Polygonaceae family.

It is a shrub characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually oval to lanceolate in shape. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, and it is often found in moist, shaded environments.
