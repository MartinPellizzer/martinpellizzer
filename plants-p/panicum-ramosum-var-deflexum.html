Panicum ramosum var. deflexum

Panicum ramosum var. deflexum, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a warm-season grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces fluffy, pale blue to silver panicles that bloom in late summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for native plant gardens.

Its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil make it both aesthetically pleasing and ecologically beneficial.
