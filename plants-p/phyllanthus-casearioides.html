Phyllanthus casearioides

Phyllanthus casearioides, commonly known as the red sandalwood tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by orange-red berries that are an important food source for various wildlife.
