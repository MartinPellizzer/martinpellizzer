Pilosella melaneilema

Pilosella melaneilema, commonly known as the black-leaved mouse-ear, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clustered inflorescences. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in rocky and sandy soils.

It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and shrublands.
