Prangos arabica

Prangos arabica, commonly known as the desert thistle, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the Middle East and North Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water. It has a deep root system that allows it to access underground moisture.

Prangos arabica is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
