Polyosma maliauensis

Polyosma maliauensis, commonly known as the Maliauensis gooseberry, is a shrub native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces edible berries that are typically yellow or orange in color, with a tart flavor. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
