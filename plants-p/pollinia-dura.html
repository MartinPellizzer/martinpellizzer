Pollinia dura

Pollinia dura, commonly known as the hard pollinia orchid, is a unique plant characterized by its specialized reproductive structures.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to certain regions of South America. The plant's most distinctive feature is its hard, dry pollinia, which are clustered together in a structure called a pollinarium. These pollinia are adapted for attachment to the bodies of pollinators, ensuring efficient pollination.

The botanical features of Pollinia dura make it an interesting subject for study in plant reproduction and adaptation.
