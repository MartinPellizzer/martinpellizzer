Plectrachne aristiglumis

Plectrachne aristiglumis, commonly known as the spike-spikegrass, is a grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive inflorescence, which consists of spikelets arranged in a dense, spike-like structure. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Plectrachne aristiglumis is often found in coastal dunes and is an important component of local ecosystems.
