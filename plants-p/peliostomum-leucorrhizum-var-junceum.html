Peliostomum leucorrhizum var. junceum

Peliostomum leucorrhizum var. junceum, commonly known as the white root peliostomum, is a aquatic plant native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and lakes. The plant has long, slender leaves that float on the water's surface, with roots submerged in the sediment. Its flowers are small and white, often clustered together in a loose inflorescence.

It plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms and contributing to water clarity through its filtration capabilities.
