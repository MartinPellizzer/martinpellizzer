Phreatia laxa

Phreatia laxa, commonly known as the slender phreatia, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, slender stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces large, showy flowers with a distinctive yellow-green coloration. It typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth.

Phreatia laxa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty.
