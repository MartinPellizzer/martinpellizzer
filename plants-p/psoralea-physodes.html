Psoralea physodes

Psoralea physodes, commonly known as the narrow-leaf psoralea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant features small, pea-like flowers that are typically pale yellow to greenish in color. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with several pairs of narrow, elongated leaflets.
