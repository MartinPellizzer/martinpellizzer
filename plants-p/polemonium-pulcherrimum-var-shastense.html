Polemonium pulcherrimum var. shastense

Polemonium pulcherrimum var. shastense, commonly known as Shasta larkspur, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial with striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or meadow habitats. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stems are upright and branched.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ecological benefits.
