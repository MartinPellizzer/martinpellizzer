Pittosporum maoshanense

Pittosporum maoshanense, commonly known as the Maoshan pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Pittosporaceae and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are glossy and dark green, while the flowers are usually white or pale yellow, appearing in clusters.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
