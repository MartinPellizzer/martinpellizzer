Phreatia semiorbicularis var. semiorbicularis

Phreatia semiorbicularis var. semiorbicularis, commonly known as the semiorbicular orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences bearing numerous small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the humid, shaded environments it inhabits.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or shaded gardens.
