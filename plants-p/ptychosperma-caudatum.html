Ptychosperma caudatum

Ptychosperma caudatum, commonly known as the sword fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its sword-shaped, leathery fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizome is thick and fleshy, often used in traditional medicine.

The fronds are evergreen and can reach lengths of up to 1 meter, making it a striking ornamental plant.
