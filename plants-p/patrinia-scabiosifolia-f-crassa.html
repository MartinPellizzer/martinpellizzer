Patrinia scabiosifolia f. crassa

Patrinia scabiosifolia f. crassa, commonly known as the broad-leaved Patrinia, is a flowering plant species native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, violet-blue flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shaded areas.
