Pleurothallis violaceorosea

Pleurothallis violaceorosea, commonly known as the violet rose orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, delicate flowers with a striking violet-pink coloration. The plant typically blooms during the warmer months, showcasing a unique and elegant appearance. Its leaves are usually dark green and arranged in a dense rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This orchid is often cultivated for its beauty and is appreciated by enthusiasts of rare and exotic plants.
