Panicum maguirei

Panicum maguirei, commonly known as Maguire's bluestem, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a warm-season grass that typically grows in open woodlands and disturbed sites. The plant features upright, cylindrical spikelets that are often blue-green in color, giving it its common name. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish tint, and it produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.

Panicum maguirei is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological restoration projects.
