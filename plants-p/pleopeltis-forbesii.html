Pleopeltis forbesii

Pleopeltis forbesii, commonly known as the Forbes' rock fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and clustered fronds. The leaves are typically leathery and have a distinctive, elongated shape with a prominent midrib. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on trees or rocks in tropical forests.
