Pyrostria cordifolia var. cordifolia

Pyrostria cordifolia var. cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf pyrostria, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically glossy and deep green. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
