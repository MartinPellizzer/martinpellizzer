Pleurothallis anomala

Pleurothallis anomala, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant typically features small, lance-shaped leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, airy substrates for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
