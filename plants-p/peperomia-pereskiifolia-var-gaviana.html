Peperomia pereskiifolia var. gaviana

Peperomia pereskiifolia var. gaviana, commonly known as the Gaviana peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves with a slightly serrated edge and a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white or pale green. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Its unique leaf patterns and compact growth make it a popular choice for tabletop displays and hanging baskets.
