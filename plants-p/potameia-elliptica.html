Potameia elliptica

Potameia elliptica, commonly known as the narrow-leafed rock cress, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features elliptical, narrow leaves and produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky outcrops or along riverbanks.

Potameia elliptica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens.
