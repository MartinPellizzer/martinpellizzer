Paeonia bifurcata

Paeonia bifurcata, commonly known as the bifurcated peony, is a deciduous perennial plant native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, double-layered flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Paeonia bifurcata is also valued for its aromatic foliage and its historical use in traditional Chinese medicine.
