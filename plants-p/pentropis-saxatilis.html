Pentropis saxatilis

Pentropis saxatilis, commonly known as the rock pentrap, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale blue in color. It is adapted to arid environments and has a low-growing habit to conserve moisture.

Pentropis saxatilis is often found in coastal and mountainous regions where it thrives in well-drained conditions.
