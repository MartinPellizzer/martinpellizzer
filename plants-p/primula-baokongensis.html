Primula baokongensis

Primula baokongensis, commonly known as the Baokong primrose, is a rare flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant features rosettes of basal leaves and produces clusters of tubular or bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
