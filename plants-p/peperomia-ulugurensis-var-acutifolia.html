Peperomia ulugurensis var. acutifolia

Peperomia ulugurensis var. acutifolia, commonly known as the sharp-leaved peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to Central America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves with a pointed tip, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for tabletop displays and hanging baskets.
