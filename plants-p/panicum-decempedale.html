Panicum decempedale

Panicum decempedale, commonly known as purpletop millet, is a warm-season grass native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, erect growth and distinctive purple-tinted inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist to wet soils, often found in wetlands and along stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Panicum decempedale is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in wetland ecosystems.
