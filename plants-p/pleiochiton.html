Pleiochiton

Pleiochiton, commonly known as the tropical tree fern, is a member of the Cyatheaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of arching fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that helps it anchor in moist, shaded environments. Its spores are produced on the undersides of its fronds, which are typically found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Pleiochiton is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
