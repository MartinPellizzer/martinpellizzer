Pleurothallis platyglottis

Pleurothallis platyglottis, commonly known as the flat-lip pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features slender, wiry stems and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to conserve moisture in its natural habitat.

Pleurothallis platyglottis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
