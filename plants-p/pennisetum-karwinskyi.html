Pennisetum karwinskyi

Pennisetum karwinskyi, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to southern Africa.

It features tall, slender stalks that bear fluffy, purple plumes, which are particularly showy in late summer and autumn. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green, with a slight bluish tint. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Pennisetum karwinskyi is known for its drought tolerance and ability to attract birds and beneficial insects.
