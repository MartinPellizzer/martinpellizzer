Philodendron bayae

Philodendron bayae, commonly known as the Baya philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slight variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by reddish-brown berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Philodendron bayae is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
