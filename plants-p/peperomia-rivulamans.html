Peperomia rivulamans

Peperomia rivulamans, commonly known as the river peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink, providing an ornamental appeal. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia rivulamans is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and moderate humidity to flourish.
