Parodia haselbergii f. microdasys

Parodia haselbergii f. microdasys, commonly known as the miniature star cactus, is a rare and compact cactus species native to southern Africa.

It features a spherical or slightly elongated stem covered in dense, white to pale green spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is a prized specimen among cactus enthusiasts.
