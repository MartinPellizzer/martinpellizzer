Portulaca cryptopetala

Portulaca cryptopetala, commonly known as the hidden-leaved purslane, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a low-growing annual herb with succulent, fleshy leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant has a compact growth habit, often forming dense mats on the ground. Its leaves are covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in dry environments.

Portulaca cryptopetala is well adapted to hot, sunny conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
