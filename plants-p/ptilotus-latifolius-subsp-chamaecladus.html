Ptilotus latifolius subsp. chamaecladus

Ptilotus latifolius subsp.

chamaecladus, commonly known as the broad-leaved spinifex, is a flowering plant native to Australia. It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit.
