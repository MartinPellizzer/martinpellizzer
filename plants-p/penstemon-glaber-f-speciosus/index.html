Penstemon Glaber F. Speciosus

Penstemon glaber f. speciosus is a striking cultivar of the penstemon genus known for its vibrant floral display. This plant is prized for its tall spikes of tubular flowers that bloom in a range of colors from blue to purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements add to its appeal among gardeners. This variety stands out for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators like bees and hummingbirds.
