Papaver comptum

Papaver comptum, commonly known as the slender poppy, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, erect stems that are often branched and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces striking flowers with bright orange petals that are typically 2 to 4 centimeters in diameter. Its capsule fruit contains numerous small, dark seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Papaver comptum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
