Plantago preslii

Plantago preslii, commonly known as the Preslia plantain, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a basal rosette of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a dense spike, which are borne on slender stalks. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

The species is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
