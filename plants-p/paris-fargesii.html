Paris fargesii

Paris fargesii, commonly known as the Yunnan Paris, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It belongs to the Melanthiaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
