Pleurothallis minimifolia

Pleurothallis minimifolia, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, narrow leaves and produces tiny, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments to ensure its survival.
