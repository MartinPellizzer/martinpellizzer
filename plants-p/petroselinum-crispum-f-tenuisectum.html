Petroselinum crispum f. tenuisectum

Petroselinum crispum f. tenuisectum, commonly known as slender leaf parsley, is a cultivar of parsley characterized by its finely divided, slender leaves.

This variety is a member of the Apiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and vegetables. It is typically smaller in stature compared to the common curly parsley, with a more delicate appearance. The plant is often used in culinary applications for its mild, fresh flavor.

Its botanical features make it suitable for both ornamental and edible purposes in gardens.
