Pelexia robusta

Pelexia robusta, commonly known as the robust pelexia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy stems. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine pubescence, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Pelexia robusta is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and attractive foliage.
