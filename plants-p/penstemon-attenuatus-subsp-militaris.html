Penstemon attenuatus subsp. militaris

Penstemon attenuatus subsp.

militaris, commonly known as the slender beardtongue, is a perennial herb native to the western United States. It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats such as slopes and gravelly soils.

The plant features slender, hairy stems and produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a rough texture.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to arid conditions.
