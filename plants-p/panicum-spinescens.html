Panicum spinescens

Panicum spinescens, commonly known as spiny panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a perennial grass characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. Its seeds are typically dispersed by wind, and the plant is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils.

Panicum spinescens is often found in open habitats such as fields, roadsides, and disturbed areas.
