Peperomia guaiquinimana

Peperomia guaiquinimana, commonly known as the Guaiquinimana Peperomia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, which appear in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia guaiquinimana is prized for its ornamental foliage and is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
