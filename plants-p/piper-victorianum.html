Piper victorianum

Piper victorianum, commonly known as the Victorian pepper plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many pepper species. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and small, clustered flowers. Its fruit, a small berry, contains the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.
