Phaleria pentecostalis

Phaleria pentecostalis, commonly known as the Christmas tree fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyrtomium and is characterized by its tall, tree-like structure with a central trunk and feathery fronds. Phaleria pentecostalis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
