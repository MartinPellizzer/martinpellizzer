Pleurothallis laevis

Pleurothallis laevis, commonly known as the smooth pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant prefers warm, humid environments and is often found growing in the understory of rainforests. Its leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, adapted to capture limited light in its natural habitat.

Pleurothallis laevis is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
