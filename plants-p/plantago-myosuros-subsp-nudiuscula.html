Plantago myosuros subsp. nudiuscula

Plantago myosuros subsp.

nudiuscula, commonly known as the slender plantain, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It typically grows in grassy areas, along roadsides, and in disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates.

The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowering stems bear dense clusters of small, greenish-white flowers, which are wind-pollinated.

The subspecies is distinguished by its shorter, more compact growth habit compared to other Plantago species.
