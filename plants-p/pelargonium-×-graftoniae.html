Pelargonium × graftoniae

Pelargonium × graftoniae, commonly known as the hybrid geranium, is a flowering plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Pelargonium hortorum and Pelargonium × inodorum. The plant is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that come in a variety of colors including red, pink, and white. Its foliage is typically broad and lobed, with a velvety texture.

Pelargonium × graftoniae is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
