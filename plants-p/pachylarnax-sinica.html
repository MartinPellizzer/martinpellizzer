Pachylarnax sinica

Pachylarnax sinica, commonly known as the Chinese arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves and striking spadix inflorescence. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant foliage and unique floral structure. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Pachylarnax sinica is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
