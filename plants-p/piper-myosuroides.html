Piper myosuroides

Piper myosuroides, commonly known as the African black pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, peppery fruits. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a glossy surface, while the fruits are small, dark berries that contain the aromatic seeds.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional remedies across its native regions.
