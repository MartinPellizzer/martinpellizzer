Petrocosmea thermopuncta

Petrocosmea thermopuncta, commonly known as the thermal dot orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to specific high-altitude regions.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers with a distinctive red or orange hue, often adorned with dark spots. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, temperate climates. Its flowering period typically occurs in late spring to early summer.

Due to its limited habitat and specific environmental requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species.
