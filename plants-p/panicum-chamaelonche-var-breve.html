Panicum chamaelonche var. breve

Panicum chamaelonche var. breve, commonly known as dwarf red panicum, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features fine, grass-like leaves that are typically a vibrant green, with striking red or bronze tints during the fall. The plant produces delicate, feathery plumes that add a soft, ornamental texture to gardens. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its low maintenance and ability to add color and texture to landscape designs.
