Pterocarpus chinensis

Pterocarpus chinensis, commonly known as Chinese flame tree, is a tropical leguminous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, flame-like inflorescences and pinnate leaves with elongated leaflets. The tree produces yellow or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by flat, pod-like fruits containing hard, dark seeds. Its wood is highly valued for its durability and aesthetic appeal, often used in furniture and decorative crafts.

The plant also has medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
