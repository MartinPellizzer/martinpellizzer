Pavetta viburnoides

Pavetta viburnoides, commonly known as the African snowberry, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the cooler months. The plant produces bright red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. Its leaves are elliptical with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy coating.

Pavetta viburnoides is often found in moist, shaded areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
