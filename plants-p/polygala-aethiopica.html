Polygala aethiopica

Polygala aethiopica, commonly known as the Ethiopian milkweed, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to drought conditions.

Polygala aethiopica is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along riverbanks.
