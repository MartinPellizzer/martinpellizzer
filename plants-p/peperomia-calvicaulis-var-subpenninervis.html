Peperomia calvicaulis var. subpenninervis

Peperomia calvicaulis var. subpenninervis, commonly known as the narrow-veined peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly textured surface. The plant has a short, upright stem and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often overlooked. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal water needs.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for tabletop displays and as a decorative houseplant.
