Polycarena aethiopica

Polycarena aethiopica, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its compact size and vibrant blooms.

Polycarena aethiopica is often cultivated as a houseplant and is known for its ease of care.
