Polygala polygoniflora

Polygala polygoniflora, commonly known as the many-flowered milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters. The plant has palmately divided leaves and produces small, bean-like seeds. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in horticulture.
