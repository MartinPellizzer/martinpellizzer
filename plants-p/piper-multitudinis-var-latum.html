Piper multitudinis var. latum

Piper multitudinis var. latum, commonly known as the broadleaf pepper plant, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and for its potential medicinal properties.
