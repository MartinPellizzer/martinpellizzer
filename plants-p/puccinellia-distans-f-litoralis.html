Puccinellia distans f. litoralis

Puccinellia distans f. litoralis, commonly known as the coastal panicgrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is a halophyte, meaning it thrives in saline environments, often found in coastal areas and salt marshes. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to withstand high salinity and periodic flooding, making it an important component of coastal ecosystems.

Its ecological role includes stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
