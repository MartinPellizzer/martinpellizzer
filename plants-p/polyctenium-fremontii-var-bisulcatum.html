Polyctenium fremontii var. bisulcatum

Polyctenium fremontii var. bisulcatum, commonly known as the Fremont's pearly everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in composite heads. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Its botanical features make it an important component of its native ecosystem.
