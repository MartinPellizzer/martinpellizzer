Phyllostachys edulis f. heterocycla

Phyllostachys edulis f. heterocycla, commonly known as the dwarf bamboo, is a unique variant of the bamboo species native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and shorter, more densely packed culms compared to its standard counterpart. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green, adding aesthetic value to gardens and landscapes. It is well-suited for container gardening and small spaces due to its controlled size.

This ornamental bamboo is also appreciated for its resilience and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
