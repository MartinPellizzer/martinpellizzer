Peperomia fimbribractea var. sparsipila

Peperomia fimbribractea var. sparsipila, commonly known as the sparse-bract peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, ovate leaves with a glossy, dark green surface and prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically greenish-white in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal water needs.

Its unique leaf structure and compact growth habit make it a popular choice for ornamental purposes.
