Pilosella chaetophyton

Pilosella chaetophyton, commonly known as the hairy goat's beard, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling a fuzzy beard. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which contribute to its common name. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in meadows and grasslands.

Pilosella chaetophyton is generally hardy and can thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
