Premna punctulata

Premna punctulata, commonly known as the spotted premna, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture and small, white or yellowish flowers that appear in clusters. Its fruits are small, three-lobed berries that turn red when mature.

Premna punctulata is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
