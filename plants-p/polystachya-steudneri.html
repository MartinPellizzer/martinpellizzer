Polystachya steudneri

Polystachya steudneri, commonly known as the flame orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features striking, bright red or orange flowers that are often adorned with contrasting yellow or white markings. The plant produces dense, branched inflorescences that give it its common name. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Polystachya steudneri is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
