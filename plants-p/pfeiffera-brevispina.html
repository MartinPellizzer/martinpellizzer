Pfeiffera brevispina

Pfeiffera brevispina, commonly known as the short-spined pfeiffera, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems and spiny leaves. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, adapting to harsh conditions with its water-storing tissues. Its small, clustered flowers are usually yellow or orange, adding ornamental value.

Pfeiffera brevispina is often cultivated as a decorative succulent due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
