Pultenaea trinervis

Pultenaea trinervis, commonly known as the three-veined pultenaea, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The leaves are trifoliate, with three leaflets, and the plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils.

It is often found in coastal and dry forest habitats, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for native wildlife.
