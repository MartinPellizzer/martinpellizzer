Plectranthus nodosus

Plectranthus nodosus, commonly known as the purple heart plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of purple and blue. The plant is a perennial shrub that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is popular in gardens and as a houseplant.

Plectranthus nodosus is also valued for its aromatic leaves, which can be used in herbal teas and traditional medicine.
