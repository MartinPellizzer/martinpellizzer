Plectranthus parviflorus var. australis

Plectranthus parviflorus var. australis, commonly known as the Australian narrow-leaved plectranthus, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features aromatic, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale purple to violet in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
