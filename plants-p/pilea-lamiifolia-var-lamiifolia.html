Pilea lamiifolia var. lamiifolia

Pilea lamiifolia var. lamiifolia, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rosettes of glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ease of care and attractive foliage make it a favored ornamental plant.
