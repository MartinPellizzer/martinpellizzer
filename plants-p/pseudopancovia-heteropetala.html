Pseudopancovia heteropetala

Pseudopancovia heteropetala, commonly known as the rainbow orchid, is a rare and striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multicolored petals that resemble a rainbow, hence its common name. Native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are typically clustered in inflorescences and bloom during the spring and early summer.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, Pseudopancovia heteropetala is highly sought after by botanists and horticulturists.
