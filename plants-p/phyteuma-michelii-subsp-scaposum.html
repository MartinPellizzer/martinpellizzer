Phyteuma michelii subsp. scaposum

Phyteuma michelii subsp.

scaposum, commonly known as the slender phyteuma, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It features upright, slender stems that are often reddish in color and bear narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and borders.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation in Mediterranean climates.
