Plectranthus mzimvubuensis

Plectranthus mzimvubuensis, commonly known as the Mzimvubu mint, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale purple or blue in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Plectranthus mzimvubuensis is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
