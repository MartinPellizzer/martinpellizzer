Peperomia maunakeana

Peperomia maunakeana, commonly known as the Maunakea peperomia, is a flowering plant native to Hawaii.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and small, clustered white flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is adapted to the unique conditions of the Hawaiian highlands. Its foliage is often variegated, with shades of green, silver, and pink, making it a visually striking specimen.

Peperomia maunakeana is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local insect populations.
