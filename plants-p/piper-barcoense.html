Piper barcoense

Piper barcoense, commonly known as the Barcoense pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the rainforests of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit and the presence of small, heart-shaped leaves. Its fruits are small berries that contain aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine.

Piper barcoense is valued for its potential medicinal properties and is often studied for its chemical compounds.
