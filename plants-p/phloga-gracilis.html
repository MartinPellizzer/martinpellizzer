Phloga gracilis

Phloga gracilis, commonly known as the slender phloga, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Phlogaceae and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. Its leaves are narrow and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Phloga gracilis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
