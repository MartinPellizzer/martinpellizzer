Peranema cyatheoides

Peranema cyatheoides, commonly known as the giant fern, is a large tropical fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its massive fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a robust rhizome system that supports its extensive growth. Its leaves are typically bipinnate, with a feathery appearance, and they are arranged in a distinctive, upright pattern.

Peranema cyatheoides thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
