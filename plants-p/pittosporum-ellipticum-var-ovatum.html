Pittosporum ellipticum var. ovatum

Pittosporum ellipticum var. ovatum, commonly known as the ovate pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features dark green, elliptical leaves that are glossy and slightly aromatic. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers in spring, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its hardy nature and visual interest.
