Poinsettia taiwaniana

Poinsettia taiwaniana, commonly known as the Taiwan poinsettia, is a species native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its striking red bracts that resemble petals. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, with small, inconspicuous flowers clustered in the center of the bracts.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
