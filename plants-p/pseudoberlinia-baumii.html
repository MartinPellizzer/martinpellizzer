Pseudoberlinia baumii

Pseudoberlinia baumii, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a leguminous shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces pods containing edible seeds that are rich in protein and used in various culinary traditions. It also has a deep root system that helps prevent soil erosion.

Additionally, it is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage.
