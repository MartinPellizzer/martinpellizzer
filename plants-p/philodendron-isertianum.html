Philodendron isertianum

Philodendron isertianum, commonly known as the Brazilian Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant has a vigorous growth habit and can reach lengths of several meters when supported. Its stems are typically covered in a waxy cuticle, which helps reduce water loss in its native habitat.

Philodendron isertianum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
