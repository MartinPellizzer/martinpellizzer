Pelargonium mutabile

Pelargonium mutabile, commonly known as the variable geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, colorful flowers that can range from red to pink and purple, depending on environmental conditions. The plant has alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Pelargonium mutabile is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
