Potentilla Procumbens Var. Mandonii

Potentilla procumbens var. mandonii is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America. This low-growing shrub is known for its vibrant yellow blooms that appear in late spring and early summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats. Due to its limited distribution and fragile ecosystem, it is considered a species of conservation concern. Its unique beauty and ecological significance make it an important subject for botanical study and environmental preservation.
