Podocarpus steyermarkii

Podocarpus steyermarkii, commonly known as the Philippine podocarp, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Podocarpaceae family and is characterized by its dense, needle-like leaves and cone-bearing structure. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in mountainous areas with high humidity. It plays an important role in the ecological systems of its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.

The wood of Podocarpus steyermarkii is valued for its durability and is used in construction and furniture-making.
