Paphiopedilum globulosum

Paphiopedilum globulosum, commonly known as the globular slipper orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a thick, fleshy rhizome and produces large, showy flowers with a distinctive pouch-like lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white with a red or purple throat.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique and elegant blooms.
