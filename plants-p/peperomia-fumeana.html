Peperomia fumeana

Peperomia fumeana, commonly known as the smoky peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to Central and South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a bluish or purplish tint, giving it a distinctive, almost smoky appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pale green flowers that appear in clusters during the spring or early summer. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and drought tolerance.

Peperomia fumeana thrives in well-draining soil and prefers moderate temperatures with high humidity.
