Pleurothallis lindenii var. pumila

Pleurothallis lindenii var. pumila, commonly known as the miniature pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on tree branches or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features compact, narrow leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It is noted for its compact size and is often cultivated as a miniature orchid for its ornamental value.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is relatively rare in the wild and is often sought after by orchid enthusiasts.
