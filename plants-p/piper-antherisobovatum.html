Piper antherisobovatum

Piper antherisobovatum, commonly known as the warty sanderling, is a tropical climbing vine native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in catkins. Its fruit is a small, three-lobed berry that ripens to a dark purple color.

This species is often found in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental foliage.
