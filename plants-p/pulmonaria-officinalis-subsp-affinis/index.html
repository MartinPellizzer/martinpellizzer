Pulmonaria Officinalis Subsp. Affinis

Pulmonaria officinalis subsp. affinis is a lesser-known variant of the lungwort plant, known for its striking floral displays. This subspecies thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodland areas across Europe. Its foliage features distinctive purple and green markings that add visual interest to the landscape. While similar to the common lungwort, it exhibits subtle differences in leaf shape and flower color. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in horticultural circles.
