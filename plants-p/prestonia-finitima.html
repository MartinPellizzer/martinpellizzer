Prestonia finitima

Prestonia finitima, commonly known as the Trinidad nightshade, is a species of flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, tropical environments. The plant produces small, showy flowers that are usually white or pale purple, which bloom at night. Its leaves are simple and ovate, with a smooth texture.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.
