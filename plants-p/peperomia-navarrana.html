Peperomia navarrana

Peperomia navarrana, commonly known as Navarre peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Mexico.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green or red. The plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light. It is often grown as a decorative plant for its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.

Peperomia navarrana is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments.
