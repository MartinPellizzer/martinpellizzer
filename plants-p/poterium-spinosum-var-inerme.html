Poterium spinosum var. inerme

Poterium spinosum var. inerme, commonly known as the smooth spiny holly, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible but often used more for ornamental purposes. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a hedge.

Its spiny stems and evergreen foliage make it a distinctive addition to botanical collections.
