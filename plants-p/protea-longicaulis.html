Protea longicaulis

Protea longicaulis, commonly known as the long-stemmed protea, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that resemble a cluster of small flowers. The plant features narrow, needle-like leaves that are typically covered in a fine, silvery-white bloom. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires a sunny to partially shaded environment.

Protea longicaulis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a symbol of resilience in its natural habitat.
