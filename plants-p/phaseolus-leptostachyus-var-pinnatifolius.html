Phaseolus leptostachyus var. pinnatifolius

Phaseolus leptostachyus var. pinnatifolius, commonly known as the scarlet runner bean, is a climbing legume native to the Americas.

It features compound leaves with pinnate arrangements, typically with 7 to 15 leaflets. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are often pink or red in color. Its fruit is a cylindrical pod containing several edible, kidney-shaped seeds.

This variety is valued for both its ornamental appeal and culinary use in various cuisines.
