Pruskortizia macrocarpa

Pruskortizia macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-fruited pruskortizia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces large, fleshy fruits that are an important food source for local wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Pruskortizia macrocarpa is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability.
