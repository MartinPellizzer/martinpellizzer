Potentilla Glandulosa Var. Intermedia

Common cinquefoil, scientifically known as Potentilla glandulosa var. intermedia, is a hardy perennial plant native to North America. It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and rugged, sprawling growth habit. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and wildflower meadows. Its ability to tolerate harsh conditions has made it a symbol of resilience in native plant communities. Despite its tough appearance, it plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and wildlife.
