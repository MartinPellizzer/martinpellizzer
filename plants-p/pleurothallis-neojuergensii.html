Pleurothallis neojuergensii

Pleurothallis neojuergensii, commonly known as the neojuergensii orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-white to yellowish flowers arranged in a raceme, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
