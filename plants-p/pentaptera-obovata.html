Pentaptera obovata

Pentaptera obovata, commonly known as the broad-leafed pentaptera, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, obovate leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and ornamental gardening.
