Pultenaea canaliculata var. latifolia

Pultenaea canaliculata var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved pultenaea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a textured appearance. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and dry inland regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
