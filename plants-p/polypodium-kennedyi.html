Polypodium kennedyi

Polypodium kennedyi, commonly known as Kennedy's holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is native to the southeastern United States, particularly found in Florida and Georgia. The plant has feather-like fronds that are typically divided into small, lance-shaped segments. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and along stream banks.

Its rhizome is thick and creeping, allowing it to spread and form dense colonies.
