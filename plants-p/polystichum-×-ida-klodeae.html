Polystichum × ida-klodeae

Polystichum × ida-klodeae, commonly known as the Ida-Klode fern, is a hybrid species belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its evergreen, leathery fronds that are typically dark green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces spore-bearing sorus clusters on the underside of its fronds, typical of ferns. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
