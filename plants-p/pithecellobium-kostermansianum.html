Pithecellobium kostermansianum

Pithecellobium kostermansianum, commonly known as the Kostermans' evergreen acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pod-like fruits that contain edible seeds. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry to semi-arid environments. It produces yellow flowers and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural studies.
