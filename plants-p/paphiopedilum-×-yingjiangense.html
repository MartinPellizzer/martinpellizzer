Paphiopedilum × yingjiangense

Paphiopedilum × yingjiangense, commonly known as the Yingjiang orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often strikingly colored flowers with a lip that is distinctly shaped. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its botanical features include long, strap-like leaves and a complex, spurred lip that is a key identifier.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its beauty in horticulture.
