Podolobium berberifolium

Podolobium berberifolium, commonly known as the yellow-flowered legume, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are simple and alternate, often with a slight pubescence.

The plant is adapted to arid conditions, making it resilient in dry environments.
