Praxelis

Praxelis, commonly known as the blue morning glory, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and twining stems that climb or trail along the ground. Praxelis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its rapid growth and ability to thrive in various soil types make it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
