Poa soderstromii

Poa soderstromii, commonly known as Soderstrom's bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of central Asia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, blue-green leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
