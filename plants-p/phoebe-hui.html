Phoebe hui

Phoebe hui, commonly known as the Chinese phoebe, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado. The tree is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by dark blue berries.

Phoebe hui is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
