Panicum viride subsp. italicum

Panicum viride subsp.

italicum, commonly known as Italian green panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Europe. It is characterized by its fine, grass-like foliage and produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for ecological restoration and ornamental gardening.
