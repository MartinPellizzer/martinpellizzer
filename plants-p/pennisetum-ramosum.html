Pennisetum ramosum

Pennisetum ramosum, commonly known as fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and its ability to form compact clumps. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces fluffy, feathery seed heads that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Pennisetum ramosum is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract birds and small animals.
