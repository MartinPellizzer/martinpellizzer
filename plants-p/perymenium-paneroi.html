Perymenium paneroi

Perymenium paneroi, commonly known as the tropical orchid, is a species native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a flowering plant characterized by its striking, showy blooms that typically appear in shades of yellow and orange. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its complex, intricate floral structure. Its leaves are usually broad and elliptical, with a waxy surface that helps in water retention.

Perymenium paneroi thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
