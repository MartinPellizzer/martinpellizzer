Pentas flammea

Pentas flammea, commonly known as the flame pentas, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, star-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant produces small, green, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Pentas flammea is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
