Pararistolochia tenuicauda

Pararistolochia tenuicauda, commonly known as the slender tailpipe vine, is a climbing plant belonging to the Aristolochiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, including India, China, and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its distinctive, pipe-shaped flowers that are often greenish or brownish in color. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a rough texture.

Due to its toxic properties, it is not recommended for cultivation in home gardens.
