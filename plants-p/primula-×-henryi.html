Primula × henryi

Primula × henryi, commonly known as the Chinese primrose, is a hybrid plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its attractive, bell-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–30 cm and prefers cool, moist environments with partial shade. Its leaves are oval and slightly hairy, and it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens.

Primula × henryi is a member of the Primulaceae family and is well-suited for rock gardens and woodland settings.
