Pleurothallis brenesii

Pleurothallis brenesii, commonly known as the Brenes's pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its flowers are usually white or pale green, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insect species.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
