Piper allatum

Piper allatum, commonly known as golden piper or yellow pepper plant, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into elongated, yellow berries.

The berries are often used in traditional medicine and have a mild, peppery flavor.
