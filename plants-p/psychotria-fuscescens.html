Psychotria fuscescens

Psychotria fuscescens, commonly known as the dark psychotria, is a species of flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. It produces small, dark blue to black berries that are often used in traditional medicine.
