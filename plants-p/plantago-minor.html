Plantago minor

Plantago minor, commonly known as lesser plantain, is a flowering plant belonging to the Plantaginaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, disturbed soils such as roadsides and fields. The plant has broad, oval-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. It produces small, greenish flowers arranged in a spike-like inflorescence.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and has been used historically for its healing abilities.
