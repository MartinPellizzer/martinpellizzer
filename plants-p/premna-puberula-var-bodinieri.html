Premna puberula var. bodinieri

Premna puberula var. bodinieri, commonly known as the Vietnamese nettle or Bodinier's premna, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in humid, tropical environments. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a velvety texture. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in inflorescences and are followed by small, spherical fruits.

It is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value or used in traditional medicine.
