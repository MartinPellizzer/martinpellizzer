Petrosedum × nicaeense

Petrosedum × nicaeense, commonly known as the hybrid stonecress, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae and is characterized by its compact rosettes of fleshy, succulent leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine or coastal environments.

Petrosedum × nicaeense is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
