Polypodium tuberculatum

Polypodium tuberculatum, commonly known as the tuberculate huperzia, is a species of fern that belongs to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its evergreen, leathery fronds that are typically dark green and have a rugged, tuberculate texture. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, Africa, and the Americas. It grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices.

Polypodium tuberculatum is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and can thrive in both indoor and outdoor settings with proper care.
