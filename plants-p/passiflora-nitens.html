Passiflora nitens

Passiflora nitens, commonly known as the silver passionflower, is a climbing vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that display a mix of white, blue, and purple hues. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a silvery underside, giving it its common name. Its fruit, which is edible, matures into a yellowish, fleshy berry.

Passiflora nitens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
