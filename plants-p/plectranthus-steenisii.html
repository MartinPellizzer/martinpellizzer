Plectranthus steenisii

Plectranthus steenisii, commonly known as the Natal plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to white. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Plectranthus steenisii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant fragrance.
