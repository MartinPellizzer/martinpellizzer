Pilea angolensis subsp. christiaensenii

Pilea angolensis subsp.

christiaensenii, commonly known as the Christiaensen's pilea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically oval or rounded, with a waxy texture to store water.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

This subspecies is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
