Peperomia llewelynii

Peperomia llewelynii, commonly known as the Llewelyn's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of pink or white, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia llewelynii is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and tolerating a range of indoor conditions.
