Peperomia abyssinica var. stuhlmannii

Peperomia abyssinica var. stuhlmannii, commonly known as the Abyssinian pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Ethiopia.

It features broad, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a slight reddish or purple tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
