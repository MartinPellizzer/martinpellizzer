Pulmonaria angustifolia var. lanceolata

Pulmonaria angustifolia var. lanceolata, commonly known as narrow-leaved lungwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features basal rosettes of lanceolate, hairy leaves that are often tinged with purple. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in early spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its foliage and flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
