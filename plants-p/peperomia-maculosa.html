Peperomia maculosa

Peperomia maculosa, commonly known as the spotted peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with distinctive white or silver spots, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It is well-suited for indoor growing, thriving in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Peperomia maculosa is prized for its ornamental foliage and ability to purify the air.
