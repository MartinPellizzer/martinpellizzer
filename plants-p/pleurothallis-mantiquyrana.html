Pleurothallis mantiquyrana

Pleurothallis mantiquyrana, commonly known as the Mantiqueira orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Brazil.

It is a small epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It is part of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is known for its intricate floral structure.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
