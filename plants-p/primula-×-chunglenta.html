Primula × chunglenta

Primula × chunglenta, commonly known as the hybrid primrose, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a range of colors, including white, yellow, and pink. The plant typically blooms in early spring, producing clusters of flowers on sturdy stems. Its foliage consists of broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down.

Primula × chunglenta is well-suited for rock gardens and shady borders, where it thrives in moist, well-drained soil.
