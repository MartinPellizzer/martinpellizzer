Phytelephas pavonii

Phytelephas pavonii, commonly known as the pink bat palm, is a species of palm native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces striking, purple-pink inflorescences that are both ornamental and attractively colored. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological importance, it is valued in horticulture and conservation efforts.
