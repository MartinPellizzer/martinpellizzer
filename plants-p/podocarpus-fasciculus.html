Podocarpus fasciculus

Podocarpus fasciculus, commonly known as the Chinese podocarp or narrow-leaf podocarp, is a species of coniferous evergreen tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves that are typically arranged in fascicles of three to five. The plant produces small, woody cones that contain seeds, a feature typical of the Podocarpaceae family. It thrives in temperate to subtropical climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its dense foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping and reforestation projects.
