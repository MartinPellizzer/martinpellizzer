Pyrrosia Beddomeana

Pyrrosia beddomeana is a rare and elegant fern native to the mountainous regions of southern India. It thrives in cool, shaded environments and is often found in moist, rocky crevices. This plant is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that display a striking combination of green and silver hues. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
