Peperomia scabiosa

Peperomia scabiosa, commonly known as the rough peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are often covered in a fine, raised texture, giving them a slightly rough appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white or pale purple in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and drought tolerance.

Peperomia scabiosa is valued for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments.
