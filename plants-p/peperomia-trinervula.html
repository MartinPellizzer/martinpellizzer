Peperomia trinervula

Peperomia trinervula, commonly known as the Three-Nerved Peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with a distinctive three-veined pattern, giving it its scientific name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia trinervula is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
