Poa tenerrima

Poa tenerrima, commonly known as the silver hair grass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open areas such as meadows and grasslands. Its slender, tufted growth habit and delicate inflorescence make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Poa tenerrima is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in natural habitats.
