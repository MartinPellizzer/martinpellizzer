Panicum vilvoides

Panicum vilvoides, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and dense, reddish-purple inflorescences that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in open woodlands and disturbed areas. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that becomes more reddish as the plant matures.

Panicum vilvoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in native plant landscapes.
