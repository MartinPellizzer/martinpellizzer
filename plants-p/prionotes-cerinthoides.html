Prionotes cerinthoides

Prionotes cerinthoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved broom.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

Prionotes cerinthoides is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative plant due to its compact size and attractive blooms.
