Pittosporum pullifolium var. globosum

Pittosporum pullifolium var. globosum, commonly known as the globosum pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical growth habit and glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping.
