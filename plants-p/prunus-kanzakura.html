Prunus kanzakura

Prunus kanzakura, commonly known as the Japanese cherry blossom, is a species of flowering shrub or small tree native to Japan.

It is celebrated for its beautiful pink and white flowers that bloom in early spring, creating a breathtaking natural spectacle. The plant belongs to the Rosaceae family and is closely related to other cherry species. Its flowers are typically borne in clusters and are followed by small, dark red fruits.

Prunus kanzakura is often cultivated for ornamental purposes and holds cultural significance in Japan.
