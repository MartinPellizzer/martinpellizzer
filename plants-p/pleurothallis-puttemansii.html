Pleurothallis puttemansii

Pleurothallis puttemansii, commonly known as the Puttemans' pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid tropical forests. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are often white or pale yellow in color. It has a compact growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
