Peperomia roigiana

Peperomia roigiana, commonly known as the Mexican snowplant, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to Mexico.

It features dense, velvety foliage with a unique coloration that ranges from deep green to silvery-white. The leaves are typically broad and ovate, often with a waxy texture that gives them a distinctive appearance. It is a succulent plant, capable of storing water in its thick, fleshy leaves.

While it does not produce showy flowers, its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and decorative purposes.
