Phylica disticha

Phylica disticha, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in summer, followed by bright red berries. It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in dry conditions.

The leaves are also used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
