Pedicularis langsdorffii subsp. langsdorffii

Pedicularis langsdorffii subsp.

langsdorffii, commonly known as the Langsdorff's lousewort, is a member of the Orobanchaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, spurred flowers. It is a hemiparasitic plant, meaning it partially relies on host plants for nutrients through its root system.

This subspecies is native to parts of Asia, including regions of Russia and the Himalayas.
