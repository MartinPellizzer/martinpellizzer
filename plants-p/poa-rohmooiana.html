Poa rohmooiana

Poa rohmooiana, commonly known as the Rohmooian grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly found in regions of Argentina and Chile. This perennial grass typically grows in moist, temperate climates and is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks. Its slender blades are usually less than 30 cm in height, with a tufted growth habit.

The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences, which are characteristic of many grass species.
