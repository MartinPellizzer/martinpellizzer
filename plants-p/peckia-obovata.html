Peckia obovata

Peckia obovata, commonly known as the broad-ovate peckia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate-shaped leaves and daisy-like yellow flowers. Native to the southeastern United States, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 feet and has a clumping growth habit.
