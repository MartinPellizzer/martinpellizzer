Paepalanthus plantagineus f. luxurians

Paepalanthus plantagineus f. luxurians, commonly known as the luxuriant leaf papyrus, is a variant of the papyrus plant characterized by its dense, lush foliage.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental qualities and its ability to provide habitat for small aquatic organisms.
