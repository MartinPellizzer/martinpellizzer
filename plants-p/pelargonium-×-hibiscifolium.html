Pelargonium × hibiscifolium

Pelargonium × hibiscifolium, commonly known as the hibiscifolium geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that resemble those of hibiscus, giving it its common name. The plant produces showy, single or double flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. Native to South Africa, it thrives in warm, sunny climates and well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and containers.
