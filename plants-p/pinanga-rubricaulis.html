Pinanga rubricaulis

Pinanga rubricaulis, commonly known as the red-stemmed bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araucariaceae.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like Indonesia and Malaysia. Pinanga rubricaulis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a decorative plant.
