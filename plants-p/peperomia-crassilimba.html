Peperomia crassilimba

Peperomia crassilimba, commonly known as the broad-leaf peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are often surrounded by bracts. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Peperomia crassilimba is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
