Poterium eriocarpum

Poterium eriocarpum, commonly known as the woolly burr.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its hairy, woody stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces spherical, spiny fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. Its foliage is typically grayish-green, with dense pubescence that provides a distinctive appearance.

Poterium eriocarpum thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to dry conditions.
