Pisoniella arborescens var. arborescens

Pisoniella arborescens var. arborescens, commonly known as the coastal morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a perennial vine that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of purple and pink. The plant thrives in sandy, coastal soils and is often found in dune environments. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a rough texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize coastal soils.
