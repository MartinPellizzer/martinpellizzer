Prasophyllum hartii var. parviflorum

Prasophyllum hartii var. parviflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered leek orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in open habitats such as heathlands and woodlands. It is a low-growing species, usually reaching heights of less than 30 centimeters, and is often found in coastal or inland regions of southern Australia.
