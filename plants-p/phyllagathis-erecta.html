Phyllagathis erecta

Phyllagathis erecta, commonly known as the upright phyllagathis, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a glossy surface.
