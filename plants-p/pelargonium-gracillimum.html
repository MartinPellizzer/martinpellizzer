Pelargonium gracillimum

Pelargonium gracillimum, commonly known as the slender geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has slender, upright stems and narrow, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Pelargonium gracillimum is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
