Peperomia amplexicaulis var. magnoliifolia

Peperomia amplexicaulis var. magnoliifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy, and often variegated leaves that are broad and heart-shaped, with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green or red, which are typically borne on short spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
