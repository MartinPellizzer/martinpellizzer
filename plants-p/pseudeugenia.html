Pseudeugenia

Pseudeugenia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed myrtle, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to regions of Australia and New Zealand, where it thrives in temperate climates. The plants typically have small, aromatic leaves and produce clusters of white or pink flowers. They are often found in coastal or forested areas, adapting well to various soil types.
