Papuanthes albertisii

Papuanthes albertisii, commonly known as the Albertis' piper, is a flowering plant native to the island of New Guinea.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, opposite leaves. Its flowers are unisexual, with male and female flowers occurring on separate plants.

The fruit is a small, fleshy capsule that contains several seeds.
