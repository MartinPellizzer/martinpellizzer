Peperomia blanda var. floribunda

Peperomia blanda var. floribunda, commonly known as the silver lace peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with a distinctive silver-green coloration and a slightly wavy margin. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its low maintenance and ornamental foliage make it a versatile addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
