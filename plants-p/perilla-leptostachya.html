Perilla leptostachya

Perilla leptostachya, commonly known as Chinese basil or Shiso, is a herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink to purple in color. Its leaves are often used in culinary and medicinal applications due to their aromatic properties.

Perilla leptostachya thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its flavorful foliage and ornamental value.
