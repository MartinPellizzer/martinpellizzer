Phagnalon × domingoi

Phagnalon × domingoi, commonly known as the hybrid thistle, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pink flowering heads that resemble thistles. This species is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in dry, rocky soils. It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and waste areas.

The plant's morphology reflects its adaptation to arid environments, with reduced leaf surface area and deep root systems.
