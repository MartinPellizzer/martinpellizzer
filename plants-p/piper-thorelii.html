Piper thorelii

Piper thorelii, commonly known as the African pepper or kola nut plant, is a flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical Africa and is characterized by its climbing habit and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in spikes, and it is known for its aromatic fruits. The fruit is a small, round, yellowish berry that contains a single seed.

It is often used in traditional medicine and for its aromatic properties.
