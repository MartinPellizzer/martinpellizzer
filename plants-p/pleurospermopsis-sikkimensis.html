Pleurospermopsis sikkimensis

Pleurospermopsis sikkimensis, commonly known as the Sikkim pleurosperm, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky habitats at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough texture, while its flowers are arranged in a composite head.
