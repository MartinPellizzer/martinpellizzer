Physodeira bicolor

Physodeira bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its strikingly contrasting colors, with green and white or pink streaks on its leaves and flowers. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique coloration and delicate appearance make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
