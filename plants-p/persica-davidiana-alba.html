Persica davidiana alba

Persica davidiana alba, commonly known as the white-flowered David plum.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China, characterized by its ornamental white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. Its fruit is a small, yellowish drupe that ripens in late summer.

It is valued in horticulture for its showy flowers and adaptability to various soil conditions.
