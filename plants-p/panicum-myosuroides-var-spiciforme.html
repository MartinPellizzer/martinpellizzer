Panicum myosuroides var. spiciforme

Panicum myosuroides var. spiciforme, commonly known as spiciform panicgrass, is a variant of panicgrass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a perennial grass characterized by its tall, slender stalks and dense, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, thriving in moist, nutrient-rich soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

This variant is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support biodiversity.
