Pleioblastus kiusianus

Pleioblastus kiusianus, commonly known as the Kiusi bamboo, is a species native to Japan and parts of eastern Asia.

It is a clumping bamboo that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters, with slender, gracefully arching stems. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually a dark green color, providing a lush, ornamental appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for both garden and container cultivation.

Its slow growth rate and elegant form make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
