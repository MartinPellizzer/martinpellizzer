Philodendron rex

Philodendron rex, commonly known as the Heartleaf Philodendron, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. Philodendron rex thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
