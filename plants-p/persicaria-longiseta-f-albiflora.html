Persicaria longiseta f. albiflora

Persicaria longiseta f. albiflora, commonly known as the whiteflower smartweed, is a flowering plant in the family Polygonaceae.

It is a herbaceous perennial that features slender, erect stems and alternate, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

This variety is distinguished by its pale floral color, contrasting with the typical red or pink flowers of the species.
