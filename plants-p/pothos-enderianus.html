Pothos enderianus

Pothos enderianus, commonly known as the golden pothos, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically bright yellow to green in color. The plant thrives in humid environments and requires indirect sunlight to flourish. Its rapid growth rate makes it an excellent choice for indoor hanging baskets or as a trailing plant.

Pothos enderianus is also known for its ability to purify air and is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular houseplant.
