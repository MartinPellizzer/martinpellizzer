Pelargonium × eratinum

Pelargonium × eratinum, commonly known as the scarlet geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, double flowers that range in color from red to pink, often with a contrasting central eye. The plant typically grows as an upright herb with rounded, lobed leaves that are velvety to the touch. Native to South Africa, it thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming season, which extends from spring to autumn.
