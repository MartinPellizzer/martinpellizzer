Psorospermum umbellulatum

Psorospermum umbellulatum, commonly known as the umbrella tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Psorospermum umbellulatum is also valued for its ability to tolerate shade and poor soil conditions.
