Philotheca buxifolia subsp. buxifolia

Philotheca buxifolia subsp.

buxifolia, commonly known as the waxleaf philotheca, is a small evergreen shrub native to southeastern Australia. It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a waxy texture.

The plant produces small, pale green flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value and compact growth habit, it is often used in gardens and as a container plant.
