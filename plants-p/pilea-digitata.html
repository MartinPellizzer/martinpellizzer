Pilea digitata

Pilea digitata, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers in the spring, followed by red berries that contain seeds. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea digitata is known for its easy care and ability to propagate through stem cuttings.
