Pleurothallis klotzschiana

Pleurothallis klotzschiana, commonly known as the Klotzschian orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil to flourish. It is often grown as a terrestrial or epiphytic orchid, depending on its natural habitat.

Due to its unique beauty and rarity, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
