Pothos kerrii

Pothos kerrii, commonly known as the Satin Pothos, is a tropical evergreen vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically light to dark green with a slightly textured surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by berries that mature to a bright orange-red color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and is known for its rapid growth and ability to climb or trail.

Pothos kerrii is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and air-purifying qualities.
