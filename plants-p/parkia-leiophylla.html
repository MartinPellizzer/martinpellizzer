Parkia leiophylla

Parkia leiophylla, commonly known as the smooth-leaved legume, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pod-like fruits. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers that are often clustered in racemes. Its seeds are enclosed in slender, curved pods and are dispersed by wind.

Parkia leiophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
