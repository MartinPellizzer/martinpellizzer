Peperomia conturbans

Peperomia conturbans, commonly known as the zigzag peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features upright, branching stems covered in dense, dark green, waxy leaves that have a distinctive zigzag pattern along their edges. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that are typically hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its unique leaf shape and ease of care, it is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
