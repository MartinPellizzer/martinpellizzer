Pomatocalpa falciforme

Pomatocalpa falciforme, commonly known as the sickle-shaped orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and elongated, sickle-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically pale green to yellowish in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its unique morphology and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
