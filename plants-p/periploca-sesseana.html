Periploca sesseana

Periploca sesseana, commonly known as the African moonflower, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, nocturnal flowers that open at night. Periploca sesseana is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
