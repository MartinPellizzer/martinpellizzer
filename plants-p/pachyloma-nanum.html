Pachyloma nanum

Pachyloma nanum, commonly known as the miniature pachyloma, is a small, succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features compact, rounded stems that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hair. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native arid environment.

Pachyloma nanum is typically grown as a decorative succulent due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
