Piper tenue

Piper tenue, commonly known as the slender pepper vine, is a climbing herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant features slender, woody stems with opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. Its small, greenish-white flowers are borne in clusters, and the fruit is a three-lobed, fleshy capsule.

Piper tenue is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in some indigenous cultures.
