Perezia dugesii var. typica

Perezia dugesii var. typica, commonly known as the Duges' perezia, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native habitat.
