Pittosporum pentandrum var. formosanum

Pittosporum pentandrum var. formosanum, commonly known as the Formosan pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to Taiwan.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers in clusters, which are typically followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
