Phebalium pauciflorum

Phebalium pauciflorum, commonly known as the few-flowered phebalium, is a small evergreen shrub native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the family Proteaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers in rounded clusters during the spring and summer seasons. Its leaves are narrow, leathery, and arranged alternately along the stems.

Phebalium pauciflorum is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in heathland and coastal scrub environments.
