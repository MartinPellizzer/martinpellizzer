Pilea rotundata

Pilea rotundata, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features glossy, round, and slightly waxy leaves that are typically a deep green, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters, followed by spherical, red fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea rotundata is known for its ease of care and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments.
