Phyllanthus cyrtophylloides

Phyllanthus cyrtophylloides, commonly known as the African spurge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical Africa and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, clustered flowers. Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and pharmacological research.
