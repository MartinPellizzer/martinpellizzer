Pleurothallis tenuiflora

Pleurothallis tenuiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate floral structure and specialized pollination mechanisms.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often found in cloud forests and is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
