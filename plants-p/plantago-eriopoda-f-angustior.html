Plantago eriopoda f. angustior

Plantago eriopoda f. angustior, commonly known as narrow-leaved plantain, is a variant of the common plantain species.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, which are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and along stream banks.

This variety is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
