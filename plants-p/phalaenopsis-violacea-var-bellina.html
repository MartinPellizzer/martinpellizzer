Phalaenopsis violacea var. bellina

Phalaenopsis violacea var. bellina, commonly known as the Bellina orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, long-lasting flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink and violet. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous blooms, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, often with a waxy texture, and it thrives in bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
