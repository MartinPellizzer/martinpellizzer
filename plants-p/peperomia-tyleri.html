Peperomia tyleri

Peperomia tyleri, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery or white powdery coating, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of green or red, which are followed by reddish-brown berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia tyleri is relatively low maintenance and drought-tolerant, ideal for beginners.
