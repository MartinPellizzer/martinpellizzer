Psychanthus biligulatus

Psychanthus biligulatus, commonly known as the two-colored psychanthus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bi-colored leaves that display a mix of green and yellow hues. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Psychanthus biligulatus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and aesthetic appeal.
