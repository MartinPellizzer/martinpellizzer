Phebalium buckinghamii

Phebalium buckinghamii, commonly known as the pink wattle, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features small, bright pink flowers that bloom in spring, attracting native pollinators. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice in gardens and landscaping.
