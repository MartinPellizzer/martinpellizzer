Pilea brachypila

Pilea brachypila, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with silver and green markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact size and ease of care make it an ideal plant for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
