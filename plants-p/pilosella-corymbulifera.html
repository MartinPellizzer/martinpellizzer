Pilosella corymbulifera

Pilosella corymbulifera, commonly known as the clustered hairvetch, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Pilosella corymbulifera is often found in grassy areas and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.
