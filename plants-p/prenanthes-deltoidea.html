Prenanthes deltoidea

Prenanthes deltoidea, commonly known as arrowhead or arrowleaf, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically arrowhead-shaped, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable plant for wetland restoration and ecological studies.
