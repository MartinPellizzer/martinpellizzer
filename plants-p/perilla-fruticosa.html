Perilla fruticosa

Perilla fruticosa, commonly known as shiso, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a perennial shrub that can grow up to 1.5 meters tall, featuring lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive aromatic scent. The plant produces small, pale purple flowers that develop into oval, dark purple berries. Its leaves are often used in culinary applications, while the seeds are valued for their essential oils.

Perilla fruticosa thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for both its ornamental and practical uses.
