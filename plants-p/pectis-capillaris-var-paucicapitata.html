Pectis capillaris var. paucicapitata

Pectis capillaris var. paucicapitata, commonly known as the sparse-headed pectis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in compact clusters. It is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
