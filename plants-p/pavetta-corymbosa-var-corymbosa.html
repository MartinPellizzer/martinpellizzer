Pavetta corymbosa var. corymbosa

Pavetta corymbosa var. corymbosa, commonly known as the red-flowered pavetta, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and southern Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree with opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, showy red or pink flowers, which are borne in corymbs, giving it its species name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in moist forest understories.

The plant has a woody stem and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and ornamental value.
