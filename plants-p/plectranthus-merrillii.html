Plectranthus merrillii

Plectranthus merrillii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and produces spikes of purple or white flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
