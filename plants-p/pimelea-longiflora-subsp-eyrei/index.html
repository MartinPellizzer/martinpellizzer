Pimelea Longiflora Subsp. Eyrei

Long-flowered pimelea, scientifically known as Pimelea longiflora subsp. eyrei, is a striking native plant found in the rugged landscapes of south-western Australia. With its distinctive elongated flowers and vibrant yellow hue, it stands out among the diverse flora of the region. This subspecies is particularly adapted to the dry, nutrient-poor soils of its habitat, showcasing remarkable resilience. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for native gardens and conservation efforts. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively rare and underappreciated species in the wild.
