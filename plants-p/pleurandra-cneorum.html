Pleurandra cneorum

Pleurandra cneorum, commonly known as the Cneorum pleurandra, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, narrow leaves that are typically gray-green in color, with a fuzzy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
