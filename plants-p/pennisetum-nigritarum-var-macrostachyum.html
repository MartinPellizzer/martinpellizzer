Pennisetum nigritarum var. macrostachyum

Pennisetum nigritarum var. macrostachyum, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that produce dense, fluffy plumes of purple to pinkish flowers, which are highly ornamental. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adding a striking visual contrast. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its seasonal display of colorful inflorescences makes it a favorite among gardeners for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
