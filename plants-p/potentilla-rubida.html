Potentilla rubida

Potentilla rubida, commonly known as scarlet sandspurry, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches heights of 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features bright red or orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are small, oval-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Potentilla rubida thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open habitats such as prairies and disturbed areas.
