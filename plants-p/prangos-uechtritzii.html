Prangos uechtritzii

Prangos uechtritzii, commonly known as the Echtritz's thistle, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Prangos uechtritzii is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been cultivated in some horticultural collections.
