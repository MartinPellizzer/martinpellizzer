Prunus acuminata f. microbotrys

Prunus acuminata f. microbotrys, commonly known as the dwarf cherry or miniature cherry, is a compact, ornamental shrub native to China.

It features small, rounded leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy underneath. The plant produces delicate, pinkish-white flowers in early spring, which are followed by small, red berries. It is valued for its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal in gardens.

This form is a naturally occurring variant of Prunus acuminata, known for its unique size and floral characteristics.
