Poa pratensis var. planiculmis

Poa pratensis var. planiculmis, commonly known as the flat-leaf meadow grass, is a variant of the common bentgrass.

It is characterized by its flat, narrow leaves and compact growth habit, making it suitable for turfgrass applications. This variety is typically found in temperate regions, thriving in well-drained soils. It has a fine texture and is often used in lawns and sports fields due to its durability.

The plant is also valued for its ability to withstand moderate foot traffic and recover quickly from damage.
