Philotria minor

Philotria minor, commonly known as the lesser philotria, is a small herbaceous plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Philotria minor is characterized by its delicate structure and is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
