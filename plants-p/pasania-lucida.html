Pasania lucida

Pasania lucida, commonly known as the shining pasania, is a small evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or swampy areas. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, often with a slight waxy coating. Its small, white flowers bloom in clusters during the spring, adding ornamental value to the landscape.

Pasania lucida is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
