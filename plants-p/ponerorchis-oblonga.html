Ponerorchis oblonga

Ponerorchis oblonga, commonly known as the broadleaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, grass-like foliage and produces striking, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and meadows. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a prominent lip that is often darker in color.

Ponerorchis oblonga is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
