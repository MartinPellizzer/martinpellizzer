Poterium ancistroides var. tournefortianum

Poterium ancistroides var. tournefortianum, commonly known as the Tournefortianum poinsettia, is a striking ornamental plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy bracts that resemble flowers, which are actually modified leaves. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in warm, sunny conditions. Its growth habit is typically compact, making it suitable for container gardening or as a potted plant.
