Peperomia debilipes var. dimorpha

Peperomia debilipes var. dimorpha, commonly known as the dwarf peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which are borne on short, erect spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
