Polygala eriocephala

Polygala eriocephala, commonly known as the woolly milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, sandy or rocky soils. The plant features slender, upright stems with small, clustered leaves. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, arranged in dense, globular inflorescences.

It is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
