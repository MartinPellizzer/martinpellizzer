Pistacia nemausensis

Pistacia nemausensis, commonly known as the Nemausus pistachio, is a species of flowering plant in the Anacardiaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its small, leathery leaves and yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are similar to true pistachios but are not widely cultivated for commercial use.
