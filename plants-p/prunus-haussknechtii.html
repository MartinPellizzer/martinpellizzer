Prunus haussknechtii

Prunus haussknechtii, commonly known as the Sierra Madre plum, is a small deciduous tree native to the high elevations of the Sierra Madre Occidental in Mexico.

It typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, temperate climates. The plant produces small, white flowers in early spring, followed by edible, sweet-tart plum fruits. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a serrated margin.

Prunus haussknechtii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance and fruit.
