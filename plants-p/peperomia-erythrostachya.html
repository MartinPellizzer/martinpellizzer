Peperomia erythrostachya

Peperomia erythrostachya, commonly known as the red-stemmed peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly textured surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of red or pink, which appear during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia erythrostachya is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
