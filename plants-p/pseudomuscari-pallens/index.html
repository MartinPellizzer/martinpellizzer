Pseudomuscari Pallens

Pseudomuscari pallens is a striking member of the Amaryllidaceae family known for its delicate, bell-shaped flowers. This plant is native to southern Europe and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. Its pale blue to white blooms appear in late spring, adding a subtle elegance to gardens. It is often mistaken for true muscari due to its similar appearance. Pseudomuscari pallens is a favorite among gardeners seeking both beauty and low maintenance.
