Paphiopedilum lawrenceanum var. hyeanum

Paphiopedilum lawrenceanum var. hyeanum, commonly known as the Hyeanum slipper orchid, is a rare and highly sought-after species native to the mountainous regions of Vietnam.

It is characterized by its unique, intricate flower structure with a pouch-like lip that resembles a slipper, a defining feature of the Paphiopedilum genus. The plant typically produces pale green to yellowish flowers with a contrasting red or pink throat, and its foliage consists of broad, dark green leaves. It thrives in cool, humid environments with partial shade and requires well-draining, acidic soil for optimal growth.

Due to its rarity and beauty, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
