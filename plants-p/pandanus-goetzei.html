Pandanus goetzei

Pandanus goetzei, commonly known as the giant pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pandanaceae.

It is native to the Pacific Islands, including parts of Southeast Asia and Australia. The plant is characterized by its tall, tree-like structure with long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. Its inflorescence consists of a branched cluster of flowers that are typically yellow or green in color.

Pandanus goetzei plays an important ecological role in its native habitats, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
