Pollinia imberbis

Pollinia imberbis, commonly known as the hairless pollinia orchid, is a unique orchid species characterized by its specialized reproductive structures.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to certain regions of Southeast Asia. The plant produces pollinia, which are masses of pollen that are adapted for specific pollination mechanisms. These pollinia are typically found in the orchid's flower structure and are often transferred by specific insect pollinators.
