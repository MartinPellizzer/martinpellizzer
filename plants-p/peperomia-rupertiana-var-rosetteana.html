Peperomia rupertiana var. rosetteana

Peperomia rupertiana var. rosetteana, commonly known as the Rosette Peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, rosette-like arrangement of fleshy, waxy leaves that are typically green with reddish or purple undersides. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, which appear on short, erect stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage and ease of care make it a desirable addition to modern plant collections.
