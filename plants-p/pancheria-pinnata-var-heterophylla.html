Pancheria pinnata var. heterophylla

Pancheria pinnata var. heterophylla, commonly known as the variable pinnate pancheria, is a tropical plant characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology.

It features compound leaves with alternating leaflets, which vary in shape and size across different parts of the plant, giving it the "heterophylla" designation. The leaves are typically broad and lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface depending on the environment. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for studies in plant morphology and adaptation.
