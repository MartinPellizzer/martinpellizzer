Pinalia szetschuanica

Pinalia szetschuanica, commonly known as the Sichuan pinyon, is a rare gymnosperm native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It belongs to the Pinaceae family, which includes pines, spruces, and firs, and is characterized by its coniferous structure. The plant produces small, scale-like leaves and cone-bearing structures, typical of its family. It thrives in cool, moist environments at high elevations, often found in forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
