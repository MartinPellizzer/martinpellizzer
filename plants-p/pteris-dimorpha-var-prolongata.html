Pteris dimorpha var. prolongata

Pteris dimorpha var. prolongata, commonly known as the elongated water fern, is a variant of the common water fern, characterized by its distinctive fronds.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features bipinnate fronds with elongated pinnae, giving it a unique appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near water bodies.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and aquatic habitats.
