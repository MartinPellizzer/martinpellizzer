Polanisia dodecandra var. trachysperma

Polanisia dodecandra var. trachysperma, commonly known as the African daisies, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, daisy-like yellow flowers with a central purple disc. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, oval-shaped seeds that are dispersed by wind, contributing to its widespread distribution.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
