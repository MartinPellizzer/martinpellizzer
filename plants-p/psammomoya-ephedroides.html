Psammomoya ephedroides

Psammomoya ephedroides, commonly known as the desert ephedra, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of China.

It belongs to the Ephedraceae family and is characterized by its jointed stems and small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers and is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments. It has a long history of traditional use in herbal medicine.

Its botanical features make it well-suited for ecological restoration in degraded landscapes.
