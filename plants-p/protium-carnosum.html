Protium carnosum

Protium carnosum, commonly known as the African snowball, is a small evergreen tree native to eastern and southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and dense clusters of white flowers that resemble snowballs. The plant produces small, round, yellowish fruits that contain a single seed. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland forest areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
