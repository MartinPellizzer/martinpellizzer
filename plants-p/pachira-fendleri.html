Pachira fendleri

Pachira fendleri, commonly known as the Mexican money plant, is a species of flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its thick, woody stems and glossy, heart-shaped leaves. Pachira fendleri is also noted for its air-purifying properties and ease of care.
