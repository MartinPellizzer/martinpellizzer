Pyrus hookeriana

Pyrus hookeriana, commonly known as Hooker's pear, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Chile. The plant is characterized by its deciduous leaves, which are alternate and ovate in shape. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring.
