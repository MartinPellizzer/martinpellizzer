Pyrus iwatensis

Pyrus iwatensis, commonly known as the Iwat Islands pear, is a rare species native to the Philippines.

It is a deciduous tree belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its smooth, gray bark and alternate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in early spring, followed by edible, sweet fruits that are typically harvested in late summer.
