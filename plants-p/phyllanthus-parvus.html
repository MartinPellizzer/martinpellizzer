Phyllanthus parvus

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of tiny yellowish-green flowers. It often grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks.
