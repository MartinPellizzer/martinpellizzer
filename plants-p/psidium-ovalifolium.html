Psidium ovalifolium

Psidium ovalifolium, commonly known as the oval-leaf guava, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its oval-shaped leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are similar in appearance to guavas but have a distinct flavor. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Psidium ovalifolium is also valued for its ornamental qualities and potential medicinal uses.
