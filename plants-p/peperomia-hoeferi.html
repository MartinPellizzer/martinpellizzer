Peperomia hoeferi

Peperomia hoeferi, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to South America.

It features thick, waxy, silver-green leaves that are often marked with dark veins, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia hoeferi is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive foliage.
