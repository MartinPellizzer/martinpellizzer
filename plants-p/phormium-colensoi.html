Phormium colensoi

Phormium colensoi, commonly known as the Colenso's flax, is a striking evergreen plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sword-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored with red, orange, or yellow margins. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with showy, violet to purple flowers that bloom in summer. It is well adapted to coastal environments and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil.

Phormium colensoi is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience.
