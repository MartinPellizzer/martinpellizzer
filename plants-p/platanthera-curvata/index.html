Platanthera Curvata

Platanthera curvata is a rare and delicate orchid native to specific regions of Europe and Asia. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wet meadows. This plant is known for its slender, pale green stems and clusters of small, fragrant white or greenish flowers. Due to habitat loss and climate change, it faces significant conservation challenges. Its unique beauty and ecological importance make it a subject of both scientific study and botanical admiration.
