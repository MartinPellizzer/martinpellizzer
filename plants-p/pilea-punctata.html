Pilea punctata

Pilea punctata, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rounded, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a silvery or white underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in late spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular indoor plant.

Pilea punctata is noted for its ease of care and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments.
