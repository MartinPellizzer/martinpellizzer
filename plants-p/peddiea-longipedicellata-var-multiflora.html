Peddiea longipedicellata var. multiflora

Peddiea longipedicellata var. multiflora, commonly known as the multiflora peddiea, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, pedicellate inflorescences, which give it its scientific name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are small and clustered, producing a delicate, aromatic scent that attracts pollinators.
