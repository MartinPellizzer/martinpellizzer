Potentilla sordida proles decipiens

Potentilla sordida proles decipiens, commonly known as the deceptive gloomy potentilla, is a low-growing shrub native to the alpine regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, narrow leaves and small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments. Its scientific name reflects its somber appearance and the misleading nature of its flowers, which are not particularly showy.

It is often found in mountainous regions where it contributes to the alpine flora diversity.
