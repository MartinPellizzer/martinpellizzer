Peperomia flavescens var. flavescens

Peperomia flavescens var. flavescens, commonly known as the golden peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically bright green with a yellowish or golden hue on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a desirable ornamental plant.
