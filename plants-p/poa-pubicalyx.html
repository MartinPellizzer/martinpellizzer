Poa pubicalyx

Poa pubicalyx, commonly known as the slender meadow grass, is a species of grass native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and slender, hairless leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and sandy soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences with tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Poa pubicalyx is often found in coastal and alpine regions, where it contributes to the ecological stability of these environments.
