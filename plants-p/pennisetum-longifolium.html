Pennisetum longifolium

Pennisetum longifolium, commonly known as giant feather grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves that can reach lengths of up to 3 meters. The plant produces panicles of small, fluffy flowers that are often white or pale green, giving it a feathery appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for ornamental gardens and landscaping.

Pennisetum longifolium is also valued for its ability to attract birds and insects, contributing to ecological diversity.
