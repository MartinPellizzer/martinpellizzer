Protea globularia

Protea globularia, commonly known as the globular protea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its striking, spherical inflorescence. The plant features dense clusters of small, colorful flowers that are typically pink, red, or white. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Protea globularia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
