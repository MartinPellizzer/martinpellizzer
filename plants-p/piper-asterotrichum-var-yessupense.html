Piper asterotrichum var. yessupense

Piper asterotrichum var. yessupense, commonly known as the Yessup's pepper vine, is a climbing plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that develop into elongated, fleshy fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical rainforests.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
