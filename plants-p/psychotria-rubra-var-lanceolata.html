Psychotria rubra var. lanceolata

Psychotria rubra var. lanceolata, commonly known as the red Psychotria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

The plant belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes coffee and gardenias, and is known for its ornamental value. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
