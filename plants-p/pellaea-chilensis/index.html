Pellaea Chilensis

Pellaea chilensis is a unique fern native to the coastal regions of southern Chile. It thrives in rocky and sandy soils where few other plants can survive. This species is known for its compact growth and delicate, lance-shaped leaves. Its ability to endure harsh environmental conditions makes it a resilient plant. It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardiness.
