Primulina qintangensis

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as mountainous regions. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered with a waxy coating, which helps retain moisture.
