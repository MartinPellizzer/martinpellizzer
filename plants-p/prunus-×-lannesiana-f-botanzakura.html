Prunus × lannesiana f. botanzakura

Prunus × lannesiana f. botanzakura, commonly known as the Botanzakura cherry, is a unique cultivar of the Prunus genus, characterized by its striking floral display.

It is a hybrid of Prunus lannesiana, known for its vibrant, showy flowers that range from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized shrub or tree, with dense, upright growth habit and glossy, dark green foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardens.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in landscape design for its aesthetic appeal.
