Populus yunnanensis

Populus yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan poplar, is a deciduous tree native to southwestern China.

It belongs to the Salicaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and distinctive bark. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and is often found in mountainous regions. It plays an important role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and contributing to soil stability.

Populus yunnanensis is also valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine.
