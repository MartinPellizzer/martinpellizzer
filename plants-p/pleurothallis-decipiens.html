Pleurothallis decipiens

Pleurothallis decipiens, commonly known as the deceptive pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, loose soil for optimal growth.

Due to its delicate appearance and unique flowering pattern, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
