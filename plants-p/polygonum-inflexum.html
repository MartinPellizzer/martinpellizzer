Polygonum inflexum

Polygonum inflexum, commonly known as the zigzag knotweed, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in moist environments such as wetlands and stream banks.

Polygonum inflexum is often used in traditional medicine and has ornamental value in gardens.
