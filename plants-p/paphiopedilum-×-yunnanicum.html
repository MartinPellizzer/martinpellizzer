Paphiopedilum × yunnanicum

Paphiopedilum × yunnanicum, commonly known as the Yunnan slipper orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped pouch that houses its flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or lithophyte, thriving in shaded, humid environments. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white with intricate patterns, and it produces a single, showy bloom per stem.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
