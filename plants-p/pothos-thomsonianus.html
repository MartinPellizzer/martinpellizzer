Pothos thomsonianus

Pothos thomsonianus, commonly known as the Thai ivy, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically bright green with a slightly variegated pattern. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments and prefers indirect light. Its stems can grow quite long, making it suitable for hanging baskets or trained on trellises.

Pothos thomsonianus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care, making it a popular houseplant.
