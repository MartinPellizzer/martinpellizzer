Peperomia rhodophlebia

Peperomia rhodophlebia, commonly known as the red-veined peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves with prominent red veins that contrast beautifully against its deep green foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers arranged in spikes, which appear in the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia rhodophlebia is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal.
