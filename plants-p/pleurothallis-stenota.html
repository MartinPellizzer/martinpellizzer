Pleurothallis stenota

Pleurothallis stenota, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil for optimal growth. Its leaves are usually narrow and elongated, contributing to its overall elegant appearance.

Pleurothallis stenota is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
