Parilia malabarica

Parilia malabarica, commonly known as the Malabar parilia, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its showy yellow or orange inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often covered in a fine pubescence.
