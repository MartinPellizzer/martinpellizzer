Phoenicaulis

Phoenicaulis, commonly known as the red lily, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking red or pink flowers. The plant features strap-shaped leaves that are often bluish-green in color. Phoenicaulis typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with a bushy appearance.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
