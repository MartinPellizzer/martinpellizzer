Pentas graniticola

Pentas graniticola, commonly known as the granite pentas, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are typically narrow and gray-green, with a fuzzy texture that helps reduce water loss.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
