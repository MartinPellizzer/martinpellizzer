Parietaria heterophyla

Parietaria heterophyla, commonly known as the common wall rue, is a flowering plant native to Europe, North Africa, and parts of Asia.

The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, typically appearing in late spring to early summer. Parietaria heterophyla thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and rocky crevices.
