Premna cumingiana f. glabrescens

Premna cumingiana f. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-leaved premna, is a variant of the Premna cumingiana species, which belongs to the Verbenaceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to parts of Southeast Asia, including India and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves, which are opposite and ovate in shape.
