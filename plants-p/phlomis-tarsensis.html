Phlomis tarsensis

Phlomis tarsensis, commonly known as Turkish heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or dry soils, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments. The plant features upright stems and lance-shaped leaves with a grayish-green hue. Its showy, violet-blue flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Phlomis tarsensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
