Pittosporum ellipticum var. decorum

Pittosporum ellipticum var. decorum, commonly known as the silver-leaf pittosporum, is a deciduous shrub native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are glossy on top and silvery white underneath, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers in spring, which are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique foliage and seasonal blooms make it a valuable addition to native plant landscapes.
