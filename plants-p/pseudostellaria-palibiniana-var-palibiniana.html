Pseudostellaria palibiniana var. palibiniana

Pseudostellaria palibiniana var. palibiniana, commonly known as the narrow-leaved starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, branching stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or along streambanks.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the genus Pseudostellaria.
