Peperomia lancilimba

Peperomia lancilimba, commonly known as the narrow-leaved peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which are clustered in dense spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia lancilimba is a low-maintenance plant suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation in suitable climates.
