Peperomia palmana var. oxystachya

Peperomia palmana var. oxystachya, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery or white underside, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.

Its slow growth rate and ease of care make it an ideal plant for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
