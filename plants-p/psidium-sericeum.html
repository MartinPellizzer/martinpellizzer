Psidium sericeum

Psidium sericeum, commonly known as silverleaf guava, is a small evergreen tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-white undersides on its leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pink in color. Its fruit is a small, round berry with a sweet and tangy flavor.

Psidium sericeum is often grown for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and unique appearance.
