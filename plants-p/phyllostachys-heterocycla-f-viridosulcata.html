Phyllostachys heterocycla f. viridosulcata

Phyllostachys heterocycla f. viridosulcata, commonly known as the green-striped bamboo, is a clumping bamboo species native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems that often exhibit a striking green stripe running along the length of the culms. The leaves are typically narrow and elongated, with a smooth, grass-like texture. This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and garden design.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
