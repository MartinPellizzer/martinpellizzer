Pithecellobium fanshawei

Pithecellobium fanshawei, commonly known as the Guangxi pithecellobium, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its pinnate leaves and small, pod-like fruits. The plant is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, well-drained soils.
