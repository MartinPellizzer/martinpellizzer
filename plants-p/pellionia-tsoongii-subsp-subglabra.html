Pellionia tsoongii subsp. subglabra

Pellionia tsoongii subsp.

subglabra, commonly known as the smooth-leaved pellionia, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its slender, trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy.

The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its foliage is popular in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to cascade from containers or hanging baskets.
