Picris laxa

Picris laxa, commonly known as the slender goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, branching stems with lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in clusters, producing small seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

It thrives in dry, open habitats and is often found in meadows and rocky areas.
