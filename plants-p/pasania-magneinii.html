Pasania magneinii

Pasania magneinii, commonly known as the African rock orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features a slender stem with a single, showy flower that is usually white or pale pink in color. Its blooms are often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along riverbanks.

Pasania magneinii is adapted to arid conditions and has a short growing season.
