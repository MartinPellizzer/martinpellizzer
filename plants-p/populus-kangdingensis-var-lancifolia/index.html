Populus Kangdingensis Var. Lancifolia

Populus kangdingensis var. lancifolia is a rare and unique plant species native to the high-altitude regions of western China. It belongs to the Populus genus, which includes well-known trees like aspen and poplar. This variant is distinguished by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves and its ability to thrive in cold, mountainous climates. Its limited distribution makes it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Studying this plant offers insights into adaptation strategies in extreme environments.
