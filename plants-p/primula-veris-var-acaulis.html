Primula veris var. acaulis

Primula veris var. acaulis, commonly known as the slender primrose, is a variant of the common primrose native to Europe.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its elongated, upright stems and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, often forming a rosette at the base. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides.

The plant is noted for its early spring blooming and is valued in horticulture for its delicate appearance.
