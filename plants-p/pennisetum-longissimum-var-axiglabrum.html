Pennisetum longissimum var. axiglabrum

Pennisetum longissimum var. axiglabrum, commonly known as the silky blue grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features slender, arching stems that produce tall, bluish-green plumes, which add a soft, ethereal appearance to gardens. The plant is characterized by its fine, silky texture and its ability to thrive in full sun to partial shade. It is often used in landscaping for its visual appeal and low maintenance requirements.

Its foliage remains vibrant throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for ornamental purposes.
