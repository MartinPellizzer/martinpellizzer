Peperomia beckeri

Peperomia beckeri, commonly known as the Becker's peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to South America.

It features dense, glossy, dark green foliage with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia beckeri is an ideal choice for indoor gardening due to its ease of care and attractive appearance.
