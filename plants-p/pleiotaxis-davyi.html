Pleiotaxis davyi

Pleiotaxis davyi, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and it often forms clumps or spreads via stolons.

Pleiotaxis davyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
