Panicum pseudoagrostis

Panicum pseudoagrostis, commonly known as slender panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often hairy. Its inflorescence consists of a loose panicle of small, spikelet-bearing branches.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in erosion control due to its dense growth habit.
