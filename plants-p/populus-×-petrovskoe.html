Populus × petrovskoe

Populus × petrovskoe, commonly known as the Petrovskoe poplar, is a hybrid deciduous tree belonging to the Salicaceae family.

It is characterized by its fast growth rate and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions, making it suitable for reforestation and windbreaks. The tree produces catkin-like inflorescences, with male and female flowers occurring on separate plants. Its leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with a smooth or slightly serrated margin.

The bark is smooth and grayish in young trees, becoming fissured with age.
