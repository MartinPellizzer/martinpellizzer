Phacelia californica var. egena

Phacelia californica var. egena, commonly known as the California phacelia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a herbaceous annual with slender, branching stems and small, fluffy blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The species is valued for its nectar-rich flowers, which attract a variety of pollinators.
