Pinalia bicristata

Pinalia bicristata, commonly known as the two-crown fern, is a tropical plant characterized by its unique frond structure.

It features two distinct, overlapping crowns of leaves that give it its common name. The plant belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to Southeast Asia. Its fronds are typically broad and leathery, with a dark green coloration.

Pinalia bicristata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in shaded garden environments.
