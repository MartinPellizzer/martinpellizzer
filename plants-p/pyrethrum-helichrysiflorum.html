Pyrethrum helichrysiflorum

Pyrethrum helichrysiflorum, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow daisy-like flowers with white centers, which bloom throughout the summer months. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit and is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

The botanical name reflects its resemblance to the genus Helichrysum, with "helichrysiflorum" meaning "golden flower.".
