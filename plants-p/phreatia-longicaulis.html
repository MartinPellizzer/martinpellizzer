Phreatia longicaulis

Phreatia longicaulis, commonly known as the long-stemmed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender inflorescence and striking, showy flowers that are often fragrant. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and surrounding substrates.

Phreatia longicaulis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
