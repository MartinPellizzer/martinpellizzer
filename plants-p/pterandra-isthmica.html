Pterandra isthmica

Pterandra isthmica, commonly known as the Isthmian pterandra, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central America, particularly the Isthmus of Panama. The plant is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves and distinctive, spadix-like inflorescence. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
