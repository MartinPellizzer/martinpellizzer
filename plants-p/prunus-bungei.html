Prunus bungei

Prunus bungei, commonly known as the Chinese plum or Bunge plum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers in early spring, followed by edible, sweet berries that ripen in late summer. Prunus bungei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and fruit.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
