Pleurothallis oligantha

Pleurothallis oligantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments within the rainforest canopy. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is closely related to other species within the Pleurothallis genus.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often sought after by orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike.
