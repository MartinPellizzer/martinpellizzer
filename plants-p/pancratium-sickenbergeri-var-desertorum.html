Pancratium sickenbergeri var. desertorum

Pancratium sickenbergeri var. desertorum, commonly known as the desert lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a red throat, blooming in late summer. The plant has a fibrous root system and produces a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. It is adapted to survive in dry conditions, storing water in its bulbous stem.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in xeriscapes and rock gardens.
