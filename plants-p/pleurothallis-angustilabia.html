Pleurothallis angustilabia

Pleurothallis angustilabia, commonly known as the narrow-lipped pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, linear leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. Its flowers are usually arranged in a spike and bloom during the rainy season.

Pleurothallis angustilabia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
