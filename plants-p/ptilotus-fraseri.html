Ptilotus fraseri

Ptilotus fraseri, commonly known as the Fraser's spinifex, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, spiky inflorescences. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy soils. Its small, showy flowers are typically yellow or orange, attracting various pollinators.
