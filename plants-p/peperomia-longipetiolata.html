Peperomia longipetiolata

Peperomia longipetiolata, commonly known as the long-petioled peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with prominent veins and long petioles, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically greenish-white in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its ability to thrive in low light and minimal watering.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for tabletop displays and hanging baskets.
