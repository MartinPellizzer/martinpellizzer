Podocarpus distichus var. maialis

Podocarpus distichus var. maialis, commonly known as the Maile or Hawaiian Christmas tree, is a member of the Podocarpaceae family.

It is a slow-growing evergreen tree that typically reaches heights of up to 15 meters. The tree has dense, dark green foliage with two-ranked leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance. It is native to the Hawaiian Islands and thrives in humid, tropical climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm regions.
