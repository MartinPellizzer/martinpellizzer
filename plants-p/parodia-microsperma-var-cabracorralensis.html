Parodia microsperma var. cabracorralensis

Parodia microsperma var. cabracorralensis, commonly known as the Cabracorralensis Cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a cylindrical stem with a short, narrow neck and is covered in white to pale yellow spines. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
