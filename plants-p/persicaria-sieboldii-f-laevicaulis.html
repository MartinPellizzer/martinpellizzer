Persicaria sieboldii f. laevicaulis

Persicaria sieboldii f. laevicaulis, commonly known as the smoothleaf polygonum, is a flowering plant in the family Polygonaceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glabrous. The plant produces small, clustered flowers with five petals, typically in shades of pink or red. It thrives in moist environments such as wet meadows or streambanks.

Its botanical features make it a notable species for ecological and ornamental purposes.
