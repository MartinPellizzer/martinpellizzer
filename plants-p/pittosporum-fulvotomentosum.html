Pittosporum fulvotomentosum

Pittosporum fulvotomentosum, commonly known as the golden-flowered pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, aromatic foliage and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are typically dark green and have a slightly rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping and conservation efforts.
