Plantago pflanzii var. hauthalii

Plantago pflanzii var. hauthalii, commonly known as the Hauthal's plantain, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features rosettes of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces erect flowering stems with small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry grasslands and rocky areas.

This variety is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
