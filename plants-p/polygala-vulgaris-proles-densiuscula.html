Polygala vulgaris proles densiuscula

Polygala vulgaris proles densiuscula, commonly known as the dense-stemmed milkweed, is a perennial herb native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that are often densely covered with fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on short pedicels. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

This species is often found in grassy areas and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.
