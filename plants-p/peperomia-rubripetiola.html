Peperomia rubripetiola

Peperomia rubripetiola, commonly known as the red-veined peperomia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and vibrant, waxy leaves with striking red veins. The leaves are typically oval or elliptic in shape and can vary in color from deep green to reddish hues. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
