Prunus cerasifera var. luteoglobata

Prunus cerasifera var. luteoglobata, commonly known as the yellow cherry or golden cherry tree, is a cultivar of the common cherry tree.

It is characterized by its striking yellow foliage, which turns bright orange in the fall. The leaves are ovate with a smooth margin and a glossy surface. It produces small, sweet, edible fruits that are similar to cherries but often less tart.

This tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often planted in gardens for its vibrant seasonal color.
