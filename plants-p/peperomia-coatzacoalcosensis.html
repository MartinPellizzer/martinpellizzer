Peperomia coatzacoalcosensis

Peperomia coatzacoalcosensis, commonly known as the Coatzacoalcos peperomia, is a species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, aiding in water retention. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color.

This species is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
