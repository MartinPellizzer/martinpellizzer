Pogostemon quadrifolius

Pogostemon quadrifolius, commonly known as the four-leaf clover plant, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, herbaceous plant with square stems and opposite leaves that often have a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its unique leaf structure, it is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
