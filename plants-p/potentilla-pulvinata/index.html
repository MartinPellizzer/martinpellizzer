Potentilla Pulvinata

Potentilla pulvinata is a striking shrub native to the mountainous regions of western North America. It is known for its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in late spring and early summer. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings. Its unique pulvinated leaves, which can be folded or rolled, add to its botanical intrigue. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the world of cultivated plants.
