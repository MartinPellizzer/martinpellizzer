Physocarpa

Physocarpa, commonly known as the "bottle tree," is a genus of flowering plants in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

These plants are native to various regions of Africa, the Americas, and parts of Asia. The fruit is a fleshy, berry-like structure that contains several seeds.
