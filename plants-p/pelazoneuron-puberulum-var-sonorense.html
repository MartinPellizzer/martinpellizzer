Pelazoneuron puberulum var. sonorense

Pelazoneuron puberulum var. sonorense, commonly known as the Sonoran Desert lily, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northwestern Mexico.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are covered in a fine layer of hair, giving it a fuzzy appearance.
