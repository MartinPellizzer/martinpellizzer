Pleurothallis tempestalis

Pleurothallis tempestalis, commonly known as the storm orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with intricate patterns. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and decaying organic matter.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
