Peckoltia

Peckoltia, commonly known as the dwarf pleco, is a small, freshwater catfish native to South America.

It is characterized by its elongated body and distinctive, laterally compressed shape. The plant-like appearance of its body, with a series of bony plates, has led to its association with aquatic plants in its natural habitat. Peckoltia species typically thrive in slow-moving, densely vegetated waters.

They are popular in aquariums due to their peaceful nature and unique appearance.
