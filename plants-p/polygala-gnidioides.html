Polygala gnidioides

Polygala gnidioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions. The plant features slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that are usually pale green or yellowish in color. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and it produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Polygala gnidioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and flowering habits.
