Peperomia ioeides

Peperomia ioeides, commonly known as the silver velvet peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, velvety foliage with a silvery-gray hue, which gives it its common name. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a waxy texture, making it an attractive indoor plant. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or red, followed by tiny, round berries.

This species prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.
