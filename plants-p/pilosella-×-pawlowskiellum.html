Pilosella × pawlowskiellum

Pilosella × pawlowskiellum, commonly known as the hybrid groundsel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and ray florets. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It features a basal rosette of narrow, silver-tomentose leaves and produces yellow flowers in the summer.
