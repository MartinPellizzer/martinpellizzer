Phreatia kaniensis

Phreatia kaniensis, commonly known as the Kani's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique morphological features, including a slender stem and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
