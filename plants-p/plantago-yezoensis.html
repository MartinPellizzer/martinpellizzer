Plantago yezoensis

Plantago yezoensis, commonly known as Yezo plantain, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It features rosettes of broad, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces erect flowering stalks that bear small, greenish-white flowers in dense clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its leaves are used in traditional medicine for their soothing and healing properties.
