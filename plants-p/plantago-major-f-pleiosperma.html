Plantago major f. pleiosperma

Plantago major f. pleiosperma, commonly known as the common plantain, is a variant of the common plantain species.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in lawns, gardens, and disturbed soils. The plant features broad, oval-shaped leaves with a distinctive waxy coating and prominent veins. It produces small, greenish flowers arranged in dense spikes, which are often hidden beneath the leaves.

This variety is notable for its ability to thrive in a wide range of environmental conditions.
