Polystichopsis aristata

Polystichopsis aristata, commonly known as the silver fern, is a species of fern native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its distinctive, silver-colored fronds that are adorned with fine, silky hairs. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest undergrowth or along stream banks. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds, typical of many fern species.

The plant's unique appearance and adaptability make it a notable component of its native ecosystems.
