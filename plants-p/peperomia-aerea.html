Peperomia aerea

Peperomia aerea, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with a distinctive silvery or white underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, which are followed by tiny, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia aerea is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its compact size and attractive foliage.
