Polypodium rheedei

Polypodium rheedei, commonly known as the Rheede's sword fern, is a tropical fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its long, narrow, and often serrated fronds. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its rhizome is creeping and produces both sterile and fertile fronds.

Polypodium rheedei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
