Panicum pedersenii

Panicum pedersenii, commonly known as the Pedersen's grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to certain regions of southern Africa, particularly South Africa. This perennial grass typically grows in open, sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often tinged with red or purple.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in degraded landscapes.
