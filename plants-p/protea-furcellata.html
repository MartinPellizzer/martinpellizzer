Protea furcellata

Protea furcellata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical inflorescence composed of numerous white to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in fynbos vegetation, thriving in well-drained, acidic soils. Its foliage consists of narrow, leathery leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating.

Protea furcellata is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in supporting local pollinators.
