Plectranthus zebrarum

Plectranthus zebrarum, commonly known as the zebra plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features distinctive, wavy, silver-green leaves with a striking zebra-like pattern of dark green stripes. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from violet to white, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate sunlight, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and ease of care contribute to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
