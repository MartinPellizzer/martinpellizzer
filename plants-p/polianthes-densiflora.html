Polianthes densiflora

Polianthes densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered amaryllis, is a striking tropical plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves and clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped blooms that are typically borne on a single stem. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often grown as a decorative plant in gardens and as a houseplant.
