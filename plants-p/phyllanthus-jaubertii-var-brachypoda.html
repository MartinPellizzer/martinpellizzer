Phyllanthus jaubertii var. brachypoda

Phyllanthus jaubertii var. brachypoda, commonly known as the short-stemmed phyllanthus, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered leaves and yellowish flowers. It is often used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
