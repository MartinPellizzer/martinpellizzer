Pascalia subvaginata

Pascalia subvaginata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pascalia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

Pascalia subvaginata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
