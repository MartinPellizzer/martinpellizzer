Peperomia exclamationis

Peperomia exclamationis, commonly known as the Exclamation Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to Brazil.

It features dense, glossy dark green leaves with a waxy texture and distinctive white veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of green or red. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it an ideal indoor plant.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for terrariums and tabletop displays.
