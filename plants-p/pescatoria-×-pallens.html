Pescatoria × pallens

Pescatoria × pallens, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Pescatoria and pallens species.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Botanically, it is notable for its complex hybrid structure and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
