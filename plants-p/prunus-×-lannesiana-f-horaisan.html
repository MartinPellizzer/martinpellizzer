Prunus × lannesiana f. horaisan

Prunus × lannesiana f. horaisan, commonly known as the Japanese cherry blossom or sakura, is a decorative shrub or small tree native to Japan.

It is characterized by its delicate, pink to white flowers that bloom in early spring, creating a stunning visual display. The plant belongs to the Rosaceae family and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens and public spaces. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and turn vibrant shades of red in autumn.

The fruit is a small, hard drupe that matures in late summer.
