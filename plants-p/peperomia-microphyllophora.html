Peperomia microphyllophora

Peperomia microphyllophora, commonly known as the miniature fern peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, and it produces delicate, fern-like inflorescences. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable addition to shaded or low-light environments.
