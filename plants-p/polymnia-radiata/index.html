Polymnia Radiata

Polymnia radiata is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant known for its vibrant, showy blooms. Native to the tropical regions of South America, it thrives in warm, humid climates with plenty of sunlight. Its bright, colorful petals resemble a cluster of tiny stars, giving it its common name "Star Flower." This plant is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and ease of care. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively unknown species in the broader horticultural community.
