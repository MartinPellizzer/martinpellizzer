Pleurothallis microcharis

Pleurothallis microcharis, commonly known as the miniature charis orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming during the wet season. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining, organic-rich substrates.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
