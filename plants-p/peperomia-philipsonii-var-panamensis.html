Peperomia philipsonii var. panamensis

Peperomia philipsonii var. panamensis, commonly known as the Panamanian peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Central America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
