Peperomia silvarum

Peperomia silvarum, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It features waxy, dark green leaves with a distinctive silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Due to its attractive foliage and ease of care, it is a popular choice for beginners and experienced plant enthusiasts alike.
