Peperomia aceramarcana

Peperomia aceramarcana, commonly known as the red-leaf peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically deep green with reddish veins, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or purple in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia aceramarcana is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
