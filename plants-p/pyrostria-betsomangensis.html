Pyrostria betsomangensis

Pyrostria betsomangensis, commonly known as the Betsomang palm, is a rare and endemic species native to southern Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized palm characterized by its slender trunk and pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with both male and female flowers, typical of many palm species. Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and limited distribution, making it a subject of interest for conservationists.

Due to habitat loss, it faces threats to its survival in the wild.
