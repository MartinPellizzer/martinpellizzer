Phreatia robusta

Phreatia robusta, commonly known as the robust orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its aerial roots and ability to grow on trees or other structures. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Phreatia robusta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
