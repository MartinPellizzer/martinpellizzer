Pilea basicordata

Pilea basicordata, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are round and glossy, often with a slight waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear on short stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular indoor plant.

Pilea basicordata is known for its ease of care and ability to propagate through leaf cuttings.
