Phyllanthus phillyreifolius var. oblongifolius

Phyllanthus phillyreifolius var. oblongifolius, commonly known as the oblong-leaved phyllanthus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate, and oblong-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically in the axils of the leaves. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage.
