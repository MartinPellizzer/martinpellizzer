Piper cuernavacanum

Piper cuernavacanum, commonly known as the Cuernavaca pepper or Mexican pepper, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as the common black pepper. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into elongated, curved fruits. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped with a distinctive aroma.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a spice in traditional cuisines.
