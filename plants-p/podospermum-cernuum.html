Podospermum cernuum

Podospermum cernuum, commonly known as the hanging pimpernel, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Podospermum cernuum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
