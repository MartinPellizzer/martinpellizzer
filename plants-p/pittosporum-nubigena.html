Pittosporum nubigena

Pittosporum nubigena, commonly known as the cloud fern pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and distinctive, fragrant flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. It produces small, round berries that are typically yellow or red when mature.

Pittosporum nubigena is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
