Peperomia mindoroensis

Peperomia mindoroensis, commonly known as the Mindoro peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, with a deep green coloration. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually clustered in spikes, which are often hidden beneath the foliage. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal water needs.

Peperomia mindoroensis is valued for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for plant enthusiasts.
