Pittosporum rehderianum var. ternstroemioides

Pittosporum rehderianum var. ternstroemioides, commonly known as the New Zealand Pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath, often with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in terminal umbels, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native New Zealand forests.
