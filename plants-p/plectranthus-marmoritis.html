Plectranthus marmoritis

Plectranthus marmoritis, commonly known as the marble plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, waxy, and often variegated leaves that display a marble-like pattern of green and white. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of purple and pink, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Plectranthus marmoritis is a popular ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
