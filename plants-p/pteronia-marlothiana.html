Pteronia marlothiana

Pteronia marlothiana, commonly known as the marloth's pteronia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.

Pteronia marlothiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
