Potamopitys hydropiper

Potamopitys hydropiper, commonly known as the water pine.

It is a unique coniferous plant native to the tropical rainforests of South America. This species is characterized by its needle-like leaves and cone-bearing structure, typical of conifers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near water sources.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered an endangered species.
