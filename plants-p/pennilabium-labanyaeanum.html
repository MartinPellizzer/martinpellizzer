Pennilabium labanyaeanum

Pennilabium labanyaeanum, commonly known as the Labanyaean orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and the Philippines. This plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. The leaves are typically linear and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
