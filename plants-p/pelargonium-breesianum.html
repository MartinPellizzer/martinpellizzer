Pelargonium breesianum

Pelargonium breesianum, commonly known as the Bressa geranium, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive, fragrant flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Pelargonium breesianum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
