Physotrichia diplolophioides

Physotrichia diplolophioides, commonly known as the broad-leaved grass.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are usually arranged in a dense rosette. Its flowers are small and clustered in panicle-like inflorescences, typically with three stamens.

The species is characterized by its fibrous root system and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.
