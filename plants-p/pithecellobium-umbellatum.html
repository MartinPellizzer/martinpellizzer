Pithecellobium umbellatum

Pithecellobium umbellatum, commonly known as the yellow clusterpod, is a shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are often clustered in umbels, giving it its scientific name. Its seeds are encased in hairy, elongated pods that mature into a dark brown color.

The plant is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine.
