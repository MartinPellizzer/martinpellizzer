Paracaryum polycarpum

Paracaryum polycarpum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive. It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and ornamental value.

Paracaryum polycarpum is known for its ability to produce multiple flower spikes from a single stem.
