Pilea ecboliophylla

Pilea ecboliophylla, commonly known as the silver mottled pilea, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features rounded, waxy leaves with a distinctive silvery mottling that gives it a striking appearance. The plant typically grows to a height of about 15 to 30 centimeters, making it ideal for indoor cultivation. Its foliage is dense and glossy, with leaves that are slightly elongated and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Pilea ecboliophylla prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardeners.
