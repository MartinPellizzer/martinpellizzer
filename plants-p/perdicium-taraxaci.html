Perdicium taraxaci

Perdicium taraxaci, commonly known as the dandelion fly.

This plant is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae, characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Its life cycle includes a rosette of leaves followed by a flowering stem, making it a common sight in meadows and gardens.
