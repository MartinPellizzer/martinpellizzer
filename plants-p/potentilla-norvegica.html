Potentilla norvegica

Potentilla norvegica, commonly known as Norwegian cinquefoil, is a perennial flowering plant native to northern Europe.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves with five leaflets. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

Potentilla norvegica is also used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
