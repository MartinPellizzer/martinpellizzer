Pandanus peyrierasii

Pandanus peyrierasii, commonly known as the Peyrieras' pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is a member of the Pandanaceae family and is characterized by its long, sword-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically grouped in clusters at the base of the leaves. Its fibrous roots and sturdy stems make it well adapted to coastal and wetland environments.

Due to its ornamental value and cultural significance, it is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
