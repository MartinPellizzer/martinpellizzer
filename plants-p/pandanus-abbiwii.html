Pandanus abbiwii

Pandanus abbiwii, commonly known as the white pandanus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves that are typically light green to white in color. The plant produces large, clustered inflorescences with striking yellow flowers. It thrives in coastal and wetland environments, often growing near mangroves.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
