Poa pugionifolia

Poa pugionifolia, commonly known as the swordgrass, is a species of grass native to southern South America.

It is characterized by its narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Poa pugionifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
