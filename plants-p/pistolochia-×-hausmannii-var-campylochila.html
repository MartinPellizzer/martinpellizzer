Pistolochia × hausmannii var. campylochila

Pistolochia × hausmannii var. campylochila, commonly known as the Campylochila pistolochia, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This variant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage and manageable growth habit.
