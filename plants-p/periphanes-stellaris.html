Periphanes stellaris

Periphanes stellaris, commonly known as the starry periphanes, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Periphanes stellaris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
