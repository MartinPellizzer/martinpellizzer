Pennisetum humile f. elatius

Pennisetum humile f. elatius, commonly known as the slender fountain grass, is a compact, ornamental grass that features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, feathery plumes.

It is native to Africa and thrives in full sun to partial shade, preferring well-drained soil. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and is often used in gardens for its visual appeal and ability to tolerate drought conditions. It blooms in late summer to early fall, producing airy, light green to pinkish flower spikes.

This variety is valued for its low maintenance and ability to add texture and movement to landscapes.
