Pearsonia mesopontica

Pearsonia mesopontica, commonly known as the Greek pearsonia, is a rare and endemic plant species native to the island of Crete.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
