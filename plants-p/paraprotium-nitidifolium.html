Paraprotium nitidifolium

Paraprotium nitidifolium, commonly known as the glossy-leaf pandanus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad, and glossy green leaves that are arranged in a fan-like pattern. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or green in color. Its roots are fibrous and often used in traditional medicine.

The plant thrives in humid environments and is often found near coastal areas or in wetlands.
