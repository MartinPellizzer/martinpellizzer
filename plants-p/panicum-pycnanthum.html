Panicum pycnanthum

Panicum pycnanthum, commonly known as little bluestem, is a native grass species found throughout much of North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green blades and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types, including sandy and clay soils. This plant is often used in native plant gardens and restoration projects due to its drought tolerance and ecological benefits. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically reddish-brown in color.

Panicum pycnanthum plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
