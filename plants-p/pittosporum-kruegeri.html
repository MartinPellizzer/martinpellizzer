Pittosporum kruegeri

Pittosporum kruegeri, commonly known as the Krueger's pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features dark green, glossy leaves that are oval in shape and have a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, which are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Pittosporum kruegeri is relatively low maintenance and tolerant of light frosts.
