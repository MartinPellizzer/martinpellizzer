Pilea granmae

Pilea granmae, commonly known as the Cuban begonia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Cuba.

It features rounded, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and prominent, wavy margins. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance make it a desirable addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
