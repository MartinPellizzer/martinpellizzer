Prunus Cerasifera Subsp. Orientalis

Prunus cerasifera subsp. orientalis is a striking ornamental tree known for its vibrant pink and white flowers. It is a subspecies of the common cherry, often mistaken for its more familiar relatives. This plant thrives in temperate climates and is popular in urban landscaping due to its hardy nature. Its showy blooms appear in early spring, adding color to gardens and streets. Despite its beauty, it requires proper care to avoid issues like fruiting and disease.
