Piper lundellii

Piper lundellii, commonly known as the Lundell's pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its vine-like growth habit and small, heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by berries.

The plant is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
