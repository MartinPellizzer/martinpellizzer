Portulaca prolifera

Portulaca prolifera, commonly known as the spreading purslane, is a low-growing, succulent plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features fleshy, rounded leaves and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant is characterized by its ability to spread via trailing stems and produce numerous small, oval-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a common weed in gardens and disturbed areas.

Portulaca prolifera is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
