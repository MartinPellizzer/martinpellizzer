Phragmipedium stenophyllum

Phragmipedium stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf phragmipedium, is a species of orchid native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration pattern. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white with prominent purple or brown markings.

This orchid requires high humidity and indirect light to thrive in cultivation.
