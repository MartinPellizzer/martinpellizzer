Polygala mendesii

Polygala mendesii, commonly known as the Cape dune polygala, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, spire-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in coastal and inland dune environments. It produces small, usually white or yellowish flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
