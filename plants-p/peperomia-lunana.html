Peperomia lunana

Peperomia lunana, commonly known as the Moonlight Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval or heart-shaped, often with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green or red, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia lunana is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and moderate humidity.
