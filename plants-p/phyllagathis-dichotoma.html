Phyllagathis dichotoma

Phyllagathis dichotoma, commonly known as the dichotomous phyllagathis, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and a distinctively aromatic scent. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and are usually pale green or white in color.

Phyllagathis dichotoma is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
