Philodendron brewsterense

Philodendron brewsterense, commonly known as the Brewster's philodendron, is a climbing plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and yellow. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical plant collections.
