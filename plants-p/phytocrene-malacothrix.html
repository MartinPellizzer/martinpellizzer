Phytocrene malacothrix

Phytocrene malacothrix, commonly known as the spiral leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming during the wet season. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
