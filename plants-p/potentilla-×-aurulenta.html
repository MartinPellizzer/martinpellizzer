Potentilla × aurulenta

Potentilla × aurulenta, commonly known as the golden potentilla, is a hybrid shrub native to the mountainous regions of central and eastern Europe.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, adding a vibrant touch to gardens. Its foliage is typically dark green, with a slight silvery sheen on the underside, contributing to its visual appeal.

Potentilla × aurulenta is relatively low maintenance and thrives in well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
