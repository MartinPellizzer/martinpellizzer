Peperomia allagotacta

Peperomia allagotacta, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features succulent, thick, and fleshy leaves that are typically grayish-green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia allagotacta is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
