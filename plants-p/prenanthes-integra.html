Prenanthes integra

Prenanthes integra, commonly known as the roundleaf waterleaf, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are smooth and often have a waxy texture. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in umbels and bloom in late summer.

Prenanthes integra is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
