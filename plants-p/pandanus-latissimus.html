Pandanus latissimus

Pandanus latissimus, commonly known as the broadleaf pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Indo-Pacific region.

It is characterized by its large, strap-shaped leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its roots system is fibrous and helps it thrive in moist, well-drained soils.

The plant is often used in traditional crafts and is also valued for its ornamental appeal.
