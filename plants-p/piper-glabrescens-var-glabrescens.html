Piper glabrescens var. glabrescens

Piper glabrescens var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth pepper plant, is a member of the Piperaceae family.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into elongated, slender fruits. Native to tropical regions of South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a unique subject for study in both horticulture and ethnobotany.
