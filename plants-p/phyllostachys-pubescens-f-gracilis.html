Phyllostachys pubescens f. gracilis

Phyllostachys pubescens f. gracilis, commonly known as the graceful bamboo, is a compact, ornamental bamboo species native to China.

It features slender, upright stems that grow in dense clumps, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. The leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and covered in fine, silky hairs, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

This variety is prized for its elegant form and is often used in ornamental gardening and landscape design.
