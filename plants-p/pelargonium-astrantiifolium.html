Pelargonium astrantiifolium

Pelargonium astrantiifolium, commonly known as the Astrantiifolium geranium, is a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and distinctive, heart-shaped leaves with a silvery underside. The plant produces small, pale pink to white flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens and containers due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.

Its foliage remains attractive throughout the year, making it a popular choice for both gardeners and horticultural enthusiasts.
