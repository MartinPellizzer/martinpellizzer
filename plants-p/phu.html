Phu

Phu, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, including China, India, and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its showy, papery bracts that resemble small lanterns, which are often used in ornamental gardening. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, growing in dense inflorescences.

Phu thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.
