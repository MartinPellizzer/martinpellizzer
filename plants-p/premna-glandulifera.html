Premna glandulifera

Premna glandulifera, commonly known as the glandular premna, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to parts of Asia, including China, India, and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy and have glandular dots. Its small, tubular flowers are typically pink or purple and are arranged in clusters.

Premna glandulifera is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions.
