Prasophyllum catenemum

Prasophyllum catenemum, commonly known as the chain-leaved orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to the southern regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces a single, slender spike of white or pale pink flowers. The plant is adapted to grow in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open habitats such as heathlands and grassy woodlands. Prasophyllum catenemum is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is known for its delicate appearance and specific ecological requirements.
