Protea magnifica

Protea magnifica, commonly known as the giant protea, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family, which is known for its diverse and showy floral structures. The plant features large, cone-shaped inflorescences composed of numerous small, brightly colored flowers. Its leaves are leathery and evergreen, often with a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Protea magnifica is adapted to nutrient-poor soils and thrives in regions with a Mediterranean climate.
