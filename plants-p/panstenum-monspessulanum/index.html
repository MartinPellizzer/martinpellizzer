Panstenum Monspessulanum

Panstenum monspessulanum is a hardy shrub native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas and Mediterranean climates. Known for its small, green leaves and clusters of yellow flowers, it adds a unique touch to gardens and landscapes. This plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions. It is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
