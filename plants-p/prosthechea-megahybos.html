Prosthechea megahybos

Prosthechea megahybos, commonly known as the giant prosthechea, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, fleshy pseudobulbs and long, arching stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, adapted to capture moisture from the humid air.

Prosthechea megahybos thrives in warm, moist environments and is often found growing on trees in the canopy of tropical forests.
