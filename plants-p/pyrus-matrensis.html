Pyrus matrensis

Pyrus matrensis, commonly known as the Matrenskaya pear, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to the European region, particularly found in Russia and Ukraine. The plant is characterized by its deciduous nature, producing small, yellowish flowers in the spring. Its fruit, a pear-like fruit, is typically harvested in late summer and is known for its sweet and aromatic flavor.

Pyrus matrensis is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its edible fruit.
