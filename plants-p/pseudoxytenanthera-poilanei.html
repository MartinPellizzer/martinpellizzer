Pseudoxytenanthera poilanei

Pseudoxytenanthera poilanei, commonly known as the Pilea-like plant, is a unique species within the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers and heart-shaped leaves, which are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. Native to Madagascar, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a desirable addition to botanical gardens and homes.
