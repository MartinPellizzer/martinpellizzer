Peperomia disticha

Peperomia disticha, commonly known as the Shiny Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, usually in shades of white or pale pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia disticha is noted for its low maintenance and ability to adapt to a variety of indoor conditions.
