Peliosanthes splendens

Peliosanthes splendens, commonly known as the golden torch tree, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and its showy, golden-yellow inflorescences that resemble torches. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers arranged in dense clusters, which are followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a landscape plant.
