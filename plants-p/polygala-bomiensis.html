Polygala bomiensis

Polygala bomiensis, commonly known as the Bomi polygala, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, spicate inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as mountain forests. Its leaves are lanceolate and opposite, with small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
