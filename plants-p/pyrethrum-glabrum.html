Pyrethrum glabrum

Pyrethrum glabrum, commonly known as the smooth pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant produces fine, hairy seeds that are dispersed by the wind. Its leaves are typically narrow and gray-green in color, with a slightly fuzzy texture.

Pyrethrum glabrum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used historically in herbal medicine.
