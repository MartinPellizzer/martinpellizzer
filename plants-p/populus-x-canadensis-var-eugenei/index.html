Populus × Canadensis Var. Eugenei

Canadian poplar, scientifically known as Populus × canadensis var. eugenei, is a fast-growing tree native to eastern North America. It is widely cultivated for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. This variety is particularly valued for its ornamental qualities, including its striking white bark and vibrant spring foliage. The tree has also been used in reforestation efforts due to its resilience and adaptability. However, its aggressive growth can make it a challenge to manage in certain environments.
