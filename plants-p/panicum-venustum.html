Panicum venustum

Panicum venustum, commonly known as the ornamental grass, is a perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, arching stems that produce feathery, silver-green plumes, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring minimal water once established. Its foliage is often used in landscape design for its visual appeal and ability to add texture.

Panicum venustum is also valued for its role in attracting pollinators and providing habitat for small wildlife.
