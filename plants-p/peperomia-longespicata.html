Peperomia longespicata

Peperomia longespicata, commonly known as the long-spined peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically oval or lance-shaped, with a waxy texture and a variety of colors ranging from deep green to variegated patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish, followed by elongated, spiky fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia longespicata is known for its ability to tolerate low light conditions and requires minimal water, making it relatively easy to care for.
