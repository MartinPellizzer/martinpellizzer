Poa commersonii

Poa commersonii, commonly known as Commerson's bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and forms dense, low-growing mats. The plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along stream banks. Its inflorescence consists of a single spikelet, which is often hidden within the foliage.

Poa commersonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping for its durability and aesthetic qualities.
