Peperomia asperuloides

Peperomia asperuloides, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact evergreen perennial native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white or pale pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
