Peckia macrophylla

Peckia macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf peckia, is a striking member of the Araliaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its roots are known for their medicinal properties, often used in traditional herbal remedies.

Peckia macrophylla thrives in moist, shaded environments and is native to parts of Southeast Asia.
