Prunus jamasakura f. kokonoye-odora

Prunus jamasakura f. kokonoye-odora, commonly known as the sweet scenting cherry, is a cultivar of the Japanese cherry tree.

It is characterized by its pale pink to white flowers that emit a subtle, sweet fragrance. The plant produces small, red fruits that are edible but not commonly consumed. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Its foliage turns a vibrant red in the fall, adding to its aesthetic value.
