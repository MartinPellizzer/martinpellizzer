Perilomia scutellarioides

Perilomia scutellarioides, commonly known as the shieldleaf plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Perilomia scutellarioides is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.
