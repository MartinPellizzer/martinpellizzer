Peperomia aguacatensis var. orosiana

Peperomia aguacatensis var. orosiana, commonly known as the Orosina peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to Mexico.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a velvety texture and produces small, clustered white flowers. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil. It is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.

Its compact size and attractive appearance make it suitable for hanging baskets or as a tabletop plant.
