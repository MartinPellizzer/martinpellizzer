Pterocarpus zehntneri

Pterocarpus zehntneri, commonly known as the African rosewood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and compound leaves. The tree produces yellow to orange flowers followed by distinctive pod-like fruits containing flat, dark seeds.

Its wood is highly valued for its rich color and durability, making it important in traditional and contemporary woodworking.
