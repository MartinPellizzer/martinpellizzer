Plectranthus tenuicaulis

Plectranthus tenuicaulis, commonly known as narrow-leaved plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color, arranged in clusters along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is a popular choice among gardeners.
