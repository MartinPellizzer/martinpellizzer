Peperomia pseudosilvarum

Peperomia pseudosilvarum, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically silver or gray-green in color, with a waxy texture that helps it retain moisture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white, often emerging from the axils of the leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia pseudosilvarum is relatively easy to care for, requiring minimal watering and tolerating a range of environmental conditions.
