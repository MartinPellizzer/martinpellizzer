Podocarpus guatemalensis subsp. allenii

Podocarpus guatemalensis subsp.

allenii, commonly known as the Guatemalan podocarp, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to Central America. It belongs to the Podocarpaceae family and is characterized by its needle-like leaves and small, woody cones.

The plant is typically found in humid, tropical montane forests at moderate elevations. It has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 20 meters.

The species is valued for its durable wood and is sometimes used in ornamental horticulture.
