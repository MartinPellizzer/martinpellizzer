Pilea ophioderma

Pilea ophioderma, commonly known as the silver mitten plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Urticaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in shaded, humid environments. Pilea ophioderma is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
