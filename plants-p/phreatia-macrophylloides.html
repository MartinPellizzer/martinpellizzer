Phreatia macrophylloides

Phreatia macrophylloides, commonly known as the giant leafed phreatia, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree trunks in humid, shaded forest environments. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

This orchid thrives in high humidity and requires consistent moisture and indirect sunlight for optimal growth.
