Podocarpus montanus

Podocarpus montanus, commonly known as the Chinese Podocarp, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Podocarpaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

Podocarpus montanus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in horticulture and reforestation efforts.
