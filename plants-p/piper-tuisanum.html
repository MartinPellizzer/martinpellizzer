Piper tuisanum

Piper tuisanum, commonly known as the Tuisanum pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit and has small, heart-shaped leaves. Its fruits are tiny, three-lobed berries that are often used in traditional medicine.

The plant's botanical features make it a subject of interest in both ecological and pharmacological studies.
