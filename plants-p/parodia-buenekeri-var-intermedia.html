Parodia buenekeri var. intermedia

Parodia buenekeri var. intermedia, commonly known as the "Cactus of the Desert," is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a dense covering of white to pale green spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and rock gardens.
