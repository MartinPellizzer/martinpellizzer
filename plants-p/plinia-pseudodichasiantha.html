Plinia pseudodichasiantha

Plinia pseudodichasiantha, commonly known as the Brazilian starfruit, is a species of plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces edible fruits that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are simple and opposite, with a glossy surface.

The botanical features of Plinia pseudodichasiantha make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological studies.
