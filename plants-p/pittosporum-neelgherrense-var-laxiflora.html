Pittosporum neelgherrense var. laxiflora

Pittosporum neelgherrense var. laxiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to the eastern Himalayas.

It features glossy, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and emit a pleasant fragrance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in loose inflorescences. Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns red when mature.

It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas at high altitudes.
