Pteris quadriaurita var. christii

Pteris quadriaurita var. christii, commonly known as the Christ's water fern, is a rare and ornamental fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically dark green with a silvery underside. The plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its unique morphology and aesthetic appeal make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and conservation efforts.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable and requires careful cultivation to ensure its survival.
