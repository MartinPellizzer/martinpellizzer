Pleopeltis taeniata

Pleopeltis taeniata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf rock fern, is a species of epiphytic and lithophytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features dark green, elongated, and often narrow leaves that are densely packed on its rhizome. The fronds are typically pinnately compound, with a distinctive, serrated margin. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or rocky surfaces.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
