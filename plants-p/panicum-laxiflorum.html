Panicum laxiflorum

Panicum laxiflorum, commonly known as slenderflower panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, fluffy flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a rough texture.

Panicum laxiflorum is often used in ornamental gardening due to its delicate appearance and adaptability.
