Prismatocarpus cliffortioides

Prismatocarpus cliffortioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pandanus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Pandanaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and elongated, narrow leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically arranged in compact inflorescences. Its leaves are often used in traditional crafts and for their aromatic properties.

The species is adapted to coastal and wetland environments, making it a hardy and resilient plant.
