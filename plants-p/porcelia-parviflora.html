Porcelia parviflora

Porcelia parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered porcelia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white or pale pink flowers clustered in dense spikes. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Due to its delicate appearance and compact size, it is often appreciated in horticultural collections for its ornamental value.
