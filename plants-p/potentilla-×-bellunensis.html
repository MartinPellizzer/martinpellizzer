Potentilla × bellunensis

Potentilla × bellunensis, commonly known as the Bellunense Cinquefoil, is a hybrid shrub native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, yellow, five-petaled flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous habitats.

Potentilla × bellunensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh environmental conditions.
