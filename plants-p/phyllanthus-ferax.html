Phyllanthus ferax

Phyllanthus ferax, commonly known as the African spurge, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and bright green color. Its flowers are yellow and occur in clusters, producing small, round fruits.

It is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.
