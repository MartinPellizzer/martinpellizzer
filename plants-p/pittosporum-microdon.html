Pittosporum microdon

Pittosporum microdon, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pittosporum, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to New Zealand.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy on top with a lighter underside. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in late summer, followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Pittosporum microdon is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
