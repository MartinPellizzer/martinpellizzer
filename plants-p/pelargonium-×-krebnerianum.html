Pelargonium × krebnerianum

Pelargonium × krebnerianum, commonly known as the krebnerianum geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The leaves are usually heart-shaped with a serrated edge, and the plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and containers. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This species is popular among gardeners for its hardiness and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
