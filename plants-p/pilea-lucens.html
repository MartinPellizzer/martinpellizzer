Pilea lucens

Pilea lucens, commonly known as the shining pilea, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically light green with a slight variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear on short stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Pilea lucens is a popular houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
