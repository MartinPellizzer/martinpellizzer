Panicum semirugosum

Panicum semirugosum, commonly known as roughside grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, linear leaves with a rough, textured surface, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grassy areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important component of native ecosystems and a potential ornamental plant.
