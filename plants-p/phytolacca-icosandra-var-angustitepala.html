Phytolacca icosandra var. angustitepala

Phytolacca icosandra var. angustitepala, commonly known as narrow-leaved physic nut, is a variant of the physic nut plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are typically narrower than those of the typical variety.
