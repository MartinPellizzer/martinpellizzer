Phagnalon linnaeanum

Phagnalon linnaeanum, commonly known as the Linnaean phagnalon, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, gray-green leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads.
