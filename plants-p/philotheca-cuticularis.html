Philotheca cuticularis

Philotheca cuticularis, commonly known as the roughleaf sarsaparilla, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its rough, hairy stems and dark green, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Its fruit is a small, capsule that contains several seeds.

Philotheca cuticularis is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to coastal and inland regions.
