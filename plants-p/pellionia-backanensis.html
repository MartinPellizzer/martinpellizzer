Pellionia backanensis

Pellionia backanensis, commonly known as the Bornean velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the family Araceae, which includes plants such as philodendrons and peace lilies. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves covered in a dense, velvety pubescence, giving it its common name. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family.

Pellionia backanensis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing along the forest floor.
