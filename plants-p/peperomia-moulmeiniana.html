Peperomia moulmeiniana

Peperomia moulmeiniana, commonly known as the Moulmein peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences of white to pale pink flowers, which are borne on short, erect stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia moulmeiniana is valued for its ornamental foliage and is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
