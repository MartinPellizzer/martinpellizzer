Piper prismaticum var. villosulum

Piper prismaticum var. villosulum, commonly known as the velvetleaf betel, is a member of the Piperaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by red, fleshy berries that are edible. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in some horticultural settings.
