Phyteuma orbiculare f. glabrum

Phyteuma orbiculare f. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth-leaved phyteuma, is a botanical variant of the phyteuma species native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its rounded, glabrous (smooth) leaves and showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is well adapted to arid conditions. It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.
