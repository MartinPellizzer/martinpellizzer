Poa Kilimanjarica

Kilimanjaro grass, scientifically known as Poa kilimanjarica, is a rare and endemic plant species found exclusively in the high-altitude regions of Mount Kilimanjaro in Tanzania. This unique grass thrives in the cool, moist microclimates of the mountain's summit, where it adapts to extreme environmental conditions. Its delicate, tufted blades and vibrant green color make it a distinctive feature of the region's alpine ecosystem. Due to its limited distribution and sensitivity to climate change, the plant faces increasing threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this botanical wonder for future generations.
