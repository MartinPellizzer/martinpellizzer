Pilosella × obornyana subsp. valdeciliata

Pilosella × obornyana subsp.

valdeciliata, commonly known as the valley pilosella, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and is native to the mountainous regions of South America.

The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are typically covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.
