Parodia wagneriana

Parodia wagneriana, commonly known as the Wagner's moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its spherical, segmented stem that resembles a moon. The plant typically has a vibrant green body with white or yellow spines, and it produces yellow flowers during the warmer months. It is a popular choice for succulent gardens due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.

Parodia wagneriana is relatively easy to care for, requiring well-draining soil and minimal water.
