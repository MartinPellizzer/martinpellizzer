Pittosporum gayanum

Pittosporum gayanum, commonly known as the Sri Lankan pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to Sri Lanka.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant produces fragrant white flowers that are clustered in umbels, which bloom in the spring. Its leaves are elliptical, dark green, and have a leathery texture.

The fruit is a small, round berry that turns red when mature, containing several seeds.
