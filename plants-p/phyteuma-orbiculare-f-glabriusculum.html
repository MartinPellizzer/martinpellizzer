Phyteuma orbiculare f. glabriusculum

Phyteuma orbiculare f. glabriusculum, commonly known as the smooth-leaved phyteuma, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its orbicular, hairy leaves and showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. This subspecies is typically found in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has a compact growth habit and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
