Peperomia villilimba

Peperomia villilimba, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings, giving it a striking visual appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia villilimba is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
