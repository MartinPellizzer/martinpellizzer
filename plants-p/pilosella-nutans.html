Pilosella nutans

Pilosella nutans, commonly known as the nodding cat's-ear, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, spoon-shaped leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that droop downward. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in meadows and grassy areas. The plant produces small, oval seeds that are dispersed by wind.

It is often found in open, disturbed habitats and is considered a common weed in some regions.
