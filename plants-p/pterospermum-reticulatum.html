Pterospermum reticulatum

Pterospermum reticulatum, commonly known as the net-veined pterospermum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub characterized by its compound leaves with a distinctive net-like venation pattern. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by elongated pods containing flat, winged seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
