Polyscias solomonensis

Polyscias solomonensis, commonly known as the Solomon Islands hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and showy, white flowers with a prominent spadix. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some regions for horticultural purposes.
