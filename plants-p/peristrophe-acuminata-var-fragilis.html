Peristrophe acuminata var. fragilis

Peristrophe acuminata var. fragilis, commonly known as the slender leaf senna, is a species of flowering plant in the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is native to parts of Africa and is often found in moist, open habitats. It has a slender stem and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Due to its fragile appearance, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
