Pelargonium angulatum

Pelargonium angulatum, commonly known as the angular geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive, angular leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces clusters of small, bright red or pink flowers that bloom in summer. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant color and compact size.

Pelargonium angulatum prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
