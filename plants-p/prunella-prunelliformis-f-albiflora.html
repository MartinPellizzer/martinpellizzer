Prunella prunelliformis f. albiflora

Prunella prunelliformis f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered selfheal, is a rare and striking variant of the selfheal plant.

It is characterized by its pale to white flowers, which contrast with the typical blue or purple hues of the species. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, hairy textures, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
