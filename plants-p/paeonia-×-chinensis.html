Paeonia × chinensis

Paeonia × chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese peony, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, white, and yellow. The plant has compound, pinnate leaves that are dark green and arranged alternately along the stems. Its root system is thick and fleshy, adapted to store nutrients and water.

Paeonia × chinensis is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and has a long history in traditional Chinese medicine.
