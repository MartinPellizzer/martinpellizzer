Primula multiceps

Primula multiceps, commonly known as the Chinese primrose, is a flowering plant native to China and neighboring regions.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and mountainous regions. Its leaves are oval-shaped with a smooth texture and a slightly hairy underside.

Primula multiceps is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
