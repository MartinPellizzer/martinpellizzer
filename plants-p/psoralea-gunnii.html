Psoralea gunnii

Psoralea gunnii, commonly known as the dwarf psoralea, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions. It has a compact growth habit and is well adapted to arid environments.

Psoralea gunnii is also notable for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, which benefits soil fertility.
