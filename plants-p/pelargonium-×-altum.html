Pelargonium × altum

Pelargonium × altum, commonly known as the giant geranium, is a hybrid plant that exhibits striking botanical features.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 5 inches in diameter, typically in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has a robust, upright growth habit with broad, dark green leaves that are often lobed or notched. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for both container gardening and in-ground planting.

Pelargonium × altum is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens for its dramatic floral display.
