Podochilus brachiatus

Podochilus brachiatus, commonly known as the feather orchid, is a tropical plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like inflorescence. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually small and colorful, ranging from white to pink or purple.

Podochilus brachiatus is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
