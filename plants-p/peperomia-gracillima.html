Peperomia gracillima

Peperomia gracillima, commonly known as the slender peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating, giving them a distinctive, waxy appearance. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia gracillima is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.
