Protea cucullata

Protea cucullata, commonly known as the helmet protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence that resembles a helmet, hence its common name. The plant features a sturdy, woody stem with dense clusters of tubular flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. Native to the Western Cape region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight.

Protea cucullata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also valued in traditional medicine.
