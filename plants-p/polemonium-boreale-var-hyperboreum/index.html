Polemonium Boreale Var. Hyperboreum

Polemonium boreale var. hyperboreum is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high Arctic regions of northern Europe and North America. It thrives in harsh, cold environments where few other plants can survive. Its small, bell-shaped flowers are typically blue or purple, adding a striking touch of color to the snow-covered landscape. The plant is often found growing in rocky soils and on mountain slopes. Due to its limited habitat and vulnerability to climate change, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
