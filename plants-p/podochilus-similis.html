Podochilus similis

Podochilus similis, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries such as Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Podochilus similis is often found growing in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth.
