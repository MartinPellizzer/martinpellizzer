Panicum ensifolium var. breve

Panicum ensifolium var. breve, commonly known as little bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green blades that turn coppery in the fall. This plant is often found in prairies and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils. It plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for landscaping and restoration projects.
