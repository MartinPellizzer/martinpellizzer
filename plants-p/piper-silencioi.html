Piper silencioi

Piper silencioi, commonly known as the silent pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, elongated leaves and clusters of tiny, greenish-white flowers. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns red when mature.

Piper silencioi is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
