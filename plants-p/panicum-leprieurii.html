Panicum leprieurii

Panicum leprieurii, commonly known as blue panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue-gray foliage and feathery, panicle-like flower heads that appear in late summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in gardens and landscapes for its unique color and texture.
