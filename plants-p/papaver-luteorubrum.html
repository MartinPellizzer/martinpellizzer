Papaver luteorubrum

Papaver luteorubrum, commonly known as the yellow-red poppy, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its striking orange to red flowers with a yellow center, which bloom in late summer. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous elliptical seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or arid environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
