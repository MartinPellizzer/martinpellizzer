Ptilagrostis yadongensis

Ptilagrostis yadongensis, commonly known as the Yadong grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region. The plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny flowers.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
