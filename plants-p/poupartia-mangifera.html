Poupartia mangifera

Poupartia mangifera, commonly known as the mango tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South Asia.

It belongs to the Anacardiaceae family and is characterized by its large, lance-shaped leaves and fragrant flowers. The tree produces edible fruits with a thick, fibrous pulp surrounding a single seed. Its botanical features include a smooth, grayish bark and a strong, deep root system.
