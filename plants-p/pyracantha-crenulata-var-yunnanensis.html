Pyracantha crenulata var. yunnanensis

Pyracantha crenulata var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan firethorn, is a deciduous shrub native to southwestern China.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny branches and clusters of small, orange-red berries that persist through winter. The plant features elliptical, dark green leaves that turn reddish in autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Its berries are an important food source for birds and wildlife during the colder months.
