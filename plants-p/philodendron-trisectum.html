Philodendron trisectum

Philodendron trisectum, commonly known as the three-lobed philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive three-lobed structure, giving it its common name. The plant has a vigorous growth habit and can reach lengths of several meters when provided with adequate support. Its foliage is typically a deep green, with a waxy texture that helps it thrive in humid environments.

Philodendron trisectum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ability to purify indoor air.
