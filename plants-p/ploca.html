Ploca

Ploca, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its compact rosette of velvety leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in a variety of colors, including purple, blue, and white. Ploca thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.
