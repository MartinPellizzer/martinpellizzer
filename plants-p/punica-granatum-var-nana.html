Punica granatum var. nana

Punica granatum var. nana, commonly known as the dwarf pomegranate, is a compact variety of the pomegranate plant.

It is characterized by its small, rounded leaves and vibrant red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are similar in taste to the standard pomegranate but on a much smaller scale. It is well-suited for container gardening due to its compact size and ornamental value.

This variety is also valued for its ability to thrive in a variety of climates, making it a versatile choice for both ornamental and edible purposes.
