Phyllanthus balansanus var. glaber

Phyllanthus balansanus var. glaber, commonly known as the smooth phyllanthus, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically glabrous, meaning they are smooth and lack hair. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by spherical, fleshy fruits. It is often found in dry, disturbed habitats and is adapted to thrive in arid conditions.

The species is valued for its ornamental potential and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
