Polygala subamara

Polygala subamara, commonly known as the slender milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae, which includes a variety of herbs and shrubs. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to dry conditions. Its flowers are small and often clustered, with distinctive features that attract specific pollinators.
