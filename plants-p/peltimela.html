Peltimela

Peltimela, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant features showy, daisy-like blooms with yellow centers and white to pink petals. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette.

Peltimela is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in dry, well-drained soils.
