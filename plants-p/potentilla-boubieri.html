Potentilla boubieri

Potentilla boubieri, commonly known as the Chinese firecracker, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a red or orange center. The plant has a compact growth habit with upright stems and dense foliage. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Potentilla boubieri is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.
