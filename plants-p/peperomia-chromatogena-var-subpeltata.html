Peperomia chromatogena var. subpeltata

Peperomia chromatogena var. subpeltata, commonly known as the Variegated Peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features small, waxy leaves with striking variegation of white, yellow, and green, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant has a short, upright stem and produces small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and decorative purposes.
