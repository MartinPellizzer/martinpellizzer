Phyllostachys castillonii var. inversa

Phyllostachys castillonii var. inversa, commonly known as the reverse bamboo, is a unique variant of the bamboo genus Phyllostachys.

It is characterized by its striking reverse coloration, where the culms (stems) display a contrasting hue to the typical green of most bamboos. This variety is native to China and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. Its foliage is dense and often exhibits a silvery or bluish tint, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Due to its distinctive appearance, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its aesthetic value.
