Pterostylis lustra

Pterostylis lustra, commonly known as the glossy helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests and understoreys. The plant features a slender, leafless stem with a single, showy, greenish-yellow flower that resembles a helmet. Its inflorescence consists of a single resupinate flower with a distinctive, elongated lip.

Pterostylis lustra is notable for its specialized pollination mechanism, relying on specific insect species for reproduction.
