Piper recessum

Piper recessum, commonly known as the dwarf pepper plant, is a member of the Piperaceae family.

It is a small shrub native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and emits a mild, peppery scent. Its small green flowers are followed by dark purple berries that contain the aromatic seeds.

Due to its unique appearance and fragrance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
