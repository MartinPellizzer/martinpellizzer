Philodendron pipolyi

Philodendron pipolyi, commonly known as the Mexican evergreen philodendron, is a climbing tropical plant native to Central America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves that are often adorned with prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Philodendron pipolyi is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
