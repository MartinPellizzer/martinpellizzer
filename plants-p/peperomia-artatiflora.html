Peperomia artatiflora

Peperomia artatiflora, commonly known as the laceleaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with a distinctive veined pattern and a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia artatiflora is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
