Piper pumilum

Piper pumilum, commonly known as the dwarf pepper plant, is a small shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various species of pepper plants. The plant typically grows to a height of less than 1 meter and features small, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.

The fruit is a small, berry-like structure that turns from green to red as it matures.
