Pelargonium erectum

Pelargonium erectum, commonly known as the upright geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The leaves are typically rounded or heart-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in garden settings.
