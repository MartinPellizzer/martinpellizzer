Polygala wenxianensis

Polygala wenxianensis, commonly known as Wenxian polygala, is a flowering plant species native to China.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, spiny fruits that contain seeds. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
