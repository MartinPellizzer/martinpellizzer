Philodendron obtusilobum

Philodendron obtusilobum, commonly known as the heartleaf philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or cream patterns, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant has aerial roots that help it climb and attach to supports, and its stems are typically thick and fibrous. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Philodendron obtusilobum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
