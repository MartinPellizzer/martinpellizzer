Piper laxispicum

Piper laxispicum, commonly known as the creeping pepper vine, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its trailing growth habit, with small, opposite leaves that are often glossy and dark green.
