Pilosella macranthela

Pilosella macranthela, commonly known as the showy pearly everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Its foliage is covered in fine, soft hairs, contributing to its common name and aiding in moisture retention.
