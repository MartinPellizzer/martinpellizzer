Pyrostria mandrarensis

Pyrostria mandrarensis, commonly known as the Madagascar palm, is a slow-growing succulent native to Madagascar.

It features a thick, columnar stem with a spiny crown of leaves that resemble a palm. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into red, berry-like fruits. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Pyrostria mandrarensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
