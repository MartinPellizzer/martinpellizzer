Pachynema junceum

It is a herbaceous plant that typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, bogs, and along the edges of ponds. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spike-like clusters, which are usually wind-pollinated.

Pachynema junceum plays a role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
