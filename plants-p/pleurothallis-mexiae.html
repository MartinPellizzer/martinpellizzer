Pleurothallis mexiae

Pleurothallis mexiae, commonly known as the Mexican pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, tropical environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is part of the Pleurothallis genus, which includes many species with similar morphological traits.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique appearance and delicate blooms.
