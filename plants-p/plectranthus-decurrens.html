Plectranthus decurrens

Plectranthus decurrens, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of tubular, violet-blue flowers. The plant is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is popular in gardens and as a houseplant.

Plectranthus decurrens is also valued for its aromatic foliage and ability to attract pollinators.
