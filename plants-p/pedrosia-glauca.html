Pedrosia glauca

Pedrosia glauca, commonly known as the blue pedrosia, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a compact growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure.

Pedrosia glauca is often cultivated in rock gardens due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
