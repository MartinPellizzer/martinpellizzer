Protea dekindtiana

Protea dekindtiana, commonly known as the De Kindt's protea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its striking, cone-shaped inflorescence. The plant features showy, brightly colored bracts that surround the small, true flowers. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections worldwide.
