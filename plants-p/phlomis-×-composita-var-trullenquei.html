Phlomis × composita var. trullenquei

Phlomis × composita var. trullenquei, commonly known as the Trulleni Phlomis, is a hybrid plant belonging to the mint family, Lamiaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and features narrow, gray-green leaves with a fuzzy texture. The plant produces showy, sparsely branched inflorescences of pink to purple flowers, which bloom in late summer. It is native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe and is often found in rocky or limestone soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to dry, well-drained conditions.
