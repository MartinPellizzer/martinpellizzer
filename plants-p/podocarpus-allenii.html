Podocarpus allenii

Podocarpus allenii, commonly known as the dwarf podocarp, is a small evergreen tree native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Podocarpaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and conical shape. The plant produces small, scale-like leaves and has a unique reproductive system involving both male and female cones. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and montane regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and used in horticultural landscapes.
