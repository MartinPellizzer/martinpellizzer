Paeonia × yunnanensis

Paeonia × yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan peony, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant produces compound, trifoliate leaves with serrated margins. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

The Yunnan peony is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
