Pleurothallis caniceps

Pleurothallis caniceps, commonly known as the golden pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces delicate, pendulous flowers that are usually yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its roots are often covered in a dense, white mycorrhizal coating, which aids in nutrient absorption.

Pleurothallis caniceps is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
