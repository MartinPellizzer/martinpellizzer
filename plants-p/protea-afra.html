Protea afra

Protea afra, commonly known as the African protea, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences composed of numerous white to pink flower heads. The plant produces nectar-rich flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and birds. Its leaves are leathery and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems.

Protea afra thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
