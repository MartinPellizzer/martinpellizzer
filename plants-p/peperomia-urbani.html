Peperomia urbani

Peperomia urbani, commonly known as the urban peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions.

It features dense, upright growth with small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale green to red flowers during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Due to its resilience and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for low-maintenance plant collections.
