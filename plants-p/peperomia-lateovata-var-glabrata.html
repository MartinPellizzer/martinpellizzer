Peperomia lateovata var. glabrata

Peperomia lateovata var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth-leaf peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It features ovate to elliptical leaves that are usually smooth and glossy, with a deep green coloration. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or white, which appear during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it an ideal plant for containers or as part of a foliage arrangement.
