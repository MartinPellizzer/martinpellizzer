Pittosporum buxifolium

Pittosporum buxifolium, commonly known as the silver leaf pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and silvery white underneath, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique foliage and seasonal blooms make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
