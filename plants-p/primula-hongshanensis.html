Primula hongshanensis

Primula hongshanensis, commonly known as the Hongshan primrose, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. It has oval, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
