Piper citrifolium

Piper citrifolium, commonly known as the citron leaf pepper, is a tropical climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, citrus-scented leaves and small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant produces berries that turn from green to yellow or orange as they mature. Its aromatic foliage and unique appearance make it a popular ornamental plant.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
