Peperomia hyporhoda

Peperomia hyporhoda, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, velvety foliage and compact growth habit, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. The plant features small, ovate leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. It produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically borne on short spikes.

Peperomia hyporhoda prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, with moderate watering to prevent root rot.
