Philodendron wilburii var. wilburii

Philodendron wilburii var. wilburii, commonly known as the Wilburii Philodendron, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among plant enthusiasts for its striking foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
