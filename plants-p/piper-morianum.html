Piper morianum

Piper morianum, commonly known as the African pepper or Morian's pepper, is a climbing shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by three-lobed, berry-like fruits. Its leaves are ovate with a smooth or slightly hairy surface, and it has a strong, peppery aroma.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and used in traditional medicine.
