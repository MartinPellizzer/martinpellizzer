Peperomia cladara

Peperomia cladara, commonly known as the Shiny Leaf Peperomia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. The plant has a compact growth habit, making it ideal for indoor cultivation. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia cladara is known for its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments.
