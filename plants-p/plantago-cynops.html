Plantago cynops

Plantago cynops, commonly known as greater plantain, is a perennial herb native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It features a robust, upright stem with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in dense clusters at the top of the stem. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their soothing and healing properties.

The plant is also a common weed in cultivated areas and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
