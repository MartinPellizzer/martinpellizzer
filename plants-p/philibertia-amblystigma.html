Philibertia amblystigma

Philibertia amblystigma, commonly known as the narrow-leaved filbert, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring. It is often found in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid conditions.
