Plectranthus koualensis

Plectranthus koualensis, commonly known as the Cameroon plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to western Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture and produces small, tubular flowers in shades of purple and blue. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.

Plectranthus koualensis thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.
