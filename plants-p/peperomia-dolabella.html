Peperomia dolabella

Peperomia dolabella, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to Central and South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a striking silvery-white underside, giving it a unique, almost metallic appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that emerge from the axils of the leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia dolabella is a low-maintenance plant that is well-suited for indoor cultivation.
