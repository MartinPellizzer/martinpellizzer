Pastinaca atropurpurea

Pastinaca atropurpurea, commonly known as purple top turnip, is a biennial plant belonging to theApiaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive purple stems and leaves, which are often used in ornamental gardening. The plant produces yellow flowers in umbels, typical of the carrot family. Its roots are typically small and less edible compared to other members of the genus.
