Primula merrilliana

Primula merrilliana, commonly known as the Hainan primrose, is a flowering plant native to the island of Hainan in China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are broadly ovate with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy surface.

Primula merrilliana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
