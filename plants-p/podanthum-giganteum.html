Podanthum giganteum

Podanthum giganteum, commonly known as the giant podanthum, is a striking member of the Gesneriaceae family native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with contrasting white throats. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its lush appearance.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
