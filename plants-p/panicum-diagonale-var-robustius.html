Panicum diagonale var. robustius

Panicum diagonale var. robustius, commonly known as stiff panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, tufted foliage. The plant produces panicles of spikelets that are typically greenish or purplish in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in open woodlands and grassy areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens and restoration projects.
