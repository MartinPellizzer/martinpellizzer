Paeonia × papaveracea

Paeonia × papaveracea, commonly known as the Chinese peony or peony hybrid, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the芍药属 (Paeonia) genus and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, with compound leaves divided into three to five leaflets. Its botanical features include a rhizomatous root system and flowers that bloom in early to mid-spring.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
