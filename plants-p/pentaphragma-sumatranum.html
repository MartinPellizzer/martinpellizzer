Pentaphragma sumatranum

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It typically grows in wetland or marshy habitats, preferring moist and shaded environments.
