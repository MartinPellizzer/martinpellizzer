Pelargonium schoenlandii

Pelargonium schoenlandii, commonly known as the Cape geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range from pink to red. The plant features lobed, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets and containers.

Pelargonium schoenlandii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
