Petrea kohautiana f. alba

Petrea kohautiana f. alba, commonly known as the white-flowered puka, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
