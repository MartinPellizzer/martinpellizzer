Periptera macrantha

Periptera macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered periptera, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has opposite, oval-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a fragrant aroma. Its flowers are typically borne in inflorescences at the ends of branches, making it visually striking.

Periptera macrantha is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
