Peperomia caudulilimba var. longependula

Peperomia caudulilimba var. longependula, commonly known as the hanging peperomia, is a unique species characterized by its trailing stems and elongated, pendulous leaves.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among indoor gardeners for its compact size and attractive foliage.
