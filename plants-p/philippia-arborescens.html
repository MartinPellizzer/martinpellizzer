Philippia arborescens

Philippia arborescens, commonly known as the tree philippia, is a species of flowering plant in the Apocynaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly found in regions of Brazil, Peru, and Argentina. Philippia arborescens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
