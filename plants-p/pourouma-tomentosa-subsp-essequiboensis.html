Pourouma tomentosa subsp. essequiboensis

Pourouma tomentosa subsp.

essequiboensis, commonly known as the Essequibo river bamboo, is a member of the Marantaceae family. It is a clump-forming, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet, tropical environments.
