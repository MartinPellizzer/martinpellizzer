Polygala rehmannii

Polygala rehmannii, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. Its leaves are often trifoliate, and it produces pods that contain small, round seeds.

Polygala rehmannii is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
