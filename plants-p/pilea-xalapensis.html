Pilea xalapensis

Pilea xalapensis, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to Mexico.

It features round, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slight variegation, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact growth habit and low maintenance requirements make it an ideal plant for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
