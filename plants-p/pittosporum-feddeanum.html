Pittosporum feddeanum

Pittosporum feddeanum, commonly known as the New Zealand pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery bloom. The plant produces small, white or cream-colored flowers that are followed by bright orange or red berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

Pittosporum feddeanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and floral display.
