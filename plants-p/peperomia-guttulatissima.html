Peperomia guttulatissima

Peperomia guttulatissima, commonly known as the spotted peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with distinctive white or pale spots, giving it a unique visual appeal. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia guttulatissima is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
