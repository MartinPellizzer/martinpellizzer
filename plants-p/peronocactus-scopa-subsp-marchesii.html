Peronocactus scopa subsp. marchesii

Peronocactus scopa subsp.

marchesii, commonly known as the Marchesi's hedgehog cactus, is a succulent native to Mexico. It features a short, cylindrical stem with prominent areoles and spines, typical of the Cactaceae family.

The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring, adding ornamental value. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

This subspecies is often cultivated for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
