Phymatopteris crenatopinnata

Phymatopteris crenatopinnata, commonly known as the crenate frond fern, is a species of fern native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.
