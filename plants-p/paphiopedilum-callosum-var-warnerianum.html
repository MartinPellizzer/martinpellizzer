Paphiopedilum callosum var. warnerianum

Paphiopedilum callosum var. warnerianum, commonly known as the Warner's Ghost Orchid, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, long-lasting flowers that are typically pale green to yellow with intricate patterns. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in shaded, moist environments. Its leaves are elongated and glossy, often arranged in a fan-like pattern.

This variety is highly valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and challenging cultivation requirements.
