Proustia angustifolia var. mollis

Proustia angustifolia var. mollis, commonly known as the soft-leaved narrowleaf proustia, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are soft to the touch, giving it its varietal name. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a desirable addition to native plant gardens.
