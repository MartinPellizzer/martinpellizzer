Peperomia dotana

Peperomia dotana, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance houseplant native to tropical regions.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery-white underside, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has upright, branching stems and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or green. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, requiring minimal watering.

Peperomia dotana is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive indoors with proper care.
