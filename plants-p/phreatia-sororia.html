Phreatia sororia

Phreatia sororia, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pale green leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and rain.
