Polygonoides

Polygonoides, commonly known as the false nettle, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Urticaceae.

These plants are characterized by their opposite leaves, often with a hairy texture, and small, inconspicuous flowers. The genus includes several species, some of which are used in traditional medicine or as ornamental plants.
