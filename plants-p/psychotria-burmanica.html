Psychotria burmanica

Psychotria burmanica, commonly known as Burmese Psychotria, is a species of flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Myanmar, Thailand, and Vietnam. The plant is characterized by its elliptic to ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly pubescent surface. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, with prominent yellow stamens.
