Pleurothallis amesiana

Pleurothallis amesiana, commonly known as the Ames's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant grows as an epiphyte, often found on trees in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrate.

Pleurothallis amesiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
