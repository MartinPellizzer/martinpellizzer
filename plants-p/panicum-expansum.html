Panicum expansum

Panicum expansum, commonly known as the broadleaf panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and wide, blade-like leaves that are often hairy along the edges. The plant produces fluffy, seed-bearing spikes that are typically yellowish to reddish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open areas such as fields and disturbed sites.

Panicum expansum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
