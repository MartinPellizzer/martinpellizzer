Peperomia kyimbilana

Peperomia kyimbilana, commonly known as the Congo peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Central Africa.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and ability to thrive in low-light conditions. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.

Peperomia kyimbilana prefers well-draining soil and moderate humidity for optimal growth.
