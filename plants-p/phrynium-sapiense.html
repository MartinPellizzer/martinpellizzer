Phrynium sapiense

Phrynium sapiense, commonly known as the giant arrow leaf, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces clusters of yellowish-green flowers that are often followed by spiky, red berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Araceae, which includes other ornamental and economically important plants.
