Pennisetum ruppellii

Pennisetum ruppellii, commonly known as purple top grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that grow up to 6 feet in height, adorned with vibrant purple-tinted leaves that turn reddish in the fall. The plant produces fluffy, silver-gray seed heads that add visual interest throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Pennisetum ruppellii is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
