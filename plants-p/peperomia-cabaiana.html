Peperomia cabaiana

Peperomia cabaiana, commonly known as the Cabaiana pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features succulent, dark green leaves with a slightly waxy texture and a distinctive, narrow shape. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Peperomia cabaiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and air-purifying qualities.
