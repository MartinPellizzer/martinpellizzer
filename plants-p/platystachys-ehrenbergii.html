Platystachys ehrenbergii

Platystachys ehrenbergii, commonly known as the silverleaf honeysuckle, is a shrub native to southern China.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a leathery texture.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
