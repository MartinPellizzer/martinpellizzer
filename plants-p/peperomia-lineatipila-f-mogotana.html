Peperomia lineatipila f. mogotana

Peperomia lineatipila f. mogotana, commonly known as the Mogotana peperomia, is a compact, trailing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with a striking dark green color and prominent, wavy veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental foliage, it is often used as a decorative plant in hanging baskets or as a ground cover.
