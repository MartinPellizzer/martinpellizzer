Pseudostellaria heterantha var. heterantha

Pseudostellaria heterantha var. heterantha, commonly known as the heterantha starwort, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes. The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
