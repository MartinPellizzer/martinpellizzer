Peperomia opaca

Peperomia opaca, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green, velvety leaves that are often heart-shaped or ovate in form. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that are typically borne in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to both novice and experienced plant collectors.
