Peperomia dauleana

Peperomia dauleana, commonly known as the Dauleana Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Andean regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate to lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that appear in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia dauleana is prized for its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
