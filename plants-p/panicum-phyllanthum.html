Panicum phyllanthum

Panicum phyllanthum, commonly known as the silverleaf panicum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and silver-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in panicles, which are often clustered at the tips of the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Due to its drought tolerance, it is also used in xeriscaping and erosion control in arid environments.
