Phyllagathis xanthosticta

Phyllagathis xanthosticta, commonly known as the yellow-streaked piper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded forest understories, where it thrives in humid and warm conditions.

The fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that contains a single seed, and the plant is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
