Polygala leucothyrsa

Polygala leucothyrsa, commonly known as the white-veined milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery foliage and small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. It is often found in open grasslands and along riverbanks, where it contributes to local biodiversity.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
