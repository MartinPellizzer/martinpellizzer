Poa recta

Poa recta, commonly known as upright bentgrass, is a species of grass native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial plant that forms dense, mat-like growth patterns. The leaves are narrow, grass-like, and often have a bluish-green hue. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Poa recta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and turfgrass management.
