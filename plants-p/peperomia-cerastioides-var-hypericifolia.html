Peperomia cerastioides var. hypericifolia

Peperomia cerastioides var. hypericifolia, commonly known as the variegated peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of white, yellow, and green, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its slow growth rate and low maintenance requirements make it ideal for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
