Praxelis asperulacea

Praxelis asperulacea, commonly known as the roughleaf praxelis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are covered in fine hairs, giving them a rough texture.

It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the spring and summer months.
