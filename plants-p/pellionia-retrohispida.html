Pellionia retrohispida

Pellionia retrohispida, commonly known as the silver velvetleaf, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a houseplant.
