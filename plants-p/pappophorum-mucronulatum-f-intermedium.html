Pappophorum mucronulatum f. intermedium

Pappophorum mucronulatum f. intermedium, commonly known as the intermediate short-pedicelled grass, is a variant of the species Pappophorum mucronulatum.

It is a low-growing perennial grass that typically forms dense mats or tufts. The plant is characterized by its short, compressed stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

This plant is often found in dry, open habitats such as rocky slopes and sandy soils.
