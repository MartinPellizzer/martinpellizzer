Picea pungens var. compacta

Picea pungens var. compacta, commonly known as the compact form of the blue spruce, is a variant of the blue spruce native to the United States.

It is characterized by its dense, pyramidal shape and silvery-blue needles that provide a striking visual contrast. The plant is evergreen, with needle-like leaves that are short and stiff, contributing to its compact appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its cold hardiness and slow growth rate make it suitable for smaller garden spaces.
