Pauletia racemosa

Pauletia racemosa, commonly known as the clustered pauletia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apocynaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. Pauletia racemosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
