Pharbitis polymorpha

Pharbitis polymorpha, commonly known as morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiral-shaped seed pods and vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that open in the morning. The plant belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is known for its rapid growth and climbing habit. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a velvety texture.
