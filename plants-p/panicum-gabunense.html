Panicum gabunense

Panicum gabunense, commonly known as Gabon grass, is a species of grass native to Central Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers.

Botanically, it is part of the Poaceae family and is valued for its resilience and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
