Phlomis samia subsp. maroccana

Phlomis samia subsp.

maroccana, commonly known as Moroccan tarragon, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features upright stems covered in fine, silky hairs and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly aromatic scent.

The plant produces showy, pale yellow to golden flower clusters in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological gardens.
