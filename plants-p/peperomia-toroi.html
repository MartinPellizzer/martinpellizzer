Peperomia toroi

Peperomia toroi, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically silver or grayish-white in color, with a textured surface that gives it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white, followed by bright red berries. It is a popular houseplant due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia toroi is well-suited for indoor environments and is often used as a decorative foliage plant.
