Pilosella vansoestii subsp. megavasconica

Pilosella vansoestii subsp.

megavasconica, commonly known as the giant hairgrass, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies.
