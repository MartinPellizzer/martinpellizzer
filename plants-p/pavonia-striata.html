Pavonia striata

Pavonia striata, commonly known as the scarlet pavonia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a perennial shrub that produces showy, bright red or pink bract-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage is dark green and often has a slightly hairy texture.

Pavonia striata is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
