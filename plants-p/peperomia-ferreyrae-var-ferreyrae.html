Peperomia ferreyrae var. ferreyrae

Peperomia ferreyrae var. ferreyrae, commonly known as the Ferreyra's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
