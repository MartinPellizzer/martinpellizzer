Pyrrosia stellata

Pyrrosia stellata, commonly known as the star fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped fronds that give it its common name. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually bright green and have a leathery texture.

Pyrrosia stellata is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of care.
