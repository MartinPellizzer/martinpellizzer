Pternopetalum radiatum

Pternopetalum radiatum, commonly known as the golden pteropetalum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and showy, yellowish-white flowers. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a leathery texture with a fragrant scent.
