Polygonum dichotomum var. robustum

Polygonum dichotomum var. robustum, commonly known as the giant knotweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Asia.

It features upright, branched stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant has heart-shaped, alternate leaves with a rough texture and prominent veins. Its small, greenish-white flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in late summer.

It is known for its rapid growth and can become invasive in certain environments.
