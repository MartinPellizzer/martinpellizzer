Peperomia garcia-barrigana

Peperomia garcia-barrigana, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with a distinctive silvery-white underside, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that emerge from the leaf axils. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Peperomia garcia-barrigana is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in humid environments.
