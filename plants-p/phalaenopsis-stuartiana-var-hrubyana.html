Phalaenopsis stuartiana var. hrubyana

Phalaenopsis stuartiana var. hrubyana, commonly known as the Hruby's phalaenopsis, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically blooms multiple times a year, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. It prefers bright, indirect light and consistent humidity.

Its striking appearance and ease of care contribute to its popularity among orchid enthusiasts.
