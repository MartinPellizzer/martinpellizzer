Philodendron krugii

Philodendron krugii, commonly known as the Krug's philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves with prominent veins, and it produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil. It is often cultivated for its attractive foliage and is relatively easy to care for in a humid environment.

Philodendron krugii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.
