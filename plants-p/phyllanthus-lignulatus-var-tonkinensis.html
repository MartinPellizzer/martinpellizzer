Phyllanthus lignulatus var. tonkinensis

Phyllanthus lignulatus var. tonkinensis, commonly known as Vietnamese milkweed, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, well-drained soils.

The species is noted for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
