Polygonum sagittatum var. sieboldii

Polygonum sagittatum var. sieboldii, commonly known as the Siebold's arrowhead, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and have a distinctive arrowhead shape. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along streambanks.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in wetland habitats.
