Phlomis knorringiana

Phlomis knorringiana, commonly known as Knorring's blazingstar, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features tall, erect stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height, covered with soft, hairy foliage. The plant produces showy, spiky flower clusters in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture, while its root system is deep and fibrous.

This species is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient plant in harsh conditions.
