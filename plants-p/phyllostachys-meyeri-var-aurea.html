Phyllostachys meyeri var. aurea

Phyllostachys meyeri var. aurea, commonly known as the golden bamboo, is a variant of the Meyer bamboo species.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow foliage, which contrasts beautifully with its green stems. This plant is a clumping bamboo that grows in dense, upright clusters, making it ideal for ornamental landscaping. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth texture and a vibrant color that remains consistent throughout the growing season.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
