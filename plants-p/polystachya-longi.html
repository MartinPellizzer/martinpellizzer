Polystachya longi

Polystachya longi, commonly known as the long-stalked polystachya, is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are typically elongated and strap-like, often with a waxy surface to reduce water loss.

Polystachya longi is valued in horticulture for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
