Pelargonium × medusulum

Pelargonium × medusulum, commonly known as the angel's trumpets, is a hybrid geranium species characterized by its striking, pendulous flowers.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped blooms that range in color from white to deep pink, often with a velvety texture. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, with dark green, glossy leaves and a compact, bushy habit. It is native to South Africa and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a potted plant.
