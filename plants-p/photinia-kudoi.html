Photinia kudoi

Photinia kudoi, commonly known as the redleaf photinia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its attractive, glossy, dark green leaves that turn bright red in the fall. The plant produces small, white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring. It is often cultivated as an ornamental shrub due to its vibrant foliage and compact growth habit.

Photinia kudoi prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
