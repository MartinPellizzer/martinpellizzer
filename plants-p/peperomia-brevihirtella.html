Peperomia brevihirtella

Peperomia brevihirtella, commonly known as the compact peperomia, is a small, compact shrub native to Central and South America.

It features upright, succulent-like stems covered in dense, velvety foliage that ranges from green to reddish hues. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale green, arranged in spikes along the stems. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
