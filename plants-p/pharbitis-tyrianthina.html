Pharbitis tyrianthina

Pharbitis tyrianthina, commonly known as the scarlet runner bean, is a climbing vine native to the Americas.

It produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically bright red or orange in color. The plant has palmate leaves with three to five lobes, and its fruit is a long, cylindrical pod containing edible seeds. It thrives in warm climates and requires full sun for optimal growth.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also cultivated for its nutritious beans.
