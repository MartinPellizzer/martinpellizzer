Pyrrhocactus huascensis

Pyrrhocactus huascensis, commonly known as the Huascensis Cactus, is a species of cactus native to Peru.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in spines. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, adapting to the harsh conditions of its native habitat. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, blooming during the dry season.

Pyrrhocactus huascensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in low-water conditions.
