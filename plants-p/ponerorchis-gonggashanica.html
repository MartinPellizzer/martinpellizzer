Ponerorchis gonggashanica

Ponerorchis gonggashanica, commonly known as the Gonggashan orchid, is a rare and endemic species found in the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and specialized growth habits. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often on rocky substrates or in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually small and delicate, with a complex arrangement of petals and sepals.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species, highlighting the importance of conservation efforts.
