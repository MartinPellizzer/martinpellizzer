Paphiopedilum liemianum

Paphiopedilum liemianum, commonly known as the Liemian orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive lip. The plant has a sympodial growth habit and prefers shaded, humid environments. Its flowers are typically pale yellow with a deep orange or red lip, and it blooms in late spring to early summer.

It is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
