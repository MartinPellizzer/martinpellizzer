Pilosella hypoleuca

Pilosella hypoleuca, commonly known as the whitehead mouse-ear, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Its leaves are usually grayish-green and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its common name.

Pilosella hypoleuca is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
