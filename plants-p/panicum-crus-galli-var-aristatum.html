Panicum crus-galli var. aristatum

Panicum crus-galli var. aristatum, commonly known as purple lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and fluffy, purple-tinted seed heads that appear in late summer. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in open fields and along roadsides. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that becomes more vibrant in the autumn.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
