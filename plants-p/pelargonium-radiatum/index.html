Pelargonium Radiatum

Pelargonium radiatum is a striking plant known for its vibrant, showy flowers and unique foliage. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in warm, sunny conditions and is often cultivated as a decorative garden plant. Its leaves are deeply lobed and often have a velvety texture, adding visual interest to any landscape. The plant produces clusters of small, colorful blooms that range from pink to red, depending on the variety. Due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements, Pelargonium radiatum is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
