Pediocactus bradyi

Pediocactus bradyi, commonly known as the Brady's cactus, is a species native to the Chihuahuan Desert in Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed structure and spiny areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It has a shallow root system adapted to arid conditions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat threats, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
