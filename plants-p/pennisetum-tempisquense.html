Pennisetum tempisquense

Pennisetum tempisquense, commonly known as giant feather grass, is a tall, ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features long, narrow, silver-blue leaves that give it a distinctive, feathery appearance. The plant is known for its striking, upright growth habit and is often used in landscaping for its visual appeal. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Pennisetum tempisquense is also valued for its ability to attract birds and other wildlife.
