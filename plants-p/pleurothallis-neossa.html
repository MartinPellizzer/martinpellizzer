Pleurothallis neossa

Pleurothallis neossa, commonly known as the Neossa orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering stems. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Pleurothallis neossa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
