Pyrrocoma insecticruris

Pyrrocoma insecticruris, commonly known as the insect-legged pyrrocoma, is a unique plant species characterized by its striking, intricate floral structure.

It features a tall, slender stem with clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. The plant's most distinctive feature is its unusual inflorescence, which resembles the legs of an insect, giving it its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in temperate regions with cool, humid climates.

Due to its rarity and specialized habitat requirements, it is considered a delicate and ecologically significant species.
