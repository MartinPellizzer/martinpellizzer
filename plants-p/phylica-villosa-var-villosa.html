Phylica villosa var. villosa

Phylica villosa var. villosa, commonly known as the silky phylica, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and Mediterranean climates.

Its ornamental value and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
