Potentilla argentea subsp. macrotoma

Potentilla argentea subsp.

macrotoma, commonly known as silver cinquefoil, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is characterized by its silver-white undersides on the leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in meadows and rocky habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
