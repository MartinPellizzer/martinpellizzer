Peperomia rubrimaculata

Peperomia rubrimaculata, commonly known as the red-spotted peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features oval, waxy leaves with a distinctive red or reddish-brown spotting on the upper surface, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that emerge from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia rubrimaculata is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and ornamental foliage.
