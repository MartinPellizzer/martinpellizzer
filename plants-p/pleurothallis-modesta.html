Pleurothallis modesta

Pleurothallis modesta, commonly known as the modest orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and green. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that help it conserve moisture in its natural habitat.

Pleurothallis modesta is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by enthusiasts of rare orchids.
