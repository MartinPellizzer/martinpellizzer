Pentameris pictigluma var. gracilis

Pentameris pictigluma var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, flame-like orange-red bracts that surround its small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and indoor cultivation.
