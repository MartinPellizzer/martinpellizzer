Pyrethrum scopulorum

Pyrethrum scopulorum, commonly known as the rock pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings. Its blooms are typically short-lived, lasting only a few days, but they attract a variety of pollinators.

Pyrethrum scopulorum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
