Paraserianthes

Paraserianthes, commonly known as the flame tree or bracken fern tree, is a genus of flowering plants in the legume family, Fabaceae.

The leaves are typically compound, with small, oval-shaped leaflets, and the trees can grow quite tall, reaching up to 20 meters in height. Paraserianthes species are often used in ornamental horticulture due to their striking floral displays and adaptability to various soil types.
