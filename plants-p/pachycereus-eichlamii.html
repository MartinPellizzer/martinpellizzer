Pachycereus eichlamii

Pachycereus eichlamii, commonly known as the Eichler's prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its robust, cylindrical stems that are covered in spiny areoles. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its fruit, known as a tuna, is edible and has a sweet, tangy flavor.

Pachycereus eichlamii is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
