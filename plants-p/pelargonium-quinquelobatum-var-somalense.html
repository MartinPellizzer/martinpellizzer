Pelargonium quinquelobatum var. somalense

Pelargonium quinquelobatum var. somalense, commonly known as the Somali geranium, is a flowering plant native to the Horn of Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand dry conditions.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a notable species within the Geraniaceae family.
