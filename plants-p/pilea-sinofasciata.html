Pilea sinofasciata

Pilea sinofasciata, commonly known as the Chinese money plant or silver dollar plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Urticaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its round, waxy leaves that resemble silver dollars. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. It is a popular houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.

Pilea sinofasciata prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.
