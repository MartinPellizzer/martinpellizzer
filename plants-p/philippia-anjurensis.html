Philippia anjurensis

Philippia anjurensis, commonly known as the Anjurensis Philodendron, is a species of flowering plant in the Araceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It produces striking inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe.

Philippia anjurensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ease of care in indoor settings.
