Peperomia cacuminicola

Peperomia cacuminicola, commonly known as the pink peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with a pink or reddish hue, particularly along the edges. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or red, adding a subtle ornamental appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia cacuminicola is prized for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
