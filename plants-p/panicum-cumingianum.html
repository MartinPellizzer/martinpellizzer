Panicum cumingianum

Panicum cumingianum, commonly known as Cuming's panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and feathery, panicle-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slightly rough texture.

It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its distinctive appearance and adaptability to various soil conditions.
