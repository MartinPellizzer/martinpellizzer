Pittosporum perryanum var. linearifolium

Pittosporum perryanum var. linearifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery-white pubescence. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that are typically borne in clusters, followed by small, red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
