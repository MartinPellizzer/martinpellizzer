Pavonia piauhyensis

Pavonia piauhyensis, commonly known as the Piauhy pavonia, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters, with pinnately compound leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
