Pleuropetalum sprucei

Pleuropetalum sprucei, commonly known as the Himalayan bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

Its leaves are lanceolate and covered with a dense layer of fine hairs, which help protect it from harsh environmental conditions.

Pleuropetalum sprucei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in temperate climates.
