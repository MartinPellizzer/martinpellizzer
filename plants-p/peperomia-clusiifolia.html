Peperomia clusiifolia

Peperomia clusiifolia, commonly known as the flame peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish or purplish tinge on the underside, giving it a striking visual appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in moderate light conditions.

Peperomia clusiifolia is valued for its ornamental foliage and air-purifying qualities.
