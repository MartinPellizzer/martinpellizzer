Peperomia conulifera var. tenuispica

Peperomia conulifera var. tenuispica, commonly known as the narrow-leaved peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, dark green foliage with narrow, slightly curled leaves that give it a unique, ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate moisture.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container cultivation.
