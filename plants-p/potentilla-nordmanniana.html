Potentilla nordmanniana

Potentilla nordmanniana, commonly known as the Nordmann's cinquefoil, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, yellow, five-petaled flowers. The plant has finely divided, feathery leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Potentilla nordmanniana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.
