Pelargonium × revolutiflorum

Pelargonium × revolutiflorum, commonly known as the showy geranium, is a hybrid species within the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features rounded, velvety leaves with a distinctive waxy coating. Native to South Africa, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a potted plant.
