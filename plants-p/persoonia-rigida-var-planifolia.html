Persoonia rigida var. planifolia

Persoonia rigida var. planifolia, commonly known as the flat-leaved rigid persoonia, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are often arranged in a spiral pattern along its stems. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland heathlands.

The species is noted for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
