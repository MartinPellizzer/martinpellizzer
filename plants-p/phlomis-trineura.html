Phlomis trineura

Phlomis trineura, commonly known as the Narrowleaf Heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pale blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its aromatic foliage and showy flowers make it an attractive addition to gardens.
