Pyrus simonsii

Pyrus simonsii, commonly known as the European whitebeam, is a deciduous tree native to Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its small, white flowers that bloom in spring. The tree produces small, edible berries that are typically yellow or red in color. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a serrated edge.

Pyrus simonsii is often found in woodlands and is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits.
