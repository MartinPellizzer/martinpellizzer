Phyllanthus rosmarinifolius

Phyllanthus rosmarinifolius, commonly known as lemon leaf or lemon thyme, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, lemon-scented leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, yellow flowers and is known for its resilience in arid conditions. Its leaves contain essential oils that contribute to its medicinal properties.

It is widely cultivated for both ornamental and therapeutic purposes.
