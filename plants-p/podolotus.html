Podolotus

Podolotus, commonly known as the silver dollar plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with a silvery or blue-gray hue. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Podolotus is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
