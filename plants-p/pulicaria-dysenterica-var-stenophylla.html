Pulicaria dysenterica var. stenophylla

Pulicaria dysenterica var. stenophylla, commonly known as narrow-leaved wormwood, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features grayish-green, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Its aromatic properties and medicinal uses have made it of interest in traditional herbal practices.
