Peperomia chucanebana

Peperomia chucanebana, commonly known as the Chucanebana peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink, giving it an ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, clustered in spikes along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia chucanebana is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
