Primula × bergmansiae

Primula × bergmansiae, commonly known as the Bergman's primrose, is a hybrid species belonging to the Primulaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. It features a compact growth habit with hairy leaves and produces clusters of flowers on long, slender stems.

This ornamental plant is often cultivated for its beauty and is well-suited for rock gardens and shady borders.
