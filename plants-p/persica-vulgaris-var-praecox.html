Persica vulgaris var. praecox

Persica vulgaris var. praecox, commonly known as the early peach.

This variety is a cultivar of the domesticated peach, characterized by its early blooming and fruiting habits. It typically produces small, sweet fruits with a yellow or pinkish hue. The plant is a deciduous shrub or small tree with simple, alternate leaves and showy pink or white flowers.

It thrives in temperate climates and is valued for its ornamental and edible qualities.
