Potentilla erecta f. sciaphila

Potentilla erecta f. sciaphila, commonly known as the shade-loving potentilla, is a compact, low-growing shrub native to shaded forest environments.

It features small, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape and have a smooth margin. The plant produces clusters of delicate, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often used in garden settings where other plants struggle in low-light conditions.
