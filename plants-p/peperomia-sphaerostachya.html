Peperomia sphaerostachya

Peperomia sphaerostachya, commonly known as the globe peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color, followed by spherical, fleshy fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia sphaerostachya is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
