Peperomia puberuliformis

Peperomia puberuliformis, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, waxy foliage with a distinctive velvety texture, giving it its common name. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape and often display a rich green color with subtle variegation. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale green, which are borne on short, upright spikes.

This plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance for low light and minimal water requirements.
