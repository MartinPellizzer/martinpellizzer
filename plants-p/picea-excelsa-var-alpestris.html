Picea excelsa var. alpestris

Picea excelsa var. alpestris, commonly known as the alpine white spruce, is a variant of the white spruce native to high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It is a coniferous evergreen tree characterized by its slender, pyramidal shape and silver-blue foliage. The leaves are needle-like, short, and often covered with a whitish bloom, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in cold, moist environments and is adapted to withstand harsh alpine conditions.

This plant plays a significant role in the ecological balance of its native habitats.
