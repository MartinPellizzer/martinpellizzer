Pistorinia hispanica f. erecta

Pistorinia hispanica f. erecta, commonly known as the upright form of the Spanish pistorinia, is a rare and distinctive plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
