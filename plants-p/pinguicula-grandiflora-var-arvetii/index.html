Pinguicula Grandiflora Var. Arvetii

Pinguicula grandiflora var. arvetii is a unique carnivorous plant native to the alpine regions of southern Europe. It thrives in rocky, nutrient-poor environments where other plants struggle to survive. This variant is distinguished by its large, showy flowers and specialized trapping structures designed to capture tiny insects. Its ability to absorb nutrients through its leaves makes it an important species in its ecosystem. Despite its beauty, it remains a rare and delicate plant requiring specific care to flourish.
