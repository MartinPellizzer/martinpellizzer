Plantago cavaleriei

Plantago cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese plantain, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-like arrangement of broad, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense spikes, typically blooming in the summer months. It is adapted to thrive in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

The species is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
