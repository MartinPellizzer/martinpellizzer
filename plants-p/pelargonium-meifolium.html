Pelargonium meifolium

Pelargonium meifolium, commonly known as the meifolium geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant has serrated, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Pelargonium meifolium is often cultivated as a decorative plant for gardens and indoor spaces.
