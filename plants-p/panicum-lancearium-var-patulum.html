Panicum lancearium var. patulum

Panicum lancearium var. patulum, commonly known as the broadleaf panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically green and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous pale to brownish florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or disturbed areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a common weed in agricultural settings.
