Penelopeia sphaerica

Penelopeia sphaerica, commonly known as the globe-shaped penelopeia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its round, fleshy leaves that form a dense, globular shape. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Penelopeia sphaerica is often cultivated for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
