Pilosella acrothyrsa

Pilosella acrothyrsa, commonly known as the woolly cat's ear, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features dense, woolly white hairs on its leaves and stems, giving it a distinctive appearance. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates.

Pilosella acrothyrsa produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the spring and early summer.
