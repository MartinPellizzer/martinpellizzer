Pausia orientalis

Pausia orientalis, commonly known as the oriental pausia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, clustered in racemes, and range in color from white to pale pink.

Pausia orientalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
