Panicum neurophyllum

Panicum neurophyllum, commonly known as the slender panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. Its flowers are typically pale green to brown, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Panicum neurophyllum is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds.
