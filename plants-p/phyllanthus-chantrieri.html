Phyllanthus chantrieri

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers clustered in cup-like structures called cyathia. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a glossy appearance.
