Potentilla Hirta Var. Divaricata

Potentilla hirta var. divaricata is a lesser-known yet fascinating member of the Rosaceae family. Native to the mountainous regions of central Asia, this plant thrives in harsh, alpine environments. Its distinctive silver-gray foliage and clusters of yellow flowers make it a standout in its natural habitat. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated in horticultural circles. This article explores its ecological significance and potential for cultivation in challenging gardens.
