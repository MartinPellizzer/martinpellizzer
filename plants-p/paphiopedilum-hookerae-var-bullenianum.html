Paphiopedilum hookerae var. bullenianum

Paphiopedilum hookerae var. bullenianum, commonly known as the Bullen's lady's slipper, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the mountainous regions of Thailand.

It features a unique pouch-shaped lip that is a hallmark of the Paphiopedilum genus, often adorned with intricate patterns and a vibrant color palette. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree trunks or branches in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually a striking combination of yellow and green, with a deep orange or red pouch.

Due to its rarity and specific ecological requirements, it is highly sought after in conservation and horticultural circles.
