Phreatia concinna

Phreatia concinna, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees in humid, tropical rainforest environments. The plant features narrow, strap-like leaves and produces pendulous inflorescences with fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in high light conditions and requires consistent moisture and good air circulation.

Phreatia concinna is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
