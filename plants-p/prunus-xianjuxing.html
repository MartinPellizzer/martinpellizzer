Prunus xianjuxing

Prunus xianjuxing, commonly known as the Xianjuxing cherry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its showy, single or double pink to white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has ovate to lanceolate leaves with serrated edges, and its fruits are small, dark red drupes. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and floral display.

Prunus xianjuxing prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes in temperate climates.
