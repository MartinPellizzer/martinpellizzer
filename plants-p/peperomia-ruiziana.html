Peperomia ruiziana

Peperomia ruiziana, commonly known as the Ruiz's pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and glossy, dark green leaves. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color.

Peperomia ruiziana is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
