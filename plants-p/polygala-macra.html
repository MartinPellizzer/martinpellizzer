Polygala macra

Polygala macra, commonly known as the tall milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the genus Polygala, which is part of the family Polygalaceae. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, pinkish to purple flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are alternate and pinnately compound, often with a hairy texture.

Polygala macra is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
