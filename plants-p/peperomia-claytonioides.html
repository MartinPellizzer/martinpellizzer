Peperomia claytonioides

Peperomia claytonioides, commonly known as the silver mitten plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a silvery-white underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Peperomia claytonioides is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to enhance the aesthetic of indoor spaces.
