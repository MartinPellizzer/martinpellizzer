Podalyria argentea var. racemulosa

Podalyria argentea var. racemulosa, commonly known as silverleaf podalyria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
