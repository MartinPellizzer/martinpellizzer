Peperomia diaphana

Peperomia diaphana, commonly known as the translucent peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features waxy, oval-shaped leaves that are pale green with a translucent, almost glass-like appearance, giving it a unique aesthetic. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and white, which are typically hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
