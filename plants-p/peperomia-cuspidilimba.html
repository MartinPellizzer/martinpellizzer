Peperomia cuspidilimba

Peperomia cuspidilimba, commonly known as the pointed-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with a pointed tip, often displaying a vibrant green coloration. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate moisture.

Peperomia cuspidilimba is appreciated for its ornamental foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
