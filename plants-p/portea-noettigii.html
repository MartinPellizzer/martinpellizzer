It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant features elliptical, leathery leaves with a smooth surface and a distinct midrib. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and typically have five petals.

Portea noettigii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique foliage.
