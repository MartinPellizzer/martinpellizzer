Paeonia albiflora f. pilosella

Paeonia albiflora f. pilosella, commonly known as the white peony with downy foliage, is a cultivar of the peony plant native to Asia.

It is characterized by its pale pink to white flowers with a subtle fragrance, which bloom in early spring. The leaves are covered in a fine, soft down, giving the plant its distinctive appearance. This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and for its ability to thrive in well-drained soil.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters.
