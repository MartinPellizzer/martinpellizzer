Pennisetum imberbe

Pennisetum imberbe, commonly known as fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted clumps of slender, arching blades that create a fountain-like appearance. The plant produces fluffy, white to pale pink seed heads that are highly ornamental and attract birds and insects. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Pennisetum imberbe is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
