Pinalia baniae

Pinalia baniae, commonly known as the banana leaf plant, is a tropical species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad, and glossy green leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and as a decorative element in landscaping.

Pinalia baniae is also valued for its ability to purify the air and enhance the aesthetic appeal of indoor spaces.
