Pittosporum tobira var. calvescens

Pittosporum tobira var. calvescens, commonly known as the Sweet Pittosporum, is a compact evergreen shrub native to New Zealand.

It features fragrant, white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by bright red berries in late summer. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that provide year-round foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
