Polypodium thouarsii

Polypodium thouarsii, commonly known as the tropical wall fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen fronds that often grow on rocky or shaded surfaces. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense mats. Its leaves are typically leathery and have a distinctive, intricate pattern of veins.

Polypodium thouarsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
