Pteris quadriaurita var. major

Pteris quadriaurita var. major, commonly known as the giant water fern, is a fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, pinnately compound fronds that can reach lengths of up to 2 meters. The fronds are typically dark green with a silvery underside, and they have a distinctive, delicate appearance. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found near water bodies.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to purify water.
