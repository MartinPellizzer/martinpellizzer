Pinus elliottii subsp. austrokeysensis

Pinus elliottii subsp.

austrokeysensis, commonly known as the Australian slash pine, is a variant of the slash pine species native to southern Australia. It is a tall, fast-growing evergreen conifer with needle-like leaves and cones that mature to release seeds.

The plant typically thrives in well-drained sandy soils and is adapted to coastal and inland regions. It plays a significant role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and contributing to soil stability.

Its botanical features make it an important species for both ecological and commercial purposes.
