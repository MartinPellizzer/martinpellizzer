Peperomia argyroneura

Peperomia argyroneura, commonly known as the silver-veined peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves with striking silver veins that contrast beautifully with its dark green foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, followed by dark red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia argyroneura is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
