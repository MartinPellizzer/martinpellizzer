Plectranthus wightii

Plectranthus wightii, commonly known as the silverleaf plectranthus, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features succulent, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Plectranthus wightii is also valued for its aromatic foliage and ability to attract pollinators.
