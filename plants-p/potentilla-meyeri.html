Potentilla meyeri

Potentilla meyeri, commonly known as Meyer's cinquefoil, is a low-growing perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from May to July. The plant has slender, woody stems and dark green, serrated leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Potentilla meyeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in colder climates.
