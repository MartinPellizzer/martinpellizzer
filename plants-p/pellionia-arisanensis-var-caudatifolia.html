Pellionia arisanensis var. caudatifolia

Pellionia arisanensis var. caudatifolia, commonly known as the hairy-leafed pellionia, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive trailing growth habit. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value lies in its lush foliage, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
