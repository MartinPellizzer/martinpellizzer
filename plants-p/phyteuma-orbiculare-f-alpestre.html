Phyteuma orbiculare f. alpestre

Phyteuma orbiculare f. alpestre, commonly known as the alpine phyteuma, is a botanical variant of the common phyteuma.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to mountainous regions of Europe. The plant features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are ovate and hairy, adapted to withstand cold and arid conditions.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats.
