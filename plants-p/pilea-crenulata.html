Pilea crenulata

Pilea crenulata, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its round, waxy leaves that resemble small coins, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that bloom in spring. It is a popular indoor plant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.

Pilea crenulata thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
