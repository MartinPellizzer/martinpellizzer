Pelargonium imperiale

Pelargonium imperiale, commonly known as the imperial geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, lobed leaves and showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 60 centimeters. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from spring to autumn.
