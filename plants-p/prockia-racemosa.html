Prockia racemosa

Prockia racemosa, commonly known as the clustered broom.

It is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its dense clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant has slender, branching stems covered in fine, soft hairs. Its leaves are opposite, narrow, and slightly hairy, often with a reddish or purplish hue.

Prockia racemosa is adapted to rocky and dry soils, making it a hardy species often found in coastal and arid environments.
