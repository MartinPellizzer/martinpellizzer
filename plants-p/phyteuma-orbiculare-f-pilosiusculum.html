Phyteuma orbiculare f. pilosiusculum

Phyteuma orbiculare f. pilosiusculum, commonly known as the hairy orbiculate phyteuma, is a horticultural form of the orbiculate phyteuma.

It is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe, characterized by its hairy, cylindrical stems and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The foliage is typically lance-shaped with a rough, textured surface. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its vibrant summer blooms.
