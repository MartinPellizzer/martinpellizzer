Podosemum setosum

Podosemum setosum, commonly known as the spiny pod plant, is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and legume pods.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is often found in open woodlands and savannas. The plant features prominently spined pods that enclose its seeds, providing protection and aiding in seed dispersal. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, with a distinctive violet or purple coloration.

Podosemum setosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
