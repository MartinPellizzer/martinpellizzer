Pennisetum chudeaui

Pennisetum chudeaui, commonly known as the narrowleaf fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height, with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often tinged with silver or blue. The plant produces fluffy, feathery seed heads that are highly ornamental and attract birds and insects. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Pennisetum chudeaui is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
