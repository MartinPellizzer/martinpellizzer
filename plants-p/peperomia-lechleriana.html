Peperomia lechleriana

Peperomia lechleriana, commonly known as the lechleriana peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and red, which are borne on short, erect spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia lechleriana is relatively low-maintenance and suitable for beginners due to its resilience and ease of care.
