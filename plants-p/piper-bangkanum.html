Piper bangkanum

Piper bangkanum, commonly known as the Bangkanum pepper or wild pepper, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, green, berry-like fruits that turn red when mature, containing aromatic seeds. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The botanical characteristics of Piper bangkanum make it an important species in both ecological and medicinal contexts.
