Philadelphus shikokianus var. lancifolius

Philadelphus shikokianus var. lancifolius, commonly known as the Japanese Mock Orange, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its showy flowers and pleasant fragrance.
