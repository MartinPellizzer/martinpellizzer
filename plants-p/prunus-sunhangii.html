Prunus sunhangii

Prunus sunhangii, commonly known as the Sunhang cherry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its delicate pink to white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, sweet, edible fruits that are similar to cherries but have a distinct flavor. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate with a smooth margin.

Prunus sunhangii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its spring blossoms.
