Pycreus elliottianus var. humilis

Pycreus elliottianus var. humilis, commonly known as the Elliott's pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow ray florets and green disc florets. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and is known for its aromatic foliage. It is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

The species is also valued for its potential in horticultural and ornamental use.
