Peperomia emiliana

Peperomia emiliana, commonly known as the emiliana peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, usually in shades of white or pale green. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia emiliana is relatively low maintenance and drought-tolerant, ideal for beginners.
