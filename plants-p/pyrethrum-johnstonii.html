Pyrethrum johnstonii

Pyrethrum johnstonii, commonly known as Johnston's pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant produces small, spiky bracts that surround the flower head, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and heathlands.

Its blooms are typically short-lived but attract a variety of pollinators.
