Phalaenopsis difformis

Phalaenopsis difformis, commonly known as the irregular moth orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, leathery leaves and produces clusters of long, pendulous flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant is known for its relatively long blooming period, often lasting several months. It prefers bright, indirect light and moderate humidity, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique flower shape and color variations make it a striking ornamental plant.
