Peperomia fawcettii

Peperomia fawcettii, commonly known as the Fawcettii Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features thick, waxy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually white or pale green. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia fawcettii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
