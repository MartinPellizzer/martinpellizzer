Pelargonium × hammersleiae

Pelargonium × hammersleiae, commonly known as the ivy-leaved geranium, is a hybrid plant that belongs to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and dark green, heart-shaped leaves that resemble ivy leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, depending on the variety. It is often used in hanging baskets and as a ground cover due to its vigorous growth and ornamental appeal.

Pelargonium × hammersleiae prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
