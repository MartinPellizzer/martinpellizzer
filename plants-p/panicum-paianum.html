Panicum paianum

Panicum paianum, commonly known as the yellow panicgrass, is a grass species native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and produces fluffy, yellowish inflorescences that resemble a panicle. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it adaptable to various environmental conditions. Its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes contribute to its success in disturbed habitats.

Panicum paianum is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
