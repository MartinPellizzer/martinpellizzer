Pseudoyoungia gracilipes

Pseudoyoungia gracilipes, commonly known as the slender leaf orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and small, fragrant flowers. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. The plant typically grows to a height of 30-60 cm, with slender stems and clustered inflorescences.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and conservation efforts.
