Penaea cneorum

Penaea cneorum, commonly known as the Cyprus cypress, is a small evergreen tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its conical shape and scale-like leaves that are dark green on top and pale underneath. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by spherical, blue-gray fruits. Its bark is smooth and reddish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

Penaea cneorum is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
