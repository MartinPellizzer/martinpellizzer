Phrynium jucundum

Phrynium jucundum, commonly known as the Happy Plant, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It features broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically green with a metallic sheen, often adorned with a prominent white or yellow midrib. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow or orange, which bloom in the late afternoon. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sun exposure.

Phrynium jucundum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
