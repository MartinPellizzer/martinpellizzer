Persoonia pinifolia

Persoonia pinifolia, commonly known as the needle-leaved persoonia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in heathlands and open forests.

Persoonia pinifolia is known for its ecological importance, providing habitat and nectar for native pollinators.
