Photinia guerreris

Photinia guerreris, commonly known as the Guerreran rose.

It is a species of shrub or small tree native to Mexico, specifically the state of Guerrero. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, red-bracted inflorescences that appear in spring. It belongs to the Rosaceae family, which includes roses and other flowering plants.

Photinia guerreris is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and floral display.
