Pouteria perrieri

Pouteria perrieri, commonly known as the Perrier's soursop, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and fragrant, white flowers. The tree produces a unique fruit with a spiky exterior and a soft, sweet interior, similar to a soursop. The plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities and potential economic value in the fruit industry.
