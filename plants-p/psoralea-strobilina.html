Psoralea strobilina

Psoralea strobilina, commonly known as the mountain psoralea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, arid environments. Its seed pods are cylindrical and contain several small, rounded seeds.

Psoralea strobilina is often found in coastal and inland regions of southern Australia.
