Pilea clarkei

Pilea clarkei, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rounded, fleshy leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters, followed by red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Its unique appearance and ease of care have made it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
