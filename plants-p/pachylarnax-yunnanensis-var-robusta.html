Pachylarnax yunnanensis var. robusta

Pachylarnax yunnanensis var. robusta, commonly known as the Yunnan robust lichen, is a unique lichen species found in the mountainous regions of Yunnan, China.

It belongs to the family Parmeliaceae and is characterized by its dense, crustose growth form that adheres tightly to rock surfaces. The thallus is typically pale yellow to orange in color, with a smooth and leathery texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found on calcareous substrates.

This lichen is of particular interest to botanists due to its ecological significance and potential medicinal properties.
