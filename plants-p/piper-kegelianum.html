Piper kegelianum

Piper kegelianum, commonly known as Kegelian pepper, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many pepper species. The plant is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of tiny, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruits are small, berry-like structures that mature to a dark purple color.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
