Pediocactus mesae-verdae

Pediocactus mesae-verdae, commonly known as the Sierra de las Nieves cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globose cactus with a compact, spiny body that typically grows to a height of about 10 centimeters. The plant features a short, cylindrical stem covered in white to pale yellow spines. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-yellow, emerging from the areoles.

Pediocactus mesae-verdae is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.
