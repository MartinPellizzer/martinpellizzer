Peperomia rejecta

Peperomia rejecta, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features rosettes of waxy, silver-gray leaves that are slightly hairy, giving it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in late summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia rejecta is ideal for indoor cultivation due to its ability to tolerate low light conditions and minimal watering needs.
