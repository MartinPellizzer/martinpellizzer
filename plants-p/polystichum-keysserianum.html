Polystichum keysserianum

The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found growing in forest understories. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds in clusters called sori, which are protected by a thin, kidney-shaped indusium.

Polystichum keysserianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in shady garden settings.
