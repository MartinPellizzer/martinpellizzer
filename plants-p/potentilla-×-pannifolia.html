Potentilla × pannifolia

Potentilla × pannifolia, commonly known as the Himalayan cinquefoil, is a hybrid shrub native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces showy, yellow to orange flowers in late spring to early summer, which are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for alpine gardens.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies of high-altitude flora.
