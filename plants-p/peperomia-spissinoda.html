Peperomia spissinoda

Peperomia spissinoda, commonly known as the dense-leaved peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are densely packed, giving it a lush, bushy appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia spissinoda is relatively low maintenance and suitable for beginners due to its tolerance for dry conditions.
