Paeonia × suffruticosa var. linyanshanii

Paeonia × suffruticosa var. linyanshanii, commonly known as the Linyi Mountain peony, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its robust growth and thick, woody stems that often branch near the base. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral displays.
