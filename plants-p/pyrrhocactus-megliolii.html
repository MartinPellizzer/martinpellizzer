Pyrrhocactus megliolii

Pyrrhocactus megliolii, commonly known as the red-hair cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, spiny stems that are typically reddish in color. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable plant in botanical collections.
