Poa zhirmunskii

Poa zhirmunskii, commonly known as the Zhirmunskii bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and dense, dark green foliage. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in cooler, temperate climates. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets that are arranged in a compact, cylindrical cluster.

Poa zhirmunskii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping for its hardy and drought-resistant qualities.
