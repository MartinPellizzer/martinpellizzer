Pelargonium autumnale

Pelargonium autumnale, commonly known as the autumn geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with serrated, lobed leaves. Its flowers come in a range of colors, including red, pink, and white, and are borne in dense clusters.

Pelargonium autumnale is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and as a bedding plant.
