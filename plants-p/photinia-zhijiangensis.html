Photinia zhijiangensis

Photinia zhijiangensis, commonly known as the Zhijiang photinia, is a species of shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Its compact growth habit and attractive leaves make it a popular choice for landscaping.
