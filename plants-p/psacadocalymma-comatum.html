Psacadocalymma comatum

Psacadocalymma comatum, commonly known as the slender-leaved pincushion.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth habit and small, clustered flowers. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
