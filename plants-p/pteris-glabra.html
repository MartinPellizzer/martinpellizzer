Pteris glabra

Pteris glabra, commonly known as the smooth water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Pteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds that are typically light green and have a smooth, glossy surface. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions, including parts of Asia, Africa, and the Americas. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found growing near water sources or in humid forests.

Pteris glabra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
