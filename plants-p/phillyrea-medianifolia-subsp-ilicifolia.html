Phillyrea medianifolia subsp. ilicifolia

Phillyrea medianifolia subsp.

ilicifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Phillyrea, is a shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, elliptical, glossy dark green leaves that are usually arranged alternately along the stems.

The plant produces tiny, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, followed by small, leathery fruits that are typically red or orange when mature. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to withstand drought and poor soil conditions.
