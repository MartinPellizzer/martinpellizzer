Phyllagathis tonkinensis

Phyllagathis tonkinensis, commonly known as the Tonkin phyllagathis, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam and southern China. The plant is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a glossy surface with a slightly serrated margin.
