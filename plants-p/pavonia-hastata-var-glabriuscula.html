Pavonia hastata var. glabriuscula

Pavonia hastata var. glabriuscula, commonly known as the smooth pavonia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, featuring upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of showy, red or pink flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its foliage is generally smooth and lacks the pubescence found in other varieties of Pavonia.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.
