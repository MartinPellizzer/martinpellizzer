Podocarpus laubenfelsii

Podocarpus laubenfelsii, commonly known as the Laubenfels's podocarp, is a small evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Podocarpaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, cone-like structures. The plant has needle-like leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around the stem. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland to montane forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
