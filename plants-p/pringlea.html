Pringlea

Pringlea, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage that reflects light and gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. Pringlea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in coastal environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a notable species in New Zealand's flora.
