Phyllanthus pudens

Phyllanthus pudens, commonly known as the small-leaf phyllanthus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Nigeria and Ghana. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and riverbanks.

The species is noted for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various health purposes.
