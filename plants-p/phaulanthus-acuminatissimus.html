Phaulanthus acuminatissimus

Phaulanthus acuminatissimus, commonly known as the narrowleaf phaulanthus, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
