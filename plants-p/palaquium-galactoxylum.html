Palaquium galactoxylum

Palaquium galactoxylum, commonly known as the yellow mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers.
