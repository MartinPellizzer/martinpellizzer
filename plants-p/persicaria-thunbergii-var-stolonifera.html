Persicaria thunbergii var. stolonifera

Persicaria thunbergii var. stolonifera, commonly known as Japanese knotweed, is a vigorous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped, reddish-veined leaves and produces small, pinkish-white flowers in clusters. The plant spreads primarily through rhizomes, making it highly invasive in many regions. Its rapid growth and ability to thrive in various soil types contribute to its ecological impact.

Botanically, it is characterized by its stoloniferous growth habit and distinctive foliage.
