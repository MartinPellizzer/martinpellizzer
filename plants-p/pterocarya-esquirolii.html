Pterocarya esquirolii

Pterocarya esquirolii, commonly known as the Chinese wingnut, is a deciduous tree native to China and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Juglandaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive compound leaves and unique winged fruits. The plant produces large, edible seeds encased in a hard, woody shell. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
