Polygonum × wilmsii

Polygonum × wilmsii, commonly known as the Chinese knotweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Polygonaceae family.

It is characterized by its stout, reddish stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late summer. It is native to East Asia and is often found in moist, shady environments such as stream banks and woodland areas.

Due to its rapid growth and invasive potential, it is sometimes considered a weed in certain regions.
