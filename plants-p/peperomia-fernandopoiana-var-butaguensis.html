Peperomia fernandopoiana var. butaguensis

Peperomia fernandopoiana var. butaguensis, commonly known as the Butaguensian Peperomia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub characterized by its glossy, ovate leaves that are often dark green with a slight reddish tinge. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental appeal and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
