Peperomia madagascariensis

Peperomia madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically oval or elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia madagascariensis is valued for its ornamental foliage and air-purifying qualities.
