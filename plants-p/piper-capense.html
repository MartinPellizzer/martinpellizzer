Piper capense

Piper capense, commonly known as the Cape pepper or African pepper, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a climbing shrub that belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants. The plant produces small, green, berry-like fruits that turn red when mature. These fruits are used in traditional medicine and as a spice due to their pungent flavor.

Its botanical features include opposite, ovate leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers.
