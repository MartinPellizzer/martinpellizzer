Protea baumii subsp. robusta

Protea baumii subsp.

robusta, commonly known as the Robusta Protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family. It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences composed of numerous bright pink to red flowers, which are clustered in a rounded, urn-shaped structure.

The plant typically grows in coastal fynbos vegetation, thriving in well-drained, sandy soils and full sun exposure. Its foliage consists of narrow, leathery leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique and vibrant floral display.
