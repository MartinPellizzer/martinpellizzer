Palala polyspherula

Palala polyspherula, commonly known as the golden globe tree, is a striking member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. Its flowers are small and clustered, often hidden within the leaf axils, and it produces unique, spherical seed pods.

Palala polyspherula is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
