Pyrus densiflora

Pyrus densiflora, commonly known as the Japanese whitebeam, is a deciduous tree native to Japan.

It is characterized by its dense, small, white flowers that bloom in early spring. The tree has small, oval-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and pale underneath. Its fruit is a small, yellowish pome that matures in late summer.

Pyrus densiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in a variety of soil conditions.
