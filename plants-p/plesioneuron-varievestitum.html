Plesioneuron varievestitum

Plesioneuron varievestitum, commonly known as the variegated plesioneuron, is a rare and delicate fern native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery fronds that exhibit a striking variegation of green and cream colors. The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments, typically found in the understory of tropical forests. Its reproductive structures, including spore-bearing sporangia, are located on the undersides of the fronds.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species in its natural range.
