Prunus damascena var. praecox

Prunus damascena var. praecox, commonly known as early sweet cherry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom early in the spring, often before the leaves emerge. The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is cultivated for its edible, sweet fruits. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, and it is relatively hardy in temperate climates.

The variety is notable for its early blooming habit, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.
