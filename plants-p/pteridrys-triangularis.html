Pteridrys triangularis

Pteridrys triangularis, commonly known as the triangular fern, is a species of fern characterized by its distinctive triangular-shaped fronds.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit, with fronds that are usually bipinnate or tripinnate. Its leaves are typically bright green and have a delicate, lace-like appearance.

Pteridrys triangularis thrives in humid environments and is often found in shaded, moist forest understories.
