Peperomia nemoralis

Peperomia nemoralis, commonly known as the creeping peperomia, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features fleshy, ovate leaves that are often glossy and arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia nemoralis is valued for its compact size and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
