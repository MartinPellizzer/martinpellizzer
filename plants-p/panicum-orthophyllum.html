Panicum orthophyllum

Panicum orthophyllum, commonly known as the upright panicgrass, is a species of grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils.

Botanically, it is classified within the Poaceae family, which includes many other grasses.
