Physalis schraderiana

Physalis schraderiana, commonly known as the silverleaf gooseberry, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange berries enclosed in a papery, lantern-like calyx. Native to South America, particularly Brazil, it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant has hairy, lobed leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruits.
