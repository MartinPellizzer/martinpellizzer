Polystichum apicisterile

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and rocky areas. Its spore-bearing structures, called sori, are clustered on the underside of the fronds.
