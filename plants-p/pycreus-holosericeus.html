Pycreus holosericeus

Pycreus holosericeus, commonly known as the white-flowered pycreus, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, white flowers with a subtle fragrance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical rainforests. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, contributing to its overall elegant appearance.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
