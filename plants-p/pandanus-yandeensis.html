Pandanus yandeensis

Pandanus yandeensis, commonly known as the Yande palm, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pandanaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves that are often used in local crafts. Its inflorescence consists of a long, branched spike that bears both male and female flowers.

The plant is valued not only for its ornamental appearance but also for its cultural and practical uses in traditional communities.
