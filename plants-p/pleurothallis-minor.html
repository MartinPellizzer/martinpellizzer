Pleurothallis minor

Pleurothallis minor, commonly known as the miniature pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, linear leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It is part of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is known for its intricate floral structure.

Due to its small size and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
