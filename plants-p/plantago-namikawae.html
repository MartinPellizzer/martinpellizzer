Plantago namikawae

Plantago namikawae, commonly known as the Namikawa's plantain, is a flowering plant species native to Japan.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
