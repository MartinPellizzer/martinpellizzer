Protasparagus aspergillus

Protasparagus aspergillus, commonly known as the rough spiny plant, is a unique member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, succulent stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered white or yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring. It is native to arid and semi-arid regions, where it thrives in well-drained soils.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in xeriscaping and as an ornamental plant in drought-prone areas.
