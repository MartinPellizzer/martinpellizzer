Polygala alba var. brachystachya

Polygala alba var. brachystachya, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, including China and Japan.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, and it produces small, bead-like fruits that contain the seeds.
