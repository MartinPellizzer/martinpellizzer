Phyllostachys robustiramea

Phyllostachys robustiramea, commonly known as the robust bamboo, is a species of grass in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically green and smooth in texture. It is a clump-forming bamboo that is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Due to its rapid growth and adaptability, it is also valued for ecological purposes such as erosion control.
