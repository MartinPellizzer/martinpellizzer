Portulaca repens

Portulaca repens, commonly known as creeping purslane, is a low-growing, succulent plant native to temperate regions.

It features fleshy, rounded leaves and small, vibrant flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a common weed in gardens and disturbed areas. Its stems spread along the ground, forming dense mats that help prevent soil erosion.

Portulaca repens is also valued for its nutritional content, as it contains high levels of omega-3 fatty acids and antioxidants.
