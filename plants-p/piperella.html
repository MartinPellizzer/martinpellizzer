Piperella

Piperella, commonly known as the pepper plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and elongated fruits. The plant produces a variety of peppery fruits, which are often used in traditional cuisines. Its leaves are also utilized in herbal remedies due to their medicinal properties.

Piperella thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
