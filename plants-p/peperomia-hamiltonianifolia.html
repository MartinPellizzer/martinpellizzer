Peperomia hamiltonianifolia

Peperomia hamiltonianifolia, commonly known as the Hamiltonian peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, waxy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces delicate, spathaceous inflorescences that bear tiny, pale green to white flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and ornamental foliage, it is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal rather than its flowers.
