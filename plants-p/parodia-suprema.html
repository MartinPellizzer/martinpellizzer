Parodia suprema

Parodia suprema, commonly known as the Queen of the Night cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its smooth, ribbed stems and spiny areoles. The plant produces large, showy night-blooming flowers that are typically white or pale pink. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its striking appearance and nocturnal flowering habit, it is a popular choice for desert gardens and collections.
