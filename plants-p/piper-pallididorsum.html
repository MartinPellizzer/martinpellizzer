Piper pallididorsum

Piper pallididorsum, commonly known as the pale dorsum pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, opposite leaves. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.

The fruit is a small, three-lobed berry that contains the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine.
