Pilea fairchildiana

Pilea fairchildiana, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features round, fleshy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in spring, followed by small, round berries. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in indirect light.

The plant's unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for houseplants.
