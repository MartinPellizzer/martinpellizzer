Prunus × vavilovii

Prunus × vavilovii, commonly known as the Vavilov plum, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the Caucasus region and is characterized by its ornamental value due to its showy flowers and attractive foliage. The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth texture and a dark green color.

The fruit is a small, yellowish drupe that ripens in late summer, adding to its horticultural appeal.
