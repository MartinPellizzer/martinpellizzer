Penstemon mensarum

Penstemon mensarum, commonly known as the Sierra penstemon, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and dry habitats, such as mountain slopes and meadows. It is a perennial species that produces vibrant blooms in the summer months.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
