Pendusalpinx

Pendusalpinx, commonly known as the hanging alpine plant, is a unique species characterized by its trailing growth habit and delicate, fern-like foliage.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often overlooked but play a role in its reproductive cycle.
