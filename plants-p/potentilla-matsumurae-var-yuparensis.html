Potentilla matsumurae var. yuparensis

Potentilla matsumurae var. yuparensis, commonly known as the Yuparensis cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in South America.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its small, showy yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.
