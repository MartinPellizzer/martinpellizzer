Primula jonardunii

Primula jonardunii, commonly known as the Jonardun primrose, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically blooms in early spring, producing clusters of white or pale pink petals. Its leaves are oval and slightly hairy, adapted to the cold, high-altitude environment.

Primula jonardunii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens and alpine settings.
