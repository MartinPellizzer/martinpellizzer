Paneroa stachyofolia

Paneroa stachyofolia, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems covered in silvery-white hairs, which give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, blue or violet flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough, textured surface.

Paneroa stachyofolia is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and hardy nature.
