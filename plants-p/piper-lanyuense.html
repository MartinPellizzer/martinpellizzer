Piper lanyuense

Piper lanyuense, commonly known as Lanyu pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the island of Lanyu, which is part of the Orchid Island in Taiwan. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by elongated, cylindrical fruits.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and restricted geographic distribution.
