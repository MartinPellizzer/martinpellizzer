Pelargonium formosissimum

Pelargonium formosissimum, commonly known as the Cape marigold, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red flowers and dark green, velvety leaves. The plant is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

The flowers are not only ornamental but also attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
