Platyaechmea dealbata

Platyaechmea dealbata, commonly known as the white-veined giant star.

It is a striking bromeliad native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, broad, waxy leaves that form a rosette, creating a central cup that collects rainwater. Its flowers are typically white or pale green, with intricate, star-shaped petals.

Platyaechmea dealbata thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to flourish.
