Pleurothallis werneri

Pleurothallis werneri, commonly known as the Werneri orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

This epiphytic plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. It features small, delicate flowers that are often white or pale green, with intricate patterns on the petals. The plant has a compact growth habit and is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

It is also notable for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
