Peperomia choroniana

Peperomia choroniana, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, waxy, ovate leaves that are typically green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia choroniana is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and tolerating a range of indoor conditions.
