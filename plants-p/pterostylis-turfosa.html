Pterostylis turfosa

Pterostylis turfosa, commonly known as the Turf Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its small, greenish-brown flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and heathlands. Its leaves are grass-like, and it forms dense clusters near the ground.

The Turf Orchid is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique floral structure and ecological significance.
