Phragmotheca amazonica var. leticiana

Phragmotheca amazonica var. leticiana, commonly known as the Amazonian starflower, is a rare and endemic species found in the dense rainforests of the Amazon Basin.

It is a flowering plant characterized by its striking, star-shaped blooms that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often near water sources such as rivers and swamps. Its leaves are broad and glossy, adapted to capture maximum sunlight in the undergrowth.

The species is noted for its unique floral structure and is of interest to botanists studying tropical flora.
