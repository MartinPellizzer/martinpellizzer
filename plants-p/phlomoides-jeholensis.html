Phlomoides jeholensis

Phlomoides jeholensis, commonly known as the Jehol phlomoides, is a perennial herb native to northern China.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its square stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color.

This species is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.
