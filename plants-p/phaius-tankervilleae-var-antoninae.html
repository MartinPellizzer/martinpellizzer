Phaius tankervilleae var. antoninae

Phaius tankervilleae var. antoninae, commonly known as the Antonina's tiger orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, tiger-striped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange with deep red or brown markings. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or terrestrial orchid, often found in shaded, humid environments. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This variety is prized for its vibrant blooms and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
