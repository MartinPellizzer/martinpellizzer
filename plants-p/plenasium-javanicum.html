Plenasium javanicum

Plenasium javanicum, commonly known as the Java plume or Java fern, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species in both ecological and medicinal contexts.
