Pueraria Javanica

Pueraria javanica is a vigorous climbing legume native to Southeast Asia. It is known for its rapid growth and ability to form dense thickets. The plant produces large, heart-shaped leaves and purple flowers that bloom in summer. It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits. However, it can become invasive if not managed properly.
