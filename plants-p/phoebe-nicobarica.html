Phoebe nicobarica

Phoebe nicobarica, commonly known as the Nicobar phoebe, is a small evergreen tree native to the Andaman and Nicobar Islands.

It typically grows to a height of 10 to 15 meters, with a dense canopy and smooth, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by spherical, fleshy fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland and hill forest areas.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the local flora and ecosystem.
