Paracaryum densum

Paracaryum densum, commonly known as the dense fern, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, feathery fronds that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil. Its reproductive structures, known as sori, are typically found on the underside of the fronds.

Paracaryum densum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
