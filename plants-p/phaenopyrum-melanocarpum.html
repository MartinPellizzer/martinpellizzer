Phaenopyrum melanocarpum

Phaenopyrum melanocarpum, commonly known as the black-fruited phaenopyrum, is a shrub native to the subtropical regions of southern China.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and fruit structure.
