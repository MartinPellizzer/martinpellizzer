Portulaca grandiflora f. aurantiaca

Portulaca grandiflora f. aurantiaca, commonly known as the Orange Moss Rose, is a striking variant of the Moss Rose species.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange flowers that bloom throughout the summer months. The plant is a succulent, featuring fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slightly waxy texture. Native to South Africa, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its compact growth habit and showy blooms make it an attractive addition to any garden setting.
