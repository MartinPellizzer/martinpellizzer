Peperomia brevicaulis

Peperomia brevicaulis, commonly known as the compact peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Central and South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often glossy and vary in color from deep green to reddish tones. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance for low light and minimal water requirements.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for tabletop displays and decorative arrangements.
