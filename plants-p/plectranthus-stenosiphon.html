Plectranthus stenosiphon

Plectranthus stenosiphon, commonly known as the narrow-lipped plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface and produces small, tubular flowers in shades of purple or violet. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile addition to gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and easy care requirements, it is popular among gardeners and horticulturists.
