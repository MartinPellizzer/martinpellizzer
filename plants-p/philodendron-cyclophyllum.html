Philodendron cyclophyllum

Philodendron cyclophyllum, commonly known as the giant philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length. The plant has a vigorous growth habit and can reach heights of several meters when provided with adequate support. Its leaves are typically a deep green with a slightly glossy texture.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.
