Pluchea rubicunda

Pluchea rubicunda, commonly known as redweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with slender stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are usually alternate, simple, and covered in fine hairs.
