Plectrurus

The plectrurus is a fascinating genus of geckos found primarily in the arid regions of southern Africa. These small, nocturnal reptiles are known for their unique adaptations to survive in harsh desert environments. They possess specialized toe pads that allow them to climb vertical surfaces with ease. Their diet mainly consists of insects and other small invertebrates. Studying plectrurus offers valuable insights into the evolution of reptilian life in extreme climates.
