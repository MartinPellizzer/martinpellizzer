Pteris pteridioides

Pteris pteridioides, commonly known as the Chinese water fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that resemble a lacy pattern. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near streams or in humid forests. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce clusters of small, spore-bearing leaves.

Pteris pteridioides is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant.
