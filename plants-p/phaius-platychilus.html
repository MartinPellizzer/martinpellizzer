Phaius platychilus

Phaius platychilus, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a distinctive, broad, and often dark green foliage. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and conservation efforts.
