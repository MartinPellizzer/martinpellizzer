Pygeum bachmaense

Pygeum bachmaense, commonly known as the Bachma African cherry.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the arid regions of southern Africa. The plant is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and dark green, glossy leaves. Its flowers are small and yellowish, arranged in clusters, and it produces small, red, fleshy fruits.

The plant is valued for its hard, durable wood and has some medicinal uses in traditional medicine.
