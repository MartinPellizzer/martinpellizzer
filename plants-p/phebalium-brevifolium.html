Phebalium brevifolium

Phebalium brevifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved peacock flower, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

The plant is part of the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is known for its aromatic foliage. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.
