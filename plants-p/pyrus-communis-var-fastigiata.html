Pyrus communis var. fastigiata

Pyrus communis var. fastigiata, commonly known as the upright pear tree, is a compact, columnar variety of the common pear.

It features narrow, upright growth habit with dense foliage, making it suitable for small spaces or ornamental purposes. The leaves are dark green and glossy, providing a striking visual contrast against its white or pinkish flowers in spring. It produces small, edible pears that are typically harvested in late summer.

This variety is valued for its unique shape and ornamental appeal in gardens and landscapes.
