Priva bahiensis

Priva bahiensis, commonly known as the Bahia privet, is a shrub or small tree native to Brazil.

It features opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, berry-like fruits. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

Priva bahiensis is also used in landscaping due to its tolerance of urban conditions.
