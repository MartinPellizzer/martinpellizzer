Pterostylis hians

Pterostylis hians, commonly known as the ghost orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests and understoreys. Its flowers are pollinated by insects, particularly thrips, which are attracted to the nectar-rich labellum.
