Peperomia coenosa

Peperomia coenosa, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery underside, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia coenosa is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
