Pelexia scintillans

Pelexia scintillans, commonly known as the glittering pelexia, is a flowering plant native to the wetlands of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped white flowers that emit a faint, sparkling appearance under certain light conditions. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in marshy areas and along the edges of water bodies.

Pelexia scintillans is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable humidity and water conditions.
