Peperomia pellucidoides

Peperomia pellucidoides, commonly known as the glassy peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to Brazil.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are often translucent or semi-transparent, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, which appear in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia pellucidoides is ideal for indoor cultivation due to its resilience and ornamental foliage.
