Psacalium nanum

Psacalium nanum, commonly known as the dwarf psacalium, is a small shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters and features dense, compact foliage. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Its leaves are generally oval-shaped with a smooth surface and a slight pubescence.

Psacalium nanum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and attractive appearance.
