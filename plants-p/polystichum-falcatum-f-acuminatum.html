Polystichum falcatum f. acuminatum

Polystichum falcatum f. acuminatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved wood fern, is a fern species characterized by its slender, lance-shaped fronds.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is native to regions of Asia, including China and Japan. The fronds are typically bipinnate, with a delicate, feathery appearance that adds ornamental value to gardens. Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural and ecological applications.
