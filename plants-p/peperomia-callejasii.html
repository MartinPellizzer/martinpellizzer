Peperomia callejasii

Peperomia callejasii, commonly known as the Callejas' peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Mexico.

It features glossy, ovate to elliptical leaves that are often dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, typically arranged in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia callejasii is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
