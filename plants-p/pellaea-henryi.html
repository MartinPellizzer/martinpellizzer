Pellaea henryi

Pellaea henryi, commonly known as Henry's sword fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Pellaeaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are short and thick, supporting the growth of its foliage.

Pellaea henryi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
