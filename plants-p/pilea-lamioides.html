Pilea lamioides

Pilea lamioides, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Pilea lamioides is known for its ease of care and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
