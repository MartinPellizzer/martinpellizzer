Paphiopedilum philippinense var. philippinense

Paphiopedilum philippinense var. philippinense, commonly known as the Philippine velvet slipper orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short rhizome and a basal rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves. The plant produces a single, showy inflorescence with a single flower that has a distinctive, pouch-like lip. Its flowers are typically pale yellow to greenish-white with a contrasting purple or red lip.

This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and is adapted to the tropical climate of its native habitat.
