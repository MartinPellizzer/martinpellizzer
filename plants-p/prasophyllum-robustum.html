Prasophyllum robustum

Prasophyllum robustum, commonly known as the robust orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the temperate regions of southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant typically grows in sandy or loamy soils and thrives in open, sunny habitats. Its roots are fibrous, and it is often found in heathlands and coastal dunes.

Prasophyllum robustum is a perennial species that is well adapted to the seasonal climate of its native range.
