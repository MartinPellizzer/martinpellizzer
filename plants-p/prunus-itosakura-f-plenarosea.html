Prunus itosakura f. plenarosea

Prunus itosakura f. plenarosea, commonly known as the Japanese cherry blossom with double flowers, is a cultivar of the Prunus itosakura species.

It is characterized by its profuse display of double pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant is a deciduous shrub or small tree, native to Japan, and thrives in temperate climates. Its flowers are highly ornamental, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and floral displays.

The foliage turns a vibrant green in summer, providing year-round visual interest.
