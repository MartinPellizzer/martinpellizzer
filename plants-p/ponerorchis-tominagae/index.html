Ponerorchis Tominagae

Tominaga's ghost orchid, scientifically known as Ponerorchis tominagae, is a rare and enigmatic flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China. This orchid is named for its ethereal appearance and elusive nature, which has made it a subject of fascination among botanists and conservationists. It thrives in cool, humid environments, often growing on the bark of old trees in dense forests. Due to its limited distribution and habitat destruction, it is classified as critically endangered. The plant's unique beauty and ecological significance highlight the urgent need for its protection.
