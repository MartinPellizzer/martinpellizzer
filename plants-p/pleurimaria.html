Pleurimaria

Pleurimaria, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, powdery hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are alternate and ovate, with a leathery texture.

Pleurimaria is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and drought tolerance.
