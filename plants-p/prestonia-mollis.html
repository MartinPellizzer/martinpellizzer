Prestonia mollis

Prestonia mollis, commonly known as the pink trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is a climbing shrub that produces showy, tubular, pink to purple flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with hairy undersides and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Prestonia mollis is also valued for its fragrant flowers and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
