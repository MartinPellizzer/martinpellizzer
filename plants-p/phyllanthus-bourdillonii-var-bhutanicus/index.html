Phyllanthus Bourdillonii Var. Bhutanicus

Phyllanthus bourdillonii var. bhutanicus is a unique variant of the Phyllanthus genus native to the high altitudes of the Eastern Himalayas. This plant is distinguished by its compact growth habit and distinctive leaf morphology. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species. Its ecological importance and ornamental value have sparked interest among botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
