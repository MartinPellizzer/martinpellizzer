Phymatodes masaskei

Phymatodes masaskei, commonly known as the Malawi orchid, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in cool, humid environments. Its flowers are usually small to medium in size, with intricate patterns and vibrant colors.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
