Piper okamotoi

Piper okamotoi, commonly known as the Okamoto's pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. It produces small green flowers that develop into elongated, peppery fruits.

Due to its unique flavor and potential medicinal properties, it has attracted interest among botanists and herbalists.
