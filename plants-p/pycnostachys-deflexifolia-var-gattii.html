Pycnostachys deflexifolia var. gattii

Pycnostachys deflexifolia var. gattii, commonly known as the Gattii's drooping stamen plant, is a variant of the Pycnostachys genus within the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its drooping stamens and distinctive leaf morphology, with leaves that are often recurved and have a velvety texture. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, preferring moist, well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually clustered in whorled inflorescences, displaying vibrant colors ranging from pink to red.

This variant is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to shaded environments.
