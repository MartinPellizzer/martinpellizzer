Pennilabium acuminatum

Pennilabium acuminatum, commonly known as the slender lip orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, lip-shaped petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or stream sides. Its flowers are usually small and come in shades of white, pink, or purple.

Pennilabium acuminatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
