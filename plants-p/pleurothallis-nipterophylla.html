Pleurothallis nipterophylla

Pleurothallis nipterophylla, commonly known as the pink-tipped orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is a small epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features slender, dark green leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers with a pinkish hue. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments to ensure its survival.
