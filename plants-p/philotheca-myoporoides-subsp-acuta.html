Philotheca myoporoides subsp. acuta

Philotheca myoporoides subsp.

acuta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved myopor, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia. It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white or pale blue flowers.

The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often found in heathlands and coastal areas, where it contributes to local biodiversity.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
