Parnassia wightiana var. ornata

Parnassia wightiana var. ornata, commonly known as the ornate mountain buttercup, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its showy, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive central pattern. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky slopes. Its leaves are palmately divided, and the flowers often have a subtle fragrance.
