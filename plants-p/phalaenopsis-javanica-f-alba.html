Phalaenopsis javanica f. alba

Phalaenopsis javanica f. alba, commonly known as the white phalaenopsis, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, white flowers that bloom in a raceme, often with a subtle fragrance. The plant features broad, dark green leaves that are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

This cultivar is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and ease of care.
