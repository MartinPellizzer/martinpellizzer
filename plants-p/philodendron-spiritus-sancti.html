Philodendron spiritus-sancti

Philodendron spiritus-sancti, commonly known as the Holy Spirit Philodendron, is a tropical vine native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, dark green leaves that often have a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red berries, which are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
