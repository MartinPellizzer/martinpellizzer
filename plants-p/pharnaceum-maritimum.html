Pharnaceum maritimum

Pharnaceum maritimum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple ray flowers and spiny, lobed leaves. Native to coastal regions of Europe and western Asia, it thrives in salty, well-drained soils. The plant is often found in dunes and maritime habitats, where it plays a role in stabilizing sand and preventing erosion.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in coastal areas.
