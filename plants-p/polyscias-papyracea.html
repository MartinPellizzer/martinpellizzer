Polyscias papyracea

Polyscias papyracea, commonly known as the paper-leaf sycamore, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, papery texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by dark blue berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses around the world.
