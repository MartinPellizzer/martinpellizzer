Platycentrum rubrovenium

Platycentrum rubrovenium, commonly known as the red-veined fern, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its intricate fronds with deep red veins against a lush green background.

Native to the humid forests of Southeast Asia, it thrives in high light conditions and requires consistent moisture to flourish. The plant's foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and unique coloration. Its growth habit is clump-forming, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation in suitable climates.

Due to its specific care requirements, it is considered a challenging but rewarding plant for experienced gardeners.
