Patagonium inconspicuum

It is characterized by its thin, pale green to yellowish thallus with a finely granular texture. This lichen typically grows on bark and rock surfaces in the arid and semi-arid regions of southern South America. It is notable for its ability to survive in extreme environmental conditions, including high UV exposure and low nutrient availability.

Due to its inconspicuous appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and poorly understood species within its ecosystem.
