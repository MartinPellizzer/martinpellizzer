Platanus nepalensis

Platanus nepalensis, commonly known as the Himalayan sycamore, is a deciduous tree native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically lobed and have a rough, hairy texture. The tree produces distinctive, spherical fruit clusters that contain small, winged seeds. Its bark is thick, flaky, and often exhibits a mottled appearance.

Platanus nepalensis is valued for its ecological role and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
