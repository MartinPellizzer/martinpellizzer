Polypodium distans var. glabratum

Polypodium distans var. glabratum, commonly known as the smooth shield fern, is a variant of the common shield fern.

It is a terrestrial fern that typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. The plant features a creeping rhizome with upright, lance-shaped fronds that are usually dark green and glossy. Its spore-producing structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

This variety is characterized by its smooth, glabrous texture, distinguishing it from other forms of Polypodium distans.
