Polembryum jussieui

Polembryum jussieui, commonly known as the Jussieui polembryum, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the humid rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy surface. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
