Pandanus laosensis

Pandanus laosensis, commonly known as the Lao pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pandanaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Laos, Cambodia, and Vietnam. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts and as a food wrap. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in branched inflorescences.

The plant thrives in tropical climates and is often found growing near water bodies or in moist, well-drained soils.
