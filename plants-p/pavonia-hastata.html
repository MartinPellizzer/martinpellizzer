Pavonia hastata

Pavonia hastata, commonly known as the scarlet fanflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features showy, bright red to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and a sprawling growth habit, often reaching heights of up to 3 feet. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

Pavonia hastata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
