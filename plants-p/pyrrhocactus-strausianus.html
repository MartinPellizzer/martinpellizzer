Pyrrhocactus strausianus

Pyrrhocactus strausianus, commonly known as the red-tipped cactus, is a unique member of the Cactaceae family.

It is native to the arid regions of South America, particularly in Argentina and Bolivia. This plant is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems and vibrant red tips on its spines, which give it its common name. The flowers are typically yellow or orange, blooming in the spring.

It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water requirements.
