Panax vietnamensis var. langbianensis

Panax vietnamensis var. langbianensis, commonly known as the Langbian ginseng, is a rare and endemic species native to the mountainous regions of Vietnam.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, fleshy, and often hairy leaves.
