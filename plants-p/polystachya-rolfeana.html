Polystachya rolfeana

Polystachya rolfeana, commonly known as the Rolfe's spikegrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly found in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its tall, grass-like appearance with clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
