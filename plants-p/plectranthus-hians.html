Plectranthus hians

Plectranthus hians, commonly known as the purple-leaf plectranthus, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically purple or violet in color, with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of purple or white, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Plectranthus hians is also valued for its aromatic properties and is often used in herbal remedies.
