Pachyphytum kimnachii

Pachyphytum kimnachii, commonly known as the Kimnach's stonecrop, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, typical of desert plants. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
