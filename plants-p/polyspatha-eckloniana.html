Polyspatha eckloniana

Polyspatha eckloniana, commonly known as the Zulu lily, is a striking herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Polyspatha eckloniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its dramatic floral display.
