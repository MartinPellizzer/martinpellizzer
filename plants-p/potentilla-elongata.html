Potentilla elongata

Potentilla elongata, commonly known as the slender cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the northern regions of North America.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a distinctive five-lobed shape.

Potentilla elongata thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, streambanks, and forest edges.
