Pilea bambuseti

Pilea bambuseti, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and prefers indirect light.

Pilea bambuseti is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
