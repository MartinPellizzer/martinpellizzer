Populus alba f. nivea

Populus alba f. nivea, commonly known as the white poplar or snow poplar, is a cultivar of the white poplar tree.

It is characterized by its dense, white to pale yellow foliage that gives it a striking appearance. The leaves are ovate to elliptical in shape and have a finely toothed margin. This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and parks.

It prefers full sun and well-drained soil, and it is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
