Polemonium schizanthum

Polemonium schizanthum, commonly known as the showy lady's slipper, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features striking, showy flowers that range from white to pink and purple, with a distinctive pouch-like shape at the base. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, with lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. It thrives in moist, woodland habitats and is known for its ability to bloom in late spring to early summer.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
