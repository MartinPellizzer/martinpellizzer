Peperomia turfosa

Peperomia turfosa, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, giving it a distinctive, almost moss-like appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color, which are not particularly showy. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its ability to thrive in low light and minimal water conditions.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for use in terrariums and as a decorative houseplant.
