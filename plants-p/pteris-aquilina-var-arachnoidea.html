Pteris aquilina var. arachnoidea

Pteris aquilina var. arachnoidea, commonly known as the spider fern, is a species of fern characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia. The plant features a creeping rhizome with numerous short, upright fronds that are finely divided and covered in tiny, hair-like structures. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forests or along stream banks.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant displays.
