Polygala tonkinensis

Polygala tonkinensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are alternate and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, and the plant produces small, dry fruits that contain several seeds.
