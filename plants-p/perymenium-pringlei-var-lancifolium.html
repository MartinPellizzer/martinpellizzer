Perymenium pringlei var. lancifolium

Perymenium pringlei var. lancifolium, commonly known as the lancifoliate perymenium, is a rare and distinctive plant species native to specific regions of South America.

Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
