Pennisetum alopecuroides var. albiflorum

Pennisetum alopecuroides var. albiflorum, commonly known as silver fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It features narrow, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive, ornamental appearance. The plant produces fluffy, white plumes during the summer months, adding visual interest to gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it suitable for a variety of climates.
