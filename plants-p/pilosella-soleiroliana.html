Pilosella soleiroliana

Pilosella soleiroliana, commonly known as the soleiroliana hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers and hairy stems, which give it its scientific name. Native to southern Europe, it thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom from late spring to early summer.

Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
