Pentacalia cubensis

Pentacalia cubensis, commonly known as Cuban starflower, is a flowering plant native to Cuba.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its star-shaped, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.
