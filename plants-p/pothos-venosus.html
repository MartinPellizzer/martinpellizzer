Pothos venosus

Pothos venosus, commonly known as the marble queen pothos, is a trailing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, waxy leaves that display a striking pattern of light and dark green veins. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and is known for its ability to purify indoor air. Its rapid growth makes it an excellent choice for hanging baskets or shelves.

Pothos venosus is relatively low-maintenance, requiring minimal watering and fertilization.
