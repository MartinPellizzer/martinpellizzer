Phacelia divaricata var. wrangeliana

Phacelia divaricata var. wrangeliana, commonly known as Wrangel Island phacelia, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, clustered blue or violet flowers. The plant typically thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold climates. It plays a role in supporting local pollinators in its native habitat.

Its morphology reflects its evolutionary adaptation to harsh environmental conditions.
