Polygonum tropicum

Polygonum tropicum, commonly known as the tropical knotweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions.

It features upright stems that are often reddish or purple in color, covered with small, sticky hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture, contributing to its distinct appearance.

Polygonum tropicum is often found in moist, disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of tropical environments.
