Paracaryum strictum

Paracaryum strictum, commonly known as the African violets, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and showy, clustered flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows as a compact, upright shrub with hairy stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and relatively easy to care for in a home environment.
