Pultenaea heterochila

Pultenaea heterochila, commonly known as the variable pultenaea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland and heathland environments.

Pultenaea heterochila is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
