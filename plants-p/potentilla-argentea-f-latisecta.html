Potentilla argentea f. latisecta

Potentilla argentea f. latisecta, commonly known as silverleaf cinquefoil, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its silvery-white undersides on the leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or streamside habitats. Its flowers are usually pink or white, appearing in clusters of five.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and long blooming period.
