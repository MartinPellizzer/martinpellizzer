Psilocarya eximia

Psilocarya eximia, commonly known as the sacred mushroom, is a species of psilocybin-containing fungi found primarily in the tropical regions of Central and South America.

The cap of Psilocarya eximia is typically small, with a pale to reddish-brown color, and the stem is often slender and fibrous.
