Prunus mahaleb f. compacta

Prunus mahaleb f. compacta, commonly known as the dwarf cherry or compact cherry, is a compact form of the European cherry.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and small, dark red to black fruits. The plant produces fragrant white flowers in early spring, which are followed by edible berries. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and hardiness.

This variety is well-suited for small gardens and container growing.
