Panicum nigromarginatum

Panicum nigromarginatum, commonly known as black-margin grass, is a flowering plant species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets, which are typically wind-pollinated. Its leaves often have a dark-colored margin, giving it its common name.

Panicum nigromarginatum is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
