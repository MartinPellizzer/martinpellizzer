Panicum steudelianum

Panicum steudelianum, commonly known as the slender panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces fluffy, yellowish-green inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
