Pinus italica

Pinus italica, commonly known as the Italian stone pine, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and dense, dark green foliage. The tree produces long, needle-like leaves that are typically arranged in pairs. Its cones are woody and contain edible seeds known as pine nuts.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its contribution to local ecosystems.
