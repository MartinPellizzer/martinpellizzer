Peperomia distachyos var. subrhombica

Peperomia distachyos var. subrhombica, commonly known as the Rhombic Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. Its rhomboid-shaped leaves give it a distinctive appearance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
