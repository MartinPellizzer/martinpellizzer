Panicularia tenuifolia

Panicularia tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender panicled bellflower, is a flowering plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, woodlands, and along stream banks.

Panicularia tenuifolia is a perennial herb that blooms in late spring to early summer.
