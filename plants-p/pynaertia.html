Pynaertia

Pynaertia, commonly known as the false hellebore, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy rhizomes and large, waxy leaves that are often dark green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces striking, umbrella-shaped inflorescences with small, greenish-white flowers. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
