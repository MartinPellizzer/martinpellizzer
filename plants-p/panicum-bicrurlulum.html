Panicum bicrurlulum

Panicum bicrurlulum, commonly known as the curly panicgrass, is a warm-season grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and the presence of tightly curled, hair-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in full sun and thrives in well-drained soils, making it a hardy choice for ornamental and erosion control purposes. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that adds visual interest to landscapes.

Panicum bicrurlulum is often used in native plant gardens due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to attract pollinators.
