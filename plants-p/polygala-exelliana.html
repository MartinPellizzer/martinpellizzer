Polygala exelliana

Polygala exelliana, commonly known as the Natal milk-vetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with slender, branching stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal and inland grasslands.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
