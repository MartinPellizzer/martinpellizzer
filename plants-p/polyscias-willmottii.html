Polyscias willmottii

Polyscias willmottii, commonly known as the Willmott's sycamore, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its dense, glossy, dark green foliage. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by red, fleshy berries that are edible for some bird species. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
