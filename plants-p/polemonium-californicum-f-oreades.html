Polemonium californicum f. oreades

Polemonium californicum f. oreades, commonly known as the Sierra larkspur, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Polemoniaceae family and is characterized by its tall, spindly stems and clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or open habitats, such as meadows and slopes, where it thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The Sierra larkspur is adapted to arid environments and is known for its resilience in harsh mountainous conditions.
