Persicaria lapathifolia subsp. oligoclada

Persicaria lapathifolia subsp.

oligoclada, commonly known as narrowleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It typically grows in moist or wet environments such as marshes, ditches, and stream banks.

The plant features upright stems that are often reddish in color, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a slightly hairy surface. The small, greenish-white flowers are arranged in dense spikes and bloom in late summer.

It is characterized by its slender leaves and is often found in wetland habitats where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
