Pilosella duplex

Pilosella duplex, commonly known as the doubleleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands. The plant features a basal rosette of hairy, spoon-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters. Its scientific name reflects the distinctive double structure of its leaves.

Pilosella duplex is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
