Pennisetum vulcanicum

Pennisetum vulcanicum, commonly known as the flame grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that produce feathery, red-orange plumes, which give it its distinctive appearance. The plant is known for its vibrant color and architectural form, making it a popular choice in gardens and landscapes. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring minimal water once established.

Pennisetum vulcanicum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators and its low maintenance requirements.
