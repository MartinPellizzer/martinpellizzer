Paphiopedilum villosum var. villosum

Paphiopedilum villosum var. villosum, commonly known as the hairy paphiopedilum, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, often branched stem that bears several ovate to elliptical leaves. The plant produces a single, pendulous flower with a distinctive, pouch-like lip that is covered in fine, hairy structures. Its flowers are typically pale green to yellowish in color, with a contrasting purple or reddish hue on the lip.

This species prefers shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.
