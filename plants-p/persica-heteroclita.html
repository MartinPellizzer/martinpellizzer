Persica heteroclita

Persica heteroclita, commonly known as the variable peach.

It is a species of wild peach native to southern China and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its small, hairy leaves and pink to white flowers. Its fruits are typically small, yellow or orange, and have a sweet, aromatic flavor.

Persica heteroclita is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of edible fruits.
