Primula × leudrensis

Primula × leudrensis, commonly known as the hybrid primrose, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the British Isles.

It is characterized by its clustered, bell-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland or shaded garden settings.

Primula × leudrensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is a popular choice in horticulture.
