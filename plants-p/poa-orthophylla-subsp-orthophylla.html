Poa orthophylla subsp. orthophylla

Poa orthophylla subsp.

orthophylla, commonly known as the upright bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of South America. It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant typically thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of spikelets, which are usually tinged with purple or brown.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticultural settings.
