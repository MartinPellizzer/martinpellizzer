Panicum diagonale var. uniglume

Panicum diagonale var. uniglume, commonly known as the narrowleaf panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny flowers, which are typically wind-pollinated. Its growth habit is usually clump-forming, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
