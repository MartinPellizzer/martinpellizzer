Persicaria plattensis

Persicaria plattensis, commonly known as the slender smartweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and other moist environments. The plant features alternate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered flowers with five petals. It blooms from late spring to early fall, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Persicaria plattensis is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in partial to full sunlight.
