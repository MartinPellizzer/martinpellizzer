Pelargonium degener

Pelargonium degener, commonly known as the degener geranium, is a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, delicate flowers in shades of pink and white. The leaves are typically rounded with a slightly serrated edge, and the plant has a compact form that makes it suitable for container gardening. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This plant is often appreciated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
