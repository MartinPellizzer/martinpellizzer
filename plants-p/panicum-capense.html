Panicum capense

Panicum capense, commonly known as Cape panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial grass that typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color that helps it reflect sunlight.

Panicum capense is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
