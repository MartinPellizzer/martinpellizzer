Panicum crus-galli var. aquaticum

Panicum crus-galli var. aquaticum, commonly known as water foxtail, is a grass species native to wetland environments.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, blade-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces fluffy, spike-like inflorescences that contain small, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in moist soils and is often found in marshes, pond edges, and along stream banks.

Water foxtail is an important component of wetland ecosystems, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
