Primula prolifera var. gracilis

Primula prolifera var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender primrose, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It features upright, hairy stems that bear clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white to pale yellow. The leaves are ovate to elliptic, with a hairy surface and a slightly serrated margin. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded woodland areas.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
