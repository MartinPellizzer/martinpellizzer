Pisonia brevipetiolata

Pisonia brevipetiolata, commonly known as the short-stemmed pisonia, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its delicate, white, clustered flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, elliptical leaves with short petioles, giving it its specific epithet. It thrives in coastal areas and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
