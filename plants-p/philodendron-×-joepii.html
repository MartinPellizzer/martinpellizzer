Philodendron × joepii

Philodendron × joepii, commonly known as the Joe Pineapple.

This hybrid plant is a member of the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that often display a striking variegation of green, white, and gold. It originates from a cross between Philodendron bipinnatifidum and Philodendron selloum, resulting in a unique combination of traits from both parent species. The plant is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

Its foliage can grow quite large, making it a striking addition to indoor spaces.
