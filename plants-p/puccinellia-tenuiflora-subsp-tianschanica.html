Puccinellia tenuiflora subsp. tianschanica

Puccinellia tenuiflora subsp.

tianschanica, commonly known as the Tianshan slenderflower grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia. It typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences with tiny flowers. It is adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions, including drought and cold temperatures.

Its botanical features make it an important component of its native ecosystems.
