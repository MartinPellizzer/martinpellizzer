Polystachya lawrenceana var. hislopii

Polystachya lawrenceana var. hislopii, commonly known as the Hislop's bamboo orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is known for its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies in suitable habitats.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
