Potentilla Recta Proles Pilosa

Potentilla recta proles pilosa is a hardy shrub known for its resilience in harsh environments. It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring and early summer. The plant thrives in rocky soils and is often found in alpine regions. Its fuzzy leaves provide insulation against cold temperatures. This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
