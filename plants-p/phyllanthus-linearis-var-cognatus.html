Phyllanthus linearis var. cognatus

Phyllanthus linearis var. cognatus, commonly known as the narrowleaf spurge, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, often with a distinctive floral structure. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing drought tolerance.

The species is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
