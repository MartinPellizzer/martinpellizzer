Panicum sphaerocarpum

Panicum sphaerocarpum, commonly known as the globe panic grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical inflorescence that gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Panicum sphaerocarpum is often used in ornamental gardening due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
