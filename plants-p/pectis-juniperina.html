Pectis juniperina

Pectis juniperina, commonly known as the Juniper Bottlebrush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, bottlebrush-like inflorescences. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces vibrant yellow to orange flowers that bloom in spring. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid conditions.

Pectis juniperina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
