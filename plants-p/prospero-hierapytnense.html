Prospero hierapytnense

Prospero hierapytnense, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a rare flowering plant native to the humid rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and intricate, fragrant blooms that resemble miniature orchids. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat destruction, it is considered endangered and is protected in several countries.
