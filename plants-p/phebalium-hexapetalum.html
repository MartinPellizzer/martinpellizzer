Phebalium hexapetalum

Phebalium hexapetalum, commonly known as the six-petaled honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, which are usually white or pale yellow. Its botanical characteristics include opposite leaves and a woody stem structure.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
