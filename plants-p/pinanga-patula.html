Pinanga patula

Pinanga patula, commonly known as the slender bamboo palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant features slender, upright stems with long, pinnate leaves that give it a graceful appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Pinanga patula is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
