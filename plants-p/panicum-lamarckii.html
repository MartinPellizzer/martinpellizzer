Panicum lamarckii

Panicum lamarckii, commonly known as the silvergrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, silver-green blades with a distinctive metallic sheen, which gives it its common name. The plant produces fluffy, pale pink or white inflorescences that resemble plumes, enhancing its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping and erosion control.

Its rapid growth and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for ornamental and ecological landscaping.
