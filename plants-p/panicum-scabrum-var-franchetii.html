Panicum scabrum var. franchetii

Panicum scabrum var. franchetii, commonly known as rough bluestem, is a perennial grass native to Asia.

It is characterized by its coarse, hairy leaves and open, panicle-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has a fibrous root system and is often used for erosion control due to its hardiness.

The species is valued for its ornamental value and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
