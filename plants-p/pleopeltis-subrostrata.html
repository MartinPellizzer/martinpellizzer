Pleopeltis subrostrata

Pleopeltis subrostrata, commonly known as the narrow-lobed rock fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, dense foliage and dark green, leathery fronds. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its rhizomes are short and often covered with a layer of moss-like tissue.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
