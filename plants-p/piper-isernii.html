Piper isernii

Piper isernii, commonly known as the Iserni pepper or Iserni pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that eventually develop into the peppery fruits.

The plant is valued for its aromatic leaves and fruits, which are used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.
