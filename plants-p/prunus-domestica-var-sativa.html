Prunus domestica var. sativa

Prunus domestica var. sativa, commonly known as the common European plum.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant produces small, sweet to tart fruits that are typically harvested in the summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly toothed margin.

The flowers are white or pink and appear before the leaves in early spring.
