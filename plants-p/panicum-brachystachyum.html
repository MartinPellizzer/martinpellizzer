Panicum brachystachyum

Panicum brachystachyum, commonly known as little spike grass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compact, tufted growth habit and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for dryland environments. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color that adds ornamental value.

It is often used in landscaping for erosion control and as a low-maintenance ornamental plant.
