Polyalthia schefferi

Polyalthia schefferi, commonly known as the Scheffer's flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with ovate to elliptic leaflets, and it produces small, round fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
