Pilosella Substoloniflora

Pilosella substoloniflora is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of Europe. It is known for its unique growth habits and small, white blossoms that appear in early summer. This species thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas. Due to habitat loss, it faces significant threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this beautiful and lesser-known plant.
