Phacelia Frigida Subsp. Dasyphylla

Frigid phacelia, scientifically known as Phacelia frigida subsp. dasyphylla, is a rare flowering plant adapted to extreme cold environments. It thrives in high-altitude regions where temperatures often drop below freezing. This subspecies is characterized by its compact growth and dense clusters of blue-violet flowers. Despite its beauty, it remains poorly understood due to its remote habitat and limited distribution. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique and fragile species from environmental threats.
