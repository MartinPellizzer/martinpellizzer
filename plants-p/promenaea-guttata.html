Promenaea guttata

Promenaea guttata, commonly known as the spotted pittosporum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pittosporaceae.

It is native to New Zealand and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with distinctive yellow or orange spots. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Promenaea guttata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and hardy nature.

It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for a variety of garden settings.
