Plectranthus klossii

Plectranthus klossii, commonly known as the Kloss's plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves, often with a velvety texture. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color.

Plectranthus klossii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in well-drained soil with moderate sunlight.
