Pinalia aeridostachya

Pinalia aeridostachya, commonly known as the silverleaf pinalia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a sunny to partially shaded environment.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
