Pleopeltis elmeri

Pleopeltis elmeri, commonly known as Elmer's rock fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant features delicate, lanceolate leaves with a glossy surface and a distinctive midrib. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on trees or rocks in tropical forests.
