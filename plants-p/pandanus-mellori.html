Pandanus mellori

Pandanus mellori, commonly known as the giant pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Indo-Pacific region.

It is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that eventually develop into edible fruits. Its roots and leaves are used in traditional crafts and for medicinal purposes.

Pandanus mellori thrives in coastal areas and is often found in mangrove ecosystems.
