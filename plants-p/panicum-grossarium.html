Panicum grossarium

Panicum grossarium, commonly known as the rough panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, rough-textured leaves. The plant produces fluffy, spike-like inflorescences that contain small, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in open, sunny habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it an important component of grassland ecosystems.
