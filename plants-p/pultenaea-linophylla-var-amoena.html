Pultenaea linophylla var. amoena

Pultenaea linophylla var. amoena, commonly known as the silky pultenaea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in spring, attracting nectar-feeding insects. It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils in open woodlands and scrublands.
