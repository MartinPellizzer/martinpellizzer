Parodia faustiana var. tenuispina

Parodia faustiana var. tenuispina, commonly known as the narrow-spined cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to South America.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with dense, pale green to bluish-gray spines. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique spination and compact form make it a popular choice for succulent collections.
