Peperomia reflexa var. angustifolia

Peperomia reflexa var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features upright stems with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are usually dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically greenish-white in color, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and attractive foliage, it is often used as a decorative plant in both indoor and outdoor settings.
