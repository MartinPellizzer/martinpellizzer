Pennisetum asperum

Pennisetum asperum, commonly known as purple lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and fluffy, purple-tinted seed heads that appear in late summer. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it adaptable to various climates. Its feathery appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Pennisetum asperum is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in dry environments.
