Phyllanthus klotzschianus

Phyllanthus klotzschianus, commonly known as the African spurge, is a flowering plant native to tropical and southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree with opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive foliage.
