Peperomia psilostigma

Peperomia psilostigma, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, glossy dark green leaves with a silvery-blue hue on the underside, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for tabletop displays and as a decorative houseplant.
