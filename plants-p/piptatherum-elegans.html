Piptatherum elegans

Piptatherum elegans, commonly known as the slender grass or elegant grass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows in open woodlands, meadows, and along stream banks, preferring moist, well-drained soils. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often tinged with silver or blue-green hues. Its inflorescence consists of a loose panicle of small, spikelet-bearing branches, which are usually green to brown in color.

Piptatherum elegans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant landscaping.
