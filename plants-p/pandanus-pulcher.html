Pandanus pulcher

Pandanus pulcher, commonly known as the beautiful pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves that often form a rosette at the base. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or green in color. Its unique appearance makes it a popular ornamental plant in tropical gardens.

The leaves are also used in traditional crafts and as thatching materials in some regions.
