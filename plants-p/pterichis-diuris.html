Pterichis diuris

Pterichis diuris, commonly known as the morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its heliotropic leaves that follow the sun's movement throughout the day. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue, purple, or white. Pterichis diuris thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its rapid growth and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
