Prunus jamasakura f. crepuscularis

Prunus jamasakura f. crepuscularis, commonly known as the twilight cherry, is a unique cultivar of the Japanese cherry tree.

It is characterized by its pale pink to almost white flowers that bloom in the early evening, creating a striking visual effect during twilight. The plant is a deciduous shrub or small tree, typically reaching heights of 3 to 6 meters. Its leaves are elliptical, with a glossy dark green surface and a serrated edge.

The botanical features of this cultivar make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening, particularly in regions with temperate climates.
