Plantago atrata var. sudetica

Plantago atrata var. sudetica, commonly known as the Sudetic plantain, is a perennial herb native to Central and Eastern Europe.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and prominent veins. Its flowering stems bear dense clusters of small, greenish-white flowers.

This variety is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
