Panicum argyrotrichum var. tenue

Panicum argyrotrichum var. tenue, commonly known as slender silverhair grass, is a grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-haired foliage and slender, upright growth habit. The plant typically thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping and erosion control. Its delicate appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

It produces small, fluffy seed heads that add visual interest throughout the growing season.
