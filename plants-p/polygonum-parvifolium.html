Polygonum parvifolium

Polygonum parvifolium, commonly known as the small-leaved smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist environments such as wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks. The plant features alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink or red, and they bloom in late summer.

Polygonum parvifolium is known for its ability to thrive in wet conditions and is often used in ecological restoration projects.
