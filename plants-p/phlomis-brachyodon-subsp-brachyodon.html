Phlomis brachyodon subsp. brachyodon

Phlomis brachyodon subsp.

brachyodon, commonly known as narrowleaf phlomis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows in rocky or dry soils, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments.

The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowering period occurs in summer, producing clusters of small, pale yellow to brownish flowers.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
