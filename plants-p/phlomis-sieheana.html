Phlomis sieheana

Phlomis sieheana, commonly known as Siehe's blazingstar, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun, making it well adapted to arid environments. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped, gray-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Phlomis sieheana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
