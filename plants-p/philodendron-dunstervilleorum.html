Philodendron dunstervilleorum

Philodendron dunstervilleorum, commonly known as the Dunsterville's philodendron, is a climbing plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant.
