Pandanus menicostigma

Pandanus menicostigma, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pandanaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. Its inflorescence consists of a spiky, branched structure that bears small, fragrant flowers.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appearance and its ability to thrive in coastal and wetland environments.
