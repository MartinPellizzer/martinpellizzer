Podocarpus ledermannii var. ledermannii

Podocarpus ledermannii var. ledermannii, commonly known as the Ledermann's podocarp, is a evergreen coniferous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, scale-like leaves that are typically arranged in whorls. The tree produces cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs, typical of conifers. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
