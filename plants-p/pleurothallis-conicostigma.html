Pleurothallis conicostigma

Pleurothallis conicostigma, commonly known as the conical-stigma orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, with a unique conical structure on the stigma. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, organic substrates for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its aesthetic appeal.
