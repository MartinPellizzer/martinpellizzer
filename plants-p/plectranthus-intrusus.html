Plectranthus intrusus

Plectranthus intrusus, commonly known as the purple-leaf plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green with a purple underside. It produces small, tubular flowers in shades of purple and blue, which bloom in clusters.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowering display.
