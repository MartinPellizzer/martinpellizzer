Pierrebraunia × brauniorum

Pierrebraunia × brauniorum, commonly known as the hybrid pierrebraunia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is a sterile hybrid resulting from the cross between Pierrebraunia and Braunia, two genera within the same family. The plant typically exhibits characteristics of both parent species, with showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue or violet. It is native to the Canary Islands and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
