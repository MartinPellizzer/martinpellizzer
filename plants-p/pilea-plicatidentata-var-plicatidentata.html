Pilea plicatidentata var. plicatidentata

Pilea plicatidentata var. plicatidentata, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are often variegated with silver and green hues. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers that are followed by round, red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
