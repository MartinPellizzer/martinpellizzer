Polygonum pensylvanicum var. eglandulosum

Polygonum pensylvanicum var. eglandulosum, commonly known as the smooth smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern North America.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as swamps, marshes, and stream banks. The plant features upright stems that are smooth and often reddish in color, especially towards the tips. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and have a distinctive reddish hue on the underside.

The small, clustered flowers are usually pink to red and appear in late summer to early fall.
