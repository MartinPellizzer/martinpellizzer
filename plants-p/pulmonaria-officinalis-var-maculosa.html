Pulmonaria officinalis var. maculosa

Pulmonaria officinalis var. maculosa, commonly known as spotted lungwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features basal rosettes of dark green, velvety leaves with distinctive red or purple spots. The plant produces upright stems bearing clusters of bell-shaped flowers, typically pink or purple, in early spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and meadows.

Its ornamental value and historical use in traditional medicine contribute to its significance in botanical gardens and herbal practices.
