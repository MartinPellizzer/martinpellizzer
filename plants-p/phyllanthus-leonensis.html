Phyllanthus leonensis

Phyllanthus leonensis, commonly known as the lemon tree or lemon shrub, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant produces yellowish fruits that contain a single seed, which is often used in traditional medicine.
