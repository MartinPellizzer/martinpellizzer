Peperomia glanduligera var. villosissima

Peperomia glanduligera var. villosissima, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a velvety texture, and its stems are often covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, depending on the variety. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its attractive foliage and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
