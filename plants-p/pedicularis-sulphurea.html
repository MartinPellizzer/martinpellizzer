Pedicularis sulphurea

Pedicularis sulphurea, commonly known as the golden monkey orchid, is a member of the Orobanchaceae family and is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

The plant is notable for its specialized pollination mechanism, which involves attracting specific insects with its vivid coloration and aromatic scent.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many regions.
