Prenanthes erucifolia

Prenanthes erucifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf water hemp, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist environments such as wet meadows and stream banks.
