Piper morator

Piper morator, commonly known as the Morator pepper vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when mature, containing aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are often used in herbal remedies due to their medicinal properties.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for both its ornamental and pharmacological potential.
