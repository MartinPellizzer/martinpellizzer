Pyrrhocactus horridus var. robustus

Pyrrhocactus horridus var. robustus, commonly known as the rugged pyrrhocactus, is a unique cactus species native to arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its thick, ribbed stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in dry environments. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking visual element to its habitat. Its spiny exterior serves as a defense mechanism against herbivores and environmental stressors.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often cultivated in xeriscaped gardens and rockeries.
