Pithecoseris pacourinoides

Pithecoseris pacourinoides, commonly known as the pacourinoides orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and fibrous root system. The plant typically produces a single erect stem bearing several oval, dark green leaves. Its flowers are small, usually white or greenish-white, and are arranged in a loose spike.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
