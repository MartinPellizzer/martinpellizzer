Polyscias macrocarpa

Polyscias macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-leaf glory bush, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, glossy, ovate to elliptic leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by spherical, fleshy fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate shade.

Polyscias macrocarpa is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
