Pterostylis concinna

Pterostylis concinna, commonly known as the slender greenhood, is an orchid native to eastern Australia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a single, slender stem and a single, hooded flower that is typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant produces a single, erect, lance-shaped leaf and blooms from late summer to autumn. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys.

The orchid is characterized by its unique floral structure, which is adapted for pollination by specific insects.
