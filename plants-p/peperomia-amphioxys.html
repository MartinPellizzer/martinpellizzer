Peperomia amphioxys

Peperomia amphioxys, commonly known as the narrow-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, narrow, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are clustered in spikes, which appear in the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is often used as a decorative plant in shaded areas.
