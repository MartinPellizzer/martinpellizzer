Peperomia gracilipeduncula

Peperomia gracilipeduncula, commonly known as the slender-stemmed peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are usually dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces delicate, raceme-like inflorescences with tiny, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its compact size and foliage make it a popular choice for terrariums and tabletop gardens.
