Piper dilatatum f. diversifolium

Piper dilatatum f. diversifolium, commonly known as the variable-leaf pepper plant, is a unique variant of the *Piper dilatatum* species.

It is characterized by its diverse leaf morphology, which includes variations in shape, size, and venation patterns. This plant belongs to the Piperaceae family, which is known for its aromatic and often medicinal properties. Native to Central and South America, it thrives in humid, tropical environments.

Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine and as a spice due to the presence of essential oils and alkaloids.
