Phalaenopsis × Leucorrhoda Var. Casta

Phalaenopsis × leucorrhoda var. casta is a rare and captivating orchid known for its striking white and pink petals. This hybrid orchid combines the elegance of Phalaenopsis with the unique characteristics of leucorrhoda, creating a visually stunning bloom. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect sunlight. Its long-lasting flowers make it a favorite among orchid enthusiasts and collectors. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to flourish and maintain its vibrant colors.
