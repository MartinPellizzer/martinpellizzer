Psoralea palmeri

Psoralea palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's psoralea, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, such as sandy soils and rocky plains. Its seed pods are hairy and contain several small, kidney-shaped seeds.
