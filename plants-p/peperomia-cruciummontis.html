Peperomia cruciummontis

Peperomia cruciummontis, commonly known as the Mountain Cross Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It features dense, velvety foliage with deep green leaves that are often adorned with striking red or purple veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically white or pale green, which are followed by capsule fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to both novice and experienced plant collectors.
