Plumeria filifolia

Plumeria filifolia, commonly known as the silky plumeria, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant has long, narrow, filament-like leaves that give it its common name. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal.

Plumeria filifolia is also used in traditional medicine and for making incense.
