Prunus domestica var. cerasifera

Prunus domestica var. cerasifera, commonly known as the cherry plum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces small, sweet, yellow or red fruits that are typically harvested in late summer. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are oval to elliptical in shape with a smooth margin. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental and edible landscaping.

Its flowers are white or pink, appearing in early spring before the leaves emerge.
