Polypodium pycnolepis

Polypodium pycnolepis, commonly known as the dense-leaf polypody, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and Africa. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its fronds are usually leathery and have a dense arrangement of small, lance-shaped leaves.

Polypodium pycnolepis is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and can thrive in shaded garden settings.
