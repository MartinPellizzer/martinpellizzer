Peperomia borburatensis

Peperomia borburatensis, commonly known as the Brazilian peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, often with a bluish-gray or silver-green hue. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, usually green or reddish, which are followed by dark brown, elongated seed capsules. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia borburatensis is valued for its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
