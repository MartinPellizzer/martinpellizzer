Polygala difficilis

Polygala difficilis, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, often pink or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are usually small and opposite, and it produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Polygala difficilis is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
