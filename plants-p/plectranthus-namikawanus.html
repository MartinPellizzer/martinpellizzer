Plectranthus namikawanus

Plectranthus namikawanus, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and vibrant, velvety flowers that come in a range of colors. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Plectranthus namikawanus is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
