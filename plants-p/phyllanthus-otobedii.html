Phyllanthus otobedii

Phyllanthus otobedii, commonly known as African nightshade, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant produces red, fleshy fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. It is known for its adaptability to various soil types and can thrive in both dry and moist conditions.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
