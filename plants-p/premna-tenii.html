Premna tenii

Premna tenii, commonly known as the silver vine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, berry-like fruits. It is a member of the Verbenaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Premna tenii thrives in moist, shaded environments and is relatively easy to care for in a garden setting.
