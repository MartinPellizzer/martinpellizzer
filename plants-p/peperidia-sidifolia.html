Peperidia sidifolia

Peperidia sidifolia, commonly known as the swordleaf peperomia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperidia sidifolia is relatively low maintenance and suitable for both novice and experienced plant growers.
