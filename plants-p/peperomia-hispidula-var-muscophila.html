Peperomia hispidula var. muscophila

Peperomia hispidula var. muscophila, commonly known as the mossy peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, glossy, dark green foliage with a velvety texture, and its leaves are often covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a moss-like appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pale green flowers that are typically hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to shaded or semi-shaded environments.
