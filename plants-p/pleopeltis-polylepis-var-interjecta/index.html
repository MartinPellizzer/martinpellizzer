Pleopeltis Polylepis Var. Interjecta

Cushion fern, scientifically known as Pleopeltis polylepis var. interjecta, is a unique and resilient plant species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia. Its compact, low-growing form allows it to thrive in shaded, humid environments where light is limited. The plant's dense foliage creates a soft, cushion-like appearance, making it a striking addition to both natural and cultivated landscapes. Its adaptability to moist, well-drained soil makes it a popular choice for terrariums and indoor gardening. Despite its delicate look, the cushion fern is remarkably hardy and capable of surviving in a variety of ecological conditions.
