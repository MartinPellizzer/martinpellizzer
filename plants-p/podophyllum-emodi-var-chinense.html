Podophyllum emodi var. chinense

Podophyllum emodi var. chinense, commonly known as Chinese mayapple, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Berberidaceae and is characterized by its broad, waxy leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces a distinctive fruit that turns from green to red as it matures. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.
