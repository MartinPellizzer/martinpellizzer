Peperomia persuculenta

Peperomia persuculenta, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, often adorned with silver or white veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate moisture.

Peperomia persuculenta is prized for its ornamental foliage and is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
