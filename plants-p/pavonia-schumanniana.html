Pavonia schumanniana

Pavonia schumanniana, commonly known as the scarlet pavonia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, bright red or pink bract-like flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough texture, while its stems are often hairy.

Pavonia schumanniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
