Podochilus steffensianus

Podochilus steffensianus, commonly known as the Java orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, often in shades of white, pink, or purple.

The leaves are elongated and grass-like, contributing to its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
