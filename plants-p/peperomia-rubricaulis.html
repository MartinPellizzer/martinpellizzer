Peperomia rubricaulis

Peperomia rubricaulis, commonly known as the red-stemmed peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish or bronze tinge on the undersides. The plant produces delicate, clustered inflorescences with tiny, pinkish-white flowers during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia rubricaulis is valued for its ornamental foliage and low maintenance requirements.
