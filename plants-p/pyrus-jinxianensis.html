Pyrus jinxianensis

Pyrus jinxianensis, commonly known as the Jinxian pear, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and clusters of white flowers that bloom in early spring. The tree produces small, sweet fruits that are typically harvested in late summer. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been cultivated for both its fruit and aesthetic appeal.

However, it is relatively rare in cultivation and requires specific growing conditions to thrive.
