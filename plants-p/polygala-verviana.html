Polygala verviana

Polygala verviana, commonly known as the slender milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. The plant features slender stems with small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered in spicate inflorescences, usually in shades of pink or purple.

Polygala verviana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
