Photinia lucida

Photinia lucida, commonly known as the glossy leaf photinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, glossy, dark green leaves that turn bright red in the fall. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by red, fleshy berries that are edible for birds. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
