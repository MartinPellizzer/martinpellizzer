Pulicaria stocksii

Pulicaria stocksii, commonly known as the pale centaury, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, and its leaves are narrow, silvery-gray, and covered in fine hairs. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. It is also known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.

Pulicaria stocksii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, supporting pollinators in its native habitat.
