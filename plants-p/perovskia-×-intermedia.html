Perovskia × intermedia

Perovskia × intermedia, commonly known as Russian sage, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Lamiaceae.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and spikes of blue-lilac flowers that bloom in summer. Native to the Caucasus region, it is a hardy perennial that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. The plant's compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting flowers and drought tolerance.
