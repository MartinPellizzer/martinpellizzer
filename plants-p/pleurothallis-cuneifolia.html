Pleurothallis cuneifolia

Pleurothallis cuneifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees, deriving moisture and nutrients from the air and rain. The plant features delicate, narrow leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with high air circulation.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often sought after by orchid enthusiasts and botanists.
