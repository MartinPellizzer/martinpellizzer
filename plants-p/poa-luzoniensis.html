Poa luzoniensis

Poa luzoniensis, commonly known as Luzon grass, is a species of grass native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry blades. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and forest clearings. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

Poa luzoniensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
