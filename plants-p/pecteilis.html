Pecteilis

Pecteilis, commonly known as the yellow pearly everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its showy, yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces seed heads that resemble small, pearly spheres. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Pecteilis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
