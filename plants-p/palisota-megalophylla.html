Palisota megalophylla

Palisota megalophylla, commonly known as the giant fern or large-leaf fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad, and often deeply lobed fronds that can reach several feet in length. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests or along stream banks. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

The fronds are often used in traditional medicine and as ornamental plants due to their striking appearance.
