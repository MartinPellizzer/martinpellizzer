Platyclinis odorata

Platyclinis odorata, commonly known as the sweet-scented fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that emit a subtle, pleasant fragrance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its leaves are typically light green and have a slightly waxy texture.

Platyclinis odorata is valued not only for its ornamental appeal but also for its potential medicinal properties.
