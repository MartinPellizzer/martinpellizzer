Pavonia hotteana

Pavonia hotteana, commonly known as the Brazilian flame flower, is a shrub native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant red to orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often hairy and have a rough texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for warm climates.

Pavonia hotteana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in landscaping and garden design.
