Pinanga nenga var. pachystachya

Pinanga nenga var. pachystachya, commonly known as the broad-stemmed bamboo palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, feather-like leaves that give it a lush, ornamental appearance. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments and prefers well-draining soil. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ability to tolerate low light conditions.

Its growth habit makes it suitable for both indoor and outdoor landscaping in tropical climates.
