Pentopetia androsaemifolia var. cordifolia

Pentopetia androsaemifolia var. cordifolia, commonly known as the heart-leaf pentopetia, is a variant of the pentopetia genus native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous shrub with lanceolate, opposite leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in inflorescences that resemble umbels.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various soil types.
