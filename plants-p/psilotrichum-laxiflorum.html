Psilotrichum laxiflorum

Psilotrichum laxiflorum, commonly known as the slender-flowered psilotrichum, is a small, herbaceous plant belonging to the family Psilotaceae.

It is characterized by its simple, unbranched stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces tiny, greenish flowers that are often concealed among the foliage. It is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices.

Due to its limited distribution and delicate nature, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native habitat.
