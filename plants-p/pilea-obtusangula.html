Pilea obtusangula

Pilea obtusangula, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its round, fleshy leaves that resemble small coins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically hidden beneath its leaves. It is a popular houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
