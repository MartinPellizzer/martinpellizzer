Petunia × atkinsiana

Petunia × atkinsiana, commonly known as the Atkinsiana petunia, is a hybrid species within the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a range of colors, including purple, blue, and white. The plant is typically an annual or perennial herb, depending on the climate, and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure. Its foliage is heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are slender and branching.

This species is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
