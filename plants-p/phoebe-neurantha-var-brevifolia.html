Phoebe neurantha var. brevifolia

Phoebe neurantha var. brevifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved phoebe, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically less than 5 cm in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually pale green to white in color. Its fruit is a small, woody capsule containing several seeds.

This species is often valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in horticulture for its dense foliage and adaptability to various soil types.
