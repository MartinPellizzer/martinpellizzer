Phoebe granatensis

Phoebe granatensis, commonly known as the granadilla phoebe, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Lauraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly in countries like Colombia and Venezuela. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, round drupe with a smooth, shiny exterior.

The botanical features of Phoebe granatensis make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological studies.
