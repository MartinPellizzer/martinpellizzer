Pteris undulatipinna

Pteris undulatipinna, commonly known as the wavy pinna fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Pteridaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, wavy fronds that give it a unique and elegant appearance. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with long, creeping rhizomes that help it spread and colonize new areas. Its fronds are typically bipinnate, with a delicate, feathery structure that enhances its ornamental value.

Pteris undulatipinna is often found in shaded, moist environments, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens and shaded landscapes.
