Pleurothallis lindleyana

Pleurothallis lindleyana, commonly known as the Lindley's orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and yellow, often clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, organic-rich substrates.

Due to its unique beauty and rarity, it is often sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
