Peperomia glabella var. nigropunctata

Peperomia glabella var. nigropunctata, commonly known as the Black Spotted Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves with a distinctive dark purple to black speckling, giving it a striking visual appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant among plant enthusiasts.
