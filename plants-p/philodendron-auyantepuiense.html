Philodendron auyantepuiense

Philodendron auyantepuiense, commonly known as the Angel's Wings Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to the dense rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant has a unique ability to climb by wrapping its stems around supports, which is an adaptation to its natural habitat. Its foliage is often a deep green with a slightly glossy texture.

It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
