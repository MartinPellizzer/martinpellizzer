Papaver tianschanicum

Papaver tianschanicum, commonly known as the Tian Shan poppy, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange petals and a prominent black seed capsule. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It belongs to the Papaveraceae family, which includes other well-known species like the opium poppy.

The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat.
