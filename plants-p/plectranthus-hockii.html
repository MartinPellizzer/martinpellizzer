Plectranthus hockii

Plectranthus hockii, commonly known as the Blue Ghost Plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are usually narrow and slightly hairy, with a distinctive grayish-blue color.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
