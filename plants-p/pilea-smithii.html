Pilea smithii

Pilea smithii, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial shrub native to China.

It features rounded, glossy, dark green leaves that are slightly notched and resemble small coins, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Pilea smithii is easy to care for and often used in terrariums or as a decorative indoor plant.
