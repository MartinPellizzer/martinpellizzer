Pennisetum ovale

Pennisetum ovale, commonly known as fountain grass, is a tall, ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features slender, arching blades that form a dense, fountain-like shape, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that add visual interest throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Pennisetum ovale is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract birds and beneficial insects.
