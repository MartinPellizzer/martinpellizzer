Prosthechea arminii

Prosthechea arminii, commonly known as the Armin's filmy fern, is a member of the Polypodiaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant's rhizomes are short and often covered in a thin, membranous sheath. Its fronds are usually dichotomously branched and have a distinctive, almost translucent appearance.

Prosthechea arminii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high air humidity for optimal growth.
