Pilea wrightiana var. microphylla

Pilea wrightiana var. microphylla, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, low-maintenance houseplant native to China.

It features rounded, glossy leaves that are smaller than those of the standard Pilea wrightiana, giving it a more delicate appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique leaf shape and ease of care have made it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
