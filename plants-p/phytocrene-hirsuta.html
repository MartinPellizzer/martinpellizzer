Phytocrene hirsuta

Phytocrene hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy phytocrene, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.
