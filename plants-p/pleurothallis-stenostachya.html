Pleurothallis stenostachya

Pleurothallis stenostachya, commonly known as the narrow-stalked pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of tiny, white or pale green flowers. Its flowers are often fragrant and bloom during the wet season.

Pleurothallis stenostachya is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
