Panicum akoense

Panicum akoense, commonly known as the Akoense grass, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and feathery plumes. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its seeds are small and wind-dispersed, contributing to its ability to spread across various environments.

Panicum akoense is often used in traditional medicine and for soil stabilization in agricultural areas.
