Philadelphus pekinensis var. laxiflorus

Philadelphus pekinensis var. laxiflorus, commonly known as the Chinese mock orange, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has slender, arching branches and oval-shaped leaves with a smooth texture. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in garden settings.
