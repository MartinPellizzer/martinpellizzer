Prestonia gracilis

Prestonia gracilis, commonly known as the slender prestonia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It is a climbing vine that produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a slight pubescence. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in humid, shaded environments.

Its botanical significance lies in its ornamental value and its role in local ecosystems.
