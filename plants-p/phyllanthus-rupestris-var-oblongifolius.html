Phyllanthus rupestris var. oblongifolius

Phyllanthus rupestris var. oblongifolius, commonly known as the rock phyllanthus, is a succulent plant native to arid and rocky regions.

It features compact, branching stems covered in a dense layer of pale green to grayish-white hair. The leaves are elongated and oval-shaped, with a waxy surface that helps reduce water loss. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

This plant is well adapted to drought conditions and thrives in full sun with minimal water.
