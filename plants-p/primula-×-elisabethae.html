Primula × elisabethae

Primula × elisabethae, commonly known as the Elizabeth primrose, is a hybrid plant in the Primulaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 15–30 cm and prefers cool, moist environments. It has dark green, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
