Pelargonium × annesleyanum

Pelargonium × annesleyanum, commonly known as the Annesleyan geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a range of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically features rounded, serrated leaves that are dark green and hairy. Native to South Africa, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

It is widely cultivated as an ornamental plant for gardens and container growing.
