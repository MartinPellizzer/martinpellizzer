Piper dilatatum var. broadwayi

Piper dilatatum var. broadwayi, commonly known as the Broadway pepper plant, is a variant of the broadleaf pepper species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its broad, ovate leaves with a glossy green surface and prominent veins. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers followed by elliptical, dark purple berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to tropical gardens and indoor plant collections.
