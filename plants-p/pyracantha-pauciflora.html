Pyracantha pauciflora

Pyracantha pauciflora, commonly known as the Chinese firethorn, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features thorny stems and small, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright orange-red berries in the fall. These berries are edible but have a tart flavor and are often used in culinary applications.

Pyracantha pauciflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in various climates.
