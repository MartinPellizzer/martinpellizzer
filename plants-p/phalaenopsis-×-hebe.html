Phalaenopsis × hebe

Phalaenopsis × hebe, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Phalaenopsis and Hebe.

It is a sympodial orchid with a compact growth habit and produces showy, long-lasting flowers in various colors. The plant typically has broad, fleshy leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining potting media.

This hybrid is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of care.
