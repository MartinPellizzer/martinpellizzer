Panicum pennatum

Panicum pennatum, commonly known as the feather grass, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery plumes that give it a graceful appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas such as meadows and prairies. Its botanical features include narrow, grass-like leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences.

Panicum pennatum is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
