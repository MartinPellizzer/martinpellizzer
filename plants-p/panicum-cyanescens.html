Panicum cyanescens

Panicum cyanescens, commonly known as blue panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking blue-green foliage, which becomes more pronounced under certain environmental conditions. The plant typically grows in open areas, such as fields and roadsides, and thrives in well-drained soils. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small, blue-green flowers.

Panicum cyanescens is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various climates.
