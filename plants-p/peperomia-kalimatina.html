Peperomia kalimatina

Peperomia kalimatina, commonly known as the Kalimatina peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to Indonesia.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale green, which appear in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Due to its ornamental foliage, it is often grown as a decorative houseplant.
