Peperomia fragilissima

Peperomia fragilissima, commonly known as the brittle peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its delicate leaves, it requires careful handling to avoid damage.
