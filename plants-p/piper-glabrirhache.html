Piper glabrirhache

Piper glabrirhache, commonly known as the smooth-leaved pepper vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its smooth, glossy leaves and small, clustered greenish-white flowers. Its fruits are typically three-lobed berries that mature to a dark purple color.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in some indigenous communities.
