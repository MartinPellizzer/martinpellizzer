Pleurothallis jamaicensis

Pleurothallis jamaicensis, commonly known as the Jamaican pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the Caribbean region.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, tropical environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It is part of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ornamental value.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered threatened in its native range.
