Pourouma melinonii subsp. glabrata

Pourouma melinonii subsp.

glabrata, commonly known as the smooth-peltate areca, is a species of palm native to South America. It is characterized by its large, pinnately compound leaves and smooth, glabrous leaf sheaths.

The plant typically grows in wetland or swampy habitats, preferring warm and humid climates. Its trunk is often swollen and covered with persistent leaf bases, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been introduced to other regions for horticultural purposes.
