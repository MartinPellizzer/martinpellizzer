Pultenaea retusa

Pultenaea retusa, commonly known as the slender pultenaea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in spikes, which bloom in spring. Its seed pods are slender and curved, typical of the genus Pultenaea.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions and sandy soils.
