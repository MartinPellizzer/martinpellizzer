Paeonia revelieri

Paeonia revelieri, commonly known as the Chinese peony, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and its stems are covered in thorns. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a striking visual element to the landscape.
