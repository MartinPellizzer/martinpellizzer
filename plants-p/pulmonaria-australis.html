Pulmonaria australis

Pulmonaria australis, commonly known as the Australian lungwort, is a perennial herb native to Australia.

It features rosettes of dark green, velvety leaves with distinctive silver veins. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade, making it a suitable addition to woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and early spring flowering.
