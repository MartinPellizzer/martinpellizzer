Peperomia estrellensis

Peperomia estrellensis, commonly known as the Estrellensis Peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features glossy, ovate leaves that are often dark green with a waxy texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, and it is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements.

Peperomia estrellensis thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.
