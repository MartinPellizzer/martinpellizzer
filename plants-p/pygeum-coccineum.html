Pygeum coccineum

Pygeum coccineum, commonly known as the red sycamore, is a deciduous tree native to parts of southern Europe and North Africa.

Pygeum coccineum is often found in dry, sunny environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical uses in traditional medicine.
