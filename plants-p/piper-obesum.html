Piper obesum

Piper obesum, commonly known as the fat pepper or bird's eye pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its small, green, and slightly hairy leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by small, round, and peppery fruits. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their aromatic and medicinal properties.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its culinary and medicinal uses.
