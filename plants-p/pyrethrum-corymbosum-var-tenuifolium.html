Pyrethrum corymbosum var. tenuifolium

Pyrethrum corymbosum var. tenuifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces a variety of pyrethrins, which are natural insecticides used in organic pest control. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is widely appreciated in both horticulture and herbal medicine.
