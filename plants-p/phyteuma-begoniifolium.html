Phyteuma begoniifolium

Phyteuma begoniifolium, commonly known as the begoniifolium phyteuma, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features striking, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange with a distinctive, often bilaterally symmetrical shape. The plant has heart-shaped to ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its unique appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile choice for rock gardens or borders.

Due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular plant among gardeners interested in cultivating native or hardy species.
