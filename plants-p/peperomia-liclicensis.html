Peperomia liclicensis

Peperomia liclicensis, commonly known as the Liclicensis Peperomia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a compact, low-growing plant with thick, waxy leaves that are typically green or variegated in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in hue. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia liclicensis is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.
