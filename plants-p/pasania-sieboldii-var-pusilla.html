Pasania sieboldii var. pusilla

Pasania sieboldii var. pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf pasania, is a small, herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features small, elliptical leaves with a smooth texture and a bluish-green hue. Its flowers are clustered in umbels, producing pale yellow to white blooms.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
