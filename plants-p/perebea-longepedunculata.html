Perebea longepedunculata

Perebea longepedunculata, commonly known as the long peduncle perebea, is a tropical shrub native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces oval, woody fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understorey of tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
