Portulaca brevioperculata

Portulaca brevioperculata, commonly known as the short-beaked portulaca, is a species of flowering plant in the family Portulacaceae.

It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in the morning. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it well adapted to dry environments.

Portulaca brevioperculata is often found growing in rocky or sandy soils along the edges of grasslands.
