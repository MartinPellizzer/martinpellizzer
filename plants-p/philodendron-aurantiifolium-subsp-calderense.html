Philodendron aurantiifolium subsp. calderense

Philodendron aurantiifolium subsp.

calderense, commonly known as the Red-leaf Philodendron, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its vibrant, reddish-orange foliage. It belongs to the Araceae family and is native to the rainforests of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru.

The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a waxy texture and a unique, often variegated coloration that adds visual interest to any indoor space. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil to flourish.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts and interior designers.
