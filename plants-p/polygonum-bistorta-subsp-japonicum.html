Polygonum bistorta subsp. japonicum

Polygonum bistorta subsp.

japonicum, commonly known as Japanese bistort, is a perennial herb native to East Asia. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides.

The plant features upright, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a reddish underside. Its flowers are small, clustered in spikes, and are usually pink to purple in color.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in wet conditions.
