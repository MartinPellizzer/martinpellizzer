Platymiscium floribundum var. nitens

Platymiscium floribundum var. nitens, commonly known as the shining flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow to orange-red flowers that appear in dense clusters, giving it a fiery appearance. The leaves are typically alternate, simple, and ovate, with a glossy green surface. The tree produces small, round, woody fruits that contain several seeds.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
