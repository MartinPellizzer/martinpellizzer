Pedicularis friderici-augusti subsp. montenegrina

Pedicularis friderici-augusti subsp.

montenegrina, commonly known as the Montenegro beard vetch, is a perennial herb native to the Balkan region. It belongs to the family Orobanchaceae and is characterized by its glandular-hairy stems and showy, spurred flowers.

This subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its distinctive appearance.
