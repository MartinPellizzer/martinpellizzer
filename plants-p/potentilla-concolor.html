Potentilla concolor

Potentilla concolor, commonly known as the smooth cinquefoil, is a perennial flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or rocky areas.

Potentilla concolor is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
