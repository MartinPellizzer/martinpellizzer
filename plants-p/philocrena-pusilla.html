Philocrena pusilla

Philocrena pusilla, commonly known as the small rock cress, is a small flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas. The plant has small, basal leaves and produces clusters of tiny, white or pale yellow flowers. It is an annual or biennial species, depending on the climate.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to harsh, sunny environments.
