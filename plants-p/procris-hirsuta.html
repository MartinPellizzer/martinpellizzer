Procris hirsuta

Procris hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy procris, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, which contribute to its common name. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in clusters.

Procris hirsuta is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks.
