Premna cumingiana f. dentata

Premna cumingiana f. dentata, commonly known as the serrated premna, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants. The leaves are ovate to elliptical, with serrated margins, and are often pubescent beneath. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale purple, arranged in clusters.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
