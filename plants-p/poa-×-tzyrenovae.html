Poa × tzyrenovae

Poa × tzyrenovae, commonly known as the Tzyrenovae bluegrass, is a hybrid grass species native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted blades and a bluish-green coloration, typical of many bluegrass species. The plant typically grows in temperate climates and is often found in meadows and grasslands. It has a clump-forming growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in horticulture.
