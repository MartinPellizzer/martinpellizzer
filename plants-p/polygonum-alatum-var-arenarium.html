Polygonum alatum var. arenarium

Polygonum alatum var. arenarium, commonly known as the sand polygonum, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It is characterized by its distinctive, winged stems that give it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in sandy or rocky habitats.

Due to its adaptability, it is sometimes used in xeriscaping and ecological restoration projects.
