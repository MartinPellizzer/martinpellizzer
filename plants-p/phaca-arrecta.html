Phaca arrecta

Phaca arrecta, commonly known as the upright phaca, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. Phaca arrecta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
