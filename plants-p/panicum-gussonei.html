Panicum gussonei

Panicum gussonei, commonly known as the Gussone's panicgrass, is a species of grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are often clustered at the base of the stems. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a common sight in open, disturbed areas.

Botanically, it is noted for its ability to form dense mats, which can help stabilize soil in certain environments.
