Podocarpus capuronii var. woltzii

Podocarpus capuronii var. woltzii, commonly known as the Woltzii podocarp, is a compact, evergreen shrub or small tree native to Madagascar.

It features dense, dark green foliage with leathery, oval-shaped leaves that provide a lush appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by spherical, woody cones that mature to a reddish-brown color. It is well-suited for container gardening and can thrive in shaded to partially sunny conditions.

Its ornamental value lies in its attractive foliage and adaptability to various soil types.
