Pavonia stipularis

Pavonia stipularis, commonly known as the scarlet gladeclover, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 feet. The plant features showy, bright red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive red or purple tint on the underside.

Pavonia stipularis is often found in moist, open habitats such as prairies and woodland edges.
