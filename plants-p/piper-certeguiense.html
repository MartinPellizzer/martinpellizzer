Piper certeguiense

Piper certeguiense, commonly known as the Cerroguiense pepper, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Ecuador.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with opposite, ovate leaves that have a rough texture. Its small, greenish-white flowers are borne in clusters, and it produces small, round berries that turn red when mature.

The plant is valued for its aromatic leaves and has traditional uses in local medicine.
