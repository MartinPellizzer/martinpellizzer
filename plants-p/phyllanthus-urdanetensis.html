Phyllanthus urdanetensis

Phyllanthus urdanetensis, commonly known as the UrdaNet sycamore, is a shrub native to South America, particularly found in regions of Venezuela and Colombia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in cyathia, typical of the genus Phyllanthus.

It is often found in moist, tropical environments and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
