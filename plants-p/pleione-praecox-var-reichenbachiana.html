Pleione praecox var. reichenbachiana

Pleione praecox var. reichenbachiana, commonly known as the early pleione, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically blooms in early spring, making it a sought-after ornamental in horticulture. It thrives in cool, humid environments with partial shade and well-draining soil.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled settings.
