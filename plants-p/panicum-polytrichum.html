Panicum polytrichum

Panicum polytrichum, commonly known as the red panic grass, is a species of grass native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces dense, red-brown inflorescences that are often used in floral arrangements. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a fibrous texture that contributes to its ornamental appeal.

Panicum polytrichum is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in certain landscapes.
