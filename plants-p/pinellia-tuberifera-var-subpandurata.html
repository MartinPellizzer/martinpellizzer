Pinellia tuberifera var. subpandurata

Pinellia tuberifera var. subpandurata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pinellia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features rhizomatous growth and produces clusters of narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant bears white or pale purple flowers arranged in umbels, followed by small, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands.

Its tuberous roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
