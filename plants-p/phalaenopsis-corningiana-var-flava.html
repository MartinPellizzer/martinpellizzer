Phalaenopsis corningiana var. flava

Phalaenopsis corningiana var. flava, commonly known as the Yellow Corning Orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which are often adorned with subtle green or white markings. This variety is known for its compact growth habit and ability to bloom repeatedly throughout the year under proper care. The plant typically features broad, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
