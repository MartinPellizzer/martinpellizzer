Prunus jamasakura f. stellata

Prunus jamasakura f. stellata, commonly known as the star-form Japanese cherry, is a unique variant of the Japanese cherry tree.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped flowers, which bloom in early spring. The plant is native to Japan and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is closely related to other cherry species.

The foliage is typically dark green, and the tree has a compact, rounded shape.
