Piper dependens

Piper dependens, commonly known as the hanging pepper or climbing pepper, is a tropical vine native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its long, trailing stems and small, opposite leaves. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.

The fruit is a small, three-lobed berry that turns from green to red when mature.
