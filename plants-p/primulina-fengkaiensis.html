Primulina fengkaiensis

Primulina fengkaiensis, commonly known as the Fengkai primula, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or mountainous regions. It has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is often found in humid, temperate climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
