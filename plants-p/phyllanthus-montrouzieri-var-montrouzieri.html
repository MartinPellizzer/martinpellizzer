Phyllanthus montrouzieri var. montrouzieri

Phyllanthus montrouzieri var. montrouzieri, commonly known as the African date palm, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, glossy, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, arranged in clusters, and it produces small, fleshy fruits that are edible.

The plant is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
