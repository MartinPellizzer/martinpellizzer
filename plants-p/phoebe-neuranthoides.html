Phoebe neuranthoides

Phoebe neuranthoides, commonly known as the small-leaf phoebe, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central America and northern South America. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically less than 5 cm in length.
