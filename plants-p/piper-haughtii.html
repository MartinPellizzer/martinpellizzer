Piper haughtii

Piper haughtii, commonly known as the Mexican pepper plant, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters. It produces small, round berries that are typically green when immature and turn red when ripe.

Piper haughtii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in herbal medicine.
