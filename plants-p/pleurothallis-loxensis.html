Pleurothallis loxensis

Pleurothallis loxensis, commonly known as the Loxen's pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant has delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is part of the genus Pleurothallis, which includes over 1,000 species.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
