Pennisetum dichotomum var. subplumosum

Pennisetum dichotomum var. subplumosum, commonly known as the purple fountain grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender stalks with feathery, purple-tinted plumes that add a dramatic visual element to gardens. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.

Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a slightly blue-green hue that enhances its ornamental appeal.
