Paphiopedilum Javanicum Var. Virens

Paphiopedilum javanicum var. virens is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the tropical regions of Indonesia. Known for its striking greenish-yellow flowers and velvety foliage, it captivates botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. This variant of the Paphiopedilum genus is prized for its unique coloration and elegant structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful cultivation to flourish. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure species in the world of orchids.
