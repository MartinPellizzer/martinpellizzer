Piper monostachyos

Piper monostachyos, commonly known as the small-fruit pepper plant, is a tropical climbing shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when mature, containing aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are often used in herbal remedies due to their pungent and medicinal properties.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
