Prieurella

Prieurella, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a genus of aquatic plants belonging to the family Nymphaeaceae.

It is characterized by its floating leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom above the water surface. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, particularly India and Southeast Asia. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
