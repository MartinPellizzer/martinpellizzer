Pleurothallis ocellus

Pleurothallis ocellus, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a small epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. Its flowers are often adorned with intricate patterns and markings, which contribute to its ornamental value.

Pleurothallis ocellus is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
