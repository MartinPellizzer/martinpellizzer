Phyllorkis aurantiaca

Phyllorkis aurantiaca, commonly known as the golden flame lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer, adding vibrant color to gardens. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are narrow and pointed, contributing to its elegant appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique coloration and seasonal blooming make it a popular choice among horticultural enthusiasts.
