Plectranthus longipetiolatus

Plectranthus longipetiolatus, commonly known as the long-petioled plectranthus, is a herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features opposite, ovate leaves with long petioles, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale purple to pink in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for warm climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
