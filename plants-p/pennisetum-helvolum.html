Pennisetum helvolum

Pennisetum helvolum, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and striking purple to pink plumes that appear in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Pennisetum helvolum is also valued for its ability to attract birds and pollinators.
