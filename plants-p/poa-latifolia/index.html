Poa Latifolia

Poa latifolia is a tall grass species native to the temperate regions of the northern hemisphere. It is commonly found in moist, well-drained soils along stream banks and in meadows. Known for its striking blue-green foliage, this plant adds a unique visual element to its surroundings. Its ability to thrive in diverse conditions makes it a popular choice for ornamental and ecological landscaping. Despite its beauty, it can sometimes become invasive in certain environments.
