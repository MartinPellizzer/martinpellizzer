Polianthes bicolor

Polianthes bicolor, commonly known as the rainbow orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful flowers that range from white to pink, red, and purple, often in a gradient pattern. The plant produces long, slender stems with narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically a deep green. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Polianthes bicolor is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice for gardens and indoor spaces.
