Poa bulbosa f. patens

Poa bulbosa f. patens, commonly known as the spreading meadow grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, often spreading stems. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences with narrow, blade-like leaves. It is typically found in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides.

This variety is noted for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions and its resilience to environmental stress.
