Pyrethrum leucopilodes

Pyrethrum leucopilodes, commonly known as the white-flowered pyrethrum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white ray florets and yellow disk florets. The plant produces small, hairy leaves and thrives in well-drained soils with full sun exposure.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its mild insecticidal properties.
