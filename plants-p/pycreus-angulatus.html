Pycreus angulatus

Pycreus angulatus, commonly known as the angular pycreus, is a rare and lesser-known species within the Pycreus genus.

It is characterized by its distinctive angular leaf shape and small, clustered flowers. Native to specific regions in South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant has a short stature and is often found in dense, forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a challenging species for cultivation.
