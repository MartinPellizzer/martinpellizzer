Polystichum minimum

Polystichum minimum, commonly known as the silver fern, is a small evergreen fern native to the cool, moist forests of eastern North America.

It features delicate, finely divided, silver-gray fronds that give it a distinctive and elegant appearance. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10-15 centimeters, making it a compact and ornamental addition to shaded gardens. It prefers partial to full shade and well-drained, acidic soil, thriving in woodland environments.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and as a decorative plant in containers.
