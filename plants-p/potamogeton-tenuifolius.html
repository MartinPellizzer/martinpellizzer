Potamogeton tenuifolius

Potamogeton tenuifolius, commonly known as narrow-leaved pondweed, is an aquatic plant native to freshwater habitats across Europe, Asia, and North America.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually arranged in dense clusters, and it thrives in shallow, slow-moving waters.
