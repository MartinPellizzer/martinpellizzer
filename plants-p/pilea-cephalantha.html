Pilea cephalantha

Pilea cephalantha, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rounded, glossy dark green leaves that are typically shaped like a coin, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea cephalantha is relatively low maintenance and can be propagated easily through stem cuttings.
