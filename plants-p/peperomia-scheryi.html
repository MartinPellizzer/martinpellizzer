Peperomia scheryi

Peperomia scheryi, commonly known as the Schery's pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are typically green with reddish or bronze undersides, adding visual interest. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to avoid root rot.

Peperomia scheryi is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
