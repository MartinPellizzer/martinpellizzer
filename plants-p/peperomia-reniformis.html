Peperomia reniformis

Peperomia reniformis, commonly known as the kidney leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, kidney-shaped leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate watering.

Peperomia reniformis is prized for its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
