Pleurothallis chamaestelis

Pleurothallis chamaestelis, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, yellowish-green flowers with a slight fragrance. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or under controlled conditions.
