Piper leptostachyon

Piper leptostachyon, commonly known as the slender-stalked pepper plant, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many pepper species. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, narrow fruits. Its leaves are typically ovate with a slightly hairy texture.

The plant is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
