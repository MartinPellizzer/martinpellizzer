Peucedanum galbanum

Peucedanum galbanum, commonly known as the galbanum plant, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and finely divided, fern-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring. Its seeds are enclosed in small, hairy pods, and the plant is often found in dry, rocky soils.

Peucedanum galbanum is also valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine.
