Phragmipedium lindleyanum var. sargentianum

Phragmipedium lindleyanum var. sargentianum, commonly known as the Sargent's phragmipedium, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively manageable cultivation requirements.
