Pleurothallis minutissima

Pleurothallis minutissima, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tiny, delicate flowers and compact growth habit, often making it one of the smallest orchid species. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with intricate patterns that attract specific pollinators.

Due to its small size and rarity, it is a sought-after specimen among orchid enthusiasts and botanists.
