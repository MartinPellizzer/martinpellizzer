Protium sessiliflorum

Protium sessiliflorum, commonly known as the sessile-flower breadfruit, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Sapindaceae and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and elliptical leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are borne in clusters, followed by edible, starchy fruits similar to breadfruit. The plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities and potential medicinal applications.
