Panicum haplocladum

Panicum haplocladum, commonly known as slender panicgrass, is a species of grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as disturbed soils and sandy areas. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers.

Panicum haplocladum is often found in wetland environments and is adapted to tolerate periodic flooding.
