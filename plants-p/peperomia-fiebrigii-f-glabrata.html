Peperomia fiebrigii f. glabrata

Peperomia fiebrigii f. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth-leaved peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically smooth and glossy, with a dark green hue. The plant produces delicate, spathiflorous inflorescences that bear tiny, red or orange flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental foliage and compact growth habit, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
