Phreatia caulescens

Phreatia caulescens, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its delicate appearance and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
