Peperomia pubirhachis

Peperomia pubirhachis, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, velvety leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly textured surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia pubirhachis is a low-maintenance plant that is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
