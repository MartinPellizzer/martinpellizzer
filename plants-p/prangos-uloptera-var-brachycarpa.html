Prangos uloptera var. brachycarpa

Prangos uloptera var. brachycarpa, commonly known as the short-seeded goat's beard, is a rare and lesser-known botanical species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, branched stems and distinctive, feathery inflorescences. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which are often surrounded by modified leaves called bracts.
