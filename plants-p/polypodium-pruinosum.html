Polypodium pruinosum

Polypodium pruinosum, commonly known as the glossy polypody, is a fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, leathery fronds that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. The plant typically grows on rocks or tree trunks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in forest ecosystems.
