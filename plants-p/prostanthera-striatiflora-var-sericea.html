Prostanthera striatiflora var. sericea

Prostanthera striatiflora var. sericea, commonly known as the silky lemon-scented gum, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features aromatic, narrow leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional Indigenous medicine.
