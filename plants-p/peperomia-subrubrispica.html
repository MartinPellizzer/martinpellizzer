Peperomia subrubrispica

Peperomia subrubrispica, commonly known as the red-bristled peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, upright growth with glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish or maroon hue on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia subrubrispica is relatively low maintenance and suitable for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
