Polygala ohlendorfiana

Polygala ohlendorfiana, commonly known as the golden trumpet or ohlendorf's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features a slender, branched stem with small, opposite leaves. Its most distinctive feature is its showy, yellow or orange flowers that bloom in late summer.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
