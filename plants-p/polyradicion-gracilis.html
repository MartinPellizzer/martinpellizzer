Polyradicion gracilis

Polyradicion gracilis, commonly known as the slender leaf fern, is a delicate and graceful fern species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, arching fronds that are typically light green and finely divided, giving it a feathery appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, moist soil to flourish. Its reproductive structures, including spore-bearing sorus, are located on the underside of its fronds.

Polyradicion gracilis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
