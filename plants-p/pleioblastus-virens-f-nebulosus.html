Pleioblastus virens f. nebulosus

Pleioblastus virens f. nebulosus, commonly known as the clouded bamboo, is a variant of the common bamboo species characterized by its distinctive, pale, bluish-green foliage.

This ornamental plant is native to East Asia and is prized for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and landscapes. The foliage exhibits a unique mottled or cloud-like appearance, giving it its common name. It is a clump-forming plant that grows to a moderate height, making it suitable for both container and in-ground cultivation.

The plant is relatively low maintenance and thrives in partial shade to full sun, depending on the climate.
