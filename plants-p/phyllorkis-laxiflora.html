Phyllorkis laxiflora

Phyllorkis laxiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered phyllorkis, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers and narrow leaves. The plant typically grows in heathlands and open forests, preferring well-drained soils. It produces white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators.
