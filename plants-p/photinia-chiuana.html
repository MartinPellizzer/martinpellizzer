Photinia chiuana

Photinia chiuana, commonly known as the Chinese firethorn, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its bright red berries and dense, glossy foliage. The plant produces small, white flowers in the spring, which are followed by the striking red fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it suitable for hedges and garden borders.
