Peperomia mitoensis

Peperomia mitoensis, commonly known as the Mito's pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color.

Peperomia mitoensis is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive foliage.
