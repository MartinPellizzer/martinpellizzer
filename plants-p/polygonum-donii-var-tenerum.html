Polygonum donii var. tenerum

Polygonum donii var. tenerum, commonly known as the softleaf smartweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of East Asia, including China and Japan.

It is characterized by its erect, herbaceous stems that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or red, often in late summer. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate in shape and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Polygonum donii var. tenerum is often found in moist environments such as wet meadows and marshy areas.
