Polygala oligantha

Polygala oligantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, often branched stems and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, with a unique structure that attracts specific pollinators.

The species is of interest in botanical studies due to its ecological role and potential medicinal properties.
