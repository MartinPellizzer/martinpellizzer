Polypodium graminellum

Polypodium graminellum, commonly known as the grass fern, is a small, evergreen fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It has a distinctive, creeping rhizome that produces upright, slender fronds with a grass-like appearance. The fronds are typically dark green and have a soft, velvety texture. It prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in horticulture.
