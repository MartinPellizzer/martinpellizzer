Planchonella asterocarpon

Planchonella asterocarpon, commonly known as the star-fruited hibiscus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by distinctive star-shaped fruits. Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in horticultural studies.

It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
