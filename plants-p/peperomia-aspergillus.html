Peperomia aspergillus

Peperomia aspergillus, commonly known as the rough peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a slightly textured surface, giving it a unique, rugged appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia aspergillus is known for its ability to purify the air and add a touch of greenery to any space.
