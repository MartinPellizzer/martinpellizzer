Peperomia calvicaulis

Peperomia calvicaulis, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface, and it has short, sturdy stems that often form a dense, bushy appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, though they are not showy. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance for low light and minimal water requirements.

Peperomia calvicaulis is often grown as a decorative houseplant because of its attractive foliage and ease of care.
