Pandanus hooglandii

Pandanus hooglandii, commonly known as the highland pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Pandanaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or green in color. Its fibrous leaves are often used in traditional crafts and for thatching.

Pandanus hooglandii thrives in humid, coastal environments and is adapted to withstand salt spray and strong winds.
