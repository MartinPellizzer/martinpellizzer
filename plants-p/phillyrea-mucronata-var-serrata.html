Phillyrea mucronata var. serrata

Phillyrea mucronata var. serrata, commonly known as the serrated Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, oval leaves with serrated edges, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in racemes. These flowers are followed by small, woody fruits that contain a single seed.

Phillyrea mucronata var. serrata is often found in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
