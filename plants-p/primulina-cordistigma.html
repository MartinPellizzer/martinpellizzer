Primulina cordistigma

Primulina cordistigma, commonly known as the heart-leaf arrowroot, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
