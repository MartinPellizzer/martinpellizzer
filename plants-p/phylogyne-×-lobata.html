Phylogyne × lobata

Phylogyne × lobata, commonly known as the hybrid evergreen shrub, is a botanical hybrid resulting from the cross between Phylogyny and Lobata species.

It typically exhibits characteristics of both parent plants, such as dense foliage and adaptability to various soil types. The plant is native to regions with temperate climates and thrives in well-drained soils. It produces small, fragrant flowers that attract pollinators, contributing to its ecological value.

Its ornamental appeal makes it a popular choice for landscaping and garden design.
