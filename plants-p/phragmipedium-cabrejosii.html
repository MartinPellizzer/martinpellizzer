Phragmipedium cabrejosii

Phragmipedium cabrejosii, commonly known as the Cabrejosii phragmipedium, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant features strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its striking floral display.
