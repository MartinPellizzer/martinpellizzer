Phalaenopsis amboinensis var. flavida

Phalaenopsis amboinensis var. flavida, commonly known as the yellow phalaenopsis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendant inflorescences that bear clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The flowers typically exhibit a range of yellow to white hues, with some varieties displaying green or red markings. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its relatively easy care requirements.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent humidity and well-draining soil.
