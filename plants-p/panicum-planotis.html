Panicum planotis

Panicum planotis, commonly known as flatleaf panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its flat, broad leaves and dense, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It is well adapted to dry conditions and can thrive in poor soil environments.

Panicum planotis is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and resilience.
