Pleomele spicata

Pleomele spicata, commonly known as the spiny pleomele, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, leathery leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its robust growth habit makes it suitable for use in ornamental landscaping.

However, it is important to note that the plant contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
