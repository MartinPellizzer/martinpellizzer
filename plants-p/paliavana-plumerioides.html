Paliavana plumerioides

Paliavana plumerioides, commonly known as the Madagascar plumeria, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant has smooth, grayish bark and lance-shaped leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Paliavana plumerioides is also valued for its use in traditional medicine in some regions.
