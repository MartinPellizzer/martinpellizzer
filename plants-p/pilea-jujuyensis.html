Pilea jujuyensis

Pilea jujuyensis, commonly known as the Jujuy begonia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Andean regions of Argentina.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, delicate white flowers with red stamens, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea jujuyensis is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and moderate temperatures.
