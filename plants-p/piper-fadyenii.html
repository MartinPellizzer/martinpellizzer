Piper fadyenii

Piper fadyenii, commonly known as the Fadyenii pepper or African pepper, is a tropical vine native to regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants such as black pepper. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when mature, containing aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and cuisine. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly peppery scent, contributing to its ornamental value.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
