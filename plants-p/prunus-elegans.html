Prunus elegans

Prunus elegans, commonly known as the elegant cherry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring, typically in shades of white or pale pink. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a serrated edge.

Prunus elegans thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.
