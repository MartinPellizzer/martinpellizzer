Primulina yingdeensis

Primulina yingdeensis, commonly known as the Yingde primrose, is a rare and endangered species native to China.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, conservation efforts are crucial for its survival.
