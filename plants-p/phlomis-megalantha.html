Phlomis megalantha

Phlomis megalantha, commonly known as the large-flowered mullein, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of the Mediterranean.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of white, fuzzy hairs.

Phlomis megalantha is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
