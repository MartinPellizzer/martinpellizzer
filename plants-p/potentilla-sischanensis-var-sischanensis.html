Potentilla sischanensis var. sischanensis

Potentilla sischanensis var. sischanensis, commonly known as the Sichuan potentilla, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, often white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or alpine settings. Its leaves are typically dark green and finely toothed, adding visual interest throughout the growing season.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
