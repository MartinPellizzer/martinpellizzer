Polygonum corymbosum var. densiflorum

Polygonum corymbosum var. densiflorum, commonly known as red polygonum, is a variant of the common smartweed.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America, characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, reddish-purple flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist environments such as wetlands, streambanks, and disturbed soils.

This plant is often found in the eastern and central United States, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various insects.
