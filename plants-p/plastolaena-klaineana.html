Plastolaena klaineana

Plastolaena klaineana, commonly known as the red-flowered hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green, providing a striking contrast to its showy blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish.

Plastolaena klaineana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
