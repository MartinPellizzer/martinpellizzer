Pennilabium poringense

Pennilabium poringense, commonly known as the Poringense orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pendulous flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, with a unique coloration that ranges from white to pale pink.

Pennilabium poringense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
