Pseudolycopodiella brevipedunculata

Pseudolycopodiella brevipedunculata, commonly known as the small-branch clubmoss, is a small, evergreen fern-like plant belonging to the family Lycopodiaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and its clusters of tiny, scale-like leaves. Due to its unique morphology and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists studying ancient plant lineages.
