Peperomia crassispica

Peperomia crassispica, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, and its stems are often reddish in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually green or reddish-brown. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia crassispica is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
