Petalochilus gracillimus

Petalochilus gracillimus, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink. The plant has thin, wiry stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is a prized species among orchid enthusiasts.
