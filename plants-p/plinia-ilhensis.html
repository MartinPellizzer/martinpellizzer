Plinia ilhensis

Plinia ilhensis, commonly known as the Ilha Beauty, is a species of plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in humid environments. Its leaves are elliptical and have a glossy surface, while its fruit is small and edible.

Plinia ilhensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and has potential uses in traditional medicine.
