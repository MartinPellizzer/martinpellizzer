Podochilus congestus

Podochilus congestus, commonly known as the clustered orchid, is a tropical plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its compact, branching growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in dense clusters. Its roots are fibrous and often grow in shaded, moist environments.

Podochilus congestus is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
