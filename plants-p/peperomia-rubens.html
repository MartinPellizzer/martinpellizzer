Peperomia rubens

Peperomia rubens, commonly known as the red peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish tinge on the undersides. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that emerge from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia rubens is relatively low maintenance and suitable for beginners due to its tolerance of moderate care conditions.
