Phaseolus hirtuz

Phaseolus hirtuz, commonly known as hairy runner bean, is a flowering plant in the legume family.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces pods that contain edible beans, similar to other Phaseolus species. It is native to the Americas and thrives in warm, temperate climates.

Its botanical features make it a valuable crop for both culinary and agricultural purposes.
