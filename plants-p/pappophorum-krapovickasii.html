Pappophorum krapovickasii

Pappophorum krapovickasii, commonly known as the Krapovickas' pappus grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

Pappophorum krapovickasii is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient choice for xeriscaping.
