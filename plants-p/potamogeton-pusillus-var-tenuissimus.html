Potamogeton pusillus var. tenuissimus

Potamogeton pusillus var. tenuissimus, commonly known as the slender pondweed, is a submerged aquatic plant native to freshwater environments in North America.

It typically grows in shallow, slow-moving waters and has long, narrow leaves that are often ribbon-like in appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually found in clusters near the water surface. It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.

Its rapid growth can sometimes lead to dense vegetation, affecting water flow and oxygen levels in the habitat.
