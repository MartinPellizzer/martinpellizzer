Protium heptaphyllum var. aromaticum

Protium heptaphyllum var. aromaticum, commonly known as the aromatic protium, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its smooth, dark-barked trunk and dense, lance-shaped leaves.
