Pelargonium luteum

Pelargonium luteum, commonly known as the yellow geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and succulent, hairy leaves. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and thrives in full sun. It is a popular choice for gardens due to its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

Pelargonium luteum is also known for its adaptability to various soil types and drought-resistant properties.
