Pandanus syozoi

Pandanus syozoi, commonly known as the New Caledonian pandanus, is a flowering plant native to the island of New Caledonia.

It belongs to the Pandanaceae family and is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in coastal or lowland areas.

The plant is valued for its ornamental leaves and has cultural significance in the region.
