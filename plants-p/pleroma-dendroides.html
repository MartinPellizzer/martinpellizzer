Pleroma dendroides

Pleroma dendroides, commonly known as the tree-like pleroma, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its woody stems and dense foliage, which give it a tree-like appearance. The plant produces small, showy flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a smooth texture.

Pleroma dendroides is often found in humid, tropical environments where it thrives in partial to full sunlight.
