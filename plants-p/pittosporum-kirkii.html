Pittosporum kirkii

Pittosporum kirkii, commonly known as the silver kirk, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in coastal and forested areas, preferring well-drained soils and moderate climates.

Pittosporum kirkii is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.
