Paphiopedilum wenshanense f. album

Paphiopedilum wenshanense f. album, commonly known as the White Wenshan Orchid, is a rare and unique hybrid orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking white petals and sepals, which contrast beautifully with its green foliage. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by enthusiasts due to its elegance and rarity.

The species is also noted for its slow growth and specific care requirements, making it a challenging but rewarding plant to grow.
