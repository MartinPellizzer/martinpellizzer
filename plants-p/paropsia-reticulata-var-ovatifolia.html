Paropsia reticulata var. ovatifolia, commonly known as the reticulated paropsia with ovate leaves, is a member of the Araliaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves with a distinctive reticulate venation pattern. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
