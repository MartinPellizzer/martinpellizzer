Primula rheiniana

Primula rheiniana, commonly known as the Rhine primrose, is a flowering plant native to Central Europe.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are oval and covered in a fine, fuzzy texture.

Primula rheiniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its early spring blooms.
