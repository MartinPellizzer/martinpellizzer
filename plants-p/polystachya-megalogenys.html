Polystachya megalogenys

Polystachya megalogenys, commonly known as the giant flame orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy flowers that resemble flames, typically in shades of red and orange. The plant produces clusters of elongated, fleshy leaves that help it store water in its natural habitat. Its flowering period occurs during the warmer months, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Polystachya megalogenys is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
