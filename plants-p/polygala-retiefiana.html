Polygala retiefiana

Polygala retiefiana, commonly known as the Retief's milkweed, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, and it produces small, round seed pods that release tiny seeds when mature.

It is often found in fynbos vegetation and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance.
