Pilosella kalksburgensis subsp. pleiantha

Pilosella kalksburgensis subsp.

pleiantha, commonly known as the Kalksburg hairstem, is a flowering plant native to Central Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellowish to whitish composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in grassy habitats and has a low, spreading growth habit. Its leaves are usually hairy, giving it the common name "hairstem.".

It is a perennial species that thrives in temperate climates.
