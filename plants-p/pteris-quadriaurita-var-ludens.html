Pteris quadriaurita var. ludens

Pteris quadriaurita var. ludens, commonly known as the golden fern, is a strikingly ornamental fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It features fronds that are bipinnate, with a distinctive golden hue on the underside, giving it its common name. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining, acidic soil. Its compact size and elegant appearance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and container cultivation.

The fronds are evergreen, adding a lush, tropical feel to any setting.
