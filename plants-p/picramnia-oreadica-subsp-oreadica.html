Picramnia oreadica subsp. oreadica

Picramnia oreadica subsp.

oreadica, commonly known as the mountain picramnia, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fruits are dry, capsule-like structures that release small seeds when mature.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or woodland habitats.
