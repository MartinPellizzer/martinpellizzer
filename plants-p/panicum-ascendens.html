Panicum ascendens

Panicum ascendens, commonly known as the ascending panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and loose, fluffy panicles that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.

Panicum ascendens is often used in ornamental gardening due to its graceful, airy look.
