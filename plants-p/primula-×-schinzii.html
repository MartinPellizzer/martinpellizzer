Primula × schinzii

Primula × schinzii, commonly known as the hybrid primrose, is a flowering plant belonging to the Primulaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Primula vulgaris and Primula veris, showcasing a combination of traits from both parents. The plant typically features showy, bell-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Its foliage consists of broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, downy texture.
