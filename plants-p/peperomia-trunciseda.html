Peperomia trunciseda

Peperomia trunciseda, commonly known as the Truncated Peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, often adorned with reddish or purplish veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color, typical of the Piperaceae family. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
