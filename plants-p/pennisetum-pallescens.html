Pennisetum pallescens

Pennisetum pallescens, commonly known as silver beard grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and upright growth habit, making it a visually striking ornamental plant. The plant produces fluffy, panicle-like inflorescences that add a soft, feathery appearance to gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, preferring dry to moderately moist conditions.

Pennisetum pallescens is often used in xeriscaping and as a border plant due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
