Persicaria yokusaiana

Persicaria yokusaiana, commonly known as the Japanese knotweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes species such as buckwheat and sorrel. Persicaria yokusaiana is known for its rapid growth and has been introduced to various parts of the world, sometimes becoming invasive.
