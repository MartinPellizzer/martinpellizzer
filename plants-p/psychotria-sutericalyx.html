Psychotria sutericalyx

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is native to parts of Central and South America.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and traditional herbal practices.
