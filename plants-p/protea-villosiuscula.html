Protea villosiuscula

Protea villosiuscula, commonly known as the silky protea, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, fluffy, white bracts that surround the actual flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal and mountainous regions, thriving in well-drained, acidic soils. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening, though it requires specific care to flourish.

The silky protea plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing nectar for various pollinators.
