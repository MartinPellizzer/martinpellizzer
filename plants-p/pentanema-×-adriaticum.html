Pentanema × adriaticum

Pentanema × adriaticum, commonly known as the Adriatic pentanema, is a hybrid plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Primulaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant typically features small, white or pale pink flowers clustered in umbels, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine pubescence, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
