Panicum viride var. giganteum

Panicum viride var. giganteum, commonly known as giant green panicgrass, is a tall, perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it adaptable to various environments.

Its rapid growth and dense foliage make it a useful plant for erosion control and ornamental purposes.
