Pleurothallis simpliciglossa

Pleurothallis simpliciglossa, commonly known as the simple-lipped pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with elongated, narrow leaves and a compact inflorescence. The flowers are typically white or pale yellow with a distinctive, simple lip that sets it apart from other pleurothallis species. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its delicate appearance and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts who appreciate its unique botanical features.
