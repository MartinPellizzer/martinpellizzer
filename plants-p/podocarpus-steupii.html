Podocarpus steupii

Podocarpus steupii, commonly known as the Steup's podocarp, is a species of conifer native to Southeast Asia.

It is a medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has needle-like leaves that are dark green and glossy, arranged spirally along the stems. Its cones are small and woody, containing seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Podocarpus steupii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture and landscaping.
