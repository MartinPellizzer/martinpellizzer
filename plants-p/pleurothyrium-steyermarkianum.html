Pleurothyrium steyermarkianum

Pleurothyrium steyermarkianum, commonly known as the Brazilian pepper tree, is a member of the Myrtaceae family.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant has simple, opposite, elliptic leaves that are glossy and dark green. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and white, typically appearing in clusters.

The fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that turns red when mature.
