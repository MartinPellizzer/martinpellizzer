Pleurothallis myriantha

Pleurothallis myriantha, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with intricate, fragrant petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil for optimal growth.

Pleurothallis myriantha is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
