Piper nitidulilaminum

Piper nitidulilaminum, commonly known as the glossy leaf pepper, is a tropical climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and small, clustered flowers that develop into peppery fruits. Its botanical features make it a unique subject for horticultural study.

However, it is not widely cultivated for commercial use.
