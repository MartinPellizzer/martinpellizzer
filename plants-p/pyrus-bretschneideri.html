Pyrus bretschneideri

Pyrus bretschneideri, commonly known as the Chinese white pear, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its broad ovate leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The tree produces pomes, which are fleshy fruits with a sweet, aromatic flavor. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of edible fruit.

This species is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
