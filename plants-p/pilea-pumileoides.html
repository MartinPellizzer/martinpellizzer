Pilea pumileoides

Pilea pumileoides, commonly known as the miniature pilea or dwarf pilea, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features rosettes of rounded, fleshy leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a slight waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance for low light and minimal water requirements.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for tabletop displays and hanging baskets.
