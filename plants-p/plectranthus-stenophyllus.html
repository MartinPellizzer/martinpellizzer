Plectranthus stenophyllus

Plectranthus stenophyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, tubular flowers in clusters, typically in shades of purple or violet.

Plectranthus stenophyllus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant fragrance.
