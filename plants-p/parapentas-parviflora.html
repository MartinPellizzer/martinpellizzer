Parapentas parviflora

Parapentas parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered parapentas, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, small flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a subtle aromatic scent. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
