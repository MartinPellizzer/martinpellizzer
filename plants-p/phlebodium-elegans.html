Phlebodium elegans

Phlebodium elegans, commonly known as the elegant wall fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, slender fronds that are often divided into narrow, lance-shaped segments. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in the understory of rainforests. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help it anchor to surfaces while absorbing moisture from the air.

Phlebodium elegans is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in controlled indoor environments.
