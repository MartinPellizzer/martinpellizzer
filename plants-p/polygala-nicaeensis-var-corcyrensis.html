Polygala nicaeensis var. corcyrensis

Polygala nicaeensis var. corcyrensis, commonly known as the Corcyrene milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, wiry stems that often grow in dense clusters, and its leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in spicate inflorescences, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and limestone areas.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
