Poeppigia cyanocarpa

Poeppigia cyanocarpa, commonly known as the blue-fruited pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves and produces small, fragrant white flowers. The plant's fruits are distinctive for their bright blue color and are borne in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

Poeppigia cyanocarpa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native habitats.
