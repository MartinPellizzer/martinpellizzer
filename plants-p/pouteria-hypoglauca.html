Pouteria hypoglauca

Pouteria hypoglauca, commonly known as the yellow spondias, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and elliptical leaves with a glossy upper surface. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that give way to edible, sweet, and fleshy fruits resembling small mangoes.
