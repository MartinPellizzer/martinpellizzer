Platymiscium floribundum var. floribundum

Platymiscium floribundum var. floribundum, commonly known as the purple flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red flowers that appear in dense clusters during the dry season. The leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate with a smooth margin, providing a lush green canopy. The tree produces small, round fruits that contain seeds enclosed in a woody capsule.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species in tropical and subtropical landscapes.
