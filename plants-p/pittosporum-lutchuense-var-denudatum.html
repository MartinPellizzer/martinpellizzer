Pittosporum lutchuense var. denudatum

Pittosporum lutchuense var. denudatum, commonly known as the slender pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, branching structure and small, glossy leaves that are typically dark green. The plant produces fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, often followed by small, orange-red berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland forests or along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
