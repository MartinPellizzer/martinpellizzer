Peschiera solandri

Peschiera solandri, commonly known as the Solander's orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature a combination of yellow and green hues. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions.

Botanically, it is notable for its intricate floral structure and specialized pollination mechanisms.
