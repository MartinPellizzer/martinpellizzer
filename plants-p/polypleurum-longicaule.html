Polypleurum longicaule

Polypleurum longicaule, commonly known as the long-stemmed fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, pinnately divided leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical forests. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The fronds are also used as ornamental plants in horticulture due to their striking appearance.
