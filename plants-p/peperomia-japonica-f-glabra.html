Peperomia japonica f. glabra

Peperomia japonica f. glabra, commonly known as the smooth-leaved Japanese pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features thick, waxy, and smooth-textured leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for container gardening and indoor spaces.
