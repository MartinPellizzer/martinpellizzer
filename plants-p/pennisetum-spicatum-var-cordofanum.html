Pennisetum spicatum var. cordofanum

Pennisetum spicatum var. cordofanum, commonly known as giant blazing grass, is a tall, ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a striking silver-gray hue on the underside. The plant produces tall, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in late summer, adding a dramatic visual display to gardens. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for landscapes in warm climates.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a valuable addition to both residential and commercial settings.
