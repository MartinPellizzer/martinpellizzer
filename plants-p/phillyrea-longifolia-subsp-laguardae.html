Phillyrea longifolia subsp. laguardae

Phillyrea longifolia subsp.

laguardae, commonly known as the Laguarda Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula. It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring.

The leaves are typically oval-shaped with a leathery texture and a distinctive aromatic scent. This subspecies is adapted to rocky and coastal environments, thriving in well-drained soils.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.
