Poicillopsis acuminata

Poicillopsis acuminata, commonly known as the slender moss, is a species of moss belonging to the family Pottiaceae.

It is characterized by its small, elongated leaves that are often curved and have a distinct acuminate tip. This moss typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as on tree trunks or in rocky crevices. Its structure includes a well-developed stem with rhizoids that anchor it to the substrate.

Poicillopsis acuminata is known for its ability to withstand desiccation, making it a common sight in various temperate regions.
