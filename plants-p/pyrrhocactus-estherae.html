Pyrrhocactus estherae

Pyrrhocactus estherae, commonly known as Esther's firecactus, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rounded shape and vibrant red or orange flowers. The plant features cylindrical stems covered in spines, which help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. Its flowers bloom in late spring and are typically solitary, adding a striking visual contrast to its green foliage.

Pyrrhocactus estherae is well adapted to full sun and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
