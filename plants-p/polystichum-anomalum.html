Polystichum anomalum

Polystichum anomalum, commonly known as the zigzag fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzag arrangement of fronds, which gives it its common name. The plant features dark green, leathery leaves that are deeply divided into narrow, lance-shaped segments. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest undergrowth.

Polystichum anomalum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
