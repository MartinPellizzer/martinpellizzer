Plectronia umbrosa

Plectronia umbrosa, commonly known as the shadow fern, is a unique fern species native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that create a lush, green canopy. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread slowly and form dense clusters.

The spores are produced on the underside of the fronds, contributing to its reproductive strategy in the wild.
