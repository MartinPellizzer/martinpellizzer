Peperomia chillonensis

Peperomia chillonensis, commonly known as the Chilean peperomia, is a species native to South America, particularly Chile.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color.

Peperomia chillonensis is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.
