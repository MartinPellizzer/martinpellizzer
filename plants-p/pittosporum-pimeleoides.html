Pittosporum pimeleoides

Pittosporum pimeleoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, which are usually white or pale yellow. These flowers are followed by small, round berries that turn red when mature.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.
