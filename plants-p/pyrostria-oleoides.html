Pyrostria oleoides

Pyrostria oleoides, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a waxy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscapes.
