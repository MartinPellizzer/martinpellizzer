Piper sprengelianum

Piper sprengelianum, commonly known as the Sprengel's pepper vine, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of flowering plants. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a rough texture with prominent veins.

The fruit is a small, three-lobed berry that turns from green to yellow or red as it matures.
