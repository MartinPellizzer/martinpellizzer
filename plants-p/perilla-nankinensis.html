Perilla nankinensis

Perilla nankinensis, commonly known as Chinese basil or Japanese perilla, is a species of flowering plant in the Lamiaceae family.

It is native to East Asia and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and purple flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its leaves, which are used in traditional Chinese and Japanese cuisines. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Perilla nankinensis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.
