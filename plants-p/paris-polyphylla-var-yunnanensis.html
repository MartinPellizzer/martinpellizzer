Paris polyphylla var. yunnanensis

Paris polyphylla var. yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan Paris, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Melanthiaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers, which bloom in early summer. Its leaves are typically compound, with long, slender segments.

Yunnan Paris is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional medicinal uses in Chinese herbal medicine.
