Pilea plataniflora

Pilea plataniflora, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that resemble small, round coins. The plant produces clusters of white, spadix-like flowers surrounded by a greenish-white spathe. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Pilea plataniflora is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
