Pithecellobium campestre

Pithecellobium campestre, commonly known as the wild mango or yellow acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of the Americas.

Its seed pods are typically woody and contain edible seeds, though the plant is more valued for its ornamental and ecological roles.
