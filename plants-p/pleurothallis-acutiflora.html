Pleurothallis acutiflora

Pleurothallis acutiflora, commonly known as the sharp-flowered pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and its ability to thrive in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often clustered in a raceme. Its leaves are usually dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in moisture retention.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
