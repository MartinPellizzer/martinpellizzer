Polyscias commersonii

Polyscias commersonii, commonly known as the evergreen hibiscus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil.

Polyscias commersonii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage and vibrant floral display.
