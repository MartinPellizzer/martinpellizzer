Photinia notoniana var. angustata

Photinia notoniana var. angustata, commonly known as the narrowleaf photinia, is a shrub or small tree native to Australia.

It features small, narrow leaves that are typically glossy and dark green, with a slightly serrated margin. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in spring, which are followed by red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

The variety is noted for its adaptability to different environmental conditions.
