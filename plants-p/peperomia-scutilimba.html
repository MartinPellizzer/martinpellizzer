Peperomia scutilimba

Peperomia scutilimba, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are often covered in a silvery or grayish pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia scutilimba is relatively low-maintenance and resistant to pests, making it an ideal plant for both novice and experienced growers.
