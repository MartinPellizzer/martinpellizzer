Pleurothallis nectarifera

Pleurothallis nectarifera, commonly known as the nectar-sac orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure, with showy, fragrant flowers that attract pollinators. The plant typically produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with distinctive nectar-filled sacs at the base of the petals. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological significance, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
