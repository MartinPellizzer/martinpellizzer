Peperomia nervosovenosa

Peperomia nervosovenosa, commonly known as the veiny peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves with prominent veins that give it its distinctive appearance. The plant typically produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in spikes. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Peperomia nervosovenosa is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and ornamental foliage.
