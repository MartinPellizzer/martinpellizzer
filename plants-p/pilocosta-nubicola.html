Pilocosta nubicola

Pilocosta nubicola, commonly known as the African orchid, is a flowering plant native to sub-Saharan Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, showy flowers that often display a range of colors. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomatous structure allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

Pilocosta nubicola is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
