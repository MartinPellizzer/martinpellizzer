Pedicularis aquilina

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, spurred flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. Pedicularis aquilina is noted for its adaptability to harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient species in its native range.
