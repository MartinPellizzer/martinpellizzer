Pyrrhosa glabra

Pyrrhosa glabra, commonly known as the smooth pyrrhosa, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. Pyrrhosa glabra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
