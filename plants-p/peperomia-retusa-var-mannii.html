Peperomia retusa var. mannii

Peperomia retusa var. mannii, commonly known as the Silver Sword Peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a striking silver or white margin that gives it its common name. The plant has a short, sturdy stem with dense, clustered foliage that creates a bushy appearance. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Peperomia retusa var. mannii thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.
