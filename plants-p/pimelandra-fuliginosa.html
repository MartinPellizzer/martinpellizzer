Pimelandra fuliginosa

Pimelandra fuliginosa, commonly known as the black velvet plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features dark, velvety foliage with a glossy texture, and its flowers are typically small, fragrant, and borne in clusters. The plant thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.

Pimelandra fuliginosa is also valued for its ability to purify indoor air.
