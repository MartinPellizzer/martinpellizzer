Planchonella obovata var. dubia

Planchonella obovata var. dubia, commonly known as the Dubia planchonella, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by yellowish-orange fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
