Peperomia ovatilimba

Peperomia ovatilimba, commonly known as the broad-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It features glossy, ovate-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color, arranged in clusters at the base of the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia ovatilimba is valued for its ornamental foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
