Pentas pubiflora var. longistyla

Pentas pubiflora var. longistyla, commonly known as the long-styled pentas, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright red, tubular flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which give it its species name "pubiflora.". Its long, slender stamens and pistil are distinctive features, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ability to attract pollinators like birds and insects.
