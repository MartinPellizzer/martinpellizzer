Piper flexuosum

Piper flexuosum, commonly known as the climbing pepper or wild pepper, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its long, trailing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. It produces tiny green flowers that develop into red, peppery fruits.

Piper flexuosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of culinary spices.
