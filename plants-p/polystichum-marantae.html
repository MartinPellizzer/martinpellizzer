Polystichum marantae

Polystichum marantae, commonly known as the Marantae fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its feathery, lance-shaped fronds that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its elegant appearance and ability to add a lush, tropical feel to indoor or outdoor spaces.

Polystichum marantae is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced gardeners.
