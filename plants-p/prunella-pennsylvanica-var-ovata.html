Prunella pennsylvanica var. ovata

Prunella pennsylvanica var. ovata, commonly known as smooth blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a member of the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is characterized by its square, hairy stems and opposite, ovate-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges, and is often found in sandy or loamy soils.

Smooth blueweed is typically found in the southeastern and mid-Atlantic regions of the United States.
