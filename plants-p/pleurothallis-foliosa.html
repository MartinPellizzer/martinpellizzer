Pleurothallis foliosa

Pleurothallis foliosa, commonly known as the leafy pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant prefers humid, shaded environments and thrives in well-draining, acidic soil. Its leaves are usually broad and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Due to its unique appearance and specific habitat requirements, it is a sought-after plant among orchid enthusiasts.
