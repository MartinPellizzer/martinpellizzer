Pogostemon chinensis

Pogostemon chinensis, commonly known as Chinese betony, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and spikes of small, two-lipped flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Pogostemon chinensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
