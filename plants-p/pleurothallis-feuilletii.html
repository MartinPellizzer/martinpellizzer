Pleurothallis feuilletii

Pleurothallis feuilletii, commonly known as the Feuillet's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other plants without deriving nutrients from them. Its foliage consists of narrow, strap-like leaves that help it conserve moisture in its natural habitat.

This orchid is prized by horticulturists for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
