Phoebe montana

Phoebe montana, commonly known as the mountain phoebe, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America and Mexico.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes other aromatic plants such as laurel and avocado. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are typically elliptical and have a glossy surface. Its small, fragrant flowers are usually white or pale yellow and appear in clusters.

The fruit is a small, dark blue drupe that contains a single seed.
