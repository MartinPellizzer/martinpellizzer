Peliosanthes cupuliformis

Peliosanthes cupuliformis, commonly known as the hooded cycad, is a unique plant belonging to the Cycadaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, umbrella-like crown of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant has a stout, unbranched trunk that is often covered in a fibrous, spongy material. Its leaves are pinnate, with a dark green color and a waxy texture.

Peliosanthes cupuliformis is native to southern China and is adapted to grow in tropical and subtropical climates.
