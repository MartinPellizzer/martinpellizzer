Pycreus debilissimus

Pycreus debilissimus, commonly known as the weak pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellowish to white flowers that are typically grouped in clusters. It is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
