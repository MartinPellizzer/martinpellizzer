Ptilotrichum canescens var. canescens

Ptilotrichum canescens var. canescens, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and yellow composite flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in open, sandy or rocky soils and is often found in grassland habitats. It has a taproot system and produces achenes with pappus for wind dispersal.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
