Palenga

Palenga, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, colorful flowers. The plant thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth. Its flowers bloom in a variety of colors, including pink, purple, white, and blue, making it a favorite among gardeners.
