Polygala densifolia var. lutescens

Polygala densifolia var. lutescens, commonly known as the yellow mistflower, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, feathery inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its yellowish flowers are produced in dense clusters, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
