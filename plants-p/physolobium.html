Physolobium

Physolobium, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling flames. The plant has compound leaves with long, narrow leaflets and produces small, woody pods that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The botanical features of Physolobium make it a popular choice for gardens and public spaces.
