Pythonium bulbiferum

Pythonium bulbiferum, commonly known as the bulb-bearing python plant, is a unique succulent native to arid regions.

It features thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in dry environments. The plant produces small, bulb-like structures along its stems, which are actually modified leaves used for water storage. Its leaves are typically gray-green with a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Pythonium bulbiferum is valued in horticulture for its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
