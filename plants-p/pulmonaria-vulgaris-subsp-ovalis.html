Pulmonaria vulgaris subsp. ovalis

Pulmonaria vulgaris subsp.

ovalis, commonly known as the oval lungwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It features rosettes of basal, oval-shaped leaves that are covered in a distinctive blue or purple powdery coating.

The plant produces spikes of bell-shaped, pink or purple flowers in early spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Its foliage and flowers contribute to its ornamental value in gardens and natural habitats.
