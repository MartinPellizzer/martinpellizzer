Pauridia vaginata var. brevistigmata

Pauridia vaginata var. brevistigmata, commonly known as the narrow-stigma pauridia, is a rare flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces delicate, white to pale yellow flowers with prominent stigmas, which are shorter and more compact than in other varieties. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
