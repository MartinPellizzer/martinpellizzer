Peperomia subvillosa f. dumauseana

Peperomia subvillosa f. dumauseana, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, low-growing perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, ovate to elliptical leaves that are typically covered in a fine, velvety pubescence, giving it a soft, textured appearance. The leaves are usually dark green with a slightly waxy coating, and the plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
