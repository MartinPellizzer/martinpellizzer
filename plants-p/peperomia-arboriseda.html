Peperomia arboriseda

Peperomia arboriseda, commonly known as the Shiny Leaf Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its ornamental value comes primarily from its foliage rather than its flowers, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia arboriseda is relatively low maintenance, making it ideal for beginners and those with limited space.
