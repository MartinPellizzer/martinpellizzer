Polygala maravillasensis

Polygala maravillasensis, commonly known as the Maravillasense milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Mexico.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing adaptations to arid environments. Its small, compound leaves and unique floral structure contribute to its ecological significance.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species within its native range.
