Phaeopappus ruprechtii

Phaeopappus ruprechtii, commonly known as the Ruprecht's phaeopappus, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

The plant is distinguished by its hairy, glandular leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months.
