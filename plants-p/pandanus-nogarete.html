Pandanus nogarete

Pandanus nogarete, commonly known as the Nogarete pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves that form a dense, feather-like crown. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that grow in clusters at the tips of the leaves. Its roots are fibrous and often used in traditional crafts and construction.

The Nogarete pandanus is well adapted to coastal and sandy environments, making it a hardy species in tropical regions.
