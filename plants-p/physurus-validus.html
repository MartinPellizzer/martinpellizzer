Physurus validus

Physurus validus, commonly known as the validus spurge, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant has smooth, green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid environments.

Physurus validus is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
