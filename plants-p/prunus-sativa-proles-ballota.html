Prunus sativa proles ballota

Prunus sativa proles ballota, commonly known as the Ballota cherry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces small, sweet, edible fruits that are often used in jams and desserts. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape. Its flowers are typically pink or white, appearing in clusters before the fruit develops.

The Ballota cherry is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical use in traditional medicine.
