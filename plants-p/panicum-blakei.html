Panicum blakei

Panicum blakei, commonly known as Blake's panicgrass, is a flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the southeastern United States, particularly Florida and the Carolinas. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets.

Panicum blakei is often found in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and disturbed soils.
