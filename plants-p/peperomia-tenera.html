Peperomia tenera

Peperomia tenera, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a distinctive silvery underside, giving it a striking visual appeal. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It is a popular houseplant due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it ideal for container gardening and indoor spaces.
