Phlomis lurestanica

Phlomis lurestanica, commonly known as Persian mullein, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Iran.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features tall, erect stems covered in soft, woolly hairs, and it produces showy clusters of yellow to orange flowers. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Phlomis lurestanica is adapted to dry, rocky environments and is often found in high-altitude regions.
