Phyllanthus sinensis var. dalbergioides

Phyllanthus sinensis var. dalbergioides, commonly known as the Chinese date tree or Indian date palm, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree with dense, leathery leaves that are typically elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by edible, fleshy fruits resembling dates. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit production.

The species is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
