Paravitex

Paravitex, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release numerous tiny seeds.
