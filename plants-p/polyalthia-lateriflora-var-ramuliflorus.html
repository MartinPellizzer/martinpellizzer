Polyalthia lateriflora var. ramuliflorus

Polyalthia lateriflora var. ramuliflorus, commonly known as the ramuli-flowered polyalthia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom along the branches. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
