Poterium verrucosum var. sericeum

Poterium verrucosum var. sericeum, commonly known as the silky sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Compositae and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features silver-gray foliage with densely packed, soft hairs that give it a silky appearance. Its small, daisy-like flowers are typically white or pale yellow, appearing in clusters.
