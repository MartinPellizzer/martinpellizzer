Prunus pseudocerasus var. hortensis

Prunus pseudocerasus var. hortensis, commonly known as the Chinese date plum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It produces small, sweet, yellow or orange fruits that are edible and often used in traditional Chinese medicine. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The flowers are typically white or pink and appear before the leaves in early spring.
