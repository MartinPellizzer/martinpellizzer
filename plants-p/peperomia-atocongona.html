Peperomia atocongona

Peperomia atocongona, commonly known as the Atocongo peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Colombia.

It features thick, fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating, known as trichomes. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia atocongona is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.
