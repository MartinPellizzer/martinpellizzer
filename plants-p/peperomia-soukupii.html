Peperomia soukupii

Peperomia soukupii, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to Brazil.

It features thick, waxy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and red, which appear in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
