Phyllanthus hylodendron

Phyllanthus hylodendron, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, smooth, green fruits that turn red when mature. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its growth habit includes a shrub or small tree form, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.
