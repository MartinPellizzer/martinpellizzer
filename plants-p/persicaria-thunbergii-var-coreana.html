Persicaria thunbergii var. coreana

Persicaria thunbergii var. coreana, commonly known as Korean smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features upright, branching stems that are typically covered in fine hairs. The leaves are lance-shaped, alternate, and often have a reddish or purplish hue, especially under stress or in full sun. It produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or red, blooming in late summer.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
