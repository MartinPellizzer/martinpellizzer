Pentaphylloides parvifolia

Pentaphylloides parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved pentaphylloids, is a rare and delicate plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant produces tiny, pale green flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by small, berry-like fruits.
