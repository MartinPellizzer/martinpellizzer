Pilosella woronowiana

Pilosella woronowiana, commonly known as the alpine mouse-ear, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. Pilosella woronowiana is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
