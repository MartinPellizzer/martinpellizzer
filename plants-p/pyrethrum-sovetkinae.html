Pyrethrum sovetkinae

Pyrethrum sovetkinae, commonly known as the Siberian pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces pyrethrin, a natural insecticide, which is used in organic pest control. It thrives in cool, temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in various horticultural settings.
