Peperomia perinduta

Peperomia perinduta, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dark green, velvety leaves with a slightly waxy texture, often adorned with subtle veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its attractive foliage and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
