Polygonum punctatum var. confertiflorum

Polygonum punctatum var. confertiflorum, commonly known as the dense-flowered smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and distinctive nodes along the stem. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink to red in color, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, sunny environments such as wet meadows and stream banks.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense stands, making it a common sight in wetland habitats.
