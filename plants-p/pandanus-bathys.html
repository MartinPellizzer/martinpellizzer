Pandanus bathys

Pandanus bathys, commonly known as the deep-sea pandanus, is a rare and enigmatic plant species found in extreme marine environments.

It thrives in deep waters, where it has adapted to high pressure and low light conditions. Its structure consists of long, flexible leaves that help it withstand strong ocean currents. The plant's reproductive system is unique, relying on specialized spores for propagation in its challenging habitat.

Despite its ecological significance, little is known about its full botanical characteristics due to the difficulty of studying it in its natural environment.
