Pilosella Glomerata Subsp. Kunzii

Pilosella glomerata subsp. kunzii is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of Europe. It is known for its small, clustered white flowers and its ability to thrive in rocky or sandy soils. This subspecies is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats, where it faces harsh environmental conditions. Due to its limited range and vulnerability to habitat loss, it is considered a species of conservation concern. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it an important subject for botanical study.
