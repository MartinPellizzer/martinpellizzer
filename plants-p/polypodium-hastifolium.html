Polypodium hastifolium

Polypodium hastifolium, commonly known as the broad-leafed fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air. Its fronds are usually broad and lanceolate, with a dark green color and a leathery texture.

Polypodium hastifolium thrives in humid environments and is often found in shaded, moist forests.
