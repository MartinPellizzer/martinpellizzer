Pilosella dobromilensis

Pilosella dobromilensis, commonly known as the Dobromil mountain everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which give it its genus name "Pilosella.". It thrives in rocky and alpine environments, often growing at elevations above 1,500 meters.
