Parodia lenninghausii

Parodia lenninghausii, commonly known as the Leninghaus's pincushion cactus, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its dense, spiny, spherical shape. The plant features short, cylindrical stems covered in white to grayish spines, and it produces yellow flowers during the summer months.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
