Phyllanthus subapicalis subsp. sequoiifolius

Phyllanthus subapicalis subsp.

sequoiifolius, commonly known as the sequoia-leaved phyllanthus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Euphorbiaceae. It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania.

The plant is characterized by its alternate, simple leaves that are often elliptical or lanceolate in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.
