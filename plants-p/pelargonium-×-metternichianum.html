Pelargonium × metternichianum

Pelargonium × metternichianum, commonly known as the scented geranium, is a hybrid shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves, which emit a fragrant scent when touched. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and features broad, velvety foliage. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in shades of pink, red, or white.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for container gardening and ornamental use.
