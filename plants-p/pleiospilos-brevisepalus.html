Pleiospilos brevisepalus

Pleiospilos brevisepalus, commonly known as the short-stemmed African snowball, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded shape and thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
