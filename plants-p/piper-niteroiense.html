Piper niteroiense

Piper niteroiense, commonly known as the Brazilian pepper vine, is a tropical climbing shrub native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and emits a mild, peppery scent. Its small greenish-white flowers are followed by small, round berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.

The plant is often found in humid, shaded environments in the understory of tropical forests.
