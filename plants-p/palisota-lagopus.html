Palisota lagopus

Palisota lagopus, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features heart-shaped, silvery-green leaves that are covered in fine, reflective hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a capsule containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Palisota lagopus is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky slopes and wastelands.
