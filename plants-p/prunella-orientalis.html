Prunella orientalis

Prunella orientalis, commonly known as self-heal, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a low-growing herb with square stems and opposite leaves that are usually hairy. The plant produces small, spike-like clusters of blue or violet flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, woodlands, and along stream banks.

Prunella orientalis is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
