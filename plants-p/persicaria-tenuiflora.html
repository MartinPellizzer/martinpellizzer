Persicaria tenuiflora

Persicaria tenuiflora, commonly known as slenderflower knotweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, white to pinkish flowers are arranged in dense spikes and bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically less than 2 inches long.

It is often found in moist habitats such as streambanks and wet meadows.
