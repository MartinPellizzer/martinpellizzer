Protea Afra Subsp. Kilimandscharica

The protea afra subsp. kilimandscharica is a striking member of the proteaceae family native to southern africa. it is known for its vibrant, cone-shaped flowers that range from white to pink and red. this plant thrives in rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions. it plays an important role in local ecosystems and is valued for its ornamental beauty. its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardeners and conservationists alike.
