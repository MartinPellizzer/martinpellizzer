Pancratium tubulosum

Pancratium tubulosum, commonly known as the slender torch lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and striking yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces a distinctive, narrow, tubular shape that gives it its scientific name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often growing in coastal or sandy environments.

The flowers are known for their fragrance and are sometimes used in traditional medicine.
