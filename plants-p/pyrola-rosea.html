Pyrola rosea

Pyrola rosea, commonly known as the pink wintergreen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the northeastern United States and eastern Canada.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its low-growing habit and evergreen foliage. Pyrola rosea is also known for its aromatic leaves and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
