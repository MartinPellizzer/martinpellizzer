Peperomia irrasa

Peperomia irrasa, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that are often overlooked. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia irrasa is popular as a decorative indoor plant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
