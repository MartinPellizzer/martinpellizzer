Phyllanthus longiflorus

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, clustered yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain small, dark seeds.
