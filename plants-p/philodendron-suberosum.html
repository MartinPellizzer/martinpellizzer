Philodendron suberosum

Philodendron suberosum, commonly known as the velvetleaf philodendron, is a climbing plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers, which are followed by red berries containing seeds. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical plant collections.
