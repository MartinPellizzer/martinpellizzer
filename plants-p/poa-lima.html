Poa lima

Poa lima, commonly known as the Chilean grass or silvergrass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-blue foliage and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate climates and is often found in coastal areas and mountainous regions. Poa lima has narrow, elongated leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hair, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in landscaping and erosion control.
