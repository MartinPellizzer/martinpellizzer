Poa sandbergii

Poa sandbergii, commonly known as Sandberg's bluegrass, is a perennial grass native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and tufted growth habit. This plant thrives in dry, open habitats such as sagebrush steppe and rocky soils. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

Poa sandbergii is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support local wildlife.
