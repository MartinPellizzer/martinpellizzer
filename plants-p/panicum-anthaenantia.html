Panicum anthaenantia

Panicum anthaenantia, commonly known as the red panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, often reddish-colored inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy texture.

Panicum anthaenantia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
