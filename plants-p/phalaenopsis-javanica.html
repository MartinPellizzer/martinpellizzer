Phalaenopsis javanica

Phalaenopsis javanica, commonly known as the Javanese moth orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendant-like inflorescences that bear clusters of fragrant, moth-like flowers. The plant typically has broad, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Phalaenopsis javanica is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its elegant appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
