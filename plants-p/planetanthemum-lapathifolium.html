Planetanthemum lapathifolium

Planetanthemum lapathifolium, commonly known as the common rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, pink or white flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ability to tolerate drought conditions makes it a hardy and low-maintenance plant.
