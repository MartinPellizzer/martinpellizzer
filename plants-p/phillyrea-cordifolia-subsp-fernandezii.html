Phillyrea cordifolia subsp. fernandezii

Phillyrea cordifolia subsp.

fernandezii, commonly known as the Canary Island Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands. It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage with dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape.

The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, spherical berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to coastal and rocky environments.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in Mediterranean climates.
