Polygala baikiei

Polygala baikiei, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, often climbing stems. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in racemes. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and usually have a hairy texture.

Polygala baikiei is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
