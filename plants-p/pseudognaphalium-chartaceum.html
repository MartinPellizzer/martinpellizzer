Pseudognaphalium chartaceum

Pseudognaphalium chartaceum, commonly known as the paperleaf tansy, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, herbaceous stems and small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The leaves are often described as papery and grayish-green, with a fuzzy texture.
