Phyteuma hedraianthifolium f. graminifolium

Phyteuma hedraianthifolium f. graminifolium, commonly known as the grass-leaved phyteuma, is a variant of the phyteuma species characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is native to specific regions in Europe. The plant typically produces tall, slender stems with clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of white to pale blue. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
