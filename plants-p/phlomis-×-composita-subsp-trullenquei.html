Phlomis × composita subsp. trullenquei

Phlomis × composita subsp.

trullenquei, commonly known as the Trullenquean hybrid phlomis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan Peninsula. It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal and ornamental value.

The plant features tall, erect stems covered in soft, hairy foliage, and produces showy, pale blue to purple flowers in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping in Mediterranean climates.
