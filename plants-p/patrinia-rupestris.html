Patrinia rupestris

Patrinia rupestris, commonly known as stonecrop patrinia, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in rocky or well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Patrinia rupestris is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
