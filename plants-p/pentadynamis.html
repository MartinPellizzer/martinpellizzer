Pentadynamis

Pentadynamis, commonly known as the five-lobed orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

The plants typically grow in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Pentadynamis species are often epiphytic, meaning they grow on other plants without being parasitic.
