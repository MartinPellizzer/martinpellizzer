Premna dallachyana var. typica

Premna dallachyana var. typica, commonly known as the glossy privet.

It is a shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white, tubular flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture.
