Piper Elongatum Var. Salviifolium

Piper elongatum var. salviifolium is a unique variant of the pepper plant known for its striking foliage. This plant features elongated leaves that resemble the leaves of the sacred basil, giving it its distinctive name. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in warm and humid environments. Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it remains a lesser-known species in the world of horticulture.
