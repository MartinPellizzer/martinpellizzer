Premna abbreviata

Premna abbreviata, commonly known as the small-leaved reasonleaf, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in panicles, and it produces small, round fruits that are slightly hairy.

The plant is often found in moist, shaded areas and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
