Pentas lanceolata var. carnea

Pentas lanceolata var. carnea, commonly known as the pink pentas, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a contrasting yellow or white center. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are opposite and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Its compact growth habit and long blooming period make it a versatile ornamental plant.
