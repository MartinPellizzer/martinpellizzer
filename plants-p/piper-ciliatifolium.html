Piper ciliatifolium

Piper ciliatifolium, commonly known as the velvetleaf pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with fine cilia along the edges, giving it a velvety texture. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into three-lobed berries.

The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
