Papiria spiralis

Papiria spiralis, commonly known as spiral paper plant, is a unique species native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its spiraling, ribbon-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes many tropical plants with similar growth habits. Its leaves are often used in traditional crafts due to their fibrous texture and durability.
