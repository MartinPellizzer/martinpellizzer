Pittosporum fortunei

Pittosporum fortunei, commonly known as the Fortune's pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage, which is dark green and glossy, and its fragrant white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible but not particularly palatable. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Pittosporum fortunei is also valued for its ability to tolerate coastal conditions and its use in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal.
