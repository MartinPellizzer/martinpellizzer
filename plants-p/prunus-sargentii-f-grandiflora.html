Prunus sargentii f. grandiflora

Prunus sargentii f. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered sargent cherry, is a cultivar of the Prunus sargentii species, which is native to the eastern United States.

This ornamental shrub or small tree is characterized by its showy, pink to white flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The foliage is typically dark green and provides a striking contrast to the blossoms. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural displays.
