Pilea uninervis

Pilea uninervis, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Araceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including China, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by spathes.

Pilea uninervis is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
