Phaseolus lathyroides subf. nanus

Phaseolus lathyroides subf.

nanus, commonly known as the dwarf scarlet runner bean, is a lesser-known variant of the scarlet runner bean. It is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Americas, particularly Central and South America.

The plant features slender, climbing stems with hairy leaves and produces vibrant red or orange flowers. Its small, bean-like pods are edible and often used in traditional cuisines.

Botanically, it is characterized by its compact growth habit and unique floral and fruit morphology.
