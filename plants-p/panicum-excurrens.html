Panicum excurrens

Panicum excurrens, commonly known as the wandering grass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, often sprawling growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in disturbed soils or open habitats. It is adapted to thrive in warm climates with well-drained soil.

Panicum excurrens is sometimes used in ornamental gardening due to its distinctive appearance and ability to spread easily.
