Physoceras lageniferum

Physoceras lageniferum, commonly known as the bladder bladder or bladder wort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bladder-like flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. The plant grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in wet meadows or along stream banks. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The species is notable for its unique floral structure and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
