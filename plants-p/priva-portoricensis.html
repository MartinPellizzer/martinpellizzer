Priva portoricensis

It is a member of the Oleaceae family, which includes other well-known species such as lilac and jasmine. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Priva portoricensis plays a role in local ecosystems but is also considered an invasive species in some regions outside its native habitat.
