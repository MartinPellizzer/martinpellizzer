Platanthera leptopetala

Platanthera leptopetala, commonly known as the slender lady's tresses, is a species of orchid native to the northern hemisphere.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and dense, dark green leaves. The plant produces a spike of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wet meadows.

Platanthera leptopetala is known for its delicate appearance and is often found growing in association with specific fungal species.
