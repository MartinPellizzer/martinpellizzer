Pelargonium emelianum

Pelargonium emelianum, commonly known as the Emelianum geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically features serrated, lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It blooms in shades of red, pink, and white, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Pelargonium emelianum prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, thriving in temperate climates.
