Panicum puberulum

Panicum puberulum, commonly known as silver panicgrass, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. It produces fluffy, pale green to brown seed heads that are often used in ornamental gardening.

Panicum puberulum is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
