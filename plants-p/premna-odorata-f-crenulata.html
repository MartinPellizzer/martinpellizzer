Premna odorata f. crenulata

Premna odorata f. crenulata, commonly known as the fragrant premna, is a variant of the Premna odorata species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves that are often slightly wavy or notched at the edges. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are typically covered with a fine, soft pubescence, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
