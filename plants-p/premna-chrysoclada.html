Premna chrysoclada

Premna chrysoclada, commonly known as the golden-leafed premna, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, whitish pubescence. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and are usually white or pale yellow in color.

The plant is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive foliage.
