Pycreus macrostachyos

Pycreus macrostachyos, commonly known as the large-stalked pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy yellow flowers with a central disc, surrounded by ray florets, and has a bushy growth habit.
