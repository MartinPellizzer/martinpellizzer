Pholidota minahassae

Pholidota minahassae, commonly known as the Minahassa pholcid, is a species of pholcid spider found in Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Pholcidae, which includes ground spiders known for their robust bodies and long legs. The spider's exoskeleton is typically covered in fine hairs, providing it with a textured appearance. It is primarily nocturnal and inhabits moist environments such as forests and caves.

Its venom is relatively mild and used mainly for subduing small prey.
