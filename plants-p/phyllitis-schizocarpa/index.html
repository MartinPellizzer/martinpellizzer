Phyllitis Schizocarpa

Prairie lousewort, scientifically known as Phyllitis schizocarpa, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the prairies of North America. It thrives in dry, open habitats where sunlight is abundant and competition from other plants is minimal. Despite its small size, it plays a vital role in maintaining the ecological balance of its environment. The plant's unique appearance, with its pinkish-purple flowers and hairy stems, makes it both visually striking and ecologically significant. Conservation efforts are essential to protect this rare species from habitat loss and environmental changes.
