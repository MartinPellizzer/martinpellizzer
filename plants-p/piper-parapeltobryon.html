Piper parapeltobryon

Piper parapeltobryon, commonly known as the Bornean pepper vine, is a climbing plant native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by berries that are typically red when mature. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly serrated margin.

The plant is characterized by its vigorous growth and is often found in humid, shaded environments.
