Pyrethrum flaveolum var. alpestre

Pyrethrum flaveolum var. alpestre, commonly known as the alpine pyrethrum, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and prefers full sun exposure. Its foliage consists of finely divided, gray-green leaves that are aromatic and contain pyrethrin, a natural insecticide.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in pest control.
