Perissocoeleum phylloideum

Perissocoeleum phylloideum, commonly known as the leaf-like perissocoele, is a rare and unusual plant species characterized by its unique morphological features.

The plant possesses specialized perissocoeles, which are modified structures that serve both photosynthetic and water-retention functions.
