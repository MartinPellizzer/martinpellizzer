Poa langtangensis

Poa langtangensis, commonly known as the Langtang grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of Nepal and Tibet, where it thrives in cold and arid environments. The plant is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems that often form dense mats on rocky slopes. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions.

Poa langtangensis plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems in its alpine habitat.
