Plectranthus helferi

Plectranthus helferi, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm climates and is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance.

Plectranthus helferi is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
