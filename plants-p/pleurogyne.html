Pleurogyne

Pleurogyne, commonly known as the "butterfly plant," is a genus of flowering plants in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to Australia and is characterized by its showy, butterfly-like flowers that are typically pink or white. The leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a glossy surface. Pleurogyne species are often found in coastal or sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments.
