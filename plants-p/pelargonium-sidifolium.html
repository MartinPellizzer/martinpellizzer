Pelargonium sidifolium

Pelargonium sidifolium, commonly known as the diamond plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with thick, fleshy leaves that are often adorned with a waxy coating. The leaves are typically triangular or diamond-shaped, giving the plant its common name. It produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or containers due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
