Phytelephas orbignyana

Phytelephas orbignyana, commonly known as the areca palm, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of yellowish-white flowers, which are followed by hard, woody fruits. The seeds inside the fruits are encased in a fibrous, spongy layer.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
