Phragmipedium longifolium var. splendidum

Phragmipedium longifolium var. splendidum, commonly known as the splendid lady's slipper, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch, typically in shades of pink and white. The plant has long, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its rarity and horticultural appeal, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts.
