Persica domestica var. fragrans

Persica domestica var. fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant peach.

This variety is characterized by its sweet, aromatic fruits that develop from showy, pink to white flowers. The plant is a deciduous tree with alternate, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The flowers and fruits are both attractive and valuable for ornamental and culinary purposes.
