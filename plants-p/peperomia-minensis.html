Peperomia minensis

Peperomia minensis, commonly known as the Minensis Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces delicate, spathaceous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which bloom during the warmer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Peperomia minensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and air-purifying qualities.
