Potentilla plattensis var. millefolia

Potentilla plattensis var. millefolia, commonly known as the silverleaf potentilla, is a perennial shrub native to the western United States.

It features small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has finely divided, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
