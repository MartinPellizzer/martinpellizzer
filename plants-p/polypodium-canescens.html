Polypodium canescens

Polypodium canescens, commonly known as the hairy shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its fronds that are covered with fine, hairy structures, giving it a distinctive appearance. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. The fronds are typically bipinnate, with a broad, shield-like shape.

It is often found growing on rocks or tree trunks in humid climates.
