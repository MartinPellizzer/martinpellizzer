Podolepis hieracioides

Podolepis hieracioides, commonly known as the common rock rose.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa. The plant features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, adapted to withstand arid conditions.

Podolepis hieracioides thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or Mediterranean climates.
