Phyllodium pulchrum

Phyllodium pulchrum, commonly known as the beautiful phyllodium, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, narrow, and often hairy leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Phyllodium pulchrum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
