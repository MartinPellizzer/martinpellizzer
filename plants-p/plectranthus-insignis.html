Plectranthus insignis

Plectranthus insignis, commonly known as the African violet or velvet leaf, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb with hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of violet, pink, or white, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a versatile addition to both gardens and containers.
