Polyotidium huebneri

Polyotidium huebneri, commonly known as the Hübner's polyotidium, is a small, annual herbaceous plant native to certain regions of Europe.

It typically grows to a height of about 10–30 centimeters and features simple, alternate leaves that are usually lance-shaped or ovate. The flowers are small and clustered in spikes, with five petals that are often pink or white in color. It blooms during the summer months and is known for its delicate appearance and preference for moist, well-drained soils.

This plant is often found in meadows and along the edges of woodland areas.
