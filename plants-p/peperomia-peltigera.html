Peperomia peltigera

Peperomia peltigera, commonly known as the velvetleaf peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are often slightly wavy and have a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically borne in clusters on short pedicels. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia peltigera is relatively low maintenance and resistant to pests, contributing to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
