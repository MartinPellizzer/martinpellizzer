Phyteuma globulariifolium

Phyteuma globulariifolium, commonly known as the globeleaf phyteuma, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features distinctive, rounded, lobed leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
