Phlomis × tunceliensis

Phlomis × tunceliensis, commonly known as the Turkish firethorn, is a hybrid perennial plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, orange-red tubular flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are typically gray-green and hairy, providing a striking contrast to the vivid flowers.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its ornate appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
