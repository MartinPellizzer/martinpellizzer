Polygonum lapathifolium var. virescens

Polygonum lapathifolium var. virescens, commonly known as the greenleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia.

It features upright stems that are often reddish or greenish in color, and it produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red. The leaves are lance-shaped with a slightly hairy surface, and they tend to be broader towards the base of the plant. This species is often found in moist soils along stream banks, wetlands, and disturbed areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping for its vibrant foliage and flowering display.
