Pavonia coxii

Pavonia coxii, commonly known as the scarlet pavonia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, bright red to orange-red bract-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage is characterized by ovate to lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture.

Pavonia coxii is valued in gardens for its vibrant floral display and adaptability to various growing conditions.
