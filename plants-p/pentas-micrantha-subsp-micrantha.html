Pentas micrantha subsp. micrantha

Pentas micrantha subsp.

micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered pentas, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink.

The plant produces numerous small, showy blooms throughout the growing season, making it a popular ornamental species. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and slightly hairy, with a distinct aromatic scent.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
