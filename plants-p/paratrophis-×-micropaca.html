Paratrophis × micropaca

Paratrophis × micropaca, commonly known as the hybrid paratrophis, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Gesneriaceae.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically display a combination of red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This hybrid is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
