Pogonia buchananii

Pogonia buchananii, commonly known as Buchanan's pogonia, is a rare and striking orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its intricate, fragrant flowers that feature a unique combination of colors, including pink, white, and purple. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist woodlands with rich, organic soil. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy coating, which helps retain moisture.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is listed as endangered in several states, making conservation efforts crucial for its survival.
