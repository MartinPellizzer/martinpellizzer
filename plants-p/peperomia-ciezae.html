Peperomia ciezae

Peperomia ciezae, commonly known as the Cieza's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink, giving it an ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Peperomia ciezae is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
