Peperomia nudipetiola

Peperomia nudipetiola, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery or pale green waxy coating, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate watering.

Peperomia nudipetiola is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and low maintenance requirements.
