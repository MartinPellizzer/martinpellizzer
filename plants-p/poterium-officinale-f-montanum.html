Poterium officinale f. montanum

Poterium officinale f. montanum, commonly known as the mountain tansy, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small, yellowish-green flower heads that resemble dandelions. This plant thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine regions. It has a strong aromatic scent and contains essential oils that have been used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to harsh mountain environments.
