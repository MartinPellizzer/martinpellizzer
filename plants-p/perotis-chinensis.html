Perotis chinensis

Perotis chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese periwinkle, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun exposure.

Perotis chinensis is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
