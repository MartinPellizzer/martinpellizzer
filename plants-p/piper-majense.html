Piper majense

Piper majense, commonly known as the Java pepper or long pepper, is a flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, including India, Indonesia, and the Philippines. The plant produces small, elongated berries that are used both as a spice and in traditional medicine. Its leaves are often used in culinary applications, adding a spicy flavor to various dishes.

The plant thrives in humid, tropical climates and is typically cultivated for its aromatic fruits.
