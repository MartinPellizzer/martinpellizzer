Pleurothallis reginae

Pleurothallis reginae, commonly known as the Queen's Pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. It is noted for its compact size and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable in some regions.
