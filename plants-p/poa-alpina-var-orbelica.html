Poa alpina var. orbelica

Poa alpina var. orbelica, commonly known as the alpine bluegrass, is a variant of the alpine bluegrass species native to high-altitude regions.

It is a low-growing perennial grass with narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences containing numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and mountainous regions.

Its adaptability to harsh climates makes it an important component of alpine ecosystems.
