Pelargonium Violiflorum

Purple geranium, scientifically known as Pelargonium violiflorum, is a strikingly beautiful plant native to South Africa. It is celebrated for its vibrant purple flowers and lush green foliage that add a touch of elegance to any garden or indoor space. This species belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is commonly grown for its ornamental value. Its adaptability to various growing conditions makes it a popular choice among both novice and experienced gardeners. With its unique appearance and easy care requirements, the purple geranium is a favorite in botanical collections and home gardens alike.
