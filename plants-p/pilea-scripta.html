Pilea scripta

Pilea scripta, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a popular houseplant native to China.

It features rosettes of round, waxy leaves that are often used as currency in traditional Chinese culture. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is relatively easy to care for, making it a favorite among indoor gardeners.

Pilea scripta can be propagated through leaf cuttings, which makes it an accessible plant for propagation and sharing.
