Pennisetum schliebenii

Pennisetum schliebenii, commonly known as the Schlieben's fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that produce fluffy, white plumes during the flowering season. The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and is often grown for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Pennisetum schliebenii is also valued for its ability to attract birds and other wildlife.
