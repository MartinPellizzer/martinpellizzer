Philippia elsieana

Philippia elsieana, commonly known as the Elsie's lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense rosette at the base.

Philippia elsieana is well-suited for rock gardens and moist, well-drained soils, making it a popular choice among gardeners.
