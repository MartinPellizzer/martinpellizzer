Pittosporum dallii

Pittosporum dallii, commonly known as the silver pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped, dark green leaves that have a silvery underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by bright red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Pittosporum dallii is also valued for its ability to attract native birds and insects.
