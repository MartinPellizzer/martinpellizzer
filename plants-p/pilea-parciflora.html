Pilea parciflora

Pilea parciflora, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its round, succulent leaves that resemble small coins, hence its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. It is a popular houseplant due to its easy care and attractive foliage.

Pilea parciflora thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
