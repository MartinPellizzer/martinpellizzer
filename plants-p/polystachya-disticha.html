Polystachya disticha

Polystachya disticha, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzagging inflorescence and clusters of small, pale green to white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and swamps. Its leaves are elongated and often have a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss.

Polystachya disticha is valued for its unique appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
