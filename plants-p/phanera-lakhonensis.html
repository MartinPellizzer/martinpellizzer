Phanera lakhonensis

Phanera lakhonensis, commonly known as the Lakhon orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand, where it thrives in tropical rainforests. The plant is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It is often found growing on tree trunks or branches, exhibiting an epiphytic growth habit.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
