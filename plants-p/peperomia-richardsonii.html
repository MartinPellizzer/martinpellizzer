Peperomia richardsonii

Peperomia richardsonii, commonly known as the velvet plant, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia richardsonii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and ability to adapt to indoor conditions.
