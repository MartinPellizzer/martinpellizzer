Pennisetum depauperatum

Pennisetum depauperatum, commonly known as the slender fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces tall, slender inflorescences that resemble a fountain. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats and is adapted to arid environments. It has fibrous roots and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Pennisetum depauperatum is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in degraded areas.
