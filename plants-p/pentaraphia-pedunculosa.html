Pentaraphia pedunculosa

Pentaraphia pedunculosa, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pendulous inflorescences. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and stream banks. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink.

The species is noted for its unique floral structure and is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
