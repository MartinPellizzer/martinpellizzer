Pleurospermum yulungense

Pleurospermum yulungense, commonly known as Yulung Mountain Wormwood, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy stems. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in rocky or dry habitats and is often found in alpine regions.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
