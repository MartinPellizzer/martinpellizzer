Pelargonium × bluntianum

Pelargonium × bluntianum, commonly known as the hybrid geranium, is a flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The leaves are typically rounded with a slightly waxy texture, and the plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is relatively easy to care for in garden settings.

Pelargonium × bluntianum is a popular choice for borders, containers, and hanging baskets due to its vibrant blooms and compact size.
