Pellionia radicans var. chikushiensis

Pellionia radicans var. chikushiensis, commonly known as the Chikushi trailing pellionia, is a low-growing evergreen perennial native to Japan.

It features delicate, heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental foliage.

This variety is valued for its ability to cascade gracefully from hanging baskets or containers.
