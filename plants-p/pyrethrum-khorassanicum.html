Pyrethrum khorassanicum

Pyrethrum khorassanicum, commonly known as the Afghan pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant produces fine, hair-like structures called trichomes, which contain natural insecticides called pyrethrins. These compounds make it effective for pest control in organic gardening.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid climates.
