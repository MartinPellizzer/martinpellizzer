Petunia calycina

Petunia calycina, commonly known as the calycine petunia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters and has hairy, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Petunia calycina is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for.
