Phaius Grandiflorus

Phaius orchid, scientifically known as Phaius grandiflorus, is a striking member of the orchid family native to Southeast Asia. This plant is celebrated for its large, showy flowers that bloom in vibrant shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in wet, shaded environments and is often found in marshy areas and along riverbanks. The Phaius orchid is prized by botanists and horticulturists for its ornamental value and ecological significance. Its unique structure and reproductive strategies make it a fascinating subject for study and conservation efforts.
