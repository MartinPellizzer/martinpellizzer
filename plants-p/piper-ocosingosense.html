Piper ocosingosense

Piper ocosingosense, commonly known as the Ocosingosense pepper vine, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various pepper species. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with long, slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are followed by berries that turn red when mature.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and potential medicinal properties.
