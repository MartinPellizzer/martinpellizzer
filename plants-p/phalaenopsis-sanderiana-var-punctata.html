Phalaenopsis sanderiana var. punctata

Phalaenopsis sanderiana var. punctata, commonly known as the speckled phalaenopsis, is a hybrid orchid prized for its elegant, long-lasting blooms.

It features striking, velvety petals with distinctive speckles or spots, giving it a unique appearance. Native to Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its adaptability and minimal care requirements.

The plant's pseudobulbs store water, allowing it to tolerate short periods of drought.
