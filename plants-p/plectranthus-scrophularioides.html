Plectranthus scrophularioides

Plectranthus scrophularioides, commonly known as the purple heart plant, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant is notable for its striking violet to purple tubular flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Plectranthus scrophularioides thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.
