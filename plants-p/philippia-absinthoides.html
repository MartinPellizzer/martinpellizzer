Philippia absinthoides

Philippia absinthoides, commonly known as the silverleaf philippia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.

Philippia absinthoides is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
