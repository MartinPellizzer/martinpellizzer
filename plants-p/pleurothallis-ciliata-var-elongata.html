Pleurothallis ciliata var. elongata

Pleurothallis ciliata var. elongata, commonly known as the elongated ciliata orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, linear leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its inflorescence is typically pendulous, with flowers arranged in a raceme.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
