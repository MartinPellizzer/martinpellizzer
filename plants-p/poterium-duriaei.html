Poterium duriaei

Poterium duriaei, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, bright orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species.

Poterium duriaei is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
