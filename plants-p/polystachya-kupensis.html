Polystachya kupensis

Polystachya kupensis, commonly known as the Kupensis spikegrass, is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is a terrestrial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as tropical and subtropical forests. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing numerous small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, often arranged in a dense rosette.

Polystachya kupensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique appearance.
