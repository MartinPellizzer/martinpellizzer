Polyalthia macropoda

Polyalthia macropoda, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a striking visual display. The plant has pinnate leaves with numerous small leaflets and produces woody capsules containing seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.
