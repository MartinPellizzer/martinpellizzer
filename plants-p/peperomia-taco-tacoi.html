Peperomia taco-tacoi

Peperomia taco-tacoi, commonly known as the Mexican pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Central America.

It features dense, glossy green foliage with a slightly textured surface, and its small, tubular flowers bloom in shades of red and purple. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and ease of care contribute to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
