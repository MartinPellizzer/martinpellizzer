Phania cajalbanica

Phania cajalbanica, commonly known as the Ceylon star anise, is a flowering plant native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and distinctive fruit. The plant produces small, star-shaped berries that are used in traditional medicine and as a spice. Its botanical features include opposite leaves, fragrant flowers, and a unique fruit structure.
