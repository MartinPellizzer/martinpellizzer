Piper leyteanum

Piper leyteanum, commonly known as Leyte pepper, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing vines and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its fruit is a dark purple, three-lobed berry that contains the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine.

The plant's botanical features make it distinct within its genus, with its unique morphology and chemical composition.
