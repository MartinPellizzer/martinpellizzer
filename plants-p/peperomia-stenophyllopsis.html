Peperomia stenophyllopsis

Peperomia stenophyllopsis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green, elongated leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia stenophyllopsis is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
