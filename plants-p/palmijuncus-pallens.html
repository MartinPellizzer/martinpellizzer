Palmijuncus pallens

Palmijuncus pallens, commonly known as the slender palm, is a flowering plant belonging to the Arecaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnate leaves that are often feather-like in appearance. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, round fruits.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in warm climates for its aesthetic appeal.
