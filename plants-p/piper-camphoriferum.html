Piper camphoriferum

Piper camphoriferum, commonly known as camphor pepper, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruit is a drupe that contains a single seed, and it is often cultivated for its medicinal and aromatic properties.

The plant's essential oils, particularly camphor, are used in traditional medicine and as a natural remedy for various ailments.
