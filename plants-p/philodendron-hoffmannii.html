Philodendron hoffmannii

Philodendron hoffmannii, commonly known as the Hoffmann's philodendron, is a tropical vine native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by bright red berries that are edible for birds. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its rapid growth and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
