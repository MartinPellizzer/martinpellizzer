Pyrethrum pallidum f. bilbilitanum

Pyrethrum pallidum f. bilbilitanum, commonly known as the pale pyrethrum, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its pale yellow to white flowers, which are typically arranged in dense clusters. The plant is native to regions of southern Europe and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are hairy to the touch.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its mild insect-repelling properties.
