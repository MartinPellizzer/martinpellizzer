Pecteilis Mutica

Pecteilis mutica is a rare and fascinating plant native to the coastal regions of California. It thrives in sandy soils and is often found along the shoreline where the tides meet the land. This plant is known for its unique appearance, featuring small, white flowers that bloom in the spring. Despite its delicate look, it is remarkably resilient, surviving harsh coastal conditions. Its presence is a sign of a healthy ecosystem, making it an important indicator species for coastal conservation efforts.
