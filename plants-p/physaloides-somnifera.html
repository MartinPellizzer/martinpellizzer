Physaloides somnifera

Physaloides somnifera, commonly known as the sleepy nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades such as tomatoes and peppers. The plant is characterized by its bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. Its fruit, which is a fleshy berry, contains seeds and is often used in traditional medicine.

However, the plant is toxic and should not be ingested without proper knowledge.
