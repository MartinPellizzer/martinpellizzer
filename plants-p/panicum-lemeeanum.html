Panicum lemeeanum

Panicum lemeeanum, commonly known as lemmee's panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, blade-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it adaptable to a variety of environments.

Due to its ornamental value and resilience, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
