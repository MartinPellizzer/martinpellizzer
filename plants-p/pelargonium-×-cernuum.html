Pelargonium × cernuum

Pelargonium × cernuum, commonly known as the hanging geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The leaves are usually rounded or heart-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

It is often used in hanging baskets or as a ground cover due to its compact growth and ornamental value.
