Pleroma robustum

Pleroma robustum, commonly known as the robust pleurothallis, is a flowering plant native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often colorful flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually fleshy and adapted to retain moisture in the humid, misty environments it inhabits.

Pleroma robustum is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
