Premna cumingiana var. pierreana

Premna cumingiana var. pierreana, commonly known as the Pierre's premna, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Nigeria and Cameroon. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in whorls along the stems.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens.
