Polygala crucianelloides

Polygala crucianelloides, commonly known as the crucianella polygala, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas. The plant features small, delicate flowers that are usually pink or purple in color, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, adapted to reduce water loss in arid environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
