Piper simile

Piper simile, commonly known as the pink pepper plant, is a tropical vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its small, bright pink berries that resemble peppercorns, hence its common name. The plant belongs to the Piperaceae family and is closely related to black pepper. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly glossy appearance.

The fruit develops in clusters and is often used as a spice in culinary applications.
