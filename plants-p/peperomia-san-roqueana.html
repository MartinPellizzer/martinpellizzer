Peperomia san-roqueana

Peperomia san-roqueana, commonly known as the San Roque Peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, often adorned with subtle markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers indirect light, making it an ideal choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a popular ornamental plant among plant enthusiasts.
