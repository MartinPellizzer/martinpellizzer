Protium nervosum

Protium nervosum, commonly known as the Indian beech, is a deciduous tree native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Burseraceae and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and alternate, simple leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by spherical, woody fruits containing three seeds. The tree plays an important ecological role in its native habitats, providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
