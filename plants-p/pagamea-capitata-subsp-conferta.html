Pagamea capitata subsp. conferta

Pagamea capitata subsp.

conferta, commonly known as the dense-headed pagamea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in shrubland and rocky habitats, preferring well-drained soils. Its subsp. conferta form is distinguished by its dense, compact inflorescences and shorter, more robust stems.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
