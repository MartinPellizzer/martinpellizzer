Pithocarpa achilleoides

Pithocarpa achilleoides, commonly known as the African snowball, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its small, white, clustered flowers that resemble snowballs. The plant has a woody stem with a spongy, white pith, which is a distinctive botanical feature. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a slightly hairy surface.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
