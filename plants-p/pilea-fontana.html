Pilea fontana

Pilea fontana, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features round, succulent leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea fontana is known for its ability to purify the air and is relatively easy to care for.
