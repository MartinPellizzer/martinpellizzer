Potentilla megalontodon

Potentilla megalontodon, commonly known as the giant tooth potentilla, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant's leaves are typically oval-shaped with a serrated edge, and it thrives in well-drained, rocky soils. Potentilla megalontodon is known for its hardy nature and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.

It is often found in alpine meadows and is valued for its ornamental appeal in garden settings.
