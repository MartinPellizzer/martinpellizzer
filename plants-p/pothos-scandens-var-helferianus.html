Pothos scandens var. helferianus

Pothos scandens var. helferianus, commonly known as the Helferian pothos, is a vigorous trailing vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly waxy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red berries. The plant is highly adaptable, thriving in a variety of light conditions but preferring bright, indirect sunlight. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.

Its rapid growth rate makes it suitable for hanging baskets or as a climbing plant in greenhouses.
