Peperomia huantana var. enenyasana

Peperomia huantana var. enenyasana, commonly known as the Huantana enenyasana, is a rare and unique species native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and distinctive, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
