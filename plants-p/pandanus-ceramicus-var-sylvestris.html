Pandanus ceramicus var. sylvestris

Pandanus ceramicus var. sylvestris, commonly known as the wild pandanus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with fragrant, yellowish flowers. Its fibrous leaves are harvested for weaving mats and baskets.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or swampy areas.
