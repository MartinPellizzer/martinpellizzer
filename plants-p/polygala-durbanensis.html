Polygala durbanensis

Polygala durbanensis, commonly known as the Durban milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, often pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy or gravelly soils. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
