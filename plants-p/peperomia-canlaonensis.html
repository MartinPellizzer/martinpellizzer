Peperomia canlaonensis

Peperomia canlaonensis, commonly known as the Canlaon peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery or white pubescence. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in spikes.

Peperomia canlaonensis is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.
