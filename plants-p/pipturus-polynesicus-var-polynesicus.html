Pipturus polynesicus var. polynesicus

Pipturus polynesicus var. polynesicus, commonly known as the Polynesian tree fern, is a species native to the Pacific Islands.

It is a member of the Cyatheaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in coastal and lowland areas. Its rhizomes are used in traditional medicine by indigenous communities.
