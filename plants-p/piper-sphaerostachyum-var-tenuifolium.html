Piper sphaerostachyum var. tenuifolium

Piper sphaerostachyum var. tenuifolium, commonly known as the slender-leaf sphaerostachyum pepper, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers. Its fruit is a small, yellowish berry that ripens to a dark color.

The botanical features of this plant make it a unique subject for horticultural study and potential medicinal research.
