Polystichum stimulans var. delavayi

Polystichum stimulans var. delavayi, commonly known as the Delavay's sword fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the mountainous regions of southwest China.

It features fronds that are typically bipinnate, with a dark green coloration and a leathery texture. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forest understories. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help anchor it to rocky or shaded soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in shaded garden settings.
