Pavetta indica var. polyantha

Pavetta indica var. polyantha, commonly known as the pink pavetta, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact shrub form and clusters of small, fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
