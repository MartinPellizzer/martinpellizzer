Phalaenopsis lueddemanniana var. purpurea

Phalaenopsis lueddemanniana var. purpurea, commonly known as the purple phalaenopsis, is a flowering orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and long-lasting, showy flowers that range from white to deep purple. The plant produces inflorescences with numerous blooms, often arranged in a raceme, and features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining potting media.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is popular in horticultural trade.
