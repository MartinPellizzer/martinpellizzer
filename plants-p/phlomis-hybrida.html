Phlomis hybrida

Phlomis hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid globe thistle, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and striking spherical flower heads that range in color from blue to purple. The plant features lance-shaped, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs, contributing to its drought-resistant nature. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy choice for gardens.

Phlomis hybrida is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
