Prunus × fruticosa

Prunus × fruticosa, commonly known as the cherry plum, is a hybrid shrub or small tree belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in early spring. The plant typically bears small, sweet-tart fruits that are edible and often used in jams and preserves. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a glossy green surface with finely serrated edges.

Prunus × fruticosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various soil types.
