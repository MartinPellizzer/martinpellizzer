Pteris brevinervis

Pteris brevinervis, commonly known as the narrow-nerved water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Pteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically less than 30 centimeters in length. The plant has a creeping rhizome and produces spores in clusters on the underside of its fronds. It thrives in moist, shady environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Pteris brevinervis is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
