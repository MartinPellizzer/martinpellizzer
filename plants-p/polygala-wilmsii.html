Polygala wilmsii

Polygala wilmsii, commonly known as the Wilmsii milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are usually pink or purple in color, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, adapting to its harsh environmental conditions.

Polygala wilmsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
