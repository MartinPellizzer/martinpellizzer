Poa linkii

Poa linkii, commonly known as the Link's bentgrass, is a species of grass native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and slender, dark green blades. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and along stream banks. Poa linkii has small, spikelet-based inflorescences and produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity in its native habitat.
