Paepalanthus fraternus var. fraternus

Paepalanthus fraternus var. fraternus, commonly known as the common arrowroot.

This plant is a perennial herb native to South America, particularly found in regions of Brazil, Argentina, and Paraguay. It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges, and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in spike-like inflorescences.
