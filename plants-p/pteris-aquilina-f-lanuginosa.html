Pteris aquilina f. lanuginosa

Pteris aquilina f. lanuginosa, commonly known as the silky water fern, is a fern species characterized by its soft, velvety fronds.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Southeast Asia. The plant features bipinnate leaves with a distinctive, feathery appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near water sources.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant collections.
