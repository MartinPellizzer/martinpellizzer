Poterium cinctum

Poterium cinctum, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features basal leaves that are deeply lobed and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often reddish in color. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in a distinctive crown-like arrangement at the top of the stem.

Poterium cinctum thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.
