Polygonum mariae

Polygonum mariae, commonly known as Mary's polygonum, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants commonly referred to as buckwheats. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It features slender stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink.

Its botanical characteristics make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.
