Persica vulgaris var. compressa

Persica vulgaris var. compressa, commonly known as the flat peach.

This variant is a cultivar of the peach tree, characterized by its flattened or compressed fruit shape. It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is native to regions with temperate climates. The plant produces sweet, aromatic fruits that are often used in culinary applications.

Its botanical features include hairy stems, broad leaves, and a compact growth habit.
