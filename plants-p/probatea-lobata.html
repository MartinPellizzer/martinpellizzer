Probatea lobata

Probatea lobata, commonly known as the lobed arrowhead, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically light green with a waxy texture. The plant thrives in shallow, slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in marshes and ponds. Its rhizomes spread horizontally, allowing it to colonize large areas quickly.

Probatea lobata is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
