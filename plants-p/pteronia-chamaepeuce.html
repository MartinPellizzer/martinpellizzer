Pteronia chamaepeuce

Pteronia chamaepeuce, commonly known as the Cape marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in coastal and sandy areas, preferring well-drained soil and full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers range in color from white to yellow.

It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its hardiness and attractive appearance.
