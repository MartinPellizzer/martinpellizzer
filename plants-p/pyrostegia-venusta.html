Pyrostegia venusta

Pyrostegia venusta, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, pointed leaflets. Its bark is smooth and often has a reddish hue.

Pyrostegia venusta is often cultivated as an ornamental tree due to its vibrant floral display.
