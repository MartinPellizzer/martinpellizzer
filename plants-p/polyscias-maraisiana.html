Polyscias maraisiana

Polyscias maraisiana, commonly known as the Marais Island sycamore, is a member of the Araliaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a large evergreen shrub or small tree, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter in color underneath. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by orange-red berries.

Polyscias maraisiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is valued for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
