Pyrularia oleifera

Pyrularia oleifera, commonly known as the Chinese sandmat, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, spiny, globe-shaped fruits. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in sandy or rocky soils.

Pyrularia oleifera is known for its hardy nature and ability to survive in harsh climatic conditions.
