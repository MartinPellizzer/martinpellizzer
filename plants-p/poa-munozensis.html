Poa munozensis

Poa munozensis, commonly known as the Munoz grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to southern South America, particularly found in Argentina and Chile. This perennial grass is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems. The leaves are narrow and often have a bluish-gray coloration, which helps in reducing water loss in arid environments.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat and is of interest to botanists studying plant adaptations to harsh climates.
