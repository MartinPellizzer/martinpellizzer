Peperomia tatei

Peperomia tatei, commonly known as the silver mohair plant, is a compact, low-maintenance houseplant native to Brazil.

It features dense, silver-gray foliage with a velvety texture, giving it a unique, ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers, though it is more prized for its foliage than its blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia tatei is known for its ability to tolerate dry conditions, making it a popular choice for beginners and those with busy schedules.
