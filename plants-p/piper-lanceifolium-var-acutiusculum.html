Piper lanceifolium var. acutiusculum

Piper lanceifolium var. acutiusculum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pepper plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by black, peppery fruits that are used in traditional medicine and cuisine.

This variety is valued for its aromatic foliage and medicinal properties.
