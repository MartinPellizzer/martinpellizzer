Pedicularis Resupinata Var. Pubescens

Purple monkeyflower, scientifically known as Pedicularis resupinata var. pubescens, is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America. With its vivid purple petals and unique spurred structure, it stands out among the flora of its habitat. This species thrives in cool, moist environments, often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes. Its delicate appearance belies its resilience, adapting to harsh conditions with remarkable tenacity. The purple monkeyflower is not only a botanical curiosity but also a symbol of the fragile beauty of high-altitude ecosystems.
