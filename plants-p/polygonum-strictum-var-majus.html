Polygonum strictum var. majus

Polygonum strictum var. majus, commonly known as redroot smartweed, is a herbaceous annual plant native to North America.

It features upright, branched stems that are reddish in color, often with a purple tinge. The leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and have a slightly hairy surface. Its small, clustered flowers are typically pink to red, appearing in late summer to early fall.

The plant thrives in moist soils and is often found in wetlands, ditches, and disturbed areas.
