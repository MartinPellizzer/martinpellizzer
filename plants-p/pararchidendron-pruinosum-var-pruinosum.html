Pararchidendron pruinosum var. pruinosum

Pararchidendron pruinosum var. pruinosum, commonly known as the silky leaf tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the rainy season. It is typically found in lowland tropical forests and is adapted to humid, shaded environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic qualities.
