Pimpinella sinica

Pimpinella sinica, commonly known as Chinese茴香, is a biennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes plants like carrots and parsley. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers in umbels. Its seeds are used in traditional Chinese medicine and cuisine.
