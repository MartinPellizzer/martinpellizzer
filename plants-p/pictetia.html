Pictetia

Pictetia, commonly known as the sea holly, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its striking, often spiky, blue or purple flowers that resemble the shape of a holly leaf. The plants typically grow in coastal areas and are well adapted to saline environments. Pictetia species are often found in sandy or rocky soils along shorelines.
