Pleurothallis galerasensis

Pleurothallis galerasensis, commonly known as the Galeras orchid, is a species of orchid native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a fragrant scent that attracts pollinators.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and contributes to the biodiversity of its native habitat.
