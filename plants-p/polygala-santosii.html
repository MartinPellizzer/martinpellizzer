Polygala santosii

Polygala santosii, commonly known as the Santos' milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the island of Socotra.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its unique, spiny stems and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in arid and rocky habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with a distinctive shape that resembles a milkweed flower.

Polygala santosii is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
