Pilea elegans var. latifolia

Pilea elegans var. latifolia, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its round, fleshy leaves that resemble small coins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by bright orange berries. It is a compact, bushy plant that thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Pilea elegans var. latifolia is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
