Polygonum diffusum

Polygonum diffusum, commonly known as broadleaf knotweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Asia.

It features upright stems that are often hairy and can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. The leaves are broadly ovate to rhomboidal, with a smooth or slightly toothed margin. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces small, three-angled berries.

This plant is often found in moist soils along streambanks and in wetlands.
