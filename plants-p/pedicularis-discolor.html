Pedicularis discolor

Pedicularis discolor, commonly known as the Fairy Slipper, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

Pedicularis discolor is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
