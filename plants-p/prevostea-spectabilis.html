Prevostea spectabilis

Prevostea spectabilis, commonly known as the showy prevostea, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that consist of numerous small, tubular flowers clustered together. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or mountainous habitats. Its flowers are usually bright yellow or orange, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
