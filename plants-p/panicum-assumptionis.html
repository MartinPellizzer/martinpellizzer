Panicum assumptionis

Panicum assumptionis, commonly known as the purpletop grass, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and vibrant purple flower heads that appear in late summer. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in open woodlands and prairies. Its seeds are an important food source for birds and small mammals.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
