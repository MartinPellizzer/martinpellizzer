Polypodium endresii

Polypodium endresii, commonly known as the Endres' shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its shield-shaped fronds, which are typically leathery and have a distinctive, compact growth habit. Native to tropical regions of Central and South America, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. The fronds are usually dark green and have a rough, textured surface.

It is often found growing on rocky substrates or in dense forest understories.
