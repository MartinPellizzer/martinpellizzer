Pleurothallis undulata

Pleurothallis undulata, commonly known as the undulating pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. Its inflorescence is usually short and consists of several blooms arranged closely together.

Pleurothallis undulata is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
