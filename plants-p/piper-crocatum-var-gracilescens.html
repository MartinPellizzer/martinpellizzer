Piper crocatum var. gracilescens

Piper crocatum var. gracilescens, commonly known as the slender pepper plant, is a tropical vine belonging to the Piperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces tiny, greenish-white flowers that develop into small, round berries. These berries are typically harvested when mature for their peppery flavor, which is used in culinary applications.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
