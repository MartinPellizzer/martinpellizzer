Pennisetum sphacelatum

Pennisetum sphacelatum, commonly known as purple top grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and distinctive purple-tinted inflorescence. The plant typically grows in open woodlands and grasslands, thriving in well-drained soils. Its fibrous roots make it a valuable plant for erosion control.

Pennisetum sphacelatum is also used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
