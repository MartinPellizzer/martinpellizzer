Panicum viciniflorum

Panicum viciniflorum, commonly known as inland dropseed, is a grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, grass-like foliage and produces small, delicate flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and sandy soils. It has a clump-forming growth habit and is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value.

Panicum viciniflorum is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
