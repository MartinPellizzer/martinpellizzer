Peucedanum italicum

Peucedanum italicum, commonly known as cow parsley or Queen Anne's lace, is a biennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features finely divided, fern-like leaves and produces fluffy, white umbels of flowers that resemble lace. The plant is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and disturbed soils. It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes other aromatic herbs like parsley and dill.

Its seeds are small, dry, and often used in traditional herbal remedies.
