Philodendron luisae

Philodendron luisae, commonly known as the Luisae philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically a vibrant green, with some varieties displaying variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by berries that turn from green to red as they mature. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

Philodendron luisae is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
