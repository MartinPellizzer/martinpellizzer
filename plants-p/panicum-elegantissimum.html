Panicum elegantissimum

Panicum elegantissimum, commonly known as the elegant panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and delicate, feather-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers, which are often wind-pollinated. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its ornamental value comes from its graceful appearance and ability to add texture to landscapes.
