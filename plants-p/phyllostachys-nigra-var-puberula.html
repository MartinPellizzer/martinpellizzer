Phyllostachys nigra var. puberula

Phyllostachys nigra var. puberula, commonly known as the black bamboo, is a variant of the black bamboo species native to China.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black culms that are covered with a fine, silky pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant is a clump-forming grass that can grow quite tall, reaching heights of up to 10 meters in optimal conditions. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a glossy green color that contrasts with the dark stems.

This bamboo is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking appearance and resilience in various climates.
