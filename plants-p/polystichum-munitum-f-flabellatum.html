Polystichum munitum f. flabellatum

Polystichum munitum f. flabellatum, commonly known as the fern-leaf sword fern, is a cultivar of the sword fern species native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It is characterized by its large, deeply lobed, and often fan-shaped fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its fronds are typically a dark green with a silvery underside, and it produces sporangia on the undersides of its leaves.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in horticultural settings.
