Pleioblastus guilongshanensis

Pleioblastus guilongshanensis, commonly known as the Guilongshan bamboo, is a species native to China.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, elongated culms that often form dense clumps. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually a vibrant green, providing ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturalists alike.
