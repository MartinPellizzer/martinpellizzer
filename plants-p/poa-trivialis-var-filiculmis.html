Poa trivialis var. filiculmis

Poa trivialis var. filiculmis, commonly known as the slender meadow grass, is a variant of the common bentgrass.

It is a low-growing perennial grass native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-based flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it adaptable to a variety of ecological niches.
