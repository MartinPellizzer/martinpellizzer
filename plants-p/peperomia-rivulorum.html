Peperomia rivulorum

Peperomia rivulorum, commonly known as the river peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Peperomia rivulorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care in indoor settings.
