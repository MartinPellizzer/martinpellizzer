Pittosporum kahananum

Pittosporum kahananum, commonly known as the Kahananum pittosporum, is a small evergreen shrub native to New Zealand.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
