Phelypaea tuberosa

Phelypaea tuberosa, commonly known as the tuberous marsh fern, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and tuberous roots. Phelypaea tuberosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
