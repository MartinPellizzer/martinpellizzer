Pittosporum patulum

Pittosporum patulum, commonly known as the New Zealand pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features dark green, leathery leaves that are often glossy and have a distinct, aromatic scent. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
