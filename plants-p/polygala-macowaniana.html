Polygala macowaniana

Polygala macowaniana, commonly known as the Macowan's milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, with a distinctive hooded structure.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging conditions.
