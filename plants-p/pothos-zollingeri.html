Pothos zollingeri

Pothos zollingeri, commonly known as the golden pothos, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, glossy green leaves with a subtle yellowish or golden tint, giving it its common name. The plant is known for its vigorous growth and ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular houseplant. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers, though it is rarely seen in cultivation.

Pothos zollingeri is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ease of care.
