Pyrrhocactus lissocarpus

Pyrrhocactus lissocarpus, commonly known as the smooth-fruited pyrrhocactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruits are small, smooth, and contain seeds.

Pyrrhocactus lissocarpus is typically found in rocky or arid environments where it thrives with minimal water.
