Physematium obtusum

Physematium obtusum, commonly known as the broadleaf water fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are typically floating on the water surface. The plant has a complex rhizome system that anchors it to submerged substrates. Its spores are released into the water, allowing for propagation in suitable aquatic environments.

Physematium obtusum plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
