Panicum viride var. humifusum

Panicum viride var. humifusum, commonly known as green panic grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bright green and can grow in a variety of soil types. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers, which are wind-pollinated. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for ornamental and erosion control purposes.

Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine and as a forage crop in some regions.
