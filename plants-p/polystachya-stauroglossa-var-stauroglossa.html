Polystachya stauroglossa var. stauroglossa

Polystachya stauroglossa var. stauroglossa, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescences that display shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are broad and leathery, adapted to retain moisture in its natural habitat.

The flowers bloom in late summer to early autumn, attracting various pollinators with their vibrant colors and sweet fragrance.
