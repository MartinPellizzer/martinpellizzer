Podocarpus montanus var. diversifolius

Podocarpus montanus var. diversifolius, commonly known as the variable podocarp, is a species of evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and can grow up to 30 meters in height. The leaves are usually alternate and vary in shape, with some being lanceolate and others ovate, giving it its varietal name. It produces small, spherical cones that contain edible seeds.

This plant is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
