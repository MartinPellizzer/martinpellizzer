Prevostea lucida

Prevostea lucida, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows up to 15 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that have a silvery underside, giving it its common name. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by edible, round fruits.

Prevostea lucida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and parks for its aesthetic qualities.
