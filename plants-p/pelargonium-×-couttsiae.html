Pelargonium × couttsiae

Pelargonium × couttsiae, commonly known as the Regal Geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly serrated edge, adding to its ornamental appeal. This plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden beds and containers.

It is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and is a popular choice in both ornamental gardening and cut flower arrangements.
