Podocarpium podocarpum var. fallax

Podocarpium podocarpum var. fallax, commonly known as the fallax variety of the common podocarp, is a unique botanical variant characterized by its distinct leaf morphology and growth habits.

This variety exhibits a more compact growth form compared to the typical podocarp, with leaves that are narrower and more elongated. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in temperate forest understories. The plant's foliage displays a rich green color, which can shift to a reddish hue under certain environmental conditions.

Botanically, it is noted for its resilience to humid conditions and its ability to form dense, low-growing mats.
