Piper schlimii

Piper schlimii, commonly known as the dwarf sapphire plant, is a member of the Piperaceae family.

It is a low-growing, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
