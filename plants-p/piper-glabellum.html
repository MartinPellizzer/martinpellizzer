Piper glabellum

Piper glabellum, commonly known as the smooth pepper vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by elongated, slender fruits. Its leaves are typically ovate with a smooth surface, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

The plant is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage.
