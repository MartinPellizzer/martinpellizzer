Peperomia longisetosa

Peperomia longisetosa, commonly known as the long-hair peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are usually dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of green or red, which are followed by small, round berries. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and tolerance for dry conditions.

Peperomia longisetosa is valued for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
