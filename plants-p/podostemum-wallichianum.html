Podostemum wallichianum

Podostemum wallichianum, commonly known as the Himalayan water fern, is a unique aquatic plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It thrives in fast-flowing streams and rivers, where its rhizomes anchor to rocks. The plant has small, delicate leaves that are often submerged, with some floating on the water surface. Its flowers are typically borne on slender stalks and are adapted for water pollination.

Podostemum wallichianum plays an important ecological role in its habitat by providing shelter for aquatic organisms.
