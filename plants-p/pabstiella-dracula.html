Pabstiella dracula

Pabstiella dracula, commonly known as the vampire orchid, is a rare and enigmatic plant belonging to the orchid family.

It is characterized by its striking, dark red petals and velvety texture, which give it an eerie, almost supernatural appearance. Native to the misty highlands of Central America, it thrives in cool, humid environments with rich, well-drained soil. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom during the late spring and early summer.

Due to its rarity and unique beauty, it is highly sought after by botanists and collectors alike.
