Peperomia umbellifera

Peperomia umbellifera, commonly known as the umbrella peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are often green with reddish or purple undersides, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, white, umbrella-shaped inflorescences, which are characteristic of its common name. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia umbellifera is relatively low-maintenance, requiring moderate watering and minimal pruning.
