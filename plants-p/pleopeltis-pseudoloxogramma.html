Pleopeltis pseudoloxogramma

Pleopeltis pseudoloxogramma, commonly known as the false loxogramma fern, is a member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is a terrestrial fern that typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas.
