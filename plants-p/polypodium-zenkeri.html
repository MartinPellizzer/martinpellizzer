Polypodium zenkeri

Polypodium zenkeri, commonly known as the Zenker's shield fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant features long, narrow fronds with a distinctive, shield-shaped appearance, which gives it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on tree trunks or rocks.

Polypodium zenkeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
