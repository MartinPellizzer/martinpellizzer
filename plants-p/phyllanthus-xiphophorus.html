Phyllanthus xiphophorus

Phyllanthus xiphophorus, commonly known as the African starwort, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

Phyllanthus xiphophorus is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various health purposes.
