Poincianella clementis

Poincianella clementis, commonly known as the red flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers that appear in dense, showy clusters during the wet season. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets that are usually arranged in pairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Poincianella clementis is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
