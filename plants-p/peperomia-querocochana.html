Peperomia querocochana

Peperomia querocochana, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color, often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia querocochana is a low-maintenance plant that is well-suited for indoor cultivation.
