Proustia pyrifolia

Proustia pyrifolia, commonly known as the fireleaf plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly South Africa, and is characterized by its striking, lobed leaves that resemble flames.
