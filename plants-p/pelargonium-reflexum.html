Pelargonium reflexum

Pelargonium reflexum, commonly known as the trailing geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and bright, showy flowers. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture and produces flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets and containers.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, Pelargonium reflexum is widely cultivated in gardens and as a decorative plant.
