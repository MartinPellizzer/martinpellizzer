Pleroma sulcatum

Pleroma sulcatum, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spiral-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a distinctive pattern of spots. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

Pleroma sulcatum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
