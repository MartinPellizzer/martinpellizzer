Pteris horizontalis

Pteris horizontalis, commonly known as the Japanese spleenwort, is a fern native to East Asia.

It features delicate, fan-shaped fronds that are often used in ornamental gardening. The plant has a creeping rhizome that allows it to spread along the ground or over rock surfaces. It prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
