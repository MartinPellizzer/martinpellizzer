Ploesslia floribunda

Ploesslia floribunda, commonly known as the flowering ploesslia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, brightly colored flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant typically features narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a dense rosette pattern. Its botanical structure includes fibrous roots and a flowering stem that rises above the foliage.

Ploesslia floribunda is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth or along stream banks.
