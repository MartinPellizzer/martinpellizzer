Podostemum elongatum

Podostemum elongatum, commonly known as the elongated water plant, is a unique aquatic plant found in fast-flowing streams and rivers.

It is characterized by its slender, ribbon-like leaves that are adapted to thrive in turbulent water environments. The plant has small, inconspicuous flowers that are often submerged, and its seeds are dispersed by water currents. It plays an important role in stabilizing riverbanks and providing habitat for aquatic organisms.

Due to its specialized habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
