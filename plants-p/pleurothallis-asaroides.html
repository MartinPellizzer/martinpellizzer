Pleurothallis asaroides

Pleurothallis asaroides, commonly known as the asaroides orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments within the understory of forests. The plant features a short rhizome with a dense cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are usually small and pale green or white, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

It is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and adaptability to certain growing conditions.
