Psychilis × tudiana

Psychilis × tudiana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Psychilis orchid species.

It is a tropical plant that thrives in warm, humid environments. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers in a variety of colors, often with intricate patterns. It has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Botanically, it is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in greenhouse settings.
