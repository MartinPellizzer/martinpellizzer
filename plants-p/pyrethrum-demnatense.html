Pyrethrum demnatense

Pyrethrum demnatense, commonly known as the Demnat's pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of East Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers, which are composed of ray and disk florets. The plant produces pyrethrin compounds, which are natural insecticides used in organic pest control. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a cool climate with moderate rainfall.

Pyrethrum demnatense is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its ecological benefits.
