Phalaenopsis malipoensis

Phalaenopsis malipoensis, commonly known as the Malipo orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its elegant, long-lasting flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often arranged in a fan shape.

This orchid thrives in humid, well-lit environments and is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relative ease of care.
