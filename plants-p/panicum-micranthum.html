Panicum micranthum

Panicum micranthum, commonly known as little bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and tufted inflorescences that turn golden in the fall. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in prairies and open woodlands. It plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for wildlife.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
