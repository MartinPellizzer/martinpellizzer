Peperomia inaudax

Peperomia inaudax, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features broad, velvety, deep green leaves with a waxy texture and a distinctive reddish-purple underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pale green flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate moisture.

Peperomia inaudax is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
