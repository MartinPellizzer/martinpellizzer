Pothos chapelieri

Pothos chapelieri, commonly known as the Variegated Pothos, is a tropical vine native to Madagascar.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a striking combination of green, white, and yellow variegation. The plant is known for its vigorous growth and ability to climb or trail, making it popular as a houseplant. Its adaptability to low light and minimal care requirements contribute to its popularity.

Botanically, it belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies.
