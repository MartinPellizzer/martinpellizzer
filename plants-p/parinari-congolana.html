Parinari congolana

Parinari congolana, commonly known as African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and broad, lance-shaped leaves. Parinari congolana also has medicinal uses in traditional African medicine.
