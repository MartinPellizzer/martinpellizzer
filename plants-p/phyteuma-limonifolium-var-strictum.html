Phyteuma limonifolium var. strictum

Phyteuma limonifolium var. strictum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved phyteuma, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features upright, branching stems that bear showy, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange. The leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a slight bluish tint. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and prolonged blooming period.
