Panicum conjugens

Panicum conjugens, commonly known as silver panicum, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and slender, erect growth habit. The plant produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by wind, contributing to its ability to colonize disturbed soils. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in open woodlands and prairies.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens and erosion control.
