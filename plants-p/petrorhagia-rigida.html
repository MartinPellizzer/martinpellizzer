Petrorhagia rigida

Petrorhagia rigida, commonly known as the rigid petrorhagia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that produces vibrant yellow flowers in the spring. The plant has a woody stem and small, gray-green leaves that are adapted to arid environments. It is often found in rocky soils and coastal areas, thriving in full sun.

Petrorhagia rigida is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to withstand drought and poor soil conditions.
