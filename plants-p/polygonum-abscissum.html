Polygonum abscissum

Polygonum abscissum, commonly known as redroot smartweed, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its upright, reddish stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in pink or red hues, typically in late summer. It thrives in moist soils and is often found in wetlands, ditches, and along stream banks.

Polygonum abscissum is an annual or biennial species that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
