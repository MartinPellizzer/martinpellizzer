Phylica constricta var. constricta

Phylica constricta var. constricta, commonly known as the narrowleaf phylica, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant is often found in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. It has a compact growth habit and is sometimes used in ornamental gardening.

The species is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in degraded landscapes.
