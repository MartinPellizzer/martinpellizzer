Papaver × inexpertum

Papaver × inexpertum, commonly known as the hybrid poppy, is a botanical hybrid resulting from the cross between Papaver rhoeas (common poppy) and Papaver orientale (oriental poppy).

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to orange, with a distinctive black center. The plant is a herbaceous annual or biennial, often reaching heights of 30 to 60 centimeters. Its foliage consists of deeply lobed, dark green leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem.

Papaver × inexpertum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and floral arrangements.
