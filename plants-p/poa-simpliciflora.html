Poa simpliciflora

Poa simpliciflora, commonly known as the simple-flowered grass, is a species of grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with tiny, greenish flowers. It thrives in moist, temperate climates and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Due to its delicate appearance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.
