Phylica alba

Phylica alba, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, white, flame-like inflorescences that bloom in late summer. The plant has feathery, silver-green leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Phylica alba is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
