Pyrus pyraster f. cardiaca

Pyrus pyraster f. cardiaca, commonly known as the heart-shaped pear, is a cultivar of the common pear tree.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves, which give it its botanical name. The plant is a deciduous tree that produces small, sweet, and edible fruits. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for both its ornamental qualities and its edible yield.
