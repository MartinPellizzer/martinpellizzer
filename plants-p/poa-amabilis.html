Poa amabilis

Poa amabilis, commonly known as the beautiful bluegrass, is a species of grass native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences with tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Poa amabilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
