Pelargonium cardiophyllum

Pelargonium cardiophyllum, commonly known as the heart-leaf geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom in clusters. It is a perennial shrub that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a garden plant.
