Polyscias royenii

Polyscias royenii, commonly known as the Royal Polyscias, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features compound leaves with elongated, serrated leaflets that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by dark blue, spherical berries. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
