Pseuderanthemum parishii

Pseuderanthemum parishii, commonly known as the pink pseuderanthemum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.
