Plicouratea vernicosa

Plicouratea vernicosa, commonly known as the glossy leaf vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that give it a glossy appearance, and its small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments and is often found in rainforests. Its stems are flexible and can climb using tendrils, allowing it to reach sunlight.

Plicouratea vernicosa is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
