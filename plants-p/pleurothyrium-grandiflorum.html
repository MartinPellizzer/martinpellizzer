Pleurothyrium grandiflorum

Pleurothyrium grandiflorum, commonly known as the large-flowered pleurothyrium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in humid, lowland forest environments. The plant is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain several seeds.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
