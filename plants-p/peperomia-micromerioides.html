Peperomia micromerioides

Peperomia micromerioides, commonly known as the dwarf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It features small, ovate to elliptical leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces tiny, reddish-brown spore-bearing structures called sporangia, which are clustered in dense, spike-like formations. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
