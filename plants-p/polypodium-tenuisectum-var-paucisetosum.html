Polypodium tenuisectum var. paucisetosum

Polypodium tenuisectum var. paucisetosum, commonly known as the slender-leaf wall fern, is a variety of polypodioid fern characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices, where it can absorb moisture from the air. The plant's rhizome is creeping, allowing it to spread gradually and form dense mats. Its fronds are usually lance-shaped with a smooth or slightly serrated margin.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with high humidity and partial shade.
