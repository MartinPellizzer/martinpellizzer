Piper recreoense

Piper recreoense, commonly known as the aromatic pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the rainforests of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its vine-like growth habit and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically greenish-white and clustered in spikes, while the fruit is a small, elongated berry.

Piper recreoense is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
