Pelargonium × princeanum

Pelargonium × princeanum, commonly known as the Royal Pelargonium, is a hybrid geranium species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a distinctive dark center. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, featuring rounded leaves with a serrated edge. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Pelargonium × princeanum is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
