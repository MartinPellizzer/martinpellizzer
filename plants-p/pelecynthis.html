Pelecynthis

Pelecynthis, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Pelecynthis is also valued for its fast growth and adaptability to urban environments.
