Palmorchis duckei

Palmorchis duckei, commonly known as the Ducke's palm orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its striking, palm-like leaves and delicate, greenish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil. Its conservation status is vulnerable due to habitat loss and limited distribution.

This orchid is of particular interest to botanists due to its distinctive morphology and ecological significance.
