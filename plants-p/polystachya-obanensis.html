Polystachya obanensis

Polystachya obanensis, commonly known as the Oban orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Scottish Highlands.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in damp, shaded habitats such as mossy woodlands and rocky outcrops. It is a perennial herb that thrives in cool, humid conditions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
