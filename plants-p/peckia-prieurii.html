Peckia prieurii

Peckia prieurii, commonly known as the Prieur's peckia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant produces small, yellow to orange disk flowers surrounded by white to pink ray florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Peckia prieurii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
