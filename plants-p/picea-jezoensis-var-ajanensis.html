Picea jezoensis var. ajanensis

Picea jezoensis var. ajanensis, commonly known as the Ajanensis spruce, is a variant of the Japanese white pine.

It is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the Korean Peninsula and parts of northeastern China. The plant has needle-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged spirally along the branches. It produces cone-shaped seed-bearing structures that are characteristic of the Pinaceae family.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
