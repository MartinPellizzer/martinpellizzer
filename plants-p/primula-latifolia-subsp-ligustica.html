Primula latifolia subsp. ligustica

Primula latifolia subsp.

ligustica, commonly known as the Ligustic Primrose, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink.

The plant typically grows in moist, woodland environments and thrives in partial to full shade. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slightly larger leaves and more robust growth compared to the typical form of *Primula latifolia*.
