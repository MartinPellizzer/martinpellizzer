Peperomia baueriana var. brisbaniana

Peperomia baueriana var. brisbaniana, commonly known as the Brisbane peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Australia.

It features thick, fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and red, which are borne on short, erect spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and attractive foliage, it is often used as a decorative plant in shaded areas.
