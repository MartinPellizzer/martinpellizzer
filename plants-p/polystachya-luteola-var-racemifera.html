Polystachya luteola var. racemifera

Polystachya luteola var. racemifera, commonly known as the yellow-stemmed sword fern, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, wiry stems and lanceolate, glossy green leaves. The plant produces clusters of yellowish-green to reddish-brown flowers that bloom in raceme-like inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
