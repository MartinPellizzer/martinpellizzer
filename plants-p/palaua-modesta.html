Palaua modesta

Palaua modesta, commonly known as the modest palaua, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Araliaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
