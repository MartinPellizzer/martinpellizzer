Primula pekinensis

Primula pekinensis, commonly known as the Peking primrose, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its clustered, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments, often in forested areas. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in a fine, silky hair.

Primula pekinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
