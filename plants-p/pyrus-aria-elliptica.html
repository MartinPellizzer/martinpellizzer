Pyrus aria elliptica

Pyrus aria elliptica, commonly known as the elliptical pear.

This species is a deciduous tree native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It features elliptical, dark green leaves that are often clustered at the base of the stems. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, followed by edible, sweet fruits.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to various soil types.
