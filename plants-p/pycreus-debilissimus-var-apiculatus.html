Pycreus debilissimus var. apiculatus

Pycreus debilissimus var. apiculatus, commonly known as the slender pycreus, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant prefers shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories. Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and limited distribution.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
