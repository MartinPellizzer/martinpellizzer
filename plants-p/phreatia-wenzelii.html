Phreatia wenzelii

Phreatia wenzelii, commonly known as the Wenzel's orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments within forest understories. The plant features a pseudobulb that supports its growth and stores water, which is essential for its survival in humid conditions. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with intricate patterns, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Phreatia wenzelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts interested in rare orchid species.
