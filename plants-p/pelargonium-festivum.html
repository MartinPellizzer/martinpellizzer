Pelargonium festivum

Pelargonium festivum, commonly known as the festive geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, producing upright stems with serrated, lobed leaves. Its flowers are borne in clusters and are often used in ornamental gardening due to their striking appearance.

Pelargonium festivum is also valued for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
