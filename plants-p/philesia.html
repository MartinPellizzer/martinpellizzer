Philesia

Philesia, commonly known as the love vine, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apocynaceae.

It is characterized by its woody stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth, glossy surface. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, often with a subtle fragrance. Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically significant in its native habitats.
