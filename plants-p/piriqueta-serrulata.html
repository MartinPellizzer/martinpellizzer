Piriqueta serrulata

Piriqueta serrulata, commonly known as the serrated piriqueta, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in rocky or dry soils, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are narrow and serrated, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Piriqueta serrulata is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
