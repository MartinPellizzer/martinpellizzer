Polygonum strictum

Polygonum strictum, commonly known as narrowleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous annual plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist soils along stream banks, wetlands, and disturbed areas. The plant features upright, slender stems that are often branched and covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly toothed margin, and it produces small, clustered flowers that range from pink to red in color.

Polygonum strictum is often found in wet or semi-wet environments and is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
