Peperomia gibbsiae

Peperomia gibbsiae, commonly known as the Gibbs' peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically borne in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia gibbsiae is prized for its ornamental foliage and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
