Phelipanche tenuiflora

Phelipanche tenuiflora, commonly known as the slenderflower morning glory, is an annual herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple.
