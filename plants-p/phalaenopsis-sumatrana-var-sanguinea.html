Phalaenopsis sumatrana var. sanguinea

Phalaenopsis sumatrana var. sanguinea, commonly known as the Sumatran red phalaenopsis, is a hybrid orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces showy, pendulous flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often with a velvety appearance. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
