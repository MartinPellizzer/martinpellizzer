Pinus armandi var. farjonii

Pinus armandi var. farjonii, commonly known as the Farjon's Chinese pine, is a rare and unique variant of the Chinese pine species.

It is characterized by its distinctive needle-like leaves and conical shape, typical of many pine species. This plant is native to specific regions in China, where it grows in mountainous areas with well-drained soil. Its cones are usually small and woody, containing seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
