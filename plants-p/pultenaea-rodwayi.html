Pultenaea rodwayi

Pultenaea rodwayi, commonly known as the Narrow-leaved Bush Pea, is a small shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Pultenaea rodwayi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
