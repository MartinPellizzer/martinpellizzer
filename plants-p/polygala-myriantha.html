Polygala myriantha

Polygala myriantha, commonly known as the many-flowered milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, delicate flowers. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are often arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Polygala myriantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
