Peperomia boldinghii

Peperomia boldinghii, commonly known as the Boldinghii peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish or purple tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and drought tolerance.

Peperomia boldinghii thrives in well-draining soil and prefers moderate to high humidity levels.
