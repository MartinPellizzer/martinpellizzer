Plectrachne danthonioides

Plectrachne danthonioides, commonly known as coastal panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Australia.

It is a grass species that typically grows in sandy or saline soils, often found in dune environments. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is well adapted to harsh coastal conditions, including salt spray and strong winds.

Its ecological importance includes stabilizing dunes and providing habitat for coastal wildlife.
