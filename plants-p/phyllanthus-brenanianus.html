Phyllanthus brenanianus

Phyllanthus brenanianus, commonly known as the Indian cheesewood, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and rough, bark-like leaves.
