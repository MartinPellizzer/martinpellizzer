Pennisetum ciliare var. setigerum

Pennisetum ciliare var. setigerum, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender stalks that produce fluffy, purple-tinted plumes, which add a dramatic visual element to gardens. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green hue and a distinctive silver-white underside. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it both aesthetically pleasing and ecologically beneficial, attracting pollinators and providing habitat for small wildlife.
