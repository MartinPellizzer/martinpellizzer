Pavonia friisii

Pavonia friisii, commonly known as the red flame bush, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, upright growth. The plant is characterized by its showy, red-orange bracts that resemble flowers, though they are actually modified leaves. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Pavonia friisii thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
