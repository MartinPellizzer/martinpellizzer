Paphiopedilum xichouense

Paphiopedilum xichouense, commonly known as the Xichou butterfly orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its unique, butterfly-like flowers with a prominent lip and intricate petal structure. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often on rocky substrates. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, and it produces a single spike of flowers during the spring or early summer.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
