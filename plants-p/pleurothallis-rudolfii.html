Pleurothallis rudolfii

Pleurothallis rudolfii, commonly known as the Rudolf's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean region of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often with intricate patterns on the petals. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to conserve moisture in its natural habitat.

Pleurothallis rudolfii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
