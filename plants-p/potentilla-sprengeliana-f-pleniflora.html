Potentilla sprengeliana f. pleniflora

Potentilla sprengeliana f. pleniflora, commonly known as the double flowered cinquefoil, is a horticultural form of the species Potentilla sprengeliana.

It is characterized by its dense, double flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, adding a decorative element to gardens. The plant is a shrub with upright growth, featuring small, serrated leaves that are typically green. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value lies in its showy blooms, which appear in late spring to early summer.
