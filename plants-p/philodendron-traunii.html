Philodendron traunii

Philodendron traunii, commonly known as the Traunii Philodendron, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil.

Philodendron traunii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage.
