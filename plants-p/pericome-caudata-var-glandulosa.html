Pericome caudata var. glandulosa

Pericome caudata var. glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular pericome, is a species of flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, glandular-structured leaves that give it its specific variety name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
