Panicularia australasica

Panicularia australasica, commonly known as the Australian panicled grass, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, paniculate inflorescences that are often composed of numerous tiny florets. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is commonly found in coastal and inland regions.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
