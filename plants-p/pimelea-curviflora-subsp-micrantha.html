Pimelea curviflora subsp. micrantha

Pimelea curviflora subsp.

micrantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved panicled geebung, is a small shrub native to southeastern Australia. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant is typically found in heathlands and rocky soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. It has a compact growth habit and is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value.

The subspecies is distinguished by its smaller flowers and more compact form compared to the main species.
