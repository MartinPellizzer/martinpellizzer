Pelargonium × glabrescens

Pelargonium × glabrescens, commonly known as the zonal geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, five-petaled flowers in a range of colors, including red, pink, and white. The leaves are typically rounded with distinctive color zones, giving the plant its common name. It is a popular ornamental plant cultivated for its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.

Native to South Africa, it thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.
