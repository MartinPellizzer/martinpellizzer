Phillyrea angustifolia subsp. marotii

Phillyrea angustifolia subsp.

marotii, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Phillyrea, is a shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region. It features narrow, leathery leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers followed by small, woody fruits. It is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous areas. Phillyrea angustifolia subsp.

marotii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
