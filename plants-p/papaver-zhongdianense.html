Papaver zhongdianense

Papaver zhongdianense, commonly known as the Zhongdian poppy, is a flowering plant native to the Yunnan Province in China.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to orange, with a distinctive, saucer-shaped structure. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, brown seeds. It thrives in high-altitude environments, often found in meadows and rocky areas.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical studies.
