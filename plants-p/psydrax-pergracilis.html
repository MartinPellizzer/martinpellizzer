Psydrax pergracilis

Psydrax pergracilis, commonly known as the slender psychotria, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by red, fleshy berries. It is often found in shaded, moist habitats such as forests and undergrowth.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is occasionally cultivated in horticultural settings.
