Pityrodia exserta

Pityrodia exserta, commonly known as the slender pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Pittosporaceae and is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal and lowland areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its fruit is a small, fleshy capsule that contains several seeds.

Pityrodia exserta is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
