Primulina subulatisepala

Primulina subulatisepala, commonly known as the narrow-lobed gloxinia, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage and showy, tubular flowers. Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
