Philodendron peperomioides

Philodendron peperomioides, commonly known as the marble queen philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves that often feature a striking variegation of light green and cream. The plant has a vining growth habit and can be trained to climb supports or allowed to trail from hanging baskets. Its foliage is evergreen, providing year-round visual interest in indoor environments.

It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
