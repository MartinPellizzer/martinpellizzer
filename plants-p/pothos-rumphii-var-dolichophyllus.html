Pothos rumphii var. dolichophyllus

Pothos rumphii var. dolichophyllus, commonly known as the long-leaf pothos, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length, giving it a distinctive, lush appearance. The plant thrives in humid environments and prefers indirect sunlight, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its aerial roots allow it to climb and attach to surfaces, contributing to its vine-like growth habit.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and ease of care.
