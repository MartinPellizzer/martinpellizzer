Peperomia macedoana

Peperomia macedoana, commonly known as the Macedonian pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features dense, glossy green leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape, often with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically greenish-white in color, which are followed by small, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia macedoana is relatively low-maintenance and suitable for beginners due to its tolerance for moderate watering and indoor conditions.
