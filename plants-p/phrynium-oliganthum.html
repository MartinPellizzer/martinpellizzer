Phrynium oliganthum

Phrynium oliganthum, commonly known as the arrow leaf plant, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in spikes. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its foliage is used in traditional medicine and as a decorative plant in ornamental gardens.
