Parthenium confertum var. intermedium

Parthenium confertum var. intermedium, commonly known as the narrowleaf ironweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and feathery leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. It produces small, yellowish to brownish flowers that bloom in late summer to early fall.

The species is known for its ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient component of native plant communities.
