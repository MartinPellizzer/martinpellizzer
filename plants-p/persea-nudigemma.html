Persea nudigemma

Persea nudigemma, commonly known as the smooth-leaved persimmon, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are smooth and leathery in texture. The tree produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by oval-shaped fruits with a sweet, edible pulp. Its fruit is often used in local cuisines and traditional medicine.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
