Podocarpus subtropicalis subsp. medogensis

Podocarpus subtropicalis subsp.

medogensis, commonly known as the Yunnan podocarp, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the subtropical regions of China. It belongs to the family Podocarpaceae and is characterized by its dense, needle-like foliage and small, cone-like fruits.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in mountainous areas with high humidity. It has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 20 meters, making it a valuable species for reforestation and ornamental purposes.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the subtropical forest ecosystems in its native habitat.
