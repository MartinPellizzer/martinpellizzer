Pleurothallis hians

Pleurothallis hians, commonly known as the silver orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on tree branches or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers with intricate patterns. It thrives in high humidity and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its beauty and rarity, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
