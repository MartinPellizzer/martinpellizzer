Phalaenopsis lindenii

Phalaenopsis lindenii, commonly known as the White Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender pseudobulbs and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of white, fragrant flowers that bloom repeatedly throughout the year. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining potting mix.

Phalaenopsis lindenii is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of care.
