Phalaenopsis pallens var. trullifera

Phalaenopsis pallens var. trullifera, commonly known as the Trullifera Phalaenopsis, is a hybrid orchid originating from the Philippines.

It is characterized by its long, slender leaves and clusters of white to pale pink flowers with a distinctive, almost translucent appearance. The plant is an epiphyte, typically growing on trees in tropical rainforests, and it thrives in high humidity and indirect light. It produces flowers throughout the year under proper care, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique bloom structure and elegant foliage make it a prized ornamental plant in horticulture.
