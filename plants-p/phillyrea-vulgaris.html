Phillyrea vulgaris

Phillyrea vulgaris, commonly known as the common Phillyrea, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by spherical, woody fruits. Phillyrea vulgaris is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
