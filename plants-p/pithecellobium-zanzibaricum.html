Pithecellobium zanzibaricum

Pithecellobium zanzibaricum, commonly known as the Zanzibar pisonia, is a tropical shrub native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are often consumed by local communities.

Its growth habit and ornamental features make it a valued species in horticulture and ecological restoration.
