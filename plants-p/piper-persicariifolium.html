Piper persicariifolium

Piper persicariifolium, commonly known as the purpleleaf pepper plant, is a tropical species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep purple or reddish hue. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into elongated, cylindrical fruits. It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant.
