Plectronia carinata

Plectronia carinata, commonly known as the mountain holly or holly fern, is a species of flowering plant in the family Polygonaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Plectronia carinata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage.
