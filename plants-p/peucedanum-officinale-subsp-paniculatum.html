Peucedanum officinale subsp. paniculatum

Peucedanum officinale subsp.

paniculatum, commonly known as greater cow-weed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It features slender, branched stems that grow up to 1 meter in height, often with a hairy texture.

The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in compound umbels, typical of the Apiaceae family. Its leaves are finely divided and have a mild, aromatic scent.
