Phragmipedium dariense

Phragmipedium dariense, commonly known as the Darien phragmipedium, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and green. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that help store water and nutrients. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil for optimal growth.

Phragmipedium dariense is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
