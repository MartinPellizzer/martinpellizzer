Paxiuscula rosularis

Paxiuscula rosularis, commonly known as the Rosular rose.

This plant is a flowering species characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, rose-like flowers. It typically features dense clusters of small, fragrant blooms that appear in late spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its botanical name reflects its resemblance to roses and its small, clustered growth form.
