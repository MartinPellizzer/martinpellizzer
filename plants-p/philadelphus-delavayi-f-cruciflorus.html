Philadelphus delavayi f. cruciflorus

Philadelphus delavayi f. cruciflorus, commonly known as the Chinese mock orange, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its showy blooms and pleasant fragrance.
