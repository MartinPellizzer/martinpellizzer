Pteris grevilleana f. ornata

Pteris grevilleana f. ornata, commonly known as the ornate water fern, is a fern species characterized by its delicate, intricate fronds.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features a short, creeping rhizome with bright green, bipinnate fronds that are often variegated with white or silver markings. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near water sources.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant collections.
