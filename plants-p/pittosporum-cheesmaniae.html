Pittosporum cheesmaniae

Pittosporum cheesmaniae, commonly known as the Cheesman's pittosporum, is a flowering shrub native to New Zealand.

It features fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that appear in late summer to early autumn. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a waxy texture. It is typically found in coastal areas and can thrive in a variety of soil types.

Pittosporum cheesmaniae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscapes.
