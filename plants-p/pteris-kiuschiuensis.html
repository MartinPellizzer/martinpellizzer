Pteris kiuschiuensis

Pteris kiuschiuensis, commonly known as the Korean water fern, is a species of fern native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are creeping and often covered in a layer of fine, scale-like structures.

Pteris kiuschiuensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
