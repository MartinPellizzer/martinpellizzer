Pelargonium gratum

Pelargonium gratum, commonly known as the angel's tears geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features upright stems and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The leaves are typically rounded and have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-draining soil.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and containers.
