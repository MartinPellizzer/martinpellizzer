Polygala misella

Polygala misella, commonly known as the narrow-leaved milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and opposite, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds.

It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions where it thrives in well-drained conditions.
