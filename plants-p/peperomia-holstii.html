Peperomia holstii

Peperomia holstii, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to South America.

It features dense, velvety foliage with a rich green hue and glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
