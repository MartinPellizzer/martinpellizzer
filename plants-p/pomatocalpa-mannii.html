Pomatocalpa mannii

Pomatocalpa mannii, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features a short, creeping rhizome and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it blooms during the rainy season.

Pomatocalpa mannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
