Pinanga vonmohlii

It is characterized by its dense, clumping growth habit and slender, hollow stems that resemble bamboo. Pinanga vonmohlii thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in coastal forests or on rocky slopes.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
