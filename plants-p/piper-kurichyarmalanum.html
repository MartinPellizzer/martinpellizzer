Piper kurichyarmalanum

Piper kurichyarmalanum, commonly known as the Kurichyarmalanum pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the Western Ghats of India, where it thrives in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruits are typically small berries that mature to a bright red color.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and fruiting characteristics.
