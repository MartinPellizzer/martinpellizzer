Peperomia pichinchae

Peperomia pichinchae, commonly known as the Pichincha peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to the Andes Mountains of Ecuador.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for collectors and houseplant enthusiasts.
