Pancratium australasicum

Pancratium australasicum, commonly known as the Australian yellow lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa and Australia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers, which bloom in spring and summer. The plant has strap-like leaves that are narrow and grass-like in appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Pancratium australasicum is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
