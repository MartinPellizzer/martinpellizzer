Polypodium wilsonii

Polypodium wilsonii, commonly known as Wilson's huperzia, is a species of fern that belongs to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its evergreen, leathery fronds that are typically dark green and have a distinctive, intricate pattern of veins. Polypodium wilsonii is often used in traditional medicine and is also valued as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance.
