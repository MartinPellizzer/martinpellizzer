Phalaenopsis denevei var. alba

Phalaenopsis denevei var. alba, commonly known as the White Phalaenopsis, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, long-lasting white flowers that bloom in a spike with multiple buds. The plant has broad, dark green leaves that are arranged in a fan shape, providing a lush backdrop for its floral display. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining potting mix.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
