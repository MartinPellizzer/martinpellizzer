Pittosporum truncatum var. tsaii

Pittosporum truncatum var. tsaii, commonly known as the Chinese pittosporum, is a shrub native to China.

It features dark green, glossy leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces fragrant white flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The varietal form, tsaii, is distinguished by its specific leaf and flower characteristics.
