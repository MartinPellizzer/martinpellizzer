Pedicularis elephas

Pedicularis elephas, commonly known as the giant monkey orchid, is a striking member of the Orobanchaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe, particularly in areas with high altitudes and cool climates. Due to its rarity and specific environmental requirements, it is considered a delicate and protected species in many regions.
