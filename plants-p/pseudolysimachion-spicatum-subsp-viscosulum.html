Pseudolysimachion spicatum subsp. viscosulum

Pseudolysimachion spicatum subsp.

viscosulum, commonly known as the sticky false lousewort, is a member of the Orobanchaceae family. It is a hemiparasitic plant that attaches to the roots of other plants to obtain some of its nutrients.
