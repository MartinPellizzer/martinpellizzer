Poa levipes

Poa levipes, commonly known as the slender meadowgrass, is a species of grass native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is a perennial herb with slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, wetlands, and along stream banks.

Poa levipes plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
