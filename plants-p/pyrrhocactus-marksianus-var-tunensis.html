Pyrrhocactus marksianus var. tunensis

Pyrrhocactus marksianus var. tunensis, commonly known as the Tunisian pyrrhocactus, is a succulent plant native to North Africa.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stems and spiny, leaf-like structures. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in arid climates.
