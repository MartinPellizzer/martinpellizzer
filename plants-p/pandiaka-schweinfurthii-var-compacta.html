Pandiaka schweinfurthii var. compacta

Pandiaka schweinfurthii var. compacta, commonly known as the compact pandiaka, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It features small, dark green, succulent leaves that are arranged in a dense, bushy form, which helps it conserve water in its native habitat. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, typically blooming in spring or early summer. It is well adapted to full sun and well-draining soil, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.
