Phylica villosa var. pedicellata

Phylica villosa var. pedicellata, commonly known as the hairy phylica, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces woody, cone-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting local wildlife.
