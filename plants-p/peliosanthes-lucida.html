Peliosanthes lucida

Peliosanthes lucida, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a visually stunning display. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peliosanthes lucida is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and public landscapes.
