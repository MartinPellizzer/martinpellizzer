Polygala sphenoptera var. minor

Polygala sphenoptera var. minor, commonly known as the lesser sandmat, is a flowering plant belonging to the Polygalaceae family.

It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant is characterized by its delicate, branched stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open, dry habitats.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
