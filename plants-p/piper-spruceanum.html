Piper spruceanum

Piper spruceanum, commonly known as the red spruce or spruce pepper, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper-like plants. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and has a woody stem with a rough, bark-like texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
