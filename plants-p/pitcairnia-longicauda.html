Pitcairnia longicauda

Pitcairnia longicauda, commonly known as the long-tailed pitcairnia, is a striking bromeliad native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its long, narrow, grass-like leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces vibrant, showy inflorescences with brightly colored flowers, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and indoor collections.
