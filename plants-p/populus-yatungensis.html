Populus yatungensis

Populus yatungensis, commonly known as the Yatung poplar, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Salicaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and distinctive bark. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

This species is often used in reforestation projects due to its ecological benefits and hardiness.
