Phalaenopsis lueddemanniana

Phalaenopsis lueddemanniana, commonly known as the Lueddemann's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves and its ability to produce multiple flower spikes with showy, fragrant blooms. The flowers are typically white or pale pink and have a velvety texture. This epiphytic plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining potting media.

It is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
