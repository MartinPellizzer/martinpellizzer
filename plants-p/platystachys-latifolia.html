Platystachys latifolia

Platystachys latifolia, commonly known as Chinese cliffbrake, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and long-lasting, delicate flowers.
