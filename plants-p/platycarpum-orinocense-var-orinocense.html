Platycarpum orinocense var. orinocense

Platycarpum orinocense var. orinocense, commonly known as the Orinoco flatfruit, is a species native to South America, particularly Venezuela and Colombia.

It is a small evergreen tree characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces oval, fleshy fruits that are typically yellow or orange when mature. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
