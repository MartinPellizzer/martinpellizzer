Pandanus solms-laubachii

Pandanus solms-laubachii, commonly known as the Solomon Islands pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pandanaceae.

It is native to the islands of the Solomon Islands and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences at the top of the plant. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found growing in coastal areas or near freshwater sources.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has cultural significance in the regions where it is native.
