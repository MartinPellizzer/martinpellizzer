Pilea argentea

Pilea argentea, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features round, fleshy leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a silvery underside, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its tolerance for occasional neglect.

Its unique leaf shape and easy care make it a popular choice for indoor gardens and offices.
