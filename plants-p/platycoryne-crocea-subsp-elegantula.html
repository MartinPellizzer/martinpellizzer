Platycoryne crocea subsp. elegantula

Platycoryne crocea subsp.

elegantula, commonly known as the elegant yellow orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its delicate, yellowish-green flowers with a distinctive, elongated labellum.

The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a conservation concern in many regions.
