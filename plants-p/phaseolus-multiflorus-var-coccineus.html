Phaseolus multiflorus var. coccineus

Phaseolus multiflorus var. coccineus, commonly known as the scarlet runner bean, is a tropical legume native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its vigorous climbing habit and large, showy red or orange flowers. The plant produces elongated, edible pods that contain kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its culinary uses.
