Polyscias aherniana

Polyscias aherniana, commonly known as the Ahern's gloryberry, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped, glossy dark green leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by edible, orange-red berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in some horticultural gardens.
