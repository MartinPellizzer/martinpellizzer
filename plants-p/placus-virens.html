Placus virens

Placus virens, commonly known as the green sprout, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, which includes many economically important crops. The plant is characterized by its slender stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in early spring.
