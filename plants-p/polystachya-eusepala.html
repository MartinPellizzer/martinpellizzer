Polystachya eusepala

Polystachya eusepala, commonly known as the elegant spike orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of delicate, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and lance-shaped, providing a striking contrast to its floral display.

Polystachya eusepala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
