Potamogeton × nitens f. vadosus

Potamogeton × nitens f. vadosus, commonly known as the aquatic plant with submerged leaves, is a hybrid species within the Potamogeton genus.

It is characterized by its finely dissected, grass-like leaves that are typically found in freshwater environments. This plant thrives in shallow, slow-moving waters and is often found in ponds, lakes, and wetlands. Its reproductive structures include small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually submerged or floating.
