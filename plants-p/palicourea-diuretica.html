Palicourea diuretica

Palicourea diuretica, commonly known as Brazilian nightshade, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers and elliptical leaves. The plant contains various alkaloids, which contribute to its medicinal properties. It is often used in traditional medicine for its diuretic and anti-inflammatory effects.
