Polycycnis trullifera

Polycycnis trullifera, commonly known as the umbrella tortoise plant, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of fleshy, spiky leaves that form a compact, rounded shape resembling a tortoise. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its distinctive appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for succulent gardens and rockeries.
