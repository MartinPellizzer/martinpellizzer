Polyschistis

Polyschistis, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

These plants are characterized by their compact, rosette-like growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers. The leaves are typically velvety and arranged in a dense, upright formation. Polyschistis species are popular houseplants due to their ability to thrive in low-light conditions and their ornamental value.

They produce small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of purple, pink, and white.
