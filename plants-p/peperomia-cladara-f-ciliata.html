Peperomia cladara f. ciliata

Peperomia cladara f. ciliata, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive reddish or purplish hue on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically green or reddish, which are followed by tiny, winged seeds. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and ease of care contribute to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
