Pomaderris obcordata

Pomaderris obcordata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed glory bush, is a small to medium-sized shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It is well-suited for coastal and dry garden environments due to its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
