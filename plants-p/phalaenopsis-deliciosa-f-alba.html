Phalaenopsis deliciosa f. alba

Phalaenopsis deliciosa f. alba, commonly known as the white phalaenopsis orchid, is a hybrid orchid prized for its elegant, white blooms.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia, where it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant features broad, dark green leaves and produces long, drooping inflorescences with multiple flowers. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and ability to bloom repeatedly.

The alba form is distinguished by its pure white petals and lack of coloration, making it a striking ornamental plant.
