Poa nymanii

Poa nymanii, commonly known as the New Zealand hairgrass, is a perennial grass species native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and produces slender, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in temperate climates. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its common name.

Poa nymanii is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and forest margins.
