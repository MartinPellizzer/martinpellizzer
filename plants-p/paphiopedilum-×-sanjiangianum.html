Paphiopedilum × sanjiangianum

Paphiopedilum × sanjiangianum, commonly known as the Sanjiang Orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its unique, intricate flower structure with a specialized lip that aids in pollination. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It features dark green, leathery leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
