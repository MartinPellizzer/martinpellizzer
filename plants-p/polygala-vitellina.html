Polygala vitellina

Polygala vitellina, commonly known as yellow milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, yellowish flowers clustered in dense spikes. The plant produces small, bean-like pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in arid or semi-arid environments and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.

Polygala vitellina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
