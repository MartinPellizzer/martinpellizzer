Pectis peruviana

Pectis peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian pectis, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Peru.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Pectis peruviana is often found in rocky soils and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
