Polyscias paniculata

Polyscias paniculata, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or the umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, glossy, dark green, palmate leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers arranged in panicles, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in humid, warm environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value.

Polyscias paniculata is also valued for its air-purifying properties and adaptability to indoor conditions.
