Polygonum amphibium var. muehlenbergii

Polygonum amphibium var. muehlenbergii, commonly known as Muehlenberg's knotweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features upright, slender stems that are often reddish in color and bear small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It is known for its ability to thrive in wet conditions and is often found in riparian zones.
