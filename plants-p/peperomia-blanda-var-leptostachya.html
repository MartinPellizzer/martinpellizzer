Peperomia blanda var. leptostachya

Peperomia blanda var. leptostachya, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green foliage with a silvery underside, giving it a striking, almost metallic appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pale green flowers that are typically hidden among the leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its slow growth rate and compact form make it ideal for container cultivation and as a decorative houseplant.
