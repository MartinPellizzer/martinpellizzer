Pennisetum intertextum

Pennisetum intertextum, commonly known as blue oat grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive blue-gray foliage and delicate, oat-like seed heads that appear in late summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 4 feet and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish sheen that gives it a unique appearance.

Pennisetum intertextum is often used in ornamental landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and drought tolerance.
