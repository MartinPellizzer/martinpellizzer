Portulaca cayennensis

Portulaca cayennensis, commonly known as the Cayenne portulaca, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a low-growing annual herb that thrives in hot, arid environments. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to red, often blooming throughout the summer months. Its leaves are fleshy and succulent, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

Portulaca cayennensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and resilience in dry conditions.
