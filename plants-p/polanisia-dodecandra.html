Polanisia dodecandra

Polanisia dodecandra, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white to pink petals. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Polanisia dodecandra is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
