Pittosporum pulchrum var. foliosum

Pittosporum pulchrum var. foliosum, commonly known as the silver-leaf pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of silvery-white hair, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring, followed by small, orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique foliage and seasonal blooms make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
