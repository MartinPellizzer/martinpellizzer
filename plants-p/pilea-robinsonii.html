Pilea robinsonii

Pilea robinsonii, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a favored houseplant among plant enthusiasts.
