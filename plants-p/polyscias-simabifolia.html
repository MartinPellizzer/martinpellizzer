Polyscias simabifolia

Polyscias simabifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved polycarp, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, ovate to elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy on the upper surface. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in clusters, followed by orange-red berries that contain the seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its attractive foliage.
