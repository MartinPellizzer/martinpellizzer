Pittosporopsis nervosa

Pittosporopsis nervosa, commonly known as the narrow-veined pittosporus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pittosporaceae.

It is native to New Zealand and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are narrow and have prominent veins, giving it its common name.

It is often found in forest understories and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
