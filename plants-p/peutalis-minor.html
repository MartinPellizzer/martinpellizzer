Peutalis minor

Peutalis minor, commonly known as the lesser peppermint, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically white or pale purple, arranged in whorls along the stem.

Peutalis minor is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic benefits.
