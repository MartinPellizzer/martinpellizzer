Piper tuberculatum var. rigidomembranaceum

Piper tuberculatum var. rigidomembranaceum, commonly known as the rigid membranous pepper plant, is a tropical species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves with a distinct, waxy texture and a slightly serrated margin. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, followed by small, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its foliage has a mild peppery scent, which is more pronounced when the leaves are crushed.
