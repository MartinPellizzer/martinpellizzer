Pilea weberbaueri

Pilea weberbaueri, commonly known as the Weberbauer's nerve plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves with prominent veins that give it its common name. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Its unique foliage and ease of care make it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
