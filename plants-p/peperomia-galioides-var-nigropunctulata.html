Peperomia galioides var. nigropunctulata

Peperomia galioides var. nigropunctulata, commonly known as the black-spotted peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves with a distinctive dark green to almost black coloration, often adorned with small, dark spots. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia galioides var. nigropunctulata is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
