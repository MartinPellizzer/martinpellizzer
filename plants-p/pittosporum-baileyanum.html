Pittosporum baileyanum

Pittosporum baileyanum, commonly known as the Baileya pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features elliptical, dark green leaves with a glossy surface and a distinctive, fragrant flower that resembles a cluster of small white blossoms. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that are typically red or orange when mature. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Pittosporum baileyanum is also valued for its ability to provide habitat for native wildlife.
