Parodia krasuckana

Parodia krasuckana, commonly known as the Krasucka Parodia, is a species of cactus native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense covering of white to pale green spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for succulent collectors and desert gardens.
