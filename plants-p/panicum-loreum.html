Panicum loreum

Panicum loreum, commonly known as purpletop panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to the United States.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and distinctive purple-tinted foliage. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in prairies and open woodlands.

Panicum loreum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local wildlife.
