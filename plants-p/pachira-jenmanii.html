Pachira jenmanii

Pachira jenmanii, commonly known as the Jenman's pachira, is a species of flowering plant in the Araceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features large, waxy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. It produces clusters of small, white flowers followed by bright red berries.

Pachira jenmanii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
