Pittosporum javanicum

Pittosporum javanicum, commonly known as the Java Pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage, which consists of dark green, leathery leaves with a glossy texture. The plant produces fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in terminal umbels. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Pittosporum javanicum is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of essential oils.
