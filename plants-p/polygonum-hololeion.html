Polygonum hololeion

Polygonum hololeion, commonly known as the red root sedges, is a perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants commonly referred to as buckwheats and sedges. The plant is characterized by its grass-like leaves and distinctive red-tinged roots, which are often used in traditional herbal medicine.
