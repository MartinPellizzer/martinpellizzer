Protea platysperma

Protea platysperma, commonly known as the flat-seeded protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence composed of numerous white to pinkish flowers arranged in a spherical or cylindrical shape. The plant is native to the Western Cape region and thrives in well-drained, acidic soils. Its unique floral structure attracts various pollinators, including birds and insects.

Protea platysperma is also valued for its ornamental use in horticulture due to its long-lasting blooms and aesthetic appeal.
