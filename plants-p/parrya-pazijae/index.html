Parrya Pazijae

Parrya pazijae is a rare and enigmatic plant species discovered in the high-altitude regions of northern Chile. It belongs to the Apiaceae family, known for its aromatic and often edible members. This plant is notable for its unique morphology and limited geographic distribution. Due to its elusive nature, very little is known about its ecology and conservation status. Its discovery has sparked interest among botanists and conservationists alike.
