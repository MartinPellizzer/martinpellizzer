Plectranthus adenanthus

Plectranthus adenanthus, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
