Peperomia bistortifolia

Peperomia bistortifolia, commonly known as the zigzag peperomia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and distinctive, waxy, ovate leaves that often have a zigzag or undulating edge. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in terminal clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia bistortifolia is relatively low maintenance and resistant to pests, contributing to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
