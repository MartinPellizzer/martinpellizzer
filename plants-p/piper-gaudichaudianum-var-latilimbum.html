Piper gaudichaudianum var. latilimbum

Piper gaudichaudianum var. latilimbum, commonly known as the broad-leaved pepper plant, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature. It is cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of spices.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural and culinary purposes.
