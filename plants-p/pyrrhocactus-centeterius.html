Pyrrhocactus centeterius

Pyrrhocactus centeterius, commonly known as the red-horned cactus, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stems that are often covered in red or orange spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its spiny structure. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for desert environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and cactus enthusiasts.
