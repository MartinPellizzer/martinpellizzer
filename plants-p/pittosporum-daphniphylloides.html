Pittosporum daphniphylloides

Pittosporum daphniphylloides, commonly known as the silver-leaved pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that have a silvery underside, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Pittosporum daphniphylloides is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
