Peritassa flaviflora

Peritassa flaviflora, commonly known as the yellow-flowered peritassa, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has slender, woody stems and heart-shaped leaves with a smooth, glossy surface. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Peritassa flaviflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
