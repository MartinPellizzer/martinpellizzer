Pasania craterophora

Pasania craterophora, commonly known as the mountain hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a yellow center. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough, hairy texture.
