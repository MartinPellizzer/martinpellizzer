Proustia pungens var. oblongifolia

Proustia pungens var. oblongifolia, commonly known as the spiny pulteneyaea, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces striking yellow flowers in summer, which are borne on tall, branched stems. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
