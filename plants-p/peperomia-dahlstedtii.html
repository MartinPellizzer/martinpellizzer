Peperomia dahlstedtii

Peperomia dahlstedtii, commonly known as the Silver Mound Peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a distinctive silvery-white underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate moisture.

Peperomia dahlstedtii is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
