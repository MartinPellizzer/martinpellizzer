Pleurothallis markii

Pleurothallis markii, commonly known as the Mark's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structures. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
