Psychotria membranifolia

Psychotria membranifolia, commonly known as the "Bladderleaf Psychotria," is a member of the Rubiaceae family, native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height, featuring opposite, elliptical leaves with a leathery texture. Psychotria membranifolia is known for its chemical complexity, containing various alkaloids, some of which have been studied for their potential pharmacological properties.
