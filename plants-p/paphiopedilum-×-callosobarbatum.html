Paphiopedilum × callosobarbatum

Paphiopedilum × callosobarbatum, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Paphiopedilum barbatum and Paphiopedilum callosum.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its sympodial growth habit and fleshy roots. The plant produces showy, butterfly-shaped flowers in shades of pink, white, and yellow, often with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
