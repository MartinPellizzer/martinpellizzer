Pilea rufa

Pilea rufa, commonly known as the red-veined begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves with distinctive red veins. The plant produces small, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in clusters. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Pilea rufa is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
