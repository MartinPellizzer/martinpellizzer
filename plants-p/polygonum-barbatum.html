Polygonum barbatum

Polygonum barbatum, commonly known as Chinese knotweed, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its upright stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that appear in dense clusters during the summer months. It is often found in moist environments such as stream banks and wet meadows.

Polygonum barbatum is known for its rapid growth and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for various health purposes.
