Parodia microsperma subsp. mesembrina

Parodia microsperma subsp.

mesembrina, commonly known as the Mesembra Parodia, is a succulent cactus native to southern Africa. It features a globular or cylindrical stem with prominent areoles and spines.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are typically short-lived. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

This subspecies is prized in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
