Polystichum clemensiae

Polystichum clemensiae, commonly known as the Clemens' wood fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the temperate regions of the northern hemisphere.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to form dense colonies, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Polystichum clemensiae is valued for its elegant foliage and its ability to thrive in shaded environments with minimal maintenance.
