Pennilabium luzonense

Pennilabium luzonense, commonly known as the Luzon orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are elongated and lance-shaped, often covered in a fine layer of hair.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.
