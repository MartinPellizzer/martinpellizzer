Pelargonium magnificiens

Pelargonium magnificiens, commonly known as the magnificent geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a range of colors including red, pink, and white. The plant features upright stems and heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Pelargonium magnificiens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
