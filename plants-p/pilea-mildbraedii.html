Pilea mildbraedii

Pilea mildbraedii, commonly known as the African violet, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features a rosette of velvety, dark green leaves with a slightly fuzzy texture. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, typically in spring and summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea mildbraedii is relatively low-maintenance, requiring moderate watering and regular fertilization during its growing season.
