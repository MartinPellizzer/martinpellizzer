Pinarophyllon flavum

Pinarophyllon flavum, commonly known as the yellow-flowered pineleaf, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and adaptability to harsh environments.
