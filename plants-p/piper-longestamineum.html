Piper longestamineum

Piper longestamineum, commonly known as the longamineum pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. Its small, clustered flowers are typically green or yellow, and it produces small, peppery fruits.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in warm climates.
