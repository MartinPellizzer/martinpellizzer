Prunus dolichobotrys

Prunus dolichobotrys, commonly known as the Chinese bird cherry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its slender, often multi-stemmed growth habit. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers in early spring, followed by edible, sweet-tart berries. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and ovate with a serrated margin.

Prunus dolichobotrys is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate flowers and attractive fruit.
