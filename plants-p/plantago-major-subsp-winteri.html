Plantago major subsp. winteri

Plantago major subsp.

winteri, commonly known as the common plantain, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It features a rosette of broad, oval-shaped leaves with a waxy surface and prominent veins.

The plant produces small, greenish flowers in dense spikes during the summer months. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in lawns, roadsides, and wastelands.

Its extensive root system allows it to tolerate various environmental conditions and make it a resilient weed.
