Philodendron daguense

Philodendron daguense, commonly known as the Daguense Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by reddish-brown berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a decorative plant.
