Phillyrea medianifolia subsp. jordanii

Phillyrea medianifolia subsp.

jordanii, commonly known as Jordanian Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green.

The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences, followed by small, woody fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rocky or arid environments.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and aesthetic appeal.
