Peristylus tridentatus

Peristylus tridentatus, commonly known as the three-toothed peristylus, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Peristylus tridentatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
