Pentaraphia reticulata var. obovata

Pentaraphia reticulata var. obovata, commonly known as the reticulated obovate fern, is a species native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, lanceolate leaves that are often adorned with a distinctive reticulate pattern. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests or along stream banks. Its rhizome is thick and fibrous, supporting the growth of its foliage.

The varietal form obovata is distinguished by its obovate leaf shape, which sets it apart from other varieties within the species.
