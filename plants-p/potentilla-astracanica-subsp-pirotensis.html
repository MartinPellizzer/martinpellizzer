Potentilla astracanica subsp. pirotensis

Potentilla astracanica subsp.

pirotensis, commonly known as the Pirot pineapple. This subspecies is a low-growing shrub characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and bright yellow flowers.

It is native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans, particularly in Serbia and Bosnia and Herzegovina. The plant features small, elliptical leaves with a hairy texture and produces fruit that resembles a pineapple, hence its common name.

It is adapted to rocky and dry habitats, showcasing resilience to harsh environmental conditions.
