Philodendron loefgrenii

Philodendron loefgrenii, commonly known as the Loefgren's philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length, with a deep green coloration. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a houseplant.
