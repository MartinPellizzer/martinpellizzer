Peperomia daiquiriana

Peperomia daiquiriana, commonly known as the Pink Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a distinctive pinkish or reddish hue on the underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia daiquiriana is relatively low maintenance and suitable for beginners due to its adaptability and ease of care.
