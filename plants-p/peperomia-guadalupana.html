Peperomia guadalupana

Peperomia guadalupana, commonly known as the Guadalupan pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Mexico.

It features thick, fleshy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white to green flowers, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia guadalupana is relatively low maintenance and drought-tolerant, suitable for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
