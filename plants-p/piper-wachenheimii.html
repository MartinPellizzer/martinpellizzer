Piper wachenheimii

Piper wachenheimii, commonly known as the Wachenheim's pepper, is a climbing shrub native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes numerous species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into elongated, berry-like fruits. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped with a rough texture and a strong, peppery scent.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
