Panicum breviscrobs

Panicum breviscrobs, commonly known as the short-stemmed grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of a panicle made up of small, spikelet-bearing branches.

Panicum breviscrobs is adapted to withstand drought and poor soil conditions, making it a resilient species in its native environment.
