Primulina hochiensis var. ovata

Primulina hochiensis var. ovata, commonly known as the Hochiennis ovate primula, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, bell-shaped flowers that come in a range of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in mountainous regions with well-drained soil.

Its leaves are ovate and glossy, contributing to its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
