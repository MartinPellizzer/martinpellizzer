Prunus armeniaca var. xiongyueensis

Prunus armeniaca var. xiongyueensis, commonly known as the Xiongyue peach, is a variant of the peach tree native to China.

It is characterized by its medium-sized, oval-shaped fruits with a smooth, thin skin and a sweet, juicy flesh. The plant typically grows in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Its flowers are white with a slight pinkish tinge, appearing in early spring before the leaves emerge.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and culinary use in traditional Chinese cuisine.
