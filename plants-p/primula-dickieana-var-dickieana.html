Primula dickieana var. dickieana

Primula dickieana var. dickieana, commonly known as the Dickie's primrose, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-drained, loamy soils. Its leaves are usually broad and heart-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in cool, humid climates.
