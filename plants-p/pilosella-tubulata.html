Pilosella tubulata

Pilosella tubulata, commonly known as the slender mouse-ear, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features small, hairy leaves and produces yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters. It typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring temperate climates.

Pilosella tubulata is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
