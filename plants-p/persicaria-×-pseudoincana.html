Persicaria × pseudoincana

Persicaria × pseudoincana, commonly known as the hybrid smartweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Polygonaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, often with a reddish or purple tinge. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red, arranged in spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface, and often have a reddish midrib.

Persicaria × pseudoincana is native to North America and is often found in wetlands, along stream banks, and in disturbed soils.
