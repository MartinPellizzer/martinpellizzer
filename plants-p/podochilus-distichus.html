Podochilus distichus

Podochilus distichus, commonly known as the two-ranked orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with a short, horizontal rhizome. Its leaves are linear and arranged in two vertical rows, giving it its common name.

The flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and often appear in clusters, contributing to its ornamental value.
