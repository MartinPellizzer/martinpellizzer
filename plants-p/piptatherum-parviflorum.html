Piptatherum parviflorum

Piptatherum parviflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the eastern United States.

Its small, spike-like inflorescence bears tiny, two-lobed flowers that are usually greenish or reddish in color.
