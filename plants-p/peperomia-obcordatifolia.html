Peperomia obcordatifolia

Peperomia obcordatifolia, commonly known as the heart-shaped peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color, emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its low maintenance requirements and attractive foliage make it a favored ornamental plant.
