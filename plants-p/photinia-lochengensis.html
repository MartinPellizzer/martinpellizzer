Photinia lochengensis

Photinia lochengensis, commonly known as the Locheng photinia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to China, specifically the Yunnan Province, and is characterized by its attractive, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by red berries that are an important food source for birds. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and vibrant foliage.

The species is also valued for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
