Pachira barrigon

Pachira barrigon, commonly known as the golden pachira, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking yellow flowers followed by orange berries that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Pachira barrigon is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
