Pandanus otemanuensis

Pandanus otemanuensis, commonly known as the Otemanu pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Pandanus.

It is native to the Pacific islands, particularly parts of French Polynesia. The plant is characterized by its long, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a fan shape. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences.

The species is valued for its ornamental appearance and has cultural significance in some island communities.
