Pilosella × pseudolactucella

Pilosella × pseudolactucella, commonly known as the false buttonweed, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is a hybrid species that occurs naturally in various regions of Europe and parts of Asia. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. It produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense, button-like heads.

Its foliage is usually covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive, fluffy appearance.
