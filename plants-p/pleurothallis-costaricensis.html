Pleurothallis costaricensis

Pleurothallis costaricensis, commonly known as the Costa Rican pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, tropical environments. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It is part of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate floral structure.

Due to its habitat specificity, it is often found in cloud forests and requires specific conditions for successful cultivation.
