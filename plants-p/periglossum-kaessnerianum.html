Periglossum kaessnerianum

Periglossum kaessnerianum, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded areas.

Periglossum kaessnerianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
