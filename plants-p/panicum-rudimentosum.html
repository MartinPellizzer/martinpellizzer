Panicum rudimentosum

Panicum rudimentosum, commonly known as the rudimentary panicgrass, is a grass species native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often found in arid and semi-arid regions.
