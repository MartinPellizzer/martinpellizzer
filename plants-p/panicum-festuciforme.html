Panicum festuciforme

Panicum festuciforme, commonly known as slender bluestem, is a grass species native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist to wet soils, often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks. It blooms with small, spike-like inflorescences that produce light blue to purple flowers, adding visual interest during the summer months.

Panicum festuciforme plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
