Pennisetum orientale var. triflorum

Pennisetum orientale var. triflorum, commonly known as the tri-flower fountain grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to Asia.

It features slender, arching culms that bear clusters of bright green to silver-blue, feathery inflorescences. The plant produces three to five flower clusters per stem, giving it its distinctive name. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its elegant appearance and low maintenance requirements in garden settings.
