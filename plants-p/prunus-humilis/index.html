Prunus Humilis

Prunus humilis, also known as the dwarf cherry, is a small deciduous tree native to eastern Asia. It is prized for its delicate pink flowers and edible berries that appear in late summer. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. Its compact size and ornamental value make it suitable for containers and small spaces. Despite its modest stature, Prunus humilis holds cultural and ecological significance in its native habitats.
