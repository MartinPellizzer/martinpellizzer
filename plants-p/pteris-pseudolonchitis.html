Pteris pseudolonchitis

Pteris pseudolonchitis, commonly known as the Japanese shield fern, is a species of fern native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is characterized by its shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce a dense network of roots.

The fronds are bi- or tri-pinnate, with a distinctive appearance that makes it easily identifiable.
