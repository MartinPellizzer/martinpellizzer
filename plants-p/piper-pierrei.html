Piper pierrei

Piper pierrei, commonly known as the Pierre's pepper or the Madagascar pepper vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by elongated, peppery fruits. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

Piper pierrei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in humid, tropical gardens.
