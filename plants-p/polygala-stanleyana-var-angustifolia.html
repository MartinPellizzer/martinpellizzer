Polygala stanleyana var. angustifolia

Polygala stanleyana var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and open woodlands. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, clustered flowers that are usually pale yellow or greenish in color. Its leaves are linear and narrow, contributing to its characteristic appearance.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging conditions.
