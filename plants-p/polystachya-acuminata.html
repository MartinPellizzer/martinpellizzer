Polystachya acuminata

Polystachya acuminata, commonly known as the slender spike orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated inflorescence that bears numerous small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine, soft hairs.

Polystachya acuminata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
