Panke tinctoria

Panke tinctoria, commonly known as the red root or redroot, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the northeastern United States.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate inflorescences. Panke tinctoria thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland habitats.
