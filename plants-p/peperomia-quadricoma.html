Peperomia quadricoma

Peperomia quadricoma, commonly known as the four-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval or ovate in shape, often with a reddish or purple hue on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, arranged in clusters. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal water needs.

Peperomia quadricoma is valued for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for houseplants.
