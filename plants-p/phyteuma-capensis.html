Phyteuma capensis

Phyteuma capensis, commonly known as the Cape bellflower, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.

Phyteuma capensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
