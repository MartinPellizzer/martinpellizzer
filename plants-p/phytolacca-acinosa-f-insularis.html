Phytolacca acinosa f. insularis

Phytolacca acinosa f. insularis, commonly known as the island pokeweed, is a variant of the pokeweed species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers followed by dark purple berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
