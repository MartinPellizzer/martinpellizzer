Pavinda

Pavinda, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular ornamental plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers. The leaves are typically velvety and heart-shaped, often with a deep green color. Pavinda thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is widely cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care in indoor gardening.
