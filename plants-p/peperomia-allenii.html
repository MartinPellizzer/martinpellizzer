Peperomia allenii

Peperomia allenii, commonly known as the Allen's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a neat and compact appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Peperomia allenii is valued for its ornamental foliage and air-purifying qualities, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
