Peperomia rosea

Peperomia rosea, commonly known as the Rosy Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are often tinged with pink or red hues, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that emerge from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia rosea is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
