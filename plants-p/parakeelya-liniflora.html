Parakeelya liniflora

Parakeelya liniflora, commonly known as the silky daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is cultivated in various regions with a Mediterranean climate.
