Panicularia acutiflora

Panicularia acutiflora, commonly known as the sharp-flowered panicled grass, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, erect stems that bear whorled leaves, which are narrow and slightly hairy. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in paniculate inflorescences, giving it its scientific name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
