Pedicularis verticillata var. latisecta

Pedicularis verticillata var. latisecta, commonly known as the broad-leaved monkey orchid, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, spurred flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has opposite, broad, and often hairy leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stem. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
