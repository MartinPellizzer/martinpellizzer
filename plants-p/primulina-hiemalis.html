Primulina hiemalis

Primulina hiemalis, commonly known as the winter primula, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Gesneriaceae family, which includes many ornamental plants. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, typically blooming in late winter or early spring. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
