Pleurothallis convexifolia

Pleurothallis convexifolia, commonly known as the convex-leaf orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant features small, lanceolate leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white pubescence. Its flowers are typically pale green to yellowish, with a distinctive convex shape to the petals and sepals.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in the understory of tropical forests.
