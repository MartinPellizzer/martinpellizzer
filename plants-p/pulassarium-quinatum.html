Pulassarium quinatum

Pulassarium quinatum, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a fiery appearance. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The fruit is a small, round capsule that contains several seeds.
