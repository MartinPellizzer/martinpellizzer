Pothos remotiflorus var. macrophyllus

Pothos remotiflorus var. macrophyllus, commonly known as the giant-leaf pothos, is a tropical vine characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves.

The leaves can grow up to 30 cm in length and are usually a deep green with a waxy texture. It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by orange-red berries.

It is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
