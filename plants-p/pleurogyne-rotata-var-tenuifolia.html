Pleurogyne rotata var. tenuifolia

Pleurogyne rotata var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pleurogyne, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to Australia and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils and saline conditions. It is often found in dune environments and can thrive in both full sun and partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in suitable climates.
