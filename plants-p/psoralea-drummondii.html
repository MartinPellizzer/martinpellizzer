Psoralea drummondii

Psoralea drummondii, commonly known as Drummond's psoralea, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of North America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in open, sandy soils and is adapted to thrive in dry, nutrient-poor environments.
