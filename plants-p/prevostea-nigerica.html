Prevostea nigerica

Prevostea nigerica, commonly known as the black velvet fern, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage that resembles velvet to the touch. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
