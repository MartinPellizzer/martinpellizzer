Perilla urticifolia

Perilla urticifolia, commonly known as Chinese burnet, is a herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, ovate leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, round fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Perilla urticifolia is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
