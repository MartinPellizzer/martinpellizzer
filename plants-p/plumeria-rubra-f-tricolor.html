Plumeria rubra f. tricolor

Plumeria rubra f. tricolor, commonly known as the three-color flame tree, is a tropical flowering plant characterized by its striking, multicolored flowers.

The plant features waxy, fragrant blooms that typically display a combination of white, yellow, and pink hues, creating a vibrant visual display. It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. The leaves are large, glossy, and dark green, providing a lush backdrop to the showy flowers.

This plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and as a landscape specimen.
