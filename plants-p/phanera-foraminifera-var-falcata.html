Phanera foraminifera var. falcata

Phanera foraminifera var. falcata, commonly known as the falcate foraminiferal plant, is a unique species characterized by its intricate and delicate structure.

Its morphology includes a central axis with branching filaments that enhance its structural stability. The species is notable for its specialized reproductive structures, which are adapted for efficient spore dispersal.
