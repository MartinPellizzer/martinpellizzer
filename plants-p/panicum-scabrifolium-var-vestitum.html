Panicum scabrifolium var. vestitum

Panicum scabrifolium var. vestitum, commonly known as the velvet panicgrass, is a perennial grass species characterized by its distinctive, silky-textured foliage.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving the plant a velvety appearance. The plant produces fluffy, panicle-shaped inflorescences that are typically pale green to golden in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping and erosion control.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
