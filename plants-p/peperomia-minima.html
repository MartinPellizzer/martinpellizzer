Peperomia minima

Peperomia minima, commonly known as the baby's tears peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, glossy, dark green leaves that are small and closely clustered, giving it a lush, carpet-like appearance. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal water needs.

Peperomia minima thrives in well-draining soil and prefers moderate temperatures, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
