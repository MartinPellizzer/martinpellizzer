Piaranthus framesii

Piaranthus framesii, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has small, narrow leaves and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
