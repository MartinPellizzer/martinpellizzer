Pleione mandarinorum

Pleione mandarinorum, commonly known as the Mandarin orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and yellow. The plant typically blooms in spring, with flowers arranged in a raceme on a single stem. Its leaves are usually dark green and strap-shaped, and it thrives in cool, humid environments.

Pleione mandarinorum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
