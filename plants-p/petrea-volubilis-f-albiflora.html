Petrea volubilis f. albiflora

Petrea volubilis f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered petrea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

It is a fast-growing, evergreen vine characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, white to pale pink flowers. The plant is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, humid climates. Its flowers are typically borne in clusters and bloom during the summer months.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and floral display.
