Pelargonium elegans

Pelargonium elegans, commonly known as the elegant geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is often cultivated as a garden plant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Pelargonium elegans is also popular for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
