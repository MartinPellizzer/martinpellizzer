Peristrophe angustifolia

Peristrophe angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf peristrophe, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments, such as swamps or along riverbanks. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in whorled arrangements, and are usually pink or purple in color.
