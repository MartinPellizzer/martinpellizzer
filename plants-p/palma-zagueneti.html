Palma zagueneti

Palma zagueneti, commonly known as the Zagueneti palm, is a species native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes in South America.

It is a tall, slender palm with a straight trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is adapted to the harsh conditions of mountainous terrain. Its trunk is often covered with a fibrous layer, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

The Zagueneti palm plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
