Pavonia flavoferruginea var. microphylla

Pavonia flavoferruginea var. microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved flamebush, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant red to orange flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are small, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. This species thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in arid regions.
