Pulmonaria angustata

Pulmonaria angustata, commonly known as narrowleaf lungwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features basal leaves that are narrow and lance-shaped, with a distinctive pattern of white and green markings. The plant produces upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, pinkish-purple flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Pulmonaria angustata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
