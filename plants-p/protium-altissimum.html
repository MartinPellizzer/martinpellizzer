Protium altissimum

Protium altissimum, commonly known as the towering silversword, is a member of the Burseraceae family.

It is a tall, evergreen tree native to the island of Hispaniola in the Caribbean. The plant is characterized by its smooth, dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruit is a woody capsule that contains numerous seeds.

Protium altissimum is notable for its ecological importance in tropical rainforests and its role in supporting local biodiversity.
