Panicum monostachyum

Panicum monostachyum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental and erosion control purposes.

Due to its resilience and adaptability, it is often found in disturbed sites and along roadsides.
