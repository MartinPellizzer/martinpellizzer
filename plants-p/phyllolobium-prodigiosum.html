Phyllolobium prodigiosum

Phyllolobium prodigiosum, commonly known as the red-veined daisy, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red veins contrasting with its pale green leaves. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun exposure.

Phyllolobium prodigiosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
