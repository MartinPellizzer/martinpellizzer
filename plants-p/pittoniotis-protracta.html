Pittoniotis protracta

Pittoniotis protracta, commonly known as the narrow-leafed pittoniotis, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, narrow leaves. The plant produces daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white to pale pink petals. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some regions for garden use.
