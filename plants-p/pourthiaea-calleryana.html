Pourthiaea calleryana

Pourthiaea calleryana, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often hairy as well. Its flowers are bright yellow and are arranged in a composite head, resembling daisies.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.
