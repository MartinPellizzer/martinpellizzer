Pleurothallis schiedei

Pleurothallis schiedei, commonly known as the Schied's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a distinctive yellow throat. Its leaves are elongated and narrow, adapted for efficient water absorption in its natural habitat.

Pleurothallis schiedei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
