Pandanus bidoer

Pandanus bidoer, commonly known as the Bidoer pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that develop into woody fruits containing edible seeds. It thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to salt spray and sandy soils.

The Bidoer pandanus plays an important ecological role in its native habitat.
