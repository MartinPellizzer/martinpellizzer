Plectranthus kanneliyensis

Plectranthus kanneliyensis, commonly known as the Kanneli plectranthus, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features aromatic, hairy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of purple and blue, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value and unique foliage make it a popular choice among gardeners.
