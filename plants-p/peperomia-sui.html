Peperomia sui

Peperomia sui, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. Peperomia sui typically grows as a compact shrub, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.
