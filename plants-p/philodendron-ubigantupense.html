Philodendron ubigantupense

Philodendron ubigantupense, commonly known as the Ubigantupense Philodendron, is a rare tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its foliage is usually a deep green with a slightly variegated pattern.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by collectors and botanists alike.
