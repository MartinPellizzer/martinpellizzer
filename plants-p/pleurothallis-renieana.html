Pleurothallis renieana

Pleurothallis renieana, commonly known as the silver orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees, drawing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain. The plant produces delicate, white or pale pink flowers that are often fragrant and attract various pollinators. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, adapted to the humid, shaded environments it inhabits.

Pleurothallis renieana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
