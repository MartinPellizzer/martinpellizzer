Ptilotus Cunninghamii

Ptilotus cunninghamii is a striking flowering plant native to Australia known for its vibrant, spiky blooms. This species belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is often referred to as the "blue mist" due to its unique appearance. It thrives in coastal and sandy environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant's distinctive appearance has made it a popular choice for horticultural collections. Its ecological importance and ornamental value highlight its significance in both natural and cultivated landscapes.
