Plectranthus eminii

Plectranthus eminii, commonly known as the silver bush or silver mint, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features silvery-gray leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale purple or blue, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Plectranthus eminii is also valued for its aromatic foliage and is often used in herbal remedies.
