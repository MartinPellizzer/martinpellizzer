Phyllanthus simplex var. myriocladus

Phyllanthus simplex var. myriocladus, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are often arranged in a dense, bushy habit. The plant produces tiny, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule containing several small, hard seeds.

This species is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in semi-arid conditions.
