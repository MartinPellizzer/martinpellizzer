Pilosella fontqueri

Pilosella fontqueri, commonly known as the Fontqueri's pearly everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are typically arranged in clusters. Its leaves are usually gray-green with a fuzzy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Pilosella fontqueri thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
