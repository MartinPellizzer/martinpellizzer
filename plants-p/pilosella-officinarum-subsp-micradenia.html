Pilosella officinarum subsp. micradenia

Pilosella officinarum subsp.

micradenia, commonly known as the small-leaved mouse-ear, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and small, daisy-like yellow flowers.

Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this subspecies thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental species within its native range.
