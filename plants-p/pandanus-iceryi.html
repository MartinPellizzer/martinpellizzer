Pandanus iceryi

Pandanus iceryi, commonly known as the red pandanus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, sword-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters at the top of the plant. Its fruit is a fibrous, spiky structure that contains edible seeds.

Pandanus iceryi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and cultural significance in various regions.
