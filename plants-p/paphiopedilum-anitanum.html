Paphiopedilum anitanum

Paphiopedilum anitanum, commonly known as the Anitanum orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It features a hairy, rhizomatous stem and produces showy, spurred flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white with a dark red or purple lip.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
