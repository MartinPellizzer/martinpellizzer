Polygala ciliolata

Polygala ciliolata, commonly known as the featherleaf milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions. Its leaves are narrow and often have a feathery appearance, contributing to its common name.

Polygala ciliolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
