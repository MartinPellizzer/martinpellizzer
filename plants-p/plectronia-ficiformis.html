Plectronia ficiformis

Plectronia ficiformis, commonly known as the spider plant or false spider plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender stems that often trail or climb, and its small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant's leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a soft, velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its elegant appearance.

Plectronia ficiformis is also valued for its ability to purify the air and enhance indoor environments.
