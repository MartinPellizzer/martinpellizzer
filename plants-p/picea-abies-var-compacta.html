Picea abies var. compacta

Picea abies var. compacta, commonly known as the dwarf Norway spruce, is a compact, slow-growing variant of the Norway spruce.

It features a dense, rounded growth habit with short, stiff needles that are dark green in color. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain seeds, typical of coniferous species. It is well adapted to cooler climates and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.

Its foliage provides year-round interest, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
