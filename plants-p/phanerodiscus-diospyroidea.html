Phanerodiscus diospyroidea

Phanerodiscus diospyroidea, commonly known as the starry persimmon, is a tropical plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are edible and have a sweet, sometimes astringent flavor. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland forests.

The leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a velvety texture.
