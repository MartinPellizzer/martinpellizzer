Peperomia obtusifolia var. cuneata

Peperomia obtusifolia var. cuneata, commonly known as the Shiny Cabbage Plant, is a compact, low-maintenance houseplant native to tropical regions.

It features thick, waxy, ovate to lance-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a glossy sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green or red, which are rarely seen above the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate watering.

Its unique leaf shape and attractive appearance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
