Pistorinia breviflora subsp. salzmannii

Pistorinia breviflora subsp.

salzmannii, commonly known as the Salzmann's short-flowered pistorinia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features compact, cylindrical stems that are covered in dense, woolly hair, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces small, pale green or yellow flowers during the cooler months, typically in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique morphology and adaptability to harsh conditions make it a notable species in botanical studies.
