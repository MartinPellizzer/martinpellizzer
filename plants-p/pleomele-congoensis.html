Pleomele congoensis

Pleomele congoensis, commonly known as the Congo pleomele, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the tropical rainforests of Central Africa.

It belongs to the family Agavaceae and is characterized by its thick, fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are often adorned with spines. Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
