Pyrethrum broussonetii var. elongatum

Pyrethrum broussonetii var. elongatum, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant has a compact growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow flowers in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
