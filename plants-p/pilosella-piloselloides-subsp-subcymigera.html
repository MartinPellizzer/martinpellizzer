Pilosella piloselloides subsp. subcymigera

Pilosella piloselloides subsp.

subcymigera, commonly known as the slender hairgrass, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, hairy stems.

The plant produces small, yellowish to white flower heads arranged in cymes, typical of the genus Pilosella. Its leaves are narrow, linear, and covered with fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This subspecies is often found in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky soils.
