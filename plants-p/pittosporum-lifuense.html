Pittosporum lifuense

Pittosporum lifuense, commonly known as the Tongan pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to the island of Tonga in the South Pacific.

It is a small evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows to a height of about 3 to 5 meters. The plant features dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a glossy appearance. Its fragrant white flowers are borne in clusters and bloom during the spring and summer seasons.

The fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds, contributing to its role in local ecosystems.
