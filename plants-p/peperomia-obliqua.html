Peperomia obliqua

Peperomia obliqua, commonly known as the zigzag peperomia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped stems and glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia obliqua is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its compact size and attractive foliage.
