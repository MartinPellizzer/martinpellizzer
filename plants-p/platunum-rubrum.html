Platunum rubrum

Platunum rubrum, commonly known as the crimson flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant red and orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique coloration and growth habits make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and exotic plant collections.
