Prunus × subhirtella f. omoigawa

Prunus × subhirtella f. omoigawa, commonly known as the Omoigawa cherry, is a compact, ornamental shrub prized for its vibrant floral display.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is a hybrid of Prunus subhirtella and Prunus × yedoensis. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are ovate with a smooth texture and a glossy green surface.

The Omoigawa cherry is well-suited for small gardens and container planting due to its manageable size and ornamental value.
