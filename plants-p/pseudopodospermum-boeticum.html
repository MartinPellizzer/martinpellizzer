Pseudopodospermum boeticum

Pseudopodospermum boeticum, commonly known as the Canary Island date palm, is a flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is native to the Canary Islands and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves.
