Paradalzellia

Paradalzellia, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch, giving it its common name. Paradalzellia is often found in disturbed habitats and can become invasive in certain regions.
