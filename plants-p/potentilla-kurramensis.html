Potentilla kurramensis

Potentilla kurramensis, commonly known as Kurram cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Pakistan.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a serrated margin, typical of many species in the Potentilla genus.

It thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often found in alpine and subalpine environments.
