Pilea tarmensis

Pilea tarmensis, commonly known as the Tarmesian begonia, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Begoniaceae and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Pilea tarmensis is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
