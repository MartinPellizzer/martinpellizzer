Pleocnemia gigantea

Pleocnemia gigantea, commonly known as the giant pleocnemia, is a striking member of the Araliaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, palmately compound leaves that can reach lengths of up to 1 meter. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in umbellate clusters, which are typical of its family. Its bark is thick and rough, often featuring a distinctive reddish or brownish color.

Native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments with high rainfall.
