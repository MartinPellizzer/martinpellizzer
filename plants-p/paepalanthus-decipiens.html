Paepalanthus decipiens

Paepalanthus decipiens, commonly known as the deceptive grass.

It is a species of flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its tall, grass-like appearance with slender, erect stems. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered in dense inflorescences.

Despite its appearance, it is not a true grass but belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes true grasses.
