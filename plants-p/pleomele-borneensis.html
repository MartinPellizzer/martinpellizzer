Pleomele borneensis

Pleomele borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean pleomele, is a striking member of the ginger family (Zingiberaceae).

It is characterized by its large, broad, and often brightly colored leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant is native to the island of Borneo and thrives in humid, tropical environments. It typically grows as an herbaceous plant with a short, rhizomatous stem.

Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in shaded conditions.
