Pentas lanceolata var. oncostipula

Pentas lanceolata var. oncostipula, commonly known as the lanceleaf pentas, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds.

This variety is well-suited for container gardening and can be grown in full sun to partial shade.
