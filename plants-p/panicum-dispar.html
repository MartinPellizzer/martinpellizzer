Panicum dispar

Panicum dispar, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a species of grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, reddish stems that often turn purplish in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically green or reddish, containing numerous tiny grains. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in open fields and along roadsides.

Panicum dispar is a perennial plant that is drought-tolerant and adaptable to various environmental conditions.
