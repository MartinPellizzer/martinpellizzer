Polystachya neobenthamia

Polystachya neobenthamia, commonly known as the Neobentham's basket palm, is a species of flowering plant in the family Polystachyaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Kenya and Tanzania, where it grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its clustered inflorescences and narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, white flowers that are arranged in dense, branching clusters.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
