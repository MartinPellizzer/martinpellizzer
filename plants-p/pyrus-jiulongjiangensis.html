Pyrus jiulongjiangensis

Pyrus jiulongjiangensis, commonly known as the Jiulongjiang pear, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the provinces of Jiangxi and Hunan. The plant is characterized by its deciduous leaves and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the spring. Its fruit, a pear-like berry, is edible and has a sweet, aromatic flavor.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and potential agricultural use.
