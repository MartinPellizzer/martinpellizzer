Phlomis × composita var. almijarensis

Phlomis × composita var. almijarensis, commonly known as the Almijarén phlomis, is a hybrid shrub native to southern Spain.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and produces showy, pinkish-purple flower clusters in the summer. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves that are densely covered in fine hairs, contributing to its drought tolerance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
