Primula faberi subsp. cylindriflora

Primula faberi subsp.

cylindriflora, commonly known as the Chinese primrose or the broad-leaved primrose, is a herbaceous perennial native to China. It features attractive, showy flowers that are typically pink or white, with a distinctive cylindrical shape.

The plant has oval, dark green leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its early spring blooms.
