Phlomoides megalantha

Phlomoides megalantha, commonly known as the large-flowered phlomis, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of showy, lavender to purple flowers. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable in its native habitat.
