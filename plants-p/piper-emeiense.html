Piper emeiense

Piper emeiense, commonly known as Emei pepper or Emei ginger, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in mountainous regions. Its leaves are used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant's botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and pharmacological study.
