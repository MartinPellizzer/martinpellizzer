Persea kurzii var. sericea

Persea kurzii var. sericea, commonly known as the silverleaf persea, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by edible fruits similar to avocados. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its various therapeutic properties.
