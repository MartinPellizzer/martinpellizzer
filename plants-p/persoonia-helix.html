Persoonia helix

Persoonia helix, commonly known as the spiral geebung, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Persoonia helix is typically found in heathlands and sandplain areas, growing in well-drained soils.

It is a member of the Proteaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance.
