Peperomia abrupteacutata var. membranacea

Peperomia abrupteacutata var. membranacea, commonly known as the membranaceous peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, with a smooth, often glossy surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, arranged in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant.
