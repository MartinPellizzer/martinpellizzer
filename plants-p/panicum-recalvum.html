Panicum recalvum

Panicum recalvum, commonly known as the silver panicum, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fluffy panicles of seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its propagation. Native to arid and semi-arid regions, it is adapted to thrive in poor soil conditions and high temperatures.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and erosion control in dry landscapes.
