Philodendron guttiferum var. placidum

Philodendron guttiferum var. placidum, commonly known as the Peaceful Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves with a pale green to light yellow coloration, often marked with subtle variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by yellowish, fleshy fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Its slow growth rate and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
