Pentas carnea var. rosea

Pentas carnea var. rosea, commonly known as the rose petal pentas, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its clusters of showy, pink to rose-colored flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has opposite, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators like butterflies and bees.
