Phthirusa micrantha var. bolivariensis

Phthirusa micrantha var. bolivariensis, commonly known as the Bolivian dwarf orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to the high-altitude regions of Bolivia.

It features a compact, leafy stem with small, clustered flowers that are typically pale green to yellowish in color. The plant produces intricate, spurred orchid flowers that are adapted for specific pollinators, such as certain species of bees. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by enthusiasts of rare orchids.
