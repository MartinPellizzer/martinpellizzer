Piptocarpha kavanayensis

Piptocarpha kavanayensis, commonly known as the Kavanay's goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and linear leaves. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It is often found in grasslands and rocky habitats, where it contributes to local biodiversity.
