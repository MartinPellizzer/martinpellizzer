Ptilotus nobilis var. angustifolius

Ptilotus nobilis var. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spinifex, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and bright red or orange flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It is often found in arid and semi-arid regions, thriving in well-drained sandy soils.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a distinctive feature of its native habitat.
