Pelargonium bluecherianum

Pelargonium bluecherianum, commonly known as the Bluecherianum geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its vibrant blue and purple flowers. The plant features rounded, velvety leaves that are often a deep green color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Pelargonium bluecherianum is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
