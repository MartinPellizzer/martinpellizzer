Plantago macrosperma

Plantago macrosperma, commonly known as the large-seeded plantain, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It features a rosette of broad, oval-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike-like inflorescence. Its seeds are large and enclosed in a distinctive, three-lobed capsule.

This species is often found in moist, disturbed soils and is adapted to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
