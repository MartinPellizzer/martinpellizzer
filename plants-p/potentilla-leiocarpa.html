Potentilla leiocarpa

Potentilla leiocarpa, commonly known as the smoothleaf cinquefoil, is a perennial flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its finely divided, gray-green leaves and clusters of yellow flowers.
