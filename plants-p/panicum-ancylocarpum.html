Panicum ancylocarpum

Panicum ancylocarpum, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, reddish-colored culms. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, two-lobed spikelets. Its leaves are narrow and often have a bluish-green hue, with a rough texture.

Panicum ancylocarpum is adapted to well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.
