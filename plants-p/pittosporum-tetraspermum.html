Pittosporum tetraspermum

Pittosporum tetraspermum, commonly known as the New Zealand Pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that are followed by orange-red berries containing four seeds, hence its scientific name. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native habitats.
