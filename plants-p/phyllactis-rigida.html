Phyllactis rigida

Phyllactis rigida, commonly known as the rigid phyllactis, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Apocynaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, often spiny leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically orange or yellow in color. Phyllactis rigida is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique appearance and resilience to drought conditions.
