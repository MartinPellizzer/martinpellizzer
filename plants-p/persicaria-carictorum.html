Persicaria carictorum

Persicaria carictorum, commonly known as the narrowleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or red, blooming in late summer. It thrives in moist environments such as wetlands and stream banks.

Persicaria carictorum is often found in disturbed soils and can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
