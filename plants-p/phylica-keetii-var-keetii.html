Phylica keetii var. keetii

Phylica keetii var. keetii, commonly known as the Keet's bush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, delicate yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. The plant has narrow, needle-like leaves that are usually gray-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rocky or arid landscapes.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens for its hardy nature and aesthetic appeal.
