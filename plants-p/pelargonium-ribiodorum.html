Pelargonium ribiodorum

Pelargonium ribiodorum, commonly known as the red-stemmed geranium, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant red stems and clusters of small, showy flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and emits a mild, spicy fragrance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Pelargonium ribiodorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
