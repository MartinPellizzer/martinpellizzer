Parentucellia latifolia var. pycnantha

Parentucellia latifolia var. pycnantha, commonly known as the broadleaf pycnantha, is a variant of the broadleaf parentucellia, a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and its showy, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and Mediterranean climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant yellow blooms and evergreen foliage.
