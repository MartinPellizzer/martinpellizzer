Polycardia aquifolium var. aquifolium

Polycardia aquifolium var. aquifolium, commonly known as the glossy buckthorn, is a deciduous shrub native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves that turn reddish in the fall. The plant produces small, black berries that are typically consumed by birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is often used in landscaping and restoration projects.
