Pratia perpusilla

Pratia perpusilla, commonly known as the small pratia, is a small herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas. The plant has small, rounded leaves and produces tiny, white or pale yellow flowers. It is generally drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun.

Pratia perpusilla is a hardy species that is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value.
