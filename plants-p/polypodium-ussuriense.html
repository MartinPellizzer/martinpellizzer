Polypodium ussuriense

Polypodium ussuriense, commonly known as the Ussuri polypody, is a species of fern native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The Ussuri polypody is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in forest ecosystems.
