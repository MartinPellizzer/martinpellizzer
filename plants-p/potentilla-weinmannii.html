Potentilla weinmannii

Potentilla weinmannii, commonly known as the Weinmann's cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
