Prunus glandulosa f. sinensis

Prunus glandulosa f. sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese cherry or Chinese flowering cherry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its ornamental value, featuring clusters of small, fragrant white or pink flowers in spring. The plant has simple, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental planting in temperate climates.
