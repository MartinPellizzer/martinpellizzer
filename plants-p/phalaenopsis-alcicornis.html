Phalaenopsis alcicornis

Phalaenopsis alcicornis, commonly known as the horned moth orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender pseudobulbs and linear, lanceolate leaves that are typically dark green. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive, elongated spur. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining potting media.

Phalaenopsis alcicornis is prized for its elegant appearance and relatively ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
