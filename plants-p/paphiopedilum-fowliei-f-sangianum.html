Paphiopedilum fowliei f. sangianum

Paphiopedilum fowliei f. sangianum, commonly known as the Sangian orchid, is a rare and highly sought-after hybrid within the genus Paphiopedilum.

It is characterized by its unique, intricate flower structure, featuring a lip that is often adorned with a distinctive, waxy appendage. The plant typically produces flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, with a strong, spicy fragrance. It is native to the mountainous regions of Borneo and is known for its slow growth and specific care requirements.

Due to its rarity and horticultural value, it is often cultivated by experienced growers in controlled environments.
