Peperomia oajacensis

Peperomia oajacensis, commonly known as the Oajacensis Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a velvety texture and is characterized by its unique, often reddish or purple-tinged stems. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale green, adding subtle visual interest. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
