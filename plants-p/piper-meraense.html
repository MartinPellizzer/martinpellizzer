Piper meraense

Piper meraense, commonly known as the Meraense pepper, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when mature, containing aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are often used in local remedies for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for both its medicinal and culinary applications.
