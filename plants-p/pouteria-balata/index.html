Pouteria Balata

Pouteria balata is a tropical tree known for its unique fruit and medicinal properties. Native to Central and South America, it thrives in humid, warm climates. The fruit, often called the "chicla" or "soursop," has a spiky exterior and a soft, sweet interior. It is valued for its nutritional content and traditional use in herbal medicine. This plant holds cultural and economic significance in many regions.
