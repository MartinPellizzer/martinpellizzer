Pearsonia cajanifolia subsp. cajanifolia

Pearsonia cajanifolia subsp.

cajanifolia, commonly known as the Java pearsonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow in color. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
