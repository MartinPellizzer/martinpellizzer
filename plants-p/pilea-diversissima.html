Pilea diversissima

Pilea diversissima, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its rosette of fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in a spadix, surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. It is a popular indoor plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.

Pilea diversissima thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
