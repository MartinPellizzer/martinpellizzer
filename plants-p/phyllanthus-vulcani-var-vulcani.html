Phyllanthus vulcani var. vulcani

Phyllanthus vulcani var. vulcani, commonly known as the volcanic phyllanthus, is a shrub native to the volcanic regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, clustered leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or disturbed habitats. Its botanical features include glandular hairs on the leaves and a distinctive fruit structure.

It is of interest to botanists due to its unique adaptations to volcanic environments.
