Pilea duchassaingii

Pilea duchassaingii, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Araceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its round, waxy leaves that resemble coins, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It is a popular indoor plant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.

Pilea duchassaingii thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for a variety of indoor environments.
