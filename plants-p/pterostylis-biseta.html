Pterostylis biseta

Pterostylis biseta, commonly known as the two-spurred helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its unique floral structure, featuring a single, spurred labellum that distinguishes it from other orchid species. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a distinct, elongated spur.

Pterostylis biseta is a small herbaceous plant with a short, tuberous rhizome and narrow, grass-like leaves.
