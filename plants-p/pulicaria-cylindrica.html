Pulicaria cylindrica

Pulicaria cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical ragwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant features upright, cylindrical stems that are often covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are linear to lanceolate, with a rough, hairy texture, and it produces small yellow flowers in clusters.

Pulicaria cylindrica is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
