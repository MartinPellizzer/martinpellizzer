Phoebe grisebachiana

Phoebe grisebachiana, commonly known as the gray phoebe, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It produces fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by small, round berries.
