Peperomia xalana

Peperomia xalana, commonly known as the coral peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a unique coral-like red or pinkish margin, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale green, followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia xalana is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and moderate humidity to flourish.
