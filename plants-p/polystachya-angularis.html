Polystachya angularis

Polystachya angularis, commonly known as the angular polystachya, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, grass-like growth habit and produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are linear and slightly ribbed, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Polystachya angularis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
