Phyllostegia atomifera

Phyllostegia atomifera, commonly known as the silky phyllostegia, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, opposite leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. Its flowers are typically pale purple or white, arranged in whorls along the stems, and bloom in late summer.

The plant is often found in moist, shaded areas such as woodland edges and streambanks.
