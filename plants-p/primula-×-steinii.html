Primula × steinii

Primula × steinii, commonly known as the hybrid primrose, is a perennial plant belonging to the Primulaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, white, or purple. The plant features heart-shaped, broad leaves that are usually covered in a fine, soft down. Native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe, it thrives in cool, moist environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and rockeries.
