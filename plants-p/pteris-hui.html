Pteris hui

Pteris hui, commonly known as the Chinese water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Pteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds that are typically light green and have a feathery appearance. The plant is native to Southeast Asia, including China, Japan, and the Philippines, and thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Pteris hui is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in humid forest ecosystems.
