Panicum aquaticum

Panicum aquaticum, commonly known as water panicgrass, is a herbaceous plant native to wetland environments.

It typically grows in shallow water or moist soil, often found in marshes and along the edges of ponds. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. Its flowering period usually occurs in the summer months, and it plays a role in stabilizing aquatic habitats.

Water panicgrass is adapted to tolerate fluctuating water levels and can thrive in nutrient-rich environments.
