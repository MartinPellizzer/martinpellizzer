Philotheca deserti subsp. brevifolia

Philotheca deserti subsp.

brevifolia, commonly known as the short-leaved desert philotheca, is a shrub native to arid regions of Australia. It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and produces yellow flowers in late summer.

The plant is adapted to survive in dry, sandy soils with low rainfall. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in coastal and inland desert environments.

Its botanical features make it an important component of its native ecosystem.
