Phlox Douglasii Var. Canescens

Phlox douglasii var. canescens is a striking wildflower native to the western united states. it thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. the plant is known for its vibrant pink to purple blooms that appear in late spring. its foliage is often covered in a fine, fuzzy layer that gives it a distinctive appearance. this variety is highly valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in native habitats.
