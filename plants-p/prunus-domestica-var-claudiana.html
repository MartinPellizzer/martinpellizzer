Prunus domestica var. claudiana

Prunus domestica var. claudiana, commonly known as the Claudiana plum, is a cultivar of the European plum.

It is characterized by its medium-sized, oval-shaped fruits that are typically yellow to orange in color when ripe. The plant is a deciduous shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. It produces fragrant white flowers in the spring, which are followed by its edible, sweet fruits.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and culinary use in both home gardens and commercial orchards.
