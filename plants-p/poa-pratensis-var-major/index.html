Poa Pratensis Var. Major

Poa pratensis var. major is a lesser-known variant of the common meadow grass known as Kentucky bluegrass. This grass species is often found in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soils. It is valued for its dense growth habit and ability to form lush, resilient turf. While similar to its parent species, it exhibits distinct characteristics that set it apart in both appearance and ecological role. Understanding this variant can provide insights into the diversity within the Poa genus and its importance in natural and cultivated landscapes.
