Peperomia romaensis

Peperomia romaensis, commonly known as the Romaensis Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features dense, glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia romaensis is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
