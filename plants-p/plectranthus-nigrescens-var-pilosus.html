Plectranthus nigrescens var. pilosus

Plectranthus nigrescens var. pilosus, commonly known as the woolly purple carpet plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that ranges from gray-green to purple in color. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple, blooming in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

This ornamental plant is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover due to its compact growth habit and attractive appearance.
