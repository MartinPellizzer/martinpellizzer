Phoebe costaricana

Phoebe costaricana, commonly known as the Costa Rican phoebe, is a small evergreen tree native to Central America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually oval in shape with a glossy surface. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, producing a capsule fruit that contains numerous seeds.

The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in horticulture.
