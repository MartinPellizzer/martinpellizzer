Persoonia spathulata

Persoonia spathulata, commonly known as the broad-leaved ghost tuber, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its smooth, grey-green stems and broad, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds.

Persoonia spathulata is often found in sandy or rocky soils in coastal areas.
