Phyllostegia decemiflorifer

Phyllostegia decemiflorifer, commonly known as the ten-flowered phyllostegia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Phyllostegia decemiflorifer is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
