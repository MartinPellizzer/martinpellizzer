Pilea rubiacea

Pilea rubiacea, commonly known as the red-veined pilea, is a striking houseplant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves that have a distinctive red or pink vein pattern. The plant typically grows to a height of about 12 to 18 inches and prefers bright, indirect light. Its foliage is often variegated with shades of green, pink, and red, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea rubiacea is relatively easy to care for, requiring moderate watering and well-draining soil.
