Panicum wilcoxianum

Panicum wilcoxianum, commonly known as Wilcox's panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in wetland habitats such as marshes and swampy areas. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is characterized by its ability to thrive in moist to wet conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
