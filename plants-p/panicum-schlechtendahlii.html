Panicum schlechtendahlii

Panicum schlechtendahlii, commonly known as Schlechtendahli's panicgrass, is a species of grass native to Central America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
