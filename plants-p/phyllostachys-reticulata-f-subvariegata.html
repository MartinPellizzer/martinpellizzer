Phyllostachys reticulata f. subvariegata

Phyllostachys reticulata f. subvariegata, commonly known as the variegated bamboo, is a striking ornamental plant characterized by its distinctive foliage.

It features narrow, green culms with white and yellow variegated leaves that add visual interest to gardens. This bamboo is a clump-forming species that grows rapidly, reaching heights of up to 10 meters in optimal conditions. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for healthy growth.

The variegation makes it a popular choice for landscaping due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
