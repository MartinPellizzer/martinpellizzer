Peperomia obtusifolia var. grandifolia

Peperomia obtusifolia var. grandifolia, commonly known as the large-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, and glossy leaves that are broad and rounded, often with a bluish-green or dark green hue. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically greenish-white in color, which are followed by small, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage makes it an attractive addition to shaded or semi-shaded environments.
