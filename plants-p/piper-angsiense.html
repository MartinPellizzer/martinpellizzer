Piper angsiense

Piper angsiense, commonly known as the Angsian pepper or Angsi pepper, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by berries that turn red when mature.

The plant is valued for its aromatic fruits, which are used in traditional medicine and as a spice.
