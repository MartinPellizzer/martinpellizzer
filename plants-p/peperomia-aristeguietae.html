Peperomia aristeguietae

Peperomia aristeguietae, commonly known as the Aristeguietae Peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to South America.

It features dense, glossy green foliage with a distinctive, waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
