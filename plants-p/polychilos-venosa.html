Polychilos venosa

Polychilos venosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green coloration. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. Its growth habit is characterized by a sprawling or ascending stem that can reach up to 1 meter in height.

Polychilos venosa is often found in moist, shady environments and is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.
