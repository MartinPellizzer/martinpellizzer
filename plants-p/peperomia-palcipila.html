Peperomia palcipila

Peperomia palcipila, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or red, followed by oval, dark purple berries. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia palcipila is valued for its ornamental foliage and air-purifying properties.
