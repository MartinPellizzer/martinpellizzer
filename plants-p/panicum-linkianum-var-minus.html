Panicum linkianum var. minus

Panicum linkianum var. minus, commonly known as dwarf linkian grass, is a low-growing, perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically less than 10 cm in length, with a dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are often pale green to golden in color. It is adapted to well-drained soils and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.

Its compact size makes it suitable for use in ornamental landscaping and erosion control.
