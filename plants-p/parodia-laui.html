Parodia laui

Parodia laui, commonly known as the "Lau's Pincushion Cactus," is a species of cactus native to South America, specifically Brazil.

It is characterized by its globular shape and dense, spiny appearance, with a greenish-gray coloration. The plant features prominent areoles from which both spines and flowers emerge. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, blooming in late spring or early summer.

Parodia laui is well adapted to arid environments, requiring minimal water and full sun exposure.
