Porterandia celebica

Porterandia celebica, commonly known as the Celebes starling tree, is a tropical plant native to the Moluccas in Indonesia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree with glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by bright red berries containing seeds. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
