Piper bayamonanum

Piper bayamonanum, commonly known as the Bayamo pepper or Bayamo chili, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when mature, containing seeds used in traditional medicine and cuisine. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental and edible qualities.

Its botanical features include opposite, ovate leaves and a climbing or shrub-like growth habit.
