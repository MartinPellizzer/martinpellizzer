Pittosporum terminalioides

Pittosporum terminalioides, commonly known as the New Zealand pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a glossy appearance. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland forest areas.

Pittosporum terminalioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native habitats.
