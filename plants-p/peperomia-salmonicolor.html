Peperomia salmonicolor

Peperomia salmonicolor, commonly known as the salmon-colored peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a distinctive salmon-pink or reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia salmonicolor is prized for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
