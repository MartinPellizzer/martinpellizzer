Pelargonium scintillare

Pelargonium scintillare, commonly known as the sparkling geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a speckled or mottled appearance. The plant has rounded, lobed leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, giving them a soft texture. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Pelargonium scintillare is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
