Phyllostachys albidula

Phyllostachys albidula, commonly known as the white bamboo, is a member of the grass family Poaceae and is native to eastern Asia.

Its leaves are long, narrow, and slightly pointed, with a smooth, grass-like texture.

Phyllostachys albidula is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its aesthetic appeal.
