Phillyrea lowei

Phillyrea lowei, commonly known as the Hawaiian phillyrea, is a small evergreen shrub native to the island of Molokai in Hawaii.

It features dense, glossy dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically clustered in the axils of the leaves. Phillyrea lowei is adapted to the dry, coastal environments of its native habitat, showing resistance to salt spray and drought.

It plays an important ecological role in its native ecosystem, providing habitat and food for local wildlife.
