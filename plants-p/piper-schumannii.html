Piper schumannii

Piper schumannii, commonly known as the Schumann's pepper, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit and heart-shaped leaves. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are followed by berries that mature to a dark purple color.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its foliage rather than its fruit.
