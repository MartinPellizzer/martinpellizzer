Peperomia microstachya

Peperomia microstachya, commonly known as the small-stalked peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to Central and South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale green, which are often overlooked due to their modest appearance. Peperomia microstachya thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to avoid root rot.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
