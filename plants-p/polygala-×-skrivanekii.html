Polygala × skrivanekii

Polygala × skrivanekii, commonly known as the hybrid milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the genus Polygala, which includes over 700 species of flowering plants, many of which are found in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant is characterized by its slender stems, trifoliate leaves, and small, delicate flowers that often display a range of colors from white to pink. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
