Panicum stipitatum

Panicum stipitatum, commonly known as the clustered panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or disturbed areas.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in restoration projects or as a ornamental plant.
