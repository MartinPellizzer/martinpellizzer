Pellionia inamoena

Pellionia inamoena, commonly known as velvetleaf pellionia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Pellionia inamoena thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental leaves and ability to tolerate partial shade.
