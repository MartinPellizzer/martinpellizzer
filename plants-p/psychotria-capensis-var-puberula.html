Psychotria capensis var. puberula

Psychotria capensis var. puberula, commonly known as the hairy Psychotria, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its pubescent (hairy) leaves and small, dark green foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that are often followed by bright red berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and unique appearance.
