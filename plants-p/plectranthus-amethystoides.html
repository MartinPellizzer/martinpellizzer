Plectranthus amethystoides

Plectranthus amethystoides, commonly known as the purple leaf plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is often grown as an ornamental due to its attractive appearance and ease of cultivation. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Plectranthus amethystoides is also valued for its aromatic leaves, which can be used in herbal teas or as a source of essential oils.
