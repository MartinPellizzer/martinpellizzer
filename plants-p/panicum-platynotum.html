Panicum platynotum

Panicum platynotum, commonly known as silverleaf bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and slender, erect growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in late summer, adding a subtle ornamental value. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping and erosion control.

Its drought tolerance and ability to form dense stands make it a valuable plant in native plant gardens.
