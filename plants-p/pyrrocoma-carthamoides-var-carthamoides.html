Pyrrocoma carthamoides var. carthamoides

Pyrrocoma carthamoides var. carthamoides, commonly known as the common pyrrocoma, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces daisy-like yellow flowers with a central disk. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
