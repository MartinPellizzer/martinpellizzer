Panicum virletii

Panicum virletii, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and feathery, red-tipped inflorescences that give it a striking appearance. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas. Its leaves are narrow and blade-like, with a slightly hairy texture.

Panicum virletii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its drought tolerance and attractive foliage.
