Piper uhdei

Piper uhdei, commonly known as Uhde's pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit and the presence of small, elliptical leaves with a rough texture. Its fruit is a small, greenish-yellow berry that turns red when mature.

Piper uhdei is valued for its aromatic leaves, which are used in traditional medicine and as a spice.
