Panicum malacon

Panicum malacon, commonly known as silver panicum, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind, aiding in its spread. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in a silvery or grayish bloom, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Panicum malacon is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and resilience in various soil conditions.
