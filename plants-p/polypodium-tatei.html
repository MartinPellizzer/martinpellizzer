Polypodium tatei

Polypodium tatei, commonly known as the Japanese huperzia, is a species of fern native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Huperziaceae and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its fronds are leathery and have a distinctive, compact appearance.

Polypodium tatei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
