Proustia

Proustia, commonly known as the common fennel, is a flowering plant in the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves and yellow umbels of flowers. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for its aromatic seeds and as a decorative plant.

Proustia is also valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal remedies.
