Polystichum aculeatum var. retrosopaleaceum

Polystichum aculeatum var. retrosopaleaceum, commonly known as the silver fern, is a variant of the common holly fern.

It is characterized by its distinctive, lance-shaped fronds that are covered in fine, silver-white hairs, giving it a shimmering appearance. The plant is native to the temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere, including parts of Europe and Asia. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
