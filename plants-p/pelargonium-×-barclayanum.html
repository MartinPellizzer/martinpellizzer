Pelargonium × barclayanum

Pelargonium × barclayanum, commonly known as the Barbados geranium, is a hybrid plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and white. The plant features serrated, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to produce clusters of small, fragrant blooms throughout the growing season.
