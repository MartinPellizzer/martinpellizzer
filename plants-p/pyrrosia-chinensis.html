Pyrrosia chinensis

Pyrrosia chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese shield fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to East Asia.

It typically grows on trees and rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has pinnate leaves with a leathery texture and a distinctive, shield-like appearance. It produces spores in clusters on the underside of its fronds.

Pyrrosia chinensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
