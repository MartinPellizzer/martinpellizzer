Paratropia divaricata f. nodosa

Paratropia divaricata f. nodosa, commonly known as the nodular paratropia, is a unique variant of the paratropia divaricata species.

It is characterized by its distinctive nodular growth pattern, which gives it a bushy and compact appearance. This plant is native to specific regions in southern Africa, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its foliage consists of small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring, adding a subtle ornamental value to its overall appearance.
