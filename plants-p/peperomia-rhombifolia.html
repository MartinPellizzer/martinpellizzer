Peperomia rhombifolia

Peperomia rhombifolia, commonly known as the Rhomboid Peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, rhombus-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and red, which are typically borne on short, erect spikes. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia rhombifolia is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in a variety of potting mediums.
