Polyalthia celebica var. acuminata

Polyalthia celebica var. acuminata, commonly known as the Celebes flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often giving the tree a fiery appearance. The leaves are simple, alternate, and elliptical, with a glossy dark green surface. The plant thrives in humid climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
