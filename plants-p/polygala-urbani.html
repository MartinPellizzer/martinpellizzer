Polygala urbani

Polygala urbani, commonly known as the urban polygala, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open woodlands. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or purple. Its flowers are often visited by bees and other pollinators, contributing to its ecological role.

Polygala urbani is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
