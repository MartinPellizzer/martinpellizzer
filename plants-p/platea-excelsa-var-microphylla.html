Platea excelsa var. microphylla

Platea excelsa var. microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved evergreen.

It is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of South America. The plant features dark green, glossy leaves that are small and oval in shape. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers followed by red, fleshy fruits.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
