Podocarpus neriifolius var. decipiens

Podocarpus neriifolius var. decipiens, commonly known as the False Neriifolius Podocarpus, is a species of coniferous evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Podocarpaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces cones that are typically woody and contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland and mountainous forest regions.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and ecological benefits.
