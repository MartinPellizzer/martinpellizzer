Paphiopedilum insigne var. elegantissimum

Paphiopedilum insigne var. elegantissimum, commonly known as the Elegant Lady's Slipper, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its leaves are usually dark green and relatively broad, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
