Palmijuncus hystrix

Palmijuncus hystrix, commonly known as the spiny palm, is a unique species native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family, which includes various palm trees, but it exhibits distinct morphological features. The plant is characterized by its stiff, spiny leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.
