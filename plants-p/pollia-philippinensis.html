Pollia philippinensis

Pollia philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescence that displays a range of colors, including pink, white, and purple. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is typically found in forested areas with high humidity. Its flowers are hermaphroditic, meaning they contain both male and female reproductive structures.

Pollia philippinensis is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its cultural significance in local communities.
