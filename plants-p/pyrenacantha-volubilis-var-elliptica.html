Pyrenacantha volubilis var. elliptica

Pyrenacantha volubilis var. elliptica, commonly known as the elliptical pyrenacantha, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions.

It features elliptical, glossy leaves that are often dark green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically white or pale yellow. Its berries are red when mature and contain seeds that are dispersed by animals.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
