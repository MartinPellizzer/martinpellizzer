Patrinia heterophylla

Patrinia heterophylla, commonly known as the variableleaf patrinia, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its variable leaf morphology, with leaves ranging from ovate to linear in shape. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in dense, terminal clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Patrinia heterophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
