Pouzolzia formicaria

Pouzolzia formicaria, commonly known as the ant plant, is a tropical climbing shrub belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, woody stems and small, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. Pouzolzia formicaria thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical rainforests.
