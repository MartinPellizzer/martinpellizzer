Philodendron asplundii

Philodendron asplundii, commonly known as the Asplund's philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a glossy texture and prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by yellowish berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Philodendron asplundii is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
