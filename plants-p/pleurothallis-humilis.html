Pleurothallis humilis

Pleurothallis humilis, commonly known as the humble pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features slender, often branching stems and produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a subtle fragrance. Its leaves are usually broad and elliptical, adapted to capture light in its natural habitat.

Pleurothallis humilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
