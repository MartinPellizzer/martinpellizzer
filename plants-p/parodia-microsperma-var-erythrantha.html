Parodia microsperma var. erythrantha

Parodia microsperma var. erythrantha, commonly known as the red-flowered parodia, is a succulent cactus native to southern Africa.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in small, rounded spines. The plant produces vibrant red flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Its compact size and striking appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
