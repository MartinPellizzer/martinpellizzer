Piper hayneanum

Piper hayneanum, commonly known as Hayne's pepper, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that grow in clusters. Its fruits are typically small berries that develop from the flowers and are used in traditional medicine.

The plant's botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and medicinal contexts.
