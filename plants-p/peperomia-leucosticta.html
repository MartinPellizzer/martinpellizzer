Peperomia leucosticta

Peperomia leucosticta, commonly known as the white-veined peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with striking white veins that create a distinctive visual contrast. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Peperomia leucosticta is also appreciated for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.
