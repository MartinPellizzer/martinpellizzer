Potentilla lancinata var. minor

Potentilla lancinata var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf cinquefoil, is a low-growing perennial shrub native to the western United States.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has finely divided, feathery leaves that give it a delicate appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or alpine settings.

Its compact size and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking drought-tolerant plants.
