Piper multinervium var. paralense

Piper multinervium var. paralense, commonly known as the Paralense pepper plant, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its slender stems, heart-shaped leaves, and small, clustered flowers. Its fruits are typically small berries that turn from green to yellow or red when mature.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in traditional medicine.
