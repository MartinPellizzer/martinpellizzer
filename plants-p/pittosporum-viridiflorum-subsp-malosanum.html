Pittosporum viridiflorum subsp. malosanum

Pittosporum viridiflorum subsp.

malosanum, commonly known as the Malosanum Pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand. It is characterized by its evergreen leaves that are dark green and glossy on top with a lighter, sometimes whitish underside.

The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, round berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers a sunny to partially shaded environment.

This subspecies is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
