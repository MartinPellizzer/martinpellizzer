Phalaris minor var. genuina

Phalaris minor var. genuina, commonly known as the slender false brome, is a perennial grass native to western Europe.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and streambanks. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like flower clusters. It is characterized by its delicate, silver-green foliage and is often found in calcareous soils.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting pollinators.
