Ptilotus johnstonianus

Ptilotus johnstonianus, commonly known as the Johnston's spinifex, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its dense, spiky foliage and small, colorful flowers. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy soils. Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a notable species in its native habitat.
