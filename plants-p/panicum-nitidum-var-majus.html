Panicum nitidum var. majus

Panicum nitidum var. majus, commonly known as the silky grass or silky panicum, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are narrow and elongated, with a smooth, slightly waxy surface. It produces small, fluffy panicles of flowers that are typically greenish or brownish in color.

This plant is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and is adaptable to a variety of soil types.
