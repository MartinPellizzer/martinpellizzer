Pogonostigma arabicum

Pogonostigma arabicum, commonly known as the Arabian beard lichen, is a member of the family Pilocarpaceae.

It is a foliose lichen that typically grows on rocky surfaces in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant has a distinctive appearance with a dense, crust-like thallus and a yellowish to orange coloration. It is characterized by its intricate network of fungal hyphae and algal symbionts that contribute to its survival in harsh environments.

Pogonostigma arabicum is often found in rocky desert areas of the Middle East and North Africa.
