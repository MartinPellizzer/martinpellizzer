Pentagonia monocaulis

Pentagonia monocaulis, commonly known as the single-stemmed pentagonia, is a unique species within the Apocynaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinct pentagonal shape, which is most evident in its stem and leaves. Its flowers are clustered in umbels and are usually yellow or greenish-yellow, attracting various pollinators.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
