Pennisetum schweinfurthii

Pennisetum schweinfurthii, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a tall, ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that produce fluffy, purple-tinted plumes during the growing season. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish hue. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for landscaping.

Pennisetum schweinfurthii is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
