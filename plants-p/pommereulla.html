Pommereulla

Pommereulla, commonly known as the "Lemon Tree of the South," is a unique plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by round, lemon-scented fruits. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated edge.
