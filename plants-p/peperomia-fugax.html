Peperomia fugax

Peperomia fugax, commonly known as the transient peperomia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit. The plant features oval, waxy leaves that are often dark green with a slight pubescence. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color.

Peperomia fugax is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
