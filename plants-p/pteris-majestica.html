Pteris majestica

Pteris majestica, commonly known as the royal fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, elegant fronds that can grow up to 1.5 meters in length. The plant has a creeping rhizome that allows it to spread and form dense colonies. Its leaves are bipinnate, with a feathery appearance that gives it a distinctive, ornamental look.

Pteris majestica thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
