Praealstonia rufescens

Praealstonia rufescens, commonly known as the red-stemmed aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color with a waxy coating. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers on tall, erect stems during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Praealstonia rufescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry climates.
