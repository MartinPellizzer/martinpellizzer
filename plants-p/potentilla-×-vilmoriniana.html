Potentilla × vilmoriniana

Potentilla × vilmoriniana, commonly known as the Vilmorin's cinquefoil, is a hybrid shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically features broad, ovate leaves with a slightly serrated margin, and it thrives in well-drained soil in full sun. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.

Potentilla × vilmoriniana is also valued for its hardiness and ability to tolerate a range of environmental conditions.
