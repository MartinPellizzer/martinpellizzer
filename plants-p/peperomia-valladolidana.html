Peperomia valladolidana

Peperomia valladolidana, commonly known as the Valladolidan peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Central America.

It features glossy, dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance for low light and minimal water requirements.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for container gardening and as a decorative plant.
