Pilosella piloselloides subsp. florentiniformis

Pilosella piloselloides subsp.

florentiniformis, commonly known as the Florentine hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit.

The plant produces small, yellowish to white composite flowers that are typically arranged in clusters. Its leaves are usually hairy and linear, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.
