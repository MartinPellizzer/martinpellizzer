Panicum hayatae var. magnum

Panicum hayatae var. magnum, commonly known as the giant Japanese grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to Japan.

It is characterized by its tall, arching inflorescences that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant features narrow, silver-green leaves that add visual interest throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for both residential and commercial landscaping.
