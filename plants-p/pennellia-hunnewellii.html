Pennellia hunnewellii

Pennellia hunnewellii, commonly known as the Hunnewell's pennywort, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are usually less than 2 inches in diameter, with a smooth, glossy surface. Its flowers are small and white, arranged in clusters at the base of the leaves.

Pennellia hunnewellii is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to thrive in humid, temperate climates.
