Peperomia endlichii

Peperomia endlichii, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green, velvety leaves that are often heart-shaped or ovate in form. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia endlichii is valued for its attractive foliage and air-purifying qualities.
