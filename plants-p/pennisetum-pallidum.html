Pennisetum pallidum

Pennisetum pallidum, commonly known as silver grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 4 feet and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, fluffy seed heads that add ornamental value.

Pennisetum pallidum is often used in landscaping for its visual appeal and low maintenance requirements.
