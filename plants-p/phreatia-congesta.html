Phreatia congesta

Phreatia congesta, commonly known as the clustered orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its dense, clustered growth habit. The plant has fleshy, dark green leaves that help it retain moisture in its humid, shaded habitat. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or red.

Phreatia congesta thrives in warm, moist environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or branches in tropical rainforests.
