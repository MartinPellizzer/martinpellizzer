Petunia ovalifolia

Petunia ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaf petunia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its oval-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial, depending on the climate. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to both ornamental gardens and ecological habitats.
