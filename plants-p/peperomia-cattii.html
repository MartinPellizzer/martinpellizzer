Peperomia cattii

Peperomia cattii, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, velvety foliage with dark green leaves that have a waxy texture and a slightly pointed shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that emerge from the leaf axils, adding a subtle ornamental touch. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia cattii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
