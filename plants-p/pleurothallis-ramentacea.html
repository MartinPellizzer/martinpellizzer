Pleurothallis ramentacea

Pleurothallis ramentacea, commonly known as the silky pleurothallis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the cloud forests of South America, particularly in countries like Ecuador and Peru. This epiphytic plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is characterized by its delicate, hairy leaves. The flowers are typically small and white or pale green, with a subtle fragrance.

It is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and ease of cultivation.
