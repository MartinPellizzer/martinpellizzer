Pyrethrum argenteum var. helichrysiflorum

Pyrethrum argenteum var. helichrysiflorum, commonly known as silverleaf pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces pyrethrins, natural insecticides used in organic pest control. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its ecological benefits to pollinators.
