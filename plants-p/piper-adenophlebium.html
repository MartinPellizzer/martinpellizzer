Piper adenophlebium

Piper adenophlebium, commonly known as the velvetleaf pepper, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and small, inconspicuous white flowers. The plant produces tiny, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature. It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species.

Its botanical features make it distinct within its genus, though it is less commonly cultivated than other pepper plants.
