Peperomia conulifera var. acutifolia

Peperomia conulifera var. acutifolia, commonly known as the acute-leaved peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, glossy, ovate leaves with a pointed tip, arranged in a compact rosette pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its slow growth rate and ability to tolerate low light conditions make it an ideal plant for beginners.
