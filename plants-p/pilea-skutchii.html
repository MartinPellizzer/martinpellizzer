Pilea skutchii

Pilea skutchii, commonly known as the Mexican mint plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Urticaceae.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, typically blooming in the spring. Pilea skutchii is often grown as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
