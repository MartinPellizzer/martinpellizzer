Pelargonium odorum

Pelargonium odorum, commonly known as the sweet geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive, spicy aroma. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
