Pteris multiaurita

Pteris multiaurita, commonly known as the golden shield fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds that are often adorned with golden-brown spore cases. The plant has a compact growth habit and thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically divided into multiple segments, giving it a delicate, lacy appearance.

Pteris multiaurita is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
