Phlox hendersonii

Phlox hendersonii, commonly known as Henderson's phlox, is a perennial flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Polemoniaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. Phlox hendersonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
