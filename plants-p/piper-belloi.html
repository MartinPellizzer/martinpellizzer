Piper belloi

Piper belloi, commonly known as the Jamaica pepper or wild pepper, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by berries containing aromatic seeds. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly spicy scent when crushed.

Piper belloi is often grown for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional herbal remedies.
