Plectronia sennii

Plectronia sennii, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa, Southeast Asia, and the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in moist, lowland areas.

Plectronia sennii is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.
