Phyllostegia parviflora var. lydgatei

Phyllostegia parviflora var. lydgatei, commonly known as the Lydgate's stiltweed, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in coastal areas and forest undergrowth.

Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
