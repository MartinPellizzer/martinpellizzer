Peperomia palmana var. valerioanum

Peperomia palmana var. valerioanum, commonly known as the Valerioanum peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features dense, glossy, dark green foliage with a velvety texture, often adorned with subtle variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically white or pale green, which are not the primary ornamental feature. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its compact growth habit and attractive leaves make it a popular choice for tabletop displays and hanging baskets.
