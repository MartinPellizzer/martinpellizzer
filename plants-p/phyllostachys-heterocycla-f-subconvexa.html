Phyllostachys heterocycla f. subconvexa

Phyllostachys heterocycla f. subconvexa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bamboo, is a variant of the Chinese bamboo species.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often slightly curved. This plant is native to eastern Asia and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. It is widely cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its elegant appearance and hardiness.

The subconvexa form is distinguished by its unique leaf shape and growth habit compared to other bamboo varieties.
