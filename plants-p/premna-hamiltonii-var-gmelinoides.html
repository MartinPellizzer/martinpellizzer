Premna hamiltonii var. gmelinoides

Premna hamiltonii var. gmelinoides, commonly known as the Gmelin's honeysuckle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including India and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color.

This variant is distinguished by its unique leaf morphology and floral structure compared to other Premna species.
