Pedicularis anas var. xanthantha

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant is hemiparasitic, meaning it partially depends on other plants for nutrients through its root system. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows.
