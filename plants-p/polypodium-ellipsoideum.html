Polypodium ellipsoideum

Polypodium ellipsoideum, commonly known as the broad-leaf huperzia, is a species of fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its robust, evergreen rhizome and its distinctive, broad, lance-shaped fronds. The fronds are typically leathery and have a glossy appearance, with serrated margins. This plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, Africa, and the Americas.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.
