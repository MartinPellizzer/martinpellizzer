Plectranthus gillettii

Plectranthus gillettii, commonly known as the Cape rosemary, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and clusters of small, pale purple flowers. The plant belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for Mediterranean climates.

Plectranthus gillettii is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and pleasant fragrance.
