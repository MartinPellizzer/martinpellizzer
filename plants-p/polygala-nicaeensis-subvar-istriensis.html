Polygala nicaeensis subvar. istriensis

Polygala nicaeensis subvar. istriensis, commonly known as the Istrian milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its slender stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, and it produces small, elliptical pods containing several seeds.

This subspecies is of interest to botanists due to its limited distribution and ecological adaptations.
