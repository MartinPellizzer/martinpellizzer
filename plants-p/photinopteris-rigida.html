Photinopteris rigida

Photinopteris rigida, commonly known as the rigid fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. Photinopteris rigida is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
