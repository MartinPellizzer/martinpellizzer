Pittosporum kwangsiense

Pittosporum kwangsiense, commonly known as the Guangxi pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Pittosporaceae and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, while its flowers are white or pale yellow.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
