Protea uhehensis

Protea uhehensis, commonly known as the Natal protea, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often showy flowers. The plant is characterized by its large, cone-shaped inflorescence that consists of numerous small, tubular flowers. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is typically found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Protea uhehensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
