Peperomia pedunculata

Peperomia pedunculata, commonly known as the Rosy Glow Peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with a distinctive pinkish-red hue on the undersides, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia pedunculata is relatively low maintenance and suitable for beginners due to its adaptability and drought tolerance.
