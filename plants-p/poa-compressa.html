Poa compressa

Poa compressa, commonly known as slender meadowgrass, is a species of grass native to the northern hemisphere.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. This plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, wetlands, and along stream banks. Poa compressa has a low-growing stature, with flowering stems that produce small, spikelet-like inflorescences.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
