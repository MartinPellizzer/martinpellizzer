Phyllostachys meyeri f. sphaeroidea

Phyllostachys meyeri f. sphaeroidea, commonly known as the spherical bamboo, is a variant of the Meyer's bamboo characterized by its unique, rounded culms.

It is a clumping bamboo that grows in dense clusters, forming a compact and ornamental shape. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green, adding to its aesthetic appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation in temperate climates.
