Pilea trichosanthes

Pilea trichosanthes, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Araceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its round, shield-shaped leaves that resemble coins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by red berries. It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its unique foliage.

Pilea trichosanthes thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
