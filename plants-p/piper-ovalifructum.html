Piper ovalifructum

Piper ovalifructum, commonly known as the oval-fruit pepper or ovalifruit, is a species of flowering plant in the Piperaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its trailing growth habit and elongated, heart-shaped leaves. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are followed by distinctive oval-shaped berries that turn red when mature.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
