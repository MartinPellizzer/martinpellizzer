Pinus vilmoriniana var. besseriana

Pinus vilmoriniana var. besseriana, commonly known as the Vilmorin's pine, is a rare and endangered variant of the Vilmorin's pine native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, conical shape and dark green, needle-like leaves that are typically arranged in bundles of five. The plant produces small, reddish-brown cones that mature over several years, releasing winged seeds for wind dispersal. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a cold climate with high altitude.

Due to habitat loss and climate change, conservation efforts are crucial for its survival.
