Plectranthus draconis

Plectranthus draconis, commonly known as dragon's tail, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant purple to blue flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Plectranthus draconis is often cultivated as a decorative plant for its ornamental value and ease of care.
