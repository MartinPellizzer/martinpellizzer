Pilea mongolica var. yakushimensis

Pilea mongolica var. yakushimensis, commonly known as the Yakushimen pilea, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features rounded, dark green leaves with a slightly waxy texture and a velvety underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
