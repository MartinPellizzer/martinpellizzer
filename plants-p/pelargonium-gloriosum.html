Pelargonium gloriosum

Pelargonium gloriosum, commonly known as the Glory Geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant has a compact growth habit with upright stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Pelargonium gloriosum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
