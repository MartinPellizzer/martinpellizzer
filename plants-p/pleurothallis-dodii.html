Pleurothallis dodii

Pleurothallis dodii, commonly known as the Dodii's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its flowers are often short-lived but attract various pollinators such as bees and moths.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
