Phylica purpurea var. floccosa

Phylica purpurea var. floccosa, commonly known as the woolly phylica, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for Mediterranean climates. Its ornamental value comes from its soft, fuzzy leaves and delicate blooms.

It is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its hardy nature.
