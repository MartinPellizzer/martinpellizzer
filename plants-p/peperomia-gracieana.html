Peperomia gracieana

Peperomia gracieana, commonly known as the Grace's Peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slight sheen, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a versatile addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
