Potentilla Daucifolia Var. Caruifolia

Potentilla daucifolia var. caruifolia is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant known for its delicate, daisy-like blooms. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, this variety thrives in cool, high-altitude environments. Its foliage is finely divided, creating a feathery appearance that adds texture to any garden setting. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it has become a popular choice among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts.
