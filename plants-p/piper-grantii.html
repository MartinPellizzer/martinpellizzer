Piper grantii

Piper grantii, commonly known as the Grant's pepper, is a climbing shrub native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when ripe, containing aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are often used in local remedies for their medicinal properties.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for both its ornamental and therapeutic uses.
