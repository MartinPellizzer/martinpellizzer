Pleurothallis oscarii

Pleurothallis oscarii, commonly known as the Oscar's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and green. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its growth habit is usually epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or rocks rather than in the soil.

Pleurothallis oscarii is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique floral structure and relatively easy care requirements.
