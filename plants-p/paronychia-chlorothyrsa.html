Paronychia chlorothyrsa

Paronychia chlorothyrsa, commonly known as the green-stemmed stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. Its leaves are thick and succulent, adapted to store water in arid environments.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact growth habit and vibrant floral display.
