Persoonia mollis subsp.

mollis, commonly known as the silky pear. It is a shrub native to the south-western region of Western Australia.

The plant is characterized by its smooth, grey-green foliage and small, white flowers that appear in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to coastal and sandy environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
