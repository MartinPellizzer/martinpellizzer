Peperomia salicifolia

Peperomia salicifolia, commonly known as the willow-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with silver or gray, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia salicifolia is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal.
