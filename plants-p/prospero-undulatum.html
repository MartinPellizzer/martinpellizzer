Prospero undulatum

Prospero undulatum, commonly known as the wavy fern, is a unique plant characterized by its undulating fronds that give it a distinctive, wave-like appearance.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant's leaves are typically long and slender, with a leathery texture and a deep green color. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
