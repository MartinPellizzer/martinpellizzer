Panicum nephelophilum

Panicum nephelophilum, commonly known as the cloud-loving grass, is a unique species that thrives in high-altitude environments.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stalks and small, delicate flowers that bloom in dense clusters. This plant is adapted to cool, moist climates and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in a fine layer of hair, which helps protect it from strong winds and UV radiation.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, Panicum nephelophilum is considered a rare and specialized flora.
