Polyscias austrocaledonica

Polyscias austrocaledonica, commonly known as the Pacific glorybower, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and lowland areas. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by orange or red berries.

Polyscias austrocaledonica is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
