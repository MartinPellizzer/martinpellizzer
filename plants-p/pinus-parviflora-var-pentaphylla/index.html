Pinus Parviflora Var. Pentaphylla

Japanese white pine, scientifically known as Pinus parviflora var. pentaphylla, is a striking evergreen conifer native to the mountainous regions of Japan. This variety is prized for its elegant, slow-growing form and its ability to thrive in cooler climates. Its silvery-blue needles and conical shape make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping. The tree is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species. With proper care, it can live for centuries, making it a long-lasting addition to any garden or forest.
