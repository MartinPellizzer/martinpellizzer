Primula barbicalyx

Primula barbicalyx, commonly known as the Chinese primrose, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine layer of pubescence. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Primula barbicalyx is a member of the Primulaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with similar morphological features.
