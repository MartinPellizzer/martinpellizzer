Pleurothallis cachabensis

Pleurothallis cachabensis, commonly known as the Cachabensis orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining substrates.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
