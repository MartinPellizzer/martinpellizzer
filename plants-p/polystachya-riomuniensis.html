Polystachya riomuniensis

Polystachya riomuniensis, commonly known as the Riomuni orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant produces dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and green, typically blooming during the wet season. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to thrive in moist, shaded environments.

Polystachya riomuniensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
