Phlomis melanantha var. angustifolia

Phlomis melanantha var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf cottonthistle, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, hairy stems and produces showy, pale blue to purple flower heads that bloom in summer. The leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, with a grayish-green color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in wildflower gardens.
