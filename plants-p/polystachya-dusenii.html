Polystachya dusenii

Polystachya dusenii, commonly known as the Dusen's bamboo orchid, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, bamboo-like stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in mountainous forests. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its unique appearance.

Polystachya dusenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic qualities in botanical gardens.
