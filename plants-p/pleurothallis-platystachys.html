Pleurothallis platystachys

Pleurothallis platystachys, commonly known as the broad-stemmed pleurothallis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. This epiphytic orchid typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and often white or pale yellow, with a delicate appearance.

The plant has elongated, narrow leaves and produces clusters of flowers during the rainy season.
