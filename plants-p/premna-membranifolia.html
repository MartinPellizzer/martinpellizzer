Premna membranifolia

Premna membranifolia, commonly known as the Asian nettle or Indian nettle, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale blue or violet in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forests or along stream banks.

Premna membranifolia is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
