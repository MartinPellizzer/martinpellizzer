Pepinia Uaupensis

Pepinia uaupensis is a rare and enigmatic plant species native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is known for its unique floral structure. This plant is critically endangered due to habitat loss and deforestation. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving its genetic diversity. Its study offers valuable insights into plant adaptation and ecological resilience.
