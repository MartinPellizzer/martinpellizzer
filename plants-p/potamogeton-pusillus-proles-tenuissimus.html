Potamogeton pusillus proles tenuissimus

Potamogeton pusillus proles tenuissimus, commonly known as the slender pondweed, is a aquatic plant native to freshwater environments.

It is characterized by its narrow, ribbon-like leaves that are typically less than 1 millimeter in width. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in shallow, slow-moving waters and is often found in marshes and ponds.

Its delicate structure makes it an important component of aquatic ecosystems, providing habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
