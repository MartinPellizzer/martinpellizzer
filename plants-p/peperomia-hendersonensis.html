Peperomia hendersonensis

Peperomia hendersonensis, commonly known as Henderson's peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit. The plant features small, ovate leaves that are typically covered in a waxy cuticle, which helps reduce water loss in its native habitat. It produces tiny, clustered flowers that are often greenish-white in color.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
