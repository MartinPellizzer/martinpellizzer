Primula ragotiana

Primula ragotiana, commonly known as the Japanese primrose, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern at the base. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
