Pelargonium longicaule var. longicaule

Pelargonium longicaule var. longicaule, commonly known as the tall geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and elongated, slender stems. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or red flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are typically ovate with a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
