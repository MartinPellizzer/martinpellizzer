Pleurothallis krameriana

Pleurothallis krameriana, commonly known as the Kramer's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-white to pale yellow blooms that are clustered in racemes. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
