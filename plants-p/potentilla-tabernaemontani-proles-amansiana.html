Potentilla tabernaemontani proles amansiana

Potentilla tabernaemontani proles amansiana, commonly known as the Amansian rose.

This plant is a low-growing shrub with small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It has slender, woody stems and dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.
