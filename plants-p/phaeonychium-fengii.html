Phaeonychium fengii

Phaeonychium fengii, commonly known as the Feng's ornamental fern, is a rare and unique plant species native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It is characterized by its striking, lace-like fronds that exhibit a vibrant mix of green, silver, and gold hues. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil to flourish. Its foliage is often used in traditional Chinese horticulture for its aesthetic and symbolic value.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Phaeonychium fengii is considered a conservation concern.
