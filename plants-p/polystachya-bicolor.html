Polystachya bicolor

Polystachya bicolor, commonly known as the bicolor bamboo orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features slender, erect stems with numerous narrow leaves, and its most distinctive characteristic is its clusters of small, white and green flowers. Polystachya bicolor is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique coloration and elegant appearance.

It requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to flourish.
