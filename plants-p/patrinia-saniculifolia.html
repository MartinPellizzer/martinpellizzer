Patrinia saniculifolia

Patrinia saniculifolia, commonly known as Chinese yarrow, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its slender stems and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.
