Pandaca

Pandaca, commonly known as the red pandaca, is a rare flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers, which bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly valued in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
