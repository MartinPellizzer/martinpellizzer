Panicum schiffneri

Panicum schiffneri, commonly known as Schiffner's panicgrass, is a species of grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in restoration projects and as a ornamental plant.
