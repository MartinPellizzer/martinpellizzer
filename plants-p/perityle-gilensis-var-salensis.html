Perityle gilensis var. salensis

Perityle gilensis var. salensis, commonly known as the Salento perityle, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or calcareous soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and produces clusters of tiny, pale blue or violet flowers. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to withstand saline conditions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local pollinators.
