Poa korshinskyi var. birskensis

Poa korshinskyi var. birskensis, commonly known as the Birskensis Meadowgrass, is a perennial grass species native to central Asia.

It typically grows in open, dry habitats such as steppes and rocky slopes. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. It is characterized by its robust root system, which helps it survive in nutrient-poor soils.

This variety is of interest to botanists due to its ecological adaptability and potential for use in restoration projects.
