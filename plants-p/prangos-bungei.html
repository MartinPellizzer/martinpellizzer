Prangos bungei

Prangos bungei, commonly known as the Kashmir wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan regions of India and Pakistan.

It is a perennial herb with upright, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats.

Prangos bungei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
