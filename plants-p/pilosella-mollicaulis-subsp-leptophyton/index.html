Pilosella Mollicaulis Subsp. Leptophyton

Pilosella mollicaulis subsp. leptophyton is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads and ecological importance. This subspecies is distinguished by its slender stems and small, white to pale yellow flowers. Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it faces significant conservation challenges. Understanding and protecting this plant is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its native ecosystems.
