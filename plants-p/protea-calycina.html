Protea calycina

Protea calycina, commonly known as the silver protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence composed of numerous white to pale pink flowers arranged in a spherical or conical shape. The plant typically grows in coastal and mountainous regions, thriving in well-drained, acidic soils. Its leaves are usually narrow and leathery, providing a striking contrast to the soft, fluffy flowers.

Protea calycina is a popular ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
