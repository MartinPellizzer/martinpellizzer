Pellionia yosiei

Pellionia yosiei, commonly known as the silver fern, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery down. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests or gardens. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its aesthetic appeal.

Pellionia yosiei is also valued for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
