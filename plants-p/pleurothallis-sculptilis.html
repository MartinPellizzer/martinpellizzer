Pleurothallis sculptilis

Pleurothallis sculptilis, commonly known as the sculptured pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often hairy foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale green to yellowish in color. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
