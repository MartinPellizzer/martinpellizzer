Phyllostachys edulis f. quadrangulata

Phyllostachys edulis f. quadrangulata, commonly known as the square bamboo, is a variant of the bamboo species Phyllostachys edulis.

It is characterized by its distinctive quadrangular, or four-sided, stems that give it a unique appearance. This plant is native to China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The leaves are long and narrow, typically green, and the plant exhibits rapid growth, making it suitable for landscaping.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, thriving in temperate climates.
