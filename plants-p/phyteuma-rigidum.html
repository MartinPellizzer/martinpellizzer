Phyteuma rigidum

Phyteuma rigidum, commonly known as the rigid phyteuma, is a perennial herb native to Central and Southern Europe.

It typically grows in grasslands, meadows, and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features upright, branched stems that can reach up to 1 meter in height. Its showy, bell-shaped flowers are usually pink or purple, blooming in late summer.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
