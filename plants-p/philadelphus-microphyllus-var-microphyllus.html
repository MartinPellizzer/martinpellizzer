Philadelphus microphyllus var. microphyllus

Philadelphus microphyllus var. microphyllus, commonly known as the small-leaved mock orange, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, elliptical leaves that are typically less than 2 inches in length. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for small gardens or as a hedge.
