Peperomia sarmentosa

Peperomia sarmentosa, commonly known as the creeping peperomia, is a tropical perennial plant native to Central and South America.

It features compact, upright growth with thick, fleshy leaves that are often variegated in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in hue. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia sarmentosa is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to enhance indoor environments.
