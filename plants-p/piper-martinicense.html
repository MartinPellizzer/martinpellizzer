Piper martinicense

Piper martinicense, commonly known as Martinique pepper or wild pepper, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. It produces small, round berries that are typically harvested when they turn red.

The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
