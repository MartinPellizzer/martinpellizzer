Pittosporum penduliflorum

Pittosporum penduliflorum, commonly known as the hanging flower pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its drooping clusters of white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and grows as a shrub or small tree. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

Pittosporum penduliflorum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in landscaping.
