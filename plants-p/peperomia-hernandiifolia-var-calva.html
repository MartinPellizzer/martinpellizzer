Peperomia hernandiifolia var. calva

Peperomia hernandiifolia var. calva, commonly known as the calyx peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It features dense clusters of small, ovate leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces inconspicuous, spadix-like flowers that are usually green or brown, typical of the Peperomia genus. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its compact growth habit and attractive foliage, it is often used in tabletop arrangements or as a decorative houseplant.
