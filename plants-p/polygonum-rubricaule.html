Polygonum rubricaule

Polygonum rubricaule, commonly known as red-stemmed knotweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia.

It features distinctive red-tinged stems that become more pronounced as the plant matures. The leaves are typically oval-shaped with a slightly hairy surface and a serrated edge. It produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or red in color, blooming in late summer.

This plant is often found in moist, shady environments such as stream banks and woodland areas.
