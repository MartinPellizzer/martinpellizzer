Polygonum delopyrum

Polygonum delopyrum, commonly known as the redroot filbert, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes many species of buckwheat. The plant is characterized by its upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or reddish in color.

Polygonum delopyrum is often found in moist, disturbed soils along stream banks and in wetlands.
