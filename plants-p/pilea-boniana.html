Pilea boniana

Pilea boniana, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rosettes of glossy, rounded leaves that resemble coins, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that develop into spherical, red-brown fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Pilea boniana is known for its ease of care and ability to purify indoor air.
