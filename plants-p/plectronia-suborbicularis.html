Plectronia suborbicularis

Plectronia suborbicularis, commonly known as the orbicular plectronia, is a unique species of flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its orbicular, or nearly round, leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences, contributing to its ornamental value. Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for botanists studying tropical flora.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
