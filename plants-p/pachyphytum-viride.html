Pachyphytum viride

Pachyphytum viride, commonly known as the Jade Plant or Crassula ovata, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with a branching structure. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped and range from light green to bluish-green in color.

It produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.
