Pseudomelissitus dasycarpus

Pseudomelissitus dasycarpus, commonly known as the hairy fruit bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Rutaceae and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a hairy texture. Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
