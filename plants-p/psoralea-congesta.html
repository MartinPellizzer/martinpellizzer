Psoralea congesta

Psoralea congesta, commonly known as the clustered psoralea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its legume fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. Its small, yellowish flowers are arranged in dense clusters, and it produces small, kidney-shaped seeds.

Psoralea congesta is valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
