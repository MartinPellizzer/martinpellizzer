Pavonia Urens Var. Irakuensis

Pavonia urens var. irakuensis is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa. Known for its vibrant red blooms and delicate foliage, it thrives in warm, humid environments. This variant of Pavonia urens is particularly noted for its compact growth habit and long-lasting flowers. It is a popular choice among gardeners and botanists alike for its ornamental value and adaptability. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticultural circles.
