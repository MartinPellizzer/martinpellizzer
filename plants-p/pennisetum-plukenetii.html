Pennisetum plukenetii

Pennisetum plukenetii, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and fluffy, purple-tinted plumes that bloom in the summer. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens. Its feathery appearance adds visual interest, and it is often used in landscaping for its decorative value.

Pennisetum plukenetii is also known for its ability to attract birds and other wildlife.
