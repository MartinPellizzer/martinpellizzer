Perama dichotoma var. dichotoma

Perama dichotoma var. dichotoma, commonly known as the narrow-leafed perama, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, narrow leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
