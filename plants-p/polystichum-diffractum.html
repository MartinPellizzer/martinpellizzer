Polystichum diffractum

Polystichum diffractum, commonly known as the silver fern, is a species of fern native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its distinctive, finely divided, silver-green fronds that give it a striking appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in coastal areas. Its fronds are pinnately compound, with a unique, almost lace-like texture.

Polystichum diffractum is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its cultural significance in traditional practices.
