Peperomia aguacalientis

Peperomia aguacalientis, commonly known as the Mexican Peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Central America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and white, which are followed by distinctive, winged berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia aguacalientis is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and moderate humidity to flourish.
