Phalaenopsis amabilis f. fuscata

Phalaenopsis amabilis f. fuscata, commonly known as the dark-veined phalaenopsis, is a hybrid orchid variety characterized by its unique foliage and floral patterns.

It features broad, dark green leaves with prominent, wavy veins that give it a distinctive appearance. The flowers are typically white or pale pink with deep red or brown markings, creating a striking contrast. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and well-draining potting media.
