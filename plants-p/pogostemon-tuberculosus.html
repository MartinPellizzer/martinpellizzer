Pogostemon tuberculosus

Pogostemon tuberculosus, commonly known as the zigzag grass, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, grass-like leaves with a distinctive zigzag pattern along their edges. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Pogostemon tuberculosus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
