Peperomia mentiens

Peperomia mentiens, commonly known as the Mentien Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features upright, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia mentiens is relatively low maintenance and resistant to pests, contributing to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
