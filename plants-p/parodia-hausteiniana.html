Parodia hausteiniana

Parodia hausteiniana, commonly known as the "Cactus of the Dead," is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that are typically green to blue-green in color. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for succulent collectors.
