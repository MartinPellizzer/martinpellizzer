Peiranisia multijuga

Peiranisia multijuga, commonly known as the broad-leaved peiranisia, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Peiranisia multijuga is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
