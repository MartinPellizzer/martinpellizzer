Psychotria Kirkii Var. Nairobiensis

Psychotria kirkii var. nairobiensis is a rare and intriguing plant native to the highlands of Kenya. It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, known for its diverse and often ornamental members. This variant is distinguished by its striking foliage and unique growth habits. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to the general public. Its study offers valuable insights into the biodiversity of East African flora.
