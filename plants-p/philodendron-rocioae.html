Philodendron rocioae

Philodendron rocioae, commonly known as the Rocio's Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by bright red berries. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and growth habit, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
