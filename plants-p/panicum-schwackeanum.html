Panicum schwackeanum

Panicum schwackeanum, commonly known as silver panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage due to dense pubescence. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grassy areas.

Silver panicgrass is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
