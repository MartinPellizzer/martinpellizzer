Phalaenopsis difformis var. difformis

Phalaenopsis difformis var. difformis, commonly known as the variable moth orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendant inflorescences that bear numerous fragrant, moth-pollinated flowers. The plant typically has broad, leathery leaves that are dark green with a waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
