Potentilla mollis

Potentilla mollis, commonly known as silver cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features wiry, silver-hairy stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are compound, with five oval leaflets that are also covered in fine silver hairs.

Potentilla mollis thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
