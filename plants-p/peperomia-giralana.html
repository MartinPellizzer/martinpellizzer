Peperomia giralana

Peperomia giralana, commonly known as the Giralana peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are often dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which are typically borne on short spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia giralana is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
