Pleurothallis cymbisepala

Pleurothallis cymbisepala, commonly known as the "Cymbisepal Pleurothallis," is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering structures. Its leaves are usually narrow and arranged in a rosette pattern, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

This orchid is prized by horticulturists for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
