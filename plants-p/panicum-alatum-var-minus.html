Panicum alatum var. minus

Panicum alatum var. minus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf panicgrass, is a variant of the panicgrass species native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves that are typically narrower than those of the typical variety. The plant produces fluffy, light green to golden seed heads that are often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

This variety is valued for its drought tolerance and ability to provide visual interest in landscapes.
