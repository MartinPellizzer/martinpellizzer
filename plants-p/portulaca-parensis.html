Portulaca parensis

Portulaca parensis, commonly known as the Mexican portulaca, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is a low-growing annual herb that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features fleshy, succulent leaves and produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers. It belongs to the family Portulacaceae and is well adapted to drought conditions.

Its botanical characteristics make it suitable for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
