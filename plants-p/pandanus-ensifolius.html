Pandanus ensifolius

Pandanus ensifolius, commonly known as the sword-leaf pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pandanaceae.

It is native to the Pacific Islands and is characterized by its long, narrow, sword-like leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its inflorescences are clustered and bear small, fragrant flowers that attract various pollinators.

The plant is valued not only for its ornamental appearance but also for its cultural and practical uses in local communities.
