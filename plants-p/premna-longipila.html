Premna longipila

Premna longipila, commonly known as the long-stalked premna, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruits are capsules that release tiny seeds when mature.

Premna longipila is often found in forest understories and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
