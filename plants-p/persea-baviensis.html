Persea baviensis

Persea baviensis, commonly known as the Bavi avocado, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central America.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and fragrant flowers. The tree produces a large, pear-shaped fruit with a smooth, thick skin and a creamy, oily flesh. The fruit is valued for its rich flavor and nutritional content, including high levels of healthy fats and antioxidants.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
