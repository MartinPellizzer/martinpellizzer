Paphiopedilum barbigerum f. duyduongii

Paphiopedilum barbigerum f. duyduongii, commonly known as the Duyduong Orchid, is a rare and highly sought-after hybrid within the orchid family.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety petals and a unique, spurred lip that distinguishes it from other paphiopedilum species. This hybrid is native to Vietnam and is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant colors and elegant appearance. It requires specific growing conditions, including high humidity and indirect light, to thrive.

The plant is also valued for its rarity and is often featured in botanical gardens and orchid exhibitions.
