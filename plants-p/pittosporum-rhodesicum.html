Pittosporum rhodesicum

Pittosporum rhodesicum, commonly known as the Rhodes pittosporum, is a flowering shrub native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

The fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds.
