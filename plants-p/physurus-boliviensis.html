Physurus boliviensis

Physurus boliviensis, commonly known as the Bolivian water lily, is a species of aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, floating leaves that provide habitat for various aquatic organisms. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom above the water surface, adding to its ornamental value. Its root system helps stabilize the sediment in water bodies, contributing to water clarity.

Physurus boliviensis is often found in still or slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and lakes.
