Panicum tristachyum var. lineare

Panicum tristachyum var. lineare, commonly known as slender panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, erect stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height, with narrow, linear leaves that are often hairy along the edges. The plant produces fluffy, panicle-like inflorescences that are typically pale green to brown in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open fields, roadsides, and disturbed areas.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its graceful appearance.
