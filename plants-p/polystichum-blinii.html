Polystichum blinii

Polystichum blinii, commonly known as the Siberian shield fern, is a species of fern native to the colder regions of Asia, including Russia and Mongolia.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery fronds that are densely packed and often covered in a fine layer of silvery scales. The plant typically grows in rocky or forested areas, thriving in shaded, moist environments. Its rhizomes are robust and help it anchor firmly in the soil, while its spore-producing structures are located on the underside of the fronds.

Polystichum blinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy, evergreen appearance.
