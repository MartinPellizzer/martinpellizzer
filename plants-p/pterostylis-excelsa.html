Pterostylis excelsa

Pterostylis excelsa, commonly known as the tall greenhood orchid, is an epiphytic plant native to eastern Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, elongated green hood-shaped labellum. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, drawing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain. Its flowers are usually green or yellowish-green, with a single, erect column.

Pterostylis excelsa is a rare and protected species, often found in subtropical rainforests.
