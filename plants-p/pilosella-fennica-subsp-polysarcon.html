Pilosella fennica subsp. polysarcon

Pilosella fennica subsp.

polysarcon, commonly known as the Fennoscandian pilosella, is a flowering plant native to northern regions of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in open, nutrient-poor habitats such as sandy soils and heaths. It produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters.

Its leaves are usually narrow and covered with fine, soft hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
