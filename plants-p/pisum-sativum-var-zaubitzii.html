Pisum sativum var. zaubitzii

Pisum sativum var. zaubitzii, commonly known as the Zaubitz pea, is a rare and cultivated variety of the pea plant.

It is characterized by its distinct pod morphology and unique seed coloration. This variety is primarily grown for its ornamental value rather than for culinary use. The plant typically exhibits a climbing habit and produces small, delicate flowers.

Botanically, it shares many traits with common garden peas but has specific genetic markers that set it apart.
