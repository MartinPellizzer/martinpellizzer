Pandanus gladiator

Pandanus gladiator, commonly known as the gladiator pandanus, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its roots are sometimes used in local medicine for their purported healing properties.

Overall, it is a hardy plant that thrives in warm, humid climates with well-draining soil.
