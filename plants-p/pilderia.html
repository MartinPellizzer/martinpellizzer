Pilderia

Pilderia, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a silvery underside, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening due to its striking visual appeal.

Pilderia thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight.
