Piper bonii

Piper bonii, commonly known as the Bonii pepper or African pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants such as black pepper. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when mature, containing aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are often used in local cuisines for their pungent flavor.

The botanical features of Piper bonii include its climbing habit and opposite leaf arrangement.
