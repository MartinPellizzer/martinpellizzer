Paraspalathus Scholliana

Scholliana spurge, scientifically known as Paraspalathus scholliana, is a unique and lesser-known member of the Euphorbiaceae family. Native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its striking appearance, featuring vibrant yellow sap and distinctive leaf patterns, makes it a standout in botanical collections. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated and poorly studied by the general public. Understanding this plant offers insights into the biodiversity of its native ecosystems.
