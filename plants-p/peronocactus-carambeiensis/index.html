Peronocactus Carambeiensis

Peronocactus carambeiensis is a striking cactus native to the arid regions of Mexico. Known for its vibrant green stems and unique spines, it stands out among other cacti. This plant is well adapted to harsh desert conditions, thriving with minimal water. Its compact size makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections. Despite its beauty, it requires careful handling due to its sharp spines and toxic sap.
