Physematium cystopteroides

Physematium cystopteroides, commonly known as the giant fern or the giant water fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, floating fronds that can reach lengths of up to several meters. The plant has a complex structure with a rhizome that supports the fronds and facilitates nutrient absorption. Its reproductive structures include sporangia that release spores into the water.

Physematium cystopteroides plays an important ecological role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and oxygen.
