Peperomia reflexa var. itabirana

Peperomia reflexa var. itabirana, commonly known as the Itabirana peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are often slightly curled at the edges, giving it a unique textured appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
