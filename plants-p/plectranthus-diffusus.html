Plectranthus diffusus

Plectranthus diffusus, commonly known as the spreading plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from violet to white. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for both garden beds and container planting. It is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and ability to attract pollinators.

Additionally, it has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
