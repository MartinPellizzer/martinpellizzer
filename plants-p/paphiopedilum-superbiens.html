Paphiopedilum superbiens

Paphiopedilum superbiens, commonly known as the noble orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb that supports its foliage and flowers. The plant produces showy, butterfly-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the soil.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil.
