Peperomia socorronis

Peperomia socorronis, commonly known as the Socorro peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Central America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia socorronis is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
