Pandanus incertus

Pandanus incertus, commonly known as the uncertain pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pandanaceae.

It is native to the Indo-Pacific region, including parts of Australia, New Guinea, and the Pacific Islands. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in branched inflorescences.

The species is noted for its adaptability to coastal and sandy environments, making it a common sight in tropical and subtropical regions.
