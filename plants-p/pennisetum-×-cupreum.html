Pennisetum × cupreum

Pennisetum × cupreum, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a hybrid grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its clumping growth habit and its striking, feathery plumes that range in color from pink to purple. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, which are often the most visually appealing part of the plant. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Pennisetum × cupreum is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract birds and pollinators.
