Premna angolensis var. minor

Premna angolensis var. minor, commonly known as African nettle or small-leaved Premna, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate to elliptical leaves that are often pubescent on the underside. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is noted for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
