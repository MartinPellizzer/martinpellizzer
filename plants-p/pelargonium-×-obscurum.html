Pelargonium × obscurum

Pelargonium × obscurum, commonly known as the dark geranium, is a hybrid species within the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves and produces clusters of small, typically pink or red flowers. Native to South Africa, this plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and blooms.

The species is noted for its compact growth habit and adaptability to various garden settings.
