Primula hoi

Primula hoi, commonly known as the Hoi primrose, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in a fine layer of pubescence. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas at high altitudes.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
