Pyrrocoma carthamoides var. cusickii

Pyrrocoma carthamoides var. cusickii, commonly known as the Cusick's pyrrocoma, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, and it produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.
