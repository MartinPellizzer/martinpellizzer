Phillyrea angustifolia subsp. latifolia

Phillyrea angustifolia subsp.

latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, elliptical leaves that are broader than those of the typical Phillyrea angustifolia.

The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the spring. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds.

This subspecies is adapted to well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
