Perula rubiginosa

Perula rubiginosa, commonly known as the rusty perula, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in shrub form, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The rusty perula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
