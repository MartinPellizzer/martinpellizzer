Phyteuma orbiculare f. pilosum

Phyteuma orbiculare f. pilosum, commonly known as the hairy globe thistle, is a cultivar of the globe thistle species.

It is characterized by its spherical, blue to purple flower heads that are surrounded by a dense layer of bracts. The plant features grayish-green, hairy foliage that adds a distinctive texture to its appearance. It is a hardy perennial that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.

This cultivar is often grown in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
