Potentilla × moeszii f. porciusii

Potentilla × moeszii f. porciusii, commonly known as the Porcius rose.

This compact shrub is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and small, dark green leaves. It produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in early summer, adding a vibrant touch to gardens. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and thrives in well-drained soil.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
