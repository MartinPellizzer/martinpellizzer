Pilea lamiifolia

Pilea lamiifolia, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or pilea peperomioides, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Pilea lamiifolia is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.
