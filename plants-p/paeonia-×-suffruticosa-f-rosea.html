Paeonia × suffruticosa f. rosea

Paeonia × suffruticosa f. rosea, commonly known as the Rosea Tree Peony, is a hybrid species of peony native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, shrub-like form, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of pink to white, often with a distinct red or pink center. Its foliage consists of palmate, dark green leaves that are deeply lobed.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and is relatively hardy, thriving in well-drained soil and full sun.
