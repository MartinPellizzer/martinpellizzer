Pleurothallis longirostris

Pleurothallis longirostris, commonly known as the long-beaked orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive elongated lip. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrates.

Pleurothallis longirostris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
