Persicaria × intercedens

Persicaria × intercedens, commonly known as the hybrid Persicaria, is a perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, pinkish-red flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and wetland restoration projects.
