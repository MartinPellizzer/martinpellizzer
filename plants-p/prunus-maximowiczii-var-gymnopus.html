Prunus maximowiczii var. gymnopus

Prunus maximowiczii var. gymnopus, commonly known as the Chinese bird cherry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its ornamental value due to its showy white to pink flowers. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. Its fruits are small, drupes that turn red when mature, and it is often cultivated for its spring blooms.

It thrives in well-drained soils and partial shade to full sun, making it a popular choice in gardens.
