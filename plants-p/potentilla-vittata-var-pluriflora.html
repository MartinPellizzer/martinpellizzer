Potentilla vittata var. pluriflora

Potentilla vittata var. pluriflora, commonly known as the many-flowered cinquefoil, is a perennial shrub native to temperate regions of Asia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with five leaflets, giving it the common name "cinquefoil.". It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable addition to rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long flowering period.
