Piper gibsonii

Piper gibsonii, commonly known as the Gibson's pepper, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by elliptical, edible berries. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly serrated edge.

The plant is valued for its aromatic leaves and is often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.
