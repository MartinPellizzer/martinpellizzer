Phyteuma × orbiculariforme

Phyteuma × orbiculariforme, commonly known as the broad-leaved phyteuma, is a hybrid perennial plant native to Europe.

It is characterized by its large, showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from pale yellow to deep orange. The plant typically grows to a height of 40–60 cm and features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, loamy soil.

This species is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
