Piper dukei

Piper dukei, commonly known as the Duke's pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its fruit, which is a small, dark purple berry, contains the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.

Piper dukei is valued for its unique flavor and potential medicinal properties.
