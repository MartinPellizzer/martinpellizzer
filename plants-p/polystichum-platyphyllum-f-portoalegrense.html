Polystichum platyphyllum f. portoalegrense

Polystichum platyphyllum f. portoalegrense, commonly known as the Porto Alegre fern, is a unique fern species characterized by its dense, compact fronds.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is native to the humid forests of southern Brazil. The plant features lanceolate, dark green leaves with a smooth, leathery texture. Its fronds are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, forming a compact, upright clump.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden environments.
