Plumeria alba var. inodora

Plumeria alba var. inodora, commonly known as the White Plumeria, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, waxy flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant has simple, lance-shaped leaves and produces fruit that contains numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its flowers are typically used in floral arrangements and traditional ceremonies.
