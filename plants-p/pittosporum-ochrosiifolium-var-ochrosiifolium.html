Pittosporum ochrosiifolium var. ochrosiifolium

Pittosporum ochrosiifolium var. ochrosiifolium, commonly known as the golden-leaved pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically bright yellow to golden green in color, providing striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, fragrant white or yellow flowers, which are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

This variety is relatively drought-tolerant once established, making it suitable for coastal and garden landscapes.
