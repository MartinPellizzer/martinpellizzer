Prunus aureliana

Prunus aureliana, commonly known as the Aureliana cherry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Japan.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, often preceding the foliage. The plant has elliptical leaves that turn a striking red or orange in the autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its compact size and seasonal color changes make it a valuable addition to landscaping efforts.
