Polygala orbicularis

Polygala orbicularis, commonly known as the orbicular milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its orbicular (round) leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in dense spikes. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. It belongs to the family Polygalaceae, which includes a variety of herbs and shrubs.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
