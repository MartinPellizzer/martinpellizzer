Pholacilia diversifolia

Pholacilia diversifolia, commonly known as the diverse-leaf pholacilia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that vary in color from light green to deep emerald. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
