Pachyrhizus mollis

Pachyrhizus mollis, commonly known as the sweet potato vine, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and trailing growth habit, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are followed by edible tuberous roots. Its roots are rich in nutrients and are widely cultivated for food in many regions.

The species is also valued for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
