Pronephrium nothofageti

Pronephrium nothofageti, commonly known as the Tasmanian rainforest fern, is a species native to the cool temperate rainforests of Tasmania.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its lanceolate, dark green leaves with a smooth, waxy surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its reproductive structures are adapted to the moist, shaded environments where it thrives.
