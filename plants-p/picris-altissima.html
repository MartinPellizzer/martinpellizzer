Picris altissima

Picris altissima, commonly known as greater burnet, is a perennial herb native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It features a robust root system and upright, branched stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height. The plant is characterized by its dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom from summer to autumn. Its leaves are narrow, grass-like, and often covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland, grasslands, and along roadsides.
