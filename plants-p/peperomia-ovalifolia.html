Peperomia ovalifolia

Peperomia ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaf peperomia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or brown, which are followed by bright red or orange berries. It is a popular houseplant due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia ovalifolia is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments.
