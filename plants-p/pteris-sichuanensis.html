Pteris sichuanensis

Pteris sichuanensis, commonly known as the Sichuan water fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are creeping and help in the spread of the plant.

The fronds are often used in traditional medicine due to their purported health benefits.
