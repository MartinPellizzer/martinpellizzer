Piper coralfalgense

Piper coralfalgense, commonly known as coral falgense pepper, is a climbing shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in spikes. Its fruits are berries that turn red when mature, containing aromatic seeds.

The botanical features of Piper coralfalgense make it a unique and interesting subject for botanical study.
