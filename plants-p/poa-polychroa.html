Poa polychroa

Poa polychroa, commonly known as the rainbow grass, is a species of grass native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multi-colored foliage that ranges from blue-green to purple and silver, creating a striking visual effect. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and meadows. Its slender, tufted growth habit and delicate inflorescence make it a distinctive feature in its native habitat.

Poa polychroa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
