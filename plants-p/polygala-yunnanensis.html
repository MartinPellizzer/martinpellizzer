Polygala yunnanensis

Polygala yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous regions, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, with intricate structures that attract specific pollinators.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
