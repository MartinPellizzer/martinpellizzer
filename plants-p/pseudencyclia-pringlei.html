Pseudencyclia pringlei

This species is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and a crown of large, pinnate leaves that can reach up to two meters in length. Pseudencyclia pringlei is a dioecious plant, meaning it has separate male and female individuals, and it produces cones instead of flowers.
