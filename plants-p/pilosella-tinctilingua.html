Pilosella tinctilingua

Pilosella tinctilingua, commonly known as the purple yarrow, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and clusters of small, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to purple. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this plant thrives in sunny, well-drained soils. It is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and disturbed areas.

Pilosella tinctilingua is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
