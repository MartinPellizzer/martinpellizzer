Plantago montisdicksonii

Plantago montisdicksonii, commonly known as the Montis Dickson's plantain, is a species of flowering plant in the family Plantaginaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Mexico, where it thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. The plant features rosettes of broad, lance-shaped leaves covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, greenish flowers in dense clusters. It is adapted to high-altitude environments and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
