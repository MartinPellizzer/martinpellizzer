Prunella × beguinotii

Prunella × beguinotii, commonly known as the hybrid selfheal, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the mint family, Lamiaceae.

It is a natural hybrid between Prunella vulgaris and Prunella lacryssa, characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces spikes of blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges and meadows.

Prunella × beguinotii is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional use in herbal medicine.
