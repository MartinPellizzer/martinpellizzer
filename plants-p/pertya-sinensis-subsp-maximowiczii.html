Pertya sinensis subsp. maximowiczii

Pertya sinensis subsp.

maximowiczii, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia. It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are borne in clusters. It is often cultivated for its ornamental foliage rather than its flowers.

It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
