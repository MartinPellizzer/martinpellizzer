Potentilla shockleyi var. shockleyi

Potentilla shockleyi var. shockleyi, commonly known as the Shockley's cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring and summer months. Its leaves are usually narrow and have a rough, hairy texture.

It is well adapted to drought conditions and thrives in well-drained soils.
