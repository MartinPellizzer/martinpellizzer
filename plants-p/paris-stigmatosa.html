Paris stigmatosa

Paris stigmatosa, commonly known as the Chinese lily or the white lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and moths.
