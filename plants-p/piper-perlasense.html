Piper perlasense

Piper perlasense, commonly known as the pearl vine, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of flowering plants known for their aromatic properties. The plant is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, white to pale green flowers. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly glossy surface.

Piper perlasense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
