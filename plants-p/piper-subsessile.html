Piper subsessile

Piper subsessile, commonly known as the slender pepper vine, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various species of pepper plants. The plant features small, opposite leaves that are usually ovate to elliptical in shape. Its flowers are unisexual, with both male and female flowers occurring on the same plant.

The fruit is a small, three-lobed berry that ripens to a dark purple color.
