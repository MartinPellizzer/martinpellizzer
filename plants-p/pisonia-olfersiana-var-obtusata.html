Pisonia olfersiana var. obtusata

Pisonia olfersiana var. obtusata, commonly known as the broad-leaved pisonia, is a flowering plant native to the Indo-Pacific region.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, attracting various pollinators. The leaves are broad and ovate, with a smooth surface and a slightly waxy texture. This species thrives in coastal areas and is often found in tropical and subtropical climates.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual display.
