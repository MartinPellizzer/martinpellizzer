Parnassia caroliniana

It belongs to the family Ranunculaceae and is characterized by its distinctive white, star-shaped flowers that resemble a crown. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wetlands. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three leaflets that are usually deeply lobed.
