Primula minutissima

Primula minutissima, commonly known as the dwarf primrose, is a small flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It is characterized by its tiny, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows to a height of less than 10 centimeters, with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

Due to its delicate appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
