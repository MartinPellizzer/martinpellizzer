Phalaenopsis parishii var. lobbii

Phalaenopsis parishii var. lobbii, commonly known as the Lobb's phalaenopsis, is a rare and highly sought-after orchid native to the mountainous regions of Borneo.

It is characterized by its elegant, long-lasting flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and relatively slow growth rate compared to other phalaenopsis species. It prefers bright, indirect light and consistent humidity, making it a challenging but rewarding plant for experienced growers.

The plant's unique floral patterns and rarity make it a prized specimen in the orchid world.
