Polypodium pecten

Polypodium pecten, commonly known as the feather fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that resemble a bird's wing, giving it its common name. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, Africa, and the Americas. Its rhizome is creeping and often covered with a thick, fibrous layer.

Polypodium pecten thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
