Pilea magambensis

Pilea magambensis, commonly known as the African velvet plant, is a tropical shrub native to eastern Africa.

It features palmate leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green coloration. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
