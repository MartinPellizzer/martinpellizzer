Polypodium streptophyllum

Polypodium streptophyllum, commonly known as the zigzag huperzia, is a fern-like plant belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzag-shaped leaves that are often folded or twisted. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. It has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread and form dense mats.
