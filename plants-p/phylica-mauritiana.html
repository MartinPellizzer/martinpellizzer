Phylica mauritiana

Phylica mauritiana, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or semi-arid regions.

Phylica mauritiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
