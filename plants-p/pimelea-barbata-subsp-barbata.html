Pimelea barbata subsp. barbata

Pimelea barbata subsp.

barbata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved geebung, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia. It is a shrub characterized by its slender, often spindly stems and narrow, linear leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and coastal areas.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
