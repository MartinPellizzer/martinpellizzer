Prenanthes altissima f. integra

Prenanthes altissima f. integra, commonly known as the tall arrowhead, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves that are typically borne in a rosette at the base of the plant. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, rich soils and is often found in wetlands or along the edges of ponds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting local wildlife.
