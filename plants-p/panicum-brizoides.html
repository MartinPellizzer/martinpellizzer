Panicum brizoides

Panicum brizoides, commonly known as slender panicgrass, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

Panicum brizoides plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
