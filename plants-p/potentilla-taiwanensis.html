Potentilla taiwanensis

Potentilla taiwanensis, commonly known as the Taiwan cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically compound, with five leaflets, giving it the common name "cinquefoil.".

Potentilla taiwanensis is often found in rocky or mountainous regions, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
