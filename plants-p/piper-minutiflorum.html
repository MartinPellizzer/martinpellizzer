Piper minutiflorum

Piper minutiflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers and glossy, dark green leaves. It produces small, peppery fruits that are used in traditional medicine and as a spice.

Piper minutiflorum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.
