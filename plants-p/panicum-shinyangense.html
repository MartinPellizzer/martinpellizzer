Panicum shinyangense

Panicum shinyangense, commonly known as the Shinyang grass, is a species of grass native to China.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit. The plant features narrow, linear leaves with a smooth, shiny surface, giving it its common name. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

This species is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks.
