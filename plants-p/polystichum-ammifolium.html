Polystichum ammifolium

Polystichum ammifolium, commonly known as the sandstone fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery fronds that are typically divided into narrow, lance-shaped segments. This plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils, often found in mountainous or coastal regions. The fronds are evergreen, providing year-round structure and texture in its native habitat.

It is well adapted to harsh environments, making it a resilient and hardy species.
