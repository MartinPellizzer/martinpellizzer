Protea ramosa

Protea ramosa, commonly known as the red-stemmed protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, branched stems and showy, inflorescence-like structures composed of numerous small flowers. The plant produces vibrant pink to red bracts that surround the true flowers, which are small and clustered in the center. Protea ramosa thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in supporting local wildlife.
