Paspalum scrobiculatum var. orbiculare

Paspalum scrobiculatum var. orbiculare, commonly known as the orbiculate paspalum, is a grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, pale-green flowers. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability in landscaping.
