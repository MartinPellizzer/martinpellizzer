Pleurothallis fossulata

Pleurothallis fossulata, commonly known as the fissured pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. The plant grows as an epiphyte, often found on trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually elongated and leathery, adapted to retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Pleurothallis fossulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
