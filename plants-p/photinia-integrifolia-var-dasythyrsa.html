Photinia integrifolia var. dasythyrsa

Photinia integrifolia var. dasythyrsa, commonly known as the glossy leaf photinia, is a shrub or small tree native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green, glossy leaves that are typically entire, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, followed by red or orange berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.

The varietal form dasythyrsa is distinguished by its more robust growth and thicker foliage compared to the typical form.
