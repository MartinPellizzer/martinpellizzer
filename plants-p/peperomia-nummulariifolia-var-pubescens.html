Peperomia nummulariifolia var. pubescens

Peperomia nummulariifolia var. pubescens, commonly known as the velvety peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for plant enthusiasts.
