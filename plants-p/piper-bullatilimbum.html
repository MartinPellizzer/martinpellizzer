Piper bullatilimbum

Piper bullatilimbum, commonly known as the bullate pepper vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, clustered berries that are typically green when immature and turn red when mature. Its leaves are heart-shaped with a rough texture and emit a pungent odor.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional remedies for various ailments.
