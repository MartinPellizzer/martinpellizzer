Polystachya albescens subsp. albescens

Polystachya albescens subsp.

albescens, commonly known as the white-flowered torch orchid, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae). It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil.

This plant is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, white or pale yellow flowers that resemble torches. The leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged along the stem.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.
