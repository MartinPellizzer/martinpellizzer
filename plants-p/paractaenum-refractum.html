Paractaenum refractum

Paractaenum refractum, commonly known as the translucent leaf plant, is a unique species characterized by its semi-transparent foliage.

Its leaves exhibit a delicate, waxy texture that allows light to pass through, creating an ethereal appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical rainforests. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are pollinated by specific insects.

Due to its rarity and unusual characteristics, it is a subject of interest in botanical research.
