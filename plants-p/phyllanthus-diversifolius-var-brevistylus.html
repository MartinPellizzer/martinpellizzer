Phyllanthus diversifolius var. brevistylus

Phyllanthus diversifolius var. brevistylus, commonly known as the short-styled phyllanthus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Kenya and Tanzania, where it grows in woodland and savanna habitats. The plant is characterized by its alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves with prominent venation. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces capsule fruits containing numerous tiny seeds.

This plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
