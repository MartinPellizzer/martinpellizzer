Polypodium tosaense

Polypodium tosaense, commonly known as Tosa polypody, is a species of fern native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizome is often covered in a dense layer of brownish, hairy scales.

Polypodium tosaense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
