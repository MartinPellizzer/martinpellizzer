Pinanga macrospadix

Pinanga macrospadix, commonly known as the giant bamboo palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, feather-like leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces striking, spadix-shaped inflorescences that are often surrounded by colorful bracts. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a striking addition to both natural and cultivated landscapes.
