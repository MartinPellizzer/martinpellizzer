Phreatia densiflora var. vietnamensis

Phreatia densiflora var. vietnamensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese dense-flowered orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species found primarily in the mountainous regions of Vietnam.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, clustered inflorescences. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in various shades of white and pale pink, which bloom during the wet season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically in secondary forests or along stream banks.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
