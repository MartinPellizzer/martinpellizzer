Pourthiaea villosa var. laevis

Pourthiaea villosa var. laevis, commonly known as the smooth porthiaea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of soft hairs. This variant differs from the typical variety by its smoother leaf surface and reduced hairiness.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.
