Pimelea brevifolia subsp. modesta

Pimelea brevifolia subsp.

modesta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved riceflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia. It is a shrub with narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellow flowers in late summer.
