Piper brachypodon var. hirsuticaule

Piper brachypodon var. hirsuticaule, commonly known as the hairy-stemmed pepper plant, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into tiny, peppery fruits.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and mild peppery flavor, making it suitable for both garden cultivation and culinary use.
