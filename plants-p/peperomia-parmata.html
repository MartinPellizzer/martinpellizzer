Peperomia parmata

Peperomia parmata, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to South America.

It features succulent, ovate leaves that are typically silver or gray-green in color, with a waxy texture that helps it retain moisture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that are usually hidden beneath its foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia parmata is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and occasional pruning to maintain its compact form.
