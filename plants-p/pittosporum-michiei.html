Pittosporum michiei

Pittosporum michiei, commonly known as the Chinese pittosporum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and have a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers in spring, which are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for ornamental gardens.

The species is valued for its attractive foliage and seasonal flowering.
