Paphiopedilum villosum var. gratrixianum

Paphiopedilum villosum var. gratrixianum, commonly known as the Gratrixian velvet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves and produces fragrant, yellowish-brown flowers with a distinctive lip. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral structure.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered in some regions.
