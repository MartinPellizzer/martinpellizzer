Polygala nicaeensis subsp. carniolica

Polygala nicaeensis subsp.

carniolica, commonly known as the Carniolan milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula. It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, herbaceous stems and small, clustered flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid conditions. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, and it produces small, elongated pods that contain the seeds.

This subspecies is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological niche.
