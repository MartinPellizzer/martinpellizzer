Panicum bergii

Panicum bergii, commonly known as Berg's panicgrass, is a species of grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically green or brown in color. It thrives in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in restoration projects and as a forage crop.
