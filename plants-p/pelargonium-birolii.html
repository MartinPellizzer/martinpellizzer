Pelargonium birolii

Pelargonium birolii, commonly known as the Birolii geranium, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a fragrant scent, which is a common trait among pelargonium species. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance, Pelargonium birolii is often cultivated by gardeners in temperate climates.
