Pterocarpus nobilis

Pterocarpus nobilis, commonly known as Indian rosewood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, dense wood that is highly valued for its strength and aesthetic appeal. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by flat, pod-like fruits containing several seeds. Its leaves are alternate, compound, and have a feathery appearance.

The tree is also known for its medicinal properties, with various parts used in traditional medicine.
