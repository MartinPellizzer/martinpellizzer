Phyllanthus rivae

Phyllanthus rivae, commonly known as the river phyllanthus, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils near water sources such as rivers and streams. The plant features small, elliptical leaves with a smooth, glossy surface and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. Its fruit is a small, spherical capsule containing several seeds.

Phyllanthus rivae is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
