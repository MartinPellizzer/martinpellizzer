Petunia × meleagris

Petunia × meleagris, commonly known as the African violet, is a hybrid plant that combines traits from petunias and meleagris species.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Violaceae family, characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers and dense, velvety foliage. The plant is native to tropical regions and thrives in warm, humid climates with well-draining soil. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.

Petunia × meleagris requires indirect sunlight and regular watering to maintain its health and blooming cycle.
