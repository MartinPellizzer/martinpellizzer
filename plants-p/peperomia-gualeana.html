Peperomia gualeana

Peperomia gualeana, commonly known as the Gualean peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit. The plant features thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval to heart-shaped, with a glossy surface. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish or white in color.

Peperomia gualeana is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.
