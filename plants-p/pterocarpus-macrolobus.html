Pterocarpus macrolobus

Pterocarpus macrolobus, commonly known as the African桃花心木, is a tropical tree native to Africa, particularly found in countries like Ghana and Nigeria.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and produces distinctive yellow flowers. The tree is valued for its dense, durable wood, which is highly sought after in the furniture and construction industries. Its fruit is a hard, woody pod that contains several seeds.

Due to overharvesting and habitat loss, Pterocarpus macrolobus is considered an endangered species.
