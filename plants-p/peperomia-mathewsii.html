Peperomia mathewsii

Peperomia mathewsii, commonly known as the Mathews' Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which are followed by elongated, spiny fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and attractive foliage, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant.
