Piper chuvya

Piper chuvya, commonly known as the Chuvya pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant produces small, elongated fruits that are typically harvested when they turn red or yellow. The leaves are heart-shaped and have a peppery aroma, similar to other members of the pepper family.

It is valued both for its culinary use and its ornamental appeal in gardens.
