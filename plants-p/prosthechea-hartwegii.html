Prosthechea hartwegii

Prosthechea hartwegii, commonly known as the Hartweg's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air.

Prosthechea hartwegii is noted for its resilience and adaptability to various ecological conditions.
