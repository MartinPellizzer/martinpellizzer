Peperomia rubramenta

Peperomia rubramenta, commonly known as the red-veined peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves with striking red veins against a deep green background. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its ornamental foliage, it is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal rather than its flowers.
