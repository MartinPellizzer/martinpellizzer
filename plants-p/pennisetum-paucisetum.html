Pennisetum paucisetum

Pennisetum paucisetum, commonly known as the narrowleaf fountain grass, is a species of grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often grayish-green in color. The plant produces fluffy, pinkish-white seed heads that are highly ornamental and attract birds and insects. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Pennisetum paucisetum is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in a variety of climates.
