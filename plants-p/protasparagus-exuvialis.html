Protasparagus exuvialis

Protasparagus exuvialis, commonly known as the desert asparagus, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa.

Protasparagus exuvialis is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ability to thrive in harsh conditions.
