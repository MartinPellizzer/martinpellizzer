Piper addisonii

Piper addisonii, commonly known as Addison's pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the rainforests of Queensland, Australia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of tiny, greenish-yellow flowers. Its fruit is a small, capsule-like structure that contains several seeds.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Piper addisonii is considered a rare and endangered species.
