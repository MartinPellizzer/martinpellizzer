Pavonia Arenaria

Pavonia arenaria is a hardy annual plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern united states. it thrives in sandy and well-drained soils, often found in coastal and desert environments. its small, showy flowers range in color from pink to red, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. despite its delicate appearance, it is highly resilient to drought and heat. this plant plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems.
