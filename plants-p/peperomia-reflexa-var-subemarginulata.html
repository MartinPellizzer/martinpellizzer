Peperomia reflexa var. subemarginulata

Peperomia reflexa var. subemarginulata, commonly known as the margin-leaf peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with a distinctive wavy margin, contributing to its ornamental appeal. The plant has small, inconspicuous flowers that appear in clusters, typically in shades of white or green. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique leaf shape and ease of care make it a popular choice for plant enthusiasts.
