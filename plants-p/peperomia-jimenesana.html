Peperomia jimenesana

Peperomia jimenesana, commonly known as the Jimenez's peperomia, is a species native to South America, particularly found in Brazil.

It is a compact, low-growing plant with thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in hue. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and ease of care have made it a favored plant among succulent enthusiasts.
