Premna schimperi

Premna schimperi, commonly known as the African nettle or Ethiopian nettle, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia and surrounding regions.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters tall, characterized by opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, three-lobed fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The leaves are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
