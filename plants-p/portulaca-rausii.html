Portulaca rausii

Portulaca rausii, commonly known as the Indian moss or carpetweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming annual that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. The plant produces small, vibrant yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its succulent leaves are fleshy and often covered in a fine, powdery coating.

Portulaca rausii is often used in ornamental gardening for its drought tolerance and aesthetic appeal.
