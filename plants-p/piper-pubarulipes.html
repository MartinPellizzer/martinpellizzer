Piper pubarulipes

Piper pubarulipes, commonly known as the hairy pipevine, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and dark green, heart-shaped leaves covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, typically arranged in clusters.

Piper pubarulipes is often found in humid, shaded environments such as rainforests.
