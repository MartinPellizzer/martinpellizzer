Piper stellatum

Piper stellatum, commonly known as star pepper or star anise pepper, is a tropical climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. Its fruits are small, berry-like structures that mature to a dark purple or black color.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and for the mild peppery flavor of its fruits.
