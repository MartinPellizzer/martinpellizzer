Paphiopedilum Ciliolare Var. Miteauanum

Miteauanum paphiopedilum, scientifically known as Paphiopedilum ciliolare var. miteauanum, is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. This unique variant of the ciliolare orchid is distinguished by its velvety petals and intricate floral structure. Its name honors the region where it was first discovered, highlighting its geographical significance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific care to flourish. Due to its rarity and beauty, it has become a sought-after specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
