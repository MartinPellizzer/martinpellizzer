Phymatosorus Parksii

Phymatosorus parksii is a rare and enigmatic fern native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. This species is known for its unique morphology and limited geographic distribution. It thrives in cool, humid environments, often found in shaded forest understories. Its delicate fronds and distinctive spore structures make it a subject of scientific interest. Despite its beauty, Phymatosorus parksii remains poorly understood and critically endangered.
