Photinia glabra var. fokienensis

Photinia glabra var. fokienensis, commonly known as the Fokienensis Photinia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features glossy, dark green foliage that provides a striking visual contrast against its showy, pink to red flowers. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible for birds but not for humans. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden conditions.
