Pancratium barcinonense

Pancratium barcinonense, commonly known as the Cape of Good Hope lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange. The plant blooms in spring and is adapted to grow in coastal environments with well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique addition to Mediterranean and subtropical gardens.
