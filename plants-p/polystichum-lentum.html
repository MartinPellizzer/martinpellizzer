Polystichum lentum

Polystichum lentum, commonly known as the broad-leafed wood fern, is a perennial fern native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It features a robust rhizome and produces large, bipinnate fronds that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The fronds are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, with a distinct, elongated shape. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in horticulture for its aesthetic and ecological benefits.
