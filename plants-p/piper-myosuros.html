Piper myosuros

Piper myosuros, commonly known as the mouse-ear pepper or sacred pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the Piper genus in the family Piperaceae, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of tiny, greenish-white flowers. Its fruit is a small, dark purple berry that contains a single seed.

Piper myosuros is often cultivated for its aromatic leaves and traditional medicinal uses.
