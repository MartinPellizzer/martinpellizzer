Palhinhaea steyermarkii

Palhinhaea steyermarkii, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant with a sympodial growth habit, characterized by its rhizomatous structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered threatened and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
