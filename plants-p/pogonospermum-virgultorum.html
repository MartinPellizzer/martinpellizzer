Pogonospermum virgultorum

Pogonospermum virgultorum, commonly known as the featherleaf spurge, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring.
