Pittosporum spathulatum

Pittosporum spathulatum, commonly known as the broad-leaf pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its broad, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces fragrant, white or cream-colored flowers that bloom in spring. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms.

Pittosporum spathulatum prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
