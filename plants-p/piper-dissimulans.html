Piper dissimulans

Piper dissimulans, commonly known as the hidden pepper vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are often hidden among its dense foliage. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn red when mature.

Piper dissimulans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance and potential medicinal properties.
