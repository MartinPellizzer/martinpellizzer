Prasophyllum viretrum

Prasophyllum viretrum, commonly known as the green leek orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in open heathlands and woodland areas, often in well-drained soils. The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces a spike of greenish-white flowers. It blooms during the late winter to early spring season.

Prasophyllum viretrum is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in coastal regions.
