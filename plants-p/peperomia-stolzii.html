Peperomia stolzii

Peperomia stolzii, commonly known as the Stolz's pepper plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, followed by spore-bearing structures called sporangia. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate moisture.

Peperomia stolzii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental foliage and low maintenance requirements.
