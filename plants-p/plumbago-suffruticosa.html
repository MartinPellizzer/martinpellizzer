Plumbago suffruticosa

Plumbago suffruticosa, commonly known as blue plumbago, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring woody stems and dense foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, bright blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The plant is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.
