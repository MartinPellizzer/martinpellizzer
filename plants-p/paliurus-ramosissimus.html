Paliurus ramosissimus

Paliurus ramosissimus, commonly known as the Chinese whitethorn, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny branches and small, pale yellow flowers that appear in clusters. The plant produces small, red, fleshy fruits that are edible but often have a tart taste. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky areas.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in horticulture and traditional medicine.
