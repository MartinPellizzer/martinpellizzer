Pelargonium patulum var. grandiflorum

Pelargonium patulum var. grandiflorum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is typically dark green and velvety, adding to its ornamental appeal.

It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its adaptability and aesthetic value.
