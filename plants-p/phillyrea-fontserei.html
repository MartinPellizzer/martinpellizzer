Phillyrea fontserei

Phillyrea fontserei, commonly known as the Spanish Phillyrea, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features narrow, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and have a glossy appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by small, spherical fruits. It is adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, making it suitable for coastal and arid environments.

Phillyrea fontserei is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
