Poa novae-zelandiae

Poa novae-zelandiae, commonly known as New Zealand grass, is a species of grass native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences with tiny flowers.

Poa novae-zelandiae is well adapted to the temperate climate of New Zealand and plays a role in local ecosystems.
