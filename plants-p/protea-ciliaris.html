Protea ciliaris

Protea ciliaris, commonly known as the silver protea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant features a tall, woody stem with large, leathery leaves that are often covered in a silvery or white powdery coating. Its flowers are typically pink or red, with a distinctive, brush-like appearance.

Protea ciliaris is well adapted to nutrient-poor soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.
