Pittosporum tonkinense

Pittosporum tonkinense, commonly known as the Tonkin pittosporum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pittosporaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam and southern China. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage, which is glossy and dark green, and its fragrant, white to cream-colored flowers. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several small, elliptical seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
