Pavia mollis

Pavia mollis, commonly known as the soft pavia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial shade. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a soft, velvety texture.

Pavia mollis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
