Pimelodendron amboinicum

Pimelodendron amboinicum, commonly known as the Amboina pimelodendron, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its woody stem and dense, evergreen foliage. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in lowland forests. Its leaves are elongated and have a glossy surface, while its flowers are small and clustered.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
