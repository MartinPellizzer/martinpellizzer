Polypodium persimile

Polypodium persimile, commonly known as the slender cliff fern, is a species of fern found in tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows on rocky surfaces or in shaded, moist environments. Its rhizomes are often covered in a layer of sori, which are clusters of spore-producing structures.

Polypodium persimile is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
