Piper densespicatum

Piper densespicatum, commonly known as the dense-hair pepper or thick-hair pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its dense pubescent leaves and small, clustered flowers. It produces small, greenish-yellow fruits that are often used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for studies in plant morphology and ecological adaptation.
