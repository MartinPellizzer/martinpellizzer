Platystele jungermannioides

Platystele jungermannioides, commonly known as the narrow-leafed pygmy orchid, is a small, epiphytic orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its delicate, strap-like leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, deriving moisture and nutrients from the air and rain. Its root system is specialized for absorbing water from the humid environment, and it thrives in shaded, moist conditions.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
