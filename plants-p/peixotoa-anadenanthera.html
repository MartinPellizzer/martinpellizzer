Peixotoa anadenanthera

Peixotoa anadenanthera, commonly known as the sacred tree or copaiba, is a large tropical tree native to South America, particularly Brazil.

The tree produces yellowish flowers and pods that contain seeds surrounded by a fibrous, spongy material. It is valued for its resin, which has been traditionally used for medicinal and ritual purposes.
