Ptilotus obovatus var. lancifolius

Ptilotus obovatus var. lancifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spinifex, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well-suited for xeriscaping and coastal gardens.
