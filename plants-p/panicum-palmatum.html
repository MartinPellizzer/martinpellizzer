Panicum palmatum

Panicum palmatum, commonly known as Japanese bloodgrass, is a striking ornamental grass native to Japan.

It features slender, arching blades with a distinctive reddish-pink hue that becomes more pronounced in the fall. The plant produces delicate, feathery plumes that add movement and texture to gardens. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Panicum palmatum is low maintenance and drought-tolerant, making it a popular choice for landscaping.
