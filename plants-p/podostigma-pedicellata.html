Podostigma pedicellata

Podostigma pedicellata, commonly known as the slender podostigma, is a rare aquatic plant found in slow-moving freshwater habitats.

It is characterized by its unique root system that anchors it to submerged rocks. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. Its leaves are narrow and ribbon-like, adapted for efficient water flow.
