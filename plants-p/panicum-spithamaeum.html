Panicum spithamaeum

Panicum spithamaeum, commonly known as the silver panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and slender, erect growth habit. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping and drought-tolerant gardens. Its small, spike-like inflorescences produce light green to golden seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Panicum spithamaeum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.
