Piper brewsteri

Piper brewsteri, commonly known as Brewster's pepper vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when mature, containing aromatic seeds. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and used in traditional medicine in some regions.
