Photinia bartlettii

Photinia bartlettii, commonly known as the Chinese firethorn, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit and showy red berries. The plant produces small white flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red fruits that persist into winter. Its foliage is typically glossy and dark green, providing ornamental value in gardens.

It is often used as a hedge or screens due to its dense growth and thorny stems.
