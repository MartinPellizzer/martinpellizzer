Peperomia spathophylla var. monteverdensis

Peperomia spathophylla var. monteverdensis, commonly known as the Monteverde peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the cloud forests of Costa Rica.

It features broad, waxy, dark green leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns of silver or white veins. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or red, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to indoor growing conditions.
