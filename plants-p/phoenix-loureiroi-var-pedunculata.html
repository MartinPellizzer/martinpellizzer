Phoenix loureiroi var. pedunculata

Phoenix loureiroi var. pedunculata, commonly known as the Chinese fan palm, is a distinctive species native to southern China.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to 2 meters in diameter, giving it a striking visual presence. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers that bloom in panicles, followed by edible fruits resembling small dates. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively cold-hardy compared to other palms.
