Peperomia tenuicaulis

Peperomia tenuicaulis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green, narrow leaves that are often slightly curled at the edges. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of pink or white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia tenuicaulis is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
