Peperomia nandalana

Peperomia nandalana, commonly known as the Nandalana peperomia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated in color. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in hue.

Peperomia nandalana is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
