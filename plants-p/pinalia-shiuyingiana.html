Pinalia shiuyingiana

Pinalia shiuyingiana, commonly known as the Shuiying mountain pine, is a rare and endemic species native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It is a coniferous evergreen tree characterized by its slender, upward-curving cones and needle-like leaves. The plant thrives in cool, humid climates and is often found in forested areas with well-drained soil. Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and limited distribution, making it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat loss, Pinalia shiuyingiana is considered vulnerable and requires protection to ensure its survival.
