Piper yui

Piper yui, commonly known as the Yui pepper or Yui long pepper, is a climbing shrub native to the humid forests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various pepper species. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are used in traditional medicine and culinary applications. Its leaves are often used as a spice, while the fruits are harvested when mature for their aromatic properties.

The plant thrives in warm, moist environments and requires a support structure for its climbing growth habit.
