Perovskia angustifolia

Perovskia angustifolia, commonly known as Russian sage, is a perennial herb native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and spikes of blue-lilac flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a square, woody stem and is often used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
