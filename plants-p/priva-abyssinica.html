Priva abyssinica

Priva abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian privet, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in moist, fertile soils. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and have a slightly rough texture.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.
