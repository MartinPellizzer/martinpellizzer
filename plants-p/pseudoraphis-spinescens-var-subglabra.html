Pseudoraphis spinescens var. subglabra

Pseudoraphis spinescens var. subglabra, commonly known as the smooth spiny grass, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and compact growth habit, forming low, mat-like clusters. The leaves are typically gray-green in color, with reduced or absent pubescence compared to other varieties of Pseudoraphis. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

This variety is prized for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
