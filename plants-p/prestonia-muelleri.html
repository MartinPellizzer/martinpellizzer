Prestonia muelleri

Prestonia muelleri, commonly known as the Mueller's prestonia, is a climbing plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The flowers are typically showy, with five petals and a distinctive corona, often in shades of yellow or orange. It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, flat seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
