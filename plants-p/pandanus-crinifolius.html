Pandanus crinifolius

Pandanus crinifolius, commonly known as the bottlebrush pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces striking, bottlebrush-like inflorescences that are both ornamental and used in cultural practices. Its leaves are fibrous and can be woven into mats and baskets.

Pandanus crinifolius thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to salty air and sandy soils.
