Peperomia menkeana

Peperomia menkeana, commonly known as the Menke's peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy coating. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white in color.

Peperomia menkeana is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.
