Pleurothallis gracilicolumna

Pleurothallis gracilicolumna, commonly known as the slender column pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features elongated, narrow pseudobulbs and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted for efficient water absorption in its natural habitat.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
