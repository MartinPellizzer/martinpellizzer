Platystachys patens

Platystachys patens, commonly known as the creeping indigofera, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is a low-growing perennial that features slender, trailing stems and small, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces delicate, pale blue to purple flowers in racemes, which bloom in the summer months. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
