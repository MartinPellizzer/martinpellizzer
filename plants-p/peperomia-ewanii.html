Peperomia ewanii

Peperomia ewanii, commonly known as the Ewan's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia ewanii is relatively low maintenance and drought-tolerant, suitable for both beginners and experienced plant enthusiasts.
