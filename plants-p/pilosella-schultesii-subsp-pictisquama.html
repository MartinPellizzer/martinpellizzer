Pilosella schultesii subsp. pictisquama

Pilosella schultesii subsp.

pictisquama, commonly known as the painted pincushion, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and showy yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

It is often found in mountainous areas of Europe and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
