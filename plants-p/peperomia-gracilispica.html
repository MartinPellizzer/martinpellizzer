Peperomia gracilispica

Peperomia gracilispica, commonly known as the slender-spiked peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features upright, branching stems covered in small, glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy texture. The plant produces delicate, spiky inflorescences that bear tiny, pale green to white flowers, which are characteristic of the peperomia genus. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive foliage, it is often used as a decorative plant in both home and office settings.
