Pyrethrum elegans

Pyrethrum elegans, commonly known as the elegant pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a central yellow disk. The plant produces fine, hair-like structures called phytoceramides, which contribute to its natural insecticidal properties. Pyrethrum elegans thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its botanical features make it both aesthetically pleasing and useful in organic pest control.
