Panicum pampinosum

Panicum pampinosum, commonly known as pampas grass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, arching stems and fluffy, silver-white plumes that appear in late summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a rough texture, and it can reach heights of up to 2 meters.

Panicum pampinosum is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its striking appearance and resilience.
