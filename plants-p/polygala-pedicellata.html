Polygala pedicellata

Polygala pedicellata, commonly known as the slender milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, often pink or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or stream banks. Its leaves are usually small and opposite, and the flowers are clustered in racemes.

Polygala pedicellata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
