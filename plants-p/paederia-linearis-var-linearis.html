Paederia linearis var. linearis

Paederia linearis var. linearis, commonly known as the slender paederia, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features slender, ascending stems that can reach lengths of several meters, often twining around supports. The leaves are alternate, simple, and lanceolate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its small, greenish-white flowers are typically arranged in clusters and are followed by small, round fruits.

This plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and along stream banks.
