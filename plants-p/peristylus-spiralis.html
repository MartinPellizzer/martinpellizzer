Peristylus spiralis

Peristylus spiralis, commonly known as the spiral peristyle, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves around the stem, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Peristylus spiralis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a garden setting.
