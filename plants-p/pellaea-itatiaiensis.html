Pellaea itatiaiensis

Pellaea itatiaiensis, commonly known as the Itatiaia spleenwort, is a species of fern native to South America.

It belongs to the family Pellaeaceae and is characterized by its small, delicate fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often on rocky substrates. Its spore-bearing structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Pellaea itatiaiensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
