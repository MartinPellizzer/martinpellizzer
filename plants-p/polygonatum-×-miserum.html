Polygonatum × miserum

Polygonatum × miserum, commonly known as the Chinese Solomon's seal, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asparagaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, rhizomatous stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a whorled pattern along the stem.

Polygonatum × miserum is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
