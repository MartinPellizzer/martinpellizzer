Peperomia klopfensteinii

Peperomia klopfensteinii, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia klopfensteinii is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
