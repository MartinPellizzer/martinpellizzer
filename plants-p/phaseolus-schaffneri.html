Phaseolus schaffneri

Phaseolus schaffneri, commonly known as the scarlet runner bean, is a climbing legume native to the Americas.

It produces striking red or purple flowers and edible bean pods that mature into yellow or brown seeds. The plant has compound leaves with oval-shaped leaflets and is often cultivated for both its ornamental value and edible beans. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Phaseolus schaffneri is a member of the Fabaceae family, which includes many other leguminous plants.
