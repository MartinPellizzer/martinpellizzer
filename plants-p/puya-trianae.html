Puya trianae

The plant produces an inflorescence of bright red flowers that bloom only once in its lifetime, typically after several years of growth. Its unique structure and vibrant blooms make it a popular subject in botanical studies and horticulture.

Puya trianae is also notable for its ability to survive in extreme environmental conditions, including high altitudes and cold temperatures.
