Pyrus baccata var. oblonga

Pyrus baccata var. oblonga, commonly known as the Chinese white pear, is a variety of the common pear tree.

It is characterized by its small, white flowers and oval-shaped fruits that are typically harvested in late summer. The leaves are glossy and dark green, providing ornamental value throughout the growing season. This plant is native to China and is often cultivated for its fruit and aesthetic appeal.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sunlight for optimal growth.
