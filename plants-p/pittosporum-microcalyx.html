Pittosporum microcalyx

Pittosporum microcalyx, commonly known as the small-fruited pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers a sunny to partially shaded location.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping.
