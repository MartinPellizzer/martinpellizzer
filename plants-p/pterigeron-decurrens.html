Pterigeron decurrens

Pterigeron decurrens, commonly known as the creeping pteris, is a fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features delicate, linear fronds that often trail along the ground, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth. Its rhizomes spread horizontally, allowing it to colonize areas efficiently.

The leaves are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, contributing to its ornamental value.
