Plectranthus densiflorus

Plectranthus densiflorus, commonly known as the Cape verbena, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, bright green foliage and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from spring to autumn.
