Premna alstonii

Premna alstonii, commonly known as Alston's premna, is a shrub or small tree native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Verbenaceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers arranged in clusters, followed by oval, woody fruits. It is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forest understories.
