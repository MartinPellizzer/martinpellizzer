Peperomia moreliana

Peperomia moreliana, commonly known as the Shiny Leaf Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, often in shades of green or red. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia moreliana is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
