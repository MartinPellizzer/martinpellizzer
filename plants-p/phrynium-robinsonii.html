Phrynium robinsonii

Phrynium robinsonii, commonly known as the Robinson's phrynium, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and greenhouses.
