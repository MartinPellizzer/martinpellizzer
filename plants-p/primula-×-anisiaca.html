Primula × anisiaca

Primula × anisiaca, commonly known as the Japanese primrose, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Primulaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of pink, white, and yellow. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and it thrives in cool, moist environments. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and floral arrangements.

The species is native to East Asia, including Japan and China.
