Polypodium pleurosorum

Polypodium pleurosorum, commonly known as the broad-leafed wall fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and fronds that are typically bi- or tri-pinnate. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found on rocky or wooded areas. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds in sporangia clustered in sori.

The botanical features of Polypodium pleurosorum make it a distinctive and ecologically significant species in its native habitats.
