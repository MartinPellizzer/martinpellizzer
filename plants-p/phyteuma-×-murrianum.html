Phyteuma × murrianum

Phyteuma × murrianum, commonly known as the hybrid garden phyteuma, is a flowering plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and prefers well-drained soil in full sun to partial shade.
