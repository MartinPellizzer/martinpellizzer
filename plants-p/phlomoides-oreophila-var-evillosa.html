Phlomoides oreophila var. evillosa

Phlomoides oreophila var. evillosa, commonly known as the mountain phlomoides, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from pale blue to violet. It typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils in mountainous areas.
