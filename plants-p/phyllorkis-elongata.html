Phyllorkis elongata

Phyllorkis elongata, commonly known as the slender leaf orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to certain regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves and small, delicate orchid flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale pink. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in shaded, forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
