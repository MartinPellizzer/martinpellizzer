Pinellia angustata

Pinellia angustata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pinellia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its tuberous rhizomes and arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Pinellia angustata is valued in traditional medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
