Podospermum decumbens

Podospermum decumbens, commonly known as the prostrate sarsaparilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically spreads along the ground, forming dense mats. The plant features small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are clustered in umbels and are typically white or pale green, attracting various pollinators.

Podospermum decumbens is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
