Phillyrea medianifolia subsp. caballeroi

Phillyrea medianifolia subsp.

caballeroi, commonly known as the Canary Island Phillyrea, is a shrub or small tree native to the Canary Islands. It features small, elliptical, dark green leaves that are opposite or sub-opposite along the stems.

The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers during the spring and summer months. Its fruit is a small, yellowish drupe that ripens in the autumn.

This species is adapted to rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous areas.
