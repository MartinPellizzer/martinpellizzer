Pyxidium viride

Pyxidium viride, commonly known as the green pyxidium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to parts of Africa and is characterized by its small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
