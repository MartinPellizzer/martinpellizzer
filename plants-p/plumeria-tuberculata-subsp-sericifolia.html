Plumeria tuberculata subsp. sericifolia

Plumeria tuberculata subsp.

sericifolia, commonly known as the Hawaiian plumeria, is a tropical shrub native to the Hawaiian Islands. It is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to yellow and pink.

The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a waxy texture. The plant produces distinctive, fleshy fruits that contain numerous seeds.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its beauty and cultural significance in Hawaiian traditions.
