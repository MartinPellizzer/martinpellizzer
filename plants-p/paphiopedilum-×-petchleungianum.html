Paphiopedilum × petchleungianum

Paphiopedilum × petchleungianum, commonly known as the Petchleung hybrid, is a hybrid orchid that combines traits from various Paphiopedilum species.

It is characterized by its unique floral structure, featuring a specialized lip that is often brightly colored and intricately patterned. The plant typically produces showy, long-lasting flowers in shades of white, pink, and yellow. It prefers warm, humid environments with high light intensity and well-draining soil.

This hybrid is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
