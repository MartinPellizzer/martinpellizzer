Pandanus lindenii

Pandanus lindenii, commonly known as the giant pandanus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in branched inflorescences. Its roots are fibrous and often used in traditional crafts and construction.

The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near water sources in its natural habitat.
