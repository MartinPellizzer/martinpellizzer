Pycreus spissiflorus

Pycreus spissiflorus, commonly known as the dense-flowered pycreus, is a species of flowering plant native to specific regions in Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces white or pale yellow blooms, which are arranged in tight inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it remains relatively rare in horticultural trade.
