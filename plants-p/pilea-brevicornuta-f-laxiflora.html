Pilea brevicornuta f. laxiflora

Pilea brevicornuta f. laxiflora, commonly known as the Shrub Pilea, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features dense, rounded foliage with elliptical leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental appeal and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
