Pilea papuana

Pilea papuana, commonly known as the New Guinea pilea, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and striking white flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with a compact form. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ornamental value comes from its lush foliage and unique floral display.
