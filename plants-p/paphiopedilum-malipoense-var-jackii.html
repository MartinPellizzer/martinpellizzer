Paphiopedilum malipoense var. jackii

Paphiopedilum malipoense var. jackii, commonly known as the Malipo lady's slipper, is a rare and striking orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch, which is a unique feature among orchids. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded forest environments, thriving in well-drained, acidic soils. Its foliage consists of broad, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle.

This variant is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its rarity and ornamental beauty.
