Pleurothallis sulphurea

Pleurothallis sulphurea, commonly known as the sulfur yellow orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees, drawing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain. The plant produces delicate, yellowish-green flowers that are often clustered in small inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with high air circulation.

Due to its striking color and unique appearance, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts and horticulturists.
