Panicum columbianum

Panicum columbianum, commonly known as Colombian panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes and riverbanks. The plant has slender, cylindrical stems and produces dense, fluffy panicles of small, spikelet-based flowers. Its leaves are narrow and often have a bluish-green hue.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
