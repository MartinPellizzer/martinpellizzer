Panicum elatius

Panicum elatius, commonly known as tall bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and small, blue-green leaves that are often hairy. This plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a common sight in prairies and open woodlands. Its flowering spikes appear in late summer, producing fluffy, pale green to brown seeds.

Panicum elatius is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing food for wildlife.
