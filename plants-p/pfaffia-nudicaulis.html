Pfaffia nudicaulis

Pfaffia nudicaulis, commonly known as yellow snakeroot, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a low-growing perennial with small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in moist, shaded environments. It is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Due to its limited range, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
