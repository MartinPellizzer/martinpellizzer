Phrynium congestum

Phrynium congestum, commonly known as the clustered arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. Its inflorescence consists of clustered, spadix-like structures surrounded by bracts.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
