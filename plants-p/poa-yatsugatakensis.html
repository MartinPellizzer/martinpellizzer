Poa yatsugatakensis

Poa yatsugatakensis, commonly known as the Yatsugatake bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its fine, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions. It has a clump-forming growth habit and is adapted to high-altitude conditions.

Poa yatsugatakensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in alpine ecosystems.
