Polystachya ridleyi

Polystachya ridleyi, commonly known as the Ridley's spikegrass, is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is a terrestrial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces tall, slender stems with numerous small, clustered inflorescences. Its flowers are usually white or pale green, with a delicate, grass-like appearance.

Polystachya ridleyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
