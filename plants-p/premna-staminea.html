Premna staminea

Premna staminea, commonly known as the white senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture and a distinctive white or pale green underside. Its flowers are small, clustered in panicles, and are usually white or yellowish in color.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
