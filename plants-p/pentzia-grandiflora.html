Pentzia grandiflora

Pentzia grandiflora, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces fluffy, wind-dispersed seeds.

Pentzia grandiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
