Portulaca oleracea var. sativa

Portulaca oleracea var. sativa, commonly known as garden purslane, is an annual herbaceous plant native to warm temperate regions.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in the sun. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in cultivated gardens and disturbed areas. Its leaves are edible and rich in nutrients such as omega-3 fatty acids and vitamins.

The species is also known for its ability to tolerate drought and saline conditions.
