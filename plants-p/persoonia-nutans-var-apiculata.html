Persoonia nutans var. apiculata

Persoonia nutans var. apiculata, commonly known as the slender-stemmed geebung, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its erect, slender stems and dense clusters of small, white or cream-colored flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and linear, often with a slight hairy texture. This variety is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and open forests.

It plays an important role in its ecosystem by providing nectar for native pollinators.
