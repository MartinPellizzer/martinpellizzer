Phyllanthus lathyroides f. decoratus

Phyllanthus lathyroides f. decoratus, commonly known as the ornamental phyllanthus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in complex inflorescences. It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Due to its slow growth and drought tolerance, it is well-suited for container gardening and xeriscaping.
