Premna hamiltonii

Premna hamiltonii, commonly known as the Hamilton's premna, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like India, Sri Lanka, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Premna hamiltonii is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
