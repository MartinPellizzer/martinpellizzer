Pittosporum podocarpum var. hejiangense

Pittosporum podocarpum var. hejiangense, commonly known as the Hejiang pittosporum, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features simple, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial shade, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological studies and horticultural use.
