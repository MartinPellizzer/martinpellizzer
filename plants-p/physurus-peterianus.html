Physurus peterianus

Physurus peterianus, commonly known as the silver fern, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-white variegation on its broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers that are followed by red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental landscapes.
