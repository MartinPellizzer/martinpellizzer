Pulicaria baluchistanica

Pulicaria baluchistanica, commonly known as the Baluchistan tansy, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Pakistan and Afghanistan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features silver-gray foliage with a fuzzy texture, adapted to withstand harsh, dry conditions. It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Pulicaria baluchistanica is often found in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in full sun.
