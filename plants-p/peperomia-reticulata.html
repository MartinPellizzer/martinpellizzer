Peperomia reticulata

Peperomia reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves with a distinctive network of veins, giving it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia reticulata is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor conditions.
