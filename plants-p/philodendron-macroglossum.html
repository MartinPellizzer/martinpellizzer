Philodendron macroglossum

Philodendron macroglossum, commonly known as the giant leaf philodendron, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in length. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect sunlight. Its foliage is often a deep green, with some varieties displaying variegation.

Philodendron macroglossum is popular as a houseplant due to its dramatic appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
