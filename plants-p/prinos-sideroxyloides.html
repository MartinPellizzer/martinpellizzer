Prinos sideroxyloides

Prinos sideroxyloides, commonly known as the ironwood tree, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters. The plant produces small, leathery fruits that contain one or two seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to coastal and rocky environments.

Prinos sideroxyloides is valued for its hard, durable wood and is sometimes used in traditional craftsmanship.
