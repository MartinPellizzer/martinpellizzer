Pleurothallis jupiter

Pleurothallis jupiter, commonly known as the Jupiter orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant typically produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with intricate patterns. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrate.

Pleurothallis jupiter is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded, humid environments.
