Panicum brevifolium var. heterostachyum

Panicum brevifolium var. heterostachyum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of North America.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies, meadows, and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often slightly hairy. Its inflorescence consists of a loose, open panicle with spikelets that are usually two-ranked.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
