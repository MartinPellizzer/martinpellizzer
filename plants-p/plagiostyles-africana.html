Plagiostyles africana

Plagiostyles africana, commonly known as the African plagiostyles, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
