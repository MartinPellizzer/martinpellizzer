Pleurothallis scintillata

Pleurothallis scintillata, commonly known as the sparkling pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on tree branches or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with intricate patterns. It has narrow, strap-like leaves and a short, branched stem.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts and botanists.
