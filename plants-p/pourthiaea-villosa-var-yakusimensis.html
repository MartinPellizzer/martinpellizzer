Pourthiaea villosa var. yakusimensis

Pourthiaea villosa var. yakusimensis, commonly known as the Yakusima velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, velvety foliage. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically yellow or white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
