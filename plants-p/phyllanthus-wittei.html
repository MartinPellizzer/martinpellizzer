Phyllanthus wittei

Phyllanthus wittei, commonly known as the African moonseed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by spherical, smooth fruits. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly waxy texture.

Phyllanthus wittei is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
