Perilla Frutescens Var. Arguta

Perilla frutescens var. arguta is a versatile and aromatic plant known for its unique flavor and medicinal properties. Native to East Asia, it is commonly used in traditional cuisines and herbal remedies. Its leaves and seeds are prized for their rich nutritional content and distinctive peppery taste. The plant thrives in warm climates and is easy to cultivate in home gardens. This variety stands out for its adaptability and potential health benefits.
