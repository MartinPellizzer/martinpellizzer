Potentilla kleiniana subsp. anemonifolia

Potentilla kleiniana subsp.

anemonifolia, commonly known as the alpine cinquefoil, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It features small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that give it a delicate appearance. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold, alpine environments.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in gardens with similar conditions.
