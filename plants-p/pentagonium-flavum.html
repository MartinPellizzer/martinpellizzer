Pentagonium flavum

Pentagonium flavum, commonly known as the golden pentagon, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat. The plant has palmate leaves with five distinct lobes, giving it a unique and recognizable appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
