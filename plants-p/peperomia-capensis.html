Peperomia capensis

Peperomia capensis, commonly known as the African violet or velvet leaf, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a succulent with thick, fleshy leaves that are usually dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple, often arranged in clusters. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Peperomia capensis is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
