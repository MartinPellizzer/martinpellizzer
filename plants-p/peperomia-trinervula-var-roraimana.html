Peperomia trinervula var. roraimana

Peperomia trinervula var. roraimana, commonly known as the Roraima peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a velvety texture and prominent veins that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among indoor gardeners for its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
