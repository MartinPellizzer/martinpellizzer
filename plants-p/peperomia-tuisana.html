Peperomia tuisana

Peperomia tuisana, commonly known as the Tuisana Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, which appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and ease of care, it is well-suited for container cultivation.
