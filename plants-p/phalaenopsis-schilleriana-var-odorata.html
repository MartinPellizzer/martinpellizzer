Phalaenopsis schilleriana var. odorata

Phalaenopsis schilleriana var. odorata, commonly known as the fragrant phalaenopsis, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, arching inflorescences that bear clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant has broad, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining potting media.

This variety is prized for its elegant appearance and subtle, long-lasting fragrance.
