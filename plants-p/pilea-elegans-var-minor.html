Pilea elegans var. minor

Pilea elegans var. minor, commonly known as the Chinese money plant or dollar plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by round, green fruit that resembles a miniature melon. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it an ideal houseplant for both novice and experienced growers.
