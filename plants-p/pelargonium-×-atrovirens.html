Pelargonium × atrovirens

Pelargonium × atrovirens, commonly known as the dark green geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves and showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to purple. Native to South Africa, this species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant. The plant prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it adaptable to various growing conditions.

Its foliage and blooms contribute to its popularity in both indoor and outdoor settings.
