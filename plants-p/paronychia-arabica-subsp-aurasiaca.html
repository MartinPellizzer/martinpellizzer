Paronychia arabica subsp. aurasiaca

Paronychia arabica subsp.

aurasiaca, commonly known as the Aurasiaca stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia. It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adapted to store water in its native environment.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
