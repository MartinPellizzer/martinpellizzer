Poa ambigua

Poa ambigua, commonly known as the ambiguous grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial grass that typically forms dense, mat-like colonies. The plant has narrow, elongated leaves and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. It is often found in moist, disturbed soils in temperate regions.

Its botanical characteristics make it adaptable to a variety of environmental conditions.
