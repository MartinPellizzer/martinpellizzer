Prostanthera florifera

Prostanthera florifera, commonly known as the showy honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are ovate and emit a strong, minty scent when crushed.

Prostanthera florifera is often found in rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental and aromatic qualities.
