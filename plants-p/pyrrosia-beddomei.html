Pyrrosia beddomei

Pyrrosia beddomei, commonly known as the beddome's rock fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on tree trunks and rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has lance-shaped, leathery leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It produces small, spore-bearing structures called sori on the underside of its fronds.

Pyrrosia beddomei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
