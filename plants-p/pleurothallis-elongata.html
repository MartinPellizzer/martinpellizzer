Pleurothallis elongata

Pleurothallis elongata, commonly known as the elongated pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in the understory of humid forests. The plant features elongated, strap-like leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in high humidity and partial shade, making it a popular choice for greenhouse cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is often appreciated by orchid enthusiasts.
