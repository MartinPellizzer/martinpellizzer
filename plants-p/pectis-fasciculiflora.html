Pectis fasciculiflora

Pectis fasciculiflora, commonly known as the clustered pectis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and has narrow, linear leaves that are often hairy. Its bright yellow flowers bloom in clusters, making it visually striking.
