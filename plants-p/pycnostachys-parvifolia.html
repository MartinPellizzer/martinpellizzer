Pycnostachys parvifolia

Pycnostachys parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, feathery foliage and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of red and orange. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Its unique leaf structure and vibrant floral display make it a popular ornamental plant.
