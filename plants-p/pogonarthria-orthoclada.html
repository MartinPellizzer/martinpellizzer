Pogonarthria orthoclada

Pogonarthria orthoclada, commonly known as the spikegrass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Pogonarthria orthoclada is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion.
