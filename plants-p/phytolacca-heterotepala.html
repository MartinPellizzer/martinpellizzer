Phytolacca heterotepala

Phytolacca heterotepala, commonly known as the Chinese pokeweed, is a herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers followed by fleshy, red berries. It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is known for its latex-containing sap.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine but contains toxic compounds that require caution in handling.
