Pleurothallis falcatiloba

Pleurothallis falcatiloba, commonly known as the narrow-leafed pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on tree branches or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, linear leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is relatively rare in the wild and requires careful care in cultivation.
