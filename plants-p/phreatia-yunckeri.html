Phreatia yunckeri

Phreatia yunckeri, commonly known as the Yuncker's bromeliad, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This plant is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a central water-holding cup. The inflorescence consists of a spike of small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color.

Phreatia yunckeri thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in the understory of forests.
