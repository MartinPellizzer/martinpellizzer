Pleione formosana

Pleione formosana, commonly known as the Formosan pleione, is a species of orchid native to Taiwan.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant has a pseudobulb structure, which stores water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually elongated and grass-like, and it thrives in shaded, moist environments.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
