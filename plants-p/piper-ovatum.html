Piper ovatum

Piper ovatum, commonly known as the Java pepper or mountain pepper, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in shaded, moist environments.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant in both traditional medicine and horticulture.
