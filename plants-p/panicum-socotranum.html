Panicum socotranum

Panicum socotranum, commonly known as Socotra dragon's blood tree, is a unique plant native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its distinctive umbrella-shaped crown and reddish-brown, resinous sap. The plant belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is adapted to the arid climate of its native habitat. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, pale green flowers.

The sap of Panicum socotranum has been historically used for medicinal and dyeing purposes.
