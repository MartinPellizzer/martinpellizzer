Pennisetum dowsonii

Pennisetum dowsonii, commonly known as the purple fountain grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to southern Africa.

It features tall, slender stalks that produce fluffy, purple-pink plumes during the summer months. The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves that are often a bluish-green color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Pennisetum dowsonii is also valued for its ability to attract birds and other wildlife.
