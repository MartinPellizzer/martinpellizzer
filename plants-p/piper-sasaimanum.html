Piper sasaimanum

Piper sasaimanum, commonly known as the Sassafras pepper or wild pepper, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are peppery and are used in traditional medicine and cuisine.

The plant thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland forests.
