Pennisetum spicatum var. echinurus

Pennisetum spicatum var. echinurus, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and feathery, purple-tinted plumes that give it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for landscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ability to attract birds and butterflies further enhance its ecological value.
