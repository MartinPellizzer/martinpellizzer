Peperomia glabella var. melanostigma

Peperomia glabella var. melanostigma, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, dark green leaves with a velvety texture, and its stems are often reddish in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
