Panicum glaucum var. pumilum

Panicum glaucum var. pumilum, commonly known as little bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its blue-green foliage and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often tinged with red or purple.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for wildlife.
