Pelea clusiifolia var. cuneata

Pelea clusiifolia var. cuneata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf evergreen.

It is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves with a pointed tip. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by bright red berries. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture.

Its foliage is evergreen, providing year-round visual interest in gardens.
