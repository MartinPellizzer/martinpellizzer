Primula × variiflora

Primula × variiflora, commonly known as the variable primrose, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Primulaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that come in a range of colors, including white, yellow, and pink. The plant typically grows to a height of 15–30 cm and features broad, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland or shaded garden settings.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
