Pilosella Cryptomastix Subsp. Roxensis

Pilosella cryptomastix subsp. roxensis is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads and diverse ecological roles. This subspecies is distinguished by its unique leaf structure and small, yellowish blooms that appear in late spring. Due to habitat loss and climate change, it faces increasing threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this species for future generations.
