Peperomia opiziana

Peperomia opiziana, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its small, clustered flowers are usually greenish-white or reddish in color.

Peperomia opiziana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and low maintenance requirements.
