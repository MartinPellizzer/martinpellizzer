Peperomia berteroana

Peperomia berteroana, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery or white-veined underside, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia berteroana is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and moderate humidity to flourish.
