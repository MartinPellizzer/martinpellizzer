Psoralea tenuiflora

Psoralea tenuiflora, commonly known as narrow-flowered psoralea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grasslands and woodlands. It has small, trifoliate leaves and produces pods that contain several seeds.
