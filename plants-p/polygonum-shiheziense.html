Polygonum shiheziense

Polygonum shiheziense, commonly known as the Shihezi knotweed, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its distinctive leaf shapes and flowering structures. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as riverbanks and wetlands. Its leaves are lanceolate with a prominent midrib, and it produces small, clustered flowers in late summer.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for potential medicinal properties.
