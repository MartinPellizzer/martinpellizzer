Philodendron lemorae

Philodendron lemorae, commonly known as the Lemon Tree Philodendron, is a climbing plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with yellow or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers, which are typically followed by round, yellowish fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Philodendron lemorae is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
