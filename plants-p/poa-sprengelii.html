Poa sprengelii

Poa sprengelii, commonly known as Sprengel's blue grass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are narrow and bluish-green, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Poa sprengelii is valued in horticulture for its ability to form dense, low-maintenance lawns in shaded or high-altitude gardens.
