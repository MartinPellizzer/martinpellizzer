Peucedanum nagpurense

Peucedanum nagpurense, commonly known as the Nagpuren mountain angelica, is a perennial herb native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic compounds and distinctive umbellate inflorescences. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves and produces small, white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.
