Portulaca fluvialis

Portulaca fluvialis, commonly known as river portulaca, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a succulent species that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often blooming throughout the growing season. Its leaves are fleshy and succulent, adapted to store water in arid environments.

Portulaca fluvialis is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate drought conditions.
