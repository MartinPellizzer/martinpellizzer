Protium icicariba var. icicariba

Protium icicariba var. icicariba, commonly known as the Caribbean tamarind, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Caribbean region.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and compound leaves that are alternate and pinnate. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody, elongated pods containing edible seeds. Its botanical features make it an important species in local ecosystems and traditional medicine.

The tree is also valued for its ornamental appeal and potential economic uses.
