Panicum petilum

Panicum petilum, commonly known as the delicate grass, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, erect stems that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft, fuzzy appearance. The leaves are narrow and linear, with a bluish-green hue that enhances its ornamental value. It produces small, fluffy panicles of flowers that bloom in late summer, adding a delicate touch to gardens.

This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
