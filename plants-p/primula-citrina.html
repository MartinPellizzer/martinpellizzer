Primula citrina

Primula citrina, commonly known as the yellow primrose, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are oval-shaped with a slightly hairy texture.

Primula citrina is a popular choice for gardeners due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
