Pavetta catophylla var. glabra

Pavetta catophylla var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth pavetta, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are typically smooth and glabrous, giving it its varietal name. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a small, fleshy capsule that contains several seeds.

This species is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and along stream banks.
