Picris hieracioides subsp. villarsii

Picris hieracioides subsp.

villarsii, commonly known as the Alpine Groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It is a low-growing perennial herb with yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant has finely divided, grass-like leaves and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. It is often found in meadows and mountainous areas where it contributes to the biodiversity of high-altitude ecosystems.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to cold and harsh environmental conditions.
