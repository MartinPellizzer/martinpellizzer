Pogostemon longifolius

Pogostemon longifolius, commonly known as the long-leaved pogostemon, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, tubular flowers are typically purple or violet in color and are arranged in whorls along the stems.

Pogostemon longifolius is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
