Potentilla recta var. firma

Potentilla recta var. firma, commonly known as upright potentilla, is a compact, deciduous shrub characterized by its dense, upright growth habit.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green on top and slightly hairy underneath. The plant produces clusters of delicate, yellowish-white flowers in early summer, which are attractive to pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
