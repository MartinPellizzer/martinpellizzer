Phaseolus monophyllus var. unifoliolatus

Phaseolus monophyllus var. unifoliolatus, commonly known as the single-leaf scarlet runner bean, is a tropical legume native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its unique foliage, featuring large, solitary leaves with a bright red or purple underside. The plant produces elongated, bean-like pods that are typically red or orange in color when mature. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its edible beans.

This variety is notable for its striking appearance and is popular in both gardens and agricultural settings.
