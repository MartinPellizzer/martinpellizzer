Pelargonium × anosmon

Pelargonium × anosmon, commonly known as the scented geranium, is a hybrid plant within the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its aromatic foliage, which emits a variety of scents depending on the variety, such as citrus, mint, or rose. The plant typically has rounded, lobed leaves and produces small, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink, red, or white. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its pleasant fragrance and ability to thrive in a range of growing conditions.

Pelargonium × anosmon is often used in gardens and as a houseplant, valued for both its aesthetic appeal and therapeutic properties.
