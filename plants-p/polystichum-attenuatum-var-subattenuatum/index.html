Polystichum Attenuatum Var. Subattenuatum

Polystichum attenuatum var. subattenuatum is a rare and elegant fern native to the mountainous regions of southern China. This variant of the common holly fern is distinguished by its slender, gracefully arching fronds that taper towards the tips. Its foliage displays a deep green hue with a subtle sheen that catches the light beautifully. Often found in shaded, moist environments, it thrives in cool, humid climates. Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is highly sought after by plant enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
