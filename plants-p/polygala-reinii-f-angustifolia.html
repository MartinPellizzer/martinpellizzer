Polygala reinii f. angustifolia

Polygala reinii f. angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaved milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its slender, angular stems and small, delicate flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and flowering display.
