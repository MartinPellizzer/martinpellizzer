Phillyrea subangustifolia subsp. martonnei

Phillyrea subangustifolia subsp.

martonnei, commonly known as the Narrow-leaved Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy.

The plant produces white, clustered flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by small, spherical berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rocky or arid environments.

The species is notable for its aromatic foliage and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.
