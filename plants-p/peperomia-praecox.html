Peperomia praecox

Peperomia praecox, commonly known as the early peperomia, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features small, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and arranged in a whorled pattern along the stems. It produces tiny, clustered flowers that are usually greenish-white in color.

Peperomia praecox is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation in both indoor and outdoor settings.
