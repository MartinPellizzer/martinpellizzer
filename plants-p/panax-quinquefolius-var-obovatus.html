Panax quinquefolius var. obovatus

Panax quinquefolius var. obovatus, commonly known as the red ginseng variant, is a botanical variant of the American ginseng plant.

It is characterized by its distinctive reddish coloration, which develops during the drying process. The plant is native to the Appalachian region of the United States and thrives in shaded, moist forest environments. Its roots are valued for their medicinal properties, including potential immune-boosting and anti-inflammatory effects.

This variant is often cultivated for use in traditional herbal remedies and health supplements.
