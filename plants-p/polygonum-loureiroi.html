Polygonum loureiroi

Polygonum loureiroi, commonly known as redroot smartweed, is a herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes many species of flowering plants known for their distinctive bracts. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a reddish tint, while the flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences.

Polygonum loureiroi is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
