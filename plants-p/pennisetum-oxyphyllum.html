Pennisetum oxyphyllum

Pennisetum oxyphyllum, commonly known as fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted, fountain-like growth habit and slender, arching blades that often form a dense, rounded clump. The plant produces fluffy, silver-gray seed heads that are highly ornamental and attract birds and insects. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Pennisetum oxyphyllum is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in a variety of climates.
