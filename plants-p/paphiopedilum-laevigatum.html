Paphiopedilum laevigatum

Paphiopedilum laevigatum, commonly known as the smooth-lip orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with a single, elongated leaf and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive lip. The flowers are typically pink to purple in color and have a unique, intricate structure. It prefers shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
