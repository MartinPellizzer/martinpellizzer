Pilea rupipendia

Pilea rupipendia, commonly known as the Shiny Pilea, is a striking evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Pilea rupipendia is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
