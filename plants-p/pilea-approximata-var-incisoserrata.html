Pilea approximata var. incisoserrata

Pilea approximata var. incisoserrata, commonly known as the Shingle Plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves with a distinctive wavy, serrated margin. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow-green flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique leaf shape and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant.
