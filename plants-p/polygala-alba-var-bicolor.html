Polygala alba var. bicolor

Polygala alba var. bicolor, commonly known as the white and pink milkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in sandy or loamy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and naturalized landscapes.
