Polygala chodatiana

Polygala chodatiana, commonly known as the Chodat's milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Polygala chodatiana is often found in open woodlands and along riverbanks, where it contributes to local biodiversity.
