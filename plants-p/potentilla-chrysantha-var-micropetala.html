Potentilla chrysantha var. micropetala

Potentilla chrysantha var. micropetala, commonly known as the narrow-leaved potentilla, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are densely packed along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or alpine settings.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a valued addition to native plant gardens.
