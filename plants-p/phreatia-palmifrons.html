Phreatia palmifrons

Phreatia palmifrons, commonly known as the giant phreatia, is a tropical orchid native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences and can grow up to several meters in height. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture in its root system. Its leaves are typically broad and sword-shaped, contributing to its striking appearance.

Botanically, it is notable for its complex floral structure and its role in supporting local biodiversity.
