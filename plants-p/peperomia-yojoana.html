Peperomia yojoana

Peperomia yojoana, commonly known as the Shiny Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a compact and decorative appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and white, which are followed by tiny, red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia yojoana is relatively low-maintenance, requiring minimal watering and moderate temperatures for optimal growth.
