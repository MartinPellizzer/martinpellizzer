Pleurospermum dentatum

Pleurospermum dentatum, commonly known as the toothed pleurospermum, is a perennial herb native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It features a robust root system that allows it to survive in dry conditions, and its leaves are typically narrow and hairy, reducing water loss. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in late summer or early autumn. It is often found in rocky soils and can thrive in full sun.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-adapted to harsh environments, and it is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
