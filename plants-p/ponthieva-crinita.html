Ponthieva crinita

Ponthieva crinita, commonly known as the hairy ponthieva, is a member of the Aroid family (Araceae).

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, ornate leaves that are often covered in fine, hairy textures. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of many aroid species.

Ponthieva crinita is valued in horticulture for its striking appearance and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
