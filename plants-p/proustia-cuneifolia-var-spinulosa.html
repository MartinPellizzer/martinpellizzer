Proustia cuneifolia var. spinulosa

Proustia cuneifolia var. spinulosa, commonly known as the spiny leaf proustia, is a unique variant of the proustia genus native to specific regions of southern Africa.

This plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often adorned with small, spiny projections, giving it a distinctive appearance. The foliage is typically green with a slightly hairy texture, and the plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of yellow or white. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is appreciated by botanists and gardeners alike.
