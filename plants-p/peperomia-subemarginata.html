Peperomia subemarginata

Peperomia subemarginata, commonly known as the marginate peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with a distinctive white or silver margin that contrasts with its deep green center. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Peperomia subemarginata is valued for its attractive foliage and air-purifying qualities.
