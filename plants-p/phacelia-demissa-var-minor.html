Phacelia demissa var. minor

Phacelia demissa var. minor, commonly known as the lesser phacelia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender stems and small, blue to purple flowers. The plant features opposite, linear to lanceolate leaves that are usually hairy. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open areas such as meadows and disturbed sites.

Its nectar-rich flowers attract a variety of pollinators, including bees and butterflies.
