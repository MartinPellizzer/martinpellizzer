Polypodium masaskei

Polypodium masaskei, commonly known as the Masai fern, is a species of fern native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its intricate fronds with a distinctive, lace-like appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The fronds are also valued for their ornamental qualities in horticulture.
