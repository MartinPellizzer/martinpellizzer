Pimpinella magna var. pinnatifida

Pimpinella magna var. pinnatifida, commonly known as the broad-leaved cow-wheat, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

This species is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and stream banks.
