Protea caffra subsp. mafingensis

Protea caffra subsp.

mafingensis, commonly known as the Mafinga protea, is a striking South African flowering plant belonging to the Proteaceae family. It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences composed of numerous bright pink to red floral bracts.

The plant is native to the eastern regions of South Africa, particularly in the KwaZulu-Natal province. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires a cool, humid climate for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
