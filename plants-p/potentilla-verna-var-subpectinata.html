Potentilla verna var. subpectinata

Potentilla verna var. subpectinata, commonly known as the early potentilla, is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically features delicate, upright stems with small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has alternate, ovate leaves that are often hairy and have a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Its botanical characteristics make it an attractive and hardy addition to gardens with limited space.
