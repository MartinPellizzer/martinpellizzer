Presliophytum heucherifolium

Presliophytum heucherifolium, commonly known as the silverleaf ice plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
