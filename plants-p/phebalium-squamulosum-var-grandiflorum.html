Phebalium squamulosum var. grandiflorum

Phebalium squamulosum var. grandiflorum, commonly known as the large-flowered squamulous phebalium, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, scale-like hairs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or red, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
