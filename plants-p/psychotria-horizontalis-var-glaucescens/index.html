Psychotria Horizontalis Var. Glaucescens

Psychotria horizontalis var. glaucescens is a unique and striking plant known for its vivid blue-green foliage and delicate white flowers. Native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, this variety thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are often covered in a waxy coating that gives them a distinctive shimmer under sunlight. Despite its beauty, it is relatively uncommon in cultivation due to its specific care requirements. This plant is not only aesthetically pleasing but also holds cultural and medicinal significance in its native regions.
