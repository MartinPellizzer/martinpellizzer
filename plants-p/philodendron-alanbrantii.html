Philodendron alanbrantii

Philodendron alanbrantii, commonly known as the Alan Brant Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy green leaves that are often variegated with light green or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by elongated, finger-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
