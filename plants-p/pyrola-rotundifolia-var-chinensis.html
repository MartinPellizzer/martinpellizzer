Pyrola rotundifolia var. chinensis

Pyrola rotundifolia var. chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese roundleaf wintergreen, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features dense, dark green, oval-shaped leaves that remain on the plant year-round. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to cool, humid climates and acidic soils.
