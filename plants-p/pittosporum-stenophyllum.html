Pittosporum stenophyllum

Pittosporum stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are typically followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.

Pittosporum stenophyllum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native ecosystems.
