Peperomia lorentzii var. boliviensis

Peperomia lorentzii var. boliviensis, commonly known as the Bolivian peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features dense, glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which are borne on short, upright spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

This variety is prized for its ornamental foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
