Peperomia confusa

Peperomia confusa, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, often adorned with silver or white veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable addition to both indoor and outdoor landscapes.
