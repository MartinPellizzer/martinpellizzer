Phalaenopsis bastianii f. melenciana

Phalaenopsis bastianii f. melenciana, commonly known as the Melenciana orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, long-lasting flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, and thrives in humid, warm environments. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, and it produces multiple flower spikes that can last for several months.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and ease of cultivation.
