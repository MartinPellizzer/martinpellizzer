Polytaenium lineatum var. intramarginale

Polytaenium lineatum var. intramarginale, commonly known as the narrow-leaved polytaenium, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polytaeniaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves that are often arranged in a dense rosette pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences at the base of the leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate regions with high humidity.

Its botanical structure reflects adaptations to its specific ecological niche, making it a notable species in its native habitat.
