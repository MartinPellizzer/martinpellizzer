Phlomis ornata var. minor

Phlomis ornata var. minor, commonly known as the lesser hedgehog garlic, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems covered in soft, grayish-green foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of purple or pink, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens and dry landscapes.
