Poa keysseri

Poa keysseri, commonly known as Keyser's bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats. Its leaves are narrow and have a bluish-green color, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Poa keysseri is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil in high-altitude ecosystems.
