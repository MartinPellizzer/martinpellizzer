Plagiostachys strobilifera

Plagiostachys strobilifera, commonly known as the clustered spikegrass, is a perennial grass native to parts of Asia, particularly China.

It is characterized by its dense, spike-like inflorescences that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional landscaping.
