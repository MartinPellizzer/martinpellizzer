Pleurothallis spectabilis

Pleurothallis spectabilis, commonly known as the showy pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and its ability to thrive in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces strikingly colorful flowers, often in shades of yellow, red, and orange, which are typically borne on short, branched inflorescences. Its leaves are usually ovate to elliptical in shape, with a smooth surface and a waxy texture.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by enthusiasts for its aesthetic appeal in greenhouse settings.
