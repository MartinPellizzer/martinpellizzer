Pleurostachys buchtienii

Pleurostachys buchtienii, commonly known as the Buchtienii grass, is a perennial grass species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant typically features narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green hue and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
