Polygala herbiola

Polygala herbiola, commonly known as the narrow-leaved milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polygalaceae.

It is native to parts of Asia, including China and India, and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Polygala herbiola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
