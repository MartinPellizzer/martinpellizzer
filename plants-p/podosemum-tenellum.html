Podosemum tenellum

Podosemum tenellum, commonly known as the slender podosemum, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often elliptical in shape. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, arranged in clusters, and it produces capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and understudied species in botanical research.
