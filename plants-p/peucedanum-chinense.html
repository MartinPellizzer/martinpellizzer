Peucedanum chinense

Peucedanum chinense, commonly known as Chinese cowparsley, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic compounds and often hollow stems. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in umbels. Its seeds are enclosed in a fibrous, prickly husk, typical of the genus.

Peucedanum chinense is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
