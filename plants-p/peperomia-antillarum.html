Peperomia antillarum

Peperomia antillarum, commonly known as the Caribbean peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

It is a compact, upright shrub with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, often green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia antillarum is relatively low maintenance and resistant to pests, contributing to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
