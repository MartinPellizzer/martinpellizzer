Polystichum polyblepharon var. scabiosum

Polystichum polyblepharon var. scabiosum, commonly known as the scabious holly fern, is a variant of the holly fern species native to parts of Asia.

It is a perennial evergreen fern that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features dark green, leathery fronds with a distinctive scabrous texture on the underside. Its spore-bearing structures, called sori, are arranged in clusters along the fronds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often cultivated in gardens for its foliage.
