Piper boorsroae

Piper boorsroae, commonly known as the Boorsroae pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are used in traditional medicine and culinary applications. Its leaves are often used for their aromatic properties, and the plant is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves.

It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
