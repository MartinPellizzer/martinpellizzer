Pilea macrantha

Pilea macrantha, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features round, fleshy leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white or pale yellow, which bloom in spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea macrantha is known for its easy care and ability to purify the air, adding both aesthetic and functional value to any space.
