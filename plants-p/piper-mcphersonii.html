Piper mcphersonii

Piper mcphersonii, commonly known as the McPherson's pepper vine, is a climbing shrub native to the rainforests of Queensland, Australia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various species of pepper plants. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by berries that turn red when mature. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understorey of tropical forests.

Due to its restricted distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
