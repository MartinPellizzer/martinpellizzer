Piper dumeticola

Piper dumeticola, commonly known as the dwarf pepper plant, is a species of flowering vine native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, green berries that mature to a dark red color, containing aromatic seeds. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped with a rough texture and emit a mild peppery scent when crushed.

Piper dumeticola is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of essential oils.
