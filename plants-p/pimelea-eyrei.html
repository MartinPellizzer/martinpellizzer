Pimelea eyrei

Pimelea eyrei, commonly known as the pink pimelea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features small, showy pink or white flowers that bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and a compact growth habit. It is often found in sandy or gravelly soils in coastal and inland regions.

Pimelea eyrei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
