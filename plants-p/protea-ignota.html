Protea ignota

Protea ignota, commonly known as the mountain protea, is a species native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its striking, cone-shaped inflorescence. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates. Its flowers range in color from white to pink and yellow, attracting various pollinators.

Protea ignota is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
