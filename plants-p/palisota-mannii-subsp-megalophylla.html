Palisota mannii subsp. megalophylla

Palisota mannii subsp.

megalophylla, commonly known as the giant-leaf arrowroot, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of Africa. It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in rainforests or along riverbanks.

Its rhizomes are used in traditional medicine and as a starchy food source in some regions.
