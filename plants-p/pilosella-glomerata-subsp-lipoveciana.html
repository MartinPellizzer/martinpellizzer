Pilosella glomerata subsp. lipoveciana

Pilosella glomerata subsp.

lipoveciana, commonly known as the Balkan hairy hawk's-beard, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan Peninsula. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has small, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slightly different morphology compared to the typical form of Pilosella glomerata.
