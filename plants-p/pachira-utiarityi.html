Pachira utiarityi

Pachira utiarityi, commonly known as the Amazonian money plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers followed by round, woody fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Pachira utiarityi is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
