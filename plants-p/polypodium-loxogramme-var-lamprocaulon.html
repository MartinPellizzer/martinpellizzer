Polypodium loxogramme var. lamprocaulon

Polypodium loxogramme var. lamprocaulon, commonly known as the glossy-leaf wall fern, is a variant of the common polypody.

It is a fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae, characterized by its evergreen, leathery fronds. The fronds are typically bi- or tri-pinnate, with a distinctive glossy, dark green appearance. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found growing on rocks or tree trunks in tropical and subtropical regions.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive foliage.
