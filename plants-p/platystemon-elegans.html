Platystemon elegans

Platystemon elegans, commonly known as the elegant star-flower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its showy, star-shaped blue or purple petals that bloom in late spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spherical fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and delicate appearance, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
