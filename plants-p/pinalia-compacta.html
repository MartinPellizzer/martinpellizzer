Pinalia compacta

Pinalia compacta, commonly known as the compact pincushion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features dense, rounded clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow, grass-like, and arranged in a compact rosette.

Pinalia compacta is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
