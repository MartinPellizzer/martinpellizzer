Paraderris Cuneifolia

Coneflower, scientifically known as Paraderris cuneifolia, is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of South America. Its unique appearance features showy, cone-shaped flowers that come in a range of vibrant colors. This species is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. It is also a valuable addition to wildlife habitats, providing nectar for bees and butterflies. With its hardy nature and visual appeal, Paraderris cuneifolia is a popular choice for gardeners and landscapers alike.
