Pyrethrum crithmifolium

Pyrethrum crithmifolium, commonly known as sea fennel, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, finely divided leaves and small yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often found growing in coastal areas and sandy soils due to its tolerance for salty and well-drained conditions. Its botanical features include a taproot system and a basal rosette of leaves.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
