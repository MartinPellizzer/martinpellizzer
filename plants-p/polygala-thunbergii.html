Polygala thunbergii

Polygala thunbergii, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features slender, erect stems with opposite, pinnately compound leaves. Its small, yellowish-green flowers are clustered in dense, globular inflorescences.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or rocky areas.
