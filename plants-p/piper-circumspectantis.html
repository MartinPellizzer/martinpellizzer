Piper circumspectantis

Piper circumspectantis, commonly known as the cautious pepper, is a tropical climbing vine native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in spikes. Its leaves are heart-shaped with a rough texture and emit a mild peppery scent.

The fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to red as it matures.
