Picris wagenitzii

Picris wagenitzii, commonly known as Wagenitz's picris, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and is native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It has a taproot system and produces small, bract-like leaves.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological studies and traditional herbal medicine.
