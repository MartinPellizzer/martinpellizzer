Pleioblastus distichus f. nezasa

Pleioblastus distichus f. nezasa, commonly known as the Nezasa bamboo, is a compact, clumping bamboo species native to Japan.

It features slender, dark green culms with a smooth, sometimes slightly hairy surface. The leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and have a glossy appearance, contributing to its ornamental value. It is well-suited for small garden spaces and container cultivation due to its slow growth rate.

This variety is prized for its elegant form and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types with moderate watering.
