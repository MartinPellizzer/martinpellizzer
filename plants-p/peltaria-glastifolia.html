Peltaria glastifolia

Peltaria glastifolia, commonly known as the smoothleaf peltaria, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of the northern hemisphere.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually oval to lance-shaped with a smooth margin. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences, often white or pale green in color.

Peltaria glastifolia is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other shade-tolerant flora.
