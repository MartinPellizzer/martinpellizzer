Peucedanum abrotanifolium

Peucedanum abrotanifolium, commonly known as wild carrot or poison hemlock, is a biennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its finely divided leaves and umbel-shaped flowers.
