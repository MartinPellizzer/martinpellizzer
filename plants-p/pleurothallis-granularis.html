Pleurothallis granularis

Pleurothallis granularis, commonly known as the granular orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and green. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in a fine, granular texture, giving it its common name.

Pleurothallis granularis thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
