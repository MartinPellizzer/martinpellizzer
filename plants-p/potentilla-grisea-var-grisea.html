Potentilla grisea var. grisea

Potentilla grisea var. grisea, commonly known as silver cinquefoil, is a low-growing perennial plant native to North America.

It features small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer, typically from June to August. The plant has hairy, gray-green foliage that gives it a distinctive silvery appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This hardy species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.
