Pilea holstii

Pilea holstii, commonly known as the Mexican Ice Plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Mexico.

It features small, rounded leaves that are typically silver-white to pale green in color, often with a waxy coating. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Pilea holstii is known for its ease of care and is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
