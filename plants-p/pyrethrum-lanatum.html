Pyrethrum lanatum

Pyrethrum lanatum, commonly known as the woolly pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its botanical features make it both an ornamental and potentially useful plant in natural pest control.
