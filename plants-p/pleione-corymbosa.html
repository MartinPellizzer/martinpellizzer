Pleione corymbosa

Pleione corymbosa, commonly known as the clustered pleione, is a species of orchid native to the Himalayas and Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments. The plant produces a single stem with several flowers, each having a showy, spurred lip. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with intricate patterns that attract pollinators.

Pleione corymbosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
