Peltandra undulata

Peltandra undulata, commonly known as the waxy leaf plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, waxy, undulating leaves that are typically bright green and have a distinctive, wavy margin. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
