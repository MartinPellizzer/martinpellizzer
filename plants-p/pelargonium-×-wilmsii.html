Pelargonium × wilmsii

Pelargonium × wilmsii, commonly known as the African marigold, is a hybrid species within the Geraniaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with dark green, velvety leaves. Its flowers are borne in clusters and are often used in ornamental gardening due to their striking appearance.

Pelargonium × wilmsii prefers full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for coastal and Mediterranean climates.
