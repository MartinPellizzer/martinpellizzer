Plumeriopsis ahouai

Plumeriopsis ahouai, commonly known as the Ahouai flame tree, is a species native to Madagascar.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy texture.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
