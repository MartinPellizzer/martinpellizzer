Piper infossum

Piper infossum, commonly known as the Java pepper or long pepper, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant produces small, elongated berries that are harvested when mature for their peppery flavor. These berries contain essential oils and have been used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important crop in both culinary and medicinal contexts.
