Pterostylis pusilla var. prominens

Pterostylis pusilla var. prominens, commonly known as the prominent hooded orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and a single, hooded flower with a distinctive, elongated lip. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a prominent, spurred labellum.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other orchid species in its habitat.
