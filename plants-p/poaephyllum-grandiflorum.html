Poaephyllum grandiflorum

Poaephyllum grandiflorum, commonly known as the grandiflorum daisy, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

Poaephyllum grandiflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate, daisy-like appearance.
