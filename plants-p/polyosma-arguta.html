Polyosma arguta

Polyosma arguta, commonly known as the narrow-leafed polyosma, is a shrub native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in dense inflorescences, blooming in late spring.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
