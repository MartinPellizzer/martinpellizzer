Pleurothallis carinata

Pleurothallis carinata, commonly known as the ridged pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and elongated, often curved leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or red streaks. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and delicate blooms, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
