Petrea rugosa var. casta

Petrea rugosa var. casta, commonly known as the pink shower tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking pink or white flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a visually stunning display. The plant has ovate, glossy leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is adaptable to various soil types.

Its rapid growth and fragrant blossoms make it a popular choice for landscaping and floral arrangements.
