Prenanthes rubicunda

Prenanthes rubicunda, commonly known as redroot or redroot aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy purple to red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and often have a reddish tint, which contributes to its common name.

The roots are reddish in color and can be used in traditional herbal medicine.
