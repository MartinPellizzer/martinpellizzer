Pleurostachys tenuiflora var. floribunda

Pleurostachys tenuiflora var. floribunda, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pleurostachys, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in loose clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
