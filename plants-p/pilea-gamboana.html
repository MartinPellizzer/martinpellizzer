Pilea gamboana

Pilea gamboana, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by round, green fruits. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Pilea gamboana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
