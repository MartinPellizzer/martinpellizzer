Peperomia cililimba var. tiavina

Peperomia cililimba var. tiavina, commonly known as the Tiavina peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Madagascar.

It features fleshy, dark green leaves with a textured surface and a subtle waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for collectors and indoor gardeners.
