Pittosporum ovatum

Pittosporum ovatum, commonly known as the silver pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage that often has a silvery or whitish underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The leaves are evergreen, and the plant is often used in hedges or as a specimen plant due to its hardy nature.
