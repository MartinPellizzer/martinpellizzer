Polypodium chinense

Polypodium chinense, commonly known as the Chinese water fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically bioluminescent in the dark. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizomes are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant's unique appearance and ecological role make it an important species in its native habitats.
