Potentilla curta

Potentilla curta, commonly known as dwarf cinquefoil, is a low-growing perennial plant native to North America.

It typically reaches a height of only a few inches, with a mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, five-petaled flowers that are usually yellow or white, blooming in the summer months. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Potentilla curta thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky areas.
