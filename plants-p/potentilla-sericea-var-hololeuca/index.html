Potentilla Sericea Var. Hololeuca

Potentilla sericea var. hololeuca is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant known for its silvery foliage and vibrant yellow blooms. Native to the Pacific Northwest of the United States, this variety thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade. Its delicate, fern-like leaves provide a soft texture that contrasts beautifully with its bold, showy flowers. Often used in garden design for its ornamental value, it is a favorite among horticulturists and landscape designers. This plant not only adds visual interest to gardens but also supports local pollinators with its nectar-rich flowers.
