Phalaenopsis lobbii f. flava

Phalaenopsis lobbii f. flava, commonly known as the Yellow Phalaenopsis, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, golden-yellow flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia, where it thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant features long, arching inflorescences with numerous blooms that can last for several months. Its foliage consists of broad, dark green leaves that are often glossy and lance-shaped.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
