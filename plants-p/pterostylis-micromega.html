Pterostylis micromega

Pterostylis micromega, commonly known as the dwarf greenhood, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. The plant features a slender, often reddish stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flower is small, greenish-white, and has a distinctive hood-like structure.

Pterostylis micromega is a rare and endangered species, highlighting the importance of conservation efforts for its survival.
