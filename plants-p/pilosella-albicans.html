Pilosella albicans

Pilosella albicans, commonly known as the white goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and foliage with soft, woolly textures. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically narrow, hairy, and arranged in a basal rosette.
