Pycnostylis sacleuxii

Pycnostylis sacleuxii, commonly known as the African star grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks.

Pycnostylis sacleuxii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
