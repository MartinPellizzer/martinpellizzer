Pyrethrum nitens

Pyrethrum nitens, commonly known as the white pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of white to pale yellow. The plant produces small, cylindrical flower heads composed of numerous fine rays, which are typical of the genus Pyrethrum. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for cultivation in Mediterranean climates.

Pyrethrum nitens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
