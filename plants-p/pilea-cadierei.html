Pilea cadierei

Pilea cadierei, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features heart-shaped, glossy, dark green leaves that are slightly wavy along the edges. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Pilea cadierei is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.
