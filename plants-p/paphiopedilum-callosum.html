Paphiopedilum callosum

Paphiopedilum callosum, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces striking, showy flowers with a unique lip that resembles a pouch. The plant prefers shaded, humid environments and well-draining soil. Its flowers are typically pink to purple in color, with a velvety texture.

It is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
