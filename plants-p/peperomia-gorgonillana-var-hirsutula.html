Peperomia gorgonillana var. hirsutula

Peperomia gorgonillana var. hirsutula, commonly known as the hairy peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves with a distinctive hairy texture, giving it a unique, textured appearance. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its slow growth rate and ability to tolerate dry conditions make it a popular choice for beginners in plant care.
