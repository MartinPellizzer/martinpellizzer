Platylobium reflexum

Platylobium reflexum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed everlasting pea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its small, pea-like flowers that are typically pale yellow to cream in color. The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
