Peperomia freireifolia

Peperomia freireifolia, commonly known as the Freire's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces delicate, red-bracted flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia freireifolia is known for its low maintenance and ability to adapt well to a variety of indoor environments.
