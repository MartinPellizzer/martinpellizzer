Pultenaea paleacea var. pauciflora

Pultenaea paleacea var. pauciflora, commonly known as the sparse-flowered pultenaea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in heathlands and dry sclerophyll forests, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and appear in clusters during spring.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting native wildlife.
