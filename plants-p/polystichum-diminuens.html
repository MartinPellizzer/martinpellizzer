Polystichum diminuens

Polystichum diminuens, commonly known as the dwarf shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and leathery, dark green fronds that are often adorned with a whitish bloom on the underside. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories. Its rhizomatous growth allows it to spread slowly and form dense colonies over time.

Polystichum diminuens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and rockeries.
