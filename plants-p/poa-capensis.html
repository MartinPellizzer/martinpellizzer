Poa capensis

Poa capensis, commonly known as the Cape grass, is a perennial grass native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, bluish-green leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Poa capensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping and erosion control.
