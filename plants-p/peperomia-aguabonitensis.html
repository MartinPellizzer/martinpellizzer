Peperomia aguabonitensis

Peperomia aguabonitensis, commonly known as the Water Beauty Peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features small, waxy, elliptical leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or brown, which are followed by small, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia aguabonitensis is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
