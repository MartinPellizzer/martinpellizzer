Pelargonium × politum

Pelargonium × politum, commonly known as the hybrid geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to purple. The leaves are usually serrated and have a distinctive, slightly fuzzy texture. This plant is native to South Africa and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a potted plant.

It prefers full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a versatile choice for various gardening environments.
