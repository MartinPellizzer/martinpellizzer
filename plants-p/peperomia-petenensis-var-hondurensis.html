Peperomia petenensis var. hondurensis

Peperomia petenensis var. hondurensis, commonly known as the Honduras peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Central America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval or elliptical in shape, often with a bluish-green or dark green hue. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color, which appear in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and ornamental foliage, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant.
