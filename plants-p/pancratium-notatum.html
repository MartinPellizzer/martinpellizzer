Pancratium notatum

Pancratium notatum, commonly known as the yellow nutmeg or sea daffodil, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region and parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically blue-green in color. Pancratium notatum is often found growing in coastal areas, sandy soils, and disturbed sites.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
