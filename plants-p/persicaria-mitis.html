Persicaria mitis

Persicaria mitis, commonly known as slender knotweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It features slender, upright stems that are often reddish in color, and it produces small, white to pinkish flowers in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist environments such as wetlands and stream banks.

Persicaria mitis is often used in wetland restoration due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for wildlife.
