Plenasium aureum

Plenasium aureum, commonly known as the golden star fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, feather-like fronds that radiate from a central stem, creating a star-like appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture to flourish. Its leaves are typically bright green with a slight yellowish hue, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Plenasium aureum is often cultivated as a decorative plant in indoor settings due to its aesthetic value and relatively easy care requirements.
