Peperomia chutanka

Peperomia chutanka, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pale green flowers that are typically borne on short, erect spikes. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its ability to thrive in low light conditions and its tolerance for dry soil.

Peperomia chutanka is prized for its ornamental foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
