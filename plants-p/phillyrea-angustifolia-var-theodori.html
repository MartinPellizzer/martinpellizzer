Phillyrea angustifolia var. theodori

Phillyrea angustifolia var. theodori, commonly known as the Theodore's narrowleaf phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and slightly glossy. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by small, leathery fruits. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils.

This variety is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
