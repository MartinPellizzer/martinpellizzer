Pyrethrum platyrachis

Pyrethrum platyrachis, commonly known as the broadleaf pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant produces small, spherical seed heads that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by the wind. Pyrethrum platyrachis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its foliage and flowers contain natural compounds that have insecticidal properties.
