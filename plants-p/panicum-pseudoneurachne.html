Panicum pseudoneurachne

Panicum pseudoneurachne, commonly known as the narrowleaf panicgrass, is a grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Due to its hardy nature, it is considered a resilient species in its native habitat.
