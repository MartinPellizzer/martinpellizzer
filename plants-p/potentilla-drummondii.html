Potentilla drummondii

Potentilla drummondii, commonly known as Drummond's cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, consisting of five oval-shaped leaflets, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Drummond's cinquefoil is often found in prairies, open woodlands, and rocky areas, showcasing its adaptability to various environmental conditions.
