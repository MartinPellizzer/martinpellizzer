Podochilus niveus

Podochilus niveus, commonly known as the white orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, white flowers that bloom in clusters along the stem. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are long and narrow, often with a waxy texture.

Podochilus niveus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
