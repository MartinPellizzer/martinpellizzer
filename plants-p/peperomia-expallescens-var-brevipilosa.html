Peperomia expallescens var. brevipilosa

Peperomia expallescens var. brevipilosa, commonly known as the Compact Peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish or purple hue on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale green, emerging from the leaf axils. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and minimal water requirements.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for tabletop displays and hanging baskets.
