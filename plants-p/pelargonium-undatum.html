Pelargonium undatum

Pelargonium undatum, commonly known as the sweet geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red. The leaves are typically rounded and have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

The plant is also valued for its fragrant blooms and ability to attract pollinators.
