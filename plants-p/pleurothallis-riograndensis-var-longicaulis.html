Pleurothallis riograndensis var. longicaulis

Pleurothallis riograndensis var. longicaulis, commonly known as the long-stemmed rainforest orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features slender, wiry stems and elongated, lance-shaped leaves that help it thrive in low-light conditions. Its flowers are usually small and delicate, with a variety of colors ranging from white to pink and purple.

This variety is noted for its unique morphology and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in specialized horticultural settings.
