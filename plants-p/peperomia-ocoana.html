Peperomia ocoana

Peperomia ocoana, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are dark green with a velvety texture, often adorned with pale veins. The plant produces small, pale green to white flowers in clusters, which are typically borne on short, erect stalks. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
