Platystachys pruinosa

Platystachys pruinosa, commonly known as the silverleaf honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray, fuzzy leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically blooming in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for ornamental gardens.

Its unique foliage and seasonal blooms make it a popular choice for landscape design.
