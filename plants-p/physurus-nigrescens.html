Physurus nigrescens

Physurus nigrescens, commonly known as the black velvet fern, is a unique plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage that resembles velvet to the touch. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its striking appearance and rarity, it is of interest to botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
