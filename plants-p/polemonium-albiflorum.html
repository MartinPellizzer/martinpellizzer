Polemonium albiflorum

Polemonium albiflorum, commonly known as white-flowered Jacob's ladder, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Polemoniaceae family and is characterized by its cascading clusters of white to pale pink flowers. The plant features slender, branched stems that produce opposite, ovate leaves with toothed margins. It typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and blooms in late spring to early summer.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for shaded garden settings.
