Prunus domestica var. ovoidea

Prunus domestica var. ovoidea, commonly known as the oval-shaped European plum, is a cultivar of the European plum tree.

It is characterized by its oval, firm fruits that are typically yellow or reddish in color. The plant is a deciduous shrub or small tree that produces small, fragrant flowers in the spring. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its culinary uses and ornamental appeal in gardens.
