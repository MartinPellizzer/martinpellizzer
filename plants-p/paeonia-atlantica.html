Paeonia atlantica

Paeonia atlantica, commonly known as the Atlantic peony, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans and Turkey.

It features large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has deeply lobed, dark green leaves and a robust root system that helps it thrive in rocky, well-drained soils. It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.

Paeonia atlantica is also noted for its resilience to harsh environmental conditions.
