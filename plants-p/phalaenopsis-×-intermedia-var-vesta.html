Phalaenopsis × intermedia var. vesta

Phalaenopsis × intermedia var. vesta, commonly known as the Orchid Queen, is a hybrid orchid prized for its elegant, long-lasting blooms.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia and thrives in warm, humid environments. The plant features broad, dark green leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. It is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements.

Its botanical beauty makes it a favored ornamental plant in both gardens and homes.
