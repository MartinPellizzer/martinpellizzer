Pellegrinia pubiflora

Pellegrinia pubiflora, commonly known as the woolly pellgrinia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, hairy foliage that helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale pink or white. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
