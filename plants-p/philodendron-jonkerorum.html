Philodendron jonkerorum

Philodendron jonkerorum, commonly known as the Jonker's philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a glossy texture and prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and plant enthusiasts.
