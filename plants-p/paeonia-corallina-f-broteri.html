Paeonia corallina f. broteri

Paeonia corallina f. broteri, commonly known as the Broteri peony, is a rare and striking botanical variant of the coral peony.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range from pale pink to deep red, with a distinctive coral-like coloration. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of China and is known for its robust growth and long blooming period. Its foliage consists of large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and velvety in texture.

This cultivar is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and unique floral characteristics.
