Pelargonium ramosissimum

Pelargonium ramosissimum, commonly known as the rock geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features small, rounded leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Pelargonium ramosissimum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
