Peperomia pallida var. rurutensis

Peperomia pallida var. rurutensis, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are pale silver to bluish-green in color, with prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, typically appearing in late spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its low maintenance and unique foliage make it an attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
