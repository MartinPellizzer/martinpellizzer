Peperomia glassmanii

Peperomia glassmanii, commonly known as the Glassman's peperomia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in shaded, humid environments. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit, with small, waxy leaves that are often variegated in color. It produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, and it is valued as a popular houseplant due to its low maintenance requirements.

Peperomia glassmanii is also noted for its ability to tolerate low light conditions, making it an ideal choice for indoor gardening.
