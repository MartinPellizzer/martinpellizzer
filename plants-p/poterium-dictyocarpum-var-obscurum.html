Poterium dictyocarpum var. obscurum

Poterium dictyocarpum var. obscurum, commonly known as the dark-leafed fern, is a variant of the species native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and compact growth habit. The fronds are typically lance-shaped with a smooth, leathery texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

The plant's botanical significance lies in its unique morphological features and ecological role in tropical ecosystems.
