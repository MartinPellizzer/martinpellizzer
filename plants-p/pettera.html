Pettera

Pettera, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact rosette of velvety, waxy leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, white, and blue. Pettera prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
