Persicaria stricta

Persicaria stricta, commonly known as upright knotgrass, is a flowering plant in the Polygonaceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia and is characterized by its upright growth habit and slender stems. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and often have a reddish or purplish tinge.

Persicaria stricta is typically found in moist soils along streambanks, wet meadows, and other damp habitats.
