Peperomia cordilimba

Peperomia cordilimba, commonly known as the heart-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are heart-shaped and often have a deep green color with reddish veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow, followed by spindle-shaped berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Peperomia cordilimba is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
