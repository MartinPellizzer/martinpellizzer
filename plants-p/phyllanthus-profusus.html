Phyllanthus profusus

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and smooth, often waxy leaves. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are alternate and have a distinctive, almost heart-shaped appearance.
