Prunus jamasakura f. kuchibeni-odora

Prunus jamasakura f. kuchibeni-odora, commonly known as the Kuchibeni-odora cherry blossom, is a variant of the Japanese cherry tree known for its distinct fragrance.

This cultivar is characterized by its pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a delicate aesthetic to gardens and parks. The plant is native to Japan and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. Its leaves are dark green and oval-shaped, providing a lush backdrop to the blossoms.

The Kuchibeni-odora variety is prized not only for its ornamental value but also for its subtle, sweet scent that enhances the sensory experience of spring.
