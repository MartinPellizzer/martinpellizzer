Pedicularis Macrosiphon Var. Tribuloides

Pedicularis macrosiphon var. tribuloides is a rare and striking member of the Orobanchaceae family known for its unique floral morphology. This variant is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and thrives in cool, moist environments. Its distinctive red-orange petals and elongated sepals make it a subject of botanical interest. Due to its rarity and ecological significance, conservation efforts are essential to protect this fascinating flora.
