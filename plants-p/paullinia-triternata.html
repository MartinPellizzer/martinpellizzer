Paullinia triternata

Paullinia triternata, commonly known as the Brazilian pepper tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often trifoliate, giving it its scientific name.
