Picris hieracioides

Picris hieracioides, commonly known as goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to Europe, North Africa, and western Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, branched stems and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and roadsides. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers bloom from late spring to early autumn.

Picris hieracioides is also known for its medicinal uses in traditional herbal remedies.
