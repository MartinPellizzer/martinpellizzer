Phaca glaberrima

Phaca glaberrima, commonly known as the smooth phaca, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The flowers are yellow and bloom in the summer months, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
