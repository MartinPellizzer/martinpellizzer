Polystichum brongniartianum

Polystichum brongniartianum, commonly known as the Brongniart's wood fern, is a species of fern native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The fronds are bi- or tri-pinnate, with a dark green, leathery texture and a distinctive, narrow, elongated shape. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded, forested areas.

This fern is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with appropriate conditions.
