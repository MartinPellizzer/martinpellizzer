Pedicularis comosa subsp. campestris

Pedicularis comosa subsp.

campestris, commonly known as the field monkey orchid, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Orobanchaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, spurred flowers that resemble a monkey's face.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has hairy stems and leaves that are opposite and lance-shaped.
