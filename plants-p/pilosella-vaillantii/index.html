Pilosella Vaillantii

Pilosella vaillantii is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe. It is known for its small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. This species thrives in rocky and open habitats, often found in alpine meadows and slopes. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Protecting Pilosella vaillantii is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its fragile ecosystem.
