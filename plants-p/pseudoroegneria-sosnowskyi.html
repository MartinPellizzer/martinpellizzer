Pseudoroegneria sosnowskyi

Pseudoroegneria sosnowskyi, commonly known as Sosnowsky's foxtail, is a perennial grass native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and dense, spike-like inflorescences that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive, feathery flowers.
