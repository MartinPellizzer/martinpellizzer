Peperomia pinulana

Peperomia pinulana, commonly known as the pineapple peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and silver, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, followed by tiny, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant.
