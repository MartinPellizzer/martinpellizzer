Parasenecio tsinlingensis

Parasenecio tsinlingensis, commonly known as the Tsingling parasenecio, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in well-drained soils. It is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
