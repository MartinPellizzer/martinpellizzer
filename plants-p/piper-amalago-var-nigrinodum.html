Piper amalago var. nigrinodum

Piper amalago var. nigrinodum, commonly known as black node pepper, is a variant of the Piper amalago species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black nodes on the stem, which give it its distinctive appearance. The plant is a climbing vine with opposite, ovate leaves that have a slightly serrated margin. Its small, greenish-white flowers are followed by elliptical, dark red to black berries.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
