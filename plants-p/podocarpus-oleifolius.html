Podocarpus oleifolius

Podocarpus oleifolius, commonly known as the African olive or yellow podocarp, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub that can grow up to 10 meters tall. The plant has lance-shaped, leathery leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It produces small, fragrant yellow flowers followed by woody, cone-like fruits containing edible seeds.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional medicinal uses.
