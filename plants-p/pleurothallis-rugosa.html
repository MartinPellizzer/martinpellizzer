Pleurothallis rugosa

Pleurothallis rugosa, commonly known as the wrinkled pleurothallis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This epiphytic plant typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are small and often pale or greenish in color, with a unique, wrinkled appearance.

The plant's foliage is dense and leathery, adapted to retain moisture in its natural habitat.
