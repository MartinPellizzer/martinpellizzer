Panicum ventricosum

Panicum ventricosum, commonly known as purple lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and distinctive purple-tinted inflorescences. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green hue and produces small, spike-like clusters of flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Panicum ventricosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone regions.
