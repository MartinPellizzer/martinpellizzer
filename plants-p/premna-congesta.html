Premna congesta

Premna congesta, commonly known as the clustered premna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Verbenaceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in summer. Its fruits are small, three-lobed berries that contain seeds.
