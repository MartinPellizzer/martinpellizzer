Philodendron smaragdinum

Philodendron smaragdinum, commonly known as the Emerald Heart Philodendron, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a glossy texture. The plant has a climbing habit and can grow quite large if provided with adequate support. Its leaves often develop a striking variegation of light green and white patterns.

Philodendron smaragdinum thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its lush appearance.
