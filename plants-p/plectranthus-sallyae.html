Plectranthus sallyae

Plectranthus sallyae, commonly known as Sally's mint, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a slightly fuzzy appearance. It produces small, tubular flowers in shades of purple and blue, which bloom in clusters during the summer months.

The plant is valued for its aromatic foliage and is often used in ornamental gardening.
