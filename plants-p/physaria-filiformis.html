Physaria filiformis

Physaria filiformis, commonly known as the slender bladderpod, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its seed pods are inflated and contain several small, rounded seeds.

Physaria filiformis is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to thrive in arid environments.
