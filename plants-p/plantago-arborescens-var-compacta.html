Plantago arborescens var. compacta

Plantago arborescens var. compacta, commonly known as the compact plantain, is a perennial herb native to regions of Europe and Asia.

It features a rosette of broad, waxy, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in dense clusters during the summer months. It is adapted to grow in dry, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.

Its medicinal properties have been traditionally used for their soothing and anti-inflammatory effects.
