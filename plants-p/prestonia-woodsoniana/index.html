Prestonia Woodsoniana

Woodson's prestonia, scientifically known as Prestonia woodsoniana, is a rare and endangered flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central America. This unique species belongs to the family Apocynaceae, commonly known as the dogbane family, and is characterized by its striking floral structures and medicinal properties. Found primarily in the cloud forests of Costa Rica, it thrives in humid, shaded environments with rich, well-drained soil. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, conservation efforts are critical to its survival. Its beauty and ecological significance make it a subject of both scientific interest and botanical fascination.
