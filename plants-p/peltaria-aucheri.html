Peltaria aucheri

Peltaria aucheri, commonly known as the Persian shield, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Iran and Afghanistan.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its striking, shield-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Peltaria aucheri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and is often used in rock gardens or as a container plant.
