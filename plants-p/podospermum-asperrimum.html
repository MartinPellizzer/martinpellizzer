Podospermum asperrimum

Podospermum asperrimum, commonly known as the rough podospermum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal areas. The plant produces small, showy flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are characterized by their rough, hairy texture, which gives the plant its common name.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
