Panicum imbelle

Panicum imbelle, commonly known as little bluestem, is a grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and tufted inflorescence that turns golden in the fall. This plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for wildlife.

Panicum imbelle is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
