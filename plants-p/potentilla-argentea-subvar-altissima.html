Potentilla argentea subvar. altissima

Potentilla argentea subvar. altissima, commonly known as silver cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its silver-white underside leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. It is a perennial shrub that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscaping.
