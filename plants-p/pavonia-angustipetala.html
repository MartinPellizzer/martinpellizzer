Pavonia angustipetala

Pavonia angustipetala, commonly known as narrow-leaved pavonia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet. The plant features slender, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of pink to red flowers. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Pavonia angustipetala is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
