Ponerorchis gongshanensis

Ponerorchis gongshanensis, commonly known as the Gongshan ghost orchid, is a rare and enigmatic orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique and intricate floral structure. The plant produces small, pale green to white flowers that are often overlooked due to their subtle appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in mountainous regions with rich, moist soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a critically endangered species.
