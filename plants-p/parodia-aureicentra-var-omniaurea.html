Parodia aureicentra var. omniaurea

Parodia aureicentra var. omniaurea, commonly known as the golden crown cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to southern Africa.

It features a dense covering of white to pale yellow spines, which give it its common name. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its green body. Its stems are ribbed and can develop a reddish hue when exposed to full sunlight.

This variety is prized for its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
