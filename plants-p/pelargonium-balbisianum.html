Pelargonium balbisianum

Pelargonium balbisianum, commonly known as the Balbisian geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, typically blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Pelargonium balbisianum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for gardens and landscapes.
