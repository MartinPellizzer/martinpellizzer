Peperomia tenuipila

Peperomia tenuipila, commonly known as the narrow-leaf peperomia, is a compact evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slight waxy coating, and it produces delicate, spadix-like inflorescences. The plant has a short, thick stem and is often grown as a houseplant due to its low maintenance requirements. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage and compact growth habit make it a popular choice for collectors and indoor gardeners.
