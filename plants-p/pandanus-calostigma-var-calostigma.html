Pandanus calostigma var. calostigma

Pandanus calostigma var. calostigma, commonly known as the red pandanus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves that are typically bright green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that develop into three-lobed, fibrous fruits. It thrives in humid, coastal environments and is often used in traditional crafts due to its durable leaves.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and landscapes.
