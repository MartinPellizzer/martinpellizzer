Peperomia grantii

Peperomia grantii, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with a distinctive silvery-gray hue and prominent veins, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically greenish-white in color, which are followed by dark red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia grantii is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and moderate humidity to flourish.
