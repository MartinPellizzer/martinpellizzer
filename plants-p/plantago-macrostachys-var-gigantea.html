Plantago macrostachys var. gigantea

Plantago macrostachys var. gigantea, commonly known as the giant plantain, is a variant of the common plantain species.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, greenish flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

This variety is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
