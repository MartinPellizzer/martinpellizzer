Pyrola rotundifolia subsp. chinensis

Pyrola rotundifolia subsp.

chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese roundleaf wintergreen, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family. It is characterized by its small, evergreen leaves that are round or oval in shape.

The plant produces white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. It typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often on rocky or humus-rich soils.
