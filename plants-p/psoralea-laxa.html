Psoralea laxa

Psoralea laxa, commonly known as the slender psoralea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stem and small, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in spring. Its seeds are enclosed in pods and are adapted for dispersal by wind.
