Platyaechmea distichantha f. albiflora

Platyaechmea distichantha f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered star cactus, is a hybrid form of the species Platyaechmea distichantha.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its compact, rounded shape and its striking white flowers. The plant features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are adapted to store water, typical of cacti. Its flowers are usually arranged in a star-like pattern, adding to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
