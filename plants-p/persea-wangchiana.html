Persea wangchiana

Persea wangchiana, commonly known as the Yunnan avocado, is a small evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a smooth, green drupe that matures to a yellowish color, containing a single seed.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential economic importance in horticulture.
