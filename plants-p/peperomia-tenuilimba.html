Peperomia tenuilimba

Peperomia tenuilimba, commonly known as the narrow-leaf peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, waxy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly textured surface. The plant produces delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia tenuilimba is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
