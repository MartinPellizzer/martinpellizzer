Pelargonium × domesticum

Pelargonium × domesticum, commonly known as the geranium, is a hybrid plant species within the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, five-petaled flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and purple. The plant typically has rounded, lobed leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and as houseplants.

Pelargonium × domesticum prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
