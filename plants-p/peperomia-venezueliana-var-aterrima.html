Peperomia venezueliana var. aterrima

Peperomia venezueliana var. aterrima, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, waxy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that emerge from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
