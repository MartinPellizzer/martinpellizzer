Pontederia reflexa

Pontederia reflexa, commonly known as purple rush or pickerelweed, is a perennial aquatic plant native to the wetlands of the Americas.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that grow in dense clusters, often with a waxy coating. The plant produces showy, spike-like inflorescences with purple or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Its roots help stabilize soil in wetland environments, and it is often used in wetland restoration projects.

Pontederia reflexa is also a valuable food source for waterfowl and other wetland wildlife.
