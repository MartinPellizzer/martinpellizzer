Pandanus fatyanion

Pandanus fatyanion, commonly known as the giant pandanus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish-green inflorescences that bloom during the dry season. Its roots are fibrous and help stabilize the soil in its natural habitat.

The plant is often used in traditional crafts and as a source of thatch.
