Polygonum elatiusculum

Polygonum elatiusculum, commonly known as the small-leaf smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It typically grows in wet or moist soils, such as marshes, pond edges, and stream banks. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often reddish or purple-tinged, and it produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or red. Its stems are usually erect and can reach heights of up to 1 meter.

Polygonum elatiusculum is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed or wetland habitats, making it an important component of aquatic and riparian ecosystems.
