Pyrus lantana

Pyrus lantana, commonly known as the common whitebeam, is a deciduous tree native to Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its white to pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. The tree has small, elliptical leaves that are typically covered in a fine, whitish down. Its fruit is a small, edible pome that ripens in late summer.

Pyrus lantana is often found in woodlands and along stream banks, where it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.
