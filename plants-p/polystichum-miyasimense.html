Polystichum miyasimense

Polystichum miyasimense, commonly known as the Miasimense sword fern, is a species of fern native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its leathery, dark green fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its sporangia are clustered in sori on the underside of the fronds, a common feature in ferns.

Polystichum miyasimense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
