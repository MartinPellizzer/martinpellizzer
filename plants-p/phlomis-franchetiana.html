Phlomis franchetiana

Phlomis franchetiana, commonly known as the Franchet's blazingstar, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and aromatic foliage. Its showy yellow-orange flower heads appear in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Phlomis franchetiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance and resilience.
