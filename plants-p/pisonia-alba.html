Pisonia alba

Pisonia alba, commonly known as the white pisonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its showy, white, clustered flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant produces small, round, yellow fruits that contain numerous seeds. Pisonia alba typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters.

Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.
