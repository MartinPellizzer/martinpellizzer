Psilostrophe tagetina

Psilostrophe tagetina, commonly known as the golden crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow ray flowers and bristly foliage. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
