Piper listeri

Piper listeri, commonly known as the Hawaiian pepper plant, is a flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the Hawaiian Islands and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically green when mature. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in coastal areas.

Due to its restricted range, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
