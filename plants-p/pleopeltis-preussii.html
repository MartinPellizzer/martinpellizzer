Pleopeltis preussii

Pleopeltis preussii, commonly known as the Preuss's rock fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, dense foliage and rhizomes that anchor it to tree trunks or rocks. The fronds are typically leathery and have a dark green color with a rough texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in indoor gardens.
