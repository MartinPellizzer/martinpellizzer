Pultenaea montana

Pultenaea montana, commonly known as mountain pultenaea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Pultenaea montana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native ecosystems.
