Paronychia coquimbensis var. coquimbensis

Paronychia coquimbensis var. coquimbensis, commonly known as the Coquimbo dandelion, is a flowering plant native to Chile.

Its succulent nature allows it to store water, making it well-adapted to its harsh native habitat.
