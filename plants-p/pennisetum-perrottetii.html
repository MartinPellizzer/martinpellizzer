Pennisetum perrottetii

Pennisetum perrottetii, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that produce dense, fluffy plumes of purple to pinkish-white flowers in the fall. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often tinged with silver or blue, adding visual interest throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Pennisetum perrottetii is known for its drought tolerance and ability to attract birds and insects.
