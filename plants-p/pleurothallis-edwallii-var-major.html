Pleurothallis edwallii var. major

Pleurothallis edwallii var. major, commonly known as the giant pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous blooms, making it a striking addition to any collection. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a dense rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This orchid thrives in high humidity and partial shade, requiring careful attention to watering and light conditions for optimal growth.
