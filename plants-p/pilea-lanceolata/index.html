Pilea Lanceolata

Pilea lanceolata is a charming evergreen plant known for its elegant, lance-shaped leaves. Native to China, it thrives in shady, humid environments and is popular among indoor gardeners. Its compact size and lush foliage make it an ideal choice for small spaces and tabletop displays. Easy to care for, it requires minimal watering and indirect sunlight to flourish. This plant adds a touch of natural beauty to any home or office setting.
