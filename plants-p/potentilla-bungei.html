Potentilla bungei

Potentilla bungei, commonly known as the Bunge cinquefoil, is a perennial flowering plant native to central Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun conditions. Its leaves are usually divided into five leaflets, giving it the common name "cinquefoil.".

Potentilla bungei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
