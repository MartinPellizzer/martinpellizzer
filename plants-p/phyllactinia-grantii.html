Phyllactinia grantii

Phyllactinia grantii, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spherical clusters of white to pale yellow flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Phyllactinia grantii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
