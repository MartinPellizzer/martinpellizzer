Polystachya suaveolens

Polystachya suaveolens, commonly known as the sweet-scented bamboo, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features tall, slender stems with clusters of small, fragrant flowers that emit a pleasant aroma. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, contributing to its bamboo-like appearance.

Polystachya suaveolens is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its subtle, sweet fragrance.
