Pleomele angustifolia var. angustior

Pleomele angustifolia var. angustior, commonly known as the narrow-leafed pleomele, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, often in shades of white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and shaded garden settings.
