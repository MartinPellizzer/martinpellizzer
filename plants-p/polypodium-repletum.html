Polypodium repletum

Polypodium repletum, commonly known as the hairy shield fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The fronds are pinnately divided and can reach lengths of up to 1 meter.
