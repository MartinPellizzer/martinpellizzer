Podochilus cumingii

Podochilus cumingii, commonly known as the Cuming's orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including countries like India, Thailand, and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts for its delicate beauty.
