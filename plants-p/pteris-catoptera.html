Pteris catoptera

Pteris catoptera, commonly known as the mirror fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds that resemble a fan. The fronds are typically green and have a smooth, glossy surface, which contributes to its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
