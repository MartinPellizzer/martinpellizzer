Picnomon spinosum

Picnomon spinosum, commonly known as the spiny picnomon, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny, fleshy stems and small, clustered leaves that help reduce water loss. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a vibrant touch to its desert habitat. Its adaptations, such as thick cuticles and deep root systems, allow it to thrive in nutrient-poor, sandy soils.

Picnomon spinosum is often used in rock gardens due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
