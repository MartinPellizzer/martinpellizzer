Protasparagus oliveri

Protasparagus oliveri, commonly known as the Oliver's protasparagus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Asparagaceae and is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and small, clustered leaves.
