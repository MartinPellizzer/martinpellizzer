Physaria goodrichii

Physaria goodrichii, commonly known as the Goodrich's bluestem, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard plants, and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers in late spring, which are clustered in racemes. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Physaria goodrichii is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
