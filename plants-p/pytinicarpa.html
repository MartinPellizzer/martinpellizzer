Pytinicarpa

Pytinicarpa, commonly known as the starfruit tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically oval or elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into the distinctive star-shaped fruit. The fruit, which is edible, has a sweet and tangy flavor with a crisp texture.

Pytinicarpa is valued not only for its fruit but also for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and unique fruit appearance.
