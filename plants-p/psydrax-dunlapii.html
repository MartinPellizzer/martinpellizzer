Psydrax dunlapii

Psydrax dunlapii, commonly known as the red-flowered psidium, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that develop into edible, red berries resembling small tomatoes. These berries are often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil and partial shade.
