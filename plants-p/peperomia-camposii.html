Peperomia camposii

Peperomia camposii, commonly known as the Brazilian peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically oval or elliptical in shape, with a waxy surface that helps it retain moisture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color, which are followed by small, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage, it is often used as a decorative plant in both home and office environments.
