Peperomia sylvestris

Peperomia sylvestris, commonly known as the forest peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features upright stems and oval, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia sylvestris is valued for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
