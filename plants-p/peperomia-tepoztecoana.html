Peperomia tepoztecoana

Peperomia tepoztecoana, commonly known as the Tepozteco peperomia, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a compact, low-growing plant with thick, fleshy leaves that are often variegated with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Peperomia tepoztecoana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
