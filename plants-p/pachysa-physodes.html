Pachysa physodes

Pachysa physodes, commonly known as the showy pachysandra, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a waxy texture, and its flowers are small, white, and clustered in umbels. The plant thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to form a dense ground cover.

Pachysa physodes is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing habitat and food for various insects and birds.
