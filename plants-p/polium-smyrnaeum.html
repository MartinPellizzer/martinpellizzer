Polium smyrnaeum

Polium smyrnaeum, commonly known as the Smyrna thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features spiny leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its botanical characteristics include a robust root system and a preference for well-drained soils.

It is often found in dry, rocky areas and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
