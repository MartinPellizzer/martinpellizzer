Pilosella retractata

Pilosella retractata, commonly known as the narrowleaf mouse-ear, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragworts, and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, giving it a distinctive appearance.
