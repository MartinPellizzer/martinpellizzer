Piper hoehnei

Piper hoehnei, commonly known as the Hoehne's pepper or Brazilian pepper tree, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces small, spherical berries that are typically red when ripe. It is often grown for its ornamental value and has a long history of use in traditional medicine.

However, it can become invasive in certain environments if not managed properly.
