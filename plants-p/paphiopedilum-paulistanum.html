Paphiopedilum paulistanum

Paphiopedilum paulistanum, commonly known as the Paulista orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Brazil.

It features a pseudobulb with a single, fleshy leaf and produces a single or few showy flowers with a distinctive lip. The flowers are typically pink or purple with yellow markings, and they bloom during the dry season. The plant prefers shaded, moist environments and has a unique root system adapted to its habitat.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
