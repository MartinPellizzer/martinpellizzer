Pelargonium × resurgens

Pelargonium × resurgens, commonly known as the regal geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and purple. The plant typically features upright stems and heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. Native to South Africa, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Pelargonium × resurgens is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
