Pelexia hamata

Pelexia hamata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pelexia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland habitats. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, pale blue or purple flowers. It has a fibrous root system and is often found in open, well-drained soils.

Pelexia hamata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
