Phyllotaenium lindenii var. magnificum

Phyllotaenium lindenii var. magnificum, commonly known as the golden star hibiscus, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers with a prominent central stigma.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia, where it thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that provide a striking contrast to its showy blooms. The flowers typically open in the morning and close in the evening, adding a unique charm to its botanical appeal.
