Piper corylistachyum var. magnifolium

Piper corylistachyum var. magnifolium, commonly known as the giant arrowleaf pepper, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in spikes. Its fruits are berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded, humid environments.
