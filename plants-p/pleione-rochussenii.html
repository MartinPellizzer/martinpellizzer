Pleione rochussenii

Pleione rochussenii, commonly known as the Rochussen's pleione, is a rare and elegant orchid native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to survive in its cool, high-altitude habitat. Its leaves are usually broad and grass-like, and it thrives in shaded, moist environments with well-drained soil.

Due to its beauty and rarity, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
