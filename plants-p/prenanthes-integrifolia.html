Prenanthes integrifolia

Prenanthes integrifolia, commonly known as the smooth arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features arrow-shaped leaves that are smooth and entire, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as wetlands and stream banks.

Its foliage and floral structure make it a valuable plant for wetland restoration and ecological gardening.
