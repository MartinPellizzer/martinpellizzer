Persicaria longiseta f. breviseta

Persicaria longiseta f. breviseta, commonly known as the short-haired smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its distinctive nodes and swollen base. The plant features slender, erect stems that are often reddish in color, and its leaves are lanceolate with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white, and it thrives in moist, sunny environments such as wet meadows and stream banks.

This variant is distinguished by its shorter, more compact inflorescences compared to the typical form.
