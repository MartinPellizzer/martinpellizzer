Psychotria Calorhamnus

Psychotria calorhamnus is a striking member of the Rubiaceae family known for its vibrant red flowers and toxic properties. Native to the cloud forests of Central and South America, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine, though caution is advised due to its potent alkaloids. The plant's unique appearance and ecological significance make it a subject of both scientific interest and cultural reverence. Despite its beauty, Psychotria calorhamnus remains a mysterious and sometimes dangerous species.
