Persea kadooriei

Persea kadooriei, commonly known as the Kadoorie's persimmon, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves with a glossy surface. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by oval, orange-red fruits with a sweet, astringent flavor. Its habitat is primarily in lowland rainforests, where it thrives in humid, shaded conditions.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has cultural significance in its native region.
