Pittosporum glabratum var. angustifolium

Pittosporum glabratum var. angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features elliptical, glossy, dark green leaves that are typically narrow and pointed. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
