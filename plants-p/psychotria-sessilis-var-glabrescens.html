Psychotria sessilis var. glabrescens

Psychotria sessilis var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-leaved psychotria, is a species of flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glabrous, lanceolate leaves and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
