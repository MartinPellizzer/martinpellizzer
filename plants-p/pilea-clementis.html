Pilea clementis

Pilea clementis, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features glossy, round, slightly wavy leaves that resemble small coins, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in spring, followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Pilea clementis is known for its ease of care and ability to purify indoor air.
