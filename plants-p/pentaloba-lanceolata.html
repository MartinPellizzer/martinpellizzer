Pentaloba lanceolata

Pentaloba lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf pentaloba, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests or undergrowth. Its botanical features include simple, alternate leaves and a fibrous root system.

It is often found in association with other understory plants in humid, tropical climates.
