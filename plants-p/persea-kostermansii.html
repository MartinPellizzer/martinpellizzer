Persea kostermansii

Persea kostermansii, commonly known as the Koster's avocado, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Caribbean islands.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant flowers. The tree produces small, yellowish-green fruits that are typically harvested when mature. The fruit has a thick, leathery skin and a creamy, nutty flavor when ripe.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential culinary uses.
