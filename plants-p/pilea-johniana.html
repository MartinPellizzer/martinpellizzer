Pilea johniana

Pilea johniana, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features round, shield-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea johniana is valued for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care, though it can become invasive if not properly managed.
