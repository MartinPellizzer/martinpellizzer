Parapteroceras speciosum

Parapteroceras speciosum, commonly known as the ornate sea hare, is a species of sea slug found in tropical and subtropical marine environments.

The plant-like appearance of its body is due to the presence of a calcareous, branching skeleton that resembles a coral or algae. This unique morphology aids in camouflage and provides structural support.

Parapteroceras speciosum plays a role in marine ecosystems by contributing to the biodiversity of shallow coastal waters.
