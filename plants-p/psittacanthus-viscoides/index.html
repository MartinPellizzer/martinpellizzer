Psittacanthus Viscoides

Velvetleaf, scientifically known as Psittacanthus viscoides, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia. Its velvety, waxy leaves give it a unique texture that sets it apart from other foliage plants. This species is popular among indoor gardeners for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in low-light conditions. It features clusters of small, delicate white flowers that bloom throughout the year. Despite its beauty, it requires careful watering and well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
