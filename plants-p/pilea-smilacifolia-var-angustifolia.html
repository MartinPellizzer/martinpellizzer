Pilea smilacifolia var. angustifolia

Pilea smilacifolia var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pilea, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a waxy texture and a dark green coloration. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.
