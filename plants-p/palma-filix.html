Palma-filix

Palma-filix, commonly known as the sacred palm, is a unique plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves. The plant is often found in humid, tropical climates with well-drained soil. Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ornamental and ecological purposes.

However, it is relatively rare and requires specific care to thrive in cultivation.
