Protea alopecuroides

Protea alopecuroides, commonly known as the silver protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that resemble a cluster of flowers, with white to pinkish petals and a central yellow to orange style. The plant is native to the Western Cape region and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. It is a shrub that can grow up to 1.5 meters tall, featuring narrow, leathery leaves.

Protea alopecuroides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its long-lasting blooms.
