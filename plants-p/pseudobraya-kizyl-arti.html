Pseudobraya kizyl-arti

Pseudobraya kizyl-arti, commonly known as the Kizyl-arti bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

Pseudobraya kizyl-arti is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
