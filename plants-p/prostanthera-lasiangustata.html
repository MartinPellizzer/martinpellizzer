Prostanthera lasiangustata

Prostanthera lasiangustata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue to purple in color, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are narrow and serrated, with a fragrant scent when crushed.

Prostanthera lasiangustata is often found in heathlands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
