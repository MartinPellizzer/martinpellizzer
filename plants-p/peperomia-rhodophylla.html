Peperomia rhodophylla

Peperomia rhodophylla, commonly known as the red-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, and often variegated leaves that range in color from deep green to red and purple, depending on light exposure. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage makes it a standout ornamental plant for both indoor and outdoor settings.
