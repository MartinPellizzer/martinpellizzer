Panicum brizanthum

Panicum brizanthum, commonly known as purpletop millet, is a flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the southeastern United States and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and clusters of purple or pinkish flowers that bloom in the summer. It is often used as a ornamental grass due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.

Panicum brizanthum is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for wildlife.
