Pogospermum parviflorum

Pogospermum parviflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered pogospermum, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shady environments. The plant features small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
