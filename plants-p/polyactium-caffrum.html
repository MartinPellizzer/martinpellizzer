Polyactium caffrum

Polyactium caffrum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant has fleshy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight to flourish.

Polyactium caffrum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
