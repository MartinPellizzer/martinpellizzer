Prinos nitidus

Prinos nitidus, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of fine, velvety hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, often appearing in shades of yellow or green.

Prinos nitidus is often found in moist, shaded environments and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
