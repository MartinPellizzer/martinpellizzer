Phaulopsis symmetrica

Phaulopsis symmetrica, commonly known as the symmetric phalaenopsis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, symmetrical flower structure and vibrant, often bi-colored petals. The plant typically produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in succession throughout the growing season. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the humid, shaded environments where it naturally occurs.

Phaulopsis symmetrica is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
