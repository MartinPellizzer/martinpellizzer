Polycarpon robbairea

Polycarpon robbairea, commonly known as the Robbairea morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, twining stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.
