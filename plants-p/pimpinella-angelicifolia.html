Pimpinella angelicifolia

Pimpinella angelicifolia, commonly known as angel's wings, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are finely divided and often covered in a soft, silvery down. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in umbels, typical of the Apiaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Pimpinella angelicifolia is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and historical medicinal uses.
