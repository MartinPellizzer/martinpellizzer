Pyrus monopyrena

Pyrus monopyrena, commonly known as the Pyrenean pear, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to the Pyrenees mountain range, where it thrives in temperate climates. Pyrus monopyrena is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
