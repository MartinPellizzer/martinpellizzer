Pelargonium × hesilrigeae

Pelargonium × hesilrigeae, commonly known as the orange-leaf geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red, and its distinctive orange or red-veined leaves. Native to South Africa, this plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and containers. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Pelargonium × hesilrigeae is also popular for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
