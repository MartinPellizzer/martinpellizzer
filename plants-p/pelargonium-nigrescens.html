Pelargonium nigrescens

Pelargonium nigrescens, commonly known as the dark geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its dark purple to black foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, pink or white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. It is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its unique coloration and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a decorative plant.
