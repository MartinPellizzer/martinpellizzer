Protasparagus macowanii

Protasparagus macowanii, commonly known as the Macowan's asparagus, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and purple. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping.
