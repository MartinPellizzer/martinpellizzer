Piperi betlum

Piperi betlum, commonly known as betel nut.

It is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia, belonging to the Piperaceae family. The plant is characterized by its large, elongated leaves and produces a fruit known as the betel nut, which is the seed of the fruit. The tree can grow up to 15 meters tall and thrives in warm, humid climates.

It is widely cultivated for its nut, which is used in traditional chewing practices and has various medicinal properties.
