Pectis oligocephala var. oligocephala

Pectis oligocephala var. oligocephala, commonly known as the narrowhead pectis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, annual herb with slender stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or gravelly soils.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
