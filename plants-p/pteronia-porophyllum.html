Pteronia porophyllum

Pteronia porophyllum, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces showy, clustered white or pale blue flowers that bloom in winter. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Pteronia porophyllum is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
