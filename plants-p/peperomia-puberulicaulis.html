Peperomia puberulicaulis

Peperomia puberulicaulis, commonly known as the hairy-stemmed peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive textured appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia puberulicaulis is relatively easy to care for, requiring minimal watering and moderate humidity levels.
