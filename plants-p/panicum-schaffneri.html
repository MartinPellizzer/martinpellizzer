Panicum schaffneri

Panicum schaffneri, commonly known as the Schaffner's grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of a panicle of spikelets, which are small, clustered flower clusters.

Panicum schaffneri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
