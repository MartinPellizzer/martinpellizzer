Pogostemon imberbis

Pogostemon imberbis, commonly known as the smooth pogostemon, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The leaves are used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties.
