Pandorea pandorana subsp. austrocaledonica

Pandorea pandorana subsp.

austrocaledonica, commonly known as the Northern Territory pandorea, is a climbing shrub native to northern Australia. It features woody stems with thorn-like projections and heart-shaped, dark green leaves.

The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is often cultivated in gardens and natural reserves.
