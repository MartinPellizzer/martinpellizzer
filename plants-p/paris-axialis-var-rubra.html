Paris axialis var. rubra

Paris axialis var. rubra, commonly known as the red-flowered wood sorrel, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive reddish hue, and its flowers are typically pink or red, with five petals. The plant grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks. It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its tuberous roots.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
