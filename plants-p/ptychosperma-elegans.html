Ptychosperma elegans

Ptychosperma elegans, commonly known as the slender sago palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery, pinnate leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by black, spherical fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens.
