Peperomia obtusifolia f. pusilla

Peperomia obtusifolia f. pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery bloom, giving them a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
