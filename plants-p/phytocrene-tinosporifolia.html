Phytocrene tinosporifolia

Phytocrene tinosporifolia, commonly known as the Tinospora leaf plant, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually heart-shaped or ovate. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by elongated, berry-like fruits. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional medicinal uses in some cultures.
