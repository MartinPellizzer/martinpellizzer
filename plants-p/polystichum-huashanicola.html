Polystichum huashanicola

Polystichum huashanicola, commonly known as the Huashan sword fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its lanceolate, serrated leaves that form a dense, upright rosette. The fronds are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, with spore-producing structures on the underside. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its unique morphology and habitat make it a notable species in the study of fern botany.
