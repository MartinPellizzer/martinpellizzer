Pleurothallis scandens

Pleurothallis scandens, commonly known as the climbing orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its vine-like growth habit. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Pleurothallis scandens is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
