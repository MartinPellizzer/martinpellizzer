Philodendron genevieveanum

Philodendron genevieveanum, commonly known as the Genevieve's Philodendron, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns of light and dark green. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its foliage can reach impressive sizes, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its lush appearance.
