Pyrus malus var. strepens

Pyrus malus var. strepens, commonly known as the Chinese white pear, is a variant of the common pear species native to China.

It is characterized by its pale yellow to white fruit with a smooth, thin skin. The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree, reaching heights of up to 8 meters. Its leaves are broad and ovate, with a smooth margin and a matte green upper surface.

The flowers are white and clustered, appearing in early spring before the leaves emerge.
