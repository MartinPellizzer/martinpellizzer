Peperomia × enervis

Peperomia × enervis, commonly known as the variegated peperomia, is a hybrid species within the Peperomia genus.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking variegated leaves that feature white and green patterns. Its foliage is often adorned with a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in its natural habitat.

Peperomia × enervis is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
