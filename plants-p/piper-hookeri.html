Piper hookeri

Piper hookeri, commonly known as the Hooker's pepper vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers that develop into peppery berries. It thrives in humid, warm environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is cultivated in some botanical gardens and greenhouses.
