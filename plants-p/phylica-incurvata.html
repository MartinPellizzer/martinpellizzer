Phylica incurvata

Phylica incurvata, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Proteaceae and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, red or orange flowers. The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in traditional medicine.
