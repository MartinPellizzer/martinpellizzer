Phoenix tenuis

Phoenix tenuis, commonly known as the slender date palm, is a species of palm native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its slender, unbranched trunk and narrow, feather-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that develop into dates, though it is less productive than other date palm species. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring well-drained soil and full sun.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
