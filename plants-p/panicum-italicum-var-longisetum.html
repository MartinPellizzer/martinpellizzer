Panicum italicum var. longisetum

Panicum italicum var. longisetum, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, tufted foliage. The plant produces long, slender inflorescences that bear small, spikelet-like flowers, which are typically reddish in color. Its foliage is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a versatile choice for garden settings.
