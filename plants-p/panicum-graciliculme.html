Panicum graciliculme

Panicum graciliculme, commonly known as slender-panicle grass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, branching inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as fields and roadsides. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a rough texture, and it produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.
