Palisota mannii

Palisota mannii, commonly known as the African waterleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and striking purple spathe. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near water bodies. Its foliage is used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Palisota mannii is also cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance.
