Primulina luochengensis

Primulina luochengensis, commonly known as the Luocheng primula, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, bell-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in mountainous regions with cool climates. Its foliage consists of broad, ovate leaves with a velvety texture.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
