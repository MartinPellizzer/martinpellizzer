Philodendron burle-marxii

Philodendron burle-marxii, commonly known as the Burle Marx Philodendron, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in length. The plant has a climbing habit and produces small, inconspicuous flowers. Its foliage is often variegated with white and green patterns, making it a popular ornamental plant.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.
