Poa fontqueri

Poa fontqueri, commonly known as the Fontqueri bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-blue foliage and tufted growth habit, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers.

Poa fontqueri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
