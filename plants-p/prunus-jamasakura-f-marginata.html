Prunus jamasakura f. marginata

Prunus jamasakura f. marginata, commonly known as the Japanese cherry blossom with a white margin, is a cultivar of the Prunus jamasakura species.

It is characterized by its delicate pink to white flowers, which have a distinctive white edge or margin. This ornamental tree is native to Japan and is highly valued for its aesthetic appeal during the spring blooming season. The leaves are typically ovate and turn a vibrant green during the growing season.

It is often planted in gardens and public spaces to enhance visual beauty and mark the arrival of spring.
