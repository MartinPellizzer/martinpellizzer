Prenanthes ochroleuca

Prenanthes ochroleuca, commonly known as the yellow arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves and yellowish-white spadix. The plant typically grows in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial to full shade. Its flowers are clustered in a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe.

Prenanthes ochroleuca is often found in wetlands and along stream banks, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
