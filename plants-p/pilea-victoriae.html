Pilea victoriae

Pilea victoriae, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its rosette of glossy, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It is a popular houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.

Pilea victoriae thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.
