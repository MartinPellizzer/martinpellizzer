Pelargonium × wuerthianum

Pelargonium × wuerthianum, commonly known as the German geranium, is a hybrid plant originating from crosses between Pelargonium hortorum and Pelargonium × odoratum.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically has heart-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated edge, and it thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight. It is often cultivated as a bedding plant or for its ornamental value in gardens.

Pelargonium × wuerthianum is known for its adaptability and ease of care, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced gardeners.
