Panicum tuberculatum

Panicum tuberculatum, commonly known as rough panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves with a rough, textured surface. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, two-lobed flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grassy areas.

Panicum tuberculatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
