Polygala chinensis var. dissitiflora

Polygala chinensis var. dissitiflora, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are alternate and pinnately compound, with small, oval-shaped leaflets.

The flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, with a distinctive structure that attracts insect pollinators.
