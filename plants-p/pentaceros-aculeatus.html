Pentaceros aculeatus

Pentaceros aculeatus, commonly known as the spiny sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny leaves and showy, blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in salty, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy coastal habitats. Its unique morphology helps it withstand harsh maritime conditions.

Pentaceros aculeatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in coastal ecosystems.
