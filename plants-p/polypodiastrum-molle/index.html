Polypodiastrum Molle

Polypodiastrum molle is a unique fern-like plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia. It is known for its delicate, feathery fronds that resemble those of true ferns, though it belongs to the genus Polypodiastrum. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest floors. Its ability to adapt to various soil types makes it a resilient species. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the botanical world.
