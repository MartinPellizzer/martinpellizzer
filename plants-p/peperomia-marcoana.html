Peperomia marcoana

Peperomia marcoana, commonly known as Marcoana Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically lance-shaped and covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental purposes in both home and office settings.
