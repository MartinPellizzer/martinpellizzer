Plantago radicata f. gigantea

Plantago radicata f. gigantea, commonly known as the giant plantain, is a variant of the common plantain species.

It is a perennial herb that features large, broad, heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environments.

Its robust growth and adaptability make it a notable species in botanical studies.
