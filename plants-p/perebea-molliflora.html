Perebea molliflora

Perebea molliflora, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a soft, velvety texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Perebea molliflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
