Pyrethrum glabratum

Pyrethrum glabratum, commonly known as the smooth pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant produces fine, hairless leaves and has a compact growth habit. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in natural pest control due to its pyrethrin content.

Pyrethrum glabratum thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.
