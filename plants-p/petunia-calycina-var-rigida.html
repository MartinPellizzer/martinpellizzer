Petunia calycina var. rigida

Petunia calycina var. rigida, commonly known as the rigid petunia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and compact, bushy form. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of blue, violet, and white, which bloom profusely throughout the growing season. Its foliage is dark green and slightly hairy, adding texture to its appearance.

This variety is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of care.
