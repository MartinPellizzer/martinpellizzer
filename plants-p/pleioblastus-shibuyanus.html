Pleioblastus shibuyanus

Pleioblastus shibuyanus, commonly known as the Shibuya bamboo, is a species of grass-like plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its slender, arching culms that often form dense clumps. The plant has narrow, bright green leaves that are smooth and slightly glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It is well-suited for shady or partially sunny environments and is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic value.

Due to its delicate appearance, it requires careful maintenance to thrive in garden settings.
