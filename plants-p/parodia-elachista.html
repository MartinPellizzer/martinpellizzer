Parodia elachista

Parodia elachista, commonly known as the miniature moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It is a small, globular plant with a compact, rounded stem that often develops a distinct, colorful crown. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are typically short-lived but striking. It is well adapted to drought conditions, storing water in its fleshy stems.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
