Premna alstonii var. mollis

Premna alstonii var. mollis, commonly known as silky premna, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
