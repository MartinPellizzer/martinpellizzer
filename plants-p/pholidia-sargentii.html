Pholidia sargentii

Pholidia sargentii, commonly known as the Sargent's holly, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are elliptical, leathery, and have a glossy appearance, often with a reddish or purplish tinge on the underside.

The fruit is a small, spherical berry that turns from green to red when mature, containing several seeds.
