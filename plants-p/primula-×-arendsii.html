Primula × arendsii

Primula × arendsii, commonly known as the Arendsii primrose, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Primulaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that come in a range of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–30 cm and produces foliage that is broadly ovate with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.

The species is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and is prized for its resilience and aesthetic appeal.
