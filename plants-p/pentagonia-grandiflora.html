Pentagonia grandiflora

Pentagonia grandiflora, commonly known as the giant pentagonia, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has palmate leaves with five lobes, giving it its scientific name. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical regions.

Pentagonia grandiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
