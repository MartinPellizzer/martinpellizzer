Pyrorchis

Pyrorchis, commonly known as the flame orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family.

These plants are native to Australia and are characterized by their striking, colorful flowers that resemble flames. The flowers typically feature vibrant red, orange, or yellow hues, which are highly attractive to pollinators. Pyrorchis species are often found in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in full sun conditions.

They are relatively easy to care for, making them popular among gardeners and orchid enthusiasts.
