Panicum italicum

Panicum italicum, commonly known as Italian grass or Italian wheatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Europe.

It features fine, wiry leaves that are typically blue-green in color, and it produces small, delicate panicles of flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in landscaping for its dense, soft foliage. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Panicum italicum is also valued for its ability to provide habitat for beneficial insects.
