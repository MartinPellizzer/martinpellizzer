Polyscias eupteronoides

Polyscias eupteronoides, commonly known as the broad-leaf soursop, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by yellow, egg-shaped fruits. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued both ornately and for its medicinal properties.
