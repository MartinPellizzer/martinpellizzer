Peperomia claussenii

Peperomia claussenii, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery-white underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and drought tolerance.

Peperomia claussenii thrives in well-draining soil and prefers moderate to warm temperatures.
