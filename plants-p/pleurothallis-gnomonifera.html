Pleurothallis gnomonifera

Pleurothallis gnomonifera, commonly known as the "Pineapple Orchid," is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small to medium-sized orchid that typically grows as an epiphyte on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a slender, branching stem with numerous small, clustered flowers that are usually yellow or greenish-white in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern around the stem.

Pleurothallis gnomonifera is valued for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant due to its specific care requirements.
