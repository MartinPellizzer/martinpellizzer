Pelargonium ochroleucum

Pelargonium ochroleucum, commonly known as the yellow-leaved geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its bright yellow leaves and showy pink to red flowers. Pelargonium ochroleucum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
