Paronychia polygonifolia var. elegantior

Paronychia polygonifolia var. elegantior, commonly known as the elegant cushion plant, is a low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of fleshy, blue-green leaves that form a compact, cushion-like structure. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
