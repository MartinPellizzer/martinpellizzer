Pitcairnia anomala

Pitcairnia anomala, commonly known as the red pitahaya or dragon fruit, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, Peru, and Ecuador. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of nutritious fruit.
