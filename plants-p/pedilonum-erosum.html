Pedilonum erosum

Pedilonum erosum, commonly known as the scarlet pimpernel, is a small flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and bright yellow flowers that open in the morning and close at dusk. The plant thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides. Its rapid growth and ability to spread through seeds make it a common weed in many regions.

The scarlet pimpernel has been historically associated with resilience and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
