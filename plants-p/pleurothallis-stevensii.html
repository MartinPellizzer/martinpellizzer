Pleurothallis stevensii

Pleurothallis stevensii, commonly known as the Stevens' orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, clustered inflorescences with small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it conserve moisture in its natural habitat.

Pleurothallis stevensii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
