Piriqueta douradinha

Piriqueta douradinha, commonly known as the golden tamarind.

It is a flowering plant native to Brazil, characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. Its botanical name is *Pithecolobium douradinho*, and it belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in tropical ecosystems.
