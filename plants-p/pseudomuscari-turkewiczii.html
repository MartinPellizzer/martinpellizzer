Pseudomuscari turkewiczii

Pseudomuscari turkewiczii, commonly known as Turkewicz's false muscari, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like foliage and clusters of bell-shaped, bluish-purple flowers. The plant typically blooms in early spring and is adapted to well-drained soils in full sun to partial shade.
