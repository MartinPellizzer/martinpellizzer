Pedilonum aristiferum

Pedilonum aristiferum, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking silver-white leaves and showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in spring. The plant belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and peppers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or arid environments.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
