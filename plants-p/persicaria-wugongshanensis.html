Persicaria wugongshanensis

Persicaria wugongshanensis, commonly known as the Wugongshan knotweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Polygonaceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Wugongshan region of Hubei Province. The plant is characterized by its upright stems, heart-shaped leaves, and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
