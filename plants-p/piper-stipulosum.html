Piper stipulosum

Piper stipulosum, commonly known as the slender pipevine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Piperaceae, which includes many species of climbing plants. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers and heart-shaped leaves. It typically grows as a vine, often climbing or trailing along the ground.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in tropical botany and horticulture.
