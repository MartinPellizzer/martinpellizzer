Polygala longicaulis var. flavicoma

Polygala longicaulis var. flavicoma, commonly known as the yellow-flowered milkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, often branched stems and small, clustered flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, while the flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow in color. This variant is distinguished by its yellowish hue compared to other varieties of Polygala longicaulis.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or scrub habitats.
