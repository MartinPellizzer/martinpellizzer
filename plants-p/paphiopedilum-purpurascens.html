Paphiopedilum purpurascens

Paphiopedilum purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-flowered slipper orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique slipper-shaped lip, which is a defining feature of the Paphiopedilum genus. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. Its flowers are usually purple or reddish-purple, with a contrasting white or yellow throat.

Paphiopedilum purpurascens is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty and fragrance.
