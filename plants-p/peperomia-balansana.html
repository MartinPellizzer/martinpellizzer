Peperomia balansana

Peperomia balansana, commonly known as the Balansan peperomia, is a compact, low-growing evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread slowly and form dense clumps.

It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.
