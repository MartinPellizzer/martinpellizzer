Pleurothallis holstii

Pleurothallis holstii, commonly known as the Holst's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a variety of colors ranging from white to pink and purple.

Pleurothallis holstii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
