Ptilotus gardneri var. inermis

Ptilotus gardneri var. inermis, commonly known as the smooth ptilotus, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, spiny seed pods. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are alternate and ovate, with a rough texture.

It is often found in sandy or gravelly soils in arid or semi-arid regions.
