Paphiopedilum appletonianum f. viride

Paphiopedilum appletonianum f. viride, commonly known as the green-flowered slipper orchid, is a rare and highly sought-after hybrid orchid.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped pouch that resembles a miniature shoe. The plant typically features dark green leaves and vibrant green flowers with a subtle yellowish tint. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often grown by orchid enthusiasts for its aesthetic appeal.
