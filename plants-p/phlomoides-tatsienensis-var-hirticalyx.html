Phlomoides tatsienensis var. hirticalyx

Phlomoides tatsienensis var. hirticalyx, commonly known as the hairy-leaf phlomoides, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features lanceolate leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color, arranged in whorled clusters.

This species is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
