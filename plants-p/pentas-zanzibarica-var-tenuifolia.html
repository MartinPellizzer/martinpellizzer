Pentas zanzibarica var. tenuifolia

Pentas zanzibarica var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the slenderleaf pentas, is a flowering plant native to Tanzania.

It is characterized by its delicate, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, giving the plant its varietal name "tenuifolia," which means "slender leaf.". It thrives in warm, sunny climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

This species is well-suited for gardens and containers, requiring well-drained soil and regular watering.
