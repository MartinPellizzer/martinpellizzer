Polygala amoenissima

Polygala amoenissima, commonly known as the sweet polygala, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

The flowers are usually pink or white, with a unique structure that resembles a small, intricate cup.
