Phyteuma orbiculare var. cordifolium

Phyteuma orbiculare var. cordifolium, commonly known as the heart-leaf phyteuma, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Europe.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pale yellow to orange, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are lanceolate to ovate, with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
