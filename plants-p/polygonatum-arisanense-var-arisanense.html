Polygonatum arisanense var. arisanense

Polygonatum arisanense var. arisanense, commonly known as the Arisan yellow flag.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia, particularly found in Japan and China. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers in late spring. Its leaves are lanceolate and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
