Phytocrene crinipes

Phytocrene crinipes, commonly known as the laceleaf fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that give it a lace-like appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining, nutrient-rich soil. Its reproductive structures include spores rather than seeds, typical of ferns.

Phytocrene crinipes is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
