Pinanga nuichuensis

Pinanga nuichuensis, commonly known as the Philippine bamboo palm, is a unique species native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its clustered growth habit and slender, arching leaves. Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
