Prunus laurocerasus f. otinii

Prunus laurocerasus f. otinii, commonly known as the Variegated Laurel Cherry, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to southern Europe.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with striking white and gold variegation, adding visual interest to gardens. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in spring, followed by edible, dark purple berries. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for hedging and container gardening.
