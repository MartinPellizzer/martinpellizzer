Piper cowanii

Piper cowanii, commonly known as the Cowan's pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. It produces tiny, greenish-white flowers that develop into small, round berries.

Piper cowanii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and potential medicinal properties.
