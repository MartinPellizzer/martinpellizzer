Potentilla Pusilla Var. Viridis

Potentilla pusilla var. viridis is a small, hardy shrub known for its delicate yellow flowers and compact growth habit. Native to rocky and alpine regions, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions. Its foliage is often a striking green, adding visual interest to gardens and natural landscapes. Despite its size, it plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and enhancing biodiversity.
