Pothos tirunelveliensis

Pothos tirunelveliensis, commonly known as the Tirunelveli pothos, is a climbing vine native to southern India.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically variegated with light green and yellow patterns. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers indirect sunlight. It has small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by berries containing seeds.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and vertical landscaping.
