Polypodium elasticum

Polypodium elasticum, commonly known as the elastic fern, is a member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is a perennial fern that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. The plant is characterized by its delicate, pinnately divided fronds that can reach lengths of up to 30 centimeters. Its rhizomes are wiry and elastic, giving the plant its common name.

Polypodium elasticum is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in humid conditions.
