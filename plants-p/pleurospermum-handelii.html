Pleurospermum handelii

Pleurospermum handelii, commonly known as the African tansy, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy, grayish-green leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in the spring and summer months.

Pleurospermum handelii is often used in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
