Portesia

Portesia, commonly known as the purple orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet and purple flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid forest environments. Its leaves are broad and glossy, often with a waxy texture that helps retain moisture.

Portesia blooms during the rainy season, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat.
