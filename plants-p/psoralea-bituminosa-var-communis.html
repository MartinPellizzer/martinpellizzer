Psoralea bituminosa var. communis

Psoralea bituminosa var. communis, commonly known as the black psoralea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in shrubland or grassland habitats. Its seeds are enclosed in pods and are adapted for dispersal by wind or animals.
