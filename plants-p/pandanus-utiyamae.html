Pandanus utiyamae

Pandanus utiyamae, commonly known as the Utiyama pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pandanaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of the Pacific, particularly found in parts of Micronesia. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. Its inflorescence consists of a branched cluster of small, fragrant flowers.

The species is valued not only for its ornamental appearance but also for its cultural significance in local communities.
