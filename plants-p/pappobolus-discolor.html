Pappobolus discolor

Pappobolus discolor, commonly known as the yellow grass blister beetle plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellowish to orange-colored flowers. The plant produces small, hairy seeds enclosed in a pappus, which aids in wind dispersal. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
