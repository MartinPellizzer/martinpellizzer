Pilosella brachiata subsp. matrensis

Pilosella brachiata subsp.

matrensis, commonly known as the Matrenskaya pilosella, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in alpine or steppe environments. Its leaves are usually hairy and lobed, while the flowers are small and yellow, forming dense clusters.
