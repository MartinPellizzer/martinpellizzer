Palinetes

Palinetes, commonly known as *Eupatorium cannabinum*, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe, western Asia, and northern Africa, often found in wet meadows and along stream banks. The plant features tall, slender stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. It produces small, clustered white or greenish flowers that bloom in late summer.

Palinetes is known for its aromatic leaves and has been used historically in herbal medicine.
