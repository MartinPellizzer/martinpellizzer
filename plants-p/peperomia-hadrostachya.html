Peperomia hadrostachya

Peperomia hadrostachya, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features dense, waxy, silver-gray foliage with a velvety texture, making it visually striking. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia hadrostachya is a low-maintenance plant that is well-suited for indoor cultivation.
