Phyllanthus phillyreifolius var. rotundifolius

Phyllanthus phillyreifolius var. rotundifolius, commonly known as the round-leaf phyllanthus, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, rounded leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along its stems. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, spherical fruits. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The botanical characteristics of this variety make it an interesting subject for horticultural study and conservation efforts.
