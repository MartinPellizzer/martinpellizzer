Pilea diffusa

Pilea diffusa, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically round or heart-shaped, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea diffusa is relatively low maintenance and can be propagated easily through stem cuttings.
