Polygonum mucronulatum var. rubrifolium

Polygonum mucronulatum var. rubrifolium, commonly known as red-leaf smartweed, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its distinctive reddish foliage, which becomes more pronounced as the plant matures. The species belongs to the buckwheat family (Polygonaceae) and typically grows in moist, disturbed soils such as wetlands or along stream banks. Its small, greenish-white flowers appear in dense clusters during the summer months.

The plant is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
