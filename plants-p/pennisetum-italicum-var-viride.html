Pennisetum italicum var. viride

Pennisetum italicum var. viride, commonly known as green fountain grass, is a perennial ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features slender, upright clumps with narrow, grass-like leaves that are bright green and slightly hairy. The plant produces delicate, plume-like inflorescences that turn golden or reddish in autumn, adding visual interest to gardens. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Pennisetum italicum var. viride is drought-tolerant and suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.
