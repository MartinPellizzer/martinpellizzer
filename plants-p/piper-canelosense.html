Piper canelosense

Piper canelosense, commonly known as the Canelos pepper or Canelos pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into elongated, peppery fruits. It is often grown for its aromatic and spicy fruits, which are used in traditional cuisines.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
