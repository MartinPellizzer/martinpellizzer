Piper setulosum

Piper setulosum, commonly known as the rough pepper or slender pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant, and it produces small, peppery fruits.

Piper setulosum is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
