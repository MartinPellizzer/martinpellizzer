Pleurospa reticulata

Pleurospa reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined pleurospa, is a unique member of the family Pleurospaceae.

It is characterized by its intricate, reticulate venation pattern on its leaves, which gives it a distinct appearance. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are usually broad and elongated, with a waxy surface that helps reduce water loss.

The flowers are small and clustered, often producing a subtle fragrance that attracts pollinators.
