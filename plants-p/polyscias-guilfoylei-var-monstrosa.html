Polyscias guilfoylei var. monstrosa

Polyscias guilfoylei var. monstrosa, commonly known as the Variegated Polyscias, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with striking white or yellow variegation patterns. The plant typically grows in a compact, bushy form, making it suitable for indoor cultivation. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and as a houseplant.
