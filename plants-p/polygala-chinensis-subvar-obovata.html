Polygala chinensis subvar. obovata

Polygala chinensis subvar. obovata, commonly known as the broad-ovate milkwort, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, small flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces seed pods that split open to release its tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.

This subvariety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
