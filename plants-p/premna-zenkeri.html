Premna zenkeri

Premna zenkeri, commonly known as the African nettle, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy on the underside. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, round berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The leaves are sometimes used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
