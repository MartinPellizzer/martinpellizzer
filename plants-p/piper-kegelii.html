Piper kegelii

Piper kegelii, commonly known as the Kegel's pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its fruits are small berries that contain aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine.

Piper kegelii is valued for its unique flavor and potential medicinal properties.
