Polygonum huichunense

Polygonum huichunense, commonly known as the Huichun smartweed, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and clusters of small, often pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, fertile soils along riverbanks and in wetlands. Its stems are often reddish and covered with fine hairs, while its leaves are lanceolate with a prominent midrib.

Polygonum huichunense is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
