Plumeria kerrii

Plumeria kerrii, commonly known as the Hawaiian plumeria, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to yellow and orange. The plant has smooth, pale green bark and thick, waxy leaves that are opposite in arrangement. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are often used in leis and traditional ceremonies.

Plumeria kerrii is well adapted to coastal and dry environments, making it a hardy plant in tropical climates.
