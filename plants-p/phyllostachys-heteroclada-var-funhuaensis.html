Phyllostachys heteroclada var. funhuaensis

Phyllostachys heteroclada var. funhuaensis, commonly known as the Funhua bamboo, is a variant of the bamboo genus Phyllostachys.

It is characterized by its slender, erect culms that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth texture and a vibrant green color. This species is native to China and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in both traditional and modern horticultural settings.
