Polygonum longistylum var. omissum

Polygonum longistylum var. omissum, commonly known as the slender knotweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of East Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. The plant features slender, erect stems that are often covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are lance-shaped with a pointed tip and are arranged alternately along the stem.

The small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences and bloom in the summer months.
