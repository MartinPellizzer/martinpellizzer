Piper obrutum

Piper obrutum, commonly known as the African pepper or wild pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in spikes, and it produces small, round berries that turn dark when mature.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
