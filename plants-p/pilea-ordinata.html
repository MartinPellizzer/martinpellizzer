Pilea ordinata

Pilea ordinata, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rosettes of glossy, round, dark green leaves that resemble coins, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in spring, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Pilea ordinata is known for its ease of care and ability to purify indoor air.
