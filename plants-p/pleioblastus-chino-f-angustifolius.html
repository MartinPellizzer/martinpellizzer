Pleioblastus chino f. angustifolius

Pleioblastus chino f. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Chinese bamboo, is a cultivar of the genus Pleioblastus, which belongs to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping due to its elegant appearance and hardy nature. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

The plant is also noted for its ability to form dense clumps over time, making it a popular choice for garden borders and rock gardens.
