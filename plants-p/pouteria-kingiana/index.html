Pouteria Kingiana

Pouteria kingiana is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America. It is known for its large, glossy leaves and fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its edible fruits, which are prized for their unique flavor. The tree can grow quite tall, making it a striking addition to any landscape. Its adaptability and beauty make it a popular choice among gardeners and botanists alike.
