Platanthera tipuloides subsp. nipponica

Platanthera tipuloides subsp.

nipponica, commonly known as the Japanese lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to East Asia. It is characterized by its tall, erect stem bearing a dense, loose spike of pale green to white flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and meadows. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and ecological importance in its native range.
