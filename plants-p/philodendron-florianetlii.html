Philodendron florianetlii

Philodendron florianetlii, commonly known as the Emerald Queen, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant is a climbing species, often found growing along tree trunks or in shaded areas. Its foliage is known for its vibrant color and glossy appearance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, requiring regular watering to maintain its lush, healthy growth.
