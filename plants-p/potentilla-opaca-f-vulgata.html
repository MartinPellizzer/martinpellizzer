Potentilla opaca f. vulgata

Potentilla opaca f. vulgata, commonly known as the common potentilla, is a low-growing shrub native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, wiry stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
