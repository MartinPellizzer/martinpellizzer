Palala maingayi

Palala maingayi, commonly known as the red-flowered rock heath.

It is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Australia, particularly in the Northern Territory and Western Australia. The plant is characterized by its striking red blooms that appear during the dry season. It typically grows on rocky outcrops and in sandy soils, adapting well to arid environments.

Palala maingayi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens that can accommodate its specific growing conditions.
