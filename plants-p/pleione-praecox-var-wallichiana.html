Pleione praecox var. wallichiana

Pleione praecox var. wallichiana, commonly known as the early pleione, is a species of orchid native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its erect, leafy stems and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically blooms in early spring, making it one of the earliest flowering orchids in its habitat. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in montane forests.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and ecological role in alpine ecosystems.
