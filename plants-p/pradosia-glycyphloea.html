Pradosia glycyphloea

Pradosia glycyphloea, commonly known as the sweet-leaved pradosia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, glossy dark green leaves that have a distinctive sweet scent when crushed. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by elliptical, woody fruits.
