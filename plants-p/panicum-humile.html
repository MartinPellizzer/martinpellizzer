Panicum humile

Panicum humile, commonly known as little panicgrass, is a small annual grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows to a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters and has slender, hairy stems. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, pale green to golden florets. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slightly rough texture.

Panicum humile is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to full sun conditions.
