Pteris submiaoensis

Pteris submiaoensis, commonly known as the Submiao water fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds that are typically light green in color. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the undersides of the fronds.

Pteris submiaoensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
