Panicum viride var. aristatum

Panicum viride var. aristatum, commonly known as green panic grass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, green blades with a distinctive tufted appearance at the base. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically pale green to yellowish in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for ornamental and erosion control purposes.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
