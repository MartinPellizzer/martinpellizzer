Peperomia stehleana var. houelmonti

Peperomia stehleana var. houelmonti, commonly known as the Houelmont's peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are often dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and white, typically appearing in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
