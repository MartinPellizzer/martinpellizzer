Persicaria morrisonensis

Persicaria morrisonensis, commonly known as Morrison's smartweed, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes various species of flowering plants known for their distinctive bract structures. Persicaria morrisonensis is often found in wetlands and can serve as an important component of aquatic and riparian ecosystems.
