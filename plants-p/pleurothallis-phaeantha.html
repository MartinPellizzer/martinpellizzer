Pleurothallis phaeantha

Pleurothallis phaeantha, commonly known as the golden pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, often with intricate patterns on the petals. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, adapted to conserve moisture in its natural habitat.

Pleurothallis phaeantha is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
