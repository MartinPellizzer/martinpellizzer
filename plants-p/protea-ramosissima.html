Protea ramosissima

Protea ramosissima, commonly known as the silver protea, is a species native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, showy inflorescences. The plant features a woody stem with dense, overlapping bracts that form a spherical or conical shape. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with long, slender styles that give it a unique appearance.

Protea ramosissima thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
