Peperomia gibbonsii

Peperomia gibbonsii, commonly known as the Gibbons' peperomia, is a compact, low-growing herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a glossy texture and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. The plant has a rhizomatous root system and often forms clumps, making it suitable for container gardening or as a ground cover. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia gibbonsii is valued for its ornamental leaves and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor spaces.
