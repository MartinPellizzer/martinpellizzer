Phinaea rubida

Phinaea rubida, commonly known as the crimson flame orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red petals and yellow stamens, which give it a vibrant and eye-catching appearance. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to thrive. Its flowers bloom during the summer months, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Phinaea rubida is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its role in local ecosystems.
