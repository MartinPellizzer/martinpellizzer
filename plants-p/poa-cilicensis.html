Poa cilicensis

Poa cilicensis, commonly known as the Cilician grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the region of Cilicia, which is now part of southern Turkey. This plant is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and slopes.

Poa cilicensis is often found in mountainous areas and is adapted to temperate climates.
