Pandanus calathiphorus

Pandanus calathiphorus, commonly known as the bottlebrush pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and long, narrow leaves that form a dense crown. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. Its fruit is a spiny, fibrous structure that contains edible seeds.

Pandanus calathiphorus is often used in traditional crafts and has cultural significance in various island communities.
