Piper sangabrielanum

Piper sangabrielanum, commonly known as the Sangabriel pepper, is a flowering plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, green berries that mature to a dark purple color, and it is valued for its aromatic leaves and fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded, moist environments.

Due to its unique flavor and potential medicinal properties, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
