Portulaca geniculata

Portulaca geniculata, commonly known as the zigzag purslane, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a low-growing annual herb characterized by its fleshy, succulent stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the morning and close by midday. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and gardens.

Portulaca geniculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
