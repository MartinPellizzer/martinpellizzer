Pleurothallis embreei

Pleurothallis embreei, commonly known as the Embree's pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It is known for its unique floral structure and is often found in the understory of tropical rainforests.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicate and less commonly cultivated species.
