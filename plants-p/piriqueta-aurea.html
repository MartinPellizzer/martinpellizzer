Piriqueta aurea

Piriqueta aurea, commonly known as the golden piriqueta, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Piriqueta aurea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
