Platysace heterophylla var. heterophylla

Platysace heterophylla var. heterophylla, commonly known as the variable plumevine, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmately lobed leaves that vary in shape depending on environmental conditions. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are often borne on long, slender peduncles.
