Paronychia beauverdii

Paronychia beauverdii, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features silver-gray leaves with a fuzzy texture, and its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in umbels. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
