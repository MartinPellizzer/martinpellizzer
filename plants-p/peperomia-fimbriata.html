Peperomia fimbriata

Peperomia fimbriata, commonly known as the fringed peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves with distinctive fringed edges, giving it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, adding a subtle touch of color to its foliage. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Peperomia fimbriata prefers well-draining soil and moderate watering, making it an ideal choice for beginners in plant care.
