Parodia microsperma var. rubristaminea

Parodia microsperma var. rubristaminea, commonly known as the red-stemmed parodia, is a compact, globular cactus native to southern Africa.

It features a dense, spiny exterior with short, cylindrical stems that are typically a pale greenish-white color. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are borne on short, hairy pedicels. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it is well adapted to arid environments with minimal water requirements.

This variety is prized for its unique coloration and compact growth habit in succulent collections.
