Polygonum virginianum var. glaberrimum

Polygonum virginianum var. glaberrimum, commonly known as smooth smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its distinctive nodes and often reddish stems. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth texture, contributing to its common name.

Smooth smartweed is often found in moist soils along stream banks and in wet meadows.
