Ptychosperma advenum

Ptychosperma advenum, commonly known as the Australian umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to northern Australia.

It features a distinctive umbrella-like canopy formed by its broad, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ptychosperma advenum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
