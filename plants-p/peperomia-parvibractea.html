Peperomia parvibractea

Peperomia parvibractea, commonly known as the small-bracted peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a velvety texture and small, inconspicuous flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.

Peperomia parvibractea is also valued for its ability to purify indoor air and its adaptability to various indoor environments.
