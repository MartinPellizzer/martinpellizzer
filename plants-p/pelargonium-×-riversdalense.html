Pelargonium × riversdalense

Pelargonium × riversdalense, commonly known as the Riverdal geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The leaves are typically rounded with a slightly hairy surface, and the plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
