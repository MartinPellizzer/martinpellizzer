Pultenaea scabra var. microphylla

Pultenaea scabra var. microphylla, commonly known as the rough pultenaea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features narrow, slightly hairy leaves that are typically less than 2 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry sclerophyll forests.

This variety is distinguished by its smaller leaves compared to the typical form of Pultenaea scabra.
