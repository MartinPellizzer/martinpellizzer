Pleurothallis cuneifolia var. parviflora

Pleurothallis cuneifolia var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered narrow-leaf orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to thrive. Its flowers are often clustered in inflorescences and bloom during the rainy season.

Due to its delicate appearance and specific habitat requirements, it is a sought-after plant among orchid enthusiasts.
