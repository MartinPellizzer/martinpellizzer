Pitcairnia foliacea

Pitcairnia foliacea, commonly known as the velvet leaf pitcairnia, is a striking bromeliad native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety green foliage that forms a rosette shape, giving it a lush, tropical appearance. The plant produces striking inflorescences with vibrant red bracts that contrast beautifully with its dark green leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Pitcairnia foliacea is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of care in the right conditions.
