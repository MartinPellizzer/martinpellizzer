Pritchardia glabrata

Pritchardia glabrata, commonly known as the smooth-leafed palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the Hawaiian Islands, specifically found on the islands of Oahu and Maui. The plant is characterized by its slender trunk and pinnately compound leaves that are smooth and dark green. It produces small, fragrant yellow flowers followed by orange-red fruits containing seeds.

Pritchardia glabrata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also significant in Hawaiian culture and ecology.
