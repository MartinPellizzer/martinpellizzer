Premna pyramidata

Premna pyramidata, commonly known as the square-stemmed or square-leafed premna, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its square-shaped stems and opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers followed by small, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Premna pyramidata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
