Pilea rotundata var. rotundata

Pilea rotundata var. rotundata, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rounded, thick, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Its unique leaf shape and ease of care contribute to its popularity among indoor gardeners.
