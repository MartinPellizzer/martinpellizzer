Phoenicanthemum bennettianum

Phoenicanthemum bennettianum, commonly known as Bennett's phoenicanthemum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange inflorescences. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils. It produces small, tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in suitable climates.
