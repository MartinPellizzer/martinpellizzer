Pernettya ciliaris

Pernettya ciliaris, commonly known as the silverleaf cowberry, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the northern regions of North America.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically silver-white on the underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces tiny, red berries that are edible and often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in boreal forest ecosystems.

Pernettya ciliaris is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
