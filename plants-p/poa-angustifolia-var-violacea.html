Poa angustifolia var. violacea

Poa angustifolia var. violacea, commonly known as violet grass, is a variant of the slender meadowgrass species.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green blades and distinctive violet-tinged inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in temperate regions. It is often found in open woodlands, meadows, and along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
