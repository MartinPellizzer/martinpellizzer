Panicum simile

Panicum simile, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a warm-season grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and long, narrow leaves that are often tinged with red or bronze hues. This plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping. It produces fluffy, seed-filled spikelets that add visual interest during the fall and winter months.

Panicum simile is also valued for its ability to withstand drought and poor soil conditions.
