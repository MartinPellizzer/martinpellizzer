Pseudoeriocoma Constricta

Pseudoeriocoma constricta is a rare and unique plant native to the high altitude regions of South America. It is known for its distinctive appearance and specialized adaptations to its harsh environment. This species belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. Its small, clustered flowers and hairy stems make it easily identifiable. Despite its beauty, Pseudoeriocoma constricta is threatened by habitat loss and climate change.
