Pilea thymifolia

Pilea thymifolia, commonly known as the thyme-leaved pilea, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with a slightly textured surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.

Pilea thymifolia prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
