Phyllostachys makinoi f. wuyishanensis

Phyllostachys makinoi f. wuyishanensis, commonly known as the Wuyi bamboo, is a variant of the giant bamboo species native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, gracefully arching culms that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a smooth, glossy surface and a deep green color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

This bamboo is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
