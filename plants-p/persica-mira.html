Persica mira

Persica mira, commonly known as the miraculous peach.

This plant is a species of peach native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It is characterized by its large, fuzzy, yellow-orange fruits that ripen in late summer. The leaves are broad and ovate, with a slightly serrated edge.

Persica mira is valued for its ornamental appeal and its unique, sweet-tart flavor.
