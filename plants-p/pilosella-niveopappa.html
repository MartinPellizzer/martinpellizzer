Pilosella niveopappa

Pilosella niveopappa, commonly known as the white-haired pearlyweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its leaves are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance.
