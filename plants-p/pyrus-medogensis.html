Pyrus medogensis

Pyrus medogensis, commonly known as the Chinese white pear, is a species of pear native to China.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall, featuring a broad, rounded canopy. The leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate with a serrated margin. Its flowers are white and fragrant, appearing in clusters during the spring.

The fruit is a pear-shaped apple, typically white to yellow in color, and is known for its sweet and juicy flesh.
