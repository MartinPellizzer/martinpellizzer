Psilotrichum gnaphalobryum

Psilotrichum gnaphalobryum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed spikemoss, is a member of the family Psilotaceae and is characterized by its unique morphology.

The plant has reduced leaves and is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Psilotrichum gnaphalobryum plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
