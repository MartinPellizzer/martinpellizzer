Phaseolus vulgaris var. doloensis

Phaseolus vulgaris var. doloensis, commonly known as the Doloensis bean, is a variety of common bean native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its small, elongated pods and yellow or white seeds. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with compound leaves and upright stems. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its edible seeds.

This variety is valued for its adaptability and nutritional content.
