Pyrethrum waldsteinii

Pyrethrum waldsteinii, commonly known as the blue pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet ray florets and yellow disk florets. The plant produces small, spherical flower heads that are often clustered in dense inflorescences. Its botanical features include hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges.

Pyrethrum waldsteinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in the production of natural insecticides due to its pyrethrin content.
