Persicaria densiflora

Persicaria densiflora, commonly known as denseflower smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks. Its dense inflorescence gives it the common name, and it often forms dense mats that can help stabilize soil.

Persicaria densiflora is also known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions and its role in supporting local wildlife.
