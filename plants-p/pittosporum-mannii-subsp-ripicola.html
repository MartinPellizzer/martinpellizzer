Pittosporum mannii subsp. ripicola

Pittosporum mannii subsp.

ripicola, commonly known as the coastal pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand. It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that appear in spring.

The plant typically grows in coastal areas, tolerating salty air and wind-blown conditions. Its bark is smooth and often peels in thin, papery layers.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging coastal environments.
