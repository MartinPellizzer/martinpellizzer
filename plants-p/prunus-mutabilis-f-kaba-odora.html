Prunus mutabilis f. kaba-odora

Prunus mutabilis f. kaba-odora, commonly known as the Kaba-odora cherry, is a unique cultivar of the Prunus genus.

It is characterized by its striking color change in its flowers, which shift from white to pink and then to deep red as the bloom progresses. The plant is native to Japan and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens. Its foliage is typically dark green and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blossoms.

The Kaba-odora cherry is also noted for its subtle, sweet fragrance, which enhances its appeal in both cultivated and natural settings.
