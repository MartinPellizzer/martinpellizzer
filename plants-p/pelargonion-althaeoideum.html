Pelargonion althaeoideum

Pelargonion althaeoideum, commonly known as the althaeoid geranium, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems and heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long flowering period.
