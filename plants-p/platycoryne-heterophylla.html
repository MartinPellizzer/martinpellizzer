Platycoryne heterophylla

Platycoryne heterophylla, commonly known as the variable flat-leaf orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Australia and is characterized by its unique, often variable leaf shapes and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests or under dense vegetation. Its leaves are usually flat and broad, varying in shape depending on environmental conditions.
