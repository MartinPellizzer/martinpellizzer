Phlox shepherdii

Phlox shepherdii, commonly known as the Shepherds' phlox, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features showy clusters of pink to lavender flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, and it has a compact growth habit.

Phlox shepherdii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.
