Pedicularis semibarbata subsp. charlestonensis

Pedicularis semibarbata subsp.

charlestonensis, commonly known as the Charleston beardtongue, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States. It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, spurred flowers that range in color from pink to purple.

This subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and ecological importance in native plant gardens.
