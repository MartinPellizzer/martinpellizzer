Polygala sanariapoana

Polygala sanariapoana, commonly known as the Sanariapoana milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family, which includes a variety of herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its delicate, often pale yellow or white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, adapted to thrive in dry or semi-arid environments.
