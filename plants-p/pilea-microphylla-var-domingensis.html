Pilea microphylla var. domingensis

Pilea microphylla var. domingensis, commonly known as the Mexican pilea or domingensis pilea, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Mexico.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are oval in shape with a slightly pointed tip. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that bloom in spring. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a favored houseplant.
