Phyllanthus merinthopodus

Phyllanthus merinthopodus, commonly known as the African star apple, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by distinctive, fleshy, star-shaped fruits.
