Polyscias sorongensis

Polyscias sorongensis, commonly known as the Bird's Nest Plant, is a member of the Araliaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and landscape design.
