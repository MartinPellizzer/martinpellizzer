Peperomia monsterifolia

Peperomia monsterifolia, commonly known as the giant peperomia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This plant is characterized by its large, waxy, and often variegated leaves that can grow up to 15 inches in length. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage makes it a striking addition to any plant collection.
