Polypodium sursumcurrens

Polypodium sursumcurrens, commonly known as the ascending polypody, is a fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizome is creeping and produces numerous short, upright sporangium-bearing shoots.

The species is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
