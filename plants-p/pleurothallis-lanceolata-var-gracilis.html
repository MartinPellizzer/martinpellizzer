Pleurothallis lanceolata var. gracilis

Pleurothallis lanceolata var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale yellow. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its growth habit is epiphytic, meaning it often grows on trees or rocks in its natural habitat.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
