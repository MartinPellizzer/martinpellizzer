Polythecium nitidum

Polythecium nitidum, commonly known as the smooth thistle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in dry, rocky habitats. Its leaves are covered in a fine, waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions.

The plant is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory properties.
