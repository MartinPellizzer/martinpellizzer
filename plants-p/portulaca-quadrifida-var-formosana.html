Portulaca quadrifida var. formosana

Portulaca quadrifida var. formosana, commonly known as the Chinese ice plant, is a succulent plant native to East Asia.

It features fleshy, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The leaves are thick and rounded, often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens or containers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate drought conditions.
