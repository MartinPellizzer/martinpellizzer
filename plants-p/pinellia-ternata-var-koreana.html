Pinellia ternata var. koreana

Pinellia ternata var. koreana, commonly known as Korean Pinellia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, white, spadix-like flowers surrounded by a green spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its medicinal properties, it is also valued in traditional herbal medicine.
