Peperomia okarana

Peperomia okarana, commonly known as the Okarana peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features dense, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture and often have a slight sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color, which appear in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia okarana is prized for its ornamental foliage and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
