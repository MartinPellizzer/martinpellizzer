Pelargonium hararense

Pelargonium hararense, commonly known as the Harare geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The leaves are typically rounded and slightly hairy, providing a textured appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
