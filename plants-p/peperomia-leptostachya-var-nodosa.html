Peperomia leptostachya var. nodosa

Peperomia leptostachya var. nodosa, commonly known as the nodular peperomia, is a compact, low-growing perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy, and often variegated leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish or reddish in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and minimal water requirements.

Its unique leaf texture and compact growth make it a popular choice for container gardening and as a decorative foliage plant.
