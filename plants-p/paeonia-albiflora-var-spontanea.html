Paeonia albiflora var. spontanea

Paeonia albiflora var. spontanea, commonly known as the white peony or white garden peony, is a deciduous perennial shrub native to East Asia.

It features showy, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has large, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green and velvety in texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant blossoms and its ability to attract pollinators.
