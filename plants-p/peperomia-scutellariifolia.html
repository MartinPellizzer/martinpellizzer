Peperomia scutellariifolia

Peperomia scutellariifolia, commonly known as the shield-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate to lance-shaped leaves that are often dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically green or reddish, which are followed by small, round berries. It is valued for its ornamental foliage and is often grown as a houseplant due to its low maintenance requirements.

Peperomia scutellariifolia prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
