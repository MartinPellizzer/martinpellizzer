Pourthiaea impressivena var. urceolocarpa

Pourthiaea impressivena var. urceolocarpa, commonly known as the African violet or velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, velvety leaves that are often deep green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale purple in hue. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
