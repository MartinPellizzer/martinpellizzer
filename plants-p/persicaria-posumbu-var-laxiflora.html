Persicaria posumbu var. laxiflora

Persicaria posumbu var. laxiflora, commonly known as the slender flowered smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features upright stems that are often branched and covered in fine hairs. The leaves are lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly serrated margin, and they alternate along the stem. Small, greenish-white flowers are arranged in loose clusters at the tips of the branches.

This plant is often found in wet or moist habitats such as marshes and stream banks.
