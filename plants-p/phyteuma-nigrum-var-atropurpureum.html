Phyteuma nigrum var. atropurpureum

Phyteuma nigrum var. atropurpureum, commonly known as purple evening primrose, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe.

It features striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and a distinct, slightly hairy stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its botanical interest lies in its unique coloration and adaptability to various growing conditions.
