Phanera ornata var. kerrii

Phanera ornata var. kerrii, commonly known as the Kerri's flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets, and its bark is smooth and often reddish in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
