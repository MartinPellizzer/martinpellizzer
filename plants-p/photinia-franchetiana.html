Photinia franchetiana

Photinia franchetiana, commonly known as the Chinese photinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that turn vibrant red in the fall, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during the spring, which are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for temperate climates.

Its compact growth habit and seasonal color changes make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
