Polyscias cumingiana

Polyscias cumingiana, commonly known as the Cuming's glorybower, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its dense, glossy, dark green leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by orange-red berries.
