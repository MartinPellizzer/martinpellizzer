Pittosporum moluccanum

Pittosporum moluccanum, commonly known as the Moluccan pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to Indonesia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters tall. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a slightly waxy texture. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and typically white or pale yellow, appearing in clusters.

The fruit is a small, spherical berry that turns red when mature, containing several seeds.
