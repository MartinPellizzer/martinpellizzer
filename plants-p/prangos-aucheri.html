Prangos aucheri

Prangos aucheri, commonly known as the Persian shield, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that ranges from green to purple, adding a striking visual element to gardens. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is a popular choice among gardeners seeking a striking yet relatively low-maintenance plant.
