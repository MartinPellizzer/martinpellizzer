Polypodium synsorum

Polypodium synsorum, commonly known as the broad-leaf huperzia, is a species of fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its broad, leathery, evergreen leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including countries such as China, India, and Japan. Its rhizome is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
