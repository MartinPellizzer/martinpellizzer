Paronychia nivea proles davei

Paronychia nivea proles davei, commonly known as the white-flowered stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it an ideal plant for arid environments.
