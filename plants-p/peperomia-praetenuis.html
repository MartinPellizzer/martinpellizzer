Peperomia praetenuis

Peperomia praetenuis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a neat and compact appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that are borne on short, erect spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Due to its attractive foliage and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
