Photinia chingshuiensis

Photinia chingshuiensis, commonly known as the Chingshui photinia, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically the Yunnan province, and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy red or pink flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its flowers are borne in clusters and bloom in the spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
