Peperomia claytonioides var. longiscapa

Peperomia claytonioides var. longiscapa, commonly known as the long-leaved peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features rosettes of thick, waxy, dark green leaves with a distinctive elongated shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its low maintenance and attractive foliage make it a versatile addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
