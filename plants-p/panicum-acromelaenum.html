Panicum acromelaenum

Panicum acromelaenum, commonly known as crimson grass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and vibrant red or purple panicles that bloom in the summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for ornamental gardens. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a slight reddish tinge.

It is often used in landscaping for its striking visual appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
