Panicum sendulskyae

Panicum sendulskyae, commonly known as Sendulsky's panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to the grasslands of eastern Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers, typically in shades of brown or red. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to seasonal drought conditions.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
