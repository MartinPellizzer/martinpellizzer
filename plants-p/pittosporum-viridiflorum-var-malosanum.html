Pittosporum viridiflorum var. malosanum

Pittosporum viridiflorum var. malosanum, commonly known as the Malosanum Pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually greenish-yellow in color, which bloom in spring. These flowers are followed by elliptical, woody fruits that contain seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
