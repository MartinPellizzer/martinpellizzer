Phyllostachys pubescens f. quadrangulata

Phyllostachys pubescens f. quadrangulata, commonly known as the bamboo with four-sided stems, is a cultivar of the bamboo genus Phyllostachys.

It is characterized by its distinctive quadrangular, or four-sided, stem structure, which sets it apart from other bamboo species. The plant is native to China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique appearance. It grows as a clump-forming bamboo, reaching heights of up to 10 meters, and prefers well-drained soil and full sun.

This variety is also valued for its use in landscaping and traditional Chinese medicine.
