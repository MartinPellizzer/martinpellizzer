Pennisetum cylindricum

Pennisetum cylindricum, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that can grow up to 3 meters in height, with narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green to bluish-green in color. The plant produces dense, cylindrical inflorescences that are often tinged with purple or pink, giving it its common name. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Pennisetum cylindricum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as birds and butterflies.
