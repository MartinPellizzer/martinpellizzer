Psythirhisma

It is characterized by its unique, pale green or white, leafless stems and its striking, translucent flowers that resemble ghostly apparitions. Native to the wetlands of Florida and Cuba, it thrives in swampy, shaded environments with high humidity. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees without deriving nutrients from them.
