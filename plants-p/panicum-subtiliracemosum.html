Panicum subtiliracemosum

Panicum subtiliracemosum, commonly known as the delicate raceme grass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and fine, feather-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in loose, branching inflorescences called racemes. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Due to its ornamental appeal and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
