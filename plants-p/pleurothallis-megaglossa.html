Pleurothallis megaglossa

Pleurothallis megaglossa, commonly known as the giant lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated flowers with large, showy lips that are often brightly colored. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees and deriving moisture and nutrients from the air and rain. Its leaves are usually dark green and broad, adapted to capture sunlight in its shaded habitat.

Pleurothallis megaglossa is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
