Polygonatum daminense

Polygonatum daminense, commonly known as the Chinese Solomon's Seal, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, white or greenish flowers. Its berries are typically red or black and contain several seeds.

Polygonatum daminense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and traditional medicinal uses.
