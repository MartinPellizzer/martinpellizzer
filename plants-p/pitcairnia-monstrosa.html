Pitcairnia monstrosa

Pitcairnia monstrosa, commonly known as the Monster Pitcairnia, is a striking bromeliad native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, colorful, and often showy inflorescence that can reach several feet in height. The plant features a rosette of broad, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Pitcairnia monstrosa is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of care.
