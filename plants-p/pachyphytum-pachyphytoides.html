Pachyphytum pachyphytoides

Pachyphytum pachyphytoides, commonly known as the Mexican sun rose, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting to arid environments. The plant produces vibrant orange or yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its compact growth habit and striking appearance make it an attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor collections.
