Parathesis longipedicellata

Parathesis longipedicellata, commonly known as the long-stemmed parathesis, is a unique plant species characterized by its elongated, slender stems.

It belongs to the family Parathesiaceae and is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia. The plant features small, clustered leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow and are arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences.

Due to its limited distribution and specialized habitat, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
