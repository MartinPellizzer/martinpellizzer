Panicum stipiflorum

Panicum stipiflorum, commonly known as narrow-leaved panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers, typically in shades of green or brown. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Panicum stipiflorum is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
