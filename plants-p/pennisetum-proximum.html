Pennisetum proximum

Pennisetum proximum, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a tall, ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that bear long, narrow leaves with a striking purple hue at the base. The plant produces fluffy, silver-white plumes during the summer months, which add visual interest to gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for landscaping.

Pennisetum proximum is also valued for its ability to attract birds and butterflies.
