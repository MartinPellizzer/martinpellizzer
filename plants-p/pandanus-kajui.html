Pandanus kajui

Pandanus kajui, commonly known as the Solomon Islands pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, sword-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into fibrous, spiky fruits. Its leaves are fibrous and durable, making them valuable for weaving and construction.

Pandanus kajui thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to salt spray and sandy soils.
