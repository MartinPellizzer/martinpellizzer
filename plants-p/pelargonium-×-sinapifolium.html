Pelargonium × sinapifolium

Pelargonium × sinapifolium, commonly known as the Chinese geranium, is a hybrid species native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a white or yellow center. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are slightly hairy and emit a strong, spicy scent. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Pelargonium × sinapifolium is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and aromatic qualities.
