Pipturus ruber

Pipturus ruber, commonly known as the redleaf fern, is a species native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.

The botanical features of Pipturus ruber make it an interesting subject for both ecological studies and horticultural cultivation.
