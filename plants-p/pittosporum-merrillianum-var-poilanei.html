Pittosporum merrillianum var. poilanei

Pittosporum merrillianum var. poilanei, commonly known as the Polian's pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage that ranges from dark green to bronze in color. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are fragrant and appear in clusters. These flowers are followed by small, round berries that are typically red when mature.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
