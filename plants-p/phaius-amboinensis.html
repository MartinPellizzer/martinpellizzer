Phaius amboinensis

Phaius amboinensis, commonly known as the Amboina orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in summer. The plant has broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces a single, often fragrant flower per stem. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Phaius amboinensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
