Polygonum Coccineum Var. Pratincola

Polygonum coccineum var. pratincola is a strikingly colorful plant known for its vibrant red and yellow foliage. It is a popular choice for ornamental gardens due to its unique appearance and hardy nature. This variety thrives in a variety of soil types and is relatively easy to care for. Its leaves change color with the seasons, making it a dynamic addition to any landscape. Whether grown in containers or in the ground, it adds visual interest and beauty to outdoor spaces.
