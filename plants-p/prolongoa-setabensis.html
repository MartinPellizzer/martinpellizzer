Prolongoa setabensis

Prolongoa setabensis, commonly known as the Setabense orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and lance-shaped, with a smooth surface.

Prolongoa setabensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
