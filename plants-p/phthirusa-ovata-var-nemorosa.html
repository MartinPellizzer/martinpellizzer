Phthirusa ovata var. nemorosa

Phthirusa ovata var. nemorosa, commonly known as the forest shieldflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to woodland environments.

It features small, shield-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
