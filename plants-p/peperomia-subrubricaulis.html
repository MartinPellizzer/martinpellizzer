Peperomia subrubricaulis

Peperomia subrubricaulis, commonly known as the red-stemmed peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features upright, branching stems that are reddish in color, adding visual interest to its foliage. The plant produces small, clustered leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia subrubricaulis is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
