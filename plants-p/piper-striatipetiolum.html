Piper striatipetiolum

Piper striatipetiolum, commonly known as the striped petiole pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves with a serrated margin. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are borne in clusters and are followed by elongated, slender fruits.

The plant is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
