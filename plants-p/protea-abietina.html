Protea abietina

Protea abietina, commonly known as the silver protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence composed of numerous white to pale pink flowers arranged in a spherical or conical shape. The plant is native to the Western Cape region and thrives in acidic, well-drained soils. It is often found in fynbos vegetation, which is adapted to the region's Mediterranean climate.

Protea abietina is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in local ecosystems and horticulture.
