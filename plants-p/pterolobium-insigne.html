Pterolobium insigne

Pterolobium insigne, commonly known as the African sycamore, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall, with a broad, spreading canopy. Its bark is smooth and greyish, often marked with fissures.

The plant is adapted to various soil types and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.
