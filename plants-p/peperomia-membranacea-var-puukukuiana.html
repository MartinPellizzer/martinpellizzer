Peperomia membranacea var. puukukuiana

Peperomia membranacea var. puukukuiana, commonly known as the Puukukuiana peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of the Pacific.

It features broad, waxy, ovate leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and ease of care make it a favored ornamental plant among plant enthusiasts.
