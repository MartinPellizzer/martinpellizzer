Peperomia pereskiifolia var. rubricaulis

Peperomia pereskiifolia var. rubricaulis, commonly known as the red-stemmed peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish or purplish hue on the underside. The plant produces tiny, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink, adding a subtle ornamental appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

This variety is prized for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
