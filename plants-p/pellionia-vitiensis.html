Pellionia vitiensis

Pellionia vitiensis, commonly known as the Vitiensis Pellionia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Pacific, particularly Vanuatu.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features small, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of tiny white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some horticultural settings for its attractive foliage.
