Pleurothallis carinilabia

Pleurothallis carinilabia, commonly known as the "Carnation Orchid," is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often resembling carnations in appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique beauty and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
