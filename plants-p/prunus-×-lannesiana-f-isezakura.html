Prunus × lannesiana f. isezakura

Prunus × lannesiana f. isezakura, commonly known as the Izezakura cherry, is a unique hybrid cultivar prized for its ornamental value.

It is a cross between Prunus lannesiana and Prunus × yedoensis, resulting in a plant with distinctive floral characteristics. The Izezakura is noted for its double blooms, which are often a combination of pink and white petals, creating a striking visual effect. It typically grows as a small tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

This cultivar is well-suited for gardens and landscapes, offering both aesthetic appeal and seasonal interest.
