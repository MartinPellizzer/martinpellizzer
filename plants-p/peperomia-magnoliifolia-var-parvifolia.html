Peperomia magnoliifolia var. parvifolia

Peperomia magnoliifolia var. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaf magnolia peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are smaller and more rounded compared to the typical variety, giving it a dense, bushy appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often hidden among the foliage, with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a versatile addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
