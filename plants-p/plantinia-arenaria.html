Plantinia arenaria

Plantinia arenaria, commonly known as the sandy plantain, is a flowering plant native to sandy and coastal environments.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dunes or disturbed areas. It plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and providing habitat for small invertebrates.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
