Pleurothallopsis reichenbachiana

Pleurothallopsis reichenbachiana, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short, horizontal rhizome and produces a single, leafy stem. The plant is characterized by its striking golden-yellow flowers arranged in a dense, star-like cluster. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
