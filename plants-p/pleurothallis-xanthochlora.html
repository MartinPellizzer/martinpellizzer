Pleurothallis xanthochlora

Pleurothallis xanthochlora, commonly known as the yellow-stemmed orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, organic-rich substrates.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
