Phylica anomala

Phylica anomala, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, silvery-green leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often found in coastal regions and can thrive in sandy or rocky soils. It has a shrub-like growth habit and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical areas.
