Piper tsuritkubense

Piper tsuritkubense, commonly known as the Tsuritkubense pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves with serrated margins. Its small, greenish-white flowers are followed by small, fleshy berries that contain the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine.

The plant is valued for its unique flavor and potential medicinal properties.
