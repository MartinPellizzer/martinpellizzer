Phelipanche hohenackcri

Phelipanche hohenackcri, commonly known as the common witchhazel, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a serrated edge.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies for various ailments.
