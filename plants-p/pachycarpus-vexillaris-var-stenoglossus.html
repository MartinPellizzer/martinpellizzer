Pachycarpus vexillaris var. stenoglossus

Pachycarpus vexillaris var. stenoglossus, commonly known as the narrow-lipped pachycarpus, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting to its harsh environment. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique morphology and resilience make it a popular choice for rock gardens and dryland landscapes.
