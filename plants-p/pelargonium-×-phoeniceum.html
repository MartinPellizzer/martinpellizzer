Pelargonium × phoeniceum

Pelargonium × phoeniceum, commonly known as the scarlet geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers and dark green, velvety leaves. Native to South Africa, this plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun exposure. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a container plant.

The plant produces showy, five-petaled blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
