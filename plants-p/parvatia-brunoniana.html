Parvatia brunoniana

Parvatia brunoniana, commonly known as the Brunoniana parvatia, is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy surface.
