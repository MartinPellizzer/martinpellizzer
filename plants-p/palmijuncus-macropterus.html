Palmijuncus macropterus

Palmijuncus macropterus, commonly known as the giant fan palm, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its large, feather-like fronds.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is native to certain regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features a tall, slender trunk with a smooth, greyish-brown surface. Its leaves are arranged in a fan shape, giving it a distinctive and elegant appearance.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its visual appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
