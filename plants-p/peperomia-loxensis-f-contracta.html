Peperomia loxensis f. contracta

Peperomia loxensis f. contracta, commonly known as the compact peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions.

It features small, rounded leaves that are typically a deep green color with a slightly glossy texture. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and minimal water requirements.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for container gardening and as a decorative plant.
