Podostemum ovatum

Podostemum ovatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf water primrose, is a semi-aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows in shallow, slow-moving freshwater environments such as streams and ponds. The plant has slender, ribbon-like leaves that are often submerged, with small, white flowers that bloom above the water surface. Its root system is adapted to anchor in rocky substrates, and it plays a role in stabilizing aquatic ecosystems.

Podostemum ovatum is also valued for its ornamental appeal in water gardens.
