Peperomia blanda var. reticulata

Peperomia blanda var. reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features thick, waxy leaves with a distinctive net-like venation pattern, giving it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant typically grows to a height of about 6 to 12 inches, making it ideal for tabletop displays or small spaces. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its foliage is often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink, adding visual interest to any indoor setting.
