Pandanus vandermeeschii

Pandanus vandermeeschii, commonly known as the Vandermeesch's pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pandanaceae.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or green in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in coastal or wetland areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential ecological and horticultural significance.
