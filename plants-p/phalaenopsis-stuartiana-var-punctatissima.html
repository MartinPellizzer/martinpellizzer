Phalaenopsis stuartiana var. punctatissima

Phalaenopsis stuartiana var. punctatissima, commonly known as the spotted phalaenopsis, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, long-lasting blooms that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often marked with distinctive spots or streaks. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
