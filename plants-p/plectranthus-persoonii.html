Plectranthus persoonii

Plectranthus persoonii, commonly known as the African violet, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features upright stems and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. The leaves are typically velvety and heart-shaped, often with a distinctive silver or grayish underside. It is widely cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.

This species thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.
