Peperomia tetraphylla var. sinensis

Peperomia tetraphylla var. sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese Peperomia, is a compact, low-growing herbaceous plant native to China.

It features dense, fleshy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, usually greenish-white in color, which are followed by small, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it suitable for container planting and as a decorative houseplant.
