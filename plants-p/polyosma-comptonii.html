Polyosma comptonii

Polyosma comptonii, commonly known as the Compton's polyosma, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae, which includes eucalyptus and tea trees. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, white or pale pink flowers in clusters, typically during spring.
