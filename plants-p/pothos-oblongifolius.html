Pothos oblongifolius

Pothos oblongifolius, commonly known as the marble queen pothos, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically variegated with light green and cream patterns. The plant thrives in low-light conditions and is known for its ability to purify indoor air. Its stems can grow up to several meters long, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets or trained on trellises.

Pothos oblongifolius is also valued for its ease of care and adaptability to various indoor environments.
