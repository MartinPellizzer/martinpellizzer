Pelargonium ladysmithianum

Pelargonium ladysmithianum, commonly known as the Ladysmith geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to red. The leaves are typically rounded and slightly hairy, providing a textured appearance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
