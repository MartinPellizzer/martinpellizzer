Pleioblastus sanmingensis

Pleioblastus sanmingensis, commonly known as the Sanming bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Guangxi and Guangdong provinces. This bamboo is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
