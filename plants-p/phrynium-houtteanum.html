Phrynium houtteanum

Phrynium houtteanum, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It features large, broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with distinctive purple veins. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Its foliage is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
