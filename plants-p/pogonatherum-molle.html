Pogonatherum molle

Pogonatherum molle, commonly known as silky grass or velvet grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its fine, soft, and silky leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowering spikes are usually small and inconspicuous, producing tiny grains that are dispersed by wind.

Pogonatherum molle is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
