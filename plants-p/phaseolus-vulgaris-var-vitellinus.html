Phaseolus vulgaris var. vitellinus

Phaseolus vulgaris var. vitellinus, commonly known as the yellow bean.

This variety is characterized by its distinctive yellow seeds, which are smaller and more oval in shape compared to typical green beans. It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is native to the Americas. The plant produces climbing vines with trifoliate leaves and produces pods that contain the yellow seeds.

It is cultivated for both its seeds and as a ornamental plant due to its vibrant color.
