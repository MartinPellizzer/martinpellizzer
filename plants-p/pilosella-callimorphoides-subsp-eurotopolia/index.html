Pilosella Callimorphoides Subsp. Eurotopolia

Pilosella callimorphoides subsp. eurotopolia is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of Europe. It is part of the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads and ecological importance. This subspecies is characterized by its small, white or pale yellow blooms and hairy foliage. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered in many areas. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving its unique genetic makeup and ecological role.
