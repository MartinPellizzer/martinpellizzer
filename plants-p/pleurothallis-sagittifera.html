Pleurothallis sagittifera

Pleurothallis sagittifera, commonly known as the arrow-shaped pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often epiphytic on trees or rocks. Its leaves are usually elongated and grass-like, and it has a relatively short flowering period.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and specialized orchid species.
