Potamogeton × suecicus f. ysanensis

Potamogeton × suecicus f. ysanensis, commonly known as the Ysanensis pondweed, is a hybrid aquatic plant native to Europe.

It is a submerged perennial that forms dense vegetation in freshwater habitats such as lakes and slow-moving streams. The plant has long, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around the stem. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, with staminate and pistillate flowers occurring on separate plants.

It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various water organisms.
