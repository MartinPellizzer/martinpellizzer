Podanthum supinum

Podanthum supinum, commonly known as the prostrate podanthum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that typically reaches a height of less than 10 centimeters. The plant features small, oval-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are pale yellow and are borne in clusters along the stems.

It is often cultivated as a ground cover due to its dense growth habit and ornamental appeal.
