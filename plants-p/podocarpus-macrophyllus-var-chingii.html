Podocarpus macrophyllus var. chingii

Podocarpus macrophyllus var. chingii, commonly known as the Chinese podocarp or Ching's podocarp, is a slow-growing evergreen conifer native to China.

It features narrow, dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged spirally along the stems. The plant produces small, spherical cones that mature to a reddish-brown color. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its dense, compact growth habit.

This variety is relatively cold-hardy and can thrive in a range of soil types, making it adaptable to various garden settings.
