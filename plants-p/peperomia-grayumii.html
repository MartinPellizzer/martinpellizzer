Peperomia grayumii

Peperomia grayumii, commonly known as the grayumii peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink, giving it an ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia grayumii is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
