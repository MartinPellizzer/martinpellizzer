Pavetta macrothyrsa var. nitida

Pavetta macrothyrsa var. nitida, commonly known as the shining pavetta, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, glossy leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale pink flowers that bloom in the late afternoon. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological studies.
