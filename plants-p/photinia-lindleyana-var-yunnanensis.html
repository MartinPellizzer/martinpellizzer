Photinia lindleyana var. yunnanensis

Photinia lindleyana var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan photinia, is a shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that turn reddish in the fall. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping.
