Poiretia multiflora

Poiretia multiflora, commonly known as the multiflora poiretia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically produces oval-shaped leaves that are glossy and have a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Poiretia multiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
