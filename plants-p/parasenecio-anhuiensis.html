Parasenecio anhuiensis

Parasenecio anhuiensis, commonly known as the Anhui gentian, is a flowering plant native to China, specifically the Anhui province.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Parasenecio anhuiensis is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
