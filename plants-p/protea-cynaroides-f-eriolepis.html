Protea cynaroides f. eriolepis

Protea cynaroides f. eriolepis, commonly known as the silver protea, is a variant of the king protea, native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, pale silver-blue bracts that encase a cluster of smaller, white flowers. The plant belongs to the Proteaceae family and is known for its showy, long-lasting blooms. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

This cultivar is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often used in garden displays and floral arrangements.
