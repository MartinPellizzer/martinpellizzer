Pothos latifolius

Pothos latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf pothos, is a trailing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that can grow up to 10 inches in length, making it a striking addition to indoor spaces. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot. Its ability to climb or trail makes it versatile for hanging baskets or shelves.

It is also noted for its air-purifying properties, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
