Pleurothallis longiserpens

Pleurothallis longiserpens, commonly known as the long-leaf pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant has slender, wiry stems and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and yellow. Its leaves are elongated and often twisted, contributing to its unique appearance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
