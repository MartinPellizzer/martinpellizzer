Plukenetia sinuata

Plukenetia sinuata, commonly known as the wax nut or yellow mallow, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its palmate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain a single seed surrounded by a waxy coating. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.
