Phalaenopsis latisepala

Phalaenopsis latisepala, commonly known as the broad-sepaled orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendant flower spikes that bear numerous showy, white to pale pink flowers. The plant has thick, fleshy roots that help it absorb moisture from the air and rain. Its leaves are broad and dark green, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

This species is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home environment.
