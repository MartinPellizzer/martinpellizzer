Peperomia leucandra

Peperomia leucandra, commonly known as the White-veined Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves with striking white veins against a deep green background. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia leucandra is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
