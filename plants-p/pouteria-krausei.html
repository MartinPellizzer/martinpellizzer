Pouteria krausei

Pouteria krausei, commonly known as the Brazilian starfruit or yellow starfruit, is a tropical tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and broad, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into the edible, star-shaped fruit. The fruit has a sweet, tropical flavor and is often consumed fresh or used in culinary applications.
