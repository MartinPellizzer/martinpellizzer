Polystichum rufopaleaceum

Polystichum rufopaleaceum, commonly known as the red-veined fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

Polystichum rufopaleaceum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
