Pilea dussii

Pilea dussii, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its round, glossy leaves that resemble small coins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are typically hidden beneath its foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Pilea dussii is a popular houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
