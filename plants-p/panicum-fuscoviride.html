Panicum fuscoviride

Panicum fuscoviride, commonly known as green panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves with a distinctive greenish hue, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or disturbed sites.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in erosion control or as a forage crop.
