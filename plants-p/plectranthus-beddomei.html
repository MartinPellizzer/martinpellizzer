Plectranthus beddomei

Plectranthus beddomei, commonly known as the African violet, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive grayish-green color. Its flowers are clustered in dense spikes and come in shades of purple, pink, and white.

Plectranthus beddomei is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
