Polhillia xairuensis

Polhillia xairuensis, commonly known as the Karoo starwood, is a unique flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in a star-like pattern, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is adapted to the arid conditions of the Karoo region.
