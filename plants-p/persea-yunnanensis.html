Persea yunnanensis

Persea yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan avocado, is a species of evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its large, leathery leaves and fragrant flowers. The tree produces a pear-shaped fruit with a smooth, green skin and a creamy, oily pulp. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has limited use in commercial agriculture.

The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.
