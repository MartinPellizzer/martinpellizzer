Passiflora kuethiana

Passiflora kuethiana, commonly known as the Kuthi passionflower, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that feature a unique blend of red, blue, and yellow hues. The plant produces edible berries that are often used in local cuisines. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped with a velvety texture.

Passiflora kuethiana is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
