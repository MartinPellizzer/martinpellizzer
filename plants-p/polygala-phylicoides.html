Polygala phylicoides

Polygala phylicoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and often have a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss.

The flowers are usually pink or purple and are adapted for pollination by insects.
