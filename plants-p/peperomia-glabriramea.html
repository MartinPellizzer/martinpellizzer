Peperomia glabriramea

Peperomia glabriramea, commonly known as the smoothleaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, with a smooth, glossy surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its slow growth rate and low maintenance requirements make it suitable for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
