Prostanthera wilkieana

Prostanthera wilkieana, commonly known as the Wilkie's mintbush, is a small shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue or purple, arranged in clusters.

Prostanthera wilkieana is well adapted to dry, rocky habitats and is often found in heathlands and open forests.
