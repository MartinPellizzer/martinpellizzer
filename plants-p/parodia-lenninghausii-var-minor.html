Parodia lenninghausii var. minor

Parodia lenninghausii var. minor, commonly known as the miniature moon cactus, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to South America.

It features a swollen, globular stem with a distinctive green or blue-green coloration, often adorned with small, white or pale yellow areoles. The plant produces delicate, yellow or orange flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It is well-suited for container gardening due to its small size and ease of care.

Its unique appearance and manageable growth make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
