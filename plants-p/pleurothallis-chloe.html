Pleurothallis chloe

Pleurothallis chloe, commonly known as the Chloe orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that often display a range of colors, including white, yellow, and green. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments.

Its botanical features include strap-like leaves and a compact, branching structure that supports its unique floral display.
