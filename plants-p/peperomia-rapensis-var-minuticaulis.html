Peperomia rapensis var. minuticaulis

Peperomia rapensis var. minuticaulis, commonly known as the miniature peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to South America.

It features dense, upright rosettes of small, waxy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish or purple hue on the underside. The plant produces tiny, short-lived flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rockeries.
