Premna flavescens

Premna flavescens, commonly known as the yellow-flowered premna, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in dense inflorescences, often appearing in late spring to early summer.

The fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that contains a single seed.
