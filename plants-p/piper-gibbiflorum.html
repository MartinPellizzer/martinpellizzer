Piper gibbiflorum

Piper gibbiflorum, commonly known as the waxy begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive waxy, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
