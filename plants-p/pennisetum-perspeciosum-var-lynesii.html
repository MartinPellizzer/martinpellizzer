Pennisetum perspeciosum var. lynesii

Pennisetum perspeciosum var. lynesii, commonly known as Lyne's fountain grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to southern Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that produce feathery, silver-green plumes during the summer months. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a landscape accent. Its foliage is narrow and upright, adding a vertical element to garden designs.

It is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
