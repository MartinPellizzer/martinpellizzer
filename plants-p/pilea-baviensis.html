Pilea baviensis

Pilea baviensis, commonly known as the Bavi pilea, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features dense, rounded foliage with small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and slightly glossy. The plant produces delicate, white flowers with five petals, which bloom in spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and ease of care contribute to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
