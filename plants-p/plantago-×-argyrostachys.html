Plantago × argyrostachys

Plantago × argyrostachys, commonly known as silver hoary plantain, is a hybrid perennial plant native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that is covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, which are wind-pollinated. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows.

Due to its hardy nature and ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
