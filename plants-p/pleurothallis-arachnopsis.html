Pleurothallis arachnopsis

Pleurothallis arachnopsis, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, spider-like flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrate.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
