Polybotrya appendiculata var. costulata

Polybotrya appendiculata var. costulata, commonly known as the Costulated Polybotrya, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polybotryaceae and is characterized by its unique, spirally arranged leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
