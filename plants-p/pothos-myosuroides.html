Pothos myosuroides

Pothos myosuroides, commonly known as the devil's ivy, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically light to dark green in color. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers, though these are rarely seen in cultivation.

Pothos myosuroides is also noted for its rapid growth and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
