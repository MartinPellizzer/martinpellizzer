Prasoxylon klanderi

Prasoxylon klanderi, commonly known as the Klanderi wood tree, is a rare and ancient plant species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark-colored wood, which is highly valued for its durability and unique grain patterns. The plant exhibits a slow growth rate, contributing to the scarcity of its timber. Its leaves are broad and glossy, and it produces small, fragrant flowers that attract a variety of pollinators.

Due to habitat loss and overharvesting, Prasoxylon klanderi is now considered critically endangered.
