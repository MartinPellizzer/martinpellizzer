Polypodium stenobasis

Polypodium stenobasis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf wall-r Fern, is a species of fern that belongs to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped fronds that are typically less than 30 centimeters in length. This fern is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including countries like India, China, and Indonesia. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often growing on rocks or tree trunks.

Its rhizomes are fibrous and help anchor it to its substrate while also storing nutrients.
