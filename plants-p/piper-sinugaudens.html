Piper sinugaudens

Piper sinugaudens, commonly known as the Philippine pepper vine, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. Its small, greenish-white flowers are followed by bright red berries that are edible.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and vibrant fruit.
