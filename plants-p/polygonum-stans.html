Polygonum stans

Polygonum stans, commonly known as red root or redstem filaree, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants commonly referred to as buckwheats. The plant is characterized by its upright, branching stems that are typically red or purple in color. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink or white, and it produces distinctive, three-lobed seed capsules.

Polygonum stans is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
