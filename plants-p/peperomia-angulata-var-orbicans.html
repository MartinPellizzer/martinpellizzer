Peperomia angulata var. orbicans

Peperomia angulata var. orbicans, commonly known as the rounded peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are rounded and often have a bluish or silver hue, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique leaf shape and easy care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
