Polystichum boboense

Polystichum boboense, commonly known as the Bobo fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant features bipinnate leaves with a distinctive dark green color and a leathery texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Polystichum boboense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant in tropical gardens.
