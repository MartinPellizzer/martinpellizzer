Pentas speciosa

Pentas speciosa, commonly known as the flame flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces clusters of five-petaled blooms, which are arranged in dense, rounded inflorescences. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Pentas speciosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
