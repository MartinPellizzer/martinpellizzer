Premna odorata var. vitiensis

Premna odorata var. vitiensis, commonly known as the Vitiensis Premna, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, round fruits containing a single seed.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
