Premna pubescens var. subglabra

Premna pubescens var. subglabra, commonly known as the smooth premna, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically smooth or slightly hairy, depending on the variety. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, spherical berries. It is often found in moist, shaded forest understories and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

Its foliage is used in traditional medicine and as a source of fiber in some regions.
