Popowia dictyoneura

Popowia dictyoneura, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a shrub native to the Americas, characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, and its fruits are capsule-like structures that split open to release seeds. Popowia dictyoneura is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

It has been studied for its potential medicinal properties and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
