Persicaria pallida

Persicaria pallida, commonly known as white knotweed, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes species like buckwheat and smartweed. The plant features slender, erect stems that often branch near the top, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a distinctive reddish tint. White knotweed produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in late summer.

It is often found in moist soils along stream banks and in wet meadows.
