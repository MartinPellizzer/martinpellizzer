Poecilostachys alleizettei

Poecilostachys alleizettei, commonly known as the Alleizette's grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family, Poaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly found in regions of Brazil and Paraguay. The plant is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

While not widely cultivated, it is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and potential ecological role in its native habitat.
