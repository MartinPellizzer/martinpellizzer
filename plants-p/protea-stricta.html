Protea stricta

Protea stricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves and showy, cone-shaped inflorescences that display a range of colors, including white, pink, and red. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to the Mediterranean climate of the Western Cape region. It is often found in fynbos vegetation and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Protea stricta plays an important ecological role by providing nectar for pollinators such as bees and birds.
