Ptychosperma cunninghamianum

Ptychosperma cunninghamianum, commonly known as the rainforest palm, is a tropical plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in diameter. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by black, spherical fruits that contain a single seed. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its unique appearance and ecological role make it an important species in its native habitat.
