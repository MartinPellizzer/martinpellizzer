Polystachya ecuadorensis

Polystachya ecuadorensis, commonly known as the Ecuadorian feather-orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the Andean regions of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its striking, feathery inflorescence composed of numerous small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest habitats at high elevations. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine, hairy structures.

Polystachya ecuadorensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
