Peperomia pterocaulis f. stipiticarpa

Peperomia pterocaulis f. stipiticarpa, commonly known as the stipitate peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to South America.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a distinctive waxy texture and a unique, slightly swollen base on its stems. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, with a smooth margin and a glossy surface. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor conditions.

It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often overlooked in favor of its attractive foliage.
