Plecosorus

Plecosorus, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil. Its foliage is often velvety and comes in various shades of green, while the flowers range from purple to white.

Plecosorus is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
