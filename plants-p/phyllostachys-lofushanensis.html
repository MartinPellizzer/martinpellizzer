Phyllostachys lofushanensis

Phyllostachys lofushanensis, commonly known as Lufushan bamboo, is a species of grass in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green, providing aesthetic value in ornamental landscaping. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

This bamboo is also valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
