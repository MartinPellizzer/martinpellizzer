Phyllostegia pilosicincta

Phyllostegia pilosicincta, commonly known as the hairy-veined phyllostegia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. Its small, tubular flowers are typically pink or purple and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Phyllostegia pilosicincta is often found in moist, wooded areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
