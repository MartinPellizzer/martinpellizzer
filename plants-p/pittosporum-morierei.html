Pittosporum morierei

Pittosporum morierei, commonly known as the Morier's pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The fruit it produces is a small, red berry that contains several seeds.
