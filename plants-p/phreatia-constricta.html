Phreatia constricta

Phreatia constricta, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, strap-like leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and green. This epiphytic plant typically grows on trees in humid, tropical environments. It thrives in well-drained, loose soil and requires high levels of light and moisture.

Phreatia constricta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
