Plectranthus ciliatus

Plectranthus ciliatus, commonly known as the purple heart plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its striking purple and pink tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Plectranthus ciliatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
