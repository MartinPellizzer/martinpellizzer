Potentilla × leteae

Potentilla × leteae, commonly known as the hybrid shrub, is a deciduous plant native to the mountainous regions of central and eastern Europe.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in early summer, which are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a smooth or slightly serrated margin.

Potentilla × leteae is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
