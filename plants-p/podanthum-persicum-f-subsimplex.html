Podanthum persicum f. subsimplex

Podanthum persicum f. subsimplex, commonly known as Persian snowball.

This compact shrub features dense clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. It has small, oval, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant is native to the mountains of Central Asia and thrives in well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and cold hardiness.
