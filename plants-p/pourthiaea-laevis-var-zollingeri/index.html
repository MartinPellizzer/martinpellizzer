Pourthiaea Laevis Var. Zollingeri

Pourthiaea laevis var. zollingeri is a rare and striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America. Known for its delicate, waxy leaves and vibrant floral displays, it captivates botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. This variant of Pourthiaea laevis is particularly noted for its unique coloration and growth habits. It thrives in cool, moist environments, often found in cloud forests and mountainous terrain. Due to its limited distribution and fragile nature, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
