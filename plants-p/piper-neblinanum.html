Piper neblinanum

Piper neblinanum, commonly known as the misty pepper or cloud pepper, is a climbing shrub native to the humid forests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its finely toothed leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in catkins. Its fruits are small, spherical berries that turn from green to yellow when mature.

Piper neblinanum is often found in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its aromatic properties in traditional medicine.
