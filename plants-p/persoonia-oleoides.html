Persoonia oleoides

Persoonia oleoides, commonly known as the silky oxalis, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features small, bright green leaves with a silky, waxy texture. The plant produces yellow flowers with five petals, often appearing in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions.

Persoonia oleoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
