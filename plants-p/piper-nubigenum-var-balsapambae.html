Piper nubigenum var. balsapambae

Piper nubigenum var. balsapambae, commonly known as the Balsam Pungent Pepper, is a tropical vine native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many pepper species. The plant is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and clusters of tiny, greenish-yellow flowers. Its fruits are berries that mature to a dark purple color, containing a single seed.

This variant is notable for its unique chemical composition, which contributes to its distinctive flavor and potential medicinal properties.
