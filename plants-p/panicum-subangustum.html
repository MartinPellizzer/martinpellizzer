Panicum subangustum

Panicum subangustum, commonly known as narrowleaf panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along stream banks. The plant has slender, narrow leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often reddish in color. Its flowering period usually occurs in late summer to early fall.

Panicum subangustum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in wetland ecosystems.
