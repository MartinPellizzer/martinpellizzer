Pycnocoma angustifolia

Pycnocoma angustifolia, commonly known as the narrowleaf pycnocoma, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in regions of Brazil, Peru, and Bolivia.

Pycnocoma angustifolia is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and flowering habit.
