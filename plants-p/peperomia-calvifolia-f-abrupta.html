Peperomia calvifolia f. abrupta

Peperomia calvifolia f. abrupta, commonly known as the abrupta form of the calvifolia peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a velvety texture and small, clustered white flowers that appear in late spring or early summer. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape and often have a slight waxy coating. This variety is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and minimal water requirements.

It is prized for its attractive appearance and ease of care.
