Pelea niuensis

Pelea niuensis, commonly known as the Niue daisy, is a flowering plant native to the island of Niue in the South Pacific.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant features a low-growing rosette of narrow, fleshy leaves and produces small, white to pale yellow flowers with a central disc. It is adapted to the humid, tropical climate of its native habitat and thrives in well-drained soil.

Pelea niuensis is often found in coastal areas and is an important part of the local flora.
