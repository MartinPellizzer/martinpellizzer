Pentacalia buchtienii

Pentacalia buchtienii, commonly known as the Buchtien's pentacalia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant features a compact growth habit with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is not widely commercially available.
