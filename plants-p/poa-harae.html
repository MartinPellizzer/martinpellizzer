Poa harae

Poa harae, commonly known as the Harae bentgrass, is a species of grass native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and is often found in moist, temperate environments. The plant has narrow, blade-like leaves that are typically less than 2 millimeters wide. It produces small, spikelet-based flowers that are usually green to brown in color.

Poa harae is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in horticultural settings.
