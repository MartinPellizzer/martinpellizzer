Piper yamaranguilaense

Piper yamaranguilaense, commonly known as the Yamaranguila pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Piper.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly in Venezuela and Colombia. The plant is characterized by its vine-like growth habit and compound leaves with serrated edges. Its small, greenish-white flowers are followed by berry-like fruits that contain aromatic seeds.

Due to its unique botanical features and potential medicinal properties, it is of interest to botanists and researchers.
