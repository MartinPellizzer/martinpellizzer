Phyllanthus amicorum

Phyllanthus amicorum, commonly known as the friendship plant, is a small, evergreen shrub native to tropical Africa.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, round fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Due to its medicinal properties, it is also used in traditional herbal remedies.
