Phylica strigosa var. elongata

Phylica strigosa var. elongata, commonly known as the long-stemmed silver bush, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features narrow, cylindrical leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid conditions and requires minimal water once established.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
