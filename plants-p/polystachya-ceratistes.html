Polystachya ceratistes

Polystachya ceratistes, commonly known as the horned polystachya, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae).

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its tall, erect stems and dense clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, often covered with a fine layer of hairs.

Polystachya ceratistes is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and along stream banks.
