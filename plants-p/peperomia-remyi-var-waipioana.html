Peperomia remyi var. waipioana

Peperomia remyi var. waipioana, commonly known as the Waipioana peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Hawaii.

It features thick, waxy, and often variegated leaves that are typically oval to heart-shaped. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers, which are borne in clusters on short pedicels. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
