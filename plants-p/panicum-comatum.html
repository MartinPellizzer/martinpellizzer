Panicum comatum

Panicum comatum, commonly known as the silky panic grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and fluffy, silver-gray inflorescences that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping and erosion control. Its rapid growth and ability to reseed itself make it a popular choice for ornamental and ecological landscaping.

Panicum comatum is also valued for its role in supporting local wildlife, including birds and insects.
