Primulina hiepii

Primulina hiepii, commonly known as the Hiepii globose.

It is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to the humid, shaded environments of tropical regions. This plant is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and clusters of small, trumpet-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate to high humidity for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings.
