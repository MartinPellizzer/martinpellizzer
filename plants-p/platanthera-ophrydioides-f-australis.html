Platanthera ophrydioides f. australis

Platanthera ophrydioides f. australis, commonly known as the Australian lady's tresses, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and long, slender stems. The plant produces a striking inflorescence of white or pale pink flowers arranged in a loose, loose raceme. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and wetlands.
