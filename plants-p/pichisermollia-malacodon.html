Pichisermollia malacodon

Pichisermollia malacodon, commonly known as the Malacodon orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that come in shades of white and pink, often with a distinctive lip petal. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.
