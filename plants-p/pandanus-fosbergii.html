Pandanus fosbergii

Pandanus fosbergii, commonly known as the Fosberg pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pandanaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of the Pacific, including parts of Hawaii and other islands. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by woody, spiky fruits.

Pandanus fosbergii is well adapted to coastal and sandy environments, making it a resilient and hardy species.
