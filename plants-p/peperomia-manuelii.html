Peperomia manuelii

Peperomia manuelii, commonly known as the Manuel's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia manuelii is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and moderate humidity to flourish.
