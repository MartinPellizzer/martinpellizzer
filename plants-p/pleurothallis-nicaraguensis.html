Pleurothallis nicaraguensis

Pleurothallis nicaraguensis, commonly known as the Nicaraguan pleurothallis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid native to Central America, particularly Nicaragua. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, with its roots anchored in the substrate. Its flowers are usually small and white or pale green, with a distinctive labellum.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, organic soil for optimal growth.
