Pteridium yunnanense

Pteridium yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan brake fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Dennstaedtiaceae and is characterized by its fronds that are typically bipinnate, with a distinctive, often leathery texture. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories or along stream banks. Its sporangia are clustered in sori on the underside of the fronds, a typical feature of ferns.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
