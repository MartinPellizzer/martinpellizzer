Prestonia speciosa

Prestonia speciosa, commonly known as the showy prestonia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red. The plant is evergreen, with glossy, oval-shaped leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in coastal areas or dense forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant in warm regions.
