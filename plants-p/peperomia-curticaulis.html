Peperomia curticaulis

Peperomia curticaulis, commonly known as the rough-stemmed peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are often dark green with a slightly textured surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and ease of care contribute to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
