Phyllanthus nobilis var. guyanensis

Phyllanthus nobilis var. guyanensis, commonly known as the Guyana noble phyllanthus, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Guyana and parts of the Amazon basin.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical rainforests, thriving in humid and shaded environments. The plant features opposite, simple leaves with a glossy surface and produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several small, round seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for potential medicinal properties.
