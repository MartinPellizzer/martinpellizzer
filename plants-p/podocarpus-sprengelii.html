Podocarpus sprengelii

Podocarpus sprengelii, commonly known as the Chinese podocarp or Sprengel's podocarp, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Podocarpaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, spherical cones. The plant has needle-like leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around the stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Podocarpus sprengelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
