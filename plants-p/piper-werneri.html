Piper werneri

Piper werneri, commonly known as the Werneri pepper or Werneri's pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when mature, containing the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are often used for their medicinal properties, including antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory effects.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for both its ornamental and therapeutic uses.
