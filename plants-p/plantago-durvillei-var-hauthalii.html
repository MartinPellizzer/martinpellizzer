Plantago durvillei var. hauthalii

Plantago durvillei var. hauthalii, commonly known as Hauthal's plantain, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features rosettes of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Plantaginaceae family.
