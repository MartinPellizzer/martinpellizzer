Pilea montis-wilhelmi

Pilea montis-wilhelmi, commonly known as the mountain pilea, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to the high altitudes of Central America.

It features rounded, waxy leaves that are typically light green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring or early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its slow growth and low maintenance requirements make it an ideal plant for beginners and experienced growers alike.
