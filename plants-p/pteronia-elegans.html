Pteronia elegans

Pteronia elegans, commonly known as the silver frost plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Pteronia elegans is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
