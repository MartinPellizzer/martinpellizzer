Pennisetum rangei

Pennisetum rangei, commonly known as the red grass or red oat grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and vibrant red or purple plumes that appear in late summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas and is often found in disturbed soils. Pennisetum rangei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping and floral arrangements.

It is also known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and climates.
