Phyllostachys nigra var. punctata

Phyllostachys nigra var. punctata, commonly known as the black bamboo with spots, is a variant of the black bamboo species native to China.

It is characterized by its dark green to black culms that are adorned with distinct white or yellow spots along their length. The plant is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and is known for its tall, slender growth habit, often reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are long, narrow, and slightly drooping, contributing to its elegant appearance.

This bamboo is prized for ornamental purposes and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its aesthetic value.
