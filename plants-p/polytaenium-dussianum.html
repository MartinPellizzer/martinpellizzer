Polytaenium dussianum

Polytaenium dussianum, commonly known as the Dusky Polytaenium, is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded, forested areas. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread slowly through underground stems.

Due to habitat loss, Polytaenium dussianum is considered endangered and is the subject of conservation efforts.
