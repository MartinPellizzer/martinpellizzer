Pleurothallis tapantiensis

Pleurothallis tapantiensis, commonly known as the Tapanti orchid, is a species of orchid native to Costa Rica.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is part of the Orchidaceae family, known for its diverse and often colorful flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually small, with a variety of colors, and it blooms during the wet season.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
