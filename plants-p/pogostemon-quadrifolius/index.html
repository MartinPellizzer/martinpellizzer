Pogostemon Quadrifolius

Pogostemon quadrifolius is a unique aquatic plant known for its striking leaf patterns and medicinal properties. It thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments and is native to parts of Asia. The plant's leaves are often adorned with red and green markings, making it a popular choice for ornamental ponds. It has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its purported health benefits. Its cultivation requires careful attention to water quality and light conditions.
