Panicum platyphyllum

Panicum platyphyllum, commonly known as flatleaf panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its flat, broad leaves and slender, branched inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has a fibrous root system and produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to a variety of environmental conditions.
