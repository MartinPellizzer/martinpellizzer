Primulina tribracteata

Primulina tribracteata, commonly known as the velvet leaf.

It is a member of the Gesneriaceae family, native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of pink or red. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant.

Its unique foliage and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and plant enthusiasts.
