Poa nigropurpurea

Poa nigropurpurea, commonly known as purple purplegrass, is a species of grass native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive purple-tinged leaves and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small purple flowers.

Poa nigropurpurea is often found in alpine meadows and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticultural settings.
