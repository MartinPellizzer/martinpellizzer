Peperomia strawii

Peperomia strawii, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features glossy, waxy leaves that are typically a mix of green and silver hues, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has a short, stout stem with dense foliage, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are rarely seen. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to avoid root rot.

Peperomia strawii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
