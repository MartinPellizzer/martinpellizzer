Pseudostellaria × biseulsanensis

Pseudostellaria × biseulsanensis, commonly known as the hybrid starwort, is a flowering plant in the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Pseudostellaria hirsutella and Pseudostellaria japonica, characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
