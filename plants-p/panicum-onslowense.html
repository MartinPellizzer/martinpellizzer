Panicum onslowense

Panicum onslowense, commonly known as the Onslow panicgrass, is a species of grass native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems. The plant typically produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear delicate, awned florets. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or inland regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
