Pterotheca ruppellii

Pterotheca ruppellii, commonly known as the Cape dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features yellow ray flowers surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers, typical of the dandelion genus. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Pterotheca ruppellii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
