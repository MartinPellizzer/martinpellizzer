Psychilis tudiana

Psychilis tudiana, commonly known as the tropical orchid, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that often come in vibrant colors. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees without deriving nutrients from them. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Psychilis tudiana is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting biodiversity in its native habitat.
