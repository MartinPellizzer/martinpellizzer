Piper faroense

Piper faroense, commonly known as the Faroese pepper or Faroese long pepper, is a flowering plant native to the Faroe Islands.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and opposite, ovate leaves. Its fruit is a small, elongated drupe that turns red when mature.

Piper faroense is notable for its unique ecological adaptation to the harsh climate of its native region.
