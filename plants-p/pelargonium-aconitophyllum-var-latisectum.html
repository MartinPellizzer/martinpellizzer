Pelargonium aconitophyllum var. latisectum

Pelargonium aconitophyllum var. latisectum, commonly known as the broad-leaf geranium, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. The plant has palmately lobed leaves with a distinctive, almost fern-like appearance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant blooms and unique foliage structure.
