Polygonum afyonicum

Polygonum afyonicum, commonly known as the Afyon polygonum, is a flowering plant species native to Turkey.

It belongs to the family Polygonaceae, which includes plants commonly referred to as buckwheats. The plant is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of small, often reddish flowers. It typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Polygonum afyonicum is of interest in botanical studies due to its unique morphology and potential ecological roles in its native habitat.
