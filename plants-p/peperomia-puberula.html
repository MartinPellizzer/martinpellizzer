Peperomia puberula

Peperomia puberula, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features fleshy, ovate leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety pubescence, giving it a distinctive textured appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of green or red, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia puberula is known for its low maintenance and ability to adapt to a variety of indoor conditions.
