Phoradendron flavens var. australe

Phoradendron flavens var. australe, commonly known as the Australian flame tree, is a species of flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a woody vine that typically grows along tree trunks and can reach lengths of several meters. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a smooth surface and a pointed tip.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
