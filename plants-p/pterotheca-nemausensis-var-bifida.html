Pterotheca nemausensis var. bifida

Pterotheca nemausensis var. bifida, commonly known as the bifid pterotheca, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. It is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
