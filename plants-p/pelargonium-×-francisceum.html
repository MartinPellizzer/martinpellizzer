Pelargonium × francisceum

Pelargonium × francisceum, commonly known as the French geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has rounded, velvety leaves with a distinctive serrated edge. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Pelargonium × francisceum is widely cultivated as an ornamental plant for gardens and indoor spaces.
