Phillyrea medianifolia var. costei

Phillyrea medianifolia var. costei, commonly known as the Coste's Phillyrea, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, ovate to elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter, sometimes hairy underside. The plant produces clusters of white, fragrant flowers in the spring, which are followed by small, woody fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Phillyrea medianifolia var. costei is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in coastal and semi-arid environments.
