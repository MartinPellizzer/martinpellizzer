Phalaenopsis × veitchiana

Phalaenopsis × veitchiana, commonly known as the Orchid Queen, is a hybrid orchid prized for its long-lasting blooms and elegant appearance.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia, where it thrives in tropical climates. The plant features broad, dark green leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It is typically grown as a houseplant and requires bright, indirect light and consistent humidity.

Phalaenopsis × veitchiana is known for its resilience and ease of care, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
