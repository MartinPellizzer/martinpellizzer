Pleurothallis chionopa

Pleurothallis chionopa, commonly known as the white-haired pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves and clusters of small, white or pale green flowers. Its flowers are often fragrant and bloom during the cooler months.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
