Plectranthus eriocalyx

Plectranthus eriocalyx, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often grown as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Plectranthus eriocalyx is also valued for its medicinal properties, with some traditional uses in herbal remedies.
