Paepalanthus Geniculatus

Paepalanthus geniculatus is a unique plant native to the Andes mountains of South America. It is known for its striking, colorful flowers that bloom in the high-altitude regions. This species is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. Its habitat is often characterized by harsh conditions, including strong winds and low temperatures. Despite these challenges, it thrives and plays an important role in its ecosystem.
