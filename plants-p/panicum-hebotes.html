Panicum hebotes

Panicum hebotes, commonly known as the purple lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and its distinctive purple-tinged inflorescence. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Panicum hebotes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in erosion control due to its hardy nature.
