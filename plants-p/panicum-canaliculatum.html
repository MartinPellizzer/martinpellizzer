Panicum canaliculatum

Panicum canaliculatum, commonly known as the narrow-ribbed grass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is a perennial grass that typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields. The plant features narrow, linear leaves with prominent veins and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its flowers are usually pale green to brown, and it is wind-pollinated.

Panicum canaliculatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
