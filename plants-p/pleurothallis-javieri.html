Pleurothallis javieri

Pleurothallis javieri, commonly known as the Javieri's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other plants without deriving nutrients from them. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, loose soil for optimal growth.

Pleurothallis javieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
