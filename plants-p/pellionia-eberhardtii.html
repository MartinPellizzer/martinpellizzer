Pellionia eberhardtii

Pellionia eberhardtii, commonly known as the silver pellionia, is a low-growing evergreen perennial native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features finely divided, glossy green leaves that are often covered in a silvery or white powdery coating, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pale yellow flowers that are typically clustered in spikes. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used as a ground cover in tropical gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor cultivation in warm climates.
