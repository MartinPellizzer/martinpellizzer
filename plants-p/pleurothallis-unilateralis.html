Pleurothallis unilateralis

Pleurothallis unilateralis, commonly known as the unilateral pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that often display a range of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments.

Its botanical features include narrow, strap-like leaves and a compact growth habit, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
