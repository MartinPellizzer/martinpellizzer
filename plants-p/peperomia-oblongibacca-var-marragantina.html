Peperomia oblongibacca var. marragantina

Peperomia oblongibacca var. marragantina, commonly known as the Variegated Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with striking variegation of white, cream, and green, making it a visually appealing ornamental plant. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and red, typical of the Peperomia genus. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to avoid root rot.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for container gardening and indoor spaces.
