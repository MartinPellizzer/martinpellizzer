Pultenaea juniperina var. leiocalyx

Pultenaea juniperina var. leiocalyx, commonly known as the silver-leafed pultenaea, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery-grey pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring. Its foliage provides habitat for various insects and small animals.

It is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates.
