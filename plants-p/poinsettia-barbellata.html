Poinsettia barbellata

Poinsettia barbellata, commonly known as the "Christmas star," is a striking plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its vibrant red bracts, which are modified leaves that resemble flowers. Its foliage is typically green with a waxy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers.
