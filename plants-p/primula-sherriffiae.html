Primula sherriffiae

Primula sherriffiae, commonly known as the Sherriff's primrose, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as woodlands and mountainous regions. Its foliage consists of broad, ovate leaves with a velvety texture.

Primula sherriffiae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
