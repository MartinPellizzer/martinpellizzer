Paraphlomis jiangyongensis

Paraphlomis jiangyongensis, commonly known as Jiangyong grass, is a perennial herb native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan Province.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves, often covered with fine hairs. It produces small, two-lipped flowers that are typically purple or blue in color.

This species is valued for its potential medicinal properties and is often used in traditional Chinese medicine.
