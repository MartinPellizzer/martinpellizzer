Philodendron martineti

Philodendron martineti, commonly known as the Brazilian Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy, and dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by bright red berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Philodendron martineti is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
