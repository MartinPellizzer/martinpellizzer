Piper obsessum

Piper obsessum, commonly known as the sacred pepper or wild pepper, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper. The plant is characterized by its small, green, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of tiny white flowers. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

Piper obsessum is often found growing in humid, shaded environments in the undergrowth of tropical forests.
