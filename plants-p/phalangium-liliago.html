Phalangium liliago

Phalangium liliago, commonly known as the Lily Spider Plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery-white bloom. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It is native to South Africa and thrives in well-drained soil with indirect sunlight.

This low-maintenance plant is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
