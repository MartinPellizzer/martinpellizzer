Piper urdanetanum

Piper urdanetanum, commonly known as the Urdaneta pepper, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are followed by small, round berries that turn red when mature.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
