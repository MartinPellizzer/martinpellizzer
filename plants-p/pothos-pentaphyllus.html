Pothos pentaphyllus

Pothos pentaphyllus, commonly known as the five-leaf pothos, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically hidden within a spathaceous bract. Its leaves are typically dark green with a glossy texture and can vary in size depending on growing conditions.

Pothos pentaphyllus is a popular indoor plant due to its low maintenance requirements and aesthetic appeal.
