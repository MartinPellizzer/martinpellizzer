Premna mucronata

Premna mucronata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of about 3 meters. The plant produces small, pale greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, often in the morning. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate with a pointed tip.

The bark is smooth and pale, and the plant is often found in moist, shaded areas of tropical forests.
