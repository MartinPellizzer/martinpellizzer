Pepinia Beachiae

Pepinia beachiae is a rare and unique plant native to the coastal regions of southern California. It thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is often found in areas with mild coastal climates. This plant is known for its small, fragrant flowers that bloom in the spring. Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, Pepinia beachiae is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are ongoing to protect this delicate species and its natural habitat.
