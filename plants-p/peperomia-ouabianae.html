Peperomia ouabianae

Peperomia ouabianae, commonly known as the Rainbow Plant, is a strikingly colorful foliage plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features vibrant, waxy leaves that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow, creating a striking visual display. The plant is known for its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its unique leaf patterns and minimal care requirements.

Peperomia ouabianae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to add a splash of color to any indoor space.
