Plantago tubulosa f. maxima

Plantago tubulosa f. maxima, commonly known as the giant plantain, is a robust perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces tall, erect flowering stems that bear clusters of small, tubular flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated as an ornamental or for its medicinal properties.
