Peckia glabra

Peckia glabra, commonly known as the smooth peckia, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features simple, lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy. Its small, white flowers appear in clusters during the spring and early summer.

Peckia glabra is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local insect populations.
