Pleurothallis barahonensis

Pleurothallis barahonensis, commonly known as the Barahon orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with a delicate, compact growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
