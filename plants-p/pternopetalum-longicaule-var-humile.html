Pternopetalum longicaule var. humile

Pternopetalum longicaule var. humile, commonly known as the dwarf long-stemmed pternopetalum, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, which are usually white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
