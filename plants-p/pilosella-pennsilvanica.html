Pilosella pennsilvanica

Pilosella pennsilvanica, commonly known as the Pennsylvania goatherb, is a perennial flowering plant native to North America.

Pilosella pennsilvanica is often found in open woods, meadows, and disturbed sites, and it plays a role in supporting pollinators.
