Polium saxicola

Polium saxicola, commonly known as the saxicola grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a small, perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in mountainous regions. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its small yellow flowers are arranged in compact clusters, and it is adapted to harsh, arid environments.

Polium saxicola is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats across Europe and parts of Asia.
