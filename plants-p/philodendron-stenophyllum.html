Philodendron stenophyllum

Philodendron stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf philodendron, is a climbing tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by reddish-brown berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Due to its attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and foliage-focused plant collections.
