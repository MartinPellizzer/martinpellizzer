Pittosporum kweichowense

Pittosporum kweichowense, commonly known as the Guizhou pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its evergreen leaves that are glossy and dark green, often with a fragrant aroma. The plant produces small, white flowers that are typically clustered together, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
