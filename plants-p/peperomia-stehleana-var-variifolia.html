Peperomia stehleana var. variifolia

Peperomia stehleana var. variifolia, commonly known as the variegated peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions.

It features glossy, ovate leaves with striking variegation of white, yellow, and green patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental foliage, it is popular as a houseplant for its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
