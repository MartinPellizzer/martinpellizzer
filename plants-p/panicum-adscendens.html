Panicum adscendens

Panicum adscendens, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and elongated, narrow leaves that are typically bright green to reddish in color. The plant produces fluffy, pale yellow to brown panicles as inflorescences, which are often used in ornamental landscaping. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and erosion control.

Due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal, it is a popular choice for both residential and commercial landscapes.
