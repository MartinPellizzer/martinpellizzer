Peperomia stellata

Peperomia stellata, commonly known as the star peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and silver, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia stellata is valued for its ornamental foliage and air-purifying qualities.
