Pseudocentrum Hoffmannii

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae, known for its diverse and often strikingly beautiful members. This species is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts.
