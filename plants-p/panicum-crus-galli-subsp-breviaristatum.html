Panicum crus-galli subsp. breviaristatum

Panicum crus-galli subsp.

breviaristatum, commonly known as little bluestem, is a grass species native to North America. It is characterized by its fine, bluish-green leaves and compact, spike-like inflorescences.

The plant typically grows in open, dry habitats such as prairies and woodlands. It is well adapted to drought conditions and is often used in ecological restoration projects.

Its flowering period occurs in late summer, producing small, pale blue to purple flowers.
