Piper diversifolium

Piper diversifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly in countries like Brazil and Colombia. The plant is characterized by its broad, elliptical leaves and small, clustered greenish-white flowers. It produces small, peppery fruits that are used in local cuisines and traditional medicine.

Piper diversifolium is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique appearance.
