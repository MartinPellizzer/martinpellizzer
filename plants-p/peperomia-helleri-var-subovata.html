Peperomia helleri var. subovata

Peperomia helleri var. subovata, commonly known as the Heller's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically oval or egg-shaped, with a waxy texture and a vibrant green color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, arranged in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
