Polygala stephaniana

Polygala stephaniana, commonly known as the Cape York milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has a sprawling growth habit with hairy stems and small, ovate leaves. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal or inland regions.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
