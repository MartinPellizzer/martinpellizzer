Phaeiris × violipurpurea

Phaeiris × violipurpurea, commonly known as the purple viola, is a hybrid plant that combines traits from its parent species.

It typically features vibrant purple or violet flowers with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often dark green in color. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.

Botanically, it belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its short stature and compact growth habit.
