Phlomoides Mongolica

Phlomoides mongolica is a rare and resilient plant native to the arid regions of Mongolia. It thrives in harsh environments with limited water and extreme temperature fluctuations. This species belongs to the family Lamiaceae, known for its aromatic and often medicinal properties. Its unique adaptations make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Understanding Phlomoides mongolica is key to preserving biodiversity in fragile ecosystems.
