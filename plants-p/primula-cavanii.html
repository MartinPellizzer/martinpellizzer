Primula cavanii

Primula cavanii, commonly known as the Chinese primrose, is a perennial flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, white, and purple. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters, with oval, hairy leaves that are dark green in color.

It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
