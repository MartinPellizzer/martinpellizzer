Pyrethrum tabrisianum

Pyrethrum tabrisianum, commonly known as the African pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant produces small, spherical seed heads that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by the wind. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Pyrethrum tabrisianum is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
