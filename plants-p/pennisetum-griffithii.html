Pennisetum griffithii

Pennisetum griffithii, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that produce fluffy, purple-pink plumes during the flowering season. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green with a slight blue or silver hue. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping and drought-tolerant gardens.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
