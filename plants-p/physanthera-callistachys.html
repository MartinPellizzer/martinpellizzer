Physanthera callistachys

Physanthera callistachys, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, papery bracts that resemble small lanterns, which turn red or orange in the fall. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Physanthera callistachys is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in temperate climates.
