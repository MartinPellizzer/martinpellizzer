Peperomia marmorata var. argentea

Peperomia marmorata var. argentea, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features waxy, ovate leaves with a striking silver and green mottled pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers, though it is primarily valued for its foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its compact size and ornamental leaves make it a popular choice for tabletop displays and hanging baskets.
