Peperomia wibomii

Peperomia wibomii, commonly known as the Wibomii peperomia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and glossy, ovate leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color.

Peperomia wibomii is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and drought tolerance.
