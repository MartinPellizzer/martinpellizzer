Pitcairnia ensifolia

Pitcairnia ensifolia, commonly known as the sword fern pitcairnia, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, Colombia, and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its long, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 2 meters in length. It produces striking red bracts that enclose its small, white flowers, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Pitcairnia ensifolia thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in cloud forests.
