Phlomoides brachystegia

Phlomoides brachystegia, commonly known as the short-stemmed phlomoides, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant typically grows as a perennial herb with square stems and opposite leaves. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pale blue or purple, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
