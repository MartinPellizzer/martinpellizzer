Podocarpus wangii

Podocarpus wangii, commonly known as the Chinese podocarp or Wang's podocarp, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Podocarpaceae and is characterized by its needle-like leaves and cone-bearing structure. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and is adapted to temperate to subtropical climates. Its foliage is often used in traditional Chinese medicine, and it is also valued for its ornamental and timber qualities.

The species plays an important role in the ecological systems of its native habitat.
