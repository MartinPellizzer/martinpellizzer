Polystachya caespitifica subsp. latilabris

Polystachya caespitifica subsp.

latilabris, commonly known as the broad-lipped bamboo orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family. It is a herbaceous perennial that forms dense clumps of grass-like foliage.

The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing numerous small, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and specialized horticulture.
