Phebalium maxwellii

Phebalium maxwellii, commonly known as Maxwell's hakea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and distinctive floral structures. The plant produces yellow to orange flowers that are clustered in cylindrical inflorescences, attracting nectar-feeding birds and insects. Phebalium maxwellii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
