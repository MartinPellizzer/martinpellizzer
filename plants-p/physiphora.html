Physiphora

Physiphora, commonly known as the leafy spurge, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, silver-gray leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters. Its rapid growth and ability to spread through seeds and rhizomes make it a challenging weed to control.
