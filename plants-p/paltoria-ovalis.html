Paltoria ovalis

Paltoria ovalis, commonly known as the oval paltoria, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its oval-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and woody stems that become more pronounced with age.

Paltoria ovalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
