Pilea peploides

Pilea peploides, commonly known as the velvet pilea, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact growth habit and attractive appearance make it a desirable ornamental plant.
