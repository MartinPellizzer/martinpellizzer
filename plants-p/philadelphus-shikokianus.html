Philadelphus shikokianus

Philadelphus shikokianus, commonly known as the Japanese mock orange, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
