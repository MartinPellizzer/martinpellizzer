Philodendron caracaraiense

Philodendron caracaraiense, commonly known as the Caracarai philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves. The plant produces striking spadix flowers surrounded by a showy spathe, typical of its family. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens for conservation and study.
