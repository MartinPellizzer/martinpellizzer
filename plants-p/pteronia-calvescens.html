Pteronia calvescens

Pteronia calvescens, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers with a central disk. The plant typically grows in a compact, bushy form, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in fine hairs.

Pteronia calvescens is well adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often used in ornamental gardening.
