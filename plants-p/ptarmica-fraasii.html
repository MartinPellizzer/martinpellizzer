Ptarmica fraasii

Ptarmica fraasii, commonly known as the fraas's pincushion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of small, pinkish to white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow, gray-green, and covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.
