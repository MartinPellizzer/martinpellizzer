Pandiaka heudelotii var. spicata

Pandiaka heudelotii var. spicata, commonly known as the spicate pandiaka, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, spikelike inflorescences. The plant produces vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to red, attracting various pollinators. Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a decorative plant.
