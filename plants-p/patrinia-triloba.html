Patrinia triloba

Patrinia triloba, commonly known as Chinese dropwort, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants such as mint and sage. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Patrinia triloba is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for various health purposes.
