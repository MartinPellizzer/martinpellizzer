Pleurothallis cactantha

Pleurothallis cactantha, commonly known as the cactus orchid, is a species native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, loose soil.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
