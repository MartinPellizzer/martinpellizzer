Portulaca rosae

Portulaca rosae, commonly known as rose moss, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming structure with fleshy, succulent leaves. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that range in color from pink to red, often resembling miniature roses. It thrives in well-drained sandy soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Portulaca rosae is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
