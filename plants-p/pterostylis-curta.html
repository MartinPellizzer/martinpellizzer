Pterostylis curta

Pterostylis curta, commonly known as the short-stalked helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It is a small plant with a single, slender leaf and a distinctive, helmet-shaped flower that is typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant produces a single spike bearing a single flower, which is often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understoreys.

Pterostylis curta is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique floral morphology and specialized pollination mechanisms.
