Piper stellipilum

Piper stellipilum, commonly known as the star-hair pepper, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and heart-shaped leaves. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments within the understory of tropical forests.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
