Pavonia schimperiana var. tomentosa

Pavonia schimperiana var. tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly pavonia, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, soft appearance. The plant produces showy, pink to red bract-like flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
