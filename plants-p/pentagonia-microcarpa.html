Pentagonia microcarpa

Pentagonia microcarpa, commonly known as the dwarf pentagonia, is a small shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain several seeds.

Pentagonia microcarpa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
