Peristeria laeta

Peristeria laeta, commonly known as the silver star orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped white flowers with a yellow center. The plant produces aerial roots and thrives in humid, shaded environments. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its elegant appearance.

Peristeria laeta requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to flourish.
