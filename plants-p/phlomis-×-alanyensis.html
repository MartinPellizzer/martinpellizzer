Phlomis × alanyensis

Phlomis × alanyensis, commonly known as the hybrid everlasting phlomis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of the Eastern Mediterranean.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its tall, upright stems and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple. Its leaves are typically gray-green and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments.

Phlomis × alanyensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
