Pinguicula Vulgaris Var. Gypsophila

Ghost plant, scientifically known as Pinguicula vulgaris var. gypsophila, is a unique carnivorous species that thrives in harsh, arid environments. With its delicate, translucent leaves and ability to trap insects, it has captivated botanists and nature enthusiasts alike. This plant is native to regions with limestone soils, where it forms small, ephemeral colonies. Its ghostly appearance and unusual feeding habits make it a fascinating subject of study. Despite its fragile nature, the Ghost plant plays a vital role in its ecosystem.
