Pithecellobium microchlamys

Pithecellobium microchlamys, commonly known as the small-chalmer's pithecellobium, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

Its leaves are compound, with small, oval-shaped leaflets, and it has a shallow root system adapted to nutrient-poor soils.
