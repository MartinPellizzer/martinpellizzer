Piper nodosum

Piper nodosum, commonly known as the creeping pepper vine, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.

The fruit is a small, three-lobed berry that turns from green to red as it matures.
