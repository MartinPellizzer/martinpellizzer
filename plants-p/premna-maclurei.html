Premna maclurei

Premna maclurei, commonly known as Maclure's premna, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to New Zealand and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green to yellowish in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Premna maclurei is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
