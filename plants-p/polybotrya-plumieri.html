Polybotrya plumieri

Polybotrya plumieri, commonly known as the flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, flame-like inflorescences that display a mix of red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-drained soil.

Polybotrya plumieri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice in tropical gardens.
