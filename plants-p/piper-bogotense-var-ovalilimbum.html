Piper bogotense var. ovalilimbum

Piper bogotense var. ovalilimbum, commonly known as the oval-leaved pepper plant, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a member of the Piperaceae family, characterized by its climbing habit and aromatic foliage. The plant features ovate, glossy leaves with a distinctively waxy surface, and its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in spikes. It produces small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
