Polygonum strigosum var. hastatosagittatum

Polygonum strigosum var. hastatosagittatum, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive arrowhead shape, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes and stream banks. Its flowers are small, clustered in spikes, and are usually pink or red in color.

It is often used in wetland restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for wildlife.
