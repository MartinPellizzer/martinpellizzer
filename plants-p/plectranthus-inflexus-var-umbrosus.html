Plectranthus inflexus var. umbrosus

Plectranthus inflexus var. umbrosus, commonly known as the dark plectranthus, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features aromatic, hairy leaves that are typically dark green and slightly wrinkled. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to pink, often blooming in summer. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for both ornamental and medicinal purposes.
