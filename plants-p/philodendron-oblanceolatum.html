Philodendron oblanceolatum

Philodendron oblanceolatum, commonly known as the heartleaf philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by bright red berries, which are edible but often overlooked. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and humidity to flourish.

Its striking foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
