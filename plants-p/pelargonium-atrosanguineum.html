Pelargonium atrosanguineum

Pelargonium atrosanguineum, commonly known as the dark red geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its dark red to almost black flowers. The plant features velvety foliage with a deep green color and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its striking color and compact growth habit.
