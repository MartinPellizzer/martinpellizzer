Philodendron grandifolium var. hookeri

Philodendron grandifolium var. hookeri, commonly known as the Hookeri philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length, often with a waxy, glossy surface. The plant has a vigorous growth habit and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity.

Its variegated leaf patterns make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
