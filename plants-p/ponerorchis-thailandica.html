Ponerorchis thailandica

Ponerorchis thailandica, commonly known as the Thai orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky areas. Its roots are mycorrhizal, forming symbiotic relationships with fungi to enhance nutrient absorption.

Ponerorchis thailandica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
