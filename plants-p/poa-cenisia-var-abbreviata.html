Poa cenisia var. abbreviata

Poa cenisia var. abbreviata, commonly known as the short-stemmed meadowgrass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, temperate environments such as meadows and grasslands. The plant has slender, upright stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
