Panicum microcarpon var. isophyllum

Panicum microcarpon var. isophyllum, commonly known as slender panicgrass, is a grass species native to warm temperate and tropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to golden florets, which are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or disturbed areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
