Pyrenaria oidocarpa

Pyrenaria oidocarpa, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub with a short, woody stem. Pyrenaria oidocarpa thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
