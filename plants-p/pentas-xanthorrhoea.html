Pentas xanthorrhoea

Pentas xanthorrhoea, commonly known as the yellow flame plant, is a flowering shrub native to South Africa.

It features showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are arranged in a whorled pattern along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
