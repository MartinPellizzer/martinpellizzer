Pedilanthus laurocerasifolius

Pedilanthus laurocerasifolius, commonly known as the Brazilian starfruit tree, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

The plant produces small, round fruits that resemble starfruits, though they are not related to the common starfruit (Carambola). The plant is also known for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and landscapes.
