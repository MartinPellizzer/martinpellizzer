Phyllostachys parvifolia f. lignosa

Phyllostachys parvifolia f. lignosa, commonly known as the compact bamboo, is a compact form of the narrow-leaf bamboo.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and narrow, dark green leaves. The plant is native to China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is noted for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of climates.
