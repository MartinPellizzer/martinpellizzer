Phillyrea rosmarinifolia var. viridifolia

Phillyrea rosmarinifolia var. viridifolia, commonly known as the green-leaf Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage, with leaves that are typically a rich green color. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, woody fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental leaves make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
