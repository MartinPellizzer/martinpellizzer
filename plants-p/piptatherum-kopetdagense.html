Piptatherum kopetdagense

Piptatherum kopetdagense, commonly known as the Kopetdag grass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in steppes and sandy soils.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
