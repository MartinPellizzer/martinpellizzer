Polygonum nanetense

Polygonum nanetense, commonly known as narrowleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swales, and along stream banks. The plant features slender, upright stems with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in dense, terminal spikes and bloom in late summer.

Polygonum nanetense is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various insects and wildlife.
