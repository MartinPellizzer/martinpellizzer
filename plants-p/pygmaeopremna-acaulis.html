Pygmaeopremna acaulis

Pygmaeopremna acaulis, commonly known as the tree of life, is a small, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged oppositely on the stems. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has a reputation for being easy to care for.

The plant is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
