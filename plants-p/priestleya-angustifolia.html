Priestleya angustifolia

Priestleya angustifolia, commonly known as the narrowleaf priestleya, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and clusters of bell-shaped flowers. Priestleya angustifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
