Pentacarya heliotropoides

Pentacarya heliotropoides, commonly known as the sunflower tree, is a member of the Asteraceae family and is native to South America.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall, characterized by its smooth, gray bark and palmate leaves.
