Phyllanthus decaryanus var. manambia

Phyllanthus decaryanus var. manambia, commonly known as the African spurge, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that contain seeds covered in a sticky, resinous substance. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
