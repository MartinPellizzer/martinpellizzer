Phyllanthus cyclanthera var. scabrellus

Phyllanthus cyclanthera var. scabrellus, commonly known as the rough-stemmed spurge, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features opposite, ovate leaves with a rough, textured surface, and small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters. The plant produces capsules that contain numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it identifiable by its distinctive leaf texture and growth habit.
