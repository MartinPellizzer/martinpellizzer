Premna vestita

Premna vestita, commonly known as the white-flowered premna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, white or pale green, and are clustered in dense inflorescences.

It is often found in moist, shaded areas and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
