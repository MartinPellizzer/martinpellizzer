Phalaenopsis hebe var. amboinensis

Phalaenopsis hebe var. amboinensis, commonly known as the Amboinensis orchid, is a species native to the Maluku Islands of Indonesia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its upright, slender stem and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-draining soil. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy texture, which helps in moisture retention.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
