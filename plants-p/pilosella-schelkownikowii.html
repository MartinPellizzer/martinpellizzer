Pilosella schelkownikowii

Pilosella schelkownikowii, commonly known as the Schelkunov's goat's beard, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine, white hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Pilosella schelkownikowii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
