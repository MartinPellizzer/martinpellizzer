Pogostemon speciosus var. speciosus

Pogostemon speciosus var. speciosus, commonly known as the purple pogostemon, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It features lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive purple underside, which becomes more pronounced in sunlight. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color, arranged in dense spikes. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for water gardens and bog landscapes.
