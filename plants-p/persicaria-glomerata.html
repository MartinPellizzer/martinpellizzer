Persicaria glomerata

Persicaria glomerata, commonly known as clustered knotweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like buckwheat and sorrel. The plant features upright stems covered in small, reddish hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a slightly rough texture. It produces dense clusters of tiny, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late summer.

Persicaria glomerata is often found in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
