Penaea lanceolata

Penaea lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments such as rainforests. Its flowers are often fragrant and come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple.

Penaea lanceolata is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
