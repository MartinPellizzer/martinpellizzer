Poa × loliacea

Poa × loliacea, commonly known as the meadow grass or silvergrass, is a hybrid species within the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray blades that give it a distinctive appearance. This plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It forms dense clumps and has a rhizomatous root system that aids in its spread.

Poa × loliacea is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
