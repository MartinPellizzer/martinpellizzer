Paullinia urvilleoides

Paullinia urvilleoides, commonly known as the Urville's soursop, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Amazon Basin.

It belongs to the Sapindaceae family and is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
