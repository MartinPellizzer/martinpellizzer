Picea abies var. ellwangeriana

Picea abies var. ellwangeriana, commonly known as the Ellwanger fir, is a variant of the Norway spruce.

It is characterized by its narrow, conical shape and dark green, needle-like leaves. The cones are typically short and cylindrical, with a distinctive reddish-brown hue. This variety is native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
