Phyllosma capensis

Phyllosma capensis, commonly known as the Cape phyllosma, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Phyllosma capensis is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
