Ptilotus exaltatus var. pallidus

Ptilotus exaltatus var. pallidus, commonly known as the pale spinifex, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub with wiry stems and small, pale green to greyish leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a characteristic feature of its native habitat.
