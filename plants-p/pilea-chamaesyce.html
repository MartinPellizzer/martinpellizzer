Pilea chamaesyce

Pilea chamaesyce, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically bright green with a slight waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ease of care and attractive foliage make it a favored houseplant worldwide.
