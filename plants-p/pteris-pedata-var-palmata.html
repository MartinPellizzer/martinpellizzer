Pteris pedata var. palmata

Pteris pedata var. palmata, commonly known as the palmate water fern, is a fern species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features a distinctive frond structure with a fan-like, palmate shape, giving it its common name. The fronds are typically divided into several lobes, creating a delicate, lacy appearance. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near water sources.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and aquatic habitats.
