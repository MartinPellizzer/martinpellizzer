Polyscias suborbicularis

Polyscias suborbicularis, commonly known as the round-leaf polycarpic, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, ovate to round leaves that are typically glossy and dark green, with a smooth texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers followed by dark purple berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture for its attractive foliage.

Polyscias suborbicularis is also valued for its ability to tolerate partial shade and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
