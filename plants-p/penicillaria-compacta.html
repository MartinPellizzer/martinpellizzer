Penicillaria compacta

Penicillaria compacta, commonly known as the compact penicillaria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple in color, blooming in early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it conserve moisture.

The species is adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.
