Potentilla × cornazii

Potentilla × cornazii, commonly known as the Cornazii cinquefoil, is a hybrid shrub native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant features dark green, serrated leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white or silver hairs. It is well adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, making it a hardy plant suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant flowers and compact form, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
