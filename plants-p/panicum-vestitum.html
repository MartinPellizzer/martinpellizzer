Panicum vestitum

Panicum vestitum, commonly known as the dressed panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear minute, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.

Its botanical features make it an important component of certain ecosystems and a potential candidate for ecological restoration projects.
