Plectaneia thouarsii var. macrocarpa

Plectaneia thouarsii var. macrocarpa, commonly known as the giant plectaneia, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, palm-like leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by the development of woody, elongated fruits. Its unique morphology and habitat make it a subject of interest in botanical studies.

However, it is considered rare and is often found in specific, humid forest environments.
