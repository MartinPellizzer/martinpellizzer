Pholidota sororia var. djamuensis

Pholidota sororia var. djamuensis, commonly known as the Jambi pholidota, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Pholidota genus, characterized by its unique leaf structure and growth habits. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to several meters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a waxy surface, which helps in reducing water loss in humid environments.
