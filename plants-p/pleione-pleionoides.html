Pleione pleionoides

Pleione pleionoides, commonly known as the silver-veined orchid, is a rare and striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of China, India, and Nepal. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and elongated, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. Its unique appearance and habitat make it a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered endangered in many areas.
