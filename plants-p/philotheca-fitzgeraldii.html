Philotheca fitzgeraldii

Philotheca fitzgeraldii, commonly known as Fitzgerald's philotheca, is a small, flowering shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is found in regions such as New South Wales and Victoria. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the spring and summer months. It is often found in open habitats like woodlands and heathlands.

Philotheca fitzgeraldii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
