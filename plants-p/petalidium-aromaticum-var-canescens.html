Petalidium aromaticum var. canescens

Petalidium aromaticum var. canescens, commonly known as the silverleaf petalidium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers. The plant has silvery-gray foliage due to a dense covering of fine hairs, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its unique appearance and fragrance make it a desirable ornamental plant in certain horticultural settings.
