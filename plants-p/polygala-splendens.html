Polygala splendens

Polygala splendens, commonly known as the splendour milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, often showy flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually narrow and linear, while its flowers are clustered in dense, spikelike inflorescences.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
