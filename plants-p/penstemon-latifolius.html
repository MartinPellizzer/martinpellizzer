Penstemon latifolius

Penstemon latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf penstemon, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and open habitats, such as meadows and slopes. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, providing a striking visual contrast to the vibrant floral display.

Penstemon latifolius is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in full sun.
