Phyllostachys heterocycla f. holochrysa

Phyllostachys heterocycla f. holochrysa, commonly known as the golden bamboo, is a variant of the clumping bamboo species native to China.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow culms, which contrast beautifully with its lush green foliage. This ornamental bamboo grows in dense clumps, reaching heights of up to 10 meters, and is highly valued for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and landscapes. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it adaptable to various climates.

Due to its rapid growth and ease of maintenance, it is a popular choice for both residential and commercial landscaping.
