Pelea rotundifolia

Pelea rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf pelea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its round, leathery leaves that are typically a deep green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Pelea rotundifolia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
