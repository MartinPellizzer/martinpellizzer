Peperomia kamoloana

Peperomia kamoloana, commonly known as the Kamoloana Peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which are followed by reddish-brown berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia kamoloana is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
