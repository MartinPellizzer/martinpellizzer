Pentas purpurea subsp. mechowiana

Pentas purpurea subsp.

mechowiana, commonly known as the Mechowian pentas, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its vibrant, funnel-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to purple.

The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and lanceolate, with a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
