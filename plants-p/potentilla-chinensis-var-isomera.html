Potentilla chinensis var. isomera

Potentilla chinensis var. isomera, commonly known as the Chinese cinquefoil, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, yellow, five-petaled flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and a compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant flowers and hardy nature.
