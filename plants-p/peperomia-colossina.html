Peperomia colossina

Peperomia colossina, commonly known as the giant peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purple hue on the underside, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Due to its compact size and ornamental foliage, it is a popular choice for container gardening and as a decorative plant.
