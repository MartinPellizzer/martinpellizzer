Panicum teneriffae var. roseum

Panicum teneriffae var. roseum, commonly known as the roseum panic grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to the Canary Islands.

It features slender, upright stems covered in soft, pinkish-red foliage that gives it a distinctive, feathery appearance. The plant produces delicate, fluffy seed heads that add a subtle, ethereal touch to its overall look. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its unique coloration and graceful form make it a popular choice for modern landscaping designs.
