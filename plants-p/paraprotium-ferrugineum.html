Paraprotium ferrugineum

Paraprotium ferrugineum, commonly known as the rusty shield-leaf palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, pinnately compound leaves with a distinctive reddish-brown coloration on the underside. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a drupe that contains a single seed.

Paraprotium ferrugineum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions.
