Physostegia virginiana var. reducta

Physostegia virginiana var. reducta, commonly known as the narrow-leaf false dragonhead, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic foliage. The plant features slender, lance-shaped leaves and produces spikes of pale blue to purple flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges and wetlands.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
