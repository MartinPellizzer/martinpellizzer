Paphiopedilum gratrixianum f. gralherianum

Paphiopedilum gratrixianum f. gralherianum, commonly known as the Gralherian Paphiopedilum, is a rare and unique orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, long-lasting flowers that display a vivid combination of pink and white hues. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid, often found in shaded, moist environments within forest understories. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, with a waxy texture that helps retain moisture.

This hybrid form is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and limited availability.
