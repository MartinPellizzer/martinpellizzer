Phyllanthus anisolobus

Phyllanthus anisolobus, commonly known as the African spurge, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It features small, alternate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, followed by small, spherical fruits. It is known for its ability to thrive in poor soils and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for ecological and medicinal research.
