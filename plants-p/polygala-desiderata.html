Polygala desiderata

Polygala desiderata, commonly known as the desired pogonia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a member of the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, orchid-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and thrives in partial shade. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many areas.
