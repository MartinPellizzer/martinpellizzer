Peperomia ionophylla

Peperomia ionophylla, commonly known as the silver mitten plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features dense, velvety foliage with leaves that have a distinctive silver-white underside, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia ionophylla is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and moderate humidity to flourish.
