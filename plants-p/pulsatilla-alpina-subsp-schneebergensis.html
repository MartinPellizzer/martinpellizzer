Pulsatilla alpina subsp. schneebergensis

Pulsatilla alpina subsp.

schneebergensis, commonly known as the Schneeberg anemone, is a rare alpine plant native to Central Europe. It is characterized by its showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes. It has a rosette of basal leaves that are hairy and often covered in a waxy coating.
