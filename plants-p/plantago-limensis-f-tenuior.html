Plantago limensis f. tenuior

Plantago limensis f. tenuior, commonly known as the narrow-leaved plantain, is a variant of the common plantain species.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a dense spike, which are wind-pollinated. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and along trails.

This subspecies is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
