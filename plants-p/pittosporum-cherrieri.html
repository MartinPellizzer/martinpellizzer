Pittosporum cherrieri

Pittosporum cherrieri, commonly known as the golden pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically orange or red in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Pittosporum cherrieri is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
