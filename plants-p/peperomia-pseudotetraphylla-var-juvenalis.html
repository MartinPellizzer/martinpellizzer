Peperomia pseudotetraphylla var. juvenalis

Peperomia pseudotetraphylla var. juvenalis, commonly known as the Baby Peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features small, waxy, ovate leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a slight sheen. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale green, which are not showy but contribute to its ornamental value. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its slow growth rate and compact form make it a popular choice for tabletop displays and hanging baskets.
