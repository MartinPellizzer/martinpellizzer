Phillyrea media var. angustifolia

Phillyrea media var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaved Phillyrea, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, leathery leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in the spring, which are followed by small, spherical berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rocky or coastal gardens.

Phillyrea media var. angustifolia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
