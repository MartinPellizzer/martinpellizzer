Philodendron arbelaezii

Philodendron arbelaezii, commonly known as the Emerald Queen, is a tropical climbing plant native to Colombia.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a slight variegation. The plant has a unique aerial root system that allows it to climb and anchor itself to trees or other structures. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its foliage is often prized for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
