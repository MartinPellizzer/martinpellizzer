Phegopteris amplificata

Phegopteris amplificata, commonly known as the giant chain fern, is a large, tropical fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect fronds that can reach up to 3 meters in height. The plant has a complex rhizome system that supports its growth in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are bioluminescent and have a unique, segmented structure.

This fern is often found in rainforests and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
