Peperomia staudtii

Peperomia staudtii, commonly known as the Staudt's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which are borne on short, erect spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia staudtii is relatively low maintenance and suitable for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
