Pseuderanthemum latifolium

Pseuderanthemum latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf pseuderanthemum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
