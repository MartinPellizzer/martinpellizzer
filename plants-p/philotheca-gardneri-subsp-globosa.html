Philotheca gardneri subsp. globosa

Philotheca gardneri subsp.

globosa, commonly known as the globular philotheca, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia. It is characterized by its dense, spherical growth habit and small, aromatic leaves.

The plant produces clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
