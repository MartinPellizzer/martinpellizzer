Pogostemon fraternus var. nigrescens

Pogostemon fraternus var. nigrescens, commonly known as black pogostemon, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are often hairy and have a distinct aromatic scent. Its small, tubular flowers are typically purple or blue, arranged in whorls along the stem.

This variety is noted for its dark-colored foliage, which gives it its common name.
