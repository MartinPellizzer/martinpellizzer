Pterostylis repanda

Pterostylis repanda, commonly known as the broad-leaved greenhood, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It features a single, erect stem with a basal rosette of broad, ovate leaves. The plant produces a single, greenish-yellow flower with a distinctive hood-like labellum. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys.

The species is notable for its unique morphology and is part of the Orchidaceae family.
