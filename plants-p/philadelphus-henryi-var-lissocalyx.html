Philadelphus henryi var. lissocalyx

Philadelphus henryi var. lissocalyx, commonly known as the smoothleaf mockorange, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The leaves are ovate and have a smooth texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

This variety is valued for its showy blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
