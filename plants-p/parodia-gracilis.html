Parodia gracilis

Parodia gracilis, commonly known as the slender moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and pale green or blue-green stem, which often has a slight bluish tint. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
