Phyllostachys pubescens f. obtusangula

Phyllostachys pubescens f. obtusangula, commonly known as the broad-leafed bamboo, is a decorative bamboo species characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves.

It is a clump-forming plant that grows in dense, upright clusters, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping. The leaves are typically a vibrant green, with a slightly hairy texture on the underside. This variety is prized for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in a range of soil conditions.

It is often used in gardens and as a privacy screen due to its height and dense growth habit.
