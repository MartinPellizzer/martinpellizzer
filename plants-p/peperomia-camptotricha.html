Peperomia camptotricha

Peperomia camptotricha, commonly known as the zigzag peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink, adding visual interest to any indoor space. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, which appear in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia camptotricha is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
