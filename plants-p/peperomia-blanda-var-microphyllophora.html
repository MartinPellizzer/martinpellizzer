Peperomia blanda var. microphyllophora

Peperomia blanda var. microphyllophora, commonly known as the small-leaf peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features broad, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a subtle sheen, and it produces small, inconspicuous white flowers. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for indoor cultivation, where it can be grown in pots or as part of a succulent collection.
