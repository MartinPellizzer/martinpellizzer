Pyrus Rokujodaisanensis

Rokujodai pear, scientifically known as Pyrus rokujodaisanensis, is a rare and ancient fruit tree native to Japan. It is celebrated for its unique six-seed pears, which are believed to symbolize good fortune and prosperity. This plant thrives in the cool, humid climates of mountainous regions, where it has been cultivated for centuries. Its delicate leaves and fragrant blossoms make it a beloved ornamental and agricultural species. Despite its cultural significance, the Rokujodai pear remains a closely guarded secret among traditional growers.
