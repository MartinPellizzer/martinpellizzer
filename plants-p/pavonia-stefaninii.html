Pavonia stefaninii

Pavonia stefaninii, commonly known as the Stefanini's Pride-of-Peru, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to red bract-like flowers that resemble those of the traditional Pride-of-Peru (Pavonia hastata). The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters, with lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
