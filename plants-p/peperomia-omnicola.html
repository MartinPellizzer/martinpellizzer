Peperomia omnicola

Peperomia omnicola, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, velvety leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or red, depending on the variety. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
