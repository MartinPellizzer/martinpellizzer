Piptatherum chinense

Piptatherum chinense, commonly known as Chinese needlegrass, is a perennial grass native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Piptatherum chinense is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
