Peperomia angularis var. bethaniana

Peperomia angularis var. bethaniana, commonly known as the Bethaniana Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features small, rounded leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a waxy texture. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale pink, emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
