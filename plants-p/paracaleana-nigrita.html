Paracaleana nigrita

Paracaleana nigrita, commonly known as the black orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Australia.

It is characterized by its dark purple to blackish-brown flowers, which are often covered in a dense layer of velvety hair. The plant produces a single spike of flowers, typically with a few to several blooms, each featuring a distinctive three-lobed lip. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern at the base.

Paracaleana nigrita is a rare and endangered species, requiring specific humid and shaded conditions to thrive.
