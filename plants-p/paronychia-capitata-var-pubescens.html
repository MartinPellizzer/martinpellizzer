Paronychia capitata var. pubescens

Paronychia capitata var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy leaf ice plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in arid conditions with minimal care.
