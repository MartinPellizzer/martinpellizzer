Polydragma

Polydragma, commonly known as the goldenweed, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Polygonaceae.

It consists of several species, most of which are native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere. The plants typically have simple, alternate leaves and produce small, clustered flowers with five petals. They are often found in moist soils along stream banks or in wet meadows.

The genus is characterized by its distinctive, bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.
