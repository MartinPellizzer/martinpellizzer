Phelipanche pouyanii

Phelipanche pouyanii, commonly known as the Moroccan morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is a herbaceous annual that produces striking blue to violet flowers, often with a white or yellow center. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils. Its stems are hairy and tend to sprawl along the ground, forming a dense cover.
