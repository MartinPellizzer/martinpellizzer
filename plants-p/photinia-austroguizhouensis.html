Photinia austroguizhouensis

Photinia austroguizhouensis, commonly known as the Guizhou photinia, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically the Guizhou province, and is characterized by its distinctive red or pink flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, with glossy, elliptical leaves that provide a striking contrast to its showy blooms. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

The species is also noted for its hardy nature and adaptability to various soil types.
