Phylica simii

Phylica simii, commonly known as the Natal silver bush, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant is adapted to nutrient-poor soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained conditions. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Phylica simii also plays a role in supporting local wildlife, including birds and insects.
