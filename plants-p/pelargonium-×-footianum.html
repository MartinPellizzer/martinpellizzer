Pelargonium × footianum

Pelargonium × footianum, commonly known as the hybrid geranium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy, often brightly colored flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features serrated, lobed leaves that are typically hairy or velvety to the touch. It is native to South Africa and is widely cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and blooms.

Pelargonium × footianum thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade conditions.
