Pleurothallis cobanensis

Pleurothallis cobanensis, commonly known as the Coban orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is part of the orchid family, Orchidaceae. This plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. It features small, delicate flowers that are often pale green or white, with a distinctive lip.

Pleurothallis cobanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
