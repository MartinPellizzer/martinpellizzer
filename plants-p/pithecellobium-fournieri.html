Pithecellobium fournieri

Pithecellobium fournieri, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain edible seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
