Pelargonium × basiicum

Pelargonium × basiicum, commonly known as the African geranium, is a hybrid species within the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges, and its stems are often covered in fine hairs. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a garden plant.
