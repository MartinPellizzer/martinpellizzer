Palala mollissima

Palala mollissima, commonly known as the velvet fern, is a unique plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is well adapted to arid conditions. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are usually green or white, and it produces seeds in small capsules.

Palala mollissima is of interest to botanists due to its specialized adaptations and limited distribution.
