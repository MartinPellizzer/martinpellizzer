Polypogon × ascendens

Polypogon × ascendens, commonly known as the ascending grass, is a hybrid plant that belongs to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. Its inflorescence consists of a loose panicle with spikelets that are often tinged with purple or brown.

Polypogon × ascendens is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a resilient species in its native range.
