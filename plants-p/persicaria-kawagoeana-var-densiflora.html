Persicaria kawagoeana var. densiflora

Persicaria kawagoeana var. densiflora, commonly known as the denseflower smartweed, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its distinctive leaf shapes and clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as wetlands and stream banks. Its dense clusters of small, pinkish flowers bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators.

The leaves are lance-shaped with a reddish tint, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
