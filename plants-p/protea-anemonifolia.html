Protea anemonifolia

Protea anemonifolia, commonly known as the Anemone Protea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences. The plant features bright pink to red bracts that enclose the small, white flowers, creating a striking visual display. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Protea anemonifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
