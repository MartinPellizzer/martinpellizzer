Pyracantha koidzumii var. taitoensis

Pyracantha koidzumii var. taitoensis, commonly known as Taito pyracantha, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features dense, thorny branches and produces clusters of small, orange-red berries that persist through winter. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and winter interest. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is particularly valued for its hardiness and suitability in small garden spaces.
