Persicaria sieboldii f. viridialba

Persicaria sieboldii f. viridialba, commonly known as the silverleaf knotweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are predominantly silver-white in color, with a subtle green undertone. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, appearing in late summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in water gardens and bog landscapes.
