Phrynium dubium var. terrestre

Phrynium dubium var. terrestre, commonly known as the terrestrial phrynium, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are often dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that are borne on tall, slender spikes. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
