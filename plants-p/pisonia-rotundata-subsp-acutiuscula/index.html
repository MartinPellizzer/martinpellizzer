Pisonia Rotundata Subsp. Acutiuscula

Pisonia rotundata subsp. acutiuscula is a rare and striking plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its distinctive appearance, it features vibrant, showy flowers that attract a variety of pollinators. This subspecies is often found in coastal and lowland habitats, thriving in humid tropical climates. Its unique morphology and ecological role make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Despite its beauty, it faces threats from habitat loss and environmental changes.
