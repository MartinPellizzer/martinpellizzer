Pandanophyllum zippelianum

Pandanophyllum zippelianum, commonly known as the Zippel's pandan, is a unique plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Pandanaceae, which includes other aromatic plants like the common pandan. The plant is characterized by its long, narrow leaves that emit a distinct, sweet fragrance when crushed. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically appearing in greenish-white hues.

The Zippel's pandan is valued not only for its aromatic properties but also for its potential medicinal uses in traditional herbal medicine.
