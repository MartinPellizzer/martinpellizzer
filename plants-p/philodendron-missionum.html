Philodendron missionum

Philodendron missionum, commonly known as the Mexican evergreen philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with lighter green or white markings. The plant has a rapid growth rate and can reach lengths of several meters when provided with adequate support. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Philodendron missionum is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
