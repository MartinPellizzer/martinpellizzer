Pleurothallis stalkyi

Pleurothallis stalkyi, commonly known as the Stalk's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-white to pale yellow blooms that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a subject of interest for botanical researchers and orchid enthusiasts.
