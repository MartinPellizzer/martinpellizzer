Polygala weddelliana

Polygala weddelliana, commonly known as the Weddell's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, often pink or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are usually small and hairy, and it produces small, bean-like pods that contain its seeds.

It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and shrublands.
