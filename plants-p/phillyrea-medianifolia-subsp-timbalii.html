Phillyrea medianifolia subsp. timbalii

Phillyrea medianifolia subsp.

timbalii, commonly known as the Timballo phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically evergreen and have a leathery texture.

The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by small, spherical fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slightly different leaf morphology compared to other Phillyrea species.
