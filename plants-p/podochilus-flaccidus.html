Podochilus flaccidus

Podochilus flaccidus, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, hanging flower spikes. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are often fragrant. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Podochilus flaccidus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
