Phaedranassa schizantha var. ignea

Phaedranassa schizantha var. ignea, commonly known as the flame violet, is a strikingly colorful orchid native to South America.

It features vibrant red and orange flowers with a distinctive, elongated shape that sets it apart from other orchid species. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its leaves are ovate and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to its showy blooms.

The variety 'ignea' is particularly noted for its intense, fiery hues, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
