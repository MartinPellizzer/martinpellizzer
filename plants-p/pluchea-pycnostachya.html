Pluchea pycnostachya

Pluchea pycnostachya, commonly known as roughleaf heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and typically herbaceous growth habit. The plant features narrow, rough-textured leaves and produces small, clustered yellow flowers. It is often found in moist, shady environments such as forests and stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
