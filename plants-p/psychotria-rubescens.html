Psychotria rubescens

Psychotria rubescens, commonly known as the red psychotria, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and striking red or orange flowers. The plant produces small, dark blue to black berries that are edible but have a strong, bitter taste. It is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical rainforests.

Psychotria rubescens is also notable for its use in traditional medicine and its role in the ecosystem as a food source for various wildlife species.
