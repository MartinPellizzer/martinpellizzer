Paris Polyphylla Var. Taitungensis

Taitung paris, scientifically known as Paris polyphylla var. taitungensis, is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Taiwan. This perennial herb is part of the Melanthiaceae family and is celebrated for its unique, showy flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. Found primarily in the Taitung and Hualien areas, it thrives in cool, moist environments and is often seen in forest understories. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, the plant is considered vulnerable to environmental changes and human encroachment. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserve this botanical gem for future generations.
