Ptilotus polystachyus f. rubriflorus

Ptilotus polystachyus f. rubriflorus, commonly known as the red-flowered spinifex, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Australia.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and showy, red or pink flowers. The plant is adapted to harsh, dry environments and thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. Its flowers are typically clustered in panicles and bloom during the warmer months.

It is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
