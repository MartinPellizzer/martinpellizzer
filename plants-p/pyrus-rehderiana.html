Pyrus rehderiana

Pyrus rehderiana, commonly known as the Rehder's pear, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its deciduous habit and ornamental value. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a serrated margin, contributing to its visual appeal.

Pyrus rehderiana is often cultivated for its aesthetic qualities and has been used in horticultural settings for its adaptability and resilience.
