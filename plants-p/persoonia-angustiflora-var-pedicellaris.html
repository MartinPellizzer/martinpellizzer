Persoonia angustiflora var. pedicellaris

Persoonia angustiflora var. pedicellaris, commonly known as the narrow-leaved mistflower, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and clusters of small, white or cream-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in heathlands and dry sclerophyll forests, preferring well-drained soils. The species is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
