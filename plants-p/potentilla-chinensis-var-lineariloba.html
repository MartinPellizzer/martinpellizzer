Potentilla chinensis var. lineariloba

Potentilla chinensis var. lineariloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its slender, linear leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its ornamental value comes from its delicate foliage and showy inflorescences, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

It is also valued for its hardiness and ability to tolerate various environmental conditions.
