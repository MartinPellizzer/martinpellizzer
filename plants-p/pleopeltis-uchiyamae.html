Pleopeltis uchiyamae

Pleopeltis uchiyamae, commonly known as the Uchiyama's rock fern, is a species of epiphytic and lithophytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, dense fronds that often grow on tree trunks or rocky surfaces. Pleopeltis uchiyamae is often found in humid, shaded environments, such as tropical rainforests.
