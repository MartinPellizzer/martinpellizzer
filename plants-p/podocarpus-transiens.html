Podocarpus transiens

Podocarpus transiens, commonly known as the Chinese podocarp, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Podocarpaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, rounded cones. The tree typically grows in humid, tropical to subtropical climates and thrives in well-drained soils. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine, and the wood is valued for its durability and fine grain.

Podocarpus transiens plays an important ecological role in forest ecosystems, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
