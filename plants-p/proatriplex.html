Proatriplex

Proatriplex, commonly known as desert sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of North America.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming structure. The plant has small, green, fleshy leaves that help it retain water in harsh environments. It produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color.

Proatriplex is well adapted to sandy soils and can thrive in conditions with minimal water availability.
