Piper austrosinense

Piper austrosinense, commonly known as the southern China pepper vine, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many pepper species. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in spikes. Its fruits are initially green and turn red when ripe, containing small, black seeds.

The leaves are heart-shaped with a slightly rough texture and emit a mild peppery aroma.
