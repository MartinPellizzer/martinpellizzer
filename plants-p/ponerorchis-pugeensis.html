Ponerorchis pugeensis

Ponerorchis pugeensis, commonly known as the Puge orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to the Yunnan province in China.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, delicate flowers with a unique coloration. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is typically found in high-altitude grasslands and rocky areas. Its botanical features include pseudobulbs and a system of aerial roots that help it adapt to its challenging environment.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
