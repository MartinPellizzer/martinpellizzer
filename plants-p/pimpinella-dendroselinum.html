Pimpinella dendroselinum

Pimpinella dendroselinum, commonly known as the tree angelica, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, branching stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in umbels, typical of the Apiaceae family. Its leaves are compound, with finely divided leaflets, giving it a feathery appearance.
