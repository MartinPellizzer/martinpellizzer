Polypodium attenuatum

Polypodium attenuatum, commonly known as the slender wall fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated fronds that often grow in a cascading manner. The plant typically thrives in moist, shaded environments such as rocky crevices or forest floors. Its rhizome is creeping and produces a dense network of roots that help anchor it to surfaces.

The spores are released from the sporangia located on the underside of the fronds, contributing to its reproductive strategy.
