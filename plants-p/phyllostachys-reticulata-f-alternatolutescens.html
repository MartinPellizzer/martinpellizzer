Phyllostachys reticulata f. alternatolutescens

Phyllostachys reticulata f. alternatolutescens, commonly known as the golden bamboo or yellow bamboo, is a variant of the common bamboo species.

It is characterized by its striking yellow-green foliage, which contrasts beautifully with its typical green leaves. This ornamental bamboo is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and landscapes. It grows in clumps and produces slender, green culms with a distinct yellowish hue.

The plant is relatively hardy and thrives in well-drained soil with partial to full sunlight.
