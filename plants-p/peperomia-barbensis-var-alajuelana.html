Peperomia barbensis var. alajuelana

Peperomia barbensis var. alajuelana, commonly known as the Alajuela peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Costa Rica.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and white, which are borne on short pedicels. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it suitable for container cultivation and as a decorative plant in shaded areas.
