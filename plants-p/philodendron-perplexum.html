Philodendron perplexum

Philodendron perplexum, commonly known as the perplexing philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly variegated pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by berries containing seeds. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique leaf morphology and growth habits make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
