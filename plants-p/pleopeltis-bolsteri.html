Pleopeltis bolsteri

Pleopeltis bolsteri, commonly known as the Bolster's pleated fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, pleated fronds that give it a unique, ornamental appearance. The plant typically grows on trees or rocky surfaces, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, which helps it retain moisture.

Pleopeltis bolsteri is valued in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in tropical gardens.
