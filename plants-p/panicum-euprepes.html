Panicum euprepes

Panicum euprepes, commonly known as the scarlet panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a warm-season grass that thrives in full sun and well-drained soils. The plant produces tall, slender stalks with clusters of bright red to orange bracts, which are its most distinctive feature. It is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its striking appearance.

Botanically, it belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its narrow leaves and inflorescence structure.
