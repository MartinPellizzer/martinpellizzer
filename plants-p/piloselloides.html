Piloselloides

Piloselloides, commonly known as field thistle, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and showy yellow or purple flower heads. The plants are typically found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. They have a taproot system and can spread through both seeds and vegetative means.
