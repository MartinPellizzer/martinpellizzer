Pelargonium tenuicaule

Pelargonium tenuicaule, commonly known as the slender-stemmed geranium, is a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thin, wiry stems and small, often hairy leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright red or pink flowers, which bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Due to its compact growth habit, it is often used as a decorative plant in gardens and indoor settings.
