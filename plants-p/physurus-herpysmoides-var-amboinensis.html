Physurus herpysmoides var. amboinensis

Physurus herpysmoides var. amboinensis, commonly known as the Amboina nightshade, is a species of flowering plant in the Solanaceae family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and dark green, ovate leaves. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or blue in color.
