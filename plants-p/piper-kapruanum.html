Piper kapruanum

Piper kapruanum, commonly known as Kapruanum pepper or Kapruanum long pepper, is a climbing vine native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants such as black pepper. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are used in traditional medicine and as a spice. Its leaves are often used in local cuisines for their aromatic properties.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
