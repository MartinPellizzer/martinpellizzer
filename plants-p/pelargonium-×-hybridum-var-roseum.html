Pelargonium × hybridum var. roseum

Pelargonium × hybridum var. roseum, commonly known as the roseum geranium, is a hybrid species within the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, double-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to deep red. The plant typically features broad, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular in gardens and as a potted plant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
