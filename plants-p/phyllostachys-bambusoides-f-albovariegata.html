Phyllostachys bambusoides f. albovariegata

Phyllostachys bambusoides f. albovariegata, commonly known as the white-variegated bamboo, is a variant of the silver bamboo species.

It is characterized by its striking white and green variegated leaves, which add visual interest to gardens. This ornamental bamboo is native to East Asia and thrives in temperate climates. It grows as a clump-forming plant, reaching heights of up to 6 meters.

Its foliage provides a unique texture and color contrast, making it a popular choice for landscaping.
