Piper leptoneuron

Piper leptoneuron, commonly known as the slender pepper vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

The fruit is a small, three-lobed berry that turns from green to yellow when mature.
