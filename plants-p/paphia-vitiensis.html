Paphia vitiensis

Paphia vitiensis, commonly known as the Vitiensis Paphiopedilum, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with a unique lip that is often brightly colored. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Paphia vitiensis is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
