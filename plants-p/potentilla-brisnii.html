Potentilla brisnii

Potentilla brisnii, commonly known as the alpine potentilla, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Potentilla brisnii thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.
