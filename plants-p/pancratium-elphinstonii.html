Pancratium elphinstonii

Pancratium elphinstonii, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and sword-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils. It blooms during the summer months, producing clusters of bright yellow flowers that attract pollinators.

Pancratium elphinstonii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
