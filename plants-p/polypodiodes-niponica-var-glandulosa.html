Polypodiodes niponica var. glandulosa

Polypodiodes niponica var. glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular polypodiodes, is a fern native to eastern Asia.

It features delicate, lance-shaped fronds with a glossy, dark green surface. The plant produces small, spore-bearing structures called sporangia on the underside of its leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and garden cultivation.
