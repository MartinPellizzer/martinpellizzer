Philodendron inaequilaterum subsp. anthoblastum

Philodendron inaequilaterum subsp.

anthoblastum, commonly known as the Anthoblastum philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to the Amazon rainforest. It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with cream or white markings.

The plant has a fibrous root system and produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical plant collections.
