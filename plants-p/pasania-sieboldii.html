Pasania sieboldii

Pasania sieboldii, commonly known as the Japanese star anise.

It is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia, particularly Japan and China. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruits that are used in traditional medicine and as a spice. Its leaves are opposite and oval, with a slightly hairy surface.

The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.
