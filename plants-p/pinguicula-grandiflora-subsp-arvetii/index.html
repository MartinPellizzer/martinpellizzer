Pinguicula Grandiflora Subsp. Arvetii

Pinguicula grandiflora subsp. arvetii is a unique carnivorous plant native to specific regions of southern Europe. It is known for its ability to trap and digest small insects using sticky, glandular leaves. This subspecies thrives in moist, nutrient-poor environments such as bogs and wet meadows. Its flowers are striking, often displaying vivid colors that attract pollinators. Despite its beauty, it is a delicate plant that requires careful cultivation and conservation efforts.
