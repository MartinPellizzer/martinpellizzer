Piper subpeltatum var. sidifolium

Piper subpeltatum var. sidifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pepper plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes numerous species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers. It produces small, green berries that mature to a dark purple color.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden environments.
