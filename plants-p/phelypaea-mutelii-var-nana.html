Phelypaea mutelii var. nana

Phelypaea mutelii var. nana, commonly known as the dwarf phelypaea, is a compact, herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically a vibrant green, often with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces delicate, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the wet season. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for ornamental gardening in humid climates.
