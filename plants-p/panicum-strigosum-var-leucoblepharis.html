Panicum strigosum var. leucoblepharis

Panicum strigosum var. leucoblepharis, commonly known as silverleaf panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which results from dense pubescence covering the leaves. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and along roadsides. Its inflorescence consists of a loose panicle with spikelets that are often pale to whitish in color.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
