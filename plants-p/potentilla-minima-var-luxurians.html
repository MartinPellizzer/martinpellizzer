Potentilla minima var. luxurians

Potentilla minima var. luxurians, commonly known as the dwarf cinquefoil, is a low-growing perennial shrub native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features small, delicate yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has hairy, upright stems and pinnately compound leaves with five leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact size and ornamental value, it is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover.
