Pleopeltis asterolepis

Pleopeltis asterolepis, commonly known as the star-leaved huperzia, is a species of fern that belongs to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped arrangement of leaves, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its fronds are leathery and have a rough texture, with spore-producing structures on the underside.

Pleopeltis asterolepis is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
