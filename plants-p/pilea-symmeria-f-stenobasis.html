Pilea symmeria f. stenobasis

Pilea symmeria f. stenobasis, commonly known as the Shingleplant, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features dense clusters of small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces inconspicuous yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by small, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable ornamental plant.
