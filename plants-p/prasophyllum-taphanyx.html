Prasophyllum taphanyx

Prasophyllum taphanyx, commonly known as the narrow-leaved orchid, is a species of orchid native to Australia.

It is a small, terrestrial plant that typically grows in heathlands and sandy soils. The plant features a slender stem with a single or few flowers, which are usually pale green or white. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is adapted to thrive in nutrient-poor environments.
