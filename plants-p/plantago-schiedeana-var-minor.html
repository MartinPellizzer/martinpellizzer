Plantago schiedeana var. minor

Plantago schiedeana var. minor, commonly known as the lesser plantain, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, disturbed soils such as along roadsides or in meadows. The plant features basal rosettes of broad, lance-shaped leaves with hairy surfaces. It produces small, greenish flowers in a spike-like inflorescence, which are wind-pollinated.

This species is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
