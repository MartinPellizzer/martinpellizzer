Pfundia

Pfundia, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a popular epiphytic plant native to South America.

It belongs to the genus Schlumbergera and is characterized by its segmented, fleshy stems that resemble a cactus. The plant produces vibrant, tubular flowers in a variety of colors, typically blooming during the holiday season. Pfundia thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a favorite among indoor gardeners.
