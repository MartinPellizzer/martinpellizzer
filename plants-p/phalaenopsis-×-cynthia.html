Phalaenopsis × cynthia

Phalaenopsis × cynthia, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a popular epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a velvety texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent humidity and moderate temperatures.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a houseplant and for commercial floral arrangements.
