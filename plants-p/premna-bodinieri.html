Premna bodinieri

Premna bodinieri, commonly known as the Vietnamese nettle or Vietnamese glorybower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. It is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forests and along stream banks.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
