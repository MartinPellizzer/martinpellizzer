Piper leprieurii

Piper leprieurii, commonly known as the Leprieur's pepper or the African pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, greenish-yellow flowers. It produces small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
