Pomaderris Wendlandiana

Pomaderris wendlandiana is a unique shrub native to western australia known for its striking silver-green foliage. it thrives in coastal and sandy soils and is often found in heathland environments. this plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand harsh conditions. it features small white flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticulture.
