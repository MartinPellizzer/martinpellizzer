Phyllactis spathulata

Phyllactis spathulata, commonly known as the broad-leaved phyllactis, is a species of flowering plant in the family Phyllacanthaceae.

It is characterized by its broad, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. It is native to parts of Southeast Asia and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Phyllactis spathulata is adapted to grow in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories.
