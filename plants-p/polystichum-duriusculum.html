Polystichum duriusculum

Polystichum duriusculum, commonly known as the hardy wood fern, is a species of fern native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach up to two meters in height. The plant has a robust rhizome system that allows it to thrive in shaded, moist environments. Its leaves are typically divided into numerous narrow segments, giving it a feathery appearance.

Polystichum duriusculum is well adapted to woodland habitats and is often found in areas with rich, organic soil.
