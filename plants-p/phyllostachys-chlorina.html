Phyllostachys chlorina

Phyllostachys chlorina, commonly known as the green bamboo, is a species of grass in the bamboo family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its slender, green stems that often have a smooth, almost glassy appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers and is typically grown for its ornamental value. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Phyllostachys chlorina is a fast-growing plant that is often used in landscaping and as a decorative element in gardens.
