Pennisetum pentastachyum

Pennisetum pentastachyum, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features tall, slender stalks that produce dense, fluffy plumes of purple or pink flowers, which appear in late summer to early autumn. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adding visual interest throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Pennisetum pentastachyum is also valued for its ability to attract birds and other pollinators.
