Panicum chlorostachyum

Panicum chlorostachyum, commonly known as blue panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, bluish-green leaves and dense, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has a fibrous root system and can thrive in a variety of soil types.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
