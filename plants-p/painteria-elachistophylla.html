Painteria elachistophylla

Painteria elachistophylla, commonly known as the velvet leaf.

This plant is native to the rainforests of Brazil and is characterized by its unique, velvety, dark green foliage. It is a member of the Malvaceae family, which includes hibiscus and cotton. The leaves are thick and leathery, providing a striking visual contrast to its pale yellow flowers.
