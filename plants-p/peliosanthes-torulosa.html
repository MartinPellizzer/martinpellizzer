Peliosanthes torulosa

Peliosanthes torulosa, commonly known as the golden trumpet tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. The plant belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is known for its rapid growth and ornamental value in gardens. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a glossy surface, while its bark is smooth and light-colored.

The species is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display and adaptability to various soil types.
