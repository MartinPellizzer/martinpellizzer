Polygala sprengeliana

Polygala sprengeliana, commonly known as the Sprengel's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, spindly stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellowish, with a distinctive spurred structure at the base of each bloom.

It is often found in dry, open habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
