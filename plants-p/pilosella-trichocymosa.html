Pilosella trichocymosa

Pilosella trichocymosa, commonly known as the hairy leaf mouse-ear, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers and hairy stems and leaves. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. The plant has finely divided, silvery-green leaves that give it a distinctive appearance.

Its flowers bloom in the summer, producing yellow centers surrounded by white ray florets.
