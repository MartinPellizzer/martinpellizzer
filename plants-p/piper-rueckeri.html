Piper rueckeri

Piper rueckeri, commonly known as the Rucker's pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by small, peppery fruits.

Piper rueckeri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has a mild peppery flavor, making it suitable for use in culinary applications.
