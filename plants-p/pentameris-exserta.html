Pentameris exserta

Pentameris exserta, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and bell-shaped flowers. The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
