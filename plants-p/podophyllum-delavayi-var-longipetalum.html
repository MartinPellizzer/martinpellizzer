Podophyllum delavayi var. longipetalum

Podophyllum delavayi var. longipetalum, commonly known as the long-petaled podophyllum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It belongs to the family Podophyllaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, clustered growth habit. The plant features striking, bright red or orange berries that are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are typically broad, heart-shaped, and have a waxy texture.

The variety longipetalum is distinguished by its elongated petals, which give it a unique appearance compared to other members of the species.
