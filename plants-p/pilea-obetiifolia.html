Pilea obetiifolia

Pilea obetiifolia, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features rounded, fleshy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Pilea obetiifolia is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
