Pennisetum niloticum

Pennisetum niloticum, commonly known as giant pennisetum, is a tall, perennial grass native to Africa.

It features slender, arching leaves that can grow up to several meters in height, with a dense, tufted inflorescence at the top. The plant is characterized by its ability to thrive in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping. Its rapid growth and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for ornamental and erosion control purposes.

The plant also plays a role in traditional medicine and livestock fodder in some regions.
