Poteranthera genliseoides

Poteranthera genliseoides, commonly known as the silverleaf germander, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated for both aesthetic and therapeutic purposes.
