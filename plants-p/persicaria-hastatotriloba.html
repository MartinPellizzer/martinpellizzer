Persicaria hastatotriloba

Persicaria hastatotriloba, commonly known as the three-lobed smartweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes other species like buckwheat and sorrel. The plant features alternate, heart-shaped leaves with three distinct lobes, giving it its scientific name. It produces small, greenish flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in late summer.
