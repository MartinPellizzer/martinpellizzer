Pelexia huntii

Pelexia huntii, commonly known as the yellow pelexia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun.

Pelexia huntii is often found in wetlands and along stream banks, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.
