Philodendron inaequilaterum

Philodendron inaequilaterum, commonly known as the Variegated Philodendron, is a climbing tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves with a striking combination of green, white, and yellow variegation. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread and climb effectively. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.
