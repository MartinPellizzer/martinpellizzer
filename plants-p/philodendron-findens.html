Philodendron findens

Philodendron findens, commonly known as the Findens philodendron, is a climbing plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy green leaves that are often variegated with cream or white markings. The plant has a vigorous growth habit and produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Philodendron findens is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
