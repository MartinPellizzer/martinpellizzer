Pleurothallis morganii

Pleurothallis morganii, commonly known as the Morgan's orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America, particularly Colombia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with intricate patterns on the petals. It has a short, compact growth habit and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a threatened species in its native range.
