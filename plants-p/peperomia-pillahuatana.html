Peperomia pillahuatana

Peperomia pillahuatana, commonly known as the Pillahuata peperomia, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is a member of the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant features small, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. It produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color.

Peperomia pillahuatana is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.
