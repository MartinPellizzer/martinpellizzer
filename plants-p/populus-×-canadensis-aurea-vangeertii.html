Populus × canadensis aurea-vangeertii

Populus × canadensis aurea-vangeertii, commonly known as the golden sunset poplar, is a hybrid deciduous tree that exhibits striking golden foliage.

It belongs to the Salicaceae family and is characterized by its fast growth rate and ornamental value. The leaves are ovate with a serrated margin and turn a brilliant yellow in the fall. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for landscaping in temperate climates.

This cultivar is prized for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
