Pelargonium hornemannii

Pelargonium hornemannii, commonly known as the hornemannii geranium, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family, characterized by its succulent leaves and showy flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually pink or red, with five petals and prominent stamens.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
