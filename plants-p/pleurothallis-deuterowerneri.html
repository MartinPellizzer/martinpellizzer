Pleurothallis deuterowerneri

Pleurothallis deuterowerneri, commonly known as the Werneri's pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its epiphytic nature allows it to grow on trees and rocks in its natural habitat.

Due to its rarity and specific ecological requirements, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
