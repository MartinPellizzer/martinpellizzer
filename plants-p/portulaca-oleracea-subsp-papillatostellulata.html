Portulaca oleracea subsp. papillatostellulata

Portulaca oleracea subsp.

papillatostellulata, commonly known as the starry purslane, is a succulent plant characterized by its fleshy, thick stems and leaves. It produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color.

This subspecies is native to tropical and subtropical regions and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in disturbed areas, such as roadsides and gardens.

The plant is adapted to hot, dry environments and has a short growing season.
