Piptadenia rubescens

Piptadenia rubescens, commonly known as the red flame tree, is a tropical tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers that appear in clusters, giving it its common name. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and it has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its bark is thick and often fissured, and the tree can grow up to 20 meters tall.
