Phlomis × composita

Phlomis × composita, commonly known as the hybrid garden phlomis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features tall, upright stems covered in soft, hairy foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a grayish or silver hue due to the dense covering of fine hairs.

Phlomis × composita is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
