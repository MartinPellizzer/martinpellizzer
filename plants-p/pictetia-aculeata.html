Pictetia aculeata

Pictetia aculeata, commonly known as the spiny pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features dark green, glossy leaves that are often covered in small, stiff spines. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
