Peltogyne venosa var. densiflora

Peltogyne venosa var. densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered ironwood, is a tropical tree native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hard, heavy wood and dense foliage, which contributes to its common name. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in spikes. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland and hill forest areas.

The species is valued for its durable timber and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
