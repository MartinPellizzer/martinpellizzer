Podocarpus magnifolius

Podocarpus magnifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf podocarp, is a species of coniferous evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and can grow up to 30 meters tall. The plant has scale-like leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stems. It produces small, yellowish cones that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This species is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and is also valued for its timber.
