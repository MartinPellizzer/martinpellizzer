Pogostemon Pentagonus

Five-angled pogostemon, scientifically known as Pogostemon pentagonus, is a unique and intriguing plant species native to Southeast Asia. This herbaceous perennial is characterized by its distinct five-angled stems and striking, lance-shaped leaves that catch the eye in any garden. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. With its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it has gained attention among botanists and horticulturists alike. The plant's adaptability and aesthetic appeal make it a fascinating subject for study and cultivation.
