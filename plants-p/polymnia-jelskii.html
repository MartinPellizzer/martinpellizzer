Polymnia jelskii

Polymnia jelskii, commonly known as the Jelskii's polymnia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Polygonaceae, which includes plants known for their distinctive leaf structures and often colorful flowers. The plant features alternate, lanceolate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, typically producing a striking display of red or pink blooms.

Polymnia jelskii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
