Pilea kiwuensis

Pilea kiwuensis, commonly known as the African velvet plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Urticaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy, dark green surface and a soft, fuzzy texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color.

Pilea kiwuensis is often grown as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
