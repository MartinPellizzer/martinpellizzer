Platycodon grandiflorus var. planicorollatus

Platycodon grandiflorus var. planicorollatus, commonly known as the flat bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in late summer. The plant has oval, dark green leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a valued addition to botanical collections.
