Pentadesma leptonema var. klainei

Pentadesma leptonema var. klainei, commonly known as the slender-leaved pentadesma, is a variant of the pentadesma genus native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves that are often pubescent on the underside. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
