Pilosella novosibirskensis

Pilosella novosibirskensis, commonly known as the Siberian mouse-ear, is a small flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single bloom. The plant features silver-gray leaves covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Pilosella novosibirskensis is typically found in rocky or alpine habitats, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
