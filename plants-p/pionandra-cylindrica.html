Pionandra cylindrica

Pionandra cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical pionandra, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. Pionandra cylindrica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance once established.
