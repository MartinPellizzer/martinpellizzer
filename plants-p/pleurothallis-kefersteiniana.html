Pleurothallis kefersteiniana

Pleurothallis kefersteiniana, commonly known as the Kefers Stein's pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, elongated leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is noted for its intricate floral structure.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
