Parquetina

Parquetina, commonly known as the "false arrowhead," is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. Parquetina is also known for its medicinal properties, with various parts of the plant used in traditional medicine.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
