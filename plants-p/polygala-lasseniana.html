Polygala lasseniana

Polygala lasseniana, commonly known as the Lassen's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its small, intricate flowers that resemble those of the genus Milkvetch. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.
