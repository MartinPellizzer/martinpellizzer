Pleione formosana f. nivea

Pleione formosana f. nivea, commonly known as the white-flowered pleione, is a rare and striking orchid native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, white petals and a distinctive greenish-yellow lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments at high altitudes, often on rocky substrates. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a delicate beauty to its natural habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in conservation efforts.
