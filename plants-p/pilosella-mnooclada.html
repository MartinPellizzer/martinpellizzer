Pilosella mnooclada

Pilosella mnooclada, commonly known as the slender pilosella, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright, hairy stems and small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Native to Central and Eastern Europe, this plant thrives in meadows and open woodlands. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Pilosella mnooclada is often found in disturbed soils and is considered a short-lived perennial species.
