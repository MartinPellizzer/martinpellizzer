Peperomia kamerunana

Peperomia kamerunana, commonly known as the Cameroon peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the rainforests of Cameroon.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval to lance-shaped, with a vibrant green color and a smooth texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which are borne on short, erect stems. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia kamerunana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
