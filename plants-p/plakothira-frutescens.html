Plakothira frutescens

Plakothira frutescens, commonly known as the tropical milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, featuring opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright orange or red flowers, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas or near water sources.

Plakothira frutescens is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
