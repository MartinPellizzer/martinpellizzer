Peperomia pennellii

Peperomia pennellii, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features waxy, silver-green leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive rosette growth habit. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that emerge from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia pennellii is relatively low maintenance and resistant to pests, contributing to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
