Philodendron callosum

Philodendron callosum, commonly known as the velvet leaf philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, waxy leaves that often have a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers, which are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Philodendron callosum is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
