Pleurothallis reichenbachiana

Pleurothallis reichenbachiana, commonly known as the Reichenbach's pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with intricate patterns on the petals. Its leaves are usually dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
