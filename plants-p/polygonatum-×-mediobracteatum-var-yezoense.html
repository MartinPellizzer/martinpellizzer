Polygonatum × mediobracteatum var. yezoense

Polygonatum × mediobracteatum var. yezoense, commonly known as Yezo yellow flag.

It is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asparagaceae family, characterized by its creeping rhizomes and dense clusters of yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a fan-like pattern, while the inflorescence consists of multiple small, fragrant flowers.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
