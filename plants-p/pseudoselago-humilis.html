Pseudoselago humilis

Pseudoselago humilis, commonly known as the low pseudoselago, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. Its flowers are white and clustered in umbels, adding a delicate appearance to the plant.

It is often used in rock gardens due to its hardy nature and ornamental value.
