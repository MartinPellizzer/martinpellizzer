Pleione thuniana

Pleione thuniana, commonly known as the orchid of the mountains, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the Himalayas and parts of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant features a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients, supporting its growth in high-altitude regions. Its flowers, typically white or pale pink, are showy and bloom in spring, adding ornamental value.

Pleione thuniana is valued in horticulture for its beauty and resilience in shaded, humid conditions.
