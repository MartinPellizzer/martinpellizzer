Pterocephalus frutescens var. frutescens

Pterocephalus frutescens var. frutescens, commonly known as the Chinese thornapple, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and spiny branches. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring, followed by spherical, spiny fruits containing seeds. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in arid and semi-arid regions.

The plant has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its potential medicinal properties.
