Piper malamiri

Piper malamiri, commonly known as the Malamiri pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with slender stems that often require support. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a glossy surface, while the flowers are small and greenish-white in color.

The fruit is a small, elongated berry that turns red when mature, containing several seeds.
