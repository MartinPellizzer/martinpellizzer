Phylica recurvifolia

Phylica recurvifolia, commonly known as the silver bush, is a small shrub native to South Africa.

It features narrow, silver-gray leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators. It is well adapted to arid environments and thrives in full sun.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
