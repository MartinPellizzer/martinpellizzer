Pilea sublucens

Pilea sublucens, commonly known as the Shiny Pilea, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a glossy sheen that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Pilea sublucens is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
