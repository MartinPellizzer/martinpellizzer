Pulmonaria azurea

Pulmonaria azurea, commonly known as blue lungwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features striking blue and purple flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, hairy textures, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, woodland environments and prefers partial to full shade.

Pulmonaria azurea is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
