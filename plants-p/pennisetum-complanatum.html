Pennisetum complanatum

Pennisetum complanatum, commonly known as blue oat grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue-green foliage and delicate, oat-like seed heads that appear in late summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 feet and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is drought-tolerant and prefers well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Pennisetum complanatum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
