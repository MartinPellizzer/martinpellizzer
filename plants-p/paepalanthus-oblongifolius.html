Paepalanthus oblongifolius

Paepalanthus oblongifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leafed puya, is a striking plant native to the Andes region of South America.

It is a member of the Xanthorrhoeaceae family and is characterized by its tall, spire-like inflorescence composed of numerous brightly colored bracts.
