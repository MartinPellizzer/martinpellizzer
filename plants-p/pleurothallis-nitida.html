Pleurothallis nitida

Pleurothallis nitida, commonly known as the silver orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features slender, wiry stems and produces clusters of small, white or pale green flowers. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, adapted for efficient water absorption.

Pleurothallis nitida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
