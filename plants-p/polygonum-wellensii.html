Polygonum wellensii

Polygonum wellensii, commonly known as the wellensii knotweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants commonly referred to as buckwheats and knotweeds. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit, with slender, branching stems that often produce clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a distinctive ribbed appearance.

Polygonum wellensii is often found in moist, sandy soils along riverbanks and in open woodland areas.
