Paepalanthus giuliettiae

Paepalanthus giuliettiae, commonly known as the Giuliettia pincushion.

It is a flowering plant native to the Andean region of South America, specifically in Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and showy, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
