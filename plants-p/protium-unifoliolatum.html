Protium unifoliolatum

Protium unifoliolatum, commonly known as the single leaf sycamore, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by woody, egg-shaped fruits. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood and is also used in traditional medicine.
