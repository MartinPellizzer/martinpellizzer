Phoebe tenuirhachis

Phoebe tenuirhachis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf phoebe, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale green, followed by small, round berries. It is native to tropical regions of Central America and parts of the Caribbean.

Phoebe tenuirhachis thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.
