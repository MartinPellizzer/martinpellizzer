Potentilla Chrysantha Var. Thuringiaca

Potentilla chrysantha var. thuringiaca is a lesser-known yet striking member of the rose family known for its vibrant yellow flowers. Native to central Europe, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun conditions. Its compact growth habit makes it an ideal choice for rock gardens or border plantings. The variety 'thuringiaca' is particularly noted for its robustness and ability to withstand harsh weather. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated in modern horticulture.
