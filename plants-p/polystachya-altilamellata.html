Polystachya altilamellata

Polystachya altilamellata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lanceolate, dark green leaves. The plant produces panicles of small, white to pale yellow flowers with prominent, elongated sepals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Polystachya altilamellata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
