Paraserianthes lophantha var. kostermansii

Paraserianthes lophantha var. kostermansii, commonly known as the Koster's serianthes, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, which enhances soil fertility. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in lowland forests and disturbed areas.

Its wood is valued for its durability and is used in construction and furniture-making.
