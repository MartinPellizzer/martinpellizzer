Plectranthus pachythyrsus

Plectranthus pachythyrsus, commonly known as the woolly plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, fuzzy leaves that give it a distinctive, soft appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale purple to blue in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Plectranthus pachythyrsus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and ease of care.
