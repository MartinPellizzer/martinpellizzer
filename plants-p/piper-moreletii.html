Piper moreletii

Piper moreletii, commonly known as the Morelet's pepper or the Mexican chili pepper, is a tropical shrub native to Central America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature. These berries are used in traditional medicine and as a spice, valued for their pungent flavor.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to humid, warm climates with well-drained soil.
