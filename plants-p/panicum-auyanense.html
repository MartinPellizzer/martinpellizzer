Panicum auyanense

Panicum auyanense, commonly known as the Auyanense Panicgrass, is a species of grass native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in dry, open habitats and is often found in disturbed soils.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its native range.
