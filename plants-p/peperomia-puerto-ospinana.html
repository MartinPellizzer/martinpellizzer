Peperomia puerto-ospinana

Peperomia puerto-ospinana, commonly known as the Ospina's Peperomia, is a species native to South America, specifically Colombia.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Peperomia puerto-ospinana is relatively low-maintenance and suitable for indoor cultivation.
