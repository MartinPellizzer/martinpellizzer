Plectranthus rariflorus

Plectranthus rariflorus, commonly known as the rareflower plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Plectranthus rariflorus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can be used in rock gardens or as a container plant.
