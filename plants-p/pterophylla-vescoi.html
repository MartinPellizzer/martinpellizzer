Pterophylla vescoi

Pterophylla vescoi, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland or swampy areas.

Pterophylla vescoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
