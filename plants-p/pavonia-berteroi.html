Pavonia berteroi

Pavonia berteroi, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features showy, violet to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

Pavonia berteroi is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
