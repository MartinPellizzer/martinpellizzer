Peperomia hydrocotyloides

Peperomia hydrocotyloides, commonly known as the water peperomia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia hydrocotyloides is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
