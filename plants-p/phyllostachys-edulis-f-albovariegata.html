Phyllostachys edulis f. albovariegata

Phyllostachys edulis f. albovariegata, commonly known as the white-variegated bamboo, is a cultivar of the bamboo species Phyllostachys edulis.

It is characterized by its striking white and green variegated leaves, which provide a distinctive visual appeal. This bamboo is known for its upright growth habit and can reach heights of up to 10 meters under optimal conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

The plant is also valued for its ability to provide a dense, lush foliage that can be used in landscape design.
