Primula qiupuensis

Primula qiupuensis, commonly known as the QiuPu primrose, is a perennial flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are usually broad and heart-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Primula qiupuensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
