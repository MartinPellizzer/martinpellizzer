Populus yatungensis var. crenata

Populus yatungensis var. crenata, commonly known as the Yatungensis Poplar with wavy leaves, is a variant of the Populus yatungensis species native to China.

It is characterized by its distinctive serrated, wavy-edged leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant is a deciduous tree that typically grows in temperate climates, often found in forested areas. Its botanical features include heart-shaped leaves and a fast-growing habit, making it a notable species in ecological studies.

It is also valued for its ornamental qualities and potential uses in reforestation projects.
