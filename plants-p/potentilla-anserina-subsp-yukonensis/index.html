Potentilla Anserina Subsp. Yukonensis

Yukon buttonbush, scientifically known as Potentilla anserina subsp. yukonensis, is a hardy shrub native to the Yukon region of Canada. This plant is recognized for its striking white flowers that bloom in late summer, adding a unique visual appeal to its natural habitat. It thrives in wetland environments and is often found near water sources such as rivers and lakes. The Yukon buttonbush is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing food and shelter for various wildlife species. Its resilience and adaptability make it a notable plant in the study of northern flora.
