Portulaca boliviensis

Portulaca boliviensis, commonly known as the Bolivian moss rose, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a low-growing annual herb that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom throughout the dry season. Its fleshy, succulent leaves help it store water, enabling it to survive in drought conditions.

Portulaca boliviensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and resilience.
