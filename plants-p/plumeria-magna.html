Plumeria magna

Plumeria magna, commonly known as the giant plumeria, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, waxy flowers that come in shades of white, yellow, and pink. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its bark is smooth and pale, and it produces ornamental, fleshy fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Plumeria magna is widely cultivated for its beauty and is often used in floral arrangements and traditional ceremonies.
