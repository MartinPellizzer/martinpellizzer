Prionotrichon flexuosum

Prionotrichon flexuosum, commonly known as the flexible feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, flexible stems and feathery, delicate inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as fields and roadsides. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Prionotrichon flexuosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
