Poa imperialis

Poa imperialis, commonly known as the imperial grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, leafy blades that often have a bluish-green hue. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences, which contain both male and female flowers. It is native to the high altitudes of the Andes in South America, where it thrives in cool, moist environments.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, Poa imperialis is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
