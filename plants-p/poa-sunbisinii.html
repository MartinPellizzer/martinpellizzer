Poa sunbisinii

Poa sunbisinii, commonly known as the sunbisinii grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to specific regions in Europe, particularly in the mountainous areas of the Alps and Carpathians. This plant is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and fine, needle-like leaves. It typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a common sight in alpine meadows.

Poa sunbisinii is often noted for its ecological importance in supporting local biodiversity and its role in stabilizing soil in rocky or steep terrain.
