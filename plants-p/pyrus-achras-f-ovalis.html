Pyrus achras f. ovalis

Pyrus achras f. ovalis, commonly known as the oval-leaved pear.

This cultivar is a variant of the African pear, characterized by its elliptical leaves and smaller, less showy flowers compared to the typical species. It is native to southern Africa and thrives in subtropical climates. The fruit is edible, with a mild, sweet flavor, and is often used in local cuisines.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
