Phlomis × composita var. malacitana

Phlomis × composita var. malacitana, commonly known as the Malacitana Globe Thistle, is a perennial plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its rosette of silver-gray, woolly leaves and produces striking spherical blue or purple flower heads. The plant is typically found in rocky or limestone soils, thriving in full sun. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
