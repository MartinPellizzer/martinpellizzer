Piper pelliticaule

Piper pelliticaule, commonly known as the slender pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and clusters of tiny, white flowers. It produces small, peppery fruits that are used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.

Its botanical features make it an important species for both ecological and medicinal research.
