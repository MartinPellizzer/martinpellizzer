Polianthes alboaustralis

Polianthes alboaustralis, commonly known as the white-flowered white yarrow, is a perennial plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, white to pale yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, gray-green leaves that are aromatic and slightly hairy.

Polianthes alboaustralis is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in landscaping.
