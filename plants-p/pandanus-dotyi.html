Pandanus dotyi

Pandanus dotyi, commonly known as the Solomon Islands pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is a member of the Pandanaceae family and is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically clustered in branched inflorescences. Its roots and leaves are utilized in local cultures for various purposes, including food and medicine.

The plant thrives in coastal and wetland environments, often growing near mangroves or in sandy soils.
