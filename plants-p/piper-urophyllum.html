Piper urophyllum

Piper urophyllum, commonly known as the yellow pepper vine, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and northern South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, round berries that turn from green to yellow as they mature.

The plant is often grown as an ornamental due to its vibrant foliage and unique fruit.
