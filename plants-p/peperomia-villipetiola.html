Peperomia villipetiola

Peperomia villipetiola, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to avoid root rot.

Peperomia villipetiola is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and low maintenance requirements.
