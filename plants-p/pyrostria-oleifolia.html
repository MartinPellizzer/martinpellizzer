Pyrostria oleifolia

Pyrostria oleifolia, commonly known as the oil fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, feathery fronds that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers and relies on wind pollination. Its rhizomes contain a high concentration of oils, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Pyrostria oleifolia thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
