Piletophyllum emodi

Piletophyllum emodi, commonly known as the Himalayan snowbell, is a rare flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a slender, upright stem with simple, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.
