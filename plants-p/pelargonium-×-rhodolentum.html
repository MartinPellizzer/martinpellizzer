Pelargonium × rhodolentum

Pelargonium × rhodolentum, commonly known as the coral geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that range from red to pink and orange, often with a distinctive coral-like appearance. The plant typically features velvety, dark green leaves with a slightly serrated edge. It is a compact shrub that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Pelargonium × rhodolentum is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
