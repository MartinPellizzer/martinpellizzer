Peperomia enantiostachya var. distachya

Peperomia enantiostachya var. distachya, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a distinctive silver or grayish underside, giving it a unique visual appeal. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters, though it is primarily grown for its foliage rather than its blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate watering.

Its compact size and ornamental leaves make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
