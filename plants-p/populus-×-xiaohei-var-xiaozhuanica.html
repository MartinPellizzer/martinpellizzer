Populus × xiaohei var. xiaozhuanica

Populus × xiaohei var. xiaozhuanica, commonly known as the Xiaozhuan poplar, is a hybrid deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Salicaceae family and is characterized by its slender, often multi-stemmed growth habit. The leaves are simple, alternate, and typically have a serrated margin, turning yellow in the fall. It produces small, catkin-like flowers in the spring, followed by narrow, elongated fruits.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
