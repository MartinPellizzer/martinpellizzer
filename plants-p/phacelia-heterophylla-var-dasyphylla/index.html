Phacelia Heterophylla Var. Dasyphylla

Bluecurls, scientifically known as Phacelia heterophylla var. dasyphylla, is a striking flowering plant native to the western United States. Its most distinctive feature is its vibrant blue or purple flower clusters, which resemble curling tendrils. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping and native gardens. With its unique appearance and ecological benefits, Bluecurls plays an important role in supporting pollinators and enhancing biodiversity. Its adaptability and beauty make it a valuable addition to both natural and cultivated landscapes.
