Piper tikalense

Piper tikalense, commonly known as the Maya pepper or Tikal pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central America, particularly Belize, Guatemala, and northern Honduras. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and elongated, thin fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly aromatic scent.

Piper tikalense is valued for its potential medicinal properties and is sometimes cultivated for its unique flavor in local cuisines.
