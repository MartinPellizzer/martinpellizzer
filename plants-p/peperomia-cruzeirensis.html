Peperomia cruzeirensis

Peperomia cruzeirensis, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that range in color from deep green to reddish purple. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia cruzeirensis is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
