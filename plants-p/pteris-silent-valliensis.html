Pteris silent-valliensis

Pteris silent-valliensis, commonly known as the silent vallien fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Pteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically bright green with a subtle sheen. The plant produces sporangia on the underside of its fronds, which are clustered in sori, a feature common among ferns. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
