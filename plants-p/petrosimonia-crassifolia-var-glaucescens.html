Petrosimonia crassifolia var. glaucescens

Petrosimonia crassifolia var. glaucescens, commonly known as the blue-green petrosimonia, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of Central Asia.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, providing a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding to its ornamental value. It is well adapted to dry climates and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
