Plumeria tenorei

Plumeria tenorei, commonly known as the Madagascar plumeria, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are typically pollinated by bees and other insects.

The bark is smooth and pale, and the plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in warm climates.
