Possira grandiflora

Possira grandiflora, commonly known as the giant flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, red-orange flowers that can reach up to 10 inches in diameter, making it a dramatic addition to any garden. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in its humid, shady habitat. It thrives in partial to full shade and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its impressive appearance, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in warm climates.
