Piper otto-huberi var. otto-huberi

Piper otto-huberi var. otto-huberi, commonly known as the Otto-Huber's pepper plant, is a tropical vine belonging to the Piperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often a deep green. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by tiny, peppercorn-like fruits that turn red when mature. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
