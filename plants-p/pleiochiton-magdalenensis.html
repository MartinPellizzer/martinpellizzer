Pleiochiton magdalenensis

Pleiochiton magdalenensis, commonly known as the Magdalena fern, is a species of tree fern native to Colombia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizome system is robust, allowing it to anchor firmly in moist soil.
