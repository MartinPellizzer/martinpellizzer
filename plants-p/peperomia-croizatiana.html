Peperomia croizatiana

Peperomia croizatiana, commonly known as the Croizat's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia croizatiana is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
