Peperomia bracteiflora var. stigmatifera

Peperomia bracteiflora var. stigmatifera, commonly known as the spotted peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are often marked with distinctive red or purple spots, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Due to its ornamental foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and bonsai cultivation.
