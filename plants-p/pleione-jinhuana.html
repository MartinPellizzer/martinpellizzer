Pleione jinhuana

Pleione jinhuana, commonly known as the Jinhuang Orchid, is a rare and striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to China.

It is characterized by its elegant, lance-shaped leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant thrives in cool, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions with high humidity. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive yellow throat, and it is highly valued for its ornamental beauty.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, Pleione jinhuana is considered an endangered species.
