Philodendron hastifolium var. inops

Philodendron hastifolium var. inops, commonly known as the "Cobra Plant," is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that often have a deep green color with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Philodendron hastifolium var. inops is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
