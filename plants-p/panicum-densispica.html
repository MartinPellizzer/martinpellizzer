Panicum densispica

Panicum densispica, commonly known as stiff panicgrass, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, fluffy seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in open woodlands and disturbed areas.

Panicum densispica is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local wildlife.
