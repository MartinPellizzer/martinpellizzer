Pyrus mucronata

Pyrus mucronata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pear.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are white or pale yellow, clustered in inflorescences, and appear in early spring.

The fruit is a small, round pear-like berry that ripens in late summer.
