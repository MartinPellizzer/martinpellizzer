Peperomia zarzalana

Peperomia zarzalana, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically silver or gray-green in color, with a waxy coating that helps it retain moisture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white, which appear in clusters at the base of the leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia zarzalana is relatively easy to care for and is appreciated for its attractive foliage and air-purifying qualities.
