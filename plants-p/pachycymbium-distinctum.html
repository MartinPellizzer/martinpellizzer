Pachycymbium distinctum

Pachycymbium distinctum, commonly known as the slender dwarf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant features a short, thick rhizome and produces pseudobulbs that support its foliage. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is often sought after by orchid enthusiasts.
