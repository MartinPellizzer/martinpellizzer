Peperomia quatrometralis

Peperomia quatrometralis, commonly known as the four-ribbed peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features broad, waxy, dark green leaves with prominent veins that give it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and minimal maintenance requirements, it is well-suited for beginners and space-constrained environments.
