Potentilla argentea var. johanniniana

Potentilla argentea var. johanniniana, commonly known as silver potentilla, is a low-growing shrub characterized by its silvery-white undersides on its leaves.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is native to regions of Europe and western Asia. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal maintenance once established.
