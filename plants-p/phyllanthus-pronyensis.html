Phyllanthus pronyensis

Phyllanthus pronyensis, commonly known as the Proneys' spurge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. Due to its adaptability, it is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain areas.
