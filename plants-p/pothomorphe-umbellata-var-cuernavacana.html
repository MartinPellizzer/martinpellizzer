Pothomorphe umbellata var. cuernavacana

Pothomorphe umbellata var. cuernavacana, commonly known as the Cuernavaca bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
