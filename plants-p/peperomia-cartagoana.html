Peperomia cartagoana

Peperomia cartagoana, commonly known as the Cartagoan peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to Central America.

It features oval, glossy, dark green leaves with a slightly waxy texture and a prominent central vein. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia cartagoana is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and moderate humidity to flourish.
