Pittosporum eriocarpum

Pittosporum eriocarpum, commonly known as the woolly pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by bright orange berries. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

Pittosporum eriocarpum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is used in landscaping and conservation efforts.
