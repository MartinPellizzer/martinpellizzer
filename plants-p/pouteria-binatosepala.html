Pouteria binatosepala

Pouteria binatosepala, commonly known as the two-sepaled spondias, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible, yellow fruits with a sweet, custard-like pulp.
