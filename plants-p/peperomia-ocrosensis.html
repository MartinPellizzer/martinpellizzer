Peperomia ocrosensis

Peperomia ocrosensis, commonly known as the Ocros peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It is a compact, low-growing plant with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually borne in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia ocrosensis is valued for its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
