Preslianthus detonsus

Preslianthus detonsus, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant typically grows in shrubland and grassland habitats, often in well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds.

Preslianthus detonsus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
