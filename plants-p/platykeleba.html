Platykeleba

Platykeleba, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a unique plant with striking botanical features.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its lance-shaped, silver-green leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale blue, often with a subtle purple hue. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light for optimal growth.

Platykeleba is native to tropical and subtropical regions, where it often grows in shaded, humid environments.
