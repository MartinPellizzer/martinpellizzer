Pulsatilla tongkangensis f. rosa

Pulsatilla tongkangensis f. rosa, commonly known as the Tongkang rose pasqueflower, is a rare and unique botanical variant native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

This fern-like plant is noted for its ornamental value and is of interest to botanists due to its limited distribution and unique floral characteristics.
