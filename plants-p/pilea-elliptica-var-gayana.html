Pilea elliptica var. gayana

Pilea elliptica var. gayana, commonly known as the Chinese money plant or Thai jade plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring or summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ability to propagate through leaf cuttings adds to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
