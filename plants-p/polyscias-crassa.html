Polyscias crassa

Polyscias crassa, commonly known as the broad-leafed goosefoot, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped or ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used in traditional medicine.

Its foliage is valued for ornamental purposes in landscaping and indoor gardening.
