Pilea senarifolia

Pilea senarifolia, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features round, glossy leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Pilea senarifolia is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.
