Poincianella acapulcensis

Poincianella acapulcensis, commonly known as the Acapulco flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that appear in clusters, giving it a vibrant and showy appearance. The plant has pinnate leaves that are often used in traditional medicine by local communities. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its rapid growth rate makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.
