Peperomia stehleana var. tardigranulata

Peperomia stehleana var. tardigranulata, commonly known as the Snowy Peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features dense, velvety foliage with a pale green to almost white coloration, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardens and terrariums.
