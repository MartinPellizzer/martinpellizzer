Pachira nitida

Pachira nitida, commonly known as the slender pachira, is a species of flowering plant in the Araceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers that are typically followed by bright red berries. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.

Pachira nitida prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.
