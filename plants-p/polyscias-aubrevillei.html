Polyscias aubrevillei

Polyscias aubrevillei, commonly known as the African nettle, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Polyscias aubrevillei thrives in moist, shaded environments and is relatively easy to cultivate.
