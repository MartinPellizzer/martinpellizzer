Pennisetum leekei

Pennisetum leekei, commonly known as the silver grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and silvery-blue foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind, aiding in its propagation. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping and ornamental gardens.

Pennisetum leekei is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract birds and insects.
