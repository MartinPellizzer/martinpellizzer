Physalis megistocarpa

Physalis megistocarpa, commonly known as the giant husk tomato, is a flowering plant in the nightshade family.

It is characterized by its large, yellow-orange berries enclosed in a papery calyx. The plant is native to South America and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. Its leaves are ovate with hairy surfaces, and the stems are often hairy as well.

The fruit is edible when fully ripe and is used in various culinary applications.
