Peperomia tenuispica

Peperomia tenuispica, commonly known as the narrow-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features upright, branching stems covered in dense, velvety foliage with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically white or pale green, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and ability to thrive in well-draining potting mix.

Peperomia tenuispica is valued for its attractive appearance and ease of care, making it a popular choice for collectors and indoor gardeners alike.
