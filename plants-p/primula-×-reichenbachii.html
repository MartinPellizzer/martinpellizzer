Primula × reichenbachii

Primula × reichenbachii, commonly known as the Chinese primrose, is a hybrid species native to China and neighboring regions.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically blooms in early spring, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking early-season color. It prefers cool, moist conditions and well-drained soil, often thriving in partial shade.

Its ornamental value and ease of cultivation have made it a favored plant in both garden beds and container gardening.
