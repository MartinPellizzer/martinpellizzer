Pyrenacantha ruspolii

Pyrenacantha ruspolii, commonly known as the red-flowered pyrenacantha, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, tubular red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically orange or red in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Pyrenacantha ruspolii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
