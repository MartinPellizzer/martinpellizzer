Pandanus christophersenii

Pandanus christophersenii, commonly known as the Christensen's pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pandanaceae.

It is native to the Pacific Islands, including parts of Micronesia and the Marshall Islands. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and long, sword-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of many pandanus species.

The plant thrives in coastal and sandy soils, often growing near the shoreline in tropical climates.
