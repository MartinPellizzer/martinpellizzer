Pelargonium luteolum

Pelargonium luteolum, commonly known as the yellow geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and succulent, fleshy leaves. The plant typically grows in a compact, bushy form, with upright stems that bear clusters of small, five-petaled blooms. Its leaves are usually rounded or heart-shaped and may have a slightly hairy texture.

Pelargonium luteolum is well-suited for gardens and containers, thriving in full sun and well-draining soil.
