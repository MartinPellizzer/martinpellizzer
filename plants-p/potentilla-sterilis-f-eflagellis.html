Potentilla sterilis f. eflagellis

Potentilla sterilis f. eflagellis, commonly known as the slender potentilla, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its slender, often prostrate stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically produces yellow or cream-colored flowers that bloom in early summer. It is native to arctic and alpine regions of Europe and Asia, thriving in cold, rocky environments.

Its botanical features make it well-adapted to harsh climates, with a compact growth habit and hairy foliage.
