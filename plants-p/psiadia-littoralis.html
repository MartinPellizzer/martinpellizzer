Psiadia littoralis

Psiadia littoralis, commonly known as the coastal daisy, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in sandy or rocky coastal environments, demonstrating strong adaptability to salt spray and wind. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Psiadia littoralis is often used in coastal landscaping due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
