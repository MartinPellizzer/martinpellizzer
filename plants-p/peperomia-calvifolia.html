Peperomia calvifolia

Peperomia calvifolia, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, velvety foliage with deep green, ovate leaves that have a slight waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically greenish-white in color, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia calvifolia is known for its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
