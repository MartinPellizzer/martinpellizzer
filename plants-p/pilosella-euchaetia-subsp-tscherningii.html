Pilosella euchaetia subsp. tscherningii

Pilosella euchaetia subsp.

tscherningii, commonly known as the alpine mouse-ear, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its low-growing habit and dense, woolly foliage.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in rocky and gravelly soils, often found in mountainous environments.
