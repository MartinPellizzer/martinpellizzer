Pleione hubeiensis

Pleione hubeiensis, commonly known as the Hubei pleione, is a rare and elegant orchid native to the mountainous regions of Hubei Province in China.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically blooms in late spring, with petals that range from white to pale pink, often featuring intricate markings. It thrives in cool, shaded environments with high humidity, making it a challenging but rewarding species for orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered and requires careful conservation efforts.
