Panicum moomomiense

Panicum moomomiense, commonly known as the Moomomoi grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central America, particularly Mexico and Guatemala. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers.

Panicum moomomiense is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
