Peltopus origami

Peltopus origami, commonly known as the folded leaf plant, is a unique species characterized by its intricate, folded leaves that resemble origami paper.

Native to the humid rainforests of Southeast Asia, it thrives in shaded, moist environments with high humidity. The plant's leaves are typically a deep green, with subtle variegation that enhances its ornamental appeal. It produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a delicate touch to its appearance.

Due to its slow growth and specific care requirements, it is a sought-after plant among collectors and enthusiasts.
