Prunus jamasakura f. reflexa

Prunus jamasakura f. reflexa, commonly known as the Japanese cherry blossom variant with reflexed petals, is a cultivar of the Prunus jamasakura species.

It is characterized by its distinctive, backward-curving petals that give it a unique appearance compared to typical cherry blossoms. This plant is native to Japan and is often cultivated for its ornamental value during the spring blooming season. The flowers are pale pink to white, and the tree has a compact, rounded form.

It is relatively hardy and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.
