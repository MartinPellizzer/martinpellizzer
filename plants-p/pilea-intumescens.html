Pilea intumescens

Pilea intumescens, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its round, fleshy leaves that resemble small coins, which give it its common name. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color.

Pilea intumescens is a popular indoor plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
