Pilosella tabergensis

Pilosella tabergensis, commonly known as the Alpine mouse-ear, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble single flowers. The plant features small, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in rocky and open habitats, often found at high altitudes.
