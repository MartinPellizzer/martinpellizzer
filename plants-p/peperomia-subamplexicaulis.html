Peperomia subamplexicaulis

Peperomia subamplexicaulis, commonly known as the broad-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are broad at the base and taper toward the tip, often with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters, typically during the summer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and drought tolerance.

Its unique leaf shape and easy care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
