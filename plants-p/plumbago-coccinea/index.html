Plumbago Coccinea

Plumbago coccinea, also known as the coral trumpetvine, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas. It is renowned for its vibrant pink to red tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual impact. This plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. Its unique appearance and long-lasting blooms make it a favorite among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it can be invasive in some areas, requiring careful management to prevent overgrowth.
