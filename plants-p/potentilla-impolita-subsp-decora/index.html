Potentilla Impolita Subsp. Decora

Potentilla impolita subsp. decora is a striking shrub native to the mountainous regions of western North America. Known for its vibrant yellow flowers and delicate foliage, it adds a touch of wild beauty to its natural habitat. This subspecies is often found in rocky soils and open meadows, thriving in harsh alpine conditions. Its unique appearance makes it a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike. Despite its ornamental value, it remains relatively obscure in the broader horticultural world.
