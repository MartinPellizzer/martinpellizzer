Pinalia micholitziana

Pinalia micholitziana, commonly known as the Micholitz's pincushion, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth habit. The plant produces small, tubular yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
