Pyrrocoma brachycephala

Pyrrocoma brachycephala, commonly known as the narrow-headed pyrrocoma, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
