Premna yunnanensis

Premna yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan premna, is a flowering plant native to China, particularly Yunnan Province.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale purple. It is often found in moist, shaded areas of forests and is valued for its ornamental qualities.

The species is also used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
