Pleurothallis cutucuensis

Pleurothallis cutucuensis, commonly known as the Cutucu orchid, is a species native to the Amazon rainforest of South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features slender, upright pseudobulbs and produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a faint fragrance. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Pleurothallis cutucuensis is part of the vast Pleurothallis genus, which includes over 1,000 species of orchids.
