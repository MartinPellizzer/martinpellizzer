Pappophorum robustum

Pappophorum robustum, commonly known as the robust pappophore, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, sturdy stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-dispersed seeds covered in silky hairs. It is typically found in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to thrive in environments with moderate to low moisture levels.
