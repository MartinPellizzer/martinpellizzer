Peperomia binispica

Peperomia binispica, commonly known as the two-tailed peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elongated and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia binispica is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
