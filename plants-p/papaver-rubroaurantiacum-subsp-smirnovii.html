Papaver rubroaurantiacum subsp. smirnovii

Papaver rubroaurantiacum subsp.

smirnovii, commonly known as the Smirnov's poppy, is a rare and striking member of the Papaveraceae family. It is characterized by its vibrant red to orange-red petals that bloom in late spring to early summer.
