Peperomia columnaris

Peperomia columnaris, commonly known as the upright peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features upright, cane-like stems that are often covered in a waxy, light green to grayish-blue sheen. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or white, which are typically hidden beneath its foliage. Its leaves are usually oval or elliptical, with a smooth texture and a glossy surface.

Peperomia columnaris thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it an ideal choice for indoor gardens.
