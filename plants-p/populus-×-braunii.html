Populus × braunii

Populus × braunii, commonly known as the Chinese poplar, is a hybrid species within the Populus genus.

It is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in catkins, which are a common feature among poplar species.

This tree is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in reforestation efforts due to its hardiness and adaptability.
