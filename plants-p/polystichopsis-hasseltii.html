Polystichopsis hasseltii

Polystichopsis hasseltii, commonly known as the silver fern, is a species of fern native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, silver-hued fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in forest understories. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Polystichopsis hasseltii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable humidity and light conditions.
