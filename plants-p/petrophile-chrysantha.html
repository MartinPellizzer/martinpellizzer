Petrophile chrysantha

Petrophile chrysantha, commonly known as the yellow mistflower, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds.

Petrophile chrysantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local wildlife.
