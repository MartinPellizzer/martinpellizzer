Piper scortechinii

Piper scortechinii, commonly known as the Brazilian pepper tree, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. It is an evergreen shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall.

Due to its invasive nature in some regions, it is sometimes considered a weed.
