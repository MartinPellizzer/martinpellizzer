Phillyrea mucronata var. lanceolata

Phillyrea mucronata var. lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf Phillyrea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Phillyrea mucronata var. lanceolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in mild climates.
