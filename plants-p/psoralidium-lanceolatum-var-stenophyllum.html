Psoralidium lanceolatum var. stenophyllum

Psoralidium lanceolatum var. stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved psoralidium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of southern Africa.

It features linear, grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and have a bluish-green hue. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually greenish-yellow in color, which bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or rocky outcrops.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
