Peperomia griggsii

Peperomia griggsii, commonly known as the Griggs' peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly textured surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia griggsii is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.
