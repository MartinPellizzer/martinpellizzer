Premna puberula var. puberula

Premna puberula var. puberula, commonly known as silky sarsapilla, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves that give it a silken texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in spikes. Its fruits are capsule-like structures containing numerous tiny seeds.

It is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forests and along stream banks.
