Panicum myriostachyum

Panicum myriostachyum, commonly known as the Chinese silver grass, is a perennial grass species native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, fluffy seed heads that resemble clouds, giving it a striking appearance. The plant typically grows in open areas, such as fields and roadsides, and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a silvery-blue hue that adds to its ornamental value.

Panicum myriostachyum is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
