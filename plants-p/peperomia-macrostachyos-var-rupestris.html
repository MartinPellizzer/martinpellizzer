Peperomia macrostachyos var. rupestris

Peperomia macrostachyos var. rupestris, commonly known as the rock peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically gray-green with a distinctive velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
