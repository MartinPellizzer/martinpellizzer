Petrosavia stellaris

Petrosavia stellaris, commonly known as the star petrosavia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its star-shaped, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, adapted to reduce water loss.

Petrosavia stellaris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
