Peperomia franciscoi

Peperomia franciscoi, commonly known as the Francisco's Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to South America.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, waxy leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purple tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia franciscoi is appreciated for its ornamental foliage and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
