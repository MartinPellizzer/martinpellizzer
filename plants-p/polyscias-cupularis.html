Polyscias cupularis

Polyscias cupularis, commonly known as the umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like canopy of dark green, leathery leaves that provide shade and a lush appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by yellowish fruits containing three seeds. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight.

Polyscias cupularis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor environments.
