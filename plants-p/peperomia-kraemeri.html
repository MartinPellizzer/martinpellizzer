Peperomia kraemeri

Peperomia kraemeri, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings, giving it a striking visual appeal. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its ability to thrive in low light conditions and its tolerance for dry air.

Peperomia kraemeri is also valued for its compact growth habit and minimal maintenance requirements.
