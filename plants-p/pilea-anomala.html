Pilea anomala

Pilea anomala, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its rosette of thick, waxy, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically hidden beneath the leaves. It is a popular indoor plant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.

Pilea anomala thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
