Prosthechea hastata

Prosthechea hastata, commonly known as the spurred orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with distinctive spurred petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in moist, organic soil.

Prosthechea hastata is notable for its unique floral structure and ecological role in the rainforest ecosystem.
