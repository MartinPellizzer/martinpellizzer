Pediomelum cyphocalyx

Pediomelum cyphocalyx, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rock rose, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Pediomelum cyphocalyx is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
