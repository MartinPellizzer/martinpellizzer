Premna capitata

Premna capitata, commonly known as the clusterleaf or white-flowered premna, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and opposite, ovate leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers in clusters, which are borne in the axils of the leaves. Its fruit is a small, three-lobed capsule that contains several seeds.

Premna capitata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
