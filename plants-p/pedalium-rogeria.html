Pedalium rogeria

Pedalium rogeria, commonly known as the African starfruit or African pear, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and simple, alternate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by fleshy, star-shaped fruits that are edible.
