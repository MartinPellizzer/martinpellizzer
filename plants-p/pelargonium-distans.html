Pelargonium distans

Pelargonium distans, commonly known as the spreading geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it produces small, spherical fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Pelargonium distans is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
