Panicum lacustre

Panicum lacustre, commonly known as water panicgrass, is a species of grass native to wetland environments.

It typically grows in marshy areas and along the edges of lakes and ponds. The plant has slender, cylindrical stems that are often slightly hairy, and its leaves are narrow with a bluish-green color. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers.

This plant plays an important role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various insects and birds.
