Petrea brevicalyx

Petrea brevicalyx, commonly known as the broad-leaved pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, often followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Petrea brevicalyx is also valued for its ability to tolerate partial shade and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
