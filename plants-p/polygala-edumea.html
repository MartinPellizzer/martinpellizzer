Polygala edumea

Polygala edumea, commonly known as the Edumea milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and often hairy, while its stems are erect and branched.

The flowers are arranged in racemes and are notable for their spurred petals, which are a distinctive feature of the genus Polygala.
