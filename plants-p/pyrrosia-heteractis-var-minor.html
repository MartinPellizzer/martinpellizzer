Pyrrosia heteractis var. minor

Pyrrosia heteractis var. minor, commonly known as the lesser rock fern, is a small, epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on rocks or tree trunks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has delicate, pinnately divided fronds with a bluish-green coloration. Its sporangia are clustered in sori on the underside of the leaves.

This variety is notable for its compact size and unique leaf morphology.
