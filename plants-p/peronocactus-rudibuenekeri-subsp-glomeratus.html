Peronocactus rudibuenekeri subsp. glomeratus

Peronocactus rudibuenekeri subsp.

glomeratus, commonly known as the clustered Peronocactus, is a small, globular cactus native to Argentina. It is characterized by its dense, spiny stems that form a compact, rounded shape.

The plant produces yellow flowers in late spring, which are clustered at the stem tips. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
