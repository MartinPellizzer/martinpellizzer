Potentilla versicolor var. versicolor

Potentilla versicolor var. versicolor, commonly known as the variable potentilla, is a flowering plant in the rose family.

It is characterized by its showy, colorful flowers that typically range from yellow to red, depending on the variety and environmental conditions. The plant has slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ability to produce multiple blooms throughout the growing season adds ornamental value to gardens.
