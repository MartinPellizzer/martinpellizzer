Pentaspadon officinalis

Pentaspadon officinalis, commonly known as the medicinal fern, is a member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its rhizomes contain various bioactive compounds that have been studied for their potential health benefits.

The botanical features of Pentaspadon officinalis make it an important species in both ecological and medicinal contexts.
