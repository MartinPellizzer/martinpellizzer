Peperomia maransara

Peperomia maransara, commonly known as the Maransara Peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically oval or heart-shaped, with some varieties displaying variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white, typical of the Peperomia genus. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental foliage, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
