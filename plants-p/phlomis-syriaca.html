Phlomis syriaca

Phlomis syriaca, commonly known as Syrian blazingstar, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of bright yellow flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Phlomis syriaca is valued for its ornamental beauty and its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
