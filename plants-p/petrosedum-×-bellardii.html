Petrosedum × bellardii

Petrosedum × bellardii, commonly known as the hybrid stonecrop, is a low-growing succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features fleshy, overlapping leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color, adapted to store water. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Petrosedum × bellardii is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
