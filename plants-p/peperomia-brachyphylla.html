Peperomia brachyphylla

Peperomia brachyphylla, commonly known as the compact peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, with a glossy green surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green or red, which are borne on short spikes. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and drought tolerance.

Peperomia brachyphylla is often grown as a decorative plant for its attractive foliage and ease of care.
