Polystachya galeata var. babilonii

Polystachya galeata var. babilonii, commonly known as the Babylonian sword fern, is a variant of the Polystachya galeata species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial fern characterized by its sword-shaped, evergreen fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers on slender, erect spikes, which are a distinguishing feature. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and shaded landscapes.
