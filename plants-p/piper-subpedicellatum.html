Piper subpedicellatum

Piper subpedicellatum, commonly known as the slender pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. It produces tiny greenish-white flowers that are followed by small, round berries.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage and unique appearance.
