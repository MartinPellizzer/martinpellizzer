Proustia pyrifolia f. glandulosa

Proustia pyrifolia f. glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular flameleaf, is a distinctive plant characterized by its striking, flame-like foliage.

The leaves are typically lance-shaped and exhibit a vibrant red or orange hue, which intensifies with age. This variety is notable for its dense, glandular hairs covering the leaf surfaces, which contribute to its unique texture and appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The plant is native to southern Africa and is valued for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to various climates.
