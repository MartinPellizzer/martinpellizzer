Peperomia lancifolia var. grandifolia

Peperomia lancifolia var. grandifolia, commonly known as the large-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are lance-shaped and often have a bluish-green hue with prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or purple, emerging from leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its slow growth and attractive foliage, it is often used as a decorative plant in both home and office settings.
