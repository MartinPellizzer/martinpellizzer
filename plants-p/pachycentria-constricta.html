Pachycentria constricta

Pachycentria constricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow to greenish flowers, which are typically borne in the axils of the leaves.
