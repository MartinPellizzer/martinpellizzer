Pratia rugulosa

Pratia rugulosa, commonly known as the roughleaf pratia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its small, oval leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is often found in open woodlands and heathlands, preferring well-drained soils.

Pratia rugulosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
