Polygonum plebeium var. elegans

Polygonum plebeium var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant knotweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like buckwheat and sorrel. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically alternate, ovate, and have a slightly toothed margin.

It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.
