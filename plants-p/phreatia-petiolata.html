Phreatia petiolata

Phreatia petiolata, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant features a slender, upright stem with clustered leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil, and it often thrives in humid, tropical climates.

Phreatia petiolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
