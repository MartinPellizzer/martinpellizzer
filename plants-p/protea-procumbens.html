Protea procumbens

Protea procumbens, commonly known as the dwarf protea, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing evergreen shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features dense, leathery foliage and produces clusters of showy, pinkish-white flowers. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and used in floral arrangements.
