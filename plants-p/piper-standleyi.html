Piper standleyi

Piper standleyi, commonly known as the Mexican pepper plant, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruit is a small, berry-like structure that turns red when mature.

Piper standleyi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
