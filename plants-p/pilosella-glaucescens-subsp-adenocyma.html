Pilosella glaucescens subsp. adenocyma

Pilosella glaucescens subsp.

adenocyma, commonly known as the glaucous pilosella, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its leaves are hairy and often have a bluish-green hue, while the flowers are usually yellow with a central disk.
