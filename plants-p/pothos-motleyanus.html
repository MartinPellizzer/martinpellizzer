Pothos motleyanus

Pothos motleyanus, commonly known as the Variegated Pothos, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, waxy leaves that display a striking combination of green, white, and yellow variegation. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular houseplant. Its aerial roots allow it to climb and trail, adapting well to various indoor environments.

Pothos motleyanus is also known for its ability to purify the air, enhancing its appeal as a decorative and functional plant.
