Pelexia oestrifera var. angustifolia

Pelexia oestrifera var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf pelexia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal regions and is adapted to arid conditions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is not widely commercially available.
