Peperomia bracteiflora

Peperomia bracteiflora, commonly known as the bracteiflora pepper plant, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light. It is relatively drought-tolerant, making it a low-maintenance houseplant.

Its unique foliage and compact growth habit make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
