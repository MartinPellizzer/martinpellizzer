Peperomia lyallii

Peperomia lyallii, commonly known as Lyall's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to New Zealand.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia lyallii is known for its low maintenance and ability to adapt to a variety of growing conditions.
