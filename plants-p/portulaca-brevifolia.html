Portulaca brevifolia

Portulaca brevifolia, commonly known as the shortleaf purslane, is a low-growing annual plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, succulent leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, exhibiting drought tolerance due to its thick, waxy leaves. Its growth habit is prostrate, allowing it to spread across the ground, and it often forms dense mats.

Portulaca brevifolia is also known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions, making it a resilient species in harsh climates.
