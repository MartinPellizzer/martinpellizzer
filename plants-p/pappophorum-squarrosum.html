Pappophorum squarrosum

Pappophorum squarrosum, commonly known as the squarrosum grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically wind-pollinated. Its flowers are usually inconspicuous, with reduced floral structures typical of grasses.

Pappophorum squarrosum is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
