Pleioblastus molliculus

Pleioblastus molliculus, commonly known as the soft bamboo, is a species of grass native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, hollow stems and fine, feather-like leaves that give it a delicate appearance. This plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its graceful growth habit and ability to thrive in shaded environments. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and is commonly found in woodland areas.

The plant is also valued for its use in traditional Chinese medicine.
