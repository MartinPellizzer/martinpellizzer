Polygonum kinashii

Polygonum kinashii, commonly known as the Kinashii knotweed, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes species known for their distinctive bracts and small flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped with a smooth or slightly toothed margin.

It produces clusters of tiny, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late summer.
