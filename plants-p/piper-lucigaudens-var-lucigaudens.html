Piper lucigaudens var. lucigaudens

Piper lucigaudens var. lucigaudens, commonly known as the Lucigaudens pepper plant, is a tropical climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piper genus in the family Piperaceae, which includes various pepper species. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves and small, clustered flowers that develop into peppery fruits. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value and unique fruiting habit, it is cultivated in some regions for both aesthetic and culinary purposes.
