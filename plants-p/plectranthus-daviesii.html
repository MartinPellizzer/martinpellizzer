Plectranthus daviesii

Plectranthus daviesii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.

Plectranthus daviesii thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
