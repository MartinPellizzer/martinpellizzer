Pennisetum amethystinum

Pennisetum amethystinum, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its striking purple plumes that appear in late summer, adding vibrant color to gardens. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Pennisetum amethystinum is also valued for its ability to attract birds and butterflies to the garden.
