Potentilla × fintelmanni

Potentilla × fintelmanni, commonly known as the shrub cinquefoil, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, low-growing shrub form and produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders. Its foliage is usually dark green, and it is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness.

Potentilla × fintelmanni is also noted for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
