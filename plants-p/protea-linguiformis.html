Protea linguiformis

Protea linguiformis, commonly known as the flat-leaf protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence composed of numerous floral units that resemble petals. The plant typically grows in fynbos vegetation, thriving in well-drained, acidic soils. Its flowers are usually pink to red in color, with a distinctive flat or spoon-shaped appearance.

Protea linguiformis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also important in local ecosystems.
