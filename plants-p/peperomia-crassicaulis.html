Peperomia crassicaulis

Peperomia crassicaulis, commonly known as the broad-leaved peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy, ovate leaves that are often dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically green or red, which appear at the base of the leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia crassicaulis is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
