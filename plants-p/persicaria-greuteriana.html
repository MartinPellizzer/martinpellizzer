Persicaria greuteriana

Persicaria greuteriana, commonly known as the Greuter's knotweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Persicaria greuteriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing mountainous terrain.
