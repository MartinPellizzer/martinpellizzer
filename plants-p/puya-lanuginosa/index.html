Puya Lanuginosa

Puya lanuginosa is a striking plant native to the Andes mountains of South America. It is known for its tall, spiky inflorescence and its ability to thrive in high-altitude environments. This species belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is often found growing in rocky or arid regions. Its unique appearance and resilience make it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively rare and is often difficult to cultivate outside its natural habitat.
