Potentilla bolzanensis var. astelligera

Potentilla bolzanensis var. astelligera, commonly known as the Bolzan's potentilla, is a low-growing shrub native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It features small, dark green leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is often cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
