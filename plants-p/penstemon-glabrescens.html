Penstemon glabrescens

Penstemon glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth beardtongue, is a perennial flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It is well adapted to arid environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Penstemon glabrescens is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
