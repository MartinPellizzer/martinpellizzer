Papaya heterophylla

Papaya heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf papaya, is a species native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

It is a member of the Caricaceae family and is characterized by its alternate, simple, and often variegated leaves. The plant produces yellow-orange fruits that are typically small and have a mild, sweet flavor. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
