Potentilla biflora var. lahulensis

Potentilla biflora var. lahulensis, commonly known as the Lahul Himalayan cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers. The plant typically thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, alpine environments. Its leaves are usually serrated and have a slightly hairy texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
