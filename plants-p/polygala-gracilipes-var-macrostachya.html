Polygala gracilipes var. macrostachya

Polygala gracilipes var. macrostachya, commonly known as the long-stemmed milkwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, pale green to white flowers arranged in loose clusters. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

This variant is distinguished by its longer floral stems compared to the typical form of Polygala gracilipes.
