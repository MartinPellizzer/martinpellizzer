Pleurothallis ricii

Pleurothallis ricii, commonly known as the Ricii orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers that are typically arranged in a loose inflorescence. Its leaves are linear and often clustered at the base, contributing to its compact appearance.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat preferences, it is a subject of interest for orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike.
