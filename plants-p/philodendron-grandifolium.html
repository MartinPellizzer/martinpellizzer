Philodendron grandifolium

Philodendron grandifolium, commonly known as the giant-leaf philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length, making it a striking ornamental plant. The leaves are typically a deep green with a waxy texture and often have a prominent midrib. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

This plant is popular among indoor gardeners due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
