Podocarpus kawaii

Podocarpus kawaii, commonly known as the Japanese podocarp or Kawaii podocarp, is a small evergreen tree native to southern Japan.

It belongs to the Podocarpaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, cone-like fruits. The plant has scale-like leaves that are tightly packed, giving it a compact and ornamental appearance. It is often cultivated as a garden plant due to its hardiness and ability to thrive in shaded environments.

The species is also valued for its use in traditional Japanese landscaping and as a source of timber.
