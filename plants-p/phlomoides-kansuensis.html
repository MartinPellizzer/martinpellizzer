Phlomoides kansuensis

Phlomoides kansuensis, commonly known as the Kansu phlomoides, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically pale blue or violet, arranged in whorls along the stem.
