Pelargonium sweetianum

Pelargonium sweetianum, commonly known as the sweet geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated edge, and it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a potted plant.

Pelargonium sweetianum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
