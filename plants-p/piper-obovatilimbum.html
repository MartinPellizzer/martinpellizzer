Piper obovatilimbum

Piper obovatilimbum, commonly known as the broad-leafed pepper vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, and the plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

However, it is not typically used for culinary purposes like other Piper species.
