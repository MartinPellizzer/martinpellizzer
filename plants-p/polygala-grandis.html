Polygala grandis

Polygala grandis, commonly known as the large milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southeastern China.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, spire-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, while its flowers are usually pale pink or white.

Polygala grandis is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
