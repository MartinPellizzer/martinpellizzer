Pedilochilus clemensiae

Pedilochilus clemensiae, commonly known as the Clements' rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green. The plant typically grows in rocky or shaded environments, often on limestone substrates. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil and rock surfaces.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in specialized gardens.
