Pittosporum hosmeri

Pittosporum hosmeri, commonly known as the Hosmer's pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible for birds but not for humans. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers a sunny to partially shaded location.

Pittosporum hosmeri is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various climates.
