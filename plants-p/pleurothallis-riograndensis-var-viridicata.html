Pleurothallis riograndensis var. viridicata

Pleurothallis riograndensis var. viridicata, commonly known as the Emerald Pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and green, often clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and horticultural appeal, it is a sought-after plant among orchid enthusiasts.
