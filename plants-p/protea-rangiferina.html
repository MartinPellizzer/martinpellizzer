Protea rangiferina

Protea rangiferina, commonly known as the silver protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence composed of numerous white to cream-colored flowers arranged in a spherical shape. The plant is native to the Western and Eastern Cape provinces of South Africa, where it thrives in nutrient-poor soils. Its unique morphology and striking appearance make it a popular ornamental plant.

The species is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
