Pilosella Spathophyllopsis

Pilosella spathophyllopsis is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It is known for its unique appearance, featuring small, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. This species thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often growing in alpine environments. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered vulnerable to environmental changes. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
