Pecteilis bassacensis

Pecteilis bassacensis, commonly known as the Cambodian water lily, is a unique aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, floating leaves that provide shade and habitat for aquatic life. The plant produces white, fragrant flowers that bloom above the water surface, adding aesthetic value to its environment. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the water, contributing to water purification.

Pecteilis bassacensis plays an important role in maintaining the ecological balance of its wetland habitats.
