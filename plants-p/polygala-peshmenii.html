Polygala peshmenii

Polygala peshmenii, commonly known as the Peshmenii milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces distinctive, spiny pods that contain the seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It is adapted to survive in dry, sandy soils and is often found in steppe and semi-desert environments.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
