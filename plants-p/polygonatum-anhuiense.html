Polygonatum anhuiense

Polygonatum anhuiense, commonly known as Anhui yellow tube, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features long, slender stems that bear lanceolate leaves and produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. It is typically found in forested areas with moist, well-drained soil.

Polygonatum anhuiense is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
