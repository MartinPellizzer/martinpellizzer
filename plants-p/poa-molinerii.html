Poa molinerii

Poa molinerii, commonly known as the Moliner's bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and bluish-green foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its inflorescence consists of a compact panicle, bearing small, spikelet-like flowers.

Poa molinerii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping for its delicate appearance.
