Pelargonium luridum

Pelargonium luridum, commonly known as the yellow geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which bloom in spring and summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Pelargonium luridum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and containers.
