Planchonella disticha

Planchonella disticha, commonly known as the broad-leafed hibiscus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 meters. The plant features simple, alternate, and ovate leaves with a smooth surface and a pointed tip. Its flowers are showy, with five petals, and are usually yellow or orange in color.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, round seeds.
