Poa sugawarae

Poa sugawarae, commonly known as the Sugawara bentgrass, is a species of grass native to Japan.

It is a low-growing perennial plant that typically forms dense mats on rocky or sandy soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is well adapted to harsh environmental conditions, including high altitude and poor soil.

Poa sugawarae is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological significance in its native habitat.
