Phyllanthus mindouliensis

Phyllanthus mindouliensis, commonly known as the African spurge, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are often elliptical in shape and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences, producing yellowish to greenish blooms.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional African medicine for various ailments.
