Prunus persica f. uninensis

Prunus persica f. uninensis, commonly known as the dwarf peach.

This is a compact, ornamental form of the peach tree, characterized by its small size and showy flowers. It produces small, sweet fruits that are typically less vigorous than those of the standard peach. The plant is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often used in gardens for its ornamental value.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun for optimal growth and fruit production.
