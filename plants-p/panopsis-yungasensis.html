Panopsis yungasensis

Panopsis yungasensis, commonly known as the Yungas panicled grass, is a flowering plant native to the Yungas region of Bolivia.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, branching stems. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species in its native habitat.
