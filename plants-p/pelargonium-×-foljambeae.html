Pelargonium × foljambeae

Pelargonium × foljambeae, commonly known as the variegated geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage that features a mix of green, white, and sometimes pink or red hues. The plant typically produces showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of red, pink, or purple, which bloom in spring and summer. Native to South Africa, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and container planting.
