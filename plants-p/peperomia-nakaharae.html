Peperomia nakaharae

Peperomia nakaharae, commonly known as the Nakahara's peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant features thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish or purple hue on the underside. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color.

Peperomia nakaharae is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.
