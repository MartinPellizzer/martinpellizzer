Pleurothallis litotes

Pleurothallis litotes, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

This epiphytic plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in the understory of rainforests. It produces small, delicate flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow, which are often clustered in a raceme. The plant has strap-like leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern around its stem.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a popular choice for collectors and enthusiasts of rare orchids.
