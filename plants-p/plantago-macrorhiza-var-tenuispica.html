Plantago macrorhiza var. tenuispica

Plantago macrorhiza var. tenuispica, commonly known as slenderleaf plantain, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features a rosette of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are slightly hairy and have a waxy surface. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in a dense spike, which are borne on long, slender stalks. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, along roadsides, and in disturbed areas.

This variety is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
