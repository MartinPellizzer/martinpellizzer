Pinus densiflora var. liaotungensis

Pinus densiflora var. liaotungensis, commonly known as the Liaotung pine, is a variant of the Japanese white pine.

It is native to eastern China, particularly the Liaotung Peninsula, and thrives in temperate climates. The plant is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and slender, upward-curving needles. Its cones are typically elongated and have a distinctive, slightly curved shape.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
