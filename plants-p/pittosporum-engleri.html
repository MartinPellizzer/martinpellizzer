Pittosporum engleri

Pittosporum engleri, commonly known as the New Zealand Pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 8 meters. The plant features dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a glossy texture. Its fragrant white or pale yellow flowers appear in clusters during the spring, followed by orange-red berries that are edible for birds.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is valued for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
