Prunus domestica var. cerea

Prunus domestica var. cerea, commonly known as the dwarf European plum, is a compact variety of the European plum tree.

It is characterized by its small stature, making it ideal for small gardens or container cultivation. The plant produces small, sweet, and juicy plums that are typically harvested in late summer. Its leaves are dark green and oval-shaped, with a slightly serrated edge.

The dwarf European plum is also valued for its ornamental appeal, featuring attractive spring blossoms and a hardy nature.
