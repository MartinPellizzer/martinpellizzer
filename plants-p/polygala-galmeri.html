Polygala galmeri

Polygala galmeri, commonly known as the Cape jasmine or white polygala, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils. It has narrow, linear leaves and a compact, shrub-like growth habit.

Polygala galmeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive floral display.
