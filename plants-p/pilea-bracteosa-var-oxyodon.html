Pilea bracteosa var. oxyodon

Pilea bracteosa var. oxyodon, commonly known as the Shingle Plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features dense clusters of small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a waxy texture. The plant produces inconspicuous, greenish-white flowers that are followed by small, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique leaf arrangement and low maintenance requirements contribute to its ornamental appeal.
