Pultenaea verticillata

Pultenaea verticillata, commonly known as the clustered pultenaea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are arranged in whorls of three to five around the stems. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom from late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland and heathland environments.

Pultenaea verticillata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in native gardens for its hardy nature and seasonal flowering.
