Polygonella basiramia

Polygonella basiramia, commonly known as the Basiramia sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

Polygonella basiramia is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ecological role in supporting biodiversity.
