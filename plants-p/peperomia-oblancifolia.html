Peperomia oblancifolia

Peperomia oblancifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate to lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers in clusters, which are followed by reddish-brown berries. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia oblancifolia is valued for its attractive foliage and air-purifying qualities.
