Peperomia smithiana

Peperomia smithiana, commonly known as the Smith's Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which are borne on short, erect spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive foliage, it is often used as a decorative plant in both home and office settings.
