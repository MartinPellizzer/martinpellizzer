Paphiopedilum villosum f. wangliangii

Paphiopedilum villosum f. wangliangii, commonly known as the Wangliang's velvet orchid, is a hybrid cultivar prized for its unique floral characteristics.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia, particularly China. The plant features a compact growth habit with dark green, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically pale yellow to orange, with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns.

This cultivar is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively ease of cultivation.
