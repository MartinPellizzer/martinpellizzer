Primulina sichuanensis var. sichuanensis

Primulina sichuanensis var. sichuanensis, commonly known as the Sichuan primulina, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive foliage. This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
