Pilosella flagellariformis subsp. jaromirensis

Pilosella flagellariformis subsp.

jaromirensis, commonly known as the Jaromir's slender pearly everlasting, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in dense, compact inflorescences.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slender, thread-like leaves and a compact growth habit. It thrives in dry, rocky or sandy soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine regions.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
