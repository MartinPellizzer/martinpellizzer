Primulina heterochroa

Primulina heterochroa, commonly known as the heterochroa African violet, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically has velvety, dark green leaves with a waxy texture, which help it retain moisture in its natural habitat. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and greenhouse cultivation.
