Pentzia sphaerocephala

Pentzia sphaerocephala, commonly known as the globe-headed pincushion, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense, spiny foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a striking contrast to its green, fleshy leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
