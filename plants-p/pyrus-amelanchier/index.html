Pyrus Amelanchier

The Pyrus amelanchier, commonly known as the serviceberry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to North America. It is celebrated for its ornamental value, featuring delicate white flowers in spring and showy pink or red berries in summer. This versatile plant thrives in a variety of soil types and is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal. Its edible fruits are cherished in culinary traditions, adding a unique flavor to jams and desserts. The Pyrus amelanchier also plays an important role in supporting local wildlife and biodiversity.
