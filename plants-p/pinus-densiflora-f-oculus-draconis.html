Pinus densiflora f. oculus-draconis

Pinus densiflora f. oculus-draconis, commonly known as the Dragon's Eye Pine, is a rare and distinctive variant of the Japanese black pine.

It is characterized by its unique, bulbous cones that resemble a dragon's eye, giving it its striking common name. The plant exhibits dense, dark green foliage with a compact growth habit, making it a visually appealing ornamental tree. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly valued in horticulture and botanical collections.
