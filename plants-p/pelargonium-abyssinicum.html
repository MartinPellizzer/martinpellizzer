Pelargonium abyssinicum

Pelargonium abyssinicum, commonly known as the Abyssinian geranium, is a species native to Ethiopia.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The leaves are typically rounded or heart-shaped with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
