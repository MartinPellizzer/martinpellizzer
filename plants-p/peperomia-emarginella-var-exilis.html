Peperomia emarginella var. exilis

Peperomia emarginella var. exilis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces delicate, white or pale pink flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for tabletop displays and hanging baskets.
