Prenanthes rubida

Prenanthes rubida, commonly known as the red stem aster, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features slender, erect stems that are often reddish in color, especially when mature. The plant produces small, clustered white to pinkish flowers with ray florets, typically blooming in late summer to early fall. Its leaves are lance-shaped and alternate along the stem, often with a rough texture.

Prenanthes rubida thrives in moist to wet habitats, such as marshes and wet meadows, and is often found in the eastern and central United States.
