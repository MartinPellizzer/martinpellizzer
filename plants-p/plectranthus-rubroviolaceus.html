Plectranthus rubroviolaceus

Plectranthus rubroviolaceus, commonly known as the purple velvet plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that ranges from dark green to purple in color. Its flowers are typically violet or purple, clustered in whorls along the stems.

It is often grown as an ornamental plant for its attractive appearance and ability to thrive in various conditions.
