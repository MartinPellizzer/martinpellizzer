Polygala karensium

Polygala karensium, commonly known as the Karen's milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, often branched stems and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive spurred structure that attracts pollinators like bees and butterflies.

The leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a soft, hairy texture.
