Pandanus koordersii

Pandanus koordersii, commonly known as the Koorders' pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pandanaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. Its inflorescence consists of a large, branched cluster of small, fragrant flowers.

Pandanus koordersii is valued not only for its ornamental appearance but also for its cultural and practical uses in local communities.
