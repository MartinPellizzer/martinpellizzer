Peperomia flagelliformis

Peperomia flagelliformis, commonly known as the thread-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features slender, upright stems that bear small, ovate, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for succulent and foliage enthusiasts.
