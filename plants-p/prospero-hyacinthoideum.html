Prospero hyacinthoideum

Prospero hyacinthoideum, commonly known as the blue hyacinth, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features strap-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant blooms and long-lasting display.
