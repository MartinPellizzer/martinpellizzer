Poa brizoides

Poa brizoides, commonly known as the silver hair grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern South America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and tufted growth habit, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a hairy texture that contributes to its common name.

Poa brizoides is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
