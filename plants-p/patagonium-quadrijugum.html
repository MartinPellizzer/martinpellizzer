Patagonium quadrijugum

Patagonium quadrijugum, commonly known as the four-winged grass.

It is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America, particularly in the Andes. The plant is characterized by its unique four-winged seeds, which are adapted for wind dispersal. It thrives in cold, arid environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the local ecosystem.
