Podocarpus pruinosus

Podocarpus pruinosus, commonly known as the glossy cupressus or velvet cypress, is a evergreen coniferous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features scale-like leaves that are typically dark green and covered with a waxy coating, giving it a glossy appearance. The plant produces small, spherical cones that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Podocarpus pruinosus is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in horticulture and landscaping.
