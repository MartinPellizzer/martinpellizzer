Platycoryne latipetala

Platycoryne latipetala, commonly known as the broad-petaled flat-lobed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive broad, flat petals and sepals, which give it its scientific name. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a subtle fragrance.

Platycoryne latipetala is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
