Primula decora

Primula decora, commonly known as the Decorative Primrose, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has hairy, linear leaves that are often arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial to full shade.

Primula decora is valued in horticulture for its early blooming habit and delicate floral display.
