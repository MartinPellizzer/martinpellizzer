Pantlingia paradoxa

It is characterized by its striking, violet-blue petals that resemble the colors of twilight. The plant has a unique morphology, with a short, sturdy stem and clustered leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs. Its flowers bloom only during the late summer months, making it a seasonal wonder in its natural habitat.
