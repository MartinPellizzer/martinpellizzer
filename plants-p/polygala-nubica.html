Polygala nubica

Polygala nubica, commonly known as the mountain polygala, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a member of the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.
