Poa molinerii var. subtilis

Poa molinerii var. subtilis, commonly known as the slender meadow grass, is a variant of the meadow grass species native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial grass with narrow, fine leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that contain both male and female flowers, typical of the Poaceae family. It thrives in moist, temperate environments and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it an important component of grassland ecosystems.
