Pentacalia subglomerosa

Pentacalia subglomerosa, commonly known as the clustered pentacalia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of small, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
