Phyllanthus confusus

Phyllanthus confusus, commonly known as the confused phyllanthus, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features small, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant, and it produces small, spherical fruits.

The plant is characterized by its simple, clustered inflorescences and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
