Peperomia hispidula var. ellipticifolia

Peperomia hispidula var. ellipticifolia, commonly known as the velvety peperomia, is a compact, trailing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are elliptical in shape and covered in fine, velvety hairs, giving it a soft, textured appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that emerge from the axils of the leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardens and hanging baskets.
