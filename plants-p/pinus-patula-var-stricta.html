Pinus patula var. stricta

Pinus patula var. stricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Mexican pine, is a variant of the Mexican pine native to Mexico.

It is a coniferous evergreen tree characterized by its slender, columnar growth habit and dense foliage. The needles are typically longer and more narrow compared to the standard Pinus patula, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces cones that are often used in horticulture and ornamental gardening.

This variety is valued for its adaptability and aesthetic appeal in landscaping.
