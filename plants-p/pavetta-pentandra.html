Pavetta pentandra

Pavetta pentandra, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often pubescent. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

The fruit is a capsule that releases small, wind-dispersed seeds.
