Panicum garadei

Panicum garadei, commonly known as the Garade's bluestem, is a grass species native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces small, blue-green spikelets that are distinctive in appearance. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and savanna habitats. It is adapted to seasonal droughts and can survive in areas with low rainfall.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in arid and semi-arid regions.
