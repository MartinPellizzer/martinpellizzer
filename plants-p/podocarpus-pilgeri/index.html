Podocarpus Pilgeri

Pilger's podocarp, scientifically known as Podocarpus pilgeri, is a rare and ancient conifer native to the mountainous regions of southern China. This evergreen tree is characterized by its dense foliage and unique cone structure, which sets it apart from other members of the Podocarpaceae family. Despite its ecological significance, it remains critically endangered due to habitat loss and overexploitation. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and propagate this species, highlighting its importance in biodiversity. Its survival is a testament to the resilience of nature in the face of human impact.
