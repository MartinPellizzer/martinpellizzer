Pteracanthus nobilis

Pteracanthus nobilis, commonly known as the silverleaf fern, is a tropical plant characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to humid regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features a creeping rhizome with clusters of small, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.
