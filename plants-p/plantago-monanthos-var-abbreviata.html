Plantago monanthos var. abbreviata

Plantago monanthos var. abbreviata, commonly known as the short-stalked plantain, is a variant of the common plantain species.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its broad, oval-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, along roadsides, and in disturbed areas.

This variety is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in herbal remedies.
