Pennisetum weberbaueri

Pennisetum weberbaueri, commonly known as purple fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and fluffy, purple-tinted plumes that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in dense, fountain-like inflorescences. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Pennisetum weberbaueri is also valued for its ability to attract birds and butterflies.
