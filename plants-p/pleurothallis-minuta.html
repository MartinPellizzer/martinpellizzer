Pleurothallis minuta

Pleurothallis minuta, commonly known as the miniature pleurothallis, is a small orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, slender stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often clustered in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

Due to its size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for terrariums and miniature garden displays.
