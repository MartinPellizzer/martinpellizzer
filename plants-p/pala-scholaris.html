Pala scholaris

Pala scholaris, commonly known as the scholar's palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, slender trunk with a smooth, grayish-brown bark and can grow up to 15 meters in height. The plant has large, pinnate leaves that are glossy and can reach lengths of up to 3 meters, making it a dramatic addition to any landscape. Its growth habit is typically upright, with a single trunk and a crown of fronds that give it a graceful appearance.

Pala scholaris thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
