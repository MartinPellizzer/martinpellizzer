Pisonia ambigua var. lilloana

Pisonia ambigua var. lilloana, commonly known as the Lillo's pisonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that consist of small, white to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to several meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly serrated margin.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in warm climates for its attractive floral display.
