Phyllanthus lathyroides f. minor

Phyllanthus lathyroides f. minor, commonly known as the dwarf phyllanthus, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, oval leaves that are typically arranged in a dense, bushy form. The plant produces tiny, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, round fruits. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various soil conditions.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
