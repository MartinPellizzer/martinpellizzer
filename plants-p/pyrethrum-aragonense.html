Pyrethrum aragonense

Pyrethrum aragonense, commonly known as the Aragon pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces small, spherical seed heads that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by the wind. Its foliage consists of finely divided, fern-like leaves that are aromatic and often used in herbal remedies.

Pyrethrum aragonense is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in natural pest control.
