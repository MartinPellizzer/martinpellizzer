Platysepalum tessmannii

Platysepalum tessmannii, commonly known as the Hainan star orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to China.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant has a compact growth habit with a short, stocky stem and is often found in shaded, humid environments. Its leaves are elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a threatened species in its native range.
