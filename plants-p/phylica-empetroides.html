Phylica empetroides

Phylica empetroides, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal and mountainous regions, characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters, often in winter. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, adapted to withstand coastal conditions.

Phylica empetroides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
