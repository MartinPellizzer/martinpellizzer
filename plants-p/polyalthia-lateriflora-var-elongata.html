Polyalthia lateriflora var. elongata

Polyalthia lateriflora var. elongata, commonly known as the long-flowered satinwood, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, pendulous inflorescences that bear small, fragrant white flowers. The leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a glossy surface. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
