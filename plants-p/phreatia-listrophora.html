Phreatia listrophora

Phreatia listrophora, commonly known as the narrow-leafed phreatia, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, strap-like leaves and produces pendulous, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the soil and surrounding air.

Phreatia listrophora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
