Pelargonium × venustum

Pelargonium × venustum, commonly known as the hybrid geranium, is a flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Pelargonium hortorum and Pelargonium × inquinans. The plant is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. It typically grows as an upright shrub with rounded, velvety leaves.

Pelargonium × venustum is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.
