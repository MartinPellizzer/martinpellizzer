Polypodium pteropus var. minor

Polypodium pteropus var. minor, commonly known as the Lesser Shield Fern, is a fern species native to Southeast Asia and parts of the Pacific.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. Its fronds are usually dark green with a leathery texture and can reach lengths of up to 30 centimeters.

This variety is closely related to other polypodium species and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
