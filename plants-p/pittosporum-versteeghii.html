Pittosporum versteeghii

Pittosporum versteeghii, commonly known as the New Zealand pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage, with lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. The plant produces fragrant white or cream flowers in clusters, which are typically followed by small, berry-like fruits. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

Pittosporum versteeghii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
