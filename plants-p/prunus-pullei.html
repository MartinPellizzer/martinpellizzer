Prunus pullei

Prunus pullei, commonly known as the Chinese plum or Chinese date, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically yellow or red when ripe, often used in traditional Chinese medicine and cuisine.

Prunus pullei thrives in temperate climates and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
