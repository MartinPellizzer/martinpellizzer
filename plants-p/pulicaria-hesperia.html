Pulicaria hesperia

Pulicaria hesperia, commonly known as the Narrow-leaved Groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial with small, yellow composite flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Pulicaria hesperia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, supporting various pollinators.
