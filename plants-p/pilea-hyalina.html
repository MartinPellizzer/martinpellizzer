Pilea hyalina

Pilea hyalina, commonly known as the glassy pilea, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, waxy leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in the spring. It is a popular houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.

Pilea hyalina prefers indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.
