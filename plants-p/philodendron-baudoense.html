Philodendron baudoense

Philodendron baudoense, commonly known as the Baudo philodendron, is a climbing plant native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy green leaves. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the floral structures typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a houseplant.
