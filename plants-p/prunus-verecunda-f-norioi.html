Prunus verecunda f. norioi

Prunus verecunda f. norioi, commonly known as the dwarf cherry or Japanese dwarf cherry, is a compact, ornamental shrub native to Japan.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by bright red berries in late summer. The plant has a rounded, bushy growth habit and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for container gardening.

Its unique foliage and seasonal blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental purposes.
