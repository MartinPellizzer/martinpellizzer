Potentilla heptaphylla var. thuringiaca

Potentilla heptaphylla var. thuringiaca, commonly known as the Thuringian potentilla, is a perennial shrub native to Central Europe.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking hardy, low-maintenance plants.
