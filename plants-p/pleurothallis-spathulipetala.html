Pleurothallis spathulipetala

Pleurothallis spathulipetala, commonly known as the spatulate petal pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lanceolate leaves and produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a unique spatulate petal shape. Its blooming period typically occurs during the warmer months, and it is prized for its ornamental value in horticulture.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments to ensure its survival.
