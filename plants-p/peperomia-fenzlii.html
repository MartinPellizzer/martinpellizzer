Peperomia fenzlii

Peperomia fenzlii, commonly known as the Fenzli's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia fenzlii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
