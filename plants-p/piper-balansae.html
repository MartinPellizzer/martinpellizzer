Piper balansae

Piper balansae, commonly known as the Balansae pepper or the Madagascar pepper, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant is characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, heart-shaped leaves. It produces small green flowers that transition to red berries, which are edible and have a peppery flavor.

Piper balansae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a spice in local cuisines.
