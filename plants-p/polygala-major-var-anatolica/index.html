Polygala Major Var. Anatolica

Anatolian milkvetch, scientifically known as Polygala major var. anatolica, is a rare and resilient plant native to the Anatolian region of Turkey. This plant belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery foliage and small, intricate flowers. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and open landscapes. Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it faces significant conservation challenges. Despite its modest appearance, the Anatolian milkvetch holds cultural and ecological importance in its native habitat.
