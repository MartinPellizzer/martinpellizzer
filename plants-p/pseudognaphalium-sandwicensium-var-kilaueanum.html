Pseudognaphalium sandwicensium var. kilaueanum

Pseudognaphalium sandwicensium var. kilaueanum, commonly known as the Kilauea everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to the arid and salty conditions of its habitat. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.
