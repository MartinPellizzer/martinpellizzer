Peperomia galioides var. menkeana

Peperomia galioides var. menkeana, commonly known as the velvet leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features broad, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green color, often adorned with silver or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of green or purple, which are followed by elliptical, winged fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ornamental foliage makes it a valuable addition to shaded or low-light environments.
