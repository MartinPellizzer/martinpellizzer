Perezia seemannii

Perezia seemannii, commonly known as the Cape Peninsula orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in rocky, coastal environments, where it thrives in well-drained soil. Its flowers bloom during the summer months, adding a unique ornamental value to its natural habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a rare and protected species.
