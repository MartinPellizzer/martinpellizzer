Plectranthus meeboldii

Plectranthus meeboldii, commonly known as the blue plectranthus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features square, hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to white, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its aromatic foliage and ornamental value make it a sought-after plant in horticulture.
