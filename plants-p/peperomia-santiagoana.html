Peperomia santiagoana

Peperomia santiagoana, commonly known as the Santiagoan peperomia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a compact, low-growing plant with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green or variegated with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Peperomia santiagoana is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
