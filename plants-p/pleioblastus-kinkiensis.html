Pleioblastus kinkiensis

Pleioblastus kinkiensis, commonly known as the Kinki bamboo, is a species of grass-like plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its slender, arching shoots and fine, feather-like leaves. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and forms dense clumps over time. Its foliage is often a vibrant green, and it can reach heights of up to 2 meters.

Pleioblastus kinkiensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and garden design.
