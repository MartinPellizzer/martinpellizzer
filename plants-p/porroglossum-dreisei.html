Porroglossum dreisei

Porroglossum dreisei, commonly known as the dwarf iron plant, is a succulent perennial native to southern Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil to thrive.

Porroglossum dreisei is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and drought tolerance.
