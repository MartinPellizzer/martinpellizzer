Parodia malyana var. igneuiflora

Parodia malyana var. igneuiflora, commonly known as the fiery torch cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a dense covering of light green to yellowish spines, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique coloration and compact size make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
