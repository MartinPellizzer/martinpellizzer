Panicum machrisianum

Panicum machrisianum, commonly known as silver panicum, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage due to dense pubescence. The plant produces fluffy, pinkish to reddish inflorescences that are often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ability to spread via rhizomes make it a resilient choice for landscaping in arid environments.
