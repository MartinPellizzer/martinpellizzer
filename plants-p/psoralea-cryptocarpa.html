Psoralea cryptocarpa

Psoralea cryptocarpa, commonly known as the hidden fruit psoralea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its small, pale yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant produces small, hairy pods that contain several elliptical seeds. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.
