Peperomia dasystachya

Peperomia dasystachya, commonly known as the narrow-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish-white in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Peperomia dasystachya is valued for its ornamental foliage and air-purifying qualities.
