Pisum sativum var. willichii

Pisum sativum var. willichii, commonly known as the dwarf garden pea, is a variety of the common pea plant.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit, making it ideal for small garden spaces. The plant produces small, edible pods that contain several seeds, similar to other pea varieties. It thrives in cool climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for both ornamental and culinary purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and nutritious seeds.
