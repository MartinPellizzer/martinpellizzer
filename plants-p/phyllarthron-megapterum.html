Phyllarthron megapterum

Phyllarthron megapterum, commonly known as the giant feather fern, is a unique plant characterized by its large, feathery fronds that can reach lengths of over three meters.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to tropical regions of South America. The plant's fronds are composed of numerous small, needle-like pinnae that give it a distinctive, airy appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid rainforests.

Its botanical structure supports its role as an important component of the forest understory ecosystem.
