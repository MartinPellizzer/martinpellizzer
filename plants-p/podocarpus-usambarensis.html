Podocarpus usambarensis

Podocarpus usambarensis, commonly known as the Usambarensis podocarp, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the Eastern Arc Mountains of Tanzania.

It belongs to the Podocarpaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and cone-bearing reproductive structures. The tree typically grows in humid, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests. Its wood is valued for its durability and is used in construction and furniture-making.

The plant plays a significant role in its ecosystem by providing habitat and contributing to soil stability.
