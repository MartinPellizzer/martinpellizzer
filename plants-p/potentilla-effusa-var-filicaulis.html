Potentilla effusa var. filicaulis

Potentilla effusa var. filicaulis, commonly known as the fern-leaved cinquefoil, is a perennial shrub native to North America.

It features compound leaves with a fern-like appearance, consisting of multiple pairs of leaflets. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
