Parsonsia chiapensis

Parsonsia chiapensis, commonly known as the Chiapas flameflower, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

Parsonsia chiapensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in well-drained soil with partial to full sunlight.
