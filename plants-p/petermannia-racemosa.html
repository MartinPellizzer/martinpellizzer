Petermannia racemosa

Petermannia racemosa, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant orchid-like flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has a compact, bushy appearance with linear leaves. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or white and bloom in spring.

Petermannia racemosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
