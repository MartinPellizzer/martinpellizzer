Podocoma bartsiifolia

Podocoma bartsiifolia, commonly known as the Cape York thornveld.

It is a shrub native to South Africa, characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, pale green leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, adapting well to poor soils and high temperatures.

Podocoma bartsiifolia is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
