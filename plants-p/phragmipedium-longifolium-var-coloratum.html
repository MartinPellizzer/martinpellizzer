Phragmipedium longifolium var. coloratum

Phragmipedium longifolium var. coloratum, commonly known as the coloratum orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that are often a vibrant green with a waxy texture. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
