Pilosella praealta subsp. stellata

Pilosella praealta subsp.

stellata, commonly known as the starry pearly everlet, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are pinnately divided and covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The showy yellow flowers appear in summer and are followed by fluffy, white seed heads that aid in wind dispersal.
