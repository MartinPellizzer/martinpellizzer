Pseudostellaria europaea

Pseudostellaria europaea, commonly known as European starwort, is a small flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in meadows and along stream banks. The plant features upright, branching stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, white or pink, and arranged in clusters, giving it a star-like appearance.

It is often found in temperate regions of Europe and has historical uses in herbal medicine.
