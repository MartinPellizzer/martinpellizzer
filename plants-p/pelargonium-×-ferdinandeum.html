Pelargonium × ferdinandeum

Pelargonium × ferdinandeum, commonly known as the Chinese geranium, is a hybrid plant native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The leaves are typically rounded and have a slightly fuzzy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This plant is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various climates.
