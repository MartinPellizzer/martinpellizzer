Pleione croockewitii

Pleione croockewitii, commonly known as the Crookewit pleione, is a rare and elegant orchid species native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking, showy flowers with a complex pattern of colors, typically featuring a pale yellow base with deep red and purple markings. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments with well-drained soil, often growing in shaded forest understories. Its blooming period occurs in late winter to early spring, making it a sought-after specimen among orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
