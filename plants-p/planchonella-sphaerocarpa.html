Planchonella sphaerocarpa

Planchonella sphaerocarpa, commonly known as the round-fruited native lime, is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or yellow, followed by spherical, green fruits that turn yellow when mature. The fruit contains a single seed enclosed in a fibrous, fleshy pulp.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
