Piper darienense

Piper darienense, commonly known as the Darien pepper, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper and long pepper. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are small berries that turn red when mature, and they are often used in traditional medicine.

The plant's botanical features make it an important species in the study of tropical flora and medicinal plants.
