Polypodium subdrynariaceum

Polypodium subdrynariaceum, commonly known as the slender wall fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on tree trunks and rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has long, narrow leaves that are often divided into segments, giving it a delicate, feathery appearance. Its fronds are usually dark green and can reach lengths of up to 50 centimeters.

This fern is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
