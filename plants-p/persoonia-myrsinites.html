Persoonia myrsinites

Persoonia myrsinites, commonly known as the silverleaf persoonia, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine, white hairs, contributing to its silvery hue.

Persoonia myrsinites is often grown in gardens for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
