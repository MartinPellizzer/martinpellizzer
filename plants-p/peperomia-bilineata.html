Peperomia bilineata

Peperomia bilineata, commonly known as the two-lined peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slight variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia bilineata is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
