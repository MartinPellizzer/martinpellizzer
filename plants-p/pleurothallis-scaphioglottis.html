Pleurothallis scaphioglottis

Pleurothallis scaphioglottis, commonly known as the hooded pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, with a distinctive hood-like structure on the petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, loose soil for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
