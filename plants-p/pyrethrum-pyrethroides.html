Pyrethrum pyrethroides

Pyrethrum pyrethroides, commonly known as the African pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers and serrated leaves. The plant produces small, oval-shaped seeds that are dispersed by wind. Its flowering period typically occurs during the warmer months, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Pyrethrum pyrethroides is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
