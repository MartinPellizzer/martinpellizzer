Polygonum sieboldii var. compactum

Polygonum sieboldii var. compactum, commonly known as the Japanese knotweed or compact Japanese knotweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features dense clusters of small, pinkish to reddish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has stout, somewhat woody stems that are often reddish in color. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a rough texture and a prominent midrib.

It is known for its aggressive growth and can become invasive in certain environments.
