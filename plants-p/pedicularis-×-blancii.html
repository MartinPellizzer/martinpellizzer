Pedicularis × blancii

Pedicularis × blancii, commonly known as the hybrid monkey orchid, is a member of the Orobanchaceae family and is native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its striking, spurred flowers that range in color from yellow to orange-red. Its flowers are adapted for pollination by bees and other insects, with the spurred structure facilitating nectar access.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
