Potamogeton × zizii var. connecticutensis

Potamogeton × zizii var. connecticutensis, commonly known as the Connecticut pondweed, is a hybrid aquatic plant native to North America.

It is a perennial species that typically grows in shallow freshwater environments such as ponds and lakes. The plant has long, ribbon-like leaves that are usually dark green and float just beneath the water surface. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often submerged, and it plays a role in providing habitat for aquatic organisms.

Its presence can indicate good water quality due to its sensitivity to pollution.
