Physurus maurevertii

Physurus maurevertii, commonly known as the Maurevert's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, preferring moist soils and full sun to partial shade. Its slender, triangular stems are often adorned with small, inconspicuous flowers that bloom in summer.
