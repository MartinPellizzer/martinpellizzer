Pithecellobium flexicaule

Pithecellobium flexicaule, commonly known as the flexible pithecellobium, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its small, yellow flowers are followed by elongated pods containing edible seeds.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and potential use in agroforestry systems.
