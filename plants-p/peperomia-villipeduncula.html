Peperomia villipeduncula

Peperomia villipeduncula, commonly known as the coral peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Central and South America.

It features dense clusters of small, ovate leaves that are typically green with a reddish or maroon tinge on the underside. The plant produces delicate, sparsely branched inflorescences with tiny, white to pale pink flowers. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.

Peperomia villipeduncula thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate moisture.
