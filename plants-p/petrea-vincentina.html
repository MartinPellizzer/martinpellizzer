Petrea vincentina

Petrea vincentina, commonly known as the pink shower tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking pink and white flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are opposite on the stems, and it can grow as a shrub or small tree. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun.

Petrea vincentina is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
