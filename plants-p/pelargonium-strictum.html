Pelargonium strictum

Pelargonium strictum, commonly known as the upright geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The leaves are typically rounded and have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

This species is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
