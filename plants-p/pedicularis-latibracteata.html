Pedicularis latibracteata

Pedicularis latibracteata, commonly known as the broad-bract lousewort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

Its leaves are often reduced to bracts, which are broad and leaf-like, contributing to its common name.
