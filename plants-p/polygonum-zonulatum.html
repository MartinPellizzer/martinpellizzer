Polygonum zonulatum

Polygonum zonulatum, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a flowering plant species native to parts of Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which is characterized by its distinctive leaf shapes and often reddish stems. The plant is known for its striking, elongated leaves that curve and twist, giving it its common name. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color.

Polygonum zonulatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
