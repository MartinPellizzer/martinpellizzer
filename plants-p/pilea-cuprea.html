Pilea cuprea

Pilea cuprea, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its round, copper-colored leaves that give it a unique and attractive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by red berries. It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

Pilea cuprea is also known for its ability to purify the air and improve indoor air quality.
