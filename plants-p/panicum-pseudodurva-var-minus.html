Panicum pseudodurva var. minus

Panicum pseudodurva var. minus, commonly known as little bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and tufted inflorescences that turn golden in the fall. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and forage for wildlife.
