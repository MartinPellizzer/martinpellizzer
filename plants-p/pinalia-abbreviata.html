Pinalia abbreviata

Pinalia abbreviata, commonly known as the short-stemmed pinyon.

It is a shrub-like plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant features small, needle-like leaves and produces tiny, clustered flowers. Its seeds are enclosed in a hard, woody capsule, typical of the Pinaceae family.

Pinalia abbreviata is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability.
