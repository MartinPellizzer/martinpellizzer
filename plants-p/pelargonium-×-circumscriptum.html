Pelargonium × circumscriptum

Pelargonium × circumscriptum, commonly known as the Zebra Geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive mottled leaves with a variegated pattern of green, white, and purple, resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, typically blooming in spring and summer. It is a compact shrub, often used in garden settings for its ornamental value.

Native to South Africa, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
