Podocarpus guatemalensis var. allenii

Podocarpus guatemalensis var. allenii, commonly known as the Allen's podocarp, is a member of the Podocarpaceae family and is native to Central America.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows in humid, tropical climates. The plant has dense, dark green foliage with leathery leaves that are often arranged in whorls. Its cones are small and typically contain two to three seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
