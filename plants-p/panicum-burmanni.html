Panicum burmanni

Panicum burmanni, commonly known as Burmann's panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant typically produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. Its leaves are narrow and blade-like, often with a bluish-green hue.

Panicum burmanni is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and can thrive in disturbed or open habitats.
