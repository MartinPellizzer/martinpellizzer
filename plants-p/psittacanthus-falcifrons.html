Psittacanthus falcifrons

Psittacanthus falcifrons, commonly known as the golden shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
