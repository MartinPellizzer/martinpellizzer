Peperomia humilis var. humilis

Peperomia humilis var. humilis, commonly known as the dwarf peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slight sheen, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is prized for its compact size and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
