Prunus serrulata kojima

Prunus serrulata kojima, commonly known as the Kojima cherry, is a species of flowering shrub or small tree native to Japan.

It is characterized by its delicate, pink to white flowers that bloom in early spring, often before the leaves emerge. The plant has ovate to elliptical leaves with serrated edges, giving it its scientific name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

The Kojima cherry is also valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators during the spring season.
