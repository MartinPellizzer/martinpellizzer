Pleurothallis Sylvatica

Pleurothallis sylvatica is a rare and delicate orchid native to the cloud forests of South America. Known for its small, intricate blooms and lush green foliage, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. This species is often overlooked due to its modest size and subtle appearance. Despite its quiet presence, it plays a vital role in its ecosystem. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique plant from habitat loss and over-collection.
