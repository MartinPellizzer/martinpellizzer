Pterostylis semirubra

Pterostylis semirubra, commonly known as the red-stemmed helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. The plant features a slender, hairy stem and produces a single, showy greenish-yellow flower with a distinctive helmet-shaped labellum. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

The species is part of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is known for its specialized pollination mechanisms.
