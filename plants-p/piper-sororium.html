Piper sororium

Piper sororium, commonly known as the female pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing vines and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.

The fruit is a small, dark purple berry that contains the seeds.
