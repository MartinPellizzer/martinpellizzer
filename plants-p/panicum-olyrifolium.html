Panicum olyrifolium

Panicum olyrifolium, commonly known as silver hairgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-tipped foliage that gives it a distinctive, almost ethereal appearance. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats, such as swamps and marshes, and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small, spikelet-bearing stems.

Panicum olyrifolium plays an important role in wetland ecosystems by stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
