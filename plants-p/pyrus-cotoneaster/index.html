Pyrus Cotoneaster

Cotoneaster, scientifically known as Pyrus cotoneaster, is a versatile and widely cultivated shrub native to various regions of Asia and Europe. This plant is prized for its ornamental value, featuring vibrant berries and showy flowers that attract pollinators and add visual interest to gardens. Despite its common name, it is not a true apple tree but belongs to the Rosaceae family, sharing some characteristics with the apple genus. Its adaptability to different soil types and climates makes it a popular choice for landscaping and horticulture. However, it is important to note that some species can be invasive in certain regions, requiring careful management.
