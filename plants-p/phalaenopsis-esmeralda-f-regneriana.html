Phalaenopsis esmeralda f. regneriana

Phalaenopsis esmeralda f. regneriana, commonly known as the Emerald Phalaenopsis, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant green foliage and long-lasting, showy flowers.

This variety belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia, where it thrives in tropical climates. It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often a striking emerald green, complementing its white or pale pink blooms. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, and requires well-draining potting media and bright, indirect light.

It is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
