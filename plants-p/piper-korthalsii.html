Piper korthalsii

Piper korthalsii, commonly known as the African pepper plant, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of flowering plants known for their aromatic properties. The plant is characterized by its vine-like growth habit and small, heart-shaped leaves. It produces clusters of tiny, greenish-white flowers that develop into small, black berries.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
