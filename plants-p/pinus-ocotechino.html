Pinus ocotechino

Pinus ocotechino, commonly known as the Chinese white pine, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, pyramidal shape and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. The tree produces small, cone-like structures that contain its seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its needles are typically long and blue-green in color, providing a distinctive appearance.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
