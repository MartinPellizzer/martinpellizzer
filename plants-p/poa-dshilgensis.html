Poa dshilgensis

Poa dshilgensis, commonly known as the Dshilgen grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in Kazakhstan and Mongolia. This perennial grass is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, leafy stems. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets.

It is adapted to cold and arid environments, making it a hardy species found in alpine and steppe habitats.
