Pelargonium × melancholicum

Pelargonium × melancholicum, commonly known as the dark-leaved geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its deep green, almost black foliage and clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. Native to South Africa, this species thrives in warm, sunny conditions and well-drained soil. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The plant is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
