Phyllostachys bambusoides f. marliacea

Phyllostachys bambusoides f. marliacea, commonly known as the Marliacea bamboo, is a variant of the bamboo species characterized by its unique, compact growth habit.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are often a striking silver-white or variegated color, which sets it apart from typical green bamboo varieties. The plant is a clump-forming grass that thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes and container planting.

This cultivar is known for its slow growth and ability to add texture and visual interest to gardens.
