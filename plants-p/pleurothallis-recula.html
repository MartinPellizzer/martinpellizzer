Pleurothallis recula

Pleurothallis recula, commonly known as the miniature orchid or dwarf orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has slender, wiry stems and produces delicate, small flowers in shades of white and pink. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to conserve moisture in its natural habitat.

Pleurothallis recula is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
