Phalaenopsis × leucorrhoda var. cynthia

Phalaenopsis × leucorrhoda var. cynthia, commonly known as the Cynthia Phalaenopsis, is a hybrid orchid prized for its elegant, long-lasting blooms.

It features large, showy flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a subtle pinkish tinge on the petals. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, and thrives in warm, humid environments. Its foliage consists of dark green, strap-like leaves that are dense and compact.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relative ease of care.
