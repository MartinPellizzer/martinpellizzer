Pernettya trinervia

Pernettya trinervia, commonly known as the dwarf cowberry, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the northern regions of Europe and Asia.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or white, followed by red, fleshy berries that are edible. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in heathland and forest understory environments.

Pernettya trinervia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
