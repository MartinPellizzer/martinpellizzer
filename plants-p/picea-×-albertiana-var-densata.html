Picea × albertiana var. densata

Picea × albertiana var. densata, commonly known as the dense spruce, is a hybrid conifer native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and dark green, needle-like foliage. The plant produces cones that are typically oval-shaped and contain small, winged seeds. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found in subalpine and alpine environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
