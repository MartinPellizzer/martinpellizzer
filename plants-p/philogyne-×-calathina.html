Philogyne × calathina

Philogyne × calathina, commonly known as the hybrid hibiscus, is a botanical hybrid resulting from the cross between Philogyne and Calathina species.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant is a shrub or small tree, native to tropical and subtropical regions. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
