Philodendron linganii

Philodendron linganii, commonly known as the silver-barked philodendron, is a climbing tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves with a distinctive silvery sheen on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and hanging baskets.
