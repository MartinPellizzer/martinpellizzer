Primula faberi

Primula faberi, commonly known as the Chinese primrose, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, white, and purple. The plant typically grows in woodland areas and has hairy leaves that help it retain moisture.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a potted plant.
