Persica vulgaris var. laevis

Persica vulgaris var. laevis, commonly known as the smooth peach.

It is a variant of the peach tree characterized by its smooth, hairless skin on the fruit. The plant is native to regions of Asia and has been widely cultivated for its sweet, juicy fruits. The leaves are alternate, simple, and have a serrated margin.

It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
