Poa hookeriana

Poa hookeriana, commonly known as Hooker's bluegrass, is a species of grass native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green blades and forms dense, low-growing mats. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It has narrow, slightly rolled leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

Poa hookeriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
