Palicourea pariensis

Palicourea pariensis, commonly known as the Brazilian star apple, is a climbing shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, elliptic leaves with slightly serrated edges. Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is cultivated in some regions for both horticultural and culinary purposes.
