Potentilla doubjonneana

Potentilla doubjonneana, commonly known as the Doubjonnean cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a hairy texture.

Potentilla doubjonneana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and vibrant floral display.
