Protea caffra subsp. caffra

Protea caffra subsp.

caffra, commonly known as the silver protea, is a striking South African plant characterized by its large, showy inflorescence. It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is native to the Western and Eastern Cape provinces.

The plant features a tall, slender stem with a dense cluster of white to pale pink flowers that resemble a cluster of small bells. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, often covered in a silvery-gray coating.

This species is well adapted to the Mediterranean climate of its native region, thriving in well-drained soils and full sunlight.
