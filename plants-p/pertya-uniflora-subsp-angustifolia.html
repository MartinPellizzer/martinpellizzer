Pertya uniflora subsp. angustifolia

Pertya uniflora subsp.

angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pertya, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America. It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale pink.

The plant prefers well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats. It is a perennial species that thrives in temperate climates with mild winters.
