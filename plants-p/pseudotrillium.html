Pseudotrillium

Pseudotrillium, commonly known as the false trillium, is a member of the Melanthiaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern North America. The plant features a single, erect stem with three broadly ovate leaves that are usually pale green. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink and are borne at the base of the leaves.

Pseudotrillium thrives in moist, shaded woodlands and is often found in forest understories.
