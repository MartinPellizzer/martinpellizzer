Pelargonium pavoninum

Pelargonium pavoninum, commonly known as the rainbow geranium, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant features upright stems and heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking colorful, low-maintenance ornamental plants.

Its unique appearance and adaptability contribute to its appeal in both cultivated and natural settings.
