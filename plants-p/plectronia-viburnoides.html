Plectronia viburnoides

Plectronia viburnoides, commonly known as the silverleaf viburnum, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, white, clustered flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant has dark green, lance-shaped leaves that turn yellow in the fall. Its fruit is a small, red, fleshy drupe that attracts birds.

Plectronia viburnoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
