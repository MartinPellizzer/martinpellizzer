Peperomia caudulilimba

Peperomia caudulilimba, commonly known as the trailing peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate vein patterns, contributing to its ornamental appeal. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia caudulilimba is favored for its ease of care and ability to thrive in indoor environments.
