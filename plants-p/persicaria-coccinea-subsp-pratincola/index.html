Persicaria Coccinea Subsp. Pratincola

Persicaria coccinea subsp. pratincola is a strikingly colorful plant known for its vibrant red and orange flowers. It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, commonly referred to as the buckwheat family. This subspecies is native to parts of Asia and is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects. The plant's ability to thrive in diverse conditions highlights its adaptability and ecological importance.
