Peltaria isatoides

Peltaria isatoides, commonly known as yellow rocket, is a biennial or annual plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its smooth, alternate leaves that are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in fields, along roadsides, and in woodland edges.

Peltaria isatoides is an herbaceous plant that can spread through seeds and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
