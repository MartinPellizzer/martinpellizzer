Panicum gobariense

Panicum gobariense, commonly known as the Gobari grass, is a species of grass native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous florets.

Panicum gobariense is often used for forage and erosion control due to its hardy nature.
