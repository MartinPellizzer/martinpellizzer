Peperomia corazonicola

Peperomia corazonicola, commonly known as the Heartleaf Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and often have a reddish or purple tint on the underside. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish, arranged in clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Peperomia corazonicola is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
