Pilea herniarifolia

Pilea herniarifolia, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a small, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Pilea herniarifolia is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
