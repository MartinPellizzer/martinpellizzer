Philodendron oxycardium

Philodendron oxycardium, commonly known as the Mexican heartleaf philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a waxy, glossy surface and a deep green color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Philodendron oxycardium is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
