Pendulina intricata

Pendulina intricata, commonly known as the lacey pendulum vine, is a unique plant characterized by its intricate, cascading foliage.

It features delicate, thread-like leaves that drape in elegant, flowing patterns, giving it a visually striking appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually displaying shades of white and pale pink.

Pendulina intricata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to create a lush, hanging effect in gardens.
