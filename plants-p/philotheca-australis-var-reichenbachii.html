Philotheca australis var. reichenbachii

Philotheca australis var. reichenbachii, commonly known as the narrow-leafed mountain tea, is a perennial shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features aromatic, narrow leaves with a rough texture and a distinctive, pungent scent when crushed. The plant produces small, white, tubular flowers that bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens and native plant collections.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as a component of local ecosystems and its use in traditional herbal medicine.
