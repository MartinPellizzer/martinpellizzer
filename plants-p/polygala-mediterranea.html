Polygala mediterranea

Polygala mediterranea, commonly known as the Mediterranean milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny environments. The plant features slender stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. Its leaves are usually narrow and slightly hairy, adapting to the arid conditions of its habitat.

It plays an important role in local ecosystems by providing food and habitat for various insects and small animals.
