Phanera franckii

Phanera franckii, commonly known as the African orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that often feature a mix of colors. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and help it anchor in the soil while absorbing nutrients.

Phanera franckii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
