Plectranthus erubescens

Plectranthus erubescens, commonly known as the red-leaf plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its striking red or purple leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and prefers well-drained soil and full sun. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant foliage and is also used in traditional medicine.

Plectranthus erubescens is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for gardens and container planting.
