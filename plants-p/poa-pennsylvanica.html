Poa pennsylvanica

Poa pennsylvanica, commonly known as Pennsylvania bluegrass, is a cool-season grass native to the northeastern United States.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green blades and tufted growth habit, making it a popular choice for lawns and pastures. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It has a deep root system that helps prevent soil erosion and improves soil structure.

Poa pennsylvanica is also valued for its ability to withstand moderate foot traffic and its low maintenance requirements.
