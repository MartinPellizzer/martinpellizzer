Potamogeton ulei

Potamogeton ulei, commonly known as water milfoil, is a submerged aquatic plant native to North America.

It has slender, branching stems that are often found in shallow, freshwater environments such as lakes and ponds. The plant produces small, scale-like leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stem. Its flowers are tiny and clustered in spike-like inflorescences, typically appearing in late summer.
