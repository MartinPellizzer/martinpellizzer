Protea erosa

Protea erosa, commonly known as the coastal protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences composed of numerous small flowers arranged in a spherical or conical shape. The plant typically grows in coastal regions, thriving in well-drained, sandy soils and full sunlight. Its flowers come in a range of colors, including pink, white, and red, and are often used in floral arrangements.

Protea erosa is also valued for its ecological role, providing nectar for pollinators and habitat for various insects.
