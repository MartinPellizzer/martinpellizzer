Ptilotus sericostachyus f. floribundus

Ptilotus sericostachyus f. floribundus, commonly known as the silverleaf flax or velvet flax, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery foliage and showy, spike-like inflorescences that range in color from pink to purple. The plant produces small, wind-pollinated flowers, and its seeds are dispersed by animals and wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
