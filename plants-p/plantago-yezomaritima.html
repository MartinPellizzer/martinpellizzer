Plantago yezomaritima

Plantago yezomaritima, commonly known as Yezomaritima plantain, is a species of flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its broad, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in spikes, which are typically borne on slender stalks. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands or along riverbanks.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional herbal remedies for various ailments.
