Peltanthera floribunda

Peltanthera floribunda, commonly known as the showy peltanthera, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This plant is often found in woodland habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
