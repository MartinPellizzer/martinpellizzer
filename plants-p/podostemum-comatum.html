Podostemum comatum

Podostemum comatum, commonly known as the aquatic basketweed, is a unique aquatic plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its small, floating leaves that are often clustered in dense, basket-like formations. The plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater habitats, such as streams and rivers, where it attaches to rocks or submerged surfaces. Its flowers are typically small and inconspicuous, with a distinctive two-lipped structure.
