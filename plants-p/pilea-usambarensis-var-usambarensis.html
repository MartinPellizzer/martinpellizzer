Pilea usambarensis var. usambarensis

Pilea usambarensis var. usambarensis, commonly known as the African violet, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Africa.

It features rosettes of dark green, velvety leaves with a distinctive waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
