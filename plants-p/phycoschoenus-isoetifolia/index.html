Phycoschoenus Isoetifolia

Island rush, scientifically known as Phycoschoenus isoetifolia, is a unique plant species native to specific island environments. It thrives in coastal areas where salt spray and sandy soils create challenging growing conditions. This plant is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and ability to stabilize fragile shorelines. Despite its resilience, it remains a rare and ecologically significant species. Its presence is often an indicator of healthy, undisturbed coastal ecosystems.
