Peperomia rotundifolia var. subelliptica

Peperomia rotundifolia var. subelliptica, commonly known as the Round-leaf Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features glossy, rounded leaves that are typically a deep green, often with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it suitable for use in hanging baskets or as a tabletop plant.
