Psittacanthus cinctus

Psittacanthus cinctus, commonly known as the golden button fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. Psittacanthus cinctus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
