Polybotrya kalbreyeri

Polybotrya kalbreyeri, commonly known as the Kalbreyer's spider-orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its intricate, spider-like appearance with long, thin petals and a distinctive, elongated lip. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils in mountainous regions. It blooms in late winter to early spring, producing clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
