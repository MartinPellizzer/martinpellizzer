Populus wenxianica

Populus wenxianica, commonly known as Wenxian poplar, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It belongs to the Salicaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and yellowish-green bark. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Populus wenxianica is considered a conservation concern.
