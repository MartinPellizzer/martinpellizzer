Papaver grande

Papaver grande, commonly known as the large poppy, is a striking flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant features a robust stem with lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its capsule fruit contains numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Papaver grande is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated for centuries in gardens and botanical collections.
