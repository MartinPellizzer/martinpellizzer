Piper acreanum

Piper acreanum, commonly known as the African pepper plant, is a flowering shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many pepper species. The plant features small, green, heart-shaped leaves and produces tiny, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruits are small, spherical berries that turn red when mature.

Piper acreanum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in some African cultures.
