Polygonatum × desoulavyi var. mediobracteatum

Polygonatum × desoulavyi var. mediobracteatum, commonly known as the Chinese Solomon's Seal, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asparagaceae family.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and produces clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers. The leaves are typically lanceolate, arranged in a basal rosette, and have a smooth, glossy surface. Its ornamental value is appreciated in horticulture for its attractive foliage and delicate floral display.
