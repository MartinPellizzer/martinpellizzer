Polygala chinensis var. hirsuta

Polygala chinensis var. hirsuta, commonly known as hairy Chinese milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that are typically pale yellow or greenish in color. The leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
