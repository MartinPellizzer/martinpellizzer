Phyllostachys puberula var. nana

Phyllostachys puberula var. nana, commonly known as the dwarf bamboo, is a compact variety of the bamboo genus Phyllostachys.

It is characterized by its short, stout stems and dense, dark green foliage. This plant is native to China and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its compact size and attractive appearance.

The variety is cold-hardy and can tolerate a range of environmental conditions, making it a versatile choice for gardeners.
