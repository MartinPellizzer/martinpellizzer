Pleiocraterium verticillare

Pleiocraterium verticillare, commonly known as the spiral leaf plant, is a unique species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it a visually striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Araliaceae and is known for its adaptability to moist, shaded environments.
