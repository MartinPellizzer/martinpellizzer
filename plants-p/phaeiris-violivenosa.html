Phaeiris violivenosa

Phaeiris violivenosa, commonly known as the violet viola, is a flowering plant belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking violet-blue petals and heart-shaped leaves. Native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
