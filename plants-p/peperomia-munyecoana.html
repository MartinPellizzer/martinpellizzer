Peperomia munyecoana

Peperomia munyecoana, commonly known as the Munyecoana peperomia, is a species native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub characterized by its glossy, ovate leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale purple, typically blooming in the spring or early summer. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia munyecoana is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
