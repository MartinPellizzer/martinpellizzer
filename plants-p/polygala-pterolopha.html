Polygala pterolopha

Polygala pterolopha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae, which is known for its distinctive floral structures. The plant features slender stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically narrow and linear. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish, with a unique shape that resembles a small, twisted tube.

It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.
