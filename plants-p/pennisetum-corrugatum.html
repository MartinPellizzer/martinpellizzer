Pennisetum corrugatum

Pennisetum corrugatum, commonly known as blue oat grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that are often tinged with blue or silver, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces fluffy, oat-like seed heads that are highly ornamental and attract birds and insects. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Pennisetum corrugatum is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
