Pleurothallis colorata

Pleurothallis colorata, commonly known as the colorful pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, colorful flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white, often in clusters. Its leaves are typically narrow and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

Pleurothallis colorata is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its striking blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
