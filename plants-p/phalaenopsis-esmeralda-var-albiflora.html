Phalaenopsis esmeralda var. albiflora

Phalaenopsis esmeralda var. albiflora, commonly known as the Emerald Dragon Orchid, is a hybrid orchid prized for its striking, waxy white flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features broad, dark green leaves and produces long, pendulous flower spikes that bloom multiple times throughout the year. Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for both indoor and greenhouse cultivation.

The variety is particularly noted for its elegant, almost translucent petals that resemble dragon scales.
