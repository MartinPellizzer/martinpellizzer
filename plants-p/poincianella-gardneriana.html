Poincianella gardneriana

Poincianella gardneriana, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in dense clusters, creating a striking visual display. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, narrow leaflets that give it a feathery appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Poincianella gardneriana is also valued for its ability to provide shade and its role in supporting local biodiversity.
