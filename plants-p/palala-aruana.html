Palala aruana

Palala aruana, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, vibrant red petals and a subtle fragrance. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in rainforests. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Palala aruana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
