Piper marowynense

Piper marowynense, commonly known as the Marowini pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its vine-like growth habit and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to red when mature.

Piper marowynense is valued for its aromatic leaves and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
