Podocarpium podocarpum var. szechuenense

Podocarpium podocarpum var. szechuenense, commonly known as the Sichuan podocarp, is a rare evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It belongs to the Podocarpaceae family and is characterized by its dense, needle-like leaves that provide year-round foliage. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain its seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in cool, moist climates with well-drained soil, often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable species for botanical research and conservation efforts.
