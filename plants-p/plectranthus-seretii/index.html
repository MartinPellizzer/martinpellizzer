Plectranthus Seretii

Plectranthus seretii is a unique and lesser-known plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the mint family and is characterized by its striking violet and green foliage. This plant is often grown as an ornamental due to its attractive appearance and easy care requirements. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial sunlight, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor environments. Despite its beauty, Plectranthus seretii remains relatively obscure in the horticultural world.
