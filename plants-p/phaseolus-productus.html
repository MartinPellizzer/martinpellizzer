Phaseolus productus

Phaseolus productus, commonly known as the scarlet runner bean, is a climbing legume native to the Americas.

It produces vibrant red or orange flowers and elongated, edible pods that contain round, yellow seeds. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets and thrives in warm, sunny conditions. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a food source.

Its botanical features make it well-suited for garden cultivation and agricultural use.
