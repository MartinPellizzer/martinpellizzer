Pandiaka involucrata

Pandiaka involucrata, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves and bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.

Pandiaka involucrata is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
