Pitcairnia camposii

Pitcairnia camposii, commonly known as the Brazilian pineapple plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a dense, water-holding structure at the center. The plant produces striking, long-lasting inflorescences with vibrant orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated as a decorative plant in many regions.
