Piper pulogense

Piper pulogense, commonly known as the Borneo pepper or Malayan pepper, is a flowering plant in the Piperaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Borneo, and thrives in tropical rainforests. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with opposite, ovate leaves that have a glossy surface. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in spikes, and it produces small, peppery fruits that are used in traditional medicine.

The plant has a pungent aroma and is valued for its medicinal properties in local communities.
