Primula oreocharis

Primula oreocharis, commonly known as the Yunnan primrose, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has lance-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and are arranged in clusters at the top of the stems.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
