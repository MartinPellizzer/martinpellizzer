Phreatia crassiuscula

Phreatia crassiuscula, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and mountainous regions. The plant features a short, underground rhizome with several fleshy, strap-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in humid, tropical gardens.
