Parodia aureispina var. erythrostaminea

Parodia aureispina var. erythrostaminea, commonly known as the red-spined pincushion cactus, is a compact, spineless cactus native to southern Africa.

It features a rounded, globular stem with a greenish-gray color and produces striking red or orange flowers during the summer months. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable ornamental plant.
