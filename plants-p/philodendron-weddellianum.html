Philodendron weddellianum

Philodendron weddellianum, commonly known as the Weddellianum philodendron, is a climbing tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by bright red berries, which are an important food source for local wildlife. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical plant collections.
