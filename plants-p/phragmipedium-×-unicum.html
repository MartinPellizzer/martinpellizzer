Phragmipedium × unicum

Phragmipedium × unicum, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a tropical plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Phragmipedium laeve and Phragmipedium sanderianum, resulting in a unique combination of traits from both parents. The plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers with intricate patterns and a strong, pleasant fragrance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Its popularity among orchid enthusiasts is due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
