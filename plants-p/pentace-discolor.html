Pentace discolor

Pentace discolor, commonly known as the African milk tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Pentaceae and is characterized by its unique, palmate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces a milky sap that has been used traditionally for its medicinal properties. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural collections.
