Paphiopedilum sinicum

Paphiopedilum sinicum, commonly known as the Chinese velvet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves and striking, showy flowers with a unique lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white with a deep purple or red lip.

Paphiopedilum sinicum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
