Pelargonium Iocastum

Pelargonium iocastum is a rare and striking species of geranium native to southern Africa. Known for its vibrant, showy flowers and unique leaf patterns, it has captured the attention of botanists and gardeners alike. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a favorite among horticultural enthusiasts. Its distinctive appearance and limited distribution make it a valuable addition to botanical collections. Despite its beauty, Pelargonium iocastum remains relatively obscure in the broader plant world.
