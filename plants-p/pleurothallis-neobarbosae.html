Pleurothallis neobarbosae

Pleurothallis neobarbosae, commonly known as the Neobarbosa orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often with a subtle greenish or pinkish hue. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, organic substrates for optimal growth.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicate and specialized orchid species.
