Pelargonium × mutabile

Pelargonium × mutabile, commonly known as the hybrid geranium, is a popular ornamental plant characterized by its showy, often bicolor flowers.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is known for its ability to produce flowers in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically has lobed leaves and thrives in full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for gardens and containers. It is also valued for its frost resistance and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.

Pelargonium × mutabile is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and ease of cultivation.
