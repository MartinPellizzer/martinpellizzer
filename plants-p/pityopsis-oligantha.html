Pityopsis oligantha

Pityopsis oligantha, commonly known as the narrowleaf goldeneye, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.
