Prunus oblonga

Prunus oblonga, commonly known as the Chinese date or longan, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, ovate leaves and fragrant, white flowers. The tree produces small, round fruits that are edible and have a sweet, juicy flavor. Prunus oblonga thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit production.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both horticulture and traditional medicine.
