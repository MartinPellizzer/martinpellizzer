Pseudosasa owatarii

Pseudosasa owatarii, commonly known as the Japanese bamboo or Owatari bamboo, is a clumping bamboo species native to Japan.

It features slender, upright culms that are typically green and often have a slight reddish tint. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are bright green and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil.

Pseudosasa owatarii is valued for its elegant appearance and is commonly used in ornamental gardening and landscape design.
