Phalaenopsis mariae var. alba

Phalaenopsis mariae var. alba, commonly known as the white phalaenopsis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, long-lasting white flowers that bloom along the stem. The plant has thick, fleshy roots and broad, dark green leaves that are often pleated. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect sunlight.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and ease of care.
