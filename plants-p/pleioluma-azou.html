Pleioluma azou

Pleioluma azou, commonly known as the Azou orchid, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically features slender stems and lance-shaped leaves, which help it thrive in humid environments. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pale pink.

Pleioluma azou is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to grow in shaded conditions.
