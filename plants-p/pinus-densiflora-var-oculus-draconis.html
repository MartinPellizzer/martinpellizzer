Pinus densiflora var. oculus-draconis

Pinus densiflora var. oculus-draconis, commonly known as the Dragon's Eye Pine, is a rare variant of the Japanese black pine.

It is characterized by its distinctive, deeply furrowed bark and dense, dark green foliage. The tree exhibits a unique growth pattern, often forming a compact, rounded shape with a thick crown. Its cones are typically small and elongated, containing edible seeds that are a food source for various wildlife.

This variant is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and resilience in diverse environmental conditions.
