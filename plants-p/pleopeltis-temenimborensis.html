Pleopeltis temenimborensis

Pleopeltis temenimborensis, commonly known as the Temenimborensis fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant has a unique rhizome system that supports its growth in humid, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically bright green and can reach lengths of up to several meters.
