Pyrethrum barrelieri

Pyrethrum barrelieri, commonly known as the African pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant produces small, spherical seed heads that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Pyrethrum barrelieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated for its potential in natural pest control due to the presence of pyrethrins in its flowers.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners in arid regions.
