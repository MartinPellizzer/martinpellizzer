Phaseoloides glaucescens

Phaseoloides glaucescens, commonly known as the silverleaf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
