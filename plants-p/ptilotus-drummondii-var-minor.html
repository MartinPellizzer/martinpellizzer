Ptilotus drummondii var. minor

Ptilotus drummondii var. minor, commonly known as the lesser spinifex, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Australia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with wiry stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry conditions with minimal water requirements.

Its unique appearance and ecological role make it an important component of its native habitat.
