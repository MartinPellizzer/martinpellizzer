Parodia aureispina var. grandiflora

Parodia aureispina var. grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a spiny, ribbed stem with a pale green to bluish-gray coloration. The plant produces large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in rock gardens.
