Peucedanum sativum

Peucedanum sativum, commonly known as cow parsley or parsley weed, is a flowering plant in the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small, clustered white or greenish-yellow flowers. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia and thrives in moist, temperate climates. It often grows in meadows, along roadsides, and in disturbed soils.
