Pyrrosia stolzii

Pyrrosia stolzii, commonly known as the Stolz's rock fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, rhizomatous growth habit and the production of small, clustered fronds. The fronds are typically leathery and have a dark green color with a glossy appearance. This plant is often found growing on rocks or tree trunks in humid, shaded environments.

Pyrrosia stolzii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
