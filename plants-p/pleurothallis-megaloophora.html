Pleurothallis megaloophora

Pleurothallis megaloophora, commonly known as the large-lobed pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is a small to medium-sized epiphyte, typically growing on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It is characterized by its intricate inflorescence and is often found in tropical cloud forests.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
