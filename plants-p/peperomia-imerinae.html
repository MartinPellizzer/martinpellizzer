Peperomia imerinae

Peperomia imerinae, commonly known as the Crimson Queen Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with striking red veins, making it a visually appealing houseplant. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia imerinae is relatively low maintenance, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.
