Pearcea abunda

Pearcea abunda, commonly known as the abundant pearcea, is a perennial herb native to specific regions in South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale green, which bloom in the spring. Its fruit is a small, fleshy capsule that contains several seeds.

Pearcea abunda is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
