Pleurothallis pleurothalloides var. major

Pleurothallis pleurothalloides var. major, commonly known as the large-leaf pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in the understory of forests. Its leaves are usually elongated and lance-shaped, with a smooth texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
