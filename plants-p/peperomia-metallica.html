Peperomia metallica

Peperomia metallica, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to South America.

It features thick, fleshy, waxy leaves that are typically silver-gray in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white, which appear in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia metallica is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
