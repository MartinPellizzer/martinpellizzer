Pavonia kunthii var. mollis

Pavonia kunthii var. mollis, commonly known as the soft pavonia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage and clusters of pink to red bract-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, rich soils and thrives in partial to full shade. It is a perennial shrub that can reach heights of up to 3 feet.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping in shaded garden environments.
