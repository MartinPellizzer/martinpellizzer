Pilea cordistipulata

Pilea cordistipulata, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are heart-shaped with a slightly wavy margin. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by distinctive, round, red berries. It is a compact, low-maintenance plant that thrives in indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its ornamental foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
