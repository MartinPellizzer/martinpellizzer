Phlomis scythica

Phlomis scythica, commonly known as Scythian mullein, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, gray-green, fuzzy leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or dry garden environments.

Phlomis scythica is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
