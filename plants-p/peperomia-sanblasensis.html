Peperomia sanblasensis

Peperomia sanblasensis, commonly known as the San Blas peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to Central America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, usually in shades of green or red. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.

Its low maintenance and attractive foliage make it a favored ornamental plant.
