Pennilabium kidmancoxii

Pennilabium kidmancoxii, commonly known as the Kidman's lip.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to arid regions of Australia. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, clustered in spikes, and usually have a distinctive purple or pink hue.

It is adapted to survive in low-water environments, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
