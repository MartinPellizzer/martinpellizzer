Prescottia pubescens

Prescottia pubescens, commonly known as the hairy Prescottia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, fragrant flowers. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. It typically grows in humid, shaded environments in the Andes region.

Prescottia pubescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
