Panicum prieurii

Panicum prieurii, commonly known as the golden panicgrass, is a warm-season grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and striking golden-yellow inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color.

Panicum prieurii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and as a wildlife habitat.
