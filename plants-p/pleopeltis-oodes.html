Pleopeltis oodes

Pleopeltis oodes, commonly known as the Oodes fern, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, leathery fronds that are typically divided into narrow, lance-shaped segments. The plant produces small, clustered spores on the underside of its leaves, a feature typical of ferns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its unique morphology and ecological adaptations make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
