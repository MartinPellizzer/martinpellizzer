Potentilla pedata f. glabrescens

Potentilla pedata f. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth potentilla, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its dense, rounded form and small, glossy leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in early summer, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact size and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a hardy, decorative plant.
