Pleurothallis xenion

Pleurothallis xenion, commonly known as the silver moon orchid, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, elongated flowers that often have a silvery or pale green hue. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are small but striking, often featuring intricate patterns and a fragrant scent.

Pleurothallis xenion is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique beauty.
