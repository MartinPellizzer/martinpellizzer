Phyteuma spicatum var. tetramerum

Phyteuma spicatum var. tetramerum, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple and pink. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
