Prosaptia stenobasis

Prosaptia stenobasis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered flowers. Native to certain regions of Africa, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils, often found in wetlands or marshy areas.
