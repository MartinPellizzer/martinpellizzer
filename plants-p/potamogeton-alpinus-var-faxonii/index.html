Potamogeton Alpinus Var. Faxonii

Potamogeton alpinus var. faxonii is a rare aquatic plant native to high-altitude lakes in the Alps and other mountainous regions. It thrives in cold, clear waters with low nutrient levels and minimal disturbance. This variant is distinguished by its compact growth habit and unique morphological features compared to the standard species. Its presence often indicates pristine water conditions and ecological stability. Due to its limited distribution and sensitivity to environmental changes, it is considered a valuable indicator species for aquatic ecosystems.
