Pleurothallis perennis

Pleurothallis perennis, commonly known as the everlasting orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often blooming throughout the year. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and resilience.
