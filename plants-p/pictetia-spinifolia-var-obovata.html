Pictetia spinifolia var. obovata

Pictetia spinifolia var. obovata, commonly known as the broad-leafed pictetia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are obovate in shape, with a smooth or slightly ribbed surface. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
