Parasamanea landakensis

Parasamanea landakensis, commonly known as the Madagascar fan palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that radiate from a central stem, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant has a robust trunk that can grow quite tall, often reaching several meters in height. Its leaves are typically green and broad, providing a lush canopy that supports various forms of wildlife.

This species is well adapted to the humid and warm climate of its native habitat.
