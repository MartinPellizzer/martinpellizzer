Philadelphus delavayi var. delavayi

Philadelphus delavayi var. delavayi, commonly known as the Delavay's mock orange, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, often in clusters. The plant has dark green, elliptical leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its pleasant fragrance and showy blooms.
