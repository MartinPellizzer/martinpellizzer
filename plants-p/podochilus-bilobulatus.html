Podochilus bilobulatus

Podochilus bilobulatus, commonly known as the two-lobed orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in clusters. Its leaves are usually ovate to lanceolate, with a smooth surface and a waxy texture.

The orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
