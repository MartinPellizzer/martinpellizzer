Picea abies var. elegans

Picea abies var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant fir, is a variant of the Norway spruce characterized by its refined appearance.

It features slender, upright cones and elongated, needle-like leaves that are typically a deep green. This variety is prized for its ornamental value in horticulture due to its graceful form and dense foliage. It thrives in cool, moist climates and prefers well-drained, acidic soils.

The elegant fir is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various garden settings.
