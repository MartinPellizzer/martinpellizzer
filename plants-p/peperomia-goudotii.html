Peperomia goudotii

Peperomia goudotii, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features thick, waxy, and textured leaves that range in color from deep green to reddish hues, often with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia goudotii is relatively low maintenance and drought tolerant, requiring minimal watering once established.
