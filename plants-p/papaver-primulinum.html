Papaver primulinum

Papaver primulinum, commonly known as the primrose poppy, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers, which bloom in early spring. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Papaver primulinum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
