Polygala sparsiflora var. ukirensis

Polygala sparsiflora var. ukirensis, commonly known as the Kieri polygala, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, and it produces distinctive, spiral-shaped seed pods.

It is a rare and endemic species, often found in specific regions of the Western Cape.
