Pilea pendula

Pilea pendula, commonly known as the weeping pilea, is a unique houseplant characterized by its trailing stems and clusters of small, heart-shaped leaves.

Native to tropical regions of Mexico, it thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil. The plant's foliage is typically a vibrant green, with some varieties displaying variegated patterns. It is known for its ability to cascade beautifully from hanging baskets or shelves.

Pilea pendula is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
