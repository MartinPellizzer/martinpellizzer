Pithecellobium jiringa

Pithecellobium jiringa, commonly known as the Jiringa or Jiringa tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to parts of Southeast Asia.

The plant is often found in moist, tropical forests and is adapted to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.
