Piper sirenasense

Piper sirenasense, commonly known as the Sirena's Sense pepper, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants such as the common black pepper. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in spikes, and its leaves are typically heart-shaped with a glossy surface. The fruit, which is a small, round berry, contains the seeds used for its aromatic properties.

It is valued for its unique flavor and is often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.
