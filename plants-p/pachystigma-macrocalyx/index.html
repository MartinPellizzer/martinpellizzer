Pachystigma Macrocalyx

Pachystigma macrocalyx is a unique and striking plant known for its large, showy flowers. Native to the arid regions of South Africa, it thrives in harsh, dry environments. This species belongs to the Aizoaceae family, commonly called the ice plant family. Its thick, fleshy leaves help it store water, making it well adapted to drought. The plant's vibrant blooms make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
