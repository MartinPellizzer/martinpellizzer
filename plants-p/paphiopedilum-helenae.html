Paphiopedilum helenae

Paphiopedilum helenae, commonly known as Helen's lady's slipper, is a rare and exotic orchid native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It is characterized by its striking, slipper-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple with intricate patterns. The plant has a terrestrial growth habit, with a fleshy rhizome and broad, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in cool, humid environments with partial shade and well-draining soil.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
