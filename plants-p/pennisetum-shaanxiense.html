Pennisetum shaanxiense

Pennisetum shaanxiense, commonly known as the Shaanxi grass, is a perennial grass native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces panicles of small, fluffy flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Pennisetum shaanxiense is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in wetland restoration projects.
