Paphiopedilum chamberlainianum var. latifolium

Paphiopedilum chamberlainianum var. latifolium, commonly known as the broad-leafed slipper orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, leathery leaves and distinctive slipper-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white with a prominent lip.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in horticulture.
