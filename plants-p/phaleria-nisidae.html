Phaleria nisidae

Phaleria nisidae, commonly known as the Nisidae leaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a glossy appearance.

The flowers are small and clustered, followed by oval-shaped fruits that contain a single seed.
