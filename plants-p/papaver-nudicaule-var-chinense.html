Papaver nudicaule var. chinense

Papaver nudicaule var. chinense, commonly known as the Chinese poppy, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its delicate, papery petals that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in temperate climates. It produces a capsule fruit containing numerous small seeds.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical significance in traditional Chinese medicine.
