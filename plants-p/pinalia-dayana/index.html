Pinalia Dayana

Dayana's pinalia, scientifically known as Pinalia dayana, is a rare and striking plant native to the humid cloud forests of South America. This unique species is named in honor of the botanist Dayana Morales, who played a pivotal role in its discovery and classification. With its vibrant green leaves and delicate, star-shaped flowers, it stands out as a botanical marvel in its ecosystem. Pinalia dayana thrives in cool, moist environments and is highly sensitive to changes in its habitat. Due to its rarity and ecological significance, conservation efforts are crucial to ensuring its survival.
