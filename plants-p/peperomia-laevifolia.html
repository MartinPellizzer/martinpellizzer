Peperomia laevifolia

Peperomia laevifolia, commonly known as the smooth-leaf peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, glossy, ovate leaves that are smooth and waxy, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of greenish-white, which are typically borne on short spikes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage, it is often used as a decorative plant in both homes and offices.
