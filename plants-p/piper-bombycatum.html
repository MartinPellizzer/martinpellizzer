Piper bombycatum

Piper bombycatum, commonly known as the African black pepper, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by black, peppery fruits that are used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly aromatic scent.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in local remedies for various ailments.
