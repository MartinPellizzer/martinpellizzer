Paepalanthus ramosissimus

It is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its tall, branched stems and showy, spathaceous inflorescences. Paepalanthus ramosissimus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and thrives in well-drained, sunny conditions.
