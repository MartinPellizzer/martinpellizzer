Plectranthus daoi

Plectranthus daoi, commonly known as the African violet, is a herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or blue in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate light conditions.

Plectranthus daoi is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.
