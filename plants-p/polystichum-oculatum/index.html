Polystichum Oculatum

Polystichum oculatum is a striking fern known for its distinctive appearance and hardy nature. Native to temperate regions of the northern hemisphere, it thrives in moist, shady environments. Its leaves, or fronds, are deeply divided and often feature a unique eye-like pattern on the underside. This plant is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and ease of care. It adds a touch of elegance to both natural and cultivated landscapes.
