Polypodium hornei

Polypodium hornei, commonly known as the Horsfield's water fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, frond-like leaves that are typically bioluminescent under certain conditions. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests. Its rhizomes are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant's unique appearance and ecological role make it an important species in its native habitats.
