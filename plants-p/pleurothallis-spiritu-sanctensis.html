Pleurothallis spiritu-sanctensis

Pleurothallis spiritu-sanctensis, commonly known as the Holy Spirit Pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments within cloud forests. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate floral structure.

Due to its habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
