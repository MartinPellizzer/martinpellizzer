Populus × tomentosa var. multilenticellia

Populus × tomentosa var. multilenticellia, commonly known as the Chinese white poplar, is a variant of the poplar tree native to China.

It is characterized by its smooth, white bark that often develops lenticels, giving it a distinctive appearance. The tree has opposite, simple leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers in catkins, which are typical of the Populus genus.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
