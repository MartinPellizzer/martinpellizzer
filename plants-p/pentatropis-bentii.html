Pentatropis bentii

Pentatropis bentii, commonly known as the bentii pentatropis, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to certain regions of Central America and is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Pentatropis bentii is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories.
