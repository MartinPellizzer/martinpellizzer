Platanthera gracilis

Platanthera gracilis, commonly known as the slender lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to moist woodlands and meadows in North America.

The plant has a fibrous root system and produces a single, narrow leaf that is often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.
