Pleurothallis erymnochila

Pleurothallis erymnochila, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, velvety purple flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually dark green and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts and botanists.
