Portulaca schenkii

Portulaca schenkii, commonly known as the Schenk's moss rose, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Portulacaceae and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Its flowers, which range in color from pink to red, bloom in summer and are often used in ornamental gardening.

Portulaca schenkii is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
