Pilea shaferi

Pilea shaferi, commonly known as the Shaver's begonia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and cream. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers in spring, which are borne on short, upright stalks. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and ease of care have made it a sought-after plant among plant enthusiasts.
