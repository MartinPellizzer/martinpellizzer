Pavonia glechomifolia f. karachensis

Pavonia glechomifolia f. karachensis, commonly known as the Karachenskaya pavonia, is a variant of the pavonia plant native to Central Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of small, pink to red flowers. Its botanical characteristics include a robust root system and a branched stem structure.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to harsh climates.
