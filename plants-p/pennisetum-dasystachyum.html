Pennisetum dasystachyum

Pennisetum dasystachyum, commonly known as giant feather grass, is a tall, ornamental grass native to Africa.

It features long, narrow leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adding a striking visual element to gardens. The plant produces feathery plumes that bloom in late summer, creating a dramatic display. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its upright growth habit and minimal maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for landscaping.
