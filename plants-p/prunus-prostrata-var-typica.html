Prunus prostrata var. typica

Prunus prostrata var. typica, commonly known as the prostrate cherry or dwarf cherry, is a low-growing shrub native to China.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces delicate white or pink flowers in early spring, followed by small, edible berries. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact form and attractive foliage.

The species is well-suited for rock gardens and as a ground cover in shady areas.
