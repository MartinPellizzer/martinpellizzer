Peperomia santanana

Peperomia santanana, commonly known as the Santa Ana peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Brazil.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slightly textured surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Peperomia santanana is known for its ability to tolerate low light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it an ideal plant for beginners.
