Penthorum sedoides var. chinense

Penthorum sedoides var. chinense, commonly known as Chinese water dropwort, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, upright stems with opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are typically reddish or purple in color. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters at the base of the leaves, which bloom in early summer. Its roots are fibrous and contain compounds that have been used in traditional medicine.

This species is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
