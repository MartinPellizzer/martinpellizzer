Ponerorchis cucullata var. calcicola

Ponerorchis cucullata var. calcicola, commonly known as the calcicola orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or limestone soils, adapting to alkaline conditions. The plant features a short, stocky rhizome with a dense cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, often with a subtle fragrance.

This variety is prized for its resilience and unique habitat preferences.
