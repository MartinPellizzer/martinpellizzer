Pongamia chinensis

Pongamia chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese pongam tree, is a deciduous leguminous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are typically trifoliate or pinnate, and its distinctive yellowish-green flowers. The tree produces elongated pods containing seeds that are rich in oil, making it valuable for biofuel and industrial applications. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

The plant also has medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
