Ptilotus declinatus

Ptilotus declinatus, commonly known as the blue bush morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue or purple flowers that bloom in the morning and close by midday. The plant has a sprawling growth habit with hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Ptilotus declinatus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
