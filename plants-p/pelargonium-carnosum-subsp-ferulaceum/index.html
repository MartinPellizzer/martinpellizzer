Pelargonium Carnosum Subsp. Ferulaceum

Pelargonium carnosum subsp. ferulaceum is a unique and lesser-known species within the Geraniaceae family. Native to southern Africa, this plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing remarkable adaptations to harsh conditions. Its succulent leaves and compact growth habit make it an attractive choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in horticultural circles. This article explores its characteristics, habitat, and potential for cultivation.
