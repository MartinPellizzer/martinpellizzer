Peperomia hydnostachya

Peperomia hydnostachya, commonly known as the water peperomia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, ovate leaves that are often dark green with a slight waxy texture. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, typically appearing in late spring or early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia hydnostachya is relatively low maintenance, requiring moderate watering and minimal fertilization.
