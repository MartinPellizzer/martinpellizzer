Prunus Maritima Var. Gravesii

Coastal cherry, scientifically known as Prunus maritima var. gravesii, is a hardy shrub that thrives in coastal environments. This plant is native to the eastern coast of North America, particularly in regions with sandy soils and salt spray. It is well adapted to withstand harsh conditions such as strong winds and salty air. The Coastal cherry produces small, edible fruits that are an important food source for wildlife. Its resilience and beauty make it a valuable addition to coastal ecosystems.
