Polaskia chende

Polaskia chende, commonly known as the Chende prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, spiny stems that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant produces large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it has a deep root system that allows it to thrive in arid environments.

Polaskia chende is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens in warm climates.
