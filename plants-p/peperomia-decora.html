Peperomia decora

Peperomia decora, commonly known as the Decorative Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink, giving it an ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia decora is relatively low-maintenance and suitable for both tabletop displays and hanging baskets.
