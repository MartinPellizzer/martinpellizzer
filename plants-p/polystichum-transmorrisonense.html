Polystichum transmorrisonense

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically dark green with a metallic sheen. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments, often found in shaded forest understories where it can receive adequate humidity. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species in conservation efforts.
