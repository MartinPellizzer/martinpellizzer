Pyrola elata

Pyrola elata, commonly known as the white wintergreen, is a low-growing evergreen perennial native to the northern forests of North America.

The plant produces small, bell-shaped white flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded, moist environments.

Pyrola elata is a hardy plant that can survive harsh winter conditions due to its waxy leaf coating.
