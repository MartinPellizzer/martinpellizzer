Parodia liliputana

Parodia liliputana, commonly known as the miniature moon cactus, is a compact cactus species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its small, rounded stem and vibrant, colorful flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a shallow root system and thrives in well-draining soil. It requires bright sunlight and minimal water to prevent root rot.

Due to its small size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for container gardening.
