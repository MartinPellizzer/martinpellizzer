Piper constipatispicum var. tonsum

Piper constipatispicum var. tonsum, commonly known as the Tonsum pepper plant, is a perennial vine native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper species such as black pepper and long pepper. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are typically harvested when they turn from green to red or yellow. Its leaves are heart-shaped with a slightly serrated edge, and the plant has a distinctive, pungent aroma.

The botanical characteristics of this variety make it a unique subject of study in tropical botany and horticulture.
