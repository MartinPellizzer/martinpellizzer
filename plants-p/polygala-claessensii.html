Polygala claessensii

Polygala claessensii, commonly known as the Cape Flats polygala, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, spindly stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom during the spring and summer seasons.

It is a relatively rare species and is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
