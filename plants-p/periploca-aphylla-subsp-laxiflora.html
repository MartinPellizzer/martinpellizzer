Periploca aphylla subsp. laxiflora

Periploca aphylla subsp.

laxiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered periploca, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that dehisces to release small, winged seeds.

This species is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient component of its native ecosystems.
