Philodendron ligulatum var. ovatum

Philodendron ligulatum var. ovatum, commonly known as the heartleaf philodendron, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly variegated appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by green, berry-like fruits that turn red when mature. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Its rapid growth and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
