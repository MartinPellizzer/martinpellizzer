Pteris zippelii

Pteris zippelii, commonly known as the Zippel's water fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce clusters of small, fertile spore-bearing leaves.

Pteris zippelii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
