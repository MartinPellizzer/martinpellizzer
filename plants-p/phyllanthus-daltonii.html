Phyllanthus daltonii

Phyllanthus daltonii, commonly known as Dalton's spurge, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which includes many species with milky sap. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, featuring opposite, simple leaves that are often elliptical in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in cyathia, a characteristic feature of the Euphorbiaceae family.

The plant is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.
