Phyllagathis rotundifolia

Phyllagathis rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaved phyllagathis, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its small, round leaves and produces small, white or pale pink flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
