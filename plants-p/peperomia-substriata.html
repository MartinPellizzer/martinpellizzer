Peperomia substriata

Peperomia substriata, commonly known as the Striped Peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with distinctive white or silver markings along the midribs, giving it a striking visual appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Peperomia substriata is valued for its ornamental foliage and air-purifying qualities.
