Parinari nonda

Parinari nonda, commonly known as the yellow-leaf parinari, is a small to medium-sized tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. The species is also noted for its ability to thrive in partial shade and its role in local ecosystems.
