Pimelea linifolia

Pimelea linifolia, commonly known as the silky pimelea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub with small, needle-like leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Pimelea linifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native gardens.
