Peperomia maribiana

Peperomia maribiana, commonly known as the Maribian Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, providing a dense, foliage-rich appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its slow growth and minimal care requirements, it is well-suited for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
