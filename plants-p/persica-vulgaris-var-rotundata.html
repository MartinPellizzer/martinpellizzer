Persica vulgaris var. rotundata

Persica vulgaris var. rotundata, commonly known as the round-leaved peach.

It is a variant of the peach tree characterized by its rounded, broad leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its fruits are typically smaller and less sweet compared to common peaches.

This variety is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its unique foliage.
