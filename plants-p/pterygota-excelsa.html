Pterygota excelsa

Pterygota excelsa, commonly known as the royal fern, is a species of fern that belongs to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its large, pinnately compound fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a creeping rhizome that produces both vegetative and reproductive shoots. Its leaves are typically bright green and have a distinctive, elegant appearance.

Pterygota excelsa thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
