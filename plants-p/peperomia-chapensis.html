Peperomia chapensis

Peperomia chapensis, commonly known as the Chapman's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features fleshy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters. The plant is well adapted to humid environments and thrives in partial to full shade. Its ornamental value lies in its attractive foliage rather than its flowers.

Peperomia chapensis is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
