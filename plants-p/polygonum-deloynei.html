Polygonum deloynei

Polygonum deloynei, commonly known as Deloyne's knotweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants commonly referred to as buckwheats and smartweeds. The plant is characterized by its stout, reddish stems and heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. Its small, greenish-white flowers appear in clusters and bloom from late spring to early summer.

Polygonum deloynei is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and stream banks.
