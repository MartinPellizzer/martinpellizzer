Paphiopedilum hennisianum

Paphiopedilum hennisianum, commonly known as the Hennisian velvet slipper orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped pouch that serves as a pollination trap. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments with rich, well-drained soil. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with intricate patterns that aid in attracting pollinators.

Paphiopedilum hennisianum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
