Panicum enslinii

Panicum enslinii, commonly known as the Enslin panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, erect culms. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, two-lobed spikelets. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue when fresh.

Panicum enslinii is often found in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.
