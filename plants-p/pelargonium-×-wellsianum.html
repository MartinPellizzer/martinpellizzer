Pelargonium × wellsianum

Pelargonium × wellsianum, commonly known as the lemon-scented geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its fragrant, lemon-scented leaves and showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to red. Native to South Africa, this plant thrives in warm, sunny conditions and well-drained soil. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and aromatic foliage.

Pelargonium × wellsianum is also used in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
