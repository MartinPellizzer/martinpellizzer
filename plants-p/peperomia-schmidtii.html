Peperomia schmidtii

Peperomia schmidtii, commonly known as the Schmidt's peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It features dense, glossy foliage with ovate to lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia schmidtii is known for its low maintenance and ability to adapt well to a variety of indoor environments.
