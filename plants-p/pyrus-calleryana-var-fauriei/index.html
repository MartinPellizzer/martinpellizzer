Pyrus Calleryana Var. Fauriei

Pyrus calleryana var. fauriei is a popular ornamental tree known for its striking white blossoms and vibrant fall color. Often mistaken for the Chinese elm, it is a member of the rose family and is widely planted in urban landscapes. This variety is prized for its adaptability to various soil types and climate conditions. Its rapid growth and dense foliage make it a common choice for street trees and privacy screens. However, it has also become an invasive species in some regions, raising concerns about its ecological impact.
