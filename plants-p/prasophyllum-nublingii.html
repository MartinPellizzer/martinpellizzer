Prasophyllum nublingii

Prasophyllum nublingii, commonly known as the Nubling's leek orchid, is an Australian native plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is a terrestrial herb with a rosette of grass-like leaves and produces a single flowering stem. The flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a distinctive, narrow spur. It is found in heathlands and open forests, often in nutrient-poor soils.

This species is listed as vulnerable due to habitat loss and land clearing.
