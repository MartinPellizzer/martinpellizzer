Pogonia commersonii

Pogonia commersonii, commonly known as the Commerson's pink, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking pink and white flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces a single spike of flowers with a distinctive, fragrant scent.

Pogonia commersonii is considered a rare and endangered species, requiring specific habitat conditions for successful growth.
