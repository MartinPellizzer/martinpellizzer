Parasenecio phyllolepis

Parasenecio phyllolepis, commonly known as the leafy parasenecio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its foliage is broad and leathery, often forming a dense rosette.

The flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters, contributing to its ornamental value.
