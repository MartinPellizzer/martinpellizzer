Peperomia turquinana

Peperomia turquinana, commonly known as the Turquinana peppergrass, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that emerge from the leaf axils, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for container gardens and shaded landscapes.
