Phyllanthus lauterbachianus

Phyllanthus lauterbachianus, commonly known as the African spurge, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a shrub native to southern Africa, characterized by its small, clustered leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain seeds coated in a sticky substance. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
