Piper chuarense

Piper chuarense, commonly known as the Chuarense pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are followed by elongated, slender berries that ripen to a bright red color.

Piper chuarense is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
