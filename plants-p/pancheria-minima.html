Pancheria minima

Pancheria minima, commonly known as the small pancheria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Pancheria minima is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
