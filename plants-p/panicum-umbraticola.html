Panicum umbraticola

Panicum umbraticola, commonly known as the shadow grass, is a unique species adapted to low-light environments.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, delicate spikelets that are typically green or pale in color. It thrives in moist, shaded areas such as forest floors or rocky crevices.

Its ability to tolerate shade makes it an important component of understory vegetation in certain ecosystems.
