Peperomia fuscispica

Peperomia fuscispica, commonly known as the dark-leaved peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are often adorned with silver or white markings, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and white, which are typically borne on short, clustered inflorescences. Peperomia fuscispica thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and collection.
