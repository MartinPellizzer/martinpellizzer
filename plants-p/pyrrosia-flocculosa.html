Pyrrosia flocculosa

Pyrrosia flocculosa, commonly known as the silver-velvet fern, is a fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Polypodiaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, soft, and fuzzy fronds. The plant typically grows on rocks or tree trunks in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are pinnately divided and often covered in a fine, silvery down.
