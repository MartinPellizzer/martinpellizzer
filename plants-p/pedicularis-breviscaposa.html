Pedicularis breviscaposa

Pedicularis breviscaposa, commonly known as the short-capped lousewort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orobanchaceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and small, bilaterally symmetric flowers that are typically pink or purple in color.
