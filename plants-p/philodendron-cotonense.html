Philodendron cotonense

Philodendron cotonense, commonly known as the Cotonense Philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with light green and cream patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by yellowish-white berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its rapid growth and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
