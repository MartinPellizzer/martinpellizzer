Pyrrosia gralla

Pyrrosia gralla, commonly known as the golden shield fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The fronds are usually leathery and dark green, with a distinctive golden hue on the underside. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or rocks.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in a tropical or subtropical setting.
