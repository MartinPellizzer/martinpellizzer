Polyscalis

Polyscalis, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers that bloom in yellow or green hues. The fruit of Polyscalis is a small, hairy pod containing several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and medicinal contexts.
