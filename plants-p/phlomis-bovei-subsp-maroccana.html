Phlomis bovei subsp. maroccana

Phlomis bovei subsp.

maroccana, commonly known as Moroccan milkweed, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and hairy leaves.

The plant produces showy, pink to purple flower clusters that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
