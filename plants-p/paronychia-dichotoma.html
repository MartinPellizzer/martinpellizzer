Paronychia dichotoma

Paronychia dichotoma, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a taproot system and produces achenes with pappus for wind dispersal. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy environments.

Its botanical features make it an attractive addition to rock gardens and native plant collections.
