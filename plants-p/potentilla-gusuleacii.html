Potentilla gusuleacii

Potentilla gusuleacii, commonly known as the Carpathian cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky or mountainous areas, preferring well-drained soils. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Potentilla gusuleacii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats.
