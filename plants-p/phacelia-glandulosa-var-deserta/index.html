Phacelia Glandulosa Var. Deserta

Phacelia glandulosa var. deserta is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern united states. it belongs to the borage family and is known for its vibrant blue to purple blooms that appear in late spring. this variant is particularly adapted to harsh desert conditions, thriving in sandy and rocky soils with minimal water. its unique morphology and ecological role make it an important species for local biodiversity. due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
