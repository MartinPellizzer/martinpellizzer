Pternandra cogniauxii

Pternandra cogniauxii, commonly known as the African sandthorn, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in dry, sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, and its leaves are alternate and pinnately compound. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

The fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that contains seeds.
