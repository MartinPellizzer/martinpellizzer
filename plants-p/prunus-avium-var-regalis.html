Prunus avium var. regalis

Prunus avium var. regalis, commonly known as the Royal Cherry, is a cultivar of the sweet cherry tree.

It is characterized by its large, dark red fruits with a rich, sweet flavor. The plant is deciduous, shedding its leaves in the fall, and thrives in temperate climates. It has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for smaller gardens.

The flowers are white and fragrant, appearing in early spring before the leaves.
