Phyllanthus allemii

Phyllanthus allemii, commonly known as African spurge, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family and typically grows as a shrub or small tree. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in cyathia, which are characteristic of the Euphorbiaceae family.

Phyllanthus allemii is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
