Pilea pteropodon

Pilea pteropodon, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It is a low-growing perennial with heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it an ideal choice for indoor gardening.
