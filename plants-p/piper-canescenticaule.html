Piper canescenticaule

Piper canescenticaule, commonly known as the rough-stemmed pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its hairy, rough-textured stems and small, clustered leaves. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by small, round berries.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
