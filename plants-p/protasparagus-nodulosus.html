Protasparagus nodulosus

Protasparagus nodulosus, commonly known as the nodular asparagus, is a unique plant belonging to the Asparagaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, branching structure and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant produces pale green to yellowish flowers, which are typically arranged in compact clusters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its overall appearance.

Protasparagus nodulosus is often found in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.
