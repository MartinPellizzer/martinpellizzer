Phlomis ornata

Phlomis ornata, commonly known as the ornate mullein, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky or sandy soils. The plant features tall, erect stems with soft, velvety leaves that are gray-green in color. Its showy, clustered flowers are usually pink to purple in hue, appearing in late summer.

Phlomis ornata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used historically for its medicinal properties.
