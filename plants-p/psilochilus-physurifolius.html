Psilochilus physurifolius

Psilochilus physurifolius, commonly known as the velvet leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant features dark green, velvety leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape, with a dense pubescence. Its inflorescence consists of a raceme of small, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers.

Psilochilus physurifolius is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
