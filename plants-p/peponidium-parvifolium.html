Peponidium parvifolium

Peponidium parvifolium, commonly known as the small-leaf melon, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and hairy stems. Peponidium parvifolium is also valued for its ornamental appeal due to its attractive foliage and unique flowering pattern.
