Phillyrea barceloi subsp. knochei

Phillyrea barceloi subsp.

knochei, commonly known as the Canary Island Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands. It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and yellowish flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. It is adapted to the Mediterranean climate, featuring drought resistance and salt tolerance.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in arid regions.
