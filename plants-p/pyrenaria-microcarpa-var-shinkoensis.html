Pyrenaria microcarpa var. shinkoensis

Pyrenaria microcarpa var. shinkoensis, commonly known as the Shinkoensis variety of the Japanese holly, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features small, glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter on the underside. The plant produces tiny, red berries that are an attractive feature in the winter months. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and suitability for garden landscapes.
