Peperomia perinduta var. nashii

Peperomia perinduta var. nashii, commonly known as the Nashii Peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slight reddish tint on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and white, which are followed by tiny, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
