Psylliostachys hymenostegius

Psylliostachys hymenostegius, commonly known as the black bamboo, is a species of grass found primarily in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves with a smooth, dark green surface. Its inflorescence consists of dense clusters of small, spike-like flowers.

This bamboo is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
