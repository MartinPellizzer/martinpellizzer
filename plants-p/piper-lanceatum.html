Piper lanceatum

Piper lanceatum, commonly known as the lanceleaf pepper, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into berries. Its fruits are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its potential medicinal properties.
