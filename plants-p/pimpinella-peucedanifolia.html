Pimpinella peucedanifolia

Pimpinella peucedanifolia, commonly known as the European cow parsley, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in umbrella-like clusters.
