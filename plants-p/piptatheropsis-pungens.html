Piptatheropsis pungens

Piptatheropsis pungens, commonly known as the spiked broom.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
