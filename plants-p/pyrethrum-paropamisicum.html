Pyrethrum paropamisicum

Pyrethrum paropamisicum, commonly known as the Ethiopian pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow petals and a central disk. The plant produces pyrethrin, a natural insecticide, making it valuable in organic pest control. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental and agricultural uses.

Its botanical features include hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves.
