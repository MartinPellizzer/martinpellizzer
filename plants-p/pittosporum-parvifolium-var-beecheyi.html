Pittosporum parvifolium var. beecheyi

Pittosporum parvifolium var. beecheyi, commonly known as the small-leaved pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features elliptical, dark green leaves that are typically less than 5 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland forest areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
