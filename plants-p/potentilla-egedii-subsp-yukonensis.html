Potentilla egedii subsp. yukonensis

Potentilla egedii subsp.

yukonensis, commonly known as Yukon potentilla, is a low-growing shrub native to the arctic and alpine regions of North America. It typically features small, rounded leaves and produces clusters of yellow flowers in the summer.

The plant is adapted to harsh environments, thriving in well-drained, rocky soils. It is often found in tundra and dry grassland habitats.

Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine plantings.
