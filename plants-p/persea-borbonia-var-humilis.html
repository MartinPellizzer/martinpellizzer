Persea borbonia var. humilis

Persea borbonia var. humilis, commonly known as the dwarf avocado or lesser avocado, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes other aromatic trees and shrubs. The plant features simple, alternate, ovate leaves with a glossy green upper surface and a lighter underside. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters.

The fruit is a small, smooth, green berry that matures to a yellowish color, containing a single large seed.
