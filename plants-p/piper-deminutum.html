Piper deminutum

Piper deminutum, commonly known as the dwarf pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and clusters of tiny, peppery fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of rainforests.

Due to its unique appearance and mild peppery flavor, it is occasionally cultivated as an ornamental or for culinary use.
