Plethiandra

Plethiandra, commonly known as the orchid vine, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and the presence of showy, often fragrant flowers. Plethiandra species are often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands.
