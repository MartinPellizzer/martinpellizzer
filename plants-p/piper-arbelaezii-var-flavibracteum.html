Piper arbelaezii var. flavibracteum

Piper arbelaezii var. flavibracteum, commonly known as the yellow bract pepper, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing habit and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its most distinguishing feature is the yellowish bracts that enclose the small, greenish flowers.

The fruit is a small, berry-like structure that ripens to a dark purple color.
