Phyteuma orbiculare var. patens

Phyteuma orbiculare var. patens, commonly known as the spreading globe flower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features showy, clustered flowers that are typically pink to purple in color, blooming from late spring to early summer. The plant has rounded, orbicular leaves that are often hairy and arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and compact growth habit.
