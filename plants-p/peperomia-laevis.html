Peperomia laevis

Peperomia laevis, commonly known as the smooth peperomia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a smooth texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. Peperomia laevis prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is a low-maintenance plant that is popular as a houseplant due to its compact size and attractive foliage.
