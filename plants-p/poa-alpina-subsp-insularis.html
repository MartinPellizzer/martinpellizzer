Poa alpina subsp. insularis

Poa alpina subsp.

insularis, commonly known as the island mountain grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae. It is a low-growing perennial species adapted to cold and alpine environments.

The plant typically forms dense mats with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that contain both male and female flowers.

This subspecies is found in high-altitude regions of various islands, including parts of the Arctic and subarctic areas.
