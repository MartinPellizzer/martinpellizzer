Phlomis microphylla

Phlomis microphylla, commonly known as narrow-leaved mullein, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branched stems covered with dense, woolly hairs. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flower clusters that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and covered with a soft, fuzzy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
