Pittosporum oblongifolium

Pittosporum oblongifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features elliptical, dark green leaves that are often glossy and have a distinct midrib. The plant produces small, white flowers that are fragrant and attract pollinators such as bees and birds. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds, contributing to its propagation.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and hardy nature.
