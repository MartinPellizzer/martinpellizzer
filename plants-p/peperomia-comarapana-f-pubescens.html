Peperomia comarapana f. pubescens

Peperomia comarapana f. pubescens, commonly known as the velvety peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or red, often emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
