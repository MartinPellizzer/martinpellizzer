Paronychia arabica var. longiaristata

Paronychia arabica var. longiaristata, commonly known as the long-stemmed African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and hairy, and it thrives in full sun with well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and drought tolerance.
