Peperomia pseudofurcata

Peperomia pseudofurcata, commonly known as the zigzag peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are often marked with silver or white veins, creating a striking visual effect. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia pseudofurcata is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
