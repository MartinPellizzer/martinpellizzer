Pavonia kotschyi var. glabrescens

Pavonia kotschyi var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth pavonia, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, pink to purple flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, which can become smoother as the plant matures. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for xeriscaping.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
