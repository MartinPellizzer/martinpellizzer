Pavonia veneta

Pavonia veneta, commonly known as the scarlet pavonia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, crimson-red bract-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage is lance-shaped and often has a slightly hairy texture.

Pavonia veneta is valued in gardens for its vibrant color and ability to attract pollinators.
