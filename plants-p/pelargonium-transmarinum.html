Pelargonium transmarinum

Pelargonium transmarinum, commonly known as the Transmarinum geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant produces bright pink to red flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. Its leaves are typically rounded with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Pelargonium transmarinum is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various soil conditions.
