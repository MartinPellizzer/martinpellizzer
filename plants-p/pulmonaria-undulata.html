Pulmonaria undulata

Pulmonaria undulata, commonly known as the wavy lungwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to central and eastern Europe.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of oval, hairy leaves that often have a wavy or undulating margin. Pulmonaria undulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to naturalize in suitable conditions.
