Psittacanthus cuneifolius subsp. coronatus

Psittacanthus cuneifolius subsp.

coronatus, commonly known as the crowned haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, blue-green leaves with white to yellowish markings.

The plant produces small, white flowers on short, upright stems during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
