Podochilus undulatus

Podochilus undulatus, commonly known as the wavy podochilus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, wavy-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for collectors and horticulturists.
