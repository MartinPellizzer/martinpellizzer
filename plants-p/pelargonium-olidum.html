Pelargonium olidum

Pelargonium olidum, commonly known as the sweet geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers and succulent, lobed leaves. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Pelargonium olidum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
