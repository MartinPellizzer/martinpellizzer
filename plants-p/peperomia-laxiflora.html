Peperomia laxiflora

Peperomia laxiflora, commonly known as the broad-leaved peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate to lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or red, which are borne on short, erect inflorescences. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate moisture.

Peperomia laxiflora is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
