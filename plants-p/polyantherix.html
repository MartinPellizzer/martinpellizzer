Polyantherix

Polyantherix, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It features showy, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in dense clusters, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. The plant has pinnate leaves composed of several elliptical leaflets, and its bark is smooth and often fissured. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

The botanical name Polyantherix reflects its many flowers, highlighting its ornamental value in gardens.
