Peperomia philipsonii

Peperomia philipsonii, commonly known as the Philippine peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval or elliptical in shape, often with a bluish-green or dark green hue. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, followed by small, round berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia philipsonii is relatively low-maintenance, requiring minimal watering and moderate humidity to flourish.
