Peperomia klugiana

Peperomia klugiana, commonly known as the Klug's peperomia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub with glossy, dark green leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia klugiana is relatively low-maintenance and makes an attractive addition to indoor gardens.
