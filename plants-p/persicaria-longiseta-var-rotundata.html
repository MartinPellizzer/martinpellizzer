Persicaria longiseta var. rotundata

Persicaria longiseta var. rotundata, commonly known as roundleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are smooth and slightly waxy, with a broad, rounded form. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters at the ends of its stems. It thrives in moist, sunny environments such as wetlands and marshes.

Its botanical characteristics make it identifiable by its distinctive leaf shape and clustered inflorescences.
