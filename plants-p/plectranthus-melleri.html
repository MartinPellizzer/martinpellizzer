Plectranthus melleri

Plectranthus melleri, commonly known as the woolly mint, is a herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features soft, fuzzy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple, often blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Plectranthus melleri is also valued for its aromatic foliage, which releases a minty scent when crushed.
