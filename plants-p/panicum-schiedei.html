Panicum schiedei

Panicum schiedei, commonly known as red panic grass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its erect, slender stems and dense, fluffy inflorescences that appear in late summer. The plant produces small, spikelet-based flowers that are typically pink to reddish in color, giving it a striking visual appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping and ornamental gardens.

Its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions contributes to its popularity among gardeners and landscapers.
