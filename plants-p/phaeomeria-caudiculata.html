Phaeomeria caudiculata

Phaeomeria caudiculata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved phaeomeria, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its short, thickened stem that stores water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. Its leaves are narrow and fleshy, adapted to reduce water loss through transpiration.

Phaeomeria caudiculata is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its distinctive form and resilience.
