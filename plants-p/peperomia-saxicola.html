Peperomia saxicola

Peperomia saxicola, commonly known as the rock peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, waxy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a neat and compact appearance. The plant thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish. It is particularly suited for indoor cultivation due to its resilience and minimal care requirements.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for succulent and air plant collections.
