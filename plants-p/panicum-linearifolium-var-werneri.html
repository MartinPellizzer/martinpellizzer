Panicum linearifolium var. werneri

Panicum linearifolium var. werneri, commonly known as Werneri's slender panicgrass, is a grass species native to certain regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and compact, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, pale green to brownish flowers that are wind-pollinated.

This variant is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and drought tolerance.
