Paloue macrostachya

Paloue macrostachya, commonly known as the giant sedge, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges, rushes, and grasses. The plant features tall, narrow leaves and produces large, showy inflorescences that are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in wet or moist environments, such as marshes and swampy areas.
