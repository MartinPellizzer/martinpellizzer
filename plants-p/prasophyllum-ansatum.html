Prasophyllum ansatum

Prasophyllum ansatum, commonly known as the purple leek orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the wetlands of southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect flowering stems that bear a single, showy purple or pink flower. The plant has a short, fibrous root system and produces a basal rosette of linear, grass-like leaves. It blooms during the cooler months, typically between August and November.

Prasophyllum ansatum is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is adapted to wet, swampy environments.
