Phyllostegia ternaria

Phyllostegia ternaria, commonly known as the three-parted phyllostegia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

Phyllostegia ternaria thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas.
