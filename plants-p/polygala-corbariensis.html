Polygala corbariensis

Polygala corbariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its delicate, often pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in open woodlands and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It has slender, branching stems and small, pinnate leaves.

Its unique floral structure and limited distribution make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
