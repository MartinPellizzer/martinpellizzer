Physochlaina albiflora

Physochlaina albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered nightshade, is a member of the Solanaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and showy white or pale blue flowers. The plant produces small, toxic berries that are often mistaken for edible fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is native to regions of southern Africa.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
