Phillyrea medianifolia subsp. bercialis

Phillyrea medianifolia subsp.

bercialis, commonly known as the Bercial Phillyrea, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula. It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage with elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are followed by woody, spherical fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to Mediterranean climates.
