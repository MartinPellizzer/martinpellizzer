Peperomia dussii

Peperomia dussii, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery or grayish bloom, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are typically borne in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Peperomia dussii is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
