Pavonia arabica var. procumbens

Pavonia arabica var. procumbens, commonly known as the prostrate pavonia, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the Middle East and North Africa.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial that produces clusters of small, pink to red flowers. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are typically opposite and ovate in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant blooms and drought tolerance.
