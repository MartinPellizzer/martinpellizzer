Pterostylis peakalliana

Pterostylis peakalliana, commonly known as the Peakall's hooded orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. The plant features a slender, leafless stem with a single, hooded flower that is often pale green or yellowish in color. Its unique morphology includes a labellum that is divided into two lobes, enhancing its pollinator attraction.

The orchid is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is known for its specialized adaptations to its ecological niche.
