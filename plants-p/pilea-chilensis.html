Pilea chilensis

Pilea chilensis, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

Its unique leaf shape and air-purifying qualities make it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
