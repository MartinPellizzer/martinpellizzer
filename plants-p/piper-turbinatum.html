Piper turbinatum

Piper turbinatum, commonly known as the Turbinated Pepper or Turban Pepper, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes many species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns red when mature, containing a single seed.

Piper turbinatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of spices.
