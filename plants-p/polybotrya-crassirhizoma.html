Polybotrya crassirhizoma

Polybotrya crassirhizoma, commonly known as the thick-rooted fern, is a perennial plant belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its robust, creeping rhizomes that give it its common name. The plant produces feather-like fronds that are typically dark green and lanceolate in shape. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a notable species in its native habitat.
