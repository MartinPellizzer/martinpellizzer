Pilea multiflora

Pilea multiflora, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Pilea multiflora is known for its ease of care and ability to adapt to various growing conditions.
