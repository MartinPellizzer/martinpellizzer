Photinia brandisii

Photinia brandisii, commonly known as the redleaf photinia, is a shrub or small tree native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange-red leaves, which provide vibrant color throughout the year. The plant produces small, white flowers in spring, followed by red or orange berries that are attractive to birds. Its foliage is evergreen, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Photinia brandisii thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
