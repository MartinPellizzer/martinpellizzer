Penstemon subglaber

Penstemon subglaber, commonly known as the smooth penstemon, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant has smooth, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a whorled pattern along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscapes.

Penstemon subglaber is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
