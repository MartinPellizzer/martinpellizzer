Peperomia seemanniana

Peperomia seemanniana, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green, velvety leaves that are often heart-shaped or ovate in form. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Peperomia seemanniana is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
