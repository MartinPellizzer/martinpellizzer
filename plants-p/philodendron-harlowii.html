Philodendron harlowii

Philodendron harlowii, commonly known as the Harlowii philodendron, is a climbing vine native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns of light green veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Due to its unique foliage and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
