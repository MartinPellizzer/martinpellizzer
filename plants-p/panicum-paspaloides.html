Panicum paspaloides

Panicum paspaloides, commonly known as blue grama, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green blades and compact growth habit. This plant thrives in dry, sandy soils and is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing forage for wildlife.

Its drought tolerance makes it a valuable species in ecological restoration projects.
