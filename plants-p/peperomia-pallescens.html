Peperomia pallescens

Peperomia pallescens, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to South America.

It features dense, waxy leaves that are typically a silvery-gray or pale green, giving it a distinctive, almost metallic appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its attractive foliage and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
