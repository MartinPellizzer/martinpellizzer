Polygonum bistorta var. linearifolium

Polygonum bistorta var. linearifolium, commonly known as slender bistort, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features upright, hairy stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height. The leaves are lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly toothed margin, and are typically arranged alternately along the stem. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense spikes, and range in color from pink to red, blooming in late summer.

This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and stream banks.
