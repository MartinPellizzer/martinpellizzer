Peperomia brittonii

Peperomia brittonii, commonly known as the Britton's peperomia, is a compact evergreen perennial native to the Caribbean.

It features glossy, dark green, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically followed by bright red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable ornamental plant.
