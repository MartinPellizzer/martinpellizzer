Peperomia tradescantiifolia

Peperomia tradescantiifolia, commonly known as the glossy-leaf peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions.

It features thick, waxy, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and a striking metallic sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental purposes in both indoor and outdoor settings.
