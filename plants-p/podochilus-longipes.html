Podochilus longipes

Podochilus longipes, commonly known as the longipes orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Podochilus longipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
