Primula sinensis var. striata

Primula sinensis var. striata, commonly known as the variegated primrose, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with white and green patterns. The plant produces showy, funnel-shaped flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens, containers, and shaded borders.
