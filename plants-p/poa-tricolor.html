Poa tricolor

Poa tricolor, commonly known as the three-color grass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its striking tricolored blades, which display shades of green, blue, and silver, giving it a unique appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate climates and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and along stream banks.

Poa tricolor is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal.
