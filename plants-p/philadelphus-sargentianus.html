Philadelphus sargentianus

Philadelphus sargentianus, commonly known as Sargent's mockorange, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are dark green on top and hairy underneath. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its botanical characteristics make it suitable for gardens and landscapes in temperate climates.
