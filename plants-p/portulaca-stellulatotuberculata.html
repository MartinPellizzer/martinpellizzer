Portulaca stellulatotuberculata

Portulaca stellulatotuberculata, commonly known as the starry cushion portulaca, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It features fleshy, rounded leaves that are typically green or reddish in color, adapted to store water. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often blooming in the morning. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
