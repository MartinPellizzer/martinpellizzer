Pyracantha formosana

Pyracantha formosana, commonly known as the Formosan firethorn, is a deciduous shrub native to Taiwan.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces bright orange-red berries in the fall, which are attractive to birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its hardy nature and showy fruits make it a valuable addition to landscapes in temperate climates.
