Polygonum spergulariiforme

Polygonum spergulariiforme, commonly known as the narrowleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its distinctive nodes and often reddish stems. The plant typically grows in moist soils along streambanks, wetlands, and disturbed areas. Its leaves are lanceolate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface, and it produces small, greenish flowers in clusters.

The plant is known for its ability to thrive in wet environments and is often used in ecological restoration projects.
