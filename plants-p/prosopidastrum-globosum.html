Prosopidastrum globosum

Prosopidastrum globosum, commonly known as the globular spurge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit and produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters. The plant has smooth, waxy leaves that are typically green and slightly hairy. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
