Peperomia galioides var. aprica

Peperomia galioides var. aprica, commonly known as the sun peperomia, is a compact, trailing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale pink flowers during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for hanging baskets and as a decorative plant.
