Phyllarthron bojeranum

Phyllarthron bojeranum, commonly known as the Bojeranum fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests or along stream banks. Its rhizome is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

The leaves are usually bi- or tri-pinnate, giving the plant a lush, ornamental appearance.
