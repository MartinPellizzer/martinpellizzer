Pallenis spinosa subsp. maroccana

Pallenis spinosa subsp.

maroccana, commonly known as the Moroccan spiny pallenis, is a flowering plant native to North Africa. It is characterized by its spiny stems and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white.

The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and coastal areas. It has adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.

Its unique morphology and ecological adaptations make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
