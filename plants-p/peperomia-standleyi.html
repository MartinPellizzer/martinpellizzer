Peperomia standleyi

Peperomia standleyi, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically silver or gray-green in color, with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and drought tolerance.

Peperomia standleyi is prized for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments.
