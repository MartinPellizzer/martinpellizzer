Piper hostmannianum var. glabrirameum

Piper hostmannianum var. glabrirameum, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed pepper plant, is a variant of the Piper hostmannianum species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and lance-shaped leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. Its fruits are berries that mature to a dark purple or black color, containing a single seed.

This variant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in tropical gardens.
