Prionopsis chapmanii

Prionopsis chapmanii, commonly known as the Chapman's pincushion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of small yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is known for its drought tolerance and is often found in fynbos vegetation.
