Peperomia circinnata var. parvifolia

Peperomia circinnata var. parvifolia, commonly known as the compact peperomia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and minimal water requirements.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for container gardening and as a decorative plant.
