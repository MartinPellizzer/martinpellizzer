Pelargonium × latidentatum

Pelargonium × latidentatum, commonly known as the double sunset geranium, is a hybrid plant that exhibits distinctive botanical features.

It is characterized by its dense, double-layered flowers that resemble sunsets, with vibrant hues of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has rounded, lobed leaves that are typically covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-draining soil, making it suitable for container gardening.

Pelargonium × latidentatum is widely cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
