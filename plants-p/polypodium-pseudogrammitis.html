Polypodium pseudogrammitis

Polypodium pseudogrammitis, commonly known as the false sword fern, is a species of fern that belongs to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is a terrestrial plant characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and fronds that are typically divided into segments. The fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, with a distinctive shape that resembles a sword. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
