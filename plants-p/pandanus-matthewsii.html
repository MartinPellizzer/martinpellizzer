Pandanus matthewsii

Pandanus matthewsii, commonly known as the Solomon Islands pandanus, is a member of the Pandanaceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to the Pacific Islands, particularly the Solomon Islands. The plant is characterized by its pinnately compound leaves, which are often used in traditional crafts. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of many pandanus species.

The plant thrives in coastal and lowland areas, adapting well to humid, tropical climates.
