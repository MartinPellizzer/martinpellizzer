Polygala leptophylla var. leptophylla

Polygala leptophylla var. leptophylla, commonly known as the slender-leaved milkwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial to full shade. The plant features small, delicate flowers that are usually pink or white, arranged in clusters along the stems. Its leaves are narrow and elongated, often with a slightly hairy texture.

It is valued in native plant gardens for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
