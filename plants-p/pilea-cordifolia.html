Pilea cordifolia

Pilea cordifolia, commonly known as the Chinese money plant, is a popular indoor plant native to China.

It is characterized by its round, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and thick, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Pilea cordifolia is known for its ease of care and ability to purify the air, making it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
