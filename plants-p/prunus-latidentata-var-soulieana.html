Prunus latidentata var. soulieana

Prunus latidentata var. soulieana, commonly known as the Chinese bird cherry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It features small, white flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by edible, sweet berries. The plant has ovate to elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and have a slightly serrated edge. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively hardy in temperate climates.

The berries are also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
