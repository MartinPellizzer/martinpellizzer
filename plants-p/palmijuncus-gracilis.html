Palmijuncus gracilis

Palmijuncus gracilis, commonly known as the slender palm fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and pinnate leaves that give it a delicate, fern-like appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its reproductive structures are small, clustered spore-bearing cones located at the base of the fronds.

Palmijuncus gracilis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its elegant foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
