Piper puraceanum

Piper puraceanum, commonly known as the Peruvian pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Peru and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with dark green, opposite leaves and small, clustered flowers. It produces small, peppery fruits that are used in traditional medicine and local cuisines.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and ethnobotanical contexts.
