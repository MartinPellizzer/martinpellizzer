Polygonatum stewartianum

Polygonatum stewartianum, commonly known as Stewart's Solomon's seal, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its arching stems and clusters of white or pale green flowers.
