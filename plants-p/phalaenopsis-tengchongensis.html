Phalaenopsis tengchongensis

Phalaenopsis tengchongensis, commonly known as the Tengchong orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its elegant, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of long, white or pale pink flowers. This epiphytic plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.

The plant's botanical features make it a unique addition to both natural and cultivated ecosystems.
