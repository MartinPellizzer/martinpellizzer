Piper modestum

Piper modestum, commonly known as the modest pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces small, green berries that turn red when ripe, and these berries contain the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and culinary applications. Piper modestum is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and its ability to climb or trail along the ground.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its potential medicinal properties.
