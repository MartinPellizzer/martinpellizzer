Polystachya latifolia

Polystachya latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved polystachya, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically arranged in racemes. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some horticultural collections.
