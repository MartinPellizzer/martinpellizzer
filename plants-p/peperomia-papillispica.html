Peperomia papillispica

Peperomia papillispica, commonly known as the papillate peperomia, is a species of flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with a textured, papillate surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are clustered in spikes. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Peperomia papillispica is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
