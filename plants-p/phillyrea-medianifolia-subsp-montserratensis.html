Phillyrea medianifolia subsp. montserratensis

Phillyrea medianifolia subsp.

montserratensis, commonly known as the Montserrat Phillyrea, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands. It is characterized by its small, elliptical, evergreen leaves that are glossy and arranged alternately along the stems.

The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, spherical berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

This subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and ecological significance in its native habitat.
