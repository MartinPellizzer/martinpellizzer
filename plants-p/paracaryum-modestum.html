Paracaryum modestum

Paracaryum modestum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight to flourish. Its botanical features include a short, thick stem and hairy leaves that help reduce water loss.

Paracaryum modestum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
