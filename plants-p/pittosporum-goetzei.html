Pittosporum goetzei

Pittosporum goetzei, commonly known as the New Zealand pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are oval in shape and have a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

Pittosporum goetzei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in horticulture for landscaping purposes.
