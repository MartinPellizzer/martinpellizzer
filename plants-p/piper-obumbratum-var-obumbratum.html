Piper obumbratum var. obumbratum

Piper obumbratum var. obumbratum, commonly known as the shadow pepper, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces berries that range in color from green to red, depending on their maturity. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in some botanical gardens and traditional medicine practices.
