Polycnemum maroccanum

Polycnemum maroccanum, commonly known as Moroccan knotweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that often grow in dense clusters, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters at the tips of the stems. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Polycnemum maroccanum is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
