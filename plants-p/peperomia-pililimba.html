Peperomia pililimba

Peperomia pililimba, commonly known as the velvet peperomia, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It features thick, fleshy, and often velvety leaves that are typically green or reddish in color, with some varieties displaying variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white, clustered in spikes at the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Peperomia pililimba is relatively easy to care for, requiring minimal water and moderate humidity to flourish.
