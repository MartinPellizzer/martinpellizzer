Pilea danxiaensis

Pilea danxiaensis, commonly known as the Danxia pilea, is a rare and unique species native to southern China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, followed by spathaceous inflorescences. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is a sought-after plant among collectors and botanists.
