Peutalis nodosa

Peutalis nodosa, commonly known as the nodular orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, nodular growth pattern on its stems. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. Peutalis nodosa thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for both horticulturists and botanists.
