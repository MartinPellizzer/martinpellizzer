Pleurothallis deregularis

Pleurothallis deregularis, commonly known as the irregular pleurothallis, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a compact rosette.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
