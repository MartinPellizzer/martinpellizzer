Pelargonium jensenii

Pelargonium jensenii, commonly known as the Jensen's geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers. Pelargonium jensenii is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and containers due to its hardiness and aesthetic value.
