Phyllanthus eriocarpus

Phyllanthus eriocarpus, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its small, clustered leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are covered in a dense, velvety coating. It is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
