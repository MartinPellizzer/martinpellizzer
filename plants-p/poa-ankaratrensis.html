Poa ankaratrensis

Poa ankaratrensis, commonly known as the Ankaratren grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of the Ethiopian Highlands, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. This plant is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and fine, bluish-green leaves. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets.

Due to its unique habitat and limited distribution, Poa ankaratrensis is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
