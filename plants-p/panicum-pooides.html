Panicum pooides

Panicum pooides, commonly known as little bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green blades that turn coppery in the fall. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and woodlands. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that mature into fluffy seeds.

Panicum pooides is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing habitat and food for wildlife.
