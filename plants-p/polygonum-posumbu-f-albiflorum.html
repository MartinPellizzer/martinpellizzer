Polygonum posumbu f. albiflorum

Polygonum posumbu f. albiflorum, commonly known as the white-flowered smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polygonaceae, characterized by its distinctive nodes and often swollen leaf bases. The plant features slender, erect stems that are usually covered with fine hairs. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and are typically white or pale pink in color.

It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
