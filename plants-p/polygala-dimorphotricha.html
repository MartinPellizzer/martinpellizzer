Polygala dimorphotricha

Polygala dimorphotricha, commonly known as the two-hair polygala, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its unique floral structure, which includes two distinct types of stamens. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowers are small and usually pale green or yellow, with a distinctive hood-like shape.
