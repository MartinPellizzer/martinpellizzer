Prunus Sativa Proles Dumetorum

Blackthorn, scientifically known as Prunus sativa proles dumetorum, is a hardy shrub native to temperate regions of Europe and western Asia. It is characterized by its thorny branches and small, dark berries that ripen in late summer. Despite its prickly appearance, the plant has a long history of use in traditional medicine and as a source of fruit. Its resilience in harsh conditions makes it a common sight in hedgerows and woodland edges. The blackthorn's ecological role is significant, supporting various wildlife species throughout the year.
