Phyllanthus guianensis

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, tropical environments and is often found in forest understories. It produces a capsule fruit that contains numerous tiny seeds.
