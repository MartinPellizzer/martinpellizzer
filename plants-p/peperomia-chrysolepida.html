Peperomia chrysolepida

Peperomia chrysolepida, commonly known as the golden leaf peperomia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually white or pale green. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
