Pleione massangeana

Pleione massangeana, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a rare and striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, including China, India, and Myanmar. The plant produces showy, golden-yellow flowers with intricate patterns that resemble intricate lace. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its rarity and beauty, it is highly valued in horticulture and conservation efforts.
