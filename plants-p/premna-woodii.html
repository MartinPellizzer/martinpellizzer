Premna woodii

Premna woodii, commonly known as the Chinese nettle or wild nettle, is a flowering plant species native to eastern Asia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features opposite, heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges and produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters. Its stems are often covered in fine hairs, and it has a fibrous root system.

Premna woodii is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
