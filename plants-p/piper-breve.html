Piper breve

Piper breve, commonly known as the dwarf pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, upright growth habit and dark green, glossy leaves. It produces tiny, greenish-white flowers that develop into small, peppery fruits.

Piper breve is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has mild medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
