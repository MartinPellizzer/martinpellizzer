Petrosedum montanum f. lunigianicum

Petrosedum montanum f. lunigianicum, commonly known as the Lunigianum stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit. The leaves are typically fleshy, with a bluish-green coloration, and are arranged in a tight, upright manner. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months.

This plant is well adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, thriving in full sun to partial shade.
