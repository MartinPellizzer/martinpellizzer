Peperomia pallida var. longespicata

Peperomia pallida var. longespicata, commonly known as the silver peperomia, is a compact, trailing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are pale silver to bluish-gray in color, with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish, emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
