Prinos integer

Prinos integer, commonly known as the African snowberry, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, white, bell-shaped flowers that appear in clusters, followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. The plant has smooth, dark green leaves and a compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is often cultivated in gardens for both beauty and wildlife support.
