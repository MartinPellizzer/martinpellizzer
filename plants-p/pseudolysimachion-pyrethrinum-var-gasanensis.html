Pseudolysimachion pyrethrinum var. gasanensis

Pseudolysimachion pyrethrinum var. gasanensis, commonly known as the Gasanensis pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant is notable for its production of pyrethrin, a natural insecticide used in organic pest control. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique chemical composition and ornamental value, it is of interest to both horticulturists and researchers.
