Pandanus benignus

Pandanus benignus, commonly known as the slender pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pandanaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. Its inflorescence consists of a branched cluster of small, fragrant flowers.

The plant thrives in humid, coastal environments and is often found growing near the shoreline.
