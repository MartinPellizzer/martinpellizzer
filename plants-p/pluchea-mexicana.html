Pluchea mexicana

Pluchea mexicana, commonly known as Mexican groundcherry, is a flowering plant native to Central America and parts of the southern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Pluchea mexicana is often found in moist, disturbed soils and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
