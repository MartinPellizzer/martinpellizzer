Potentilla matsumurae var. pilosior

Potentilla matsumurae var. pilosior, commonly known as the silky cinquefoil, is a perennial shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its finely hairy stems and leaves, which give it a soft, velvety appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow, five-petaled flowers in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
