Pentzia globifera

Pentzia globifera, commonly known as the globeflower, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and clustered, globe-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade. Its unique floral structure makes it a striking addition to rock gardens and xeriscapes.

Pentzia globifera is also valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
