Peperomia dodecatheontophylla

Peperomia dodecatheontophylla, commonly known as the twelve-leaved peperomia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features a rosette of fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, pale green to white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, giving it a unique appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
