Piper humblotii

Piper humblotii, commonly known as the Humblot's pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into small, dark purple berries.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage and unique appearance.
