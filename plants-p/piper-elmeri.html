Piper elmeri

Piper elmeri, commonly known as Elmer's pepper, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants such as black pepper. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that develop into elongated, reddish berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and botanical research.
