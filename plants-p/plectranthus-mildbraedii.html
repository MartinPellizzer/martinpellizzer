Plectranthus mildbraedii

Plectranthus mildbraedii, commonly known as the Cape verbena, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Plectranthus mildbraedii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
