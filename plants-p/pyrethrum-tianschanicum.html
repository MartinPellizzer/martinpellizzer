Pyrethrum tianschanicum

Pyrethrum tianschanicum, commonly known as the Tianshan pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the Tianshan mountain range in Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers and finely divided leaves. The plant is known for producing pyrethrin, a natural insecticide used in organic pest control. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.

Its resilience to harsh environmental conditions makes it a notable species in alpine ecosystems.
