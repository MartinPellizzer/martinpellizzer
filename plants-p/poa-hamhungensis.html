Poa hamhungensis

Poa hamhungensis, commonly known as the Hamhung grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the Korean Peninsula, specifically found in the region of Hamhung. This perennial grass typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous areas. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various insects and small animals.
