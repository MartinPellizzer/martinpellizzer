Pseudobombax ellipticoideum

Pseudobombax ellipticoideum, commonly known as the Mexican red maple, is a deciduous tree native to southern Mexico.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its elliptical, ovate leaves that turn red in the fall. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm regions.
