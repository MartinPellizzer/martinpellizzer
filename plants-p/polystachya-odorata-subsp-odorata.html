Polystachya odorata subsp. odorata

Polystachya odorata subsp.

odorata, commonly known as the fragrant polystachya, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa. It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that emit a subtle fragrance.

The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, with a smooth texture.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral scent and foliage.
