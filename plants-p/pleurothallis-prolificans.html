Pleurothallis prolificans

Pleurothallis prolificans, commonly known as the prolific orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces numerous small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are typically ovate and arranged in a rosette pattern, facilitating efficient water retention.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in both greenhouses and as a houseplant.
