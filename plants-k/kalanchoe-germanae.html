Kalanchoe germanae

Kalanchoe germanae, commonly known as the German kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture, adapted to store water. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, usually during the winter months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight to flourish.

Kalanchoe germanae is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its resilience and aesthetic appeal.
