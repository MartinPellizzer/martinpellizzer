Kinkina

Kinkina, commonly known as the "butterfly plant," is a tropical species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, which includes plants like calla lilies and peace lilies. The plant features striking, colorful bracts that resemble butterflies, giving it its common name. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and can grow quite large.

Kinkina thrives in humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
