Kopsia vinciflora

Kopsia vinciflora, commonly known as the Vietnamese kopsia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, clustered in umbels, and range in color from white to pink.

Kopsia vinciflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
