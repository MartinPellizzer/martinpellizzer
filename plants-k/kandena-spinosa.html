Kandena spinosa

Kandena spinosa, commonly known as the spiny kandena, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its woody stem and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
