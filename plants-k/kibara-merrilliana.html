Kibara merrilliana

Kibara merrilliana, commonly known as the Malawi orchid, is a species of orchid native to the high-altitude regions of Malawi.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in rocky or forested areas, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Kibara merrilliana is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in its native habitat.
