Kunzea

Kunzea, commonly known as the coastal tea tree, is a genus of flowering plants in the myrtle family, Myrtaceae.

It is native to Australia and includes several species, such as Kunzea sericea and Kunzea robusta. These plants are typically shrubs or small trees, characterized by aromatic leaves and small, often white or pink flowers. Kunzea species are adapted to coastal environments and are often found in heathlands and open woodlands.

They are valued for their ornamental appeal and their use in traditional medicine and aromatic products.
