Koeleria gracilis var. fulcrata

Koeleria gracilis var. fulcrata, commonly known as the slender wild oat, is a perennial grass native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. The plant features slender, cylindrical stems and small, spike-like inflorescences with pale blue to purple flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

It is adapted to arid conditions and plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native environment.
