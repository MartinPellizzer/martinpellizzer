Kunzea opposita var. opposita

Kunzea opposita var. opposita, commonly known as the narrow-leaved kunzea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native gardens.
