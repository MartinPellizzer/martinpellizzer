Kalanchoe tubiflora

Kalanchoe tubiflora, commonly known as the snow plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers during the winter months. Its leaves are often covered in a fine, powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss.

Kalanchoe tubiflora is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
