Koeleria biseta

Koeleria biseta, commonly known as the desert brome, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, blue to purple flowers. It is well adapted to drought conditions and can thrive in low-nutrient environments.
