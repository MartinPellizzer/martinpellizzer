Kleinia chimanimaniensis

Kleinia chimanimaniensis, commonly known as the Chimanimani daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. It produces small, oval leaves that are covered in a fine, white pubescence.

Kleinia chimanimaniensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
