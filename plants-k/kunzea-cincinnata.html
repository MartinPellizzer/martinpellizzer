Kunzea cincinnata

Kunzea cincinnata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved kunzea, is a small evergreen shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in heathlands and coastal areas, thriving in well-drained sandy soils. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. It produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months.

Kunzea cincinnata is valued for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in horticulture for its ornamental qualities.
