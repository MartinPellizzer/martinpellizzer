Knautia flaviflora

Knautia flaviflora, commonly known as the yellow knautia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in soft, gray-green foliage. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with ray florets surrounding a central disc of tubular flowers.

Knautia flaviflora thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable addition to rock gardens and alpine landscapes.
