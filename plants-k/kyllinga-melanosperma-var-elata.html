Kyllinga melanosperma var. elata

Kyllinga melanosperma var. elata, commonly known as blackseed kyllinga, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features upright, grass-like stems that are often reddish or purplish in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white to green flowers followed by dark, spherical seeds. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a slightly hairy texture.

Kyllinga melanosperma var. elata is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique seed structure.
