Kleinodendron

Kleinodendron, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features velvety, dark green leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. Kleinodendron is popular as a houseplant due to its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its ornamental value.

It requires well-draining soil and moderate watering to prevent root rot.
