Kalanchoe × hummeliae

Kalanchoe × hummeliae, commonly known as the "Hummel's kalanchoe," is a hybrid plant native to South Africa.

It is a succulent with fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are often covered in a layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Kalanchoe × hummeliae is popular as a decorative plant due to its long-lasting blooms and low maintenance requirements.
