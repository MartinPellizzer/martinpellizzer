Karpatiosorbus parviloba

Karpatiosorbus parviloba, commonly known as the Carpathian shrub.

It is a deciduous shrub native to the Carpathian Mountains in Europe. The plant features small, white flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by red berries in the autumn. Its leaves are elliptical and have a slightly hairy texture.

Karpatiosorbus parviloba is valued for its ornamental qualities and its ability to thrive in rocky, well-drained soils.
