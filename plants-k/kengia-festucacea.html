Kengia festucacea

Kengia festucacea, commonly known as the grassy kengia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in dense tufts, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or rocky areas.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
