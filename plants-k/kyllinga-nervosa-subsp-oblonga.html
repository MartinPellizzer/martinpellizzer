Kyllinga nervosa subsp. oblonga

Kyllinga nervosa subsp.

oblonga, commonly known as narrowleaf spikerush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. It typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of water bodies, showcasing its adaptability to moist environments.

The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces spike-like inflorescences that bear small, clustered flowers. Its botanical structure includes a rhizomatous root system that aids in anchoring and nutrient absorption. Kyllinga nervosa subsp.

oblonga plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
