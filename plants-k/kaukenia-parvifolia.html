Kaukenia parvifolia

Kaukenia parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved kaukenia, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, small leaves and clusters of pale yellow to white flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or alpine habitats. Its botanical features include a compact stature and a preference for well-drained soils.

Kaukenia parvifolia is of interest to botanists due to its unique adaptations to its harsh ecological niche.
