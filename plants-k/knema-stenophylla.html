Knema stenophylla

Knema stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf knema, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, and it produces small, fleshy berries as its fruit.

Knema stenophylla is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique foliage.
