Kengia thoroldii

Kengia thoroldii, commonly known as the red kengia, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically red or purple in color, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant produces vibrant orange or yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Kengia thoroldii is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
