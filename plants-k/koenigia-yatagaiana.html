Koenigia yatagaiana

Koenigia yatagaiana, commonly known as the Yatagaian stonecrop, is a perennial succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, allowing it to store water efficiently. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and purple during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its adaptability to harsh environments makes it a hardy and resilient species in botanical collections.
