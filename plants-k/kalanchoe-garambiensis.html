Kalanchoe garambiensis

Kalanchoe garambiensis, commonly known as the Garambien kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, a feature typical of many succulents. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often blooming in the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Kalanchoe garambiensis is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
