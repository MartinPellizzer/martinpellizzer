Kalmia polifolia subsp. occidentalis

Kalmia polifolia subsp.

occidentalis, commonly known as the western mountain laurel, is a shrub native to the western United States. It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its evergreen foliage.
