Kalanchoe beauverdii var. parviflora

Kalanchoe beauverdii var. parviflora, commonly known as the dwarf kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight powdery coating. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

This variety is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its compact size and vibrant blooms.
