Keteleeria fortunei var. cyclolepis

Keteleeria fortunei var. cyclolepis, commonly known as the cyclolepis Chinese fir, is a variant of the Chinese fir native to China.

It is a medium-sized evergreen conifer with a conical shape and dark green, needle-like leaves. The plant produces small, cylindrical cones that mature to a reddish-brown color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

This species is valued for its timber and is also cultivated for ornamental purposes.
