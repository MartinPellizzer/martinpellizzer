Kniphofia wyliei

Kniphofia wyliei, commonly known as the Wylie's torch lily, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Hemerocallidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, fiery red and orange inflorescences that resemble torches. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that form a rosette at the base. It produces showy, short-lived flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn.

Kniphofia wyliei is well-suited for rock gardens and requires well-drained soil and full sun to thrive.
