Knema flavostamina

Knema flavostamina, commonly known as the yellow-stemmed knema, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Knema flavostamina is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its aromatic wood.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
