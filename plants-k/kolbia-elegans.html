Kolbia elegans

Kolbia elegans, commonly known as the elegant kolbia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
