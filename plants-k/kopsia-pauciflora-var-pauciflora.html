Kopsia pauciflora var. pauciflora

Kopsia pauciflora var. pauciflora, commonly known as the slender kopsia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. It blooms during the summer months and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
