Knautia subscaposa f. robusta

Knautia subscaposa f. robusta, commonly known as the robust knautia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features lobed, serrated leaves that are often hairy, contributing to its rugged appearance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, loamy soils.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
