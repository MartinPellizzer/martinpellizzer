Koeleria Brevifolia Var. Monticola

Alpine koeleria, scientifically known as Koeleria brevifolia var. monticola, is a hardy plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia. It thrives in cold, high-altitude environments where few other plants can survive. This low-growing grass-like species is characterized by its compact rosettes and delicate blue-green foliage. Its resilience to harsh weather conditions makes it a valuable addition to alpine gardens and restoration projects. Despite its small size, Alpine koeleria plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.
