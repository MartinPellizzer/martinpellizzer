Kunthia mollis

Kunthia mollis, commonly known as the silver hair grass, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-gray stems and fine, hairy leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale blue, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. Kunthia mollis thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Its unique morphology and habitat make it a notable species in the study of high-altitude flora.
