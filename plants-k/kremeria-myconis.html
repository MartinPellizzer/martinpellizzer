Kremeria myconis

Kremeria myconis, commonly known as the fairy lantern, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces unique, lantern-like seed pods that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky or sandy soils.

Kremeria myconis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated for its distinctive appearance.
