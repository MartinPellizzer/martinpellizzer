Karpatiosorbus seyboldiana

Karpatiosorbus seyboldiana, commonly known as the Carpathian rowan, is a shrub native to the Carpathian Mountains in Europe.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, spiny growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers in spring, followed by red or orange berries in autumn. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a serrated margin.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas or along rocky slopes.
