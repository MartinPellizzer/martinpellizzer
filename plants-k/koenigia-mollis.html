Koenigia mollis

Koenigia mollis, commonly known as the silver carpet plant, is a low-growing succulent native to the Canary Islands.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery bloom, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic and hardy nature.
