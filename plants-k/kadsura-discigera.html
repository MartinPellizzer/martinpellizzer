Kadsura discigera

Kadsura discigera, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or the kadsura, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often veined with white. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
