Kleinia hanburyana

Kleinia hanburyana, commonly known as Hanbury's ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white, waxy hair, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers during the summer months, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
