Kyllinga albogracilis

Kyllinga albogracilis, commonly known as white slender kyllinga, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically light green to white in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered in spikes. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Kyllinga albogracilis is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its delicate appearance.
