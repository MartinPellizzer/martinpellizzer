Kardomia prominens

Kardomia prominens, commonly known as the tall kardomia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its upright growth habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by pod fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.

Kardomia prominens is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
