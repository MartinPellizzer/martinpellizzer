Kalanchoe pentheri

Kalanchoe pentheri, commonly known as the Christmas chandelier, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and clusters of fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that form a rosette. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers during the winter months, which is a unique feature for its genus. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Kalanchoe pentheri is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and seasonal blooming.
