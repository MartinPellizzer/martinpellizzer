Knautia legionensis var. latiorifolia

Knautia legionensis var. latiorifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed knautia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan region.

It features upright stems covered in soft, hairy foliage and produces clusters of pink to purple flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and often broader than those of the typical variety, giving it its varietal name. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, loamy soils.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
