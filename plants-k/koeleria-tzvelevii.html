Koeleria tzvelevii

Koeleria tzvelevii, commonly known as the Tzvelev's feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and delicate, feathery inflorescences. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with fine, hairy textures that help reduce water loss.

Koeleria tzvelevii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in harsh climates.
