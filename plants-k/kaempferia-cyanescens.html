Kaempferia cyanescens

Kaempferia cyanescens, commonly known as the Blue Orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flower petals. The plant typically grows as a rhizomatous herb with strap-shaped leaves and a compact inflorescence. Its flowers are often fragrant and bloom in shades of blue, purple, and white.

Kaempferia cyanescens is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its traditional use in herbal medicine.
