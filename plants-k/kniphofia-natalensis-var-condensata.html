Kniphofia natalensis var. condensata

Kniphofia natalensis var. condensata, commonly known as the Natal torch or Natal red hot poker, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

The plant is characterized by its tall, sword-shaped leaves and striking clusters of red, orange, or yellow tubular flowers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
