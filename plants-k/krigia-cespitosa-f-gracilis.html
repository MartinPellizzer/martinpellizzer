Krigia cespitosa f. gracilis

Krigia cespitosa f. gracilis, commonly known as the slender spurge or slender krigia, is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features dense, mat-forming growth with small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is adapted to well-drained soils.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to serve as a ground cover in rock gardens or borders.
