Kochia parodii var. contracta

Kochia parodii var. contracta, commonly known as the compact kochia, is a dwarf shrub native to the western United States.

It features small, spiny leaves and produces vibrant, flame-colored bracts that attract pollinators. This plant is adapted to arid environments and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.

The compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and container planting.
