Kaempferia kamthornii

Kaempferia kamthornii, commonly known as the Thai white ginger lily, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking white to pale pink flowers and narrow, strap-like leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread and form dense clumps.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in local cultures.
