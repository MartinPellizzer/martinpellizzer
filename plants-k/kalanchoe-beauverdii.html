Kalanchoe beauverdii

Kalanchoe beauverdii, commonly known as the Christmas chandelier, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features upright, branching stems that bear clusters of bright yellow flowers during the winter months. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements.

The flowers are often used in floral arrangements, adding a vibrant touch to holiday decor.
