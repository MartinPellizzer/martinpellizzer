Kalanchoe × trageri

Kalanchoe × trageri, commonly known as the Christmas chandelier, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are often fleshy and covered in a powdery white coating. The plant produces clusters of vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow during the winter months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Kalanchoe × trageri is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
