Kadurias reflexa

Kadurias reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed kadurias, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like inflorescence. The plant features narrow, reflexed leaves and yellow-orange flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Kadurias reflexa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
