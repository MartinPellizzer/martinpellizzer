Koelreuteria paniculata f. fastigiata

Koelreuteria paniculata f. fastigiata, commonly known as the upright form of the Chinese lantern tree, is a cultivar characterized by its narrow, columnar growth habit.

It features small, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing a dense canopy. The plant produces showy, fragrant panicles of white to pale yellow flowers in the spring, followed by distinctive orange-pink seed pods in the fall. It is a hardy, deciduous tree that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.

This cultivar is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and compact size.
