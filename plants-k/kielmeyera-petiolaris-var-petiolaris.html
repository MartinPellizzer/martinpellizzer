Kielmeyera petiolaris var. petiolaris

Kielmeyera petiolaris var. petiolaris, commonly known as the Brazilian pepper tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Brazil.

It features compound leaves with elongated, pointed leaflets that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers followed by red, fleshy berries that are edible for some bird species. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.

However, it can become invasive in certain regions if not managed properly.
