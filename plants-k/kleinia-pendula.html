Kleinia pendula

Kleinia pendula, commonly known as the hanging Kleinia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that hang downward, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Kleinia pendula is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
