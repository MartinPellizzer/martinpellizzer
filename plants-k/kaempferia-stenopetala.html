Kaempferia stenopetala

Kaempferia stenopetala, commonly known as the narrow-petaled ginger, is a tropical herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other gingers and related plants. The plant features sword-shaped, dark green leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. Its rhizome is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
