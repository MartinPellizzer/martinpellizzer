Krynitzkia micrantha var. lepida

Krynitzkia micrantha var. lepida, commonly known as the smooth-leaved krynitzkia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, erect stems that are often covered in a fine, soft pubescence. The leaves are lanceolate, with a smooth texture and a bluish-green hue, contributing to its ornamental appeal. Its small, white flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, typically blooming in late spring to early summer.

This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
