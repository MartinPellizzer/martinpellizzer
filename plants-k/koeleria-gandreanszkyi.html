Koeleria gandreanszkyi

Koeleria gandreanszkyi, commonly known as the Hungarian grass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Europe.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a blue-green coloration.

Koeleria gandreanszkyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and ecological restoration projects.
