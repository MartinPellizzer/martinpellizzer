Kaempferia laotica

Kaempferia laotica, commonly known as the Laotian flame orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers that often display shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and grows from a pseudobulb, typical of many orchid species. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Kaempferia laotica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
