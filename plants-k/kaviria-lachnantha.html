Kaviria lachnantha

Kaviria lachnantha, commonly known as the silverleaf kaviria, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showing adaptability to dry conditions.

Kaviria lachnantha is often found in rocky soils and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
