Koeleria gracilis var. pilifera

Koeleria gracilis var. pilifera, commonly known as the slender oatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows, rocky slopes, and disturbed areas. The plant features fine, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale blue or greenish flowers. Its foliage is often covered with soft, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and its role in stabilizing soil in arid environments.
