Knema pierrei

Knema pierrei, commonly known as the white knema, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the rainforests of northern Australia and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the wet season. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several small, flat seeds.

Knema pierrei is often found in moist, shaded environments and plays a role in the ecological dynamics of its habitat.
