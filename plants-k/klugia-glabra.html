Klugia glabra

Klugia glabra, commonly known as the smooth klugia, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in arid environments. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, white flowers bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months.

Klugia glabra is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
