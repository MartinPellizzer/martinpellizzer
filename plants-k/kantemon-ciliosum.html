Kantemon ciliosum

Kantemon ciliosum, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release numerous tiny seeds.

Kantemon ciliosum is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and agricultural fields.
