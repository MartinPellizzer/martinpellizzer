Kuruna

Kuruna, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular ornamental plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Kuruna is known for its ability to bloom throughout the year under proper care.

Its compact size and vibrant flowers make it a favorite among indoor gardeners.
