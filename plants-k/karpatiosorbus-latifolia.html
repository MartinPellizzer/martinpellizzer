Karpatiosorbus latifolia

Karpatiosorbus latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf rowan, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its broad, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine white pubescence. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, followed by red or orange berries in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas or along rocky slopes.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
