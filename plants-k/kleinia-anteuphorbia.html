Kleinia anteuphorbia

Kleinia anteuphorbia, commonly known as the Cape rose or fireplant, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and fleshy, often reddish stems. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. Its leaves are usually small, fleshy, and may have a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Kleinia anteuphorbia is well adapted to arid environments and is often used in rock gardens or as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance.
