Knema andamanica subsp. andamanica

Knema andamanica subsp.

andamanica, commonly known as the Andaman knema, is a small evergreen tree native to the Andaman Islands. It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, dark-brown bark and elliptical, glossy leaves.

The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by edible, spherical berries. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
