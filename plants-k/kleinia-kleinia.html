Kleinia kleinia

Kleinia kleinia, commonly known as the silver leaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is drought-tolerant and often used in rock gardens due to its ornamental value.
