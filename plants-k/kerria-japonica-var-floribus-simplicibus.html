Kerria japonica var. floribus-simplicibus

Kerria japonica var. floribus-simplicibus, commonly known as the simple-flowered Japanese kerria, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically in clusters of three to five. The leaves are oval-shaped with a smooth texture and a dark green color during the growing season. This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and ornamental value in gardens.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a versatile choice for landscaping.
