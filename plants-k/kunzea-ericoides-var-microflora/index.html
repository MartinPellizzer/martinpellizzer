Kunzea Ericoides Var. Microflora

Kunzea ericoides var. microflora is a unique shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Australia. This variant is known for its small, dense foliage and ability to thrive in harsh, salty environments. It plays an important role in stabilizing dunes and preventing erosion along the shoreline. The plant's adaptability makes it a valuable species for ecological restoration projects. Its delicate appearance contrasts with its tough survival traits, making it both resilient and aesthetically interesting.
