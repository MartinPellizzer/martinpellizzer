Kaempferia concinna

Kaempferia concinna, commonly known as the flame orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows as a rhizomatous herb, with narrow, strap-like leaves that are often a deep green. Its flowers are usually pink to red in color, with a distinctive yellow or orange lip.

Kaempferia concinna thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
