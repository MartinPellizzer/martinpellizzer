Kleinia alata

Kleinia alata, commonly known as the silver dwarf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant features dense, woolly leaves that help it retain moisture in its arid habitat. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Kleinia alata thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
