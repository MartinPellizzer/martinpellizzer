Kersia

Kersia, commonly known as the yellow bell, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its striking yellow, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to two meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Kersia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
