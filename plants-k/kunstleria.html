Kunstleria

Kunstleria, commonly known as the feather grass, is a genus of flowering plants in the grass family, Poaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, slender blades that often form a soft, flowing appearance. The plants typically grow in wet or marshy environments, making them common in wetlands and along riverbanks. They produce small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered in spikelets.

Kunstleria species are valued for their ornamental qualities and ecological benefits in supporting local wildlife.
