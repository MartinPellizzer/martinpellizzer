Knautia lancifolia var. sarajevensis

Knautia lancifolia var. sarajevensis, commonly known as the Sarajevo knautia, is a perennial herb native to the Balkan region.

It features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of pink to purple flowers, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded locations.

This variety is valued for its ornamental value in gardens and its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
