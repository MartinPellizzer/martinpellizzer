Koevia

Koevia, commonly known as the desert dandelion, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant has hairy, narrow leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Koevia is adapted to survive in harsh environments, with deep roots that help it access water.

It is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
