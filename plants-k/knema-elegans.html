Knema elegans

Knema elegans, commonly known as the elegant knema, is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by edible, fleshy berries that are an important food source for various wildlife. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland and hill forest regions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
