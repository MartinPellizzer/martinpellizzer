Kinginda multiflora

Kinginda multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered kinginda, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, often found in the Cape Province. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom throughout the warmer months. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

Kinginda multiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
