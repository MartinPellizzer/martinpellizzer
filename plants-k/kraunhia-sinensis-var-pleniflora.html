Kraunhia sinensis var. pleniflora

Kraunhia sinensis var. pleniflora, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of garden settings.
