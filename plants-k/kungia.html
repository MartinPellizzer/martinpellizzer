Kungia

Kungia, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

These plants are characterized by their compact growth habit and showy, velvety flowers that come in a variety of colors. The leaves are typically dark green and velvety to the touch, often with a waxy coating. Kungia species are popular as ornamental plants due to their beauty and ease of care.

They thrive in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
