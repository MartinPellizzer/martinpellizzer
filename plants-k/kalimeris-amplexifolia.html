Kalimeris amplexifolia

Kalimeris amplexifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf fleur-de-lis, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy yellow flowers with a distinctive central disk surrounded by ray florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Kalimeris amplexifolia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
