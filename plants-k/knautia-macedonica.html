Knautia macedonica

Knautia macedonica, commonly known as Macedonian knautia, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and thrives in full sun. Its showy, pink to red flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.
