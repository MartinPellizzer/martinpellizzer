Kitagawia formosana

Kitagawia formosana, commonly known as the Formosan orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in mountainous regions. Its flowering period occurs during the spring, and it is highly valued for its ornamental beauty.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
