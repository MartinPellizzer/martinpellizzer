Kabulianthe honigbergeri

Kabulianthe honigbergeri, commonly known as the golden star cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in soft, white spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, which are clustered at the ends of the stems. Kabulianthe honigbergeri prefers well-drained soil and full sun exposure, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid climates.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for succulent gardens and rockeries.
