Knowltonia multiflora

Knowltonia multiflora, commonly known as the Florida flame flower, is a strikingly beautiful plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its large, showy, red-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in wetlands and swampy areas, thriving in moist, acidic soils. It has a woody stem with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Knowltonia multiflora is also known for its unique ability to produce a strong, spicy scent, which attracts pollinators like bees and butterflies.
