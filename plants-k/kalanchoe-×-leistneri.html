Kalanchoe × leistneri

Kalanchoe × leistneri, commonly known as the hybrid kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically during the winter months. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and long blooming period.

Kalanchoe × leistneri is also known for its ability to propagate through plantlets that grow along the edges of its leaves.
