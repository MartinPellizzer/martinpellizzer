Kyllinga odorata var. dunensis

Kyllinga odorata var. dunensis, commonly known as the Dunedin grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to New Zealand.

It features slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikes, which are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and wetland habitats.

Kyllinga odorata var. dunensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
