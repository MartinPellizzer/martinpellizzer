Kaempferia sawanensis

Kaempferia sawanensis, commonly known as the Sawan orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows as a rhizomatous herb, with fleshy, sword-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually borne on erect spikes and can display a variety of hues, including red, orange, and yellow.

Kaempferia sawanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
