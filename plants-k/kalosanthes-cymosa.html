Kalosanthes cymosa

Kalosanthes cymosa, commonly known as the clustered star orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Kalosanthes cymosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated for its delicate appearance.
