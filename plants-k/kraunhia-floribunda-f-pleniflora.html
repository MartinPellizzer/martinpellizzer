Kraunhia floribunda f. pleniflora

Kraunhia floribunda f. pleniflora, commonly known as the full-flowered common kraunhia, is a herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its extended flowering period provides continuous color throughout the growing season.
