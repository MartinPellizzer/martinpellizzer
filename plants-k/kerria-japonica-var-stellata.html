Kerria japonica var. stellata

Kerria japonica var. stellata, commonly known as the star kerria, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its fragrant, yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that turn red in the fall. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
