Koeleria cristata var. elegantula

Koeleria cristata var. elegantula, commonly known as the elegant hairgrass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features dense, tufted clusters of fine, silver-blue foliage that resemble a soft, flowing mane. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that are often used in ornamental gardening for their visual appeal. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its compact size and attractive appearance make it a popular choice for both landscaping and ecological restoration projects.
