Kneiffia velutina

Kneiffia velutina, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Kneiffia velutina is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.

It is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
