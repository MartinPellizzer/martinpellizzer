Kungia schoenlandii var. schoenlandii

Kungia schoenlandii var. schoenlandii, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers with a central orange or red disk, which bloom in spring and summer. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and is often grown as a ornamental in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Kungia schoenlandii var. schoenlandii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
