Kielmeyera pulcherrima

Kielmeyera pulcherrima, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the spring and summer. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

The tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
