Kerria japonica var. typica

Kerria japonica var. typica, commonly known as Japanese kerria, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan and Korea.

It features showy, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, often preceding the emergence of leaves. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves with serrated edges and a glossy green surface. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Kerria japonica var. typica is valued for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
