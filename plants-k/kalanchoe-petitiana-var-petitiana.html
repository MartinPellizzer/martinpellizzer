Kalanchoe petitiana var. petitiana

Kalanchoe petitiana var. petitiana, commonly known as the miniature kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically fleshy and covered in a fine layer of white, powdery wax. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow during the winter months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance, it is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative houseplant.
