Kentia chaunostachys

Kentia chaunostachys, commonly known as the Kentia palm, is a species native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that give it a graceful appearance. The plant has a slow growth rate and is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light conditions. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a waxy texture, contributing to its ornamental value.

Kentia chaunostachys is also noted for its ability to thrive in humid environments, making it a popular choice for tropical and subtropical gardens.
