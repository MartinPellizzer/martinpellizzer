Karpatiosorbus udvardyana

Karpatiosorbus udvardyana, commonly known as the Carpathian rowan, is a species of flowering plant native to the Carpathian Mountains in Central and Eastern Europe.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its small, white to pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces red berries in the autumn, which are a food source for various birds and small mammals.
