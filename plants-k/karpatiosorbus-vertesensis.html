Karpatiosorbus vertesensis

Karpatiosorbus vertesensis, commonly known as the Transylvanian rowan, is a rare and endangered species native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its small, yellowish-white flowers and red berries. The plant typically grows in rocky or forested areas at high altitudes. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have serrated edges.

Due to habitat loss and climate change, it faces significant conservation challenges.
