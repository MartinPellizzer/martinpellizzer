Kalanchoe × estrelae

Kalanchoe × estrelae, commonly known as the Estrela Kalanchoe, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often blooming in winter. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its low maintenance and striking appearance, it is widely cultivated as an ornamental plant.
