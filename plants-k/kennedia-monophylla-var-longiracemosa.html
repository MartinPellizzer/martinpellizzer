Kennedia monophylla var. longiracemosa

Kennedia monophylla var. longiracemosa, commonly known as the long raceme Kennedia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features slender, wiry stems and is characterized by its clusters of small, bright red flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are narrow and linear, often appearing in pairs along the stems. This variety is noted for its delicate, feathery appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

It prefers well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.
