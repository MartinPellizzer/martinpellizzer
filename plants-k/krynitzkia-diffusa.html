Krynitzkia diffusa

Krynitzkia diffusa, commonly known as the silverleaf krynitzkia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white, powdery wax, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
