Kigelianthe madagascariensis var. grevei

Kigelianthe madagascariensis var. grevei, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a unique and rare flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree bark in humid, shaded forest environments.

Its botanical significance lies in its distinctive morphology and limited distribution, making it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
