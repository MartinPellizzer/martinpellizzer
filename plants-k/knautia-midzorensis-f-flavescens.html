Knautia midzorensis f. flavescens

Knautia midzorensis f. flavescens, commonly known as the yellow-flowered knautia, is a rare and distinctive cultivar of the knautia species.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which contrast with the typical pink or purple hues of the parent species. The plant is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun conditions. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped, serrated leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to attract pollinators.
