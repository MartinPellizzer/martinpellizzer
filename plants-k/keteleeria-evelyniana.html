Keteleeria evelyniana

Keteleeria evelyniana, commonly known as the Evelyn's fir, is a species of conifer native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and dense, pyramidal crown. The tree produces small, cone-like structures that contain winged seeds, typical of many coniferous species. Its needles are short and glossy, providing a dense foliage that offers habitat for various wildlife.

This plant is often found in mountainous regions, thriving in cool, moist climates.
