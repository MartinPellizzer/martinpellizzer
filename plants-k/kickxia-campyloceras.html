Kickxia campyloceras

Kickxia campyloceras, commonly known as the woolly bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers with a distinctive woolly texture. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, contributing to its fuzzy appearance. It typically blooms in late spring to early summer, producing flowers in shades of pink to purple.

Kickxia campyloceras is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
