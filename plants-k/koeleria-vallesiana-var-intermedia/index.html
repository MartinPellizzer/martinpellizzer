Koeleria Vallesiana Var. Intermedia

Intermediate koeleria, scientifically known as Koeleria vallesiana var. intermedia, is a lesser-known but ecologically significant plant species native to the mountainous regions of western North America. This grass-like plant thrives in high-altitude environments, often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes where few other species can survive. Its unique adaptations to cold and arid conditions make it a resilient component of fragile ecosystems. Despite its beauty and ecological value, intermediate koeleria remains underappreciated and under-studied in botanical research. Understanding its biology and habitat is crucial for conservation efforts in rapidly changing mountain environments.
