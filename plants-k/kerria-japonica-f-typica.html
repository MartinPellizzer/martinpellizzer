Kerria japonica f. typica

Kerria japonica f. typica, commonly known as Japanese kerria, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan and Korea.

It features bright yellow, semi-double flowers that bloom in early spring, often before the leaves emerge. The plant has arching, slender stems with serrated, dark green leaves that turn red in autumn. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden borders or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant foliage and long-lasting blooms.
