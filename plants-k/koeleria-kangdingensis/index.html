Koeleria Kangdingensis

Kangding grass, scientifically known as Koeleria kangdingensis, is a rare and endemic plant species found exclusively in the high-altitude regions of Sichuan province in China. This delicate grass thrives in cold, rocky environments and is adapted to the harsh climatic conditions of its native habitat. Its discovery in the Kangding area has sparked significant interest among botanists and conservationists due to its unique ecological role. The plant is characterized by its slender stems and small, grass-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. Due to its limited distribution and fragile ecosystem, Kangding grass is considered a vulnerable species requiring careful protection.
