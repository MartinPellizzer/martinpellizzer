Kalanchoe laxiflora subsp. stipitata

Kalanchoe laxiflora subsp.

stipitata, commonly known as the Cape Town kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tint.

The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright sunlight to flourish.

Its resilience to drought makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
