Koeleria novozelandica var. parvula

Koeleria novozelandica var. parvula, commonly known as the small-flowered koeleria, is a low-growing perennial grass native to New Zealand.

It features slender, wiry stems and small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in open habitats such as grasslands and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
