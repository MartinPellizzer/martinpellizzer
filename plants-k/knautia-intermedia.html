Knautia intermedia

Knautia intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate knautia, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to purple daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and hairy, and its stems are often branched.

Knautia intermedia is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
