Karpatiosorbus mergenthaleriana

Karpatiosorbus mergenthaleriana, commonly known as the Carpathian rowan, is a rare and relict species native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and red berries. The plant typically grows in rocky or forested areas at high altitudes. Its leaves are elliptical with serrated edges, and it has a shrub-like growth habit.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
