Kalanchoe densiflora

Kalanchoe densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the winter months, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Kalanchoe densiflora is also known for its ability to propagate easily through stem cuttings.
