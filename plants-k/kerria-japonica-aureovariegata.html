Kerria japonica aureovariegata

Kerria japonica aureovariegata, commonly known as the golden variegated kerria, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features attractive, lobed leaves with a striking yellow and white variegation pattern. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow, single flowers in early spring. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its compact growth habit and seasonal color changes make it a versatile addition to landscaping.
