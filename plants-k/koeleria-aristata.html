Koeleria aristata

Koeleria aristata, commonly known as the needlegrass, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of North America.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny blue to purple flowers. It is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Koeleria aristata is a low-growing perennial that plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat.
