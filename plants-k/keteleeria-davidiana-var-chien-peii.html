Keteleeria davidiana var. chien-peii

Keteleeria davidiana var. chien-peii, commonly known as the Chien-Pei Chinese fir, is a variant of the Chinese fir native to eastern China.

It is a coniferous evergreen tree characterized by its needle-like leaves and cone-bearing structure. The plant belongs to the family Pinaceae and is valued for its timber and ecological role in forest ecosystems. It typically grows in mountainous regions with well-drained soils and moderate climates.
