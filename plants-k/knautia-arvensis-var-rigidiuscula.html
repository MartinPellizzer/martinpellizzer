Knautia arvensis var. rigidiuscula

Knautia arvensis var. rigidiuscula, commonly known as the rigid knautia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe.

It features upright, hairy stems that bear clusters of pink to purple flowers resembling those of the common knautia. The plant produces small, oval leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
