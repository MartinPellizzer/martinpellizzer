Kickxia sieberi

Kickxia sieberi, commonly known as the Siberian bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy, bell-shaped blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It belongs to the Campanulaceae family, which includes other bellflowers.

Kickxia sieberi is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
