Kickxia corallicola

Kickxia corallicola, commonly known as the coral-like bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, clustered fruits that contain the seeds.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.
