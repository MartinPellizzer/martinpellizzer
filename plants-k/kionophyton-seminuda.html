Kionophyton seminuda

Kionophyton seminuda, commonly known as the slender kionophyton, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming growth habit. The plant features narrow, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss. It produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and white during the spring and summer months.

Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
