Kambala

Kambala, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight to flourish. Its vibrant blooms come in a variety of colors, including purple, blue, and white.

Kambala is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
