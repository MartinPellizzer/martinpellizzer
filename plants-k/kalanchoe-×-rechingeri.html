Kalanchoe × rechingeri

Kalanchoe × rechingeri, commonly known as the Christmas chrysanthemum, is a hybrid plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces fleshy, succulent leaves with a waxy coating, which help it retain moisture in arid conditions. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its long blooming period and ability to thrive in various light conditions.

Kalanchoe × rechingeri is often used in floral arrangements and as a houseplant because of its low maintenance requirements.
