Kaempferia spoliata

Kaempferia spoliata, commonly known as the Purple Orchid or Spoliata Ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its striking purple and white flowers. The plant typically grows as a rhizomatous herb with fleshy, sword-shaped leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural and conservation purposes.
