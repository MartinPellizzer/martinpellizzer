Kunzea petrophila

Kunzea petrophila, commonly known as the rock kunzea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. It is often found in coastal and inland regions, thriving in well-drained conditions.

Kunzea petrophila is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
