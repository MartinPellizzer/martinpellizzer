Kohautia ramosissima

Kohautia ramosissima, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its segmented, branching stems that bear clusters of colorful flowers. The plant produces vibrant blooms in shades of red, pink, orange, and white, typically during the holiday season. Its leaves are modified into flattened, fleshy stems that store water, and it thrives in bright, indirect light with well-draining soil.

Kohautia ramosissima is a popular ornamental plant due to its long blooming period and ease of care.
