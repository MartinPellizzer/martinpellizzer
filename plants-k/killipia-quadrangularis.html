Killipia quadrangularis

Killipia quadrangularis, commonly known as the four-angle grass.

It is a perennial herb native to the coastal regions of southern Africa. The plant features slender, upright stems that are often quadrangular in cross-section, giving it its scientific name. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are typically arranged in dense spikes, and it produces small, three-lobed seeds.

Killipia quadrangularis is adapted to sandy or rocky soils and is often found in dune environments.
