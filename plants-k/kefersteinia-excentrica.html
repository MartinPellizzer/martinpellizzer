Kefersteinia excentrica

Kefersteinia excentrica, commonly known as the eccentric kefersteinia, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant produces clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Native to southern Africa, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun conditions.
