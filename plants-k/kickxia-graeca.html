Kickxia graeca

Kickxia graeca, commonly known as the Greek bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking blue or violet bell-shaped flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Kickxia graeca is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
