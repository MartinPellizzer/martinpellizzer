Kleinia madagascariensis

Kleinia madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and small, star-shaped flowers. The plant thrives in arid and rocky environments, showcasing adaptations to drought and nutrient-poor soils. Its unique morphology and ability to store water in its leaves make it well-suited for xerophytic conditions.

Kleinia madagascariensis is also notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.
