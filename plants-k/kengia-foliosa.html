Kengia foliosa

Kengia foliosa, commonly known as the flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant has a thick, woody trunk and is known for its rapid growth rate. Its leaves are compound, consisting of multiple leaflets arranged in a feather-like pattern.

Kengia foliosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant floral display and adaptability to various soil types.
