Knautia × intercedens

Knautia × intercedens, commonly known as the hybrid knautia, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its clustered, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, with lance-shaped leaves that are hairy and serrated. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from late spring to early autumn.
