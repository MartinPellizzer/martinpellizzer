Kosteletzkya Depressa

Kosteletzkya depressa is a fascinating flowering plant native to the southeastern United States. It thrives in wetland environments and is known for its unique, showy blooms. This species is often found in marshes and along the edges of ponds. Its ability to adapt to waterlogged soils makes it an important part of wetland ecosystems. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to the general public.
