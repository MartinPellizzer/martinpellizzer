Korthalsia angustifolia var. gracilis

Korthalsia angustifolia var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender leaf korthalsia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Apocynaceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant is native to tropical regions of Africa and is often found in moist, shaded environments. It has a compact growth habit and is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.
