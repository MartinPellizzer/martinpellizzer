Knema korthalsii subsp. rimosa

Knema korthalsii subsp.

rimosa, commonly known as the broad-leaved knema, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae. It is native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia, particularly in regions of Malaysia and Indonesia.

The plant is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are often glossy and dark green in color. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, followed by woody capsules containing numerous small seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
