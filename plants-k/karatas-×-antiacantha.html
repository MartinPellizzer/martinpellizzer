Karatas × antiacantha

Karatas × antiacantha, commonly known as the hybrid hibiscus, is a botanical cross between *Hibiscus syriacus* and *Hibiscus cannabinus*.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and its stems are often hairy and woody. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The flowers bloom in late summer, adding vibrant color to gardens throughout the growing season.
