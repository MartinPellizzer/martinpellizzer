Kigelia africana

Kigelia africana, commonly known as the sausage tree, is a deciduous tree native to Africa.

It is characterized by its large, elongated, woody fruits that resemble sausages, which contain a single, hard-shelled seed. The tree produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, with a strong scent. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in various cultures.
