Kungia schoenlandii

Kungia schoenlandii, commonly known as the red-stemmed milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive red stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many milkweed species known for their milky sap.
