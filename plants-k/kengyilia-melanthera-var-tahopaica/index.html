Kengyilia Melanthera Var. Tahopaica

Kengyilia melanthera var. tahopaica is a rare and fascinating plant native to the high elevations of the Andes. This variant of Kengyilia melanthera is known for its striking violet to purple flowers and its unique ecological adaptations. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats. Due to its limited distribution and fragile ecosystem, it is considered endangered by conservationists. Studying this plant offers valuable insights into the biodiversity and resilience of mountain flora.
