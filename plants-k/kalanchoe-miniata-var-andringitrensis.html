Kalanchoe miniata var. andringitrensis

Kalanchoe miniata var. andringitrensis, commonly known as the Andringitra kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of orange and yellow, which bloom during the dry season. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for collectors and gardeners in tropical and subtropical regions.
