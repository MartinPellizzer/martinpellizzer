Keetia ornata

Keetia ornata, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in dense clusters, often resembling flames. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate or elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Keetia ornata is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
