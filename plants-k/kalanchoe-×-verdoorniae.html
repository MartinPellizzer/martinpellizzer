Kalanchoe × verdoorniae

Kalanchoe × verdoorniae, commonly known as the hybrid kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle, which helps it retain water in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming in the spring or early summer. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its resilience and long-lasting blooms.

Kalanchoe × verdoorniae is also valued for its ability to thrive in well-draining soil and minimal water conditions.
