Kibara myrtoides

Kibara myrtoides, commonly known as the African myrtle, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes well-known plants like eucalyptus and clove. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, white or pink flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its bark and leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along riverbanks.
