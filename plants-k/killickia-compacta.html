Killickia compacta

Killickia compacta, commonly known as the dwarf rockrose, is a low-growing perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, compact growth with small, often blue or purple flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has aromatic, evergreen leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Killickia compacta is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
