Kelleronia splendens

Kelleronia splendens, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking yellow and green foliage. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Kelleronia splendens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
