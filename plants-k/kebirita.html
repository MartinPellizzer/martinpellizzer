Kebirita

Kebirita, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically has velvety, dark green leaves with a waxy texture. Kebirita thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
