Kalanchoe lubangensis

Kalanchoe lubangensis, commonly known as the lubangensis kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are often covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss.

Kalanchoe lubangensis is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance requirements and aesthetic appeal.
