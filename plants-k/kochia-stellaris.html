Kochia stellaris

Kochia stellaris, commonly known as burning bush, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, feather-like leaves that turn red or orange in the fall. The plant produces small, green flowers that develop into inflated, seed-filled pods. Kochia stellaris is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking seasonal color changes.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it adaptable to a variety of garden environments.
