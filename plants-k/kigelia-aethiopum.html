Kigelia aethiopum

Kigelia aethiopum, commonly known as the sausage tree, is a deciduous tree native to Africa.

It is characterized by its long, curved, sausage-like fruits that hang from the branches. The tree has smooth, grey bark and produces fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are large, heart-shaped, and alternate along the branches.

The plant is valued for its unique appearance and has traditional uses in various cultures.
