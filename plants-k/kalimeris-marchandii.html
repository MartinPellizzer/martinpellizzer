Kalimeris marchandii

Kalimeris marchandii, commonly known as the Chinese aster, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers range in color from white to pink and yellow.

Kalimeris marchandii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
