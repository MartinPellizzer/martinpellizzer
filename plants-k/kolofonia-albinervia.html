Kolofonia albinervia

Kolofonia albinervia, commonly known as the white-veined kolofonia, is a shrub native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive white veins on its leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its latex is milky and can be used in traditional medicine.
