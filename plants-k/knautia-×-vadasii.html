Knautia × vadasii

Knautia × vadasii, commonly known as the hybrid knautia, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is a natural hybrid between Knautia arvensis and Knautia montana, resulting in a plant with distinctive, fluffy, pink to purple flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils and full sun. Its foliage is hairy and lance-shaped, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.

Knautia × vadasii is valued for its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
