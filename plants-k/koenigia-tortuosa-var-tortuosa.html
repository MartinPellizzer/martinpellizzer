Koenigia tortuosa var. tortuosa

Koenigia tortuosa var. tortuosa, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a distinctive zigzag or wavy margin. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and unique appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
