Knema latericia f. olivacea

Knema latericia f. olivacea, commonly known as the olive-knema, is a variant of the knema plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree characterized by its glossy, ovate leaves that are typically a deep green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by fleshy, orange-red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland and hill forest ecosystems.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
