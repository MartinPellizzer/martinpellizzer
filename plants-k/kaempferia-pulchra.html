Kaempferia pulchra

Kaempferia pulchra, commonly known as the red ginger or flame ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a rhizomatous herb with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish tint. The plant produces striking, showy inflorescences with bright red or orange flowers that resemble those of ginger. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Kaempferia pulchra is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
