Kopsia aegyptiaca var. trinacria

Kopsia aegyptiaca var. trinacria, commonly known as the Trinacrian kopsia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub characterized by its upright growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from spring to early autumn.
