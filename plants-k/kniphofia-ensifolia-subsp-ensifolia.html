Kniphofia ensifolia subsp. ensifolia

Kniphofia ensifolia subsp.

ensifolia, commonly known as the swordleaf torch lily, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its tall, upright stems and large, sword-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green.

The plant produces showy, red-orange inflorescences that resemble torches, making it a popular ornamental species. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring minimal water once established.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
