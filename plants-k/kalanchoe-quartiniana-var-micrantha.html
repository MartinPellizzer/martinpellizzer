Kalanchoe quartiniana var. micrantha

Kalanchoe quartiniana var. micrantha, commonly known as the compact kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red during the winter months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

This variety is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms.
