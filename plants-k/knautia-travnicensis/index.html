Knautia Travnicensis

Knautia travnicensis is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Croatia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. This species is characterized by its vibrant pink to purple blooms and finely divided foliage. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
