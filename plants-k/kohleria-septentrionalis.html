Kohleria septentrionalis

Kohleria septentrionalis, commonly known as the northern kohleria, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy texture and a short, creeping stem structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value.

Kohleria septentrionalis is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
