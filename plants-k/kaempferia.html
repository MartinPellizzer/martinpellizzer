Kaempferia

Kaempferia, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Zingiberaceae.

These plants are native to Southeast Asia and are characterized by their striking, often colorful leaves that fold upward at night, resembling prayer. Some species are also used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
