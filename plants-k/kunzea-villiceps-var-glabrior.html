Kunzea villiceps var. glabrior

Kunzea villiceps var. glabrior, commonly known as the smooth kunzea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically smooth and glabrous. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathland and coastal environments.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and native landscaping.
